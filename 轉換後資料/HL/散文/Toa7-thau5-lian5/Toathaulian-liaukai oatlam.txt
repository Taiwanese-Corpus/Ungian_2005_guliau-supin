Lan2 kam2 liau2-kai2 Oat8-lam5
Toa7-thau5-lian5
(Hak8-seng Tai5-oan5 Gi2-bun5 Chhiok-chin3-hoe7 seng5-oan5)
Choe3-kin7 u7 peng5-iu2 beh khi3 Oat8-lam5 chhit-tho5, li2-heng5-thoan5 ka7 in kong2 Oat8-lam5 ti7-an bo5 ho2, chiN5 m7-thang theh8 chhut-lai5 sng3, bo5, kiaN e7 hou7 lang5 chhiuN2……chiah-e5 ai3 chu3-i3 e5 tai7-chi3, hou7 goa2 siuN7 tioh8 ka-ti7 chu5--ni5 khi3 Ou5-chi3-beng5-chhi7 chhit-tho5 e5 keng-giam7, hit chun7 goa2 khoaN3 siau7-kai3 Oat8-lam5 e5 li2-iu5-chheh, chheh--nih sia2 e5 chu3-i3 su7-hang7 ma7 kong2 ti7-an bo5 ho2, koan-kong-kheh am3-si5 siong7 ho2 m7-thang ti7 ke-a2-lou7 loa7-loa7-so5. M7-koh khi3 chit8 choa7 tng2--lai5, chiah hoat-hian7 Oat8-lam5 bo5 hiah khiong2-pou3.

Oat8-lam5, kong2--khi2-lai5 chiok seng5 ji7, saN-chap8 tang cheng5 e5 Tai5-oan5, tng beh ui3 long5-chhoan keng-che3 ng3 kang-giap8 choan2-heng5, khoan5-keng2 kiam2-chhai2 u7 khah la3-sap, koan-kong-kheh ma7 e7 hong5 kong3 phan2-a2, m7-koh Oat8-lam5-lang5 jiat8-cheng5 koh ho2 tau3-tin7. Hit pai2 khi3, chhiang5-chai7 u7 Oat8-lam5-lang5 mng7 goa2 si7 an2-choann2 e7 siuN7 beh khi3 in hia chhit-tho5, ki5-sit8 si7 thak8 iu2-koan Ou5 Chi3-beng5 leng2-to7 Oat8-lam5 jin5-bin5 tui-kiu5 tok8-lip8 kian3-kok e5 lek8-su2-chheh liau2-au7, chiah e7 siuN7 beh khi3 kam2-siu7--chit8-e7.

Ou5 Chi3-beng5 (it-pat-kiu2-khong3~it-kiu2-liok8-kiu2), chit e5 ji7-chap8 se3-ki2 e5 A-chiu kek-beng7-chia2, chhoa7-nia2 Oat8-lam5-lang5 ian2-to2 Hoat-kok kap Bi2-kok nng7 e5 te3-kok chu2-gi7 kok-ka, it-kiu2-su3-ngou2-ni5 kian3-lip8 Oat8-lam5 Bin5-chu2 Kiong7-ho5-kok (Pak-oat8), it-kiu2-chhit-liok8-ni5 kian3-lip8 Oat8-lam5 Sia7-hoe7 Chu2-gi7 Kiong7-ho5-kok. Oat8-lam5-lang5 tek-ko tau3 chhai3-to, bih ti7 pong7-khang lai7-te2, tui3-khong3 u7 sian-chin3 bu2-khi3 e5 Hoat-kok kap Bi2-kok. Ti7 Tiong-kok kun-tui7 beh kai3-jip8 Oat8-lam5 kai2-hong3 chian3-cheng e5 si5, Ou5 Chi3-beng5 bat kong2 koe3 “kam-goan7 phiN7 Hoat-kok-lang5 e5 chhau3-phui3, ma7 bo5-ai3 chiah8 Tiong-kok-lang5 e5 sai2.” Kian-chhi5 iong7 Oat8-lam5 ka7-ti7 e5 lek8-liong7 cheng-chhu2 bin5-chok8 tok8-lip8.

Ou5-chi3-beng5-chhi7 u7 chiann5 che7 Tai5-siong ti7 hia cho3 seng-li2, lai5 Tai5-oan5 e5 Oat8-lam5 lo5-kang ham7 Oat8-lam5 sin-niu5 ma7 chit8 tang pheng7 chit8 tang khah che7, m7-koh lan2 tui3 li7 Tai5-oan5 hiah-ni7 kin7 e5 Oat8-lam5 kap Ou5 Chi3-beng5 soah hiah-ni7 chhenn-hun7. Oat8-lam5 kian3-kok chin3-cheng5 e5 lek8-su2 kap Tai5-oan5 put-chi2-a2 sio-siang5, lan2 kam2 u7 beh khoaN3 in e5 lek8-su2 keng-giam7 thang chau2-chhoe7 lan2 ka-ti7 e5 chhut-lou7?
