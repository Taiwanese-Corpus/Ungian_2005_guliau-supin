
PhaiN2 tek chhut ho2 sun2;
Ki5-lin5 siN ku-lun2.

PhaiN2竹出好筍
麒麟生龜lun2

Chu3-kai2: Phou2-thong lang5 a2-si7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3 bo5-bak8-te7 e5 lang5 oe7 siN-chhut u7 chai5-tiau7, u7 kiat-chhut e5 kiaN2; Hoan2-bin7 khoaN3, ho2-khoaN3, u7 koan5, u7 lat8 e5 ki5-lin5 soah siN-chhut put-tiong3-iong7 e5 kiaN2-sun. Bok8-koai3 ki5-lin5 oe7 choat8-cheng2.
註解：普通人a2是hou7人看無目地e5人會生出有才tiau7,有傑出e5囝；反面看，好看、有權、有力e5麒麟soah生出不中用e5囝孫。莫怪麒麟會絕種。
