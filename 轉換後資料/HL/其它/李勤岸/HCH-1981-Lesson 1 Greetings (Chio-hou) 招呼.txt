Lesson 1, Greetings (Chio-hou) 招呼

Dialogue 2    

Teacher: Li2 ho2./ How are you!/ 你好。
Student: Li2 ho2./ How are you!/ 你好。
Teacher: Goa2 kong2 Tai5-oan5-oe7;/ I speak Taiwanese; / 我講台灣話;
     li2 thiaN u7--bo5?/ do you understand?/ 你聽有無？
Student: ThiaN bo5./ I don’t./ 聽無。
Teacher: Goa2 koh kong2--chit8-pian3./ I say it again./ 我koh 講一遍。
               An2-ne thiaN u7--bo5?/ Then do you understand?/ 按呢聽有無？
Student: ThiaN u7--ah.  To-sia./ I understand.  Thank you./ 聽有啊。多謝。
Teacher: Chit-ma2 ha7-kho3. Chai3-hoe7./ The class is over now.  Good-bye./ Chit-ma2 下課。再會。
Student: Chai3-hoe7./ Good-bye./ 再會。
