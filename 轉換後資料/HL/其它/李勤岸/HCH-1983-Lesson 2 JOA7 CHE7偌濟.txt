Lesson 2		JOA7 CHE7	偌濟		HOW MANY
#1983
Dialogue 1      
A.	Lin2 Tai5-gu2 kho3 u7 joa7-che7 hak8-seng?	How many students does your Taiwanese class have?
	恁台語課有偌濟學生？

B.	Goan2 u7 chap8-ji7  e5 hak8-seng.			We have twelve students.
阮有十二個學生。

A.	Lin2 u7 kui2 e5 lau7-su?				How many teachers do you have?
恁有幾個老師？
	
B.	Kan-na chit8 e5 nia7-tiaN7.			Only one.
干單一個nia7-tiaN7。

A.  An2-ne lau7-su chin sin-khou2.			So the teacher is very burdened.
按呢老師真辛苦。
