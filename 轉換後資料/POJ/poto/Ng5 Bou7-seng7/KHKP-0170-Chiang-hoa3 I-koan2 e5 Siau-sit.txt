
Chiang-hoa3 I-koan2 e5 Siau-sit
Ng5 Bou7-seng7 ki3--e5     
Ti7 Se-lek8 1897 ni5-thau5 Lan5 I-seng ti7 Chiang-hoa3 siat-lip8 I-koan2, kau3-taN u7 nng7 ni5 goa7. Kho2-sioh ti7 Chiang-hoa3 iau2-boe7 u7 sou2-chai7 thang hou7 peN7-lang5 toa3, put-ko3 chiam7-chhiaN2 eng7 pai3-tng5-piN e5 chhu3 cho3 tak8 jit8 khoaN3 cheng3 hoat-ioh8 e5 lou7-eng7 na7- tiaN7. Ia7 ti7 Toa7-sia7 u7 chhong3 chit8 e5 sou2- chai7 thang hou7 30 goa7 e5 phoa3-peN7 lang5 toa3, si7 in-ui7 Chiang-hoa3 joah8-thiN chin- hip, Sian-seN ai3 pi7-su2 hia, ia7 thang soa3 i- peN7 lai5 li7-ek lang5. Chha-put-to thong-ni5 8 goeh8 jit8 ti7-chia, 4 goeh8 jit8 ti7 Toa7-sia7. Goa2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 I-seng kong2 chit nng7-ni5- goa7 tu5 chhut-khi3 khoaN3--e5 i2-goa7, lai5 chiu7- i e5 lang5 ti7 Chiang-hoa3 u7 2291 lang5, ti7 Toa7-sia7 u7 1358 lang5; ke3-kiong7 3649 lang5. Chiah e5 lang5 chhe2-un5 tak8-e5 u7 lai5 chha- put-to 4,5 pai2, in khah-che7 si7 choh-sit- lang5. In e5 peN7 khah-che7 si7 kha-thiaN3, bak8-chiu thiaN3, koaN5-jiat8-cheng3. Ia7 ku7- ni5 u7 tang7 to-hoat--e5 si7 379 lang5.    
Ia7 tak8 jit8 u7 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 in thiaN, put- lun7 si7 ti7-chia a2-si7 ti7 Toa7-sia7 u7 tit-tioh8 Chiu--Sian-seN, Hau7-hi TiuN2-lo2 kap kui2-na7 lang5 e5 pang-chan7. Sui-bong2 hiah e5 lai5 i-koan2 e5 lang5, bo5 long2-chong2 tak8 le2-pai3- jit8 lai5 le2-pai3, m7-ku2 thang ng3-bang7 in khah chai Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2, ia7 pat8-jit8 beh hoat-seN kau3 kiat-sit8.    Chong2--si7 chit8-hang7 thang hou7 lan2 Tai5- lam5-siok8 e5 hoe7-iu2 in-ui7 Chiang-hoa3 lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3. Tui3 Moai5 Bok8-su Lan5 I-seng lai5 Chiang-hoa3 kau3 taN, hou7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 it-hoat khui-khoah. Chu2 e5 mia5-siaN it-hoat po3-iong5. In-ui7 Bok8-su chin7 sim- lek8, thoan5 Chu2 e5 to7, put-lun7 si5, put-lun7 te7, un-sun5 in2-chhoa7, pek-chhiat ka3-si7, liah8 ka-ti7 cho3 lou5-pok8. I-seng iah8 si7 an2- ni, thong koan5 tak8-pian3, bo5 kou3-chip hoat- tou7, thiaN3 Chu2, thiaN3 lang5, bo5 iau3-kin2 pun2- sin, put-lun7 saN-keN poaN3-me5, put-lun7 lou7 hng7, lou7 kin7, na7 u7 kin2-kip e5 peN7-cheng3, lai5 chhiaN2--i, i liam5-piN kap i khi3 i. Sou2- i2 khi3 goa7-bin7 i, si7 kiu3 chin-che7 lang5 bian2 si2. Na7 tu2-tioh8 peN7 siong-tiong7 e5 lang5, be7 i-tit, put-lun7 si7 bin7 phaiN2 khoaN3, a2-si7 chhau3-noaN7 phaiN2 bi7, bo5 lang5 kaN2 khoaN3 kaN2 oa2, I-seng chiu7 kong2, lan2 e5 sim pi2 che khah phaiN2. I chiu7 kong2 to7-li2 thak8 Seng3-chheh hou7 i thiaN, ti3-kau3 hou7 hak8-seng siu7 kam2-kek, than3 i e5 khoan2. Che- si7 tai7-liok8 kong2 in nng7-ui7 e5 Sian-seN hou7 lin2 thang hoaN-hi2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3. Na7 beh kong2 kau3 liau2 si7 chin-che7, chhin- chhiuN7 goa2 pun2-sin siu7 in e5 kam2-kek lai5 tit-tioh8 chin toa7 e5 li7-ek.    
Koh chit8-hang7 tioh8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 chia u7 sou2-chai7 thang hou7 peN7-lang5 toa3, khah u7 ki-hoe7 thang hou7 in bat to7-li2 kap peh8-oe7-ji7, soa3 bang7 hiah e5 chiu7 i e5 lang5 chiong sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5 to7-li2 khng3 ti7 in e5 sim lai5 chiu7-kin7 Chu2.    
Ia7 chit8-hang7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 u7 thiaN lan2 ki5-to2: Lok8-kang2, Tang-toa7-tun u7 siat- lip8 Seng3-hoe7.  Koh tioh8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 in na2 heng-ong7, na2 khui-khoah.  Ia7 tioh8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 Gu5-ma7-thau5, Gou7-chhe, Ka-tau5 hit-si3 e5 lang5 u7 Seng3-hoe7.  In-ui7 Moai5 Bok8-su kap chit8 e5 hiaN-ti7 Hok- chhun hiaN khi3 hia kong2 to7-li2, chin ho2 ki-hoe7, ti7 Gu5-ma7-thau5 u7 soe3 chit8-keng chhu3 cho3 hioh kha e5 sou2-chai7, si7 Hok- chhun-hiaN toa3 ti7-hia tak8 jit8 si3-ke3 kong2 to7-li2, tak8 le2-pai3 u7 chap8-goa7 lang5 tiaN7-tioh8 teh chu7-chip8. Tui3 hia koe3 jip8 khi3 thiaN-e5, u7-si5 chap8-goa7 lang5,u7-si5 ji7-chap8 goa7- lang5. Ia7 koh Hoan-oah hit-si3 e5 lang5 ia7 chin-che7, lan2 tioh8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 Toa7- oh8 na2 che7 lang5 ai3 khi3 thak8, bian2 tit khiam3 kang.   
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 170 koan3,1899 ni5 5 geh8).
