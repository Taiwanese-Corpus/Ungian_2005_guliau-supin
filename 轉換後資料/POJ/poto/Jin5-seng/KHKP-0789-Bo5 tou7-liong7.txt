

 
Bo5 tou7-liong7
Jin5-seng
U7 chit8 jit8, Siong7-te3 chiau3 I e5 kong-gi7 sim2-phoaN3 lang5, Chiu7 kio3 thiN-sai3 chu7-chip8 cheng3-lang5, chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5 kau3 I e5 kong-cho7-cheng5.  Tak8 lang5 chiau3 in e5 sou2 kiaN5 lai5 po3-eng3 in.  Kau3 chit8 e5 cha-bou2-lang5, peng5-seng sou2 cho3 chin bo5 kho2-chhu2, chiu7 kio3 thiN-sai3 beh ka7 i thoa khi3 hiat ti7 te7-gek8 chhim-ian.  Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chit8-e7 beh kau3 chhim-ian e5 khang-khau2 e5 si5, hng7-hng7 khoaN3-tioh8 hoe2-ian kap hoe2-chih8 chhin-chhiuN7 kau-liong5 teh ka2 chui2, chin thang kiaN e5 khoan2-sit, chit8-chun7 chit8-chun7 tui3 chhim-ian chheng3 kau3 khong-tiong.  Mui2 pai2 khoaN3 hoe2 chheng3 chiuN7 koan5 e5 si5, chiu7 thiaN-tioh8 chhi-chham2 ai-khau3 e5 siaN.  Hit e5 pi-chham2 e5 au5-kio3, sit8-chai7 kau3-giah8 hou7 thiaN e5 lang5 e7 chhang3 mng5-kng2.  Koh khoaN3 che7-che7 Mou5-kui2 seN choe3 chin phaiN2 khoaN3 e5 heng5-chong7, pe2-siu2 ti7 chhim-ian e5 khang-khau2.  Chit8-e5 chit8-e5 eng7 thih-chhe ka7 lang5 chhe--loh8-khi3.  Hiat chit8 e5 loh8-khi3, chiu7 khoaN3-tioh8 Mou5-kui2 hian2-chhut in im-hiam2, leng2-chhio3, giang3-ge5 hoaN-hi2 e5 bin7.  Na7-si7 mui2 pai2 thiN-sai3 bo5 m7 ui7-tioh8 siu7 hiat-loh8 e5 leng5-hun5 lai5 lau5 bak8-sai2.
Hit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 khoaN3-tioh8 chit e5 Cheng5-keng2, chap8-ji7-hun e5 kiaN, m7-kaN2 koh chin3-cheng5, tit8-tit8 khau3 oan-ong2, oan-ong2, kiu5 Siong7-te3 tai3-liam7 i bat choe3 tam7-poh8 e5 ho2-su7; chiu7 kun3-kiong7 m7-goan7 siu7 hiat-loh8 ti7 chhim-ian.
Siong7-te3 chiu7 kio3 hit e5 thiN-sai3 ka7 i mng7; i it-seng tau3-ti2 bat choe3 sim2-mih8 ho2.  I chiu7 kong2, bou2 ni5 bou2 goeh8 bou jit8, in tng teh piaN3 han-chu5 e5 si5, bat u7 chit8 e5 khit-chiah8 pun tui3 in hia koe3, kio3 iau, ka7 i chhun tng5-chhiu2 e5 si5, i u7 tui3 han-chu5-tui keng2 chit8 tiau5 3, 4 niu2 tang7 e5 pun i.  Siong7-te3 kong2 an2-ni ho2, kio3 i khiu2 hit tiau5 han-chu5 kap han-chu5 tin5-a2 ka7 i giu2 to3 khi2-lai5.  Bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 i e5 sin-piN u7 chit8 e5 chhu3-piN po5-a2 khoaN3-tioh8 i hiah ho2-un7, u7 thang hou7 thiN-sai3 ka7 i giu2 to3 khi2-khi3, chiu7 kin2 tui3 i e5 saN-a2-ki ka7 i giu2--leh; hiam3 kong2, “Ho2-liang7 chim2-a2, hou7 goa2 png7 hok-khi3 leh,” chu7 an2-ni khiN5 i e5 saN-a2-ki koh siu7 giu2 to3 khi2-khi3.   
Kau3 poaN3 khong-tiong, hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 hut-jian5 kam2-kak kong2 goa2 thai2 e7 chiah tang7, si7 siaN2-hoe3 ka7 goa2 khiN5--leh, goa2 chit tiau5 seN3-mia7 han-chu5-kin-a2 na7 tng7--khi3, chiu7 si2.  AN3-thau5 chit8 khoaN3, tou si7 chit8 e5 chhu3-piN a-po5 ah beh png7 i e5 hok-khi3 lai5 bang7 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  I chit8 si5 chin siu7-khi, chhut siaN ka7 i me7 kong2, “Li2 beh si2, m7-bian2 lai5 teh kau-tiN5,” chu7 an2-ni khui kha eng7 kha-chiN ka7 i cham3--loh8-khi3.  Hit e5 a-po5-a2 aih8 chit8-e7, chu7 an2-ni siak to3 loh8-khi3.  Chong2-si7 bo5 phah-sng3 i sou2 giu2 e5 han-chu5-tin5-a2 ia7 soa3 tng7--khi3. Hut-jian5 thiaN chit8 e5 siaN kong2, “Hu7-jin5-lang5 ah! kho2-sioh li2 chin bo5 liong7.” 
Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chu7 an2-ni khui siaN khau3—chhut-lai5.  HiahN cheng-sin5, chiah chai i si7 bang7-kiN3.  Tui3 hit si5 khi2, i toa7-toa7 hoan2-hoe2, jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2, koh jit8-jit8 sit8-heng5 u7 tou7-liong7.
ChhiaN2 thak8 I Iok-han7 te7 4 chiuN 20, 21, “Na7 u7 lang5 kong2, goa2 thiaN3 Siong7-te3, ia7 oan3-hun7 i e5 hiaN-ti7, chiu7-si7 peh8-chhat8--e5; in-ui7 be7 thiaN3 sou2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 e5, chiu7 be7 thiaN3 sou2 be7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 Siong7-te3.  ThiaN3 Siong7-te3 e5 ia7 tioh8 thiaN3 i e5 hiaN-ti7, che si7 lan2 tui3 I sou2 siu7 e5 beng7-leng7.”
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 789 ho7, 1954 ni5 9 geh8)
