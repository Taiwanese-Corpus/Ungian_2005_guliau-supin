
Pian-chip8 Hoe5-eng3
Chin to-sia3 Tai5oanji7 e5 goa7-kok thok8-chia2 ka7 goan2 chi-chhi5 kap kou2-le7. “Tai5-oan5 Gi2-bun5 Lo5-ma2-ji7-hoa3” si7 goan2 chit hun7 khan-but8 e5 bok8-phiau. Lan2 ma7 chai-iaN2 che m7 si7 chit8 hang7 kan2-tan e5 tai7-chi3. Kap lan2 tui3-tek8..e5 si7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 e5 Han3-jin5 e5 jin7-tong5 kap Han3-ji7 i3-sek. Lan2 khia7 ti7 chit e5 Tai5-oan5 lek8-su2 siong7 e5 sip8-ji7 lou7-khau2, to2-goa7 u7 hiong-ok e5 Tiong-kok teh tng-tan2, to2-lai7 u7 kok-ka jin7-tong5 loan7-chhau-chhau e5 kok-bin5 teh chhi7-bu2 chhi7- chha7. Tai5-oan5-lang5 i5-it e5 oah8-lou7 chi2-u7 chit8 tiau5: thoat-li7 Tiong-kok/kiaN5-jip8 se3-kai3, chhiat-tng7 Han3-chok8 ui-toa3/lam2-pho7 Hai2-iuN5 Te7-kiu5.
Tai5oanji7 bak8-cheng5 iu5-goan5 si7 Ko-hiong5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Gian2-sip8-hoe7 e5 hoe7-khan. Bian2-hui3 sang3 hou7 Hoe7-oan5 thak8. Ki5-tha thok8-chia2 na7 u7 siuN7 beh tau3 chan3-chou7, e7-sai2 koan-khoaN2 hou7 pun2 Gian2-sip8-hoe7. Lou2-lat8.
