
 
Sau3-chiu2 chheN
Ho5 Hi-jin5
Bo5 loa7-ku2 ti7 thiN-teng2 teh-beh chhut chit8 liap8 siang7 te7 it toa7 e5 sau3-chiu2 chhiN .  Ti7 Sin-lek8 5 goeh8 thau5 e7 khoaN3 te7 it beng5.  Phah-sng3 hit-tiap8 lan2 e5 bak8-sin5  kim-kim khoaN3 e7 tioh8 .  Chit liap8 chhiN un7-choan2 chit8 chiu-ui5 tioh8 75 ni5 ku2 .  Tak8 me5-jit5 sou2 kiaN5 e5 lou7 300,0000 e5 Tai5-oan5 li2.  Chiau3 an2-ni sng3 chit8 hun ku2 sou2  kiaN5 e5 lou7 si7 2000 goa7 Tai5-oan5 li2 .  Chit liap8 chhiN cheng5 pai2 chhut-hian7 ti7 Ia5-sou  kang3-seng 1835 ni5.  Lang5 an3-chaiN2-iuN7e7 seng chai, ti7 sim2-mih8 ni5 , sim2-mih8  goeh8 i e7 hoat-hian7 ? In-ui7 ti7 1682 ni5 u7 chit8 e5 kang2-kiu3 thian-bun5 kai3 gau5 e5  phok-hak8 su eng7 chhian- li2-kiaN3 siong5-se3  khoaN3 thiN u7 toa7 liap8 e5 sau3-chiu2 chheN ,  chhui-sng3 hit liap8 chheN un7-choan2 thiN-teng2 e5 jit8 , tioh8 u7 75 ni5 tou7 chhut-hian7  chit8-pai2 .  I chiu7 chha5-kho2 tak8-kok cheng5 e5 su2-ki3 ; ko2-jian5 e7 tit chai ti7 1607 ni5 ,  1531 ni5 , 1456 ni5 , chi2 3 ni5 kek8 toa7 e5 sau3-chiu2 chheN u7 sio-soa3 chhtu-hian7, 75  ni5 tu2-tu2 chit8 tian5 tou7.  Kho2-kian3 beng5-beng5 e5 pin5-ku3 si7 siang7 hit liap8 chhiN teh  un7-choan2.  I chiu7 chham3-gu2 ti7 1758 ni5 e7 koh chhut-hian7.  Kau3 si5 ko2-jian5 u7 iaN2 .  TaN bo5 loa7-ku2 chit liap8 chheN beh koh-chai3 lai5 .      
Cheng5 Tai5-oan5 lang5 khoaN3 chit ho7 chheN liah8 cho3-koai3 kong2 , thiN-i3 teh-  beh hoan2-loan7.  Cha2-si5 pat8 kok ia7 si7 ho5 koh-iuN7. U7-e5 liah8 cho3 ho2 tiau7-thau5 ;  chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 pai2 ti7 1066 ni5 , Au-lo5-pa chit8-e5 tai7-sin5 liah8 cho3 si7 i bo5 loa7-ku2  beh cho3 Eng-kok e5 ong5.  Ti7 1456 ni5 liong2 kun e5 peng tui3-bin7 chat-iaN5 beh   sio-thai5 khoaN3-kiN3 chit liap8 chheN chiu7 kiaN, chhong-hong5 to5-chau2.  Kou2 cha2 e5  lang5 na7 khoaN3-kiN3 sau3-chiu2 chheN oan5-a3 si7 liah8 cho3 phaiN2 tiau7-thau5, kong2 si7 teh-  beh sio-thai5 , ki-hng , chai-e7 , kok-ong5 si2 , chiah e5 tai-chi3 .      
TaN lan2 tioh8 than3 chit si5 kiah8-bak8 khoaN3-thiN , in-ui7 ti7 1985 ni5 lan2 chit poe3 e5 lang5 tai7-khai3 kaN2 chhoe7 bo5 kui-e5 ti7 chit te7-bin7-chiuN7 , thang koh-chai3 khoaN3 chit liap8 chheN ; i2-keng kui ti7 chhong3-cho7 chheN-sin5 u2-tiu7 ban7-mih8 e5 toa7 Chu2- chaiN2. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 299 koan3, 1910 ni5 2 geh8)
