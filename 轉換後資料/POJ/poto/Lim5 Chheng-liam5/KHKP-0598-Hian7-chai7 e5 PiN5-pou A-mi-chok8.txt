
Hian7-chai7 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8 kap A-mi-chok8
(Lim5 Chheng-liam5)
Teh-beh kong2-khi2 hian7-chai7 e5 su7, ia7 tioh8 kong2 tam7-poh8 ke3-khi3 e5 cheng5-heng5 lai5 chiap-soa3.
In-ui7 goa2 u7 thak8-tioh8 12 geh8 ho7 eK5au-hoe7-po3, u7 chit8 phiN thoe3 PiN5-pou-chok8 teh pian7-kai2 in e5 oan-ong2, goa2 e5 sim put-chi2 siu7 kam2-tong7, sou2-i2 sia2 chitphiN choan-choan lai5 lun7 Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8.  (Ia7 au7-lai5 soa3 lun7 tang-pou7e A-mi-chok8).
Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8, pun2 u7 36 sia7,40 goa7 ni5 cheng5, in e5 lang5-giah8 put-chi2 choe7, kau3-hoe7 ia7 chin heng-khi2.  Hit-tiap8 in chin hu3-ju7, in-ui7 in u7 “kong-chou,” “su-chou” thang siu.  Chiu7-si7 ku7 cheng3-hu2 e5 si5-tai7, u7 tek8-piat8 khoaN2-thaiin7.  Ti7 tak8 sia7 e5 hu7-kun7 e5 thou2-toe7,Han3-jin5 na7 khi3 khai-khun2, kau3 siu-seng5 e5 si5, mui2 ni5 tioh8 lap8-chou, kau in e5 thau5-bak8.  Thau5-bak8 tu5 kong-hui3 i2-goa7,chhun--e5, tioh8 chiau3 teng, khau2, pun hou7 tak8 hou7.  (Hit-si5 kau3-hoe7 e5 keng-hui3, bo5 tui3 ko3-jin5 toe5, long2 si7 thau5-bak8 tui3 kong-chou lai5 chi-chhut).  Su-chousi7 in na7 boe7 chhan5 bo5 to2-tng7, boe2 chhan5 e5 lang5, mui2 ni5 iu5-goan5 tioh8 lap8 tam7-poh8 hou7 boe7-chu2.  Chit nng7 khoan2---“Kong-chou,” “su-chou,” chong2 mia5 kio3-choe3“hoan-chou.” In-ui7 u7 chit-khoan2 “hoan-chou” thang siu, sou2-i2 in (Pin5-pou-chok8)khah pin5-toaN7.  U7 lang5 bian2 sim2-mih8 choe3-kang, iu5-goan5 oe7-tit seng-oah8.
Kau3 sin cheng3-hu2 leng2 Tai5 liau2-su7,keng-ke3 10 sou3 ni5, chiu7 chit khoan2 e5 “hoan-chou” ka7 i hoe3-bo5.  U7 eng7 Kong-che3-koan3 (kio3-choe3 “Pou2-chhiong-kim”),lai5 pou2-chou7 in.  Kho2-sioh in boe7-hiau2 po2-siu2, keng-ke3 bo5 kui2 ni5, chiu7 long bo5--khi3.  Tui3 an2-ni, in e5 seng-oah8 khah khun3-lan5, kou-put-chiong tioh8 si3-soaN3, si3-koe3 khi3 chhe7 thau5-lou7, tui3 an2-i2, tak8 sia7 e5 jin5-sou3 chiam7-chiam7 khah chio2, u7-e5 chhun 10 sou3 hou7, u7-e5 chhun 5, 6 hou7,u7 sou2-chai7 long2 bo5.
TaN in e5 si3-soaN3 tah-loh8 khi3 ?
1.Ui-tioh8 seng-oah8-siong7 e5 koan- he7, in e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khah ai3 ke3 Han3- jin5. Sou2-i2 hou7 Han3-jin5 kap Lai7-toe7 lang5 chhoa7 chin-choe7 khi3.  Tui3 an2-ni, in e5 siN-thoaN3, tit8-tit8 kiam2-chio2, sou2-i2 hian7-chai7 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8 khah chio2.
2. Hian7-chai7 sou2 chun5-chai7 Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8, toa7-pou7-hun7 si7 chhian-soa2 ti7 Tang-pou7.  Chong2-si7 ti7 Koe-lang5, Tai5-pak ia7 u7.  Tang-pou7 te7-it choe7 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7-si7 ti7 Hoe-lian5-kang2 koe hu7-kun7 e5 toe7-hng, te7-ji7 si7 ti7 Tai5-tang koe hu7-kun7, ia7 Giok8-li2 chng, Ma2-tai7-an, Li2-hi5-be2 long2 u7.  Koh chit8 pou7-hun7 si7 khia7-khi2 ti7 Tang-pou7 e5 ian5-hai2, tui3 Iam5-lian5-khiN thau3 kau3 Toa7-chiam-chioh8, chiau3 goa2 sou2-chai, u7 pah-goa7 hou7. 
TaN goa2 sia2 chiah e5 lek8-su2 beh chhong3 sim2-mih8 ?  Chiu7-si7 tui3 kau3-hoe7 chit hong-bin7, put-chi2 u7 koan-he7 ti7-teh.  In-ui7 goa2 bat khoaN3-tioh8 siau3-lian5 e5 Thoan5-kau3-su, ia7 kong2-tioh8 Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8, chiu7 hian2-chhut iam3-chian7 kap oan3-hun7 e5 khoan2-sit. Goa2 khoaN3-liau2 put-chi2 siong-sim, in-ui7 lan2 na7 chhut-chai7 in teh pang3-sak to7-li2, si7 lan2 hian7-tai7 Thoan5-kau3-ka e5 kian3-siau3 !  I2-cheng5 e5 lang5 chio-hou, ia7 lan2 boe7-oe7 poe5-iuN2 ! (Che tioh8 chu3-i3).  
Hian7-chai7 iau2 ti7 Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8, tui3 ti7 chong-kau3 sim2-mih8 khoan2 ?  Toa7-pou7-hun7 iau2 si7 Ki-tok-kau3.  Sui-jian5 in e5 sia7-lai7 pai3-tng5 bo5--khi3, m7-si7 long2-chong2 bo5.  U7-e5 si7 i5-choan2 ti7 khah lau7-jiat8 e5 sou2-chai7. 
Hian7-si5 Lam5-iong5 sam-kun7 (goan5-pun2 36 sia7 e5 toe7-hng), iau2 u7 8 keng e5 pai3-tng5 kap kang2-gi7-sou2, tak8 keng long2 u7 PiN5-pou-chok8 kap Han3-jin5 saN-kap le2-pai3.  Sui-jian5 u7 chit8 poaN3 keng bo5, iau2- ku2 u7 kui2-na7 keng choe3 le2-pai3 e5 lang5-giah8, si7 PiN5-pou-chok8 khah choe7.  Sui-jian5 u7-e5 li7 pai3-tng5 khah hng7, bo5 lai5 choe3 le2-pai3.  Thoan5-kau3-su na7 chai-iaN2, thang khi3 chio-hou in, in-ui7 in iu5-goan5 si7 Ki-tok-kau3.
 

Hian7-chai7 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8 kap A-mi-chok8

(Lim5 Chheng-liam5)
Gi5-lan5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8 chhian-soa2 ti7 Tang-pou7--e5, tui3 chong-kau3 sim2-mih8 khoan2.
1.Khia7-khi2 ti7 Hoe-lian5-kang2 hu7-kun7 e5 sou2-chai7, 10 hun7 u7 9 hun7 siong5-siong5 siu2 le2-pai3, hok8-sai7 Chu2.
2.Khia7-khi2 ti7 Tai5-tang hu7-kun7 e5 sou2-chai7, chha-put-to 10 hun7 e5 9 hun7 li7-khui to7-li2, piN3-choe3 se3-siok8.
ChaiN2-iuN7 an2-ni koh-iuN7 hiah choe7 ?
Chiau3 phi2-jin5 chhui-li2, chiu7-si7 tui3 sia tkau3 ti7 Hoe-lian5-kang2 e5 toe7-hng u7 30 cho2-iu7 ni5 ku2.  Lek8-lai5 tam-jim7 e5 thoan5-to7-su 9 mia5, ki5-tiong 6 mia5 i2-keng bat keng-ke3 tiam3 Gi5-lan5 e5 toe7-hng. (Hit 6 mia5 chiu7-si7 :---- Kai Pat-po2, Lim5 Chheng-liam5, Chan Chheng-kang, Li2 Chui2-chhia, Lau5 Iok-han7, Keh Cheng).
Chiah e5, pun2-jian5 kap in (PiN5-pou-chok8) saN-bat, ia7 chai in e5 seng3-cheng5,lai5 in2-chhoa7 in, kap in chhin-chhiat kau-poe5, ti3-kau3 in bo5 pang3-sak to7-li2.
Na7-si7 Tai5-tang lek8-lai5 e5 thoan5-to7-su,m7-bat u7 chit8 e5 i2-cheng5 bat keng-ke3 toa3 Gi5-lan5 e5 toe7-hng, sou2-i2 m7-chai hiah e5 PiN5-pou-chok8 pun2-jian5 si7 Gi5-lan5 e5 hoe7 iu2, ti3-kau3 sit-loh8 poe5-iuN2, tui3 an2-ni in (PiN5-pou-chok8) khah choe7 bo5 lai5 le2-pai3, au7-lai5 soa3 pai3 ngou2-siong7.  In-ui7 in (PiN5-pou-chok8)e5 seng3 chin loan2-jiok8, na7 bo5 ti3-i3 lai5 poe5-iuN2 in, chiu7 chin khoai3 siu7-be5.
3. Khia7-khi2 ti7 ian5-hai2 e5 sou2-chai7 eP5iN-pou-chok8, khah siau3-lian5--e5 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 to7-li2, khah choe7 he3--e5, sui-jian5 i2-cheng5 bat nia2 soe2-le2,chit-tiap8 teh pai3 kui2-sin5.
Si3 ni5 cheng5, goa2 u7 kap Li2 Chui2-chhia Thoan5-to7-su choe3-tin7 khi3 hai2-khau2. keng-ke3 kui2-na7 sia7,u7 chhe7-tioh8 3, 4 kee5 hoe7-iu2, iau2 teh him-bou7 to7-li2.  Chit8 ke si7 goan5 Thoan5-to7-su Kai Pat-po2 e5 ka-chok8.  In khoaN3-tioh8 goan2 khi3, u7 i3-goa7e hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i2-cheng5 be7-bat u7 thoan5-kau3-su kau3 hit sou2-chai7.  Chiu7 tiau3-chip8 kui2-na7 chap8 lang5 lai5 thiaN to7-li2.  Kho2-sioh siau3-lian5 lang5 kap hu7-jin5-lang5 Han3-oe7 thiaN boe7 chheng-chho2, boe7 siu7 kam2-tong7.
Au7-pai2 goa2 koh khi3 khah choe7 ui7, iu7-koh chhe7-tioh8 kui2-na7 ke sin3 Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7.  (Hiah e5 su7-cheng5 i2-cheng5 goa2 i2-keng bat sia2 loh8 Kau3-hoe7-po3).
Hoan-tng2 ni5, u7 chit8 e5 lau7 hiaN-ti7(PhoaN Tah), tui3 Giok8-li2 lai5 chhoa7 goa2 poaN5-soaN kiaN5-lou7 kau3 Chioh8-hou7-soaN3,keh jit8 koh ian5 hai2-kiN5 chiuN7 pak, keng-ke3 2, 3 jit8 chiah kau3 Chui2-lian5-be2.  Chiu7 chai Tang-pou7 chit choa7 ian5-hai2 si7 sim2-mih8 teng2 ho7 e5 lang5 teh khia7-khi2 Khah lam5-peng5, chiu7-si7 Pou-nih lang5 (goan5-toe2 si7 Lang-pou7 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8) kap A-mi-chok8 teh khia7-khi2.  Khah pak-peng5,si7 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8 (goan2-toe2 si7 Gi5-lan5 soa2-lai5 e5 PiN5-pou-chok8.  Ti7-chia in chheng-choe3 Ka-le2-oan2-a2) kap A-mi-chok8 the khia7-khi2.
Ti7 hit sou2-chai7 e5 Ka-le2-oan2-a2 kap A-mi-chok8 teh tong5-hoa3, lan2 khoaN3-kiN3 in bo5 siaN2 chai si7 Ka-le2-oan2-a2, a2-si7 A-mi-a2, in-ui7 in teh saN kau-poe5, saN ke3-chhoa7.	
Goa2 i2-cheng5 u7 kong2, Gi5-lan5 PiN5-pou7-Chok8 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ai3 ke3 Han3-jin5,taN ti7 chia(Tang-pou7)A-mi-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ai3 ke3 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8,(chiu7-si7 PiN5-pou7-chok8). U7-e5 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8 hou7 A-mi chok8 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chio,chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 the saN-chham.	
In teh kong2-oe7,A-mi chok8 oe7-hiau2 Ka-le2-oan2-a2 oe7, Ka-le2-oan2 chok8 ia7 oe7-hiau2 A-mi-a2 oe7. Phi2-jin5 teh ng3-bang7,tioh8 u7 chit8 e5 thoan5-to7-su,choan-jim7 lai5 choe3-kang ti7 chit e5 hai2-khau2 e5 sou2-chai7,ng2-bang7 oe7 in2-chhoa7 chiah e5 PiN5-pou chok8 lai5 hok8-to3 chin-li2,is7 soa3 thoan5 Hok-im ti7 A-mi chok8 e5 tiong-kan.  Chong2-si7 teh-beh choe3 chit khoan2 e5 kang, m7-si7 sui5-pian7 e5 thoan5-kau3-su sou2 oe7 tit thang choe3--e5.  In-ui7:----
(1) Tioh8 sin-the2 iong2-kiaN7, oe7 kham-tit lo5-tong7, in-ui7 hoe7-iu2 si3-soaN3, lou7-toukhi5-khu oan2-siap8.
(2) Tioh8 kam-goan7 hi-seng, thun-lun2 kan-khou2, bo5 khoaN3-khin hoan-chok8.
(3) Tioh8 u7 chin-sit8 ai3 kiu3 leng5.
(4) Tioh8 bat in e5 khiuN-khau2 (ti7-hiae5 PiN5-pou chok8, Han3-oe7 boe7 thong).
Hian7-si5 goa2 khoaN3 u7 chit8 ui7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chin hah8, chiu7-si7 Khou2 Lam5-bian sang3.  I sit3-chai7 u7 thoan5-kau3 e5 cheng-sin5, chinti3-i3 in e5 pun2-chok8 kap A-mi chok8, lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  (I pun2-sin si7 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8, i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 si7 A-mi chok8).  Ie5 sin-the2 ia7 boe7 lam2, i ui7-tioh8 chin-li2 chin kam-goan7 tioh8-boa5.  Ka-le2-oan2 chok8, A-mi chok8, Han3-oe7, Kok-gu2, 4 khoan2 e5 oe7, i long2 chin lau2-lian, na7 beh thoan5-kau3 chin li7-pian7.  Goa2 bat kap i choe3-tin khi3 hai2-khau2 chit8 choa7, thiaN iteh kong2 to7-li2, u7-si5 eng7 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8 e5 oe7, u7-si5 eng7 A-mi chok8 e5 oe7, thiaN e5 lang5 chin siu7 kam2-tong7, u7-e5 kong2, lin2 na7 khah siong5 lai5 kong2, hou7 goan2 khah beng5-pek8, goan2 e5 ngou2-siong7 chiu7 behhiN3-sak.
Ti7 sin-lek8 12 geh8 tiong, u7 siu-tioh Khou2 Lam5-bian2 (許南勉) sang3, kap Chhoan-to2 (川島文治) sang3, in nng7 lang5 e5 phoe, kong2 in teh-beh chhut-hoat khi3 hai2-khau2 pou3-to7.  Au7-lai5 na7 u7 tit-tioh8 ho2 siau-sit, phi2-jin5 oe7 koh po3 hou7 liat8-ui7 chai.Chit e5 Chhoan-to2 sang3, si7 sun5-chui7 e5 A-mi chok8, ia bat Kok-gu2 kap Han3 oe7.  Chit nng7 lang5 (Khou2 sang3 kap Chhoan-to2 sang3) u7 siong-tong e5 ti3-sek, tui3 jip8 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 koh chhut-lai5, sin-giong2 sim chin toa7, thoan5-kau3 e5 cheng-sin5 hoat8-phoat, in choe3 A-mi chok8 e5 thoan5-kau3-chia2, chiong-lai5 oe7 he7 chin ho2 e5 toe7-ki.  Lan2 chhut tam7-poh8 chiN lai5 hou7 in choe3 thoan5-kau3 e5 sou2-hui3, si7 chin u7 ke3-tat8 !  Liat8-ui7 iu2-sim thiaN3 Chu2, thiaN3 lang5 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7 ah !  Ng-bang7 lan2 oe7 siau3-liam7-tioh8 chiah-e5 tiam3 ti7 am3-am3 e5 A-mi chok8, kap ti7-hia sit-be5 e5 Ka-le2-oan2 chok8, in si7 lan2 e5 sio2-ti7, lan2 tioh8 khan-kiu3 in.  Sou2-i2 ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 chhut-lat8 hian3-kim ti7 Soan-to7-hoe7, thang choe3 chit nng7 ui7 e5 thoan5-kau3 e5 hui3-iong7 .(Au7-ho7 beh koh sia2 A-mi chok8 e5 cheng5-heng5).
