TiuN Hok8-chu5 Hoe7-tiuN2 
         TOJ si7 Tai5-oan5-ji7, chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7, ma7 ho7-cho3 Peh8-oe7-ji7; cha2-cheng5 long2 kio3-cho3 Kau3-hoe7 Lo5-ma2-ji7. In-ui7 goan5-te2 si7 Kau3-hoe7 chah--lai5..e5. TOJ chit-e5 su5 si7 Ko-hiong5 Tai5-gi2 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Gian2-sip8-hoe7 e5 a-che2 a-hiaN tau3-tin7 siuN7--chhut--lai5..e5. Iong7 chit-e5 su5 lai5 cho3 lan2 chap8-chi3 e5 mia5 kam2-kak chin su3-phoe3, chin tau3-tah. Chit8 hong-bin7 hian2-beng5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 pun2-chiaN5 chiu7 u7 ka-ki7 e5 bun5-ji7  kap tu3-chok e5 lek8-su2 su7-sit8, ma7 siang7-si5 tut8-hian2 lan2 tui3 Han3-ji7 e5 hoan2-kam2! Tai5-oan5-lang5 pun2-te2 tioh8 m7-si7 Han3-jin5, bo5 sim2-mih8 to7-li2 teng7-kui tioh8 ai3 iong7 Han3-ji7!  Che m7-si7 lan2 ti7 chia khang-chhui3 pou7-chih8 ou-peh8 loan7-su2 kong2..e5. Cheng5 kui2 jit8 Ma2-kai PeN7-iN7 Lim5 Ma2-li7 i-su hoat-piau2 koan-he7 HLA e5 lun7-bun5 chiu7 chin chheng-chho2 iong7 i-hak8 kho-hak8 e5 hong-hoat tit8-chiap cheng3-beng5 lan2 chin cha2 tioh8 chai-iaN2, khiok chhoe7 bo5 i-hak8 cheng3-kiN3 e5 su7-sit8. Lau2-sit8 kong2, chiaN3-kang2 e5 Han3-jin5, Tiong-hoa5 Jin5-bin5 Kiong7-ho5-kok Sia7-hoe7 Kho-hak8-iN7 Gi2-gian5 Gian2-kiu3-sou2 e5 sou2-tiuN2 Li7 Siok-siong, ma7 chu2-tiuN su2-iong7 Lo5-ma2-ji7!
	Lan2 chhut-pan2 TOJ, chiu7-si7 beh ka7 se3-kai3 soan-po3: Tai5-oan5-lang5 bo5 phah-sng3 beh hong3-khi3 in e5 bo2-gi2 kap bun5-ji7! Bi2-kok ti7 1996 u7 seng5-lip8 chit8-e5 chou-chit ho7-cho3 Terralingua, ti7 hia chin beng5-pek8 u7 kong2-khi2: gi2-gian5 to-iuN7-seng3 ju2 toa7 e5 te7-hng, seng-but8 e5 to-iuN7-seng3 ma7 ju2 beng5-hian2. Tai5-oan5 chiu7-si7 ki5-tiong e5 chit8 sou2-chai7. Seng-but8 to-iuN7-seng3 chiu7-si7 “chi-in”(Eng-gi2 si7 gene), e5 to-iuN7-seng3. M7-chiah kong2 po2-liu5 gi2-gian5 chiu7-si7 po2-liu5 seng-but8 e5 chi-in kap but8-chiong2! An2-ne lan2 e5 kiaN2-sun chiah e7-tit-thang eng2-oan2 hiang2-siu7 te7-kiu5 e5 chu-goan5. OaN7-oe7 lai5 kong2, chiu7-si7 linguistic diversity/ biodiversity/ cultural diversity 3 hang7 hou7-siong u7 chin chhim e5 ian-goan5. 

TOJ chhut-khan 1 ni5	
TaN, lan2 TOJ chhut-khan boan2 1 ni5, lan2 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Gian2-sip8-hoe7 ma7 “seng-li2” ju2 cho3 ju2 toa7! Lan2 sui-bong2 bo5 sim2-mih8 chiN5, ma7 khiam3 kha-chhiu2, m7-ku2 lan2 ma7 sio-kang7 e7 ke3-siok8 koh khok--loh8--khi3. M7-na7 an2-ne, lan2 i2-keng ka7 Loe7-cheng3-pou7 sin-chheng2 ho2-se3, beh chou TLH(Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Hiap8-hoe7, kan2-chheng Tai5-lo5-hoe7, TLH). Lan2 an3-sng3 beh ti 8 goeh8 19 cheng3-sek seng5-lip8. Lan2 beh iong7 un7-tong7, kau3-hak8/gian2-kiu3  kap sia2-chok/chhut-pan2 3 e5 hong-hoat lai5 lo5-lek8. Koh khah ng3-bang7 lan2 Gian2-sip8-hoe7 e5 che2-hiaN cho3-hoe2 lai5 sia2. Sia2-chhut li2 e5 hoaN-hi2 kap pi-siong; ma7 sia2-chhut li2 e5 kia3-bang7 kap ki5-thai7. Ka7 lan2 chit-te7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 e5 iaN2-jiah lau5-loh8-khi3 hou7 lan2 e5 kiaN2-sun thang chai-bat. Ma7 the3 chit-liap8 te7-kiu5 lau5-loh8 po2-kui3 e5 gi2-gian5 kap but8-chiong2!
