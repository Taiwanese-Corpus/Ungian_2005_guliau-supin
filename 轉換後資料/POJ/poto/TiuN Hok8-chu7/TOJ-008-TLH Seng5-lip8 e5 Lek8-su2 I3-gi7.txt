TiuN Hok8-chu5 Hoe7-tiuN2          TLH(Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Hiap8-hoe7 ) koat-teng7 beh ti7 2001 ni5 8 goeh8 19 seng5-lip8. Lan2 beh iau3-kiu5 cheng3-hu2 3 toa7 su7-hang7:1.	Tui-jin7 i2-keng u7 180 ni5 ku2 lek8-su2 e5 Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7, chiu7-si7 Kau3-hoe7 Lo5-         ma2-ji7 e5 bun5-ji7 te7-ui7.Chhiok-seng5 Tai5-oan5 oe7(Goan5-chu7-bin5 oe7-gi2, Kheh-ka-oe7 kap Ho7-lo2-oe7) cho3         Hoat-teng7-gi2(Official Languages).3.	    Tok-chhiok cheng3-hu2 sit8-si kong-peng5 cheng3-gi7 e5 to-gi2-bun5 cheng3-chhek.Chiu7 un7-tong7 e5 pun2-chit lai5 kong2, che ma7 si7 Lo5-ma2-ji7 un7-tong7 e5 teng5-thau5-seN. Lan2long2 chai-iaN2 ti7 1920 ni5-tai7, lan2 e5 sian5-pai2 Chhoa3 Poe5-hoe2 sian-seN in hit-tin7 lang5 chiu7 siuN7 beh thui-sak LMJ. M7-ku2 siu7-tioh8 ke2-pau5 e5 Han3-bin5-chok8 i3-sek e5 ti3-sek hun7-chu2 hoan2-tui3, bo5 seng5-kong. Au7--lai5 koh tu2 tioh8 Thai3-peng5-iuN5 Chian3-cheng, chiu7 an2-ne bo5-hoeh bo5-boe2 si2--khi3. Chin bo5-chhai2. 	Lan2 chit-ma2 e5 LMJ un7-tong7 kap 20 ni5-tai7 hit-e5 u7 sim2-mih8 cheng-chha? Se3-kai3-koan! Cha2-cheng5 e5 un7-tong7 kan-ta han7-tiaN7 ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 pun2-thou2. Chit-ma2 lan2 long2 chai-iaN2, jin5-lui7 e5 gi2-gian5 si7 te7-kiu5 e5 chu-goan5. Gi2-gian5 m7-na7 si7 ki-pun2 jin5-koan5, koh si7 e7 eng2-hiong2 te7-kiu5 ian2-hoa3(evolution) e5 tiong7-iau3 in-sou3 e5 chit8-hang7! Lan2 u7 an3-sng3 koh lai5 beh kap se3-kai3 tak8 sou2-chai7 e5 gi2-gian5, bun5-hoa3 kap but8-chiong2 po2-hou7 thoan5-the2 lai5 lian5-lok8, saN hiap8-chou7, hi-bang7 the3 Tai5-oan5 po2-sioh gi2-gian5, ma7 the3 te7-kiu5 po2-poe3 but8-chiong2. Gan2-si5-cheng5 lan2 i2-keng kap Ho5-lan5 e5 Leiden Tai7-hak8 u7 tit8-chiap e5 chiap-chhiok8. Henning Kloeter phok-su7 kap Lloyd Haft phok-su7 long2 i2-keng lai5-koe3 lan2 gian2-sip8-hoe7 hong2-bun7 kap khai-kang2, ma7 tui3 lan2 e5 sou2-cho3 u7 chin chhim e5 li2-kai2 kap jin7-tong5.
