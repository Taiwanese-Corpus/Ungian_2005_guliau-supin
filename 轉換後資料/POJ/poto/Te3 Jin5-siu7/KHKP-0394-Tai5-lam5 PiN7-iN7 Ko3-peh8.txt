                     
Tai5-lam5 PiN7-iN7 Ko3-peh8
Te3 Jin5-siu7 
Cheng5 goeh8 jit8 u7 kong2 lan2 Tai5-lam5 i7-koan2 e5 hak8-seng soah kang m7 koh cho3.  Goan2 beh than3 chit e5 ki-hoe7 siat sin e5 hoat-tou7. I-seng-koan2 sng3 m7 si7 I- hak8-hau7. Lang5 na7 beh cho3 i-seng tioh8 keng-koe3 Cheng3-hu2 e5 I-hak8-hau7. Chong2- si7 lan2 u7 ho2 ki-hoe7 thang ka3 khan3-hou7- hu7. Ia7 chit ho7 e5 kang khah hap8 ti7 hu7-jin5 lang5, in e5 khoan2-sit si7 khah chu5- sim, khah chim-chiok khan3-hou7 piN7- lang5. Au7-lai5 goan2 beh chiong Tai5-lam5 khan3-hou7-hu7, cho3 khan3-hou7 hoan7-chia2, lam5 lu2 long2 u7, chhin-chhiuN7 Jit8-pun2 a2-si7 Eng-kok sio-siang7 e5 hoat-tou7. N`g-bang7 chou2-ke e5 Soan-kau3-su e7 phai3 chit8 nng7 e5 khan3-hou7-hu7 lai5 tau3 siat chit8 keng khan3-hou7 hak8-hau7.   
Hit-tiap8 beh seng ka3 khan3-hou7-hak8, chhin-chhiuN7 ki3 sou2 pi7-pan7 hit pun2 chheh “Lai7 goa7 kho khan3-hou7-hak8”. Chit pun2 si7 eng7 peh8-oe7-ji7 lai5 in3; lai7-bin7 m7-na7 kong2- khi2 khan3-hou7-hak8, kai2-pho3-hak8, seng-li2- hak8, phou2-thong e5 i-hak8 ia7 u7 ki3-teh.  Thak8 chha-put-to saN ni5 e7 chut-giap8. Au7-lai5 ke chit8 ni5 beh soa3 ka3 chou7-san2-hak8.  Phi2- jin5 teh pi7-pan7 chit8 pun2 chheh chou7-san2- hak8. Beh in3 peh8-oe7-ji7. Ia7 beh eng7 chin che7 tou5 piau2-beng5 sou2 beh kong2 e5 su7. Ng3-bang7 au7-lai5 lan2 e5 chi2-moe7 e7 tit-tioh8  chin che7 li7-ek; m7-tan7-na7 li7-ek ti7 ka-ti7, ia7 li7-ek ti7 chhu3-piN, li7-ek ti7 kau3-hoe7.  
Goan2 chit-tiap8 ai3 liam5-piN koh chiap- lap8 lak8 e5 lang5 cho3 khan3-hou7-hu7. Ai3 sim2-mih8 teng2-ho7 e5 lang5 ? Tioh8 u7 hak8-bun7. Na7 keng-koe3 kong hak8-hau7, lu2 hak8-hau7 khah ho2; peh8-oe7-ji7 tioh8 e7 hiau2 thak8, koh e7 hiau2 sia2. Tioh8 i2-keng jip8 kau3-hoe7, a2-si7 put-chi2 ti3-i3 ai3 thiaN to7-li2. Tioh8 un-jiu5 bo5 pin5-toaN7, gau5 thiaN3-thang3 phoa3-piN7 lang5. Sin-the2 tioh8 iong2-kiaN7. Ni5-hoe3 tui3 18 chi3 25 hoe3.  
 
Na7 cho3 khan3-hou7-hu7 tioh8 oh8 saN ni5, thak8 chheh, kho2 chheh, na7 kho2 tioh8  beh chiong chut-giap8 cheng3-su hou7 in.  
Cho3 khan3-hou7-hu7 m7 thang siuN7 ai3 beh than3 chiN5,chong2-si7 beh ti7 chia kong2 ti7 i-koan2 lai7 e5 si5 in e5 sin-kim si7 chaiN2-iuN7. TaN-chiah jip8 e5 si5 thau5 chit8 ni5 chit8 goeh8 jit8 nng7 khou-poaN3, chiah8 i-koan2; chit e5 chiN5 beh he7 in e5 chhiu2, cho3 chap8-su7 e5 lou7-eng7.  Te7 ji7 ni5 chit8 goeh8 jit8 saN khou, te7 saN ni5 si3 khou, te7 si3 ni5 gou7 khou. Khan3- hou7-hu7 na7 tiam3 i-koan2 khah ku2, i e5 sin-kim hit si5-chun7 beh koh phah-sng3. Teh jip8 e5 si5 iu5-goan5 beh hou7 in saN su i-koan2 lai7 sou2 beh chheng7 e5 saN-khou3, koh gou7 lak8 nia2 kun5-a2. Na7 u7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 bo5 beng5-peh8 chhiaN2 tai7-ke lai5 chhoe7 I-seng-niu5, a2-si7 I-seng, in hoaN-hi2 kong2- beng5.  
Chit-tiap8 khan3-hou7-hu7 iau2-boe7 kau3- giah8 thang khan3-hou7 ta-pou lang5 keng, chong2-si7 au7-lai5 na7 siat ho2-se3 hiah e5 keng iu5-goan5 beh koh khui. U7 chit8 khoan2 lam5 hoan7-chia2 e5 piN7 hou7 khan3-hou7-hu7 lai5 the3 in siat-hoat khah m7 ho2-se3; beh chhiaN2 2 e5 khah tiong-lian5 e5 ta-pou lang5 lai5 cho3 chit ho7 kang khah hap8-gi5. Chit-tiap8 teh chiap-lap8 hu7-jin5 lang5 kap gin2-a2, ta-pou gin2-a2 chun2 tui3 chap8 hoe3 i2-ha7 chiah  thang jip8 lai5 i-ti7.  
Khan3-hou7-hu7 sou2 beh oh8 e5 su7 : si7 phou2- thong khan3-hou7-hak8, che3 ioh8, thiah ioh8, liau7-li2 chhiu2-sut8-sek, siau-tok8-hoat, tam7-poh8 se3-khun2-hak8, chou7-san2-hak8, chiah e5 su7.  
Au7-goeh8 jit8 e5 Po3 beh kong2-khi2 khan3-hou7 e5 chit si7 chaiN2-iuN7, u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek ti7- teh, m7 si7 ha7-chian7 e5 kang, si7 chun-kui3 e5 kang, put-chi2 u7 lou7-eng7, thang po3-iong5 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ki-tok e5 to7-li2.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-ohe7-po3 te7 394 koan3, 1918 ni5 1 geh8)
