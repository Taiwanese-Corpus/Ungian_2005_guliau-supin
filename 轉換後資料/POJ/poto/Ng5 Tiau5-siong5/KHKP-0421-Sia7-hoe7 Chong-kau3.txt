
Sia7-hoe7 : Chong-kau3
 Ng5 Tiau5-siong5 ki3  
Chit e5 te5-bak8 phah-sng3 u7 che7-che7 ui7 e5 bok8-su kap sian-siN bat kong2-khi2, liat8-ui7 tai7-khai3 ia7 long2 beng5-pek8.  Goa2 siuN7 ai3 koh chit8 pai2 lai5 tam5-lun7, ng3-bang7 u7 sio2-khoa2 li7-ek, kiam2-chhai2 e7 cho3 tiam3 chit e5 phu5-phio3 e5 se3-kan e5 li7-ek.  
Hian7-si5 sia7-hoe7 tiong sou2 cho3 e5 su7-giap8, lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 chit-giap8, kok teng2 e5 kho-hak8: Chu7-jian5 Kho-hak8 (Natural Science) Bun5-beng5 Kho-hak8 (Cultural Science) Li2-siong2 Kho-hak8, (Ideal Science).  Chiau3 khoaN3 kap chong-kau3 long2 bo5 koan-he7.  Chong-kau3 leng7-goa7 chit8 hang7 mih8, long2 si7 lun7 sin5-leng5-chiuN7 e5 tai7-chi3.  Kho-hak8, su7-giap8, sou2 teh cho3 e5 bok8-tek bo5 saN-tang5.   
Koh u7 lang5 siuN7 tui3 kou2-cha2 chong-kau3 chiu7-si7 chit8 kho, ki5-u5 e5 kho-hak8 ia7 si7 ka-ti7 chit8 kho.  Khiok chiau3 hian7-si5 khoaN3 si7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni; m7-ku2 na7 ka7 i khe-kho2 hit e5 khi2-thau5,chiu7 e7 chai che7-che7 kho-hak8 si7 tui3 chong-kau3 pun chhut-lai5-e5.  Lai5 ki2-khi2 kui2-na7 hang7.  
Hoat-hak8, Cheng3-ti7-hak8, ti7 Ku7-iok Siong7-te3 beng7-leng7 Mou5-se ti7 Se-nai2-soaN cho3 Chap8-tiau5 kai3.  Che chiu7-si7 hoat-lut8 e5 khai-chou2, kin-a2-jit8 iau2 u7 chun5 ti7 hoat-lut8 e5 lai7-te2.  Tiat-hak8, Lun5-li2, sim-li2-hak8 u7 be7 li7-khui-tit e5 koan-he7 ti7-teh. Im-gak8, put-lun7 ti7 Tang-iuN5 a2-si7 Se-iuN5, goan5-pun2 long2 si7 ti7 hian3-che3 keng3 Sin5 e5 si5 teh eng7-e5.  Kau3-iok8 kap i-hak8, kou2-cha2 long2 si7 kau3-hoe7, si7-bio7, bok8-su, hoe5-siuN7 sou2 cho3 e5 kang.  TaN hian7-si5 chiah e5 che7-che7 long2 ka-ti7 tok8-lip8 lah. An2-ni thang kong2 kho-hak8 si7 tui3 chong-kau3 pun chhut-lai5-e5.  M7-na7 an2-ni, sia7-hoe7 sou2 teh cho3 e5 su7-giap8 ia7 che7-che7 si7 tui3 chong-kau3 chhut.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chu5-hui7-iN7, kou-ji5-iN7, kiu3-che3-sou2, chhiah sip8-ji7-sia7 long2 si7 lan2 Ki-tok-kau3 chit e5 thiaN3 lang5 e5 “thiaN3”--ji7 siN-chhut. Na7-si7 chit si5 bo5 chong-kau3 e5 lang5 ia7 teh cho3, soa3 piN3-cho3 chhou-siok8.  
Lun7 chong-kau3 thang kong2 si7 cheng3 hang7 e5 pun2-goan5.  Phi3-ju7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 chang5 ko-le5-chhai3, lai7-bin7 u7 chit8-hah8 chit8-hah8.  Cheng3 hang7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hiah e5 hah8.  Na7-si7 ko-le5-chhai3 chit8-hah8 chit8-hah8 peh liau2 e7 bo5--khi3, m7-ku2 chong-kau3 si7 kau3 chit liap8 te7-kiu5 long2 leng2--khi3, long2 bo5 poaN3 lang5 ti7 se3-kan, i ia7 si7 ti7-teh, chiu7-si7 bo5 khi2-thau5 bo5 soah-boe2.  TaN beh an3-chaiN2-iuN7 siuN7 chit e5 chong-kau3 ?  Thang kong2 si7 chit8 liap8 cheng2-chi2, Ia5-sou bat siat phi3-ju7 kong2 si7 koa3-chhai3-chi2 (Ma2-kho2 4 : 31). Che chin-chiaN3 u7 i3-su3 ah ! Cheng2-chi2 si7 u7 oah8-mia7 u7 lat8.  I na7 ia7 loh8 te7, e7 hoat ge5, e7 teng3 kin.  Kho-hak8 na7 u7 Chong-kau3 chiu7 u7 oah8-mia7, u7-lat8.  Lat8 tui3 to2-ui7 lai5 ? Tui3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5.  Ia5-sou ti7 se3-kan siong5-siong5 tui3 lang5 kong2, “An-sim.”  “Tioh8 an-sim.”  Chit ku3 koh u7 chit8 e5 i3-su3, si7 kong2, “Tioh8 goan5-khi3.” Goan5-khi3 chiu7-si7 siN-khi2 khui3-lat8 e5 i3-su3. Pian3-sui7-e5 tioh8 siN goan5-khi3 chiu7 e7 kiaN5. Ia5-sou si2 liau2 hak8-seng chhin-chhiuN7 iuN5 bo5 bok8-chia2, sit-bang7 lek-taN2.  Ia5-sou chhut-hian7 an-ui7 in, in chiu7 ho2-taN2 e7 thoan5 to7-li2.  Chit ku3 “An-sim” si7 lan2 chong-kau3 e5 toa7 khui3-lat8.  Ti7 chit se3-kan si5-si5 e5 an-ui3.   
Chit se3-kan u7 chin che7 thang hou7 lang5 kiaN, ti3-kau3 lan2 e5 cheng-sin5 beh soe-jiok8 nng2-chiaN2 chhin-chhiuN7 pian3-sui7-e5.  U7-si5 jiok8-the2 phoa3-piN7 chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 hiat-lau7 12 ni5 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5; u7-si5 tu2-tioh8 gek8-keng2 chhin-chhiuN7 Ia5-sou e5 hak8-seng tu2-tioh8 toa7 hong-eng2.  Peng5-iu2 bo5 kau3-giah8 e5 an-ui3, si2-sit khun2-tiok8, hian7-chai7 se3-kan bo5 kau3-giah8 e5 tong5-cheng5. Na7-si7 lan2 tui3 Ia5-sou e5 oe7 ti7 lan2 e5 hi7-khang piN teh kong2, “Tioh8 an-sim, goa2 kap li2 ti7-teh.”  Hit si5 lan2 beh koh siN-khi2 toa7 lat8.  Sit-pai7 beh koh tit-tioh8 seng5-kong, gek8-keng2 chhi-chham2 beh tit-tioh8 ng3-bang7.  Beh tit-tioh8 se3-kan lang5 cho3 be7 kau3-e5, chiu7-si7 Sin5 e5 lat8 kap an-ui3.   
Lan2 na7 tak8 jit8 ui7-tioh8 jiok8-the2 lai5 tioh8-boa5, ke3-kau3 e7 pai7-hoai7 e5 bi2-niu5, na7-si7 cheng-sin5-chiuN7 bo5 thang siu7 an-ui3, chiu7 chit se3-kan u7 sim2-mih8 hok-khi3 mah ? Na7 bo5 tit-tioh8 chong-kau3 e5 an-ui3 hou7 lan2 e5 cheng-sin5 boan2-chiok, toa3 se3-kan chhin-chhiuN7 te7-gek8.  Sou2 cho3 m7 si7 chiaN3 bok8-tek, jiok8-the2 si7 cho3 ki-khi3 teh eng7 na7-tiaN7, si7 bong5 bok8-tek e5 seng-oah8.  
Se3-kan e5 chiN5-chai5, mia5-siaN si7 sim2-mih8 ? Bo5 khah-iaN5 chhut Ai-kip8 teh pai3 kim gu5-a2 siong7, te7 18 se3-ki2 Se-Au tai7-liok8 e5 khang hun-chiong na7-tiaN7.  
TaN kai-chai3 u7 lan2 e5 Chong-kau3 hou7 lan2 oah8-mia7, khui3-lat8 kap an-ui3.  Put-lun7 cho3 sim2-mih8 chit-giap8 thang tit-tioh8 an-sim lip8-beng7.  Che chiu7-si7 lan2, m7-na7 si7 lan2, chiu7-si7 sia7-hoe7 cheng3 lang5 kap chong-kau3 be7 e7 li7-tit e5 koan-he7 tiam2. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 421 koan3,1920 ni5 4 geh8)
