
Lun7 Lu2-kang
Koeh Tiau2-seng5
Kou2-cha2 Siong7-te3 chhong3-cho7 A-tong lai5 koan2-li2 se3-kai3.  Au7-lai5 chiu7 koh chhong3-cho7 Ha7-oa lai5 pang-chan7 A- tong. Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 chiaN5 cho3 oan5-choan5 e5 ke. A-tong chin7 i e5 chit.  Ha7-oa chin7 i e5 kang, lai5 liau7-li2 Siong7-te3 sou2 chou7 sui2-sui2 e5 se3-kai3.  Kho2-sioh hian7-kim che7-che7 hu7-jin5 lang5, pang3-sak u7 lou7-eng7 e5 kang, lai5 cho3 u7 hai7 ti7 sia7-hoe7 e5 hong- siok8 e5 tai7-chi3.  U7-e5 ke-lai7 m7 ti7-li2, kiaN2-ji5 bo5 chin7 pun2-hun7 ka3-si7; put-ko3 chiah8- pa2 si3-ke3 chau2, lai5 oh8 phaiN2 hong-siok8, siu-cheng2 pun2-sin, tai7-ke saN tau3-sui2. Che m7-na7 u7 gai7-tioh8 hong-siok8, sit8-chai7 u7 gai7-tioh8 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 thian-seng3.  TaN lau5 Ki-tok-kau3 choan-choan si7 ai3 kai2-liong5 sia7-hoe7 hong-siok8, chhoa7 lang5 khi3- sia5 kui-cheng3.  Sou2-i2 ti7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 lu2-kang m7-thang m7 gian2-kiu3.  
I.Ke-lai7 e5 lu2-kang. 
(1)Tioh8 eng7 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7 kam2-hoa3 kia2-ji5.  Eng7 ho2 tek-heng7 ho2 bou5-iuN7 lai5 kam2-hoa3 lang5, chit ho7 lat8 toa7 koh si7 ku2-tng5.  Kou2-cha2 Bun5-ong5 si7 eng7 i e5 un5-jiu5 ho2 tek-heng7; sou2-i2 thiaN-tioh8 i e5 mia5-e5, tai7-ke hoaN-hi2 sun7-hok8. Na7-si7 eng7 peng-to bu2-lat8 si7 chio2, koh si7 chiam7- si3 sun7-hok8 na7-tiaN7.  Tiu7-ong5 kap Hang7- u2, i si7 eng7 i e5 kiong5-beng2, ti3-kau3 bo5 ku2 kok chiu7 biat8-bo5.  
TaN hu7-jin5 lang5 ai3 hou7 i e5 kiaN2 chiong-lai5 cho3 ho2 lang5 , pun2-siu tioh8 u7 ho2 tek- heng7 thang hou7 kiaN2 oh8; in-ui7 se3-kiaN2 be7 hiau2 hun-piat ho2 bai2, put-ko3 chiong sou2 khoaN3-e5 lai5 oh8.  Chhi3 khoaN3 koe2-in3 na7-si7 khek ku, sou2 in3 e5 koe2 ia7 si7 ku; mou5-he7 bo2 kiaN5 than2-hoaiN5, i e5 kiaN2 iah8 kiaN5 than2- hoaiN5. Ia5-sian2-piat ai3 pai3 put8, i e5 cha-bou2- kiaN2 ia7 oh8 i.  Tui3 chia thang chai cho3 lau7-bo2 pun2-sin e5 seng3-keh, kap kiaN2-ji5 si7 u7 toa7 koan-he7. Bat khoaN3-kiN3 hu7-jin5 lang5 teh phah-kiaN2, lang5 mng7 i phah siaN2-su7 ? I chiu7 kong2 chit e7 gin2-na2 gau5 peh8-chhat8, gau5 phaiN2-chhui3. Ki5-sit8 pun2-sin kai3 gau5 peh8-chhat8, ai3 kong2 phaiN2- chhui3; i eng7 chit ho7 kau3-hoat bo5 koh iuN7 mou5-he7 bo2 teh  
ka3 kiaN2, kiaN2 than2-tit8. TaN cho3 lang5 e5 lau7-bo2 pun2-sin tioh8 toan- chiaN3, cho3 kiaN2-ji5 e5 bou5-iuN7, pun2-sin ia7 tioh8 tui3-tiong7 ki5-to2 ai3 thak8 Seng3-keng, ai3 siu2 Chu2-jit8, heng5-tong7 ku2-chi2 tak8 hang7 chiau3 to7-li2, chiu7 bian2 loa7 giam5 i e5 kiaN2 ia7 e7 oh8 i e5 ho2, chiong-lai5 cho3 ho2 lang5 .  The5-mou5-thai3 e5 lau7-bu2 kap goa7-ma2 si7 jiat8-sim e5 lang5, The5-mou5-thai3 ia7 oh8 i. Beng5-tiau5 thau5 chit8 e5 Hong5-te3 i e5 Hong5-hou7 pun2-sin ho2 tek-heng7 khiam7- seng2, bo5 loa7-ku2 kok-lai7 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 che7-che7 oh8 i.  Kiu3-chu2 kong2, "Ho2 chhiu7 kiat ho2 koe2-chi2, phaiN3 chhiu7 kiat phaiN2 koe2-chi2".  Sou2-i2 hu7-jin5 lang5 pun2-sin tioh8 u7 ho2 bou5-iuN7 thang hou7 kiaN2 oh8.  
(2)Tioh8 eng7 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 ho2 hoat- tou7 ka3 kiaN2-ji5.  Eng7 ho2 tek-heng7 lai5 kam2- hoa3 lang5, chit ho7 si7 ti7 sim-lai7 the2-heng5 lai7 e5 tek-iok8; eng7 ho2 hoat-tou7 lai5 ka3 lang5, che si7 the2-heng5 goa7 e5 kau3-iok8 nng7 hang7 m7-thang khiam3-khoeh. Gin2-na2 e5 seng3 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chui2, cho3 lau7- bo2--e5, ti7 chit si5 na7 bo5 chim-chiok, kiaN- liau2 i e5 kiaN2 be7 e5 cho3 ho2 lang5. Chhi3-siuN7 Beng7-chu2 e5 lau7-bo2 u7 saN pai2 soa2 chhu3; chit8 pai2 kin7 ti bong7-pou, Beng7-chu2 chiu7 ai3 oh8 tai5 si2 lang5, koh chit8 pai2 kin7 ti7 ke- chhi7, Beng7-chu2 ai3 oh8 cho3 seng-li2, koh chit8 pai2 kin7 ti7 oh8-tng5, Beng7-chu2 chiu7 ai3 thak8-chheh.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, Kiu7 ang5 chiu7 piN3 cho3 ang5, kin7 ou chiu7 piN3 cho3 ou. Koh kong2, Kap ho2 lang5 kiaN5 u7 pou3 thang kiN, kap phaiN2 lang5 kiaN5 u7 kiaN thang siN. Lan2 m7 thang kan-ta ai3 hou7 kiaN2-ji5 tit-tioh8 hak8-bun7, hou7 i khi3 thak8 ti7 bo5 chong-kau3-e5 oh8-tng5, chiong5-lai5 sin3 bo5 sin5, kap Ki-tok cho3 tui3-tek8. A2-si7 ui7-tioh8 chai5-li7 hou7 i kap se3-siok8 lang5 toa3, ti3-kau3 bo5 siu2 le2-pai3, bo5 ku2 chiu7 poe7 kau3. A2-si7 hou7 i kap se3-siok8 gin2-a2 cho3 hoe2, oh8 che7-che7 phaiN2 oe7. Ti7 chit si5 cho3 lau7-bo2--e5 tioh8 eng7 ho2 hong-hoat lai5 chhu3-ti7 i e5 kiaN2, oh8 Beng7-bo2 e5 ho2 hong-hoat.
Se3-kai3 e5 keng chheh kai3-che7.  Chong2-si7 choe3 te7-it e7 poe5-iuN2 lang5 e5 seng3-tek, e7 siu-iong2 lang5 e5 sin5-leng5, tu5-khi3 Seng3- keng i2-goa7 bo5 pat8 mih8 chheh.  Sou2-i2 hu7- jin5-lang5 eng-kai tioh8 chin7 sim siat-hoat ka3 i e5 kiaN2 bat Seng3-keng.  Chhi3 siuN7 The5-mou5-thai3 i tui3 se3-han3 chiu7 bat Seng3- keng, i e5 tek-heng7 hiah toan-chong, bo5 m7 si7 tui3 lau7-bu2 e5 gau5 ka3-si7. Beng7-chu2 chit8 pai2 pin5-toaN7 thak8 chheh, i e5 lau7-bu2  
khoaN3 liau2 sim iu-bun7, siuN7 khoaN3 eng7 sim2- mih8 hoat-tou7 thang kio3-chheN2 kiaN2 e5 sim, au7-lai5 chiu7 koah tng7 pou3-kui lai5 keng2- kai3 i e5 kiaN2, Beng7-chu2 ko2-jian5 toa7 hoe2- gou7, chiong-lai5 cho3 toa7 mia5-siaN e5 lang5, che2 sit8-chai7 si7 tui3 i e5 lau7-bu2 e5 ho2 hoat- tou7 ka3 i.  
U7-si5 bat khoaN3-kiN3 gin2-a2 toa7-toa7 han3 iau2 be7-hiau2 thak8 b, ch, chh, chiah8 pa2 si3- ke3 thit-tho5 oh8 phaiN2 chhui3 kap lang5 oan-ke, jip8 le2-pai3-tng5 kiauh8-kiauh8 jiang2 bo5 sok-cheng7, ki5-to2 e5 si5 gau5 chhong3-ti7 lang5, m7 na7 hoe7-iu2 e5 kiaN2 u7 chit ho7 khoan2, thiaN kong2 bou2 thoan5-to7 e5 kiaN2 koh khah gau5 kiau2-jiau2.  Che m7 na7 be7 thang e5 gin2- a2 cho3 phaiN2, ka-ti7 e5 kiaN2 to an2-ni beh- thai2 kaN2 iok-sok pat8 lang5 e5 gin2-a2 tioh8 sok-cheng7.  Sou2-i2 kau3-hoe7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 m7 thang hiam5 gin2-a2 phaiN2 ka3-si7 bo5 hoat- tit, tioh8 siuN7 sai kap hou2 si7 phaiN2 hiong5-ok e5 siu3, iau2-ku2 hou7 lang5 ka3 kau3 m7 kaN2 ka7 lang5, him ka3 kau3 e7 cho3 hi3.  Ia7 m7 thang kong2 bo5 eng5 thang ka3-si7 i, tioh8 siuN7 Beng7-chu2 e5 lau7-bu2 kai3 san3 tioh8 keN pou3 than3 chiN5 hou7 kiaN2 thak8-chheh, iau2- ku2 ka tng7 pou3, liau2 che7-che7 chiN5 lai5 ka3 kiaN2; taN lan2 tioh8 oh8 i chiaN3 u7 hap8 ti7 Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 kiaN2-ji5 thok-tiong7 lan2 ka3-si7 e5 chek-seng5.  
II.Kau3-hoe7 e5 lu2-kang 
(1)Tioh8 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5 ka3 Chu2- jit8-oh8. Gin2-a2 ti7 chap8 hoe3 i2-ha7 khah u7 siu7 tioh8 ka-teng5 e5 kau3-iok8, na7 chap8 hoe3 i2-siong7, u7-e5 tioh8 cho3 kang, u7-e5 tioh8 jip8 oh8 thak8-chheh, koh u7-e5 si7-toa7 lang5 bo5 tu2-ho2 e5 hoat-tou7 thang ka3 i. Tui3 an2-ni sin3 To7 e5 sim chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 pang3-sang.  Sou2-i2 siat Chu2-jit8-oh8 chiu7-si7 ai3 pou2 in ti7 lak8 jit8 lai7 sou2 sit-loh8--e5.  
Lun7 kau3 e7 chai gin2-a2 seng3-cheng5, u7 chu5-ai3 un-jiu5 e5 sim, chiu7-si7 hu7-jin5- lang5. Sou2-i2 Se-kok Tiong-hak8 i2-ha7 e5 oh8- tng5 khah che7 si7 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5 cho3 kau3-oan5. Koh pun2-kok Iu3-ti7 oan5 (chiu7-si7 ka3 gin2-a2 oh8-tng5) e5 sian-seN ia7- si7 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5. Tui3 an2-ni thang chai hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 ai3-cheng5 kap gin2-a2 si7 u7 tit8-chiap e5  
thian-seng3. Lan2 e5 Chu2-jit8-oh8 sui-jian5 bo5 hun-piat toa7-lang5 a2-si7 gin2-a2, m7 ku2 toa7 poaN3 lai5 kong2 i, si7 ai3 li7-ek gin2-a2. Sou2-i2 lan2 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5 cho3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 sian-seN si7 chin hap8.  
Koh e7 hou7 hu7-jin5-lang5 chai kau3-hoe7 heng-soe chhoa7 lang5 jip8 Seng3-kok in ia7 u7 chek-seng5.  
(2)Tioh8 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5 cho3 pou3-to7 su. Hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 kang si7 chan7 cha-pou lang5 sou2 cho3 be7 tit kau3-e5. Hian7-si5 hong- khi3 boe7 khui, hu7-jin5-lang5 iau2 kou3-siu2 ku7- koan3, na7 toa3 ti7 pang5-lai7 m7 bat kiN3-tioh8 cha-pou lang5, che chiu7 chheng cho3 si7 chhim-ke lu2, u7 ho2 mia5-siaN, na7 tah8 chhut hou7-teng7 goa7 thiaN-tioh8 cha-pou lang5 kong2 sim2-mih8, chiu7 liah8-cho3 si7 kian3-siau3. Chit ho7 lang5 beh hou7 i thiaN-tioh8 Hok-im, sin3-than3 to7-li2 sit8-chai7 si7 oh-tit. In-ui7 cha-pou lang5 pou3-to7 long2 si7 ti7 lau7-jiat8 e5 sou2-chai7.  
Lan2 na7 eng7 hu7-jin5-lang5 pou3-kau3 ti7 chit ho7 ke si7 chin hap8, in-ui7 hu7-jin5-lang5 tui3 hu7-jin5-lang5 khah ho2 chhin-kin7. Koh chhut-jip8 ti7 chit ho7 sou2-chai7 bo5 kim3-khi7. Koh u7-e5 hou7 lang5 chhoa7 khi3 cho3 se3-i5 au7 lai7 bo5 tek-thiong2, siu7-tioh8 che7-che7 kan-khou2 bo5 tang3 kong2 siN-cheng5 khoai3-lok8 hok-khi3 i2-keng sit-bang7. Lu2 pou3-to7 su sun7 ki-hoe7 thoan5-kau3 ti7 chit ho7 ke, un2-tang3 tit-tioh8 toa7 hoan-geng5, chhin-chhiuN7 iau tit-tioh8 png7; chhui3-ta tit-tioh8 kam-choaN5, hou7 i thang tit- tioh8 Kiu3-chu2 e5 an-ui3, ng3-bang7 lai5-seng e5 hok-khi3. Hian7-kim hu3-ka e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 che7-che7 u7 chiah8 chhai3, khah che7 si7 chiah8 chhai3 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, sun7 chit e5 ki-hoe7 khi3 in2-iu2 in. TaN lan2 kau3-hoe7 e5 lu2 thoan5-to7 su sou2-i2 m7 thang kiam2-chio2, soa3 khah su chiah8 chhai3 e5 lang5.

(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 346-347 koan3, 1914 ni5 1-2 geh8)
