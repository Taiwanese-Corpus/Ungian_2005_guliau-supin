

Kiat-sit8 Pah-poe7.
U5-seng ki3  
Bi2-kok Ka-ni5-ki ong si7 se3-kai3 chhut-mia5 e5 ho2-giah8 lang5, chu5-sian7-ka, peng5-ho5 lun7-chia2.  I ti7 1838 ni5, 11 goeh8 25 jit8 chhut-si3.  Lau7-pe7 si7 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 san3-hiong lang5.  Se3-han3 e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 lau7-pe7 than3-chiah8 e5 kan-khou2, chiu7 lip8-chi3 kong2, Pat8 jit8 tek-khak tioh8 chiong ke-lai7 e5 san3-hiong-kin khau hiat-kak8. Chiu7 hun3-chi3 thak8-chheh, ia7 tu2-ho2 u7 An-lah8-sun3 Tai7-cho3, chiong i 400 pun2 chheh, teh chioh siau3-lian5 lang5 kap gin2-na2 thak8; i chiu7 mui2 pai3-lak8 khi3 ka7 i chioh.  Au7-lai5 siu7 kam2-kek, chi3-goan7 kong2, Goa2 pat8 jit8 na7 u7 chiN5, tek-khak beh siat Tou5-su-koan2, hou7 lang5 khoaN3 bo5 chiN5.  
Chap8-saN hoe3 e5 si5, beh khi3 Bi2-kok than3-chiah8, kho2-sioh bo5 sou2-hui3, kai-chai3 u7 ho2-sim peng5-iu2 chan3-seng5. Tai7-seng kau3 Bi2-kok, si7 cho3 phang2-chit kang-tiuN5 e5 kang. Chap8-lak8 hoe3 e5 si5, khi3 cho3 tian7-sin3 kiok e5 phoe3-tat8-hu. Au7-lai5 khi3 jip8 hoe7-sia7; hit-tiap8 i2-keng u7 than3 tam7-poh8, chiu7 hun7 chui2-iu5 hoe7-sia7. Tu2-tioh8 Bi2-kok in-ui7 pang3 lou5-chai5 e5 su7, Lam5 Pak khi2 chian3-cheng, i chiu7 the3 kok-ka kam-tok kun-su7 thih-to7. Au7-lai5 Lam5-kun e5 Li2 Chiong-kun toa7 pai7, Pak-kun thai5-iaN5, i chiu7 soah kam-tok e5 kang, khi3 ka-ti7 chhong3-lip8 3 e5 thih-kang-tiuN5, chhun kui2-na7 ek e5 chai5-san2. Tui3 an2-ni piN3-cho3 kng3-thih kai3 e5 ong5. Au7-lai5 toa7 khong3-tiong i e5 su7-giap8, chiong i e5 kng3-thih hoe7-sia7 chou-chit cho3 Lian5-hap8 kng3-thih-hoe7 toa7 Hoe7-sia7, toa7-toa7 oah8-tang7.  
Liau2-au7 chiong chit e5 su7-giap8, long2 kau-tai3 au7-chin3 e5 lang5, ka-ti7 cho3 eng5-sin lang5. Chap8-peh ni5-ku2, choan bu7 cho3 li7-ek se3-kan, te7-it ho2 bou5-iuN7 e5 lang5.  Ti7 1901 ni5 chin bo5 eng5 e5 si5, tui3 sit8-giap8 the3-un2 i2-lai5, soaN3 i sou2 chek-chu7 e5 chai5-san2, lai5 hian3 cho3 sia7-hoe7 jin5-to7 kap chu5-sian7 peng5-ho5 e5 lou7-eng7, sit8-chai7 si7 chin che7. I2-goa7 iau2 chin-che7 ki3 be7 liau2.  
I ti7 chit lou7-boe2 ui7-tioh8 se3-kai3 peng5-ho5 e5 su7, toa7 chin7-lat8 phoe-mia7. Kho2-sioh tui3 se3-kai3 hoat toa7 chian3-cheng i2-lai5, toa7 pi-siong i hiah che7 ni5 sou2 tioh8-boa5 khou2-sim e5 Peng5-ho5 phoa3-lih8. Soa3 ti3-kau3 sin5-keng soe-jiok8, ku2-ku2 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5, kau3 chit pang soa3 koe3-sin. Chhit peh chap8 ni5 ku2 chhut-lat8 ui7-tioh8 ka-ti7 kap se3-kan lai5 tioh8-boa5, sit8-chai7 si7 thang kiong-keng3 siau3-liam7 bo5 soah. 
Ia7 thang hou7 lan2 oh8 kui2-na7 hang7 :- 
1.An-lah8-sun3 Tai7-cho3 e5 chheh e7-hiau2 li7-iong7, bo5 khng3 teh chhi7 chian2-a2. Seng3-chheh kong2, u7 ki-hoe7 thang tui3 cheng3 lang5 kiaN5 ho2, chiu7 tioh8 kiaN5. Bo5 lun7 toa7 sio2, hoaN-hi2 chin7-sim hian3, long2 e7 li7-ek lang5 : chhin-chhiuN7 gin2-na2 e5 5 e5 piaN2, 2 boe2 hi5 ia7 si7 kong-hau7 hui-siong5.  
2. Ka-ong e5 lip8-chi3, chioh chheh  thak8 kau3 seng5-kong. Sui si7 “Khit-chiah8 he7 toa7-goan7”, na7-si7 “ Iu2-chi3 su7 keng3-seng5 ”. Sou2-i2 siau3-lian5 lang5, m7 thang ui7-thioh8 san3-hiong lai5 choat8-bang7 pi-koan, iau3-kin2 tioh8 lip8-chi3 : “ Jin5 iu2 sian7-goan7, Thian pit chiong5 chi.”  
3. Ka-ong gau5 cho3 ho2-su7.  Ka-ong chhut-sin liau2, bo5 poe7-iok. Cha2 siu7-tioh8 lang5 e5 li7-ek, taN chhut-lat8 li7-ek lang5. I2 cheng5 ti7 se3-kai3, si7 se3-kai3 e5 hu3-ong; taN ti7 thiN-teng2 ia7 si7 ho2-giah8, in-ui7 i u7 kia3 be7 sng3-tit e5 gin5 ti7 Thian-kok Gin5-hang5. Koh i e5 khui3-lat8 ia7 si7 eng2-oan2 ti7 chit se3-kan teh oah8-tang7.  
TaN lan2 u7 chiN5, tioh8 oh8 Ka-ong gau5 eng7 i e5 chiN5 lai5 be2 be7-pai7-hoai7 e5 ki-giap8; m7 thang chiu2 kau3 hou7 thang5 chiah8, a2-si7 poe-khi3.  TaN lan2 u7 chai5-tiau5 ia7 tioh8 theh8 chhut-lai5 li7-ek sia7-hoe7, chiah be7 khang-khang khng3 kau3 siN-sian pai7-hoai7 khi3. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 420 koan3,1920 ni5 3 geh8)
