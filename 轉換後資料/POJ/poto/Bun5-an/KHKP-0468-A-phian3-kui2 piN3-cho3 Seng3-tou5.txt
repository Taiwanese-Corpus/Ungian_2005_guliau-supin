A-phian3-kui2 piN3-cho3 Seng3-tou5.
Bun5--si7 Au ek8  
Lang5 siong5-siong5 chheng-hou i cho3 lau7 Mou5-se, in-ui7 i e5 bin7 hoat-chhut eng5- kng, chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3 e5 Mou5-se.  
Chiong5-chian5 boe7 thiaN-tioh8 Ia5-sou e5 e5 hok-im, i si7 ti7 choe7-ok tiong, hit-si5 bin7-mau7 long2 bo5 chio3-kng, hoan2-tng2 si7 ng5-sng koh san2-gian3. I e5 a-phian3-hun-  chhoe si7 thih choe3--e5, i bo5 ai3 chha5--e5, si7 eng7 chit ho7 khah li7-pian7 thang kong3 tui3- tek8, in-ui7 i put-si5 ai3 kap lang5 oan-ke saN-phah. I -hian3 chiah8 tiau5, sin-the2 na2 lam2, soa3 lian5-lui7-tioh8 i chit8-kha chit8- chhiu2 pian3-sui7; na7 beh khi3 khah hng7 e5 sou2-chai7, tioh8 che7 chhia, a2-si7 che7 kio7 chiah e7 kau3.  
Tui3 i seng5-siu7 to7-li2 e5 liau2-au7, Siong7-te3 e5 koan5-leng5 u7 hian2-beng5, ka7 i thau2- pang3, Mou5-se chiu7 it-chin7 pian3-oaN7, seng- khu chiam7-chiam7 iong2-kiaN7, chiu7 na2 gau5 kiaN5. U7 chit8 ui7 e5 bok8-su cho3 kan-cheng3, kong2 i bat chit8-pai2 khoaN3 Mou5-se kiaN5 kau3 phou3 goa7 lou7, kau3 le2-pai3-tng5.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, i e5 kha-chhiu2 iau2 chin oah8-tang7, bin7-mau7 iah8 hoaN-hoaN hi2-hi2, lai5 hian2- chhut Chu2 e5 eng5-kng.  
Lau7 Mou5-se i2-keng cho3 Ki-tok-tou5, chek-si5 chhut pin5-ku3.  Tit-tioh8 kiu3 liau2- au7, chiu7 khi3 chhoe7 i chiong5-chian5 sou2 ham7-hai7 e5 chok8-tiuN2, ka7 in kong2, “Tek- choe7 lin2 e5 lang5 si7 goa2, chhut-chai7 lin2 heng5-hoat8, bo5 lun7 eng7 phah, koaiN-loh8 kaN, a2-si7 hou7 goa2 si2 iah8 thang.”  In hiah e5 lang5 thiaN i chiah lau2-sit8 jin7-i e5 choe7, tai7-ke keng3-tiong7, bo5 chit8 e5 kaN2 liah8 i. I heng5-tong7 ku2-chi2 chin u7 hah8 to7-li2, tak8 e5  
an-hioh-jit8 ong2-hoe5 kiaN5 nng7-phou3 lou7 kau3 chit8 keng pai3-tng5, teh liau7-li2 hit  lai7-bin7 e5 chap8-su7, ia7 pian3 thoan5 to7-li2, in2-chhoa7 che7-che7 lang5 lai5 bat Kiu3-chu2, ti3-kau3 siaN5-chhi7 e5 peh-siN3 siu7 kam2-kek.  
U7 chit8-pai2 goa7-kok e5 Bok8-su kap Mou5- se khi3 hia sun5 Seng3-hoe7, hou7 chit e5 ku7 koaN-hu2 chhiaN2, hu3 te5-piaN2-hoe7.  Kau3 ge5-mng5, chiu7 u7 siaN5-nih8 loa7-che7 e5 hoe7-tiuN2 lai5 hu3, i2-keng chiah8 te5, hit e5 ku7 koaN-hu2 khi2-lai5 tui3 Bok8-su kong2, “Goan2 chhiaN2 li2 lai5 chia, si7 in-ui7 li2 ti7 chit siaN5 lai7-goa7 sou2 cho3 e5 kang, sit8-chai7 cho3 goan2 e5 toa7 iu2- ek.”  Chiah oat8-lin3-tng2 tui3 Mou5-se kong2, “Kin-a2-jit8 koh khah tek8-piat8 chhiaN2 li2, in-ui7 goan2 khoaN3 li2 ko2-jian5 u7 sin3 e5 pin5-ku3. Goan2 put-chi2 chai-iaN2 li2 cheng5 e5  phaiN2 phin2-heng7. TaN u7 kai2-ok chiong5- sian7 kap cha2-si5 toa7 cheng-chha, chit ho7 e5 cheng3-ku3 ti7 goan2 e5 bin7-thau5- cheng5. ThiaN-kiN3 lin2 ai3 ti7-chia khi2 sin e5 pai3-tng5, put-ko3 sou2 te5 e5 gin5 bo5 kau3-giah8, goan2 koaN-hu2 u7 su-niu5 beh pou2-chiok.”  
Bok8-su thiaN in e5 ho2 oe7, chin u7 kam2- sim, chiu7 kong2 kui2-ku3 ka7 in seh-sia7, soa3 kong2, “Goan2 kau3 lin2 chun-kui3 e5 siaN5- chhi7, m7-na7 beh chin2-kiu3 Mou5-se na7-tiaN7, ia7 toa7 ng3-bang7 lam5-hu7 lo2-iu3 peN5-peN5 e7 sin3 Ia5-sou, chhin-chhiuN7 Mou5-se cho3 sin- chhong3-cho7 e5 lang5.”  
Hit ni5 siaN5-chhi7-nih8 e5 chok8-tiuN2 chiau3 ku7-khoan2 u7 siat ian5-siah8; che sng3-si7 ni5- ni5 toa7 lau7-jiat8 e5 su7. Hit-tiap8 m7-na7 kio3 Mou5-se lai5 hu3, soa3 chhiaN2 i che7 toa7-ui7. Chiau3-le7 hit e5 toa7-ui7 si7 tioh8 lau5 hou7 koaN- hu2 a2-si7 te7-it e5 hak8-bun7 e5 lang5. TaN, in an2- ni ho2 khoan2-thai7 chit8 e5 peng5-siong5 e5 lau7 lang5, liah8 i cho3 chun-kui3, si7 hian2-beng5 in hit jit8 ti7 Bok8-su e5 bin7-cheng5 sou2 o-lo2 e5 oe7 si7 chin-sit8 bo5 ke2.  
U7 chit8-pai2 Bok8-su chhut-goa7 e5 si5 bat ti7 Mou5-se e5 chhu3 keh-me5, tu2-teh khun3 hit-tiap8 chiu7 khoaN3 i chiong chit8 ki khang-kan he7 oa2 mng5-au7. Bok8-su mng7, “SiaN2-su7 chhong3 an2-ni ?”  I in3 kong2, “Li2 kiam2 m7-chai goa2 si7 chhau3-hi7-lang5 ? Siat-su2 chhat8 lai5 sak-khui mng5, chiu7 hit ki kan phah-ka-lauh8; an2-ni thang kio3 goa2 chhiN2, bian2-tit mih8-kiaN7 hou7 chhat8 chhiuN2.”  Chong2-si7 Mou5-se bo5 siuN7 kau3 cho3  
chhat8 e5, si5-siong5 tui3 chau3-kha  iah khang nng3-koe3, khah khoai3 chhiuN2 lai7-bin7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  
Bou2 jit8 Mou5-se ka7 bok8-su kong2 i sou2 tu2- tioh8 e5 su7. I kong2, Goa2 beh khun3 e5 si5-chun7, hut-jian5 kha toa7 thiaN3, na2 ku2 na2 siong- tiong7, kou-put-chiong tioh8 khi3 chau3-kha hiaN5 sio-chui2; chim3-liau2 chiu7 ho2, koh to2 teh khun3. Bin5-a2-chai3 goa2 khi3 chau3-kha chu2-png7, chiu7 khoaN3 ti7 piah-nih8 ui chit8 khang chin toa7, liam5-piN si3-ke3 sun5, ai3 chai chhat8 u7 theh8 siaN2-hoe3 khi3 a2 bo5 ? I2 -keng khoaN3-liau2 bo5 khiam3 poaN3-hang7, chiah chai Siong7-te3 e5 ho2-i3, hou7 goa2 u7 kha-thiaN3 e5 kan-khou2.  Tu2-ho2 hit-si5 chiu7 khi3 chau3- kha, chhat8 tioh8-kiaN to5-siam2.”  
Iu7-koh chit8-pai2 i bat mng7 bok8-su kong2, “Siong7-te3 hou7 goa2 bang7-kiN3 chit8 e5 bang7, chiu7 khoaN3-tioh8 gou7 siang chhau2-e5, chit8 siang 30 chiN5, li2 phah-sng3 che si7 sim2-mih8 e5 i3-su3 ?” Bok8-su in3 kong2, “Goa2 be7 hiau2 oan5-bang7, m7-chai si7 sim2-mih8 tiau7-thau5.”  Chong2-si7 Mou5-se lou7-boe2 tit- tioh8 bek8-si7. Kau3 gou7 goeh8 29 jit8 i koh liam7 i e5 bang7, gou7-siang chhau2-e5, chit8 siang 30 chiN5. Hit jit8 loh8 toa7-hou7, Mou5-se chiu7 ka7 ke-lai7 e5 lang5 kong2, “TaN goa2 i2- keng beng5-pek8 hit e5 tiau7-thau5 e5 i3-su3, chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 beh po3 goa2 chai, im- chui2 e5 chai-e7 beh kau3, taN tioh8 chun2-pi7, koaN2-kin2 chiong lan2 po2-poe3 e5 mih8-kiaN7 khng3 ti7 lau5-teng2.” Chhu3-piN thau5-boe2 bo5 sim2-mih8 sin3, sou2-i2 bo5 tai7-seng pi7-pan7. Bin5-a2-chai3 ko2-jian5 u7-iaN2 loh8-hou7, toa7-chui2 kau3, chi2-u7 Mou5-se kap i khah u7 tui3-tiong7 e5 mih8 ti7 lau5-teng2 long2 bo5 siu7-hai7.  
Tui3 Chi-na2 kek-beng7 siat Bin5-chu2-kok  liau2 peh-siN3 chin chhi-chham2.  Hap8-kai tng-peng tioh8 po2-hou7 peh-siN3, chong2-si7 in hoan2-tng2 piN3-cho3 hoe3-peng chin kiong5- po7, si3-ke3 khi3 chhiuN2-kiap, bo5 lun7 si7 chu- pun2-ka a2-si7 khah pin5-chian7 e5 lang5, long2 siu7 toa7 siong-hai7. Na7-u7 lang5 chou2-chi2, in liam5-piN khui-chheng3 lai5 phah. Bou2 jit8 u7 khi3 lah, ki5-tiong teh thou2-khui3 si5-kan kin2 koe3 e5 lang5 ia7 be7 chio2.  Jin5-seng oan2- jian5 ti7 bin5-bang7 tiong--nih8 chit8-iuN7.  Ui7- tioh8 an2-ni, chiu7 ti7 ka-teng5, ti7 kau3-hoe7, che7-che7 Ki-tok-tou5, i2-kip8 che7-che7 siau3- lian5 e5 lam5-lu2, tai7-ke saN-chhoa7, na2 chun2 koh chit8 pai2 beh khi3 Pek-li7-heng5 e5 be2- cho5-nih8 lai5 hong2-bun7 hit e5 hui-siong5 chhut-si3 e5 eN-a, chiu7-si7 khi3 kau3-hoe7 hu3 chiok Kiu3-chu2-tan7.  
Tui3 ti7 I e5 Chiok-hok, chiu7 chian5-tou5 iu2-bong7 e5 sin3-chia2 beh koh ke-thiN oah8- khi3, lian5 soe-jiok8 lo2-mou7 e5 lau7-lang5 ia7 e7 it-hoat iong2-chong3, chiu7 siau3-lian5 lang5 ka-poe7 hoat8-phoat oah8-tang7, tai7-ke e7 thang chun2-pi7 seng5-chiap teh-beh lai5 e5 sin-ni5.  Tui3 ti7 Kiu3-chu2-tan7 au7 na7 u7 tit- tioh8 chit-ho7 kek8-ho2 e5 hau7-ko2, chiu7 thang kiong-hi2.  
Ah !  Ki-tok e5 hok-im si7 leng5-tek keng-seng e5 oah8-lou7, sou2-i2 tng-si5 Chu2 Ia5- sou tui3 Ni5-ko-ti2-bo2 kong2, ‘Lang5 na7 bo5 teng5-thau5-siN be7 khoaN3-kiN3 Siong7-te3 e5 kok”  (Han7. 3 : 3 ). Na7 tit-tioh8 Ki-tok e5 hok-im lai5 teng5-thau5-siN e5 lang5, sui-jian5 lian5-lian5 na2 lau7, iau2-ku2 leng5-tek tui3- hoan2 na2 iong2-chong3.  
ChhiaN2 mng7, Seng3-tan7 si7 siaN2-hoe3 ? M7-bian2 kong2; liat8-ui7 i2-keng beng5-beng5 pek8-pek8.  M7-si7 pat8 hang7, chiu7-si7 Sin5 e5 Hoa3-sin (Incarnation) chiaN5-cho3 lang5 lai5 chhut-si3 ti7 se3-kan. Tui3 chit-e5 lan2 tit in- hui7, khe2-hoat oah8-lou7. Chong2-si7 ti7 Kiu3- chu2-tan7 m7-na7 kan-ta teh ki3-liam7 chit8 e5 Seng3-chia2 lai5 se3-kan beh kiu3 lan2 thoat- li7 choe7-ok na7-tiaN7; ia7 m7-si7 kan-ta chiok- ho7 hit e5 sia2-sin the3 lan2 si2, lai5 oan5-seng5 jin5-ai3 hit e5 ui2-jin5 na7-tiaN7. Ki5-sit8 ia7 si7 teh kam2-sia7 thiN-te7 e5 Chu2-chaiN2 choan5- leng5 e5 Siong7-te3. In-ui7 Siong7-te3 e5 chiok- hok chhiong-moa2 ti7 te7-bin7-chiuN7, Siong7- te3 e5 pu3-u7 si7 bu5-chin7-chong5, Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3-thang3 eng7 lan2 lang5 iu2-han7 e5 ti3-lek8 be7 chhek-tok8-tit, poaN5-koe3 ti7 lan2 e5 siong2- siong7 (KhoaN3 I-hut-sou2 3 : 19). 
Ai3 e5 kek8-tou7 m7-si7 eng7 tin-tiong7 e5 but8- phin2 e7 oaN7-tit. Ai3 e5 chi3-siong7, ai3 e5 kek8-  
tiam2 tioh8 u7 pau-ham5 hi-seng ti7 hit tiong-kan, sou2-i2 lan2 sin3 Siong7-te3 sit8-chai7 u7 chiong I pun2-sin hian3 hou7 lan2 tai7-ke ( Han7. 3 : 16 ). An2-ni chit-ho7 sit8-su7 kiam2 m7-si7 Kiu3-chu2-tan7 e5 pun2-i3 mah ?  
Hi-seng hian3-sin si7 ThiN-pe7 e5 pun2- seng3. Lan2 tai7-ke u7 siu7-tioh8 chit ho7 e5 jiat8-cheng5. Lan2 tui3 ti7 chit ho7 hi-seng hian3-sin-tek e5 cheng-sin5 tit-tioh8 an-jian5, tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3 (KhoaN3 I2-sai3-a, 53 chiuN). Ia5-sou i2-keng u7 chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 e5 khoan2-sit, chiu7 khut ka-ti7 sun7-hok8 kau3 ti7 si2, sim7-chi3 si2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3” (Hui- lip8-pi2 2 : 8).  
Liat8-ui7 ah ! Chu2 Ia5-sou eng7 I chun- kui3 e5 si2 hian3 hou7 lan2, ia7 lan2 chu7-kim i2-hou7 beh ui7-tioh8 I cho3 siaN2-hoe3 ?  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 468 koan3,1924 ni5 3 geh8)
