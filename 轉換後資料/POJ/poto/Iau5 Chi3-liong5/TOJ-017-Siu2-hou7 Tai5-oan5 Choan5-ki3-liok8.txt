“Li2 cheng5 Chong2-thong2 Teng-hui” hoat-khi2 lek8-su2-seng3 e5 oah8-tang7 ng3-bang7 u7 chhiau-koe3 100-ban7 lang5 “chhiu2-khan-chhiu2 siu2-hou7 Tai5-oan5,” goa2 ti7 bang7-lou7 khoaN3-tioh8 “sou3-ui7 tian2-chong5 lek8-su2 數位典藏歷史” teh tin-kiu5 hip-siong7 gi7-kang e5 si5, goa2 koaN2-kin2 ti7 bang7-lou7 lai5 oan5-seng5 po3-mia5. Che si7 lan2 400-ni5 lai5 thau5 chit8-pai2 e5 toa7 tai7-chi3, choan5-the2 u7 kiong7-tong5 li2-liam7 e5 peh-siN3 cho3-hoe2 beh lau5--loh8 chin sui2 e5 lek8-su2 ki3-su7, goa2 tong-jian5 ai3 chham-ka koh ai3 siuN7 pan7-hoat m7-si7 ti7 hia khia7 na7-tiaN7, in-ui7 kan-ta ti7 hia khia7 siuN-koe3-thau5 bo5-chhai2.
2-goeh8 28-jit8 si7 pai3-lak8 thiN-khi3 chin ho2. Iau2-boe7 9-tiam2 goa2 to7 koaN2-kin2 khui-chhia khi3 Lam5-a2-kheN toaN7 goa2 sou2 hun-phoe3 tioh8 Tai5-thng5 lou7-toaN7 long2-chong2 u7 nng7 kong-li2 e5 chek-jim7-khu. “Tai5-it-soaN3” seng2-kong-lou7 i2-keng u7 chin che7 lang5 pi2 goa2 khah cha2, ti7 tak8-e5 tiam3 teh cheng2-li2 tiuN3-pang5 chun2-pi7 se3-ki kap toa7-ki e5 ki5-a2, chun2-pi7 kun2-chui2 kap i2-thau5-a2, the5-kiong pit-iau3 e5 i-liau5 khi3-chhai5... KhoaN3-tioh8 tai7-ke long2 cho3 kah chiok hoaN-hi2. Siang7 tiong7-iau3..e5 long2-si7 chu7-tong7 chu7-hoat lai5 chham-ka. Goan5-pun2 goa2 si7 beh seng liau2-kai2 hian7-tiuN5 to7 ho2, khoaN3 tioh8 an2-ne goa2 ma2-siong7 kai2-pian3 sim-i3 lau5 ti7 hian7-tiuN5 tau3 cho3 khang-khoe3, tong5-si5 koh hiong3-cheng5 chau2 kau3 Kong-san ian5-lou7 tak8 sou2-chai7 long2 i2-keng chin che7 lang5 ti7 lou7-piN, ti7 chhiu7-a2-kha teh theng3-hau7 2 tiam2 28 hun e5 oah8-tang7, tai7-ke long2 chhio3-bi-bi. Hit-si5 goa2 khoaN3 chit8-e7-a2 chhiu2-pio2--leh!  Chiah 10 tiam2 goa7 na7-tiaN7.
Si5-kan lu2 pek-kin7 chiah8 tiong-tau3 au7 to7 jin5-san jin5-hai2, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh pan7 toa7 hi2-ian3, ma7 na2 teh cho3-chio3--leh. Lou7-piN bo5 khia7-ke bo5 kong-u2, si7 tui3 to2-ui7 pian3 hiah che7 lang5 chhut--lai5?  Jin5-bin5 e5 khui3-lat8 ti7 chit-si5 hian2-hian7, long2-si7 phoh-sit8 kou2-i3 koh kho2-ai3 e5 hiong-chhin lai5 chham-u2..e5!  Chin-chiaN3 u7 kau3 che7 lang5, u7 che7 lun5-i2 e5 hiong-chhin, u7 choan5-ke toa7-toa7 se3-se3 long2 chhut-tong7..e5, u7 chit8-tui3 chit8-tui3 e5 siau3-lian5-ke, lian5 thiong2-but8 (kau2-a2, niau-a2) ma7 chhoa7-chhoa7 chhut lai5 tau3 nau7-jiat8, Ko-hiong5 koh-u7 chu7-iong7 e5 khi3-chhia kai2-chong cho3 hoe-chhia long2-chong2 10 goa7 tai5.  Ti7 jit8-thau5 tiong-ng bo5 lang5 hiam5 joah8, ian5-lou7 e5 kang-chhiuN2 u7 thau5-ke bian2-hui3 the5-kiong liang5-chui2 kap chui2-ko2, u7 lang5 chhut-lai5 tai3-nia2 chhiuN3 koa, u7 lang5 toa7-siaN hoah! Goa2 hip-siong7 hip kah chin kam2-tong7, hut-jian5 kam2-kak lan2 e5 hiong-chhin lam5..e5 long2 chin ian-tau5, lu2..e5 long2 chin iau-kiau bi2-le7, sui-jian5 lai7-saN lau5 koaN lau5 kah tam5-kou5-kou5, it-chhe3 e5 hu3-chhut long2 chin u7 i3-gi7!  Khia7-ti7 lou7-piN chun2-pi7 beh khan-chhiu2 siu2-hou7 Tai5-oan5 e5 hiong-chhin si7 che7 kah pian3 kui-chhop kui-chhop khia7 cho3-hoe2, u7-e5 sou2-chai7 si7 chhiau-koe3 6-7 pai5, na7 chiau3 thong2-ke3-hak8 e5 ke3-sng3 hong-hoat lai5 thong2-ke3, choan-ka ti7 lou7-boe2 u7 hoat-piau2 eng-kai si7 chhiau-koe3 250 ban7 lang5 i2-siong7 lai5 chham-u2 chit8-pai2 e5 oah8-tang7.
