
Chong2-tok e5 ko3-si7
Kin7-lai5 Tai5-oan5 e5 Chong2-tok, chiu7-si7 Nai2-bok8 Tai7-jin5, tiau3 chiah-e5 toa7-koaN khi3 Tai5-pak thang thiaN i kong2 ti7-li2 Tai5-oan5 e5 kui-ki2. Au7--lai5 chhut chit8-e5 ko3-si7  thong-ti koaN-oan5 kap peh-seN3; sit8-chai7 si7 chin ho2-i3, hou7 lang5 khoaN3-liau2 thang  hoaN-hi2. Lan2 tioh8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 hiah- e5 bun5-bu2 koaN-oan5 long2 chiau3 an2-ni kiaN5, ng3-bang7 Tai5-oan5 e5 hai2-su7 toa7 heng-ong7, peh-seN3 tak8 sou2-chai7 an-un2 khoaN3-oah8.  TaN chiong chit-e5 ko3-si7 ke2-soeh peh8- oe7-ji7, lai5 in3 ti7 e7-te2 hou7 lin2 khoaN3.    
Heng-khi2 Cheng3-su7 e5 Chong2-thau5-su7.     
Ku7-ni5 6 goeh8 kian3-ti3 Tai5-oan5 e5 Chong2-tok-hu2 ti7 chit-e5 thou2-te7, tng hit- si5 tak8 sou2-chai7 be7 bian2-tit hou7 chan5-jim2 e5 peng, hui2-lui7 e5 chhat8 khou2-chhou2, thong hai2-su7 khah che7 si7 piN3-cho3 chian3-tiuN5. Kau3 kin-ni5 3 goeh8 chiu7 gi7-tiaN7 Chong2- tok-hu2 e5 lut8-le7 tiau5-khoaN2; taN u7 lioh8-a2 heng-khi2 lai5 keng-eng5 cho3-chiaN5 chit-e5 tai7-chi3; khiok-si7 kiaN-liau2 pah-hang7 su7 long2 loan7-loan7, tak8-hang7 e5 cheng3-su7 che7-che7 koh-iuN7. Sou2-i2 u7 sin-siat toa7 sio2 e5 cheng3-su7, thang hou7 thong hai2-su7 long2 siang7 chit8-e5 lut8-hoat, khin5-khin5 lai5 chun- than3. Kau3 taN keng-koe3 7 goeh8-jit8 chiah khoaN3-kiN3 kin ki saN chiau3-eng3, hoeh- meh8 saN koan3-thong; chiong5-kim i2-au7 pan7 cheng3-su7 e7 tu2-ho2. Tu5 bo5 kan-siap8 tai7-kiok8 i2-goa7, it-khai3 ui2 te7-hng koaN chun2 i sui5-pian7 khi3 pan7, Chong2-tok-hu2 chu7-jian5 chong2-lang2 choan5 kiok8 e5 cheng3- su7, lai5 seng2-chhat ti7-li2 e5 kong-lo5.    
Hian7-si5 gi2-tiaN7 te7-hng koaN sou2 koan2--e5, tioh8 koh siat ha7-kip e5 cheng3-su7-sou2; chiu7- si7 chiong pun2-te7 ho2 mia5-siaN e5 lang5 lai5 siat i cho3 ui2-goan5, thang hou7 si7-toa7 e5 i3- kian3 kap peh-seN3 e5 cheng5-heng5 e7 tit siong-thong, che sit8-chai7 iau3-kin2. Lun7 kau3 thong-heng5 e5 chiong-theng5 i2-keng seng5-an3, theng3-hau7 me5-ni5 4 goeh8 chiah it-khai3 thong-heng5.  
Pun2 hai2-su7 e5 hong-siok8 thou2-im long2 kap loe7-te7 [Jit8-pun2] koh-iuN7, sou2 u7 e5 cheng3-su7 ho7-leng7 bo5 m7-tioh8 kho3 keng2-li7 (ge5-iah8) pan7-li2. Au7--lai5 beh ke-khiN khah  
che7 keng2-li7, thang ti7 boe7 seN-khi2 chai-ho7 e5 si5 lai5 seng ti5-hong5, ti7 bo5 pi7-pan7 e5 jit8 chhiau-liah8 hui2-lui7, chiu7 liong5-bin5 e7 tit an-jian5 cho3 kan-ki2 thau5-lou7; an2-ni thang ng3-bang7 te7-hng kap cheng3-su7 che5-cheng2 heng-khi2.    
Ti7-li2 peh-seN3 te7-it iau3-kin2--e5, si7 chai7-ti7 khoan-iong5 kap giam5-gai5 tioh8 tu2-ho2, in-tian2 kap ui-giam5 cho3-hoe2 kiaN5, hou7 peh- seN3 kiaN-ui3 ui-giam5 lai5 siau3-liam7 in-tek; bang7 ti7 pun2 hai2-su7 chin-chiaN3 e7 khoaN3- kiN3 an2-ni ah ! Chong2-si7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 kiN3- tioh8 goan2 na7 m7-si7 kiaN-ui3 chiu7-si7 hoaiN5-hoaiN5 keh8, khap8-chit8-tioh8 eng7 bu2-ban7 leng5-jiok8 khoan2-thai7 goan2, hit-ho7 khoan2 long2-si7 kho2-ouN3. Na7-si7 pun2 hai2-su7 si7 tui3 kau-chian3 e5 kong-hau7 lai5 tit-tioh8, chiaN3 u7 peng-be2 kau3 te7. Kau3 chheng3-ian siau-bo5 e5 si5, loe7-te7 e5 seng-li2-lang5 toe3 au7-piah3 chiam7-chiam7 kau3, long2- si7 ka2-chia3 thai5-iaN5 e5 se3-thau5 lai5 khi-hu7 pun2-te7 e5 peh-seN3, put-lun7 be2-be7 soe3-chhu3 siong5-siong5 bo5 chiau3 cheng5-li2, ham7-hai7 liong5-bin5 long2 bo5 liah8-cho3 iau3-kin2. Bun5- bu2 koaN-oan5 ia7-si7 ai3 eng7 ui-giam5 se3-thau5 lai5 ap--lang5, na7 u7 chit8-e5 m7 than3 beng7- leng7--e5 a2-si7 u7 gi5-ngai2 e5 choe7-ko3--e5, chiu7 ku3-chai7 in pak8 chhut-chai7 in ah, u7-e5 sut u7-e5 phah, long2 bo5 te3 thang sou3 oan-ong2, soa3 saN-kap khng3 oan3-hin7, jiok8-e5 li7 tioh8- kiaN khu7-phaN3,kiong5-e5 li7 hoan2-tng2 khong3-ku7. Na7-si7 ti7 peng-be2 loan-loan7 e5 jit8, eng2 be7 bian2-tit u7 mau5-peng7; na7 kau3- ku2 bo5 seng2-chhat, oan3-hin7 chiu7 na2 kiat, bin5-sim chiu7 na2 poan7, che kap cheng3-su7 u7 toa7 kan-gai7, sit8-chai7 m7-si7 sio2-khoa2. Iu7-koh hian7-kim thong hai2-su7 i2-keng peng5-tiaN7, pah-hang7 e5 hoat-tou7 cho3 chit8-e7 oaN7 sin, eng-kai tioh8 seng an-ui3 peh- seN3 e5 sim, che chai7-ti7 liau7-li2 te7-hng e5 lang5. In iau3-kin2 tioh8 saN-kap keng2-kai3 jin7-chin chun-than3 lai5 siat-hoat, thang lai5 se2-chheng ku7-te2 e5 pe7-peng7. Siat-su2 na7 u7-e5 kaN2 chiong hit-ho7 po7-giok8 e5 su7 lai5 khoan2-thai7 peh-seN3, che sit8-chai7 si7 biau2-si7 goan2 e5 cheng3-su7, leng5-jiok8 goan2 e5 kok-ka; bo5-lun7 si7 koaN a2-si7 peh-seN3 tek- khak beh giam5-pan7 ti7-choe7, koat-toan3 bo5 beh khoan-iong5.    
Hoan7-na7 u7 khui-khoah thou2-san2, seng-li2 khi2-sek, poaN-un7 kau-thong , oe7-seng, [si7 teh chha5-kho2 khoaN3 sim2-mih8 mih8 e7 hai7--tioh8 peh-seN3], ka3-si7 lang5--e5, hiah-e5 tai7-chi3 tak8-hang7 kai3 iau3-kin2 tioh8 tho3- tong3 kau3 chiau5-pi7; che m7-na7 ui7-tioh8 chong2-koan2 pun2 hai2-su7 kap hou7 choh-sit lang5 soa3 lai5 chia toa3 e5 lou7-eng7, ia7 si7 cho3  
Tai5-oan5 peh-seN3 e5 hok-khi3, ia7-si7 ho2 cheng3-su7 e5 ho2 in-hui7. Na7-si7 u7 koan-siap8 tai7-kiok8 e5 su7, Chong2-tok-hu2 i2-keng gi7-tiaN7 chai7-an3, chiu7 eng-kai tioh8 koaN2-kin2 lai5 thong-heng5. Iu7-koh na7 kan-ta siok8-ti7 chit e5 te7-hng e5 su7, chiu7 chun2 in e5 koaN-tiuN2 li7-pian7 siat-hoat khi3-pan7.    
Tai5-oan5 peh-seN3 koan3-si3 e5 hong-siok8, si7 siok8-ti7 ni5-chhim goeh8-ku2 teh tui3- tiong7--e5, tu5 toa7 keh8-tioh8 kok-ka e5 teng7- le7, koh toa7 gai7-tioh8 cheng3-su7 e5 i2-goa7, it- khai3 m7-sai2 hoe3-tu5, chhin-chhiuN7 piN7 thau5- chang, pak8-kha, i2-kip8 ti3-bo7 chheng7-saN, khoaN3 beh ke2-oaN7 a2-m7, sui5-pian7 chhut- chai7 lang5 e5 i3-su3. Lun7-kau3 kim3 a-phian3- hun hit-chan5 su7, chu7-jian5 u7 gi7-tiaN7 e5 tiau5-khoaN2, si7 chiau3 chhu3-su7 chiam7- chiam7 ke2. Ki5-u5 e5 ho2 hong-siok8 u7 iu2-ek ti7 se3-kan e5 kau3-hoa3-e5, chiu7 tioh8 ku2-tng5 lau5-teh.    
Teng2-bin7 pai5-liat8 chiah-e5 tiau5-khoaN2, si7 te7-hng sou2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 eng7 e5 cheng3-su7. Chit-tiap8 si7 lioh8-a2 tai7-khai3 lai5 kong2-khi2 chiah-e5 chai7-an3 e5 tiau5-khoaN2 na7-tiaN7; na7 ai3-beh kong2 khah chim-chiok, chiu7 leng7- heng5 chiaN3 koh chhut ko3-si7 hou7 lin2 chai.

Cheng2-che5 koaN-oan5 e5 lut8-le7.    
Lun7 kau3 cho3 koaN-hu2--e5 chiu7-si7 cheng3- su7-hu2 e5 ki-koan, ia7-si7 ti7-li2 kau3-hoa3 e5 kin pun2; in teh tng-chit cho3 koaN chu7-jian5 tioh8 toan-chiaN3 lut8-hoat, tui3-tiong7 tek- heng7 kong-gi7, lai5 eng5-kng in e5 chit-hun7. Sou2-i2 ti7 chiah e5 heng5-hoat8 e5 hoat-tou7 i2- goa7, ia7 u7 siat lut8-le7 hou7 chiah e5 koaN-hu2 thang chun-than3.    
TaN tui3 Tai5-oan5 kap PheN5-ou5 siok8 goan2 Koan2-hat liau2-au7, sng3-si7 keng-eng5 bo5 kui2-jit8,pah-poaN e5 su7 it-chin7 si7 taN chiah heng-khi2, hoan7-na7 cheng3-hu2 e5 sou2 kiaN5 sou2 cho3, i2-kip8 koaN-hu2 e5 heng5-tong7 ku2-chi2, si7 hou7 pun2-kok goa7-kok e5 lang5 kong- cheng3 teh khoaN3. In hian7-jim7 cho3 koaN ti7 chit sou2-chai7--e5 eng-kai tioh8 koan-sim pan7 lut8-hoat, kin2-sin7 chin7 in e5 chit, thang lai5 po3-iong5 cheng3-su7 e5 ui-giam5, kap hian2-beng5 ti7-li2 e5 kong-lo5.    
 
Lun7 cho3 koaN-hu2 e5 lang5 sou2 iau3-kin2--e5 chiu7-si7 tioh8 kiong-keng3 khin5-kin2 chheng-liam5 kong-to7 cheng3-tit8 kin2-sin7 kin-chat, an2-ni chiah si7 chun-keng3 si7-toa7, chin7-tiong e5 to7-li2. Chong2-si7 teng2-si-koaN e5 beng7-leng7 iau3-kin2 tioh8 chun-than3; na7 u7 lang5 i-e5 sim teh siuN7 che u7 sio2-khoa2 bo5 tu2-ho2, i eng-kai tioh8 pin2-beng5 in-e5 i3- kian3, pi7-pan7 hou7 teng2-si-koaN keng2-soan2 chhai5-chhu2, siat-su2 na7 m7-chun2, i ia7 tioh8  pang3-sak ka-ti7 e5 i3-kian3 lai5 than3 beng7- leng7. Hoan7-na7 seng2-chhat tai7-chi3 chih- chiap lang5, tioh8 choan-sim ti3-i3 beh kiu5 na2 ho2, siu-cheng2 seng-khu kou3-siu2 liong5- sim, tioh8 khiam7-seng2 phok-sou3 chheng- liam5, beh eng7 lang5 a2-si7 siu2-ki2, tioh8 kong- piN5 toan-chiaN3 bo5 phian-su, na7 siok8 ti7 ki- bit8 e5 su7, tioh8 kin2-sin7 sio2-sim. Teng2-bin7 chit kui2-na7 hang7 tai7-chi3 kai3 iau3-kin2 tioh8 ti3-i3 keng2-kai3, siat-su2 cho3 bo5 kau3-giah8 chiu7 beh an3-choaN2-iuN7 thang po2-choan5 lan2 cho3 koaN e5 the2-bian7, beh an3-choaN2-iuN7 lai5 po3-iong5 lan2 cheng3-su7-hu2 e5 ui-hong sin3-sit8? Cho3 koaN-thau5 e5 lang5 iau3-kin2  tioh8 cheng2-che5 lut8-hoat, giam5-kin2 lai5 hian2-beng5 chun2-chat, kou2-bu2 bian2-le7 i sou2 koan2- hat e koaN-oan5, ng3-bang7 bo5 kiaN5m7- tioh8. Siat-su2 na7 u7 bo5 chun-than3 hang5- hok8 lut8-hoat sou2 ki3--e5,chiu7 beh sui5-si5 chha5-mng7, chiau3 chek-hoat8 ti7-choe7 e5 kui-le7 hun-piat tang7-khin lai5 pan7, koat- toan3 bo5 beh iong5-un2 liong7-cheng5.
