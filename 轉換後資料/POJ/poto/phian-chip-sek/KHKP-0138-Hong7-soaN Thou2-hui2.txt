
Hong7-soaN Thou2-hui2
Ti7 6 goeh8 23 jit8 11 tiam2 cheng u7 chit8 e5 Jit8-pun2 Heng5-chhun Bin5-cheng3 chi- pou7 koaN-oan5 beh khi3 Pang-liau5.  Li7 Tang- kang2 chha-put-to phou3-poaN3-lou7 in kau3- chit8 sou2-chai7 ho7-cho3 Chhek-khut.  Ti7-hia tu2-tioh8 50 e5 thou2-hui2, hou7 in chhiuN2 $2000, lang5 soa3 hou7 in thai5-si2, iah8 cham7 i e5 thau5-khak koaN7 si3-ke3 teh un5-chng phai3 ho2-giah8 lang5 e5 gin5.  U7 un5 tui3 Tek-a2-kha- chng soa3 hia keh-me5, sit8-chai7 chin kho2- sioh, u7 lian5-lui7 sian7-liong5 e5 peh-seN3.  Toa7- oh8 hak8-seng Ong5 Chang5 sia2 phoe kong2, “Goan2 khoaN3 chit-ho7 gui5-hiam2 e5 se3-bin7 khiok u7 siuN7 beh chhiaN2 siam2 pat8-ui7, m7- ku2 chit chng-nih8 phah-lo5 m7-chun2 lang5 chau2 kong2 tioh8 seN tang5 seN, si2 tang5 si2. Lang5 na7 chau2 mih8 beh chhiuN2, kiam2- chhai2 soa3 siu7-hai7 ia7 kaN2.  Goan2 tui3 an2-ni be7-tit chau2, na7 beh chau2 khiok iu5-goan5 e7 li7, chong2-si7 kiaN-liau2 lang5 khoaN3 goan2 bo5, beh io5-tang7 giau5-gi5, soa3 sun7 ki-hoe7 lai5 hai7 sin3 Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7, sit8-chai7 khi3-to3 chin liong2-lan5.  M7 chai beh chaiN2-iuN7? In-ui7 goa2 tu2-a2 tui3 Hu2-siaN5 to3-lai5 kau3 Tang-kang2, hit-si5 u7 chit8 e5 hian3-peng mng7 goa2 tui3 to2-ui7 lai5?  Goa2 kong2 tui3 Hu2-siaN5.  I kong2, Li2 to2-khi3?  Goa2 kong2 beh khi3 Tek-a2-kha.  I kong2, Tek-a2-kha long2-chong2 si7 phaiN2-lang5 lliau2-liau2.  Chit kin7-jit8 koh in-ui7 chit-chan5-su7, sou2-i2 koh- khah thang kiaN.”  
  Au7-lai5 hiah e5 chhat u7 khi3 chak ti7 SoaiN7-a2-kha.  (SoaiN7-a2-kha li7 Tek-a2-kha chha-put-to chit8 li2 lou7 na7-tiaN7).  Koe3 chit8 nng7 jit8 Jit8-pun2 peng tui3 Tang-kang2, Chui20te2-liau5, kap Pang-liau5 khi3 hia phah thou2-hui2 chiu7 soaN3-soaN3.  Hit-chun7 chiaN5 chap8 e5 chng-thau5 hou7 peng sio-khi3. Chang5-a2 iah8 hou7 in liah8 khi3 Pang-liau5 koaiN chit8 me5, chong2-si7 tu2-tioh8 bo5 sim2- mih8 tai7-chi3 chiu7 koh pang3 i to3-lai5.  
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 138 koan3,1896 ni5 9 geh8).
