
ChheN-hoan thai5 lang5
Kin7-lai5 Tai5-oan5 e5 chheN-hoan put-chi2 kiong5-hiong beh thai5 lang5.  Ti7 cheng5- soaN kap au7-soaN long2 si7 an2-ni.  Goan2 chih-tioh8 Pou-li2-sia7 Thoan5-to7 Sian-seN, PhoaN Chheng-kho3, e5 phoe kong2 :---     
Goa2 ai3 thong-ti chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 siok8 Toa7-lam3 e5 hiaN-ti7, PhoaN Mau5-keh, Kai-tan, ti7 sin-lek8 3 goeh8 2 ho7 khi3 cho3 ai3-iong2 hou7 chheN-hoan thai5-khi3, chin7 kho2-sioh!  Chit e5 gin2-na2 jin5-chai5 khiok ho2, chu-chit ia7 be7 bai2.  Gin2-na2 chiu thang khi2-chhu2 lau2-sit8 tiong-hou7.  Ti7 Pou-li7-sia7 Kong-hak8-hau7 thak8 e5 chut-giap8 seng.  Kin-ni5 i ai3 khi3 Tiong-oh8 thak8, put-ko3 kau3 chiaN goeh8 chiN5-hang7 khah te2 khoeh, be7 thang khi3, ji5-boe2 hou7 lang5 chhiaN3 khi3 cho3 ai3-iong2 soa3 the3 lang5 si2, khiok m7-si7 koaN-hu2 phai3-e5.  I5 e5 lau7-pe7 si7 Kai-tan, Sa-bong.     
KoaN-hu2 phai3 lang5 khui ai3-lou7 tui3 kin-ni5 1 goeh8 6 ho7 chiu7 tang7-kang.  Khui u7 nng7 goeh8 jit8, chheN-hoan long2 m7-kaN2 tin2- tang7, tiam7-tiam7-a2 hou7 in khui.  TaN kau3 Lo5-chui2-khe thau5 chiu7-si7 Sng-soaN-kha e5 sou2-chai7.  Pun2 teh siuN7 chit pang chheN- hoan kaN2 e7 tau5-hang5, bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 sin-lek8 3 goeh8 2 ho7 teng2-pou hut-jian5- kan u7 chit8 iaN5 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 te7 lak8 tui7.  Sou2 thai5 e5 chiu7-si7 sun5-cha 1 mia5, ai3- iong2 10 mia5, kiong7 11 mia5.     
Chit8 e5 sun5-cha kap 3 e5 ai3-iong2 chau2 chhut-lai5.  Ti7 lai7-bin7--e5 thai5-liau2, chiu7 koh tui jiok chau2 chhut lai5--e5.  Tui khah loh8-lai5 tu2-tioh8 chit8 pan teh cho7 tin5-kio5, lai5 hu7-kin7 tit8-tit8 phah chheng3. Hit jit8 thai5 tioh8 saN e5 chheN-hoan, tioh8 siong e5 chheN-hoan ia7 chin che7.  In-ui7 lang5 khah chio2, chheN-hoan khah che7 chiu7 m7-kaN2 koh jiok-oa2 khi3 thai5, sou2-i2 sin-si kap tioh8 siong-e5 long2 hou7 chheN- hoan gia5 to3-tng2-khi3.  Hit-me5 koh sio- thai5 thau3-me5.     
Te7-ji7 jit8 koh thai5-tioh8 4 e5 chheN-hoan, ke3-kiong7 thai5 7 e5 chheN-hoan to3-tng2. Hit jit8 chheN-hoan beh lai5 ui5, thiaN-kiN3 kong2 tai7-seng kui2-e5-a2 khang chhiu2 lai5 kap ai3-iong2 chhit-tho5.  Chit8 e5 chheN- hoan tui3 phau3-chi2 ka7 gia5 chit8 siuN teh- beh chau2.  Sun5-cha khoaN3-kiN3 ka7 i sian3 chhui3-phoe2, chheN-hoan chit8 e7 hoah chiu7 in e5 to-chheng3 long2 hou7 sa-sa khi3.  ChheN-hoan liam5-piN khi2-u3 ui5-lai5, hiah e5 lang5 long2 khang chhium2 tiam7-tiam7-a2 hou7 thai5 bo5 hoat-i-ta-oa5.  Chheng3 hou7 chhiuN2 chap8 it ki, phau3-chi2 hou7 chhiuN2 6 siuN khi3.     
Pou-li2-sia7 e5 Chi-thiaN-tiuN2 chai-iaN2 liam5- piN koh khioh ai3 iong2 chin che7, ia7 chhiaN2 liok8-kun e5 peng 200 goa7 mia5 liam5-piN chiuN7 chheN-hoan-sia7 khi3.  TaN u7 5 jit8 lah, iau2-boe7 chai u7 koh si2-sit loa7-che7 lang5. Hit e5 chhaN-hoan-sia7 tui3 Pou-li2-sia7 kau3 hia tioh8 kiaN5 3 jit8 ku2 chiah e7 kau3-ui7. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 289 koan3, 1909 ni5 4 geh8)
