
Hun5-lim5 e5 Thou2-hui2
Si3-goeh8-kan Hun5-lim5-koan7 e5 thou2-hui2 chin seng7, u7 chiaN5 chheng lang5 chu7-chip8 ti7 soaN-lai7, siong5-siong5 chhut-lai5 cheng- piang3.  Tau2-lak8 ke Jit8-pun2 lang5 e5 tiam3 ia7 hou7 in cheng.  Gou7-goeh8 chhe 3 Jit8- pun2 peng it-jiap8 mia5 khi3 beh liah8 chhat8, tian-to3 hou7 chhat ui5, phah-si2 2, 3 lang5, ia7 tioh8-siong 4, 5, e5.  Au7-lai5 Jit8-pun2 chu7-chip8 in-e5 peng chiaN5-chheng mia5, khi3 sio-chng; ke3-kiong7 sio chha-put-to 30 chng; bi2 chhek, ke-si che7-che7 hou7 in sio--tioh8, ia7 u7 phah-si2 nng7 saN pah lang5. Tui3 hit-tiap8 thou2-hui2 na2-seng7; kau3 5 goeh8 18 phah Lim5-ki2-pou.  Hit-si5 u7 chit8- e5 goa7-kok lang5 hou7 chhat8 phah-si2, chiu7- si7 Bong7-bai5 lang5, Kheng3-ki3 e5 thau5-ke, chha-put-to 70 hoe3 e5 lang5.  Hit cha2-khi2 u7 tui3 Lim5-ki2-pou khi2-sin ai3 khi3 Chip8-chip8, lou7--nih8 tu2-tioh8 chhat8, kaN2-si7 khoaN3 i cho3 Jit8-pun2 lang5 chiu7 ka i thai5.     
Kau3 5 goeh8 20 Tau2-lak8 ia7 hou7 thou2-hui2 phah; ia7 phah Chhi3-tong5-hang7, IN5-na5-a2, Po2-tau2-a2.  Gou7-goeh8 28 phah Lok8-kang2, phah be7 khui tioh8 the3.  Hit-si5 Lok8- kang2 ke u7 sio chha-put-to 300 keng chhu3 kap tiam3-thau5.  Au7-lai5 Jit8-pun2-peng tit8-tit8 phah chin3-cheng5.  Tau2-lak8 ke ia7 hou7 in tho2 to3-tng2.     
U7 lang5 kong2 Chip8-chip8 chham Po- sia7 ia7 hou7 chhat8 chiam3-khi3.  Ka-gi7-siaN5 kap Tang-toa7-tun (chiu7-si7 sin-siat e5 Tai5- oan5-hu2-siaN5 ) ia7 hou7 in kong, kong be7 khui. Chiang-hoa3 bo5 phah; kui2-na7-jit8 u7 koaiN sia5-mng5 m7-chun2 lang5 sui5-pian7 chhut- jip8.     
Hit-si5 Tau2-lak8 peng khi3 sio chng, lan2 thiaN to7-li2 e5 hiaN-ti7 chit8-lang5 u7 chhut-lai5 ka7 i khoaN3, bo5 gi5-gou7 hou7 in phah-si2.  Ia7 u7 chit8-e5 jip8-kau3 e5 hiaN-ti7, mia5 kio3 Im3-hiaN, i-e5 chhu3-theh8 chham hoe3-mih8 long2 hou7 in sio--tioh8; hiaN-ti7 chiu7 khioh tam7-poh8 bou7 i cho3 sou2-hui3.  Leng7-goa7 bo5 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 hiaN-ti7 a2-si7 pai3-tng5 u7 siu7 sim2-mih8 hai7.     
Lan2 khoaN3 Tai5-oan5 e5 keng2-gu7 chhin- chhiuN7 chit-ho7 e5 khoan2, tioh8 pek-chhiat kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 si7-toa7 lang5 chiau3 to7-li2 lai5 khoan2-thai7 peh-siN3, peh-siN3 thang hoaN-hi2 sun7-than3.  “Tioh8 the3 ong5 kap toa7-koaN ki5-kiu5, hou7 lan2 chit8-si3-lang5 kiong-keng3, toan-hong, peng5-an bo5 tai7- chi3” (I The5-mou5-thai3 2 : 2).  
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 137 koan3,1896 ni5 8 geh8)
