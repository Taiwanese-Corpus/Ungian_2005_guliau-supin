Lau5 Tiong-kian Bok8-su ti7 Iu5-thai3 kok
Goan2 toa3 ti7 Ai-kip8 Khai-jiu5 (Cairo) e5 sou2-chai7 gou7 jit8 ku2. U7 peh ti7 Toa7 Kim- ji7-thah e5 boe2-liu, sit8-chai7 kan-khou2 peh, chong2-si7 kong-keng2 khoaN3 chin hng7. Ia7 u7 khi3 ti7 Phok-but8-koan2 khoaN3 chit kin-ni5 khui-tioh8 e5 Hoat-lo2 bong7 e5 po2-mih8 chin hi-han2; hit e5 kim-lui7 sng3 be7 liau2.    
Ti7 3 goeh8 16 ho7 che7 am3 chhia khi3 tang-pak, kau3 thiN-kng chiu7 kau3 ti7 Ka- sat (chiu7-si7 cha2 Hui-li7-su7 lang5 e5 siaN5), ia7 teng2-pou 10 tiam2 cheng chiuN7 Ia5-lou7- sat-leng2.    
In-ui7 hian7-si5 u7 chu7-tong7-chhia, chiu7 lu2-heng5 khah li7-pian7.  Thau5-chit8 jit8 goan2 u7 khoaN3 Hoe5-hoe5-kau3 e5 toa7 Le2-pai3- si7 (Mosque of Omar) chiu7-si7 ti eng2-koe3 Seng3 Tian7 e5 te7. Ia7 u7 khi3 ti7 Iu5-thai3 lang5 Ai-khau3-sou2, in iu5-goan5 teh khau3 pun2- kok sou2 sit-loh8 e5 eng5-kng, thiaN-liau2 sit8- chai7 kho2-lin5. Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 pi2 hai2-bin7 koan5 2000 chhioh, goan2 tui3 hia khi3 ti7 Si2 Hai2, pi2 hai2-bin7 ke7 1400 chhioh, li7 25 Eng-li2. U7 koe3 Ia5-li7-ko, ia7 khoaN3 Iok- tan3 ho5. Ti7 Si2 Hai2 se2-ek8; chhi3-khoaN3 tim5, be7 tim5, in-ui7 chui2 hiah-nih8 kiam5!    
Keh jit8 khi3 ti7 Pek-li7-heng5; ti7 lou7-nih8 khoaN3-kiN3 Liap8-kiat e5 bong7. Ti7 Pek-li7-heng5 u7 sou2 liah8 cho3 Chu2 chhut-si3 e5 be2- tiau5, m7-chai si7 a2 m7-si7.  Kho2-sioh hit sou2-chai7 tui3 ti7 Lo5-ma2 kau3 kap Hi-liap8-kau3 khian3-sng2 e5 chng-thaN7 chiu7 long2 sit- loh8 hit e5 pun2-lai5 e5 bin7-bok8.    
Tng2-lai5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 chiu7 khi3 Ka- na2 soaN, chia in-ui7 u7 che7-che7 kian3-tiok chiu7 oh-tit siong2-siong7 cha2-jit8 e5 chong7-thai3. Ia7 u7 khoaN3 Pit-su7-tai7 e5 ti5, kap hit e5 liah8-cho3 Chu2 gia5 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 lou7 (Via Dolorosa).  Le2-pai3-jit8 khi3 Seng3 Bong7 Pai3-tng5. Chit keng sui-jian5 si7 iN5-iN5 e5 chin toa7 keng, iau2-ku2 ti7 piN-a2 u7 hun kui2- na7 keng, hou7 Thian-chu2, Hi-liap8, Su7-li7-a, Ai-kip8 (Coptic) chiah e5 Ki-tok-kau3 siang7 chit8 si5 sui5-e5 cho3 ka-ti7 e5 le2-pai3-sek!! Chin kiau2-jiau2 be7-e7 chong3-giam5. Ia7 u7 Eng-kok e5 keng2-chhat teh siu2, kiaN-liau2 u7 saN-chiN !     
Li7-khui hia khi3 pak-si3. Ti7 e7-tau3-si5 keng-koe3 Sat-ma2-li7-a, ia7 u7 lim Nga2-kok e5 cheN2 e5 chui2 chin liang5. U7 oa2 Ki5-li2- sim kap I2-pat e5 soaN-kha, ia7 u7 khi3 Si7-kiam3 (Nablus, chit sou2-chai7 ti7 kin7-lai5 e5 te7-tang7 u7 toa7 sun2-hai7). Ti7-hia u7 kou2- cha2 Sat-ma2-li-a phai3 e5 lau7 che3-si, i u7 chiong kek8 cha2 Mou5 se Ngou2-keng e5 bun5 hou7 goan2 khoaN3. Koh koe3 soaN chiu7 keng koe3 Ia5-su-liat8 e5 peN5-iuN5.  Che si7 pui5-te7, cheng5 boe7 bat khoaN3 pui5-te7 ti7 Ka-lam5 ! Chiah kau3 Na2-sat-lek8; ti7 chia sou2 siu7 e5 kam2-chhiok oh-tit kong2. U7 thang khoaN3 Ma2-li7-a e5 cheN2, ia7 u7 peh hit peng5 e5 soaN. Ti7 hia khoaN3-kiN3 chheh-bin7 sou2 ki3 e5 kong-keng2 chiu7-si7 lan2 Seng3-to7 u7 chhut-mia5 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 pak-peng5 li7 60 Eng-li2 u7 Hek-bun5 soaN kham3-seh teh sih8.    
Li7 Na2-sat-lek8 koe3 Ka-na2 siaN5 chiu7 e7- pou 4 tiam2 kau3 Ka-li7-li7 Hai2 e5 The5-pi2-li7-a, chiu7-si7 ti7 hai2 e5 sai-lam5. Chit e5 te7-hng si7 chin ke7, pi2 hai2-bin7 ke7 682 chhioh. Ti7 Chu2 e5 si5 Hi-lut8 An-the5-phaN3 u7 i e5 ong5-kiong ti7 kin7-hu7 e5 soaN-piN; ia7 u7 chhut-mia5 e5 jiu5-ng5 choaN5-ti7-hia. Hian7-si5 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 u7 siat i-koan2 tu2 ti7 hai2-kiN5, goan2 u7 toa3 ti7 hia kap Song3 Kou-niu5  (Hazel chiu7-si7 He-su3) cho3-hoe2. Goan2 tui3 hia che7 chun5-a2 ti7 hai2-bin7 ia7 u7 khi3 Ka-pek-long5. Koh put-si5 teh khoaN3 hit e5 gek8-soaN Hek-bun5. Kho2-sioh si5- kan chin pek, chiu7 beh liam5-piN tng2-khi3 Te7-tiong-hai2 piN ai3 koe3 Le2-pai3-jit8 ti7 Ka- bit8 soaN. (Teng2-bin7 chiah-e5 si7 tui3 Lau5 Bok8-su e5 phoe ek8--e5.  Liam5 Bok8-su ia7 kap i khi3)  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 521 koan3,1928 ni5 8 geh8)
