
Tang-kang2 hoe2-chai
Chai7 ku7-lek8 chiaN-goeh8 16 me5, Tang-kang2 u7 sit-hoe2.  Teng2-tiong-ke sai peng5 bin7 hit choa7 long2 si7 chhau2-tiam3, sio chiong-kin7 tiam3-bin7 18 kham2 giah8.  Tak8 kham2 e5 tiam3-au7 u7 khi2 kui2-na7 keng khia7-ke.  Ke3-kiong7 sio chhit-peh chap8 hou7, oa2 kang2-kiN5 e5 sou2-chai7 u7 kau2 keng bo5 sio-tioh8 na7-tiaN7, chong2-si7 ia7 chin gui5-hiam2.  Hit e5 ian5-kou3 si7 tui3 chit8 lang5 nng7 saN jit8 cheng5 u7 be2 chiaN5 ban7 kin hoe-pou5, eng7 gu5-leng-te7 te2, khng3 ti7 chhau2-chhu3.  Chit nng7 jit8 u7 loh8-hou7, kaN2 si7 u7 phoah-tioh8 hoe-pou5, an2-ni hoat-hoe2 khi2-lai5.  Pak hong ia7 chin chhio, sou2-i2 chiah sio hiah kin2, chin oh phah, ia7 chin koaN5.  Kai-chai3 chit kui2 jit8 u7 loh8 tam7-poh8 hou7, chhu3-teng2 khah tam5, iu7-koh tui3-bin7 hit choa7 long2-si7 hia7 tiam3.  Siat-su2 m7-si7 an2-ni chiu7 e7 koh khah siong-tiong7.     
Siong7 kho2-lin5--e5, chiu7-si7 chit8 e5 lau7 hu7- jin5-lang5, 50 goa7 hoe3, hou7 sio si2.  Si7 in-ui7 i theh8 mih8 chhut-lai5, beh koh jip8-khi3 theh8, chu7 an2-ni be7 chhut-lai5; i e5 chhu3 kap khi2-hoe2 e5 chhu3 keh-piah.  Koh au7-boe2 bo5 lou7, tioh8 tui3 tiam3-khau2 chiah u7 lou7; an2-ni ti3-kau3 song3-sit siN3-mia7.  Che chhin-chhiuN7 Lo5-tek e5 bou2, it-tioh8 mih8 bo5 iau3-kin2 seN3-mia7.     
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 276 koan3, 1908 ni5 3 geh8)
