
 
Pai5-oan5-chok8 e5 ke3-chhoa7
Ng5 Sou3-ngou5
Pai5-oan5-chok8 e5 ke3-chhoa7 kap lan2 piN5-te7 u7 toa7 cheng-chha, thiaN-liau2 ia7 u7 kho2-chhu2, ia7 u7 chhu3-bi7, ia7 u7 kho2-lin5 e5 sou2-chai7.  Sou2-i2 it-hoat kam2-kak tioh8 thoan5-to7-li2, chiong Ki-tok e5 kng chhio7 in.  TaN ai3 kan2-tan lai5 kai3-siau7.
1.	Cho3-chhin.
Pai5-oan5-chok8 in e5 cho3-chhin, khi2-chhou bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 eng7 moai5-lang5 kai3-siau7.  Chiu7-si7 lam5 lu2 na7 tat8 kau3 kiat-hun e5 ni5-hoe3, lam5 chheng-lian5 tioh8 ai3 kau3 lu2 chheng-lian5 e5 sou2-chai7 siong5-siong5 khi3 thit-tho5, chhiuN3-koa, kong2-oe7 saN kau-poe5, u7 si5 ti7 chia, u7 si5 ti7 hia, au7-lai5 chiah chhoe7-chhut i3-khi3 saN ho5-hap8 e5 lang5 lai5 chin3-heng5 chhin-su7.  Che si7 in tui3 kou2-cha2 i2-lai5 e5 hong-siok8, si7 in cho3-chhin e5 khi2-thau5, bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 to7 ai3 an2-ni.  Chit khoan2 e5 kau-poe5, in e5 oe7 kio3-cho3.  Siat-su2 na7 u7 chheng-lian5 khah bo5 chhu3-bi7 ti7 chit-e5, chiu7 lau7-lang5 ai3 kong-kek, beh kong2 “Li2 bo5 beh khi3 sim2-mih8 lang5 beh phit-phoe3 li2.”  In lam5 lu2 e5 kau-poe5, sui-jian5 si7 tai7-ke e5 kong-jin7, iau2-ku2 si7 boe7 eng7-tit su-khia kau-poe5, tioh8 ai3 tiam3 ti7 ka-teng5 si7-toa7-lang5 e5 kam-tok e7 lai5 kong-jian5 kau-poe5 chiah oe7 eng7-tit.  Koh peng5-siong5 e5 le7, lu2 chheng-lian5 m7-bat khi3 chhoe7 lam5 chheng-lian5.
2.	Chhin-su7 e5 seng5-lip8. 
In lam5 lu2 an2-ni kau-poe5, na7 chhoe7-chhut u7 tau5-hap8--e5, lam5 chit peng5 chiu7 iong moai5-lang5 khi3 cho3-chhin.  Ia7 lu2 chit peng5, moai5-lang5 na7 lai5 kong2, chiah khui chhin-chok8 hoe7-gi7 lai5 chham-siong5. Siat-su2 chhin-chok8 tiong na7 u7 chit8 lang5 bo5 chan3-seng5, sui-si7 lam5 lu2 chheng-lian5 chin i3-ai3, ia7 si7 tioh8 soah--khi.  Na7 u7 long2 chan3-seng5, lam5 lu2 liong2-hong chiah teng7 ki5-jit8 lai5 chham-siong5 pheng3-le2.  (In teh cho3 pheng3-le2 e5 mih8, chiu7-si7 tiaN2, am-kng, ti-thau5, gu5-le5, kou2-chu (古珠), chiu2, ti, gu5, chiN5).  Pheng3-le2 na7 chham-siong5 ho2-se3, chiah koat-teng7 kiat-hun e5  
jit8.  (In khah-siong5 eng7 u7 goeh8 e5 jit8). Jit8 na7 teng7 liau2, ti7 chap8-goa7 jit8 cheng5 chiu7 cheng sek-a2 cho3 sek-a2 chiu2, kau3 si5 thang chhiaN2 lang5-kheh. Iu7-koh sio-siang5 ti7 hit e5 chap8-goa7 jit8 lai7, chheng-lian5 lam5 lu2, a2-si7 chhin-lang5 peng5-iu2, tak8 me5 tioh8 ai3 lai5 lu2-ka thiau3-bu2 chhiuN3-koa.  Na7-si7 thau5-bak8 tioh8 ai3 pak8 chin koan5 e5 chhian-chhiu hou7 sin-niu5 kap it-poaN e5 peng5-iu2 haiN3, tak8 am3 thit-tho5 kau3 chin am3. Che si7 in kiat-hun cheng5 e5 nau7-jiat8, ia7 si7 in sou2 tui3-tiong7 e5, na7 bo5 cho3 sek-a2 chiu2, bo5 thiau3-bu2 e5 kiat-hun, chiu7-si7 si7-toa7-lang5 bo5 chan3-seng5, ka-ki7 chu7-iu5 e5 kiat-hun.
3.	Kiat-hun-sek.
Kiat-hun-sek si7 ti7 lu2-ka lai5 ku2-heng5.  Kau3 hit e5 tong-jit8, sin-niu5, kiaN2-sai3 kap it-poaN e5 lang5 long2 seng7-chong kau3 ti7 chng-tiong hoe7-hap8 e5 kong-tiaN5, ti7 hia chhiuN3-koa, thiau3-bu2, chiah8 chiu2 lai5 nau7-jiat8.  Na7-si7 thau5-bak8 tioh8 chin-choe7 lang5 lai5 haiN3 chhian-chhiu.  An2-ni chiu7-si7 in e5 kiat-hun tian2-le2-sek, ia7 chit e5 tian2-le2-sek si7 tai7-khai3 tui3 e7-pou si5 e5 3, 4 tiam2 cheng khi2, kau3 hit am3 e5 poaN3-mi5 au7 chiah u7 soah, u7-si5 kau3 thiN-kng ia7 bat.  TaN sek na7 soah, kiaN2-sai3 e5 peng5-iu2 tioh8 lai5 aiN7 sin-niu5 khi3 kiaN2-sai3 e5 chhu3.  Kau3 hit si5 sin-niu5 kap i e5 chhin-lang5, peng5-iu2, tioh8 lau5 li7-piat8 e5 bak8-sai2, au7-lai5 chiah hou7 lang5 aiN7--khi3.  Chong2-si7 u7-si5 sek cho3 liau2-au7, sin-niu5 bo5 sui5-si5 khi3 kiaN2-sai3 e5 chhu3, chu7 an2-ni koh tng2-khi3 in tau keh kui2-na7 jit8 chiah khi3 e5, ia7 si7 u7.
I2-siong7 sou2 kong2 si7 it-poaN lang5 e5 kiat-hun-sek, chong2-si7 na7-si7 thau5-bak8 e5 kiat-hun chiu7 m7-si7 an2-ni na7-tiaN7.  U7-si5 beh chhoa7 chit8 e5 sin-niu5 lai5 kau3 chhu3, tioh8 ai3 chhoa7 kui2-na7 jit8 chiah oe7 kau3, che si7 chaiN2-iuN7?  Chiu7-si7 ti7 chit tiong-kan sin-niu5 ai3 beh tui3 kiaN2-sai3 lai5 tho2 chin-choe7 mih8, in teh kong2, Chhi7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 beh tui3 i lai5 tit-tioh8 mih8, chiu7-si7 chit e5 si5 na7-tiaN7, sou2-i2 chin7 sou2 oe7 chit e5 si5-kan tioh8 ka7 i ian5-chhian5 hou7 i ku2-ku2.  TaN in chit tiong-kan beh eng7 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 tit-tioh8 kiaN2-sai3 e5 mih8, chiu7-si7 sin-niu5 hou7 lang5 aiN7 kau3 tou5-tiong u7 chhiu7-a2 e5 sou2-chai7, i to7 tui3 chhiu7-a2-ki khiu2--leh, an2-ni chiu7-si7 i m7 kiaN5.  Hit-si5 kiaN2-sai3 chit peng5 to7 koh theh8 mih8-kiaN7 a2-si7 chiN5 khi3 ka7 i kou-chiaN5, kio3 i to7 pang3, thang koh kiaN5.  Chong2-si7 hit e5 mih8-kiaN7 hou7 i khoaN3 liau2 na7 bo5 boan2-chiok, tioh8 ai3 hou7 i kau3  
boan2-chiok chiah oe7 eng7-tit, na7 bo5, i to m7 kiaN5.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3, m7-si7 chit8 pai2 a2-si7 nng7 pai2 na7-tiaN7, si7 chiau3 sin-niu5 e5 sou2 cho3, beh kui2 pai2, long2 tioh8 chiau3 an2-ni.  Goa2 chhin-sin chai, chit8 e5 thau5-bak8 khi3 chhoa7 chit8 e5 thau5-bak8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, chhoa7 3 jit8 chiah kau3 chng-kha, tui3 chng-kha kau3 chhu3 30 hun e5 lou7 na7-tiaN7, chong2-si7 sin-niu5 m7-kiaN5, koh kou-chiaN5 chap8-goa7 pai2, chhian5 8 tiam2 goa7 cheng e5 si5-kan, ke eng7-liau2 20 goa7 te3 pou3, koh saN-kap lai5 chhoa7 e5 lang5 e5 chiN5 ia7 long2 kheng5 chhut-lai5 sio-thiN.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 hong-siok8, ku7-koan3 e5 lang5 liah8 cho3 chin the2-bian7, khoaN3 cho3 chin iau3-kin2.  Chong2-si7 kin7-lai5 khah u7 ti3-sek e5 lang5 ia7 kam2-kak an2-ni si7 m7-ho2, u7 chiam7-chiam7 teh kai2. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 789 ho7, 1954 ni5 9 geh8)
