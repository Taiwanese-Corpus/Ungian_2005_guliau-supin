I-hak8 Tai5-gi2 Gian2-tho2-hoe7

Babuza

Ku3-ni5 12-goeh8 18 kap 19 nng7 jit8, Ko-hiong5 I-hak8 Tai7-hak8 beh ki2-pan7 kho2-leng5 si7 Tai5-oan5 thau5-chit8-pai2 e5 I-hak8 Tai5-gi2 Gian2-tho2-hoe7 (GTH). Chit-e5 GTH si7 lan2 kap Ko-I hap8-chok e5 chok-phin2. Lan2 hu7-chek e5 pou7-hun7 si7 an-pai5 gi7-theng5, chhoe7 lun7-bun5 hoat-piau2-jin5 kap pou7-hun7 e5 heng5-cheng3 su7-kang. Ki5-tha e5 Ko-i chhu2-li2. TeN7 Si-chong i-su kap goa2 long2 u7 hoat-piau2 lun7-bun5. Li2 Khin5-hoaN7 kau3-su7 kap Chiu2 Ui5-bun5 kau3-su7 ma7 i2 Gi2-gian5-hak8-ka e5 sin-hun7 the5-chhut in tui3 tai7-hak8 e5 Tai5-gi2-bun5 kau3-iok8 e5 keng-giam7 kap the5-gi7. Lan2 koe3-khi3 phah-piaN3 10 goa7 tang chong2-si7 koh u7 sin e5 seng5-chiu7 chhut--lai5 lah. Che chin thang hou7 lan2 kam2-kak hoaN-hi2 kap an-ui3! Hi-bang7 au7-bin7 koh u7 chin-che7  hak8-hau7 kap thoan5-the2 ke3-siok8 cho3--loh8--khi3. Lan2 koh ke3-siok8 piaN3!
