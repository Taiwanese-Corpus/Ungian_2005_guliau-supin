Hou7 A-ma2 e5 phoe

A-ma2, siu-phoe peng5-an,
Kiong-hi2, “Sin-ni5 khoai3-lok8”, choe3-kin7 goa2 bo5-eng5 teh hoan-sia2 “Tai5-oan5 Chok8-kun5 Koan-he7” chit-pun2-chheh e5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 pan2.  Te7-it-pai2 chham-u2 hoan-sia2 e5 su7-kang, chiah-chai choe7-choe7 chhui-tong7 Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 e5 chian5-poe3, in sou2 hu3-chhut e5 sin-khou2, si7 boe7-oe7 heng5-iong5--e5!  Goa2 u7 chit8-e5 lau7-su kong2, na7-si7 iong7 ni5-hoe3 lai5 sng3-siau3, an2-ne li2 e5 Tai5-gi2 chiu7-si7 96 hun loh!  Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, siuN2-su3 goan2 chit8-e5 ho2 a-ma2, e7-tit-thang ka3 goan2 chiah-e5 si7-se3 hak8-sip8 I e5 kha-jiah.
U7 chit8-e5 kou3-su7 kong2, ti7 15 se3-ki2 Tek-kok e5 Niu2-lun5-po2(Nuremberg), u7 2-hiaN-ti7, toa7-hiaN kio3-cho3 A-pek Khe3-tek8 (亞伯契特), sio2-ti7 si7 Gai7-pek-tek8 (艾伯特), 18 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 tiong-kan, in si7 lau2-toa7 kap lau2-ji7.  Nng7-hiaN-ti7 chu7 se3-han3 chiu7 chin u7 ge7-sut8 e5 thian-hu3, iah8 long2 lip8-chi3 toa7-han3 liau2-au7 beh thak8 “Niu2-lun5-po2 Ge7-sut8 hak8-iN7”.  M7-ku2 chhu3--nih8 chin song3-hiong, bo5 hoat-tou7 sun7-than3 in-e5 i3, nng7-hiaN-ti7 chiu7 chham-siong5 iong7 tan3 gin5-kak-a2 koat-teng7, iaN5-e5 lang5 thak8 tai7-hak8, su-e5 lang5 loh8 khong3-khiN cho3 khou2-kang than3-chiN5 chhi7-ke, kiong3-eng3 hak8-hui3.  Kiat-ko2, A-pek Khe3-tek8 hiaN5 tit-tioh8 thak8-chheh e5 ki-hoe7, Gai7-pek-tek8 chi2 u7 loh8 khong3-khiN cho3 khou2-kang. 
Si5-kan koe3 liau2 chin kin2, 4 ni5 au7, A-pek Khe3-tek8 hak8-seng5 hoe5-hiong, hiong-chhin 






                                             tek8-piat8 the3 i pan7 chit8-tiuN5 kheng3-chiok-hoe7, u7 choe7-choe7 lang5 siu7 iau-chhiaN2, Gai7-pek-tek8 iah8-i7 ki5-tiong e5 chit8-e5.  Ti3-sia7-su5 e5 si5, A-pek Khe3-tek8 kam2-sia7 hiong-chhin kap sia7-hoe7 jin5-su7 e5 chai-poe5, choe3-au7, i iah8 kam2-sia7 i e5 hiaN-ti7 Gai7-pek-tek8. I kong2, “goa2 beh hian3-chiuN7 goa2 chi3-toa7 e5 kam2-sia7 hou7 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 Gai7-pek-tek8, chun-chiau3 goan2 e5 iok-sok, 4 ni5 lai5, goa2 khi3 thak8-chheh, i loh8 khong3-khiN cho3 khou2-kang chhi7-ke koh chai3 pang-chan7 goa2 thak8-chheh.  TaN goa2 e5 sim-goan7 oan5-seng5 lah!  Hian7-chhu2-si5 eng-kai si7 goan2 nng7-lang5 kak-sek hou7-siong kau-oaN7 e5 si5-chun7.” Sio2-ti7 Gai7-pek-tek8 in3-kong2, “siuN ban7 lah!  4 ni5 lai5 goa2 e5 kha-chhiu2 m7-chai at-chih8 loa7-choe7-pai2, hian7-chhu2-si5 e5 goa2 i2-keng si7 kha-chhiu2 ban7-tun7, bo5 hoat-tou7 koh kiah8-pit oe7-tou5 loh! It-chhe3 long2 siuN ban7--lah!” Toa7-hiaN thiaN-liau2 hui-siong5 siong-sim.
Au7-lai5 toa7-hiaN A-pek Khe3-tek8 (亞伯契特) tui3 sio2-ti7 hit-siang chhou-pe5-pe5 e5 chhiu2 tit-tioh8 leng5-kam2, iong7-sim the2-hoe7 hoe7-seng5 chit8-siang “Ki5-to2 e5 chhiu2” e5 tou5, chiaN5-cho3 siau3-liam7 i-e5 hiaN-ti7 ui7 i sou2 hu3-chhut e5 it-chhe3.
Chit8-siang “Ki5-to2 e5 chhiu2” ti7 hian7-chhu2-si5 se3-kai3 kok-te7 hui-siong5 chhut-mia5 ji5-chhiaN2 thong-lang5-chai.  Tui3 15 se3-ki2 liu5-thoan5 kau3 taN, i bo5 in-ui7 si5-kan lai5 the3-sek, hoan2-tng2 si7 chit-siang chhou-pe5-pe5 e5 chhiu2, hou7 lan2 u7 giong2-bong7, hou7 lan2 e5 ng3-bang7 sio-lo7, siang7-si5 iah8 hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 bo5-han7 e5 khui3-lat8.  A-ma2 li2 kong2 si7-m7-si7 neh!
Ia5-ho5-hoa-i2-lek8!
Eng-lam5 2004.1.1.
