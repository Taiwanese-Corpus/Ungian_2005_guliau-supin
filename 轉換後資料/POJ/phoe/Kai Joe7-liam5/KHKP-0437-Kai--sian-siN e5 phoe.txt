Kai--sian-siN e5 phoe


Goa2 khi3 tham3 hoe7-iu2 tu2-tioh8 i e5 gin2-na2 phoa3-piN7, goa2 ka7 i kong2 u7 gui5-hiam2 e5 hoan5-lo2 tioh8 chiu7 i-seng, i kong2, KaN2 m7 bian2, che si7 ThiN-pe7 e5 chi2-i3, u7 mia7 m7 ui3 piN7.  Koh saN le2-pai3 si2; i ia7 sin3 chu3-si2. Koh u7 chit8 e5 hoe7-iu2 ka7 goa2 kong2 i u7 gu5 si2, tui3 koaiN5 soaN u7 chit8 te3 chioh8 iok nng7 chheng-kun lak-loh8-lai5 gu5-tiau5, gu5 hou7 i kham3-si2.  Goa2 mng7 i2-cheng5 bat lak mah ? I kong2 siong5-siong5 u7, chit8 pai2 thiaN-kiN3 hu7-hu7 hau2 chiu7 kin2 chau2, u7 chit8 te3 kui2-na7 pah-kun lak-loh8-lai5, bo5 chau2 tek-khak si2; iah8 siong5-siong5 lak-loh8-lai5 ti-tiau5, tiu7-tiaN5.  Goa2 ka7 i kong2 hiah gui5-hiam2 tioh8 soa2 ui7 m7 thang tiam3 hia.  I kong2, He ia7 u7 iau3-kin2 ! SiN si2 si7 ThiN-pe7 chu3 ho2-ho2, goa2 kong2 ThiN-pe7 kam2 u7 chu3-tiaN7 li2 m7 thang poaN-soa2 ? Siong7-te3 hou3-seng chi-tek si7 ai3 lang5 oah8, kiam2 u7 ka7 lang5 chu3-tiaN7 tioh8 chu7-chin7 mah ?  Siong7-te3 ai3 ho2 oan3 phaiN2, hiah e5 poah8-kiau2, chiah8 a-phian3, choe3 chhat8, thai5 lang5, kiam2 si7 Siong7-te3 ka7 i chu3-tiaN7 mah ?  
Se3-siok8 lang5 kong2 thiNchu3-tiaN7 si7 houN3, jin7 thiN-nih8 u7 chit8 e5 ban7-koan5 e5 Chu2. Hoe7-iu2 koh khah chai thiN chu3-tiaN7. Chong2-si7 m7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 se3-siok8 lang5 e5 sin3-giong2.  u7 piN7 tioh8 chiah8 ioh8, gui5-hiam2 tioh8 siam2-pi7, m7 eng-kai chiu7 m7 thang phe-si2.  Lang5 na7 jiat8-sim sin3 Chu2 siong5-siong5 ki5-to2, siat-su2 sou2 oe7 tu2-tioh8 e5 hai7 ThiN-pe7 ia7 oe7 kiu3 i, thai2 thang chip-i3 si7 chu3-tiaN7 ? 
Hun e5 Hai7
 Tan5 Lok8  
Lang5 e5 sin-the2 u7 saN hang7 e5 iau3-sou3, kio3-cho3 cheng-sin5, leng5-hun5, jiok8-the2. Chit saN hang7 u7 bit8-chhiat e5 koan-he7, sou2-i2 kin7-lai5 e5 chong-kau3-ka toa7-toa7 teh chu3-i3.  Siat-su2 lan2 lang5 e5 sin-the2 na7 u7 chit8 pou7-hun7 siu7 siong, chiu7 cheng-sin5 ia7 be7 bian2-tit siu7 kam2-eng3.  Chhin-chhiuN7  lang5 na7 u7 ui7-piN7, chiu7 hit e5 lang5 chin khoai3 siu7-khi3, sim-chi3 ia7 loan2-jiok8. Cheng-sin5 na7 ia3-sian7, chiu7 sin-the2 ia7 ia3-sian7.  Na7 ai3 chai lang5 e5 sin-the2 u7 kian7-khong a2-bo5, tioh8 khoaN3 i e5 ui7-chong7 e5 kiong5-jiok8; ui7 na7 lam2 e5 lang5, i e5 cheng-sin5 ut-chut bo5 song2-khoai3.  Sin-the2 na7 bo5 kian7-khong, ia7 u7 koan-he7 ti7 leng5-hun5; sou2-i2 na7 lang5 tu2-tioh8 kan-khou2 phoa3-piN7, chiu7 i e5 sin3-giong2 oe7 io5-tang7.  Hian5-jin5 kong2, Kian7-khong e5 cheng-sin5, tiam3 ti7 kian7-khong e5 sin-the2; chit ku3 oe7 sit8-chai7 u7 iaN2 lah.  
Tui3 Au-chiu chian3-cheng peng5-ho5 i2-lai5, kok-kok u7 kian3-sek e5 lang5, the5-khi2 kim3 chiu2, kim3 hun, cho3 hian7-si5 te7-it iau3-kin2 e5 bun7-te5.  Hian7-si5 Bi2-kok che3-chiu2 e5 su7-giap8, long2-chong2 kim3-chi2. Kim3-leng7 chit8-e7 chhut, peh-siN3 m7 kaN2 hoan2-tui3, choe7-choe7 lang5 long2 chan3-seng5.  Chong2-si7 tiong-kan u7 kan-khiau2 e5 seng-li2 lang5, ai3 chiong cho3 chiu2 e5 ki-khi3, theh8 lai5 Tiong-kok khai-giap8.  Ho2 kai-chai3 Bi2-kok e5 soan-kau3-su chin7 lat8 kap in khong3-gi7, kau3 lou7-boe2 bo5 chiaN5. Lou7-kok ia7 m7 chun2 chheng-lian5-lang5 chiah8 hun, chiah8 chiu2.  Chiu2 e5 hai7 si7 cheng3 lang5 long2 chai, m7 bian2 goa2 ke kong2.  TaN goa2 sou2 ai3 kong2-e5, chiu7-si7 hun e5 hai7, kap lan2 u7 sim2-mih8 koan-he7 ? Ai3 in2-khi2 chhut-mia5 e5 i-seng sou2 cheng3-beng5 e5 su7-sit8, ki3 ti7 e7-te2 lai5 hou7 liat8-ui7 cho3 chham-kho2.  
Tian5-goan5 Phok-su7 kong2, Phou2-thong e5 kng2-hun-chhau2, chit8 ki e5 lai7-bin7, u7 chit8 tih e5 nicotine (hun-chhau2 e5 tok8-sou3). I-tin5 Hak8-su7 kong2, eng7 chit8 tih e5  nicotine, saN hun kan e7 hai2-si2 chit8 chiah kau2.  Lou7-kok u7 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 e5 phok-su7 kong2, chiong chit8 chiah thou3-a2 koaiN ti7 tu5-nih8. tak8 jit8 pun5 hun-ian ti7 thou3-a2 e5 seng-khu, m7 bian2 koe3 nng7 goeh8-jit8 chiu7 e7 si2.  Au7-lai5 phoa3 pak siong5-se3 giam7, chiah chai si7 nicotine thau3-jip8 phoe5-hu, chhim-jip8 hiat-ek8, hai7-tioh8 sim-chong7.  Siong5-kang Phok-su7 kong2, Hun-chhau2 kang-tiuN5 cho3 kang e5 hu7-jin5 lang5, pun2-sin sui-jian5 bo5 chiah8 hun, chong2-si7 liu5-san2 chin-choe7.  Siat-su2 na7 tit-tioh8 an-choan5 seng-san2--e5, i e5 kiaN2-ji5 ia7 choe7-choe7 cha2 si2.  Hi3-lo5-piN7, ui7-jiok8-piN7 e5 goan5-in, choe7-choe7 si7 tui3 pun2-sin kap si7-toa7 lang5 chiah8 hun, chiah8 chiu2 e5 kiat-ko2.  
Hak8-seng chiah8 hun te7-it hai7 nau2-lek8, koh i e5 sin-the2 pi2 bo5 chiah8 hun e5 hak8-seng peng5-kin khah khin.  U7 chit8 keng Tai7-hak8 e5 kau3-su, po3-ko3 hak8-seng jip8 hak8 hit-si5, chhi3-giam7 e5 seng5-chek kong2,  Chiah8 hun-e5 peng5-kin pi2 bo5 chiah8-e5 peng5-kin si7 khah chio2 tiam2. Tui3 an2-ni lai5 khoaN3, hun e5 hai7 m7-na7 hai7 lang5 e5 sin-the2, ia7 cho2-gai7 lan2 lang5 ti3-sek e5 hoat-tat8.  
Chiah8 hun e5 khi2-thau5 si7 tui3 ia2-ban5 lang5, au7-lai5 bun5-beng5 lang5 ia7 oh8 chiah8, chiu7 chu7-chok chhong-beng5, ke kang che3-cho7, cheng2-lui7 na2 chhut na2 che7. TaN chiaN5-cho3 hong-siok8, chiu7 tai7-ke saN khoaN3 ai3 oh8 chiah8, na2 be7 chiu7 na2 u7 siau, cheng3-hu2 than3 chiN5 jit8-jit8 choe7, peh-siN3 liau2 chiN5 jit8-jit8 san3.  Hian7-si5 lan2 pun2 kok e5 peh-siN3 tak8 ni5 lap8 cheng3-hu2 kan-ta hun hui3, iok u7 1,0000,0000 khou3; tui3 goa7-kok lai5-e5 bo5 sng3 chai7-lai7.  Chiah8 hun e5 lang5 chit8 ni5, chit8 ni5 choe7; na7 khoaN3 kin7-lai5 lo5-tong7 sia7-hoe7 e5 chheng-lian5, bo5 chiah8 hun e5 lang5 chin chio2, chiu7 shai-iaN2. 
 Chit ho7 ok-siok8 m7 chai kau3 sim2-mih8 si5 chiah oe7 hoe3-bo5 ? Chiah8 hun u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek leh ? U7 lang5 kong2, Chiah8 hun ke2 iu-bun7; koh u7 lang5 kong2, Chiah8 hun tu5 tok8 thang5.  Che sit8-chai7 si7 chho3-gou7 e5 su-siong2, m7 chai chiah8 e5 lang5 i e5 kian7-khong e5 sin-the2 beh chiam7-chiam7 piN3-cho3 soe-jiok8.  
Chhi3-siuN7 Hau3-keng kong2, Tui3 pe7-bo2 sou2 seng5-siu7 e5 sin-the2 na7 m7 kaN2 ka7 i siong-hai7, chiah si7 iu2-hau3.  Iu7-koh Seng3-keng kong2, Lin2 e5 sin-the2 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 tian7. Kho2-kian3 lang5 bo5 ian5-kou3 hai7 ka-ki7 e5 seng-khu, che m7-na7 cho3 put-hau3 e5 kiaN2, ia7 si7 cho3 poe7-gek8 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5.  
Lan2 e5 Chu2 kong2, Lin2 si7 se3-kan e5 kng, te7-chiuN7 e5 iam5; kng kap iam5 long2 si7 lang5 sou2 toa7 iau3-eng7 e5 mih8.  Chiu7-si7 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5--e5 tioh8 tam-tng kiu3 lang5 e5 leng5-hun5, kap kai2-liong5  sia7-hoe7  e5 chek-seng5.  Kin-a2-jit8 sia7-hoe7 cheng2-cheng2 m7 ho2 e5 sip8-koan3, oh-tit thang kong2 kau3 chin7.  Chhin-chhiuN7  chiah8 hun e5 phaiN2 sip8-koan3, bo5 koh-iuN7 chiong Siong7-te3 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5 chiN5-chai5 hiat-loh8 hoe2-kheN piN3 chiaN5 ian-bu7.  M7-na7 hai7 ka-ti7 e5 sin-the2, ia7 u7 koan-he ti7 to7-tek-chiuN7, sit8-chai7 si7 kho2-lin5 ah !  
Kiu3-se3-kun m7 chun2 chiah8 hun e5 lang5 jip8 kau3-hoe7.  Na7 u7 chiah8 hun e5 lang5 ai3 jip8 kau3-hoe7 hit-si5, tioh8 long2 pang3-sak. Che sit8-chai7 chin ho2 e5 hong-hoat, hou7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 thang cho3 bou5-iuN7. Hian7-si5 Tai5-oan5 Lam5-pou7 e5 hoe7-iu2 e5 tiong-kan, goa2 phah-sng3 chi3-chio2 u7 500 lang5 chiah8 hun, na7 mui2 lang5 peng5-kin chit8 ni5 cho3 6 khou lai5 ke3-sng3, chit8 ni5 kan chong2-ke3 3000 khou.  Na7 chiong chiah e5 gin5 lai5 pou2-chou7 kau3-hoe7, chiu7 bian2 hoan5-lo2 chai5-cheng3 khoat-hoat8, Chu2 e5 to7-li2 oh khui-khoah lah !  
TaN lan2 oa2-kho3 Chu2, bo5 hou7 but8-iok8 pe2-pak8 m7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 se3-siok8 lang5 cho3 mih8 e5 lou5-chai5.  Lang5 na7 ko2-jian5 sin3 Chu2, ke2 hun si7 bo5 oh e5 li2 lah.  Ki-tok-tou5 tioh8 cho3 hong-hiam2 tiong e5 chioh8-thiau7, m7 thang than3 se3-siok8 teh phu5-tiam5.  Ia7 tioh8 cho3 oah8-hi5, m7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 si2-hi5 toe3 chui2 teh phiau-liu5.  
Ng3-bang7 cheng3 kau3-hoe7 e5 jim7-chit-chia2 kam-goan7 khoaN3 hun cho3 chit8 khoan2 e5 tui3-tek8, sit8-heng5 ke2-tu5, thang cho3 hoe7-iu2 e5 bou5-iuN7.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, Chui2-thau5 chheng, chui2-boe2 chiu7 chheng. 
Kai Sian-siN e5 phoe
Tam7-chui2 tiong Hak8-hau7 liat8-ui7 seng-tou5 peng5-an !  
Tui3 goan2 kap lin2 saN li7-khui kau3 chit-tiap8, ti7 lou7-tiong u7 tit-tioh8 peng5-an.  Chui2-lou7 chin piN5-thaN2.  Sui-bong2 ti7 toa7-iuN5 thiN-khi3 put-chi2 leng2, siong5-siong5 u7 loh8 seh, iau2-ku2 hong-eng2 put-chi2 tiam7-cheng7.  
Tui3 HoaiN5-pin kau3 Ka-na2-tai7 tah chun5 15 jit8 ku2, ti7 lou7-tiong bo5 sim2-mih8 thang khoaN3, chi2-u7 kui2-na7 be2 keng-hi5 na7-tiaN7. Chit8 jit8 u7 chit8 be2 put-chi2 toa7 be2, tai7-iok u7 si3 tng7 tng5, phu5 oa2 ti7 chun5-nih8.  I e5 thau5-khak chha-put-to u7 chit8 tng7 khoah.  
Kau3 Vancouver, u7 kau3-hoe7 kap peng5-iu2 lai5 tan2-chih goan2. Goan2 ti7 hia 2 jit8 ku2, toa3 ti7 cha2-ni5 thong se3-kai3 Marathon soan2-chhiu2 e5 lang5 e5 tau. Hit e5 soan2-chhiu2 si7 mia5 kio3 Dick Grant. I soe3-han3 e5 si5 na7 tah he2-chhia, a2-si7 che7 be2-chhia chiu7 hin5; sou2-i2 kou-put-chiong tioh8 lian7-sip8 chau2.  Tui3 an2-ni i chiaN5 choe3 se3-kai3 e5  soan2-chhiu2.  
Li7-khui Vancouver goan2 koh che7 he2-chhia, keng-ke3 Lok8-ki soaN lai5 kau3 chit e5 sou2-chai7. Ka-na2-tai7 e5 he2-chhia put-chi2 toa7 tiuN. Mi5-si5 u7 pi7-pan7 bin5-chhng5, jit8-si5 u7 chau3-kha thang hou7 lang5 chu2-chiah8. An2-ni sou2-hui3 khah seng2. Na7-si7 ti7 chhia-lai7 sit8-tong5 chiah8, tak8 hang7 chin kui3. Koe-nng7 1 liap8 4 kak gun5, keng-chio chit8 koeh 6 kak gun5, kam-a2 chit8 liap8 7 kak. Na7-si7 ti7 chhia-lai7 u7 thih-chau3, ka-ki7 pi7-pan7 chiu7 chin seng2. Ti7 chhia-lai7 goan2 tak8 tng3 u7 chu2-png7, put-chi2 li7-pian7 kap sim-sek.  Pat8 e5 tah-kheh put-chi2 piaN3-se3, long2 si7 ko-teng2 e5 chong2-phou3, chin gau5 chu2.  Keng-ke3 Lok8-ki soaN, keng2-ti3 kau3-kek8 sui2. SoaN-teng2 long2 si7 seh, soaN-kha si7 chhiu7-na5 kap chhan5-hng5. Na7-si7 koan5 e5 soaN long2 si7 chit8 ban7 chhioh  
i2-siong7. Lok8-ki soaN ke3 liau2 (2 jit8, 1 mi5) chiu7 keng-ke3 chit8 e5 toa7 piN5-iuN5. Chit e5 sou2-chai7 chhut beh8 kap gu5, be2 chin choe7.  
Hian7-chai7 goan2 tiam3 chit e5 sou2-chai7 si7 ti7 chng-nih8, khah koan5 hai2 1800 chhioh. ThiN-khi3 ti7 chia put-chi2 leng2; cha7-hng kap kin-a2-jit8 u7 loh8 seh; chui2 ti7 goa7-bin7 u7 kian peng poaN3-chhun3 kau7. Na7-si7 lin2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 teh iat8-siN3 chin joah8.  Ti7 chit chng-nih8 sou2-chai7 e5 peh-siN3 put-chi2 khai-hoa3 kap bun5-beng5. Choh-sit lang5 khah choe7 ka-ki7 u7 chu7-tong7-chhia. Chhu3-lai7 ke-ke hou7-hou7 ia7 u7 tian7-oe7.  
Teng2 le2-pai3 goan2 ti7 chit8 keng chng-nih8 e5 kong-hak8-hau7 choe3 le2-pai3. Chu2-li2 le2-pai3--e5, si7 chit8 e5 choh-sit lang5; jih8-khim5--e5, si7 tai7-hak8 e5 chut-giap8-seng. U7 6 tiuN chu7-tong7-chhia kap 2 tiuN be2-chhia chai3 chiah e5 choh-sit lang5 khi3 le2-pai3.  Chhia long2 si7 in ka-ki7 e5.  Hian7-chai7 goan2 toa3 e5 sou2-chai7 li7 chit e5 sio2 koe-chhi7 phou3 poaN3 lou7.  Chit e5 koe-chhi7 chi2-u7 700 lang5. Iau2-ku2 sou2-chai7 put-chi2 hoat-tat8.  U7 3 e5 i-seng, 3 e5 pian7-hou7-su7, 4 keng le2-pai3-tng5, chit8 keng lu2-koan2 3 chan3 lau5, chit8 keng sio2-hak8-hau7, chit8 keng tiong-hak8-hau7, ia7 chit8 kham2 tiam3 kap Hu2-cheng5-koe siong7 toa7 kham2-e5 piN5 toa7. 
 Cha7-hng koe-nih8 e5 lang5 u7 chhiaN2 goan2 khi3 ian2-soat. Goa2 u7 kong2-khi2 Tam7-chui2 Tiong Hak8-hau7 e5 su7 hou7 in thiaN. In put-chi2 ai3 chai lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 tai7-chi3. 
Koh nng7 le2-pai3 goan2 beh li7-khui chia khi3 To-lun5-to siaN5. To-lun5-to si7 toa7 sou2-chai7 u7 50 goa7 ban7 lang5. Tui3 chia kau3 hia, che7 he2-chhia tioh8 3 jit8 ku2. N`g-bang7 ti7 hia tit-tioh8 ho2 ki-hoe7 thang po3 in chai lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 siau-sit.  
Chit tiuN phoe kau3 ui7 e5 si5 lin2 i2-keng teh chhi3-giam7. Bang7 lin2 tai7-ke sio2-sim chiau3-kou3 lin2 e5 sin-the2. Ia7 siong5-siong5 ti3-i3 po2-siu2 lin2 e5 phin2-heng7. Goan7 lan2 e5 ThiN-pe7 jit8-jit8 chiau3-kou3 pi3-iu7 lin2.
