
      
A-ma2 peng5-an,
Li2 kin-ni5 eng-kai si7 95 hoe3 lah houN ! Teng2 chit8-hong phoe, m7-chai goa2 sia2-liau2 u7 ho2-se3 bo5? Iau2-ku2 chin ki5-thai7 li2 chit-ui7 Tai5-gu2 Lo5-ma2-ji7 e5 ko-chhiu2, pang3 chit8-koa2-a2 phiat-pou7 hou7 goa2, m7-thang am3-kham3 ou5 ! Sui-bong2 Koh-oah8-chiat i2-keng koe3-liau2 chin ku2, m7-ku2 chit-e5 kou3-su7 hou7 goa2 siu7 chin toa7 e5 kek-le7, goa2 tek-khak tioh8 ai3 kap li2 saN-kap hun-hiong2.
U7 chit8-jit8, Bi2-kok e5 kho-hak8-ka 
Ai3-tek8-seng (愛迪生), i e5 sit8-giam7-sek hoe2-sio, sou2 u7 gian2-kiu3 e5 chu-liau7 long2 ou-iu2 khi3. U7 chit8-e5 ki3-chia2 lai5 hong2-mng7--i, ki3-chia2 an2-ni mng7, “Li2 e5 sit8-giam7-sek hoe2-sio, li2 u7 sim2-mih8 kam2-siong2?”  Ai3-tek8-seng hoe5-tap kong2, “Goa2 hui-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.”, “Siong7-te3 ia7 bo5 po2-pi3 li2, li2 kam2-sia7 sim2-mih8?” (tap) “Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 ka7 goa2 chho3-ngou7 e5 gian2-kiu3 long2 sio-tiau7, goa2 kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 goa2 nai7-sim, sin3-sim kap ti3-hui7, ke3-siok8 gian2-kiu3, e7-tit-thang hou7 koh-khah choe7 e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 ho2-chhu3.” Ki3-chia2 koh mng7, “Li2 e5 sit8-giam7-sek hoe2-sio, li2 e5 hiN7-khang ia7 chha-put-to beh chhau3-hiN7-lang5 lah, mih-sai2 (乜使) koh kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3?”. “Che si7 goa2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 e5 te7-ji7 e5 goan5-in. Chhau3-hiN7-lang5 hou7 goa2 thiaN boe7 tioh8 o-lo2, goa2 chiah boe7 kiau-ngou7.  ThiaN boe7 tioh8 phoe-pheng5, bian2-tit hou7 goa2 sit-bong7.” Hit-ui7 ki3-chia2 thiaN-liau2 chin ia3, kong2, “Lan2 mai3 koh kong2 Siong7-te3, lai5 kong2 li2 hoat-beng5 joa7-che7 mih8-kiaN7”.  Ai3-tek8-seng hoe5-tap kong2, “Goa2 bo5 hoat-beng5 mih8-kiaN7.” Ki3-chia2 kong2, bo5 an3-sng3 Ki-tok-tou5 ia7 oe7 kong2 peh8-chhat8, li2 beng5-beng5 hoat-beng5 chin-che7 mih8-kiaN7, khiok kong2 bo5, phi3-ju7 tian7-hoe2”. Ai3-tek8-seng kong2, “Tian7-hoe2 siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 seng5-hun7 si7 tian7, tian7 si7 Siong7-te3 chhong3-cho7--e5, goa2 chi2-put-ko3 si7 hoat-hian7 tian7, ka7 tian7 chhoa7-jip8 po-le5 lai7-bin7, hou7 i hoat-kng. Sou2-i2 goa2 m7-si7 hoat-beng5, chi2 si7 hoat-hian7 na7-tiaN7”. “Na7 chiau3 li2 an2-ni kong2, chhiaN2-mng7 li2 it-seng tiong choe3 ui2-tai7 e5 hoat-hian7 si7 sim2-mih8?” Ai3-tek8-seng kong2, “chiu7 si7 Siong7-te3 thiaN3 se3-kan-lang5, Siong7-te3 chioh Ia5-sou e5 kang3-seng, siu7-khou2 kap koh-oah8, ka7 I e5 thiaN3 hian2-beng5--chhut--lai5”Sui-bong2 sit8-giam7-sek hoe2-sio, koh chhau3-hiN7-lang5, Ai3-tek8-seng iu5-goan5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, chit-chiong2 sun7-hok8 e5 cheng-sin5, chiu7 si7 ka7 Ia5-sou koh-oah8 e5 keng-giam7 ti7 i e5 seng-oah8 tiong hian2-beng5--chhut--lai5. Kou3-su7 sia2 kau3 chia, m7-chai A-ma2 si7-m7-si7 i2-keng chai-iaN2 Siong7-te3 e5 khe2-si7 si7 sim2-mih8?  A2-si7 i2-keng nia2-siu7 Siong7-te3 hong-hu3 e5 un-tian2 neh! I2-keng am3 lah, goan7 Siong7-te3 su3-hok hou7 li2 bo5 soah.
