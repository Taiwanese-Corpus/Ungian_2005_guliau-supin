
Bun5 Kou-niu5 e5 Phoe
Eng7 phoe kia3 hou7 liat8-ui7 e5 cheng3 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 siu-chai.  Goa2 li7-khui Tai5-oan5 e5 si5, chiu7 long2 bo5 phah-sng3 goa2 be7-tit thang koh koe3-khi3 kap lin2 tai7-ke sio-kiN3.  ThiaN-tioh8 Eng-kok e5 Kong-hoe7 sou2 gi7-tiaN7--e5, goa2 e5 sim tong3-be7-tiau5, chin m7-kam; in-ui7 oa2 si3-chap8 ni5 ku2, goa2 toa3 ti7 lin2 e5 tiong-kan, kap lin2 saN chhut-jip8 lai5 choe3 Chu2 e5 kang. soa3 khoaN3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 tui3 tang, sai, lam5, pak e5 Seng3-hoe7 na2 khui-khoah, ia7 u7 na2 che7 lang5 kui-hok8 lai5 sin3 Ia5-sou.    
TaN tui3 ti7 sou2 lek8-koe3 e5 su7, goa2 e5 sim koh khah tek8-piat8 e5 i3-su3 kap lin2 saN kiat-lian5, ia7 m7-kaN2 be7-ki3-tit lin2 siong5-si5 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7, chhin-bit8 e5 thiaN3-thang3, che sit8-chai7 kau3 eng2-oan2 choe3 ki3-liam7.    
Sou2 thiaN3 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2, goa2 tu2-tioh8 chit-ho7 e5 chou2-gai7, m7-kaN2 hiam5, chiu7 chai si7 Siong7-te3 e5 ho2 chi2-i3, ia7 e7 choe3 lan2 e5 iu2-ek.  Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, " KiN3-na7 keng2-kai3 e5 su7, ti7 tng-si5 chiau3-khoaN3 m7-si7 hoaN-hi2, si7 iu-bun7, na7-si7 *au7--lai5, chiu7 ti7 hiah e5 tui3 keng2-kai3 lai5 lian7-tat8 e5 lang5, kiat peng5-an e5 koe2-chi2 " ( Hi-pek-lai5 12:11 ).    
Sui-jian5 ti7 chit se3-kan be7-e7 koh sio-kiN3, iau2-ku2 m7-thang hoan5-lo2 kau3 siuN koe3-thau5.  Lan2 na7 choe3 Ia5-sou tiong-sin3 e5 lou5-pok8, chiu7 un2-tang3 ti7 Thian-tong5 e7 koh saN-kiN3, soa3 hiong2-siu7 eng2-oan2 e5 hok-khi3 ti7 Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5.    
Chiau3-khoaN3, chit se3-kai3 e5 lou7-boe2 si7 na2 chhin-kin7. Ti7 Khe2-si7-liok8 6:4 u7 chit8 ku3 kong2, "Tui3 te7-chiuN7 chhiuN2-toat8 ho5-peng5, hou7 lang5 saN-thai5."  Hian7-kim chit chat e5 Seng3-chheh ko2-jian5 u7 eng3-giam7. Iu7-koh ti7 Lou7-ka 21:10 u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, "Peh-siN3 beh khi2-lai5 kong-kek peh-siN3, kok ia7 beh khi2-lai5 kong-kek kok.  Te7 beh toa7 tin2-tang7, ia7 che7-che7 sou2-chai7 u7 ki-hng un-ek8."  "Lang5 tui3 kiaN-hiaN5 i2-kip8 theng3-hau7 sou2 beh kau3 ti7 thiN-e7 e5 su7 chiu7 hun7-khi3; in-ui7 thiN-siong7 beh tin2-tang7,"  "Ban7-mih8 ki3-jian5 beh an2-ni siau-biat8, chiu7 lin2-ti7 seng3 kap keng3-khian5 tioh8 choe3 sim2-mih8 ho7 e5 lang5, lai5 ng3-bang7, soa3 chhui-pek Siong7-te3 e5 jit8 kau3" (2 Pi2-tek 3:11 ).  "Sou2-i2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 ah, lin2 ki3-jian5 ng3-bang7 chiah-e5, tioh8 in-khin2 hou7 lin2 bo5 la5-sam5 bo5 ha5-chhu5, an-jian5 kiN3-tioh8 I" ( 2 Pi2-tek 3:14 ).  "Tioh8 kin2-sin7 keng2-seng2 lai5 ki5-to2, in-ui7 lin2 m7-chai hit e5 ki5 si7 ti7 sim2-mih8 si5. "    
Goa2 chin ti3-i3 ka7 lin2 tai7-ke chheng2-an. Goan7 lin2 ban7-hok.  "Ia7 goan7 lin2 tiam3 ti7 Ki-tok e5 lang5 long2 tit peng5-an. "  
Ti7 Eng-kok, Bun5--si7 An sia2.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 491 koan3,1926 ni5 2 geh8)
