   Thok8-chia2 hoe5 phoe  . 
Thak8 “Sit-khi3 Bo2-gi2 Se3-kai3 e5 Tai5-oan5 Gin2-a2”             					Gwou kak
 
    Chit PhiN Bun5 e5 Kam2-chhiok8
Tai5-oan5 choe7-choe7 e5 chian5-poe3 kap sian5-pai2 ti7 Tai5-bun5 un7-tong7 e5 thui-sak koe3-theng5 tiong, goa2 kan-taN si7 chit8 e5 chi-chhi5-chia2 nia5. Tui3 Tai5-bun5 e5 thai3-tou7 si7 beh kong2 m7 kiaN5, beh khoaN3 m7 sia2. Siau-kek8 e5 thai3-tou7 sui5 tioh8 Tai5oanji7 Siang-goeh8-khan e5 chhut-pan2, tauh8-tauh8-a2 teh kai2-pian3. Chit-ma2 chhoe7-khang chhoe7-phang7 siuN7 beh choe3 chit8 e5 Tai5-bun5 e5 the2-giam7-chia2 an2-ni chiah tioh8.
	Chit phiN bun5 ti7 iu3-ji5 kau3-iok8 u7 tiam2 chhut kui2-na7 e5 tiong7-iau3 e5 chui2-choaN5-thau5. Chiu7 si7 si5-kan-ki5, kau3-chai5, khoan5-keng2, cheng3-chhek, pe7-bu2 kap iu3-kau3 lau7-su. Chiah e5 chui2-choaN5-thau5, tai7 seng iau3-kin2..e5 si7 iu3-ji5 siu7-ka3 bo2-gi2 e5 si5-kan-ki5.   
	SaN-hoe3 au7 kau3 peh-hoe3 cheng5 e5 gin2-a2-phi2 chiu7 ai3 chiaN5 chu7-jian5 siu7-ka3 bo2-gi2, in oh8 siaN2 ki3 siaN2. Che chiu7 si7 thian-hu3 ka2-soat, oaN7 khah peh8-oe7 lai5 kong2 chiu7 si7 kio3 chiong3 chai5.  
	Tai5-gi2 kau3-chai5 si7 u7 khah khiam3-khoeh. Bun5-hoa3 si7 pau-koat choe7-choe7 e5 chan5-chhu3, u7 but8-chit, sim5-leng5, koan-liam7, ke3-tat8-koan------;iu2-heng5..e5 kap bu5-heng..e5. Chiah toa7 e5 su7-kang ng3-bang7 tai7-ke cho3-hoe2 lai5 chhong3-cho7, an2-ni chhai3-sek chiah e7 tit chheN-chhau, nau7-jiat8 chiau5-chng5.
	Kau3-iok8 khoan5-kheng2 che ai3 pun saN e5 sou2-chai lai5 hoe7. 1. Chhu3--nih8. Si3-toa7-lang5 m7 thang u7 Tai5-oan5 i3-sek bo5 Tai5-gi2 ti3-sek, piN3 choe3 A-kong A-ma2 boe7 gian3 kong2 Tai5-gi2, kan-a2-sun m7 chai oh8 Tai5-gi2 e5 koai3 hian7-siong7. 2. Sio2-hak8, iu3-ti3-hng5. Chiah e5 sou2-chai7 m7 si7 Eng-gi2 pan, chiaN3-im pan, beh bo5 chiu7 si7 chai5-ge7-pan. Chin bo5 lok8-koan. M7-ku2 tiau5-liu5 teh pian3, saN ni5 chit8 lun5, ho2 bai2 chiau3 lun5, chiap8 piN3 tioh8 e7 chhut-thau5-thiN. 3. Sia7-hoe7. Lan2 e7-sai2 siau7-kai3 lan2 e5 li2-liam7 hou7 lan2 e5 chhin-chiaN5 peng5-iu2, eng2-hiong2 chit8-e5 sng3 chit8-e5. Se3-chun7 hou7 ma7 be7 kham tit chiap8 loh8; chit8 kha jiah, chit8 kha pou7.
Ti7 cheng3-chhek siong7, A-piN2..a2 tong3-soan2, kai2-tiau5 oaN7-tai7 e5 toa7 tai7-chi3 chiu7 e7 hoat-seng, ui7 sim2-mih8 ku7 cheng3-chhek be7 tit kai2! Ng3-bang7 sin cheng3-hu2 ka7 Tai5-gi2 e5 thui-heng5 na2 chhiuN7 Pak-kiaN-gi2 ti7 50 ni5-tai7 an2-ni chhong3. Bin5-chin3 tong2 si5-siong5 hoah hiu kong2, Tai5-oan5 si7 lan2 e5 bo2-chhin, Tai5-gi2 chiu7 si7 bo2-chhin e5 nng7 liap8 lau7 ni-pou2. M7 thang soan2-ki2 si5 theh8 lai5 suh suh teh, 
soan2 soah chiu7 hiat toh-kak. Put hau3 kiaN2--ah.
	Ti7 lau7-su chit8 hong-bin7, in siong5 siong5 eng7 Pak-kiaN-gi2 teh ka3 Tai5-gi2, chit e5 giam5-tiong7 seng3 si7 lan2 siong7 kiaN-hiaN5..e5. Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni e5 le7,
Lau7-su: 各位小朋友，老師現在教你們台語  好不好？
Sio2 hak8-seng: 好。
Lau7-su: 小朋友，老師問你們台語的la5-li2是什麼動物呢？1.穿山甲， 2.穿山乙，3.穿山丙。小朋友，你們猜猜看是哪一種動物？
Sio2 hak8-seng: 是1，穿山甲。
Lau7-su: 嗯！小朋友答對了！好聰明啊！那台語的Chhui3 pit8-a2是什麼鳥類啊？1.綠繡眼，2.綠繡鼻，3.綠繡口，小朋友是哪一種鳥類啊？
Sio2 hak8-seng:是1.綠繡眼。
	Tui3 chit8 e5 le7 khoaN3--khi2--lai5, lau7-su kap sio2 hak8-seng e5 eng3-tap chi2 u7 la5-li2 kap chhui3 pit8-a2 nng7 su5 si7 Tai5-gi2-im na7-tiaN7. An2-ni chiu7 si7 lan2 e5 bo2-gi2 kau3-hak8? Bo5 chho3, kui-chang5 ho2 ho2. Koh iu3-ji5 e5 pe7-bu2 chhoe7 u7 kui2 khau2-chau3 tui3 Tai5-gi2 u7 i3-sek koh u7 jin7-bat..e5? Che chiaN3 si7 lan2 sou2 tam-sim e5 sou2-chai.
	Teng2 ian5..e5 a-kong a-ma2 in e5 hoe3-thau5 ti7 60 kau3 70 chit kha-tah8-a2..e5, in e5 Tai5-gi2 su5-lui7 oh8-tit kap po2-chun5-lut8 iok-liok8 si7 70 kau3 80 pha-sian3-touh(%). Tai5-gi2 su5-lui7 e5 po2-chun5 sui5 ni5-ki2 e5 kang3-ke7 toe3 teh ha7-kang3. Chim-chiok thiaN hian7-chhu2-si5 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 teh tui3-tam5 chiu7 e7 tit in3-cheng3. Kui phang5 lang5 cho3-hoe2 long2 kong2 Pak-kiaN gi2, liam7 chit8 koa2-a2 Tai5-gi2. Che ma7 lou7-hian7 chhut Tai5-gi2 e5 bi7-lai5.
	Siat-su2 teng2-bin7 e5 i7-siong7 koh ke3-siok8 sun5-hoan5--loh8--khi3, keng-koe3 20 tang au7 lau7-toa7-lang5 chiap-in2 se-hong, chhun..e5 koh u7 joa7 chhoe7 oe7 tit kong2 chhut chiaN3-kang2 e5 Tai5-gi2? Che m7 si7 pi-koan, che si7 su7-sit8. 
	Tai5-gi2 kiam2 tioh8 oh8? Goan2 mng7.
