Kim Kou-niu5 e5 Phoe
Thak8 Kau3-hoe7-po3 e5 peng5-iu2, peng5- an. Ko Kou-niu5 kap goa2 i2-keng kau3-ui7 ti7 Canada (Ka-na2-tai7), keng-koe3 gou7 goeh8- poaN3-jit8 ku2 e5 lou7-theng5, iah8 u7 si7-chhat chin che7 koh-iuN7 e5 sou2-chai7. TaN ai3 beh ka7 ti7 Tai5-oan5 che7-che7 peng5-iu2 chheng2-an.    
Ku7-ni5 1928 A. D.  Chap8- goeh8-boe2 goan2 li7-khui Tai5-oan5, te7-it tai7-seng theng5-khun3 ti7 Hongkong (Hiong-kang2). Ti7 lou7-tiong u7 keng-koe3 E7-mng5 kap SoaN3-thau5, Hiong-kang2 si7 chin sui2 e5 sou2- chai7, chha-put-to thong goan2 sou2 keng- iu5 e5 tou-chhi7 te7-it sui2 e5 chit8 e5.    
Tui3 Hiong-kang2 goan2 chit8 me5-hng e5 lou7-theng5 che7 se3-chiah chun5 khi3 Kang- mng5. Hia u7 Ka-na2-tai7 e5 Soan-to7-hoe7 ti7 hia, SaN-ni5 cheng5 hia e5 Soan-kau3-su, Bok8-su, I-seng, kap Sian-seN in-ui7 chian3- loan7 e5 ian5-kou3 koaN2-kin2 to5-chau2 khi3 Hiong-kang2. Kai-chai3 kau3-hoe7 e5 chhu3 bo5 siu7-phah-hai7, iah8 taN i2-keng kui2-na7 e5 Soan-kau3-su tng2-khi3 hia cho3-kang. Tai7- ke put-chi2 hoan-geng5, an-jian5 bo5 su7.    
Tui3 Hiong-kang2 goan2 u7 khi3 Kng2-tang. Kng2-tang si7 chin kin2 pian3-oaN7, siaN5- chhiuN5 long2 thiah, khah khoah e5 ke-lou7 u7 teh cho7, chin-che7 toa7 keng chhu3 u7 teh khi2. Ti7 kek-beng7 e5 si5-tai7 i2-keng u7 oan5-seng5 chiah-nih8 che7, goa2 siuN7 kok-ka peng5-tiaN7 e5 si5-chun7 tek-khak e7 toa7 hoat-tian2.    
Tui3 Hiong-kang2 kau3 Singapore (Sin- ka-pho) ia7 gou7-jit8 e5 chui2-lou7 chiam7- chiam7 khah sio-lo7, chap8-ji7 goeh8 thiN chhin-chhiuN7 Tai5-oan5 joah8-thiN e5 khi3- hau7.  Mui2-jit8 u7 loh8-hou7, sou2-i2 khi3-hau7 u7 khah un-ho5. Chhiu7-bak8 put-chi2 che7-iuN7, iah8 chin sui2. Jin5-khau2 chin hok-chap8, tak8-kok e5 lang5 long2 u7 ti7 hia. Ma2-lai5 lang5 i2-goa7 u7 chin-che7 Lam5 In3-tou7 e5 lang5, iah8 u7 chin-che7 Tiong-kok lang5, khah che7 si7 tui3 E7-mng5 kap SoaN3-thau5 khi3. Eng-kok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 iah8 u7 Soan- kau3-su ti7 Singapore kap chiu-ui5 e5 te7- hng teh cho3-kang.    
 
Ti7 Singapore goan2 oaN7 chun5 che7 chap8- jit8 ku2 kau3 Calcutta (Ka-ni2-kok-tap) In3-tou7, lou7-tiong u7 theng5 nng7 jit8 ti7 Rangoon (Giong2-kang) Bian2-tian7. Goan2 kau3 chia iah8 u7 tu2-tioh8 E7-mng5 lang5, koh khi3 chiu7 long2 bo5 tu2-tioh8.    
In3-tou7 si7 hui-siong5 toa7 e5 kok, u7 che7-che7 chiong2 e5 khi3-hau7, un-tou7 kap kong- keng2. Jin5-khau2 chin che7, 300,000,000 lang5. Khah che7 si7 siok8 nng7 chiong2- Hindu kap Moslem (Hoe5-hoe5 kau3-phai3). Gian5-gu2 jin5-chok8, hong-siok8 kap chong- kau3 long2 bo5 saN-tang5, liong2-peng5 put- chi2 keng3-cheng. Kok-lai7 hun-cho3 che7- che7 seng2, u7-e5 si7 In3-tou7 lang5 e5 ong5-chok8 sou2 koan2, u7-e5 si7 Eng-kok cheng3-hu2 tit8- hat. Jin5-khau2 hiah che7, long5-giap8 iau2- ku2 si7 chiau3 kou2-cha2 e5 hoat-tou7, sou2-i2 peh- seN3 chhin-chhiuN7 put-chi2 san3-hiong e5 khoan2. Khah che7 toa3 ti7 chng-sia7, u7 kui2-chap8 a2-si7 kui2-pah hou7 na7-tiaN7. Chhu3 si7 thou5 cho3-e5, chhu3-teng2 kham3-chhau2. Hak8-hau7 e5 gin2-na2 put-chi2 chio2, iah8 cha- bou2 gin2-na2 iau2 khah chio2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang thak8-chheh. Cha-bou2 gin2-na2 chap8-saN a2-si7 chap8-si3 hoe3, u7-e5 iau2 khah cha2, chiu7 chhut-ke3. KoaN7-tiaN7 liau2 chiu7 long2 be7 pian3-oaN7 tit. Sui-jian5 iau2-boe7 teng7-hun e5 si5, ta-pou-lang5 koe3-sin, cha-bou2-lang5 chiu7 piN3-cho3 koaN2-hu7, be7 sai2-tit koh- chai3 khi3 ke3, chit khoan2 e5 hong-siok8 hou7 kui2-na7 ban7 e5 gin2-na2 koaN2-hu7 chhi-chham2, kan-khou2.  Kui2-na7 e5 khah khai-hoa3 e5 ta-pou-lang5 kap hu7-jin5-lang5 u7 teh un7- tong7 thiat-hoe3 chit e5 hong-siok8.    
Chin-che7 Se-iuN5 e5 Soan-kau3-su u7 khi3 ti7 In3-tou7 cho3-kang. Ki-tok Kau3-hoe7 e5 jin5-sou3 sui-jian5 chio2, m7-ku2 chin u7 se3-lek8, chhut-lat8 teh kai2-tu5 chiong2-chiong2 e5 phaiN2 ku7-koan3. Ka-na2-tai7 Lian5-beng5 Kau3-hoe7 u7 kang-chok ti7 Tiong In3-tou7, iah8 u7 Soan-kau3-su kap kau3-hoe7 cho3-kang e5 lang5 ti7 chap8 sou2-chai7, hia e5 chong2 jin5-sou3 u7 3,000,000 lang5.    
Tui3 Bombay (Beng7-mai2) khi2-sin kau2-jit8 ku2 keng-ke3 Arabian Sea (A- lat8-pek hai2) kap Ang5-hai2, lou7-boe2 jit8 keng-koe3 Suez Canal (Sou-i5-su7 un7-bo5) chiu7-si7 lang5 sou2 kian3-tiok e5 un7-ho5 ti7 soa-bou5 e5 tiong-kan. 
 
Goan2 u7 oan-lou7 khi3 Palestine (Pa-lek-su7-tin3) chin sim-sek peh khi3 kau3 Jerusalem (Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2) chiu7-si7 ti7 koan5-soaN, si3-ui5 long2 chioh8-thau5, 2000 chhioh pi2 hai2-bin7 khah koan5. Tui3 hia goan2 chiu7 loh8-khi3 Jericho (Ia5-li7-ko) kap Dead Sia (Si2-hai2) 1300 chhioh pi2 hai2- bin7 khah ke7. Lou7-tiong keng-koe3 Ka- na2-soaN e5 soaN-kha, koe3 Pek-tai7-ni5 koe3 Ia5-sou siu7 chhi3 e5 soaN, kau3 Iok-tan2-ho5 khau2.  Goan2 siuN7 cheng5 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 e5 lai5-lek8, siuN7 I ti7 chiah e5 soaN-kok, peng5-iuN5, ho5-hai2 oah8-tang7. Che si7 lau5 teh hou7 lan2 thang siau3-liam7 I e5 it-seng, pi2 pai3-tng5, toa7 chhu3, sip8-ji7-ke3 a2-si7 pat8 khoan2 e5 chhu3, lang5 khi2, lai5 ki3-liam7 I, khah e7 hou7 lang5 kam2-kek. Tui3 Ia5-lou7- sat-leng2 goan2 che7 chu7-tong7-chhia khi3 Na2-sat-lek kau3 Tiberias, si3, gou7 tiam2 cheng ku2 na7-tiaN7, lou7-tiong keng-koe3 Nablous kap Jacobâ€™s Well (Nga2-kok e5 cheN2), chit e5 sou2-chai7 si7 chin-chiaN3 e5 kou2- chek. Goan2 ti7-chia lim chiN2-chui2, siuN7-tioh8 chai7-cha2 Ia5-sou ti7-chia kap Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2-oe7, khou2-khng3 i m7- thang kan-ta chiau3-kou3 jiok8-the2 na7-tiaN7, tioh8 him-soan7 leng5-hun5 e5 pou2-siong2.    
Hian7-si5 e5 Pa-lek-su7-tin3 si7 hou7 lang5 chu3-bak8, m7-na7 i e5 koe3-khi3 e5 lek8-su2, si7 Seng3-keng e5 sou2-chai7, iah8 i e5 hian7-kim kap chiong-lai5 e7 hou7 lang5 chu3-bak8. I e5 peh-seN3 (Iu5-thai3 lang5) tui3 goa7-kok long2 teh to3-lai5 pun2-kok, ti7-chia keng-eng5 long5-giap8 lai5 hok8-to3 in e5 kok-the2. U7-e5 si7 in-ui7 chong-kau3 chiah to3-lai5, u7-e5 si7 in-ui7 cheng3-ti7 kap kok-bin5 e5 su-siuN2. In tai7-ke put-chi2 ti3-i3, chin7-lat8, lan2 thang khoaN3 in e5 chiong-lai5 si7 chaiN2-iuN7.    
Kan-ta si3-jit8 ti7 Egypt (Ai-kip8) ti7 Cairo (Khai-gi7-lo5) e5 chiu-ui5 chham- koan kong-keng2. Pyramids (chhut- mia5 e5 saN-kak-heng5 e5 bong7) Sphynx (chhut-mia5 e5 Chioh8-siong7) kap Phok- but8-koan2, lai7-bin7 u7 chin-che7 po2-poe3, si7 tui3 kou2-cha2 hong5-te3 e5 bong7 sou2 kut8-khi2- lai5 e5. Goan2 iah8 u7 khi3 khoaN3 chit8-ho7 kou2-cha2 bong7 kap bio7, iah8 u7 khi3 khoaN3 Hoe5-hoe5-kau3 e5 Pai3-tng5. Keng-koe3 Te7- tiong-hai2 tioh8 ai3 si3-jit8 ku2, hai2-eng2 si7 thong goan2 e5 lou7-theng5 te7-it toa7. Kau3 Italy (I2-tai7-li7) u7 chin-che7 kou2-chek si7 Au-chiu te7-it chhut-mia5 e5, chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3 Pi2-tek Le2-pai3-tng5, hui-siong5 sui2, hui-siong5 ho2-khoaN3, iah8 iau2-ku2 u7 chin- che7 pat8-ui7 e5 toa7 le2-pai3-tng5.   
I2-tai7-li7, tak8-ui7 long2 si7 phu5-to5-hng5, kap bi2-sut8, tou5-oe7, khek-siong7 put-chi2 chhu3-bi7, put- chi2 ho2-khoaN3. Goan2 toa3 hia si3 le2-pai3, ti7-hia chin sim-sek, si5-kan na2 chhin- chhiuN7 chin te2 chin khoai3-koe3.    
Goan2 che7 hoe2-chhia keng-koe3 Switzerland (Sui7-se-kok), chin sui2 e5 ou5 kap soaN e5 keng2-ti3.  Tui3 hia keng-koe3 Pa-li2 kau3 Lun5-tun siaN5. Thang hoaN-hi2 koh-chai3 chit8 pai2 kau3 lan2 pun2 sou2-chai7, Eng-gu2 e7 thong. Lun5-tun si7 se3-kai3 te7-it toa7 tou-chhi7, jin5-khau2 si7 thong Tai5-oan5 e5 jin5-sou3 e5 teng5-poe7. Sui-jian5 hiah toa7, u7 che7-che7 kong-hng5 kap khui-khoah e5 sou2- chai7. Ti7 chhun-thiN u7 che7-che7 chheN- chhui3 e5 chhiu7-bak8, sui2-sek e5 hoe. I e5 ke-lou7 put-si5 che7-che7 lang5 ong2-lai5 bo5 eng5. Chin-che7 lek8-su7-chiuN7 chhut-mia5 e5 chhu3 kap kou2-chek. Na7-si7 beh koaN2-kin2 khoaN3 long2-chong2 e5 sou2-chai7, chit8 le2-pai3 iah8 bo5 kau3, tioh8 chit8 goeh8-jit8 chiah khoaN3 e7- liau2. Chong2-si7 na7 beh chim-chiok khoaN3, tioh8 chit8 tang chiah khoaN3 e7-liau2.    
Tui3 Eng-kok kau3 Montreal (Ka-na2-tai7 e5 toa7 tou-chhi7), peng5-siong5 tioh8 ai3 chhit- peh jit8, iah8 ti7 gou7 -goeh8 e5 si5-chun7 put- chi2 koaN5. Loe7-te7 e5 kang-ou5 e5 peng long2 teh iuN5, lau5 chhut khi3 goa7-hai2. Peng- soaN, chiu7-si7 kui-soaN e5 peng phu5 ti7 hai2- bin7, tui3 pak-peng5 e5 chui2-lou7 lau5 loh8-lai5 lam5-peng5. Put-si5 ta3-bu7.  Chui2-le5 e5 siaN put-si5 teh tan5, mi5-jit8 hou7 lang5 kiaN-hiaN5.    
Chong2-si7 peh ni5 ku2 e5 li7-piat8 liau2-au7, keng-koe3 gou7 goeh8-jit8 e5 lu2-heng5 liau2-au7, khoaN3 che7-che7 sim-sek chhu3-bi7 e5 sou2-chai7, kong-keng2 liau2-au7, goan2 tai7-ke u7 chit khoan2 tek8-piat8 e5 kam2-kek thang koh chit8 pai2 kau3-ui7 ti7 pun2 sou2-chai7.    
Tak8-kok u ka-ti7 e5 sui2 e5 heng5-chong7, chong2-si7 Ka-na2-tai7 ti7 gou7-goeh8 kap lak8- goeh8 e5 si5, si7 be7 khah su pat8 sou2-chai7. Sng-seh i2-keng bo5 lah, hoe-bak8 ti7 hoe-hng5 a2-si7 chu7-jian5 khui ti7 chhau2-pou e5 sou2-chai7 put-chi2 bou7-seng7. Ti7 chhi7-lai7 e5 ke-lou7 e5 liong2-peng5 u7 toa7-chang5 chhiu7, i e5 chhiu7-hioh8 chheN-chhui3 hou7 lang5 chin him-sian7.   
Ti7 chhi7-goa7, chhau2 kap ngou2- kok kap han-chu5-hng5 si7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 e5 khoah-khoah jiong5 e5 te7-chian.    
Ka-na2-tai7 si7 hui-siong5 sui2 ti7 chhun- thiN kap ha7-thiN.  Chin hoaN-hi2, thang koh kap goan2 e5 chhin-lang5 peng5-iu2 cho3-hoe2.  Ti3-i3 ka7 lin2 tai7-ke chheng2-an.
