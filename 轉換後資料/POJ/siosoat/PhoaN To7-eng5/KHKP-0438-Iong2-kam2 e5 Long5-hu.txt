

Iong2-kam2 e5 Long5-hu
PhoaN To7-eng5  
Ti7 A-hui-li7-ka-chiu kin7 ti7 bou2 hai2-piN e5 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 thau5-mng5 chhui3-chhiu peh8 e5 lau7 choh-sit lang5 ku2-ku2 khia7 ke ti7 hia.  I na7 bo5 sou2 su7-su7 chiu7 siong5-siong5 chhut-khi3 khia7 ti7 hai2-kiN5 teh khoaN3 goa7-hai2 sou2 teh keng-koe3 e5 chun5-chiah.  
U7 1 jit8 chit e5 lau7-peh ah, tiam3 ti7 chhu3-lai7 e5 si5, hut-jian5-kan khi2 toa7 hong-thai.  Koh tiap-a2-ku2 chiu7 u7 1 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 chhiN-hiong5 chau2 lai5 po3 kong2, Chit-tiap8 u7 chit8 chiah chun5 hoan7-hong, phah kau3 chhui3-kou5-kou5.  I chek-si5 khia5 chit8 chiah sou2 ai3-sioh e5 be2 khi3 kau3 hai2-kiN5. Hong-eng2 sui-jian5 chin toa7, iau2-ku2 hiah e5 chui2-siu2 kio3 kiu3 lang5 kek8 pi-siong e5 siaN thiaN hian7-hian7.  I siuN7 ai3 beh khi3 kiu3 in, m7-ku2 ti7 sin-piN bo5 poaN3 chiah chun5.  SiuN7-liau2 bo5 pian3-pou7, kou-put-chiong phau2 hit chiah be2 siu5-loh8 chui2 khi3 kiu3 hiah e5 siu7 chham2 e5 chui2-siu2.  
Ti7 hoaN7-teng2 u7 chin che7 lang5 khoaN3 i teh khi3, tai7-ke kiaN kau3 beh phoa3-taN2, chong2--si7 chit e5 lau7 choh-sit peh ah, long2 bo5 sio2-khoa2 tah-hiahN, ho2-taN2 tit8-tit8 kap be2 siu5 kau3 chun5 kong3-phoa3 e5 sou2-chai7. Theng5 bo5 chiah8 chit8 poah hun ku2, i kap be2 long2 koh siu5 to3-tng2, ti7 be2-teng2-nih8 u7 chai3 2 mia5 chui2-siu2, cheng3 lang5 khoaN3 liau2, be7 kim3-tit khui-siaN phah-chhiu2 hoaN-hi2 o-lo2 i e5 iong2-kam2 kau3 an2-ni.  I long2 bo5 theng5-khun3, liam5-piN koh khi2-sin khi3 kiu3 2 lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni ke3-kiong7 u7 7 pai2.  Te7 it lou7-be2 pai2 koh khi3, ti7 be2-teng2 iu5-goan5 hou7 2 lang5 che7, siu5 to3-tng2 lai5 kau3 poaN3-cham7, i e5 seng-khu nng2-kauh8-kauh8, soa3 hou7 toa7 eng2 kham3-bat8, put-ti khi3-hiong3.  I pun2-sin song3-sit siN3-mia7, chong2--si7 hit chiah be2 kap 14 mia5 e5 chui2-siu2 long2 an-jian5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  
He2!  Chit 14 mia5 e5 chui2-siu2 m7 chai beh lau5 loa7-che7 e5 bak8-sai2 lai5 kam2-sia7 chit e5 lau7 in-jin5!  TaN lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou m7-na7 ui7-tioh8 14 lang5 na7-tiaN7, I u7 kam-goan7 pang3-sak siN3-mia7 lai5 chin2-kiu3 choan5 se3-kai3 e5 jin5-lui7.  Sou2-i2 lan2 eng-kai ka-poe7 chheng-chan3 Kiu3-chu2 bu5-liong7 bu5-sou3 e5 thiaN3-thang3.“Lang5 ui7-tioh8 peng5-iu2 hian3 siN3-mia7, thiaN3 bo5 u7 pi2 chit-e5 khah toa7--e5.”“Lan2 iau2-ku2 cho3 choe7-jin5 e5 si5, Ki-tok the3 lan2 si2.”   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 438 koan3, 1921 ni5 9 geh8)
