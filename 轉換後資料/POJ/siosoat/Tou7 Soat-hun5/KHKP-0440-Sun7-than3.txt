

Sun7-than3
Tou7--si7 Soat-hun5   
Bat chit8 jit8 u7 saN e5 gin2-na2 ti7 lou7-nih8 cho3-hoe2 teh chhit-tho5, hut-jian5-kan thiaN-kiN3 in lau7-bu2 e5 siaN teh kio3 in.“Hong7--a2, A-kim, Ban7-chhun!  Lai5 lah! Kin2-kin2 lai5!”In-ui7 in m7 ai3 khi3, Hong7--a2 chiu7 kong2,“An2-nia5 m7 chai-iaN2 lan2 si7 ti7-chia, m7 bian2 khi3.  KaN2 si7 beh kah lan2 khi3 boe2 mih8, a2-si7 pho7 sio2-ti7 chhit-tho5. Lan2 ka7 teN3-m7-chai, chiu7 ho2.”A-kim iah8 kong2,“Tu2-a2 teh choe3 siang7 ho2 chhit-tho5 e5 si5, an2-nia5 put-si3 teh kio3 lan2, m7 bian2 khi3.”  
Chong2--si7 Ban7-chhun long2 bo5 kong2 poaN3 ku3, khi2-lai5 choe7 i khi3.  Nng7 e5 chi2-moe7-a2 chiu7 siuN7 kong2,“TaN i to3-khi3 lah, chiu7 kau3-giah8, lan2 m7 bian2.”In chiu7 koh chhit-tho5, m7-ku2 khiam3 Ban7-chhun thang choe3-phoaN7 si7 khah bo5 hiah-nih8 sim-sek, iah8 in e5 liong5-sim kaN2 ia7 lioh8-a2 teh chhak8 in.  Sou2-i2 koh koe3 poaN3 tiam2 cheng ku2, in chiu7 to3-khi3 chhu3-nih8, ai3 beh khoaN3 in hiaN si7 teh choe3 siaN2-hoe3. Chhoe7 i bo5, chiu7 mng7 in lau7-bu2, Ban7-chhun khi3 to2-ui7.  In lau7-bu2 chiu7 kong2, I u7 khi3 kong-hng5 chhit-tho5, lin2 a-chek tu2-a2 lai5, beh chhoa7 lin2 long2 khi3, hou7 lin2 thang che7 chu7-choan5-chhia, soa3 boe2 mih8 hou7 lin2 chiah8.  Goa2 eng7 toa7 siaN kio3 lin2, Chong2--si7 kan-ta Ban7-chhun lai5 na7-tiaN7. Lin2 a-chek boe7 thang theng3 hau7, sou2-i2 cho3 in khi3.  
Chi2-moe7-a2 chiu7 hoan5-lo2 kong2, TaN hai7 lah!  Li2 thai3 bo5 ka7 goan2 kong2 si7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3?  Goan2 na7 chai-iaN2 an2-ni, tek-khak hoaN-hi2 lai5.  Lau7-bu2 chiu7 kong2, M7-ku2 Ban7-chhun i iah8 m7 chai-iaN2, chong2--si7 u7 thiaN goa2 teh kio3, chiu7 liam5-piN lai5.  A-kim kap Hong7--a2 chiu7 tiam7-tiam7, teh saN khoaN3, bo5 koh kong2 sim2-mih8 oe7.  In-ui7 in chai-iaN2 si7 ka-ki7 e5 chho3-gou7, au7 pai2 chiu7 tioh8 hoaN-hi2 than3.   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 440 koan3, 1921 ni5 11 geh8)
