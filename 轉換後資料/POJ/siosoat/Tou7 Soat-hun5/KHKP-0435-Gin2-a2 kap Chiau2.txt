
Gin2-a2 kap Chiau2

(Tou7 Soat-hun5 ki3.)
Ti7 Na2-ui kok bat u7 chit8 e5 chiu2-koaN2 hu7-jin5-lang5 kap i kou-toaN e5 hau7-siN, ho7-choe3 Khoan-liong7, toa3 ti7 chit8 keng soe3 keng chhu3.  Mui2 ni5 kiN3-na7 chhun-thiN si5 u7 chit8 chiah koan3-chiau2 lai5 choh-siu7 ti7 in chhu3-nih8, tiam3 hia siN-nng7 pu7-kiaN2.  Kau3 chiau2-a2-kiaN2 lioh8-a2 khah toa7 chiah, tu2-ho2 si7 chhiu-thiN leng2-khi3 e5 si5-chun7 in chiu7 chhoa7 khi3 toa3 ti7 lam5-peng5 sio-lo7 e5 sou2-chai7, keh ni5 e5 chhun-thiN chiah koh tng2-lai5.  Tui3 Khoan-liong7 iu3-chiN2 e5 si5 long2 si7 an2-ni,ni5 koe3 ni5 i chin hoaN-hi2 tan2 hit chiah chiau2 tng2-lai5 e5 si5, in-ui7 tak8 jit8 ai3 chhong3 chiah8 mih8 hou5 i.  Hit chiah chiau2-a2 chin koai3 ai3 hou7 in chhi7, sou2-i2 I kap i e5 lau7-bu2 nng7 lang5 chin thiaN3 i.  Khoan-liong7 na7 ka7 i khou, i chiu7 poe-loh8-lai5 chiah8, in-ui7 chiau2-a2 kap i chin sek8-sai7.
Khoan-liong7 khah toa7-han3 chiu7 khi3 choe3 chui2-siu2.  Thau5 chit8 pai2 koe3 hai2 e5 si5, in hit chiah chun5 u7 tui3 A-hui-li7-ka chiu chiuN7-hoaN7, ti7-hia tu2-tioh8 hai2-chhat8 soa3 hou7 in liah8-khi3 boe7 choe3 lou5-chai5.  Koe3 chit8 nng7 ni5 Khoan-liong7 e5 lau7-bu2 long2 bo5 chiap-tioh8 i e5 siau-sit, sim the choat8-bang7, phah-sng3 i e5 kiaN2 kaN2 i2-keng si2.  Ki5-sit8 bo5 si2, si7 teh siu7 chham2, koat3 hih-lian7, toa3 ti7 chit8 e5 kek8 chan5-jim2 e5 lang5 e5 chhu3 teh ka7 i choe3 kang.  I e5 sim toa7-toa7 siau3-liam7 chou2-ke kap i e5 lau7-bu2, teh siuN7 m7 chai kau3 sim2-mih8 si5 e7 thang koh kiN3-tioh8?  KiaN-liau2 eng2-oan2 bo5 ki-hoe7 ia7 ku2 kaN2.
Tu2-ho2 chit8 jit8 Khoan-liong7 teh choe3 kang e5 si5, ngou2-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 chiah koan3-chiau2 ti7 i e5 thau5-khak teng2 poe-lai5 poe-khi3.  I e5 sim be7 bian2-tit to3-tng2 siuN7 cha2-jit8 tiam3 in chhu3 e5 cheng5-keng2 chiu7 chhut-siaN ka7 i khou.  He2!  Chin ki5-koai3, chiau2-a2 liam5-piN hioh-loh8-lai5, i chiah jin7-tioh8 si7 i cha2-si5 e5 peng5-iu2.  Khoan-liong7 chin hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7-kou3-tit!  SiuN7 chhut chit8 hang7 e5 ke3-ti3 kong2, “Kau3 koan3-chiau2 na7 beh koh poe-khi3 pak-peng5, goa2 chiu7 beh sia2 chit8 tiuN phoe,pak8 ti7 i e5 kha, hou7 i toa7 to3-khi3 goan2 tau,goan2 lau7-bo2 na7 khoaN3-tioh8 chiu7 e7 chai”.  Kau3 koh keh tang e5 chhun-thiN hit chiah koan3-chiau2 chiau3-goan5 khi3 Na2-ui-kok chhoe7 i e5 ku7 siu7.  Khoan-liong7 e5 lau7-bo2 khoaN3 i koh tng2-lai5 chin hoaN-hi2,in-ui7 siuN7-tioh8 i e5 kiaN2 cha2-tang-si5 e5 sou2 cho3 sim koh khah m7 kam, chin thiaN3-sioh.  Hut-jian5 khoaN3-tioh8 chit8 tiuN phoe pak8 ti7 chiau2-a2 kha, chiu7 kin2-kin2 ka7 i thau2 khui, theh8 khi2-lai5 thak8, chai si7 i e5 kiaN2 sou2 sia2 e5 oe7.  In pun2-chng e5 lang5 thiaN-liau2 iah8 thoe3 i hoaN-hi2.  U7 chit8 e5 bok8-su ka7 in kong2, “TaN lan2 tioh8 chin7-lat8 lai5 khi3 kiu3 i”.  Liam5-piN u7 lang5 chhut-thau5 khi3 te5 chiN5, cheng3 tai7-ke chiau3 in sou2 e7 lai5 chan3-seng5: m7 na7 an2-ni, in u7 lang5 ho2-taN2 khi3 pin2-kiu5 Hong5-te3.  Hong5-te3 thiaN-liau2 be7 bian2-tit lau5 bak8-sai2, piau2 tong5-cheng5 siat hoat-tou7 lai5 chin2-kiu3.  Koh koe3 bo5 kui2 jit8 kau chiu7 pi7-pan7 chian3-chun5 beh khi3 A-hui-li7-ka lai5 kiu3 hit e5 siau3-lian5 e5 lou5-chai5.  Joah8 thiN iau2-boe7 koe3 e5 si5, chian3-chun5 chiu7 to3-tng2-lai5, iah8 chhoa7 Khoan-liong7 kau3 i sou2 thiaN3 e5 lau7-bu2 e5 sin-piN.  Cheng3 lang5 put-chi2 hoaN-hi2 hoan-geng5 i, soa3 cho3-hoe2 khi3 pai3-tng5 lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 e5 in.
Kau3 au7-lai5 Khoan-liong7 ti7 pai3-tng5 i2-kip8 in chhu3 e5 chhu3-teng2, u7 chhai7 chit8 chiah koan3-chiau2 e5 tang5-siong7, hou7 lang5 thang chai Siong7-te3 kiu3 i e5 siN3-mia7 e5 hoat-tou7 si7 eng7 hit chiah chiau2-a2.
Chong2-si7 khah chai-iaN2 e5 lang5 long2 chhut-lat8 kong2, Na7 m7 si7 hit e5 gin2-na2 tui3 khi2-thau5 e5 si5 ho2 khoan2-thai7 hit chiah koan3-chiau2, i kaN2 oe7 khi3 pat8 keng chhu3 choh-siu7, chiu7 khoan-liong7 au7-lai5 un2-tang3 bo5 lou7 thang tit-tioh8 kiu3.
