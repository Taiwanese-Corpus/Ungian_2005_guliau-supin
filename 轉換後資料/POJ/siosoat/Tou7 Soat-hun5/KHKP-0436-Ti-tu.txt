

Ti-tu
Tou7 Soat-hun5
Gin2-na2 khah siong5 khoaN3-tioh8 ti-tu chiu7 put-chi2 kiaN, koh iam3-ouN3 in.  In-ui7 chiau3 in teh siuN7 ti-tu si7 la5-sam5 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8; su5-put-ti Siong7-te3 iah8 bat eng7 in cho3 I e5 khi3-khu7, lai5 kiaN5 I e5 seng3-chi2.  
Chiau3 goa2 sou2 chai Siong7-te3 chi3-chio2 tek-khak u7 nng7 pai2 bat eng7 ti-tu lai5 pang-chan7 I e5 peh-siN3.  Ti7 Sou-kek-lan5 chha-put-to 600 ni5 cheng5 u7 chit8 e5 chin ho2 e5 ong5 tu2-tioh8 chai-lan7.  Chiu7-si7 Eng-kok e5 ong5 u7 chhoa7 kun-peng lai5 kong-kek khah-iaN5 i, soa3 chhim-chiam3 kui2-na7 e5 siaN5, ti3-kau3 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 peh-siN3 chin kan-khou2.  Kun-peng chau2 soaN3, lian5 ong5 pun2-sin ia7 tioh8 to5-chau2 khi3 bih ti7 soaN-nih8, toa3 ti7 chit8 keng te7-it song3-hiong e5 peh-siN3 e5 chhu3.  
Ti7 bou2 cha2-khi2, ong5 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 e5 si5, sim teh khoa3-lu7 i e5 kok, chiu7 chin ai3 siuN7-chhut khoaN3 u7 ho2 e5 hong-hoat thang kiu3 in.  Hut-jian5-kan u7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 chiah ti-tu ti7 chhu3-teng2 e5 toa7 ki iN5-a2 teh kiN-bang7.  I tai7-seng tiau3 ti7 chit8 tiau5 si boe2, hiN3-lai5 hiN3-khi3, chhin-chhiuN7 hiN3 chhian-chhiu e5 khoan2; in-ui7 ai3 khan si kau3 pat8 ki iN5-a2 thang kiN-bang7.  Ong5 siong5-se3 khoaN3, chiu7 chai thau5 chit8 pai2 ti-tu siuN7 beh hiN3 kau3 hit peng5 iN5-a2, m7-ku2 i bo5 kau3-giah8 lat8 thang hiN3 kau3 hia.  Te7 ji7 pai2 i koh khah chhut-lat8, chong2--si7 iu5-goan5 si7 bo5 lou7-eng7.  Ong5 chiu7 ka-ti7 teh siuN7 kong2,“TaN chit chiah ti-tu kiam2 m7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 pun2-sin.  Goa2 nng7 pai2 u7 toa7 chhut-lat8, ai3 beh kiu3 goa2 e5 peh-siN3, bo5 phah-sng3 tak8 pai2 hou7 tui3-tek8 khah-iaN5. TaN goa2 beh koh chim-chiok ka7 i khoaN3, chit chiah ti-tu kau3 te7 saN pai2 na7 oe7 seng5-kong, chiu7 goa2 beh liah8 chit-e5 cho3 tiau7-thau5, chai Siong7-te3 ai3 goa2 koh chit8 pai2 chhut-lat8, ng3-bang7 hou7 goa2 e5 kok oe7-tit chu7-iu5.”  
TaN ti-tu kau3 te7 saN pai2 ko2-jian5 u7 hiN3 kau3 hit ki iN5-a2, ong5 khoaN3 liau2, chiu7 chong3-taN2, khi2-lai5 koh to3-khi3 kiaN-siaN5; bo5 loa7-ku2 e5 tiong-kan i u7 tit-tioh8 koh khah che7 peng lai5 toe3 i, au7 --lai5 in u7 khah-iaN5 tui3-tek8.  Che kiam2 m7 si7 Siong7-te3 eng7 chit chiah ti-tu lai5 chi2-si7 i mah?  
Koh koe3 300 ni5 Siong7-te3 iah8 u7 koh chit8 pai2 bat eng7 ti-tu lai5 cho3 I toa7 e5 lou7-eng7. Hit-si5 Sou-kek-lan5 lang5 chin che7 in-ui7 ai3 chiau3 ka-ti7 e5 liong5-sim lai5 keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3, chiu7 hou7 chan5-jim2 e5 lang5 khun2-tiok8.  In che7-che7 siu7 lang5 koaN2-chhut, to5-siam2 khi3 soaN-nih8 bih.  Bat chit8 pai2 kui2-na7 lang5 ti7 soaN-nih8 hou7 tui3-tek8 tui-jip; in kau3 ti7 chit8 e5 soaN-tong7 chiu7 jip8-khi3 bih.  Liam5-piN u7 chit chiah ti-tu lai5 kiN-bang7 ti7 tong7-khau2.  Bo5 loa7-ku2 peng chiu7 kau3, teh chhoe7 chiah e5 lang5.  Teh-beh jip8 tong7 e5 si5, u7 khoaN3-kiN3 tit-u bang7, chiah siuN7 tek-khak bo5 lang5 jip8-khi3 bih ti7 lai7-bin7; siat-su2 u7, chiu7 ti-tu bang7 un2-tang3 bo5 an2-ni ho2-ho2.  
Teh po2-hou7 I e5 peh-siN3 e5 Siong7-te3 u7 an2-ni siat-hoat, hou7 ti-tu tu2-a2 hit-si5 lai5 kiN-bang7, iah8 hou7 i tu2-ho2 kiN ti7 chit e5 tong7-khau2 e5 sou2-chai7, thang po2-choan5 thiaN3 I e5 lang5 e5 siN3-mia7. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 436 koan3, 1921 ni5 7 geh8)
