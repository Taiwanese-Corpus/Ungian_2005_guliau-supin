Tun3-gou7
Khou2 Hui7-chong
Goa2 tu2 lai5 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 e5 te7-it e5 cha2-khi2, in-ui7 ti7 chng-kha i2-keng iong2-seng5 e5 sip8-koan3, chiah gou7-tiam2 goa7 tioh8 cheng-sin5 ah, to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2 am3-sng3, si7 khi2--lai5 khah ho2 leh, iah si7 ke3-siok8 to2 leh khah ho2? In-ui7 iah bo5 liau2-kai2 chit e5 sou2-chai7 e5 chong7-hong2, goa2 bo5 siuN7-beh in-ui7 goa2 chit8-e5 lang5 e5 koan-he7 khi3 eng2-hiong2-tioh8 ki5-tha pat8-lang5 e5 hioh-khun3 si5-kan. Ti7 goa2 e5 piN-a2 u7 chap8 kui2 e5 tiam3-goan5, chit8-e5 chit8-e5 to2 ti7 hia, na2-chhiuN7 han-chi5 khoaN2. U7 lang5 chhui3 khui-khui, u7 lang5 bak8-chiu poaN3-bi poaN3-bi, ma7 u7 lang5 phak leh khun3, kui e5 pang5-keng long2-si7 chhau3-koaN7-sng-bi7. Chiah ji7-goeh8 tiong, thiN-khi3 tioh8 na2 joah8-thiN kang7-khoan2 joah8, iu5-ki5 chit kui2 kang khi3-hau7 pian3-hoa3 chiok toa7.  Chia e5 lang5 m7-chai tau3-te2 siaN2-mih si5-chun7 chiah e7 peh--khi2-lai5 leh? Goa2 chit8-bin7 leh siuN7 kou2-cha2 se3-han3 si5-chun7 e5 tai7-chi3, chit8-bin7 koh leh chhai-chhek kap su-kho2 tou-chhi7-lang5 e5 seng-oah8 hong-sek. Hit e5 si5-chun7 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 e5 chai2-khi2 u7 chhi7-tiuN5 koh-chiong3 boe7 mih8-kiaN7 e5 kio3-boe7-siaN, hou7 lang5 chin siau3-liam7 hit chiong3 lau7-jiat8 e5 ke-keng2.  Toa3 ti7 gun2 chhu3-piN e5 gin2-a2 ma7 long2 chiok cha2 tioh8 khi2--lai5, ka7 in chhu3--li2 tau3 boe7 iu5-chiah8-koe2. Tu2 thiaN-tioh8 beh poaN-khi3 chng-kha e5 si5-chun7, goa2 sim-lai7 ma7 bo5 siaN2 goan7-i3 li7-khui Tai5-pak, tan7-si7 tan2 kau3 toa7-lang5 ka7 chhu3--li2 toa7-se3-hang7 mih8-kiaN7 long2 poaN-khi3 gu5-chhia teng2, ka7 gun2 ma7 pho7-khi3 gu5-chhia teng2, chit8 lou7 haiN3 leh haiN3 leh hiong3 chhau2-te7 e5 lou7 chhut-hoat, goa2 e5 sim-lai7 soah u7 kong2-boe7-chhut e5 hoaN-hi2.  A-pa3 kap a-bu2 chhoa7 goa2 poaN-khi3 chng-kha e5 cheng5-keng2 it-tit8 kau3 taN ma7 ia2 phu5 ti7 goa2 e5 nau2-hai2 tiong.  Goa2 cheng5 chit8 kang e5 thau2-cha2 u7 khi3 kou2-cha2 goa2 se3-han3 si5 toa3-koe3 e5 ke-a2 seh8-seh8 kiaN5-kiaN5, m7-koh, i2-keng kah ki3-ek tiong e5 chhu3-piN keh-piah bo5-kang7 ah. Lau5-a2-chhu3 long2 pian3 ku7--khi3 ah, chit8 chiong2 siok-bok8 bo5-nai7 e5 sim-cheng5 chhiong-boan2 ti7 goa2 e5 sim-lai7. Hiong5-hiong5 goa2 thau5 chit8 kai2 hoat-kak si5-kan koe3-liau2 si7 hiah-ni7-a2 kin2, bak8-chiu khoaN3-boe7-tioh8 e5 si5-kan e5 lau5-sit, ki5-sit8 ma7 si7 kah khe-liu5 e5 lau5-sit si7 kang7-khoan2, chit8-tiam2 chit8-tiam2 ka7 thou5-soa kap chioh8-thau5 un7-khi3 kau3 khe-chui2 e5 chhut-hai2-khau2. Lan2 lang5 e5 jiok8-the2 ma7-si7 te3 si5-kan e5 choan2-pian3, chiam7-chiam7 siau-boa5 pian3 lau7, pian3 bo5-lou7-iong7--khi3. Goa2 tioh8 ti7 chit khoan2 ou-peh8 loan7-siuN7 tiong su-kho2 chiok che7 tai7-chi3, ti7 chit e5 tiong-kan, khun3 ti7 goa2 piN-a2 e5 lang5 khoaN3--khi3-lai5 long2 ia2 bo5 beh peh--khi3-lai5 khoan2, u7 e5 koh khun3 kah kouN7-kouN7-kio3.  Jit8-thau5 ma7 tauh8-tauh8-a2 ui3 thang-a2-mng5 e5 chit8 phang7 chio3--jip8-lai5, thiN ma7 kng--khi2-lai5 ah. Ke-a2-lou7 ong2-hoe5 e5 kha-pou7-siaN ma7 chiam7-chiam7 bo5-eng5 khi2-lai5. KhoaN3-tioh8 goa2 cha-hng koaN7--lai5 e5 hit kha phoe5-siuN-a2 ia2-koh khng3 ti7 goa2 khun3 e5 sou2-chai7 e5 piN-a2, kam2-kak na2-chhiuN7 ka-ki7 bo5 chheng7-saN khia7 ti7 tak8-ke e5 bin7-thau5-cheng5 kang7-khoan2, chiok kian3-siau3 e5. Goan5-pun2 goa2 siuN7-beh tan2 tak8-ke iah-boe7 peh--khi2-lai5 chin3-cheng5, tioh8 ka7 siu-khi3 tu5-a2 lai7, m7-koh, goa2 koh bo5-goan7-i3 bo5 chun-tiong7 pat8-lang5 chhin3-chhai2 khi3 khui tu5-a2, sou2-i2 chi2-ho2 tan2 tak8-ke long2 khun3 chhiN2 liau2-au7 chiah koh kong2. Goa2 iu7-koh siuN7-khi2 goan2 lau7-pe7 kap lau7-bu2, su-hiong e5 sim-cheng5 hiong5-hiong5 chiam3-boan2 tioh8 goa2 e5 sim-koaN. Chit-ma2 goa2 sin-the2 khiam3-an e5 lau7-bu2 eng3-kai khi2-chhng5 leh chhi7-ke.  Hiong3 thang-a2-mng5 ka7 khoaN3--khi3,  tui3-bin7 chhu3-piN e5 chhu3-lai7-te2 ou-so5-so5,  khoaN3-boe7-chhut u7 lang5 khi2-chhng5 e5 khoan2. Goa2 thau-thau leh siuN7, Tai5-pak e5 cha2-khi2 chin-chiaN3 si7 bo5-liau5, na7-si7 ti7 chng-kha, tu5-liau2 peN7-lang5 i2-goa7, si7 bo5-lang5 e7 khun3 kau3 hiah-ni7-a2 oaN3. SiuN7-tioh8 i2-au7 ka-ki7 ma7-si7 ai3 kap pat8-lang5 kang7-khoan2 koe3 chit khoan2 oaN3-khi2-chhng5 e5 jit8-chi2, na2-chhiuN7 chit8-phiN3 ou-hun5 tioh8 kham3 ti7 goa2 e5 sim-koaN-thau5 ah. Iu5-ki5, thau5-ke cheng5 chit8 kang hou7 goa2 chhut-khi3 Tai5-pak ke-thau5 kiaN5-kiaN5 leh, goa2 siuN7 kho2-leng5 si7 in-ui7 goa2 bo5 cho3 khang-khoe3, sou2-i2 chiah boe7 chhin-chhiuN7 in hiah-ni7-a2 thiam2. Koe3 chit khoan2 e5 seng-oah8, ka-ki7 kam2 chin-chiaN3 u7 ki-hoe7 e7 chhut-thau5 leh? Hoe5-siong2 thau5-ke Li2 Ong7-hok cha-am3 tui3 goa2 kong2 e5 oe7, ti7 goa2 e5 sim-koaN-thau5 lai7, chit8 pai2 iu7-koh chit8 pai2 e5 su-kho2, lu2 lai5 lu2 kam2-kak ka-ki7 tui3 ka-ki7 e5 chian5-tou5 bang5-bang5 hai2-hai2, sim-cheng5 si7 lu2 lai5 lu2 ut-chut. Goa2 beh an2-choaN2 chiah e7-tang3 tut8-phoa3 khun3-keng2, u7 chhut-thau5-thiN e5 chit8 kang leh? Lu2 siuN7 goa2 lu2 pi-siong, sou2-i2 khah kin2 ka7 a-bu2 hou7 goa2 e5 hiuN-hoe2 tiah an5-an5, chhui3--li2 O-mi5-to5 Hut8 seh8-seh8-liam7, hi-bang7 sin5-beng5 e7-tang3 po2-pi3, ji5-chhiaN2 chi2-si7 goa2 beh an2-choaN2 kiaN5 chiah ho2. Goa2 siuN7 siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 iah-si7 ai3 kho3 ka-ki7 e5 phah-piaN3. Na7-si7 u7 hak8-bun7 koh ka-siong7 phah-piaN3, it-teng7 e7-sai2 tut8-phoa3 bak8-cheng5 e5 khun3-keng2, kiaN5-chhut chit8 tiau5 kong-beng5 e5 lou7 lai5.  M7-koh, goa2 eng-kai ai3 khi3 tui-kiu5 to2-chit8-mng5 e5 hak8-bun7 leh?  U7-chiN5-lang5 e5 kiaN2, e7-sai2 thak8 i-kho, tan7-si7 chhiuN7 goa2 chit khoan2, chham kok-tiong long2 jip8-boe7-khi3 e5 lang5, goa2 siuN7 chi2-ho2 tan2-thai7 sin5-beng5 e5 an-pai5 ah. Thou2-te7-kong, Ma2-chou2 Po5, si7-m7-si7 se3-kan sou2-u7 e5 lang5 long2 chhin-chhiuN7 tok8-ioh8 kang7-khoan2? Kam2 si7 kong2 lang5 e5 pun2-seng3 si7 sian7-liong5 leh? 
“Ui5-tek ah, goa2 ma7 ka7 lin2 a-pa3 kong2--koe3 ah, goa2 kio3 li2 lai5 goa2 e5 tiam3 si7 ui7-tioh8 beh ka7 li2 hun3-lian7 cho3 chit8 e5 u7-lou7-iong7 e5 lang5. Sou2-i2, goa2 hi-bang7 li2 e7-sai2 chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 chng-kha si5 kang7-khoan2 phah-piaN3, na7-si7 an2-ne, li2 chu7-jian5 tioh8 u7 chian5-tou5 loh8.”
Thau5-ke Li2  Ong7-hok sian-siN tui3 goa2 kong2 e5 oe7, ti7 goa2 e5 nau2-hai2 tiong phu5-chhut, i na2-chhiuN7 si7 iu2-i3 beh tiam2-chhiN2 goa2. Keng-koe3 a-pa3 peng5-iu2 e5 siau7-kai3, goa2 lai5-kau3 i e5 tiam3 chiah8-thau5-lou7, i chhin-chhiat ji5-chhiaN2 chit8 hoan kou2-le7 e5 oe7, hou7 goa2 kam2-kak chin-chiaN3 kam2-tong7, bak8-sai2 kiong7-beh lau5--chhut-lai5. M7-koh, goa2 chin kin2 tioh8 chin2-chok khi2-lai5, sui-jian5 kong2 goa2 chit khoan2 e5 piau2-hian7 si7 chng-kha gin2-a2 chin chu7-jian5, bo5-ke2-sian e5 piau2-hian7, tan7-si7 siong7-ho2 iah-si7 m7-ai3 hou7 pat8-lang5 khoaN3--tioh8, gou7-hoe7 kong2 goa2 si7 chit8 e5 ai3-khau3 e5 nng2-kha-he5 chiah ho2. Thau5-ke sui-jian5 bo5 kong2 beh ka7 goa2 hun3-lian7 cho3 tiam3-lai7 e5 keng-li2 jin5-chai5, m7-koh, i tui3 goa2 kong2 chiok che7 chin kam2-sim, chin kou2-bu2 e5 oe7. I kong2:
“Ui5-tek ah, goa2 ma7 chai-iaN2 li2 chin ai3 thak8-chheh. Lin2 lau7-pe7 u7 ka7 goa2 kong2 li2 chin siuN7-beh ke3-siok8 seng-hak8. Lang5 oah8 ti7 u7 li2-siong2、u7 bok8-piau, si7 chit8 kiaN7 chin ho2 e5 tai7-chi3. Tan7-si7, m7-chai li2 u7 ho2-ho2 siuN7--koe3 bo5? It-poaN-lang5 kong2 e5 hak8-bun5 chi2-si7 iong7 lai5 tit-tioh8 sia7-hoe7 te7-ui7 e5 chit8 chiong2 kang-ku7 nia7-nia7, si7 beh iong7 lai5 kai2-sian7 ka-ki7 e5 seng-oah8, che ia2 tioh8 si7 khai-thok pun2-sin seng-oah8 e5 chit8 chiong2 hak8-bun7. Na7 chiau3 an2-ne kong2--khi2-lai5, ki5-sit8 kah khiam7-chiN5 si7 kang7-khoan2 e5 i3-su3. Sou2-i2, goa2 ai3 li2  chu7  kin-a2-jit8  khai-si2, ho2-ho2-a2  toe2  goa2 oh8  cho3-seng-li2.  Cho3 seng-li2 tioh8-si7 chit8 chiong2 hak8-bun7, li2 kong2 kam2 m7-si7 leh? Chi3-chio2, goa2 pun2-lang5 tioh8 u7 chit khoan2 e5 keng-giam7. It-poaN leh piN3 Bun5-hoa3 un7-tong7 e5 siau2-lian5-lang5, ki5-sit8 long2-si7 chit8-koa2 bo5-leng5, bo5 siaN2 chhut-thoat e5 bi2-thang5 nia7-nia7.  Lang5 chi2-iau3 u7-chiN5, tioh8 chu7-jian5 u7 lang5 hong7 li2 ui5 sin-su7. Ji5-chhiaN2, sit8-che3 leh thui-tong7 kap chiang2-ap lan2 bun5-hoa3 hoat-tian2 e5 lang5 ma7 long2 si7 chit khoan2 lang5 leh. Sou2-i2, goa2 jin7-ui5 chi2-iau3 ho2-ho2 than3-chiN5 tioh8 e7-sai2 loh8. Goa2 pun2-lang5 ma7 si7 kan-taN tiong-hak8 pit-giap8 nia7-nia7, tan7-si7 na7-si7 u7 su-iau3, goa2 ma7-si7 e7-sai2 khai-chiN5 chhiaN2 chit8 e5 tai7-hak8 pit-giap8 e5 lang5 lai5 the3 goa2 cho3 khang-khoe3. U7 lang5 kong2, Tai5-oan5 chiN5 im kha-bak8, li2 ma7 kho2-leng5 thiaN-koe3 chit ku3 oe7? Che tioh8-si7 siong-giap8 e5 cheng-sin5, an2-ne li2 liau2-kai2 a2 bo5?”
“Si7, goa2 liau2-kai2 ah.”
Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ne kong2, tan7-si7 goa2 iah-si7 kam2-kak koai3-koai3, bo5-hoat-tou7 oan5-choan5 siong-sin3 i tui3 goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7. Sui-jian5, i kong2--e5 chu7-jian5 u7 i e5 to7-li2, tan7-si7, chhut-jip8 sia7-hoe7, kam2 chin-chiaN3 kho3 an2-ne tioh8 e7 u7 kau3 ah? Goa2 sim-lai7 u7 tam7-poh8-a2 bo5-kam-goan7, chin siuN7-beh the5-chhut bo5-kang7 e5 khoaN3-hoat lai5 chham-siong5. Tan7-si7, goa2 kiaN e5 che kan-taN si7 goa2 chit e5 chng-kha gin2-a2 thian-chin long7-ban7 e5 siuN7-hoat nia7-nia7. Thau5-ke sou2 tai7-piau2 e5 siuN7-hoat kho2-leng5 si7 chi2 ti7 sia7-hoe7 siong7 u7-thau5 u7-bin7 e5 lang5 e5 siuN7-hoat, in u7-hoat-tou7 keng-eng5 chiah-ni7-a2 toa7 e5 khi3-giap8, i sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 eng-kai si7 bo5-m7-tioh8. Goa2 u7 chiah-ni7-a2 gau5 e5 thau5-ke, au7-pai2 tng2-khi3 chng-kha si5, goa2 e7-sai2 hiong3 chng-kha  e5  chhin-chiaN5  iah-si7  peng5-iu2,  ho2-ho2-a2  chhau3-toaN7 chit8-e7 leh. Thau5-ke iah-u7 kong2, “lang5” chit e5 ji7 sui-jian5 si7 kan-taN nng7 phiat nia7-nia7, m7-koh, lan2 oah8 ti7 chit e5 se3-kan siong7, it-teng7 iah-si7 ai3 hou7-siong pang-bang5 kap hap8-chok, khah an2-choaN2 kong2, kho3 chit8 e5 lang5 siuN7-beh u7 chhut-thau5-thiN, iah-si7 chiaN5 piaN3 leh. Goa2 siuN7 i kong2 e5 koh u7 tam7-poh8-a2 to7-li2. Ti7 ou-peh8 loan7-siuN7 e5 tiong-kan, cha2-khi2 chheng-sin e5 khong-khi3 i2-keng u7 sio2-khoa2 pian3 khah lah-sap, goa2 sim7-chi3 kam2-kak heng-kham2 u7 bo5 siaN2 song2-khoai3. Na7-si7 kong2 bun5-hoa3 un7-tong7 e5 kiat-ko2, oan5-choan5 sit-pai7, tan7-si7 lan2 e5 bun5-hoa3 un7-tong7 na3-e7 iah-si7 it-tit8 hou7 sou2-ui7 e5 sin-su7 khan leh kiaN5 leh? Che tai7-khai3 tioh8-si7 lan2 it-poaN-lang5 bo5-hoat-tou7 liau2-kai2 e5 sou2-chai7 loh. U7 chit8 nng7 e5 lang5 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7 peh--khi2-lai5, goa2 ma7 kin2 toe3 in khi2--lai5, chau2-khi3 pian7-sou2. 
 Goan2 khun3 e5 sou2-chai7 si7 ti7 chhng-khou3 e5 ji7-lau5, sou2-i2, mui2 chit8 kang e5 cha2-khi2 long2 thiaN-boe7-tioh8 tu5-pang5 u7 jim7-ho5 e5 tong7-cheng7. Mui2 chit8 kang, goan2 long2 si7 seng ti7 chhng-khou3 chit peng5 se2-bin7 se2-liau2-au7, chiah toe3 seng lai5 e5 ki5-tha tiam3-goan5 khi3 thau5-ke hia chiah8-png7. It-tit8 kau3 goa2 lai5 e5 te7-saN kang, goa2 hoat-kak chit8 kiaN7 chin chhu3-bi7 e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 kam2-kak goan2 thau5-ke gia5 e5 pit tioh8 na2 chhi7 ah-a2 e5 lang5 theh8 e5 tek-ko kang7-khoan2, tiam3-lai7 e5 sou2-u7 tiam3-goan5 tioh8 na2 ah-a2 kang7-khoan2. Ah-a2 si7 toe3 chhi7 ah-a2 e5 lang5 e5 tek-ko hong-hiong3 lai5 koat-teng7 in beh khi3 to2-ui7. Che kho2-leng5 si7 goa2 chit e5 chng-kha-lang5 tui3 tou-chhi7 seng-oah8 he7 chiok toa7 e5 kia3-bang7, chiah e7 u7 chit khoan2 siuN7-hoat. Sou2-u7 e5 lang5 long2 che7 chit8 lian3 ui5 leh chiah8-png7, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 long2 si7 bo5-eng5 leh pe-png7,  che kho2-leng5 si7 lo5-tong7-chia2 e5 kui-ki2. Lo5-tong7-chia2 ai3 kin2 ka7 png7 chiah8 hou7 liau2 khi3 cho3-sit, sou2-i2, chiah8-png7 kin2 soah chu7-jian5 pian3-cho3 lo5-tong7 kai-chan3 e5 phou2-phian3 hian7-siong7.  KhoaN3-tioh8 in chiah8-png7 bo5 pou7 tioh8 it-tit8 thun-khi3 pak-tou2 lai7 hit khoan2 piaN3-si3 thun piaN3-si3 chiah8 e5 chiah8-hoat, goa2 kam2-kak che kam2 e7 kah in ti7 siong-giap8 siong7 ui7-tioh8 beh tit-tioh8 koh-khah toa7 e5 li7-ek e5 sim-thai3 si7 kang7-khoan2 leh. Lu2 siuN7 goa2 lu2 kam2-kak goa2 m7-chai beh an2-choaN2 lai5 leng2-to7 chit phoe lang5. Goa2 siuN7 goa2 si7 kah in oah8 ti7 bo5-kang7 se3-kai3 e5 lang5. Tan7-si7, goa2 ma7 liau2-kai2 thau5-ke chit8-khai-si2 tioh8 beh kio3 goa2 koan2-siau3 si7 it-phian3 ho2-i3. Tu5-liau2 koan2-siau3 i2-goa7 e5 si5-kan, goa2 ma7 kah ki5-tha e5 tiam3-goan5 kang7-khoan2, ai3 khia7 ti7 kui7-tai5 e5 au7-piah, chhiu2--li2 theh8 chit8 ki chhio, lai5 chio-hou hiam5-tang hiam5-sai e5 lang5-kheh. Goa2 kam2-kak chit khoan2 e5 khang-khoe3 sit8-chai7 m7-si7 chit8 e5 cha-pou-lang5 eng-kai cho3 e5, khia7 ti7 kui7-tai5 hia e5 hit khoan2 chu-bi7 sit8-chai7 chin oan3-khi3, iu5-ki5 si7 tu2-tioh8 lu2-seng3 e5 lang5-kheh, ai3 ti7 in bin7-thau5-cheng5 chhui3-chhio3 bak8-chhio3, tam3-thau5 soeh-to-sia7, hou7 goa2 kam2-kak chiok boe7-koan3-si3. M7-koh, goa2 chai-iaN2 bo5 koan3-si3 ma7-si7 boe7-sai2, sou2-i2, goa2 tiaN7-tiaN7 chin7-liong7 kou2-le7 goa2 ka-ki7 kong2: chin kin2 tioh8 e7 koan3-si3, goa2 eng-kai si7 e7-tang3 sek-eng3 chit khoan2 e5 khang-khoe3 chiah tioh8. Ti7 koan2-siau3-phou7 hong-bin7, goa2 ma7 tiaN7-tiaN7 ka7 chioh-chiN5 hou7 lang5 kap ka7 lang5 chioh-chiN5, chit nng7 hang7 mih8-kiaN7 chhong3 to3-peng2 khi3. Sou2-i2, goa2 tioh8-ai3 tiaN7-tiaN7 ti7 goa2 e5 thau5-khak-lai7 put-toan7 e5 lian7-sip8, hi-bang7 goa2 u7-hoat-tou7 e7-tang3 ka7 in ki3-tiau5. Goa2 siuN7 lan2 lang5 oah8 ti7 chit e5 se3-kan, na2-chhiuN7 chong2-si7 chau2 boe7 khui chioh-chiN5  hou7  lang5  kap  ka7  lang5  chioh-chiN5  chit  nng7 chiong2 kap chiN5 u7 koan-he7 e5 oah8-tong7, tan7-si7, ma7 sam-put-gou7-si5 tioh8-si7 khi3 siu7-tioh8 chit nng7 chiong2 heng5-ui5 sou2 khun3-jiau2. Sou2-ui7 chhiah-ji7 chit e5 su5, ma7 si7 goa2 lai5-kau3 Tai5-pak liau2, goa2 chiah chin-chiaN3 liau2-kai2 i e5 i3-su3. Tui3 goa2 lai5 kong2, goa2 e5 khang-khoe3 na7-si7 kan-taN su-iau3 ki3-siau3 nia7-nia7, bo5-su-iau3 khi3 eng3-hu3 lang5-kheh, goa2 siuN7 chit hun7 thau5-lou7 eng3-kai si7 chit8 hun7 khin-sang e5 thau5-lou7. M7-koh, su7-sit8 m7-si7 an2-ne, goa2 su-iau3 chio-hou lang5-kheh. Chit8 e5 bo5-chiN5 e5 chng-kha gin2-a2, tiaN7-tiaN7 ai3 kap hia u7-chiN5 u7-se3 e5 kui3-hu7-jin5 eng3-siu5, chiap-chhiok, sit8-chai7 m7-si7 goa2 e5 choan-tiong2. Kan-taN siuN7--tioh8, goa2 tioh8 thau5-khak-thiaN3. In-ui7 goa2 si7 ti7 khah san2-chhiah-lang5 e5 ka-teng5 chhut-si3, sou2-i2, goa2 chiok kan2-tan tioh8 e7-tang3 liau2-kai2 san3-chhiah-lang5 boe2 mih8-kiaN7 e5 sim-li2. Na7-si7 u7-kho2-leng5, goa2 goan7-i3 chin7-liong7 ka7 mih8-kiaN7 boe7 khah siok8 hou7 in. Tan7-si7, chit-ma2 e5 seng-li2-lang5 cho3-seng-li2 e5 sim-thai3 si7 put-koan2 si7 ho2-giah8-lang5 iah-si7 san3-chhiah-lang5, long2 beh ka7 mih8-kiaN7 e5 ke3-siau3 khui chiok koan5 hou7 in, sou2-i2 san3-chhiah-lang5 bo5-hoat-tou7 ma7-si7 ai3 khai hiah-ni7-a2 che7 chiN5 lai5 ka7 in boe2. Ma7 si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, goa2 long2 chin7-liong7 phiah-khui chiap-thai7 lang5-kheh e5 khang-khoe3. Goan2 e5 tiam3 mui2 ko3 goeh8 long2 ai3 ku2-pan7 chit8 kai3 e5 cho7-tam5-hoe7. Chit e5 cho7-tam5 hoe7 e5 bok8-tek, siong7 chu2-iau3 peng7-m7-si7 beh thiaN thau5-ke kong2 chit8-koa2 gou7-si3-saN iah-si7 lian5-lok8 tiam3-lai7 tak8-ke e5 kam2-cheng5, siong7 chu2-iau3 si7 beh hou7 gun2 u7 ki-hoe7 lai5 hak8-sip8 chit8-koa2 siong-giap8 siong7 e5 kho3-teng5. Thau5-ke ti7 ou-pang teng2-koan5 toa7-toa7 sia2 siong-giap8 chu2-gi7 kui2 e5 toa7-ji7, chin jin7-chin kai2-soeh siaN2-mih si7  chu-pun2  chu2-gi7  hou7  gun2  liau2-kai2.  Tan7-si7, goa2 iah-si7 thiaN-liau2 bu7-sa3-sa3. Tau3-te2 si7 thau5-ke i kong2-liau2 bo5-chheng-chho2 leh? Iah-si7 goa2 ka-ki7 hak8-sek bo5 kau3? M7-koh, goa2 ka-ki7 chai-iaN2 goa2 khiam3-khoat sin3-sim si7 chin-chiaN3 e5. KhoaN3-tioh8 piN-a2 ki5-tha e5 lang5, ui3 lu2-tiam3-goan5 kau3 sin ui5 lau7-kou2-tong e5 keng-li2, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 long2 tiam7-chih-chih, chin jin7-chin leh thiaN. 
“In-ui7 tioh8-si7 an2-ne, sou2-i2, lan2 tui3-thai7 lang5-kheh it-teng7 ai3 u7 le2-mau7. Che choat8-tui3 m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 beh tui-kiu5 li7-ek nia7-nia7, i si7 chit8 chiong2 sia7-hoe7 su-siong2. E7-tang3 iong7 lai5 cho3-ui5 lan2 cho3-lang5 e5 chit8 e5 ki-pun2 e5 to7-li2.”
Goa2 si7 chiok bo5-kan2-tan chiah siuN7-chhut chit e5 kiat-lun5, tan7-si7, goa2 iah-si7 bo5-hoat-tou7 liau2-kai2, ui7-tioh8 beh pa-kiat lang5-kheh, keng2-jian5 u7 chiah-ni7-a2 toa7 e5 hak8-bun7 kap su-siong2 ti7 lai7-te2. Sui-jian5, goa2 u7 ui3 chit lai7-te2 liau2-kai2 khah che7 sia7-hoe7 e5 hian7-siong7, cheng7-ka khah che7 e5 kian3-sek. Tan7-si7 beh kio3 goa2 ke-chhui3 pian3 ah-chhui3, tong-jian5 goa2 iah-si7 cho3-boe7-kau3. Ti7 goa2 e5 se3-kai3 tiong, goa2 jin7-ui5 li2-lun7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 m7-bian2 siuN che7, chi2-iau3 e7-tang3 tui3 lang5 u7 seng5-i3, an2-ne tioh8 e7-sai2 ah. Na7-si7 kan-taN beh iong7 pa-kiat, ou-peh8 loan7 po lai5 hong7-thai7 lang5-kheh e5 si5-chun7, goa2 kam2-kak che kah sam-chhit-a2 u7 siaN2-mih bo5-kang7 leh? Lau2-sit8 kong2, goa2 chin bo5 siuN7-beh ke3-siok8 lau5 ti7 tiam3-lai7 cho3--loh8-khi3. Goa2 tak8-kang long2 leh pai3-thok ThiN-kong-peh, hi-bang7 i e7-tang3 hou7 goa2 ti7 gin5-hang5 iah-si7 ti7 ki5-tha kong-si chhoe7-tioh8 thau5-lou7. Thau5-ke sui-jian5 it-tit8 kou2-le7 goa2 ai3 jin7-chin thak8-chheh, tan7-si7, goa2 m7-chai beh an2-choaN2 thak8 khi2, goa2 kan-taN e7-tang3 ti7 ki3-siau3 liau2, u7-eng5 e5 si5-kan, thak8 chit8-koa2 po3-choa2 nia7-nia7. Tiam3-lai7 u7 kui-teng7, siong7-pan si5-kan boe7-sai2 khoaN3-chheh, na3 u7 kho2-leng5 e7-sai2 jin7-chin thak8-chheh leh? Goa2 bo5-hoat-tou7, chi2-ho2 sia2-phoe hou7 a-pa3 kap a-nia5, kap in chham-siong5. Goa2 kong2 goa2 kam-goan7 tng2-khi3 chng-kha cho3 khah chhou-siok8 e5 khang-khoe3, na7-si7 an2-ne, goa2 iah-u7 si5-kan thak8 chit8-koa2 chheh. Ji5-chhiaN2, a-bu2 e5 sin-the2 ma7 bo5 siaN2 kian7-khong. Ki3-jian5 it-sim beh chhut-lai5 chiah8-thau5-lou7, tioh8 eng-kai chhoe7 khah u7 i3-gi7 e5 khang-khoe3 cho3 chiah tioh8. Goan2 chhu3--li2 m7-si7 u7 chu-pun2 e5 u7-chiN5 ka-teng5, ka2-su2 na7-si7 u7 chu-pun2 lai5 kong2, koh lai5 pou3-liau7 e5 lai5-goan5 si7 iong7 phoe3-kip e5. Sou2-i2, goa2 e5 chiong-lai5 si7 bo5-kho2-leng5 cho3 pou3-tiam3 e5 thau5-ke. Na7-si7 an2-ne kong2, goa2 sit8-chai7 bo5-su-iau3 ti7 chia ke3-siok8 long7-hui3 si5-kan ah. A-pa3, goa2 ma7 bo5-goan7-i3 kong2, goa2 ti7 su7-giap8 siong7 iah-boe7 seng5-kong chin3-cheng5 tioh8 an2-ne tng2-khi3 lan2 chng-kha. Ui7-tioh8 beh kou3-choan5 thau5-ke e5 bin7-chu2, goa2 siuN7 goa2 eng-kai seng tng2-khi3 chng-kha, tan2 leng7-goa7 chhoe7-tioh8 thau5-lou7 liau2-au7 chiah-koh khi2-lai5 Tai5-pak. Goa2 sia2-phoe hou7 a-pa3 su-iau3 iong7 Han3-ji7 lai5 sia2, ui7-tioh8 beh sia2 chit tiuN phoe, khai-liau2 goa2 chiok che7 cheng-sin5.  Ho2-ka-chai3 kong-hak8-hau7 pit-giap8 liau2, ti7 goa7-khau2 leh chiah8-thau5-lou7 e5 si5-chun7, mui2 chit8 am3, goa2 long2 kah a-pa3 oh8 thak8 Lun5-gi2, sou2-i2, chit-ma2 tu2-a2-ho2 e7-sai2 iong7-tioh8. Koe3-khi3, goa2 ma7 chhi3 iong7 Han3-ji7 sia2 koe3 phoe, goan5-te2 si7 ui7-tioh8 chhu3-piN keh-piah e7 lai5 pai3-thok goa2 the3 in sia2-phoe, goa2 kiaN goa2 e7 lau3-khui3, sou2-i2 tek8-piat ka-kiong5 khi3 oh8 e5, siuN7-boe7-kau3 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7  koh  chin-chiaN3  u7-lou7-iong7 ah.  M7-koh,  a-pa3  sia2-phoe lai5 kong2, a-bu2 khoaN3-tioh8 goa2 sia2 e5 phoe tui3 goa2 u7 chit khoan2 e5 siuN7-hoat kam2-kak chiok sit-bong7.  A-pa3 kong2, u7 chit8 ku3 siok8-gi2, chit8 li2 thong tioh8 ban7 su7 kai2, chi2-iau3 e7-tang3 oan5-oan5 choan5-choan5 liau2-kai2 chit8 hang7 tai7-chi3 e5 to7-li2, ki5-tha sou2-u7 e5 tai7-chi3 chha-put-to ma7-si7 e7-tang3 liau2-kai2. Sou2-i2, in hi-bang7 goa2 ki3-jian5 i2-keng leh siong7-pan, tioh8 ai3 the5-chhut nai7-sim kap i3-chi3, chin-chiaN3 lai5 oh8 chit8-koa2 mih8-kiaN7, khip-siu jin5-seng e5 keng-giam7 chiah tioh8.  A-pa3 tioh8 an2-ne kong2 ah, goa2 iah e7-tang3 an2-choaN2 leh? Chi2-ho2 bin7-phoe5 kek khah kau7 leh, iong2-kam2 lai5 bin7-tui3 hian7-sit8 ah. Goa2 siuN7 goa2 chi2-iau3 ti7 chia ka7 ki3-siau3 e5 ki-chhou2 phah hou7 ho2, oh8 e7-hiau2, goa2 au7-pai2 khi3 siaN2-mih sou2-chai7 long2 e7-sai2 cho3 koan2-siau3 e5 khang-khoe3. Sou2-i2, goa2 koat-sim beh ke3-siok8 lau5 ti7 chia ho2-ho2-a2 phah-piaN3, goa2 e5 bok8-tek8 peng7-m7-si7 chun2-pi7 chiong-lai5 beh cho3 toa7-thau5-ke, goa2 chi2-put-ko3 si7 siuN7-beh chhiong-sit8 goa2 ka-ki7 nia7-nia7. Sou2-i2, goa2 chiam7-si5 ka7 sim siu--tng2-lai5, choan-sim lai5 cho3 goa2 tiam3-goan5 ki3-siau3 e5 khang-khoe3. 
Tioh8 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7, goa2 keng3-jian5 khi3 ai3-tioh8 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2.  Kho2-leng5 in-ui7 goa2 ai3 koan2-siau3 ji5-chhiaN2 ai3 kou3-tiam3, sou2-i2, goa2 e5 kin7-si7 ma7 lu2 lai5 lu2 chhim. Mui2 chit8 kang cho3 tai7-chi3 e5 si5-chun7, goa2 long2 it-teng7 ai3 koa3 bak8-kiaN3 chiah khoaN3 u7 mih8-kiaN7. Goa2 e5 bin7-sek pun2-te2 tioh8 khah peh8, peng5-siong5-si5, goa2 ma7 bo5 chhiuN7 ki5-tha e5 tiam3-goan5 kang7-khoan2 hiah-ni7-a2 ai3 kong2-chhio3. Sou2-i2, in ka7 goa2 ho7 chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 ‘lau7-kou2-tong2’. Goa2 chit e5 lau7-kou2-tong2 e7 khi3 tam5 loan5-ai3, ki5-sit8 si7 in-ui7 goa2 khi3 tu2-tioh8  goa2  se3-han3  si5-chun7  toa3  ti7  Tai5-pak  chhu3-piN  e5  cha-bou2 gin2-a2. Goan2 tau goan5-pun2 toa3 ti7 Kang2-tou, ti7 goa2 chhit hoe3 e5 hit tang, a-pa3 thiaN-tioh8 chng-kha chit8 e5 peng5-iu2 e5 khou2-khng3, i kong2, ti7 tou-chhi7 lai7 leh chau2-chong5 siuN7-beh u7 chhut-thau5-thiN e5 chit8 kang, tan7-si7, koh bo5 pa2-ak e5 si5-chun7, put-ju5 tng2-lai5 chng-kha hoat-tian2 ma7 khah u7 chian5-tou5. In-ui7 an2-ne, gun2 tau tioh8 ui3 Tai5-pak poaN-tng2-khi3 chng-kha, boe2 chit8 te3 thou2-te7, khai-si2 keng-eng5 chit8 keng phah-chhek-a2 e5 tiam3. Tan7-si7, ma7 bo5 in-ui7 an2-ne than3-tioh8 toa7 chiN5. Thau5-tu2-a2 kong2-tioh8 e5 hit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, tioh8-si7 lau5 ti7 goa2 ki3-ek tiong e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, i e5 mia5 kio3 A-lan5. 
Goa2 te7-it kai2 tu2-tioh8 A-lan5 si7 ti7 u7 chit8 tang e5 chiaN--goeh8, A-lan5 chheng7 chit8 su chin sui2 e5 saN, beh khi3 chiap ke3-chhut-khi3 goa7-te7 e5 a-chi2 tng2-lai5 chhu3--li2. A-lan5 chheng7 hiah-ni7-a2 sui2 e5 saN, ji5-chhiaN2 koh che7 kio7 e5 koan-he7, goan5-pun2 tioh8 chin u7 toa7-sio2-chia2 khi3-phai3 e5 A-lan5, khoaN3--khi2-lai5 koh-khah sui2, koh-khah iau-kiau. Goa2 kap chit8 tin7 kau5-gin2-a2 khia7 ti7 A-lan5 in tau mng5-kha-khau2 leh tau3-lau7-jiat8. KhoaN3-tioh8 A-lan5 hiah-ni7-a2 sui2, goa2 khoaN3 kau3 chhui3-khui-khui, chhui3-noa7 kiong5-beh lau5--chhut-lai5. Tioh8 ti7 hit-tang-si5, ki5-tiong u7 chit8 e5 gin2-a2 thiau-kang beh khi-hu7 A-lan5, i toa7-siaN hoah kong2 A-lan5 beh chhut-ke3 loh, A-lan5 beh cho3 sin-niu5 loh. Goa2 kan-taN khoaN3-tioh8 A-lan5 bak8-thau5 kat-kat, bo5 siaN2 hoaN-hi2. ‘Phui3’ chit8 siaN, chhui3-noa7 tioh8 hiong3 gun2 chit peng5 phui3--koe3-lai5. Sou2-u7 e5 gin2-a2 long2 chau2-liau2-liau2, A-lan5 e5 chhui3-noa7 tu2-a2-ho2 phui3 ti7 goa2 e5 bin7 teng2-koan5. Tan2 goa2 hoat-hian7 hoat-seng siaN2-mih tai7-chi3 i2-keng boe7-hu3 ah. Sou2-i2, goa2 chi2-ho2 bo5-siaN2 song2-khoai3 khau3-bin7 khau3-bin7 tng2-khi3 chhu3--li2. Kau3 chhu3 liau2-au7, goa2 koh kiaN a-pa3 si7-m7-si7 u7 khoaN3-tioh8 tu2-a2 hoat-seng e5 tai7-chi3. A-lan5 e5 thau5-mng5 pak8 liau2 na2-chhiuN7 chhan5-ne khoan2, i e5 bin7 chiok kou2-chui, na2-chhiuN7 ang-a2. Tan7-si7, i hoat phi5-khi3 e5 si5-chun7 keng3-jian5 si7 hiah-ni7-a2 chhiah. Sui-jian5 i si7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, khoaN3-tioh8 i hit khoan2 chhiah-pe5-pe5 e5 bin7, goa2 ma7 kiong5-beh tong3-boe7-tiau5 siuN7-beh ka7 phah. Tioh8 an2-ne goa2 iah bo5 ki-hoe7 kah A-lan5 kong2 tioh8 oe7, gun2 tioh8 kui-ke-hoe2 poaN-khi3 chng-kha ah. Khi3 chng-kha liau2-au7, goa2 ma7 tiaN7-tiaN7 e7 khi3 siuN7-tioh8 A-lan5. I2-au7 chiam7-chiam7 toa7-han3, goa2 chiah liau2-kai2 si7 an2-choaN2 A-lan5 e7 hiah-ni7-a2 siu7-khi3, i siu7-khi3 m7-si7 bo5 goan5-in e5. A-lan5 chu7 se3-han3 tioh8 si7 chit8 e5 cha2-sek8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, i it-tit8 jin7-ui5 kong2, in tau sou2-u7 e5 chai5-san2 kap ke-si eng3-kai long2-si7 i e5 mih8-kiaN7. Ji5-chhiaN2, i choat8-tui3 bo5 beh ke3-khi3 pat8 e5 sou2-chai7. I tui3 i e5 a-bu2 kong2, chit e5 chhu3 si7 i e5, eng3-kai chhut-khi3 e5 si7 i e5 a-hiaN. A-tek ah, li2 khoaN3 leh, i chit khoan2 e5 ko3-seng3 u7 siaN2-lang5 kaN2 chhoa7 i cho3 bou2 leh? 
A-lan5 e5 lau7-bu2 hi-bang7 goa2 e7-tang3 the3 i kong2 kui2 ku3 oe7, tan7-si7 goa2 m7-chai beh an2-choaN2 kong2 khah ho2, kan-taN e7-tang3 gong7-gong7 khia7 ti7 hia, m7-chai beh ju5-ho5 si7 ho2. Ko3-seng3 hiah-ni7-a2 kiong5 e5 A-lan5 khi3 hou7 lang5 kong2 beh chhut-ke3, tong-jian5 si7 e7 siu7-khi3. Chi2-si7 hit-tong-si5 khoaN3-tioh8 A-lan5 kou2-chui e5 heng5-iaN2, goa2 khoaN3 kah gong7--khi3, sou2-i2, m7-chiah e7 siam2-phiah boe7-hu3, nge7-si2 khi3 hou7 chhui3-noa7 phui3--tioh8. Ma7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, goa2 chiah e7 tiaN7-tiaN7 khi3 siuN7-tioh8 A-lan5. Ti7 chng-kha, chhiuN7 goa2 chit khoan2 e5 siau2-lian5-lang5 chu7-jian5 u7 chiaN5 che7 moe5-lang5-po5 e7 lai5 goan2 tau the5-chhin, tan7-si7 goa2 liau2-kai2, na7 bo5 chhiuN7 A-lan5 hit khoan2 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, goa2 si7 boe7 chhin3-chhai2 tap-eng3 e5.
Sui-jian5 kong2 goa2 si7 hiah-ni7-a2 su-liam7 A-lan5, tan7-si7 goan2 nng7 lang5 ki3-jian5 tioh8 i2-keng hun-khui ti7 bo5-kang7 e5 sou2-chai7, beh koh u7 tau3-tin7 e5 ki-hoe7 si7 bo5 siaN2 hi-bang7. Chit-ma2 i2-keng m7-si7 chit8 e5 long7-ban7 e5 si5-tai7 ah, ti7 chit e5 i2 kim-chiN5 ui5 chu2 e5 sia7-hoe7, i2 A-lan5 chit khoan2 tiau5-kiaN7 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2, chha-put-to i2-keng hou7 to2 chit8 e5 sou2-chai7, ho2-giah8-lang5 e5 kiaN2 phaN7--khi3 ah. Sou2-i2, goa2 lau2-cha2 tioh8 bo5 koh leh siau3-liam7 u7-kho2-leng5 e7-tang3 kah A-lan5 tau3-tin7. Ma7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, goa2 lai5-kau3 Tai5-pak liau2-au7, sui-jian5 u7 siuN7-tioh8 kou2-cha2 si5-tai7 e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 peng7 bo5 tek8-piat khi3 siuN7-tioh8 A-lan5. M7-koh, m7-chai si7 ho2-mia7 iah-si7 phaiN2-mia7, goa2 hoai5-gi5 A-lan5 tioh8 ti7 goan2 tiam3 keh-piah e5 chap8-hoe3-tiam3 leh chiah8-thau5-lou7. Mui2 chit8 kang e5 cha2-khi2, goa2 ti7 tiam3 thau5-cheng5 e5 teng5-a2-kha leh sau3-thou5-kha si5, goa2 long2 e7 khi3 khoaN3-tioh8 keh-piah tiam3 chit8 e5 na2-chhiuN7 A-lan5 e5 lu2-tiam3-goan5, hai7 goa2 it-tit8 siuN7-beh chai-iaN2, tau3-te2 hit e5 si7-m7-si7 A-lan5 leh? Tioh8 ti7 goa2 mui2 chit8 kang ou-peh8 loan7-siuN7 e5 jit8-chi2 tiong, u7 chit8 kang cha2-khi2, hou7 goa2 khi3 tu2-tioh8 A-lan5 kap in ma-ma lai5 gun2 tiam3 boe2 mih8-kiaN7. Hiong5-hiong5 khoaN3-tioh8 in, goa2 ma7 bo5 siuN7 hiah che7, chhong-chhong pong7-pong7 tioh8 sau3-chhiu2 theh8 leh, chau2-khi3 mng7 kong2, li2 si7-m7-si7 A-lan5? Ah li2 si7 a-m2 tioh8 m7? Goa2 si7 Ui5-tek la. Kou2-cha2 toa3 ti7 lin2 keh-piah Ong5 Chin3-san e5 hau7-seN Ui5-tek la. Hou7 goa2 kiaN-chit8-e7, A-lan5 e5 lau2-bu2 piau2-hian7 chhut chit hu3 boe7-tang3 siong-sin3 e5 piau2-cheng5, gang7 ti7 hia. SiuN7 kui poaN3 pou chiah hou7 i siuN7-tioh8, chhio3 kong2, ah, li2 si7 A-tek houhN. I2-keng hiah toa7-han3 ah, chha-put-to boe7-jin7-chit ah. Goa2 ma7 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7 chiah khi3 siuN7-tioh8, thau5-tu2-a2 ka-ki7 chhong-pong7 e5 heng5-ui5 sit8-chai7 si7 chiok phaiN2-se3. 
A-lan5 kap in lau2-bo2 peng7 bo5 tek8-piat piau2-hian7 chhut chiok hoaN-hi2 e5 piau2-cheng5 iah-si7 tui3 koe3-khi3 chiok hoai5-liam7 e5 sim-cheng5. Goa2 siuN7, tou-chhi7 e5 lang5 si7 boe7 it-tit8 oah8 ti7 koe3-khi3. Chhiu2 theh8-tioh8 sau3-chhiu2, goa2 soah kam2-kak m7-chai beh ju5-ho5 si7 ho2.

Goa2 khoaN3-tioh8 A-lan5 peh8-iu3-iu3 e5 chhiu2, ki2-tioh8 chha-put-to chit8 e5 lang5 chheng2-chaiN2 toa7 hou7 hoe2 sio--koe3 e5 khang.“Ui7-siaN2-mih lang5 beh sio li2 e5 hou7-soaN3 leh?”
Goa2 na2 mng7, ma7 na2 liau2-kai2 chit ki hou7-soaN3 tui3 A-lan5 e5 tiong7-iau3-seng3. Ui3 A-lan5 in tau e5 keng-che3 chong7-hong2 lai5 khoaN3, A-lan5 chiah8-thau5-lou7 ma7 i2-keng si3-gou7-tang ah. Tan7-si7, i e5 sin-sui2 ma7 chiah chit8 ko3 goeh8 chap8-peh khou nia7-nia7. I beh boe2 chit8 ki tat8 chap8-ji7, chap8-saN khou e5 hou7-soaN3 peng7-m7-si7 hiah-ni7-a2 kan2-tan e5 tai7-chi3. Boe2-tioh8 siok8 mih8-kiaN7, iong7 bo5 nng7 kai2 tioh8 phaiN2--khi3, sou2-i2, i khiam7 poaN3 tang e5 chiN5 lai5 boe2 chit8 ki khah ho2 e5 hou7-soaN3. Iong7-tioh8 chiah ho2 e5 hou7-soaN3, i tong-jian5 si7 e7 theh8-chhut-lai5 tian2 hou7 chhu3-piN keh-piah e5 lang5 khoaN3. Goa2 e7-tang3 liau2-kai2 i theh8 hou7 in khoaN3 hit khoan2 hoaN-hi2 e5 sim-cheng5. Sui-jian5 si7 kan-taN sio chit8 khang se3-se3 khang-a2, tui3 A-lan5 lai5 kong2 kho2-leng5 si7 sio-tioh8 i e5 te7-ji7 seN3-mia7 kang7-khoan2. Goa2 kam2-kak chit e5 sio A-lan5 hou7-soaN3 e5 lang5 sit8-chai7 si7 kho2-ou3, chin siuN7-beh the3 A-lan5 chhut chit8-khau2 oan3-khi3. 
“Ka7 goa2 hou7-soaN3 sio chit8 khang e5 lang5 ma7 m7-si7 thiau-kang e5 la. U7 chit8 e5 lu2-tiam3-goan5 ka7 goa2 kong2, thau5-ke chiah8-hun e5 si5-chun7, bo5 chu3-i3 khi3 ka7 iah-boe7 hoa--khi3 e5 hoan-a2-hoe2 lak-jip8-khi3 goa2 e5 hou7-soaN3. Sou2-i2, chiah e7 chin phaiN2-un7 khi3 sio-tioh8 chit8 khang.”
“Goan5-lai5 si7 an2-ne ou, sui-jian5 kong2 i m7-si7 thiau-kang, tan7-si7, i kam2 ka-ki7 long2 m7-chai-iaN2 leh?”
“I u7 khoaN3-tioh8 tong-jian5 si7 chai-iaN2 chiah tioh8.”
“Ho2 loh8 la, A-lan5, goa2 siuN7 li2 tioh8-ai3 khoaN3 khah khui leh. U7 si5-chun7, chit khoan2 tai7-chi3 li2 ka7 tong3-cho3 si7 li2 khah soe, e7-tang3 an2-ne siuN7, li2 e7 kam2-kak khah ho2-koe3 lah.”
Ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7, goa2 e7-tang3 oan5-choan5 liau2-kai2 si7 an2-choaN2 A-lan5 e7 hiah-ni7-a2 siong-sim. Chit e5 pi-kiok8 e5 hoat-seng, chu2-iau3 e5 goan5-in si7 in-ui7 thau5-ke khoaN3--tioh8 e5 hit khang kap chit e5 lu2-tiam3-goan5 sou2 khoaN3--tioh8 e5 hit khang sou2 tai7-piau2 e5 i3-gi7 si7 bo5-kang7 e5. Ui7-tioh8 hou7-soaN3 khi3 hou7 sio chit8 khang tioh8 hiah-ni7-a2 siong-sim, che khiong2-kiaN e5 m7-si7 ho2-giah8-lang5 e7-tang3 liau2-kai2 e5 tai7-chi3. Ka2-su2 na7 u7 siuN7--tioh8, ma7 bo5-kho2-leng5 goan7-i3 ui7-tioh8 chiah8 chit8 ki hun lai5 poe5 chit8 e5 lu2-tiam3-goan5 chap8-ji7-saN khou. 
“A-lan5, m7-thang ui7-tioh8 hou7-soaN3 e5 tai7-chi3 koh siu7-khi3 loh8. Chit8 ki hou7-soaN3 peng7 bo5-hoat-tou7 lai5 tai7-piau2 chit8 e5 lang5. Chu7-chiong5 goa2 lai5-kau3 Tai5-pak liau2-au7, goa2 chiah liau2-kai2, goa2 sou2 chun-keng3 e5 lang5 si7 kha tah8 sit8 te7 e5 lang5, peng7-m7-si7 hit khoan2 chheng7-saN chheng7 kah sui2-sui2-sui2 e5 lang5. Tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 oh8 pat8-lang5 si7 bo5-hoat-tou7 lai5 piau2-hian7-chhut chit8 e5 lang5 e5 ko3-seng3. A-lan5, li2 kho2-leng5 ma7 chai-iaN2, tiam3-lai7 e5 lang5 long2 kio3 goa2 lau7-kou2-tong2. M7-koh, goa2 kin-ni5 ma7 chiah tu2-a2 ji7-chap8 hoe3. Goa2 na7 bo5 ki3 m7-tioh8 li2 kin-ni5 si7 chap8-kau2 hoe3 hou7?”
A-lan5 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7 ma7 i2-keng bo5 leh khau3 ah. I kiaN5-chhut pang5-keng, goa2 ma7 toe3 i kiaN5--chhut-lai5, che7 ti7 tu2-a2 poaN--lai5 e5 i2-a2. 
“A-lan5, se3-han3 e5 si5-chun7, li2 phui3-chhui3-noa7 ti7 goa2 e5 bin7 teng2-koan5 chit kiaN7 tai7-chi3, li2 iah e7-ki3-tit bo5? Hit e5 si5-chun7, li2 si7 chit e5 ko3-seng3 chin kiong5 e5 lang5.”
A-lan5 kong2: “Kam2 u7 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3, goa2 na3 e7 long2 boe7-ki3-tit leh?” I piau2-hian7-chhut chin bo5-hoat-tou7 siong-sin3 e5 piau2-
cheng5, m7-koh, i e5 bin7 i2-keng u7 bi5-bi5-a2 leh chhio3 ah. A-lan5 the3 goa2 chiong te5-poe-a2 thin5 te5 thin5 hou7 tiN7. 
“Goa2 chin hoai5 liam7 lan2 se3-han3 si5-chun7 e5 tai7-chi3. Tan7-si7, goa2 chit-ma2 tioh8 tih-beh khi3 cho3-peng ah. Sou2-i2, goa2 thiau-kang lai5 lin2 tau hiong3 lin2 ko3-si5. Sui-jian5, cheng3-sek jip8-khi3 cho3-peng eng3-kai si7 ti7 me5-ni5 e5 tai7-chi3, m7-koh, goa2 beh seng tng2-khi3 chng-kha ho2-ho2 toan3-lian7 goa2 e5 sin-the2. Na7-si7 ke3-siok8 lau5 ti7 Tai5-pak, khiong2-kiaN goa2 e5 sin-the2 e7 lu2 lai5 lu2 chha, lu2 lai5 lu2 bo5-lou7-iong7. Ti7 chia, lah-sap e5 khong-khi3, ut-chut e5 tai7-chi3 kui-toa7-tui, sim-cheng5 it-tit8 bo5 kong2 kai3 ho2.”
Kong2 kau3 chia, goa2 hoat-hian7 chhu3-piN keh-piah toa3 tau3-tin7 e5 a-po5, long2 ti7 piN-a2 leh khoaN3 ho2-hi3 khoan2, thau-thau-a2 leh kong2 se3-siaN-oe7. A-lan5 e5 sim-cheng5 i2-keng chin loan7 ah, goa2 bo5 goan7-i3 koh in-ui7 goa2 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 hou7 i chhau-hoan5. Sou2-i2, goa2 siuN7 goa2 tiam7-tiam7 lai5 thiaN i kong2 siaN2-mih, ma7 ho2 ke liau2-kai2 A-lan5 e5 tai7-chi3. A-lan5 thiaN-liau2 goa2 e5 oe7 ma7 na2-chhiuN7 siu7-tioh8 kam2-tong7 e5 khoaN2, i e5 bak8-chiu it-tit8 khoaN3 thou5-kha, na2-chhiuN7 si7 leh hoai5-liam7 koe3-khi3 e5 tai7-chi3. I e5 bin7-sek ma7 ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7 pian3-liau2 khah ho2-khoaN3, i e5 bak8-chiu ma7 sam-put-gou7-si5 e5 khoaN3 ui3 goa2 chia lai5. 
“Goa2 bo5 goan7-i3 ke3-siok8 koe3 chit khoan2 e5 jit8-chi2 ah. Chit-ma2 tu2-a2-ho2 u7 chit8 e5 chin ho2 e5 ki-hoe7 lai5 kai2-pian3. Ka2-su2 goa2 e5 i3-goan7 na7 u7-hoat-tou7 lai5 sit8-hian7, na7 an2-ne lau7-kou2-tong2 tioh8 e7 pian3-cho3 a-peng-ko loh.”
“Na7 an2-ne tioh8 chiok ho2 ah. Goa2 e7 kia3 mih8-kiaN7 khi3 hou7 li2.”
“A-lan5, li2 m7-bian2 kia3 mih8-kiaN7 lai5 hou7 goa2, li2 chi2-iau3 u7 si5-kan sia2-phoe lai5 hou7 goa2 tioh8 e7-sai2 loh.”
Chit8-si5-kan, goa2 na2-chhiuN7 u7 hit chiong2 i2-keng khi3 cho3-peng e5 sim-cheng5 kang7-khoan2, bak8-sai2 si7 kiong5-beh lau5--chhut-lai5, oe7 ma7 kiong5-beh kong2 boe7 loh8-khi3. 
“Ui5-tek hiaN, li2 chin liau2-put-khi2, si7 an2-choaN2 lang5 kio3 li2 lau7-kou2-tong2 li2 long2 boe7 siu7-khi3 leh?”
“Goa2 na7-si7 chin-chiaN3 siu7-khi3, goa2 m7 tioh8 ai3 kah lang5 oan-ke? Sou2-i2, goa2 siuN7-beh iong7 si5-tiau7 thau5-lou7 lai5 hou7 tak8-ke liau2-kai2 goa2 e5 iong2-khi3. Hia e5 long2 si7 chit8-koa2 m7-kaN2 si5-thau5-lou7 e5 nng2-kha-he5. Ka2-su2, goa2 na7 e7-tang3 ti7 tak8-ke e5 bin7-thau5-cheng5 the5-chhut si5-chit, goa2 siuN7 che it-teng7 si7 chit8 kiaN7 chin chhu3-bi7 e5 tai7-chi3.”
“Kong2 e5 ma7 si7.”
“A-lan5, goa2 leng7-kang chiah koh lai5 pai3-hong2 li2 ho2 ah. Kin-a2-jit8 i2-keng chin oaN3 ah, chiah chhiaN2 li2 the3 goa2 hiong3 a-m2 mng7 an.”
“Iah cha2 leh ma7.”
“Goa2 siuN7 goa2 iah si7 leng7-kang chiah-koh lai5 pai3-hong2 ho2 ah.”
Goa2 ui3 i2-a2 khia7--khi2-lai5, khoaN3-tioh8 A-lan5 e5 bak8-chiu leh khoaN3 goa2, goa2 e5 bin7 na2-chhiuN7 ang5--khi2-lai5. SiuN7-tioh8 goa2 iah u7 chiok che7 oe7 siuN7-beh kong2, tan7-si7, kan-taN kong2 bo5 chit8-poaN3 nia7-nia7, goa2 na2 khi3 hou7 lang5 ui3 au7-piah-bin7 ka7 goa2 khian-tiau5 leh kang7-khoan2. 
“Ui5-tek hiaN, li2 na7 chin-chiaN3 khi3 cho3-peng, goa2 it-teng7 e7 sia2-phoe hou7 li2.”
“Ho2, goa2 chiah tan2 li2 e5 phoe.”
Goa2 ui3 A-lan5 in tau kiaN5--chhut-lai5, chiap-chhiok-tioh8 goa7-khau2 chheng-liang5 e5 khong-khi3, goa2 na2-chhiuN7 kam2-siu7-tioh8 A-lan5 leh sia2-phoe hou7 goa2 e5 hit chiong2 kam2-kak. A-lan5 na7 chin-chiaN3 sia2-phoe hou7 goa2, goa2 ma7 iah-si7 ka-ki7 chit8 e5 lang5, siuN7-tioh8 chia, goa2 u7 chit8 chiong2 kong2-boe7-chhut e5 siok-bok8 ti7 sim-koaN-lai7. Ka7 A-lan5 chit8 e5 lang5 pang3 ti7 chhu3--li2, goa2 tui3 A-lan5 e5 ka-chok8 ma7 u7 kam2-kak tioh8 put-boan2. Gia5-thau5 khoaN3-tioh8 moa2 thiN e5 liu5-chhiN, goa2 phah-sng3 beh ian5 khe-a2-piN kiaN5-tng2-khi3 sok-sia2. Chit e5 si5-chun7, goa2 u7 chit8 chiong2 siuN7-beh pang3-sat it-chhe3, khai-si2 goa2 leng7-goa7 chit8 chiong2 bo5-kang7 e5 jin5-seng e5 chhiong-tong7. M7-chai si7 an2-choaN2, goa2 keng3-jian5 siuN7-beh kiaN5 ti7 Tam7-chui2-ho5 piN, toa7-siaN chhiuN3-chhut kun-tui7 tiong tiaN7-tiaN7 leh chhiuN3 e5 chin3-heng5-khek. Tut8-jian5-kan, goa2 hoat-hian7 tioh8 goa2 e5 koa-siaN si7 hiah-ni7-a2 kau7, hiah-ni7-a2 iong2.
