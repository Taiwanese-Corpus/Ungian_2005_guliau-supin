Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 The5-Mou5-Thai3 E5 Phe
(Chian5 su)
	
Te7 1 ChiuN

	1) Goa2 si7 Po2-lo5, hong7 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Siong7-chu2 kap lan2 e5 hi-bong7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 beng7-leng7 lai5 cho3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 Su3-tou5,  2) sia2 phe hou7 chai7 sin3-giong2-siong7 cho3 goa2 e5 sit8-chai7 e5 kiaN2 e5 The5-mou5-thai3. Goan7 Thian-hu7 Siong7-chu2 kap lan2 e5 Chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou siuN2-su3 li2 in-hui7, jin5-chu5 kap peng5-an.

Keng2-kai3 ke2 e5 to7-li2:
	3) Goa2 boeh khi3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 si5, goa2 u7 chhiaN2 li2 koh ke3-siok8 toa3 I2-hut-sou2, hou7 li2 thang beng7-leng7 hit kui2 e5 lang5, m7-thang koh ka3-si7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 to7-li2,  4) ia7 m7-thang koh koan-sim bo5-iaN2-bo5-jiah e5 thoan5-soat kap lo3-lo3-tng5 e5 chok8-phou2, in-ui7 che put-tan7 be7 pang-chan7 lang5 li2-kai2 Siong7-chu2 e5 ke3-oe7, tian-to3 e7 in2-khi2 cheng-lun7. I e5 ke3-oe7 u7 sin3-sim chiah e7-tang3 li2-kai2.  5) Goa2 kio3 li2 an2-ne beng7-leng7 in e5 bok8-tek, si7 ui7 boeh hoan3-khi2 jin5-ai3; chit e5 jin5-ai3 chiu7-si7 tui3 sun5-kiat e5 sim, chheng-pek8 e5 liong5-sim, sun5-cheng3 e5 sin3-giong2 san2-seng chhut-lai5 e5.  6) U7 lang5 li7-khui chiah-e5, soah chau2-jip8 be5-tou5, tho2-lun7 bo5 i3-gi7 e5 tai7-chi3.  7) In ai3 cho3 Lut8-hoat e5 lau7-su, tan7-si7 in sou2 kong2 e5, a2-si7 in sou2 chu2-tiuN e5, ka-ti7 ia7 bo5 beng5-pek8.
	8) Lan2 chai-iaN2 lang5 na7 hap8-hoat lai5 un7-iong7 Lut8-hoat, Lut8-hoat si7 ho2 e5.  9) Lan2 ia7 chai-iaN2 Lut8-hoat m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 bo5-choe7 e5 lang5 lai5 che3-teng7 e5, si7 ui7-tioh8 put-hoat kap bo5 sun7-chiong5 e5, bo5 khian5-seng5 e5 kap u7-choe7 e5, bo5 sin3-kau3 e5 kap bo5 seng3-kiat e5, thai5 lau7-pe7 kap thai5 lau7-bo2 e5, ui7 thai5-si2 lang5 e5,  10) hoan7 sia5-im5 e5, houN3 lam5-sek e5, pang2-phio3 e5, kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5, cho3 gui7-cheng3 e5, a2-si7 ui7-tioh8 ki5-tha ui5-hoan2 kian7-choan5 e5 kau3-gi7 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 che3-teng7 e5.  11) Chit e5 cheng3-thong2 e5 kau3-gi7, chiu7-si7 chin-hok Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7 e5 Hok-im, si7 goa2 siu7 kau-thok lai5 soan-iong5 e5.

Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 e5 lin5-bin2:
	12) Goa2 kam2-sia7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou siuN2-su3 goa2 lek8-liong7, in-ui7 I khoaN3 goa2 cho3 tiong-sit8 e5, jim7-beng7 goa2 ui7 I hok8-bu7.  13) Sui-jian5 goa2 i2-cheng5 si7 chit8 e5 siat-tok8-chia2, pek-hai7-chia2, bu2-jiok8-chia2, goa2 siong7-chhiaN2 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 lin5-bin2. Che si7 in-ui7 goa2 iau2-boe7 sin3 e5 si5, m7-bat chiah cho3 e5.  14) Iu7-koh lan2 e5 Chu2 toa7-toa7 siuN2-su3 goa2 in-tian2, hou7 goa2 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 lai5 tit-tioh8 sin3-sim kap jin5-ai3.  15) Ki-tok Ia5-sou kang3-se3 lai5 kiu3 choe7-jin5, chit ku3 oe7 e7 siong-sin3-tit, ia7 u7 oan5-choan5 chiap-lap8 e5 ke3-tat8. Goa2 chai7 chiah-e5 choe7-jin5 tiong si7 te7-it phaiN2 e5,  16) tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 iau2-ku2 lin5-bin2 goa2 e5 bok8-tek, si7 ui7-tioh8 Ia5-sou Ki-tok tui3 goa2 tai7-seng hian2-si7 I chap8-hun e5 khoan-iong5, thang hou7 goa2 chiaN5-cho3 au7-lai5 e7 lai5 sin3-giong2 I, lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng e5 lang5 e5 bou5-hoan7.  17) Goan7 kong-eng5 kap eng5-iau7 kui hou7 ban7-se3 e5 Ong5, put-biat8-e5, khoaN3 be7-tioh8-e5, ui5-it e5 Siong7-chu2, it-tit8 kau3 ban7-se3 ban7-tai7! Amen.
	18) Goa2 e5 kiaN2 The5-mou5-thai3 a3, goa2 chiau3 i2-cheng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5 koan-he7 li2 e5 tai7-chi3 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, boeh chiong chit e5 beng7-leng7 kau-thok li2, hou7 li2 thang chun-chiau3 chiah-e5 oe7, ho2-ho2 lai5 iong2-kam2 hun3-chian3,  19) lai5 po2-chhi5 sin3-sim kap chheng-pek8 e5 liong5-sim. U7 lang5 pang3-sak liong5-sim, in e5 sin3-giong2 soah pho3-biat8 khi3.  20) Ki5-tiong u7 Hu2-bi2-nai2 kap A-lek8-san-tai7, goa2 i2-keng chiong in kau hou7 Sat-tan3 khi3, thang hou7 in siu7 ka3-si7 chiah be7 koh kong2 siat-tok8 e5 oe7.

Te7 2 ChiuN

Chai7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 chong5-pai3:
	1) Sou2-i2 goa2 tai7-seng khoan3-ko3 li2 tioh8-ai3 ui7 cheng3-lang5 khun2-kiu5, ki5-to2, tai7-kiu5, kam2-sia7;  2) ui7 kun-ong5 kap it-chhe3 e5 koaN-oan5 ki5-kiu5, hou7 lan2 cho3-su7 khian5-seng5 chun-giam5, e7-tang3 koe3 an-cheng7 ho5-peng5 e5 seng-oah8.  3) An2-ne lai5 ki5-kiu5 si7 ho2 e5, ia7-si7 hou7 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Siong7-chu2 hoaN-hi2 e5.  4) I ai3 cheng3-lang5 long2 tit-tioh8 kiu3, lai5 bat chin-li2.  5) In-ui7 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-chu2, chai7 Siong7-chu2 kap lang5 e5 tiong-kan, ia7 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 tiong-po2, chiu7-si7 cho3 lang5 e5 Ki-tok Ia5-sou.  6) I hi-seng ka-ti7, ui7 cheng3-lang5 siok8-choe7. Chit e5 tai7-chi3 chai7 Siong7-chu2 chi2-teng7 e5 jit8-chi2 cheng3-sit8 chhut-lai5.  7) Goa2 ui7-tioh8 an2-ne pi7 jim7-beng7 cho3 thoan5-kau3-chia2, cho3 Su3-tou5, chai7 sin3-giong2-siong7, chin-li2-siong7 cho3 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 lau7-su. Goa2 si7 teh kong2 sit8-chai7 oe7, m7-si7 teh kong2 peh8-chhat8.
	8) Sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 cha-pou-lang5 bo5-lun7 toa3 sim2-mih sou2-chai7, sim-lai7 mai2 phau7 siu7-khi3 a2-si7 cheng-chip, tioh8 khian5-seng5 giah8 siang-chhiu2 khi2-lai5 ki5-to2.  9) Goa2 ia7 ai3 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 chheng7-chhah tioh8-ai3 phok-sou3, eng7 khiam-jiong7 kap toan-cheng3 e5 sim-cheng5 lai5 chong-sek ka-ti7; m7-thang piN7 ki5-heng5 koai3-chong7 e5 thau5-chang, a2-si7 chhah kim-khi3, a2-si7 koa3 chin-chu, a2-si7 chheng7 ko-kui3 e5 saN.  10) Tioh8 eng7 ho2 tek-heng7 lai5 chong-sek, chiah u7 sek-hap8 chu7-chheng cho3 khian5-seng5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5.  11) Hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8-ai3 it-sim sun7-chiong5, cheng7-cheng7 lai5 siu7 ka3-si7.  12) Goa2 m7 chun2 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5 ka3-si7 to7-li2, a2-si7 lai5 koan2-hat cha-pou-lang5; in tioh8-ai3 cheng7-cheng7.  13) In-ui7 A-tong tai7-seng siu7-cho7, jian5-au7 chiah Ha7-oa.  14) Iu7-koh m7-si7 A-tong siu7 khi-phian3, chiu7-si7 hu7-jin5-lang5 siu7 khi-phian3 lai5 ham7-loh8 choe7-ok.  15) Tan7-si7 hu7-jin5-lang5 na7-si7 u7 chiat-che3 lai5 po2-chhi5 sin3-giong2, jin5-ai3 kap seng3-kiat, chiu7 e7 iu5 seN-kiaN2 lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.

Te7 3 ChiuN

Kau3-hoe7 tiong e5 leng2-to7-chia2:
	1) Lang5 na7 chin-ai3 boeh cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 leng2-to7-chia2, i chiu7-si7 khat-bong7 boeh cho3 ho2 e5 kang-chok. Chit ku3 si7 chin-sit8 e5 oe7.  2) Sou2-i2 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 leng2-to7-chia2, eng-kai tioh8 bo5 khoat-tiam2, chi2-u7 chit8 e5 thai3-thai3, tioh8 giam5-siok, chu7-che3, heng5-ui5 toan-cheng3, ho2-ho2 khoan2-thai7 chhut-goa7-lang5, gau5 ka3-si7,  3) be7 tian-tian-chui3, be7 ia2-ban5, tioh8 un-jiu5, be7 kap lang5 oan-ke, be7 tham-chai5,  4) gau5 koan2-li2 ka-ti7 e5 ka-teng5, koan2-kau3 kiaN2-ji5 sun7-chiong5 i, chun-keng3 i.  5) Lang5 na7 be7-hiau2 koan2-li2 ka-ti7 e5 ka-teng5, an3-choaN2 e7-hiau2 ti7-li2 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7.  6) M7-thang hou7 sin jip8-kau3 e5 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 leng2-to7-chia2, kiaN-liau2 i e7 chu7-khoa, lai5 siu7 kap Mou5-kui2 kang7-khoan2 e5 phoaN3-choe7.  7) I chai7 kau3-hoe7 goa7 ia7 tioh8 u7 ho2 mia5-siaN, chiah be7 hou7 lang5 kong2 si7-hui, iah lai5 ham7-loh8 Mou5-kui2 e5 khoan5-tho3.

Kau3-hoe7 tiong e5 hu3-chou7-chia2:
	8) Cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 hu3-chou7-chia2 ia7-si7 kang7-khoan2, tioh8-ai3 u7 ui-giam5, m7-thang chit8 e5 chhui3 nng7 e5 chih8, m7-thang lim-chiu2 koe3-thau5 a2-si7 tham put-gi7 e5 chiN5-chai5,  9) tioh8-ai3 eng7 sun5-kiat e5 liong5-sim lai5 kian-chhi5 to7-li2 e5 o3-gi7.  10) Chiah-e5 lang5 ia7 tioh8-ai3 tai7-seng siu7 kho2-giam7; in na7 bo5 sim2-mih thang hoan7-hiam5, chiah thang hou7 in cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 hu3-chou7-chia2.  11) In e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ia7-si7 kang7-khoan2, tioh8-ai3 toan-cheng3, be7 kong2 lang5 e5 phaiN2-oe7, tioh8-ai3 leng2-cheng7, cho3-su7 tiong-sit8.  12) Cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 hu3-chou7-chia2 tioh8-ai3 chi2-u7 chit8 e5 thai3-thai3, gau5 koan2-li2 in e5 kiaN2-ji5 kap ka-ti7 e5 ka-teng5.  13) In-ui7 ho2-ho2 chin7 chit-bu7 e5, e7 tit-tioh8 ho2 e5 te7-ui7, ia7 chiah e7-tang3 tong5-tong5 kong2 chhut lan2 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 e5 sin3-giong2.

Ui2-tai7 e5 o3-pi3:
	14) Goa2 teh sia2 chit tiuN phe hou7 li2 e5 si5, hi-bong7 liam5-piN e7-tang3 khi3 li2 hia.  15) Tan7-si7 goa2 na7 ian5-chhian5 ban7 khi3, chit tiuN phe boeh hou7 li2 chai-iaN2 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 ka-teng5 eng-kai tioh8-ai3 an3-choaN2 cho3 tai7-chi3. Chit e5 ka-teng5 chiu7-si7 eng2-seng e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, chin-li2 e5 toa7-thiau7 kap te7-ki.  16) Lan2 e5 chong-kau3 e5 o3-gi7 sit8-chai7 chin ui2-tai7, long2 bo5 gi5-bun7 e5 i5-te7!
Ki-tok eng7 lang5 e5 heng5-the2 lai5 hian2-hian7.
I iu5 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 cheng3-sit8.
I hou7 thian-sai3 khoaN3-kiN3.
I hou7 lang5 thoan5-iong5 kau3 ban7-pang.
I siu7 choan5 se3-kai3 e5 lang5 sin3-giong2.
I siu7 the5-ko khi3 eng5-iau7 e5 thian-siong7.

Te7 4 ChiuN

Ke2 e5 lau7-su:
	1) Seng3 Leng5 beng5-beng5 kong2 au7-lai5 u7 chit8-koa2 lang5 e7 pang3-sak sin3-giong2, khi3 thiaN-chiong5 hiah-e5 be5-hek8 lang5 e5 sin5 kap mou5-kui2 e5 ka3-si7.  2) Chiah-e5 ka3-si7 si7 tui3 kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5 lang5 e5 gui7-sian7 lai5 e5. In e5 liong5-sim i2-keng hou7 mou5-kui2 e5 thih-in3 sio si2-khi3 a3.  3) In kim3-chi2 kiat-hun, kim3-chi2 chiah8-mih8. Chiah-e5 chiah8-mih8 pun2-lai5 si7 Siong7-chu2 chhong3-cho7 e5, boeh hou7 u7 sin3 iu7-koh bat chin-li2 e5 lang5 eng7 kam2-sia7 e5 sim lai5 hiong2-siu7 e5.  4) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 chhong3-cho7 it-chhe3 e5 mih8 long2 si7 ho2 e5, lang5 na7 eng7 kam2-sia7 e5 sim-cheng5 lai5 nia2-siu7, chiu7 bo5 chit8 hang7 thang kim3-khi7;  5) in-ui7 thong-koe3 Siong7-chu2 e5 oe7 kap ki5-to2, e7 hou7 tak8-hang7 mih8 piN3-cho3 sin5-seng3 e5.

Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 ho2 pok8-jin5:
	6) Li2 na7 chiong chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 ka3-si7 hiaN-ti7, li2 chiu7-si7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 ho2 pok8-jin5, piau2-si7 li2 si7 siu7 sou2 sin3 e5 to7-li2 kap sou2 hok8-chiong5 e5 cheng3-to7 lai5 poe5-iong2 e5.  7) Tioh8 siam2-pi7 chhin-chhiuN7 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 hit khoan2 bo5 sin3-giong2 e5, bo5-iaN2-bo5-jiah e5 thoan5-soat. Tioh8-ai3 thoan3-lian7 li2 ka-ti7 lai5 koe3 khian5-seng5 e5 seng-oah8.  8) In-ui7 thoan3-lian7 seng-khu sui-jian5 u7 li7-ek, tan7-si7 hun3-lian7 khian5-seng5 keng3-ui3 Chu2, u7 bu5-han7 e5 li7-ek, in-ui7 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 hian7-se3 kap lai5-se3 e5 seN3-mia7 e5 eng3-un2.  9) Chit ku3 oe7 e7 siong-sin3-tit, ia7 u7 oan5-choan5 chiap-lap8 e5 ke3-tat8.  10) Ui7 chit e5 bok8-tek lan2 lai5 teh sin-khou2 hun3-tou3, in-ui7 lan2 e5 hi-bong7 long2 koa3 chai7 eng2-seng e5 Siong7-chu2. I si7 cheng3-lang5 e5 Kiu3-chu2, iu5-ki5-si7 sin3-tou5 e5 Kiu3-chu2.
	11) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, li2 tioh8 ka7 in beng7-leng7, ka7 in ka3-si7.  12) M7-thang in-ui7 li2 iau2 siau3-lian5 lai5 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-khin, tioh8-ai3 chai7 gian5-su5, heng5-ui5, jin5-ai3, sin3-giong2, sun5-kiat-siong7 lai5 cho3 sin3-tou5 e5 bou5-hoan7.  13) Kau3 goa2 khi3 li2 hia, li2 tioh8 choan-bu7 soan-thok8 Seng3-keng, khoan3-bian2 ka3-si7 lang5.  14) M7-thang hut-liok8 li2 sou2 u7 e5 Seng3 Leng5 e5 in-su3, chiu7-si7 iu5 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5 e5 soan-pou3 kap iu5 tiuN2-lo2 an3-chhiu2 lai5 su3 hou7 li2 e5 in-su3.  15) Li2 tioh8-ai3 choan-sim ti3-i3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, cheng3-lang5 chiu7 e7 khoaN3 chhut li2 e5 chin3-pou7.  16) Li2 tioh8 chu3-i3 li2 ka-ti7 kap li2 e5 kau3-hun3, ke3-siok8 lai5 cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3. An2-ne cho3 chiu7 e7 kiu3-tioh8 li2 ka-ti7 i2-kip8 thiaN li2 e5 kau3-hun3 e5 lang5.

Te7 5 ChiuN

Tui3 sin3-tou5 e5 chek-jim7:
	1) M7-thang chek-pi7 lau7-lang5, tioh8 khou2-khng3 i chhin-chhiuN7 teh tui3-thai7 lau7-pe7, tui3-thai7 siau3-lian5-lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 teh tui3-thai7 hiaN-ti7,  2) tui3-thai7 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 teh tui3-thai7 lau7-bo2, tui3-thai7 siau3-lian5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 teh tui3-thai7 chi2-moe7, long2 tioh8 eng7 oan5-choan5 sun5-kiat e5 sim.
	3) Tioh8-ai3 keng3-tiong7 sit8-chai7 kou-toaN e5 koaN2-hu7.  4) Tan7-si7 koaN2-hu7 na7-si7 u7 kiaN2 a2-si7 u7 sun, tioh8-ai3 hou7 kiaN2-sun tai7-seng oh8 tui3 in ka-ti7 e5 ke-lai7 e5 lang5 iu2-hau3, lai5 po3-tap iuN2-chhi7 in toa7-han3 e5 si7-toa7-lang5, in-ui7 che si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5.  5) Sit8-chai7 kou-toaN e5 koaN2-hu7 ka-ti7 chit8 e5 bo5 i-oa2, chi2-u7 giong2-bong7 Chu2, me5-jit8 ke3-siok8 teh ki5-kiu5 kap ki5-to2.  6) Tan7-si7 hong3-chhiong3 e5 koaN2-hu7, sui-jian5 teh oah8, ki5-sit8 si7 si2-khi3 a3.  7) Tioh8-ai3 ka7 koaN2-hu7 beng7-leng7 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, in chiah be7 siu7 lang5 chek-pi7.  8) Lang5 na7 bo5 chiau3-kou3 ka-ti7 e5 chhin-chiaN5, tek8-piat8 si7 i e5 ka-chok8, chiu7-si7 ui5-poe7 to7-li2, pi2 bo5 sin3-giong2 e5 lang5 koh-khah phaiN2.
	9) Iau2-boe7 chiuN7 lak8-chap8 hoe3 e5 koaN2-hu7 be7-eng7-tit liat8-jip8 koaN2-hu7 e5 mia5-chheh, iu7-koh tioh8-ai3 chi2-u7 kiat-hun chit8 pai2,  10) u7 cho3 chin-che7 ho2-su7 e5 cheng3-ki3; chhin-chhiuN7 u7 iong2-iok8 kiaN2-ji5, gau5 chiap-thai7 chhut-goa7-lang5, se2 sin3-tou5 e5 kha, kiu3-chou7 siu7-lan7 e5 lang5, choan5-sim cho3 ho2-su7 teng2-teng2 e5 koaN2-hu7, chiah e7-eng7-tit liat8-jip8 mia5-chheh.  11) Tan7-si7 siau3-lian5 e5 koaN2-hu7 m7-thang liat8-jip8 mia5-chheh; in-ui7 in cheng5-iok8 chhiong-tong7 ui5-poe7 Ki-tok e5 si5, in chiu7 siuN7-boeh koh ke3;  12) an2-ne in pang3-sak choe3-chhou e5 se3-goan7, lai5 siu7 teng7-choe7.  13) Ji5-chhiaN2 in oh8 pin7-toaN7, koe3-ke koe3-hou7 chhit-tho5, iu7-koh m7-na7 pin7-toaN7, ia7 kong2 eng5-oe7, chhap eng5-su7, kong2 m7 eng-kai kong2 e5 tai7-chi3.  14) Sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 siau3-lian5 e5 koaN2-hu7 koh ke3, seng-iok8 kiaN2-ji5, koan2-li2 ka-teng5, chiah be7 hou7 tui3-tek8 u7 ki-hoe7 thang lai5 ke kong2-oe7;  15) in-ui7 i2-keng u7 lang5 hoan-thau5 toe3 Sat-tan3 khi3 a3.  16) Siat-su2 ti7 sin3-tou5 e5 ke-lai7 u7 koaN2-hu7, tioh8-ai3 ka7 in hu5-iong2, m7-thang hou7 kau3-hoe7 hu7-tam chit e5 tang7-taN3, kau3-hoe7 chiah e7-tang3 hu5-iong2 sit8-chai7 kou-toaN e5 koaN2-hu7.
	17) Gau5 ti7-li2 kau3-hoe7 e5 tiuN2-lo2, tek8-piat8 si7 chin7-lek8 teh kong2 to7-li2 teh kau3-hun3 e5, tioh8-ai3 siu7 ka-poe7 e5 piau2-iong5.  18) In-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 kong2, “Teh thoa chioh8-lun2 e5 gu5, m7-thang ka7 i koa3 gu5-chhui3-lam.” Iu7-koh kong2, “Kang-lang5 eng-kai nia2 kang-chiN5.”  19) Tui3 tiuN2-lo2 e5 khong3-ko3, na7 bo5 nng7 saN e5 lang5 cho3-cheng3, m7-thang chiap-siu7.  20) Hoan7-choe7 e5 lang5, tioh8 toa3 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 ka7 in chek-pi7, thang hou7 ki5-tha e5 lang5 khi2 kiaN-sim.
