Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Ko-Lim5-To Lang5 E5 Phe
(Hou7 su)

Te7 1 ChiuN

	1) Goa2 si7 Po2-lo5, chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, cho3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 Su3-tou5. Goa2 kap lan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 The5-mou5-thai3, sia2 chit tiuN phe hou7 Ko-lim5-to-chhi7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, i2-kip8 Hi-lah8 choan5 kok e5 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5:
	2) Ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.

Po2-lo5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2:
	3) Lan2 lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 Thian-hu7, chiu7-si7 jin5-chu5 e5 Thian-hu7, siuN2-su3 lan2 it-chhe3 an-ui3 e5 Siong7-chu2.  4) I chai7 lan2 it-chhe3 hoan7-lan7 tiong an-ui3 lan2, thang hou7 lan2 ia7 e7-tang3 eng7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 an-ui3, lai5 an-ui3 hiah-e5 tu2-tioh8 kok-chiong2 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5.  5) Tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 siu7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 khou2-lan7, lan2 ia7 oa2-kho3 I lai5 hiong2-siu7 I ui2-tai7 e5 an-ui3  6) Siat-su2 goan2 siu7 khou2-lan7, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 an-ui3 kap chin2-kiu3; siat-su2 goan2 tit-tioh8 an-ui3, lin2 ia7 e7 tit-tioh8 an-ui3, iu7-koh tit-tioh8 lek8-liong7 lai5 jim2-siu7 goan2 sou2 jim2-siu7 e5 khou2-lan7.  7) Sou2-i2 goan2 tui3 lin2 sou2 phau7 e5 hi-bong7 long2 be7 tong7-iau5; goan2 chai-iaN2 lin2 kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 siu7-khou2, chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne lin2 ia7 kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 siu7 an-ui3.
	8) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goan2 ai3 lin2 chai-iaN2 goan2 chai7 A-se-a Seng2 sou2 siu7 e5 khou2-lan7. Goan2 sou2 taN e5 taN3 hui-siong5 toa7 koh tang7, goan2 soah song3-sit it-chhe3 oah8 e5 hi-bong7.  9) Goan2 kam2-kak hou7 lang5 phoaN3 si2-heng5 a7. Tan7-si7 chit e5 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 goan2 put eng-kai oa2-kho3 ka-ti7, chi2-u7 tioh8-ai3 oa2-kho3 hou7 si2-lang5 koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Siong7-chu2.  10) I tui3 chit khoan2 seN-si2 e5 koan-thau5 ka7 goan2 kiu3 chhut-lai5, chiong-lai5 ia7 e7 ka7 goan2 kiu3; goan2 hi-bong7 I e7 koh-chai3 kiu3 goan2,  11) in-ui7 lin2 the3 goan2 ki5-kiu5, lai5 teh pang-chou7 goan2. Sou2-i2 ui7 goan2 sou2 hian3 hiah che7 ki5-to2, it-teng7 e7 siu7 chiap-lap8, Siong7-chu2 e7 ka7 goan2 chiok-hok; iah chin-che7 lang5 e7 ui7 goan2 ka7 I kam2-sia7.

Po2-lo5 kai2-pian3 ke3-ek8:
	12) Goan2 kam2-kak chin eng5-heng7 e5 tai7-chi3 chiu7-si7: goan2 e5 liong5-sim ka7 goan2 cho3-cheng3 kong2, goan2 chai7 chit se3-kan e5 seng-oah8, iu5-ki5-si7 goan2 kap lin2 e5 koan-he7, long2 chun-chiau3 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 e5 sun5-kiat kap seng5-sit8 lai5 teh kiaN5, oa2-kho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 e5 lek8-liong7, peng3 bo5 oa2-kho3 lang5 e5 ti3-hui7.  13) In-ui7 goan2 sia2 hou7 lin2 e5, long2 si7 lin2 e7-hiau2 thak8, e7-tang3 liau2-kai2 e5. Goa2 hi-bong7 lin2 kau3 lou7-boe2 e7 thiat-te2 liau2-kai2.  14) Lin2 hian7-chai7 chi2-u7 it-ti poan3-kai2 e5 tai7-chi3, thang chai7 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 Jit8-chi2, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 e7 khoa-iau7 lin2, lin2 ia7 lai5 khoa-iau7 goan2.
	15) Goa2 in-ui7 khak-sin3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, sou2-i2 khi2-thau5 ke3-ek8 boeh khi3 lin2 hia, thang hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 teng5-poe7 e5 chiok-hok.  16) Goa2 ke3-ek8 boeh khi3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 si5, kap tui3 hia tng2-lai5 e5 si5 boeh oat-khi3 lin2 hia, thang hou7 lin2 sang3 goa2 chhut-hoat khi3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2.  17) Goa2 ke3-ek8 chit e5 tai7-chi3 e5 si5, kam2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 teng7-seng3? Goa2 ke3-ek8 tai7-chi3 e5 si5, kam2 iong7 chu7-su e5 tong7-ki lai5 ke3-ek8, liam5-piN kong2 “si7, si7,” liam5-piN kong2 “m7-si7, m7-si7”?  18) Chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-chu2 si7 sin3-sit8 e5, goa2 kap lin2 sou2 iok e5, peng3 m7-si7 liam5-piN “si7,” liam5-piN “m7-si7.”  19) In-ui7 Se-lah, The5-mou5-thai3 kap goa2 pun2-sin, chai7 lin2 tiong-kan sou2 soan-kang2 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok m7-si7Chit8-ui7 liam5-piN “si7,” liam5-piN “m7-si7” e5. I kong2 “si7,” chiu7-si7 “si7.”  20) in-ui7 I kheng2-teng7 Siong7-chu2 it-chhe3 e5 tah-eng3; chiu7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, lan2 chiah oa2-kho3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok kong2 “Amen” lai5 kui kong-eng5 hou7 Siong7-chu2.  21) In-ui7 hou7 goan2 kap lin2 e5 seng-oah8 kian-kou3 lai5 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8 e5 si7 Siong7-chu2; Siong7-chu2 pun2-sin ka7 lan2 keng2-soan2,  22) ka7 lan2 tng3-in3 lai5 piau2-si7 I e5 sou2-iu2-koan5, siuN2-su3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 toa3 lan2 sim-lai7, po2-cheng3 I boeh siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 ban7-but8.
	23) Goa2 hou-kio3 Siong7-chu2 ka7 goa2 cho3-cheng3 --- I chai-iaN2 goa2 e5 lai7-sim! Ui7-tioh8 boeh khoan-iong5 lin2, goa2 chiah koat-i3 m7 khi3 Ko-lim5-to.  24) Goan2 bo5 boeh kiong5-che3 lin2 tioh8 sin3 sim2-mih, in-ui7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim u7 kian-kou3. Goan2 si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 chu7-ki2 e5 khoai3-lok8, kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 teh kang-chok e5.

Te7 2 ChiuN

	1) Sou2-i2 goa2 chiu7 koat-i3 bo5 boeh koh khi3 lin2 hia lai5 hou7 lin2 pi-siong.  2) Siat-su2 goa2 na7 hou7 lin2 pi-siong, iau2 u7 chhun sim2-mih lang5 thang ka7 goa2 kou2-le7? Chi2-u7 goa2 hou7 in pi-siong hiah-e5 lang5 nia7-nia7.  3) In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 chiah sia2 hit tiuN phe hou7 lin2 --- chiu7-si7 goa2 bo5 ai3 khi3 lin2 hia, lai5 hou7 hiah-e5 eng-kai hou7 goa2 hoaN-hi2 e5 lang5, tian-to3 lai5 hou7 goa2 pi-siong. In-ui7 goa2 siong-sin3 goa2 hoaN-hi2 e5 si5 lin2 ia7 hoaN-hi2.  4) Hit tiuN phe si7 goa2 phau7 toa7 thong3-khou2 kap iu-bun7 e5 sim-cheng5, lau5 chin-che7 bak8-sai2 sia2 hou7 lin2 e5, m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 pi-siong, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 chai-iaN2 goa2 loa7-nih ai3 lin2 tai7-ke.

Sia3-bian2 hoan7-choe7 e5 lang5:
	5) Na7 u7 lang5 hou7 lang5 pi-siong, i m7-si7 hou7 goa2 pi-siong e5, si7 hou7 lin2 pi-siong e5; choe3-chio2 ia7 hou7 lin2 chit8 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 pi-siong. Goa2 kong2 an2-ne, si7 in-ui7 goa2 bo5 ai3 tui3 lin2 kong2 siuN koe3-hun7.  6) Chit e5 lang5 hou7 lin2 toa7 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 an2-ne ka7 i chhu2-hoat8 i2-keng kau3-giah8 a7.  7) Sou2-i2 lin2 tioh8 ka7 i sia3-bian2, ka7 i kou2-le7, m7-thang hou7 i pi-siong koe3-thau5 lai5 choat8-bong7.  8) In-ui7 an2-ne, goa2 khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 hou7 i chai-iaN2 lin2 sit8-chai7 teh ai3 i.  9) Goa2 sia2 hit tiuN phe hou7 lin2, ia7-si7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 li2-iu5; goa2 ai3 chai-iaN2 lin2 loa7 gau5 jim2-siu7 kho2-giam7, u7 si5-siong5 goan7-i3 lai5 hok8-chiong5 goa2 e5 chi2-si7 a2 bo5.  10) Na7 u7 lang5 cho3 m7-tioh8 lin2 tioh8 ka7 i khoan-bian2. Goa2 ia7 ka7 i khoan-bian2. goa2 siat-su2 sit8-chai7 u7 khoan-bian2 sim2-mih choe7-ko3, goa2 khoan-bian2 si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 chai7 Ki-tok e5 bin7-cheng5 khoan-bian2 e5,  11) an2-ne chiah be7 siu7 Sat-tan3 khi-phian3; in-ui7 lan2 chai-iaN2 i e5 kui2-ke3 si7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5.

Po2-lo5 chai7 Tek-lo5-a e5 hoan5-lo2:
	12) Goa2 khi3-kau3 Tek-lo5-a thoan5 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im e5 si5, hoat-hian7 ti7 hia Chu2 i2-keng ui7 hit e5 kang-chok khui chit8 tiau5 lou7 a7.  13) Tan7-si7 in-ui7 chhoe7 bo5 lan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 The5-to, goa2 hui-siong5 hoan5-lo2. Sou2-i2 ka7 hia e5 lang5 si5-piat8 chiu7 khi3 Ma2-ki5-tun3.

Oa2-kho3 Ki-tok e5 seng3-li7:
	14) Tan7-si7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2! In-ui7 lan2 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8, si5-siong5 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 leng2-to7, lai5 chham-ka Ki-tok chian3-seng3 e5 iu5-heng5. Chhin-chhiuN7 phang e5 khi3-bi7 chheng3 kau3 moa2-si3-ke3, Siong7-chu2 eng7 lan2 lai5 hou7 ban7-bin5 jim7-sek Ki-tok.  15) In-ui7 lan2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Ki-tok sio hou7 siong7-chu2 e5 phang-hiuN, chheng3 khi3-kau3 teh boeh tit-tioh8 kiu3 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan, ia7 chheng3 khi3-kau3 teh-boeh biat8-bong5 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan.  16) Tui3 hiah-e5 teh-boeh biat8-bong5 e5 lang5, che si7 e7 kek-si2 lang5 e5 chhau3-bi7; tui3 hiah-e5 teh-boeh tit-tioh8 kiu3 e5 lang5, che si7 e7 siuN2-su3 seN3-mia7 e5 phang-bi7. Na7 an2-ne sim2-mih lang5 u7 chai5-leng5 thang cho3 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3?  17) Goan2 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 ki5-tha hiah che7 lang5, chiong Siong7-chu2 e5 to7-li2 chhin-chhiuN7 siok8-hoe3 taN teh be7; in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 chhe-phai3 goan2 lai5 cho3 Ki-tok e5 pok8-jin5, sou2-i2 goan2 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 seng5-sit8 lai5 soan-kang2 to7-li2.

Te7 3 ChiuN

Sin Beng5-iok e5 pok8-jin5:
	1) Goan2 an2-ne kong2 kam2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 iu7-koh teh chu7-khoa? Goan2 kam2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ki5-tha e5 lang5, u7 su-iau3 pat8-lang5 the3 goan2 sia2 chhui-chian3 e5 phe hou7 lin2, a2-si7 u7 su-iau3 lin2 sia2 phe ka7 goan2 chhui-chian3?  2) Lin2 chiu7-si7 goan2 e5 chhui-chian3 e5 phe, sia2 ti7 goan2 e5 sim-lai7, thang hou7 tak8 lang5 chai-iaN2, tak8 lang5 thak8.  3) Beng5-beng5 lin2 si7 Ki-tok chhin-sin sia2 e5 phe, keng-koe3 goan2 e5 chhiu2 lai5 sang3 e5. Chit tiuN phe m7-si7 eng7 bak8-chui2 sia2 ti7 chioh8-pan2 e5, si7 eng7 eng2-seng Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 sia2 ti7 lang5 e5 sim-lai7 e5.
	4) Goan2 thong-koe3 Ki-tok tui3 Siong7-chu2 u7 sin3-sim chiah an2-ne kong2.  5) In-ui7 ti7 goan2 lai7-bin7 bo5 chit8 hang7 e7-tang3 hou7 goan2 chu2-tiuN goan2 u7 chu-keh thang cho3 chit khoan2 kang-chok. Goan2 e5 chu-keh si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5:  6) in-ui7 I siuN2-su3 goan2 chu-keh lai5 ui7 Sin e5 Beng5-iok hok8-bu7, chit e5 Sin e5 Beng5-iok peng3 m7-si7 eng7 bun5-ji7 sia2 e5 lut8-hoat, si7 iu5 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 lip8-iok e5. Eng7 bun5-ji7 sia2 e5 lut8-hoat e7 toa3 si2-bong5 lai5, tan7-si7 Seng3 Leng5 e7 siuN2-su3 seN3-mia7.
	7) Lut8-hoat si7 eng7 bun5-ji7 khek ti7 chioh8-pai5-teng2 e5. Pan-pou3 Lut8-hoat e5 si5 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7 u7 hian2-hian7 chhut-lai5. Sui-jian5 eng5-iau7 siau-sit khi3, in-ui7 Mou5-se e5 bin7-nih8 e5 kng hui-siong5 kiong5, sou2-i2 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang kim-kim ka7 i khoaN3. Lut8-hoat e5 chit-bu7 si7 toa3 si2-bong5 lai5. Chit e5 Lut8-hoat pan-pou3 e5 si5, na7 u7 chit khoan2 e5 eng5-iau7,  8) siok8 ti7 Seng3 Leng5 e5 chit-bu7 e5 eng5-iau7 kam2 m7-si7 koh-khah toa7?  9) Ka7 lang5 phoaN3-choe7 e5 chit-bu7 na7 u7 hit khoan2 eng5-iau7, an2-ne ka7 lang5 soan-phoaN3 bo5 choe7 e5 chit-bu7 kam2 m7-si7 koh-khah eng5-iau7?  10) Lan2 e7-eng7-tit kong2, in-ui7 hian7-chai7 u7 ke chin kng e5 eng5-iau7, sou2-i2 koe3-khi3 hiah-nih8 kng e5 eng5-iau7 soah siau-sit khi3.  11) Hit e5 kng chit8-si5-a2-ku2 e5, na7 u7 eng5-iau7, hit e5 eng2-oan2 chun5-siok8 e5 kam2 m7-si7 u7 koh-khah toa7 e5 eng5-iau7?
	12) In-ui7 goan2 u7 chit khoan2 e5 hi-bong7, sou2-i2 goan2 chin ho2-taN2 lai5 kong2.  13) Goan2 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 Mou5-se tioh8 eng7 se-a2-kin ta3 bin7, chiah be7 hou7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 bin7 e5 kong-hui chiam7-chiam7 soe-bi5, choe3-au7 soah bo5-khi3.  14) In e5 ti3-lek8 sit8-chai7 long2 tun7-khi3; kau3 kin-a2-jit8 in thak8 Ku7-iok e5 si5, in e5 ti3-lek8 iau2-ku2 eng7 kang7 hit te3 se-a2-kin ta3 le3. Lang5 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8 e5 si5, hit te3 se-a2-kin chiah e7-tang3 theh8-tiau7.  15) Sim7-chi3 kin-na2-jit8, in tak8 pai2 thak8 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat e5 si5, in e5 ti3-lek8 iau2-ku2 hou7 hit te3 se-a2-kin ta3-le3.  16) Tan7-si7 koan-he7 Mou5-se, Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2: “I bo5-lun7 sim2-mih si5 kui-hiong3 Chu2, se-a2-kin chiu7 e7 theh8-tiau7.”  17) Ti7 chit toaN7 sou2 kong2 e5 “Chu2” chiu7-si7 Seng3 Leng5; u7 Chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 u7 chu7-iu5.  18) An2-ne lan2 tai7-ke bo5 ta3 se-a2-kin e5 bin7, chiu7 e7 hoan2-sia7 Chu2 e5 eng5-iau7; chit e5 eng5-iau7 si7 tui3 Chu2, chiu7-si7 tui3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 e5, hou7 lan2 it-tit8 cheng7-ka eng5-iau7, pian3-seng5 kap I kang7-khoan2 e5 heng5-chong7.

Te7 4 ChiuN

Hui5-ang3-a2 lai7 e5 leng5-seng3 e5 po2-poe3:
	1) Siong7-chu2 ki3-jian5 chu5-pi siuN2-su3 goan2 chit e5 chit-bu7, goan2 chiu7 be7 song3-khi3.  2) Goan2 khi3-choat8 it-chhe3 pi3-bit8 kian3-siau3 e5 heng5-ui5; bo5 khi-phian3 lang5, ia7 bo5 khiok-kai2 Siong7-chu2 e5 to7-li2. Goan2 cheng3-cheng3-tong5-tong5 soan-kang2 chin-to7, seng-oah8 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, chin7-liong7 hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 liong5-sim lai5 tui3 goan2 u7 ho2 in3-siong7.  3) In-ui7 goan2 soan-kang2 e5 Hok-im na7 u7 am-kham3, he si7 kan-taN tui3 teh-boeh biat8-bong5 e5 lang5 am-kham3 e5.  4) In bo5 sin3, si7 in-ui7 in e5 sim hou7 chit se3-kan e5 sia5-sin5 kim3 chai7 ou-am3-tiong. Sia5-sin5 hou7 in be7-tang3 khoaN3-kiN3 chio3 ti7 in sin-siong7 e5 kong-beng5. Chit e5 kong-beng5 si7 tui3 koan-he7 Ki-tok kong-eng5 e5 Hok-im lai5 e5. Ki-tok chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 siau3-siong7.  5) In-ui7 goan2 m7-si7 teh soan-thoan5 goan2 ka-ti7, goan2 soan-iong5 Ia5-sou Ki-tok si7 Chu2, iah goan2 ka-ti7 ui7-tioh8 Ia5-sou lai5 cho3 lin2 e5 lou5-pok8.  6) Hit-ui7 beng7-leng7 “Kong-beng5 tui3 ou-am3 tiong chio3 chhut-lai5” e5 Siong7-chu2, chiu7-si7 hou7 I e5 kong-beng5 chio3 chai7 lan2 sim-lai7 e5 Siong7-chu2, lai5 hou7 lan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7 hian2-chhut chai7 Ki-tok e5 bin7-nih8.
	7) Tan7-si7 goan2 chiah-e5 u7 chit e5 leng5-seng3 e5 po2-poe3 e5 lang5, tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 phou2-thong e5 hui5-ang3-a2, lai5 teh piau2-si7 choe3-ko e5 koan5-peng3 siok8 ti7 Siong7-chu2, peng3 m7-si7 siok8 ti7 goan2.  8) Goan2 to3-chhu3 tu2-tioh8 khun3-khou2, tan7-si7 be7 khut-hok8; u7-si5-a2 e7 khi2 giau5-gi5, tan7-si7 m7-bat choat8-bong7;  9) u7 chin-che7 tek8-jin5, tan7-si7 m7-bat bo5 peng5-iu2; sui-jian5 u7-si5-a2 siu7-tioh8 giam5-tiong7 e5 siong-hai7, tan7-si7 bo5 si2-bong5.  10) Goan2 bo5-lun7 khi3-kau3 tah-ui7, goan2 e7 si2-biat8 e5 seng-khu to kap Ia5-sou e5 si2-bong5 tong5-chai7, thang hian2-si7 I e5 seN3-mia7 ia7 ti7 goan2 e5 seng-khu.  11) Goan2 it-seng si5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 ui7 Ia5-sou lai5 si2 e5 gui5-hiam2, thang lai5 hian2-beng5 I e5 seN3-mia7 ti7 goan2 chit e5 e7 si2-biat8 e5 seng-khu.  12) Che piau2-si7 si2-bong5 ti7 goan2 lai7-bin7 teh chok-iong7; tan7-si7 seN3-mia7 ti7 lin2 lai7-bin7 teh chok-iong7.
	13) Seng3-keng kong2, “Goa2 u7 sin3 chiah kong2.” Phau7 kang7-khoan2 e5 sin3-giong2 e5 cheng-sin5, goan2 ia7 si7 u7 sin3 chiah kong2 chhut-lai5.  14) In-ui7 goan2 chai-iaN2 hou7 Chu2 Ia5-sou hok8-hoat8 e5 Siong7-chu2, ia7 e7 hou7 goan2 kap Ia5-sou cho3-hoe2 hok8-hoat8, in2-chhoa7 goan2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 khi3 I e5 bin7-cheng5.  15) Chiah-e5 it-chhe3 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2; Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 chit8-e7 phou2-phian3 hou7 lu2 che7 lang5, in chiu7 e7 lu2 chiap8 hong7-hian3 kam2-in e5 ki5-to2 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.

Sin3-giong2 e5 seng-oah8:
	16) In-ui7 an2-ne goan2 choat8-tui3 be7 song3-khi3. Sui-jian5 goan2 e5 jiok8-the2 chiam7-chiam7 teh soe-jiok8, tan7-si7 goan2 e5 sim-leng5 chit8-jit8 chit8-jit8 keng-sin.  17) Goan2 sou2 siu7 chit e5 sio2-sio2 chiam7-si5 e5 khun3-khou2, e7 ui7 goan2 toa3 kek8 ho2, eng2-oan2, pi2 chit e5 khun3-khou2 koh-khah toa7 e5 kong-eng5 lai5 hou7 goan2.  18) Goan2 sou2 tiong7-si7 e5, m7-si7 khoaN3 e7 tioh8 e5, si7 khoaN3 be7 tioh8 e5. KhoaN3 e7 tioh8 e5 si7 chiam7-si5 e5, tan7-si7 khoaN3 be7 tioh8 e5 si7 eng2-oan2 e5.

Te7 5 ChiuN

	1) In-ui7 goan2 chai-iaN2 lan2 chai7 chit te7-siong7 teh toa3 e5 pou3-phang5, na7 hou7 lang5 thiah-tiau7, Siong7-chu2 e7 chai7 thian-siong7 siuN2-su3 lan2 chit8 keng chhu3 thang toa3. Chit keng si7 I chhin-chhiu2 khi2 e5, si7 eng2-kiu2 chun5-siok8 ti7 thiN-teng2 e5.  2) Lan2 thou2-toa7-khui3, khat-bong7 u7 chit8 keng thian-siong7 e5 chhu3, chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 nia2 saN lai5 chheng7 le7;  3) in-ui7 lan2 na7 chheng7 hit nia2 saN, chiu7 be7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 chhiah-sin lou7-the2.  4) Lan2 toa3 ti7 chit te7-siong7 e5 pou3-phang5, kam2-kak put-an lai5 oan3-than3; peng3 m7-si7 ai3 boeh chhiah-sin lou7-the2, si7 ai3 boeh koh thah8 loh8-khi3, thang hou7 oah8-mia7 lai5 khek-hok8 e7 si2-biat8 e5.  5) Ka7 lan2 an-pai5 chit e5 kai2-pian3 e5 si7 Siong7-chu2. I iu7-koh siuN2-su3 lan2 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 po2-cheng3 I boeh siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 it-chhe3.
	6) Sou2-i2 goan2 si5-siong5 chhiong-moa2 iong2-khi3. Goan2 chai-iaN2 lan2 iau2-teh chu3-tiong7 jiok8-the2 seng-oah8 e5 tiong-kan, chiu7-si7 oan2-li7 Chu2 e5 chhu3.  7) In-ui7 lan2 cho3-su7 si7 oa2-kho3 sin3-giong2, peng3 m7-si7 oa2-kho3 bak8-chiu sou2 khoaN3 e5.  8) Goan2 chhiong-moa2 iong2-khi3, chhiat-bong7 li7-khui chit e5 jiok8-the2, lai5 kap Chu2 cho3-hoe2 toa3.  9) Sou2-i2 pi2 ki5-tha jim7-ho5 e5 tai7-chi3, goan2 koh-khah hi-bong7 bo5-lun7 toa3 chia a2-si7 toa3 hia, e7-tang3 lai5 hou7 I hoaN-hi2.  10) In-ui7 lan2 tai7-ke long2 tioh8 khi3-kau3 Ki-tok e5 bin7-cheng5 siu7 I e5 sim2-phoaN3, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 thang an3-chiau3 i chai7 se3-kan sou2 cho3 e5, bo5-lun7 si7 ho2 e5 a2-si7 phaiN2 e5, lai5 siu7 eng3-tit e5 po3-tap.

Oa2-kho3 Ki-tok kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-bok8:
	11) In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 chai-iaN2 tioh8 keng3-ui3 Siong7-chu2, sou2-i2 chiah chin7-liong7 lai5 khou2-khng3 lang5. Siong7-chu2 chai-iaN2 lan2 kah chin thiat-te2. Goa2 hi-bong7 lin2 chai7 sim-lai7 ia7 chhneg-chheng-chho2-chho2 lai5 liau2-kai2 goa2.  12) Goan2 m7-si7 siuN7-boeh koh tui3 lin2 tian2 ka-ti7; sit8-chai7 si7 boeh hou7 lin2 u7 li2-iu5 lai5 khoa-iau7 goan2, thang hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 eng3-hu3 hiah-e5 lang5; in chiu7-si7 khoa-iau7 lang5 e5 goa7-mau7, m7 khoa-iau7 lang5 e5 phin2-keh e5 lang5.  13) Goan2 na7 cheng-sin5 put(**bo5) cheng3-siong5, che si7 ui7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2. Goan2 cheng-sin5 na7 cheng3-siong5, che si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2.  14) In-ui7 lan2 siu7 Ki-tok e5 thiong2-ai3 e5 khong3-che3, ki3-jian5 siong-sin3 chit8 e5 lang5 ui7 cheng3-lang5 lai5 si2, an2-ne chiu7-si7 cheng3-lang5 ia7 kap I cho3-hoe2 si2. 15) I ui7 cheng3-lang5 si2, si7 boeh hou7 teh oah8 e5 lang5 m7-thang koh ui7 ka-ti7 lai5 oah8, tioh8 ui7 the3 cheng3-lang5 si2 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Ki-tok lai5 oah8.
	16) An2-ne lan2 m7-thang koh-chai3 eng7 lang5 e5 phiau-chun2 lai5 phoaN3-toan3 jim7-ho5 lang5. Sui-jian5 lan2 u7 chit8 si5 chiau3 lang5 e5 phiau-chun2 lai5 phoaN3-toan3 Ki-tok, tan7-si7 goan2 bo5 koh an2-ne cho3.  17) Bo5-lun7 sim2-mih lang5 na7 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8, i chiu7-si7 sin e5 siu7-cho7-but8: chiu7-si7 ku7 e5 i2-keng koe3-khi3, sin e5 lai5 a7.  18) Chiah-e5 it-chhe3 long2 si7 Siong7-chu2 cho3 e5. I thong-koe3 Ki-tok chiong lan2 chiah-e5 tek8-jin5 pian3-cho3 I e5 peng5-iu2, iu7-koh siuN2-su3 lan2 chit e5 chit-bu7, thang hou7 ki5-tha e5 lang5 ia7 lai5 cho3 I e5 peng5-iu2.  19) Goan2 sou2 thoan5 e5 to7-li2 chiu7-si7 kong2, Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 Ki-tok hou7 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 lai5 cho3 I e5 peng5-iu2. Siong7-chu2 bo5 tui-kiu3 in e5 choe7-ko3. I u7 chiong chit e5 hou7 cheng3-lang5 lai5 cho3 I e5 peng5-iu2 e5 to7-li2 kau hou7 goan2.
	20) Sou2-i2 goan2 ti7 chia ui7 Ki-tok teh kong2-oe7, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-chu2 chhin-sin chioh goan2 e5 chhui3 teh khou2-khng3 lin2: goan2 the3 Ki-tok khun2-kiu5 lin2, lin2 tioh8 hou7 Siong7-chu2 lai5 ka7 lin2 hoa3-tek8 ui5-iu2.  21) Ki-tok long2 m7-bat hoan7-choe7, tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 hou7 I lai5 taN lan2 e5 choe7, che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lan2 in-ui7 kap I kiat-hap8, lai5 hun-hiong2 Siong7-chu2 e5 kong-gi7.

Te7 6 ChiuN

	1) Sou2-i2 goan2 kap Siong7-chu2 cho3-hoe2 kang-chok e5 lang5, khoan3-ko3 lin2 tai7-ke: lin2 ki3-jian5 nia2-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7, chiu7 m7-thang ong2-hui3 chit e5 in-hui7.  2) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 kong2:
“Goa2 hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8 e5 si5, u7 thiaN lin2 e5 ki5-kiu5. Chai7 chin2-kiu3 e5 jit8-chi2, Goa2 u7 pang-chou7 lin2.”
Lin2 tioh8 thiaN! Hian7-chai7 chiu7-si7 nia2-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 e5 si5, kin-na2-jit8 chiu7-si7 tit-kiu3 e5 jit8-chi2!
	3) Goan2 bo5 ai3 jim7-ho5 lang5 lai5 chek-pi7 goan2 e5 thoan5-kau3 kang-chok, sou2-i2 chin7-liong7 bo5 ai3 hong5-hai7 jim7-ho5 lang5.  4) Tian-to3 bo5-lun7 cho3 sim2-mih tai7-chi3, goan2 eng7 choe3-toa7 e5 nai7-sim lai5 jim2-siu7 hoan7-lan7, kan-lan5, khun3-khou2, lai5 piau2-si7 goan2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 lou5-pok8.  5) Goan2 hou7 lang5 phah, hou7 lang5 liah8 khi3 koaiN, siu7 cheng3-lang5 kong-kek; lo5-tong7 koe3-thau5, lian5-jit8 bo5 khun3 bo5 chiah8.  6) Goan2 eng7 sun5-kiat, ti3-sek, nai7-sim, chu5-sian7, oa2-kho3 Seng3 Leng5, eng7 goan2 sun5-chin e5 ai3-cheng5,  7) chin-li2 e5 oe7, kap Siong7-chu2 e5 lek8-liong7, lai5 piau2-si7 goan2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 lou5-pok8. Goan2 eng7 cheng3-gi7 lai5 cho3 kong-kek kap chu7-oe7 e5 bu2-khi3.  8) Goan2 siu7 kong-eng5, ia7 siu7 thi2-jiok8, siu7 bu2-jiok8, ia7 siu7 chan3-iong5. Goan2 hou7 lang5 khoan2-thai7 cho3 be5-hek8 lang5 e5, m7-ku2 goan2 kong2 chin-li2;  9) hou7 lang5 khoaN3-cho3 bu5-beng5 siau2-chut, m7-ku2 cheng3-lang5 long2 bat goan2; hou7 lang5 khoaN3-cho3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 si2-lang5, tan7-si7 lin2 khoaN3, goan2 teh oah8. Sui-jian5 siu7 chhu2-hoat8, m7-ku2 bo5 hou7 lang5 thai5-si2;  10) sui-jian5 u7 pi-siong e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 si5-siong5 khoai3-lok8; goan2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 pin5-kiong5, tan7-si7 goan2 hou7 chin-che7 lang5 hu3-ju7; goan2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 poaN3-hang7, tan7-si7 sit8-chai7 tak8 hang7 u7.
	11) Ko-lim5-to e5 peng5-iu2 a3! goan2 i2-keng than2-pek8 tui3 lin2 kong2 a3, goan2 e5 tou7-liong7 ia7 pang3 chin khoah a3.  12) Goan2 m7-bat tui3 lin2 bo5 tou7-liong7, si7 lin2 tui3 goan2 bo5 tou7-liong7 e5.  13) TaN goa2 ka7 lin2 khoaN3-cho3 kiaN2-ji5 lai5 ka7 lin2 kong2: goan2 an3-choaN2 tui3-thai7 lin2, lin2 ia7 tioh8 an2-ne lai5 tui3-thai7 goan2. Lin2 e5 tou7-liong7 tioh8 pang3 khah khoah le3!

Keng2-kai3 pat8-kau3 e5 eng2-hiong2:
	14) M7-thang chiong bo5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 khoaN3-cho3 tong5-teng2 e5, siuN7-boeh lai5 kap in kau-chhap, in-ui7 be7-eng7-tit an2-ne. Kong-cheng3 kap put-hoat kam2 e7-tang3 cho3-hoe2? Kong-beng5 kap ou-am3 kam2 e7-tang3 toa3 cho3-hoe2?  15) Ki-tok kam2 e7-tang3 kap Mou5-kui2 ho5-hap8? Sin3 Chu2 e5 kap bo5 sin3 Chu2 e5 u7 sim2-mih kiong7-thong e5 sou2-chai7?  16) Siong7-chu2 e5 seng3-tian7 kap pat8-kau3 e5 ngou2-siong7 kam2 e7-tang3 tho3-hiap8? In-ui7 lan2 si7 eng2-seng Siong7-chu2 e5 seng3-tian7! Chiau3 Siong7-chu2 pun2-sin kong2:
“Goa2 boeh kap in toa3 kang7 chhu3 lai7, seng-oah8 chai7 in tiong-kan, Goa2 boeh cho3 in e5 Siong7-chu2, boeh hou7 in cho3 Goa2 e5 chu2- bin5.”
17) Sou2-i2 Chu2 kong2:
“Lin2 tioh8 li7-khui in, tioh8 kap in hun-li7. 
M7-thang chiap-chhiok bo5 chheng-khi3 e5, 
An2-ne Goa2 chiu7 e7 ka7 lin2 chiap-siu7.  
18) Goa2 boeh cho3 lin2 e5 lau7-pe7, 
Boeh hou7 lin2 cho3 Goa2 e5 kiaN2-ji5, 
Choan5-leng5 e5 Chu2 an2-ne kong2.”

Te7 7 ChiuN

	1) Chhin-ai3 e5 peng5-iu2, chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 long2 si7 tui3 lan2 tah-eng3 e5! Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 kiat-cheng7 ka-ti7, siau-ti5 it-chhe3 e7 u3-oe3 lan2 jiok8-the2 kap leng5-hun5 e5 tai7-chi3, keng3-ui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 seng-oah8, chin7-liong7 chiaN5-cho3 oan5-choan5 sin5-seng3 e5.

Po2-lo5 e5 hoaN-hi2:
	2) Tioh8 iong5-lap8 goan2 toa3 lin2 sim-lai7. Goan2 m7-bat tui3 lang5 cho3 put-cheng3, m7-bat hai7 lang5 tui7-loh8, ia7 m7-bat li7-iong7 lang5.  3) Goa2 m7-si7 kong2 an2-ne lai5 teh chek-pi7 lin2; in-ui7 goa2 i2-cheng5 ia7 u7 kong2-koe3, lin2 si7 goan2 chhin-ai3 e5. Bo5-lun7 lan2 oah8 a2-si7 si2, lan2 si5-siong5 cho3-hoe2.  4) Goa2 choat8-tui3 sin3-nai7 lin2, lin2 si7 goa2 e5 kong-eng5! Chai7 goan2 it-chhe3 hoan7-lan7 tiong, goan2 iau2-ku2 u7 chap8-hun e5 an-ui3, chhiong-moa2 hoaN-hi2.
	5) Goan2 lian5 lai5-kau3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 liau2-au7 ia7 bo5 hiu-sek. To3-chhu3 tu2-tioh8 khun3-lan5, goa7-bin7 u7 hun-cheng, lai7-sim u7 khiong2-hong5.  6) Tan7-si7 an-ui3 song3-khi3 e5 lang5 e5 Siong7-chu2, an-pai5 The5-to lai5 ka7 goan2 an-ui3.  7) Put-tan7 si7 in-ui7 i lai5, thiaN-tioh8 i po3-ko3 lin2 an3-choaN2 ka7 i an-ui3, goan2 ia7 tit-tioh8 hui-siong5 e5 an-ui3.  I ka7 goan2 kong2 lin2 loa7-nih teh su-liam7 goa2, loa7-nih thong3-hoe3 lin2 koe3-khi3 e5 heng5-ui5, loa7-nih jiat8-sim the3 goa2 pian7-kai2, sou2-i2 goa2 hian7-chai7 koh-khah hoaN-hi2.
	8) Siat-su2 goa2 hit tiuN phe hou7 lin2 pi-siong, goa2 ia7 be7 in-ui7 sia2 hit tiuN lai5 phau7-khiam2. Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 hit tiuN phe chiam7-si5 hou7 lin2 pi-siong e5 si5, kho2-leng5 u7 kam2-kak m7-koe3-sim.  9) Tan7-si7 goa2 hian7-chai7 chin hoaN-hi2 --- che m7-si7 in-ui7 goa2 hou7 lin2 pi-siong, si7 in-ui7 lin2 e5 pi-siong hou7 lin2 kai2-pian3 chok-hong. Hit e5 pi-siong si7 Siong7-chu2 an-pai5 e5, sou2-i2 goan2 bo5 hou7 lin2 siu7-tioh8 sun2-hai7.  10) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 an-pai5 hit e5 pi-siong lai5 hou7 lin2 hoe3(**hoe2)-kai2, lai5 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3, sou2-i2 bo5 sim2-mih thang kam2-kak m7-koe3-sim! Tan7-si7 se3-siok8 e5 pi-siong e7 hou7 lang5 si2-bong5.  11) Lin2 khoaN3 Siong7-chu2 an-pai5 lin2 chit e5 pi-siong, u7 san2-seng sim2-mih kiat-ko2: chit e5 pi-siong hou7 lin2 pian3-seng5 loa7-nih jin7-chin, loa7-nih jiat8-sim boeh lai5 cheng3-beng5 lin2 e5 chheng-pek8! Chit chiong2 hun3-khai3, chiah-nih8 kiaN-sim, chit khoan2 su-liam7, chiah-nih8 jiat8-sim, chiah-nih8 iong2-iok8 lai5 khian2-chek put-hoat e5 heng5-ui5! chai7 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, bo5-lun7 tui3 tah chit8 tiam2 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, lin2 i2-keng cheng3-beng5 bo5 chho3-gou7 a7.
	12) Sou2-i2 sui-jian5 goa2 sia2 hit tiuN phe hou7 lin2, he m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 hit e5 cho3 m7-tioh8 e5 lang5, a2-si7 hit e5 chiah8-khui e5 lang5 sia2 e5. Goa2 sia2 hit tiuN, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, hou7 lin2 beng5-pek8 lin2 tui3 goan2 e5 jiat8-seng5 u7 loa7 chhim.  13) Chiu7 si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, goan2 tit-tioh8 an-ui3.
	Goan2 put-tan7 tit-tioh8 an-ui3; thiaN-tioh8 The5-to kong2-khi2 lin2 tai7-ke an3-choaN2 hou7 i sim-lai7 bo5 khoa3-li7, goan2 chiu7 koh-khah hoaN-hi2.  14) Goa2 tui3 i tian2 lin2 e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 sit8-chai7 bo5 hou7 goa2 lau3-khui3. Goan2 koe3-khi3 long2 tui3 lin2 kong2 sit8-chai7 oe7. Kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, goan2 tui3 The5-to sou2 tian2 e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 i2-keng cheng3-beng5 si7 sit8-chai7 e5.  15) Sou2-i2 i chit8-e7 siuN7-khi2 lin2 tai7-ke sim-kam i3-goan7 lai5 hok8-chiong5, kiong-kiong-keng3-keng3 lai5 hoan-geng5 i, i tui3 lin2 e5 ai3-cheng5 chiu7 lu2 chhim.  16) Goa2 hui-siong5 hoaN-hi2 e7-tang3 oan5-choan5 sin3-nai7 lin2!

Te7 8 ChiuN

Ki-tok-tou5 e5 hong7-hian3:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ai3 hou7 lin2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 tui3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 kok kau3-hoe7 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3.  2) In siu7-tioh8 chhian-sin ban7-khou2 e5 giam5-keh e5 kho2-giam7. In sui-jian5 chin san3, tan7-si7 in-ui7 hiah-nih8 khoai3-lok8, sou2 i2 hui-siong5 khong2-khai3 lai5 lok8-koan.  3) Goa2 sit8-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2, in u7 chin7 in e5 lek8-liong7, sim7-chi3 chhiau-koe3 in e5 lek8-liong7 lai5 chin2-che3.  4) In chu7-tong7, jiat8-sim khun2-kiu5 goan2 tioh8 in2-chun2 in chham-ka kiu3-che3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5.  5) Che si7 chhiau-koe3 goan2 sou2 ki5-thai7 e5! In tai7-seng chiong chu7-ki2 hian3 hou7 Chu2, jian5-au7 chun-chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, ia7 chiong in ka-ti7 hian3 hou7 goan2.  6) Sou2-i2 goan2 chiu7 khoan3-ko3 i2-keng khai-si2 chit hang7 kang-chok e5 The5-to, kio3 i tioh8 ke3-siok8, iu7-koh pang-chou7 lin2 oan5-seng5 chit hang7 tek8-piat8 e5 chu5-sian7 kang-chok.  7) Lin2 ki3-jian5 chai7 kok hong-bin7; chiu7-si7 sin3-sim, khau2-chai5, ti3-hui7, pang-chou7 lang5 e5 jiat8-seng5, i2-kip8 tui3 goan2 e5 ai3-sim long2 chin chhiong-chiok, sou2-i2 goan2 ai3 lin2 tui3 chit hang7 chu5-sian7 kang-chok ia7 tioh8 khong2-khai3.
	8) Goa2 kong2 an2-ne m7-si7 boeh beng7-leng7 lin2, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 chai-iaN2 pat8-lang5 loa7-nih jiat8-sim pang-chou7 lang5, lai5 khoaN3 lin2 ka-ti7 e5 ai3-sim u7 loa7-nih sun5-chin.  9) In-ui7 lin2 chai-iaN2 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 in-hui7: I sui-jian5 chin hu3-ju7, tan7-si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2, chu7-goan7 pian3-cho3 pin5-kiong5, thang eng7 I e5 pin5-kiong5 lai5 hou7 lin2 hu3-ju7.
	10) Koan-he7 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, goa2 e5 i3-kian3 si7 an2-ne: taN lin2 choe3-ho2 tioh8 oan5-seng5 lin2 ku7-ni5 sou2 khai-si2 e5 tai7-chi3. Lin2 put-tan7 si7 siong7 tai7-seng sit8-heng5 e5, ia7-si7 goan7-i3 sit8-heng5 e5.  11) An2-ne lin2 tioh8 ke3-siok8 lai5 oan5-seng5, tioh8 chin7 lin2 e5 lek8-liong7 khi3 cho3.  12) In-ui7 lin2 na7 u7 lok8-koan e5 jiat8-seng5, Siong7-chu2 e7 chiap-lap8 lin2 chiong u7 e5 theh8 chhut-lai5 lok8-koan, bo5 ai3 lin2 chiong bo5 e5 theh8 chhut-lai5 lok8-koan.
	13) Goa2 m7-si7 siuN7-boeh kio3 lin2 taN tang7-taN3 lai5 kiu3-che3 pat8-lang5;  14) lin2 hian7-chai7 ki3-jian5 hu3-ju7, tioh8 kiu3-che3 pin5-kiong5 e5 lang5, che si7 kong-to7 e5 tai7-chi3. An2-ne lin2 pin5-kiong5, in hu3-ju7 e5 si5, in chiu7 e7 kiong3-kip lin2. Na7 an2-ne siang-peng5 chiu7 e7 kong-peN5.  15) Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Tun2-chek chin-che7 e5 lang5 Ia7 bo5 chhun,
Tun2 chek chit8-sut-a2 e5 lang5 Ia7 bo5 khiam3.”

The5-to kap i e5 tang5-phoaN7:
	16) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 hou7 The5-to chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 chiah-nih jiat8-sim lai5 pang-chou7 lin2.  17) I put-tan7 hoaN-hi2 chiap-siu7 goan2 e5 iau-kiu5, ji5-chhiaN2 hui-siong5 jiat8-sim boeh pang-chou7 lin2, chu7-tong7 koat-i3 boeh khi3 lin2 hia.  18) Goan2 boeh chhe-phai3 chit8 e5 hiaN-ti7 kap i cho3-hoe2 khi3. Chit e5 hiaN-ti7 chai7 soan-iong5 Hok-im e5 kang-chok siong7, siu7 kok kau3-hoe7 e5 chheng-chan3.  19) Put-tan7 an2-ne, goan2 ui7 Chu2 e5 eng5-iau7, sit8-heng5 chu5-sian7 e5 kang-chok, lai5 piau2-beng5 goan2 goan7-i3 pang-chou7 lang5 e5 si5, kok kau3-hoe7 jim7-beng7 i lai5 kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 kang-chok.
	20) Goan2 kiaN in2-khi2 lang5 e5 put-peng5, sio2-sim lai5 chhu2-li2 chiah-e5 sou3-bok8 chin toa7 e5 lok8-koan.  21) Goan2 e5 bok8-tek put-tan7 si7 chai7 Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, chiu7-si7 chai7 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 ia7 cho3 cheng3-cheng3-tong-tong e5 tai7-chi3.
	22) Sou2-i2 goan2 boeh chhe-phai3 chit8 e5 hiaN-ti7 kap in cho3-hoe2 khi3; goan2 u7 ka7 i kho2-giam7 chin-che7 pai2, chai-iaN2 i si5-siong5 jiat8-sim pang-chou7 lang5. TaN i ki3-jian5 hiah-nih8 sin3-nai7 lin2, i e7 koh-khah jiat8-sim pang-chou7.  23) Kong2 kau3 The5-to, i si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh oan7-chou7 lin2, kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 teh kang-chok e5 tang5-phoaN7; koan-he7 boeh kap i cho3-hoe2 khi3 e5 ki5-tha e5 hiaN-ti7, in si7 tai7-piau2 kok kau3-hoe7 lai5 teh eng5-iau7 Ki-tok.  24) Lin2 tioh8 tui3 in piau2-si7 ai3-sim, thang hou7 kok kau3-hoe7 seng5-jin7 lin2 e5 ai3-sim, lai5 chai-iaN2 goan2 sou2 khoa-chiong2 lin2 e5 tai7-chi3 peng3 bo5 m7-tioh8.

Te7 9 ChiuN

Pang-chou7 sin3 Ki-tok e5 hiaN-ti7:
	1) Koan-he7 oan7-chou7 Iu5-thai3 Seng2 e5 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 sit8-chai7 bo5 su-iau3 sia2.  2) Goa2 chai-iaN2 lin2 u7 goan7-i3 boeh oan7-chou7. Lin2 e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 i2-keng u7 tui3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 lang5 khoa-iau7. Goa2 u7 kong2, “Hi-lah8 e5 hiaN-ti7 chu7 ku7-ni5 khi2 chiu7 goan7-i3 boeh oan7-chou7.” Lin2 e5 jiat8-seng5 kam2-tong7 in toa7 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5.  3) TaN goa2 boeh chhe-phai3 chiah-e5 hiaN-ti7 khi3 lin2 hia, goa2 khoa-iau7 lin2 boeh lok8-koan e5 tai7-chi3 chiah be7 pian3-cho3 khang-chhui3 teh pou7-chih8; tan7-si7 chiau3 goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, lin2 e7 chun2-pi7 lai5 oan7-chou7.  4) Na7 m7-si7 an2-ne, ban7-it Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 lang5 kap goa2 cho3-hoe2 khi3, khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 long2 bo5 chun2-pi7, goa2 tui3 lin2 e5 sin3-jim7 chiu7 long2 lok8-khong, an2-ne mai3 kong2 lin2 e5 kian3-siau3, goan2 e7 koh-khah kian3-siau3.  5) Sou2-i2 goa2 siuN7-kong2 u7 su-iau3 chhui-pek chiah-e5 hiaN-ti7 pi2 goa2 khah tai7-seng khi3 lin2 hia, chiong lin2 tah-eng3 boeh hian3 e5 lok8-koan chun2-pi7 hou7 pian7. An2-ne goa2 kau3-ui7 e5 si5 lin2 e5 lok8-koan chiu7 pian7-pian7, piau2-si7 lin2 si7 goan7-i3 koan-hian3 e5, m7-si7 pi7-pek chiah hian3 e5.
	6) Lin2 tioh8 e7-ki3-tit: cheng2-chi2 ia7 chio2 e5 lang5, siu-seng5 e7 chio2; cheng2-chi2 ia7 che7 e5 lang5, siu-seng5 e7 chin-che7.  7) Sou2-i2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5, tioh8 chiau3 i sou2 koat-teng7 e5 lai5 lok8-koan, m7-thang siuN7 m7-kam, a2-si7 bian2-kiong2 lai5 cho3; in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 thiaN3 hoaN-hi2 lok8-koan e5 lang5.  8) Siong7-chu2 e7-tang3 siuN2-su3 pi2 lin2 sou2 su-iau3 e5 koh-khah che7, thang hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 eng3-hu3 lin2 sou2 khiam3 e5, sim7-chi3 iau2 u7 chhun, thang lai5 cho3 it-chhe3 e5 ho2-su7.  9) Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“I khong2-khai3 siuN2-su3 hou7 san2-chhiah-lang5,
I e5 jin5-chu5 eng2-kiu2 chun5-siok8.”
10) Kiong3-kip cheng2-chi2 hou7 ia7-chi2-e5, iu7 siuN2-su3 in chiah8-niu5 e5 Siong7-chu2, ia7 e7 kiong3-kip lin2 sou2 su-iau3 e5 cheng2-chi2, ji5-chhiaN2 hou7 hiah-e5 cheng2-chi2 tiong2-seng5, hou7 lin2 khong2-khai3 e5 heng5-ui5 san2-seng hong-hu3 e5 siu-seng5.  11) I e7 hou7 lin2 si5-siong5 hu3-chiok, thang-ho2 si5-siong5 khong2-khai3, lai5 hou7 chin-che7 lang5 keng-koe3 goan2 e5 chhiu2 tit-tioh8 lin2 e5 lok8-koan, lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2.  12) In-ui7 lin2 sou2 cho3 chit e5 hok8-bu7, put-tan7 eng3-hu3 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5 khoat-hoat8, ji5-chhiaN2 ia7 e7 hou7 in tui3 Siong7-chu2 san2-seng ban7-hun e5 kam2-in.  13) Iu7-koh in-ui7 u7 lin2 chit e5 hok8-bu7 lai5 cho3 cheng3-ki3, chin-che7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 seng5-jin7 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im teh hok8-chiong5, khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 tui3 in i2-kip8 ki5-tha e5 lang5 khong2-khai3 lok8-koan, e7 lai5 kui eng5-iau7 hou7 Siong7-chu2.  14) Iu7-koh in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 lin2 tek8-piat8 e5 in-hui7, in e7 phau7 chin toa7 e5 ai3-sim ui7 lin2 ki5-to2.  15) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 lan2 be7-tang3 kou2-ke3 e5 in-su3!

Te7 10 ChiuN

Po2-lo5 pian7-kai2 i e5 thoan5-kau3 kang-chok:
	1) Goa2 Po2-lo5 chhin-sin khou2-khng3 lin2. Lang5 kong2 goa2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5 chin khiam-pi, tan7-si7 bo5 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, tui3 lin2 chin bo5 kheh-khi3. Goa2 eng7 Ki-tok e5 un-jiu5 kap jin5-chu5 khun2-kiu5 lin2:  2) Goa2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, m7-thang hou7 goa2 put-tek-i2 tui3 lin2 bo5 kheh-khi3; tui3 hiah-e5 phe-pheng5 goa2 an3-chiau3 se3-siok8 e5 tong7-ki teh cho3-su7 e5 lang5, goa2 siong-sin3 goa2 e7 tui3 in bo5 kheh-khi3.  3) U7-iaN2, goan2 toa3 ti7 chit se3-kan; tan7-si7 goan2 m7-si7 kin-ki3 se3-siok8 e5 tong7-ki lai5 teh chian3-tou3.  4) Goan2 chai7 chian3-tou3 tiong sou2 eng7 e5 bu2-khi3, m7-si7 chit se3-kan e5 bu2-khi3, si7 eng7 Siong7-chu2 kiong5-lek8 e5 bu2-khi3 lai5 teh hui2-hoai7 iau3-sai3. Goan2 hui2-hoai7 hi-ke2 e5 gian5-lun7;  5) goan2 siau-ti5 hong5-hai7 lang5 jim7-sek Siong7-chu2 hiah-e5 it-chhe3 chu7-ko e5 chou2-gai7-but8; goan2 ap-hok8 it-chhe3 e5 su-siong2 hou7 in sun7-chiong5 Ki-tok.  6) Lin2 cheng3-beng5 lin2 oan5-choan5 tiong-sim liau2-au7, goan2 chiu7 boeh lai5 chek-hoat8 sou2 u7 bo5 tiong-sim e5 heng5-ui5.
	7) Lin2 koan-chhat su7-but8 si7 teh koan-chhat goa7-piau2. Na7 u7 lang5 jim7-ui5 i chu7-ki2 si7 siok8 Ki-tok e5, an2-ne i tioh8 hoan2-seng2 i ka-ti7, in-ui7 i siok8 Ki-tok, goan2 ia7 siok8 Ki-tok.  8) Siat-su2 goa2 u7 tam7-poh8-a2 koe3-thau5 khoa-iau7 Chu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 goan2 e5 koan5-peng3, goa2 be7 kam2-kak kian3-siau3. Chit e5 koan5-peng3 si7 boeh kian3-lip8 lin2 e5 tek-heng7, m7-si7 boeh hui2-hoai7 lin2 e5.  9) Goa2 bo5 ai3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 sia2 chit tiuN phe lai5 boeh haN2-kiaN lin2.  10) U7 lang5 it-teng7 e7 kong2, “Po2-lo5 e5 phe sia2 tioh8 li7-hai7 koh kiam5, tan7-si7 i kap lan2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5 chin un-jiu5, sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 ia7 bo5 an3-choaN2 chin tek8-piat8.”  11) Chit khoan2 e5 lang5 tioh8 chai-iaN2 goan2 bo5 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, phe lai7 sou2 sia2 e5 tai7-chi3, kap goan2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, sou2 boeh cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 bo5 chha.
	12) Tong-jian5 goan2 m7 kaN2 chiong goan2 ka-ti7 lai5 kap hiah-e5 chiong ka-ti7 kou2-ke3 hiah koan5 e5 lang5 lai5 chha-piat8 a2-si7 pi2-kau3. In na2 hiah gong7! In siat ka-ti7 e5 phiau-chun2 lai5 kou2-ke3 ka-ti7, iu7-koh eng7 ka-ti7 e5 phiau-chun2 lai5 phoaN3-toan3 ka-ti7.  13) Tan7-si7 lun7 khi2 goan2 ka-ti7, goan2 sou2 khoa-iau7 e5 be7 chhiau-koe3 it-teng7 e5 kai3-han7; e7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 hun-phoe3 hou7 goan2 e5 kang-chok e5 hoan7-ui5 lai7. Che pau-ham5 goan2 ti7 lin2 hia e5 kang-chok chai7-lai7.  14) Lin2 ki3-jian5 chai7 hiah-e5 hoan7-ui5 lai7, goan2 teng2-pai2 khi3 lin2 hia thoan5 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im e5 si5, peng3 bo5 chhiau-koe3 hoan7-ui5.  15) Sou2-i2 goan2 be7 chhiau-chhut Siong7-chu2 ka7 goan2 an-pai5 e5 hoan7-ui5, lai5 tian2 pat8-lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3. Goan2 tian-to3 hi-bong7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim lu2 cheng7-tiong2, goan2 e7-tang3 chai7 lin2 tiong-kan si5-siong5 chun-chiau3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 che3-teng7 e5 hoan7-ui5, lai5 cho3 lu2 toa7 e5 kang-chok.  16) An2-ne goan2 chiu7 e7-tang3 khi3 lin2 hia i2-goa7 e5 pat8 te7-khu soan-pou3 Hok-im, m7-bian2 chai7 pat8-lang5 e5 kai3-han7 lai7, khoa-iau7 i2-keng oan5-seng5 e5 kang-chok.
	17) Seng3-keng ki3-chai3 kong2, “Na7 u7 lang5 ai3 khoa-khau2, tioh8 khoa-iau7 Chu2 sou2 cho3 e5.”   18) In-ui7 siu7 Chu2 hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8 e5, si7 siu7 Chu2 chheng-chan3 e5 lang5, m7-si7 chu7-chheng ka-ti7 si7 ho2 e5 lang5.

Te7 11 ChiuN

Po2-lo5 kap ke2 Su3-tou5:
	1) Siat-su2 goa2 tam7-poh8-a2 tong3-gong7, ng3-bang7 lin2 ka7 goa2 iong5-jim2. ChhiaN2 lin2 tioh8 ka7 goa2 iong5-jim2!  2) Goa2 chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-chu2 thiaN3 lin2 kah e7 khi2 chit-tou3; in-ui7 lin2 chhin-chhiuN7 sun5-kiat e5 chai7-sek-li2, goa2 tah-eng3 boeh phit-phoe3 hou7 chi2-u7 chit8 e5 tiong7-hu Ki-tok.  3) Chhin-chhiuN7 Ha7-oa hou7 choa5 e5 hoa-gian5 khau2-gi2 phian3-khi3, goa2 khiong2-kiaN lin2 e5 sim e7 siu7 iu2-hek8, e7 pang3-sak lin2 tui3 Ki-tok moa2-pak sun5-kiate5 choan5-seng5.  4) In-ui7 na7 u7 lang5 khi3 lin2 hia soan-iau7 leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 Ia5-sou, peng3 m7-si7 goan2 sou2 soan-iau7 Hit-ui7, lin2 chiu7 sui5-pian7 chiap-siu7; lin2 ia7 chiap-siu7 leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 seng3-leng5 kap hok-im, he kap lin2 tui3 goan2 sou2 nia2-siu7 e5 Seng3 Leng5 kap Hok-im oan5-choan5 bo5 kang7-khoan2.
	5) Goa2 siong-sin3 goa2 chit8-sut-a2 ia7 be7 su lin2 hiah-e5 chin tek8-piat8 e5 ‘su3-tou5’!  6) Bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 goa2 kong2-oe7 si7 goa7-hang5 e5, tan7-si7 chai7 ti3-sek hong-bin7 peng3 m7-si7 goa7-hang5 e5; chit tiam2 goa2 bo5-lun7 sim2-mih si5, bo5-lun7 chai7 sim2-mih cheng5-heng5, i2-keng u7 tui3 lin2 piau2-beng5.
	7) Goa2 bian2-hui3 tui3 lin2 soan-pou3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Hok-im; ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 ui2-tai7 e5, goa2 chiong ka-ti7 kang3-ke7. goa2 an2-ne cho3 kam2 m7-tioh8?  8) Goa2 ti7 lin2 hia teh kang-chok e5 tiong-kan, goa2 e5 sou2-hui3 si7 pat8 keng kau3-hoe7 hu3 hou7 goa2 e5. E7-eng7-tit kong2 goa2 pak8-siap pat8 keng kau3-hoe7 lai5 pang-chou7 lin2.  9) Goa2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 tiong-kan, u7 su-iau3 chiN5 e5 si5 ia7 bo5 ma5-hoan5 lin2 lai5 pang-chou7 goa2; in-ui7 goa2 sou2 su-iau3 e5, tui3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 lai5 e5 hiaN-ti7 long2 u7 toa3 lai5 hou7 goa2; koe3-khi3 si7 an2-ne, chiong-lai5 ia7-si7 an2-ne; goa2 be7 thoa-lui7 lin2!  10) Ki3-jian5 sin3-hong7 Ki-tok e5 chin-li2, goa2 kaN2 kong2 bo5-lun7 khi3-kau3 A-kai-a Seng2 e5 tah chit8 ui7, to e7-tang3 po2-chhi5 goa2 chit e5 chu7-khoa.  11) Si7 an3-choaN2 goa2 kong2 an2-ne? Si7 in-ui7 goa2 bo5 thiaN3 lin2 si7 bo5? Siong7-chu2 chai-iaN2 goa2 u7 thiaN3 lin2.
	12) Ui7-tioh8 boeh pi7-bian2 ki5-tha hiah-e5 chu7-chheng “su3-tou5” e5, chia3 te5 kong2 in ia7 eng7 kap goan2 kang7-khoan2 e5 hong-hoat teh kang-chok, goan2 boeh koh ke3-siok8 hian7-chai7 e5 kang-chok.  13) Hiah-e5 lang5 m7-si7 chin-chiaN3 e5 su3-tou5, in si7 ke2 su3-tou5, cho3-su7 kan-cha3, ke2 kah na2 chhin-chhiuN7 Ki-tok chin-chiaN3 e5 Su3-tou5.  14) Che bo5 sim2-mih hi-ki5! Lian5 Sat-tan3 ia7 e7-tang3 chiong i ka-ti7 ke2 cho3 kong-beng5 e5 thian-sai3!  15) Sou2-i2 Sat-tan3 e5 lou5-pok8 na7 boeh ke2 kah na2 chhin-chhiuN7 cheng3-gi7 e5 hok8-bu7-chia2, peng3 bo5 sim2-mih khun3-lan5. Choe3-au7 in e7 siu7-tioh8 in sou2 cho3 e5 po3-eng3.

Po2-lo5 cho3 Su3-tou5 e5 khun3-khou2:
	16) goa2 koh kong2 chit8 pai2: lin2 m7-thang siuN7-cho3 goa2 si7 gong7-lang5. Tan7-si7 lin2 na7-si7 an2-ne siuN7, chi3-chio2 tioh8 chun2-cho3 gong7-lang5 ka7 goa2 khoan2-thai7, thang hou7 goa2 u7 tam7-poh8-a2 thang chu7-khoa.  17) Tong-jian5 goa2 chim2-ma2 teh kong2 e5 m7-si7 Chu2 sou2 ai3 goa2 kong2 e5; goa2 sit8-chai7 chhin-chhiuN7 gong7-lang5 teh kong2 chit hang7 chu7-khoa e5 tai7-chi3.  18) Tan7-si7 ki3-jian5 u7 chin-che7 lang5 kan-taN ui7-tioh8 se3-siok8 e5 li2-iu5 lai5 chu7-khoa, goa2 ia7 boeh an2-ne.  19) Lin2 ka-ti7 hiah-nih8 chhong-beng5, sou2-i2 goan7-i3 iong5-jim2 gong7-lang5!  20) Na7 u7 lang5 ka7 lin2 chhe-eng7, ka7 lin2 li7-iong7, ka7 lin2 ham7-hai7, ka7 lin2 khoaN3-khin, a2-si7 ka7 lin2 sian3 chhui3-phe2, lin2 long2 e7 iong5-jim2.  21) Goa2 chin kian3-siau3 tioh8 chu7-jin7: goan2 koe3-khi3 siuN bo5 taN2 m7 kaN2 an2-ne cho3!
	Tan7-si7 na7 u7 lang5 kaN2 tian2 sim2-mih --- goa2 chhin-chhiuN7 gong7-lang5 teh kong2 --- goa2 ia7 kaN2 tian2.  22) In si7 Hi-pek-lai5-lang5 a2 m7-si7? goa2 ma7 si7. In si7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 si7 bo5? goa2 ma7 si7. In si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 kiaN2-sun a2 m7-si7? Goa2 ma7 si7.  23) In si7 Ki-tok e5 pok8-jin5 a2 m7-si7? goa2 kong2-tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh khi2-siau2 --- tan7-si7 goa2 si7 pi2 in khah ho2 e5 pok8-jin5! goa2 pi2 in khah lo5-khou2. goa2 che7-kaN che7 pi2 in khah che7 pai2, goa2 hou7 lang5 eng7 piN phah khah che7 e7, goa2 pi2 in khah chiap8 tu2-tioh8 seN-si2 e5 koan-thau5.  24) Goa2 hou7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 eng7 piN phah gou7 pai2, mui2 pai2 saN-chap8-kau2 e7;  25) hou7 Lo5-ma2-lang5 eng7 piN phah saN pai2, u7 chit8 pai2 hou7 lang5 tim3 chioh8-thau5; saN pai2 tu2-tioh8 chun5 long3-phoa3, u7 chit8 pai2 toa3 hai2-nih phiau-liu5 ji7-chap8-si3 sio2-si5.  26) Li2-heng5 tiong chin-che7 pai2 tu2-tioh8 toa7-chui2 kap chhiuN2-kiap e5 gui5-hiam2, tu2-tioh8 Iu5-thai3 tong5-pau kap Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 ham7-hai7; ia7 u7 ke-chhi7 e5 gui5-hiam2, khong3-ia2 e5 gui5-hiam2, hai2-iuN5 e5 gui5-hiam2, ke2 peng5-iu2 e5 gui5-hiam2.  27) U7 lo5-tong7; u7 thoa-boa5; siong5-siong5 cho3 kah bo5 khun3; iu7 iau iu7 chhui3-ta; chiap8-chiap8 chiah8 bo5 pa2, bo5 sou2-chai7 thang siam2-pi7 hong-song, a2-si7 bo5 saN thang chheng7.  28) Mai3 kong2 pat8 hang7, ui7-tioh8 sou2 u7 e5 kau3-hoe7, mui2 jit8 hoan5-lo2 khoa3 ti7 sim-thau5.  29) Na7 u7 lang5 loan2-jiok8, goa2 kam2 be7 kam2-kak loan2-jiok8? Na7 u7 lang5 hoan7-choe7, goa2 e5 sim-hoe2 kam2 be7 toh8 khi2-lai5.
	30) Goa2 na7 tioh8 chu7-khoa, boeh chu7-khoa piau2-si7 goa2 loan2-jiok8 hiah-e5 tai7-chi3.  31) Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 Thian-hu7 Siong7-chu2 --- goan7 I e5 mia5 eng2-oan2 siu7 chan3-bi2 --- I chai-iaN2 goa2 m7-si7 teh kong2 peh8-chhat8.  32) Goa2 ti7 Tai7-ma2-sek e5 si5, A-li2-tat8-ong5 chhiu2-e7 e5 chong2-tok, pou3-ti3 oe7-peng toa3 siaN5-mng5 boeh liah8 goa2.  33) Tan7-si7 u7 lang5 eng7 bi2-na5 tui3 siaN5-chhiuN5 e5 phoa3-khiah ka7 goa2 lui7 loh8-lai5 hou7 goa2 thoat-li7 i e5 chhiu2.

Te7 12 ChiuN

Hian2-si7 kap khe2-si7:
	1) sui-jian5 bo5 li7-ek, goa2 bo5 chu7-khoa be7-eng7-tit. TaN goa2 boeh lai5 kong2-khi2 Chu2 hian2-hian7 lai5 ka7 goa2 khe2-si7 e5 tai7-chi3.  2) Goa2 chai-iaN2 u7 chit8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 chap8-si3 ni5 cheng5 pi7 chhoa7 khi3-kau3 choe3 ko e5 thiN-teng2 (goa2 m7 chai-iaN2 che si7 sit8-chai7 hoat-seng e5 tai7-chi3, a2-si7 Siong7-chu2 tui3 i hian2-si7 e5 --- Siong7-chu2 chiah chai-iaN2).  3) Goa2 koh kong2 chit8 pai2, goa2 chai-iaN2 chit e5 lang5 pi7 chhoa7 khi3-kau3 Lok8-hng5 (goa2 m7 chai-iaN2 che si7 sit8-chai7 hoat-seng e5 tai7-chi3, a2-si7 siong7-chu2 tui3 i hian2-si7 e5 --- Siong7-chu2 chiah chai-iaN2),  4) ti7 hia i thiaN-kiN3 be7-tang3 eng7 oe7 piau2-beng5, lang5 e5 chhui3 be7-eng7-tit lau7-siap8 e5 tai7-chi3.  5) Sou2-i2 goa2 boeh tian2 chit e5 lang5 e5 tai7-chi3 --- tan7-si7 ti5-liau2 chu7-khoa hiah-e5 cheng3-beng5 goa2 loan2-jiok8 e5 tai7-chi3 i2-goa7, bo5 boeh chu7-khoa goa2 ka-ti7 e5 tai7-chi3.  6) Siat-su2 goa2 ai3 boeh chu7-khoa, ia7 bo5 sng3-cho3 gong7-lang5, in-ui7 goa2 boeh kong2 sit8-chai7 oe7.  Tan7-si7 goa2 m7 chu7-khoa, in-ui7 goa2 bo5 ai3 lang5 pi2 in sit8-chai7 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 sou2 cho3 e5, thiaN-kiN3 goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, ka7 goa2 kou2-ke3 khah koan5.
	7) Ui7-tioh8 m7 hou7 goa2 in-ui7 tit-tioh8 o3-biau7 e5 khe2-si7 lai5 hong-sin5, u7 goa2 chit8 e5 chin kan-khou2 e5 jiok8-the2-siong7 e5 cheng3-thau5. Chit e5 cheng3-thau5 chhin-chhiuN7 Sat-tan3 e5 su3-chia2 ka7 goa2 taN2-tiap8, hou7 goa2 be7 hong-sin5.  8) Koan-he7 chit e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 ki5-kiu5 Chu2 saN pai2, kiu5 I chiong chit e5 cheng3-thau5 ti5-tiau7.  9) I in3 goa2 kong2, “Li2 sou2 su-iau3 e5 si7 Goa2 e5 in-hui7; in-ui7 li2 loan2-jiok8 e5 si5 Goa2 e5 lek8-liong7 choe3 kiong5.” An2-ne goa2 choe3 hoaN-hi2 lai5 chu7-khoa goa2 e5 loan2-jiok8, thang kam2-kak Ki-tok e5 khui3-lat8 teh po2-hou7 goa2.  10) Ui7-tioh8 Ki-tok, goa2 tui3 loan2-jiok8, bu2-jiok8, kan-lan5, pek-hai7, khun3-khou2 kam2-kak moa2-i3. In-ui7 goa2 loan2-jiok8 e5 si5 chiu7 e7 kian-kiong5.

Po2-lo5 koan-hoai5 Ko-lim5-to-lang5:
	11) Goa2 chhin-chhiuN7 gong7-lang5 teh kong2-oe7 --- tan7-si7 che si7 lin2 pek goa2 an2-ne cho3 e5. Lin2 si7 eng-kai ka7 goa2 chheng-chan3 e5 lang5. In-ui7 goa2 sui-jian5 bo5 sim2-mih liau2-put-khi2, tan7-si7 chit8-sut-a2 ia7 be7 su lin2 hiah-e5 chin tek8-piat8 e5 “su3-tou5”.  12) Eng7 pah-poaN e5 jim2-nai7 chai7 lin2 tiong-kan sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, cheng3-beng5 goa2 si7 Su3-tou; chiu7-si7 u7 sin5-chek, ki5-su7 kap chhiau chu7-jian5 e5 kang-chok.  13) Goa2 ti5-liau2 bo5 ma5-hoan5 lin2 pang-chou7 goa2 i2-goa7, kam2 u7 khoan2-thai7 lin2 pi2 pat8 e5 kau3-hoe7 khah bai2? ChhiaN2 lin2 goan5-liong7 goa2 chiah-nih8 bo5 kong-to7!
	14) Hian7-chai7 goa2 chun2-pi7 te7-saN pai2 boeh khi3 lin2 hia --- iah goa2 bo5 boeh iau-kiu5 lin2 sim2-mih. Goa2 sou2 su-iau3 e5 si7 lin2, m7-si7 lin2 e5 chiN5. In-ui7 kiaN2-ji5 m7-bian2 ui7 pe7-bo2 chek-thiok, tan7-si7 pe7-bo2 tioh8 ui7 kiaN2-ji5 chek-thiok.  15) Ui7-tioh8 boeh pang-chou7 lin2, goa2 e7 hoaN-hi2 hian3 it-chhe3 goa2 sou2 u7 e5, sim7-chi3 goa2 ka-ti7 e5 seN3-mia7 ia7 boeh hian3 chhut-lai5. goa2 chiah-nih8 thiaN3 lin2, lin2 kam2 e7 in-ui7 an2-ne tian-to3 khah bo5 ai3 goa2?
	16) An2-ne lin2 e7 tong5-i3 kong2 goa2 koe3-khi3 bo5 thoa-lui7 lin2. Tan7-si7 u7 lang5 e7 kong2 goa2 kui2-ke3 to-toan, u7 kong2 peh8-chhat8 lai5 ham7-hai7 lin2.  17) Kiu3-keng3 an3-choaN2 ka7 lin2 ham7-hai7? Goa2 kam2 u7 chioh chhe-phai3 khi3 e5 lang5 e5 chhiu2 lai5 hou7 lin2 chiah8-khui?  18) Goa2 khun2-kiu5 The5-to khi3, ia7 chhe-phai3 iau2 chit8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 hiaN-ti7 kap i cho3-hoe2 khi3. The5-to kam2 u7 hou7 lin2 chiah8-khui? I kap goa2 kam2 m7-si7 kin-ki3 kang7-khoan2 e5 tong7-ki teh cho3-su7, kam2 m7-si7 chhai2-chhu2 kang7-khoan2 e5 heng5-tong7?
	19) Tai7-khai3 lin2 siuN7-kong2 goan2 toa3 lin2 bin7-cheng5 long2 siuN7-boeh pian7-hou7 goan2 ka-ti7. M7-si7 an2-ne! chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, goan2 chun-chiau3 Ki-tok e5 i3-su3 kong2-oe7, iu7-koh chhin-ai3 e5 peng5-iu2 a3, goan2 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh pang-chou7 lin2.  20) Goa2 kap goa2 sou2 ki5-thai7 e5 bo5 kang7-khoan2, lin2 ia7 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 kap lin2 sou2 ki5-thai7 e5 bo5 kang7-khoan2. Goa2 kiaN-iN2 e7 khoaN3-tioh8 hun-cheng, chit-tou3, phaiN2 seng3-te7, chu7-su, hui2-pong3, kong2 kha-chiah-au7-oe7, kiau-ngou7, hun-loan7.  21) Goa2 khiong2-kiaN au7-pai2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, Siong7-chu2 e7 chai7 lin2 bin7-cheng5 hou7 goa2 kian3-siau3, goa2 e7 ui7-tioh8 chin-che7 lang5 khau3, in-ui7 in koe3-khi3 hoan7-choe7, bo5 hoe3(**hoe2)-kai2 in sou2 cho3 e5 bo5 to7-tek e5 tai7-chi3, sia5-im5 kap im5-loan7 e5 heng5-ui5.

Te7 13 ChiuN

Choe3-au7 e5 keng2-ko3 kap chheng2-an:
	1) TaN che si7 goa2 te7-saN pai2 boeh khi3 lin2 hia. Seng3-keng kong2: “Bo5-lun7 kiam2-ki2 sim2-mih, tioh8 u7 nng7 e5 a2-si7 saN e5 cheng3-jin5 e5 cheng3-gian5.”  2) Goa2 ai3 ka7 lin2 koe3-khi3 u7 hoan7-choe7 e5 lang5 kap ki5-tha it-chhe3 e5 lang5 kong2; goa2 te7-ji7 pai2 khi3 ti7 lin2 hia e5 tiong-kan ia7 u7 kong2-koe3, taN goa2 ki3-jian5 bo5 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2, boeh koh ka7 lin2 kong2 chit8 pai2: goa2 au7-pai2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 to lan5-bian2 siu7 chek-hoat8.  3) Lin2 e7 tit-tioh8 lin2 sou2 su-iau3 it-chhe3 e5 cheng3-ki3, lai5 cheng3-beng5 Ki-tok chioh goa2 e5 chhui3 teh kong2-oe7. I tui3-thai7 lin2 peng3 be7 loan2-jiok8; tian-to3 chai7 lin2 tiong-kan piau2-si7 I e5 koan5-leng5.  4) Sui-jian5 in-ui7 I loan2-jiok8, chiah chai7 sip8-ji7-ke3 siong7 pi7 chhu2 si2-heng5, tan7-si7 I oa2-kho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5 iau2-ku2 teh oah8. Goan2 kap I kiat-hap8, sou2-i2 ia7 si7 loan2-jiok8 e5; tan7-si7 goan2 oa2-kho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5, e7 kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 oah8, u7 khui3-lat8 thang lai5 tui3-thai7 lin2.
	5) Lin2 tioh8 kho2-giam7 ka-ti7, kiam2-tho2 ka-ti7, khoaN3 lin2 u7 teh koe3 sin3-giong2 e5 seng-oah8 a2-bo5. Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou ti7 lin2 lai7-bin7? Na7 m7 chai-iaN2 chiu7 oan5-choan5 bo5 kip8-keh.  6) Goa2 siong-sin3 lin2 chai-iaN2 goan2 m7-si7 bo5 kip8-keh e5.  7) Goan2 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 hou7 lin2 long2 be7 cho3 m7-tioh8 --- che peng3 m7-si7 boeh piau2-si7 goan2 u7 kip8-keh, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 cho3 cheng3-tong e5 tai7-chi3, sui-jian5 goan2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 kip8-keh e5.  8) In-ui7 goan2 be7-eng7-tit cho3 jim7-ho5 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 ui5-poe7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-li2, chi2-u7 ui7 chin-li2 lai5 cho3.  9) Lin2 na7 e7 kian-kiong5, goan2 loan2-jiok8 ia7 sim-kam i3-goan7. Sou2-i2 goan2 ia7 ki5-kiu5 hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 oan5-choan5 e5.  10) In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 bo5 ti7 lin2 hia e5 tiong-kan, chiah sia2 chit tiuN phe. Che si7 ui7-tioh8 goa2 khi3-kau3 lin2 hia e5 si5, m7-bian2 eng7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 goa2 e5 koan5-lek8, si7 boeh kian3-lip8 lin2 e5 tek-heng7, m7-si7 boeh ka7 lin2 pho3-hoai7.
	11) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, taN goa2 sia2 kau3 chia ui5-chi2! Lin2 tioh8 chin7-lek8 seng5-choan5; thiaN goa2 e5 chhui3 hou7-siong ho5-bok8, ho5-peng5 siong-chhu3. Goan7 chu5-ai3 kap ho5-peng5 e5 Siong7-chu2 kap lin2 tong5-chai7.
	12) Lin2 tioh8 iong7 chhin-bit8 e5 ak-chhiu2 lai5 hou7-siong chheng2-an.
	Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 tai7-ke ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.
	13) Goan7 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 in-hui7, Siong7-chu2 e5 chu5-ai3, Seng3 Leng5 e5 kau-gi7(**gi5) kap lin2 tai7-ke tong5-chai7.
