Iok-Han7 Hok-Im

Te7 1 ChiuN

SeN3-mia7 e5 Seng3-to7:
	1) Siong7-chu2 chhong3-cho7 u2-tiu7 i2-cheng5, Seng3-to7 chiu7 i2-keng chun5-chai7. Seng3-to7 kap Siong7-chu2 tong5-chai7. Seng3-to7 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2.  2) Tui3 siong7 khi2-thau5 Seng3-to7 chiu7 kap Siong7-chu2 tong5-chai7.  3) Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 I lai5 chhong3-cho7 ban7-but8; bo5 chit8 hang7 m7-si7 thong-koe3 I lai5 chhong3-cho7 e5.  4) Seng3-to7 pun2-sin u7 seN3-mia7; chit e5 seN3-mia7 chiu7-si7 jin5-lui7 e5 kng.  5) Chit e5 kng chio3 chai7 ou-am3 tiong; ou-am3 m7-bat iaN5-koe3 chit e5 kng.
	6) Siong7-chu2 chhe-phai3 chit8 e5 lang5 lai5; i e5 mia5 kio3-cho3 Iok-han7.  7) Chit e5 lang5 lai5 ka7 kng cho3 kian3-cheng3, thang hou7 choan5 jin5-lui7 thiaN i e5 oe7 lai5 sin3.  8) I pun2-sin m7-si7 hit e5 kng; chi2-u7 lai5 ka7 hit e5 kng cho3 kian3-cheng3.  9) Hit e5 kng si7 chin e5 kng, teh boeh lai5 chit e5 se3-kan ka7 choan5 jin5-lui7 chio3-kng.
	10) Seng3-to7 i2-keng ti7 se3-kan; sui-jian5 Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 I lai5 chhong3-cho7 u2-tiu7, tan7-si7 se3-kan-lang5 m7-bat I.  11) I lai5-kau3 I e5 pun2-thou2, I ka-ti7 e5 lang5 bo5 hoan-geng5 I.  12) Tan7-si7 chiap-lap8 I, lai5 sin3 I e5 lang5, I chiu7 siuN2-su3 in tek8-koan5, hou7 in chiaN5-cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  13) In m7-si7 an3-chiau3 chu7-jian5 e5 seng-iok8, ia7 m7-si7 thong-koe3 lang5 e5 jiok8-iok8 lai5 chhut-si3 e5; in si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.
	14) Seng3-to7 chiaN5-cho3 lang5, tiam3 ti7 lan2 e5 tiong-kan. Lan2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 I e5 eng5-iau7, chhiong-moa2 in-hui7 kap chin-li2! Chit e5 eng5-iau7 si7 I cho3 Thian-hu7 e5 Kou-kiaN2 sou2 tit-tioh8 e5.
	15) Iok-han7 ui7 I cho3 kian3-cheng3, toa7-siaN kong2: “’U7 Chit8-ui7 toe3 goa2 e5 au7-bin7 lai5, tan7-si7 pi2 goa2 khah ui2-tai7, in-ui7 goa2 iau2-boe7 chhut-si3 I chiu7 chun5-chai7!’ Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 si7 teh chi2 Chit-ui7.”
	16) I i2-keng eng7 hong-seng7 e5 in-hui7 lai5 chiok-hok lan2 tai7-ke, hou7 lan2 in-siong7 ka-in.  17) Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 Mou5-se pan-pou3 Lut8-hoat, tan7-si7 in-hui7 kap chin-li2 si7 thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 e5.  18) Bo5 lang5 bat khoaN3-kiN3 Siong7-chu2, chi2-u7 ti7 Thian-hu7 e5 sin-piN hit ui7 Kou-kiaN2 i2-keng chiong Siong7-chu2 hian2-beng5 chhut-lai5 a3.

Si-sian2-chia2 Iok-han7 e5 kian3-cheng3: (Ma2 3:1-12; Kho2 1:1-8; Lou7 3:1-18)
	19) E7-bin7 si7 si-sian2-chia2 Iok-han7 sou2 cho3 e5 kian3-cheng3. Ti7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 Iu5-thai3-kau3 e5 leng2-to7-chia2, chhe-phai3 hiah-e5 che3-si kap Li7-bi7-lang5 khi3 mng7 Iok-han7, kong2, “Li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  20) I than2-pek8 , kong-khai seng-beng5 kong2, “Goa2 m7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a.”  21) In koh mng7 i kong2, “Na7-si7 an2-ne, li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5? Li2 si7 I2-li7-a si7 bo5?” Iok-han7 in3 kong2, “Goa2 m7-si7!” “An2-ne li2 si7 hit-ui7 tai7-gian5-jin5 si7 bo5?” Iok-han7 ia7 in3 kong2, “M7-si7.”  22) In chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Kiu3-keng3 li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5? ChhiaN2 li2 ka7 goan2 kong2, hou7 goan2 thang tng2-khi3 hoe5-tap hiah-e5 chhe-phai3 goan2 lai5 e5 lang5. Li2 ka-ti7 kong2 li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  23) Iok-han7 kong2, “Chhin-chhiuN7 tai7-gian5-jin5 I2-sai3-a sou2 kong2 e5:
‘Goa2 chiu7-si7 ti7 khong3-ia2 teh hoah hit e5 siaN, kong2: Lin2 tioh8 siu-tit8 Chu2 sou2 boeh kiaN5 e5 to7-lou7.’”
	24) Chiah-e5 lang5 si7 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5.  25) In koh mng7 Iok-han7 kong2, “An2-ne, li2 na7 m7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a, ia7 m7-si7 I2-li7-a, ia7 m7-si7 hit ui7 tai7-gian5-jin5, li2 an3-choaN2 ka7 lang5 se2-le2?”  26) Iok-han7 in3 in kong2: “Goa2 eng7 chui2 ka7 lang5 se2-le2; u7 chit8-ui7 lin2 m7-bat e5, khia7 ti7 lin2 e5 tiong-kan.  27) I cho3 goa2 e5 au7-bin7 lai5, tan7-si7 goa2 lian5 ka7 I thau2 e5-toa3 ia7 be7-kham-tit.”
	28) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 si7 ti7 Iok-tan3-ho5 e5 tui3-hoaN7 Pek-tai7-ni5 hoat-seng e5. Iok-han7 ti7 hia teh ka7 lang5 se2-le2.

Siong7-chu2 e5 ko-iuN5:
	29) Keh-tng2-jit8, Iok-han7 khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou tui3 i chia teh lai5, chiu7 kong2: “Lin2 khoaN3! Chit ui7 chiu7-si7 siau-ti5 se3-kan-lang5 e5 choe7-ok e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 Ko-iuN5.  30) ‘U7 Chit8-ui7 boeh cho3 goa2 e5 au7-bin7 lai5, tan7-si7 I pi2 goa2 khah ui2-tai7, in-ui7 goa2 iau2-boe7 chhut-si3 i2-cheng5, I chiu7 i2-keng u7 chun5-chai7.’ Goa2 an2-ne kong2 chiu7-si7 teh chi2 Chit-ui7.  31) Sui-jian5 goa2 lai5 eng7 chui2 ka7 lang5 se2-le2 e5 bok8-tek, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 jim7-sek I, tan7-si7 su7-sit8-siong7 goa2 ka-ti7 i2-cheng5 ia7 m7-bat I.”
	32) Iok-han7 iu7-koh cho3 kian3-cheng3 kong2: “Goa2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Seng3 Leng5 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 chiah hun2-chiau2, tui3 thiN-teng2 kang3 loh8-lai5 hioh ti7 I e5 teng2-bin7.  33) Goa2 ka-ti7 i2-cheng5 ia7 m7-bat I, tan7-si7 chhe goa2 lai5 eng7 chui2 ka7 lang5 se2-le2 e5 Siong7-chu2 ka7 goa2 kong2: ‘Li2 na7 khoaN3-kiN3 Seng3 Leng5 kang3 loh8-lai5 hioh ti7 I e5 teng2-bin7 Hit-ui7, I chiu7-si7 boeh eng7 Seng3 Leng5 ka7 lang5 se2-le2 e5.’  34) TaN Goa2 u7 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3 a3! Sou2-i2 goa2 lai5 cho3 kian3-cheng3 kong2: “Chit-ui7 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2.”

Ia5-sou chio bun5-tou5:
	35) Keh-tng2-jit8, Iok-han7 kap i nng7 e5 bun5-tou5 koh ti7 hia teh khia7 e5 si5,  36) Ia5-sou tui3 hia keng-koe3; Iok-han7 kim-kim ka7 I khoaN3, kong2, “Lin2 khoaN3! Siong7-chu2 e5 Ko-iuN5.”  37) Iok-han7 hit nng7 e5 bun5-tou5 thiaN-kiN3 i an2-ne kong2, chiu7 toe3 Ia5-sou khi3.  38) Ia5-sou oat8 koe3-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 in teh toe3, chiu7 mng7 in kong2, “Lin2 teh chhoe7 sim2-mih8?” In ka7 I kong2, “Liap8-pi2, Li2 toa3 ti7 tah-ui7?” (Liap8-pi2 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 “Lau7-su.”)  39) I ka7 in kong2, “Lin2 toe3 Goa2 lai5-khi3 khoaN3.” In chiu7 khi3 khoaN3 Ia5-sou teh toa3 e5 sou2-chai7, Hit jit8 in chiu7 kap Ia5-sou cho3-hoe2 toa3. (Hit e5 si5 chha-put-to si7 e7-pou-si5 si3 tiam2.)
	40) ThiaN Iok-han7 e5 oe7 lai5 toe3 Ia5-sou hit nng7 e5 lang5, ki5-tiong u7 chit8 e5 si7 Se-bun5 Pi2-tek e5 sio2-ti7 An-tek-liat8.  41) I keh-tng2 chai2-khi2 thau3-cha2 chiu7 khi3 chhoe7 i e5 a-hiaN Se-bun5, ka7 i kong2, “Goan2 i2-keng chhoe7 tioh8 Bi2-sai3-a.” --- “Bi2-sai3-a” i3-su3 chiu7-si7 “Ki-tok”.  42) I chiu7 chhoa7 Se-bun5 khi3 Ia5-sou hia. Ia5-sou kim-kim ka7 i khoaN3, kong2: “Li2 si7 Iok-han7 e5 kiaN2 Se-bun5. Goa2 boeh ka7 li2 hou7(**ho7)-cho3 Ki-hoat.” (Ki-hoat kap Pi2-tek kang7-khoan2 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 chioh8-poaN5.)

Ia5-sou keng2-tiau3 Hui-lek8 kap Na2-tan3-giap8:
	43) Keh-tng2-jit8 Ia5-sou koat-teng7 boeh khi3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2. I tu2-tioh8 Hui-lek8 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Lai5 toe3 Goa2.”  44) (Hui-lek8 si7 Pek-sai3-tai7-lang5, si7 An-tek-liat8 kap Pi2-tek e5 tong5-hiong.)  45) Hui-lek8 tu2-tioh8 Na2-tan3-giap8, chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Mou5-se ti7 Lut8-hoat e5 chheh kap chiong3 tai7-gian5-jin5 sou2 ki3-chai3 Hit-ui7 Bi2-sai3-a, goan2 i2-keng chhoe7-tioh8 a3! I chiu7-si7 Iok-sek e5 kiaN2, Na2-sat-lek8-lang5 Ia5-sou!”  46) Na2-tan3-giap8 in3 kong2, “Tui3 Na2-sat-lek8 hit khoan2 sou2-chai7, kam2 e7-tang3 chhut sim2-mih8 ho2-mih88?” Hui-lek8 kong2, “Li2 lai5 khi3 khoaN3!”
	47) Ia5-sou khoaN3-kiN3 Na2-tan3-giap8 kiaN5 oa2-lai5 I hia e5 si5, chiu7 kong2, “Li2 khoaN3! I chiah si7 chin-chiaN3 e5 I2-sek-liat8-lang5! si7 chit8 e5 bo5 hi-ke2 e5 lang5!”  48) Na2-tan3-giap8 mng7 I kong2, “Li2 an3-choa2 e7 bat goa2?” Ia5-sou in3 i kong2, “Hui-lek8 iau2-boe7 ka7 li2 kio3 e5 si5, Goa2 chiu7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 li2 ti7 bu5-hoa-ko2-chhiu7 kha.”  49) Na2-tan3-giap8 ka7 I kong2, “Liap8-pi2, Li2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2! Li2 si7 I2-sek-liat8 e5 Kun-ong5!”  50) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “In-ui7 Goa2 ka7 li2 kong2 Goa2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 li2 ti7 bu5-hoa-ko2-chhiu7 kha, an2-ne li2 chiah sin3 Goa2 si7 bo5? Li2 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 pi2 che koh-khah ui2-tai7 e5 tai7-chi3!”  51) I koh ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2, lin2 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 thiN khui, Siong7-chu2 e5 chiong3 thian-sai3 toa3 Jin5-chu2 e5 teng2-bin7 teh khi2-khi2-loh8-loh8.”

Te7 2 ChiuN

Ka-na2 e5 hun-ian3:
	1) Nng7 jit8 au7, ti7 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 e5 Ka-na2 u7 hun-le2. Ia5-sou e5 lau7-bo2 ia7 u7 chham-ka.  2) Ia5-sou kap I e5 bun5-tou5 ia7 hou7 in chhiaN2 khi3 hu3 chit e5 hun-le2.  3) Sou2 chun2-pi7 e5 chiu2 long2 lim liau2 e5 si5, Ia5-sou e5 lau7-bo2 chiu7 tui3 I kong2, “In bo5 chiu2 a3.”  4) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Bo2-chhin! Li2 m7-thang kio3 Goa2 tioh8 an3-choaN2 cho3. Goa2 e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3!”  5) I e5 lau7-bo2 chiu7 ka7 hiah-e5 chhe-eng7 e5 lang5 kong2, “I bo5-lun7 hoan-hu3 lin2 cho3 sim2-mih8, lin2 tioh8 chiau3 an2-ne khi3 cho3!”
	6) An3-chiau3 Iu5-thai3-kau3 kiat-cheng7 e5 chong-kau3 gi5-sek, ti7 hia u7 khng3 lak8 liap8 chioh8 cho3 e5 chui2-kng, mui2 liap8 e7-tang3 te2 ji7-saN-chap8 ka-lun5.  7) Ia5-sou ka7 hiah-e5 chhe-eng7 e5 lang5 kong2, “Chiah-e5 chui2-kng, to3 chui2 to3 hou7 tiN7.” In chiu7 to3 kah moa2 kiN5.”  8) Ia5-sou koh ka7 in kong2, “TaN lin2 iuN2 chit8-koa2 chui2 chhut-lai5, phang5 khi3 hou7 ian3-sek8 e5 hu7-chek-jin5.” In chiu7 phang5 khi3 hou7 i.  9) Hit e5 ian3-sek8 e5 hu7-chek-jin5 chit8-e7 lim khoaN3-bai7 le3; hiah-e5 chui2 i2-keng pian3-cho3 chiu2 a3. I m7 chai-iaN2 chiah-e5 chiu2 si7 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5 e5; iuN2 chui2 chhut-lai5 hiah-e5 chau2-toh-e5 chiah chai-iaN2. Ian3-sek8 e5 hu7-chek-jin5 chiu7 kio3 sin kiaN2-sai3 lai5,  10) ka7 i kong2: “Tai7-ke long2 si7 tai7-seng chhut ho2 chiu2, theng3-hau7 lang5-kheh lim kah chui3-chui3, chiah chhut khah bai2 e5 chiu2. Li2 tian-to3 chiong siong7 ho2 e5 chiu2 lau5 kau3 chim2-ma2!” 
	11) Che chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou te7-it pai2 ti7 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 e5 Ka-na2 sou2 cho3 e5 sin5-chek. I an2-ne lai5 hian2-si7 I e5 eng5-iau7; I e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 sin3 I.
	12) Jian5-au7, Ia5-sou kap I e5 lau7-bo2, hiaN-ti7 i2-kip8 I e5 bun5-tou5 loh8-khi3 Ka-pek-long5-siaN5. In toa3 hia toa3 bo5 kui2 jit8.

Ia5-sou chheng Seng3-tian7: (Ma2 21:12-13; Kho2 11:15-17; Lou7 19:45-46)
	13) Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 PoaN5-koe3-cheh teh-boeh kau3 a3, Ia5-sou chiu7 chiuN7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2-siaN5.  14) I khoaN3-kiN3 seng-li2-lang5 ti7 Seng3-tian7 lai7 teh be7 gu5, iuN5, hun2-chiau2, ia7 u7 lang5 che7 ti7 chiN5-toh-a2 teh oaN7-chiN5.  15) I chiu7 eng7 soh-a2 cho3 chit8 tiau5 piN, ka7 hiah-e5 lang5 kap in e5 iuN5, gu5 long2 tui3 Seng3-tian7 koaN2 chhut-khi3; ia7 chhia-to2 chiN5-toh-a2, hou7 in e5 chiN5 soaN3-ia7-ia7.  16) Iu7-koh ka7 be7 hun2-chiau2 e5 kong2, “Chiah-e5 mih88 long2 theh8 chhut-khi3! M7-thang chiong Goa2 e5 Hu7-chhin e5 chhu3 cho3 chhi7-tiuN5!”  17) I e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 siuN7-khi2 Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Goa2 tui3 Li2 e5 Seng3-tian7 e5 jiat8-cheng5, ti7 Goa2 sim-lai7 chhin-chhiuN7 hoe2 teh toh8.”
	18) Iu5-thai3-lang5 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Li2 e7-tang3 cho3 sim2-mih8 sin5-chek lai5 cheng3-beng5 Li2 u7 koan5 lai5 an2-ne cho3?”  19) Ia5-sou in3-tap in kong2, “Lin2 chiong chit keng Seng3-tian7 thiah-tiau7, saN jit8 lai7 Goa2 chiu7 boeh koh khi2 khi2-lai5.”  20) Iu5-thai5-lang5 chiu7 tui3 I kong2: “Chit keng Seng3-tian7 khi2 si3-chap8-lak8 ni5 chiah khi2 khi2-lai5; Li2 saN jit8 lai7 chiu7 boeh koh khi2 khi2-lai5 si7 bo5?”
	21) Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou sou2 kong2 e5 Seng3-tian7, si7 teh chi2 I ka-ti7 e5 seng-khu.  22) Sou2-i2 Siong7-chu2 hou7 Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 e5 tiong-kan hok8-hoat8 e5 si5, I e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 siuN7-khi2 I bat kong2 chiah-e5 oe7; in chiu7 sin3 Seng3-keng, ia7 sin3 Ia5-sou sou2 kong2 e5 oe7.

Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 cheng3-lang5 e5 sim:
	23) Ia5-sou ti7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 koe3 PoaN5-koe3-cheh e5 si5, u7 chin-che7 lang5 khoaN3-tioh8 I sou2 cho3 e5 sin5-chek, in chiu7 sin3 I.  24) Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou bo5 sin3-jim7 in, in-ui7 I long2 chai-iaN2 cheng3-lang5 e5 sim.  25) In-ui7 I e7-tang3 khoaN3-chhut lang5 e5 lai7-sim, sou2-i2 bo5 su-iau3 lang5 ka7 pat8-lang5 cho3-cheng3.

Te7 3 ChiuN

Ia5-sou kap Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2:
	1) U7 chit8 e5 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, kio3-cho3 Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2, si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 leng2-to7-chia2.  2) U7 chit8 am3 i lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou kong2: “Liap8-pi2, goan2 chai-iaN2 Li2 si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5 Lau7-su; na7 m7-si7 Siong7-chu2 kap i tong5-chai7, chiu7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 cho3 Li2 sou2 cho3 chiah-e5 sin5-chek.”  3) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2: lang5 na7 m7-si7 tiong7-sin chhut-si3, chiu7 be7-tang3 khoaN3-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok.”  4) Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2 kong2: “Lang5 chiah8 kah ni5-lau7, kam2 e7-tang3 tiong7-sin chhut-si3? I kam2 chin-chiaN3 e7-tang3 koh jip8-khi3 bo2-thai lai5 teng5-koh chhut-si3?”  5) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2: lang5 na7 bo5 tui3 chui2 kap Seng3 Leng5 lai5 chhut-si3, chiu7 be7-tang3 jip8-khi3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok!  6) Tui3 jiok8-the2 chhut-si3 e5, chiu7-si7 jiok8-the2, tui3 Seng3 Leng5 chhut-si3 e5, chiu7-si7 sin5-leng5.  7) M7-thang in-ui7 Goa2 ka7 li2 kong2: ‘Lin2 long2 tioh8 tiong7-sin chhut-si3,’ lai5 kam2-kak ki5-koai3.  8) Hong boeh chhoe khi3 tah-ui7 chiu7 chhoe khi3 tah-ui7. Li2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 i e5 siaN; tan7-si7 m7-chai i si7 tui3 tah-ui7 chhoe lai5 e5, ia7 m7-chai i boeh chhoe khi3 tah-ui7. Mui2 chit8 e5 tui3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 chhut-si3 e5 lang5 ia7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne.”  9) Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Na2 e7 hoat-seng chit khoan2 tai7-chi3?”  10) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “Li2 si7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 ui2-tai7 e5 lau7-su. Li2 lian5 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 ia7 be7 liau2-kai2 si7 bo5?”  11) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2: Goan2 kong2-khi2 goan2 sou2 chai e5, ia7 ui7-tioh8 goan2 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 lai5 cho3-cheng3; tan7-si7 lin2 m7 chiap-siu7 goan2 sou2 cho3 e5 kian3-cheng3!  12) Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 chit e5 se3-kan e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 na7 m7-kheng2 sin3 Goa2, ka2-su2 Goa2 na7 ka7 lin2 kong2 thiN-teng2 e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 kam2 boeh sin3?  13) Tui3 thian-siong7 kang3 loh8-lai5 e5 Jin5-chu2 i2-goa7, bo5 lang5 bat chiuN7 thiN!”
	14) “Chhin-chhiuN7 Mou5-se chai7 khong3-ia2, chiong tang5-choa5 kui3 toa3 thiau-a2-teng2, Jin5-chu2 ia7 tek-khak tioh8 an2-ne pi7 the5-ko khi2-lai5,  15) thang hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 sin3 I e5 lang5 lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.  16) Siong7-chu2 chiah-nih8 thiaN3 se3-kan-lang5, sou2-i2 chiah chiong I e5 Kou-kiaN2 siuN2-su3 in, thang hou7 sou2 u7 sin3 I e5 lang5 m7-bian2 biat8-bong5, lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.  17) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 chhe-phai3 I e5 KiaN2 lai5 se3-kan, m7-si7 boeh sim2-phoaN3 se3-kan-lang5, si7 boeh hou7 se3-kan-lang5 thong-koe3 I lai5 tit-kiu3.”
	18) “Sin3 I e5 lang5 be7 siu7 sim2-phoaN3, m7-sin3 I e5 lang5 i2-keng siu7 phoaN3-choe7 a7, in-ui7 i bo5 sin3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kou-kiaN2.  19) Kng i2-keng lai5-kau3 se3-kan, tan7-si7 se3-kan-lang5 ai3 ou-am3 bo5 ai3 kong-beng5, in-ui7 in e5 heng5-ui5 si7 sia5-ok e5, chiu7-si7 kin-ki3 an2-ne chiah ka7 in teng7-choe7 e5.  20) Cho3 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 oan3-hin7 kng, bo5 ai3 kng e5 sou2-chai7, an2-ne i e5 heng5-ui5 chiah be7 hian2-lou7 chhut-lai5;  21) tan7-si7 chun-heng5 chin-li2 e5 lang5, ai3 lai5 kng e5 sou2-chai7, an2-ne chiah e7 piau2-si7 i e5 heng5-ui5 si7 chun-chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 lai5 cho3 e5.”

Ia5-sou kap si-sian2-chia2 Iok-han7:
	22) Jian5-au7, Ia5-sou kap I e5 bun5-tou5 khi3 kau3 Iu5-thai3 e5 te7-hng, kap in cho3-hoe2 toa3 hia, ka7 lang5 se2-le2.  23) Si-sian2-chia2 Iok-han7 ia7 ti7 kin7 Sat-leng2 e5 Ai-loan2 ka7 lang5 se2-le2, in-ui7 hia u7 chin-che7 chui2. Bin5-chiong3 lai5 i hia hou7 i se2-le2.  24) (Hit e5 si5, Iok-han7 iau2-boe7 hou7 lang5 koaiN-loh8 kaN-gak8.)
	25) U7 chit8-koa2 Iok-han7 e5 bun5-tou5 kap chit8 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 teh pian7-lun7 kiat-cheng7 e5 chong-kau3 gi5-sek.  26) In chiu7 lai5 kiN3 Iok-han7 kong2: “Liap8-pi2, li2 khoaN3, ti7 Iok-tan3-ho5 e5 tui3-hoaN7 bat kap li2 cho3-hoe2, li2 u7 ka7 I cho3-cheng3 Hit-e5 lang5 ia7 teh ka7 lang5 se2-le2; iu7-koh cheng3-lang5 long2 khi3 I hia.”  27) Iok-han7 in3 kong2: “Na7 m7-si7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 e5, lang5 be7-tang3 tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8.  28) Goa2 bat kong2: ‘Goa2 m7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a; goa2 si7 hong7-phai3 lai5 cho3 I e5 sian-hong e5.’ Goa2 kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, lin2 e7-tang3 ka7 goa2 cho3 kian3-cheng3.  29) Sin-niu5 sui-jian5 si7 sin-long5 e5, m7-ku2 sin-long5 e5 peng5-iu2 khia7 ti7 piN-a teh thiaN, thiaN-tioh8 sin-long5 e5 siaN chiu7 chin hoaN-hi2.  Chit khoan2 e5 hi2-lok8, taN oan5-choan5 si7 goa2 e5 a7.  30) I tek-khak e7 lu2 ui2-tai7; goa2 e7 lu2 soe-bi5.”

Siong7-chu2 sou2 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5:
	31) “Tui3 thiN-teng2 lai5 e5, si7 pi2 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 khah ui2-tai7; tui3 te7-nih8 chhut-lai5 e5, si7 siok8 ti7 te7-nih8, iu7-koh sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 ia7 si7 te7-siong7 e5 tai7-chi3. Tui3 thiN-teng2 lai5 Hit-ui7 si7 pi2 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 khah ui2-tai7.  32) I ui7-tioh8 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3, sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5 su7 lai5 cho3 kian3-cheng3; tan7-si7 bo5 lang5 boeh chiap-siu7 I e5 kian3-cheng3!  33) Chiap-siu7 I e5 kian3-cheng3 e5 lang5, cheng3-beng5 Siong7-chu2 si7 chin-sit8 e5.  34) Siong7-chu2 sou2 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5, kong2 Siong7-chu2 e5 oe7; in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 bo5 han7-liong7 siuN2-su3 Seng3 Leng5 hou7 I.  35) Thian-hu7 ai3 Seng3-chu2, chiong it-chhe3 long2 kau-tai3 ti7 I e5 chhiu2-thau5.  36) Sin3 Seng3-chu2 e5 lang5 e7 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng; m7-kheng2 sin3 Seng3-chu2 e5 lang5, put-tan7 be7-tang3 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng, tian-to3 e7 eng2-oan2 siu7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 toa7 siu7-khi3.”

Te7 4 ChiuN

Ia5-sou kap Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5:
	Hoat-li7-saiph3ai e5 lang5 u7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 Ia5-sou pi2 Iok-han7 siu khah che7 bun5-tou5, ia7 ka7 khah che7 bun5-tou5 se2-le2.  2) (Ki5-sit8 Ia5-sou pun2-sin m7-bat ka7 lang5 se2-le2, si7 I e5 bun5-tou5 ka7 lang5 se2-le2 e5.)  3) Ia5-sou chit8-e7 chai-iaN2 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, chiu7 li7-khui Iu5-thai3 Seng2, koh to3 tng2-khi3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2.  4) I boeh tng2-khi3 hia tioh8-ai3 keng-koe3 Sat-ma2-li7-a Seng2.
	5) Ia5-sou lai5-kau3 Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 chit8 e5 siaN5-chhi7 kio3-cho3 Su7-ka, oa2-kin7 Nga2-kok hou7 i e5 kiaN2 Iok-sek hit te3 thou2-te7.  6) Nga2-kok e5 kou2-cheN2 chiu7-si7 ti7 hia, in-ui7 Ia5-sou kiaN5-lou7 kiaN5 kah chin thiam2, chiu7 che7 toa3 kou2-cheN2 piN. Hit e5 si5, chha-put-to si7 tiong-tau3 a3.
	7) Tu2 u7 chit8 e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5 chhiuN7-chui2. Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Chui2 pun Goa2 lim chit8-e7.”  8) (Hit e5 si5, I e5 bun5-tou5 jip8-khi3 siaN5-lai7 teh be2 chiah8-mih88.)  9) Hit e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2: “Sim2-mih8? Li2 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5, goa2 si7 Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 cha-bou2-lang5, an3-choaN2 Li2 ka7 goa2 tho2 chui2 lim?” (I kong2 an2-ne, si7 in-ui7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5 bo5 lai5-ong2.)  10) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “Li2 ka2-su2 na7 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-su3, iu7-koh chai-iaN2 ka7 li2 tho2 chui2 lim e5 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5, li2 chiu7 e7 khun2-kiu5 I, I chiu7 e7 siuN2-su3 li2 oah8-chui2.”  11) Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2: “Sin-seN, Li2 bo5 chhiuN7-chui2 e5 chui2-thang2, iu7-koh chit e5 kou2-cheN2 chin chhim, Li2 boeh tui3 tah-ui7 iuN2 oah8-chui2 lai5?  12) Chit e5 kou2-cheN2 si7 goan2 chou2-sian Nga2-kok lau5 hou7 goan2 e5. I ka-ti7 kap i e5 kiaN2-sun i2-kip8 i e5 cheng-seN, long2 lim chit e5 kou2-cheN2 e5 chui2. Li2 kam2 kaN2 kong2 Li2 pi2 Nga2-kok khah ui2-tai7?”  13) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “Lim chit e5 kou2-cheN2-chui2 e5 lang5 long2 e7 koh chhui3-ta;  14) tan7-si7 lim goa2 boeh siuN2-su3 i e5 chui2 e5 lang5, chiu7 eng2-oan2 be7 chhui3-ta. In-ui7 Goa2 boeh hou7 lang5 e5 chui2, toa3 i e5 lai7-bin7 e7 piN3-cho3 chui2-choaN5, e7 it-tit8 chheng3 chhut-lai5 hou7 lang5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.”  15) Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2: “Sin-seN, chhiaN2 chiong chit khoan2 chui2 hou7 goa2. An2-ne goa2 chiu7 long2 be7 chhui3-ta, ia7 m7-bian2 lai5-kau3 chia chhiuN7-chui2.”  16) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Khi3 kio3 li2 e5 tiong7-hu kap li2 tau3-tin7 lai5 chia.”  17) Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, “Goa2 bo5 tiong7-hu.” Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “Li2 kong2 li2 bo5 tiong7-hu, an2-ne tioh8.  18) Li2 bat kap gou7 e5 lang5 kiat-hun, hian7-chai7 kap li2 teh tong5-ki e5, m7-si7 li2 sit8-chai7 e5 tiong7-hu. Li2 an2-ne kong2 bo5 m7-tioh8.”  19) Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ka7 I kong2: “Sin-seN, goa2 khoaN3, Li2 si7 tai7-gian5-jin5!  20) Goan2 Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5 e5 chou2-sian, long2 ti7 chit e5 soaN-teng2 keng3-pai3 Siong7-chu2, tan7-si7 lin2 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kong2 keng3-pai3 Siong7-chu2 e5 sou2-chai7 tioh8 toa3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2.”  21) Ia5-sou kong2: “Hu7-jin5-lang5 a3, li2 tioh8 sin3 Goa2, lin2 be7 toa3 chit e5 soaN-teng2, ia7 be7 toa3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 keng3-pai3 Thian-hu7 hit e5 si5 teh-boeh kau3 a3.  22) Lin2 Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5 sit8-chai7 m7 chai-iaN2 lin2 teh keng3-pai3 sim2-mih8; goan2 Iu5-thai3-lang5 chai-iaN2 goan2 teh keng3-pai3 sim2-mih8. In-ui7 chin2-kiu3 si7 tui3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 lai5 e5.  23) Chin-sit8 e5 keng3-pai3-chia2 boeh eng7 sim-leng5 kap chin-li2 lai5 keng3-pai3 Thian-hu7, hit e5 si5 teh-boeh kau3 a3, su7-sit8-siong7 i2-keng lai5-kau3 chia a. Thian-hu7 ai3 lang5 an2-ne lai5 keng3-pai I.  24) Siong7-chu2 si7 Sin5-leng5, sou2-i2 keng3-pai3 I e5 lang5, tioh8-ai3 eng7 sim-leng5 kap chin-li2 lai5 keng3-pai3 I.”
	25) Hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, “Goa2 chai-iaN2 Bi2-sai3-a, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok, e7 lai5. I lai5 e5 si5, e7 ka7 goan2 kong2 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3.”  26) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Goa2 chiu7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a, chim2-ma2 teh kap li2 kong2-oe7.”
	27) Hit e5 si5, I e5 bun5-tou5 tng2-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou kap chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 teh kong2-oe7, kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3; tan7-si7 in bo5 lang5 mng7 kong2, “Li2 boeh chhong3 sim2-mih8?” a2-si7 mng7 kong2, “Li2 an3-choaN2 teh kap chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2-oe7?”
	28) Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7 chiong i e5 chui2-pan5 khng3 toa3 hia, koaN2-kin2 khi3 siaN5-lai7, ka7 lang5 kong2:  29) “Lai5-khi3 khoaN3, u7 chit8 e5 lang5 chiong goa2 koe3-khi3 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, long2 ka7 goa2 kong2 chhut-lai5! I bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 si7 Bi2-sai3-a!”  30) ThiaN-tioh8 an2-ne, cheng3-lang5 chiu7 tui3 siaN5-lai7 chhut-lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou.
	31) Hit e5 tiong-kan bun5-tou5 khun2-kiu5 Ia5-sou kong2, “Liap8-pi2, chhiaN2 Li2 chiah8.”  32) Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2, “Goa2 u7 lin2 long2 m7 chai-iaN2 e5 chiah8-mih88 thang chiah8.”  33) Bun5-tou5 chiu7 hou7-siong mng7 kong2, “Kam2 u7 lang5 sang3 chiah8-mih88 hou7 I?”  34) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Chun-heng5 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 e5 chi2-i3, oan5-seng5 I e5 kang-chok, chiu7-si7 Goa2 e5 chiah8-niu5.  35) Lin2 kam2 m7-si7 u7 chit8 ku3 oe7 kong2, ‘Koh si3 goeh8-jit8 chiu7 boeh siu-seng5 a7’? goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2, lin2 khoaN3 si3-bin7 e5 chhan5-hng5! Ngou2-kok i2-keng sek(**sek8) a3, thang ho2 siu-seng5 a7!  36) Siu-koah e5 lang5 teh nia2 kang-chiN5, ia7 ui7-tioh8 eng2-seng teh siu-chip8 ngou2-kok, an2-ne thang hou7 ia7-cheng2-e5 kap siu-koah-e5 cho3-hoe2 hoaN-hi2.  37) In-ui7 siok8-gi2 kong2, ‘Chit8 e5 ia7-cheng2, leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 siu-koah,’ chit ku3 oe7 sit8-chai7 kong2 liau2 chin tioh8.  38) Goa2 chhe lin2 khi3 siu-koah lin2 bo5 keng-choh tioh8 e5 chhan5-hng5. Pat8-lang5 keng-choh e5, lin2 hun-hiong2 in sou2 lo5-khou2 e5 seng5-ko2.”
	39) Toa3 ti7 hit e5 siaN5-chhi7 e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5 u7 chin-che7 lang5 sin3 Ia5-sou, in-ui7 hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 cho3-cheng3 kong2: “I chiong goa2 koe3-khi3 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, long2 ka7 goa2 kong2 chhut-lai5.”  40) Sou2-i2 Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5 lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 si5, khun2-kiu5 I toa3 in hia; I chiu7 toa3 hia toa3 nng7 jit8.
	41) ThiaN-tioh8 Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2, u7 ke chin-che7 lang5 lai5 sin3 I;  42) in chiu7 ka7 hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2: “Goan2 taN i2-keng m7-si7 in-ui7 u7 thiaN-tioh8 li2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 chiah sin3, si7 in-ui7 goan2 chhin-hiN7 u7 thiaN-tioh8, iu7-koh chai-iaN2 I sit8-chai7 si7 se3-kai3 e5 Kiu3-chu2.”

Ia5-sou i-ho2 toa7-koaN e5 hau7-seN:
	43) Ti7 hia toa3 nng7 jit8 liau2-au7, Ia5-sou li7-khui hia, khi3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2.  44) In-ui7 Ia5-sou ka-ti7 bat beng5-beng5 kong2-khi2 tai7-gian5-jin5 chai7 ka-ti7 e5 kou3-hiong be7 siu7 lang5 chun-keng3;  45) tan7-si7 I tng2-lai5 kau3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 e5 si5, Ka-li7-li7-lang5 ka7 I hoan-geng5, in-ui7 in ia7 u7 khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 koe3-cheh, u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou ti7 hia koe3-cheh e5 si5 sou2 cho3 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3.
	46) Ia5-sou koh lai5-kau3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 e5 Ka-na2, chiu7-si7 I chiong chui2 pian3-cho3 chiu2 e5 sou2-chai7. Ti7 hia u7 chit8 ui7 toa7-koaN, i e5 kiaN2 ti7 Ka-pek-long5 teh phoa3-peN7.  47) Chit e5 lang5 chit8-e7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 Ia5-sou tui3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2 lai5-kau3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2, chiu7 khi3 kiN3 Ia5-sou, khun2-kiu5 Ia5-sou khi3 Ka-pek-long5 i-ti7 i e5 kiaN2, in-ui7 i e5 kiaN2 teh-boeh si2 a3.  48) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Lin2 na7 bo5 khoaN3-tioh8 sin5-chek kap ki5-su7, lin2 it-teng7 m7 sin3.”  49) Hit e5 toa7-koaN ka7 I kong2, “Lau7-su, than3 goa2 e5 gin2-na2 iau2-boe7 si2, chhiaN2 Li2 lai5-khi3.”  50) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Li2 tng2-khi3, li2 e5 kiaN2 be7 si2.” Hit e5 lang5 sin3 Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2 e5 oe7, chiu7 tng2-khi3.  51) I tng2-khi3 kau3 poaN3-lou7 e5 si5, i e5 pok8-jin5 lai5 ka7 i po3-ko3, kong2, “Li2 e5 kiaN2 be7 si2 a3.”  52) I chiu7 mng7 in khoaN3 i e5 kiaN2 tui3 sim2-mih8 si5 khi2 ho2 khi2-lai5. In ka7 i kong2, “Chang-e7-tau3 chit8 tiam2, jiat8 chiu7 the3-khi3.”  53) In lau7-pe7 chiu7 chai-iaN2 hit e5 si5-kan tu2-tu2 si7 Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Li2 e5 kiaN2 be7 si2,” hit e5 si5. I kap choan5-ke e5 lang5 chiu7 long2 sin3 Ia5-sou.
	54) Che chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou tui3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2 tng2-lai5 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 liau2-au7, te7-ji7 pai2 sou2 cho3 e5 sin5-chek.

Te7 5 ChiuN

Ia5-sou i-ho2 chit8 e5 phoa3-peN7 38 ni5 e5 lang5:
	1) Jian5-au7 u7 chit8 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 cheh-ki5, Ia5-sou chiu7 khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2.  2) Ti7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 kio3-cho3 IuN5-a2 e5 Toa7-mng5 piN, u7 chit8 e5 chui2-ti5, Hi-pek-lai5-oe7 kio3-cho3 Pit-su-tai7. Ti7 chui2-ti5 piN u7 gou7 e5 chau2-long5.  3) U7 chin-che7 peN7-lang5, chheN-me5-e5, pai2-kha-e5, poan3-sui7-e5 to2 ti7 chiah-e5 chau2-long5. (In teh theng3-hau7 chui2 tin2-tang7;  4) in-ui7 Chu2 e5 thian-sai3 u7-tang-si5-a2 e7 loh8-lai5 chui2-ti5, phah chui2 hou7 i tin2-tang7; chui2 tin2-tang7 liau2-au7 siong7 tai7-seng loh8-khi3 e5 peN7-lang5, bo5-lun7 i tioh8 sim2-mih8 peN7, long2 e7 ho2-khi3.)  5) Ti7 hia u7 chit8 e5 i2-keng phoa3-peN7 saN-chap8-peh ni5 e5 peN7-lang5.  6) Ia5-sou khoaN3-kiN3 i to2 ti7 hia, chai-iaN2 i i2-keng phoa3-peN7 chin ku2 a3, chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 u7 ai3 li2 e5 peN7 ho2-khi3 bo5?”  7) Hit e5 peN7-lang5 in3 kong2: “Sin-seN, chui2 tin2-tang7 e5 si5, bo5 lang5 thang pho7 goa2 loh8-khi3 chui2-ti5. Goa2 iau2-boe7 kau3 hia e5 si5, pat8-lang5 chiu7 tai7-seng loh8-khi3 a3." ”8) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Peh khi2-lai5, gia5 li2 e5 chhng5-phou khi2-lai5 kiaN5.”  9) Hit e5 lang5 chek-si5 ho2-khi3, chiu7 gia5 i e5 chhng5-phou khi2-lai5 kiaN5.
	Hit jit8 tu2-tu2 si7 An-hioh-jit8.  10) Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 ka7 hit e5 hou7 Ia5-sou i-ho2 khi3 e5 lang5 kong2: “Kin-na2-jit8 si7 An-hioh-jit8. Chiau3 lan2 e5 Lut8-hoat, li2 m7 eng-kai gia5 li2 e5 chhng5-phou khi2-lai5 kiaN5.”  11) I ka7 in in3 kong2: “Ka7 goa2 i-ho2 hit e5 lang5 tui3 goa2 kong2, ‘gia5 li2 e5 chhng5-phou khi2-lai5 kiaN5.”  12) In chiu7 mng7 i kong2: “Kio3 li2 gia5 chhng5-phou khi2-lai5 kiaN5 hit e5 lang5 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  13) Tan7-si7 hit e5 lang5 m7 chai-iaN2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 ka7 i i-ho2 khi3 e5. In-ui7 ti7 hit e5 sou2-chai7 u7 chin toa7 tin7 e5 lang5, Ia5-sou chiah e7-tang3 liu chhut-khi3.
	14) Au7-lai5 Ia5-sou ti7 Seng3-tian7 tu2-tioh8 hit e5 lang5, chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Li2 taN i2-keng ho2-khi3 a3. In u7 chiah8 siong7-chhiaN2 ia7 si2-khi3.”  59) Chiah-e5 to7-li2 si7 Ia5-sou ti7 Ka-pek-long5 e5 hoe7-tng5 teh kau3-hun3 lang5 e5 si5 kong2 e5.

Eng2-seng e5 oe7:
	60) U7 chin-che7 I e5 bun5-tou5 thiaN-tioh8 an2-ne chiu7 kong2, “Chiah-e5 oe7 thiaN-liau2 chin chhak8-hiN7! Sim2-mih8 lang5 thiaN-liau2 tong3 e7 tiau5?”  61) Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 bun5-tou5 in-ui7 thiaN chiah-e5 oe7 tui3 I teh phau7 put-peng5, chiu7 ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 kong2 an2-ne, lin2 thiaN liau2 bo5 kah-i3 si7 bo5?  62) Ka2-su2 lin2 na7 khoaN3-kiN3 Jin5-chu2 seng-thian tng2-khi3 I goan5-lai5 teh toa3 e5 sou2-chai7, lin2 e7 an3-choaN2?”  63) Hou7 lang5 seN3-mia7 e5 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Leng5; jiok8-sin si7 bo5 ke3-tat8 e5. Goa2 sou2 ka7 lin2 kong2 e5 oe7, chiu7-si7 Seng3 Leng5, chiu7-si7 seN3-mia7.  64) Tan7-si7 lin2 tiong-kan iau2-ku2 u7 lang5 long2 bo5 boeh siong-sin3.” (In-ui7 Ia5-sou chu7 khi2-thau5 chiu7 chai-iaN2 sim2-mih8 lang5 bo5 sin3 I, sim2-mih8 lang5 boeh chhut-be7 I.)  65) I ke3-siok8 kong2: “Sou2-i2 Goa2 chiah ka7 lin2 kong2: na7 bo5 Thian-hu7 in2-chun2, chiu7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 lai5 kau3 Goa2 chia.”
	66) Tui3 chit e5 si5 khi2, I e5 bun5-tou5 u7 chin-che7 lang5 li7-khui I, bo5 boeh kap I tau3-tin7.  67) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 hit chap8-ji7 e5 bun5-tou5 kong2: “Iah lin2 ne3 --- lin2 ia7 boeh li7-khui si7 bo5?”  68) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek in3 I kong2: “Chu2 a3, Li2 u7 eng2-seng e5 to7-li2. Goan2 iau2 tioh8 koh khi3 chhoe7 sim2-mih8 lang5?  69) Goan2 u7 sin3, ia7 chai-iaN2 Li2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3-chia2.”  70) Ia5-sou chiu7 in3 kong2: “Goa2 kam2 m7-si7 keng2-soan2 lin2 chap8-ji7 e5 lang5? Tan7-si7 lin2 tiong-kan u7 chit8 e5 si7 Mou5-kui2!”  71) I kong2 an2-ne, si7 chi2 Ka-liok8 e5 Se-bun5 e5 kiaN2 Iu5-tai7; i sui-jian5 si7 chap8-ji7 e5 bun5-tou5 e5 chit8 e5, au7-lai5 e7 chhut-be7 Ia5-sou.

Te7 7 ChiuN

Ia5-sou kap I e5 hiaN-ti7:
	1) Jian5-au7 Ia5-sou toa3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 li2-heng5. I m7 ai3 khi3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2, in-ui7 chit8-koa2 Iu5-thai3-lang5 teh siong3 ki-hoe7 ai3-boeh sat-hai7 I.  2) Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Tah-pou3-phang5-cheh teh-boeh kau3 a3.  3) Ia5-sou e5 hiaN-ti7 chiu7 ka7 I kong2: “Tioh8 li7-khui chit e5 sou2-chai7, khi3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2, thang hou7 Li2 e5 bun5-tou5 e7-tang3 khoaN3-tioh8 Li2 sou2 teh cho3 e5 tai7-chi3.  4) Lang5 na7 ai3 chhut-mia5 chiu7 bo5 tiam7-tiam7-a2 cho3 tai7-chi3. Li2 ki3-jian5 teh cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7 tioh8 chhut-bin7 hou7 se3-kan-lang5 khoaN3.”  5) (In-ui7 lian5 I e5 hiaN-ti7 ia7 m7 sin3 I.)  6) Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3; tan7-si7 lin2 e5 si5, chhin3-chhai2-si5 to u7.  7) Se3-kan-lang5 be7 oan3-hin7 lin2, kan-taN teh oan3-hin7 Goa2, in-ui7 Goa2 put-si5 teh chi2-cheng3 in sia5-ok e5 chok-hong.  8) Lin2 cho3 lin2 khi3 koe3 chit e5 cheh; in-ui7 Goa2 e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3.”  9) Ia5-sou an2-ne ka7 in kong2 liau2-au7, chiu7 ka-ti7 lau5 toa3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2.

Ia5-sou koe3 Tah-pou3-phang5-cheh:
	10) Ia5-sou e5 hiaN-ti7 khi3 koe3-cheh liau2-au7, Ia5-sou ia7 khi3 koe3-cheh, tan7-si7 bo5 kong-khai, tiam7-tiam7-a2 khi3.  11) Cheh-ki5 e5 tiong-kan, Iu5-thai3-lang5 teh chhoe7 Ia5-sou, kong2, “Hit e5 lang5 ti7 tah-ui7?”  12) Kun5-chiong3 e5 tiong-kan u7 chin-che7 lang5 teh chhi7-bu2-chhi7-chhuh8 gi7-lun7 Ia5-sou e5 tai7-chi3. U7 lang5 kong2, “I si7 ho2 lang5.” Ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “M7-si7, I teh be5-hek8 bin5-chiong3.”  13) Tan7-si7 bo5 lang5 kaN2 kong-jian5 lai5 kong2-khi2 I e5 tai7-chi3, in-ui7 in kiaN Iu5-thai3-lang5.
	14) Kau3 cheh-ki5 chha-put-to koe3 chit8 poaN3 e5 si5, Ia5-sou chiu7 khi3 Seng3-tian7 khai-si2 ka3-si7 lang5.  15) Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 kam2-kak bok8-beng5 ki5-biau7, kong2, “Chit e5 lang5 bo5 siu7 kau3-iok8, na2 e7 chiah-nih8 u7 hak8-bun7?”  16) Ia5-sou chiu7 in3 in kong2: “Goa2 e5 kau3-hun3 m7-si7 tui3 Goa2 ka-ti7 lai5 e5, si7 tui3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 lai5 e5.  17) Lang5 na7 goan7-i3 chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 lai5 kiaN5, chiu7 e7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 e5 kau3-hun3 si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5, a2-si7 Goa2 chiau3 ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3 lai5 teh kong2 e5.  18) Lang5 na7 ui7-tioh8 i ka-ti7 lai5 kong2-oe7, si7 teh kiu5 i ka-ti7 ko3-jin5 e5 eng5-iau7; tan7-si7 kiu5 chhe-phai3 i lai5 hit-ui7 e5 eng5-iau7 e5, hit e5 lang5 si7 chin-sit8 e5, ti7 i e5 sim-lai7 chiu7 bo5 put-gi7.  19) Mou5-se kam2 bo5 tui3 lin2 pan-pou3 Lut8-hoat? Tan7-si7 lin2 tiong-kan bo5 kah chit8 e5 chun-heng5 chit e5 Lut8-hoat! Lin2 an3-choaN2 siuN7 boeh hai7-si2 Goa2?”  20) Cheng3-lang5 in3 kong2, “Li2 teh khi2-siau2?” Sim2-mih8 lang teh siuN7-boeh hai7-si2 Li2?”  21) Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Goa2 chai7 An-hioh-jit8 cho3 chit8-hang7 toa7 tai7-chi3, lin2 chiu7 long2 hiah-nih8 tioh8-kiaN.  22) Mou5-se beng7-leng7 lin2 si-heng5 kat-le2, lin2 chiu7 chai7 An-hioh-jit8 ia7 ka7 lang5 si-heng5 kat-le2. (Su7-sit8-siong7 chit e5 le2-gi5, m7-si7 tui3 Mou5-se khai-si2 e5, si7 tui3 lin2 e5 chou2-sian khai-si2 e5!)  23) Ka2-su2 lang5 e7-eng7-tit chai7 An-hioh-jit8 chiap-siu7 kat-le2, chiah be7 khi3 hoan7-tioh8 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat, an2-ne Goa2 chai7 An-hioh-jit8 hou7 chit8 e5 lang5 kui seng-khu long2 ho2-khi3, lin2 an3-choaN2 e7 hiah siu7-khi3?  24) M7-thang khoaN3 goa7-piau2 lai5 phoaN3-toan3 lang5, tioh8-ai3 chiau3 kong-to7 lai5 ka7 lang5 phoaN3-toan3.”

I si7 Bi2-sai3-a si7 bo5?
	25) U7 chit8-koa2 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2-lang5 kong2: “Chit e5 lang5 kam2 m7-si7 in siuN7-boeh ka7 I sat-hai7 Hit-e5?  26) TaN I ti7 chia kong-jian5 teh kong2-oe7, ia7 bo5 lang5 tui3 I kong2 poaN3 ku3 oe7. Kam2 e7 chiah-e5 koaN-tiuN2 sit8-chai7 jim7-teng7 chit e5 lang5 si7 Bi2-sai3-a?  27) Bi2-sai3-a lai5 e5 si5, bo5 lang5 e7 chai-iaN2 I si7 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5 e5! Tan7-si7 lan2 long2 chai-iaN2 chit e5 lang5 e5 lai5-lek8.”
	28) Ia5-sou ti7 Seng3-tian7 teh kau3-hun3 lang5 e5 si5, toa7 siaN kong2: “Lin2 kong2 bat Goa2, ia7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 e5 lai5-lek8! Goa2 m7-si7 chiau3 Goa2 ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3 lai5 e5; chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 si7 sin3-sit8 e5, tan7-si7 lin2 m7-bat I.  29) Tan7-si7 Goa2 bat I, in-ui7 Goa2 chiu7-si7 tui3 Hit-ui7 hia lai5 e5, ia7-si7 I chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5.”  30) In chiu7 siuN7-boeh liah8 I, tan7-si7 bo5 lang5 tui3 I he7-chhiu2, in-ui7 I e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3.  31) Kun5-chiong3 e5 tiong-kan u7 chin-che7 lang5 sin3 I, kong2, “Bi2-sai3-a lai5 e5 si5, I kam2 e7 pi2 chit e5 lang5 cho3 khah che7 sin5-chek?”

Chhe oe7-peng boeh liah8 Ia5-sou:
	32) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 thiaN-kiN3 kun5-chiong3 chhi7-bu2-chhi7-chhuh8 teh gi7-lun7 Ia5-sou e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7 kap che3-si-tiuN2 phai3 Seng3-tian7 e5 oe7-peng khi3 boeh liah8 Ia5-sou.  33) Ia5-sou chiu7 tui3 in kong2: “Goa2 koh kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 bo5 loa7-ku2, chiu7 boeh tng2-khi3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 hia.  34) Lin2 e7 boeh chhoe7 Goa2, ia7 chhoe7 be7 tioh8; in-ui7 Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lin2 be7-tang3 khi3.”  35) Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 hou7-siong kong2: “Chit e5 lang5 tau3-te2 si7 siuN7-boeh khi3 tah-ui7 lai5 hou7 lan2 chhoe7 be7-tioh8? I si7 siuN7-boeh khi3 chhoe7 hiah-e5 hun-soaN3 ti7 Hi-lah8 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5, lai5 ka3-si7 Hi-lah8-lang5 si7 bo5?  36) Chit e5 lang5 kong2: ‘Lin2 e7 boeh chhoe7 Goa2, ia7 chhoe7 be7 tioh8; in-ui7 Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, lin2 be7-tang3 khi3.’ I chit ku3 oe7 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3?”

Eng2-seng e5 chui2-choaN5:
	37) Cheh-ki5 e5 choe3-au7 hit jit8, ia7-si7 choe3 tiong7-iau3 e5 jit8, Ia5-sou khia7 khi2-lai5 toa7 siaN kong2: “Na7 chhui3-ta e5 lang5, tioh8 lai5 Goa2 chia lim.  38) Chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, ‘Eng2-seng e5 choaN5-chui2 e7 tui3 sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5 e5 sim-lai7 chheng3 chhut-lai5!’”  39) Ia5-sou an2-ne kong2 si7 teh chi2 sin3 I e5 lang5 teh-boeh nia2-siu7 e5 Seng3 Leng5. Hit e5 si5 iau2-boe7 siuN2-su3 Seng3 Leng5 hou7 lang5, in-ui7 Ia5-sou iau2-boe7 tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7.

Cheng3-lang5 gi7-lun7 hun-hun:
	40) Kun5-chiong3 e5 tiong-kan u7 lang5 thiaN-tioh8 chiah-e5 oe7, chiu7 kong2, “Chit e5 lang5 chin-chiaN3 si7 hit-ui7 Tai7-gian5-jin5.”  41) Ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “Chit e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a.” Tan7-si7 ia7 u7 lang5 kong2: “Bi2-sai3-a kam2 e7 tui3 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 chhut-lai5?  42) Seng3-keng kam2 m7-si7 ki3-chai3 kong2, ‘Bi2-sai3-a si7 Tai7-pit8 e5 kiaN2-sun, e7 tui3 Tai7-pit8 e5 kou3-hiong Pek-li7-heng5 chhut-sin.’”  43) Sou2-i2 koan-he7 Ia5-sou e5 tai7-chi3, cheng3-lang5 soah gi7-lun7 hun-hun.  44) Ki5-tiong u7 lang5 siuN7-boeh liah8 Ia5-sou, tan7-si7 bo5 lang5 he7-chhiu2.

Iu5-thai3-kau3 e5 chi2-to7-chia2 m7 sin3 Ia5-sou:
	45) Seng3-tian7 e5 oe7-peng tng2-lai5 kau3 che3-si-tiuN2 kap Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 hia, in chiu7 mng7 oe7-peng kong2, “Lin2 an3-choaN2 bo5 ka7 I liah8 lai5?”  46) Oe7-peng in3 kong2, “M7-bat u7 lang5 kong2-oe7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit e5 lang5 an2-ne.”  47) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 tui3 in kong2: “Lin2 ia7 hou7 I be5-khi3 a3 si7 bo5?  48) KoaN-tiuN2 kap Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, kam2 u7 lang5 sin3 I?  49) Chit tin7 m7-bat Lut8-hoat e5 gong7-lang5 tioh8 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8!”  50) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 tiong-kan, u7 chit8 e5 i2-cheng5 bat lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2, ka7 in kong2:  51) “Chiau3 lan2 e5 Lut8-hoat, bo5 tai7-seng sim2-mng7 kap tiau-cha lang5 sou2 cho3 e5, kam2 e7-eng7-tit ka7 lang5 teng7-choe7?”  52) In chiu7 in3 i kong2: “Li2 ia7-si7 Ka-li7-li7-lang5 si7 bo5? Li2 gian2-kiu3 Seng3-keng chiu7 e7 chai-iaN2 Ka-li7-li7 Seng2 be7 chhut tai7-gian5-jin5.”  53) Cheng3-lang5 chiu7 long2 tng2-khi3 chhu3-nih(**nih8).

Te7 8 ChiuN

Hoan7 kan-im5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5:
	1)  Ia5-sou khi3 Kan-na2-soaN.  2) Keh-tng2 chai2-khi2 cha2-cha2 I koh lai5 Seng3-tian7. Cheng3-lang5 chip8 oa2-lai5 I hia; I chiu7 che7 loh8-khi3 kau3-hun3 in.  3) Hit e5 si5, keng-hak8-chia2 kap Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, chhoa7 chit8 e5 teh hoan7 kan-im5 e5 si5 hou7 lang5 liah8-tioh8 e5 cha-bou2-lang5 lai5, hou7 i khia7 toa3 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5.  4) In chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2: “Lau7-su, chit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 ti7 hoan7 kan-im5 e5 hian7-tiuN5 hou7 lang5 liah8-tioh8 e5.  5) Chai7 Lut8-hoat-siong7, Mou5-se beng7-leng7 lan2 tioh8 eng7 chioh8-thau5 tim3-si2 chit khoan2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5. TaN Li2 siuN7 an3-choaN2?”  6) In kong2 an2-ne si7 siuN7-boeh chhi3-tham3 Ia5-sou, thang lai5 ko3 I. Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou aN3 loh8-khi3 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 toa3 thou5-kha sia2 ji7.  7) M7-ku2 in it-tit8 ka7 I mng7; Ia5-sou chiu7 khia7 khi2-lai5 ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 tiong-kan bo5-choe7 e5 lang5, tai7-seng ka7 i tim3 chioh8-thau5.”  8) I chiu7 koh aN3 loh8-khi3 toa3 thou5-kha sia2 ji7.  9) Tan7-si7 thiaN-kiN3 chiah-e5 oe7, chiu7 tui3 khah lau7 e5 chit8-e5 chit8-e5 liu chhut-khi3, soah chhun Ia5-sou kap iau2 khia7 ti7 hia hit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 nia7-nia7.  10) Ia5-sou chiu7 khia7 khi2-lai5 ka7 i kong2: “Hu7-jin5-lang5 a3, in hiah-e5 lang5 ti7 tah-ui7? Bo5 lang5 ka7 li2 teng7-choe7 si7 bo5?”  11) Cha-bou2-lang5 in3 kong2, “Sin-seN, bo5 poaN3 e5.” Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Goa2 ia7 bo5 boeh ka7 li2 teng7-choe7. Li2 thang tng2-khi3, taN m7-thang koh hoan7-choe7.”)

Ia5-sou si7 se3-kai3 e5 kng:
	12) Ia5-sou iu7-koh ka7 cheng3-lang5 kong2: “Goa2 si7 se3-kai3 e5 kng. Kin-toe3 Goa2 e5 lang5 it-teng7 be7 kiaN5 am3-lou7, tian-to3 u7 seN3-mia7 e5 kng.”  13) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 tui3 I kong2, “Li2 ui7-tioh8 Li2 ka-ti7 cho3 kian3-cheng3, Li2 e5 kian3-cheng3 bo5 hau7.”  14) Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Sui-jian5 Goa2 ui7 ka-ti7 cho3 kian3-cheng3, Goa2 e5 kian3-cheng3 si7 chin-sit8 e5, in-ui7 Goa2 chai-iaN2 Goa2 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5, boeh khi3 tah-ui7;  tan7-si7 lin2 m7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5, Goa2 boeh khi3 tah-ui7.  15) Lin2 eng7 lang5 e5 phiau-chun2 phoaN3-toan3 lang5; Goa2 bo5 phoaN3-toan3 lang5.  16) Siat-su2 Goa2 na7 phoaN3-toan3 lang5, Goa2 e5 phoaN3-toan3 si7 chin-sit8 e5, in-ui7 goa2 m7-si7 ka-ti7 chit8 e5 phoaN3-toan3, si7 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5 Thian-hu7 kap Goa2 cho3-hoe2.  17) Chiau3 lin2 ka-ti7 e5 Lut8-hoat u7 kui-teng7 kong2, u7 nng7 e5 lang5 lai5 cho3 kian3-cheng3 chiah u7 hau7.  18) Goa2 ui7 Goa2 ka-ti7 cho3 kian3-cheng3; chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5 Thian-hu7 ia7 ui7 Goa2 cho3 kian3-cheng3.”  19) In chiu7 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Li2 e5 Thian-hu7 ti7 tah-ui7?” Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Lin2 m7-bat Goa2, ia7 m7-bat Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7. Lin2 na7 bat Goa2, ia7 chiu7 e7 bat Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7.”
	20) Ia5-sou ti7 Seng3-tian7 lai7 teh kau3-hun3 lang5 e5 si5, si7 ti7 hian3-kim-siuN e5 piN-a kong2 chiah-e5 oe7. In-ui7 I e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3, sou2-i2 bo5 lang5 he7-chhiu2 ka7 I liah8.

Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, lin2 be7-tang3 khi3:
	21) I iu7-koh ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 boeh khi3 a3. Lin2 e7 chhoe7 Goa2, iah8 lin2 e7 si2 chai7 lin2 e5 choe7-ok tiong. Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lin2 be7-tang3 khi3.”  22) Sou2-i2 Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 kong2: “I kong2 ‘Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lin2 be7-tang3 khi3.’ I kong2 an2-ne si7 siuN7-boeh chu7-sat si7 bo5?”  23) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 si7 tui3 e7-bin7 chhut-lai5 e5, Goa2 si7 tui3 teng2-bin7 lai5 e5. Lin2 si7 siok8 ti7 chit e5 se3-kai3 e5; Goa2 m7-si7 siok8 ti7 chit e5 se3-kai3 e5.  24) In-ui7 an2-ne, Goa2 chiah kong2 lin2 e7 si2 chai7 lin2 e5 choe7-ok tiong. Lin2 na7 m7 sin3 Goa2 chiu7-si7 Hit-ui7, lin2 e7 si2 chai7 lin2 e5 choe7-ok tiong.”  25) In chiu7 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?” Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Goa2 kam2 m7-si7 chu7 khi2-thau5 chiu7 tiaN7-tiaN7 teh ka7 lin2 kong2.  26) Koan-he7 lin2 e5 tai7-chi3, Goa2 u7 chin-che7 oe7 thang kong2, ia7 u7 chin-che7 tai7-chi3 thang ka7 lin2 phoaN3-choe7. Tan7-si7 Goa2 chi2-u7 boeh chiong goa2 tui3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 sou2 thiaN e5, soan-pou3 hou7 se3-kan-lang5. I si7 chin-sit8 e5.”
	27) In be7-tang3 liau2-kai2 I si7 teh kong2 Thian-hu7 e5 tai7-chi3.  28) Sou2-i2 Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 ka7 Jin5-chu2 the5-ko khi2-lai5 liau2-au7, lin2 chiu7 e7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 chiu7-si7 Hit-ui7, ia7 e7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5 bo5 chit8 hang7 chiau3 Goa2 ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3. Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, long2 si7 chiau3 Thian-hu7 sou2 ka3 Goa2 e5.  29) Chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 kap Goa2 tong5-chai7. I m7-bat pang3 Goa2 ka-ti7 chit8 e5, in-ui7 Goa2 put-si5 cho3 I sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5 tai7-chi3.”  30) Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 e5 si5, u7 chin-che7 lang5 sin3 I.

Chu7-iu5-jin5 kap lou5-le7:
	31) Ia5-sou chiu7 tui3 hiah-e5 sin3 I e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kong2: “Lin2 na7 siong5-siong5 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7, lin2 chiu7 sit8-chai7 si7 Goa2 e5 bun5-tou5,  32) ia7 chiu7 e7 bat chin-li2. Chit e5 chin-li2 chiu7 e7 hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5.”  33) In chiu7 in3 I kong2: “Goan2 si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 kiaN2-sun, iau2 m7-bat cho3-koe3 lang5 e5 lou7-le7! Li2 kong2, ‘Lin2 e7 tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5,’ chit ku3 oe7 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3?”  34) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: it-chhe3 hoan7-choe7 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 choe7-ok e5 lou5-le7.  35) Lou5-le7 chai7 ka-teng5 lai7 bo5 kou3-teng7 e5 te7-ui7, tan7-si7 hau7-seN e5 te7-ui7 si7 kou3-teng7 e5.  36) Sou2-i2 Seng3-chu2 na7 hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5, lin2 chiu7 e7 cho3 sit8-chai7 u7 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5.  37) Goa2 chai-iaN2 lin2 si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 kiaN2-sun; tan7-si7 lin2 siuN7-boeh sat-hai7 Goa2, in-ui7 lin2 bo5 ai3 chiap-siu7 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7.  38) Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, chiu7-si7 Goa2 ti7 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 hia sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 e5; iah lin2 sou2 cho3 e5, chiu7-si7 tui3 lin2 lau7-pe7 sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5.”
	39) In chiu7 in3 Ia5-sou kong2, “Goan2 e5 chou2-sian si7 A-pek-lah8-han2!” Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Lin2 na7-si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 kiaN2-sun, lin2 chiu7 e7 kap A-pek-lah8-han2 cho3 kang7-khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3.  40) Goa2 put-ko3 si7 chiong tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia oh8-lai5 e5 chin-li2 kong2 hou7 lin2 thiaN, lin2 soah siuN7-boeh sat-hai7 Goa2. A-pek-lah8-han2 (**peng7) bo5 cho3 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3.  41) Lin2 si7 teh cho3 lin2 lau7-pe7 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3.” In ka7 I kong2: “Goan2 m7-si7 put-gi7 e5 kiaN2-ji5. goan2 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Hu7-chhin, chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2!”  42) Ia5-sou chiu7 tui3 in kong2: “Siong7-chu2 na7 chin-chiaN3 si7 lin2 e5 Hu7-chhin, lin2 it-teng7 e7 koh-khah ai3 Goa2, in-ui7 Goa2 si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5, iu7-koh goa2 hian7-chai7 ti7 chia. goa2 m7-si7 chiau3 Goa2 ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3 lai5 e5, si7 I chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5.  43) An3-choaN2 lin2 be7-tang3 liau2-kai2 Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5? Che si7 in-ui7 lin2 bo5 leng5-lek8 thang liau2-kai2 Goa2 e5 oe7.  44) Lin2 e5 lau7-pe7 chiu7-si7 Mou5-kui2; lin2 it-tit8 siuN7-boeh sit8-heng5 lin2 lau7-pe7 e5 iok8-bong7. I chu7 khi2-thau5 chiu7-si7 sat-jin5 e5 hiong-siu2. I bo5 tiong-sit8 kian-chhi5 chin-li2, in-ui7 ti7 i e5 lai7-bin7 long2 bo5 chin-li2. I kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5 si5, chiu7-si7 teh piau2-hian7 i e5 pun2-seng3 nia7-nia7, in-ui7 i si7 kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5, ia7-si7 kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5 lang5 e5 lau7-pe7.  45) Tan7-si7 Goa2 kong2 chin-li2; in-ui7 an2-ne lin2 chiah m7 sin3 Goa2.  46) Lin2 e5 tiong-kan sim2-mih8 lang5 e7-tang3 chi2-cheng3 Goa2 u7 choe7? Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 chin-li2, lin2 an3-choaN2 m7 sin3 Goa2?  47) Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 thiaN Siong7-chu2 e5 oe7; in-ui7 lin2 m7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5, sou2-i2 lin2 chiah m7 thiaN.”

Ia5-sou kap A-pek-lah8-han2:
	48) Iu5-thai3-lang5 in3 Ia5-sou kong2: “ Goan2 kong2 Li2 si7 Sat-ma2-li7-a-lang5, ia7 si7 tioh8-iau e5, an2-ne kam2 m7-tioh8?”  49) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 bo5 tioh8-iau. Goa2 kiong-keng3 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7, lin2 tian-to3 bu2-jiok8 Goa2.  50) Goa2 bo5 kiu5 ka-ti7 e5 eng5-iau7. U7 chit8 ui7 teh ui7 Goa2 kiu5 eng5-iau7, I e7 cho3 sim2-phoaN3-chia2.  51) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: lang5 na7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7, chiu7 eng2-oan2 be7 si2!”  52) Iu5-thai3-lang5 in3 I kong2: “TaN goan2 khak-sit8 chai-iaN2 Li2 sit8-chai7 tioh8-iau. A-pek-lah8-han2 i2-keng si2 a3, chiong3 tai7-gian5-jin5 ia7 si2 a3; tan7-si7 Li2 iau2-ku2 kong2: ‘Lang5 na7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7, chiu7 eng2-oan2 be7 si2!’  53) Goan2 e5 chou2-sian A-pek-lah8-han2 si2-khi3 a3. Li2 kam2 e7 pi2 A-pek-lah8-han2 koh-khah ui2-tai7? Iu7-koh chiong3 tai7-gian5-jin5 ia7 si2-khi3 a3. Li2 siuN7 kong2 Li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  54) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 na7 kiu5 Goa2 ka-ti7 e5 eng5-iau7, Goa2 e5 eng5-iau7 chiu7 long2 bo5 ke3-tat8. Eng5-iau7 Goa2 e5 si7 Goa2 e5 Hu7-chhin, chiu7-si7 Hit-ui7 lin2 kong2 si7 lin2 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5.  55) Lin2 m7-bat Hit-ui7, tan7-si7 Goa2 bat I. Goa2 na7 kong2 Goa2 m7 bat I, Goa2 chiu7-si7 kap lin2 kang7-khoan2 kong2 peh8-chhat8 e5. Tan7-si7 Goa2 sit8-chai7 bat I, Goa2 ia7 chun-siu2 I e5 oe7.  56) Lin2 e5 chou2-sian A-pek-lah8-han2, hoaN-hi2 boeh khoaN3 Goa2 lai5-lim5 e5 jit8-chi2. I sit8-chai7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3, ia7 chin hoaN-hi2.”  57) Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Li2 iau2-boe7 gou7-chap8 hoe3. An3-choaN2 bat khoaN3-kiN3 A-pek-lah8-han2?”  58) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2, ‘A-pek-lah8-han2 iau2-boe7 chhut-si3, chiu7 u7 Goa2.’”  59) In chiu7 khioh chioh8-thau5 boeh ka7 I tim3; tan7-si7 Ia5-sou iam2-sin chiu7 tui3 seng3-tian7 chhut-khi3.

Te7 9 ChiuN

Ia5-sou i-ho2 chit8 e5 chu7 chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5 e5 lang5:
	1) Ia5-sou teh kiaN5-lou7 e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 chu7 chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5 e5 lang5.  2) I e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 mng7 I kong2: “Lau7-su, si7 chit e5 lang5 pun2-sin, a2-si7 i e5 pe7-bo2 hoan7-choe7, i chiah e7 chu7 chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5?”  3) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Che m7-si7 in-ui7 i pun2-lang5, a2-si7 in pe7-bo2 hoan7-choe7, I chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh chai7 i e5 sin-siong7 hian2-chhut Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5.  4) Than3 iau2 jit8-si5, lan2 tioh8 lai5 cho3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 e5 kang-chok. Am3-si5 teh-boeh kau3 a3; jit8-thau5 na7 am3 chiu7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 cho3 kang.  5) Goa2 ti7 chit e5 se3-kan e5 tiong-kan, Goa2 chiu7-si7 se3-kai3 e5 kng.”  6) I kong2 an2-ne liau2-au7, chiu7 phui3 chhui3-noa7 loh8-khi3 thou5-kha, eng7 chhui3-noa7 chhiau-thou5, kou5 toa3 hit e5 lang5 e5 bak8-chiu,  7) ka7 i kong2, “Khi3 Se-lo5-a e5 chui2-ti5 se2.” (Se-lo5-a e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 “Pi7 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5.”) Hit e5 chheN-me5-e5 chiu7 khi3 hia se2; to3 tng2-lai5 e5 si5 chiu7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3.
	8) I e5 chhu3-piN kap i2-cheng5 bat khoaN3-kiN3 i cho3 khit-chiah8 teh pun e5 lang5 chiu7 kong2, “Chit e5 lang5 kam2 m7-si7 siong5-siong5 che7 teh pun hit e5?” U7 lang5 kong2, “I chiu7-si7 hit e5.”  9) Ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “M7-si7; kap i boeh chhin-chhiuN7 nia7-nia7.” Tan7-si7 i ka-ti7 kong2, “Goa2 chiu7-si7 hit e5.”  10) Sou2-i2 in chiu7 mng7 i kong2, “Na7 an2-ne li2 e5 bak8-chiu si7 an3-choaN2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5?”  11) I in3 kong2: “Ho7-cho3 Ia5-sou hit e5 lang5 chhiau-thou5 ka7 goa2 kou5 toa3 bak8-chiu, ka7 goa2 kong2, ‘Khi3 Se-lo5-a e5 chui2-ti5 se2.’ sou2-i2 goa2 chit8-e7 khi3 se2, chiu7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3.”  12) In mng7 i kong2, “Hit e5 lang5 ti7 tah-ui7?” I in3 kong2, “Goa2 m7 chai-iaN2.”

Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 sim2-mng7 hit e5 chheN-me5-e5:
	13) In chiu7 chhoa7 i2-cheng5 chheN-me5 hit e5 lang5 khi3 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 hia.  14) Ia5-sou chhiau-thou5 ka7 i i-ho2 bak8-chiu hit jit8, tu2-tu2 si7 An-hioh-jit8.  15) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 koh mng7 i khoaN3 i e5 bak8-chiu si7 an3-choaN2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5. I in3 in kong2, “I eng7 chit8 ko5 thou5 kou5 toa3 goa2 e5 bak8-chiu. Goa2 khi3 se2 chiu7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3.”  16) U7 chit8-koa2 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 kong2, “Cho3 chit e5 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 choat8-tui3 m7-si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5, in-ui7 I bo5 chun-siu2 An-hioh-jit8.” Ia7 u7 lang5 gil7un kong2, “Choe7-jin5 an3-choaN2 e7-tang3 cho3 chit khoan2 e5 sin5-chek?” Kiat-kiok8 in e5 i3-kian3 soah hun-liat8.
	17) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 koh mng7 hit e5 chheN-me5-e5 kong2, “Hit e5 hou7 li2 bak8-chiu u7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 lang5, li2 siuN7 I si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?” I in3 kong2, “I si7 tai7-gian5-jin5.”  18) Iu5-thai3-lang5 iau2-boe7 kio3 hit e5 lang5 e5 pe7-bo2 lai5 i2-cheng5, m7 siong-sin3 i khah-cha2 chheN-me5 au7-lai5 koh-chai3 u7 khoaN3-kiN3.  19) Iu5-thai5-lang5 mng7 i e5 pe7-bo2 kong2: “Chit e5 lang5 sit8-chai7 si7 lin2 kiaN2 si7 bo5? Lin2 kong2, i chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5; taN i an3-choaN2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 a3?”  20) I e5 pe7-bo2 in3 in kong2: “Goan2 chai-iaN2 chit e5 si7 goan2 e5 kiaN2, ia7 chai-iaN2 i chhut-si3 chiu7 chheN-me5;  21) m7-ku2 taN i an3-choaN2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3, goan2 m7 chai-iaN2. Sim2-mih8 lang5 ka7 i i-ho2 i e5 bak8-chiu, goan2 ia7 m7 chai-iaN2. Mng7 i pun2-lang5; i si7 toa7-lang5 a7; i ka-ti7 e5 tai7-chi3 ka-ti7 e7-hiau2 kong2 a3.”  22) In pe7-bo2 si7 in-ui7 kiaN Iu5-thai3-lang5, sou2-i2 chiah an2-ne kong2; in-ui7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 tai7-ke i2-keng tong5-i3 na7 u7 lang5 seng5-jin7 Ia5-sou cho3 Bi2-sai3-a, tioh8-ai3 tui3 hoe7-tng5 ka7 i koaN2 chhut-khi3.  23) In-ui7 an2-ne in pe7-bo2 chiah kong2, “I si7 toa7-lang5 a7; mng7 i ka-ti7!”
	24) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 te7-ji7 pai2 koh kio3 i2-cheng5 chheN-me5 hit e5 lang5 lai5, ka7 i kong2: “Toa3 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, li2 tioh8 kong2 lau2-sit8 oe7. Chiau3 goan2 khoaN3, hit e5 lang5 it-teng7 si7 choe7-jin5.”  25) I in3 kong2: “I si7 choe7-jin5 a2 m7-si7, goa2 m7 chai-iaN2. Goa2 kan-taN chai-iaN2 chit8 hang7 tai7-chi3, chiu7-si7 goa2 i2-cheng5 si7 chheN-me5-e5, tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 a3.”  26) In chiu7 mng7 i kong2: “I ka7 li2 cho3 sim2-mih8? I eng7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 hou7 li2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3?”  27) I in3 kong2: “Goa2 i2-keng ka7 lin2 kong2 a3; lin2 ia7 m7 thiaN goa2 e5 oe7. Lin2 an3-choaN2 boeh koh mng7? Lin2 ia7 siuN7 ai3-boeh cho3 I e5 bun5-tou5 si7 bo5?”  28) In chiu7 eng7 phaiN2-chhui3 ka7 i me7 kong2: “Li2 si7 hit e5 lang5 e5 bun5-tou5; goan2 si7 Mou5-se e5 bun5-tou5.  29) Goan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 bat ka7 Mou5-se kong2-oe7. Kong2-kau3 chit e5 lang5, lian5 I tui3 tah-ui7 lai5 e5, goan2 ia7 m7 chai-iaN2.”  30) Hit e5 lang5 in3 in kong2: “TaN che tioh8 chin ki5-koai3 a3! I i-ho2 goa2 e5 bak8-chiu, lin2 iau2-ku2 m7 chai-iaN2 I si7 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5 e5?  31) Goan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 m7-thiaN choe7-jin5 e5 oe7; tan7-si7 keng3-ui3 Siong7-chu2, chun-heng5 I e5 chi2-i3 e5 lang5, Siong7-chu2 chiah e7 thiaN i e5 oe7.  32) Chu7 chhong3-se3 i2-lai5 iau2 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 kong2 u7 lang5 i-ho2 chhut-is3 chiu7 chheN-me5 e5 lang5!  33) Chit e5 lang5 na7 m7-si7 tui3 siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5, I chiu7 be7-tang3 cho3 chit khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3!”  34) In chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Li2 chai7 choe7-ok tiong chhut-si3, chai7 choe7-ok tiong tiong2-seng5. Li2 ia7 kaN2 siuN7-boeh lai5 ka3-si7 goan2, si7 bo5?” In chiu7 tui3 hoe7-tng5 ka7 i koaN2 chhut-khi3.

Cheng-sin5-siong7 e5 chheN-me5:
	35) Ia5-sou u7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 in ka7 i koaN2 chhut-khi3, tu2-tioh8 i e5 si5 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 u7 sin3 Jin5-chu2 bo5?”  36) Hit e5 lang5 in3 kong2, “Sin-seN a3, sim2-mih8 lang5 si7 Jin5-chu2? ChhiaN2 Li2 ka7 goa2 kong2, hou7 goa2 thang lai5 sin3 I.”  37) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2, “Li2 u7 khoaN3-tioh8 I a3; hian7-chai7 teh kap li2 kong2-oe7 e5 chiu7-si7 I.”  38) I kong2, “Chu2 a3, goa2 u7 sin3.” I chiu7 kui7 loh8-khi3 pai3 Ia5-sou.
	39) Ia5-sou kong2: “Goa2 lai5 chit e5 se3-kan si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh sim2-phoaN3 --- thang hou7 chheN-me5-e5 u7 khoaN3-kiN3, hou7 bak8-kim-e5 pian3-cho3 chheN-me5-e5.”  40) U7 chit8-koa2 kap Ia5-sou ti7 hia e5 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, thiaN-kiN3 an2-ne chiu7 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Goan2 kam2 ia7 si7 chheN-me5-e5, si7 bo5?”  41) Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Siat-su2 lin2 na7-si7 chheN-me5-e5, lin2 chiu7 bo5 choe7, tan7-si7 lin2 ki3-jian5 kong2, ‘Goan2 si7 bak8-kim-e5,’ an2-ne lin2 goan5-chai7 iau2-ku2 u7 choe7.”

Te7 10 ChiuN

IuN5-tiau5 e5 phi3-ju7:
	1) “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: boeh jip8-khi3 iuN5-tiau5 e5 lang5, na7 bo5 tui3 mng5 jip8-khi3, tui3 pat8 sou2-chai7 poaN5 jip8-khi3, i chiu7-si7 chhat8-a2, chiu7-si7 kiong5-to7.  2) Tui3 mng5 jip8-khi3 e5 lang5 chiah si7 iuN5 e5 bok8-chia2.  3) Kou3-mng5-e5 ka7 i khui mng5; iuN5 ia7 e7 thiaN i e5 siaN. I chit8-chiah chit8-chiah kio3 i ka-ti7 e5 iuN5 e5 mia5, chhoa7 in chhut-lai5.  4) I pang3 ka-ti7 e5 iuN5 chhut-lai5 liau2-au7, chiu7 cho3 iuN5 e5 thau5-cheng5 kiaN5, iuN5 ia7 toe3 i kiaN5, in-ui7 iuN5 e7 jin7-tit i e5 siaN.  5) IuN5 m7 toe3 chheN-hun7-lang5; tian-to3 e7 siam2-pi7 i, in-ui7 be7 jin7-tit i e5 siaN.”
	6) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2 chit e5 phi3-ju7; tan7-si7 in be7 li2-kai2 I sou2 kong2 e5 i3-su3.

Ia5-sou si7 ho2 bok8-chia2:
	7) Sou2-i2 Ia5-sou chiu7 koh ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: goa2 chiu7-si7 iuN5-tiau5 e5 mng5.  8) It-chhe3 pi2 Goa2 khah tai7-seng lai5 e5 lang5 long2 si7 chhat8-a2, si7 kiong5-to7; iuN5 ia7 m7 thiaN in.  9) Goa2 chiu7-si7 mng5; lang5 na7 keng-koe3 Goa2 chit e5 mng5 jip8-lai5 e5, e7 tit-tioh8 kiu3, e7 sui5-pian7 chhut-chhut-jip8-jip8, e7 chhoe7-tioh8 bok8-chhau2.  10) Chhat8-a2 lai5, long2 si7 boeh lai5 thau-liah8, sat-hai7, hui2-biat8. Goa2 lai5 si7 boeh hou7 in tit-tioh8 seN3-mia7, ji5-chhiaN2 boeh hou7 in tit-tioh8 chhiong-chiok e5 seN3-mia7.”
	11) “Goa2 si7 ho2 e5 bok8-chia2. Ho2 e5 bok8-chia2 ui7-tioh8 iuN5 pang3-sak ka-ti7 e5 seN3-mia7.  12) Hou7 lang5 chhiaN3 e5 m7-si7 bok8-chia2, iuN5 ia7 m7-si7 i ka-ti7 e5, sou2-i2 chit8-e7 khoaN3-kiN3 chhai5-long5 lai5, chiu7 pang3-sak iuN5 to5-chau2; chhai5-long5 chiu7 chiong iuN5 ka7-khi3, ia7 e7 koaN2-soaN3 iuN5-kun5.  13) I an2-ne to5-chau2 si7 in-ui7 i put-ko3 si7 hou7 lang5 chhiaN3 e5 nia7-nia7, tui3 iuN5 long2 bo5 koan-sim.  14-15) Goa2 si7 ho2 e5 bok8-chia2. Tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Thian-hu7 bat Goa2, Goa2 ia7 bat Thian-hu7 an2-ne, Goa2 bat Goa2 e5 iuN5, Goa2 e5 iuN5 ia7 bat Goa2. Iu7-koh Goa2 hoaN-hi2 ui7-tioh8 iuN5 pang3-sak Goa2 e5 seN3-mia7.  16) Goa2 iau2 u7 ki5-tha e5 iuN5, m7-si7 siok8 ti7 chit e5 iuN5-tiau5 e5. Goa2 tioh8-ai3 chhoa7 in lai5. In e7 thiaN Goa2 e5 siaN, ia7 e7 chiaN5-cho3 chit8 kun5, siok8 ti7 chit8 e5 bok8-chia2.”
	17) “Thian-hu7 ai3 Goa2, si7 in-ui7 Goa2 goan7-i3 pang3-sak Goa2 e5 seN3-mia7, thang koh tit-tioh8.  18) Bo5 lang5 e7 chhiuN2 Goa2 e5 seN3-mia7 khi3, si7 goa2 ka-ti7 goan7-i3 pang3-sak e5. Goa2 u7 koan5 thang pang3-sak seN3-mia7, ia7 u7 koan5 thang koh tit-tioh8.Che si7 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 beng7-leng7 Goa2 cho3 e5.”
	19) In-ui7 Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 tiong-kan iu7-koh hoat-seng i3-kian3 chhiong-tut8.  20) In e5 tiong-kan u7 chin-che7 lang5 kong2, “I tioh8-iau, teh khi2-siau2. Lin2 an3-choaN2 boeh thiaN I e5 oe7?”  21) Tan7-si7 ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “Che m7-si7 tioh8-iau e5 lang5 kong2 e5 oe7! Mou5-kui2 kam2 e7 hou7 chheN-me5-e5 u7 khoaN3-kiN3?”

Ia5-sou hou7 Iu5-thai5-lang5 khi3-choat8:
	22) Chai7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 u7 ki2-heng5 hong7-hian3 Seng3-tian7 e5 tian2-le2; hit e5 si5 si7 tang-thiN.  23) Ia5-sou ti7 Seng3-tian7 lai7 e5 Sou2-lo5-bun5 e5 chau2-long5 teh kiaN5-lai5 kiaN5-khi3.  24) Iu5-thai5-lang5 chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou ui5 le7, kong2, “Li2 boeh koh hou7 goan2 chhui-chhek loa7 ku2? Li2 na7 si7 Bi2-sai3-a, tioh8 than2-pek8 ka7 goan2 kong2.”  25) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 i2-keng u7 ka7 lin2 kong2 a3, tan7-si7 lin2 m7 sin3. Goa2 hong7 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 koan5 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, lai5 teh ka7 Goa2 cho3-cheng3.  26) Tan7-si7 lin2 m7 sin3, in-ui7 lin2 m7-si7 Goa2 e5 iuN5.  27) Goa2 e5 iuN5 thiaN Goa2 e5 siaN; Goa2 bat in, in ia7 lai5 toe3 Goa2.  28) Goa2 siuN2-su3 in eng2-oan2 e5 seN3-mia7; in eng2-oan2 be7 si2-bong5; in-ui7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 tui3 Goa2 e5 chhiu2 ka7 in chhiuN2-khi3.  29) SiuN2-su3 hiah-e5 iuN5-a2 hou7 goa2 chit ui7 Thian-hu7, pi2 it-chhe3 khah ui2-tai7; bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 tui3 Thian-hu7 e5 chhiu2 ka7 in chhiuN2-khi3.  30) Goa2 kap Thian-hu7 si7 it-the2 e5.”
	31) Iu5-thai3-lang5 iu7-koh khioh chioh8-thau5 khi2-lai5 boeh ka7 I tim3.  32) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 i2-keng u7 cho3 chin-che7 Thian-hu7 hou7 goa2 cho3 e5 ho2-su7 hou7 lin2 khoaN3 a3. Lin2 si7 ui7-tioh8 goa2 sou2 cho3 e5 tah chit8 hang7 tai7-chi3, boeh eng7 chioh8-thau5 tim3 Goa2?”  33) Iu5-thai3-lang5 in3 I kong2: “Goan2 eng7 chioh8-thau5 boeh ka7 Li2 tim3, m7-si7 in-ui7 Li2 cho3 ho2-su7, si7 in-ui7 Li2 kong2 siat-tok8 e5 oe7! Li2 put-ko3 si7 chit8 e5 lang5, tan7-si7 chu7-chheng Li2 si7 Siong7-chu2.”  34) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Lin2 e5 Lut8-hoat kam2 m7-si7 u7 sia2 kong2, ‘goa2 kong2 lin2 si7 sin5’?  35) Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 e5 oe7 eng2-oan2 si7 chin-li2; ki3-jian5 Siong7-chu2 chheng-hou hiah-e5 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 oe7 e5 lang5 cho3 sin5,  36) na7 an2-ne, Thian-hu7 keng2-soan2 Goa2, chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 chit e5 se3-kan, an3-choaN2 Goa2 kong2 ‘Goa2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2,’ lin2 chiu7 chi2-chek Goa2 kong2 ‘Li2 kong2 siat-tok8 e5 oe7’?  37) Goa2 na7 m7-si7 teh cho3 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 kang-chok, lin2 chiu7 m7-bian2 sin3 Goa2,  38) tan7-si7 Goa2 na7-si7 teh cho3 Thian-hu7 e5 kang-chok, lin2 na7 m7 sin3 Goa2 e5 oe7, chi3-chio2 ia7 tioh8 sin3 Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5. An2-ne lin2 chiu7 e7 khak-sit8 chai-iaN2 Thian-hu7 ti7 goa2 e5 lai7-bin7, goa2 ia7 ti7 Thian-hu7 e5 lai7-bin7.”
	39) In iu7-koh siuN7-boeh liah8 I; tan7-si7 I chiu7 to5-chhut in e5 chhiu2.  40) Ia5-sou iu7-koh koe3-khi3 Iok-tan3-ho5 e5 tui3-hoaN7, khi3-kau3 Iok-han7 khah-cha2 teh ka7 lang5 se2-le2 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 toa3 hia.  41) U7 chin-che7 lang5 lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou. In kong2: “Iok-han7 sui-jian5 bo5 cho3 sin5-chek; tan7-si7 koan-he7 chit e5 lang5, Iok-han7 sou2 kong2 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 u7 iaN2.”  42) Ti7 hia chiu7 u7 chin-che7 lang5 sin3 Ia5-sou.

Te7 11 ChiuN

Liap8-sat-lou7 e5 si2-bong5:
	1) U7 chit8 e5 Pek-tai7-ni5-lang5 hou7(**ho7)-cho3 Liap8-sat-lou7, teh phoaN3-peN7. Pek-tai7-ni5 chiu7-si7 Ma2-li7-a kap i e5 a che2 Ma2-tai7 toa3 e5 hiong-chhoan.  2) (Chit e5 Ma2-li7-a chiu7-si7 eng7 phang-iu5 to3 toa3 Chu2 e5 kha, eng7 i e5 thau5-mng5 ka7 I chhit hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5. Teh phoa3-peN7 hit e5 Liap8-sat-lou7 chiu7-si7 in e5 sio2-ti7.)  3) Chit nng7 e5 chi2-moe7 phai3 lang5 khi3 Ia5-sou hia ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 chhin-ai3 e5 peng5-iu2 teh phoa3-peN7.”  4) Ia5-sou thiaN-tioh8 chit e5 siau-sit chiu7 kong2: “I be7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 peN7 si2. I phoa3-peN7 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hian2-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7, thang hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2 i-ho2 i e5 peN7 lai5 tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7.”
	5) Ia5-sou chin thiaN3 Ma2-tai7 kap i e5 sio2-moe7 i2-kip8 Liap8-sat-lou7.  6) I thiaN-tioh8 Liap8-sat-lou7 teh phoa3-peN7, chiu7 koh toa3 hit e5 sou2-chai7 ke theng5-liu5 nng7 jit8.  7) Jian5-au7 I chiu7 ka7 bun5-tou5 kong2, “Lan2 koh tng2 lai5-khi3 Iu5-thai3 Seng2.”  8) Bun5-tou5 ka7 I kong2: “Lau7-su, chit bo5 loa7 ku2, ti7 hia e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiah boeh eng7 chioh8-thau5 ka7 Li2 tim3-si2, Li2 siuN7-boeh koh tng2-khi3 hia si7 bo5?”  9) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Jit8-si5 kam2 m7-si7 u7 chap8-ji7 tiam2-cheng? Lang5 na7 jit8-si5 lai5 kiaN5-lou7, i chiu7 be7 poah8-to2, in-ui7 i u7 chit e5 se3-kai3 e5 kng thang khoaN3-lou7.  10) Tan7-si7 lang5 na7 am3-si5 lai5 kiaN5-lou7 chiu7 e7 poah8-to2, in-ui7 bo5 kng thang ka7 i chio3-lou7.”  11) Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 liau2-au7 iu7-koh ka7 in kong2: “Lan2 e5 peng5-iu2 Liap8-sat-lou7 teh khun3, tan7-si7 goa2 boeh lai5-khi3 ka7 i kio3 chheN2 khi2-lai5.”  12) Bun5-tou5 chiu7 ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3! I na7 si7 khun3-khi3, chiu7 it-teng7 e7 ho2 khi2-lai5.”  13) Ia5-sou si7 kong2 Liap8-sat-lou7 si2-khi3, tan7-si7 in siuN7 kong2 Ia5-sou si7 teh kong2 i sit8-chai7 teh khun3.  14) Sou2-i2 Ia5-sou chiu7 beng5-beng5 ka7 in kong2: “Liap8-sat-lou7 si2-khi3 a3.  15) Tan7-si7 Goa2 ui7 lin2 lai5 hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i si2-khi3 e5 si5, goa2 bo5 ti7 hia, thang hou7 lin2 lai5 sin3 Goa2. TaN lan2 lai5-khi3 i hia.”  16) Kio3-cho3 siang-seN-a2 e5 To-ma2 ka7 i e5 bun5-tou5-phoaN7 kong2, “Lan2 ia7 kap Lau7-su cho3-hoe2 lai5-khi3 si2.”

Ia5-sou chiu7-si7 hok8-hoat8, chiu7-si7 seN3-mia7:
	17) Ia5-sou khi3 kau3-ui7 e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 Liap8-sat-lou7 i2-keng hou7 lang5 khng3 ti7 bong7-lai7 khng3 si3 jit8 a7.  18) Pek-tai7-ni5 li7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 khah bo5 nng7 eng-li2;  19) u7 chin-che7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 in-ui7 Liap8-sat-lou7 si2-khi3, lai5 ti7 hia boeh an-ui3 Ma2-tai7 kap Ma2-li7-a.
	20) Ma2-tai7 chit8-e7 thiaN-tioh8 Ia5-sou teh lai5, chiu7 chhut-khi3 boeh geng5-chiap I, tan7-si7 Ma2-li7-a che7 ti7 chhu3-lai7.  21) Ma2-tai7 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2: “Chu2 a3, Li2 na7 u7 ti7 chia, goan2 sio2-ti7 chiu7 be7 si2-khi3!  22) Sui-jian5 hian7-chai7 u7 khah ban7 a7, tan7-si7 goa2 chai-iaN2 Li2 bo5-lun7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 kiu5 sim2-mih8, siong7-chu2 it-teng7 e7 eng3-un2 Li2.”  23) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Lin2 sio2-ti7 e7 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5.”  24) Ma2-tai7 ka7 I kong2, “Goa2 chai-iaN2 chai7 boat8-jit8 lang5 hok8-hoat8 e5 si5, i e7 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5.”  25) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “Goa2 chiu7-si7 hok8-hoat8, chiu7-si7 seN3-mia7, sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5, sui-jian5 si2-khi3 ia7 e7 oah8;  26) hian7-chai7 teh oah8 lai5 sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5, chiu7 eng2-oan2 be7 si2. Li2 u7 an2-ne sin3 bo5?”  27) I in3 kong2: “Chu2 a3, u7 la3; goa2 sin3 Li2 si7 Bi2-sai3-a, si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2, chiu7-si7 boeh lai5 se3-kan Hit-ui7.”

Ia5-sou lau5 bak8-sai2:
	28) Ma2-tai7 kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 liau2-au7, tng2-khi3 tiam7-tiam7-a2 ka7 i e5 sio2-moe7 Ma2-li7-a kong2, “Lau7-su lai5 a7. I teh kio3 li2.”  29) Ma2-li7-a chit8-e7 thiaN-tioh8 an2-ne, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 khia7 khi2-lai5 boeh khi3 kiN3 I.  30) (Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou iau2-boe7 jip8-lai5 chng-nih, iau2 ti7 Ma2-tai7 khi3 geng5-chiap I e5 sou2-chai7.)  31) Hiah-e5 kap Ma2-li7-a ti7 chhu3-nih teh ka7 i an-ui3 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 chit8-e7 khoaN3-kiN3 Ma2-li7-a koaN2-kin2 peh khi2-lai5, chiu7 chhut-khi3, in ia7 chiu7 toe3 i khi3, siuN7 kong2 i boeh khi3 bong7-nih8 khau3.
	32) Ma2-li7-a lai5-kau3 Ia5-sou hia. Chit8-e7 khoaN3-kiN3 I, chiu7 phak loh8-khi3 I e5 kha-cheng5, ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 na7 u7 ti7 chia, goan2 sio2-ti7 chiu7 be7 si2-khi3!”  33) Ia5-sou khoaN3-tioh8 i teh khau3, phoaN7 i lai5 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 ia7 teh khau3, chiu7 chin thong3-sim, thou2 chit8-e7 toa7 khui3,  34) mng7 in kong2, “Lin2 ka7 i khng3 ti7 tah-ui7?” In ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, lai5-khi3 khoaN3.”  35) Ia5-sou chiu7 lau5 bak8-sai2.  36) Iu5-thai3-lang5 kong2, “Lin2 khoaN3, I loa7-nih thiaN3 i!”  37) Tan7-si7 in e5 tiong-kan u7 lang5 kong2, “I hou7 chheN-me5-e5 u7 khoaN3-kiN3, kam2 be7-tang3 hou7 chit e5 lang5 mai3 si2?”

“Liap8-sat-lou7, chhut lai5!”
	38) Ia5-sou iu7-koh chin thong3-sim, khi3-kau3 bong7-nih8. Hit e5 bong7 si7 soaN-tong7, u7 chit8 te3 chioh8 that ti7 bong7-khau2.  39) Ia5-sou kong2, “Soa2-khui hit te3 chioh8!” Si2-lang5 e5 a-che2 Ma2-tai7 ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, i i2-keng si2-khi3 si3 jit8 a7, it-teng7 e7 chin chhau3.”  40) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 kam2 bo5 ka7 lin2 kong2 lin2 na7 u7 sin3-sim, chiu7 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7?”  41) Sou2-i2 in chiu7 soa2-khui hit te3 chioh8. Ia5-sou khoaN3 thiN kong2: “Hu7-chhin, Goa2 kam2-sia7 Li2 u7 thiaN Goa2 e5 ki5-kiu5.  42) Goa2 chai-iaN2 Li2 put-si5 to teh thiaN Goa2 e5 ki5-kiu5, tan7-si7 Goa2 an2-ne kong2 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 khia7 ti7 Goa2 chiu-ui5 e5 lang5 siong-sin3 kong2 Goa2 si7 Li2 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5.”  43) Kong2 an2-ne liau2-au7, Ia5-sou chiu7 chin toa7 siaN hoah kong2, “Liap8-sat-lou7, chhut-lai5!”  44) Hit e5 si2-lang5 chiu7 chhut-lai5. I e5 kha-chhiu2 eng7 pou3 pau-le, bin7 eng7 kin-a2 ta3-le3. Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 in kong2, “Ka7 i thau2-khui, hou7 i ka-ti7 kiaN5 tng2-khi3.”

Sat-hai7 Ia5-sou e5 im-bou5: (Ma2 26:1-5; Kho2 14:1-2; Lou7 22:1-2)
	45) U7 chin-che7 lai5 chhoe7 Ma2-li7-a e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5, khoaN3-tioh8 Ia5-sou sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 chiu7 sin3 I.  46) Tan7-si7 ki5-tiong u7 chit8-koa2 khi3 chhoe7 Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, ka7 in po3-ko3 Ia5-sou sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3.  47) Sou2-i2 che3-si-tiuN2 kap Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 tiau3-chip8 kong-gi7-hoe7. In kong2: “Chit e5 lang5 cho3 chin-che7 sin5-chek. TaN lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 lai5 eng3-hu3!  48) Lan2 na7 pang3 hou7 I an2-ne ke3-siok8 khi3 cho3, tak8-lang5 long2 e7 lai5 sin3 I; Lo5-ma2-lang5 ia7 e7 lai5 hui2-biat8 lan2 e5 Seng3-tian7 kap lan2 e5 bin5-chok8.”  49) Ki5-tiong u7 chit8 e5 kio3-cho3 Kai-a-hoat, si7 lun5 tioh8 hit ni5 e5 Toa7-che3-si, ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 long2 m7-bat poaN3-hang7!  50) Lin2 ia7 be7-hiau2 siuN7 kong2 leng5-kho2 hou7 chit8 e5 lang5 the3 cheng3-lang5 lai5 si2, pi2 choan5 bin5-chok8 lai5 biat8-bong5 khah ho2.” 51) I an2-ne kong2 m7-si7 chiau3 i ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3 kong2 chhut-lai5 e5; i cho3 hit ni5 e5 Toa7-che3-si teh I7-gian5 kong2 Ia5-sou boeh ui7 choan5 bin5-chok8 lai5 si2.  52) put-tan7 kan-taN ui7-tioh8 choan5 bin5-chok8, ia7 boeh chiong hun-soaN3 khi3 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5, long2 ka7 in chip8-oa2 lai5 cho3 it-the2.  53) Tui3 hit jit8 khi2, Iu5-thai3-lang5 chiu7 ke3-bou5 boeh sat-hai7 Ia5-sou.  54) In-ui7 an2-ne Ia5-sou chiu7 bo5 boeh koh kong-jian5 toa3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 tiong-kan chhut-jip8; I li7-khui hit e5 sou2-chai7, khi3-kau3 oa2-kin7 khong3-ia2 e5 te7-hng, siam2-pi7 khi3 chit8 e5 ke-a2 kio3-cho3 I2-hoat-lian5, kap I e5 bun5-tou5 chiam7-si5 toa3 hia.
	55) Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 PoaN5-koe3-cheh teh-boeh kau3 a3. Chai7 PoaN5-koe3-cheh i2-cheng5, u7 chin-che7 lang5 tui3 kok hiong-chhoan lai5-kau3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2, boeh ki2-heng5 Kiat-cheng7-le2.  56) In long2 teh chhoe7 Ia5-sou, chu7-chip8 toa3 Seng3-tian7 e5 tiaN5-nih8 e5 si5, chiu7 hou7-siong mng7 kong2, “Lin2 siuN7 an3-choaN2? I kam2 e7 lai5 koe3 cheh?" 57) In-ui7 hit e5 si5 che3-si-tiuN2 kap Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 i2-keng u7 hoat chhut beng7-leng7, kong2 na7 u7 lang5 chai-iaN2 Ia5-sou ti7 tah-ui7, tioh8-ai3 lai5 po3, hou7 in thang khi3 liah8 I.

Te7 12 ChiuN

Chai7 Pek-tai7-ni5 siu7 boah-iu5: (Ma2 26:6-13; Kho2 14:3-9)
	1) PoaN5-koe3-cheh e5 lak8-jit8 cheng5, Ia5-sou khi3-kau3 Pek-tai7-ni5, chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 tiong hou7 i koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Liap8-sat-lou7 teh toa3 e5 sou2-chai7.  2) In ti7 hia pi7-pan7 chheN-chhau chhiaN2 Ia5-sou. Ma2-tai7 lai5 su(**su7)-hau7 I. Liap8-sat-lou7 ia7 si7 kap Ia5-sou che7 kang7 toh e5 chit8 e5.  3) Hit e5 si5, Ma2-li7-a theh8 chit8 kin kek8 ko-kui3 koh sun5-chhui3 e5 Na2-tat8 e5 phang-iu5, lai5 boah Ia5-sou e5 kha, iu7-koh eng7 ka-ti7 e5 thau5-mng5 ka7 I chhit. Kui chhu3-lai7 chhiong-moa2 phang-iu5 e5 phang-bi7.  4) U7 chit8 e5 bun5-tou5, chiu7-si7 au7-lai5 boehchhut-be7 Ia5-sou e5 Ka-liok8 e5 Iu5-tai7, kong2:  5) “Na2 m7 theh8 chiah-e5 phang-iu5 khi3 be7 saN-pah e5 gin5-oan5, lai5 kiu3-che3 san3-chhiah-lang5?”  6) I kong2 an2-ne (**peng7) m7-si7 i u7 koan-sim san3-chhiah-lang5, si7 in-ui7 i si7 chhat8-a2, siong5-siong5 thau-theh8 i teh chiang2-koan2 e5 kong-kim.  7) Ia5-sou chiu7 kong2: “Chhut-chai7 chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 khi3 cho3! Hou7 i chiong hiah-e5 phang-iu5 lau5 kau3 Goa2 e5 bai3-chong3 e5 jit8-chi2.  8) Lin2 siong5-siong5 u7 san3-chhiah-lang5 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2, tan7-si7 goa2 be7 siong5-siong5 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2.”

Sat-hai7 Liap8-sat-lou7 e5 im-bou5:
	9) U7 chit8 toa7 tin7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 chai-iaN2 Ia5-sou ti7 Pek-tai7-ni5 chiu7 lai5, put-tan7 ui7-tioh8 boeh kiN3 Ia5-sou, ia7 ui7-tioh8 boeh khoaN3 Ia5-sou hou7 i tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Liap8-sat-lou7.  10) Sou2-i2 chiah-e5 che3-si-tiuN2 chiu7 koat-i3 lian5 Liap8-sat-lou7 ia7 boeh ka7 i sat-hai7,  11) in-ui7 u7 chin-che7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 ui7-tioh8 Liap8-sat-lou7, li7-khui in, khi3 sin3 Ia5-sou.

Ia5-sou chin3-jip8 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2-siaN5: (Ma2 21:1-11; Kho2 11:1-11; Lou7 19:28-40)
	12) Keh-tng2-jit8, lai5 koe3 PoaN5-koe3-cheh e5 cheng3-lang5 thiaN-kiN3 Ia5-sou boeh lai5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2.  13) In chiu7 giah8 chang-chhiu7-ki, chhut-khi3 geng5-chiap I, hoah kong2: 
“Ho5-san3-na2! (Chan3-bi2 Siong7-chu2!) Goan7 hong7 Chu2 e5 mia5 lai5 e5 siu7 chiok-hok! Goan7 I2-sek-liat8 e5 kun-ong5, siu7 chiok-hok!”
14) Ia5-sou chhoe7-tioh8 chit8 chiah li5-a2-kiaN2 chiu7 peh khi2-khi3 khia5, chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
15) “Sek-an e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2; m7-bian2 kiaN! Li2 khoaN3! Li2 e5 Ong5 khia5 li5-a2-kiaN2 lai5 a7.”
16) Hit tong-si5 bun5-tou5 be7 liau2-kai2 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 au7-lai5 Ia5-sou hok8-hoat8 liau2-au7 tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7 e5 si5, in chiah siuN7 khi2 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 chit hang7 tai7-chi3 si7 teh chi2 Ia5-sou. Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 lang5 ia7 sit8-chai7 chiau3 an2-ne teh ka7 I hoan-geng5.
	17) Ia5-sou kio3 Liap8-sat-lou7 tui3 bong7 chhut-lai5, hou7 i tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 hit e5 si5, hiah-e5 kap Ia5-sou cho3-hoe2 ti7 hia e5 cheng3-lang5, chiong sou2 hoat-seng e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 lang5 cho3 kian3-cheng3.  18) Cheng3-lang5 long2 chhut-khi3 geng5-chiap I, si7 in-ui7 in u7 thiaN-kiN3 Ia5-sou cho3 chit hang7 sin5-chek.  19) Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5 chiu7 hou7-siong kong2, “Lan2 khah an3-choaN2 cho3, ia7 bo5-chhai2-kang a. Lin2 khoaN3, choan5 se3-kai3 e5 lang5 long2 tui-sui5 I khi3 a3!”

Hi-lah8-lang5 lai5 chhoe7 Ia5-sou:
	20) Cheh-ki5 tiong, khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 tiau5-pai3 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan, u7 kui2-na7 e5 Hi-lah8-lang5.  21) Chiah-e5 lang5 lai5 kiN3 Ka-li7-li7 e5 Pek-sai3-tai7-lang5 Hui-lek8, kiu5 i kong2, “Sin-seN, goan2 chin ai3 kiN3 Ia5-sou.”  22) Hui-lek8 chiu7 khi3 ka7 An-tek-liat8 kong2. An-tek-liat8 kap Hui-lek8 chiu7 cho3-hoe2 khi3 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2. 23) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “TaN Jin5-chu2 siu7 eng5-iau7 e5 si5 kau3 a3!  24) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: chit8 liap8 beh8-a2 na7 bo5 lak loh8-khi3 thou5-kha si2, goan5-chai7 si7 chit8 liap8 beh8-a2 nia7-nia7; tan7-si7 na7 chit8-e7 si2-khi3, chiu7 e7 kiat chin-che7 liap8 beh8-a2.  25) Ai3-sioh ka-ti7 e5 seN3-mia7 e5 lang5, chiu7 e7 song3-sit seN3-mia7; bo5 tham-loan5 i chai7 chit e5 se3-kan e5 seN3-mia7 e5 lang5, chiu7 e7 po2-chun5 i e5 seN3-mia7 lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.  26) Na7 boeh su7-hong7 Goa2 e5 lang5, tioh8-ai3 kin-toe3 Goa2; bo5-lun7 Goa2 ti7 tah-ui7, su7-hong7 Goa2 e5 lang5 ia7 e7 ti7 hia. Lang5 na7 su7-hong7 Goa2, Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 ia7 e7 chun-tiong7 i.”

Ia5-sou i7-gian5 I e5 si2-bong5:
	27) “Chit-chun7 Goa2 e5 sim-koaN chin kan-khou2, Goa2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2 chiah ho2? Goa2 boeh lai5 kong2: ‘Hu7-chhin a3, kiu3 Goa2 thoat-li7 chit e5 si5-khek,’ si7 bo5? Be7-eng7-tit! Goa2 lai5 chit e5 se3-kan e5 bok8-tek chiaN3-chiaN3 si7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 si5-khek chiah lai5 e5.  28) Hu7-chhin a3, goan7 Li2 eng5-iau7 Li2 e5 mia5.” Hit e5 si5, tui3 thiN-teng2 u7 siaN lai5, kong2, “Goa2 i2-keng eng5-iau7 Goa2 e5 mia5 a7. goa2 boeh koh-chai3 eng5-iau7 chit8 pai2.”
	29) Khia7 ti7 hia e5 kun5-chiong3 thiaN-kiN3 chit e5 siaN, chiu7 kong2, “Teh tan5-lui5.” Ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “Si7 thian-sai3 teh ka7 Ia5-sou kong2-oe7.”  30) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Chit e5 siaN-im, m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 Goa2, si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 lai5 e5.  31) Hian7-chai7 chiu7-si7 chit e5 se3-kai3 siu7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 si5; chit se3-kai3 e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 e7 siu7 thui-hoan.  32) Goa2 na7 tui3 te7-siong7 pi7 the5-ko e5 si5, chiu7 e7 khip-in2 ban7-bin5 lai5 Goa2 chia.”  33) (Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, si7 piau2-beng5 I boeh an3-choaN2 si2.)  34) Kun5-chiong3 chiu7 in3 I kong2: “Lut8-hoat e5 chheh u7 kau3-hun3 goan2 kong2 Bi2-sai3-a si7 eng2-oan2 chun5-chai7 e5. Li2 an3-choaN2 kong2 Jin5-chu2 it-teng7 tioh8 pi7 the5-ko khi2-lai5? Chit e5 Jin5-chu2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  35) Ia5-sou chiu7 in3 in kong2: “Hit e5 kng iau2-ku2 e7 chai7 lin2 tiong-kan kng tiap(**tiap8)-a2-ku2. Lin2 than3 u7 kng e5 si5 tioh8-ai3 kiaN5, chiah be7 hou7 ou-am3 ka7 lin2 jia-kham3 khi3. Ti7 ou-am tiong teh kiaN5 e5 lang5, m7 chai i boeh khi3 tah-ui7.  36) Lin2 than3 u7 kng e5 si5, tioh8-ai3 sin3 chit e5 kng, thang hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 kong-beng5 e5 kiaN2-ji5.”

Iu5-thai3-lang5 m7-sin3:
	Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 liau2-au7, chiu7 li7-khui in, iam2-sin hou7 in bo5 khoaN3-kiN3.  37)
Ia5-sou sui-jian5 ti7 in e5 bin7-cheng5 cho3 chiah-nih8 che7 e5 sin5-chek, in iau2-ku2 m7 sin3 I.  38) Che si7 boeh eng3-giam7 tai7-gian5-jin5 I2-sai3-a sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, kong2:
“Chu2 a3, sim2-mih8 lang5 u7 siong-sin3 goan2 sou2 thoan5 e5 sin3-sek? Chu2 u7 tui3 sim2-mih8 lang5 hian2-si7 I e5 koan5-leng5?”
39) In be7-tang3 siong-sin3 e5 li2-iu5 chiu7-si7 an2-ne, in-ui7 I2-sai3-a iu7-koh kong2:
40) “Siong7-chu2 hou7 in e5 bak8-chiu chheN-me5, hou7 in e5 sim hun7-tun7 khi3. Na7 bo5, in e5 bak8-chiu e7 khoaN3-kiN3, in e5 sim e7 li2-kai2, lai5 kui-hiong3 Goa2, hou7 Goa2 ka7 in i-ho2 khi3.
41) I2-sai3-a an2-ne kong2, si7 in-ui7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 eng5-iau7, sou2-i2 teh kong2 I e5 tai7-chi3.
	42) Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ne, lian5 hiah-e5 Iu5-thai5-lang5 e5 leng2-siu7(**siu3) ia7 u7 chin-che7 lang5 sin3 Ia5-sou; put-ko3 in-ui7 kiaN Hoat-li7-sai3-phai3 e5 lang5, in m7-kaN2 kong-jian5 kong2 chhut-lai5, kiaN-liau2 hou7 in tui3 hoe7-tng5 koaN2 chhut-khi3.  43) In hou7 lang5 o-lo2, pi2 Siong7-chu2 e5 ka-chiong2 khoaN3 khah tang7.

Ia5-sou e5 keng3-ko3:
	44) Ia5-sou chiu7 toa7 siaN kong2: “Sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5 m7-si7 sin3 Goa2, si7 sin3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7.  45) KhoaN3-kiN3 goa2 e5 lang5, si7 khoaN3-kiN3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7.  46) Goa2 chiu7-si7 kng, lai5 chit e5 se3-kai3, thang hou7 sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5 m7-bian2 lau5 toa3 ou-am3 tiong.  47) Lang5 na7 thiaN Goa2 e5 ka3-si7 bo5 chun-siu2 e5, Goa2 bo5 boeh ka7 i sim2-phoaN3. In-ui7 Goa2 m7-si7 lai5 boeh ka7 se3-kai3 sim2-phoaN3; si7 boeh lai5 chin2-kiu3 se3-kai3.  48) Khi3-choat8 Goa2, m7 chiap-lap8 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7 e5 lang5, u7 chit8 e5 sim2-phoaN3-chia2 e7 ka7 i sim2-phoaN3. Chiu7-si7 Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 to7-li2, chai7 boat8-jit8 e7 ka7 i sim2-phoaN3.  49) In-ui7 Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, m7-si7 tui3 Goa2 ka-ti7 lai5 e5, long2-si7 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5 Thian-hu7 beng7-leng7 goa2 tioh8 kong2 sim2-mih8, tioh8 thoan5 sim2-mih8.  50) Goa2 chai-iaN2 I e5 beng7-leng7 chiu7-si7 eng2-seng; sou2-i2 Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5, long2 si7 chun-chiau3 I e5 chi2-si7 lai5 kong2 e5.”

Te7 13 ChiuN

Ia5-sou ka7 bun5-tou5 se2-kha:
	1) PoaN5-koe3-cheh cheng5, Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 boeh li7-khui chit e5 se3-kan khi3 Thian-hu7 hia e5 si5 i2-keng kau3 a3. I ki3-jian5 thiaN3 chit se3-kan I ka-ti7 e5 lang5; taN I boeh piau2-si7 thiaN3 in kau3 choe3-au7.
	2) Ia5-sou kap bun5-tou5 teh chiah8 boan2-chhan e5 si5, Mou5-kui2 i2-keng chin3-jip8 khi3 Ka-liok8 e5 Se-bun5 e5 kiaN2 Iu5-tai7 e5 sim-lai7, hou7 i koat-i3 boeh chhut-be7 Ia5-sou.  3) Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 Thian-hu7 i2-keng chiong it-chhe3 e5 koan5 kau ti7 I e5 chhiu2-thau5, ia7 chai-iaN2 I ka-ti7 si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5, ia7 boeh koh to3 tng2-khi3 Siong7-chu2 hia.  4) Sou2-i2 I chiu7 li7-khui boan2-chhan e5 toh, thng3 I e5 goa7-saN khi2-lai5, theh8 chit8 tiau5 kin-a2 ha5 toa3 io-nih.  5) Jian5-au7 to3-chui2 toa3 bin7-thang2, chiu7 khai-si2 ka7 bun5-tou5 se2-kha, eng7 ha5 ti7 io-nih e5 kin-a2 ka7 in chhit.  6) I lai5-kau3 Se-bun5 Pi2-tek hia, Pi2-tek ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 boeh ka7 goa2 se-kha si7 bo5?”  7) Ia5-sou in3 kong2, “Goa2 teh cho3 e5, li2 hian7-chai7 be7 beng5-pek8, tan7-si7 au7-lai5 chiu7 e7 beng5-pek8.”  8) Pi2-tek ka7 I kong2, “Li2 chhian-ban7 m7-thang ka7 goa2 se2-kha!” Ia5-sou in3 i kong2, “Goa2 na7 bo5 ka7 li2 se2, li2 chiu7 kap Goa2 long2 bo5 koan-lian5!”  9) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek  chiu7 ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, na7-si7 an2-ne, m7-na7 goa2 e5 kha, lian5 goa2 e5 chhiu2 kap thau5-khak ia7 ka7 goa2 se2!”  10) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “Lang5 na7 se2 seng-khu se2 liau2-au7, kui seng-khu chiu7 long2 chheng-khi3 a3, chi2-u7 su-iau3 se2-kha chiu7 ho2. Lin2 tai7-ke long2 chheng-khi3 a3, tan7-si7 m7-si7 lin2 tak8 e5 long2 chheng-khi3 a3.”  11) (Goan5-lai5 Ia5-sou i2-keng chai-iaN2 sim2-mih8 lang5 boeh chhut-be7 I, sou2-i2 I chiah kong2. “M7-si7 lin2 tak8 e5 long2 chheng-khi3 a3.”)
	12) Ia5-sou ka7 in se2-kha liau2-au7, koh chheng7 goa7-saN, chiu7 to3 tng2-khi3 boan2-chhan e5 toh, ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 chai-iaN2 Goa2 tu2-chiah ui7 lin2 sou2 cho3 e5 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3 bo5?  13) Lin2 chheng-hou Goa2 cho3 ‘Lau7-su’, chheng-hou7 Goa2 cho3 “Chu2’; lin2 chheng-hou an2-ne tioh8; Goa2 sit8-chai7 si7.  14) goa2 cho3 lin2 e5 Chu2, cho3 lin2 e5 Lau7-su siong7-chhiaN2 ka7 lin2 se2-kha, lin2 ia7 tioh8-ai3 hou7-siong ka7 lang5 se2-kha.  15) Goa2 ka7 lin2 an2-ne cho3, lau5 chit8 e5 bou5-hoan7, thang hou7 lin2 chiau3 an2-ne khi3 cho3.  16) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: Lou5-le7 be7 pi2 i e5 chu2-lang5 khah toa7; pi7 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5, ia7 be7 pi2 chhe-phai3  i lai5 e5 khah toa7.  17) Lin2 ki3-jian5 chai-iaN2 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 na7 chiau3 an2-ne khi3 sit8-heng5, chiu7 sit8-chai7 u7 hok-khi3.”
	18) “Goa2 m7-si7 teh kong2 lin2 tai7-ke long2-chong2 an2-ne; Goa2 chai-iaN2 Goa2 sou2 keng2-soan2 chiah-e5 lang5 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2 e5; tan7-si7 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 chit ku3 tioh8-ai3 eng3-giam7; chiu7-si7 kong2: ‘Kap Goa2 cho3-hoe2 chiah8-png7 e5 lang5, u7 lang5 e7 poe7-gek8 Goa2.’  19) TaN tai7-chi3 iau2-boe7 hoat-seng i2-cheng5, goa2 tai7-seng ka7 lin2 kong2, hou7 lin2 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng e5 si5, thang sin3 goa2 chiu7-si7 Hit-ui7.  20) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: chiap-lap8 Goa2 sou2 chhe-phai3 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 chiap-lap8 Goa2; chiap-lap8 Goa2 e5, chiu7-si7 chiap-lap8 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7.”

I7-gian5 u7 lang5 boeh chhut-be7 Ia5-sou: (Ma2 26:20-25; Kho2 14:17-21; Lou7 22:21-23)
	21) Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 liau2-au7, sim-lai7 chiu7 chin kan-khou2, toan3(*7)-gian5 kong2: “Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: lin2 e5 tiong-kan u7 chit8 e5 lang5 boeh chhut-be7 Goa2.”  22) Bun5-tou5 chiu7 hou7-siong khoaN3-lai5 khoaN3khi3, m7 chai-iaN2 I si7 teh kong2 sim2-mih8 lang5.  23) Bun5-tou5 e5 tiong-kan u7 chit8 e5 Ia5-sou chin thiaN3 e5, che7 ti7 Ia5-sou e5 seng-khu piN.  24) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek chiu7 tim3-thau5, se3 siaN ka7 i kong2, “Mng7 khoaN3 I si7 teh kong2 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  25) Hit e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 pheng7 toa3 Ia5-sou e5 heng-cheng5, mng7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, i si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  26) Ia5-sou in3 kong2, “Goa2 boeh un3 chit8 te3 mi7-pau theh8 hou7 i, hit e5 chiu7-si7.” Ia5-sou chiu7 un3 hit te3 mi7-pau, theh8 hou7 Ka-liok8 e5 Se-bun5 e5 kiaN2 Iu5-tai7.  27) Chit8-e7 chiah8-tioh8 hit te3 mi7-pau, Sat-tan3 chiu7 jip8-khi3 i e5 sim-lai7. Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 siuN7-boeh cho3 e5, koaN2-kin2 khi3 cho3.”  28) Kang7-toh e5 lang5, bo5 lang5 chai-iaN2 Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2 an2-ne si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3.  29) In-ui7 Iu5-tai7 teh koan2 kong-kim, sou2-i2 in tiong-kan u7 lang5 siuN7-cho3 Ia5-sou si7 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 khi3 be2 lan2 koe3-cheh ai3 eng7 e5 mih88,” a2-si7 kio3 i khi3 chin2-che3 san3-chhiah-lang5.  30) Iu5-tai7 chiah8 hit te3 mi7-pau liau2-au7, chiu7 sui5-si5 chhut-khi3. Hit e5 si5 si7 am3-si5.

Sin e5 kai3-beng7:
	31) Iu5-tai7 chhut-khi3 liau2-au7, Ia5-sou chiu7 kong2: “TaN Jin5-chu2 siu7-tioh8 eng5-iau7 a7, Siong7-chu2 ia7 thong-koe3 Jin5-chu2 lai5 siu7-tioh8 eng5-iau7 a7.  32) Siong7-chu2 ki3-jian5 thong-koe3 Jin5-chu2 siu7-tioh8 eng5-iau7, Siong7-chu2 ia7 boeh chai7 I e5 bin7-cheng5 hou7 Jin5-chu2 tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7, (**peng7-chhiaN2) sui5-si5 chiu7 boeh hou7 I tit-tioh8.  33) Chhin-ai3 e5 bun5-tou5 a3, Goa2 koh kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 bo5 loa7 ku2. Lin2 e7 chhoe7 Goa2, chhin-chhiuN7 Goa2 bat ka7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kong2-koe3, taN Goa2 ia7 ka7 lin2 kong2, ‘Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lin2 be7-tang3 khi3.’ 34) Goa2 hou7 lin2 chit8 tiau5 sin e5 kai3-beng7: tioh8 siong-chhin siong-ai3; chhin-chhiuN7 Goa2 ai3 lin2, lin2 ia7 tioh8 an2-ne siong-chhin siong-ai3.  35) Lin2 na7 an2-ne siong-chhin siong-ai3, cheng3-lang5 chiu7 e7 chai-iaN2 lin2 si7 Goa2 e5 bun5-tou5.”

I7-gian5 Pi2-tek m7-jin7 Chu2: (Ma2 26:31-35; Kho2 14:27-31; Lou7 22:31-34)
	36) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 boeh khi3 tah-ui7?” Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Goa2 boeh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, li2 hian7-chai7 be7-tang3 toe3 Goa2 khi3. Tan7-si7 au7-lai5 li2 chiu7 e7 toe3 Goa2 khi3.”  37) Pi2-tek ka7 I kong2, “Chu2 a3, an3-choaN2 goa2 hian7-chai7 be7-tang3 toe3 Li2 khi3? Goa2 kam-goan7 ui7-tioh8 Li2 lai5 si2!”  38) Ia5-sou in3 kong2: “Li2 sit8-chai7 boeh ui7-tioh8 Goa2 lai5 si2 si7 bo5? goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2, ke thi5 i2-cheng5, li2 e7 saN pai2 m7-jin7 Goa2.”

Te7 14 ChiuN

Ia5-sou chiu7-si7 boeh khi3 Thian-hu7 hia e5 to7-lou7:
	1) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Lin2 e5 sim m7-thang chhau-hoan5. Lin2 tioh8 sin3 Siong7-chu2, ia7 tioh8 sin3 Goa2.  2) Ti7 goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 chhu3-nih u7 chin-che7 pang5-keng. Goa2 boeh khi3 ka7 lin2 pi7-pan7 sou2-chai7. Na7 m7-si7 an2-ne Goa2 chiu7 bo5 ka7 lin2 kong2 chit e5 tai7-chi3.  3) Goa2 na7 khi3 ka7 lin2 pi7-pan7 sou2-chai7 liau2-au7, it-teng7 e7 koh lai5 chhoa7 lin2 khi3 Goa2 hia; Goa2 ti7 hia, lin2 ia7 thang ti7 hia.  4) Goa2 boeh khi3 hia hit tiau5 lou7 lin2 it-teng7 chai-iaN2.”  5) To-ma2 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Chu2 a3, goan2 m7 chai-iaN2 Li2 boeh khi3 tah-ui7; an3-choaN2 e7 chai-iaN2 hit tiau5 lou7?”  6) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2: “Goa2 chiu7-si7 to7-lou7, chin-li2, seN3-mia7. Na7 bo5 keng-koe3 Goa2, bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 khi3-kau3 Thian-hu7 hia.  7) Lin2 ki3-jian5 bat Goa2, lin2 ia7 chiu7 e7 bat Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7. Tui3 hian-chai7 khi2 lin2 bat I, peng3(**peng7)chhiaN2 i2-keng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 I a.”
	8) Hui-lek8 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Chu2 a3, chiong Thian-hu7 hian2-si7 hou7 goan2 khoaN3, goan2 chiu7 sim-boan2 i3-chiok!”  9) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “Hui-lek8, Goa2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 chiah-nih8 ku2, li2 iau2 m7-bat Goa2 si7 bo5? KhoaN3-kiN3 Goa2 e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 i2-keng khoaN3-kiN3 Thian-hu7. Li2 an3-choaN2 kong2, ‘Chiong Thian-hu7 hian2-si7 hou7 goan2 khoaN3’?  10) Li2 m7 sin3 Goa2 ti7 Thian-hu7 e5 lai7-bin7, Thian-hu7 ia7 ti7 Goa2 e5 lai7-bin7 si7 bo5? Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 e5 oe7, m7-si7 tui3 Goa2 chhut-lai5 e5. Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, si7 toa3 ti7 Goa2 lai7-bin7 e5 Thian-hu7 chhin-sin cho3 e5.  11) Lin2 tioh8 sin3 Goa2 ti7 Thian-hu7 e5 lai7-bin7, Thian-hu7 ti7 Goa2 e5 lai7-bin7. Na7 bo5, chi3-chio2 ia7 tioh8 khoaN3 Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 sin3 Goa2.  12) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: sin3 Goa2 e5 lang5, ia7 e7 cho3 Goa2 teh cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, siong7-chhiaN2 e7 cho3 pi2 Goa2 cho3 e5 koh-khah toa7 e5 tai7-chi3, in-ui7 Goa2 teh-boeh tng2-khi3 Thian-hu7 hia.  13) Lin2 hong7 Goa2 e5 mia5 bo5-lun7 kiu5 sim2-mih8, Goa2 it-teng7 boeh siuN2-su3 lin2, thang hou7 Thian-hu7 thong-koe3 Seng3-chu2 lai5 tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7.  14) Lin2 na7 hong7 Goa2 e5 mia5 ka7 Goa2 kiu5 sim2-mih8, Goa2 ia7 it-teng7 boeh siuN2-su3 lin2.”

Eng3-un2 siuN2-su3 Seng3 Leng5:
	15) “Lin2 na7 keng3-ai3 Goa2, chiu7 tioh8-ai3 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 kai3-beng7.  16) Goa2 boeh khun2-kiu5 Thian-hu7, I chiu7 e7 siuN2-su3 leng7-goa7 chit8 ui7 An-ui3-chia2 hou7 lin2, chiu7-si7 Chin-li2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, thang kap lin2 eng2-oan2 tong5-chai7.  17) Se3-kan e5 lang5 be7-tang3 chiap-siu7 Chin-li2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, in-ui7 ia7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 I, ia7 m7-bat I; tan7-si7 lin2 bat I, in-ui7 I kap lin2 tong5-chai7, ia7 toa3 ti7 lin2 sim-lai7.”
	18) “Goa2 bo5 boeh pang3 lin2 cho3 kou-ji5; Goa2 boeh koh lai5 lin2 chia.  19) Koh bo5 loa7-ku2, se3-kan-lang5 chiu7 be7 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2, tan7-si7 lin2 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2, in-ui7 Goa2 oah8, lin2 ia7 e7 oah8.  20) Kau3 hit chit8 jit8, lin2 chiu7 e7 chai-iaN2 Goa2 ti7 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 lai7-bin7, lin2 ti7 Goa2 e5 lai7-bin7, Goa2 ia7 ti7 lin2 e5 lai7-bin7.”
21) “Chiap-siu7 Goa2 e5 kai3-beng7, ia7 u7 chun-siu2 e5 lang5, chiah si7 keng3-ai3 Goa2 e5 lang5. Keng3-ai3 Goa2 e5 lang5, goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 it-teng7 e7 thiaN3 i; Goa2 ia7 boeh thiaN3 i, ia7 chiong Goa2 pun2-sin hian2-si7 hou7 i.”  22) Iu5-tai7 --- m7-si7 hit e5 Ka-liok8 e5 Iu5-tai7 --- mng7 Ia5-sou kong2: “Chu2 a3, Li2 si7 an3-choaN2 boeh tui3 se-kan-lang5 hian2-si7?”  23) Ia5-sou in3 i kong2: “Lang5 na7 keng3-ai3 Goa2, chiu7 it-teng7 e7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 oe7. Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 ia7 e7 thiaN3 i. goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 kap Goa2 ia7 boeh lai5-kau3 i hia, kap i tong5-chai7.  24) Bo5 keng3-ai3 Goa2 e5 lang5 chiu7 bo5 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 oe7. Lin2 sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5 oe7 m7-si7 goa2 ka-ti7 e5, si7 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 oe7.”
25) “Goa2 iau2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, goa2 i2-keng u7 ka7 lin2 kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 a7.  26) Tan7-si7 Hit-ui7 An-ui3-chia2, chiu7-si7 Thian-hu7 eng7 Goa2 e5 mia5 boeh chhe-phai3 lai5 e5 Seng3 Leng5, e7 ka3-si7 lin2 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 e7 hou7 lin2 siuN7-khi2 Goa2 sou2 ka7 lin2 kong2 it-chhe3 e5 oe7.”
27) “Goa2 lau5 peng5-an hou7 lin2; Goa2 chiong Goa2 ka-ti7 e5 peng5-an siuN2-su3 lin2. Goa2 sou2 siuN2-su3 lin2 e5 peng5-an, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 chit se3-kan e5 peng5-an. Lin2 e5 sim m7-thang chhau-hoan5 ia7 m7-thang kiaN-hiaN5.  28) Lin2 i2-keng u7 thiaN-kiN3 Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 a3, ‘goa2 teh-boeh li7-khui a, tan7-si7 boeh tng2-lai5 lin2 chia.’ Lin2 na7 u7 keng3-ai3 Goa2, chiu7 e7 hoaN-hi2 Goa2 teh-boeh khi3 Thian-hu7 hia, in-ui7 Thian-hu7 pi2 Goa2 khah toa7.  29) TaN tai7-chi3 iau2-boe7 hoat-seng i2-cheng5, Goa2 i2-keng tai7-seng ka7 lin2 kong2 a3, thang hou7 lin2 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng e5 si5 lai5 siong-sin3.  30) Goa2 bo5 boeh koh ke kap lin2 kong2 a3, in-ui7 chit e5 se3-kai3 e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 teh lai5 a7. I be7-tang3 khong3-che3 Goa2;  31) tan7-si7 se3-kan-lang5 tioh8 chai-iaN2 Goa2 keng3-ai3 Thian-hu7, ia7 tioh8-ai3 chai-iaN2 Thian-hu7 beng7-leng7 Goa2 cho3 e5, Goa2 tak8-hang7 chiau3 an2-ne teh cho3.”
“Khi2-lai5, lan2 lai5-khi3.”

Te7 15 ChiuN

Ia5-sou si7 chin e5 phu5-to5-chhiu7:
	1) “Goa2 si7 chin e5 phu5-to5-chhiu7, goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 si7 chai-poe5 phu5-to5 e5.  2) Goa2 e5 chhiu7-oe na7 bo5 kiat koe2-chi2, I chiu7 long2 ka7 in chian2-tiau7; na7 u7 kiat koe2-chi2 e5, I chiu7 ka7 in siu-chian2, thang hou7 i kiat koh-khah che7 koe2-chi2.  3) TaN in-ui7 lin2 chiap-siu7 Goa2 sou2 ka3-si7 e5 oe7, lin2 i2-keng siu-cheng2 ho2 a3.  4) Lin2 tioh8 kap Goa2 kiat-hap8, Goa2 ia7 e7 kap lin2 kiat-hap8. Phu5-to5-oe na7 bo5 lian5-chiap ti7 phu5-to5-chhiu7, ka-ti7 chiu7 be7-tang3 kiat koe2-chi2; chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne, lin2 na7 bo5 kap Goa2 kiat-hap8, ia7-si7 be7-tang3 kiat koe2-chi2.”
	5) “Goa2 si7 phu5-to5-chhiu7, lin2 si7 phu5-to5-oe. Kap Goa2 kiat-hap8 e5 lang5, Goa2 ia7 kap i kiat-hap8, chit khoan2 e5 lang5 chiu7 e7 kiat chin-che7 koe2-chi2; lin2 na7 kap Goa2 hun-li7, chiu7 be7-tang3 cho3 sim2-mih8.  6) Lang5 na7 bo5 kap Goa2 kiat-hap8 le7, chiu7 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 phu5-to5-oe hou7 lang5 tan3 chhut-khi3 goa7 bin7, soah ta khi3; lang5 e7 siu-chip8 chit khoan2 e5 chhiu7-oe ka7 in hiat loh8-khi3 hoe2-nih sio.  7) Lin2 na7 kap Goa2 kiat-hap8, Goa2 e5 oe7 na7 lau5 ti7 lin2 e5 sim-lai7, an2-ne lin2 bo5-lun7 kiu5 sim2-mih8, long2 e7 ka7 lin2 seng5-choan5.  8) Lin2 na7 kiat chin-che7 koe2-chi2, sit8-chai7 lai5 cho3 goa2 e5 bun5-tou5, an2-ne Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 chiu7 siu7-tioh8 eng5-iau7.  9) Chhin-chhiuN7 Thian-hu7 thiaN3 Goa2, Goa2 ia7 an2-ne thiaN3 lin2; lin2 tioh8 po2-chhi5 Goa2 e5 ai3-sim.  10) Lin2 na7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 kai3-beng7, chiu7 e7 po2-chhi5 Goa2 e5 ai3-sim, chhin-chhiuN7 Goa2 u7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 kai3-beng7 lai5 po2-chhi5 I e5 ai3-sim.”
	11) “Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 an2-ne si7 boeh hou7 Goa2 e5 hi2-lok8 lai5 chiaN5-cho3 lin2 e5 hi2-lok8, hou7 lin2 e5 hi2-lok8 e7-tang3 sip8-choan5 sip8-bi2.  12) Chhin-chhiuN7 Goa2 thiaN3 lin2 an2-ne, lin2 tioh8-ai3 siong-chhin siong-ai3. Che si7 Goa2 e5 kai3-beng7.  13) Lang5 na7 ui7-tioh8 i e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 hi-seng ka-ti7 e5 seN3-mia7, bo5 chit8 hang7 pi2 che koh-khah ui2-tai7 e5 ai3-sim.  14) Lin2 na7 u7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 kai3-beng7, chiu7-si7 Goa2 e5 peng5-iu2.  15) Goa2 bo5 boeh koh kio3 lin2 cho3 lou5-pok8 a7, in-ui7 lou5-pok8 m7 chai-iaN2 i e5 chu2-lang5 teh cho3 sim2-mih8; Goa2 kio3 lin2 cho3 peng5-iu2, in-ui7 Goa2 tui3 goa2 e5 Thian7-hu7 sou2 thiaN-tioh8 e5 it-chhe3, long2 u7 ka7 lin2 kong2 a3.  16) M7-si7 lin2 keng2-soan2 Goa2, si7 Goa2 keng2-soan2 lin2, jim7-beng7 lin2 khi3 kiat chin-che7 koe2-chi2; hou7 lin2 e5 koe2-chi2 eng2-kiu2 chun5-siok8.  An2-ne lin2 na7 hong7 Goa2 e5 mia5 bo5-lun7 tui3 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 kiu5 sim2-mih8, I chiu7 e7 siuN2-su3 lin2.  17) Lin2 tioh8-ai3 siong-chhin siong-ai3. Che chiu7-si7 Goa2 e5 beng7-leng7.”

Se3-kan e5 oan3-hin7:
	18) “Se3-kan-lang5 na7 oan3-hin7 lin2, lin2 tioh8 chai-iaN2 se3-kan-lang5 tai7-seng oan3-hin7 Goa2, chiah oan3-hin7 lin2.  19) Lin2 na7-si7 siok8 ti7 chit e5 se3-kan, se3-kan-lang5 chiu7 e7 chun2-cho3 i ka-ti7 e5 lang5 lai5 ai3 lin2. In-ui7 lin2 m7-si7 siok8 ti7 chit se3-kan, si7 Goa2 tui3 se3-kan ka7 lin2 keng2-soan2 chhut-lai5 e5, sou2-i2 se3-kan-lang5 chiah e7 oan3-hin7 lin2.  20) Lin2 tioh8 e7-ki3-tit Goa2 sou2 ka7 lin2 kong2-koe3 e5 oe7, kong2, ‘Lou5-pok8 be7 pi2 i e5 chu2-lang5 khah toa7.’ In na7 u7 pek-hai7 Goa2, ia7 it-teng7 e7 pek-hai7 lin2; in na7 u7 chun-siu2 Goa2 e5 ka3-si7, ia7 e7 chun-siu2 lin2 e5 ka3-si7.  21) Tan7-si7 in-ui7 lin2 si7 Goa2 e5 bun5-tou5, in chiu7 e7 an2-ne lai5 tui3-thai7 lin2, in-ui7 in m7-bat Hit-ui7 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 e5.  22) Ka2-su2 Goa2 na7 bo5 lai5 ka3-si7 in, in chiu7 bo5 choe7; tan7-si7 taN in bo5 hoat-tou7 thang pian7-kai2 in e5 choe7 a7.  23) Oan3-hin7 Goa2 e5 lang5, ia7-si7 oan3-hin7 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7.  24) Goa2 ti7 in e5 tiong-kan, na7 bo5 cho3 m7-bat lang5 cho3 e5 kang-chok, in chiu7 bo5 choe7; tan7-si7 in i2-keng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5 kang-chok, siong7-chhiaN2 oan3-hin7 Goa2 kap Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7.  25) An2-ne che si7 boeh eng3-giam7 in e5 Lut8-hoat sou2 ki3-chai3 e5 oe7, kong2, ‘In bo5-ian5-bo5-kou3 oan3-hin7 Goa2!’”
	26) “Hit ui7 An-ui3-chia2 chiu7-si7 chin-li2 e5 Leng5, e7 tui3 Thian-hu7 hia lai5. Hit ui7 si7 Goa2 boeh tui3 Thian-hu7 hia chhe-phai3 lai5 lin2 chia e5. I e7 ui7 Goa2 cho3-cheng3.  27) Lin2 ia7 e7 ka7 Goa2 cho3-cheng3, in-ui7 lin2 chu7 khi2-thau5 chiu7 kap Goa2 cho3-hoe2.”

Te7 16 ChiuN

	1) “Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim be7 khi2 tong7-iau5.  2) Lang5 e7 tui3 hoe7-tng5 ka7 lin2 koaN2 chhut-khi3, ji5-chhiaN2 boeh sat-hai7 lin2 e5 lang5, long2 e7 siuN7-cho3 i an2-ne cho3 si7 teh su7-hong7 Siong7-chu2, hit e5 si5 teh-boeh kau3 a3.  3) In si7 in-ui7 m7-bat Thian-hu7, ia7 m7-bat Goa2 chiah e7 cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3.  4) Tan7-si7 Goa2 chiong chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 lin2 kong2, si7 ui7-tioh8 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng e5 si5 na7 kau3, thang hou7 lin2 siuN7-khi2 Goa2 ka7 lin2 keng2-kai3 e5 oe7.”

Seng3 Leng5 e5 kang-chok:
	“Goa2 tui3 khi2-thau5 bo5 chiong chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 lin2 kong2, si7 in-ui7 Goa2 iau2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2.  5) Tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 Goa2 teh-boeh tng2-khi3 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5 Hit-ui7 hia, lin2 tiong-kan ia7 bo5 lang5 mng7 Goa2 kong2, ‘Li2 teh-boeh khi3 tah-ui7?’  6) TaN Goa2 chit8-e7 ka7 lin2 kong2 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 chiu7 moa2-simiu-bun7.  7) Tan7-si7 Goa2 chiong sit8-cheng5 ka7 lin2 kong2: Goa2 khi3 si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 e5 li7-ek; Goa2 na7 bo5 khi3, hit ui7 An-ui3-chia2 chiu7 be7 lai5-kau3 lin2 chia. Tan7-si7 Goa2 na7 khi3, Goa2 chiu7 boeh chhe-phai3 I lai5-kau3 lin2 chia.  8) I lai5 e5 si5, I e7 tui3 se3-kan-lang5 chi2-cheng3 in koan-he7 choe7-ok, koan-he7 cheng3-gi7, koan-he7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 chho3-gou7 e5 koan-liam7.  9) Koan-he7 choe7-ok e5 chho3-gou7, si7 in-ui7 in m7 sin3 Goa2;  10) koan-he7 cheng3-gi7 e5 chho3-gou7 si7 in-ui7 Goa2 teh-boeh khi3 Thian-hu7 hia, lin2 be7 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2;  11) koan-he7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 chho3-gou7 si7 in-ui7 chit e5 se3-kan e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 i2-keng siu7 sim2-phoaN3 a3.”
	12) “Goa2 iau2 u7 chin-che7 tai7-chi3 boeh ka7 lin2 kong2, tan7-si7 lin2 hian7-chai7 iau2-ku2 be7-tang3 liau2-kai2.  13) Tan7-si7 Hit-ui7 chin-li2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 e5 si5, I chiu7 e7 leng2-to7 lin2 liau2-kai2 it-chhe3 e5 chin-li2, in-ui7 I be7 chiau3 I ka-ti7 e5 i3-su3 lai5 kong2. I e7 chiong I sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5 kong2 chhut-lai5, ia7 e7 chiong bi7-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 lin2 kong2.  14) Seng3 Leng5 e7 eng5-iau7 Goa2, in-ui7 I e7 chiong tui3 Goa2 nia2-siu7 e5, long2 thoan5 hou7 lin2.  15) Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 sou2 u7 e5 it-chhe3 long2 si7 Goa2 e5; sou2-i2 Goa2 chiah kong2, ‘Seng3 Leng5 e7 chiong tui3 Goa2 nia2-siu7 e5 chin-li2, long2 thoan5 hou7 lin2.’”

Iu-chhiu5 kap hi2-lok8:
	16) “Koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 be7-tang3 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2, jian5-au7 koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 e7 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2.”  17) U7 kui2-na7 e5 bun5-tou5 hou7-siong kong2: “I ka7 lan2 kong2, ‘Koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 be7-tang3 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2, jian5-au7 koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 e7 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2,’ iu7-koh kong2, ‘In-ui7 Goa2 teh-boeh khi3 Thian-hu7 hia,’ che si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3?”  18) Sou2-i2 in chiu7 kong2: “I kong2 chit ku3 ‘koh bo5 loa7 ku2’ kiu3-keng3 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3? goan2 m7 chai-iaN2 I sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 e5 i3-su3.”  19) Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 in siuN7 ai3 mng7 I; chiu7 ka7 in kong2: “In-ui7 Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2. ‘Koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 be7-tang3 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2; jian5-au7 koh bo5 loa7 ku2, lin2 chiu7 e7 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2,’ lin2 teh mng7-lai5-mng7-khi3 si7 ui7-tioh8 chit ku3 e5 i3-su3 a2 m7-si7?  20) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: lin2 e7 thi5-khau3 lau5 bak8-sai2, tan7-si7 se3-kan-lang5 e7 hoaN-hi2; lin2 e7 pi-siong, tan7-si7 lin2 e5 pi-siong e7 pian3-cho3 hi2-lok8.  21) Hu7-jin7-lang5 seN-kiaN2 e5 si5, e7 kam2-kak thong3-khou2, in-ui7 i seng-san2 e5 si5 kau3 a3; tan7-si7 eN-a2 chit8 e7 seN chhut-lai5, hoaN-hi2 chit8 e5 lang5 chhut-si3 lai5 chit se3-kan, i soah be7-ki3-tit seng-san2 e5 thong3-khou2.  22) Hian7-chai7 lin2 ia7 si7 an2-ne teh pi-siong, tan7-si7 Goa2 boeh koh kiN3 lin2. Lin2 e5 sim chiu7 e7 chhiong-moah2i-lok8, peng3(**peng7)chhiaN2 lin2 e5 hi2-lok8, bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 ka7 lin2 chhiuN2-khi3.”
	23) Hit e5 jit8-chi2 na7 kau3, lin2 chiu7 be7 koh mng7 Goa2 sim2-mih8. Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 lin2 kong2: lin2 bo5-lun7 tui3 Thian-hu7 kiu5 sim2-mih8, I it-teng7 e7 eng7 Goa2 e5 mia5 siuN2-su3 lin2.  24) Kau3 hian7-chai7, lin2 iau2 m7-bat hong7 Goa2 e5 mia5 kiu5 sim2-mih8. Lin2 tioh8 kiu5, chiu7 e7 tit-tioh8, an2-ne lin2 e5 hi2-lok8 chiu7 sip8-choan5 sip8-bi2.”

Khek-hok8 se3-kai3:
	25) “Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, Goa2 koe3-khi3 long2 si7 eng7 phi3-ju7 ka7 lin2 kong2. Goa2 be7 koh eng7 phi3-ju7 ka7 lin2 kong2 a3, boeh chiong Thian-hu7 e5 tai7-chi3 beng5-beng5 ka7 lin2 kong2, hit e5 si5 teh-boeh kau3 a3.  26) Hit e5 jit8-chi2 na7 kau3, lin2 e7 hong7 Goa2 e5 mia5 ki5-kiu5 I; goa2 chiu7 bo5 boeh tui3 lin2 kong2 Goa2 boeh the3 lin2 kiu5 Thian-hu7.  27) In-ui7 Thian-hu7 pun2-sin chin thiaN3 lin2, si7 in-ui7 lin2 u7 keng3-ai3 Goa2, ji5-chhiaN2 u7 siong-sin3 Goa2 si7 tui3 Thian-hu7 lai5 e5.  28) Goa2 tui3 Thian-hu7 hia lai5, lai5-kau3 se3-kan, taN boeh li7-khui se3-kan khi3 Thian-hu7 hia.”
	29) I e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 kong2: “TaN Li2 beng5-beng5 tui3 goan2 teh kong2, long2 bo5 eng7 phi3-ju7.  30) TaN goan2 chai-iaN2 Li2 tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 chai, ia7 bo5 su-iau3 lang5 mng7 Li2. In-ui7 an2-ne, goan2 siong-sin3 Li2 si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 hia lai5 e5.”  31) Ia5-sou in3 in kong2: “Hian7-chai7 lin2 chiah sin3 si7 bo5?  32) Lin2 khoaN3! Si5 boeh kau3 a3, ki5-sit8 i2-keng kau3 a3, lin2 e7 hou7 lang5 phah-soaN3 khi3, chit8 lang5 kiaN5 chit8 lou7, pang3 Goa2 ka-ti7 chit8 e5. Tan7-si7 su7-sit8-siong7 Goa2 m7-si7 ka-ti7 chit8 e5, in-ui7 u7 Thian-hu7 kap Goa2 tong5-chai7.  33) Goa2 chiong chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 lin2 kong2, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 kap Goa2 kiat-hap8 lai5 tit-tioh8 peng5-an. Ti7 se3-kan lin2 e7 tu2-tioh8 chin-che7 khou2-lan7, tan7-si7 lin2 tioh8 iong2-kam2! Goa2 i2-keng khek-hok8 se3-kai3 a3.”

Te7 17 ChiuN

Ia5-sou ui7 bun5-tou5 ki5-to2:
	1) Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7 liau2-au7, chiu7 giah8-thau5 khoaN3 thiN, kong2: “Hu7-chhin a3, hit e5 si5 i2-keng kau3 a3! Kiu5 Li2 eng5-iau7 Li2 e5 KiaN2, thang hou7 Li2 e5 KiaN2 lai5 eng5-iau7 Li2.  2) In-ui7 Li2 siuN2-su3 I koan5-(**peng7) lai5 thong2-ti7 ban7-bin5, hou7 I thang siuN2-su3 eng2-seng hou7 it-chhe3 Li2 sou2 kau-tai3 I e5 lang5.  3) Che chiu7-si7 eng2-seng, chiu7-si7 bat Li2 si7 Tok8-it e5 chin Sin5, i2-kip8 bat Li2 sou2 chhe-phai3 lai5 e5 Ia5-sou Ki-tok.  4) goa2 i2-keng oan5-seng5 Li2 sou2 kau-tai3 Goa2 cho3 e5 kang-chok, chai7 te7-siong7 lai5 eng5-iau7 Li2 a3.  5) Thian-hu7 a3! Eng7 chhong3-cho7 thiN-te7 i2-cheng5 Goa2 kap Li2 kiong7-iu2 e5 eng5-iau7, hian7-chai7 chai7 Li2 e5 bin7-cheng5 lai5 eng5-iau7 Goa2.”
	6) “Li2 tui3 se3-kai3 keng2-soan2 chhut-lai5 kau-tai3 Goa2 e5 lang5. goa2 i2-keng hou7 in bat Li2 e5 mia5 a7. In si7 Li2 e5. Te7 19 ChiuN

	1) Jian5-au7 Pi2-liap8-to chiu7 kio3 lang5 chhoa7 Ia5-sou lai5, kio3 in eng7 piN ka7 I phah.  2) Peng-su7 chiu7 eng7 chhi3-a2 piN7 chit8 e5 ong5-koan ti3 toa3 I e5 thau5-khak, hou7 I moa chit8 nia2 chi2-sek e5 tng5-phau3.  3) In chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5-kau3 I e5 bin7-cheng5 kong2, “Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 ong5, ban7-soe3!”, chiu7 ka7 I sian3 chhui3-phe2.
	4) Pi2-liap8-to iu7-koh chhut-khi3 goa7-bin7 tui3 cheng3-lang5 kong2: “Lin2 khoaN3! 	8) Pi2-liap8-to thiaN-kiN3 in kong2 an2-ne chiu7 lu2 tioh8-kiaN.  9) I iu7-koh jip8-khi3 Chong2-tok-hu2 mng7 Ia5-sou kong2, “Li2 si7 tui3 tah-ui7 lai5 e5?” Tan7-si7 Ia5-sou long2 bo5 ka7 i in3-tap.  10) Pi2-liap8-to chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou kong2: “Li2 m7-kheng2 ka7 goa2 kong2 si7 bo5? Sou2-i2 liah8 Goa2 kau hou7 li2 hit e5 lang5 e5 choe7 koh-khah tang7.”  12) Pi2-liap8-to thiaN-tioh8 an2-ne, chiu7 siuN7 ai3-boeh pang3 Ia5-sou; tan7-si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 it-tit8 hoah kong2: “Li2 na7 pang3 chit e5 lang5, li2 chiu7 m7-si7 Lo5-ma2 Hong5-te3 e5 peng5-iu2. Hit e5 sou2-chai7 kio3-cho3 “Phou-chioh8-te7” --- Hi-pek-lai5-oe7 kio3-cho3 Ka-pa-tai7.  14) Hit jit8 si7 PoaN5-koe3-cheh e5 cheng5-jit8, si5-kan chha-put-to si7 tiong-tau3. Pi2-liap8-to ka7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kong2, “Lin2 khoaN3, che si7 lin2 e5 ong5.”  15) In chiu7 hoah kong2, “Hou7 I si2, hou7 I si2! Ka7 I teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3!” Pi2-liap8-to ka7 in kong2, “Ai3 goa2 ka7 lin2 e5 ong5 teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3 si7 bo5?” Che3-si-tiuN2 in3 kong2, “Lo5-ma2 Hong5-te3 i2-goa7, goan2 bo5 kok-ong5.”  16) Jian5-au7 Pi2-liap8-to chiu7 chiong Ia5-sou kau hou7 in khi3 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3.

Ia5-sou pi7 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3: (Ma2 27:32-44; Kho2 15:21-32; Lou7 23:26-43)
	In chiu7 thoa Ia5-sou khi3.  17) I poe7 ka-ti7 e5 sip8-ji7-ke3, chhut-khi3 siaN5-goa7, kau3 chit8 e5 mia5 kio3-cho3 ‘thau5-khak-oaN2’ e5 sou2-chai7, Hi-pek-lai5-oe7 kio3-cho3 Kok-kok-tha.  18) In chiu7 toa3 hia ka7 Ia5-sou teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3; kang7-si5 ia7 u7 nng7 e5 lang5 hou7 lang5 teng3, chit8 peng5 chit8 e5, Ia5-sou ti7 tiong-ng.  19) Pi2-liap8-to sia2 chit8 e5 ko3-si7 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 teng2-bin7. Chit e5 ko3-si7 sia2 kong2: “Na2-sat-lek8-lang5 Ia5-sou, Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Ong5.”  20) In-ui7 Ia5-sou hou7 lang5 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 sou2-chai7 li7 siaN5-lai7 bo5 loa7 hng7, iu7-koh ko3-si7 si7 eng7 Hi-pek-lai5-oe7, Lah8-teng-oe7, Hi-lah8-oe7 sia2 e5, sou2-i2 u7 chin-che7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 khoaN3 bat.  21) Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Che3-si-tiuN2 chiu7 ka7 Pi2-liap8-to kong2, “Li2 e5 ko3-si7 m7-thang sia2 ‘Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Ong5,’ tioh8 sia2 ‘Chit e5 lang5 kong2: Goa2 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Ong5.’”  22) Pi2-liap8-to in3 kong2, “Goa2 sia2 loh8-khi3 chiu7 ho2 a3.”
	23) Peng-su7 ka7 Ia5-sou teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3 liau2-au7, chiu7 theh8 I e5 goa7-saN, pun-cho3 si3 hun7, chit8 e5 peng-su7 pun chit8 hun7; iau2 u7 chit8 nia2 lai7-saN. Chit nia2 lai7-saN bo5 kap choa7, si7 teng2-e7 sio-thau3, chit-cho3 chit8 te3 e5.  24) Sou2-i2 in tai7-ke chham-siong5 kong2, “Chit nia2 lan2 mai3 thiah-khui, put-ji5 lai5 thiu-khau khoaN3 sim2-mih8 lang5 thiu-tioh8.” An2-ne si7 teh eng3-giam7 Seng3-keng e5 oe7 kong2: 
“In saN-kap pun Goa2 e5 goa7-saN, ui7 Goa2 e5 lai7-saN lai5 thiu-khau.”
Peng-su7 chiaN-sit8 an2-ne cho3.
	25) Ia5-sou e5 bo2-chhin kap I e5 a-i5(**,) Kek-liu5-pa e5 bou2 Ma2-li7-a, kap Boat8-tai7-liap8-lang5 Ma2-li7-a long2 khia7 ti7 Ia5-sou e5 sip8-ji7-ke3 piN.  26) Ia5-sou khoaN3-kiN3 I e5 bo2-chhin kap I sou2 thiaN3 hit e5 bun5-tou5 khia7 ti7 hia, chiu7 ka7 I e5 bo2-chhin kong2, “Bo2-chhin, chit e5 si7 li2 e5 kiaN2!”  27) Jian5-au7 iu7 tui3 hit e5 bun5-tou5 kong2, “Chit e5 si7 li2 e5 bo2-chhin!” Tui3 hit e5 si5 khi2, hit e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 chhoa7 Ia5-sou e5 bo2-chhin khi3 in chhu3 kap i cho3-hoe2 toa3.

Ia5-sou e5 si2-bong5: (Ma2 27:45-56; Kho2 15:33-41; Lou7 23:44-49)
	28) Jian5-au7, Ia5-sou chai-iaN2 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3 taN long2 i2-keng oan5-seng5 a7, ui7-tioh8 boeh eng3-giam7 Seng3-keng e5 oe7, chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 chhui3-ta.”  29) U7 chit8 e5 pan5 te2 sng-khi3 e5 chiu2 khng3 ti7 hia; in chiu7 eng7 hai2-mi5 chim3 hiah-e5 chiu2, pak8 toa3 chit8 ki chhiuN, tu khi3-kau3 I e5 chhui3.  30) Ia5-sou chit8-e7 suh-tioh8 chit e5 sng-khi3 e5 chiu2, chiu7 kong2, “TaN it-chhe3 long2 oan5-seng5 a7.” Thau5 chiu7 kau loh8-khi3, khui3 chiu7 tng7-khi3.

Ia5-sou hou7 lang5 tuh8 heng-kham2-piN:
	31) Hit jit8 si7 Pai3-gou7, Iu5-thai3-lang5 lai5 chheng2-kiu5 Pi2-liap8-to, tioh8 beng7-leng7 chiong hit saN e5 lang5 e5 kha-kut kong3-tng7 liau2-au7, chiong in e5 si2-the2 poaN-chau2. Iu5-thai3-lang5 bo5 ai3 chai7 An-hioh-jit8 chiong sin-si lau5 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2, in-ui7 hit e5 An-hioh-jit8 si7 toa7 e5 cheh-jit8.  32) Sou2-i2 peng-su7 chiu7 lai5 chiong kap Ia5-sou tang5-che5 hou7 lang5 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 hit nng7 e5 lang5 kha-kut, chit8-e5 chit8 e5 ka7 in kong3-tng7.  33) Tan7-si7 in lai5-kau3 Ia5-sou hia e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou i2-keng si2-khi3 a3, chiu7 bo5 kong3-tng7 I e5 kha-kut.  34) Tan7-si7 u7 chit8 e5 peng-su7, eng7 chhiuN chhak8 Ia5-sou e5 heng-kham2-piN; sui5-si5 u7 hoeh kap chui2 lau5 chhut-lai5.  35) Che si7 chit8 e5 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 lang5 cho3-cheng3 e5. I e5 kian3-cheng3 si7 khak-sit8 e5. I chiong i sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 su7-sit8 kong2 chhut-lai5, thang hou7 lin2 lai5 siong-sin3.  36) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng, si7 boeh eng3-giam7 Seng3-keng e5 oe7, kong2, “I e5 kut bo5 chit8 ki e7 hou7 lang5 phah-tng7 khi3.”  37) Seng3-keng leng7-goa7 u7 chit8 toaN7 kong2, “In e7 giong2-bong7 in sou2 chhak8 e5 lang5.”

An-chong3 Ia5-sou: (Ma2 27:57-61; Kho2 15:42-47; Lou7 23:50-56)
	38) Au7-lai5, u7 chit8 e5 A-li7-ma2-thai3-lang5 Iok-sek, lai5 kiu5 Pi2-liap8-to in2-chun2 i nia2 Ia5-sou e5 ui5-the2. (I in-ui7 kiaN Iu5-thai3-lang5, pi3-bit8 lai5 teh cho3 Ia5-sou e5 bun5-tou5.) Pi2-liap8-to in2-chun2 i, Iok-sek chiu7 lai5 nia2 Ia5-sou e5 ui5-the2 khi3.  39) Te7-it pai2 am3-si5 lai5 kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 Ni5-ko-ti2-bou2 ia7 toa3 chha-put-to chit8 pah kin e5 but8-ioh8 chhiau tim5-hiuN e5 phang-liau7 lai5.  40) In nia2 Ia5-sou e5 ui5-the2, chiau3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 bai5-chong3 e5 koan3-le7, boah phang-liau7 liau2-au7, eng7 iu3-te7-a2-pou3 pau khi2-lai5.  41) Ti7 Ia5-sou hou7 lang5 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 hoe-hng5. Hit lai7-bin7 u7 chit8 e5 sin e5 bong7, iau2 m7-bat chong3-koe3 lang5.  42) In-ui7 hit jit8 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 An-hioh-jit8 e5 cheng5-jit8, iu7-koh bong7 ia7 chin kin7, in chiu7 ka7 Ia5-sou an-chong3 toa3 hia.

Te7 20 ChiuN

Khang-khang e5 bong7: (Ma2 28:1-8; Kho2 16:1-8; Lou7 24:1-12)
	1) Le2-pai3-jit8 chai2-khi2 thau3-cha2 thiN iau2-boe7 kng e5 si5, Boat8-tai7-liap8-lang5 Ma2-li7-a lai5-kau3 bong7-nih8, khoaN3-kiN3 bong7-khau2 e5 chioh8-thau5 i2-keng soa2-khui a.  2) I chiu7 chau2 lai5 chhoe7 Se-bun5 Pi2-tek kap Ia5-sou sou2 thiaN3 e5 iau2 chit8 e5 bun5-tou5, ka7 in kong2: “U7 lang5 tui3 bong7-nih8 chiong Chu2 poaN-chau2 khi3 a3! goan2 m7 chai in ka7 I an-ti3 ti7 tah-ui7!”  3) Pi2-tek kap iau2 hit e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 chhut-hoat khi3 bong7-nih8.  4) In nng7 e5 cho3-hoe2 chau2; tan7-si7 iau2 hit e5 bun5-tou5 chau2 pi2 Pi2-tek khah kin2, tai7-seng kau3 bong7-nih8.  5) I aN3 loh8-khi3 khoaN3 bong7-lai7, khoaN3-kiN3 kan-taN hit te3 iu3-te7-a2-pou3 khng3 ti7 hia; tan7-si7 i bo5 jip8-khi3.  6) Toe3 i au7-bin7 lai5 e5 Se-bun5 Pi2-tek, liam5-piN ia7 kau3. I chiu7 tit8-tit8 jip8-khi3 bong7-lai7. I khoaN3-kiN3 hit te3 iu3-te7-a2-pou3 khng3 ti7 hia,  7) ia7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 pau Ia5-sou e5 thau5-khak e5 kin-a2, bo5 kap iu3-te7-a2-pou3 khng3 cho3-hoe2, chih khi2-lai5 khng3 ti7 leng7-goa7 chit8 sou2-chai7.  8) Hit e5 si5, tai7-seng lai5-kau3 bong7-nih8 hit e5 bun5-tou5 ia7 jip8-khi3 lai7-bin7, chit8-e7 khoaN3-kiN3 an2-ne chiu7 sin3.  9) (Kau3 hit e5 si5, in iau2 bo5 liau2-kai2 Seng3-keng kong2 Ia5-sou it-teng7 tioh8 ai3 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong hok8-hoat8.)  10) Jian5-au7 hit nng7 e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 tng2-khi3 in chhu3.

Ia5-sou hian2-hian7 hou7 Boat8-tai7-liah8-lang5 Ma2-li7-a khoaN3: (Ma2 28:9-10; Kho2 16:9-11)
	11) Ma2-li7-a khia7 ti7 bong7-goa7 teh khau3. I na2 khau3 na2 aN3 loh8-khi3 khoaN3 bong7-lai7 e5 si5,  12) khoaN3-kiN3 nng7 ui7 chheng7 peh8 saN e5 thian-sai3, che7 ti7 an-ti3 Ia5-sou e5 sin-si e5 sou2-chai7, chit8 ui7 che7 ti7 thau5 hit peng5, chit8 ui7 che7 ti7 kha hit peng5.  13) Hit nng7 ui7 thian-sai3 ka7 i kong2, “Hu7-jin5-lang5, li2 an3-choaN2 teh khau3?” I in3 kong2, “In-ui7 u7 lang5 chiong goa2 e5 Chu2 poaN chau2-khi3. Goa2 m7 chai-iaN2 in ka7 I khng3 ti7 tah-ui7!”  14) Chiah-e5 oe7 kong2 soah, Ma2-li7-a chit8-e7 oat8 koe3-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou khia7 ti7 hia, tan7-si7 be7 jin7-tit I chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou.  15) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Hu7-jin5-lang5, li2 an3-choaN2 teh khau3? Li2 teh chhoe7 sim2-mih8 lang5?” Ma2-li7-a siuN7-cho3 I si7 kou3 hoe-hng5-e5, chiu7 in3 I kong2, “Sin-seN, na7-si7 li2 ka7 I poaN chau2-khi3 e5, chhiaN2 li2 ka7 goa2 kong2 li2 ka7 I khng3 ti7 tah-ui7. Goa2 boeh khi3 ka7 I poaN tng2-lai5.”  16) Ia5-sou ka7 i kio3 chit8 siaN “Ma2-li7-a!” Ma2-li7-a chiu7 oat8-tng2-sin, eng7 Hi-pek-lai5-oe7 kio3 I, “Liap8-pho-ni5!” (I3-su3 chiu7-si7 ‘Lau7-su’.)  17) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “M7-thang ka7 Goa2 lam2-le3, in-ui7 Goa2 iau2-boe7 seng-thian khi3-kau3 Thian-hu7 hia. Li2 tioh8 khi3 Goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 hia, ka7 in kong2, ‘Goa2 boeh seng-thian khi3-kau3 Goa2 e5 Thian-hu7 ia7-si7 lin2 e5 Thian-hu7 hia, khi3-kau3 Goa2 e5 Siong7-chu2 ia7-si7 lin2 e5 Siong7-chu2 hia!”  18) Boat8-tai7-liap8-lang5 Ma2-li7-a chiu7 khi3 ka7 bun5-tou5 po3 kong2, “Goa2 i2-keng u7 kiN3-tioh8 Chu2 a3,” ia7 ka7 in po3-ko3 Chu2 sou2 ka7 i kong2 e5 oe7.

Ia5-sou hian2-hian7 hou7 I e5 bun5-tou5 khoaN3: (Ma2 28:16-20; Kho2 16:14-18; Lou7 24:36-49)
	19) Kang7 hit jit8 chiu7-si7 Le2-pai3-jit8 e5 am3-si5, bun5-tou5 teh chu7-chip8 e5 sou2-chai7, in-ui7 kiaN Iu5-thai3-lang5, mng5 long2 koaiN khi2-lai5, Ia5-sou chiu7 lai5 khia7 toa3 in e5 tiong-kan, ka7 in kong2, “Goan7 lin2 peng5-an!”  20) Chiah-e5 oe7 kong2 liau2-au7, chiu7 chiong I siang-chhiu2 kap I e5 heng-kham2-piN po3 in khoaN3. Bun5-tou5 khoaN3-kiN3 Chu2 chiu7 chin hoaN-hi2.  21) Ia5-sou iu7-koh ka7 in kong2: “Goan7 lin2 peng5-an! Chhin-chhiuN7 Thian-hu7 chhe-phai3 Goa2 lai5, Goa2 ia7 boeh chhe-phai3 lin2 khi3.”  22) Chiah-e5 oe7 kong2 soah, chiu7 tui3 in pun5 chit8-e7 khui3 kong2: “Lin2 nia2-siu7 Seng3 Leng5!  23) Lin2 na7 sia3-bian2 lang5 e5 choe7, in e5 choe7 chiu7 tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2; lin2 na7 bo5 sia3-bian2 lang5 e5 choe7, in e5 choe7 chiu7 be7 tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2.”

Ia5-sou kap To-ma2:
	24) Chap8-ji7 e5 bun5-tou5 tiong e5 chit8 e5, To-ma2, kio3-cho3 Te2-thou2-ma2 (i3-su3 chiu7-si7 saing-seN-a2), Ia5-sou lai5 e5 si5, i tu2 bo5 kap in cho3-hoe2 ti7 hia.  25) Sou2-i2 ki5-tha e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Goan2 u7 kiN3-tioh8 Chu2 a3!” Tan7-si7 To-ma2 in3 kong2: “Goa2 na7 bo5 khoaN3-tioh8 I e5 chhiu2 e5 teng-a2 jiah, na7 bo5 chhng goa2 e5 chng2-thau5-a2 jip8-khi3 I e5 teng-a2-khang, bo5 chhng goa2 e5 chhiu2 jip8-khi3 I e5 heng-kham2-a2-piN, goa2 choat8-tui3 m7 sin3.”
	26) Chit8 le2-pai3 au7, bun5-tou5 iu7-koh chu7-chip8 ti7 chhu3-lai7, To-ma2 ia7 kap in cho3-hoe2 ti7 hia. Mng5 long2 i2-keng koaiN-le, tan7-si7 Ia5-sou lai5 khia7 toa3 in e5 tiong-kan, kong2, “Goan7 lin2 peng5-an!”  27) Jian5-au7 ka7 To-ma2 kong2: “Li2 e5 chng2-thau5-a2 chhun lai5 chia, bong Goa2 e5 chhiu2; chhun li2 e5 chhiu2 chhut-lai5, chhng jip8-lai5 Goa2 e5 heng-kham2-a2-piN! M7-thang koh hoai5-gi5, tioh8 ai3 sin3.”  28) To-ma2 in3 I kong2, “Goa2 e5 Chu2, goa2 e5 Siong7-chu2!”  29) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2: “Li2 in-ui7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2 chiah sin3, si7 bo5? Hiah-e5 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 Goa2, siong7-chhiaN2 u7 sin3 e5 lang5, sit8-chai7 u7 hok-khi3.”

Chit pun2 chheh e5 bok8-tek:
	30) Ia5-sou ti7 bun5-tou5 e5 bin7-cheng5, leng7-goa7 u7 cho3 chin-che7 e5 sin5-chek, m7-ku2 bo5 ki3-chai3 ti7 chit pun2 chheh.  31) Ki3-chai3 ti7 chit pun2 chheh chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 siong-sin3 Ia5-sou chiu7-si7 Bi2-sai3-a, chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2; ia7 thang hou7 lin2 thong-koe3 chit e5 sin3-sim, oa2-kho3 I e5 mia5 lai5 tit-tioh8 seN3-mia7.

Te7 21 ChiuN

Ia5-sou hian2-hian7 hou7 chhit e5 bun5-tou5 khoaN3:
	1) Au7-lai5 Ia5-sou koh chit8 pai2 chai7 The5-pi2-li7-a e5 ou5-piN, hian2-hian7 hou7 bun5-tou5 khoaN3. I hian2-hian7 e5 cheng5-heng5 chiu7-si7 an2-ne:  2) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek, kap kio3-cho3 siang-seN-a2 e5 To-ma2, kap Ka-li7-li7 e5 Ka-nal2ang Na2-tan3-giap8, kap Se-pi2-thai3 e5 nng7 e5 kiaN2, i2-kip8 i2-goa7 nng7 e5 bun5-tou5 long2 cho3-hoe2 ti7 hia.  3) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek ka7 in kong2. “Goa2 boeh lai5 khi3 liah8 hi5.” In ka7 i kong2 “Goan2 ia7 boeh kap li2 khi3.” In chiu7 chhut-hoat khi3 che7 chun5, m7-ku2 kui-me5 liah8 long2 bo5 hi5.  4) Kau3 thiN boeh kng, Ia5-sou lai5 khia7 ti7 hoaN7-teng2; tan7-si7 bun5-tou5 be7 jin7-tit I si7 Ia5-sou.  5) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2. “Peng5-iu2 a3, lin2 liah8 u7 hi5 bo5?” In in3 kong2, “Bo5 poaN3 boe2.”  6) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2, “Lin2 toa3 chun5 e5 chiaN3-peng5 pha-bang7, an2-ne chiu7 liah8 u7.” In chiu7 pha loh8-khi3; in-ui7 pha-tioh8 chin-che7 hi5, soah thoa be7 khi2-lai5.  7) Ia5-sou sou2 thiaN3 hit e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7 ka7 Pi2-tek kong2, “I si7 Chu2!” Se-bun5 Pi2-tek chit8-e7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 si7 Chu2, hit e5 si5 i tu2-tu2 thng3-pak-theh, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 moa bin7-teng2-saN, thiau3 loh8-khi3 chui2-nih.  8) In-ui7 in li7-khui liok8-te7 bo5 loa7 hng7, chha-put-to chit8 pah ba7 nia7-nia7, ki5-tha e5 bun5-tou5 che7 toa3 hit chiah chun5, chiong hi5-a2 moa2-moa2 e5 bang7 thoa lai5-kau3 hoaN7-nih8.  9) In peh khi2-lai5 hoaN7-teng2 e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 hia u7 hoe2-thoaN3-hoe2 teh toh8, teng2-bin7 u7 khng3 hi5 kap mi7-pau teh hang.  10) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2, “Lin2 tu2-chiah pha-tioh8 e5 hi5, liah8 kui2 boe2-a2 lai5.”  11) Se-bun5 Pi2-tek chiu7 tng2-khi3 chun5-nih8, thoa bang7 khi2-lai5 hoaN7-teng2; kui bang7 toa7 boe2 hi5 moa2-moa2, long2-chong2 u7 chit8 pah gou7-chap8 saN boe2. Sui-jian5 chiah-nih8 che7, bang7 ia7 bo5 phoa3-khi3.  12) Ia5-sou ka7 in kong2, “Lai5 chiah8 cha2-tng3.” Bun5-tou5 chai-iaN2 I si7 Chu2; bo5 lang5 kaN2 mng7 I kong2, “Li2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?”  13) Ia5-sou kiaN5 oa2-lai5, theh8 mi7-pau hou7 in, ia7 theh8 hi5 hou7 in.
	14) Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 tiong hok8-hoat8 liau2-au7, hian2-hian7 hou7 bun5-tou5 khoaN3, chit pai2 si7 te7 saN pai2.

Ia5-sou kap Pi2-tek:
	15) In chiah8 cha2-tng3 liau2-au7, Ia5-sou ka7 Se-bun5 Pi2-tek kong2, “Iok-han7 e5 kiaN2 Se-bun5, li2 u7 pi2 in chiah-e5 lang5 khah keng3-ai3 Goa2 bo5?” Pi2-tek kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 chai-iaN2 goa2 keng3-ai3 Li2.” Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Li2 tioh8 chhi7 Goa2 hiah-e5 iuN5-a2-kiaN2.”  16) Ia5-sou te7-ji7 pai2 koh mng7 i kong2, “Iok-han7 e5 kiaN2 Se-bun5, li2 u7 keng3-ai3 Goa2 bo5?” Pi2-tek kong2, “Chu2 a3, Li2 chai-iaN2 goa2 keng3-ai3 Li2.” Ia5-sou iu7-koh ka7 i kong2, “Li2 tioh8 chiau3-kou3 Goa2 hiah-e5 iuN5-a2.”  17) Ia5-sou te7-saN pai2 ka7 i mng7 kong2, “Iok-han7 e5 kiaN2 Se-bun5, li2 u7 keng3-ai3 Goa2 bo5?” In-ui7 Ia5-sou te7 saN pai2 mng7 i kong2, “Li2 u7 keng3-ai3 Goa2 bo5?” Pi2-tek chiu7 iu-bun7 khi2-lai5, in3 Ia5-sou kong2, “Chu2, Li2 tak8-hang7 long2 chai-iaN2 Li2 chai-iaN2 goa2 keng3-ai3 Li2.” Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 i kong2: “Li2 tioh8 chhi7 Goa2 hiah-e5 iuN5-a2.  18) Goa2 sit8-sit8-chai7-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2: li2 siau3-lian5 e5 si5, ka-ti7 pak8 khou3-toa3, sui5-pian7 khi3 li2 ai3 khi3 e5 sou2-chai7; tan7-si7 li2 kau3 ni5-lau7 e5 si5, li2 e5 siang-chhiu2 chhun chhut-lai5, pat8-lang5 e7 ka7 li2 pak8 io pak8 an-an, chhoa7 li2 khi3 li2 m7 ai3 khi3 e5 sou2-chai7.”  19) (Ia5-sou kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, si7 teh chi2 Pi2-tek e7 an3-choaN2 si2, lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.) Chiah-e5 oe7 kong2 liau2-au7, Ia5-sou chiu7 ka7 I kong2, “Toe3 Goa2 lai5 khi3.”

Ia5-sou kap ki5-tha e5 bun5-tou5:
	20) Pi2-tek chit8-e7 oat8 koe3-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou sou2 thiaN3 hit e5 bun5-tou5 teh toe3 in, --- chiu7-si7 chai7 boan2-chhan e5 si5, pheng7 toa3 Ia5-sou e5 heng-cheng5 mng7 kong2, “Chu2! Si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 boeh chhut-be7 Li2?” hit e5 bun5-tou5 .  21) Pi2-tek chit8-e7 khoaN3-kiN3 i, chiu7 tui3 Ia5-sou kong2, “Chu2 a3, chit e5 lang5 ne3?”  22) Ia5-sou ka7 i kong2, “Goa2 na7 ai3 i lau5 kau3 Goa2 koh lai5, kap li2 u7 sim2-mih8 koan-he7? Li2 tioh8 toe3 Goa2 lai5-khi3.”  23) Sou2-i2 chit ku3 oe7 chai7 hiaN-ti7 e5 tiong-kan, liu5-thoan5 kong2 chit e5 bun5-tou5 be7 si2. Ki5-sit8 Ia5-sou m7-si7 kong2 i be7 si2, si7 kong2, “Goa2 na7 ai3 i lau5 kau3 Goa2 koh lai5, kap li2 u7 sim2-mih8 koan-he7?”
	24) Chit e5 bun5-tou5 chiu7-si7 ui7-tioh8 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 cho3 kian3-cheng3, chiong chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 ki3-liok8 khi2-lai5 e5. Lan2 chai-iaN2 i e5 kian3-cheng3 si7 chin-sit8 e5.

Kiat-lun7:
	25) Ia5-sou iau2 u7 cho3 ki5-tha chin-che7 tai7-chi3. Siat-su2 na7 boeh chiong hiah-e5 tai7-chi3 chit8-hang7 chit8-hang7 long2 sia2 chhut-lai5, goa2 siuN7, choan5 se3-kai3 ia7 bo5 sou2-chai7 thang iong5-lap8 hiah-e5 ki3-liok8 e5 chheh.
