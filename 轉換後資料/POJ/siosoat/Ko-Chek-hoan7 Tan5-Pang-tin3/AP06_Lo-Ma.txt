Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Lo5-Ma2 Kau3-Hoe7 E5 Phe

Te7 1 ChiuN

Chheng2-an:
	1) Goa2 si7 Po2-lo5, Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 pok8-jin5. Siong7-chu2 tiau3 goa2 cho3 Su3-tou5, soan2-phai3 goa2 lai5 thoan5 tui3 I lai5 e5 Hok-im.  2) Chit e5 Hok-im si7 kou2-cha2 Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 I e5 tai7-gian5-jin5 ti7 Seng3-keng lai7 sou2 eng3-un2 e5.  3) Che si7 koan-he7 I e5 KiaN2, lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 tai7-chi3. Chiau3 I e5 jin5-seng3 lai5 kong2, I si7 chhut-si3 cho3 Tai7-pit8 e5 kiaN2-sun.  4) Chiau3 I e5 leng5-seng3, chiu7-si7 chiau3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 kong2, Siong7-chu2 hou7 I tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, eng7 chit e5 toa7 koan5-leng5 lai5 piau2-si7 I si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2.  5) Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 I siuN2-su3 goa2 cho3 Su3-tou5 e5 tek8-koan5, hou7 goa2 ui7 Ki-tok in2-chhoa7 ban7-bin5 lai5 sin3-chiong5 Hok-im;  6) ki5-tiong ia7 u7 pau-ham5 lin2 tai7-ke, in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 i2-keng u7 tiau3 lin2 lai5 kui-siok8 Ia5-sou Ki-tok.  7) Sou2-i2 goa2 sia2 phe hou7 lin2 tai7-ke toa3 ti7 Lo5-ma2 e5 lang5; lin2 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 thiaN3, i2-keng siu7 keng2-tiau3 lai5 cho3 I e5 chu2-bin5 e5.
	Goan7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.

Kam2-sia7 kap ki5-to2:
	8) Siong7 tai7-seng, goa2 thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok ui7-tioh8 lin2 tai7-ke kam2-sia7 goa2 e5 Siong7-chu2; in-ui7 choan5 se3-kai3 long2 u7 thiaN-tioh8 lin2 e5 sin3-tek.  9) Goa2 choan5-sim choan5-i3 teh su7-hong7 Siong7-chu2, lai5 soan-kang2 koan-he7 I e5 KiaN2 e5 Hok-im. Chit ui7 Siong7-chu2 e7-tang3 cheng3-beng5 goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 si7 su7-sit8 e5.  10) Siong7-chu2 chai-iaN2 goa2 tak8-pai2 ki5-to2 e5 si5, si5-siong5 ui7 lin2 teh ki5-kiu5. Goa2 khun2-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 hou7 goa2 e7-tang3 chiau3 I e5 chi2-i3 u7 chit8 jit8 khi3 hong2-bun7 lin2;  11) goa2 chin ai3 khi3 kiN3 lin2, bok8-tek si7 thang  kap lin2 hun-hiong2 leng5-seng3 e5 in-su3, lai5 hou7 lin2 kian-kiong5.  12) Goa2 kong2 an2-ne, chiu7-si7 lin2 khoaN3-tioh8 goa2 e5 sin3-sim, goa2 khoaN3-tioh8 lin2 e5 sin3-sim, hou7-siong lai5 tit-tioh8 kou2-le7.
	13) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ai3 lin2 chai-iaN2, goa2 kui2-na7-pai2 ke3-ek8 boeh khi3 hong2-bun7 lin2, tan7-si7 si5-siong5 u7 tai7-chi3 ka7 goa2 chou2-tong3. Goa2 boeh khi3 chhoe7 lin2 e5 bok8-tek chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 ti7 ki5-tha e5 kok-ka sou2 cho3 an2-ne, boeh tui3 lin2 e5 tiong-kan tit-tioh8 chit8-koa2 lang5 lai5 kui Chu2.  14) Bo5-lun7 si7 u7 khai-hoa3 e5, a2-si7 bi7 khai-hoa3 e5; si7 u7 kau3-iok8 e5, a2-si7 bo5 kau3-iok8 e5, goa2 tui3 in long2 u7 gi7-bu7.  15) Sou2-i2 goa2 ia7 jiat8-chhiat hi-bong7 ai3 thoan5 Hok-im hou7 lin2 hiah-e5 toa3 ti7 Lo5-ma2 e5 lang5.

Hok-im si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 toa7 koan5-leng5:
	16) Goa2 bo5 ui7-tioh8 sin3 Hok-im lai5 kam2-kak kian3-siau3, in-ui7 Hok-im si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5 boeh chin2-kiu3 it-chhe3 u7 sin3 e5 lang5, tai7-seng si7 boeh chin2-kiu3 Iu5-thai3-lang5, jian5-au7 chiah Hi-lah8-lang5.  17) In-ui7 chit e5 Hok-im si7 boeh khe2-si7 Siong7-chu2 choaN2-iuN7 hou7 lang5 kap I ho5-hap8 e5 to7-li2. Chit e5 ho5-hap8 chi2-u7 thong-koe3 si2-chiong it-koan3 e5 sin3-giong2 chiah e7-tang3 tit-tioh8. Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Thong-koe3 sin3-sim kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 lang5 chiah e7 oah8.”

Jin5-lui7 e5 choe7-ok:
	18) Siong7-chu2 tui3 it-chhe3 put-khian5 put-gi7 e5 lang5, tui3 thiN-teng2 hian2-beng5 I e5 toa7 siu7-khi3, chiah-e5 lang5 eng7 put-gi7 lai5 chou2-chi2 chin-li2 e5 chin3-tian2.  19) Ki3-jian5 Siong7-chu2 u7 tui3 in khe2-si7, sou2-i2 in tui3 Siong7-chu2 e5 jim7-sek eng-kai tioh8 chin chheng-chho2 chiah tioh8.  20) Chu7 Siong7-chu2 chhong3-cho7 u2-tiu7 i2-lai5, I hou7 lang5 khoaN3 be7 tioh8 e5 pun2-seng3, chiu7-si7 I eng2-oan2 e5 tai7-leng5 kap sin5-seng3, lang5 na7 khoaN3-tioh8 I sou2 chhong3-cho7 e5 mih8 chiu7 e7 beng5-pek8. Sou2-i2 in long2 bo5 pian7-kai2 e5 i5-te7;  21) in sui-jian5 bat Siong7-chu2, siong7-chhiaN2 bo5 chun-tiong7 I cho3 Siong7-chu2, ia7 m7-kheng2 ka7 I kam2-sia7. In e5 su-siong2 tian-to3 pian3-cho3 bo5 bok8-tek, in bo5 li2-kai2-lek8 e5 sim chhiong-moa2 ou-am3.  22) In chu7-chheng cho3 chhong-beng5 e5, tian-to3 pian3-cho3 gong7-lang5.  23) In eng7 e7 si2-biat8 e5 lang5, khim5-siu3, thang5-lui7 teng2-teng2 e5 heng5-the2 che3-cho7 ngou2-siong7, lai5 chong5-pai3, bo5 boeh keng3-pai3 put-biat8 e5 Siong7-chu2.
	24) In-ui7 an2-ne Siong7-chu2 chiah chhut-chai7 in chiau3 sim-lai7 e5 su-iok8 khi3 cho3 bo5 chheng-khi3 e5 tai7-chi3, eng7 in e5 jiok8-the2 hou7-siong lai5 cho3 kian3-siau3 e5 tai7-chi3.  25) In eng7 hi-ke2 lai5 tai7-the3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-li2, keng3-pai3 su7-hong7 siu7-cho7-but8, m7 keng3-pai3 su7-hong7 Cho7-but8-chu2; I si7 eng-kai siu7 chan3-bi2 kau3 ban7-se3 ban7-tai7 e5. Amen.
	26) Sou2-i2 Siong7-chu2 chhut-chai7 in chiau3 in e5 iok8-cheng5 khi3 cho3 kian3-siau3 e5 tai7-chi3; in e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 m7 ai3 chu7-jian5 e5 jiok8-the2 koan-he7, ai3 bo5 chu7-jian5 e5 jiok8-the2 koan-he7.  27) Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne, cha-pou-lang5 ia7 pang3-sak hu-hu7-kan cheng3-siong5 e5 jiok8-the2 koan-he7, hou7-siong iok8-hoe2 chhiong-tong7, cha-pou kap cha-pou cho3 kian3-siau3 e5 tai7-chi3, soah lai5 siu7-tioh8 in ka-ti7 pian3-thai3 eng3-tit e5 po3-eng3.
	28) In ki3-jian5 siuN7-kong2 bo5 pit-iau3 jim7-sek Siong7-chu2, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 chhut-chai7 in khi3 cho3, in e5 i3-liam7 chiu7 hu2-pai7 khi3, in e5 heng5-ui5 bo5 cheng3-tong.  29) In chhiong-moa2 kok-chiong2 e5 put-gi7, sia5-ok, tham-sim, ok-tok8; moa2-sim oan3-tou3, bou5-sat, cheng-chip, kan-cha3 kap phaiN2 sim-heng; in chiaN5-cho3 kong2 kha-chiah-au7 oe7 e5,  30) hui2-pong3 e5, oan3-hin7 Siong7-chu2 e5, eng7 po7-lek8 e5, kiau-ngou7 e5, khoa-khau2 e5, ke3-bou5 phaiN2-tai7 e5, ngou2-gek8 pe7-bo2 e5,  31) bo5 liong5-sim e5, bo5 siu2-iok e5, bo5 cheng3-gi7 e5, bo5 lin5-bin2 e5.  32) Chiah-e5 lang5 beng5-beng5 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 e5 kai3-beng7 u7 kui-teng7 kong2 cho3 chiah-e5 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 long2 eng-kai tioh8 si2, in put-tan7 ka-ti7 an2-ne cho3, siong7-chhiaN2 ia7 chan3-tong5 pat8-lang5 an2-ne cho3.

Te7 2 ChiuN

Siong7-chu2 kong-gi7 e5 sim2-phoaN3:
	1) Peng5-iu2 a3, sou2-i2 bo5-lun7 li2 si7 sim2-mih lang5, li2 na7 phoaN3-toan3 lang5 e5 choe7-ko3, li2 chiu7 bo5 pian7-kai2 e5 i5-te7; in-ui7 li2 phoaN3-toan3 pat8-lang5 li2 ka-ti7 ia7 lai5 cho3 kang7-khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3. An2-ne li2 phoaN3-toan3 pat8-lang5 chiu7-si7 teh teng7 ka-ti7 e5 choe7.  2) Tan7-si7 lan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 tui3 cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 e5 sim2-phoaN3 chin kong-to7.  3) Peng5-iu2 a3, lang5 cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, li2 ka7 in phoaN3-toan3, li2 ka-ti7 ia7 cho3 kang7-khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3, li2 siuN7-kong2 an2-ne e7-tang3 siam2-pi7 Siong7-chu2 e5 sim2-phoaN3 si7 bo5?  4) A2-si7 li2 m7 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 tui3 li2 jin5-chu5 e5 bok8-tek si7 boeh in2-chhoa7 li2 hoe2-kai2, soah lai5 khoaN3-khin Siong7-chu2 hong-hu3 e5 jin5-chu5, khoan-iong5 kap iong5-jim2 si7 bo5?  5) Tan7-si7 li2 e5 sim kou3-chip koh bo5 boeh hoan2-hoe2, sou2-i2 li2 e7 ke-thiN Siong7-chu2 chai7 siu7-khi3 e5 jit8; chiu7-si7 I hian2-beng5 kong-gi7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 jit8, tui3 li2 toa7 hoat siu7-khi3.  6) I e7 chiau3 tak8-lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 ka7 in po3-eng3.  7) Heng5-sim cho3 ho2-su7, tui-kiu5 eng5-iau7 kap kong-eng5 i2-kip8 put-biat8 e5 seN3-mia7 e5 lang5, Siong7-chu2 e7 siuN2-su3 in eng2-seng;  8) tan7-si7 tui3 hiah-e5 chu7-su, poe7-gek8 chin-li2, tian-to3 hok8-chiong5 put-gi7 e5 lang5, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 e7 eng7 hun3-khai3 kap siu7-khi3 lai5 ka7 in po3-eng3.  9) It-chhe3 cho3 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 e7 siu7-tioh8 hoan7-lan7 kap khun3-khou2, in-ui7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e7 tai7-seng tu2-tioh8, au7-lai5 chiah Hi-lah8-lang5.  10) Tan7-si7 it-chhe3 cho3 ho2-su7 e5 lang5, Siong7-chu2 e7 chiong eng5-iau7, kong-eng5 kap peng5-an siuN2-su3 in, Iu5-thai3-lang5 e7 tai7-seng tit-tioh8, au7-lai5 chiah Hi-lah8-lang5.  11) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 it-si7 tong5-jin5.
	12) Bo5 siu7 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5, sui-jian5 be7 siu7 chit e5 Lut8-hoat e5 phoaN3-choe7, tan7-si7 in na7 hoan7-choe7 ia7 e7 biat8-bong5; u7 siu7 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5, lang5 na7 hoan7-choe7, chiu7 e7 kin-ki3 chit e5 Lut8-hoat siu7 sim2-phoaN3.  13) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 bo5 jim7-teng7 thiaN Lut8-hoat e5 lang5 cho3 gi7-jin5, chi2-u7 jim7-teng7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 lang5 cho3 gi7-jin5.  14) Bo5 siu7 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 Goa7-pang-lang5, chiau3 pun2-seng3 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 na7 u7 sek-hap8 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat e5 si5, in sui-jian5 bo5 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat, in pun2-sin chiu7-si7 in e5 kui-lut8.  15-16) In e5 heng5-ui5 si7 teh piau2-si7 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7 si7 khek ti7 in e5 sim-lai7. Chiau3 goa2 sou2 thoan5 e5 Hok-im, Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, lai5 sim2-phoaN3 lang5 sim-lai7 e5 pi3-bit8 hit Jit8, in e5 liong5-sim ia7 kap Lut8-hoat cho3-hoe2 lai5 ka7 in cho3-cheng3, iu7-koh in e5 li2-seng3 ia7 e7 ka7 in khian2-chek, ia7 e7 the3 in pian7-hou7.

Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Lut8-hoat:
	17) TaN li2 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 an3-choaN2? Li2 chu7-chheng cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5, oa2-kho3 Lut8-hoat, khoa-iau7 lin2 e5 Siong7-chu2;  18) lin2 siu7-tioh8 Lut8-hoat e5 hun3-iok8, chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, e7-hiau2 hun-piat8 si7-hui;  19) li2 chu7-sin3 pun2-sin si7 ka7 chheN-me5 e5 lang5 chhoa7-lou7-e5, si7 tiam3 ti7 ou-am3-tiong e5 lang5 e5 kng,  20) si7 gong7-lang5 e5 kau3-to7-chia2, se3-han2 gin2-na2 e5 lau7-su. Li2 khak-sin3 li2 e5 Lut8-hoat u7 pau-ham5 it-chhe3 e5 ti3-sek kap chin-li2.  21) Li2 ka3-si7 pat8-lang5, an3-choaN2 bo5 ka3-si7 li2 ka-ti7? Li2 thoan5 to7-li2 kio3 lang5 m7-thang thau-theh8, li2 u7 thau-theh8 bo5?  22) Li2 kong2 m7-thang hoan7 kan-im5, li2 u7 hoan7 kan-im5 bo5? Li2 iam3-ouN3 pai3 ngou2-siong7, li2 u7 khi3 chhiuN2-toat8 bio7-u2 e5 mih8 bo5?  23) Li2 khoa-iau7 u7 Lut8-hoat, li2 u7 ui5-poe7 Lut8-hoat lai5 bu2-jiok8 Siong7-chu2 a2 bo5?  24) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Goa7-pang-lang5 e7 lai5 siat-tok8 Siong7-chu2 e5 mia5, si7 lin2 e5 chek-jim7.”
	25) Li2 na7 u7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat, kat-le2 chiu7 u7 li7-ek, tan7-si7 li2 na7 ui5-hoan7 Lut8-hoat, li2 e5 kat-le2 chiu7 kap bo5 kat-le2 kang7-khoan2.  26) An2-ne bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 na7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, i sui-jian5 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 kam2 be7-tang3 ka7 i sng3-cho3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5?  27) Bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 na7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat, in e7 ka7 lin2 teng7-choe7, in-ui7 lin2 sui-jian5 u7 Lut8-hoat e5 beng5-bun5, iu7-koh u7 siu7 kat-le2, siong7-chhiaN2 lai5 ui5-hoan2 Lut8-hoat.  28) Kiu3-keng3 sim2-mih khoan2 e5 lang5 chiah si7 chin-chiaN3 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5, chin-chiaN3 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5? Peng3(**peng7) m7-si7 chhut-si3 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5, kan-taN jiok8-the2 siong7 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5.  29) Chi2-u7 lai7-sim si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5, chiah si7 chin-sit8 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5, sim-leng5 e5 kat-le2 chiah si7 chin-sit8 e5 kat-le2. Che si7 leng5-seng3-siong7 e5 bun7-te5, m7-si7 bun5-ji7-siong7 e5 bun7-te5: e7 ka7 chit khoan2 lang5 chan3-iong5 e5 si7 Siong7-chu2, peng3(**peng7) m7-si7 lang5.

Te7 3 ChiuN

1) An2-ne cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5, u7 sim2-mih iu-tiam2? U7 siu7 kat-le2 e5, u7 sim2-mih li7-ek?  2) Chai7 kok hong-bin7 u7 chin-che7 ho2-chhu3. Te7-it, Siong7-chu2 u7 chiong I e5 oe7 kau-thok in.  3) Siat-su2 u7-e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 na7 bo5 tiong-sit8, chiu7 e7 an3-choaN2? In bo5 tiong-sit8, kam2 e7 hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 sin3-sit8 sit-hau7 khi3?  4) Choat8-tui3 be7! Sui-jian5 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 long2 e7 kong2 peh8-chhat8, Siong7-chu2 choat8-tui3 si7 chin-sit8 e5; chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Li2 kong2-oe7 e5 si5, e7 hou7 lang5 jim7-teng7 li2 sou2 kong2 e5 si7 tioh8 e5, Li2 siu7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 si5, e7 tit-tioh8 seng3-li7.”
	5) Ka2-su2 lan2 sou2 cho3 e5 put-gi7 lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kong-gi7 koh-khah beng5-hian2, lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? Chiau3 se3-kan-lang5 e5 khoaN3-hoat lai5 kong2, Siong7-chu2 tui3 lan2 hoat toa7 siu7-khi3 kam2 u7 m7-tioh8 si7 bo5?  6) Choat8-tui3 m7-si7! Siong7-chu2 na7 m7-si7 kong-gi7 e5, I na2 e7-tang3 sim2-phoaN3 se3-kan?
	7) Ka2-su2 goa2 e5 hi-gui7 put-sit8 e7 hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-sit8 koh-khah hian2-beng5 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2, an3-choaN2 goa2 iau2 tioh8 koh cho3 chit8 e5 choe7-jin5 siu7 teng7-choe7?  8) Lan2 na2 bo5 ai3 kong2: “Ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 ho2-su7 hian2-beng5, lan2 lai5 cho3 phaiN2 tai7-chi3.” Su7-sit8-siong7 u7 lang5 hui2-pong3 oan-ong2 goan2 u7 an2-ne kong2. In siu7 teng7-choe7 si7 li2-sou2-tong-jian5 e5.

Se3-kan bo5 gi7-jin5:
	9) An2-ne lan2 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5, u7 pi2 lang5 khah iu-siu3 bo5? Long2 bo5! In-ui7 goa2 i2-keng u7 chi2-chek kong2, Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Hi-lah8-lang5 long2 siu7 choe7-ok e5 khong3-che3.  10) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Long2 bo5 gi7-jin5, lian5 chit8 e5 ia7 bo5;  11) Bo5 lang5 liau2-kai2, ia7 bo5 lang5 tham3-kiu5 Siong7-chu2.  12) It-chhe3 e5 lang5 li7-khui cheng3-to7, long2 tui7-loh8 khi3. Bo5 lang5 cho3 ho2-su7, lian5 chit8 e5 ia7 bo5.  13) In e5 na5-au5 chhin-chhiuN7 bong7-khong3-khut. In eng7 chhui3-chih8 khi-phian3 lang5; In e5 chhui3-tun5 u7 choa5 e5 tok8;  14) In e5 chhui3 chhiong-moa2 chiu3-gian5 tok8-gi2.  15) In sat-siong lang5 chin bin2-chiat8;  16) In sou2 kiaN5-koe3 e5 lou7 chiu7 u7 hui2-biat8 kap pi-chham2.  17) In m7-bat ho5-peng5 e5 lou7,  18) In gan2-tiong bo5 keng3-ui3 Siong7-chu2.”
	19) Lan2 chai-iaN2 Lut8-hoat it-chhe3 e5 tiau5-bun5, long2 si7 teh chi2 siu7 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5 lai5 kong2 e5, si7 boah hou7 lang5 bo5 pian7-kai2 e5 i5-te7, hou7 choan5 se3-kai3 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 sim2-phoaN3.  20) Sou2-i2 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, bo5 chit8 e5 lang5 e7-tang3 kho3 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8; in-ui7 Lut8-hoat si7 boeh hou7 lang5 jim7-sek sim2-mih si7 choe7.

Thong-koe3 sin3-giong2 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8:
	21) TaN Siong7-chu2 hou7 lang5 tit-tioh8 kap I ho5-hap8 e5 lou7 i2-keng hian2-beng5 chhut-lai5. Chit tiau5 lou7 kap Lut8-hoat si7 leng7-goa7 chit8 hoe5 su7, ji5-chhiaN2 Lut8-hoat kap tai7-gian5-jin5 u7 cheng3-gian5 chit hang7 tai7-chi3;  22) chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 hou7 lang5 thong-koe3 sin3-giong2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 kap I ho5-hap8. Chit e5 ho5-hap8 Siong7-chu2 hou7 it-chhe3 u7 sin3 e5 lang5 long2 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8, oan5-choan5 long2 bo5 ka7 lang5 chha-piat8.  23) In-ui7 cheng3-lang5 long2 i2-keng hoan7-choe7, soah song3-sit Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7.  24) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou ui7 lan2 lai5 oan5-seng5 e5 kiu3-siok8, bo5 tiau5-kiaN7 siuN2-su3 lang5 in-su3, hou7 lang5 lai5 tit-tioh8 kap I ho5-hap8.  25) Siong7-chu2 eng7 Ia5-sou e5 hoeh cho3 che3-phin2, lai5 siok8-hoe5 it-chhe3 u7 sin3 e5 lang5. Siong7-chu2 an2-ne cho3 si7 boeh piau2-si7 I kiu3-siok8 e5 kong-gi7. In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 khoan-iong5 bo5 tui-kiu5 koe3-khi3 e5 se3-tai7 e5 lang5 sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7-ko3;  26) tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 Siong7-chu2 ui7-tioh8 boeh piau2-si7 I e5 kong-gi7, sou2-i2 hian2-beng5 I pun2-sin si7 kong-gi7 e5, ia7 hou7 sin3 Ia5-sou e5 lang5 lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8.
	27) An2-ne lang5 kam2 iau2 u7 sim2-mih thang khoa-khau2? Oan5-choan5 bo5! Si7 sim2-mih li2-iu5? Si7 kho3 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat si7 bo5? M7-si7, si7 kho3 sin3-sim.  28) In-ui7 lan2 e5 kiat-lun7 chiu7-si7 lang5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8, m7-si7 oa2-kho3 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, chi2-u7 oa2-kho3 sin3-sim chiah e7-tang3 tit-tioh8.  29) Siong7-chu2 kam2 kan-taN si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 Siong7-chu2? I ia7-si7 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 Siong7-chu2, kam2 m7-si7? Tong-jian5 I ia7-si7 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 Siong7-chu2.  30) Ki3-jian5 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-chu2, I e7 hou7 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 Goa7-pang-lang5, long2 si7 kin-ki3 in e5 sin3-sim, lai5 tit-tioh8 kap I ho5-hap8.  31) An2-ne lan2 in-ui7 sin3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 hoe3-ti5 Lut8-hoat a2 m7-si7? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7; Tian-to3 si7 teh chi-chhi5 Lut8-hoat.

Te7 4 ChiuN

Eng7 A-pek-lah8-han2 cho3 le7:
	1) An2-ne koan-he7 lan2 bin5-chok8 e5 chou2-sian A-pek-lah8-han2, lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? I u7 hoat-kian3 sim2-mih?  2) Ka2-su2 A-pek-lah8-han2 na7 in-ui7 i sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 khoaN3-cho3 gi7-jin5, i it-teng7 u7 sim2-mih thang khoa-khau2 chiah tioh8. Tan7-si7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 bin7-cheng5, i bo5 sim2-mih thang khoa-khau2.  3) In-ui7 Seng3-keng kong2 sim2-mih? “A-pek-lah8-han2 sin3 Siong7-chu2, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.”  4) Cho3-kang e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 kang-chiN5, be7-eng7-tit sng3-cho3 si7 in-tian2, si7 eng3-tit e5.  5) Tan7-si7 bo5 cho3-kang e5 lang5, i na7 kan-taN siong-sin3 Hit-ui7 hou7 put-khian5 e5 lang5 chiaN5-cho3 gi7-jin5 e5 Siong7-chu2, i e7 in-ui7 i e5 sin3-sim lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.  6) Chhin-chhiuN7 Tai7-pit8 ia7 u7 kong2, put-koan2 i u7 cho3 sim2-mih tai7-chi3, siong7-chhiaN2 hou7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5 e5 lang5 u7 hok-khi3, I kong2:
7) “Put-hoat e5 heng5-ui5 tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2, Choe7-ok tit-tioh8 boah-siau e5 lang5, sit8-chai7 u7 hok-khi3.  8) Chu2 bo5 boeh ka7 i sng3-cho3 u7 choe7 e5 lang5, sit8-chai7 u7 hok-khi3.”
	9) TaN chit e5 hok-khi3 si7 chi2-u7 boeh hou7 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 a2 m7-si7? M7-si7, ia7 u7 boeh hou7 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5. In-ui7 lan2 u7 kong2, “A-pek-lah8-han2 in-ui7 u7 sin3 Siong7-chu2, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.”  10) Che si7 chai7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5 cheng5-heng5 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5 e5? Hit e5 si5 i i2-keng siu7 kat-le2, a2-si7 iau2-boe7? He si7 i iau2-boe7 siu7 kat-le2 i2-cheng5, m7-si7 siu7 kat-le2 liau2-au7 e5 tai7-chi3.  11) I au7-lai5 siu7 kat-le2. I e5 kat-le2 put-ko3 si7 chit8 chiong2 e5 ki3-ho7, lai5 teh cheng3-sit8 i iau2-boe7 siu7 kat-le2 i2-cheng5 chiu7 in-ui7 u7 sin3 Siong7-chu2, lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5 a7. Sou2-i2 i chiaN5-cho3 sui-jian5 bo5 siu7 kat-le2, tan7-si7 u7 siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 e5 goan5-chou2, lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 in jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.  12) I ia7-si7 cho3 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 e5 goan5-chou2. Che m7-si7 in-ui7 in u7 siu7 kat-le2, si7 in-ui7 in toe3 lan2 e5 goan5-chou2 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 bou5-iuN7, chai7 iau2-boe7 siu7 kat-le2 i2-cheng5 chiu7 sin3 Siong7-chu2.

Thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 nia2-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2:
	13) Siong7-chu2 eng3-un2 A-pek-lah8-han2, boeh hou7 i kap hou7 i e5 hou7-e3 seng5-siu7 se3-kai3. I eng3-un2 i, m7-si7 in-ui7 i u7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat, si7 in-ui7 i u7 siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2, hou7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 i seng5-jin7 cho3 gi7-jin5.  14) Ka2-su2 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 lang5 chiah e7-tang3 cho3 ke3-seng5-jin5, an2-ne sin3-giong2 chiu7 long2 piN3-cho3 bo5 i3-gi7, Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 chiu7 piN3-cho3 bo5 ke3-tat8.  15) Sit8-che3-siong7, Lut8-hoat put-ko3 si7 teh in2-khi2 Siong7-chu2 e5 toa7 siu7-khi3 nia7-nia7; in-ui7 na7 bo5 lut8-hoat chiu7 be7 hoan7-hoat.
	16) In-ui7 an2-ne Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 si7 kin-ki3 sin3-sim, oan5-choan5 si7 tui3 in-tian2 lai5 e5. Chit e5 eng3-un2 si7 boeh siuN2-su3 hou7 A-pek-lah8-han2 it-chhe3 e5 hou7-e3, m7-si7 kan-taN boeh hou7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 lang5, ia7-si7 boeh hou7 chhin-chhiuN7 A-pek-lah8-han2 an2-ne u7 sin3 Siong7-chu2 e5 lang5. In-ui7 A-pek-lah8-han2 si7 lan2 it-chhe3 sin3-tou5 e5 goan5-chou2.  17) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Goa2 i2-keng hou7 li2 cho3 ban7-bin5 e5 goan5-chou2.” Chit e5 eng3-un2 si7 chai7 i sou2 siong-sin3 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 sou2 nia2-siu7 e5. Chit ui7 Siong7-chu2 chiu7 si7 hou7 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, beng7-leng7 bo5 chun5-chai7 e5 piN3-cho3 u7 chun5-chai7 e5.
	18) A-pek-lah8-han2 chai7 it-chhe3 e5 cheng5-heng5 long2 bo5 hi-bong7 e5 si5, siong7-chhiaN2 phau7 hi-bong7 lai5 siong-sin3; chiau3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 kong2, “Boeh hou7 li2 e5 hou7-e3 chiah-nih(**nih8) che7,” i chiau3 an2-ne lai5 chiaN5-cho3 “ban7-bin5 e5 goan5-chou2.”  19) Sui-jian5 i siuN7-tioh8 i e5 seng-khu na2 chhin-chhiuN7 si2-khi3 a3, in-ui7 i i2-keng chiaN5-pah hoe3 a3, iu7-koh siuN7-tioh8 Sat-lah ia7 i2-keng be7 hoai5-thai, tan7-si7 i e5 sin3-sim long2 bo5 tong7-iau5.  20) I bo5 in-ui7 m7-sin3 lai5 giau5-gi5 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2, i e5 sin3-sim tian-to3 kian-kiong5 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.  21) I choat8-tui3 khak-sin3 Siong7-chu2 e7-tang3 seng5-chiu7 I sou2 eng3-un2 e5.  22) In-ui7 i u7 chit khoan2 e5 sin3-sim, Siong7-chu2 chiah ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.
	23) “Ka7 i jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5” chit ku3, m7-si7 kan-taN ui7-tioh8 A-pek-lah8-han2 lai5 ki3-chai3 e5.  24) Chit ku3 ia7-si7 ui7-tioh8 lan2 sia2 e5; lan2 chiah-e5 siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2 hou7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 lang5, ia7 tek-khak e7 hou7 Siong7-chu2 jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5.  25) Ia5-sou ui7-tioh8 lan2 e5 choe7-ko3 hou7 lang5 chhu2 si2-heng5, ia7 ui7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 hou7 I koh-oah8 khi2-lai5.

Te7 5 ChiuN

Kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 hau7-ko2:
	1) TaN lan2 ki3-jian5 kho3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 lang5, lan2 thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chiu7 kap Siong7-chu2 u7 ho5-bok8 e5 koan-he7.  2) Thong-koe3 lan2 e5 sin3-sim, I in2-chhoa7 lan2 chin3-jip8 lai5 hian7-chai7 lan2 sou2 teh hiong2-siu7 e5 in-thiong2 tiong. Lan2 tioh8 hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2 giong2-bong7, lai5 hun-hiong2 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng5-iau7.  3) M7-na7 an2-ne nia7-nia7, lan2 chai7 hoan7-lan7 tiong ia7 tioh8 hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2; in-ui7 lan2 chai-iaN2 hoan7-lan7 e7 san2-seng nai7-sim,  4) nai7-sim e7-tang3 khek-hok8 chhi3-lian7, khek-hok8 chhi3-lian7 e7 san2-seng hi-bong7;  5) chit e5 hi-bong7 be7 hou7 lan2 sit-bong7, in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 e5 jin5-ai3 thong-koe3 I sou2 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, koan3 jip8-lai5 ti7 lan2 e5 sim-lai7.
	6) In-ui7 lan2 iau2-ku2 loan2-jiok8 e5 si5, Ki-tok chiau3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 an-pai5 e5 jit8-chi2 the3 choe7-jin5 si2.  7) Chin han2-tit u7 lang5 the3 gi7-jin5 lai5 si2, bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 u7 lang5 kaN2 the3 ho2-lang5 hi-seng seN3-mia7.  8) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 u7 hian2-beng5 I loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) thiaN3 lan2, in-ui7 lan2 iau2-ku2 si7 choe7-jin5 e5 si5, Ki-tok ui7-tioh8 lan2 lai5 si2.  9) Ki3-jian5 lan2 i2-keng thong-koe3 I e5 si2-bong5 lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8, an2-ne lan2 chiu7 koh-khah e7-eng7-tit khak-sin3 kong2 lan2 e7 thong-koe3 Ki-tok tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3, lai5 thoat-li7 Siong7-chu2 e5 toa7 siu7-khi3!  10) In-ui7 lan2 iau2-ku2 cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 tek8-jin5 e5 si5, I thong-koe3 I e5 KiaN2 e5 si2-bong5, lai5 hou7 lan2 kap I ho5-kai2, na7 an2-ne lan2 koh-khah e7-tang3 khak-sin3 kong2 lan2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 ho5-kai2 e5 lang5, e7 thong-koe3 eng2-seng e5 Ki-tok lai5 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3!  11) M7-na7 an2-ne, lan2 siong7-chhiaN2 thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 khoa-iau7 Siong7-chu2, in-ui7 thong-koe3 I, lan2 taN i2-keng tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-kai2 a3.

A-tong kap Ki-tok:
	12) Choe7-ok thong-koe3 chit8 e5 lang5 chin3-jip8 lai5 se3-kan, chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne, si2-bong5 ia7 thong-koe3 choe7-ok chin3-jip8 lai5 se3-kan; in-ui7 cheng3-lang5 long2 hoan7-choe7, kiat-ko2 choan5 jin5-lui7 long2 tioh8 si2.  13) In-ui7 Lut8-hoat iau2-boe7 pan-pou3 i2-cheng5, se3-kan chiu7 i2-keng u7 choe7-ok, tan7-si7 ki3-jian5 bo5 lut8-hoat, choe7-ok ia7 be7-tang3 sng3-cho3 si7 choe7-ok.  14) Tan7-si7 tui3 A-tong kau3 Mou5-se chit toaN7 ki5-kan, si2-bong5 khong3-che3 choan5 jin5-lui7, lian5 hiah-e5 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 A-tong ui5-hoan2 kai3-beng7 lai5 hoan7-choe7 e5 lang5 ia7 tioh8 si2.
	Chit e5 A-tong chiu7-si7 teh i7-piau2 teh boeh lai5 e5 Ki-tok.  15) Tan7-si7 A-tong e5 choe7-ko3 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 in-su3 be7-pi2-tit. In-ui7 chit8 e5 lang5 hoan7-choe7 hai7 cheng3-lang5 tioh8 si2, tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 kap I thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chit8 e5 lang5, bo5 tiau5-kiaN7 hong-hong-hu3-hu3 siuN2-su3 cheng3-lang5 e5 in-su3 si7 koh-khah toa7 e5!  16) Hit e5 lang5 hoan7 e5 choe7 ia7 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 in-su3 be7-pi2-tit. Ui7-tioh8 hit chit8 pai2 e5 hoan7-choe7, jin5-lui7 chiu7 siu7 soan-phoaN3 “U7-choe7”; tan7-si7 keng-koe3 chin-che7 pai2 e5 hoan7-choe7 liau2-au7, jin5-lui7 tit-tioh8 jin5-chu5, siu7 soan-phoaN3 “Bo5-choe7!”  17) Ui7-tioh8 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 choe7-ko3, si2-bong5 chiu7 in-ui7 hit e5 lang5 lai5 thong2-hat cheng3-lang5, tan7-si7 hiah-e5 siu7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 hong-hu3 e5 in-hui7 i2-kip8 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 in-su3 e5 lang5, koh-khah e7-tang3 thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chit8 e5 lang5, lai5 it-seng chhin-chhiuN7 kun-ong5 koe3-jit8.
	18) Sou2-i2 chhin-chhiuN7 ui7-tioh8 chit8 pai2 e5 choe7-ko3, cheng3-lang5 soah long2 siu7 teng7-choe7, an2-ne ia7 ui7-tioh8 chit8 pai2 kong-gi7 e5 chok-ui5, cheng3-lang5 tit-tioh8 sia3-choe7 kap eng2-seng.  19) Ui7-tioh8 chit8 e5 lang5 poe7-gek8 e5 kiat-ko2, cheng3-lang5 chiaN5-cho3 choe7-jin5. Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne ia7 ui7-tioh8 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 sun7-chiong5 e5 kiat-ko2, cheng3-lang5 chiaN5-cho3 gi7-jin5.
	20) Pan-pou3 Lut8-hoat si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh cheng-ka choe7-ko3, tan7-si7 choe7-ok na7 lu2 cheng7-ka, Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 chiu7 cheng-ka koh-khah che7.  21) Kiat-ko2 chhin-chhiuN7 choe7-ok eng7 si2-bong5 lai5 thong2-hat jin5-lui7 an2-ne, Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 eng7 kong-gi7 lai5 thong2-ti7 jin5-lui7, in2-chhoa7 lang5 thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.

Te7 6 ChiuN

Khi3-choat8 choe7-ok kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8 lai5 oah8:
	1) An2-ne lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? Lan2 kam2 thang ke3-siok8 chai7 choe7-ok tiong seng-oah8, lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 cheng7-ka koh-khah che7?  2) Choat8-tui3 m7-si7! Lan2 ki3-jian5 i2-keng kap choe7-ok toan7-choat8 koan-he7 e5 lang5, na2 e7-eng7-tit koh ke3-siok8 chai7 choe7-ok tiong seng-oah8?  3) Lin2 it-teng7 chai-iaN2 lan2 siu7 se2-le2 lai5 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 siu7 se2-le2 lai5 kap I tang5-che5 si2, tang5-che5 siu7 bai5-chong3, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh chhin-chhiuN7 Ki-tok thong-koe3 Thian-hu7 e5 eng5-iau7 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 an2-ne, hou7 lan2 ia7 thang lai5 koe3 sin e5 seng-oah8.
	5) Ka2-su2 lan2 na7 kap Ki-tok kiat-seng5 it-the2 chhin-chhiuN7 I an2-ne lai5 si2, lan2 ia7 e7 kap Ki-tok kiat-seng5 it-the2 lai5 chhin-chhiuN7 I an2-ne lai5 tit-tioh8 hok8-hoat8.  6) In-ui7 lan2 chai-iaN2 lan2 ku7 e5 chu7-ngou2 i2-keng kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 siu7 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hui2-biat8 siu7 choe7-ok khu-sok e5 chu7-ngou2, lan2 chiah m7-bian2 koh cho3 choe7-ok e5 lou5-le7.  7) In-ui7 lang5 na7 si2, chiu7 thoat-li7 choe7-ok e5 khu-sok.  8) Ka2-su2 lan2 na7 u7 kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 si2, lan2 khak-sin3 lan2 ia7 e7 kap I cho3-hoe2 koh-oah8.  9) Lan2 chai-iaN2 Ki-tok i2-keng tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, choat8-tui3 be7 koh-chai3 si2, si2-bong5 chiu7 long2 be7 koh ka7 i koan2-hat.  10) In-ui7 Ki-tok si2 lai5 kap choe7-ok toan7-choat8 koan-he7, chi2-u7 si2 chit8 pai2, bo5 koh te7-ji7 pai2; taN Ki-tok teh oah8 si7 ui7 Siong7-chu2 teh oah8.  11) Kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, lin2 tioh8 siuN7-kong2 lin2 si7 kap choe7-ok toan7-choat8 koan-he7 lai5 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, ui7 Siong7-chu2 lai5 teh oah8 e5 lang5.
	12) Sou2-i2 lin2 m7-thang koh hou7 choe7-ok lai5 koan2-hat lin2 e5 biat8-bong5 e5 jiok8-the2, hou7 lin2 lai5 sun7-chiong5 jiok8-the2 e5 su-iok8.  13) Lin2 ia7 m7-thang koh chiong lin2 e5 chi-the2 kau hou7 choe7-ok cho3 put-gi7 e5 khi3-ku7, tian-to3 tioh8 cho3 chit8 e5 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 lang5, hian3 lin2 pun2-sin hou7 Siong7-chu2, ia7 tioh8 hian3 lin2 seng-khu e5 kok pou7-hun7 hou7 Siong7-chu2 cho3 kong-gi7 e5 khi3-ku7.  14) In-ui7 choe7-ok be7 koh koan2-hat lin2; lin2 hian7-chai7 m7-si7 chai7 lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat ha7, si7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 tiong teh seng-oah8.

Kong-gi7 e5 lou5-pok8:
	15) TaN tioh8 an3-choaN2? Ki3-jian5 lan2 m7-si7 chai7 lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat ha7, si7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 tiong teh seng-oah8, lan2 chiu7 e7-eng7-tit sui5-pian7 hoan7-choe7 si7 bo5? Choat8-tui3 be7-eng7-tit!  16) Lin2 kam2 m7-chai, lin2 na7 hian3 lin2 ka-ti7 cho3 lou5-pok8 lai5 sun7-hok8 hit e5 lang5, lin2 chiu7-si7 lin2 sou2 teh sun7-hok8 hit e5 lang5 e5 lou5-pok8. Lin2 na7-si7 cho3 choe7-ok e5 lou5-pok8, kiat-ko2 chiu7-si7 si2-bong5. Na7 sun7-chiong5 Siong7-chu2, kiat-ko2 chiu7-si7 tit-tioh8 kap I ho5-hap8.  17) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, lin2 koe3-khi3 cho3 choe7-ok e5 lou5-pok8, taN u7 choan5-sim teh sun7-chiong5 sou2 thoan5-siu7 hou7 lin2 e5 kau3-hun3 e5 chin-li2.  18) Lin2 tui3 choe7-ok tiong pi7 sek-hong3 chhut-lai5, i2-keng chiaN5-cho3 kong-gi7 e5 lou5-pok8 a7.  19) In-ui7 lin2 jin5-seng3 e5 loan2-jiok8, goa2 boeh eng7 se3-kan-lang5 e5 kong2-hoat lai5 kong2. Tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 lin2 i2-cheng5 u7 kau lin2 pun2-sin hou7 u3-oe3 kap put-hoat cho3 lou5-pok8 lai5 cho3 put-hoat e5 tai7-chi3, taN lin2 ia7 tioh8 an2-ne hian3 lin2 pun2-sin cho3 kong-gi7 e5 lou5-pok8 lai5 cho3 seng3-kiat e5 tai7-chi3.
	20) In-ui7 lin2 i2-keng cho3 choe7-ok e5 lou5-pok8 e5 si5, lin2 bo5 siu7 kong-gi7 e5 sok-pak8.  21) TaN lin2 chit-chun7 siuN7-cho3 kian3-siau3 e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 i2-keng cho3 liau2 u7 tit-tioh8 sim2-mih li7-ek? Cho3 hiah-e5 tai7-chi3 e5 chiong-kiok8 si7 si2-bong5.  22) Tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 lin2 i2-keng tui3 choe7-ok pi7 sek-hong3 lai5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 lou5-pok8, lin2 sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 kiat-ko2, si7 hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 seng3-kiat e5, choe3-au7 e7 tit-tioh8 eng2-seng.  23) In-ui7 choe7-ok e5 po3-eng3 si7 si2-bong5, tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-su3 si7 kap lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok kiat-hap8 sou2 tit e5 eng2-seng.

Te7 7 ChiuN

Eng7 hun-in cho3-le7:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 si7 tui3 bat lut8-hoat e5 lang5 teh kong2. Lin2 it-teng7 chai-iaN2 lang5 chi2-u7 teh oah8 e5 tiong-kan chiah tioh8 siu7 lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat.  2) Ki2-le7 lai5 kong2, i2-keng kiat-hun e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, tiong7-hu iau2 teh oah8 e5 tiong-kan, tioh8 siu7 hoat-lut8 e5 sok-pak8 lai5 siok8 i e5 tiong7-hu; tan7-si7 tiong7-hu na7 si2, i chiu7 m7-bian2 koh siu7 chit tiau5 hun-in-hoat e5 sok-pak8.  3) Sou2-i2 tiong7-hu iau2 teh oah8 e5 si5, hu7-jin5-lang5 na7 kap lang5 su-thong, i chiu7 e7 hou7 lang5 kio3-cho3 im5-hu7; tan7-si7 tiong7-hu na7 si2, hoat-lut8-siong7 i si7 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5, sui-jian5 kap pat8 e5 cha-pou-lang5 kiat-hun, ia7 m7-si7 hoan7 kan-im5.  4) Goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 a3, kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, lin2 i2-keng kap Lut8-hoat toan7-choat8 koan-he7 a7, in-ui7 lin2 i2-keng chiaN5-cho3 Ki-tok e5 seng-khu e5 chit8 pou7-hun7 a7, taN lin2 si7 siok8 pat8 e5, chiu7-si7 siok8 Hit-ui7 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Ki-tok, thang ui7 Siong7-chu2 cho3 ho2-su7.  5) In-ui7 lan2 chiau3 lang5 e5 pun2-seng3 teh seng-oah8 e5 si5, Lut8-hoat sou2 in2-khi2 e5 choe7-ok e5 cheng5-iok8, toa3 lan2 e5 seng-khu e5 kok pou7-hun7 teh chok-long7, lai5 san2-seng si2-bong5.  6) Tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 lan2 i2-keng bo5 siu7 Lut8-hoat e5 khu-sok, i2-keng kap hit e5 sok-pak8 lan2 e5 Lut8-hoat toan7-choat8 koan-he7 a7. Sou2-i2 lan2 teh su7-hong7 Siong7-chu2 si7 eng7 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5 sin hong-sek, bo5 koh-chai3 eng7 lut8-hoat tiau5-bun5 e5 ku7 hong-sek.

Lut8-hoat kap choe7:
	7) An2-ne lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? Lut8-hoat pun2-sin chiu7-si7 choe7 a2 m7-si7? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7! Tan7-si7 chi2-u7 thong-koe3 Lut8-hoat, goa2 chiah chai-iaN2 choe7-ok si7 sim2-mih. Ka2-su2 Lut8-hoat na7 bo5 kong2, “M7-thang tham-sim,” goa2 chiu7 m7 chai-iaN2 tham-sim si7 sim2-mih.  8) Tan7-si7 choe7-ok seng5 ki-hoe7 thong-koe3 kai3-beng7, ti7 goa2 e5 lai7-bin7 chok-long7, in2-khi2 kok chiong2 e5 tham-sim. In-ui7 na7 bo5 lut8-hoat, choe7-ok chiu7 bo5 sng3-cho3 choe7.  9) Goa2 i2-cheng5 bat bo5 siu7 lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat teh seng-oah8, tan7-si7 it-tan3 siu7 kai3-beng7 e5 koan2-hat, choe7 chiu7 oah8 khi2-lai5, goa2 soah si2-khi3.  10) Kai3-beng7 pun2-lai5 si7 boeh hou7 lang5 oah8 e5, tan7-si7 chiau3 goa2 e5 the2-giam7, tian-to3 hou7 goa2 si2-khi3;  11) in-ui7 choe7-ok seng5 ki-hoe7 li7-iong7 kai3-beng7 lai5 khi-phian3 goa2, ia7 li7-iong7 kai3-beng7 lai5 hai7-si2 goa2.
	12) An2-ne Lut8-hoat pun2-sin si7 sin5-seng3 e5, kai3-beng7 ia7-si7 sin5-seng3 e5, si7 kong-cheng3 e5, si7 ho2 e5.  13) An2-ne lai5 kong2, hit e5 ho2 e5 soah piN3-cho3 hai7-si2 goa2 e5 si7 bo5? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7 an2-ne! Hai7-si2 goa2 e5 si7 choe7-ok, in-ui7 choe7-ok iong7 hit e5 ho2 e5 lai5 hai7-si2 goa2, lai5 piau2-beng5 choe7-ok e5 pun2-seng3. An2-ne choe7 li7-iong7 kai3-beng7 piN3-cho3 kek8-tou7 e5 ok-tok8.

Lang5 e5 nng7 chiong2 pun2-seng3:
	14) Lan2 chai-iaN2 Lut8-hoat si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5; tan7-si7 goa2 put-ko3 si7 chit8 e5 e7 si2 e5 lang5, pi7 chhut-be7 hou7 choe7-ok cho3 lou5-le7 e5.  15) In-ui7 goa2 sit8-chai7 be7 liau2-kai2 goa2 sou2 cho3 e5; in-ui7 goa2 bo5 khi3 cho3 goa2 sou2 ai3 cho3 e5, tian-to3 khi3 cho3 goa2 sou2 iam3-ouN3 e5.  16) Goa2 na7 khi3 cho3 goa2 sou2 m7 ai3 cho3 e5, che si7 teh piau2-beng5 goa2 u7 tong5-i3 Lut8-hoat si7 ho2 e5.  17) Sou2-i2 teh cho3 goa2 bo5 goan7-i3 cho3 e5 sit8-chai7 m7-si7 goa2, si7 tiam3 ti7 goa2 lai7-bin7 e5 choe7-ok.  18) In-ui7 goa2 chai-iaN2 liong5-sian7 bo5 tiam3 ti7 goa2 e5 lai7-bin7, chiu7-si7 bo5 tiam3 ti7 goa2 e5 pun2-seng3 e5 lai7-bin7. Sui-jian5 goa2 lai7-sim goan7-i3 boeh cho3 ho2-su7, iau2-ku2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang cho3.  19) Goa2 bo5 cho3 goa2 sou2 ai3 cho3 e5 ho2-su7, tian-to3 khi3 cho3 goa2 sou2 bo5 ai3 cho3 e5 phaiN2 tai7-chi3.  20) Ka2-su2 goa2 sou2 bo5 ai3 cho3 e5 goa2 khi3 cho3, hit e5 teh cho3 e5, taN sit8-chai7 m7-si7 goa2 pun2-sin, si7 tiam3 ti7 goa2 lai7-bin7 e5 choe7-ok.
	21) An2-ne goa2 hoat-hian7 tioh8 chit8 e5 goan5-chek: chiu7-si7 goa2 ai3-boeh cho3 ho2 e5 si5, phian-phian-a2 khi3 cho3 phaiN2.  22) In-ui7 goa2 lai7-sim e5 chu7-ngou2 i3-ai3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Lut8-hoat,  23) tan7-si7 goa2 chai-iaN2 goa2 e5 jiok8-the2 lai7 u7 leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 goan5-chek teh kap goa2 e5 li2-ti3 sou2 chan3-tong5 e5 hoat-chek tou3-cheng. Leng7-goa7 hit e5 goan5-chek, liah8 goa2 khi3 cho3 choe7-ok e5 hoat-chek e5 siu5-hoan7, hit e5 choe7-ok e5 hoat-chek tiam3 ti7 goa2 e5 jiok8-the2 lai7 teh chok-long7.  24) Goa2 cho3-lang5 na2 e7 chiah-nih8 chheN(**chhi)-chham2! Sim2-mih lang5 e7-tang3 kiu3 goa2 thoat-li7 chu3-tiaN7 e7 si2 chit e5 jiok8-the2?  25) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, I thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chin2-kiu3 goa2. An2-ne chong2-kong2 chit8 ku3: chit8 hong-bin7 goa2 eng7 li2-ti3 lai5 chun-heng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 Lut8-hoat, leng7-goa7 chit8 hong-bin7, goa2 eng7 jiok8-the2 e5 pun2-seng3 lai5 hok8-chiong5 choe7-ok e5 hoat-chek.

Te7 8 ChiuN

Kap Seng3 Leng5 kiat-hap8 e5 seng-oah8:
	1) Na7-si7 an2-ne, hian7-chai7 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 hiah-e5 lang5, chiu7 be7 siu7 teng7-choe7.  2) In-ui7 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, sou2-i2 siuN2-su3 seN3-mia7 e5 Seng3 Leng5 e5 hoat-chek, i2-keng kiu3 goa2 thoat-li7 choe7-ok kap si2-bong5 e5 hoat-chek a3.  3) In-ui7 lang5 e5 pun2-seng3 loan2-jiok8, Lut8-hoat be7-tang3 the3 lan2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7-si7 kiu3 lan2 thoat-li7 choe7-ok kap si2-bong5 e5 hoat-chek chit e5 tai7-chi3, Siong7-chu2 i2-keng ka7 lan2 cho3 a3. Siong7-chu2 u7 chhe-phai3 I ka-ti7 e5 KiaN2 chiaN5-cho3 lang5, chhai2-chhu2 u7 choe7 e5 lang5 e5 jiok8-the2 lai5 siau-ti5 choe7-ok, eng7 chit e5 jiok8-the2 lai5 ka7 choe7-ok teng7-choe7.  4) Siong7-chu2 an2-ne cho3, si7 boeh hou7 lan2 bo5 chiau3 pun2-seng3 teh seng-oah8, sun7-chiong5 Seng3 Leng5 teh seng-oah8 e5 lang5, thang seng5-choan5 Lut8-hoat kong-gi7 e5 iau-kiu5.
	5) In-ui7 chiau3 pun2-seng3 teh seng-oah8 e5 lang5, koan-sim jiok8-iok8 e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 sun7-chiong5 Seng3 Leng5 teh seng-oah8 e5 lang5, koan-sim Seng3 Leng5 e5 tai7-chi3.  6) In-ui7 koan-sim jiok8-iok8 e5 chiong-kiok8 si7 si2-bong5, tan7-si7 koan-sim Seng3 Leng5 e5 chiong-kiok8 si7 eng2-seng kap peng5-an.  7) An2-ne koan-sim jiok8-iok8 e5 lang5 si7 tui3-tek8 Siong7-chu2, in-ui7 i bo5 sun7-hok8 Siong7-chu2 e5 Lut8-hoat, ki5-sit8 ia7 be7-tang3 sun7-hok8.  8) Sun7-chiong5 jiok8-iok8 teh seng-oah8 e5 lang5 be7-tang3 hou7 Siong7-chu2 hoaN-hi2.
	9) Su7-sit8-siong7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 na7 tiam3 ti7 lin2 lai7-sim, lin2 chiu7 be7 sun7-chiong5 jiok8-iok8, e7 sun7-chiong5 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 seng-oah8. Ka2-su2 lang5 na7 bo5 Ki-tok e5 Seng3 Leng5 ti7 i e5 lai7-sim, i chiu7 m7-si7 siok8 Ki-tok e5 lang5.  10) Ka2-su2 Ki-tok na7 tiam3 ti7 lin2 lai7-sim, sui-jian5 lin2 e5 jiok8-the2 in-ui7 hoan7-choe7 lai5 si2-bong5, Seng3 Leng5 iau2-ku2 e7 in-ui7 lin2 u7 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 lai5 siuN2-su3 lin2 eng2-seng.  11) Ka2-su2 hou7 Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 na7 tiam3 ti7 lin2 e5 lai7-sim, an2-ne hou7 Ki-tok tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Siong7-chu2, thong-koe3 I toa3 ti7 lin2 lai7-sim e5 Seng3 Leng5, ia7 e7 siuN2-su3 sin e5 seN3-mia7 hou7 lin2 e7 si2-bong5 e5 jiok8-the2.
	12) An2-ne, chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, lan2 u7 khiam3-che3, chong2-si7 m7-si7 khiam3 jiok8-iok8 e5 che3, sou2-i2 lan2 m7-thang sun7-chiong5 jiok8-the2 e5 iok8-bong7 lai5 seng-oah8.  13) Ka2-su2 lin2 na7 sun7-chiong5 jiok8-the2 e5 iok8-bong7 lai5 seng-oah8, lin2 it-teng7 e7 si2, tan7-si7 lin2 na7 oa2-kho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, chiong seng-khu e5 phaiN2 heng5-ui5 oan5-choan5 ti5-biat8, lin2 chiu7 e7 oah8.  14) In-ui7 hou7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 in2-chhoa7 e5 lang5 long2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  15) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 lin2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, bo5 hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 lou5-le7, bo5 in2-chhoa7 lin2 to3-tng2-khi3 koe3 kiaN-hiaN5 e5 seng-oah8. Lin2 sou2 nia2-siu7 e5 Seng3 Leng5 hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5. Lan2 ia7 oa2-kho3 chit e5 Seng3 Leng5, hou-kio3 Siong7-chu2 kong2, “A-pa! Goa2 e5 Hu7-chhin a3!”  16) Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 kap lan2 e5 sim-leng5 cho3-hoe2 cheng3-beng5 lan2 siSi7ong-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  17) Ki3-jian5 lan2 si7 I e5 kiaN2-ji5, lan2 chiu7-si7 I e5 ke3-seng5-jin5. Lan2 ki3-jian5 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 ke3-seng5-jin5, lan2 chiu7-si7 kap Ki-tok peN5-peN5 cho3 kiong7-tong5 e5 ke3-seng5-jin5 in-ui7 lan2 na7 kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 siu7 khou2-lan7, lan2 chiu7 e7 kap I cho3-hoe2 hun-hiong2 eng5-iau7.

Chiong-lai5 e5 eng5-iau7:
	18) Chiau3 goa2 sou2 siuN7, hian7-chai7 lan2 sou2 teh siu7 e5 khou2-lan7, kap teh-boeh tui3 lan2 hian2-beng5 e5 eng5-iau7 long2 be7-pi2-tit.  19) Siu7-cho7-but8 chin jiat8-chhiat teh giong2-bong7 Siong7-chu2 chiong I e5 kiaN2-ji5 hian2-beng5 chhut-lai5.  20) In-ui7 siu7-cho7-but8 be7-tang3 tat8-to3 in e5 bok8-tek m7-si7 chu7-goan7 e5, si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 hou7 in an2-ne e5. Tan7-si7 siu7-cho7-but8 iau2-ku2 phau7 chit e5 hi-bong7:  21) chiu7-si7 u7 chit8 jit8 in ia7 e7 siu7 sek-hong3 thoat-li7 pai7-hoai7 e5 khu-sok, hun-hiong2 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 eng5-iau7 e5 chu7-iu5.  22) In-ui7 lan2 chai-iaN2 it-chhe3 siu7-cho7-but8 kau3 hian7-chai7 iau2-ku2 chhin-chhiuN7 tu2-tioh8 seng-san2 e5 thong3-khou2 cho3-hoe2 teh ai-ai-kio3.  23) M7-na7 siu7-cho7-but8, lian5 lan2 chiah-e5 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 thau5-chui2 e5 koe2-chi2, chiu7-si7 tit-tioh8 Seng3 Leng5 chiah-e5 lang5, ia7 ka-ti7 sim-lai7 teh thou2-khui3 theng3-hau7 Siong7-chu2 ka7 lan2 siu-lap8 cho3 I e5 kiaN2-ji5, lai5 siok8-hoe5 lan2 choan5-sim choan5-leng5.  24) In-ui7 lan2 u7 chit e5 hi-bong7 chiah tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3. Tan7-si7 lan2 na7 khoaN3 tioh8 lan2 sou2 hi-bong7 e5, an2-ne he chiu7 m7-si7 sit8-chai7 e5 hi-bong7. In-ui7 i2-keng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5, kam2 u7 lang5 boeh koh-chai3 hi-bong7?  25) Ka2-su2 lan2 na7 teh khat-bong7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 e5, lan2 chiu7 tioh8 nai7-sim jiat8-chhiat theng3-hau7.
	26) Seng3 Leng5 ia7 an2-ne lai5 teh hu5-chhi5 lan2 e5 loan2-jiok8. In-ui7 lan2 m7 chai-iaN2 eng-kai tioh8 an3-choaN2 ki5-to2, Seng3 Leng5 ka-ti7 eng7 lang5 e5 oe7 sou2 piau2-hian7 be7 chhut-lai5 e5 thou2-khui3 lai5 the3 lan2 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2.  27) KhoaN3-thau3 lang5 e5 sim-su e5 Siong7-chu2 ia7 chai-iaN2 Seng3 Leng5 e5 i3-su3, in-ui7 Seng3 Leng5 chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, the3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 ki5-kiu5.
	28) Lan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 hou7 ban7-su7 hiap8-tiau hap8-chok, lai5 hou7 hiah-e5 keng3-ai3 I e5, chiu7-si7 chiau3 I e5 chi2-i3 siu7 keng2-soan2 e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 li7-ek.  29) In-ui7 hiah-e5 Siong7-chu2 tai7-seng keng2-soan2 e5, I su7-sian koat-teng7 boeh hou7 in hun-hiong2 I e5 KiaN2 e5 heng5-siong7, thang hou7 I e5 KiaN2 cho3 toa7 ka-teng5 tiong e5 toa7-kiaN2.  30) Sou2-i2 I sou2 su7-sian koat-teng7 e5, I ka7 in tiau3 lai5; I sou2 tiau3 lai5 e5, I ka7 in jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5; I sou2 jin7-teng7 cho3 gi7-jin5 e5, I chiong I e5 eng5-iau7 siuN2-su3 in.

Siong7-chu2 e5 jin5-ai3:
	31) TaN kong2 kau3 chia, lan2 boeh koh kong2 sim2-mih? Siong7-chu2 na7 ui7 lan2, sim2-mih lang5 e7-tang3 kap lan2 cho3 tui3-tek8?  32) I bo5 sioh I ka-ti7 e5 KiaN2, tian-to3 ui7-tioh8 lan2 cheng3-lang5 chiong I cho3 hi-seng, na7 an2-ne I kam2 be7 chiong it-chhe3 ban7-but8 ia7 bo5 tiau5-kiaN7 siuN2-su3 lan2?  33) Sim2-mih lang5 e7-tang3 khong3-ko3 Siong7-chu2 e5 soan2-bin5? Soan-gian5 in bo5-choe7 e5, kam2 m7-si7 Siong7-chu2 pun2-sin?  34) Na7 an2-ne sim2-mih lang5 e7-tang3 ka7 in teng7-choe7? Si7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou a2 m7-si7? M7-si7. I i2-keng si2-bong5, siong7-chhiaN2 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, taN che7 ti7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chiaN3-peng5 teh the3 lan2 ki5-kiu5!  35) Sim2-mih e7-tang3 hou7 lan2 kap Ki-tok e5 jin5-ai3 keh-li7? Si7 hoan7-lan7, a2-si7 khun3-khou2, a2-si7 pek-hai7, a2-si7 iau-go7, a2-si7 pin5-kiong5, a2-si7 gui5-hiam2, a2-si7 si2-bong5 a2 m7-si7?  36) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Goan2 ui7-tioh8 li2 si5-si5-khek-khek ti7 seN-si2 e5 gui5-hiam2 tiong. Goan2 hou7 lang5 khoan2-thai7 cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 teh-boeh hou7 lang5 thai5 e5 iuN5.”
	37) Be7, tian-to3 oa2-kho3 thiaN3 lan2 Hit-ui7, chai7 chiah-e5 cheng5-heng5 ha7, lan2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 oan5-choan5 e5 seng3-li7 a7!  38) In-ui7 goa2 khak-sin3 bo5-lun7 si7 si2, si7 oah8, si7 thian-sai3, si7 thian-siong7 e5 chip-cheng3-chia2 a2-si7 koan5-lek8-chia2, si7 hian7-chai7 a2-si7 chiong-lai5,  39) si7 thian-siong7 e5, a2-si7 te7-ha7 e5 sia5-leng5, a2-si7 jim7-ho5 chit8 e5 siu7-cho7-but8, long2 be7-tang3 hou7 lan2 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 jin5-ai3 keh-li7. Chit e5 jin5-ai3 chiu7-si7 thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou lai5 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5.

Te7 9 ChiuN

Siong7-chu2 kap I e5 soan2-bin5:
	1) Goa2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 kong2 chin-sit8 e5 oe7, bo5 kong2 peh8-chhat8; goa2 e5 liong5-sim siu7 Seng3 Leng5 e5 leng2-to7, cheng3-beng5 goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 si7 su7-sit8 e5.  2) Goa2 ui7-tioh8 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7, goa2 e5 tong5-pau loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) toa7 iu-bun7, sim-lai7 u7 loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) toa7 e5 thong3-khou2.  3) Ui7-tioh8 in, goa2 sim7-chi3 kam-goan7 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8, siu7 keh-choat8 lai5 li7-khui Ki-tok.  4) In si7 I2-sek-liat8 e5 bin5-chok8, Siong7-chu2 siu-lap8 in cho3 kiaN2-ji5, hou7 in hun-hiong2 I e5 eng5-iau7. Siong7-chu2 kap in lip8-iok, siuN2-su3 in Lut8-hoat, chi2-si7 in chong5-pai3 e5 le2-gi5; in u7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2.  5) In si7 I2-sek-liat8 cheng3 chok8-tiuN2 e5 hou7-e3; chiau3 hiat-thong2 lai5 kong2, Ki-tok ia7 siok8 ti7 I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8. Goan7 Siong7-chu2 ban7-but8 e5 Chu2-chaiN2 siu7 chan3-bi2 kau3 ban7-se3 ban7-tai7. Amen.
	6) Tan7-si7 an2-ne m7-si7 kong2 Siong7-chu2 e5 oe7 piN3-cho3 bo5 hau7-khi3. In-ui7 chhut-si3 cho3 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 e5, bo5 tek-khak long2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 soan2-bin5.  7) A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 hou7-e3 ia7 bo5 tek-khak long2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5. Seng3-keng kong2, “Tui3 I2-sat seN e5 chiah e7 sng3 cho3 si7 li2 e5 hou7-e3.”  8) Chit ku3 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 kong2, tui3 jiok8-the2 chhut-si3 e5 bo5 it-teng7 long2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5; chi2-u7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 e5 kiaN2-ji5, chiah e7-tang3 sng3-cho3 si7 sit8-chai7 e5 hou7-e3.  9) In-ui7 eng3-un2 e5 oe7 si7 kong2: “Si5 na7 kau3, Goa2 e7 koh-chai3 lai5, Sat-lah e7 seN chit8 e5 hau7-seN.”
	10) M7-na7 an2-ne, in-ui7 Li7-pek-ka seN hit nng7 e5 kiaN2 si7 kang7 lau7-pe7, in lau7-pe7 chiu7-si7 lan2 e5 chou2-sian I2-sat.  11-12) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 ui7-tioh8 boeh oan5-choan5 hu5-hap8 I pun2-sin e5 bok8-tek, lai5 tek8-piat8 keng2-soan2 ki5-tiong chit8 e5 kiaN2, I tui3 Li7-pek-ka kong2, “Toa7-han3 e5, e7 su7-hong7 se3-han3 e5.” In iau2-boe7 chhut-si3, tong-jian5 iau2-boe7 cho3 sim2-mih ho2-phaiN2 su7 i2-cheng5, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 an2-ne kong2; sou2-i2 Siong7-chu2 e5 keng2-soan2 m7-si7 kin-ki3 lang5 e5 heng5-ui5, si7 kin-ki3 I e5 in-tiau3.  13) Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2,
“Goa2 thiaN3 Nga2-kok, oan3-hin7 I2-so3.”
14) An2-ne lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? Siong7-chu2 bo5 kong-to7 a2 m7-si7? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7!  15) In-ui7 I tui3 Mou5-se kong2,
“Goa2 boeh lin5-bin2 Goa2 sou2 ai3 lin5-bin2 e5 lang5, Goa2 boeh the2-thiap Goa2 sou2 ai3 the2-thiap e5 lang5.”
16) Sou2-i2 in-tiau3 m7-si7 chai7 ti7 lang5 e5 i3-chi3 a2-si7 lou2-lek8, si7 chai7 ti7 Siong7-chu2 e5 lin5-bin2.  17) In-ui7 chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3, I u7 tui3 Hoat-lo2 kong2, “Goa2 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 bok8-tek lai5 the5-poat8 li2 cho3 ong5, si7 boeh eng7 li2 lai5 hian2-beng5 Goa2 e5 tai7-leng5, boeh hou7 Goa2 e5 mia5 thoan5 khi3-kau3 choan5 se3-kai3.”  18) Sou2-i2 Siong7-chu2 boeh lin5-bin2 sim2-mih lang5, chiu7 lin5-bin2 i; boeh hou7 sim2-mih lang5 kou3-chip, chiu7 hou7 i kou3-chip.

Siong7-chu2 e5 siu7-khi3 kap lin5-bin2:
	19) Li2 it-teng7 e7 tui3 goa2 kong2, “Ki3-jian5 si7 an2-ne, an3-choaN2 Siong7-chu2 iau2 teh chi2-chek lang5? Beng5-beng5 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 hoan2-khong3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3!”  20) Tan7-si7 peng5-iu2 a3, li2 si7 sim2-mih lang5, li2 kaN2 pian7-pok Siong7-chu2? Hui5-a2 kam2 e7 tui3 sio-hui5-e5 kong2, “Li2 na2 e7 ka7 goa2 cho3 an2-ne?”  21) Sio-hui5 e5 lang5 kam2 bo5 koan5 thang chiau3 i e5 i3-su3 eng7 kang7 hit tui thou5 lai5 cho3 chit8 e5 ko-kui3 e5 hui5-a2 kap chit8 e5 chhou-siok8 e5 hui5-a2?
	22) Siong7-chu2 sou2 cho3 e5 tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne. Sui-jian5 Siong7-chu2 ai3 boeh hian2-beng5 I e5 siu7-khi3, ai3 boeh hou7 lang5 beng5-pek8 I e5 koan5-leng5, iau2-ku2 eng7 ban7-hun e5 nai7-sim lai5 jim2-siu7 hiah-e5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 siu7-khi3 eng-kai siu7 hui2-biat8 e5 lang5.  23) Che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lang5 beng5-pek8 Siong7-chu2 tui3 hiah-e5 tit-tioh8 lin5-bin2 e5 lang5, sou2 siuN2-su3 hong-seng7 e5 eng5-iau7. Chiah-e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 su7-sian chun2-pi7 boeh hou7 in thang nia2-siu7 I e5 eng5-iau7 e5.  24) Hiah-e5 bong5-tiau3 e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 lan2 tai7-ke. Siong7-chu2 m7-na7 tui3 Iu5-thai3 bin5-chok8 e5 tiong-kan, ia7 tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 tiong-kan lai5 keng2-tiau3 lan2.  25) I ti7 Ho5-se-a Su u7 an2-ne kong2:
“M7-si7 Goa2 e5 peh-seN3 e5, Goa2 boeh ka7 in kio3-cho3 ‘Goa2 e5 peh-seN3,’ Bo5 tit-tioh8 thiong2-ai3 e5, Goa2 boeh ka7 in kio3-cho3 ‘Goa2 chhin-ai3 e5.’  26) Khah-cha2 ka7 in kong2, ‘Lin2 m7-si7 Goa2 e5 peh-seN3,’ hit e5 sou2-chai7, Ti7 hit e5 sou2-chai7, Goa2 boeh ka7 in kio3-cho3 eng2-seng Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.”
27) Koan-he7 I2-sek-liat8, I2-sai3-a ia7 soan-pou3 kong2: “Sui-jian5 I2-sek-liat8 e5 kiaN2-sun e5 sou3-bok8 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 hai2-soa hiah-nih(**nih8) che7, chi2-u7 hiah-e5 sou2 chhun sio2 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 chiah e7 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3;  28) in-ui7 Chu2 tui3 te7-siong7 e5 phoaN3-koat si7 sin3-sok iu7-koh si7 koat-teng7 seng3 e5.”  29) Chhin-chhiuN7 I2-sai3-a Su i2-cheng5 sou2 i7-gian5 kong2: “Na7 m7-si7 Ban7-kun e5 Chu2 ka7 lan2 lau5 chit8-koa2 hou7-e3, lan2 chiu7 kho2-leng5 chiaN3-cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 Sou2-to-ma2, kho2-leng5 chhin-chhiuN7 Gou5-mou5-lah8 siu7 hui2-biat8 a7.”

I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8 kap Hok-im:
	30) An2-ne lan2 boeh an3-choaN2 kong2? Bo5 tui-kiu5 boeh kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 Goa7-pang-lang5, tian-to3 thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8.  31) Tan7-si7 I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8 sui-jian5 teh tui-kiu5 hit e5 e7 hou7 in kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 hoat-chek, m7-ku2 chit e5 hoat-chek in chhoe7 bo5 tioh8.  32-33) Si7 sim2-mih goan5-in?  In-ui7 in bo5 eng7 sin3-sim lai5 tui-kiu5, tian-to3 siuN7-kong2 in e7-tang3 kin-ki3 in e5 heng5-ui5 lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8. Chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Li2 khoaN3, Goa2 chiong e7 hou7 lang5 that poah8-to2 e5 toa7-chioh8, e7 hou7 lang5 chhu7-to2 e5 chioh8-thau5 he7 toa3 Sek-an-siaN5; Tan7-si7 sin3-kho3 I e5 lang5 be7 tu2-tioh8 sit-bong7.”
In chiu7-si7 khi3 that-tioh8 hit te3 chioh8-thau5 poah8-to2.

Te7 10 ChiuN

1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 e5 sim sou2 khat-bong7, tui3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 ki5-kiu5 e5, chiu7-si7 I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  2) In chin jiat8-sim keng3-pai3 Siong7-chu2, che goa2 e7-tang3 ka7 in cho3-cheng3. Tan7-si7 in e5 jiat8-seng5 bo5 kin-ki3 sit8-chai7 e5 liau2-kai2.  3) In-ui7 in bo5 jim7-sek Siong7-chu2 hou7 lang5 kap I ho5-hap8 e5 lou7, bo5 sun7-chiong5 Siong7-chu2 hou7 lang5 kap I ho5-hap8 e5 to7-li2, in tian-to3 siuN7-boeh kian3-lip8 ka-ti7 e5 lou7.  4) In-ui7 Ki-tok kiat-sok Lut8-hoat e5 hau7-iong7, lai5 hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 u7 sin3 e5 lang5, tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8.

Chin2-kiu3 si7 ui7-tioh8 choan5 jin5-lui7:
	5) Koan-he7 chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, Mou5-se u7 sia2 kong2: “Chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7 e5 lang5, e7 kho3 Lut8-hoat lai5 oah8.”  6) Tan7-si7 koan-he7 thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, i u7 an2-ne kong2: “Li2 m7-thang toa3 li2 e5 sim-lai7 kong2, ‘Sim2-mih lang5 e7 peh-chiuN7 thiN?’” (Chiu7-si7 khi3 chhoa7 Ki-tok loh8-lai5);  7) “Ia7 m7-thang kong2, ‘Sim2-mih lang5 e7 loh8-khi3 im-hu2?’” (Chiu7-si7 tui3 si2-lang5 e5 tiong-kan chhoa7 Ki-tok khi2-lai5).
8) “Siong7-chu2 e5 to7-li2 ti7 li2 e5 sin-piN, ti7 li2 e5 chhui3 kap ti7 li2 e5 sim-lai7;”
chit ku3 si7 sim2-mih i3-su3? I3-su3 chiu7-si7, goan2 sou2 soan-kang2 e5 sin3-giong2 e5 to7-li2.  9) Li2 na7 eng7 chhui3 soan-gian5 “Ia5-sou si7 Chu2,” sim-lai7 siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2 u7 hou7 Ia5-sou tui3 si2-lang5 e5 tiong-kan koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, li2 chiu7 e7 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3.  10) In-ui7 lang5 ti7 sim-lai7 siong-sin3, chiu7 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8; eng7 chhui3 soan-gian5, chiu7 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3.  11) Seng3-keng ki3-chai3 kong2, “Sin3-kho3 I e5 lang5 long2 be7 tu2-tioh8 sit-bong7.”  12) In-ui7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Hi-lah8-lang5 long2 bo5 chha-piat8. Siong7-chu2 si7 ban7-bin5 e5 Chu2, I hong-hong-hu3-hu3 chiok-hok it-chhe3 hou-kio3 I e5 lang5.  13) In-ui7 Seng3-keng ki3-chai3 kong2, “Na7 hou-kio3 Chu2 e5 mia5 e5 lang5, long2 e7 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3.”
	14) Tan7-si7 in na7 bo5 sin3, in na2 e7 hou-kio3 I? In na7 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 I e5 to7-li2, in na2 e7 sin3 I? Na7 bo5 lang5 soan-iong5 Hok-im, in na2 e7 thiaN-tioh8?  15) Na7 bo5 lang5 hong7-phai3, na2 u7 lang5 soan-iong5 Hok-im? Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Thoan5-pou3 Hok-im e5 lang5 e5 kha-pou7 loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) bi2-biau7!”  16) M7-ku2 in bo5 long2 chiap-siu7 chit e5 Hok-im. I2-sai3-a kong2, “Chu2 a3! Sim2-mih lang5 u7 siong-sin3 goa2 sou2 thoan5 e5 sin3-sek?”  17) An2-ne sin3-giong2 si7 tui3 thiaN sin3-sek lai5 e5; sou2 thiaN e5 sin3-sek si7 thong-koe3 soan-kang2 Ki-tok lai5 e5.
	18) Tan7-si7 goa2 mng7 lin2: “In kam2 chin-chiaN3 long2 m7-bat thiaN-tioh8 Hok-im?” Tong-jian5 bat thiaN-tioh8 --- chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“In e5 siaN-im thoan5 kau3 choan5 se3-kai3, In e5 oe7 thoan5 kau3 thiN-piN7 hai2-kak.”
19) Tan7-si7 goa2 koh mng7 lin2: “I2-sek-liat8-lang5 kam2 bo5 beng5-pek8 Hok-im?” Goa2 tai7-seng lai5 in2-iong7 Mou5-se sou2 kong2 e5. I kong2:
“Goa2 boeh hou7 lin2 tui3 m7-chiaN5 kok-ka e5 jin5-bin5 khi2 chit-tou3, Goa2 boeh hou7 lin2 bu5-ti e5 kok-ka e5 kok-bin5 khi2 siu7-khi3.”
20) Iu7-koh I2-sai3-a koh-khah ho2-taN2 kong2:
“Goa2 hou7 bo5 teh chhoe7 Goa2 e5 lang5 chhoe7-tioh8, Goa2 tui3 bo5 teh tui-kiu5 Goa2 e5 lang5 hian2-hian7.”
21) Tan7-si7 koan-he7 I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8, i kong2:
“Goa2 kui-jit8 chhun-chhut siang-chhiu2 teh theng3-hau7 hiah-e5 bo5 sun7-chiong5 iu7-koh poe7-gek8 e5 peh-seN3.”

Te7 11 ChiuN

Siong7-chu2 lin5-bin2 I2-sek-liat8 bin5-chok8:
	1) An2-ne goa2 mng7 lin2: “Siong7-chu2 kam2 u7 pang3-sak I e5 peh-seN3?” Choat8-tui3 bo5 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3! In-ui7 goa2 pun2-sin ia7-si7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5, si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 hou7-e3, si7 Pian7-nga2-bin2-chok8 e5 chit8 hun7-chu2.  2) Siong7-chu2 peng3(**peng7) bo5 pang3-sak I chu7 khi2-chhou chiu7 keng2-soan2 e5 peh-seN3. Lin2 it-teng7 chai-iaN2 Seng3-keng u7 chit8 toaN7 ki3-su7, koan-he7 I2-li7-a tui3 Siong7-chu2 khong3-ko3 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 kong2:  3) “Chu2 a3, in thai5-si2 Li2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5, hui2-hai7 Li2 e5 che3-toaN5; chi2-u7 chhun goa2 ka-ti7 chit8 e5, in ia7 siuN7-boeh siu-sip8 goa2 e5 seN3-mia7.”  4) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 an3-choaN2 in3-tap i? “Goa2 u7 ui7-tioh8 ka-ti7 lau5 chhit chheng e5 m7-bat kui7-pai3 ke2(**koe3) sin3 Pa-lek8 e5 lang5.”  5) Hian7-chai7 ia7-si7 an2-ne, u7 chhun chit8 sio2 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5, si7 Siong7-chu2 eng7 I e5 in-hui7 keng2-soan2 e5.  6) Siong7-chu2 e5 keng2-soan2 ki3-jian5 si7 tui3 I e5 in-hui7 lai5 e5, chiu7 m7-si7 kin-ki3 lang5 e5 heng5-ui5. Na7 m7-si7 an2-ne, Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 chiu7 i2-keng m7-si7 sit8-chai7 e5 in-hui7.
	7) An2-ne kiat-ko2 ji5-ho5? I2-sek-liat8-lang5 sou2 tui-kiu5 e5, in bo5 tit-tioh8; chi2-u7 siu7 keng2-soan2 e5 sio2 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 u7 tit-tioh8. Ki5-i5 e5 lang5 chiaN5-cho3 kou3-chip m7 thiaN Siong7-chu2 e5 khoan3-ko3.  8) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Siong7-chu2 hou7 in e5 sim ba5-pi3, Hou7 in e5 bak8-chiu bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, Hou7 in e5 hiN7-a2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3, Kau3 kin-na2-jit8 iau2-ku2 si7 an2-ne.”
9) Tai7-pit8 ia7 kong2:
“Goan7 in e5 ian3-sek8 piN3-cho3 oah8-tauh kap lo5-bang7, piN3-cho3 in e5 chou2-gai7 kap po3-eng3,  10) Goan7 in e5 bak8-chiu chheN-me5 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, In e5 kha-chiah-phiaN eng2-oan2 hou7 tang7-taN3 teh kah khiau-ku khi3.”
	11) An2-ne goa2 koh mng7 lin2: I2-sek-liat8-lang5 poah8-to2 si7 eng2-oan2 peh be7 khi2-lai5 si7 bo5? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7 an2-ne! In-ui7 in hoan7-choe7, tian-to3 hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3, lai5 hou7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 khi2 chit-tou3.  12) Ki3-jian5 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 e5 choe7-ko3 to e7 hou7 se3-kai3 tit-tioh8 hu3-chiok, in e5 tui7-loh8 to e7 hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 tit-tioh8 hu3-ju7. An2-ne I2-sek-liat8-lang5 choan5-pou7 na7 tit-tioh8 chin2-kiu3 e5 si5, Siong7-chu2 e5 chiok-hok kam2 be7 koh-khah toa7!

Siong7-chu2 chin2-kiu3 Goa7-pang-lang5:
	13) TaN goa2 si7 tui3 lin2 Goa7-pang-lang5 teh kong2. Ki3-jian5 goa2 siu7 chhe-phai3 lai5 cho3 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 Su3-tou5, goa2 tui3 goa2 soan-kau3 e5 su3-beng7 kam2-kak chin kong-eng5.  14) Goa2 e5 bok8-tek si7 boeh hou7 goa2 e5 tong5-pau chit-tou3, khoaN3 e7-tang3 lai5 ka7 in kiu3 chit8-koa2 lang5 be7.  15) Ki3-jian5 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 siu7 Siong7-chu2 pang3-sak lai5 hou7 se3-kan-lang5 tiong7-sin tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 hoe-hok8 ho5-bok8, na7 an2-ne I2-sek-liat8-lang5 siu7 Siong7-chu2 chiap-lap8 e5 si5, kiu3-keng3 e7 an3-choaN2? Kam2 m7-si7 chhut-si2 jip8 seng!
	16) Ka2-su2 hong7-hian3 hou7 Siong7-chu2 thau5-a2 hit te3 mi7-pau si7 seng3-kiat e5, kui te3 mi7-hun2-chhe3 ia7 chiu7-si7 seng3-kiat e5; ka2-su2 chhiu7-kin si7 seng3-kiat e5, chhiu7-oe ia7 chiu7-si7 seng3-kiat e5.  17) Ka2-su2 chiong lang5 sou2 chai-poe5 e5 kan-na2 chhiu7-oe tok khi2-lai5, iah chiong ia2-seng e5 kan-na2 chhiu7-oe (chiu7-si7 lin2 Goa7-pang-lang5) chiap loh8-khi3, lai5 cho3-hoe2 hun-hiong2 kang7 chit8 chang5 chhiu7-kin e5 iong2-hun7,  18) an2-ne li2 chiu7 m7-thang tui3 hiah-e5 tok khi2-lai5 e5 chhiu7-oe lai5 khoa-khau2. Li2 na7 khoa-khau2, li2 tioh8-ai3 chai-iaN2 m7-si7 li2 teh hu5-iong2 kin, tian-to3 si7 kin teh hu5-iong2 li2.
	19) An2-ne li2 it-teng7 e7 kong2, “Hiah-e5 chhiu7-oe hou7 lang5 tok khi2-lai5, goa2 chiah e7-tang3 hou7 lang5 chiap loh8-khi3.”  20) An2-ne tioh8. In in-ui7 m7 sin3 chiah hou7 lang5 tok khi2-lai5, li2 in-ui7 u7 sin3 chiah khia7 e7 chai7. M7-thang u7 chu7-ko e5 sim-su, tian-to3 tioh8 kiaN-hiaN5.  21) Siong7-chu2 ki3-jian5 bo5 sioh goan5-lai5 e5 chhiu7-oe, I ho5-pit tioh8 sioh li2!  22) An2-ne lan2 chiu7 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) chu5-pi, ia7 loa7(**joa7)-nih(**nih8) giam5-keh; tui3-thai7 hiah-e5 tui7-loh8 e5 lang5 I chin giam5-keh, tui3-thai7 li2, I chin chu5-pi, I chiu7 tui3 li2 chu5-pi. Te7 12 ChiuN

Te7 13 ChiuN

Te7 14 ChiuN

“Chu2 kong2, ‘Goa2 ki3-jian5 si7 eng2-seng e5 Siong7-chu2, cheng3-lang5 e7 lai5 kui7-pai3 Goa2, Cheng3-lang5 e7 soan-gian5 Goa2 si7 Siong7-chu2.’”

	19) Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 chin7-lek8 lai5 chhiok-chin3 ho5-peng5 e5 kang-chok, lai5 cho3 hou7-siong kian3-lip8 tek-heng7 e5 su7.  20) M7-thang ui7-tioh8 chiah8-mih8 lai5 hui2-hoai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kang-chok. Lang5 na7 cho3 ka-ti7 siuN7-cho3 ho2 e5 tai7-chi3, iah be7 siu7 liong5-sim e5 chek-pi7, hit e5 lang5 chiu7 sit8-chai7 u7 hok-khi3.  23) Tan7-si7 lang5 na7 phau7 gi5-sim lai5 chiah8, chiu7 siu7 teng7-choe7; in-ui7 i sou2 cho3 e5 bo5 kin-ki3 sin3-sim. Bo5-lun7 sim2-mih tai7-chi3 na7 m7-si7 kin-ki3 sin3-sim long2 si7 choe7-ok.

Te7 15 ChiuN

Tioh8 hau7-hoat Ia5-sou Ki-tok:
	1) Lan2 chiah-e5 sin3-sim kian-kiong5 e5 lang5, eng-kai ka7 loan2-jiok8 e5 lang5 tau3 taN tang7-taN3, m7-thang kou3 lan2 ka-ti7.  2) Lan2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 khoaN3-kou3 ka-ti7 e5 chhu3-piN, hou7 in tit-tioh8 li7-ek, lai5 kian3-lip8 in e5 tek-heng7.  3) In-ui7 Ki-tok ia7 bo5 kou3 ka-ti7, tian-to3 chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2: “In tui3 li2 sou2 kong2 bu2-jiok8 e5 oe7, long2 kang3 loh8-lai5 Goa2 e5 sin-siong7.”  4) In-ui7 chai7 Seng3-keng sou2 sia2 e5 long2 si7 ui7 boeh ka3-si7 lan2, thang hou7 lan2 tui3 Seng3-keng sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 nai7-sim kap kou3-le7 lai5 phau7 hi-bong7.  5) Goan7 siuN2-su3 jim2-nai7 kap kou2-le7 e5 Siong7-chu2, hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 hau7-hoat Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 bou5-iuN7, hou7-siong it-sim it-i3,  6) hou7 lin2 tang5-sim tang5-siaN eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2, lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 Thian-hu7.

Ban7-bin5 e5 Hok-im:
	7) Sou2-i2 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 Ki-tok iong5-lap8 lin2 an2-ne, lin2 tioh8 hou7-siong iong5-lap8 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.  8) Goa2 kong2, Ki-tok chiaN5-cho3 Iu5-thai3 bin5-chok8 e5 pok8-jin5, lai5 eng3-giam7 Siong7-chu2 tui3 liat8-chou2 e5 eng3-un2, an2-ne lai5 hian2-beng5 Siong7-chu2 si7 sin3-sit8 e5,  9) iu7-koh e7-tang3 hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 lai5 chan3-bi2 Siong7-chu2 e5 lin5-bin2. Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 boeh chai7 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 tiong-kan chan3-bi2 Li2, ChhiuN3-koa chan3-bi2 Li2 e5 mia5.”  10) Iu7-koh kong2: “Goa7-pang-lang5 a3, tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 cho3-hoe2 hoaN-hi2!”  11) Iu7-koh-chai3 kong2: “Goa7-pang-lang5 a3, long2 tioh8 chan3-bi2 Chu2, Ban7-bin5 a3, long2 tioh8 lai5 chan3-bi2 I!”  12) Iu7 koh-chai3 I2-sai3-a ia7 kong2: “Ia5-se e5 hou7-e3 e7 lai5; I e7 khi2-lai5 thong2-ti7 Goa7-pang-lang5; Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 hi-bong7 e7 thok chai7 I e5 sin-siong7.”
	13) Goan7 siuN2-su3 hi-bong7 e5 Siong7-chu2, hou7 lin2 sin3 I e5 lang5, chhiong-moa2 it-chhe3 e5 hi2-lok8 kap ho5-peng5, thang oa2-kho3 Seng3 Leng5 e5 tai7-leng5, lai5 tit-tioh8 hong-hong-hu3-hu3 e5 hi-bong7.

Po2-lo5 e5 soan-kau3 su3-beng7:
	14) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 khak-sin3 lin2 u7 chap8-hun e5 liong5-sian7, chhiong-moa2 it-chhe3 e5 ti3-sek, ia7 e7-tang3 hou7-siong khou2-khng3.  15) Tan7-si7 goa2 chit tiuN phe e5 lai7-bin7, u7 sia2 kui2 tiam2-a2 khah bo5 kheh-khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lai5 the5-chheN2 lin2. Goa2 chiah-nih(**nih8) bo5 kheh-khi3 si7 in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 u7 siuN2-su3 goa2 tek8-koan5,  16) lai5 cho3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 pok8-jin5 ui7 Goa7-pang-lang5 kang-chok. Goa2 cho3 che3-si thoan5 tui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5 Hok-im, thang hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 chiaN5-cho3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 hoaN-hi2, Seng3 Leng5 sou2 seng3-hoa3 e5 che3-phin2.  17) In-ui7 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, goa2 ui7 su7-hong7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 cho3 e5 kang-chok kam2-kak khoa-iau7.  18-19) Pat8 hang7 tai7-chi3 goa2 m7-kaN2 kong2, goa2 chi2-u7 toa7-taN2 boeh lai5 kong2 Ki-tok thong-koe3 goa2 e5 gian5-su5 kap heng5-ui5, eng7 sin5-chek kap chhiau chu7-jian5 e5 leng5-lek8, eng7 Seng3 Leng5 e5 tai7-leng5 lai5 hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 sun7-chiong5 Siong7-chu2. An2-ne goa2 tui3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 seh8-kau3 I2-li7-li2-kou2, to3-chhu3 chin7 goa2 e5 choan5-lek8 lai5 thoan5 koan-he7 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im.  20) Goa2 chai7-lai5 e5 chi3-bong7 si7 ai3 boeh khi3 iau2 m7-bat thiaN-tioh8 Ki-tok e5 mia5 e5 sou2-chai7 thoan5 Hok-im, an2-ne goa2 e5 kang-chok chiah be7 kap pat8 lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 tiong5-hok8.  21) Chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2, 
“Hiah-e5 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 I e5 siau-sit e5 lang5 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 I, Hiah-e5 m7-bat thiaN-tioh8 I e5 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 e7 liau2-kai2.”

Po2-lo5 ke3-oe7 hong2-bun7 Lo5-ma2:
	22) In-ui7 chit e5 li2-iu5, goa2 put-si5 siu7 chou2-tong3 be7-tang3 khi3 lin2 hia.  23) Ki3-jian5 chai7 chiah-e5 te7-khu goa2 i2-keng oan5-seng5 goa2 e5 kang-chok a3, iu7-koh chin-che7 ni5 lai5, goa2 it-tit8 khat-bong7 boeh khi3 lin2 hia.  24) TaN goa2 boeh khi3 Se-pan-ga5 e5 si5, goa2 ng3-bang7 e7 sun7-lou7 tui3 lin2 hia khi3; u7 chit8 toaN7 si5-kan thang kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 khoai3-lok8 seng-oah8 liau2-au7, chiah siu7 lin2 song3-heng5 lai5-khi3 Se-pan-ga5.  25) Tan7-si7 goa2 chim2-ma2 boeh khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 oan7-chou7 hia e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5.  26) In-ui7 Ma2-ki-tun3 Seng2 kap A-kai-a Seng2 e5 kau3-hoe7, kam-sim lok8-i3 koan-hian3 chit8 phit chiN5 boeh pang-chan7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 tiong e5 san3-chhiah-lang5.  27) In chin-chiaN3 kam-sim lok8-i3 an2-ne cho3, su7-sit8-siong7 in u7 pang-chan7 hiah-e5 san3-chhiah-lang5 e5 gi7-bu7. In-ui7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 ki3-jian5 u7 chiong in leng5-seng3-siong7 e5 hok-khi3 pun hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5, Goa7-pang-lang5 chiu7 eng-kai eng7 in but8-chit-siong7 e5 hok-khi3 lai5 pang-chou7 Iu5-thai3-lang5.  28) Sou2-i2 goa2 na7 chiong chit chan5 tai7-chi3 pan7 chheng-chho2, chiong chit phit koan-khoan2 kau hou7 in liau2-au7, goa2 chiu7 boeh keng-koe3 lin2 hia khi3 Se-pan-ga5.  29) Goa2 ia7 chai-iaN2 goa2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, e7 toa3 Ki-tok hong-seng7 e5 chiok-hok khi3 hou7 lin2.
	30) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 oa2-kho3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok i2-kip8 eng7 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5 jin5-ai3, khun2-kiu5 lin2 kap goa2 cho3-hoe2 chin7-lek8 ui7 goa2 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2,  31) hou7 goa2 thoat-li7 Iu5-thai3 Seng2 hiah-e5 bo5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5, ia7 hou7 goa2 toa3 khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 koan-khoan2, e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 ti7 hia e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8.  32) An2-ne chiau3 Siong-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, goa2 chiu7 thang hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2 khi3 lin2 hia kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 hiong2-siu7 hiu-sek.  33) Goan7 siuN2-su3 peng5-an e5 Siong7-chu2 kap lin2 tai7-ke tong5-chai7. Amen.

Te7 16 ChiuN

Chheng2-an:
	1) Goa2 ka7 lin2 kai3-siau7 lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 chi2-moe7 Hui-pi2. I si7 ti7 Kian-kek-li2 e5 kau3-hoe7 teh hok8-bu7.  2) ChhiaN2 lin2 khoaN3 i si7 peN5-peN5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5, chin7 lin2 cho3 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5 pun2-hun7 lai5 ka7 i chiap-thai7. I na7 u7 khiam3-eng7 lin2 pang-bang5 sim2-mih, chhiaN2 lin2 ka7 i hiap8-chou7; in-ui7 i kau3-taN u7 hu5-chou7 chin-che7 lang5; goa2 pun2-sin ia7-si7 ki5-tiong e5 chit8 e5.
	3) ChhiaN2 lin2 the3 goa2 ka7 teh su7-hong7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 tong5-su7 Pek-ki-lah kap A-ku-lah chheng2-an;  4) in bat ui7-tioh8 goa2 mou7 seN3-mia7 e5 gui5-hiam2. M7-na7 kan-taN goa2 teh kam2-sia7 in, lian5 it-chhe3 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 kau3-hoe7 ia7 teh ka7 in kam2-sia7.  5) Ti7 in ka-teng5 teh chu7-hoe7 e5 kau3-hoe7, chhiaN2 lin2 ia7 the3 goa2 ka7 in chheng2-an.
	ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 goa2 sou2 keng3-ai3 e5 I2-pai3-ni5-thou2 chheng2-an; i si7 A-se-a Seng2 e5 siong7 tai7-seng lai5 sin3 Ki-tok e5 lang5.  6) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 Ma2-li7-a chheng2-an; i ui7-tioh8 lin2 chin lo5-khou2 kang-chok.  7) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 goa2 e5 Iu5-thai3 tong5-pau, ia7 si7 bat kap goa2 cho3-hoe2 che7-kaN e5 An-to-ni5-kou2 i2-kip8 Iu5-ni5-a chheng2-an; in si7 Su3-tou5 tiong chin u7 beng5-bong7 e5, iu7-koh pi2 goa2 khah tai7-seng cho3 Ki-tok8-tou5.
	8) ChhiaN2 lin2 the3 goa2 ka7 goa2 sou2 keng3-ai3 sin3 Chu2 e5 peng5-iu2 Am3-pek-li7 chheng2-an.  9) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 teh su7-hong7 Ki-tok e5 lan2 e5 tong5-su7 Ni2-pa-lou5 kap goa2 sou2 keng3-ai3 e5 Su7-tai7-kou2 chheng2-an.  10) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 sit8-chai7 teh su7-hong7 Ki-tok e5 A-pi2-li7 chheng2-an. ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 A-li2-to-pou3 chit8 ke lang5 chheng2-an.  11) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 goa2 e5 Iu5-thai3 tong5-pau Hi-lo5-thian chheng2-an. ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 Na2-ki5-sou3 chit8 ke sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 chheng2-an.
	12) ChhiaN2 lin2 the3 goa2 ka7 ui7 Chu2 teh lo5-khou2 e5 Thou2-hui5-na2 kap Thou2-hu3-sat chheng2-an. ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 goa2 chhin-ai3 e5 Pi2-sek chheng2-an; i ui7 Chu2 chin lo5-khou2 kang-chok.  13) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 siu7 Chu2 keng2-soan2 e5 Lou2-hu kap i e5 lau7-bo2 ia7 chiu7-si7 goa2 kio3-cho3 lau7-bo2 e5, chheng2-an.  14) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 A-sun3-ki5-thou2, Hut-lek8-kan, Hek-bi2, Pat-lo5-pa, Hek-ma2, I2-kip8 kap in cho3-hoe2 e5 chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7 chheng2-an.  15) ChhiaN2 lin2 ka7 Hui-lo5-lo5-kou2 kap Iu5-li7-a, Ni5-li7-a kap i e5 sio2-moe7 O-lim5-pa, i2-kip8 kap in cho3-hoe2 e5 it-chhe3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 chheng2-an.
	16) Tioh8 eng7 tong5-pau-ai3 e5 ak-chhiu2 hou7-siong chheng2-an. Ki-tok it-chhe3 e5 kau3-hoe7 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.

Choe3-au7 e5 chi2-si7:
	17) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, hiah-e5 ui5-poe7 lin2 sou2 nia2-siu7 e5 ka3-si7, hou7 lang5 hun tong2-phai3 a2-si7 hou7 lang5 hoan7-choe7 hiah-e5 lang5, goa2 khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 keng2-kai3, tioh8 siam2-pi7 in.  18) In-ui7 chit khoan2 lang5 m7-si7 teh su7-hong7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ki-tok, tian-to3 si7 teh su7-hong7 in ka-ti7 e5 pak-tou2; in eng7 hoa-gian5 khiau2-gi2 lai5 iu2-hek8 sun5-chin e5 lang5 e5 sim.  19) In-ui7 lin2 sun7-chiong5 Hok-im chit khoan2 e5 ho2 mia5-siaN i2-keng thoan5-kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7. Sou2-i2 goa2 ui7-tioh8 lin2 hoaN-hi2. Goa2 ai3 lin2 choan5-sim cho3 ho2-su7, m7-thang kap phaiN2-su7 kau-chhap.  20) An2-ne siuN2-su3 peng5-an e5 Siong7-chu2 liam5-piN e7 chiong Sat-tan3 hun2-chhui3 chai7 lin2 kha-e7. Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 in-hui7 kap lin2 tong5-chai7.
	21) Goa2 e5 tong5-su7 The5-mou5-thai3, i2-kip8 goa2 e5 Iu5-thai3 tong5-pau Lou7-kiu5, Ia5-sun, Sou2-se-pa-tek ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.  22) The3 Po2-lo5 sia2 chit tiuN phe e5 goa2 Tek-tiu, cho3 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.  23) Ka-iu5, chiu7-si7 cho3 chu2-lang5 ho2-ho2 khoan2-thai7 goa2 e5, ia7-si7 the5-kiong3 in e5 chhu3 cho3 choan5 kau3-hoe7 chip8-hoe7 e5 sou2-chai7 e5, ia7 boeh ka7 lin2 chheng2-an. Pun2-chhi7 e5 koe3-ke3 I2-lah-tou kap lan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 Koat-thou2 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.  24) Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 in-hui7 kap lin2 tai7-ke tong5-chai7. Amen.

Siong7-chan3:
	25) Goan7 eng5-iau7 kui Siong7-chu2! I e7-tang3 hou7 lin2 sin3-sim kian-kiong5. Che chiu7-si7 goa2 sou2 thoan5 e5 Hok-im, chiu7-si7 soan-iong5 Ia5-sou Ki-tok. Che chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 o3-biau7 e5 khe2-si7, chiu7-si7 ban7-kou2 i2-lai5 un2-chong5 e5,  26) tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 i2-keng chiau3 eng2-seng Siong7-chu2 e5 beng7-leng7, thong-koe3 tai7-gian5-jin5 e5 chheh hian2-beng5 chhut-lai5, thang in2-chhoa7 ban7-bin5 lai5 sin3-chiong5 Hok-im.  27) Goan7 eng5-iau7 thong-koe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok kui i5-it choan5-ti3 e5 Siong7-chu2, kau3 ban7-se3 ban7-tai7. Amen.
