Iok-Han7 Te7-Ji7 TiuN Phe

	1) Goa2 cho3 TiuN2-lo2 e5, sia2 phe hou7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 keng2-soan2, goa2 chin-chiaN3 chhin-ai3 e5 hu-jin5 kap i e5 kiaN2-ji5. Put-tan7 goa2 chit8 e5, bat chin-li2 e5 lang5 to long2 keng3-ai3 lin2,  2) in-ui7 chit e5 chin-li2 chun5-chai7 ti7 goan2 sim-lai7, ia7 e7 eng2-oan2 kap goan2 tong5-chai7.
	3) Goan7 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Thian-hu7 e5 KiaN2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, chai7 chin-li2 kap ai3-sim siong7, siuN2-su3 lan2 in-tian2, lin5-bin2 kap peng5-an.

Chin-li2 kap Ai3-sim:
4) Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 li2 chit8-koa2 kiaN2-ji5, u7 chun-chiau3 lan2 tui3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 siu7 e5 kai3-beng7, chun-heng5 chin-li2 teh kiaN5, goa2 kam2-kak hui-siong5 e5 hoaN-hi2.  5) Chhin-ai3 e5 hu-jin5 a3, taN goa2 khun2-kiu5 li2, tai7-ke tioh8 siong-chhin siong-ai3. Goa2 sia2 an2-ne hou7 li2, m7-si7 sin e5 beng7-leng7. Che si7 lan2 tui3 khi2-chhou chiu7 u7 e5 kai3-beng7.  6) Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 ai3-sim chiu7-si7 lan2 eng-kai chun-siu2 Siong7-chu2 e5 kai3-beng7 lai5 seng-oah8. Lin2 chu7 khi2-chhou chiu7 thiaN-kiN3 e5 kai3-beng7 chiu7-si7 an2-ne: lin2 tai7-ke tioh8 eng7 ai3-sim lai5 seng-oah8.
	7) U7 chin-che7 be5-hek8 lang5 e5, i2-keng chhut-lai5 ti7 se3-kan, in bo5 boeh seng5-jin7 kong2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok si7 chhu2 jiok8-sin lai5 chiaN5-cho3 lang5 e5. Chit khoan2 lang5 chiu7-si7 be5-hek8 lang5 e5, si7 Ki-tok e5 tek8-jin5.  8) Lin2 tioh8 kin2-sin7, chiah be7 song3-sit lin2 kang-chok e5 bok8-tek, an2-ne lin2 chiu7 e7 tit-tioh8 hong-hu3 e5 po3-tap.
	9) Na7 u7 lang5 put-tan7 bo5 chun-siu2 Ki-tok e5 ka3-si7, siong7-chhiaN2 chhiau-chhut hoan7-ui5, ti7 i e5 gan2-tiong chiu7 bo5 Siong7-chu2. Lang5 na7 u7 chun-siu2 Ki-tok e5 ka3-si7, i e5 sim-lai7 chiu7 u7 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Seng3 Chu2.  10) Na7 u7 lang5 khi3 lin2 hia, bo5 thoan5 chit e5 ka3-si7, m7-thang hoan-geng5 i jip8-khi3 lin2 chhu3, ia7 m7-thang ka7 i chheng2-an.  11) In-ui7 na7 ka7 i chheng2-an, i sou2 cho3 e5 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 ia7 u7 li2 e5 hun7.

Choe3-au7 e5 Chheng2-an:
	12) Goa2 iau2 u7 chin-che7 tai7-chi3 boeh ka7 lin2 kong2, tan7-si7 goa2 bo5 ai3 koh long7-hui3 choa2-pit; goa2 hi-bong7 khi3 kiN3 lin2, lai5 kap lin2 bian7-tam5, an2-ne lan2 chiu7 e7 hui-siong5 khoai3-lok8.
	13) Siu7 Siong7-chu2 keng2-soan2 e5 li2 e5 chi2-moe7 e5 kiaN2-ji5, ia7 ka7 li2 chheng2-an.
