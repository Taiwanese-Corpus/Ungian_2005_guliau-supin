Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Ko-Lim5-To Lang5 E5 Phe
(Chian5 su)

Te7 1 ChiuN

Chheng2-an kap kam2-sia7:
	1) Goa2 si7 Po2-lo5, hong7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, siu7 soan2-tiau3 cho3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 Su3-tou5, goa2 kap lan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 Sou2-the5-ni5,  2) sia2-phe hou7 chai7 Ko-lim5-to Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 sia2 hou7 siu7 seng3-hoa3 lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, siu7 soan2-tiau3 lai5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5, ia7 sia2 hou7 ti7 kok sou2-chai7 teh hou-kio3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 mia5 e5 lang5, Ki-tok si7 in e5 Chu2, ia7 si7 lan2 e5 Chu2.
	3) Goan7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.
	4) Goa2 si5-siong5 the3 lin2 teh kam2-sia7 goa2 e5 Siong7-chu2, in-ui7 I thong-koe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou i2-keng u7 siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7.  5) In-ui7 lin2 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8, Siong7-chu2 chai7 kok hong-bin7 hou7 lin2 chin hu3-chiok, it-chhe3 e5 khau2-chai5 kap ti3-sek ia7 pau-ham5 chai7-lai7.  6) Goan2 sou2 kian3-cheng3 koan-he7 Ki-tok e5 sin3-sek i2-keng chai7 lin2 sim-lai7 teng3-kin a,  7) sou2-i2 lin2 jiat8-chhiat teh theng3-hau7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok hian2-hian7 e5 tiong-kan, bo5 khiam3 poaN3 hang7 leng5-seng3 e5 in-su3.  8) I ia7 e7 hou7 lin2 kian-kou3 kau3 boat8-jit8, thang hou7 lin2 chai7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chai3-lim5 e5 jit8-chi2, bo5 chit8 hang7 thang hou7 lang5 chek-pi7.  9) Siong7-chu2 si7 sin3-sit8 e5, I soan2-tiau3 lin2 lai5 kap I e5 KiaN2, lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok kiat-hap8, lai5 hun-hiong2 I e5 seN3-mia7.

Kau3-hoe7 e5 hun-cheng:
	10) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 eng7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 koan5-peng3 khou2-khng3 lin2; lin2 tai7-ke kong2-oe7 tioh8 it-ti3, lin2 tiong-kan m7-thang u7 hun-cheng; tioh8 it-sim it-i3 ui7 kang7 chit8 e5 bok8-phiau lai5 thoan5-kiat.  11) goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7, Kek-lai5-ke e5 lang5 ka7 goa2 kong2 lin2 tiong-kan u7 hun-cheng.  12) Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 hun-cheng chiu7-si7, lin2 chit8 lang5 kong2 chit8 khoan2 oe7. Chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 Po2-lo5”; u7-e5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 A-pho-lo5”; u7-e5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 Pi2-tek”; ia7 u7-e5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 Ki-tok”.  13) Ki-tok kam2 u7 hou7 lang5 thiah-khui khi3? Po2-lo5 kam2 u7 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 the3 lin2 si2? Lin2 kam2 u7 hong7 Po2-lo5 e5 mia5 lai5 siu7 se2-le2?
	14) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, goa2 ti5-liau2 Ki-li7-su-pou3 kap Ka-iu5 i2-goa7, bo5 ka7 lin2 poaN3 lang5 se2-le2.  15) Sou2-i2 lin2 bo5 lang5 e7-eng7-tit kong2 u7 hong7 goa2 e5 mia5 lai5 nia2 se2-le2.  16) (Tioh8, tioh8, goa2 ia7 u7 ka7 Su-the5-hoan2-a chit8 ke lang5 se2-le2, i2-goa7 u7 koh ka7 pat8-lang5 se2-le2 a2 bo5, goa2 be7-ki3-tit a3.)  17) Ki-tok m7-si7 chhe-phai3 goa2 lai5 ka7 lang5 se2-le2. I chhe-phai3 goa2 lai5 thoan5 Hok-im, m7-si7 eng7 ti3-hui7 e5 khau2-chai5 lai5 soan-kang2, chiah be7 hou7 Ki-tok chai7 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 e5 si2-bong5 piN3-cho3 bo5 i3-gi7.

Ki-tok si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5 kap ti3-hui7:
	18) Ki-tok si2 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 e5 to7-li2, hiah-e5 teh kiaN5 boat8-bong5 e5 lou7 e5 lang5, khoaN3 cho3 si7 bo5-i3-bo5-su3 e5, tan7-si7 lan2 chiah-e5 teh kiaN5 tit-kiu3 e5 lou7 e5 lang5, khoaN3-cho3 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5.  19) In-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Goa2 boeh hui2-biat8 u7 ti3-hui7 e5 lang5 e5 ti3-hui7, ia7 boeh chou2-gai7 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5 e5 chhong-beng5.”
20) An2-ne u7 ti3-hui7 e5 lang5 ti7 tah-ui7? Keng-hak8-chia2 ti7 tah-ui7? Chit se3-kan e5 gau pian7-lun7 e5 lang5 ti7 tah8-ui7? Siong7-chu2 kam2 bo5 hou7 chit se3-kan e5 ti3-hui7 piN3-cho3 bo5-i3-bo5-su3 e5?  21) An3-chiau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 ti3-hui7, se3-kan-lang5 ki3-jian5 be7-tang3 eng7 in e5 ti3-hui7 lai5 jim7-sek Siong7-chu2, I chiu7 goan7-i3 eng7 goan2 sou2 soan-iong5 “bo5-i3-bo5-su3” e5 to7-li2 lai5 chin2-kiu3 hiah-e5 sin3 I e5 lang5.  22) Iu5-thai3-lang5 iau-kiu5 hian2 sin5-chek, Hi-lah8-lang5 tui-kiu5 ti3-hui7,  23) tan7-si7 goan2 soan-iong5 Ki-tok pi7 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2; chit e5 to7-li2 tui3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, si7 sin3-giong2 e5 chou2-gai7, tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, si7 bo5-i3-bo5-su3 e5.  24) Tan7-si7 tui3 hiah-e5 Siong7-chu2 sou2 soan2-tiau3 e5 lang5, bo5-lun7 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 a2-si7 Hi-lah8-lang5, Ki-tok chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5, si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 ti3-hui7.  25) Siong7-chu2 e5 gu5-gong7 sit8-chai7 si7 pi2 lang5 e5 ti3-hui7 khah chhong-beng5, Siong7-chu2 e5 loan2-jiok8, sit8-chai7 pi2 lang5 e5 khui3-lat8 koh-khah kiong5-tai7.
	26) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, chhiaN2 lin2 hoe5-siong2 lin2 siu7 soan2-tiau3 e5 si5 e5 cheng5-heng5. Chiau3 se3-kan it-poaN e5 phiau-chun2, lin2 bo5 kui2 e5 si7 chhong-beng5 e5, si7 u7 se3-lek8 e5, si7 siong7-liu5 kai-kip chhut-sin e5.  27) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 keng2-soan2 se3-kan-lang5 khoaN3-cho3 gong7 e5 tai7-chi3, lai5 hou7 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5 kian3-siau3. Siong7-chu2 keng2-soan2 se3-kan-lang5 khoaN3-cho3 loan2-jiok8 e5 tai7-chi3, lai5 hou7 khut-kiong5 e5 lang5 siu7 thi2-jiok8.  28) Siong7-chu2 koh keng2-soan2 se3-kan-lang5 sou2 khoaN3-khin e5, sou2 biau2-si7 e5, siuN7-cho3 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 tai7-chi3, lai5 pho3-hai7 hiah-e5 lang5 sou2 khoaN3-tang7 e5.  29) Che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 be7-tang3 toa3 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 khoa-iau7.  30) Siong7-chu2 hou7 lin2 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, hou7 Ki-tok lai5 chiaN5-cho3 lan2 e5 ti3-hui7, hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 kap I ho5-hap8, hou7 lan2 chiaN5-cho3 I e5 chu2-bin5, iu7-koh hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 kiu3-siok8.  31) Sou2-i2 chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Na7 u7 lang5 ai3 khoa-khau2, eng-kai tioh8 khoa-iau7 Chu2 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3.”

Te7 2 ChiuN

Soan-iong5 Ki-tok pi7-teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 i2-cheng5 khi3 lin2 hia soan-kang2 Siong7-chu2 o3-pi3 e5 chin-li2 e5 si5, peng3 bo5 eng7 chhim-o3 e5 gian5-su5 a2-si7 ti3-hui7.  2) In-ui7 goa2 toa3 ti7 lin2 hia e5 tiong-kan, u7 koat-i3 ti5-liau2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, i2-kip8 I si2 chai7 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 e5 to7-li2 i2-goa7, bo5 ai3 soan-kang2 sim2-mih.  3) Goa2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5 chin loan2-jiok8 iu7-koh kiaN kah phih8-phih8-chhoah.  4) Goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, sou2 soan-iong5 e5 to7-li2, bo5 eng7 khau2-biau7 ti3-hui7 e5 gian5-su5 lai5 ka7 lang5 soat-hok8, si7 kho3 Seng3 Leng5 e5 tai7-leng5 lai5 cheng3-sit8 e5.  5) Goa2 an2-ne cho3 e5 bok8-tek m7-si7 boeh hou7 lin2 eng7 lang5 e5 ti3-hui7 lai5 kian3-lip8 sin3-giong2, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 oa2-kho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5 lai5 kian3-lip8.

Siong7-chu2 e5 ti3-hui7:
	6) Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ne, goan2 tui3 leng5-seng3 tiong2-seng5 e5 lang5 kong2 ti3-hui7 e5 oe7. Tan7-si7 he m7-si7 chit se3-kan e5 ti3-hui7, ia7 m7-si7 chit se3-kan e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 e5 ti3-hui7, in-ui7 chit se3-kan e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 i2-keng chiam7-chiam7 teh soe-bi5 a7.  7) Goan2 sou2 kong2 e5 si7 Siong7-chu2 o3-biau7 e5 ti3-hui7. Chit e5 ti3-hui7 si7 Siong7-chu2 ui7 lan2 e5 eng5-iau7, chhong3-cho7 u2-tiu7 i2-cheng5 chiu7 i2-keng ke3-oe7 ho2-se3, it-tit8 un2-chong5 kau3 hian7-chai7 e5.  8) Chit se3-kan e5 thong2-ti7-chia2 bo5 chit8 e5 liau2-kai2 chit e5 ti3-hui7; siat-su2 in na7 u7 liau2-kai2, chiu7 be7 chiong eng5-iau7 e5 Chu2 teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3.  9) Tan7-si7 chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Siong7-chu2 ui7-tioh8 keng3-ai3 I e5 lang5 sou2 chun2-pi7 khi2-lai5 e5; chiu7-si7 bak8-chiu sou2 m7-bat khoaN3-kiN3, hiN7-khang sou2 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3, lang5 e5 sim-koaN sou2 laiu7-siong2 be7 kau3 e5.”
10) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 Siong7-chu2 i2-keng u7 thong-koe3 Seng3 Leng5 tui3 lan2 khe2-si7 a7. Seng3 Leng5 ban7-su7 chai-iaN2 kah chin thiat-te2, lian5 Siong7-chu2 chhim-o3 e5 ke3-oe7 ia7 chai-iaN2.  11) Koan-he7 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3, tioh8-ai3 i ka-ti7 e5 sim-leng5 chiah chai-iaN2; kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, chi2-u7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 chiah chai-iaN2 koan-he7 Siong7-chu2 it-chhe3 e5 tai7-chi3.  12) Lan2 sou2 nia2-siu7 e5, m7-si7 chit se3-kan e5 leng5, si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5 Seng3 Leng5, thang hou7 lan2 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 in-su3.  13) Chiah-e5 in-su3, goan2 m7-si7 eng7 lang5 e5 ti3-hui7 sou2 ka3 e5 oe7, si7 eng7 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 ka3 e5 oe7 lai5 teh kong2 e5, chiong leng5-seng3 e5 tai7-chi3 kang2-kai2 hou7 u7 leng5-seng3 e5 lang5 thiaN.  14) Thian-seng chu7-jian5 e5 lang5 bo5 chiap-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 e5 in-hui7; chiah-e5 in-hui7 tui3 in khoaN3 khi2-lai5 long2 bo5 i3-su3. In be7-tang3 liau2-kai2, in-ui7 hiah-e5 in-su3 e5 ke3-tat8 tioh8 eng7 leng5-seng3 e5 gan2-kong chiah e7-tang3 phoaN3-toan3.  15) U7 leng5-seng3 e5 lang5 e7-tang3 phoaN3-toan3 ban7-su7 e5 ke3-tat8, tan7-si7 i ka-ti7 e5 ke3-tat8 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 ka7 i phoaN3-toan3.  16) Seng3-keng ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Sim2-mih lang5 chai-iaN2 Chu2 e5 sim-i3? Sim2-mih lang5 bat cho3 I e5 kou3-bun7?”
Tan7-si7 goan2 u7 Ki-tok e5 sim-chi3.

Te7 3 ChiuN

Siong7-chu2 e5 pok8-jin5:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 i2-cheng5 tui3 lin2 kong2-oe7 e5 si5, be7-tang3 ka7 lin2 khoaN3-cho3 u7 leng5-seng3 e5 lang5, chi2-u7 e7-tang3 ka7 lin2 khoaN3-cho3 chit se3-kan e5 phou2-thong-0lang5, khoaN3-cho3 tui3 Ki-tok e5 sin3-giong2 iu3-ti7 e5 lang5 lai5 tui3 lin2 kong2-oe7.  2) Goa2 bo5 eng7 png7, tioh8 eng7 gu5-leng ka7 lin2 chhi7, in-ui7 lin2 iau2-ku2 be7-kham-tit chiah8 teng7 e5 mih8. Sim7-chi3 hian7-chai7 lin2 iau2-ku2 be7-tang3 siau-hoa3,  3) in-ui7 lin2 iau2-ku2 chiau3 se3-siok8 e5 chok-hong teh seng-oah8. Lin2 e5 tiong-kan u7 chit-tou3 hun-cheng, an2-ne kam2 m7-si7 teh cheng3-beng5 lin2 si7 se3-siok8 e5 lang5, chiau3 se3-kan-lang5 e5 chok-hong teh seng-oah8?  4) Lin2 u7 lang5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 Po2-lo5,” ia7 u7 lang5 kong2, “Goa2 siok8 A-pho-lo5,” lin2 chit khoan2 e5 chok-hong kam2 m7-si7 kap se3-siok8-lang5 kang7-khoan2?
	5) Kiu3-keng3 A-pho-lo5 si7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5 lang5? Po2-lo5 si7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5 lang5? goan2 put-ko3 si7 Chu2 e5 lou5-pok8, in2-chhoa7 lin2 lai5 sin3 Chu3 e5. Goan2 kok-lang5 long2 si7 chiau3 Chu2 sou2 hun-phoe3 hou7 goan2 e5 kang-chok teh hok8-bu7.  6) Goa2 ia7 cheng2-chi2, A-pho-lo5 ak-chui2, tan7-si7 hou7 hit liap8 cheng2-chi2 seng5-tiong2 e5 si7 Siong7-chu2.  7) Sou2-i2 ia7-chi2-e5 kap ak-chui2-e5 long2 bo5 iau3-kin2, chi2-u7 hou7 i seng5-tiong2 Hit-ui7 Siong7-chu2 chiah si7 tiong7-iau3 e5.  8) Ia7-chi2 e5 lang5 kap ak-chui2 e5 lang5 long2 kang7-khoan2; Siong7-chu2 e7 an3-chiau3 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 kang-chok e5 hun-liong7 hou7 in kang-chiN5.  9) goan2 si7 kap Siong7-chu2 cho3-hoe2 kang-chok e5 lang5; lin2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chhan5-hng5. Lin2 ia7 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chhu3-theh8.  10) Chiau3 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 goa2 e5 in-hui7, goa2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 e5 lau2-lian7 e5 khi2-chhu3 sai-hu7, u7 phah te7-ki. Pat8-lang5 teh khi2-chhu3 toa3 chit e5 te7-ki-teng2. Tan7-si7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 chu3-i3 khoaN3 i an3-choaN2 teh khi2.  11) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 i2-keng u7 phah i5-it bu5-ji7 e5 te7-ki chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, i2-goa7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 phah leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 te7-ki.  12) Na7 u7 lang5 eng7 kim, gin5, po2-chioh8, a2-si7 eng7 bok8-chai5, chhau2, tiu7-chhau2 khi2-chhu3 toa3 chit e5 te7-ki teng2,  13) mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 kang-hu chai7 Sim2-phoaN3 e5 Jit8-chi2 e7 hian2-lou7 chhut-lai5. Hit e5 Jit8-chi2 chit8-e7 kau3, moa2-si3-ke3 to si7 hoe2, hit e5 hoe2 e7 kho2-giam7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 kang-chok e5 ke3-tat8.  14) Na7 u7 lang5 khi2-chhu3 hou7 hoe2 sio be7 khi3, i chiu7 e7 tit-tioh8 po3-siuN2;  15) i sou2 khi2 e5 na7 hou7 hoe2 sio-khi3, chiu7 be7 tit-tioh8 po3-siuN2, tan7-si7 i pun2-sin e7 tit-tioh8 kiu3, m7-ku2 e7 tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 tui3 hoe2-tiong to5-seng chhut-lai5.
	16) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 lin2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3-tian7, iah Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 toa3 ti7 lin2 lai7-bin7?  17) Na7 u7 lang5 hui2-hoai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3-tian7, Siong7-chu2 e7 hui2-biat8 hit e5 lang5; in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3-tian7 si7 sin5-seng3 e5, iah lin2 chiu7-si7 hit keng Seng3-tian7.
	18) Lin2 m7-thang khi-phian3 ka-ti7. Lin2 tiong-kan na7 u7 lang5 chiau3 chit se3-kan e5 phiau-chun2 siuN7-kong2 i si7 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5, i tioh8 tai7-seng cho3 gong7-lang5, chiah e7-tang3 cho3 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5.  19) In-ui7 chit se3-kan e5 ti3-hui7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 gan2-tiong si7 bo5-i3-bo5-su3 e5. Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2:
“Siong7-chu2 hou7 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5 tiong3-tioh8 in ka-ti7 e5 khui2-ke3.”
20) Iu7-koh kong2:
“Chu2 chai-iaN2 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5 e5 su-siong2 si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5.”
21) Sou2-i2 lin2 long2 m7 thang khoa-iau7 lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3. Su7-sit8-siong7 it-chhe3 long2 si7 lin2 e5:  22) bo5-lun7 Po2-lo5. A-pho-lo5, a2-si7 Pi2-tek; bo5-lun7 chit se3-kai3, si7 seng-chun5 a2-si7 si2-bong5, si7 hian7-chai7 a2-si7 chiong-lai5, it-chhe3 long2 si7 lin2 e5,  23) iah lin2 si7 siok8 Ki-tok, Ki-tok si7 siok8 Siong7-chu2.

Te7 4 ChiuN

Ki-tok e5 Su3-tou5:
	1) Lin2 tioh8 ka7 goan2 khoaN3-cho3 Ki-tok e5 pok8-jin5, Siong7-chu2 o3-biau7 e5 chin-li2 e5 koan2-ke.  2) Cho3 koan2-ke te7-it iau3-kin2 e5, chiu7-si7 tioh8-ai3 tui3 chu2-lang5 tiong-sit8.  3) Lin2 boeh ka7 goa2 phoaN3-toan3 a2-si7 lang5 boeh ka7 goa2 chhai5-phoaN3, goa2 khoaN3 khi2-lai5 long2 si7 sio2-su7; lian5 goa2 ka-ti7 ia7 bo5 phoaN3-toan3 ka-ti7.  4) Goa2 long2 m7 bat siu-tioh8 liong5-sim e5 chek-pi7, tan7-si7 an2-ne be7-eng7-tit cheng3-beng5 kong2 goa2 bo5 choe7; ka7 goa2 sim2-phoaN3 e5 si7 Chu2.  5) Sou2-i2 si5-ki5 iau2-boe7 kau3 lin2 m7-thang phe-pheng5 lang5. Choe3-au7 e5 sim2-phoaN3 tioh8-ai3 theng3-hau7 kau3 Chu2 e5 lai5-lim5. I e7 eng7 kng chiong ou-am3-tiong e5 pi3-bit8 chio3 kah chheng-chheng-chho2-chho2, e7 chiong lang5 sim-lai7 e5 ke3-bou5 hian2-lou7 chhut-lai5. Hit e5 si5 tai7-ke e7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 tit-tioh8 eng3-tit e5 o-lo2.
	6) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ui7-tioh8 lin2, ki2 goa2 ka-ti7 kap A-pho-lo5 cho3 le7 lai5 kong2-khi2 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, thang hou7 lin2 khoaN3 goan2 e5 bou5-iuN7 lai5 hak8-sip8 chit ku3 oe7 e5 i3-su3, chiu7-si7 kong2, “Tioh8 chun-chiau3 Seng3-keng lai5 seng-oah8.” An2-ne lin2 chiah be7 chu7-chun chu7-tai7, iong2-hou7 chit8 e5 lai5 hoan2-tui3 pat8 e5.  7) Sim2-mih lang5 hou7 li2 tek8-piat8 e5 te7-ui7? Li2 sou2 u7 e5 tah chit8 hang7 m7-si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 nia2-siu7 e5? Na7-si7 nia2-siu7 e5, li2 an3-choaN2 chu7-khoa cho3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 m7-si7 nia2-siu7 e5.
	8) Lin2 i2-keng tak8 hang7 u7 a7! Lin2 i2-keng ho2-giah8 a7! Lin2 i2-keng pang3-sak goan2 khi3 cho3 ong5 a7! Goa2 chin ng3-bang7 lin2 sit8-chai7 cho3 ong5 a7, an2-ne goan2 ia7 thang kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 cho3 ong5!  9) Chiau3 goa2 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, Siong7-chu2 chiong chiah-e5 Su3-tou5, chhin-chhiuN7 siu7 teng7 si2-choe7 teh si7-chiong3 e5 hoan7-lang5, ka7 goan2 pai5 toa3 heng5-liat8 e5 siong7 au7-bin7, lai5 teh hou7 u2-tiu7 tiong e5 chiong3 thian-sai3 kap cheng3-lang5 khoaN3.  10) Goan2 ui7-tioh8 Ki-tok cho3 gong7-lang5; tan7-si7 lin2 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8 chiaN5-cho3 chhong-beng5 e5! goan2 loan2-jiok8, tan7-si7 lin2 iong2-chong3! Goan2 siu7 biau2-si7, tan7-si7 lin2 siu7 chun-keng3!  11) It-tit8 kau3 chit e5 si5-chun7, goan2 pak-tou2 ia7 iau, chhui3 ia7 ta; seng-khu chheng7 phoa3-saN; hou7 lang5 phah; to3-chhu3 liu5-long7;  12) goan2 kho3 siang-chhiu2 thoa-boa5 tou7-koe3-jit8. Lang5 phaiN2-chhui3 me7 goan2 e5 si5, goan2 ka7 in chiok-hok; lang5 ka7 goan2 pek-hai7, goan2 chiu7 jim2-nai7;  13) lang5 ka7 goan2 bu2-jiok8, goan2 chiu7 chhin-chhiuN7 in3-tap in. goan2 i2-keng chiaN5-cho3 chit se3-kan e5 lah-sap-mih8; kau3 chit e5 si5-chun7, iau2-ku2 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-cho3 si7 lang5 e5 pun3-so3-tui!
	14) Goa2 sia2 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lin2 kam2-kak kian3-siau3; goa2 tian-to3 khoaN3 lin2 cho3 chhin-ai3 e5 kiaN2-ji5, boeh lai5 ka7 lin2 khou2-khng3.  15) Lin2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5, sui-jian5 u7 seng5-chhian seng5-ban7 e5 kau3-to7-chia2, tan7-si7 lau7-pa7 chi2-u7 chit8 e5. In-ui7 goa2 thoan5 Hok-im hou7 lin2, thong-koe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou lai5 chiaN5-cho3 lin2 e5 lau7-pe7.  16) Sou2-i2 goa2 khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 hau7-hoat goa2 e5 bou5-iuN7.  17) In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 chiah chhe-phai3 The5-mou5-thai3 khi3 lin2 hia. Goa2 khoaN3 i cho3 chhin-ai3 e5 kiaN2-ji5, a2-si7 chit8 e5 chin tiong-sit8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5. I e7 chiong goa2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 seng-oah8 hong-sek, i2-kip8 goa2 chai7 kok sou2-chai7 kok kau3-hoe7 kau3-hun3 lang5 e5 tai7-chi3 kong2 hou7 lin2 chai.
	18) Lin2 u7 lang5 siuN7-cho3 goa2 bo5 boeh khi3 lin2 hia, chiu7 chu7-chun chu7-tai7.  19) Tan7-si7 Chu2 na7 kheng2, goa2 liam5-piN7 e7 khi3 lin2 hia, iah goa2 sou2 ai3 chai-iaN2 e5, m7-si7 chiah-e5 chu7-chun chu7-tai7 e5 lang5 teh kong2 sim2-mih, si7 ai3 chai-iaN2 in kiu3-keng3 e7-tang3 cho3 sim2-mih.  20) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok m7-si7 chai7 gian5-su5, si7 chai7 koan5-leng5.  21) Lin2 ai3 tah chit8 hang7? Ai3 goa2 giah8 chhoe5-a2 khi3 lin2 hia, a2-si7 ai3 goa2 phau7 jin5-ai3 kap un-jiu5 e5 sim-cheng5 khi3?

Te7 5 ChiuN

Khai-ti5 im5-loan7-e5:
	1) Su7-sit8-siong7 u7 lang5 ka7 goa2 po3-ko3 kong2 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan u7 hoat-seng im5-loan7 e5 tai7-chi3. Lin2 chit khoan2 im5-loan7 lian5 bi7-sin3-chia2 ia7 be7-tang3 iong5-un2-tit; chiu7-si7 kong2 u7 lang5 siu-liu5 in au7-bo2 cho3 bou2.  2) An2-ne lin2 an3-choaN2 iau2-ku2 teh chu7-chun chu7-tai7? Lin2 eng-kai tioh8 pi-siong, chiong cho3 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5, tui3 lin2 e5 tiong-kan ka7 i koaN2 chhut-khi3 chiah tioh8.  3) goa2 lang5 sui-jian5 bo5 ti7 lin2 hia, goa2 e5 sim-leng5 ti7 lin2 hia, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 pun2-sin sit8-chai7 u7 ti7 lin2 hia, i2-keng hong7 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 mia5 ka7 hoan7 chit khoan2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5 phoaN3-choe7 a7.  4) Chiu7-si7 lin2 chu7-hoe7 e5 si5, goa2 e5 sim-leng5 ia7 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2, eng7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 tai7-leng5,  5) tioh8 chiong chit khoan2 lang5 kau hou7 Sat-tan3, lai5 hou7 i e5 jiok8-the2 biat8-bong5, thang hou7 i e5 leng5-hun5 chai7 Chu2 e5 Jit8-chi2 tit-tioh8 kiu3.
	6) Lin2 an2-ne chu7-khoa chin m7 ho2. Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 chit8-sut-a2 kaN3-bo2 chiu7 e7 hou7 kui sng5 e5 mi7-hun2-chhe3 hoat khi2-lai5?  7) Ui7-tioh8 boeh chiaN5-cho3 sin e5 mi7-hun2-chhe3, lin2 tioh8 chiong ku7 e5 kaN3-bo2 ti5 hou7 chheng-khi3. Su7-sit8-siong7 lin2 pun2-lai5 chiu7-si7 bo5 chham kaN3 e5 mi7-hun2-chhe3, in-ui7 lan2 e5 PoaN5-koe3-cheh e5 ko-iuN5 Ki-tok i2-keng ui7 lan2 hi-seng a.  8) Sou2-i2 lan2 koe3-cheh m7-thang eng7 ku7 e5 kaN3-bo2, m7-thang eng7 ok-tok8 sia5-ok e5 kaN3-bo2, tioh8 eng7 seng5-sit8, sun5-chin, bo5 chham kaN3-bo2 e5 mi7-pau.
	9) goa2 u7 sia2 chit8 tiuN phe kio3 lin2 m7-thang kap im5-loan7 e5 lang5 kau-chhap.  10) Goa2 peng3 m7-si7 teh chi2 chit se3-kan e5 im5-loan7-e5, tham-sim-e5, cha3-khi-e5, a2-si7 pai3 ngou2-siong7 e5 bi7-sin3-chia2; na7-si7 an2-ne lin2 soah tioh8 thoat-li7 chit se3-kan.  11) Goa2 sou2 sia2 e5 sit8-chai7 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 teh chi2 im5-loan7-e5, a2-si7 tham-sim-e5, a2-si7 pai3 ngou2-siong7-e5, a2-si7 hui2-pong3-e5, a2-si7 ai3 chiah8-chiu2-chui3-e5, a2-si7 cha3-khi-e5 hiah-e5 iu2-beng5 bu5-sit8 e5 sin3-tou5, lin2 m7-thang kap i kau-chhap; lian5 kap chit khoan2 lang5 cho3-hoe2 chiah8 png7 ia7 m7-thang.  12-13) Phe-pheng5 kau3-hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5 kap goa2 u7 sim2-mih koan-he7? Siong7-chu2 e7 ka7 in sim2-phoaN3. Kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5 lin2 kam2 m7-si7 tioh8 ka-ti7 sim2-phoaN3? Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Lin2 tioh8 chiong hit e5 sia5-ok e5 lang5 tui3 lin2 tiong-kan koaN2 chhut-khi3.”

Te7 6 ChiuN

Khong3-ko3 hiaN-ti7:
	1) Lin2 sin3-tou5 e5 tiong-kan na7 u7 lang5 kap pat8 e5 hiaN-ti7 hoat-seng kiu3-hun e5 si5, i kam2 kaN2 khi3 bi7-sin3-chia2 e5 hoat-koaN e5 bin7-cheng5 ko3 i, iah bo5 khi3 kiu5 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 the3 in kai2-koat?  2) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e7 sim2-phoaN3 se3-kai3? Se3-kai3 na7 tioh8 siu7 lin2 sim2-phoaN3, lin2 kam2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang sim2-phoaN3 sio2-sio2 e5 tai7-chi3?  3) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 lan2 to boeh sim2-phoaN3 thian-sai3 a3, ho5-hong3 chit se3-kan jit8-siong5 e5 tai7-chi3!  4) Lin2 na7 tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 sio2-sio2 e5 tai7-chi3 su-iau3 kai2-koat e5 si5, kam2 boeh chhiaN2 kau3-hoe7 bo5 chun-tiong7 e5 goa7-lang5 ka7 lin2 phoaN3-koat?  5) Goa2 an2-ne kong2 si7 boeh hou7 lin2 kian3-siau3! Lin2 lai7-bin7 kam2 chiaN5-sit8 bo5 chit8 e5 chhong-beng5 e5 lang5, e7-tang3 lai5 kai2-koat lin2 hiaN-ti7-kan e5 kiu3-hun?  6) HiaN-ti7 kam2 tioh8 lai5 ko3 hiaN-ti7, siong7-chhiaN2 chai7 bo5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 lai5 sio-ko3?  7) Sit8-che3-siong7 lin2 hiaN-ti7 lai5 sio-ko3 i2-keng si7 toa7 chho3-gou7. Lin2 na2 bo5 ai3 kam-goan7 lai5 jim2-siu7 khek-khui? Na2 bo5 ai3 leng5-kho2 lai5 siu7 khi-phian3?  8) Lin2 tian-to3 cho3 khek-khui lang5 e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 khi-phian3 lang5, sim7-chi3 lai5 khi-phian3 lin2 e5 hiaN-ti7.  9) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 put-gi7 e5 lang5 be7-tang3 seng5-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok? Lin2 m7-thang khi-phian3 ka-ti7; bo5-lun7 si7 im5-loan7-e5, pai3 ngou2-siong7-e5, hoan7 kan-im5-e5, cho3 lam5-chhiong-e5, a2-si7 hoan7 lam5-sek-e5,  10) cho3-chhat8-e5, tham-sim-e5, chiu2-kui2, hui2-pong3-e5, khi-phian3-lang5-e5, long2 be7-tang3 seng5-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok.  11) Lin2 u7-e5 i2-cheng5 si7 an2-ne; tan7-si7 oa2-kho3 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 mia5 i2-kip8 lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5, lin2 i2-keng se2 chheng-khi3, chiaN5-cho3 seng3-kiat e5, lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 a7.

Eng7 sin-the2 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2:
	12) U7 lang5 e7 kong2, “Goa2 tak8 hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 e7-eng7-tit cho3.” An2-ne kong2 bo5 m7-tioh8, tan7-si7 bo5 it-teng7 tak8 hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 u7 li7-ek. Goa2 ia7 e7-eng7-tit kong2, “Goa2 tak8 hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 e7-eng7-tit cho3”; tan7-si7 goa2 bo5 boeh hou7 sim2-mih tai7-chi3 lai5 ka7 goa2 khong3-che3.  13) U7 lang5 kong2, “Chiah8-mih8 si7 ui7-tioh8 pak-tou2, pak-tou2 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh chiah8 mih8-kiaN7.” An2-ne bo5 m7-tioh8, tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 e7 chiong chit nng7 hang7 long2 hui2-hoai7. Seng-khu m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lang5 loan7-im5 e5, si7 ui7-tioh8 Chu2, iah Chu2 si7 ui7-tioh8 seng-khu.  14) Siong7-chu2 hou7 Chu2 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, I ia7 e7 eng7 I e5 tai7-leng5 hou7 lan2 koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5.
	15) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 lin2 e5 seng-khu si7 Ki-tok e5 chi-the2? An2-ne goa2 kam2 e7-eng7-tit theh8 Ki-tok e5 chi-the2 lai5 cho3 chhiong-ki e5 seng-khu e5 chit8 pou7-hun7? Choat8-tui3 be7-eng7-tit!  16) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 kap chhiong-ki hoat-seng jiok8-the2 koan-he7 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 jiok8-the2-siong7 kap i kiat-seng5 it-the2? In-ui7 Seng3-keng kong2, “Nng7 lang5 e7 kiat-seng5 it-the2.”  17) Tan7-si7 kap Chu2 kiat-hap8 e5 lang5, sim-leng5-siong7 kap I kiat-seng5 it-the2.
	18) Lin2 tioh8 siam2-pi7 sia5-im5. Lang5 sou2 hoan7 it-chhe3 e5 choe7 be7 eng2-hiong2 i e5 seng-khu; tan7-si7 hoan7 sia5-im5 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 tui3 i ka-ti7 e5 seng-khu hoan7-choe7.  19) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 lin2 e5 seng-khu si7 toa3 ti7 lin2 lai7-bin7 Hit-ui7 Seng3 Leng5 e5 Seng3-tian7, iah Seng3 Leng5 si7 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 lin2 e5? Lin2 m7-si7 lin2 ka-ti7 e5.  20) Lin2 si7 Siong7-chu2 eng7 koan5-ke3 be2-lai5 e5. Sou2-i2 tioh8 eng7 lin2 e5 seng-khu lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.

Te7 7 ChiuN

Hun-in bun7-te5:
	1) TaN lai5 lun7 lin2 sia2 lai5 mng7 goa2 e5 tai7-chi3.
	Cha2-pou-lang5 mai3 kap cha-bou2-lang5 hoat-seng koan-he7 khah ho2.  2) Tan7-si7 in-ui7 u7 hoat-seng chin-che7 im5-loan7 e5 su7-cheng5, sou2-i2 mui2 chit8 e5 cha-pou-lang5 tioh8 u7 i ka-ti7 e5 thai3-thai3, mui2 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8 u7 i ka-ti7 e5 tiong7-hu.  3) Tiong7-hu tui3 thai3-thai3 tioh8 chin7 eng3-chin7 e5 gi7-bu7, thai3-thai3 ia7 tioh8 tui3 tiong7-hu chin7 eng3-chin7 e5 gi7-bu7.  4) Thai3-thai3 tui3 i ka-ti7 e5 seng-khu bo5 chu2-koan5, tiong7-hu chiah u7; kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, tiong7-hu tui3 i ka-ti7 e5 seng-khu ia7 bo5 chu2-koan5, thai3-thai3 chiah u7.  5) Na7 m7-si7 hu-chhe hou7-siong tong5-i3 boeh chiam7-si5 mai3 cho3-hoe2 thang lai5 choan-sim ki5-to2, be7-eng7-tit hou7-siong ki7-choat8; tan7-si7 jian5-au7 tioh8 koh khoe-hok8 cheng3-siong5 e5 hu-chhe koan-he7, an2-ne lin2 chiah be7 sit-khi3 chu7-che3 lai5 siu7 Sat-tan3 e5 iu2-hek8.  6) Goa2 kong2 an2-ne, si7 in2-chun2 lin2 an2-ne cho3, m7-si7 teh beng7-leng7 lin2.  7) Goa2 ai3 tai7-ke e7-tang3 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 an2-ne; tan7-si7 kok-lang5 long2 u7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 nia2-siu7 tek8-su5 e5 in-su3, chit e5 lang5 chit khoan2, hit e5 lang5 hit khoan2.
	8) Tui3 hiah-e5 bo5 kiat-hun-e5 kap chiu2-koaN2-e5, goa2 khng3 in chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 an2-ne, ke3-siok8 tok8-sin khah ho2.  9) Tan7-si7 in na7 be7-tang3 chu7-che3, chiu7 tioh8 kiat-hun; in-ui7 na7 boeh hou7 iok8-hoe2 lai5 sio-sim, iau2 si7 kiat-hun khah ho2.  10) Goa2 beng7-leng7 i2-keng kiat-hun e5 lang5 (che m7-si7 goa2 e5 beng7-leng7, si7 Chu2 e5 beng7-leng7): thai3-thai3 be7-eng7-tit li7-khui i e5 tiong7-hu.  11) Siat-su2 li7-khui, chiu7 m7-thang koh ke3. Na7 bo5, chiu7 tioh8 koh kap tiong7-hu ho5-ho2; tiong7-hu ia7 be7-eng7-tit li5 thai3-thai3.
	12) Tui3 ki5-tha e5 lang5 goa2 boeh khou2-khng3 in (che si7 goa2 e5 i3-kian3, m7-si7 Chu2 kong2 e5): na7 u7 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 thai3-thai3 m7-si7 sin3-tou5, m7-ku2 thai3-thai3 goan7-i3 kap i cho3-hoe2 seng-oah8, i be7-eng7-tit ka7 i li7-hun.  13) Iu7-koh na7 u7 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kap bi7-sin3-chia2 e5 cha-pou-lang5 kiat-hun, k7-ku2 tiong7-hu na7 goan7-i3 kap i cho3-hoe2 seng-oah8, i be7-eng7-tit kap tiong7-hu li7-hun.  14) In-ui7 iau2-boe7 sin3 Chu2 e5 tiong7-hu e7 thong-koe3 sin3 Chu2 e5 thai3-thai3 lai5 kui-siok8 Siong7-chu2; iau2-boe7 sin3 Chu2 e5 thai3-thai3 ia7 e7 thong-koe3 sin3 Chu2 e5 tiong7-hu lai5 kui-siok8 Siong7-chu2. Na7 m7-si7 an2-ne in e5 kiaN2-ji5 chiu7 chhin-chhiuN7 bi7-sin3-chia2 e5 kiaN2-ji5; tan7-si7 su7-sit8-siong7 in si7 kui-siok8 Siong7-chu2 e5.  15) Tan7-si7 hu-chhe bo5 sin3 Chu2 hit peng5 na7 boeh li7-khui, chiu7 chhut-chai7 i khi3. Hit e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 tiong7-hu a2-si7 thai3-thai3, chai7 chit khoan2 cheng5-heng5 bo5 siu7 sok-pak8. In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 tiau3 lin2, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 peng5-an koe3-jit8.  16) Lin2 sin3 Chu2 e5 thai3-thai3, lin2 long2 chai-iaN2 kiu3 tiong7-hu si7 lin2 e5 pun2-hun7. Lin2 sin3 Chu2 e5 tiong7-hu, lin2 ia7 long2 chai-iaN2 kiu3 thai3-thai3 si7 lin2 e5 pun2-hun7.

Sin3-tou5 tioh8 siu2-ki2 an-hun7:
	17) Mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 eng-kai tioh8 chiau3 Chu2 e5 an-pai5, chiau3 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 i e5 si5 e5 chong7-thai3 ke3-siok8 lai5 seng-oah8. Che si7 goa2 tui3 it-chhe3 e5 kau3-hoe7 sou2 beng7-leng7 e5.  18) U7 lang5 siu7 kat-le2 liau2-au7 chiah siu7 keng2-tiau3 a2 bo5? Na7 an2-ne i chiu7 m7-thang siuN7-boeh am-kham3 kat-le2 e5 ki3-hou7. U7 lang5 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 lai5 siu7 keng2-tiau3 a2 bo5? Na7 u7, i chiu7 m7-thang siu7 kat-le2.  19) U7 siu7 kat-le2 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 long2 bo5 iau3-kin2. Siong7 iau3-kin2 e5 chiu7-si7 chun-heng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 kai3-beng7.  20) Mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 po2-chhi5 i siu7 keng2-tiau3 e5 si5 e5 sin-hun7.  21) Li2 siu7 keng2-tiau3 e5 si5, si7 teh cho3 lou5-le7 a2 m7-si7? M7-bian2 koa3-i3; tan7-si7 na7 u7 ki-hoe7 thang tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5, tioh8 pa2-ak hit e5 ki-hoe7.  22) In-ui7 cho3 lou5-le7 e5 si5 siu7 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 e5, chiu7-si7 siu7 Chu2 sek-hong3 e5 chu7-iu5-jin5; chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne, cho3 chu7-iu5-jin5 e5 si5 siu7 Ki-tok keng2-tiau3 e5, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok e5 lou5-pok8.  23) Lin2 si7 eng7 koan5-ke3 be2-lai5 e5; sou2-i2 m7-thang cho3 lang5 e5 lou5-le7.  24) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 siu7 keng2-tiau3 e5 si5, si7 sim2-mih sin-hun7, chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 tioh8 po2-chhi5 hit e5 sin-hun7.

Tok8-sin-chia2 i2-kip8 koaN2-hu7 e5 bun7-te5:
	25) Koan-he7 tok8-sin e5 bun7-te5, goa2 bo5 siu7-tioh8 Chu2 e5 beng7-leng7, tan7-si7 cho3 chit8 e5 tit-tioh8 Chu2 e5 lin5-bin2 chiaN5-cho3 e7 sin3-nai7-tit e5 lang5, goa2 boeh the5-kiong3 chit8 e5 i3-kian3.  26) Kho2-li7 tioh8 hian7-chai7 e5 khun3-khou2, goa2 siuN7, cha-pou-lang5 tioh8 po2-chhi5 hian7-chong7 khah ho2.  27) Li2 u7 siu7 thai3-thai3 e5 thoa-lui7 bo5? Na7 u7, m7-thang siuN7-boeh thoat-li7. Li2 si7 tok8-sin a2 m7-si7? Na7 si7, m7-thang siuN7-boeh chhoa7-bou2.  28) Tan7-si7 li2 na7 chhoa7-bou2, m7-si7 hoan7-tioh8 choe7; chai7-sek-li2 na7 ke3-ang ia7 m7-si7 hoan7-tioh8 choe7. Tan7-si7 hiah-e5 kiat-hun e5 lang5, e7 siu7-tioh8 jit8-siong5 e5 khun3-khou2. Goa2 ng3-bang7 lin2 pi7-bian2 chit chiong2 khun3-khou2.
	29) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2: Boat8-jit8 teh-boeh kau3 a3. Tui3 chim2-ma2 khi2, u7 thai3-thai3 e5 tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 thai3-thai3 e5 lai5 koe3-jit8;  30) teh khau3 e5 tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 teh khau3 e5; teh hoaN-hi2 e5 tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 teh hoaN-hi2 e5; be2 mih8 e5 lang5 tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 in chhiu2-lai7 bo5 poaN3-hang7 mih8;  31) teh li7-iong7 chit se3-kan e5 chai5-but8 e5 lang5, tioh8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 teh li7-iong7. In-ui7 chit se3-kan e5 hian7-chong7 koh i5-chhi5 bo5 loa7 ku2 a3.
	32) Goa2 chin ai3 lin2 bo5-hoan5-bo5-lo2. Bo5 kiat-hun e5 cha-pou-lang5 koan-sim Chu2 e5 tai7-chi3, bok8-tek si7 boeh an3-choaN2 lai5 hou7 chu2 hoaN-hi2.  33) U7 kiat-hun e5 cha-pou-lang5 koan-sim se3-kan e5 tai7-chi3, bok8-tek si7 boeh an3-choaN2 lai5 be2 thai3-thai3 e5 hoan-sim. An2-ne i chiu7 e7 sam-sim liong2-i3.  34) Bo5 kiat-hun e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 a2-si7 chai7-sek-li2 koan-sim Chu2 e5 tai7-chi3, bok8-tek si7 boeh hou7 jiok8-the2 kap sim-leng5 long2 kui-siok8 Siong7-chu2; tan7-si7 u7 kiat-hun e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, koan-sim se3-kan e5 tai7-chi3, bok8-tek si7 boeh an3-choaN2 lai5 hou7 tiong7-hu hoaN-hi2.  35) goa2 kong2 an2-ne si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 e5 li7-ek, m7-si7 boeh ka7 lin2 sok-pak8, si7 boeh hou7 lin2 cho3 cheng3-tong7 e5 tai7-chi3, it-sim it-i3 lai5 su7-hong7 Chu2.
	36) Na7 u7 lang5 siuN7-kong2 pang3 hou7 i e5 cha-bou2=kiaN2 chiah8 kah siuN che7 hoe3 iau2-boe7 chhut-ke3, an2-ne chin put eng-kai, bo5 the3 cha-bou2-kiaN2 phah-sng3 be7-eng7-tit, i e7-eng7-tit chiau3 i e5 i3-su3 khi3 cho3; i na7 hou7 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 chhut-ke3, an2-ne m7-si7 hoan7-choe7.  37) Leng7-goa7 chit8 hong-bin7, na7 u7 lang5 koat-sim kian-kiong5, bo5 siu7 sim2-mih ap-pek, oan5-choan5 e7-tang3 ka-ti7 cho3-chu2, koat-sim hou7 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 mai3 chhut-ke3, i sou2 cho3 e5 si7 ho2 e5 tai7-chi3.  38) Chong2 kong2 chit8 ku3, hou7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 chhut-ke3 si7 ho2 e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 bo5 hou7 i chhut-ke3 si7 koh-khah ho2 e5 tai7-chi3.
	39) Tiong7-hu iau2 teh oah8 e5 tiong-kan, thai3-thai3 bo5 chu7-iu5; tan7-si7 tiuong7-hu si2 liau2-au7, i chiu7 e7-eng7-tit chu7-iu5 kap i kah-i3 e5 lang5 kiat-hun; m7-ku2 it-teng7 tioh8 ke3 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5.  40) chong2-si7 i na7 chiu2-koaN2 mai3 koh ke3 khah heng7-hok. Che si7 goa2 e5 i3-kian3, siong7-chhiaN2 goa2 siuN7, goa2 ia7 u7 siu7 Siong7-chu2 Seng3 Leng5 e5 khe2-si7.

Te7 8 ChiuN

Che3 ngou2-siong7 liau2-au7 e5 chiah8-mih8:
	1) TaN lai5 lun7 che3 ngou2-siong7 liau2-au7 e5 chiah8-mih8. Tong-jian5 lan2 long2 chai-iaN2 “lan2 long2 u7 ti3-sek.” M7-ku2 ti3-sek e7 hou7 lang5 kiau-ngou7; ai3-sim e7 hou7 lang5 kian3-lip8 tek-heng7.  2) Na7 u7 lang5 siuN7-cho3 i bat tai7-chi3, i sit8-chai7 si7 iau2 m7-bat i eng-kai bat e5 tai7-chi3.  3) Tan7-si7 keng3-ai3 Siong7-chu2 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 bat e5 lang5.
	4) Sou2-i2 lun7-khi2 che3 ngou2-siong7 liau2-au7 e5 chiah8-mih8: lan2 chai-iaN2 ngou2-siong7 sou2 teh tai7-piau2 e5 sin5-beng5, ti7 chit se3-kan su7-sit8-siong7 bo5 chun5-chai7; lan2 chai-iaN2 “chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-chu2, i2-goa7 bo5 koh u7 pat8 e5.”  5) Sui-jian5 thian-siong7 te7-siong7 u7 hou7 lang5 chheng-hou cho3 sin5 e5, sui-jian5 u7 chin-che7 “sin5”, ia7 u7 chin-che7 “chu2”,  6) tan7-si7:
“Lan2 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-chu2, chiu7-si7 Thian-hu7; I si7 ban7-but8 e5 chhong3-cho7-chia2; lan2 si7 ui7-tioh8 I teh seng-chun5 e5. Lan2 chi2-u7 chit8 ui7 Chu2, chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok; ban7-but8 si7 thong-koe3 I lai5 chhong3-cho7 e5; lan2 si7 oa2-kho3 I teh seng-chun5 e5.”
	7) Tan7-si7 m7-si7 tak8 lang5 long2 u7 chit e5 ti3-sek. U7-e5 pai3 ngou2-siong7 pai3 liau2 koan3-si3, kau3 kin-na2-jit8 in chiah8 chit khoan2 chiah8-mih8 e5 si5, iua2-ku2 siuN7-cho3 hiah-e5 che3 ngou2-siong7 e5 chiah8-mih8 si7 ngou2-siong7 e5; in e5 liong5-sim loan2-jiok8, siuN7-kong2 chiah8-tioh8 chit khoan2 chiah8-mih e7 jiam2-tioh8 lah-sap.  8) Chiah8-mih8 be7 hou7 lan2 khah chhin-kin7 Siong7-chu2. Lan2 mai3 chiah8 ia7 be7 song3-sit sim2-mih; lan2 chiah8 ia7 be7 tit-tioh8 sim2-mih.  9) Tioh8 kin2-sin7, m7-thang hou7 lin2 chit khoan2 chu7-iu5-koan5 lai5 in2-khi2 sin3-sim pok8-jiok8 e5 lang5 hoan7-choe7.  10) Na7 u7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 u7 ti3-sek e5 lang5, toa3 ti7 hok8-sai7 ngou2-siong7 e5 bio7-lai7 teh chiah8, in e5 liong5-sim ki3-jian5 loan2-jiok8, kam2 be7 khoaN3 lin2 e5 iuN7, toa7-taN2 lai5 chiah8 che3 koe3 ngou2-siong7 e5 mih8?  11) An2-ne lin2 e5 ti3-sek e7 hui2-biat8 loan2-jiok8 e5 lang5, i ia7-si7 Ki-tok ui7 i lai5 si2 e5 hiaN-ti7.  12) Lin2 na7 an2-ne lai5 tek-choe7 lin2 e5 hiaN-ti7, siong-tioh8 in loan2-jiok8 e5 liong5-sim, chiu7-si7 teh tek-choe7 Ki-tok.  13) Sou2-i2 chiah8 che3 ngou2-siong7 e5 bah, na7 e7 in2-khi2 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 hoan7-choe7, goa2 chiu7 choat8-tui3 m7 koh chiah8 bah, chiah be7 hou7 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 hoan7-choe7.

Te7 9 ChiuN

Su3-tou5 e5 koan5-han7 kap pun2-hun7:
	1) Goa2 kam2 bo5 chu7-iu5? Goa2 kam2 m7-si7 Su3-tou5? goa2 kam2 m7-bat kiN3-tioh8 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou? Lin2 sin3 Chu2 kam2 m7-si7 goa2 thoan5-kau3 e5 seng5-ko2?  2) Siat-su2 pat8-lang5 bo5 ka7 goa2 seng5-jin7 cho3 Su3-tou5, lin2 it-teng7 u7 ka7 goa2 seng5-jin7! In-ui7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 Ki-tok-tou5, chiu7-si7 goa2 cho3 Su3-tou5 e5 in3-cheng3.
	3) Lang5 phe-pheng5 goa2 e5 si5, goa2 boeh an2-ne pian7-beng5:  4) Goa2 kam2 bo5 koan5 thang chiah8, thang l;im?  5) goa2 kam2 bo5 koan5 thang lai5 kap kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 chi2-moe7 kiat-hun,m khi3 kok sou2-chai7 thoan5-to7 e5 si5 chhoa7 i cho3-hoe2 khi3, chhin-chhiuN7 ki5-tha e5 Su3-tou5, Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7, i2-kip8 Pi2-tek an2-ne?  6) Kam2 kan-taN goa2 kap Pa-na2-pa ui7-tioh8 seng-oah8 bo5 thoa-boa5 be7-eng7-tit?  7) Kam2 u7 lang5 cho3-peng chiah8 ka-ti7? U7 sim2-mih lang5 keng-eng5 phu5-to5-hng5, bo5 chiah8 hng5-lai7 e5 phu5-to5? U7 sim2-mih lang5 chhi7 chit8 tin7 iuN5, long2 bo5 lim i ka-ti7 e5 iuN5-leng?  8) goa2 kam2 kan-taN chiau3 se3-kan e5 cheng5-li2 an2-ne teh kong2? Lut8-hoat kam2 m7-si7 ia7 u7 an2-ne kong2?  9) Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Gu5 teh lun2 beh8-a2 e5 si5, m7-thang ka7 i kao3 gu5-chhui3-lam.” An2-ne Siong7-chu2 si7 teh koan-hoai5 gu5 si7 bo5?  10) I kam2 m7-si7 sit8-chai7 ui7-tioh8 lan2 cheng3-lang5 chiah an2-ne kong2? tong-jian5 che si7 ui7-tioh8 lan2 sia2 e5. In-ui7 keng-choh e5 lang5 kap siu-koah e5 lang5, tioh8 ng3-bang7 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 in siu-seng5 e5 hun7-giah8 lai5 kang-chok.  11) Goan2 na7 ui7 lin2 ia7 sim-leng5-siong7 e5 cheng2-chi2, tui3 lin2 siu-koah but8-chit-siong7 e5 li7-ek, an2-ne kam2 e7 siuN koe3-hun7?  12) Pat8-lang5 na7 u7 koan5 thang tui3 lin2 iau-kiu5 an2-ne, goan2 kam2 m7-si7 koh-khah u7 koan5 thang iau-kiu5?
	Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ne, goan2 long2 m7-bat li7-iong7 chit e5 koan5. goan2 tian-to3 jim2-siu7 it-chhe3, khah be7 khi3 chou2-gai7 tioh8 Ki-tok Hok-im e5 chin3-tian2.  13) Lin2 kam2 m7-chai-iaN2 ti7 Seng3-tian7 teh kang-chok e5 lang5, oa2-kho3 seng3-tian7 lai5 seng-oah8, ti7 che3-toaN5 teh su7-hong7 e5 lang5, hun-hiong2 che3-toaN5 e5 mih8?  14) Sou2-i2 Chu2 ia7 u7 beng7-leng7 soan-pou3 Hok-im e5 lang5 tioh8 oa2-kho3 Hok-im lai5 seng-oah8.  15) Tan7-si7 chiah-e5 koan5 goa2 long2 m7-bat li7-iong7 kah chit8 hang7. Goa2 ia7 m7-si7 sia2 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 boeh iau-kiu5 lin2 chiau3 an2-ne lai5 tui3-thai7 goa2; na7 boeh an2-ne, goa2 leng5-kho2 si2 khah ho2! Bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 hou7 goa2 sou2 khoa-iau7 e5 tai7-chi3 piN3-cho3 iu2-beng5 bu5-sit8.  16) Goa2 na7 thoan5 Hok-im, che peng3 bo5 sim2-mih thang khoa-khau2. Goa2 si7 bo5 an2-ne cho3 be7-eng7-tit. Goa2 na7 bo5 thoan5 Hok-im, chiu7 u7 toa7 chai-ho7.  17) Goa2 na7 chu7-goan7 lai5 teh cho3 chit e5 kang-chok, chiu7 u7 koan5 thang iau-kiu5 po3-tap; ki3-jian5 goa2 si7 siuN7-cho3 gi7-bu7 lai5 teh cho3, che chiu7-si7 teh cho3 Siong7-chu2 sou2 ui2-thok goa2 e5 chit-bu7.  18) Na7 an2-ne goa2 e5 po3-tap kiu3-keng3 si7 sim2-mih? Goa2 e5 po3-tap chiu7-si7 u7 tek8-koan5 bian2-hui3 lai5 thoan5 Hok-im, bo5 kian-chhi5 thoan5 Hok-im e5 koan5-li7.
	19) Sui-jian5 goa2 si7 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5, m7-si7 lang5 e5 lou5-le7, put-ko3 ui7-tioh8 chin7-liong7 lai5 chheng-chhu2 khah che7 lang5, goa2 chu7-goan7 lai5 cho3 cheng3-lang5 e5 lou5-le7.  20) goa2 ti7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 tiong-kan teh kang-chok e5 si5, chiu7 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 seng-oah8, thang lai5 cheng-chhu2 Iu5-thai3-lang5. Tui3 hiah-e5 siu7 Mou5-se e5 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5, sui-jian5 goa2 pun2-sin bo5 siu7 Lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat, goa2 ia7 chiaN5-cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 siu7 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5, thang lai5 cheng-chhu2 siu7 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5;  21) goa2 ti7 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 tiong-kan teh kang-chok e5 si5, chiu7 chiaN5-cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 Goa7-pang-lang5, thang lai5 cheng-chhu2 Goa7-pang-lang5. Ki5-sit8 goa2 m7-si7 bo5 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5, si7 siu7 Ki-tok e5 hoat-chek koan2-hat e5.  22) Tui3 loan2-jiok8 e5 lang5, goa2 chiu7 chiaN5-cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 loan2-jiok8 e5, thang lai5 cheng-chhu2 loan2-jiok8 e5. Tui3 kok chiong2 lang5, goa2 chiaN5-cho3 kap in kang7-khoan2 e5, thang chin7 it-chhe3 e5 lek8-liong7 lai5 kiu3 chit8-koa2 lang5.  23) Goa2 sou2 cho3 e5, long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 Hok-im, thang lai5 kap cheng3-lang5 hun-hiong2 Hok-im e5 chiok-hok.  24) Lin2 kam2 m7 chai-iaN2 chai7 the2-iok8-tiuN5 chham-ka keng3-sai3 e5 lang5 long2 piaN3-se3 chau2, tan7-si7 tit-tioh8 koan-kun e5 lang5 chiah u7 chit8 e5 nia7-nia7? Lin2 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne piaN3-se3 chau2, thang lai5 tit-tioh8 koan-kun. 25) Mui2 chit8 e5 chham-ka pi2-sai3 e5 lang5, chai7 kok hong-bin7 long2 tioh8 khek-khou2 thoan3-lian7. In an2-ne cho3, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh tit-tioh8 e7 pai7-hoai7 e5 goat8-kui3-koan, tan7-si7 lan2 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh tit-tioh8 be7 pai7-hoai7 e5 eng5-koan.  26) Sou2-i2 goa2 chau2, m7-si7 bo5 bok8-phiau teh chau2, goa2 phah-kun5, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 phah chhut-khi3 e5 kun5 lok8-khong.  27) Goa2 khou2-lian7 ka-ti7 e5 seng-khu lai5 ka7 i khong3-che3, chiah be7 tui3 pat8-lang5 soan-kang2 Hok-im liau2-au7, ka-ti7 soah lai5 lok8-soan2.

Te7 10 ChiuN

Keng2-kai3 pai3 ngou2-siong7:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ai3 lin2 chai-iaN2 lan2 e5 chou2-sian long2 siu7 hun5 e5 po2-hou7, long2 u7 keng-koe3 Ang5-hai2;  2) long2 chai7 hun5-lai7 kap hai2-nih siu7 se2-le2 lai5 kui Mou5-se,  3) long2 chiah8 kang-khoan2 leng5-seng3 e5 chiah8-mih8,  4) lim kang7-khoan2 leng5-seng3 e5 im2-liau7; in-ui7 in sou2 lim e5, si7 tui3 hit te3 toe3 in teh kiaN5 e5 leng5-seng3 e5 chioh8-poaN5 chhut-lai5 e5. Hit te3 chioh8-poaN5 chiu7-si7 Ki-tok.  5) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 tui3 in toa7-pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 bo5 moa2-i3; sou2-i2 in chai7 khong3-ia2 tiong si2 kah hoaiN5-hoaiN5-keh8-keh8.
	6) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 lan2 cho3 chian5-le7, keng2-kai3 lan2 m7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 in siau3-siuN7 boeh cho3 phaiN2 tai7-chi3.  7) Ia7 m7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 in u7-e5 khi3 pai3 ngou2-siong7. Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2, “Cheng3-lang5 che7 loh8-khi3 chiah8, che7 loh8-khi3 lim, khi2-lai5 loan7-thiau3 loan7-sng2.”  8) Lan2 m7-thang hoan7 kan-im5, chhin-chhiuN7 in u7-e5 hoan7-tioh8, chit8 jit8 e5 tiong-kan soah si2 nng7-ban7 saN-chheng e5.  9) Lan2 m7-thang chhi3-tham3 Chu2, chhin-chhiuN7 in u7-e5 an2-ne cho3, soah hou7 choa5 ka7 si2-khi3.  10) M7-thang kong2 put-peng5, in-ui7 in u7-e5 an2-ne cho3, soah hou7 Hui2-biat8 e5 Thian-sai3 hui2-biat8 khi3.
	11) Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 chai7 in tiong-kan hoat-seng, lai5 hou7 lan2 cho3 chian5-le7, iu7-koh ki3-chai3 khi2-lai5 keng2-kai3 lan2 chai7 chit e5 boat8-se3 teh seng-chun5 e5 lang5.
	12) Sou2-i2 ka-ti7 siuN7-cho3 khia7 chai7-chai7 e5 lang5, tioh8 kin2-sin7 chiah be7 poah8-to2.  13) Lin2 sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 chhi3-lian7, bo5 chit8 hang7 m7-si7 lang5 peng5-siong5 sou2 teh keng-giam7 e5. Siong7-chu2 si7 sin3-sit8 e5. I be7 hou7 lin2 tu2-tioh8 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang jim2-siu7 e5 chhi3-lian7, lin2 siu7 chhi3-lian7 e5 si5, I ia7 e7 chun2-pi7 chit8 tiau5 chhut-lou7, hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 jim2-siu7.
	14) Sou2-i2 chhin-ai3 e5 hiaN-ti7, lin2 m7-thang pai3 ngou2-siong7.  15) Goa2 ka7 lin2 khoaN3-cho3 bat cheng5-li2 e5 lang5 teh ka7 lin2 kong2; goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 lin2 tioh8 ka-ti7 phoaN3-toan3.  16) Lan2 phang5 khi2-lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 hit poe chiok-hok e5 poe, lan2 na7 lim, kam2 m7-si7 teh hun-hiong2 Ki-tok e5 hoeh? Lan2 peh-khui hit te3 mi7-pau, lan2 na7 chiah8, kam2 m7-si7 teh hun-hiong2 Ki-tok e5 seng-khu?  17) In-ui7 mi7-pau chiah u7 chit8 ki nia7-nia7, hit ki mi7-pau lan2 tai7-ke long2 u7 hun7; an2-ne lan2 sui-jian5 u7 chin-che7 lang5, tan7-si7 long2 siok8 kang7 chit8 e5 seng-khu.
	18) ChhiaN2 khoaN3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 sin3-giong2 kap in e5 le2-gi5: hiah-e5 chiah8 che3-hian3 liau2-au7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 e5 lang5, kam2 m7-si7 kap in sou2 che3-hian3 Hit-ui7 Siong7-chu2 cho3-hoe2 hiong2-siu7 hiah-e5 che3-phin2?  19) An2-ne goa2 kiu3-keng3 si7 teh kong2 sim2-mih? Si7 teh kong2 che3 ngou2-siong7 liau2-au7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 u7 sim2-mih ke3-tat8? A2-si7 teh kong2 hit e5 ngou2-siong7 chin-chiaN3 u7 chun5-chai7 si7 bo5?  20) M7-si7, goa2 si7 teh kong2 Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 che3-phin2 si7 teh che3 Mou5-kui2, m7-si7 hong7-hian3 hou7 Siong7-chu2. Goa2 bo5 ai3 lin2 kap Mou5-kui2 kau-chhap.  21) Lin2 be7-tang3 lim chu2 e5 poe ia7 boeh lim Mou5-kui2 e5 poe; be7-tang3 chiah8 Chu2 e5 toh ia7 boeh chiah8 Mou5-kui2 e5 toh.  22) Lan2 ai3-boeh kek hou7 chu2 siu7-khi3 si7 bo5? Lan2 kam2 pi2 I khah u7 lat8 si7 bo5?
	23) Lin2 kong2, “Tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 e7-eng7-tit cho3.” Lin2 kong2 an2-ne tioh8, tan7-si7 bo5 it-teng7 tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 u7 li7-ek. Lin2 kong2, “Tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 e7-eng7-tit cho3” --- tan7-si7 bo5 it-teng7 tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 e7 kian3-lip8 tek-hang7.  24) Tak8 lang5 long2 m7-thang tui-kiu5 ka-ti7 e5 li7-ek, tioh8 tui-kiu5 pat8-lang5 e5 li7-ek.
	25) Ti7 bah-tiam3 teh be7 e5 bah cho3 lin2 chiah8, m7-bian2 ui7-tioh8 liong5-sim e5 put-an lai5 mng7-tang-mng7-sai;  26) in-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 kong2, “Chit se3-kai3 kap se3-kai3 ban7-but8 long2 si7 Chu2 e5.”
	27) Ka2-su2 bo5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 chiau-thai7 lin2, lin2 na7 ai3 khi3, pai5 ti7 lin2 bin7-cheng5 e5 mih8 cho3 lin2 chiah8; m7-bian2 ui7-tioh8 liong5-sim e5 put-an lai5 mng7 sim2-mih.  28) Tan7-si7 na7 u7 lang5 ka7 lin2 kong2, “Che si7 khi3 bio7-nih8 pai3 e5,” na7 an2-ne, ui7-tioh8 ka7 lin2 kong2 hit e5 lang5 kap ui7-tioh8 liong5-sim e5 peng5-an, lin2 chiu7 m7-thang chiah8;  29) goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 liong5-sim si7 teh chi2 hit e5 lang5 e5, m7-si7 lin2 ka-ti7 e5.
	Lin2 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 e7 mng7 kong2: “Goa2 e5 chu7-iu5 an3-choaN2 tioh8 hou7 pat8-lang5 e5 liong5-sim lai5 ka7 goa2 chi-phoe3?  30) goa2 na7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 liau2-au7 chiah lai5 kap in cho3-hoe2 chiah8, ui7-tioh8 chiah8 kam2-sia7 liau2 e5 mih8, an3-choaN2 tioh8 siu7 lang5 tiong3-siong?”
	31) Sou2-i2 lin2 bo5-lun7 chiah8 sim2-mih, lim sim2-mih, a2-si7 cho3 sim2-mih, long2 eng-kai ui7-tioh8 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2 lai5 cho3.  32) bo5-lun7 tui3 Iu5-thai3-lang5, Hi-lah8-lang5 a2-si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, mn7-thang eng7 lin2 e5 heng5-ui5 lai5 chou2-gai7 in.  33) Lin2 tioh8 khoaN3 goa2 e5 bou5-iuN7, chai7 kok hong-bin7 chin7-liong7 pang-chou7 cheng3-lang5, bo5 kiu5 ka-ti7 e5 li7-ek, kiu5 cheng3-lang5 e5 li7-ek thang hou7 in tit-tioh8 kiu3.

Te7 11 ChiuN

	1) Chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 oh8 Ki-tok, lin2 ia7 tioh8 oh8 goa2.

Ta3 thau5-khak lai5 keng3-pai3:
	2) Goa2 tioh8 ka7 lin2 o-lo2, in-ui7 lin2 put-si5 e7-ki3-tit goa2, iu7-koh goa2 sou2 thoan5-siu7 hou7 lin2 e5 kau3-hun3, lin2 long2 u7 teh kian-siu2.  3) Tan7-si7 goa2 ai3 lin2 chai-iaN2 Ki-tok si7 cheng3-lang5 e5 thau5, cha-pou-lang5 si7 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 thau5, Siong7-chu2 si7 Ki-tok e5 thau5.  4) Cha-pou-lang5 teh ki5-to2 a2-si7 teh thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 sin3-sek e5 si5, thau5-khak na7 ta3 le3, si7 teh bu2-jiok8 i e5 thau5-khak, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok.  5) Cha-bou2-lang5 teh ki5-to2 a2-si7 teh thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 sin3-sek e5 si5, thau5-khak na7 bo5 ta3 le3, si7 teh bu2-jiok8 i e5 thau5-khak, chiu7-si7 i e5 tiong7-hu.  6) cha-bou2-lang5 e5 thau5-khak na7 bo5 ta3 le3, i e5 thau5-mng5 put-ju5 ka7 i chian2-tiau7 khah ho2; tan7-si7 cha-bou2-lang5 na7 siuN7 kong2 thau5-mng5 ka7 i chian2-tiau7 a2-si7 thi3-tiau7 chin kian3-siau3, thau5-khak chiu7 tioh8 ta3 le3.  7) cha-pou-lang5 e5 thau5-khak bo5 eng-kai ta3 le3, in-ui7 cha-pou-lang5 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 siau3-siong7 kap eng5-iau7; tan7-si7 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 cha-pou-lang5 e5 eng5-iau7.  8) Cha-pou-lang5 m7-si7 tui3 cha-bou2-lang5 cho7 chhut-lai5 e5, m7-ku2 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 tui3 cha-pou-lang5 chhong3-cho7 chhut-lai5 e5;  9) cha-pou-lang5 m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 cha-bou2-lang5 chiah siu7 chhong3-cho7 e5, tan7-si7 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 ui7-tioh8 cha-pou-lang5 chiah siu7 chhong3-cho7 e5.  10) Sou2-i2 cha-bou2-lang5 tioh8 ta3 se-a2-kin lai5 piau2-si7 u7 koan5-peng3 ti7 i e5 thau5-khak teng2, che ia7-si7 ui7-tioh8 keng3-ui3 chiong3thian-sai3.  11) Tan7-si7 chiau3 Chu2 e5 an-pai5, cha-bou2-lang5 bo5 i-nai7 cha-pou-lang5 be7-eng7-tit, cha-pou-lang5 bo5 cha-bou2-lang5 ia7 be7-eng7-tit;  12) in-ui7 chhin-chhiuN7 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 tui3 cha-pou-lang5 lai5 chhong3-cho7 chhut-lai5 e5, cha-pou-lang5 ia7-si7 tui3 cha-bou2-lang5 lai5 chhut-si3 e5; tan7-si7 ban7-but8 si7 Siong7-chu2 chhong3-cho7 e5.
	13) Lin2 tioh8 ka-ti7 phoaN3-toan3 khoaN3-bai7 le3, cha-bou2-lang5 thau5-khak bo5 ta3 le3, lai5 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 kam2 u7 sek-hap8?  14) chiau3 lang5 e5 pun2-seng3, cha-pou-lang5 e5 thau5-mng5 na7 tng5, kam2 be7 hou7 lin2 kam2-kak chin kian3-siau3?  15) Tan7-si7 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 thau5-mng5 na7 tng5, kam2 m7-si7 i e5 kong-eng5? In-ui7 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 tng5 thau5-mng5 si7 siuN2-su3 hou7 i lai5 jia-kham3 e5.  16) Na7 u7 lang5 siuN7-boeh pian7-pok chit hang7 tai7-chi3, goa2 chi2-u7 e7-tang3 kong2 ti7 goan2 tiong-kan kap Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7 lai7, kan-taN u7 chit khoan2 sip8-koan3 nai7-nia7.

Chu2 e5 Boan2-chhan:
	17) goa2 boeh ka7 lin2 chi2-si7 e7-bin7 hit kui2 hang7 e5 si5, m7-si7 boeh ka7 lin2 o-lo2, in-ui7 lin2 e5 chu7-hoe7, pe3-hai7 pi2 li7-ek khah che7.  18) Te7-it, goa2 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2 kau3-hoe7 chu7-hoe7 e5 si5, lin2 tiong-kan u7 hun-liat8. Che goa2 tam7-poh8-a2 u7 siong-sin3.  19) In-ui7 lin2 tiong-kan lan5-bian2 u7 put-tong5 e5 i3-kian3, an2-ne lin2 tiong-kan chin-chiaN3 e7-kho3-tit e5 lang5 chiah e7 hian2-beng5 chhut-lai5.  20) Lin2 chu7-chip8 e5 si5, m7-si7 lai5 boeh chiah8 chu2 e5 Boan2-chhan;  21) in-ui7 tak8 e5 koaN2-kin2 chiah8 i ka-ti7 e5 am3-tng3, sou2-i2 u7-e5 pak-tou2 teh iau, u7-e5 lim kah chiu2-chui3.  22) lin2 toa3 chhu3-nih kam2 be7-tang3 chiah8, be7-tang3 lim? lin2 si7 boeh khoaN3-khin Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, hou7 san3-lang5 kian3-siau3 si7 bo5? Goa2 tioh8 tui3 lin2 kong2 sim2-mih? goa2 tioh8 ka7 lin2 o-lo2 si7 bo5? Tui3 chit hang7 tai7-chi3 goa2 bo5 boeh ka7 lin2 o-lo2.
	23) Goa2 sou2 thoan5-siu7 hou7 lin2 e5, si7 tui3 Chu2 nia2-siu7 e5: chiu7-si7 kong2 Chu2 Ia5-sou hou7 lang5 chhut-be7 hit chit8 am3, theh8 mi7-pau khi2-lai5,  24) kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 liau2-au7 peh-khui kong2, “Che si7 Goa2 e5 seng-khu, si7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 hi-seng e5. Lin2 tioh8 an2-ne cho3, lai5 ki3-liam7 Goa2.”  25) Boan2-chhan au7 I ia7 chiau3 an2-ne, phang5 poe khi2-lai5 kong2, “chit poe si7 sin e5 beng5-iok, si7 eng7 Goa2 e5 hoeh sou2 lip8 e5. Lin2 tak8 pai2 lim e5 si5, tioh8 an2-ne cho3, lai5 ki3-liam7 Goa2.”  26) In-ui7 it-tit8 kau3 Chu2 e5 chai3-lim5, lin2 tak8 pai2 chiah8 chit te3 mi7-pau, lim chit poe, chiu7-si7 teh soan-pou3 I e5 si2-bong5.
	27) Sou2-i2 bo5-lun7 sim2-mih-lang5, na7 bo5 hap8-sek lai5 chiah8 hit te3 mi7-pau, a2-si7 lim Chu2 e5 poe, chiu7 e7 siat-tok8 tioh8 Chu2 e5 seng-khu kap hoeh.  28) Lang5 tioh8 tai7-seng chu7-ki2 seng2-chhat, chiah lai5 chiah8 hit te3 mi7-pau, lim hit poe;  29) in-ui7 na7 bo5 chheng-chheng-chho2-chho2 jim7-sek Chu2 e5 seng-khu, teh chiah8 teh lim e5 si5, chiu7-si7 teh phoaN3 i ka-ti7 e5 choe7.  30) Chiu7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, lin2 chiah e7 chin-che7 lang5 loan2-jiok8, phoa3-peN7, c hiah e7 chit8-koa2 lang5 si2-khi3.  31) Lan2 na7 u7 tai7-seng seng2-chhat, chiu7 be7 an2-ne lai5 siu7 sim2-phoaN3;  32) tan7-si7 ki3-jian5 Chu2 teh ka7 lan2 sim2-phoaN3, I si7 teh ka7 lan2 koan2-kau3, hou7 lan2 m7-bian2 kap se3-kan-lang5 cho3-hoe2 siu7 teng7-choe7.
	33) Sou2-i2 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 chu7-chip8 cho3-hoe2 boeh chiah8 Chu2 e5 Boan2-chhan e5 si5, tioh8 sio-tan2.  34) Na7 u7 lang5 pak-tou2 iau, tioh8 toa3 chhu3-nih tai7-seng chiah8, an2-ne lin2 e7 chu7-hoe7 chiah be7 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 phoaN3-choe7. Ki5-tha e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5 chiah lai5 kai2-koat.

Te7 12 ChiuN

Seng3 Leng5 e5 in-su3:
1) TaN lai5 lun7 koan-he7 leng5-seng3 e5 in-su3.
	Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 ai3 lin2 khah chheng-chho2 le7.  2) Lin2 chai-iaN2 lin2 iau2-boe7 sin3 Chu2 e5 si5, chhut-chai7 lang5 ka7 lin2 in2-iu2 khi3 pai3 be7 kong2-oe7 e5 ngou2-siong7.  3) Sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 lin2 beng5-pek8, siu7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 Seng3 Leng5 kam2-tong7 e5 lang5 bo5 chit8 e5 e7 kong2, “Ia5-sou tioh8 siu7 chiu3-me7”, iu7-koh ti5-liau2 siu7-tioh8 Seng3 Leng5 kam2-tong7 i2-goa7, bo5 lang5 e7 kong2, “Ia5-sou si7 Chu2.”
	4) In-su3 u7 chin-che7 khoan2, tan7-si7 siuN2-su3 in-su3 e5 Seng3 Leng5 kang7 chit8 ui7.  5) Su7-hong7 e5 hong-sek u7 chin-che7 khoan2, tan7-si7 long2 su7-hong7 kang7 chit8 ui7 Chu2.  6) Chok-iong7 e5 hong-sek u7 chin-che7 khoan2, tan7-si7 iong7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 hong-sek tui3 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 teh chok-iong7 e5, si7 kang7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-chu2.  7) Seng3 Leng5 chai7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 sin-siong7 sou2 hian2-si7 e5, sui-jian5 chit8 lang5 chit8 khoan2, tan7-si7 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 cheng3-lang5 e5 li7-ek.  8) Seng3 Leng5 siuN2-su3 chit8 e5 lang5 kong2 to7-li2 chin u7 ti3-hui7, kang7 hit ui7 Seng3 Leng5 siuN2-su3 leng7-goa7 chit8 e5 lang5 kau3-hun3 lang5 chin u7 ti3-sek,  9) kang7 chit8 ui7 Seng3 Leng5 siuN2-su3 sin3-sim hou7 chit e5, kang7 hit ui7 Seng3 Leng5 siuN2-su3 i-peN7 e5 in-su3 hou7 hit e5,  10) siuN2-su3 chit8 e5 hian2 sin5-chek, chit8 e5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5, chit8 e5 e7-hiau2 hun-piat8 chin-sin5 kap ke2-sin5, chit8 e5 e7 jip8-sin5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 oe7-gi2, chit8 e5 e7-hiau2 kai2-soeh ki5-biau7 e5 oe7-gi2.  11) Chiah-e5 in-su3 long2 si7 kang7 chit8 ui7 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5, chiu7-si7 chiau3 I e5 i3-su3 hun-phoe3 hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5.

Chit8 e5 seng-khu u7 chin-che7 pou7-hun7:
	12) Chhin-chhiuN7 seng-khu chiah u7 chit8 e5 nia7-nia7, m7-ku2 u7 chin-che7 pou7-hun7, seng-khu sou2 u7 e5 pou7-hun7 sui-jian5 hiah-nih8 che7, ki5-sit8 si7 it-the2, Ki-tok ia7-si7 an2-ne.  13) In-ui7 iu5 chit8 ui7 Seng3 Leng5 lan2 tai7-ke siu7 se2-le2 lai5 chiaN5-cho3 it-the2, bo5-lun7 lan2 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 a2-si7 goa7-pang-lang5, si7 lou5-le7 a2-si7 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5, long2 nia2-siu7 kang7 chit8 ui7 Seng3 Leng5.
	14) Seng-khu m7-si7 kan-taN chit8 e5 pou7-hun7 nia7-nia7, u7 chin-che7 pou7-hun7.  15) Ka2-su2 kha na7 kong2, “In-ui7 goa2 m7-si7 chhiu2, sou2-i2 goa2 bo5 siok8 ti7 seng-khu,” ui7-tioh8 chit e5 li2-iu5 kha be7-eng7-tit kong2 bo5 siok8 ti7 seng-khu.  16) Ka2-su2 hiN7-a2 na7 kong2, “In-ui7 goa2 m7-si7 bak8-chiu, sou2-i2 goa2 bo5 siok8 ti7 seng-khu,” ui7-tioh8 chit e5 li2-iu5 hiN7-a2 be7-eng7-tit kong2 bo5 siok8 ti7 seng-khu.  17) Ka2-su2 cheng2-ko5 e5 seng-khu na7-si7 chit8 lui2 bak8-chiu, an2-ne boeh eng7 sim2-mih lai5 thiaN? Ka2-su2 cheng2-ko5 e5 seng-khu na7-si7 chit8 e5 hiN7-a2, an2-ne boeh eng7 sim2-mih lai5 phiN7?  18) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 chiau3 I e5 chi2-i3 chiong kok pou7-hun7 an2-ne ka7 lan2 an-pai5toa3 seng-khu.  19) Kok pou7-hun7 ka2-su2 long2 si7 kang7 chit8 khoan2 e5, an2-ne na2 u7 chiaN5 chit8 e5 seng-khu?  20) Seng-khu sui-jian5 u7 chiah-nih8 che7 pou7-hun7, ki5-sit8 si7 it-the2.  21) Bak8-chiu be7-eng7-tit tui3 chhiu2 kong2, “Goa2 bo5 khiam3-eng7 li2;” thau5-khak ia7 be7-eng7-tit tui3 kha kong2, “Goa2 bo5 khiam3-eng7 li2.”  22) Be7-eng7-tit an2-ne. Lan2 khoaN3-cho3 seng-khu khah loan2-jiok8 e5 pou7-hun7, tian-to3 khah iau3-kin2.  23) Lan2 siuN7-cho3 pi-chian7 e5 pou7-hun7, tioh8 chng hou7 khah chun-kui3. Khah bai2 e5 pou7-hun7 tioh8 chng hou7 khah ho2-khoaN3,  24) tan7-si7 ho2-khoaN3 e5 pou7-hun7 chiu7 bo5 su-iau3 chng. Siong7-chu2 chiong seng-khu e5 kok pou7-hun7 tau3 cho3-hoe2, siuN2-su3 khah toa7 e5 kong-eng5 hou7 khah bo5 kong-eng5 e5 pou7-hun7,  25) thang hou7 seng-khu be7 hun-liat8, hou7 kok pou7-hun7 hou7-siong koan-chiau3.  26) Na7 u7 chit8 pou7-hun7 kan-khou2, ki5-tha e5 pou7-hun7 chiu7 long2 toe3 i kan-khou2; na7 u7 chit8 pou7-hun7 siu7 o-lo2, ki5-tha e5 pou7-hun7 chiu7 long2 kap i cho3-hoe2 hoaN-hi2.
	27) Lin2 tai7-ke si7 Ki-tok e5 seng-khu. Mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 si7 chit e5 seng-khu e5 chit8 pou7-hun7.  28) Siong7-chu2 chai7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 sou2 an-pai5 e5: te7-it si7 Su3-tou5, te7-ji7 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5, te7-saN si7 kau3-su, jian5-au7 si7 hian2 sin5-chek e5 lang5, koh koe3-khi3 si7 tit-tioh8 in-su3 lai5 i-peN7 e5, pang-chou7 pat8-lang5 e5, pan7 heng5-cheng3 e5, i2-kip8 jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5.  29) In kam2 long2 si7 Su3-tou5? In kam2 long2 si7 tai7-gian5-jin5? In kam2 long2 si7 kau3-su? In kam2 long2 e7 hian2 sin5-chek?  30) Kam2 long2 u7 tit-tioh8 i-peN7 e5 in-su3? Kam2 long2 e7 jip8-sin5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2? Kam2 long2 e7-hiau2 kai2-soeh ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2?  31) Lin2 tioh8 chin7-lek8 tui-kiu5 koh-khah ho2 e5 in-su3.
	TaN goa2 boeh lai5 ka7 lin2 chi2-si7 chit8 tiau5 choe3-ho2 e5 lou7:

Te7 13 ChiuN

Jin5-ai3:
	1) Ka2-su2 goa2 cheng-thong lang5 kok-chiong2 e5 oe7-gi2, sim7-chi3 e7 kong2 thian-sai3 e5 gi2-gian5, tan7-si7 na7 bo5 jin5-ai3, goa2 kong2-oe7 be7 su teh kong3 toa7-lo5 a2-si7 teh phah chhim2-chheN5.  2) Ka2-su2 goa2 u7 tit-tioh8 in-su3 thang thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, iu7-koh liau2-kai2 it-chhe3 e5 o3-pi3, u7 it-chhe3 e5 ti3-sek, ka2-su2 goa2 u7 chin toa7 e5 sin3-sim thang i5-san to3 hai2, tan7-si7 na7 bo5 jin5-ai3, goa2 chiu7 long2 bo5 lou7-eng7.  3) Ka2-su2 goa2 chiong goa2 sou2 u7 e5, theh8 chhut-lai5 kiu3-che3 lang5, sim7-chi3 hi-seng goa2 e5 seng-khu lai5 hou7 lang5 sio, tan7-si7 na7 bo5 jin5-ai3, chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 tui3 goa2 choan5-jian5 bo5 li7-ek.
	4) Jin5-ai3 chiu7-si7 khoan-iong5, chhin-chhiat, jin5-ai3 chiu7-si7 bo5 chit-tou3, bo5 chu7-khoa, bo5 kiau-ngou7,  5) bo5 cho3 bo5 le2-mau7 e5 tai7-chi3, bo5 chu7-su chu7-li7, bo5 phaiN2 seng3-te7, bo5 khiok hin7.  6) Jin5-ai3 bo5 hoan-geng5 put-gi7, jiat8-ai3 chin-li2,  7) jim2-siu7 it-chhe3, tui3 ban7-su7 u7 sin3-sim, tui3 ban7-su7 u7 hi-bong7, tui3 ban7-su7 u7 jim2-nai7.
	8) Jin5-ai3 si7 eng2-kiu2 chun5-siok8 e5. Tan7-si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 in-su3 e7 bo5-khi3. Jip8-sin5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5 in-su3 e7 theng5-chi2; ti3-sek ia7 e7 bo5-khi3;  9) in-ui7 lan2 e5 ti3-sek bo5 oan5-choan5, lan2 sou2 thoan5-tat8 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 ia7 si7 chit8 pou7-hun7 nia7-nia7;  10) tan7-si7 it-tan3 oan5-choan5 e5 na7 lai5, bo5 oan5-choan5 e5 chiu7 e7 siau-sit khi3.  11) Goa2 cho3 gin2-na2 e5 si5, goa2 e5 kong2-hoat, siuN7-hoat i2-kip8 li2-lun7 long2 chin iu3-ti7; taN goa2 ki3-jian5 si7 toa7-lang5 a7, goa2 chiong iu3-ti7 e5 chok-hong long2 hong3-khi3.  12) Lan2 hian7-chai7 chhin-chhiuN7 teh chio3-kiaN3, bu7-bu7 khoaN3 be7 beng5, tan7-si7 kau3 hit e5 si5-chun7, e7 tit8-chiap bin7 tui3 bin7 sio-kiN3; hian7-chai7 goa2 e5 ti3-sek bo5 oan5-choan5, tan7-si7 kau3 hit e5 si5-chun7, goa2 e7 oan5-choan5 chai-iaN2, chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-chu2 oan5-choan5 chai-iaN2 goa2 e5 it-chhe3.  13) TaN chit saN hang7 e7 eng2-oan2 chun5-siok8: chiu7-si7 sin3-tek, hi-bong7 kap jin5-ai3; ki5-tiong choe3 ui2-tai7 e5 chiu7-si7 jin5-ai3.

Te7 14 ChiuN

Seng3-Leng5 e5 in-su3:
	1) Lin2 tioh8 tui-kiu5 jin5-ai3, ia7 tioh8 chin7-lek8 khat-bong7 leng5-seng3 e5 in-su3, tek8-piat8 si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 in-su3.  2) In-ui7 jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5 lang5, m7-si7 teh kap lang5 kong2, si7 teh kap Siong7-chu2 kong2 e5. I sui-jian5 siu7-tioh8 Seng3 Leng5 e5 chi2-si7 teh kong2 o3-pi3 e5 chin-li2, tan7-si7 bo5 lang5 thiaN u7.  3) Tan7-si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 lang5, si7 tui3 lang5 teh kong2-oe7, lai5 kian3-lip8 lang5 e5 tek-heng7, lai5 kou2-le7 lang5, lai5 an-ui3 lang5.  4) Jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5 lang5, chi2-u7 kian3-lip8 i ka-ti7 e5 tek-heng7; tan7-si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 lang5, e7 cho7-chiu7 kau3-hoe7.  5) Goa2 chin ai3 lin2 long2 e7 jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, tan7-si7 koh-khah hi-bong7 lin2 e7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3. Jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5 lang5, na7 bo5 lang5 kai2-sek i sou2 kong2 e5, lai5 cho7-chiu7 kau3-hoe7e5 tek-heng7, an2-ne thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 lang5 chiu7 pi2 i khah ui2-tai7.  6) Sou2-i2 chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, ka2-su2 goa2 khi3 lin2 hia, na7 kan-taN jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, bo5 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 khe2-si7, a2-si7 hou7 lin2 sin e5 ti3-sek, a2-si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, a2-si7 ka7 lin2 kau3-hun3, an2-ne tui3 lin2 u7 sim2-mih li7-ek?  7) Kap an2-ne kang7-khoan2, bo5 seN3-mia7 e5 gak8-khi3, chhin-chhiuN7 siau a2-si7 khim5, im-tiau7 na7 bo5 pian3-hoa3, lang5 chiu7 m7-chai si7 teh chau3 sim2-mih khek.  8) La-pa e5 im-tiau7 na7 bo5 chun2-khak, sim2-mih lang5 boeh chun2-pi7 kau-chian3?  9) Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne, lin2 jip8-sin5 sou2 kong2 chhut-lai5 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, i3-su3 na7 bo5 chheng-chho2, sim2-mih lang5 e7-tang3 liau2-kai2 lin2 sou2 kong2 e5? Lin2 be7 su hiong3 khong-tiong teh kong2-oe7.  10) Se3-kan u7 chin-che7 chiong2 e5 gi2-gian5, tak8 chiong2 long2 u7 i3-su3.  11) Sou2-i2 goa2 na7 m7-bat sou2 teh kong2 e5 gi2-gian5 e5 i3-su3, goa2 tui3 hit e5 teh kong2 e5 lang5 si7 chit8 e5 goa7-kok-lang5. Hit e5 teh kong2 e5 lang5, tui3 goa2 ia7 si7 chit8 e5 goa7-kok-lang5.  12) Sou2-i2 lin2 ki3-jian5 chin7-lek8 teh tui-kiu5 Seng3 Leng5 e5 in-su3, lin2 koh-khah tioh8 chin7-liong7 sian7-iong7 hiah-e5 in-su3, lai5 cho7-chiu7 kau3-hoe7.
	13) Sou2-i2 jip8-sin5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 e5 lang5, tioh8 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 i in-su3 lai5 kai2-sek i sou2 kong2 e5.  14) goa2 na7 an2-ne lai5 ki5-to2, he si7 goa2 er5 sim-leng5 teh ki5-to2, tan7-si7 goa2 e5 li2-ti3 be7 tit-tioh8 hau7-ko2.  15) An2-ne eng-kai tioh8 an3-choaN2 cho3? goa2 boeh eng7 sim-leng5 lai5 ki5-to2, ia7 boeh eng7 li2-ti3 lai5 ki5-to2; goa2 boeh eng7 sim-leng5 lai5 chan3-siong7, ia7 boeh eng7 li2-ti3 lai5 chan3-siong7.  16) In-ui7 lin2 na7 chi2-u7 eng7 sim-leng5 lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, chham-ka chu7-hoe7 e5 phou2-thong-lang5 kah-si7 m7-chai lin2 teh kong2 sim2-mih, lin2 teh kam2-sia7 e5 si5 kam2 e7-tang3 kong2 “Amen”?  17) Li2 kam2-sia7 e5 ki5-to2 sui-jian5 chin ho2, tan7-si7 be7-tang3 hou7 pat8-lang5 kian3-lip8 tek-heng7.  18) Goa2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 hou7 goa2 jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2 pi2 lin2 tai7-ke kong2 khah-che7;  19) tan7-si7 kau3-hoe7 chu7-hoe7 e5 si5, goa2 leng5-kho2 kong2 gou7 ku3 hou7 lang5 thiaN u7 e5 oe7 lai5 kau3-hun3 pat8-lang5, bo5 ai3 jip8-sin5 lai5 kong2 chit8 ban7 ku3 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2.
	20) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, siuN7 tai7-chi3 m7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 gin2-na2 hiah-nih8 iu3-ti7. Koan-he7 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 ang5-eN-a2 hiah-nih8 chheng-pek8, tan7-si7 siuN7 tai7-chi3 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 toa7-lang5 hiah-nih8 lau2-lian7.  21) Lut8-hoat e5 chheh u7 ki3-chai3 kong2: Chu2 kong2,
“Goa2 boeh eng7 goa7-kok-lang5 e5 chhui3-chih8, Boeh eng7 i7-pang-lang5 e5 chhui3-tun5, Lai5 tui3 chit e5 bin5-chok8 kong2-oe7. Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ne, in iau2-ku2 e7 m7 thiaN Goa2 e5 chhui3.”
22) Sou2-i2 jip8-sin5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, m7-si7 ui7 sin3-chia2, si7 ui7 bi7-sin3-chia2 lai5 cheng3-beng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-leng5, tan7-si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 bi7-sin3-chia2, si7 ui7-tioh8 sin3-chia2 lai5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 tai7-leng5 e5 cheng3-ki3.  23) Sou2-i2 choan5 kau3-hoe7 chu7-chip8 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 na7 jip8-sin5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, phou2-thong-lang5 a2-si7 bi7-sin3-chia2 chit8-e7 jip8-lai5, kam2 be7 kong2 lin2 si7 teh khi2-siau2?  24) Tan7-si7 tai7-ke na7 long2 teh thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 si5, bi7-sin3-chia2 a2-si7 phou2-thong-lang5 chit8-e7 jip8-lai5, thiaN-tioh8 hiah-e5 lang5 e5 oe7, e7 chu7-kak i pun2-sin e5 choe7, lai5 ka-ti7 phoaN3-choe7,  25) i sim-lai7 e5 pi3-bit8 chiu7 e7 hian2-lou7 chhut-lai5. An2-ne i chiu7 e7 phak loh8-khi3 keng3-pai3 Siong7-chu2, soan-gian5 kong2, “Siong7-chu2 sit8-chai7 u7 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan.”

Kau3-hoe7 e5 tiat8-su7:
	26) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, an2-ne eng-kai tioh8 an3-choaN2? Lin2 chu7-hoe7 e5 si5, mui2 chit8 e5 long2 u7 sim2-mih thang kong3-hian3, u7 lang5 chhiuN3 seng3-si, u7-e5 kau3-hun3 lang5, u7-e5 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 khe2-si7, u7 lang5 e7 jip8-sin5 kong2-chhut ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, u7 lang5 e7 ka7 i kai2-sek; chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 long2 tioh8 ui7 cho7-chiu7 kau3-hoe7 lai5 cho3.  27) Na7 u7 lang5 jip8-sin5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2, tioh8 han7-tiaN7 nng7 e5, siong7-che7 saN e5 lun5-liu5 kong2, ia7 tioh8 u7 chit8 e5 thang ka7 in kai2-sek;  28) ka2-su2 bo5 lang5 thang ka7 in kai2-sek, in chai7 chu7-hoe7 tiong chiu7 tioh8 tiam7-tiam7, lai5 tui3 i ka-ti7 a2-si7 tui3 Siong7-chu2 kong2.  29) Tioh8 hou7 nng7 e5 a2-si7 saN e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5 lai5 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, hou7 ki5-tha e5 lang5 lai5 phoaN3-toan3 in sou2 kong2 e5.  30) Tan7-si7 che7-teh chham-ka chu7-hoe7 e5 lang5, na7 u7 lang5 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 khe2-si7, hit e5 teh kong2 e5 lang5, tioh8 theng5-chi2 kong2-oe7.  31) Lin2 tai7-ke long2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3, thang hou7 cheng3-lang5 tit-tioh8 kau3-hun3 kap kou2-le7.  32) Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5 tioh8 ka-ti7 cho3-chu2 lai5 khong3-che3 i sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 leng5-kam2,  33) in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 m7-si7 bo5 tiat8-su7 e5 Siong7-chu2, si7 ho5-peng5 e5 Siong7-chu2.
	34) Chu7-hoe7 e5 tiong-kan hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8 tiam7-tiam7, in-ui7 in be7-eng7-tit kong2-oe7, che si7 it-chhe3 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5 kau3-hoe7 e5 koan3-le7. Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 lut8-hoat ia7 u7 ki3-chai3 kong2 hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8-ai3 hok8-chiong5.  35) In na7 ai3 hak8-sip8 sim2-mih, tioh8 toa3 chhu3-nih mng7 ka-ti7 e5 tiong7-hu, in-ui7 hu7-jin5-lang5 chai7 chu7-hoe7 tiong kong2-oe7 chin bo5 sek-hap8.
	36) Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-to7 kam2 si7 tui3 lin2 chhut-lai5 e5? A2-si7 kan-taN thoan5 hou7 lin2 nia7-nia7?  37) Na7 u7 lang5 siuN7-cho3 i si7 thoan5-tat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 e5 lang5, a2-si7 u7 leng5-seng3 e5 in-su3 e5 lang5, i tioh8 jim7-sek goa2 sou2 sia2 hou7 lin2 e5 si7 Chu2 e5 beng7-leng7.  38) Na7 u7 lang5 bu5-si7 chit e5 tai7-chi3, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 e7 ka7 i bu5-si7.
	39) Sou2-i2 chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, lin2 tioh8 tui-kiu5 cho3 tai7-gian5-jin5 e5 in-su3, m7-thang kim3-chi2 lang5 jip8-sin5 kong2 ki5-biau7 e5 gian5-gi2,  40) tak8 hang7 tai7-chi3 tioh8 hap8-gi5 an3-chiau3 tiat8-su7 lai5 cho3.

Te7 15 ChiuN

Ki-tok e5 hok8-hoat8:
	1) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 i2-cheng5 thoan5 hou7 lin2 e5 Hok-im, goa2 boeh koh ka7 lin2 the5-chheN2. Chit e5 Hok-im lin2 u7 chiap-siu7, ia7 u7 oa2-kho3 chit e5 Hok-im lai5 kian-kou3 lin2 e5 sin3-sim.  2) Lin2 na7 kian-siu2 goa2 sou2 thoan5 hou7 lin2 e5 Hok-im, chit e5 Hok-im chiu7 e7 hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 kiu3 --- na7 bo5, lin2 sin3-liau2 chiu7 bo5 tat8-tioh8 bok8-tek.
	3) Goa2 i2-keng chiong goa2 pun2-sin sou2 nia2-siu7 tioh8 e5, chiu7-si7 choe3 tiong7-iau3 e5 thoan5 hou7 lin2, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3, ui7 lan2 e5 choe7-ok si2-bong5;   4) siu7 bai5-chong3; iu7-koh chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3, te7 saN jit8 Siong7-chu2 hou7 I koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5;  5) I hian2-hian7 hou7 Pi2-tek khoaN3, jian5-au7 hou7 chap8-ji7 Su3-tou5 khoaN3.  6) Jian5-au7 I hian2-hian7, kang7-si5 hou7 gou7-pah-goa7 e5 hiaN-ti7 khoaN3, hiah-e5 lang5 sui-jian5 u7-e5 i2-keng si2-khi3 a3, tan7-si7 toa7-pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 hian7-chai7 iau2 teh oah8.  7) I2-au7 I hian2-hian7 hou7 Nga2-kok khoaN3, jian5-au7 hian2-hian7 hou7 long2-chong2 e5 Su3-tou5 khoaN3.
	8) Goa2 sui-jian5 si7 chit8 e5 chhin-chhiuN7 iau2-boe7 kau3-goeh8 chiu7 chhut-si3 e5 lang5, choe3-au7 I ia7 u7 hian2-hian7 hou7 goa2 khoaN3.  9) Goa2 si7 su3-tou5 tiong siong7 se3 e5. Goa2 be7-kham-tit hou7 lang5 chheng-hou cho3 Su3-tou5, in-ui7 goa2 i2-cheng5 u7 pek-hai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7.  10) Tan7-si7 u7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 chiah u7 kin-na2-jit8 e5 goa2, I sou2 siuN2-su3 goa2 e5 in-hui7 peng3 bo5 ong2-hui3 khi3. goa2 tian-to3 pi2 in tai7-ke khah sin-khou2 kang-chok, tan7-si7 sit8-chai7 m7-si7 goa2 e5 pun2-su7, si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 ti3-im3 goa2 e5.  11) Sou2-i2 bo5-lun7 si7 goa2 a2-si7 in, che chiu7-si7 goan2 sou2 soan-iong5 e5, che ia7-si7 lin2 sou2 siong-sin3 e5.

Lan2 e5 hok8-hoat8:
	12) Chai7-lai5 goan2 soan-iong5 kong2 Ki-tok tui3 si2-lang5 e5 tiong-kan koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, lin2 an3-choaN2 u7 lang5 kong2 bo5 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3?  13) Na7 bo5 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 bo5 hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 chiah tioh8;  14) Siong7-chu2 na7 bo5 hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, an2-ne goan2 sou2 soan-iong5 e5 si7 khang-hi e5, lin2 sou2 siong-sin3 e5 chiu7-si7 bo5 kin-ki3 e5.  15) Put-tan7 si7 an2-ne, goan2 chiu7 piN3-cho3 teh ka7 Siong7-chu2 cho3 ke2 cheng3-jin5, in-ui7 goan2 ka7 Siong7-chu2 cho3-cheng3 kong2 I hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2-lai5; tan7-si7 na7 bo5 si2-lang5 sit8-chai7 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 bo5 hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2 lai5 chiah tioh8.  16) In-ui7 na7 bo5 hou7 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 bo5 hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2-lai5;  17) ka2-su2 Siong7-chu2 bo5 hou7 Ki-tok koh-oah8 khi2-lai5, lin2 e5 sin3-giong2 chiu7-si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5, lin2 chiu7 iau2-ku2 ti7 choe7-ok-tiong teh seng-oah8.  18) Na7 an2-ne hiah-e5 si2-khi3 e5 Ki-tok-tou5, ia7 i2-keng biat8-bong5 a7.  19) Lan2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 hi-bong7, na7-si7 kan-taN han7-tiaN7 chai7 hian7-se3 nia7-nia7, lan2 chiu7-si7 jin5-lui7 e5 tiong-kan siong7 kho2-lin5 e5.
	20) Tan7-si7 su7-sit8-siong7 Siong7-chu2 i2-keng hou7 Ki-tok tui3 si2-lang5 e5 tiong-kan koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 a3, che chiu7-si7 teh po2-cheng3 Siong7-chu2 e7 hou7 si2-khi3 e5 lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5.  21) In-ui7 ki3-jian5 si2-bong5 thong-koe3 chit8 e5 lang5 lai5, si2-lang5 e5 hok8-hoat8 ia7 thong-koe3 chit8 e5 lang5 lai5.  22) Tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 it-chhe3 e5 lang5 ui7-tioh8 A-tong hoan7-choe7 lai5 si2-bong5, in ia7 e7 in-ui7 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8, si2 liau2-au7 koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5.  23) Tan7-si7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 chiau3 sun7-su7: Ki-tok si7 thau5 chit8 e5, jian5-au7 I lai5-lim5 e5 si5, si7 hiah-e5 siok8 Ki-tok e5 lang5.  24) Koh koe3-khi3 boat8-jit8 e7 lai5. Hit e5 si5, I chiong it-chhe3 e5 koan5-peng3, koan5-ui, koan5-lek8 siau-biat8 liau2-au7, e7 chiong Ong5-kok kau hou7 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7.  25) In-ui7 Ki-tok tioh8 thong2-ti7 kau3 Siong7-chu2 hou7 it-chhe3 e5 tek8-jin5 lai5 khut-hok8 toa3 I e5 kha-e7.  26) Choe3-au7 tioh8 siu7 hui2-biat8 e5 tek8-jin5 si7 si2-bong5.  27) In-ui7 Seng3-kneg u7 kong2, “Siong7-chu2 hou7 ban7-but8 khut-hok8 toa3 Ki-tok e5 kha-e7.” Tong-jian5 hit ku3 “Ban7-but8 beng5-beng5 bo5 pau-ham5 Siong7-chu2 pun2-sin, in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 si7 hou7 ban7-but8 lai5 khut-hok8 toa3 Ki-tok e5 kha-e7 e5.  28) Tan7-si7 ban7-but8 long2 kui-sun7 Ki-tok liau2-au7, Seng3 Chu2, Ki-tok pun2-sin e7 lai5 kui Siong7-chu2 koan2-hat, chiu7 si7 lai5 kui Hit-ui7 hou7 ban7-but8 lai5 kui-sun7 Ki-tok e5 Siong7-chu2. An2-ne Siong7-chu2 chiu7 thang lai5 oan5-chaon5 koan2-hat ban7-but8.
	29) Si2-lang5 na7 be7 hok8-hoat8, hiah-e5 ui7 si2-lang5 siu7 se2-le2 e5, si7 ui7-tioh8 sim2-mih bok8-tek? Na7 long2 bo5 si2-lang5 koh0-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3, in an3-choaN2 boeh ui7 si2-lang5 siu7 se2-le2?  30) Goan2 an3-choaN2 ia7 tioh8 si5-si5-khek-khek lai5 mou5-hiam2?  31) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, lan2 peN5-peN5 sin3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 lang5, lin2 si7 goa2 e5 khoa-iau7. Tng chit e5 khoa-iau7 goa2 kaN2 kong2, goa2 tak8 jit8 teh mou7 se3-mia7 e5 gui5-hiam2.  32) Goa2 chai7 I2-hut-sou2 kap chhin-chhiuN7 ia2-siu3 hiah-e5 lang5 tou3-cheng. He na7-si7 kan-taN chiau3 se3-kan-lang5 e5 tong7-ki, kiu3-keng3 tui3 goa2 u7 sim2-mih li7-ek? Na7 bo5 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7 tioh8 chiau3 in sou2 kong2 e5, “Lan2 e7 chiah8 tioh8 chiah8, e7 lim tioh8 lim, kin-siok8 bin5-na2-chai3 tioh8 si2.”  33) Lin2 m7-thang hou7 lang5 phian3-khi3:
“Kau phaiN2 peng5-iu2 e7 pho3-hoai7 ho2 tek-heng7.”
34) Lin2 tioh8 kak-chheN2; m7-thang koh hoan7-choe7, in-ui7 u7 lang5 bo5 jim7-sek Siong7-chu2. Goa2 kong2 an2-ne si7 boeh hou7 lin2 kian3-siau3 e5.

Jiok8-the2 e5 hok8-hoat8:
	35) M7-ku2 u7 lang5 e7 mng7 kong2, “Boeh an3-choaN2 hou7 si2-lang5 koh-oah8 khi2-lai5? In boeh eng7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5 seng-khu lai5?”  36) Gong7-lang5! Lin2 ia7 loh8-khi3 e5 cheng2-chi2 na7 bo5 si2-khi3, chiu7 be7 koh hoat khi2-lai5.  37) Lin2 sou2 ia7 loh8-khi3 e5 cheng2-chi2, bo5-lun7 si7 beh8-a2, a2-si7 pat8 khoan2 ngou2-kok, put-ko3 si7 chit8 liap8 cheng2-chi2 nmia7-nia7. I e5 heng5-the2 kap au7-lai5 sou2 hoat khi2-lai5 e5, bo5 kang7-khoan2.  38) Siong7-chu2 chiau3 I e5 chi2-i3, siuN2-su3 heng5-the2 hou7 cheng2-chi2, chit8 khoan2 cheng2-chi2 chit8 khoan2 heng5-the2.
	39) It-chhe3 e5 jiok8-the2 m7-si7 long2 kang7-khoan2 e5. Lang5 u7 lang5 e5 jiok8-the2, siu3-lui7 chit8 khoan2, chiau2-lui7 chit8 khoan2, hi5-lui7 leng7-goa7 chit8 khoan2.  40) U7 thian-siong7 e5 the2, ia7 u7 te7-siong7 e5 the2; tan7-si7 thian-siong7 e5 the2 u7 chit8 chiong2 kong-hui, te7-siong7 e5 the2 u7 leng7-goa7 chit8 chiong2 kong-hui.  41) Thai3-iong5 u7 chit8 chiong2 kong-hui, goeh8-kiu5 u7 leng7-goa7 chit8 chiong2 kong-hui, chheN ia7 u7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 kong-hui; mui2 chit8 liap8 chheN e5 kng-tou7 ia7 long2 bo5 kang7-khoan2.
	42) Si2-lang5 e5 hok8-hoat8 ia7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne. Chhin-chhiuN7 ia7 loh8-khi3 e5 cheng2-chi2, tai5 loh8-khi3 e5, si7 e7 hu2-pai7 e5. Koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5, si7 be7 hu2-pai7 e5.  43) Ia7 loh8-khi3 e5, si7 pi-chian7 e5. Koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5, si7 eng5-iau7 e5. Ia7 loh8-khi3 e5 si7 loan2-jiok8 e5. Koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5, si7 iong2-chong3 e5.  44) Tai5 loh8-khi3 e5, si7 thian-seng chu7-jian5 e5 jiok8-the2. Koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5, si7 leng5-seng3 e5 the2. Na7 u7 thian-seng chu7-jian5 e5 jiok8-the2, tong-jian5 ia7 u7 leng5-seng3 e5 the2.  45) Sou2-i2 Seng-keng ki3-chai3 kong2: “Thau5 chit8 e5 lang5, A-tong chiaN5-cho3 oah8 e5 lang5. Choe3-au7 hit e5 A-tong chiaN5-cho3 siuN2-su3 seN3-mia7 e5 lang5.”  46) Tan7-si7 tai7-seng u7 e5, m7-si7 leng5-seng3 e5 the2, si7 thian-seng chu7-jian5 e5 jiok8-the2. Jian5-au7 chiah u7 leng5-seng3 e5 the2.  47) Thau5 chit8 e5 A-tong si7 tui3 te7-siong7 chhut e5, si7 eng7 thou5 cho3 e5. Hit e5 te7-ji7 e5 A-tong si7 tui3 thiN-teng2 lai5 e5.  48) Siok8 te7-siong7 e5 lang5 long2 chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 eng7 thou5 cho3 e5. siok8 thiN-teng2 e5 lang5 long2 chhin-chhiuN7 Hit-ui7 tui3 thiN-teng2 lai5 e5.  49) Tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 u7 hit e5 eng7 thou5 cho3 e5 lang5 e5 heng5-siong7, lan2 ia7 e7 toa3 Hit-ui7 thiN-teng2 e5 lang5 e5 heng5-siong7.
	50) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 hiat-jiok8 e5 the2 be7-tang3 seng5-siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok; e7 biat8-bong5 e5 ia7 be7-tang3 seng5-siu7 put-biat8 e5.  51) Lin2 tioh8 chu3-i3 thiaN, goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2 chit8 hang7 o3-pi3 e5 tai7-chi3: lan2 m7-si7 long2 e7 si2.  52) Choe3-au7 e5 la-pa tan5 e5 si5, chit8-si5-a2 ku2, chit8-bak8-nih-a2 ku2, lan2 long2 e7 pian3-hoa3; in-ui7 la-pa e7 tan5, si2-lang5 e7 koh-oah8 khi2lai5, chiaN5-cho3 eng2-oan2 be7 si2 e5, iah lan2 e7 pian3-hoa3.  53) In-ui7 e7 biat8-bong5 e5, tioh8 piN3-cho3 put-biat8 e5; e7 si2 e5, tioh8 chiaN5-cho3 be7 si2 e5.  54) E7 biat8-bong5 e5, piN3-cho3 put-biat8 e5; e7 si2 e5, chiaN5-cho3 be7 si2 e5 e5 si5, Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 e5 oe7 chiu7 e7 eng3-giam7; chiu7-si7 kong2:
“Si2-bong5 hou7 seng3-li7 thun-chiah8 loh8-khi3 a3.” 55) “Si2-bong5 a3, li2 e5 seng3-li7 ti7 tah-ui7? Si2-bong5 a3, li2 e5 tok8-chhi3 ti7 tah-ui7?”
56) Si2-bong5 e5 tok8-chhi3 chiu7-si7 choe7-ok. Choe7-ok e5 koan5-se3 chiu7-si7 tui3 Lut8-hoat lai5 e5.  57) Kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok siuN2-su3 lan2 seng3-li7.  58) Sou2-i2 chhin-ai3 e5 hiaN-ti7, lin2 tioh8 kian-kiong5, m7-thang tong7-iau5. Ki3-jian5 chai-iaN2 lin2 ui7 Chu2 sou2 lo5-khou2 e5 m7-si7 tou5-lo5 bu5-kong, tioh8 si5-siong5 chin7-lek8 lai5 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang-chok.

Te7 16 ChiuN

Bou7-koan:
	1) Koan-he7 bou7-koan pang-chou7 Siong7-chu2 chu2-bin5 e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 tioh8 chiau3 goa2 ka7 Ka-liap8-thai3 e5 kok kau3-hoe7 sou2 chi2-si7 e5 khi3 cho3.  2) Tak8-le2-pai3 e5 te7-it jit8, mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 an3-chiau3 i e5 siu-jip8, poah chit8-koa2 khi2-lai5 khng3. An2-ne m7-bian2 goa2 khi3 lin2 hia e5 si5, chiah lai5 khai-si2 bou7-koan.  3) Goa2 kau3 lin2 hia e5 si5, e7 chhe-phai3 lin2 sou2 soan2 chhut-lai5 e5 lang5, toa3 goa2 e5 kai3-siau7-phe kap lin2 e5 le2-but8 khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2.  4) Na7-si7 goa2 ia7 khi3 khah ho2-se3, goa2 e7 kap in cho3-hoe2 khi3.

Po2-lo5 e5 ke3-oe7:
	5) Goa2 keng-koe3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 liau2-au7 boeh khi3 lin2 hia. In-ui7 goa2 si7 kan-taN boeh tui3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 keng-koe3 nia7-nia7.  6) Goa2 tai7-khai3 e7 toa3 lin2 hia, bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 e7 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 koe3 tang-thiN. An2-ne bo5-lun7 boeh koh khi3 tah-ui7, lin2 thang sang3 goa2 chhut-hoat.  7) Chit pai2 goa2 bo5 ai3 kan-taN khi3 lin2 hia theng5-kha nia7-nia7; Chu2 na7 in2-chun2, goa2 ng3-bang7 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 toa3 chit8-cham7-a2.  8) Goa2 boeh toa3 I2-hut-sou2, toa3 kau3 Gou7-sun5-cheh;  9) in-ui7 u7 chin ho2 e5 ki-hoe7 thang cho3 u7 hau7-ko2 e5 kang-chok, iu7-koh hoan2-tui3 e5 lang5 ia7 chin-che7.
	10) The5-mou5-thai3 na7 khi3, i toa3 ti7 lin2 hia e5 tiong-kan tioh8 ho2-le2 khoan2-thai7 i. Mai3 hou7 i hoan5-lo2. I ia7 kap goa2 kang7-khoan2, teh cho3 Chu2 e5 kang-chok.  11) sou2-i2 lin2 long2 m7-thang khoaN3-khin i. Tioh8 sang3 i peng5-an chhut-mng5 lai5 goa2 chia, in-ui7 goa2 teh theng3-hau7 i kap hiah-e5 hiaN-ti7 cho3-hoe2 lai5.
	12) Koan-he7 hiaN-ti7 A-pho-lo5, goa2 chai3-saN chai3-si3 kio3 i kap hiah-e5 hiaN-ti7 khi3 lin2 hia; tan7-si7 i bok8-chian5 long2 bo5 siuN7-boeh khi3. Na7 u7 ki-hoe7 i chiu7 e7 khi3.

Choe3-au7 e5 chheng2-an:
	13) Lin2 tioh8 keng2-seng2, sin3-sim tioh8 kian-kou3, tioh8 u7 tai7-tiong7-hu e5 khi3-khai3, tioh8 kian-kiong5.  14) Bo5-lun7 cho3 sim2-mih tai7-chi3, tioh8 eng7 ai3-sim lai5 cho3.
	15) Chu-ui7 hiaN-ti7, goa2 boeh khun2-kiu5 lin2; lin2 bat Su7-the5-hoan2-a3 chit8 ke lang5; in chai7 A-kai-a siong7 tai7-seng lai5 sin3 Chu2, iu7-koh choan5-sim ui7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 teh hok8-bu7.  16) Goa2 khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 hok8-chiong5 chit khoan2 lang5, i2-kip8 it-chhe3 kap in cho3-hoe2 teh kang-chok lo5-khou2 e5 lang5.  17) Su7-the5-hoan2-a3, Hok-tou5-lah kap A-kai-kou2 lai5, goa2 chin hoaN-hi2. Sui-jian5 lin2 bo5 lai5, in lai5 pou2 lin2 e5 khoat.  18) In hou7 goa2 kap lin2 e5 sim tit-tioh8 kou2-le7. Lin2 tioh8 chun-tiong7 chit khoan2 lang5.
	19) A-se-a e5 kok kau3-hoe7 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an. A-ku-lah, Pek-ki-lah, kap chu7-chip8 ti7 in chhu3-lai7 e5 kau3-hoe7, cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 hiaN-ti7 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.  20) Sou2-u7 e5 hiaN-ti7 long2 teh ka7 lin2 chheng2-an. Lin2 tioh8 iong7 tong5-pau-ai3 e5 ak-chhiu2 lai5 hou7-siong chheng2-an.
	21) Goa2 Po2-lo5 chhin-pit ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.  22) Na7 u7 lang5 bo5 keng3-ai3 Chu2, in tioh8 siu7 chiu3-me7. Marana tha ---i3-su3 chiu7-si7 Chu2 a3, chhiaN2 Li2 lai5.  23) Goan7 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 in-hui7 kap lin2 tong5-chai7.  24) Goa2 tui3 sin3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 lin2 tai7-ke piau2-si7 ban7-hun e5 ai3-sim.
