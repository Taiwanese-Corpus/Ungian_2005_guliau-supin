Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Ka-Liap8-Thai3-Lang5 E5 Phe

Te7 1 ChiuN

	1-2) Goa2 Po2-lo5 i2-kip8 kap goa2 cho3-hoe2 ti7 chia e5 cheng3 hiaN-ti7, sia2 phe hou7 Ka-liap8-thai3 te7-hng kok kau3-hoe7. Goa2 cho3 Su3-tou5, m7-si7 lang5 ka7 goa2 siu7-koan5 e5, ia7 m7-si7 chiau3 lang5 e5 hong-sek ka7 goa2 jim7-beng7 e5, chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok i2-kip8 hou7 I tui3 si2-lang5 tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 tiau3 goa2 lai5 cho3 e5.
	3) Goan7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.
	4) Ui7-tioh8 boeh chin2-kiu3 lan2 thoat-li7 hian7-chai7 chit e5 sia5-ok e5 se3-tai7, Ki-tok chun-chiau3 lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 e5 chi2-i3, ui7-tioh8 lan2 e5 choe7-ko3 lai5 hi-seng I pun2-sin.  5) Goan7 eng5-iau7 kui hou7 siong7-chu2, kau3 ban7-se3 ban7-tai7! Amen.

I5-it e5 Hok-im:
	6) Lin2 hou7 goa2 chin tioh8-kiaN! An3-choaN2 lin2 hiah kin2 chiu7 poe7-poan7 Hit-ui7 thong-koe3 Ki-tok e5 in-hui7 lai5 tiau3 lin2 e5 Siong7-chu2, khi3 sin3 pat8-khoan2 e5 hok-im!  7) Su7-sit8-siong7 bo5 pat8-khoan2 e5 hok-im, goa2 an2-ne kong2 si7 in-ui7 u7 chit8-koa2 lang5 teh ka7 lin2 jiau2-loan7, siuN7-boeh khiok-kai2 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im.  8) Tan7-si7 ban7-it goan2 a2-si7 tui3 thiN-teng2 lai5 e5 thian-sai3, na7 tui3 lin2 thoan5 kap goan2 sou2 thoan5 hou7 lin2 e5 Hok-im bo5 kang7-khoan2, i eng-kai tioh8 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8!  9) Chit e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 i2-cheng5 bat kong2 koe3, chim2-ma2 boeh koh kong2 chit8 pai2: na7 u7 lang5 thoan5 hou7 lin2 e5 hok-im, kap lin2 i2-keng chiap-siu7 e5 Hok-im bo5 kang7-khoan2, i eng-kai tioh8 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8!
	10) Goa2 an2-ne kong2, kam2 si7 siuN7-boeh cheng-chhu2 lang5 e5 hoan-sim? M7-si7, goa2 chi2-u7 siuN7-boeh hou7 Siong7-chu2 hoaN-hi2. Goa2 kam2 si7 siuN7-boeh be2 lang5 e5 hoan-sim? Goa2 na7 iau2-ku2 siuN7-boeh be2 lang5 e5 hoan-sim, goa2 chiu7 m7-si7 Ki-tok e5 pok8-jin5.

Po2-lo5 ju5-ho5 chiaN5-cho3 Su3-tou5:
	11) HiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 ai3 lin2 chai-iaN2, goa2 sou2 thoan5 e5 Hok-im m7-si7 chiau3 lang5 e5 i3-su3 cho3 e5.  12) In-ui7 goa2 m7-si7 tui3 lang5 nia2-siu7 chit e5 Hok-im, ia7 m7-si7 tui3 lang5 hak8-sip8 e5, si7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok pun2-sin ka7 goa2 khe2-si7 e5.
	13) Lin2 i2-keng u7 thiaN-kiN3 goa2 i2-cheng5 cho3 chit8 e5 Iu5-thai3 kau3-tou5 e5 keng-lek8. U7 thiaN-kiN3 goa2 loa7-nih8 chan5-jim2 lai5 pek-hai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7, kek8-lek8 siuN7-boeh ka7 i pho3-hoai7.  14) Goa2 tui3 Iu5-thai3 kau3-gi7 e5 jiat8-sim, iaN5-koe3 toa7 pou7-pun7 kap goa2 kang7 ni5-poe3 e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5. Goa2 tui3 goan2 chou2-sian e5 thoan5-thong2 hui-siong5 jiat8-kong5.
	15) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 eng7 I e5 in-hui7, goa2 iau2-boe7 chhut-si3 chiu7 koat-teng7 boeh keng2-soan2 goa2, tiau3 goa2 lai5 su7-hong7 I.  16) Siong7-chu2 koat-i3 boeh tui3 goa2 khe2-si7 I e5 KiaN2, hou7 goa2 thang tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 thoan5 koan-he7 I e5 Hok-im e5 si5, goa2 long2 bo5 khi3 kap lang5 chham-siong5.  17) Goa2 ia7 bo5 khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 chhoe7 hiah-e5 pi2-goa2 khah tai7-seng cho3 Su3-tou5 e5. Goa2 sui5-si5 khi3 A-lat-pek, jian5-au7 koh tng2-khi3 Tai7-ma2-sek.  18) SaN ni5 au7 goa2 chiah khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 pai3-hong2 Pi2-tek, kap i cho3-hoe2 toa3 poaN3 goeh8-jit8 ku2.  19) Goa2 ti5-liau2 Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7 Nga2-kok i2-goa7 long2 bo5 kiN3-tioh8 pat8 e5 Su3-tou5.
	20) Goa2 sia2 hou7 lin2 e5, choat8-tui3 si7 su7-sit8 e5, chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 goa2 bo5 kong2 peh8-chhat8.
	21) Jian5-au7 goa2 khi3 Su7-li7-a kap Ki-li7-ka e5 kok sou2-chai7.  22) Hit e5 si5-chun7 Iu5-thai3 te7-hng Ki-tok kau3-hoe7 e5 hoe7-iu2, iau2 m7-bat kiN3-tioh8 goa2 e5 bin7.  23) In kan-taN thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2: “Hit e5 khah-cha2 ka7 lan2 pek-hai7 e5 lang5, hian7-chai7 teh thoan5 i khah-cha2 siuN7-boeh pho3-hoai7 e5 sin3-giong2!”  24) Sou2-i2 in chiu7 ui7-tioh8 goa2 lai5 eng5-iau7 Siong7-chu2.

Te7 2 ChiuN

Po2-lo5 i2-kip8 ki5-tha e5 Su3-tou5:
	1) Chap8-si3 ni5 au7, goa2 kap Pa-na2-pa tau3-tin7 tng2-khi3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2; goa2 ia7 sun7-soa3 chhoa7 The5-to kap goa2 cho3-hoe2 khi3.  2) Goa2 si7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 khe2-si7 chiah khi3 e5. Goa2 kap hiah-e5 u7 beng5-bong7 e5 lang5 su-jin5 bian7-tam5 e5 si5, chiong goa2 tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 sou2 soan-iong5 e5 Hok-im ka7 in ke2-soeh. Goa2 bo5 ai3 koe3-khi3 kap hian7-chai7 e5 phun-pho tou5-lo5 bu5-kong.  3) Goa2 e5 tang5-phoaN7 The5-to sui-jian5 si7 Hi-lah8-lang5, ia7 bo5 hou7 lang5 kiong5-pek lai5 siu7 kat-le2,  4) sui-jian5 u7 chit8-koa2 lang5 ke2 cho3 hiaN-ti7 lai5 chham-ka chit e5 thoan5-the2, u7 siuN7-boeh ka7 i si-heng5 kat-le2. Chiah-e5 lang5 cho3 cheng-tham3, chhang3 jip8-lai5 boeh tham3 lan2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 chu7-iu5 kiu3-keng3 si7 sim2-mih khoan2 e5. In ai3 lan2 chiaN5-cho3 Lut8-hoat e5 lou5-le7.  5) Goan2 lian5 chit8-si5-a2 ku2 to bo5 tui3 in khut-hok8, bok8-tek si7 boeh hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 po2-choan5 Hok-im e5 chin-li2.
	6) Tan7-si7 hiah-e5 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-cho3 u7 beng5-bong7 e5 --- bo5-lun7 in si7 sim2-mih khoan2 lang5, tui3 goa2 long2 bo5 khah-choah8, in-ui7 Siong7-chu2 bo5 khoaN3 goa7-mau7 lai5 tui3-thai7 lang5 --- goa2 sou2 kong2 hiah-e5 u7 beng5-bong7 e5 lang5, (NNpeng7) bo5 sim2-mih ko-kian3 thang ka7 goa2 chi2-si7.  7) Tian-to3 in seng5-jin7 kong2, Siong7-chu2 u7 kau-thok goa2 su3-beng7 lai5 thoan5 Hok-im hou7 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 I kau-thok su3-beng7 hou7 Pi2-tek lai5 thoan5 Hok-im hou7 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5.  8) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 siu7-koan5 hou7 Pi2-tek cho3 u7 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 e5 Su3-tou5, I ia7 siu7-koan5 hou7 goa2 cho3 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 e5 Su3-tou5.  9) Nga2-kok, Pi2-tek, i2-kip8 Iok-han7, chiah-e5 hou7 lang5 chun-keng3 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 tiong-sim jin5-but8 e5, seng5-jin7 Siong7-chu2 u7 siuN2-su3 goa2 chit e5 tek8-piat8 e5 in-su3; sou2-i2 in u7 kap Pa-na2-pa, ia7 u7 kap goa2 ak-chhiu2. Goan2 tong5-i3 kong2 goan2 boeh tui3 Goa7-pang-lang5 thoan5-kau3, in boeh tui3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 thoan5-kau3.  10) In tui3 goan2 ui5-it e5 iau-kiu5, chiu7-si7 goan2 tioh8-ai3 koan-sim in hia e5 san2-chhiah-lang5. Chit hang7 tai7-chi3 si7 goa2 chai7-lai5 chiu7 jiat8-sim teh cho3 e5.

Po2-lo5 chai7 An-the5-o chek-pi7 Pi2-tek:
	11) Pi2-tek lai5-kau3 An-the5-o e5 si5, goa2 tng-bin7 ka7 i hoan2-tui3, in-ui7 i beng5-beng5 u7 chho3-gou7.  12) U7 chit8-koa2 Nga2-kok chhe-phai3 lai5 e5 lang5 iau2-boe7 kau3-ui7 i2-cheng5, Pi2-tek long2 kap Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 hiaN-ti7 cho3-hoe2 chiah8-png7. Tan7-si7 chiah-e5 lang5 kau3 liau2-au7, i soah siam2-pi7, bo5 ai3 koh kap in cho3-hoe2 chiah8-png7, in-ui7 i kiaN hiah-e5 chu2-tiuN tioh8 si-heng5 kat-le2 e5 lang5.  13) Ki5-tha e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 ia7 oh8 Pi2-tek cho3 su7 bo5 teng7-kian3, lian5 Pa-na2-pa ia7 hou7 in thoa loh8-khi3 cho3 chit khoan2 sio2-taN2 e5 heng5-ui5.  14) Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 in li7-khui Hok-im chin-li2 e5 cheng3-to7 e5 si5, chiu7 toa3 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 tui3 Pi2-tek kong2: “Li2 si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5, m7-ku2 seng-oah8 chhin-chhiuN7 Goa7-pang-lang5, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 Iu5-thai3-lang5. An2-ne li2 na2 u7 chu-keh thang bian2-kiong2 Goa7-pang-lang5 lai5 koe3 chhin-chhiuN7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 seng-oah8?”

Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Goa7-pang-lang5 to iu5 sin3-sim tit-kiu3:
	15) Lan2 si7 chhut-si3 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5, m7-si7 chhut-si3 cho3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 khoaN3-cho3 choe7-jin5 e5 Goa7-pang-lang5.  16) Tan7-si7 lan2 chai-iaN2 lang5 m7-si7 thong-koe3 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, si7 tioh8 sin3-kho3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chiah e7-tang3 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8. Lan2 ia7 u7 siong-sin3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou, bok8-tek m7-si7 boeh thong-koe3 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, si7 boeh thong-koe3 sin3-kho3 Ki-tok hou7 lan2 thang tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 ho5-hap8. In-ui7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 thong-koe3 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 e5 ho5-hap8.  17) An2-ne lan2 na7 siuN7-boeh thong-koe3 Ki-tok lai5 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8, kiat-ko2 soah kap Goa7-pang-lang5 peN5-peN5 cho3 choe7-jin5, --- an2-ne si7 kong2 Ki-tok teh kou2-le7 lang5 hoan7-choe7 a2 m7-si7? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7.  18) Goa2 na7 khai-si2 koh kian3-cho7 goa2 sou2 thiah-tiau7 e5, an2-ne chiu7-si7 cheng3-beng5 goa2 si7 Lut8-hoat e5 ui5-hoan7-chia2.  19) Chiau3 Lut8-hoat e5 koan-tiam2 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, goa2 i2-keng hou7 Lut8-hoat chhu2-si2 a3, che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 goa2 e7-tang3 ui7 Siong7-chu2 lai5 oah8. Goa2 i2-keng kap Ki-tok cho3-hoe2 hou7 lang5 teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 a3,  20) sou2-i2 hian7-chai7 goa2 teh oah8, i2-keng m7-si7 goa2 pun2-sin teh oah8, si7 Ki-tok toa3 ti7 goa2 lai7-bin7 chiah teh oah8 e5. Goa2 hian7-chai7 teh oah8 chit e5 seN3-mia7, si7 in-ui7 sin3 Siong7-chu2 e5 KiaN2 chiah teh oah8 e5. Ki-tok thiaN3 goa2, ui7-tioh8 goa2 hi-seng I e5 seN3-mia7.  21) Goa2 bo5 ki7-choat8 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7. Siat-su2 lang5 na7 thong-koe3 Lut8-hoat, e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8, an2-ne Ki-tok si2 liau2 chiu7 bo5 bok8-tek a3!

Te7 3 ChiuN

Lut8-hoat a2-si7 sin3-sim:
	1) Lin2 chiah-e5 gong7 Ka-liap8-thai3-lang5 a3! Sim2-mih lang5 eng7 iau-sut8 ka7 lin2 be5-hek8 khi3? Ia5-sou Ki-tok hou7 lang5 teng3 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3 si2 e5 cheng5-heng5, kam2 bo5 beng5-beng5 biau5-sia2 chhut-lai5 ti7 lin2 bak8-chiu cheng5 hou7 lin2 khoaN3 a3?  2) Goa2 chi2-u7 ai3 mng7 lin2 chit8 hang7 tai7-chi3: lin2 nia2-siu7 Seng3 Leng5, si7 in-ui7 lin2 sit8-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, a2-si7 in-ui7 lin2 thiaN Hok-im lai5 siong-sin3?  3) Lin2 na2 e7 hiah-nih8 gong7? Lin2 kho3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 khai-si2 leng5-seng3 e5 seng-oah8, taN lin2 siuN7-boeh kho3 jiok8-sin e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 oan5-seng5 lin2 e5 chin2-kiu3 si7 bo5?  4) Lin2 siu7 hiah che7 sin-khou2 e5 keng-giam7 kam2 long2 bo5 i3-gi7? It-teng7 u7 i3-gi7 chiah tioh8!  5) Siong7-chu2 siuN2-su3 lin2 Seng3 Leng5, chai7 lin2 tiong-kan hian2 sin5-chek, I an2-ne cho3 kam2 si7 in-ui7 lin2 sit8-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7, a2-si7 in-ui7 lin2 thiaN Hok-im lai5 siong-sin3?
	6) Tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng u7 chi2 A-pek-lah8-han2 kong2: “I siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2, in-ui7 i u7 chit e5 sin3-sim, Siong7-chu2 ka7 i chiap-lap8 cho3 gi7-jin5.”  7) TaN lin2 tioh8 chai-iaN2 u7 sin3-sim e5 lang5 chiah si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 sit8-chai7 e5 kiaN2-sun.  8) Seng3-keng su7-sian u7 khoaN3-chhut kong2, Siong7-chu2 boeh hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5 thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 kap I ho5-hap8. Sou2-i2 Seng3-keng su7-sian u7 tui3 A-pek-lah8-han2 soan-iong5 Hok-im kong2: “Siong7-chu2 boeh thong-koe3 li2 chiok-hok te7-siong7 e5 ban7-bin5.”  9) An2-ne hiah-e5 u7 sin3 e5 lang5 kap u7 sin3-sim e5 A-pek-lah8-han2 peN5-peN5 siu7 chiok-hok.
	10) Tan7-si7 hiah-e5 kho3 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7 e5 lang5, long2 e7 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8. In-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2: “Lang5 na7 bo5 si5-si5 khek-khek chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 chheh sou2 ki3-chai3 mui2 chit8 tiau5 kai3-beng7, tioh8 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8!”  11) TaN bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 thong-koe3 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8, che si7 chin beng5-hian2 e5 tai7-chi3; in-ui7 Seng3-keng ki3-chai3 kong2: “Thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 lang5 chiah e7 oah8.”  12) Tan7-si7 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 tong7-ki m7-si7 kin-ki3 sin3-sim. Seng3-keng u7 kong2: “Chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat e5 kai3-beng7 e5 lang5, chi2-u7 oa2-kho3 chun-heng5 mui2 chit8 tiau5 kai3-beng7 chiah e7 oah8.”
	13) Tan7-si7 Ki-tok the3 lan2 jim2-siu7 chek-hoat8, lai5 kiu3-siok8 lan2 thoat-li7 Lut8-hoat e5 chek-hoat8, in-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 ki3-chai3 kong2: “Mui2 chit8 e5 hou7 lang5 tiau3-si2 toa3 chha5-ke3-teng2 e5 lang5, tioh8 siu7 chiu3-me7.”  14) Ki-tok an2-ne cho3 e5 bok8-tek, si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh chiong Siong7-chu2 sou2 eng3-un2 hou7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 chiok-hok, ia7 e7 thong-koe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou lai5 siuN2-su3 hou7 Goa7-pang-lang5, ia7 si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lan2 oa2-kho3 sin3-sim, lai5 nia2-siu7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 eng3-un2 e5 Seng3 Leng5.

Lut8-hoat kap eng3-un2:
	15) HiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 boeh ki2 chit8 e5 jit8-siong5 e5 tai7-chi3 cho3 le7 lai5 kong2: lang5 e5 ui5-chiok it-tan3 chhiam-teng7 liau2-au7, chiu7 bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 ka7 i hoe3-ti5 a2-si7 siu-kai2.  16) TaN Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 si7 kap A-pek-lah8-han2 i2-kip8 kap i e5 hou7-e3 iok-teng7 e5. Eng3-un2 e5 loe7-iong5 m7-si7 kong2, “kap i e5 kiaN2-kiaN2-sun-sun”, chhin-chhiuN7 teh chi2 chin-che7 lang5. Hit e5 eng3-un2 si7 kong2, “kap li2 e5 hou7-e3,” si7 teh chi2 kan-taN chit8 e5 lang5. Chit e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 Ki-tok.  17) Goa2 e5 i3-su3 si7 an2-ne: Siong7-chu2 kap A-pek-lah8-han2 lip8 chit e5 beng5-iok, iu7-koh tah-eng3 boeh siu2-iok. Si3-pah saN-chap8 ni5 au7 sou2 pan-pou3 e5 Lut8-hoat, be7-tang3 hoe3-ti5 hit e5 beng5-iok, ia7 be7-tang3 chhu2-siau Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2.  18) Ka2-su2 ke3-seng5-koan5 na7 si7 kin-ki3 Lut8-hoat lai5 e5, an2-ne chiu7 m7-si7 kin-ki3 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 a3. Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 chiau3 I e5 eng3-un2 bo5 tiau5-kiaN7 siuN2-su3 ke3-seng5-koan5 hou7 A-pek-lah8-han2.
	19) Na7 an2-ne Lut8-hoat u7 sim2-mih kong-iong7? Lut8-hoat si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lang5 chai-iaN2 sim2-mih si7 put-hoat, chiah chiam7-si5 che3-teng7 e5. Chit e5 Lut8-hoat e5 hau7-iong7 si7 kau3 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 sou2 chi2 Hit-ui7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 hou7-e3 lai5-lim5 e5 si5 ui5-chi2. Lut8-hoat si7 keng-koe3 tiong-po2 e5 chhiu2, thong-koe3 thian-sai3 lai5 pan-pou3 e5.  20) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 tit8-chiap eng3-un2 A-pek-lah8-han2 pun2-lang5, sou2-i2 bo5 su-iau3 tiong-po2. Che si7 Siong7-chu2 chhin-sin eng3-un2 e5.

Lut8-hoat e5 bok8-tek:
	21) Na7-si7 an2-ne Lut8-hoat kap Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 u7 ti2-chhiok a2 m7-si7? Choat8-tui3 m7-si7! In-ui7 sou2 pan-pou3 e5 Lut8-hoat na7 e7-tang3 hou7 lang5 tit-tioh8 seN3-mia7, an2-ne lang5 chiu7 e7-tang3 oa2-kho3 Lut8-hoat lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8.  22) Tan7-si7 Seng3-keng u7 kong2, u2-tiu7 ban7-but8 long2 siu7 choe7-ok e5 koan5-se3 sou2 khong3-che3. Che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 hiah-e5 u7 sin3 e5 lang5 e7-tang3 nia2-siu7 sou2 eng3-un2 e5 in-hui7. Chit e5 in-hui7 chi2-u7 siuN2-su3 hou7 siong-sin3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lang5.
	23) Tan7-si7 kho3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3 chit e5 si5-tai7 iau2-boe7 kau3 i2-cheng5, chiu7-si7 it-tit8 kau3 teh-boeh lai5 chit e5 kho3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3 e5 chin-to7 hian2-hian7 chhut-lai5 e5 si5 ui5-chi2, lan2 long2 hou7 Lut8-hoat kam3-kim3 cho3 siu5-hoan7.  24) An2-ne kau3 Ki-tok lai5-lim5 ui5-chi2, Lut8-hoat si7 cho3 lan2 e5 kau3-to7-chia2, thang hou7 lan2 thong-koe3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8.  25) Ki3-jian5 kho3 sin3-sim lai5 tit-kiu3 hit e5 si5-tai7 i2-keng lai5-lim5 a7, lan2 chiu7 bo5 koh su-iau3 chit e5 kau3-to7-chia2.
	26) In-ui7 thong-koe3 sin3-sim, lin2 tai7-ke long2 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 lai5 chiaN5-cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  27) In-ui7 lin2 nia2-siu7 se2-le2 lai5 kap Ki-tok kiat-hap8, an2-ne lin2 chiu7 i2-keng u7 Ki-tok e5 tek8-seng3 a3.  28) an2-ne chiu7 bo5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 kap Goa7-pang-lang5 e5 hun-piat8, bo5 lou5-le7 kap chu7-iu5-jin5 e5 hun-piat8, ia7 bo5 cha-pou-lang5 kap cha-bou2-lang5 e5 hun-piat8: in-ui7 lin2 long2 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8 chiaN5-cho3 it-the2.  29) Lin2 na7 siok8 Ki-tok, lin2 chiu7-si7 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 kiaN2-sun, chiu7 e7-tang3 ke3-seng5 Siong7-chu2 sou2 eng3-un2 e5.

Te7 4 ChiuN

	1) Goa2 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 an2-ne: chai5-san2 e5 ke3-seng5-jin5, i sui-jian5 si7 it-chhe3 chai5-san2 e5 sou2-iu2-chia2, tan7-si7 iau2-boe7 seng5-lian5 i2-cheng5 kap lou5-le7 bo5 chha-piat8.  2) I e5 lau7-pe7 sou2 chi2-teng7 e5 jit8-chi2 iau2-boe7 kau3 i2-cheng5, i tioh8 siu7 kam3-hou7-jin5 kap hoat-teng7 tai7-li2-jin5 e5 koan2-kau3.  3) Lan2 chai7 leng5-seng3-siong7 iau2-boe7 seng5-lian5 i2-cheng5, siu7 thong2-hat u2-tiu7 e5 sin5-leng5 khong3-che3 cho3 lou5-le7.  4) Tan7-si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 chi2-teng7 e5 si5-jit8 chit8-e7 kau3, I chiu7 chhe-phai3 I e5 KiaN2, tui3 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5 chhut-si3, chai7 Iu5-thai3 Lut8-hoat e5 koan2-hat ha7 seng5-tiong2.  5) An2-ne si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh kai2-kiu3 hiah-e5 siu7 Lut8-hoat koan2-hat e5 lang5, thang hou7 lan2 chiaN5-cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.
	6) Ui7-tioh8 boeh cheng3-beng5 lin2 si7 I e5 kiaN2-ji5, Siong7-chu2 chhe-phai3 I e5 KiaN2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 jip8-lai5 lan2 e5 sim-lai7. Chit e5 Seng3 Leng5 hou7 lan2 hou-kio3 kong2, “A-pa! Goa2 e5 Hu7-chhin!”  7) An2-ne lai5, li2 chiu7 i2-keng m7-si7 cho3 lou5-le7 a7, si7 cho3 kiaN2-ji5 a7. Li2 ki3-jian5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 e5 kiaN2, I chiu7 hou7 li2 chiaN5-cho3 ke3-seng5-jin5.

Po2-lo5 tui3 Ka-liap8-thai3-lang5 e5 koan-hoai5:
	8) Koe3-khi3 lin2 m7-bat Siong7-chu2 e5 si5, lin2 cho3 sin5-leng5 e5 lou5-le7, hiah-e5 sin5-leng5 kin-pun2 m7-si7 chin-sin5.  9) Tan7-si7 hian7-chai7 lin2 ki3-jian5 bat Siong7-chu2 --- tian-to3 goa2 eng-kai tioh8-ai3 kong2 Siong7-chu2 bat lin2, lin2 an3-choaN2 boeh koh hoan-thau5 tng2-khi3 siu7 hiah-e5 loan2-jiok8 koh bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 sin5-leng5 e5 khong3-che3, cheng5-goan7 koh cho3 in e5 lou5-le7?  10) Lin2 sim7-chi3 jiat8-sim siu2 tek8-piat8 e5 jit8, tek8-piat8 e5 goeh8, kap ni5-cheh!  11) Goa2 ui7 lin2 chin tam-iu! Goa2 ui7 lin2 sin-khou2 kang-chok kam2 long2 tou5-lo5 bu5-kong?
	12) HiaN-ti7 a3, in-ui7 goa2 i2-keng oh8 lin2 e5 chok-hong, taN lin2 ia7 tioh8 lai5 oh8 goa2 e5 chok-hong. Lin2 long2 m7-bat hou7 goa2 chiah8-khui.  13) Lin2 e7-ki3-tit goa2 thau5 chit8 pai2 u7 ki-hoe7 khi3 lin2 hia thoan5 Hok-im, si7 in-ui7 goa2 seng-khu u7 peN7.  14) Sui-jian5 tong-si5 goa2 e5 kian7-khong cheng5-heng5 u7 hou7 lin2 chin toa7 e5 ma5-hoan5, tan7-si7 lin2 bo5 ka7 goa2 khoaN3-khin, ia7 bo5 ka7 goa2 ki7-choat8. Lin2 tian-to3 chhin-chhiuN7 teh hoan-geng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 thian-sai3 an2-ne lai5 hoan-geng5 goa2; chhin-chhiuN7 teh chiap-thai7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou an2-ne lai5 chiap-thai7 goa2!  15) Tong-si5 lin2 siuN7-kong2 hiah-nih8 hok-khi3 e7-tang3 kap goa2 cho3-hoe2, taN long2 be7-ki3-tit a3, si7 bo5? Lin2 tong-si5 e5 jiat8-seng5 goa2 e7-eng7-tit an2-ne kong2: na7 kho2-leng5, lin2 ia7 kam-goan7 iah lin2 e5 bak8-chiu chhut-lai5 hou7 goa2.  16) TaN in-ui7 goa2 tui3 lin2 kong2 chin-sit8 e5 tai7-chi3, soah pian3-cho3 lin2 e5 tek8-jin5 a2 m7-si7?
	17) Hiah-e5 ki5-tha e5 lang5 tui3 lin2 piau2-si7 chhin-chhiat, tan7-si7 in e5 iong7-i3 (NNpeng7) m7-si7 ho2 e5. In e5 i3-tou5 si7 ai3-boeh hou7 lin2 kap goa2 keh-li7, thang hou7 lin2 chhin-chhiuN7 in tui3-thai7 lin2 an2-ne, lai5 kap in chhin-jiat8.  18) Siu7 lang5 jiat8-seng5 chiau-thai7, pun2-lai5 si7 ho2 e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7 tioh8 u7 tong7-ki, iu7-koh tioh8 si2-chiong it-koan3, m7-thang kan-taN goa2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5 chiah an2-ne.  19) Goa2 chhin-ai3 e5 kiaN2-ji5 a3! Ui7-tioh8 lin2, goa2 tioh8 koh siu7 chhin-chhiuN7 lau7-bo2 teh seN-kiaN2 e5 thong3-khou2, it-tit8 kau3 Ki-tok e5 pun2-seng3 ti7 lin2 sim-lai7 cho7-seng5 khi2-lai5 ui5-chi2.  20) Goa2 chin hi-bong7 chim2-ma2 e7-tang3 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2, thang eng7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 thai3-tou7 lai5 tui3-thai7 lin2. In-ui7 goa2 ui7 lin2 siuN7 kah chin bo5 pan7-hoat!

Ha7-kah kap Sat-lah e5 le7:
	21) Goa2 lai5 mng7 lin2 hiah-e5 ai3-boeh chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat e5 lang5: lin2 kam2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3 Lut8-hoat e5 chheh u7 sia2 sim2-mih?  22) Ti7 hia u7 ki3-chai3 kong2 A-pek-lah8-han2 u7 nng7 e5 kiaN2, chit8 e5 si7 cha-bou2 lou5-le7 seN e5, iau2 chit8 e5 si7 chu7-iu5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 seN e5.  23) Hit e5 tui3 cha-bou2 lou5-le7 chhut-si3 e5 kiaN2, si7 chiau3 se3-kan chu7-jian5 e5 hong-hoat chhut-si3 e5, tan7-si7 hit e5 tui3 chu7-iu5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chhut-si3 e5 kiaN2, si7 thong-koe3 Siong7-chu2 e5 eng3-un2 lai5 chhut-si3 e5.  24) Che si7 chit8 chiong2 e5 phi3-ju7: chit nng7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7-si7 teh tai7-piau2 nng7 e5 beng5-iok. Chit nng7 e5 beng5-iok e5 chit8 e5 si7 tui3 Se-nai2-soaN lai5 e5, chiu7-si7 Ha7-kah. I e5 kiaN2 chhut-si3 cho3 lou5-le7.  25) Ha7-kah tai7-piau2 A-lat-pek e5 Se-nai2-soaN, si7 teh chi2 hian7-chai7 e5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2-siaN5, in-ui7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 kap siaN5-lai7 e5 peh-seN3 long2 si7 lou5-le7.  26) Tan7-si7 thian-siong7 e5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 si7 chu7-iu5 e5, chiu7-si7 lan2 e5 bo2-chhin.  27) In-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 kong2:
“Be7 hoai5-in7, m7-bat seN-kiaN2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8 hoaN-hi2! M7-bat tu2-tioh8 seN-kiaN2 e5 thong3-khou2 e5, tioh8 chhut-siaN hoan-hou! In-ui7 hou7-lang5 pang3-sak e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e7 pi2 u7 tiong7-hu e5 khah che7 kiaN2-ji5.”
	28) HiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 chhin-chhiuN7 I2-sat, si7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 eng3-un2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  29) Hit e5 si5, chiau3 se3-kan chu7-jian5 e5 hong-hoat chhut-si3 e5 kiaN2, pek-hai7 hit e5 thong-koe3 Siong7-chu2 e5 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 chhut-si3 e5 kiaN2; hian7-chai7 ia7 si7 an2-ne.  30) Tan7-si7 Seng3-keng kong2 sim2-mih? Seng3-keng kong2: “Chiong cha-bou2 lou5-le7 kap i e5 kiaN2 koaN2 chhut-khi3; in-ui7 cha-bou2 lou5-le7 e5 kiaN2 choat8-tui3 be7-eng7-tit kap chu7-iu5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 kiaN2 saN-kap pun lau7-pe7 e5 chai5-san2.”  31) HiaN-ti7 a3, sou2-i2 lan2 m7-si7 cha-bou2 lou5-le7 e5 kiaN2-ji5, lan2 si7 chu7-iu5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 kiaN2-ji5.

Te7 5 ChiuN

Po2-chhi5 lin2 e5 chu7-iu5:
	1) Ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5, Ki-tok ka7 lan2 sek-hong3! Sou2-i2 lin2 tioh8 cho3 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5 khia7 hou7 chai7, m7-thang koh hou7 lang5 ka7 lin2 koa3 lou5-le7 e5 ke5.
	2) Goa2 Po2-lo5 ka7 lin2 kong2, lin2 tioh8 chu3-i3 thiaN: ka2-su2 lin2 na7 boeh siu7 kat-le2, an2-ne Ki-tok chiu7 tui3 lin2 long2 bo5 li7-ek.  3) Goa2 boeh koh kiong5-tiau chit8 pai2. Mui2 chit8 e5 boeh siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5, eng-kai tioh8 chun-siu2 mui2 chit8 tiau5 Lut8-hoat.  4) Lin2 chiah-e5 siuN7-boeh chun-heng5 Lut8-hoat lai5 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 i2-keng kap Ki-tok toan7-choat8 koan-he7 a7. Lin2 i2-keng sit-loh8 siong7-chu2 e5 in-hui7 a7.  5) Thong-koe3 Seng3 Leng5, lan2 jiat8-chhiat teh theng3-hau7, ng3-bang7 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 kap Siong7-chu2 ho5-hap8. Chit e5 ng3-bang7 chi2-u7 oa2-kho3 sin3-sim chiah e7-tang3 tat8-seng5.  6) In-ui7 lan2 na7 kap Ki-tok Ia5-sou kiat-hap8, u7 siu7 kat-le2 a2-si7 bo5 siu7 kat-le2 long2 bo5 chha-piat8; choe3 tiong7-iau3 e5, chiu7-si7 tioh8-ai3 u7 thong-koe3 jin5-ai3 lai5 piau2-hian7 chhut-lai5 e5 sin3-sim.
	7) Lin2 i2-cheng5 cho3 liau2 chin ho2! Sim2-mih lang5 ka7 lin2 chou2-tong3, kio3 lin2 mai3 sun7-chiong5 chin-li2?  8) Chit e5 khoan3-iu2 m7-si7 tui3 tiau3 lin2 e5 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5.  9) Siok8-gi2 kong2, “Chit8-sut-a2 kaN3-bo2 chiu7 e7 hou7 kui te3 e5 mi7-hun2-chhe3 hoat khi2-lai5.”  10) Tan7-si7 goa2 iau2-ku2 siong-sin3 lin2. In-ui7 lan2 kap Chu2 kiat-hap8, hou7 goa2 khak-sin3 kong2 lin2 be7 chhai2-chhu2 kap goa2 tui3-lip8 e5 kian3-kai2; iu7-koh teh jiau2-loan7 lin2 e5, bo5-lun7 si7 sim2-mih lang5, i it-teng7 e7 siu7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chek-hoat8.
	11) HiaN-ti7 a3, ka2-su2 goa2 na7 iau2 teh soan-thoan5 u7 kat-le2 e5 pit-iau3, goa2 an3-choaN2 iau2 tioh8 siu7 lang5 e5 pek-hai7? Ka2-su2 goa2 na7 an2-ne thoan5, hiah-e5 siong-sin3 tioh8 kat-le2 e5 lang5, chiu7 be7 tui3 goa2 sou2 soan-iong5 Ki-tok sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2 phau7 hoan2-kam2.  12) Hiah-e5 teh jiau2-loan7 lin2 e5 lang5, goa2 khou2 in m7 ka-ti7 iam khi2-lai5, koah khi2-lai5.
	13) Goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 si7 siu7 keng2-tiau3 lai5 cho3 chu7-iu5 e5 lang5. Tan7-si7 m7-thang chioh chit e5 chu7-iu5 lai5 cho3 lin2 jiok8-iok8 hong3-chhiong3 e5 ki-hoe7. Tian-to3 tioh8 eng7 ai3-sim lai5 hou7-siong hok8-bu7.  14) In-ui7 choan5-pou7 e5 Lut8-hoat long2 pau-ham5 ti7 chit8 ku3 oe7 e5 lai7-bin7; chiu7-si7 “Lin2 eng-kai tioh8 ai3 jin5 ju5 ki2.”  15) Lin2 tioh8 kin2-sin7. Lin2 na7 chhin-chhiuN7 ia2-siu3 sio ka7 sio thun2-chiah8, kiaN-liau2 lin2 e7 hou7-siong chau5-biat8.

Seng3 Leng5 i2-kip8 lang5 e5 pun2-seng3:
	16) Goa2 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 kong2: lin2 tioh8 chiau3 Seng3 Leng5 e5 leng2-to7 lai5 seng-oah8, choat8-tui3 m7-thang hou7 lin2 e5 pun2-seng3 e5 iok8-bong7 tit-tioh8 boan2-chiok.  17) In-ui7 lan2 pun2-seng3 e5 iok8-bong7 si7 ui5-poe7 Seng3 Leng5 e5 i3-su3. Seng3 Leng5 e5 i3-su3 si7 kap lang5 pun2-seng3 e5 iok8-bong7 siong-hoan2: chit nng7 hang7 hou7-siong tui3-lip8, an2-ne chiu7-si7 kong2 lin2 be7-tang3 sui5-sim sou2-iok8.  18) Seng3 Leng5 na7 ka7 lin2 leng2-to7, lin2 chiu7 be7 siu7 Lut8-hoat e5 khong3-che3.
	19) Lang5 e5 pun2-seng3 sou2 cho3 chhut-lai5 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 chin beng5-hian2, chhin-chhiuN7 kan-im5, put-kiat, loan7-im5;  20) ngou2-siong7 chong5-pai3, sia5-sut8, kiat oan-siu5, tou3-cheng, chit-tou3, siu7-khi3, chu7-su, hun-tong2 kiat-phai3,  21) oan3-tou3, chiu2-chui3, hoa-thian chiu2-te7 teng2-teng2 e5 su7. Goa2 i2-keng u7 ka7 lin2 keng2-kai3, goa2 koh ka7 lin2 keng2-kai3 chit8 pai2: hiah-e5 cho3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5, be7-tang3 ke3-seng5 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok.
	22) Tan7-si7 Seng3 Leng5 e7 san2-seng jin5-ai3, hi2-lok8, ho5-peng5, jim2-nai7, chhin-chhiat, sian7-liong5, tiong-sit8,  23) khiam-pi, i2-kip8 chu7-che3. Chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 bo5 ui5-hoan2 tah chit8 tiau5 lut8-hoat.  24) Hiah-e5 siok8 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 lang5, i2-keng chiong in pun2-seng3 it-chhe3 e5 sia5-cheng5 su-iok8 long2 teng3-si2 toa3 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 a3.  25) Ki3-jian5 lan2 kho3 Seng3 Leng5 teh seng-oah8, lan2 ia7 tioh8 chun-chiau3 Seng3 Leng5 e5 chi2-i3 lai5 cho3 tai7-chi3.  26) Lan2 m7-thang kiu5 hi-eng5, m7-thang hou7-siong kek-khi3, ia7 m7-thang hou7-siong oan3-tou3.

Te7 6 ChiuN

Hun-tam pat8-lang5 e5 tang7-taN3:
	1) HiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 na7 khoaN3-kiN3 lang5 ham7-loh8 sim2-mih koe3-sit, lin2 chiah-e5 u7 leng5-seng3 e5 lang5 tioh8 eng7 un-jiu5 e5 sim lai5 ka7 i kiu3-cheng3. Lin2 ka-ti7 ia7 tioh8 sio2-sim, chiah be7 siu7 in2-iu2.  2) Lin2 tioh8 hou7-siong hun-tam pat8-lang5 e5 tang7-taN3, an2-ne lin2 chiu7 e7 oan5-seng5 Ki-tok e5 hun3-leng7.  3) Na7 u7 lang5 khoaN3-tang7 ka-ti7, su7-sit8 si7 bo5 sim2-mih chai5-leng5, i si7 teh khi-phian3 ka-ti7.  4) Mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 seng2-chhat i ka-ti7 e5 heng5-ui5. I e5 heng5-ui5 na7-si7 ho2 e5, i chiu7 e7-tang3 ka-ti7 lai5 kou2-le7 i e5 seng5-chiu7, bo5 su-iau3 kap pat8-lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 lai5 pi2-kau3.  5) In-ui7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 long2 tioh8 taN i ka-ti7 e5 taN3.
	6) Hak8-sip8 to7-li2 e5 lang5, tioh8 hou7 i e5 lau7-su lai5 kap i hun-hiong2 it-chhe3 e5 ho2-mih8.
	7) M7-thang khi-phian3 ka-ti7: Siong7-chu2 hou7 lin2 be7 kun2-chhio3-tit. Lang5 na7 cheng3 sim2-mih, chiu7 siu-seng5 sim2-mih.  8) I na7 ia7 cheng2 toa3 i pun2-seng3 e5 iok8-bong7 e5 hng5-lai7, i chiu7 e7 tui3 chit e5 hng5 lai5 siu-seng5 biat8-bong5; i na7 ia7 cheng2 toa3 Seng3 Leng5 e5 hng5-lai7, i chiu7 e7 tui3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 siu-seng5 eng2-seng.  9) Lan2 cho3 ho2-su7 m7-thang siuN7 sian7; lan2 na7 bo5 poan3-tou5 ji5-hoe3, si5 kau3 chiu7 e7 siu-seng5.  10) Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 pa2-ak ki-hoe7 tui3 cheng3-lang5 lai5 cho3 ho2-su7, tek8-piat8 si7 tui3 hiah-e5 chai7 sin3-giong2-siong7 siok8 ti7 lan2 kang7 chit e5 toa7 ka-teng5 e5 lang5.

Choe3-au7 e5 chheng2-an:
	11) TaN goa2 chhin-pit lai5 sia2 kui2-ku3-a2; lin2 khoaN3, goa2 sia2 kah chiah-nih8 toa7-ji7!  12) Hiah-e5 chai7 goa7-piau2-siong7 ai3 chng the2-bian7 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 siuN7-boeh kiong5-pek lin2 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5. In ui5-it e5 bok8-tek si7 boeh pi7-bian2 ui7 Ki-tok sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2 lai5 siu7 pek-hai7.  13) Lian5 hiah-e5 siu7 kat-le2 e5 lang5 ka-ti7 ia7 bo5 chun-siu2 Lut8-hoat; in ai3 lin2 siu7 kat-le2, hou7 in e7-tang3 khoa-khau2 kong2 lin2 u7 hok8-chiong5 chit e5 goa7-piau2-siong7 e5 le2-gi5.  14) Na7 si7 goa2, goa2 chi2-u7 boeh khoa-iau7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2; thong-koe3 Ki-tok e5 sip8-ji7-ke3, tui3 goa2 khoaN3 khi2-lai5, se3-kan it-chhe3 long2 si2-khi3 a; tui3 se3-kan khoaN3 khi2-lai5, goa2 i2-keng si2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3-teng2 a3.  15) U7 siu7 kat-le2, bo5 siu7 kat-le2 long2 bo5 iau3-kin2. Iau3-kin2 e5 chiu7-si7 cho3 chit8 e5 sin e5 siu7-cho7-chia2.  16) Chiau3 chit e5 chun2-chek lai5 seng-oah8 e5 lang5, goan7 peng5-an i2-kip8 lin5-bin2 kap in tong5-chai7, ia7 kap it-chhe3 Siong7-chu2 e5 chu2-bin5 tong5-chai7.
