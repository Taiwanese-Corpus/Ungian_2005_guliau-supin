
Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2
	

Asia Jilimpo
Tian7-si7 sin-bun5 kong2 u7 1 e5 toa7-boe2 lou5-moa5 koe3-sin, sek-tiuN5 e5 pian2-a2, boan2-lian5, hoe-khou, hoe-na5 che7 gah pai5 tui3 toa7-lou7 piN--khi3, sang3 chhut-soaN e5 tin7-thau5, hoe-chhia ka7 kui2 na7 tiau5 ke-a2-lou7 that gah chat8-kho2-kho2, kui e5 kau-thong long2 loan7--khi3, tiuN5-bin7 chin niau, bo5-su it-poaN e5 sia7-hoe7 hain5-tat8.
Goan2 kou3-hiong si7 chin tan-sun5 e5 sou2-chai7, kong2--lai5 chin sim-sek, ma7 kang7-khoan2 u7 lou5-moa5, goa2 siuN7 sia7-hoe7 to7-si7 sia7-hoe7, bo5-lun7 siaN2-mih8 sou2-chai7 to kok hang5 kok giap8, lou5-moa5 ti7 sia7-hoe7 pian3-cho3 phou2-thong e5 hang5-giap8--a, koh-khah sio-kang5--e5 si7 Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 chhut soaN e5 tiuN5-bin7 ma7 chin hong-tong7.
U7 1 pai2, goa2 tng2 khi3 kou3-hiong, lou7--nih soah that-chhia, chai-iaN2 tiaN7-tioh8 si7 u7 lang5 teh chhut-soaN, koh khoaN3 tioh8 A-pa ma7 toe3 lang5 leh sang3 chhut-soaN, siuN7-kong2 kam2-si7 toh 1 e5 chhin-chaN5 koe3-sin, A-pa kio3 goa2 ma7 ai3 sang3 chiuN7 soaN-thau5, kong2: “Kui chng chhut-soaN e7 hiah nau7-jiat8--e5 ma7 chiah Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 nia5.” 
Chu7 goa2 cho3 gin2-a2 to7 bat Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2, i pun2-mia5 pun2-seN3 soah bo5 siaN2 lang5 chai.  E7 ki3-tit u7 1 e5 chai2-khi2, goa2 phaiN7 chheh-phaiN7-a2 beh hak8-hau7, hit kang lun5 tioh8 goa2 cho3 ‘chu5 ju2 sng’, ai3 khah cha2 khi3 piaN3-sau3, chhut kau3 chng goa7, thiN chiah phah-phu2-kng nia5, khoaN3-tioh8 Lou5-moa5 Siong5-a2 khia5 thih-be2 ui3 tui3-bin7 lop-som7 lop-som7 tah--lai5, kau3 goa2 bin7-thau5-cheng5 soah hioh--loh8-lai5, mng7 goa2 si7 siang2 e5 hau7-seN, nah e7 chiah piaN3-se3, hiah cha2 to7 beh khi3 hak8-hau7.  
Chng-lai7 lang5 long2 chai-iaN2 i am3-si5-a2 e7 khi3 Ji7-lim5 ke-a2 chiu2-ka kap lang5 lim sio-chiu2, poah8-kiau2, thau3-cha2 chiah e7 tng2 lai5 chng--nih khun3.  Goa2 ka7 i po3 goan2 A-pa e5 mia5, koh kai2-soeh kong2 kin-a2-jit8 si7 goa2 ‘chu5 ju2’, chiah tioh8 chiah cha2 chhut mng5.  I kong2 goa2 si7 koai gin2-a2, ai3 jin7-chin thak8 chheh, m7-thang toa7-han3 chhin-chhiuN7 i an2-ni, bo5 poaN3-pou7 chhu2 chiah tioh8 cho3 lou5-moa5, i kong2 beh hou7 goa2 chiN5 be2 si3-siu3-a2, piau2-si7 kou2-le7, nah chai khou3-te7-a2 jim5 bo5 chiN5, chiah phaiN2-se3 phaiN2-se3 kong2: “Cha-me5 kiau2-khui3 chin bai2, su gah ta-ta, seng khiam3--leh, leng7-kang iaN5 kiau2 chiah hou7--li2.  Goa2 na7 boe7 ki3-tit, li2 tng7 tiioh8 goa2 ai3 e7 ki3-tit ka7 goa2 kong2.” 
Pang3-oh8 tng2 khi3 chhu3--nih, goa2 ka7 A-pa kong2 chai2-khi2 tng7 tioh8 Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 e5 tai7-chi3. A-pa ma7 goa2 gin2-a2 lang5 kong2 oe7 m7-thang bo5-toa7-bo5-se3, au7-pai2 ai3 kio3 ‘Siong5 chek--a’. Sui-bong2 Siong5 chek--a2 u7 an2-ni kong2--koe3, au7-jit8 goa7 na7 koh tu2--tioh8, be7-sai2 ka7 i tho2 chiN5.
Goan2 chng ng3 tang si7 Lau7-io5, hit chng kap goan2 chng chia li5 bo5 joa7 hng7, u7 1 tiau5 bong7-a2-pou lou7 sio thang3, lou7 piN e5 chhan5 1-poaN3 Lau7-io5 lang5 1 poaN3 goan2 chng e5, A-lok8--a2 in tau choh e5 chhan5 to7 kap Lau7-io5 lang5 kham2 Chiong--a2 sio keh-kai3, hit chun7 chui2 iau2 chin khoeh, ng3 1 tiau5 Ji2-a2 kau teng2-lau5 Lo5-chui2-khe chui2 in2--lai5, Lau7-io5 seng kau3, in chui2 im kau3 giah8 chiah u7-thang tioh8 goan2 chng, poaN3-me5 ai3 u7 lang5 sun5 chhan5 kou3 chui2.  Ti7 chhan5-thau5-a2 khui 1 khiah, hou7 chui2 jip8 chhan5--nih, koh cho3 1 khut lo5-chui2 ko khut-a2.  Chui2 jip8--lai7 seng in2 khi3 chit khut, chui2--nih e5 thou5-soa lau5 ti7 khut-a2 lai7, im khi3 chhan5--nih e5 chui2 chiah be7 ko-ko, pai7 tioh8 tiu7-chang5, e7-sai2 kong2 chin hui3-khi3.  Teng2-khu chhan5 chui2 na7 phoaN3-tiN7, to7 e7 ka7 chin3-chui2-khiah-a2 that--khi2-lai5, e7-khu chiah u7 chui2 thang im, na7 im u7 kau3-giah8 bo5 that chui2-khiah, im-phong2-phong2 tiu7-a2 e7 noa7 chang5.  Lok8--a2 ai3 e-hian5-a2 sng2 im-gak8, choh chhan5 hong3-hong3, in lau7-pe7 bo5 hoaN-hi2, poaN3-me5 e7 phai3 i khi3 kou3 chhan5-chui2.
	Hit me5, Lok8--a2 ui7-tioh8 te7 2 kang beh khi3 kap lang5 chhut se-gak8-tui7, khoaN3 keh-piah khu kham3 Chiong--a2 e5 chhan5-chui2 chiah8 gah chha-put-to--a, to7 ka-ti7 khi3 ka7 lang5 e5 chhan5-thau5 khiah-a2 that--khi2-lai5, ka7 chui2 lau3--loh8-lai5 im ka-ti7 e5 chhan5.  Koe3 bo5 joa7 ku2, Chiong--a2 lai5, chin siuN7-khi3, iong7 kut8 chui2-khiah-a2 e5 ti5-thau5 peN3 kong3 Lok8--a2, ka7 i phah gah to2 ti7 thou5-kha, kui sin-khu hoeh, pe5 tng2 khi3 chhu3--nih, thiN to ia7 beh kng--a2, chhu3-piN-keh-piah to ia7 long2 cheng-sin5, chun2-pi7 chiah8 chai2-khi2 beh loh8 chhan5, khoaN3 tioh8 Lok8--a2 hou7 Lau7-io5 lang5 khi-hu7, chiong3-lang5 chin put-boan2, tak8-e5 hoah kong2 beh khi3 Lau7-io5 chhoe7 kham2 Chiong sng3 siau3, chit si5-chun7, u7 lang5 kong2: “Kham2 Chiong--a2 hoan pi3-pa3, lang5 chin phaiN2, lan2 bo5 i hoat, iau2-si7 ai3 kio3 Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 lai5.” 
Hit e5 si5-kan, Siong5--a2 iau2 boe7 tng2--lai5, Lok8--a2 in a-pa kap kui2 e5 chhu3-piN to7 khi3 lou7-thau5 tan2, bo5 joa7 ku2, to7 tan2--tioh8-a2. Siong5--a2 thih-be2 oat8--leh to7 beh khia5 khi3 Lau7-io5, chng-lai7 lang5 toe3 au7-piah beh khi3, Siong5--a2 kong2: “Chit-khoan2 tai7-chi3 kap lin2 bo5 ti7-tai7, goa2 ka-ti7 lai5 khi3 to7 ho2.” 
Siong5-se3 e5 keng-koe3 si7 an2-choaN2, goan2 chng--nih bo5 siaN2 lang5 chai, Lau7-io5 lang5 lau3-khui3 tai7 ia7 be7 kong2--chhut-lai5, boe2--a2 si7 goan2 hit pan u7 Lau7-io5 gin2-a2 kong2 hou7 goa2 thiaN--e5.  Kham2 Chiong lang5 toa7-khou-pe2, ti7 Lau7-io5 cho3 lang5 to7 chin pa3, lang5-ian5 m7-si7 chin ho2.  Hit chai2-khi2, Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 khi3 kau3 te3, ti7 mng5-khau2-tiaN5 hoah: “Kham2 Chiong--a2 chhut--lai5 !” 	
Siong5--a2 chhoh kham2 Chiong--a2 bo5 eng-kai khi-hu3 kou2-i3 lang5, Chiong--a2 bo5 thang tioh8 kau3 Siong5--a2 chhim-mng5-tah8-hou7 lai5 kau3 in tau chhang3-chhiu, chiN3 oa2 khi3 beh phah Siong5--a2. Nah chai Siong5--a2 si7 u7 kun5-thau5 te2--e5, sai2 1 e5 se3, chhut chhiu2 khi2 khioh Chiong--a2 e5 kun5, sun7 se3 ka7 i in2 lat8 poah khi3 piN--a2, Chiong--a2 bo5 2 e7 chhiu2, to7 phak ti7 thou5-kha, Siong5--a2 hoan-hu3 i ai3 chhoan5 pin-nng5 hun lai5 Tek-ui5-a2 chng chhiaN2, thong chng seh8 1 lin3 hoe7 sit-le2.  
Than3 Siong5--a2 oat8 thau5 beh khia5 thih-be2, Chiong--a2 giah8 1 ki pun3-chhiam2 ui3 au7-piah thau lak, Siong5--a2 chhiu2-au7-khiau khi3 hou7 chhiam2--tioh8, poah8 loh8 thih-be2, khoaN3 Chiong--a2 pun3-chhiam2 lai7-kiam3-kiam3 koh chiN3--lai5, to7 ka7 thih-be2 leng5--khi2-lai5, tong3-cho3 bu2-khi3 teh hoe5 pun3-chhiam2, bo5 kui2 e7 chhiu2, Chiong--a2 chhiam2-a2 hou7 thih-be2 e5 chhiu2-hoaN7-a2 ke2 lak--khi3, Siong5--a2 ka7 thih-be2 phiaN ti7 thou5-kha, oaN7 khioh hit ki pun3-chhiam2 cho3 ke-si, lak tioh8 Chiong--a2 e5 pak-tou2 piN, Chiong--a2 kui7--loh8 hoah: “m7 kaN2--a”, tap-eng3 beh phang5 pin-nng5 hun lai5 ka7 Lok8--a2 hoe7 sit-le2.  Siong5--a2 iong7 chhui3 ka7 leh saN-a2 ng3-boe2, leng7-goa7 1 chhiu2 khi3 lih 1 liau loh8--lai5, iong7 chhui3 kap chhiu2 phoe3-hap8, ka7 chhiu2-au7-khiau pau--leh chi2 hoeh to7 thih-be2 hu5--khi2-lai7, khia5 tng2 khi3 Tek-ui5-a2 khun3.
Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 tng2 lai5 chng--nih long2 bo5 kong2 siaN2, hit me5 chiau3-siong5 khi3 Ji7-lim5 ke-a2 lim sio-chiu2 poah8-kiau2, hoan-tng2-kang chai2-khi2, thih-be2 khia5 chhut Ji7-lim5 ke-a2 to7 khi3 hou7 heng5-su7 liah8--khi3, kong2 si7 Lau7-io5 lang5 Chiong--a2 po3-an3, ko3 i siong-hai7, u7 pak-tou2 piN lak 1-khang e5 siong-toaN cho3 cheng3-beng5.  Siong5--a2 ti7 keng2-chhat-kiok8 bo5 kong2 goan5-in, kan-taN seng5-jin7 u7 ka7 Chiong--a2 lak 1 khang, bu2-khi3 si7 hit ki Chiong--a2 in tau e5 pun3-chhiam2.  In-ui7 Siong5--a2 thai3-tou7 ho2, kheng2 kap heng5-su7 hap8-chok, m7-koh i si7 siong-hai7 choe7 e5 lui7-hoan7, to7 ka7 i phoaN3 6 kou3 goeh8 tou5-heng5, pun3-chhiam2 si7 bu2-khi3, tong-jian5 ma7 but8-siu.  
Siong5--a2 leh koaiN e5 si5, tiaN7-tiaN7 u7 chheng7 gah chin phaN7 e5 kou-niu5 lai5 goan2 chng--nih, kong2 si7 Ji7-lim5 chiu2-ka e5 sio2-chia2, 1 pai2 lai5 long2 kui2-na7-e5 tau3-tin7, kiaN Siong5--a2 in a-bu2 3 tng3 put tou7, koaN7 chhai3 theh8 bi2 lai5 chu2 png7 hou7 lau7-hoe3-a2 lang5 chiah8.  In ma7 lun5-pan khi3 tham3 kaN, kong2 Siong5--a2 ti7 lang-a2 lai7 ho2-ho2, chhiaN2 in a-bu2 bian2 ui7 i hoan5-lo2.  Siong5--a2 in lau7-bu2 sng3 phaiN2-mia7, siau3-lian5 to7 si2 ang, seN 2 e5 hau7-seN long2 chin put-tong2, 1 e5 cho3 lou5-moa5 hou7 lang5 phui3 tham5 phui3 noa7, 1 e5 khi3 ti7 kha-sau-keng-a2 kou3 khau2 cho3 sam-chhit-a2, i 70 hoe3--a, pun2-te2 to7 khiau-ku, koh ti7 chng--nih kiaN5 lou7 long2 thau5 le5-le5, piau2-si7 cho3 lang5 kian3-siau3, kam2-kak chin kho2-lian5.
Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 koaiN ni5-poaN3 chiah chhut--lai5, i chin3-cheng5 u7 an3 leh ka2-sek, ti7 ka2-sek tiong na7 koh hoan7 an3, koaiN bo5 liau2--e5 ai3 koh han5, m7-chiah e7 ke koaiN 1 ni5, goan2 hia e5 lang5 kong2 he hou7-cho3 ‘kia3 choe7’.  Lok8--a2 kap in a-pa bat khi3 bin7-hoe7, khi3 hou7 Siong5--a2 me7, kong2 chit khoan2 sou2-chai7 m7-si7 it-poaN choh-sit e5 kou2-i3 lang5 la5--e5, 1 lang5 1 tou5 1 khoan2 mia7, choh-sit lang5 kai3 kan-khou2, kui-ni5-thang3-thiN long2 cho3 gah pi2 gu5 khah thiam2, i to7-si7 m7-tam-su-iaN5 be7 kham-tit chhau, m7-chiah e7 kiaN5 chit tiau5 oai-lou7, hou7 chou2-kong-a2 kian3-siau3, Lok8--a2 heng3 sng2 gak8-khi3, ma7-si7 ho2 tai7-chi3, chong2-si7 pheng7 i cho3 tek-ke-a2 khah sit8, tng2--khi3 ai3 kut-lat8, the3 Tek-ui5-a2 lang5 chhut 1 khau2 khui3.
Chng--nih bo5 khoaN3--tioh8 Lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 si7 su3-siong5 e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 bo5 siaN2 koai3-ki5, phou2-thong-si5-a2 bo5 tai7-chi3 i ia7 han2-tit kap chng-lai7 lang5 lai5 khi3.  ThiaN-kong2 Siong5--a2 chhut-kaN liau2, bo5 sui5 tng2--lai5, kong2 khi3 pat8-ui7-a2 the3 lang5 kou3 kiau2-keng than3 koa2 sou2-hui3, it-tit8 kau3 2 ni5 koe3, goa2 chiah koh khoaN3 tioh8 i.  Hit chai2-khi2, beh kho2 chhi3, goa2 khah cha2 beh khi3 hak8-hau7 cho3 chun2-pi, tu2 chhut chng to7 thiaN outoubai2 e5 siaN, si7 Siong5--a2 oaN7 khia5 ki-chhia.  KhoaN3 tioh8 goa2, to7 tong3 tiam7, goa2 chit pai2 e7 ki3-tit A-pa e5 kau-tai3, kio3 1 siaN ‘Siong5 chek--a2’, i tai7-khai3 ki3-chai5 bai2, koh mng7 goa2 si7 siang2 e5 hau7-seN, goa2 koh po3 A-pa e5 mia5, i o-lo2 goa2 koai, to7 cho3 i khi3.  
Goa2 koh kiaN5 kui2 pou7, i soah oat8-thau5 tng2--lai5, mng7-kong2 i bat in2 koe3 1 e5 hak8-seng gin2-a2 kong2 na7 iaN5 kiau2 u7 chiN5 beh hou7 i sou2-hui3 be2 si3-siu3-a2, hit e5 kam2 si7 goa2 ? Goa2 siuN7 tioh8 A-pa e5 oe7, m7 kaN2 in3, kan-taN lioh8-lioh8-a2 tim3 1 e7 thau5.  Siong5 chek--a2 kong2 goa2 gin2-a2 lang5 be7 sai2 kong2 oe7 bo5 siu2 iok-sok, goa2 tap-eng3 i beh ka7 i the5-chiN5, i si7 ki3-ti3 bo5-kai3-ho2 e5 lang5, goa2 gin2-a2 lang5 nah e7 ki3-ti3 ia7 bo5 ho2 ? To7 chhut chhiu2 ui3 au7-khou3-te7-a2 jim5 1 e5 te2 chiN5 e5 phoe5-giap-a2 chhut--lai5, tu hou7--goa2, kio3 goa2 siu--khi2-lai5, goa2 m7 kaN2 theh8, i kong2 goa2 na7 bo5 theh8--khi3, i kin2-cha2-ban7 ma7 e7 koh poah8 su liau2-liau2, khah-su hou7 goa2 cho3 sou2-hui3 kap kau hak8-hui3.
Goa2 ka7 phoe5-giap-a2 kau hou7 A-pa, lai7-te2 u7 3000 kui2 khou, hit chun7 the3 lang5 koah tiu7-a2 1 kang chiah than3 30 nia5.  A-pa hou7 goa2 1 khou gun5, i kong2 beh chhoa7 goa2 khi3 ka7 chiN5 han5 hou7 Lou5-moa5 Siong5 chek--a2, tai7-khai3 si7 i lim sio-chiu2-chui3, chiah e7 hou7 goa2 hiah che7 chiN5.
Siong5--a2 chhut--khi3-a, in a-bu2 kong2 Siong5-a2 e5 tai7-chi3 kap i bo5 koan-he7, bo5-lun7 ho2 tai7 iah phaiN2 tai7, phoe5-pau-a2 i m7 kheng2 the3 hau7-seN siu--khi2-lai5.  Na7 beh to7 tan2 bin5-a2-chai3 i tng2--lai5 chiah koh lai5.
Hoan-tng2-kang, lou5-moa5 Siong5--a2 bo5 tng2--lai5, boe2--a2 thiaN-kong2 ti7 ke--nih u7 lang5 poah8 phaiN2 kiau2, pian2 lang5 e5 chiN5, Siong5 chek--a2 the3 lang5 khi3 beh tho2 1 e5 kong-to7, soah khi2 tong5-tut8, chho3-chhiu2 phah--si2-lang5, tong-tiuN5 hou7 keng2-chhat liah8--khi3, chit pai2 to7 phoaN3 chin tang7--a.
He chiN5 A-pa long2 m7 kaN2 tang7--tioh8, it-tit8 kau3 goa2 kok-hau7 chhut-giap8, kho2 tiau5 tou-chhi7 e5 seng2-chhou-tiong, bo5 chiN5 thang chu3-chheh, A-pa chiah theh8 lai5 iong7, kong2 ai3 kam2-liam7 leh koaiN-kaN e5 Siong5 chek--a2, phoe5-giap-a2 e7-sai2 lau5 leh cho3 ki3-liam7.  Boe2--a2 goa2 to7 long2 ti7 tou-chhi7 thak8 chheh, seng-oah8.  Siong5 chek--a2 chhut-kaN si7 kui2-ni5 au7 e5 tai7-chi3.
Goa2 sia2 chit phiN m7-si7 beh ka7 lou5-moa5 pian3-cho3 eng-hiong5, ma7 m7-si7 u7 siaN2 ki5-koai3 e5 li2-iu5, kan-taN si7 chiau3 goa2 sou2 chai--e5 sia2--chhut-lai5 nia5.
