
Siau2 Tek--a2 liah8 gu5
				

Asia Jilimpo
Goa2 siong7 heng3 chiah8 e5 liau7-li2 si7 o-a2, sasimi kap gu5-pai5. Goan2 kou3-hiong Lok8-kang2, Hong-oan2 ian5-hai2 it-tit8 kau3 Hun5-lim5 koan7 e5 Beh8-liau5, Tai5-se, Si3-ou5, Khau2-ou5, si7 Tai5-oan5 san2 o-a2 siong7 ho2 e5 hai2-ek8, chia e5 o-a2 pui5, m7-koh m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Bi2-kok--e5 hiah toa7 mih, sit8-chai7 si7 choan5 se3-kai3 han2-iu2 e5 ho2 chiah8-mih8.  Goa2 m7-si7 beh kong2 koan-he o-a2 e5 tai7-chi3, goan2 kui ke-hoe2-a2 long2 chiah8 o-a2, che bo5 siaN2 thang kong2, goan2 i--a2 bo5 chan3-seng5 goa2 chiah8 sasimi kap gu5-pai5.
Sasimi si7 hi5-a2 chheN chiah8, goan2 A-i kong2 be7-su chheN-hoan--leh, kiaN chiah8 liau2 phah phaiN2 sin-the2, che goa2 e7-tang3 li2-kai2. Kong2 tioh8 gu5-pai5, to7 chin hui3-khi3, choh-sit lang5 kap gu5 e5 koan-he7, m7-si7 3, 2 ku3 oe7 kong2 e7 chheng-chho2--e5, goa2 kong2 siau2 Tek--a2 liah8 gu5 e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 lin2 chai, to7 e7-tang3 li2-kai2 choh-sit lang5 kau3 taN iau2 kian-chhi5 bo5 beh chiah8 gu5-bah e5 sim-cheng5.
Tek--a2 in tau u7 chiaN5 kah te7, 7 hiaN-ti7-a2 chiu2 chit te3-a2 thou2-te7 sng3 u7-khah kan-khou2.  Kong2--lai5 Tek--a2 in si7 se3-tai7 toa3 chia e5 lang5, nah e7 thou2-te7 pi2 lang5 khah chio2 ? ThiaN-kong2 in a-thai3 e5 si5-tai7, in Chng--ka e5 thou2-te7 si7 hu3-kin7 siong7 toa7 phian3--e5, liau2-au7 1 tai7 koh 1 tai7, lam5-teng siuN ong7, it-tit8 pun ke-hoe2, in a-chou2 si7 5 hiaN-ti7, 1 lang5 tit 5 hun 1, in a-kong koh 6 hiaN-ti7, chhun pun 6 hun 1, Tek--a2 u7 1 e5 a-peh 4 e5 a-chek, an2-ni in a-pa ma7-si7 chhun 6 hun 1 e5 chai5-san2 thang pun nia5, sng3-siau3 khah ho2--e5, e7-sai2 tau3 sng3--1-e7, 5 hun 1 koh 6 hun 1, Tek--a2 in a-pa tit e5 chai5-san2 si7 chou2-thai3 e5 180 hun chi 1, bian2-kong2 ma7 1 te3-a2 nia5.
7 hiaN-ti7-a2 choh kah-goa7 te7, jit8-chi2 beh an2-choaN2 koe3 ? Koh kong2, lioh8-lioh8-a2 to7 long2 e7-tang3 chhoa7 bou2--a, kin2-cha2-ban7 ma7 ai3 hun-khui chiah8, an2-ni, 1 e5 lang5 ma7 chiah pun bo5 2 hun te7, che si7 Tek--a2 in lau7-pe7 siong7 hoan5-lo2 e5 tai7-chi3.  Tek--a2 cho3 toa7-kiaN2 e5 lang5, ai3 pi2 sio2-ti7-a2 taN khah che7 chek-jim7, chu7 siau3-lian5 si5-tai7 khi2, to7 lip8-chi3 beh hak thou2-te7, u7 kang to7 than3, e7-sai2 kong2 cho3 gah bo5-me5-bo5-jit8, chng--nih e5 lang5 m7-chiah kha-chhng au7 kio3 i ‘siau2 Tek--a2’, che m7-si7 oan5-choan5 leh keng5-the2, phi3-siuN3--i, u7 o-lo2 e5 i3-su3 chai7 lai7.
Tek--a2 13 hoe3 khi2 to7 loh8 chhan5, sai2, le5, phang2, thoa loa7-tang5, phah lak8-tak8, hoaN7 chhiuN2-pe7, tak8 hang7 long2 e7.  Che m7-si7 lang5 gau5, si7 gu5 leh thoa leh le5, Tek--a2 ka-ti7 choh m7-chiaN5 sit nia5, toa7-pou7-hun7 e5 si5-kan long2 khi3 in2 sit-thau5, than3 kang.  E7-sai2 kong2 chu7 cho3 gin2-a2 e7-hiau2 chhi7 gu5 khi2, Tek--a2 kap in tau hit chiah gu5 to7 na2 hou7 un7-mia7 e5 soh-a2 pak8 cho3 1 hoe2, 1 kang 24 tiam2-cheng tiN5 an5-an5.  
Nah e7 kong2 24 tiam2-cheng? Thou2-te7 chio2 e5 lang5 chhu3 ma7 toa3 se3 keng, lang5 khun3 khah eh8--leh bo5-iau3-kin2, hit chiah gu5 khah an2-choaN2 ma7 tioh8 1-keng gu5-tiau5.  Tek--a ui7-tioh8 sioh hit chiah gu5, ka7 gu5-tiau5 piaN3 gah chiok chheng-khi3, pang3 jio7 pang3 sai2 sui5 to5 ka7 chheng, an2-ni khah bo5 bang2-thang5.  Hit si5, tu2 chian3-au7, jit8-chi2 bo5 siaN2 peng5-sun7, u7 lang5 chhut-lai5 cho3 chhat8, tou-chhi7 to7 chian2-liu2-a2 thau chiN5-gin5, chng-kha bo5 siaN2 tat8-chiN5--e5, thau liah8 ke liah8 ah si7 su3-siong5--e5, thau khan gu5 sng3 toa7 chhat8-kou2.  Chit khoan2 chhat8-a2 gu5 m7-kaN2 khan khi3 gu5-hi hoah ke3, u7--si7 thai5-thai5 phoa3-phoa3--leh cho3 bah-gu5 be7.  Hit si5 han2-kong2 keh-piah chng u7 lang5 phang3-kiN3 gu5, Tek--a2 m7-kaN2 khun3, ti7 gu5-tiau5 kou3 kui me5, boe2--a2 kui-khi3 ti7 hia ne bang2-ta3, chhu chhau2-chhioh8-a2 khun3.  
Tek--a2 kou3 gu5 pi2 lang5 leh kou3 bou2 khah the2-thiap, un-jiu5.  Gu5 kiaN joah8, Tek--a2 ka7 chhiang5 chui2 na2-chhiuN7 se2 sin-khu an2-ni, chhut mng5 ma7 long2 chah 1 ki tng5 hia-kong2, kau-a2 piN sui5-si5 iuN2 chui2 ka7 lam5.  Na7 u7 pi2-sai3 ‘Chheng-khi3 gu5’, na2 chit si5 e5 ‘chheng-che5 pau5-pau5’ an2-ni, A-tek--a2 hit chiah gu5 tek-khak jip8-soan2.  Siau2 Tek--a2 chiah8 kau3 20 goa7 iau2-boe7 chhoa7, gu5 pi2 bou2 khah tiong7-iau3.  	
Chit e5 si5-tai7 u7 ‘khou5-thi5-i2’, hit si5 ma7 u7 cheng-seN-a2 leh tioh8-che, cheng-chha bo5 chit khoan2 su5 nia5. Tek--a2 hit chiah pou2-poe3 gu5 soah khi3 oe3--tioh8, be7 chiah8 be7 khun3, su3-ki bo5 lat8, chhiaN3 siu3-i lai5 chu3-sia7 ma7 bo5 siaN2 kiaN5-khi3, lang5 toe3 gu5 leh be7 chiah8 be7 khun3, pi2 gu5 khah chiah8-lat8, siau2 Tek--a2 kui e5 lang5 san2-ki-loh8-hioh8, lian7 in 6 e5 sio2-ti7 kap 3 e5 sio2-moe7 to leh ui7 i chhiau-hoan5.  I-hak8 e5 mih8-kiaN7 m7-si7 choh-sit lang5 sou2 e7-tang3 li2-kai2--e5, chu3-sia7, koan3 ioh8-a2, me5 jit8 an2-ni chiau3-kou3, iau2-si7 bo5 hoat-tou7 kai2-kiu3 hit chiah gu5 e5 siN3-mia7.
Si2-gu5 beh hou7 lang5 chhia khi3 sio-tiau7 hit chai2-khi2, Tek--a2 1 e5 lang5 che7 ti7 kau-a2 piN, khoaN3 kau-a2-chui2 iu-iu-a2 lau5, hong 1 chun7 1 chun7 ui3 tek-a2 boe2 io5--loh8-lai5, ka7 kau-a2 te2 e5 hun5-iaN2 kap khong2 e5 thiN so gah 1 leng7 1 leng7.  Chia si7 i tak8 kang khan gu5 lai5 ko7-ek8 e5 sou2-chai7, i pang3 siaN hau2, be7-su si2 pe7 si2 bu2 hit khoan2 hau2-siaN, ka7 thau3-cha2 e5 chhan5-hng5 ta3 1 ian5 ai-chhiu5 e5 bong5-bu7.
Koe3 kui2 kang, u7 lang5 po3-kong2 Tek-ui5-a2 u7 gu5 beh be7--lang5, kio3 A-tek--a2 khi3 siong3 gu5.  Hit khau2 chau3 si7 loh8-kau3--e5, kong2 u7 kui2 na7 hou7 cho3-hoe2 beh i5-bin5 poaN khi3 Pa-se khai-khun2, khi3 hia ma7 tioh8 gu5, m7-koh che7 chun5-a2 kong2-ai3 kui2 kou3 goeh8 chiah e7 kau3, gu5 be7-tang3 be2 chun5-phio3.  Long2-chong2 u7 6 hou7, tak8 hou7 long2 u7 1 chiah gu5, u7 2 chiah i2-keng in2--lang5-a.  4 chiah long2 khoaN3--khi3-lai5 iong2-iong2, chhut ke3 koh chin kong-to7, chng--nih kap i tau3-tin7 khi3 e5 lang5, long2 kou2-bu2 A-tek--a2 hak 1 chiah khi2--lai5, A-tek--a2 4 hou7 chau2 lai5 chau2 khi3, tak8 chiah gu5 siong3 liau2 koh siong3, 1 kang be7-tang3 cho3 koat-teng7, hoan-tng2-kang koh khi3 khoaN3, lang5 kong2 chhoa7 bou2 tui3-khoaN3 ma7 bo5 siong3 hiah kang-hu.
Koe3 1 le2-pai3, A-tek--a2 iau2-boe7 koat-sim, 3 chiah to ia7 hou7 lang5 be2--khi3-a, chhun 1 chiah nia5, i ia7 bo5 siaN2 thang keng2, chu2-lang5-ka si7 chin tiong-hou7 e5 lang5, kong2 A-tek--a2 na chiN5 khah khoeh, e7-tang3 koh kiam2, i u7 thiaN lang5 kong2 A-Tek--a2 sioh gu5 chhut-mia5--e5, ka7 gu5 be7 hou7--i, chu2-lang5-ka chin hong3-sim, chit chiah gu5 m7-si7 u7 siaN2 khiap-si3 bo5 lang5 beh be2, si7 chu2-lang5 m7-kam chhin3-chhai2 be7, ui7-beh po3-tap i tui3 che ka-teng5 kong3-hian3, kam-goan7 siok8-be7 hou7 thiaN3 gu5 na2 thiaN3 bou2-kiaN2 e5 A-tek.
Chhu3 piN long2 khng3 A-tek ka7 be2--loh8-lai5, khi3 gu5-hi si7 liah8 bo5 chiah-ni7 siok8 koh iong2--e5, gu5 to7-si7 gu5, iong2-iong2--a2 e7-kham-tit sai2 to7 ho2.  A-tek--a2 soah khi2 gong7-sin5, i kong2 1 chiah gu5 si7 beh cho3-hoe2 1-si3-lang5--e5, nah e7-sai2 chhin3-chhai2 liah8--1-chiah.  Koh kong2, i siuN7-beh chhoe7 na2-chhiuN7 i koe3-sin hit chiah kang7-khoan2--e5 chiah beh be2.  Thong-siong5 ‘Koe3-sin’ si7 iong7 ti7 lang5--e5, A-tek--a2 ka7 i e5 gu5 si2--khi3 kong2 si7 ‘Koe3-sin’ to7 chai-iaN2 gu5 ti7 i sim-lai7 chiam3 siaN2 khoan2 e5 te7-ui7.
Koe3 1 kou3-goeh8, A-tek--a2 iau2-si7 bo5 gu5 thang sai2, choh-sit-phoaN7 long2 chhio3 i siuN tit8, gu5 si7 beh iong7 lai5 ka7 lang5 tau3 cho3 khang-khoe3--e5, A-tek--a2 soah leh kap gu5 poah8 kam2-cheng5, chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 si2 bou2 3 ni5 lai7 m7-kaN2 beh chhoa7 sin-niu5 kang7-khoan2. 1 iuN7 chhau2 chhi7 pah khoan2 gu5, beh nah u7 kap i goan5-seng hit chiah kang7-khoan2 e5 gu5.  Hu3-kin7 Po2-tau2, khah loh8-nam5 e5 Pak-kang2, long2 u7 toa7 gu5-chhi7, to7-si7 lang5 chheng-cho3 si7 ‘Gu5-hi’--e5, A-tek long2 khi3 chhoe7--koe3, to7-si7 keng2 bo5 1 chiah tau5 i i3--e5.  Beh iong7 gu5 e5 si5, seng ka7 chhu3-piN poah--1-pou-a2, thiap lang5 koa2 chhau2-liau7 chiN5.
Tek--a2 in lau7-pe7 mng7 i kong2: “Hit chiah gu5 na7 iong2-iong2, lan2 to7 ka7 liah8--tng2-lai5, hoaiN5-tit8 si7 1 chiah gu5 nia5, thah tioh8 kho2-lu7 hiah ku2, bo5 gu5 si7 beh an2-choaN2 choh-sit, li2 kan-taN chit cham7-a2 bo5 khi3 in2 khang-khoe3 to7 liau2 joa7 che7 khi3--a ?” 
Tek--a2 in3-kong2: “Gu5 u7 gu5-seng3, kan-taN iong2, na7 m7 chai seng3, i be7 thiaN lan2 ka3.  Lang5 e7-tang3 iong7 kong2-oe7 sio chai-iaN2 seng3, gu5 kap lan2 bo5 oe7 kong2, beh chai seng3 khah oh, hou7 goa2 ke khoaN3 kui2--chiah-a2, bo5 chha hit kui2 kang.  Goa2 m7-si7 hiam5 kui3, si7 kiaN liah8 tioh8 phaiN2 seng3-te7 e5 gu5, he to7 chin hui3-khi3 !”  
U7 1 e5 le2-pai3-jit8, beh-tau3, Tek-ui5-a2 lang5 kui ke khan hit chiah gu5 lai5, kong2-si7 beh sang3 hou7 Tek--a2, chu2-lang5 kong2 hit chai2-khi2 khi3 cho3 le2-pai3, u7 ka7 gu5 e5 tai7-chi3 kong2 hou7 kau3-hoe7 e5 bok8-su kap hoe7-iu2 thiaN, hit chiah ti7 in tau 1-si3 gu5 kap kui ke-hoe2-a2 long2 chiok chhin-kin7, in ka7 gu5 tong3-cho3 ka-teng5 e5 1 hun7-chu2, be7-tang3 chhoa7 i khi3 Pa-se to leh kan-khou2-sim--a, beh ka7 be7-tiau7, ban7-put-it na7 tu2-tioh8 ok chu2-lang5, kiaN e7 khek-khui tioh8 hit chiah gu5.  Choan5 kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5 chai-iaN2 A-tek sioh gu5, i sai2 gu5 e5 si5, chhiu2--nih ma7 e7 theh8 tin5-tiiau5, m7-koh bo5 lang5 khoaN3 koe3 i ka hit chiah gu5 sut--koe3.  Sui-bong2 1 chiah gu5 e7-sai2 be7 put-chi2-a2 che7 chiN5, chong2-si7, khah che7 chiN5 ma7 bo5 lang5 leh be7 bou2 be7 kiaN2, hit chiah gu5 tui3 in lai5 kong2, to7 na2-chhiuN7 chit khoan2--e5.
Chng-kha lang5 chin tiau-tit8, bo5-ian5 bo5-kou3 lang5 1 chiah gu5 beh hou7--in, siu e5 lang5 sim-lai7 be-tit-koe3, koe3 3 kang, A-tek--a2 chhiaN3 1 chhut toa7-hi3 khi3 Tek-ui5-a2 chng poaN, kong2-si7 beh sang3-piat8 li5 hiong khi3 hng7-hng7 m7-chai toh-ui7 Pa-se e5 6 hou7 lang5. Tak8-e5 long2 chai i si7 tit lang5 1 chiah gu5 be7 koe3-sim, choan-kang tap-sia7--lang5-e5.  Hi3-peN5 kha e5 toa7 chhiu7 piN, A-tek--a2 khan hit chiah gu5 ti7 hia, tai5-a2 teng2 lo5-kou2 pat-im long3-long3 kio3, A-tek--a2 it-tit8 leh kap gu5 kong2 oe7, kong2 siaN2 ma7 kan-taN i chai nia5.  Chu2-lang5 e5 gin2-a2 ti7 piN--a2 ma7 bo5 khoa3 khoaN3 hi3, khoaN3 gu5 kap A-tek--a2 leh hou7-siong sek8-sai7, sio-chiN beh chhi7 chia3-boe2 hou7 gu5 chiah8, kau3 beh-am3-a2 si5, A-tek--a2 beh khan gu5 tng2--khi3, siang hong-bin7 long2 lau5 bak8-sai2, u7 lang5 kong2 u7 khoaN3 tioh8 hit chiah gu5 ma7 leh lau5 bak8-sai2.  Chu2-lang5 kui ke ti7 mng5-khau2-tiaN5 bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 A-tek--a2 kap gu5 e5 sin-iaN2, sai-peng5 ang5-ang5 e5 jit8-thau5-kng ka7 in e5 iaN2 giu2 gah chin tng5 chin hng7.
Hit chiah gu5 liah8--lai5 liau2-au7, A-tek--a2 kang7-khoan2 an2-ni le5 an2-ni sai2, ma7 m7-bat ka7 phah, m7-koh, kam2-kak bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 khah cha2 hit chiah hiah chhin-kin7, lang5 cho3-hoe2 to e7 u7 kam2-cheng5 siong7 e5 bo5-kang5, tui3 gu5, bian2-kong2 ma7 e7 u7 bo5-kang5 e5 kam2-cheng5 chha-i7.  Siong7 chio2, A-tek--a2 to7 bo5 ti7 gu5-tiau5 khun3--a, i beh chhoa7 bou2--a.
A-tek--a2 25 hoe3 chiah chhoa7 bou2, hm5-lang5-po5--a2 lai5 po3 chhin-chiaN5, kong2-si7 Koe3-kau-a2 lang5, li5 in chng bo5 joa7 hng7, kio3 A-tek u7 eng5 khi3 thau siong3--1-e7, iah-si7 iok si5-kan tui3-khoaN3--1-e7.  A-tek khi3 khoaN3 1 pai2, koh-si7 hng7-hng7 khoaN3 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 e5 1 e5 heng5-a2 nia5, to7 kong2: “Lang5 na7 bo5 sio khi3-hiam5 to7 ho2--a !”
Hm5-lang5-po5--a2 goan5-pun2 siuN7-kong2 chit khou liah8 1 chiah gu5 ai3 siuN7 hiah ku2, chhoa7 1 e5 bou2 m7-tioh8 ai3 siong3 kui-ni5.  Nah chai tai7-chi3 pian3 hiah kan2-tan, than3 chit e5 ang5-pau-le2 koh chin khin-kho2, chhui3 chhio3 bak8-chhio3 ka7 lang5 kong2: “Aih--ioh, A-tek--a2 liah8 gu5 pi2 chhoa7 bou2 khah teng2-chin, bok8-koai3 lang5 kio3 i ‘siau2 Tekm5a’ !” 
Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3, ti7 hit khoan2 pha-hng e5 ni5-tai7 eng-kai m7-si7 joa7 hi-han2--e5.  Kong2--lai5 goan2 chhu3--nih e5 teng2 1 poe3 long2 iau2 bo5 ai3 chiah8 gu5-bah, to7 e7-tang3 li2-kai2--a.
