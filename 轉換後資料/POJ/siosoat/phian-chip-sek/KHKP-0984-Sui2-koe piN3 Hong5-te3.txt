
Sui2-koe piN3 Hong5-te3
Kou2-cha2 kou2-cha2 u7 chit8 e5 hong5-te3, i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 long2 chin sui2, ki5-tiong soah-be2 cha-bou2-kiaN2 te7 it sui2.  Kiong-siaN5 e5 hu7-kun7 u7 chit8 e5 chin am7 e5 toa7 chhiu7-na5, chhiu7-na5-lai7 u7 chit8 chang5 chin lau7 kou2-chhiu7 Phou5-the5 chhiu7(菩提樹), chhiu7-thau5 hia u7 chit8 khut chui2-choaN5, na7 thiN-khi3 joah8 e5 si5, chit e5 soe3 cha-bou2-kiaN2 chiu7 khi3 chhiu7-na5-lai7, che7 tiam3 chui2-choaN5 piN chhiu-chhin3, na7 bo5 liau5 e5 si5, chiu7 theh8 eng7 ng5-kim sou2 choe3 hit liap8 kiu5 hiat koan5 hiat ke7 lai5 sin5, i chin ai3 chit khoan2 e5 thit-tho5.  U7 chit8 pai2 sin5 liau2 liu3-chhiu2, ka-lauh8 ti7 thou5-kha, soa3 lin3 tui3 chui2-khut loh8-khi3, bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 i tim5 loh8-khi3, chui2 chin chhim, te2 khoaN3 boe7 tioh8.  Ong5 lu2 tiam3 hia hau2, na2 hau2 na2 toa7 siaN,hau2 long2 boe7 soah.  Hut-jian5-kan u7 chit8 e5 siaN tui3 ong5 lu2 kong2:  Si7  an2-choaN3, hau2 kau3 an2-ni, lian5 chioh8-thau5 ma7 oe7 siong-sim?  Ong5 lu2 siuN7 chit e5 siaN m7-chai tui3 to2-ui7 lai5? Ti7 i e5 sin-piN si3-koe3 khoaN3, khoaN3 tioh8 chit8 chiah chin pui5 e5 sui2-koe-a2, phaiN2 khoaN3 e5 thau5-khak chhun tui3 chui2-bin7 chhut-lai5.  Ah!  Li2 ti7 chui2-lai7 teh bun3-lai5 bun3-khi3, goa2 ng5-kim e5 kiu5 ka-lauh8 tui3 chui2-te2 loh8-khi3, thai2 boe7 m7-kam lai5 hau2!  Sui2-koe-a2 in3 i:Tiam7-tiam7, m7-bian2 hau2, goa2 u7 hoat-tou7, goa2 na7 khioh khi2-lai5 hou7 li2, li2 beh sim2-mih8 po3-tap goa2?  KhoaN3 li2 ai3 sim2-mih8?  Goa2 e5 saN,goa2 e5 chin-chu po2-chioh, goa2 ti3 ng5-kim e5 bian2-liu5?  Sui2-koe-a2 kong2, hiah e5 goa2 long2 bo5 ai3, li2 na7 u7 thiaN3 goa2, lai5 kap goa2 choe3 peng5-iu2, chiah8 png7 e5 si5 kap li2 che7 choe3-hoe2, chin sui2 ng5-kim e5 phiat-a2 hou7 goa2 chiah8, chin sui2 e5 poe hou7 goa2 lim, koh hou7 goa2 khun3 tiam3 li2 kho2 ai3 e5 bin5-chhng5, li2 na7 kap goa2 iok-sok chiah e5, goa2 chiu7 loh8-lai5 ka7 li2 khioh.  Ong5 lu2 kong2, Ho2, ho2, kiu5 na7 khioh khi2 lai5 hou7 goa2, bo5-lun7 li2 ai3 sim2-mih8, long2 kap li2 iok-sok (chhui3 si7 an2-ni in2 i, sim si7 siuN7 gong7 sui2-koe, ou peh8 kong2, li2 tiam3 chui2-lai7, kap li2 e5 tang5-phoaN7 saN-kap chhiuN3 koa oe7 eng7-tit, ia7 siuN7 beh kap lang5 choe3 peng5-iu2).
Sui2-koe thiaN tioh8 i hoaN-hi2 kap i iok-sok,thau5-khak chiu7 bun3 loh8 chui2-te2, ke3 bo5 joa7-ku2 chiu7 koh phu5 khi2-lai5, kiu5 ka7 ti7 chhui3-nih, pun5 tui3 chhau2-a2 hia khi3.  Ong5 lu2 koh khoaN3 tioh8 hiah sui2 e5 thit-tho5-mih8, chiu7 chin hoaN-hi2, khioh khi2-lai5; na2 thiau3 na2 kiaN5 than2-hoaiN5 chau2 to3 khi3.  Sui2-koe-a2 toa7 siaN kong2, Theng3-hau7 leh, theng3-hau7 leh! chhoa7 goa2 choe3-hoe2 khi3, goa2 boe7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 li2 kiaN5 hiah kin2.  Sui-jian5 joa7 toa7 siaN ia7 bo5 chhai2-kang, i bo5 teh ka7 thiaN,koaN2-kin2 to3-khi3.  Kau3 chhu3, sui2-koe-a2 e5 su7 long2 boe7 ki3-tit.  Kho2-lin5 e5 sui2-koe bo5 sou2-chai7 thang toa3, chiu7 koh to3-tng2 khi3 chui2-khut-a2.
  Keh jit8, ong5 lu2, hong5-te3 kap ke-lai7 choan5-pou7 e5 lang5 che7 toh, eng7 ng5-kim e5 phiat-a2 teh chiah8 e5 si5, thiau3 chit8-e7, thiau3 chit-e7, tui3 tai7-li2-chioh8 e5 gim5-a2 tit8-tit8 thiau3 khi2-lai5, chiu7 phah mng5. Hong5-te3 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 siang7 soah-be2 e5 lai5 ka7 goa2 khui mng5, ong5 lu2 chiu7 khi3, sim2-mih8 lang5 teh kio3 mng5?  Chit8-e7 khui, khoaN3 tioh8 hit chiah sui2-koe-a2 lai5 che7 ti7 mng5-khau2, mng5 chhut toa7 lat8 kham7 loh8-khi3, koh ka7 koaiN leh, to3-tng2 lai5 che7 ti7 i e5 toh-ui7, chin hoan5-lo2.  Hong5-te3 chai i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 sim-koaN phok8-phok8-chheng2, chiu7 mng7, li2 teh kiaN sim2-mih8, si7 kui2-a2 beh ka7 li2 liah8 khi3 si7 bo5?  M7 si7 , si7 tho2-iam3 e5 sui2-koe-a2.  Sui2-koe-a2 chhe7 li2 beh chhong3 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3?  Pa pa, cha-hng goa2 khi3 chhiu7-na5-lai7, che7 ti7 chui2-choaN5 piN, goa2 ng5-kim e5 kiu5 ka-lauh8 tui3 chui2-khut-a2 loh8 khi3, goa2 ti7 hia teh hau2, sui2-koe-a2 khioh khi2-lai5 hou7 goa2, iit-tit8 pai3-thok goa2 tioh8 kap i choe3 peng5-iu2,boe7 kham-tit i kou-chiaN5, chiu7 in2 i, kap i iok-sok, goa2 siuN7 sui2-koe-a2 si7 tiam3 ti7 chui2-nih, thai2 oe7 lai5 kau3 chia, chit tiap8 ti7 goa7 bin7 beh jip8-lai5.  Iu7-koh phah mng5, toa7 siaN kio3,“Ong5 e5 soah-be2 cha-bou2-kiaN2, ka7 goa2 khui mng5.  Li2 boe7 ki3-tit mah?  Li2 cha2 jit8 ka7 goa2 kong2, ti7 chheng-liang5 e5 chui2-piN.  Ong5 e5 soah-be2 cha-bou2-kiaN2, ka7 goa2 khui.
Hong5-te3 thiaN-kiN3 chiu7 kong2, Li2 kap lang5 iok-sok e5 tai7-chi3, bo5-lun7 chaiN2-iuN7, tioh8 chiau3 iok-sok khi3 khui.  Mng5 khui, sui2-koe-a2 te3 ong5 lu2 thiau3 jip8-lai5, che7 tiam3 i e5 i2-a2 kha, li2 ka7 goa2 liah8 khi2-lai5 teng2-bin7, ong5 lu2 teh tiu5-tu5.  Au7-lai5 hong5-te3 e5 beng7-leng7, tui3 i2-a2 kau3 ti7 toh-teng2, che7 tiam3 toh-teng2 chiu7 kong2, Li2 ng5-kim e5 poe theh8 khah oa2--lai5, lan2 nng7 lang5 thang choe3-hoe2 lim, ong5 lu2 chiau3 an2-ni choe3, tak8 lang5 long2 chai, he m7-si7 ong5 lu2 sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5.  Sui2-koe-a2 tit8-tit8 chiah8, ong5 lu2 chit8 chhui3 to thun boe7 loh8-khi3.  Sui2-koe-a2 chiu7 kong2, Pak-tou2 chiah8 pa2, sian7-sian7, chhoa7 goa2 lai5-khi3 li2 chin sui2 e5 pang5-keng, sui2-sui2 kin3 e5 chhu-kun chhu teh, lan2 2 lang5 tiam3 hia khun3 kiam2 m7 chin  
ho2?  Ong5 lu2 hau2 chhut-lai5, soa3 kiaN, leng2-leng2 e5 sui2-koe-a2 bong to bo5 ai3, koh kong2 beh tiam3 goa2 chheng-khi3 e5 bin5-chhng5 khun3, hit-tiap8 hong5-te3 siu7-khi3, kong2, Bo5-lun7 chaiN2-iuN7, ti7 li2 kan-khou2 e5 si5 pang-chan7 li2, au7-lai5 chiu7 bo5 beh chhap lang5, kiam2 u7 hit ho7 li2-khi3.  Ong5 lu2 chiong nng7 ki chng2-thau5-a2 tui3 sui2-koe-an2 i teh chiuN7 khi3 lau5-teng2, he tiam3 piah-kak hia, ong5 lu2 ka-ki7 to2 tiam3 bin5-chhng5, sui2-koe-a2 thiau3 chhut-lai5 kong2, Chin sian7, chin sian7, goa2 ai3 chhin-chhiuN7 li2 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 song2-khoai3, ka7 goa2 liah8 khi2-lai5, na7 m7, goa2 beh tui3 lin2 lau7-pe7 siong7-sou3.  Ong5 lu2 thiaN liau2 chin siu7-khi3, sui2-koe-a2 liah8 khi2-lai5, toa7 lat8 tui3 piah-nih siak khi3, TaN hou7 li2 khi3 song2-khoai3, tho2-iam3 e5 sui2-koe-a2.
  Chit8-e7 ka7 khoaN3, ka-lauh8 loh8-lai5 koh m7-si7 sui2-koe-a2, si7 chit8 e5 chin sui2, hou7 lang5 e5 bak8-chiu khoaN3 tioh8 chin i3-ai3 e5 hong5 thai3-chu2, chit e5 thai3-chu2 ong5 lu2 e5 lau7-pe7 khoaN3 liau2 ia7 chin kah-i3, chit e5 thai3-chu2 si7 ong5 lu2 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2, ia7 si7 hong5-te3 e5 kiaN2-sai3.  Thai3-chu2 tui3 ong5 lu2 kong2, SiuN7 bin5-a2-jit8 saN-kap to3-lai5 khi3 ka-ki7 e5 kok.  Keh thiN-kng, jit8-thau5 kio3 in khi2-lai5 e5 si5, chit8 tiuN be2-chhia 8 chiah peh8 be2, thau5-khak ti3 peh8 To5-chiau2 (鴕鳥) e5 mng5, ng5-kim e5 lian7-a2, chhia-au7 khia7 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5 hong5-te3 chin7-tiong e5 jin5-sin5.  Chit e5 tiong-sin5 tui3 tian7-ha7 piN3 sui2-koe-a2 e5 si5 chin siong-sim, kiaN-liau2 sim-chong7 piak-phoa3, phah 3 e5 thih-khou khou tiam3 heng-kham2, chit tiuN be2-chhia si7 lai5 ngia5-chih siau3-lian5 hong5-te3 to3-khi3 in pun2 kok, hou7 in nng7 lang5 jip8-khi3 che7 lai7-bin7, i iu5-goan5 khia7 ti7 chhia-au7, hong5-te3 hou7 ong5 lu2 kiu3 chhut-lai5 hoaN-hi2 kau3 tong3 boe7 tiau5.  Chhia kiaN5 bo5 joa7 hng7, hut-jian5-kan thiaN tioh8 chit8 siaN “khiang,” chhin-chhiuN7 kong3-phoa3 sim2-mih8 mih8 e5 khoan2.  Thai3-chu2 si3-koe3 khoaN3 kong2, be2-chhia phaiN2-khi3. Tian7-ha7, m7-si7 be2-chhia phaiN2, si7 goa2 heng-kham2 e5 khou-a2, chin toa7 hoan5-lo2 e5 si5 khou--e5.  (Li2-che7 ti7 chui2-khut e5 si5-chun7), ti7 lou7-tiong chit8 pai2 koh chit8 pai2 thiaN-kiN3 khiang-khiang e5 siaN, mui2 chit8 pai2 thai3-chu2 chiu7 siuN7 si7 be2-chhia phaiN2-khi3, che si7 tian7-ha7 kiu3 chhut-lai5 chin khoai3-lok8 e5 jit8, i sim-koaN chin song2-khoai3, khou ti7 heng-kham2 e5 khou-a2 long2 pe soaN3-khi3.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 984 ho7, 1966 ni5 6 geh8)
