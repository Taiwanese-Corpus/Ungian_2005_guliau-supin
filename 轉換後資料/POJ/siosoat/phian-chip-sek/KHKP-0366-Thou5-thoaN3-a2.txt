
Thou5-thoaN3-a2
Bou2 kok u7 hiaN-ti7 nng7 lang5 cho3 tiau5-lai7 e5 bun5-koaN.  HiaN-ko u7 seN chit8-e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 mia5 kio3 Po2-sian7.  Kau3 14 hoe3, Pe7-bo2 long2 koe3-ong2, chiu7 hou7 in chek chhoa7 khi3 chiau3-kou3.  Sio2-ti7 ia7 u7 seN nng7-e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2; chit8-e5 mia5 kio3 Po2-chu, chit8-e5 mia5 kio3 Po2-gek8.  Po2-chu chit si5 12 hoe3, Po2-gek8 13 hoe3.  In lau7-pe7 chit-si5 siu-the2 ti3-peN7, chiu7 si5 koaN hoe5-ke iong2-peN7.
In chim2 chin thiaN3 i nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, mui2 jit8 kan-ta kou3 se-chng.  Po2-sian7 mui2 jit8 liau7-li2 chu2-chiah8 se2 i-chiuN5,ia7 tioh8 keng2 thou5-thoaN3, ti3-kau3 seng-khu siong5-siong5 u7 thou5-thoaN3 e5 la5-sam5, soa3 ka7 i kio3-cho3 Thou5-thoaN3-a2.  Chong2-si7 hiah-nih8 tioh8-boa5 iah8 m7-bat siu7-khi3, sng3 si7 chin ho2 lu2-tek.  Tui3 lai5 in chek tau 7 ni5 ku2, i nng7 e5 sio2-moe7 chng-thaN7 sui2-sui2 eng5-eng5, i long2 m7 bat kheng-hun, ka-ti7 tak8 jit8 jin7-chit li2-ke.  
Chit-si5 tu2-tioh8 Thai3-chu2 beh chhoa7-bo2, tiau3 thong-kok e5 chai7-sek-lu2, tioh8 lai5 hou7 i chhiaN2.  Hit-tiap8 in nng7 e5 sio2-moe7 che7 chhia beh khi3 hu3 Thai3-chu2 e5 ian5-siah8.  Thou5-thoaN3-a2 ka7 in chim2 pin2 i ia7 ai3 khi3; in chim2 kap i nng7 e5 sio2-moe7 long2 hiam5 i kong2, “Li2 chit pan7 ia7 kaN2 thi2 khui-chhui3 kong2 beh khi3, chin be7 kian3-siau3 leh!.”  Hou7 in chim2 kap nng7-e5 sio2-moe7 chek-pi7, bin7 ang5-ang5 chiu7 koh jip8-khi3 thou5-thoaN3-keng, bak8-sai2 na2 loh8-hou7, chong2-si7 m7-kaN2 hau2 chhut siaN.
Hut-jian5 u7 chit tiuN be2-chhia che7 chit8-e5 chin7 lau7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5, mng7 Po2-sian7 kong2, “Hau2 siaN2-su7?  Goa2 si7 li2 e5 chou2-machai2 li2 e5 kan-khou2, taN li2 ia7 thang khih3u Thai3-chu2 e5 ian5-siah8.”  Chiu7 chiong koain2-a2 loah8 Po2-sian7 e5 seng-khu, chek-si5 piN3-cho3 chheng7 chin sui2 e5 si-tiu5; ia7 eng7 koaiN2-a2 phah nng7 te3 chioh8, chek-si5 piN3-cho3 chit8 siang gek8-e5, chiu7 hoan-hu3 ikong2, “Li2 khi3 tioh8 m7-thang koe3 12 tiam2 tng2-lai5.”  Po2-sian7 pai3-sia7 chou2-ma2 che7 chhia chiu7 khi3  Chong2-si7 hit-si5 lang5 long2 i2-keng teh lim-chiah8 lah!
Thai3-chu2 khoaN3-kiN3 Po2-sian7 hi2 chhut bong7-goa7, pun2-sin theh8 ho2 mih8 hou7 i chiah8, chiah8 poaN3-sek8 liau2, khoaN3-kiN3 teh-beh 12 tiam2, Po2-sian7 chiu7 khi2-sin chhut-khi3; chin ku2 long2 bo5 koh lai5.  Thai3-chu2 chin iu-bun7, si3-ke3 long2 chhoe7 bo5, ia7 m7-chai i e5 seN3-mia5 chu7-chi2.  Au7-lai5 khoaN3-kiN3 toh-kha chit8-siang gek8-e5, chiu7 chiong gek8-e5 chhe Khim-chhe khi3 tak8 sou2-chai7 hou7 chai7-sek-lu2 e5 cha-bou2-gin2-na2 chheng7.  Na7 chheng7 tu2-ho2 hit-e5 chiu7-si7 Thai3-chu2-hui.  Khim-chhe nia2-chi2 chek-si5 khi3.
Hit-tiap8 Po2-sian7 tng2-lai5 kau3 chhu3 iau2-boe7 12 tiam2, i-chiuN5 iu5-goan5 piN3-cho3 kap ku7-si5 tang5, in chim2 long2 m7-chai i khi3 hu3 Thai3-chu2 e5 ian5-siah8.  I nng7-e5 sio2-moe7 kau3 4 tiam2 chiaN3 tng2-lai5, chiu7 ka7 in nia5 kong2, “Kin-a2-jit8 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8-e5 chin-sui2, pi2 sian-lu2 iaN5-koe3 chin che7.”Po2-sian7 ti7 thou5-thoaN3-keng aN3-thau5 chhut lai5 khoaN3 kong2, “Kiam2 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2?”  Po2-chu, po2-gek8 chin siu7-khi3,chek-pi7 i kong2, “Li2 chit-e5 na2 kui2 ia7 teh be7 kian3-siau3.”  Soa3 bo5-i3 bo5-i3 koh kiu-jip8 thou5-thoaN3-keng.
Keh jit8 Khim-chhe lai5 kau3 in tau,kio3 na7 u7 chai7-sek-lu2 long2 tioh8 lai5 chhi3 chheng7 gek8-e5, chheng7 na7 tu2-ho2, chiu7-si7 cho3 Thai3-chu2-hui.  Po2-chu, Po2-gek8 khoaN3 tioh8 gek8-e5 khah se3, chiu7 ti7 lai7-bin7 cham7 chit8-te3 kha-chiuN2, kin2-kin2 chiong pou3 chat ho2, chiu7 beh lai5 chheng7.  Khim-chhe khoaN3-kiN3 kong2, “M7-thang phah la5-sam5 gek8-e5, in-ui7 li2 e5 kha m7-si7 chu7-jian5-e5 iau2 teh lau5-hoeh leh.”  Po2-sian7 ti7 lai7-bin7 ia7 beh chhut lai5 chheng7.  In chim2 kap Po2-chu, Po2-gek8 long2 me7 i, “Li2 be7 kian3-siau3 ia7 kaN2 chhut khi3 chheng7.”Khim-chhe thiaN-kiN3 kong2, “M7-thang hun-piat sui2-bai2, kan-ta chhu2 chheng7 e7 hah8 kha, tioh8 lai5 chheng7.”  Chhut lai5 chit8-e7 chheng7 tu2-tu2 hap8 kha.  Khim-chhe liam5-piN pai3-i cho3 Sio2-chu2-bo2.
Teng2-bin7 sou2 ki3 sui-jian5 si7 siau2-soat,iau2-ku2 u7 thang cho3 lan2 e5 ka3-si7; in-ui7 Ia5-sou bat kong2, “Ai3 beh cho3 toa, tioh8 cho3 lang5 e5 chhe-eng7; iu7 koh chu7-kom5e e7 kang3-loh8 cho3 se3.”(Hoan-ek8)
