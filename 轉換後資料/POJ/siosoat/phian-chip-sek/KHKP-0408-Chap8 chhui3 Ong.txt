
Chap8 chhui3 Ong

Chap8-chhui3 ong ni5-hoe3 lau7-lah!  Thau5-mng5 peh8 chhin-chhiuN7 gin5-si, su3-chi bo5 bah, chhiN-kin phu5 khi2, oan2-jian5 ti-tu bang7, chhui3-phoe2 kut thong2-chhut chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 tui3 e5 soaN-chiam, chong2-si7 bak8-chiu iau2 lai7, khoaN3 chheh hau2-tit eng7 bak8-kiaN3.  KhoaN3 i e5 bin7-mau7 thang kiN3 chhut u7 keng-koe3 se3 kan toa7 iu ut e5 thou5-khut.
U7 chit8 jit8 lai5 liau2 chit8 tiuN phoe, thiah khui chit8-e7 khoaN3, bin7 hian7 tiu5-tu5 liau5 soa3 thou2-khui3, phoe lai7 sou2 ki3 si7 lang5 the ka7 i po3 chit8 e5 sim-pu7.  Chit chan5 si7 hi2-su7, ong thai2-tho2 thou2-khui3?  In-ui7 choe3 chhin-ai3 e5 tang5-phoaN7 chhit-ni5 cheng5 kap i cho3 eng2-oan2 li7-piat8, kin-a2-jit8 beh u7 sim-pu7 lah, chong2-si7 khoaN3 be7 tioh8,koh ti7 chit chan5 ia7 bo5 thang kap i chham-siong5.  Haih!  Ong e5 thou2-khui3 thai3 e7 bo5 in-toaN leh.
Chong2-si7 ong ti7 mng2-keng2 e5 thou2-khui3u pi2 che khah pi-ai--e5, goa2 e5 chhiu2 nachhoah2, chit ki pit na2 chun2 bo5 lat8 thang koh kiah8, m7-ku2 na7 m7-sia2 chhut, sim2-mih8 lang5 e7 chai ong e5 pi-chham2, sim2-mih8 lang5 e7 bat si7-toa7 lang5 e5 in?  Sou2-i2 bian2-kong2 sia2 chhut-lai5 hou7 kiN3-na7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 lang5 thian-liong5 tin2-tang7.
Chap8-chhui3 ong u7 kui2-na7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, chong2-si7 long2 chhut-ke3.  Chiau3 in e5 un7-mia5 u7-e5 cham2-jian5 khoaN3-oah8, siong7 bai2 iau2 bian2 khiam3-khoeh saN-tng3.  Tng hit si5 chhin-ai3 e5 bou2 iu7 koe3-sin, hau7-seN iu7 chhut-goa7 thak8 chheh; sou2-i2 si5 thau5-lou7, cho3 toaN-sin e5 lau7-lang5.
Hai7!  Ong ni5-hoe3 hiah che7, seng-khu chu7-jian5 soe-jiok8, koh tai3 toa7 khui3,siong5-siong5 ai3 sau3; na7 toa7 khi2 e5 si5, kui mi5 bo5 khun3, tng koaN5-thiN e5 si5, i e5 sau3 koh-khah li7-hai7, chhin3-koaN7 tap8-tap8 tin,sau3 liau2 lian5-soa3 chhoan2, hit e5 chhoan2 na2-chun2 beh choat8-khui3.  Ai-ah!  Ong e5 sin-mia7 chiah lam2, sim2-mih8 lang5 u7 siuN7 tioh8 kho2-lin5 leh!  Chong2-si7 ka-ti7 thun-siaN bian2-kiong2 khi2-lai5 koaN7-chheh chhut-mng5 cho3 si3-hng e5 heng5-siong, tu2-tioh8 ho2 ki-hoe7 lian5 chhoan2 tai3 sau3 cho3 Hok-im e5 po3-iong5, chiong be7 chheh e5 chiN5 tou7-oah8, ia7 beh khan-seng5 i e5 kiaN2 kau3 chiaN5-lang5.  KhoaN3 i iau-ki sit-tng3, kha-pou7 bo5 lat8, tioh8 thok koaiN2 pang-chan7, ti7 chit-si5 e5 chham2-keng2 sim2-mih8 lang5 beh ka7 i chiau3-kou3, sou2 siu7-tioh8 e5 lo5-khou2 beh ka7 sim2-mih8 lang5 kong2, in-ui7 chhin-ai3 e5 tang5-phoaN7 koe3-sin lah!  Se3-kan iau2 u7 sim2-mih8 lang5 si7 ti-im leh, bu5-ji7-ko3 kan-ta teh hou7 ong ke-thiN thou2-khui3 na7 tiaN7.
U7 lang5 khoaN3 i an2-ni u7 kho2-lin5 i batlau5 i keh mi5, ho2 khoan2-thai7 i, chong2-siong7 e5 cho3 lang5 peng5-seng se3-ji7, m7-kaN2 hou7 lang5 siuN tioh8-boa5, chiu7 liam5-piN khi2-sin koh khi3 pat8 ui7.
Ti7 hit-si5 ong e5 pi-chham2, pong5-piau e5 lang5 to e7 hiau2 kho2-lin5, an3-choaN2 i e5 si7-se3 iau2 tiam7-tiam7?  U7 lah, siau-sit kau3 ui7,u7 chit8 e5 kiaN2-sai3 khoan3 liau2 put jim2,chiu7 ngia5-chih ong khi3 toa3 i hia, ia7 kam-goan7 hok8-sai7.  TaN ong m7-bian2 koh lo5-khou2 chhut mng5 lah!  Chong2-si7 i e5 thou2-khui3 iu5-goan5 bo5 khah chio2, chiu7 i sin-the2 e5 loan2-jiok8 ia7 bo5 ke khah iong2.
Ong e5 cho3 lang5 peng5-seng m7 bat tui3 lang5 kong2 i e5 kan-khou2, put-ko3 ti7 i khun3-khi3 e5 si5, thiaN i teh chhan, chiah chai ie5 ia3-sian7 kan-khou2 sit8-chai7 chin chhim.
Ong tui3 jip8 kiaN2-sai3 e5 mng5 m7 goan7 peh8-peh8 hou7 lang5 hok8-sai7, chhin-chhiuN7 tak8 jit8 ka3 sun bat ji7, ti3-kau3 bo5 loa7-kul2ong e5 thak8 Seng3-chheh, u7-si5 seng-khu khah khoaN3-oah8 chiu7 chhut-khi3 tiam3-ach2e, u7-si5 pou3-to7, u7-si7 thak8 sin-bun5 houl7ang thiaN.  E5-hng cha2-khi2 chhoa7 kiaN2-sai3 chit8 ke le2-pai3; chiau3 i teh kong2, i tui3 tit-tioh8 to7-li2 chit8-si3 lang5 han2-tit lang3.  Thang chai i u7 it-sim kui Chu2, u7 eng7 chheng-khi3 e5 sin3 kap kian-kou3 ekh5o, sui-si7 seng-khu na2 chun2 beh hoa e5 hoe2, m7-ku2 leng5-hun5 sit8-chai7 iong2-kiaN7.
Tui3 hit jit8 ong chiap-tioh8 phoe e5 liau2-au7 ki3-jian5 thou2-khui3 bo5 lau7 tang5-phoaN7 thang siong-liong5, chiu7 sia2 phoe kap kui2-na7 e5 si7-se3 chham-siong5; na2 e7 chai in choan5-jian5 bo5 liam7 si7-toa7 lang5 ni5 lau7 e5 pi-keng2, u7-e5 kong2 khah ban7 teh, u7-e5 kong2 m7-ho2.  Ong khoaN3-phoa3,am3-chiN7 ka-ti7 siuN7 goa2 chiah lau7 lah,chhin-chhiuN7 beh hoa e5 hoe2, saN-chhian5 si3-chhian5 kiaN liau2 bo5 thang siu7 sim-pu7 e5 hok8-sai7; sou2-i2 ka-ti7 kai2-koat, hoe5-hok chhiang3 le2-pai3.
Tng ong mng2-ni5 lioh8-a2 beh kiam2-chio2 i thou2-khui3 e5 si5, hut-jian5 jiam2-tioh8 phoa3-piN7, piN7-se3 na2 tang7, chiah8 ioh8 bo5 khahho2, chiu7 koat-i3 ai3 beh khi3 khoaN3 kiaN3-kapbe7 koe3 mng5 e5 sim-pu7, chiu7 khi2-sin ko2-jian5 chiau3 i sou2 ai3.  Bo5 phah-sng3 Siong7-chu2 bo5 beh than3 lang5 e5 i3-su3, kengji3an toat8 i e5 hoe3-siu7, ong beh tng7-khui3 e5 si5 bin7 hian2 chhio3-iong5, kiam2 m7-si7 hoaN-hi2 beh khi3 chhoe7 i ku2-ku2 li7-piat8 e5 tang5-phoaN7, m7-ku2 iau2 u7 thiaN-tioh8 i chin se3-siaN e5 thou2-khui3, thang chai i e5 thou2-khui3 iau2 lau5 ti7 se3-kan.
Goan7 thiN-e7 cho3 lang5 e5 kiaN2-e5 tioh8 gau5 iu2-hau3 si7-toa7 lang5, na7 kau3 si7-toa7 lang5 koe3-sin, beh iu2-hau3 chiu7 ti5-liau2.  Tioh8 keng3 lin2 e5 pe7-bo2, chiah thang ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa lin2 Siong7-te3 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5 thou2-te7, chiah8 tng5 hoe3-siu7.
