Ti-hong5 tiN-gian5 bit8-gi2

(KhoaN3 tui3-bin7 e5 tou5.)
U7 chit8-chiah ou-a teh-bah ti7 chhiu7-teng2 teh-beh chiah8.  U7 chit8-chiah soaN-kau2 khoaN3-kiN3, chiu7 chau2 lai5 chhiu7-kha phou-thaN2--i kong2,”Cheng7 goa2 it-seng m7-bat khoaN3-kiN3 chiau2 e5 mng5 chhin-chhiuN7 ou-a-hiaN li2 chiah peh8 chiah sui2, li2 e5 seng-khu iah8 put-chi2 cheng2-che5, phiau-ti3.  TaNchiah e5 m7-bian2 koh kong2, chong2-si7 li2 e5 siaN kaN2 cham2-jiam5 ho2 thiaN.  Li2 e5 siaN na7 chhin-chhiuN7 li2 e5 seng-khu hiah sui2, goaphah2-sng3 bo5 poaN3 chiah chiau2 kaN2 kap li2 pi2.” Hit-e5 ou-a thiaN-kiN3 i-e5 tiN-gian5 bit8-gi2, pak-tou2-lai7 long2 phu7-phu7-kun2, kha-chhiu2 ngiauh8-ngiauh8-tang7, hoaN-hi2 kau3 tong3-be7-tiau5.
Na7-si7 teh siuN7, Chit chiah soaN-kau2 kaN2 e7 li5-li5-a2 giau5-gi5 goa2 e5 siaN bo5 kau3 kek8 ho2,siuN7 tioh8 hau2 hou7 i thiaN, bian2-tit i teh gi5. Tu2-a2 khui-chhui3 teh hau2, hit-te3 bah chiu7 ka-lauh8.  Hit chiah soaN-kau2 ka7 hit-te3 bah cho3 i io5-io5 pai2-pai2, tit8-tit8 kho5 tit8-tit8 khi3, chiaN3 ian5-lou7 chhio3 chit-e5 ou-a hiN7-khang khin, e7 po-so--tit.  (KhoaN3 Long2-sim ju7-gian5 te7 7 bin7).
