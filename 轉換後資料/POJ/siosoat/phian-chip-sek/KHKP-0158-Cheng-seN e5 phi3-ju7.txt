Cheng-seN e5 phi3-ju7
Tui3 Han3-ji7 e5 Goat8-po3 hoan-ek8--e5  
U7 chhiaN3-kang e5 choh-sit-lang5 ka7 chu2-lang5 chhi7 cheng-seN, chit8-e7 ku2 sio2-khoa2 e7 bat cheng-seN e5 oe7.  U7 chit8-jit8 ti7 koaiN cheng-seN e5 sou2-chai7 tin2-tang7.  Gu5 hut-jian5 bak8-chiu toa7-lui2 ma3-ma3-kio3 kong2, Ho2--ah!  Bo5 lang5 e7 iaN5--goa2; peN5-te7 bou7-seng7 e5 chhau2 chhut-chai7 goa2 khun3 chiah8, ti7 hit tiong-kan chu2-lang5 ho2 khoan2-thai7 goa2.  Lin2 kiam2 thang kap goa2 pi2 mah?  
Be2 tui3 piN-a2 sai2 phiN7-khang-kng2 chhio3--i, kong2, Chu2-lang5 iau2-ku2 ka7 goa2 leng7-goa7 pi7-pan7 chit8-e5 sou2-chai7; chhin-chhiuN7 li2 kiam2 thang kap lang5 kong2 mah?  
IuN5 io5-io5 pai2-pai2 tui3 goa7-bin7 lai5 kong2, Che long2-si7 m7-chai ka-ti7 e5 oe7, poe7 tang7-taN3 kiaN5 hng7-lou7 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 e5 khui3-lat8 mah?  Na7-si7 goa2 m7-sai2 koa3 gu5-taN, m7 bat hou7 lang5 khia5, chiaN5-jit8 eng5-eng5 teh chhit-tho5, bo5 lang5 pi2 goa2 khah khoai3-lok8.  Lin2 iau2-ku2 kaN2 khoa-khau2 mah?  
Oe7 kong2 boe7-soah, koh u7 teh au5-kio3 ti7 thiaN-tng5--e5 kong2, Goa2 tak8-pai2 teh chiah8, chu2-lang5 tek-khak tioh8 tai7-seng chhi3 chu-bi8, koh chhut-chai7 goa2 chhut-jip8 ti7 pang5-keng, long2 bo5 sio2-khoa2 kim3-khi7.  Lin2 long2 si7 goa7-bin7 lang5, thai3-tho2 m7 hang5-hok8 ti7 goa2 kha-e7.  Cheng3-lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 khiok si7 chu2-lang5 sou2 thiaN3 e5 kau2; chiu7 long2 tiam7-khi3 bo5 oe7.  
Bo5 phah-sng3 koh u7 chit8-e5 khia7-teh kong2, Goa2 chai lah, thoa-chhia--e5, poe7 tang7-taN3--e5, ka cho3 jiong5-pou3--e5, kou3-mng5--e5, long2 ka-ti7 liah8-cho3 tek-kai2 mah? Chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 e5 mng5-hou7 long2 tioh8 chu2-lang5 khui koaiN, koh kiaN-liau2 goa2 thun be7-loh8--khi3, sou2-i2 ai3 goa2 chiah8 kau3 pa2, chhian2 khoaN3 goa2 e5 pui5-peh8 phauh-phauh toa7.  Lin2 eng-kai tioh8 hoan2-hoe2 oe7 kong2 m7-tioh8.  Ka7 i khoaN3-khi2-lai5 si7 ou-bin7 e5 toa7-ti, kui-kun5 long2 m7 hoaN-hi2.  
Tu2-tu2-a2 teh saN-chiN3 khau2-kak e5 si5-chun7, chu2-lang5 kap chit8-e5 tou5-chi2 kau3, kong2, Goa2 u7 ho2 e5 lang5-kheh, chhiaN2 the3 goa2 keng2-soan2 ho2 e5 chu-bi7.  Tou5-chi2 chim-chiok khoaN3, kau3 boe2 eng7 chit8 tiau5 soh-a2 liu3 hit chiah ti am7-kun2 chiu7 khi3. 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 158 koan3, 1889 ni5 5 geh8)
