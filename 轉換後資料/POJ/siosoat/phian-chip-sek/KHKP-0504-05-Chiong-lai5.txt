
Chiong-lai5
Hoan-ek8   
Bou2 sou2-chai7 Tiong-hak8-hau7 u7 chit8 e5 hak8-seng seN3 Lim5 mia5 Ki5-seng7, peng5-sou3 hong3-chhiong3, bo5 ai3 bian2-kiong2.  U7 chit8 me5 kap in e5 tang5-phoaN7 toa3 hak8-liau5 lai7 poah8 pai5-a2, kau3 hoan saN-tiam2 goa7 cheng, chiah jip8 khi3 khun3; sian-siN sou2 ka3 e5 kho3-theng5, bo5 un-sip8 poaN3-hang7. Sou2-i2 keh jit8 teh thak8-chheh e5 si5, mng7 i lek8-su2, in3 be7-tit chhut; kah i sng3-sut8, sng3 be7-tit lai5; kio3 sia2 Eng-bun5, sia2 bo5 lou7 khi3; kah i oat8-liam7 Kok-gu2, ia7-si7 oat8-liam7 be7-tit chhut.  
I hit-si5 ka-ti7 siuN7,“Goa2 khia7-teh ia7 be7 pi2 pat8-lang5 khah se3-han3, sin-chheng7 ia7 be7 pi2 pat8-lang5 khah bai2.  Lun7-khi2 bun5-ji7, long2 si7 in3 be7-tit chhut; sui-jian5 sian-siN bo5 chek-pi7, goa2 ka-ti7 kiam2 long2 m7-chai thang kian3-siau3?”Sou2-i2 hit jit8 chheh thak8 liau2 e5 si5, chau2 jip8-khi3 kau3 pang5-lai7, chiu7 chiong chheh chhut-lat8 tui3 toh-teng2 piaN--loh8-khi3, thou2 chit8 e5 toa7-khui3, kong2,“Goa2 Lim5 Ki5-seng7, sui-jian5 bo5 chai5-tiau7, lian5-tau3 soa3 pi2 Ong5 Bun5-it pi2 be7 chiuN7 mah?  Lek8-su2 i in3 e7 chhut, sng3-sut8 ia7 sng3 e7 lai5; Eng-bun5 i sia2 e7 tioh8; Kok-gu2 ia7 oat8-liam7 e7-tit chhut. HaiN3-ah!  Tui3 taN liau2-au7, goa2 tioh8 hun3-chi3, eng7 sim. Chiong-lai5 bo5 lun7 sim2-mih8 kho3-theng5, goa2 tioh8 ai3 tai7-seng u7-pi7 ho2-ho2, cho3 kau3 m7-thang su--lang5, chiah be7 hou7 lang5 thi2-chhio3; ia7 thang ke3-kau3 chit e5 lau3-khui3 to3-tng2 lai5, hou7 lang5 khoaN3-khoaN3 leh!” 
 Chiong-lai5 Lim5 Ki5-seng7, na7 m7-si7 kin-me5 ti7 Li2 Chu2-bi2 e5 pang5-lai7 teh bong pai5-a2, chiu7-si7 hit-me5 ti7 IuN5 Kiok-jin5 e5 pang5-keng teh kiaN5-ki5, kho3-theng5 iu5-goan5 bo5 pi7-pan7 poaN3-hang7.  
Chiong-lai5 Lim5 Ki5-seng7, siat-su2 m7-si7 chit me5 ti7 Ong5 Ek8-jin5 pang5-lai7 teh kong2 chhiau3-tam5, chiu7-si7 hit-me5 ti7 ka-ti7 pang5-keng teh khoaN3 siau2-soat; kho3-theng5 ia7-si7 chit8 hang7 to bo5 khi3 u7-pi7.  
Lim5 Ki5-seng7 hian7-chai7 si7 chit khoan2, Chiong-lai5 ia7 si7 chit khoan2, koh-khah chiong-lai5 iu5-goan5 si7 chit khoan2.  

U7 chit8 cha2-khi2, Lim5 Ki5-seng7 iau2 toa3 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teh cho3 bang7, hut-jian5-kan thiaN-kiN3 pang5-mng5 phah kau3 pin3-pong3-kio3.  U7 chit8 tiuN tian7-po3 tui3 mng5-phang7 nng--jip8-lai5.  I chiu7 bak8-chiu joe5-joe5 teh, chiong hit tiuN tian7-po3 thiah-khui lai5 khoaN3, goan5-lai5 si7 po3 lau7-pe7 si2--khi3 e5 phaiN2 siau-sit.  I chiu7 be7 kim3-tit“Ai--oh!”chit8 siaN, chiu7 toa7 khau3--khi2-lai5. Hit-si5 chiu7 khi3 Lau7-tiuN2 pin2 kou3-ke3.  
TaN hau7-tiuN2 khoaN3-kiN3 hit tiuN tian7-po3 chu7-jian5 tioh8 chun2 i to3-khi3; to3-lai5 kau3 in chhu3--nih8, be7 bian2-tit tioh8 siat-hoat bai5-chong3 in lau7-pe7 e5 su7.  Kau3 tai7-chi3 beng5-pek8 liau2-au7, u7 chit8-jit8 in lau7-bo2 kio3 i jip8-khi3 pang5-nih8, ka7 i kong2,“Ki5-seng7 ah!  Cheng5 lin2 lau7-pe7 ti7-teh e5 si5, sui-jian5 than3 bo5 loa7-che7 chiN5, goan2 nng7 lang5 khiu5-khiu5 khiam7-khiam7, ke-lai7 e5 khai-eng7, kap li2 hak8-hau7 e5 sou2-hui3, iau2-ku2 bian2-kiong2 e-lai5 sak-khi3.  Hian7-chai7 si7 be7-e7 lah!  Lin2 che2-hu ti7 Pak-kiaN e5 Iu5-pian7-kiok8 teh chiah8 thau5-lou7.  Li2 khi3 chhoe7 i, khoaN3 i e7 ka7 li2 tau3-chhoe7 chit8 e5 chit-giap8 bo5?  Lu2-hui3 na7 bo5 kau3, soa3 chiong goa2 e5 chiam-a2-thau5-chhah theh8 khi3 tng3.  Bou5 u7 su7-giap8 thang cho3 e5 si5, li2 tioh8 chin7-sim khin5-khin5 khi3 cho3.  Goa2 taN ni5-hoe3 chiah che7 lah, sou2 kho3 si7 li2 chit8 lang5.  Li2 na7 chiau3 cheng5 e5 khoan2, m7-chai thiN-te7 loa7-tang7, goa2 chiu7 oah8-oah8 hou7 li2 khi3--si2 lah!”Ki5-seng7 in3, kong2, “M7-bian2 li2 lau7-lang5 hoan-hu3. Goa2 na7 bou5 u7 su7-giap8, chu7-jian5 tioh8 khin5-kin2 bian2-kiong2 lai5 pan7.  Na7 bo5, m7-tan7-na7 lian5-lui7 tioh8 che2-hu, kap hou7 li2 lau7-lang5 khoa3-lu7, goa2 ka-ti7 beh than3 chit8 oaN2-png7 chiah8, ma7 soa3 hui3-khi3.  An2-nia5!  Goa2 sit8-chai7 ka7 li2 kong2, goa2 i2-keng lip8-chim3 na7 ti7 Pak-kiaN bou5-tioh8 su7-giap8, m7-tan7-na7 ho2-ho2 khi3 pan7, si5-kan-goa7 iau2-ku2 beh ke khoaN3 koa2 chheh, kiu5 koa2 sin e5 ti3-sek, thang pi7-pan7 chiong-lai5 e5 hoat-tat8.”  
In lau7-bu2 thiaN kau3 chia, chiu7 chah8--loh8-khi3 kong2, “Goa2 kiaN2 ah!  Li2 u7 chit ho7 e5 chi3-khi3, goa2 mih8-sai2 ke hoan-hu3? Li2 na7 e7 an2-ne, chiu7 lin2 lau7-bo2 ti7 chhu3--nih8 kan-kan khou2-khou2, ia7 kan-khou2 chit8 e5 tat8.”Ki5-seng7 kap in lau7-bo2 kong2 chiah e5 oe7 liau2, chiu7 tiaN7-tioh8 chit8 e5 jit8-chi2 khoan2 heng5-li2, kap in lau7-bo2 sio-si5, khi2-sin khi3 Pak-kiaN chhoe7 in che2-hu lah.  

Kau3 Pak-kiaN e5 si5, in che2-hu ho2-le2 khoan2-thai7 i, chiu7 si3-koe3 ka7 i chhoe7 thau-lou7, hui3-liau2 chin-che7 sim-sin5, chiah bou5 tiaN7-tioh8, hou7 i toa3 Iu5-pian7-kiok8 cho3 Au-bun5 kiam2-sin3-seng (kiam2-cha goa7-kok e5 phoe-sin3).  SiaN3 chai i kau3 Pak-kiaN, chiu7 chiong toa3 ke-lai7 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, it-khai3 pang3 hiat-kak8.  Chhut khin5 bo5 chiuN7 chit8 le2-pai3 ku2, chiu7 cho3 nng7, saN chan5 e5 m7-tioh8; m7-si7 phoe-sin3 kiam2 chho3 khi3, chiu7-si7 bo5 chiau3 si5-kan chhut-khin5, khah-ban7 nng7-tiam2 goa7 cheng chiah kau3 su7-bu7-sou2.  Iu5-pian7-kiok8-tiuN2 khoaN3-liau2 be7 eng7-tit, chiu7 loh8-beng7 ka7 i si5.  In che2-hu sui-jian5 thiaN3-thang3 i, ia7 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang tau3 pang-chan7, kou-put-chiong sang3 sou2-hui3 hou7 i, kah i to3-khi3 chhu3--nih8.  
TaN, Ki5-seng7 e5 lau7-bo2, sng3-si7 chit8 e5 ui3 kian3-siau3, u7 khi3 chiat e5 ho2 lau7-bo2.  Tui3 in tiong7-hu si2-au7, ka-poe7 e5 khin5-khou2, me5-jit8 phang2-chit cho3 chiam-chi2.  Goan5-pun2 u7 khng3 chit8 e5 toa7 ng3-bang7 ti7-teh, chiu7-si7 choan-choan ti7 Ki5-seng7 e5 sin-chiuN7; ng3-bang7 Ki5-seng7 toa3 Pak-kiaN, ke3-bou5 su7-giap8, e7-tit khin5-khiam7 lai5 seng5-ke ti3-hoe2.  U7 chit8-jit8 pou3 chit-liau2 e5 si5, kap keh-paih e5 toa7-so2 kong2 eng5-oe7, i hoaN-hoaN hi2-hi2 chiong Ki5-seng7 beh chhut-mng5 hit-si5 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, kong2 hou7 toa7-so2 thiaN.  Toa7-so2 thiaN-liau2 chiu7 kong2,“Li2 u7 chit khoan2 e5 kiaN2-ji5, chin thang kiong-hi2; lun7-kau3 goa2 chiu7 chin kho2-lian5.”In nng7-e5 chit8 lang5 chit8 ku3 iau2 teh kong2 e5 si5, hut-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 Ki5-seng7 chau2 jip8-lai5, be7 bian2-tit toa7-kiaN giau5-gi5. In lau7-bo2 chiu7 koaN2-kin2 mng7 i, kong2, “Li2 an3-choaN2-iuN7 liam5-piN to3-lai5?”Ki5-seng7 in3, kong2,“M7-bian2 kong2!  M7-bian2 kong2!  Si7 goa2 chin phaiN2-un7; sim-lai7 siuN7 beh cho3 ho2, to cho3 be7 chiaN5, chiu7-si7 an2-ni2 lah.”  
“Goa2 kau3 Pak8-kiaN e5 si5, goan2 che2-hu ka7 goa2 chian3 ti7 Iu5-pian7-kiok8, cho3 au7-bun5 e5 kiam2-sin3-seng.  Goa2 pun2 lip8-chi3 beh khin5-kin2 lai5 pan7, bo5 siuN7 chhut-khin5 e5 te7-ji7 jit8, hit e5-hng ti7 hi3-hng5 khoaN3 hi3, siuN am3 to3 lai5.  Te7-saN jit8 khun3 kau3 chap8-tiam2 cheng chiah khi2-lai5, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 pi7-pan7 chiah8-png7, kau3 chap8-it tiam2 cheng chiah kau3 su7-bu7-sou2.  KhoaN3-kiN3 Iu5-pian7-kiok8-tiuN2 to bo5 ke kong2 oe7, liah8-cho3 bo5 iau2-kin2, au7-koe3 khah-cha2 lai5 khi3, chiu7 e7 eng7-tit.  Te7-lak8 jit8 cha2-khi2, goa2 
khia7 hit keng chhu3 e5 keh-piah, teh poah8 pai5-a2.  Goa2 ia7 ham in i7; hit jit8 si7 pai3-lak8.  Goa2 teh bong pai5 a2 e5 si5-chun7, pun2-te2 siuN7 beh khi2-sin lai5 khi3.  Au7--lai5 siuN7, kin-a2-jit8 poaN3-jit8 na7-tiaN7, bo5 khi3 bo5 iau2-kin2; bin5-a2-jit8 khah-cha2 lai5 khi3 chiu7 oe7 sai2-tit.  Kau3 te7-chhit jit8 sui-jian5 si7 Le2-pai3-jit8, chiau3-le7 iau2 u7 kui2 tiam2 cheng tioh8 pan7 su7.  Goa2 beh jip8 Iu5-pian7-kiok8 e5 si5, goan2 che2-hu u7 it-giu7 it-chap8 ka7 goa2 kong2.  Goa2 hit-jit8 to2 ti7 bin5 chhng5, ia7 siuN7 beh lai5 khi3; au7--lai5 siuN7, kin-a2-jit8 si7 Le2-pai3-jit8, tak8 ui7 to u7 hioh, bo5 khi3 ma7 si7 bo5 iau2-kin2.  Goa2 chiu7 an2-ni bo5 khi3.”  
“Tui3 an2-ni Iu5-pian7-kiok8-tiuN2 hiam5 goa2, kong2, m7-si7 siuN ban7, bo5 chiau3 si5-kan, chiu7-si7 khiam3-khin5.  sou2-i2 te7-ji7 le2-pai3 e5 pai3-it, goa2 khi3 su7-bu7-sou2 e5 si5, i chiu7 tng-bin7 kah goa2 khi3 chhoe7 pat8-mih8 thau5-lou7.”  
Ki5-seng7 kong2 kau3 chia, in lau7-bo2 i2-keng khi3 kau3 hun7-lun7-loh8--khi3.  Ki5-seng7 koaN2-kin2 ka7 i chhah--teh, chiu7 kong2, “Li2 lau7-lang5 m7-bian2 siu7-khi3 kau3 an2-ne. Goa2 put-ko3 chit-tiap8 kiaN5-tioh8 phaiN2-un7. KhoaN3 mia7--e5 ka7 goa2 kong2, na7 chit e5 phaiN2-un7 koe3, chiu7 ho2 lah.”  
In lau7-bo2 long2 m7 cho3-siaN.  Chhut-chai7 Ki5-seng7 an3-choaN2-iuN7 kong2, i long2 tiam7-tiam7, m7 in3 i.  Tui3 an2-ni chit8-jit8 koe3 chit8-jit8, lim, chiah8, chiam7-chiam7 kiam2-chio2, me5-jit8 to2 toa3 bin5-chhng5--nih8, chin-chiaN3 oah8-oah8 hou7 i khi3--si2 liau2. 
TaN chit e5 Ki5-seng7 tui3 in lau7-pe7 si2 au7, i2-keng chiu7 chin san3, koh chiap-tioh8 in lau7-bo2 si2, chiu7 chiong sou2 khia7 e5 chhu3, be7 lai5 cho3 song-hui3, kau3 song-su7 beng5-pek8, chhun chit8-e5 na2 lo5-han3 e5 khoan2.  U7 chit8-jit8 i e5 ku7 tong5-chhong Ong5 Bun5-it tui3 siaN5-mng5-khang keng-koe3, khoaN3-kiN3 Ki5-seng7 to2 ti7 thou5-kha teh khun3, kah chit8-nia2 phoa3-noa7 e5 chhau2-chhioh8, be7 kim3-tit toa7 kiaN sit-sek, chiu7 kong2,“Ki5-seng7 hiaN kin7-lai5 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3.  An3-choaN2 piN3 kau3 an2-ne?”Ki5-seng7 in3 i, kong2, “M7-bian2 kong2, m7-bian2 kong2, si7 goa2 kiaN5 tioh8 phaiN2-un7.  Chong2--si7 che put-ko3 chiam7-si5 an2-ne cho3, chiong-lai5 chiu7 tioh8 ke2-oaN7, ke2-oaN7 lah.  TaN li2 beh 
to2-khi3?” Ong5 Bun5-it kong2, “Goa2 chit8 goeh8-jit8 cheng5 ti7 Hok-im-tng5 cho3 le2-pai3.  Ng5 Bok8-su sang3 goa2 chit-pun2 Seng3-keng, goa2 toa3 to3-khi3 chhu3-nih8 khoaN3, khoaN3 liau2 put-chi2 u7 to7-li2.  In-ui7 an2-ni, goa2 koat-i3 beh jip8 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 lai5 thak8-chheh, thang pi7-pan7 chiong-lai5 cho3 Thoan5-to7-su, ka7 sia7-hoe7 cho3 chit8-tiam2-a2 hok8-bu7 e5 kang-hu.  Ng5 Bok8-su si7 thau5-nau2 kek8 chheng-chho2 e5 lang5; kap i kong2 oe7, khah iaN5-koe3 thak8 loa7-che7 chheh.  Goa2 kin-a2-jit8 beh lai5 khi3 tham3 i, li2 ia7 cho3-hoe2 lai5 khi3 kiaN5-kiaN5 leh, ho2--m7?  Na7 boeh, goa2 thng3 chit8-nia2 hiu5-a2-lam hou7 li2 chheng7, goa2 chheng7 hiu5-a2 chiu7 ho2.”Ki5-seng7 kong2,“An2-ne goa2 chiu7 kap li2 lai5 khi3 chhit-tho5-lo5-leh.” Ong5 Bun5-it chiu7 thng3 hiu5-a2-lam khi2-lai5 hou7 i chheng7.  Nng7 lang5 saN-kap tui3 Hok-im-tng5 lai5.  Kau3-ui7 e5 si5, Ong5 Bun5-it chiu7 siau7-kai3 hou7 Ng5 Bok8-su kap Ki5-seng7 sio-bat.  Nng7 lang5 kong2 kui2-ku3-a2 tho3-thau5-oe7, Ki5-seng7 chiu7 chiong tui3 Hak8-hau7 teh thak8-chheh khi2, kau3 in lau7-bo2 si2 e5 phaiN2-un7 kan-khou2, it-gou7 it-chap8, tit8-tit8 kong2 hou7 Ng5 Bok8-su thiaN.  
Ng5 Bok8-su i2-keng thiaN-liau2, chiu7 eng7 toan-chiaN3 e5 bin7-sek ka7 i kong2, “Peng5-iu2 ah, li2 e5 mau5-peng7 chiu7-si7 ti7 chit e5 sou2-chai7.  Tai7-tiong7-hu hian7-chai7 lip8-chi3, hian7-chai7 chiu7 tioh8 khi3 kiaN5. Hian7-chai7 m7 khi3 kiaN5, kong-im su7 chian3, jit8-goat8 ju5 so; bak8 chit8-nih8 ku2, chiong-lai5 liam5-piN piN3-chiaN5 hian7-chai7.  Koh chit8-e7 m7 khi3 kiaN5, chiong-lai5 iu7 piN3-chiaN5 hian7-chai7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni chiau3-ku7 bo5 ke2-piN3, chit8-jit8 thoa-thih8 koe3 chit8-jit8, sit8-chai7 e7 gou7 lang5 kau3 si2 lah.  Seng3-keng u7 kong2,‘Li2 tioh8 than3 jit8--si5 cho3 kang; me5--si5 na7 kau3, li2 chiu7 be7 cho3 lah.’Li2 ti7 hak8-hau7, pe7-bo2 long2 ti7-teh e5 si5, thang kong2 si7 li2 e5 jit8--si5.  Kau3 chit-tiap8 khiok si7 li2 e5 me5--si5; li2 chiu7-si7 beh cho3, khui3-lat8 ia7 be7 than3 li2 e5 sim-goan7 lah.  Chong2--si7 li2 na7 kheng2 koat-i3 hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3, iau2 sng3 be7 siuN-ban7.”  
Ki5-seng7 thiaN-liau2 toa7 hiau2-gou7, chiu7 kong2, “Goan5-lai5 si7 an2-ni; goa2 tui3-taN i2-au7, chai ka-ti7 tioh8 an3-choaN2 lai5 iok-sok lah.”Ng5 Bok8-su kong2,“Li2 na7 u7 eng5, chek-koan2 lai5 chia, tai7-ke tam5-lun7.”Ki5-seng7 kong2, “Chin to-sia7 chin lou2-lat8!” Hit-si5 Ong5 Bun5-it koh kap Ng5 Bok8-su kong2 kui2 ku3-oe7, chiu7 kap Ki5-seng7 to3-khi3.  

Lim5 Ki5-seng7 tui3 chit-pang siu7 Ng5 Bok8-su e5 ka3-si7 liau2-au7, toa7-toa7 u7 chheN2-gou7, mui2 le2-pai3-jit8 chiu7 lai5 Ng5 Bok8-su hia le2-pai3.  Ng5 Bok8-su khoaN3 i chiam7-chiam7 u7 chin-chiaN3 hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3 lah, chiu7 thiap chiN5 hou7 i ti7 Sin5-hak8-hau7 thak8-chheh.  Kau3 chit-tiap8 khiok chiaN5 cho3 Kiu3-chu2 chit8-ui7 e5 iong2-chiong3 lah.  
Chiau3 teng2-bin7 chit-chan5 sou2 khoaN3, kau3-giah8 thang chai Kiu3-chu2 u7 toa7 koan5-leng5, e7 ke2 lang5 e5 ku7-seng3, oaN7 lang5 e5 phin2-heng7.  
Siok8-gu2 kong2,“San kho2 i5, seng3 put-kho2 kai2.”Lang5 ti7 chit se3-kan long2 u7 i chit8 hang7 e5 mau5-peng7, chit8 hang7 e5 phoa3-keh ti7-teh.  Che7-che7 e5 poah8-kiau2 lang5, bat hou7 koaN-hu2 liah8 khi3 kong3 kha-chhng, kong3 kau3 noa7-hap-hap; chit8-pai2 koe3 chit8-pai2 i ia7 be7 kian3-siau3, be7 ke2-piN3.  Cho3 chhat8--e5 ia7-si7 an2-ni, hou7 koaN--lang5 liah8 khi3 koaiN, chit8 koe3 koh chit8 koe3, piN3-chiaN5 koan3-to7, khoaN3 kaN-gak8 na2-chun2 chhin-chhiuN7 in tau. Chiah8 a-phian3 ia7-si7 an2-ni.  u7-si5 hoan7-choe7 hong5 liah8 khi3 koaiN, tui3 an2-ni chiu7 ke2--khi2-lai5, na7-si7 chhut-lai5 chiu7 koh chiah8.  Iu5-goan5 chit8-pai2 koe3 chit8-pai2, long2 ke2 be7 khi2-lai5.  
Chong2--si7 na7 tui3 Ia5-sou Seng3-kau3 siat-lip8 e5 i-koan2, lai5 kiu5 ke2 hun e5, chiu7 long2 ke2 kau3 li7--li7-li7.  Che si7 bo5 pat8-mih8 e5 in-toaN, in-ui7 siu7-tioh8 kiu3-chu2 e5 ka3-si7, chai che m7-ho2 chiu7 m7-kaN2 koh khi3 hoan7-tioh8.  M7-tan7-na7 chiah8 a-phian3--e5, chiu7-si7 poah8-kiau2, cho3-chhat8, i2-kip8 hong3-chhiong3 koan3-si3, pin5-toaN7 seng5-seng3, tak8 ho7 e5 phaiN2-lang5, chit8-e7 siu7-tioh8 to7-li2 e5 ka3-si7, chiu7 e7 oaN7-sin lai5 teng5-thau5-siN. Boh8-kioh8 hit e5 Lim5 Ki5-seng7, goan5-pun2 si7 u7 chai5-tiau7 e5 lang5, put-ko3 hong3-tong7 pin5-toaN7 e5 sip8-koan3, i ia7 siuN7 chiong-lai5 tioh8 ke2-piN3, chiong-lai5 m7-thang an2-ni. M7-ku2 boe7 siu7 Seng3-keng e5 ka3-si7, iau2-ku2 si7 nng2-chiaN2 bo5 lat8, bo5 koat-toan3 e5 sim, sou2-i2 chit8-e7 tit-tioh8 to7-li2, chiu7 cho3 Chu2 toa7 lou7-eng7 e5 kun-koaN. 
 Ia5-sou bat kong2,“Goa2 lai5 m7-si7 beh chio ho2-lang5, si7 beh chio phaiN2-lang5 hoan2-hoe2 na7-tiaN7.” Chin-chiaN3 si7 u7-iaN2. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 504~505 koan3, 1927 ni5 3~4 geh8)
