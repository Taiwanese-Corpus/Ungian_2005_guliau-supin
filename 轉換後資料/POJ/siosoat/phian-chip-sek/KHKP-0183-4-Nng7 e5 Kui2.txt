
Nng7 e5 Kui2
Hoan-ek8   
Bou2 jit8 u7 nng7 e5 kui2 ti7 ke--nih8 kiaN5-iu5, chit8-e5 si7 pu3-kui2, chit8-e5 si7 kiong5-kui2.  Pu3-kui2 hiaN-ti7 gou7 lang5, toa7--e5 mia5 Ke-kha-kui2, te7-ji7--e5 mia5 Chng-oe7-kui2, te7-saN--e5 mia5 Hang2-lang5-kui2, te7-si3--e5 mia5 Kong-phian3-kui2, te7-gou7--e5 mia5 Cheng-leng5-kui2. In e5 pe7 mia5 Chheng-ni5-kou2-koai3, saN-kap liu5-thoan5 kong2 chit gou7 e5 kui2 bat nau7-koe3 phoaN3-koaN.  Kiong5-kui2 hiaN-ti7 chap8 lang5, toa7--e5 mia5 Hou5-tou5-kui2, te7-ji7--e5 mia5  Hong3-tong7-kui2, te7-saN--e5 mia5 Thoa-boa5-kui2, te7-si3--e5 mia5 Kip-hong-kui2, te7-gou7--e5  mia5 Hun7-kui2, te7-lak8--e5 mia5 Kan-kui2, te7-chhit--e5 mia5 Khut-si2-kui2, te7-peh--e5 mia5  Kong5-kui2, te7-kau2--e5 mia5 Chiu2-kui2, te7-chap8--e5 mia5 IuN5-ian-kui2.  In e5 pe7 mia5 Ban7-lian5-kui2, saN-kap liu5-thoan5 kong2 chit chap8 e5 kui2 bat nau7 koe3 heng5-khu7.  
Pu3-kui2 gu7-tioh8 kiong5-kui2 mng7 kong2, se3-kan kim-gin5 moa2 te7, li2 choaN2-iuN7 ti3-kau3 chiah-ni7 pin5-kiong5--leh?  Kiong5-kui2 thou2-khui3 kong2, pah-poaN ke3-chhek phah-sng3, ia7 bo5 hong-hoat thang hoat-chai5, chhiaN2 lau2-ko su3 chit8 e5 ho2 hoat-sut8, thang kiu3 goa2 hoe2 sio kau3 bak8-bai5 e5 kin2-kip.  Pu3-kui2 koh kong2, Ke-ke chhu3-chhu3 long2 u7 hu7-jin5 chu2-li2, na7 chhng jip8-khi3 i lai7-bin7, chiong i-e5 gin2-a2 chiaN3-chhiu2 neh8 to3-chhiu2 teN7, khiu2 an5 m7-sai2 pang3-leng7.  I chiu7 bak8-chiu tiau3 chiuN7 koan5, ka7 chhui3-khi2 kin, lau5 peh8-phoeh8-noa7. A2-si7 chioh lang5 e5 chhui3 ka7 i kong2, si7 ke-lai7 koe3-ong2 e5 lau7-lang5, chai7 im-kan khiam3-khoeh gin5-chiN5 khai-eng7.  thiaN che oe7, i tek-khak sin3 si7 kui2 beh tho2 chiN5-gin5 sai2-eng7, chiu7 e7 koaN2-kin2 sio gin5-choa2 ti7 mng5-khau2, a2-si7 sip8-ji7-lou7, saN-chhe lou7-khau2 a2-si7 bio7-cheng5 bong7-au7, sio-hoa3 keng-i chhau2-e5 kah-be2.  Li2 chiu7 thang chiong hiah-e5 toa3 khi3 sai2-eng7.  Tak8 jit8 chiau3 an2-neN cho3.  Na2-li2 u7 koh pin5-kiong5 e5 cheng5-li2--ah, che chiu7-si7 hoat-chai5 e5 ho2 hoat-tou7.  Kiong5-kui2 tah chhiu2 chhio3 kong2, Leng2-kau3 leng2-kau3, kiong-keng3 ip-jia7 chiu7 khi3, koaN2-kin2 chau2-kau3 chit8-keng chhu3 e5 mng5-khau2.  Hut-jian5-kan thiaN-kiN3 lai7-bin7 e5 gin2-a2 teh hau2, am3-cheN7 kiah8-bak8 thau-khoan3 
mng5-lai7, khoaN3 mng5-sin5 tho2-ti7 long2 bo5 ti7-teh, hong-bong2 chin3-cheng5 jip8 lai7-bin7, si3-ke3 chit8-e7 khoaN3, tu2-ho2 cheng3 sin5-beng5 ia7 bo5 chai7 ui7, chiu7 hoaN-hi2 kong2, kin-a2-jit8 thang tek-i3 hoat-chai5--liau2.  Sui5-si5 chiong gin2-a2 chiaN3-chhiu2 neh8 to3-chhiu2 teN7, khiu2-lak8 nng7 pian3, chek-si5 bak8-chiu peng5-peh8, siang-chhiu2 ban2-me, chhui3-khi2 ka7-an5, lian7 hau2 to be7 hau2.  Chit-e5 gin2-a2 e5 lau7-pe7 mia5-kio3 Biau2-si7-kui2, in-ui7 i peng5-sou3 kai3 m7-sin3 sia5-kui2. Chit8-si5 chhut-mng5 liam5-piN to3-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 gin2-a2 chit-ho7 khoan2, chiu7 chiong kui2 toa7 me7 chit8 tiuN5, koh khi3-pun5-pun5 kau3 mng5-sin5 tho2-ti7 e5 ui7-cheng5, toa7-siaN chek-pi7 kong2, “Goa2 hok8-sai7 li2 chiah che7 ni5, goan5-lai5 si7 bang7 li2 chou2-tong3 sia5-mou5 kui2-koai3 bo5 jip8-lai5 chok-long7.  KhoaN3 kau3 kin-a2-jit8, li2 iu5-goan5 be7-e7 chou2-tong3 i, hok8-sai7 li2 bo5 ek,”soa3 eng7 chhiu2 chiong thou5-cho7 chha5-tiau e5 ngou2-siong7 siak ti7 chioh-thau5--nih8 kha3 cho3 kui2-na7 te3.  Oat8-tng2 sin khi3 kau3 toa7 thiaN cheng3 sin5-beng5 e5 bin7-cheng5 me7 kong2,“Goa2 chhe-it chap8-gou7 sio hiuN hok8-sai7 li2, si7 bang7 li2 po2-pi3 goan2 chit8-ke toa7-se3 peng5-an.  Kin-a2-jit8 khoaN3--lai5, lin2 ia7 si7 put-chong3-iong7--lah,”chiu7 chhun chhiu2 chiong choa2 kou5 e5 sin5-siong7 thoa-loh8--lai5, eng7 hoe2 sio-lio-khi3.  Hit-tiap8 mng5-sin5, thou2-ti7-kong, chai5-sin5, be2-sin5, gu5-ong5, chui2-chhau2 tai7-ong5, long2 chhut-goa7 iu5-oan2, bo5 loa-ku2 chiong3 ke-sin5 tang5-che5 to3--lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 sin5-ui7 long2 khang-khang, chiaN3-chiaN3 tiu5-tu5 teh siuN7, m7-chai sim2-mih8 in-toaN.  Hut-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 pang5-keng teh jiang2-nau2, chiu7 khi3 chha5-kho2, goan5-lai5 khiok si7 in-ui7 chit8-e5 kiong5-kui2 chai7 ka chok-chong7, chiaN3 beng5-pek8, cheng3 sin5-beng5 chit-si5 ia7 siu7-khi3, tui3 kiong5-kui2 hoah kong2 chit8-si3 bo5 ti7-teh.  Li2 thai3 ho2-taN2, jia2-chhut che toa7 ho7-toan, ti3-kau3 hai7 goan2, bo5 tang3 i-oa2 e5 te7-ui7?  Liam5-piN kio3 te7-hng-kui2 eng7 thih-lian7 chiong kiong5-kui2 pak8--khi3, ke3-sang3 kau3 Seng7-hong5-ia5 bin7-thau5-cheng5, phah sou2-pah-e7 pan2-poe, koh gia5 chit8 bin7 ke5 so2 ti7 bio7-khau2 si7-chiong3.  
U7 chit8 jit8 pu3-kui2 kiaN5 kau3 seng7-hong5-ia5 bio7 khau2, khoaN3-kiN3 kiong5-kui2 gia5 thau5-ho7-ke5 ti7-hia, mng7 kong2, Ai3-te7 hoan7 sim2-mih8 choe7, gia5-ke5 koaN7-so2?  Kiong5-kui2 lau5 bak8-sai2 kong2, chiong-cheng5 Jin5-hiaN ka3 goa2 hoat-chai5 e5 ho2 
hong-hoat, gu5-te7 chiau3 lau2-ko e5 oe7 khi3 kiaN5.  SiaN3 chai biau2-si7 kui2 hit-ho7-lang5, kian-sim m7 sin3, m7 na7 bo5 beh hian3 choa2-be2 gin5-chiN5, hoan2-tng2 chiong mng5-sin5 tho2-ti7 kap cheng3 sin5-beng5 e5 sin5-siong7 chit8 che5 sio-hoe2 siak phoa3, ti3-kau3 chhiok-hoan7 cheng3 Sin5-beng5 siu7-khi3, kio3 te7-hong-kui2 lai5 khun2-pak8 goa2, ke3-sang3 kau3 Seng5-hong5-iab5?n-cheng5, pan2-poe phah liau2, iu7 gia5 ke5 tiam3 chia. ThiaN lau2-ko e5 oe7, pun2 ui7-tioh8 beh kiu5 hoat-chai5, tian-to3 koh khah chham2.  Pu3-kui2 thiaN-liau2, ki2 kiong5-kui2 me7 kong2, bok8-koai3 li2 tioh8 pin5-kiong5, gia5-ke5 ia7 bo5 khak-khui, li2 lian7 chhong-beng5 kian3-sek to bo5, hoan2-tng2 oan3-hin7 goa2. Ki3-jian5 beh jip8 jin5-ke chok-chong7, tai7-seng tioh8 khoaN3 hit ke u7 sin3 kui2 a2 bo5; na7 m7 sin3 chiu7-tioh8 hng7-hng7 siam2-pi7, bian2-tit thoa-lui7 siu7-khou2.     
Kiong5-kui2 mng7 kong2 beh an3-choaN2 e7 chai lang5 sin3 a2 m7-sin3?  Pu3-kui2 in3 kong2, se3-chiuN7 seng-li2 be2-be7 tek-khak u7 koa3 ji7-ho7 chio-pai5, sin3 kui2 e5 lang5 ia7 tek-khak u7 chio-pai5, hek8-si7 mng5 teng2 tah chiong-kui5, mng5-sin5; hek8-si7 teng3 ngou2-lui5-oaN2; hek8-si7 kou5 kim-hu5, ng5-pio2-choa2, kap chu-se-hu5; hek8-si7 thau5-khak kat chu-se-ji7, chhiu2-kut liam5 chu-se-chiu3; hek8-si7 sia2 Khiong-thai3-kong ti7 chhiuN5 teng2; hek8-si7 pang5-mng5 teng2 sia2 kiat-seng ko chiau3; hek8-si7 tui3-mng5 khek chioh8-kam2-tong; hek8-si7 koa3 thai5 lang5 e5 to; hek8-si7 chhiu2--nihn8 koa3 phu5-to5-kin e5 khoan5-a2.  Che long2 si7 kiaN-kui2 sin3-kui2 e5 chio-pai5.  Na7 jip8 chit ho7 jin5-ka chok-chong7, tiaN7-tioh8 tek-i3 hoat-chai5.  
Na7-si7 jip8 biau2-si7 kui2-e5 e5 ke, pit-tiaN7 khang hui3-liau2 sim-ki, u7-si5 iau2 tioh8 khek-khui siu7-khou2.  Chiong5-kim i2-au7, tioh8 khah liu5-sin5--teh, kiN3-tioh8 sin3 Ki-tok e5 lang5 chiu7 tioh8 hng7-hng7 siam2-pi7 khah ho2. Kiong5-kui2 iu7-koh mng7 kong2, Sin3 Ki-tok-e5, i u7 sim2-mih8 chio-pai5?  Pu3-kui2 koh ka7 i kong2, Thong se3-kai3 tak8-ui7 to u7, hit poe3 lang5 sin u7 sin3-tek tong3 cho3 tin5-pai5, ia7 kiah8 po2-kiam3, chiu7-si7 chin-sin5 e5 Seng3-chheh.  Chit teng2 e5 lang5 chiong5-lai5 to m7-kheng2 liau2 poaN3-iN5 hou7 kui2.  Kiong5-kui2 hoaN-hi2 kong2, Lau2-ko ko2-jian5 ti3-sek ko-beng5, phian3-chai5 e5 hoat-sut8, hang2 lang5 e5 biau7-ke3, goa2 kau3 chit-si5 chiaN3 beng5-pek8. Pu3-kui2 saN-si5 chiu7 khi3.  
An2-ni kho2-kian3 m7-sin3 kui2, kui2 ia7 m7-kaN2 lai5.  Kou2-cha2 lang5 kong2, Mng5-lai7 u7 kun-chu2, mng5-goa7 kun-chu2 lai5; mng5-lai7 u7 siau2-jin5, mng5-goa7 siau2-jin5 lai5.  Seng3-keng kong2, Li2 na7 ku7-choat8 kui2, kui2 chiu7 the3-pi7 li2; li2 na7 chhin-kin7 chin-sin5, chin-sin5 chiu7 chhin-kin7 li2.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, Sio choa2 chiN5 na2 che7, in2-kui2 na2 to.  Hoan7 lan2 se3-kan lang5, chhiat-chhiat boh8-tit sin3 sio kim-gin5-choa2, chhau2-e5, kah-be2, i2-kip8 hit ho7 chiong-kui5 mng5-sin5 chu-se-ji7 e5 hu5-chiu3, Khiong-thai3-kong thai3-san chioh8-ji7, kiat-seng ko-chiau3 e5 ji7, teng3 ngou2-lui5-oaN2 hiah e5 hong-hoat.  Che si7 jia2 mou5-kui2 e5 chio-pai5.  Tong-si5 mou5-kui2 nia2 Ia5-sou kau3 koan5-soaN, ki2 ban7-kok e5 eng5-hoa5 hu3-kui3 kong2, Li2 na7 pai3 goa2, goa2 chiong it-khai3 siuN2-su3 Li2.  Ia5-sou in3 kong2, Eng-kai pai3 Chu2 li2 e5 Siong7-te3.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 mou5-kui2 chiu7 li7-khui Ia5-sou.   
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 183~184 koan3,1900 ni5 6~7 geh8)
