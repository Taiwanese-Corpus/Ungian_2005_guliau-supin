Tah8-oe7-thau5 (preface)
“Aukele sin5-oe7 kou3-su7” e7-sai2 kong2 si7 Hawaii siong7 kou2-lo2--e5 chit8-e5 bin5-kan kou3-su7. I goan5-pun2 si7 hoat-seng ti7 Tahiti kun5-to2 e5 sin5-oe7 kou3-su7, hou7 cha2-ki5 e5 i5-bin5 chah koe3-lai5 Hawaii--e5.  Chit-e5 kou3-su7 chu2-iau3 si7 teh kong2 chit8-e5 siau2-lian5 e5 Hawaii goan5-chu7-bin5, an2-choaN2 che7-chun5, khi3 chau2-chhoe7 sin-e5 to2-su7, sui-jian5 koe3-theng5 chhian-sin-ban7-khou2, siong7 boe2-a2 ma7 si7 khek-hok8 khun3-lan5, phah-pai7 sou2-u7 e5 tek8-jin5, iu7-koh chhoa7 tioh8 chit8-e5 chiaN5 sui2 e5 ang-i5 cho3 khan-chhiu2.
Chit-khoan2 kou3-su7 tak8 si3-ke3 long2 u7, m7-koh Aukele sin5-oe7 lai7-te2 u7 chit8-koa2 thoan5-soat si7 kan-taN Polynesian lang5 chiah u7--e5, chhin-chhiuN7 kong2: tng Aukele cho-siu7 gui5-hiam2 e5 si5, tiaN7-tiaN7 teh ka7 I keng2-ko3 e5 tin3-ka sin5-beng5 Lono, iong7 chiau2-mou cho3--e5, e7-tang3 po2-hou7 Aukele koh ka7 tek8-jin5 piN3-chiaN5 hoe2-hu e5 ui5-kin; pang-chou7 Aukele kai2-kiu3 sit-chong khi3 e5 hiaN-ko kap tit8-a2 e5 “SeN3-mia7-chi-chui2”, the5-kiong hou7-sin-hu5 hou7 Aukele e5 “Ui2-tai7 e5 Mo-o A-chou2” teng2-teng2, long2-si7 chiaN5 tek8-su5 koh chhu3-bi7.
Lai5!  TaN Lan2 kap Aukele cho3-hoe2 lai5 khi3 mou7-hiam2.  Siong7 ho2-taN2 e5 Aukele! Siong7 iong2-kam2 e5 Aukele!

I sim-lai7 chiok hi-bang7 e7-tang3 kap in cho3-hoe2 sng2, sou2-i2 hou7 lang5 ki7-choat8 ma7 bo5 oan3-than3. I chit-ma2 i2-keng si7 chit8-e5 siau3-lian5-ke--a, seN-cho3 put-chi2-ak2oan koh ian-tau5. M7-koh in hia e5 hiaN-ko iu5-goan5 ka7 i tong3-cho3 si7 chit8-e5 ai3-khau3 ai3 toe3-lou7 e5 se3-han3 sio2-ti7 laikh5oaN-thai7.  I-e5 hiaN-ko khah e7 chai-iaN2 ti7 in bo5 ti7-leh ech5…t-tang lai7. Ui2-tai7 e5 Mo-o A-chou2 (Great Mo-o Woman)
Tng-tang Aukele cheng-sin5 liau2-au7, i liah8-chun2 ka-ki7i-keng chheN-me5--a. Chit8-pou7 chit8-pou7 tah8-jip8 si2-bong5 e5 kai3-soaN3
(Death Comes Stalking)
Aukele chhiu2 theh8 hou5-lou5, kin2 chhiong khi3 peN5-tai5 e5 piN--a.
“Thiau3!” Aukele tui3 Iku-lani kap “Chi3-seng3” toa7-siaN hoah.
“Tong3--leh, be7-sai2 chit!” Kama-kahi beng7-leng7 kong2.
Aukele i2-keng thiau3 loh8-khi3 hai2--nih8, khai-si2 siu5 li5-khui phang5-chun5, i chai-iaN2 i siong7 se3-han3 e5 hiaN-ko choat8-tui3 m7-kaN2 ui5-poe7 Kama-kahi e5 beng7-leng7.
Tng i khoaN3-tioh8 hai2-kiN5 u7 chit8-e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 teh io5 chit8-ki iong7 chiau2-mou cho3 e5 ki5-a2 si5, tut8-jian5-kan chit8-chun7 kim-kng, hou7 i-e5 bak8-chiu thi3 long2 be7-khui.  Soa3 loh8-lai5 Aukele hng7-hng7 khoaN3-kiN3 Kama-kahi hit-chiah phang5-chun5 teh toh8-hoe2, m7-taN7 teh chhut-ian, hoe2 koh toh8 kah chiok iam7.
Ti7 i iau2 be7 siu5 kau3 hai2-kiN5 e5 si5, hit-chiah phang5-chun5,i-e5 chap8-e5 hiaN-ko kah i-e5 tit8-a2 --- choan5-pou7 long2 i2-keng siau-sit ti7 hai2--nih8 loh.
 
“Goa2 kho2-lian5 e5 hiaN-ti7, lin2 keng3-jian5 chong3-sin hai2-te2, lian5 hou7 lang5 siu-si e5 ki-hoe7 to7 bo5!  Aukele ian5-lou7 khau3 ian5-lou7 liam7. In-ui7 lang5 chiok thiam2, soah lai5 to2 tiam3 lou7-piN khun3 khi3, m7-koh chhiu2 iu5 si7 ka7 hou5-lou5 giap8 tiau5-tiau5. Koe3 bo5 goa7-ku2, i khi3 hou7 kau2-pui7-siaN kap chi-chi-chak8-chak8 e5 kong2-oe7 siaN chha2 cheng-sin5.
“Aukele, cheng-sin5--ouh! Si2-bong5 i2-keng chiam7-chiam7 pek-kin7--loh!”
Aukele lang5 che7 khit-lai5. bak8-chiu si3-ke3 khoaN3, m7-ku2 chham chit8-chiah hou5-sin5 bang2-a2 too7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3.  I hiong5-hiong5 siuN7 tioh8 che eng3-kai si7 an3 hou5-lou5 lai7 chhut-lai5 e5 Lono e5 siaN. 
“Lono chou2! li2 kam2 e7-sai2 ka7 goa2 kong2 beh an2-choaN2 chiah ho2?” Aukele kong2.
Lono in3-kong2: “Lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka i2-keng phai3 nng7-e5 Kupua lai5 beh thai5 li2, chit nng7-e5 kuapua si7 sin7-thang5-a2 kap niau2-chhi2-a2 che2, li2 e7 sai2 tit8-chiap kio3 in-e5 mia5, in na7 tioh8-kiaN to7 e7 khia7 toa3 li2 chit-peng5, ka7 li2 tau3-saN-kang7.”
Bo5 goa7-ku2 chit-nng7-e5 Kupua chhut-hian7 ti7 soa-pou teng2-thau5, chit8-e5 thau5 seng5 sin7-thang5-a2, chit-e5 seng5 niau2-chhi2-a2, Aukele lang5 khia7 khi2-sin lai5 kiaN5 hiong3 in nng7-ui7:
“lin2 nng7-e5 kou-niu5-a2 chhiaN2!  sin7-thang5-a2 chi2!  niau2-chhi2-ach2i!”
Nng7-e5 kou-niu5-a2 chit8-si5 bin7-a2 chheN-sun2-sun2, na2-chhiuN7 chiok kiaN-hiaN5 e5 khoan2: chit-e5 phiau-phiat e5 siau3-lian5-ke na2 e7 chai-iaN2 goan2-e5 mia5?  Chai-iaN2 Kupua e5 leng5-lek8!  I eng3-kai ma7-si7 Kupua chiah tioh8!  siang2 e7 chai-iaN2 i u7 goa7 li7-hai7--leh?  Leng5-kho2 kap i cho3 peng5-iu2, m7-thang kap i cho3-tui3 chiah ho2.
 
“Hoan-geng5! Hoan-geng5! To2-ui7 lai5 e5 chheN-hun7 lang5-kheh,” sin5-thang5-a2 chi2 kong2-oe7: “si7 sim2-mih8 hong ka7 li2 sian3 lai5 ‘Halani jit8-loh8-chi-to2’--neh?”
“Goa2 si7 ui7-tioh8 tui-kiu5 ho5-peng5 lai5--e5.”Aukele hoe5-tap: “Ti7 li7 chia chiok hng7 e5 “Thian-tong5-chi-te7” to7 bat thiaN lang5 kong2 lin2 chia e5 to2-su7 chiaN5 sui2.”
“Li2-e5 chun5--leh! Chit-ku2 ti7 to2-ui7?” niau2-chhi2 che2 toe3-leh mng7.
Chit-nng7-e5 Kupua hou7-siong7 teh sai2-bak8-boe2. “I2-keng hou7 lan2-e5 lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka hui2-biat--a!” sin5-thang5-a2 che2 kong2: “Chit-ma2 phai3 goan2 beh lai5 thai5 li2!”
“Lin2 kam2 chin-chiaN3 e7 an2-ne cho3?” Aukele mng7-kong2.
Sin5-thang5-a2 u7 chit8-sut-a2 tiu5-tu5: “lan2 na2 u7 kho2-leng5 thai5-sat lan2 chhoe7 be7-tioh8 e5 lang5--leh? lan2 ui3 soa-pou kau3 soaN-teng2 si3-ke3 chhoe7 thau3-thau3, chham chit8-chiah hou5-sin5 bang2-a2 long2 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, tioh8--houN7?”
Niau2-chhi2-a2 che2 tim3-thau5 piau2-si7 tong5-i3: Moela e5 hiN7-khang hiah-nih8-a2 lai7 to bo5 thiaN-kiN3 u7 chheN-hun7-lang5 lai5-koe3, lan2 tong-jian5 sim2-mih8 ma7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3.”
“Mahalo! lou2-lat8!” Aukele ka7 in soeh to-sia7, sim-lai7 tam7-poh8-a2 hoai5-gi5.  Lou7-boe2 chit-nng7-e5 Kupua to7 li5-khui i.
Bo5 goa7-ku2 Lono a-chou2!  ChhiaN2 ka7 goa2 kong2 beh an2-choaN2 eng3-hu3 chiah ho2.” Aukele ai-kiu5 kong2.
Lono ke3-siok kong2: “Lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka phai3 i si3-e5 chiau2-a2 Kupua lai5 beh thai5 li2, li2 e7-sai2 tit8-chiap kio3 in-e5 mia5, kang7-khoan2 in to7 e7 khia7 toa3 li2 chit-peng5, the3 li2 kong2-oe7.”
Aukele seh8-sin khoaN3-tioh8 an3 soaN-lun lai5 e5 si3-e5 siau3-lian5 e5 cha-pou-lang5 --- seN-cho3 chiaN5 koan5, put-chi2-a2 iong2-kiaN7 koh ian-tau5. Aukele kiaN5 hiong3-cheng5 kap in phah chio-hou, bak8-chiu liah8 hiong3 ha5 ti7 in-e5 khou3-thau5 u7 chhai2-sek e5 malo kim-kim-khoaN3, chiah chiap-leh kap in kong2-oe7.
“Lin2 tak8-e5 ho2! Peh8-chiau2-hiaN, phu2-chiau2-hiaN, thou5-chiau2-hiaN, chheN-chiau2-hiaN.”
Ki5-tiong e5 saN-e5 siau3-lian5-ke khai-si2 khi3-phut-phut kiaN5 hiong3 i chit-peng5 koe3--lai5. leng7-goa7 hit-e5 koaN2-kin2 kain7 tong3 tiau5--leh.
“Li2 ho2, chheN-hun7 lang5-kheh!” hit-e5 siau3-lian5-ke kong2: “Li2 kam2 si7 lai5 “Sang3-bah-chhi7-hou2”, houN? li2 kam2 chai-iaN2 chia si7 goan2 toa7-chi2-thau5-a2 e5 te7-poaN5?” i to7-si7 “Teng2-kang2 u7 mia5-siaN, e7-kang2 u7 chhut-mia5” e5 lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka!”
“Goa2 si7 thiau-kang lai5 chiam3 i-e5 te7-poaN5--e5.” Aukele in3-kong2.
Peh8-chiau2-hiaN thiaN liau2 chiaN5 hoaN-hi2, iong7 chiok se3 e5 siaN-im kong2: hiaN-ko, hoan7-se3 i to7-si7 Namaka e5 tui3-chhiu2! SiuN7 khoaN3 mai7--leh, i bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 e7-tang3 the3 lan2 chhut chit8-khau2-khui3--ou7!
Chit saN-e5 hiaN-ti7 chiaN5 sin7-tiong7 teh kho2-lu7 chit-chan5 tai7-chi3: “I2-au7 lan2 to7 m7-bian2 koh i3 hit-lo7 “Konane” hiat chioh8-thau5 e5 pa2-hi3--loh.” Phu2-chiau2-hiaN kong2.
“I2-au7 lan2 to7 m7-bian2 koh chhoan5 chiah8-liau7 hou7 hit-chiah m7-chiaN5 kau2-a2 chiah8--a2.” Thou5-chiau2-hiaN chiok hoaN-hi2 teh kong2.
 
“I2-au7 m7-bian2 koh thiaN chit8-e5 chap8-liam7 e5 cha-a2-lang5 e5 beng7-leng7--a.” chheN-chiau2-hiaN chiaN5 kek-tong7 toe3-leh kong2.
“Kam2 ai3 chhoa7 chit-e5 chheN-hun7-lang5 kap lan2 cho3-hoe2 tng2-khi3?” peh8-chiau2-hiaN bin7 tai3 chhio3-iong5 teh mng7.
“E3! Ho2!” ki5-tha saN-e5 ma7 tang5-che5 hoah--chhut-lai5.
Peh8-chiau2-hiaN seh8-sin tng2-hiong3 Aukele. ”Namaka e7 pang3 kau2-a2 chhut-lai5 ka7 li2 ka7.  Li2 kam2 u7 hoat-tou7 po2-hou7 li2 ka-ki7?”
“Bo5 bun7-te5.”
“An2-ne chiaN5 ho2, koh kong2 hou7 khah chheng-chho2--leh. Chhian-ban7 m7-thang jip8-khi3 i-e5 chhu3-lai7.  M7-thang chiah8 i chhiaN2 li2 e5 mih8-kiaN7, m7-thang khoaN3 bo5-khi2 goan2 toa7-chi2-thau5-a2, i si7 chit8-e5 hui-siong5 u7 ui-lat8 e5 ang-i5.”
“Tan2--chit8-e7!” Aukele phah-khui i-e5 hou5-lou5, theh8 chhut chiau2-mou cho3 e5 ui5-kin, moa-tiam3 sin-khu, chiah-kohka7 hou5-lou5 koa3 kham3--khit-lai5: “Goa2 chun2-pi7 ho2-a2.”
Chit-tin7 lang5 kiaN5 koe3 soa-pou, thau5-cheng5 chhah chit8-ki chiau2-mou cho3 e5 ki5-a2, peh8-chiau2-hiaN toa7-siaN hoah kio3: “Toa7-chi2-thau5-a2, goan2 i2-keng chhoe7 tioh8 hit-e5 hou7 Moela pui7 e5 lang5 tng2--lai5-loh.”
“Kio3 i jip8-lai5.” chhu3-lai7 thoan5-chhut e5 siaN-im.
“Li2 ka7 goa2 chhut-lai5!” Aukele m7-taN7 ku7-choat8, koh an2-ne hoah kong2.
“Li2 teh beng7-leng7 goa2?” sui5-au7 chit8-e5 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 bin7 chhut-hian7 ti7 mng5-kha khau2, chit-e5 cha-bou2-lang5 chiong5-lai5 m7-bat khoaN3-koe3 chhin-chhiuN7 Aukele chiah-nih8 ian-tau5 e5 siau3-lian5-ke, lang5 koh koan5 toa7, thau5-mng5 tng5-tng5 ou-kim ou-kim, chit-e5 cha-bou2-lang5 bak8-chiu hiong5-kai3-kai3 teh khoaN3 Aukele.
“Li2 si7 siang2? Chiah toa7-taN2 kaN2 beng7-leng7 goa2 chhut-lai5?” i mng7-kong2.
Aukele kiaN5 hiong3 thau5-cheng5, khui-chhui3 kong2:“Goa2 si7 Aukele. 
An3 “Thian-tong5-chi-te7” lai5--e5.  
Aukele, Iku kap Kapapa siong7 thiaN3-thang3 e5 hau7-seN;”  
Aukele, “Ou-am3-chi-sin5” Mo-o e5 kim-sun.  
Aukele, tham3-hiam2-chia2.  
Aukele, m7 kiaN-si2 e5 lang5.”
Namaka an3 thou5-kha ka7 chiau2-mou cho3 e5 ki5-a2 poeh8--khit-lai5, piaN3-mia7 teh iaN7, ma2-siong7 to7 u7 chit8-phiN3 hoe2-hai2 ka7 Aukele ui5 tiau5--leh.
Tng hoe2 hoa khi3 e5 si5, Aukele iu5-goan5 khia7 kah ho2-se3 ho2-se3. Chit8-tiam2-a2 to bo5 sun2-hai7--tioh8.  Mo-o e5 hong5-hoe2 ui5-kin chin-chiaN3 kiu3-tioh8 i.
Namaka khoaN3-tioh8 chiok siu7-khi3: “Moela!” i toa7-siaN hoah kio3, chit8-chiah chiok toa7-chhai7 e5 kau2-a2 ma2-siong7 chhiong lai5 i-e5 sin-khu piN.
“Ka7 hou7 si2!” i beng7-leng7.
 
Chit-chiah toa7-chiah kau2 “phok8!” chit8-e7 pa3-tui3 Aukele khi3. Aukele ka7 i-e5 ui5-kin thng3 loh8-lai5 haiN3 nng7-saN-e7, thiau3 kah chit8-poaN3 e5 chit-chiah toa7-chiah kau2, tut8-jian5 piN3-seng5 ti7 thou5-kha e5 chit8-tui hoe2-hu. Namaka liah8 hoe2-hu kim-kim-khoaN3, bo5 saN2 siong-sin3 i sou2 khoaN3--tioh8 e5 khoan2 i kui-e5 lang5 soah gong7-sin5 gong7-sin5, chit8-ku3 oe7 to kong2 be7 chhut-chhui3, sim-lai7 chiok oan3-hun7 Aukele, ma7 chiaN5 m7-kam sit-khi3 Moela.
“I kam2-si7 chiaN5 phaiN2 tui3-hu3 e5 lang5, toa7-chi2-thau5-a2?” peh8-chiau2-hiaN teh mng7: “Chit-e5 lang5 e5 iong2-khi3 chham ui-lek8, eng3-kai e7-tang3 kap li2 pi2 chit8-e7-a2 koan5-ke7, kam2 m7-si7?”
“Bo5 m7-tioh8!” Namaka in3 kong2, m7-koh i-e5 gan2-sin5 iu5-goan5 hiong5-kai3-kai3: “Lan2 tak8-e5 lai5 ka7 hoan-geng5 chit8--e7!” I siang-chhiu2 phah saN-e7 phok-a2, e7-kha--chhiu2-lang5 ma2-siong7 khoan2-chhut kui2-loh8 poaN5 e5 liau7-li2 chhut-lai5 --- Aukele khoaN3 kah kiong5-beh lau5 chhui3-noa7, in-ui7 ti7 hai2-siong7 hiah-nih8 ku2 e5 si5-kan kin-pun2 to7 m7-bat khoaN3-kiN3 chiah chheN-chhau phong-phai3 e5 chhai3-sek.
Hang-e5 soaN-ti bah, pu5-e5 hai2-hi5 kap han-chi5, kap iu3-mi7-mi7 e5 soaN-ou7-a2. I-e5 chhui3-noa7 i2-keng hou7 chia-e5 san-tin-hai2-bi7 siaN5 kah chhap-chhap tih--a.
“To-sia7, chok8-tiuN2!” ChiaN5 phaiN2-se3, goa2 u7 chiu3-choa7 koe3, kan-taN e7-sai2 chiah8 goa2 chah lai5 e5 mih8-kiaN7 nia7-nia7.” Ika7 hou5-lou5-koa3 phah-khui, theh8-chhut hit-mi7 chai3-seng e5 chhiu7-hioh8-a2, tu kau3 i-e5 chhui3-kiN5.
Namaka na2-chhiuN7 teng3-su e5 ke-kak kang7-khoan2, thau5-khak le5-le5 tiam7-chih-chih, chit8-ku3 oe7 to bo5 kong2, tian-to2 si7 chit-si3-chiah chiau2-hiaN-ti7 khia7 ti7 piN-a2 chiok hoaN-hi2 teh him-siong2 chit-chhut ho2-hi3.
 
Mou5-sin5-a2 Halulu (Halulu-the-evil-one)
Jit8-thau5 chhiah-iaN7-iaN7 koa3 ti7 thiN-teng2, Aukele ti7 chiau2-hiaN-ti7 e5 chhu3--nih8 khun3 kah chiok loh8-bin5--e5.  Tiam3 hoe7-gi7-sek lai7, Namaka tng teh kap i e5 hiaN-ti7 khui-hoe7 tho2-lun7. “Aukele si7 siang2? i tau3-te2 u7 saN2 sin5-ki5 e5 ui-lat8?”  Namaka teh mng7: “siuN7 be7-kau3 i koh e7-tang3 phiah koe3 goa2-e5 chiau2-mou-ki5-a2, ka7 Moela pian3-seng5 hoe2-hu, koh chai-iaN2 liau7-li2 u7 tok8!  I tui3 lan2 e5 ui-hiap8 sit8-chai7 chiok toa7--e5.  Lan2 it-teng7 ai3 ka7 i thai5 tiau7!” 
“Be7-sai2 tit,” peh8-chiau2-hiaN kong2: “Lan2 be7-sai2 an2-necho3.”
Namaka e5 bak8-chiu na2 hoe2 teh toh8, sih--leh sih--leh: “Ui7-saN2-mih8?”
“Na7-si7 i oah8--leh, tui3 lan2 kho2-leng5 khah iu2-li7,” ki5-tiong chit8-e5 hiaN-ti7 hoe5-tap: “siuN7 khoaN3-mai7--leh, toa7-chi2-thau5-a2!  Ti7 li2 thong2-ti7 “Halani jit8-loh8-chi-to2” e5 chit kui2 tang, kam2 bat chhut-hian7 chiah-nih8 li7-hai7 e5 lang5?  Ji5-chhiaN2 koh chiah-nih8 ian-tau5?  Na7-si7 ka7 i thai5-sat, tui3 li2 tau3-te2 u7 sim2-mih8 ho2-chhu3--leh?  Ui7-sim2-mih8 bo5-ai3 i piN3-chiaN5 li2 e5 lang5--leh? kap Aukele kiat-hun! Ka2-su2 u7 i ti7 sin-khu piN, li2 to7 e7 chiaN5-cho3 thian-ha7 bu5-tek8--a”
Khi3-hun ban7-ban7-a2 teh pian3-hoa3, goan5-pun2 ti7 Namaka bin7--nih8 e5 hun3-nou7, chiam7-chiam7 pian3-cho3 kan-sin5-a2 chhio3--lou. “Li2 kong2 liau2 chin tioh8,” i seng5-jin7 kong2:“m7-koh ti7 goa2 tong5-i3 beh ke3 hou7 chit-e5 chheN-hun7-lang5 chin3-cheng5, i pit-su ai3 seng kap mou5-sin5-a2 Halulu kiN3 chit8-bin7.”
Phu2-chiau2-hiaN khong3-gi7 kong2: “Li2 kam2 chin-chiaN3 kam ka7 i sang3-khi3 kap hit-e5 choan-bun5 teh chiah8 lang5-bah e5 Halulu kiN3-bin7? Kau3-taN na2-chhiuN7 iau2 bo5 lang5 u7 hoat-tou7 an3 i-hia thoat-sin!”
Namaka tim3-thau5 kong2: “HeN3!  Goa2 chai! na7 bo5, kam2 u7 sim2-mih8 khah ho2 e5 kho2-giam7 hong-hoat--leh?  Na7-si7 Aukele e7-tang3 an-choan5 thoat-sin, goa2 to7 ke3 hou7 i.”
Sui-jian5 si3-e5 hiaN-ti7 long2 bo5 chan3-seng5 chit-e5 pan7-hoat, m7-koh Namaka iu5-goan5 chiaN5 kian-chhi5 an2-ne cho3.
Tan2 Aukele khun3-pa2 cheng-sin5, ui3 chai3-seng-hioh8 e5 chhiu7-hioh8 khip-siu eng5-iong2, to7 khi3 chhoe7 lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka.
Lu2-chok8-tiuN2 Namaka khoaN3-tioh8 Aukele, bin7-a2 chhio3--leh chhio3--leh: “Aukele, leng5-lek8 kang7-khoan2 e5 lang5 bo5 it-teng7 ai3 cho3 tek8-jin5, goa2 siuN7 beh khi3 hai2-kiN5 hioh-khun3 chit8-kang, li2 kam2 u7 chhu3-bi7 beh cho3-hoe2 lai5-khi3?”
Aukele chiap-siu7 i-e5 iau-chhiaN2, chah tioh8 I e5 hou5-lou5 toe3 Namaka khi3, thiN-khi3 chiaN5 ho2, thiN-teng2 chit8-sut-a2 hun5 to bo5, Namaka keng2 chit8-ui7 khah oa2 hai2-kiN5 e5 toa7-chioh-thau5 che7 loh8-lai5, put-si5 u7 hai2-eng2 chhiang5 koe3-lai5.  Chit-e5 si5-chun7 Aukele poan3-sin3-poan3-gi5, kam2-kak tioh8 chiaN5 hoai5-gi5, tau3-te2 li2 Namaka si7 teh piN3 saN2-mih8 bang2--leh?  I chu3-i3 teh khoaN3 hai2-chui2.
“E7 kiaN--simh?”  Namaka teh ka7 Aukele thi2-chhio3.  Sui5-au7 I chhiong hiong3 hai2-kiN5, thiau3-jip8 hai2--nih. Aukele koaN2-kin2 ti7 soa-pou teng2 ou2 chit8-e5 khang beh ka7 hou5-lou5 tai5 khit-lai5.  Ti7 i beh chiong soa-a2 kham3-khi2-lai5 chin3-cheng5, iu7-koh thiaN tioh8 Lono e5 siaN-im. 
“Si2-bong5 i2-keng poe hiong3 chia lai5--loh, Aukele!  Mou5-sin5-a2 Halulu i2-keng beh chiah8 Li2--a!”
Tut8-jian5-kan jit8-thau5 khi3 hou7 chit8-tah ou-iaN2 kham3 tiau5-leh, poan3-khong tiong poe lai5 chit8-chiah toa7-chiau2 na2-chhiuN7 lah8-hioh8 teh liah8 ke-a2-kiaN2 kang7-khoan2, hiong3 Aukele chhiong koe3-lai5. Aukele hiam2-hiam2-a2 be7-hu3 theh8 i hit-kha hou5-lou5, to7 hou7 chit-chiah toa7-chiau2 kau hiong3 thiN-teng2 poe chau2 khi3.
Chit-chiah toa7-chiau2 poe khi3 chiaN5 ku2, poe lai5-kau3 chit8-e5 chiok chhim koh bo5 lang5 e5 soaN-kheN. Chiah chiong Aukele pang3 loh8-lai5, to7 ma2-siong7 poe chau2. Aukele si3-khou lian2-tng2 khoaN3-khoaN3--leh, hoat-kak chit-e5 sou2-chai7 saN-peng5 si7 chioh8-piah, chit8-peng5 si7 hiong-kong5 e5 hai2-eng2, siuN7 beh li7-khui chit-e5 sou2-chai7 si7 bo5 hiah-nih kan2-tan. Aukele chau2-koe3 chit8-phiN3 chiaN5 khoah e5 peN5-pou, ti7 beh keng-koe3 “Ia7-heng5-lu2” e5 chhu3 e5 si5, “Ka7 liah8 khit-lai5!” Ka-moho-alii teh hoah, m7-koh chit-e5 cha-bou2-lang5 na2-chhiuN7 bo5 thiaN tioh8 e5 khoan2.  I it-tit8 chong5 lai5, chong5 kausi3ong kia7 e5 lou7, jip8-khi3 pong7-khang lai7-bin7, siong7 boe2-al2ai-kau3 lama chhiu7-na5.  I sun7 tioh8 tok8 chhiu7-na5 tiong-ngeh8-eh8 e5 se3-tiau5 lou7 it-tit8 chau2,  Ka-moho-alii iu5-goan5 toe3 ti7 i e5 au7-piah, jiok kah chiok an5--e5.
“Ka7 liah8 khit-lai5!” Ka-moho-alii kang7-khoan2 kohhoah chit8-pai2. Te7-saN-e5 siu2-ui7 iong7 iI e5 kun3-a2 hiong3 Aukelemau koe3-khi3, m7-koh mau bo5 tioh8. Ka-moho-alii siu7-khikah3 liah8-kong5, iong7 pou2-thau5 phut chit8-chang5 chhiu7-a2 tu2-siau3.  In-ui7 an2-ne chit8-tui koe2-chi2 soah lian3-lian3 loh8-lai5,khian tioh8 Aukele e5 thau5-khak, keng-kah-thau5 kah kha-chiah-phiaN.  Chit8-chiong2 kong2 be7 chhut-lai5 e5 thiu-thiaN3, 
 
liam-mih kui sin-khu thiaN3 thau3-thau3. I kam2-kak na2-chhiuN7lu2-lai5 lu2-bo5-lat8. Chit-e5 si5-chun7 thiaN tioh8 Lono chiaN5 se3-siaNteh kong2: “Aukele, “SeN3-mia7-chi-chui2”!  Kin2 chip chit8-chhui3!”
Ian5-lou7 chau2, ian5-lou7 ka7 hou5-lou5 that-a2 giu2-khui, ka7 hou5-lou5 tu oa2 i e5 chhui3-tun5, hou7 chui2 lau5 jip8-khi3 i e5 na5-au5. I e5 thiu-thiaN3 ma2-siong7 long2 siau-sit khi3, lat8-thau5 koh hoe-hokt8!g lai5. I ka7 hou5-lou5 that-a2 that to2 tng2-khi3, koh ke3-siok kiaN5.  Beh kau3 tek-na5 chin3-cheng5, i it-tit8 chau2 ti7 to2-chhiu2 peng5,siam2-khui phian3-lang5 e5 be5-hun5-tin7, chiau3 i goan5-lai5 e5 lou7 kiaN5 tng2-khi3.
“Ka7 liah8 khit-lai5!” Ka-moho-alii kang7-khoan2 koh hoah chit8-pai2. Te7-ji7-e5 siu2-ui7 iong7 i e5 toa7-ki kek sia7 an3 Aukele,m7-koh sia7 bo5 tioh8. Chiok siu7-khi3 e5 Ka-moho-alii iong7 hit-ki kim-sih-sih e5 pou2-thau5 hiong3 Aukele phut loh8-khi3.  Aukele chit8-e7 siam2, hoat-kak lang5 soah khia7 ti7 tek-na5 be5-hun5-tin7 lai7-bin7.  To2-chhiu2. chiaN3-chhiu2 thau5-cheng5 au7-piah peng5!  M7-koan2 i an2-choaN2 chong5 long2 sis7i-lou7, Ka-moho-alii i2-keng lu2-lai5 lu2-chiap-kin7 i--a. Chit-es5i-chun7 i thiaN tioh8 Lono se3-kah-siaN teh kong2: “Aukele!  Li2-e5 sin5-ki5 to-a2!  Iong7 i lai5 khui-lou7!”
Ian5-lou7 kiaN5, ian5-lou7 poeh chhut hit-ki to-a2 khui-chhut chit8-tiau5 lou7. Beh kau3 loulu chhiu7-na5 chin3-cheng5, i it-tit8 kiaN5ti7 chiaN3-chhiu2 peng5 e5 lou7, phiah-bian2 khi3 bong tioh8 chhiu7-hioh8.
“Ka7 liah8 khit-lai5!” Ka-moho-alii chiok toa7-siaN khi2-kong5 teh jiang2. Te7-saN-e5 siu2-ui7 iong7 i-e5 kapu, hiong3 Aukele kong3 loh8-khi3, m7-koh siong3-liau2 bo5 chun2, kapu long3 tioh8 thou5-kha liau2-au7, soah thiau3 to2 tng2 lai5.  Chiok sit-bong7 e5 Ka-moho-alii kang7-khoan2 iong7 i e5 pou2-thau5 hiong3 Aukele phut loh8-khi3.
Chit-e5 si5-chun7 e5 Aukele na7-si7 bo5 siuN7 beh thau5-khak hou7 lang5 phoa3 cho3 nng7-peng5, i kan-taN u7 chit8-e5 soan2-tek8!  Kha-chhiu2 me2-liah8 e5 Aukele koaN2-kin2 thiau3 jip8-khi3 louluia5-chi2 chhiu7-na5--nih.  Sui-jian5 siam2 koe3 ka-moho-alii e5 pou2-thau5, m7-koh i hoat-kak loulu chhiu7 seN liau2 
 
chiok chat8--e5, kin-pun2 to7 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang seh8 koe3-khi3. I khi3 houchhi7u-a2 “liah8” tiau5--leh.
Tng-teh kan-sin5 chhio3 e5 Ka-moho-alii, chiok hiau5-pai io5-io5-pai2-pai2 chit8-pou7 chit8-pou7 an3 i chit-peng5 koe3-lai5.
Kiong5-beh chhun siong7 boe2-a2 chit8-khau2-khui3 e5 Aukele, se3-siaN teh mng7: “Lono!  Goa2 koh lai5 beh an2-choaN2?”
“Li2-e5 hong5-hoe2 ui5-kin!  Hui2-biat8 chia-e5 loulu ia5-chi2-chhiu7!” Lono beng7-leng7.  Aukele kin2 ka7 i e5 ui5-kin sa chhut-lai5 seh8.  Soa3 loh8-lai5 to7-si7 chit8-chun7 chiok toa7-pha e5 hoe2 kap chit8-thoan5 ian-bu7.  Tng chia-e5 ian-bu7 siau-sit e5 si5,chhun loh8-lai5 e5 kan-taN si7 chit8-koa2 hoe2-hu nia7-nia7. Tong5-si5an chit-tui hoe2-hu au7-piah kiaN5--koe3-lai5 si7 --- Ka-moho-alii!
Aukele koaN2-kin2 chau2 li7-khui.  Ka-moho-alii kang7-khoan2 toe3 ti7 au7-piah piaN3-mia7 tui. Aukele chau2 kah e7-sai2 tit khoaN3-tioh8 an3 pong7-khnag chhio7--jip8-lai5 e5 jit8-thau5 kng.  Put-ko3, Ka-moho-alii si7 lu2-jiok lu2-kin2.  Aukele piaN3 i siong7 boe2 chit8-khau2-khui3, hiong3 pong7-khang chhiong khit--lih.  Tng i peh khit--lih chiuN7-kia7 e5 lou7 e5 si5, bo5 se3-ji7 soah chhu7-to2.  Ka-moho-alii hoah kah chiok toa7-siaN, thiau3 hiong3 Aukele sin-khu. Chit-kai3, Aukele peng7 bo5 mng7 Lono beh an2-choaN2 cho3.  I ka7 i e5 kha kiukhit-lai5, piaN3-mia7 iat8 i e5 chhiu2-kut, lang5 thoaN5 poe khit-lai5.  Lang5 it-tit8 phu5 khit-lai5, poe chhut ou-am3.  poe chhut “Am3-me5-chi-khut”. lai5 kau3 chiok iam7 e5 jit8-thau5 e7-bin7.
Toe3 ti7 au7-piah e5 Ka-moho-alii bak8-chiu hou7 kng-soaN3 chhio7 kah thau5-hin5 bak8-am3, toa7-siaN chhan chit8-e7 to7 koh kiu to2 tng2-khi3.  Aukele ke3-siok poe, poe li7-khui “Am3-me5-chi-khut” poe koe3 hai2-bin7, poe hiong3 chhu3--nih.
Tng i poe kau3 cha2-cheng5 chun5 phang2-kiN3 e5 sou2-chai7, Aukele ka7 hou5-lou5 that-a2 giu2-khui, ka7 “SeN3-mia7-chi-chui2”lam5 tui3 hai2--nih.
I teh tan2-thai7 ki5-chek chhut-hian7, it-tit8 ti7 hai2-bin7 teng2-thau5 seh8-lai5 seh8-khi3.  Si5-kan ti7 ki5-thai7 tiong chit8-hun chit8-bio2 koe3, m7-koh sim2-mih8 to bo5 hoat-seng, chham chun5 a2 to bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, mai3 kong2 si7 a-hiaN kap tit8-a2, kan-taN thiaN-tioh8 sui5 hai2-hong thoan5 lai5 e5 hau2-kio3 siaN.
“Aukele!  Be7-sai2!” he si7 Namaka e5 siaN, tng i teh poe ui3 Aukele chit-peng5 lai5 chhoe7 i, “Kan-taN goa2 e7-sai2 kiu3 li2-e5 hiaN-ti7! “SeN3-mia7-chi-chui2” kam2 long2 iong7 oan5--a, Aukele?”
Aukele toa7-lat8 io5 hou5-lou5, kiat-ko2 kan-taN thiaN tioh8 chiok se3-siaN e5 chui2-siaN: “Chhun chit8-sut-a2 nia7-nia7.”  I chiaN5 siong-sim teh kong2.
“Ka7 to3 koe3-lai5 goa2-e5 chhiu2--nih.” Namaka ian5-lou7 beng7-leng7, ian5-lou7 ka7 i e5 chhiu2-te5 kiu cho3 chit8-e5 te2-chui2 e5 poe-a2-heng5. Aukele chiau3 i kong2 e5 khi3 cho3.  Namaka ka7 chia-e5 chui2 phoah tui3 e7-bin7 e5 hai2--nih khi3, koh na2 beng7-leng7 teh kong2: “Poe tng2-khi3, m7-thang oat8-thau5 khoaN3!”
Sui-jian5 chiaN5 m7 cheng5-goan7, Aukele ma7-si7 khai-si2 poe tng2-khi3 chhu3--nih. Chit8-bin7 poe chit8-bin7 teh siuN7:“seN3-mia7-chi-chui2” kam2 chin-chiaN3 u7 hau7?  Kam2 e7 in-ui7 siuNchio2, soah bo5 hau7-ko2--neh? Au7-piah chhin-chhiuN7 u7 chit8-e5 chiok kiong5 na2 khip-chioh8 e5 lek8-liong7 teh ka7 giu2, beh kio3 i oat8-thau5 khoaN3--i kiong5-beh tong3 be7-tiau5--a. “Lono, chhiaN2 su3 hou7 goa2 lek8-liong7.” Aukele chhui3--nih seh8-seh8 liam7.
I bak8-chiu liah8 thau5-cheng5 kim-kim khoaN3, khoaN3-kiN3 si3-e5 chiau2-hiaN-ti7 poe an3 chit-peng5 koe3-lai5, koh hoah kah chiok hoaN-hi2.  Iu5 koh u7 niau2-chhi2-che2 kap sin7-thang5-a2-chem2a khia7 ti7 hai2-kiN5 hiong3 i 
 
iat8-chhiu2.  Che si7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7--neh?  Peh-khui bak8-chiu kim-kim siong3: “Ah!  He sicha7-pou e5 png7-thiaN, goa2 oh8 e7-hiau2 poe e5 sou2-chai7, he si7 Namaka e5 chhu3, goa2 bat hioh ti7 i e5 chhu3-boe2-teng2 koe3!  Tioh8!  Chia tioh8-si7 goa2 kap Namaka kiong7-tong5 kian3-lip8 eka5-hng5.” Siong7 boe2-a2  i khoaN3-tioh8 poe ti7 e7-kha-bin7 e5 Namaka.
“Li2 chit-ma2 e7-sai2 oat8-thau5 khoaN3--a.” Namaka ti7 i e5 hiN7 piN kong2.
Aukele oat8-thau5 khoaN3 to2 tng2-khi3 hai2--nih.  KhoaN3-kiN3 hit-chiah sit-chong e5 toa7-chiah chun5 sai2 an3 hoaN7-kiN5 koe3-lai5, Iku-lani kap “Chi3-seng3” khia7 ti7 chun5-thau5 teh hiong3 i iat8-chhiu2.
Aukele chiok hoaN-hi2--e5, seh8-sin khoaN3 Namaka.“kam2-sia7 li2, Namaka!  M7-koh tan2--chit8-e7!”  I chiong ti7 Namaka chhiu2-thau5 e5 hou5-lou5 theh8 koe3-lai5: “Ka7 li2-e5 chhiu2-te5 thian2-khui.”
ChiaN5 ki5-koai3, Namaka keng3-jian5 koai-koai-a2 chiau3 i e5 i3-su3. Si3-tih chui2 tih loh8-khi3 i e5 chhiu2-te5 tang-tiong.  I bo5 saN2 liau2-kai2, tau3-te2 Aukele si7 teh piN3 saN2-mih8 bang2?  Kan-taN liah8 in ang kim-kim khoaN3.
“Goa2 chai-iaN2 li2 ka7 Moela lau3-kau khi3--a.” Aukele kong2.
“Ou5! Goa2 chai--a?” chhio3 bi5-bi5 e5 Namaka ka7 chit kui2-tih chui2 tih loh8-khi3 thou5-kha, iah8 to7-si7 Moela hou7 Aukele piN3-cho3 hoe2-hu e5 sou2-chai7. “Mau2” chit8-siaN khoai3-lok8 e5 kau2-pui7 siaN, Moela iu7-koh e7-tang3 che7 ti7 Namaka e5 sin-khu piN--lou3.
In chit8-ke-hoe2 saN-e5, Aukele, Namaka kap Moela ---ang-sai3, khan-chhiu2 kap kou3-chhu3 e5 kau2-a2 --- hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2 cho3-tin7 kiaN5 khi3 hai2-kiN5 chiap-thai7 tng-teh chu7-hoe7 e5 chhin-chiaN5.
