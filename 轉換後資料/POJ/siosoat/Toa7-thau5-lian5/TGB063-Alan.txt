# A-lan5 kap i e5 chi-moe7-a2-phoann7

Tai7-hak8 lian5-kho2 chhim2 kho2 soah, A-lan5 sui5 kap Siu3-bi2 ham7 Ka-bun5 chit nng7 e5 chi2-moe7-a2-phoann7 sio-chio khi3 hai2-pinn-a2 sng2 chui2, siunn7 beh ho2-ho2-a2 pang3-sang--1-e7, sann tang lai5, tek8-piat8 si7 ko-sann chit toann7 si5-kan, ui7-tioh8 beh piann3 lian5-kho2, e7-sai2 kong2 tu5-khi3 chhu3--nih kap hak8-hau7, bo5 ki5-thann sou2-chai7 thang khi3, tak8-kang pak8 ti7 chheh-tui lai7-te2, bo5 khi2-siau2 ma7 e7 phoa3-penn7, tann, chong2-sng3 kho2 soah--a, e7-tang3 thau2 chit8 khau2-khui3--a.

Kin-a2-jit8 e5 jit8-thau5 put-chi2-a2 me2, Thai3-peng5-iunn5 hou7 jit8-thau5-kng chhio7 kah ou-kim ou-kim, chhenn-chhui3 e5 Hai2-hoaN7 Soann-meh8 khia7 ti7 pinn--a2. E-pou si5-a2, be7-chio2 lang5 lai5 ti7 hai2-kinn5 sng2 chui2 khioh chioh8-thau5, pinn--a2 kiam cho3 chhah-chhia iong7 e5 kong2-tiunn5, u7 be7 ki-a2-peng, ou-lian2 kap hang ian-chhian5 e5 tann3-a2; thau5-cheng5 hai2-bin7 khoann3 e7 tioh8 chap8 goa7 chiah hi5-chun5-a2 tng teh siu bang7-a2, u7 e5 kha-chhiu2 khah me2--e5, i2-keng tng2-lai5 ti7 hai2-kinn5 ka7 liah8--tioh8 e5 hi5-a2 chng-siunn, toa3 hu7-kin7 e5 lang5 e7 tit8-chiap ti7 chia be2 hi5-a2.

“Aih~ au sann tang to7 ui7-tioh8 kin-a2-jit8, chong2-sng3 kiat-sok--a.” A-lan5 thou2 chit8 khau2-khui3, be7-su beh ka7 chit sann tang e5 ut-chut chioh chit khau2-khui3 cho3-hoe2 thau2-pang3--chhut-lai5.

“Siunn7 be7 kau3 u7-iann2 kiat-sok--a neh, kam2-kak ka2-na2 u7 siann2-mih mih8-kiann7 ui3 seng-khu lai7-te2 bo5--khi3.” Siu3-bi2 chiap-soa3 A-lan5 e5 oe7-boe2 kong2--loh8-khi3.

“Aih--ioh~ lin2 siunn7-siunn7 hiah che7 beh chhong3-a2? Tiong7-iau3--e5 si7 koh lai5 e7-sai2 ho2-ho2-a2 siau2--chit8-e7-a, ka7 chit sann tang bo5 sng2--tioh8-e5 long2 pou2-pou2--tng2-lai5.” Ka-bun5 tan2 chit kang tan2 chiok ku2--a, tui3 i chit chiong2 be7-ai3 thak8-chheh e5 lang5 lai5 kong2, nge7 hong5 ah leh kho2 tai7-hak8 si7 siong7 kan-khou2 e5 tai7-chi3.

“Lang5 e5 pak-tou2 teh kio3--a-lah, lan2 lai5-khi3 chiah8 hi5-a2 ho2--bo5?” A-lan5 e5 cheng2-thau5-a2 ki2 tui3 hi5-chun5-a2 kho3-hoann7 e5 sou2-chai7, i e5 pak-tou2 ui3 thau5-tu2-a2 to7 it-tit8 teh kio3--a.

“Iau-kui2 gin2-a2 khoann3 lang5 liah8-hi5 tng2--lai5 to7 teh lau5 chhui3-noa7--a hounnh.” Tu2-chiah sng2 chui2 sng2 beh nng7 tiam2-cheng ku2, Siu3-bi2 tng teh giau-gi5 kin-a2-jit8 A-lan5 nah e7 chiah kham-tit iau, A-lan5 sui5 to7 khui-chhui3 tho2-chiah8--a.

“Thiann li2 an2-ne kong2, goa2 e5 pak-tou2 ma7 teh kio3--a, Siu3-bi2, lan2 lai5 khoann3 Hok-peh--a2 kin-a2-jit8 u7 liah8-tioh8 siann2-mih hi5-a2.” Ka-bun5 khia7--khi2-lai5, kap A-lan5 Siu3-bi2 chau2 khi3 chhoe7 Hok-peh--a2.

Hok-peh--a2 kap Siu3-bi2 in lau7-pe7 si7 oann7-thiap--e5, nng7 ke-hoe2-a2 pi2 kang7 ka-chok8-a2 koh khah chhin, Siu3-bi2 in lau7pe7 chhiang5-chai7 kap Hok-peh--a2 cho3-hoe2 chhut-hai2 liah8-hi5. Cheng5 kui2 kang Siu3-bi2 in lau7-pe7 siu7-siong, kau3 tann koh be7-tang3 chhut-hai2, Hok-peh--a2 tak8 kang long2 e7 kio3 in hau7-senn liah8 kui2 boe2 hi5-a2 khi3 Siu3-bi2 in tau.

“Enn5~ hit e5 kam2 m7-si7 A-sau2? Siu3-bi2, li2 e5 a-na2-tah teh tan2--li2 neh.” A-lan5 khoann3 A-sau2 ui3 thau5-cheng5 kiann5--koe3-lai5, liah8-tioh8 ki-hoe7 ka7 Siu3-bi2 khoe.

Siu3-bi2 ka7 A-lan5 gin5: “Siann2-mih a-na2-tah, li2 bo5 siunn7 beh chiah8 hi5-a2--a hounnh?”

“Lan2 beh chiah8 lang5 a-na2-tah e5 hi5-a2 tioh8-ai3 khah pa-kiat--e5-lah.” Ka-bun5 toa7-siann chhio3--chhut-lai5, na2 chhio3 na2 li7 Siu3-bi2 hng7-hng7, na7 bo5, tan2--leh hou7 i bok--tioh8 to7 chai thiann3.

A-sau2 si7 Hok-peh--a2 e5 toa7-han3 hau7-senn, pian7-na7 hak7-hau7 pang3-ka2 to7 toe3 Hok-peh--a2 chhut-hai2 liah8-hi5, i kap A-lan5 in kang7 kai3, kin-a2-jit8 kang7-khoan2 tu2 kho2 soah, e-pou to7 lai5 chia ka7 in lau7-pe7 tau3-sann-kang7. Pun2-te2 i tui3 Siu3-bi2 kan-tann si7 chhiunn7 hiann-moe7-a2 e5 kam2-cheng5, kau3 in seng sann-ni5-a2, tak8 kang pang3-oh8 liau2-au7 sio-chio khi3 tou5-su-koan2 thak8-chheh, cho3-hoe2 e5 si5-kan che7--a, kong2 e5 oe7-te5 ma7 toe3 leh cheng-ka, tek8-piat8 si7 tng teh chun2-pi7 kho2-chhi3, sim-koann-thau5 e5 ap-lek8 na7-si7 pinn--a2 u7 lang5 thang kong2, chong2--si7 khah khin-sang, si5-kan ku2--lai5, A-sau2 tui3 Siu3-bi2 e5 kam2-cheng5 ma7 pang3 ju2 tang7, tong5-oh8 khoann3 in tak8 jit8 cho3-phoann7 tng2 chhu3, nng7 ke-hoe2-a2 koh hiah chhin--tioh8, tong-jian5 ma7 ka7 in nng7 e5 sak-cho3-tui. A-lan5 kap Ka-bun5 chai-iann2 A-sau2 tui3 Siu3-bi2 u7 i3-su3, siunn7 beh jiok--i, long2 e7 the3 A-sau2 che3-cho7 ki-hoe7.

“Siu3-bi2, A-lan5, Ka-bun5, lin2 lai5 hai2-kinn5-a2 chhit-tho5--ou?” A-sau2 khoann3-tioh8 Siu3-bi2, bak8-chiu long2 kim--khi2-lai5-a.

“A-sau2, goan2 A-bi2 kong-chu2 siunn7 beh chiah8 hi5--lah, lin2 kin-a2-jit8 kam2 u7 liah8-tioh8 siann2-mih hi5-a2?” A-lan5 chhiunn2 ti7 Siu3-bi2 khui-chhui3 chin3-cheng5 seng kong2.

Siu3-bi2 si7 chai7-te7 e5 Pangcah, bo2-gi2 thiann u7 si7 iau2 thiann u7, m7-koh bo5 siann2 e7-hiau2 kong2, A-lan5 kap Ka-bun5 u7 tang-si5-a2 long2 kio3 i A-bi2 kong-chu2. Siu3-bi2 in lau7-pe7 kap Hok-peh--a2 si7 oann-thiap e5 koan-he7, nng7 ke ong2-lai5 long2 si7 kong2 Ho7-lo2-oe7, m7-chiah Siu3-bi2 Ho7-lo2-oe7 ma7 kong2 kah put-chi2-a2 lian3-tng2, kap A-lan5 Ka-bun5 in ma7 khah chiap8 kong2 Ho7-lo2-oe7.

“Oeh~ m7-chai si7 siang2 khoann3 lang5 hi5-chun5-a2 liah8-hi5 tng2--lai5, pak-tou2 to7 khai-si2 teh kio3--e5 hounnh? A-sau2, si7 chit nng7 chiah teh lau5 chhui3-noa7--lah.” Siu3-bi2 chhun-chhiu2 liam3 A-lan5 e5 chhui3-phoe2, am3-si7 i m7-thang koh ou-peh8 kong2-oe7.

“Hah...hah...A-lan5 kap Ka-bun5 i2-keng hou7 li2 ka3 kah pian3 chiah8-hi5 choan-ka--a. Goan2 a-pa u7 lau5 chit8-koa2 ian-a2-hi5, e7-sai2 theh8 lai5 hang.” A-sau2 chio in sann e5 lau5 ti7 hai2-pinn-a2 hang hi5-a2, than3 tu2 kho2 soah, A-sau2 ma7 siunn7 beh hioh-chhoan2--chit8-e7. In si3 e5 lang5 kiann5 oa2 Hok-peh--a2 hia.

“Pa5-a2, goan2 siunn7 beh lai5 hang ian-a2-hi5, am3-si5 goa2 to7 bo5 beh tng2 chhu3 chiah8-png7--a-ou.” A-sau2 na2 kap in lau7-pe7 kong2-oe7, na2 ui3 thang2-a2-te2 ka7 ian-a2-hi5 liah8 chhut-lai5 thai5.

“A-peh, khoann3--khi2-lai5 kin-a2-jit8 e5 siu-seng5 be7-bai2--ou.” Siu3-bi2 ka7 Hok-peh--a2 ai-sat-chuh.

“A-bi2--ou, ah kho2 kah an2-choann2?” Hok-peh--a2 chhio3-chhio3 khoann3 Siu3-bi2 in, i tui3 Siu3-bi2 to7 na2-chhiunn7 tui3 ka-ti7 e5 cha-bou2-kiann2 kang7-khoan2.

“A-peh, Siu3-bi2 i bo5 bun7-te5--lah! It-teng7 e7 tiau5--e5-lah!” A-lan5 the3 Siu3-bi2 in3, kong2 sit8-chai7--e5, Siu3-bi2 e5 seng5-chek it-tit7 long2 be7-bai2.

“Tioh8--lah! A-peh, an--lah! Su-inn7 eng3-kai bo5 bun7-te5......, goa2 si7 kong2 Siu3-bi2--lah, li2 ma7 chai-iann2 goa2 to khah be7-ai3 thak8-chheh, na7 u7 chit8 keng hak8-hau7 e7-sai2 hou7 goa2 thak8, goa2 tui3 goan2 pa5--a2 to7 kau-tai3 e7 koe3--a.” Ka-bun5 phainn2-se3 phainn2-se3 an2-ne kong2, na7 hou7 in lau7-pe7 thiann--tioh8, i to7 tho2 phoe5 thiann3--a.

“Iau2 m7-chai--leh, ai3 tan2 hong3-png2 chiah chai-iann2--lah. A-peh, tan2--leh beh cho3-hoe2 hang hi5-a2--bo5?” Siu3-bi2 kong2 soah, kap A-lan5 Ka-bun5 kiann5 koe3-khi3 ka7 Hok-peh--a2 tau3-sann-kang7.

“Lin2 siau3-lian5--e5 hang to7 ho2, goa2 lau7-hoe3-a2 koh teh kap lin2 tau3-lau7-jiat8? Goa2 tan2--leh ka7 hi5-a2 chai3 khi3 chhi7-tiunn5 liau2-au7, beh khi3 chhoe7 lin2 lau7-pe7, goa2 khoann3 i hioh chiah ku2, lian5 an2-choann2 liah8-hi5 ma7 be7-ki3--a.”

Hok-peh--a2 chu7-te2 si7 Pin5-tong-lang5, siau3-lian5 si5-chun7 chhiang5-chai7 lai5 Hoa-lian liah8-hi5, si7-toa7-lang5 koe3-sin liau2-au7, to7 poann koe3-lai5 Hoa-lian toa3, kap Siu3-bi2 in lau7-pe7 to7-si7 ui3 siau3-lian5-si5 liah8-hi5 sek8-sai7 kau3 tann--e5, A-sau2 si7 ti7 Hoa-lian chia chhut-si3--e5, goa7-ma2 si7 Pangcah, sng3--khi2-lai5 A-sau2 ma7 u7 1/4 Pangcah e5 hoeh-thong2, A-sau2 chit e5 mia5 to7-si7 in a-ma2 ho7--e5.

“A-sau2, goa2 seng khi3 chhi7-tiunn5, chia iau2 u7 chit8-koa2 he5-a2, cho3-hoe2 hang-hang--leh, lin2 m7-theng sng2 kau3 siunn oann3--ou.” Hok-peh--a2 ka7 ke-si siu-siu--leh, to7 chai3 hi5-a2 khi3 chhi7-tiunn5 be7--a.

“Siu3-bi2, A-sau2, goan2 seng khi3 kam2-a2-tiam3 be2 liang5--e5, lin2 seng khi2-hoe2--ou.” A-lan5 ham7 Ka-bun5 ka7 Siu3-bi2 sai2 chit8 e7 bak8-boe2, to7 chau2 kah bo5 khoann3-kinn3 iann2--a.

Theng3-hau7 A-lan5 ham7 Ka-bun5 ka7 liang5--e5 be2--tng2-lai5, Siu3-bi2 kap A-sau2 i2-keng ka7 hoe2 khi2 ho2 teh hang--a.

“Joah8--lang5 ti7 hai2-kinn5-a2 hang-bah siong7 song2--lah!” A-sau2 ka-ti7 kong2 kah chiok song2--e5. Siu3-bi2 sui5 ka7 i phoah leng2-chui2: “Lan2 nah u7 hang bah? Si7 hang hi5-a2 ho2--bo5!”

“Aih--ioh, chha bo5 joa7 che7--lah, li2 khoann3 A-lan5 e5 chhui3-noa7 lau5 kah kui thou5-kha--a.” Ka-bun5 ke2-iann2 chhun-chhiu2 beh sin5 A-lan5 e5 chhui3-noa7, soah hou7 A-lan5 phah--tng2-khi3.

“Si7 kong2 boai3 hou7 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 khi2 lih hi5-chun5-a2 teng2, bo5, goa2 u7-iann2 chiok siunn7 beh kap li2 kang7-khoan2 chhut-hai2 khoann3-mai7--leh, ka2-na2 be7-bai2 sng2 hounnh?” A-lan5 tui3 A-sau2 an2-ne kong2.

Siu3-bi2 ka7 A-lan5 thuh-chhau3 kong2: “Pai3-thok--e5, li2 lian5 che7 kong-chhia to e7 hin5 e5 lang5, koh kong2 beh toe3 lang5 chhut-hai2 liah8-hi5, li2 ma7『幫幫忙』, goa2 khoann3 kau3-si5 lian5 tann2-chiap to bo5 kau3 li2 thou3--ou.”

“Lang5 kong2 ian-a2-hi5 si7 san3-chhiah-lang5 teh chiah8--e5, m7-koh goa2 iau2 si7 siong7 kah-i3 chiah8 ian-a2-hi5, tek8-piat8 si7 tu2 liah8--tioh8 e5 ian-a2-hi5, u7 chhinn, khi3-bi7 koh chan2.” A-sau2 na2 hang na2 kong2. Ian-a2-hi5 it-poann-lang5 kong2 si7 khah chhau3-chian7 e5 hi5-a2, m7-koh na7 e7-hiau2 chiah8 hi5--e5, to7 be7 an2-ne kong2--a.

Si3 e5 lang5 na2 oe7-sian na2 chhng2 hi5-a2, chit8 thang2 ian-a2-hi5 kap he5-a2, bo5 sann tiam2-cheng ku2 to7 chiah8 kah chheng-khi3 tang-tang--a, A-lan5, Siu3-bi2, Ka-bun5 kap A-sau2 to2 ti7 soa-pou teng2-koan5 khoann3 thinn-teng2 e5 goeh8-niu5, poe-leng5-ki put-si5 ui3 in thau5-khak teng2 poe--koe3, kok-lang5 tng2-khi3 kau3 chhu3, ma7 chap8-tiam2 goa7--a.

Tan2-thai7 hong3-png2 e5 jit8-chi2, A-lan5 ka7 chhu3--nih tau3 choh-chhan5; Ka-bun5 ti7 chit8 keng ka-pi-tiam3 cho3 part-time; Siu3-bi2 tng2-khi3 Widi pou7-lok8, kap chit8-koa2 chhut-goa7 thak8-chheh pang3-ka2 tng2--lai5 e5 siau3-lian5-lang5, chhoa7 pou7-lok8 lai7-te2 e5 gin2-a2 thak8-chheh. Lang5 kong2 e5 “au7-soann” chiah-e5 pou7-lok8, chu-goan5 kap chu-sin3 iau2 si7 kap tou-chhi7 be7 pi2--tit, pou7-lok8 lai7-te2 i3-sek tioh8 chit e5 bun7-te5 ju2 lai5 ju2 giam5-tiong7, to7 chio pou7-lok8 lai7-te2 e5 siau3-lian5--e5, li7-iong7 joah8--lang5 kap koann5--lang5 hak8-hau7 pang3-ka2 e5 si5-kan, siat-ke3 chit8-koa2 oah8-tang7, ka3 gin2-a2 thak8-chheh, oh8 tian7-nau2. In sann e5 ma7 put-si5 chio--leh khi3 khoann3 tian7-iann2, seh8-ke. A-sau2 kang7-khoan2 tak8 kang toe3 Hok-peh--a2 chhut-hai2 liah8-hi5.

Teh thiam chi3-goan7 e5 si5, A-lan5 bo5 beh chiau3 in lau7-pe7 e5 i3-su3, nng7 e5 lang5 soah khi2 chhiong-tut8, lou7-boe2 A-lan5 ka7 ka-ti7 koainn ti7 pang5-keng lai7-te2, ui3 siong7 hng7 e5 hak8-hau7 khai-si7 thiam, thiam ho2 to7 sui5 ka7 toann-a2 kau--chhut-khi3, in lau7-pe7 chai-iann2 liau2-au7, ka7 A-lan5 me7 kah kiong7 beh chhau3-thau5. Boe2--a2, A-lan5 kho2-tiau5 Tai5-pak Tai7-hak8, Siu3-bi2 u7-iann2 tiau5 Hoa-lian Su-inn7, A-sau2 ti7 Tang-hoa5 Tai7-hak8, Ka-bun5 tioh8 Goan5-ti3 Tai7-hak8.

Le2-pai3 tiong-tau3, A-lan5 Siu3-bi2 Ka-bun5 sann e5 lang5 iok ti7 Ka-bun5 cho3 part-time hit keng ka-pi-tiam3.

“Lin2 nng7 e5 long2 chau2 kah hiah hng7--tioh8, chhun goa2 chit8 e5 lang5 lau5 ti7 Hoa-lian, u7-kau3 bo5 i3-su3--e5.” Siu3-bi2 chiann5 m7-kam i e5 chi2-moe7-a2-phoann7 beh li7-khui Hoa-lian khi3 goa7 koan7-chhi7 thak8 tai7-hak8.

“To7 m7-si7 bo5 beh koh tng2--lai5-a, na7 pang3-ka2 to7 e7-sai2 koh kinn3-bin7--a, li2 ma7 e7-sai2 lai5 chhoe7--goan2-a, si7 kong2 goa2 tioh8-ai3 khi3 chhoe7 khoann3 u7 part-time e5 sit-thau5--bo5, su-lip8 e5 hak8-hui3 u7-kau3 kui3--e5, be7-su si7 iong7 chhiunn2--e5, kiann--si2-lang5.” Ka-bun5 it-hiong3 khah lok8-koan, chit-ma2 i kho2-tioh8 su-lip8 e5 hak8-hau7, chhu3--nih e5 keng-che3 ap-lek8 koh ju2 toa7--a, chong2--si7 i jin7-ui5 u7 hak8-hau7 thang thak8 to7 e7-tang3 thau-chhio3--a, koh lai5 to7-si7 hoat-hui i『打工女王』e5 kong-lek8, than3 koa2 seng-oah8-hui3.『打工女王』si7 tong5-oh8 ka7 i ho7--e5, Ka-bun5 ui3 ko-it to7 khai-si2 chhoe7 part-time cho3--a, si7 ko-sann hit toann7 si5-kan in lau7-pe7 m7-chun2 chiah bo5 cho3--e5.

“Tioh8 a7, goan2 ah8 m7-si7 be7 tng2--lai5-a, koh-chai3 kong2, goa2 e7-tang3 li7 chhu3 hng7-hng7 siong7 ho2, khah bian2 kui kang teh kap goan2 chhu3--nih e5 lang5 oan-ke.” A-lan5 ka7 i thiam chi3-goan7 hit toann7 kou3-su7 kong2 hou7 Siu3-bi2 ham7 Ka-bun5 thiann. A-lan5 oan-na ki5-thai7 koh lai5 e5 tai7-hak8 seng-oah8, oan-na m7-kam tioh8 li7-khui chiah-ni7 sui2 e5 kou3-hiong kap i e5 peng5-iu2.

# Tai7-hak8 seng-oah8

Lang5 kong2 tai7-hak8 si7 hou7 li2 sng2 si3 tang, A-lan5 khak8-sit8 u7 chit-lo7 kam2-siu7, koh thiat-te2 chip-heng5. Khai-hak8 kau3 tann bo5 chit8 goeh8-jit8, A-lan5 to7 kap toa3 kang7 sok-sia7 pang5-keng e5 peng5-iu2 tau3-tin7 kah chiok bah8 a7, ti7 in hit pan ma7 kau-tioh8 chiann5 che7 ma3-chih e5 tong5-oh8. Chit8 hak8-ki5 loh8--lai5, m7-tann7 kap peng5-iu2 ka7 toa7 Tai5-pak te7-khu sng2 kah beh peng2--koe3, koh hong5 hong cho3『翹課女王』.

Chit kang, A-lan5 kap kang7 pang5 e5 A-phiah, Bi2-leng5 ham7 Nga2-hui7 teh chiah8 hak8-tiunn2 sang3--lai5 e5『期中考貢品』, na2 khai-kang2 na2 khoann3-chheh. In tak8-pai2 to sai2 chinn kha-chhng--a chiah beh pang3, phou2-thong si5-a2 kho3 bo5 teh siong7, chheh bo5 teh thak8, tan2 kau3 beh kho2-chhi3--a, chiah thau3-me5 bo5 khun3 cho3 pit-ki3, ma7 put-si5 chit8 kang2--loh8-khi3 to7 kong2 beh kui me5, kiat-ko2 si7 chheh bo5 thak8 liau2, ma7 bo5 khun3--tioh8, koe3 tng2 kang to7 e7 thiann-tioh8 in si3 e5 ti7 hia ai--a.

『隔壁房的阿芳上禮拜又回家了, 真誇張, 幾乎每個禮拜都回家.』Bi2-leng5 ui3 goa7-khau2 thin5-te5--jip8-lai5 teh kong2, khoann3--lai5 it-teng7 koh oat khi3 keh-piah pang5 la7-le--a.
