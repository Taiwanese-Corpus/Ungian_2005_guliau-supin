[1]	Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 oe7 lim5-kau3 Mou5-li7-sa lang5 Bi2-ka, chiu7-si7 ti7 Iu5-tai7 Ong5 Iok-than2, A-hap-su, Hi-se-ka e5 si5, sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 lun7-kau3 Sat-ma2-li7-a kap Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 su7.

	12). Kong-phoa3--e5 ti7 in e5 thau5-cheng5 chiuN7-khi3; in kong-phoa3 tit8-tit8 keng-ke3 kau3 siaN5-mng5; koh tui3-hia chhut-lai5; in e5 ong5 choe3-thau5 kiaN5, Ia5-ho5-hoa chhoa7 in khi3.

[3]	Goa2 kong2, Nga2-kok chiah e5 siu2-leng2, I2-sek-liat8-ke chiah e5 koaN-tiuN2 ah, lin2 tioh8 thiaN; lin2 kiam2-m7-tioh8 chai-iaN2 kong-peng5 mah?  2). [4]	Kau3 hiah e5 jit8 e5 liau2-au7, Ia5-ho5-hoa tian7 e5 soaN beh kian3-lip8 ti7 chiah e5 soaN e5 teng2-bin7, poaN5-ke3 long2-chong2 e5 soaN-nia2; ban7-kok e5 peh-siN3 long2 beh lai5 kui i.  2).
