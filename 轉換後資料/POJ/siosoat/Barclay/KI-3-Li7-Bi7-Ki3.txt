	Li7-Bi7-Ki3	(3)
[1]	Ia5-ho5-hoa kio3 Mou5-se tui3 hoe7-bou7-lai7, ka7 i kong2,  2). Li2 tioh8 hoan-hu3 I2-sek-liat8 lang5 ka7 in kong2, Lin2 tiong-kan na7 u7 lang5 hian3 le2-mih8 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa, tioh8 tui3 gu5-kun5 iuN5-kun5 tiong hian3 cheng-siN choe3 le2-mih8.
	3). I e5 le2-mih8 na7 tui3 gu5-kun5 chhu2 choe3 sio-che3, chiu7 tioh8 hian3 chit8 chiah bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 gu5-kang2; tioh8 ti7 hoe7-bou7 mng5-khau2 hian3 i, thang ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 tit-tioh8 chiap-lap8.  4). I tioh8 hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 sio-che3 e5 cheng-siN e5 thau5-khak-teng2, chiu7 ui7-tioh8 i siu7 chiap-lap8, thoe3 i siok8-choe7.  5). I tioh8 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 thai5 gu5-kang2-a2; A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun choe3 che3-si e5, tioh8 hian3 hit e5 huih, chiong huih hiu3 ti7 hoe7-bou7 mng5-khau2, toaN5 e5 si3-chiu-ui5.  6). I tioh8 pak sio-che3 e5 cheng-siN e5 phe5, chhiat i e5 bah chit8-te3 chit8-te3.  7). Che3-si A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun tioh8 eng7 he2 he7 ti7 toaN5-teng2, eng7 chha5 pai5 ti7 he2 e5 teng2-bin7.  8). A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun choe3 che3-si--e5, tioh8 chiong bah-te3 kap thau5 kap iu5, pai5 ti7 toaN5-teng2 he2--nih e5 chha5.  9). Tok8-tok8 sio-che3 e5 chong7-hu2 kap thui2, i tioh8 eng7 chui2 soe2 i; che3-si chiu7 tioh8 chiong it-chhe3 long2 sio ti7 toaN5-teng2 choe3 sio-che3, hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.
	10). I e5 le2-mih8, na7 tui3 iuN5-kun5-tiong lai5 choe3 sio-che3, hek8-si7 mi5-iuN5, hek8-si7 soaN-iuN5, tioh8 eng7 bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 kang--e5 lai5 hian3.  11). Tioh8 ti7 toaN5-piN e5 pak--si3, thai5 i ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5; A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun choe3 che3-si--e5, tioh8 eng7 i e5 huih hiu3 ti7 toaN5 e5 si3-chiu-ui5.  12). Tioh8 ka7 i chhiat chit8-te3 chit8-te3, lian5 thau5 kap iu5; che3-si tioh8 pai5 ti7 toaN5-teng2 he2--nih e5 chha5.  13). Tok8-tok8 chong7-hu2 kap thui2, tioh8 eng7 chui2 soe2 i; che3-si tioh8 chiong it-chhe3 long2 hian3, sio ti7 toaN5-teng2; che si7 sio-che3, hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.
	14). I hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 le2-mih8, na7 eng7 chiau2-chiah choe3 sio-che3, chiu7 tioh8 hian3 ka-chui a2-si7 chiN2-a2-kiaN2 choe3 le2-mih8.  15). Che3-si tioh8 toa3 chiau2-a2 kau3 toaN5-cheng5, chun7-tng7 i e5 thau5, sio ti7 toaN5-teng2; i e5 huih tioh8 lek8 ti7 toaN5-piN.  16). Koh tioh8 chiong chiau2 e5 kui kap i e5 mng5 tu5--khi3, hiat ti7 toaN5-piN e5 tang--si3, he2-hu e5 sou2-chai7.  17). Tioh8 khiu2 chiau2 e5 nng7 sit8; chiong i thiah-khui, chong2-si7 m7-thang phoa3 hou7 i tng7; che3-si tioh8 sio i ti7 toaN5-teng2 he2--nih e5 chha5; che si7 sio-che3, hian3-hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.

[2]	Na7 u7 lang5 hian3 sou3-che3 choe3 le2-mih8 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa, tioh8 eng7 iu3 e5 mi7-hun2 choe3 i e5 le2-mih8; tioh8 thin5 iu5 ti7 hi2 teng2-bin7, he7 ju2-hiuN ti7 hi2 teng2-bin7.  2). Toa3 kau3 A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun choe3 che3-si-e5 hia; i chiu7 tui3 iu3 e5 mi7-hun2-tiong chhu2-chhut chit8-pe2 lai5, kap tam7-poh8 iu5, i2-kip8 long2-chong2 e5 ju2-hiuN; che3-si chiu7 sio i ti7 toaN5-teng2 choe3 ki3-liam7; si7 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.  3). Sou3-che3 sou2 chhun-e5, tioh8 kui hou7 A-lun5 kap i e5 kiaN2-sun; che si7 hian3-hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa he2-che3-tiong choe3 chi3-seng3-e5.
	4). Li2 na7 toa3 he2-lou5 sou2 pek e5 le2-mih8 choe3 sou3-che3, tioh8 eng7 iu3 mi7-hun2 e5 bo5-kaN3-piaN2, eng7 iu5 tiau-ho5--e5, a2-si7 bo5-kaN3 e5 poh8-piaN2 eng7 iu5 boah--e5.  5). Na7 eng7 chian-poaN5 chian e5 le2-mih8 choe3 sou3-che3, chiu7 tioh8 eng7 tiau-ho5 iu5 bo5-kaN3 e5 iu3 mi7-hun2.  6). Tioh8 peh kui-te3, thin5 iu5 ti7 teng2-bin7; che si7 sou3-che3.  7). Na7 eng7 tiaN2-a2 chu2 e5 le2-mih8 choe3 sou3-che3; chiu7 tioh8 eng7 iu5 kap iu3 mi7-hun2 choe3.  8). Tioh8 chiong chiah e5 mih8 choe3 sou3-che3, toa3 kau3 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, kau-tai3 che3-si, hou7 i toa3 kau3 toaN5-cheng5.  9). Che3-si tioh8 tui3 sou3-che3-tiong theh8-chhut choe3 ki3-liam7-e5, sio ti7 toaN5-teng2; si7 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.  10). Sou3-che3 sou2 chhun-e5 tioh8 kui hou7 A-lun5 kap i e5 kiaN2-sun; che si7 hian3-hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 he2-che3-tiong choe3 chi3-seng3-e5.  11). KiN3-na7 lin2 hian3-hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 sou3-che3, long2 m7-thang eng7 kaN3 choe3 i, in-ui7 kaN3 kap bit8 lin2 long2 m7-thang sio i, lai5 choe3 he2-che3 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa.  12). Chiah e5 mih8 lin2 tioh8 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa, choe3 khi2-thau5-sek8 e5 le2-mih8; chong2-si7 m7-thang chiuN7 toaN5-teng2 choe3 phang-bi7.  13). KiN3-na7 hian3 choe3 sou3-che3 e5 le2-mih8, long2 tioh8 eng7 iam5 tiau-ho5; ti7 li2 sou3-che3 e5 teng2-bin7 m7-thang khiam3-kheh lin2 e5 Siong7-te3 lip8-iok e5 iam5; li2 it-chhe3 e5 le2-mih8, long2 tioh8 phoe3 iam5 lai5 hian3.
	14). Na7 eng7 khi2-thau5 seng5-sek8 e5 mih8 choe3 sou3-che3, hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 le2-mih8, tioh8 eng7 sin e5 sui7, chiu7-si7 ke3-lun2, ke3 hang e5 ho2 ngou2-kok, hian3 i choe3 lin2 khi2-thau5 seng5-sek8 e5 sou3-che3.  15). Ia7 tioh8 thin5 iu5 ti7 hi2 teng2-bin7, he7 ju2-hiuN ti7 hi2 teng2-bin7; che si7 sou3-che3.  16). Che3-si tioh8 chiong hi2 tiong-kan, choe3 ki3-liam7-e5, chiu7-si7 ke3-lun2 e5 sui7 chit8-hun7, kap iu5 chit8-hun7, i2-kip8 it-chhe3 e5 ju2-hiuN, long2 sio i; che si7 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 he2-che3.

[3]	I e5 le2-mih8 na7 si7 sia7-un e5 che3, hek8-si7 tui3 gu5-kun5-tiong hian3, bo5-lun7 si7 kang--e5, si7 bu2--e5, tioh8 eng7 bo5 ha5-chhu5--e5 hian3 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5.  2). I tioh8 hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 le2-mih8 e5 thau5-khak-teng2, thai5 i ti7 hoe7-bou7 e5 mng5-khau2; A-lun5 kiaN2-sun choe3 che3-si--e5, tioh8 eng7 huih hiu3 ti7 toaN5 e5 chiu-ui5.  3). Tui3 sia7-un-che3 e5 tiong-kan, tioh8 chiong he2-che3 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa; eng7 kham3 chong7-hu2 e5 iu5, kap chong7-hu2 sou2 u7 e5 iu5,  4). i2-kip8 nng7 liap8 io-chi2, kap in sou2 u7 e5 iu5, chiu7-si7 oa2-kun7 io siang-peng5 e5 iu5, kap koaN--nih e5 bang7-se, kap io-chi2, it-khai3 theh8--khi2-lai5.  5). A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun tioh8 sio i ti7 toaN5--nih sio-che3 e5 teng2-bin7, chiu7-si7 ti7 he2--nih e5 chha5; si7 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 phang-phang e5 he2-che3.
	6). I e5 le2-mih8 choe3 sia7-un e5 che3 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5, na7 si7 tui3 iuN5-kun5-tiong, hek8-si7 kang--e5, hek8-si7 bu2--e5, tioh8 eng7 bo5 ha5-chhu5-e5.  7). Na7 hian3 chit8 chiah iuN5-a2-kiaN2 choe3 le2-mih8, tioh8 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 hian3.  8). Ia7 tioh8 hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 i e5 le2-mih8 e5 thau5-khak-teng2, thai5 i ti7 hoe7-bou7-cheng5; A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun tioh8 chiong i e5 huih hiu3 ti7 toaN5 e5 chiu-ui5.  9). Tui3 sia7-un-che3 e5 tiong-kan, tioh8 chiong he2-che3 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa; hit tiong-kan e5 iu5, kap i ka-nng5-ki pui5 e5 iuN5-be2, tui3 oa2-kun7 chit-kut e5 sou2-chai7 theh8--khi2-lai5, i2-kip8 kham3 chong7-hu2 e5 iu5, kap chong7-hu2 sou2 u7 e5 iu5,  10). i2-kip8 nng7 liap8 io-chi2, kap in sou2 u7 e5 iu5, chiu7-si7 oa2-kun7 io siang-peng5 e5 iu5, kap koaN--nih e5 bang7-se, kap io-chi2, it-khai3 theh8--khi2-lai5.  11). Che3-si tioh8 sio i ti7 toaN5-teng2; si7 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 he2-che3 e5 chiah8-mih8.
	12). I e5 le2-mih8 na7 si7 soaN-iuN5, tioh8 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 hian3.  13). Tioh8 hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 i e5 thau5-khak, thai5 i ti7 hoe7-bou7-cheng5; A-lun5 e5 kiaN2-sun tioh8 chiong huih hiu3 ti7 toaN5 e5 chiu-ui5.  14). I tioh8 tui3 che hian3 i e5 le2-mih8 choe3 he2-che3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa.  15). Eng7 kham3 chong7-hu2 e5 iu5, kap chong7-hu2 sou2 u7 e5 iu5, i2-kip8 nng7 liap8 io-chi2, kap in sou2 u7 e5 iu5, chiu7-si7 oa2-kun7 io siang-peng5 e5 iu5, kap koaN-teng2 e5 bang7-se, kap io-chi2, it-khai3 theh8--khi2-lai5.  16). Che3-si tioh8 sio i ti7 toaN5-teng2, choe3 he2-che3 e5 chiah8-mih8, chhu2 i e5 phang-phang-e5; iu5 long2 si7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5.  17). Ti7 lin2 it-chhe3 khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7, iu5 kap huih long2 m7-thang chiah8; che beh choe3 lin2 tak8 se3-tai7 eng2-oan2 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 le7.

[4]	Ia5-ho5-hoa hoan-hu3 Mou5-se kong2,  2). Li2 tioh8 hoan-hu3 I2-sek-liat8 lang5 kong2, Na7 u7 lang5 hoan7-choe7, chho3-gou7 hoan7-tioh8 Ia5-ho5-hoa sou2 beng7-leng7 m7-thang kiaN5 e5 chit8-hang7;  3). na7 siu7 boah-iu5 e5 che3-si hoan7-choe7, lian5-lui7 peh-siN3 ti7 choe7, chiu7 tioh8 in-ui7 i sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7, eng7 bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 gu5-kang2-a2, hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa choe3 siok8-choe7-che3.  4). I tioh8 khan gu5-kang2-a2 kau3 hoe7-bou7 mng5-khau2, ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 gu5-kang2-a2 e5 thau5-khak-teng2, thai5 gu5-kang2-a2 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5.  5). Siu7 boah-iu5 e5 che3-si tioh8 chhu2 tam7-poh8 gu5-kang2 e5 huih, toa3-kau3 hoe7-bou7.  6). Che3-si tioh8 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 un3-huih, ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, tui3 seng3-sou2 e5 tiuN3-li5 cheng5, hiu3 huih chhit-pai2.  7). Che3-si koh tioh8 eng7 tam7-poh8 huih boah ti7 hoe7-bou7-lai7, ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, hiuN-toaN5 hiah e5 kak; i ia7 tioh8 eng7 gu5-kang2-a2 e5 huih long2 piaN3 ti7 hoe7-bou7 mng5-khau2 sio-che3 e5 toaN5-kha.  8). I tioh8 eng7 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 gu5-kang2-a2 sou2 u7 e5 iu5 long2 theh8--khi2-lai5, eng7 kham3 chong7-hu2 e5 iu5, kap chong7-hu2 sou2 u7 e5 iu5,  9). i2-kip8 nng7 liap8 io-chi2, kap in sou2 u7 e5 iu5, chiu7-si7 oa2-kun7 io siang-peng5 e5 iu5, kap koaN--nih e5 bang7-se, kap io-chi2, it-khai3 theh8--khi2-lai5, kap tui3 sia7-un-che3 e5 gu5-kang2-a2 sou2 theh8--khi2-lai5-e5 chit8-iuN7;  10). che3-si tioh8 sio hiah-e5 ti7 sio-che3 e5 toaN5-teng2.  11). Gu5-kang2-a2 e5 phe5 kap sou2 u7 e5 bah, kap i e5 thau5, i e5 thui2, i e5 chong7-hu2, i e5 pun3,  12). chiu7-si7 ka-nng5-chiah gu5-kang2-a2 tioh8 toa3 chhut iaN5-goa7 kau3 chheng-khi3 e5 toe7, piaN3 he2-hu e5 sou2-chai7; eng7 he2 sio ti7 chha5-teng2, ti7 piaN3 he2-hu e5 sou2-chai7 sio i.
	13). Na7 si7 I2-sek-liat8 choan5-hoe7 kiaN5-chho3, kiaN5 Ia5-ho5-hoa sou2 beng7-leng7 m7-thang kiaN5 e5 chit8-hang7 su7, ti3-kau3 u7 choe7, tai7-chi3 iau2-ku2 un2-bat8, hoe7-chiong3 khoaN3 bo5 tioh8 e5.  14). Kau3 sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7, lang5 chit8-e7 chai, hoe7-chiong3 chiu7 tioh8 hian3 chit8 chiah gu5-kang2-a2-kiaN2 choe3 siok8-choe7-che3; khan kau3 hoe7-bou7-cheng5.  15). Hoe7-tiong e5 tiuN2-lo2 chiu7 tioh8 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 hoaN7-chhiu2 ti7 gu5-kang2-a2 e5 thau5-khak-teng2, gu5-kang2-a2 tioh8 thai5 ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5.  16). Siu7-boah-iu5 e5 che3-si tioh8 chhu2 tam7-poh8 gu5-kang2-a2 e5 huih, toa3 kau3 hoe7-bou7;  17). che3-si tioh8 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 un3 huih ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, ti7 tiuN3-li5 e5 thau5-cheng5, hiu3 chhit pai2.  18). Koh tioh8 eng7 tam7-poh8 huih boah ti7 hoe7-bou7-lai7, ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5 e5 toaN5 hiah e5 kak; i ia7 tioh8 eng7 sou2 u7 e5 huih, long2 piaN3 ti7 hoe7-bou7 e5 mng5-khau2 sio-che3 e5 toaN5-kha.  19). Tioh8 chiong sou2 u7 e5 iu5 long2 theh8--khi2-lai5, sio ti7 toaN5-teng2.  20). Tioh8 kiaN5 ti7 chit chiah gu5-kang2-a2, chhin-chhiuN7 sou2 kiaN5 ti7 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 gu5-kang2-a2, tioh8 chiau3 an2-ni kiaN5; che3-si tioh8 an2-ni thoe3 in siok8-choe7, in chiu7 beh tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2.  21). I tioh8 chiong gu5-kang2-a2 toa3-chhut iaN5-goa7 sio--i, chhin-chhiuN7 sio thau5-chit8-chiah gu5-kang2-a2 chit8-iuN7; che si7 hoe7-chiong3 e5 siok8-choe7-che3.
	22). KoaN-tiuN2 na7 u7 hoan7-choe7, chho3-gou7 kiaN5 Ia5-ho5-hoa i e5 Siong7-te3 sou2 beng7-leng7 m7-thang kiaN5 e5 chit8-hang7 su7, ti3-kau3 u7 choe7;  23). sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7 kau3 i ka-ki7 chai, chiu7 tioh8 khan chit8 chiah bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 soaN-iuN5-kang2 choe3 i e5 le2-mih8.  24). Tioh8 hoaN7 i e5 chhiu2 ti7 soaN-iuN5 e5 thau5-khak-teng2, thai5 i ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, ti7 thai5 sio-che3 e5 cheng-siN e5 sou2-chai7; che si7 siok8-choe7-che3.  25). Che3-si tioh8 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 theh8 tam7-poh8 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 huih, boah ti7 sio-che3 e5 toaN5 hiah e5 kak, chiong huih piaN3 ti7 sio-che3 e5 toaN5-kha.  26). Sou2 u7 e5 iu5, i long2 tioh8 sio ti7 toaN5-teng2, chhin-chhiuN7 sia7-un-che3 e5 iu5 chit8-iuN7; lun7-kau3 i e5 choe7, che3-si tioh8 an2-ni ka7 i siok8-choe7, i chiu7 beh tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2.
	27). Peh-siN3-tiong na7 u7 lang5 chho3-gou7 hoan7-choe7, kiaN5 Ia5-ho5-hoa sou2 beng7-leng7 m7-thang kiaN5 e5 chit8-hang7 su7, ti3-kau3 u7-choe7,  28). sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7 kau3 i ka-ki7 chai, i in-ui7 sou2 hoan7 e5 choe7, chiu7 tioh8 khan chit8-chiah bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 soaN-iuN5-bu2, choe3 i e5 le2-mih8.  29). Tioh8 hoaN7 i e5 chhiu2 ti7 siok8-choe7 cheng-siN e5 thau5-khak-teng2, ti7 sio-che3 e5 sou2-chai7 thai5 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 cheng-siN;  30). che3-si tioh8 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 theh8 tam7-poh8 iuN5-huih, boah ti7 sio-che3 e5 toaN5 hiah e5 kak; sou2 u7 e5 huih long2 tioh8 piaN3 ti7 toaN5-kha--nih.  31). Tioh8 chiong i sou2 u7 e5 iu5 long2 theh8--khi2-lai5, chhin-chhiuN7 theh8 sia7-un-che3 e5 iu5 chit8-iuN7, che3-si tioh8 sio i ti7 toaN5-teng2, ti7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 bin7-cheng5, choe3 phang-bi7; che3-si tioh8 ui7 i siok8-choe7, i chiu7 beh tit-tioh8 sia3-bian2.
	32). I na7 khan chit8 chiah mi5-iuN5-a2 choe3 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 le2-mih8, chiu7 tioh8 khan chit8-chiah bo5 ha5-chhu5 e5 iuN5-bu2.  33). Tioh8 hoaN7 i e5 chhiu2 ti7 siok8-choe7 cheng-siN e5 thau5-khak-teng2; ti7 thai5 sio-che3 e5 sou2-chai7, thai5 siok8-choe7-che3 e5 cheng-siN.  34). Che3-si tioh8 eng7 chng2-thau5-a2 theh8 tam7-poh8 siok8-choe7-che3 cheng-siN e5 huih, boah ti7 sio-che3 e5 toaN5 hiah e5 kak, sou2 u7 e5 huih long2 tioh8 piaN3 ti7 toaN5-kha--nih.  35).
