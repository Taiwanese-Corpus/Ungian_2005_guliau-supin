	Iok-Han7 E5 Phoe Te7 Sam Su (64)
	1). Choe3 tiuN2-lo2 e5 phoe kia3 hou7 sou2 thiaN3 e5 Ka-iu5, chiu7-si7 goa2 ti7 chin-li2 sou2 thiaN3 e5.
	2). Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, goa2 goan7 li2 ban7 su7 oe7 heng-thong iong2-kiaN7, chhin-chhiuN7 li2 e5 leng5-hun5 heng-thong chit8 iuN7.  3). In-ui7 u7 hiaN-ti7 lai5 kan-cheng3 li2 sou2 chun5 e5 chin-li2, chhin-chhiuN7 li2 ti7 chin-li2 teh kiaN5, goa2 chiu7 chin hoaN-hi2.  4). Goa2 thiaN-kiN3 goa2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 ti7 chin-li2 teh kiaN5, goa2 e5 hoaN-hi2 bo5 khah toa7 ti7 chit e5.
	5). Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, kiN3-na7 li2 sou2 khoan2-thai7 chhut-goa7 e5 hiaN-ti7 e5, si7 tiong-sin3 e5 sou2 kiaN5.  6). In bat ti7 kau3-hoe7 e5 bin7-cheng5 kan-cheng3 li2 e5 thiaN3-thang3; li2 na7 hap8 ti7 Siong7-te3 ka7 in song3-heng5, chiu7-si7 choe3 ho2 su7.  7). In-ui7 in ui7-tioh8 I e5 mia5 chhut-goa7, bo5 tui3 goa7-pang lang5 theh8 sim2-mih8 mih8.  8). Sou2-i2 hit ho7 lang5, lan2 eng-kai chih-chiap in, hou7 lan2 ui7-tioh8 chin-li2 choe3 tang5 tioh8-boa5 e5.
	9). Goa2 i2-keng u7 sia2 tam7-poh8 hou7 kau3-hoe7; tok8-tok8 in tiong-kan ai3 choe3 thau5 e5 Tiu-tek8-hui bo5 chiap-lap8 goan2.  10). Sou2-i2 goa2 na7 lai5, beh ki3-tit i sou2 kiaN5 e5 su7, chiu7-si7 i eng7 phaiN2 oe7 lam7-sam2 kong2 goan2; koh bo5 liah8 chit-e5 choe3 kau3-giah8, pun2-sin m7 chiap-lap8 hiaN-ti7, u7 lang5 ai3 chiap-lap8, i chiu7 chou2-tong3, soa3 kek in chhut kau3-hoe7.  11). Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, m7 thang oh8 phaiN2, tioh8 oh8 ho2.  KiaN5 ho2 e5 si7 tui3 Siong7-te3 chhut; kiaN5 phaiN2 e5 be7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 Siong7-te3.  12). Ti2-bi2-tiu hou7 cheng3 lang5 sou2 kan-cheng3, chin-li2 ia7 ka-ki7 choe3 i e5 kan-cheng3; goan2 ia7 choe3 kan-cheng3; li2 chai goan2 e5 kan-cheng3 si7 chin.
	13). Goa2 u7 choe7-choe7 hang7 ai3 sia2 hou7 li2, na7-si7 m7 ai3 eng7 pit bak8 sia2 hou7 li2;  14). si7 ng3-bang7 kin2-kin2 kiN3 li2, lan2 chiah beh tng-bin7 kong2.  15). Goan7 li2 peng5-an.  Chiah e5 peng5-iu2 ka7 li2 chheng2-an.  ChhiaN2 li2 sui5 chit8 mia5 ka7 hiah e5 peng5-iu2 chheng2-an.
