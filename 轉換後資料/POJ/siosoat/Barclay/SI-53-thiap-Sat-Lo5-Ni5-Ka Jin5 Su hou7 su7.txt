		Thiap-Sat-Lo5-Ni5-Ka Jin5 Su (53) Hou7 Su
[1]	Po2-lo5 kap Se-lah i2-kip8 The5-mou5-thai3 phoe kia3 hou7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka lang5 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 tiam3-ti7 lan2 e5 Pe7 Siong7-te3 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5:  2). goan7 lin2 tui3 Pe7 Siong7-te3 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok tit-tioh8 un-tian2 peng5-an.
	3). HiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 eng-kai ui7-tioh8 lin2 siong5-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, che pun2 si7 hap8-gi5 e5, in-ui7 lin2 e5 sin3 it-hoat tiong2-toa7, koh lin2 cheng3 lang5 tak8 e5 saN-thiaN3 e5 sim long2 chhiong-chiok;  4). ti3-kau3 goan2 ti7 Siong7-te3 chiah e5 kau3-hoe7 chiong lin2 lai5 khoa-khau2, in-ui7 lin2 ti7 it-chhe3 e5 khun2-tiok8 kap sou2 siu7 e5 hoan7-lan7 tiong, u7 chun5 thun-lun2 kap sin3 e5 sim;  5). che chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 kong-gi7 sim2-phoaN3 beng5 e5 pin5-ku3, hou7 lin2 thang sng3 choe3 oe7 kham-tit ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 kok, chiu7-si7 lin2 ui7-tioh8 i siu7-khou2 e5.  6). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 chiau3 kong-gi7, beh eng7 hoan7-lan7 po3-eng3 hiah e5 hou7 lin2 tu2-tioh8 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5,  7). ia7 beh hou7 lin2 chiah e5 siu7 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5 kap goan2 tang5 tit-tioh8 an-hioh, chiu7-si7 ti7 Chu2 Ia5-sou kap I u7 koan5-leng5 e5 thiN-sai3, ti7 he2-iam7 tiong,  8). tui3 thiN hian2-hian7 e5 si5, lai5 po3-oan ti7 hiah e5 m7 bat Siong7-te3 kap m7 sun7-hok8 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 hok-im e5;  9). chit ho7 lang5 beh siu7 heng5-hoat8, chiu7-si7 eng2-oan2 e5 biat8-bo5, li7-khui Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 kap I koan5-leng5 e5 eng5-kng,  10). tng Chu2 lim5-kau3 e5 jit8, chiu7-si7 I beh ti7 chiah e5 seng3-tou5 tit-tioh8 eng5-kng, ia7 ti7 hit jit8 I beh ti7 long2-chong2 sin3 e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 o-lo2, in-ui7 goan2 tui3 lin2 sou2 kan-cheng3 e5 lin2 u7 sin3.  11). Ui7-tioh8 an2-ni goan2 ia7 siong5-siong5 thoe3 lin2 ki5-to2, goan7 lan2 e5 Siong7-te3 khoaN3 lin2 choe3 oe7 kham-tit ti7 sou2 siu7 e5 tiau3, koh eng7 koan5-leng5 lai5 oan5-moa2 it-chhe3 sou2 him-bou7 e5 ho2, kap tui3 sin3 sou2 choe3 e5 kang,  12). hou7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 mia5 ti7 lin2 tit-tioh8 eng5-kng, lin2 ia7 ti7 I tit-tioh8 eng5-kng, chiau3 lan2 e5 Siong7-te3 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 un.

[2]	HiaN-ti7 ah, lun7-kau3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lim5-kau3, kap lan2 chiah-e5 chu7-chip8 kui I, goan2 kiu5 lin2,  2). bo5 lun7 tui3 sin5, a2-si7 tui3 oe7, a2-si7 tui3 phoe ka2-mou7 goan2 e5 mia5 e5, kong2 Chu2 e5 jit8 hian7-chai7 kau3 lah, lin2 e5 sim-chi3 boh8-tit khoai3-khoai3 io5-tang7, ia7 boh8-tit kiaN-hiaN5;  3). bo5 lun7 eng7 sim2-mih8 hoat, it-khai3 boh8-tit hou7 lang5 in2-iu2; in-ui7 hit jit8 iau2-be7 kau3, tek-khak tai7-seng u7 poe7-kau3 e5 su7, kap hit e5 choe7-ok e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 tim5-lun5 e5 lang5, chhut-hian7;  4). i si7 choe3 tui3-tek8, ia7 chu7-ko poaN5-ke3 it-chhe3 chheng choe3 Siong7-te3 kap siu7 keng3-pai3 e5; sim7-chi3 che7 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 tian7, piau2-beng5 choe3 Siong7-te3.  5). Goa2 iau2 kap lin2 ti7-teh e5 si5, bat chiong chiah e5 su7 ka7 lin2 kong2, lin2 kiam2 boe7 ki3-tit mah?  6). TaN hit e5 chou2-tong3 i, hou7 i kau3 i e5 ki5 chiah chhut-hian7 e5, lin2 ia7 chai.  7). In-ui7 put-hoat e5 pi3-bit8 i2-keng teh un7-tong7, tok8-tok8 hian7-kim u7 chit8 e5 chou2-tong3 i e5, tit8-kau3 hit e5 chou2-tong3 e5 tui3 tiong-ng tu5-khi3.  8). Hit si5 hit e5 put-hoat e5 beh chhut-hian7, Chu2 Ia5-sou beh eng7 I e5 khau2-khi3 lai5 biat8 i, eng7 I lim5-kau3 e5 hian2-hian7 lai5 hoe3-bo5 i.  9). Hit e5 put-hoat e5 lim5-kau3 si7 chiau3 Sat-tan3 e5 un7-heng5, eng7 it-chhe3 e5 koan5-leng5, sin5-jiah, kap ke2 e5 tiau7-thau5,  10). koh eng7 it-chhe3 put-gi7 e5 khui2-khiat kiaN5 ti7 hiah e5 teh tim5-lun5 e5, in-ui7 in bo5 chiap-lap8 chin-li2 e5 thiaN3 lai5 tit kiu3.  11). Ui7-tioh8 an2-ni Siong7-te3 chiong chha-biu7 e5 un7-heng5 kang3 hou7 in, hou7 in sin3 peh8-chhat8;  12). hou7 kiN3-na7 m7 sin3 chin-li2, khi3 hoaN-hi2 put-gi7 e5, long2 siu7 gi2-toan3.
	13). Na7-si7 Chu2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 eng-kai thoe3 lin2 siong5-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, in-ui7 Siong7-te3 tui3 khi2-thau5 keng2 lin2, beh hou7 lin2 tit kiu3, si7 chai7-ti7 Sin5 e5 chiaN5-seng3 kap sin3 chin-li2.  14). Ui7-tioh8 chit-e5, I tui3 goan2 sou2 thoan5 e5 hok-im bat tiau3 lin2, lai5 tit-tioh8 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 eng5-kng.  15). Sou2-i2, hiaN-ti7 ah, tioh8 khia7-chai7; lin2 sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 thoan5-siu7, bo5 lun7 si7 tui3 chhui3 kong2 e5, a2-si7 tui3 goan2 e5 phoe, hoan7 sou2 hak8-sip8 e5, tioh8 chip-siu2 i.
	16). Lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, kap lan2 e5 Pe7 Siong7-te3, chiu7-si7 thiaN3 lan2, ia7 ti7 un-tian2 chiong eng2-oan2 e5 an-ui3 kap ho2 e5 ng3-bang7 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5,  17). goan7 I an-ui3 lin2 e5 sim, kian-kou3 lin2 ti7 it-chhe3 ho2 e5 sou2 kiaN5, ho2 e5 sou2 kong2.

[3]	HiaN-ti7 ah, kiat-be2 lai5 kong2, lin2 tioh8 thoe3 goan2 ki5-to2, hou7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 liu5-heng5 tit-tioh8 eng5-kng, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan chit8 iuN7;  2). koh hou7 goan2 tit-tioh8 thoat-li7 ti7 bo5 li2 e5 phaiN2 lang5; in-ui7 lang5 bo5 long2 u7 sin3.  3). Chu2 chiu7-si7 sin3-sit8, I beh kian-kou3 lin2, koh po2-hou7 lin2 bian2 hou7 hit e5 phaiN2 e5 ham7-hai7.  4). Lun7-kau3 lin2, goan2 ti7 Chu2 chhim sin3, goan2 sou2 beng7-leng7 e5, lin2 hian7-kim u7 teh kiaN5, au7-lai5 ia7 beh kiaN5 i.  5). Goan7 Chu2 chhoa7 lin2 e5 sim jip8 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3 kap Ki-tok e5 thun-lun2.
	6). HiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 ti7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 mia5 beng7-leng7 lin2, kiN3-na7 hiaN-ti7 bo5 hoat-tou7, bo5 chiau3 goan2 sou2 thoan5-siu7 e5 lai5 kiaN5, lin2 tioh8 li7-khui i.  7). In-ui7 lin2 ka-ki7 chai tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 oh8 goan2; in-ui7 goan2 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan be7 bat bo5 hoat-tou7;  8). ia7 m7 bat peh8-peh8 chiah8 lang5; hoan2-tng2 si7 tioh8-boa5 kan-khou2, mi5-jit8 choe3 kang, bian2-tit thoa-lui7 lin2 tiong-kan chit8 lang5.  9). M7 si7 in-ui7 goan2 bo5 koan5, tok8-tok8 si7 ai3 eng7 pun2-sin choe3 lin2 e5 bou5-iuN7, hou7 lin2 oh8 goan2.  10). In-ui7 goan2 iau2 kap lin2 ti7-teh e5 si5, bat an2-ni beng7-leng7 lin2 kong2, Na7 u7 lang5 bo5 beh choe3 kang, i ia7 tioh8 m7 thang chiah8.  11). Si7 goan2 thiaN-kiN3 lin2 tiong-kan u7 lang5 sou2 kiaN5 bo5 hoat-tou7, long2 bo5 choe3 kang, kan-ta choe3 hun7-goa7 e5 su7.  12). Chhin-chhiuN7 chit ho7 lang5 goan2 tiam3-ti7 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok beng7-leng7 in, koh khng3 in, tioh8 thiam5-cheng7 choe3 kang, ka-ki7 than3 chiah8.  13). HiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 kiaN5 ho2 su7, m7 thang ia3-sian7.  14). Na7 u7 lang5 bo5 sun7-hok8 goan2 chit tiuN phoe sou2 kong2 e5, lin2 tioh8 ka7 i ki3-teh, ia7 m7 thang kap i kau-poe5, hou7 i ka-ki7 kian3-siau3.  15). Chong2-si7 boh8-tit khoaN3 i choe3 kiu5-siu5, tioh8 keng2-kai3 i chhin-chhiuN7 hiaN-ti7.
	16). Goan7 peng5-an e5 Chu2, tak8 si5 tak8 hang7 su7, pun2-sin siuN2-su3 lin2 peng5-an. Goan7 Chu2 kap lin2 cheng3 lang5 ti7-teh.
	17). Goa2 Po2-lo5 chhin-pit chheng2-an lin2; ti7 tak8 tiuN phoe eng7 chit-e5 choe3 ki3-ho7; goa2 chiu7-si7 an2-ni sia2.  18). Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 un-tian2 kap lin2 cheng3 lang5 ti7-teh.
