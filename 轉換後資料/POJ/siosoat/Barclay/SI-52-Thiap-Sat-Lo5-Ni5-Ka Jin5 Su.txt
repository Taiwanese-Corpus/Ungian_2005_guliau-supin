		Thiap-Sat-Lo5-Ni5-Ka Jin5 Su	(52)
				Chian5 Su
[1]	Po2-lo5 kap Se-lah i2-kip8 The5-mou5-thai3 phoe kia3 hou7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka lang5 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 tiam3-ti7 Pe7 Siong7-te3 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5; goan7 lin2 tit-tioh8 un-tian2 peng5-an.
	2). Goan2 ui7-tioh8 lin2 cheng3 lang5 siong5-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, ti7 ki5-to2 e5 tiong-kan siau3-liam7 lin2;  3). ti7 Siong7-te3 lan2 e5 Pe7 e5 bin7-cheng5, tng5-tng5 siau3-liam7 lin2 u7 sin3 chiu7 kiaN5-choe3, u7 jin5-ai3 chiu7 tioh8-boa5, u7 ng3-bang7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok chiu7 thun-lun2.  4). Siong7-te3 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 chai lin2 e5 siu7 keng2-soan2.  5). In-ui7 goan2 e5 hok-im kau3 ti7 lin2, m7 si7 chai7-ti7 kong2 oe7 na7-tiaN7, si7 chai7-ti7 koan5-leng5, chai7-ti7 Seng3 Sin5, kap kian-kou3 e5 sin3; chhin-chhiuN7 lin2 chai goan2 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan, ui7-tioh8 lin2 lai5 chiaN5-choe3 sim2-mih8 teng2-ho7 e5 lang5.  6). Koh lin2 i2-keng ti7 choe7-choe7 khou2-lan7, eng7 Seng3 Sin5 sou2 siuN2-su3 e5 hoaN-hi2, lai5 seng5-siu7 to7-li2, chiu7 oh8 goan2 ia7 oh8 Chu2,  7). ti3-kau3 lin2 choe3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 A-kai-a long2-chong2 sin3 e5 lang5 e5 bou5-iuN7.  8). In-ui7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 tui3 lin2 thoan5-chhut, m7 na7 ti7 Ma2-ki5-tun3 kap A-kai-a na7-tiaN7, chiu7-si7 lin2 ng3 Siong7-te3 e5 sin3 i2-keng tak8 sou2-chai7 po3-iong5, ti3-kau3 goan2 m7 bian2 kong2 sim2-mih8.  9). In-ui7 in ka-ki7 po3-beng5 kong2 goan2 kau3 lin2 tiong-kan si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2, ia7 lin2 chaiN2-iuN7 u7 li7-khui ngou2-siong7, kui ti7 Siong7-te3, lai5 hok8-sai7 oah8 koh chin e5 Siong7-te3;  10). ia7 theng3-hau7 I e5 KiaN2 tui3 thiN loh8-lai5, chiu7-si7 I tui3 si2-lang5 tiong hou7 I koh-oah8 e5 Ia5-sou, si7 hit e5 chin2-kiu3 lan2 chhut chiong-lai5 e5 siu7-khi3 e5.

[2]	In-ui7, hiaN-ti7, lin2 ka-ki7 chai goan2 cheng5 kau3 lin2 tiong-kan m7 si7 khang-khang;  2). tok8-tok8 ti7 Hui-lip8-pi2 i2-keng tai7-seng siu7 khou2 tu2-tioh8 leng5-jiok8, chiau3 lin2 sou2 chai e5, goan2 ti7 choe7-choe7 cheng-chian3 e5 tiong-kan, oa2-kho3 lan2 e5 Siong7-te3, tit-tioh8 ho2-taN2 tui3 lin2 kong2 Siong7-te3 e5 hok-im.  3). In-ui7 goan2 e5 khoan3-bian2 m7 si7 chhut ti7 chha-biu7, m7 si7 chhut ti7 bo5 chheng-khi3, ia7 m7 si7 eng7 kan-cha3,  4). chiu7-si7 goan2 hou7 Siong7-te3 giam7-ke3, chiah eng7 hok-im thok-tiong7 goan2, goan2 chiu7 chiau3 an2-ni lai5 kong2, m7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hou7 lang5 hoaN-hi2 e5, chiu7-si7 hou7 giam7 goan2 e5 sim e5 Siong7-te3 hoaN-hi2.  5). In-ui7 goan2 long2 be7 bat eng7 thiam2-mi7 e5 oe7, chiau3 lin2 sou2 chai e5, ia7 bo5 eng7 chng-thaN7 lai5 jia-kham3 tham-sim, Siong7-te3 choe3 kan-cheng3.  6). Goan2 choe3 Ki-tok e5 su3-tou5, sui-jian5 thang kah lang5 keng3-tiong7 goan2, chong2-si7 m7 bat tui3 lang5 kiu5 eng5-kng, ia7 bo5 tui3 lin2, ia7 bo5 tui3 pat8 lang5.  7). Goan2 chiu7-si7 un-jiu5 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan, chhin-chhiuN7 lin-bu2 io ka-ki7 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  8). Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni goan2 in-ui7 loan5-bou7 lin2, chiu7 m7 na7 Siong7-te3 e5 hok-im, lian5 ka-ki7 e5 siN3-mia7 ia7 hoaN-hi2 hou7 lin2; in-ui7 lin2 si7 goan2 sou2 thiaN3-thang3 e5.  9). HiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 ki3-tit goan2 e5 tioh8-boa5 kap kan-khou2, ai3 bian2-tit thoa-lui7 lin2 chit8 lang5, chiu7 mi5-jit8 choe3 kang, lai5 thoan5 Siong7-te3 e5 hok-im hou7 lin2.  10). Lin2 choe3 kan-cheng3, Siong7-te3 ia7 choe3 kan-cheng3, goan2 tui3 lin2 sin3 e5 lang5, choe3-lang5 si7 chaiN2-iuN7 seng3-kiat, kong-gi7, bo5 thang hiam5;  11). chhin-chhiuN7 lin2 chai goan2 chaiN2-iuN7 khoan2-thai7 lin2 tak8 lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 lau7-pe7 khoan2-thai7 ka-ki7 e5 kiaN2-ji5, u7 khoan3-bian2 lin2, ia7 an-ui3, ia7 kan-cheng3;  12). si7 beh hou7 lin2 kiaN5 tai7-chi3 hap8 ti7 Siong7-te3, chiu7-si7 tiau3 lin2 jip8 I e5 kok kap eng5-kng e5.
	13). Sou2-i2 goan2 ia7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 bo5 soah, in-ui7 lin2 tui3 goan2 seng5-siu7 sou2 thiaN Siong7-te3 e5 to7-li2 e5 si5, lin2 chiap-lap8 i, bo5 liah8-choe3 lang5 e5 to7-li2, chiu7-si7 liah8-choe3 Siong7-te3 e5 to7-li2, (sit8-chai7 chin-chiaN3 si7), che chiu7-si7 chhut-lat8 kiaN5 ti7 lin2 sin3 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan.  14). In-ui7, hiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 u7 oh8 Iu5-thai3 toe7, tiam3-ti7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou, Siong7-te3 chiah e5 kau3-hoe7, in-ui7 lin2 siu7-tioh8 lin2 tang5 chok8 e5 ham7-hai7, chhin-chhiuN7 in siu7-tioh8 Iu5-thai3 lang5 ham7-hai7 chit8 iuN7.  15). Iu5-thai3 lang5 hai7-si2 Chu2 Ia5-sou kap chiah e5 sian-ti, ia7 kek-tiok8 goan2; in bo5 hou7 Siong7-te3 hoaN-hi2, koh kap cheng3 lang5 choe3 tui3-tek8;  16). goan2 ka3-si7 goa7-pang lang5, beh hou7 in tit-tioh8 kiu3, in chou2-tong3 goan2, ti3-kau3 in e5 choe7-ok si5-si5 teh koan3-moa2; na7-si7 siu7-khi3 kau3 ti7 in i2-keng kek8-thau5.
	17). HiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 chiam7-si5 kap lin2 li7-piat8, seng-khu li7-piat8 sim bo5 li7-piat8, chiu7 un-khun5 na2 chhiat chin ai3 kiN3-tioh8 lin2 e5 bin7.  18). In-ui7 goan2 ai3 chiu7-kun7 lin2, goa2 Po2-lo5 chit8 pai2 koh chit8 pai2 ai3 khi3, na7-si7 Sat-tan3 chou2-tong3 goan2.  19). In-ui7 goan2 e5 ng3-bang7 kap hoaN-hi2, i2-kip8 sou2 khoa-khau2 e5 bian2-liu5 si7 sim2-mih8?  Kiam2 m7 si7 lin2 tng lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou koh-lai5 e5 si5, ti7 I e5 bin7-cheng5 mah?  20). In- ui7 lin2 si7 goan2 e5 eng5-kng kap hoaN-hi2.

[3]	Sou2-i2 goan2 i2-keng lun2 boe7 tiau5, chiu7 tiaN7-tioh8 beh toaN-sin toa3 ti7 Nga2-tian2,  2). chhe goan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 The5-mou5-thai3, i ti7 Ki-tok e5 hok-im choe3 Siong7-te3 e5 chhe-eng7, lai5 kian-kou3 lin2, koh ui7-tioh8 lin2 e5 sin3 lai5 an-ui3 lin2,  3). bian2-tit u7 lang5 hou7 hiah e5 hoan7-lan7 sou2 io5-tang7, in-ui7 lin2 ka-ki7 chai lan2 tu2-tioh8 che si7 pun2 tiaN7-tioh8 teh.  4). In-ui7 goan2 kap lin2 ti7-teh e5 si5 bat tai7-seng ka7 lin2 kong2, lan2 oe7 tu2-tioh8 hoan7-lan7, au7-lai5 ko2-jian5 an2-ni, si7 lin2 sou2 chai.  5). Sou2-i2 goa2 ki3-jian5 boe7 koh lun2-tit, chiu7 chhe lang5 ai3 chai lin2 e5 sin3 chaiN2-iuN7, kiaN-liau2 hit e5 chhi3-e5 u7 sou2 chhi3 lin2, chiu7 goan2 e5 tioh8-boa5 kui ti7 khang-khang.  6). Chong2-si7 taN The5-mou5-thai3 tui3 lin2 hia lai5 kau3 goan2 chia, chiong lin2 e5 sin3 kap thiaN3 e5 ho2 siau-sit po3 goan2 chai, koh kong2-khi2 lin2 siong5-siong5 siau3-liam7 goan2, him-bou7 beh kiN3-tioh8 goan2, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 ai3 kiN3-tioh8 lin2 chit8 iuN7;  7). sou2-i2, hiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 ti7 it-chhe3 khun3-pek hoan7-lan7 e5 tiong-kan, tui3 lin2 e5 sin3, ui7-tioh8 lin2 siu7 an-ui3.  8). In-ui7 lin2 na7 khia7-chai7 ti7 Chu2, goan2 chiu7 oah8.  9). Goan2 ti7 lan2 e5 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7-cheng5 ui7-tioh8 lin2 chin hoaN-hi2, goan2 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 it-chhe3 e5 hoaN-hi2, oe7 eng7 sim2-mih8 e5 kam2-sia7 ui7-tioh8 lin2 po3-tap Siong7-te3 ah?  10). goan2 mi5-jit8 chhiat-chhiat ki5-to2, ai3 kiN3-tioh8 lin2 e5 bin7, lai5 pou2-moa2 lin2 e5 sin3 sou2 bo5 kau3.
	11). Goan7 lan2 e5 Pe7 Siong7-te3, kap lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou in2-chhoa7 goan2 piN5-thaN2 e5 lou7 chiu7-kun7 lin2.  12). Ia7 goan7 Chu2 hou7 lin2 saN-thiaN3, soa3 thiaN3 cheng3 lang5 it-hoat chhiong-moa2, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 e5 thiaN3 lin2,  13). ti3-kau3 kian-kou3 lin2 e5 sim chiaN5-seng3 bo5 thang hiam5, ti7 lan2 e5 Pe7 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7-cheng5, ti7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou kap I long2-chong2 e5 seng3-tou5 koh lai5 e5 si5.

[4]	HiaN-ti7 ah, kiat-be2 lai5 kong2, goan2 ti7 Chu2 Ia5-sou kiu5 lin2 khng3 lin2, chiau3 lin3 cheng5 sou2 bat tui3 goan2 siu7, tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 kiaN5 lai5 hou7 Siong7-te3 hoaN-hi2 e5, lin2 i2-keng teh kiaN5, taN tioh8 koh khah chhut-lat8 kiaN5.  2). In-ui7 lin2 chai goan2 tui3 Chu2 Ia5-sou u7 sim2-mih8 beng7-leng7 hou7 lin2.  3). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3, chiu7-si7 lin2 e5 chiaN5-seng3, si7 ai3 lin2 li7-khui im5-loan7.  4). Ai3 lin2 tak8 lang5 oe7 hiau2-tit eng7 seng3-kiat kap chun-kui3 siu2 ka-ki7 e5 seng-khu,  5). bo5 eng7 su-iok8 e5 cheng5, chhin-chhiuN7 m7 bat Siong7-te3 e5 goa7-pang lang5;  6). ti7 chit chan5 su7 ia7 bo5 ke3-kai3, chhim-hoan7 i e5 hiaN-ti7; in-ui7 kiN3-na7 chit ho7 su7 Chu2 si7 sin-oan e5, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 bat ka7 lin2 kong2 lai5 kan-cheng3 e5.  7). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 tiau3 lan2, m7 si7 ti3-kau3 u3-oe3, chiu7-si7 ti7 chiaN5-seng3.  8). Sou2-i2 ku7-choat8 e5 m7 si7 ku7-choat8 lang5, si7 ku7-choat8 Siong7-te3, chiu7-si7 eng7 I e5 Seng3 Sin5 hou7 lin2 e5.
	9). Lun7 thiaN3 hiaN-ti7 m7 bian2 sia2 phoe hou7 lin2, in-ui7 lin2 ka-ki7 siu7-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 ka3-si7, hou7 lin2 tai-ke saN-thiaN3,  10). si7 lin2 ko2-jian5 u7 kiaN5 i ti7 thong Ma2-ki5-tun3 e5 cheng3 hiaN-ti7.  Chong2-si7, hiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 khng3 lin2 tioh8 it-hoat an2-ni;  11). ia7 tioh8 tui3-tiong7 thiam5-cheng7, lai5 choe3 ka-ki7 e5 su7, chhin-chhiu2 choe3 lin2 e5 kang, chiau3 goan2 sou2 bat hoan-hu3 lin2 e5,  12). hou7 lin2 ng3 goa7-bin7 lang5 toan-chiaN3 lai5 kiaN5, ia7 ka-ki7 bo5 sou2 khiam3-kheh.
	13). HiaN-ti7 ah, lun7-kau3 teh khun3 e5 lang5 goan2 m7 ai3 lin2 m7 chai, bian2-tit lin2 iu-bun7 chhin-chhiuN7 hiah e5 bo5 ng3-bang7 e5 lang5 chit8 iuN7.  14). In-ui7 lan2 na7 sin3 Ia5-sou u7 si2 u7 koh-oah8, chiu7 siok8 Ia5-sou lai5 khun3 e5 lang5, Siong7-te3 ia7 beh an2-ni chhoa7 in kap Ia5-sou saN-kap lai5.  15). In-ui7 goan2 hong7 Chu2 e5 beng7-leng7, eng7 chit-e5 ka7 lin2 kong2, chiu7-si7 Chu2 koh lai5 e5 si5, lan2 chiah-e5 iau2 oah8-teh e5 lang5, boe7-oe7 tai7-seng hiah e5 teh khun3 e5 lang5.  16). In-ui7 Chu2 beh pun2-sin tui3 thiN loh8-lai5, u7 toa7 au5-kio3, u7 chi3 toa7 thiN-sai3 e5 siaN, ia7 u7 Siong7-te3 e5 sau3-kak, chiu7 hiah e5 siok8 Ki-tok i2-keng si2 e5 lang5 beh tai7-seng khi2-lai5.  17). Au7-lai5 lan2 iau2 oah8-teh e5 lang5 beh kap in siu7 chiap-lap8 ti7 hun5-lai7, jip8 khong-tiong ngia5-chih Chu2; an2-ni lan2 chiu7 eng2-oan2 kap Chu2 saN-kap ti7-teh.  18). Sou2-i2 tioh8 eng7 chiah e5 oe7 saN an-ui3.

[5]	HiaN-ti7 ah, lun7-kau3 si5-ki5 m7 bian2 sia2 phoe hou7 lin2.  2). In-ui7 lin2 ka-ki7 siong5-se3 chai, Chu2 e5 jit8 kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 chhat8 thau3 mi5 lai5.  3). Lang5 tng teh kong2, Peng5-an un2-tang3, e5 si5, chai-e7 hut-jian5 kau3 ti7 in, chhin-chhiuN7 siN kiaN2 e5 kan-khou2 kau3 ti7 u7 sin-in7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chit8 iuN7, in hiah e5 lang5 boe7-oe7 chau2-li7.  4). Na7-si7 hiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 m7 si7 ti7 am3 e5 tiong-kan, ti3-kau3 hit jit8 chhin-chhiuN7 chhat8 lai5 jip-tioh8 lin2.  5). In-ui7 lin2 long2 si7 kng-beng5 e5 lang5, jit8-si5 e5 lang5; lan2 m7 si7 siok8 ti7 mi5-si5 e5, ia7 m7 si7 ou-am3 e5.  6). Sou2-i2 lan2 m7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 ki5-u5 e5 lang5 teh khun3; tioh8 keng2-seng2 chiat-che3.  7). In-ui7 khun3 e5 lang5 si7 mi5-si5 khun3, chiu2-chui3 e5 lang5 si7 mi5-si5 chui3.  8). Na7-si7 lan2 siok8 jit8-si5 e5 tioh8 chiat-che3, chheng7 sin3 kap jin5-ai3 choe3 hou7-sim-kiaN3, eng7 tit kiu3 e5 ng3-bang7 choe3 thau5-khoe.  9). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 tiaN7-tioh8 lan2 m7 si7 lai5 tu2-tioh8 siu7-khi3, chiu7-si7 beh tui3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  10). I thoe3 lan2 si2, hou7 lan2, hek8-si7 chhiN2 hek8-si7 khun3, kap I tang5 oah8.  11). Sou2-i2 tioh8 saN an-ui3, tai7-ke saN kian3-lip8 tek-heng7, chiau3 lin2 sou2 teh choe3 e5.
	12). HiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 kiu5 lin2, hoan7-na7 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan tioh8-boa5, tiam3-ti7 Chu2 ti7-li2 lin2, keng2-kai3 lin2 e5, lin2 tioh8 bat i,  13). ui7-tioh8 in sou2 choe3 e5 kang, lin2 tioh8 ti7 jin5-ai3 toa7-toa7 chun-keng3 in.  Lin2 tai7-ke tioh8 ho5-bok8.  14). HiaN-ti7 ah, goan2 khng3 lin2 tioh8 keng2-kai3 hiah e5 bo5 hoat-tou7 e5, an-ui3 hiah e5 loe2-chi3 e5, hu5-chhi5 hiah e5 loan2-jiok8 e5, eng7 khoan-iong5 khoan2-thai7 cheng3 lang5.  15). Kin2-sin7 boh8-tit eng7 phaiN2 po3 phaiN2; hek8-si7 tai7-ke saN khoan2-thai7, a2-si7 khoan2-thai7 cheng3 lang5, tioh8 siong5-siong5 tui-jip hit e5 ho2.  16). Tioh8 siong5-siong5 hoaN-hi2;  17). tioh8 ki5-to2 bo5 soah;  18). hoan7 su7 kam2-sia7, in-ui7 chit-e5 si7 Siong7-te3 tiam3-ti7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou ng3 lin2 e5 chi2-i3.  19). M7 thang phah-hoa Seng3 Sin5;  20). m7 thang khoaN3-khin sian-ti e5 oe7,  21). hoan7 su7 tioh8 chhat-giam7;  22). ho2-e5 tioh8 chip-siu2; tioh8 li7-khui tak8 iuN7 e5 phaiN2.
	23). Goan7 peng5-an e5 Siong7-te3 ka-ki7 hou7 lin2 it-chin7 chiaN5-seng3; koh goan7 lin2 e5 sin5 kap hun5 i2-kip8 seng-khu ti7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok koh-lai5 e5 si5, long2 tit-tioh8 po2-choan5, bo5 thang hiam5.  24). Tiau3 lin2 e5 si7 sin3-sit8, I ia7 beh chiaN5 i.
	25). HiaN-ti7 ah, tioh8 thoe3 goan2 ki5-to2.
	26). Tioh8 eng7 seng3 e5 saN-chim lai5 chheng2-an long2-chong2 e5 hiaN-ti7.
	27). Goa2 ki2 Chu2 lai5 hoan-hu3 lin2, eng7 chit tiuN phoe thak8 hou7 cheng3 hiaN-ti7 thiaN.
	28). Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 un-tian2 kap lin2 ti7-teh.
