	HAP-PA-KOK	(35)
[1]	Sian-ti Hap-pa-kok sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 bek8-si7.
	2). Ia5-ho5-hoa ah, goa2 au5-kio3, Li2 bo5 thiaN, beh kau3 ti7-si5 ah? goa2 in-ui7 kiong5-po7 kio3-khou2, Li2 iau2 bo5 chin2-kiu3.  3). Li2 siaN2-su7 hou7 goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 choe7-ok? heng5-gek8 e5 su7, Li2 siaN2-su7 tiaN7-tiaN7 khoaN3? hui2-biat8 kap kiong5-po7 ti7 goa2 bin7-cheng5, saN-chiN saN-phah long2 hoat-khi2.  4). In-ui7 an2-ni lut8-hoat pang3-sang, bo5 kiaN5 kong-peng5; phaiN2-lang5 ui5-khun3 gi7-lang5; sou2-i2 sim2-phoaN3 bo5 cheng3-tit8.
	5). Lin2 tioh8 ti7 liat8-kok-tiong khoaN3 lai5 siuN7 i; toa7-toa7 gong5-ngiah8; in-ui7 tng lin2 e5 jit8, Goa2 beh kiaN5 chit8-hang7 su7, sui-jian5 ka7 lin2 kong2, lin2 ia7 m7-sin3.  6). KhoaN3 ah, Goa2 beh heng-khi2 Ka-lek8-ti2 lang5, chiu7-si7 chan5-jim2 so3-po7 e5 pang-kok, pian3-kiaN5 choan5-toe7, pa3-chiam3 hiah-e5 m7-si7 siok8 i khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7;  7). i e5 ui-hong thang kiaN, phoaN3-toan3 kap koan5-se3, long2 chhut ti7 ka-ki7 e5 i3-su3.  8). In e5 be2 pi2 pa3 khah-kin2, pi2 e5-hng-si5 e5 chhai5-long5 khah-beng2; be2-peng pai5-liat8 khui-khoah, be2-peng tui3 hng7-hng7 lai5; in chhin-chhiuN7 eng kin2-kin2 pe beh lai5 chiah8.  9). In lai5 beh kiaN5 kiong5-po7; in e5 bin7 hiong5-hiong5 chin3-cheng5; chu7-chip8 sou2 liah8 e5 lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 soa hiah-choe7.  10). I biau2-si7 kun-ong5, ki-chhi3 kui3-chok8; leng2-chhio3 long2-chong2 e5 oe7-sou2; khi2 thou5-tui lai5 kong-chhu2--i.  11). Tui3 an2-ni i chhin-chhiuN7 hong sau3 ke3-khi3, i keng-ke3 chiu7 hoan7-choe7; liah8 ka-ki7 e5 khui3-lat8 choe3 siong7-te3.  12). Ia5-ho5-hoa goa2 e5 Siong7-te3 goa2 e5 Seng3--e5 ah, Li2 kiam2 m7-si7 tui3 eng2-oan2 chiu7 u7 mah? goan2 boe7 ti3-kau3 si2, Ia5-ho5-hoa ah, Li2 ui7-tioh8 sim2-phoaN3 lai5 siat-lip8 i, Chioh8-poaN5 ah, Li2 ui7-tioh8 khian3-chek lai5 pi7-pan7 i.  13). Li2 e5 bak8-chiu chheng-khi3 bo5 beh khoaN3 sia5-ok, bo5 beh khoaN3 heng5-gek8, kiaN5 khui2-cha3--e5, Li2 siaN2-su7 tiaN7-tiaN7 khoaN3 in ah? phaiN2-lang5 thun-biat8 pi2 i khah kong-gi7--e5, Li2 siaN2-su7 cheng7-cheng7 ah?  14). Li2 siaN2-su7 hou7 lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 hai2--nih e5 hi5, koh chhin-chhiuN7 teh so5 e5 thang5, bo5 thau5-bak8 koan2-li2 ah?  15). I eng7 kau it-chin7 tio3--in, i eng7 bang7-a2 liah8 in, eng7 toa7-bang7 pha in; ui7-tioh8 an2-ni, i hoaN-hi2 khoai3-lok8.  16). Chiu7 hian3-che3 hou7 i e5 bang7-a2, sio-hiuN hou7 i e5 toa7-bang7; in-ui7 in tui3 chiah-e5 tit-tioh8 pui5-sui2 e5 hun7-giah8, kap chhiong-chiok e5 chiah8-mih8.  17). I kiam2 thang piaN3-khang i e5 bang7, siong5-siong5 thai5-si2 liat8-kok e5 lang5, ia7 bo5 po2-sioh mah?

[2]	Goa2 beh khia7 chiu2-kiN-lau5, khia7 ti7 iaN5-che7 koan-khoaN3, khoaN3 I tui3 goa2 kong2 sim2-mih8 oe7, goa2 thang eng7 sim2-mih8 oe7 lai5 sou3-beng5.  2). Ia5-ho5-hoa in3 goa2 kong2, Sia2 chit e5 bek8-si7, beng5-beng5 khek ti7 pan2--nih8, hou7 thak8 e5 lang5 ia7 thang chau2.  3). In-ui7 chit e5 bek8-si7 iau2-u7 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 ki5, beh kin2-kin2 kiat-kiok8, koat-toan3 bo5 peh8-chhat8; sui-jian5 ian5-chhian5, iau2-ku2 tioh8 theng3-hau7; in-ui7 tek-khak oe7 kau3, bo5 koh ian5-chhian5.  4). KhoaN3 ah, i e5 sim-sin5 chu7-ko, ti7 i e5 lai7-bin7 bo5 cheng3-tit8; tok8-tok8 gi7-lang5 beh tui3 i e5 sin3 lai5 oah8.  5). Chiu7-si7 khi-phian3--e5, kiau-ngou7 e5 lang5 bo5 tiam3 ti7 ke-lai7; khui-khoah i e5 sim sou2-ai3--e5, chhin-chhiuN7 im-hu2, iah8 chhin-chhiuN7 si2 boe7-oe7 moa2-chiok, thong2-it ban7-kok kui i, chu7-chip8 ban7 peh-siN3 kui i.  6). Chiah-e5 cheng3-lang5 kiam2 bo5 beh eng7 siok8-gu2 lai5 phoe-pheng5 i, eng7 phi3-ju7 lai5 ki-chhi3 i, kong2, Chek-chu7 choe7-choe7 bo5 siok8 ka-ki7 e5 chai5-but8, beh joa7-ku2? theh8 choe7-choe7 chun2-tng3 e5 mih8, ka-ki7 tam-hu7, hit-ho7 lang5 u7 chai-e7 ah.  7). Ka7 li2--e5, kiam2 bo5 hut-jian5 khi2-lai5, kiau2-jiau2 li2--e5, kiam2 bo5 chhiN2--khi2-lai5, li2 chiu7 beh choe3 in sou2 chhiuN2 e5 mih8?  8). In-ui7 li2 chhiuN2-kiap choe7-choe7 kok, liat8-kok-tiong sou2 chhun e5 lang5 ia7 beh chhiuN2-kiap li2; si7 in-ui7 li2 thai5-lang5 lau5-huih, tui3 keng2-lai7 kap siaN5-chhi7, kap ki5-tiong khia7-khi2 e5 lang5, sou2 kiaN5 kiong5-po7 e5 ian5-kou3.
	9). Ui7 ka-ki7 e5 ke chek-chu7 put-gi7 e5 chiN5, beh choh-siu7 ti7 koaiN5 e5 sou2-chai7, ng3-bang7 thoat-li7 hiong-ok e5 se3-lek8, hit-ho7 lang5 u7 chai-e7 ah.  10). Li2 biat8 choe7-choe7 peh-siN3, sou2 ke3-bou5--e5, leng5-jiok8 ka-ki7 e5 ke, koh hoan7-choe7 hai7 ka-ki7 e5 siN3-mia7.  11). Piah-tiong e5 chioh8 beh au5-kio3, chha5-liau7-tiong e5 thong-niu5 beh in3 siaN.
	12). Eng7 thai5-lang5 lau5-huih lai5 khi2 siaN5, eng7 choe7-giat8 siat-lip8 siaN5-chhi7--e5, u7 chai-e7 ah.  13). KhoaN3 ah, cheng3 peh-siN3 sou2 lo5-lok8--e5 hou7 he2 sio--khi3, liat8-kok tioh8-boa5 kau3 ia3-sian7--e5 kui ti7 khang-khang, kiam2 m7-si7 long2 chhut ti7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa mah?  14). In-ui7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 eng5-kng beh hou7 choan5-toe7 long2 bat, chhin-chhiuN7 chui2 chhiong-moa2 iuN5-hai2 chit8-iuN7.
	15). Hou7 i e5 chhu3-piN lim, koh chham beng2-liat8 e5 chiu2 hou7 i lim kau3 chui3, thang khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 e7-the2, hit-ho7 lang5 u7 chai-e7 ah.  16). Li2 sou2 chhiong-moa2--e5, si7 leng5-jiok8, m7-si7 eng5-kng; li2 iah8 tioh8 lim, hian2-chhut si7 be7 siu7 kat-le2--e5; Ia5-ho5-hoa chiaN3-chhiu2 e5 poe beh oat8-tng2 lai5 hou7 li2 lim, li2 e5 leng5-jiok8 beh kham3-bat8 li2 e5 eng5-kng.  17). Koh li2 tui3 Li7-pa-lun3 sou2 kiaN5 e5 kiong5-po7, i2-kip8 chan5-hai7 ia2-siu3 hou7 i kiaN-hiaN5 e5 su7, long2 beh jia-kham3 li2; si7 in-ui7 li2 thai5-lang5, tui3 keng2-lai7 siaN5-chhi7, kap ki5-tiong khia7-khi2 e5 lang5, sou2 kiaN5 kiong5-po7 e5 ian5-kou3.
	18). Tiau-khek e5 siong7 u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek, chiah hou7 cho7 i e5 lang5 tiau-khek i ah? chu3 e5 ngou2-siong7 kap ke2 e5 sian-siN, u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek, chiah hou7 cho7 i e5 lang5 oa2-kho3 i, lai5 cho7 e2-kau2 e5 ngou2-siong7 ah?  19). Tui3 chha5-thau5 kong2, Tioh8 chhiN2; tui3 e2-kau2 e5 chioh8-thau5 kong2, Khi2-lai5, hit-ho7 lang5 u7 chai-e7 ah.  Chit-e5 kiam2 oe7 ka3-si7 lang5 mah? KhoaN3 ah, si7 eng7 kim eng7 gun5 pau--e5, i e5 lai7-bin7 long2 bo5 oah8-khi3.  20). Tok8-tok8 Ia5-ho5-hoa ti7 I e5 seng3-tian7, choan5-toe7 ti7 I e5 bin7-cheng5 long2 tioh8 cheng7-cheng7.

[3]	 Sian-ti Hap-pa-kok e5 ki5-to2, eng7 "Si-ki5-o-lok8" tiau7.
	2). Ia5-ho5-hoa ah, goa2 thiaN-kiN3 Li2 e5 siau-sit chiu7 kiaN-hiaN5.
	Ia5-ho5-hoa ah, kiu5 Li2 ti7 chiah e5 ni5 e5 tiong-kan, koh heng-khi2 Li2 e5 sou2-choe3;
	Ti7 chiah e5 ni5 e5 tiong-kan hian2-hian7;
	Ti7 siu7-khi3 e5 si5 ki3-tit lin5-bin2.
	3). Siong7-te3 tui3 The5-ban7 lai5,
	Seng3--e5 tui3 Pa-lan5 soaN kau3.		[Se3-lah8]
	I e5 eng5-kng jia-kham3 chiah e5 thiN;
	I e5 mia5-siaN chhiong-moa2 toe7.
	4). I e5 chhan3-lan7 chhin-chhiuN7 kng;
	Tui3 I e5 chhiu2 u7 chhut kng-soaN3.
	Ti7-hia I e5 leng5-lek8 khng3-teh.
	5). Ti7 I e5 thau5-cheng5 u7 un-ek8 liu5-heng5,
	Ti7 I e5 au7-bin7 u7 jiat8-cheng3 hoat-chhut.
	6). I khia7-teh, chiu7 niu5 toa7-toe7;
	I koan-khoaN3, koaN2-soaN3 liat8-pang;
	Eng2-oan2 ti7-teh e5 soaN lih8-khui;
	Ku2-tng5 e5 soaN-nia2 pang-ham7;
	I e5 sou2-kiaN5, tui3 cha2 i2-lai5 si7 an2-ni.
	7). Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 Kou2-san e5 pou3-piN5 tu2-tioh8 hoan7-lan7;
	Bi2-tian5 toe7 e5 tiuN3-li5 gih8-gih8-chhoah.
	8). Ia5-ho5-hoa ah, Li2 khia5 Li2 e5 be2,
	Che7 ti7 Li2 chin2-kiu3 e5 chhia,
	Kiam2-si7 bo5 hoaN-hi2 chiah e5 kang,
	Siu7-khi3 chiah e5 ho5,
	Thong3-hun7 iuN5-hai2 mah?
	9). Li2 e5 keng long2 lou7-chhut;
	Tui3 chiah e5 chi-phai3 sou2 chiu3-choa7--e5, long2 si7 chin-sit8 e5 oe7.		[Se3-lah8]
	Li2 eng7 kang-ho5 pun-khui toa7-toe7.
	10). SoaN-nia2 khoaN3-kiN3 Li2, chiu7 kiaN-hiaN5;
	Toa7-chui2 thau3-hong keng-ke3;
	Chhim-ian chhut i e5 siaN;
	Toa7-eng2 chheng3-koaiN5.
	11). In-ui7 Li2 e5 chiN3 sia7-chhut kng;
	Li2 e5 chhiuN e5 kng iam7-iam7 sih;
	Jit8 kap geh8 theng5-chi2 ti7 i e5 ui7-chhu3.
	12). Li2 siu7-khi3 lai5 pian3-kiaN5 toe7-chiuN7,
	Li2 toa7 siu7-khi3, phah liat8-kok chhin-chhiuN7 phah ngou2-kok.
	13). Li2 chhut--lai5 beh kiu3 Li2 e5 peh-siN3,
	Chin2-kiu3 Li2 e5 siu7-boah-iu5--e5;
	PhaiN2-lang5 ke-tiong e5 thau5-bak8, Li2 phah-chhui3 i,
	Lou7-hian7 i e5 kun-ki kau3 chin7-thau5.	[Se3-lah8]
	14). Li2 eng7 i ka-ki7 e5 chhiuN chhak8-thau3 i chian3-su7 e5 thau5;
	In lai5 chhin-chhiuN7 kng2-le5-hong, beh koaN2-soaN3 goa2;
	In sou2 hoaN-hi2--e5, si7 am3-chiN7 thun-chiah8 song3-hiong lang5.
	15). Li2 khia5-be2 tah8 ti7 hai2--nih:
	Chiu7-si7 tah8 gia5-eng2 e5 toa7-chui2.
	16). Goa2 thiaN-kiN3, goa2 e5 sim-tng5 kiau-ka2:
	Ui7-tioh8 hit e5 siaN, goa2 e5 chhui3-tun5 kheh8-kheh8-tio7;
	Kut-hai5 hiu2-hoai7, goa2 ti7 goa2 e5 sou2-chai7 ngiauh8-ngiauh8-chhoah.
	Goa2 chi2-u7 tiam7-tiam7 theng3-hau7 chai-lan7 e5 jit8 kau3,
	Chhim-hoan7 keng2-lai7 e5 peh-siN3 chiuN7--lai5.
	17). Sui-jian5 bu5-hoa-ko2 chhiu7 bo5 hoat-iuN7,
	Phu5-to5-chhiu7 bo5 kiat ke2-chi2,
	KaN-na2-chhiu7 iah8 bo5 siu-seng5,
	Chhan5-hng5 bo5 chhut gou7-kak,
	Tiau5-lai7 bo5 poaN3-chiah iuN5,
	Gu5-tiau5 ia7 bo5 gu5,
	18). Goa2 iau2-ku2 beh in-ui7 Ia5-ho5-hoa lai5 hoaN-hi2,
	In-ui7 kiu3 goa2 e5 Siong7-te3 lai5 khoai3-lok8.
	19). Chu2 Ia5-ho5-hoa si7 goa2 e5 khui3-lat8;
	I hou7 goa2 e5 kha chhin-chhiuN7 lok8-bu2;
	Koh hou7 goa2 kiaN5 ti7 goa2 koaiN5 e5 sou2-chai7.
	20). Chit-e5 koa kau hou7 gak8-koaN, eng7 goa2 si-hian5 e5 gak8-khi3 lai5 chhiuN3.
