Ui7-tioh8 chit-e5 goa2 tioh8-boa5, chiau3 I chhut-lat8 kiaN5 ti7 goa2 lai7-bin7 e5 kong-iong7 lai5 cheng-chian3.

[2]	In-ui7 goa2 ai3 lin2 chai goa2 ui7-tioh8 lin2, kap hiah e5 toa3 ti7 Lo2-ti2-ka e5, i2-kip8 kiN3-na7 ti7 jiok8-the2 be7 bat kiN3-tioh8 goa2 e5 bin7 e5, u7 chaiN2-iuN7 cheng-chian3,  2). thang hou7 in e5 sim siu7 an-ui3, ti7 jin5-ai3 saN kiat-lian5, tui3 chhiong-moa2 e5 hiau2-ngou7 tit-tioh8 it-chhe3 e5 pu3-u7, lai5 chhim chai Siong7-te3 e5 o3-biau7, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok;  3). hoan7-na7 ti3-hui7 kap chai-bat e5 po2-poe3 long2 khng3 ti7 I.  4). Goa2 kong2 an2-ni, bian2-tit lang5 eng7 ki5-kha2 e5 oe7 lai5 be5-hek8 lin2.  5). In-ui7 goa2 jiok8-the2 li7-khui lin2, sin5 kap lin2 ti7-teh, hoaN-hi2 khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 e5 cheng2-choe5, i2-kip8 lin2 sin3-kho3 Ki-tok e5 kian-kou3.
	6). Sou2-i2 lin2 ki3-jian5 chiap-lap8 Chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou, chiu7 tioh8 chiau3 an2-ni tiam3-ti7 I lai5 kiaN5,  7). i2-keng teng3 kun ti7 I, chiu7 tit8-tit8 siu7 khi2-cho7, tui3 sin3 lai5 kian-kou3, chiau3 lin2 sou2 bat siu7 e5 ka3-si7, moa2-moa2 kam2-sia7.
	8). Tioh8 kin2-sin7 bian2-tit hou7 lang5 eng7 i e5 tiat-hak8 kap khang-khang e5 bo5-iaN2 oe7 lai5 be5-hek8 lin2, chiau3 lang5 e5 ui5-thoan5, chiau3 se3-kan e5 siau2-hak8, m7 si7 chiau3 Ki-tok.  9). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 seng3-chit e5 hong-seng7 long2 chiau5-pi7 tiam3-ti7 I.  10). Lin2 tiam3-ti7 I ia7 tit-tioh8 hong-seng7; I si7 it-chhe3 pan7 cheng3-su7 chiang2-koan5 e5 e5 thau5.  11). Tiam3-ti7 I lin2 ia7 i2-keng siu7 kat-le2, m7 si7 eng7 chhiu2 lai5 kiaN5 e5 kat-le2, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok e5 kat-le2, thng3-khi3 jiok8-the2 e5 seng-khu;  12). i2-keng ti7 soe2-le2 kap I tang5 bai5-chong3, chiu7 ti7 chit e5 soe2-le2 ia7 kap I tang5 khi2-lai5, si7 tui3 sin3 Siong7-te3 e5 kong-iong7, chiu7-si7 tui3 si2-lang5 tiong hou7 I khi2-lai5 e5.  13). Koh lin2 cheng5 ui7-tioh8 choe7-koa3 kap jiok8-the2 e5 bo5 kat-le2 lai5 si2 e5, taN Siong7-te3 hou7 lin2 kap Ki-tok tang5 koh-oah8, i2-keng sia3-bian2 lan2 long2-chong2 e5 choe7-koa3,  14). chhat-siau kong-kek lan2 chiah e5 kui-le7 e5 ji7-iuN7, chiu7-si7 tui3-tek8 lan2 e5, teng3 i ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3, lai5 tui3 tiong-kan tu5 i;  15). ki3-jian5 pai7 hiah e5 pan7 cheng3-su7 e5, chiang2-koan5 e5, chiu7 kong-heng5 hian2-beng5 in, ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 iaN5 in lai5 tai7-iat8.
	16). Sou2-i2 boh8-tit in-ui7 sou2 chiah8 sou2 lim e5, a2-si7 ui7-tioh8 choeh-ki5, chhoe it, i2-kip8 an-hioh-jit8 e5 ian5-kou3, hou7 lang5 gi2-gi7;  17). chiah-e5 long2 si7 chiong-lai5 e5 su7 e5 iaN2, na7-si7 heng5 chiu7-si7 Ki-tok e5.  18). 	20). Lin2 na7 kap Ki-tok tang5 si2, thoat-li7 se3-kan e5 siau2-hak8, chaiN2-iuN7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 oah8 ti7 se3-kan, lai5 ka-ki7 hang5-hok8 ti7 chiah e5 kui-le7, chhin-chhiuN7 kong2,  21). [3]	Sou2-i2 lin2 na7 bat kap Ki-tok tang5 koh-oah8, chiu7 tioh8 kiu5 ti7 teng2-bin7 e5 su7;  Ki-tok ti7 hia, che7 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 peng5.  2).
