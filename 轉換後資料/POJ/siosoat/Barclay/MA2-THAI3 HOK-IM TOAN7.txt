2	Tng Hi-lut8 ong5 e5 si5, Ia5-sou i2-keng chhut-si3 ti7 Iu5-thai3 e5 Pek-li7-heng5, u7 kui2-na7 e5 phok-su7 tui3 tang-hng lai5 kau3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2,  2 kong2, Chhut-si3 choe3 Iu5-thai3 lang5 e5 ong5 e5 ti7 ta2-loh8? in-ui7 goan2 ti7 tang-hng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 I e5 chhiN, chiah lai5 beh pai3 I.  3 Hi-lut8 ong5 thiaN-kiN3 chiu7 peh-pak, thong Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 iah8 si7 an2-ni.  4 Chiu7 chu7-chip8 cheng3 che3-si-thau5, kap peh-siN3-kan e5 keng-hak8-su7, mng7 in Ki-tok tioh8 ti7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 chhut-si3.  5 In ka7 i kong2, Ti7 Iu5-thai3 e5 Pek-li7-heng5; in-ui7 sian-ti u7 ki3-chai3 kong2,

7 Tui3 an2-ni, Hi-lut8 am3-chiN7 tiau3 phok-su7, tui3 in siong5-se3 chha5 hit e5 chhiN chhut-hian7 e5 si5.  8 Chiu7 chhe in khi3 Pek-li7-heng5 kong2, Lin2 khi3 siong5-se3 tham3-thiaN hit e5 eN-a2 e5 su7; chhe7-tioh8 chiu7 lai5 ka7 goa2 kong2, hou7 goa2 ia7 thang khi3 pai3 I.  9 In thiaN ong5 e5 oe7 chiu7 khi3; hut-jian5 tang-hng sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 e5 chhiN choe3 thau5 chhoa7 in, kau3 eN-a2 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 hioh ti7 hit teng2-bin7.  10 In khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 chhiN, hoaN-hi2 kau3 boe7 kou3-tit.  11 Jip8 chhu3, khoaN3-kiN3 eN-a2 kap I e5 lau7-bu2 Ma2-li7-a; chiu7 phak-loh8 pai3 eN-a2; khui in e5 po2-ah8, hian3 le2-mih8 hou7 I, chiu7-si7 ng5-kim, ju2-hiuN, but8-ioh8.  12 Koh ti7 bin5-bang7-tiong tit-tioh8 chi2-tiam2, kah in m7 thang to3-tng2 khi3 chiu7-kun7 Hi-lut8, chiu7 tui3 pat8 tiau5 lou7 to3-khi3 pun2 sou2-chai7.
	13 In to3-khi3 liau2, u7 Chu2 e5 thiN-sai3 ti7 Iok-sek bin5-bang7-tiong chhut-hian7 kong2, Khi2-lai5, chhoa7 eN-a2 kap I e5 lau7-bu2, chau2 khi3 Ai-kip8, toa3 hia kau3 goa2 ka7 li2 kong2; in-ui7 Hi-lut8 beh chhe7 eN-a2 lai5 biat8 I.  14 I chiu7 khi2-lai5, thau3-mi5 chhoa7 eN-a2 kap I e5 lau7-bu2 khi3 Ai-kip8.;  toa3 hia kau3 Hi-lut8 si2;  15 si7 beh eng3-giam7 Chu2 thok sian-ti sou2 kong2 e5,  kong2, Goa2 bat tiau3 goa2 e5 kiaN2 chhut Ai-kip8.
	16 Hit si5 Hi-lut8 chai hou7 phok-su7 siat-khi3, chiu7 toa7 siu7-khi3, chhe lang5 chiong Pek-li7-heng5 kap chiu-ui5 sou2 u7 e5 ta-pou gin2-na2, chiau3 i tui3 phok-su7 siong5-se3 chha5 e5 si5, hoan7-na7 nng7 he3 i2-ha7 e5 it-chin7 thai5.  17 An2-ni eng3-giam7 cha2 thok sian-ti Ia5-li7-bi2 sou2 kong2 e5, kong2,
	18 Ti7 Liap8-ma2 thiaN-kiN3 siaN,
	Thi5-khau3 koh toa7 siong-pi,
	Liap8-kiat khau3 i e5 kiaN2-ji5;
	M7 goan7 siu7 an-ui3, in-ui7 in bo5--khi3.
	19 Hi-lut8 i2-keng si2, Chu2 e5 thiN-sai3 ti7 Ai-kip8, ti7 Iok-sek bin5-bang7-tiong chhut-hian7 kong2,  20 Khi2-lai5, chhoa7 eN-a2 kap I e5 lau7-bu2 khi3 I2-sek-liat8 toe7; in-ui7 hiah-e5 ai3 hai7 eN-a2 e5 siN3-mia7 e5 i2-keng si2 lah.  21 I chiu7 khi2-lai5, chhoa7 eN-a2 kap I e5 lau7-bu2 kau3 I2-sek-liat8 toe7.  22 Chong2-si7 thiaN-kiN3 A-ki-lo2 soa3-chiap i e5 lau7-pe7 Hi-lut8 ti7 Iu5-thai3 choe3 ong5, chiu7 m7 kaN2 khi3 hia; ki3-jian5 ti7 bin5-bang7-tiong tit-tioh8 chi2-tiam2, chiu7 the3-khi3 Ka-li7-li7 e5 keng2-lai7.  23 Kau3 chit8 e5 siaN5, mia5 Na2-sat-lek8, tiam3 hia khia7-khi2; si7 beh eng3-giam7 cha2 thok chiah e5 sian-ti sou2 kong2 e5 kong2, I beh kio3 choe3 Na2-sat-lek8 lang5.

3	 Tng hit si5, kiaN5 soe2-le2 e5 Iok-han7 lai5, ti7 Iu5-thai3 e5 khong3-ia2 soan-thoan5 kong2,  2 Lin2 tioh8 hoan2-hoe2; in-ui7 thian-kok kun7 lah.  3 Chit lang5 chiu7-si7 Chu2 thok sian-ti I2-sai3-a sou2 chi2 e5, kong2,

	13 Hit si5 Ia5-sou tui3 Ka-li7-li7 kau3 Iok-tan3, chiu7-kun7 Iok-han7, beh tui3 i siu7 soe2-le2.  14 Na7-si7 Iok-han7 chou2-chi2 I kong2, Goa2 hap8-kai tui3 li2 siu7 soe2-le2, li2 hoan2-tng2 chiu7-kun7 goa2 mah?  15 Ia5-sou in3 i kong2, TaN chhiaN2 un2 goa2, in-ui7 lan2 hap8-gi5 tioh8 an2-ni, lai5 chin7 long2-chong2 e5 gi7. Chiu7 un2 I.  16 Ia5-sou i2-keng siu7 soe2-le2, liam5-piN tui3 chui2 chiuN7-lai5: thiN hut-jian5 ui7-tioh8 I khui, I chiu7 khoaN3-kiN3 Siong7-te3 e5 Sin5, chhin-chhiuN7 chiN2-a2 kang3-lim5 ti7 I e5 teng2-bin7.  17 Koh tui3 thiN u7 siaN kong2, Chit-e5 si7 goa2 thiaN3 e5 KiaN2, goa2 sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5.

4	Hit si5 Ia5-sou hou7 Seng3 Sin5 chhoa7 kau3 khong3-ia2, beh siu7 Mou5-kui2 chhi3.  2 I2-keng kim3-chiah8 si3-chap8 jit8-mi5, au7-lai5 chiu7 iau.  3 Chhi3-e5 oa2-lai5 ka7 I kong2, Li2 na7 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2, chiu7 kah chiah e5 chioh8 piN3 choe3 piaN2.  Na7-si7 I in3 kong2,  4 U7 ki3-chai3 kong2, Lang5 e5 oah8, m7 na7 oa2-kho3 piaN2, chiu7-si7 oa2-kho3 tui3 Siong7-te3 e5 chhui3 sou2 chhut long2-chong2 e5 oe7.  5 Mou5-kui2 chiu7 chhoa7 I kau3 seng3 siaN5, hou7 I khia7 ti7 tian7 e5 be2-liu, ka7 I kong2,  6 Li2 na7 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2, tioh8 ka-ki7 tio5-loh8-khi3; in-ui7 u7 ki3-chai3 kong2,

	23 Ia5-sou pian3 kiaN5 Ka-li7-li7, ti7 in e5 hoe7-tng5 ka3-si7, soan-thaon5 thian-kok e5 hok-im, i-ho2 peh-siN3-kan tak8 iuN7 e5 cheng3-thau5, kap tak8 iuN7 e5 piN7-thiaN3.  24 I e5 mia5-siaN po3-iong5 kau3 thong Su7-li7-a; hoan7 u7 ti3 piN7 e5, tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 iuN7 e5 cheng3-thau5 kap thang3-thiaN3 e5, hoan7-tioh8 kui2 e5, iuN5-hin5 e5, pian3-sui7 e5, chhoa7 in long2 lai5 chiu7-kun7 I, I chiu7 i-ho2 in.  25 Hit si5, tui3 Ka-li7-li7, Te-ka-pho-li7, Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2, Iu5-thai3, kap Iok-tan3 goa7, u7 choe7-choe7 tin7 e5 lang5 lai5 te3 I.
