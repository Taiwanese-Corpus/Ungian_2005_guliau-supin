		
		Pi2-Tek E5 Phoe Hou7 Su(61)

[1]	Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lou5-pok8, ia7 si7 I e5 su3-tou5, Se-bun5 Pi2-tek, phoe kia3 hou7 hiah e5 kap goan2 saN-kap tit-tioh8 tang5 chit iuN7 po2-poe3 e5 sin3, ti7 lan2 e5 Siong7-te3 kap Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 gi7 e5:  2). goan7 un-tian2 peng5-an , tui3 lin2 chhim chai Siong7-te3 kap lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou, ke-thiN choe7-choe7 hou7 lin2;  3). in-ui7 Siong7-te3 e5 koan5-leng5 i2-keng eng7 it-chhe3 koan-he7 oah8-mia7 kap keng3-khian5 e5 su7 siuN2-su3 lan2, tui3 ti7 chhim chai hit e5 eng7 ka-ki7 e5 eng5-kng kap ho2-tek tiau3 lan2 e5;  4). tui3 chiah-e5 i2-keng u7 po2-poe3 kap kek8 toa7 e5 eng3-un2 siuN2-su3 lan2, beh hou7 lin2 tui3 chiah e5 eng3-un2 thoat-li7 se3-kan tui3 su-iok8 sou2 ti3 e5 pai7-hoai7, tit-tioh8 u7 hun7 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 seng3-cheng5.  5). Ui7-tioh8 an2-ni, lin2 tioh8 chin7-lat8 un-khun5, ti7 lin2 e5 sin3 ke-thiN ho2-tek, ti7 ho2-tek ke-thiN ti3-sek,  6). ti7 ti3-sek ke-thiN chun2-chat, ti7 chun2-chat ke-thiN thun-lun2, ti7 thun-lun2 ke-thiN keng3-khian5,  7). ti7 keng3-khian5 ke-thiN thiaN3 hiaN-ti7, ti7 thiaN3 hiaN-ti ke-thiN thiaN3 cheng3-lang5.  8). In-ui7 lin2 na7 u7 chiah-e5 kau3 chhiong-moa2, chiu7 beh hou7 lin2 ti7 chhim chai lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok put-ti3 pin5-toaN7 bo5 kiat-sit8,  9). In-ui7 bo5 chiah-e5 e5 lang5 chiu7 chhiN-mi5, chi2 u7 khoaN3 kun7-kun7, i2-keng boe7 ki3-tit i cheng5 e5 choe7 u7 soe2-chheng.  10). HiaN-ti7 ah, sou2-i2 tioh8 koh khah un-khun5, lai5 kian-kou3 lin2 e5 siu7 tiau3 siu7 keng2.  In-ui7 lin2 kiaN5 chiah-e5, chiu7 eng2-oan2 boe7 poah8-to2.  11). Si7 tui3 an2-ni beh toa7-toa7 siuN2-su3 lin2 jip8 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok eng2-oan2 ti7-teh e5 kok.
	12). Sou2-i2 lin2 sui-jian5 i2-keng chai chiah e5 su7, koh kian-kou3 ti7 sou2 u7 e5 chin-li2, goa2 beh siong5-siong5 hou7 lin2 ki3-tit,  13). Goa2 iau2 tiam3 ti7 chit e5 pou3-piN5 e5 si5, tiam2-chhiN2 lin2 hou7 lin2 oe7 ki3-tit, goa2 phah-sng3 si7 eng-kai;  14). ka-ki7 chai thiah goa2 e5 pou3-piN5 e5 si5 kun7 lah, chiau3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok sou2 chi2-si7 goa2 e5.  15). Goa2 ia7 beh un-khun5, hou7 lin2 ti7 goa2 ke3-si3 e5 liau2-au7, put-si5 oe7 ki3-tit chiah e5 su7.  16). In-ui7 goan2 peng5-sou3 hou7 lin2 chai lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 koan5-leng5 kap I e5 kang3-lim5, m7 si7 eng7 ki5-khiau2 bo5 iaN2-chiah oe7, chiu7-si7 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3 I e5 toa7 ui-giam5.  17). In-ui7 I tui3 Pe7 Siong7-te3 tit-tioh8 chun-kui3 eng5-kng, hit si5 tui3 kek8 toa7 e5 eng5-kng u7 chit ho7 siaN kau3 ti7 I, kong2, Chit-e5 si7 goa2 thiaN3 e5 KiaN2, goa2 sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5.  18). Chit e5 siaN tui3 thiN chhut, goan2 kap I ti7 seng3-soaN e5 si5 thiaN-kiN3.  19). Koh lan2 u7 sian-ti e5 oe7 oe7 koh khah kian-kou3, lin2 ti3-i3 chiah-e5 si7 ho2, chhin-chhiuN7 teng chio3 ti7 ou-am3, kau3 ti7 thiN-kng, khe2-beng5-chhiN chhut-hian7 ti7 lin2 e5 sim e5 si5.  20). Te7 it, tioh8 seng chai, kiN3-na7 seng3-keng tiong sian-ti e5 oe7, bo5 thang than3 su-khia e5 i3-su3 lai5 khui-thiah.  21). In-ui7 be7 u7 sian-ti e5 oe7 tui3 lang5 e5 i3-su3 chhut; chiu7-si7 tui3 Siong7-te3 lai5 e5 lang5, hou7 Seng3 Sin5 kam2-tong7 lai5 kong2.

[2]	Na7-si7 ti7 peh-siN3 e5 tiong-kan, bat u7 ke2 e5 sian-ti, an2-ni ti7 lin2 e5 tiong-kan ia7 teh-beh u7 ke2 e5 sian-siN, am3-chiN7 in2-chin3 hiah e5 hai7 lang5 kau3 tim5-lun5 e5 iN7-toan jip8-lai5, chiu7 siok8-hoe5 in e5 Chu2-chaiN2 in ia7 m7 jin7, ka-ki7 chhu2 kin2-kin2 kau3 e5 tim5-lun5.  2). Beh u7 choe7-choe7 lang5 than3 in e5 sia5-im5, chin-li2 e5 lou7 beh ui7-tioh8 in lai5 siu7 hui2-pong3.  3). In tui3 tham-sim beh cho7-chok oe7, lai5 lut lin2; tui3 cha2 i2-lai5 in e5 sim2-phoaN3 bo5 ian5-chhian5, in e5 tim5-lun5 bo5 hioh-khun3.  4). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 ki3-jian5 bo5 sioh hoan7-choe7 e5 thiN-sai3, si7 hiat in ti7 am3-hu2, kau in ti7 ou-am3 e5 khiN, siu kim3 teh theng3-hau7 sim2-phoaN3;  5). koh bo5 sioh kou2-cha2 e5 se3-kai3, hou7 toa7 chui2 kau3 ti7 hiah e5 bo5 keng3-khian5 e5 lang5 e5 se3-kai3, tok8-tok8 po2-hou7 soan-thoan5 gi7-li2 e5 Na2-a hit poeh lang5;  6). koh hou7 Sou2-to-ma2, Ngou5-mou5-lah8 hiah e5 siaN5 choe3 he2-hu, gi2-tiaN7 in biat8-bo5, he7-teh choe3 au7 se3-tai7 bo5 keng3-khian5 e5 lang5 e5 kam3-kai3;  7). tok8-tok8 chin2-kiu3 gi7 lang5 Lo5-tek, chiu7-si7 siong5-siong5 ui7-tioh8 bu5-li2 e5 lang5 sia5-im5 e5 sou2-kiaN5 lai5 chhim-chhim iu-bun7 e5,  8). in-ui7 chit e5 gi7 lang5 khia7-khi2 ti7 in tiong-kan, ia7 khoaN3-kiN3 ia7 thiaN-kiN3, jit8-jit8 in-ui7 in put-hoat e5 sou2 choe3 lai5 khou2-chhou2 i gi7 e5 sim;  9). Chu2 chai thang chin2-kiu3 keng3-khian5 e5 lang5 thoat-li7 iu2-hek8, ia7 chai thang siu-kim3 put-gi7 e5 lang5 kau3 sim2-phoaN3 e5 jit8, siu7 heng5-hoat8.  10). koh khah iau3-kin2 si7 hiah e5 than3 jiok8-the2 kiaN5 ti7 u3-oe3 e5 su-iok8, ia7 biau2-si7 pan7 cheng3-su7 e5; in toa7 taN2, chu7-si7, chiu7 siat-tok8 chun-kui3 e5, ia7 bo5 kiaN.  11). Sui-jian5 thiN-sai3 khui3-lat8 koan5-leng5 khah toa7, iau2-ku2 be7 bat ti7 Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 eng7 pong3-tok8 e5 oe7 chek-choe7 in.  12). Tok8-tok8 chiah e5 lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 leng5-seng3 e5 oah8 mih8, seng-seng5 tioh8 hou7 lang5 liah8 lai5 pai7-hoai7 e5, siat-tok8 in sou2 m7 chai e5, beh ti7 in e5 pai7-hoai7 lai5 siu7 pai7-hoai7, tu2-tioh8 kan-khou2 choe3 put-gi7 e5 kang-chiN5;  13). in liah8 jit8-si5 e5 ian3-lok8 choe3 hoaN-hi2, si7 la5-sam5 si7 ha5-chhu5, kap lin2 tang5 ian5-siah8, ian3-lok8 ti7 in e5 ho5-ai3-sek8;  14). in e5 bak8-chiu long2 si7 im5-sek, hoan7 choe7 bo5 soah, be5-hek8 sim bo5 kian-kou3 e5 lang5, in e5 sim koaiN3-si3 tham chai5, hap8-kai siu7 chiu3-chou2;  15). li7-khui chiaN3 lou7 lai5 sit-be5, than3 Pi3-ni2 e5 kiaN2 Pa-lan5 e5 lou7, i si7 it-tioh8 put-gi7 e5 kang-chiN5;  16). na7-si7 ui7-tioh8 ka-ki7 e5 hoan7-hoat lai5 tu2-tioh8 chek-pi7; boe7 kong2 oe7 e5 lu5 kong2 lang5 e5 oe7, lai5 chou2-chi2 sian-ti e5 lam7-sam2.  17). In si7 bo5 chui2 e5 choaN5, kong5 hong sou2 sau3 e5 hun5-bu7, u7 ou-ou am3-am3 lau5-teh theng3-hau7 in.  18). In-ui7 in kong2 khang-hu khoa-khau2 e5 oe7, ti7 jiok8-the2 e5 su-iok8, eng7 sia5-im5 lai5 iu2-hek8 hiah e5 tu2-tu2 thoat-li7 kiaN5 chha-biu7 e5 lang5 e5 chhiu2 e5;  19). eng3-un2 in chu7-iu5, ia7 ka-ki7 choe3 pai7-hoai7 e5 lou5-chai5, in-ui7 lang5 hou7 sim2-mih8 khah-iaN5; chiu7 choe3 hit hang7 e5 lou5-chai5.  20). In-ui7 in i2-keng tui3 chhim chai Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 tit-tioh8 thoat-li7 se3-kan hiah e5 u3-oe3, na7 koh-chai3 hou7 hiah e5 u3-oe3 sou2 tiN5 sou2 ap-che3, chiu7 hit ho7 lang5 lou7-be2 e5 su7 pi2 tai7-seng khah siong-tiong7.  21). In-ui7 ki3-jian5 chai gi7 e5 lou7, ia7 keng3-jian5 poe7-biu7 sou2 thoan5 hou7 in seng3 e5 beng7-leng7, m7 tat8-tioh8 in long2 m7 chai koh khah ho2.  22). Hit ho7 lang5 e5 keng2-hong2 si7 hap8 ti7 chin e5 siok8-gu2 sou2 kong2, Kau2 to3-tng2 chiah8 i sou2 thou3 e5, ti i2-keng soe2 koh noa3 ti7 thou5-be5 e5 tiong-ng.

[3]	 Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, goa2 hian7-chai7 sou2 sia2 hou7 lin2 e5 si7 te7 ji7 tiuN e5 phoe; ti7 chit nng7 tiuN si7 beh hou7 lin2 ki3-liam7, lai5 tiam2-chhiN2 lin2 sun5-choan5 e5 sim;  2). hou7 lin2 ki3-tit seng3 e5 sian-ti chham3-gu2 e5 oe7, i2-kip8 Kiu3-chu2 tui3 lin2 e5 su3-tou5 sou2 beng7-leng7 e5.  3). Te7 it tioh8 chai e5, ti7 hiah e5 lou7-be2 e5 si5-jit8 beh u7 hi3-hiok e5 lang5 chhut-lai5, chiau3 ka-ki7 e5 su-iok8 lai5 kiaN5 e5, hi3-hiok kong2,  4). I eng3-un2 koh lai5 taN ti7 ta2-loh8?  In-ui7 tui3 liat8-chou2 khun3-khi3 i2-lai5, ban7 hang7 mih8 tui3 khi2-thau5 chhong3-cho7 chiau3 ku7 bo5 koh-iuN7.  5). In-ui7 in hiah e5 lang5 kou3-i3 boe7 ki3-tit e5, chiu7-si7 tui3 cha2 u7 chiah e5 thiN, koh u7 toe7 tui3 chui2 chhut, tui3 chui2 lai5 chiaN5, long2 si7 tui3 Siong7-te3 e5 to7;  6). Tui3 chiah e5 thiN toe7 tng hit si5 e5 se3-kan hou7 chui2 hoan3-lam7 lai5 biat8-bo5.  7). Tok8-tok8 hian7-kim chiah e5 thiN kap toe7 si7 tui3 chit e5 to7 khng3-teh, lai5 theng3-hau7 he2, lau5-teh kau3 bo5 keng3-khian5 e5 lang5 siu7 sim2-phoaN3 kap biat8-bo5 e5 jit8.
	8). Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, u7 chit8 hang7 lin2 m7 thang boe7 ki3-tit, chiu7-si7 Chu2 khoaN3 chit8 jit8 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 chheng ni5, chit8 chheng ni5 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 jit8.  9). Chu2 bo5 ian5-chhian5 ti7 sou2 eng3-un2, chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 u7-e5 khoaN3 choe3 ian5-chhian5 e5, chiu7-si7 khoan-iong5 lin2, m7 ai3 u7 lang5 tim5-lun5, si7 ai3 lang5 long2 hoan2-hoe2.  10). Na7-si7 Chu2 e5 jit8 beh kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 chhat8, hit jit8 hiah e5 thiN beh u7 toa7 siaN hoe3-khi3, hiah e5 goan5-chit siu7 sio lai5 siau-biat8, toe7 kap ki5-tiong sou2 u7 e5 mih8 ia7 sio-lio-khi3.  11). Ban7 mih8 ki3-jian5 beh an2-ni siau-biat8, chiu7 lin2 ti7 seng3 kap keng3-khian5 tioh8 choe3 sim2-mih8 ho7 e5 lang5,  12). lai5 ng3-bang7 soa3 chhui-pek Siong7-te3 e5 jit8 kau3; ui7-tioh8 an2-ni chiah e5 thiN oe7 hou7 he2 siau-biat8, goan5-chit siu7 sio, chiu7 iuN5-khi3?  13). Na7-si7 chiau3 I sou2 eng3-un2, lan2 ng3-bang7 sin e5 thiN sin e5 toe7, gi7 tiam3 ti7 in tiong-kan.
	14). Sou2-i2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 ki3-jian5 ng3-bang7 chiah-e5, tioh8 un-khun5 hou7 lin2 bo5 la5-sam5 bo5 ha5-chhu5 an-jian5 kiN3-tioh8 I.  15). Koh tioh8 khoaN3 lan2 e5 Chu2 e5 khoan-iong5 choe3 chin2-kiu3 e5 in-iu5; chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 Po2-lo5, chiau3 sou2 siuN2-su3 i e5 ti3-hui7, ia7 bat sia2 phoe hou7 lin2;  16). ti7 i long2-chong2 e5 phoe teh lun7 chiah e5 su7 ia7 si7 an2-ni kong2; ki5-tiong u7 oh-tit beng5 e5, m7 chai-iaN2 bo5 kian-kou3 e5 lang5 koe2 hou7 i chhoah8-khi3, chhin-chhiuN7 koe2-chhoah8 ki5-u5 e5 seng3-keng, ti3-kau3 ka-ki7 tim5-lun5.  17). Sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 ah, an2-ni lin2 ki3-jian5 tai7-seng chai, tioh8 ti5-hong5 bian2-ti3 hou7 bu5-li2 e5 lang5 e5 chha-biu7 sou2 in2-iu2, lai5 sit-loh8 ka-ki7 e5 kian-kou3.  18). Tok8-tok8 tioh8 chin3-pou7 ti7 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 un-tian2, i2-kip8 bat I.  Goan7 eng5-kng kui I, tui3 hian7-kim kau3 eng2-oan2 e5 jit8.  A3-beng7.
