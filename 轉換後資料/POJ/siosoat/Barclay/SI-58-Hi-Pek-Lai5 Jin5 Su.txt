[1]	Siong7-te3 ti7 chai7-cha2 i2-keng thok hiah e5 sian-ti, eng7 choe7-choe7 toaN7, choe7-choe7 hong-hoat, ka7 liat8-chou2 kong2;  2). taN ti7 hiah e5 jit8 e5 lou7-be2, thok I e5 KiaN2 ka7 lan2 kong2; chiu7-si7 I sou2 siat-lip8 choe3 seng5-chiap ban7 mih8 e5 hou7-su5, iah8 bat tui3 I lai5 chhong3-cho7 chiah e5 se3-kai3.  3). I si7 Siong7-te3 e5 eng5-iau7 sou2 hoat-chhut e5 kng, si7 Siong7-te3 pun2-chit e5 chin siong7, I eng7 I koan5-leng5 e5 oe7 teh hu5-chai3 ban7 mih8, i2-keng chiaN5 chheng-kiat choe7 e5 su7, chiu7 che7 ti7 kek8 koaiN5 e5 thiN-teng2, toa7 ui-giam5 e5 e5 toa7 peng5;  4). chiaN5-choe3 pi2 chiah e5 thiN-sai3 khah toa7, hap8 ti7 I sou2 seng5-chiap e5 mia5, pi2 in khah chun-kui3.  5). 	11). In beh biat8-bo5; tok8-tok8 li2 ku2-tng5 ti7-teh:

13). Siong7-te3 bat tui3 ta2-loh8 chit8 e5 thiN-sai3 kong2,
	Che7 goa2 e5 toa7 peng5,
	Theng3-hau7 goa2 he7 li2 e5 tui3-tek8 choe3 li2 e5 kha-tah8-i2?
14). In kiam2 m7 si7 long2 choe3 chhe-eng7 e5 sin5, hong7-chhe lai5 hok8-sai7 teh-beh tit kiu3 e5 lang5 mah?

[2]	Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 it-hoat ti3-i3 sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5, kiaN-liau2 lan2 oe7 ti3-kau3 liu-khi3.  2). In-ui7 tui3 thiN-sai3 sou2 thoan5 e5 to7-li2, siong7-chhiaN2 si7 kian-kou3, ia7 kiN3-na7 kan-hoan7 ui5-keh8 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 tu2-tioh8 eng-kai siu7 e5 po3-eng3;  3). lan2 na7 hut-liok8 chiah-nih8 toa7 e5 chin2-kiu3 kiam2 oe7 to5-siam2 mah?  Chit e5 chin2-kiu3 ki3-jian5 si7 Chu2 khi2-thau5 kong2 e5, thiaN-kiN3 e5 lang5 kan-cheng3 kau3 sit8-sit8 hou7 lan2;  4). koh Siong7-te3 eng7 chiah e5 sin5-jiah, ki5-su7, tak8 hang7 e5 koan5-leng5, kap Seng3 Sin5 e5 siuN2-su3, long2 chiau3 ka-ki7 e5 chi2-i3, kap in saN-kap kan-cheng3.
	5). In-ui7 goan2 sou2 kong2 teh lai5 e5 se3-kai3 Siong7-te3 be7 bat hou7 i hang5-hok8 ti7 thiN-sai3.  6). Tok8-tok8 u7 kan-cheng3 ti7 bou2 sou2-chai7 e5, kong2,
	Lang5 si7 sim2-mih8, hou7 li2 siau3-liam7 i?
	Lang5 e5 kiaN2 si7 sim2-mih8, hou7 li2 khoaN3-kou3 i?
	7). Li2 hou7 i lioh8-a2 khah-su thiN-sai3,
	Koh eng7 eng5-kng chun-kui3, hou7 i choe3 bian2-liu5,
	Koh siat-lip8 i ti7 li2 e5 chhiu2 sou2 cho7 e5 e5 teng2-bin7;
	8). Hou7 ban7 mih8 long2 hang5-hok8 ti7 i e5 kha-e7.
I2-keng hou7 ban7 mih8 long2 hang5-hok8 i, chiu7 bo5 chhun chit8 hang7 mih8 bo5 hang5-hok8 i.  Chong2-si7 kau3 taN lan2 iau2-be7 khoaN3-kiN3 ban7 mih8 long2 hang5-hok8 i.  9). Tok8-tok8 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 lioh8-a2 khah su thiN-sai3 e5 Ia5-sou, ui7-tioh8 siu7 si2 e5 kan-khou2 tit-tioh8 eng5-kng chun-kui3 choe3 bian2-liu5, thang tui3 Siong7-te3 e5 un, ui7-tioh8 cheng3 lang5 siu7 si2.  10). In-ui7 ban7 mih8 sou2 ui7-tioh8 I, ban7 mih8 sou2 tui3 I e5, teh chhoa7 choe7-choe7 kiaN2 kau3 ti7 eng5-kng, chiu7 hou7 kiu3 in e5 siu2-leng2 tui3 khou2-lan7 lai5 chiaN5 oan5-choan5, pun2 si7 hap8-gi5.  11). In-ui7 hit e5 hou7 lang5 chiaN5 seng3 e5, kap hiah e5 tit-tioh8 chiaN5 seng3 e5, long2 tui3 chit8-e5 chhut; sou2-i2 I chheng in choe3 hiaN-ti7 bo5 liah8 choe3 kian3-siau3;  12). kong2,
	Goa2 beh po3-iong5 li2 e5 mia5 ti7 goa2 e5 hiaN-ti7,
	O-lo2 li2 ti7 hoe7-tiong.
13). Koh kong2,
	Goa2 beh oa2-kho3 I.
Koh kong2,
	KhoaN3 ah! goa2 kap Siong7-te3 sou2 hou7 goa2 e5 kiaN2-ji5.
14). TaN chiah e5 kiaN2-ji5 ki3-jian5 saN-tang5 u7 huih bah e5 the2, I pun2-sin iah8 piN5-piN5 u7; beh eng7 si2 lai5 hoe3-bo5 u7 si2 e5 koan5-se3 e5, chiu7-si7 Mou5-kui2;  15). iah8 beh hou7 it-seng kiaN-ui3 si2 kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 choe3 lou5-chai5 e5, long2 tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.  16). In-ui7 I bo5 khan-kiu3 thiN-sai3, si7 khan-kiu3 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 hou7-e3.  17). Sou2-i2 hoan7 su7 I tioh8 kap hiaN-ti7 saN-tang5, thang ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 su7 choe3 lin5-bin2 tiong-sin3 e5 che3-si-thau5, in-ui7 peh-siN3 e5 choe7 hian3 ban2-hoe5 e5 che3.  18). In-ui7 I i2-keng siu7 khou2 lai5 tu2-tioh8 chhi3, chiu7 oe7 pang-chan7 chiah e5 siu7 chhi3 e5 lang5.

[3]	Sou2-i2 lin2 chiah e5 seng3 e5 hiaN-ti7, tang5 tit-tioh8 thiN tiau3 e5, tioh8 chhim siuN7 lan2 sou2 seng5-jin7 choe3 Su3-chia2, choe3 Che3-si-thau5 e5 Ia5-sou;   2). I chin7-tiong ti7 siat-lip8 I e5, chhin-chhiuN7 Mou5-se ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 choan5 ke chit8 iuN7.  3). In-ui7 I sng3 si7 oe7 kham-tit siu7 eng5-kng iaN5-ke3 Mou5-se chhin-chhiuN7 kian3-lip8 ke e5, pi2 ke khah chun-kui3.  4). In-ui7 ke long2 u7 lang5 kian3-lip8 i, tok8-tok8 kian3-lip8 ban7 mih8 e5 chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3.  5). Koh Mou5-se choe3 lou5-pok8, ko2-jian5 chin7-tiong ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 choan5 ke, lai5 kan-cheng3 chiong-lai5 beh thoan5 e5 su7;  6). tok8-tok8 Ki-tok choe3 KiaN2, ti7-li2 I e5 ke; lan2 na7 chip-siu2 tui3 ng3-bang7 sou2 u7 e5 ho2-taN2 kap khoa-khau2, kian-kou3 kau3 be2, chiu7 choe3 I e5 ke.  7). Sou2-i2, chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3 Sin5 kong2:
	Kin-a2-jit8 lin2 na7 thiaN-kiN3 I e5 siaN,
	Chiu7 m7 thang kong-ngi7 lin2 e5 sim, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 kek goa2 siu7-khi3 e5 si5,
	Ti7 khong3-ia2 chhi3-tham3 goa2 e5 jit8.
	9). Ti7 hia lin2 e5 liat8-chou2 giam7 lai5 chhi3 goa2,
	Koh lek8-ke3 si3-chap8 ni5 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 e5 sou2 kiaN5.
	10). Sou2-i2 goa2 bo5 hoaN-hi2 hit tai7 e5 lang5,
	Kong2, In e5 sim siong5-siong5 sit-be5,
	M7 bat goa2 chiah e5 lou7;
	11). Goa2 chiu7 ti7 siu7-khi3 tiong chiu3-choa7, kong2,
	In toan3-toan3 boe7 tit thang jip8 goa2 e5 an-hioh.
12). HiaN-ti7 ah, lin2 tioh8 kin2-sin7, kiaN-liau2 lin2 tiong-kan kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 khng3 m7 sin3 e5 phaiN2 sim, li7-khui chin oah8 e5 Siong7-te3;  13). sou2-i2 lin2 tioh8 than3 iau2 u7 thang kio3 choe3 kin-a2-jit8 e5, jit8-jit8 saN khoan3-bian2; kiaN-liau2 lin2 tiong-kan u7-e5 hou7 choe7 be5--khi3, lai5 ngi7-sim:  14). in-ui7 lan2 na7 chhi5-siu2 khi2-thau5 e5 chhim sin3, kian-kou3 kau3 lou7-be2, chiu7 u7 hun7 ti7 Ki-tok:  15). tng iau2 teh kong2,
	Kin-a2-jit8 lin2 na7 thiaN-kiN3 I e5 siaN,
	Chiu7 m7 thang kong-ngi7 lin2 e5 sim, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 kek goa2 siu7-khi3 e5 si5.
16). In-ui7 thiaN lai5 kek siu7-khi3 e5, si7 chi7-chui7 ah?  kiam2 m7 si7 te3 Mou5-se chhut Ai-kip8 e5 cheng3 lang5 mah?  17). Siong7-te3 si3-chap8 ni5 ku2 sou2 bo5 hoaN-hi2 e5 si7 chi7-chui7 ah?  kiam2 m7 si7 hoan7-choe7, sin-si to2 ti7 khong3-ia2 e5 lang5 mah?  18). Siong7-te3 chiu3-choa7 m7 chun2 jip8 I e5 an-hioh e5, na7 m7 si7 m7 sin3-than3 e5 lang5, si7 chi7-chui7 ah?  19). Tui3 an2-ni khoaN3, in boe7 oe7 jip8, chiu7-si7 in-ui7 m7 sin3.

[4]	Sou2-i2 u7 jip8 I e5 an-hioh e5 eng3-un2 lau5-teh hou7 lan2, chiu7 tioh8 kiaN-hiaN5, kiaN-kiN3 lin2 tiong-kan kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 tit bo5 tioh8.  2). In-ui7 hok-im u7 thoan5 hou7 lan2, chhin-chhiuN7 thoan5 hou7 in, chong2-si7 sou2 thiaN e5 oe7 bo5 li7-ek in, in-ui7 thiaN e5 lang5 bo5 eng7 sin3 kap to7-li2 saN-hap8.  3). In-ui7 lan2 i2-keng sin3 e5, si7 jip8 chit e5 an-hioh; chiau3 I sou2 kong2,
	Goa2 chiu7 ti7 siu7-khi3 tiong chiu3-choa7 kong2,
	In toan3-toan3 boe7 tit thang jip8 goa2 e5 an-hioh;
ki5-sit8 chiah e5 kang, tui3 chhong3-se3-kan i2-lai5, i2-keng chiaN5 lah.  4). In-ui7 lun7-kau3 te7 chhit jit8, ti7 bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 an2-ni kong2, Siong7-te3 ti7 te7 chhit jit8 beng5-pek8 I long2-chong2 e5 kang lai5 an-hioh;  5). iah8 ti7 chit sou2-chai7 koh kong2,
	In toan3-toan3 boe7 tit-thang jip8 goa2 e5 an-hioh.
6). Sou2-i2 chit e5 an-hioh iau2 teh theng3-hau7 u7 lang5 jip8, na7-si7 hiah e5 tai7-seng tit-tioh8 hok-im e5, in-ui7 m7 sin3-than3, chiah bo5 jip8;  7). lek8-ke3 choe7-choe7 ni5, ti7 Tai7-pit8 e5 chheh-lai7, I koh teng7 chit8 jit8, kong2, Kin-a2-jit8, chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2,
	Kin-a2-jit8 lin2 na7 thiaN-kiN3 I e5 siaN,
	Chiu7 m7 thang kong-ngi7 lin2 e5 sim.
8). In-ui7 Iok-su-a na7 bat eng7 an-hioh siuN2-su3 peh-siN3, chiu7 au7-lai5 tek-khak bo5 kong2-khi2 pat8 jit8.  9). Tui3 an2-ni khoaN3, iau2 u7 an-hioh ka7 Siong7-te3 e5 peh-siN3 lau5-teh.  10). In-ui7 jip8 i e5 an-hioh e5 si7 beng5-pek8 i long2-chong2 e5 kang lai5 an-hioh,  11). chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-te3 beng5-pek8 I e5 kang lai5 an-hioh.  Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 chin7-lat8 lai5 jip8 chit e5 an-hioh, bian2-tit u7 lang5 poah8-to2 ti7 siang7 hit iuN7 e5 m7 sin3.  12). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 e5 to7 si7 oah8 e5, si7 u7 kong-hau7 e5, pi2 it-chhe3 siang-bin7 e5 kiam3 khah lai7, hoan7-na7 hun5 kap sin5, kut-chat kap kut-chhe2, bo5 m7 chhi3-jip8 phoa3-khui, sim-lai7 chiah e5 liam7-thau5 kap chu2-i3 iah8 bo5 m7 kam3-chhat.  13). Koh sou2 chhong3-cho7 e5, ti7 I e5 bin7-cheng5 bo5 chit8 hang7 kham3-bat8: chiu7-si7 ban7 mih8 hian-khui lou7-chhut ti7 sim2-phoaN3 lan2 e5 e5 bak8-cheng5. 
	14). Sou2-i2 lan2 i2-keng u7 toa7 e5 che3-si-thau5, Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2 Ia5-sou, keng-ke3 hiah e5 thiN, chiu7 tioh8 chip-siu2 sou2 seng5-jin7 e5.  15). In-ui7 lan2 sou2 u7 e5 che3-si-thau5 m7 si7 boe7-oe7 the2-thiap lan2 long2-chong2 e5 loan2-jiok8; chiu7-si7 hoan7 su7 piN5-piN5 u7 siu7 chhi3, na7-si7 bo5 choe7.  16). Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 ho2-taN2 chiu7-kun7 un-tian2 e5 cho7-ui7, thang siu7 lin5-bin2, ia7 tit-tioh8 un-tian2, choe3 kip8-si5 e5 pang-chan7.

[5]	In-ui7 tui3 lang5 e5 tiong-kan sou2 keng2 e5 che3-si-thau5 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 lang5, ti7 koan-he7 Siong7-te3 e5 su7, lai5 siat-lip8, thang ui7-tioh8 choe7-koa3 hian3 le2-mih8 kap che3-su7.  2). I ki3-jian5 hou7 nng2-chiaN2 sou2 ui5-khun3, chiah oe7 the2-liong7 m7 chai-iaN2 kap sit-be5 e5 lang5;  3). sou2-i2 i eng-kai in-ui7 pun2-sin, chhin-chhiuN7 in-ui7 peh-siN3, ui7-tioh8 choe7 lai5 hian3-che3.  4). Koh chit e5 chun-kui3, m7 si7 lang5 ka-ki7 chhu2, chi2 u7 hou7 Siong7-te3 tiau3 e5, chhin-chhiuN7 A-lun5 chit8 iuN7.  5). Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni, Ki-tok iah8 m7 si7 ka-ki7 chun choe3 che3-si-thau5, chiu7-si7 hit-e5 tui3 I kong2,
	Li2 chiu7-si7 goa2 e5 KiaN2, Goa2 kin-a2-jit8 siN lin2 e5.
6). Ti7 pat8 ui7 iah8 kong2,
	Li2 eng2-oan2 choe3 che3-si,
	Chiau3 Bek8-ki-se2-tek e5 pan-liat8.

Ki-tok ti7 I tiam3 jiok8-the2 e5 jit8, eng7 chhut-lat8 au5-kio3, koh lau5 bak8-sai2, hian3 ki5-to2 khun2-kiu5 hou7 hit e5 oe7 kiu3 I chhut si2 e5, koh in-ui7 I e5 keng3-khian5 tit-tioh8 thiaN-kiN3,  8). sui-jian5 choe3 KiaN2, iah8 tui3 sou2 siu7 chiah e5 khou2-lan7 lai5 oh8 sun7-hok8;  9). i2-keng chiaN5-choe3 oan5-choan5, chiu7 choe3 hoan7-na7 sun7-hok8 I e5 lang5 eng2-oan2 chin2-kiu3 e5 kun-goan5;  10). hou7 Siong7-te3 chheng I choe3 che3-si-thau5, chiau3 Bek8-ki-se2-tek e5 pan-liat8.
	11). Lun7-kau3 chit-e5, goan2 u7 choe7-choe7 oe7 ai3 kong2, m7 ku2 oh-tit koe2-beng5, in-ui7 lin2 hi7-khang tun7 e5 ian5-kou3.  12). In-ui7 lun7-kau3 si5 e5 ku2, lin2 tioh8 ka-ki7 choe3 sian-siN, na7-si7 iau2 khiam3-eng7 lang5 chiong Siong7-te3 e5 oe7 chiah e5 siau2-hak8 e5 khi2-thau5 ka3 lin2; koh chiaN5-choe3 khiam3-eng7 lin, bo5 khiam3-eng7 ngou2-kok.  13). Hoan7-na7 chiah8 lin e5, bo5 lau2-lian7 gi7 e5 to7-li2; in-ui7 i si7 eN-a2.  14). Tok8-tok8 tiong2-seng5 e5 lang5 chiah8 ngou2-kok, in e5 sim-khiau3 tui3 lian7-sip8 lai5 lian7-tat8, kau3 oe7 hun-piat ho2 phaiN2.

[6]	Sou2-i2 Ki-tok to7-li2 e5 chhou-hak8, lan2 chhiaN2 pang3-he7 teh, lai5 ti3-i3 chin3-pou7 kau3 oan5-choan5; bian2 koh he7 toe7-ki, chiu7-si7 hoan2-hoe2 lai5 thoat-li7 chiah e5 si2-si2 e5 sou2 kiaN5, kap sin3 Siong7-te3, chiah e5 soe2 e5 le2-sou3,  2). i2-kip8 hoaN7 chhiu2, si2 lang5 koh-oah8, kap eng2-oan2 e5 sim2-phoaN3, chiah e5 kau3-hun3.  3). Siong7-te3 na7 chun2, goan2 chiu7 beh an2-ni kiaN5.  4). In-ui7 lang5 i2-keng bat tit-tioh8 kng chio3, siu7-tioh8 thiN-nih8 e5 un-su3, u7 hun7 ti7 Seng3 Sin5,  5). koh siu7-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 ho2 oe7, kap teh lai5 e5 se3-kai3 chiah e5 koan5-leng5,  6). au7-lai5 poe7-poan7, chiu7 boe7-oe7 hou7 in koh oaN7-sin lai5 hoan2-hoe2; in-ui7 in ka-ki7 koh teng3 Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3, lai5 hian2-beng5 leng5-jiok8 I.  7). 	9). 	13). TaN Siong7-te3 eng3-un2 A-pek-lah8-han2 e5 si5, in-ui7 bo5 khah toa7 e5 thang ki2 lai5 chiu3-choa7, chiu7 ki2 pun2-sin lai5 chiu3-choa7, kong2,  14).
