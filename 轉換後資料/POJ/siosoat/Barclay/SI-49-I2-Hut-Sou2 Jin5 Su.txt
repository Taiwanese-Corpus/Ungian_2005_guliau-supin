	11). [3]	In-ui7 an2-ni, goa2 Po2-lo5, ui7-tioh8 lin2 goa7-pang lang5 choe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 siu5-hoan7,   2). lin2 na7 bat thiaN-kiN3 Siong7-te3 ui7-tioh8 lin2 sou2 siuN2-su3 goa2 un-tian2 e5 chit-hun7,  3). chiu7-si7 eng7 khe2-si7 hou7 goa2 chai hit e5 o3-biau7, chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 i2-keng u7 tai7-liok8 sia2 e5,  4). lin2 thak8 i, chiu7 oe7 hiau2-tit goa2 chai-iaN2 Ki-tok e5 o3-biau7,  5). si7 cheng5 tai7 be7 bat hou7 lang5 chai e5, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 hian7-kim ti7 Seng3 Sin5 khe2-si7 I chiah e5 seng3 e5 su3-tou5 sian-ti chit8 iuN7,  6). chiu7-si7 goa7-pang lang5 ti7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou, tui3 hok-im, tang5 choe3 hou7-su5, tang5 choe3 chit8 e5 sin-the2, saN-kap u7 hun7 ti7 sou2 eng3-un2 e5;  7). goa2 choe3 chit e5 hok-im e5 chhe-eng7, chiau3 Siong7-te3 un-tian2 e5 siuN2-su3, chiu7-si7 chiau3 I e5 koan5-leng5 sou2 un7-heng5 lai5 siuN2-su3 goa2 e5,  8).
