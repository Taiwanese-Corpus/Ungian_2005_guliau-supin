[1]	Tui3 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3 choe3 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 su3-tou5 Po2-lo5, kap hiaN-ti7 The5-mou5-thai3, phoe kia3 hou7 Siong7-te3 e5 kau3-hoe7 ti7 Ko-lim5-to e5, i2-kip8 pian3 A-kai-a long2-chong2 e5 seng3-tou5;  2). goan7 lin2 tui3 lan2 e5 Pe7 Siong7-te3 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok tit-tioh8 un-tian2 peng5-an.
	3). Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 Siong7-te3, ia7 si7 I e5 Pe7, chiu7-si7 lin5-bin2 e5 Pe7, siuN2-su3 tak8 iuN7 an-ui3 e5 Siong7-te3, tit-tioh8 o-lo2.  4). 	12). [3]	Goan2 kiam2 si7 chit tiap8 chiah koh ka-ki7 ku2-chian3 mah?
