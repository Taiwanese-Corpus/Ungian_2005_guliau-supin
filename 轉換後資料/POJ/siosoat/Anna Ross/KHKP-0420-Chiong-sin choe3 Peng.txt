

Chiong-sin choe3 Peng
Anna Ross sia2: Tan5 Leng5-thong ek8
Tui3 chiah e5 thak8 chit e5 sit8-chai8 e5 kou3-su7 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5, goa2 ai3 kong2-beng5 goa2 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 sia2 chit phiN.  Goa2 u7 nng7 hang7 e5 in-toaN.  Thau5 chit8 hang7, chit e5 15 he3 e5 gin2-a2 chi3-goan7 chit8-si3-lang5 beh choe3 Ia5-sou e5 peng, goa2 ng3-bang7 li2 siu7 kam2-tong7, chiong li2 ka-ki7 ia7 chi3-goan7 choe3 Ia5-sou e5 peng.  Te7 ji7 hang7, goa2 ng3-bang7 li2 thak8 chit e5 kou3-su7 liau2-au7 Seng3 Sin5 beh pang-chan7 li2 chai tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 choe3.  
Ti7 chit te7 ji7-chap8 se3-ki2, lan2 e5 Ong5 Ia5-sou Ki-tok u7 chit8 e5 toa7 e5 chian3-cheng, I u7 khiam3-eng7 chit8 tui7 toa7 tui7 siau3-lian5 e5 peng.  I sit8-chai7 chin gau5 eng7 siau3-lian5 e5 lang5.  Ui-liam5 si7 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 gin2-a2, i 15 he3 put-chi2 toa7-han3, seng-khu san2-san2, sou2-i2 i e5 hak8-seng phoaN7 kio3 i Kut-thau5.  I chhut-si3 chiu7 chhin-chhiuN7 peng e5 khoan2, sou2-i2 in na7 ai3 thit-tho5 chhin-chhiuN7 peng e5 khoan2, i put-si5 hou7 lang5 keng2 choe3 tui7-tiuN2, ia7 chheng7 ku7 e5 peng saN.  I eng7 chin ho2 e5 beng7-leng7 hou7 i e5 peng-tui7 chin3 kun chhau-lian7.  Chit e5 Ui-liam5 chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok sou2 toa7 him-bou7 e5 peng e5 chit8-e5.  I put-chi2 ai3 chi3-goan7 choe3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 peng, chong2--si7 m7 chai tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 choe3.  M7-ku2 i u7 chit8 e5 hak8-seng-phoaN7 bo7-choe3 Iok-han7, Iok-han7 u7 chit8 e5 toa7 hiaN jiam2-tioh8 hi3-piN7 to2 ti7 piN7 chhng5-nih8.  Chit e5 toa7 hiaN chai chit e5 si2-sit u7 na2 tit8-tit8 pek-oa2 ti7 i e5 sin-khu, i chiu7 oat8 to3-tng2 te3 Ia5-sou, ia7 chi3-goan7 choe3 chit e5 toa7 e5 Tui7-tiuN2 e5 peng.  In-ui7 li2 chai Ia5-sou m7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit se3-kan e5 peng-tui7.  I iu5-goan5 chin hoaN-hi2 tiau3 u7-piN7 e5 lang5 choe3 i e5 peng, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 ho2 lang5 chit8 poaN-iuN7.  Ia7 sit8-chai7 u7-e5 phoaN3-piN7 iau2-ku2 choe3 chin ho2 e5 peng, chhin-chhiuN7 chit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 an2-ni.  
Iok-han7 e5 toa7-hiaN to2-teh sau3 kau3 beh si2, taN i beh ui7-tioh8 i sin e5 Chu2-lang5 choe3 sim2-mih8?  I chiu7 thoe3 i e5 sio2-ti7 Iok-han7 ki5-to2, un-sun5 in2-chhoa7 i chhin-kun7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok.  Bo5 joa7-ku2 i iu5-goan5 siu7 tiau3 choe3 chit khoan2 e5 chhe-eng7 chhin-chhiuN7 i e5 hiaN e5 khoan2.  TaN Iok-han7 beh chaiN2-iuN7 choe3?  I put-chi2 tui3-tiong7 Kut-thau5, in si7 peng5-iu2.  Iok-han7 siuN7 beh chhi3-khoaN3 khi3 in2-chhoa7 i.  In chit8 
pai2 pai3-lak8 e7-pou saN-kap choe3 tin7, Iok-han7 eng7 long2-chong2 e5 su7 ka7 Ui-liam5 kong2, lun7 khi2 i e5 toa7-hiaN kap i pun2-sin e5 su7, ia7 chiong chit-e5 chi2-si7 i hou7 i iu5-goan5 thang lai5 lian5-hap8 ti7 Ki-tok e5 peng-tui7.  
Iok-han7 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 put-chi2 chhi3-chhak8-tioh8 Ui-liam5.  I hit am3 to3-khi3 chhu3-nih8 siuN7 kau3 i e5 sim chhin-chhiuN7 he2 teh toh8.  Goa2 ai3 choe3 Ki-tok e5 peng, eng7 goa2 it-seng choe3 i e5 chhe-eng7. Chong2--si7 i m7 chai tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 choe3, ia7 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 choe3 khi2.  I chai i si7 u7 choe7 e5 lang5,boe7 kham-tit choe3 chit khoan2 seng3 e5 chhe-eng7, na7-si7 Iok-han7 kong2 Ia5-sou in-ui7 u7 choe7 e5 lang5 lai5 se3-kan.  I chai tioh8 thak8 Seng3-keng, m7 ku2 thak8 liau2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 choe3 i e5 li7-ek. I chai tioh8 ki5-to2, sou2-i2 i chiu7 ki5-to2, chong2--si7 Siong7-te3 chhin-chhiuN7 li7-khui i hng7-hng7.  Ti7 joah8-thiN te2-te2 e5 mi5-si5, i an2-ni siuN7, hoan-lai5 hoan-khi3 ia7 thong mi5 ki5-to2, chong2--si7 kau3 thiN tu2-a2 kng e5 si5 i chiu7 khi2-lai5 chheng7 saN.  I beh khi3 chhe7 Iok-han7, ng3-bang7 khoaN3 i oe7 pang-chan7 i a2-boe7?  Chong2--si7 i kau3 Iok-han7 e5 chhu3 e5 si5, i khoaN3-kiN3 ian-tang5 bo5 chhut he2-hun.  I chiu7 khoaN3 si3-ui5 ia7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 ki ian-tang5 chhut he2-hun. I chai si7 siuN cha2 iau2-be7 u7 lang5 khi2-lai5. M7 ku2 i m7 to3-tng2-khi3; i tiam3 ho5-kiN5 kiaN5 chiuN7 kiaN5 loh8, kau3 i khoaN3-kiN3 Iok-han7 e5 chhu3 teh chhut he2-hun.  Au7--lai5 Iok-han7 chhut-lai5 chiu7 Ui-liam5 chiong i e5 kan-khou2 ka7 i kong2.  Iok-han7 chhi3-khoaN3 oe7 pang-chan7 i boe7, chong2--si7 i siuN7 lai5 siuN7 khi3, kan-ta put-si5 chhe7 chhut chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 i e5 peng5-iu2 chhin-chhiuN7 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 chin chhian2-hian7 e5 su7, ia7 i boe7 oe7 hou7 i tit-tioh8 khoaN3-kiN3.  
Au7--lai5 Iok-han7 kong2, Ui-liam5, li2 tiam3 chit peng5 ho5-kiN5, ia7 goa2 lai5 khi3 hit peng5. Li2 thoe3 goa2 ki5-to2 hou7 goa2 thang tit-tioh8 tu2-ho2 e5 oe7 hou7 li2, goa2 iu5-goan5 in-ui7 chit hang7 lai5 ki5-to2.  Goa2 na7 tit-tioh8 e5 si5 chiu7 to3-tng2 lai5 ka7 li2 kong2.  Sou2-i2 chit nng7 e5 gin2-a2 saN pun-khui, chit8 lang5 chit8 peng5 tiam3 ho5-kiN5 kiaN5 khi2 kiaN5 loh8.  Ui-liam5 thoe3 Iok-han7 ki5-to2, hou7 i oe7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 sou2 beh hou7 Ui-liam5 e5 oe7.  Bo5 joa7-ku2 Iok-han7 e5 bin7 kng-kng tui3 ho5-nih8 chiu7 ke3-lai5.  I kong2, Ui-liam5, goa2 u7 tit-tioh8 sou2 beh hou7 li2 e5 oe7, Ui-liam5 chin chu3-i3 thiaN.  Iok-han7 chiu7 kong2: Che chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7: Lan2 long2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 sit-be5 e5 iuN5.  An2-ni si7 sit8-chai7 mah? Ui-liam5 kong2, Si7, an2-ni u7 iaN2, goa2 taN khoai3-khoai3 beng5-pek8.  Lan2 tak8 lang5 long2 
hiong3 lan2 ka-ki7 e5 lou7 khi3 kiaN5, che u7 iaN2 mah?  Ui-liam5 koh in3 kong2, Tioh8, goa2 it-seng long2 si7 chiau3 goa2 ka-ki7 e5 lou7 khi3 kiaN5.  Au7--lai5 Iok-han7 ban7-ban7 kong2 lou7-be2 ku3 kong2, Siong7-te3 eng7 lan2 it-chhe3 e5 choe7-koa3 he7 ti7 Ia5-sou, an2-ni u7-iaN2 mah? Hit ji7 he7--ji7 hut-jian5 hou7 Ui-liam5 thiaN-liau2 chin beng5.  Siong7-te3 chiong goa2 long2-chong2 e5 choe7-koa3 he7 ti7 Ia5-sou, choe7 koa3 he7 ti7 I ia7 li7-khui goa2.  Ui-liam5 teh thiaN ia7 an2-ni teh siuN7.  
Iok-han7 koh mng7 kong2, Ui-liam5, an2-ni u7-iaN2 mah?  Ui-liam5 in3 kong2, Si7, si7, an2-ni sit8-chai7 u7-iaN2.  Siong7-te3 eng7 goa2 long2-chong2 e5 choe7 he7 ti7 Ia5-sou, choe7-koa3 si7 he7 ti7 i ia7 li7-khui goa2.  Goa2 taN chu7-iu5 lah, eng2-oan2 chu7-iu5 thang choe3 I e5 chhe-eng7.  Ia7 Ui-liam5 chin7 jiat8-sim choe3 i sin e5 Chu2-lang5 e5 chhe-eng7, chiu7-si7 eng7 ke3-chiN5 boe2-e5.  I khun5-khun5 aN3 hi7-khang thiaN Ia5-sou e5 ka3-si7, ia7 chin7-tiong choe3 kang koh ti3-i3 ai3 in2-chhoa7 pat8 lang5 lai5 kui oa2 Chu2.  Ui-liam5 iau2-be7 siu7 tiau3 to3-tng2 khi3 thian-kok khah bo5 chit8 ni5 i2-cheng5, goa2 kap i hoaN-hi2 choe3 tin7 chit8 le2-pai3 ku2, hit si5 iok-liok8 37 he3.  I chiu7-si7 ka7 goa2 kong2 chit e5 kou3-su7 e5 lang5.  Goa2 ia7 chin hoaN-hi2 chiong chit-e5 ka7 lin2 kong2.    
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 420 koan3, 1920 ni5 3 geh8)
