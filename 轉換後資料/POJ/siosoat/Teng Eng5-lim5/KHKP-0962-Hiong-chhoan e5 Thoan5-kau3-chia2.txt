
Hiong-chhoan e5 Thoan5-kau3-chia2 
Teng Eng5-lim5
Le2-pai3 e5 jin5-sou3 tit8-tit8 kiam2-chio2, chu7-hoe7 chiam7-chiam7 leng2-tam7.
－Chit keng hiong-chhoan e5 T.M. kau3-hoe7 ti7 chiaN5 chap8 ni5 cheng5 hou7 lang5 khoaN3 choe3 si7 hoe7-iu2 choe7, keng-che3 ho2, sin3-tou5 jiat8-sim e5 kau3-hoe7; na7-si7 kun7-lai5 hian2-jian5 teh soe-the3 lah.
Lim5 Tiong-iong5 Thoan5-to7 kui7 ti7 pai3-tng5 e5 kang2-tai5 cheng5 teh ki5-to2; kau-chip8 ti7 i sim-lai7 e5 thong3-khou2 thang tui3 chhui3-nih e5 thou2-khui3 chham2-chhoeh, sim7-chi3 lau5 bak8-sai2 e5 siaN piau2-hian7 chhut-lai5.  Ki5-to2 soah, chhin-chhiuN7 chin ia3-sian7 e5 khoan2-sit, jip8-lai5 che7 ti7 thak8-chheh ui7, chhih8 toh-teng2 e5 jit8-kong-teng toh8, chhiu2 lam2-heng, thau5-khak kiah8 koaiN5 teh tim5-su; chit8-bou7 chit8-bou7 e5 hoe5-ek tui3 i e5 nau2-lai7 chau2 ke3-khi3.
Nng7-ni5 cheng5: I chhoa7 hu7-jin5-lang5 Siok-cheng kap nng7 e5 kiaN2-ji5 Keng3-it, Sin3-it lai5 kau3 chit keng kau3-hoe7 hu3-jim7.  Hu-hu7 long2 phau7 tioh8 moa2-hoai5 e5 jiat8-cheng5 beh ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang, su7-sit8 ia7 si7 an2-ni; in u7 choan5-sim piaN3-mia7 ti7 sou2 kau-tai7 e5 chit-hun7.  I tek8-piat8 u7 pou3-to7 e5 un-su3, sou2-i2 tui3 goa7 e5 kang-chok; bo5-lun7 tai7-chiong3 pou3-to7, a2-si7 ko3-jin5 pou3-to7 long2 chin7 sou2 oe7 e5 teh choe3.  Am3-si5 na7 bo5 chu7-hoe7, chiu7 chio hoe7-iu2, tiong2-chip khi3 tak8 chng-sia7 pou3-to7, choe3 hoan3-teng, pun pou3-to7-toaN.  U7 si5 i ka-ki7 chit8 e5 lang5 khi3 kap hiah e5 boe7 ioh8-a2 e5 ong5-lok8-sian che7, ti7 koe-lou7 piN kang2-kai2 hok-im,chio lang5 lai5 tit peng5-an ioh8, hit e5 jiat8-cheng5 bo5 khah su hiah e5 lang5.  Jit8-si5 ti7 lou7-nih, a2-si7 kok ka-teng5 khi3 kap sia7-hoe7 kok-sek, kok-iuN7 e5 bi5-sin3-chia2 chiap-chhiok tam5-to7 ...... I an2-ni choe3, chiu7-si7 i chai-iaN2 che si7 kin-a2-jit8 hiong-chhoan kau3-hoe7 ban2-kiu3 the3-pou7 e5 i5-it hong-hoat.  U7-iaN2! lang5 sui-jian5 ngi7-sim bo5 hiah khoai3 lai5 sin3, m7-ku2 u7 choe3 kang pit-teng7 u7 siu-seng5, ti7 chit8 nng7 ni5 bo5 ia3-sian7 e5 kang-chok ha7, ia7 tit-tioh8 5 hou7 e5 choan5-ke kap kui2-na7 e5 chheng-lian5 lai5 bou7-to7, u7 e5 ia7 siu7 soe2-le2, na7-si7 an2-ni iau2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang eng3-hu3 tou-chhi7 e5 siau-lou7(供不應求).  Nng7 ni5 cheng5 i lai5 hu3-jim7 e5 si5 kau3-hoe7 u7 30 hou7, taN u7 ke-thiN 5  
hou7, eng-kai si7 35 hou7 chiah tioh8, m7-ku2 sit8-chai7 chiah chhun 20 goa7 hou7 na7-tiaN7, sou2 bo5 khi3 e5 lang5 tong-jian5 long2 si7 siu7 si5-tai7 e5 kip pian3, kang-giap8 sia7-hoe7 e5 eng2-hiong2 lau5 chhut-khi3 ti7 goa7-bin7 lah; ji5-chhiaN2 chia e5 lang5 toa7-pou7-hun7 si7 khah jiat8-sim, keng-che3 ia7 pi2-kau3 khah ho2 e5 lang5.  Chhin-chhiuN7 choe3 I-seng e5 Lau5 TiuN2-lo2; choe3 toa7 su7-giap8 e5 Tan5 TiuN2-lo2; choe3 seng-li2 e5 Ong5 Chip-su7, a2-si7 kong-bu7-oan5 Sim2 Kho-tiuN2, Lou5 Lau7-su teng2-teng2, in long2 si7 kau3-hoe7 e5 toa7 thiau7-chioh8.  In e5 li7-khui na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 keng chhu3 e5 tong3-liong5 chiam7-chiam7 siu7 thiah chau2 chit8-iuN7.  Sui-jian5 in ti7 tou-chhi7 e5 kau3-hoe7 iu5-goan5 ia7 ke3-siok8 in e5 jiat8-sim, na7-si7 chhan5-chng e5 kau3-hoe7 na2 lai5 na2 giam5-tiong7.  Le2-pai3-jit8 khoaN3-kiN3 chheng-chheng(清清) ban7-ban7 (慢慢) e5 hoe7-iu2 teh choe3 le2-pai3, lai7-bin7 40 liau5 i2-a2 kau3-giah8 hou7 chit8 lang5 che7 chit8 liau5 koh u7 chhun.  I siu-eng2 bun5-nga2 e5 kang2-to7 hong-sek, boe7 thang chhi3-kek chiah e5 lau7-lang5 kap hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 siau-hoa3; u7 si5 chi2-u7 chiaN5 choe3 chit8 tiuN5 e5 chhui-bin5-khiok chhiuN3 hou7 in an-bin5 jip8 thian-kok na7-tiaN7; hoe7-iu2 chit8 phian3 jiat8-seng5 e5 he2 jip8-lai5 keng3-pai3, hoan2-tng2 toa3 tioh8 chit8 te3 leng2-tam7 e5 peng tng2-khi3.  In es5in-sim kiam2-chhai2 chiu7 ti7 chit khoan2 e5 khong-khi3 tiong leng2-tam7 lah.
Kun7-lai5 i chai-iaN2 hoe7-iu2 chiam7-chiam7 teh bo5 hoan-geng5 i.  che thang tui3 ti7 ki5-to2-hoe7 in e5 ki5-to2 khoaN3--chhut-lai5:“ Siong7-te3 ah! kiu5 Li2 e5 Seng3 Sin5 chhiong-moa2 Li2 e5 kang-lang5, hou7 i gau5 choe3 kang,hou7 goan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 chhin-chhiuN7 i2-cheng5 e5 khoan2-sit ...........”
Kim e5-am3 si7 i choe3 kan-khou2 sim, choe3 siu7 chhi3-kek e5 chit8 am3, in-ui7 ki5-to2-hoe7 soah,ni5 lau7 e5 Bok8 TiuN2-lo2 khoaN3 tioh8 ki5-to2-hoe7 e5 jin5-sou3 chio2 kau3 chhiN-chhin3, theh8 chhut i e5 cheng3-gi7-kam2, tng i e5 bin7-cheng5 bo5 soe3-ji7 kong2:“Sian-siN ah! khoaN3-mai7 leh, chiah-nih chio2 lang5 teh chu7-hoe7, li2 kiam2 bo5 khoaN3-tioh8?” “Goa2 chai, na7-si7 goa2 pi2 lin2 khah kan-khou2 sim, goa2 peng3 m7-si7 bo5 chin7 goa2 e5 khui3-lat8,chong2-si7 m7-chai goa2 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 choe3?” “Goan2 e5 iau-kiu5 si7: chi2 iau3 li2 hoe5-hok8 chhin-chhiuN7 nng7 ni5 cheng5 e5 cheng5-heng5 chiu7 ho2.”“Bok8 TiuN2-lo2, kau3-hoe7 soe-the3 e5 goan5-in, m7-thang long2-chong2 koai3 goa2, che si7 si5-tai7 e5 hian7-siong7, kin-a2-jit8 e5 hiong-chhoan kau3-hoe7 tai7-pou7-hun7 long2 siang7 khoan2 e5 keng2-gu7.  Kau3-hoe7 pun2-lai5 hiah e5 toa7-thiau7 long2 chhut-khi3 lah, sin lai5 e5 hoe7-iu2 beh cho7-chiu7 kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 hiah e5 lang5, m7-si7 chin khoai3 e5 su7.”“Che si7 su7-sit8, goa2 m7-kaN2 hou2-jin7; m7-ku2 lan2 khoaN3 cha2-si5 e5  
thoan5-kau3-chia2 ui7 sim2-mih8 oe7 thang hou7 kau3-hoe7 tit-tioh8 heng-ong7, ji5 kin-a2-jit8 e5 thoan5-to7-chia2 boe7 tang3 nih? Goa2 e5 i3-su3, si7 lan2 eng-kai tioh8 koh hun3-hoat chiah tioh8.”“To-sia7, goa2 chiah chin7 sou2 oe7 e5 lai5 phah-piaN3, chhiaN2 lin2 ia7 kap goa2 hap8-chok.”

Hut-jian5 i thou2 chit8 e5 toa7-khui3 e5 siaN kiaN-chhiN2 teh khun3 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 Siok-cheng.“EN5! Tiong-iong5, li2 iau2-be7 khun3, hian7-chai7 i2-keng si7 poaN3-mi5 lah.”  Siok-cheng tui3 bin5-chhng5 peh khi2-lai5, lai5 che7 ti7 i e5 sin-piN, koh kong2, “U7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 hou7 li2 sit-bin5?”“Siok-cheng, goa2 siuN7 beh li7-khui thoan5-to7 e5 kang.”“Li2 m7-thang ui7-tioh8 Bok8 TiuN2-lo2 sou2 kong2 hiah e5 oe7 lai5 sit-chi3.”  Siok-cheng an-ui3 i kong2, “Bok8 TiuN2-lo2 e5 oe7 sui-jian5 u7 khah bo5 kheh-khi3, m7-ku2 i sou2 kong2 e5 peng3 m7-si7 m7-tioh8.  I si7 lau7 toa7-lang5, lan2 si7 chheng-lian5 lang5, i tui3 lan2 e5 keng2-ko3, si7 hou7 lan2 khah oe7 kin2-sin7 kap kek-khi2 lan2 choe3 kang e5 jiat8-seng5, che tui3 lan2 si7 u7 li7-ek e5.”“Goa2 m7-si7 siong-sim Bok8 TiuN2-lo2 e5 oe7, goa2 si7 kam2-kak goa2 bo5 kau3 khui3-lat8 thang hou7 Chu2 eng7, m7-thang khang chiam3 thou2-toe7, put-ju5 li7-khui kau3-kai3 lai5 khi3 choe3 pat8 khoan2 e5 kang-chok.  Ia7 eng7 lan2 sou2 choe3 oe7 tit kau3 e5 si5-kan kap kim-chiN5 lai5 hong7-hian3 hou7 Chu2, che kap choe3 thoan5-to7 piN5-piN5 siang7 khoan2, ji5-chhiaN2 tui3 Siong7-te3 kap lang5 long2 khah peng5-an.”“An2-ni, li2 si7 m7 kam-goan7 ka7 Chu2 choe3 kang, beh to5-pi7 su3-beng7 si7 bo5?”“M7-si7, goa2 chin chu7-kak; goan7 choe3 lou5-pok8 tioh8 siu7 lang5 siat-tok8 chit ku3 oe7.”“Li2 e5 oe7 u7 mau5-tun2.”  Siok-cheng chhio3-chhio3 kong2, “Li2 ki3-jian5 goan7 choe3 lou5-pok8,lim chit e5 khou2-poe, ui7 sim2-mih8 iu7-koh siuN7 beh li7-khui su3-beng7 khi3 choe3 pat8 khoan2 e5 kang?” “In-ui7 goa2 kam2-kak boe7 thang eng5-kng Siong7-te3 li7-ek lang5 ah.”“Na7-si7 an2-ni bo5 iau3-kin2, lan2 e5 Chu2 si7 ai3 mng7 lan2 u7 teh choe3 bo5? I bo5 mng7 choe3 liau2 an2-choaN2, chi2 iau3 lan2 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7-cheng5 bo5 kian3-siau3 I  e5 kang, an2-ni chiu7 ho2.  Chong2-si7 ka2-su2 lan2 na7 siam2-pi7 chit e5 su3-beng7, it-teng7 boe7 thang tit-tioh8 chiok-hok.  Goa2 siong5-siong5 thiaN kong2 i2-cheng5 poan3-tou5 ji5-hoe3 e5 kang-lang5, kau3 be2 long2 hou7 Siong7-te3 bo5 hoaN-hi2, tu2-tioh8 bo5 ho2 kiat-ko2.”“Siok-cheng, li2 e5 kian3-kai2 u7 tam7-poh8 chho3-gou7, li2 kong2 na7 bo5 choe3 thoan5-to7, Siong7-te3 bo5 chiok-hok, goa2 bo5 chan3-seng5 li2 chit ku3 oe7.  “Thoan5-to7” chit  
nng7 ji7 m7-si7 pau-ham5 u7 chip-chiau3 e5 bok8-su, thoan5 to7 na7-tiaN7.  Hian7-chai7 lan2 teh kiong5-tiau “Sin3-tou5 kai-peng,” “Kang-giap8 chit-hek8 thoan5-to7.”  Chit e5 i3-su3 si7 kong2, “Mui2 chit8 e5 hoe7-iu2 long2 si7 thoan5-to7-jin5.”  Choe3 chit8 e5 kau3-chit-chia2 ia7 m7-si7 chi2-u7 tak8 jit8 koaiN ti7 pai3-tng5, chhin-chhiuN7 bio7-kong chu2-chhi5 le2-pai3, chu7-hoe7, sun5 hoe7-iu2 na7-tiaN7, ki5-thaN e5 jit8 eng5-eng5 bo5 choe3 su7.  Kin-a2-jit8 e5 sin3-tou5 eng-kai tioh8 khia7 ti7 ka-ki7 e5 kong-ui7 siong7 lai5 choe3 kng, choe3 iam5 e5 su3-beng7, kian3-cheng3 Ki-tok.  Sou2-i2 goa2 siuN7 hiah e5 peng5-si5 ti7 sia7-hoe7 teh hok8-bu7 jin5-kun5, ia7 siang7-si5 ti7 kau3-hoe7 teh choe3 kang e5 sin3-tou5 chiah si7 si5-tai7 e5 thoan5-to7-chia2.  Na7 an2-ni beh an2-choaN2 kong2 Siong7-te3 bo5 hoaN-hi2, hoan2-tng2 si7 hou7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chiok-hok chiah tioh8; su7-sit8 goa2 ma7 khoaN3 chit khoan2 e5 lang5 chin-chiaN3 hou7 Siong7-te3 chiok-hok e5 lang5 chin-choe7.  put-ko3 ia7 u7 chin-choe7 li7-khui kau3-kai3 liau2-au7, soa3 li7-khui Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, goa2 to m7-kaN2 kong2.”“Goa2 chan3-seng5 li2 chiaN5-choe3 chit e5 si5-tai7 e5 thoan5-kau3-chia2, na7-si7 bo5 chan3-seng5 li2 li7-khui kau3-kai3, in-ui7 goa2 kiaN-liau2 li2 chit khoan2 e5 li2-siong2, kau3 u7 chit8 jit8 phian-tiong7 ti7 se3-kan lai5 li7-khui Siong7-te3 ia7 bo5 tek-khak.”“Ho2! goa2 chai li2 e5 i3-su3, goa2 goan7 pe7 chit ki si5-tai7 e5 sip8-ji7-ke3.”  Tiong-iong5 hut-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 nng7 e5 kiaN2-ji5 khun3 kau3 chin sui2, soa3 kong2, “M7-ku2 lan2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 chiong-lai5 m7-thang koh hou7 in u7 hun7 chit e5 kan-khou2; goa2 hian7-chai7 chiah chai, ui7 sim2-mih8 chin-choe7 thoan5-kau3-chia2 e5 chu2-te7 chin chio2 lang5 ke3-seng5 chit e5 kang-chok e5 goan5-in lah.”“Soe3-kiaN2 iau2-be7 toa7-han3, lan2 m7-chai Siong7-te3 chiong-lai5 ju5-ho5 ka7 in an-pai5, lan2 boe7 thang kong2 sim2-mih8; put-ko3 bo5-lun7 in chiong-lai5 u7 choe3 thoan5-to7-chia2, a2-si7 choe3 pat8 khoan2 e5 kang, lan2 ti7 chit e5 si5 long2 tioh8 ho2-ho2 chai-poe5 in tui3 Siong7-te3 eng-kai u7 e5 su3-beng7-kam2 chiah tioh8.”
Siok-cheng kong2 liau2, Tiong-iong5 put-chi2 kam2-kek i e5 sin3-sim, tui3 i e5 chhiu2 khan--leh, nng7 lang5 chiu7 kui7 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2 ki5-to2.
Ki5-to2 soah, khoaN3 goa7-bin7 e5 thang-a2, tui3 tang-peng5 sia7-jip8 chit8 tiau5 kng-soaN3, chiah chai thiN i2-keng kng lah.
