兒童宗教故事
CHOK-CHIA2 E5 OE7
Chit e5 kou3-su7 si7 kin-ku3 Ma2-thai3 25: 40, ai3 beh ka3-si7 soe3-kiaN2 m7-thang khoaN3-khin pi-bi5 e5 lang5, tioh8 u7 thiaN3-sim lai5 thiaN3 hiah e5 loan2-jiok8 bo5 i-oa2 e5 lang5.
Ng3-bang7 Seng3-keng kho, Seng3-si kho e5 hak8-seng i2-kip8 kok-ui7 kau3-oan5 long2 oe7-thang tit-tioh8 chit8-pun2,ka-teng5 e5 pe7-bu2 ia7 thang thak8, soah lai5 ka3-si7 in e5 soe3-kiaN2.
Sou2 u7 e5 tou5-oe7 si7 pai3-thok chit8-ui7 chi2-moe7 lai5 oe7--e5 toa7-toa7 soe3-sia7 i.
Chit e5 si7 kek8 bi5-soe3 e5 chheh, iau2-ku2 ng3-bang7 u7 hun7 ti7 Chu2 e5 kok e5 kang lai5 hou7 che7-che7 soe3-kiaN2 tit-tioh8 pang-chan7.
  ChhiaN2 chham-kho2 i2-ha7 e5 Seng3-keng.

Ng5 Hoai5-un.1955, 9, 22.

Ma2-li7 si7 chit8 e5 chin kho2-ai3 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2.  I chhut-si3 bo5 loa7-ku2 i e5 lau7-pe7 chiu7 li7-khui se3-kan.  Sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 chi2-u7 chit8-e5 lau7-bu2 thang oa2-kho3 na7-tiaN7.
  In-ui7 i e5 lau7-pe7 lau7-bu2 si7 chhut-goa7 lang5, li7-khui kou3-hiong hng7-hng7 lai5 kau3 lam5-pou7 e5 chit8-e5 tou-chhi7 teh than3-chiah8.  I e5 lau7-pe7 si7 chit8 e5 put-chi2 toa7-keng koh chhut-mia5 e5 kang-chhiuN2 e5 kang-oan5.  Ma2-li7 e5 lau7-pe7 si2 e5 si5 Ma2-li7 iau2-ku2 si7 chit8 e5 moa2 2 hoe3 e5 se3-han3 eN-a2 na7-tiaN7.  Sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 long2 m7-bat i e5 lau7-pe7 si7 siN-cho3 sim2-mih8 khoan2.  Chi2-u7 tui3 sia2-chin kap tui3 Ma2-li7 e5 lau7-bu2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 lai5 chai i e5 lau7-pe7 na7-tiaN7. 
  Ma2-li7 e5 lau7-bu2 tak8-me5-hng pho7 Ma2-li7 ti7 i e5 heng-cheng5 lai5 kong2-khi2 i e5 lau7-pe7 e5 su7 hou7 i thiaN.  U7-si5 kong2-kau3 lau7-bu2 ka-ki7 lau5 bak8-sai2.  Ma2-li7 e5 lau7-bu2 tui3 i sou2 oa2-kho3 e5 tiong7-hu si2 liau2-au7 chiu7 ka-ki7 thoa-boa5, u7-si5 ka7 lang5 se2-saN, u7-si5 ti7 chhu3-lai7 cho3-saN chiong2-chiong2 e5 lo5-khou2 lai5 teh seng-oah8, pang-chan7 Ma2-li7.  Lau7-bu2 m7-kam hou7 Ma2-li7 tu2-tioh8 iau-go7 a2-si7 bo5 kau3-giah8 saN thang chheng7.  Sou2-i2 chit e5 kho2-lin5 e5 koaN2-hu7 bo5-lun7 hong-thai a2-si7 loh8-hou7, bo5-lun7 koaN5-joah8 long2 tioh8 pho7 Ma2-li7 tak8-si3-koe3 khi3 ka7 lang5 se2-saN.  U7-si5 chin bo5 khui3-lat8 chhin-chhiuN7 beh to2-loh8-khi3, u7-si5 siuN7-kau3 chian5-tou5 e5 hng7 chiu7 chin choat8-bang7, iau2-ku2 na7 siuN7-tioh8 iu3-iu3 e5 Ma2-li7, koh siuN7-tioh8 i e5 tiong7-hu lim5-chiong beh-si2 e5 si5 sou2 kong2 e5oe, “Ka-lian5, li2 na7 u7 thiaN3 goa2, li2 tioh8 m7-thang pang3-sak Ma2-li7, li2 tioh8 iong2-kam2 lai5 po2-hou7 Ma2-li7,hou7 i m7-bian2 tu2-tioh8 bo5-pe7 e5 chhiN-chham2.”  Hit e5 si5 chit e5 kho2-lin5 e5 koaN2-hu7 tioh8 chin7 i e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 thoa-boa5 cho3-kang, hou7 ma2-li7 oe7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 pat8-e5 gin2-na2 chit8-iuN7 u7 beng5-long2 e5 bin7, u7 sui2 e5 saN thang chheng7, bo5 tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 kan-khou2.
  Sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 ti7 chit e5 gau5 thiaN3 i, gau5 chiau3-kou3 i e5 lau7-bu2 e5 heng-cheng5-lai7 kam2-kak hui-siong5 heng7-hok, i long2 bo5 u7 sim2-mih8 khiam3-khoeh, in-ui7 i e5 lau7-bu2 gau5 kiong-kip i.  I e5 bin7 moa2-moa2 si7 hoaN-hi2, i e5 bak8-chiu chhin-chhiuN7 chhiN-lek8-sek e5 ou5-chui2, chin-sui2.  I e5 chhui3 chin kou2-chui, siong5-siong5 teh gim5-si,sui-jian5 si7 bo5 pe7 kiaN2, iau2-ku2 lang5-lang5 long2 chin thiaN3 i.
Ma2-li7 ti7 chit-khoan2 e5 khoan5-keng2 chiam7-chiam7 toa7-han3.  I e5 lau7-bu2 na7 khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 kiaN2 chiah-nih8 kou2-chui, kho2-ai3 e5 si5, i long2 boe7 ki3-tit i e5 kan-khou2 kap chham2-keng2.  I e5 sim-lai7 u7 ng3-bang7 kap hoaN-hi2,chi2-u7 i na7 siuN7-tioh8 si2-khi3 e5 
tiong7-hu e5 si5, chiu7 siuN7 kong2 siat-su2 tiong7-hu na7 bo5 si2, chiu7 i si7 chit8 e5 se3-kan choe3 heng7-hok e5 lang5, lau7-pe7 khoaN3-tioh8 kiaN2 chiah-nih8 sui2 m7-chai beh loa7 hoaN-hi2?
  “Ma2-li7, li2 e5 A-pa ti7 to2-ui7?” ti7 me5-si5 Ka-lian5 na7 pho7-oa2 heng-cheng5 lai5 mng7 e5 si5, Ma2-li7 chiu7 ki2 thiN kong2:
 “Ti7 Ia5-sou hia!”
 “Li2 u7 ai3 khi3 Ia5-sou hia chhoe7 li2 e5 A-pa a2-bo5?” Lau7-bu2 na7 koh mng7, chiu7 kong2: “Ai3”
 “Li2 e5 A-pa chin thiaN3 li2, i ti7 thian-tong5 e5 sou2-chai7 teh theng2-hau7 Ma2-li7, hia long2 bo5 kan-khou2 bo5 si2-sit.  Ia5-sou cho3 ong5 ti7 hia.”
 Ka-lian5 kong2 kau3 chit e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 lau5 bak8-sai2 moa2-bin7.  I siuN7-tioh8 i sou2 thiaN3 e5 tiong7-hu pang3 loan2-jiok8 e5 bu2-kiaN2 ti7 chit e5 chhut-goa7 e5 sou2-chai7,koh siuN7-tioh8 i e5 lo5-khou2 e5 si5, boe7-bian2-tit u7 bak8-sai2.
Iau2-ku2 ti7 tiong7-hu chong3-sek e5 si5 kau3-hoe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7 sou2 gim5 e5 si,
“Long2 bo5 koh saN-li7, long2 bo5 koh saN-li7, long2 bo5 koh saN-li7, ti7 hia long2 bo5 saN-li7,” siong5-siong5 ti7 i e5 sim-lai7 teh gim5, hou7 i cho3 chin-toa7 e5 an-ui3.  I siong5-sin3 u7 chit8-jit8 oe7-thang kap sou2-thiaN3 e5 tiong7-hu saN-kiN3, Ia5-sou ia7 beh chhit-khi3 i e5 bak8-sai2.
Ma2-li7 4 hoe3 e5 si5 Ka-lian5 chiu7 chhoa7 kau3 chit8-keng le2-pai3-tng5 khi3 jip8 Chu2-jit8-oh8.  I chin ng3-bang7 Ma2-li7 oe7-thang tit-tioh8 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 chiok-hok, oe7-thang kiaN5 ti7 sin3-giong2 e5 lou7.  Sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 oe7-hiau2-tit e5 si5 chiu7 chiong Ia5-sou kap thian-tong5 e5 su7 kong2 hou7 ithiaN.  Tak8-me5 na7 Ma2-li7 khun3 liau2 e5 si5, chiu7 kui7 ti7 i e5 bin5-chhng5-cheng5 lai5 ui7-tioh8 Ma2-li7 e5 su7 ki5-to2 Chu2.Ka-lian5 ia7 ka3 Ma2-li7 ki5-to2, sou2-i2 Ma2-li7 tak8 saN-tng3 e5 chiah8-png7 long2 u7 kam2-sia7 ki5-to2.  Ka-lian5 ia7 ka3-si7 Ma2-li7 oe7-hiau2 gim5-si.  Ma2-li7 put-tan3 jiok8-the2 tit-tioh8 lau7-bu2 e5 poe5-iuN2, lian5 i e5 leng5-seng3 ia7 tit-tioh8 i e5 lau7-bu2 e5 poe5-iuN2.
U7 chit8-jit8 Ma2-li7 kap i e5 lau7-bu2 khi3 se2-saN liau2 beh tng2-lai5 e5 si5 ti7 lou7-nih8 khoaN3-tioh8 chit8-tin7 gin2-na2 teh khioh chng-a2 khian3 chit8-e5 kho2-lin5, chhiN-mi5 e5 lau7 A-peh, Ka-lian5 kin2-kin2 chau2 khi3 chou2-tong3 hiah e5 gin2-na2, chiong hit-ui7 lau7 A-peh chhoa7 kau3 an-choan5 e5 sou2-chai7, ia7 chiong Ma2-li7 chhiu2-nih8 sou2-theh8 e5 piaN2 hou7 i.
“Ma2-li7, lan2 m7-thang khi-hu7 kho2-lin5 e5 lang5, tioh8 ai3 pang-chan7 hit-khoan2 khit-chiah8 lang5, na7 bo5 Chu2 Ia5-sou bo5 hoaN-hi2.”  Tng2 lai5 chhu3 e5 si5, Ka-lian5 chiu7 chiong chit e5 su7 lai5 ka3-si7 Ma2-li7.
U7 chit8-jit8 Ma2-li7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 chiah kau2-a2-kiaN2 ti7 chhu3-piN teh hau2, chiu7 pho7 hit-chiah kau2-a2-kiaN2 lai5 hou7 Ka-lian5,
“Ma-ma, chit-chiah kau2-a2-kiaN2 teh hau2 A-bu2, A-bu2, lah!”
“Kho2-lin5 bo5 A-bu2 e5 kau2-a2-kiaN2!  Ma2-li7, li2 khi3 theh8 png7 lai5 hou7 i chiah8, phah-sng3 chin iau.”
Ka-lian5 khoaN3 Ma2-li7 chau2-khi3, sim-lai7 ka-ki7 chin hoaN-hi2 koh kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 hou7 Ma2-li7 u7 chit-chiong2 e5 tong5-cheng5 kap thiaN3-sim.
Ka-lian5 tek8-piat chu3-i3 le2-pai3-jit8 thang chhoa7 Ma2-li7 khi3 kau3 Chu2-jit8-oh8.  Sou2-i2 na7 pai3-lak8 kau3, Ka-lian5 chiu7 ka7 Ma2-li7 soe2 sin-khu, koh pi7-pan7 beh chheng7 e5 saN ho2-se3, hou7 Ma2-li7 ia7 thang hoaN-hi2 lai5 tan2-hau7.
“Ma2-li7, bin5-na2-jit8 si7 le2-pai3-jit8, sou2-i2 li2 tioh8 khah cha2 khi3 khun3, thang chheng7 sui2-saN khi3 le2-pai3.  Li2 u7 hoaN-hi2 bo5?”

“Ma-ma, goa2 chin hoaN-hi2, goan2 lau7-su beh kong2 kou3-su7 hou7 goan2 thiaN.”  Ma2-li7 e5 siaN tek8-piat8 hiang2-liang7.  Ke3-jit8 Ka-lian5 chiu7 chun2-pi7 it-chhe3 ho2-se3, chhoa7 i kau3 ti7 le2-pai3-tng5.  Ma2-li7 ti7 lou7-tiong u7 thiaN-kiN3 pai3-tng5 e5 cheng teh tan5, hou7 i e5 sim ke-thiN hoaN-hi2, ia7 u7 tu2-tioh8 chin-choe7 e5 se3-han3 peng5-iu2 hou7 in e5 A-hiaN chhoa7 lai5 kau3 pai3-tng5.  Tai7-ke long2 chin khoai3-lok8.  Le2-pai3-tng5 koan5-koan5 e5 cheng-lau5 tui3 chin-hng7 chiu7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3, hit e5 teng2-koan5 u7 sip8-ji7-ke3,pai3-tng5 e5 toa7-mng5, sui2 e5 hoe-hng5, chhiN-chhui3 e5 chhiu7-bak8, chheng-khi3 e5 soa-li7-lou7, giam5-siok e5 le2-pai3-tng5 si7 hou7 Ma2-li7 e5 sim chin hoaN-hi2, u7 toa7 e5 in3-siong7.
“Ma2-li7, li2 ia7-bo5 ka7 lau7-su kong2 peng5-an!”  Ka-lian5 jip8-khi3 e5 si5, Hoa5 thai3-thai3 chiu7 kau3 i e5 bin7-cheng5 lai5 chheng2-an.
“Ma2-li7 peng5-an!  Li2 chin gau5 cha2, Lau7-su kin-a2-jit8 beh kong2 chin ho2 thiaN e5 kou3-su7 hou7 lin2 thiaN, li2 oe7 ki3-tit bo5?”
Hoa5 thai3-thai3 e5 siaN chin-chiaN3 sui2 koh chhin-chhiat.  I si7 Hoa5 I-seng e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, put-chi2 gau5 pang-chan7 lang5 koh u7 thiaN3-sim.  I e5 bin7 si5-si5 chin hoaN-hi2, kng-beng5.  U7 Chu2 e5 khui3-lat8 kap thiaN3-thang3 moa2-chhut ti7 i e5 bin7-iong5.
Hoa5 thai3-thai3, siong5-siong5 ba5-hoan5 li2!”  Ka-lian5 kong2:  
“M7-thang an2-ni kong2, lan2 long2 ti7 Chu2 e5 lai7-bin7 bo5 kheh-khi3.”
Hoa5 thai3-thai3 kong2 an2-ni chiu7 chhoa7 Ma2-li7 kau3 iu3-ti7-kho e5 kau3-sek.  I2-keng u7 10 goa7 e5 ji5-tong5 chu7-chip8 ti7 hia, Hoa5 thai3-thai3 hou7 in an-cheng7, chiu7 ka3 in gim5-si.
Ka-lian5 ti7 kau3-sek e5 au7-bin7 teh che7, chim-chiok lai5 teh khoaN3 Ma2-li7 u7 teh thiaN, teh gim5 a2-bo5.  U7-si5 Ma2-li7 na7 bo5 chu3-i3, i chiu7 khi3 Ma2-li7 e5 sin-piN:
“Ma2-li7 tioh8 ai3 thiaN lau7-su teh kong2 m7-thang khoaN3 pat8-ui7!”
Hoa5 thai3-thai3 chiu7 eng7 Liap8-sat-lou7 e5 kou3-su7 kong2 hou7 in thiaN:
“Kho2-lin5 e5 khit-chiah8 Liap8-sat-lou7, i tak8-jit8 long2 si7 giah8-thau5 teh ng3 thiN, sui-jian5 i si7 chit8 e5 kan-khou2 e5 lang5, iau2-ku2 i bo5 giau5-gi5 sit-chi3,sou2-i2 si2 e5 si5 Ia5-sou chiu7 chhe thiN-sai3 lai5 ngia5-chih i khi3 kau3 ti7 thian-tong5, ti7 hia long2 bo5 kan-khou2, bo5 iau-go7, bo5 lau5 bak8-sai2, eng2-oan2 oa2 ti7 A-pek-liap8-han2 e5 heng-cheng5.
Sou2-i2 lan2 sin3 Ia5-sou khah-iaN5 chit e5 se3-kan e5 khun3-khou2, lan2 ia7 beh kau3 ti7 hit e5 hok-khi3 e5 thian-tong5.”
Ma2-li7 thiaN-liau2 e5 si5 iau2-ku2 hong2-hut, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 hit e5 thiN-sai3 sou2 pau-ui5 e5 thian-tong5 chit8-iuN7 i e5 sim-lai7 chhiong-moa2 tioh8 peh8-saN thiN-sai3 e5 iaN2-siong7.  Chit e5 kou3-su7 hou7 Ma2-li7 chin-toa7 e5 in3-siong7, ti3-kau3 Ma2-li7 long2 bo5 boe7-ki3-tit.  Ma2-li7 choe3 ai3 thiaN e5 chiu7 si7 thiN-sai3 e5 su7.  I chai Liap8-sat-lou7 an2-choaN2 ti7 hit e5 thiN-sai3 sou2 toa3 e5 thian-tong5 tit-tioh8 hoaN-hi2 heng7-hok.  
“Ka-lian5 li2 e5 kan-khou2 goa2 long2 chai, chong2-si7 se3-kan e5 kan-khou2 si7 bo5 loa7-ku2, lan2 ti7 thian-tong5 e5 hok-khi3 khah iau3-kin2, li2 m7-thang sit-chi3, siong5-siong5 ki5-to2 lai5 iaN5 it-chhe3 e5 kan-khou2, Chu2 Ia5-sou tek-khak beh siau3-liam7 li2 e5 kan-khou2.”
Le2-pai3 soah Ka-lian5 kap Ma2-li7 beh tng2-khi3 e5 si5 Hoa5 thai3-thai3 kin2-kin2 tui3 au7-bin7 lai5, an-ui3 Ka-lian5 e5 sim.
“Hoa5 thai3-thai3 chin to-sia7 li2, siong5-siong5 tit-tioh8 li2 e5 ho2 an-ui3 kap che7-che7 mih8-kiaN7 hou7 goa2 chin put-jim2 sim.”  Ka-lian5 e5 bak8-sai2 tih-tam5 Ma2-li7 e5 chhiu2.
“Li2 m7-bian2 khoa3-lu7 chiah-e5, long2 si7 Chu2 e5 thiaN3 na7-tiaN7, li2 tioh8 ai3 ui7-tioh8 Ma2-li7 lai5 khia7-chai7, li2 na7 koe3-thau5 sit-chi3 siong-sim, na7 bo5 thang pang-chan7 Ma2-li7 e5 si5, Ma2-li7 e5 chian5-tou5 chiu7 koh khah chhi-chham2.”
Hoa5 thai3-thai3 eng7 i e5 chhiu2 hoaN7 ti7 Ma2-li7 e5 thau5-khak lai5 kong2 hou7 Ka-lian5 thiaN.

Ka-lian5 chhoa7 Ma2-li7 tng2-lai5 kau3 ti7 i sou2 soe3 e5 chhu3 e5 si5, chiu7 chiong sou2 thiaN-kiN3 e5 kou3-su7 koh soat-beng5 hou7 Ma2-li7 thiaN.
Li2 e5 A-pa ia7 si7 ti7 hit e5 sou2-chai7 teh tan2-hau7 lan2.  Ti7 hia e5 lang5 long2 si7 tui3 khou2-lan7 tiong chhut-lai5 e5, long2 si7 ti7 se3-kan tek-seng3 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5.  Ti7 hia bo5 u7 lau5 bak8-sai2 thou2-khui3, u7 chheng-chheng ban7-ban7 thiN-sai3 chheng7 peh8-saN teh gim5-si o-lo2 Chu2 Ia5-sou.”
Ka-lian5 pho7 Ma2-li7 ti7 i e5 heng-cheng5 an2-ni kong2:  “Ma-ma, ti7-hia Ia5-sou kiam2 beh thiaN goa2 gim5 e5 si?  Ma2-li7 tui3 lau7-bu2 e5 heng-cheng5 khi2-lai5 an2-ni mng7.”
“Beh! Ma2-li7, Ia5-sou tek-khak beh kong2 Ma2-li7 e5 siaN-im chin-chiaN3 sui2.”
Ka-lian5 koh chit8-pai2 pho7-oa2 Ma2-li7.
Ka-lian5 e5 chhiu2 in-ui7 koe3-thau5 e5 lo5-tong7 chin-chiaN3 chhou, ia7 u7 chin-che7 chiam-siong.  I jit8-si5 long2 si7 chhut-goa7 khi3 soe2-saN, mi5-si5 si7 cho3-saN.
Ma2-li7 jit8-si5 ti7 lau7-bu2 e5 sin-piN khoaN3 lau7-bu2 soe2-saN, mi5-si5 Ka-lian5 ti7 Ma2-li7 teh khun3 e5 chhng5-piN khoaN3 Ma2-li7 teh khun3 lai5 cho3-saN.
U7 si5 Ka-lian5 ti7 poaN3-mi5 kam2-kak tioh8 chin bo5-phoaN7, kou-tok8 bo5 i-oa2, chong2-si7 long2 ti7 ki5-to2 tiong lai5-tit-tioh8 Chu2 e5 an-ui3.
“Chu2 na7 kap goa2 ti7-teh siaN2-lang5 beh tui3-tek8 goa2”  Ka-lian5 siong5-siong5 siuN7-tioh8 Bok8-su sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 lai5 chong3-taN2.  

Ka-lian5 tit8-tit8 cho3-kang, ti3-kau3 i sin-the2 soah chin loan2-jiok8, chhin-chhiuN7 teh beh to2-loh8-khi3, chong2 si7 i iau2-ku2 siuN7-tioh8 i e5 chek-jim7 e5 toa7, chiu7 si7 tioh8 ai3 poe5-iuN2 Ma2-li7, tioh8 ai3 in-ui7 Ma2-li7 lai5 khia7-chai7, ia7 ki5-to2 Chu2 e5 pang-chan7 lai5 tit-tioh8 khui3-lat8.  Sou2-i2 sui-jian5 chin loan2-jiok8 iau2-ku2 chiau3-siong5 thoa-boa5.
Chong2-si7 sio-joah8 e5 he7-thiN chiam7-chiam7 koe3-khi3, chhiu-thiN lai5, chhiu7-hioh8 long2 hou7 pak-hong phah-loh8, thiN-toe7 ta-so3, bo5 seng-khi3 e5 si5, Ka-lian5 soah to2-chhng5 boe7 peh khi2-lai5.  Ti7 chit si3 gou7 ni5 sou2 chek lai5 e5 lo5-khou2 chhin-chhiuN7 chin7-pong7 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang koh ke3-siok8.
Ka-lian5 sui-si7 to2-chhng5, iau2-ku2 siuN7-tioh8 i sou2 tioh8 ai3-cho3 e5 kang-chok hiah choe7 chiu7 to2 boe7-tiau5, beh koh khi2-lai5 cho3-kang, chong2-si7 kam2-kak u7 chin toa7 e5 jiat8, sit8-chai7 chin khun3-lan5 thang khi2-lai5.  I kam2-kak sim-thau5 chin koh-iuN7, chhin-chhiuN7 hoe2 teh sio, jian5-au7 bo5 loa7-ku2 i chiu7 thou3 ou-ang5 e5 hoeh chhut-lai5.  Ka-lian5 chiu7 m7-chai lang5 khi3, bo5 loa7-kui2 chheN2 e5 si5, chiu7 khoaN3-tioh8 ti7 i e5 sin-piN teh hau2 kho2-lin5 e5 Ma2-li7 kap lau5-kha teh toa3 e5 lau7 A-po5.
“Ka-lian5, li2 chheN2 lah houN3? li2 long2 cho3 siuN koe3-thau5 chiah oe7 an2-ni, goa2 kiam2 m7-si7 siong5-siong5 kio3 li2 tioh8 ai3 chu3-i3 sin-the2.  TaN li2 an2-ni, bo5 khi3 kio3 i-seng lai5 kiam2 oe7 sai2-tit?”

Hit-si5 A-po5 thiN-sai3 u7 chiong i e5 saN-a2-ku thau2-khui, pang3-loh8 lai5 hou7 Ma2-li7.
Ma2-li7 beh chau2 kam2-kak tioh8 bo5 lat8, chong2-sii7 chit8-pou7 chit8-pou7 lai5 chau2, ko2-jian5 i kam2-kak i ia7 oe7 chau2 jip8 hit e5 khong-tiong.  I chin-chiaN3 kiaN A-kim7 chiu7 phoe-mia7 lai5 chau2, chong2-si7 chin bo5 lat8, kai-chai3 A-po5 e5 saN-a2-ku hou7 i khiN5-tiau5, i chiu7 tit-tioh8 thiN-sai3 e5 pang-chan7 lai5 chiam7-chiam7 chiuN7-kau3 hit e5 thian-kun e5 chhia-teng2 khi3.  Thian-kun chiap-tioh8 Ma2-li7 chiu7 toa7 hoaN-hi2, toa7-siaN o-lo2, gim5-si tit8-tit8 lai5 chau2 chiuN7 thiN-teng2.  A-kim7 e5 siaN kap iaN2 long2 li7-khui hng7-hng7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 lah.
Ma2-li7 ti7 chhau2-teng2 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 khit-chiah8 A-po5 i2-keng pian3-oaN7 choe3 i e5 lau7-bu2 Ka-lian5 e5 heng5-chong7 chhut-lai5, lau7-bu2 toa7 hoaN-hi2 toa7 khoai3-lok8 lai5 lam2 oa2 Ma2-li7 ti7 hoai5-lai7.
“Ma2-li7! Ma2-li7!” Ma2-li7 e5 hoaN-hi2 long2 boe7-thang heng5-iong5-tit, i chi2-u7 kio3 “Ma-ma!”  Chit8-siaN, chiu7 pho7-oa2 Ma-ma ti7 i e5 choan5 sin-khu.

ThiN-sai3 chin hoaN-hi2 toa7-siaN gim5-si.  Ma2-li7 chiu7 koh khun3-khi3.
“Ma2-li7, li2 tioh8 an-sim khun3, taN bo5 koh u7 lang5 thang hou7 li2 kiaN-hiaN5, li2 e5 siu7-khou2 long2 liau2 lah, lan2 beh jip8 hit e5 kng-beng5 e5 kok, si7 Ia5-sou cho3 Ong5 e5 sou2-chai7, ti7 hia bo5 kou-toaN, bo5 pi-chham2, bo5 lau5 bak8-sai2, si7 thang eng2-oan2 hiong2-siu7 peng5-an, khoai3-lok8 e5 sou2-chai7.
Lau7-bu2 Ka-lian5 an2-ni kong2 lai5 ai3-bu2 peng5-an tehkhun3 e5 Ma2-li7;
Ma2-li7 chiu7 kap thian-kun chiam7-chiam7 tui3 thiN-teng2 chiuN7-khi3.
Ke3 cha2-khi2 u7 ho2-sim e5 A-kou, phang5 chiah8-mih8 beh lai5 hou7 Ma2-li7 chiah8.  I in-ui7 bo5-eng5 koh sin-the2 bo5 ho2-se3, soah nng7 saN jit8 bo5 theh8 poaN3-hang7 lai5 hou7 Ma2-li7, sou2-i2 kam2-kak chin khoa3-lu7.
I kau3 chhau2-liau5 e5 si5, hit e5 chhau2-liau5 e5 mng5 u7 tui3 goa7-bin7 so2 khi2-lai5, si7 tui3 A-kim7 koaN2-chhut Ma2-li7 e5 si5 so2 e5 --
A-kou kiaN chit8 e7 --,
“Sim2-mih8 lang5 so2 chit e5 mng5?  Ma2-li7 si7 khi3 to2-ui7?”
I e5 sim-lai7 chin tioh8-kip, chiu7 kin2-kin2 tui3 thang-a2 beh lai5 khoaN3 Ma2-li7 u7 ti7 lai7-bin7 a2-bo5, A-kou chit8 e7 khoaN3, lai7-bin7 u7 chin ko3-iuN7 e5 an-cheng7, koh ti7 hit e5 tiu7-chhau2 teng2 u7 khoaN3-tioh8 Ma2-li7 tiam7-tiam7 to2 ti7 hia, i e5 bin7 ng3 ti7 thiN, chin peng5-an e5 khoan2-sit.
Chong2-si7 A-kou khoaN3-liau2 u7 chin tioh8-kiaN, Ma2-li7 si7 m7-si7 teh khun3!
“Ma2-li7!”  A-kou kin2-kin2 chhut-siaN lai5 kio3 Ma2-li7.  Chong2-si7 long2-bo5 hoan2-eng3.  A-kou kin2-kin2 phah-khui hit e5 so2, lai5 khui-mng5 jip8-khi3, i e5 chhiu2 e5 chiah8-mih8 long2 hiat-kak8 lah.  A-kou kin2-kin2 oa2 lai5 Ma2-li7 e5 sin-piN, i chhun-chhiu2 beh pho7 Ma2-li7, chhun-kau3 chit8-poaN3 soah m7-kaN2 bong tioh8 Ma2-li7, in-ui7 i2-keng beng5-pek8 Ma2-li7 si7 si2-khi3 lah!
A-kou kin2-kin2 to2-the3 lai5 toa7 siong-sim, lau5 bak8-sai2.
“Kho2-lin5, bo5 lang5 khoaN3-kou3 e5 Ma2-li7!  Kho2-lin5 e5 Ma2-li7!  Ma2-li7!”
A-kou sit8-chai7 ui7-tioh8 Ma2-li7 toa7 siong-sim, kin2-kin2 kui7-loh8 lai5 ki5-to2.
Kho2-lin5 soe3-soe3 e5 Ma2-li7 i2-keng ti7 kou-toaN, kiaN-hiaN5, iau-go7, pi-chham2 e5 tiong-kan lai5 si2-khi3.
Chong2-si7 bo5 lang5 chai-iaN2 Ma2-li7 ti7 chit e5 pi-chham2 siu7-khou2 e5 chhau2-tui-teng2 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 it-chhe3 e5 bin5-bang7.  Na7 u7 lang5 chai-iaN2, lang5 beh him-soan7 Ma2-li7 e5 si2; si7 ho5-teng2 e5 ki5-biau7 ho5-teng2 e5 heng7-hok, khoai3-lok8.
