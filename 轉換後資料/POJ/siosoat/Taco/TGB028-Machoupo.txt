3--goeh8 e5 Ma2-chou2-po5
Taco
Hit e5 ta-so3 tan2 bo5 mui5-hou7 e5 3--goeh8, jit8-chi2 ma7-si7 ai3 koe3.

Chit8-ni5 chit8-pai2 e5 Ma2-chou2-senn, ti7 tiong-pou7 hai2-soann3 e5 chng-a2-thau5 khai-si2 leh lau7-jiat8, pai3-pai3 si7 chng-a2-lai7 si7-toa7-lang5 e5 kai2-ioh8, phoa3-penn7 be7 ho2--e5, ti-a2 chhi7 be7 toa7--e5, chhoa7 bo5 bou2--e5, khi3 bio7--nih chhoe7 sin5-beng5 poah8-poe, am3-si5 to7 e7 ho2-khun3.

A-ma2 m7-bat ji7 ma7 m7-bat teh khoan3 sin-bun5, siang2 cho3 chong2-thong2, siang2 teh oai-ko, ti7 a-ma2 sim-lai7, eng2-oan2 pi2 be7 koe3 siang2 in chhu3 koh u7 ho2 ke3 iau2-boe7 ke3 e5 kou-niu5-a2, toh-ui7 u7 thang be2-tioh8 ang5-phiau bi2-chiu2 khah iau3-kin2.

Ku7-lek8 19, si7 chng-a2-lai7 ngia5 Ma2-chou2 e5 jit8.

Tan2 chit8-tang--a, tan2 kah kha-chhiu2 phi7-phi7-chhoah e5 a-po5 a-ma2, cha2 to7 thai5 ke thai5 ah, nng7-chiah-kha--e5, si3-ki-thui2--e5 cha2 to7 long2 khu5 ti7 bio7-khau2 ka7 ong5-lai5 teh tan2--a. Ma7 m7-chai ui3 tang-si5 khai-si2, lan2 pai3-pai3 hiah-e5 iong7 lai5 he7-goan7 e5 le2-sou3, nah e7 si7 pat8-chiong2 tong7-but8 tng7-kha tng7-chhiu2 cho3 seng-le2? Heng3-chhu3 pai3-pai3 kap koah-hiunn lai5 an-sim koe3-jit8 e5 a-ma2, tak8-ni5 na7 a-kong cho3-ki7 mai3 sng3, thai5 ke thai5 ah thai5 kah siong7 hoann-hi2--e5 to7-si7 chit-pai2.

I2-keng thau3-tiong-tau3--a, jit8-thau5 chin iam7. Thau3-cha2 to7 khi2-lai5 thai5 ke ban2-mng5 e5 a-ma2, chit-chun7, khoann3-tioh8 bin7 thau5-cheng5 kui-toh e5 chhenn-chhau, chiah thou2 chit8-khau2-khui3, bun7-bun7-a2 chhio3 kong2: “M7-chai Ma2-chou2-po5 u7 kah-i3--bo5?”

Goa2 chai a-ma2 tak8-ni5 chiah-ni7 kang-hu leh pai3-pai3, ma7-si7 in-ui7 i sim-lai7 cho3 lau7-bo2 e5 chit8-e5 chek-jim7, goa2 siong7 se3-han3 e5 a-chek, chiah8 kau3 40 thong3 hoe3 iau2-boe7 chhoa7-bou2, to7 in-ui7 a-chek bo5 siunn7 beh chhoa7, tak8-ni5 pai3-pai3 to m7-chai koh u7 joa7 che7 soe-siau5 e5 ke ai3 hong5 thai5. A-ma2 ma7 bo5 i e5 hoat, kan-tann chit8-sian pai3 koe3 chit8-sian.

“Goa2 be7-ki3-tit oann7 lan-san--e5!” A-ma2 hoah chit8-siann, ui3 lak-te7-a2 jim5 nng7-tiunn ang5-sek e5 choa2-phio3, kio3 goa2 khi3 bio7-khau2 oann7-chinn5. A-ma2 kio3 goa2 oann7 e5 gin5-a2-kak, si7 beh hou7 hiah-e5 kiann5 ti7 Ma2-chou2-po5 chhut-sun5 tui7-ngou2 thau5-cheng5, teh lang7 sai-thau5 hoah-hiu e5 khit-chiah8-kong kap khit-chiah8-po5--e5, a-ma2 ka7 goan2 kong2, hiah-e5 lang5 to7-si7 bo5 ho2-ho2-a2 thak8-chheh, chiah e7 toe3 ti7 Ma2-chou2-po5 sin-khu-pinn teh tho2-seng-oah8, a-ma2 it-tit8 long2 m7-chai, chit e5 thau5-lou7, si7 goa2 kap se3-han3 ti-ti siong7 siunn7 beh chiah8 e5 thau5-lou7. Goa2 kap ti-ti ma7 kong2 ho2--a, goa2 lang7 sai-thau5 i toe3 sai-boe2.

Goa2 chhiu2 phang5 a-ma2 kio3 goa2 khi3 oann7 e5 gin5-a2-khak, na2 kiann5 i2-keng na2 thiann-tioh8 lang5 teh pang3 phau3-a2 e5 siann. Tu2 oat-koe3 hang7-a2-khau2, to7 khoann3-tioh8 a-ma2 na2 kiann5 na2 chau2, chhui3--nih koh na2 hoah kong2: “Goa2 koh khi3 be2 chit8-koa2 kim-choa2 sui5 tng2--lai5, li2 seng tng2--khi3 m7-thang na2 chau2 na2 sng2, pai3-pai3 e5 si5 be7-tang3 sng2-chhio3……”

A-ma2 chau2 khi3 bio7-khau2 e5 kam2-a2-tiam3, hit e5 ti3 leh8-a2 phainn7 ti-kha-khou teh ka7 Ma2-chou2-po5 kui7--loh8 e5 po3-be2-a2, i2-keng kiann5 koe3 mng5-kha-khau2, a-ma2 koh iau2 m7 tng2--lai5, to7 beh be7-hu3--a, hiunn giah8--khi3-lai5, goa2 oh8 a-ma2 leh pai3 an2-ne, na2 pai3 chhui3--nih na2 liam7, chhiah-iah8-iah8 e5 jit8-thau5 ma7 lai5 tau3-lau7-jiat8.

Hiong5-hiong5 u7 chit8-e5 lang5 chong5 jip8-lai5 chhu3--nih: “Gin2-a2! Lin2 a-ma2 hun7-to2 ti7 bio7-khau2, lin2 chhu3-lai7 u7 toa7-lang5--bo5?”

Thau3-tiong-tau3, bio7-khau2 e5 tann2-ma2-ka chiok sio--e5, bio7-khau2 khia7 chit8-tin7 lang5, u7--e5 teh ka7 a-ma2 chhit-koann7, u7--e5 teh iat8-hong, kui-bin7 e5 lau7-lang5-pan kap tam5-tam5 e5 bak8-khou, jit8-thau5 iau2 ti7 thinn-teng2 tiam7-tiam7-a2 iam7.
