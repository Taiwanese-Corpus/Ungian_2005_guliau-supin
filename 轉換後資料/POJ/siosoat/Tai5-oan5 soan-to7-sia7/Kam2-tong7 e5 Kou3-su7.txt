Kam2-tong7 e5 Kou3-su7
Hou7 PA-PA E5 LE2-MIH8
Lak8-ni5 cheng5 e5 chit8 e5 Seng3-tan3 cheng5-me5, goa2 siu7 chit-bu7 sou2 pek, put-tek-put lau5 ti7 kaN-gak8 lai7 koe3 chit e5 cheh-ki5.  Keh-jit8 ti7 chheng-cheng7 e5 thau3-cha2, goa2 li7-khui kaN-gak8, kin2-kin2 ai3 tng2-khi3 goa2 e5 chhu3.  Hit si5 goa2 e5 te7-a2 lai7 moa2-moa2 e5 le2-mih8-------chiu7-si7 goa2 ui7 goa2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 sou2 pi7-pan7 e5.  Si7 chit8 e5 chin han5-leng2 e5 cha2-khi2, goa2 chiong goa7-i e5 liu2-a2 liu2 khi2-lai5, thang hong5-pi7 han5-leng2 chhak8-kut e5 leng2-hong.  Tng goa2 chhong-chhong bang5-bang5 teh kiaN5 e5 si5, na2 chun2 khoaN3-tioh8 chit8 e5 soe3-soe3 e5 iaN2 bih ti7 kaN-gak8 e5 chhiuN7-piah-e7.  Goa2 theng5-kha, siong5-se3 chit8-e7 khoaN3, ti7 hia goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, i e5 sin-chiuN7 sou2 chheng7 si7 chin poh8 e5 saN, kha-*nih8 e5oe ia7 chin phoa3.  I siang-chhiu2 lam2 chit8 pau mih8-kiaN7.  Goa2 chin ki5-koai3 i si7 chi7-chui7, siaN2-su7 ti7 chiah han5-leng2 e5 chheng-cha2 khia7 ti7 chia.  Chong2-si7 goa2 e5 ia3-sian7 iaN5-koe3 goa2 tui3 i e5 houN3-ki5-sim, goa2 chiu7 li7-khui i koh kin2 kiaN5, kiaN5 bo5 hng7, goa2 kam2-kak u7 lang5 teh te3 goa2, goa2 theng5 kha, oat8-thau5 koe3-lai5, khoaN3-kiN3 hite5 kho2-lin5 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, 
“Li2 siuN7 ai3 sim2-mih8?” Goa2 kin2-kin2 mng7 i. 
“Sian-siN, li2 si7 m7-si7 hit e5 kaN-gak8 e5 khan3-siu2?”
“Si7, li2 si7 chi7-chui7? Li2 siaN2-su7 li7-khui chhu3 lai5 chia?” 
“Sian-siN, chin sit-le2, goa2 bo5 chhu3.  Nng7 le2-pai3 cheng5 goa2 e5 lau7-bu2 si2 khi3, ti7 i lim5-chiong e5 si5 i ka7 goa2 kong2, pa-pa ti7 kaN-gak8!  I koh kong2 kiam2-chhai2 pa-pa hian7-chai7 siuN7 ai3 kiN3goa2 *la3 !  Goa2 kiu5 li2, li2 si7 m7 si7 thang hou7 goa2 jip8 khi3 kiN3 pa-pa?  Kin-a2-jit8 si7 Seng3-tan3-cheh, goa2 siuN7 ai3 sang3 i le2-mih8.”
“Bo5, ” goa2 chin chhou-lou2 in3 i kong2: “Li2 thang theng3-hau7 kau3 kui-teng7 hong2-bun7 hit jit8 chiah lai5!” Kong2 liau2 goa2 koh hiong3-cheng5 kiaN5, chong2-si7 goa2 kiaN5 bo5 kui2 pou7, goa2 hoat-kak u7 lang5 giu2 goa2 e5 goa7-i eng7 khun2-chhiat e5 siaN-im ng3 goa2 ai-kiu5 kong2: “ChhiaN2 li2 m7-thang khi3, ” goa2 koh theng5-kha, chu3-bak8 khoaN3 hit e5 pi-chham2 ai-kiu5 e5 bin7-iong5, ti7 chit-khoan2 pi-siong e5 cheng5-keng2, u7 toa7 liap8 e5 bak8-sai2 tui3 i e5 bak8-chiu tih loh8--lai5.
“Sian-siN, ” i kong2: “Siat-su2 goa2 si7 li2 e5 kiaN2, ia7 li2 e5 kiaN2 sit-loh8 lau7-bu2, i e5 lau7-pe7 koh ti7 kaN-gak8, i bo5 u7 thang khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, ia7 bo5 lang5 thiaN3 i, an2-ni li2 siuN7 i oe7 him-bou7 kiN3 i e5 lau7-pe7 boe7?  Siat-su2 che si7 chit8 e5 Seng3-tan3 jit8, li2 e5 kiaN2 lai5 kiN3 li2, ia7 goa2 si7 chit8 e5 kaN-gak8 e5 khan3-siu2, siat-su2 i iau-kiu5 goa2 iong5-un2 i theh8 le2-mih8 jip8-khi3 hou7 i e5 lau7-pe7, li2 siuN7 goa2 oe7 tui3 i kong2 “Kho2-i2” boe7?”   
Ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7, goa2 e5 na5-au5-kng2 tiN7 khi2-lai5, goa2 kam5 bak8-sai2 tui3 i kong2: “Si7 *la3 !  Soe3-kiaN2, li2 thang khi3 kiN3 li2 e5 lau7-pe7.” Goa2 chiu7 khan i e5 chhiu2, chin kin2 koh kiaN5 to3-tng2 kaN-gak8, goa2 e5 sim teh siuN7 chhu3-*nih8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.
Tng2-lai5 kau3 pan7-kong-sek i2-au7, goa2 chhiaN2 i kiaN5-kin7 hoe2-lou5, koh kio3 lang5 khi3 chhoa7 37 ho7 e5 hoan7-lang5 chhut-lai5.  Tng hoan7-lang5 kiaN5 jip8 pan7-kong-sek, khoaN3 tioh8 i e5 kiaN2 ti7 hia es5i, i e5 bin7 chek-si5 hian2-chhut chin siu7-khi3 e5 khoan2-sit.  I eng7 chhou-lou2 e5 thai3-tou7, chiong ie5 kiaN2 sak chhut-khi3, kong2: “Li2-li7, li2 lai5 chia cho3 sim2-mih8?  Li2 si7 ai3 sim2-mih8?  Kin2-kin2 tng2-khi3 ma-ma hia!”
“Pa-pa, ” i na2 kong2 na2 hau2, “Ma-ma i2-kengsi2 *la3 !  Ti7 nng7 le2-pai3 cheng5 si2-khi3, tng i si2 e5 si5, i hoan-hui3 goa2 tioh8 ho2-ho2 chiau3-kou3 sio2-ti7 Chun3-beng5, in-ui7 li2 chin thiaN3 i, i koh kio3 goa2 ka7 li2 kong2 i thiaN3 li2, chong2-si7, Pa-pa…”Kong2 kau3 chia i hau2 kau3 boe7 chhut-siaN… “Teng2 le2-pai3, Chun3-beng5 ia7 si2-khi3 *la3 !  Hian7-chai7 chi2-u7 chhun goa2 chit8-lang5, pa-pa, kin-a2-jit8 si7 Seng3-tan3-cheh, in-ui7 li2 chin thiaN3 Chun3-beng5, goa2 siuN7 li2 kiam2-chhai2 ia7 hi-bong7 tui3 i tit-tioh8 chit8 hang7 le2-mih8.”
I chiu7 thau2-khui chhiu2-*nih8 e5 chhiu2-kin, theh8 chhut chit8 kng2 chin sui2 e5 thau5-mng5 lai5, tng i chiong chit e5 mih8 khng3 ti7 lau7-pe7 e5 chhiu2 e5 si5, i kong2: “Che si7 in chiong Chun3-beng5 bai5-chong3 i2-cheng5, goa2 tui3 i e5 thau5-khak ka khi2-lai5 *e5.”
Chit si5, 37 ho7 e5 hoan7-lang5 toa7-siaN thi5-khau3 chhin-chhiuN7 gin2-na2, lian5 goa2 ia7 si7 an2-ni teh hau2, ti7 chit chiong2 cheng5-su7 e5 kek-tong7-e7, “37 ho7” chiong i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 lam2 ti7 i e5 hoai5-tiong.
Chit khoan2 sin5-seng3 e5 tiuN5-bin7, hou7 goa2 m7-kaN2 khoaN3, goa2 chiu7 khui mng5 chhut-khi3, lau5 in nng7 lang5 ti7 hia.  Chit8 tiam2-cheng au7, goa2 koh tng2-lai5, 37 ho7 pho7 i e5 kiaN2 ti7 hoe2-lou5 piN, i eng7 ia3-sian7 e5 bak8-chiu khoaN3 goa2 kong2: “Sian-siN, goa2 lian5 chit8 chiam chiN5 ia7 bo5.”  I na2 kong2 na2 thng3 i hit nia2 hoan7-lang5 chheng7 e5 goa7-i. “Ui7-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 ian5-kou3, kiu5 li2 hou7 i chheng7 chit nia2 saN tng2-khi3, m7 thang hou7 i chheng7 chiah poh8 e5 saN chhut-khi3!  Goa2 kam-goan7 ui7 lin2 cho3 jim7-ho5 e5 kang-chok lai5 pou2-poe5.  Sian-siN !  Kiu5 li2 chun2 i chheng7 chit nia2 goa7-i.” Bak8-sai2 chhin-chhiuN7 khe-chui2 tui3 chit e5 nge7-sim e5 ta-pou lang5 e5 bin7 lau5 loh8-lai5.
“Bo5, ” goa2 kong2: “Li2 thang chheng7 li2 e5 saN, goa2 boe7 oe7 hou7 li2 e5 kiaN2 an2-ni tng2-khi3.  Goa2 beh chhoa7 I khi3 goa2 e5 chhu3, hou7 goa2 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 the3 i siuN7 pan7-hoat.”
“Goan7 Siong-te3 chiok-hok li2, ” chit e5 hoan7-lang5 eng7 thi5-khau3 liau2-au7 chham2-chheh e5 siaN ka7 goa2 chiok-hok.
Goa2 chiong chit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 chhoa7 tng2-khi3, i kap goan2 saN-kap toa3 kui2-ni5 ku2.  Au7-lai5 i sin3 Chu2, chiaN5-cho3 chit8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5.
Koh keng-ke3 kui2-ni5 au7 e5 chit8-jit8, goa2 kau3 chit8 e5 chin cheng2-kiat e5 sio2 ka-teng5 tham3-hong2.  Li2-li7 kap i e5 lau7-pe7 －－hian7-chai7 i i2-keng si7 chit8 e5 teng5-thau5-siN e5 sin3-tou5.  In-ui7 iau7-lai5 hoe2-kai2, siu7 thau2-pang3 chhut kaN, hian7-chai7 i kap i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 saN-kap teh koe3 khoai3-lok8 e5 Ki-tok-hoa3 e5 seng-oah8.  Che oan5-choan5 si7 hit e5 bi5-se3 e5 Seng3-tan3 le2-mih8 phah-khui kong-nge7 e5 sim.

SENG3-TAN3-CHEH E5 SIN5-JIAH

Ku7-ni5 Seng3-tan3-cheh e5 sou3 le2-pai3 cheng5, u7 chit8 e5 le2-pai3 cha2-khi2, goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 m7 ai3 khi3 le2-pai3, hit si5 goa2 siuN7 i peng3 bo5 i3-su3 beh kiaN5 ti7 tui7-loh8 e5 lou7, chong2-si7 hit pai2 chiu7 chiaN5-choe3 i tui7-loh8 e5 khai-si2, i chhin-chhiuN7 loh8-kia7 it-tit8 lu loh8-khi3, tit8-kau3 Siong7-te3 eng7 goa2 lai5 kio3-chhiN2 i.  Che si7 goa2 chit8-si3-lang5 choe3 ki5-biau7 e5 keng-giam7.
Goan2 e5 ka-teng5 si7 chit8 e5 heng7-hok e5 Ki-tok-tou5 ka-teng5.  Lau7-pe7 si7 chit8 e5 phou2-thong e5 siong-jin5, ia7 si7 chit8 e5 ho2 e5 Ki-tok-tou5, i siong5-siong5 ai3 ui7 Ki-tok cho3 kan-cheng3, ia7 chin jiat8-sim chham-ka kau3-hoe7 e5 chu7-hoe7.  Tong-jian5 i u7 chit8 e5 chin toa7 e5 ia2-sim beh kian3-siat i e5 su7-giap8, chi2 u7 goan2 m7 chai hit e5 ia2-sim si7 joa7 toa7, na7 chai goan2 chiu7 oe7 khah ui7 i ki5-to2.  Lau2-sit8 kong2, goa2 long2 m7 bat ui7 i khoa3-lu7, goa2 siuN7 i si7 chin ho2 e5 lang5, sou2-i2 bo5 khiam3-eng7 goan2 the3 i ki5-to2.
U7 chit8-jit8, ti7 Seng3-tan3-cheh e5 2 geh8-jit8 cheng5, lau7-pe7 jip8 lai5 tu5-pang5.  Goan2 i2-keng chiah8 cha2-tng3 liau2, chun2-pi7 beh khi3 kau3-hoe7.  Chong2-si7 i chheng7 hit su beh khi3 phah kiu5 e5 saN kong2. “Kin-a2-jit8 lin2 tioh8 iong5-un2 goa2 bo5 khi3 pai3-tng5, in-ui7 goa2 kin-a2-jit8 u7 iok-hoe7, chite5 hoe7 tui3 ti7 goa2 e5 su7-giap8 si7 chin u7 ng3-bang7.”
Goa2 siuN7 goa2 e5 lau7-bu2 it-teng7 ti7 i e5 bin7 u7 chin kan-khou2 e5 piau2-cheng5, goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 chiu7 ka7 i lam2 teh, soe3-siaN tui3 i kong2 “m7-bian2 khoa3-lu7, Ma2-ka-li2, goa2 m7 si7 beh siong5-siong5 an2-ni, chit e5 hoe7(club)kau3 tang-thiN chiu7 beh kiat-sok, kin-a2-jit8 goa2 si7 siu7 la5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN e5 chiau-thai7, siat-su2 goa2 na7 tit-tioh8 i e5 hoaN-hi2, i oe7 ka7 goa2 be2 chin choe7 mih8.  Li2 chai na7 si7an-ni, lan2 si7 oe7 thang tit joa7 choe7 li7-ek, lan2 chiu7 beh toa7 seng5-kong.”
I chiong chu7-tong7-chhia e5 so2-si5 theh8 houg7oa kong2: “Poe7-ti2, kin-a2-jit li2 sai2-chhia, in-ui7 goa2 bo5 beh khi3.”
Goan2 i2-keng chun2-pi7 ho2, chiu7 sai2-chhia khi3 le2-pai3-tng5.  Goan2 long2 kam2-kak chin kan-khou2, goan2 boe7 thang siong2-siong7 lau7-pe7 bo5 lai5 le2-pai3, hoan2-tng2 ti7 le2-pai3-jit8 beh khi3 phah Ko-ni2-hu kiu5(golf) －chaiN2-iuN7 oe7 an2-ni!  Kau3 pai3-tng5, bok8-su mng7 lau7-bu2, lau7-pe7 chaiN2-iuN7 bo5 lai5, lau7-bu2 chi2-u7 in3 kong2 “Su7-giap8 siong7” chiu7 kiaN5 soa2 khi3 pat8-ui7, bian2-tit i e5 bak8-sai2 tih loh8 lai5.
Li2 kiam2-chhai2 m7 kaN2 siong-sin3 lau7-pe7 tui3 hit pai2 chiu7 bo5 koh kau3 le2-pai3-tng5 －tit8-kau3 Chu2 Ia5-sou kiu3 i chhut i e5 lo5-bang7.  I2-au7 i siong5-siong5 ti7 le2-pai3-jit8 kap Ia7-ni2-sun7 sian-siN u7 iok-hoe7, na7 bo5 chiu7 u7 pat8 e5 li2-iu5 bo5 ai3 khi3 le2-pai3.
Goa2 boe7 thang biau5-sia2 goan2 e5 sim e5 thong3-khou2 si7 joa7 toa7.  Goan2 tui3 an2-ni siong5-siong5 kui7-loh8 ui7 i ki5-to2.  Bok8-su siuN7 ai3 ban2-hoe5 i, lai5 khou2-khng3 i, chong2-si7 lau7-pe7 hoan2-tng2 siu7-khi3, bo5 ai3 koh kiN3 bok8-su.  Goan2 chi2-u7 pek-chhiat ki5-to2, koh chin7 goan2 sou2 oe7 hou7 lau7-bu2 khoai3-lok8.
Goan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 ti7 Seng3-tan3-cheh long2 u7 tek8-piat8 ian2 kek8, chit pai2 goa2 ia7 siu7 keng2 behcho3 Iok-sek.  Goa2 ki3-tit goa2 e5 a-ku7 u7 chit8 nia2 saN thang hou7 goa2 chheng7 cho3 Iok-sek, goa2 chiu7 tui3 i chioh.  Hit nia2 saN si7 peh8-sek e5, goa2 chheng7 khi2-lai5 chin tng5, kiam2-chhai2 hou7 thiN-sai3 chheng7 si7 khah hah8, chong2-si7 goa2 siuN7 pau thau5-kin chiah chheng7 hit nia2 chiu7 thang cho3 Iok-sek.
Seng3-tan3-cheh e5 sou3-jit8 cheng5, goa2 chhiaN2 lau7-pe7 tioh8 kap goan2 khi3 chham-ka Seng3-tan3 e5 kheng3-chiok hoe7, chong2-si7 i kong2 kap Ia5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN i2-keng u7 iok-hoe7.  Ia5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN siong5-siong5 hou7 lau7-pe7 toa7 e5 beng7-leng7, lau7-pe7 chin chiaN3 hok8-chiong5 i.  Goan2 chai-iaN2 i sou2 beh hu3 e5 hoe7 peng3 m7 si7 ho2 *e5. Seng3-tan3 boan2-hoe7, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 mui2 ni5 ti7 chin choe7 ki-koan teh ku2-heng5 e5 bo5 Seng3-tan3i-gi7 e5 boan2-hoe7.
TaN Seng3-tan3 me5 kau3 *la3 !  Goan2 e5 sian-siN kio3 goan2 tioh8 ti7 chhu3-*nih8 chheng7 piau2-ian2 e5 saN chheng7 hou7 pian7, in-ui7 pai3-tng5 bo5 sou2-chai7 thang hou7 hiah choe7 lang5 oaN7 saN.
Goa2 chiu7 chheng7 a-ku7 hit nia2 chin tng5 e5 saN, theng3-hau7 goa2 e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 chio goa2 khi3.  Tng goan2 sai2-chhia keng-koe3 Ia5-ni2-sun7 kong-si e5 si5, i2-keng chin am3.  Hit si5 goa2 khoaN3 tioh8 lau7-pe7 e5 chhia ti7 mng5-khau2, goa2 chek-si5 siuN7-tioh8 “ChaiN2-iuN7 goa2 m7 jip8-lai5 khi3 chio lau7-pe7 khi3 pai3-tng5?  Kiam2-chhai2 goa2 chit e5 si5-chun7 ka7 I chhiaN2, i oe7 kap goan2 khi3.”
Goa2 chiu7 kio3 goa2 e5 peng5-iu2 theng5-chhia, goa2 thng3 goa2 hit nia2 toa7-nia2 saN, beh loh8-khi3 e5 si5 lau7-bu2 io5-thau5 piau2-si7 bo5 ng3-bang7.  Goa2 chai-iaN2 lau7-pe7 ti7 ta2-loh8 chit8-keng, in-ui7 i bat chhoa7 goa2 khi3 nng7-pai2, mng5 si7 khui-khui, goa2 chiu7 jip8 khi3 hit e5 am3-am3 bo5 kng e5 toa7 thiaN, chu3-i3 thiaN im-gak8 e5 siaN si7 tui3 to2-ui7 lai5.  Goa2 thiaN-tioh8 chhiuN3-koa kap gak8-khi3 e5 siaN e5 si5, goa2 chiu7 kiaN5 kun7 u7 siaN e5 sou2-chai7. Goa2 siuN7 ai3 pai3-thok chit8 e5 lang5 kio3 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 chhut-lai5, chong2-si7 piN-a bo5 lang5, sou2-i2 goa2 chiu7 kiaN5 oa2 koaiN teh e5 mng5, thau khui chit8-phang7, beh khoaN3 lai7-bin7 e5 cheng5-heng5.
Goa2 tiam7-tiam7 khui mng5, bo5 lang5 oe7 khoaN3 tioh8 goa2, in-ui7 tian7-hoe2 bo5 chin kng, goe2 khia7 e5 sou2-chai7 si7 am3-am3.  Tui3 hia goa2 oe7-thang khoaN3 tioh8 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7. Ti7 lai7-bin7 u7 chit8-te3 tng5 e5 toh-a2, toh-teng2 u7 chiu2-kan kap choe7-choe7 chiah8-mih8, tak8 e5 lang5 toa7-siaN teh hoah, teh thiau3 ia7 teh lim.
Jian5-au7 goa2 chin chheng-chho2 khoaN3 tioh8 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 e5 bin7, goa2 sui5-si5 beng5-pek8 jip8-khi chio i ia7 si7 bo5 chhai2-kang.  I ti7 hia kiaN5 lai5 kiaN5 khi3, toa7 siaN teh hoah.  Hit si5 Ia5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN kio3 chit8 e5 lang5 theh8 hip-siong7-ki beh lai5 hip-siong, choan5-the2 hip liau2-au7, Ia5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN kio3 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 kap leng7-goa7 kui2 e5 lang5 kiaN5 lai5 khia7 ti7 goa2 e5 mng5-cheng5 beh leng7-goa7 hip chit8-tiuN.
Goa2 tioh8 toa7-kiaN, siuN7 ai3 chau2 chhut-lai5, chong2-si7 goa2 khoaN3 in bo5 hoat-hian7 tioh8 goa2 bih ti7 mng5-au7.  Goa2 koh khoaN3 in ui5-lui5 ti7 tng5-toh, Ia5-ni2-sun7 sian-siN e5 chhiu2 moa ti7 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 e5 keng-thau5, chit8-chhiu2 theh8 chiu2-poe hoah kong2 “Lim chit8 poe!  Ui7 Chu2-jit8-oh8 kau3-oan5 Li2 Cha-li2 lim chit8 poe!!”
Goa2 thiaN in liam7 lau7-pe7 e5 mia5, goa2 e5 simkan-khou2 kau3 beh piak-phoa3.  Goa2 hiam2-hiam2 toa7-siaN hoah chhut-lai5, goa2 chit8-si3-lang5 boe7 bat hiah *nih8 kan-khou2 sim khoaN3 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7.  Goa2 li7-khui hit e5 sou2-chai7 e5 si5, thiaN tioh8 in toa7-siaN teh hoah kong2 kin2-kin2 se2 goan5-pan2 khoaN3 sou2 hip e5 chaiN2-iuN7.
Goa2 to3-tng2 lai5 chhia-lai7, bo5 ka7 lau7-bu2 kongg2oa sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 *e5, goa2 m7 ai3 i sit-loh8 Seng3-tan3-me5 e5 khoai3-lok8.  Goa2 chi2-u7 ka7 i kong2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang chio lau7-pe7 chhut-lai5.  Goan2 kau3 pai3-tng5, goa2 chhut-ian2 sou2 tam-jim7 e5 chiat-bok8, Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3-thang3, chhe I e5 tok8-siN KiaN2 lai5 se3-kan beh chin2-kiu3 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 chit-khoan2 choe7-jin5, chit khoan2 e5 hok-im su3 hou7 goa2 sin e5 iong2-kam2.  Goa2 chai-iaN2 Chu2 i2-keng chin2-kiu3 goa2, ia7 goa2 si7 siok8 ti7 I *e5, goa2 khak-sin3 I oe7 ui7 goan2 khui chit8-tiau5 lou7.  Sui-jian5 u7 chit khoan2 e5 ng3-bang7, iau2-ku2 ti7 goan2 beh tng2-lai5 e5 lou7-chiuN7, goa2 chin khoa3-lu7, lau7-pe7 oe7 chiu2-chui3 tng2-lai5.
Goan2 tng2-lai5 kau3 chhu3 e5 si5, khoaN3-tioh8 lau7-pe7 e5 chhia i2-keng ti7 chhia-khou3. Hak8-hau7 ti7 hitjit8 theng5 kho3, tong5-chu2-kun chi3-goan7 kng “Sai2-pu3” e5 koaN-chha5, tin3-bin5, long5-hu, bok8-chia2 long2 chham-ka ti7 chhia-cham7 ku2-heng5 e5 liong5-tiong7 chong3-sek.
Bok8-su ui7 i thak8 chit8 phiN si “Ti7 chit e5 chu7-su e5 se3-kai3, jin5-lui7 i5-it boe7 chu7-su e5 peng5-iu2 chiu7-si7 ka-ki7 e5 kau2, kau2 choat8-tui3 be7 pang3-sak chu2-lang5, choat8-tui3 boe7 kou-hu7 a2-si7 poe7-poan7 --i”.
An-chong3 “Sai2-pu3” i2-au7, Poe3-teng-po2 e5 lang5 koh ui7 i khia7 chit8 cho7 ki3-liam7-pi ti7 soaN-teng2.  Koh u7 tham3-chiau3-teng chio3 ti7 hit teng2-bin7.  Bo5-lun7 jit8--si5, am3-si5. tng hoe2-chhia keng-koe3 e5 si5, chhia-tiuN2 oe7 chiong chit e5 kou3-su7 kong2 hou7 hoN3-ki5 e5 lu2-kheh thiaN.
Au7-lai5 chhia-tiuN2 Ai3-tek chiong “Sai2-pu3” e5 kou3-su7 sia2 cho3 chit8 pun2 sio2 chheh be7 hou7 lu2-kheh.  Tui3 an2-ni than3 200 bi2-kim, i chiong chit tiau5 chiN5 kia3-hu3 ti7 Toa7-phok-pou3 siaN5 e5 bong5-a hak8-hau7. 1946 ni5 Seng3-tan3-cheh, i toachin3-choe7 thit-tho5 mih8, thng5-a2, liu-peng-oe5 khih33 hiah e5 hak8-seng, hiah e5 hak8-seng tit-tioh8 in peng5-si5 boe7 thang tit -tioh8 e5 thit-tho5 mih8, koe3 chit e5 “in boe7 bat u7 e5 khoai3-lok8” e5 Seng3-tan3-cheh.
Tui3 chit e5 tat8-tit ki3-liam7 e5 Seng3-tan3-chehau7 e5 sou3-ni5 tiong, “Sai2-pu3” e5 kou3-su7 kam2-tong7 chin-choe7 lang5 hian3 chiN5, kau3-taN i2-keng u7 5 ban7 bi2-kim. Tui3 an2-ni oe7 thang siat-lip8 “Sai2-pu3” ki-kim, eng7 thiaN3-sim tui3 chhiN-mi5 kap e2-kau2 e5 lang5 pan7 sin e5 i-liau5 kong-chok .
 “Sai2-pu3” ki-kim hou7 chit keng bong5-a hak8-hau7 e5 chui2-chun2 the5-ko, hou7 hak8-seng e5 cheng-sin5 khoai3-lok8, tit-tioh8 thiaN3-sim e5 le2-mih8 kap kau3-iok8, ----hou7 in oe7 thang kau3 i2-cheng5 sou2 boe7 thang khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 lu2-heng5. “Sai2-pu3” ui2-tai7 e5 sim-leng5 i2-keng pang-chan7 in kiaN5 ng3 chit8 tiau5 seng5-kong e5 lou7, che long2 si7 tui3 ti7 chit chiah tiong-gi7 e5 kau2 kam2-tong7 lang5 e5 kou3-su7 sou2 san2-seng e5.
Chit8 chiah kau2 oe7 tui3 chu2-lang5 tiong-sit8 kau3 an2-ni, lan2 tui3 lan2 e5 Chu2 m7 chai chaiN2-iuN7? U7 tak8-jit8 teh theng3-hau7 lan2 e5 Chu2 koh to3-tngl2ai a2-bo5?
PHO-PHE2-TO-A
TE7-SAN se3-ki2, tng Lo5-ma2 te3-kok chin7-lat8 khun2-tiok8 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 si5, joa7-*nih8 choel7ang ui7-tioh8 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 ian5-kou3 chham2-si2, hi-seng in e5 siN3-mia7. Pho-phe2-to-a(Perpetua), chit ui7 iong2-kam2 e5 siau3-lian5 hu7-lu2, chiu7-si7 chit e5 si5-ki5 choe7-choe7 sun5-to7-chia2 e5 chit8 e5, i e5 mia5 ji7 tui3 ti7 an2-ni, siu7 liat8 ti7 seng3-kau3-su2 e5 lai7-bin7, liu5-thoan5 tai7-tai7.
Chit e5 hu7-lu2 ui7 Chu2 lai5 hi-seng e5 tai7-chi3, ti7 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 lek8-su2 tiong, thang kong2 si7 choe3 cheng-chhai2 e5 chit8 iah8. Su7-cheng5 e5 hoat-sengsi7 ti7 A .D. 203 ni5 e5 si5, te7-tiam2 si7 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka chiu e5 pak-peng5 Ka-thai3-ki e5 sou2-chai7. Hit si5 u7 chit sio2-kun5 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 siu7-liah8 koaiN-kaN. Siu7-liah8 e5 lang5 tai7-pou7-hun7 long2 si7 lou5-lekhah7 che7, chong2-si7 Pho-phe2-to-a khiok si7 chit8e kui3-khi3 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, ni5-ki2 chi2 u7 22 hoe3, heng-cheng5 pho7 chit8 e5 eN-a2.  Chit e5 siau3-lian5 e5 lu2-chu2, tu2-tioh8 chiah-nih8 thang kiaN e5 koaiN-kaN e5 keng-giam7, chit e5 thong3-khou2 lan2 longsi2uN oe7 kau3, che tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Pho-phe2-to-a sou2 sia2 e5 chit8-iuN7: “Goan2 tiam3 ti7 kaN-gak8 kuij2…t i2-au7, goa2 hui-siong5 kiaN-hiaN5, in-ui7 goa2 longb2oe bat siuN7 tioh8 thai3 oe7 chiah ou-am3. Bo5 peng5-an e5 jit8-chi2! Thang kiaN e5 iam7-jiat8!  Khan3-siu2 chhou-lou2 e5 chhiu2! Siong7 kan-khou2-sim e5, chiu7-si7 goa2 khoa3-liam7 goa2 e5 eN-a2 ti3-kau3 lai5 kan-khou2.”
Ti7 chit kui2-jit8 e5 tiong-kan, u7 nng7 kiaN7 et5ai-chi3 hou7 i e5 sim-koaN-thau5 toa7 tin2-tang7, hechiu7-si7 i chin thiaN3 i peh8 thau5-mng5 e5 lau7-pekap7 loan2-jiok8 e5 kiaN2-ji5. I e5 lau7-pe7 sim-lai7 hui-siong5 e5 kan-khou2, i boe7 beng5-liau5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 tui3 ti7 sin-kau3 siaN2-su7 chiah-nih8 khian5-seng5? SiaN2-su7 i tui3 ti7 ku7 kau3 boe7 moa2-i3? SiaN2-su7 iboe7 kap pat8 e5 lang5 kang7 khoan2 lai5 than3 hong5-te3 e5 beng7-leng7 khi3 sin3 ku7 kau3? “KiaN-hiaN5”eng7 han5-leng2 e5 chhiu2 ka7 chit e5 lau7-lang5 e5 chhiu2 khiu2 tiau5-tiau5.  I ti7 hoan5-lo2 thong3-khou2 e5 tiong-kan, it-tit8 ka7 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khou2-khng3, kio3 i tioh8 thoat-li7 chit e5 gui5-hiam2 e5 khoan5-keng2. 
Pho-phe2-to-a tui3 ti7 chit chiong2 e5 iau-kiu5 chin thai3-jian5 lai5 eng3-hu3. I kong2: “Pa-pa, likh2oaN-kiN3 khng3 ti7 chia e5 te5-kou2 mah?  I kiam2 thang eng7 pat8 e5 mia5 lai5 ka7 i kio3 *nih?” “Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 *lah”, i e5 lau7-pe7 in3 i an2-ni kong2, i koh tui3 i kong2: “I ki3-jian5 si7 te5-kou2, li2 kiam2 boe7 thang kio3 i choe3 pat8 e5 mia5 mah?” Lau7-pe7 in3 kong2: “Boe7-eng7-tit”. Pho-phe2-to-a chiu7 kong2: “Sou2-i2 goa2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, ia7 boe7 oe7 kohkio3 goa2 choe3 pat8 e5 mia5 lah.”
Koh chai3 chit8 pai2, i eng7 choe3 chhim-khekkoh kam2-tong7 lang5 e5 gian5-gu2 kong2: “Goa2 el5au-pe7 tui3 siaN5-*nih8 lai5 khoaN3 goa2, sim-lai7 hui-siong5 e5 hoan5-lo2, i kau3 soaN-teng2 lai5 khoaN3 goa2, i e5 i3-su3 si7 ai3 goa2 pang3-sak goa2 e5 koat-sim.  I kong2: “Cha-bou2-kiaN2 ah!  Li2 tioh8 kho2-lin5 goachit2 e5 thau5-mng5 peh8-chhang-chhang e5 lau7-lang5 ouh!  Kho2-lin5 li2 e5 lau7-pe7, siat-su2 goa2 nakham7-tit choe3 li2 e5 lau7-pe7; siat-su2 goa2 na7 bat eng7 goa2 chit siang chhiu2 iuN2-chhi7 li2 toa7-han3, siat-su2 goa2 na7 bat ti7 li2 cheng3 hiaN-ti7 e5 bin7-cheng5 o-lo2 li2, chhiaN2 li2 m7-thang hou7 goa2 siu7-tioh8 koe3-thau5 e5 thong3-khou2!  KhoaN3 li2 e5 hiaN-ti7, li2 e5 lau7-bu2, li2 e5 a-i5 e5 bin7-mau7, koh khoaN3 boe7 eng7-tit li7-khui li2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 e5 bin7-mau7, pang3-sak li2 e5 kiau-ngou7! M7 thang chiong lan2 e5 choan5-ke long2 hui2-biat8 lah! Siat-su2 li2 na7 tu2-tioh8 put-heng7, lan2 choan5-ke e5 lang5, chiu7 boch5…t e5 oe7 thang koh chai3 chu7-iu5 lai5 kong2 oe7 lah.”
 “Goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 in-ui7 thiaN3 goa2, sou2-i2 chiah kong2 chiah e5 oe7, i chim goa2 e5 chhiu2, phak-loh8 ti7 goa2 e5 kha-e7, nng7 lui2 bak8-chiu bak8-sai2 tit8-tit8 lau5 teh kio3 goa2, bo5 kio3 goa2 choe3 cha-bou2-kiaN2, khiok kio3 goa2 choe3 thai3-thai3.  Goa2 ui7-tioh8 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 e5 ian5-kou3, hui-siong5 e5 hoan5-lo2, in-ui7 i li7-khui goa2 e5 ka-chok8 e5 koa n-he7 chiu7 bo5 khoai3-lok8. I ui7 goa2 e5 ian5-kou3 lai5 siu7-khou2, goa2 tek-khak tioh8 an-ui3 i, kong2: “Siat-su2 Siong7-te3 na7 keng2-soan2 tioh8 goa2, goabeh2 khia7 ti7 tai5-a2 teng2, lan2 long2 chai-iaN2, lan2 m7 si7 kho3 ka-ki7 e5 khui3-lat8, si7 kho3 Siong7-te3 e5 khui3-lat8 ! I thiaN liau2 chiu7 moa2-pak iu-chhiu5 lai5 li7-khui goa2 *khi3 lah.”
Chit kui2 ku3 e5 oe7, chu-ui7 thok8-chia2 iau2 thak8 oe7 loh8-sim, chong2-si7 ia7 u7 pi2 che koh-khah pi-chham2 e5 cheng5-chiat hou7 tai7-ke sit8-chai7 put-jim2 khoaN3 loh8-khi3 !  U7 chit-jit8 siu7-liahkh8i koaiN-kaN e5 Ki-tok-tou5 siu7 toa3 kau3 kong-kiong7 chhi7-tiuN5 khi3 ku2-heng5 kong-sim2, kau3 tichit7 si5, Ki-tok-tou5 iau2 u7 pang3-li7 Ki-tok-kau3 lai5 tit-tioh8 chu7-iu5 e5 ki-hoe7, i5-it e5 tiau5-kiaN7chiu7-si7 tioh8 hok8-chiong5 hong5-te3, ui7-tioh8 hong5-te3 lai5 hong7-hian3 hi-seng, chong2-si7 mui2 chit8 e5 siu7-liah8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 long2 ku7-choat8 chit chiong2 iau-kiu5 chu7-iu5 e5 tho3-hiap8 e5 tiau5-kiaN7.
Pho-phe2-to-a an2-ni sia2 kong2: “Tng goa2 tehsiu7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 si5, goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 kap kiaN2-ji5 long2 chai7-tiuN5. Lau7-pe7 chiong goa2 tui3 tai5-a2-teng2 khiu2--loh8-lai5, kiu5 goa2 kong2: “Kho2-lin5 li2 eeN5-a2 ah!” ti7 chit chiong2 e5 cheng5-heng5 e7, chit8e keng3-khian5 e5 lau7-bu2 ia7 oe7 u7 khui3-lat8 lai5 kiong5-pian7 mah? Chit e5 toa7 e5 bu2-ai3 sui5-si5 beh chiong i e5 koat-sim chhiong-phoa3, chong2-si7 u7 chit8 e5 koh khah toa7 e5 leng5-lek8 ti7 i e5 lai7-bin7 teh un7-heng5.  Tng sim2-phoaN3 koaN tui3 goak2ong: “Li2 si7 chit8 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 mah?” Goa2 than2-peh8 in3 i kong2 “si7”
I e5 lau7-pe7 ke3-siok8 lou2-lek8, tek-khak beh houcha7-bou2-kiaN2 thoat-li7 gui5-hiam2, sui-jian5 sim2-phoaN3-koaN i2-keng kio3 i loh8 tai5-a2, chong2-si7 i iau2-ku2 oa2 ti7 sim2-phoaN3 tai5 e5 piN-a2, sou2-i2 sim2-phoaN3-koaN chiu7 eng7 i e5 kun2-a2 ka7 i kong3, chit e5 ku2-tong7 tui3 ti7 Pho-phe2-to-a tong-jian5 si7 hui-siong5 e5 taN2-kek, pi2 i ni5-lau7 e5 lau7-pe7 e5 sin-chiuN7sou2 siu7 tioh8 e5 siong koh khah thong3-khou2.
Choe3-au7 e5 phoaN3-koat, si7 phoaN3 sou2 u7 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 long2 tioh8 siu7 hiat ti7 beng2-siu3 e5 tiong-kan lai5 si2. “Choe3 toa7 e5 khoai3-lok8” Pho-phe2-to-a sia2 kong2, chiu7 si7 “Goan2 kiaN5 jip8 kaN-gak8”.
Ti7 in choe3-au7 e5 chit8 jit8, thiN phu2-kng e5 si5, “in tui3 kaN-gak8 lai7 chhut--lai5, kiaN5 lai5 iN5-kek8-tiuN5 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh chiuN7 thian-tongch5…t-iuN7, moa2-bin7 hoaN-hi2 khoai3-lok8, bo5 kiaN-hiaN5, bo5 lun2-neh.  Ti7 hia Pho-phe2-to-a kap siu7 liah8 e5 Ki-tok tou5 siu7 tioh8 kek8-ki5 chan5-jim2 e5 si2-heng5, ui7-tioh8 in sou2 kian-chhi5 e5 sin3-giong2 cho3 choe3 au7 e5 kian3-cheng3.
