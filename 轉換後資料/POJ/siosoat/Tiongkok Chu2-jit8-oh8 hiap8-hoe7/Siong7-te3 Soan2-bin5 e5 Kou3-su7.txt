Siong7-te3 Soan2-bin5 e5 Kou3-su7
?
Te2-kang2
Bi2-kok Lam5 Pak chian3-cheng e5 si5, Bi2-sou-li2 chiu u7 chin-choe7 lang5 tui3-thai7 Lian5-pang e5 kun-tui7 chin bai2.  Ui7-tioh8 beh hong5-chi2 chit khoan2 m7 ho2 e5 cheng5-heng5 ke3-siok8 hoat-seng, Lian5-pang Su-leng7-pou7 chiu7 phai3 peng thai7-pou7 chit8-koa2 lang5 koaiN ti7 kaN-nih, phah-sng3 in si7 po7-tou5, theng3-hau7 sim2-phoaN3 au7 beh ka7 in chheng3-sat, chiah e5 siu7 liah8 e5 lang5 tiong u7 chit8 lang5 kio3-choe3 Ui-liam5, sichin7 ho2 e5 chheng-lian5.  Kau3 te7 ji7 jit8, chiong-kun khiok koat-teng7 beh tui3 siu7 liah8 e5 lang5 tiong seng soan2 10 lang5 lip8-khek chheng3-sat.  Chit 10 lang5 chiu7 siu7 liah8 khia7 choe3-he2, tong-si5 Ui-liam5 si7 bo5 ti7 hit 10 lang5 tiong, chong2-si7 hit 10 lang5 u7 chit8 e5 si7 Ui-liam5 e5 chhu3-piN.  Ui-liam5 siuN7 kong2, chit e5 lang5 e5 ka-teng5 chin song3-hiong, koh u7 choe7-choe7 kiaN2-ji7, siat-su2 i na7 si2, chiah e5 gin2-na2 chiu7 bo5 lau7-pe7, chesi7 m7-si7 thai3 kho2-lin5.  Ui-liam5 e5 sim toa7 siu7 kek-tong7, sou2-i2 koat-sim khia7 ti7 su-leng7-koaN e5 bin7-cheng5 kong2, “Niu7 goa2 thoe3 chit lang5 si2 !!”  Su-leng7-koaN bo5 hoan2-tui3 chekian3-gi7, in-ui7 i chi2-u7 than3 beng7-leng7 chheng3-sat 10 lang5 chiu7 kho2-i2 lah.  Chit e5 chhu3-piN eng7 hui-siong5 e5 kam2-ke ke5 sim lai5 chiap-siu7 Ui-liam5 choe3 i e5 thoe3-sin, su7 tui3 an2-ni chiu7 koat-teng7.
Ui-liam5 khia7 ti7 hit e5 chhu3-piN pun2-lai5 tioh8 khia7 e5 sou2-chai7, i kap ki5-thaN e5 9 lang5 khia7 chit8 pai5, bin7 tui3 kiah8 chheng3 e5 peng-teng, Su-leng7-koaN ha7-leng7, Ui-liam5 kap ki5-thaN 9 e5 lang5 long2 to2 toe7 si2.  Tui3 an2-ni, Ui-liam5 e5 siN3-mia7 cheng7-sang3 hou7 i e5 chhu3-piN, i choe3 chit8 e5 thoe3-sin-chia2.
Sio2 peng2-iu2!  Li2 chai u7 chit8 ui7 pi2 Ui-liam5 khah ui2-tai7 e5 lang5, I hi-seng liau2 I ka-ki7 e5 siN3-mia7?  I si7 bo5 choe7, chong2-si7 tam-tng choan5 se3-kai3 e5 lang5 e5 choe7, kam-sim thoe3 si2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 teng2, choe3 lan2 e5 thoe3-sin, hou7 lan2 m7-bian2 tu2-tioh8 heng5-hoat8, chit e5 chiu7-si7 Chu2 Ia5-sou.  I an2-ni thiaN3 li2, thiaN3 goa2, lan2 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 kam2-sia7 I ?  Li2 goan7-i3 chiap-siu7 I choe3 ka-ki7 e5 Kiu3-chu2 mah?  
 
Phi3-ju7 kou3-su7
(1)Sio2-hiong5 si7 phaiN2 gin2-na2, bo5 ai3 thiaN ma-ma e5 oe7, sou2-i2 ma-ma koat-teng7 beh ka7 i chhu2-hoat8.  Hit si5 i e5 ko-ko Toa7-po2 chai chit e5 su7, chiu7 khi3 ka7 ma-ma kong2, i goan7-i3 thoe3 ti-tih siu7 heng5-hoat8.  Ma-ma kiah8 chit8 ki tek-a2-ki phah Toa7-po2 10 e7, phah tit hui-siong5 e5 thiaN3, Toa7-po2 chiu7 an2-ni thoe3 ti-tih siu7 heng5-hoat8.  Hit si5 Sio2-hiong5 khoaN3 liau2 sim siu7 toa7 kam2-tong7, i siuN7 i e5 ko-ko un2-tang3 chin thiaN3 i, chiah oe7 hoaN-hi2 thoe3 i siu7 heng5-hoat8.  Ichiu7 koaN2-kin2 phau2 khi3 ko-ko hia, lam2-teh lau5 bak8-sai2 kong2, “Ko-ko, goa2 hiah-nih phaiN2, li2 thai2 oe7 thiaN3 goa2 kau3 an2-ni?  Goa2 siu7 ma-ma phah chiah si7 eng-kai e5, chong2-si7 li2 khiok thoe3 goa2 siu7 choe7, ko-ko goa2 thiaN3 li2, goa2 beh chiau3 li2 sou2 kong2 choe3 koai e5 gin2-na2!”  Sio2 peng5-iu2, Ia5-sou ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 ui7 lan2 si2, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Toa-po2 ui7-tioh8 sio2-ti7 siu7 heng5-hoat8.  Li2 goan7-i3 kau3 Ia5-sou e5 bin7-cheng5 ka7 I kong2, “chhin-ai3 e5 Ia5-sou, goa2 bo5 koai, kong2 peh8-chhat8, tham-sim, ai3 oan-ke, hoat phi5-khi3, na7-si7 Li2 hiah thiaN3 goa2, thoe3 goa2 siu7 heng5-hoat8.  Kiu5 Li2 sia3-bian2 goa2 e5 choe7, goa2 kim-au7 beh than3 Li2 e5 oe7 kiaN5.”
(2)Lan5-sim e5 pe7-bu2 cha2 chiu7 si2.  U7 chit8 jit8 i phoa3-piN7 chin tang7, i e5 kou-po5 chhiaN2 i-seng lai5 ka7 i khoaN3, i-seng kong2 i e5 piN7 tioh8 su-hiat chiah oe7 oah8.  Tan7-si7 kou-po5 e5 huih-heng5 kap i e5 huih-heng5 bo5 siang7, kou-po5 kui2-na7 e5 peng5-iu2 e5 huih-heng5 ia7 bo5 siang7, sou2-i2 boe7 thang su-hiat hou7 Lan5-sim, Lan5-sim e5 piN7-se3 tit8-tit8 pian3 bai2.  I-seng chai-iaN2 ka-ki7 e5 huih-heng5 sek-hap8 Lan5-sim e5 huih-heng5, na7-si7 i si7 chit8 e5 chin bo5-eng5 e5 lang5, na7 thiu hou7 Lan5-sim, i e5 sin-the2 ia7 tong3 boe7 tiau5, chong2-si7 i hoaN-hi2 ui7-tioh8 kiu3 Lan5-sim, chiu7 koat-sim thiu huih hou7 Lan5-sim.  Au7-lai5 Lan5-sim piN7 ho2 e5 si5, kou-po5 ka7 i kong2 chit e5 su7, Lan5-sim chiu7 chau2 khi3 ka7 i-seng seh to-sia7 kong2, “I-seng, chin kam2-sia7 li2 e5 huih, kiu3 tioh8 goa2 e5 siN3-mia7, goa2 beh eng2-oan2 kam2-kek put-chin7.”  I-seng kong2, ‘’Lan5-sim, iau2 u7 chit8 e5 lang5 chiong I e5 huih hou7 li2, chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou.”  I-seng chiu7 chiong Ia5-sou siu7 teng3-si2 e5 su7 kong2 hou7 Lan5-sim thiaN, hit jit8 Lan5-sim kap i e5 kou-po5 chiap-siu7 Ia5-sou choe3 in e5 Kiu3-chu2.
 
Phi3-ju7 kou3-su7
U7 chit8 chiah chun5 beh khi3 Bi2-kok, chun5-teng2 chin-choe7 si7 u7 chiN5 e5 lang5, chiah e5 to si7 siuN7 beh khi3 Bi2-kok than3 khah choe7 chiN5 e5 lang5.
Chun5 kau3 toa7 hai2-tiong, thiN i2-keng ou-am3, tai7-ke long2 teh khun3, u7 lang5 ti7 bang7 tiong teh bang7-kiN3 than3 toa7 chiN5.  Am3-mi5 na2 chhim, hut-jian5 hai2-hong toa7 khi2, hai2-eng2 toa7 poa3, kiong5-hong po7-u2 chiap-soa3 tit8-tit8 lai5.  Chun5-lai7 e5 lang5 tui3 bang7-tiong chhiN2 khi2-lai5, khoaN3 tioh8 che cheng5-heng5, tak8 e5 to chin kiaN-hiaN5.  Na7-si7 po7-hong, po7-u2 ke3-siok8 teh hoah-hiam3, hai2-eng2 na2 lai5 na2 toa7.  Keng-ke3 3 tiam2 cheng, chun5 in-ui7 hou7 hai2-eng2 phah tong3 boe7 tiau5, chiam7-chiam7 khai-si2 tim5 loh8-khi3, chun5-lai7 e5 lang5 koh-khah si7 kiaN, ti3-kau3 toa7 siaN hoah “kiu3-mia7 !!’’  Chong2-si7 keng3-jian5 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang ban2-hoe5 chit chiah chun5, bo5 ku2 chiu7 siu7 hai2-chui2 thun loh8-khi3, cheng3-lang5 long2 tim5-loh8 hai2-tiong.
Te7 ji7 jit8, hai2-siong7 i2-keng peng5-cheng7, u7 nng7 lang5 phu5 ti7 hai2-bin7, chit8 e5 si7 mouh ti7 chun5 e5 chha5-pan2, koh chit8 e5 si7 li7-iong7 siu5 chui2 e5 pun2-su7 lai5 chi-chhi5 siN3-mia7.  Tan7-si7 kau3 chit e5 si5, khiok chit8 tiam2-a2 khui3-lat8 to eng7 chin7, na7 bo5 lang5 kiu3 i chiu7 beh bo5 mia7.  I khai-si2 eng7 loan2-jiok8 e5 siaN-im hoah kong2, “Kiu3-mia7!’’  “Kiu3-mia7!”  Hit si5 mouh ti7 chha5-pan2 hit e5 lang5 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, thiaN-tioh8 che siaN-im, chiu7 siu5-khi3 i e5 sin-piN, mng7 i kong2, ‘’Li2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5 a2 m7-si7?”  In3 kong2, “M7-si7.’’  Ki-tok-tou5 chiu7 chiong hit te3 chha5-pan2 hou7 i, soa3 ka7 i kong2, “Hi-bong7 li2 e5 siN3-mia7 iau2 oah8 teh e5 tiong-kan u7 ki-hoe7 hoan2-hoe2 choe3 Ki-tok-tou5, in-ui7 goa2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, sou2-i sui-jian5 si2 ti7 chit e5 hai2-tiong, goa2 e5 leng5-hun5 oe7 an-jian5 to3-khi3 thian-tong5.”  Hit te3 chha5-pan2 thai3 soe3, ho5 hoat-tit chi-chhi5 nng7 lang5, sou2-i2 Ki-tok-tou5 chiu7 li7-khui chit te3 pan2, niu7 hit e5 lang5 mouh teh, ka-ki7 siu5 chui2 tit8 kau3 khui3-lat8 chin7 chiu7 tim5--loh8-khi3.
 
Koh ke3 liau2 nng7 jit8, u7 chit8 chiah kiu3-mia7 chun5 lai5, toa3 chit8 e5 lang5 chiuN7 hoaN7.  Chit e5 lang5 hui-siong5 e5 kam2-kek kiu3 i e5 Ki-tok-tou5, tui3 an2-ni chiu7 kau3 le2-pai3-tng5 choe3 Ki-tok-tou5.  Chiam7-chiam7 liau2-kai2 Siong7-te3 ki5-biau7 e5 toa7 thiaN3, soa3 chiap-siu7 Ia5-sou choe3 Kiu3-chu2.  Au7-lai5 chiaN5-choe3 jiat8-sim e5 Ki-tok-tou5, siong5-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, soa3 kian3-cheng3 Siong7-te3 sou2 si-loh8 hou7 i e5 kiu3-un e5 keng-ke3.
Phi3-ju7 kou3-su7
U7 chit8 mi5-hng, teh-beh khun3 i2-cheng5, ma-ma ka7 San-san kong2 be2-iah8 e5 kou3-su7.  San-san an-cheng7 teh thiaN, in-ui7 i chin ai3 phiau-liang7 e5 be2-iah8.  Ma-ma khui-chhui3 chiu7 kong2, “Siat-su2 be2-iah8 na7 oe7 kong2-oe7, i it-teng7 ka7 li2 kong2 e7-bin7 e5 kou3-su7.’’
“Tai7-seng goa2 put-ko3 si7 chit8 chiah thou5-sek e5 soe3 be2 mou5-thang5, ti7 thou5-kha so5-lai5 so5-khi3.  Goa2 chin kiaN lang5 ka7 goa2 lap si2.  Na7 u7 lang5 kiaN5 ke3 goa2 e5 sin-piN, in oe7 khoaN3-khin goa2 kong2, “Heih!”  In-ui7 goa2 siN-choe3 thai3 phaiN2 khoa3, put-ko3 chhiu7-teng2 e5 soe3-chiah chiau2-a2 ka7 goa2 kong2, “U7 chit8 jit8 li2 chong2 oe7 piN3-choe3 chin bi2-le7 e5.”  Goa2 m7-chai in sou2 ka7 goa2 kong2 e5 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3. Han5-leng2 e5 pak-hong chhe lai5 ka7 goa2 kong2, “U chit8 jit8 li2 oe7 pian3-chiaN5 chin sui2!”  Goa2 iau2 si7 boe7 beng5-pek8.  Goa2 chi2-u7 chai-iaN2 pak-hong sit8-chai7 thai3 leng2 ouh.  Goa2 koat-teng7 pe5-chiuN7 chhiu7-ki teng2, sou2-i2 khai-si2 ban7-ban7 peh khi2-khi3 kam2-kak chin ai3 khun3, sou2-i2 goa2 chiu7 thou3 si, che3-cho7 chit8 e5 soe3 bin5-chhng5, goa2 jip8 ti7 hit lai7-bin7, na2-chun2 jip8 ti7 lang5 e5 koaN-chha5, to2 ti7 chit e5 song2-khoai3 e5 bin5-chhng5 lai7, chiu7 khun3--khi3, khun3 liau2 chit8 le2-pai3 koh chit8 le2-pai3.
Bo5 ku2, chhiu7-hioh8 khai-si2 chit8-phiN3 ka-lauh8 toe7-chiuN7, thiN-teng2 pe-lai5 pe-khi3 peh8-peh8 e5 seh, chiong goa2 e5 sio2 bin5-chhng5 e5 goa7-bin7 kham3-tit chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 nia2 sui2 e5 peh8 than2-a2.  Chiam7-chiam7 chhun-thiN chiu7 lai5, chiau2-a2 ti7 chhiu7-ki teng2 koh teh kong2, “U7 chit8 jit8 li2 oep7iN-choe3 chin bi2-le7.”  Chong2-si7 goa2 iau2 teh khun3.  Sin hoat-ge5 e5 chhiu7-hioh8 kong2, “U7 chit8 jit8 li2 oe7 piN3-choe3 chin bi2-le7.”  Tan7-si7, goa2 iau2 si7 teh ho2-khun3.  Un-loan2 e5 hou7 tih ti7 goa2 e5 bin5-chhng5 goa7-bin7, ka7 goa2 kong2, “U7 chit8 jit8 li2 oe7 piN3-choe3 chin bi2-le7.”  Che 
 
hou7-tih khin-khin kio3-chhiN2 goa2.  Goa2 chiu7 khai-si2 tui3 bin5-chhng5 peh khi2-lai5.  ChhiN-na5-sek e5 thian-kong kap chhan3-lan7 e5 jit8-kng tui3 goa2 kong2, “Li2 oe7 pian3-chiaN5 bi2-le7.”  Goa2 chin si7 boe7 beng5-pek8, ui7 sim2-mih8 in tui3 goa2 chhiuN3 chit khoan2 koa.
In-ui7 khun3-liau2 ke3 kui-e5 koaN5-thiN, goa2 e5 bin5-chhng5 soa3 chhou-chhou koh lah-sap, sou2-i2 goa2 chiu7 koat-teng7 khui chit8 e5 khang thang so5 chhut-khi3.  Goa2 chhi3 so5 khoaN3-mai7, na7-si7 goa2 kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3, in-ui7 goa2 e5 ka-chiah teng2 u7 chit8 koa2 tang7-tang7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Che si7 sim2-mih8?  Goa2 chhi3-khoaN3 oe7 thang tui3 goa2 e5 ka-chiah theh8 hiah e5 tang7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 hiat-kak8 boe7.  Ouh!  Goa2 chit8-e7 chim-chiok khoaN3, he si7 chit8 tui3 chin bi2-le7 e5 sit8, pi2 jim7-ho5 ui7-tou5-ka sou2 ui7 e5 koh-khah sui2.  Gos2 ban7-ban7 eng7 chit e5 sit8 chhi3 pe khoaN3-mai7, na2 pe na2 koaiN5, pe kau3 kng-iau7 e5 jit8-thau5 tiong.  Ta-pou gin2-na2 kap cha-bou2 gin2-na2 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 to hoah kong2, “Sui2 ah!  Be2-iah8!!”  In siuN7 beh liah8 goa2, na7-si7 goa2 kou3-i3 pe na2 koaiN5.
Chit chiah be2-iah8 khi2-chhou si7 chit8 be2 thang5, na2-chun2 si2-khi3 e5 khoan2-sit, ji5-chhiaN2 chhin-chhiuN7 bai5-chong3 ti7 bong7-lai7, tan7-si7 koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5, chiaN5-choe3 bi2-le2 e5 be2-iah8.  Ma-ma ke3-siok8 mng7 San-san kong2, “Li2 hoaN-hich2oe thang5 a2-si7 be2-iah8?”  San-san lian5-lian5 in3 kong2, “Ouh!  Ma-ma, bian2 kong2 si7 ai3 sui2 e5 be2-iah8.”
Sio2 peng5-iu2, lan2 na7 sin3 Ia5-sou, u7 chit8 jit8 ia7 beh piN3-choe3 pi2 be2-iah8 koh-khah sui2 e5 sin-the2, kap Chu2 Ia5-sou saN-kap toa3.  Lan2 tioh8 kam2-sia7 o-lo2 Chu2, in-ui7 I tui3 si2 koh-oah8, hou7 lan2 oe7 thang tit-tioh8 sin e5 oah8-mia7.
Phi3-ju7 kou3-su7
(1)Ti7 ke-tiong chin7 choe3 toa7 pun2-hun7.  
 
Sio2 peng5-iu2, Siong7-te3 tau3-ti2 ai3 lan2 mui2 jit8 choe3 sim2-mih8? Li2 bat an2-ni mng7 ke3 bo5?  Tong-jian5 lin2 to long2 chai-iaN2, Siong7-te3 ai3 lan2 bo5 oan-ke, bo5 chu7-su, bo5 pin5-toaN7, bo5 sui5-pian7 phoe-pheng5 lang5, bo5 phian3 lang5.  Siong7-te3 ai3 lan2 bol5un ti7 chhu3-nih, a2 si7 hak8-hau7 nih, tak8 jit8 eng5-kng I.  Bo5 lun7 toa7 hang7 su7, a2-si7 soe3 hang7 tai7-chi3, Siong7-te3 ai3 lan2 hou7 I hoaN-hi2.
Bat u7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, mia5 kio3 Sio2-tek, phoa3-piN7 choe7-choe7 ni5.  I e5 sim sou2 ng3-bang7 e5, chiu7-si7 oe7 thang kappat8 e5 sio2 peng5-iu2 chit8 iuN7, chu7-iu5 ti7 jit8-thau5 e5 e7-bin7 thiau3-lai5 thiau3-khi3 kap peng5-iu2 thit-tho5.  I cha2 i2-keng tho2-ia3 ti7 sek-lai7 hiah e5 bo5 pian3-oaN7 e5 ka-ku7.  Jit8-si5 ti7 goa7-bin7 hiah e5 sio2 peng5-iu2 e5 hoah kio3 e5 siaN, chi2-u7 cheng-ka i kou-tok8 e5 kam2-kak.  Ma-ma siong5-siong5 ui7-tioh8 i e5 piN7 ki5-to2, ia7 ka3-si7 Sio2-tek an2-choaN2 ka-ki7 tui3 ThiN-pe7 ki5-kiu5, hou7 i e5 piN7 cha2-jit8 ho2.  Na7-si7 Sio2-tek e5 piN7 goan5-goan5 boe7 khah khi2-sek, hou7 i kam2-kak sit-bong7.  U7 chit8 jit8, i mng7 ma-ma kong2, “Kui-jit8 to2 ti7 piN7-chhng5 e5 gin2-na2, Siong7-te3 ai3 i choe3 sim2-mih8?”  Ma-ma khoaN3 Sio2-tek, chin leng2-cheng7 ka7 i kong2, “Siong7-te3 hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 oah8-teh, to u7 I e5 i3-su3, in-ui7 lang5 oah8-teh e5 bok8-tek, si7 teh chin7 lang5 e5 pun2-hun7 lai5 eng5-kng Siong7-te3, chiau3-kou3 ka-ki7 e5 sin-the2, thiaN pe7-bu2 e5 oe7, bo5 hou7 pe7-bu2 kan-khou2 sim, ka-ki7 ia7 bo5 sit-khi3 ng3-bang7, eng2-oan2 sin3-nai7 Siong7-te3, che to si7 lang5 oah8-teh e5 chek-jim7.”  Sio2-tek thiaN ma-ma e5 oe7 liau2-au7, chiu7 bo5 koh iu-chhiu5 lah.  Ka7 ma-ma kong2, sui-jian5 i to2 ti7 piN7-chhng5-nih, beh eng7 sou2 oe7 e5 khoai3-lok8 sim-cheng5 ti7 chhu3-lai7 ke3 jit8, lai5 eng5-kng Siong7-te3.
(2)Seng5-khun2 thai7-jin5 e5 po2-kui3
Kang Lau7-su ti7 hak8-ki5 be2 choe3-au7 chit8 chiat kho3 e5 si5, eng7 tim5-tang7 e5 gu2-khi3 ka7 i e5 4 ni5 ai3-pan e5 hak8-seng saN-si5.  I chin-sit8 li7 boe7 khui hiah e5 thian-chin kho2-ai3 e5 hak8-seng, na7-si7 put-tek-i2 tioh8 li7-khui in, in-ui7 bo5 ku2 i tioh8 kau3 chin hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 kap chit8 e5 lam5-chu2 kiat-hun, sou2-i2 i i2-keng the5-chhut si5-jim7 lau7-su e5 chit.  Si3 ni5 ai3-pan e5 tong5-oh8 ia7 li7 boe7 khui in e5 Kang Lau7-su, in-ui7 i chin eng7 sim kau3-to7 in, khoan3-to7 in, chhin-chhiuN lau7-bu2 a2-si7 a-chi2 chit8 iuN7 teh khoan2-thai7 
 
hak8-seng.  Tng i tui3 hak8-seng kong2 chai3-hoe7 e5 si5, lau5 bak8-sai2, ia7 ka7 mui2 chit8 e5 hak8-seng chiok-hok hak8-giap8 chin3-pou7.  Hak8-seng sang3 Kang Lau7-su, jian5-au7 to3-khi3 chhu3-nih, ia7 kap pe7-bu2 kong2-khi2 Kang Lau7-su e5 su7.  In e5 pe7-bu2 to in-ui7 kam2-kek Kang Lau-suit-hiong3 e5 iong7 sim kau2-to7, tak8 lang5 to chun2-pi7 le2-mih8.  Tng Kang Lau7-su thau2-khui hiah e5 le2-mih8 e5 si5, ki5-tiong u7 chit8 pau hui-siong5 hi-ki5 e5.  Li2 siuN7 si7 sim2-mih8?  He si7 12 te3 eng7 chhiu2 thiN7 e5 toh-pou3 kap jio7-chu7-a2, lai7-bin7 u7 chit8 tiuNphoe sia2 kong2, “Chhin-ai3 e5 Kang Lau7-su, thiaN kong2 sang3 lang5 e5 kiat-hun le2-mih8 to ai3 sin--e5, na7-si7 goa2 chi2-u7 11khou 4 kak, sit8-chai7 boe2 boe7 khi2 sim2-mih8 kui3-tiong7 e5 sinle2-mih8.  Hian7-chai7 goa2 iong7 sou2 u7 11 khou 4 kak, boe2 pou3, chhin-chhiu2 lai5 choe3 chiah e5, hi-bong7 li2 chiong-lai5 thang iong7. Thai2 oe7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3?”  In kong2, “Si7 koaN-tiuN2 hoan-hu3 goan2 siu khi2-lai5, chi2 iau3 li2 na7 tap-eng3 goan2 i2-au7 bo5 koh sin3 Siong7-te3, kou2 chiu7 koh heng5 li2.”  Bu2-seng thiaN liau2 gong7-gong7 m7-chai beh chaiN2-iuN7, au7-lai5 kong2, “Siat-su2 li2 chiong se3-kan sou2 u7 e5 kou2 sang3 goa2, goa2 ia7 boe7 thang bo5 sin3 Siong7-te3 !”  Kong2 liau2 chiu7 choan2 ke3-lai5, kiaN5 chhut iaN5-pang5.  Tai7-ke to siuN7 kong2 i chhin-chhiuN7 teh-beh khau3--chhut-lai5.  Chit nng7 e5 su7-peng koh siuN7 chit8 e5 pan7-hoat, siong-sin3 Bu2-seng it-teng7 oe7 pang3-sak Siong7-te3.  Chiah8 am3-tng3 liau2-au7, in chiu7 kap Bu2-seng saN-kap lai5 kau3 kong-lou7 siong7, in tui3 Bu2-seng kong2, “KoaN-tiuN2 kong2, li2 chin-chiaN3 na7 koh ke3-siok8 khoaN3 Seng3-keng, chiu7 boe7 thang ti7 goan2 e5 pou7-tui7 nih, beh kio3 li2 to3-khi3.”  Che chit pian3, Siong7-te3 put-tan3 kap Bu2-seng tong5-chai7, soa3 su3 i tam2-liong7 lai5 choe3 Siong7-te3 sou2 hoaN-hi2 e5 su7, sou2-i2 Bu2-seng sui-jian5 lan5 ke3 ti3-kau3 lau5 bak8-sai2, na7-si7 long2 bo5 tiu5-tu5, long2 bo5 oat8-thau5, ng3 beh to3-khi3 i e5 chhu3 e5 lou7 tit8 kiaN5.  Chitnng7 e5 su7-peng khoaN3 liau2, koaN2-kin2 khiu2 Bu2-seng, ka7 i kong2, “Bu2-seng m7-thang khi3, si7 goan2 teh kong2-chhio3 e5, goan2 be7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 lang5 chiah-nih thiaN3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, goan2 chhim-sin3 Siong7-te3 it-teng7 kap li2 tong5-chai7.  I na7 bo5 kap li2 chit khoan2 lang5 saN-kap ti7-teh, beh kap sim2-mih8 khoan2 lang5 ti7-teh nih?  In nng7 lang5 chiu7 siu7 kam2-tong7 sin3 Siong7-te3.
Phi3-ju7 kou3-su7
Kou2 Peng5 si7 thiaN3 Siong7-te3 e5 gin2-na2, i e5 lau7-pe7 si7 chit8 e5 khian5-seng5 e5 
 
Ki-tok-tou5.  Tui3 i e5 lau7-bu2 si2 liau2-au7, in pe7-kiaN2 koh-khah si7 siN3-mia7 saN kiat-lian5.  Kou2 Sian-siN si7 chit8 e5 boe7 chhai3 e5, chong2-si7 le2-pai3-jit8 i m7-na7 bo5 khi3 choe3 seng-li2, hoan2-tng2 ka7 bok8-su theh8 chit8 koa2 hok-im thoan5-toaN, ti7 le2-pai3 e7-pou kap i e5 kiaN2 Kou2 Peng5 khi3 pun thoan5-toaN.  Chit e5 kang hou7 in pe7-kiaN2 u7 chin toa7 e5 hi2-lok8.  In siuN7, in sui-jian5 bat ji7 bo5 choe7, in ia7 oe7 ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang, chhoa7 lang5 kui Chu2.
U7 chit8 e5 le2-pai3 e7-pou, ou-hun5 bit8-pou3, saN soa3 loh8 toa7-hou7, soa3 hou7 in pe7-kiaN2 chhu2-siau chhut-khi3 pun thoan5-toaN e5 liam7-thau5.  In siuN7, kin-a2-jit8 mai3 khi3 pun, toa3 chhu3-nih lai5 khun3 chit8-e7.  Chong2-si7 Kou2 Peng5 sim-lai7 chin bo5 peng5-an, i liah8 choe3 bo5 eng-kai ui7-tioh8 lui5-kong sih-na3 loh8 toa7-hou7 chiu7 bo5 khi3 pun thoan5-toaN, sou2-i2 tui3 bin5-chhng5-nih peh khi2-lai5 che7, tui3 pa-pa kong2, “Pa-pa, Seng3-keng kiam2 m7-si7 kong2, bo5-lun7 ta2-loh8 khi3, Siong7-te3 beh kap lan2 saN-kap ti7-teh mah?  Goa2 siuN7, goa2 tioh8 lai5-khi3 pun, goa2 chhim-sin3 Siong7-te3 it-teng7 oe7 kap goa2 tong5-chai7, goa2 chiu7 bo5 kiaN it-chhe3.”
Kou2 Peng5 chheng7 hou7-saN, toa3 50 tiuN thoan5-toaN, ka-ki7 chit8 e5 chiu7 chhut-khi3.  In-ui7 loh8-hou7, chin chio2 lang5 chhut-lai5 ti7 koe-lou7, sou2-i2 bo5 iong5-iN7 chiong thoan5-toaN sangh33 chit8 e5 lang5.  I kiaN5 chin hng7 e5 lou7, chhiu2-nih e5 thoan5-toaN chiah ban7-ban7 kiam2-chio2.  Bo5 ku2, tak8 keng chhu3 e5 tian7-he2 long2 toh8 khi2-lai5, na7-si7 Kou2 Peng5 koat-sim beh pun kau3 liau2 chiah beh to3-khi3.  Choe3-au7 chhun chit8 tiuN, chong2-si7 khah kiaN5 to long2 bo5 ke3 lou7 lang5.  Hit si5 hut-jian5 chu3-i3 tioh8 hng7-hng7 u7 chit8 keng ke7-ke7 e5 chhau2-chhu3, si3-piN long2 bo5 chhu3.  I siuN7 beh chiong sou2 chhun hit tiuN thoan5-toaN sang3 hou7 hit keng chhu3 e5 lang5, na7-si7 thiN ou, hou7 toa7, koh bo5 lou7-teng, sim-lai7 u7 kiaN-hiaN5, hit si5 hut-jian5 siuN7 tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7 kong2, “Li2 bo5-lun7 ta2-loh8 khi3, goa2 beh kap li2 ti7-teh.”  Kou2 Peng5 chek-si5 chong3-taN2 khi2-lai5, boe7 ki3-tit it-chhe3, tit8-tit8 kiaN5 kau3 hit e5 chhau2-chhu3 mng5 khau2.  I kau3 ui7, chiu7 khin-khin phah mng5, chiong hit tiuN thoan5-toaN tui3 mng5-phang7 ka7 i long jip8-khi3, hoaN-hi2 to3-lai5.  
Te7 ji7 le2-pai3-jit8 cha2-khi2, Kou2 Peng5 choe3 le2-pai3 e5 si5, u7 chit8 e5 lau7-lang5 khi2-lai5 choe3 kian3-cheng3 kong2, “Goa2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 kiu3 goa2 chit8 tiau5 siN3-mia7.  Teng2 le2-pai3-jit8 am3-si5, tng goa2 hoe-sim song3-chi3, siuN7 beh chu7-sat e5 si5, tut8-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 
 
u7 lang5 phah-mng5, u7 chit8 tiuN thoan5-toaN tui3 mng5-phang7 long jip8-lai5, theng3-hau7 goa2 khi3 khui mng5 e5 si5, khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 i2-keng kiaN5 khi3 chin hng7 lah; chong2-si7 goa2 khiok in-ui7 hit tiuN thoan5-toaN lai5 chhu2-siau chu7-sat e5 liam7-thau5.  Hian7-chai7 goa2 i2-keng siong-sin3 Ia5-sou, kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 e5 un-tian2.”  I kong2 e5 si5, koh theh8 hit tiuN thoan5-toaN khi2-lai5 hou7 tai7-ke khoaN3, Kou2 Peng5 bo5 siuN7 kau3 Siong7-te3 keng3-jian5 eng7 i lai5 kiu3 chit e5 lang5.  I hui-siong5 hoaN-hi2, toa7 siaN kong2, “Pa-pa, hit tiuN thoan5-toaN si7 goa2 pun e5 !”
Kok ui7 tong5-oh8, Kou2 Peng5 hoat thoan5-toaN goa2 siuN7 li2 ia7 oe7 choe3.  Li2 ka-ki7 siuN7, si7 m7-si7 u7 Kou2 Peng5 hit khoan2 thiaN3 Siong7-te3 kap thiaN3 lang5 e5 leng5-hun5 e5 jiat8-sim a2 bo5?  Li2 si7 m7-si7 u7 chhim-sin3 Siong7-te3 kap li2 ti7-teh a2 bo5?  Li2 ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang e5 si5, li2 sin3 I oe7 su3 li2 tam2-liong7 kap ti3-hui7 mah?  Kim-jit8 Siong7-te3 ai3 eng7 li2, li2 kheng2 m7-kheng2 hou7 I su2-iong7?
