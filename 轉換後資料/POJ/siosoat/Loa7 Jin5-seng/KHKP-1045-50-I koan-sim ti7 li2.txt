
I KOAN-SIM TI7 LI2 
LOA7 JIN5-SENG 
Beh am3 lah, goa2 ti7 thak8-chheh-thiaN teh thak8-chheh, hut-jian5 bok8-su-niut5ui goa7-bin7 jip8-lai5, ka7 goa2 kong2, i to3-lai5 lah; soa3 kong2 i kin-a2-jit8 ti7 Chiong-hoa3 I-koan2 khau2 tu2-tioh8 Chhui3-ha5.  Goa2 mng7 i kong2, si7 to3 chit8 e5 Chhui3-ha5 (翠霞). I in3 kong2, si7 tiam3-ti7 Sui7-lok8 (瑞鹿)hit e5.  I koaN2-kin2 beh khi3 chu2 e5-hng tng3, chiu7 cho3 i oat-khi3 chau3-kha.
Goa2 e5 sim chin kin2, liam5-piN siuN7 tioh8 goa2 20 ni5 cheng5 khi3 jim7 in e5 kau3-hoe7 Liong5-chui2 (龍水) tong-si5 e5 cheng5-heng5.  Hit-si5 Chhui3-ha5 iok 32,3 he3, tng Liong5-chui2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 chip-su7, hoaN7 kau3-hoe7 e5 koe3-ke3, siong5-siong5 theh8 chiN5 lai5 hou7 goa2, siong5 kap goan2 che7 han5-tam5 ke3-khi3 e5 su7, sou2-i2 goa2 kap goa2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 long2 kap i chin chhin-bit8, i ia7 chin jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2.
Lun7 kau3 chit e5 chi2-be7 u7 kau3 phaiN2-mia7, i e5 lau7-bu2 chiu2-koaN2, chiu2 i kap chit8 e5 sio2-ti7.  Lau7-bu2 choe3 tiuN2-lo2, lang5 long2 kio3 i “Ia5-sou Han2--ah”, Han2 koaN teh boe7 te5-khou, na2 boe7 na2 thoan5 Ia5-sou, tek8-piat8 te7 7 jit8 bo5 boe7 choan-siu2 le2-pai3, sun5 hoe7-iu2 thoan5 Ia5-sou, sou2-i2 hit toe7-hng e5 gin2-na2 na7 tu2-tioh8 i chiu7 liam7“6 jit8 boe7 te5-khou, 7 jit8 thoan5 Ia5-sou”, tui3 an2-ni Ia5-sou Han2--ah e5 mia5 soa3 tiau5 leh.  Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 i m7-na7 u7 iong2-kiaN7 e5 kha thang lok8-lok8 kiaN5 boe7 te5-khou.  koh hou7 i oe7-hiau2 ka7 lang5 choe3 san2-po5.  Tui3 kui2 chap8 ni5 cheng5 i chiu7 kam2-kak u7 chit e5 su-iau3, chiu7 hou7 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khi3 thak8 san2-po5 (助產士), khiam7-tng5 neh-tou7 lai5 poe5-iuN2 chit echa5-bou2-kiaN2 kau3 seng5-kong.  Chhui3-ha5 20 thong2 he3, li7 chit8 ni5 30 thong2 ni5 tui3 san2-po5 hak8-hau7 pit-giap8, ti7 in Liong5-chui2 koe-a2 thang kong2 chin ho2 mia5, siN choe3 sui2, leng2-li7 koh ho2 lu2-tek, sui-bong2 ka-teng5 bo5 ho2-giah8, iau2-ku2 mui5-lang5 sio khok8-thau5.  Au7-lai5 ke3 hou7 Sui7-lok8 koe-a2 e5 Tan5 I-hak8-su7, thong koe-a2 long2 oe7 han2 khi2-lai5, che u7 choe7-choe7 goan5-in:  (1) Chhui3-ha5 si7 san3-hiong-ke chhut-sin koh lau7-pe7 cha2 ke3-sin, beh thai3 oe7 kham-tit phit-phoe3 Tan5 ka in oe7 tit ke3, in si7 Jit8-pun2 Tang-kiaN “Jit8-pun2 I-kho tai7-hak8” khi2-kho-pou7 chhut-sin e5 khi2-kho i-hak8-su7.   
(2)Tan5 Hak8-su7 in tau u7 kui2-na7 e5 hiaN-ti7 be7 pun-khui, toa7 ka-chok8, bo5 sin3 Chu2, tai7-ke hoan2-tui3 chhoa7 hit-ho7 loh8-kau3-po5, koh mng5-hong bo5 tui3.  Na7-si7 chit e5 chhin-su7 an2-choaN2 oe7 seng5?  goan5-in(1) Tan5 Sian-siN ti7 Jit8-pun2 u7 teh kiu5-to7, siong5 ti7 kau3-hoe7 chhut-jip8, u7 liau2-kai2 Ki-tok-kau3; koh Chhui3-ha5 e5 lau7-bu2 jiat8-sim siuN7 ke kiu3 chit8 e5 lang5. (2) Tan5 Sian-siN bo5 iau3-kin2 mng5-hong, ke3-chng, i tiong7 sit8-che3, lu2-chu2 gau5, sui2, leng2-li7, ho2 phin2-heng7, chu7 an2-ni chiu7 ka7 i sang3-tiaN7 chhoa7 ke3-mng5.
Ah! hok-khi3 ah! Chhui3-ha5 Sio2-chia2 lang5 long2 kong2 si7 “phaN3-chhek thiauj3…p chhng”.  Kho2-sioh ho2-keng2 bo5 ku2, chhin-chhiuN7 “Goat8 ha7 bi2-jin5” (月下美人), soat-peh8 e5 beng5-hoa, tng oa2 poaN3-mi5 seng7-khui, siap-si5-kan chit8-bak8-nih chiu7 tiau-sia7.  Chhui3-ha5 Sio2-chia2 chhut-si3 ti7 san3-hiong ke, cha2 chiu7 keng-ke3 kou-ji5 e5 seng-oah8, sit-loh8 lau7-pe7, tui3 koaN2-hu7 e5 lau7-bu2 lai5 poe5-iuN2, kui2-hun u7 khah chu7-pi-kam2.  Ka-chai3 tiong7-hu chin the2-thiap, m7-ku2 bi2-tiong u7 chit8 hang7 put-chiok, chiu7-si7 Tan5 Sian-siN m7-kaN2 kong-jian5 sin3 Chu2, goan5-in si7 thong Sui7-lok8 koe-a2 bo5 poaN3 e5 sin3 Ia5-sou jip8-kau3, koh hiaN-ti7 chhin-chiaN5 gou7-chap8, ti-ki2 e5 peng5-iu2 tai7-ke to ki-chhi3 leng2-chhio3, kong2 i kiaN-bou2 chiah te3 in bou2 khi3 chiah8-kau3, sou2-i2 khi2-thau5 kap Chhui3-ha5 Sio2-chia2 choe3-tin7 khi3 Liong5-chui2 le2-pai3 nng7-pai2-a2 chiu7 pang3-soah khi3 lah.  Tong-si5 Sui7-lok8 bo5 pai3-tng5, long2 tioh8 lai5 Liong5-chui2, saN-li7 iok tai5-li2 6 li2 lian5-lou7, beh che7 he2-chhia bo5 li7-pian7, in chhu3 khi2-kho i-iN7 kiaN5 kau3 Sui7-lok8 chhia-cham7, koh Liong5-chui2 chhia-cham7 kiaN5 kau3 le2-pai3-tng5 2 cham7 hap8 choe3-he2 iok tai5-li2 3 li2 lou7.  SaN-chap8 goa7 ni5-cheng5 kong-kiong7 khi3-chhia bo5 chiah li7-pian7, sou2-i2 Chhui3-ha5 Sio2-chia2 na7 beh to3-lai5 le2-pai3, khioh-khi2-kha eng7 kiaN5--e5 khah khoai3, Tan5 Sian-siN beh te3 i an2-ni kiaN5 bo5 koan3, chiu7 na2 pang3 leng2 khi3.
Keh-ni5 siN thau5-chiuN7-a2 si7 ta-pou--e5, lang5 e5 ki-chhi3 khoaN3-khin chiu7 khah chio2.  An2-ni Siong7-te3 un-tian2 thau5-be2 ke3 7 ni5 siN 3 e5 hau7-siN, toa7 e5 ho7 choe3 Eng-tiat8 (英哲), te7-ji7 e5 ho7 choe3 Eng-lam5(英男), te7-saN e5 ho7 choe3 Eng-beng5 (英明).  Sian-siN ai3 chit8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, ia7 thang tau3 io sio2-ti7-a2, Sian-siN-niu5 ai3 pun sin3 Chu2 e5, ng3-bang7 kap i khah tang5-sim, ko2-jian5 chiu7 tui3 Tai5-tiong ti7 jiat8-sim sin3 Chu2 e5 ka-teng5 pun chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 mia5 Siok-kheng (淑卿)lai5 choe3 cha-bou2-kiaN2, pun lai5 e5 si5 tu2 11,2 he3 lah, sian-siN-niu5 Chhui3-ha5 chin thiaN3 i, hou7 i chiap-soa3 thak8 Kong-hak8-hau7 (國民學校), au7-lai5 pit-giap8, kho2 jip8 chhou-tiong.  An2-ni chit8-ke 6 e5, koh toa7-peh  
sio2-chek khiN5-khiN5 teh choe3, i-iN7 lai7 ge5-khi2 ti7-liau5-chhng5 kui2-na7 tiuN, long7-long7-kun2 teh choe3.  Thou2-oe7 kong2,“Tng ang5, tng chheng3”, hiaN-ti7-a2 eng7 Chhui3-ha5 chi2-be7 e5 tiong7-hu choe3 tiong-sim khiN5 teh choe3.  Tai7-ke siong-an bos5u, toa7-m2 kio3 i ji7-chim2, soe3-chim2-a2 kioi3 ji7-m2, tai7-ke chhio3 gi7-gi7, chhu3-piN lin5-iu7 tai7-ke sian-siN-niu5 tng5, sian-siN-niu5 te2,si7 ni5 peh choeh le2-mih8 lang5 saN-chiN sang3.  ChiN5 u7, se3 u7, kiaN2 u7, seng-li2 tng ong7.  Tan7-si7 siok8-gu2 kong2, “ThiN u7 put-chhek e5 hong-hun5, lang5 u7 tan3-sek e5 chai-ho7, piN5-toe7 khi2 hong-pho, hou7 tui3 thiN-thang phoah loh8-lai5”.  Chhui3-ha5 28,9 he3, Eng-beng5 chiah 1 he3 goa7, Tan5 Sian-siN jiam2-tioh8 kip-cheng3 2,3 jit8e tiong-kan chit8-piN7 put-khi2, kap se3-kan, bou2 kiaN2, chhin-chhek eng2-oan2 saN-si5 choe3 i khi3.  Chhui3-ha5 tui3 si2 khau3 ke3-lai5, pang3 4 e5 kiaN2, ka-ki7 iau2 siau3-lian5, si3-piN bo5 chit8-oa2, ia7 bo5 chit8 e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 thang an-ui3, kiaN2 siong7 toa7 e5 6 he3, koh lai5 4 he3, soa3-be2-a2 2 he3, ki5-sit8 1 he3 goa7.  Sit8-chai7 ai3-khau3 bo5 bak8-sai2, kui-e5-lang5 long2 geng5 loh8-khi3, boe7 chiah8, kui-sin nng2 so5-so5.  I ka-ki7 khau3,kiaN2 chit8 e5 chiah toa7, chit8 e5 hiah toa7,long2 khiN5-khiN5 lam2-lam2 choe3 chit8-tui,lian5 pun e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ia7 khau3.  Peng5-si5 Siok-kheng chin iu2-hau3, chin thiaN-chhui3, chin gau5 thak8-chheh, ho2 phin2-heng7, gau5 tau3 io sio2-ti7, hou7 Chhui3-ha5 thiaN3 jip8 sim, an2-ni 5 e5 bu2-a2 kiaN2 pang5-keng-mng5 koaiN teh khau3, u7 si5 soa3 boe7-ki3-tit chu2-png7, chin-chiaN3 bak8-sai2 chun2 png7 thun, u7 si5 khun3 cheng-sin5,bak8-sai2 lau5 kau3 chim2-thau5 tam5 lok-lok.  Jin5-cheng5 na2 choa2 tiuN-tiuN poh8,se3-su7 na2 kiaN5-ki5 kiok8-kiok8 sin (人情如紙張張薄, 世事似棋局局新), cha-hng hou7 lang5 ngia5-ngia5 chhiaN2-chhiaN2 e5 sian-siN-niu5, hut-jian5 pian3 koaN2-hu7, u7 e5 tong5-cheng5, u7 e5 bih-chhui3, tong5-cheng5 e5 chio2, thi2-chhio3 ki-chhi3 e5 khah choe7.  Chit8-si5 Chhui3-ha5 oan2-jian5 tui3 soaN-teng2 hou7 lang5 chhia loh8 chhim-ian, m7-chai ju5-ho5 si7 ho2, ou-am3 thiN-toe7.
Chhui3-ha5 tui3 ho2-keng2 jit8-kng phou2-chio3 e5 soaN-chiam, hut-jian5 siak loh8-khi3 ban7-tng7 e5 chhim-ian e5 chit8-e5 siau3-hu7, sit8-chai7 long2 ou-am3 thiN-toe7, si3-bin7 khoaN3 bo5 lou7, jin5-bong5 ka-san3 (人亡家散), lang5 bo5-khi3 lah, Tan5-ka soaN3-khi3 lah, thou2-oe7 siok8-gu2 kong2-khi2 u7-iaN2,“Jit8-thau5 chhiah iaN7-iaN7, sui lang5 kou3 siN3-mia7”, tai7-lan7 lim5-thau5 kok chu7-hui(大難臨頭各自飛).  TaN tiong-sim jin5 but8 bo5-khi3 lah, han7 chit8 geh8-jit8 lai7 tioh8 kiau2-heng5 khai-giap8 chip-chiau3,Tan5 khi2-kho chi2 ho2 koaiN-mng5 tai7-kiat.  Tng ang5, tng chheng3, seng-li2 long7-long7 kun2, ti7-liau5-chhng5 kui2-na7 tiuN, sim2-mih8 lang5 m7-chai Sui7-lok8 Tan5 khi2-kho.  Hut-jian5 tioh8 koaiN-tiam3, koh tioh8 kiau2 pai5.  Ah! khi3-se3 it lok8 chhian tiong7 (氣勢一落千丈), thi5 chhio3 kai hui (啼笑皆非), chin-chiaN3 si7 beh choaN2-iuN7 kong2?  Toa7-hiaN tah8 toa7-sun, ke-he2 choe3 4 hun7  
pun, Chhui3-ha5 pun 1 hun7, kui2 hun chhan5 koh chit8-kham2 tiam3, chhan5 pak8 lang5 choh, siu m7-chiaN5-mih8, chit8-kham2 tiam3, tiam3-khau2 soe3 lang5 khui pou3-tiam3, au7-bin7 bo2-a2-kiaN2 ka-ki7 toa3.  Chou7-san2-su7 in-ui7 loe2-chi3 bo5 ai3 chhut-khi3 kiN3 lang5, koh gin2-na2 soe3-han3 tiN5 leh, soa3 pang3 hoe3-hoe3.  Chiong an2-ni beh tou7 5 e5 bo2-kiaN2, keng-che3 sit8-chai7 u7 kau3 kan-khou2.
Cheng-sin5-siong7 leh, koh khah chham2!  Cheng3-lang5 kong-kek na2 ban7-chiN3 chhoan-sim; Cha-hng e5 toa7-peh, sio2-chek, toa7-m2, sio2-chim2, kou, i5, ku7, kim7, chhin-chiaN5 gou7-chap8, chhu3-piN peng5-iu2, sio tu2-thau5, bin7 oat8 tui3 piN-a2, u7 e5 bih-chhui3, u7 e5 leng2-chhio3, u7 e5 ki-chhi3.  Chiau3 in e5 i3-su3, kin-a2-jit8 Tan5-ka oe7 tu2-tioh8 chit e5 chai-e7, long2 si7 tui3 Chhui3-ha5, i si7 soah-chhiN, loh8-kau3 po5-a2, toa3 ka-to-peng5, thih sau3-chhiu2, bu2 chiu2-koaN2, u7 cheng2-thoan5, taN i ia7 soah-si2 ang, thih sau3-chhiu2 Tan5-ka kin-a2-jit8 hou7 i sau3 chit8-e7 saN hun gou7 lih8.  Ang5-un7 long2 loh8 soe lah, it-chhe3 e5 choe7 long2 kui hou7 i.  Chhui3-ha5 chi2-u7 tiam7-tiam7 boe7-hiau2 hun-sou3, ia7 bo5 tang3 hun-sou3 khi2; sou2-i2 siong5-siong5 koaiN ti7 lai7-bin7 hau2, chin bo5 ai3 chhut-lai5, bo5 ai3 kiN3-lang5, bo5 ai3 kong2-oe7, chin-chiaN3 si7 kiah8-thau5 m7-khi2.  Chit8-si5 soa3 giau5-gi5 chu7-soe3 tui3 jiat8-sim e5 lau7-bu2 sou2 chiap-siu7 e5 Siong7-te3.  Kiam2 u7-iaN2 u7 Siong7-te3?  Siong7-te3 kiam2 u7-iaN2 tak8 sou2-chai7 ti7-teh?  Kiam2 u7-iaN2 chi3-kong, chi3-gi7, ia7-si7 chu5-pi lin5-bin2 e5 Siong7-te3? Na2 siuN7 na2 giau5-gi5, na7 u7-iaN2 u7 Siong7-te3, I thai3 bo5 khoaN3-kou3 goa2? I thai3 hou7 goa2 tu2-tioh8 an2-ni?  Goa2 hoan7-tioh8 sim2-mih8? Na2 siuN7 na2 giau5-gi5, kiam2 u7-iaN2 si7 bu5 sou2 put ti, choan5-leng5 e5 ThiN-pe7? Na2 u7-iaN2 goa2 ti7 chia kou-chiau2 jip8 long5-kun5, Mou5-kui2 theng2-ui chhin-chhiuN7 hau2-sai beh thun-chiah8, goa2 mi5-jit8 ai-khau3 kiu5-kio3 I e5 mia5, I na2 m7-chai, Chu2 Ia5-sou kong2 I si7 Kiu3-chu2, Siong7-te3 kong2 si7 ThiN-pe7, kau3 si5 long2 m7-bat li2, an2-ni koh keng3-pai3 kiam2 u7 li7-ek?
Sim siong5-siong5 chat8-chat8 chin loe2-chi3, peng5-si5 tiaN7 teh gim5 e5 Seng3-si, i chit-tiap8 thak8 m7-bat, ia7 bo5 i3-su3 ai3 koh gim5.
Chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-si kong2:“ThiN-pe7 goa2 chai it-seng kong-im,
Si7 Li2 ui7 goa2 an-pai5,
 
Sou2-i2 chiong-lai5 sui u7 hoan2-hok,
Goa2 goan7 chiau3 Chu2 sou2 ai3, 
TaN kiu5 ThiN-pe7 hian7-si5 chan7 goa2,
Choan-bu7 hok8-sai7 keng3-pai3.”
Chit chat si i chit8-si5 thak8 bo5?
1.Siong7-te3 na7-si7 ThiN-pe7, goa2 chu7 soe3 chiu7 keng3-pai3, lau7-bu2 si7 jiat8-sim e5 tiuN2-lo2, mi5-jit8 teh thoan5 to7-li2, goa2 ke3-lai5 se3-siok8 tiong, m7-tioh8 hou7 goa2 toa7 hoat-tian2 thang eng5-kng Siong7-te3 e5 mia5.  An2-chaiN2-iuN7 ka7 goa2 an-pai5 siau3-lian5 tioh8 chiu2-koaN2, kong2 si7, “Si7 Li2 ui7 goa2 an-pai5,” i ka-ki7 siuN7 goa2 u7 sim2-mih8 tek-choe7 Siong7-te3 kau3 hiah thiam2, tioh8 hou7 Siong7-te3 an-pai5 an2-ni?
2.   “Sou2-i2 chiong-lai5 sui u7 hoan2-hok, goa2 goan7 chiau3 Chu2 sou2 ai3”.  I ka-kisi7uN si7 chap8-ji7-ban7 hun e5 bo5-tit ta-oa5, sim-koaN chin m7-goan7 sun7-hok8, chin bai5-oan3 mia7 phaiN2, Siong7-te3 e5 lin5-bin2 ti7 to2-ui7? chin m7-goan7.
3.   “TaN kiu5 Siong7-te3 hian7-si5 chan7 goa2, choan-bu7 hok8-sai7 keng3-pai3.”  Chai7 i teh siuN7, goa2 hian7-si5 sim loan7-chhau-chhau si3-bin7 khoaN3 bo5 lou7, thau5-cheng5 u7 moa2-moa2 e5 khou2-lan7 chhi3-lian7, goa2 beh an2-choaN2 lai5 thoa chit 4 e5 kiaN2, tou7 ke3 chit e5 khou2-hai2, chhiaN5 hou7 in chiaN5-lang5?  Goa2 iau2 u7 sim thang kap li2“choan-bu7 hok8-sai7 keng3-pai3”.  Chai7 i siuN7 che si7 sun7-keng2 e5 lang5 teh kong2 e5 liang5-hong-oe7.
Se3-kan chin hian7-sit8, i iau2 siau3-lian5 chit-pai2 i tu2-tioh8 lah, siok8-gu2 kong2,“Hu3 chia2 chhim-san iu2 oan2-chhin, san3 lang5 lou7-piN bu5 kun7-jin5” (富者深山有遠親, 貧者路邊無近人).  Koh kong2, “Kim2 siong7 chai3 thiam hoa, soat tiong put sang3 than3” (錦上再添花, 雪中不送炭).Sit8-chai7 si7 an2-ni, ho2-giah8 lang5 toa3 ti7 chhim-soaN to u7 chhin-chhek, san3-hiong lang5 ti7 lou7-piN to bo5 lang5 beh kun7-oa2.  Kim2-tiu5 to chu7-te2 sui2 lah,chiah koh ka7 i an kim-hoe, san3-lang5 ti7 sng-seh tiong teh  
koaN5 ia7 bo5 lang5 beh sang3 he2-thoaN3, che si7 se3-kan e5 peng5-siong5.
I kam2-kak “Se3-kan jin5-cheng5 leng2-loan2 bu5 siong5 (世間人情冷暖無常), it-hoat loe2-chi3.  chit-si5 soa3 bo5 ai3 khi3 le2-pai3, long2 tu pun e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 Siok-kheng khi3 le2-pai3, thang soa3 kap goa7-ke e5 siN-bo2 lian5-lok8, Siok-kheng na7 lai5 le2-pai3, goa7-ma2 chiu7 mng7, “A-bu2 thai3 bo5 lai5?” Siok-kheng chong2-si7 in3 kong2,“Goan2 a-bu2 kong2 bo5-eng5”, m7 chiu7 kong2, “Thau5-khak thiaN3”, “Gin2-na2 bo5 koai”, chiok-choe7 li2-iu5 thang the.  Chong2 kong2 kian3-siau3 bo5 ai3 kiN3 lang5 e5 bin7, ia7 soa3 bo5 ai3 kiN3 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7.  An2-ni leng5-seng3 na2 tui7-loh8, u7 peng5-an bo5?  Bo5, na2 bo5 peng5-an, sim na2 kan-khou2.  Hit-si5 Chhui3-ha5 siong5 thou2-khui3, taN goa2 tau3-te2 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7?  
U7 chit8 le2-pai3 Siok-kheng khi3 le2-pai3, hit si5 i to Kok-bin5 hak8-hau7 5,6 lian5 lah, thiaN to7-li2 thiaN u7.  Tng2-khi3 hit mi5 ka7 in ma-ma kong2, “Ma-mah, lang5 bok8-su kong2, “Lou7 si7 lang5 kiaN5 chhut-lai5 e5, lang5 na7 tiaN7 tiam7-tiam7 khia7 m7 kiaN5-khi3, thau5-cheng5 eng2-oan2 to bo5 lou7.  Lang5 na7 sin3 Siong7-te3 e5 eng3-un2, thiaN-than3 I e5 oe7 ho2-taN2 chin3-cheng5, sui-jian5 au7-bin7 u7 tui-peng, thau5-cheng5 u7 toa7-hai2 chah8 tiau5 leh, lan2 kha na7 kaN2 tah8 loh8-khi3, chui2 ia7 beh ui7 lan2 pun-khui.
