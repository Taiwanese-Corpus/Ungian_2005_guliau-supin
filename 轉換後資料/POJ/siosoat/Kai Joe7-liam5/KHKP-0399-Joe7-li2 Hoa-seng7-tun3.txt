
Joe7-li2 Hoa-seng7-tun3
Kai Joe7-liam5 ek8
Chha-put-to 160 ni5 cheng5 u7 chit8 jit8 kui2-na7 lang5 ti7 Bi2-kok lam5-peng5 e5 chhiu7-na5 teh choe3 kang.  Tiong-kan u7 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 chin toa7-han3, ia7 chin chhou-iong2, iok-liok8 u7 18 he3.  I ti7 chhiu7-na5 kiaN5-lai5 kiaN5-khi3.  Hut-jian5 ti7 khoe-piN u7 thiaN-kiN3 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 siaN teh hoah kiu3 lang5.  ThiaN-kiN3 siaN chit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 sui5-si5 chau2 chhut chhiu7-na5 khi3 kau3 khoe-piN.  Hit-si5 khoaN3-kiN3 kui2-na7 lang5 teh khiu2 chit e5 hu7-jin5 lang5, in-ui7 i kiong5-kiong5 beh thiau3-loh8 chui2-nih8.  Hu7-jin5 lang5 chhut-siaN kong2, Pang3 goa2 khi3, pang3 goa2, goa2 e5 kiaN2 teh-beh tu7 si2 lah, pang3 goa2.  ThiaN-kiN3 chiah e5 oe7, siau3-lian5 sui5-si5 oe7 ki3-tit chit e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 soe3-han3 kiaN2, i chin koai, ia7 chin sui2.  Hit e7-pou i teh thit-tho5 ti7 khoe-piN, poah8-to2 soa3 hou7 chui2 koah khi3.  
Khoe-nih8 e5 chui2 chin toa7, koh long2 si7 thoaN, khoe tiong long2 u7 toa7 chioh8; u7 choe7-choe7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 gin2-na2 hou7 chui2 lau5 khi3, ia7 m7 kaN2 khi3 kiu3 i.  In kan-ta khia7 teh khoaN3.  Siau3-lian5 lang5 chit8-e7 kau3, sui5-si5 thng3 i e5 saN, bak8-chiu si3-koe3 khoaN3, khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 tiam2 peh8-peh8 ti7 khoe tiong-ng, i sui5-si5 chong5 loh8 khoe-lau5.  I chai hit tiam2 peh8-peh8-e5 si7 gin2-na2 e5 saN.  Hu7-jin5 lang5 koh chhut-siaN kong2, Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 i beh kiu3 goa2 e5 kiaN2, goa2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 kiaN2 ti7-hia lah, i koh phu5 khi2-lai5.  Cheng3 lang5 chau2 khi3 khoe-piN, khoaN3-kiN3 gin2-na2, liam5-piN hou7 khoe-lau5 phah phu5 khi2-lai5, bo5 ku2 iu7-koh hou7 chui2 koah tim5 loh8-khi3.  U7-si5 lau5 loh8 thoaN, u7-si5 lau5 tui3 toa7 chioh8 e5 sin-piN ke3.  U7-si5 chui2 kng2-le5, ka7 i phah kau3 lin-lin gou5.  U7-si5 tim5 loh8-khi3 chin ku2, u7-si5 liam5-piN koh phu5 khi2-lai5.  Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ni siau3-lian5 lang5 tit8-tit8 siu5, koat-i3 beh kiu3 hit e5 gin2-na2.  Gin2-na2 e5 lau7-bo2 chin chu3-sin5 teh khoaN3.  Teh khoaN3 hit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 chhut-lat8 siu5 ti3-i3 beh kiu3 i e5 kiaN2.  U7-si5 i siu5 kau3 chin oa2, koh 3 tng7, koh 2 tng7, koh 1 tng7 chiu7 kiu3-tioh8.  Iau2-ku2 sui-jian5 li7 chit8 tng7 i ia7 boe7-oe7 siu5 khah oa2.  

I taN teng5-pe7 chhut-lat8.  I chai koh bo5 hng7 chiu7 kau3 te7-it gui5-hiam2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 chia khoe lau5, chhin-chhiuN7 hong-thai liau2, hui-siong5 e5 thau3.  Chun5 kau3 chia ia7 siong5-siong5 tim5 loh8-khi3. Gui5-hiam2 e5 sou2-chai7 i2-keng kau3 lah.  I koh toa7 chhut-lat8, gin2-na2 hiam2-hiam2 hou7 i khiu2-tioh8.  M7-ku2 khiu2 bo5 tioh8 chiu7 koh lau5 khi3.  I koh-chai3 phe-si2 iah, phe-si2 siu5.  TaN iu7-koh khiu2 bo5 tioh8.  Chin kho2-sioh.  
Hit-si5 gin2-na2 e5 lau7-bo2 kau3-kek8 hoan5-lo2.  Koh chit8 pai2 siau3-lian5 lang5 he7-mia7 siu5, cheng3 lang5 khoaN3 kau3 tiau7 khi3. TaN i ko2-jian5 kiu3 oe7 tioh8 a2-boe7? Kau3 chia chui2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 kun2. Toa7 lang5 kap gin2-na2 long2 hou7 eng2 kham3 khi3.  TaN koh phu5 khi2-lai5.  Siau3-lian5 lang5 koh chhut-lat8 iah 2, 3 e7, cheng3 lang5 chhut-siaN hoah ban7-soe3, gin2-na2 hou7 i khiu2-tioh8 lah.  Lau7-bo2 hoaN-hi2 kau3 boe7 kou3-tit.  Chong2--si7 hoaN-hi2 liam5-piN koh-chai3 piN3 choe3 iu-bun7.  In nng7-e5 koh-chai3 tim5 loh8-khi3.  TaN in lau5-loh8 thoaN, hou7 chui2 phah-lai5 phah-khi3. Kai-chai3 si7 chit8 bak8-nih8-ku2 na7-tiaN7.  In koh phu5 khi2 lai5.  TaN i siu5 oa2 kiN5.  Chit8 chhiu2 pho7 gin2-na2, chit8 chhiu2 iah, cheng3 lang5 kin2 chau2 oa2 lai5 ka7 in khiu2 khi2-lai5. Lau7-bo2 hoaN-hi2 kau3 na2 beh siau2, chiu7 chhut-siaN kong2, Goan7 Siong7-te3 po3-tap li2 e5 un.  I tek-khak pat8 jit8 beh hou7 li2 chiaN5 choe3 gau5 lang5.  Kam2-sia7 put-chin7.  Siau3-lian5 lang5 ko2-jian5 chiaN5 chit8 e5 gau5 lang5.  Bo5 ku2 i tng-peng, choe3 koaN, lou7-be2 choe3 Bi2-kok thau5 chit8 e5 Tai7-chong2-thong2.  I e5 mia5-siaN kin-a2-jit8 moa2 thiN-e7.  Joe7-li2 Hoa-seng7-tun3 chiu7-si7 i e5 mia5.
 (Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 399 koan3,1918 ni5 6 geh8)
