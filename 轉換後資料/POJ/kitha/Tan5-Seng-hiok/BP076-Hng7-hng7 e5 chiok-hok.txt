Hng7-hng7 e5 chiok-hok
A-hui7 che2,
Ku7-ni5 e5 chit-chun7, goa2 kap Pek-ni5 hiaN tng2-khi3 Tai5-oan5.  Li2 koaN7 kui-toa7-tui chheh beh hou7 gun2 theh8--tng2-lai5,  hiah-ni7 tang7 e5 mih8-kiaN7, li2 phaiN7--leh, che7 Chiat8-un7 lai5 Pak-tau5 chhoe7--gun2. Goa2 sit8-chai7 kam2-tong7.
Goa2 u7 khi3 pai3-hong2 lin2 e5 pan7-kong-sek. Chit8 keng se3-se3, tun2 chin-che7 chheh kap chap8-chi3, koh 3-tai5 tian7-nau2. Che si7 lin2 phah-piaN3 e5 sou2-chai7. SiuN7-tioh8 chiah-ni7 kan-khou2 e5 khoan5-keng2, lin2 tiam7-tiam7-a2 bo5 oan3-than3 teh cho3, goa2 sit8-chai3 kam2-tong7.
Au7--lai5 lin2 kong2 beh poaN-chhu3, sui-jian5 gun2 iau2-boe7 u7 ki-hoe7 khi3 pai3-hong2, hi-bang7 hia si7 chit8-e5 khah kng, khah thang-hong e5 sou2-chai7, e7-tang3 hou7 lin2 tit-tioh8 khah toa7 e5 lat8, ng3 thau5-cheng5 lai5 kiaN5.
Gun2 ti7 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7, tiam7-tiam7-a2 ka7 lin2 chiok-hok, chiok lin2 siN-jit8 khoai3-lok8.
Na7 u7 ki-hoe7, chhiaN2 the3 goa2 ka7 Lah-jih hiaN chheng2-an.
