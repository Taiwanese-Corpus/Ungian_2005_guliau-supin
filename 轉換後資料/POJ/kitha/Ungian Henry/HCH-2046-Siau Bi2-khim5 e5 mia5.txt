Teng2 le2-pai3-5 chai2-khi2, goa2 khi3 Siong-san chhia-thau theh8 he2-chhia-phio3, khoaN3-tioh8 Siau Bi2-khim5 e5 soan-thoan5-chhia, Soan-thoan5-chhia teng2-bin7 e5 khan-pang2 chiaN3-peng5 e7-kha sia2 "BI-KHIM", Na7-si7 an2-ne, i kho2-leng5 u7 chi-chhi5 LMJ(Lo5-ma2-ji7), Ka2-su2 i u7 tong3-soan2, lan2 i2-au7 na7-si7 u7 siong-koan Tai5-gu2-bun5 e5 gi7-toe7, bo5-tek-khak e7-sai2 lian5-lok8 i. Siau Bi2-khim5 e5 Eng-gi2 mia5 chu7-pun2 toh8 si7 "Bi2-khim5 Hsiao". "Bi2-khim5" si7 i e5 Tai5-gi2 mia5 e5 POJ(Peh8-oe7-ji7) pheng-im, ah8 "Hsiao" si7 Hoa5-gi2 pheng-im. I ti7 Bi2-kok liu5-hak8 e5 si5-chun7, toh8 ho7 chit-e5 mia5. Ma7 chin u7 kho2-leng5 si7 pe7-bo2 chu7 i se3-han3 toh8 an2-ne ho7--e5 (in-ui7 lau7-pe7 e5 poe7-keng2).
Chai7 goa2 liau2-kai2, i peng7 bo5 tek8-piat8 chi-chhi5 ah8-si7 hoan2-tui3 POJ. I Tai5-gi2/Eng-gi2 hoan-ek8 leng5-lek8 put-chi2-a2 kiong5.
Chit-e5 le7 hou7 lan2 e5 ka3-si7: lan2 ai3 than3 gin2-a2 se3-han3 e5 si5-chun7 toh8 ka7 ho7 1 e5 "Eng-bun5 mia5", ka3 i an2-choaN2 pheng-sia2, hou7 i iong7 liau2 e7 koan3-si3. (Tan5-TeN7 Hong5-giau5)
