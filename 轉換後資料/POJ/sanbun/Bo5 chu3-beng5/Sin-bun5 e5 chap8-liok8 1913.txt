Le2-pai3-jit8 choe3 Kang oe7 Sih8-pun2.
Tai7-eng kap Tai7-bi2 kok e5 peh-siN3 khah- choe7 sng3 si7 Ia5-sou kau3 e5 lang5, sou2-i2 hiah e5 sou2- chai7 khah tui3-tiong7 Siong7-te3 e5 hoat-tou7; chhin- chhiuN7 Le2-pai3-jit8 long2 m7-sai2 choe3 kang, iah8 m7- sai2 choe3 seng-li2, peng5-siong5 e5 cheng3-su7 koaN- hu2 m7-sai2 pan7, hou7 koaN-hu2 kap peh-siN3 long2- chong2 thang thiaN to7-li2 poe5-iong2 liong5-sim. Kiam2-chhai2 lang5 chhiaN3 tng5-kang, boe7 bian2- kiong2 i Le2-pai3-jit8 choe3 kang. TaN beh hoan- ek8 chit8-chat e5 sin-bun5.    
Lang5 u7 chhai3-hng5, khoaN3-kiN3 chhu3-piN e5 tng5- kang, mia5 Jim7 teh-choe3 kang, kio3 i kong2, “Jim7-lo2-ah, Le2-pai3-jit8 cha2-khi2 tioh8 lai5 goa2 chhai3-hng5 tau3 pang-chan7 choe3 kang, li2 beh mah ?”    
Jim7-lo2 chiu7 thng3 i e5 loeh8, eng7 chhiu2 pe5 thau5- khak teh-siuN7, lai5 kong2, “Thau5-ke ah, goa2 m7-kaN2, kiaN-liau2 oe7 sih8-pun2.” 
Hng5-chu2 kong2, “Heh !M7-si7 beh hou7 li2 khang-khang tioh8-boa5, si7 beh hou7 li2 kang-chiN5.” 
Jim7-lo2 koh kong2, “Oe7 sih8-pun2.” 
Hng5-chu2 kong2, “Li2 beh-thai3-oe7 kong2 an3-ni ? Si7 oe7 ke-thiN li2 e5 chiN5; goa2 phah-sng3 li2 iah8 bo5 kau3 joa7 u7 chiN5 e5 lang5.”     
Jim7-lo2 in3 kong2, “Li2 phah-sng3 tioh8; goa2 sit8- chai7 bo5 sim2-mih8 u7 chiN5, sou2-i2 kiaN-liau2 oe7 sih8- pun2.”    
Hng5-chu2 kong2, “Li2 kong2, ‘ Oe7 sih8-pun2, ’ u7 sim2- mih8 i3-su3 ? Li2 kaN2 si7 thiaN goa2 e5 oe7, boe7 hiau2 tit.”    
Jim7-lo2 kong2, “Oe7 hiau2-tit li2 e5 i3-su3; na7-si7 goa2 ham5-ban7, ai3 kong2, kong2 boe7 lai5; li2 na7 hoaN-hi2. goa2 chiu7 un5-un5 kong2 hou7 li2 thiaN. Goa2 sit8-chai7 m7-si7 joa7 u7 chiN5 e5 
lang5, li2 kong2 hit ku3 put-chi2 tioh8; goa2 siong5-siong5 tioh8 thoa-boa5. Sui-jian5 ti7 chit se3-kan bo5 giap8, iau2-ku2 u7 hit e5 ng3-bang7 si2- au7 oe7 khah-u7. Chit ho7 e5 ng3-bang7, chiau3 goa2 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 oe7, iah8 thang kong2 si7 u7 hok-khi3. Goa2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 chhin-chhui3 u7 kong2, “ Goa2 khi3 in-ui7 lin2 pi7-pan7 sou2-chai7, ti3-kau3 goa2 sou2 ti7-teh, lin2 iah8 oe7 ti7 hia.”Cha2 it-jiap8 ni5, goa2 oh8 chi2 chit chat e5 to7-li2, tui3 hit-tiap8 i2-lai5 goa2 kap goa2 e5 bou2, na7 u7 khiam3-kheh kan-khou2 e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7 liam7 chi2 chit chat, i2-keng u7 kui2-na7 pah pai2.”    
Hng5-chu2 kong2, “HouN2, houN2; na7-si7 li2 tai7-seng kong2, oe7 sih8-pun2, chi2 lou7-be2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 u7 sim2- mih8 kan-siap8 ?”    
Jim7-lo2 kong2, “Tioh8 sia3-bian2 goa2; put-chi2 u7 kan-siap8. Goa2 ng3-bang7 au7--lai5 ti7 khah-ho2 e5 sou2- chai7 tit-tioh8 khah-ho2 e5 ki-giap8, taN kiaN-liau2 oe7 sit-loh8 hit e5 ng3-bang7, chiu7-si7 toa7 sih8-pun2. Goa2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 i2-keng khi3 pi7-pan7 sou2-chai7, taN goa2 tioh8 kiu5 I lai5 pi7-pan7 goa2 e5 sim, hou7 goa2 au7-lai5 oe7 kham tit tiam3 hit sou2-chai7, na7 bo5 chiu7-si7 kou- hu7 Chu2 e5 un-tian2. Chong2-si7 goa2 bo5 eng5 thang choan-bu7 siuN7 chit ho7 e5 tai7-chi3, tok8-tok8 Le2-pai3 jit8 chiah u7. Goa2 khi3 le2-pai3-tng5 thiaN thian- tong5 e5 su7-cheng5, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 hioh-kha ti7 thiN-lou7 e5 lou7-cham7. Thau5-ke ah, m7-thang, m7 thang; ti7 lang5 e5 jit8 tioh8 choe3 lang5 e5 kang, kiam2-chhai2 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 jit8 lai5 choe3, kia-liau2 oe7 sih8-pun2.”           
Le2-pai3-jit8 hioh-kang, iah8 oe7 choe3 Cheng-siN5 e5 Li7-ek
Tai7-bi2 kok si7 khah-toa7 e5 toe7-hng. Cha2-cheng5 be7 chhong3 he2-hun chhia e5 lou7. Hit-si5 kok-lai7 bo5 kang2 bo5 ho5 e5 sou2-chai7, chai3 lang5-kheh chai3 he3 e5 chhia put-chi2 toa7 tiuN; iah8 put-chi2 iong2, oe7 kham-tit chai3 kau3 chiaN5-pah taN3, iok-liok8 u7 chiaN5-ban7 kun. Thoa chit ho7 e5 chhia, chai3 kau3 chiah tang7 e5 chai7, chit tiuN chhia tioh8 lak8 chiah be2; hit e5 be2 iah8 si7 toa7 chiah, pi2 E7-mng5 e5 be2 si7 nng7 saN pe7 toa7.    

TaN beh lioh8-lioh8-a2 kong2 chit ho7 chhia-hu chit lang5 e5 tai7-chi3. I e5 mia5 si7 Tam5, kui2 na7 ni5 sou2 kiaN5 chai3-he3 e5 lou7 put-chi2 hng7, khi3 to3 lai5 chiu-ui5 iok-liok8 nng7 chheng li2 lou7; lou7 hng7 chai7 tang7, tioh8 ban7-ban7 kiaN5, chit8 nng7 geh8-jit8 ku2, chiah oe7 kiaN5 chiu-ui5 beng5-pek8. Lou7-tiong be2 u7-si5 oe7 ia3, u7-si5 oe7 phoa3-piN7, u7-si5 oe7 si2; han2 tit oe7 kiaN5 kau3 chiu-ui5 iah8 bo5 sit-loh8 chit8 chiah, u7-si5 sit-loh8 nng7 saN chiah, sou2-i2 tak8 tiuN chhia leng7-goa7 khan chit8 chiah be2 thang thoe3-oan7.    
KiaN5 hit tiau5 lou7 un7 he3 e5 chhia put-chi2 choe7, u7-si5 kui2 chap8 tiuN choe3 chit8 tin7 saN-te3 lai5 kiaN5. Lang5 tiam3 ti7 soaN-teng2 hng7-hng7 khoaN3-kiN3 chiah choe7 chhia, chhin-chhiuN7 sio-chiap ian5-lou7 oan7- oan7 kiaN5, khoan2-sit si7 chhin-chhiuN7chit8 be2 e5 thang5 teh so5.    
Chit8-e5 lang5 mia5 Tam5 kui2-na7 ni5 choe3 chit- ho7 e5 thau5-lou7. I u7 nng7 tiuN chit-ho7 chhia, chap8- si3 chiah be2; tak8-tiuN chhia-cheng5 pak8 lak8 chiah be2 thoa chhia, chhia-au7 pak8 chit8 chiah teh khan thang thoe3-oaN7. Chit8-tiuN chhia si7 i ka-ki7 chiang2-li2, chit8-tiuN si7 chhiaN3 lang5 lai5 chiang2-li2.    
Tam5 choe3 lang5 e5 khoan2-sit chin bong2-tong2, bo5 kiaN Siong7-te3,bo5 kiaN lang5, iah8 bo5 kiaN mou5-kui2; kek8 phaiN2-chhui3, tauh8-tauh8 siat-tok8 Siong7-te3 e5 mia5 gau5 me7 --lang5; lang5 chiu7 ho7 i si7 PhaiN2-kui2 Tam5.  
Hit-si5 Tai7-bi2 kok e5 hong-siok8 lang5 long2-chong2 thi3 chhui3-chhiu, lian5 lau7 e5 lang5 iah8 si7 an2-ni; han2 tit u7 lang5 lau5 chhui3-chhiu; siat-su2 u7, sng3 si7 kek8 chhou-lou2 e5 lang5; na7-si7 chit e5 Tam5 long2 bo5 thi3 chhui3-chhiu, bin7 hou5-hou5, lang5 chiu7 ho7 i e5 siN3 kio3 choe3 Chhiu An2-ni-siN i peng5-sou3 sou2 kiaN5 e5 lou7 tak8 sou2-chai7 lang5 ki-chhi3 kio3 i siN3 Chhiu, mia5 PhaiN2-kui2 Tam5, i iah8 bo5 iau3-kin2, tok8-tok8 phaiN2-chhui3 in3 lai5 chhio tiaN7-tiaN7.    
TaN chit ho7 e5 lang5 long2 bo5 iau3-kin2 Seng3-chheh e5 to7-li2, chu7-jian5 bo5 siu2 Le2-pai3-jit8, i tang5- pang e5 lang5 iah8 bo5 siu2, phah-sng3 Le2-pai3-jit8 kap pat8 jit8, long2 saN-tang5.    

U7 chit8 jit8, chiu7-si7 pai3-lak8 e5 jit8, kiaN5 in e5 lou7- cham7 beng5-pek8, lai5 hioh kheh-tiam3, khoaN3- kiN3 piN thau5 u7 hng5 chai hoan-beh8-sui7, sui-jian5 be7-sek8 iau2-ku2 oe7 chiah8--tit. Kau3 am3 i bong2- tong2 chio tang5-pang kui2-na7 lang5 khi3 ban2 hoan- beh8-sui7, theh8 lai5 kau3 kheh-tiam3 lai7, kiong5- kiong5 kah tiam3-chu2 e5 ke-lai7 lang5 tioh8 khi3 sah8. I chiah8 kau3 put-chi2 pa2, kau3 thiN-kng tioh8-soa phoa3-piN7, chin siong-tiong7, boe7-tit-thang kiaN5- lou7, i tang5-pang e5 lang5 choe3 in khi2-sin tai7-seng kiaN5, lau5 i kap i sou2 chhiaN3 e5 lang5 nng7 tiuN chhia tiaN7-tiaN7.
Kau3 Pai3-it thau3-cha2 seng-khu khah-ho2, chiu7 khi2-sin kiaN5 lou7 beh jip8 tang5-pang e5 lang5. I e5 be2 hioh chit8 jit put-chi2 khin-sang, khah gau5 kiaN5, hit jit8 ke kiaN5 kui2-na7 li2 lou7; Pai3-ji7 be2 iau2- ku2 khin-sang koh ke kiaN5 kui2-na7-li2 lou7; Pai3-saN iah8 si7 an2-ni, kau3 jit8-am3 chiu7 jip-tioh8 i tang5-pang e5 lang5, Pai3-si3, Pai3-gou7, Pai3-lak8 kap in saN-kap kiaN5. I long2 bo5 kap pat8 lang5 kong2 chit-e5 tai7-chi3, na7 si7 sim-lai7 tak8-jit8 teh siuN7, chiu7 tiaN7-tioh8 beh koh chhi3 khoaN3 hioh chit e5 Le2-pai3-jit8 khoaN3 chaiN2-iuN7.    
An2-ni koh kau3 Le2-pai3-jit8 cha2-khi2, i ke2 choe3 phoa3-piN7, hou7 i tang5-pang e5 lang5 khi2-sin tai7- seng kiaN5, i iu5-goan5 Pai3-saN jit8-am3 jip-tioh8, kap in kiaN5 kau3 Pai3-lak8. Tak8 Le2-pai3 kiaN5 an2- ni kau3 chiu-ui5 beng5-pek8.    
Tiam3 ti7 i e5 chhu3 saN jit8 ku2, beh koh-chai3 chhut-lou7, khoaN3 i e5 be2 kiaN5 chi2 chit8-pang pi2 tai7- seng khah iong2-kiaN7 khin-sang, chiu7 tiaN7-tioh8 i2-au7 kau3 Le2-pai3-jit8 beh hioh-khun3; koh siuN7 chi2 nng7 chiah thoe3-oaN7 be2 m7-sai2 leng7-goa7 khan, chiu7 khi3 boe7 lang5. Hit-tiap8 liau2-au7 kiaN5 gou7 ni5 ku2, lou7-nih8 e5 tiong-kan chiah e5 be2 m7-bat u7 si2 phoa3-piN7 ia3.    
Jian5-au7 hit sou2-chai7 khui he2-hun chhia e5 lou7, chiu7 hit-ho7 be2-chhia bo5 lou7-eng7, i chiu7 piN3-choe3 pat8-mih8 e5 thau5-lou7, chiu7-si7 tiam3 ti7 khoe-piN, khi2 chit8-keng chui2-chhia e5 chhu3, beh ku3 chha5-liau7, chhiaN3 chai-iaN2 e5 sai-hu7 lai5 ku3, bo5 joa7-ku2, i iah8 oe7 hiau2 tit.    

I ka-ki7 siuN7 chit e5 chui2-chhia m7-si7 bah huih kut, Le2-pai3-jit8 m7-sai2 chhin-chhiuN7 cheng-siN lai5 hioh-khun3, na7-si7 koh siuN7 Le2-pai3-jit8 theng5- kang chhia khah boe7 phaiN2, choe3-kang e5 lang5 iah8 oe7 hioh-khun3 tit-tioh8 khin-sang, chiu7 koat toan3 Le2-pai3-jit8 bo5 beh ku3 liau7.    
Thau5 chit8 Le2-pai3, i kap choe3-kang e5 lang5 ti7 chhiu7-na5 lai7 teh thit-tho5, hut- jian5 thiaN-kiN3 chai3 sam be2-chhia e5 siaN, theng5 ti7 chui2-chhia chhu3 e5 mng5-khau2, chhia-hu phah chui2-chhia mng5, kio3 siaN chin-ku2. Tam5 thau-khoaN3, long2 m7 chhut --lai5, chiah-e5 chhia-hu theng3-hau7 ku2, long2 bo5 lang5 chhut --lai5, chiu7 pang3-he7 chai3 sam e5 be2- chhia ti7 hia, chiong be2 khan tokh3i. Kau3 bin5-a2 cha2-khi2, chhia-hu koh lai5 kiN3 i, mng7 i, “Cha7-bou7-jit8 siaN2-su7 bo5 lang5 ti7 teh ?” I chiu7 in3, kong2- khi2 i Le2-pai3-jit8 theng5 kang e5 in-toaN.    
Tam5 choe3 chit-ho7 e5 kang choe7-choe7 ni5. Le2- pai3-jit8 chui2-chhia siong5-siong5 theng5-kang, m7- si7 in-ui7 beh than3 Siong7-te3 e5 kai3-lut8, tok8-tok8 phah-sng3 khah oe7 tit-tioh8 se3-kan e5 li7-ek khoaiN3-oah8 tiaN7-tiaN7, sim-koaN long2 bo5 iau3-kin2 Seng3-chheh e5 to7-li2, iu5-goan5 phaiN2-chhui3 siat tok8 Siong7-te3 e5 mia5.   
Li7 chit e5 chui2-chhia chiaN5 phou3 lou7 u7 chit8 e5 the2-bian7 choh-sit e5 lang5,chhan5-hng5 chhu3-theh8 choe7-choe7, iah8 si7 choe3 kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 tiong2-lo2. I u7 chit8 keng chhu3 choe3 cheng-siN e5 tiau5 kap toe2 gou7-kak chhau2-a2 e5 sou2-chai7; chit-keng khit he2 sio--khi3, siu-seng5 e5 si5 tit-beh kau3, bo5 sou2-chai7 thang toe2 gou7-kak kap chhau2-a2, chiu7 chhiaN3 sai- hu7 sai-a2 choe3 kang e5 lang5 choe7-choe7, kin2-kin2 lai5 khi2 sin e5 chhu3. Tu2-tioh8 Pai3-lak8 jit8 sai-hu7- thau5 ka7 hit e5 the2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “Iau2 khiam3- eng7 e5 chha5-liau7,Pai3-it jit8-tau3 na7 oe7 tit-tioh8, kau3 Pai3-saN chhu3 e5 ki-kut chiu7 oe7 khia7 tit ho2.”The2-bian7 lang5 thiaN-kiN3 chit-e5 oe7, chiu7 liam7- piN khia5 be2 khi3 chui2-chhia ka7 Tam5 kong2, “Goa2 sai-hu7-thau5 kong2 sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 chha5-liau7, li2 na7 oe7 liam5-piN ku3 hou7 goa2 Pai3-it jit8-tau3 tit- tioh8, chiu7 Pai3-saN oe7 khi2 sin chhu3 e5 ki-kut khia7-chai7; li2 oe7 a2-boe7 ?”     
Tam5 in3 kong2, “Boe7-oe7 pian7.”The2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “ChaiN2-iuN7 boe7 pian7 ?”   

Tam5 kong2, “Tioh8 Le2-pai3-jit8 lai5 ku3 chiah oe7; na7-si7 Le2-pai3-jit8 goa2 tioh8 theng5 kang.”    
The2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “Lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 li2  e5 khoan2, Le2-pai3-jit8 choe3 kang, kiam2 u7 sou2 hai7 mah ?”    
Tam5 kong2, “Goa2 ka-ki7 chhou-lou2 sou2 kiaN5 bo5 hap8 to7-li2 goa2 chai, na7-si7 kau3 Le2-pai3-jit8 goa2 tek-khak tioh8 theng5-kang.”    
The2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “Goa2 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 si7 kek8 iau3-kin2; hoan7-na7 kek8 iau3-kin2--e5, Le2-pai3-jit8 iah8 thang choe3. ”    
Tam5 kong2, “Put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 lai5 chhiaN3 lai5 boe2, na7-si7 Le2-pai3-jit8 goa2 tek-khak tioh8 theng5 kang. ”    
The2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “Goa7 ! li2 e5 sim-koaN bo5 hiah khian5-seng5.”    
Tam5 kong2, “Goa2 chai lah; Le2-pai3-jit8 si7 goa2 chu7-ki2 ai3 tit theng5 kang, goa2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hit ho7 chng-choe3 khian5-seng5 e5 lang5.”    
The2-bian7 lang5 kong2, “TaN ho2 lah; goa2 e5 tai7- chi3 chit-tiap8 pek-chhiat, li2 long2 m7-kam-goan7 pang-chan7, beng5-beng5 si7 au2-ban5; i2-au7 m7 koh eng7 li2 e5 kang, lian5 chit8-tiau5 sam iah8 m7 hou7 li2 ku3, goa2 tai7-seng thong-ti li2; li2 chit ho7 lang5 chin7 si7 kho2-ouN3, goa2 beh tui3 cheng3-lang5 chou2-tong3 m7- chhiaN3 li2 choe3 kang. Li2 na7 choe3 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, sim-koaN tui3-tiong7 to7-li2, sou2-i2 An-hioh- jit8 bo5 choe3 kang, goa2 chiu7 sia3-bian2 li2, na7 si7 chit e5 te7-hng e5 lai7-bin7, te7-it phaiN2-chhui3, gau5 siat-tok8 Siong7-te3 e5 mia5 chiu7-si7 li2.”    
The2-bian7 lang5 kong2 soah, chiu7 toa7 siu7-khi3 koaN2-kin2 khia5-be2 to3 khi3.   
Hu7-kun7 e5 hiuN-sia7 u7 chit8 lang5, kang2-kiu3 kok e5 cheng3-su7 e5 siau3-lian5 sian-siN; i iah8 m7-si7 than3 Seng3-chheh to7-li2 e5 lang5. I hit jit8 iah8 khi3 chui2- chhia, tu2-tu2 hit-si5

Lang5 phah-si2 Chhai5-long5

Hit-tiap8 si7 jit8-am3 e5 si5, chiu7 kio3 i e5 lou5-pok8, mia5 kio3 I2-lip8, ka7 i kong2, “I2- lip8 ah, lan2 chit-tiap8 koaN2-kin2 beh chhut-lou7 khi3 , li2 tioh8 pi7-pan7 be2-chhia m7 thang ian5-chhian5.” Bo5 chit8-tiap8-a2 ku2 I2-lip8 chiu7 kong2 pi7-pan7 ho2- se3. Toa7-lo2 kap i e5 bou2 kiaN2 sui5-si5 chiu7 che7 chhia; I2-lip8 ia7 che7 ti7 chhia cheng5, khan be2- soh, chek-si5 khi2-sin.    
Hit-tiap8 si7 tang-thiN e5 si5, moa2 thiN u7 chhiN put-chi2 kng. In kiaN5 kau3 lou7-cham7, chiu7 hioh- khun3, ai3 beh oaN7 be2; in-ui7 ti7 lou7 nih8 seh chin kau7, be2 kiaN5 kek8 sian7, kau3 beh poah8-to2; ia7 si7 in-ui7 kiaN5 kau3 poaN3-mi5-lah. Hit e5 lou7-cham7 e5 tiam3 chu2 chiu7 khng3 toa7-lo2 theng3-hau7 kau3 thiN phu2 kng chiah kiaN5, kong2, “M7-tan7-na7 seh kau7, thiN-khi3 chin koaN5, ia7 u7 chhai5-long5 kui-kun5 pian3 si3-koe3 kiaN5. Kui2-na7 le2-pai3 ku2 hiah e5 chhai5- long5 bo5 sim2-mih8 thang chiah8. E5-hng beh am3 e5 si5, goan2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 hiah e5 chhai5-long5, ti7 hng7- hng7 e5 chhiu7-na5 teh hau2. Kui2-na7 mi5 cheng5 u7 chhai5-long5 lai5 ti7 chia si2-koe3 seh8 toa7 siaN hau2, ai3 beh chhe7 chiah8 e5 mih8. Goa2 m7-kaN2 khui mng5, na7 chhut khi3 tek-khak peh8-peh8 sang3 goa2 e5 siN3- mia7; goa2 u7 khui thang-a2 khoaN3 hiah e5 chhai5- long5 bak8-chiu kim-kim siong3 hou7 goa2 kiaN kau3 ngauh8-ngauh8-chhoah, goa2 kau3 chit8-si3-lang5 to oe7 ki3-tit. Kau3 thiN phu8 kng chhai5-long5 chiah soaN3．khi3.    
Toa7-lo2 thiaN-kiN3 i sou2 kong2, sim7-sim7 chit8-tiap- a2 ku2; che m7-si7 i ka-ki7 u7 kiaN, si7 in-ui7 u7 bou2, kiaN2 ti7-teh, sou2-i2 kiaN-hiaN5, u7 chin3-the3 liong2-lan5 e5 i3-su3, chiu7 in3 tiam3 chu2 kong2, “Goa2 phah-sng3 thang koh ke kiaN5 chit8 lou7-cham7, in-ui7 goa2 e5 lou5-pok8 put-chi2 iong2-beng2, ia7 u7 toa7 kek8 ho2 e5 chheng3, li2 tioh8 pi7-pan7 kek8 iong2 kek8 gau5-chau2 e5 be2 lai5 hou7 goa2 oaN7 chiu7 ho2.” Tiam3 chu2 chek- si5 chiong iong2 e5 be2 lai5 pak8 ti7 chhia nih8. I2-lip8 ia7 sui5-si5 chiuN7 chhia ho2-taN2 khan be2-soh iat be2 piN liam5-piN khi2-sin khi3. Chit8 li2 kiaN5 ke3 chit8 li2, long2 bo5 tong7-cheng7, tok8-tok8 thiaN-kiN3 be2 long e5 siaN na7-tiaN7.    
Au7-lai5 kiaN5 kau3 chit8 e5 toa7 chhiu7-na5, hit sou2-chai7 chhiN-chhiN chhin3-chhin3, chhiu7 chin koaiN5 kau3 tu2-thiN jia-tioh8 chhiN-sin5, sou2-i2 chit sou2-chai7 chin- am3. In beh ke3 chit e5 chhiu7-na5 e5 lou7 na2 kiaN5 na2 am3. Kau3 chia chu2, pok8 long2 m7-kaN2 choe3 siaN, in- ui7 u7 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 keng-ke3 chit tiau5 lou7, choe7- choe7 tu2-tioh8 chhai5-long5, sit-liau2 siN3-mia7.    
KiaN5 bo5 kui2-pou7 hut-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 chin hiong-beng2 e5 siaN, I2-lip8 chai chit e5 siaN m7-si7 chhiu7- na5 e5 hong, ia7 m7-si7 hong tui3 soaN-teng2 lai5, tek- khak si7 iau-go7 hiong5-ok e5 chhai5-long5 si3-koe3 kiaN5 teh hau2. I2-lip8 chai taN tu2-tioh8 ti7 chia siN3- mia7 
tek-khak lan5 po2, chiu7 oat8-thau5 lai5 phah- chhia nih8 e5 thang-a2-mng5, beh thong-ti i e5 chu2- lang5 chai chit e5 tai7-chi3. Toa7-lo2 chiu7 an-ui3 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, “M7-sai2 kiaN, tioh8 oa2-kho3 Siong7- te3.” Hu7-jin5-lang5 thiaN in tiong7-hu7 e5 oe7, chiu7 pho7 i e5 kiaN2, the ti7 chhia-lai7, seng5-sim ki5-to2 choan5-leng5 e5 Siong7-te3 chiau3-kou3 i e5 tiong7-hu kap kiaN2 chit8 ke peng5-an. I2-lip8 chiu7 chhut-lat8 phah be2 koaN2 chin3-cheng5 chau2 khi3. Chai chit e5 tai7-chi3 put-chi2 gui5-hiam2, chhai5-long5 tek-khak oe7 tui-jip．lai5; taN beh chau2, a2-si7 beh kap chhai5-long5 tui3-tek8, a2-si7 tioh8 si2, siuN7 boe7 hiau2-tit. Chhai5- long5 e5 siaN na2 thiaN na2 kun7, lian5 kha-pou7 ia7 thiaN-kiN3, i2-keng kau3 ti7 chhia-au7.    
Au7-lai5 chhai5-long5 tui kau3 kun7-oa2, khoaN3-kiN3 chhia nih8 e5 teng kng-kng, chiu7 theng5-kha, m7- kaN2 koh tui lai5. Tiap-a2-ku2 chhai5-long5 koh-chai3 ho2-taN2 si3-ui5 pau chit tiuN chhia. Chhia nih8 e5 teng chio3 tioh8 chhai5-long5, khoaN3-kiN3 kui-kun5, seng-khu kek8 san2, bak8-chiu kim-kim, chhui3 khui-khui, chih8 thou3 tng5-tng5. noa7 it-tit lau5 chhut-lai5. Toa7-lo2 chiu7 kong2, “I2-lip8 ah, lan2 tioh8 pang3 chheng3. Li2 tioh8 siong3 cheng-cheng, chheng3 chiah thang khui-khi3. ” Hut-jian5 hit chiah choe3 thau5 e5 chhai5-long5 hou7 chheng3 phah si2, to2 ti7 seh nih8 moa2 sin si7 huih. Ki5-u5 e5 chhai5-long5 thiaN-kiN3 chheng3 e5 siaN, koh khoaN3-kiN3 thau5- chiah si2-teh, u7 chin toa7 kiaN si3-koe3 to5-chau2 jip8 ti7 chhiu7-na5 lai7, boe7 kou3-tit thang chiah8 hit chiah si2 e5 chhai5-long5.    
Bo5 chit8-khek ku2 chhai5-long5 koh chu7-chip8 kui-kun5 ui5-lai5, pi2 cheng5 khah hiong5-ok. In-ui7 chiah e5 chhai5-long5 chin iau sou2-i2 m7-kam pang3- sak chit tiuN be2-chhia; toa7-siaN chhoan2-khui3 siuN7 beh lai5 ka7 chiah8. I2-lip8 chiu7 koh phah si2 chit8 chiah; hiah e5 chhai5-long5 theng5 kha teh-hau2, bo5 sim2-mih8 kiaN-hiaN5, ia7 bo5 chau2 khi3. Koh pek- kun7 oa2-lai5, chhui3-khi2 giang3-giang3, toa7-siaN teh hau2. I2-lip8 khoaN3-kiN3 m7-si7 se3 bin7, chiu7 siuN7 taN beh chaiN2-iuN7 ? In-ui7 tioh8 koh kiaN5 chin hng7 chiah oe7 kau3 lou7-cham7. Hit-tiap8 ti7 chhia-nih8 u7 chit8 ki chha5, chiu7 eng7 soh lai5 pak8, hiat ti7 chhia-au7 hou7 chhia teh thoa, hou7 hit ki chha5 tin2-tang7 pong7-pong7 hau2, beh koaN2-soaN3 hiah e5 chhai5-long5. Na7-si7 hiah e5 chhai5-long5 long2 chai si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8, iu5-goan5 m7 kiaN bo5 beh si3- soaN3 khi3, pi2 cheng5 koh khah beng2.    
Kau3 chit-tiap8 hit tiuN chhia e5 lang5 siN3-mia7 i2-keng bo5 ng3-bang7. I2-lip8 chiu7 ka7 i e5 lou7-pok8 phoaN7 kong2, “Thau5 chiah be2 tioh8 thau2, kin2- kin2 thau2. ”Sui5-si5 chiong thau5 chiah e5 be2 thau2- khui; hit chiah be2 u7 chin kiaN, chhoan3 tui7 lou7- piN chau2-khi3. Chhai5-long5 kui-kun5 tui3 hit chiah be2 chiu7 jip khi3, saN-kap ka7 hit chiah be2 tau3 chhiuN2 khi3 chiah8. Siong7-chhiaN2 bo5 kau3-giah8, u7 chiah8 tam7-poh8 huih, pak-tou2 iau2-ku2 si7 iau, kui- kun5 koh te3 be2-chhia lai5, Chong2--si7 be2-chhia i2- keng u7 chin3-cheng5 kiaN5 kui2-na7 li2 lou7 ke3.khi3.    
Toa7-lo2 chiu7 kong2, “I2-lip8 ah, lan2 chit-tiap8 sou2 thiaN-kiN3 si7 chhiu7-nih8 e5 hong mah ? “A2-si7 hiah- e5 beng2 e5 chhai5-long5 koh te3-lai5 mah ?” I2-lip8 tai7- seng ia7 u7 thiaN-kiN3, i chai si7 chhai5-long5 e5 siaN, Chong2--si7 i put-chi2 thiaN3 chu2-lang5, m7 kam hou7 in tai7-ke kiaN-hiaN5, sou2-i2 long2 bo5 kong2-chhut. In-ui7 i e5 chu2-lang5 mng7-khi2, I2-lip8 in3 i, kong2, “Chiu7-si7 hiah-e5 chhai5-long5 koh tui lai5. ”Oe7 tu2 kong2-liau2, hiah e5 chhai5-long5 koh si3 ui5 lai5 pau chit tiuN chhia. Chhai5-long5 e5 heng5-chong7 pi2 tai7-seng teng5-pe7 phaiN2 khoaN3, in-ui7 tak8 chiah seng-khu long2 u7 bak-huih.    
I2-lip8 chiu7 kong2, “Koh thau2 thau5 chiah e5 be2.” Soh tu2 thau2-khui, be2 chiu7 chau2; chi2 u7 thiaN- kiN3 be2 hau2 chit8 siaN, i2-keng khoaN3-bo5, hit chiah(p.35) be2 chiu7-si7 hou7 chhai5-long5 sui5-si5 chiah8-liau2 khi3. Chhai5-long5 iau2-ku2 si7 iau, tek-khak ai3 beh koh chiah8 lang5 e5 huih.    
I2-lip8 sui-bong2 gau5 koaN2-be2 toa7-pou7 chau2, long2 bo5 lou7-eng7. Chit8 bak8-nih8 ku2 chhai5-long5 iu5-goan5 si7 te3 ti7 chhia au7, taN beh chaiN2-iuN7 ? Na7 beh chiong chiah e5 be2 long2 thau2 hou7 chhai5-long5 chiah8, Chong2--si7 chit-tiuN chhia boe7 tit-thang thoa kau3 lou7-cham7 e5 sou2-chai7; na7-si7 hit e5 lou7- cham7 e5 he2 i2-keng u7 khoaN3-kiN3·lah. Chi2 iau2 u7 chit8 hang7 ke3-chhek, tioh8 pun2-sin lai5 kap chhai5- long5 choe3 tui3-tek8. I2-lip8 u7 koat-toan3 ai3 beh loh8 chhia, lai5 koaN2 chiah e5 chhai5-long5.    

I2-lip8 chiu7 khin-khin phah chhia e5 thang-a2 mng5, hou7 chu2-lang5 chai i beh an2-ni choe3, ka7 chu2-lang5 kong2 , “Goa2 kiam2-chhai2 na7 hou7 chhai5- long5 thoa khi3 chiah8, ng3-bang7 chu2-lang5 tioh8 tai3- liam7 goa2 e5 bou2, kiaN2.”    
Oe7 kong2 liau2, chek-si5 loh8 chhia nng7 chhiu2 kiah8 chheng3 chiu7 pang3. Chu2-lang5 ti7 chhia lai7 thiaN-kiN3 chheng3 tan5 chit8-siaN, koh thiaN-kiN3 chit8 siaN ia7 chiu7 cheng7-cheng7. Chu2-lang5 chiu7 chai I2-lip8 tek-khak si7 khit chhai5-long5 ka7 khi3 chiah8. TaN chiah-e5 chhai5-long5 ki3-jian5 u7 tit-tioh8 lang5 e5 bah thang chiah8, chiu7 bo5 koh-chai3 siuN7 beh lai5 te3 chit tiuN chhia. Toa7-lo2 chit tiuN chhia kai-chai3 oe7 thoat-li7, chiu7 koaN2 kau3 lou7-cham7 hit-tiap8 tai7-ke ki5-to2, kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chu5- pi, kiu3 in chit8 ke e5 siN3-mia7. Chiu7 tiam3 chit8 lou7-cham7 hioh kau3 thiN-kng, Chong2--si7 tai7-ke long2 bo5 khun3, in-ui7 ng3-bang7 hit e5 chin7-tiong lou5-pok8 kiam2-chhai2 oe7 po2-choan5 i e5 siN3-mia7, oe7 tit-thang koh chai3 to3-lai5; ia7 put-chi2 kiaN hou7 chhai5-long5 ka7 si2.    
Kau3 thiN phu2-kng in chiu7 koh chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 I2-lip8 cha7-mi5 loh8 chhia e5 sou2-chai7, si3-koe3 khoaN3, peng7 bo5 I2-lip8 e5 chong-chek, chi2 u7 khoaN3- kiN3 chheng3 koh hit sou2-chai7 seh u7 choe7-choe7 huih; i2-keng chai I2-lip8 si7 hou7 chhai5-long5 ka7 khi3 chiah8, chiong sin-si thoa jip8 chhiu7-na5 lai7 khi3.lah.    
Toa7-lo2 chhim-chhim siau3-liam7 i koe2-kiu3 e5 un, ti7 hia ka7 i khia7 chit8 te3 chioh8-pi, khek ji7 kong2 beng5 chit e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 in2 khi2 chit8 chat Seng3-keng kong2, “Lang5 in-ui7 peng5-iu2 pang3-sak siN3-mia7, sou2 thiaN3 bo5 u7 khah toa7 chit e5.”(Iok-han7 15:13 ) Chit e5 chioh8-pi kau3 kin-a2-jit8 iau2-ku2 ti7-teh, hoan7 ke3 lou7 e5 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 bo5 chit8 e5 m7 thou2-khui3, o-lo2 i.    
Chit chat su7 m7-na7 hou7 lan2 siuN7 chhai5-long5 e5 phaiN2 chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 hit chat e5 tai7- chi3, koh chit e5 chu2, pok8 nng7 lang5 e5 sou2 kiaN5, iah8 hou7 lan2 siuN7 kui2-na7 hang7 kek8 ho2 e5 to7-li2. Chit-tiap8 oe7 kong2 nng7 hang7 tiaN7-tiaN7.    

Chit8 hang7. Lou5-pok8 kam-goan7 thoe3 chu2 lang5 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7, si7 hou7 lan2 siuN7 Ia5-sou chai7-cha2 thoe3 lan2 pang3-sak siN3-mia7, na7-si7 Ia5-sou e5 thiaN3- thang3 ban7-pe7 khah-iaN5; m7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 si7 soe3 thoe3 put-chi2 ho2 put-chi2 thiaN3 i e5 si7-toa7, si7 ban7-ong5 e5 Ong5 thoe3 hoan2-poan7 e5 peh-siN3, si7 thiN-toe7 chi3 seng3 e5 Chu2-chaiN2 thoe3 toe7-bin7- chiuN7 tang7-choe7 e5 lang5. Chit ho7 thiaN3-thang3 se3-kan lang5 long2 boe7 hiau2-tit siuN7. Hit e5 lou5- pok8 koe2 kiu3 chu2-lang5 seng-khu e5 siN3-mia7, hou7 i koh tiam3 toe7-bin7 put ko3 kui2 ni5 tiaN7-tiaN7 chiah soah; na7 si7 Ia5-sou koe2 kiu3 lan2 sim-sin5 e5 siN3-mia7, hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 eng2-oan2-oah8 ti7 thian-tong5, chheng-chheng ban7-ban7 ni5 bo5 soah.    
Koh chit8 hang7. Hit e5 chu2 lang5 in-ui7 lou5- pok8 e5 sou2-kiaN5, hou7 lan2 siuN7 lan2 hoan2-tng2 choe3 lou5-pok8 eng-kai chaiN2-iuN7 in-ui7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou lai5 kiaN5. Hit e5 chu2-lang5 chhim- chhim siau3-liam7 lou5-pok8, ka7 i khia7 thiau7; na7- si7 lan2 tit-tioh8 un ban7 pe7 khah toa7, ban7-hun7 e5 chit8-hun7 bo5 thang po3-tap, chiu7 tioh8 chiong ka-ki7 seng-khu, leng5-hun5 sou2 u7 sou2 oe7 chiN5-chai5 ke-giap8 khui3-lat8 chai5-tiau7, phi3-ju7 choe3 che3- hian3, kiu5 Siong7-te3 tai3-liam7 Ia5-sou kam3-lap8, siuN2-su3 Seng3-sin5 hou7 lan2 Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni lai5 hok8-sai7 kau3 chit8-si3-lang5. (KhoaN Lo5. 12:1,2.)           
Siong7-te3 u7 ThiaN Lang5 Ki5-to2, a2 bo5 ?
U7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, chap8-it he3, lang5 chhoa7 i khi3 in kong e5 chhu3, beh chheng2-an in kong ma2, kap in toa3 kui2 jit8. In kong e5 chhu3 li7 pe7-bu2 chhu3 iok-liok8 lak8 phou3 lou7 . Tiong7- kan u7 he2-chhia e5 lou7. He2-chhia u7 tin7 khah kin2, u7 tin7 khah ban7. Khah kin2 e5 kiaN5 lak8 phou3 lou7 m7-sai2 poaN3-tiam2 cheng ku2, khah ban7.e5 tioh8 nng7 saN pe7 khah ku2 . Am-lam choe3 chit8 e7 sng3 peng5-siong5 chha-put-to chit8 tiam2 cheng ku2. 
Cha-bou2 gin2-na2 tiam3 kong ma2 tau e5 si5 beng5- pek8, beh to3 khi3 pe7-bu2 e5 chhu3. Bo5 lang5 thang kap i khi3, chiu7 chhoa7 i kau3 he2-chhia e5 lou7- cham7, thiah he2-chhia toaN khan i chiuN7 chhia lai7-bin7 che7; chiu7 kap i saN-si5 , hou7 i ka-ki7 to3- khi3. Sui-jian5 bo5 chhin-lang5 thang kap i khi3, iah8 long2 m7-sai2 kiaN, bo5 lang5 oe7 ham7-hai7, kou3 chhia e5 tek8-khak chiau3-kou3.  

Chhia tiaN7-tioh8 tioh8 kau3 in hiuN-sia7 e5 si5, gin2- na2 e5 lau7-pe7 khi3 hit sou2-chai7 e5 lou7-cham7, beh chhoa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 to3-lai5.    
Hit pang he2-chhia poaN3 lou7 tu2-tioh8 toa7 gui5-hiam2, tiN5-poaN3 chhian5 poaN3 tiam2 cheng ku2. Na7- si7 Siong7-te3 chiau3-kou3, bo5 lang5 tu2-tioh8 siong- hai7, si7 khah oaN3 lai5 tiaN7-tiaN7.    
I2-keng kau3, lau7-pe7 khan soe3-han3 cha-bou2 kiaN2, chhoa7 i to3-lai5. KiaN5 lou7 e5 si5, lau7-pe7 mng7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 che7 he2-chhia e5 si5 tu2-tioh8 e5 tai7- chi3, ka7 i kong2, “Bo5 sek8-sai7 e5 lang5 kap li2 ti7 teh, li2 u7 kiaN a2 bo5 ?”  
Gin2-na2 kong2, “Bo5; tok8-tok8 chit8-pai2, tiap-a2- ku2, u7 kiaN tiaN7-tiaN7.”    
Lau7-pe7 mng7, “Hit e5 si7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 ?”      
Gin2-na2 kong2, “Goan2 kun7-oa2 hit e5 chhim- khiN teng2 e5 kio5, tit beh ke3 ·khi3 m7-chai sim2- mih8 tai7-chi3, hut-jian5 he2-chhia thut8 chau2 ke3 piN- thau5, e7-toe2 u7 chui2 iah8 u7 chioh8-poaN5, chhia lai7 cheng3 lang5 kiaN kau3 boe7 kou3-tit, au5-kio3 kong2 “Tit beh siak loh8 khi3, choe3 chit e7 bo5.khi3 !” Na7 si7 u7 jin5-ai3 e5 Chu2 chiau3-kou3 goan2; hou7 kou3 chhia e5 sai-hu7 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 gui5-hiam2, thang pi7-pan7, tu2-tu2 kau3 oe7 poah8--loh8 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 hou7 chhia ui5 chi2, koh siat hoat-tou7 to3-the3 kui-lou7 bo5 siaN2 su7. ”    
I e5 lau7-pe7 si7 put-sin3 e5 lang5, chiu7 tiN3 chhin3- chhio3, mng7 cha-bou2-kiaN2, kong2, “Li2 siaN2-su7 phah- sng3 Chu2 u7 chhap chit e5 tai7-chi3 ? M7-si7 Chu2 teh chiau3-kou3 lin2; si7 hit e5 chim-chiok e5 sai-hu7 jin7-chin kiaN5 i e5 pun2-hun7, bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 thau5-cheng5, khoaN3-kiN3 u7 gui5-hiam2, chiu7 chi2 chhia hou7 i to3-the3, koh chhong3 kau3 ho2-se3. Long2 m7-si7 Chu2, iah8 m7-si7 pat8 hang7 e5 tai7-chi3.”    
Gin2-na2 kong2, “Sim-koaN pe7.ah, chai-iaN2 e5 lang5 kong2, chhia hit tiap8 chhut kio5 piN teng2- bin7, hiam2-hiam2 beh poah8 loh8 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 sin5-jiah. Tak8 lang5 phah-sng3 tek-khak oe7 si2.”    
Lau7-pe7 thiaN-kiN3 chit e5 oe7, siuN7 i cha-bou2- kiaN2 tu2-a2 keng-ke3 chit ho7 e5 toa7 gui5-hiam2, geh8- geh8 chun3 khan i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khah oa2, kong2, “Sim-koaN kiaN2 ah, hit e5 kek8 kiaN e5 si5, li2 chaiN2- iuN7 ? U7 au5-kio3 mah ? A2 si7 u7 sit-khi3 mah ?”    
Gin2-na2 in3 kong2, “Bo5 ah; goa2 chiu7 ki5-to2 lan2 e5 ThiN-pe7, kiu5 I tioh8 chiau3-kou3 goan2, boh8 tit hou7 goan2 tu2-tioh8 chit ho7 bo5 sian7-chiong e5 si2. Ki-tok e5 ThiaN3-thang3 Kam2-kek Lan2

Tam2-ti3 kap Khoa-khau2 tu2-tioh8 Him5
Bat u7 nng7-lang5, chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 choe3 Tam2-ti3, chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 choe3 Khoa-khau2 saN-kap kiaN5 lou7, tit beh jip8 chhiu7-na5, Tam2-ti3 kong2, “Goa2 kiaN-liau2 ti7 chhiu7-na5 u7 ia2-siu3, in-ui7 goa2 khoaN3- kiN3 in e5 kha-jiah ti7 thou5 nih8.” Khoa-khau2 in3 kong2, “M7-sai2 kiaN, na7 u7 tu2-tioh8 ia2-siu3 lan2 beh ho2 taN2, saN chiau3-kou3; goa2 chin7 u7 lat8 goa2 to bo5 kiaN. ” Tu2-a2 teh kong2 e5 si5 hut-jian5 u7 thiaN-kiN3 ia2-siu3 teh hau2 e5 siaN. Khoa-khau2 si7 khah ho2 kha-chhiu2 e5 lang5; chiu7 liam5-piN peh chiuN7 chhiu7, bo5 siuN7 i peng5-iu2 e5 gui5-hiam2.    
Tam2-ti3 khah bo5 ho2 kha-chhiu2, chau2 boe7 li7, ti2-tng ia2-siu3 boe7 tiau5, Chong2-si7 siuN7 chit8 e5 pou7, seng-khu sui5-si5 phak ti7 toe7 nih8, bo5 chhoan2- khui3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 si2 khiau3-khiau3. Hut-jian5 u7 chit8 chiah toa7 chiah him5 tui3 chhim chhiu7-na5 chong5 chhut·lai5 beh ka7 Tam2-ti3.    
Khoa-khau2 ti7 chhiu7 nih8, sit taN2 ka-ki7 siuN7, kai-chai3 goa2 chau2 oe7 li7. Him5 khoaN3-kiN3 Tam2- ti3 phak ti7 toe7 nih8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 si2, chiu7 siong5- se3 phiN7 i e5 seng-khu, chhui3 soa3 oa2 i e5 hi7-a2 chhin-chhiuN7 beh kap i kong2-oe7. Tam2-ti3 tiaN7- tiaN7, long2 bo5 tin2-tang7, khah theng5 him5 phah- sng3 i chin-chiaN3 si2, chiu7 oat8-thau5 chau2 khi3 chhim chhiu7-na5 lai7.    
Khoa-khau2 khoaN3-kiN3 him5 u7 khi3 liau2 taN bo5 gui5-hiam2, chiu7 loh8-lai5. Sim-lai7 li5-li5 a2 kian3-siau3 eng7 kong2-chhio3 e5 oe7 mng7 Tam2-ti3, “Ho2 peng5- iu2 ah, hit chiah him5 u7 ka7 li2 kong2 sim2-mih8 ?” Tam2-ti3 in3 kong2, “I ka7 goa2 kong2, Khoa-khau2 ka- ki7 ho2 taN2 m7 tah8-tioh8 chin-chiaN3 ho2 taN2, koh khng3 goa2 m7 thang oa2-kho3 hit ho7 khoa-khau2 e5 lang5.”  

Iu2-ji7-ni5
Ti7 Au-lo5-pa u7 chit8 e5 kok-ong5 e5 sio2-be7, mia5 kio3 Iu2-ji7-ni5, kui2-na7 ni5 cheng5 u7 seng-khu lam2, i-seng hoan-hu3 tioh8 khi3 bou2 hai2-su7 oaN7 chui2- thou2 khoaN3 seng-khu oe7 khah iong2 bo5. Ti7 hit sou2-chai7 tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 pai2-kha, phoa3-piN7 chin kan-khou2 e5 lang5.    
Iu2-ji7-ni5 khoaN3 liau2 sim thoe3 in chin iu-bun7, siuN7 khoaN3 oe7 siat sim2-mih8 hoat hou7 in oe7 khah khoaiN3-oah8, chiu7 pek-chhiat ki5-to2 kiu5 Siong7- te3 khui lou7. Ki5-to2 liau2 koh siuN7 tioh8 khi2 chit8 keng toa7 keng chhu3 thang hou7 chiah e5 phoa3- piN7 kan-khou2 e5 lang5 toa3,iah8 beh chhiaN3 chai- iaN2 e5 lang5 chiau3-kou3 i. Chong2-si7 lang5 chin choe7, chhu3 tioh8 chin toa7 keng, kiaN-liau2 bo5 hiah choe7 chiN5. An2-ni beh chaiN2-iuN7 ? TaN i si7 ong5 e5 sio2-be7 sou2-i2 u7 choe7-choe7 chhiu2-sek, kim-khi3 (u7 soan7-chioh8,chin-chu) choe7-choe7, chiu7 koh siuN7 beh sia2 phoe kia3 hou7 ong5 mng7-khoaN3 thang boe7 chiah e5 siu2-sek a2 m7 thang, beh eng7 sou2 boe7 e5 chiN5 lai5 khi2 hit keng chhu3. Teh sia2 e5 si5 chiu7 lau5 bak8-sai2 iah8 u7 ki5-to2 kiu5 Siong7-te3 khui ong5 e5 sim chun2 i sou2 mng7.e5. Ong5 tit-tioh8 phoe chin ki5-koai3, siuN7 sio2-be7 kaN2 si7 hoat-kong5, in-ui7 chiah e5 chhiu2-sek, kim-khi3 si7 chou2-kong liu5-thoan5.e5, lau5 hou7 kiaN2-sun, iah8 si7 chin po2-poe3 e5 mih8. Iau2-ku2 phoe lai7 u7 chit8 ku3 oe7 chhi3- chhak8 ong5 e5 sim. Hit ku3 chiu7-si7, “Tioh8 siau3- liam7 Ia5-sou e5 thiaN3 lan2 lai5 chun2 goa2 sou2 ai3.” Ong5 khi2-thau5 m7-kam, lou7-be2 chiu7 chun2 i. In- sio2-be7 chiap tioh8 ong5 e5 phoe chin hoaN-hi2, sui7- si5 khi3 boe7 chhiu2-sek, eng7 sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 gun5 khi2 toa7 e5 koan2; lai7-bin7 chin song2, iah8 u7 chhiaN3 chai-iaN2 e5 lang5 lai5 kou3, Iu2-ji7-ni5 m7 na7 hian3 gun5 hou7 Siong7-te3 iah8 hian3 pun2-sin, tak8-jit8 sun5 i- koan2 chhut khui3-lat8 kong2 to7-li2 long2 boe7 ia3- sian7. Chit8 jit8 chit8 e5 song3-hiong hu7-jin5-lang5 teh beh ke3-ong2. Ong5 e5 sio2-be7 tauh8-tauh8 khi3 bai7 i, pek-chhiat khng3 i tioh8 sin3 Ia5-sou. Hu7-jin5- lang5 tu2-tu2 beh li7-khui se3-kan e5 si5, peh khi2 lai5 chim Iu2-ji7-ni5 e5 chhiu2, lau5 bak8-sai2 tih loh8. kong2, “ThiaN3goa2 e5 Iu2-ji5-ni5 kam2-sia7 Chu2 chhe li2 kau3 chit e5 hai2-su7, li2 na7 bo5 lai5 goa2 chiu7 bo5 sin3 Ia5-sou, leng5-hun5 chiu7 boe7 tit-tioh8 kiu3.” Kong2 soah sui5-si5 ke3-ong2. Jit8 chhut chio3 ti7 Iu2- ji7-ni5 e5 chhiu2, hu7-jin5-lang5 sou2 lau5 kui2-na7 tiam2 e5 bak8-sai2. Tak8 tiam2 e5 bak8-sai2 kng-liang7 chhin- chhiuN7 po2-chioh8. kong2, “Kiu3-chu2.ah, goa2 in-ui7 Li2 e5 mia5 u7 boe7 bo5 ku2-tng5 eng7 e5 soan7-chioh8, taN Li2 long2 heng5 goa2 pi2 tai7-seng ban7-pe7 khah ho2, chiu7-si7 eng2-oan2-oah8 e5 po2-chioh8.

Goa2 Thng3-pak-theh, Lin2 hou7 Goa2 Chheng7
Ti7 Bi2-kok, kau3 tang-thiN e5 si5 chui2 bin7 long2 kian peng. Toe7-khi3 si7 chin-koaN5. U7 chit8 mi5 chit8 e5 peng sun5 kiN. Teh sun5 e5 si5 u7 tu2-tioh8 chit8 e5 than3-chiah8 ·e5 beh to3-khi3 in tau.    
In-ui7 hit-mi5 loh8 sng-seh tang3, i khoaN3-kiN3 hit-e5 peng koaN5 kau3 kheh8-kheh8 tio7, chiu7 thng3 i e5 mi5 hiu5 ka7 i kong2, “ChhiaN2 li2 hiahN khi3 chheng7, goa2 khah bo5 iau3-kin2 in-ui7 goa2 tau ti7 chit ·nih8 na7-tiaN7, li2 tioh8 chit8-mi5 sun5 kau3 kng, na7 bo5 chit nia2 li2 tek-khak tong3-boe7-tiau5.”    
Kau3 thiN-kng e5 si5 chit e5 lang5 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, “Cha7-mi5 hit-e5 sun5 kiN e5 peng u7 khit i koaN5 si2.”

Hit-si5 u7 bang7-kiN3 khoaN3- kiN3 Ia5-sou chheng7 i e5 saN, chiu7 mng7 i kong2,  “Li2 chaiN2-iuN7 chheng7 goa2 e5 saN ?” Ia5-sou in3 kong2, “Li2 oe7 ki3-tit, li2 u7 chiong hiu5 hou7 hit e5 sun5 kiN e5 peng chheng7 mah ? Chit nia2 si7 hit nia2. In- ui7 Goa2 thng3-pak-theh li2 hou7 Goa2 chheng7.”(Ma2- thai3 25:35-40.)               
Gin2-na2 seng5-sit8 Ki5-to2
U7 chit8 e5 soe3 han3 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 phoa3-piN7 put-chi2 siong-tiong7, jian5-au7 i ho2. U7 chit8 jit8 kap i e5 lau7-bu2 chhut-khi3 kiaN5-kiaN5; to3-tng2 lai5 teh beh kau3 chhu3, lau7-bu2 
khoaN3-kiN3 gin2-na2 cheng7-cheng7 chhin-chhiuN7 teh siuN7 iau3-kin2 e5 tai7- chi3, koh hut-jian5 theng5 pou7, tiaN7-tiaN7 khia7, khoan2- sit si7 chhin-chhiuN7 bian2-kiong2 ka-ki7 lai5 kong2, “An2-ne2 goa2 cha7-mi5 u7 ki5-to2. ” Lau7-bu2 in3 kong2, “An2 ni．mah ? Lau7-bu2 chiu7 un5-un5-a2 liu3 i kong2 chhut i e5 i3- su3 khah beng5. Sou2 kong2 e5 chhin-chhiuN7 e7-toe2.

Goa2 chiu7 chhut-lat8 ai3 ki3-tit chi2 chit8 le2- pai3 ku2 sou2 choe3 e5 phaiN2, chiu7 khoaN3 si7 put-chi2 toa7 tui; iah8 koh siuN7 kaN2 u7 choe7-choe7 hang7 ka- ki7 boe7 ki3-tit. Chap8-hun7 Chit8

U7 chit8 e5 Le2-pai3-jit8 cha2-khi2 si5, chit8 e5 i-seng mia5 Kah, mng7 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, “Li2 cha2- khi2 beh chhut-khi3 le2-pai3 bo5 ? Goa2 in-ui7 chiah ku2 choe7-choe7 lang5 phoa3-piN put-si5 bo5 eng5, sou2- i2 kin-a2-jit8 siuN7 m7 chhut-khi3 thiaN to7-li2, beh toa3 lai7-bin7 hioh-khun3.”    
Hu7-jin5-lang5 in3 kong2, “Goa2 iah8 m7 khi3, in-ui7 cha7-jit8 goa2 piaN3 lai7-bin7 chin sian7, siuN7 m7-ai3 khi3.”    
Kong2 soah, chiu7 choe3 i khi3 the ti7 kau i2, chhiu2 hian-khui Seng3-chheh,siuN7 tioh8 thak8 kui2 chat, na7 si7 thak8 bo5 loa7-choe7 bak8-chiu chin siap, chiu7 khun3 khi3.    
Bang7-kiN3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 kui2-sin5, bin7 chin hiong5, che7 ti7 cho7-ui7. Ti7 kui2- sin5 e5 si3-ui5 u7 choe7-choe7 phaiN2 sin5 bin7 iah8 chin7 hiong5, tak8-e5 thau5-khak ti3 he2 choe3 bian2-liu5. Tak8-e5 u7 mia5 ki3 ti7 bian2-liu5, chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 Oan3- hun7, chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 Peh8-chhat8, chit8 e5 mia5 kio3 Kiau-ngou7, chit8 e5 si7 Oan3-tou3, chit8 e5 si7 PhaiN2- seng3-te7. Long2 si7 chit8 ho7 mia5.   
Che7-ui7 e5 kui2 ong5 chiu7 mng7 chiah e5 kui2-sin5 kong2, “Goa2 e5 jin5-sin5.ah, lin2 kin2-a2-jit8 Le2-pai3- jit8 u7 choe3 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 lai5 chou2-tong3 Siong7- te3 e5 kok a2 bo5 ?”Ki5-tiong chit8 e5 in3 kong2, “U7.ah, goan2 chin bo5 eng5 ah, teh chou2-tong3 lang5 khi3 le2-pai3-tng5 thiaN to7-li2.”
Kui2 ong5 kong2. “An2-ni put-chi2 ho2, lin2 eng7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 khi3 chou2-tong3 in ?”    
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 u7 be5-hek8 chit8 lang5 hou7 i siuN7 thau5-khak hin5 m7-bian2 
khi3 le2-pai3, na7- si7 goa2 chai i na7 khi3 tek-khak tit-tioh8 toa7 li7- ek, in-ui7 hit jit8 bok8-su sou2 pi7-pan7 e5 to7-li2 put- chi2 oe7 kam2-kek lang5 e5 sim.”    
Koh chit e5 kong2, “Goa2 be5-hek8 chit8 lang5 hou7 i kin-a2-jit8 khi3 tiam3 nih8, lai5 ki3 siau3. ”    
Ong5 in3 kong2, “An2-ni chin ho2, au7-lai5 ng3- bang7 i oe7 un5-a8 pang3-sak to7-li2.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 be5-hek8 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5- lang5, hou7 i siuN7 i e5 seng-khu e5 saN-a2-kun5 bo5 si5- kiaN5, thau5-khak sou2 chhah e5 chhiam-a2 bo5 sim2- mih8 ho2 khoaN3,lang5 oe7 khoaN3-khin i, i chiu7 m7 khi3.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 iah8 an2-ni be5-hek8 choe7-choe7 song3-hiong lang5, hou7 i siuN7 i na7 khi3 le2-pai3, khah ho2-giah8 e5 lang5 ai3 iam3-chian7 i, na7- si7 goa2 chai, si7 bo5-iaN2, in-ui7 choe7-choe7 ho2-giah8 lang5 put-chi2 ai3 li7-ek song3-hiong lang5, koh kap lang5 saN-kap chu7-chip8 ki5-to2, gim5-si, thak8 Seng3- chheh, si7 put-chi2 oe7 li7-ek lang5, iah8 hou7 Siong7- te3 sou2 hoaN-hi2.  
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 be5-hek8 kui2-na7 e5 ho2- giah8 lang5, hou7 in siuN7, in ai3 the2-bian7 liah8 in e5 the2-bian7 khah iau3-kin2 Siong7-te3 e5 kai3-lut8. In- chiu7 siuN7,m7 thang kiaN5 lou7 khi3 le2-pai3-tng5, na7 beh che7 kio7 mi7 hui3-khi3, sou2-i2 in chiu7 toa3 lai7- bin7, a2-si7 khi3 chit8 pou na7-tiaN7.”    
Kui2 ong5 chiu7 kong2, “Lin2 chin-chiaN3 gau5 eng7 ke3-ti3 lai5 hai7 Siong7-te3 e5 peh8-siN3.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kui2-ong5, “Goa2 iah8 u7 be5-hek8 choe7- choe7 kau3-hoe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7, hou7 in siuN7 in e5  ia3-sian7, seng-khu bo5 iong2, sou2-i2 Le2-pai3-jit8 tioh8 khah oaN3 khi2-lai5, in chiu7 boe7 hu3 le2-pai3, sou2-i2 lai5 chit8 pou tiaN7-tiaN7.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 an2-ni be5-hek8 kui2- na7 lang5, hou7 in siuN7 in si7 sio2-khoa2 
phoa3-piN7 m7 thang chhut-mng5, na7-si7 goa2 chai in eng2-jit8 tek-khak bo5 hou7 sio2-khoa2 phoa3-piN7 chou2-tong3 in choe3 seng-li2, a2-si7 choe3 kang. In chin-chiaN3 si7 gong7, in-ui7 khi3 le2-pai3 put-chi2 khah khoai3 m7 sai2 chhut-lat8, si7 che7 teh thiaN tia7-tiaN7, thiaN liau2 in e5 sim oe7 peng5-an hoaN-hi2.”    
Kui2 ong5 in3 kong2, “Chiah e5 lang5 hou7 chit ho7 sio2-khoa2 su7 chou2-tong3 in bo5 siu2 Siong7-te3 Seng3- jit8, chin-chiaN3 si7 chin7 gong7. Lin2 an2-ni in2-iu2 be5- hek8 lang5 si7 oe7 chou2-tong3 choe7-choe7 lang5 tit- tioh8 kiu3.”   
Koh chit8 e5 kui2 kong2, “Goa2 u7 put-chi2 ho2 e5 hoat-tou7, chou2-tong3 choe7-choe7 siau3-lian5 lang5 tit-tioh8 to7-li2.”    
Kui2 ong5 in3 kong2, “Che put-chi2 ho2, chou2-tong3 siau3-lian5 lang5 si7 te7 it iau3-kin2, in-ui7 siau3-lian5 lang5 khah khoai3 hak8-sip8 to7-li2, koh sim-koaN khah nng2, Seng3 Sin5 chiu7 oe7 eng7 Seng3-chheh e5 ka3-si7 kam2-kek in e5 sim. Li2 eng7 sim2-mih8 pou7- sou3 lai5 hai7 hit ho7 lang5 ?”    
Hit e5 kui2 chiu7 koh kong2, “Goa2 hou7 in liah8 se3-kan e5 hong-siok8 khah iaN5 Siong7-te3 kok e5 to7- li2. Goa2 be5-hek8 choe7-choe7 kau3-hoe7 tiong siau3- lian5 e5 chi2-be7, hou7 in m7 khi3 le2-pai3, in-ui7 ti7 lou7 nih8 kiam2-chhai2 oe7 hou7 lang5 kong2 phaiN2-oe7 a2- si7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 chiu7 kian3-siau3, in an2-ni sit-loh8 choe7-choe7 li7-ek, sim oe7 leng2, au7-lai5 ng3- bang7 in kui2-na7 ni5 bo5 chhut-mng5, chiu7 koaiN3- si3, m7 ai3 khi3.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kong2, “Goa2 iah8 u7 chou2-tong3 hu7- jin5-lang5 khi3 thiaN to7-li2.”    
Kui2 ong5 mng7, “Li2 eng7 sim2-mih8 ke3-ti3 be5- hek8 in ?”    
I in3 kong2. “M7 si7 be5-hek8 hu7-jin5-lang5 pun2-sin, si7 be5-hek8 in ta-pou lang5, hou7 in siuN7 ta-pou lang5 khi3 chiu7 ho2, ke-lai7 lang5 m7 bian2 khi3,in khah boe7 hiau2-tit thiaN, khi3 bo5 lou7-eng7, tui3 an2-ni chou2-tong3 i, Na7-si7 goa2 chai hu7-jin5-lang5 khi3 thiaN chin iau3-kin2, in-ui7 in teh chiau3-kou3 kiaN2-ji5. Siat-su2 lau7-bu2 bo5 thiaN to7-li2 thai2 oe7 ka3-si7 kiaN2- ji5 lai5 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3.”    

Kui2 ong5 kong2, “Li2 sou2 choe3 chin si7 ho2, chin gau5 phian3 lang5. Li2 m7 na7 chou2-tong3 hai7 hu7-jin5- lang5 hou7 i bo5 tit-tioh8 kiu3, iah8 hai7 kiaN2-ji5, hou7 in tui3 soe3-han3 m7 bat to7-li2, an2-ni oe7 khoai3 jip8 lan2 e5 kok lai5 choe3 phaiN2.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kui2-sin5 kong2, “Goa2 hai7 Siong7-te3 e5 kok hoat, si7 be5-hek8 lang5, hou7 in Pai3-lak8 siuN7 bo5 eng5 thang pi7-pan7 sim choan siu2 Siong7- te3 Seng3-jit8. U7 lang5 hou7 i Pai3-lak8 ai3 choe3 kang kau3 oaN3-oaN3, u7 e5 hou7 in lau5 kui2-na7 hang7 kau3 Le2-pai3-jit8 cha2-khi2 lai5 choe3; kiam2-chhai2 tam7-poh8 kang, a2-si7 tam7-poh8 khang-khe3, a2-si7 le2- pai3 soah sun7-soa3 boe2 tam7-poh8 mih8, an2-ni long2 si7 chou2-tong3 lang5 siu2 Le2-pai3-jit8 e5 hoat-tou7.”

Koh gin2-na2 tui3 soe3-han3 bo5 iok-sok chiu7 bo5 keng3-ui3 e5 sim, m7-chai thang hun-piat Siong7-te3 e5 chhu3 koh-iuN7 ti7 lang5 e5 chhu3.”    
Koh chit8 e5 kui2-sin5 kong2, “Goa2 be5-hek8 kau3- hoe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7, hou7 i thiaN to7-li2 e5 si5 khi2 m7 kam-goan7 e5 sim, bo5 liah8 hit e5 to7-li2 si7 Siong7- te3 e5, hoan2-tng2 teh siuN7 sian-siN u7 sim2-mih8 pe7- peng7 thang phoe-pheng5, a2-si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 gau5 khui-thiah, a2-si7 kan-ta ai3 hiam5 goa2 chit8 lang5; tui3 an2-ni u7 khi3, iah8 na2 bo5 khi3, to7-li2 boe7 jip8 in e5 sim.”

Sui-jian5 choe7-choe7 lang5 chu7-chip8 teh thiaN, iah8 bo5 li7-ek.”

Au7-lai5 in lau7-bu2 chhoa7 Giok8-a2 khi3 chit8 e5  chhai5-hong5 e5 tiam3, chhai5-hong5 hit-si5 teh choe3 saN nia2 saN, chiu7-si7 beh hou7 Giok8-a2 kap i nng7- e5 toa7-chi2 thang chheng7. In lau7-bu2 ka7 chhai5- hong5 kong2, “Giok8-a2 e5 saN bian2 choe3, i chit-chun7 bo5 khiam3-eng7.” To3-khi3 e5 si5 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 ai3 thau-theh8 mih8, lang5 na7 beh chhiaN2 li2, goa2 m7 kaN2 hou7 li2 khi3, an2-ni m7-bian2 chhoe3 sin saN hou7 li2”    
Hit e5-hng5 in pe7 kio3 i lai5 ka7 i kong2, “Giok8-a2 tui3 chit-si5 ‘Gin2-na2 Geh8 Pou3 m7 koh hou7 li2 siu.”    
Che si7 in-ui7 Giok8-a2 chin3-pou7 thak8- chheh in pe7 u7 kong2, tak8 geh8-jit8 beh boe2 geh8 po3 siuN2 i. “Gin2-na2 Geh8 Po3,”si7 chin sui2 e5 chheh, u7 choe7-choe7 ang-a2 tou5, ia7 u7 ho2 thak8 e5 kou3-su7.    

Giok8-a2 put-chi2 po2-sioh, tak8 geh8-jit8 tit-tioh8, i e5 sim hoaN-hi2 kau3-kek8. I e5 hiaN ti7 chi2-be7 tak8-e5 ia7 chin ai3 khoaN3, ia7 u7 chioh tang5 oh8 e5 hak8-seng khoaN3. Tak8 geh8 thak8 liau2 u7 soe3-ji7 siu, kau3 u7 lak8 pun2 chiu7 u7 theh8 kau3 chheh tiam3 kap sui2-sui2  e5 phe5.    
Giok8-a2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 14 geh8-jit8 e5 po3, taN u7 nng7 e5 toa7 pun2 chiu7-si7 lak8 pun2 kap choe3 chit8- pun2, ia7 u7 nng7 e5 thiah pun2.    
Hiah-e5 chheh si7 Giok8-a2 e5 sim sou2 ai3, siong5- siong5 theh8 chhut-lai5 thak8, lang5 lai5 i ai3 pun in khoaN3. U7- si5 in chi2-be7 ai3 mng7, “Giok8-a2 lan2 pe7 siaN2-su7 m7 koh boe2, ‘Gin2-na2 Geh8-Po3’ hou7 li2 ?”I ia7 bo5 oe7 thang in3.

Chit8 hang7 i tui3 hit si5 chai Siong7-te3 si7 kun7- kun7 e5 Siong7-te3 tak8 hang7 chai, Siong7-te3 thiaN3 i, m7 kam hou7 i khng3-bat8 i e5 choe7, ai3 i tit-tioh8 chheng-khi3 e5 sim. Koh tui3 hit si5 chai “Siong7- te3 sia3 i e5 choe7, kham3 i e5 che7-koa3, che lang5 u7 hok-khi3” Si. 32: 1,2. Na7 si7 “ka-ki7 khng3 i e5 m7 tioh8 boe7 tit-tioh8 hok-khi3.” Chin 28:13.   

Gau5 e5 Hu7-jin5-lang5 Kam2-kek Tiong7-hu
Kui2 ni5 cheng5 ti7 Tai7-bi2 kok e5 chit8 sou2-chai teh sin khui pou-thau5. U7 chit8 e5 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 choh-sit lang5, chhiaN3 chin choe7 kang teh khui hng5. I ai3 than3 ho2 thiN kin2 khui hou7 i liau2, sou2-i2 tak8 cha2-khi2 chiu7 cha2-cha2 kio3 kang khi3 khui. I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 na7 chu2-sek8, chiu7 pun5 hou7-thau5 kio3 in to3 lai5 chiah8.    
Chit8 e5 choh-sit lang5 peng5-siong5 tak8 cha2-khi2 u7 kap i e5 ke-lai7 kap i e5 kang thak8 Seng3-chheh ki5-to2; Chong2-si7 in-ui7 beh koaN2 kang soah boe7 kou3-tit choe3 le2-pai3.    
I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 i khah ti3-i3 se3-kan e5 chiN5-chai5 boe7 kou3-tit hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, chiu7 khou2-khng3 i, na7-si7 i long2 m7 thiaN; chiah siuN7 chit8 e5 hoat-tou7 hou7 i e5 tiong7-hu khoaN3 oe7 koe2- pin3 a2-boe7. Ke3 cha2-khi2, chit e5 choh-sit lang5 iu5-goan5 cha2-cha2 kio3 kang khi3 khui hng5. Kau3 jit8 chin oaN3 long2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3 pun5 ho7-thau5, tak8 e5 iau kau3 kha nng2 chhiu2 nng2. To3-lai5 kau3 te3 khoaN3 chit8 te3 toh khang-khang iah8 bo5 pai5 mih8-phe3 oaN2 phiat, i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 choe3 i cheng7- cheng7 teh thak8 Seng3-chheh. Choh-sit lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 an2-ni chiu7 jiong2 kong2, “Eh ! iah8 png7 ti7 tah-loh8 ? ”I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 in3 i kong2, “Goa2 khoaN3 lin2 chiah bo5 eng5, phah-sng3 bo5 eng5 thang chiah8.”    
Choh-sit-lang5 koh jiong2 kong2, “Bo5 eng5 thang chiah8, kiam2 oe7 oah8 mah ?”    
I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 koh in3 i kong2, “Jiok8-sin bo5 chiah8 boe7 oah8, leng5-hun5 bo5 chiah8 boe7 oah8. Jiok8-sin na7 tioh8 chiah8, leng5-hun5 iah8 tioh8 chiah8. ”   
Choh-sit-lang5 koh kong2, “Ho2, ho2, seng khi3 chu2 lai5 chiah8, chiong kim i2-au7 put-koan2 u7 eng5, bo5 eng5, tek8-khak beh choe3 le2-pai3.” I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7 khi3 chu2. Tui3 hit-tiap8 chin chit-si3- lang5 i tak8 cha2-khi2 u7 thak8 Seng3-chheh, ki5-to2.    
A3-li7-kan2 sou2 Bang7-kiN3
U7 lang5 liah8 ka-ki7 choe3 ho2 lang5 lai5 khoaN3- khin pat8-lang5. Ia5-sou chiong phi3-ju7 ka3-si7 in. Che phi3-ju7 e5 oe7 ki3-chai3 ti7 Lou7-ka 18:9.    
Cha2 u7 hit ho7 chu7-chheng choe3 ho2 e5 lang5, hian7-si5 iah8 u7. Hit ho7 e5 lang5 sit8-chai7 toa7-toa7 ka-ki7 phian3.    
Ti7 bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 Hoe5-hoe5 kau3 e5 lang5 mia5 kio3 A3-li7-kan2. Chiau3 lang5 khoaN3 si7 put-chi2 the2-bian7, koh si7 jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, in- iu7 i siong5-siong5 chin gau5 ki5-to2, kim3-chiah8, chin2- che3 song3-hiong lang5. Chong2-si7 i kiaN5 an2-ni tiau5- kou3-i3 beh hou7 lang5 khoaN3, tui3 lang5 kiu5 eng5- kng; m7-si7 seng5-sit8 oa2-kho3 Siong7-te3, si7 oa2-kho3 ka-ki7 e5 ho2, oh8 Hoat-li7-sai3 lang5 ki5-to2 kong2, “Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 goa2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 pat8- lang5.”    
U7 chit8-jit8 i teh kiaN5-lou7 e5 si5, u7 tu2-tioh8 chit8 e5 peng5-iu2. Hit e5 peng5-iu2 khoaN3-kiN3 A3-li7-kan2 u7 iu-thau5 khou2-bin7 chiu7 liah8 choe3 ki5-koai3, in-ui7 i peng5-si5 liah8 ka-ki7 si7 ho2 m7 bat u7 iu-bun7 e5 bin7-sek. TaN khoaN3 i an2-ni chiu7 mng7 i kong2, “A3-li7-kan2.ah, li2 teh iu-bun7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 ? Si7 lin2 ke-lai7 u7 lang5 phoa3-piN7 mah ? A2-si7 seng-li2 choe3 liau2 sih8-pun2 mah ?”     
A3-li7-kan2 in3 kong2, “Bo5, goan2 tau bo5 lang5 piN7, seng-li2 iah8 bo5 sih8-pun2, tok8-tok8 cha7-mi5 goa2 u7 bang7-kiN3 chit8 e5 bang7,hou7 goa2 put-chi2 kiaN sim-lai7 chin kan-khou2. Chit-tiap8 goa2 beh khi3 Su-li7-ma2 chiu7-si7 goan2 peh in tau, i si7 toa7 phok- hak8 e5 lang5 liong7-siong2 oe7 ka7 goa2 oan5 hit e5 bang7.”    
Hit e5 peng5-iu2 ki-chhi3 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 m7 thang khi3 mng7 Su-li7-ma2 i kun7-lai5 sin3 Ia5-sou thak8 Seng3-chheh, i oe7 phah-phian3 li2, ka-ki7 khoaN3 bo5 lou7 thai2-thou2 oe7 chhoa7 pat8-lang5. ”    
A3-li7-kan2 kong2, “KaN2 m7 si7 an2-ni mah ? I i2-keng kui2-na7 pai2 bat eng7 ti3-sek e5 
oe7 chi2-si7 goa2, sou2-i2 chit-tiap8 beh koh khi3 chheng2-kau3 i.”    
Kong2 soah sui5-si5 chiu7 khi3 Su-li7-ma2 in tau kap i kiaN5 le2, saN-kap che7-loh8 kong2 khi2, i sou2 bang7-kiN3 e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 in peh thiaN, kong2, “Goa2 bang7-kiN3 goa2 i2-keng ke3-ong2, sin-si hou7 lang5 tai5, leng5-hun5 bo5 si2 iau2 ti7-teh. Koh bang7-kiN3 chit8-ki toa7 thian-peng5 si7 kim．e5, thian-peng5 thiau7 koaiN5 kau3 tu2 thiN. Goa2 khia7 ti7 hia u7 sin5 beh lai5 pheng5 goa2 it-seng sou2 kiaN5 e5 ho2 phaiN2. Ho2 e5 beh he7 ti7 chiaN3 peng5 e5 pheng5-oaN2, phaiN2 e5 he7 ti7 to3 peng5 e5 pheng5-oaN2. In peh Su-li2-ma2 in3 kong2, “Li2 khoaN3-kiN3 kau3 an3-ni kaN2 oe7 hou7 li2 kiaN kau3 kheh8-kheh8 tio7  houN3 ?”   
A3-li7-kan2 in3 kong2, “Khi2-thau5 bo5 kau3 joa7 toa7 kiaN ka-ki7 siuN7 peng5-si5 e5 sou2 kiaN5 bo5 sim2-mih8 toa7 m7 tioh8, thong siaN5 e5 lang5 bo5 lang5 oe7 tat8- tit goa2 hiah keng3-khian5, gau5 siu2 kai3-lut8. Ka- ki7 siuN7 bo5 sim2-mih8 phaiN2-tai7 thang he7 ti7 to3- peng5 e5 pheng5-oaN2, sou2-i2 hit-tiap8 goa2 chiah bo5 kiaN, jian5-au7 oat8 thau5 khoaN3-kiN3 chit tin7 ou-ou chin beng2 e5 kui2 tak8-e5 kim-kim siong3 goa2, chiah chai kiaN.”    
Su-li7-ma2 koh mng7 kong2, “Iau2 u7 bang7-kiN3 pat8 mih8 bo5 ?”    
In3 kong2, U7.ah koh bang7-kiN3 chit8 tin7 ou e5 kui2 kiaN5-oa2 kau3 thian-peng5 tak8 e5 teh taN chit8 taN3 chin tang7 e5 ou mih8. Goa2 teh khoaN3 e5 si5 u7 saN hang7 hou7 goa2 chin ki5- koai3, chiu7-si7 ki5-koai3 chiah e5 ou-mih8 kau3 chiah choe7, kau3 chiah toa7, iah8 kau3 chiah tang7. Goa2 sou2 liah8 choe3 bo5 iau3-kin2 e5 su7 taN khoaN3 si7 chin tang7 e5 choe7. Kui2 tak8 e5 kau3 chiu7 piaN3 i sou2 taN e5 ti7 to2-peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2. Goa2 chiu7 ki3-tit siok8-gu2 kong2, ‘Kha poah8-to2 oe7 peh khi2 lai5, chhui3 kong2 chhut boe7 koh siu to3-tng2.’”    
Su-li7-ma2 chiu7 kong2, “Ia5-sou e5 oe7 pi2 siok8-gu2 si7 koh khah ho2. I bat kong2, ‘Goa2 ka7 lin2 kong2, kong2 oe7 lam7-sam2 tek-khak ti7 siu7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 jit8 hou7 Siong7-te3 sim2-phoaN3, in-ui7 chiau3 li2 e5 oe7 chiu7 chheng-gi7, iah8 chiau3 li2 e5 oe7 chiu7 gi2- choe7.’”(Ma2-thai3 12 :36, 37. )    
A3-li7-kan2 thou2-khui3 kong2, “Goa2 kaN2 u7 bat kong2 chit8 ban7 ku3 phaiN2 oe7 long2 boe7 sng3-tit. To3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2 tang7 kau3 beh loh8 te7-gek8. Ai- ah ! kau3 an2-ni iau2-be7 liau2,iau2 u7 khah chham2 e5 su7, chiu7-si7 hit si5 Sat-tan3 koh toa7-siaN hoan-hu3 kong2, “A3-li7-kan2 phaiN2 e5 liam7-thau5 tioh8 taN lai5 !”  ThiaN chit8 ku3 goa2 koh thou2-khui3 kong2, “Ai- ah ! liam7-thau5 iah8 tioh8 siu7 kam3-chhat mah ?”  
Su-li7-ma2 in3-kong2, “Seng3-chheh u7 ki3-chai3, phaiN2 e5 liam7-thau5 si7 tui3 sim khi2, che si7 bak la5-sam5 lang5.” (Ma2-thai3 15:19,20 )    
A3-li7-kan2 kong2, “Kui2 sui5-si5 taN lai5, Chiah e5 phaiN2 e5 liam7-thau5 chiau3 bak8-chiu khoaN3 si7 chhin- chhiuN7ou-ian, Chong2-si7 toa7-toa7 bo5 tang5, in-ui7 ou-ian si7 khin-khin, phaiN2 e5 liam7-thau5 chin chiaN3 tang7, Kui2 piaN3 ti7 to3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2, hou7 hit oaN2 loh8 koh khah ke7 kau3 jip8 chit8 e5 chhim-ian, Chhim-ian thou3-chhut he2 chih8 kap ou hun. Hit si5 goa2 hiam2-hiam2 sit-bang7, hut-jian5 ki3-tit goa2 sou2 kiaN5 e5 ho2 u7 iau2-be7 hou7 i pheng5. Goa2 siuN7 ho2 su7 un2-tang3 choe7-choe7, chiu7 toa7- siaN kio3 thiN-sai3 kong2, ‘Ho2 tioh8 kin2-kin2 taN lai5 ! Goa2 sou2 bat chin2-che3 lang5, sou2 bat kim3-chiah8, chiah e5 ho2 su7 
tioh8 kin2-me2 piaN3 ti7 chiaN3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2. Goa2 siuN7 chit-tiap8 chiaN3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2 tek-khak pi2 to3 peng5 e5 khah tang7, hou7 to3 peng5 koaiN5-koaiN5 chiaN3 peng5 ke7-ke7.    
ThiN-sai3 kui2 na7 e5 ban7-ban7 lai5, tak8 e5 teh taN chit8 taN3peh8 e5 mih8. Thau5-cheng5 e5 thiN-sai3 khui chhui3 kong2, “Lang5 kiaN5 ho2-su7 in-ui7 ai3 kui eng5- kng Siong7-te3, hit ho7 su7 sng3 si7 tang7, na7 kiaN5 ho2 su7 ai3 tit choe3 kong-lo5, hit ho7 su7 chiu7 sng3 si7 khin chhin-chhiuN7 chiau2-mng5. Li2 it-seng sou2 kiaN5 e5 ho2-su7 goan2 taN-liau2 si7 khin-khin.”    
Kong2-soah thiN-sai3 tak8 e5 piaN3 in sou2 taN e5 peh8 mih8 ti7 chiaN3 peng5 oaN2. Hiah e5 mih8 sit8-chai7 khin, thian-peng5 long2 bo5 chit8 tiam2-a2 tin2-tang7. Khou2 ah ! Goa2 sou2 kim3-chiah8 kap chin2-che3 lang5, chiah e5 su7 long2 kui ti7 bo5.    
Koh siuN7, goa2 peng5-si5 ki5-to2 hit chat su7 iau2- be7 taN lai5 pheng5, chiu7 kiu5-kio3 thiN-sai3 kong2, “Goa2 ki5-to2 e5 su7 ! Goa2 ki5-to2 e5 su7 ! Tioh8 kin2-kin2 taN lai5. Goa2 chit8 jit8 ki5-to2 u7 gou7 pai2, i2-keng ki5-to2 chiah choe7 ni5, an2-ni chiah e5 ki5-to2 e5 su7 tek-khak ke3-thau5 tang7.” Goa2 tui3 thiN-sai3 kong2-liau2 hit-si5 hou7 goa2 toa7 ki5-koai3, boe7 beng5-pek8, in- ui7 khoaN3-kiN3 hiah-e5 thiN-sai3 chiong goa2 peh8-peh8 e5 ki5-to2 kau hou7 ou kui2. Hiah e5 ou kui2 chit8 e7 chhut chhiu2 lai5 beh taN; bong-liau2 peh8-e5 liam5-piN piN3 choe3 ou, chiu7 taN khi3 piaN3-loh8 to3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2.”    
Su-li7-ma2 kong2, “Hit-e5 cheng5-heng5 chaiN2-iuN7 an2-ni, goa2 beh ka7 li2 kong2, tek-khak si7 tui3 ki5- to2 Siong7-te3 bo5 seng5-sit8, bo5 keng3-khian5. Chhui3 ki5-to2, sim siat-tok8, goa7-phe5 keng3-ui3, lai7-sim kiau-ngou7, chiu7-si7 phah la5-sam5 Siong7-te3 seng3- mia5, chhin-chhiuN7 hoan7 tioh8 te7 saN tiau5 seng3 e5 kai3-beng7, sou2-i2 boe7 sng3-tit si7 ho2, hoan2-tng2 sng3 si7 phaiN2. ThiN-sai3 chiah bo5 lai5 ka7 li2 taN, tok8-tok8 u7 kio3 hiah-e5 ou-kui2 lai5 ka7 li2 taN, hou7 li2 chai li2 it-seng ki5-to2 Siong7-te3 boe7 sng3-tit si7 ho2.A3-li7-kan2 ah, li2 u7 koh bang7-kiN3 sim2-mih8 ?”    

In3 kong2, “U7, bang7-kiN3 phaiN2 kui2 lai5 liah8 goa2, hit-si5 kiaN kau3 cheng-sin5. Peh-ah, m7 chai bat thiaN-kiN3 lang5 bang7-kiN3 phaiN2 bang7 kau3 an2-ni bo5 ?”    
Su-li7-ma2 in3 kong2, “Cheng-sin5 e5 si5, goa2 bat siuN7 chit ho7 e5 to7-li2, bat khoaN3 goa2 sou2 kiaN5 sou2 siuN7 long2-chong2 hou7 Siong7-te3 sim2-phoaN3. U7 chit8 chat Seng3-chheh hou7 goa2 kiaN kau3 ngiauh8- ngiauh8-chhoah, chiu7-si7 kong2 ‘Siong7-te3 pheng5 li2, chai li2 u7 khiam3-kheh,’ (Tan7. 5: 27. ) Goa2 long2 bo5 chit8 chat ho2 su7 thang lok ti7 chiaN3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2. A3-li7-kan2 ah, goa2 siuN7 an2-ni chiu7 kiu5-kio3 kong2, ‘Siong7-te3 ah, tioh8 lin5- bin2 goa2 u7 choe7 e5 lang5 !”)    
A3-li7-kan2 mng7 kong2, “An2-ni se3-kan lang5 long2 bo5 thang ng3-bang7 mah ? Kiam2 bo5 sim2-mih8 tang7 e5 mih8 thang lok ti7 chiaN3 peng5 e5 oaN2, hou7 to3 peng5 oe7 khah khin m7 sai2 loh8 te7-gek8 ?”    
Su-li7-ma2 in3 kong2, “Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 ! Kam2- sia7 Siong7-te3 ! M7 sai2 sit-bang7, goa2 bat khoaN3-kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 po2 huih chit8 tiam2 tih ti7 chiaN3 peng5 e5 thian-peng5-oaN2. Chit8 tiam2 pi2 to3-peng5-oaN2 sou2 toe2 e5 phaiN2 khah tang7. To3-peng5 liam5-piN khi2 chin koaiN5. Goa2 e5 choe7 long2 bo5 khi3, eng2-eng2 bo5 khi3, boe7 koh chai3 khoaN3-kiN3. Goa2 siong5-se3 khoaN3 chai-kio3 m7 si7 niau, kau2 lai5 ka7, iah8 m7 si7 chhat8 lai5 liah8, beng5-beng5 si7 goa2 e5 keh-piah eng7 chioh8-thau5 tim3-si2 e5, in-ui7 chit e5 keh-piah ui7-tioh8 tai7-chi3 m7 hoaN-hi2 goa2.    
Goa2 chit8 si5 tong3 boe7 tiau5 sim he2 long2 toh8; goa2 e5 toa7 kiaN2 iah8 beh kap i ke3-kau3, chong2-si7 goa2 siuN7 m7 thang.    
Ke3 kui2 jit8, goa2 tu2-tioh8 keh-piah chit e5 hu7- jin5-lang5, i khoaN3-kiN3 chiu7 siam2 goa2, Koh ke3 chit8 le2-pai3 goa2 e5 toa7 kiaN2 ka7 goa2 kong2, ‘Lan2 hit e5 keh-piah taN tu2-tioh8 po3-eng3 lah.’ Goa2 mng7 si7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3, i ka7 goa2 kong2, ‘In hit e5 ta- pou-lang5 tui3 khi2 chhu3 e5 ke3-nih8 poah8-loh8-lai5 tioh8 siong chin siong-tiong7. Hian7 si5 kng to3-lai5, phah- sng3 kaN2 boe7 oah8.’     
Goa2 thiaN-liau2 chiu7 siuN7 kong2, “Kho2-lin5 ah ! In ta-pou-lang5 teh-beh si2 lah. Kai-chai3 goa2 cheng5 jit8 bo5 kap i ke3-kau3,” na7 si7 to3 siuN7 kong2, goa2 sui- jian5 bo5 kap i ke3-kau3, iau2-ku2 u7 khng3 m7 kam- goan7 ti7 goa2 e5 sim, an2-ni thai2-tho2 oe7 ng3-bang7 Siong7-te3 beh sia3-bian2 goa2. Hong2-hut na2 chhin- chhiuN7 thiaN-kiN3 u7 siaN tui3 goa2 kong2, “Li2 tioh8 khi3 bai7 i.” Goa2 bo5 siaN2 ai3, sou2-i2 hit jit8 bo5 khi3. Kau3 ke3 jit8 kou-put-chiong khi3.    
Hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 lai5 ka7 goa2 khui mng5, goa2 mng7 i kong2, “Li2 e5 ta-pou-lang5 khah ho2 bo5 ?” I kong2, “Bo5.” Goa2 koh kong2, “Cham2-jian5 siong- tiong7 mah ?” I kong2. “Na7 bo5 siong-tiong7 thai2-tho2 u7 to2-teh, li2 phah-sng3 i teh m7 kaN2 chhut-lai5 mah ? Goa2 m7 ai3 lang5 lai5 chia khoaN3-khoaN3, goa2 iah8 bo5 kang thang kap lang5 kong2 eng5 e5 oe7.” Kong2 liau2 chiu7 koaiN mng5. Goa2 bo5 kiok8- chiu7 to3-lai5. Ke3 chit8 nng7 jit8 goa2 koh tu2-tioh8 chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, iu5-goan5 mng7 i kong2, “Lin2 ta- pou-lang5 taN u7 khah ho2 bo5 ?” I soe3-a2 siaN in3 kong2, “Na2 siong-tiong7, goa2 mi5-jit8 teh kou3 i, ka-ki7 long2 bo5 khun3, phah-sng3 kaN2 si7 bo5 joa7 ku2 lang5.” Teh kong2 e5 si5, bak8-sai2 ti7 i e5 bak8-kiN5. Goa2 
khoaN3-liau2 iah8 thoe3 i siong-sim, chiu7 ka7 i kong2, ‘Li2 na7 kheng2, goa2 e5-hng chiu7 khi3 thoe3 li2 kou3, hou7 li2 thang hioh-khun3.’    
I in3, ‘Ho2,’sou2-i2 goa2 chiu7 khi3, m7 na7 chit8 e5- hng, long2-kiong7 gou7 lak8 pai2. Lou7-be2 i e5 ta-pou- lang5 keng2-jian5 u7 tit-tioh8 ho2. Chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiah chiong tim3-si2 koe e5 tai7-chi3 long2-chong2 tui3 goa2 jin7.    
Hit tiap8 liau2-au7 goan2 piN3 choe3 ho2 peng5-iu2, ti7 le2-pai3-tng5 siong5-siong5 saN-kap che7, i e5 kiaN2 kap goa2 e5 kiaN2 iah8 an2-ni.   
TaN goan2 chaiN2-iuN7 oe7 an2-ni ? Beng5-beng5 si7 tui3 hit ho7 khoan-iong5 thun-lun2 e5 ke2-chi2 lai5. Chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-chheh kong2, “Siat-su2 u7 saN- hiam5 e5 tai7-chi3, tioh8 saN khoan-iong5 saN sia3-bian2, Ki-tok sia3-bian2 lin2 e5 choe7, lin2 iah8 tioh8 chhin- chhiuN7 an2-ni.”   
 TaN goa2 ka-ki7 chai Chu2 e5 peng5-an ti7 goa2 e5 sim-lai7. Sui-jian5 iau2-ku2 be7 chiau5-pi7, ti7 sim-lai7 u7 choe7-choe7 phaiN2 tioh8 koaN2-chhut, na7-si7 goa2 bo5 sit-chi3, bo5 choat8-bang7, ng3-bang7 goa2 tak8 jit8 e5 sou2-kiaN5 u7 ho2 thiaN e5 han-leng5, iah8 soa3 u7 Seng3 Sin5 e5 ke2-chi2 ti3-kau3 ‘kong-hok8 e5 e7-kha-pou5 u7 kim ham-leng5 kap siah8-liu5 lai5 saN-kaiN3.’’

Mng5 chit8 e7 khui, toa7-han3 soe3-han3 chhio3 hah-hah, bak8-chiu chhia-chhia tng2, koh kim- kim siong3 hiah e5 mih8, chhui3 chiap8-chiap8 hau2, kha chhiu2 ngiauh8-ngiauh8 tang7.
