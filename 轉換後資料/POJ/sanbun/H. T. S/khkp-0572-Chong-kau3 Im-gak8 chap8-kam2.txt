Chong-kau3 Im-gak8 chap8-kam2
H.T.S.

Kin7-lai5 Chong-kau3 im-gak8 chiam7-chiam7 hoat-tat8 chin3-po7, sit8-chai7 thang hoaN-hi2 e5 hian7-siong7. Kau3-hoe7 u7 kui2-na7 so2-chai7 cham2-jian5 ti3-i3 ti7 chit chan5, u7chou-chit Seng3-koa-tui7 lai5 teh hong7-su7 kau3-hoe7, che sit8-chai7 chin thang kiong-hi2! Chiau3 sou2 sin3, kau3-hoe7 kap im-gak8, chu7-jian5 si7 be7 thang saN pun-li7--tit e5 chit8 hang7. Sin8-giong2 seng-oah8 e5 thiat-ti2, keng3-ai3 Sion7-te3 e5 kek8-ti8, chu7-jian5 e5 seN-chhut chan3-bi2 e5 sim, sou2-i2 tioh8 eng7 im-gak8 lai2 piau2-hian7. Le2-pai3 e5 toa7 iau3-sou3 e5 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 gim5-si, chhiuN3-koa o-lo2.
San-tian5 Keng-chok (Yamada) Sian-siN kong2, “Im-gak8 theh8-lai5 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 e5 lai7-bin7, che si7 goa2 e5 liam7-goan7.” Cho3 sin2-tou5 e5 lang5 teh-beh kong2,” Im-gak8 theh8 jip8-lai5 chong-kau3 seng-oah8 e5 lai7-bin7, che si7 goan2 e5 liam7-goan7”Ho7-chhoan Hong-gan7 Sian-sin (Kagawa) kong3,” Hiong-chhoan e5 lang5 kiam2-chhai2 bo5 bi2 thang chiah8 teh chin kan-khou2, chong2-si7 chan3-bi2 Siong7-te3 e5 koa, un2 tang3 u7 tiau5 ti7 in e5 sim-lai7” Ah! Sit8-chai7 e5 sin3-tou5 bo5 lun7 tu2-tioh2 siaN2 keng2-gu7, bo5 sit-chi3, koh e5 choan-sim oa2-kho3 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3 lai5 tit peng5-an, chit8 hang7 chiu7-si7 tui3 I u7 chan3-bi2 Siong-te3 e5 koa, tiau5 ti7 I e5 sim-lai7, ia7 put-si5 u7 piau2-hian7 chan3-bi2 Siong7-te3 e5 in-toaN.

Kau3-hoe7 im-gak8 kap se3-siok8 im-gak8 u7 toa7 cheng-chha.Cheng5--chia2, si7 cho3 le2-pai3, chan3-bi2 e5 lou7-eng7, lai5 hiong3-siong7lan2 lang5 e5 chong-kau3 kam2-cheng5. Hou7--chia2, si7 kan-ta cho3 chit8 khoan2 ngo7-lok8, lai5 hou7 lang5 e5 sim chiam7-si5 hoaN-hi2 song2-khoai3 na7-tiaN7.

Lun7 kau3-hoe7 im-gak8 e5 khi2-chhou, chiu7-si7 Chu2-au7 oa2 600 ni5 e5 Lo5-ma2 Hoat-ong5 Gregorius te7 1 se3 e5 chhong3-siat; hit e5 iuN7-sek, chiu7-si7 Gregorian chant. Chu2-au7 oa2 1500 ni5 e5 si5-tai7, koh u7 Lo5-ma2 gak8-phai3 e5 chong-kau3 im-gak8 put-chi2 hoat tat8. Chit phai3 e5 tai7-piau2-chia2 kio3-cho3 Giovanni Palestrina (1526-1594). Chit e5 gak8-phai3, si7 kau3-hoe7 im-gak8 e5 hoat-tat8 su2-chiuN7, put-chi2 tiong7-iau3 e5 si5-tai7. Kin7-tai7 im-gak8 e5 khai-chou2 J.Sebastian Bach (1685-1750) e5 si5-tai7, chong-kau3 im-gak8 ia7 si7 toa7 chhiong-seng7. I it-seng sou2 hong7-su7 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 ti7 Tek-kok e5 Leipzig e5 Seng3 To-ma2 Kau3-hoe7, ti7-hia cho3 gak8-tiuN2. Kau3 taN hit e5 sou2-chai7 chheng-cho3 im-gak8 e5 beng5-sou2. Kau3 hian7-tai7, chong-kau3 im-gak8 iu5-goan5 toa7 hoat-tat8. Hian7-tai7 keng2-in-tek e5 chok-khek-chia2, chiu7-si7 Paul Hindemith (1895--). I2-siong7 sou2 ki3 3, 4 khoan2 e5 si5-tai7, kok u7 I e5 iuN7-sek; chong-kau3 im-gak8 chin chhiong-seng7, soa3 kau3 ti7 kin-a2-jit8.
Chiau3 sou2 sin3, chong-kau3 na7 ti7-teh, im-gak8 ia7 tek-khak ti7-teh. M7-na7 an2-ni, chiong-lai5 chin e5 im-gak8, chiu7-si7 tioh8 tui3 chong-kau3 im-gak8 lai5 chhut-hian7.Kou2-cha2 chhut-mia5 e5 im-gak8-ka choe7-choe7 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, ia7 chhut-mia5 e5 hap8-chhiuN3-knek8, long2 si7 koan-he7 chan3-bi2 Siong7-te3, o-lo2 Kiu3-se3-chu2.
Lou7-tek Ma2-teng kong2, “Im-gak8 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 siuN2-su3 chit8 hang7 chin sui2 chin ko-siong7 e5 ge7-sut8. Mou5-kui2 put-chi2 kiaN im-gak8, im-gak8 e7 an-ui3 lang5, hou7 lang5 e5 sim un-jiu5, hou7 I piN3-cho3 to7-tek-tek, li2-siong2-tek e5 lang5, hou7 m7-ho2 e5 su-siuN2 it-chin7 siau-bo5. Goa2 bo5 in ui7 chit hang7 ui2-tai7 e5 su7-kiaN7, lai5 pang3-sak goa2 sou2 u7 sio2-khoa2 e5 im-gak8. Hak8-hau7 e5 sian-siN na7 be7 hiau2 gim5-si, goa2 bo5 kio3 I cho3 sian-siN. Chheng-lian5 lang5 tioh8 oh8 im-gak8, in-ui7 e7 che3-cho7 sian7-liong5 koh u7 lian7-tat8 e5 su7 (士).” Chiau3 sou2 sin3, im-gak8 chun5-chai7 e5 li2-iu5, i2-kip8 hit e5 ke3-tat8, chiu7-si7 tioh8 piau2-hian7 su-siuN2 lai5 kam2-tong7 lang5. Kan-ta e7 gim5 chin ho2 thiaN, chin hiang2, an2-ni si7 bo5 kau3-giah8. Koa-su5 kap koa-phou2 tioh8 u7 oan5-choan5 e5 thong2-it, hiong5-ho5, put-kho2 hun-li7 e5 kiat-hap8 chiah e7 eng7-tit. Kin7-tai7 e5 seng-gak8-ka che7-che7 long2 khia7 ti7 siang-chhe-lou7. In iau2 m7-chai tioh8 chu3-tiong7 ti7 chit e5 so2-chai7. Kiam2-chhai2 si7 kan-ta gim5 ho2 thiaN, beh hou7 lang5 ai3-bou7 ia7 bo5 tek-khak. Na7-si7 chong-kau8 im-gak8 koh-khah be7 eng7-tit, in ui7 beh hou7 pat8 lang5 lai5 loan5-bou7 lan2 chiah lai5 gim5, choan-choan si7 in-ui7 chan3-bi2 Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3, sou2-i2 u7 piau2-hian7 I e5 chin-cheng5. Chiau3 sou2 siuN7, lang5 e5 sim na7 chheng-khi, chu7-jian5 e5 kam2-cheng5 si7 ko-siong7, sou2 piau2-hian7 e5 im-gak8 si7 sui2, si7 u7 lat8, si7 e7 kam2-tong7 lang5, ia7 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 kam3-lap8 hoaN-hi2 e5.

Chong2 kong2, koa-su5 si7 put-chi2 iau3-kin2, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chu2-lang5. Koa-phou2 si7 beh chan7 koa-su5 hou7 I e7 oah8-oah8, koh u7 lat8 thang ku2-tng5 in3-siong7 ti7 lang5 e5 thau5-nau2-lai7. Sou2-i2 koa-su5 si put-chi2 tiong7-iau3, beh hou7 I e7 chin beng5, u7 lat8 thang kam2-tong7 lang5, tioh8 put-si5 lian7-sip8, koh pun2-sin ia7 tioh8 u7 hit khoan2 e5 kam2-cheng5. Sou2-i2 na7 beh gim5 chhut-mia5 e5 chong-kau3-khek, be7 bian2-tit ai3 hoan2-seng2, chu7-ki2 m7-chai e7 kham-tit, a2-si7 u7 hit khoan2 leng5-lek8 e5 in3-siong7, chong-kau3 e5 kam2-cheng5 a2-bo5? Eng-kok kou2-kim tok8-pou7 e5 chong-kau3 Si-jin5 Alfred Tennyson e5 Si, put-chi2 u7 hoe7-oa7-tek, koh u7 im-gak8-tek e5 tek8-sek ti7-teh. Chit nng7 hang7 tiau5-kiaN7 si7 si-jin5 e5 li2-siong2. Sou2-i2 chhut-mia5 lang5 e5 Si, lang5 chin ai3 theh8-lai5 cho3 tiau7 lai5 gim5. Thang chai koa-su5 si7 chin iau3-kin2. Hoat-kok e5 chok-khiok-ka, chi2-hui-chia2, kiam tok8 chhiuN3-ka, Reynaldo Hahn (1874--), kek8 lek8 chu2-tiuN ti7 chit e5 sou2-chai7. Ki5 thaN I2-tai7-li7 e5 Tenor Enrico Caruso (1873-1921), i2-kip8 Eng-kok e5 Baritone koa-chhiu2 Sir Charles Santley, a2-si7 Tek-kok e5 toa7 seng-gak8-ka Lilli Lehmann hu-jin5 long2 u7 chu-tiuN, lun7 koa-su5 e5 hun-beng5 kap kam2-tong7-lat8 e5 tiong7-iau3-seng3.
I2-siong7 kan2-tan kong2 tam7-poh8, ng3-bang7 e7 cho3 tai7-ke e5 chham-kho2. Ki5-thaN u7 ki-hoe7 chiu7 e7 koh gi7-lun7.
