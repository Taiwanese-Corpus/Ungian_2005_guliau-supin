A-kong kap a-ma2 e5 kou3-su7_02
A-ian3

  Goan2 a-kong si7 1909 ni5 chhut-si3--e5, i keng-koe3 Chheng-kok cheng3-hu2, Jit8-pun2 cheng3-hu2 kap Tiong-hoa5 Bin5-kok cheng3-hu2 sann e5 bo5-kang7 cheng3-koan5 e5 thong2-ti7. (Choe3-kin7 ka3 Tai5-oan5 keng-che3 hoat-tian2-su2 e5 lau7-su kio3 goan2 ai3 ka7 kong2 “Jit8-ki3 si5-tai7” chit-e5 koan3-si3 kai2--koe-lai5, i ng3-bang7 goan2 seng5-jin7 jit8-pun2 hit-chun7 khak-sit8 u7 thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 hoat-lut8 kin-ku3 kap cheng3-tong-seng3, mai3 koh siu7 toa7 Tiong-kok su-siong2 chit-e5 penn7-tok8 se2-nau2) 
  Goa2 iau2 e7 ki3-tit i tiann7-tiann7 ka7 goa2 kong2, i se3-han3 e5 si5 u7 lau5-koe3 Chheng-kok e5 ti-boe2-liu (『辮子頭』), thau5-cheng5 poann3-peng5 e5 thau5-chang ai3 thi3-kng-kng, au7-piah poann3-peng5 ai3 pak8-cho3 thau5-chang-boe2 (『辮子』), thau5-chang-boe2 na7 senn-kau3 e7 cho7-chiann5 bo5 hong-pian7 e5 si5, tioh8-ai3 ka7 poann5 ti7 thau5-khak-teng2, chhiunn7 kau5-che5-thinn Sun Gou7-khong e5 kim-khou-a2 kang7-khoan2. I na2 kong2 koh e7 na2 khiu2 goa2 e5 nng7-sok thau5-chang-boe2, ti7 goa2 thau5-khak teng2 pi2 lai5 pi2 khi3. Goa2 siunn3 kau4, a-kong chiok ai3 iong7 “kau5” lai5 kap goa2 kong2-sng2-chhio3, koh e7 ka7 goa2 ka3 chiok che7 kap kau5 u7 koan-he7 e5 siok8-gi2, tiann7 kong2 kah goan2 chou2-sun-a2 nng7-e5 chhio3 kah chhia-pun3-tau2. 
  (TGB Thong-sin3 te7 45 ki5, 2003 ni5 6 goeh8)
