
 
Pai7-hoai7 Si5-jit8 e5 Thang5
Ko kim-seng Bok8-su
Thang5 u7 chin-che7 cheng2, tai7-khai3 lai5 kong2 u7 nng7-lui7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chui2-ke chiah8 tiu7-a2 thang5, tou7-kun2 e7 hou7 thou5 sang, chham5 e7 thou3 si, phang e7 pang3-bit8, chiah e5 ho7-cho3 u7 li7-ek e5 thang5. Chhau2-meh e7 hai7 sou2 cheng3 e5 gou7-kak, peh8-hia7 e7 chhia chha5, chian2-hi5 chiah8 chheh, bang2-a2 e7 teng3 lang5, chiah e5 ho7- cho3 hai7 thang5.  Lun7 si5-jit8 e5 tiong-kan kiam2 ia7 u7 hai7 thang5?  Se3-ji7 lai5 siuN7, m7- na7 chit8 cheng2; pai5-liat8 ti7 e7-bin7.     
1. Phoa3-piN7.  PiN7-thiaN3 m7-si7 lan2 sou2 ai3, chong2--si7 lang5 be7 bian2-tit e7 piN7.  Cha2-si5 Beng7-hek8 sui-jian5 beng2, Khong2-beng5 chhit pai2 pang3 i khi3, iu5-goan5 koh liah8 tioh8.  Khun-lun5 koan khiok kian-kou3, kiN-kou2 phah saN-e7 hit-si5, chiu7 hou7 Tek8- chheng chiam3 khi3.  Na7-si7 phoa3-piN chit8- e7 kau3, sui-si7 u7 ti3-sek e5 lang5, eng-hiong5 e5 chiong3, bo5 pou7 thang kim3 i boh8-tit lai5, kah i kin2-kin2 khi3; siuN7 kau3 peng5-siong5 e5 peh-siN3 koh khah si7 an2-ni.  U7-e5 thau3- jit8 to2 bin5-chhng5, chhun chit8-e5 khui3 neh-- leh neh--leh. U7-e5 na2-ku2 na2 san2, thong ni5 hiN-hiN chhan.  In sui-jian5 ai3 thak8- chheh, thak8-chheh be7-e7, sui-jian5 ai3 cho3 seng-li2, cho3 seng-li2 bo5 hoat-tit.  Khui3- lat8 tui3 an2-ni soe, chi3-khi3 tui3 an2-ni bo5. Kong-im khang koe3, sim2-mih8 hou7 i an2- ni?  Bo5 m7-si7 hou7 piN7 thang5 chhng2-khi3.     
2. Phah m7-kiN3 mih8, chhiau-chhoe7. ThiN-te7 u7 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 ui7, ban7-mih8 long2 u7 i-e5 chhu3-su7, ke-lai7 e5 mih8 ia7 tioh8 an2-ni. Siat-su2 mih8 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 ui7, loan7-loan7 choh8, chiu7 iau3-eng7 e5 si5, bo5 thang eng7. Kiam2-chhai2 i-chiuN5, kiam2-chhai2 keng- chheh, chhoe7 kui2-na7 tiam2-cheng, m7-a7 iau2-boe7 tioh8.  U7-si5 chit8-e5 bak8-kiaN3 kui3 ti7 heng-cheng5, thng3 lai5 theh8 ti7 chhiu2-nih8, chhut-khi3 chhit-tho5.  Kau3 to3-lai5 tu2-ho2 iu5-pian7 theh8 phoe lai5, chiu7 ai3 theh8 bak8- kiaN3 lai5 khoaN3 phoe; bong bo5, chiu7 chhiau  
seng-khu, sau3 thou5-kha, goa7-khau2 si3-ke3 chhoe7, tu2-tioh8 lang5 chiu7 mng7, tui3 hong5-hun e5 si5 kau3 10 tiam2-cheng, chhoe7 long2 bo5 tioh8.  Bou2 khau2-chau3 u7 ti-tiau5, ui7 bo5 kian-kou3, chit8-chiah ti soa3 chau2 chhut khi3, thong ke e5 lang5 long2 khi3 chhoe7, kau3 liah8 tioh8, i2-keng lek8-koe3 nng7 tiam2-cheng.  Chit-ke 8 lang5, kiong7 hui3 16 tiam2.  Koh mih8-kiaN7 hou7 chhat8 thau-theh8 khi3, tioh8 chhiau-chhoe7 e5 si5- kan, he m7-bian2 kong2.  Lun7-kau3 nau2- chhoe2 e5 lat8 soe-lam2, liam5-piN he7, liam5- piN be7 kiN3-tit, ui7-tioh8 an2-ni lai5 chhoe7 mih8, hoe3 si5 kai3-che7.     
3. Hong3-chhiong3 su-iok8.  Chin- chham2!  Su-iok8 teh hai7 lang5.  Su-iok8 si7 eng7 khoai3-lok8 ho2-khoaN3 lai5 jia-kham3, hou7 lang5 be7 ki3-tit au7-lai5 e5 chai-ho7.  Sou2-i2 gong7-lang5 siong5-siong5 khi3 hoa-ke liu2-hang7 lai5 chhit-tho5, phah-sng3 in kap Tong5 Beng5-hong5 teh iu5 goat8-tian7 bo5 koh-iuN7.  Bo5 loa7-ku2, phoa3-piN7 iN5-tiN5, chham2 kau3 be7 kong2 tit, beng5-chong7 soa3 piN3 chhin-chhiuN7 Thek-koai2 sian.  Chiah8 ioh8 chiu7 na7 u7 eng3-hau7, ia7 tioh8 chin che7 ni5.  Siau3-lian5 che2-te7 jip8 a-phian3-koan2, teh siuN7 chiah8 tam7-poh8 bo5 siong-kan. M7-ku2 lou7-boe2 tiau5 a-phian3, m7-ai3 chiah8 be7 soah-tit.  Tui3 an2-ni me5 soa3-chiap jit8, to2 bin5-chhng5 theng5-hun5 thou3-bu7, u7-si5 nng7 boe2-leng5 chhiuN2 chu.  Phah-sng2 jit8-chi2 chin kho2-sioh!  Iu7-koh peh-siN3 put-bok8 ai3 sio-thai5, pang-kok sit-ho5 khi2 kau-chian3, cheng-chian3 ku2-ku2, si5-jit8 tui3 an2-ni hiat-kak8, koh khah kho2-sioh!     
4. Ai3 sio-ko3.  KoaN-hu2 pun2-si7 ui7-tioh8 ti7-li2 peh-siN3 chiah u7 siat, peh-siN3 siu7 un- khut chiu7 khi3 hun-sou3, che-si7 chu7-jian5 e5 li2.  Chong2--si7 ai3 sio-ko3 e5 lang5, i khoaN3 sio-ko3 cho3 chhit-tho5; bo5-liong7 e5 lang5, phah-sng3 kiN3 koaN si7 hap8-li2, tui3 an2-ni ge5- mng5 e5 an3-kiaN7 teng5-teng5 thah8-thah8. Sio-ko3 e5 jit8 kau3 nng7-peng5 tui3 sin3, hek8- si7 chit tng5 ou peh8 oh hun-beng5, chiu7 ng3- bang7 te7-ji7 tng5, hek8-si7 kin-a2-jit8 iau2-boe7 phoaN3-koat, chiu7 bang7 au7-jit8.  Chong2--si7 tai7-chi3 u7 tong5-tut8, siong5-siong5 u7 ian5-ki5, kiam2-chhai2 3 ni5 5 ni5 an3 iau2-boe7 kiat. Se3-ji7 seng2-chhat in sou2 saN-chiN e5 chai5- san2, sit8-chai7 be7 khah kui3-khi3 sou2 lek8-koe3 e5 jit8-chi2.  Si5 na7 khi3, kiam2 e7 koh lai5?     
5. Cho3 kang be7-hiau2 siat tu2-ho2. Biau5-lek u7 lang5 chhiaN3 chit8-e5 chu2-chiah8- e5, chit8-pai2 kah i theh8 5 chiam chiN5 tah tau7-iu5 kap be2 chhang-thau5.  Hit lang5 to3-lai5, thau5-ke kah i tioh8 khi3 be2 bah.  Be2-liau2 chiu7 chhe i koh khi3 be2 tau7-koaN. Koh u7  
lang5 ti7-chia beh Chheh-pang5 heng5 siau3, Tai7-jin5-bio7 siu chhu3-soe3, I- koan2 tho2-ioh8.  I bo5 sng3 hit-e5 tiam2-cheng kap lou7-cham7 e5 chhu3-su7, chiah khi3 pan7 tai7-chi3.  Loan7-loan7 cho3, Chheh-pang5 heng5-siau3, Tai7-jin5-bio7 siu chhu3-soe3, I- koan2 tho2 ioh8.  Teng2-bin7 hit nng7 lang5 in lai5 ong2 e5 si5, kan-ta eng7 10 hun-cheng lai5 sng3, li2 khoaN3 liau2-si5 loa7-che7.     
A2-si7 u7 lang5 teh chu2-tau3, it-bi7 kou3 chu2 png7, be7 hiau2-tit than3 png7 iau2-be7 sek8 e5 si5, seng chhiat-chhai3 khi3 chu2, jian5-au7 png7 sek8 chhai3 ia7 sek8.  Kang be7 seng2, si7 koh kin2, chiu7 pai7-hoai7 si5-jit8 e5 thang5 hai7 lan2 kiam2 e7 tioh8? 
6. Cho3 kang bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 sim.  Bat khoaN3-tioh8 oh8-a2 e5 hak8-seng teh thak8- chheh, tiap-a2-ku2-a2 so kha pe5 chhiu2, tiap- a2-ku2-a2 iuN2 chui2 koan3 tou7-kau5, tiap-a2-ku2- a2 hiat kiu5 tau3 su-iaN5, tiap-a2-ku2-a2 chhiu7- kha khioh koe2-chi2, ian5-chhian5 chin ku2, chit8 pun2 chheh thak8 chiah e7 sek8.  Koh u7 lang5 bat oh8 kok-gu2, poaN3-lou7 soah-khi3, to3-khi3 choh-sit.  Kau3 au7--lai5 kap Jit8- pun2 lang5 kau-poe5, chhui3 khui thih8-thuh8 hau2, chiah hoan2-hoe2 beh koh oh8 kok- gu2.  Chong2--si7 cheng5 sou2 oh8 e5, taN be7 ki3- tit, put-tek-i2 tioh8 tui3 chhou-hak8 phah- khi2.  Iu7-koh lan2 na7 cho3 kang bo5 tiaN7- tioh8 e5 sim, chu7-jian5 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 kang, kui jit8 sou2-cho3 bo5 chhu3 bo5 su7, put-tap put-chhit8, liam5-piN thak8 Ku7-iok, liam5- piN oh8 si-tiau7, liam5-piN piaN3 lai7-bin7, liam5- piN io gin2-na2.  Kau3 e5-hng siong5-se3 siuN7 kin-a2-jit8 e5 sou2-cho3, na2 chun2 bo5 mih8. Chit8 jit8 e5 si5-chun7 kui to2-ui7, kiam2 m7-si7 ti7 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 sim e5 thang5 pak-tou2-lai7 mah?  Jit8-jit8 na7 u7 tiaN7-tioh8 kang, put- ti3 kau3 an2-ni.     
7. Bo5 hap8 si5-chun7 e5 chhit-tho5.  Chhit-tho5 e5 su7 khiok u7 li7-ek lang5, na7 bo5 chiau3- si5 lai5 cho3 u7 ham7-hai7.  Phah-kiu5 e5 lang5 ti7 kiu5-pou chau2-lai5 chau2-khi3, seng-khu teh un7-tong7, hiat-khi3 e7 liu5-thong, sin-the2 e7 iong2-chong3.  Siat-su2 kui jit8 long2 an2-ni, hit-e5 si5-jit8 chiu7 kui ti7 khang.  Tioh-ki5 si7 chhu3-bi7 e5 chhit-tho5, e7 khui lang5 e5 ti3-sek, ke-thiN lang5 e5 leng5-khiau2.  Jit8-si5 na7 tioh m7 soah, sim7-chi3 thong-me5 ia7 an2-ni, tek-khak tioh8 chit8-pai2 toa7-khun3; si5 jit8 tek-khak khi3, m7 theng3-hau7 li2 khun3 chheN2.  Cha2-jit8 goa2 khi2-thau5 oh8 tio3 hi5, liah8 tioh8 chit8-boe2 seng-khu bo5 saN  
chhun3 tng5, sim chin hoaN-hi2, soa3 be7 ki3- tit goa2 si7 ti7 chui2 piN.  He!  tio3-hi3 kai3 sim-sek.  Chong2--si7 na7 hou7 i be5-khi3, long2 bo5 an7-tiaN7 si5-kan, chiu7 hun7-lai7 e5 tai7-chi3, e7 ti3 chi tou7-goe7.  An2-ni, si7 chiong u7 lou7- eng7 e5 jit8-chi2, he7 ti7 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 sou2-chai7. HaiN7!  si5 i2-keng khi3, tui be7 tioh8.     
8. Pin5-toaN7.  Pin5-toaN7 e5 lang5 bo5 chi3- khi3, in-khin5 si7 sim2-mih8, si5-jit8 si7 po2-poe3, i-e5 sim be7 thong-tat8.  Sou2-i2 jit8-thau5 na7 loh8-soaN, i bo5 thou2-khui3, cha2-khi2-si5 jit8 chhut, i bo5 hoaN-hi2.  Ke-lai7 e5 lang5 kong2, Tang-peng5 kng lah, li2 tioh8 peh-khi2--lai5. I kong2, Ho2; na7-si7 iu5-goan5 teh ti7 bin5- chhng5--nih8 hoan-lai5 hoan-khi3, chhin- chhiuN7 mng5 ti7 mng5-khu7.  Lam2 chhiu2 koh khun3, siuN7 ai3 sio2-bin5 chit8-chheN2, khah theng5 chiah khi2-lai5.  Peng5-sou3 ai3 eng5, si3-ke3 so5 ti7 chhu3-piN thau5-boe2, lai5 kong2 sou2 m7 eng-kai kong2-e5.  Tng i teh chhut-khi3 cho3 kang, ban7-ban7 chhin-chhiuN7 tioh gu5- chhia; kau3 cho3 beng5-pek8, to3-tng2-lai5 chhin-chhiuN7 chheng3-ki-chiN3.  Chiu7 u7-si5 cho3 kang chin kin2, ia7 si7 lo2-chho2.  Koh tai7-chi3 na7 kau3 chhiu2, siong5-siong5 ka-ti7 an7-kong2, Jit8 iau2-cha2, khoai3-khoai3 sio2- theng5 chiah cho3.  U7-si5 kong2, bin5-na2-jit8 chiah cho3, be7 ban7.  Chio i cho3 ho2, chhut- chhui3 chiu7 the, kong2, Goa2 cha7-jit8 kha nau2-tioh8 be7 kiaN5, kiam2-chhai2 kong2, Thau5-thiaN3 m7-kaN2 chhoe-tioh8 hong, chhiaN2 ban7 kui2-jit8 chiah lai5 khi3.  Tui3 an2-ni, ti3-kau3 pah-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 m7-ho2, sou2 cho3 tam7-poh8, hoe3-si5 kai3-che7, chin thang oan3-hin7.     
Jit8-pun2 tu2-a2 lai5 Tai5-oan5 hit-tiap8, Sai- le5 te7-hng, u7 lang5 khioh tioh8 chit8-kng2 toa7-bong2 kng2 e5 choa2, chai si7 Jit8-pun2 lang5 e5 mih8, chong2 m7-chai si7 sim2-mih8 mih8, chiu7 khi3 tai5 ti7 thou5-nih8.  In-ui7 kiaN hou7 Jit8-pun2 lang5 chha5 chai, soa3 hou7 i thai5.  Au7--lai5 peng5-iu2 tui3 hia koe3, u7 chiong chit-e5 tai7-chi3 lai5 kap i tam7-lun7, chiah chai si7 kim-phio3.  Kut8 chhut-lai5 khoaN3 i2-keng noa7 liau2-liau2, chiu7 thou2- khui3 bo5 soah.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, It-chhun3 kong-im, it chhun3 kim.  Hoe3-si5 hoe3 jit8 e5 lang5 ia7 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni, chit-tiap8 m7-chai thang thiaN3-sioh si5-jit8, au7--lai5 hoan2-hoe2 si7 khah ti5.     
 
TaN jit8-chi2 m7-si7 lan2-e5, si7 Chu2 sou2 siuN2- su3; lan2 pang3-sak m7 eng7, si7 khi3-sak I-e5 in, seng5-siu7 lai5 m7 eng7, si7 kou-hu7 I-e5 in, eng7 bo5 chiau3 hap8-gi5, si7 ui5-keh8 I-e5 chi2-i3. Sou2-i2 tu5-khi3 thian-jian5 piN7 (m7-si7 chu7 chhu2 e5), ngou2-gu7-jian5 e5 tai7-chi3, i2-kip8 tui3 m7- chai-iaN2 lai5 phah-sng2 jit8-chi2 e5 i2-goa7, na7 bo5 po2-sioh kong-im, kiaN-liau2 u7 choe7. Chit se3-kan, bo5 chit8-lang5 bo5 hou7 pai7-hoai7 si5-jit8 e5 thang5 sou2 ui5-khun3, chiu7-si7 ai3 tu5- khi3, tu5 ia7 be7 liau2.  Chiau3 goa2 e5 gu5-kian3, lan2 tioh8 lai5 oh8 choh-sit lang5 teh chau5- biat8 hai7-thang5 e5 hoat-tou7.  Sou2 cheng3 e5 gou7-kak tioh8 thang5, ai3 tu5-khi3 be7 e7, chiu7 che7, kap hai7-thang5 kau-chian3, chiu7 hai7- thang5 ka-ti7 biat8 bo5.  Beh tu5 pai7-hoai7 si5-jit8 e5 thang5, hit-e5 hoat-tou7 tioh8 an3- choaN2?     
1. Tioh8 tiau-iong2 seng-khu.  Seng3-keng kong2, Tiau-iong2 seng-khu, hit-e5 li7-ek sio2-khoa2, keng3-khian5 hok8-sai7 Chu2, hit-e5 li7-ek toa7.  Sui-jian5 an2-ni, sin-the2 m7 chiau3-kou3, lai5 kong2 siN3-mia7 thiN chu3- tiaN7, ti3-kau3 soe-lam2, beh thai3 e7 cho3 kang. Thoa-koe3 ni5 goeh8, kiam2 u7 hap8 Siong7-te3 e5 i3-su3?  Po2-lo5 ka7 The5-mou5-thai3 kong2, Boh8-tit kan-ta lim-chui2, thang chiah8 tam7-poh8 chiu2, in-ui7 li2 e5 pi5 lam2.  Chiau3- kou3 seng-khu si7 tioh8, chiah be7 hoe3 liau2 kong-im.     
2. Tui3-tiong7 lan2 e5 kang, Tng Giau5- te3 e5 si5, toa7-chui2 hoan3-lam7 thiN-e7.  Giau5 chhe Kun2 khi3 ti7 chui2, bo5 chhai2-kang. Au7--lai5 chhe i-e5 kiaN2 mia5 U2, i chhoa7 bou2 bo5 kui2-jit8, chiu7 khi3 ti7-li2 chui2, hou7 i thang kin2 siau.  In hu7-jin5-lang5 siN-kiaN2, i 3 pai2 tui3 mng5-khau2 koe3, thiaN-kiN3 i-e5 kiaN2 teh hau2, ou ou-aN5 ou-aN5, long2 bo5 jip8-khi3 khoaN3, ia7 bo5 jip8-khi3 hioh-khun3 chiah8 tiam2-sim. Chu-chu liah8 ti7-li2 chui2 kiu3 peh-siN3 cho3 iau3-kin2.  Che si7 tui3-tiong7 i-e5 kang.  Sou2-i2 hoan7-na7 cho3 soan-kau3-su e5 lang5, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 U2-ong5 tui3-tiong7 i-e5 kang, chiu7 sou2-kiaN5 tek-khak oh8 Su3-tou5.  Goan2 choan-bu7 ki5-to2 thoan5 to7-li2.     
3. Oh8 Ia5-sou.  TaN Ki-tok toa3 se3-kan e5 si5, thiN phah phu2-kng jip8 tian7 ka3-si7 lang5, jit8 tiong-tau3 chiN2-piN thoan5 to7-li2, hong5-hun si5, soaN-teng2 ka3 hak8-seng, ian5-siah8 e5 tiong-kan, siat phi3-ju7 kio3- chheN2 cheng3-lang5, hioh-khun3 e5 si5 eng7  
che7-che7 hang7 ka3-si7 peh-siN3.  Chu2 e5 hoe3- siu7 bo5 kiong5-chin7, siong7-chhiaN2 tui3-tiong7 kong-im, ho5-hong3 lan2 chit se3-kan e5 peh- siN3? Jit8 goeh8 phu5 ti7 thiN--nih8,lan2 be7-e7 hou7 jit8 bo5 kiaN5 ti7 Ki-pian3 e5 te7,goeh8 chiam7-theng5 ti7 A-iaN5-lun5. He, jit8 goeh8 teh khi3 , ni5 m7 hou7 lan2 lau5, lan2 tioh8 chhut- lat8 lai5 tu5-khi3 chiah-e5 pai7-hoai7 si5-jit8 e5 thang5, oh8 lan2 e5 Chu2, chit8 chhun3 kong- im ia7 po2-sioh, cho3 sou2 eng-kai tioh8 cho3- e5, chiu7 lan2 e5 kang na2 che7, chiah bian2 kou-hu7 Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 lan2 e5 ni5 goeh8, ia7 bo5 kian3-siau3 lan2 oah8 ti7 se3-kan.
