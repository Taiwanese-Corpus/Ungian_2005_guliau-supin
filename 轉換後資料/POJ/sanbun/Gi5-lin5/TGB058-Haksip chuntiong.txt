Hak8-sip8 Chun-tiong7
Gi5-lin5

Ti7 5 kang e5 iaN5-tui7 lai7-te2, goa2 hoat-kak chu7 2 tang cheng5 chham-ka TGB kau3 taN e5 kai2-pian3, goa2 kam2-kak ka-ti7 "care" e5 mih8-kiaN7 bo5 kang7-khoan2--a.

Chham-ka TGB i2-lai5, goa2 kam2-kak siong7 tiong7-iau3--e5, si7 hak8-sip8 an2-choaN2 chun-tiong7. Chin3-cheng5 goa2 e5 jin7-ti7 si7: u7 le2-mau7, eng3-kai to7-si7 chun-tiong. M7-koh goa2 tauh8-tauh8-a2 the2-hoe7--tioh8, le2-mau7 kan-taN si7 chun-tiong7 e5 1 pou7-hun7, sim7-chi3 e7-tang3 kong2 si7 chun-tiong7 e5 1 chiong2 heng5-sek nia5. Na7 bo5 hoat-tou7 eng7 seng5-khun2 tan-sun5 e5 sim-thai3 kap lang5 hou7-tong7, sou2-ui7 e5 chun-tiong7 si7 khang--e5.

Hoat-lut8-he7 e5 kau3-siu7 tiaN7-tiaN7 ka7 goan2 kong2 "jin5-seng3 chun-giam5": lang5 eng3-kai si7 chu2-the2, m7-si7 kheh-the2. Chit 5 kang ti7 Sai-le5, goa2 chiah chiaN3-kang2 the2-hoe7 tioh8 chit ku3 oe7 e5 to7-li2.

Ham7 Pi-thau5-poe3 e5 chai7-te7-lang5 hou7-tong7 e5 koe3-teng5, goa2 thiaN-tioh8 ti7 chit e5 sou2-chai7 seng-oah8 e5 lang5, tui3 sia7-khu eng5-cho7 u7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 khoaN3-hoat, ma7 thiaN-tioh8 choh-sit e5 ou-ji2-sang2 tui3 Khoan5-po2-kiok8 kim3 sio tiu7-chhau2 e5 hoan2-toaN5 sim-siaN.

In si7 sia7-khu e5 seng5-oan5, si7 chin-chiaN3 leh choh-sit e5 lang5, si7 an2-choaN2 ti7 chiaN5 che7 long5-giap8 cheng3-chhek kap kang-giap8 cheng3-chhek lai7-te2, u7 hoat-tou7 hoat-siaN--e5 m7-si7 chiah-e5 ki-chan5 long5-bin5 kap kang-lang5? Nah e7 tian-to3 si7 hiah-e5 che7 ti7 pan7-kong-sek--e5 teh chu2-to7 it-chhe3? Lan2 e5 cheng3-hu2 kam2 u7 ka7 chiah-e5 long5-bin5 tong3-cho3 "lang5" teh chun-tiong7? Si7 an2-choaN2 kan-taN ka7 chiah-e5 long5-bin5 tong3-cho3 seng-san2 e5 kang-ku7?

Si7 lang5 teh kian3-lip8 the2-che3, ma7-si7 lang5 teh phah-phoa3 the2-che3; si7 lang5 teh khi2-cho7 khoan5-keng2, ma7-si7 lang5 teh pho3-hai7 khoan5-keng2. Sia7-hoe7 koan-he7 e5 ki-pun2 tan-ui7 to7-si7 "lang5", lan2 na7 bo5 hoat-tou7 kap bo5 kang7 sou2-chai7, bo5 kang7 chok8-kun5, bo5 kang7 kai-kip e5 lang5 peng5-teng2 tui3-oe7, lan2 to7 bo5 hoat-tou7 li2-kai2 ki5-thaN lang5 e5 siuN7-hoat.

ChhiuN7 lan2 chit khoan2 thak8-chheh-lang5, tiaN7-tiaN7 long2 kioh-si7 kho3-pun2 thak8--e5, lau7-su ka3--e5 to7-si7 it-chhe3, eng7 chin kiau-ngo7 e5 thai3-tou7 kap ke3-tat8-koan lai5 teh khoaN3 chit e5 sia7-hoe7, oan5-choan5 m7 chai-iaN2 sia7-hoe7 e5 khun3-keng2 ti7 toh.

Goa2 chin3-cheng5 tiaN7-tiaN7 e7 mng7: beh an2-choaN2 cho3, chiah e7-tang3 kai3-pian3 chit e5 sia7-hoe7?

Goa2 siuN7, li2-kai2--lang5, chun-tiong7--lang5, to7 si7 un7-tong7 e5 khai-si2.
