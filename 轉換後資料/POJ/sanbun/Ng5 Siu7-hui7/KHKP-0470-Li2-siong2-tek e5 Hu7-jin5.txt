Li2-siong2-tek e5 Hu7-jin5
Ng5 Siu7-hui7 
Ti7 Chu2-cheng5 pah saN, si3 ni5 e5 si5, Lo5 ma2-kok put-chi2 heng, m7-ku2 hit-si5 e5 sia7- hoe7 si7 siang7 hu2-pai7, u7 hun nng7 kai-kip, chiu7-si7 Kui3-chok8 kap Su3-bin5.  Hiah e5 kui3-chok8 in ho7-mia5 kio3 Hoat8-chok8 (Oligarchy).  Chiah e5 iu2-li7 e5 su7-giap8 a2-si7 Goan5-lo2-iN7 e5 gi7-oan5 kap ko-teng2-koaN long2 si7 Hoat8-chok8 e5 lang5 chiam3-teh. Tui3 an2-ni na2-ku2 siong7-ha7 pin5-hu3 na2 hun na2-li7, soa3 piN3-cho3 nng7 e5 kai-kip, tiong- teng2 kai-kip soa3 bo5--khi3.    
 Ti7 hit-si5 hiah e5 Hoat8-chok8 chin pu3-u7, hiah e5 kui3 hu-jin5 soa3 u7 hit-ho7 hong-siok8, chiu7-si7 siong5-siong5 tai7-ke cho3-hoe2 teh tian2 sou2-u7 chng-thaN7 e5 chong-sek-phin2; khoaN3 siaN2-lang5 u7 kim-chhiu2-chi2, siaN2- lang5 u7 siuN kim-kong-chioh8 e5 hi7-kau, siaN2- lang5 u7 siang7 tat8-chiN5 koh sui2 e5 mih8, tai7- ke khoaN3-lai5 khoaN3-khi3; an2-ni cho3 khoai3- lok8, cho3 kau-che3 chit8 hang7 e5 ki-koan.   
U7 chit8 pai2 u7 chit8 e5 kui3 hu7-jin5 mia5  Ko-ni5-li7-a (Cornelia). U7 chit8-jit8 i peng5-             si5 sou2 teh kau-poe5 e5 kui3 hu7-jin5 long2 chhoa7 lai5 in tau, iu5-goan5 koh teh tam7-lun7 tak8-lang5 sou2-u7 e5 chong-sek-phin2.    Ti7 hit-si5 hiah e5 kui3 hu7-jin5 chiu7 tui3 Ko-ni5-li7-a kong2, “TaN, li2 sou2-u7 e5 po2-poe3 si7 sim2-mih8? ChhiaN2 theh8 hou7 goan2 khoaN3.” Na7-si7 i chai3-saN ka7 in the-si5, kong2, “Goa2  bo5 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 liat8-ui7 sou2-u7 hiah-nih8 sui2 e5 po2-poe3 thang hou7 lin2 khoaN3.”   Chong2-si7 hiah e5 kui3 hu7-jin5 pek i.  Ko-ni5-li7-a chiu7 kong2, “Na7-si7 an2-ni, ho2!  Chong2-si7 goa2 e5 po2-poe3 kap lin2 khah bo5  sio-siang7.  ChhiaN2 theng3-hau7, goa2 beh lai5 khi3 theh8 hou7 lin2 khoaN3.”  Hiah e5 kui3  hu7-jin5 tai7-ke chi-bu2 chi7-chu7, kong2, “TaN, chit e5 Ko-ni5-li7-a m7-chai u7 siaN2po2-poe3 thang hou7 lan2 khoaN3.”  Tai7-ke teh tan2-hau7.  Tu2 teh tan2-hau7 e5 si5, hut-jian5 mng5 khui, chiu7 khoaN3-kiN3 i chhiu2  khan nng7 e5 sim-sek kho2-ai3 e5 ta-pou-gin2-             na2 cho3-hoe2 jip8—lai5, hou7 in khoaN3, kong2, “Che chiu7-si7 goa2 e5 po2-poe3.”                 
Chit e5 Ko-ni5-li7-a chit nng7 e5 kiaN2, toa7--e5 mia5 Tiberius Gracchus; se3-han3--e5             mia5 Caius Gracchus.  Toa7-kiaN2 ti7 Chu2-cheng5 133 ni5 u7 cho3 Lo5-ma2 e5 Hou7-bin5-koaN (Tribune of the People), chhut-lat8 kai2-liong5 sia7-hoe7-chap8 ni5 ku2, put-chi2 siu7 hiah e5 kui3-chok8 oan3, soa3 hou7 lang5 hai7-si2.  Kau3 123 ni5, sio2-ti koh chiap in hiaN e5 siu7 hoan2-tui3-tong2 giok8-sat lai5 si2.                  
Chit e5 hu7-jin5 e5 tiong7-hu cha2 si2, chin- che7 lang5 khng3 i tioh8 kai2-ke3, i toan3-toan3             m7-kheng2.  Ti7 hit-si5 u7 chit8 e5 ong5 beh tiau3 i cho3 ong5-hou7, i ia7 toan3-jian5 sia7-choat8, khoaN3 bo5 se3-kan e5 khoai3-lok8 hu3-kui3, kam-goan7 hian3 choan5-sin, choan5-lek8 lai5 kau3-iok8 i nng7 e5 kiaN2 kau3 seng5-kong cho3 kok-ka e5 toa7 tong3-liong5.   
TaN chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 sit8-chai7 chin  thang cho3 lan2 cheng3 sin3-tou5 e5 bou5-iuN7:--- 
1.I liah8 kiaN2 cho3 po2-poe3, eng7 choan5-sim lai5 iong2-iok8 kap kau3-iok8. 
2.Pang3-sak se3-kan e5 beng5-li7, li7-khui  iu2-hek8, lai5 siu2 cheng-chiat. 
 Che chin-chiaN3 si7 Li2-siong2-tek e5 hian5  hu7-jin5 lah! Hoan7 cho3 sin3-tou5 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 ah!  Lan2 ia7 tioh8 liah8 lan2 e5 kiaN2 cho3  po2-poe3, put-si5 tioh8 chu3-i3 koan-sim, eng7 tu2-ho2 e5 kau3-hoat lai5 ka3, eng7 to7-li2 lai5 in2-chhoa7 ka3-si7 i, chiu7 iu5-goan5 beh seng5-kong chiaN5-cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 tong3-liong5, bian2-tit lan2 e5 kiaN2 u7 li7-khui Siong7-te3, ham7-loh8 te7-gek8 e5 iu-bun7.      
Iu7-koh lan2 cho3 sin3-tou5 e5 ia7 thang oh8, boh8-tit koe3-thau5 tui3-tiong7 se3-kan e5  beng5-li7, e7-hiau2 li7-khui Mou5-kui2 e5 be5-hek8, lai5 siu2 Chu2 e5 ka3-si7, chiu7 lan2 chiah be7 kou-hu7 Chu2 e5 thiaN3, chiu7 thang tit-tioh8 I e5 siuN2.
