1999 Cheng3-tai7 Tai5-gi2 ian2-kang2 pi2-sai3
A-bi5

12-goeh8 22 si7 tang-cheh, ma7 si7 1999-ni5 Cheng3-Tai7-poe Tai5-gi2 ian2-kang2 pi2-sai3 e5 koat-sai3. Che si7 Cheng3-Tai7 hau7-hng5 lai7-te2 thau5 1-pai2 ki2-pan7 Tai5-gi2 ian2-kang2 pi2-sai3, A-bi5 si7 chu2-pan7-jin5.

Si7 an2-choaN2 A-bi5 e7 siuN7 beh pan7 chit-e5 pi2-sai3--le? Goa2 chu7 se3-han3 to7 tiaN7-tiaN7 chham-ka ian2-kang2 pi2-sai3, m7-koh, long2 m7-bat chham-ka koe3 Tai5-gi2--e5, chiok siuN7 beh iong7 Tai5-gi2 ian2-kang2 khoaN3 mai3--le, m7-chiah e7 ti7 Cheng3-tai7 Tai5-bun5-sia7 theh8-gi7 khoaN3 kam2 beh pan7.

Tu2-a2-ho2, A-jin5 lau7-su 10--goeh8 lai5 sia7-thoan5 ka3 goan2 1-ko3-goeh8 Tai5-gi2 kap Tai5-bun5, tak8-e5 long2 chan3-seng5 ki2-pan7 Tai5-gi2 ian2-kang2 pi2-sai3, A-jin5 lau7-su ma7 chiok A-sah-li kong2 beh tau3 bou7-khoan2.

To7 an2-ne, A-bi5 khai-si2 pan7 chit-pai2 thau5-siong2 5000-khou e5 pi2-sai3.

Pan7--loh8-khi3 chiah siuN7-tioh8 kong2, chu2-pan7-chia2 bo5 eng-kai chham-ka, u5......goa2 e5 5000-khou poe--khi3-a, goa2 koh be7-tang3 iong7 Tai5-gi2 lai5 ian2-kang2 khoaN3 mai3--le, aih! Goa2 eng-kai kio3 pat8-lang5 pan7 chiah tioh8--e5.

Sui-bong2 A-bi5 be7-tang3 chham-ka chiok phah-sng2--e5, m7-koh, chit-pian3 ki2-pan7 e5 koe3-teng5, ma7 oh8-tioh8 chiok che7 mih-kiaN7, khoaN3-tioh8 koat-sai3 hiah cheng-chhai2, ma7 chiok kam2-tong7--e5.

Koat-sai3 si7 hian7-tioN5 thiu te5-bok8 chun2-pi7--e5-ou, koh ai3 khia7 ti7 tai5-a2-teng2 kong2 7-hun-cheng--ou, chin chiaN3 bo5 kan2-tan--ne. Kiat-ko2, tak8-e5 long2 kong2 gah chiok sun7, ma7 chiok u7 aN7--e5, hou7 A-bi5 kam2-tong7 gah lau5-phiN7-chui2.

Goan2 an3-sng3 me5-ni5 beh cheng-ka chiong2-kim, ma7 beh khong3-toa7 chham-ka e5 hoan7-ui5, hou7 Cheng3-tai7 i2-goa7 e5 lang5, ma7 e7-tang3 chham-ka.

Tan2 tak8-e5 na7 thak8 tai7-hak8, lin2 it-teng7 ai3 lai5 chhioN2 chiN5, chit-ma2 khai-si2 chun2-pi7, to7 u7 ki-hoe7 tioh8-teng2--ou, iu5-ki5 si7 me5-ni5 A-bi5 pit8-giap8 liau2-au7, lin2 to7 kiam2 1-e5 chiok li7-hai7 e5 tui3-chhiu2--a.
