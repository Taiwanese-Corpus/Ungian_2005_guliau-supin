
Hau3-heng7
Toh Khia-jit8  
Chap8-geh8 e5 Po3 Thiam-ong7 Sian-siN lun7 Hau3-heng7 chin siong5-se3, chin ho2, chin iau3-kin2.  TaN goa2 m7-si7 beh sia2 lai5 pou2, iau2 u7 tui3-tiong7--e5 ai3 koh ki3 ti7 po3-choa2 siau3-liam7 bo5-soah. 
Cheng-chu2 u7 kong2, “Pah hang7 e5 ho2, te7-it si7 tioh8 u7-hau3.”  Si-keng kong2, “Lau7-pe7 siN-goa2, lau7-bu2 iuN2-chhi7 goa2. Ai ah ! siN goa2 hiah kan-khou2 tioh8-boa5; ai3 po3 i chhim-chhim e5 un, ia7 boe7-oe7.” Koh kong2,  “Boe7-oe7 thiaN3 i e5 pe7-bu2, lai5 thiaN3 pat8-lang5, si7 hoan2-poe7 e5 tek-heng7, boe7-oe7 keng3 i e5 pe7-bu2, lai5 keng3 pat8-lang5 si7 hoan2-poe7 e5 le2-sou3.”  
 Seng3-keng u7 kong2, “Biau2-si7 pe7-bu2 e5 lang5 tioh8 siu7 chiu3-chou2.”  (Sin-beng7-ki3, 27 : 16.)  “Pe7-bu2 siN li2, sui-jian5 kau3-lau7 eng-kai thiaN3 i, m7-thang pang3-sak.” (Chim-gian5 23 : 22)  “Me7 pe7-bu2 e5 lang5, i e5 teng tek-khek hoa; i e5 kng tek-khak am3.”  (Chim-gian5 20 : 20)  “Choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5, lin2 tiam3 ti7 Chu2, tioh8 sun7-than3 lin2 e5 pe7-bu2, si7 eng-kai e5.”  (I2-hut8-sou2 6 : 1-3.) “Siong7-te3 kong2, Tioh8 keng3 lin2 e5 lau7-pe7 lin2 e5 lau7-bu2.”  (Ma2-thai3  15 : 4). 
Seng3-keng kap kou2-cha2 e5 gau5-lang5 sou2 ka3-si7 lun7 iu2-hau7 e5 su7, iau2 u7 chin-choe7 kong2 boe7 chin7.  Chiau3 an2-ni lai5 siuN7 lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 te7-it iau3-kin2 si7 pai3 Siong7-te3, keng3 pe7-bu2.  Goa2 siuN7 “Hau3” boe7-oe7 chin7.  Sui-si7 an2-ni, siok8-gu2 kong2, “Pe7-bu2 thiaN3 kiaN2 tng5-lau5-chui2; kiaN2 thiaN3 pe7-bu2 si7 chhiu7-be2 io5-hong.” 
Lan2 Pak-pou7 Kau3-hoe7 iau2 nng2-chiaN2, toa7 oa2-kho3 Ka-na2-tai7 Kau3-hoe7 e5 khan-seng5; ka-ki7 chu7-ti7 e5 khui3-lat8 bo5 kau3-giah8, sou2-i2 thoan5-kau3 e5 khi3 khah hng7 ia7 si7 ti7 Tai5-oan5.  Na7-si7 Thoan5-to7-kiok8 u7-si5 ia7 phai3 pun2 sou2-chai7 e5 lang5 hou7 i to3-khi3 pun2-ke e5 pai3-tng5 lai5 li7-ek kau3-hoe7 soa3 thang hou7 in e5 pe7-bu2 khah an-sim.  Che ia7-si7 hian2-beng5 ai3 hou7 lang5 u7 ki-hoe7 thang chin7 choe3 kiaN2 e5 pun2-hun7, eng5-kng Thian-kok sin3 Chu2 e5 kai3-beng7. 
Cheng3-hu2 lip8 Su-hoan7-hak8-hau7 khah-siong5 ia7 phai3 chut-giap8-seng to3-khi3 pun2 sou2-chai7 choe3 sian-siN; che ia7-si7 ho2 e5 bou5-iuN7.  
Lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 beh heng-khi2 sit8-chai7 tai7-ke tioh8 chin7 khui3-lat8 lai5 kiaN5 hau3-heng7, chiu7 thang tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 chiok-hok, hou7 ke, kok, kau3-hoe7 thang tit-tioh8 toa7 chin3-pou7. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 464 koan3,1923 ni5 10 geh8)
