Kiu2-chiu Thoan5-to7 Lu2-heng5
Ong5 Siu2-iong2  
 Ti3-i3 chheng2-an tang5 siok8 Chu2 ti7 Tai5- oan5 e5 hiaN-chi2.  Chit pang siu7-tioh8 Chu2 e5 un-hui7, tit-tioh8 lin2 liat8-ui7 e5 ti3-im3, ti7 3 geh8 20 ho7, goa2 i2-keng tui3 KiaN-tou Tong5-chi3-sia7 Sin5-hak8-pou7 chut-giap8, sou2-i2 ai3 kap tai7-ke saN-kap kam2-sia7 Siong7 Chu2, ia7 keng3-i3 ka7 lin2 seh-sia7, ng3-bang7 chiong- lai5 iu5-goan5 oe7 tit-tioh8 lin2 e5 chi2-to7, khan- seng5, si7 goa2 e5 ban7-heng7.    
Tui3 3 geh8 3 ho7 kau3 17 ho7, Sin5-hak8-pou7 e5 chut-giap8-seng u7 siu7 Sian-siN e5 chi2-to7, khi3 Kiu2-chiu thoan5-to7. Sou2 keng-ke3 put- chi2 choe7-ui7, chong2-si7 choa2-bin7 iu2-han7, ti7- chia boe7-tit thang kong2 kau3 siong5-se3, sou2-i2 kan2-tan ai3 siau7-kai3 kui2 hang7 khah u7 koan-he7 chong-kau3 e5, chiu7 ho2.    
1.Ti7 Him5-pun2 e5 sou2 kam2 ( 4 ho7 )    
Kiu2-chiu e5 khi3-hau7 sio-lo7, lang5 e5 seng3-chit sio2-khoa2 chhou-po7, gian5-gu2 chhou-lou2, houN3 pian7-lun7, kou3-chip kau3-kek8.  Put8-kau3 chhiong-seng7, Ki-tok-kau3 oh heng, ti3-kau3 lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 soe3 keng; ti7 cha2-si5 siu7 toa7 khun2-pek, m7-ku2 ia7 u7 toa7 li7-ek.  In- ui7 tui3 chia e5 kau3-hoe7 u7 chhut put-chi2 choe e5 toa7 jin5-but8, chhin-chhiuN7 Tong5- chi3-sia7 Chong2-tiuN2 Hai2-lo2-beng5, Tai7-pan2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 Bok8-su Kiong-chhoan, a2-si7 Kim-sim Sian-siN chiah-e5, chiu7-si7.    
Ti7 Beng5-ti7 24 ni5 chia e5 kau3-hoe7 toa7 heng-khi2, hoe7-lai7 e5 sin3-giong2 kian-kou3, lam5-lu2 chheng-lian5 e5 hak8-seng tin3 toa7 to-sou3. Chiah-e5 ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 beh chin2- heng e5 ian5-kou3, u7-e5 tng3-saN boe7-khou3, khiam7-tng5 liam2-tou7, chek-chu7 chiN5 lai5 chhiaN2 ti7 Lun5-tun e5 liu5-hak8-seng Chong5- goan5 Sian-siN choe3 in e5 Bok8-su.  Siat-su2 na7 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 iu2-ui5 e5 lam5- lu2 chheng-lian5 ti7 lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7, thai2 oe7 boe7 heng ?    
 
Oa2 Him5-pun2 chhia-thau5 u7 chit8 e5 soaN kio3-choe3 " Hoe-kong-san. "  Teng2-bin7 u7 chit8 chang5 chheng5-peh put-chi2 toa7; chit chang5 ti7 Jit8-pun2 lek8-su2 tiong ia7 chin iu2- beng5.  Chong2-si7 ti7 chong-kau3-su2 tiong koh-khah u7 mia5, chiu7-si7 cha2-si5 Hai2-lo2- beng5 Phok-su7, Kiong-chhoan Bok8-su, kap Kim-sim Sian-siN teng2, kui2-na7 lang5, ui7-tioh8 sin3 Ki-tok-kau3, m7 han7-tiaN7 siu7 goa7-bin7 e5 khun2-tiok8, chiu7 in e5 pe7-bu2 ia7 si7 an2-ni, m7 jin7 in choe3 kiaN2, koaN2-tiok8 in tioh8 chu7-chin7, m7-thang kap lang5 choe3 lang5, sou2-i2 lai5 bih ti7 chit e5 soaN, kim3-chiah8 pek-chhiat ki5-to2.  Au7-lai5 kap Chu2 lip8- iok, tiaN7 tioh8 beh choe3 Chu2 e5 lo5-pok8. Sou2-i2 tai7-ke ka7 cheng2-thau5-a2 hou7 i lau5- huih, lai5 choe3 huih e5 in3, kap Chu2 lip8 eng2-oan2 e5 iok.    
2.Ti7 Ji5-giok8-to2 ( 5 ho7 ) 	   
Chit sou2-chai7 e5 lang5 kah-na2 teh kong2 Gou5-lo5, hoe- ko5-ko5, thiaN long2 bo5; chong2- si7 ho2 keng2-ti3, put-chi2 choe7 lang5 lai5-lai5 khi3-khi3.    
Lun7 cheng-sin5 siong7, a2-si7 chong-kau3 hong-bin7, peh-siN3 long2 khun3-khun3, u7-e5 iau2 teh tuh-ka7-che7.  Sui-bong2 ti7 chap8 goa7 ni5 cheng5, Siong7-te3 u7 chioh chu7-jian5 lai5 hian2-chhut I e5 khui3-lat8 kap ui-giam5-chiu7-si7 hou7 Eng-tou2 ( Sakurashima ) u7 hun3-he2, sou2 hun3-chhut e5 chioh8-thau5 hian7-si5 moa2 hai2-kiN5.  Hit-si5 e5 chong7-hong2 sit8- chai7 chhiN-chham2 kau3-kek8, chong2-si7 peh- siN3 iau2-boe7 hiau2 hoan2-hoe2 koe2-piN3 lai5 kui Chu2, thang kho2-lin5 ah !    
Na7-si7 Chu2 iau2 teh thiaN3 in, siuN2-su3 in toa7-liap8 e5 chhai3-thau5, chha-put-to u7 toa7-tau2 e5 toa7; sui-bong2 toa7-khou, iau2-ku2 chin ho2 bi7-sou3, siok8 koh u7 kiok8.  An2-ni Siong7-te3 eng7 ban7 hong-hoat teh kio3-chhiN2 in e5 sim, m7-ku2 in e5 sim teng7 chhin- chhiuN7 hiah e5 chioh8-thau5, thau5-khak chat8 chhin-chhiuN7 hiah e5 toa7-khou chhai3-tahu5.    
3.Ti7 Siau2-lim5 Thoan5-to7 ( 6 ho7 )    
Goa2 kap chit8 e5 peng5-iu2 ti7 chia e5 kau3- hoe7 u7 khui Ki-tok-kau3 e5 Kang2-ian2-hoe7, siu7 Bok8-su kap hiaN-chi2 e5 kong2-ko3, chit8 pai3-tng5 che7 kau3 bo5 lou7, kau3-kek8 e5 ho2 ki- hoe7, thang kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 to7-li2. ThiaN- kiN3 goa2 si7 lan2 Pun2-to2 lang5, tak8-e5 toa7 gong5-ngiah8, chu3-sin5 aN3 hi7-khang lai5 thiaN. Ti7-chia e5 sou2-kam2 chiu7-si7 u7 chit8 ui7 e5 sin3-chia2, chin jiat8-sim. I u7 chit8 e5 cha- bou2-kiaN2 teh thak8 Lu2-hak8-hau7, hut-jian5 tioh8 tng5-a2-piN7 soa3 si2-khi3, chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 sit-chi3, hoan2-tng2 ju2 jiat8-sim, ai3 siau3- liam7 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 . Sou2-i2 chiu7 kian3-siat chit8 keng Iu3-ti7-hng5 choe3 ki3-liam7, chiong beh hou7 in cha-bou2 kiaN2 lai5 thak8-chheh e5 chiN5, chhiaN2 sian-siN lai5 ka3-si7 hiah e5 gin2- na2. Na7 khoaN3-kiN3 chiah e5 soe3-kiaN2 chiu7 siuN7-tioh8 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2. I sou2 keng2 chit e5 su7-giap8 kau3-kek8 ho2, sou2-i2 koaN-hu2 a2-si7 chng-bin5 beh ki3-hu3 tau3 pang-chan7. Chong2-si7 i long2 m7-in2, kiaN-liau2 oe7 siu7 sok-pak8, tak8-hang7 ai3 ka-ki7 phah-sng3 liau7-li2 tam-tng lai5 li7-ek te7-hng.   
4.Ti7 Tou-siaN5 le2-pai3 soat-kau3 ( 7 ho7 )    
Kin-cha2-khi2 si7 Le2-pai3, sou2-i2 goa2 kap tang5-phoaN7 li7-khui, kou-toaN khi3 hu3 le2-pai3 choe3 chu2-li2. Loh8-chhia e5 si5 m7-bat poaN3 lang5, chong2-si7 u7 chhe chit8 lang5 lai5 tan2- chih, sui5-si5 chiu7 kap i khi3. Kau3-kek8 hoaN-hi2, thang khoaN3-kiN3 79 e5 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 hak8-seng teh tan2-chih, ai3 thiaN Chu2 e5 to7-li2.  Chiu7 sui5-si5 kong2 poaN3-tiam2 cheng hou7 in thiaN, tai7-ke ti3-i3 chu3-sin5 lai5 thiaN, chin tiam7-cheng7, kau3-kek8 e5 ho2 phin2-heng7.    
Au7-lai5 soa3-chiap le2-pai3, goa2 chiu7 soat- kau3 " Siong7-te3 si7 Ai3."  Tai7-ke keng3-khian5 thiaN3 Siong7-te3, iah8 thiaN I e5 chin-li2.  Tak8- e5 chin khian5-seng5, tng5-lai7 kau3-kek8 cheng7, hoaN-hi2 thiaN-than3 Siong7-te3 e5 beng7-leng7, choan-sim siu2 le2-pai3 tiam2-goa7 cheng.    
Hit e7-tau3 che7-toh ti7 Ki-pun2 Bok8-su e5 ke. I u7 poeh e5 kiaN2, chit8 tin7 kah-na2 hi3- pan chhio3-gi7-gi7, ka-teng5 chin oan5-moa2. In e5 gau5 ka3-si7 kiaN2-ji5, gau5 khoan2-thai7 chhut-goa7 lang5; goa2 siu7 toa7 kam2-kek. Che sit8-chai7 si7 Chu2 chin7-tiong e5 lou5-pok8.    
5.Ti7 Kiong-kia7 Ian2-soat ( 7 hou7 am3-si5 ).    
Kiu2-chiu e5 Kau3-hoe7 tiong, chia sng3-si7 put-chi2 toa7.  Pai3-tng5 khi2 ti7 siong7 lau7- jiat8 e5 seng-li2-tiuN5; sui-bong2 si7 ti7 chit e5 sou2- chai7, m7-ku2 sin3-chia2 chin jiat8-sim, boe7 su hiah e5 seng-li2-lang5.  Kau3-hoe7 oah8-tang7, Mou5-kui2 beh hai7, chhe7 bo5 khang3-phang7.    
Goan2 u7 ti7-chia khui toa7 Ian2-soat-hoe7. Kang2-ian2-chia2 si7 goa2 kap chit8 e5 peng5-iu2, chin ho2 ki-hoe7; chhi7-tiuN2, thau5-lang5 a2-si7 Tiong-hak8 kap Long5-hak8-hau7 e5 sian-siN kap hak8-seng put-chi2 choe7 lai5 thiaN. Tit-tioh8 Seng3 Sin5 e5 pang-chan7, tai7-ke kui eng5-kng ti7 Chu2.    
Chia e5 Bok8-su si7 Eng-chiN2 sang3.  I si7 goan2 e5 tong5-chhong, put-chi2 siau3-lian5, chin oah8-tang7, gau5 liau7-li2 kau3-hoe7, tui3- tiong7 Chu2-jit8-oh8, gau5 chio-hou chheng- lian5-poe3, ti7 hoe7-lai7 u7 beng5-bong7, ti7 hoe7- goa7 u7 sin3-iong7; beh khong2-khoah Thian-kok put-chi2 iu2-bong7.  
6.Ti7 Te-khu7-goan5 Kou-ji5-iN7 ( 9 ho7 ).    
Chia si7 soaN-lai7 hong-pou e5 sou2-chai7, ti7 Beng5-ti7 27 ni5 4 geh8 16 ho7, Chioh8-chiN2 Sian-siN u7 tui3 Kong-san chhian-soa2 60 goa7 e5 kou-ji5 lai5 chia khia7-khi2, kau3 kin-a2- jit8 Kou-ji5-iN7 iau2 ti7-teh.   
 Chioh8-chiN2 Sian-siN, chiu7-si7 Kou-ji5-iN7 e5 siat-lip8-chia2.  I siau3-lian5 e5 si5, tui3 Kim- sim Sian-siN siu7 soe2-le2 jip8 seng3-hoe7.  Choe3 hak8-seng e5 si5 gau5 thiaN3 tang5-phoaN7, gau5 the2-thiap, kho2-lin5 phaiN2-mia7 kiaN2. I si7 Kong-san I-hak8-hau7 e5 hak8-seng; na2 thak8-chheh, na2 pang-chan7 bo5 i-oa2 e5 soe3- kiaN2.  I koat-sim  
chiong-lai5 tiaN7-tioh8 beh hian3-sin lai5 chin2-kiu3 kou-ji5.  Sou2-i2 kau3 beh chut-giap8 hit-si5, i chiu7 chiong lak8- ni5 ku2 sou2 thak8 i-hak8 e5 chheh it-chin7 pang3-he2-sio, choan-sim ti3-i3 gian2-kiu3 poe5-iong2 kou-ji5 e5 hoat-tou7.  Sou2-i2 i e5 su7- giap8 tit-chiaN5, mia5-siaN thang3 KiaN-siaN5, ban7-lang5 eng2-oan2 teh siau3-liam7 bo5 soah. Chin eng-hiong5 chiaN3-si7 che lang5 !    
Ti7-chia e5 kou-ji5 na7 toa7-han3, chiu7 eng7 lam5-lu2 e5 kou-ji5 ka7 in hap8-hun, hou7 in chit8 keng chhu3 kap chhan5-hng5, hou7 in chiaN5 chit8 ke tok8-lip8 lai5 seng-oah8. Tak8 e5 chin kut-lat8 choe3 kang, koh chin u7 kui-ku2. Chiu7-si7 ti7 soaN-teng2 u7 chit8 e5 toa7-cheng; siat-su2 chit e5 cheng na7 tan5, bo5-lun7 ti7 soaN-nih8 a2-si7 ti7 lai7-bin7, put-lun7 teh choe3 sim2-mih8 kang, long2 hioh-khun3, chit8 choe5 thau5-khak aN3-aN3 lai5 ki5-to2 kam2- sia7 Siong7-te3.  Kau3-kek8 u7 kui-ku2, seng5- sim e5 Ki-tok-tou5. Ha-le2-lu-iah !    
7. Ti7 Ian5-kong Kang2-ian2 ( 10 ho7 ).    
Kin-e5-hng iu5-goan5 si7 goa2 kap chit8 e5 peng5-iu2 beh kang2-ian2.  In-ui7 chit e5 sou2- chai7 put-chi2 khoah, sou2-i2 tioh8 hun choe3 nng7-tui7, ti3-kau3 goa2 soa3 ka-ki7 tioh8 ti7 chit8 sou2-chai7 lai5 kang2-ian2. Chong2-si7 Chu2 u7 siuN2-su3 khui3-lat8 hou7 goa2, chin ho2 ki- hoe7 thang kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 chin-li2, hou7 put-chi2 choe7 lang5 thiaN.  SoaN3-hoe7 liau2, m7 han7-tiaN7 lai5 seh-sia7, koh ti7 hit e5 tiong- kan u7 kui2-na7 lang5 siu7 kam2-kek, sui5-si5 hoan-hu3 beh boe2 Seng3-keng kap Seng3-si, hoaN-hi2 hoan2-hoe2 lai5 kui ti7 Chu2.  Tit-tioh8 Seng3 Sin5 e5 pang-chan7, chit pang e5 thoan5- to7 put-chi2 ho2 kiat-ko2.  Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3. 
8.Ti7 Piat8-hu2 hioh-khun3 ( 12 ho7 )    
Sui-bong2 i2-goa7 u7 khi3 kui2-na7 ui7, ui7-tioh8 choa2-bin7-siong7 e5 koan-he7, kan-ta siau7- kai3 teng2-bin7 hit kui2 ui7 chiu7 ho2.    
Au7-lai5 goan2 u7 khi3 Piat8-hu2 hioh-khun3 nng7 jit8 ku2. Chiau3 lan2 sou2 chai Piat8-hu2  
(Beppu) thang kong2 si7 se3-kai3-tek8 e5 beng5- sou2, in-ui7 u7 chu7-jian5 e5 un-choaN5, ti3-kau3 ong2-lai5 e5 lang5-kheh chin-choe7, oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 Thian-tong5, m7-ku2 khak-sit8 si7 Te7-gek8 khah chhut-mia5. Lan2 e5 siok8- gu2 kong2 u7 18 e5 te7-gek8, na7-si7 ti7-chia kiam2-chhai2 iau2 u7 khah-choe7 e5.  TaN chiong khah iu2-beng5--e5, siau7-kai3 nng7,saN-e5 chiu7 ho2.    
I.Huih-ti5 Te7-gek8.    
Chiu7-si7 chit8 e5 toa7 e5 chui2-khut, choaN5- chui2 phok8-phok8-chheng3, sio kau3-kek8. Chhiu2 boe7 bong-tit; koe-nng7 he7--loh8-khi3 liam5-piN chiu7 sek8. Hit e5 chui2-sek oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 huih, eng7 chhiu2-kun ka7 i he7--loh8-khi3, sui5-si5 piN3 ang5-sek, khah soe2 to boe7 lut. Sou2-i2 kio3-choe3 Huih-ti5 Te7-gek8.    
II.Hai2 Te7-gek8.   
 Iu5-goan5 si7 chui2-khut, kau3-kek8 sio e5 choan5-chui2 tui3 chia chheng3-chhut.  Hit e5 khoah u7 77 phiaN5, sio chui2 bo5 lang5 hiaN5, tiaN7-tiaN7 200 tou7 Fahrenheit (Hoa-si7) i2- siong2. Chui2 e5 sek chhiN-leng3-leng3, chhin- chhiuN7 hai2-chui2. Sou2-i2 mia5 kio3 Hai2 Te7-gek8.    
III. He5-siuN7-thau5 Te7-gek8.    
Chit8 khut tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 He5-siuN7- thau5 e5 khoan2.  Ti7 khut-toe2 si7 he2-hu-sek e5 noa7-kou-be5, kau3-kek8 sio, puh-teh puh- teh, hit e5 puh-khi2-lai5 e5 khang-chhui3, iu5- goan5 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 He5-siuN7-thau5, sou2-i2 kio3-choe3 He5-siuN7-thau5 Te7-gek8.    
Chiau3 sou2 liu5-thoan5 kong2, ti7 kou2-cha2 u7 chit8 e5 he5-siuN7 toa3 ti7-chia, tui3 peh-siN3 sou2 khioh e5 chiN5 khi2 koan5-lau5, tak8 jit8 chhia- hoa ian3-lok8. Sui-bong2 peh-siN3 san3-hiong kan-khou2, i bo5 iau3-kin2, kan-ta kou3 ka-ki7 na7-tiaN7; sit8-chai7 kau3-kek8 bo5-to7 e5 ia2 he5- siuN7. Sou2-i2 thiN bo5 iong5-un2 i an2-ni choe3, ti3-kau3 u7 chit8 am3 thiN-toe7 ou-am3, soaN-thau5 chhut-siaN, lui5-kong sih-na3 hu7-hu7 kio3, toe7-poaN5 piak-lih8, chit e5 he5-siuN7 kap i e5 koan5-lau5 choe3 chit8-e7 biat8-bo5. Au7- lai5 hit e5 sou2-chai7 soa3 piN3-choe3 chit8 e5 khut, chiu7-si7 hian7-si5 e5 He5-siuN7-thau5 Te7-gek8.      
 
TaN ti7-chia sou2 kam2 e5 chiu7-si7 chiah e5 te7-gek8, long2 si7 teh chi2-si7 lan2 tioh8 chai chiong-lai5 e5 te7-gek8 tek-khak boe7 bian2 bo5; lan2 iau2 oah8 ti7-teh, na7 bo5 kin2 hoan2- hoe2 koe2-piN3 kui Chu2 au7-lai5 thai2 oe7 siam2- pi7 Te7-gek8 e5 heng5-hoat8......... ? (a)Tang5 siok8 ti7 Chu2 e5 lang5, bo5-lun7 kau3 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 long2 eng7 ai3 saN khoan2-thai7; bo5 hun chong-chok8, bo5 piat8 pang-kok.    
(b) Bok8-su e5 ke khah-choe7 long2 u7 kiaN2 hok. Tak8-e5 to chit8-koaN7 kah-na2-chang3 leh, long2 bo5 hiam5 choe7, tai7-ke long2 u7 thang li7-ek kau3-hoe7.  Sui-bong but8-chit- siong7 e5 seng-oah8 kiam2-chhai2 khun3-lan5, chong2-si7 song3-hiong na2 pu3-u7 e5 khoan2. Siat-su2 u7 ia7 na2 bo5 e5 khoan2, bok8-su e5 ke- teng5 seng-oah8 sit8-chai7 chin oan5-moa2. Lan2 thang kong2 se3-kan it-poaN e5 lang5 si7 teh seng-chun5, m7-si teh oah8.  Bok8-su i2-goa7 chin chio2 lang5 teh oah8.    
(c) Tak8-ui7 e5 kau3-hoe7 chun-tiong7 in e5 bok8-su, o-lo2 i, sin3-iong7, keng3-thiaN3 i; iah8 bok8-su thiaN3 kau3-hoe7, un5-jiu5 khiam- pi iuN2-chhi7 Chu2 e5 iuN5-kun5, long2 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 lang5 tau5 in e5 bok8-su e5 si7-hui, a2-si7 bok8-su hiam5 sin3-chia2. Chin-chiaN3 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 kiaN2 !    
Khun2-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 su3-hok ti7 chiah e5 kau3-hoe7, ia7 chiok-hok lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 Kau3-hoe7. Goan7 Chu2 Li2 e5 Kok lim5-kau3 ti7 goan2, khong2-khoah Thian-kok bu5- pian, si7 sim sou2 goan7.
