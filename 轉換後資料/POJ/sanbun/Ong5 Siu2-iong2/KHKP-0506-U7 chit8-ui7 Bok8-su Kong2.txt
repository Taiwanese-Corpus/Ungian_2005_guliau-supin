U7 chit8-ui7 Bok8-su Kong2
 ( Ong5 Siu2-iong2 )    
U7 chit8-ui7 Bok8-su kong2, "SiuN7-kiN3 goa2 khah-cha2, Sin5-hak8 beh chut-giap8 e5 si5, tang5-phoaN7 tak8-e5 chin hoaN-hi2, tok8- tok8 goa2 chit8-e5 chiu7 bo5 an2-ni. In-ui7 goa2 si7 tang5-phoaN7-tiong te7-it ham5-ban7. Kau3 beh chut-giap8 hit-si5, bo5-lun7 hak8- hau7, a2-si7 pai3-tng5 long2 lai5 chu3-bun5, goa2 e5 tang5-phoaN7 long2 u7 thau5-lou7, tok8-tok8 goa2 chin3-the3 bo5 pou7.  Sian-siN bo5 khoaN3- kou3, peng5-iu2 bo5 beh saN pang-chou7. kok lang5 long2 khi3 kiaN5 in e5 lou. Chut-giap8 liau2 keng-ke3 gou7 geh8-jit8, ia7 bo5 lang5 chhiaN2. Chong2-si7 goa2 long2 bo5 sit-bang7, choan- choan oa2-kho3 Chu2. I tek-khak tek8-piat8 beh chiong goa2 chit8-e5 kek8 ham5-ban7, kek8 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 lang5 lai5 eng5-kng I e5 seng3- mia5. Goa2 pek-chhiat e5 ki5-to2 Chu2 u7 thiaN-tioh8.    
Au7-lai5 u7 chit8-ui7 soe3-soe3 keng e5 kang2- gi7-sou2 sia2 chit8 tiuN phoe lai5 chhiaN2.  Hit- si5 goa2 toa7 hoaN-hi2. Che si7 Siong7-te3 teh chhe goa2, long2 bo5 mng7 sok-kim joa7-choe7; sui5-si5 khoan2 heng5-li2 chiu7 khi3. Chit8-e7 kau3- ui7, in kong2, in-ui7 kau3-hoe7 bo5 khui3-lat8, sian-siN e5 sok-kim si7 tak8 geh8-jit8 chap8-it khou-poaN3. Chong2-si7 goa2 sin3 Siong7-te3 tek-khak boe7 hou7 goa2 gou7--tioh8, choe3 goa2 an-sim chhut-lat8 choe3-kang.    
Chong2-si7 ti7 hit e5 tiong-kan, goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 bou2 hoe7-iu2 pi2 goa2 khah gau5 thak8 Seng3-keng, bou2 chi2-be7 pi2 goa2 khah gau5 ki5-to2, bou2 lang5 pi2 goa2 khah jiat8-sim. In e5 sin3-giong2 pi2 goa2 khah kian-kou3, u7 lang5 pi2 goa2 khah phok-hak8. Ah ! Goa2 sit8-chai7 si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 lah. Sit8-chai7 kau3- kek8 m7-chiaN5-mih8 e5 lang5 lah !    
Goa2 kiam2 thang khah-su sin3-chia2 ? Goa2 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 choe3 ? Thoan5 Khong2- chu2 kong2 e5 to7-li2 e5 lang5, si7 hit-e5 chhang- mia5, bun7 it i ti sip8 e5 Gan5 Hoe5, a2-si7 hit- e5 tun7-tun7 e5 Cheng-chu2 ah ?  Seng3-keng kiam2 m7-si7 kong2, Siong7-te3 e5 chin-li2 tui3 gau5-lang5 un2-bat8, ia7 hian2-chhut ti7 gu5- gong7 e5 lang5 mah ? Koh Chu2 kiam2 m7-si7 o-lo2 hit-e5 koaN2-hu7 sou2 hian3 sio2-khoa2 e5 chiN5 choe3 hoaN-hi2 mah ? Lang5 khoaN3 si7 sio2-khoa2,  
Chu2 khoaN3 si7 toa7. Chu2 tek- khak beh chiong goa2 chit8 e5 sio2-khoa2 lang5 lai5 choe3 lou7-eng7.    
Hit-tiap8 liau2-au7 ju2 kam2-tioh8 goa2 e5 su3-beng7. Goa2 it-seng kam-goan7 hou7 Chu2 eng7.  Goa m7-bat keng2 kau3-hoe7, goa2 m7-bat iau-kiu5 geh8-kip, goa2 m7-bat san3- hiong, in-ui7 goa2 ThiN-nih8 e5 Pe7 si7 pu3-u7, Pe7 sou2 u7-e5 chiu7-si7 goa2-e5."    
Tang5 tioh8-boa5 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 ah ! "Kiong5 li2 e5 chi3, chong3 li2 e5 sim; m7-sai2 kiaN, bo5-lun7 to2-loh8 khi3, li2 e5 Siong7-te3 Ia5-ho5-hoa pi3-iu7 li2"  (Iok-su-a-ki3 1:9 ) 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 506 koan3,1927 ni5 5 geh8)
