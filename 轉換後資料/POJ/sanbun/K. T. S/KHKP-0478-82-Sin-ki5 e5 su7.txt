
Sin-ki5 e5 su7
(K. T. S.)  
Kin7-jit8 ti7 Tiong-chiu u7 chit8 lang5 chhi7 chit8 chiah ti chha-put-to chap8-it goeh8-jit8 e5 ku2; koh chhi7 chit8 chiah gu5 chha-put-to si3 goeh8-jit8 e5 ku2.  Ti kap gu5 long2  si7 tui3 pat8-ui7 be2 lai5 chhi7--e5.  Chit chiah ti i2-keng be7 lang5 tiaN7-tioh8, be2 ti e5 lang5 chiu7 beh lai5 liah8 khi3 thai5, chiu7 eng7 liu3-chhoe5 ka7 i liu3, chiu7 eng7 soh teh ka7 i pak8; ti be7-bian2-tit toa7 siaN hau2 kau3 ki-ki-kio3.  Chit chiah gu5 si7 chhiah-gu5, thiaN-kiN3 i e5 ho2-iu2 teh hau2, khoaN3-kiN3 lang5 teh ka7 i pak8, chiu7 chin7-lat8 chhoah-tng7 i sou2 pak8 e5 soh, chau2 kau3 ti e5 sou2-chai7; chiong i nng7-ki e5 cheng5-kak ka7 i aN2-tiau5-teh, hau2 kau3 ouN3-ouN3-kio3; chiong i e5 kak beh tak tui3-tek8; ti3-kau3 liah8 ti e5-lang5 chau2 liau2-liau2; u7-e5 eng7 pe5 lai5 chau2-li7, u7-e5 kiah8 thui5 lai5 phah hit chiah gu5. I long2 bo5 kiaN thiaN3, sui5-jian5 hou7 lang5 tit8-tit8 phah, i iah8 long2 bo5 sio2-khoa2 e5 lun2-neh, lai5 kou3 i e5 sin-the2.  Ti chiu7 chau2 kau3 gu5-tiau5 lai7, gu5 chiu7 chau2 kau3 gu5-tiau5 e5 mng5-khau2, chiong thau5 aN3-he7 gu5-tiau5 lai7; seng-khu toa3 goa7-bin7, an2-ni lai5 po2-siu2.  
Peng5-jit8 hit chiah gu5 na7 teh chiah8 chhau2, na7 khoaN3-tioh8 chit chiah ti lai5, lian5 chhau2 iah8 bo5 khoai3 chiah8; chiu7 chiong i e5 thau5 tui3 chit chiah ti lai5 hi3-lang7.  Chit chiah ti chiu7 hoaN-hi2 to2 ti7 i e5 bin7-cheng5, gu5 chiu7 chiong i chih8 pian3-sin ka7 i se2.  An2-ni khoaN3, chit chiah gu5 kap chit chiah ti thiaN3-cheng5 m7 chai u7 loa7 toa7 ?  Chit chiah gu5 phoe-mia7 lai5 kiu3 chit chiah ti, bo5 siuN7 i pi2 chit chiah ti ke loa7 toa7 e5 kong-lo5, ke loa7-toa7 e5 chun-kui3; chaiN2-iuN7 hoaN-hi2 kap kiat-kau cho3 peng5-iu2 ?  chaiN2-iuN7 phoe-mia7 kau3 an2-ni ?  
Kin-a2-jit8 lan2 e5 Seng3-hoe7 sng3-si7 se3-kai3-siong7 te7-it bun5-beng5 e5 chong-kau3; kiam2 m7-si7 tui3 saN-thiaN3 lai5 kian3-lip8, lai5 siat-chiaN5 kian-kou3 e5 kau3-hoe7.  Hian7-si5 lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 e5 chi2-to7-chia2 kiam2 u7 long2-chong2 khah-iaN5 teng2-bin7 hit nng7 chiah e5 cheng-siN a2-bo5 ?  Na7-si7 u7 lang5 khah-iaN5, un2-tang3 bo5 ku2 choan5 Tai5 e7 long2 lai5 kui  
Chu2. Ng3-bang7 lang5-lang5 e7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 khoan2 siu7 toa7-sio2 kam2-kek; thang ti3-i3 ti7 chiah e5 si7-se3 e5 hoe7-iu2.  
Ngou2-ong5 tu5-khi3 Ngou2-siong7
2.Hian7-si5 Kun-sin e5 Ngou2-ong5 (chiu7-si7 gou7 sian chhut-mia5 e5 put8, hap8-kiong7 kio3-cho3 Ngou2-ong5).  Tak8 sian hou7 lang5 kng si3-koe3 khi3, kio3-cho3 chhut-sun5. Tui3 ku7-lek8 4 goeh8 khi2 kau3 kin-a2-jit8, chit8 sian kio3-cho3 Sam-ong5, hian7-si5 long2 ti7 kin7-chng, put-chi2 teh u7 koan5; toa7-poaN3 na7 kiaN5 kau3 hit chng, long2 si7 teh tu5 ong5-ia5, sin5-chu2 chit ho7 e5 su7.  Chit e5 Kun-sin si7 ti7 O5-liau5 e5 chng-kha, li7 O5-liau5 oa2 poaN3-li2 lou7, si3-piN long2 u7 thian-jian5 e5 chui2-lou7, thang hou7 hai2-chui2 tng2-seh8. Lan2 e5 pai3-tng5 m7-si7 ti7 O5-liau5 e5 sia7-lai7, put-ko3 si7 O5-liau5 e5 tou5-lai7 na7-tiaN7, li7 O5-liau5 oa2 nng7-li2, li7 Kun-sin oa2 li2-poaN3, chiu7 ku7-mia5 Sai-pou-lai7. In-ui7 Sai-pou-lai7 sio2 pou-thau5,koh si7 O5-liau5 tou5, chiah kio3-cho3 O5-liau5 Le2-pai3-tng5.  
Hian7-si5 ti7 hu3-kin7 e5 pou-thau5 be5-sin3 chin chhim; lang5 na7 kng khi3 ti7 in hit chng koan, long2 si7 eng7 chap8-goa7 hoe3 e5 gin2-na2 teh cho3 tang5-ki. Na7 kau3 hit te7-hng si7 chiong hit te7-hng e5 gin2-na2 cho3 tang5-ki; chhin-chhiuN7 chit kui2-jit8 ti7 Pak-bun5-su7 tang5-ki u7 chap8-e5 i2-siong7, long2-si7 gin2-na2 liau2-liau2. Chiah e5 gin2-na2 jip8 kok-ke kok-hou7 khi3 chhiaN2 lang5 lai7-bin7 e5 ong5-ia5, kap sin5-chu2 kau3 kui-tui. U7-e5 kui ang5-ke3-toh e5 ong5-ia5, sin5-chu2, choan5-pou7 long2 hou7 i chhiaN2 liau2-liau2; u7-e5 kan-ta hou7 i chhiaN2 ong5-ia5 khi3, u7-e5 kan-ta hou7 i chhiaN2 tam7-poh8 e5 sin5-chu2 khi3; chhiaN2 kau3 kang2 pah, kang2 chheng sian thiap8 kui-tui; chiah e5 gin2-na2 tang5-ki kiah8 ke-si  khi2-lai5 ka7 i ham2, u7-e5 tng7-thau5, u7-e5 tng7-chhiu2, u7-e5 tng7-kha; au7-lai5 chiu7 pang3-hoe2 sio.  U7-e5 ia7 koh chhiaN2 jip8 bio7; chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 Ka-li2 u7 che7-che7 e5 sio2 bio7-a2, chiu7 chhiaN2-chhiaN2 khi3 kui chit8 sou2-chai7; m7-na7 chhiaN2 cho3 chit8 tui, iu7-koh ka7 i cho3-chio3.  
Chiau3 an2-ni ka7 i khoaN3, si7 tui3 u7 kou2-cha2 i2-lai5 kaN2 be7-bat thiaN-kiN3 u7 chit  
khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3. Sat-tan3 kong-kek Sat-tan3. O5-liau5 e5 put8 te7-it che7, tu5 kau3 beh chheng-khi3; ti7 Sam-liau5-oan iah8 tu5 chin-che7. Che7-che7 lang5 kong2, ”TaN chhin-chhiun7 in jip8-kau3--e5, ang5-ke3-toh long2 khang-khang, long2 bo5 hok8-sai7 sim2-mih8 !”  
ChhiaN2 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-che2, chhiat-chhiat ui7-tioh8 chit chan5 su7 lai5 ki5-to2. Sat-tan3 ka-ti7 teh kong-kek, bo5 ku2 tek-khah khia7 be7 tiau5.  Hian7-si5 Ngou2-ong5 sui-jian5 toa7 heng, bo5 ku2 un2-tang3 e7 toa7 pai7; thang hou7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 kok iah8 thang kian3-lip8 ti7 chit e5 te7-hng.  ChhiaN2 tai7-ke ti7 ki5-to2 e5 si5, ui7-tioh8 chiah e5 chhi-chham2 e5 hiaN-ti7 lai5 ki5-to2, ng3-bang7 bo5-ku2 ti7 Pak-mng5-kun7 lai7 teh cho3 Sat-tan3 e5 lou5-chai5 e5 lang5, long2 e7-tit chiaN5-cho3 Seng3 Ia5-sou e5 hak8-seng.  
3. Sit-loh8 koh Tit-tioh8
U7 chit8 lang5 i2-keng chhut-khi3 liu5 che7-che7 ni5 bo5 ai3 tng2-lai5.  I e5 pe7-bo2 siong5-siong5 teh ti3-i3 ai3 i tng2-lai5, chong2-si7 i it-hoat hong3-tong7, bo5 ai3 tng2-lai5 siu7 lau7-pe7 e5 iok-sok.  I e5 pe7-bo2 iah8 u7 sia2 phoe, iah8 u7 hoan-hu3 kio3 i tioh8 tng2-lai5.  Bo5-lun7 chaiN2-iuN7 i long2 m7-thiaN.  Kau3 chit pai2 i e5 lau7-pe7 siuN7 ai3 pun2-sin khi3 chhoa7 i tng2-lai5, beh soa3 ka7 i chhoa7-bou2.  I e5 pe7-bo2 chham-siang5 liau2, i e5 pe7 chiu7 khi3 chhoa7. Beh khi3 chhoa7 i e5 kiaN2 tioh8 poaN5-soaN koe3-nia2; long2 bo5 hiam5 chiah e5 lo5-khou2, hoaN-hi2 khai che7-che7 e5 sou2-hui3, khin5-khin5 khi3 chhoe7 kau3 tioh8.  I boe7 khi3 e5 tai7-seng u7 seng tiau-cha hit e5 sou2-chai7 kap lou7-tou5. I kau3 i e5 kiaN2 e5 sou2-chai7, i e5 kiaN2 m7-chai thang chiap-lap8 i. I e5 lau7-pe7 chiah tai7-seng mng7 pat8 lang5, kong2, “Bou2-lang5 u7 tiam3 ti7-chia a2-bo5 ?” PiN-a2 u7 chit8 lang5 thiaN-kiN3, sui5-si5 khi3 po3 i e5 kiaN2 kong2, “Ti7 goa7-bin7 u7 chit8 lang5 teh chhoe7 li2.” I e5 kiaN2 chiu7 chhut-lai5, tui3 i e5 lau7-pe7 kong2, “Li2 chhoe7 bou2-lang5 beh chhong3 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 ?” in-ui7 chhut-goa7 hong3-tong7 chin ku2 soa3 be7 jin7-tit i e5 lau7-pe7 e5 ian5-kou3. I e5 lau7-pe7 tui3 i kong2,  “To beh chhoa7 li2 to3-lai5.”  I chiu7 jin7-tit i e5 lau7-pe7, chiah chai-iaN2 i e5 lau7-pe7 thiaN3 i kau3 an2-ni ; i chiu7 hoaN-hi2 in2 beh kap i tng2-lai5.  Hit-si5 chai-iaN2 i e5 lau7-pe7 un2-tang3 chin iau, chiu7 sui5-si5 hoaN-hoaN hi2-hi2 chhoa7 i e5 lau7-pe7 chhiut-khi3 ke-nih8, eng7 ho2 e5  
chhai3 lai5 khoan2-thai7 i. M7-ku2 i u7 khiam3-lang5 chin che7 chiN5. I e5 lau7-pe7 long2 hoaN-hi2 the3 i heng5; ka7 i heng5 chiN5 liau2, chiah tai7-ke saN-kap tng2-lai5. Tng2-lai5 liau2 chin gau5 liau7-li2 ke-lai7 e5 su7; i e5 lau7-pe7 lau7-bo2 i2-kip8 lai7-bin7 e5 toa7-se3 hoaN-hi2 kau3 long2 be7-kou3.  
Hian7-si5 lan2 e5 chhu3-piN chin-che7 boe7 bat lan2 e5 ThiN-pe7, na7-si7 ThiN-pe7 m7-chai u7 loa7 ti3-i3 teh chhoe7 hiah boe7 sin3 e5 lang5; Siong7-te3 teh chhui-pek lan2 tioh8 ti3-i3 lai5 pang-chan7 I kio3-chheN2 cheng3-lang5 to3-tng2-lai5.  Lan2 sui-jian5 be7-e7 kio3-chheN2 in, iau2-ku2 e7 oh8 hit e5 lang5 khi3 po3 i kiaN2, kong2, “Hia u7 chit8 lang5 teh chhe7 li2.” Iah8 e7 oh8 Hui-lek8 tui3 Na2-tan3-giap8 kong2, “Lai5 khoaN3.”  Hoe7-lai7, hoe7-goa7 pun2 si7 siang7 chit8 ke, sou2-i2 lan2 m7-thang khoaN3 khin in, liah8 in cho3 bo5 iau3-kin2.  
Lan2 te7-it tui3-tiong7 e5 lang5, te7-it tioh8 chiau3-kou3 e5 chiu7-si7 hoe7-lai7 e5 hoe7-iu2; na7 hoe7-iu2 u7 sim2-mih8 m7-tioh8, tek-khak tioh8 tai7-seng am3-cheN7 lai5 chi2-si7, tek-khak m7-thang ti7 tai5-a2-teng2 a2-si7 ti7 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 ka7 i lou7-hian7, an2-ni chiah si7 Chu2 e5 bok8-chia2, e7-tit thang chiau3-kou3 Chu2 e5 iuN5-a2. Lan2 na7 sio2-sim po2-sioh cheng3 hoe7-iu2, chiu7-si7 oh8 Chu2 e5 bou5-iuN7. Na7 kou3 tiau5 chit8 e5 ku7--e5, chiu7 khah-iaN5 tit-tioh8 kui2-na7 e5 sin--e5. Lan2 e5 chi2-to7-chia2 un2-tang3 long2 e7 chin gau5 chiau3-kou3 Chu2 e5 iuN5-kun5 houhN3 ?  
Phi2-jin5 sia2 teng2-bin7 chit saN hang7 long2 si7 chin-sit8--e5, m7-si7 chioh phi3-ju7--e5.  Sia2 chiah-e5 si7 beh hou7 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-che2 cho3 chham-kho2, m7-kaN2 tek-khak kong2 u7 sim2-mih8 thang li7-ek tai7-ke. ThiaN-liau2 ki5-tiong u7 che7-che7 e5 ko3-kek, ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 hoaN-hi2 sia3-bian2 chiu7-si7.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 478,480,482 koan,1925 ni5 1,3,5 geh8)
