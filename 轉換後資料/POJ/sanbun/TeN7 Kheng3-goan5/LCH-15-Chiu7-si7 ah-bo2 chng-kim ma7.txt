Chiu7-si7 ah-bo2 chng-kim ma7 si7 piN2-chhui3
TeN7 Kheng3-goan 5
Beh liau2-kai2 chit8 e5 lang5 e5 koh-seng3, ong2-ong2 ti7 chiu2-toh tiong kah pai5-toh-teng2 e7 tang3 liau2-kai2 chit8-koa2-a2. Iu5-ki5 si7 ti7 bin5-i3 ki-koan, chhin-chhiuN7: Hiang-bin5-tai7-piau2-hoe7, Koan7-gi7-hoe7, Chhi7-gi7-hoe7, lip8-hoat-iN7 teng2-teng2. Hiah-e5 tai7-piau2, gi7-oan5, ui2-oan5 sian-siN, ti7 gi7-su7 chip-sun5 tang-tiong, in e5 kong2-oe7 kah ki2-chi2 heng5-tong7 e7 thang hou7 lan2 khoaN3 kah chheng-chheng-chho2-chho2, sim7-chi3 in e5 ku-kha ma7 it-tit8 beh so5 chhut--lai5.
Bin5-i3-tai7-piau2 in e5 chit-chek si7 teh kam3-tok cheng3-hu2 koaN-oan5 si7 m7 si7 jin7-chin cho3 khang-khoe3, peng7 m7 si7 beh hou7 in li7-iong7 ki-hoe7 "cho3-sio2": Tiam2 chi oe7 bai5 koh kiam lau3 tou7-chai5. Kho3 in e5 "sam-chhun3 put-lan7 chi siat8", kong2 kah chhui3-kak choan5 pho, m7 bian2 chhoan2-khui3 koh m7 bian2 thun chhui3-noa7, na2-chhiuN7 ki-koan-chheng3 teh sau3-sia7, chi2 u7 chi2-chek, bo5 kou2-li7, sit8-chai7 hou7 lang5 haiN3-thau5 kiam sit-bong7. Lang5 kong2: "In--si7 sia7-hoe7 e5 hai7-thang5, pai7-lui7, bin5-sim put-an e5 kin-goan5." U7-iaN2 kui-chang5 ho2-ho2 "bo5-chho3."
Chong2-kong2 chit8 ku3, hiah--e5 lang5 chiu7 si7 "Bai2-koe kau7-chi2, bai2-lang5 kau7 gian5-gi2, ia7 chiu7 si7 ah-bo2 chng-kim ma7 si7 piN2-chhui3."
