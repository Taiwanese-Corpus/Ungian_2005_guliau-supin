Ko-hiong5 kiaN5-thau3-thau3
Ko-hiong5 Tou-hoe7 Kong-hng5
Koeh Bun5-eng
 
Ti7 Ko-hiong5, 6, 7 goeh8 e5 jit8-thau5 si7 iam7 kau3 bak8-chiu e7 ng7, thau5-khak e5 hiN5, lian5 lou7--nih8 e5 tiam2-a2-ka ma7 kan2-na2 beh iuN5--khi3--leh. Ti7 chit chiong2 jiat8-khi3 chiah iam7 e5 kui3-chiat, na7 u7 chit8 e5 kong-hng5, chhiu7-chang5 chin che7, tak8 chang5 koh chin toa7. In e5 ng2 hou7 jit8-thau5 kian3-siau3, bih kah long2 m7-kaN2 chhut-hian7. Ti7 in e5 im3-ng2 e7-kha, bi5-bi5-a2 e5 hong chit8 chun7 koh chit8 chun7 chhoe--lai5, hou7 lang5 long2 boe7-ki3-tit joah8--lang5 e5 jiat8-khi3. Che si7 goa2 sim-bak8-tiong him-sian7 e5 kong-hng5. Sui-jian5 bak8-cheng5 lan2 Ko-hiong5 iau2-bo5 chit chiong2 kong-hng5, m7-ku2 goa2 siong-sin3 koh 50 ni5 i2-au7, lan2 chit-ma2 e5 Ko-hiong5 Tou-hoe7 Kong-hng5 oe7-tit-thang tat8-kau3 goa2 sou2 him-bou7 e5 kong-hng5 chui2-chun2. In-ui7 i chit-ma2 i2-keng u7 bi7-lai5 toa7-khi3-phai3 kong-hng5 e5 chhou-phoe bou5-iuN7.

Ko-hiong5 Tou-hoe7 Kong-hng5 pun2-te2 si7 pun3-so3 iam2-bai5-tiuN5, u7 35 kong-kheng2 toa7. Ti7 i oan5-seng5 pun3-so3-tiuN5 e5 jim7-bu7 liau2-au7, Lai7-cheng3-pou7 chiah siu-hoe5, kui-ek8 choe3 kok-ka-kip e5 kong-hng5, kiam u7 po2-iok8, the5-kiong chhi7-bin5 kok-chiong2 e5 un7-tong7 tiuN5-sou2, sek-hap8 pan7 kok-chiong2 oah8-tong7 teng2 to-kong-leng5 e5 kong-hng5. In-ui7 seng5-lip8 cheng5, tiu5-pi7 tan-ui7 chiu7 siu-chip8 chit8-koa2 lau7-chang5, tiong-toa7 e5 chhiu7-chang5, tek8-piat8 phian-hiong3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 goan5-seng e5 chhiu7-chang5. Sou2-i2 lan2 kiaN5 ti7 lai7-bin7, oe7 kam2-kak chhiu7-chang5 i2-keng chin ong7, boe7 kam2-kak i chiah seng5-lip8 4 ni5 na7-tiaN7.

Goa2 kam2-kak khah toa7 e5 khoat-tiam2 si7 chhiu7-chang5 cheng3 liau2 siuN bat8. Lan2 siuN7 khoaN3 mai7--leh: siat-su2 u7 chit8 e5 pah-tang e5 kong-hng5, lai7-bin7 e5 chhiu7-chang5 long2 u7 pah ni5 ku2. Na7 an2-ne, tak8-chang5 long2 chin toa7, chin u7 hun7-liong7. Chit chiong2 kong-hng5 m7-si7 chin u7 tek8-sek koh chin u7 khip-in2-lek8? Goa2 chin hi-bang7 lan2 e5 Ko-hiong5 Tou-hoe7 Kong-hng5 oe7-tit hiong3 chit e5 bok8-phiau hoat-tian2, hou7 chhiu7-chang5 u7 koh khah toa7-chang5 e5 khong-kan kap ki-hoe7.

Leng7-goa7 koh khah hou7 lang5 ki5-thai7..e5 si7 i te7-ji7-ki5 e5 kang-teng5. Kui-ek8 tiong te7 2 ki5 e5 pou7-hun7 u7 65 kong-kheng2, u7 chhau2-pou-khu, jin5-cho7 sip-te7 kap chhiu7-na5 sit8-but8-khu. Lai7-bin7 beh choe3 san3-pou7 e5 to7-lou7-bang7, chun2-pi7 beh the5-kiong chhi7-bin5 ko-phin2-chit hioh-khun3, oah8-tong7, chhin-kin7 chu7-jian5 e5 tiuN5-sou2.

Chit e5 kong-hng5 ti7 Lam5-a2-khe, ChheN-pou-khe (青埔溪) e5 tong-sai nng7-peng5. Siau3-kai3 hou7 tak8 e5 chai-iaN2, u7 eng5 khi3 thit-tho5, khoaN3-khoaN3--leh, be7 bai2 ou5!
