
 
Ki5-iN7 e5 Pho5-to7-chhiu7

(Liam5 Tek-liat8)
Li7 Eng-kok e5 kiaN-siaN5 Lun5-tun sip3 hou3-lou7 u7 chit8 keng ong5-kiong (HamptonCourt Palace).  Hit keng khi2 i2-keng si3-pah goa7 ni5 ku2.  (Hian7-kim kok-ong5 khiok bo5 toa3 ti7-hia).  
Ti7 hit lai7-bin7 u7 cheng3 pho5-to5-chhiu7; in-ui7 thiN-khi3 leng2 e5 ian5-kou3, bo5 cheng3 tig7oa-bin7; si7 khi2 po-le5-chhu3, lai5 chai ti7 hit lai7-te2.  
Ti7 1768 ni5, kau3-taN i2-keng 159 ni5 chiok, ti7 hit ho7 po-le5-chhu3 u7 chai chit8 chang5 pho5-to5; hian7-kim iau2 oah8--teh,koh chin gau5 siN.  Bat u7 chit8 ni5 seN oa2 2200 pha; chong2-si7 tak8 ni5 u7 san--khi2-lai5, chi2-u7 lau5 500 goa7 pha na7-tiaN7.  Mui2 pha chhin3-tang7 u7 chit8 kin chi3 nng7 kin.  Koh be7 chin ho2-ke3; lan5-san be7 chit8 kinu7 3 khou 3 kak; sou2-i2 chit chang5 mui2-ni5 chhut-sek chha-put-to 1500 khou.  
M7-na7 an2-ni-seN.  Lang5 khi3 khoaN3, ia7u chiN5 thang than3.  Tak8-ni5 peh-siN3 chin-che7 tui3 si3-hng kok-kok lai5 khoaN3 chit chang5 koh-iuN7 e5 chhiu.  Jip8-mng5 kok lang5 tioh8 be2 chit8 tiuN 4 chiam chiN5 (onepenny) e5 jip8-tiuN5-kng3.  Ku7-ni5 oa2 28 banl7ang khi3 khoaN3; sou2 siu e5 chiN5 si7 oa2 chit8 ban7 nng7 chheng khou.  Koe2-chi2 teh-beh sek8 e5 si5-chun7, chiu7 na2 lau7-jiat8.  Kin-ni5 sin-lek8 8 goeh8-kan u7 7,2000 lang5 khi3 khoaN3;sou2 siu e5 chiN5 u7 saN-chheng goa7 khou.
Seng3-keng siong5-siong5 bat kong2-khip2 ho5-to5-chhiu7 e5 tai7-chi3.  Ia5-sou kong2 i si7 chin e5 pho5-to5-chhiu7, I e5 hak8-seng chiu7-si7 ki; koh hiah e5 ki, “kiN3-na7 kiat koe2-chi2 e5, I chiu7 siu hou7 i chheng-khi3, hou7 I kiat koe2-chi2 koh-khah che7” (Iok-han715 chiuN).
