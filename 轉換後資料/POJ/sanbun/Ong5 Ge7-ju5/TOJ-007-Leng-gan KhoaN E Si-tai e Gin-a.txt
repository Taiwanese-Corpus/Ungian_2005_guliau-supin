Leng2-gan2 KhoaN3 “E” Si5-tai7 e5 Gin2-a2
Ong5 Ge7-ju5
	Choe3-kin7 phah-khui tian7-si7 a2-si7 po3-choa2, jip8-bak8 sou2 khoaN3,sou2 thiaN-tioh8..e5, m7-si7 chheng siau3-lian5 chu7-sat, chiu7-si7 in khi3 sat-jin5, chhin-chhiuN7 seN3-mia7 ti7 chit-tin7 siau3-lian5 gin2-a2 e5 bak8-chiu--nih8, si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 ke3-tat8..e5, e7-tit-thang sui5-pian7 hui2-tiau7, chit8-tiam2-a2 tioh8 m7-chai ai3 tin-sioh pe7-bu2 sou2 siuN2-su3 hou7 in e5 chit-hun7 po2-kui3 le2-but8.
	Hian7-chai7 e5 gin2-a2, lan2 long2 chheng-hou in si7 “E” si5-tai7..e5, in e5 siuN7-hoat chian5-oe7, choe3-hoat chian5-oe7, si5-siong5 e7 choe3 chhut hou7 lang5 liau7-siong2 boe7 kau3 e5 tai7-chi3, “phe-chhia, cham3 pub, chiah8 tok8-phin2, chiah8 io5-thau5-oan5”. Che khoaN3 ti7 goa2 chit e5 choe3 lau7-su, i2-kip8 lau7-bu2 e5 sim-lai7, si7 thong3-khou2 koh sim-thiaN3, m7-chai chit-ma2 choe3 pe7-bu2..e5, kiam2 u7 chin7 tioh8 in choe3 si7-toa7-lang5 e5 chek-jim7--bo5?
Lan2 choe3 pe7-bu2, m7-si7 kan-ta than3-chiN5 hou7 gin2-a2 chiah8-ho2, chheng7-ho2 na7-tiaN7. Ki3-jian5 seN..in, chiu7 ai3 u7 gi7-bu7 lai5 kau3-iok8..in, si5-siong5 koan-sim..in, chhin-chhiuN7 hiah e5 phe-chhia-chok8, saN-kiN poaN3-mi5 koh ti7 goa7-bin7 teh loa7-loa7-so5, kiam2-kong2 choe3 pe7-bu2..e5, long2 m7-chai gin2-a2 i2-keng chiah oaN3 iau2 boe7 tng2--lai5, chin-chiaN3 si7 chiam2-jian5 hiah bo5 eng5--leh? Kiam2-kong2 gin2-a2 teh cho3 sim2-mih8 long2 boe7 ka7 i koan- sim?

Cheng5 chit8 cham7, kap goa2 e5 sian-siN teh khai-kang2, kong2 tioh8 hian7-chai7 e5 gin2-a2,u7 e5 long2 kan-ta chai-iaN2 beh thak8-chheh, m7-chai ai3 oah8-iong7 in sou2 oh8--tioh8 e5 ti3-sek. Iau2 e7 ki3--tit, u7 chit8 ui7 hak8-chia2 bat kong2, “Lan2 lang5 ai3 u7 saN e5 Q, IQ, EQ,kap CQ.” Ia7 chiu7 si7, ti3-hui7, cheng5-su7 e5 khong3-che3(che ia7-si7 it-poaN lang5 siong7 khiam3-khoat..e5), koh-chai3 u7 chhong3-cho7-lek8 . Oat8-thau5 khoaN3 mai7--leh, lan2 u7 joa7 choe7 e5 lang5 cho3 e7 kau3, iu5-ki5 si7 cheng5-su7 e5 koan2-li2 . Chit e5 si5-tai7 e5 gin2-a2 na7-si7 u7 put-boan2 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 si7 eng7 pok8-lek8 lai5 kai2-koat tai7-chi3, boe7-hiau2 eng7 thau5-niau2 kap khong3-che3 ka-ki7 e5 cheng5-su7, koh tui3 seN3-mia7 e5 ke3-tat8-koan oan-khiau, bo5 chun-tiong7 pat8-lang5 e5 khoan5-li7, ong2-ong2 sou2 cho7-seng5 si7 chit8-chhut koh chit8-chhut e5 pi-kiok8, kau3 hit si5 chiah hio7-hoe2, iah8 i2-keng boe7-hu3--ah.
Ki5-sit8 che na7-si7 beh koai3 gin2-a2, ma7 bo5 kong-peng5. Lan2 toa7-lang5 kiam2 u7 cho3 ho2 e5 bou5-iuN7 hou7 lan2 e5 gin2-a2 khoaN3, lan2 kiam2 u7 si5-siong5 hou7 gin2-a2 kam2-kak tioh8 chhu3 e5 un-loan2, lan2 kiam2 u7 kap gin2-a2 chhin-chhiuN7 peng5-iu2 it-poaN lai5 teh kou-thong, lan2 kiam2 u7 che3-cho7 ap-lek8 hou7 gin2-a2, hou7 in siuN7 beh to5-pi7, che chiong2-chiong2 e5 bun7-te5, lan2 cho3 lang5 pe7-bu2..e5, kiam2 u7 iong7-sim lai5 kiam2-tho2--leh?
Lan2 e5 gin2-a2 oah8 ti7 chit e5 kang2-kiu3 bin5-chu2 e5 si5-tai7, khiok si7 m7-chai beh ho2 ho2 lai5 pa2-ak che tek-lai5 put-iN7 e5 bin5-chu2, chin-chiaN3 si7 chiong ka-ki7 e5 khoai3-lok8 kian3-tiok8 ti7 pat8-lang5 e5 thong3-khou2 teng2-bin7, kun-pun2 tioh8 long2 boe7-hiau2 beh chun-tiong7 pat8-lang5 e5 khoan5-li7, chit e5 se3-kai3 chhin-chhiuN7 si7 in..e5, in siong7 kai3 toa7. KhoaN3 ti7 goa2 choe3 lau7-su e5 bak8--nih8, sim si7 ju2 lai5 ju2 han5, ma7 ju2 lai5 ju2 kin2-sin7, in-ui7 goan2 si7 khia7 ti7 kau3-iok8 te7-it-soaN3 e5 lang5, ai3 hou7 gin2-a2 u7 chit8 e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7, hi-bang7 in e5 saN e5 “Q”, choe3-chio2 ai3 cho3 kau3 chai-iaN2 ka-ki7 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3, sou2 kong2 e5 oe7-gi2,i2-kip8 cheng5-su7 e5 koan2-li2, e7-tit-thang tui3 ka-ki7 hu7-chek, koh tui3 pe7-bu2 u7 chit8-e5 kau-tai3.
