
ThiN Chu3-tiaN7
Keh Chui2-leng5
Chu3-tiaN7 si7 boe7 koe2-oaN7 e5 i3-su3, kong2 chu3-tiaN7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 kong2 ban7-mih8 si7 thiN siN-seng5 boe7 pok-tit, lang5 siong5-siong5 ai3 kong2 tak8 hang7 si7 chu3-tiaN7, ti7 soaN-nih8 tu2-tioh8 chhiN-hoan tak8 lang5 chau2-li7, chi2-u7 bou2 lang5 thai5-khi3, si7 chu3-si2.  Bou2 hoe7-iu2 chit8 ke chhit lang5 kap chhu3 hou7 toa7 chui2 lau5-khi3, lak8 lang5 si2, chi2 u7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 chap8-si3 he3 khiN5 i2-a2 hou7 chui2 lau5-khi3, khah8 ti7 tek-ui5-nih8, hou7 lang5 kiu3 khi3, che bo5 chu3-si2.  Ji7-chap8-goa7 ni5 cheng5 Sam-tiau5 liok8-kun khan2 chap8 lang5 khi3 thai5, chit8 e5 thai5 khin-siong, koh kui2-na7 tiam2 cheng ku2 koh-oah8, chau2-khi3 kou5-ioh8, hian7-si5 iau2 ti7-teh, chit e5 bo5 chu3-si2.  Bo2 sia3-jin5 i e5 pe7 si2 liau2 pang3 kui2-chap8-ban7 ke-giap8 hou7 i, au7-lai5 khai-liau2 teh ka7 lang5 pun, che chu3-tit i khit-chiah8 mia7.  Koe-lang5 Boat8-kong2 e5 thau5-ke chiong lu2-koan2 boe7 Y3,5000.  Koh ke3 nng7-jit8 sit-he2 choan5-pou7 piN3 he2-hu, boe2-chu2 chu3-liau2 chiN5 Siok8-gu2 kong2, SiN si2 thiN chu3-tiaN7; iah8 kong2, U7 kiaN2 u7 kiaN2 mia7, bo5 kiaN2 thiN chu3-tiaN7; iah8 kong2, Hou2-phaiN2 li2 e5 mia7, in-ian5 thiN chu3-tiaN7.  Se3-siok8 lang5 long2 an2-ni sin3, si7 chu3 tiaN7-tioh8.  Hoe5-hoe5 kau3 e5 sin3-giong2 kong2, Ban7-su7 thiN chu3-tiaN7.  Seng3-chheh ia7 u7 kong2, Ban7-su7 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3.  Koh u7 bo5 sin3 kau3 e5 lang5 kong2, Bo5 chu3-tiaN7, tak8 hang7 su7 ka-ki7 e5 chai5-leng5, teh choe3 kap keng2-gu7 si5-ki chiu7 chiaN5.  
TaN lai5 siuN7 chit e5 chu3-tiaN7 e5 i3-su3, hoe7-iu2 ia7 u7 lang5 tak8 hang7 an2-ni siuN7 si7 chu3-tiaN7.  Goa2 phah-sng3 ia7 u7 chho3-gou7 ti7-teh. Sui-jian5 si7 u7 tiaN7-tioh8, iah8 si7 m7 thang be5-sin3.  Siat-su2 bou2 lang5 na7 m7-choh teh che7, kam2 u7 chu3-tiaN7 i boe7 go7 u7 thang chiah8 ? Na7 ti7 liu5-heng5-piN7 teh phe-si2 thih-khi2 kam2 thang sin3 chu3-tiaN7 boe7 si2 ? Lang5 na7 hong3-taN2 phe-si2 thau5-mng5 chhi3-he2, gek8-lau5 gek8-chui2, ThiN-pe7 kam2 ia7 chu3-tiaN7 i boe7 hai7 mah ? 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 426 koan3,1920 ni5 9 geh8)
