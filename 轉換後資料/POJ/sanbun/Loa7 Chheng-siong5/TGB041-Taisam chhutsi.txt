Tai7-sam Chhut-si3 Hit Chit8-kang
Loa7 Chheng-siong5

Chit-kang, chit-e5 gin2-a2 chong2-sng3 peng5-an chhut-si3, ka-ti7 ma7 chin-chiann3 e7-tang3 hioh-chhoan2--loh8-lai5, kap tak8-e5 hun-hiong2 chit-chun7 ka-ti7 e5 sim-cheng5, kap chit-e5 gin2-a2 chhut-si3 e5 kou3-su7.

Bi2-hong5 sun7-goeh8 liau2-au7 goa2 chiah khai-si2 sio2-khoa2 e7 kin2-tiunn. Chu7 kin-ni5 7 goeh8-te2, Bi2-hong5 ui3 Tai5-oan5 tng2-lai5 Jit8-pun2 liau2-au7 to7 khah be7 penn7-kiann2--a, Gi5-lian5 ma7 tauh8-tauh8-a2 koan3-si3 po2-iok8-hng5 e5 seng-oah8, kau-tioh8 chit8-koa2 sin peng5-iu2, hou7 goan2 nng7-e5 cho3 pe7-bo2--e5, e7-sai2 khah an-sim tan2-thai7 te7-ji7-e5 gin2-a2 lai5 kau3 se3-kan.

Si5-kan tauh8-tauh8-a2 oa2-kin7, Bi2-hong5 e5 pak-tou2 iu5-goan5 tiau3 koan5-koan5, chit8-sut-a2 to bo5 chhe7-ke7 e5 hian7-siong7, chhu3-thau5-ke a-po5 kian3-pai2 khoann3-tioh8 i, long2 e7 ka7 liam7--nng7-ku3-a2, kong2 tioh8-ai3 khah kut-lat8 kiann5--leh, gin2-a2 chiah e7 khah ho2 senn; po2-iok8-hng5 hit-koa2 chhoa7 gin2-a2 chhoa7 chhut chhu3-bi7 e5 ou-ba2-sang3, koh-khah tui3 i e5 pak-tou2 u7 chhu3-bi7, tak8-pai2 na7 khoann3-tioh8 Bi2-hong5, it-teng7 e7 mng7 kong2, iau2 koh joa7 ku2 chiah beh senn, soa3--loh8-lai5 , koh lai5 ka7 Bi2-hong5 e5 pak-tou2 bong-bong so-so--leh chiah kam-goan7, lian5 hit kui2-e5 lau7-su ma7 kang7-khoan2, hai7 goa2 tiann7-tiann7 khia7 ti7 pinn--a2, m7-chai si7 ai3 chhap-chhui3 ah-si7 mai3 chiah ho2.

Koh-khah an2-choann2 kong2, chit-kai2 m7-si7 ti7 ka-ti7 e5 sou2-chai7 senn-kiann2, chhu3--nih e5 lang5 bo5 hoat-tou7 tau3-sann-kang7, in tiann7-tiann7 e7 kha3 tian7-oe7 lai5 mng7 cheng5-heng5 siann2 khoan2, si7 senn--a iah-si7 iau2-boe7. Kiat-ko2, tan2 a7 tan2, i7-san2-jit8 12 goeh8 18 kau3--a, iu5-goan5 tiam7-chhuh-chhuh, “Chiah koe3 chit8-kang nia5, bo5 iau3-kin2!” Goa2 an2-ne ka7 Bi2-hong5 kong2, ma7 ka7 ka-ti7 an-ui3.

M7-koh chit8-kang koe3 chit8-kang, soah kam2-kak si5-kan koe3 kah ju2 lai5 ju2 ban7. Pun2-te2 na7 tan2 goa2 am3-si5 ha7-kho3 tng2--lai5, to7 sng3 poann3-me5 leng2-ki-ki e5 sng-hong teh chhoe, i ma7 goan7-i3 kap goa2 khi3 goa7-khau2 bong2 san3-pou7, nng7-e5 lang5 na2 kiann5 na2 pho3-tau7, khoann3 an2-ne si7 m7-si7 chin-chiann3 e7 khah ho2 senn? Siang2 chai si5-kan thoa chit8-e7 ku2--lai5, i lian5 che choe3-au7 e5 chhiu2-toann7 ma7 hong3-khi3, tan2 i7-san2-jit8 koe3 beh oa2 chit8-le2-pai3--a, goa2 soah khoann3-tioh8 i kui-khi3 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5-teng2 khoann3 tian7-si7, piau2-cheng5 khoann3--lai5 lan2-lan2-a2, teng2-pai2 san2-kiam2 e5 si5, penn7-inn7 e5 sian-senn u7 kau-tai3, au7 le2-pai3 na7 koh iau2-boe7 senn, tioh8-ai3 tit8-chiap toa3-inn7, iong7 chhui-senn--e5 chiah u7 hoat-tou7--a. Thiann lang5 kong2, iong7 chhui-senn--e5 e7 ke khah kan-khou2, Bi2-hong5 kiann thiann3, it-teng7 chin bo5 goan7-i3 pian3-cho3 chit-chiong2 kiat-kiok8.

12 goeh8 25 kau3--a, Bi2-hong5 e5 pak-tou2 iau2 koh tiam7-tiam7, hou7 goan2 u7 chit8-e5 chiok peng5-an e5 peng5-an-me5. Siunn7 be7-kau3, hit-me5 sann-kenn-poann3-me5 chiah khun3, tng ho2-bin5 soah hou7 lang5 hiong5-hiong5 io5 cheng-sin5, bak8-chiu bui-bui khoann3--chit8-e7, Bi2-hong5 teh ka7 goa2 chhong3-ti7? Iau2 be7-hu3 khui-chhui3 kio3 i mai3 nau7--a, i tian-to3 seng ka7 goa2 kong2 si5-kan chha-put-to--a, ai3 chun2-pi7 chhut-mng5--a. Goa2 thau5-khak hoe-sa3-sa3, chit8-si5 m7-chai i e5 i3-su3, khoann3-tioh8 i teh khoan2 toa3-inn7 e5 sann-a2-khou3, chiah chai-iann2 si7 gin2-a2 teh ka7 i chhui--a.

I kong2 nng7-tiam2 khi2-lai5 ka7 a-me-a2 kah-phoe7 liau2-au7 to7 bo5 koh khun3, pak-tou2 khai-si2 chit8-chun7 chit8-chun7 teh thiu-thiann3, ma7 u7 chhut-hoeh, bok8-koai3 hit-kang chai2-khi2 i to7 kam2-kak sian7-sian7. Ui7 beh cheng-chhu2 si5-kan, bo5 kou3 goa7-khau2 koann5 kah teh-beh kiat-peng, lang5 na2 chun3 na2 se2 seng-khu, tan2 kau3 thau5-chang se2 ho2 chhoe ta, kap penn7-inn7 hou7-su7-cham7 e5 sio2-chia2 lian5-lok8, khak-teng7 chhui-chun7 na2 lai5 na2 chiap--a, i chiah ka7 goa2 kio3 cheng-sin5, sng3--lai5 tui3 goa2 iau2 koh be7-bai2.

Chit nng7-kang tu2-ho2 si7 jip8-tang i2-lai5 siong7 kai3 koann5 e5 jit8-chi2, pak-hong e5 koann5-liu5 piann3 loh8 lam5, Poann3-tian5-soann-kha hu7-kin7, tak8-kang chha-put-to chiah8-am3 pa2, khi3-un to7 kang3 kau3 leng5-tou7 teng2-e7-a2. Poann3-me5 si3-tiam2, nng7-e5 lang5 koann7 chit8-kha kha-bang2, thau5 kap am7-kun2 kiu kah kiong7 beh bo5-lou7--khi3, chhiu2 khan chhiu2, na2 kiann5 na2 ha3 sio-khi3, koann5-liu5 pek-kin7 e5 koan-he7, thinn--nih e5 chhenn chin beng5, lian5 chit8-phinn3 hun5 to bo5, goa2 koann5--lang5 tann-a2 e7-hiau2 jin7 e5『獵戶星座』, chit-si5 cha2 to7 m7-chai chau2 tah-ui7 khi3, kan-tann hng7-hng7 khoann3-tioh8 sai-peng5 thinn-teng2 u7 chit8-liap8 siam2-sih-sih e5 chhenn, ka-ti7 ti7 sim-koann-thau5 se3-se3-siann ka7 kong2, pai3-thok tioh8 po2-pi3 goan2 toa7-han3 se3-han3 long2 peng5-an--oeh!

Siang2 chai tu2 chhut-mng5 to7 tu2-tioh8 khun3-lan5, kui-tai5 chhia e5 goa7-khak cha2 to7 kiat chit8-ian5 sng-peng, che7 jip8-khi3 chhia-lai7, thau5-cheng5 siann2 to khoann3 bo5, ham7 se2 chhia-thang-a2 e5 phun3-chui2-khang to kian-tang3, loh8-chhia iong7 kin-a2 luh-luh--leh, chiah e7-tang3 sun7-li7 khi2-kiann5. Sim-lai7 chiah teh koai3-ki5, kam2 kong2 chit-e5 kau5-gin2-a2 iau2-boe7 chhut-si3, to7 chiah gau5 chau-that lau7-pe7 lau7-bo2?

Lai5 kau3 penn7-inn7, tit8-pan e5 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 khoann3--lai5 tan2 goan2 u7--chit8-khun3-a, sui5 ka7 Bi2-hong5 liah8 jip8-khi3 cho3 kiam2-cha, chiah chai i senn-mng5 khui si3-chainn2--a, i sui5 ka7 Bi2-hong5 an-pai5 ti7 chit8-keng san2-pang5, kio3 i seng ti7 san2-tai5 bin7-teng2 to2 leh hioh-khun3, na2 ka7 cho3 chhui-chun7 kap gin2-a2 sim-thiau3 e5 chhiau-im-pho chhek-teng7, goa2 chit-chun7 chiah thau5 chit8-pai2 chai-iann2, tang-si5-a2 gin2-a2 e5 sim-thiau3 hiah-ni7 kin2, chit8-hun-cheng teh-beh kau3 180 e7. Bi2-hong5 e5 chhui-chun7 na2 lai5 na2 bat8, chit-chun7 goa2 kan-tann e7-tang3 ui3 i bin7-iong5 e5 piau2-cheng5, ke-kiam2 chai-iann2 i u7 joa7 kan-khou2.

Sui-bong2 tak8-e5 long2 kong2 te7-ji7-e5 gin2-a2 e7 khah ho2 senn, m7-koh na7 to3-tng2 lai5 siunn7 beh sennn Gi5-lian5 hit tang-chun7, Bi2-hong5 ti7 hu7-san2-kho long2-chong2 thiann3 beh chit8-kang tah-tah, chit-kai2 e5 cheng5-heng5 tau3-te2 e7 an2-choann2? Ka-ti7 sim-lai7 iau2-si7 poann3 chhai-gi5. Tian-to3 si7 Bi2-hong5 khoann3--khi2-lai5 lau7-sin5-chai7-chai7, to thoa koe3 hiah che7 kang--a, i chai-iann2 che si7 choe3-au7 kai-toann7--a, koh piann3--chit8-e7, to7 e7-tang3 kiat-sok 10 goeh8-jit8 lai5, nng7-lang5 sann-kha sio i-oa2 e5 kan-khou2 jit8-chi2. Goa2 khoann3-tioh8 i e5 bin7 lioh8-lioh8-a2 hoan2-ang5, sui-bong2 u7 si5-a2 chhui3-khi2-kin nge7 ka7--leh, jim2-nai7 chit8-chun7 koh chit8-chun7 na2 hai2-eng2 tinn7-lau5 hit-khoan2 khou2-thiann3, i khoann3--lai5 soah tek8-piat8 sui2.

Hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 khai-si2 ti7 keh-piah chun2-pi7 ke-si, bo5 joa7 ku2 to7 chau2 jip8-lai5 khoann3 Bi2-hong5 e5 cheng5-heng5, chhui-chun7 khak-sit8 na2 lai5 na2 kin2, Bi2-hong5 e5 piau2-cheng5 ma7 na2 lai5 na2 thong3-khou2. Koe3 bo5 joa7 ku2, hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 lai5 ka7 goan2 mng7 chit8-koa2 bun7-te5, koh the3 Bi2-hong5 thiam san2-hu7 toa3-inn7 e5 ki-pun2 chu-liau7, lou7-boe2 mng7 goa2 kam2 u7 beh “lip8-hoe7”? Chit-ku3 Jit8-gi2 e5 i3-su3 si7 kong2 kam2 u7 beh “poe5-tong5 chai7-tiunn5”, kong2 khah peh8--e5 to7-si7 “poe5 bou2 senn gin2-a2”!

Siunn7 be7-kau3 i oe7-boe2 iau2-boe7 kong2 soah, Bi2-hong5 to7 in3 kong2: “Bo5--lah!” Kui2-a7 goeh8-jit8 cheng5, i to7 bat mng7 goa2 kang7-khoan2 e5 bun7-te5, goa2 to7-si7 an2-ne in3--e5. Goa2 bat nng7-pai2 koan-hoeh, koan kah ti7 hian7-tiunn5 hun7--khi3, sit8-chai7 m7 chai-iann2 chit-chun7 na7 khoann3-tioh8 lau5-hoeh-lau5-tih, kam2 e7 hai7 lang5 chit8-peng5 ai3 kou3 lau7-bo2 kap gin2-a2, leng7-goa7 chit8-peng5 soah tioh8-ai3 koh kiu3 lau7-pe7? Chun2 kong2 hou7 li2 kiann5 phian3 ngou2-ou5-su3-hai2, senn--lai5 si7 chit8-e5 phiau-phiat e5 cha-pou-kiann2, m7-koh tui3 cha-pou-lang5 lai5 kong2, cha-bou2-lang5 senn-kiann2 chit-chan5 tai7-chi3, eng2-oan2 oh li2-kai2, he si7 bi7-ti e5 se3-kai3, tui3 lang5 lai5 kong2, bi7-ti si7 siong7 kiann-hiann5--e5, siong7 bo5, goa2 si7 an2-ne jin7-ui5.

Hoan-thau5 khoann3 piah--nih e5 si5-cheng, chit-chun7 si7 thau3-cha2 7 tiam2 cho2-iu7, pun2-chiann5 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 siunn7 kong2 Bi2-hong5 sui5-si5 e7 senn, kio3 i na7 u7 ke khah thiann3 e5 kam2-kak, tioh8-ai3 koann2-kin2 ka7 kio3, bo5 siunn7-tioh8 Bi2-hong5 chhui-chun7 ui3 5 hun-cheng pian3-cho3 7~8 hun, lian5 hou7-su7 ma7 leh hoah koai3-ki5, kui-khi3 mng7 i u7 siunn7 beh chiah8-png7--bo5, hoan7-se3 si7 Bi2-hong5 cha-me5 bo5 chiah8 e5 koan-he7, chit-chun7 ma7 tong3 kah toa7-tng5 ko3 sio2-tng5, hou7-su7 to7 ka7 an-pai5 kau3 keh-piah e5 chhui-chun7-sek hioh-khun3, chiah8-png7 ma7 khah ho2-se3, goa2 koh ka7 khoe kong “lan2 khi3 hou7 lang5 kang3-kip--a!”

Bi2-hong5 e5 chiann3-chhiu2 toa7-tang5-sia7 tu2 chu3 liau2, chiah8-png7 khah bo5 hong-pian7, oann7 koe3-lai5 chit-peng5 chiam7-si5 hioh-khun3 liau2-au7, chhui-chun7 soah ka2-na2 ju2 chiap, Bi2-hong5 pe kui2-chhui3-a2 png7 to7 hioh--chit8-e7, goa2 siunn7 i it-teng7 chiah8 kah chin bo5 song2-khoai3. Chit-chun7 tu2-ho2 si7 jit8-ia7-pan hou7-su7 kau-chiap e5 si5-kan, leng7-goa7 chit8-e5 khah se3-han3 e5 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 lai5 chiap-pan, i khoann3 Bi2-hong5 ka2-na2 ju2 lai5 ju2 kan-khou2, to7 poah-chhiu2 lai5 the3 i thui io-chiah-kut, tan2 khah be7-thiann3 liau2-au7 , chiah li7-khui khi3 san2-pang5 chhoan5 mih8-kiann7.

Goa2 ma7 than3 ki-hoe7 theh8 phiat-a2 oann2-a2 khi3 te5-chui2-keng khng3. Ki5-sit8 chit-chun7 goa2 sim-koann-thau5 iau2 koh teh tiunn-tu5 kong2, tau3-te2 beh poe5 Bi2-hong5 “lip8-hoe7”--bo5? Chong2--si7 chai-iann2 ka-ti7 thian-seng gau5 tun3-tenn, ti7 chit-e5 si5-chun7 tek8-piat8 beng5-hian2, u7 si5 soah e7 hai7 ka-ti7 to3 khi2-chheh, hiah kan2-tan e5 tai7-chi3, chit-liap8 siunn7 siunn che7 koh siunn-koe3 bo5-eng5 e5 thau5-khak, soah ka7 tai7-chi3 siunn7 kah kui-pah lian3-tng2, “Yes” e5 li2-iu5 u7 50 e5, “No” e5 goan5-in ma7 u7 50 e5, kau3 boe2--a2 tai7-chi3 iau2-si7 be7 kai2-koat!

“Tau3-te2 beh poe5 lin2 bou2 senn-kiann2--bo5?” Lian5 ka-ti7 ma7 tui3 ka-ti7 tu7-lan7--khi2-lai5, sim-koann-thau5 iu5-goan5 bo5 tap-an3. Lang5 tng teh gong7-sin5 gong7-sin5, kiann5 to3-tng2 lai5 chhui-chun7-sek, Bi2-hong5 e5 piau2-cheng5 khoann3--lai5 koh-khah thong3-khou2--a, i khai-si2 hainn chhut-siann, goa2 e5 sin5-keng-soann3 ma7 toe3 i chit8-siann khah kin2 chit8-siann e5 hainn-chhan3 chhui an5 khi2--lai5, iau2 be7-hu3 siunn7 kong2 beh an2-choann2, chit8-siang-chhiu2 tah khi2-lih Bi2-hong5 kha-chiah-phiann, chhenn-kha pun7-chhiu2 teh oh8 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2, tau3-te2 u7-hau7 ah bo5-hau7 ka-ti7 ma7 soah kong5--khi2-lai5! Chit-chun7 Bi2-hong5 soah hiong5-hiong5 hoah kong2: “A-siong5! Kin2 khi3 kio3 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2, kong2 chui2-phe3 phoa3--a!” Goa2 soah sin5--khi3, sui5 chau2 khi3 san2-pang5 chhoe7 hou7-su7, siunn-koe3 kin2-tiunn e5 khoan2, khoann3-tioh8 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2, chit8-ku3 “chui2-phe3” e5 Jit8-pun2-oe7 lai5 kau3 chhui3--nih, soah tiau5 ti7 na5-au5 kong2 be7 chhut--lai5, ti7-ti7 tu7-tu7 u7 2~3 bio2, chiah puh chit8-ku3 kong2 “chui2 chau2--chhut-lai5-a”, tui3-hong hoan7-se3 ma7 be7-hu3 ai3-chhio3, sui5 phiann lai5 chit-peng5.

Chit-chun7 Bi2-hong5 chih-chan3 be7 tiau5--a, chit8-siann soa3 chit8-siann teh hainn thiann3, hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 kio3 i khah jim2-nai7--leh, khoann-khoann-a2 kiann5 koe3 san2-pang5 chiah kong2, siang2 chai Bi2-hong5 khok-khok hiu kong2 bo5 hoat-tou7--a, be7-su gin2-a2 sui5-si5 e7 ui3 pak-tou2-te2 chong5--chhut-lai5, kap chin3-cheng5 tiam7-tiam7 toa3 ti7 Bi2-hong5 pak-tou2-lai7, sann-put-gou7-si5 chiah peng2-peng5 e5 un-sun5 kam2-kak oan5-choan5 bo5-kang5. Tiann7-tiann7 thiann lang5 teh kong2 senn3-mia7-lat8, m7-koh goa2 koh-khah an2-choann2 ma7 siunn7 be7-kau3, si7 goan2 kiann2 ka7 goa2 ka3 siann2-mih si7 chin-chiann3 e5 senn3-mia7-lat8, ti7 che bin7-thau5-cheng5, lang5 e5 sim-su e7 chin chu7-jian5 chip8-tiong i3-chi3, kui-e5 sim-chong7 ka2-na2 hou7 chit-e5 bu5-heng5 e5 lek8-liong7 thoa leh it-tit8 khi3.

Hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 sui5 pian3-khiau3, i mouh Bi2-hong5 e5 kha, goa2 lam2 Bi2-hong5 e5 seng-khu, koh u7 leng7-goa7 chit8-e5 hou7-su7 ma7 koann2 lai5 tau3 chhah toa7-tang5 ko-a2, sann-e5 lang5 to7 an2-ne ka7 nge7 soa2 koe3-lai5 san2-pang5. Chit si5-chun7 koh lai5 nng7-e5 hou7-su7, tak8-e5 long2 ka7 chhiu2-long5 koa3--khi2-lai5, chin3-jip8 choe3-au7 koat-chian3 e5 chun2-pi7, lian5 pun2-lai5 chun2-pi7 kau-pan e5 ia7-pan hou7-su7 sio2-chia2, ma7 lau5 loh8-lai5 tau3-bo5-eng5, khoann3 i e5 piau2-cheng5, hoan7-se3 tui3 ka-ti7 tu2-chiah siunn-koe3 toa7-chu2 toa7-i3, ka7 Bi2-hong5 lau5 ti7 chhui-chun7-sek e5 koat-teng7, ma7 kam2-kak sio2-khoa2 phainn2-se3 e5 khoan2.

Hian7-tiunn5 e5 khi3-hun na2 lai5 na2 kin2-tiunn, goa2 ka2-na2 m7-bat u7 chit-chiong2 keng-giam7. Bo5 joa7 ku2, siau3-lian5 cha-bou2 i-seng ma7 kau3-ui7--a, choan5-tiunn5 tann-a2 goa2 si7 cha-pou--e5, che ka2-na2 si7 chit8-tiunn5 cha-bou2-lang5 bin7-tui3 senn3-mia7 kho2-giam7 e5 giam5-khok chian3-cheng. Chhoa7-thau5 e5 hou7-su7 sio2-chia2 it-tit8 the5-chhenn2 Bi2-hong5 chu3-i3 chhoan2-khui3, kio3 i chhui-chun7 chit8-chun7 koh chit8-chun7 lai5 e5 si5, chhut-lat8 ka7 piann3--loh8-khi3. Khoann3-tioh8 Bi2-hong5 thong3-khou2 e5 piau2-cheng5, kui-bin7 chhut-lat8 kek kah ang5-ki3-ki3. Goa2 bo5-tiunn-bo5-ti5 soah teh ou-peh8 siunn7, lang5 kong2 cha-bou2-lang5 senn-kiann2 si7 chit8-kha tah8 jip8-khi3 koann-chha5 lai7, “Bi2-hong5 kap gin2-a2 m7-chai u7 iau3-kin2--bo5?” Goa2 kui-liap8 thau5-nau2 choan5-choan5 si7 chit8-koa2 siong7 bo5 eng3-kai3 u7 e5 siunn7-hoat, m7-koh soah koh ju2 lai5 ju2 che7, na2-chhiunn7 joah8--lang5 boe2-am3-a2, toa7-hou7 lai5 chin3-cheng5 hit-koa2 ti7 teng-hoe2-kha iah8-iah8-poe e5 toa7-chui2-kau2-hia7.
