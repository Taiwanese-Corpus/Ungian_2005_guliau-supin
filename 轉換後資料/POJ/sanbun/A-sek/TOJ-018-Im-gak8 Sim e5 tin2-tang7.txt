Im-gak8 ─ Sim e5 tin2-tang7
A-sek

Im-gak8 si7 chaiN2-iuN7 khai-si2..e5? Khoai3-lok8 e5 tio5-thiau3, kan-khou2 e5 haiN-chhan? A2-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 si-sin5 sou2 kong2--e5: Gian5-gu2 kong2 boe7 chhut e5 thou2-khui3, gu2-gian5 e5 chin7-thau5, si7 im-gak8 e5 khi2-thau5.
Sit8-che3-siong7 im-gak8 pi2 gu2-gian5, ki5-thaN e5 ge7-sut8 koh khah cha2. Oe7-sai2 kong2 u7 seN3-mia7 e5 khai-si2 chiu7 u7 im-gak8. EN-a2 chhut-si3 chiu7 eng7 khau3-siaN lai5 soan-ko3 i e5 kang3-seng. Lang5 e5 sim-thiau3, hou-khip, kiaN5-lou7 long2 si7 chit8-chiong2 e5 un7-lut8. Tai7-chu7-jian5 e5 jit8-chhut, jit8-loh8, tiau5-eng2, tiau5-the3, su3-kui3 e5 lun5-hoe5 ma7-si7 un7-lut8. Lang5 seng-oah8 ti7 im-gak8 tiong, im-gak8 ti7 mui2 chit8-lang5 e5 sim-tiong.
Hi-liap8 u7 chit8-e5 sin5-oe7, kong2-tioh8 A-Pho-Lo5 ti7 hai2-kiN5 san3-pou7 e5 si5, kha khi3 that-tioh8 chit8-e5 mih8-kiaN7. "Tng！" chit8-siaN chin ho2 thiaN. AN3-loh8-khi3 khoaN3, goan5-lai5 si7 chit8-chiah si2--khi3 e5 ku, ku-khak-teng2 iau2 lau5 chit8-tiau5 kin. Hit-e5 ho2-thiaN e5 siaN chiu7 si7 tui3 chit-tiau5 kin lai5..e5. A-Pho-Lo5 chiu7 eng7 che choe3 khim5 lai5 toaN5.
Kou2-cha2-lang5 ti7 tai7-chu7-jian5 e5 tiong-kan chhoe7-tioh8 chhiu7-bak8 kap tong7-but8 e5 phoe5, kut-thau5, chiu7 khai-si2 che3-cho7 gak8-khi3 lai5 thoan5-tat8 i e5 kam2-cheng5. Ti7 phah-lah8, chiat-ki5, a2-si7 chian3-cheng e5 gi5-sek tiong, eng7 kou2 kap keng lai5 chok-gak8. Ang5-i5 chhiuN3-koa kong2-chhut pou7-lok8 e5 kou3-su7. 
Seng3-keng ki3-chai3, Mou5-Se chhoa7 I2-sek-liat8-lang5 chhut Ai-kip8, koe3-liau2 Ang5-hai2, chiu7 kap cheng3-lang5 chhiuN3-koa 



o-lo2 Siong7-te3. I e5 toa7-chi2 Bi2-li7-am3 iah8 chhoa7 cheng3 hu7-lu2 giah8 lin-long-kou2 thiau3-bu2. Biau5-sia2 chhut im-gak8 tui3 lang5 e5 tiong7-iau3-seng3.
Tai5-oan5 in-ui7 tek8-su5 e5 te7-li2 ui7-ti3, san2-seng chin hong-hu3 e5 goan5-chu7-bin5 bun5-hoa3. Se-iuN5 im-gak8 tiong sou2-u7 e5 chok-khek li2-lun7, ti7 lan2 goan5-chu7-bin5 im-gak8 tiong long2 u7, ji5-chhiaN2 koh chin u7 tek8-sek. 1943 ni5 Jit8-pun2 e5 hak8-chia2 黑澤隆朝 chiong Bunun e5 koa-khek「pasi but but」hoat piau2 ti7 se3-kai3 hoe7-gi7 teng2-bin7. Chit-siu2 koa e5 tek8-su5-seng3 kap hok8-chap8-seng3 sui5-si5 in2-khi2 choan5 se3-kai3 e5 chu3-bak8. 
Im-gak8 si7 sim e5 tin2-tang7. Tui3 SeN3-mia7 e5 khai-si2, hoan7-na7 kang-chok, loan5-ai3, a2-si7 sia7-hoe7 seng-oah8, long2 si7 im-gak8 chhong3-chok e5 lai5-goan5. Im-gak8 piau2-tat8 lang5 chin-sit8 e5 kam2-cheng5, ki3-liok8 se3-kai3 e5 chin, sian7, bi2. Chhin-chhiuN7 In3-tou7 e5 si-jin5 Tagore sou2 sia2..e5: Ti7 ban7-mih8 chhong3-cho7 e5 si5, seN3-mia7 e5 hoat-seng ti7 liu5-chui2 e5 oe7-gu2 kap soe3 koh khin e5 hong tiong.    ???
