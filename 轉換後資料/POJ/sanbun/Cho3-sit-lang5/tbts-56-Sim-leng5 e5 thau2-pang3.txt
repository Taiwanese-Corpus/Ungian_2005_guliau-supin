Sim-leng5 e5 thau2-pang3

Cho3-sit-lang5    
	Ti7 1972 ni5, u7 chit e5 Oat8-Lam5 e5 cha-bou2-gin2-a ti7 khi3-iu5-toaN5 na2 teh sio e5 si5, thian2-khui siang-chhiu2 ng3 hip-siong3-ki chau2 lai5. Chit tiuN siong3-phiN3 tit-tioh Pou-Lip-Chu2-Chiong2. Chit-e5 kiaN-lang5 e5 oe7-bin7 khek tiau5 ti7 hit-e5 bi2-kun siong7-ui7, Plummer, e5 nau2-hai2 nih 20 ni5 ku2, chiaN5-choe7 ok-bang7, si5-khek khun3-jiau2 I. Ui7 tioh8 beh phia3-bian2 chit khoan2 e5 ok-bang7, I tim5-lun5 ti7 chiu2-poe nih. Nng7-pai2 e5 hun-in long2 sit-pai7. Au7 lai5 I gu7-tioh8 chit-ui7 ki-tok-tou5, Joann, chhoa7 i khi3 kau3-tng5, chham-ka thoe3-siu-hoe7. Ti7 1990 ni5, I e5 siN3-mia7 u7 toa7 choan2-pian3, khi3 thak8 sin5 hak8-iN7, chiaN5 choe3 Ui7-li2-kong-hoe7 e5 bok8-su. Ti7 1996 ni5, I te7-it-pai2 thiaN-kiN3 hit-e5 cha-bou2-gin2-a iau2 oah8-teh. Chit-e5 cha-bou2-gin2-a siu7-siong e5 si5, hit-ui7 tit-tioh8 Pou-Lip-Chu2-Chiong2 e5 liap-iaN2-su, Nick, chiong I pho7 kau3 piN7-iN7, ti7 hia cha-bou2-gin2-a, toa3 chit-ni5 nng7-ko3-goeh8. Au7 lai5 cha-bou2-gin2-a, Kim-Hok, siu7 sang3 kau3 Cuba thak8-chheh, kiat-hun, kau3 Moscow bit-goat8-lu2-heng5, tiong-tou5 thau-chau2 kau3 bi2-kok, tit-tioh8 cheng3-ti7 e5 phiah-hou7, choe3-au7 teng7-ku ti7 Canada e5 Toronto. Ti7 1996 ni5 e5 chap8-it goeh8, Kim-Hok siu7 iau-chhiaN2 chham-ka ti7 Washington ku2-heng5 e5 Oat8-lam5 chian3-cheng-thoe3-ngou2-kun-jin5 ki3-liam7 tai7-hoe7. Ti7 hoe7-tiong, Kim-Hok kong2 : "Goa2 na7 oe7-tang3 kiN3-tioh8 hit-ui7 ka7 goa2 cha3 tioh8 siong e5 hui-heng5-oan5, goa2 beh ka7 kong2 : "Lan2 boe7-tang3 kai2-pian3 lek8-su2 pi-chham2, tan7-si7, lan2 oe7-tang3 cho3 koa2 ho2 tai7-chi3 lai5 chhiok-chin3 ho5-peng5."  Plummer ti7 hoe7 au7 kiN3-tioh8 Kim-Hok, kong2 : "Goa2 sit-chai7 khui-khiam3, chin sit-le2, Goa2 m7-si7 tiau-kang beh siong-hai7 li2 e5." Kim-hok kong2: "Goa2 goan5-liong7 ! Goa2 goan5-liong7! "  Ti7 hit-e5 si5-chun7, Plummer 24 ni5 e5 tang7-taN3 chiah tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3 .
Bo5 iong5 oan3-hun7 lai5 po3-hok, ia7 bo5 iong7 oan3-hun7 lai5 chek8-boa5 ka-ki, che-si7 tui3 siong7-te3 hia lai5 e5 sia3-bian2. Chit-khoan2 e5 sia3-bian2 hou7 siu7 koe3 siong-hai7 e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 sim-leng5 e5 thau3-pang3, ia7 hou7 siong-hai7 koe3 lang5 e5 lang5 tit-tioh8 sim-leng5 e5 thau3-pang3. Ti7 sip-ji7-ke3 teng2, Ia5-sou ki5-to2 kong2 : "Pe7-a! Sia3-bian2 In, in-ui7 In sou2 cho3 e5, In m7-chai."  Che si7 bo5 khoaN3 ka-ki7 e5 kan-khou2, lai5 khoaN3 tui3-hong e5 su-iau3 kap joan2-jiok8. Ti7 kau3-hoe7 lek-su2 nih, u7 te7-it e5 sun5-to7-chia2 Su-The5-Hoan2 ti7 siu7-lan2 e5 si5, ia7 kong3: " Chu2 a ! bo5 chiong chit-e5 choe7 kui hou7 In."  Che si7 hui-siong5 sui2, hou7 lang5 kam2-tong7 e5 ki5-to2. Ia7 si7 ki-tok ui2-tai7 e5 cheng-sin5. Ia5-sou kong2: "Lin2 na7 bo5 liau5-sia3 lang5 e5 koe3-hoan7, Lin2 e5 thiN-pe7 ia7 bo5 beh liau5-sia3 Lin2 e5 koe3-hoan7. " ( THAI 6: 15).  Lan2 na7 m7 liau5-sia3 siong-hai7 lan2 e5 lang5, lan2 e5 sim-leng5 chiu7 eng2-oan2 hou7 oan3-hun7 sok-pak8, boe7-tang3 tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3. Na7-si7 lan2 kheng2 toa7 siaN kong2 : "Goa2 sia3-bian2 Li2, Goa2 goan5-liong7 ! "  Lan2 ia7 oe7 thiaN-kiN3  Chu2 kong2 : "Li2 tit-tioh8 thau2- pang3 lah ! "
Ki-to2 : Chu2 a ! Kiu5 Li2 thau2-pang3 Goan2, hou7 Goan2 bo5 koh oan3-hun7, hak8-sip Li2 e5 lin5-bin2 .
(編註: Goan5–bun5 bo5 piau-tiau7,iu5 Loa7 Pek-Ni5 piau-tiau7)
