



 
Khun5-lo5-iaN5(Work Camp)si7 sim2-mih8?
TiuN Hong5-chhiong
－“Khun5-lo5-iaN5” si7 chiah ku2 siong5-siong5 thiaN-kiN3 e5 sin-gu2.  TaN hit e5 i3-su3 si7 sim2-mih8?  Khi2-goan5 si7 chaiN2-iuN7?  Tu2-ho2 u7 cheng5 Chheng-lian5-chhu3 e5 Chong2-kan3-su7 TiuN Hong5-chhiong Bok8-su khi3 Jit8-pun2 sit8-che3 chham-ka, hian7-te7 kian3-bun5, taN ti7 chia ka7 lan2 kai2-tap, chhiaN2 thok8-chia2 chu3-i3. — 
Joah8-thiN kau3 lah, ti7 chit e5 si5-kan lan2 u7 choe7-choe7 e5 chu7-hoe7, u7 e5 si7 choan5 seng2-seng3 e5, u7 e5 si7 Tiong-hoe7 seng3 e5, ia7 u7 e5 si7 kau3-hoe7 ka-ki7 siat-hoat e5 te7-hng seng3 e5, chiu7-si7 Ha7-leng7-hoe7, S. S. kau3-hoe7 leng5-siu-hoe7 teng2, lan2 Tai5-oan5 ia7 i2-keng u7 pat8 sou2-chai7 u7, lan2 ia7 u7 teh hoat8-tong7.  M7-ku2 ki5-tiong u7 chit8 hang7 lan2 iau2-boe7 u7 e5, chiu7-si7 goa2 chit si5 sou2 ai3 beh kai3-siau7 e5 “Khun5-lo5-iaN5” (勤勞營) chit e5 beng5-su5, si7 goa2 ka-ki7 ek8--e5, i2-goa7 na7 u7 khah tu2-ho2 e5 ng3-bang7 saN chi2-kau3.
Ui7-tioh8 lan2 Tai5-oan5 iau2-boe7 u7 e5 ian5-kou3, ku7-ni5 goa2 u7 siu7 phai3 khi3 Jit8-pun2 tit8-chiap kian3-sip8 chit e5 kang-chok, ng3-bang7 ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 ia7 thang it8-heng5.  Kin-ni5 ui7-tioh8 chun2-pi7 boe7 kau3, sou2-i2 Beng5 Bok8-su u7 kap goa2 teh siuN7, an3-sng3 ma7-ni5 chiu7 beh lai5 khai-si2.
Khun5-lo5-iaN5 e5 khi2-goan5 kap cheng-sin5 
1920 ni5 u7 chit8 kun5 e5 chheng-lian5 lam5-lu2 ti7 joah8-thiN kau3 ti7 Hoat-kok.  Ui7-tioh8 tu2-tioh8 te7 I chhu3 se3-kai3 tai7-chian3 e5 ian5-kou3, chiaN5-choe3 hong-pou e5 te7-hng, in tiam7-tiam7 eng7 ki5-to2 kap lau5 koaN7 lai5 teh choe3 chai3 kian3-siat e5 kang-tiaN5.  Chiah e5 chheng-lian5 long2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, in si7 tui3 Sui7-se, Ho5-lan5, Eng-kok, Bi2-kok, sim7-chi3 ia7 u7 cheng5 e5 tui3-tek8 Tek-kok lang5 chai7-lai7.
In sui-bong2 kok-chek8, jin5-chiong2, lek8-su2, poe7-keng2 long2 bo5 saN-tang5.  Chong2-si7 u7 chit8 tiam2 saN-tang5, chiu7-si7 hoaN-hi2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 e7-bin7 lai5 chiah8-khou2 e5 cheng-sin5, in 5 geh8-jit8 ku2 bong5-chhim2 hoe3-sit8 (忘寢癈食) lai5 hok8-bu7 ti7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 kian3-siat su7-giap8, che chiu7-si7 kin-a2-jit8 ti7 choan5 se3-kai3 ni5-ni5 ti7 choe7-choe7 kok, u7 sou3-chheng toe7-hng ia7 u7 sou3-pah-ban7 e5 chheng-lian5 sou2 teh hok8-bu7 e5 khun5-lo5-iaN5 e5 khi2-goan5.  In e5 kang si7 siu-li2 to7-lou7, kian3-tiok, phah te7-piN5, cheng3 chhiu7-a2 teng2.
Chit e5 un7-tong7 khai-si2 si7 tui3 chit8 e5 Sui7-se lang5, i si7 sou3-hak8 kau3-siu7, ia7 si7 peng5 sin3-tou5, mia5 kio3-choe3(Pierre Ceresole), sou2-i2 chit e5 khun5-lo5-iaN5 e5 un7-tong7, thang kong2 ia7 si7 sun5-chui7 e5 “Peng5-sin3-tou5 un7-tong7” si7 tui3 peng5 sin3-tou5 lai5 khai-si2, ia7 tui3 peng5 sin3-tou5 lai5 teh thui-heng5 e5 kang-chok.  Tang5-si5 chit e5 un7-tong7 ia7 si7 chhiau-oat8 jin5-chiong2, kok-keng2, chong-kau3, bin5-chok8.  “M7-si7 eng7 oe7, si7 eng7 lau5-koaN7 kang-chok,” si7 chit e5 un7-tong7 e5 phiau-gu2 kap cheng-sin5, tui3 chit e5 cheng-sin5 sou2 siN-chhut i5-it e5 bok8-tek, si7 tui3 iu2-chi3 chu7-hoat-tek thong-koe3 lo5-tong7, eng7 ho5-peng5 e5 hong-hoat lai5 kai2-koat tui3-lip8 e5 hong-hoat, iong2-seng5 chhin-sian7 e5 cheng-sin5, li7-khui kou3 ka-ki7 e5 sim lai5 hok8-bu7.  Sou2-i2 chit e5 un7-tong7 hian7-si5 si7 phou2-pian3 ti7 choan5 se3-kai3, ia7 i2-keng chiaN5-choe3 Ki-tok chheng-lian5 lam5-lu2 beh “Gian2-kiu3 sia7-hoe7 e5 sit8-giam7-sek.”
Sou2-i2 chit e5 khun5-lo5-iaN5 si7 bo5 u7 sim2-mih8 chin chhim oh-tit li2-kai2 e5 li2-lun7, cheng-sin5, a2-si7 tiat-hak8.  Khun5-lo5-iaN5 e5 tui3-siong7 m7-si7 kan-ta ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 na7-tiaN7, ia7 si7 ui7-tioh8 sia7-hoe7, ia7 si7 hit e5 sia7-hoe7 khoaN3-choe3 choe3 su-iau3 e5 su7-giap8, a2-si7 si-siat choe3 tui3-siong7 lai5 tiok8-chhiu2.  Chong2-si7 choat8-tui3 m7-thang eng2-hiong2 hit te7-hng e5 lo5-tong7-chia2 e5 seng-oah8 choe3 tiau5-kiaN7.
Khun5-lo5-iaN5 e5 cheng-sin5 te7 i si7, “Hok8-bu7,” bo5 kiu5 po3-siu5, oan5-choan5 chu7-ki2 hou2-teng7, sou2-i2 mui2 ni5 u7 chin-choe7 chheng-lian5 hoaN-hi2 hi-seng po2-kui3 e5 hiu-ha5 (休暇), put-tan3 ui7 ka-ki7 e5 kok-ka, sia7-hoe7, ia7 kau3 ti7 pat8 e5 kok-ka khi3 hok8-bu7.  Che choat8-tui3 si7 ki-pun2 ti7 Chu2 e5 thiaN3, bo5 phah sng3-poaN5, koh chham-ka-chia2 si7 chu7-chu2-tek eng3-bou7 (應募), bo5 siu7 jim7-ho5 hong-bin7 e5 ap-pek chiah chham-ka, che kap chian3-si5 e5 khun5-lo5 hong7-su7-tui7 (勤勞奉仕隊) bo5 saN-tang5.
Te7 2 si7 “Kian3-siat,” khun5-lo5-iaN5 tek-khak tioh8 u7 ku7-the2-tek e5 kian3-siat, phi3-ju7 khi2 pai3-tng5, siu-li2 to7-lou7, cho7 kio5, phou-lou7 teng2.
 
Te7 3 si7 “Hou7-siong li2-kai2,” in-ui7 sou2 chham-ka e5, khah choe7 si7 chheng-lian5, koh si7 hak8-seng, kok lang5 e5 jin5-chiong2, bin5-chok8, kok-chek8, cheng3-ti7 long2 bo5 saN-tang5.  Chiah e5 keng-koe3 sou3 le2-pai3 e5 kiong7-tong5 seng-oah8, thang tit hou7-siong li2-kai2, siau-bo5 tek8-si7 (敵視), sou2-i2 u7 lang5 kio3 khun5-lo5-iaN5 choe3 “Ho5-peng5 e5 kian3-siat.”  Si5-kan ia7 bo5 it-teng7, u7 si5 chit8-ko3 geh8, a2-si7 nng7-saN jit8 ia7 kho2-i2. Beh chham-ka e5 lang5 m7-thang siuN7 “Beh tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8?”  Si7 tioh8 siuN7 “Goa2 oe7 thang choe3 sim2-mih8, beh hian3 sim2-mih8?”  Sou2-i2 hu3-hoe7 e5 lang5 chi2-u7 toa3 “ChiN5, Si5-kan kap Seng-khu.”  I2-goa7 long2 bo5 su-iau3 sim2-mih8, bo5 ng3-bang7 lang5 e5 po3-siu5, bo5 siuN7 kiat-ko2, chi2-u7 eng7 thiaN3-sim lai5 choe3 kang, koh khah iau3-kin2 e5, si7 tioh8 oe7 ki3-tit, “M7-si7 ti7 kong2-oe7, si7 ti7 heng5-tong7.”  “Not words but Action e5 ki-pun2 cheng-sin5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 812 ho7, 1956 ni5 8 geh8)
