
Lun7 Sin3
Mui5 Bok8-su
U7-si5 lang5 thiaN chit e5 to7-li2 cham2-jian5 che7 pai2, iu5-goan5 si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 chai-iaN2,  liah8 cho3 to7-li2 teh khng3 lang5 pai3 Siong7- te3 kap kiaN5 ho2 hit nng7 hang7.  Ki5-sit8 m7- na7 an2-ni; iau3-kin2 si7 khng3 lang5 tioh8 sin3.  Sin3 sim2-mih8?  Sin3-kho3 Ia5-sou.  Sin3-kho3  Ia5-sou u7 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7?  Chin toa7 lou7-eng7.  Phi3-lun7 li2 khah ai3 kau3 thian-tong5 a2-si7 ai3 loh8 te7-gak8?  Che m7-bian2 mng7; chu7-jian5 khah ai3 kau3 thian-tong5. Na7-si7  an2-ni tioh8 sin3 chiah e7 tit kau3, peng7 bo5 pat8 mih8 e5 hoat-tou7.  Li2 beh kong2, Na7 kiaN5  ho2 chiu7 ho2, mih-sai2 tioh8 kho3 Ia5-sou?  TaN phi3-lun7 lai5 kong2, Goa2 ai3 kau3 Eng- kok, na7 chhut-lat8 kiaN5 kam2 e7 kau3?  Tioh8 sin3 chiu7 e7 thang kau3.  Kau3 kang2-khau2  tioh8 mng7 khoaN3 u7 chun5 thang koe3 E7- mng5, koe3 Hiong-kang2.  Kong2, “U7 chit8  chiah Jit8-pun2 chun5”.  
Goa2 kong2, “Jit8-pun2 chun5 goa2 m7 kaN2 tah, goa7 kok chun5 goa2 m7 kaN2 che7”.  Na7 m7 kaN2 chiu7 be7 kau3 Eng-kok.  Sin3 lai5 thiah toaN chiu7 e7 thang kau3.  
I2-keng kau3 Hiong-kang2 chiu7 koh mng7, “Ti7-chia u7 chun5 thang kau3 Eng- kok mah?”  “U7, bin5-a2-chai3 chit8 chiah Tek-kok chun5 beh khui”.  “He!  Tek- kok chun5 goa2 m7 kaN2 tah”.  Na7 m7 kaN2 tah chiu7 bo5 thang kau3 Eng-kok.  
Liat8-ui7 peng5-iu2 lan2 ai3 kau3 Thian-tong5 tioh8 m7 thang hiam5 kong2 chit e5 Ia5-sou si7 goa7 kok put8: tioh8 ho2 taN2 lai5 sin3.  Kam2 thang kong2, “M7-bian2 sin3 e7 cho3-tit”?  Si7 na2-chhin-chhiuN7 koaN-hu2 chhut ko3-si7 kio3 thou2-hui2 tioh8 lai5 tau5-hang5, m7-bian2 kiaN; iah8 thou2-hui2 kong2, “Goa2 m7-bian2 sin3 e7 cho3-tit”.  Thou2-hui2 na7 m7 sin3 chiu7 koh chit8 goeh8 jit8 koaN-hu2 beh ka7 i liah8 khi3 thai5.  Lan2 na7 m7 sin3 koh bo5 loa7 ku2 chiu7 lan2 tioh8 kiN3 Giam5-kun.  Giam5-kun chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3; m7-si7 ThiN te7 bo5 po3, si7 jit8-chi2 boe7 to3.  TaN tioh8 lai5 sin3 thang tit-tioh8 sia3-choe7, choe7 hou7 Ia5- sou taN-khi3.  
Lang5 kong2, “Lan2 cho3 ho2 chiu7 ho2”.   Tioh8; m7-ku2 lan2 e7 cho3 ho2 a2 be7?  Si7 na2-chhin-chhiuN7 seng-khu phoa3-peN7 chin ku2,chin siong-tiong7, iah8 peng5-iu2 khng3 i tioh8 chhoe7 sian-seN hou7 i taN2-tiap8, iah8 lan2 kong2, “M7-bian2 chiah8 ioh8, m7-bian2 kho3 i- seng; seng-khu na7 ho2 chiu7 ho2”.  Phoa3-peN7 tioh8 kho3 sian-seN ka7 lan2 phai3 ioh8,  ka7 lan2 thai5-koah.  U7 choe7 tioh8 kho3 Ia5- sou ka7 lan2 thoat-li7 choe7, taN2-tiap8 sin5-hun5  e5 peN7.  
Lun7 se3-cheng5 e5 su7 tak8 jit8 tioh8 eng7 sin3.  Kau-koan be2-be7 long2 tioh8 eng7 sin3  chiu7 e7 kiaN5-tit.  Lan2 Tai5-oan5 seng-li2 lang5 long2 si7 eng7 gin5-phio3.  Gin5-phio3 u7 sim2- mih8 lou7-eng7?  Chit8 tiuN tat8-tit chit8 khou gin5.  Thai2-tho2 e7 chai?  Si7 in-ui7 gin5-phio3 u7 in3 chit8 ku3 kong2, Chit tiuN toaN lan2 na7 hian3 hou7 gin5-hang5 i chiu7 beh chit8 khou gin5 heng5 lan2.  Seng3-keng si7 Thian-kok e5 gin5-phio3.   Lan2 na7 beh chai7-seN peng5-an, si2-au7 tit- tioh8 ku2-tng5 e5 hok-khi3, eng-kai tioh8 sin3 Seng3-keng e5 oe7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 sim2-mih8 oe7?  “Lin2 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 teh tioh8-boa5 kap tang7-taN3 sou2 teh--e5 tioh8 chiu7-kin7 Goa2, Goa2 chiu7 hou7 lin2 an-hioh.  Goa2 e5 pin2-taN tioh8 gia5 khi2-lai5 he7 ti7 lin2 e5 keng- thau5 teng2, soa3 tui3 Goa2 lai5 oh8, in-ui7 Goa2 un-jiu5, sim khiam-pi; chiu7 lin2 tit- tioh8 an-hioh ti7 lin2 e5 sim.  In-ui7 Goa2 e5 pin2-taN si7 khoaN3-oah8 eng7, Goa2 e5 taN3 iah8 si7 khin”.  
“Siong7-te3 chiong I e5 KiaN2 hou7 se3-kan, chiu7 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 sin3-kho3 I, m7 bian2 biat8-bo5, hoan2-tng2 oe7 tit-tioh8 eng2- oan2-oah8, I thiaN3 se3-kan kau3 an2-ni”.  “U7 chit8 ku3 oe7 thang sin3 thang seng5- siu7, chiu7-si7 Ia5-sou jip8 se3-kan beh kiu3 u7 choe7 e5 lang5, chiong5-tiong goa2 te7 it phai2”.  Tioh8 sin3 hiah e5 oe7 chiah e7 tit kau3 Thian-tong5. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 224 koan3, 1909 ni5 10 geh8)
