Ke-thau5 pheng5-lun7-ka
Eng2-tek

Tong goa7-lai5 kap chai7-te7 kai3-soaN3 hun-beng5 e5 si5, ke3-teng5-chhia unchiang e7-tang3 chiaN5-cho3 ke-thau5 pheng5-lun7-ka. In chai-iaN2, goa7-lai5--e5 tai7-piau2 "ap-pek kap pak-siap", chai7-te7--e5 tai7-piau2 "kong-gi7 kap thau2-pang3".

Tong sit8-bin5 cheng3-koan5 si7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 teh hoaN7-poaN5, koh sam-put-gou7-si5-a2 to7 leh hoah『中華民國萬歲』, koh kong2: "Siang2 na7 beh thui-hoan『中華民國』, to7-si7 goa2 e5 tek8-jin5!" e5 si5-chun7, ke-thau5 pheng5- lun7-ka to7 thau5-khak gong5-gong5, toa7-chhui3 khui-khui, bo5 hoat-tou7 koh pheng5-lun7.
