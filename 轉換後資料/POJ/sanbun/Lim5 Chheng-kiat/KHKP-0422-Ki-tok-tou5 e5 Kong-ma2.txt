
Ki-tok-tou5 e5 Kong-ma2
Lim5 Chheng-kiat  
Lan2 Ki-tok-kau3 siat ti7 Tai5-oan5 i2-keng u7 50 cho2-iu7 ni5 lah.  Tai5-oan5 e5 peh-siN3 u7 300 goa7 ban7, na7-si7 kau3 kin-na2-jit8 lai5 jip8-kau3 e5 lang5 iau2-be7 u7 chiuN7 chit8 ban7, an2-ni kiam2 m7 si7 chin chio2 mah ?  Lang5 chaiN2-iuN7 hiah m7 kam-goan7 lai5 jip8 chit e5 kau3 ? Khiok si7 in-ui7 choe7-choe7 hang7 e5 in-toaN, chong2-si7 chiau3 goa2 khoaN3 u7 chit8 hang7 put-chi2 koan-he7 e5 that-gai7. Goa2 bat mng7 kui2-na7 lang5 kong2, Peng5-iu2 ah, li2 chaiN2-iuN7 bo5 lai5 sin3-to7-li2 ?  I in3 kong2, Sin3 to7-li2 ho2 si7 ho2, sin5-beng5 bo5 chhai7, che khiok bo5 iau3-kin2, m7-ku2 kong-ma2 bo5 pai3, chit pou7 goan2 boe7 kiaN5-tit. Sit8-chai7 lan2 siat-kau3 ti7 chit e5 Tang-iuN5 e5 sou2-chai7 iu5-goan5 tioh8 tam7-poh8 the2-thiap Tang-iuN5 lang5 e5 sip8-koan3; sou2-i2 na7 oe7 chiong chit e5 kong-ma2 e5 su7 lai5 kai2-liong5 kau3 hou7 i bo5 siok8 ti7 iN7-toan e5 sou2-chai7, lai5 siuN7 chit8 e5 hoat-tou7 kau3 hou7 in iu5-goan5 u7 kong-ma2 ti7-teh, chiu7 khah khoai3 hou7 lang5 lai5 jip8 Ki-tok-kau3.  
TaN si7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 ?  Gu5-kian3 u7 kui2-na7 hang7 pai5-liat8 ti7 e7-toe2. 
 1. Tioh8 chiong lan2 e5 kong-ma2 ki3 ti7 chok8-phou2.  Sin3-tou5 tak8 ke tioh8 sit8-heng5 m7 thang ian5-chhian5.  
2. Tioh8 hip kong-ma2 e5 siong7.  Na7 bo5 siong7 tioh8 hip bong7-pai5 e5 siong7, na7 bo5 bong7-pai5 e5 siong7, tioh8 sia2 kong-ma2 e5 mia5-ji7 jip8 ti7 kiaN3-lai7 kui3 ti7 thiaN-nih8, soa3 hian2-beng5 chin chun-keng3 chin tui3-tiong7 e5 khoan2.  
3.  Mui2 ni5 tioh8 u7 tiaN7-tioh8 bou2 jit8 choe3 bou2 kong-ma2 e5 ki3-liam7 jit8.  Hit jit8 tioh8 chhiaN2 thoan5-to7 sian-siN kau3 in chhu3 choe3 tek8-piat8 ka-teng5 e5 le2-pai3. Hit jit8 thoan5-to7 sian-siN u7 ho2 ki-hoe7 thang khoan3-bian2 in ke-lai7 e5 toa7 se3, ia7 thang po3-iong5 to7-li2 hou7 hiah e5 chhu3-piN lin5-iu7 lai5 khoaN3 e5 lang5 thiaN.  Na7 pi7-pan7 tam7-poh8 lim-chiah8 e5 su7 ia7 si7 bo5 siong-kan, in-ui7 lim-chiah8 e5 su7 m7 si7 siok8 ti7 iN7-toan : put-ko3 m7 thang eng7 chiu2. 
 4. Tioh8 khi3 sun5 kong-ma2 e5 bong7. Pun2-to2 lang5 e5 sip8-koan3 mui2 ni5 ti7 chheng-beng5 cheng5 au7, chiu7 e-e tin7-tin7 khi3 thiong2-pou sun5 in kong-ma2 e5 bong7; sou2-i2 lan2 ia7 hit si5 lai5 khi3 sun5 bong7, an2-ni si7 chin ho2 ki-hoe7. 
 TaN lan2 sin3-tou5 khi3 sun5 bong7 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 ? 
1.Tioh8 chio thoan5-to7 sian-siN choe3-tin7 khi3. 
2. Tioh8 kui2-na7 ke lian5-hap8 tang5 chit8 jit8 khi3 sun5, chiu7 thoan5-to7 sian-siN e5 kang khah chio2. 
3. Tioh8 toa3 chhiN-hoe a2-si7 sim2-mih8 chhiu7-pha khi3 hian3 ti7 kong-ma2 e5 bong7, lai5 hian2-beng5 ki3-liam7 e5 chong-chek. 
4. Kau3 bong7 e5 sou2-chai7 tioh8 gim5-si ki5-to2, thoan5-to7 sian-siN tioh8 khoaN3-bian2 in e5 ka-chok8, ia7 sun7-soa3 pou3-to7 hou7 hiah e5 oa2 lai5 khoaN3 e5 lang5 thiaN. 
5. Na7 pi7-pan7 tam7-poh8 piaN2 a2-si7 moa5-chi5 lai5 pun hou7 hiah e5 chhi7-gu5 gin2-a2, ia7 si7 put-chi2 u7 sim-sek.  
Gu5-kian3 pai5-liat8 chit kui2 hang7 hou7 tai7-ke chham-kho2, na7 u7 tang5-i3 ng3-bang7 tai7-ke lai5 sit8-heng5, na7 u7 sim2-mih8 tioh8 ke2-oaN7, chhiaN2 lin2 tioh8 chhut-thau5 lai5 chham-siang7.  Bok8-tek si7 beh li7-ek kau3-hoe7, ng3-bang7 hou7 lang5 khah ai3 lai5 jip8 lan2 Ki-tok-kau3.  
Teng2-bin7 sou2 pai5-liat8--e5 ia7 bo5 hai7 tioh8 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 kau3-li2, hoan2-tng2 u7 thang pang-chan7 Pun2-to2 e5 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 chu2-tit8 lai5 in2-chhoa7 in chin7 choe3 kiaN2-sun e5 gi7-bu7.  Goa2 bat ti7 pou3-to7 e5 si5 pai5-liat8 teng2-bin7 sou2 sia2 hit kui2 hang7 hou7 lang5 thiaN, thiaN-liau2 u7 lang5 chiu7 kong2, Sian-siN thiaN li2 sou2 kong2, lun7-kau3 kong-ma2 e5 su7 lai5 kai2-liong5 chhin-chhiuN7 li2 sou2 kong2 an2-ni si7 chin ho2, m7-ku2 goan2 m7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 lin2 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 lang5 u7 an2-ni sit8-heng5.  Lin2 si7 kan-ta chhui3 teh kong2 na7-tiaN7.  
Ai-ah ! lan2 kiam2 thang hou7 in an2-ni hiam5 mah ? Lan2 kiam2 m7 tioh8 kin2 lai5 sit8-heng5, hian2-chhut lan2 ia7 u7 teh ki3-liam7 lan2 e5 chou2-hu7 mah ? KoaN-bin5 oe7 kham-tit tui3 i2-keng si2-liau2 e5 tai7-chiong3 e5 siong7 e5 bin7-cheng5 lai5 kiaN5-le2, lan2 choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5 lang5 kam2 boe7 kham-tit tui3 lan2 pe7-bu2 e5 siong7 e5 bin7-cheng5 kiaN5-le2 mah ? Kok-ka oe7 siat bou2 jit8 ki3-liam7 bou2 chiong-kun e5 jit8, lan2 kam2 boe7 siat bou2 jit8 lai5 ki3-liam7 lan2 e5 pe7-bu2 mah ?  
Gu5-kian3 sia2 chit kui2 ku3 u7 khah toa7 taN2, ng3-bang7 bo5 hap8-i3 e5 sian-siN tioh8 saN sia3-bian2.  A2-si7 u7 lang5 ai3 soa3-chiap lai5 gi7-lun7 chit chan5 e5 su7, chhiaN2 in tioh8 chhut-thau5 m7 thang tiam7-tiam7, ng3-bang7 kau3-boe2 tai7-ke keng2 hit e5 te7-it ho2 e5 gi7-lun7 lai5 than3.  A2-si7 ti7 chu7-hoe7 e5 si5 chhiaN2 tai7-ke tioh8 chim-chiok chit chan5 e5 su7. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 422 koan3, 1920 ni5 5 geh8)
