
Ki5-koai3 e5 Sin-bun5

(Lim5 Chheng-kiat ki3.)
Ti7 ku7-ni5 goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 phiN e5 sin-bun5 teh kong2 ti7 Chi-na2 bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 sin oaN7-lai5 e5 keng2-chhat-tiuN2, ioaN7-lai5 bo5 kui2-jit8 chiu7 u7 chhut nng7-khoan2 e5 beng7-leng7 hou7 lang5 put-chi2 gong5-ngiah8.  Chit8-khoan2 chiu7-si7 beng7-leng7 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 tioh8 pak8-kha.  Chu-kun thiaN liau2 put-chi2 ki5-koai3, in-ui7 hian7-si5 tak8-ui7 long2 si7 teh bian2-le7 hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8 liu5-kha, chaiN2-iuN7 hit e5 keng2-chhat-tiuN2 hoan2-tui3 leh ? Chin ho2 chhio3, ki5-sit8 i m7 si7 beng7-leng7 it-poaN e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 tioh8 pak8-kha.  Sou2 beng7-leng7--e5 chiu7-si7 hiah e5 choe3 chhiong-ki--e5 long2 tioh8 pak8-kha, ehN-koai3, an2-ni u7 sim2-mih8 koe2-seh ?  U7 lah, u7 saN hang7 chin thong.  Thau5 chit8 hang7 si7 beh hou7 jin5-ke e5 hu7-jin5 kap chhiong-ki u7 hun-piat.  Koh chit8 hang7 si7 beh hou7 choe3 chhiong-ki e5 lang5 ka-ki7 kian3-siau3.  In-ui7 u7 pak8-kha e5 ki3-jin7, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 koa3 pai5 chit8 iuN7.
Tui3 an2-ni ng3-bang7 choe3 chhiong-ki e5 lang5 e7 khah chio2.  Lou7-be2 chit hang7 koh-khah thong, chiu7 si7 beh hou7 hiah e5 m7-goan7 liu5-kha e5 lang5 hou7 i boe7 koh ian5-chhian5-tit.  In-ui7 i na7 iau2-ku2 teh pak8-kha chiu7 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 oe7 sai2-tit chiu7-kun7 i chhin-chhiuN7 chhiong-ki.  Chiau3 an2-ni lai5 ka7 i khoaN3, he sit8-chai7 si7 thian-jian5- chiok hoe7 chek-kek8 e5 ho2 hong-hoat lah. 
Te7-ji7 hang7 e5 beng7-leng7 chiu7-si7 hoan-hu3 lang5 tioh8 thi3 bak8-bai5, ai-o3 ! ai-o3 !An2-ni beh thai2 oe7 kiaN5-tit; thau5-chang thi3-liau2 to teh m7 koan3-si3 lah, taN lian5 bak8-bai5 ia7 kio3 lang5 tioh8 thi3 ?  Peng5-iuah2, chhiaN2 li2 bian2 hoan5-lo2, ki5-sit8 i m7 si7 hoan-hu3 it-poaN e5 jin5-bin5 tioh8 thi3, chiu7-si7 kan-ta beng7-leng7 choe3 he5-siuN7 e5 lang5 tioh8 thi3 bak8-bai5 na7-tiaN7.  HouN3, he u7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3 ah ?  ChhiaN2 li2 chi2-kau3 leh.  Khi2-kam2, goa2 khiok ia7 bo5 sim2-mih8 chin chai, chong2-si7 tai7-khai3 iu5-goan5 u7 saN-hang7 e5i-su3.
1.Si7 beh hou7 he5-siuN7 kap peng5-siong5 u7 ki3-jin7 thang hun-piat.  Khah cha2 Tiong-ko lang5 long2 u7 thau5-chang, na7 bo5 thau5-chang e5 chiu7-si7 he5-siuN7, taN tak8 lang5 i2-keng long2 piN5-piN5 long2 bo5 thau5-chang; sou2-i2 chiah beng7-leng7 he5-siuN7 tioh8 thi3 bak8-bai5, an2-ni chiah u7 hun-piat.
2. Si7 beh hou7 hiah e5 choe3 he5-siuN7 e5 lang5 thang ka-ki7 siong5-se3 siuN7-khoaN3.  Na7-si7 lip8-chi3 chiong-sin beh choe3 he5-siuN7, chiu7 tioh8 thi3 bai5, na7 bo5 chiu7 kok lang5 tioh8 to3-khi3 in chhu3 choe3 su7-giap8; an2-ni ia7 si7 ng3-bang7 choe3 he5-siuN7 e5 lang5 oe7 khah chio2.
3. Si7 beh hou7 he5-siuN7 kah m7-kaN2 thau choe3 phaiN2-su7.  In-ui7 i na7 hou7 lang5 liah8-tioh8 chiu7-si7 pin5-ku3 thang chai i si7 he5-siuN7, au7-lai5 chiau3 i kau3-hoat ka7 i pan7 choe7, chiau3 an2-ni khoaN3 to ia7-si7 chit8 hang7 put-chi2 u7 chhu3-bi7 e5 hoat-leng7.
TaN chhiaN2 liat8-ui7 e5 sin-su7 siok-lu2 khoaN3 lin2 beh keng2 to2 chit8 hang7 e5 chit-giap8 khi3 choe3 chiu7-si7 lah.
