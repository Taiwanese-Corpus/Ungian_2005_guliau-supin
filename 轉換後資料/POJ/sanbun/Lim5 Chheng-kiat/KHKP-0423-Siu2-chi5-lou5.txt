Bat khoaN3 sin-bun5 teh kong2 ti7 goa7-kok u7 chit8 e5 Siu2-chiN5-lou5 , i u7 chek-chu7 put-chi2 choe7 chiN5 khng3 ti7 chhu3-lai7 ,ia7 i sim-koaN siong5-siong5 teh chu3-i hiah e5 chiN5 in-ui7 kiaN liau2 oe7 sit-loh8 , Sou2-i2 siong5 siong5 chiong mng5 koaiN--teh , chiu7 theh8 chhut i sou2 chek-chu7 e5 chiN5 lai5 sng3 chit8 pian3 khoaN3--leh , sng3 liau2 chiu7 koh pau--khi2-lai5 khng3 . Chit e5 Siu2-chiN5-lou5 i2-keng chin lau7 lah , bo5 bou2 ia7 bo5 kiaN2 , in-ui7 i kiaN-liau2 chhoa7 bou2 siN kiaN2 oe7 khai liau2 i e5 chiN5 , i chi2-u7 chhi7 chit8 chiah eng-ko chiau2 ti7 chhu3-lai7 kap i choe3 phoaN7 na7-tiaN7 . Siu2-chiN5-lou5 na7 teh chiah8 png7 e5 si5 , hit chiah eng-ko ia7 kap i tiam3 toh-teng2 chiah8 , i na7 teh sng3 chiN5 e5 si5 , hit chiah eng-ko ia7 ti7 i e5 sin-piN thiau3-lai5 thiau-khi teh khoaN3 ; sou2-i tu5-khi3 hit chiah eng-ko i2-goa7 bo5 pat8 lang5 chai i e5 chiN5-gun5 khng8 ti7 to2-ui7 .      
U7 chit8 pai2 i teh sng3 chiN5 , sng3 liau2 hut-jian5 kiam2 put-chi2 choe7 , i e5 sim chin ki5-koai3 chin hoan5-lo2 . Keh kui2-na7 jit8 koh sng3 khoaN3 iu7 si7 koh-khah kiam2 , sou2-i2 hit-tiap8 i e5 sim chin hoan5-lo2 kau3 boe7 chiah8 boe7 khun3-tit , tok8-tok8 m7 chai i e5 chiN5 tui3 to2-ui7 sit-loh8 . U7 chit8 cha2-khi2 i cha2-cha2 khi2-lai5 khui mng5 hut-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 mng5-khau2 sng seh8 e5 teng2-bin7 u7 gin2-na2 e5 kha-jiah , i chiu7 siuN7 che si7 cha-mi5 u7 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 lai5 thau-theh8 i e5 chiN5 . Sou2-i2 kin2 me2 tui3 hit e5 kha-jiah tit8-tit8 theng5 tit8-tit8 chhe7 , Khi3 chhe7 kau3 chit8 tui ku7 chha5-liau7 thiap8 chin toa7-tui e5 sou2-chai7 . Ti7 hia u7 chit8 e5 khang i chiu7 aN3--loh8-khi3 khoaN3 lai7-toe2 ko2-jian5 u7 chit8 e5 chiaN5 chap8-he3 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na7 ti7-hia koaN5 kau3 gim5-gim5 chun ,i chiu7 kin2 ka7 khiu2 chhut-lai5 ia7 pun2-sin ng8--jip2-khi3 chhe7 khoaN3 i sou2 sit-loh8 e5 chiN5 u7 ti7-hia a2-bo5?Chong2-si7 chhe7 long2 bo5 , au7-lai5 chhut-lai5 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 chin koaN5 chin iau chin kho2-lin5 e5 khoan2 , i chiu7 siN-chhut lin5-bin2 e5 sim ka7 i chhoa7 to3--khi3 in chhu3 pi7-pan7 mih8 hou7 i chiah2 . I u7 chit8 jit8 teh peh chhiu7 thit-tho5 e5 si5 chhe7-tioh8 chit8 e5 chiau2-siu7 lai7-bin7 u7 chin choe7 kim poat8-a2 ti7--teh i chiu7 khi3 po3 Siu2-chiN5-lou5 chai . Siu2-chiN5-lou5 khi3 khoaN3 chiu7 jin7-tit si7 i cheng5 sou2 sit-loh8 e5 chiN5 ,chiu7 si7 teh sng3 e5 si5 hou7 hit chiah eng-ko thau-ka7 khi3 , taN koh tit-tioh8 chin hoaN-hi2 ; Sou2 i2 koh khah thiaN3 Bi2-jin5 , khoan2-thai7 i choe3 ka-ki7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 . Bi2-jin5 chin gau5 thak8 chheh ia7 u7 hou7 tang5-phoaN7 chio khi3 thak8 Chu2-jit8-oh8 . Bi2-jin5 thiaN to7-li2 put-chi2 siu7 kam2-kek , i chiu7 koat-i3 sin3 Ia5-sou . Siu2-chiN5-lou5 ia7 bo5 chou2-tong3 i . Bi2-jin5 16 he3 e5 si5 Siu2-chiN5-lou5 chiu7 si2--khi3 . I sou2 chek-chu7 e5 chiN5-gun5 long2 hou7 Bi2-jin5 tit--khi3 . Bi2-jin5 u7 siuN7 i sou2 tit-tioh2 e5 chiN5-gun5 beng5-beng5 si7 Siong7-te3 siuN2--e5 , sou2-i2 chiu7 lip8 chi3 beh chiong i pun-sin kap sou2 tit-tioh8-elai5 choe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 li7-ek . Au7-lai5 chiu7 chiong i e5 chiN5 kia3-hu3 chin choe7 ti7 le2-pai3-tng5 ,i koan2 ,hak8-hau7 , ia7 i pun2-sin koh jip8 Sin5-hak8-iN7 thak8-chheh chut-giap8 , choe3 kou-niu5 si3-koe3 po3-iong5 to7-li2 put-chi2 u7 li7-ek kau3-hoe7 . Siok8-gu2 kong2 , Lang5 teh choe3 thiN teh chhiau5 .Lan2 khi2-thau5 khoaN3-kiN3 Siu2-chiN5-lou5 teh siu2 chiN5 lak8 lang5 ai3 hiam5 i , chong2-si7 kiat-be2 lai5 khoaN3 iu5-goan5 si Siong7-te3 e5 i3-su3 .Chim-gian5 u7 kong2 , Lang5 e5 sou2 choe3 kau3-be2 long2 kui Siong7-te3 ti7-li2 .
