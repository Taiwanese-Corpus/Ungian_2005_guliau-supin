Li2 Tek-bun5 TiuN2-lo2 Sio2-toan7
Iap8 Kim-bok8
Li2 Tek-bun5 si7 Kim-pau-li2 e5 lang5, tui3 siau3-lian5 e5 si5-chun7 si7 keng-long5, choe3 seng-li2, it-seng khun5-khiam7 phok-sou3, kap lang5 chin ho2 kau-poe5.  Ti7 25 ni5 cheng5, Lau5 Tiong-kian Bok8-su u7 kap Iap8 Thou5-he2 Thoan5-to7 i2-kip8 goa2, khi3 Kim-pau-li2 pou3-to7, hit-tiap8 Li2 Tek-bun5 u7 thiaN-tioh8 to7-li2, sim kau3 kek8 hoaN-hi2. Chiu7 chio nng7 ui7 e5 sian-siN kau3 i e5 chhu3, kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i e5 lau7-bu2 kap i e5 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 thiaN.  Hit-tiap8 liau2-au7, i e5 lau7-bu2 kap ke-lai7 e5 lang5 ia7 tang5-sim kap I sin3 to7-li2.  I pun2-sin chiu7 chio i e5 peng5-iu2 tui3 Thoan5-to7-kiok8 chheng2 siat Kang2-gi7-sou2 ti7 Kim-pau-li2, Thoan5-to7-kiok8 u7 chun2 i sou2 chheng2.  Hit-tiap8 u7 phai3 IuN5 Chiong khi3 thoan5-to7, i chiu7 chhut-lat8 tau3 pang-chan7 i, tui3 an2-ni kau3-hoe7 jit8-jit8 chin3-pou7.  Au7-lai5 u7 hou7 kau3-hoe7 keng2 i choe3 TiuN2-lo2, i chin chhut-lat8 pang-chan7 kau3-hoe7. I si7 khun5-khiam7 e5 lang5, tui3 kau3-hoe7 tak8 hang7 e5 ian5-hang7, i chin khek-ki2 lai5 hian3, gau5 ho2 khoan2-thai7 lang5, ai3 kap lang5 tam5-lun7 to7-li2.
Iau2-be7 sin3 to7-li2 i2-cheng5, i e5 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 sou2 seng-san2--e5 long2 boe7 oah8, kau3 sin3 to7-li2 au7, chiu7 seng-san2 chit8 ui7 ta-pou kiaN2 mia5 Thian-su3, u7 khi3 Lai7-toe7 liu5-hak8 tng2-lai5, hian7-si5 ti7 Koe-lang5 chhi7-iah8-sou2 choe3 Siu3-i.  Kin-ni5 u7 chou-chit Tiong-iong chhi7-tiuN5, hian7-si5 u7 choe3 Sia7-tiuN2, tui3 kau3-hoe7 iu5-goan5 put-chi2 koan-sim.  Au7-lai5 u7 koh tit-tioh8 chit8 ui7 e5 ta-pou kiaN2 mia5 Chhun-seng, hian7-si5 khi3 Lai7-toe7 liu5-hak8.  I ti7 Chiau-ho5 7 ni5 chiu7 khi2 chit8 keng pai3-tng5 thiaN ti7 Kim-pau-li2 lai5 hian3, hoe7-iu2 chiu7 khi2 chit8 keng siok-sia3.  Kau3 pai3-tng5 lok8-seng5 au7, i chiu7 kap i e5 kiaN2 choe3-tin7 khi3 toa3 ti7 Pang-kio5, chong2-si7 i iu5-goan5 chin khoa3-lu7 Kim-pau-li2 kau3-hoe7, tak8 ni5 iu5-goan5 toe5-ian5 choe3 hit kau3-hoe7 e5 keng-hui3. Chin kho2-sioh, ti7 ku7-ni5 chiu7 khi2 piN7, seng-khu keng-ke3 choe7-choe7 kan-khou2.
I tu2-itoh8 kan-khou2 e5 si5, ka-ki7 ti3-i3 ki5-to2, ia7 kio3 i ke-lai7 e5 lang5 ki5-to2.  Beh li7-piat8 se3-kan i2-cheng5, i e5 kiaN2, sim-pu7, TiuN2-lo2 u7 kap i le2-pai3, i tak8 pai2 kong2 tioh8 thoe3 i jin7-choe7.  I e5 sim-pu7 kong2, A-pa li2 tui3 kau3-hoe7 u7 chiah jiat8-sim, m7 bian2 khoa3-i3.  I kong2, m7-thang an2-ni khoa-khau2, tioh8 jin7-choe7.  Kau3 8 geh8 8 jit8, hit e7-pou 2 tiam2 an-jian5 kui thian. Kau3 8 geh8 16 jit8, ti7 pun2-tng5 choe3 ko3-piat8-sek.  Chit jit8 u7 tui3 pun2-tng5 kap hu7-kun7 kap kui2-na7 ui7 kau3-hoe7 Bok8-su, thoan5-to7, tiong2-chip, hoe7-iu2 kap Ui2 Bok8-su, Kai Sian-siN, i2-kip8 Koe-lang5-chhi7 chhi7-un2 tai7-li2, kap choe7-choe7 ui7 e5 koaN-oan5, kap toe7-hng e5 iu2-lek8-chia2, ia7 u7 choe7-choe7 hoe-khoan5, tiau3-ki5, tiau3-tian7, tiau3-su5, sit8-chai7 si7 chin seng7-hoe7, che si7 hian2-beng5 i choe3 lang5 e5 kiat-ko2.  Beh li7-khui se3-kan i2-cheng5, u7 kau-tai3 i e5 kiaN2 tioh8 hian3 tam7-poh8 e5 chiN5, lai5 choe3 Koe-lang5 Kim-pau-li2 kau3-hoe7 e5 ki3-liam7 hian3-kim.  Sit8-chai7 si7 chin thang siau3-liam7 e5 TiuN2-lo2, u7 ho2 bou5-iuN7 thang hou7 lang5 oh8. Kiu5 Chu2 pang-chan7 i e5 kiaN2-ji5, oe7 chiap-soa3 i e5 kha-pou7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 607 koan3, 1935 ni5 10 geh8)
