


 
Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Koa
Tan5 Chhong-hoat
Liat8-ui7 hiaN-chi2.  Lan2 chu7 kau3-taN iau2 bo5 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 koa, kiam2 bo5 kam2-kak tui3 ka3 Peh8-oe7-ji7 u7 khun3-lan5 mah?  Goa2 chin kam2-kek ke3-khi3 e5 sian-poe3 kap lau7-su e5 ui2-tai7, m7-na7 hou7 goa2 oe7 hiau2 Peh8-oe7-ji7, koh hou7 mui2 chit8 e5 chhin-kun7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 lang5 long2 hou7 i kap in saN-bat, ti7 chia ai3 piau2 kui2-ku3 tui3 in seh-sia7.
Goa2 bat siuN7 kong2, pat8 e5 bin5-chok8 (如日本,英國) long2 u7 in e5 ji7-bu2 koa, ia7 chin ai3 phian chit e5 koa, kam2-kak tui3 kau3-hak8-siong7 ke chin li7-pian7, ia7 hou7 soe3-kiaN2 a2-si7 tu2-chiah lai5 oh8 thak8 e5 lang5 khoai3 ki3 ti7 nau2-lai7, koh ng3-bang7 hit e5 hau7-ko2 ju2-ho2.  Tu2-tu2 teh siuN7 chit e5 tai7-chi3, goan2 u7 chhoa7 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 hak8-seng khi3 Liu2-goan5 kau3-hoe7 oan2-chiok, khoaN3-kiN3 in u7 an2-ni choe3, ia7 hit e5 seng5-chek pi2 ke3-khi3 ke chin u7 khoaN3-kiN3 chin3-pou7, au7-lai5 goan2 ia7 an2-ni sit8-eng7, ko2-jian5 u7 iaN2.  TaN goa2 ai3 chiong tui3 Liu2-goan5 kau3-hoe7 sou2 oh8 hit e5 koa pun hou7 tai7-ke, ng3-bang7 tai7-ke chhi3 khoaN3-mai7.  Koh bo5 ku2 Chhun-kui3 hak8-hau7 ia7 beh kau3 lah, chhiaN2 tai7-ke eng7 oan2-tai7 e5 ke3-ek8 lai5 sau3-tu5 bun5-bong5 e5 lang5, m7-na7 an2-ni, keng3 ka hou7 khah choe7 lang5 lai5 bat Siong7-te3 e5 oe7, si7 goa2 sou2 ng3-bang7.
