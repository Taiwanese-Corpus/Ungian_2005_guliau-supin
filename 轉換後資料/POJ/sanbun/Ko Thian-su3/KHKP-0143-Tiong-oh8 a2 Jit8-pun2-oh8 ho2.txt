
Tiong-oh8 khah ho2 ,a2-si7 Jit8-pun2-oh8 khah-ho2 ?

( Ko Thian-su3 sia2 )   
 Kin7-lai5 u7 lang5 giau5-gi5 Tiong-oh8 bo5 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7 , phah-sng3 kiaN2-ji5 hou7 i jip8 Jit8-pun2-oh8 khah u7 lou7-eng7 ; sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 sia2 kui2-ji7 po3 cho3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5  chai,  hou7 in khah ho2 phah-sng3 .   
Chiau3 goa2 siuN7 Tiong-oh8 si7 put-chi2 khah-ho2 .   Oh8 Jit8-pun2 oe7 si7 chin-ho2 , chong2-si7 cho3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5 tioh8 tai7-seng phah-sng3 kui2-na7 hang7 .   
1 . Tioh8 ng3-bang7 kiaN2-ji5 ho2 kiaN2 . KiaN2-ji5 na7 phaiN2 , Jit8-pun2 oe7 gau5 kong2 u7  sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7, kiam2 m7-si7 hou7 pe7-bo2 koh-khah hoan5-lo2 mah ?  Beh hou7 kiaN2-ji5 ho2-kiaN2 , tioh8 hou7 i jip8 Tiong-oh8 , ti7-hia tak8-jit8 ka3-si7 to7-li2 , put-si5 u7 ho2 e5 sin-seN , ho2 e5 peng5-iu2 thang kau-poe5 , an2-ni put-chi2 khah u7 li7-ek ti7 phin2-heng7 .  Jit8-pun2-oh8 si7 se3-siok oh8 , hak8-seng long2-si7  se3-siok8-lang5 , e7 in2-chhoa7 lan2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 oh8 phaiN2 .  Sui-bong2 sin-seN kiam2-chhai2 cheng3-keng, m7-ku2 ia7 bo5 thang ka3-si7 lan2 Kiu3-chu2 e5 to7-li2 . Se3-siok8 lang5 khi3 jip8 hit-ho7 oh8 u7 li7-ek , lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 lai5 jip8 hit ho7 oh8 khah toa7-bin7 si7 bo5 li7-ek .  Cho3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5 na7 siuN7 ku2-tng5 e5 tai7-chi3 , chiu7 tioh8 ng3-bang7 kiaN2-ji5 ho2 kiaN2 khah iau3-kin2 .   
2 . Tioh8 siuN7 Tiong-oh8 si7 hak8-bun7 e5 oh8. Lang5 na7 bat thiN-e7 e5 tai7-chi3, e7  hiau2-tit thian-bun5 te7-li2 ,  soan3-hak8  thong-tat8 , bun5-ji7 chheng-chho2 , an2-ni chiu7-si7 hak8-bun7 . Na7 an2-ni bo5 lun7 cho3 sim2-mih8 kang long2 u7  lou7-eng7 .  Siat-su2 bo5  kau3 chin chhim , na7 tai7-liok8  bat , li7-ek ia7-si7 put-chi2 toa7 .  Tiong-oh8 u7 ka3 chit-ho7 , put-ko3 bo5 chhim na7-tiaN7 .   Chong2-si7 te7-ki na7 phah kian-kou3 , au7-lai5 beh kang2-kiu3 khah-chhim chiu7 u7 ki-hoe7 .  Jit8-pun2-oh8 hian7-si5 long2 bo5 ka3 chit-ho7 , siat-su2 au7-lai5 na7 u7 , lan2 Tiong-oh8 phah-sng3 ia7 be7 khah-su i .  In-ui7 soan3-hak8 , thian-bun5 , te7-li2 , eng7 peh8-oe7 lai5 ka3 , si7 khah-kin2 . eng7 Jit8-pun2 oe7 lai5 ka3 , si7 khah oh .  Lan2 na7 beh hou7 kiaN2-ji5 cho3 chin-chiaN3 u7 hak8-bun7 e5 lang5 , goa2 phah-sng3 tioh8 hou7 i tai7-seng lai5 thak8 Tiong-oh8 .    
3 . Tui3 Tiong-oh8 chhut-khi3 m7-si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 .  Chin che7 lang5 phah-sng3 Tiong-oh8 thak8-liau2 khang-khang ;  an2-ni  phah-sng3 si7 chin-chho3 .  Lang5 tit-tioh8  ti3-sek, beh thai2 u7 khang-khang ?  Li2 khoaN3 i be7 than3-chiN5 , i  chiN5 than3 ti7 pak-tou2-lai7 ! Ki3-jian5 u7 ti3-sek, chiu7 bo5 lun7 cho3 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 , long2 khah-iaN5 bo5 ti3-sek e5 . Ti3-sek si7 khah ho2 kim-gin5 po2-gek8  ( Chim-gian5 te7  8 chiuN , 11 , 19 chat ) .  
U7-lang5 phah-sng3 oh8  Jit8-pun2-oe7 thang cho3 thong-ek8-koaN , chit8-goeh8-jit8  than chap8-ji7-khou .  Lang5 m7-bian2 Jit8-pun2-oe7, na7 u7 ti3-sek cho3 seng-li2 ,  chit8-goeh8-jit8 kiam2 na7 than3 chap8-ji7-khou  ?  Jit8-pun2-oe7 chiong-lai5 bat e5 lang5  e7 na2-che7 , thong-ek8-koaN kiaN-liau2 e7 loh8-ke3 .  Ti3-sek bo5 loh8-ke3 , si7 e7 na2  khi2-ke3 . Goa2 kong2 oh8 Jit8-pun2 oe7 si7 ho2 , na7 u7 ki-hoe7 chiu7 tioh8 bong oh8 ; put-ko3 na7 oh8 cho3 lan2 pun2-sin e5 lou7-eng7 si7 ho2, na7 oh8 cho3 thong-ek8-koaN kiaN-liau2 m7-si7 ng3-bang7 khoaN3-oah8 e5 tai7-chi3 . N`g-bang7 cho3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5 chim-chiok siuN7 .   
Chiau3 goa2 gu5-gong7 e5 i3-kian3 , thak8 Tiong-oh8 si7 khah tioh8-hoat , e7 hou7 gin2-a2 khah ho2 phin2-heng7 khah u7 ti3-sek . Hian7-si5 e5 Jit8-pun2 oh8 kiaN-liau2 boe7 u7 an2-ni . Tiong-oh8 khah-ho2 , chit-ku3 goa2 phah-sng3 un2-tang3 thang kong2 .  N`g-bang7 na2-ku2 e7 na2 ho2 , sin-seN na2 gau5 ka3 hak8-seng , thak8-chheh e5 hoat-tou7 na2 chiau5-pi7 , chiu7 lan2 kau3-hoe7 bo5 lang5 ai3 khi3 jip8 khi3 pat8-keng oh8 .
