
Lun7 un-ek8 e5 su7
Ko Thian-su3
Un-ek8 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 kang3 e5 chai-ho7, ai3 kio3 chheN2 lang5 e5 sim.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chai7-cha2 I2-sek-liat8 peh-seN3 ti7 khong3-ia2 bat tu2-tioh8 un-ek8 ( Bin5 . 25 : 9 ) , Tai7-pit8-ong5 e5 si5 Ia5-ho5-hoa ia7 bat kang3 un-ek8 ( I I Sat . 24 : 15 ).  Che long2-si7 hian2-beng5 lang5 tek-choe7 Siong7-te3,  Siong7-te3 chiu7 siu7-khi3 , kang3 chai-e7 ti7 in tiong-kan .     
Na7-si7 an2-ni, sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5, Siong7-te3 kiam2 u7 siu7-khi3 in mah ?  Chit hang7  goa2 m7-kaN2 koat-toan3 kong2.  Chong2-si7 goa2 chai sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 na7 chin-chiaN3  keng3- khian5 hok8-sai7 Chu2, Siong7-te3 tek-khak bo5 siu7-khi3 in . TaN na7 si7 lang5 keng3-khian5 hok8-sai7 Chu2, in iu5-goan5 e7 tu2-tioh8 un-ek8 mah ? E7 .  Chu2 si7 kiu3 lan2 li7-khui choe7-ok, iau2-boe7 hou7 lan2 li7-khui se3-kan ( Iok- han7 17 : 15 ).  U7 chit8-jit8 i e7 hou7 lan2 li7- khui se3-kan, kap I eng2-eng2 saN-kap tiam3 , long2 bo5 u7 peN7-thiaN3 si2-sit e5 tai7-chi3 . Chong2-si7 hit e5 si5 iau2-boe7 kau3, lan2 iau2 ti7 se3-kan e5 si5, iu5-goan5 tioh8 kap se3-kan e5 lang5 peN5-peN5 saN-kap tu2-tioh8 kan-khou2. Goa2 chit-pai2 lai5 Tng5-soaN , tham3-thiaN chit-peng5 e5 siau-sit, chai chit e5 Ou-si2-cheng3, Chiang Choan5 tak8 sou2-chai7  chha-put-to long2 u7.  E7-mng5 put-chi2 che7, Tang5-oaN ia7 chin che7; Chioh8-be2 chin siong-tiong7. Chiang-chiu tu2-a2 teh heng.  Ki5-u5 chhin-chhiuN7 Choan5-chiu OaN-hai2 , i2-kip8 koe3-chui2 e5 hiuN-sia7, long2 soaN3-soaN3 u7.  Ti7 chit e5 un-ek8 e5 tiong-kan, lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 sit-loh8 bo5 chio2.  Che hou7 goa2 e5 sim thang3-thiaN3, put-tek-i2 tioh8 sia2 kui2- ji7 , thong-ti lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 .    
Goa2 chai Tai5-oan5 hian7-si5 u7 un-ek8, ia7 chin hoaN-hi2 boe7 u7 thiaN-kiN3 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 si2 ti7 chit e5 un-ek8.  Chong2-si7 goa2 siuN7 tioh8 chin se3-ji7 , na7 bo5 chin7 lan2 e5 pun2-hun7, chiu7 kiaN-liau2 lan2 ia7 e7 jiam2-- tioh8.  Chiau3 lang5 sou2 kang2-kiu3 e5 hoat- tou7, u7 kui2-na7-hang7 si7 lang5 e5 pun2-hun7 sou2 tioh8 cho3 .    
 
Te7-it-hang7 , Tioh8 chheng-khi3-siuN3 . Goa2 chin ki5-koai3 chit-hang7 tak8-lang5  chai, chong2-si7 Tng5-lang5 chit8-pah-lang5 bo5 chit8 lang5 kam-goan7 cho3.  Sui-bong2 khui ioh8- tiam3 e5 lang5, te7-it beng5-pek8 chit e5 li2-khi3 , koh tak8 jit8 teh khng3 pat8-lang5 chheng- khi3-siuN3, iau2-ku2 ka-ti7 ia7 si7 bo5 chheng- khi3-siuN3 . Goa2 ka-ti7 ia7-si7 un-thun e5 lang5, put-ko3 ai3 oh8 chheng-khi3-siuN3 na7-tiaN7. Goa2 chai chheng-khi3-siuN3 si7 ho2 , sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 oh8 . Goa2 teh oh8 chheng-khi3-siuN3 e5 si5, chai beh chheng-khi3-siuN3 tioh8  kut-lat8, koh tioh8 liau2 tam7-poh8 chiN5.  Kut-lat8 chiu7-si7 tioh8 tak8-jit8 in-khin5 piaN3-sau3, khah m7-bat eng7 e5 mih8 tioh8 siong5-siong5 theh8 chhut-lai5 phak8-jit8 . Mng5-khia7, pin5- tou2 , bin5-chhng5, i2-toh , tioh8 siong5-siong5 jiu5-se2 .  Chau3-kha tioh8 siong5-siong5 piaN3, m7-thang chha5 a2-si7 chia3-phoh tiaN7-tiaN7 he7 chit8-ui7 long2-bo5 poaN-soa2 . Thou5-kha tioh8 siong5-siong5 se2.  Chhu3-teng2 chit8-ni5 tioh8 chheng2-thun5 nng7-pai2; chhin-chhiuN7 chit- ho7 un-ek8 e5 si5, chit8-nng7 goeh8-jit8 chiu7 tioh8 chheng2-thun5 chit8-pai2.  Sau3-te3 tioh8 sau3-kau3 tak8-ui7 chheng-khi3, m7-thang chek-chu7 la5-sam5 ti7 chhu3-lai7 . Seng-khu tioh8 siong5-siong5 se2, m7-thang chap8-goa7-jit8 chiah se2 chit8-pai2.  I-chiuN5 ia7 tioh8 siong5-siong5 se2, m7-thang chap8-goa7-jit8 chiah se2 chit8-pai2 saN.  Chu2-chiah8 e5 lang5 tioh8 chek-tok, m7-thang chhai3 se2 bo5-chheng-khi3 chiu7 loh8-khi3 chu2.  Toh-pou3-chiap m7-thang tih loh8-khi3 oe-a2.  Chhit- oaN2 e5 toh-pou3 tioh8 put-chi2 chheng-khi3 . Lai7-bin7 siau-thau2 e5  khi3-khu7 long2 tioh8 chin teng2-chin , tioh8 se2 chin chheng-khi3, koh tioh8 kham3 chin ba7.  Na7 e7 bian2-tit, chiu7 tioh8 leng7-goa7 he7 chit8-ui7, pang5-lai7 m7-thang khng3 chit-ho7 mih8.  Chhu3-lai7 e5 kau-am5 tioh8 siong5 eng7 chui2 chhiang5. M7-na7 chhu3-lai7 tioh8 chheng-khi3, chhu3-goa7 ia7 tioh8 an2-ni.  Mng5-khau2 m7-thang chek-chu7 pun3-so3, m7-thang hou7 lang5 un-thun . Na7 u7 kau-a2, a2-si7 chhau3-chui2-khut , tioh8 siat-hoat m7-thang hou7  i-e5 tok8 jip8 lan2 e5 chhu3-lai7.  Chiah e5 su7 put-ko3 tioh8 kut-lat8 na7-tiaN7, long2 m7-bian2 liau2-chiN5. Na7 lang5 phah-sng3 kut-lat8 kan-khou2, bo5 kut-lat8 bo5 tek-khak e7 si2,hit-ho7 lang5 si7 bo5 chin7 pun2-hun7 , goa2 put-chi2 the3 i kiaN .    
Lun7 tioh8 liau2-chiN5, chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 sou2-kong2 chiah e5, na7  ka-ti7 bo5 kau3-giah8 kang thang-cho3, chiu7 tioh8 chhiaN3 lang5 lai5 pang-chan7. Chui2 tioh8  
eng7 khah-che7 , na7-bo5 kau3 tioh8 ke tam7-poh8 chiN5 chhiaN3 lang5 taN .  Seng-khu tioh8 eng7 iu5-khou a2-si7 sat-bun5 se2 , chiah khah e7 chheng-khi3 .  SaN ia7-tioh8 se2 sat- bun5 a2-si7 iu5-khou.  Bin7-pou3 toh-pouti3oh khah siong5 oaN7.  Siau-thau2 e5 khi3-khu7 , na7 eng7 siuN-ku2 , tioh8 hiat-kak8, koh be2 sin--e5.  Chhu3-lai7 na7 bo5 thang-hong, tioh8 chhiaN3 thou5-chui2 sai-hu7 lai5 ke-khui kui2-na7 e5 thang-a2, chiah e7 thong-khi3 .  Na7 chhu3 siuN-sip, la5-sam5 u7 tok8-khi3 , tioh8 soe3 pat8-keng , sui-bong2 khah ke chiN5 ia7 tioh8 hou7-i .  Che long2-si7 tioh8 liau2 tam7-poh8 chiN5 e5 tai7-chi3 .  Chong2--si7 siN3-mia7 si7 pi2 chiN5 khah kui3-khi3 , ai3 po2-hou7 siN3-mia7 e5 lang5, tek-khak tioh8 kam-goan7 liau2 tam7-poh8 chiN5 .  Teng2-bin7 sou2-kong2, long2-si7 lun7 chheng-khi3-siuN3 e5 tai7-chi3 .    
Te7-ji7-hang7 , Tioh8 eng7  kai2 tok8 e5  ioh8 . Chit-ho7 un-ek8 e5 tok8-khi3 , chiau3  lang5 kang2-kiu3 si7 u7 chit8-cheng2 e5 tok8-thang5, bak8-chiu khoaN3 be7-tioh8, tioh8 eng7  hian2- bi5-kiaN3chiah u7 khoaN3-kiN3.  Chit-ho7 tok8-thang5 na7 lip8 lang5 e5 seng-khu, chiu7  hit- e5 lang5 sng3 si7 jiam2-tioh8 hit-ho7 cheng3 , liam5-piN hoat-jiat8 hoat liap8-a2 , bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7-si2.  Chin li7-hai7 !  TaN u7 ioh8 thang thau7 chit-ho7 thang5 hou7 i si2 , chiu7-si7 Ka- pou3-lek8-sng kap kui2-na7 hang7 pat8-hang7 e5 ioh8 . Na7 eng7 chit-ho7 ioh8 hiu3 ti7 thou5-kha , chiu7 chhu3-lai7 khah bo5 tok8 ; na7 eng7 chit-ho7 ioh8 se2 seng-khu, chiu7 seng-khu bo5 tok8;  na7 eng7 chit-ho7 ioh8 se2 saN, chiu7 bo5 tok8; na7 eng7 chit-ho7 ioh8 piaN3 ti7 thang2- a2-lai7 chiu7 thang2-a2-lai7 bo5 tok8.  Siat-su2 lang5 na7 u7 chiN5, chiu7 tioh8 be2 chit-ho7 ioh8 khi3 eng7, chin-chiaN3 si7 u7 li7-ek, m7-si7 khang-khang .  Che si7 siam2-pi7 un-ek8 e5 te7-ji7 hang7 .    
Te7-saN-hang7 , Tioh8 chiah8-pa2 , chheng7-sio, cho3-kang m7-thang koe3-thau5 , khun3 m7-thang bo5 chiok-bin5 . Lang5 na7 iau khah ai3 jiam2-tioh8, lang5 na7 koaN5 ia7  khah ai3 jiam2-tioh8; lang5 na7 chhut-lat8 koe3-thau5, a2-si7 khun3 bo5 kau3-bin5, ti3-kau3 seng- khu sian7 , ia7 khah ai3 jiam2-tioh8 .  Chiah e5 ia7 put-chi2 iau3-kin2 .    
Te7-si3-hang7, Na7 un-ek8 siong-tiong7, chiu7-tioh8 chau2.  Chau2, te7-it un2-tang3, bo5 pat8-mih8 pi2 chit-hang7 khah ho2 .  Tioh8 chau2-khi3 bo5 un-ek8 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 un2 peng5-an .    
 
Te7-gou7-hang7 , Tioh8 ki5-to2 . Na7 lang5 chin-chiaN3 chiau3 goa2 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 lai5 kiaN5, chin7 pun2-hun7 i2-keng chin7, goa2 un2-tang3 kaN2 kong2 hit e5 lang5 khah be7 jiam2-tioh8.  Chong2--si7 goa2 m7-kaN2 kong2 i koat-toan3 be7 jiam2-tioh8 , in-ui7 seN3-mia7 si7  ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 chhiu2-thau5, seN-si2 long2 si7 I teh chiang2-koan2. Sou2-i2 tioh8 ki5-to2 . Lan2 u7 choe7, tioh8 jin7.  Siong7-te3 si7 lin5-bin2 e5 Siong7-te3, lan2 na7 hoan2-hoe2 choe7, eng7 seng5-sit8 khiam-pi e5 sim lai5 kiu5 I,ng3-bang7 I e7 soah I e5 siu7-khi3,lai5 thiaN lan2 e5 ki5-to2 .  Chhin-chhiuN7 chai7-cha2 lin5-bin2 hiah e5 ki5-u5 e5 peh-seN3, hoan-hu3 Chau5-biat8 e5  ThiN-sai3 “ theng5 i e5 chhiu2 ” , ng3-bang7 I chit-tiap8 ia7 lin5-bin2 lan2, hou7 chit- e5 un-ek8 kin2-kin2 soah , bo5 kau3 ti7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan. Che si7 goa2 sou2 toa7 ng3-bang7 . 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 147 koan3, 1897 ni5 6 geh8)
