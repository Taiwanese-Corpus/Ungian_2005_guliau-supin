Niau2-chhu2-peN7
H. Chiu
 Chit-ho7 peN7 si7 u7 chit8-cheng2 iu3-iu3 e5 mih8, ( u7 lang5 kong2 Se3-lan5-thang5 ,   a2-si7 kong2 , tok8 ) , jip8 lang5 e5 seng-khu , toa3 ti7 hoeh-lai7 seN-thoaN3 .  Chit-ho7 mih8 e5 seng- khu--nih8 u7 tok8; chit-ho7 tok8, e7 hou7 lang5 e5 chong7-hu2 chhut-hoeh , kap seng-khu hoat-jiat8 lai5 ti3-kau3 si2 . PeN7-lang5 e5 pun3, jio7, tham5, i2-kip8 i sou2-eng7 e5 mih8 , long2 u7 tok8 .    
Lun7 thoan5-jiam2. Niau2-chhu2 te7-it gau5 thoan5 chit-ho7 tok8.  In-ui7   niau2-chhu2 khoai3-khoai3 jiam2-tioh8, au7-lai5 thoan5 hou7 lang5. Hit-ho7 toa3-ti7 niau2-chhu2 seng-khu e5 ka-chau2 ka7 niau2-chhu2 e5 hoeh, chiah8-jip8 pak-tou2--lai7, au7-lai5 lai5 teng3-lang5 e5 si5, i u7 thou3-chhut, chiah koh suh , chiu7 chiong hit-ho7 tok8 chhin-chhiuN7 lai5 cheng3 ti7  lang5 e5 seng-khu.  Hou7- sin5 ia7 e7 thoan5 tok8, in-ui7 i ai3 si3-ke3 hioh , chiu7 i-e5 kha khoai3-khoai3 tiau5 tok8 , lai5 thoan5 he7 ti7 pat8-ui7 ; chong2-si7 i na7 jiam2-tioh8 tok8 ,chiu7 e7 si2 .Ki5-u5 bang2-a2, bat-sat , kau2-hia7 ,iu5- goan5 si7 chiau3 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 e5 khoan2; hek8-si7 ka7 lang5, hek8-si7 toa3 tok8 lai5 he7 ti7 lang5 e5 chiah8-mih8, a2-si7 lang5 seng-khu phoa3-khang e5 sou2-chai7 , long2 si7 chit-ho7 khoan2. Lun7 chit-ho7 tok8 jip8-lang5 e5 seng-khu, khah-siong5 si7 tui3 chiah8-mih8, i2-kip8 phiN7-khang, a2-si7 lang5 seng-khu u7 phoa3- khang e5 sou2-chai7 .    
Lun7 ti5-hong5 chit-ho7 peN7.  Chiau3 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 lai5 khoaN3 chiu7 chai , chiu7-si7 te7-it iau3-kin2 tioh8 liah8 niau2-chhu2 , na7 liah8- tioh8 niau2-chhu2 , i-e5 seng-khu sou2 u7 e5 ka- chau2 m7-thang hou7 i chau2-khi3 , tioh8  eng7 hoe2 ka7 i sio-khi3 . ( Chu7-jian5 tioh8 tai7-seng ka7 i phah-si2 , jian5-au7 thang eng7 hoe2 sio.) Chiah8-mih8 tioh8 se3-ji7 ; be7  ti7  ke-lou7 e5 mih8  m7-thang lam7-sam2 chiah8 . Chiah8-mih8 tek- khak tioh8 koe3-chu2, na7 koe3  chu2-kun2 si7 khah tho3-tong3 , in-ui7 kiaN-liau2  hou7-sin5 , kau2-hia7 , si3-ke3 so5, hioh, e7 toa3 tok8 lai5 ka-lauh8 ti7 hit-hang7 mih8. Sou2-chai7 tioh8 chhong3 chheng-khi3-siuN3;seng-khu tioh8 se2 ; saN-a2-khou3 ia7 tioh8 chheng-khi3; phoe7, chhioh8 tioh8 phak8. Chhu3-lai7 tioh8 hou7 jit8 chio3-jip8-khi3, ia7 boh8-tit kap hit-ho7 peN7-lang5  
kau-thong. Jit8-kng chin7 u7 lat8, m7- si7 jit8 e5 sio, si7 jit8 e5 kng lai5 chio3, chiu7 e7 hou7 hit-ho7 thang5 si2 . Chong2-si7 eng7 ioh8-chui2 lai5 se2, ia7 e7 hou7 thang5 si2.  Chong2-si7 ioh8 e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 m7-bian2 ki3, na7 chiau3 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 lai5 koan-hong5, ng3-bang7 e7 tu5-khi3 hit-ho7 peN7 . Siok8-ku2 kong2, Bo5-peN7 khah-ho2 bat ioh8 .    
Jit8-pun2-koaN kio3-lang5 liah8 niau2-chhu2, phah ioh8-chui2, a2-si7 hoe-chui2 , chhu3-lai7 tioh8 chheng-khi3, chiau3 an2-ni khoaN3 khiok si7 ho2-i3. Se3-siok8-lang5 m7-chai-iaN2; lan2 sin3 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 lang5 na7 chai, khiok tioh8 ka7 lang5 kong2, bian2-tit hou7 in kiaN- hiaN5, a2-si7 siu7-khi3 m7-hoaN-hi2 chit-ho7 tai7- chi3 . 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 216 koan3, 1903 ni5 3 geh8)
