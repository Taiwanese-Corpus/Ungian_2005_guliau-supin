Kou3 Ong7 TiuN2-lo2-niu5
Kui-jin5-pak Su-ki3
Toh Ong7 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 chhut-si3 chiu7 siok8 Chu2, 19 hoe3 chiah oan5-hun; hian7-chai7 si3 e5 hau7-siN3, saN e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, lai7-goa7 sun chap-gou7 e5.  Lun7 i ti7 se3-kan ka-teng5 gau5 tiau-tou7, tang5-sim, ho5-khi3, jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2.
Ti7 26 hoe3 tu2-tioh8 hoe2 sio-chhu3 e5 si5, chhu3, ngou2-kok, i-chiuN5 it-chin7 sio-khi3, chiu7 eng7 keng-chio-thau5 chhoah cho3 chhiam, phoe3 phoa3-pou3-chi2 koe3 jit8.  I e5 kiaN2 iau chiah8 be7 loh8-khi3, chiu7 khou2-khng3 i kong2, “ Li2 tioh8 thun-lun2 chiah8.”  Me5-jit8 kiu5-kio3 Chu2 kho2-lin5.  Keng-koe3 nng7 le2-pai3 chiu7 liam5-piN u7 lang5 sang3 tek thang khi2 chhau2-liau5-a2, ia7 u7 lang5 theh8 ku7 saN, han-chu5-chhiam, bi2, ia7 tit-tioh8 Song3 Bok8-su cho3 saN lai5 sang3.  Tui3 an2-ni ke-thiN i e5 sin3-sim, chin jin5-ai3 thiaN3 song3-hiong-lang5 kap chhu3-piN.
Kau3 Tai7-cheng3 goan5-ni5 lai5 tiam3 Pi-a2-thau5 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 in-tian2, than3 u7 chiah8 khin5-khiam7, kut-lat8, jiat8-sim, ti3-kau3 kin-a2-jit8 ka-teng5 ji7, saN-chap8 lang5, ti ji7, saN-chap8 chiah, chhan5-hng5 17 kah, gu5 si3-gou7-chap8 chiah, tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 moa2-moa2 e5 si-in.
Ti7 Sin-lek8 3 goeh8 6 ho7 hou7 Siong7-te3 tiau3 i li7-khui se3-kan, hiong2-siu7 55 hoe3.  Hoe7-chong3 chin-che7 lang5, boe7 sin3 e5 lang5 ia7 chin-che7 lai5 hui-le7.
