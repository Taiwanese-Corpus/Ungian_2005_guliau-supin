

Chu7-sin put-leng5 po2

(Lam5-a2-kheN, Gou5 Le2-ti3 ki3--e5.) 
TaN goa2 chiong chit-e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 lin2 chai, chiu7-si7 Hong7-soaN-koan7 siok8 e5 Koan-im-li2 u7 chit8 chng mia5-kio3 Kheng5-a2-na5, hia e5 lang5 hou7 Mou5-kui2 khou2-chhou2 kau3-kek8, kah in saN-ni5 tioh8 cho3 chit8-pai2 it-tiau5-chio3.  Sou2-i2 in kian3 tu2-tioh8 toa7-kho-ni5 chiu7 cho3 chio3; kin-ni5 i2-keng lun7-goeh8 chiu7-si7 toa7-kho-ni5, in chiu7 kng put8 lai5 khoaN3 jit8, ka7 in teng7 pun2-ni5 11 goeh8 13 jit8 khi2-kou2, chap8-si3 e5-hng jip8-chio3. 
Kau3-si5 in chiu7 khi3 tah chit8 cho7 toaN5 ti7 chhan5-nih8, hun lai7-goa7 toaN5, iah8 hun tang-sai toaN5, iah8 khi3 chhiaN2 sai-kong-thau5 seN3 Lim5 mia5 Gong7-hoan3, i toa3 ti7 Oan7-chau5-tiong; iah8 kap si3-e5 sai-kong-kha chham phah lo5-kou2 khok8-khiang e5 lang5.  In kau3, chiu7 pai5-toaN5 khi2-kou2 tiau3 chhak, goa7-toaN5 tiau3 saN pak, si7 Sam-kai3 sin5-siong7 e5 chhak.  Lai7-toaN5 tiau3 gou7 pak, si7 Sam-chheng kap Giok8-hong5, Chi2-bi5.  Iah8 ti7 tang-sai toaN5 tiau3 lak8 pak e5 Sip8-ji7-bun5-jin5 kap cheng3 sin5-beng5 e5 siong7. 
Hit-si5, hiah-e5 thau5-ke, lou5-chu2, kap cheng3 peh-seN3 chiu7 hoaN-hi2 pi7-pan7 toa7-ki5, niu5-soaN3, lo5-kou2, kou2-chhoe, toa7-kio7 lau7-lau7 jiat8-jiat8, cho3-hoe2 khi3 Kang-soaN-giam5 chhiaN2 Put8-chou2, soa3 chhiaN2 in pun2-chng kui2-na7 sian e5 put8, long2 chhai7 ti7 toaN5-nih8, i3-su3 si7 oa2-kho3 chiah-e5 cho3 cho7-chu2, ng3-bang7 the3 in chiuN7-thiN hun-sou3 ti7 Giok8-hong5.  In iah8 khi3 chioh 10-goa7 ki tau2-teng pai5 ti7 toaN5-nih8, tau2-teng tioh8 te2 bi2 tiN7-tiN7, bi2 e5 teng2-bin7 he7 teng-hoe2-choaN2 chit8-te3, koh thin5-iu5 moa2-moa2, teng-sim 12 tiau5, me5-jit8 tiam2-hoe2 m7-kaN2 hou7 i hoa--khi3, an2-ni si7 ho7-cho3 cheng-sin5-hoe2.  Koh ti7 toaN5-nih8 u7 Koan-im-soaN chit8-cho7, si7 eng7 choa2 kou5--e5, soaN-teng2 che7-che7 sian e5 choa2-put8; chiah-e5 put8 chai7-cha2 chin li7-hai7, chin pun2-su7 e5 iau-koai3, in-ui7 in e5 koan5-leng5 khah-su Koan-im, sou2-i2 chiaN3 hou7 i siu chiuN7 soaN lai5 cho3 chhiu2-e7.  ToaN5-cheng5 e5 siang-peng5-piN iah8 u7 nng7-sian choa2-put8, chit8-sian SoaN-sin5 khia5 chhiN-sai, chit8-sian Thou2-ti7 khia5 ng5-hou2; toaN5 e5 bin7-cheng5 u7 khia7 nng7-ki teng-ko, teng-ko e5 poaN3-tou7 u7 lui7 ng5-ki5 chit8-ki, sia2 si3 toa7-ji7 ‘KI5-KIU5-PENG5-AN’
aN in pan7 chiah-e5 mih8 long2 chiau5-pi7, sai-kong chiu7 khi2-kou2 chhiaN2 sin5 lai5 chai7 toaN5, hiah-e5 phah-khok8-khiang--e5 kap kou2-chhoe chiu7 che5-tan5 teh ho5, sai-kong chiah thak8-keng liam7-chham3. 
Kau3 14 e7-tau3, chiuN7-cho7 chin3-pio2, i3-su3 si7 chiuN7-thiN khi3 the3 peh-siN3 kiu5 peng5-an.  Liau2-au7 tui3 cho7-teng2 loh8-lai5 toaN5-lai7, tai7-ke teh thng3 i-hok8, u7-e5 thng3 iau2-boe7 liau2, hut-jian5 ti7 put8 e5 ka-chiah-au7 hoe2-toh8 chin iam7.  Tui3 an2-ni lai7-goa7-toaN5 tang-sai-toaN5 hiah-e5 put8, bo5 lun7 si7 Sam-chheng, Giok8-hong5, Chi2-bi5, Sam-kai3, Sip8-ji7-bun5-jin5, Ma2-chou2, Koan-im, SoaN-sin5, Thou2-ti7 kap hiah-e5 choa2-kou5--e5, chha5-khek--e5, thou5-sio--e5 toa7-sio2 e5 put8, i2-kip8 tau2-teng, sai-kong e5 kang3-i, lang2-taN3 kap long2-chong2 e5 mih8-kiaN7 it-chin7 hou7 hoe2 sio-lio--khi3.  Lian5 lai5 hia cho3 seng-li2--e5 kap lai5 khoaN3 cho3 chio3 e5 lang5, in e5 mih8-kiaN7 iah8 che7-che7 hoa3-hoe2--khi3. 
Hit-si5 tak8-lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 gong7-gong7 e5 khoan2-sit, sou2-i2 toaN5-lai7 e5 put8 i2-kip8 tak8-lang5 e5 mih8, long2 be7-hiau2-tit chhiuN2-chhut--lai5, iah8 m7-chai-thang soa3 toa3 chhut--lai5, chiu7 ki3-chai7 i sio kau3 chin7.
Chit-ho7 hoe2 sng3 si7 ki5-koai3 hoe2, iah8 si7 ho2-taN2 hoe2, chiaN3 kaN2 lai5 sio hiah-e5 Sam-kau3 e5 sian-thau5 kap Giok8-hong5-hai7-te3, Chi2-bi5-tai7-te3, Sam-koan-tai7-te3, Sip8-ji7-bun5-jin5, i2-kip8 Koan-im, Ma2-chou2, SoaN-sin5, Thou2-ti7, chham cheng3 sin5-beng5 long2 it-chin7 sio kau3 piN3 cho3 hoe2-hu. 
Lun7 Sam-chheng, na7 chiau3 Hong-sin5-chheh kong2, in e5 koan5-leng5 chin che7, cho3 sian--e5 long2 tioh8 pai3 in cho3 su-hu7, tak8-hang7 long2 tioh8 chheng2-kau3 in, iah8 tak8-hang7 logn2 hang5-hok8 in, in-ui7 in u7 che7-che7 hang7 e5 po2-poe3, e7 che3-ap in hiah-e5 sian-ka long2-chong2 e5 tai7-chi3.
