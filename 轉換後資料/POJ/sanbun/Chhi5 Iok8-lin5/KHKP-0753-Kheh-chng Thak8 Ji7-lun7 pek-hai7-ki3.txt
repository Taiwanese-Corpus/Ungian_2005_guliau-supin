Kheh-chng Thak8 “Ji7-lun7 pek-hai7-ki3”
Chhi5 Iok8-lin5 ki3
Ti7 Kau3-hoe7-po3 tui3 6~8 goeh8 lian5-chai3 Pa Bok8-su ti7 Ji7-lun7 e5 pek-hai7-ki3.  Sou2 kong2 hit e5 Ji7-lun7, m7-si7 teh chi2 ti7 Sai-le5 hu7-kin7 e5 toa7 chng-sia7, si7 teh chi2 ti7 Pin5-tong-koan7 siok8 Kheh-chng e5 sio2 hiuN-sia7.  Pit8-chia2 in-ui7 si7 Ji7-lun7 hu7-kin7 e5 lang5, ia7-si7 Kheh-lang5 e5 chit8 e5, tui3 chit e5 ki3-su7 put-chi2 koan-sim lai5 thak8.  Koe3-thau5 e5 kam2-kek, hou7 goa2 e5 sim chit8 bin7 chin kian3-siau3, chit8 bin7 si7 kong2 boe7 chhut e5 kam2-sia7.  Hiah e5 chit8-tin7 lang5 chit8-si5-tek e5 song2-khoai3 chiaN5-cho3 Kheh-lang5 eng2-oan2 e5 kian3-siau3; na7-si7 Pa Bok8-su sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 kian3-siau3 chiaN5 cho3 Siong7-te3 e5 eng5-kng.  Tng 1948 ni5 5 goeh8, Lai7-pou siat-kau3 50 chiu-ni5 le2-pai3-sek siong7, Ko Tok-heng5 Bok8-su e5 chiok-su5 tiong u7 kong2, “Cha2-ni5 ti7 Ji7-lun7 e5 chhau3-bi7 chiaN5 cho3 eng2-oan2 e5 phang-hiuN, pian3-moa2 ti7 choan5 Kheh-chng.” Ia7 Ban7 Bok8-su (Rev. Edsuerd Band) sou2 tu3 Pa Bok8-su e5 toan7-ki3 tiong ia7 u7 kui2-na7 iah8 Ji7-lun7 pek-hai7 e5 ki3-liok8.  Che kiam2 m7-si7 Tai5-oan5 thoan5-kau3 lek8-su2-tek e5 iau3-su7 mah?
Chin ki5-koai3 1865 ni5 6 goeh8 16 jit8 si7 Tai5-oan5 siat-kau3 86 chiu-ni5 e5 ki3-liam7-jit8, si7 Ma2 I-seng ti7 Tai5-lam5 KhoaN3-sai-ke siu7 pek-hai7 e5 jit8.  1885 ni5 9 goeh8 16 jit8 si7 Pa Bok8-su ti7 Kheh-chng tu2-tioh8 ok-seng3 e5 pek-hai7.  Ma2 I-seng e5 siu7-hai7 u7 seN-thoaN3 200 goa7 keng e5 kau3-hoe7, na7-si7 Pa Bok8-su e5 siu7-hai7 chi2-u7 seN chit8 keng Lai7-pou kau3-hoe7 na7-tiaN7.  Liat8-ui7, che si7 sim2-mih8 goan5-in?
Kong-hok8 au7 Tai5-oan5 si7 thoan5-kau3 siat-kau3 bu5-siong7 e5 ho2 ki-hoe7.  U7 loa7-ni7-che7 lang5 ti7 kang2-tai5, ti7 po3-choa2 ui7-tioh8 18 ban7 Ko-san, 7 ban7 PheN5-ou5 e5 peh-seN3 teh hoah-hiam3.  Tong-kiok8 ia7 put-chi2 koan-sim ti7 chit e5 kang, ia7 u7 siat San-te7 Soan-to7-hoe7, PheN5-ou5 Soan-to7-hoe7 teh chin3-heng5 hiah e5 kang.  Na7-si7 choan5 Tai5 60 ban7 Kheh-lang5 m7-chai u7 sim2-mih8 lang5 teh koan-sim?
Bat u7 bok8-su tui3 goa2 kong2, “Kheh-lang5” chit ku3 tioh8 pang3 bo5 khi3 chiah-ho2.  Liat8-ui7 ah! tng Jit8-pun2 si5-tai7 si5-kiok8 ti3-kau3 lan2 Tai5-oan5 lang5 tioh8 eng7 Jit8-pun2 oe7 lai5 le2-pai3 e5 si5, lan2 e5 sim-sin5 an2-choaN2? Hiah e5 liu5-hak8 ti7 Tang-kiaN e5 Tai5-oan5 lang5 kiam2 m7-si7 ia7 teh eng7 lan2 e5 oe7 teh le2-pai3 mah?
ChhiaN lan2 tioh8 chai Kheh-lang5 m7-si7 chio2-sou3, choan5 kok u7 500 ban7, choan5 Tai5 u7 60 ban7, na7-si7 chiah e5 pi2 Ko-san, PheN5-ou5 khah che7 e5 Tai5-seng2 Kheh-lang5 u7 sim2-mih8 lang5 teh koan-sim? U7 lang5 teh the5-chhiong3, bo5 lang5 chiap-lap8, u7 lang5 teh au5-kio3, bo5 lang5 beh thiaN, kiam2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 chiah kau2 teh pui7 kip-heng5 chhia mah? Sut8-chit8--e5 chiu7 koe3-khi3 bo5 tai7-chi3.
Ai3 ah!! Boe7 bian2-tit siuN koe3 kho2-lin5.  Koh chit8 pai2 ho2-taN2 tui3 tong-kiok8, sian-poe3, thok8-chia2 lai5 au5-kio3 kong2, ChhiaN2 koh lai5 goan2-bi7 Ji7-lun7 e5 phang-hiuN.  ChhiaN2 e7 ki3-tit “Kheh-chng” chit ku3 oe7, chhiaN2 siat-hoat 60 ban7 Kheh-chng kiu3-leng5 e5 kang.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 753 koan3; 1951 ni5 9 goeh8)
