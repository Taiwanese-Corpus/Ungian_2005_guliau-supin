Tek8-cheng2 Cheng-sin5-piN7 
Khng Ka-im
I-seng kong2, "Se3-kan bo5 u7 oan5-choan5 bo5 piN7 e5 lang5" "Bo5 piN7" chit ku3 m7-si7 choat8-tui3.  Tui3 goa2 e5 koan-chhat kap kam2-siong2 goa2 ai3 kong2, "Se3-kan long2 si7 cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2".  Bo5 cheng-sin5-piN7 "Chit ku3 si7 siong-tui3-tek e5 oe7, Seng3-keng kong2, "Long2 bo5 gi7-lang5, lian5 chit8-e5 ia7 bo5.........!
Cheng-sin5-piN7 tai7-ke chin kho2-lin5--i, in-ui7 na7 cheng-sin5-piN7 chiu7 piN3 cho3 bo5 lou7-eng7 lang5.  Chong2-si7 bo5 tek-khak, in-ui7 na7-si7 peh8-chhi a2-si7 lok8-thian-seng3 e5 cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2, kiam2-chhai2 pi2 ho2-lang5 khah heng7-hok, in e5 seng-oah8 si7 oan2-jian5 hai2-goa7 e5 san3-sian, ho2 lang5 boe7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 in hiah an-lok8, kiaN-liau2 si7 ho2-lang5 (ki5-sit8 si7 Tek8-cheng2 cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2) khah kho2-lin5.  Chit khoan2 m7-si7 Nau2 sin5-keng e5 piN7, chiu7-si7 seng3-keh to7-tek e5 piN7.  Cheng-sin5-piN7 e5 cheng2-lui7 put-chi2 che7; chong2-si7 Tek8-cheng2 e5 cheng-sin5-piN7 iau2 khah che7 (Lo5 1 : 29-31).  Lan2 oh-tit chai hit e5 chiong2-lui7 kap cheng3-chong7 kau3 thiat-ti2, chong2-si7 ia7 oe7 chai tam7-poh8!
1.  U7 chit8 khoan2 Cheng-sin5-piN7, si7 khah gau5 be7 ki3-tit.  Sou2-i2 Lou2 Ai-kong ka7 Khong2-chu2 kong2 u7 lang5 poaN chhu3 be7 ki3-tit poaN bou2, che sit8-chai7 chin hui3-khi3.  Chong2-si7 Khong2-chu2 ka7 i kong2 u7 koh khah tang7, ia7 koh si7 tek8-cheng2--e5, chiu7-si7 be7 ki3-tit ka-ki7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 Kiat, Tiu7 si7 lian5 pun2-sin to be7 ki3-tit, se3-kan lang5 tai7-khai3 si7 chit khoan2, gau5 khoaN3 lang5 e5 sit8-pai7, phoe-pheng5 lang5 e5 si7-hui, ka-ti7 khiok han2-tit chu7-chek.  Chin5 Si2-hong5 e5 si5, u7 chit8 e5 To7-kau3 e5 hong-su7 chau3 kong2, "Biat8-bo5 Chin5-kok e5 si7 Ou5".  Ki5-sit8 si7 khoaN3 ji7 se3 put-siau3 lai5 tiam2 chhiN2--i, ong5 soa3 liah8 cho3 si7 teh kong2 pak Ou5 e5 pian-hoan7, sou2-i2 cho7 Ban7-li2 tng5-siaN5 ai3 lai5 ti2-tng--in.  Bak8-chiu oe7 khoaN3 kau3 ban7-li2 siaN5-goa7 e5 tui3-tek8, be7 thang seng2-chhat kiong-teng5 lai7 e5 put-siau3 chu2, chit khoan2 kiam2 m7-si7 cheng-sin5-piN7 mah?  Ou5 ui5 loan7-chok lai5 lian5-lui7 kau3-hoe7, koh eng7 cheng2-cheng2 e5 ke2 pian7-hou7 lai5 kong2-chhut bo5 liong5-sim e5 oe7 e5 lang5, i e5 cheng-sin5-piN7 kiam2 m7-si7 khah siong-tiong7 mah?
2.  U7 chit8 khoan2 cheng-sin5-piN7 chiu7-si7 gau5 kong2 toa7 oe7.  Eng-kok u7 chit8 e5 song3-hiong e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 cheng-sin5-piN7 e5 si5, kong2 i si7 I-to-li7-a lu2-ong5, chiah8-png7, kong2 oe7, kiaN5 tah8 kek kah u7 chit8 e5 lu2-ong5 khoan2, pat8-lang5 khoaN3 liau2 chin ai3 chhio3.  Kiau-ngou7 si7 chit8 cheng2 e5 Tek8-cheng2 cheng-sin5-piN7, se3-kan lang5 kho3 i e5 hak8-bun7, kho-hak8, lai5 hou2-jin7 chong-kau3--e5 chiu7-si7 chit khoan2 e5 hoan7-chia2; ki5-sit8 tui3 seng3-cheng3 e5 hong-bin7 lai5 khoaN3, si7 gu5-gong7 ho2 chhio3.  Hoat-kok ti7 18 se3-ki2 toa7 kek-beng7 au7 m7 pai3 Siong7-te3, tui3 in e5 tiat-hak8 su-siuN2 kho2-an3 chit8 e5 li2-seng3 chong5-pai3, chong2-si7 kiat-kiok8 m7-si7 khah ti3-sek, chiu7-si7 m7 pai3 Chi3-chun e5 Siong7-te3, ai3 khi3 pai3 phaiN2 cha-bou2 lah! Ti7 Tiong-kok e5 lek8-su2 iu5-goan5 si7 an2-ni, khi2-thau5 chiu7 seng5-jin7 Siong7-te3, in e5 sin5-koan cham2-jian5 ko-siong7, kau3 Khong2-chu2 e5 si5 piN3-cho3 Thian, Chu-chu2 chiu7 kio3-cho3 Li2, Sou-sek chiu7 kio3-cho3 Thai3-khong lah! Ju5-kau3 e5 sin5-koan tit8-tit8 tui7-loh8.  Chi3-chun e5 Siong7-te3 jin7 boe7 beng5, soa3 kio3 i cho3 Kui2-sin5 kang3-hok khi3 lah! Kho2-lin5 kau3 kek8, kin-a2-jit8 joa7 choe7 lang5 ia7-si7 chit khoan2 pang3-sak Siong7-te3 khi3 pai3 ngou2-siong7 eng-hiong5, chu7-jian5, a2-si7 chu2-tiuN bo5 sin5, chu7-ko in tui3 keng3 sin5 u7 jin7-sek kap phoaN3 piat, ah! Kau3-kek8 e5 chho3-gou7 lah!
3.  U7 Cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2 put-chi2 ai3 kong2 ka-ti7 e5 gau5 lai5 hou7 lang5 thiaN, chit khoan2 si7 phin2-phong2--e5.  Hang7 U2 si7 eng-hiong5, chong2-si7 i si7 chit khoan2 hoan7-chia2, sou2-i2 kong2 "Hu3-kui3 na7 bo5 tng2-khi3 kou3-hiong (phin2-phong2) si7 chhin-chhiuN7 am3-si5 chheng7 sui2 saN teh kiaN5 lou7.  Cheng-hok8 400 goa7 chiu e5 kong-lek8, ui7-tioh8 an2-ni bo5-khi3".  Sou2-i2 Han5-seng me7 i si7 kau5 ti3 lang5 bo7.  Ai3 phin2-phong2 mia5-siaN, te7-ui7--e5, si7 chin che7.  Tak8-lang5 long2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Pi2-liap8-to kong2, "Kiam2 m7 chai goa2 u7 koan5......", sim2-mih8 lang5 beh chhin-chhiuN7 tit-kiu3 e5 Po2-lo5 khoaN3 hiah e5 cho3 pun3-so3, liah8 Ki-tok cho3 te7-it ho2 ah!
4.  Cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2 u7-e5 e7 phah lang5, boe7 kong2 cheng5-tit.  U7-e5 lian5 ka-ti7 e5 pe7-bo2, kiaN2-ji5 ia7 phah, sou2-i2 kou-put-chiong tioh8 kim3-kou3.  Se3-kan loa7 che7 ngou2-gek8 pe7-bo2--e5, bo5 beng5-li2--e5, bo5 chhin-lang5 cheng5--e5, bo5 lin5-bin2--e5, kiam2 m7-si7 cheng-sin5-piN7 mah! HiaN-ti7 sang3 kau hiaN-ti7 ti7 hoat-teng5, put-hau3 pe7-bo2, bo5 thiaN3 bou2-kiaN2, phah cha-bou2 kan2, heng5-hoat8 sim-pu7-a2; oan2-jian5 Cho5-chho3 kong2 leng5-khou2 goa2 kou-hu7 thiN-e7, m7-thang hou7 thiN-e7 kou-hu7 goa2.  Chit khoan2 e5 lang5 moa2-moa2 si7, sou2-i2 se3-kan chiah u7 chian3-cheng, sio-phah.  Kai-kip e5 tou3-cheng, kok-che3 e5 hun-cheng, hit e5 goan5-in ia7-si7 ti7-chia.
5.  U7 khoan2 e5 Cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2, siong5-siong5 giau5-gi5 lang5 beh hai7--i, kiaN lang5 phah--i; che si7 hiap8-pek-seng3 cheng-sin5-piN7.  Se3-kan loa7 che7 lang5 chhau3 sim-heng7, khoaN3 lang5 kong2 soe3 siaN oe7 si7 teh me7--i, toa7 siaN oe7 si7 teh hat--i.  Ti7 se3-kan bo5 poaN3 e5 peng5-iu2, chit khoan2 kiam2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Cho5-chho3 hit khoan2 to-gi5 e5 sim-koaN mah?  Hoa-to5 eng7 biau7-chhiu2 e5 i-sut8 beh i-ho2 i e5 thau5-hong, soa3 gi5 i si7 beh chhi3 i, he7 chhiu2 siu-sip8 i e5 siN3-mia7.  Cheng3-ti7-ka e7 tu2-tioh8 toa7 hoan2-tui3 si7 tui3 ta2-loh8, in e5 gui5-hiam2 si7 sim2-mih8 goan5-in.  It-chiong3 kong-seng5 ban7 kut kou e5 su7-sit8; m7 han7 tiaN7 ti7 chian3-tiuN5 na7-tiaN7, ti7 cheng3-ti7-kai3 na7 beh chhut chit8 e5 tok8-chhai5-chia2 m7 chai tioh8 kui2 ban7 lang5 cho3 i chheng3-sat e5 hi-seng! Ah! Choe7-ok sat-jin5 e5 se3-kai3 lah!
6.  Bat khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 ke-lou7 u7 koa3 chhui3-chhiu, oe7 hou2-niau liam7 siau2 oe7--e5, teh kiaN5.  He si7 ke2 siau2, siau2 beh chiN5; chong2-si7 chit khoan2 hoan7-chia2 m7-si7 chio2, pang3-sak it-chhe3 lai5 cho3 siu2 chiN5-lou5--e5, chin che7.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, Jin5 ui7 chai5 su2 sit8-chai7 u7 iaN2; siu2 chiN5-lou5 si7 si2 ti7 chiN5-nih, lai5 chong3 ti7 chiN5-nih8.  Tham-sim hou7 lan2 e5 bak8-chiu long2 khoaN3 chiN5 cho3 khit-chiah8, cho3 chhat8, kan-cha3 ia7 kam-goan7.  Bak8-chiu u7 khoaN3-kiN3 li7, chiu7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 gi7, u7 se3-kan chiu7 bo5 Ki-tok lah!
7.  U7 chit8 e5 lau7-lang5 chin phaiN2 phi5-khi3, i ti7 ke-lou7 teh be7 pau-a2, u7 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 ai3 ka7 i chhong3-ti7, chiu7 mng7 kong2 "Lau7-siok ah! Cho5-chho3 si7 chhoa7 loa7 che7 peng hou7 Kang-lam5 hoh".  In3 kong2, "83 ban7", "kiam2 m7-si7 72 ban7." "Teh kong2 saN2! 83 ban7 leh!" "Thian-thian-a2 72 ban7".  Lau7-lang5 toa7 siu7-khi3, cheN3 liau2 soa3 giah8 pun-taN khi2-lai5, ti3-kau3 i e5 mih8 long2 poah8-poah8 ti7 ke-lou7, piN-a2 e5 lang5 kong2 "lau7 peh ah! Sio cheN3 tai7, li2 e5 pau-a2 poah8 ti7 thou5-kha tioh8 khioh-khi2-lai5" I soa3 khi3 phut-phut kong2, "Poah8 ti7 thou5-kha, ia2 bo5 11 ban7 e5 lang5-be2 beh ka7 goa2 chhoa7 tui3 to2 ui7 khi3"; khi2 su-iok8 khoai3 siu7-khi3, chiu7-si7 chit khoan2 hoan7-chia2.  TaN ta2-loh8 chit8 hang7 khah iau3-kin2, se3-kan lang5 kiam2 m7-si7 pang3-sak iau3-kin2--e5, lai5 cho3 put-tiong3-iong7 e5 su7 mah! Bo5 tit-tioh8 Seng3 Sin5 e5 un-su3, ban7 su7 phin hiat-khi3 kap su-cheng5, pang3-sak kin-pun2 cheng-lun7 ki-hioh8; kau3 hoe7 lai7 saN kong-kek, kiam2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit e5 lau7-lang5 iau3-kin2 hiah e5 bo5-iaN2-chiah 11 ban7 e5 peng-be2 mah!
8.  U7 chit8 e5 Cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2 siong5-siong5 sia2 phoe hou7 Soan-kau3-su, beh tui3 i tho2 peng-tui7, chian3-chun5, hui-ki, i pun2-sin beh cho3 chong2-su-leng7 lai5 kap Gou5-lo5-su kau-chian3; che si7 i e5 cheng-sin5 chho3-loan7 sou2 siN-chhut e5 bong7-siong2.  Chit khoan2 e5 hoan7-chia2 u7-e5 ai3 chhong3 hoe2-chhia hou7 i thoat-soaN3, pang3 hoe2 sio chhu3 lai5 hoaN-hi2 khoaN3 hit e5 chham2 keng2; se3-kai3 e5 chu2 chian3-lun7 chia2 e5 su-siuN2 ia7-si7 an2-ni.  Ok-tok8 heng5-hiong long2 si7 tui3 chiah e5 chhut; chong2-si7 ok-tok8 e5 se3-tai7 ah! Tioh8 ui7-tioh8 beh lim5-kau3 li2 e5 sim2-phoaN3 kap heng5-hoat8, lai5 au5-kio3.
9.  U7 chit8 khoan2 e5 Cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2 khiok si7 be7-hiau2 hun-piat ho2 phaiN2, khoaN3-tioh8 Toa7-koaN i liah8 cho3 si7 khit-chiah2; khit-chiah8 chheng7 phoa3 saN i kong2 si7 Hong5-te3 chheng7 leng5-pou5, che si7 jin7-sek put-chiok; se3-kan lang5 loa7 che7 pai2 khoaN3 ho2 lang5 cho3 phaiN2 lang5 lai5 gek8-thai7--i, kiam2 ia7 m7-si7 an2-ni mah! Cheng-sin5-piN7--e5, khoaN3 lang5 chhoa7 sin-niu5 lau7-jiat8, liah8-cho3 si7 chhut-soaN hau3-lam5 hau3-lu2 teh che7 hun5-kio7, chiu7 khiN5 teh khau3.  KhoaN3 lang5 sang3-chong3, soa3 liah8-cho3 si7 chhoa7 sin-niu5, hah8-hah8 chhio3 hoah ban7-soe3, kio3 kiong-hi2; khiok m7-si7 kou3-i3, si7 i ai3 piau2 tong5-cheng5 be7 hiau2 tit na7-tiaN7.  Chong2-si7 se3-kan u7 loa7 che7 lang5, lang5 hoaN-hi2 e5 si5 bo5 tang5 hoaN-hi2, hoan5-lo2 e5 si5 bo5 tang5 hoan5-lo2, chit khoan2 e5 lang5 Ia5-sou ia7 siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 (Lou7-ka 7:31,32).  Oan3 lang5 pu3, chhio3 lang5 keng5; oan3 lang5 gau5, chhio3 lang5 gong7; ThiaN3 si7 thiaN kau3 lang5 iaN2, oan3 si7 oan3 kau3 kiu2-chok8.  Sui-jian5 bo5 u7 
 
cheng5-jin5 gan2-li2 kian3 se-si e5 chhok-kak, ia7 u7 thiaN3 chiu7 ai3 i oah8, oan3 chiu7 ai3 i si2 e5 phian-kian3, pat8-lang5 e5 chu-tiuN kap chin3-pou7, na7-si7 tui3 pun2-sin u7 put li7 kap put-khoai3, chiu7 put koan2 saN-chhit ji7-chap8-it "TaN2-to2" chiu7-si7 i e5 chu2-gi7 kap phiau-gu2.  Tiong-kok e5 kan-khou2 chiu7-si7 TaN2-to2 chu2-gi7" e5 liu5-tok8, sio2-khoa2 e7 thiN3-thau5--e5 chiu7 hoah "taN2-to2", liah8 chit ku3 cho3 eng-hiong5 e5 khau2-ho7, lam5-chu2 e5 chong3-gian5.  Na7-si7 chit khoan2 chiu7 u 100 e5 ChiuN2 Kai3-sek8 ia7 bo5 kau3 thang hou7 lang5 taN2-to2 lah! Cheng-sin5-piN7 tioh8 ti7-liau5, leng5-hun5 e5 piN7 koh khah tioh8 an2-ni.  Kou2-kim chi2-u7 Ia5-sou si7 oan5-choan5 e5 lang5, ki5-u5 long2 si7 Tek8-chiong2 e5 cheng-sin5-piN7 chia2, lan2 ai3 hok8-to3 i2-cheng5 hit khoan2 kian7-khong e5 cheng-sin5, tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 Boat8-tai7-liap8 e5 Ma2-li7-a lai5 chiu7-kin7 Chu2, siu7 koaN2-chhut long2-chong2 e5 kui2--A-phian3 kui2, poah8-kiau2 kui2.........--Chu7-jian5 kai3-si7 cheng-sin5-piN7 e5 toa7 piN7-iN7, Seng3-keng si7 i-tian2, Ia5-sou si7 chu2-ti7 i.  Tek8-cheng2 cheng-sin5-piN7 e5 kin-pun2 ti7-liau5 chi2-u7 an2-ni. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 Te7 616 Ho7, 1936 ni5 7 goeh8)
