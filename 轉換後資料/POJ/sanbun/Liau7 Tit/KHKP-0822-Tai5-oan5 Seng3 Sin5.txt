
Lek8-su2  Kou3-su7
Tai5-oan5 Seng3 Sin5
Liau7 Tit
Mou5-kui2 pek lang5 chiuN7 Thian-tong5.
Si3-chap8 goa7 ni5 cheng5, ti7 Tai5-lam5 e7, u7 chit8 sia7 kio3-cho3 Hai2-pou.  Chit chng che7-che7 lang5 lai5 pai3 Siong7-te3, kong2 si7 ui7-tioh8 u7 kau5-kui2 ti7 hia chin heng, lang5 hou7 i leng5-ti7 liau2 bo5 pan7-hoat, tong3-be7-tiau5, chiu7 lai5 jip8 Ki-tok-kau3.
U7 chit8 pai2, chit8 e5 chi2-moe7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ia7 khi3 hoan7-tioh8 kau5-kui2, hoat-jiat8 chin siong-tiong7.  Hit e5 chi2-moe7 chiu7 khi3 chhiaN2 Thoan5-to7 Tan5 Tiau5-beng5 Sian-siN beh lai5 ki5-to2 koaN2-kui2.  Boe7 kau3 e5 si5, hit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 hou7 kau5-kui2 chiuN7-sin, toa7 me7 Thoan5-to7 Sian-siN.  Tan5 Sian-siN kau3-ui7, chiu7 mng7 hou7 kau5-kui2 chiuN7-sin e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 kong2, “Li2 u7 kiaN Siong7-te3 bo5 ?” Hit e5 hoan7-tioh8 kau5-kui2 e5 cha-bou2-gin2-na2 in3 kong3, “KiaN!”  Thoan5-to7 Sian-siN kong2, “Na7 kiaN, chit ke si7 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, li2 an2-choaN2 kaN2 lai5 chok-long7--i ?”  Hou7  kau5-kui2 hu7-sin e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 kong2, “I pai3 Siong7-te3, tona2 u7, na2 bo5--teh, goa2 chiu7 lai5 koh8!!”
Thoan5-to7 sian-siN chiu7 the3 hit ke ki5-to2, ki5-to2 liau2, hit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 chiu7 cheng-sin5, m7-chai i tai7-seng teh me7 thoan5-to7 sian-siN, kap sou2 in3-tap e5 oe7.
▲ M7-bat Seng3-keng, kiN3 Chu2 kian3-siau3.
Ti7 45 ni5 cheng5, Tan5 Tiau5-beng5 Sian-siN kap goa2 khi3 Heng5-chhun thoan5-to7 es5i, ti7 Peh8-soa u7 Chui2-choaN5 Khu-tiuN2,Tan5 Kim-leng5 hiaN, in chit8 ke lai5 pai3 Siong7-te3 (Au7-lai5 Ko-hiong5 Pak-ia2-teng kap Chian5-kim e5 TiuN2-lo2 Tan5 Chui2-seng si7 i e5 sio2-ti7).  I e5 lau7-bo2 tak8 le2-pai3 kap kiaN2-sun kiaN5 khi3 Heng5-chhun le2-pai3. U7 chit8 pai2 phoa3-piN7 chin siong-tiong7, ka-ti7 teh siuN7 i kaN2 e7 li7-khui se3-kan? teh khoa3-lu7 i pai3 Siong7-te3 che7-che7 ni5-ku2, bo5 jiat8-sim oh8 thak8 Seng3-keng, nal7?-khui se3-kan, khi3 kiN3 Chu2 si7 chin kian3 siau3!  Sou2-i2 ti7 peN7-chhng5 tiong, kiN3-na7 khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 sun tui3 hak8-hau7 tng2-lai5, chiu7 kio3 in oa2 lai5 ka3 i ji7-bo2.
U7-lai5 phoa3-peN7 ho2 khi2-lai5, soa3 e7-Hiau2 thak8 Seng3-keng.
      ▲  Goa2 iau2 u7 iuN5.
Ti7 40 goa7 ni5 cheng5, Tau2-lak8 Chu-tan-oan, u7 chit8 e5 bo5-oe7 bo5 ku3 e5 lau2-sit8 lang5; chai7-lai5 kap sia7-hoe7 si7 bo5 kau-poe5, tui3 ki-tok-tou5 ia7 si7 bo5 siaN2 ong2-lai5, m7-bat khi3 le2-pai3.
Teh-beh lim5-chiong e5 si5, hoan-hu3 ie5 bou2, tioh8 chiau3 kau3-hoe7 e5 khoan2 ka7 itaN2-pan7; ia7 tioh8 chhiaN2 ki-tok-tou5 lai5 ka7 i cho3 chong3-sek.
Choe3-au7 kap i e5 ka-chok8 saN-si5, chin hoaN-hi2 kong2, “Chu2 kap nng7 e5 thiN-sai3 chheng7 peh8-saN, ti7 tiaN5-boe2 teh tan2 beh chhoa7 goa2 khi3 lah!!   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 822 ho7, 1957 ni5 6 geh8)
