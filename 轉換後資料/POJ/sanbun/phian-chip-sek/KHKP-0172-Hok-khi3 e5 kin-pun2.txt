
Hok-khi3 e5 kin-pun2
Chian5 bat u7 chit8 e5 hu3-kui3 toa7 koan5 e5 ong5, chong2-si7 i tak8 jit8 moa2-sim chhim- chhim teh khoa3-lu7, ti3-kau3 chin toa7 hoan5- lo2. I thiaN lang5 kong2 ti7 bou2 soaN-nih8 u7 chit8 e5 chin ti3-hui3 e5 seng3-jin5. Ong5 khi3 hit e5 seng3-jin5 sou2 toa3 e5 soaN-hiat8 chhoe7 i, chiu7 kong2,“Seng3-jin5 ah ! Goa2 lai5 chhoe7 li2 tiau5-ti5 beh chhiaN2 li2 ka3 goa2 tit-tioh8 hok-khi3 e5 hoat-tou7.”    
Seng3 -jin5 chiu7 chhoa7 ong5 khi3 kau3 koan5-koan5 e5 chioh8-kham3. Ti7 kham3-teng2 u7 chit8 chiah eng-chiau2 cho3 siu7. Seng3-jin5 tui3 ong5 mng7 kong2, “Eng-chiau2 siaN2-su7 cho3 siu7 ti7 hiah-nih8 koan5 e5 sou2-chai7 ? ”    
Ong5 in3 kong2, “KaN2 si7 ui7-tioh8 ai3 siam2-pi7 hong-hiam2.”    
Seng3-jin5 chiu7 soa3 in3 kong2, “Li2 tioh8 khoaN3 eng-chiau2 e5 iuN7. Ti7 thian-tong5 tioh8 khi2 li2 e5 chhu3 chiu7 moa2-sim e7 tit-tioh8 peng5-an ban7-hok.”  
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 172 koan3,1899 ni5 7 geh8).
