
Chu2-jit8-oh8
Sio2 peng5-iu2!  Lin2 bat sang3 le2-mih8 lang5, a2-si7 hian3 le2-mih8 hou7 Siong7-te3 lai5 piau2 si7 kam2-sia7 a2 m7-bat?  Ti7 chia u7 nng7 e5 kou3-su7 hou7 lan2 chai hian3 le2-mih8 kap kam2-sia7 e5 saN koan-he7.    
Kou3-su7 (1).
Ti7 An-the5-o siaN5-lai7 u7 chit8 keng le2-pai3-tng5, cheng3 hoe7-iu2 long2 teh chu7-chip8 choe3 le2-pai3.  U7 chit8 e5 lang5 mia5 kio3 A-ka-pou3 lai5 po3 siau-sit kong2, “Ti7  Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 kau3-hoe7 u7 tu2-tioh8 chai-e7.  Tihia7 u7 tu2-tioh8 phaiN2 ni5-tang, sou2-i2 chin-choe7 peh-siN3 kiaN-liau2 e7 go7--si2.”  An-the5-o e5 kau3-hoe7-iu2 kap pat8 sou2-chai7 e5 kau3-hoe7-iu2 na7 thiaN-tioh chit e5 siau-sit chiu7 kong2, “Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, lan2 u7 kau3-giah8, ia7 u7 chhun, sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 pun hou7 bo5 e5 lang5.”  In chiu7 chin hoaN-hi2 hian3 chiN5 thang boe2 bi2-niu5 hou7 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5.
Kou3-su7(2)
Ti7 A-hui-li7-ka chiu u7 chit8 e5 ou-bin7 choe3 bak8 e5 lang5 mia5 kio3 To-ma2.  I teh choe3 chit8 te3 thak8 Seng3-keng e5 toh thang ti le2-pai3-tng5 eng7, hou7 bok8-su thang tui3 hia thak8 Seng3-keng hou7 peh-siN3 thiaN.  Ti7 le2-pai3-jit8 cheng3-lang5 long2 beh hian3-chiN5, a2-si7 hian3 le2-mih8, thang eng7 lai5 boe2 chin-choe7 pun2 Seng3-keng, kau3-giah8 hou7 tak8 e5 lang5 long2 thang eng7.  To ma2 siuN7 i beh hian3 chit8 te3 Seng3-keng toh, i sim-lai7 chin hoaN-hi2 chhut-lat8 choe3 kang.  I eng7 te7-it ho2 e5 chha5, ti7 chit8 peng5 khek beh8-sui7, ia7 khek ji7 sia2 kong2, “Oah8-mia7 e5 bi2-niu5,” ti7 chit8 peng5 khek phu5-to5-chhiu7, ia7 sia2 kong2, si7 “Chin e5 phu5-to5-chhiu7.”  I ia7 chhat hou7 i koh-khah sui2.  Ti7 pai3-lak8 e5-hng To-ma2 chhin-chhiu2 gia5 toh kau3 ti7 le2-pai3-tng5, chiu7 kui7-loh8-khi3 hian3 hou7 Chu2.  Li7-khui le2-pai3-tng5 e5 si5, i e5 sim chhiong-moa2 kam2-sia7 kap o-lo2.  Keh jit8 si7 le2-pai3-jit8,tak8 e5 lang5 toa3 in e5 le2-mih8 lai5 beh hian3 hou7 Siong7-te3.  U7 koe, nng7, chhai3, keng-chio, han-chu5, thou5-tau7, lang5 cho3 e5 tin5-na5, kap lang5 eng7 hui5 cho3 e5 ang3.  U7 chin-choe7 khoan2 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Tak8 e5 lang5 teh siuN7  
Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3 ia7 ai3 piau2-si7 kam2-un, koh ai3 tit-tioh8 Seng3-keng hou7 in thang khoaN3, lai5 koh-khah bat Siong7-te3.  To-ma2 e5 sim si7 te7-it hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i te7-it ai3 eng7 hit te3 sui2 e5 Seng3-keng toh lai5 kam2-un.  I sim-lai7 siuN7 Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3 e5 oe7 kong2, “Lin2 tiong-kan tioh8 theh8 le2-mih8 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa; kiN3-na7 sim goan7--e5, thang theh8 i lai5, choe3 hian3 hou7 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 le2-mih8’’  (Chhut Ai-kip8-ki3 35:5).
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 767 ho7, 1952 ni5 11 geh8)
