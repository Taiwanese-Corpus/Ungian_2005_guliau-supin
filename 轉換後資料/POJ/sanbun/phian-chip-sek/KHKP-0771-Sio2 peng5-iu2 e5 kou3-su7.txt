
Sio2 peng5-iu2 e5 kou3-su7
Toe5-bak8:  Chit8 chiah lu5 kiN3-tioh8 ThiN-sai3.
Seng3-keng chat:  Bin5-sou3-ki3 22-24 chiuN.
Kou3-su7
Kou2-cha2 u7 chit8 chiah lu5 kap i e5 chu2-lang5 Pa-lan5 saN-kap khia7-khi2 ti7 chit8 tiau5 ho5-piN.  Ti7 hia u7 chhiN-chhui3 e5 chhau2-pou, chong2-si7 chit chiah lu5 na7 tioh8 chai3 Pa-lan5 khi3 pat8-ui7 e5 si5, chiu7 tioh8 li7-khui sui2 e5 ho5-piN, lai5 keng-ke3 chin choe7 tiau5 ta-so3 kham-khiat8 e5 lou7.
Chit chiah lu5 si7 chin koai, sou2-i2 i e5 chu2-lang5 chin thiaN3 i, bo5 phah i, hou7 i chiah8 kau3 pa2, koh ti7 am3-si5 pi7-pan7 ho2 es5¯-chai7 hou7 lu5 thang peng5-an hioh-khun3.
Pa-lan5 m7-si7 u7 chiN5 e5 lang5.  I chi2-u7 u7 Chit chiah lu5 na7-tiaN7, sou2-i2 na7 beh khi3 pat8-ui7, tak8 pai2 tioh8 khia5 lu5 khi3.  Pa-lan5 chin thiaN3 Siong7-te3, koh ka-ki7 oe7 hiau2 thiaN Siong7-te3 e5 siaN.  Tui3 an2-ni, si3-bin7 tak8 e5 lang5 long2 chai i si7 chin u7 ti3-sek e5 lang5.
U7 chit8 jit8, u7 kui2-na7 e5 chheng7 sui2 saN, ong5 e5 su3-chia2 toa3 le2-mih8 lai5 chhoe7 Pa-lan5.  In ai3 chhoa7 Pa-lan5 tng2-khi3, lai5 tiau-kiN3 kok-ong5.  Hit e5 kok-ong5 chin kiaN, in-ui7 u7 chin choe7 goa7-kok lang5 tui3 Ai-kip8 lai5 kau3 ti7 i e5 kok.  I phah-sng3 in chiah e5 lang5 oe7 tui3-tek8 koh khah-iaN5 i e5 kok, sou2-i2 ai3 Pa-lan5 lai5 ka7 in chiu3-chou2 .  (Chiu3-chou2  chiu7-si7 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-te3 hou7 chiah e5 lang5 tu2-tioh8 chai-e7)
Pa-lan5 chiu7 ka7 chiah e5 su3-chia2 kong2, tioh8 theng3-hau7 keh-jit khoaN3 mai7, in-ui7 i beh tai7-seng thiaN Siong7-te3 e5 siaN, kai7 kong2 tioh8 choe3 sim2-mih8.  Keh-jit8 chiu7 ka7 hiah e5 lang5 kong2, “Siong7-te3 u7 i2-keng chiok-hok chiah e5 lang5.  ( I3 -su3 chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 i2-keng u7 pang-chan7 hiah e5 lang5 ai3 hou7 in tit-tioh8 peng5 an).  Pa-lan5 chiu7 kong2, “In-ui7 Siong7-te3 u7  
chiok-hok in, goa2 koat-toan3 m7 chiu3-chou2 in.”  Sou2-i2 hiah e5 lang5 li7 khui khi3, chong2-si7 bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7 u7 koh khah toa7 e5 jin5-sin5 toa3 le2-mih8 lai5 beh sang3 hou7 Pa-lan5, koh ai3 chhoa7 i khi3 tiau5-kiN3 ong5, ia7 chiu3-chou2 hiah e5 chin choe7 chhut Ai-kip e5 goa7-kok lang5.  Pa-lang5 kong2, “Ong5 na7 eng7 moa2-chhu3 e5 kim-gun5 hou7 goa2, goa2 ia7 m7-kaN2 poaN5-ke3 goa2 e5 Siong7-te3 e5 beng7-leng7.”  Chong2-si7 Pa-lan5 koh chhiaN2 hiah e5 jin5-sin5 hit mi5 hioh ti7 i hia, hou7 i u7 si5-kan thang koh thiaN kiN3 Siong7-te3 e5 siaN.
Kiam2-chhai2 Pa-lan5 hoaN-hi2 ong5 u7 koh chit8 pai2 chhe jin5-sin5 lai5, koh kiam2-chhai2 i ai3 tit-tioh8 hiah e5 tat8-chiN5 e5 le2-mih8.  Bo5 lun7 ju7-ho5, keh-jit8 i tah-eng3 beh kap in cho3-hoe2 khi3, m7-ku2 i kong2 m7 ui5-keh8 Siong7-te3 e5 beng7-leng7.
Sou2-i2 Pa-lan5 khia5 lu5 lai5 kap hiah e5 ong5 e5 jin5-sin5 saN kap khi3.  Hit chiah lu5 long2 m7-chai sim2-mih8 tai7-chi2, tok8-tok8 chai i tioh8 koai-koai chai3 Pa-lan5 kiaN5-lou7.  M7 ku2 lu5 kam2-kak bo5 an-sim.  Phah-sng3 Pa-lan5 pun2-sin ia7 bo5 chap8-hun an-sim.  Lu5 na2 kiaN5 na2 bo5 peng5-an, chiu7 hut-jian5-kan oat8-tng2-sin li7-khui lou7 siam2-jip8 piN-thau5 chit8 khu hng5.  Pa-lan5 m7-bat phah lu5, chong2-si7 chit pai2 chin siu7-khi3, eng7 koaiN2-a2 ka7 i phah, bian2-kiong2 lu5 koh chiuN7-lou7 kiaN5.  In kiaN5 kau3 phu5-to5-hng5 lai7 e5 chit8 tiau5 oeh8 e5 lou7, lu5 koh-chai3 theng5.  Ti7 lou7-piN u7 chioh8-chhiuN5.  Lu5 kiaN5 oa2 chhiuN5, hou7 i e5 seng-khu kheh Pa-lan5 e5 kha ti7 chhiuN5 nih.  Pa-lan5 koh khah siu7-khi3, ka7 i phah.  Lu5 koh chin3-cheng5 kau3 ti7 koh-khah oeh8 e5 lou7, chiu7 to2 ti7 lou7-tiong, m7 koh kiaN5.  Chit pai2 Pa-lan5 hui-siong5 siu7-khi3, eng7 koaiN2-a2 ka7 lu5 phah kau3 chin thiam2.  Hut-jian5-kan Pa-lan5 thiaN lu5 kong2-oe7.  (Li2 bat thiaN kau2-a2 a2 si7 niau kong2 oe7 a2 m7 bat?  U7-si5 si7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 in teh kong2, “ChhiaN2 li2 hou7 goa2 thang chiah8,” a2-si7 “Lai5 kap goa2 thit-tho5.”  U7 si5 tong7-but8 oe7 hiau2 kong2-oe7 kau3 chin chheng-chho2.  Lan2 na7 thiaN3 tong7-but8, lan2 sit8-chai7 oe7 chai in sou2 kong2 e5 oe7).  Pa-lan5 chin thiaN3 i e5 lu5, taN chai-iaN2 lu5 to2 ti7 hia teh kong2, “Goa2 tui3 li2 choe3 sim2-mih8, li2 keng2-jian5 phah goa2 chi2 saN-pai2 ah?”  Pa-lan5 kong2, “Si7 in-ui7 li2 m7 thiaN chhui3.”  Lu5 koh kong2,“Goa2 kiam2 m7-si7 li2 e5 lu5 mah?  Goa2 peng5-sou3 bat tui3 li2 an2-ni kiaN5 mah?  Pa-lan5 kong2, “Bo5.”
Tu2-tu2 ti7 hit-si5, Siong7-te3 khui Pa-lan5 e5 bak8-chiu, hou7 i khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 lou7-tiong u7 thiN-sai3 teh khia7, i e5 chhiu2 puih8 to.  Pa-lan5 chiu7 chai lu5 u7 tai7-seng khoaN3 thiN-sai3, sou2-i2 m7 kiaN5, na7 kiaN5 chiu7 u7 gui5-hiam2.  Pa-lan5 chiu7 kan-khou2-sim, chai i choe3 m7-tioh8, ia7 chai lu5 si7 chin koai.  Pa-lan5 kong2, “Goa2 choe3 m7 tioh8.  Lan2 lai5 to3-tng2-khi3 lah.  Siong7-te3 m7 hou7 lan2 chin3-cheng5 khi3.”  Ti7 hit-si5 thiN-sai3 kong2,“TaN tioh8 chin3-cheng5 khi3 kiN3 kok-ong5, chong2-si7 seng5-sit8 tioh8 chiau3 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7 lai5 kong2.”  Pa-lan5 chiu7 koat-teng7 beh chin soe3-ji7 thiaN Siong7-te3 e5 siaN,koh chiau3 Siong7-te3 e5 beng7-leng7 lai5 kiaN5 kap kong2.  In chiu7 chin3-cheng7 khi3, lu5 peng5-an kiaN5 lou7 kau3 ti7 kok-ong5 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ong5 chhoa7 Pa-lan5 kau3 ti7 chit8 e5 soaN-teng2.  Ti7 soaN-kha u7 kui2-na7 chheng e5 goa7-kok lang5, u7 tah pou3-piN5 chat-iaN5 teh hioh ti7 hit e5 soaN-kok.  Ong5 kong2,“TaN li2 chiu3-chou2 hiah e5 lang5.”  Pa-lan5 kong2, “Siong7-te3 thiaN3 in.  Siong7-te3 u7 kain7 chiok-hok, goa2 thai3-tho2 oe7 chiu3-chou2 in, goa2 beh kiu5 Siong7-te3 chiok-hok.”Kok-ong5 chin siu7-khi3, sou2-i2 Pa-lan5 tioh8 koaN2-kin2 li7-khui khi3.  I long2 bo5 tit-tioh8 hiah e5 sui2 e5 le2-mih8, chong2-si7 i sim-lai7 chin peng5-an khia5 lu5 tng2-khi3 ti7 hit e5 chhiN-chhui3 e5 ho5-piN.
Li2 chai hiah e5 goa7-kok lang5 si7 sim2-mih lang5?  In si7 Mou5-se sou2 in2-chhoa7 chhut Ai-kip8 e5 I2-sek-liat8 peh-siN3.  Au7-lai5 Chu2 Ia5-sou choe3 eN a2 chhut si3 ti7 in tiong-kan, sou2-i2 chiah e5 I2-sek-liat8 peh-siN3 chin-chiaN3 u7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 chiok-hok.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 771 ho7, 1953 ni5 3 geh8)
