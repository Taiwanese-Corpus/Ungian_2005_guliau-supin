
Kian3-hak8 Te7 thai3 e7 tin2-tang7?
Tiau-cha te7-tang7 e5 hoat-siN, Se3-kai3 tak8-kok e5 pho2-pian3 kek8 bo5 peng5-teng2; sou2 hoat-hian7 e5 hoe5-sou3 kap I e5 kiong5-jiok8, tak8 sou2-chai7 cham2-jian5 bo5 siang7.   Chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 Jit8-pun2 kap Lam5 A-bi-li7-ka e5 sai-peng5-hoaN7, te7-tang7 choe3 te7-it che7.  Ngou5-lo5-su Sai-pi2-li7-a e5 pak-pou7, kap A-hui-li7-ka e5 tiong-pou7 si7 choe3 chio2; chiu7 na7 u7, ia7-si7 chin bi5-jiok8.  Sou2-I2 U-tek8-su Sian-siN, chiong Se3-kai3 e5 te7 tin2-tang7, pun cho3 saN toa7 khu-hek8.  
1.Che7 chin2 (多震).  Hoat-siN te7- tang7 choe3 te7-it che7 e5 sou2-chai7.  
2.Chio2 chin2 (寡震).  Hoat-siN te7- tang7 choe3 te7-it chio2 e5 sou2-chai7.  
3.Bo5 chin2 (無震).  Si7 bo5 te7-tang7 e5 sou2-chai7.  
Te7-tang7 choe3 te7-it che7 koh kiong5 e5 sou2-chai7 si7 Thai3-peng5-iuN5 e5 chiu-ui5, kap hoaiN5 koe3 O3-kok Au-chiu kap Tiong Bi2 e5 Te7-tiong-hai2.  Se3-kai3 e5 te7-tang7, thong2-ke3 cho3 10 hun7.  Te7-tiong-hai2 choan5-the2 iok chiam3 5 hun7.  Thai3-peng5-iuN5 chiu- ui5 iok chiam3 4 hun7, Ki5-u5 chit8-hun7 soaN3- soaN3 pun ti7 Se3-kai3 tak8 kok.  Tui3 an2-ni lai5 khoaN3, toa7 te7-chin2 tai3 (地震帶).  Kap oah8 hoe2-soaN pun5-chhut choe3 kek-liat8- tai3 (激烈帶) kap khut-khiok e5 soaN-nia2 te7, u7 choe3 bit8-chhiat e5 koan-he7.  
Se3-kai3, u7 chit8-choa7 lou7, kin7 ti7 hia e5 te7-hng, siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 te7-tang7, ho7-mia5 Te7-chin2 tai3, Tai3, ke2-soeh, si7 toa3, chhin-chhiuN7 io-toa3, khou3-toa3 hit-ho7, i3-su3, si7 hit choa7 e5 te7-hng.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 699 ho7, 1947 ni5 3 geh8)
