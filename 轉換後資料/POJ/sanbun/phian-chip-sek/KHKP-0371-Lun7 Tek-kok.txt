
Lun7 Tek-kok
Tek-kok lang5 e5 tsou2 tui3 chin tsa2 u7 toa3 ti7 Au-lo5-pa-chiu e5 pak peng5.  Nng7 chheng goa7 ni5 cheng5, in iau2-be7 u7 tit- tioh8 khai-hoa3, siong5-siong5 kap Lo5-ma2 kok sio-thai5. Au7-lai5 u7 soan kau3-su khi3 po3-iong5 to7-li2 ti7 in tiong-kan, in chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 sin3, kau3 au7-lai5 khah tsoe7 u7 hong7 Ki-tok-kau3 tsoe3 in e5 tsong  kau3.  Tit-tioh8 to7-li2 liau2-au7 in chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 khai-hoa3.  Ti Tek-kok Ia5-sou-kau3 (Protestants), pi2 Lo5-ma2 kau3 e5 lang5 u7 khah tsoe7.  Lou7-tek Ma2-teng (Martin Luther) kai2-cheng3 tsong-kau3 si7 ti7 Tek-kok, sou2-i2 tui3 hit-tiap8 loh8 lai5 Tek-kok e5 kau3-hoe7 khah bo5 hang5-hok8 ti7 Lo5-ma2 e5 kau3-ong5.    
Tek-kok hap8 tsoe3 chit8 kok si7 bo5 loa7- ku2, kui2-na7 tsap8 ni5 cheng5 na7-tiaN7.  Khah tsa2 u7 pun tsoe3 kui2-na7 kok. Chiah e5 kok e5 tiong-kan u7 chit8 kok khah kiong5, chiu7-si7 Phou-lou7-se-a (Prussia).  Tui3 chin tsa2 Phou-lo7-se-a e5 lang5 chin ai3 sio-thai5. Tsu2-au7 1864 Phou-lou7-se-a u7 kap Tan- kok sio-thai5, u7 thai5-iaN5 Tan-kok soa3 chiam3 nng7 seng2.  Tsu2-au7 1866 ni5 Phou- lou7-se-a u7 kap O3-kok sio-thai5.  Lak8 le2- -pai3 e5 ku2 u7 khah-iaN5 O3-kok kau3 O3-kok bo5-lat8, liam5-piN tioh8 hang5-hok8. Chu2-au7 1870 Phou-lou7-se-a u7 chhe7 ki-hoe7 kap Hoat-kok sio-thai5.  Thai5-iaN5 chiu7 u7 chiam3 Hoat-kok e5 te7 nng7 seng2.  Phou- lou7-se-a hiah-nih8 kiong5, chiu7 tang5 cheng2 lui7 e5 kok chin kiaN, put-tek-i2 tioh8 kap Phou-lou7-se-a lian5-hap8 choe3 Tek-kok e5 te3- kok, keng2 Phou-lou7-se-a ong5 tsoe3 lian5-hap8 te3 kok e5 hong5-te3.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 khi2 Tek- kok toa7 heng-khi2. Siong5-siong5 tsun2-pi7 pat8 jit8 beh koh sio-thai5, ng3-bang7 Au- lo2-pa chiu chiah e5 kok long2 oe7 hang5- hok8 ti7 i. Kui2-na7 pai2 hiam2-hiam2 koh- tsai3 kap Hoat-kok sio-thai5, na7-si7 Hoat- kok bo5 ai3 chiu7 kam-goan7 niu7 i.    
Tsu2-au7 1878 Tek-kok u7 kap O3-kok, I2-tai7-li7 kok tong5-beng5.  Tui3 an2-ni Hoat- kok kap Ngou5-lo5-su put-an kiaN-liau2 tong5-beng5 e5 kok chhe7 ki-hoe7 kap in sio-thai5. Au7-lai5 Ngou5-lo5-su kap Hoat- kok ai3 ti5-hong5 tui3-tek8, chiu7 soa3 tong5- beng5.  Tek-kok chit pai2 sio-thai5 si7 in- ui7 kap O3-kok tong5-beng5.  O3-kok ai3 chiam3 Sek-hui-a e5 toe7 u7 kap Sek-hui-a sio-thai5.  Ngou5-lo5-su ai3 po2-hou7 Sek-hui-a u7 iau-kiu5 O3-kok, kio3 i m7 thang kang7 Sek-hui-a. O3-kok m7 tsun2 sou2 kiu5 chiu7 Ngou5-lo5-su u7 tong7-oan5 kun-peng.  Tek- kok khoaN3 Ngou5-lo5-su u7 tong7-oan5 kun- peng chiu7 liam5-piN soan-chian3, bo5 theng3-hau7 khoaN3 Ngou5-lo5-su tiaN7-tioh8 beh kap O3-kok sio-thai5 a2-bo5. An2-ni thang tsai Tek-kok hiah-nih8 kin2 soan- chian3, si7 in-ui7 tui3 khi2-thau5 u7 khng3 ai3 sio-thai5 e5 sim, sou2-i2 liong7-tsa2 pi7-pan7, tiau5-kang beh sio-thai5.  Na7 m7 si7 an2-ni i tsaiN2-iuN7 hiah-nih8 kin2 soan-chian3 ? Tek-kok kong2 i teh sio-thai5 si7 in-ui7 Ngou5-lo5-su beh kong-kek O3-kok. M7-ku2 O3-kok kap Ngou5-lo5-su iau2 teh chham- siong5 e5 si5 Tek-kok u7 tai7-seng khi2 chhiu2 sio-thai5.  Tek-kok i2-keng u7 soan-chian3 nng7 jit8 e5 ku2, O3-kok chiah soan-chian3. Siat-su2 Tek-kok bo5 hiah-nih8 kin2 soan- chian3, chiu7 kiam2-chhai2 O3-kok kap Ngou5-lo5-su hit-tiap8 oe7 chham-siong5 ho2- se3 kau3 long2 m7 bian2 sio-thai5.       
Chit pai2 sio-thai5 Tek-kok u7 hoan7-tioh8 ban7-kok e5 kong-hoat chin tsoe7 hang7.  Phi3-ju7 chhin-chhiuN7 tui3 pe-tsun5 hiat tsa3-tan5 ti7 bo5 chian3-cheng e5 sou2-tsai7 hai7-si2 peh-siN3.  Koh u7 eng7 tsng3- chui2-tsun5 phah tim5 seng-li2 tsun5, long bo5 iau3-kin2 tsun5-lai7 e5 lang5-kheh, ta-pou, tsa-bou2 kap gin2-na2 sit-loh8 in e5 siN3-mia. Koh in khah tsa2 u7 iok beh po2-hou7 Pek8-ni2-gi7 e5 tiong-lip8, teh sio-thai5 e5 si5 liam5-piN chiu7 hoan7-tioh8 tiong-lip8. Koh i2-keng chiam3 Pek8-ni2-gi7 e5 toe7 liau2-au7, in chiu7 bo5 in-toaN thai5-si2 Pek8-ni2-gi7 kui2-na7 pah bo5 tng-peng e5 lang5 lai5 hou7 peh-siN3 khah kiaN in u7 ui-giam5.  Tek-kok iah8 si7 u7 tai7-seng eng7 oa2-su-khi3 e5 tok8 ian hai7-si2 tui3-tek8. Iah8 u7 pat8 hang7 Tek-kok sou2 choe3 lai5 ti3-kau2 hoan7-tioh8 khai-hoa3 e5 kok chian3-cheng e5 kui-ki2. Na7 beh gi7-lun7 chheng-chho2 tioh8 chin ku2.    
Tek-kok e5 peh-siN3 chit pai2 sio-thai5 si7 in-ui7 hou7 in e5 bu2-koaN phian3-khi3.  KoaN u7 boe7 sin-bun5 kong2 sio-thai5 si7 in-ui7 Ngou5-lo5-su beh hai7 in, chiam3 in e5 toe7. Ki5-sit8 m7 si7 an2-ni, si7 in-ui7 Tek-kok e5 koaN ai3 chiam3 pat8 kok e5 toe7, thang hou7 Tek-kok tsoe3 thiN-kha-e7 te7-it kiong5 e5 kok. Hian7-si5 Tek-kok e5 sin-bun5-tsoa2 teh boe7 sin-bun5 long2 tioh8 than3 koaN e5 i3-su3.  Tek-kok u7-si5 na7 tu2-tioh8 thai5-su, kok-lai7 e5 sin-bun5-po3 m7 kaN2 kong2-beng5, kiaN-liau2 koaN pan7 i e5 tsoe7.    
Tek-kok e5 koaN che kui2 tsap8 ni5 toa7 kou2-bu2 pi7-pan7 sio-thai5 e5 su7, iah8 u7 tit-tioh8 kok-lai7 che3-tso7 bu2-khi3 e5 ho2-giah8 lang5 chin chhut-lat8 tsan3-seng5, ng3-bang7 tui3 an2-ni oe7 boe7 bu2-khi3 khah tsoe7 lai5 khah than3 chiN5.    
Tek-kok e5 peh-siN3 chit pai2 hou7 in e5 koaNphian3 lai5 ti3-kau3 kok-lai7 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 hiah tsoe7 si2, sit8-tsai7 chin kho2-lin5. Hian7-kim Tek-kok e5 si2-siong kap hou7 lang5 liah8-e5 u7 saN si3 pah ban7. Siat-su2 koh sio-thai5 chit8 ni5 e5 ku2 kiam2-chhai2 tioh8 teng5-pe7 tsoe7.  Tek-kok e5 peh-siN3 u7- e5 chin gau5, koh u7-e5 chin m7 kam-goan7 chian3-cheng e5 su7, ai3 kap pat8 kok e5 lang5 ho5-ho2. Kho2-lin5 ah ! kui kok e5 peh-siN3 hou7 in e5 koaN phian3 lai5 ti3-kau3 kok-ka tu2-tioh8 toa7 e5 sun2-hoai7.  In na7 bo5 khah kin2 chhiN2-ngou7, chiu7 kiaN-liau2 kok-ka hai7- tioh8 kau3 oh-tit koh beng-khi2.  In-ui7 lian5-hap8-kok e5 i3-su3 chin koat-toan3 beh phah Tek-kok kau3 kam-goan7 heng5 sou2 chiam3 e5 toe7.  Kau3 chit-tiap8 Tek-kok li2- li3-a2 u7 khah-iaN5 e5 se3-bin7, m7-ku2 tui3 chit- tiap8 khi2 i oe7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 kan-khou2. In e5 hai2-khau2 hou7 Eng-kok e5 hai2-kun long2 hong-so2, koh in e5 chian3-tsun5 kap seng-li2 tsun5 kiaN long2 m7-kaN2 chhut-lai5. Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 bo5 thang tui3 pat8 kok tsai3 kun-peng e5 niu5-chhau2 kap peh-siN3 sou2 khiam3 e5 mih8.  Sou2-i2 ti7 Tek-kok, O3-kok hian7-si5 tak8 hang7 sou2 eng7 e5 mih8 u7 tit8-tit8 khi2-ke3.  M7-na7 an2-ni na7-tiaN7, Tek- kok, O3-kok e5 peng kap peng sou2 eng7 e5 mih8 u7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 kiam2-chio2.  Lian5- hap8-kok e5 peng kap peng sou2 eng7 mih8 u7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 ke-thiN.  Sou2-i2 chiau3 hian7-si5 e5 se3-bin7 Tek-kok oh-tit iaN5, na7- si7 chiau3 chiong-lai5 e5 chhui-li2 phah-sng3 un2-tang3 oe7 su.    

(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 370 koan3, 1916 ni5 1 geh8)
