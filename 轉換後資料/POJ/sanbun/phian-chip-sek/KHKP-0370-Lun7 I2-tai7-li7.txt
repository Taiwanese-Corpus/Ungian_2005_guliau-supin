
Lun7 I2-tai7-li7
I2-tai7-li7 kok khah tsa2 si7 ho7 tsoe3 Lo5-ma2. Tsu2 Ia5-sou chhut-si3 e5 si5 Lo5-ma2 kok sng3 si7 thiN-kha-e7 te7-it toa7 te7-it kiong5 e5 kok. Hit-tiap8 Lo5-ma2 kok sou2 koan2 e5 toe7 chin khoah, tui3 sai-peng5 e5 Se-pan-ga5 kau3 tang-peng5 e5 Pho-su-kok. Te7-tiong-hai2 hai2-hoaN7 e5 toe7, hek8-si7 tang sai lam5 pak, hit-tiap8 long2 siok8 Lo5-ma2 kok koan2. An2-ni thang tsai Lo5-ma2 kok e5 lat8 hit- tiap8 chin toa7.    
Chiau3 Lo5-ma2 e5 lek8-su2-ka u7 ki3 siat Lo5-ma2 kok si7 tui3 chin tsa2. Tsu2 cheng5 lak8 chhit pah ni5 i2-keng u7 siat kok. Khi2- thau5 si7 kun-tsu2 kok, au7-lai5 ti7 Tsu2 cheng5 gou7 pah goa7 ni5 u7 kek-beng7 pian3-oaN7 lai5 siat bin5-chu2 kok. An2-ni thang chai tui3 chin tsa2 Lo5-ma2 kok u7 bin5-koan5 e5 cheng3-ti7.    
Chiau3 Su3-tou5 Heng7-toan7 sou2 ki3, Po2-lo5 u7 thoan5-kau3 ti7 Lo5-ma2 e5 kiaN-siaN5. Kui2- na7 pah ni5 e5 ku2 kau3-hoe7 siong5-siong5 hou7 Lo5-ma2 e5 koaN khun2-tiok8. Chiau3 in kong2, Ki-tok-kau3 si7 hai7-tioh8 kok-ka; in-ui7 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 lang5 m7 kam-goan7 pai3 Lo5- ma2 hong5-te3 e5 siong7.    
Ti7 Tsu2 au7 324 ni5 Lo5-ma2 u7 hong7 Ki-tok-kau3 tsoe3 kok-ka e5 tsong-kau3. Tui3 hit-tiap8 kau3-hoe7 chiu7 bo5 koh tu2-tioh8 khun2-tiok8. Lo5-ma2 hong7 Ki-tok-kau3 tsoe3 kok-ka e5 tsong-kau3, toa3 ti7 Lo5-ma2 kau3-hoe7 e5 kam3-tok chiu7 khah u7 koan5, khah u7 eng5-kng,in-ui7 Lo5-ma2 siaN5 si7 kok-ka e5 kiaN-siaN5 koh chin lau7-jiat8. Bo5 loa7-ku2 Lo5-ma2 kau3-hoe7 e5 kam3-tok kio3 pat8 sou2-chai7 e5 kam3-tok tioh8 long2 hang5- hok8 ti7 i, kong2 i si7 chiap Pi2-tek e5 chit, thoe3 Siong7-te3 liau7-li2 kau3-hoe7. Na2 ku2 i-e5 koan5 na2 toa7, sou2-i2 hian7-kim Lo5-ma2- kau3 e5 lang5 (chiu7-si7 Thian-chu2-kau3 ) long2 chheng-hou Lo5-ma2 e5 kam3-tok choe3 kau3- ong5, kam-goan7 hang5-hok8 ti7 i. 
I2-tai7-li7 khah cha2 u7 hun tsoe3 kui2-na7 kok, koh u7 kui2-na7 seng2 hou7 Lo5-ma2 e5 kau3-ong5 koan2. In-ui7 hit-si5 Lo5-ma2 kau3- ong5 m7-na7 koan2 sim-sin e5 su7, i iah8 u7 koan2 toe7. Au7-lai5, ti7 Chu2 au7 19 se3-ki3 u7 kui2-na7 e5 I2-tai7-li7 lang5, put-chi2 thiaN3 thang3 in e5  
pun2-kok, u7 jiat8-sim kou2-bu2 kok-ka lian5-hap8 e5 su7, ng3-bang7 I2-tai7-li7 chiah e5 kok na7 long2 lian5-hap8 tsoe3 chit8 kok, chiu7 peh-siN3 khah khoaN3-oah8, kok-ka khah oe7 heng-khi2. Kou2-bu2 chit tai7-chi3 u7 saN e5 lang5 khah u7 mia5-siaN, chiu7-si7 Ka-bu2-li2 (Cavour ), Ma2-se-ni2 (Mazzini ), kap Ka-li2-pa-lek8-ti2 (Garbaldi ). Hit-si5 I2-tai7-li7 e5 pak-peng5 u7 hang5-hok8 ti7 O3-kok koan2. O3-kok chin m7 kam-goan7 I2-tai7-li7 e5 lian5-hap8, kiaN-liau2 pat8-jit8 I2-tai7-li7 khah u7 lat8, O3-kok chiu7 khah gui5-hiam2. M7- ku2 I2-tai7-li7 e5 lang5 chin koat-i3 beh lian5- hap8, chiu7 O3-kok kap i sio-thai5. Tai7-seng I2-tai7-li7 u7 khah-su O3-kok, au7-lai5 u7 khah-iaN5, chiu7 kok-ka lian5-hap8 e5 su7 tit- tioh8 seng5-kong, chong2-si7 u7 kui2-na7 seng2 lian5 ti7 O3-kok e5 toe7-kai3 iau2 siok8 ti7 O3-kok koan2, Chit-pai2 I2-tai7-li7 kap O3-kok sio-thai5 si7 in-ui7 u7 ka7 O3-kok tho2 chit kui2-na7 seng2 to3-tng2. O3-kok m7-kam-goan7 heng5, sou2-i2 nng7 kok u7 sio-thai5.    
Khah tsa2 I2-tai7-li7 e5 lang5 teh chhut- lat8 ai3 hou7 kok-ka lian5-hap8 lai5 thoat-li7 O3-kok e5 pou7-giok8, Eng-kok lang5 put-chi2 u7 tsan3-seng5 I2-tai7-li7. Tui3 hit-tiap8 I2-tai7- li7 e5 lang5 put-si5 siau3-liam7 Eng-kok. Sou2-i2 chit pai2 tu2-tioh8 toa7 chian3-cheng I2-tai7-li7 put-chi2 tsan3-seng5 Eng-kok. Chiau3 in kong2 O3-kok kap Tek-kok nng7 kok e5 cheng3-chhek si7 ap-che3 pat8 kok, Eng- kok e5 cheng3-chhek si7 hou7 pat8 kok tsu7- iu5, sou2-i2 I2-tai7-li7 tioh8 tsan3-seng5 Eng-kok.    
I2-tai7-li7 e5 peng u7 nng7 saN pah ban7, koh put-chi2 iong2-kam2. I-e5 chian3-tsun5 iah8 sng3 boe7 chio2. Iau2-be7 chian3-cheng e5 si5 I2-tai7-li7 kap Tek-kok O3-kok tong5-beng5. Tek-kok khi2-thau5 kap Eng-kok sio-thai5 e5 si5, u7 kio3 I2-tai7-li7 tioh8 lai5 kap in nng7 kok lian5-hap8 chhut-chian3. I2-tai7- li7 m7-kheng2, hiam5 O3-kok Tek-kok bo5 cheng5-li2, koh hiam5 O3-kok kap Sek- hui-a (Sek-ni2-pi2-a )sio-thai5, si7 poe7 tong5- beng5 e5 iok. Tui3 an2-ni u7 ka7 O3-kok tho2 siok8 I-kok chiah-e5 seng2, koh in-ui7 O3- kok m7 heng5 chiu7 kap i sio-thai5.    
I2-kok lang5 khah tsoe7 si7 Lo5-ma2 kau3 e5 lang5 (chiu7-si7 Thian-chu2-kau3 ), Ia5-sou-kau3 e5 lang5 khah chio2. Lun7 cheng3-ti7 I2-tai7- li7 si7 kun-chu2-kok. M7-ku2 peh-siN3 u7 tsu7- iu5 koan5. Bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 Tek-kok O3- kok kun koan5 khah toa7, bin5-koan5 khah chio2.    
 
Tui3 kui2-na7 pah ni5 cheng5 I2-tai7-li7 u7 chin tsoe7 chhut-mia5 oa7-kong. In-sou2 ui7 e5 siong7 kau3 kin-a2-jit8 iau2 ti7-teh, hian7-si5 put-chi2 tat8-chiN5, u7-e5 chit8-pak kui2-na7 tsap8 ban7 gun5. Koh u7 kui2-na7 e5 chin chhut-mia5 e5 si-ong. U7 chit8-e5 mia5 ho7 tsoe3 Tan3-te7 (Dante ), si7 gou7 lak8 pah ni5 cheng5 e5 lang5. I sou2 tu3 e5 chheh hian7-si5 iau2 ti7-teh, hoan-ek8 chin tsoe7 kok e5 ji7.  

(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 370 koan3, 1916 ni5 1 geh8)
