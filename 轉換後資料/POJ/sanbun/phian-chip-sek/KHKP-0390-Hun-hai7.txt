
 
Hun-hai7
L.T.K ek8.
I.Hun e5 khoan2-sit.    
1.Hun e5 lai5-lek8. Se-pan-ga5 e5 gau5 lang5 Kho-lun5-pou3, chhe7-tioh8 A-bi2-li7-ka chiu e5 si5, chiah khi2-thau5 khoaN3-tioh8 hit khoan2 e5 mih8. Hit sou2-chai7 e5 chhiN-hoan u7 teh chiah8 hun. Koh bo5 loa7-ku2, u7 chit8 e5 Eng-kok e5 tai7-sin5 khi3 hia, soa3 toa3 nng7 hang7 e5 mih8 to3-tng2, chiu7-si7 hun kap han-tsu5. TaN chit nng7 khoan2 e5 li7 hai7 si7 toa7 koh-iuN7.  Hun si7 te7-it ham7 hai7 lang5; han-tsu5 si7 te7-it li7-ek lang5. ChhiN-hoan pun2-jian5 phah-sng3 chiah8 hun si7 oe7 i-ho2 lang5 e5 piN7, kau3 lou7-be2 chiu7 tsai chiah8 hun si7 hou7 lang5 e5 piN7 khah siong-tiong7. Kho2-sioh bun5-beng5 kok e5 peh-siN3, khi3 than3 ia2-ban5 lang5 e5 phaiN2 hong-siok8, ti3-kau3 ban7 kok e5 lang5 toa7 poaN3 i2-keng jip8 chit e5 lo5-bang7.    
2.Hun e5 chiah8 hoat.  (1) Eng7 suh e5: si7 peng5-siong5 e5 hoat-tou7. (2) Eng7 chiah8-e5: si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 chiah8 pin-nng5 e5 khoan2; che si7 te7-it chhou-siok8 e5 hoat-tou7. (3) Eng7 sngh-e5: si7 eng7 hun-boah8 lai5 sngh ti7 phiN7-khang; chit khoan2 hian7-kim han2-tit eng7.    
3.Hun e5 boe7 hoat.  (1) Kui-khun2 boe7 hou7 hoe7-sia7. (2) Sek-ga5-hun, si7 chiong iu3-iu3 e5 hioh8 lai5 tsoe3, sng3 si7 te7-it ho2, te7-it kui3-e5. (3) Hun-ki, chiu7-si7 eng7 hun-hioh8-sai2 chham tok8-ioh8 (Opiate) lai5 tsoe3. (4) Tiau5-si-hun. Ti7 chit si3 khoan2-tiong tsoe3 te7-it toa7 hai7- e5 si7 hun-ki. Na7 chiah8 chit khoan2 khah ku2, kau3 lou7-be2 nau2-kun oe7 ba5-pi3, sit cheng-sin5, ti3-kau3 lang5 boe7 chin7 pun2-hun7.    
4.Hun e5 tok8.  I-seng chhi3-giam7-liau2, chiu7 chai hun si7 tok8-mih8, chiah chhut beng5-beng5 e5 pin5-ku3. Lang5 e5 ui7 siong5-siong5 ai3 thoat-li7 hun e5 tok8, ti3-kau3 nau2-chhe2 sio, thau5-khak-hin5, lang5 soa3 chiam7-chiam7 gong7, bin7 phaiN2-sek. Ui7 an2-ni tsoe7-tsoe7 pai2 chhi3-khoaN3 oe7 thoat- li7 chit e5 tok8-mih8, kau3 lou7-be2 bo5 lat8 soa3 hang5-hok8 i tsoe3 lou5-chai5. 
 
5.  Hun e5 chit.  (1) Ni5-ko-chin2: si7 toa7-hai7 e5 tok8-mih8.  (2) ThoaN3-sng: si7 hou7 lang5 thau5-khak hin5, soa3 ai3 khun3. (3) ThoaN3-tan-sa: che si7 hou7 lang5 e5 bah- kun oe7 chhoah, soa3 hai7 ti7 sim-tsong7. (4) A-mo-li2-a2: si7 oe7 ba5 lang5 e5 chih8, ia7 oe7 hou7 chhut noa7 e5 kun kin2 ta, ti3-kau3 sou2 chiah8 e5 mih8 boe7 li7-ek sin-khu.         
II. Kim3 hun e5 In-toaN.    
1.Hun m7 si7 bi2-niu5, ti7 hun e5 chit bo5 chit8 hang7 oe7 li7-ek lang5 e5 sin-khu.    
2.Hun oe7 lian5-lui7 lang5, ti3-kau3 choe3 hun e5 lou5-chai5, chin oh tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.    
3.Hun oe7 chou2-tong3 ngou2-chong7 liok8- hu2 kap su3-chi ngou2-koan e5 kang, soa3 hou7 sin-khu khoai3 tu2-tioh8 piN7-thiaN3.    
4.Ti7 eng3-chiap-tiong lai5 chiap8-chiap8 chiah8 hun, m7 na7 u7 sit-le2; koh oe7 hou7 bo5 chiah8 hun e5 lang5 phiN7 hit e5 bi7 lai5 chin kan-khou2.    
5.Chiah8 hun si7 tsoe3 siau3-lian5 lang5 e5 phaiN2 bou5-iuN7.    
6.Hun si7 kek8 la5-sam5 e5 mih8, oe7 hou7 chhui3-khi2 ou, hou7 chhui3 chhut phaiN2-bi7, lai5 hou7 kap i kong2 oe7 e5 lang5 kan-khou2.    
7.Phah-sng2 chiN5-gun5.         
III. Hun e5 Hai7.    
1.U7 hai7 ti7 gin2-na2.  Gin2-na2 ni5-hoe3 chiam7-chiam7 toa7, pah the2 nng2-chiaN2, na7 siong5-siong5 chiah8 hun-ki oe7 tsou2-tong3 i e5 tiong2-seng5, boe7-tit thang iong2-tsong3, sin-khu boe7 kau3 chu7-jian5 e5 toa7-han3. Liam7-thau5 boe7 gau5 siuN7, tak8 hang7 khoai3 boe7 ki3-tit, sit8-tsai7 boe7 gau5 thak8-chheh. Gin2-na2 chiah8 hun, nau2-kun oe7 chiam7- chiam7 tun7, i na2 ku2 oe7 na2 lun2-neh, i e5 chi3-khi3 tak8 jit8 na2 loan2-jiok8, ia7 tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3, i chin khoai3 siu7-khi3. Koh chit8 hang7, gin2-na2 chiah8 hun pi2 bo5 chiah8 hun e5 gin2-na2 khah bo5 le2 e5 khoan2-sit, bo5 iu2-hau3 si7-toa7 lang5, ia7 bo5 hang5- hok8 ti7 i tsu2-lang5. Che si7 gau5 e5 i-seng gian2-kiu3 liau2, sou2 chhut e5 pin5-ku3.    
2.U7 hai7 ti7 toa7 lang5.  Che si7 sim2- mih8 in-toaN?  (1) Si7 in-ui7 chiah8 sek8-hun e5 lang5 teh ka-ki7 phian3, teh phah-sng3 sek8- hun bo5 hai7. (2) Sek8-hun e5 hai7, boe7 hut- jian5-kan hian2-beng5; m7-ku2 i e5 tok8-mih8 chiam7-chiam7 teh jip8 ti7 i e5 nau2 kun bah kun, kau3 lou7-be2 ai3 koe2, chi3-khi3 e5 lat8 bo5 kau3-giah8. (3) In-ui7 khoaN3-kiN3 pat8 lang5 hiah tsoe7 teh chiah8, soa3 teh tsoe3 le2-sou3 e5 lou7-eng7.    
Chiah8 hun m7 chun2 pah-the2 lai5 choe3 choan5 kang, chin khoai3 tu2-tioh8 phoa3- piN7, boe7 gau5 ti2-tng kan-khou2 su7, khah khoai3 sit-chi3; khah khoai3 hang5-hok8 ti7 pat8 khoan2 e5 phaiN2-phiah, chhin-chhiuN7 chiah8 chiu2, chiah8 mou5-hui hiah e5 lui7. Chiah8-hun e5 lang5 i e5 chhui3-chih8 siong5- siong5 ta, i e5 hi3 hou7 hun-ian hai7, chiu7 khah gau5 chhoan2; m7-ku2 te7 it toa7 hai7, chiu7-si7 sim-chong7 khoai3 tioh8 hun hai7 e5 sim piN7.    
3. U7 hai7 ti7 ke-koan3.  (1) Liau2 chiN5, chin toa7 phah-sng2, chiu7-si7 liau2 bou2 kiaN2 sou2 eng-kai chiah8, chheng7, eng7 e5 chiN5. (2) Ka-ki7 e5 chhu3-lai7 put-si5 u7 hun e5 tok8-bi7, lian5-lui7 ke-lai7 e5 lang5, ka- ki7 teh chiah8, ke-lai7 e5 lang5 khoai3 than3 i e5 bou5-iuN7; i bo5 lat8 thang khoan3-bian2 pat8 lang5. (3) U7-si5 hu7-jin5-lang5 sin-khu loan2-jiok8, na7-si7 tiong7-hu chiah8 hun chin tsoe7, i e5 piN7 chin oh chhong3; u7-si5 i-seng khou2-khng3 tiong7-hu tioh8 kim3 chiah8 hun, i e5 bou2 chiah oe7 ho2. (4) SiN kiaN2 e5 koan-he7. Chiah8 hun ka-ki7 e5 sin-khu loan2-jiok8, oe7 lian5-lui7 kiaN2-ji5 ia7 loan2-jiok8; che si7 chu7-jian5 u7 hit e5 ui5- thoan5-seng3 e5 koan-he7.  An2-ni chiah e5 kiaN2-ji5, bo5 lat8 thang ti2-tng se3-kan e5 in2- iu2 be5-hek8, ti3-kau3 i koh khah khoai3 than3 tak8 teng2-ho7 e5 phaiN2; si7 i e5 chi3-khi3, i e5 sim-sin5, i e5 khui3-lat8, long2 i2-keng lam2.  KiaN2-ji5 lai5 ham7-loh8 ti7 chit khoan2 e5 tsoe7-koa3, tsoe3 chiah8 hun e5 si7- toa7 lang5 u7 toa7 kian3-siau3. 
 Liat8-ui7 tang5-liau5 iau2 teh chiah8 hun e5 thoan5-kau3-su, lin2 thak8 chit phiN liau2, na7 koh bo5 sui5-si5 koe2, lai5 phah-biat8 li2 pun2-sin chiah8 hun e5 phaiN2, chiu7 li2 beh chaiN2-iuN7 ka3-si7 kau3-hoe7 tioh8 koe2 tak8 hang7 e5 phaiN2 ah?        
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 390 koan3,1917 ni5 9 geh8)
