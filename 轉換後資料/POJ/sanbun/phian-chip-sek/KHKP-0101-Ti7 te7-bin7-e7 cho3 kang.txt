
Ti7 te7-bin7-e7 cho3 kang
Ti7 te7-bin7-chiuN7 in-ui7 lang5 siong5-siong5 eng7 chha5 si7 na2-ku2 na2-khoeh.  Ho2-tit, ti7 te7-bin7-e7 Siong7-te3 u7 pi7-pan7 thou5-thoaN3. Chit-e5 thou5-thoaN3 goan5-pun2 si7 chhiu7-bak8,  au7-lai5 hoa3-khi3 kiat cho3 teng7-tauh e5 mih8.  
Thou5-thoaN3 si7 thiap8 chit8-chan5 chit8- chan5.  U7-si5 hiah chan5 e5 siang-thau5-boe2  u7 khiau-khi2 lou7-chhut ti7 te7-bin7, na7-si7 hit- e5 lou7-hian7 e5 thou5-thoaN3 khah-siong5 si7 khah  ha7-teng2, e7-te2 si7 khah-ho2, sou2-i2 lang5 ai3 khui than2-khia7 e5 ham7 kut8 hit-e5 siong7- teng2--e5.  Kau3 ham7-te2 u7 khui than2-hoaiN5 e5 toa7-lou7; koh, ti7 toa7-lou7 e5 piN-a2 u7 khui  hang7-a2.  Long2 si7 am3-bong-bong.  Siang7-chhim e5 thou5-thoaN3-khong3 si7 saN-pah-goa7 tng7.  U7-si5 hiah-e5 than2-hoaiN5 e5 lou7 u7 chhut ti7 hai2-te2 e5 e7-te2.  Ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 pak-si3 e5 Ke-lang5-thau5 u7 thou5-thoaN3-khong3.  
In-ui7 thou5-thoaN3-khong3 si7 chhim, hong- khi3 oh-tit jip8, sou2-i2 lang5 khah-siong5 u7 ke- khui koh chit8-e5 ham7 ti7 toa7-lou7-be2, chiu7 ti7 chit-peng5 e5 ham7-kha u7 khi2 toa7-hoe2 beh in2 hit-peng5 e5 khi3 tui3 chia kek--khi2-lai5.  An2-ni-seN si7 hou7 hit-e5 khong3-lai7 thang khah thong-hong.  Thau5-chit8-e5 khui e5 ham7 si7 hou7 lang5 kap mih8 chiuN7-loh8 e5 lou7, si7 ho7-cho3 loh8-ham7.  Hit-peng5 sio-hoe2--e5 si7 ho7-cho3 khi2-ham7.  
Kut8 thou5-thoaN3 e5 kang si7 chin gui5- hiam2.  Thang hun-piat saN toa7-hang7:  
(1) Lang5 na7 beh chiuN7-loh8 ham7 chiu7 tioh8 tah8 ti7 thang2-lai7 hou7 teng2-bin7 e5 chhia  chhiuN7-khi2 lui7-loh8.  U7-si5 pak8-thang2 e5 thih-lian7 e7 tng7 hou7 lang5 tng3--si2.  
(2) Khong3-lai7 u7 kui2-na7 iuN7 e5 tok8-khi3.  Siang7-hiam2--e5 ho7-cho3 hoe2-khi3.  Phi3-ju7 thou5-khut-te2 u7 chhiu7-bak8 e5 mih8 teh noaN7 ti7 lai7-bin7, lang5 na7 eng7 koaiN2-a2 tuh8--loh8-khi3 kiau2-la7 hit-e5 thou5-moai5 chiu7 u7 puh kong2-a2 khi2-lai5.  He chui2-kong2-a2 lai7 e5 khi3 chiu7-si7 hoe2-khi3.  Hoe2-khi3 put-chi2 gau5 toh8, ia7 teh toh8 e5 si5 ai3 piak8.  An2-ni- seN, lang5 ti7 khong3-lai7 m7-thang eng7 peng5- siong5 e5 teng-a2.  Lang5 na7 ni  
chit8-ki tang5-chiam kap chit8-ki sio-khi3 khah-khoai3 thau3-koe3 tang5-chiam.  Sou2-i2 lan2 thang chai u7 mih8 gau5 in2 sio-khi3, u7 mih8 khah-ban7 in2.  Khong3-lai7 e5 hoe2-khi3 na7 tu2-tioh8 jiat8 kau3-giah8-toa7 chiu7 piak8, na7 bo5 chiu7 be7.  In-ui7 an2-ni kut8 thou5-thoaN3e5 sai-hu7 u7 chiong iu3-iu3 e5 tang5-lo5 pau i-e5 teng, chiu7 teng-lai7 toh8 e5 hoe2 e5 jiat8 na7 kau3 ti7  hit tang5-lo5, tang5-lo5 e7 in2 i si3-soaN3, sou2-i2 goa7-bin7 e5 hoe2-khi3 na7 tu2-tioh8 hit-e5 tang5- lo5 chiu7 jiat8 bo5 kau3-giah8-toa7 thang hou7 i piak8-phoa3.  
(3) U7-si5 ti7 khong3-lai7 chui2-choaN5 chhiong--chhut-lai5, iah8 u7-si5 piN-a2 kap  teng2-koa3 u7 pang--loh8-lai5.  Ui7-tioh8 chiah-e5 hong5-hiam2 e5 su7-cheng5 che7-che7 lang5  bat siu7 toa7-hai7.  
U7 chit8-jit8 u7 nng7-e5 kut8 thou5-thoaN3 e5 sai- hu7 ti7 khong3-lai7.  Chit8-e5 iau2-boe7 chhoa7- bou2, si7 sin3 Ia5-sou e5 lang5.  Koh chit8-e5 u7 bou2-kiaN2, u7 khoaN3-khin kiu3-se3 e5 chin-li2, iah8 u7 oan3-hin7 i saN-kap cho3 kang e5 phoaN7.   Tng hit-jit8 hit nng7-lang5 teh tiam3 ti7 loh8-ham7-kha e5 ui7.  Sin3 Chu2 e5 sai-hu7 hut-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 teng2-bin7 e5 sou2-chai7 teh pang--loh8-lai5 iah8 toa7-chui2 beh lau5 chhut- lai5.  I chai si7 toa7 hong5-hiam2, koh bo5- loa7-ku2 e7 teh in long2 si2.  Si5 u7 kau3 thang ka-ti7 chau2 poaN5-jip8 ti7 thang2-lai7 kio3 teng2- bin7 e5 lang5 kin2-kin2 chhiuN7 i khi2-lai5.  Teh-beh cho3 an2-ni i siuN7 i-e5 tui3-tek8 iau2-boe7 u7 chiap-lap8 Ia5-sou iah8 u7 bou2-kiaN2, sou2-i2 pek i  tioh8 koaN2-kin2 kiu3 i-e5 siN3-mia7.  I sui5-si5 than3 i-e5 ho2-phoaN7 e5 oe7 thoat-li7 chai-e7.   Sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 siu7 si2.  
Lan2 bat oan3-hin7 Ia5-sou cho3 I-e5 tui3-tek.  Tng I ti7 se3-kan u7 ki-hoe7 thang kiu3 ka-ti7.  Chong2-si7 bo5. I hoaN-hi2 siu7-si2 kiu3 lan2.  Lan2 eng-kai tioh8 siau3-liam7 I-e5 toa7-in, o-lo2 I bo5 soah.  
 (Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 101 koan3, 1893 ni5 9 geh8)
