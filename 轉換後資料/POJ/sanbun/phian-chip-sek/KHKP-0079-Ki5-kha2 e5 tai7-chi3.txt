
Ki5-kha2 e5 tai7-chi3
Ki5-kha2 chiu7-si7 Tiong-kok e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, chhut-si3 ti7 E7-mng5.  TaN beh  chiong i-e5 chhui3 sou2 ka7 Sian-siN kong2 e5 oe7  lai5 sia2 ti7 e7-te2, hou7 lin2 Tai5-oan5 e5 cha-bou2  gin2-na2 thak8-liau2 e7 khah beng5-pek8:  
I-e5 oe7 kong2, Goa2 chha-put-to kau3  chap8-hoe3, hit-si5 u7 Goa7-kok e5 lang5 lai5 ti7  E7-mng5 khui Lu2-oh8, ai3 ka3-si7 cha-bou2 gin2- na2, hak8-seng u7 e5 toa3 ti7 Lu2-oh8, u7 e5 jit8--si5  khi3 thak8 nia7-tiaN7.  U7 chit8 jit8 goa2 e5 lau7- pe7 chiu7 ka7 goa2 kong2, I ai3 khi3 Lu2-oh8  khoaN3-khoaN3--tleh; kah goa2 tioh8 kap i cho3-hoe2 khi3, ia7 kong2, Khiam2-chhai2 hit e5  Sian-siN beh chiap-lap8 li2 cho3 hak8-seng.   Goan2 lau7-bu2 thiaN-kiN3 che oe7, chiu7 chou2-chi2  kong2, Tek-khak m7 thang, e7 hou7 goa2 put- chi2 kiaN.  Goan2 lau7-pe7 chiah kong2, Sit8- chai7 m7-bian2 kiaN, hia e5 Sian-siN beh sui2  saN hou7 li2 chheng7, soa3 beh ka3 li2 thak8- chheh; taN lai5 khi3 ho2 mah?  
Goa2 thiaN-kiN3 lau7-pe7 kong2, Sui2 e5 saN  teh-beh hou7 goa2 chheng7, sim long2 bo5 siuN7  tui3 pat8-mih8-khi3; chhui3 chin ho2-taN2 kong2,  Goa2 ko2-jian5 bo5 chit8-tiam2-a2 kiaN, chin ai3  kap li2 khi3; taN chit chun7 lai5 khi3.  
Goan2 lau7-pe7 sui5-si5 chhoa7 goa2 khi3 kau3 te7; hit e5 Sian-siN chiu7 ka7 goa2 kong2, Li2  tioh8 tak8 jit8 lai5 thak8-chheh; m7-ku2 long2 bo5 kong2-khi2 sui2 e5 saN beh hou goa2 chheng7.  
Khi3 bo5 loa7-ku2, goan2 lau7-pe7 lau7-bu2 chiu7 kong2, Cha-bou2 gin2-na2 lang5 siong5-siong5 tui3  ke-nih8 chiuN7-loh8, chin-chiaN3 si7 put-pian7,  kui2-na7 hang7 e5 phaiN2-se3; chiu7 siuN7 beh hou7  goa2 chhiang5-chai7 toa3 ti7 oh8-nih8 koh khah  ho2.  Goan2 lau7-pe7 chiu7 khi3 mng7 Sian-siN  khoaN3 an2-ni e7 cho3-tit a2-be7?  Sian-siN  kong2, Ho2.  
Goa2 tiam3 hia bo5 kui2-jit8, sim-i3 siuN7  ai3 tng2-khi3 khoaN3 goan2 lau7-bu2; m7-na7 jit8-- si5 sim-bun7, me5--si5 ia7 si7 ut-chut, hau2  kau3 long2 be7 khun3-tit.  Sian-siN ti7 lau5-teng2 teh khun3 thiaN-kiN3 goa2 pi-ai e5 siaN,  chiu7 mng7, KhoaN3 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 siaN2-su7  m7 khun3,  
teh hau2?  Goa2 chiu7 in3 i, Si7 goa2,  goa2 chin chhi-chham2.  I chiu7 koh mng7,  Chham2 siaN2 tai7-chi3?  Goa2 ka7 i kong2, Si7 siuN7-tioh8 goan2 lau7-bu2, be7-tit to3-khi3 khoaN3  i.  Sian-siN u7 koh kong2, TaN m7 thang  hau2, goa2 beh thiaN3 li2 chhin-chhiuN7 lin2  lau7-bu2, li2 na7 cho3 kau3 koai-koai, u7 ho2 e5  phin2-heng7, goa2 ia7 teh-beh chhong3 sui2 e5  saN hou7 li2 chheng7.  Goa2 chiu7 liam5-piN  kong2, Ho2-nouh!  Koh siuN7-tioh8 u7 sui2-saN, sim-lai7 chiu7 an-ui3.  Kau3 bin5-a2-chai3 Sian-siN kio3 goa2 lai5 lau5-teng2, chiong goa2 e5 saN-a2-pan7 hou7 chhai5-hong5 pai5-pi2-pou3, beh  thang cho3.  Goa2 khoaN3 hit te3 pou3 choan5-choan5 si7 hoe, chin-sui2 kau3-kek8; loh8-lau5-liau2 chin hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7-kou3--tit.  
Goa2 kau3 hiah ku2 iau2-boe7 sin3 Ia5-sou, ia7 bo5 sim2-mih8 ai3 thiaN to7-li2 e5 khoan2-sit.   Kau3 pang3-ke3 hit si5, goan2 tai7-ke beh to3- khi3, Sian-siN chiu7 kio3 goa2 khi3, hoan- hu3 kong2, Ki5-kha2 ah!  Li2 na7 to3-khi3 iau3-kin2  tioh8 thong-ti lin2 lau7-bu2, kah i m7 thang  khoh pai3 put8, in-ui7 put8 m7 si7 oah8--e5, si7  si2--e5; ia7 be7 thiaN-kiN3, ia7 be7 kong2-oe7, be7  khoaN3-kiN3, be7 e7 tin2-tang7.  Tioh8 soa3 ka7 i  kong2, I eng-kai tioh8 pai3 thiN-teng2 e5  Siong7-te3.  Goa2 chiu7 in3 i kong2, Ho2, goa2  chiah beh ka7 i kong2.  
To3-khi3 hit e5-hng goan2 lau7-bu2 iu5-goan5 chiau3 si5-siong5 teh sio-hiuN, tiam2-chek,  pai3-put8; goa2 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, An2-nia5 ah!   Sian-siN u7 hoan-hu3 goa2 ka7 li2 kong2, M7 thang pai3-put8; put8 ki5-sit8 si7 si2 e5 mih8,  pai3 i tau3-ti2 bo5 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7.  Goan2  lau7-bu2 liam5-piN siu7-khi3, koh teh kiaN;  sui5-si5 tui3 put8 kong2, Che si7 gong7 gin2-na2  lam7-sam2 kong2-oe7, m7 bat saN-nng7, kiu5--li2  m7 thang siu7-khi3.  Goa2 chiu7 koh kong2,  Sian-siN ia7 teh kong2, Put8 si7 be7 khoaN3- kiN3, be7 thiaN-kiN3, ia7 be7 tin2-tang7, chin- chiaN3 si7 si2, bo5 oah8.  Goan2 lau7-bu2 it-hoat siu7-khi3, chiu7 am goa2 e5 chhui3 liam5-piN  pek-chhiat teh kiu5 hiah-e5 put8, tioh8  khoan-iong5 limn5-bin2, m7 thang khian3-chek  i-e5 choe7, ia7 m7 thang siu7-khi3.  Pai3-liau2  lau7-bu2 cho3 i khi3 chhong3 tai7-chi3; goa2 ka- ti7 teh siuN7, put8 m7 chai e7 siu7-khi3 lang5 a2- be7?  Chiah so5 siin7-oa2 i, chit8-e7 kaN2a7 khoaN3  put8 e5 bin7, khoaN3 i nng7-lui2 bak8-chiu chin  toa7-lui2, chhin-chhiuN7 teh gin5--goa2, goa2  chin-kiaN.  
Koe3 kui2-na7 jit8 tu2-tioh8 goan2 lau7-bu2  put-chi2 bo5 eng5, teh chhong3 tang,  chhong3 sai, soa3 beh khi3 chu2-png7, kah  goa2 tioh8 khi3 tiam2 hiuN-chek.  Goa2 siuN7  be7-hiau2--tit, chin3-the3 liong2-lan5, na7 beh  khi3 pai3 i, m7-ku2 thiaN-kiN3 Sian-siN kong2 e5  oe7.  Pai3-put8 sit8-chai7 tek-choe7 Siong7-te3;  chong2-si7 m7 ai3 keek8 goan2 bu2 e5 i3-su3, chiu7  kou-put-chiong oa2-khi3 tiam2.  Goa2 u7 tui3  hiah-e5 put8 kong2, Li2 m7 thang phah-sng3  goa2 sit8-sit8 teh pai3 li2, sit8-chai7 bo5 an2-ni, goa2 long2 m7 sin3 lin2 poaN3-e5; ia7 m7 si7 ui7-tioh8  lin2 lai5 sio-hiuN tiam2-chek, put-ko3 si7 kan- ta beh sun7 goan2 nia5 e5 beng7-leng7 nia7-tiaN7.  
HIit e5-hng khi3 khun3, bo5 tiuN-ti5 bang7-kiN3 put8 kap goa2 chin ke3-kau3, beh lai5  heng5-hoat8 goa2, siuN7--tioh8 chau2-khi3 Sian- siN in tau hou7 i chiau3-kou3 kau3 un2.  Sui5-si5  chiu7 kin2-kin2 chau2, ian5-lou7 chau2, ian5-lou7  thiaN-kiN3 put8 e5 kaha-pou7-siaN khok8-khok8  hau2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh jiok--goa2.  Kau3  Lu2-oh8 tui3 lau5-teng2 tit8-tit8 chau2 jip8 Sian- siN e5 khun3-pang5.  Sian-siN mng7 goa2, SiaN2- su7 kiaN kau3 an2-ni?  Goa2 chiong chit-e5  tai7-chi3 ka7 i kong2, Put8 chiin7 siu7-khi3 goa2,  beh jip8 lai5 liah8--goa2.  Teh kong2 e5 si5,  hong2-hut koh thiaN-kiN3 put8 e5 kha-pou7-siaN ti7 e7-kha teh pong3-pong3-hau2.  Sian-siN chhio3 kong2, M7-bian2 kiaN, li2 lai5 che7 tiam3  goa2 sin-piN chiu7 an-un2, put8 tek-khak m7  kaN2 chiuN7 lau5-teng2.  Goa2 chiu7 bih ti7 i kha-e7  teh che7 ia7 long2 bo5 kiaN.  Hoan-sin cheng- sin5 chiu7 chhiN2, chiong sou2 bang7-kiN3--e5 ka7  lau7-bu2 kong2.  I thiaN-liau2 chiu7 kong2,  Gong7 gin2-na2 m7 thang hou7 put8 siu7-khi3.   Chiau3 goa2 sim-lai7 teh siuN7, Sian-siN si7  khah toa7 put8, put8 m7 kaN2 khi3 hia; tui3  hit tiap8 goa2 chiu7 long2 bo5 kiaN put8.  
Iau2-ku2 goa2 long2 bo5 sit8-sim iau3-kin2 to7- li2.  Na7 u7-si5 kui7--teh ki5-to2 bong2 eng7 chhiu2 ng bak8-chiu; chong2-si7 am3-chiN7 cheng2- thau5-a2 lang3-phang7 teh thau khoaN3, si7  beh khoaN3 pat8 lang5 u7 an2-ni a2-bo5.  Goan2  Sian-siN siong5-siong5 ka7 goan2 hak8-seng kong2, Na7 ki5-to2 hit si5 bak8-chiu tioh8 pang3- kheh, in-ui7 lan2 kap Siong7-te3 teh kong2-oe7.  
Goa2 u7 chit8 e5-hng koh-chai3 bang7-kiN3  goan2 hak8-seng tai7-ke teh-beh ki5-to2, iau2-boe7 kui7-loh8--khi3 e5 si5, Sian-siN chiu7 ka7 goan2 kong2, Siong7-te3 ia7 ti7 lan2 e5 tiong-kan,  lin2 tai7-ke bak8-chiu tioh8 pang3 kheh-kheh  m7 thang khui.  Goa2 teh siuN7  
beh koh thau khoaN3, Siong7-te3 na7 u7 iaN2 lai5, goa2 chiu7 thang khoaN3-kiN3 I; sou2-i2 bak8-chiu bi5-khui  chit8-e7 lio2, hut-jian8 khoaN3-kiN3 kng na3-na3 tit8-tit8 pek-oa2 goa2 seng-khu, goa2 kiaN kau3 ngiauh8-ngiauh8-chhoah, chhut-siaN teh  hiam3 phah-chhiN2 goan2 lau7-bu2.  I chiu7 mng7 goa2, KiaN siaN2-su7?  Goa2 chiah ka7 i kong2, Bang7-kiN3 khoaN3-tioh8 Siong7-te3, kiaN  kau3 beh si2--khi3.  I chiu7 an-ui3 goa2, kah  goa2 m7 thang kiaN, bang7-kiN3 long2 bo5 iaN2,  ia7 m7 thang koh siuN7-khi2 hit-ho7 e5 su7.  
Goa2 chiu7 koh siuN7, khiam2-chhai2 Siong7- te3 pun2-simn e7 hou7 goa2 bang7-kiN3, si7 beh lai5  chi2-si7 goa2, hou7 goa2 na7 teh ki5-to2 si5 bak8- chiuu7 m7 thang khui.  Au7-lai5 goa2 chiaN3  m7 kaN2 khui-khoaN3; tu2-tioh8 lang5 ki5-to2,  goa2 e5 bak8-chiu siong5-siong5 u7 pang3-kheh.  
Koh bo5 loa7-ku2 goa2 chiu7 khah siong5-se3  teh thiaN Sian-siN e5 ka3-si7, Siong7-te3 u7 in-tian2 siuN2-su3 goa2, kai-chai3 Seng3-sin5 khui  goa2 e5 sim, hou7 goa2 khah e7 hiau2-tit to7-li2,  ti3-kau3 sin3 kho3 Ia5-sou; sou2-i2 in u7 chiap- lap8 goa2 cho3 kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5.
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3,te 79 tiuN, 1891 ni5 12 geh8)
