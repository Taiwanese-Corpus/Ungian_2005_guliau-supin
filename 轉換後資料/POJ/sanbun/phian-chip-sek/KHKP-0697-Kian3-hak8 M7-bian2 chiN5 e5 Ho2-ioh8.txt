Kian3-hak8 M7-bian2 chiN5 e5 Ho2-ioh8
Goa2 bo5 kong2, lin2 kiam2 ioh e7 tioh8?  E7 lah.  
Chiu7-si7 jit8-kng.  Tui3 goan5 khi2- thau5 kau3 kin-a2 jit8.  Thai3 iong5 hoat-sia7 kng, u7 chheng-chheng ban7-ban7 e5 be2-lat8, lai5 li7-ek chit-e5 se3-kai3.  Lan2 e5 siN-oah8, tui3 but8-chit hong-bin7 lai5 khoaN3, choan-choan si7 oa2-kho3 i.  TaN kan2-liok8 ki3 kui2- hang7 ti7 chia, ng3-bang7 u7 sim-sek.  
1.Tak8-jit8 tioh8 khui khun3-pang5 e5 thang, hou7 jit8-kng chio3 jip8-lai5.  Chiu7 pang5-lai7 u7 chhau3-bi7, siN-kou, chhau3-phu2, kap khong-khi3 tiong u7 thon5-jiam2 lang5 e5 bai2-kheng2, chhin-chhiuN7 kam2-mou7, chhut-phiah8, chhut chu, thai2-ko, lo5- siong, chiong2-chiong2 e5 piN7-khun2, na7 tu2-tioh8 jit8-kng, chiu7 e7 biat8-bo5.  
2.Bang2-ta3, phoe7-toaN, mi5-chioh, tah-tah-bih8, tioh8 siong5-siong5 poaN chhut-lai5 phak8 jit8.  Na7 bo5, khun3 liau2 e7 kut-thau5 sng, hong thiaN3.  PeN7-lang5 sou2 eng7 e5 chhim2-ku7, koh khah tioh8 jit8- kng siau-tok8.  
3.Seng-khu tioh8 phak8-jit8.  Che ho7-cho3 jit8-kong ek8.  
Lang5 na7 jiam2-tioh8 io5-siong piN7, tak8- jit8 tioh8 thng3 saN phak8-jit8.  Jit8-kng u7 lat8, e7 jip8 lang5 e5 sin-the2 phah-biat8 bai2-kheng2.  Lang5 e5 hoeh-lun5, u7 ang5 kap peh8.  Tai7-khai3, ang5 hoeh-lun5 na7 u7 500 liap8, peh8 hoeh-lun5 u7 chit8-liap8.  Peh8 hoeh-lun5 teh phah-biat8 tok8-mih8, chhin-chhiuN7 peng-tui7 teh phah thou2-hui2.   Jit8-kng e5 lai7-bin7 u7 chit8-chiong2 e5 kng, e7 pang-chan7 peh8 hoeh-lun5 khah kiong5, na7 siu7 chit-chiong2 e5 kng, hoeh-lun5 ti2- khong3 bai2-kheng2 e5 lat8, e7 ke-thiN 100 hun7 e5 40 kau3 70 e5 lat8.  Lang5 khi2-thau5 beh jit8-kng ek8, 5 hun cheng chiu7 ho2, m7-thang siuN ku2, au7-lai5 chiam7 chin3, kau3 10 hung teng2-teng2, seng-khu ia7-si7 chiam7 chin3, pi2-pheng7 khi2-thau5 phak8 kha, au7-lai5 phak8 kha-tang5, au7-lai5 toa7-thui2.  Lang5 na7 kam2-mou7, a2-si7 koaN5-jiat8, in m7-thang phak8, tioh8 chiam3 theng5 kau3 in e5 piN7 ho2.  
 
4.Lang5 khiam3 jit8-kng u7 sun2-hai7.    
Hoat-siN nng2-kut piN7.  Lang5 e5 sin- the2 lai7 u7 chit8-khoan2 e5 mih8, e7 kap jit8- kng lian5-hap8, lai5 che3-cho7 bi2-ta-bin2 ti2 (Vitamin D).  Khiam3 jit8-kng chiu7 be7 che3-cho7; khiam3 bi2-ta-bin2 ti2, kut be7 teng7.  Gin2-na2 na7 bo5 oan5-choan5 siu7 jit8-kng chio3, iu5-goan5 u7 hai7.  Lang5 u7 khi2 nng7 long5 e5 chhu3, lai5 chhi3-giam7.  Chit8 long5 e5 thang, u7 jip8 po-le5, chit8 long5 bo5.   Toa3 hit keng bo5 e5, hiah-e5 gin2-na2 hoat- iok8 khah ho2.  
TaN tak8-jit8 cha2 liu-liu, Siong7-te3 tui3 hng7-hng7 e5 jit8-thau5 khi2, 305,724,090 li2, sang3 chit-ho7 bo5 ke3-po2 e5 Ho2-ioh8, lai5 siuN2-su3 lan2, u7 sim-koaN e5 lang5 lah! Tioh8  kam2-in bo5 soah.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 697 ho7, 1947 ni5 1 geh8)
