
Chin7-tiong kau3 si2
Lang5 na7 chin7-tiong cho3 Chu2 e5 hak8-seng beh tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8 po3-siuN2?   Chiau3 Bek8-si7-liok8 kong2 chiu7-si7 oah8 e5 bian2-liu5.  Chong2-si7 ti7 chit se3-kan u7-si5  tui3 chin7-tiong ko2-jian5 bo5 tit-tioh8 oah8, hoan2-tng2 tu2-tioh8 si2.  Chhin-chhiuN7 kui2- na7 ni5 cheng5 Tiong-kok u7 Gi7-ho5 Thoan5- hui2 hoan2-loan7, hit-tiap8 bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 mia5-siaN e5 Ki-tok-tou5 seN3 Chou mia5 Hi-mou7, ni5 30 hoe3.  I e5 lau7-bo2 57 hoe3, toa7-chi2 36, ia7 hu7-jin5-lang5 19 hoe3.  Tng jiau2-loan7 hit si5 loa7 che7 peng5-iu2 khou2- khng3 i tioh8 chau2.  I m7; jian5-au7 choan5-ke hou7 thou2-hui2 liah8-khi3, soa3 sio in e5 chhu3-theh8 kap ke-si mih8-kiaN7.  Thou2-hui2 ka7 in pak8-teh, eng7 chhia chai3 kau3 thou2- hui2-thau5 e5 gu7-sou2 lai5 mng7 khoaN3 tioh8 an3-choaN2 pan7.  
Thou2-hui2-thau5 in3 kong2, Goa2 m7 ai3 khoaN3 in, ka7 in chai3 to3-tng2 khi3 lin2 liah8 e5 sou2-chai7 chiah ka7 in thai5 chiu7 ho2.  To3-tng2-lai5 hit-si5 tai7-ke ti7 chhia-nih8 ian5-lou7  gim5-si kong2,   
“Ia5-sou iau2 chhoa7 lou7,  
Kau3 thian-tong5 mng5-hou7,  
To7-lou7 sui-jian5 chhiN-chhin3 lan5-heng5,  
Lan2 tioh8 kian-sim ho2-taN2 chin3-cheng5:  
Goan7 Chu2 chhiu2 lai5 khan,  
Kau3 chou2-ke peng5-an.” 
Kau3 in pun2 sia7 phoe5 chit8 ui7 khang3-te7 chiu7 loh8 chhia.  In tai7-seng chiong  toa7 ki to thai5 Hi-mou7 e5 thau5, teh ng3-bang7 hiah e5 cha-bou2 lang5 khoaN3 beh  hoan2-hoe2 hang5-hok8.  I e5 lau7-bo2 ka7 in kong2, Lin2 i2-keng thai5 goa2 e5 hau7-seN tioh8  soa3 ka7 goa2 thai5, chiu7 sui5-si5 hou7 in thai5- si2.  Iau2 nng7-e5 iu5-goan5 kian-sin3.  I e5 toa7-  
chi2 kong2, Goa2 e5 lau7-bo2 kap sio2-ti7 ki3-jian5 si2, taN tioh8 thai5 goa2.  Si2-liau2 kan-ta chhun i siau3-lian5 e5 bou2, chiu7 soa3 kong2, Lin2 u7 thai5 goa2 e5 tiong7-hu, goan2 an3-nia5 chham a-che2, lau5 goa2 si7 bo5 lou7- eng7, tioh8 soa3 thai5-si2 khah ho2.  Chiau3 an2-ni khoaN3 chit si3-e5 long2 ui7-tioh8 to7-li2 chin7-tiong kau3 si2. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 296 koan3, 1909 ni5 11 geh8)
