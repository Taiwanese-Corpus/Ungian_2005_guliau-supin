
Kian3-hak8 Chhan5-eN li2 u7 siong5 khoaN3 tioh8 bo5?
Siong5, bo5 kui-ni5 long2 u7.  Put-lun7 ti7 Tai5-oan5, a2-si7 Tng5-soaN to long2 u7 khoaN3-tioh8.  Seng-khu u7 6 ki kha; nng7-tui3 sit8, toa7 koh poh8-tiah.  Sek-ti7 u7-e5 chhiah, u7-e5 lek8.  Poe e5 lat8 chin toa7, kin2 chhin-chhiuN7 hong.  Thau5-khak u7 nng7-lui2 bak8-chiu phong3-chhut koh thang-kng.  Che bak8-chiu si7 ke2--e5, chhong3 an2-ni, si7 beh phah chhiN-kiaN pat8-mih8 e5 oah8-mih8.  Chin bak8-chiu ti7 chhui3-phoe2 nih, chin se3.  Chhui3 khoah, chhui3-phoe2 teng7, sou2-i2 gau5 ka7 mih8.  
I sou2 chiah8 e5 mih8, si7 bang2-a2, hou5-sin5, boe2-iah8, hit khoan2 e5 thang5-thoa7.  Seng3-cheng5 tham chiah8, chit8-tiam2 cheng ku2, e7 chiah8 kui2-na7 chap8 chiah.  Gin2-na2 siong5-siong5 ai3 liam chhan5-eN, tioh8 khng3 i m7-thang.  In-ui7 chhan5-eN si7 teh chiah8 hai7- thang5, u7 li7-ek lan2.  
Lan2 bat khoaN3 tioh8 chhan5-eN teh poe, kin7 chui2-bin7, seng-khu au2-khiau, eng7 boe2-a2 tam-chui2.  He si7 teh siN nng7.  I chhiu-thiN pang3 nng7, kau3 me5-ni5 chhun-thiN chiah pu7-chhut.  Nng7 loh8 ti7 chui2-lai7, hek8-si7 liam5 ti7 chioh8-nih, a2-si7 tiau5 ti7 chhau2-teng2; ti7 hia pu7-chhut cho3 se3-boe2 thang5.  I u7 6 ki kha, chiam7-chiam7 toa7, chiu7 hoat-sit8.  Che thang5 mia5 chui2-khit-chiah8.  Seng-khu tng5 nng7-chhun3, khoah nng7-hun poaN3 khah ke, sek-ti7 bo5 sui2, khah oa2 chang-sek.  Seng3-cheng5 hiong-ok, siong5-siong5 ka7 se3-be2 thang5 khi3 chiah8.  Kau3 u7 chit8-e5 si5-chun7, sin-the2 ia3-sian7 bo5 lat8, chiah8-liong7 kiam2 chio2, chiu7 so5 khi3 oa2 chui2-piN e5 chhau2-teng2, khi5 ti7 hia, ka-chiah pit-lih8, u7 pat8-khoan2 e5 heng5-the2 nng3 chhut-lai5; sit8 chiu7 hoat tng5.  Che chiu7-si7 lan2 sou2 khoaN3 e5 chhan5-eN.  Sui2-sui2 e5 sin-the2, chu7-iu5 chu7-chai7, poe-lai5 poe-khi3.  Lan2 u7 him-bou7, beh tit-tioh8 chit-khoan2 bo5?  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 708 ho7, 1947 ni5 12 geh8)
