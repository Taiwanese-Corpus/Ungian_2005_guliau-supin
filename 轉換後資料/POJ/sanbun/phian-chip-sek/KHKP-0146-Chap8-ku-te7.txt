
Chap8-ku-te7
Ti7 Se-lek8 5 goeh8 te7 8 ho7, chiu7-si7 Tiong-lek8 4 goeh8 chhe 7 jit8 i2-keng kau3- han7; hit-jit8 e5 liau2-au7 tiam3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 lang5 long2 sng3 cho3 Jit8-pun2 e5 peh-siN3. Chong2-si7 lang5 m7-bian2 khoa3-lu7 kiaN-liau2 koe3 hit-jit8 in beh liam5-piN siat sim2-mih8 sin-khoan2 ai3 khou2-chhou2 peh-siN3 e5 hoat-tou7. Liong7-pit kaN2 e7 chha-put-to chhim-chiuN7 chit-tiap8 e5 khoan2, khiok si7 ng3-bang7 khoaN3 e7-tit-thang na2-ku2 na2 khoaN3-oah8.     
Chi2-u7 hun-piat chiah-e5 goan2 goa7-kok lang5 sou2 khia7-khi2 thong-siong e5 sou2-chai7. In Jip8-pun2 i2-keng u7 chhut kou3-si7 kong2 na7 u7 lang5 iu5-goan5 ai3 siok8 Chheng-kok m7-ai3 jip8 Jip8-pun2 chek8, in iau2-ku2 thang toa3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 sng3 cho3 goa7-kok lang5, chong2-si7 be7-tit-thang iu5-goan5 tak8 sou2- chai7 khia7-khi2, tek-khak tioh8 chhin- chhiuN7 goan2 tiam3 ti7 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 sou2-chai7, kio3-cho3 Chap8-ku-te7.  Tai5-lam5 siok8 e5 Chap8-ku-te7 chiu7-si7 Ki5-au7, An-peng5, Hu2-siaN5 Toa7-sai-goa7 i2-kip8 Hu2-siaN5-lai7 e5 tang-lam5-kak, chiu7-si7 goan2 Sin-lau5 si3-chiu-ui5 e5 te7-hng.  (Lang5 sou2 hong-siaN kong2 siaN5-lai7 u7 koah chit8-te3 khi2--lai5 hou7 Tai7-Eng-kok koan2 si7 bo5 iaN2, put-ko3 si7 tiah chit8-e5 Chap8-ku-te7 hou7 goa7-kok lang5 thang toa3).  Tiong-kok lang5 tiam3 ti7 hit si3-ui7 bo5 tek-khak siok8 Tai7-chheng-kok, na7 beh cho3 Jip8-pun2 e5 peh-siN3 iu5-goan5 thang.  Lang5 na7 cho3 Chheng-kok lang5 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 goa7-kok lang5 e5 khoan2, chiu7-si7 na7 chhut-goa7 tioh8 tho2 chip-chiau3, ti7 Chap8-ku-te7 goa7 ia7 be7 koan2- tit giap8, chit-chun7 na7 u7 giap8 tek-khak tioh8 be7--lang5.  Chong2-si7 phah-sng3 bo5 Tiong-kok e5 Leng2-su7-koaN beh lai5 chia toa3, thang the3 in chhut-thau5. 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 146 koan3, 1897 ni5 5 geh8)
