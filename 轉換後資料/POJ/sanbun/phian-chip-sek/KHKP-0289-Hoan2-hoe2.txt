
 
Hoan2-hoe2
Kui2-na7 ni5 cheng5 ti7 Eng-kok e5 sai- lam5 peng5 u7 chit8 chiah chun5 hoan7-hong, hou7 hong-eng2 phah khi3 hai2-kiN5, kho3-tioh8 chhian2, chiu7 tiau5 ti7-hia be7 kiaN5.  Na2-ku2 na2 hou7 hai2-soa im-tim5 chha-put-to bo5 thang ng3-bang7 koh phu5 thang kiaN5  chhut-khi3.  Chong2-si7 tan2 kau3 chui2 teh- beh koh toa7 tiN7, hit-si5 lang5 ti7 si3-piN khui  kau ng3-bang7 hai2-chui2 lau5 jip8-lai5 thang hou7 chun5 phu5 chhut-khi3.  
Kau3 chui2 teh toa7 tiN7 hit jit8 chin che7 lang5 khia7 ti7 hai2-kiN5 teh khoaN3.  In khoaN3 chui2 na2-ku2 na2-chhim, chong2-si7 chun5 iu5- goan5 tiau5-teh.  Kau3 boe2 hiah e5 lang5 hut-jian5-kan toa7 siaN han2 tah-chhiu2-chiuN2 teh toa7 hoaN-hi2.  Si7 sim2-mih8 ian5-kou3?  Si7 chun5 u7 kiaN5 chhut hai2-bin7 bo5?  M7-si7.  Si7 chun5 u7 phu5 soa2 ui7 bo5?  Ia7 m7-si7.  Na7-si7 an2-ni lang5 teh toa7 siaN han2, si7 hoaN-hi2 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3 leh!  Khiok si7 kan-ta in-ui7 khoaN3-kiN3 hit chiah chun5 teh sio2- khoa2 tin2-tang7 na7-tiaN7.  
Phi3-lun7 lang5 hoan7-choe7 na2-chun2 chhin-chhiuN7 hit chiah chun5 hoan7-hong kho3 chhian2, hou7 soa na2-im na2-tim5, teh beh hai7-khi3.  Chong2-si7 lang5 khui-thiah  to7-li2 hou7 i u7 lou7, iu7-kiam Siong7-te3 e5 in- tian2 lai5 ui5 i, kau3-boe2 i e5 sim na7 sio2-khoa2  kam2-tong7 lai5 hoan2-hoe2 chiu7 ti7 Siong7-te3 kap thiN-sai3 e5 bin7-cheng5 ui7-tioh8 i lai5  hoaN-hi2 ia7 si7 an2-ni. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 289 koan3, 1909 ni5 4 geh8)
