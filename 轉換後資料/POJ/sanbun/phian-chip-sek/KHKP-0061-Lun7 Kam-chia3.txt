Lun7 Kam-chia3

Chit gou7 goeh8 tou5 si7 oe7 goa7-kok kheh-chia3 sek8-thng5 e5 tai7-chi3.  Teng2-bin7 u7 chia3-hng5; kheh-chia3 e5 khi3-ku7 khoaN3 be7 tioh8, chong2-si7 e7-bin7 tiong-ng thang khoaN3-kiN3 thih-kng2 lau5 chia3-chiap loh8 toa7-thang2.  Ki5-u5--e5 tou5-a2-lai7 u7 lang5 sek8-thng5 ti7 thng5-tiaN2 teh la7, u7-e5 teh koe3-li7 chia3-chiap hou7 i chheng-khi3, ia7 u7-e5 sek8-liau2 iuN2-thng5 loh8 lau7-chhai3.
 Tai5-oan5 e5 kam-chia3 u7 ang5-e5 peh8-e5, nng7 iuN7; ang5-e5 kio3-cho3 ang5-chia3, peh8-e5 kio3-cho3 tek-chia3.  Chhut-sek sit8-chai7 chin7-che7, tak8-ni5 thng5 chhut put-siau2.  Na7-si7 hng5 u7 ho2 phaiN2, thng5 u7 ou peh8; ou-e5 chng-loh8 thng5-lang2, peh8-e5 iuN2-loh8 lau7-chhai3.  Kho2-sioh lan2 chit-peng5 chhong3-phou7 sek8-thng5 e5 khi3-ku7 m7-ho2, kai3-thng5 e5 kang-hu bo5 kau3, m7-tat8-tioh8 goa7-kok e5 khi3-ku7 khiau2-sit, kai3-thng5 e5 hoat-tou7 chin peh8, nia2 m7 chhin-chhiuN7 goa7-kok e5 khi3-ku7 lai5 an2-ni cho3 leh ?
 Ai3-beh chhah kam-chia3, u7 sin-chai ia7 u7 ji7-ni5.  Cham2 chia3-boe2 chit8-chat, chiong5-tiong e5 nng7-bak8 seng chim3-chui2, theng3-hau7 hng5 le5 ho2, eng7 chia3-boe2 lai5 chhah, chiu7-si7 sin-chai.  Kam-chia3 chho3-liau2, iu5-goan5 lau5 hiah-e5 ku7 chia3-thau5 hou7 i hoat, m7-bian2 koh-chai3 chhah, ho7-cho3 ji7-ni5--e5.  Hou7 in nng7-ho7 khoai3-hoat, iau3-kin2 tioh8 loh8 pui5-pun3, ni5-tang e5 hou7-chui2 ia7 tioh8 tu2-ho2; oa2 chap8-goeh8-kan i2-keng toa7-chang5 chia3-chiap kau7, chiah e7 piaN3-hng5 ho2 kheh.
 Lai5 cheng2 chit8-tiuN phou7 khi2-lai5, tioh8 u7 saN-si3-pah e5 gin5-chiN5.  Sui-jian5 phou7-teng5 si7 chhau2 tah--e5, m7-ku2 khi3-ku7 teh siong-tiong7, sou2-i2 tak8-ui7 e5 choh-sit lang5 khah siong5 tioh8 saN-kap chhut-chiN5, lai5 cheng2-phou7 khia7-koa3----chit8 koa3 u7 saN-chiah gu5, kiong7 tioh8 chiaN5-chap8 koa3.  Iu7-koh in tioh8 be2 chioh8-chhia chit8-hu3, thng5-tiaN2 lak8-chhit-e5, toa7-thang2 chit8 nng7 kha, chhia-ko5 chit8-ki.  ChhiaN3-kang e5 lang5 iu5-goan5 u7 kui2-na7 ho7, u7-e5 hiaN5-hoe2 kio3-cho3 hoe2-kong, u7-e5 teh kheh-chia3 kio3-cho3 gu5-po5, ia7 u7 sek8-thng5—e5, chiu7-si7 chheng-cho3 sai-hu7.
Lun7 pun2-te7 lang5 kheh-kam-chia3, in phou7-lai7 e5 chioh8-chhia teng2-e7 u7 nng7-te3 pang, chhia-lai7 u7 nng7-ki sim, chit8-ki loh8 chhia-ko5 hou7 chui2-gu5 teh-tioh.  Na7 tit8-tit8 tng2, chit-liap8 toe3 hit-liap8, kam-chia3 tui3 chioh8-chhia tiong-ng e7 kheh koe3-khi3, chia3-phoh e7 tui3 hiuN3-peng5 jih8-chhut--lai5.  An2-ni kheh saN-pai2, hou7 chia3-phoh ta-ta-ta, chia3-chiap chiu7 long2 tui3 e7-te2 tek-kong2-lai7 lau7-loh8 thng5-tiaN2.  In-ui7 tu2-a2 lau7-loh8 e5 si5, chit-ho7 chiap si7 chin kiaN-lang5, sou2-i2 tioh8 chham hoe-hun2 loh8-khi3 la7, ia7 tioh8 iuN2-loh8 se-thai lai5 koe3-li7 ti7 toa7-thang2; koh, tioh8 piaN3-koe3 thng5-tiaN2 hou7 i kun2, chit-tiaN2 koe3 hit-tiaN2; au7-lai5 thng5-phoeh8 khioh liau2-li7, chiu7 e7 piN3-cho3 thng5, pian7-pian7 thang chng-loh8 lang2 hoat-be7.
 TaN u7 iau3-kin2 e5 siau-sit ai3 thong-ti lin2, chiu7-si7 kin7-lai5 goan2 chou2-ke u7 chit8-e5 chhut-mia5 sian-siN teh kong2-khi2, “Kam-chia3 u7 chi2.”  Hit-e5 chi2 si7 kiat ti7 kam-chia3-boe2--nih8, se3-se3 liap8-a2 ai3 chhoe7 tioh8 eng7 ham3-kiaN3 lai5 khoaN3.  Sian-siN pun2-sin kau3-kham3 liau2, chiong kui2-liap8 cheng3-loh8 thou5-kha hou7 in kin2-kin2 hoat-khi2--lai5.  I ia7 teh phah-sng3 na7-si7 choh-sit lang5 tak8 sou2-chai7 gau5 tui3-tiong7 chit-ho7 su7, bo5-loa7-ku2 thng5 e7 khah che7, koh e7 teng5-poe7 khah pan-gi5.  Che kiam2 m7-si7 hou7 lan2 chai lai5 kang2-kiu3 Siong7-te3 sou2 chhong3-cho7 e5 mih8 chin-chiaN3 u7 toa7 lou7-eng7?  Lang5 tak8-jit8 tioh8 chiah8 e5 bi2-niu5 pun2 si7 kiam5-e5 khah siong5, m7-ku2 ngou2-bi7-lai7 iu5-goan5 ia7 tioh8 tiN, sou2-i2 thng5 si7 hou7 lan2 cho3 iau3-kin2 e5 mih8-kiaN7; chhin-chhiuN7 siok8-gi2 kong2, “Chiah8 kiam5 it-tioh8 tiN, chiah8 tiN it-tioh8 kiam5.”
(Tai5-l?m H?-si?A Kau3-hoe7 Po3 Te7 61 Ho7, 1890 ni5 5 goeh8)
