
Kam2-in e5 Sim
A-pek-liap8-han2 khah-iaN5 tui3-tek8 liau2- au7, chiu7 chiong sou2 cheng-toat8 e5 mih8  chap8 hun7 thiu chit8 hun7 hian3 hou7 Bek8- ki-se2-tek, cho3 keng3-hong7 e5 le2-but8.  Che  si7 hian2-beng5 A-pek-liap8-han2 u7 kam2-in e5 sim in-ui7 Siong7-te3 chou7 i tek-seng3.  Lang5 na7 chin chai Siong7-te3 e5 jin5-ai3 chu7-jian5 ai3 hian3 le2-gi5 lai5 piau2-beng5 kam2-sia7 e5 khian5-i3.  Tui3 lang5 teh hian3 le2-gi5 e5 khoan2-sit, chiu7 thang khoaN3 chhut hit e5 lang5 sit8-chai7 jiat8-sim teh hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 a2-bo5.  
Lun7 A-pek-liap8-han2 sou2 hian3-e5 kho2- kham cho3 cheng3 Sin3-tou5 chin3-hian3 e5 kui- le7.  Lun7 Hi-pek-lai5 lang5 e5 san2-giap8, hek8- si7 tui3 chhan5-hng5, hek8-si7 tui3 cheng-seN,  hek8-si7 tui3 chian3 seng3 sou2 tit-tioh8 chai5- but8, eng2 tioh8 seng chap8 hun7 tah8 chit8  hun7 hian3 hou7 Siong7-te3, au7-lai5 chiong ki5- u5--e5 lai5 ka-ti7 eng7.  Lun7 in ka-ti7 eng7 hit kau2 hun7 ko2-jian5 thang tit-tioh8 toa7 hok- khi3, in-ui7 i2-keng tai7-seng thiu chap8 hun7 chit8 khi2-lai5.  Chai7 in khiok siong5-siong5 siuN7, na7 bo5 an2-ni seng thiu hit chit8 hun7,  tek-khak be7 an-sim hiong2-siu7 Siong7-te3 su3 in e5 hok-khi3.  Ui7-tioh8 an2-ni-seN sou2-i2  in tioh8 siong5-siong5 chhun5-pan7 khek-ki2  e5 sim lai5 po3-tap Siong7-te3.  
Tioh8 khoaN3 in sou2 chun5 e5 sim-i3 chiu7 chai Siong7-te3 u7 tui3-tiong7 a2-bo5.  Lang5  na7 chhut tui3 seng5-sim i3-ai3 lai5 hian3, Siong7-te3 chiah hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8; siat-su2 na7 bo5 hit ho7 sit8-sim, sui-bong2 u7 hian3 iau2-ku2 Siong7-te3 liah8-cho3 chhin-chhiuN7  khang-khang.  Lun7 Hi-pek-lai5 e5 lang5 chhin-kin7 Siong7-te3 lai5 hian3 le2-mih8, chai7  in liah8 che cho3 kek8 toa7 e5 hok-khi3; na7 be7 an2-ni cho3 chiu7 eng2 liah8 cho3 te7 it  leng5-jiok8 e5 su7.	  
TaN Hi-pek-lai5 lang5 e5 hong-hoat thang cho3 lan2 cheng3 lang5 e5 bou5-iuN7.  Lan2 Ki- tok-tou5 m7 thang khah su in, tioh8 ki3-tit Po2-lo5 bat hoan-hu3 Ko-lim5-to lang5 kong2,  Mui2 chhit jit8 e5 thau5 chit8 jit8 lin2 chiah e5 sui5 lang5 chiau3 i sou2 siu jip8 lai5 chek-he7.   Lun7 lang5 mui2 ni5 e5 tek-li7 lai5 thiu chap8 hun7 chit8 khiok si7 ho2 koa3 iong5-iN7 than3 e5  hoat-tou7.  Sin3-tou5 khiok bo5 hou7 lut8-hoat pe2-pak8; na7 chhut tui3 bian2-kiong2, bo5  sim2-mih8 kam-goan7 lai5 hian3 le2-gi5, Siong7-te3 chiu7 bo5 hoaN-hi2 kam3-lap8.  Chong2-si7 na7 chin-chiaN3 seng5-sim hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, chiu7 hit ho7 bian2-kiong2 e5 khek-ki2 e7 piN3   
cho3 him-bou7 i3-ai3.  Lang5 na7 chiong sou2 cheng-toat8 e5 mih8 lai5 saN-pun, hit-tiap8 sim si7 hoaN-hi2; chong2-si7 na7 chiong hit e5 mih8 e5 siang7 ho2-e5 lai5 hian3 hou7 Chu2, sim  iau2-ku2 khah hoaN-hi2.  
Chiau3 Hi-pek-lai5 lang5 sou2 than3 e5 kui- le7, thang ka3-si7 lan2 chai te7 it toa7 e5 hok- khi3 si7 tui3 sim e7 kam-goan7 hian3 seN-chhut-lai5.  Si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Ma2-liap8-ki  kong2, “Tioh8 lap8 chap8 hun7 e5 chit8 hun7 jip8 ti7 khou3-pang5 hou7 tiam3 ti7 goa2 e5 chhu3  tit-tioh8 ho2 mih8, chiong che lai5 chhi3 Goa2, theng3-hau7 Goa2 phoa3-khui thiN e5 khiah,  chiong u7 chhun e5 hok-khi3 piaN3 loh8-lai5  hou7 li2 sng3 be7-tit liau2.” 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-poe3 te7 295 koan3, 1909 ni5 10geh8)
