
 
Leng5-jiok8 chiaN5-cho3 Eng5-kng
U7-si5 lang5 kong2-oe7 bo5 sim2-mih8 tu2-ho2, si7 tui3 in bo5 chim-chiok siuN7, kan-ta  khoaN3 goa7-phoe5, bo5 kiu3-kham3 chin-sit8.  Ti3-kau3 liah8 kian3-siau3 cho3 eng5-hian2, liah8 the2-bian7 cho3 kian3-siau3.  Chai7-cha2 su3-tou5 hou7 in e5 si7-toa7 lang5 liah8 khi3 koaiN-kaN  siu7 phah , in liah8 cho3 eng5-hian2 (Heng7-toan7 5 :40 , 41 ) ; kiaN-liau2 lan2 na7 tu2-tioh8 hit ho7 e5 tai7-chi3 chiu7 beh liah8 cho3 chin lau3-khui3.  Tong-si5 Ia5-sou si2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3, hit-tiap8 chiau3 lang5 khoaN3 si7 tu2-tioh8 toa7 kian3-siau3 kek8 leng5-jiok8; i-e5 tui3-tek8 long2 tek-seng3 the2-bian7 to3 khi3 in chhu3.  Chong2--si7 kiu3-sit8 u7 an2-ni a2-bo5 ? Lan2 tioh8 chim-chiok siuN7 chiaN3 chai, hit chit8 jit8 chi2-u7 Ia5-sou u7 mia5-siaN the2-bian7, ki5-u5 cheng3-lang5 long2 kian3-siau3 lak-lian2.  TaN lai5 siuN7 in chit8-lang5 chit8-lang5 e5 cheng5-heng5, khoaN3 u7-iaN2 si7 an2-ni a2-bo5.      
1. Pun2-tiu Pi2-liap2-to.  I si7 Lo5-ma2 kok e5 toa7-koaN, teh koan2 Iu5-thai3 Sat-  ma2-li7-a nng7 seng2 , u7 che7-che7 peng siok8 i koan2-hat , peh-seN3 long2 tioh8 sun7-than3 i ; i u7 koan5 thang hou7 lang5 si2 , ia7 u7 koan5 thang ka7 lang5 thau2-pang3.  Hit-jit8 gi2-tiaN7 Ia5-sou e5 si2-choe7 liau2-au7 i to3-khi3 ku7 ong5-kiong u7 lou5-pok8 lu2-pi7 teh hok8-sai7 i; i thang toa3 ti7 hia khoai3-lok8, bo5 lang5 kaN2 ui5-keh8 i.  An2-ni kiam2 m7-si7 toa7 mia5-siaN ?  Ki5-sit8 ti7 hit jit8 chin iu2-khui3.  In-ui7 i chai Ia5-sou si7 bo5 choe7 e5 lang5 , siuN7 beh thau2-pang3 i , m7-ku2 m7-kaN2 chu7-choan.  I si7 koaN-hu2, iau2-ku2 hou7 peh-seN3 chit8-e7 haN2, i chiu7 hang5-hok8 in.  I chin khoaN3-khin hiah-e5 che3-si thak8-chheh lang5, iau2-ku2 hou7 in ah loh8-thau5 khi3 chiu7 in, chiaN5 in-e5 phaiN2 i3-su3.  M7-na7 an2-ni , koh kau3 hian7-kim thiN-e7 ban7-kok long2 u7 tit-tioh8 hok-im, che7-che7 lang5 chai Pi2-liap8-to e5 cheng5-iu5 , m7-ku2 bo5 chit8-lang5 o-lo2 i-e5 beng5-bong7, tak8 lang5 long2 hiam5 i bo5 taN2 m7 chiaN5 koaN-hu2 e5 the2-thong2 ; in-ui7 hit jit8 thiN-e7 te7-it ho2 e5 lang5 lai5 hou7 i sim2-mng7 , i ia7 bo5 chai5-tiau7 thang po2-hou7 i.      
2. Hi-lut8-oan5.  Hit-jit8 i lai5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 che7 i-e5 cho7-ui7 , i-e5 jin5-sun5 long2  ui5 teh ka7 i phou-pai5 ; i u7 thiaN-kiN3 Ia5-sou e5 tai7-chi3, ku2-ku2 ai3 khoaN3 i.  TaN Pi2-liap8-  to ke3 Ia5-sou khi3 hou7 i pan7 ; chai7 Hi-lut8-ong5 phah-sng3 Ia5-sou un2-tang3 hoaN-hi2 than3  i-e5 i3-su3 beh kiaN5 chit8-e5 khah ki5-koai3 e5 su7, hou7 ong5 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3, khoaN3  liau2 chiu7 khah kam-sim lai5 thau2-pang3 i.  M7-ku2 Ia5-sou m7-na7 bo5 kiaN5 hit-ho7 su7 ,  lian5 kok-ong5 teh mng7 i , ia7 bo5 chhut-siaN in chit8 ku3 ; che2 si7 chaiN2-iu7 ? Si7 in-ui7 Hi-  lut8 ong5 sou2 kiaN5 e5 tai7-chi3 chin phaiN2 , u7 pai7-hoai7 jin5-lun2 ( Ma2-kho2 6 : 17 , 18 ) , m7  na7 be7 kham-tit cho3 ong5 , ia7 si7 be7 kham-tit kap lau2-sit8 lang5 kong2 oe7.  Ong5 liong7-  pit chai-iaN2 Ia5-sou e5 i3-su3, m7-kaN2 pan7 i , kan-ta hi3-lang7 i, sang3 i to3-khi3 hou7 Pi2-  liap8-to pan7.  M7-ku2 sim-lai7 am3-chiN7 u7 chin kian3-siau3, in-ui7 tng cheng3-lang5 e5  bin7-cheng5 tu2-tioh8 Ia5-sou biau2-si7 (Lou5-ka  13 : 31 , 32 ).  Au7--lai5 Hi-lut8 ong5 hou7  Hong5-te3 pian2 i-e5 ong5-ui7 chhiong-kun liu5 khi3 sai-peng5 , soa3 si2 ti7-hia , bo5 koh to3-  tng2--lai5 i-e5 pun2-kok.      
3. Che3-si thak8-chheh lang5. In liah8 Ia5-sou cho3 oan-siu5 tui3-tek8 ; Ia5-sou bat tng cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 toa7 chek-pi7 in. In ku2-ku2 ke3-bou5 beh liah8 Ia5-sou, chong--si7 bo5 ki-hoe7, liah8 be7 koe3-chhiu2.  Kau3 hit jit8 in-e5 tou5-bou5 chiaN3 chiaN5, u7 than3 in e5 sim-i3 , liah8 Ia5-sou hou7 koaN-hu2 gi2-choe7 teng3 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 , sng2-si7 tek-seng3 hong-bian7 to3-khi3 in chhu3 ; kiam2 m7-si7 chin lang5-khi3 , hian2-beng5 in-e5 pun2-su7 hou7 peh-seN3 koh khah kiaN-ui3 i ? Ki5-sit8 ia7 si7 bo5.  Hit-jit8 in saN-pai2 lau3-khui3.  Chit8 pai2 si7 tui3 Pi2-liap8-to kong2 , Kai-sat i2-goa7  goan2 bo5 pat8 e5 ong5.   Chit ku3 khiok ia7 u7 ho2 i3-su3 , m7-ku2 in kong2 hiah-e5 oe7 giam2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 teh kong2 in si7 peng5-siong5 e5 kok bo5 koh-iuN7 pat8 pang-kok , lian2-chun2 m7-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 peh-seN3 ia7 bo5 teh ng3-bang7 Bi2-sai3-a lai5 cho3 in e5 ong5.  Iu7-koh chit8-pai2 kong2, I-e5 hoeh kui ti7  goan2 i2-kip8 goan2 e5 kiaN2-sun.  Au7-lai5 chit ku3 oe7 chin-chiaN3 u7 eng3-giam7.  Tui3 hit-si5 kau3 taN Iu5-thai3-lang5 lo5-lok8 si3-soaN3 tu2-tioh8 che7-che7 hang7 e5 kan-khou2 ( Ma2-kho2  13 : 19 , 20 ).  Goa7-kok lang5 phaiN2 khoan2-thai7 in, khiok toa7-toa7 thang hiam5 : m7-ku2 ia7 thang kong2 che2 si7 in chu7-chhu2-jiok8--e5, ka-ti7 cho3 tit lai5--e5. In khoaN3 tioh8 che2  ji7-iuN7 chin-chiaN3 kian3-siau3 , be7 bian2-tit hou7 Lo5-ma2 lang5 thi2-chhio3 in ; m7-ku2 bo5  hoat-tit ta-oa5, in-ui7 Pi2-liap8-to tiau5-kang sia2 an2-ni beh phi3-siuN3 in ; in-ui7 m7 kam-goan7 hou7 in khah iaN5 khi3.  Sui-jian5 kong2 si7 tek-seng3 , iau2-ku2 in bo5 sim2-mih8  mia5-siaN.   
4. Ia5-sou e5 hak8-seng.  Ia5-sou tu2-tioh8 kau-khou2 e5 si5 i e5 hak8-seng khiok long2 bo5  tu2-tioh8 ; in long2 chau2-li7, an-jian5 to3-khi3 in chhu3.  Chong--si7 sim-lai7 bo5 sim2-mih8 an-jian5 , be7 bian2-tit u7 loe2-chi3 , in-ui7 au7-lai7 e7 hou7 lang5 hiam5 in hit me5-hng bo5 chin7-lat8 lai5 po2-hou7 in e5 sian-seN, lun2-neh to5-chau2.  Pi2-tek m7-na7 an2-ni , iu7 koh eng7 ke3-ti3 lai5 po2 i-e5 seN3-mia7 chhut-lat8 kong2 peh8-chhat8-oe7 bo5-beh jin7 Ia5-sou;  m7-ku2 i liam5-piN siuN7 khou2-chheh, be7 bian2-tit u7 thi5-khau3 lau5-bak8-sai2.  Chiau3 lan2 phah-sng3 i un2-tang3 chit8-si3-lang5 m7-kaN2 be7 ki3-tit hit me5-hng e5 sou2-cho3. Iu5-tai7 m7-na7 sioh-mia7 iu7-koh u7 than3 30 e5 gin5-chiN5; m7-ku2 kau3-boe2 chin chhi-chham2 sit8-chai7 thang kho2-lian5.  Chiau3 an2-ni khoaN3 hit-chit8-jit8 Ia5-sou e5 hak8-seng long2 u7 mau5-peng7 ti7-teh; chi2-u7 hiah-e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chin7-tiong kho2-chhu2, in u7 kiaN5-oa2  tiam3 ti7 i-e5 sip8-ji7-ke3-kha , the3 Ia5-sou hoan5-lo2 ; kau3 te7 3 jit8 i tai7-seng khi3 sun5 bong7 ,  khoaN3-kiN3 i koh-oah8.      
Chiau3 lang5 teh khoaN3, hit-si5 Ia5-sou toa7 kian3-siau3, hou7 lang5 teng3-si2 ti7 hoan7-lang5 e5 tiong-kan, i-e5 tui3-tek8 kap koe3-lou7 e5 lang5 long2 thi2-chhio3 khoaN3-khin i ; i kan-ta tiam7-tiam7 thun-lun2.  Chong--si7 lan2 chai hit-tiap8 si7 toa7 eng5-kng e5 si5 , tui3 hit-si3 i cho3 se3-kan lang5 e5 Kiu3-chu2.  I pun2-sin kong2, Goa2 na7 hou7 lang5 ku2-khi2 li7-khui te7 chiu7 in2-chhoa7 cheng3-lang5 chiu7-kin7 goa2 ( Han7. 12 : 32 ).  In-ui7 an2-ni Siong7-te3 ko-seng i kek8 koan5, hian2-hek e5 mia5 siuN2-su3 i iaN5-koe3 ban7 mia5 ( Hui. 2 : 9 ).  In-ui7 an2-ni te7-bin7-chiuN7 e5 kau3-hoe7 kap thiN-teng2 e5 thiN-sai3 eng2-oan2 o-lo2 i kong2, Hou7 lang5 thai5 e5 IuN5-ko e7 kham-tit tit-tioh8 koan5-peng3, ho2-giah8 , ti3-hui7 , chai5-leng5, chun-kui3 , eng5-hian2 , hok-khi3 ( Bek8. 5 : 12 ). Sim sou2 goan7.      
 
Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 hun-piat hi-sit8.  Na7 chin7-tiong toe3 Chu2 lai5 tu2-tioh8 khun2-  tiok8 hou7 lang5 chhio3 , che thang sng3 cho3 lan2 e5 eng5-kng : na7 khi3-choat8 Chu2 lai5 tit-  tioh8 chun-kui3 , chiN5-chai5, se3-kan e5 mia5-siaN, che thang sng3 cho3 kian3-siau3.  
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 300 koan3, 1910 ni5 3 geh8)
