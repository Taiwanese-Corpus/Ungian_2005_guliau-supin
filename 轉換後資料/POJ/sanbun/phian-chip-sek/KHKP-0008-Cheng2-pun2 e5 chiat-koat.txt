Ti7 chit kui2-chap8-ni5 e5 tiong-kan, lun7 thiN-e7 e5 kok heng-ong7, kaN2 si7 Hoe-ki5-kok te7 it khoai3; put-lun7 si7 lang5-giah8 a2-si7 chiN5-chai5; Hoe-ki5-kok na2-ku2 na2 chin3-pou7, pi2 pat8 kok ia7 khah kin2. Phi3-ju7 hit kok e5 siaN5 na7 u7 chit8-ban7 teng e5 giah8 chiong5- tiong u7 che7-che7 toa7 chai5-chu2, u7-e5 hoat kau3 u7 chiaN5 pah-ban7 gin5. TaN lun7 Chu2 e5 kang, u7 lang5 hoaN-hi2 he7 tang7-pun2; na7 thiaN-kiN3 hok-im heng-ong7 e5 siau-sit, chiu7 it-li2 chhin-chhiuN7 thiaN-- kiN3 than3-chiN5 e5 hang5-cheng5. I put-chi2 chhut-lat8, ti7 I2-li7-noaN5 Mi5-su-li7 hit-nng7 seN2 e5 tiong-kan, lai5 siat oh8 khi2 loa7-che7 keng, in2-chhoa7 gin2-na2 chhit-ban7- e5 lai5 tit-tioh8 chin-li2 e5 ka3-si7; chiah-e5 gin2- na2 e5 si7-toa7-lang5 long2-si7 m7-bat-su7-e5. Iu7-koh kui2-na7 ni5 cheng5,chit8 lang5 chhiaN2 Sin-seN, ia7 ka7 i chhut sou2-hui3, kah i khi3 kau3 Ai-o-a seN2 lai5 siat Chu2- jit8-oh8.  Hit-e5 sin-seN siat-oh8 khi2 nng7- pah-goa7 keng.  U7 chit8-ni5 ti7 bo5-sim2-mih8 iau3-kin2 to7-li2 e5 chng lai5 khi2 chit8 keng oh8, chhiaN2 kou-niuN5 cho3 sin-seN.  Hit sou2-chai7 oan3-hin7 to7-li2 e5 lang5 pun2-jian5 m7 chun2 sin-seN tiam3-hia, teh-beh koaN2 i; au7-lai5 in-ui7 bo5 loa7-che7 gin2-na2, in phah-sng3 siat be7 loh8, chiu7 pang3-leng--khi3. Tui3 chit pah-ji7 keng oh8 che7-che7 lang5, ia7 u7 toa7-han3 ia7 u7 se3-han3, sin3 Ia5-sou lai5 kian3-ti3 kau3-hoe7 lak8-chap8-ui7; ti7 hiah-e5 pai3-tng5 long2-chong2 u7 lak8 pah lang5 lai5 jin7-choe7 sin3 Kiu3-chu2.
