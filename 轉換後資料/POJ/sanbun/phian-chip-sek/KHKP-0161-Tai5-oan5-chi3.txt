Chu7 Tai5-oan5 kui Jit8-pun2 pan2-tou5 liau2-au7, u7 ke2-oaN7 che7-che7 hang7, chhiu-chhiuN7 Koan7-hun7 e5 kai3-han7, siat pan7-bu7-su7 kap phoe-kiok8, long2 u7 iau3-kin2 e5 koan5-he7, lan2 eng-kai tioh8 siong-sek8, tak8-hang7 e7 li7-pian7 m7-bian2 hui3-khi3.   
Lun7 cha2-cheng5 e5 Koan7-hun7, khiok u7 Biau5-lek8, Chiang-hoa3, Hun5-lim5, An- peng5, kap Heng5-chhun; taN hiah-e5 mia5 cho3 Koan7 bo5 teh eng7. Sin siat e5 Koan7 ThiaN u7 pai5-liat8 ti7 e7-te2; chong2-si7 sou2 ki3-e5 li2-hun7 long2 si7 chiau3 Jit8-puu2 e5 lou7-theng5 lai5 ki3. Lin2 oh8-nih8 e5 hak8-seng na7 ai3 khe-kho2, tioh8 chai Jit8-pun2 tit8-heng5 e5 chit8-li2 ti2 Chheng-kok e5 lak8-li2 lian5 780 pou7 e5 giah8; Jit8-pun2 su3-hong e5 chit8-li2 ti2 Chheng-kok e5 46.24 su3-hong li2 e5 giah8.   
1.Tai5-pak-koan7. Koan7-thiaN siat ti7 Tai5-pak siaN5-lai7, chiu7-si7 Chong2-tok toa3 ti7-hia thong2-hat choan5 Tai5 e5 cheng3-su7; Iau2-ku2 chiau3 hit-tah e5 te7-li2 leng7-goa7 u7 hun-siat Ki-liong5 kap Tam7-chui2 e5 Chi- thiaN. Tai5-pak-koan7 tang-sai tng5 iok u7  16 li2, lam5-pak 17 li2; khoah si7 chha-put-to 273 e5 su3-hong li2. Koan7-lai7 ke kap chng-sia7 e5 giah8 hap8-kiong7 1437 e5, khau2- chau3 iok u7 7,8812, thau5-chhui3 u7 49, 6468 lang5. Chiong5-tiong u7 po2-li2 14 e5; pan7- bu7-su7 13-e5.   
2.Sin-tek-koan7. Koan7-thiaN siat ti7 Tek-chham3-siaN5-lai7; tang-sai e5 kai3-han7 15 li2; lam5-pak 20 li2; khoah 330 e5 su3- hong li2. Pak-kai3 si7 Tai5-pak-koan7, lam5- kai3 si7 Tai5-tiong-koan7; na7-si7 tang-peng5 u7 toa7-soaN thong2-khi2 cho3 kai3-han7. Sai- hng chit8-phiN3 e5 peN5-pou it-tit8 kau3 hai2. Koan7-lai7 ke kap chng-sia7 e5 giah8 hap8- kiong7 1145 e5 ; khau2-chau3 u7 8,3768 e5; thau5-chhui3 2,71505 lang5; po2-li2 6 e5; pan7-bu7-su7 11 e5.   
 
3.Tai5-tiong-koan7. Tai5-tiong-koan7 1 thiaN ti7 ku7-si5 e5 Tai5-oan5-hu2-siaN5-lai7. Ti7-1 hia khi2-thau5 u7 siat nng7 e5 chi-thiaN tiam3 Chiang-hoa3 kap Pou-li2-sia7, taN i2-keng hoe3-1 bo5. Kai3-han7-lai7, tang-sai 23 li2; lam5-pak 15 li2; si3-ui5 khoah 345 e5 su3-hong li2. Sai-hng keh hai2 kap Chheng-kok e5 Choan5- chiu hng7-hng7 saN tui3-bin7. Tang-hng chit8-toaN7 kha-chiah poe7 toa7-soaN. KhoaN3 hit-e5 te7 e5 si3-bin7 hong2-hut chin-chhiuN7 Sin- tek-koan7. I-e5 ke kap chng-sia7 e5 siau3-giah8 u7 1304 e5; khau2-chau3 1,3429 e5; thau5- chhui3 49,0465 lang5; po2-li2 26 e5; pan7- bu7-su7 15 ui7.   
4.Ka-gi7-koan7. 5.Tai5-lam5-koan7. Koan7-thiaN ti7 Tai5- lam5-hu2-siaN5-lai7; koan2-hat tang-sai 15 li2; lam3-pak 8 li2; te7-bin7 u7 120 e5 su3-hong li2. 6.Hong7-soaN-koan7. Koan7-thiaN ti7 Pi- thau5-siaN5-lai7. Tang-sai e5 kai3-han7 15 li2; lam5-pak 22 li2; te7-bin7 kiong7 u7 330 e5 su3- hong li, lioh8-a2 na2-chhin-chhiuN7 saN-kak- chiam e5 khoan2. 7.Tai5-tang-koan7. Tu5-chhut pak-hng e5 Gi5-lan5-thiaN, lam5-hng e5 Hong7-soaN- koan7 sou2-siok8 e5 te7, long2 si7 Tai5-tang-koan7 koah2-hat-e5. I tang-sai e5 kai3-han7 si7 18 li2; lam5-pak 62 li2; te7-bin7 1116 su3-hong li2 e5 toa7. Lun7 i-e5 po2-li2, ke,kap chng-sia7 e5 giah8, be7-tit-thang chai kau3 siong5-se3; in- ui7 kai3-han7-lai7 u7 koan5-soaN hiam2-nia2 khah che7 si7 chhiN-hoan teh toa3; tok8-tok8 lam5- hng Pi-lam5 e5 te7 khah piN5-thaN2 na7-tiaN7. Tai5-tang-koan7 jin5-ke e5 siau3-giah8 iok u7 1,4000 e5; thau5-chhui3 chha-put-to 7,0000 lang5. Pan7-bu7-su7 iau2-boe7 tiaN7-tioh8, si7 chiau3 te7-li2 e5 li7-pian7 lai5 siat. KhoaN3 jin5- ke e5 che7-chio2, hian7-kim i2-keng u7 3 ui7.   
8.Gi5-lan5-thiaN. Chai7 Tai5-oan5 tou5 e5 tang-pak peng5. Tang-saiN e5 kai3-han7 6 li2; lam5-pak 11 li2; te7-bin7 put-ko3 33 e5 su3- hong khoah.
