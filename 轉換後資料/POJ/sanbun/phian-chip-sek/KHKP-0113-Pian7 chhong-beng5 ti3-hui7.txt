
Pian7 chhong-beng5 ti3-hui7
TaN-lun7-kau3 su7,long5,kong,siong, m7-na7 chit8-teng2-ho7 e5 lang5, bo5-koh-m7 kok u7 ti3-hui7 chhong-beng5 . Chong2-si7 eng7 ka-ti7 e5 ti3-hui7 chhong-beng5,khiok ia7 chin che7 . Na7-m7-si7 than3 in su-phian e5 i3-kian3 m7-chai thang hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3,chiu7-si7 hou7 se3-siok8 khi3-sip8 pe2-pak8 , be7-bian2-tit pian3- thong . HaiN7 ah ! chhin-chhiuN7 hiah-e5 kong2 si7 ti3-hui7 chhong-beng5 , kiu3-chin long2 bo5 ti3-hui7 chhong-beng5 ti7-teh . Che si7 sim2-mih8 ian5-kou3?Tok8-tok8 boe7-bat thiaN-kiN3 Seng3-keng sou2 kong2, “ Keng3-ui3 Siong7-te3 pian7 si7 ti3-hui7 , li7- khui phaiN2-tai7 chiu7-si7 chhong-beng5 ” hit-ho7 e5 ka3-si7 .    
Chhi3 chiong in oa2-kho3 ka-ti7 e5 su-sim lai5 lun7 , chai thak8-chheh-lang5 long7 lang5 saN-ko3 lai5 cho3 chng7-su , ng3-bang7 chiah8 pit-boe2 e5-chiN5.Choh-chhan5-lang5 be7-hiau2 poe5-iuN2 tiu7-a2,siong5-siong5 pang3-teh pha- hng . Kang-hu-lang5 cho3-chhut ki5-koai3 im5-iok8 e5 khi3-ku7 . Seng-li2-lang5 eng7 phaiN2 mih8 ke2-hoe3 lai5 phian3-lang5 . Sim7-chi3 u7- e5 pang3-sak pun2-giap8, khi3-chiah8 ge5-mng5 png7 . U7-e5 ha7-chian7 khi3-cho3 chhiong-iu le7-chut. U7-e5 hak8-sip8 iN7-toan lai5 loan hong-siok8 . U7-e5 hak8-sip8 sia5-hoat lai5 be5- hek8 lang5. Chhin-chhiuN7 chit-poe3 bo5 chiau3 hoat-bun5-keng, sit8-chai7 sng3 be7- liau2 . Tai7-khai3 in e5 sim chhin-chhiuN7 tok8-choa5 , in e5 seng3 chhin-chhiuN7 chhai5- long5 , bo5 keng3-hong7 Siong7-te3 , kaN2 cho3 phaiN2-tai7 . Chai7-siN sui-jian5 e7 thang hiau- heng7 thoat-li7 kok-hoat e5 heng5-hoat8 , si2- au7 tek-khak be7 thoat-li7-tit im-kan e5 sim2- phoaN3 , ka-ti7 khoa-khau2 in e5 ti3-hui7 , su5- put-ti in e5 ti3-hui7 i2-keng tim5-lun5 , in  e5 chhong-beng5 long2 eng7 m7 tioh8 lou7 .    
Che kiam2 m7-tioh8 chhim-chhim kho2- sioh mah ?  Chiau3 pun2-hun7 lai5 kong2 , thak8-chheh-lang5 eng-kai tioh8 cheng- kong si-hu3 bun5-ge7,choh-chhan5-lang5 eng- kai tioh8 thiN-si5 bat te7-thou2 , kang-hu-lang5 eng-kai tioh8 chin7-lat8 keng-eng5 cho3 kang- khoe3 , seng-li2-lang5 tioh8 bat hang5-cheng5 gau5 sng3-siau3 lai5 cho3 li7-lou . Chhin-chhiuN7 che si7 in pun2-seng3 pun2-hun7 sou2 eng-kai u7 e5 ti3-hui7, sou2 tioh8 chin7 e5 chhong-beng5, chi7-chui7-a2 kaN2 kong2 in m7-tioh8?An3-chaiN2-iuN7 hou7 khi3-sip8 pe2-pak8 be7-ki3-tit in e5 kin-pun2, khi3 sin3 put8-kau3 than3 Lo2-chu2, lai5 teh chiah8 chhai3 siu-heng7 , koh eng7 chha5 thou5 lai5 cho3 ngou2-siong7 . Siat-su2 thak8-chheh-lang5 e7 chiuN7-koan5, choh-chhan5- lang5 u7 chhun chhek, kang-hu-lang5 chin ki5-kha2, seng-li2-lang5 gau5 than3 chiN5, ia7 put-ko3 si7 siN-cheng5 chiam7-si5 hiong2-siu7 eng5-hian2 e5 hok-lok8, bo5 tek-khak si2-au7 e7 po2-nia2 in e5 leng5-hun5, beh-thai3 tat8-tioh8 keng3-ui3 Siong7-te3,  li7-khui phaiN2-tai7 , chiong-sin chheng-khi3,hoaN-hi2 cho3 seng3- kau3 e5 tou5 , si2-au7 seng-thian , eng2-oan2 hiong2 thian-tong5 e5 hok-khi3 ? Che sng3 si7 chin7 ti3-hui7 toa7 chhong-beng5 lah. Goan7 than3 ka-ti7 su-iok8 e5 lang5 iau3-kin2 tioh8 liong7-cha2 hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3 , tu5-khi3 phaiN2 e5 kan-ki2, koh-chai3 ia7 ho2 e5 cheng2-chi2, se3- siok8  e5 pe2-pak8 tui3 an2-ni e7 pian3-chhian , chiu7 jin5-to7 koh chun-chong5 thian-to7, cho3-hoe2 kiaN5 hit-tiau5 ho2 lou7, kiam2 m7 chin- chiaN3 ho2 mah ?
