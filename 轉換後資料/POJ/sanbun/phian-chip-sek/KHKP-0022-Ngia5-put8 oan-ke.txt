Ngia5-put8 oan-ke 
Mui2-ni5 saN-goeh8 23 jit8 chiu7-si7 Ma2-chou2-siN; ti7 Hu2-siaN5 u7 chit8-keng toa7 Ma2-chou2-bio7; si7 chhai7 toa7-ma2 chham ji7-ma2.  Au7-lai5 u7 hun ji7-ma2 e5 sin5-siong7 khi3 Pak-kang2 hok8-sai7, ho7-cho3 Pak-kang2-ma2; kui2-na7 ni5 i2-lai5, u7 hong-siaN i-e5 mia5 heng-kau3 lam5-pak ji7-lou7; si3-ui5 e5 lam5-li2 put-chi2 jiat8-sim hok8-sai7; u7-e5 khi3 Pak-kang2 sio kim, u7-e5 lai5 Hu2-siaN5, bo5 tiaN7-tioh8.  Pak-kang2 e5 ji7-ma2 u7-si5 saN-goeh8-bin7 tioh8 lai5 Hu2-siaN5, tham3 toa7-ma2.  
Kin-ni5 3 goeh8 14 jit8, u7 jip8 Hu2-lai7; boe7 kau3 siaN5-mng5 e5 si5, siaN5-lai7-goa7 e5 bio7 u7 chap8-goa7 teng2 toa7-lian2 chhut-siaN5 khi3 tan2-chih--i.  Teh-beh saN-kin7 e5 si5, tai7-ke saN-chiN ai3 seng chih--tioh8, chhiuN2 thau5-hiuN khah u7 miaN5-siaN, chau2 kau3 oan-ke; Sam-lau7-ia5-keng khah chhi3-thau5-pio, toa7-ki5 kiah8 bo5 tu2-ho2, hou7 Phou2-che3-tian7 keng2 e5 lang5 li3-li3-phoa3; tui3 hit-tiap8 chiu7 khioh-hin7.  Tu2-tu2 jip8-siaN5 e5 si5 tai7-ke chiu7 sio-phah--lah, chham be7-mih8 e5 taN3 soa3 long3-long3--to2; i2-kip8 be7 hun2-koe2 e5 au-a2 ia7 soa3 theh8--khi2-lai5 chun2 chioh8-thau5 sio-tim3; kiah8 lang5 e5 tiam3-thang-pang cho3 khi3-hai5 lai5 kong3--louh ! Chham toe3 teh sui5-hiuN  ta-pou cha-bou2 ia7 tioh8-siong put-chi2 che7 lang5; put8-kio7 u7-e5 ia7 soa3 phah-phaiN2, liong2-peng5 u7-e5 tioh8-siong chin siong-tiong7, ti3-kau3 tioh8 kng khi3 hou7 Koan7-chu2 giam7, ia7 soa3 liah8 4 e5 lang5 sang3-loh8 koan7.  
Sam-lau7-ia5-keng chin m7-goan7 to3-tng2-khi3 kio3 lang5 lai5 chah8 ti7 poaN3-lou7 beh phah--i; tu2-tu2 Phou2-che3-tian7 e5 put8-kio7 koe3, beh oa2 nng7-keng-chhu3 hng7, chiu7 hou7 i ui5--teh.  M7-ku2 Phou2-che3-tian7 kiong5 koh chhoaN3, tou2-nge7 le5--koe3-khi3; ti7-hia iu7-koh oan-ke, put8-kio7 kng-chau2 kau3 Sio2-ma2-chou2, hia e5 lang5 chiu7 lai5 ka7 in cho3 kong-chhin.  Put8-kio7 hou7 Sio2-ma2-chou2 lau5 keh chit8-mi5, chiah hou7 i kng to3--khi3.  Sam-lau7-ia5 keng e5 lang5 tioh8-siong put-chi2 che7, Phou2-che3-tian7 ia7 bo5 chio2.  
Siang7 hit-jit8 E7-thai3-chu2 kap Lam5-chhiuN2 ia7 phah kau3 put-chi2 siong-tiong7; si7 in-ui7 teng2-pang khi3 sang3 Lam5-kun-sin Ong5-ia5 chiu7 u7 chek-oan3. 
Kau3 16 jit8 Pak-kang2-ma2 beh to3--khi3, Koan7-chu2 chiu7 chiau3 chhu3-su7 pai5-liat8 hou7 in sang3 chhut siaN5, Bu2-iaN5 ia7 phai3 peng khi3 ti5-hong5, si7 kiaN-liau2 hiah-e5 put8-kio7 koh oan-ke.  Sang3 chhut-siaN5 e5 put8-kio7 kap lang5 i2-keng to3-tng2--lai5, Pak-kang2 ma2 chiu7 kiaN5; beh kin7 Chiu-a2-boe2-sia7, hia e5 lang5 ia7 lai5 chih--i, soa3 sang3 koe3 in e5 sia7, cham2-jian5 u7 hiah-hng7; hit-si5 in e5 chhu3 chiu7 hou7 hoe2 sio chin-che7 keng, soa3 bo5 lang5 thang phah-hoe2; kau3 in to3-tng2-lai5 hoe2 i2-keng hoa--lah ! Kho2-kian3 keng3-put8 e5 hai7-lang5 chin chham2--ah ! 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 22 ho7, 1887 ni5 4 goeh8)
