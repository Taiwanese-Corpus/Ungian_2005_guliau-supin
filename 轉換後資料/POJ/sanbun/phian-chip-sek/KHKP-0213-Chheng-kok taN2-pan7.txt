
Chheng-kok taN2-pan7
Tai5-oan5 iau2-boe7 hou7 Jit8-pun2 koan2 e5 si5, Tai5-oan5 lang5 long2 si7 Chheng-kok taN2-pan7, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 hian7-si5 an2-ni, u7-e5 chian2 thau5-chang, u7-e5 bo5. Chian2 thau5-chang si7 oh8 Jit8-pun2 lang5, piN7 thau5-chang si7 chiau3 Chheng-kok khoan2 Chiau3 Chheng-kok khoan2 e5 lang5 iau2-ku2 chin che7; na7 mng7 chiah-e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 khoaN3 chit-ho7 khoan2 si7 tui3 to2-ui7 lai5, kan-ta chai lang5 u7 hou7 Chheng-kok koan2, sou2-i2 chiah an2-ni , chong2 m7-chai Chheng-kok lang5 an2-choaN2-iuN7 thau5-khak ai3 piN7 chit8-e5 thau5-chang boe2.    
U7 lo2-poe3 e5 lang5 teh kong2, si7 in-ui7 cha2-chang5 Boan2-chiu siok8 Tiong-kok ,  Boan2 ong5 u7 chhe jin5-sin5 toa3 le2-mih8 lai5 chin3-kong3 Tiong-kok . Tiong-kok e5 tai7- sin5 ai3 chhong3-ti7 i, chiu7 ka7 i thi3-thau5, piN7 mng5-boe2, cho3 tng5-saN hou7 i chheng7, u7 cheng5-pou2 au7-pou2, chhong3 be2-te5 ng2, koa3 sou3-chu, chheng7 ou-hia, ti3 teng2-tai3 u7 koa3 iaN.  Ka7 i kong2, Chit-khoan2 chin ho2-khoan2, gone2-e5 Hong5-te3 ai3 chiong an2-ni po3-siuN2 li2 .   
Boan2-chiu e5 jin5-sin5 to3-khi3 chin tek-i3, Boan2 ong5 khoaN3-liau2 ia7 hoaN-hi2, chiu7 beng7-leng7 peh-siN3 thau5-mng5 tioh8 thi3, thau5-chang tioh8 piN7. Boan2-chiu lang5 khoaN3 chit-ho7 cho3 the2-bian7, ki5-sit8 si7 teh leng5-jiok8 in, in-ui7 Tiong-kok lang5 khoaN3 in cho3 cheng-siN , pi2-pheng7 in cho3 be2. Hit-nia2 saN ka7-chiah u7 pou2-koa3, si7 pi2-pheng7 be2-oaN . Heng-cheng5 u7 sou3-chu si7 pi2-pheng7 be2-lek8-soh. Chhiu2-ng2 eh8-eh8 koa3 chiam-so koh to3-pih si7 chin-chhiuN be2-te5; kha-chheng7 ou-hia ia7-si7 an2-ni . Thau5 ti3 teng2-tai3 u7 koa3 iaN, si7 na2 be2-chang, thau5-chang boe2 si7 na2 be2-boe2 . Lang5 chheng7 chit-ho7 khoan2 , na7 phak- loh8-khi3, tu2-tu2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 be2.   
Put-heng7 Tiong-kok soa3 siok8 Boan2- chiu, kok ho7 Chheng-tiau5, Chheng ong5 beng7-leng7 koaN-hu2 long2 tioh8 chiau3 chit- ho7 e5 taN2-pan7, peh-siN3 ia7 tioh8 oh8 Boan2- chiu khoan2 . Ta-pou lang5 iau3-kin2 tioh8 thi3-thau5, piN7 thau5-chang.  Tui3 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 lan2 thang chai, Leng5-jiok8 pat8-lang8 si7 leng5-jiok8 ka-ti7.   
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 213 koan3, 1902 ni5 12 geh8)
