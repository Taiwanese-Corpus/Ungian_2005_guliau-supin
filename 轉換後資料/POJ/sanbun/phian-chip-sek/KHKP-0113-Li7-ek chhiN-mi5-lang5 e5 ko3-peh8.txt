Li7-ek chhiN-mi5-lang5 e5 ko3-peh8
Thong Tiong-kok-lai7 e5 chhiN-mi5-lang5 chha-put-to u7 gou7-chap8-ban7 cho2-iu7.  Si3- ni5 cheng5,Siong7-hai2 e5 Chong2-hoe7 u7 keng2- soan2 kui2-na7 lang5 thoe3 in siat-hoat , ia7 khng3 tak8-ui7 e5 sian-siN tioh8 kap hiah-e5 lang5 chin7-lek8 tau3-pan7 .   
Lin2 i2-keng chai Hu2-sia5-lai7 kap Choan- chiu-siaN5 hian7-chai7 u7 nng7 keng oh8  hou7 chhiN-mi5-lang5 thang siu7 tak8 hang7 e5 kau3- hun3 . Hak8-seng mui2-jit8 chiau3-si5  eng7- kang thak8-chheh ia7 kiam lioh8-a2 teh oh8 kang-ge7 . Ku7-ni5 I5-sian-siN-niu5 ti7 Hu2-siaN5 chin chhut-lat8 lai5 liau7-li2 chit-chan5 su7. Kho2-sioh i-e5 seng-khu jiam2-tioh8 phoa3- piN7 , kou-put-chiong tioh8 to3-khi3 Chou2-ke . I siong5-siong5 thoe3 in tou5-bou5 kiu2-oan2, siuN7 kau3 jit8-au7 e5 tai7-chi3; sou2-i2 m7-na7 ka3 in thak8- chheh sia2-ji7 na7-tiaN7, koh ka3 in choe3 kan- ki2 thang ka-ki7 than3-chiN5, lai5 tou7-khou2, m7- bian2 chiong-sin oa2-kho3 pat8-lang5 chhi7 .    
TaN in-ui7 su7 iau2-be7 chiaN5, goan2 ai3 chai tiam3 Tai5-oan5 a2-si7 Tng5-soaN u7 hiaN-ti7 a2-si7 liat8-ui7 sian-siN kam-goan7 sia2-chhut chit8- phiN lai5 lun7-khi2 chhiN-mi5-lang5 tioh8 choe3 sim2-mih8 tu2-ho2 e5 kang-ge7 lai5 ka-ki7 tou7- chiah8 . Beh choe3 chit-phiN lun7 e5 lang5 tai7- seng tioh8 khi3 chha5-kho2 kui2-na7 hang7, chiah u7 lou7-eng7 ; chhin-chhiuN7 in pun2 sou2- chai7 chhiN-mi5 e5 lang5-giah8 u7 loa7-choe7,in e5 cheng5-heng5 iah8 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2,hian7-si5 teh choe3 sim2-mih8 thau5-lou7 , tak8-jit8 than3 loa7-choe7 . Koh , tioh8 siuN7 u7 sim2-mih8 iau2- be7 hak8-sip8 e5 kang-ge7 thang siat hou7 in choe3 , iu7-koh tioh8 ki3 hit-hang7 kang-ge7 e5 pun2-chiN5 loa7-choe7 , chhut-sek loa7-choe7 .    
Lun7 chhiN-mi5-lang5 choe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 kang chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 I-koan2-lai7 a2-si7 koe- chhi7 thak8 Seng3-keng , boe7 chheh , thoan5 to7-li2 , goa2 ia7 si7 ai3 chai hiaN-ti7 e5 i3-kian3, tioh8 chiau3 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 khah-ho2 , tek-khak tioh8 u7 lang5 khan a2 m7-bian2 .    
Na7 u7-eng5 e5 ki-hoe7,chhiaN2 tak8-ui7 e5 hiaN- ti7 lioh8-a2 lai5 the2-thiap chit-chan5  tai7-chi3 , kau3 12 geh8 chhoe-chap8-lai7 ui5-chi2 kia3 sou2 choe3 e5 lun7 thang hou7 goan2 lai5 phoe-pheng5 . Phoe-pheng5 liau2 chhu2 te7-it mia5-e5 beh siuN2 i chhit e5 gun5-chiN5 , chhu2 te7-ji7 mia5-e5 beh siuN2 i gou7-khou, te7-saN-mia5 saN-khou. Iau3-kin2 tioh8 tai7-seng chha5-kho2, au7-lai5 tak8-hang7 chiau3 chhu3-su7 siong5-se3 lai5 sia2 . Lun7 e5 toe5-bak8 chiu7-si7 :－  
 “Lun7 kau3-hoe7-goa7 e5 chhiN-mi5-lang5,in-e5 lang5-giah8, thau5 lou7,kap cheng5-heng5 ; koh , lun7 chhiN-mi5 bat-ji7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7 , in u7 sim2-mih8 thang choe3  chiu7 ka-ki7 oe7 than3 chiah8 , m7-bian2 it-khai3 oa2-kho3 pat8-lang5 .”    
Lun7 tioh8 chiong peh8-oe7-ji7 lai5 sia2, tioh8 loh8 am3-ho7, ia7 tioh8 eng7 chit8-hong lai7-bin7 chu3-beng5 bou2-ho7 chiu7-si7 sim2-mih8 lang5-e5 , tang5-che5 kia3 hou7 Kam Bok8-su . 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-poe3 te7 113 koan3, 1894 ni5 8 geh8)
