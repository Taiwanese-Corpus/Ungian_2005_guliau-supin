
Bo5 kong2, bo5 lang5 chai
Beh kong2 sim2-mih8?  Kong-hok8 In3- su-kiok8 e5 su7-giap8.  
Chit keng siaN2-lang5 siat--e5?  Goan5- pun2 si7 Pa Khek-le2 Phok-su7.  I ka-ti7 e7- hiau2 khioh-ji7, in3-chheh, chiu7 be2 chiah e5 in3-chheh e5 khi3-khu7, chiong2-chiong2 lai5 he7 ti7 Sin-lau5.  Chhe chit8-lang5 mia5 Sa-a2 khi3 E7-mng5 oh8 in3-chheh; i2-au7 tit-tioh8 chit8-lang5 Sng Iau7-hun5 lai5 hak8-sip8, tui3 an2-ni khi2-kang in3 po3-choa2.  In3-chheh e5 sou2-chai7, ho7 mia5 Chu7-tin-tong5 (聚珍堂).  Kau3 ti7 u7 chit8-cham7, kui2-na7 e5 po3-choa2 lian5-hap8 cho3 chit8-e5, chit keng chiah chheng cho3 Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3-sia7.  Ti7 1941 ni5 4 goeh8, siu7 Jit8-pun2 lang5 kim3-chi2, theng5-khan, kau3 1945 ni5, 11 goeh8 2 jit8, koh khai-pan7, chiu7 kai2- mia5 Kong-hok8 In3-su-kiok8; tui3 chia hoat-khan e5 po3-choa2, iu5-goan5 mia5 Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3.  
Hian7-chai7 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 teh liau7-li2 chit keng?  Si7 Ko Kim-seng, ko3-jin5.   Kau3-hoe7 u7 loa7-che7 chu-pun2 chou7-chan7 i?   Bo5; Siong5-ti3 Ui2-oan5-hoe7 kan-ta kong2, Chiong Kong-po3-sia7 in3-chheh e5 ke-si kap chhu3, chioh bou2-lang5, lai5 hoat-heng5 Kong-po3, an2-ni na7-tiaN7.  Ti7 chia teh cho3 kang e5 jin5-oan5 u7 6 mia5.  Song3 Chui2-khim5, Lim5 Pah-ban7, Koeh Eng5-jin5, TeN7 In-hui7, koe3-ke3 kiam hoan3-be7, Ko Tek-chiong Sian-siN-niu5, Chu2-pit  Ko Kim-seng.  Tak8-jit8 eng5-giap8 e5 si5-kan si7 8 tiam2.  Teng2-pou 8 tiam2 chiuN7-kang, le2-pai3-jit8 hioh-khun3.  Kiu3-chu2-tan3 a2-si7 koe3-ni5 chiaN-goeh8 iu5-goan5 u7 theng5-kang.  
Lun7 goeh8-kip.  Cho3 chit-khoan2 su7- giap8 e5 lang5, ti7 Tai5-lam5 chhi7, sai-hu7 mui2-goeh8 kang-chiN5 si7 2400 khou, sai-a2 si7 1400 khou.  Na7-si7 Kong-hok8 In3-su-kiok8 e5 sai-hu7, hian7-chai7 chiah nia2 1050; sai-a2 nia2 770 khou.  Koe3-ke3 nia2 1000 khou.  Gou7 lang5 sou2 nia2--e5, chong2-kiong7 4640 khou.  
Nia2 bo5 goa7-bin7 lang5 e5 chit8-poaN3 chiN5.  Kang-chiN5 bo5 koh ke-thiN, tiaN7-tioh8 be7 eng7-tit.  I2-goa7 iau2 u7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 chap8-hui3.  Su-kiok8 sou2 siu--e5, mui2-goeh8 peng5-kin u7 5000 khou.  M7-si7 bo5 lang5 be2 chheh, si7 bo5 chheh thang be7.  Ai3 in3 che7-che7 to7-li2 chheh lai5 kong-kip Tai5- oan5 e5 Kau3-hoe7, m7-ku2 chui2-goan5 se3-kang2,  
oh chhut chui2, liam5-piN khat, liam5-piN ta.  
Thau5 chit8-e7 tiok8-chhiu2, si7 chit8-ban7 khou cho3 chu-pun2, kau3 chit-tiap8 Kong- hok8 In3-su-kiok8 e5 chai5-san2 u7 16 ban7.  KhoaN3 si7 toa7 than3-chiN5, sit8-chai7 si7 hi-ham3.  Cheng5 chit8-ban7, sit8-sit8 si7 chit8-ban7, taN 16 ban7, lim2-lim2-a2 chheng-lak8 khou.  An2-ni lai5 pi2-kau3 goa2 e5 pun2-chiN5, 100 khou khai chhun 16 khou, 25 khou siau-hui3 21 khou, iau2 sin7 4 khou.  ChiN5 tit8-tit8 bo5 ke3, goan2 chia, chit8 khou gin5 be2 bo5 chit8-tiau5 iu5-chiah8-koe2.  Kong-hok8 In3-su-kiok8 e5 gui5-hiam2 ti7 bak8-cheng5 lah!  Chiah e5 lang5 e5 goeh8-kip, na7 chiau3 siong-tong lai5 sng3, tak8 goeh8-jit8, tioh8 hoat chit8-ban7 gin5 hou7 in chiah tioh8.  Chong2-si7 pun2-kiok8 sou2 siu, put kiong7 sou2 chhut, taN beh siaN2 pou7?  
Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 e5 ke3-chiN5, chit8-ni5 30 khou, hiah kui3, an3-choaN2 cho3 kang e5 lang5 nia2 chio2 chiN5?  Ku7-ni5, lang5 be2 po3-choa2, chit8-ni5 3.5 khou, u7 than3 a2-bo5, m7- bian2 koh kong2, tai7-ke beng5-pek8.  Ku7-ni5 lang5 be2 po3 1850 hun7, kin-ni5 chiah u7 1300 hun7.  Chit8 hun7 e5 po3-choa2, choa2-chiN5 tioh8 2.25, 12 hun7 si7 27 khou.  Liat8- ui7 kau 30 khou, lin2 siuN7, u7 than3 3 khou.  Kang-chiN5 bo5 sng3, thai3 u7 thang than3.   Chun2 cho3 than3 3 khou, chiu7 1300 hun7, choan5-ni5 si7 than3 3900 khou, beh hoat cho3 chit8 goeh8 e5 kang-chiN5, to bo5 kau3.  
An3-choaN2 teh i5-chhi5 goeh8-goeh8 e5 sou2-hui3?  
1.Tui3 Kong-hok8 In3-su-kiok8, be7 chap8-chheh lai5 thiam-pou2.  
2.Chiah-e5 tang5-tioh8-boa5 e5 peng5-iu2, kam-goan7 nia2 chio2-chiN5.  
3.Iu2-chi3-chia2 ki3-hu3.  Ti7 ku7-ni5 11 goeh8 e5 po3-choa2, u7 ki3 peng5-iu2 choan5-ni5-tou7 sang3 4525 khou.  Ku7-ni5 boe2, Siong7- hai2 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 sang3 8400 khou, u7 long2 pun hou7 in cho3 koe3-ni5 e5 ui3-lo5.  Hian7-chai7 iau2 u7 peng5-iu2 hoe-hoe kia3-hu3.  Che ui3-an si7 ho2, iau2-boe7 u7 kim-pun2-tek e5 ti7-liau5.  
Hian7-si5 khui chit keng, si7 kiong5-kiong5 teh cho3, chhin-chhiuN7 san3-hiong lang5, phoa3-saN phoa3-khou3 pou2-pou2 leh, lai5 jia seng-khu.  Beh koh cheng2-li2 ho2-se3, tioh8 kang2 kui2-na7 chap8 ban7.  Tau3-ti2 chit-keng lan2 khoaN3 cho3 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7?   
Kan-lan5 kau3 an2-ni, koaiN-koaiN khi2--lai5, kiam2 bo5 khah chheng-sim?  Beh kong2, chit-keng e5 li7-ek, oe7-thau5 tng5, tai7-liok8 lai5 kong2, si7 soan-thoan5 thian-kok e5 bun5-hoa3, ti7 thong Tai5-oan5, bo5 su Sin5 Hak8-hau7, ia7 bo5 su liat8-ui7 kian3-siat le2-pai3-tng5.  Tui3 chia sou2 be7-chhut e5 chheh, sou2 hoat-khan e5 po3, na7 e7 hou7 lang5 sim-sin5 peng5-an, sim-koaN ng3 Siong7-te3, an2-ni goan2 chiu7 than3 toa7 chiN5.  Ku7-ni5 11 goeh8 e5 po3-choa2 te7 2 bin7, u7 chhut Hong-oan2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 le2-pai3-tng5, hou7 hong-thai phah-to2, hia e5 hoe7-iu2 bo5 lat8 thang koh khi2.  TaN ti7 Tai5-lam5 Ng5 Chhun-chho2 hiaN, teng2 jit8 kia3-bu3 5000 khou khi3 chou7-chan7.  I kong2, Goa2 siN-jit8 m7-ai3 cho3, cho3 siN-jit8 si7 hi-hoa.  Beh chiong chit-tiau5 chiN5, hian3 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 lo7-eng7.  Goa2 thiaN an2-ni, chin hoaN-hi2.  Pun2-kiok8 than3-chiN5 chiu7-si7 che.  
Cha2-si5 Liap8-sat-lou7 tai5 ti7 bong7--nih, Ia5-sou khia7 ti7 bong7-cheng5, kah lang5 soa2- khui bong7-chioh8.  Lang5 u7 soa2, Ia5-sou ki5-to2 liau2, hoah kong2, Liap8-sat-lou7 chhut--lai5.  Si2 lang5 chiu7 chhut--lai5, chhiu2, kha chat-pou3, bin7 pau kin.  Chu2 ka7 in kong2, Thau2-khui hou7 i kiaN5 (Iok-han7 11:44).  
Iu5-thai3 lang5 e5 Bok8-su, i-e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 si2--khi3.  Ia5-sou khan i-e5 chhiu2 kong2, Cha2-bou2-gin2-na2, goa2 beng7-leng7 li2 khi-- lai5.  Cha-bou2-gin2-na2 liam5-piN khi2-lai5 kiaN5.   Chu2 hoan-hu3 tioh8 hou7 cha-bou2-gin2-na2 chiah8 (Ma2-kho2 5:43).  
TaN lan2 chit e5 Chheh-pang5, khak-sit8 u7 si2--khi3, 4 ni5 goa7 kang-tiuN5 hong-hoe3.   Goan2 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 pang-chan7, hou7 i koh-oah8 kau3 chit-tiap8; tu2-tioh8 keng-che3 khun3-khou2, chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 hit nng7-e5 lang5 khiam3 eng3-oan7.  Sim2-mih8 lang5 u7 thiaN-tioh8 Chu2 e5 siaN kong2, Thau2-khui hou7 i kiaN5, koh kong2, Tioh8 hou7 cha-bou2-gin2-na2 chiah8.  U7 chhiu2--e5, tioh8 chhun; u7 png7--e5, chhiaN2 phang5--lai5.  Chu2 teh kio3, tioh8 thau2, tioh8 hou7 i chiah8.  Kong-hok8 In3-su-kiok8 si7 tiong7-iau3 e5 pou3-kau3 ki-koan.  ChhiaN2 liat8-ui7 tioh8 lai5 ke3-siok8 pan7-li2.  I2-keng oah8, hou7 i koh si2--khi3, kiam2 bo5 kho2-lian5-tai7?    
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 700 ho7, 1947 ni5 4 geh8)
