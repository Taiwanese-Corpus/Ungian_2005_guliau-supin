
Cho3 choa2 e5 hong-hoat
Lang5 u7 eng7 che7-che7 mih8 cho3 choa2.  Ti7 Tiong-kok khah-siong5 si7 eng7 chiN2- chiN2 e5 tek ; ti7 Sai-kok na7 beh cho3 khah-ho2 e5 choa2 chiu7 u7 eng7 pou3 , si7 lang5 sou2 pang3-sak e5 ku7 i -chiuN5 , hit-ho7 . TaN, beh lioh8-a2 gi7-lun7 chiong pou3 cho3 choa2 e5 hong-hoat .        
Cho3 choa2 u7 eng7 moa5 kap mi5 e5 phoa3-pou3.  Si, tiu5, ni5 bo5 hap8, sou2-i2 chiah-e5 mih8 kap pin2-chiam, liu2-pai5, hit-ho7, tioh8 keng2-chhut lai5 tu5-khi3.  Koh chiong5-tiong hap8-eng7 e5 phoa3-pou3 tioh8 hun-piat khoan2-sit, chhou--e5 thang cho3 chhou-choa2 , iu3--e5 thang cho3 iu3-choa2 .        
Phoa3-pou7 hun-piat liau2 chiu7 u7 theh8 ti7 Koah-pou3-toh koah hou7 i chit8-te3 chit8-te3 hoaiN5 -tit8 kok si3-chhun3.  Koah-pou3 to si7 than2-khia7 chhah ti7 toh-bin7. Lang5 chiong siang-chhiu2 koah-lih8 pout3? to-chhui3.  ( KhoaN3 tui3-bin7 ang-a2-tou5 teng2-thau5-e5 . )        
Phoa3-pou3 koah-liau2 chiu7 u7 theh8-khi3 lok ti7 toa7-kng tai7-liok8 chhin-chhiuN7 hong-kou2 chit8-iuN7 , kou2 tin5-ai long2 chhut-khi3 .        
Khah-siong5 phoa3-pou3 si7 bak--tioh8 iu5 kap pat8-mih8 u3-oe3, sou2-i2 soa3 u7 loh8 kiN-chui2 lai5 sah8 hou7 la5-sam5 khi2-lai5 .        
Phoa3-pou3 sah8-liau2 chiu7 u7 theh8 jip8 Se2-thoa7-chhia hou7 chheng-khi3 e5 chui2 chhiang5 jip8-lai5 siong5-se3 se2-thoa7 .  ( KhoaN3 tui3-bin7 e5 ang-a2-tou5 tiong-ng to3-chhiu2-peng5--e5 . )        
Tui3 hit--nih8 tioh8 theh8 loh8 Thiah-li3-pou3 e5 bo7-chhia .  Ti7 lai7 -bin7 u7 che7-che7 khi2 tai7-liok8 chhin-chhiuN7 ka-to chit8 iuN7 .  In u7 thiah-li3 phoa3-pou3 kau3 chhui3-chhui3 iu3-iu3, chham chui2 si7 cho3 iuN5 kou5 -kou5 e5 mih8 ho7 -cho3 Choa2-chiuN .  ( KhoaN3 tui3-bin7 e5  ang-a2-tou5 tiong-ng chiaN3-chhiu2-peng5--e5 . )        
In-ui7 phoa3-pou3 u7 chap8-hang7 e5 sek- ti3 tu2-a2 cho3 choa2-chiuN si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 peh8, sou2-i2 lang5 u7 chiong hit-e5 choa2-chiuNau loh8 ti7 phio3-hun2 lai5 phio3 hou7 i peh8 .        
Cho3 choa2-chiuN kau3 - giah8 peh8 chiu7 u7 eng7 chheng-khi3 chui2 koe3-tiu5 hou7 phio3 -hun2 tui3 lai7-bin7 long2 khi2-lai5 .       
Chhong3 kau3 an2-ni choa2 -chiuN si7 long2 chiau5-pi7 thang cho3 choa2 , sou2 -i2 lang5 u7 theh8 koe3-khi3 ti7 Cho3 -choa2-chhia .  ( KhoaN3 tui3-bin7 ang-a2-tou5 e7-kha--e5 . )  Ti7-chia thang pau-ham5 hun-piat hong-hoat e5 si3-cham7. ( 1 ) PiaN3 choa2-chiuN ti7 choa2-chiuN-kng.  ( 2 ) Hou7 choa2-chiuN tui3 kng-kha lau5 -chhut ti7 toa7-phiN3 tang5-lo5-se , ti7-hia thoaN -poh8-poh8 kap hou7 chui2 tui3 se3 - khang- a2 siap8-chhut-lai5 .  ( 3 ) Soa3 u7 chin3 - cheng5 phou ti7 toa7-nia2 chiN-tiau5, i2-keng si7 piN3- cho3 chheN-choa2, na7-si7 chin  chhou-siap iah8 bo5 sim2-mih8 kian-teng7.  ( 4 ) Au7-lai5 hit-e5 chheN-choa2 u7 jip8 ti7 kui2 -na7 tui3 e5 chhia - kng2 e5 tiong-ng , u7- e5 geng2 i cho3 peN5 -bin7 kap kim-seN , iah8 u7- e5 si7 sio thang hang i cho3 ta , chiu7 kau3 lou7-boe2 choa2 long2 beng5-pek8 u7 soan-chhut-lai5 put-chi2 tng5, chhut-chai7 lang5 chhai5-koah cho3 lou7-eng7 .       
Lan2 e5 sim goan5-pun2 si7 pai7-hoai7 kap la5-sam5 chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 sou2 khi3-sak phoa3-pou3 chit8-iuN7 .        
Siong7-te3 ai3 kam2-hoa3 lan2 e5 sim- sin5 cho3 chheng-khi3-siuN3 kap ho2 lou7 - eng7 e5 khoan2-sit .
Lang5 siu7 pian3-hoa3 chiu7-si7 tioh8 hou7 to7-li2 kek-phoa3 sim-koaN lai5 hoan2-hoe2 , soa3 hou7  Ia5-sou e5 hoeh se2-tu5 choe7, kap Seng3-sin5 siong5-se3 iok-sok su-iok8 kau3 chin-chiaN3ke2-ku7 oaN7-sin chhin-chhiuN7 choa2 tu2-a2 cho3 chiaN5 e5 the2-sek.  Goan7 lan2 tai7-ke hoaN-hi2 tui3 Siong7-te3 siu7 ke2-piN3 , chiah hou7 I toa7 hoaN-hi2 lan2 e5 iuN7 . Sim sou2 goan7 . 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 102 koan3,1893 ni5 9 geh8)
