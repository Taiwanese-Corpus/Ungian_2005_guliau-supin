
Hai2-kau2.
Lun7 Hai2-kau2 e5 siN-chiaN5, in e5 thau5-khak si7 chhin-chhiuN7 kau2 e5 khoan2-sit ,seng-khu tng5 - tng5 na2 boe2-liu na2 sio2-se3,it-li2 chhin-chhiuN7 hi5-sin . Oa2 boe2-chhe liong2-peng5-piN u7 nng7-e5 sio2-kha chhin-chhiuN7 hi5-sit8,thau5-cheng5 u7 nng7-ki toa7-kha jiau2 lioh8-a2 chhin-chhiuN7 ah-te5 . In e5 chhui3-khi2 kek8 lai7 koa3 siap,tu2-hap8 ka7 hi5 e5 lou7-eng7 , be7 khit i kut8-chau2 . Chong2-si7 u7 lang5 kong2,Hai2-kau2 m7-na7 chiah8 hi5 nia7-tiaN7 , ia7 kiam chiah8 chhau2 . I-e5 seng-seng5 chit8-hang7 khah koh-iuN7,chiu7-si7 u7-si5 ai3 thun chioh8-thau5,in-ui7 lang5 bat liah8-tioh8 phoa3-khui pak-lai7,siong5-siong5 khoaN3-kiN3 u7 chioh8-thau5-liap8-a2 , chiah chai .
Lun7 i -e5 hou-khip ia7 si7 khah put-tong5 ,in-ui7 teh chhoan2-khui3 chin ban7,chha-put-to nng7 hun ku2 chiah chhoan2 chit8 pai2 nia7-tiaN7. In ia7 e7-kham-tit ti7 chui2-e7 chha-put-to poaN3-tiam2 cheng ku2 . Hai2-kau2 e5 seng3 ai3 tiam3 liang5-leng2 e5 sou2-chai7 , sou2-i2 oa2 ti7 pak-thau5 u7 khah che7. In-ui7 in- e5 bah e7 chiah8--tit, iu5 toa7 lou7-eng7,ia7 phoe5 kai3 tat8-chiN5,hou7 lang5 chhu2 cho3 in-chiuN5 chheng7 kek8 - sio ,sou2-i2 lang5 eng7 pou7 khi3 tng-tan2 siat- lai5 liah8 in.  KhoaN3 chit-hang7 chiu7 chai chit-ho7 seng-li2 chin toa7, chiu7-si7 tak8 ni5 u7 thang li8ah peh-ban7-goa7 chiah ; koh chit8 nia2 Hai2-kau2 phoe5 e5 hiu5 e7 tat8-tit si3-gou7 -pah e5 gin5-chiN5. 
 (Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 113 koan3,1894 ni5 8 geh8)
