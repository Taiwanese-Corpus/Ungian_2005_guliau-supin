Che3-iam5 e5 Ki-khi3
Chu7 cha2-cheng5 i2-lai5 mui2 sou2-chai7 teh phak8 iam5 long2-si7 chiong kiam5-chui2 phak8- ta, khah che7-ui7 si7 eng7 jit8 phak8-e5: Chong2-- si7 Eng-kok kap Bi2-kok e5 jit8 bo5 hiah iam7, chiaN3 iong7 thou5-thoaN3 hiaN5 tiaN2 lai5 chu2. M7-ku2 hit-e5 thou5-thoaN3 khah kui3, iam5 e5 ke3-chiN5 khah siok8, khah bo5 siaN2 than3.  Kin7-lai5 Eng-kok u7 lang5 kek-chhut chit8-e5 ki-khi3, sui-jian5 iu5-goan5 si7 eng7 thou5-thoaN3 lai5 chu2, m7-ku2 sou2 iong7 e5 thou5- thoaN3 kek8 seng2, chit8-chau3 e5 thou5-thoaN3 e7 chu2-tit kui2-na7 tiaN2 e5 iam5; pun2-chiN5 khah seng2, than3 chiN5 khah che7. Chiau3 hit-peng5 e5 kok-hoat na7 u7 kek-chhut sin ki-khi3 e5 khi3 pin2-po3 KoaN-hu2, KoaN-hu2 m7-chun2 pat8-lang5 cho3 kap i sio-siang7 e5. Pat8-lang5 na7 ai3 cho3 kap i sio-siang7, tioh8 Kong2 chiN5 hou7 hit-e5 lang5 hoaN-hi2, chiaN3 beh chun2 i cho3. 
Chim-ma2 u7 Bi2-kok che3-iam5 e5 hoe7-sia7 kap hit-e5 kek ki-khi3 e5 lang5 lip8-iok, chit- e5 che3-iam5 e5 li7-koan5 chi2-u7 hit-e5 kap i lip8- iok e5 hoe7-sia7 e7 che3-cho7--tit, thong Bi2- kok e5 lang5 long2-chong2 be7 che3-cho7--tit, beh chiong chit8-chheng ban7 gin5 ka7 i be2 chit-e5 li7-koan5; chit-e5 kek ki-khi3 e5 lang5 i2-keng in2 i. Chit-e5 lang5 si7 lan2 Eng-kok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 tiuN2-lo2. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 317 koan3, 1911 ni5 8 geh8)
