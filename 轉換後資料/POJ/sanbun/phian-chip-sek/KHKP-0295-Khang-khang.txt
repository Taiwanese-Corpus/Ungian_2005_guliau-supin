
Khang-khang
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 choan-bu7 teh kiu5 chiN5- chai5, soa3 oa2-kho3 e7 pai7-hoai7 e5 but8-giap5,  che si7 khang-khang.  
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 kiu5 chun-keng3, soa3 lo5- sim khi3 tou5-bou5 ko-kui3 e5 chit-jim7, che si7 khang-khang.  
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 theng2 hiat-khi3 e5 su-iok8, soa3 him-bou7 hit ho7 tek-kak e7 ham7-hai7  lan2-e5, che si7 khang-khang.  
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 ai3 tng5-hoe3-siu7, put-ko3 hu-tou7 kong-im bo5 chin7-tiong, cho3 i e5  kang-tiaN5, che si7 khang-khang.  
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 ti3-i3 chai7-seN e5 tai7-chi3, soa3 bo5 iau3-kin2 boe7 lai5 e5 keng2-gu7, che si7  khang-khang.  
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 it-tioh8 khoai3 koe3 e5 kong-keng2, soa3 bo5 tui3-tiong7 eng2 hok e5  te7-pou7, che si7 khang-khang. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-ohe7-po3 te7 295 koan3, 1909 ni5 10 geh8)
