

Kho2-chian3 ji5-hou7 Kho2-ho5
 “Tioh8 e7 kham-tit kap lang5 sio-thai5 chiah e7 kham-tit kap lang5 kong2 ho5.” Chit ku3 seng5-gu2 u7 hap8 ti7 Seng5-po3 sin- bun5 cheng5-jit8 sou2 chhut e5 lun7.  TaN chiong hit-e5 tai7-i3 ki3 ti7 e7-te2:--  
Eng-kok e5 ChaiN2-siong3 Cho3-ti7--si7 toa3 ti7 Sou-kiat-lan5 e5 Ai3-teng-po2 siaN5 ian2-soat kong2, Kin7-lai5 u7 put-chi2 oh e5 toa7 bun7- te5 nng7 hang7 : -- (1)  Ngou5-lo5 bo5 tiuN-ti5 sit-loh8 sio-thai5 e5 khui3-lat8, ti3-kau3 tai7- kiok8 sun2-hai7.  (2) Tek-kok e5 bo5-an7-che3 e5 chiam5-chui2-theng2-chian3 chou2-tong3 su3- thong pat-tat8 kiaN5 chun5 e5 giap8.  
Lun7 Ngou5-kok ti3-kau3 loan2-jiok8.  ChaiN2- siong3 kong2, Tek-kok tui3 Tang chian3-soaN3 tiau3 kau3 Sai chian3-soaN3 e5 kun-tui7 che7 koh iong2-beng2.  Lan2 e5 kok oh tit-tioh8 Bi2-kok e5 pang-chou7.  Chai7 kok sou2 tiau3-- lai5 e5 peng, han7-tiaN7 u7 Tek-kok tui3 Ngou5- kok e5 chian3-soaN3 tiau3 kau3 kun-tui7 gou7 hun7 e5 chit8 hun7, sou2-i2 hian7-chai7 Sai-peng5 e5 chian3-soaN3 si7 te7-it gui5-hiam2 e5 te7-ui7.  
Chit-e5 tai7-chi3, chhin-chhiuN7 Heng- teng-po2 kap Ui2-ni2-sun3 (chiu7-si7 Tek-kok e5 goan5-soe3 kap Bi2-kok e5 tai7-thong2-leng2) toa3 ti7 un7-tong7-tiuN5 sai3-hoe7 e5 sio-siang7. Chit8-e5 tui3 Ngou5-kok tiau3 tai7-kun lai5 kong-kek lan2 Hiap8-iok-kok; chit8-e5 tui3 Bi2-kok tiau3 tai7-kun lai5 pang-chou7 lan2 Hiap8-iok-kok.  Chit8-e5 kong, chit8-e5 chou7, sim2-mih8 lang5 khah-kin2 chiu7-si7 hit-e5 seng-iaN5, sim2-mih8 lang5 tai7-seng chiu7 hit- e5 khah u7 li7-ek.  Tek-kok te7-ji7 pai2 kong- kek e5 si5 teh-beh kau3.  Chit pai2 e5 kong- kek tek-khak pi2 cheng5 koh khah toa7 sio-thai5.  Chong2-si7 iaN5-su e5 eng2-hiong2 si7 u7 lek8-su2 i2-lai5 toa7 e5 tai7-chi3, se3-kai3 e5 bun5- hoa3 ia2-ban5 tui3 chit-e5 u7 hun-piat; lan2 kok e5 chun5-bong5 ia7 si7 chit-e5 koan-he7. Sou2-i2 chim2-ma2 e5 cheng5-heng5 se3-bin7, si7 sui-lang5 sou2 te7-it eng-kai chu3-i3 e5 jit8. Sui-jian5 be7-tit bian2 chit-e5 gui5-hiam2, chong2-si7 lan2 Eng-kok, Hoat-kok, Bi2- kok kap I3-kok Sai chian3-soaN3 e5 kun-tui7 long2-chong2 lian5-lok8 tang5-i3.  Bo5-lun7 Tek- kok an3-chaiN2-iuN7 beng2-iong2 kong-kek, goa2 chhim-sin3 lan2 iau2-ku2 tu2 i e7 tiau5. Theng3-hau7 kui2 goeh8-jit8 au7 Bi2-kok e5 tai7- peng long2 kau3, chiu7 chhu2 kong-phah e5 koan5-lek8, tek-khak e7 iaN5.  
 Lun7 Eng-kok chun5-lou7 e5 su7-giap8. ChaiN2-siong3 kong2, Eng-kok chun5-lou7 e5 su7-giap8 si7 na2-chun2 Hiap8-iok-kok e5 na5- au5, chit tau2-ku2-a2 hou7 lang5 thai5-koah, sui-jian5 bo5 chiap-tin7 sio-thai5 ia7 si7 su. Ku7-ni5 e5 tiong-kan chiam5-chui2-theng2- chian3 e5 eng2-hiong2 cham2-jian5 toa7, kau3 chim2-ma2 keng3-jian5 e7-tit che3-ap i, lan2 e5 hai2-kun me5-jit8 tu5-biat8 chiam5-chui2- theng2, put-chi2 chin7-lat8.  Na7-si7 na2-ku2 chiu7 lan2 kong-phah chiam5-chui2-theng2 e5 hau7-ko2 tek-khak na2 toa7.  Kau3 kin-a2-jit8 lan2 sou2 hui2 e5 chiam5-chui2-theng2 i2-keng poaN5-koe3 ti7 Tek-kok sou2 chou3--e5.   
Koh-chai3 Hiap8-iok-kok che3-cho7 e5 siong-chun5, jit8-jit8 na2-che7.  Kin-ni5 sou2 cho3 e5 tun7-sou3, i2-keng pi2 ku7-ni5 teng5-poe7. Me5-ni5 tek-khak pi2 kin-ni5 gou7-poe7 che7. Bi2-kok e5 cho7 siong-chun5 pi2 Eng-kok khah kin2.  ThiaN-kiN3 in e5 Chun5-cheng3- pou7 Chong2-tiuN2 teh kong2, i2-au7 Bi2-kok e5 siong-chun5, tak8-jit8 cho7 ho2 e5 che7-chio2, chhe2-un5 tek-khak tak8-jit8 e7 tit-tioh8 chit8 ban7 tun7 chit8 chiah e5 siong-chun5.  Koh- chai3 Jit8-pun2 kok cho7-chun5 ia7 cham2-jian5 che7.  Iu7-koh tiong-lip8-kok, chhin-chhiuN7 Na2-ui ia7 me5-jit8 keng-eng5 lai5 che3-cho7. Tui3 an2-ni khoaN3, chiam5-chui2-theng2- chian3 sui-jian5 be7-e7 khoaN3 i cho3 choan5- jian5 sit-pai7, kau3 boe2 ti7 se3-kai3 e5 chian3- cheng Hiap8-iok-kok e7 iaN5; be7-e7 hou7 chit- e5 chiam5-chui2-theng2-chian3 sou2 chou2-tong3.  
Lun7 siong-giap8 chui2-siu2.  ChaiN2- siong3 put-chi2 o-lo2 i e5 iong2-beng2, kong2, Chiap8-chiap8 khoaN3-kiN3 phah-tim5-chun5 e5 chui2-siu2 kui2-na7 me5-jit8 toa3 ti7 kiu3-seng- chun5 e5 tiong-kan, seN3-mia7 chai7 chit8 tau2- ku2-a2, chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 hioh8 chhiu7-hioh8 phiau-liu5 ti7 hai2-bin7; kau3 e7-tit chiuN7 soaN, liam5-piN  khong2-khai3 thiau3-jip8 chun5- tiong iu5-goan5 chin7 i e5 gi7-bu7.  Lan2 Eng- kok e5 chui2-siu2 e5 cheng-sin5 chiah--nih8 kian-kou3, chhin-chhiuN7 Si teh kong2, “Chiong3-chi3 seng5-seng5” (i3-su3 si7 cheng3- lang5 e5 siang7-sim ai3-kok chian5-cho3 siaN5 lai5 po2-hou7, eng2-oan2 be7 pai7-hoai7).  
(Tai7-ke thiaN-kiN3  ChaiN2-siong3 kong2 chiah-e5 oe7, hoaN-hi2 tah chhiu2). 
Lun7 chou-chit ho5-kiok8.  ChaiN2-siong3 kong2, chit pai2 sio-thai5 choan5-pou7 chham2- khok, kong2 oh-tit liau5.  Siat-su2 cheng3- hu2 ban7-it e7-tit kang2-ho5, goa2 bo5-m7 chin7 chit-e5 sim-koaN khui3-lat8 lai5 chan3-seng5. Lan2 kok-nih8 e5 tiong-kan tak8-e5 ai3 ho5 m7- ai3 chian3, in-ui7 si7 ui7-tioh8 jin5-to7 e5 khi2- kian3, chiah-e5 lang5 sui-jian5 chai chit-e5  ho5 si7 ho2, chong2-si7 m7-chai chit-e7 sui5- pian7 lai5 cho3, chiu7 ho2 e5 tai7-chi3 e7 seN- chhut m7-ho2 e5 su7.  
U7 nng7 hang7 put-chi2 gui5-hiam2 ti7 chit- nih8.  (1)  Tek-kok e5 jin5-bin5 khoaN3-kiN3 lan2 kok-nih8 e5 Sin-bun5-po3, chai chiah-e5 lang5 ai3 ho5 e5 oe7, tek-khak in tak8-e5 chiu7 kong2, Eng-kok e5 jin5-bin5 kiN3-tioh8 sio-thai5 tioh8-kiaN lau5 bak8-sai2; taN, Eng-kok e5 se3- bin7 kaN2 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 Ngou5-kok su goan2.   
An2-ne-seN chiah e5 lang5 koaN2-kiN2 ai3 beh kong2 ho5, hoan2-tng2 e7 tho2 sio-thai5, phi3-lun7 Song3-kok e5 lang5 ai3 beh pang- chan7 ng-a2 toa7 ka7 i poeh8-khi2--lai5, kiam2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ne mah ?  (2) Lan2 kok-nih in-ui7 sio-thai5 lai5 li7-khui pe7-bo2 hiaN-ti7 bou2-kiaN2, be7-tit thang cho3-hoe2 chu7-chip8 e5 lang5 i2-keng u7 chheng-chheng ban7-ban7, he, m7-ai3 sio-thai5 e5 sim-koaN tek-khak put-chi2 chhiat.  Chit8 tau2-ku2-a2 thiaN-kiN3 chit ho7 oe7, to him-bou7 chit e5 ho5-kiok8 u7 ian5-kou3, goa2 tek-khak kap pan7 kok-ka chian3-su7 e5 lang5 siong5-liong5 chan3-seng5.  
 Chiah-e5 ai3 ho5-peng5 e5 lang5 kong2-oe7 sui5-jian5 tioh8, ki5-sit8 hau7-ko2 si7 ke-thiN tui3-tek8 e5 ui-hong, soa3 biat8-bo5 lan2 chu7- ki2 e5 chi3-khi3.  Be7-e7 kong2-ho5 e5 si5-chun7, m7-thang kap lang5 kong2-ho5, khoaN3-chim2- ma2 Ngou5-kok to e7 chai lah ! Na7 ho5-kiok8 kau3 hou7 lan2 e7 phah-sng3--tit, lan2 tek- khak beh chin7 hit e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 cho3 Peh-seN3 e5 sou2-ai3 chiah ai3, peh-seN3 sou2 m7- ai3 chiah m7-ai3, to si7 cheng3-ti7 e5 iau3-kin2.   
Phi2-jin5 e5 lek8-su2 tak8-lang5 long2 chai Chhu3-lai7 san3-hiong, bo5 se3-lek8, sou2-i2 kin-a2- jit8 e7-tit tng chaiN2-siong3 e5 chit-hun7, long2 si7 chiah-e5 tong5-pau5 e5 ki2-chian3.  Siat- su2 chit8 jit8 be7 thang hou7 jin5-bin5 sin3-iong7, to si7 goa2 li7-khui chek-jim7 e5 jit8-chi2.  
Hai7--ah ! Sio-thai5 keng-koe3 si3-ni5, iaN5 su e5 ki-koan pek kau3 bak8-chiu-cheng5, lin2 chiah-e5 chu2-te7 toa3 ti7 chian3-tiuN5 hian2- chhut toa7 kong-lo5, goa2 e7-tit oa2-kho3 in lai5 an-un2 khun3; tok8 tok8 ng3-bang7 in kian-jim2 kau3 thai5-iaN5 to3-lai5 e5 jit8, to bo5 loa7-ku2--lah ! Hit-si5 tek-khak u7 chit8- khoan2 e5 sin se3-kai3, thai3-peng5 e5 khoan2-sit. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 402 koan3, 1918 ni5 9 geh8)
