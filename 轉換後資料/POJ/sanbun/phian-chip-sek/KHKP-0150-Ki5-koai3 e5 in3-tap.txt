
Ki5-koai3 e5 in3-tap
Khah-cha2 u7 chit8-e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5, i si7 cho3 un2-ki e5 thak8-chheh lang5, long5 bo5 ai3 chhap siok8-su7. Tu2--tioh8 bou2-jit8 chit8-e5 lang5-kheh lai5 tham3--i. Hit-e5 lang5-kheh chiu7 mng7 i kong2, Li2 si7 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 houhN? In3 kong2, ChiaN3-si7. Lang5-kheh chiu7 koh kong2, chiau3 lin2 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 teh kong2, “ThiN-te7-kan chi2-u7 chit8-e5 Siong7-te3 tak8 sou2-chai7 ti7-teh”. Na7 si7 goa2 long2 m7 bat khoaN3-tioh8 Siong7-te3, chiah m7-ai3 sin3; chhiaN2 li2 chhoa7 goa2 khi3 khoaN3 chiu7 beh sin3 lah.    
Hit-e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 cheng7-cheng7 bo5 in3. Theng5-chit8-e7 chiu7 aN3-loh8 thou5-kha khioh chit8-te3 thou5-oan5, tui3 hit-e5 lang5- kheh e5 thau5-khak chhut toa7-lat8 chiu7 ka7 i tim3-loh8-khi3. Hit-e5 lang5-kheh hou7 i tim3-tioh8 thiaN3, chiu7 toa7 siu7-khi3, tui3 koaN- hu2 khi3 ko3.   
KoaN-hu2 chiu7 chek-si5 khi3 tiau3 hit-e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 thak8-chheh lang5 lai5 mng7 i kong2, I chheng2-kau2, li2 ki3-jian5 m7 in3--i, chiu7 siaN2-su7 hoan2 tng2 eng7 thou5-oan5 tim3-- i, he u7 sim2-mih8 cheng5-li2 ah? Hit-tiap8 hit e5 sin3 Chu2 e5 thak8-chheh lang5, khai3- jian5 khai3-jian5 tui3 koaN-hu2 e5 bin7-cheng5 in3 kong2, Goa2 eng7 thou5-oan5 tim3 i-e5 thau5- khak, chiu7-si7 teh in-tap i sou2 mng7. Lun7 thau5-khak tim3--tioh8 e7 thiaN3, chit- ho7 si7 lang5-lang5 sou2 sin3, sou2 chai e5 cheng5- li2; m7-ku2 bo5 lang5 bat khoaN3--tioh8 hit-e5 thiaN3 seN-cho3 an3-chaiN2 iuN. Ia7 long2 bo5 chit8- lang5 kaN2-kong2, thiaN3 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 bo5 iaN2 u7 thiaN3. An3-ni Siong7-te3 si7 Sin5, bo5-heng5 bo5-siong7, eng2-leng5 seng3-the2, bu5-sou2 put- chai7. Tui3 kou2-cha2-i2-lai5 bo5 lang5 bat khoaN3- tioh8. Na7 si7 tui3 Siong7-te3 sou2 chhong3-cho7 e5 thiN-te7 ban7-mih8, thang chai u7 chit8-e5 Siong7-te3. Che si7 chu7-jian5 e5 li2-khi, bo5 thang pian7-pok. Tek-khak m7-thang in- ui7 bak8-chiu bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, soa3 m7 sin3, chiu7 kek8 toa7 chha-biu7.    
KoaN-hu2 thiaN-liau2 ki5-koai3 i sou2 in3-tap, Liah8 i-e5 oe7 si7 tioh8. Lang5-khek kian3-siau3 chiu7 khi3. 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 150 koan3, 1897 ni5 9 geh8)
