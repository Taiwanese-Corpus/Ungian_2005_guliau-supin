
 
Mou5-si7
Ti7 Lun5-tun-siaN5 e5 tang-peng5 u7 che7- che7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 cho3-hoe2 ti7-hia khia7- khi2, khah toa7 bin7 si7 teh cho3 seng-li2; chong2-si7 oh-tit than3-chiah8. Bou2-jit8 u7 chit8 e5 lau7-lang5 mia5 Mou5-si7 ti7 ke-lou7 teh kiaN5, chin koaN2, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 phoa3-piN7, pun2 sin ka-ti7 teh liam7 kong2, thiN-khi3 bo5 kau3 loa7 koaN5, u7 chhut-jit8, goa2 thai3-tho2 koaN5 kau3 an2-ni. Tan7 m7-chai beh an3-choaN2 ? kiaN-liau2 bo5 lat8 thang kiaN5 kau3 chhu3. Kin-a2-jit8 cho3 che m7 chiaN5 seng-li2, koh bo5 siaN2-lang5 be2, li7 bo5 siaN thang o; lang5 thiaN-liau2 ia7 m7 chai i teh be7 saN2-hoe3. Pak-tou2 iau kau3 phah-kiat-kiu5, chin chiaN3 kho2-lin5 tai7-ah ! Eng2 koe3 na7 than3 bo5 chiah8 i e5 chhu3-piN u7 theh8 tam7-poh8 chan7 i, taN chit moa2 bo5, in-ui7 ti7 in e5 tiong-kan sit the2-bian7, chit8 hang7 bo5 hau7-seN, koh toa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khi3 ke3 Ki-tok-tou5. Chhu3-piN long2 hiam5 i, chun2 i e5 cha-bou2- kiaN2 khi3 ke3 iN7-pang-lang5; ki5-sit8 bo5 iaN2, i pun2-sin chin m7 goan7, ti3-kau3 bo5 beh jin7 i cho3 kiaN2. Sui-jian5 kau3 hiah kan- khou2, ia7 bo5 beh hou7 i e5 kiaN2-sai3 chan7 i.     
Iu5-thai3-lang5 pun2 gau5 saN chan7, chong2- si7 in u7-e5 na7 khi3 kap iN7-pang-lang5 ke3- chhoa7, in chiu7 bo5 boeh pang-chou7 i. Iu5- thai3-lang5 e5 koe3-ni5 kau3 lah, chiau3 in e5 hong-siok8 tioh8 cho3 koe3-ni5 cheh, ia7 tioh8 khi3 hoe7-tng5 le2-pai3. Kin e5-hng lau7-lang5 siuN7 be7 tit-thang cho3 an2-ni, sim chin iu- bun7. Cha2-cheng5 i toa3 ti7 Ngou5-lo5-su-kok cho3 chhai5-hong5, khah u7 than3, m7 bian2 khoa3-lu7 chhin-chhiuN7 kin-a2-jit8 e5 bo5 kau3. Chit e5 chham2 si7 tui3 hou7 Ngou5-lo5-su koaN2.  TaN lai5 Eng-kok hut-jian5 bo5 thau5-lou7, lang5 koh lau7 lah ! chhu3-piN khoaN3-khin eng7 thi2 chhio3 e5 oe7 ka7 i kong2, TaN tioh8 khah se3-ji7 kou3 li2 e5 se3 cha-bou2-kiaN2 leh ! bian2 tit i ia7 khi3 ke3 hit-ho7 bo5 chheng- khi3 e5 lang5. Chit-ho7 oe7 thiaN-liau2 Sim- koaN be7 bian2-tit toa7 kan-khou2.    
Koh chin3-cheng5 kiaN5 bo5 loa7-hng7, khoaN3- kiN3 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 lai5 ngia5 chih i. Cha-bou2-kiaN2 khoaN3 lau7-pe7 ban7-ban7 a2 kiaN5, chiu7 khan i e5 chhiu2, bong tioh8 leng2-ki- ki, sim-lai7 kiaN, lau5 bak8-sai2 kong2, An2-pe7 ah, li2 na7 kau3 chhu3, goa2 chiu7 chau2- khi3 be2 chha5 khi2-hoe2 hou7 li2 hang, be2 tam7-poh8 bah ka7 li2 chu2 thng, chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 e5 lau7 bo2 iau2 oah8 teh tiNg72-lo5-su-kok sou2 chu2 e5. Lau7-pe7 kong2, sim-koaN kiaN2Lou7-tek, m7-thang be2 hiah e5, kim me5-hng u7 kui2-na7 hang7 khah iau3-kin2 tioh8 be2, chiu7-si7 nng7 ki lah8 chek, tam7-poh8-a2 bit8, nng7 liap8-a2 pin-ko2 (in-ui7 sim-koaN lai7 teh liau5-bang7 Bi2-sai2-a phah-sng3 e5-hng e7 kau3) Lan2 tioh8 pi7-pan7 siong5-siong5 ki3-tit Ia5-ho5-hoaseng3 e5 su7 si7 khah iau3-kin2 lan2 e5 kim3-chiah8. Lou7-tek chai lau7-pe7 sou2 tui3- tiong7 si7 hiah e5, sou2-i2 tiam7 tiam7 in3 i, Ho2.    
Chin kho2-sioh ah ! Kiu3-chu2 i2-keng kang3-seng chheng goa7 ni5 ku2, Iu5-thai3- lang5 iau2-ku2 m7-chai, tai7-ke gong7-gong7 chhin-chhiuN7 Mou5-si7 teh ng3-bang7 Bi2-sai3 a e7 lim5-kau3 hian2-chhut toa7 koan5-leng5. Chit8-che5 chhoa7 i si3-soaN3 e5 peh-seN3 tui3 tak8 pang kok to3-khi3 Ia5-lou7 sat-leng2 e5 kiaN-siaN5. Ni5 koe3 ni5, chit8 e5 cheh koh chit8 e5 cheh, Bi2-sai3-a long2 bo5 lai5, chiau3 in sou2 phah-sng3, Mou5-si7 long2 bo5 sit-bang7, tiaN7 tiaN7 kong2, TaN ng3-bang7 au7 e5 cheh.    
In kiaN5 koh chit8 te7-a2 lou7 chiu7 tu2-tioh8 sio-bat e5 chhu3-piN, si7 be7 hi5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, mia5 kio3 Li7-pek-ka. I chiu7 theng5 kha, chheng-hou i kong2, Mou5-si7 peh ah ! li2 chiah-ku2 chin toa7 te3; goa2 kio3 li2 lai5 goan2 tau che7, li2 long2 m7, Lou7-tek ah, li2 ia7 si7 an2- ni, kaN2-si7 siuN7, li2 e5 che2-hu teh khui tiam3 pi2 goan2 khah the2-bian7, Mou5-si7 kiah8 chhiu2 kong2, Li7-pek-ka ah, li2 m7 thang kong2 chit-ho7 khau-siah e5 oe7, goa2 lau7 leh, hi7- khang tang7, Lou7-tek it tioh8 ai3 than3 kui2- iN5, put-si5 koan-sim khoa3-lu7. Goan2 u7 toa7 chhut-lat8 ai3 tong3 Ma2-li7-a, i m7 thiaN khou2 khng3, cho3 i khi3 ke3 hit-ho7 lang5, goan2 chiu7 bo5 hoat--tit.     
Li7-pek-ka teN3-chh eNkong2, Lin2 siaN2-su7 hiah gong7 thiaN lang5 ke-kong2-oe7, chiu7 sit-chi3 kau3 an2-ni. Kong2, cho3 in kong2, siat-su2 na7 kong2 tioh8 goa2, goa2 chiu7 teN3 bo5 thiaN-kiN3. A2-si7 kong2, kau2 teh pui7 ! Lang5 ge5-se2 e5 oe7 bo5 iau3-kin2, cho3 li2 pang3- sim. Goa2 khaN3 li2 e5 kan-khou2 hian2-chhut ti7 bin7, kin-a2-jit8 u7 seng-li2 bo5 ? Lou7-tek the3 i  
kong2, Bo5, chi2 u7 be7 nng7 tiau5-toa3, 40 chiN5 na7-tiaN7. Beh an3 choaN2 pan7 cheh koe3- ni5, Li7-pek-ka kong2, Chin-chiaN3 kho2-lian5 tai7-ah !  TaN e5 hng tioh8 hou7 goa2 chhiaN2, hi5 kap ko-pi na7-tian7, si7 pian7 chhai3 m7 thang khi3 hiam5, Lou7-tek hoaN-hi2 in3 ho2, sim-lai7 siuN7, tek-khak e7 chiah8 kau3 pa2-pa2, koh hi5, kap ko-pi long2-si7 hah8 goan2 pe7 seng-khu sou2 khiam3 e5. Mou5-si7 ia7 siuN7 siau3- liam7 cha2-cheng5 boe7 u7 kau3 chiah kan-khou2, ia7 u7 an2-ni thang chiah8.    
KiaN5 kau3 chhu3, Lou7 o5-tek khi3 be2 chha5 khi2-hoe2 hou7 lau7-pe7 hang; chiu7 khi3 Hoe7 tng5 theh8 tam7-poh8 seng3 e5 mi7-pau chit8 tiam2 phu5-to5 chiap, bit8, kap pin-ko2 chiau3 in e5 le7. Kho2-sioh Mou5-si7 long2 be7 sio, na2- hang na2 koaN5, chiu7 ka7 Lou7-tek kong2, goak2aN-si7 kam2-tioh8, be7 tit thang khi3 hou7 Li7-pek-ka khoan2-thai7, li2 ka-ti7 khi3 ho2 mah ? Lou7-tek khoaN3 i hiah kan-khou2 sim put an kong2, an2-pe7 ah ! E5-hng goa2 m7 kam li7 li2. Chiu7 khan i khi3 to2, chhiaN2 chhu3-piN chit8 e5 khi3 ka7 Li7 pek-ka kong2.  Li7-pek-ka thiaN tioh8 chiu7 kia3 mih8 hou7 in chiah8.  Lou7-tek khoaN3 pe7 teh khun3 m7 kaN2 phah chheN2, chiu7 pai5-toh, leng7- goa7 pai5 chit8-ui7 ng3-bang7 Bi2-sai3-a e7 lai5 tang5 che7 toh. Tu2 teh pai5 e5 si5 u7 lang5 theh8 chit8 tiuN phoe, si7 in A-che kia3-e5, chhiat-chhiat kiu5 pe7 sia3-bian2 ia7 kong2, Goa2 u7 the2-thiap lau7-pe7 kap sio2-moe7 iau, koaN5, kiu5 lin2 lai5 kap goa2 toa3, hou7 goa2 chiau3-kou3 ho2 mah ?  Na7 m7, tioh8 chun2 kiaN2-sai3 theh8 mih8 kap chiN5 hou7 lin2 koe3- ni5, Lo5-tek thak8 liau2 m7 kaN2 ka7 lau7-pe7 kong2, Mou5-si7 khun3 tang7-bin5 kau3 thiN- kng long2 m7 chai Hoe7-tng5 pun5 ho7-thau5 cho3 koe3-ni5 e5 siaN-im. Khun3 chheN2, Lo5- tek oa2 i e5 sim-piN mng7 an, soa3 kiong-hi2 koe3-ni5, ka7 i kong2, Li2 chin ho2 khun3. Pe7 chiu7 eng7 Iu5-thai3 e5 oe7 ka7 i chiok-hok. Lo5 tek chin ki5-koai3 i e5 lau7 pe7 bo5 chhin- chhiuN7 teng2-ni5, iau3-kin2 koe3 sin-ni5. I teh pi7-pan7 ko-pi e5 si5 lau5 bak8-sai2 hoan5-lo2 lau7-pe7 sit cheng5-sin5, pi7-pan7 beng5-pek8 kio3 lau7-pe7 lai5 chiah8, i kong2, koai-kiaN2 ah, li2 u7 pi7-pan7 Bi2-sai3-a e5 ui7, chin ho2 ah, soa3 se3-a2 siaN kong2, mng5 ia7 tioh8 khui. Goa2 chai I kin-a2-jit8 tek-khak e7 lai5.    
Che7 loh8 ki5-to2, ki5-to2 liau2, Lou7-tek khoaN3 lau7-pe7 e5 bin7, hut-jian5 u7 toa7 kng, I chiu7 khi2-lai5 kong2, Bi2-sai3-a lai5 lah. Seng-khu gi5-gi5 chhoah, koh kong2, Lo5-tek ah !  
Bi2- sai3-a lai5 lah ! I e5 mia5 cho3 Seng3 ! Koh lian5-lian5 kio3 Lou7-tek Lo5-tek ah, Lai5 lah ! Si7-ti3, ti3 Ai ah ! Ai ah ! Si7 ti3; chhi3-a2 bian2-liu5, Kong2 liau2 poah8-to2 ti7 thou5-kha, Lo5-tek toa7 siaN thi5-khau3.  Chhu3-piN thiaN- kiN3 chau2 lai5 hu5 i khi2-lai5,tai7-ke chai sin5- hun5 i2-keng khi3 lah ! U7 lang5 khi3 po3 i e5 A-che2, in liam5-piN lai5.  Lou7-tek kong2 A-che2 ah ! Chin kho2-sioh, li2 bo5 khoaN3 tioh8 An2-pe7 e5 bin7, u7 chhut toa7 kng teh chio3. Ma2-li7-a kong2, KhoaN3, ho2 si7 ho2, chong2-si7 i khoaN3 Chu2 ti3 chhi3-a2 bian2-liu5 chin ki5- koai3, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 i si7 Ki-tok-tou5, sit8-chai7; kaN2 m7 si7. I e5 tiong7-hu kong2, I beh si2 e5 si5 bo5 thang kong2 beng5, chiau3 goa2 phah-sng3 i beng5-pek8 chai Bi2-sai3-a si7 Iu5-thai3-lang5 sou2 teng3-si2 e5 Ia5-sou cho3 Iu5-thai3 iN7-pang e5 Kiu3-Chu2 (I2-hut-sou2 2:14). Lou7-tek kong2, An-pe7 khoaN3-oah8 leh ! I chai Chu2 lai5 liau2 ah. Goan7 Ia5-ho5-hoa khui Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 sim-bak8 hou7 in chai Siong7- te3 u7 chhe tok8-seN e5 Kian2 Ia5-sou ai3 kiu3 in kau3 I sou2 the3 in pi7-pan7 e5 Sun5-siaN5.    
Iu5-thai3-lang5 pun2 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 keng2 e5 peh-siN3, ui7-tioh8 khi3-sak Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Siong7-te3 chiu7 heng5-hoat in, hou7 in si3-soaN3 moa2 thiN-e7. Sui-jian5 an2-ni, Siong7-te3 un2- tang3 iau2 teh thiaN in, chhiaN2 khoaN3 po3 e5 lang5, tioh8 the3 Iu5-thai3-lang5 ki5-to2, hou7 in u7 ki-hoe7 thang thiaN tioh8 Ia5-sou e5 hok- im, ia7 e7 hoaN-hi2 sin3, jin7 Ia5-sou cho3 Bi2-sai3-a.
