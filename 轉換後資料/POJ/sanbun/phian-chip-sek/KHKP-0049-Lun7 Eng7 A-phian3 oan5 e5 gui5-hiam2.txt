
Lun7 Eng7 A-phian3 oan5 e5 gui5-hiam2
In-ui7 chai ti7 chit-e5 Tai5-oan5 u7 che7-che7 lang5 eng7 A-phian3 oan5 ai3 ke2 A-phian3 ,  goan2 lun7 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 ai3 kong2 chit8-nng7 hang7. Chit8-hang7, chiah8 A-phian3 oan5 pi2 chiah8 hun si7 khah siong-tiong7 . Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 kong2 , Goa2 tai7 -seng chiah8 hun , au7-lai5 chiah8 A-phian3 oan5 lai5 ke2 ; na7-si7 kiaN-liau2 han2-tit u7 an2-ni . Na7 ai3 ke2  A-phian3 e5 lang5 khah iau3-kin2  tioh8 chiau3 I-koan2 e5 hoat-tou7, chiu7-si7 tioh8 lai5 toa3  I-koan2 lai7-bin7 21 jit8 , long2 bo5 chhut-mng5 ; I-seng iah8 khoaN3 hit-e5 lang5 u7 sim2-mih8 phoa3-piN7, u7 sim2-mih8 kan-khou2, chiu7 chiong eng-kai ti7 e5 ioh8 lai5 ti7 i . Lang5 na7 bo5 thang lai5 I-koan2 ke2 , thang tui3 I-koan2 be2 ke2 A-phian3 e5 ioh8 , ( long2 bo5 A-phian3 ti7 lai7-bin7 , ) ka-ti7 thang ke2 ; m7-thang phah-sng3 na7 bo5 chiah8 A-phian3 oan5 chiu7 be7 ke2--tit. Chhin-chhiuN7 Hiong-kang2 hai2-su7 si7 goan2 Goa7-kok lang5 koan2--e5 ; ti7-hia u7 che7-che7 Tng5-lang5 , u7-si5 hoan7-choe7 hou7 koaN-hu2 liah8-khi3 koaiN-kaN, na7 chiah8 hun e5 lang5 kou-put-chiong tioh8 liam5-piN ke2, long2 m7-bat tit-tioh8 A-phian3 oan5 , a2-si7 ioh8-hun2 thang un5-un5-a2 ke2 .  
Koh chit8-hang7: goan2 chai u7 lang5 eng7 A-phian3 oan5 lai5 chi2-thiaN3 , kong2 si7 te7 -it ho2. Iah8 chai Kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5 u7-si5 chiongphian3 oan5 cho3 be2-be7, a2-si7 cho3 ka-ti7 el53-eng7 . Iah8 u7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2, cho3 pe7-bo2 el5ang na7 khoaN3-kiN3 i-e5 kiaN2-ji5 u7 sau3, a2-sipak7- tou2 thiaN3, chiu7 chiong A-phian3 oanh53 i chiah8 , phah-sng3 e7 ti7 i- e5 piN7 . TaNtioh8 chai, chit-e5 long2 bo5 ho2-se3 , iah8 bo5 un2-tang3 . Siat-su2 gin2-na2 lam7-sam2 chiah8 m7-ho2 e5 mih8, au7-lai5 chiu7 pak-tou2 -thiaN3 , m7-thang A-phian3 hou7 i; na7 hou7--i, sui-jianki5am-chhai2 e7 chi2 -thiaN3, iau2-ku2 si7 chiam7-si5 na7-tiaN7 , tek-khak be7 i-ho2--i , hoan2-tngki2am-chhai2 beh hai7 i kau3-si2 . Khah ho2 ekho5an-sit , si7 eng7 tam7-poh8 Tou7 -pi-iu5 houi7 sia3 , hou7 hit-e5 m7-tioh8 e5 mih8 chau2 chhut-lai5 , gin2-na2 chiu7 ho2 .
