Im-gak8 Sui5-pit

Im-gak8 kap kun7-tai7 lang5 e5 seng-oah8, put-chi2 u7 bit8-chiap e5 koan-he7, si7 lan2 lang5 e5 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8, sou2 boe7 thang bo5--tit e5 chit8-hang7.  Lan2 lang5 e5 hi7-khang put-si5 oe7 thang thiaN-tioh8 sin khoan2 e5 im-gak8, ti3-kau3 su-siuN2 kam2-cheng5 ke chin hong-hu3, u7 sin e5 koan-liam7 seN--chhut-lai5.
Im-gak8 oe7 hou7 lang5 u7 sin e5 kam2-kak, oe7 kiong-kip lang5 u7 koan-liam7 hong-hu3 e5 chhai5-liau7, che si7 sim2-mih8 in-toaN?  Chiu7-si7 hit e5 seng3-chit kap lan2 lang5 pun2 lai5 e5 seng3-chit u7 kiong7-thong e5 seng3-chit ti7-teh.  “Jin5 chi chhou, seng3 pun2 sian7.”  Lan2 lang5 pun2-te2 e5 seng3-chit si7 ho2, eng7 im-gak8 e5 oe7 lai5 kong2, chiu7-si7 “sui2.”  Chin ho2 thiaN e5 im-gak8, chiu7-si7 chin sui2 e5 im-gak8.  Sou2-i2 lang5 e5 sim-koaN na7 ho2, chiu7 thiaN-tioh8 ho2 e5 im-gak8, oe7 siu7 kam2-hoa3, piN-cho3 chin sui2 e5 sim-koaN, kap im-gak8 tu2-tu2 oe7 tiau-ho5; sit8-chai7 si7 chin bi5-biau7 e5 mih8.
Chin, Sian7, Bi2, chit 3 hang7 na7 oan5-choan5 e7 tit tiau-ho5, chiau3 i e5 tek8-seng3 lai5 hoat-hui, chiu7 lan2 lang5 e5 seng-oah8 e7 hiong3-siong7, e7 chheng-khi3-siuN3.  Kho-hak8 iau-kiu5 “Chin,” chong-kau3 iau-kiu5 “Sian7,” ge7-sut8 iau-kiu5 “Bi2.”  Chit 3 hang7 hou7 lan2 lang5 e5 jin5-seng-koan tit-tioh8 che7-che7 e5 am3-si7, bo5 han7 e5 kau3-hun3, lai5 tiaN7-tioh8 lan2 lang5 e5 seng-oah8 phiau-chun2.  Tek8-piat8 chit e5 “Bi2” e5 seng-oah8 kap lan2 e5 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 chin u7 ian5.  Him-bou7 “Bi2” si7 chin li2-siong2, chong2-si7 na7 khiam3 “Chin” kap “Sian7” chiu7 u7 gui5-hiam2-seng3 ti7-teh.  Sou2-i2 lan2 lang5 e5 li2-siong2 na7 tui3 chong-kau3, to7-tek lai5 seN-chhut, hit e5 sui2 chiu7 u7 ke3-tat8, chiu u7 li7-ek.  Lan2 lang5 e5 sim-seng3 tui3 sui2 lai5 tiau-ho5, chiu7 lan2 e5 kam2-cheng5 ke chin chhin-bit8, chin sio-lou7; sou2-i2 e7 hou7 lang5 seN-chhut tong5-cheng5 e5 sim.  Hou7 lang5 e5 sim-koaN kiat-lian5 e5 mih8 chiu7-si7 “ai3.”  Ti7 ai3 e5 tiong-kan, lan2 e7 thang hoat-kian3-tioh8 sun5-chin e5 “sui2.”  Chong-kau3 im-gak8 si7 chit8 hang7 chin sui2 e5 mih8, na7 u7 him-bou7 i3-ai3 e5 lang5, i e5 sim-koaN e7 chiam7-chiam7 piN3 ho2, kau3 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 pun2-lai5 e5 seng3-chit.  
“Sian7.”  Na7 ai3 im-gak8 e5 lang5, iu5-goan3 e7 ai3 chong-kau3, in-ui7 chit nng7 hang7 si7 chin chiap-kin7 e5 mih8, na7 sin3 chong-kau3 e5 lang5 iu5-goan5 ai3 im-gak8, chit nng7 hang7 eng2-oan2 u7 koan-lian5 ti7-teh.
Siong7-te3 sou2 chhong3-cho7 toa7 chu7-jian5 e5 se3-kai3 si7 chin sui2, kap ge7-sut8 chin u7 ian5-hun7.  Im-gak8 si7 choe3 te7-it ko-siong7 e5 ge7-sut8, sou2-i2 eng7 che lai5 chan3-bi2 cho7-hoa3 e5 Chu2, sit8-chai7 si7 chin hap8-li2-tek e5 su7.
 Ge7-sut8-ka chin ai3 kap chu7-jian5 kau-poe5, chu7-jian5 e5 im-gak8 khap8--tioh8 sim-lai e5 khim5-soaN3, chiu7 boe7 kim3-tit bo5 chan3-bi2 Siong7-te3, kap I sou2 cho7 e5 ban7-mih8.  Lam5-hng e5 to2-su7, put-si5 u7 chin sui2 e5 hong-keng2 hian-khui ti7 bak8-chiu-cheng5.  Chin sui2 e5 to2-su7. 
“Tai5-oan5,” e7 thang hoat-kian3-tioh8 im-gak8 e5 po2-khou3.  Su3-kui3 long u7 im-gak8 ian2-chau3.  Tiam3 ti7 tou-hoe7 seng-oah8 e5 lang5, na7 chit8 pai2 khi3 soaN-kan, chhau2-te7-thit-tho5, lai5 chiap-tioh8 chu7-jian5 e5 hong-keng2, kiam2 bo5 ke chin chheng-song2 mah?  SoaN--nih8 e5 hoe, khong-tiong e5 poe-chiau2, khe-chui2 e5 siaN-im, bo5 chit8-hang7 bo5 teh huan2 beng5 cho7-but8 Chu2 e5 ai3, bo5 chit8-hang7 bo5 teh o-lo2 I e5 toa7 koan5-leng5.  Chin-chiaN3 sui2 bo5 pi2!
 Na7 chin-sit8 ai3 im-gak8 e5 lang5, tek-khak iu5-goan5 e7 hou7 im-gak8 ai3--tit.  Sou2 kong2, sou2 khoan3, sou2 thiaN tak8-hang7 si7 im-gak8, su-siuN2, kam2-cheng5, i2-kip8 tui3 jin5-seng e5 kian3-kai2 long2 u7 im-gak8 e5 khi3-bi7 ti7-teh.  Chin ai3 gian2-kiu3 im-gak8 e5 lang5, tek-khak si7 hou7 im-gak8 sou2 kam2-hoa3.  Na7 u7 chin7-sim khi3 chhoe7, tek-khak e7 tu2-tioh8; u7 him-bou7 i3-ai3, tek-khak e7 tit-tioh8.  Ui7-tioh8 ge7-sut8 lai5 cheng-chin3, chiah si7 ho2 e5 chi3-goan7.  Ko-siong7 e5 ge7-sut8, tioh8 pun hou7 pat8 lang5 saN-kap u7 hun7, chiah u7 tat8 gian2-kiu3 e5 bok8-tek.  “Hou7 lang5 e5, pi2 tui3 lang5 theh8--e5 khah u7 hok-khi3” (Su3-tou5 20:35).  Beh pun hou7 pat8 lang5, u7-si5 tioh8 khiam3 ian2-chau3-chia2 lai5 chai3-hian7, chiah e7 tat8 bok8-tek; sou2-i2 tit-tioh8 tong5-chi3 e5 oan7-chou7 si7 chin pit-iau3 e5 su7.  Bo5-lun7 im-gak8-ka, a2-si7 kau3-hoe7 e5 im-gak8 thoan5-the2, tai7-ke chin saN-ho5, tak8 lang5 e5 sim-koaN long2 chhin-chhiuN7 im-gak8 e7 tiau-ho5, an2-ni chiah e7 thang ian2-chau3 ho2 thiaN e5 im-gak8,m7-na7 pun2-sin tit-tioh8 li7-ek, soa3 e7 thang pun hou7 pat8 lang5.  SaN tong5-cheng5, oan7-chou7, che si7 jin5-seng chit8-hang7 chin sui2 e5 tai7-chi3.  “Lan2 kiaN5 ho2 m7-thang sit-chi3, in-ui7 na7 bo5 chih8-cham7, kau3 ki5 e7 siu-seng5” (Ka-liap8-thai3 6:9).  Che si7 gian2-kiu3 im-gak8 e5 lang5, sou2 tioh8 phau7 e5 cheng-sin5.
 “Siu-seng5” si7 chit8 hang7 e5 hoaN-hi2, “pun hou7 lang5” si7 koh chit8 hang7 e5 hoaN-hi2.  Long2-chong2 e5 hoaN-hi2, si7 tui3 kam2-sia7, chan3-bi2 lai5 piau2-hian7.  Chin e7 chong-kau3 sin3-giong2 e7 seN-chhut kam2-sia, chan3-bi2 e5 sim.  Kam2-sia7, chan3-bi2 e5 jin5-seng chiah u7 tong5-pau-ai3, sia7-hoe7 hong7 su7 e5 cheng-sin5 chhut-hian7.  Chong-kau3-bi7 kap ge7-sut8-bi7 e5 jin5-seng, si7 put-chi u7 i3-gi7, u7 ke3-tat8.  Che si7 lan2 lang5 e5 li-siong2 kap hi-bong7, ia7-si7 jin5-seng e5 heng7-hok.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 Te7 606 Ho7, 1935 ni5 9 goeh8)
