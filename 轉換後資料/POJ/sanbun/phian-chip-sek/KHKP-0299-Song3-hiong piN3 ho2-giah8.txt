
 
Song3-hiong piN3 ho2-giah8
Kiu3-chu2 kang3-seng 1909 ni5 7 goeh8 kan u7 chit8-e5 khit-chiah8 , seN3 Ang5 mia5  Phauh-a2 , gu7-tioh8 loh8 toa7 hou7 lai5 Hoan-oah e5 pai3-tng5 pun.  Thoan5-to7 chiu7 mng7  i-e5 in-iu5 , kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i thiaN , in2-khi2 Lou7-ka 16:20.22 chat. I chiu7 kong2 20 goa7 ni5 cheng5 i-e5 chhin-lang5 bat kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i thiaN, hit tiap8 i tiu5-tu5; i cho3 khit-chiah8 20 goa7 ni5 ku2 .      
Tui3 an2-ni Thoan5-to7 kah i tioh8 koat-toan3 , kau3 le2-pai3-jit8 ko2-jian5 tak8 le2-pai3  lai5 ; Thoan5-to7 khoaN3 i lai5 chiu7 gia5 the-i2 hou7 i che7 ; che7 ti7 tai5-toh e5 sin-piN, in-ui7  hi7-khang chin7 tang7, u7 75 hoe3 .  Lai5 le2-pai3 e5 si5 i chin hoaN-si2 ; le2-pai3-jit8 m7-bat ka7 lang5 pun, i-e5 ka-chu3 tek-kong2 kia3 i-e5 chhin-lang5 ; kah thoan5-to7-e5 tioh8 ka3 i ki5-to2 ; khoaN3 i-e5 khoan2 sim-i3 bo5 ng3 ti7 se3-kan.  Tiong-kan u7 hiaN-ti7 khoaN3 thoan5-to7 gia5 the-i2 hou7 i che7 ti7 tai5-toh e5 sin-piN put-chi2 hoaN-hi2 , u7-e5 ki5-koai3 bo5 hoaN-hi2 .      
Ti7 ku7-ni5 12 goeh8 3 jit8 kau3 poaN3-mi5 kah i-e5 chhin-lang5 chu2-png7 hou7 i chiah8 ;  chiah8-pa2 kui7 teh ki5-to2 ,hah8-hah8 chhio3 ; khui3 tng7 , to3-khi3 thian-tong5 . 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 299 koan3, 1910 ni5 2 geh8)
