Kan3-cheng3 ngia5 Put8-kut e5 Pio2
Goan2 koe3--lai5 chit-peng5 thoan5 to7-li2 siong5-siong5 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2 si7 Goa7- kok-kau3 kap in bo5 ti7-tai7, u7-e5 ia7 me7 kong2 si7 hoan-a2-kau3, eng2 ai3 cho2-tong3 lang5 kah i m7-thang jip8.  Hit-ho7 e5 oe7 kiu3-chin si7 bo5 cheng5-li2, in-ui7 Siong7-te3 si7 thong ThiN-e7 e5 Siong7-te3, m7-si7 chit8-kok e5 Siong7-te3, hit-ho7 e5 oe7 lan2 m7-thang thiaN.  Iu7-koh hia e5 kong2 e5 lang5 ka-ti7 jiat8-sim teh pai3-put8 khi2-bio7, long2 be7- hiau2-siuN7 chit-e5 put8-kau3 ki5-sit8 ia7-si7 goa7-kok-kau3, kap in koh-khah bo5 kan- siap8. Chit-e5 oe7 m7-si7 kan-ta goan2 teh chham lin2 pian7, chiu7-si7 lin2 pun2-kok e5 thak8-chheh-lang5 ia7 che7-che7 lang5 chai, lin2 e5 chheh ia7 u7 ki3-chai3 kong2 Tiong- kok pun2-te2 si7 bo5 put8, kau3 Han3-tiau5 put8- kau3 chiah tui3 In3-tou7-kok lai5; kok-ong5 peh-seN3 siu7 i be5-hek8 khi3 than3, iau2-ku2 chiong5-tiong tauh8-tauh8 u7 bat-li2 e5 lang5 siuN7 teh-beh tu5. Kau3 Tong5-tiau5 Hian3- chong e5 Hong5-te3, si7 Chu2-au7 806-821, hit-si5 u7 chit8-ki kut he7 ti7 Hong7-siong5 e5 sou2-chai7, liu5-thoan5 kong2 si7 put8-kut, m7- chai i2-keng loa7-ku2 he7 ti7-hia. In-ui7 Tong5- Hian3-chong chun2 hiah-e5 hoe5-siuN7 ngia5- chih hit-ki kut jip8 Hong5-kiong lai7, u7 gau5 e5 jin5-sin5 seN3 Han5 mia5 Ju2 tiau5-ti5 cho3 chit-tiuN pio2 khi3 kan3-cheng3 i, chau3 Hong5-te3 tioh8 tu5-biat8 hit-e5 kut, bian2-tit hou7 peh-siN3 ti3-kau3 ku2-tng5 e5 ho7-hoan7. Kho2-sioh hit-e5 Hong5-te3 bo5-beh thiaN i, tian-to3 ka7 i pian2 khi3 cho3 Tio5-chiu-hu2. I ti7-hia cheng2-tun3 hong-siok8, kau3-hoa3 peh-siN3, beng5-bong7 put-chi2 hian2-iong5; tak8-lang5 chheng i cho3 Han5 Bun5-kong, iu7-koh khi2-bio7 khia7-pi, kau3 kin-a2-jit8 iau2-ku2 teh siau3-liam7 i-e5 kong-lo5, lai5 che3-hian3.    
Chit-tiuN-pio2 ki3-chai3 Kou2-bun5 te7 chap8- it pun2, goan2 chiong hit-nih8 lai5 ke2-soeh, Chit pio2--nih8 sou2-kong2--e5 bo5 tek-khak long2 e7 pin5-sin3--tit; put-ko3 si7 ai3 hou7 lin2 chai.    
Chit pio2--nih8 sou2-kong2 e5 sin5--ji7 si7 chi2 ti7 Han5 Bun5-kong pun2-sin; si7 jin5-sin5 e5 sin5--ji7; ke2-soeh cho3 "goa2" ia7 thang.    
Sin5 bou2-miaN5 kong2: Phah-sng3 chit-e5 put8 e5 su7, si7 goa7-kok chit8-e5 hoat-sut8 nia7-  
nia7, tui3 Hou7-han3 hit-si5 chiah jip8 Tiong- kok, khi2-thau5 boe7-bat u7.  Chai7-cha2 Ng5- te3 che7-ui7 kau3 pah-ni5, hoe3-siu7 chiah8 chit8- pah it-chap8 hoe3.  Siau3-ho7 che7-ui7 peh- chap8 ni5, hoe3-siu7 chiah8 chit8-pah hoe3. Chhoan-hiok che7-ui7 chhit-chap8-kau2 ni5, hoe3-siu7 chiah8 kau2-chap8-peh hoe3.  Kho3- te3 che7-ui7 chhit-chap8 ni5, hoe3-siu7 chiah8 pah-gou7-hoe3.  Giau5-te3 che7-ui7 kau2-chap8- peh ni5, hoe3-siu7 chiah8 chit8-pah chap8-peh hoe3.  Sun3-te3 chham Tai7-u2 hoe3-siu7 long2 kau3 pah-hoe3.  Hit-si5 thiN-e7 thai3-peng5, peh-seN3 peng5-an khoai3-lok8 tng5 hoe3-siu7; na7-si7 Tiong-kok iau2-boe7 bat u7 put8. Au7- lai5 Un-tiau5 e5 Thong-ong5 ia7 chiah8 kau3 pah hoe3; Thong e5 sun Thai3-mou7 che7-ui7 chhit-chap-gou7 ni5; Bu2-teng che7-ui7 gou7- chap8-kau2 ni5, cho3 su2-ki3--e5 bo5 kong2 in e5 hoe3-siu7 kau3 to2-ui7, phah-sng3 in e5 siu7-sou3 ia7 long2 u7 chiuN7 pah-hoe3. Chiu-tiau5 Bun5- ong5 e5 hoe3-siu7 kau2-chap8 chhit hoe3; Bu2- ong5 e5 hoe3-siu7 kau2-chap8-saN hoe3; Bok8- ong5 che7-ui7 chit8-pah ni5; chit-si5 put8-kau3 iau2-boe7 jip8 Tiong-kok, m7-si7 in-ui7 hok8- sai7 put8 chiaN3 e7 tit-tioh8 an2-ni-siN. 
Kau3 Han3-tiau5 Beng5-te3, hit-si5 taN-chiah u7 put8 e5 hoat-sut8; Beng5-te3 che7-ui7 chi2-u7 chap8-peh ni5 nia7-nia7; hit-tiap8 hoan2-loan7 si2-sit sio-soa3, kok-un7 bo5 ku2-tng5. Song3, Che5, Liong5, Tin5, Goan5, Gui7, i2-hou7 hok8- sai7 put8 chiam7-chiam7 khin5-kin2, hoe3-siu7 se3-tai7 na2-te2. Sui-jian5 Liong5 Bu2-te3 che7- ui7 si3-chap8-peh ni5; i cheng5-au7 saN-koe3 sia2-sin keng3-put8; che3-hian3 chong-biau7 bo5-eng7 seng-le2; chit8-jit8 chiah8 chit8-koe3, chi2-si7 chhai3-se koe2-chi2 nia7-nia7; liau2-au7 keng3-jian5 hou7 Hou5-keng2 sou2 pek, go7-si2 ti7 Tai5-siaN5, kok ia7 soa3 biat8-bo5; hok8-sai7 put8 kiu5 hok-khi3 hoan2-tng2 u7 ho7-hoan7. Chiau3 an2-ne khoaN3 ia7 chiu7 chai put8 e5 be7 kham--tit hou7 lang5 hok8-sai7.    
Tong5 Ko-chou2 taN-chiah chiap-tioh8 Sui5 e5 thiN-ui7, chiu7 gi7-lun7 teh-beh tu5 hiah- e5 put8; kho2-sioh tng-si5 e5 jin5-sin5 ti3-sek bo5 koan5, be7-e7 chhim chai sian-ong5 e5 to7-li2, kou2-kim e5 hap8-gi5, saN-kap chhian2- beng5 seng3-to7 eng7--lai5 kiu3 chit-ho7 pe3- toan, chiu7 chit-e5 tu5-put8 e5 tai7-chi3 soa3 pang3-soah--khi3.  Sin5 siong5-siong5 oan3-hin7, tok8-tok8 khim-giong2 seng3-jin5 bun5- bu2 choan5-chai5 e5 Hong5-te3 Ban7-soe3-ia5 li2 chi3-sin5 chi3-seng3 eng-hiong5 iong2-bu2, sou3 chheng pah-ni5 lai5 bo5 lang5 e7 pi2--tit, taN-chiah che7-ui7 chiu7 m7-chun2 In3-tou7 lang5 lai5 cho3 hoe5-siuN7 sai-kong, koh m7-chun2 siat bio7-u2, sin5 eng2-eng2 phah-sng3 Ko-chou2 e5 chi3-khi3 tek-khak tui3 Ban7-soe3-ia5 e5 chhiu2 lai5 cho3-chiaN5; hian7-kim chiu7-si7 be7-tit liam5-piN cho3-chiaN5, kiam2-thang pang3 hou7-i tian-to3 seng7--mah? 
TaN thiaN-kiN3 Ban7-soe3- ia5 hou7 chiah-e5 hoe5-siuN7 khi3 Hong7-siong5 ngia5-chih put8-kut, Gu7-ka3 chiuN7-lau5 lai5 khoaN3 in kng-jip8-khi3 lai7-tian7; iu7-koh kah chiah-e5 bio7-si7 sio-lun5-liu5 ngia5-chih, saN- kap khoan2-thai7--i; sin5 sui-jian5 kek8-gong7, tek-khak chai Ban7-soe3-ia5 be7-e7 hou7 put8 be5- hek8, ti3-kau3 pi7-pan7 chit-ho7 chun-chong5 hok8-sai7 lai5 ng3-bang7 hok-khi3; tit8-tit8 si7 phah-sng2, ho2 ni5-tang lang5 hoaN-hi2-sim, than3 peh-seN3 e5 i3-su3, the3 KiaN-siaN5 thak8- chheh lang5 i2-kip8 peh-seN3 siat ki5-koan, cho3 chhit-tho5 e5 khi3-khu7 nia7-nia7; kiam2 chin-chiaN3 u7 chhong-beng5 e5 jin5-kun kheng2 lai5 sin3 chit-ho7 e5 su7--mah?  Chong2-si7 peh- seN3 gu5-chhun2 khoai3 siu7 be5-hek8, oh--tit hiau2-li2; siat-su2 khoaN3-kiN3 Ban7-soe3-ia5 an2- ne, chiu7 liah8-cho3 li2 si7 chin-chiaN3 sit8-sim teh hok8-sai7 put8; long2 beh kong2 Hong5- te3 toa7-toa7 e5 seng3-jin5 siong7-chhiaN2 choan5- sim keng3-sin3, lan2 peh-seN3 si7 ha7-chian7 e5 lang5, m7 eng-kai pi2 i khah sioh siN3-mia7; kiaN-liau2 ti3-kau3 sio thau5-khak, sio chhiu2- chaiN2, chiaN5-pah kui-kun5 lai5 sio keng-i choa2-chiN5, tui3 cha2 kau3 am3 tai7-ke sa- khoaN3-iuN7; iu7 kiaN-liau2 au7-lai5 lau7 kap chiN2 phu5-phu5 io5-io5, pang3-sak pun2-hun7 e5 su7-giap8 kan-ki2.  Na7-m7 liam5-piN kim3-chi2 ngia5 put8 e5 lang5 tui3 hiah-e5 bio7-si7 khi3 un5, tek-khak u7 cham7-chhiu2 chho3-sin lai5 khoan2-thai7 iuN2-chhi7--i; che si7 siong- hong pai7-siok8 thoan5 kau3 si3-hng hou7-lang5 chhio3, m7-si7 sio2-khoa2 e5 su7.  Lun7-kau3 put8 pun2 si7 goa7-kok e5 lang5, chham Tiong- kok gian5-gu2 bo5 kang7, i-chiuN5 koh-iuN7, chhui3 bo5 kong2 sian-ong5 e5 hoat-tou7 e5 oe7, sin bo5 chheng7 sian-ong5 hoat tou7 e5 hok8, ia7 m7-chai kun-sin5 e5 li2-gi7, ia7 m7-bat pe7- kiaN2 e5 in-cheng5.  Siat-su2 i-e5 seng-khu kau3-taN iau2 ti7-teh, hong7 kok-ka e5 beng7 lai5 kian3-tiau5, Ban7-soe3-ia5 na7-beh iong5- un2 chiap-lap8--i, put-ko3 soan-leng7 chit8- si5 hou7 i kiN3, kiaN5 chu2-pin e5 le2, siuN2-su3 i- chiuN5 chit8-tho3; kiN3-liau2 chhoa7 i chhut- khi3 keng2-goa7, boh8-tit hou7 i tiam3 keng2- lai7 be5-hek8 peh-seN3, kho2-i2; hong3-kiam i seng-khu si2 i2-keng ku2, kou-ta5 e5 kut, u3- oe3 e5 khi3, kiam2 eng-kai hou7 i jip8-lai5 Hong5-kiong-lai7?  Khong2-chu2 kong2, Keng3 kui2-sin5 tioh8 li7-khui--i.  Kou2-cha2 e5 Chu-hou5, na7 beh khi3 kok--nih8 tiau3-song, siong7-chhiaN2 tioh8 kah sai-kong ma2-i5 tai7-seng eng7 tho5-ki sau3-tu5 soe-sap-khi3, jian5-au7 chiaN3 kaN2 khi3 tiau3-song, taN, Ban7- soe3-ia5, li2 bo5 ian5-kou3 chhu2 u3-oe3 e5 kut-hai5, chhin-sin lim5-kau3 khi3 khoaN3, ia7 bo5 eng7 sai-kong, ia7 bo5 eng7 tho5-ki, chiah e5 jin5- sin5 bo5 kong2-khi2 m7-tioh8, Gu7-su2 ia7-bo5 kong2-khi2 sit-chho3, sin5 sit8-chai7 kian3-siau3. Khun2-kiu5 chiong chit-e5 kut hoat-loh8 hou7 iu2-si-koaN tan3--loh8-khi3 chui2--nih8 hoe2--nih8, eng2-oan2 biat8-choat8 ho7-kin, choat8- tng7 thiN-e7 giau5-gi5, choat8-tng7 au7-tai7 e5 be5-hek8, hou7 thiN-e7 e5 lang5 chai toa7 seng3- jin5 e5 sou2 cho3 iaN5-koe3 sim-siong5 ban7-poe7, kiam2-m7 heng-ong7 khoaN3-oah8--mah?  Put8 na7-si7 u7 leng5-siaN3, e7 chok-chong7, e7 khian3-chek, eng-kai chek-hoat8 sin5 e5 seng-khu, ThiN-kong teh kam3-chhat, sin5 m7 oan3-hin7, m7 hoan2-hoe2.     
TaN ng3-bang7 Ban7-soe3-ia5 boh8-tit ku3- chai7 sin5 pek-chhiat khun2-kiu5 kau3-kek8. Kin2-sin7 pai3-pio2 hou7 Ban7-soe3-ia5 khoaN3; sin5 chin-chiaN3 kiaN chin-chiaN3 ui3.
