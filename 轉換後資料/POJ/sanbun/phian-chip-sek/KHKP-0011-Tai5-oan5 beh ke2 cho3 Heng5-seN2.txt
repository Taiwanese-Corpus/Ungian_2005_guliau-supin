
Tai5-oan5 beh ke2 cho3 Heng5-seN2
Lan2 chit Tai-oan5 e5 te7-hng hian7-si5 KiaN-siaN5 put-chi2 tui3-tiong7 , so2-i2 gi7-lun7 beh siat-cho3 Heng5-seN2 ; i2-keng kiaN5 bun5-su hou7 To7-tai5 , To7-tai5 ia7 u7 chhut ko3-si7 thong-ti hang5-kau peh-siN3 kong2 , Si7 in-ui7 beh siat Heng5-seN2 sou2-hui3 bo5-kau3 ,kou-put-chiong tioh8 chiu7 chhut kang2-khau2 e5 hoe3-mih8 ke thiu-hun , khioh-lan5 chu7-chong2 , pang-chan7 sou2-hui3 .  Ia u7 hun-hoat Ui2-oan5 ti7 tak8 sou2-chai7 , kah lang5 tui3 chit si3-goeh8 chhe-it-jit8 khi2 ,na7 u7 beh chai3-hoe3 chhut-khau2 tioh8 po3 Ui2-oan5 chai , hou7 i cha-giam7 , soe3-li5 , chiah nia2-toaN khi3 kiaN5 .  Kau3 chit 4-goeh8 20-jit8 To7-tai5 u7 koh chhut ko3-si7 kong2-beng5 , cheng5-tiuN e5 ko3-si7 si7 lang5 khoaN3 bo5 beng5-pek8 , ti3-kau3 iau5-gian5 hui2-pong3 ; sit8-chai7 bo5-iaN2 long-chong2 beh thiu ; lun7-kau3 chhia chai3 , lang5 taN, i2-kip8 lai7-kang2 chun5-chiah lai5-ong2 , long bo5 beh thiu , na7-si7 jip8-kang2 e5 hoe3-mih8 chiau3-ku7 teh thiu ;tok8-tok8 u7 sin-siat chit-e5 hoat-tou7 , chiu7-si7 chhut-kang2 e5 hoe3-mih8 tiaN-tioh8 chit8-pah-hun beh thiu 5 li5 .  Chong2-si7 u7 lang5 teh kong2 , in-ui7 SaN-kau m7 kam-goan7 hou7 i thiu ,chit-e5 hoat-tou7 kiam2-chhai2 siat be7-loh8 .
