
Ou-bin7 Gin2-na2 Tiong-tit8-sim
(Hoan-ek8)
 Ti7 bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 pai2 ti7 Phin2-pheng5-hoe7 e5 si5, u7 nng7 chiah soe3-chiah             be2, kau-kek8 sui2. Kou3 be2 e5 lang5 si7 A-hui-li7-ka e5 ou-bin7 ta-pou-gin2-na2, mia5 kio3             Cato(Khe-to). In teh kiaN5 ti7 phau2-be2-tiuN5 e5 si5, hiah e5 be2 chin gau5 thiaN-than3 hit e5 ou-bin7 gin2-na2 e5 chhui3. Hioh-khun3 e5 si5, be2 eng7 in e5 thau5-khak lui5 Cato e5 sin-khu, chhin-chhiuN7 chin gau5 thiaN3 Cato e5 khoan2-sit. Hit-tiap u7 chit8 e5 ai3 boe2 be2 e5 lang5, mng7 ou-bin7 gin2-na2 kong2, “Li2 ai3 boe7 loa7-choe7 chiN5?” Cato kong2, “1000 khou!”  Hit e5 lang5 kong2, “Goa7! hiah kui3.  Goa2 hou7 li2 600 khou, ho2-m7?” Cato io5-thau5 m7-kheng2. Hit-si5 chin-choe7 lang5 lai5 teh khoaN3 be2, chong2-si7 bo5 lang5 ai3 boe2.  Hit e5 ai3 boe2 e5 lang5 koh lai5 kong2, “Goa2 ka7 li2 kong2, chin kui3 houhN, bo5 lang5 ai3 boe2.  Chit nng7 chiah be2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 e5? I tek-khak hoaN-hi2 boe7 goa2 600 khou.”  Kou3 be2 e5 gin2-na2 kong2, “Si7 goa2 e5 sio2-chia2 e5, na7 bo5 1000 khou i m7-boe7. Hit e5 lang5 chiu7 kong2, “TaN, goa2 ka7 li2 kong2: li2 tioh8 ka7 i an2-ni kong2, u7 chit8 chiah hut-jian5-kan pai2-kha. Li2 na7 an2-ni kong2, goa2 beh hou7 li2 pun2-sin 50 khou. Li2 boe7 goa2 600 khou chaiN2-iuN7 m7? Phah-sng3 li2 tui3 chhut-si3 kaN2 iau2 m7-bat u7 50 khou, u7 a2-bo5?” Cato khia7 ti7 be2 e5 sin-piN,thiaN hit e5 lang5 kong2 an2-ni, hut-jian5 gong5-ngiah8, ti3-kau3 be2 ia7 gong5-ngiah8. I chiu7 ng3-bin7 khoaN3 hit e5 lang5, kong2, “Li2 phah-sng3, in-ui7 Chu2 hou7 goa2 e5 phe5 ou, I soa3 hou7 goa2 e5 sim ou mah? Bo5 lah! Goa2 koat-toan3 m7-than3 li2 e5 oe7, hou7 goa2 e5 sim bak-tioh8 ou.” Ti7 piN-a2 khia7 u7 chit8 e5 ho2-le2 e5 lang5 chiu7 mng7 Cato, “Li e5 sio2-chia2 chaiN2-iuN7 ai3 boe7 i e5 be2?” Cato chiu7 in3 kong2, “Goan2 khiam3-eng7 1000 khou. Kau3 au7 le2-pai3 na7 bo5 tit-tioh8 1000 khou, chhan5-hng5 tioh8 boe7. Goa2 e5 thau5-ke khiam3-eng7 1000 khou na7-tiaN7, soa3 tu2-tioh8 phoa3-piN7. Koh chit8 hang7, goan2 e5 chhek-chhng u7 hou7 he2 sio-lio khi3, sou2-i2 kou-put-chiong goan2 sio2-chia2 tioh8 boe7 i e5 be2.”  Hit e5 ho2-le2 e5 lang5 chiu7 kong2, “TaN li2 an-sim to3-khi3, ka7 li2 e5 sio2-chia2 kong2, “Be2 boe7 liau2 lah!” Goa2 beh hou7 i 1000 khou.  Goa2 e5 lou5-pok8 ti7-chia, i beh theh8 chiN5 hou7 i, ia7 li2 tioh8 ka7 i kong2, Goa2 beh khi3 Au-chiu chit8 ni5 ku2. Goa2 bo5 ti7-teh e5 si5, li2 e5  
sio2-chia2 thang eng7 chiah e5 be2, kau3 to3-tng2 lai5 e5 si5, i na7 ai3 koh boe2 to3-tng2, ia7 hou2, goa2 hoaN-hi2 boe7 i khah siok8.     
Cato kong2: “Pat8-jit8, goa2 na7 u7 ki-hoe7, goa2 hoaN-hi2 choe3 li2 e5 chhe-eng7.”  Hit e5 lang5 kong2, “Cato, li2 i2-keng u7 pang-chan7 goa2.” Cato kong2, chaiN2-iuN7? Goa2 m7-bat li, tui3 tah-loh8 i2-keng pang-chan7 li2? In3 kong2, U7-iaN2. Si7 in-ui7 li2 hou7 goa2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang pang-chan7 pat8-lang5. Li2 tu2-a2 m7-kheng2 siu7 hit e5 boe2 be2 e5 lang5 e5 au7-chhiu2-chiN5, m7-kheng2 hou7 li2 e5 sim bak-tioh8 ou. Tui3 li2 sou2 kong2 tiong-tit8 e5 oe7, i2-keng u7 pang-chan7 goa2.”  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 470 koan3,1924 ni5 5 geh8)
