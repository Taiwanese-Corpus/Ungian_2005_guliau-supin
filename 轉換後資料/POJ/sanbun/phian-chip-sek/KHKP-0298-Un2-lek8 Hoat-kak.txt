
 
Un2-lek8 Hoat-kak
Ku7-ni5 6 goeh8 chhi7 lai7 Sam-kai3-toaN5 Chong2-tok siong7 cheng5 u7 kut8-tioh8 kim- tiau5.  Hit-si5 in-ui7 hiah-e5 cho3 kang--e5 u7 pun si3-soaN3 , cha2 u7 cha-chhut 21 tiau5 na7- tiaN7.  Kin7-jit8 u7 koh hoat-kak 6 tiau5, kap cheng5 hit 21 tiau5 , ke3-kiong7 tat8 kim 13500 khou.  TaN chiong sou2 cha-chhut e5 su7-iu5 ki3 ti7 e7-te2:--       
Kin7-jit8 u7 sun5-cha-pou2 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 teh hoe7 kia3 kim-tiau5 ti7 peng5-iu2 e5 chhu3 u7 put peng5 e5 su7, chiu7 khi3 po3-ko3 Keng2-bu7-kho3.  U7 chit8 e5 lang5 lai5 toa3 ti7 Sam- kai3-toaN5 bi2-tiam3--nih8, thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kut8-tioh8 kim-tiau5, i ia7 khi3 chhiuN2 7 tiau5 ,  kia3 4 tiau5 ti7 i e5 peng5-iu2 tau, ia7 leng7-goa7 chiong chit8 tiau5 hou7 i cho3 sia7-le2.  Chit e5 peng5-iu2 si7 PheN5-ou5 lang5, jian5-au7  tng2-khi3 PheN5-ou5.      
Keng2-bu7-kho3 u7 phai3 lang5 khi3 PheN5-ou5 cha-chhat soa3 tiau3 hit e5 lang5 lai5 mng7  kong2 , Cheng5 bou2 lang5 kia3 li2 si3 tiau5 kim-tiau5 u7 ti7-teh bo5 ? I in3 kong2 , Si3-te3 u7 heng5 i , put-ko3 i u7 chiong chit8 te3 hou7 goa2 cho3 sia7-le2.  I chiu7 theh8 hit tiau5 chhut lai5 hian3.  Siok8-ji5-hou7 chiu7 khi3 kia3 i hit lang5 e5 chhu3 cha mng7. Hit lang5 si7 toa3 ti7 Siau-lang5 po2 , Toa7-tham5 chng.  Cheng3 i2-keng u7 lai5 hian3 nng7 tiau5. Keng2-bu7-kho3 u7 khi3 kio3 hit e5 PheN5-ou5 lang5 lai5 tui3-chi2 , chong2--si7 i m7-jin7 kong2 , Sou2 kia3 e5 u7 heng5 i , nge7-nge7 cheN3 bo5.  M7-ku2 Keng2-pou7 sin3 hit e5 PheN5-ou5 lang5 heng5 i liau2 e5 oe7 si7 sit8.      
Au7-lai5 pat8 e5 Keng2-pou7 kap chit8 e5 sun5-cha-pou2 khi3 Siau-lang5-po2 hit e5 lang5 in  tau am3 cha.  I u7 ke2-cho3 pun2-to2 lang5. In khi3 chiu7 kap i oe7 tang oe7 sai , soa3  kong2 tui3 kim-tiau5 e5 su7 khi3.  Hit e5 lang5 m7 chai i si7 Keng2-pou7 beh lai5 am3-hong2  chiu7 kap i nauh-nauh kong2 , soa3 lau3-khau2 kong2 i u7 khng3 kim-tiau5 nng7 tiau5.  Keng2-pou7 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, Goa2 si7 tai7-jin5 , li2 tioh8  ka7 goa2 kong2 khoaN3 khng3 ti7 to2-ui7.  I in3 kong2 , Ti7 chhu3-lai7-nih8.  Ti7 pun2 goeh8 8 jit8 chiu7 chhe lang5 khi3 kut8 , kut8 long2 bo5 , chai i peh8-chhat8.  Au7--lai5 u7 kio3 i khi3 giam5-giam5 hun3-ju7, i chiu7  
kong2 , Si7 khng3 ti7 i chhu3-lai7 e5 tek-thiau7 lai7 ; khi3 chhoe7  ko2-jian5 u7 2 tiau5 ti7-hia.  Keng2-pou7 khoaN3 i e5 heng5-chek , phah-sng3 iau2 u7 ,chiu7 koh se3 cha. Kin7-jit8 u7 tit-tioh8 i 2 tiau5 khi3 kia3 ti7 peng5-iu2 e5 chhu3.  Che si7 tui3 i u7 cha-chhut chit 4 tiau5.      
Iu7-koh chhi7-lai7 u7 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5.  Tit-tioh8 chit8 tiau5 khi3 kia3 ti7 Cho3-bih8-ke  lang5 tau.  Kin7-jit8 ia7 u7 hoat-kak.  An2-ne ke3-kiong7 u7 6 tiau5 , kap cheng5 si7 ke3-  kiong7 27 tiau5.  Mui2-tiau5 u7 9 niu2 8 chiN5 tang7.  Si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hiuN-lou5 e5 khoan2.  
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 298 koan3, 1910 ni5 1 geh8)
