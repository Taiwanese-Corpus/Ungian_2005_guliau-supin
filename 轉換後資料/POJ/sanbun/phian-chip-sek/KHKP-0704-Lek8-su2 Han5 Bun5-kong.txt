
Lek8-su2 Han5 Bun5-kong e5 佛骨表
Ou5 Bun5-ti5
Han5 Bun5-kong e5 mia5 Han5 Ju2 (韓 愈)  (A. D. 768-823) si7 Tong5-tiau5 e5 toa7 hak8-chia2, ia7 si7 bun5-chiuN ka.  Chu7 se3 chhong-beng5, toan-cheng3, ai3 thak8-chheh.   Bo5 kiaN koan5-se3, ui7-tioh8 gi7 chin iong2-kam2, chong2-si7 ia7 be7 khoaN3-khin lang5.  
Hut8-kau3 ti7 Hou7-han3 e5 si5 thoan5-jip8 Tiong-kok.  Ti7 法翔 (Hoat-siong5) e5 Hoat-bun5-si7 u7 thah (護國真身塔), thah-lai7 u7 Sek-kia e5 chng2-thau5-a2 kut chit8 chat, chit e5 thah 30 ni5 u7 khui chit8 pai2.  Hit-si5 e5 hong5-te3 Hian3-chong, chhe lang5 khi3 gia5 chit e5 hut8-kut lai5 kiong-lai7 khng3 3 jit8.  Han5 Bun5-kong hit-si5 cho3 刑部侍郎 e5 koaN.  KhoaN3 hong5-te3 sou2 cho3 e7 hai7 lan2 Tiong-kok, sou2-i2 ho2-taN2 cho3 chau3-chiuN kan-cheng3.  Au7-lai5 ko2-jian5 jia2 hong5-te3  e5 siu7-khi3, siu7 pian2 khi3 cho3 Tio7 chiu7 chhi3-su2, hit-si5 si7 819 ni5 chiaN-goeh8, koe3 chin-che7 kan-khou2, koe3 Chin5-leng2 e5 sng-seh, sou2 cho3 e5 si u7 ti7 千家詩 lan2 long2 chai.   TaN chiong i e5 Hut8-kut-pio2 lai5 kai3-siau7 hou7 tai7-ke chai.  
(Chit ui7 Sian-siN ai3 chiong Hut8-kut- pio2, eng7 Han5-bun5, in3 chiuN7 po3; kho2- sioh pun2 kiok8, Han3-bun5 bo5 oan5-choan5, sou2-i2 goa2 hoan-ek8 peh8-oe7-ji7 in3 ti7 chia, lai5 piau2-beng5 hoan-geng5 sian-siN oan7-chou7.  Kong-po3 e5 ho2-i3.  Koh ui7-tioh8 choa2-bin7 iu2-han7, be7 thang hoan-ek8 siuN tng5, chi2- u7 chit8-pou7-hun7 na7-tiaN7).  
Chiau3 sin5 sou2 siuN7, hok8-sai7 Put8, si7 hoan-a2 e5 hoat-tou7.  Tui3 Hou7-han3 e5 si5, Put8 e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7 thoan5 jip8 Tiong-kok, chong2-si7 ti7 kou2-cha2 lan2 chia, long2 bo5 chit-ho7.  Cha2 ni5, Ng5-te3 che7 u7 chit8-pah ni5, chiah8 110 hoe3; Siau3-ho7 che7 ui7 peh-chap8 ni5, chiah8 100 hoe3; Chhoan-hiok che7 ui7 chhit-chap8-kau2 ni5, chiah8 98 hoe3; Te3 Khok che7 ui7 chhit-chap8 ni5, chiah8 105 hoe3; Te3 Giau5 che7 ui7 kau2-chap8-peh ni5, chiah8 118 hoe3; Te3 Sun3 kap U2, long2 chiah8 kang2 pah hoe3, chit-si5 thiN-e7 thai3- peng5, peh-siN3 an-lok8 tng5 hoe3-siu7, hit- tiap8 Tiong-kok long2 bo5 Put8.  
 
Au7-lai5 Un-tiau5 Thong-ong5 ia7 chiah8 100 hoe3.  Thong e5 sun, Thai3-bou7 che7- chhit-chap8-gou7 ni5, Bu2-teng che7 ui7 gou7- chap8-kau2 ni5, Su2-ki3 bo5 kong2 chiah8 loa7- che7 hoe3, chhui-chhek in e5 ni5 e5 siau3-giah8, ia7 si7 long2 bo5 kiam2-chio2 100 hoe3.  Chiu Bun5-ong5 chiah8 97 hoe3, Bu2-ong5 chiah8 93 hoe3, Bok8-ong5 che7 ui7 chit8-pah ni5.  Chit- si5 Put8 e5 hoat-tou7 iau2-boe7 thoan5-jip8 Tiong-kok, teng2-bin7 hiah-e5, m7-si7 in-ui7 hok8-sai7 Put8 chiah ti3-kau3 an2-ni.  
Han3 Beng5-te3 e5 si5, chiah u7 hok8-sai7 Put8 e5 hoat-tou7.  Beng5-te3 che7 ui7, tu2-a2 chap8-peh ni5, au7-lai5 jiau2-loan7 si2-sit sio-soa3, kok-ui7 bo5 ku2-tng5.  Song3, Che5, Liong5, Tin5, Goan5, Gui7, chiah e5 tiau5-te7 i2-au7, lang5 hok8-sai7 Put8 chiam7-chiam7 kin2-sin7, ni5-tai7 koh-khah te2.  Chi2-u7 Liong5 Bu2-te3 che7 ui7 48 ni5, cheng5 au7 3 pai2, pang3-sak seng-khu, hian3 hou7 Put8.  Chong-bio7 e5 che3-su7 chiu7 bo5 eng7 gu5 iuN5, jit8-si5 chiah8 chit8-tng3, chi2-u7 si7 chhai3 kap koe2-chi2, au7- lai5 soa3 hou7 Hou7-keng3 sou2 koaN-tiok8, go7-si2 ti7 Tai5-siaN5 (si7 tng-si5 e5 kiong-siaN5), kok ia7 biat8-bo5.  Hok8-sai7 Put8 si7 beh kiu5 hok- khi3, soa3 tu2-tioh8 chai-ho7.  Tui3 an2-ni lai5 khoaN3, chiu7 chai Put8, m7-thang hok8-sai7.  
Kin-a2-jit8 lan2 Tiong-kok e7 siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 thian-chai, jin5-chai che7-che7 e5 chhi-chham2, siat-su2 na7 Han5 Bun5-kong iau2 ti7-teh, i kiam2 m7-si7 e7 chek-pi7 Tiong- kok lang5 teh pai3 Put8 mah?  
“Chong2-si7 goa2 bat chhe goa2 e5 cheng3 lou5-pok8, sian-ti chiu7-kin7 lin2; si7 cha2-cha2 khi2-lai5 chhe i, kong2, Chhiat-chhiat goan7-lin2 m7-thang kiaN5 chit e5 kho2-ouN3 e5 su7, goa2 sou2 oan3-hin7 e5.  Tok8-tok8 in bo5 thiaN, iah8 bo5 aN3 hi7-khang, bo5 oat8-tng2 li7-khui in e5 phaiN2, iu5-goan5 sio hiuN ti7 pat8-ho7 e5 siong7- te3.  In-ui7 an2-ni, Goa2 e5 toa7 siu7-khi3 piaN3- chhut ti7 Iu5-tai7 chiah e5 siaN5” (Ia5-li7-bi2 44:4). 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 704 ho7, 1947 ni5 8 geh8)
