
Kian3-hak8 Te7-kiu5 e5 su7, li2 long2 bat bo5?
Long2 si7 bo5; tai7-liok8 si7 u7.  Goa2 lai5 ke kong2 chit8-sut-a2, chhiaN2 bong2 thiaN.   Te7-kiu5 tou5-bin7, u7 oe7 chit8-choa7 chit8-choa7, u7 than2-tit8 choa7, ia7 u7 than2-hoaiN5--e5.  Che ho7-cho3 tou7.  Choan5 kiu5-bin7 u7 360 choa7, chiu7-si7 360 tou7.  Tui3 lam5 kau3 pak e5 choa7, ho7-cho3 keng-soaN3; tui3 tang kau3 sai, ho7-cho3 hui7-soaN3.  Hui7-soaN3 chiuN7 pak, a2-si7 loh8 lam5, si7 tui3 chhek-to7 sng3 khi2.  Keng-soaN3 chhut sai, a2-si7 jip8 tang, si7 tui3 Gut-lin5-ui2-chhi3 e5 chu2-ngou2 soaN3 sng3 khi2  (Chit-tiau5 chu2-ngou2 soaN3 e5 ki-tiam2, si7 ti7 Lun5-tun kau-goa7 Thim-su ho5-piN e5 kang2, hia u7 Gut-lin5-ui2-chhi3 e5 thian-bun5-tai5).   Tui3 hia sng3 jip8 tang, tang-keng 120 tou7, chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5 e5 ui7-ti3.  Lang5 beh hun- koah thou2-te7, a2-si7 thian-bun5 su7, a2-si7 sai2 chun5 e5 lang5 long2 tui3-tiong7 chit-ho7 tou7-sou3.  
Hak8-seng teh mng7, Tai5-oan5 teng2-pou 9 tiam2, ia7 ti7 Jit8-pun2 teng2-pou kui2 tiam2?   Lang5 m7-bian2 phah tian7-po3 khi3 mng7, na7 khoaN3 te7-kiu5 tou5 e5 tou7 sou3 lai5 sng3 chiu7 chai.   Jit8-thau5 e5 kng, chit8 tiam2 cheng, chin3-heng5 15 tou7.  Jit8-pun2 ui7-ti3, ti7 tang-keng 135 tou7, Tai5-oan5 si7 tang-keng 120, an2-ni saN-li7 15 tou7, si7 chha chit8 tiam2.  Lan2 chia 9 tiam2, in hia 9 tiam2 koe3 liau2, si7 teng2-pou 10 tiam2.  Koh mng7 In3-tou7 teng2-pou kui2 tiam2?  In3-tou7 si7 ti7 tang-keng 100 tou7, li7 Tai5-oan5 20 tou7, 20 tou7 eng7 15 tou7 lai5 pun, tit-tioh8 chit8 tiam2 20 hun.  Lan2 teng2-pou 9 tiam2, in iau2-boe7 9 tiam2, sou2-i2 tioh8 tui3 lan2 e5 9 tiam2 khau3-tu5 chit8 tiam2 20 hun, chiu7 chai In3-tou7 si7 cha2-khi2 7 tiam2 40 hun.  Eng7 an2-ni lai5 chhui-sng3 thiN-e7 sim2-mih8 kok, hian7-chai7 si7 sim2-mih8 si5, lan2 long2 e7 chai.  
Te7-kiu5 u7 loa7 toa7?  Lang5 seh8 te7-kiu5 chit8-lin3, u7 7 ban7 goa7 li2.  Kap goeh8 sio- pi2, goeh8 tioh8 40 liap8 chiah u7 chit8 liap8 te7-kiu5.  Tai5-oan5 liah8 tit8 sio-chiap, tioh8 75 e5 Tai5-oan5 to2, chiah e7 thang tui3 te7-kiu5 bin7 e5 tiong-ng, khoan5 chit8-lian3.  
Te7-kiu5 chin7 pak e5 sou2-chai7, ho7 cho3 Pak-kek8; chin7 lam5 e5 sou2-chai7 ho7 cho3 Lam5-kek8, hit nng7 sou2-chai7 kek8 koaN5, thong-ni5 kian peng.  Ti7 hia, chit8 ni5 6 goeh8 jit8 jit8-si5, 6 goeh8 jit8 me5-si5.  Pak-kek8 na7 me5-si5, Lam5-kek8 chiu7 jit8-si5, chiau3 an2-ni lun5-liu5.  ChiaN3 Pak-kek8, ChiaN3 Lam5-kek8, bo5 lang5 toa3 hia.  
Tui3 hai2 lai5 khoaN3, u7 pun cho3 5 e5 iuN5.  Chiu7-si7 Thai3-peng5-iuN5, Toa7-sai-iuN5, In3- tou7-iuN5, Pak-peng5-iuN5, kap Lam5-peng5-iuN5.  
Tui3 liok8-te7 lai5 khoaN3, u7 pun cho3 6 toa7 chiu, chiu7-si7 A-se-a chiu, Au-lo5-pa chiu, A-hui-li7-ka chiu, O3-tai7-li7-a chiu, Pak A-bi2-li7-ka chiu kap Lam5 A-bi2-li7-ka chiu.  
Tui3 khi3-hau7 lai5 khoaN3, u7 joah8 e5 te7-hng, un-ho5 e5 te7-hng, koaN5 e5 te7-hng, hun cho3 5 toa7 khu-hek8.  Chiu7-si7 Jiat8-tai3, Pak Un-tai3, Lam5 Un-tai3, Pak Han5-tai3 kap Lam5 Han5-tai3.  
Choan5 se3-kai3 e5 lang5, iok u7 20 ek; Tai5-oan5 chiah u7 chhit-pah ban7, an2-ni lai5 sio-pi2, u7 285 poe7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 che7.  In na7 kong2 lai5 li2 e5 chhu3, ka7 li2 tun3 chhiu2 chheng2-an, chit8 lang5 tun3 chit8-e7, chit8 jit8 tun3 e7 liau2 be7, li2 e5 chhiu2 kau3 boe2 sim2-mih8  khoan2?  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 707 ho7, 1947 ni5 11 geh8)
