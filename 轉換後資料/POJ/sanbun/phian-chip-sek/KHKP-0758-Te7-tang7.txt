
Te7-tang7

Tui3 10 goeh8 22 ho7 e5 te7-tang7 i2-lai5, Tang5[Tang]-pou7 kau3 kin-a2-jit8 tak8-jit8 teh te7-tang7, hia e5 lang5 long2 sou-khai ti7 khang3-te7 tah chhau2-liau5 teh koe3-jit8, chin thang tong5-cheng5.
TaN ai3 kai3-siau7 koan-he7 te7-tang7 e5 siong5-sek.
1.	Te7-tang7 e5 goan5-in.
Tai7-khai3 u7 4 khoan2 e5 goan5-in: — 
1)	Hoe2-soaN-seng3 e5 te7-tang7, si7 tui3 hoe2-soaN pok8-hoat sou2 seN-khi2 e5 te7-tang7.  Chit khoan2 e5 te7-tang7 khah be7 eng2-hiong2 kau3 chin hng7, chong2-si7 in-ui7 sou2 chheng3-chhut e5 hoe2-hu kap kun2 chhiang5-chhiang5 e5 thou5-moe5, hu7-kin7 siu7 chai-hai7 chin thiam2, chhin-chhiuN7 A. D. 79 ni5, Be-su-bia hoe2-soaN (Vesuvius) pok8-hoat, ti3-kau3 Pong2-pe (Pompeii) siaN5 long2 tai5-loh8-khi3.
2)	Te7 lap-loh8-khi3 e5 te7-tang7.  Te7-khak-lai7 chioh8-hoe-seng3 e5 toa7-chioh8, hou7 te7-e7 e5 chui2 iuN5-khi3, seN-khi2 khang-tong7, te7-khak lap loh8-khi3, sou2 seN-khi2 e5 te7-tang7.  Chit khoan2 te7-tang7, ia7 eng2-hiong2 bo5 hng7, Kong-hok8-au7 Eng2-khong te7-hng e5 te7-tang7 kaN2 si7 chit ho7.
3)	Thou5-soa i5-tong7 seN-khi2 e5 te7-tang7.  Goan5-lai5 kou-seng5 hai2-te2 e5 but8-chit khah tang7, kou[kou3]-seng5 liok8-te7 e5 but8-chit khah khin, ia7 ho5-chui2 put-si5 teh lau5 thou5-soa loh8-hai2, tui3 an2-ni ho5-khau2 chiam7-chiam7 chek-soa, chiu7 te7-poaN5 e5 tang7 bo5 peng5-kin, ti3-kau3 seN-khi2 te7-poaN5 e5 pian3-‘ tong7, chiu7 seN-khi2 te7-tang7.  Chit khoan2 e5 te7-tang7 khah-siong5 si7 ti7 khe, a2-si7 ho5 e5 kin7-tau, ia7 eng2-hiong2 khah hng7, chit pai2 tang-pou7 e5 te7-tang7 kiam2-chhai2 si7 chit khoan2.
4)	Tai7-liok8 i5-tong7 sou2 seN-khi2 e5 te7-tang7.
2.	Te7-tang7 e5 theng5-tou7.
Tai7-kai3[khai3] hun-cho3 6 kip.
It kip: Tek8-piat8 chu3-i3 e5 lang5 chiah u7 kam2-kak e5 theng5-tou7.
Ji7 kip: It-poaN e5 lang5 long2 e7 kam2-kak mng5-thang sio2-khoa2 tin2-tang7 e5 theng5-tou7.
SaN kip: Chhu3 chin toa7 io5, mng5-thang ho7-siN3 io5 kau3 chhut-siaN tiau3-teh e5 mih8 long2 toa7 io5, khi3-khu7-lai7 e5 chui2 ia7 tin2-tang7 e5 theng5-tou7.
Si3 kip: Chhu3 toa7-toa7 io5, khi3-khu7-lai7 e5 chui2 e7 piaN3 chhut-khi3, si5-cheng e7 tiam7 khi3 e5 theng5-tou7.
Gou7 kip: Chhu3, piah e7 lih8-hun5, chhiuN5, ian-tang5 e7 to2 e5 theng5-tou7.
Lak8 kip: Chhu3 to2, soaN-pang, te7-lih8 e5 theng5-tou7.
Na2 chhin-chhiuN7 te7 gia5 phu5 khi2-lai5 e5 khoan2, si7 chui2-goan5 chin kin7, chiap8-chiap8 teh gia5 koan5, gia5 ke7 e5 tang7, si7 teh beh u7 toa7 te7-tang7 lai5, tioh8 kin2 chau2, na7-si7 un5-un5-a2 io5 e5 te7-tang7, chin2-goan5 khah hng7, sou2-i2 khah bo5 iau3-kin2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 758 koan3; 1952 ni5 2 goeh8)
