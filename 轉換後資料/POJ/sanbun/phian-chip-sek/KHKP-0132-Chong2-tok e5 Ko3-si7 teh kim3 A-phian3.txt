
Chong2-tok e5 Ko3-si7 teh kim3 A-phian3
Tai5-oan5 Chong2-tok Hai2-kun Tai7-Chiong3 Pek-chiok Hoa7-san.  
In-ui7 chhut ko3-si7 thong-ti lang5 chaie5 su7 ; chiau3-tit taN an3 Tai7 Jit8-pun2 Te3-kok kap Goa7-iuN5 tak8-kok sou2 gi7 hian7 teng7 tiau5-iok, pun2 hai2-su7 ia7 tioh8 chiau3 an2-nipan7. Chiong-lai5 Tai5-oan5 sou2 u7 chin3 jip8 hai2-khau2 e5 A-phian3-thou5, eng-kai chiau3 tiau5-iok it-khai3 giam5 kin3. Chong2-si7 A-phian3 koan3-si7 eng7-kau3 chiah che7 ni5 kub2e-kham-tit cho3-chit8-si5 choat8-tng7, kiaN-liau2 u7 koan-gai7 lang5 e5 seN3-mia7; tui3 taN liau2-au7 it-khai3 A-phian3-thou5 long2 kui koaN-hu2, tioh8 theng3-hau7 chim-chiok tiaN7-tioh8 e5 chiong-theng5, chun2 in teng2 the3 ioh8 cho3 lou7-eng7. Hap8-giap8 thong-ti hou7 lang5 chai, ui7-tioh8 che ko3-si7 ng3-bang7 lin2 peh-seN3 cheng3-lang5 it-the2 long2 chai, tak8-lang5 tioh8 chun-than3. Tiau5-ti5 chi2-si7.  
Beng5-ti7 29 ni5 2 goeh8 24 jit8.
