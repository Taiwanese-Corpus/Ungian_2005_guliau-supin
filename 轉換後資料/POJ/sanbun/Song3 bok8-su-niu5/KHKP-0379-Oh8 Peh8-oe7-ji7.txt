
 
Oh8 Peh8-oe7-ji7
Song3 Bok8-su-niu5     
Ka3 kap oh8 Peh8-oe7-ji7 u7 ho2 hoat-tou7 ia7 u7 m7 ho2 hoat-tou7.  Ho2 hoat-tou7 m7-na7 e7 hou7 hak8-seng khah khoai3 sek8 koh e7 chiaN5 ho2 kau3-iok8 cheng2-cheng2 e5 bok8- tek, chhin-chhiuN7 hou7 gin2-na2 oh8 sun7- than3, ti3-i3 kap choan-sim.  M7-ho2 e5 hoat- tou7 m7-na7 hou7 gin2-na2 chin oh sek8, koh e7 hou7 in oh8 bo5 than3, bo5 ti3-i3, kap pin5-toaN7.    
Ti7 Chu2-jit8 Oh8 ka3 peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 eng3-iong7 si7 beh hou7 hak8-seng thang bat Seng3-keng, au7-lai5 ti7 Seng3-chheh pan lai5 kang2-kiu3 Siong7-te3 e5 ka3-si7.  Chiah e5 hak8-seng che7-che7 ti7 in chhu3 bo5 siu7 ka3-si7, chi2-u7 chit8 le2-pai3 chit8 pai2 na7-tiaN7. Sou2-i2 ka3 e5 lang5 tioh8 chin chhiat-i3 hou in lai5 khah khoai3 chin3-pou7 bian2-tit8 kan-ta so5.  TaN ti7 e7-te2 ki3 chit e5 ka3-hoat hou7 tai7-ke lai5 chham-kho2.  
I.Ka3 Tai5-lam5 e5 ji7-bu2 (chit8 pun2 chit8 chiam). 
Bok8-tek: hou7 hak8-seng jin7-ji7. Pi7-pan7 ji7-bu2 tou5 tiau3 ti7 hak8-seng e5 bin7-cheng5, cho3-hoe2 ka3. Ki2 tou5, liam7 chit8 ji7 chit8 ji7 hou7 in thiaN, kio3 in liam7 kau3 sek8.  U7 si5 kio3 hak8-seng khi2-lai5 ka-ti7 ki2 ji7 ti7 tou5-nih8, pat8 lang5 tioh8 chu3-sin5 khoaN3 u7 tioh8 a2 m7 tioh8.  I2-keng ka3 kui2-na7 ji7 kau3 sek8- sek8, chiah hou7 hak8-seng khoaN3 ka-ti7 e5 ji7- bu2 lai5 jin7 hiah-e5. Sian-siN tioh8 sun5, lou7-boe2 cho3-hoe2 khoaN3 tou5 lai5 lian7-sip8.  
II.Ka3 E7-mng5 e5 Peh8-oe7 ji7-bu2 (chit8 pun2 chit8 chiam).   
Bok8-tek: ho7 hak8-seng e7 jin7 im kap tau3-ku3. I2-keng e7 hiau2 jin7-ji7 thang eng7 E7-mng5 e5 ji7-bu2 lai5 cho3 thok8-pun2.  M7-bian2 tui3 thau5, khah ho2 tioh8 tui3 te7-ji7 bin7 e7-te2 hiah e5 pat-im lai5 oh8 khi2.  Che tioh8 hak8-seng sui5 lang5 khoaN3 i e5 chheh. Chong2-si7 na7 u7 ou-pai5, hak8-seng iau2-boe7 lai5 e5 tai7-seng, sian-siN thang eng7 in3-pan2 ji7 lai5 sia2 sou2 beh ka3-e5 ti7 ou-pai5, iu5-goan5 chiau3 teng2-bin7 e5 hoat-tou7 lai5 ka3.  Chit e5 ji7-bu2 na7 thak8 sek8, hak8-seng chiu7 e7 hiau2 tau3-ku3. 
 
III. Ka3-to7 Bun7-tap(Chit8 pun2 saN chiam).   
Bok8-tek: hou7 hak8-seng thak8 e7 khah sun7, ia7 e7 chai i3-su3.  Chit pun2 Chin-to7 Bun7-tap u7 thang kho2-chhu2 si7 (1) Ji7 chin beng5, ku3 te2-te2, u7 chiap8-chiap8 teh liu7, bo5 liam5-piN oaN7chheN-e5.  (2) Eng7 mng7 eng7 in3 hou7 thak8 e5 lang5 khah khoai3 chai i3-su3.  (3) Tui3 chit pun2 thang chiam7-chiam7 bat to7-li2.   
 Tioh8 ti3-i3 hiah e5 be2-chi2, lian5-oeh8, toa7 pun2-ji7, tiam2-ku3, hou7 hak8-seng chai-iaN2. Na7 m7-ai3 long2-chong2 thak8 ia7 thang tiah cham7.    
Iau2 u7 kui2-na7 hang7 ti3-i3:  
(1) Na7-si7 chit8 pan e5 tiong-kan u7 saN-kui2-e5 khah e7, chong2-si7 iau2-boe7 e7 kham-tit chin3 chit8 pan e5 chu-keh, tioh8 siat-hoat lai5 pang-chan7 i e7 tit koe3 pat8 pan, m7 bian2 theng3-hau7 kau3 ni5-tou7.   
(2) Na7 u7 lang5 chin ham-ban7 toe3 be7 tioh8, tek-khak m7 thang tauh8-tauh8 hiam5; khah ho2 tioh8 khoaN3 u7 sim2-mih8 hiaN-ti7 a2-si7 chi2-moe7 thang ke ka3 i bian2- tit hou7 i sit-chi3 m7-ai3 lai5.  (Thai3 18:5,6).   
(3) Kan-ta jin7-ji7 kap tau3-ku3 be7 e7 poe5-iuN2 leng5-hun5, sou2-i2 tak8 pai2 teh ka3 e5 si5 khah ho2 tioh8 poah 10 hun ku2 eng7 khah khoai3 beng5 e5 si a2-si7 Chu2-jit8 Sin5- liong5 hit chat lai5 ka3, a2-si7 soat-beng5 Chu2 e5 lai5-lek8 hou7 in tui3 se3-han3 e7 chai thang chiu7-kun7 Chu2.   
(4) Bo5 lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5, sui-jian5 bo5 lai5 jip8 Chu2-jit8 Oh8, ia7 thang chiong chit e5 hoat-tou7 khi3 gian2-kiu3.   
(5) Ji7-bu2 thak8 liau2 bo5 liam5-piN thak8 si si7 in-ui7 hit tiong-kan u7-e5 khah chhim e5 i3-su3, khah ho2 tioh8 iu5 chhian2 jip8 chhim, chong2-si7 teh le2-pai3 e5 si5 na7 hian ka-ti7 e5 si-chheh khi2-lai5 khoaN3, chiu7 na2 e7 hiau2-tit bian2 leng7-goa7 ka3. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 379 koan3, 1916 ni5 10 geh8)
