
Lun7 kiu7 chiah8 A-phian3 e5 lang5
Chit phiN si7 San sian-siN tui3 sin-bun5 hoan-ek8--e5        
Lai7-seng2 Kong-hoe7 e5 Kam-tok, u7 kau3 SoaN- sai seng2, kong2 tu2-tioh8 pun2-toe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 ka-ki7 liau2 chiN5, siat koe2 a-phian3 kek8.  I kau3 bou2 siaN5, khoaN3 -kiN3 u7 poeh-     kau2-chap8 lang5 pai3 Siong7-te3 , long2 si7 tui3 chit-e5 koe2 a-phian3 kek8 tit-tioh8 kiu3 .        
U7 chit8-e5 pun2-toe7 Bok8-su ka-ki7 chhut chiN5 chong2-tng koe2 a-phian3 kek8 e5 chek-seng5.  Chit-e5 Bok8-su e5 sim chin7 ho2, i2-keng siat chit8-e5 kek8 ti7 bou2 sou2-chai7 , koh ai3 ke3-bou5 siat chit8 sou2-chai7; tak8 cha2-khi2 le2 -pai3 e5 si5, chit8 jit8 ke3 chit8 jit8 , siong5-siong5 chiong chit- e5 tai7-chi3 ki5-to2.  Kau3-be2 i-e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ka7 i kong2, Li2  siaN2-su7 siong5-siong5 in-ui7 chit sou2-chai7 ki5-to2, siaN2-su7 m7-khi3 siat , chhin-chhiuN7 pat8 sou2-chai7 chit8-iuN7 ?  In3 kong2, Goa2 e5 chiN5 long2 liau2, bo5 pat8-hang7 chiN5 thang choe3 chit-e5 lou7-eng7, tioh8 theng3-hau7 Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3.  Hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, HouN7 !  tioh8 eng7 joa7-choe7 chiah oe7 siat?  Kong2, HaiN7 !  na7 beh siat tioh8 u7 ji7-saN-chap8 chheng chiN5chiah oe7.  Hu7-jin5-lang5 thiaN-kiN3 chiu7 khi3, bo5 koh kong2 sim2-mih8.  Te7 ji7 cha2-khi2, chit-e5 Bok8-su iu5-goan5 in-ui7 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 ki5-to2 beng5-pek8, i-e5  hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7-kun7 i , koaN7 chit8- e5 chhiu2-kun toe2 mih8 , ka7 i kong2, Chit lai7-bin7 u7 goa2 e5 chhiu2-khoan5, hi7-kau, chiam-a2,chhiu2-sek, khi3 boe7, sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 gun5 pi2 li2 sou2 khiam3--e5 iau2 khah choe7 ; an2-ni thang khui chit- e5 koe2 a-phian3 kek8 , goa2  bian2 eng7 chiah-e5 mih8 .        
Hit-e5 Bok8-su ka7 Kam-tok kong2, Goa2 bo5 oe7 thang ka7 li2 kong2 chit e5 tai7-chi3 , m7 tsai thang khui chit keng koe2 a-phian3 kek8 choe3 te7-it hoaN-hi2, a2 -si7 tit-tioh8 hu7-jin5-lang5 oe7 khek-ki2 thai7-jin5 choe3 koh khah hoaN-hi2 .         
Kam-tok kong2, Goa2 le2 -pai3-jit8 khi3 i-e5 chhu3 , put-chi2 u7 peng5-an, chu7-jian5 u7 kap i kong2-oe7 . Goa2 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, Li2 pang3-sak chiah e5 chhiu2-sek, kiam2 bo5 khou2 mah? In-ui7 goa2 tsai Tiong-kok e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 Tang-si3 chiah-e5 kok e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, ia7 long2 si7 tiong7 chit -ho7 mih8, liah8 chng-thaN7 choe3 iau3-kin2.  TaN chit-  
e5 sian-siN-niu5 chin7 peng5-an, chiu7 khoaN3 i-e5 bin7, sui-jian5 be7 kong2-oe7, goa2 i2-keng chai i beh kong2 sim2-mih8 oe7.  
I in3 kong2, Bo5 goa chin7 hoaN-hi2; goa2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 Chu2 Ia5-sou choe3 ho2-giah8, I kiam2 bo5 hou7 lang5 ti-chiok.          
Kam-tok kong2, HaiN7 !  Peng5-iu2 ah, goa2 oh-tit thang ka7 lin2 kong2, goa2  hoaN-hi2 kau3 lau5 bak8-sai2.  I2-keng 20 ni5 ku2, u7 thoe3 SoaN-sai kap keh-piah seng2 ki5-to2, na7-si7 sui-bong2 be7 bat kau3 hia, taNthau5-chit8-pai2 kau3 chit te3, goa2 tu2-tioh8  lang5 ui7-tioh8 Chu2 e5 mia5 siu7 toa7 kan-khou2, u7-e5 sit-loh8 i-e5 mia5, u7-e5 hou7 koaN-hu2  hian2-beng5 leng5-jiok8 i , u7-e5 sit-loh8 ke-giap8 , u7 chit8 lang5 hou7 i-e5 bou2 li7-khui i, in-ui7 m7-ai3 kap pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 saN-kap khia7-khi2.  Goa2 kau3 pat8-seng2 tu2-tioh8 pat8 lang5 siu7 tang7 chhi3 -lian7, u7 chit8 lang5 hou7 lang5 koah i-e5 hi7-a2 , chha-put-to hiam2 si2 , siu7 chan5-jim2 e5 phah, koh u7 leng7-goa7 e5 leng5-jiok8 , chit-ho7 lang5  iau2-ku2 hoaN-hi2  siok8 Chu2.  In u7 an-sim chin3-pou7 to7-tek , po3-iong5 Ki-tok e5 hok-im . HaiN7 !  Goa2 e5 sim kam2-un, chin7 ai3 peng5-iu2 lin2 oe7 tit -thang thiaN--kiN3 goa2 sou2 thiaN-kiN3, chap8 hun7 chit8 hun7 e5 kan-cheng3.  Koh khoaN3-kiN3 hiah-e5 lang5 e5 bin7 kng-kng hoaN -hi2 , na7 oe7 khoaN3-kiN3 in, tek-khak ke-thiN lin2 e5 ho2-taN2, heng-khi2 toa7 ng3-bang7 ,  Tiong-kok bo5 ku2 oe7 tit-tioh8 toa7 un. 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 31 tiuN, 1888 ni5 1 geh8)
