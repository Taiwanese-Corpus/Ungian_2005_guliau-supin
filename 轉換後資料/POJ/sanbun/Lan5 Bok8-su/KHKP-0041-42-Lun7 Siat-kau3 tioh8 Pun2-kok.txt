Lun7 Siat-kau3 tioh8 Pun2-kok lang5 tam-tng
San Bok8-su
Lan2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 beng5-pek8 chit ho7 e5  tai7-chi3, in-ui7 kui2-lah8 hang7 e5 ian5-kou3; it,  chiu7-si7 oe7 eng7 ti3-sek, tu2-ho2 e5 hoat-tou7  choe3 tai7-chi3; ji7, oe7 sun7 tak8 hang7 e5 ki- hoe7; saN, oe7 bian2 gui5-hiam2.  U7 lang5  kong2, Gia5-kang, kap-a2, choa5, sam put-hap8, chiah e5 oe7; chiu7-si7 kong2, goa7-kok  lang5, peh-siN3, koaN-hu2 oh-tit saN ho5.  Lan2  ai3 tu5-khi3 chit e5 i3-su3.  Kun7-lai5 chi2 saN- teng2 ho7 e5 lang5 u7 ke-thiN sio-bat siong- sin3.  Lan2 chin-chiaN3 ai3 tai7-ke na2-ku2 na2  ho5-peng5.  Pat8 kok toa7 pian3 u7 keng-ke3  toa7 kiau2-jiau2.  U7 lang5 phah-sng3 ti7 Tiong- kok khoaN3 oe7 bian2.  KhoaN3 hian7-si5 Tiong- kok chiam7-chiam7 heng, teh kian3-ti3  choe7-choe7 hang7 iu2-ek e5 tai7-chi3; thang  ng3-bang7 tak8 hang7 e5 ho2-su7 an2-ni un5-a2  chiaN5, lang5 chiam7-chiam7 beng5-pek8, koaiN3-si3, chiu7 m7 sai2 hun-loan7.  
Lan2 tioh8 siuN7 tui3 kau3-hoe7 kian3-ti3 kau3-taN, lan2 ti7 chiah-e5 toe7-hng u7 tu2-tioh8 sim2- mih8 tai7-chi3.  Khi2-thau5 Ia5-sou-kau3 soe3- soe3, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 nng2 e5 chhiu7 teh  puh-iN2, si3-bin7 e5 lang5 khah m7 chai, sou2-i2  khah bo5 tui3-tek8.  Kau3-hoe7 na2 heng, toa7  soe3 e5 lang5 na2 khoaN3-kiN3, chiu7 na2 siuN7, TaN  beh chaiN2-iuN7!  Kau3-kham3 22 ni5 lai7-bin7,  lan2 sou2 keng-ke3 put-it.  U7 si5 beh kian3-ti3  kau3-hoe7 ti7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7, tu2-tioh8 lang5  tong3 ai3 ham7-hai7 lan2, boe7 bian2 eng7 hoat- tou7 tu--in.  U7 si5 si7-toa7-lang5 m7 siat-hoat.   U7 si5 chiau3 kong-to7 lai5 pan7.  Chong2-si7  thoat-li7 goa7-bin7 e5 tui3-tek8, iau2-ku2 oe7 tu2- tioh8 lai7-bin7 e5 tui3-tek8.  Choe7-choe7 lang5  lai5 pai3 Siong7-te3 m7 si7 in-ui7 in him-bou7  Kiu3-chu2 ai3 chiaN5 to7-tek, put-ko3 houN3-li7, a2-si7 houN3-se3, lam2 lang5 phah-sng3 oe7 piN3  beng2.   Lan2 m7 ai3 si5--in, ng3-bang7 in oe7  pian3-thong.   Chong2-si7 lan2 in-ui7 chit ho7  lang5 iat-lek8 toa7 m7 peng5-an, si7 in put  thong, ia7 hou7 pat8 lang5 put thong, i3-su3  m7 si7 chin, khoan2-sit bo5 tho3-tong3, na7-bo5  koe2-oaN7, eng2-oan2 oh-tit kiat-sit8, kiam2-chhai2 hou7 lan2 khang-khang tioh8-boa5.  Na7 bo5 eng7 sin5 kap seng5-sit8 kap Siong7-te3 saN chhin, chin-chiaN3 boe7 tiau5; an2-ni chhin-chhiuN7 khi2 chhu3 ti7 soa e5 teng2-bin7, theng3-hau7 chhin3-chhai2 toa7 su7 siN-si2, kiam2-chhai2 saN-ko3, chiu7 sit-chi3 pang3-sak Chu2.   Che sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7?  
Chiau3 kui-ku2, Ho5-iok 10 ni5 chit8-pai2  tioh8 teng5-thau5 siuN7 khoaN3 beh ke, kiam2,  koe2 a2 bo5?  Teng2 pai2 than3 chit e5 tai7- chi3 e5 si5, u7 choe7-choe7 hang7 ki5-koai3 e5 su7.   Chhin-chhiuN7 tak8 ho7 e5 iau5-gian5 kong2  lan2 eng7 tok8 ioh8 hiat ti7 chui2-chiN2, ia7 u7  pat8 mih8 ioh8 thang taN2-tiap8 lang5, beh  phian3 in hou7 in jip8-kau3.  Koh Pak-kiaN  e5 si7-toa7-lang5 kiaN5 bun5 khi3 Se-kok, kong2  kau3-hoe7 e5 le7 kap Tiong-kok e5 le7 put hap8; tek-khak tioh8 Tiong-kok e5 koaN-hu2  chiang2-li2 kau3-hoe7 chiah ho2-se3!  Che chiu7-si7 ki5-koai3 e5 oe7.  Koh u7 phok-hak8 e5 lang5  choe3 tok8 e5 chheh ho7 cho7 Phek-sia5-liok8,  ia7 si7 toa7 lam7-sam2 hiam5 kau3-hoe7 choe7- choe7 hang7 khou2-ouN3 e5 su7.  Tu2-tioh8 chit ho7  su7 m7-si7 sio2-khoa2 e5 koan-he7.  
Lun7 lan2 sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 tui3-tek8 kiam2-chhai2 oe7 hun-piat saN-ho7.  1. U7 si5 kau3-hoe7  e5 tui3-tek8 put-ko3 si7 kiong5 e5 lang5: chhin-chhiuN7 ngou2-lou7 u7 kiong5-jiok8, beng2-lam2 e5  su7.  Bou2 sou2-chai7 e5 hiaN-ti7 si7 lam2 lang5, tu2-tu2 hian3 toe7 choe3 le2-pai3-tng5 e5 lou7-eng7,  beng2 lang5 chiu7 ka-ki7 siuN7 beh than3 ki- hoe7 siN-khi2 kiau2-jiau2, khoaN3 oe7 theh8 chiN5,  chiu7 chhoa7 lang5 lai5 hoah-hiam3, liah8- chhiuN2.  Lun7 kau3-hoe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 oe7 saN ko3  a2 boe7, che bo5 tek-khak.  In kui2-lah8-e5 u7  in e5 tong2.  M7-ku2 lan2 m7 ai3 chit-ho7 e5  ke3-kau3.  
2. U7 si5 kiau2-jiau2 e5 ian5-kou3 si7 oan3-hun7  goa7-kok lang5 ia7 kaN2, ia7 bo5 beng5-pek8 lan2  sou2-choe3.  Phi3-ju7 bou2 ui7 pe5-leng5-chun5 e5 si5  u7 seng toa7 su7, koaN-hu2 ia7 m7 chou2-tong3,  chiu7 na2 hai7.  
3. U7 si5 sit8-chai7 in-ui7 kau3-hoe7 e5 khoan2- sit seng su7.  Bou2 sou2-chai7, than3 ngia5-put8  e5 si5, hiaN-ti7 m7 toe5 sia5 e5 chiN5 chiu7 toa7 ke3-kau3.  Lun7 hi3 chiN5 kap hau3 chou2 e5 su7 kiam2- chhai2 kiau2-jiau2 e5 chit8 poaN3 thang sng3 si7  in-ui7 kau3-hoe7 e5 to7-li2.  
Ju5-kau3 chin-chiaN3 beng2 ti7 Tiong-kok,  thang kiaN in siong5-siong5 ai3 hai7 Ia5-sou- kau3 e5 lang5.  Chong2-si7 Siong7-te3 iau2 ti7- teh.  I oe7 kou3 chiau2-chiah, kap hoe-chhau2,  kam2 boe7 kou3 lang5?  Chong2-si7 lan2 e5 chi3,  tioh8 kian-kou3 kho3 i.  
TaN m7 chai lan2 oe7 tai7-liok8 beng5 lan2  ti7 Tiong-kok tioh8 eng7 siaN2 hoat hou7 kau3- hoe7 heng.  U7 lang5 phah-sng3 na7-bo5 Ho5- iok chiu7 oh-tit chhoan2-khui3.  Koh u7 lang5  phah-sng3 tek-khak tioh8 koaN-hu2 tai7-seng  beng5-pek8 Ia5-sou kau3 e5 lou7-eng7, lai5 thong- ti peh-siN3, kau3-hoe7 chiah oe7 heng, khun2-tiok8 e5 su7 chiu7 khah bo5.   Che si7 chin choe7  pun2-toe7 lang5 khoaN3 Tiong-kok e5 hong-siok8, chiu7 phah-sng3 an2-ni.  Hut8-kau3 si7  tui3 goa7-kok lai5, tai7-seng bo5 hou7 Hong5-te3  hong.  KaN2 si7 choe7-choe7 lang5 khoaN3 choe3  u7 lou7-eng7, chiu7 tiaN7-tioh8 chhiaN2 Hong5-te3  hong i.  Na7-si7 lan2 bo5 kiu5 Hong5-te3 e5 se3- thau5, put-ko3 ai3 tit-tioh8 ki-hoe7 thang  kong2 to7-li2, chio hak8-seng, siat kau3, kiaN5  tak8 hang7 li7-ek lang5 e5 su7.  Ia7 ai3 pai3  Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 tak8-e5 chu7-chu2, tiaN7-tioh8  i3-su3, siN-si2 ho7-hok, tek-khak sun7 Siong7-te3.   In-ui7 Siong7-te3 pun2 si7 lang5-lang5 tioh8 pai3.   Kho2-sioh sia5 lang5 khah ai3 than3, chin-li2  khah m7 ai3.  Koh kiaN5 chit8 nng7 hang7 e5  tek-heng7 khah khoai3, na7-si7 chin7 sim,  seng3, lat8, i3, lai5 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, che  khah-oh.  U7 si5 tu2-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 lang5 m7- ai3 eng7 keng3-khian5 e5 sim liau7-li2 ke.  Koh  u7 si5 lang5 phah-sng3 kok e5 su7 kap Siong7-te3  bo5 kan-siap8, che toa7 chho3.  
Siat-su2 Ju5-kau3 e5 lang5 oe7 chai lan2 sou2  choe3 bo5 m7 li7-ek lang5 e5 seng-khu, leng5- hun5, koh ke, kok, long2-chong2, chiu7 in  m7-sai2 kiau-ngou7 khoaN3-khin.
Lan2 put-chi2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 hou7 lang5  chai lan2 sou2 hok8-sai7 long2 m7-si7 su e5 Siong7- te3, si7 kong-cheng3--e5, chiu7-si7 thiN-toe7 e5  chin Chu2, chit8-e5 e5 Siong7-te3.  Koh i i2-keng  si7 thiN-toe7 e5 Chu2 khah iaN5 ban7 u7, chiu7 i  e5 chi2-i3 tek-khak tioh8 chiaN5.  Thian-liong5  lang5-lang5 piN5-piN5 u7, chiah tioh8 piN5-piN5  tang5 sim hok8-sai7 Chu2 Ia5-ho5-hoa.  Lang5- lang5 piN5-piN5 siu7 i e5 po3-siuN2, heng5-hoat8.   Che m7-si7 khoaN3-khin se3-kan e5 si7-toa7.  Ti7   
thian-tiok kok jiau2-loan7 e5 si5 kau3-hoe7  lang5 te7 it ho2 khoan2.  Tak8 sou2-chai7 ia7 si7  an2-ni.  Se3-kan e5 si7-toa7 si7 Siong7-te3 siat- lip8.  Put-ko3 i sou2 liau7-li2 chiu7-si7 seng-khu  e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 boe7-oe7 chai sim.  Siong7-te3  chai lang5 e5 sim-toe2.  I chiu7-si7 ban7 u7 e5  Chu2.  
Lan2 siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 bu5-ti e5 lang5  au5-kio3 kong2, Hoan-a2 kau3, chiu7-si7 khoaN3  Siong7-te3 choe3 goa7-kok e5 Chu2, kap Tiong- kok bo5 kan-siap8.  Che si7 ban7-poaN e5 hai7,  na2 chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 ai3 Tiong-kok lang5  than3 koh-iuN7 e5 to7-li2, kap kok-hoat put- tong5.  Chin-chiaN3 m7 si7 an2-ni.   Lan2 e5 ka3-si7 long2 m7 si7 hoe3-bo5 pun2-toe7 chheh, a2-si7  pun2-toe7 le7, sim2-mih8 ho2 e5 i3-su3.  Siat-su2 u7  lang5 kong2, lan2 ka3 lang5 put-hau3, che m7  si7 pat8 hang7, put-ko3 si7 pe7-bu2 si7-toa7 ai3  tong3 lang5 pai3 Siong7-te3, lan2 put-tek- put tioh8 kong2 Siong7-te3 si7 khah-iaN5 lang5, lang5 kau3 lou7-be2 oe7 chai, Sun7-thiN chiah  chun5.  Lang5 lam7-sam2 khoaN3 kau3-hoe7 choe3  “hoan”, chit-e5 hai7 put-chhian2.  It, chiu7-si7  bu5-ti e5 lang5 chiu7 liam5-piN oan3-hun7 lan2.   Koh, Lo2-tia e5 lang5 phah-sng3 che kau3  si7 siok8 ti7 goa7-kok e5 koan5, kap in choe3  tui3-chhiu2, m7 si7 ho5 peng5, na2 chhin-chhiuN7  kau3 kap kok bo5 hun-piat.  Koh, chiu7-si7  bo5 lau2-sit8 e5 lang5 lai5 ka2-chia3 lan2 e5 mia5 khi3  kiaN5 i sai-khia e5 tai7-chi3.  Koh, lun7 pai3  Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 ia7 u7 m7-tioh8, bo5 iong2-chi3,  bo5 beng5-pek8 tioh8 ka-ki7 chu7-chu2 chu7-lip8  kau3-hoe7, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chu7-lip8, lai5  chhiaN2 ka-ki7 e5 sian-siN, bian2 kho3 sim2-mih8 lang5.  Che tek-khak m7 si7 chit8 jit8 oe7  chiaN5, chong2-si7 i3-su3 tioh8 thong-tat8, chiu7 na2-ku2 na2-chiaN5.  Na7 bo5 an2-ni, Ia5-sou kau3  eng2-eng2 bo5 choe3 Tiong-kok pun2-toe7 e5  sou2 siat, an2-ni put-si5 oe7 to2-hoai7.  To3-peng2  lai5 kong2, na7 si7 chin-sit8 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7  oe7 hiau2 khoaN3 ki-hoe7, oe7 hiau2 lan2 sou2 sin3 chiu7-si7 eng2-oan2 e5 to7-li2, chiu7 long2 m7 io5-tang7.  Lang5 hai7 seng-khu, boe7 hai7 sin5-hun5.  Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 kong2, chiau3  Tiong-kok e5 le7 si7 soe3 boe7 chu2-i3, long2 si7  si7-toa7 e5 koan5.  An2-ni kong2 e5 lang5 tioh8  oh8 chit-e5 sin e5 to7-li2, chiu7-si7 tak8 lang5 tioh8  sun7 Siong7-te3 te7 it.  Lan2 khoaN3 si7-soe3 lun7  ho2 phaiN2 sun7 sai-khia e5 i3, che te7-it chham2.  
Chhian-ban7 boh8-tit kho3 pat8 lang5, ka- ki7 tioh8 iong2 ka-ki7 e5 chi3.  Ho5-iok e5 li7-ek,   
hian7-chai7 u7.  Beh siaN2 chai oe7 kau3 loa7-ku2?  Lan2 ka-ki7 liau7-li2 tak8 hang7 tai7-chi3,  chiu7-si7 chiaN5 toa7 lang5 khoan2.  Na7 gau5  thun-lun2, phin2-heng7 na2 chiaN5, lang5-lang5  chiu7 chai lan2 si7 sim2-mih8 ho7 e5 lang5.  Siong7-te3 kap lan2 ti7-teh.  Lan2 tioh8 pi7-pan7 siu7  hai7.  Thai5-iaN5 lan2 seng-khu e5 su-iok8 si7  kan-lan5 tai7, iaN5 goa7-bin7 e5 tui3-tek8 m7 eng- kai-tioh8 khah oh.  Cheng5, u7 toa7 chhut  mia5 e5 lang5 tui3 chit8 kok keng-ke3 chit8  kok, chhe7 ki-hoe7 thang chin7 pun2-hun7  hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3.  Siong7-te3 hou7 i toa7  heng-thong.  Khi2-thau5 choe3 Chu2 e5 hak8-seng e5 lang5 m7-kaN2 poe7-gek8 Chu2, chiu7 si2  ia7 hoaN-hi2.  Tan3-i2-li2 e5 chheh ki3-chai3 hi2  saN lang5 ti7 he2-lou5 e5 su7, kau3-giah8 hou7 lan2  siuN7 leng5-khou2 siu7 khou2, boh8-tit gek8 Siong7- te3.  Lun7 tak8-hang7 e5 tai7-chi3 lang5 na7 boe7  kham-tit kan-khou2, chiu7 boe7 kham-tit  hok-khi3.  KhoaN3 Su3-tou5 Po2-lo5 leng5-khou2  koaiN-kaN si2, boh8-tit soah i e5 tioh8-boa5, hou7  goa7-pang e5 lang5 kap Iu5-thai3 lang5 piN5-piN5  tit-tioh8 kiu3.  KiaN5 soe2-le2 Iok-han7, leng5- khou2 si2, boh8-tit iong5-un2 phaiN2.  ChhiaN2  lan2 soe3-ji7 siuN7 khoaN3 lan2 in-ui7 sim2-mih8  tai7-chi3 kaN2 si2? 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3,te7 41~42 tiuN, 1888 ni5 9-10 geh8)
