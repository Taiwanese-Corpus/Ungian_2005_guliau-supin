
Pin5-toaN7 Niau ka7 si2 Niau2-chhu2

(Tan5 Chheng-gi7)

Ti7 goan2 e5 chhu3-lai7 niau2-chhu2 chin-choe7.  Siong5-siong5 lai5 koe-tiau5 ka7 koe-a2, ti3-kau3 koe-a2 chhi7 long2 boe7 khi2.  Goan2 chiu7 ka7 lang5 tho2 niau-a2 lai5 chhi7.  Long2-chong2 u7 chhi7 saN chiah, chit8 chiah khah siau3-lian5, nng7 chiah khah lau7.  Hit chiah khah siau3-lain5 e5, siong5-siong5 khoaN3 i ka7-tioh8 niau2-chhu2 teh lang7, lang7-liau2 na7 sian7 chiu7 chiah8, iah8 u7-si5 khoaN3 i kan-ta ka7 si2, long2 bo5 chiah8.  Iau2 hit nng7 chiah khah lau7 e5 niau long2 m7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 in ka7-tioh8 niau2-chhu2, kan-ta khoaN3-kiN3 i ka7 si2 niau2-chhu2 teh chiah8 na7-tiaN7. Siok8-gu2 kong2, Pin5-toaN7 niau, ka7 si2 niau2-chhu2, ko2-jian5 u7 iaN2.  
Kun7-lai5 Kau3-phai3 na2 choe7 lai5 Tai5-oan5,u7-e5 tui3 Chi-na2 lai5, u7-e5 tui3 Loe7-te7 lai5.  Goa2 kio3 si7 beh lai5 saN-kap choe3 kang ti7 Tai5-oan5, lai5 kiu3 Tai5-oan5 si3-pah-ban7 e5 peh-siN3, ki5-sit8 to m7 si7 an2-ni.  Siong5-se3 ka7 i gian2-kiu3 khoaN3-bai7, to si7 beh lai5 khan pian7-iuN5, kap ka7 si2 niau2-chhu2.  
Sou2 eng7 e5 chhiu2-toaN7 chin chhou, long si7 hiam5 pat8 kau3-phai3 e5 phaiN2, o-lo2 ka-ki7 e5 kau3-phai3 e5 ho2, tui3 pat8 kau3-phai3 chinbo5 le2-sou3 chin bo5 jin5-ai3, ki3-jian5 khiam3 jin5-ai3 beh chhut-lai5 thoan5 jin5-ai3 e5 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 kiam2 u7 kong-hau7 ?  Seng3-keng kong2, jin5 ai3 si7 te7-it toa7, na7 u7 jin5-ai3 chiah u7 khoan-iong5 thun-lun2 chu5-pi thiaN3-thang3, bo5 oan3-tou3, bo5 khoa-khau2,bo5 phin2-phong2.  Khia7 ti7 tai5-teng2, beh khoa-khau2 ka-ki7 e5 chin kap chheng-khi3-siuN3, phin2-phong2 se3-kan seh saN lin3,khoa-khau2 bo5 hoan7-choe7, phin2-phong2 siat-kau3 e5 kin2, i jin5-ai3 ti7 to2-ui7 ?  Tioh8 chu7-seng2, na7 bo5, oe7 hou7 Po2-lo5 Sian-siN ioh-tioh8.  
"Goa2 na7 oe7 kong2 tak8-kok e5 khiuN-khau2, kap thiN-sai3 e5 oe7, na7 bo5 jin5-ai3 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 phah-lo5 phah la5-poah8. "Koh loh8-khi3 nng7 chat, chhiaN tai7-ke ka-ki7 thak8.
