

Tu2-phu7-lo2 (Doppler) Hau7-eng3 
 Ek8-sia2 Teng Lian5-chong

Chong-a chit kui2 jit8 sio lian5-soa3 chiap tioh8 nng7-tiuN kau-thong ui5-kui e5 ang5-toaN, koh long2 u7 kap chhiau-sok e5 siong3-phiN3. Sui-bong2 hoat8-kim bo5 goa7 che7, m7-ku2 lap8-chiN5 e5 si5, sim-koaN ia7 si7 thiaN3 tiuh-tiuh. Chiah e5 chhiau-sok e5 siong3- phiN3 long2 si7 chhai7 ti7 lou7-piN a2-si7 an-choan5-to2 e5 hip-siong3-ki hip..e5. Si7 chaiN2-iuN7 chhiau-sok chiu7 hip, bo5 chhiau-sok to7 bian2?
Au3-tit-li2(Austria) e5 But8-li2 hak8-ka Tu2-phu7-lo2 ti7 1842 ni5 kong2 tioh8 siaN-pho u7 chit8-khoan2 hian7-siong7, chiu7-si7 lang5 kap chhut-siaN e5 mih8-kiaN7 hou7-siong7 oa2-kin7 e5 si5, i sou2 thiaN--tioh8 e5 siaN-pho e5 pin5-lut8 (frequency) e7 pian3 khah koan5, na7-si7 lang5 kap chhut-siaN e5 mih8-kiaN7 hou7-siong7 li7-khui e5 si5, i sou2 thiaN--tioh8 e5 pin5-lut8 e7 pian3 khah ke7. 1845 ni5 Ho5-lan5-lang5 Ballot cho3 chit8 e5 sit8-giam7 lai5 cheng3-beng5 Tu2-phu7-lo2 hau7-eng3. I chhoe7 kui2-e5 pun5 lat-pah..e5 che7 tiam3 hoe2-chhia teng2, cho3-hoe2 toa7-siaN pun5 lat-pah. Hoe2-chhia sai2 oa2--lai5 e5 si5, khia7 ti7 lou7-piN e5 lang5 thiaN-tioh8 lat-pah e5 siaN-tiau7 (frequency) ju2 lai5 ju2 koan5, hoe2-chhia li7-khui e5 si5 lang5 sou2 thiaN--tioh8 e5 soa3 ju2 lai5 ju2 ke7. M7 si7 kan-taN siaN-pho e7 san2-seng chit-khoan2 hian7-siong7, lian5 tian7-chu5-pho (chhin-chhiuN7 bo5-soaN3-tian7-pho, kng-pho) ma7 e7 chhut-hian7 Tu2-phu7-lo2 hau7-eng3.
Chit-ma2 beh lai5 kong2 Chong-a ui5-kui hou7 lang5 hip-siong7 e5 tai7-chi3. Chhai7 ti7 lou7-piN e5 hip-siong3-ki lai7-te2 u7 chit8 e5 tian7-chu5-pho hoat-sia7-khi3. I put-koan2-si5 long2 teh hoat-sia7 tian7-chu5-pho, na7-si7 chhia sai2 koe3 i e5 thau5-cheng5, chiu7 e7 ka7 chiah e5 tian7-chu5-pho hoan2-sia7 to3-tng2 khi3 hou7 hip-siong3-ki e5 tian7-chu5-pho chiap-sui-khi3. In-ui7 chhia si7 teh sai2 li7-khui hip-siong3-ki, sou2-i2 i chiap--tioh8 e5 tian7-chu5-pho e5 pin5-lut8 it-teng7 pi2 i goan5-pun2 hoat-sia7 chhut--khi3..e5 khah ke7. Chit-si5 khng3 ti7 hip-siong3-ki lai7-te2 e5 tian7-nau2 chiu7 e7 pi2-pheng7, na7-si7 pin5-lut8 kiam2-chio2 e5 sou3-liong7 bo5 cheng-chha kai3-che7, an2-ni chiu7 bo5 sng3 chhiau-sok. Na7-si7 pin5-lut8 kiam2-chio2 e5 sou3-liong7 chhiau koe3 tian7-nau2 siat-teng7 chhiau-sok e5 hoan7-ui5, chit-si5 hip-siong3-ki chiu7 hip-siong7--louh, tian7-nau2 sun7-soa3 ka7 chhia e5 sok-tou7 sng3--chhut--lai5, chhiau-sok ju2 che7 hoat8-kim chiu7 ai3 lap8 ju2 che7. Che chiu7-si7 Tu2-phu7-lo2 hau7-eng3 e5 chit8-chiong2 eng3-iong7.
Lan2 tiaN7- tiaN7 kong2, hian7-chhu2-si5 e5 U2-tiu7 si7 it-tit8 teh phong3-toa7, che si7 m7 si7 u7 sim2-mih8 kun-ku3? Am3-si5 thiN-teng2 e5 chhiN sou2 hou7 lan2 khoaN3--tioh8 e5 kng, keng-koe3 kho-hak8-ka cho3 kng-phou2 (spectrum) e5 hun-sek, m7-koan2 to2 chit8 liap8 chhiN, long2 u7 ang5-hiong3-soa2-ui7 (Red shift) e5 hian7-siong7. Chit khoan2 ang5-hiong3-soa2-ui7 chiu7-si7 kong2 chhiN e5 kok-chiong2 sek-kng, long2 teh chhu-hiong3  ang5-sek-kng lai5 pian3-sek, ia7 chiu7 si7 pho-tng5 pian3 khah tng5, pin5-lut8 pian3 khah ke7. Pin5-lut8 e7 pian3 khah ke7 e5 in-toaN, chiu7-si7 thiN-teng2 e5 chhiN it-tit8 teh li7-khui lan2, sim7-chi3 thiN-chhiN teh li7-khui lan2 e5 sok-tou7 ma7 e7-tang3 sng3--chhut--lai5. Che ma7 si7 ai3 kam2-sia7 Tu2-phu7-lo2 hau7-eng3, hou7 lan2 chai-iaN2 U2-tiu7 si7 teh phong3-toa7. Chit-ma2 lian5 ti7 lan2 hoeh-kng2 e5 hoeh, ma7 e7-tang3 iong7 Tu2-phu7-lo2 hau7-eng3 lai5 sng3-chhut i teh lau5 e5 sok-tou7.
