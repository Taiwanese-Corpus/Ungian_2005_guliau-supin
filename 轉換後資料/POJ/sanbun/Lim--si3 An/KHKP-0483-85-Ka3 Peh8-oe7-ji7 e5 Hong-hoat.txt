Ka3 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Hong-hoat
Lim5--si7 An    
Kin7-lai5 goa2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang ka3 Peh8- oe7-ji7, sou2-i2 u7 tam7-poh8 keng-giam7, koh u7 khoaN3-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 khah che7 iau2 chiau3 ku7-khoan2, khah ui5-lan5 e5 hoat-tou7, teh ka3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 hak8-seng. Chim2-moa2 ai3 chiong goa2 pun2-sin sou2 siuN7-chhut e5 hong- hoat lai5 ki3 kui2-ku3.    
ChhiaN2 tai7-ke siuN7 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 bok8- tek si7 sim2-mih8? Kiam2 m7-si7 beh ka3 to7- li2, in2-chhoa7 hak8-seng lai5 bat Chu2 Ia5-sou cho3 in e5 Kiu3-chu2? M7-ku2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 kou-put-chiong tioh8 koh ke ka3 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 tioh8 ka3 hak8-seng e7-hiau2 thak8 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Seng3-keng; in-ui7 chin-che7 gin2-na2 long2 bo5 lou7 thang leng7-goa7 oh8. Tui3 an2-ni, be7-hiau2 thak8 Seng3-keng e5 hak8-seng tioh8 sng3 si7 U7-pi7 Kho, iah8 sou2 beh ka3 in chiu7-si7 To7-li2 i2-kip8 Peh8-oe7-ji7. Cheng5-ni5 e5 Kho3-pun2 i2-keng bat the3 lan2 hun sou2 beh ka3 e5 si5-kan, chiu7-si7 phi3-ju7 lai5 kong2, na7-si7 khui-hoe7 pi3-hoe7 i2-goa7 iau2 u7 45 hun ku2, ka3 to7-li2 (chiu7-si7 kong2 kou3-su7 tioh8 10 hun a2-si7 15 hun); sou2-i2 beh ka3 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 si5-kan chiu7-si7 35 hun a2- si7 30 hun ku2. TaN chit8 le2-pai3 ka3 chit8 pai2 na7-tiaN7, sou2-i2 ti7 hit e5 30 goa7 hun ku2 e5 si5 lan2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 eng7 te7-it ho2 e5 hoat-tou7, thang hou7 hak8-seng gau5 chin3-pou7, m7-bian2 hou7 in ni5-koe3-ni5 iau2 teh thak8 Ji7-bo2. Khiok peh-hoe3 i2-ha7 e5 hak8-seng tioh8 chiau3 Iu3-ti7 Kho oah8-tang7 e5 hoat-tou7 lai5 ka3. Lun7-kau3 Iu3-ti7 Kho au7-pai2 beh sia2, chit-chun7 sou2 ki3--e5 si7 hap8 ti7 kau2-hoe3 i2- siong7 e5 hak8-seng (Tai5-oan5 hoe3).    
Tak8 keng hak8-hau7 sou2 ka3 e5 kau3-kho long2 u7 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 kau3-kho-su, chong2-si7 chiau3 goa2 khoaN3 lan2 teh eng7 khah chhin3- chhai2 e5 hoat-tou7 lai5 ka3 Peh8-oe7-ji7, ti3-kau3 hou7 hak8-seng loe2-chi3, bo5 sim2-mih8 heng3- thau5 ai3 thak8, soa3 tiaN7-tiaN7 ti7-hia teh liam7 "b, ch, chh."  Kin7-lai5 goa2 sou2 eng7 e5 kau3- kho u7 hun 4 cham7: Ji7-bo2, Peh8-oe7-ji7 Thok8-pun2, soa3 tiah Seng3-si saN-chap8 siu2 si, i2-kip8 Iok-han7 Hok-im-toan7, Su3-tou5  
 Heng7-toan7.  Na7 i2-keng thak8 chit kui2- hang7, thang kong2 si7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 chut giap8, e7 kham-tit jip8 Seng3-keng Kho.    
Lun7-kau3 Ji7-bo2 kap Thok8-pun2 tioh8 thak8 kau3 chap8-hun sek8, cho3 ho2 e5 te7-ki, au7-lai5 sou2 thak8 e5 Si i2-kip8 Seng3-chheh e7 khah-khoai3 thak8. Seng3-si m7-bian2 tui3 thau5-chit8-siu2 lai5 ka3; na7-si7 tiah khah ti7 chheh e5 tiong-ng khah be7 phoa3. Goa2 bat khoaN3 hak8-seng si-chheh e5 thau5 nng7 saN iah8 long2 phoa3-noa7!  Koh na7-si7 khah se3-han3--e5, hou7 in thak8 gin2-na2 si, si7 khah hap8. Koh iau3-kin2 m7-thang chun2 nng7 saN e5 hak8-seng pi7-pan7 siang7 chit8 siu2, in- ui7 khah be7 hiau2 thak8--e5, si7 kan-ta toe3 khah gau5 thak8--e5 sou2 liam7 e5 oe7, pun2-sin long2 bo5 chhut-lat8 jin7-ji7. Phi3-ju7 lai5 kong2, hou7 chit8 e5 hak8-seng tui3 20 siu2 thak8-khi2, kau3 50 siu2 ui5-chi2; koh chit8 e5 tui3 40 siu2 kau3 70 siu2; un5-un5.  Lun7 thak8 Seng3- keng ma7 si7 chiau3 chit e5 li2-khi3. Na7 tui3 Ma2-thai3 thak8-khi2, bo5 loa7-ku2 hit pun2 Seng3-chheh e5 thau5-cheng5 khah phaiN2- khoaN3, koh chit8 hang7 Ma2-thai3 pi2 Iok- han7 khah oh-tit thak8.  Iok-han7 Hok-im- toan7 tiaN7-tiaN7 e7 thak8-tioh8 sio-siang7 e5 oe7; chhin-chhiuN7 To7, piaN2, kng, iuN5, bok8-chia2, chheN-me5, un5-un5. Chit pun2 thak8-liau2, tioh8 thak8 khah chhim--e5 khah sin e5 oe7, sou2-i2 m7-bian2 thak8 koh chit e5 Hok-im- toan7, chiap-soa3 lai5 thak8 Su3-tou5 Heng7- toan7 khah hah8-eng7. Na7 u7 kau3-oan5 ai3 hou7 hak8-seng leng7-goa7 thak8 Chin-to7 Bun7-tap, a2-si7 sim2-mih8 gin2-na2 chheh, long2 ho2; chong2-si7 chiau3 goa2 sou2 keng- giam7 e5 lai5 ki3 kui2-ku3 hou7 liat8-ui7 cho3 chham-kho2, ng3-bang7 e7 thang in2-chhoa7 hak8-seng thak8 khah chin3-pou7. 
Ti7 chit cham7 beh chiap-soa3 teng2-goeh8 sou2 ki3--e5; chim2-a2 beh kong2-khi2 ka3 Ji7-bo2 e5 hoat-tou7.    
Kau3-oan5 eng-kai tioh8 chit8 ki ian5-pit cho3 i e5 phoaN7! I sou2 tioh8 cho3 ki3-ho7 e5, chiu7-si7 hak8-seng sou2 thak8 kau3 hia e5 sou2-chai7; na7-si7 sim2-mih8 choa7 e5 ji7 iau2-boe7 chap8-hun7 sek8,m7-thang cho3 ki3-ho7. Than3 chit e5 hoat-tou7 u7 nng7 hang7 e5 li7-ek. Hak8- seng chai i tioh8 tui3 to2-ui7 koh pi7-pan7, iah8 kau3-oan5 na7 beh koh-chai3 siu7 i e5 Ji7-bo2, a2-si7 Thok8-pun2, Si-chheh, Seng3-keng, kan-ta tioh8 khoaN3 i lou7-boe2 pai2 e5 ki3-ho7 chiu7 tui3 hit choa7 lai5 thiaN i thak8.  Te7-ji7 hang7 e5 ho2: chit e5 hoat-tou7 si7 kek hak8- seng ai3  
keng3-cheng e5 sim; sian-siN na7 han7 i tioh8 thak8 loa7-che7, kiaN-liau2 khah bo5 ai3 chhut-lat8.  Lun7 kau3-oan5 iau2 u7 chit8 hang7: khah-siong5 goa2 khoaN3 kau3-oan5 teh ka3 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 si5, in pun2-sin teh sun5 hak8-seng, bo5 kio3 hak8-seng kin7-oa2 in. TaN lan2 tioh8 the2-thiap gin2-na2 seng3; gin2- na2 chin ai3 un7-tong7, m7-thang kio3 i tiam7- tiam7 che7 siuN-ku2, in-ui7 e7 sian7, soa3 piN3- cho3 bo5 ti3-i3 e5 hak8-seng.  Khah ho2, kau3- oan5 tioh8 che7; na7 e7 thang siat-hoat che7 ti7 tui3-bin7 si7 siang7-ho2, iah8 kio3 hak8-seng chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5 khia7 ti7 i e5 sin-piN hou7 i siu7. An2-ni, gin2-na2 e7 khah oah8-tang7, kau3-oan5 khah be7 sian7, koh-khah u7 ki- hoe7 thang khoaN3 hak8-seng long2 u7 teh chhut-lat8 a2 bo5. U7 kui2-na7 keng e5 Chu2- jit8-oh8 tioh8 eng7 pai3-tng5-thiaN e5 the-i2 lai5 ka3; an2-ni-seN khah hui3-khi3, in-ui7 kau3- oan5 be7-tit-thang che7 ti7 hak8-seng e5 tui3- bin7. Na7-si7 an2-ni, chhiaN2 kau3-oan5 hou7 hak8- seng che7 chit8 choa7, iah8 i tu2-tu2 che7 ti7 te7-ji7 choa7, ti7 in e5 au7-bin7, iu5-goan5 koan2 khah e7-tioh8, iah8 kio3 in chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5 khia7 ti7 i e5 sin-piN. Na7 hak8-seng chit8-te3 i2-liau5 che7 be7-loh8, nng7 saN e5 thang kap kau3-oan5 che7 ti7 hit e5 te7 ji7 choa7. Lun7 kau3-oan5 kong2 kou3-su7 e5 si5, i iau3-kin2 tioh8 khia7, eng7 oah8-tang7 e5 khoan2 lai5 heng5-iong5 hit e5 kou3-su7. 
  Lun7 ka3 ji7-bo2 e5 hong-hoat, chiau3 goa2 bat khoaN3, khah-che7 lang5 iau2 si7 toe3 ku7- khoan2 lai5 ka3; an2-ne-seN cham2-jian5 khah ui5-lan5, koh si7 khah bo5 chiau3 kau3-siu7- hoat e5 li2. Hian7-si5 Ji7-bo2 e5 te7-ji7 bin7 si7 cho3 te7-it bin7 e5 tui3-bin7, na7-si7 hian-khui liam5-piN e7 tui3 thau5 chit8-bin7 lai5 jin7 te7-ji7 bin7 e5 ji7.  ChhiaN2 kau3-oan5 m7-thang tai7- seng cho3 chit8-e7 ka3 te7-it bin7, b ch chh, a ah aN, e, i, o, u, hiah e5 choa7.  Khah ho2, ka3 b ch chh hit choa7 liau2, chiu7 liam5-piN jin7 te7-ji7 bin7 e5 it cham7, m, p, ch, b.  Ka3 b ch chh e5 si5, hak8-seng tioh8 eng7 ka-ti7 e5 cheng2-thau5-a2 lai5 ki2, chong2-si7 kau3-oan5 m7- thang hou7 i eng7 cheng2-thau5-a2 lai5 kham3- ji7.  I na7 e7-hiau2 thak8 b ch chh loh8--lai5, kio3 i thak8 khi2--khi3; ts th t, un5-un5. Tioh8 kio3 i pun2-sin tui3 b ch chh thak8--loh8-lai5, kau3 i sou2 be7 ki3-tit e5 ji7; an2- ne-seN ka-ti7 chhoe7-tioh8, khah e7 ki3-tit. B ch chh e5 choa7 thak8 loh8--lai5, thak8 khi2-- khi3, long2 sek8, tioh8 eng7 ian5-pit cho3 ki3-ho7, iah8 kio3 i thak8 tui3-bin7 e5 te7-it cham7. Chit cham7 u7 nng7 choa7, tak8 choa7 tioh8 thak8 koe3 khi3 thak8 koe3-lai5.
