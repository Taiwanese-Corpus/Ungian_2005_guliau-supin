
M7 kin2-sin7 e7 hai7
Tio7 Si5-hoe5
Tai7-eng-kok Lun5-tun siaN5-lai7 u7 chit8-e5  sio2-ou5, tang-thiN kian-peng e5 si5, eng2 u7 siau3  lian5 gin2-a2 khi3 hit tiong-kan thit-tho5  chheng2-hoa7; in-ui7 kian-peng e5 sou2-chai7  teng7, u7 sou2-chai7 nng2, kam3-chhat chit8-e5 ou5  e5 lang5 tak8-ui7 kham3-giam7 kau3 beng5-beng5  pek8-pek8, ti7 hiah-e5 nng2 be7 kiaN5--tit e5 sou2-chai7, chhai7 chha5-pio cho3 ki3-jin7, chiu7-si7  beh hou7 lang5 siam2-pi7, bian2-tit hou7 peng- sng ham7-loh8--khi3.  
U7 chit-ho7 bong2-tong2 e5 siau3-lian5, m7- chai thang kiam2-tiam2 chu2-se3, kou3-i3 kiaN5  hit e5 hiam2-te7, ti3-kau3 che7-che7 ham7-loh8  peng-sng lai7 koaN5 si2, che long2 si7 tui3 siau3- lian5 siuN nge7-sim, m7-siu7 khoan2-khng3;  sou2-i2 u7 sin-khu si2-bo5 e5 chai-ho7.  
Che thang phi3-ju7 lang5 tiam3 se3-kan, na7  keng2 ti7 kian-peng teng7 e5 sou2-chai7 lai5 kiaN5,  thit-tho5 ia7 bo5 gui5-hiam2; phi3-ju7 lang5  tiam3 se3-chiuN7 sou2 kiaN5 e5 lou7, sou2-cho3 e5 su7  pun2 si7 peN5-peN5 pang7-pang7, bo5 gui5-hiam2  kiaN-hiaN5, na7 an2-ni, mia5-siaN thang hian2- beng5, seng-khu thang khia7-chai7, chit8-si3- lang5 an-lok8, beh thai3-e7 chin7; na7-si7 lang5  kou3-i3 khi3 kiaN5 ti7 peng-sng bo5 kian-teng7 e5  sou2-chai7 khoaN3-tioh8 chhah-pio e5 ki3-ho7, iau2 m7 siam2-pi7; che si7 phi3-ju7 lang5 m7 hok8 khou2-khng3, kiaN5 put-hoat e5 su7, cho3 hai7-lang5 e5  tai7-chi3, choan eng7 khui2-khiat, iong7 chin7 tok8-ke3; chit8-e7 pai7-lou7, siat-su2 hit si5 e7 hiau-heng7 bian2-si2, m7-ku2 thian-tong5 sit-loh8, mng7 sim m7 an, mia5-siaN toa7 pai7, kiN3 lang5  kian3-siau3; iu7 koh chiong-lai5 leng5-hun5 oh tit-tioh8 pi3-iu7, put-chi2 thang kho2-sioh!  
 
Tui3 an2-ni thang lioh8-a2 kong2-khi2 kui2- na7 hang7 lai5 keng2-kai3 lang5, phi3-ju7 u7 chit- ho7 lang5 bo5 pin5-sim kong2-oe7, pheng5-lun7  lang5 bo5 chiau3 kong-to7; hoan7-su7 bo5 seng2- chhat si7-hui, khi2-thau5 put-ko3 sio2-khoa2 e5 chha-chhok khah bo5 iau3-kin2, lou7-boe2  chiu7 koan3-si3 seng5 chu7-jian5, ju2 ku2 ju2 hai7,  kian3 kong2-oe7 bo5 chit8-ku3 chin-sit8, kau3 hou7  lang5 long2 chi2 i cho3 peh8-chhat8 lam7-sam2  e5 lang5; khoaN3 u7 kian3-siau3 a2-bo5 kian3-siau3!  
Koh, lang5 chit8-si5-li5-a2 e5 liam7-thau5 ia7  tioh8 ti5-hong5.  Na7 u7 bo5 chheng-khi3 e5 liam7- thau5 seN-khi2, chhin-chhiuN7 pang3 hoe2-chheng3 chit8-iuN7, pang3 hoe2-chheng3 tioh8  saN-hang7 chiau5-pi7; 1, chheng3; 2, hoe2-ioh8;  3, hoe2; khiam3 chit8 hang7 chiu7 be7-eng7-tit;  hoe2 chit8-e7 chhuh tioh8 hoe2-ioh8, chheng3 chiu7 tan5, chiong hoe2 chhe3 hoa, chheng3  chiu7 be7 hiang2.  Lang5 teh cho3 su7, u7 sin-khu, u7 sim, u7 liam7-thau5; sim seN-khi2  liam7-thau5, liam7-thau5 chit8-e7 hoat, sin-khu than3 i-e5 ho7-heng7, liam7-thau5 bo5 seN-khi2, sin-khu tek-khak be7 khi3 kiaN5, na7 e7  ap-che3 bo5 chheng-khi3 e5 liam7-thau5, sin-khu tek-khak bo5 cho3 put-hoat e5 su7-toan, chhin-chhiuN7 chhe3-hoa hoe2, chheng3 bo5  lou7-eng7, chiu7 be7 hai7 lang5 lah.  Lang5 tioh8  sam3-su iau2 u7 kek8 phaiN2 e5 hoat-tou7, chiu7- si7 choan oe7 chhun-kiong-tou5, kek8 chin7 ki- khiau2 e5 kang-hu, ng3-bang7 than3 toa7 chiN5,  bo5 siuN7 lang5 khoaN3 tioh8 che tou5 e7 pai7-hoai7  sim-sut8, chhoa7 lang5 im5-iok8 bo5 pat8-hang7  pi2 che khah siong-tiong7; in-ui7 lang5 khoaN3  tioh8 chit-ho7 tou5, put-lun7 kiaN5 che7 long2  teh siuN7 bo5 an-hun7 siu2-ki2 e5 su7, ti3-kau3 lam5-jin5 sit tek, hu7-li2 pai7 chiat, piN3 cho3  khim5-siu3, put seng5 jin5-lui7; che long2 tui3  oe7-tou5 hou7 lang5 an2-ni, kiam2 m7 si7 kek8 phaiN2?  
Koh, chhin-chhiuN7 poah8-kiau2 put-chi2  pai7-hoai7 lang5 e5 ki2-tong7, ka-ti7 chit8 lang5  am3-pou7 e5 ke3-bou5, phah-phian3 cheng3 lang5  e5 chiN5-chai5, sui-jian5 u7 si5 khun2 che7-che7  chiN5 ka-ti7 tek-i3, ia7 si7 u7 mia5-siaN; kau3 boe2  sin-khu long2 pai7, sim7-chi3 kheng-ke, hou7  lang5-lang5 phui3-tham5 phui3-noa7; an2-ni  iau2 u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek?  Koh, u7 iau2 khah li7-hai7, iau2 khah thang  kiaN--e5, chiu7-si7 chiah8 a-phian3, tak8 lang5  long2 chai si7 m7-ho2, long2 kiaN hou7 i hai7-tioh8;  na7-si7 khi2-thau5 kiam2-chhai2 in-ui7 sio2-khoa2  phoa3-peN7 bo5 thang i-ti7, siuN7 chiah8 kui2- poah-a2 hun, chiam7-si5 bian2 kan-khou2; a2- si7 kiam2-chhai2 in-ui7  
peng5-iu2 ho2-le2 sio- chhiaN2, the be7-tit-khi3, chiam7-chiah8 tam7- poh8 bian2 sit-loh8 i-e5 ho2-i3; hit si5 khiok bo5  m7-ho2 e5 i3-su3, chiam7-chiam7 koan3-si3 kau3  ku2 bo5 chiah8 be7 cho3-tit, chiu7 siuN7 beh  chng3-chau2, i2-keng chhin-chhiuN7 si2-choa5  tiN5-kha, bo5 ke3 thang pak lut; chiu7 soa3  kau3 hiat-khi3 kou-ta5, cheng-sin5 siau-soaN3,  sin-the2 sit lat8, soa3 lian2-chun2 chhin-chhiuN7  si2-sin-si chit8-iuN7; chai thang siuN7 chho3  to put-kip8 lah, beh siaN2 hoat ta-oa5 ah!  
Koh, chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 u7 choe7-ko3, pun2  chai tioh8 sun7-than3 Kiu3-chu2, chin-sim  hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3, m7-ku2 sim-lai7 iau2-ku2  khng3 che7-che7 iu5-i5 put-hoat e5 i3-su3, sui-u7  che7-che7 ho2 peng5-iu2 sio-khng3, chiu7 the-thok pat8 jit8--teh, kau3 pat8-jit8 iu5-goan5 si7  an2-ni, chhi3-siuN7 lang5 oah8 u7 loa7-ku2 leh, chit8-jit8-a2 koe3 chit8-jit8, chit8-bak8-nih8 ku2  lau7 beh kau3 lah, kau3 lau7 chiaN3 khang-khang teh pi-siong, chin thang kho2-sioh!   Ho5-hong3 lang5 oah8 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8, siat-su2 toan2-siu7 cha2 si2, chiu7 siuN7 beh sin3-than3  hoan2-hoe2 ke2-piN3 ia7 put-kip8 lah, lang5 tek-khak tioh8 liong7-cha2 koat-toan3 chu2 chhai5.  
Chi2 teng2-tin7 sou2 kong2 kui2-hang7, long2 si7 thang kiaN e5 su7, kha-pou7 soah m7-tu2-ho2,  chiu7 chit8-si3-lang5 hai7; chiu7-si7 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 nge7-sim m7 siu7 kau3-hoa3 e5 siau3- lian5-lang5, kou3-i3 khi3 kiaN5 ti7 peng-sng bo5  kian-teng7 e5 sou2-chai7, lui7 kau3 sin-khu si2 bo5.
