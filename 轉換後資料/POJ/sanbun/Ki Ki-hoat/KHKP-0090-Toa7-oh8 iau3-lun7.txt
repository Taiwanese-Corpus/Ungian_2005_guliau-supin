
Toa7-oh8 iau3-lun7
Ki Ki-hoat
 Bat siuN7 kou2-cha2 e5 Ong5-chia2 kian3-lip8 kok, bo5 chit8-hang7 pi2 hak8-hau7 khah chhiat-iau3. In-ui7 peh-siN3 bo5 thak8, chiu7 m7 chai to7-li2, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 gek8 bo5 tiau-tok , chiu7 be7 chiaN5 khi3-ku7 . Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 khoah-toa7 siat hak8-hau7, ti7 ke--nih8 u7 siok8-oh8, ti7 hiong-tong2 u7 siong5 su7 e5 oh8 , ti7 kok chiu7 u7 kok-lai7 e5 oh8 . Sui-si7 u7 chit kui2-teng2 e5 oh8 , chong2 iah8 u7 hun toa7-se3 . Lang5 chhut- si3 peh-hoe3, chiu7 jip8 sio2-oh8 , thau5-seng ka3- si7 i se2-sau3, in3-tui3 , chin3-the3 , le2-gak8, sia7- chiN3, khan-chhia , sng3-siau3 . Kau3 chap8- gou7-hoe3 , chiu7 jip8 toa7-oh8 , hap8-sip8 siu-sin , che5-ka , ti7-kok e5 to7-li2 . Ong5-chia2 sou2 iau3- kin2 siat-hoat hak8-hau7 , bo5-m7-si7 ai3 i-e5 kok chiaN5 cho3 bun5-bek8 e5 kok , peh-siN3 cho3-chiaN5 sun5-liong5 e5 peh-siN3 . Kou2-cha2 u7 chiah-e5 oh8 , chhian-ban7 m7 tat8-tioh8 lan2 Seng3-hoe7 kok- teng2 e5 oh8 . Lan2 Seng3-hoe7 e5 oh8 , siat u7 siau2, tiong, tai7, tok8-tok8 choe3 iau3-kin2--e5, chiu7-si7 Toa7-oh8 , si7 koan-he7 Siong7-te3-kok e5 kek8-chhiat . Kou2-cha2 e5 Toa7-oh8 put-ko3 si7 ti7-li2 pun2-kok nia7-tiaN7 . Hian7-kim Seng3- hoe7 e5 Toa7-oh8 , si7 peng5-tiaN7 se3-kan e5 lou7- eng7 . Hiap8-lek8 ai3 hou7 Siong7-te3 e5 kok ti7 se3-kan chiaN5-cho3 Seng3 e5 kok ,peh-siN3 chiaN5- cho3 kun-chu2 e5 khi3-khai3 . Toa7-oh8 e5 iau3- kin2 kau3 an2-ni, kiam2 u7 chit8-ho7 bu5-ti e5 lang5 m7 ai3 thak8 mah? chhiaN2 koh teng5- thau5 lai5 lun7--i . Tai7-liok8 u7 saN-hang7 .
I.Beh jip Toa7-oh8 tioh8 tai7-seng sam3-su .
Hoan7 kok-hang7 tai7-chi3 tek-khak tioh8 tai7- seng phah-sng3 , jian5-au7 chiah e7-chiaN5 . Siat-su2 bo5 siuN7 lai5 cho3--e5 , be7-e7 bo5 chhin- chhiuN7 gong7 khi2-thah e5 lang5 , e7 phah-te2 , be7-e7 chhiaN5-boe2 , i-e5 gong7-mia5 liu5-thoan5 kau3 ti7 hou7-se3 . Sou2-i2 beh jip8 Toa7-oh8 e5 lang5, iau3-kin2 tioh8 tai7-seng siuN7 .  
 
Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 chun5-sim houN3-li7, chiah beh lai5 thak8, ng3-bang7 kau3 chhut- |oh8 e5 si5 , tak8-goeh8 sou2-nia2 e5 sok-kim liau7- leng7 koa3 u7 chhun, che m7-si7 keng3-ui3 Ia5- ho5-hoa chin e5 ti3-hui7, m7-bat Seng3-keng e5 ian5-kou3 lah! Kiam2 bo5 thak8-tioh8 Seng3-keng kong2, “ Hou3-li7 si7 ban7-ok e5 goan5-thau5 , u7 thang chiah8-chheng7 chiu7 tioh8 ti- chiok , na7 tou5-bou5 ho2-giah8 , si7 ham7-loh8 be5- hek8 , chng3-jip8 lo5-bang7 , tim5-lek8 ti7 bo5-li2 , u7 sun2-hai7 e5 su-iok8 , kau3-boe2 tek-khak tim5-lun5 “ . Kou2-cha2 lang5 iah8 u7 kong2 , i khoaN3 hu3-kui3 chhin-chhiuN7 phu5-hu5 . Ho5- hong3 lan2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 Chu2 e5 tiau3, u7 au7-lai5 e5 toa7-po3 thang tit , kiam2 iau2-ku2 chu-chu ui7-tioh8 li7 , sim hou7 chai5 sou2-chhe mah?  Hit-ho7 e5 lang5 Chu2 teh-beh leng2- chhio3 i kong2 , “ Bu5-ti e5 lang5 ah! Kim-mi5 beh tho2 li2 e5 leng5-hun5, chiN5-chai5 beh kui ti7 sim2-mih8 lang5 ,kiam2-e7 soa3 toa3 khi3 mah?”  
Kiam2-chhai2 iah8 u7 lang5 teh siuN7 , lai5 Toa7-oh8 sou2-hui3 bo5 sim2-mih8 kau3 , tui3 an2-ni chiu7 m7-ai3 lai5 . Chit-ho7 e5 lang5 ,goa2 m7-kaN2 koat-toan3 si7-hui . Sui-jian5 si7 ui7-tioh8 chai5 lai5 m7 kam-goan7 hou7 Chu2 eng7 , goa2 bo5 tek- khah kaN2 kong2 i si7 tham-li7 e5 lang5 . Lan2 na7 eng7 Seng3-tou5 Po2-lo5 e5 khou2-keng2 , lai5 bian2-le7--i ; koh-khah biau7--e5 , chiong Chu2 it-seng ti7 se3-kan bo5 khoa3 chim2-thau5 e5 sou2-chai7 lai3 chai-poe5--i , i kiam2-chhai2 beh in2-khi2 Seng3-keng kong2 , Chhiu2 hoaN7 le5- boe2 , m7-thang oat8-thau5 khoaN3 au7-bin7 , goa2 kiaN-liau2 be7-e7 an2-ni , sou2-i2 chiah m7-kaN2 khi3 thak8 . Seng3-keng kong2 , Bo7 gou7-kak e5 gu5 m7-thang lang2 i-e5 chhui3 . Na7 hou7 i iau , beh thai3 u7-lat8 thang thoa-bo7 ah？ Chiu7 tioh8-boa5 e5 lang5 na7 khoan2-thai7 i put- chiok , be7-e7 chin7-tiong ti7 i-e5 chit , iah8 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni , sou2-i2 Toa7-oh8 e7 kau3 chiah leng2-tam7 , kaN2-si7 ui7-tioh8 an2-ni teh ?  
TaN in-ui7 goa2 chit-tiap8 ki-ti7 Toa7-oh8 chi3 bi5-se3 e5 chit8-e5 , sou2-i2 goa2 chiah ho2-taN2 kong2 . Siat-su2 goa2 chit-chun7 bo5 ti7 Toa7- oh8 , chiu7 chhian-ban7 m7-kaN2 kong2 chhut- chhui3 . Hoan7 goa2 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 , bo5 lun7 ti7 i2-keng bat siong-hoe7--e5 , a2-si7 boe7 saN- kiN3-bin7--e5 , m7-thang khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 kong2 an2-ni lai5 ka-ti7 an-ui3 , phah-sng3 goa2 si7 teh the2-thiap lin2 e5 sim ; goa2 bo5 beh . Sou2 chhiat-iau3--e5 , tioh8 hian3 pun2-sin cho3 oah8 e5 che3 . Ia5-sou ui7-tioh8 lan2 pang3-sak seng- khu , ho5-hong3 lan2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 i-e5 in hiah-toa7 , chiu7 hian3 chit8-sin hou7 i eng7, kiam2 si7 koe3-thau5 mah？  
TaN sou2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 tai7-seng phah- sng3--e5 , chiu7 tioh8 siuN7 kong2 , Ia5-sou thiaN3 goa2 ,goa2 bo5 in thang tap--i , tek-khak ai3 chiong chit8-sin thok-tiong7 i an-pai5 , sui- si7 u7 kan-khou2 , ng3-bang7 au7-lai5 e5 hok-khi3 , khah-iaN5 se3-kan e5 tai7-chi3 kau3 u7-chhun . U7 chit-teng2 e5 lang5 , si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 hoaN- hi2 kam3-lap8 e5 che3-su7 . Ti7 teng2-bin7 Siong7- te3 hoaN-hi2,ti7 e7-bin7 lang5-lang5 bo5-m7 kiong2- chhiu2 kau3 thau5-hiah8 , chheng-hou kong2 , Thang kiong-hi2 ah！thang kiong-hi2 ah ! 
II.Jip8 Toa7-oh8 e5 li7-ek m7-si7 sio2-khoa2 .
TaN ti7 toa7--e5 , chiu7 u7 tit-tioh8 toa7 e5 li7-ek . Ti7 se3--e5 chiu7 sou2 tit e5 li7-ek iah8 si7 sio2-khoa2 . Sou2-i2 khai-siat Toa7-oh8 chai-poe5 jin5-chai5 , hou7 i e7-beng5 che5-ka ti7-kok e5 to7-li2 , chit-e5 li7-ek kiam2-si7 sio2-khoa2?Kou2-cha2 e5 Toa7-oh8 put-ko3 oa2-kho3 gau5-lang5 e5 ka3-si7 , siong7- chhiaN2 li7-ek toa7 . Chiu7 hian7-kim kau3- hoe7 e5 Toa7-oh8 , u7 Chu2 e5 toa7 khui3-lat8 hu5- chou7 , sou2 siu7 e5 li7-ek , e7 bo5 khah toa7 mah？  
Chiu7 lang5 lai5 lun7--i , khi2-thau5 kian3-lip8 Seng3-hoe7 e5 si5 , ui7-tioh8 kang khoeh , kiN3 na7 u7 hiaN-ti7 khah u7 thang kho2-chhu2--e5 , e7-hiau2 liam7 si , thak8 bo5 loa7-che7 e5 Seng3- keng , tam7-poh8 e7-hiau2 ke2-seh , bo5 khoa3 hou7 i jip8 Toa7-oh8 , chiu7 hou7 i jim7-chit . Sui u7 thang kho2-chhu2 , na7-si7 iah8 lioh8-lioh8-a2 u7 khah-su . Si7 ui7-tioh8 bo5 ki-hoe7 ti7 toa7- lang5 e5 oh8 e5 ka3-si7 , tit-tioh8 toa7-lang5 e5 li7- ek e5 ian5-kou3 . Che bo5 ki5-koai3 , tak8 sou2-chai7 khi2-thau5 kian3-lip8 kau3-hoe7 e5 si5 iah8 an2-ni .  
Kau3 ti7 i2-hou7 kang khah liau7-leng7 , chiu7 e7-poe3 e5 lang5 u7 thang khah siong5-se3 siu7 tioh Toa7-oh8 e5 li7-ek ,goa2 khoaN3 seng5-sit8 u7 pin5-ki3 . Ti7 tek-heng7 e5 tiong-kan , chiu7 khah m7-kaN2 phoe-pheng5 , lun7-kau3 Seng3-keng kap hak8-bun7 khah u7 iat-lek8 , si7 goa2 ho2-taN2 kong2--i . Che m7 tan7-na7 goa2 kong2 u7 chit-ho7 cheng5-iaN2 , chiu7 iah8 be7-e7 chau2- tit lin2 e5 bak8-chiu sou2 kam3-chhat.  TaN lin2 i2-keng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Toa7-oh8 e5 kong-hau7 ,kiam2 chiah chiau3 lin2 sou2 khoaN3- kiN3 an2-ni nia7-tiaN7 , iau2 u7 koh-khah toa7 . Na7 in-khin5 siu7-ka3 ,chiong-lai5 un2-tang3 be7 kim3-tit ban7-lui2 bak8-chiu kiah8 khi2-thau5 hou7 lang5 sou2 going-bong7 . Lin2 na7 m7-sin3 cho3 lin2 lai5 thak8, sui-jian5 si7 liah8-thou3 e5 bu2- hu , iah8 kham-tit chheng cho3 Seng3-hoe7 kan-seng5 e5 lang5 . Goan7 hoe7-pek na7 u7 chu2-te7-tit8, tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 A-pek-liap8-han2, kam-goan7 hian3 i-e5 kiaN2 hou7 Siong7-te3 . Chi2 ho2 peng5-iu2, tioh8 oh8 I2-sai3-a thiaN-kiN3 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 siaN , kong2 ai3 chhe-lang5 , sui5- si5 in3-kong2 , goa2 ti7-chia, chhe--goa2 thang mah？ Chit-si5 chiaN3-si7 kip8-si5 e5 ki-hoe7 , goan7 boh8-tit che7-teh bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 chit-ho7 toa7 li7-ek hou7 i tit8-tit8 koe3- khi3 , lai5 m7 po2-sioh .  
III . Sou2 tam-tng e5 chit si7 chun-kui3 .
Koh taN thiN-e7 e5 tiong-kan, ban7-hang7 mih8 u7 kui3-chian7 e5 hun-piat . Ti7 jin5-cheng5-tiong, u7 jin5-cheng5-tiong e5 hun-piat. Ti7 chit-jim7, u7 chit-jim7 e5 hun-piat . Goa2 sou2 kong2 u2-tiu7-lai7 long2 u7 chun-pi e5 hun-piat, seng5-sit8 si7 an2-ni kong2 . Koh goa2 kong2 chai7 ti7 sou2 tam-tng e5 chit si7 chun-kui3, che si7 chaiN2-iuN7 kong2 ah?  Chiu7 goa7-lang5 khoaN3 lan2 hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5 sou2 jim7 e5 chit, si7 siau2-kiok8 koh siau2-kiok8, si7 chaiN2-iuN7 ah?  In-ui7 jim7-chit e5 lang5 khah-che7 si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 hak8-bun7, bo5 sim2-mih8 khah koh-iuN7 ti7 long5-hu . Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 siN-khi2 thi2- chhio3 e5 liam7-thau5. Sui-jian5 si7 chin-chu e5 kui3-khi3, in khoaN3-cho3 hi5-bak8-chiu e5 chhau3-chian7. Su-put-ti chit-chun , ui7-koan5--e5 chiaN3-si7 tam-tng chit-chit e5 lang5 . 
Chiu7 kau3-hoe7-lai7 bo5 sim2-mih8 chhim- chai to7-li2 e5 lang5 khoaN3 chit-e5 chit lai5 kong2--i . Sui-si7 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 long2 m7- bat to7-li2 e5 lang5 , khoaN3 cho3 chin peng5- siong5 , iah8 bo5 kau3-giah8 beng5-pek8 chit-e5 chit-e5 kek8-chhiat . I put-ko3 si7 kong2 , che si7 cho3 ho2-su7, si3-ke3 po3 lang5 bat to7-li2 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 nia7-tiaN7 . Tok8-tok8 ti7 chhim-chai to7-li2 e5 lang5, chiu7-e7 beng5-pek8 chit-e5 chit e5 kin-te2 . KhoaN3 cho3 thong se3-kan bo5 koh u7 pat8-mih8 chit, pi2 chit-e5 koh khah chun-kui3 . Che kiam2-si7 bian2-kiong2 o-lo2 mah?  M7-si7 ; chhui-chhut kou2-cha2 e5 Toa7-oh8 kau3 chhut-oh8 e5 si5 , lim2-lim2 si7 e7 ti7-li2 se3-kan e5 kok niaN7-tiaN7 . Na7-si7 hian7-kim kau3-hoe7- lai7 e5 Toa7-oh8 , kau3 chhut-khi3 jim7-chit e5 si5, si7 koan-he7 Siong7-te3 e5 kok, chek-seng5 peh-siN3 e5 leng5-hun5. Sou2-i2 u7-si5 lo5-sim , u7-si5 lo5-lek8, kiam2 e7 kim3-tit bo5-beh chin2-khi2 lang5 chiuN7 to7-hoaN7 , put-jim2 khoaN3-kiN3 i tim5-lek8 , lai5 m7 khi3 khan-kiu3--i mah？  
U7-si5 siu7-piN,kau3 e7 hou7 peh-siN3 tit-tioh8 i-e5 u2-hoa3. U7-si5 phoe-mia7, ti3-kau3 lang5- lang5 piN3-cho3 jin5-ai3 e5 hong-siok8 . Chhin- chhiuN7 an2-ni, na7 u7 lang5 kong2 si7 peng5- siong5 e5 chit-hun7, si7 tiong7 goa7-phoe5 e5 ian5-kou3 . KhoaN3-kiN3 jim7-chit e5 lang5 i-chiuN5 phok-sou3, chiu7 phah-sng3 si7 bo5 he7-loh8 e5 lang5.  Kiam2 m7-chai sian-ti I2-li7-a kap Iok-han7,sui-jian5 si7 chheng7 sou3-sou3 e5 i-chiuN5, lan2 kiam2 kaN2 kong2 in si7 peng5-siong5 lang5 , sou2 jim7 e5 chit m7-si7 chun-kui3 mah？  
Chin7-chai7 koan5--e5 si7 chit-e5 ui7 , chin7- chai7 khoah--e5 si7 chit-e5 chit . Chit-tiap8 hou7 lang5 khoaN3 cho3 peng5-siong5, au7-lai5 e7 beng5-beng5 khoaN3-kiN3 i hiong2-siu7 chun-kui3 e5 ui7 .  
TaN chit-e5 chit hiah chun-kui3 , hoan7 ai3 tam-tng e5 lang5 , Chu2 kio3--i kong2 , Lai5 . Siong7-te3 e5 kok u7 chhun e5 ui7 , chhiaN2 lin2 kiah8-thau5 khoaN3 chhan5 , gou7-kak sek8 lah , sit chin che7 , kang kek8-chio2 ,taN tioh8 koaN2- kin2 chhut lai5 tau3 siu-seng5 .  
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-poe3 te7 90 koan3, 1892 ni5 10 geh8)
