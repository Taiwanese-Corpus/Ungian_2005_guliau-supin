
THIAN-CHIN LAN7 -BAN7
Thok-ni2-su-thai3 chok
Hui7-kong ek8
Nng7 e5 cha-bou2  gin2-na2 ti7 nng7 keng chhu3-kha e5 sio2 lou7-a2 sio tu2-tioh8; tui3 chhan5-nih lau5 lai5 e5 lok8-kou-be5 chui2, long2 lau5 kau3 ti7 chit tiau5 sio2 lou7-a2, pian3 choe3 chit8 e5 chui2-khut.  Chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 chin soe3-han3, iau2 chit8 e5 khah toa7-han3 chit8 sut-a2.  In nng7 lang5 e5 lau7-bu2 long2 ka7 in chheng7 sin e5 saN.  Soe3-han3 e5 chheng7 chit8 nia2 na5-sek--e5, toa7-han3 e5 chheng7 chit8 nia2 ng5-sek--e5; koh in nng7 e5 ti7 in e5 thau5-mng5 long2 u7 pak8 chit8 lui2 e5 ang5-sek e5 iah8-a2.  Tai7-ke ti7 lou7-nih tu2-tioh8 e5 si5, si7 tu2-tu2 tui3 pai3-tng5 tng2-lai5, khi2-thau5 in teh tian2 in e5 sui2 saN, au7-lai5 saN-kap ti7 hia thit-tho5.  Hut-jian5-kan in m7-chai siuN7 an2-choaN2, siuN7 tioh8 beh loh8-khi3 chui2-khut-nih sng2 chui2.  Soe3-han3 e5 chheng7 oe5 chu7 an2-ni siuN7 beh liau5 loh8-khi3, toa7-han3 e5 ka7 i tong3 kong2:
“M7-thang an2-ni tah8 loh8-khi3, A-bi2,an2-ni lin2 a-bu2 oe7 ka7 li2 me7!  Goa2 beh thng3 oe5 khi2-lai5, li2 ma7 thng3 li2 e5 oe5 khi2-lai5.”
In thng3 oe5 khi2-lai5, lang2 kun5 chiu7 liau5 loh8-khi3.  Chui2 kau3 A-bi2 e5 kha-bak8, i chiu7 hoah kong2:
“A-li2-a2, chui2 hiah-nih chhim goa2 oe7 kiaN lah!”
“Lai5 ! ” A-li2-a2 ka7 i in3, chit8 bin7 siuN7 beh chhun chhiu2 ka7 i khan, m7-ku2 kiaN-liau2 kun5 pang3 loh8-khi3 oe7 ko7 tioh8 chui2, “M7-bian2 kiaN lah, khut-a2 boe7 koh-khah chhim a3-lah.”
 
I beh kiaN5 oa2-khi3 e5 si5, A-li2-a2 ka7 A-bik2ong:  “A-bi2, li2 tioh8 kiaN5 hou7 ho2 ou5, m7-thang that chui2.”
Tu2-tu2 an2-ni teh kong2 e5 si5, A-bi2 e5 kha that tioh8 chui2; chui2 phun3 tioh8 A-li2-a2 e5 saN, ia7 phun3 tioh8 i e5 bak8-chiu kap phiN7.  A-li2-a2 khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 saN la5-sam5 khi3, siu7-khi3 khi2-lai5, jiok beh phah A-bi2.  A-bi2 ka-ki chai, ia7 chin kiaN, koaN2-kin2 peh khi2-lai5, siuN7 beh chau2 tng2-khi3 chhu3-nih.  A-li2-a2 in ma-ma ti7 chit e5 si5 tu2-tu2 tui3 chia keng-ke3, khoaN3-tioh8 in cha-bou2-kiaN2 chit8 seng-khu bak kah ou so5-so5, chiu7-kong2:
“Li2 chit e5 chok-giat8 gin2-na2, li2 si7 an3-choaN2 bak kah chit8 seng-khu ou so5-so5!”
“A-bi2 tiau5-kou3-i3 ka7 lang5 chhong3 e5 lah!”  A-li2-a2 thiaN tioh8 ma-ma e5 siaN chiu7 bo5 koh jiok A-bi2, eng7 an2-ni in3 i e5 lau7-bu2.
A-li2-a2 e5 lau7-bu2 thiaN tioh8, chiu7 tui3 A-bi2 ka7 khiu2-teh, tui3 i am7-kun2 e5 au7-bin7 ka7 i liam3 kui2-na7 e7.
A-bi2 khau3 chin toa7 siaN thong koe-lou7 e5 lang5 long2 u7 thiaN-kiN3, A-bi2 e5 lau7-bu2 chiu7 chhut-lai5.
“Li2 si7 an2-choaN2 phah goan2 cha-bou2 kiaN2?”  I an2-ni jiong2, chiu7 khai-si2 tit8-tit8 me7 A-li2-a2 in lau7-bu2.  In nng7 e5 khai-si2 sio-me7.  Ta-pou lang5 ia7 chhut-lai5; koe-lou7 nih sui5-si5 ui5 kah ou-kham5-kham5, hi7-hoa7-kio3, bo5 lang5 teh thiaN pat8-lang5 kong2-oe7.  In tit8-tit8 me7, khiu2 lai5 khiu2 khi3,  chha-put-to kiong5 beh sio phah khi2-lai5, A-li2-a2 e5  a-ma2 chit8-pou7 chit8-pou7 kiaN5 lai5 kau3 tiong-ng, beh lai5 ka7 in kong2-ho5.
“Lin2 ma7 siuN7 khoaN3-mai7 leh!  Kin2-a2-jit8 si7 Koh-oah8-choeh, ma7 m7-thang an2-ni tiam3 ti7 chia ti7-teh sit the2-bian7, hiah-nih gong7 ti7-teh thin7 gin2-na2 piN.”
 
In long2 bo5 beh thiaN a-ma2 e5 oe7, kiong5-kiong5 beh ka7 i kheh to2-loh8-khi3.
In teh sio-me7 e5 si5, A-li2-a2 jip8-khi3 chhu3-lai7, i2-keng chiong la5-sam5 chhit chheng-khi3, koh chau2 khi3 chui2-khut-a2 e5 sou2-chai7.  I theh8 chit8 liap8 piN2-piN2 e5 chioh8-thau5 tui3 chui2-khut-a2 e5 piN-a2 ou2 chit8 choa7 kau, chui2 lau5 tui3 lou7-nih lai5.  A-bi2 ia7 theh8 chit8 te3 pang-a2 chhut-lai5, teh ka7 A-li2-a2 tau3 chhong3.  In hiah e5 lang5 na2 toa7 siaN, teh beh sio-phah e5 si5, khut-a2 e5 chui2 i2-keng lau5 kau3 a-ma2 khia7 e5 sou2-chai7.  Nng7 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 te3 hit e5 chui2 na2 hoah na2 chau2.
“A-bi2 jiok hou7 tioh8!  Jiok hou7 tioh8!”  A-li2-a2 tit8-tit8 hoah, na2 hoah na2 chhio3, tit8-tit8 chau2 tui3 chit tin7 lang5 chia lai5.
Pang-a2 ti7 chui2-teng2 teh lau5, gin2-na2 khoaN3 liau2 na2 hoaN-hi2, na2 chhio3.  A-ma2 khoaN3-tioh8 in nng7 e5, tui3 hiah e5 lang5 an2-ni kong2:
“Lin2 an2-ni kiam2 boe7 kian3-siau3, hiah-nih ai3 thin7 gin2-na2 piN,”  lang5 in nng7 e5 tou long2 boe7 ki3-tit lah, tiam3 ti7 hia teh thit-tho5!”  “Lin2 pi2 chiah e5 gin2-na2 khah gong7!”  “Ah!  Koai sun-a2!”
Hiah e5 lang5 khoaN3-tioh8, ka-ki7 kam2-kak chin kian3-siau3.  Tai7-ke soa3 chhio3 chhut-lai5, tak8 e5 lang5 tng2-khi3 in tau.
“Lin2 na7 bo5 oat8 to3-tng2 chhin-chhiuN7 gin2-na2 e5, koat-toan3 boe7 tit jip8 thian-kok.”    
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 894 ho7, 1962 ni5 9 geh8)
