

 
Lun7 Peh8-chhat8
Kh. Th. P.
 Chiau3 li2 phah-sng3 peh8-chhat8 chaiN2-iuN7 ?  Goa2 siuN7 hit e5 li7-hai7 m7-si7 sio2-khoa2, in-ui7  oe7 hai7 ka-ki7, ia7 oe7 hai7 pat8 lang5 , koh oe7 hai7 kau3-hoe7 bo5 the2-bian7, ia7 u7 hoan7-tioh8  Siong7-te3 e5 kai3-lut8.  Nga2-kok (3 : 2-7) u7 kong2,  “In-ui7 lan2 cheng3-lang5 ti7 choe7- choe7 hang7 poah8-to2.  Ti7 kong2 oe7 bo5 poah8- to2 e5 , chiu7-si7 choe3 oan5-choan5 e5 lang5, ia7 oe7 iok-sok i thong seng-khu.  Lan2 he7 be2-ka7-kiuN ti7 be2 e5 chhui3 hou7 i sun7-than3 lan2, chiu7 koan2-hat in thong seng-khu. Koh, li2 khoaN3 hiah e5 chun5, sui-jian5 hiah toa7 chiah, hou7 toa7-hong sau3 i, iu5- goan5 si7 eng7 soe3-soe3 e5 toa7 hou7 i tng2-oat, chiau3 tai7-kong e5 i3-su3 sou2 ai3.  An2-ni chih8, si7 soe3-soe3 e5 the2, iau2-ku2 kong2 toa7-oe7.  Li2 khoaN3, sio2-khoa2 e5 he2 sio chhiu7-bak8 joa7 choe7 ah ! Chih chiu7-si7 he2, ti7 lan2 pah- the2 tiong choe3 put-gi7 e5 se3-kai3, phah la5- sam5 thong seng-khu, it-seng e5 heng5-tong7 tui3 i hoat-he2; i ka-ki7 e5 he2 si7 tui3 te7-gek8 hoat.”  TaN lan2 kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5 kong2- oe7 na7 bo5 iok-sok i e5 chih8, chiu7-si7 sit-loh8 i e5 sin3-iong7, ti3-kau3 sou2 kong2 bo5 lang5 ai3 thiaN.  Siok8-gu2 u7 kong2,  “Kun-chu2 e5 lang5, kong2-oe7 ui5-pin5, siau2-jin5 e5 lang5 gu5- phe5 sia2-ji7 ia7 bo5 pin5.” 

(Ti5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 468 koan3, 1924 ni5 3 geh8)
