SaN-goeh8 te2, chiuN7 A-li2-san siuN2 eng-hoe, sun7-soa3 khi3 San-bi2-chhoan e5 Ta5-na2-i-ku5 chhit-tho5.
San-bi2-chhoan si7 goan5-chu7-bin5 Chou-chok8(鄒族) e5 pou7-loh8, Ta5-na2-i-ku5 goan5-chu7-bin5-gi2 e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 Bong5-iu-kok(忘憂谷), si7 oa2 ti7 Chan-bun5-khoe goan5-thau5 e5 sou2-chai7, ia7-si7 Tai5-oan5 kok-po2-hi5 ”kou3-hi5 “ e5 kou3-hiong. Koe3-khi3, San-bi2-chhoan si7 oa2-kho3 cheng3-cho7 lai5 i5-chhi5 seng-oah8, hui-siong5 san3-chhiah.

         Chap8-gou7-tang cheng5, chhoan-bin5 li7-iong7 thian-jian5 chu-goan5, choe3 ho5-chhoan po2-iok8 e5 khang-khoe3. Ui7-tioh8 beh po2-hou7 chit-tiau5 khoe-a2, jit8 kap me5 long2 u7 lang5 teh sun5-lo5. Keng-koe3 tai7-ke e5 phah-piaN3, chit-tiau5 khoe-a2 koh oah8--khi2--lai5--lah, kui-tiau5 khoe-a2-chui2 chheng-chheng-chheng, goa7-a2-nih8 che7 e5 hi5-a2 chu7-iu5-chu7-chai7 siu5-lai5-siu5-khi3, hou7 lang5 khoaN3-tioh8 chin kam2-tong7, koan-kong-kheh ju2-lai5 ju2-che7, in e5 seng-oah8 tit-tioh8 chin toa7 e5 kai2-sian7.
Koe3 bo5 joa7-ku2 ti7 Lam5-tau5 e5 Tan-tai7-khoe hoat-hian7 saN-chap8 chiah chui2-lok8-a2 e5 si-the2, khoe-a2-chui2, khoe-a2-piN, kui-phian3 e5 hoeh-jiah, lok8-jiong5 kap lok8-pian cha2 chiu7 hou7 lang5 koah--loh8--lai5, theh8--khi3--lah.
Tai5-oan5 chui2-lok8-a2 si7 Tai5-oan5 siong7 toa7 heng5 e5 chiah8 chhau2 e5 tong7-but8, sin-koan5 u5 178 kong-hun, keng-thau5 koan5 120 kong-hun, the2-heng5 koan5-toa7, mng5 e5 sek-chui2 kim koh sui2, ko3-seng3 un-sun5 ti7 chui2-piN ban7-ban7..a, chit8-chhui3 chit8-chhui3 teh chiah8 chhau2-a2, nng7-liap8 bak8-chiu iN5- lin3-lin3, thau5 khi-khi, liah8 lang5 kim-kim-khoaN3, chin sian7-liong5 koh chin kou2-chui.
M7-ku2, lok8-a2--ah lok8-a2, li2 kiam2 m7-chai li2 e5 kak chiok pou2, chiok tat8-chiN5? Li2 kiam2 m7 chai chin-che7 e5 Tai5-oan5 cha-pou-lang5 long2 hoai5-gi5 ka-ki7 pai7-sin7 to2-iong5, ai3 oa2-kho3 li2 e5 toa7 ki pian lai5 chong3-iong5?
