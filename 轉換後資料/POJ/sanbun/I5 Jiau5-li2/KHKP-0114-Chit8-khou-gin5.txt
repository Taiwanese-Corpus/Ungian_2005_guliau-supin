
Chit8-khou-gin5

Goa2 cheng5-jit8  khoaN3 chit8-khou-gin5 ti7 bou2 lang5 e5 chhiu2--nih8 chiu7 u7 siuN7 saN-hang7 , si7 chiau3 e7-te2 sou2 ki3-chai3 .   
1 . U7 siuN7 hit-e5 lang5 chaiN2-iuN7 tit-tioh8 hit chit8-khou-gin5 .  Hit-e5 gin5 sui-jian5 ti7 i  chhiu2--nih8 bo5 tek-khak si7 i-e5.  Lam7-sam2 tit-tioh8 gin5 u7 kui2-na7 khoan2 , chiong5-tiong thang kong2 , －si7 chhiuN2--lang5 , si7 thau-theh8--lang5 , si7 lek8-sek--lang5 , si7 koai2-phian3--lang5 , si7 khiau5-kho3--lang5 , si7 hoaiN5-chaN7--lang5 , a2-si7 poah8-iaN5-kiau2 kap chhut-be7 hai7-lang5 e5 mih8 , hit-ho7.  Chiah e5 phaiN2-lou7 peN5-peN5 thang hiam5 , iah8 lang5 na7 than chiah-e5 hiau-heng7 e5 ke3-bou5 chiu7  sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 gin5 koat-toan3 m7-si7 i-e5 .  I si7 phaiN2-lang5 , i si7 chhat8 .  
Hoan7-na7 tui3-tiong7 hoaiN5-chai5  Siong7-te3 m7-tih8, iah8  hit-e5  lang5 tek-khak  be7-tit-thang peng5-an .   
Tit-tioh8  hap8-gi5  e5 chiN5 bo5 lang5 thang hiam5 , iah8 Siong7-te3 e7 hoaN-hi2 , koh chit-ho7 chiN5 thang cho3 chin-sit8 e5 li7-ek .   
2 . U7 siuN7 hit-e5 lang5 chaiN2-iuN7 beh eng7 hit chit8-khou-gin5 .  Hit-e5 gin5 sui-jian5  si7 chiau3 kong-to7  tit-tioh8, chong2-si7 be7-tit-thang chhin3-chhai2 eng7 .  Lam7-sam2 khai-gin5 u7 kui2-na7 khoan2 , chiong5-tiong thang kong2 ,－phoa3-chiN5, eng7-gong7-chiN5 , poah8-kiau2 , than3 su-iok8 , chhia-hoa5 , pou3-lou7-lang5, hai7 pat8-lang5 , li7-ek  ka-ti7 na7-tiaN7 .  Lun7-kau3 liau2-chiN5 u7 iau3-kin2 e5 pun2-hun7 kap toa7 e5 koan-he7 .  Lang5 m7-thang phah-sng3 in-ui7 i sou2 u7-e5 si7 kong-gi7 e5 chiN5 , sou2-i2 thang chhut-chai7 i eng7 . Hit-ho7 chiN5 si7 na2-chhin-chhiuN7 tui3 Siong7-te3 lai5 , si7  Siong7-te3 kau-tai7--e5 . An2-ni-seN , lang5 eng-kai tioh8 chiau3 Siong7-te3 e5 i3-su3 lai5 khai , in-ui7  kau3 pat8-jit8 Siong7-te3 beh chha5-siau3.  U7 che7-che7 ho2-giah8 e5 lang5 tui3  i-e5 chiN5-chai5 siu7-tioh8 toa7-hai7 , iah8 u7 che7-che7 song3-hiong lang5 tui3 i-e5 bo5 sim2-mih8 chiN5 siu7-tioh8 toa7 li7-ek .   
U7-chiN5 e5 lang5 tioh8 the2-thiap bo5-chiN5--e5 , na7-bo5 , be7-bian2-tit siu7 ok-po3-KhoaN3 Lou7-ka xvI sou2 ki3-chai3 , hit-e5 ho2-giah8-lang5 kap Liap8-sat-lou7 khi2-thau5 kap kiat-boe2 e5 su7-cheng5 .     
3 . U7 siuN7 hit-e5 lang5 thang chun5 hit-chit8-khou-gin5 kau3 loa7-ku2 .  Hit-e5 gin5 sui-jian5 si7  beng5-beng5 ti7-teh , m7-ku2 bo5 thang eng2-oan2 khng3-teh.  Lang5  chit-tiap8  sou2 u7-e5 chiN5 kau3 si2 tioh8 lau5 hou7 pat8-lang5 ,  chiu7 ti7 si2-au7 long2  bo5  hit-ho7 .  Kho2-kian3 na7 bo5 leng7-goa7 chek-chu7 thiN--nih8  e5  chiN5-chai5 chiu7-si7 keng5-khou2  kau3-kek8.  Seng3-chheh tu2-tu2 chin-sit8 u7  kong2, “Sou2 khoaN3-kiN3--e5 si7 chiam7-si5 , sou2 be7 khoaN3-kiN3--e5 si7 ku2-tng5 .”  An2-ni-seN, lan2 tioh8 te7-it-toa7 tui3-tiong7 be7 pai7-hoai7 e5 mih8, chiu7 chai7-seN si2-au7 long2 u7 thang hiong2-siu7 hok-khi3 .   
Lun7-kau3 tak8-hang7 chiN5-chai5 e5 tai7-chi3 lan2 eng-kai tioh8 pek-chhiat  kiu5  Siong7-te3 chan7 lan2 keng2-seng2 bian2-tit u7 chho3-gou7 .Sim-sou2-goan7.                   I5 Jiau5-li2 sia2--e5.
