
Siau3-liam7 PhoaN Bok8-su
I5 Jiau5-li2 
Goa2 kin7-jit8 tui3 Tai5-oan5 chiap-tioh8 chit8 tiuN iu-bun7 e5 phoe, chiu7 chai peng5- sou3 sou2 him-bou7 e5 PhoaN Bok8-su i2-keng koe3-ong2.  Lun7-kau3 PhoaN Bok8-su sit8-chai7 si7 goa2 put-tek-put tioh8 eng7 choan5-sim lai5 thiaN3-thang3 him-bou7, chun-keng3 i chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 e5 chhin-hiaN chit8-poaN7-- lah.  Goa2 ia7 tek-khak chai ti7 Tai5-oan5 chiong3 kau3-hoe7 kap Ki-tok-tou5 bo5-m7 ui7- tioh8 i koe3-ong2 lai5 siong-pi.  SiuN7 PhoaN Bok8-su chai7-seN u7 che7-che7 ho2 tek-heng7. I e5 chun5-sim si5-siong5 tek-khak beh chhoe7 tak8-ho7 e5 ki-hoe7 lai5 li7-ek kau3-hoe7 lai7-goa7 e5 lang5.  Koh i ho2 tek-heng7 tiong-kan kai3 hian2-beng5--e5, chiu7-si7 seng5-sim ki5-to2 che7-che7 si5; pat8-lang5 i2-keng khun3,i3 iau2- ku2 teh ki5-to2.  Che tai7-chi3 sit8-chai7 si7 phang-phang thang chheng3-kau3 thiN- teng2, siong7-te3 tiaN7-tioh8 hoaN hi2 chiap- lap8, in-ui7 i u7 hit-ho7 keng3-khian5 e5 sim-- lah.  Na7-si7 i iu7-koh si5-si5 choan-bu7 hak8- sip8 Siong7-te3 e5 Seng3-keng, che hoat sit8- chai7 e7 kap Siong7-te3 siong-thong, ka-ti7 iah8 e7-tit u7 chai5-leng5 khui3-lat8 lai5 pang- chou7 pat8-lang5 leng5-hun5 e5 khiam3-khoeh. Sou2-i2 bo5 lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5, na7 bo5 si5-siong5 ki5-to2 khin5-khin5 thak8 Seng3-keng, chiu7 be7 tit-thang ho7-cho3 Siong7-te3 e5 ho2 lou5-pok8-- lah.  
Lun7 PhoaN Bok8-su cho3 lang5, tai7-khai3 sou2 cho3 kok hang7 su7, pit-teng7 liu5-sin5 chu3-sim, chiaN3 ti3-kau3 e7 seng5-kong. SiuN7 i ti7 Toa7-oh8 e5 si5, chap8-hun pek-chhiat thak8-chheh, sou2-i2 su7-su7 long2 e7 iaN5-koe3 tang5-phoaN7.  Kau3 i cho3 Thoan5-to7 sian-seN e5 si5, chin gau5 kam2-hoa3 lang5 e5 sim, bo5 lun7 i sou2 kau3 to2-ui7 e5 hoe7-tng5, to long2 tit- tioh8 che7 lang5 lai5 thiaN chin-li2. Koh khng3 lang5 keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3 kai3 u7 ho2 hoat-tou7, iu7 pek-chhiat bian2-le7 lang5 chhut-lat8 lai5 pang-chan7 Seng3-hoe7. Tng i ti7 Tiong-oh8 cho3 sian-seN e5 si5, ka3 chheh chap8-hun chhut-lat8 hou7 hak8-seng toa7 tit-tioh8 li7-ek; koh-khah iau3-kin2--e5, ai3 in2-chhoa7 hak8-seng kin2-kin2 kui Ia5-sou.  Chiau3 goa2 teh siuN7, chhin-chhiuN7 chit-ho7 chin7-lek8 e5 hu3- su7 chin-chiaN3 si7 lan5-tek--lah.  
Tng Tai5-lam5 taN-chiah siat Tai7-hoe7 e5 si5, i chap8-hun chin7-lat8 pang-chou7, in-ui7  
i u7 toa7 kian3-sek, sou2-i2 u7 toa7 kong-hau7, e7- tit seng5-chiu7 sou2 siat e5 sin hoat-tou7.  Kau3 Tai7-hoe7 soan2 i cho3 Bok8-su liau2-au7, kang- hu koh khah toa7 lo5-hoan5, in-ui7 i sou2 tioh8 liau7-li2--e5 u7 kau2-ui7 hoe7-tng5, tok8-tok8 i bo5 hiam5 kan-khou2, koat-i3 ai3 chin7 i e5 pun2- hun7.  Na7-si7 an2-ni lan2 chiah-e5 sou2 thiaN3- thang3 him-bou7 e5 PhoaN Bok8-su kham hou7 che7-che7 lang5 chun-keng3, in-ui7 i siong5- siong5 hian2-chhut un-jiu5 khiam-sun3 e5 bou5- iuN7.  Koh kok-su7 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 tai7- seng sou2 chhim thiaN3 e5 Thou5 Bok8-su chit8- poaN-iuN7, ia7 thang ho7-cho3 kap Mou5-se siang7 chit8-lui7 e5 lang5--lah.  
Put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 na7 kap i saN-bat tek-khak o-lo2 i e5 ho2 phin2-heng7, in-ui7 i sou2 kiaN5 e5 su7 kham cho3 lan2 e5 bou5-iuN7--lah. Siat-su2 lan2 chiah-e5 choan-sim oa2-kho3 Siong7-te3, ia7 thang cho3 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 lang5, chiu7 kap PhoaN Bok8-su bo5 koh-iuN7--lah.  
Siong7-te3 in-ui7 i e5 chin7-tiong sin3-sit8, iah8 tek-khak siuN2-su3 i eng5-kng be7 pai7- hoai7 e5 bian2-liu5. I chai7-seN sin-the2 u7 che7- che7 kan-lan5 khun3-khou2, tok8-tok8 taN chit8- e7 i kau3 e5 sou2-chai7, tek-khak tit-tioh8 eng2-oan2 thoat-li7 peN7-thiaN3 chai-lan7, lai5 hiong2- siu7 eng2-oan2 e5 peng5-an--lah.  I chai7 se3- kan e5 si5 jiat8-sim kap Ia5-sou saN kiat-lian5, taN ti7 Thian-tong5 kap Chu2 cho3-hoe2 toa3 tek-khak tit-tioh8 chiau5-pi7 e5 hok-khi3--lah.  Siat-su2 lan2 tai7-ke sou2 kiaN5 tak8-hang7 su7, e7 jiat8-sim ai3 keng3 Ia5-sou, chiu7 lan2 e5 hok-khi3, iah8 tek-khak chhiong-chiok--lah.  Ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 cho3-hoe2 tit-tioh8 che chin po2-poe3 e5 li7-ek, chin-chiaN3 si7 sim sou2 goan7.  
Choan-choan kia3 hou7 liat8-ui7 e5 Kau3-su7 kap hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 kong-ke khoaN3.  
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 178 koan3,1900 ni5 1 geh8).
