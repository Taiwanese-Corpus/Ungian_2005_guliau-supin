Che si7 goa2 khi3 hoe-tiam3 siong7-pan liau2-au7 te7-saN-pai2 koe3 cheng5-jin5-chiat. Tu2-chiah khai-kang2 e5 si5, goa2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 an2-ni kong2. “Hit-e5 ou-ji2-sang2 chin tek8-piat8. Mui2-chit8-pai2 koe3 cheng5-jin5-chiat long2 lai5 be2-hoe. Kap i2-cheng5 siang7-khoan2, tiam7-tiam7 keng2 kui2-lui2 phang-chui2 pek-hap8 kap mui5-kui3-hoe.”
“Oeh! Sio2-chia2, ma5-hoan5 li2 ka7 goa2 pak8 hoe-sok, kan2-tan tioh8 ho2.” 
I e5 khau2-khi3 thiaN--khi2--lai5 bo5 u7 jim7-ho5 e5 hi2-lok8. I e5 bin7-iong5 khoaN3--khi2--lai5 ia7 bo5 u7 jim7-ho5 e5 ki5-thai7. Goa2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chin houN3-ki5, iong7 chhi3-tham3 e5 oe7 tui3 i kong2: 
“Ou-ji2-sang2, goa2 the3 li2 pak8 khah sui2 leh!”
“M7-bian2 lah, goa2 tng2--khi3 chiu7 sui5 beh chhah ti7 kan-a2 lai7-te2. Than2-peh8 kong2, goa2 e5 khan-chhiu2 i2-keng li7-khui se3-kan, kam2-sia7 li2 e5 ho2-i3 kap pang-chan7.”
Eng-am3, goa2 kui-me5 bo5-khun3, ou-ji2-sang2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 ti7 thau5-khak lai7 seh8-lai5 seh8-khi3. Goa2 kap hu7-jin5-lang5 hou7 ou-ji2-sang2 e5 chin-ai3 kam2-tong7, goa2 e5 sim ku2-ku2 boe7-oe7 peng5-hok8. M7-chai sim2-mih8 si5-chun7, goa2 e5 bak8-chiu na2 e7 chau2 soa-a2 jip8--khi3.
