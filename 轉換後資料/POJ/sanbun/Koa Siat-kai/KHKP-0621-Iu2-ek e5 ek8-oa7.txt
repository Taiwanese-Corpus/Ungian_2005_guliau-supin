Iu2-ek e5 ek8-oa7 
Koa Siat-kai

Pun2-geh8 si7 12 geh8, chiu7-si7 lan2 tai7-ke beh ngia5-chih Kiu3-chu2 Seng3-tan3 e5 si5.  Sia2 chit nng7-e5 iu2-ek e5 ek8-oa7, lai5 saN-kap chham-khou2 :---
1.  Kho-lun5-pou3 kap nng7
Kho-lun5-pou3 (Columbus) hoat-kian3 Bi2-chiu, to3-tng2 kau3 Se-pan-ga5 kok e5 si5, u7 siu7 chin toa7 hoan-geng5.  Ti7 chit8 e5 toa7 lau7-jiat8 e5 tai7-chhan e5 si5, tang5-toh e5 tiong-kan, u7 lang5 soe3-siaN thau kong2, “Hoat-kian3 Bi2-chiu u7 sim2-mih8 ki5-koai3, put-ko3 chun5 ng3 sai-peng5 tit8-tit8 sai2, kau3-be2 chiu7 kau3 Bi2-chiu, tak8 lang5 ma7 oe7-hiau2-tit, u7 sim2-mih8 oh.”  
(Hiah e5 lang5 m7-na7 bo5 ka7 Kho-lun5-pou3 tau3 hoaN-hi2, hoan2-tng2 teh thau-kong gi7-lun7 i sou2 hoat-kian3, si7 khoai3-khoai3 e5 su7).
Kho-lun5-pou3 chai-iaN2 in an2 ni teh phoe-pheng5, chiu7 khia7 khi2-lai5, theh8 chit8-liap8 nng7 tui3 cheng3-lang5 kong2, “Lin2 u7 lang5 oe7-hiau2-tit chiong nng7 chhai7-tiau5 ti7 toh-teng2 a2-boe7 ?”
Cheng3-lang5 chiu7 theh8 nng7 beh chhai7 ti7 toh-teng2, chong2-si7 nng7 lin3-lai5 lin3-khi3, chhai7 boe7 tiau5.  Tak8 lang5 an2-ni chhi3-khoaN3, chong2-si7 nng7 ka-ki7 to2.
Au7-lai5 Kho-lun5-pou3 theh8 nng7, chhut lat8 chiong nng7 tng3 ti7 toh-teng2, nng7 chiu7 tiaN7-tiaN7 tiau5-teh, boe7 koh to2.  (Si7 in-ui7 nng7 e5 toe2 u7 phoa3, chiu7 chhai7 oe7 tiau5).
Hit-si5 hiah e5 lang5 chiu7 kong2, “Heh ! An2-ni tak8-lang5 ma7 oe7-hiau2-tit, u7 siaN2 ti7-teh.”
Kho-lun5-pou3 hit-si5 chiah tui3 cheng3-lang5 kong2, “Tioh8; sui-jian5 si7 khoai3-khoai3 e5 su7, chong2-si7 hit e5 thau5-chit8 e5 oe7-hiau2 chhong3 e5 lang5 chiah si7 gau5.  Pat8-lang5 chhong3 liau2 e5 si5, chiah lai5 gi7-lun7 kong2, Tak8 lang5 ma7 oe7-hiau2-tit, u7 siaN2 ti7-teh.  Kong2 chit khoan2 e5 oe7 si7 m7-tioh8.”
Cheng3-lang5 thiaN-liau2, chiu7 chai in ka-ki7 e5 m7-tioh8.  (In-ui7 in m7-na7 bo5 ka7 Kho-lun5-pou3 tau3 hoaN-hi2, hoan2-tng2 oan3-tou3 khin chhui3 gi7-lun7, phoe-pheng5, thau kong2 i).
11.  Bi2-sut8-ka kap ang5 e5 sek-liau7
Bou2 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8-e5 Bi2-sut8-ka, i chin gau5 ui7 tou5, chong2-si7 chin san3-chhiah.
U7 chit8-pai2, i teh ui7 chit8 tiuN chin kiat8-chok e5 tou5 e5 si5, tak8 khoan2 sek e5 sek-liau7 long2 u7, kan-ta khiam3 ang5 e5 sek-liau7.  I chiu7 tui3 chhu3-piN peng5-iu2 beh chioh tam7-poh8 chiN5, thang lai5 boe2 sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 ang5 e5 sek-liau7, chong2-si7 bo5 lang5 beh chioh i.
Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ni, iau2-ku2 i u7 chiap-soa3 lai5 ui7 i sou2 teh ui7 e5 tou5.
Kau3 u7 chit8 jit8, hit tiuN chin kiat8-chok8 e5 tou5 i2-keng ui7 chiaN5 lah.  Kho2-sioh siang5 hit-si5, Bi2-sut8-ka ia7 si2 ti7 hit tiuN tou5 e5 e7-kha.
In-ui7 bo5 koh khoaN3-kiN3 Bi2-sut8-ka chhut-lai5, chhu3-piN peng5-iu2 chiu7 jip8-khi3 lai7-bin7 khoaN3, khoaN3-kiN3 hit tiuN i2-keng ui7 chiaN5 chin kiat8-chok e5 tou5, ia7 Bi2-sut8-ka si2 ti7 hit tiuN tou5 e5 e7-kha.  Chong2-si7 hit-si5 hiah e5 lang5, saN-kap gi7-lun7 kong2, “Lan2 bo5 chiN5 chioh i, Bi2-sut8-ka bo5 chiN5, thai2-oe7 tit-tioh8 ang5 e5 sek-liau7 ah ?”
Hiah e5 lang5 m7-na7 bo5 kho2-lin5 Bi2-sut8-ka e5 si2, hoan2-tng2 teh gi5-ga7 chaiN2-iuN7 i oe7 tit-tioh8 ang5 e5 sek-liau7.
 
Bi2-sut8-ka si7 chhiat-tng7 i ka-ki7 e5 meh8, eng7 i ka-ki7 e5 huih lai5 choe3 ang5 e5 sek-liau7, lai5 ui7 chiaN5 hit tiuN chin kiat8-chok e5 tou5.
Au7-lai5, u7 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 khoaN3-tioh8 Bi2-sut8-ka e5 chhiu2, chiu7 hoah kong2, Bi2-sut8-ka u7 chhiat-tng7 i ka-ki7 e5 meh8, eng7 i ka-ki7 e5 huih choe3 ang5 e5 sek-liau7 lai5 ui7 chit tiuN tou5 lah ! Tou5 ui7 ho2,Bi2-sut8-ka ia7 si2.
Hiah e5 lang5 chit8-e7 khoaN3, chiah chai in ka-ki7 e5 m7-tioh8.
I2-siong7 chit nng7-e7 iu2-ek e5 ek8-oa7, put-chi2 hou7 lan2 thang choe3 chham-kho2.
Tng lan2 teh ngia5-chih8 Kiu3-chu2 Seng3-tan3 e5 si5, ng3-bang7 lan2 tai7-ke saN-kap eng7 sian7-i3, li2-kai2, tong5-cheng5, the2-thiap e5 sim, lai5 saN khoan2-thai7.  Che chiu7-si7 te7-it ho2 e5 saN-sang3.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 Te7 621 Ho7, ‘Koa3-chhai3-chi2 Po3 Te7 131 Ho7’ 1936 ni5 12 goeh8)
