
Lou7-tou5 Khoan-chek Lun7
P. Ch. H.
KhoaN3 Ma2-thai3 te7 chhit chiuN7 13,14 chat kong2, “Tioh8 tui3 eh8-mng5 jip8; chhoa7 lang5 kau3 si2 hit e5 mng5 si7 khoah, hit e5 lou7 si7 toa7-tiau5, jip8 e5 lang5 che7-che7; chhoa7 lang5 kau3 oah8, hit e5 mng5 si7 eh8, hit e5 lou7 si7 se3-tiau5, jip8 e5 lang5 chio2.”   
Teng2-bin7 chit nng7 chat si7 Seng3 e5 oe7, lan2 tioh8 chat-chat ki3-sim lai5 siuN7, in-ui7 u7 ho2 kap phaiN2 e5 lou7 e5 hun-piat; chhin- chhiuN7 13 chat thau5-chit8-ku3 kong2, “Tioh8 tui3 eh8-mng5 jip8”; an3-choaN2-iuN7 tioh8 tui3 eh8-mng5 jip8; mng5 na7 eh8, lou7 chu7-jian5 ia7 si7 eh8, an2-ni lou7-tou5 un2-tang3 lan5 heng5. Seng3-keng ia7 u7 ki3 kong2, lan5-heng5 tek-khak iah8 tioh8 than3 lai5 kiaN5, kau3-boe2 m7- ku2 u7 eng2-eng2 si7 hoaN-hi2 kap khoai3-lok8 ti7-teh.   
Na7-si7 hit e5 te7-ji7 ku3 kong2,“Chhoa7 lang5 kau3-si2 hit e5 mng5 si7 khoah, hit e5 lou7 si7 toa7-tiau5, jip8 e5 lang5 che7-che7”; sui-jian5 mng5 khoah, lou7 toa7-tiau5, m7-ku2 si7 chhoa7 lang5 kau3 si2; an2-ni-siN ko2-jian5 u7 ho2 kap phaiN2 e5 lou7, sou2-i2 tioh8 chim-chiok sam3-su chiah be7 kiaN5 chho3.   
Na7 siuN7 beh kiaN5 chit-tiau5 kau3-boe2 u7 hoaN-hi2 khoai3-lok8--e5, m7-ku2 mng5-lou7 eh8- se3 phaiN2 kiaN5; sui-jian5 eh8-se3 na7 u7 mng5- lou7 khang-sin-lang5 chu7-jian5 e7 tang3-koe3, na7 beh taN-taN3 a2-si7 toa3 hiah-e5 khin-khin khaing-khiang--e5, un2-tang3 be7-tit jip8 si7 mng5 eh8, lou7 se3 e5 chou2-gai7, tek-khak tioh8 pang3-sak hiat-kak8 hiah-e5; u7 chit-ho7 e5 tiong7 lan5 ti7-teh, sou2-i2 chiah ho7-cho3 eh8- se3 e5 lou7-tou5.  Phah-sng3 kaN2 tioh8 kiaN5 hit tiau5 mng5 khoah, lou7 toa7-tiau5--e5, in-ui7 lan2 na7 beh taN hit-ho7 hoaiN5-hoaiN5 keh8-keh8--e5, si7 long2 bo5 sim2-mih8 e7 chou2-tong3 lan2 e5 sou2 cho3, ia7 bo5 siaN2-lang5 e7 hiam5 lan2 taN hit-ho7; m7- ku2 Seng3-keng kong2,“Chhoa7 lang5 kau3 si2 hit-e5 mng5 si7 khoah, hit-e5 lou7 si7 toa7-tiau5”. 
TaN teng2-bin7 sou2 lun7 mng5-lou7 eh8-se3 khoah-toa7 e5 hun-piat; na7-si7 hit-e5 eh8-se3-- e5, si7 chi2 sin3 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 teh kiaN5 e5 lou7-theng5; hit e5 khoah-toa7--e5, si7 chi2 bo5 sin3 Chu2 bo5 hok8- sai7 Siong7-te3 hit teng2-ho7 lang5 sou2 kiaN5--e5. Sin3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok kap se3-siok8 sou2 kiaN5 e5 lou7 kiam2 u7 khah koh-iuN7 mah? Na7 beh khi3 bou2 siaN5-chhi7 a2-si7 pou-thau5 peN5-peN5 si7 chit8-iuN7; chong--si7 sou2 kiaN5 sou2 cho3 tai7-put siong7-tong5. Chhin-chhiuN7 hit-e5 ‘Khoan- chek Lou7-tou5’u7 hun cho3 nng7-tiau5 e5 lou7- keng3; chit8-tiau5 si7 khoah-toa7, chit8-tiau5 si7 eh8-se3, tiong-kan u7 chhai7 chit8-ki e5 chi2 lou7 pai5 sia2 kong2, na7 tui3 eng2-seng-mng5 jip8--e5, si7 tit tiong5-seng; na7 tui3 ban7-ok- thong-ku5 mng5 jip8--e5, teng7 eng2 bong5.   
Na7 tui3 chit-e5 ban7-ok-thong-ku5 mng5- lou7 jip8, ko2-jian5 u7-iaN2 kau3-boe2 tioh8 biat8- bo5 mah? ChhiaN2 khoaN3 hit-e5 tou5 chiu7 chai, teng2-bin7 iam7-hoe2 tho-thian; m7- ku2 lou7-piN kong-keng2 chin-che7, tiong-kan u7 hoa-liu2-hang7, chheng-phang e5 hun- koan2 u7 Chui3-sian-lau5, u7 chhai2-phio3 e5 kong-si, u7 pok-koa3 siong3-mia7, u7 tai7-ong5- bio7, u7 hi-po3-koan2, u7 kau-chian3 kap chhiuN2-kiap put-gi7 e5 su7, i5-chia2 kong2 put chin7; chiah e5 long2 si7 su-iok8 sou2 heng3--e5.   
TaN lai5 siuN7 hian7-kim se3-siok8 sou2 heng5- ui5--e5, ko2-jian5 in e5 lou7 chin-chiaN3 toa7 koh khoah; chhin-chhiuN7 phiau5, tou2, im2, heng5-gek8 su3-chi3, chhiuN2-kiap put-gi7 e5 chai5-li7, hi3-hiauh phu5-phiio3 e5 oe7; m7-na7 an2-ni na7 tiaN7, it-gian5 to lan5-chin7; chiah- e5 in long2 liah8 cho3 bo5 iau3-kin2 e5 su7. HeN3 m7-ku2 tioh8 koh siuN7 leh! An2-ni na7 kiaN5 kau3-chin7, te7-gek8 e5 hoe2 chiu7 lan5 to5-thoat. Na7 kau3 ti7 chit ho7 e5 heng5-hoat8 eng2-eng2 biat8-bo5. Seng3-keng kong2, boh8-tit chhut- si3 si7 khah-ho2.   
Chai3 siuN7 eng2-seng mng5-lou7 chit-tiau5 kiam2 si7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 kiaN5-e5 mah? Kiam2 u7 iaN2 kiat-boe2 tit tiong5-seng? Na7 khoaN3 hit e5 tou5 chiu7 it-bok8 liau3-jian5, teng2-bin7 u7 eng5-kng chhan3-lan7; chong--si7 lou7-piN kong-keng2 bo5 loa7-ho2, ki5-tiong chiah u7 kang2-sip8-sou2, hak8-tong5, i-iN7, chheh-kiok8 na7-tiaN7, sui-bong2 bo5 loa7 lau7-jiat8, m7-ku2 long2 si7 tiong-hou7 cheng3-tit8 e5 lou7, na7 kau3 kiat-kiok8 e5 si5-chun7 koat-toan3 e7 tit tiong5 seng. KhoaN3 II The5-mou5-thai3 4: 7, 8; Iok-han7 Hok-im-toan7 14: 13.   
Chiau3 khoaN3 chit kui2 chat long2-si7 tit kiu3 e5 to7-li2; Seng3-keng e5 oe7 ka7 lan2 kong2, lan2 na7 sin3-than3 lai5 kiaN5 kau3 lim5-chiong e5 si5, Chu2 tek-khak beh chiap-lap8 jip8 chi3-seng3 eng2-oan2-oah8 e5 kok. Eh8 kap kkhoah chit nng7-tiau5 e5 mng5-lou7 hou7 lan2 thang chham-kho2, khoaN3 tioh8 chin3 eh8-lou7 a2-si7 tioh8 tui3 khoah-lou7 jip8? 
Na7 beh kiaN5 tui3 eh8-lou7 chit-tiau5 khi3, m7- ku2 ti7 se3-kan sou2 kiaN5 sou2 cho3 put-chi2 ui5- lan5, in-ui7 chit-tiau5 lou7 u7 kim3-leng7 chin7- giam5, na7 beh cho3 tam7-poh8 put-hoat e5 su7 si7 khi3 hoan7-tioh8 kim3-leng7. Tui3 an2-ni ka-ti7 chiu7 khah lun2-nehN, na7 koh lam7- sam2 cho3 bo5 hoe2-gou7, kau3-hoe7 e5 si7-toa7 chit8-e7 chai, in beh lai5 khou2-khng3 soa3 chek-pi7, kong2 m7-thang an2-ni cho3, na7 m7 thiaN kau3-boe2 tioh8 tim5-lun5; che2 ho7 cho3 si7 mng5-lou7 eh8-se3, sou2-i2 ui5-chi lan5-heng5.   
Chit tiau5 khoah-toa7--e5, chin-chiaN3 ho2 kiaN5, khoaN3 si7 beh khi3 chiah8 a-phian3, a2- si7 beh chiah8 chiu2 lai5 phiau5, tou2, im2, teng2 e5 lam7-sam2 cho3, long2 bo5 u7 chit- ho7 kim3-leng7; sou2-i2 si7 ho7 cho3 khoah-toa7 e5 mng5-lou7.  
Thian-kok e5 mng5-lou7 ko2-jian5 m7-si7 eh8- se3; Thian-kok si7 eng2-oan2 e5 Kok. I cho3 ban7 ong5 e5 Ong5, ban7 chu2 e5 Chu2, thiN-e7 ban7-pang-kok long2 si7 cho3 I e5 siok8 kok. I cho3 sin5-leng5 e5 Chu2, koan5-peng3 long2 ti7 I e5 chhiu2-thau5, thiN-e7 kun-ong5 i2-kip8 peh- siN3; chi3-chiong e5 ki5 na7 kau3 long2 tioh8 khia7 ti7 I tai5-cheng5 lai5 siu7 sim2-phoaN3 e5 po3-eng3. Na7 ui5-hui chok-ok, Chu2 Ia5-ho5- hoa u7 toa7 khui3-lat8 thang long2-chong2 po3- eng3 in. An2-ni siN, si2, hok, long2 chai7 ti7 cho7-hoa3 e5 Chu2 Ia5-ho5-hoa chhiu2-nih8; I chiang2 chit-ho7 koan5-peng3, chiu7-si7 chi3-chun e5 Kok, se3-kan long2-chong2 e5 kok be7-e7 phit-phoe3-tit, an2-ni mng5-lou7 un2- tang3 si7 khoah-toa7 m7-si7 eh8-se3.  
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 317 koan3, 1911 ni5 8 geh8)
