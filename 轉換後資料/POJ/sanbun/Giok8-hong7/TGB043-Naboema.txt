Na5-boe2-ma2
TeN8 Giok8-hong7
　　Hong teh chhoe, hong teh chhoe, iah8-a2 iah8-a2 li2 kin2 poe~kin2 poe......
　　Goa2 u7 nng7-e5 goa7-ma2, chit8-e5 toa3 ti7 soaN-kha, chit8-e5 toa3 ti7 khe-piN. SoaN-kha--e5 si7 a-bu2 e5 chhin-seN lau7-bu2, khe-piN--e5 si7 iong2-bu2. SoaN-piN e5 a-ma2, goa2 ka7 kio3-cho3 “Na5-boe2-ma2”, i kui-si3-lang5 u7 chit8-poaN3 e5 si5-kan to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2, a-ma2 e7-poaN3-sin long2 be7-tang3 tin2-tang7, bo5 hoat-tou7 kiaN5 ma7 bo5 hoat-tou7 che7, i long2 tioh8 to2--leh, na7 beh lim-chui2 chiah8-png7, tioh8-ai3 iong7 chhiu2-au7-khiau ka7 keng-kah-thau5 keng--khi2-lai5, sin-khu chhu-chhu chiah e7--tit-leh.
　　Na5-boe2-ma2 e5 bin7 chin peh8, thau5-mou tng5-tng5, hok-siong3 hok-siong3, khoaN3--khi2-lai5 siau3-lian5-si5 eng-kai ma7 si7 chin sui2 e5 kou-niu5-a2, m7-koh, “sui2-lang5 bo5 sui2-mia7”. A-ma2 sim-koaN-te2 chin oan3-chheh, chin bu5-nai7 ma7 chin kou-toaN, I oan3-thiN oan3-te7 oan3 mia7-un7 ma7 oan3 a-kong, I koai3-choe7 a-kong m7-kam khai toa7-chiN5 chhiaN3 I-seng, chiah hai7 i chiah-ni7 chhiN-chham2 lok8-pek. I kong2 beh oah8 pi2 a-kong khah ku2, beh khoaN3 a-kong pi2 i khah cha2 si2!
　　A-ma2 seN kau2-e5 kiaN2-ji5 si7-se3 (saN-e5 cha-bou2--e5), liau2-au7 sin-khu to7 be7 tin2-tang7--a. A-ma2 chhu3--nih cheng3-te5, oa2-soaN chiah8-soaN, in e5 te5-hng5  te5-hng5 chin toa7 phian3, phoa3-peN7 chin3-cheng5, a-ma2 koh si7 Na5-boe2 chit-tah ban2-te5 e5 ko-chhiu2, m7-koh, ka7 se3-han3 ku7--a2 seN--loh8-lai5 liau2-au7, I sin-khu to7 be7 tin2-tang7--a, hoan7-se3 si7 seN-kiaN2 e5 si5 bo5 tu2-ho2--tioh8.
　　A-ma2 sui-bong2 poaN3-sui5, m7-koh i e5 chhiu2 iau2 cheng3-siong5 cheng3-siong5. Goa2 e7 ki3-tit, a-ma2 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2, toa7-pou7-hun7 si5-kan long2 teh chhiah phong3-se, I e7-hiau2 cho3-saN, kun5 kap kah-a2, chiu2-kang chin iu3, sek-chui2 phoe3 kah chin sui2-khoan2. A-ma2 tiaN7-tiaN7 chhiah e5 tou5, si7 sui2-sui2 e5 hoe-a2 chhau2-a2, teng2-bin7 koh u7 iah8-a2 the poe, a-ma2 chhiah e5 iah8-a2 koh kou2-chui koh chu7-jian5. A-ma2 siong7 sioh toa7-han3 a-chi2, I cho3 e5 saN kap kun5 long2 si7 beh hou7 a-chi2 chheng7--e5, a-chi2 si7 a-bu2 te7-it-e5 gin2-a2, hoan7-se3 si7 an2-ne, i chiah e7 tek8-piat8 ka7 thiaN3-sioh.
　　A-ma2 sui-bong2 sin-khu be7-tang3 tin2-tang7, m7-koh thau5-khak chin chheng-chhou2, ki3-seng3 bo5 khah bai2. A-ku7 kap a-kim7 tiaN7 ti7 soaN-teng2 ban2-te5,  bo5 ti7 chhu3--nih, a-ma2 e7 the3 kui ke-hoe2-a2 be2-chhai3, chhai3-hoan3-a2 e5 hoat-chai5-chhia na7 keng-koe3 in mng5-khau2-tiaN5, a-ma2 to7 e7 hoah-siaN kio3 be7-chhai3--e5 jip8--lai5.
　　Toa7-pou7-hun7 si5-kan a-ma2 long2 chit8-e5 lang5 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2. Lang5 kong2 “ku2-tng5-peN7, put-hau3-kiaN2”, na7 m7-si7 beh ka7 hong7-thai7 saN-tng3, chhu--nih e5 lang5 long2 pin7-toaN7 kap I kong2-oe7 khai-kang2. A-ma2 e5 pang5-keng ti7 ku7-chhu3 lai7-te2, bo5 kap a-ku7 in e5 sin-chhu3 cho3-hoe2. A-ma2 chin bo5-phoaN7 ma7 chin kou-toaN, goa2 kap a-bu2 tak8-pai2 tng2--khi3, a-ma2 to7 na2-chhiuN7 chiok ku2 bo5 kong2-oe7--a, it-tit8 kong2 it-tit8 mng7, long2 kong2 be7 soah.
　　Si3-ku7 chhut i3-goa7, hou7 a-ma2 taN2-kek chiaN5 toa7. Si3-ku7 kha-thau5-u siu7-siong bo5 chu3-i3--tioh8, koh khi3 chhai3-hng5 phun3 tu5-chhau2-che, liau2-au7 hoat-iam7 koh bo5 sui5 khi3 peN7-iN7, soah phoa3-siong-hong, to bo5 40--leh, to7 pang3 bou2 pang3 kiaN2 koe3-sin--khi3. Hit-toaN7 si5-kan , a-ma2 kui-kang bak8-sai2 lau5 bak8-sai2 tih, tu2-tioh8 lang5 to7 kong2: “Si7 an2-choaN2 si2--e5 m7-si7 goa2!” A-ma2 chiok choat8-bang7--e5.
　　Thak8 ko-tiong e5 si5, u7 chit8-pai2 goa2 khi3 khoaN3 chit8-chhut tian7-iaN2, kiok8-cheng5 lai7-te2 hit-e5 poaN3-sui5 e5 kho2-lian5 hu7-jin5-lang5 hou7 goa2 chin kam2-tong7, goa2 hiong5-hiong5 liong5-sim hoat-hian7, siuN7 kong2 tioh8 khi3 ka7 a-ma2 khoaN3--chit8-e7. Goa2 ka-ti7 chit8-e5 m7-kaN2 khi3, koh chhoe7 keh-piah e5 A-ju5 tau3-tin7, nng7-e5 lang5 khia5 thih-be2 khi3 khoaN3 a-ma2.
　　A-ma2 khoaN3-tioh8 goa2 chiaN5 hoaN-hi2 koh chin oan3-than3: “Goa2 si7 bo5 lou7-iong7 lang5, li2 koh u7-sim e7 siuN7-tioh8 goa2 chit-e5 a-ma2.” I theh8 si3-siu3-a2 chhut-lai5 hou7 goan2 chiah8, chhui3--nih kang7-khoan2 it-tit8 liam7 it-tit8 mng7, kong2 long2 be7 soah, A-ju5 che7 ti7 piN--a2, ma7 m7-chai beh chhong3 siaN2? Boe2--a2 a-ma2 kong2 kah thiam2--a, thin ma7 beh am3--a, goan2 chiah koaN2-kin2 soan, tu2 chong5 chhut-khi3 kau3 mng5-kha-khau2, pang5-keng lai7 a-ma2 koh sau-siaN sau-siaN it-tit8 kong2: “U7-eng5 ai3 koh lai5--heh, u7 thiaN--tioh8-bo5? U7-eng5 ai3 koh lai5 khoaN3 a-ma2......” Che si7 goa2 te7-it-pai2 ka-ti7 khi3 khoaN3 a-ma2, ma7 si7 siong7-boe2 chit8-pai2.
　　Na5-boe2-ma2 ti7 bin5-chhng5 teng2 to2 22 tang, tai3 kui sin-khu e5 peN7-thiaN3 koe3-sin--khi3. Ng3-bang7 a-ma2 e5 leng5-hun5 chhin-chhiuN7 iah8-a2, chhiong-chhut  I am3-sam3 e5 pang5-keng, poe-koe3 i khah-cha2 cheng3-cho e5 te5-hng5, poe-koe3 soaN-piN khe-kham2, poe-khi3 I me5-jit8 bang7-siong2 e5 thiN-goa7-thiN.
　　Hong teh chhoe, hong teh chhoe, iah8-a2 iah8-a2 li2 kin2 poe~kin2 poe.....
　　(TGB Thong-sin3 te7 43 ki5, 2003 ni5 4 goeh8)
