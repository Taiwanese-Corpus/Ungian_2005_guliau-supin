Lip8-ui2 Soan2-ki2 E5 I3-gi7
								
Soan2-ki2 koh lai5 lah! Tak8-pai2 na7 tu2-tioh8 soan2-ki2, tek8-piat8 si7 lip8-ui2 soan2-ki2, lan2 chiu7 khai-si2 si3-ke3 chau2-chong5, the3 hau7-soan2-jin5 giu2-phio3 kap bou7-khoan2. Hi-bang7 lan2 chi-chhi5 e5 lang5 tong3-soan2, thang hou7 TGB un7-tong7 u7 koh-khah che7, koh-khah toa7 e5 chi-chhi5 kap eng2-hiong2. M7-ku2, lek8-su2 ka7 lan2 kong2, Tai5-oan5 e5 lip8-ui2 koan-sim e5 tai7-chi3 si7 li7-ek, bak8-cheng5 ka-ki7 e5 li7-ek na7-tiaN7! Lan2 teh iau3-i3 e5 gi2-bun5 un7-tong7 in bo5 sim2-mih8 chhu3-bi7! Ti7 chit-e5 kham2-cham7 lan2 koh-lai5 beh an2-choaN2 leh?
Goa2 jin7-ui5 lan2 ai3 iong7 e7-bin7 6 hang7 tiau5-kiaN7 lai5 kiam2-giam7 Tai5-oan5 e5 cheng3-ti7 jin5-but8: Tai5-oan5 i3-sek, koe3-khi3 e5 kong3-hian3, choan-giap8 leng5-lek8, heng5-cheng3 leng5-lek8, jin5-phin2, kap bun5-hoa3 i3-sek. Chit lai7-bin7, siong7 iau3-kin2..e5 si7 bun5-hoa3 i3-sek, tek8-piat8 si7 kap gi2-gian5 u7 koan-he7..e5. Chit-e5 phiau-chun2 tek8-piat8 sek-iong7 ti7 lip8-ui2 hau7-soan2 jin5!  
Lan2 long2 chai, koh lai5 lan2 e5 chian3-tiuN5 ti7 Lip8-hoat-iN7. Lip8-hoat-iN7 lai7-te2 lan2 e5 lang5 na7 chhiau-koe3 chit8-poaN3, beh chhong3-siaN2 long2 bo5 bun7-te5. M7-ku2 na7-si7 koe3-poaN3 e5 lip8-ui2 tui3 gi2-bun5 bo5 liau2-kai2, bo5 chi-chhi5, an2-ne u7 sim2-mih8 lou7-iong7?
