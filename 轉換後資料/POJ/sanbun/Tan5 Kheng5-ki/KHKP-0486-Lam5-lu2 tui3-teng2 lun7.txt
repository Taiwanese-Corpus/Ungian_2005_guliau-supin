Lun7 chit e5 bun7-toe5 si7 put-chi2 hok-chap8, koh iah8 si7 chin oh-tit lun7 e5 su7, koh-chai3 lun7-chia2 na7 bo5 siang5, chiu7 sou2 lun7 e5 lai7-iong5 chu7-jian5 iah8 u7 koh-iuN7. Chap8 lang5 e5 lun7 u7 chap8 lang5 e5 soat, chiu7-si7 tak8-lang5 u7 i e5 hong-hiong3, u7 i e5 bok8-tek, u7 i e5 i3-kian3; chhin-chhiuN7 sim-li2-hak8-chia2, i chiu7 tui3 sim-li2 hit hong-bin7 lun7-khi2, na7-si7 seng-but8-hak8-chia2 si7 tui3 seng-but8 e5 hong-bin7 lai5 lun7-khi2; ki5-sit8 na7 beh lun7 chit e5 bun7-toe5, tioh8 ai3 tui3 kui2-na7 hong-bin7 lai5 gian2-kiu3 soat-beng5, chiah oe7 eng7-tit.  Phi3-ju7 kong2 tui3 lek8-su2 chiu7 soat-beng5, a2-si7 eng7 lam5-lu2 e5 thau5-lo2 kap the2-keh lai5 saN-pi2, a2-si7 tui3 sim-li2 chong7-thai7 teng2-teng2 lai5 pi2- kau3 soat-beng5. Chong2-si7 na7 beh chhin- chhiuN7 an2-ni soat, tek-khak tioh8 sia2 chin-tng5, sou2-i2 ti7-chia bo5 ki-hoe7. Ti7-chia sou2 lun7--e5, si7 thang kong2 beh choe3 lam5-lu2 tui3-teng2 e5 kam2-siong2 lun7 na7-tiaN7; sou2 lun7 e5 oe7 si7 tak8 lang5 long2 khoaN3 oe7 hiau2-tit, chiu7-si7 chhian2-hian7 e5 oe7.    
Lun7 lang5 teh chu2-chhiong3 koan5-li7, phah-sng3 tui3 kou2-cha2 chiu7-u7.  Sui-jian5 tui3 cha2 chiu7 lang5 oe7-hiau2 chu2-tiuN koan5-li7, iau2-ku2 bo5 chit se3-tai7 chhin-chhiuN7 hian7- si5 chiah toa7 chhut-lat8.  Koh-chai3 tui3 cha2 sou2 teh chu2-chhiong3 chit e5 koan5-li7, khah choe7 si7 lam5-chu2 e5 tiong-kan na7-tiaN7; bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 hian7-kim kong2 lu2-chu2 teh tui3 lam5-chu2 chu2-chhiong3 in e5 koan5-li7.    
TaN in sou2 iau-kiu5 e5 koan5-li7, chiu7-si7 kong2 lam5-chu2 tioh8 piN5-teng2 thai7-gu7.  Tui3 cha2 kau3 taN2, lun7 lu2-chu2 e5 te7-ui7 chin ke7, chiu7-si7 bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 hong-bin7 long2 siu7 lam5-chu2 e5 ap-che3, long2 boe7-oe7 chhun-kha chhut-chhiu2, tu2-tu2 si7 chhin- chhiuN7 lam5-chu2 e5 ki3-seng-thang5 chit8-iuN7.  
TaN chiau3 Ju5-kau3 e5 ka3-si7, lun7 hu7-tek si7 "sun7-chiong5" nng7-ji7 na7-tiaN7; chhin- chhiuN7 kong2, "Chai7 ka chiong5 hu7, chhut ka3 chiong5 hu; hu su2, chiong5 chu2." KhoaN3 chit e5 sam-chiong5 e5 ka3-si7 chiu7 chai hu7-lu2 tui3 chhut-si3 kau3 jip8 bong7, kan-ta hou7 chit ji7 "chiong5"--ji7 pak8 teh iN5-iN5-go5, eng2-oan2 bo5 chhiau-seng e5 jit8-chi2, chiu7-si7 bo5 chu7- 
iu5 e5 si5-chun7; ki3-jian5 bo5 chu7-iu5, thai3 u7 sim2-mih8 koan5-li7?  I2-keng bo5 u7 koan5-li7, thai3 u7 ng3-bang7 piN5-teng2 e5 jit8-chi2; i2-keng bo5 piN5-teng2, chu7-jian5 tioh8 sun7-than3 lang5, i e5 sin-the2 chu7-jian5 si7 siok8 pat8-lang5, m7-si7 siok8 ka-ki7 e5; i2-keng siok8 pat8 lang5 e5, chu7-jian5 tioh8 chhut- chai7 hit e5 chu2-lang5 phah-sng3.    
Khong2-chu2 kong2, "Kun-chu2 chi to7, cho7 toan hou hu-hu7, kip8 ki5 chi3 ia2".  Chit e5 i3-su3 si7 kong2 kun-chu2 e5 to7-li2 beh thong-heng5 thiN-e7 e5 tai7-seng, si7 tui3 ang-bou2 e5 koan-he7 khi2-tiam2; sou2-i2 na7 beh cho7-chiaN5 li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5, tioh8 tui3 hu-hu7 u7 hiap8-ho5, toa7-soe3 saN-chhin saN-thiaN3, chiah oe7 eng7-tit.    
Goa2 siong5-siong5 teh siuN7 chit8-ke e5 lai7-bin7 chaiN2-iuN7 u7 oan-ke saN-chiN e5 tai7-chi3 chin ki5-koai3; ka-ki7 e5 ke-teng5 siong7-chhiaN2 boe7-oe7 ho5-peng5, beh thai3-oe7 ka3 lang5 choe3 ho5-peng5.  Lun7 ke-teng5 boe7-oe7 ho5, hit e5 goan5-in u7 chin-choe7 khoan2, chong2- si7 tai7-khai3 te7-it toa7 e5 goan5-in, si7 ui7-tioh8 koan5-li7 bo5 peng5-kun jia2-khi2, chhin- chhiuN7 si7-toa7 lam7-sam2 ap-che3 si7-soe3, a2-si7 tiong7-hu phaiN2 khoan2-thai7 i e5 hu7-jin5.  Na7 oe7-hiau2 oh8 chhin-chhiuN7 Ia5-sou ka7 hak8-seng soe2 kha hit e5 cheng-sin5, phah- sng3 m7 na7 ti7 ka-teng5 tiong u7 ho5-peng5, chiu7-si7 ti7 tak8 sou2-chai7 long2 peng5-an bo5 tai7 chi3; chong2-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 ho7-choe3 chu5-ai3, m7-si7 ho7-choe3 piN5-teng2.    
TaN, lun7 lam5-lu2 na7 beh piN5-teng2 thai7-gu7, sou2 iau3-kin2 u7 nng7-hang7 e5 tiau5-kiaN7, chit8 hang7 si7 lu2-chu2 e5 kau3-iok8 tioh8 chin3-pou7 kau3 boe7 su lam5-chu2; koh chit8 hang7 si7 keng-che3 hong-bin7, ka-ki7 tioh8 u7 tok8-lip8 seng-oah8 e5 khui3-lat8, m7-bian2 oa2-kho3 ta-pou e5 pang-chan7 ia7 oe7-tit seng-oah8.    
Ki5-sit8 na7 u7 chap8-hun e5 kau3-iok8, beh tok8-lip8 seng-oah8 si7 khah khoai3. Lan2 u7 chit8 hang7 pe7-peng7, chiu7-si7 lu2-chu2 it-poaN e5 chho3-gou7, chiu7-si7 khah-choe7 long2 siuN7 beh hou7 lang5 chhi7.  Goa2 siong5-siong5 bat thiaN-kiN3 kong2, "Ke3 ang beh chiah8 ang," chit khoan2 e5 su-siuN2 chin m7-tioh8. Siok8-gu2 kong2, Chiah8 lang5 png7, hoan7 lang5 mng7." Lu2-chu2 na7 bo5 chu7-kak, tiaN7-tiaN7 khng3 chit khoan2 su-siuN2, chu7- jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2, lu2-chu2 si7 choe3 lam5-chu2 e5 ki3-seng-thang5.  Na7-si7 an2-ni, si7 kan-ta choe3 lam5-chu2 e5 gu5-lok8-phin2 na7-tiaN7; goan5-lai5 lam5-chu2 si7 kok chit8 peng5 na7- 
tiaN7, kan-ta lam5-chu2 a2-si7 kan-ta lu2-chu2, tek-khak boe7-oe7 chiaN5-choe3 oan5-choan5 e5 lang5. KhoaN3 Han3-ji7(äºº), si7 nng7-phiat kiat hap8 lai5 chiaN5, kan-ta chit8-phiat m7-chiaN5 lang5. Lun7 jin5-keh, bo5 lun7 lam5-lu2, bo5 hun-piat pi2-chhu2, sou2 u7 chha-piat8--e5 si7 chit-bu7- siong7 na7-tiaN7, chiu7-si7 lam5-chu2 u7 lam5-chu2 e5 thian-chit; lu2-chu2 u7 lu2-chu2 e5 thian- chit. Lun7 chit e5 thian-chit, si7 cho7-but8-Chu2 phoe3-hap8 ho2-ho2, sou2-i2 choat8-tui3 boe7- oe7 saN chhim-hoan7--tit. Na7-si7 lun7 pat8 hang7, chhin-chhiuN7 kong2 lam5-chu2 ti7-goa7, lu2-chu2 ti7-lai7.  Che si7 tai7-iok an2-ni kong2, chong2-si7 m7-si7 choat8-tui3 e5 teng7-li2, lun7 lu2-chu2 the2-keh oe7 su lam5-chu2, si7 ui7-tioh8 bo5 oah8-tang7, iah8 bo5 un7-tong7 sou2 ti3; koh thau5-khak khah-su ta-pou--e5 si7 ui7-tioh8 tui3 bo5 ki-hoe7 thang chhin-chhiuN7 lam5- chu2 an2-ni siu7 kau3-iok8 e5 in-toaN. ChhiaN2 khoaN3 tui3 soe3-han3 e5 si5 bo5-lun7 lam5 a2-si7 lu2, long2 hou7 in siu7 siang7-khoan2 e5 kau3- iok8, kap siang7-khoan2 e5 un7-tong7, chiu7 in au7-lai5 oe7 na2 piN5-teng2, bo5-lun7 sin-the2 a2-si7 ti3-sek.  Chong2-si7 goa2 teh kong2 piN5-teng2, i3-su3 m7-si7 kong2 lam5-chu2 na7 gou7-chhioh koan5, lu2-chu2 iu5-goan5 oe7 gou7-chhioh koan5. Na7 piN5-piN5 hou7 in siu7 siang7-khoan2 theng5-tou7 e5 kau3-iok8, chiu7 lam5-chu2 chiaN5- choe3 li2-siong2 e5 lam5-chu2, lu2-chu2 chiaN5- choe3 li2-siong2 e5 lu2-chu2, chu7 an2-ni chiu7 thang kong2 si7 piN5-teng2.
