Lun7 Hu7-jin5 chiong-lai5 e5 Su-beng7
 (Tan5 Kheng5-ku)  
TaN lai5 lun7 tam7-poh8 Tang-iuN5 e5 hu7- jin5-lang5 e5 chong7-hong2. Sui-jian5 si7 In3- tou7, ti7 kou2-cha2 lu2-chu2 si7 choe3 ka-teng5 tiong e5 lu2-ong5, chin7 u7 koan5, chin chu7-iu5, kap lam5-chu2 long2 piN5-piN5, koh lu2-chu2 e5 kau3-iok8 iah8 chin ti3-i3. Na7 khoaN3 hit pun2  chu7-chip8 chhut-mia5 e5 lu2-ong5, a2-si7 gau5 e5 lu2-su7 sou2 teh chhiuN3 e5 koa-chip8 ‘Veda’  chiu7 chai-iaN2.  Sui-si7 an2-ni, lu2-chu2 e5 toe7-ui7 kau3 au7-lai5 chiam7-chiam7 ke7, koh ui7-tioh8 hou7 Pho-lo5-bun5 kau3 e5 cheng-lu5 e5 choan-che3 ap-pek, ti3-kau3 hu7-lu2 e5 chu7- iu5 kap koan5-li7 chiam7-chiam7 bo5--khi3, kau3 au7-lai5 chiau5-chng5 piN3-choe3 lam5-chu2 e5  lou5-le7. Na7 khoaN3 hit pun2 ‘Maun’ Seng3- tian2 chiu7 chai-iaN2, u7 ki3-chai3 chin-choe7  khoaN3-khin lu2-chu2 e5 su7 ti7-teh.  
Koh ti7 Pho-su-kok ui7-tioh8 siu7 Hoe5-hoe5-kau3 kui-chek e5 sok-pak8, hian7-si5 iu5- goan5 lun7 lu2-chu2 long2 bo5 chit8-sut8-a2 e5 koan5-li7, koh chit8-si3-lang5 i choat8-tui3  boe7-tit-thang ti7 sia7-hoe7 tiong lai5 oah8- tang7.  
Ti7 Tiong-kok tui3 cha2-cha2 chiu7 u7 hit khoan2 “Sam-chiong5 chhit-khi3” e5 ka3-si7, koh chhin-chhiuN7 kong2, “Lu2-chu2 bo5 kong-su7.” Lun7 Tiong-kok bun5-beng5 e5 iau3-sou3 e5 tiong-ng, Ju5-kau3 si7 chiam3 toa7 pou7-hun7. Sou2-i2 na7 beh lun7 Tiong-kok hu7-lu2 bun7-toe5 e5 su7, tek-khak tioh8 tui3 Su3-su2 Ngou2-keng lai5 theh8 chai5-liau7. Chong2-si7 lun7 Ju5-kau3 e5 lu2-seng3-koan i2- cheng5 goa2 bat chhut chit8 pai2 lah, sou2-i2 cheng5 sou2 u7 kong2--e5, ti7-chia bo5 ai3 koh kong2. TaN ti7-chia kan-ta ai3 ke-thiN tam7- poh8 cha2-cheng5 bo5 kong2 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7- si7 ti7 Lun7-gu2 lai7-bin7 u7 kong2, “Chu2 ui7 Kong-ia2 Tiong5 kho2 chhe3--ia2, sui chai7 lui5-siat chi tiong, hui ki5 choe7--ia2, i2 ki5 chu2 chhe3--chi.” Koh ti7 pat8-ui7 u7 kong2, “Chu2 ui7 Lam5-iong5 pang iu2-to7 put-hoe3, pang bu5-to7 bian2 u7 heng5-liok8, i2 ki5 heng chi chu2 chhe3--chi.” Tui3 an2-ni thang khoaN3 chhut Tiong-kok e5 seng3-hian5 liah8 lu2-chu2 choe3 le2-mih8, a2-si7 chiong2-le7 phin2 e5 cheng3-ku3.  
 
Iah8 lun7 lam5-lu2 kan e5 le2-gi5 chok-hoat teng7 chin siong5-se3. TaN tui3 gian5-gu2- siong7, ti7 Le2-ki3 u7 ki3, kong2, “Ta-pou-lang5 bo5 kong2 chhu3-lai7 su7, cha-bou2 lang5 bo5  kong2 goa7-bin7 tai7;” koh, “Goa7-khau2 e5 su7 bo5 jip8 kau3 hou7-teng7 lai7, iah8 lai7-bin7 e5 su7  bo5 thoan5-chhut kau3 hou7-teng7 goa7.” Koh tui3 sia7-kau siong7 ka3-si7 kong2, “Lam5-lu2  na7 m7-si7 keng-ke3 beh tiaN7-tioh8, m7-thang saN-kap chai-mia5, m7-thang siu7 sang3 mih8,  koh iah8 m7-thang saN kau-poe5.”  “KiaN5- lou7 e5 si5 lam5-chu2 tui3 chiaN3-peng5, lu2-chu2  tui3 to3-peng5; chhia chiah tui3 tiong-ng.”  
Koh lun7 ti7 ka-teng5 tiong e5 ka3-si7 kong2, “Lai7-bin7 kap goa7-khau2 m7-thang  siang7 kou2-chiN2, m7-thang saN-kap soe2 ek8- thang2, bo5 saN thong-ong2 pang5-keng, koh  saN, bo5 saN-kap eng7, koh lam5-chu2 jip8 lai7-bin7 m7-thang ki2. Am3-si5 jip8--lai5 tioh8 kiah8  he2, na7 bo5 he2 chiu7 m7-thang; iah8 lu2-chu2 chhut-mng5 tek-khak tioh8 jia-bin7; am3-si5  kiaN5-lou7 e5 si5 iu5-goan5 tioh8 kiah8-he2, na7 bo5 chiu7 m7-thang.  
Koh lun7 tui3 chhin-chhek e5 ka3-si7 kong2, “Bo5-lun7 si7 ka-ki7 e5 toa7-chi2 a2-si7 sio2-be7, na7 i2-keng ke3-liau2 koh to3-tng2 lai5 thit-tho5 e5 si5, sui-si7 hiaN-ti7 iah8 m7-thang saN-kap che7, iah8 m7-thang saN-kap chiah8-png7.” “Koh sio2-chek si2 e5 si5, hiaN-so2 m7-thang oa2-tioh8 i e5 sin-si; sio2-chek tui3 hiaN-so2 e5 sin-si iah8 si7 an2-ni.”  Lun7 Tiong-kok kou2-lai5 tui3 chit e5 lam5- lu2 e5 ka3-si7 si7 chin-giam5, chong2-si7 goa2 m7- si7 kong2 chit khoan2 ka3-si7 long2 m7-ho2, put-ko3 hou7 lan2 thang siuN7, lun7 chit khoan2 e5 ka3-si7, thang kong2 si7 chhin- chhiuN7 hoat-lut8 chit8-poaN-iuN7, long2 bo5 chit8-sut8-a2 e5 thiaN3-cheng5. Sui-si7 ka-ki7 e5 chi3-chhin e5 chi2-be7, a2-si7 ka-chok8-tiong e5 chhin-lang5 iah8 tioh8 kap it-poaN e5 goa7- lang5 eng7 siang7-khoan2 e5 khoan2-thai7-hoat. Lun7 Ju5-kau3 e5 ka3-si7 sit8-chai7 u7 choe7-choe7 hang7 hou7 lang5 thang chim-chiok siuN7 e5 sou2-chai7 ti7-teh. Sou2-i2 lun7 Tiong-kok e5 hu7-lu2 tui3 cheng-sin5 e5 hoat-tat8 kap kau3- hoa3 long2 bo5 chin3-pou7, kan-ta si7 iong2-seng5 chiau5-chng5 e5 hok8-chiong5.  
Chong2-si7 tui3 Tiong-kok te7-it kek- beng7 e5 si5 khi2, chiu7 u7 hoat-chhut hit  khoan2. “Lu2-koan5 khong2-tiong e5 un7- tong7.” Koh ti7 1919 ni5, ti7 Tiong-hoa  Bin5-kok 8 ni5, chiu7 hoat-chhut “Lu2-chu2 e5 chham-cheng3-koan5;” kau3 1921 ni5-be2   
ti7 ou5-lam5-seng2 e5 hian3-hoat u7 beng5-beng5 ki3-chai3 seng5-jin7 lu2-chu2 u7 chham-cheng3  e5 koan5-li7, koh bo5 joa7-ku2 ti7 Seng2-gi7-hoe7 chiu7 u7 soan2-chhut lu2-chu2 e5 gi7-oan5. Ti7  1923 ni5 1 geh8 1 jit8 ti7 Tiong-hoa Bin5- kok-tong2 e5 soan-gian5 tiong u7 beng5-beng5 ki3 “Lam5-lu2 peng5-teng2” e5 ko3-tiau5 ti7-teh. Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni, hian7-si5 ti7 Tiong-kok,  lun7 lu2-chu2 e5 koan5-li7 kap chu7-kak chham kou2-tai7 lai5 saN-pi2 u7 thian-ian e5 cheng- chha.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 509-511 koan3,1927 ni5 8-10 geh8)
