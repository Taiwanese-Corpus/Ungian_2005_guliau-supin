Lun7 Kiat-hun
Tan5 Kheng5-ku   
Lang5 tiam3 ti7 chit se3-kan, tu5-khi2 u7 tek8-piat8 su7-cheng5 e5 lang5 i2-goa7 boe7-oe7 tok8-sin seng-oah8, chiu7-si7 tioh8 kian3-siat chit8 e5 ka-teng5 chiah oe7 eng7-tit.  Kong2 khah-beng5, chiu7-si7 tak8-lang5 tioh8 u7 chit8 e5 ho2 tang5-phoaN7 thang saN-kap khia7-khi2, saN pang-chan7.  U7 tai7-chi3 saN-kap chim- chiok, tang5-tiN tang5-khou2, u7 ho2 saN chiong2-le7, u7 phaiN2 saN tiong-khok, chiu7-si7 sou2 kong2 iN7-sin tang5-cheng5, tang5-sim kap tang5-i3 e5 ho2 tang5-phoaN7, chiah thang an-sim khoai3-lok8 keng-ke3 chit e5 se3-kan.    
Lan2 na7 ka7 i khoaN3, hit khoan2 kou-tok8 seng-oah8 e5 lang5 e5 cheng5-heng5 chiu7 chai hit e5 bo5 chhu3-bi7, bo5 sim-sek, bo5 hoaN-hi2 kap chhi-chham2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Sou2-i2 u7 pe7- bu2, hiaN-ti7, chi2-be7, chhin-chhek kap peng5-iu2 e5 lang5 si7 chin heng7-hok, in-ui7 oe7 tit-tioh8 choe7-choe7 hang7 e5 li7-pian7 kap hok-khi3.  Chong2-si7 na7 kan-ta an2-ni, ki5- sit8 si7 iau2 bo5 kau3-giah8, in-ui7 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 hiah e5 lang5 tak8-e5 u7 in e5 keng2-gu7, gi7-bu7, koan5-li7 kap su3-beng7, sou2-i2 tek- khak bo5 thang ng3-bang7 in oe7-tit thang kap lan2 tiam3 kau3 kui-si3-lang5, thang lai5 saN pang-chan7; sou2-i2 Siong7-te3 beh pou2- chiok chit hang7, chiah siat chit e5 kiat- hun e5 hoat-tou7. Sou2-i2 lun7 chit e5 hun-in e5 su7 si7 chin iau3-kin2 kap sin5-seng3, tek- khak m7-thang bo5 ti3-i3 kap hut-liok8 lai5 khoaN3-khin i.    
TaN, lun7 ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 khah-siong5 u7 teh kiaN5 e5 kiat-hun-hoat, iok-liok8 u7 si3- khoan2, chiau3 e7-toe2:   
(1) Chi2-pak ui5-hun.   
(2) Sim-pu7-a2 io.   
(3) Ho2-phaiN2 mng7 sin5-put8 e5 kiat-hun.   
(4) Pe7-bu2 chi2-teng7 e5 kiat-hun.    
Thau5 chit8-hang7 e5 kiat-hun-hoat chiu7-si7 iau2 ti7 pak-tou2-lai7 chiu7 ka7 i tiaN7-tioh8; chiu7-si7 pe7-bu2 kap pe7-bu2 liong2-peng5 chin siong-ho2, koh tu2-tioh8 siang7-geh8 beh siN-kiaN2; sou2-i2 liong2-peng5 e5 pe7-bu2 iok-sok, kong2, na7 chit8-e5 siN ta-pou, iah8 chit8-e5 siNcha-bou2--e5, chiah tai7-ke lai5 saN phit-phoe3.    
Te7-ji7 hang7 e5 kiat-hun, si7 ti7 ka-ki7 e5 kiaN2 iau2 soe3-han3 e5 si5 chiu7 thoe3 i siat- hoat lai5 io chit8 e5 cha-bou2-gin2-na2, tai7-iok kap ka-ki7 e5 ta-pou-kiaN2 ni5-he3 beh siang7- khoan2.  Ti7 iau2 soe3-han3 e5 si5 chiu7 ka7 i chhoa7 lai5 ka-ki7 e5 ke-lai7 iuN2-chhi7. Chit8- hang7 thang chhe-eng7, iah8 kau3 toa7-han3 chiu7 thang ka7 in sak choe3-tui, thang khah seng2 sou2-hui3.    
Te7-saN hang7, si7 ui7-tioh8 be5-sin3 e5 pe7-bu2 khi3 ka7 i e5 kiaN2 thiu-chhiam, khoaN3-mia7, pok-koa3 kap poah8-poe, mng7 sin5-put8; chiu7-si7 beh hou7 sin5-put8 lai5 thoe3 in e5 kiaN2-ji5 chhe7 bou2. Sou2-i2 na7 sin5-beng5 ka7 in kong2, tioh8 khi3 chhoa7 chit8 e5 oai-chhui3 koh pai2- kha e5 cha-bou2-gin2-na2 lai5 choe3 bou2; sui- jian5 m7-ai3, iau2-ku2 m7-kaN2 keh8 sin5-put8 e5 beng7-leng7, iu5-goan5 kou-put-chiong iah8 tioh8 khi3 chhoa7, chiu7-si7 choan-choan hou7 sin5-put8 chu2-i3 e5 kiat-hun-hoat.    
Te7-si3 khoan2 e5, chiu7-si7 chhut-chai7 pe7- bu2 chu2-i3.  Pe7-bu2 na7 kong2 ho2, chiu7 ho2; na7 pe7-bu2 m7-ai3 chiu7 boe7 seng5. Sou2-i2 u7 lang5 teh pai5-thek, kong2 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 kiat-hun si7 chhin-ke kap chhin-ke, iah8 chhiN-m2 kap chhiN-m2 teh kiat-hun, m7-si7 kiaN2-ji5 teh kiat-hun!    
Lun7 i2-siong7 sou2 kong2 thau5 hit saN- hang7 e5 kiat-hun-hoat tioh8 pai5-thek, iah8 chit e5 te7-si3 hang7 e5 kiat-hun-hoat tioh8 toa7 kai2-liong5 chiah oe7 eng7-tit. ChaiN2-iuN7 goa2 kong2 tioh8 toa7 kai2-liong5? In-ui7 chai7- lai5 e5 ke3-chhoa7 toa7-pou7-hun7 thang kong2 si7 bo5 chiau3 kiat-hun e5 cheng-sin5 teh kiaN5. Pe7-bu2 na7 khoaN3 ho2, chiu7 kiaN2-ji5 long2 m7- kaN2 ui5-keh8.  Theng3-hau7 kau3 chhoa7 lai5 liau2-au7 na7 m7-ho2, chiah khoaN3 si7 beh ka7 i li7-ian5, a2-si7 ka7 i lok-khng3 tiam3 ti7 au7- pang5 chiah koh chhoa7 soe3-i5. Sou2-i2 sin3- chia2 tioh8 soe3-ji7, in-ui7 be7-sin3 to7-li2 e5 lang5 ke3-chhoa7 chhin3-chhai2 oe7 eng7-tit. In khoaN3 chhoa7 bou2, si7 na2 teh boe2 oe5 chit8- iuN7; na7 boe7 hah8-chheng7, sui5-pian7 pian3- oaN7 koh boe2 pat8-khoan2--e5 oe7 eng7-tit. Chong2-si7 sin3 to7-li2 lang5 si7 bo5 siang7-khoan2, in-ui7 lan2 khoaN3 ka-ki7 e5 hu7-jin5- lang5 si7 na2 ka-ki7 e5 kha-chhiu2, sou2-i2 boe7 chhin3-chhai2-tit kan2-pian7 ka7 i  
cham2 hiat- kak8 koh oaN7 sin e5 kha-chhiu2.    
Khong2-hu-chu2 kong2, kun-chu2 e5 to7- li2 si7 tui3 hu-hu7 khi2-tiam2; koh-chai3 Seng3-keng-lai7 iah8 u7 kong2, tioh8 li7-khui pe7-bu2 khi3 kap i e5 bou2 kiat-lian5 choe3 chit8- the2.  An2-ni hou7 lan2 chai-iaN2 ke3-chhoa7 sou2 iau3-kin2 e5 si7 tioh8 tui3-tiong7 hit nng7 e5 beh kiat-choe3 ang-bou2 e5 lang5.  Chong2-si7 lan2 e5 lang5 khah-siong5 m7-si7 an2-ni. U7-e5 m7-si7 teh thoe3 kiaN2-ji5 keng2 ho2 koh gau5 e5 bou2, si7 teh iau3-kin2 lang5 e5 giap8-san2.  Koh- chai3 lu2-chu2 hit-peng5 iah8 si7 an2-ni. U7 hit-khoan2 e5 pe7-bu2 long2 bo5 teh thoe3 in e5 cha- bou2-kiaN2 keng2 ho2 e5 tiong7-hu, kan-ta na7 ho2-giah8 e5 chu2-te7 chiu7 ho2, in-ui7 pheng3- kim thang siu choe7-choe7, na2 chhin- chhiuN7 teh boe7 mih8-kiaN7 chit8-iuN7.    
TaN lan2 chai na7 si7 eng7 chiN5 teh ke3- chhoa7, chu7-jian5 si7 tui3-tiong7 chiN5, bo5 tui3- tiong7 lang5. Na7 an2-ni, chu7-jian5 chiN5 na7 khai-liau2 chiu7 bo5 ai3 tih8 lang5.  ChiN5-chai5 sui-jian5 si7 chin u7 lou7-eng7, koh iau3-kin2 e5 mih8, iau2-ku2 m7-thang khoaN3 chiN5 khah- tang7 ke3 lang5; na7 an2-ni chiu7 chin m7-tioh8.    
Kou2-cha2 ti7 Lo5-ma2-kok kong2 khi2 chit8- pai2 u7 kui2-na7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 teh chu7-hoe7 e5 si5, iah8 tak8 lang5 teh tian2 in e5 kim-gun5 po2-khi3 e5 tat8-chiN5. Tiong-kan chit8 e5 choe3 lau7-bu2 e5 lang5, chhoa7 i nng7 e5 kiaN2 oa2 ti7 sin-piN ka7 in tai7-ke kong2, i sou2 te7-it po2- poe3 e5 si7 hit nng7 e5 kiaN2. Sit8-chai7 chin u7- iaN2; koh-khah kui3-khi3 e5 soan7-chioh8 a2-si7 sim2-mih8 po2-poe3 e5 mih8 iah8-si7 si2--e5; kiat- kiok8 m7-tat8-tioh8 teh oah8 e5 soe3-kiaN2 ban7- hun e5 chit8-hun.  Lang5 na7 oe7 hiau2 tui3- tiong7 kiaN2-ji5 chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 hu7-jin5- lang5, m7 chiah thang cheng3-beng5 kong2 lang5 u7 choe3 ban7-mih8 e5 thau5 e5 chu-keh. Sou2-i2 lan2 choe3 pe7-bu2 e5 lang5 na7 beh ka7 kiaN2-ji5 chhoa7-chhin, a2-si7 ke3 cha-bou2-kiaN2 e5 si5, tek-khak tioh8 chin ti3-i3 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 tek-khak m7-thang eng7 lan2 sou2 thiaN3, sou2 kui3-tiong7, oah8-oah8 e5 hau7-siN a2-si7 cha-bou2-kiaN2, khi3 chhoa7 a2-si7 khi3 ke3 hit-khoan2 si2-si2 boe7 tin2-tang7 e5 chhan5-thou5.    
 
Lan2 tioh8 chai beh cho7-chiaN5 li2-siong2 e5 sia7-hoe7, hit e5 kun-pun2 si7 chai7 kiat-hun; in-ui7 kiat-hun si7 choe3 ka-teng5 e5 ki-chhou2, iah8 ka-teng5 si7 kok-ka kap sia7-hoe7 e5 kun- goan5. TaN li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5 sim2-mih8 lang5 u7 kian3-siat? Seng3-keng kong2, "U ti3-sek e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 oe7 kian3-ti3 ka-teng5; gu5-gong7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 tian-to3 pai7-hoai7. Lang5 na7 beh cho7-chiaN5 li2-siong2 e5 sia7-hoe7, tioh8 tai7-seng kian3-siat li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5; iah8 li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5 e5 iau3-sou3 chiu7-si7 li2-siong2 e5 hu-hu7. Seng3-keng kong2, "Ho2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 choe3 tiong7-hu e5 eng5-kng, na2 thau5-khak ti3 bian2-liu5; phaiN2--e5 si7 hou7 tiong7-hu kian3-siau3, chhin-chhiuN7 kut i2-keng hiu2-noa7." Sit8-chai7 chin u7-iaN2; chhin-chhiuN7 chit-khoan2 put-hian5 koh phaiN2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, m7-na7 i pun2-sin tioh8 siu7 thi2-chhio3, chiu7 i e5 tiong7-hu kap ke- lai7 e5 toa7-soe3 to soa3 oe7 siu7 lian5-lui7 lai5 siu7 phoe-pheng5.  Sou2-i2 lun7 ho2-phaiN2 e5 hu7-jin5- lang5 hit e5 eng2-hiong2, tui3 bak8-cheng5 lai5 khoaN3, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng sou2 kong2, chiu7-si7 toa7 koan-he7 ti7 i e5 tiong7-hu kap ka-teng5, chong2-si7 na7 ka7 i khoaN3 khah-hng7, sit8-chai7 u7 toa7 koan-he7 ti7 kok- ka kap sia7-hoe7 e5 heng-bong5.  
Lun7 ui7-tioh8 phaiN2 bou2 lai5 pai7-hoai7 kok kap biat8-bo5 ka-ki7 e5 siN3-mia7-e5 chin-choe7 kong2 boe7 liau2.  Phi3-ju7 lai5 kong2 chhin- chhiuN7 IuN5 Kui3-hui pai7-hoai7 Tong5-tiau5, iah8 Than2-ki2 pai7 Tiu7-ong5, koh Po-su7 pai7 Chiu Hiu-ong5.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, "Chha chi ho5-li5, sit chi chhian-li2," sit8-chai7 u7-iaN2.    
Ti7 beh chhoa7-chhin e5 si5 na7 bo5 chin soe3-ji7 chim-chiok tak8-hang7, kan-ta than3 su-iok8 sou2 iau-kiu5 e5, chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 kan-ta kong2 sui2-bou2 e5 lang5 au7-lai5 tek- khak oe7 ui7-tioh8 bou2 e5 sui2 lai5 tu2-tioh8 chhiN-chham2. Seng3-keng kong2, "Sui2- bou2 si7 hou7 bak8-chiu hoaN-hi2." Chhoa7 bou2 na7- si7 kan-ta beh chhoa7 lai5 hou7 bak8-chiu hoaN- hi2 e5 lang5, chiu7 eng-kai tioh8 ai3 khi3 choan- bun5 chhe7 chin sui2-e5 choe3 bou2. Chong2-si7 kiat-hun e5 cheng-sin5 m7-ku2 m7-si7 kan-ta ui7-tioh8 bak8-chiu e5 in-toaN. Goa2 khiok m7-si7 teh khou2-khng3 lang5 chhoa7 bai2-bou2, sou2-i2 ng3-bang7 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-chi2 tek- khak m7-thang gou7-kai2 goa2 e5 chin-i3.  Tioh8 tui3 thau5 khoaN3 kau3 be2, koh iah8 tioh8 chhu2 hit e5 ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 lai5 tit-tioh8 li7-ek chiah ho2; koat-toan3 m7-thang khoaN3 chit8-poaN3- ku3 lai5 jia2 siu7-khi3, ti3-kau3 khoaN3 kap sia2 e5 lang5 long2 bo5 li7-ek.    
 
Goa2 ti7 teng2-bin7 u7 kong2 m7-thang hou7 ka-ki7 e5 kiaN2 kap cha-bou2-kiaN2 kan-ta khi3 chhoa7 giap8-san2 a2-si7 khi3 ke3 ho2-giah8 lang5 e5 i3-su3, bu5-kou3 si7 khou2-khng3 lan2 choe3 pe7- bu2 e5 lang5 tioh8 oe7-hiau2 ti3-i3 liah8 lang5 choe3 chu2 (master) iah8 eng7 chiN5-chai5 choe3 pin (servant) an2-ni na7-tiaN7, choan5-jian5 m7-si7 tiau5-ti5 khou2-khng3 lang5 m7-thang kap ho2- giah8-lang5 ke3-chhoa7 e5 i3-su3, sou2-i2 chhiaN2 lan2 tioh8 liau2-kai2.    
Kong2 khah-beng5 chiu7-si7 lun7 ke3-chhoa7 te7-it tioh8 chun-tiong7 jin5-keh, ti3-tiong7 lai7-chai5 koh tioh8 chhu2 lu2-tek kap ta-pou e5 phin2-heng7 lang5-chai5. Na7-si7 lun7 kau3 ho2-giah8 a2-si7 san3-hiong, chit khoan2 si7 hu3- siok8 e5 bun7-toe5, koat-toan3 m7-si7 kiat-hun  e5 kun-pun2 e5 bun7-toe5. ChiN5 e5 lai7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 to, sou2-i2 na7 u7 lang5 gau5 ka7 i oah8-eng7 khiok si7 chin u7 li7-ek lang5, koh iah8 oe7 pang-chan7 toe7-hng, kok-ka kap sia7-hoe7 e5 hoat-tat8.  Chong2-si7 na7 ka7 i lam7-sam2 eng7 chiu7 m7-na7 oe7 hai7-si2 ka-ki7 e5 siN3-mia7, koh iah8 oe7 pai7-hoai7 hong-siok8 kap hai7-tioh8 kok-ka chham sia7-hoe7.    
Siok8-gu2 kong2, "U7 chiN5 sai2 kui2, oe7 oe- bo7;" chong2-si7 goa2 kong2, Bo5-iaN2.  Chhi3-siuN7 lang5 na7 ai3 tit-tioh8 ho2 e5 kiaN2, na7 m7-si7 oe7- hiau2 eng7 ho2 e5 hoat-tou7 lai5 in2-chhoa7 ka3- si7, koh-chai3 oe7 hiau2 hou7 kiaN2-ji5 chiu7-kun7 ho2 koh gau5 e5 sian-siN lai5 tit-tioh8 ho2 e5 kau3-iok8, chham tit-tioh8 ho2 e5 kam2-hoa3- lat8, kan-ta oa2-kho3 lan2 e5 ho2-giah8; chhin- chhiuN7 an2-ni, oe7 tit-tioh8 gau5 koh ho2 e5 kiaN2-ji5 mah? An2-ni cheng3-beng5 chiN5 m7- si7 ban7-leng5, sou2-i2 kan-ta oa2-kho3 chiN5 e5 khui3-lat8 beh lai5 tit-tioh8 li2-siong2 e5 ke3- chhoa7, kiam2-chhai2 khah-oh. Chong2 kong2, li2-siong2-tek e5 kiat-hun si7 jin5-keh kap jin5-keh e5 kiat-hap8, hu-hu7 e5 seng-oah8 si7 ai3 e5 seng-oah8.  Lang5 tiam3 ti7 se3-kan choe3 te7-it kong-eng5--e5, m7-si7 hit khoan2 oe7 koan2-hat choe7-choe7 lang5 a2-si7 oe7 hou7 choe7-choe7 lai5 hok8-sai7 kap kun-sui5 i e5, tian-to3 si7 hit khoan2 oe7 hok8-sai7 cheng3-lang5--e5.    
Kong2 khah-beng5 chiu7-si7 gau5 choe3 lang5 e5 chhe-eng7, gau5 ui7-tioh8 pat8-lang5 e5 heng7-hok kap hiong3-siong7 lai5 chhut-lat8 e5 lang5, chiah thang kong2 u7 tat8 jin5-seng e5 bok8-tek, koh-chai3 chit khoan2 e5 cheng- sin5 tu2-tu2 si7 lan2 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 chin-chhoe2. Sou2-i2 lang5 na7 oe7-hiau2 chiau3 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 cheng-sin5 lai5 kian3-siat sin-ka-teng5, chiu7  
tu2-tu2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hit khoan2 gau5 e5 lang5 khi2-chhu3 ti7 chioh8-poaN5-teng2 e5 khoan2.  In-ui7 hu-hu7 oe7-hiau2 saN hok8- sai7, chiu7-si7 ban7-hang7 tai7-chi3 choe3 tiong7- hu e5 lang5 oe7-hiau2 thiaN3 bou2, iah8 bou2 oe7- hiau2 the2-thiap tiong7-hu, tak8 hang7 saN chiau3-kou3 saN hong7-su7.  Na7 ko2-jian5 oe7 an2-ni, beh cho7-chiaN5 li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5 si7 bo5 oh.    
Chiau3 hian7-si5 Loe7-te7 e5 thong2-ke3-piau2 ka7 i khoaN3, lun7 tui3 lu2-oh8 chut-giap8 liau2-au7 khoaN3 ai3 kap sim2-mih8 teng2-ho7 e5 lang5 kiat-hun, chap8-lang5 e5 tiong-kan u7 oa2 poeh, kau2 lang5 hi-bong7 ai3 kap ho2-giap8 lang5 kiat-hun.    
Tui3 an2-ni ka7 i khoaN3 chiu7 chai-iaN2, hian7-tai7 ti7 Loe7-te7 e5 lu2-chu2 toa7-pou7-hun7 long2 iau2 bo5 sit8-chai7 e5 kak-chhiN2. Sou2-i2 beh kap lam5-chu2 piN5-teng2 iau2 bo5 hiah-nih8 khoai3, in-ui7 choe7-choe7 lang5 iau2 put-chi2 ai3 choe3 lang5 e5 thit-tho5 mih8, siuN7 ai3 m7- bian2 choe3 kang, u7 thang chhia-hoa5 ian3-lok8. Chong2-si7 lun7 lu2-chu2 e5 su-siong2, m7-na7 kan-ta Jit8-pun2 an2-ni, phah-sng3 lan2 Tai5- oan5 kaN2 iah8 si7 chha iu2-hian7.  Lun7 m7-bian2 choe3 kang, u7 thang chiah8 koh oe7-tit thang pheng3 ka-ki7 e5 i3-su3 lai5 chhia-chhi2, eng7 chiN5 li7-pian7, u7 cha-bou2-kan2 thang chhe; chit-khoan2 e5 sou2-chai7 khah-choe7 lang5 ai3 khi3, khiok si7 bo5 ki5-koai3.  Chong2-si7 na7 koh chim-chiok ka7 i siuN7, au7-lai5 oe7 tu2-tioh8 chhiN-chham2, kan-khou2, oan-ong2, bo5 te3 thang kong2.  Am3-chiN7 ka-ki7 thi5- khau3 lau5 bak8-sai2 hou7-hoe2 (regret) e5 hu7- jin5-lang5, khah choe7 iah8 si7 ti7 chit khoan2 sou2 kong2 siong7-liu5 sia7-hoe7 e5 ka-teng5-lai7.    
Na7 bo5 sit8-chai7 tit-tioh8 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 chin-li2 e5 ka3-si7 e5 ho2-giah8 chu2-te7, khah choe7 chhoa7-bou2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh oaN7-saN chit8-iuN7; chit8-e5 oaN7 ke3 chit8-e5, put-si5 khi3-ku7 oaN7-sin. Sou2-i2 ke3 ho2-giah8 e5 lang5 si7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 teh liam-khau chit8-iuN7, chiu7-si7 i e5 un7-beng7 put-teng7.  Na7 tu2- tioh8 hou7 tiong7-hu tek-thiong2 si7 ban7-heng7; na7 bo5, si7 oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 kou2-cha2 e5 hong5-hou7 teh kim3-loh8 leng2-keng chit8-iuN7. Na7 tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 keng2-gu7 e5 chi2-be7 chiu7 chai-iaN2 hit e5 chhiN-chham2 kau3 chaiN2-iuN7. Na7 bo5, ma7 bat khoaN3 tioh8 hi3 teh poaN.    
Tang-iuN5 lang5 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 hou7 chit e5 ka-chok8 chu2-gi7 hai7-tioh8, chiu7-si7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 ku7-koan3 (Conventionalism) ti3-kau3 ka-teng5 boe7 kai2-liong5, in-ui7 koh khah choe7 lang5 to si7 long2 tiam3 choe3-hoe2, iah8 sui-jian5 u7 si3-chap8 he3 e5 kiaN2-ji5 iah8 i2- 
keng chhoa7-bou2 siN-kiaN2, lian5 i e5 kiaN2 chha-put- to i2-keng oa2 ti7 beh ho2 chhoa7-chhin lah, chong2-si7 na7-si7 pe7-bu2 iau2 ti7-teh e5 si5, sui- jian5 si7 poeh-chap8-he3 iah8 si7 tak8-hang7 tai7- chi3 tioh8 hou7 i chu2-i3 siat-hoat.  Chhin- chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 chu2-gi7 e5 sia7-hoe7 beh- thai2 u7 ng3-bang7 thang tit-tioh8 li2-siong2 e5 ka-teng5?
