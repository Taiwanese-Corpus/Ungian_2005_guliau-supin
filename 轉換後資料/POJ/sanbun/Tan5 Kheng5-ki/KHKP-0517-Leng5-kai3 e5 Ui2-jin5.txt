Leng5-kai3 e5 Ui2-jin5
Tan5 Kheng5-ku  
Tai5-oan5 ku7-lai7 e5 chong-kau3, thang kong2 si7 Ju5-kau3, To7-kau3, kap Put8-kau3  chit saN hang7. Lun7 Ju5-kau3 si7 eng7 to7- tek choe3 kun-pun2, si7 pai3 thiN kap che3  chou2-sian. To7-kau3 si7 sin3 sia5-sin5, liam7- chiu3, hoah-thou2, ang-i5, to7-su7, kap pok- koa3 teng2-teng2.  Put8-kau3 si7 chhai7 ngou2- siong7, kian3-siat bio7-u2, sio-kim sio-hiuN.  
TaN pun2-to2 lang5 ui7-tioh8 chit saN-hang7 e5 ka3-si7, peh-siN3 khah choe7 tui3-tiong7 be5- sin3, sui-jian5 chhui3 kong2 to7-tek, na7-si7 sou2-kiaN5 khah choe7 si7 bo5 chiau3 an2-ni.   Chhin-chhiuN7 thak8-chheh lang5 khah- choe7 chiah8 a-phian3, chhoa7 soe3-i5, lim- thin5 kong3-phah, tian-to3 sou2 choe3 khah- choe7 long2 si7 bo5 hap8 to7-tek e5 heng5-ui5, song3-sit chin e5 chong-kau3-sim. Ti7 Ju5- kau3 u7 nng7 hong-bin7, chit8 hong-bin7 si7 kau3-iok8, koh chit8 hong-bin7 si7 chong- kau3.  TaN kau3-iok8 hit hong-bin7, sou2 oh8-e5 si7 Sam-ju7-keng, Chhian-ji7-bun5, Su3-su7 kap Ngou2-keng teng2-teng2 e5 chheh. Bok8- tek si7 kho2-kau3, chin3 siu3-chai5, tiong3-ku2 a2-si7 chiong7-goan5 kap choe3-koaN. Koh chong-kau3 hong-bin7, chong2 kong2 si7 tui3- tiong7 che3-su7; chhin-chhiuN7 “sin7-chiong  tui-oan2” si7 in e5 tek-gi7, che3-hian3 chou2- sian si7 choe3 in hau3-to7 e5 chit8-hang7.  
Ti7 Tai5-oan5, lun7 Ju5-kau3, Put8-kau3 kap To7-kau3, sui-bong2 si7 kong2 sam-kau3,  chong2-si7 ki5-sit8 lang5 teh pai3, long2 bo5 u7 khak-sit8 e5 hun-piat, chiu7-si7 khah-siong5  long2 pai5-pai5 ti7 siang7 toh-teng2, than3 tak8 lang5 e5 sou2 ai3 lai5 che3 lai5 keng3. Phi3-ju7 lai5 kong2, Koan-te3-kun, i si7 sam-kok si5- tai7 sam-kiat8 e5 chit8-e5, chiu7-si7 Koan U2, iah8 lang5 chheng i choe3 Koan-kong.  Lang5 ka7 i khi2 bio7 lai5 hok8-sai7, in-ui7 i chin u7 tiong-gi7, sou2-i2 chun-keng3 i choe3 gi7 e5 tai7-piau2-chia2.  Koh lun7 Seng5-hong5, lang5 iah8 si7 chin tui3-tiong7, na7-si7 choe3 koaN e5 lang5 koh-khah ti3-i3 hok8-sai7, in-ui7 chiau3 in siuN7 chit se3-kan u7 choe3 koaN--e5, iah8 ti7 im-kan iu5-goan5 tek-khak u7 Seng5- hong5-ia5.  Sou2-i2 ti7 ku7-lek8 5 geh8 13 jit8, long2 tioh8 kau3 Seng5-hong5-bio7 khi3 heng5-hiuN.  
 
I2-siog7 sou2 kong2--e5, si7 Ju5-kau3 e5 sin5- beng5. Lun7 To7-kau3 si7 chiau3 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2, u7 chin-choe7 e5 iN7-toan khian3- sng2. Put8-kau3 e5 tiong-kan, chhin-chhiuN7  hok8-sai7 Thian-siong7 seng3-bu2 (Ma2-chou2), i e5 lau7-pe7 si7 teh kiaN5 chun5, koh i pun2- sin bat kap lau7-pe7 sai2 chun5, sou2-i2 kiaN5 chun5 e5 lang5 long2 u7 hok8-sai2 Ma2-chou2, si7 liah8 i choe3 po2-hou7 khong3-hai2 e5 lu2-sin5.  Chong2-si7 au7-lai5 bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5, soa3 iu5-goan5 iah8 teh hok8-sai7. Koh lang5 iah8 u7 hok8-sai7 Koan-im, in-ui7 chin u7 chu5-pi, thiaN3-thang3 e5 sim, sou2-i2 lang5- lang5 e5 chhui3 to teh liam7 kong2 “tai7-chu5 tai7-pi, kiu3 lan7 kiu3 khou2, Koan-im Put8- chou2.” Lan2 lang5 loan7-loan7 hok8-sai7 long2 bo5 hun-piat, chhin-chhiuN7 peng5-siong5 lang5 lai5 hok8-sai7 kiaN5 chun5 e5 sin5 beh choe2 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7?  Koh ti7 tak8 lang5 e5 ke-lai7 long2 u7 hok8-sai7 Chau3-kun, Chhng5-bu2, ThiN-kong, Jit8-thau5-kong, Chhit-niu5-ma2, Lui5-kong, Thou2-ti7-kong, Ho2-hiaN-ti7 teng2-teng2, kong2 boe7 liau2.  TaN ti7 chit khoan2 be5-sin3 kek8 chhim e5 sou2-chai7, Kai Bok8-su eng7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 lai5 thoan5 to7-li2?  Tai7-liok8 lai5 kong2, Kai Bok8-su u7 eng7 saN-khoan2 e5 hong-hoat lai5 pang-chan7 i e5 Thian-kok kang: (1) Siat Hak8-hau7.  (2) Siat PiN7- iN7. (3) Hong-siok8 e5 kai2-liong5.  
1.Te7-it tai7-seng eng7 ko-teng2 phou2- thong e5 hak8-bun7 lai5 ka3-si7 Pak-pou7 Tai5- oan5 e5 peh-siN3, chiu7-si7 Kai Bok8-su na7- tiaN7. I sou2 kian3-ti3 e5 hak8-hau7 u7 Li2-hak8- tong5 Tai7-chu-iN7 (Oxford College), chit keng hian7-si5 teh choe3 Tam7-sui2 Tiong-oh8  e5 ku7 hau7-sia3.  Sim2-mih8 in-toaN, i siat hak8 hau7?  Chiu7-si7 m7-na7 kan-ta beh ka3- si7 Pun2-to2 lang5 bat hak8-bun7 na7-tiaN7, sit8- chai7 si7 beh iong2-seng5 Pun2-toe7 lang5 thang lai5 tau3 thoan5 Ki-tok e5 Hok-im choe3 toa7 e5 bok8-tek.  Chong2-si7 kan-ta iong2-seng5 ta-pou-lang5 bo5 kau3-giah8, in-ui7  Hok-im m7-na7 beh thoan5 hou7 lam5-chu2, koh iah8 beh kiu3 lu2-chu2; sou2-i2 Kai Bok8- su iah8 chin ti3-i3 ai3 iong2-seng5 lu2 thoan5- to7, tui3 an2-ni i iah8 u7 khi2 Lu2-hak8-tong5  (Tha Girls’ School), chiu7-si7 hian7-si5 Tam7- sui2 Lu2 Hak8-iN7 e5 chian5-sin.  Lun7 hit-si5 e5 Lu2-hak8-tong5 khiok m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hian7-si5 kong2 kan-ta si7 siau3-lian5 e5 lu2-chu2 thang jip8 na7-tiaN7; chiu7-si7 bo5-lun7 lau7 a2-si7 siau3-lian5, u7 chhut-ke3 a2-si7 iau2-be7 chhut-ke3, long2 piN5-piN5 oe7 eng7-tit.  
 
I2-goa7 iau2 u7 siat Sio2-oh8.  Lun7 kho3- theng5, tu5-khi3 Sio2-oh8 i2-goa7, khah choe7  si7 ka3 li2-hoa3, kim-chioh8-hak8, phok-but8, thian-bun5, te7-li2, lek8-su2, sou3-hak8 teng2- teng2.  Chong2-si7 tiong-kan choe3 te7-it iau3-kin2 e5 si7 to7-li2, chiu7-si7 Seng3-keng.  
2.Koh lun7 pang-chan7 i e5 Thian- kok kang e5 te7-ji7 hang7 si7 siat PiN7-iN7. Lun7  Tai5-oan5 cha2-si5 i-seng chin chio2, sou2-i2 lang5 teh taN2-tiap8 piN7-thiaN3 e5 hoat-tou7 chin kho2-lin5, siong5-siong5 kan-ta oah8- oah8 khoaN3 i si2. Sou2-i2 Kai Bok8-su kin2 siat I-koan2, mia5 kio3-choe3 Kai I-koan2.  Eng7 Se-iuN5 bun5-beng5 e5 i-hoat lai5 i-ti7 piN7-lang5, koh long2 bo5 theh8 lang5 e5 ioh8- chiN5, sou2-i2 tui3 hou7 i taN2-tiap8 peN7 lai5 sin3 to7-li2 e5 lang5 boe7 sng3-tit.  Kai Bok8-su iah8 ti3-i3 ka3-si7 i e5 hak8-seng i-piN7 e5 hoat-tou7, sou2-i2 kou2-cha2 e5 thoan5-to7 tak8 e5 to oe7-hiau i-hoat.  
3.Lun7 hong-siok8 e5 kai2-liong5, chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 kim3-chiu2, toan7-ian, liu5-kha i2-kip8 i2-goa7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 kiau2-hong- hoe7. Ti7 kin-na2-jit8 iau2 piN7 thau5-chang- be2 (pig-tail) e5 lang5, thang kong2 tu5-khi2 chit8 poaN3 e5 lau7-lang5, a2-si7 tiam3 ti7 soaN- kan e5 chhu2-toe7 lang5 i2-goa7, long2 bo5 lah, koh hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 pak8-kha, ma7 si7 ti7 kin- na2-jit8 khah choe7 long2 bo5.  
TaN lun7 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 chiah e5 hong-siok8 kai2-liong5 oe7 chiah-nih8 kin2  phou2-kip8, che toa7-pou7-hun7 si7 ui7-tioh8 Tai5- oan5 e5 cheng3-hu2 u7 ti3-i3 ti7 chit hong-bin7;  sou2-i2 eng7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 hoat-tou7, chhin- chhiuN7 u7-si5 eng7 chiong2-le7, iah8 u7-si5 eng7  kiong5-che3 e5 hong-hoat lai5 kai2-tu5 chit khoan2 phaiN2 hong-siok8 sou2-ti3, chong2-si7 pi2 cheng3-hu2 khah tai7-seng chu3-i3 ti7 chit hong-bin7--e5, si7 lan2 e5 Ki-tok-kau3. Koh lan2 sou2 eng7 e5 hoat-tou7, khah siong5 long2 si7 eng7 chiong2-le7 na7-tiaN7. In-ui7 chit khoan2 hong-siok8 kai2-liong5 choe3 te7-it tai7-seng ti3-i3 e5, chiu7-si7 Kai Bok8-su.  
TaN kiat-be2 lai5 kong2, ti7 50 ni5 cheng5 tan-jin5 tok8-ma2 tui3 hng7-hng7 lai5 Tai5-oan5,  na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chng3-jip8 hou2-khang e5 Kai Bok8-su, in-ui7 si3-bin7 bo5-chhin bo5- chhek, bo5 peng5-iu2, sou2 u7 e5 long2 si7 tui3- tek8, chiu7-si7 ai3 thi2-chhio3 i, khun2-tiok8 i,  kap ai3 hai7-si2 i e5 lang5 na7-tiaN7.  Chhin- chhiuN7 an2-ni, chaiN2-iuN7 i oe7 kian3-ti3 chiah-  
nih8 toa7 e5 chogn-kau3 su7-giap8 ah? Che sit8-chai7 si7 Siong7 Chu2 u7 khoaN3-kou3 I chin7- tiong e5 lou5-pok8, si5-khek teh pang-chan7 i, koh chit hang7, chiu7-si7 ui7-tioh8 i u7 jim2- nai7, un-khun5 kap u7 hi-seng e5 cheng-sin5 chiah oe7 an2-ni. Chhin-chhiuN7 Kai Bok8- su chit khoan2 e5 chong-kau3-ka, sit8-chai7 thang chheng-choe3 Leng5-kai3 e5 chin Ui2- jin5.  Koh i iah8 thang chheng-choe3 thoan5-siu7 Au Bi2 e5 bun5-hoa3 hou7 lan2 Pak-pou7 Tai5-oan5 e5 lang5 e5 si2-chou2 lah.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 517 koan3,1928 ni5 4 geh8)
