Hian7-chai7 e5 A-mi-chok8 
Lim5 Chheng-liam5
A-mi-chok8, chiu7-si7 tiam3 ti7 tang Tai5-oan5 khia7-khi2 e5 goan5-chu7-bin5, in goan5-bun2 bin7 bo5 chhiah-ji7, ia7 bo5 thai5 lang5 e5 hong-siok8; in sou2 tiam3, si7 ti7 piN5-iuN5 soaN-kha e5 sou2-chai7, sou2-i2 lang5 ka7 in hun-biat8 si7 “ke7-soaN hoan.”  (Pat8 khoan2, goan5-pun2 lam5-lu2, bin7 long2 tioh8 chhiah-ji7, ia7 siong5-siong5 ai3 thai5 lang5, seng-oah8 si7 tiam3 ti7 soaN-lai7, sou2-i2 lang5 ka7 i hun-biat8 si7 “koan5-soaN hoan”).
 TaN goa2 sou2 teh kong2--e5, si7 choan kong2 “ke7-soaN hoan (A-mi-chok8”).  Tui3 Hoe-lian5-kang2 kau3 Tai5-tang, chit8 choa7 piN5-iuN5, i2-kip8 ian5 hai2-piN, si3-koe3 long2 u7 A-mi-chok8 teh khia7-khi2 (tai7-iok u7 oa2 5 ban7).  Lun7 in e5 seng-oah8, choan5-pou7 si7 keng-long5, boe7-hiau2 choe3 seng-li2, ta-pou--e5,u7-si5 ai3 liah8; cha-bou2--e5, long2 tioh8 choh-sit.
 Lun7 kau3-iok8, koaN-hu2 ti7 tak8 sou2-chai7 long2 u7 ka7 in siat kong-hak8-hau7.  Koh ti7 tak8 e5 hoan-sia7 u7 siat ia-hak8, hou7 siau3-lan5 e5 lam5-lu2 oh8 kok-gu2, in chin hoaN-hi2 oh8 kok-gu2, oe7 kong2 kok-gu2 e5 siau3-lan5 lang5 khah choe7, oe7 kong2 han3-oe7--e5 chin chio2.
 Lun7 in e5 khi2-chhu3, pun2 si7 soe3-a2-keng, chhu3-lai7 eng7 tin5, koaN-chin pak8 pin2-a2 choe3 khong3-chhng5, tiong-ng lau5 chit8 e5 khang-toe7 thang khi2 he2 hang sio.  Hian7-chai7 teh kai2-cheng3, chiam7-chiam7 teh khi2 Tang-iuN5-sek, thang-a2-mng5, po-le5-tou2 thoah-mng5, chong2-si7 goa7-bin7 khoaN3 chhin-chhiuN7 Tang-iuN5-sek, lai7-bin7 khiok bo5 siang7.  Chong2-si7 kau3 chit cham7, chiu7 teh-beh gui5-hiam2 lah, in peng5-si5 si7 chin khiam7, chong2-si7 i2-keng u7 lang5 chhu3 khi2 chin song2, chiah8, chheng7 kek8 iu-teng2--e5, bo5 kui2-ni5 e5 tiong-kan, chhan5 long2 boe7--khi3, chhu3 ia7 soa3 boe7--khi3, ke-he2 long2 khai liau2-liau2.  KiaN-liau2 au7-lai5 ia7 oe7 choe7-choe7 lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni.
 Lun7 chong-kau3.  In sou2 hok8-sai7--e5, khah iau3-kin2 si7 chou2-kong, tak8-pai2 na7 siu-tang liau2-au7, chiu7 thong sia7 lian5-hap8 chiah8 chiu2 thiau3-bu2, ki3-liam7 in e5 chou2-kong, ia7 piau2-beng5 kam2-sia7 cho7-but8 Chu2, u7-e5 u7 hok8-sai7 lok8-thau5 a2-si7 ti7-thau5.  Ti7 Giok8-li2 chi-thiaN koan2-lai7 e5 A-mi-chok8 in e5 chhu3-lai7 khah choe7 u7 chhai7 sin5-sia7, ia7 ti7 choe7-choe7 e5 chng-thau5 u7 khi2 sin5-sia7, ke3-chhoa7 e5 si5,chiu7 khi3 sin5-sia7 chham-pai3, keng3-chhatmkoaN5 lip8-hoe7, chhin-chok8 ia7 long2 te3 khi3 khoaN3, an2-ni choe3 kiat-hun-sek.  Chong3-sek ia7 u7-e5 chhiaN2 he5-siuN7 lai5 liam7-keng, i2-keng u7 A-mi-chok8 e5 lang5 choe3 Tang-iuN-sek e5 he5-siuN7, siong5-siong5 hou7 lang5 chhiaN2 khi3 hu3 chong3-sek.
 Lun7 thoan5-kau3 ti7 A-mi-chok8 e5 tiong-kan, chit-tiap8 tu2 teh khai-chian3, seng5-chek ho2, i2-keng u7 tit-tioh8 kui2-na7 lang5 lai5 sin3.  Hian7-si5 na7 pou3-to7 ti7 in tiong-kan, hek8-si7 ko3-jin5 a2-si7 thoan5-the2, long2 ho2 ki-hoe7.  Ku7-ni5 12 geh8, Pou3-to7-thoan5 lai5 Koan-im-soaN e5 si5, goa2 u7 chhoa7 in khi3 tham3 hoan-sia7.  Hit-si5 u7 kui2-na7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 choe3-tin7, chiong5-tiong chit8 lang5 PhoaN--si7 A-moe2.  Lau5 Thian-lai5 TiuN2-lo2 chhiaN2 i choe3 thong-ek8, chiu7 jip8-khi3 in e5 chhu3-lai7, keng-ke3 kui2-nake7, kong2 to7-li2 hou7 in thiaN, in thiaN-liau2 chin hoaN-hi2, long2 bo5 hoan2-tui3, o-lo2 goan2 sou2 kong2--e5, si7 chin u7-iaN2, ia7 hian2-beng5 kam2-sia7 e5 khoan2, kong2, Kai-chai3 lin2 lai5 po3 goan2 chai, si7 kio3-chhiN2 goan2 e5 sim.  Lun7 thoan5-the2 e5 pou3-to7, khoaN3 Khou2 Lam5-bian2 sang3 sou2 po3--e5, chiu7 chai.  Lun7 thoan5-kau3 ti7 A-mi-chok8, chiong-lai5 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 chin3-heng5, phi2-jin5 siong5 teh gian2-kiu3, ng3-bang7 au7-geh8 beh chiap-soa3 koh sia2 tam7-poh8 hou7 liat8-ui7 choe3 chham-kho2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 Te7 602 Ho7, ‘Koa3-chhai3-chi2 Po3’ Te7 112 Ho7, 1935 ni5 5 goeh8)
