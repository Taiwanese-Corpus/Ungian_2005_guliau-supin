Jiat8-sim
K. S.
Goa2 tui3 beng5-pek8 Kiu3-chu2 e5 in-tian2 kau3 taN, siong5-siong5 siuN7 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7  hok8-sai7 Chu2.  Chai7 goa2 phah-sng3 lang5 eng-kai tioh8 jiat8-sim, na7-si7 goa2 m7 kaN2  koat-toan3 kong2; in-ui7 khoaN3-kiN3 che7-che7  lang5 liah8 hak8-bun7 khah iau3-kin2 ti7 chit- e5 jiat8-sim, liah8 chiN5-chai5 khah tui3-tiong7 ti7 chit-e5 jiat8-sim.  Koh u7 lang5 sui-bong2  jiat8-sim, na7-si7 sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 pi2 bo5 jiat8-sim e5 lang5, hoan2-tng2 koh-khah che7 chho3- gou7.  Sou2-i2 bo5 oe7 thang pai5-kai2.  Chong2-si7  goa2 m7 kaN2 an2-ni soah, tauh8-tauh8 siong5-se3  siuN7, hek8-si7 tioh8 jiat8-sim, a2-si7 kho2-i2 bian2.   In-ui7 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 kap goa2 u7 kan-siap8,  na7 tioh8 jiat8-sim, goa2 chiu7 beh kiu5 Siong7-te3 pang-chan7 goa2 jiat8-sim, na7-si7 m7-bian2,  goa2 chiu7 bo5-beh.  Kau3 ti7 kin-a2-jit8, goa2  e5 sim i2-keng koat-toan3, chai goa2 tek- khak tioh8 jiat8-sim, be7-tit thang the-si5-tit.   Sou2-i2 goa2 ai3 chiong goa2 chhian2-chhian2 e5  i3-su3, pai5-liat8 ti7 hiaN-ti7 e5 bin7-cheng5, ia7 ai3  liat8-ui7 lai5 chhi3-siuN7, khoaN3 sou2-kong2--e5  hap8-gi5 a2-bo5 hap8-gi5, ng3-bang7 chu-ui7 ko- beng5 saN chi2-si7.  
Goa2 siuN7 na7 m7 jiat8-sim, chiu7 tioh8 leng2- sim.  Leng2-sim si7 chaiN2-iuN7, chiu7-si7 iah8  Chu2 e5 tai7-chi3 cho3 bo5 iau3-kin2, choan-choan siuN7 ka-ti7 e5 tai7-chi3.  Chong2-si7 khoaN3- kau3 Chu2 e5 in-tian2 ti7 goa2 chiah toa7, goa2  lai5 liah8 I-e5 tai7-chi3 cho3 bo5 iau3-kin2, khoan2- sit bo5 siaN2 e7 cho3-tit.  Chu2 in-ui7 goa2 hou7  lang5 hai7 si2, the3 goa2 tam-tng Siong7-te3 e5  khian3-chek, ka-ti7 lai5 kan-khou2, hou7 goa2  thang khoaN3-oah8; chit-ho7 e5 in-cheng5, goa2  thang pang3 cho3 be7 ki3-tit?  Sou2-i2 lang5 na7  ka3-si7 goa2 tioh8 leng2-sim, goa2 koat-toan3 bo5  beh sin3, chiu7-si7 kong2, Goa2 tioh8 jiat8-sim.  
Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 kong2, “Goa2 khoaN3  bou2-lang5 khoan2-sit bo5 sim2-mih8 thang  hiam5, put-ko3 bo5 thang kong2 si7 jiat8-sim  na7-tiaN7.  An2-ni ia7-si7 bo5 iau3-kin2; lang5 mih- sai2 tioh8 kau3 chin jiat8-sim?  Seng3-keng kong2, ‘Sin3 chiu7 tit-tioh8 kiu3’, bo5 kong2,  “Jiat8-sim chiah e7 tit-tioh8 kiu3”.  Sou2-i2 lang5  na7 sin3, an2-ni chiu7 kau3-giah8; bo5 jiat8-sim  bo5 sim2-mih8 thang chek-pi7.  Put-ko3 boh8-tit siuN leng2-sim chiu7 ho2”.  Goa2 chiu7  kong2,  
Seng3-sin5 chi2-si7 Lo2-ti7-ka e5 kau3-hoe7  kong2, “Lin2 bo5-leng2 bo5-jiat8, goa2 beh thou3- chhut”.  Chu2 iu5-goan5 hiam5 hit-ho7 bo5-leng2 bo5-jiat8 e5 lang5.  Sou2-i2 lang5 na7 kong2  boh8-tit leng2-sim, ia7 m7-bian2 siuN jiat8-sim, hit-ho7 lang5 chiu7-si7 bo5-leng2 bo5-jiat8 e5 lang5;  Chu2 beng5-beng5 chi2-si7 i kong2, Goa2 m7-tih8,  “goa2 beh thou3-chhut”.  
Tui3 an2-ni lai5 khoaN3, leng2-sim be7 eng7-tit, bo5 leng2 bo5 jiat8 ia7 be7 eng7-tit; chiok-kian3  lang5 tek-khak tioh8 jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2.  
Kong2 kau3 chia, goa2 iau2 u7 chit8-nng7 ku3  oe7 ai3 kong2.  Chiau3 goa2 tu2-a2 sou2 kong2, lang5 tek-khak tioh8 jiat8-sim,na7-si7 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 cho3 chiah ho7-cho3 jiat8-sim, chit-hang7 bi7-bian2 iau2-si7 tiu5-tu5 e5 tai7-chi3.  Lan2-chai u7 lang5 chhut-lat8 thoan5-to7, chhut-lat8 po3- iong5 hok-im, si7 toa7 chin2-che3, thiaN3 lang5  chhin-chhiuN7 ka-ti7, chit-ho7 lang5 ti7 kau3-hoe7 chheng-cho3 jiat8-sim.  Na7-si7 iau2-ku2  u7 lang5 sui-bong2 chhut-lat8 kiu5 hak8-bun7,  chhut-lat8 kiu5 chai5-li7, chong2-si7 kau3-boe2  eng7 i-e5 hak8-bun7 chai5-li7 toa7 li7-ek Chu2 e5  kau3-hoe7; chit-ho7 e5 lang5 kiam2 chiu7 tek-khak m7-si7 jiat8-sim e5 lang5?  Chit-e5 tai7-chi3 sit8-chai7 si7 hui3-kai2, goa2 boe7 si7 toa7 jiat8-sim e5 lang5, m7-kaN2 kou2-chhiaN2 lai5 gi7-lun7.   Chong2-si7 chiau3 goa2 gu5-chhun2 e5 i3-kian3,  lang5 na7 e7 kham-tit ka-ti7 kong2, goa2 an2-ni cho3 u7 chhut-lat8 teh hok8-sai7 goa2 e5  Chu2 Ki-tok, an2-ni chiu7 kau3-giah8; bo5 lun7  sou2 cho3 si7 su7, long5, kong, siong, sou2 kiaN5  si7 kap lang5 saN-tang5 koh-iuN7, na7-si7 u7 chin7-sim teh hok8-sai7 Chu2, chiu7-si7 jiat8-sim teh hok8-sai7 Chu2 e5 lang5.  Na7-bo5, sui-jian5 chaiN2-iuN7 li7-ek kau3-hoe7, si7 chhin-chhiuN7  Iu5-thai3 lang5 hai7-si2 Kiu3-chu2, chhin-chhiuN7  Iu5-tai7 be7 Ia5-sou.  Hit-ho7 e5 lang5, Chu2 bo5  liah8 i cho3 I-e5 peh-siN3.  Siat-su2 kiaN-liau2  jiat8-sim cho3 su7 khah ai3 chho3-gou7, m7 tat8- tioh8 bo5 jiat8-sim khah bo5 hoan7-hiam5 e5  tai7-chi3; chit-ho7 e5 i3-su3 m7-si7 Chu2 sou2 hoaN- hi2.  Goa2 sin3, Chu2 ai3 lang5 jiat8-sim hok8- sai7 I, u7 chho3-gou7, Chu2 tek-khak pang- chan7 i, hou7 i na2 ku2 na2 chiaN5-seng3, kau3- boe2 hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8 i jip8 I-e5 kok.   Cheng3 hiaN-ti7, tok8-tok8 hoan5-lo2 bo5 jiat8- sim, m7-bian2 hoan5-lo2 jiat8-sim u7 sun2-hai7.  
(Tai5-lam5-hu2 Kau3-hoe7-po3,te7 87 koan3, 1892 ni5 7 geh8)
