
Lun7 Ke3-chhoa7
(Lim5 Ian3-sin3 ki3)
 Lun7 ke3-chhoa7 e5 hong-siok8, chiau3 kou2-cha2 e5 le7, lam5 saN-chap8 hoe3 chiah chhoa7, lu2 ji7-chap8 hoe3 chiah ke3.  Che si7 Chiu-kong sou2 tiaN7-tioh8 e5 le7.  Koh Bun5-tiong-chu2 e5 chheh u7 kong2,  "Hun-in lun7 chai5 i5-lou2 chi-to7, kun-chu2 put jip8 ki5-tiong," ke2-soeh chiu7-si7 kong2, ke3-chhoa7 na7 ke3-kau3 pheng3-kim si7 hoan-pang e5 hoat-tou7, kun-chu2 e5 lang5 m7 jip8 in e5 sou2-chai7 i3-su3 si7 khoaN3-khin ke3 chhoa7 beh siu pheng3-kim loa7-che7, na2-chun2 cho3 be2-be7 chit8-iuN7.  Kho2-sioh kim put-tong5 kou2, hong-siok8 na2 pi3 na2 phaiN2. Chit8 bang7 si7 cha2 ke3-chhoa7 e5 m7-ho2.  Bat khoaN3-kiN3 cha-pou-lang5 ti7 18, 19 hoe3 e5 si5 chiu7 chhoa7, cha-bou2-lang5 ti7 15, 16 hoe3 e5 si5 chiu7 ke3, ti3-kau3 bo5 kui2-hoe3 chiu7 si2; siat-su2 na7 bo5 te2-hoe3-siu7, chiu7 sou2 seN e5 kiaN2-ji5 ia7 m7-chiaN5 mih8, chhin-chhiuN7 koe2-chi2-chang5 na2 iu3-chiN2, chiu7 sou2 kiat e5 koe2-chi2 chin se3-liap8, lang5 cha2 chhoa7 ia7 si7 an2-ne. 
Koh chit8 hang7, ke3-chhoa7 e5 m7-ho2 chiu7-si7 tai7-seng phin2 pheng3-kim loa7-che7, jian5-au7 chiah tiaN7-tioh8.  Bat khoaN3-kiN3 bou2 sou2-chai7 e5 hong-siok8, chhoa7 chit8-e5 bou2 u7-e7 tioh8 sang3 chio2 600 khou, u7-e5 tioh8 sang3 500,siang7 ia7 tioh8 sang3 300 khou.  Bat u7 bou2-lang5 ka7 lang5 chioh chiN5 lai5 chhoa7; chhoa7 liau2 bo5 thang heng5, iau2-boe7 heng5 chheng-chho2 na2-chun2 tioh8 hou7 che3-chu2 seN li7-sek, kau3 seN-kiaN2 poe7 tang7-taN3 chit8-iuN7.  Chit ho7 e5 hong-siok8 sit8-chai7 m7-ho2.  Kiam2-chhai2 cho3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5 teh siuN7, goa2 seN chit e5 cha-bou2 kiaN2 chu7 se3-han3 ang5-ang5 iu3-iu3 chhi7 kau3 toa7, hou7 i chiah8 chheng7 eng7 sng3 be7 liau2, kiam2 m7-thang siu i kui2-pah gin5 lai5 chiah8 mah ?  Na7-si7 an2-ne phah-sng3 chin bo5 nga2-khi3, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 cho3 seng-li2 lang5 sng3-pun2 be7-hoe3; tioh8 siuN7 lan2 chhi7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 iau3-kin2 tioh8 keng2 ho2 kiaN2-sai3, put-chai7 beh siu gin5 loa7-che7, ho5-hong3 lan2 na7 u7 cha-pou-kiaN2 ia7 tioh8 chhoa7.  Ke3-chhut--khi3 ki3-jian5 bo5 siu gin5, chhoa7 jip8-lai5 ia7 si7 an2-ne.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, "Khou2 teng5 khou2"  Goa2 bat thiaN-kiN3 u7 chit8 teng2-ho7 lang5 chin thang cho3 bou5-hoan7.  I tui3 kiaN2-sai3 hit-peng5 kong2,  "Sui5 goa2 khi3, sui5 li2 lai5," i3-su3 si7 kong2, "Li2 bo5 hiam5 goan2 bo5 ke3-chng, goan2 ia7 bo5 hiam5 lin2 bo5 pheng3-kim," an2-ne nng7-peng5 kiam2 bo5 chin chu7-iu5 koh nga2-khi3 mah ? 
Goa2 siaN2-su7 kong2 an2-ne, si7 in-ui7 bat u7 sin3-tou5 siuN cha2 chhoa7, ti3-kau3 sou2 seN e5 kiaN2 m7 chiaN5 gin2-na2, koh pun2-sin ia7 u7 ham7-hai7.  An2-ne beh thai3 e7 hou7 lan2 e5 kiaN2-sun iong2-chong3 mash ? Siok8-gu2 kong2, "M7-bat m7-kiaN", chng5-bat chng5-kiaN." Hian7-si5 tak8-hang7 teh kai2-liong5, chit e5 chhoa7 e5 tai7-chi3 u7 koan-he7 ti7 au7 se3-tai7 e5 kiaN2-sun, koh khah tioh8 kai2-liong5; siat-su2 lam5 na7 e7 thun-lun2 kau3 24 hoe3 chiuN7, lu2 siang7 kin2 kau3 20 hoe3, chiau an2-ne lai5 ke3-chhoa7 un2-tang3 khah hap8 oe7-seng. 
Koh chit8 hang7, siok8 Chu2 e5 lang5 the ke3-chhoa7, liong2-peng5 na7 kah-i3 tioh8 theng3 liong2-peng5 e5 li7-pian7.  Bat khoaN3 Tang-kang2 e5 hong-siok8 na7 ke3-kiaN2 e5 si5 tioh8 pi7-pan7 pang5-lai5 e5 ke-si, chhin-chhiuN7 bin7-thang2-ke3, bin7-thang3, se-thau5-toh, hong5-lou5-kui7, saN-a2-tu5, i2-thau5-a2, teng2-e7-toh.  Sin-niu5 chit peng5 ki3-jian5 sang3 chiah e5 mih8, kiaN2-sai3 hit peng5 tek-khak tioh8 sang3 kui2-na7 pah e5 pheng3-kim.  Siok8-gu2 kong2, "Chit8-poah hi3 chit8-poah chhou3."  Sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 ia7 jiam2-tioh8 chit-ho7 e5 hong-siok8.  Cheng5-jit8 u7 lang5 sang3 chiah e5 chng-liam5 kiaN5 kui2-na7 phou3-lou7.  Che kiam2 m7-si7 chhak8 ke5 ka-ti7 gia5 mah ? Choe3 te7-it thang kho2-sioh e5, chit8 e5 bou2 hiaN-ti7 cho3 bou2 chi2-moe7 i2-keng ni5-goa7 ku2, hoe3-thau5 ia7 si7 che7, chong2-si7 bo5 pheng3-kim thang khi3 chhoa7.  Che kiam2 m7-si7 thang hoan5-lo2 mah ? HaiN3--ah ! Kong-keng2 na2 kiat8, chiN5 na2 oh-than3, tioh8 khiam7-seN2 chiat-che3 cho3 iau3-kin2, lau5--koa2 hian3 hou7 kau3-hoe7 lai5 thiaN3 Siong7-te3.  Goa2 kong2 chit nng7 hang7, ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 hiaN-che2 chhim-chhim khi3 siuN7, m7-thang chip-siu2 ku7-koan3.  Lan2 u7-si5 chhut-chhui3 chiu7 kong2 in si7 se3-siok8 lang5.  Kong2 chit ku3 oe7 tioh8 se3-ji7, kiaN-liau2 e7 kek-tioh8 in siu7-khi3, tioh8 siuN7 khoaN3 lan2 tak8 hang7 e5 sou2-cho3, ko2-jian5 u7 thoat-siok8 a2-bo5.
