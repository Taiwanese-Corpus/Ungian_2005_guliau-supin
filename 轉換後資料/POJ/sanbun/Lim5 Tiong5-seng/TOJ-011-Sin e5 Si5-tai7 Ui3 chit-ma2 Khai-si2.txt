
Sin e5 Si5-tai7 Ui3 chit-ma2 Khai-si2

Lim5 Tiong5-seng
2001 ni5 12 goeh8 chhoe 1, lip8-ui2 soan2-ki2 kiat-sok, Tai5-oan5 jin5-bin5 koh 1 pai2 iong7 soan2-phio3 ku7-choat8 Tiong-kok e5 khiong2-hat. M7-koan2 an2-choaN2, Tiong-kok sou2-u7 e5 thong2-chian3 ki-kha2, i2-keng long2 hou7 u7 chhim-khek lek8-su2 i3-sek e5 (chit-chiong2 i3-sek m7-si7 cheng5-su5 siau-thau2, ma7 m7-si7 ti3-sek hun7-chu2-sek..e5.) Tai5-oan5 jin5-bin5 khoaN3-phoa3, ji5-chhiaN2 koh ka7 kek to3 tng2--khi3.
E7-sai2 khak-teng7..e5 si7 Tai5-oan5 i2-keng bo5 chian3-cheng e5 gui5-ki, soa3--loh8--lai5, sin e5 lek8-su2 jim7-bu7 tioh8 lak ti7 lan2 e5 keng-kah-thau5--lah. Lan2 tioh8-ai3 ui3 loe7-cheng3, kau3-iok8, bun5-hoa3 kok hong-bin7 loh8-chhiu2, chin3-heng5 chhim-khek e5 pun2-thou2-hoa3 kang-teng5.
Kam2-sia7 thiN-kong-peh..a, lan2 chong2-sng3 u7 ki-hoe7 ui3 400 ni5 hou7 lang5 sit8-bin5 e5 lun5-hoe5 un7-mia7 lai7-te2 thau2-pang3--chhut--lai5. Kam2-sia7 Tai5-oan5 jin5-bin5; sui-jian5 in bo5 chhiuN7 ki5-tha kok-ka tok8-lip8 chian3-cheng lai7-te2, jin5-bin5 iong2-kam2 e5 piau2-hian7; m7-ku2 tak8-pai2 soan2-ki2, in chong2--si7 piau2-hian7 chhut chin koan5 e5 cheng3-ti7 ti3-hui7.
Sin e5 si5-tai7, ui3 chit-ma2 khai-si2!
(Sia2 ti7 2001/12/01 Tai5-oan5 te7 5 kai3 Lip8-hoat Ui2-oan5 Soan2-ki2 i2-au7)
