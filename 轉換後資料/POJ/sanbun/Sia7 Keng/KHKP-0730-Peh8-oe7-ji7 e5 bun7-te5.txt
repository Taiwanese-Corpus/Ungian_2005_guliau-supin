
Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 bun7-te5
Lam5-tau5: Chia7 Keng
Chheng-lian5 thoan5-khe3 e5 tai7-piau2 tui3 Tai5-lam5 e5 Ha7-leng7-hoe7 to3-lai5, u7 teh po3-ko3 kong2, koan-he7 peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 bun7-te5, u7 chit8-pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 teh chu2-tiuN kong2, “Peh8-oe7-ji7 si7 bo5 pit-iau3.” Goa2 thiaN-liau2 put-chi2 kan-khou2.  Kong2 “Peh8-oe7-ji7 bo5 pit-iau3”, chiu7-si7 kong2 “Tai5-oan5 oe7 bo5 pit-iau3.” In-ui7 peh8-oe7-ji7 kap Tai5-oan5 si7 boe7 thang pun-li7-tit--e5.  (ChhiaN2 khoaN3 Tai5-lam5 Chheh-pang5 sou2 hoat-heng5 Pa Phok-su7 e5 toan7-ki3 “?????博士?面影”).
I2-cheng5 goa2 ia7 bat thiaN-kiN3 u7 Tai5-oan5 e5 chheng-lian5 teh kong2, “Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Seng3-keng bo5 hah8-eng7; in-ui7 thak8-liau2 khah be7 thang beng5-pek8 hit e5 i3-su3.”  Goa2 thiaN ia7 put-chi2 gong5-ngiah8, in-ui7 i m7-chai, boe7 thang beng5-pek8 i3-su3 e5 goan5-in, si7 in-ui7 m7-bat Tai5-oan5 oe7 e5 ian5-kou3.  I ia7 m7-chai, peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Seng3-keng si7 Tai5-oan5 oe7 choe3 ho2 e5 bun5-hak8.
Sit8-chai7 kin-a2-jit8 e5 Tai5-oan5, phou2-pian3 e5 hian7-siong7; chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 boe7-hiau2 kong2 Tai5-oan5 oe7, ia7 boe7-hiau2 thiaN Tai5-oan5 oe7, che si7 chit8 e5 pi-chham2 e5 hian7-siong7, che chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5 50 ni5 ku2 siu7 Jit8-pun2 thong2-ti7 e5 kiat-ko2.  Jit8-pun2 lang5 kim3-chi2 lan2 kong2 Tai5-oan5 oe7, kim3 lan2 su2-iong7 Tai5-oan5 mia5-siN3.  In ia7 kim3-chi2 lan2 e5 Chu2-jit8-oh8 ka3 peh8-oe7-ji7.  In e5 i3-su3 chiu7-si7 kong2, lan2 tioh8 oan5-choan5 kai2-pian3 chiaN5-cho3 Jit8-pun2 lang5, chiah e7 thang tit-tioh8 seng-chun5.  In siuN7 ti7 Jit8-pun2 te3-kok e5 lai7-bin7, Tai5-oan5 lang5 si7 bo5 pit-iau3.  Chong2-si7 hian7-si5 i2-keng bo5 lang5 teh kim3-chi2 lan2 kong2 Tai5-oan5 oe7, ia7 bo5 lang5 teh kim3 lan2 su2-iong7 peh8-oe7-ji7 lah, lan2 kiam2 m7-ai3 tu5-khi3 chit ho7 pi-chham2 e5 hian7-siong7 mah?
Tak8 pai2 goa2 hian-khui peh8-oe7-ji7 Seng3-keng e5 si5, chiu7 oe7 tit-tioh8 chit8 khoan2 chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 lau7-bu2 e5 heng-cheng5, hit ho7 un-loan2 e5 kam2-kak.  Chit ho7 kam2-kak si7 tui3 pat8 ho7 bun5-ji7 e5 chheh sou2 boe7 thang tit-tioh8--e5.  Goa2 siuN7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 eng-kai tioh8 eng7 Tai5-oan5 oe7 ki5-to2, ia7 eng-kai tioh8 thak8 peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 Seng3-keng.					(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 730 koan3; 1949 ni5 10 goeh8)
