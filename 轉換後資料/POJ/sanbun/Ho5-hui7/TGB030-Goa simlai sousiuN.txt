Goa2 sim-lai7 sou2 siuN7--e5
Ho5-hui7

1 kang teh chiah8 tiong-tau3 e5 si5, thiaN-tioh8 sin-bun5 teh po3-sang3 Tiong-chun5 chhai5-oan5 e5 siau-sit, tng-teh siuN7 kong2, chit-e5 sin-ni5 le2-mih chiah chan2, a-bu2 chhah 1-ku3 oe7 jip8--lai5: "Chhai5-oan5 long2 ui3 e7-bin7 chhai5, ka-sin long2 ui3 teng2-thau5 ka, sou2-i2 a, kio3 li2 tioh8-ai3 ho2-ho2-a2 thak8-chheh!"

Che pun2-toe2 tio7-si7 chiaN5 bo5 peng5-teng2 e5 tai7-chi3, si7 an2-choaN2 goa2 tio7 it-teng7 ai3 chiau3 chit-e5 ki5-koai3 e5 cheng5-se3 kiaN5? Si7 an2-choaN2 m7-si7 khi3 ka7 kai2-pian3--leh?

Tio7-sng3 kong2, goa2 thak8 chiok choe7 chheh, chiaN5-cho3 teng2-chan5 e5 lang5, Kau3-si5 goa2 kam2 u7 kho2-leng5 e7 ki3-tit chit-ma2 sim-lai7 sou2 siuN7 e5 bo5 peng5-teng2?

Tio7-sng3 kong2, goa2 pian3-cho3 1-e5 cho3-kang-a2-lang5, goa2 kam2 u7 hoat-tou7 thang kai2-sian7 chit-e5 cheng5-heng5? Cho3 koh khah choe7, pat8-lang5 ma7 iu5-goan5 jin7-ui7, goa2 in-ui7 ka-ki7 si7 kang-lang5 sin-hun7, chiah teh ui7 ka-ki7 cheng-chhu2 hok-li7, cho7-chiaN5 cheng-sin5 siau-sit, bo5 peng5-teng2 e5 kiok8-se3 it-tit8 chun5-chai7......

SiuN7 kau3 chia, goa2 chi2-ho2 ka7 bu5-nai7 kap chhui3--nih e5 peh8-png7 cho3-hoe2 thun--loh8-khi3.
