
Sin3-giong2 Ui2-jin5 Te3 Hoa-sun3 Bok8-su
James Hudson Taylor (A. D. 1832 — 1905).
 Khou2 Chui2-lou7
 [Phian-chia2 chu3:  Te3 Hoa-sun3 Bok8-su chiu7-si7 Loe7-te7-hoe7 e5 chhong3-siat-chia2.  Khah-siong5 si7 chheng-hou cho3 Te3 Tek-seng Bok8-su.  Khou2 Bok8-su leng7-goa7 eng7 Te3 Hoa-sun3 e5 mia5, tan3--si7 lang5 si7 siang5 chit8-e5, ti7-chia koh ke siau7-kau3 i e5 sio2-toan7, un2-tang3 e7-thang ke-thiN tui3 Te3 Bok8-su kap Loe7-te7-hoe7 e5 jim7-bat, siang5-si5 e7-thang tit-tioh8 leng5-seng3-chiuN7 e5 kou2-le7]
Seng3 Po2-lo5 kong2, “Siong7-te3 eng7 goan2 khng3 lin2; goan2 ui7-tioh8 Ki-tok kiu5 lin2 kap Siong7-te3 koh ho5” (II Ko 5:21).  Koh kong2, “Goan2 it-khai3 bo5 that-gai7 lang5, bian2-tit chit e5 chit-hun7 hoan7-hiam5; chiu7-si7 hoan7-su7 cheng3-beng5 pun2-sin hap8 ti7 choe3 Siong7-te3 e5 chhe-eng7, si7 ti7 choe7-choe7 thun-lun2, hoan7-lan7, khiam3-khoeh, khun2-pek, siu7-phah......jiau2-loan7, tioh8-boa5, bo5 khun3, bo5 chiah8 ......” (II Ko 6:3—10).  Siu7 chheng-hou cho3 sin3-giong2 ui2-jin5, put-khut e5 su3-tou5 Te3 Hoa-sun3 Bok8-su tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 sou2 ki3, seng3 Po2-lo5 Sian-siN tui3 Ko-lim5-to kau3-hoe7 sou2 kong2 e5.  Te3 Bok8-su e5 it-seng chin siu7-khou2, chong2-si7 tui3 i jiat8-liat8 e5 sin3-giong2, put-khut e5 cheng-sin5 lai5 khek-hok8 it-chhe3 e5 khun3-lan5, kau3-be2 chiaN5-choe3 Tiong-kok loe7-te7 thoan5-to7-hiap8-hoe7 e5 chhong3-siat-chia2, I e5 it-seng thang hun cho3 e7-bin7 kui2-hang7:—
1.Tui3 chhut-si3 kau3 teng5-thau5-siN (重生) (1832—1849).
1)I e5 chhut-si3:  A. D. 1832 ni5 5 goeh8 21 jit8, chhut-si3 ti7 Eng-kok e5 Iok-ku3-sia3 e5 Ban7-su-le2.  I e5 lau7-pe7 khi2-chhou khui ioh8-tiam3, au7-lai5 u7 cho3 chit8 e5 iu2-lek8 e5 thoan5-kau3-su.  I e5 lau7-bu2 si7 bok8-su e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.
2) I e5 siau3-lian5 si5-tai7:  Khi2-chhou i u7 hoai5-gi5 e5 su-siong2.  I e5 lau7-bo2 ui7-tioh8 i sim chin kan-khou2, siong5-siong5 ui7 i pek-chhiat ki5-to2.  Kau3 15 hoe3, u7 khi3 toa3 gin5-hang5 cho3 gin5-hang5-oan5.
3) I e5 teng5-thau5-siN:  U7 chit8 jit8 i e5 lau7-bo2 ui7-tioh8 i jip8 chit8 keng pang5, eng7 so2-si5 so2 tiau5-teh, pek-chhiat ui7-tioh8 i e5 kiaN2 Te3 Hoa-sun3 kim3-chiah8 ki5-to2.  Tu2-tu2 hit-si5, i siuN7 ai3 beh thak8-chheh; chiu7 khi3 i e5 lau7-pe7 e5 chheh-tu5 theh8 chit8 pun2 “Tit oan5-seng5 Ki-tok e5 su7-giap8” e5 se3-pun2 chheh lai5 thak8.  Teh thak8 e5 tiong-kan, sim-lai7 siN-khi2 chu7-kak choe7 e5 liam7-thau5, kam2-kak tit-tioh8 tit-kiu3 e5 pit-iau3-seng3; chiu7 chek-si5 chhim-sin3 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 teng2 oan5-seng5 e5 siok8-choe7.  I chiu7 kui7 loh8 chham3-hoe2 jin7-choe7, sui5-si5 tit-tioh8 teng5-thau5-siN.  Hit-si5 tu2-tu2 i e5 lau7-bo2 ui7-tioh8 i pek-chhiat ki5-to2 seng3-li7 e5 si5.  Hit-si5 i 17 hoe3.  Ki5-to2 e5 hau7-lek8 sit8-chai7 chin toa7 ah!!
2.Tui3 lip8-chi3 kau3 lai5 ti7 Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 (A. D. 1850—1865).
1)I lip8-chi3 chun2-pi7:  I tui3 teng5-thau5-siN liau2, chiu7 kak-tit tioh8 khi3 Tiong-kok thoan5-kau3.  Tui3 hit-si5 khi2, i chiu7 tit8-tit8 chun2-pi7.  I u7 chit8 jit8, ka7 bok8-su chioh chit8-pun2 koan-he7 Tiong-kok e5 bun5-hian3 e5 chheh lai5 thak8.  Bok8-su ka7 i kong2 bo5 pan7-hoat thang khi3 Tiong-kok thoan5-to7, chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 sit-chi3, chi2-u7 jit8-ia7 pek-chhiat ki5-to2, ban7-su7 kau-thok Siong7-te3.
2)I ui7-tioh8 chun2-pi7, chiu7 khi3 thak8 Halu e5 I-hak8-hau7.  Pit-giap8 liau2, khi3 peN7-iN7 hong7-chit, chit8-bin7 hong7-chit, chit7-bin7 hak8-sip8 beh khi3 Tiong-kok thoan5-kau3, oh8 khun3-khou2 e5 seng-oah8.  Tak8-jit8 khiam7-seng2, tak8 cha2-khi2 oh8 chiah8-moe5, tak8 am3 oh8 chiah8-png7, tiong-tau3 tng3 chiah8 chit8-liap8 phong7-ko2, chiong i sou2 siu-jip8 e5 chiN5 khiam7 saN-hun7-ji7 beh cho3 thoan5-kau3 e5 lou7-eng7. I bo5 ai3 chiah8 gu5-ni (milk) kap ng5-iu5 (Butter) chit khoan2 chhia-chhi2-phin2.
3)I pang-chan7 song3-hiong lang5:  I m7-si7 chi2-u7 eng7 chhui3 kong2, i u7 khak-sit8 le7-heng5 Ki-tok e5 ka3-si7.  I u7 chit8-jit8 khi3 tham3-hong2 chit8 keng pin5-bin5 e5 ka-teng5, khoaN3-tioh8 chit8 e5 lau7-bo2 chin sui2, chhiu2-nih pho7 chit8 e5 ang5-eN-a2, bo5 ni thang hou7 i chiah8, kek8 song3-hiong, kau3 beh go7-si2 e5 khoan2, i chiu7 beh tui3 te7-a2-lai7 theh8 chhut i sou2 u7 e5 chiN5, lai5 pang-chan7 chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5.
 
4)Tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 pang-chan7:  Keh jit8 i bo5 mih8 thang chiah8, hut-jian5 theh8 iu5-pian7 e5 lang5, theh8 chit8 tiuN phoe hou7 i, lai7-bin7 he7 chit8 tiuN gin5-toaN sia2 poaN3 Eng-pong7 e5 chiN5 beh sang3 i.  Hit tiuN phoe kong2 cha7-jit8 12 tiam2 cheng ku2 e5 chhut-chu, than3 400 poe7, chit8-sut-a2 cho3 kam2-sia7 e5 i3-su3. Ai3 pang-chan7 lang5 e5, e7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 e5 pang-chan7.
5)Khi3 su2 hoe5 seng (去死回生):  I ti7 peN7-iN7 teh khin5-bu7 e5 si5, u7 chit8 jit8 teh si2-the2 kai2-pho3 (死体解剖) e5 si5, bo5 se3-ji7, jiam2-tioh8 tok8, hiam2-hiam2 si2, i pek-chhiat kim3-chiah8 ki5-to2, au7-lai5 koh tit-tioh8 oah8.  I chhim3-sin3 chit e5 chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 ai3 i khi3 Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 e5 sin3-jim7-chng7 (信任狀).
6)I chhut-hoat lai5 Tiong-kok thoan5-kau3:  I ti7 A. D. 1853 ni5, i tui3 Li7-pa-lu (Riverpool) che7 chun5 keng-ke3 6 ko3-goeh8 ku2 chiah kau3 Siong7-hai2.  Tong-si5 ti7 Siong7-hai2 u7 pai5-goa7 un7-tong7, teh pai5-thek goa7-kok lang5.  I jim2-siu7 pah-poaN e5 kan-khou2.
(1)I kap Tiong-kok lang5 cho3-hoe2 toa3. (2) I chiah8 Tiong-kok chiah8.  Chheng7 tng5-saN thah8 be2-koa3, ti3 oaN2-bo7. (4) I lau5 thau5-mng5 piN7 mng5-boe2.  Tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 Tiong-kok lang5.  Chhin-chhiuN7 Po2-lo5 kong2, Goa2 ng3 tak8-iuN7 lang5, chiaN5-cho3 tak8-iuN7 khoan2, it-khai3 si7 beh kiu3 lang5.
7)Au7-lai5 i khi3 Tiong-kok e5 loe7-te7 thoan5-kau3.
I tu2-tioh8: (1) Chhat8-be2 e5 gui5-hiam2.  (2) Tong5-chok8 e5 gui5-hiam2 (goa7-kok leng2-su7 kio3 i khi3 hoat8-kim, kong2 i khi3 khu-hek8 goa7 thoan5-to7 be7 eng7--tit.  (3) Chhi3-tiong e5 gui5-hiam2.  Hou7 lang5 pang3 hoe2 sio-chhu3, mih8-kiaN7 sio liau2-liau2.
8)I e5 kiat-hun kap kui kok:  I kap tong5-lo5-chia2 Tai7-a Kou-niu5 kiat-hun (Miss Laiyer).  Au7-lai5 ui7-tioh8 sin-the2 khiam3-an, koh to3-lai5 Eng-kok.
3.Tui3 chhong3-siat Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 hiap8-hoe7 kau3 piat8-se3 (A.D. 1866-1905).
 
1)I ti7 Eng-kok po3-ko3 thoan5-kau3 e5 ho2 siau-sit, kap tiu5-pi7 chhong3-siat Tiong-kok hiap8-hoe7.
2)Au7-lai5 tit-tioh8 15 mia5 tong5-chi3 kap che7-che7 chiN5, chiu7 ti7 A. D. 1866 ni5 5 goeh8, koh khi3 Tiong-kok e5 loe7-te7 lai5 chhong3-siat Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 hiap8-hoe7, kap kek8-lek8 thoan5-kau3.  Khi2-chhou chhoa7 15 mia5 tong5-chi3 thoan5-kau3-su kau3 Tiong-kok loe7-te7 thoan5-kau3, kau3-boe2 piN3-cho3 kui chheng mia5 toaN-ui7 e5 soan-kau3 thoan5-the2.
3)Tui3 chit8 e5 bo5 kiaN Lo5-ma2 kau3-ong5 e5 Lou7-tek-ma2-teng (Martin Luther) lai5 seng5-chiu7 chong-kau3 kai2-kek.  Tui3 chit8 e5 chou2-chi2 Hut-lan5-se kek-beng7 e5 Oe7-su-le2 (John Wesley) lai5 kau3-hoa3 choan5 Eng-kok.  Tui3 chit8 e5 m7-kiaN khou2-lan7 e5 Te3 Hoa-sun3 (James Hudson Taylor) lai5 thoan5 kau3 Tiong-kok choan5-te7.
4)I tu2-tioh8 chin toa7 e5 gek8-keng2:  I ti7 Tiong-kok loe7-te7 thoan5-kau3 e5 si5:  (1) Tu2-tioh8 3 e5 kiaN2 long2 si2 ti7 Tiong-kok.  (2) Sou2 thiaN3 e5 Bok8-su-niu5 ia7 jiam2-tioh8 jiat8-peN7 si2.  (3) Che7-che7 mih8 hou7 lang5 chhiuN2--khi3.  (4) Siu7 lang5 khun2-tiok8.  (5) Chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 sit-chi3, chi2-u7 sin3-giong2, eng7 ki5-to2, eng7 jim2-nai7 lai5 khek-hok8 it-chhe3 e5 khun3-lan5, lai5 chhong3-siat Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 hiap8-hoe7.
5)I chin7-tiong kau3 si2 (A. D. 1905).
I khek-hok8 it-chhe3 e5 hoan7-lan7, chiaN5-cho3 chit8 e5 toa7 thoan5-kau3-su7, toa7 chi2-to7-chia2.  Ti7 1887 ni5, bat ti7 chit8 ni5 e5 tiong-kan iau3-kiu5 100 e5 sin e5 soan-kau3-su lai5 pang-bang5 thoan5-kau3.  Ti7 soan-kau3-su siong7 chin han2-iu2 e5 toa7 su7-giap8.  Kau3 ti7 1905 ni5 6 goeh8 3 jit8, choe3-au7 kong2, “Choe3 ui2-tai7--e5, chi2-u7 Siong7-te3, lan2 tioh8 12 hun e5 sim lai5 sin3-kho3 Siong7-te3,” kong2-soah, i e5 leng5-hun5 eng2-kui Siong7-te3, i e5 ui5-the2 bai5-chong3 ti7 Tiong-kok e5 loe7-te7, hiong2-siu7 73 hoe3.  Kau3 ti7 A. D. 1926 ni5 e5 thong2-ke3, Te3 Bok8-su sou2 chhong3-siat e5 Tiong-kok thoan5-to7 hiap8-hoe7: (1)	Soan-kau3-su 1234 mia5.  (2)	Tiong-kok chek8 e5 thoan5-kau3-su 3944 mia5.  (3)Sin3-tou5 sou3 67500 mia5.  Kap Tai5-oan5 TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 sin3-tou5 sou3 chha-put-to che7.  Thang chai i e5 sin3-giong2 chin ho2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 817 ho7, 1957 ni5 1 geh8)
