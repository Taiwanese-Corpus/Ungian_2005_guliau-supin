
Giok8-li2 Hoe2-chai

(IuN5 Tang-hai2 ki3.)
Giok8-li2 si7 Tang-pou7 iau3-kin2 e5 te7.  Kin7-lai5 in-ui7 thou2-te7 e5 khai-khun2, kau-thong e5 li7-pian7, sou2-i2 u7 chiam7-chiam7 teh chin2-heng, lang5-giah8 ia7 na2 ke-thiN.  Lan2 e5 kang2-gi7-sou2 chhong3-siat ti7-chia kau3 chit-tiap8, thau5 boe2 oa2 3 ni5; jin5-sou3 ia7 u7 40 goa7 lang5.  Ku7-ni5 e5 ni5-boe2 u7 gi7-lun7 beh kian3-tiok sin e5 pai3-tng5.  Pun2 ke e5 sin3-chia2 i2-goa7, u7 tui3 cheng5-soaN e5 kau3-hoe7 kap Tang-pou7 e5 kau3-hoe7, i2-kip8 cheng3 chu5-sian7-ka te5 oa2 900 goa7 khou; ia7 u7 be2 chit8 keng chhu3 cho3 siok-sia3, khai 400 khou; soa3 tui3 koaN-thiaN chheng2 khi2 pai3-teng5 e5 te7.  Tu2-tioh8 Giok8-li2 chhi7-khu kai2-cheng3 e5 goan5-in, sou2-i2 sou2 chheng2-e5, ian5-oan7 kau3 chit-tiap8 iau2-boe7 tit-tioh8 hi2-kho2.
Ti7 Tai7-cheng3 9 ni5 12 goeh8 30 jit8 e7-pou chit8 tiam2 cheng hut-jian5 ti7 Giok8-li2 e5 lam5-peng5 hoan-jin5 e5 chhu3 hoat hoe2.  Hit-si5 tu2-tu2 khi2 toa7 lam5-hong, hoe2 tit8-tit8 chiuN7 pak, soa3 kau3 Giok8-li2 ke.  Long2-chong2 sio 4 tiam2 cheng ku2, ke3-kiong7 sio 178 hou7.  Sui-jian5 u7 siau hong5-chou, liok8-kun, chong3-teng, i2-kip8 keng2-chhat-tui7 long lai5 phah hoe2, m7-ku2 hong kap hoe2 chin liat8, ti3-kau3 bo5 hoat thang kai2-kiu3.  Kau3 5 tiam2 cheng hit-tiap8 hong khah se3, tai7-ke chiu7 thiah hoe2-lou7 lai5 chiat8-tngi7 e5 chin3-heng5 chiah phah hoa.
Hoe2 tng7 toh8 e5 si5, ke-lou7 e5 peh-siN3, ine5 mih8 poaN bo5 lou7 thang to5-siam2.  Che7-che7 lang5 e5 mih8-kiaN7 i2-keng chhiuN2-chhut hoe2-au5, iau2-ku2 koh hou7 iam7-hoe2 sio-khi3. Kai-chai3 chhi7-ke e5 liong2-peng5 u7 khoah e5 chhan5-iuN5 sou2 u7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 cho3 kui2-na7 hoe5 un7-poaN chiah tit-tioh8 kai2-kiu3.  Hit-si5 Giok8-li2 e5 chi-thiaN-tiuN2, ui7-tioh8 chit chan5 chap8-hun iu-bun7, pun2-sin the3 peh-sin3 gia5 chin tang7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.
Hoe2 koe3 hit mi5, khi2 pak-hong loh8-hou7,u7 lang5 bo5 chhu3 thang toa3; u7 lang5 tioht8? chhan5-iuN5 kou3 in e5 mih8-kiaN7, sou2 chhun khah bo5 chit8-poaN3 e5 chhu3, hak8-hau7 bio7-u2 long2 si7 lang5.  Chi-thiaN phai3 keng2-koaN koaN7 png7 hou7 lang5 chiah8, chham2-keng2 sit8-chai7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-tioh8 phiN7 e7 chhut sng-chui2.  Lan2 e5 kang2-gi7-sou2 kap siok-sia2 ia7 long2 hou7 hoe2 sio-khi3; sin3-chia2 sio 4 hou7.  Sit8-chai7 si7 put-chhek e5 chai-ho7.
Hit mi5 Hoe-lian5-kang2 thiaN-tiuN2 chiap-tioh8 tian7, sui5-si5 che7 chhia lai5.  ThiN-kng liam5-piN hoe7-gi7 kiu3-che3 kap chhi7-ke hok8-hoe5 e5 sian7-hou7.  Chiu7 koat-gi7 beh khi2 e5 lang5, koaN thiaN tek8-piat8 pou2-chou7 chha5-liau7 15 ki, tek 50 ki, hm5-chhau2 chit8 chheng kin; i2-goa7 iau2 u7 tin5 beh hu2-kho2 chhut-chai7 lang5 khi3 thiu.
