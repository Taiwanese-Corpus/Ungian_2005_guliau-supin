TiuN Ki-choan5    
Lang5 oah8 ti7 chit se3-kan tak8 lang5 u7 i e5 chhu3-bi7, u7 lang5 ai3 phah-lah8, u7 lang5 ai3 liah8 hi5, u7 lang5 ai3 khoaN3 ho2-khoaN3 e5 mih8, u7 lang5 ai3 thiaN ho2-thiaN e5 siaN-im, u7 lang5 ai3 phiN7 phang e5 bi7, u7 lang5 chhui3 ai3 chiah8 ho2-chiah8 e5 mih8. Lun7 chiah8-mih8 cheng2- lui7 chin-choe7. Tit8-chiap oe7 choe3 lan2 siau2- the2 e5 chu-iong2--e5 tai7-khai3 thang hun- choe3 nng7 hang7:   
1.U7 ki-seng3 but8-chit, chiu7-si7 u7 pau-ham5 Than3-sou3 e5 mih8, chhin-chhiuN7:     
(a) Nng7-peh8-chit, i e5 seng5-hun si7 Than3-sou3, Chui2-sou3, Sng-sou3, Chek-sou3, i e5 lou7-eng7 si7 oe7 hou7 lang5 e5 seng-khu tit-tioh8 se3-lek8, ia7 si7 hou7 se3-pau ke-thiN sin--e5.     
(b) Ham5-sui2 Than3-sou3.     
(c) Chi-hong: in e5 seng5-hun piN5-piN5 si7 Than3-sou3, Chui2-sou3, Sng-sou3, saN hang7 hap8-chiaN5 chiah-e5; (b) kap (c) boe7-oe7 hou7 se3-pau sin siN, chong2-si7 oe7 hou7 lang5 ke- thiN se3-lek8.   
2.Bu5 ki-seng3 e5 but8-chit, chiu7-si7 bo5 pau-ham5 Than3-sou3 e5 mih8, chhin-chhiuN7:     
(a) Iam5: i e5 seng5-hun si7 Na2-tok- liu5-bou5 (natrium) kap Iam5-sou3.  Chit hang7 mih8 khiok boe7-oe7 hou7 lang5 tit-tioh8 sin se3-lek8, a2-si7 se3-pau sin siN, m7-ku2 The2- seng5-hun e5 kiat-chiaN5, a2-si7 the2 kok ki- koan e5 Ki-leng5-siong7 u7 toa7 e5 koan-he7, chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 na7 chiah8 choan5-jian5 bo5 Iam5-hun e5 mih8, si7 pi2 long2 bo5 chiah8 mih8 iau2  
khah-khoai3 si2; an2-ni thang chai seng-khu na7 bo5 Iam5 si7 boe7 eng7-tit.     
(b) Chui2: i e5 seng5-hun si7 Chui2-sou3 kap Sng-sou3. Chit hang7 mih8 ti7 lan2 e5 seng- khu-lai7 oe7 pang-chan7 lan2 e5 siau-hoa3, Huih-ek8 e5 lai7-bin7 ia7 u7 it-teng7 e5 chui2- hun; na7 siuN-chio2, chiu7 huih piN3 khah kau7, ti3-kau3 hou7 lan2 chai chhui3-ta.  Lan2 e5 seng-khu lai7-bin7 put-si5 tioh8 u7 iok-liok8 chit8 chin e5 chui2. Ki5-thaN iau2 u7 tit8-chiap boe7-oe7 choe3 lan2 e5 chu-iong2, chhin-chhiuN7 Te5, Ko-phi, Hou5-chio, Hoan-kiuN, KiuN, Hun, Chiu2, chiah e5 mih8 u7 chin choe7 lang5 ai3 i. TaN goa2 chiong ki5-tiong Chiu2 kap Seng-li2-tek chok-iong7, chiu7-si7 kap lan2 lang5 e5 kian7-khong e5 koan-he7 tai7- liok8 kong2 kui2-ku3.  Chiu2-cheng phau3 hou7 i khah-poh8 chiu7-si7 chiu2.  
Chiu2 kek8 kau7-- e5 chiu7-si7 chiu2-cheng. PiN5-piN5 chiu2 ia7 u7 kau7-poh8 e5 cheng-chha.  Sio-chiu2 100 hun e5 tiong-kan 50 hun si7 chiu2-cheng.  Jit8- pun2 Chheng-chiu2 100 hun 15 hun e5 chiu2- cheng. Phu5-to5-chiu2 100 hun 9 hun e5 chiu2-cheng, Beh8-chiu2 (Beer) 100 hun 4 hun e5 chiu2-cheng.  Chiu2 si7 kou2-cha2 lang5 sou2 bat heng-hun3- che chhi3-kek-mih8 e5 chit8  hang7. I e5 seng5- hun chiau3 hoa3-hak8-tek lai5 kong2, si7 Than3- sou3, Sng-sou3, Chui2-sou3, chit 3 hang7 hoa3-hap8 lai5 chiaN5, hun-liong7 choe7-chio2 e5 cheng- chha, oe7 piN3-choe3 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 chiu2-cheng.  Phi3-ju7 chhin-chhiuN7 Than3- sou3 1, Chui2-sou3 4, Sng-sou3 1, hap8-chiaN5 choe3 Methyl alcohol. Than3-sou3 2, Chui2-sou3 6, Sng-sou3 1, hap8-chiaN5 choe3 Ethyl alcohol. Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni u7 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 chiu2-cheng ti7-teh. It-poaN chiu2-cheng chiu7-si7 eng7 thng5-hun kap kaN3 lai5 choe3, chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 hian7-si5 che3- thng5 hoe7-sia7 li7-iong7 thng5-bit8 teh choe3 chiu2-cheng.  Kou2-cha2 lang5 si7 eng7 ke2-chi2- chiap, khah choe7 si7 eng7 phu5-to5-chiap hou7 i hoat-kaN3 lai5 choe3; Tai5-oan5 ku7-koan3 si7 eng7 chiu2-png7 chhiau kaN3 lai5 choe3, chiu2 oe7 siN-chhut chit8 chiong2 e5 phang-bi7, hou7 lim e5 lang5 oe7 siu7 chhi3-kek, toa7-sim- khui3, ia7 khah siong-tiong7 chiu7 boe7 ki3- tit8 tak8-hang7.    
Lim-chiu2 e5 lang5 na2 choe7, chiu2 e5 siau- hui3-liong7 ia7 na2 choe7; che3-chiu2 au7-lai5 soa3 chiam7-chiam7 piN3-choe3 chit8 chiong2 e5 tek8- piat8 seng-li2, kau3 kun7-tai7 soa3 chiaN5-choe3 chit8 e5 toa7 kang-giap8; tak8 kok kang-kho Tai7-hak8 long2 u7 choe3 chiu2-kho teh gian2- kiu3 che3-chiu2 i2-kip8 pat8-hang7. Lun7 chiu2 e5 chiaN5 e5 in-toaN, ti7 40 goa7 ni5-cheng5 Louis Pasteur chiah chai chiu2 chiu7-si7 Hoa3-hak8-tek kap Seng-li2-tek e5 chok- iong7 lai5 chiaN5--e5.    
Chiu2 jip8 Tong7-but8-the2 tau3-ti2 u7 sim2- mih8 chok-iong7, si7 kau3 choe3-kun7 chiah beng5-pek8. Lang5 na7 eng7 chio2 koh poh8 koh ho2, hoa3-hak8-tek sun5-chui7 e5 chiu2 oe7 pang-chan7 lang5 e5 siau-hoa3; chong2-si7 che si7 kek8 oh, in-ui7 lang5 siu7 chiu2 chhi3-kek, na2 ku2 tioh8 na2 kiong5, liong7 ia7 tioh8 na2 choe7, chiah oe7 kam2-kak, sou2-i2 lim-chiu2 khah-siong5 si7 u7 hai7 bo5 ek, oh-tit chun2- chat.  Lim-chiu2 e5 sip8-koan3 tui3 cha2 liu5- thoan5 kau3 taN chin ku2, choe7-choe7 lang5 si7 teh oa2-kho3 che3 chiu2 boe7 chiu2 lai5 teh seng-oah8.  Bi2-kok (Au-chian3 i2-cheng5) choan5-kok iok u7 nng7-pah ban7 teh kan- siap8 che3-chiu2 e5 su7-giap8.  An2-ni thang chai chiu2 e5 kun chhng-jip8 sia7-hoe7 i2-keng chin chhim, sou2-i2 choe7-choe7 chai-iaN2 e5 lang5, sui-jian5 chai chiu2 e5 hai7-tok8 chin toe7, iau2-ku2 ai3 beh tu5-khi3 chin oh--tit.    
Lun7 chiu2 thang lim a2 m7-thang e5 bun7- te5, tui3 chin-cha2 chiu7 u7 teh gi7-lun7; u7 lang5 kong2 chiu2 si7 pah-hang7 ioh8 e5 thau5, u7 lang5 kong2 chiu2 si7 kong5-chui2, oe7 hou7 lang5 sit ti2-kak. Ki5-sit8 u7-iaN2, na7 beh ku2 i e5 sit8- le7 si7 chin-choe7, kong2 boe7 liau2, ia7 gian2- kiu3 kau3 chheng-chho2, chiu2 m7-si7 oe7 choe3 ioh8--tit. U7 chin-choe7 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 chheng- lian5 ui7-tioh8 ai3 lim-chiu2 ti3-kau3 chhi- chham2 lok8-phek, sim7-chi3 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7 ia7 u7; na7-si7 ia7 u7 lau7-lang5 in tui3 siau3-lian5 si5-tai7 chiu7 u7 teh lim-chiu2, in ia7 iong2- kiaN7, chiah8 kau3 lau7-lau7. An2-ni lan2 na7 kan-ta eng7 phou2-thong e5 koan-chhat, sit8-chai7 oh-tit chai chiu2 si7 u7 li7-ek a2-si7 sun2-hai7, sou2-i2 lun7 chiu2 thang lim a2 m7- thang, chiu2 si7 tok8 mah?  Na7-si7 tok8, si7 to2 chit8 chiong2 e5 tok8, ia7 eng7-liong7 tioh8 joa7- choe7 chiah si7 tok8?  Koh chit8 hang7: chiu2 sit8-chai7 oe7 li7-ek lang5 mah?  Tui3 chiah e5 bun7-toe5 lan2 boe7-oe7 sui5-pian7 chhin3-chhai2 tu2-tu2 an3-chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 in3.                                                              
Chiau3 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 lan2 tioh8 siuN7 nng7 khoan2:   
1.M7-thang eng7 ko3-jin5 tek8-piat8 e5 keng-giam7 lai5 cheng3-beng5 chiu2 e5 li7-ek a2-si7  
hai7.  Lan2 m7-thang siuN7 kong2, chhin- chhiuN7 goa7-kok u7 chit8 e5 iu2-beng5 e5 si-jin5 ho7-choe3 Goethe; i chit8-si3-lang5 lim u7 5000 kan e5 chiu2. A2-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 Tiong-kok cha2 u7 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 e5 si-jin5 Li2 Thai3-pek8, i ia7-si7 ai3-chiu2-ka. Lan2 m7- thang siuN7 kong2, in gau5 choe3 si, su-siong2 chhim, si7 in-ui7 lim-chiu2 e5 kiat-ko2.   
2.Tioh8 chiong choe7-choe7 lang5 e5 keng-giam7 lai5 tho2-lun7. Te7-it hou7 lan2 oe7 sin3-tit e5 chiu7-si7 po2-hiam2 hoe7-sia7. Tui3 chiah e5 Lim-chiu2-ka kap Kim3-chiu2-ka e5 ki3-liok8, chiau3 in e5 ki3-liok8 peng5-kun:

Koh chit8-khoan2 hou7 lan2 oe7 sin3-pin5-tit e5 ki3-liok8, chiu7-si7 piN7-iN7 tui3 hoan7-chia2 e5 ki3-liok8.   
(1) Tioh8 Thoan5-jiam2-piN7 e5 si5 Kim3- chiu2-ka pi2 Lim-chiu2-ka khah u7 oah8 e5 ng3-bang7, chiau3 Eng-kok (Cholera) "Ko-le-lah"(Lau3-thou3-sia3), chiu7-si7 Thoan5- jiam2 PiN7-iN7 e5 thong2-ke3. Lim-chiu2-ka 100 lang5 si2 91 lang5, Kim3-chiu2-ka 100 lang5 si2 19 lang5.   
(2) Thai5-koah (chhiu2-sut8) e5 si5 Kim3- chiu2-ka pi2 Lim-chiu2-ka khah khoai3 ho2. Koh chit8 khoan2 chiu7-si7 Thih-to7 su7-kou3 thong2-ke3 Lim-chiu2-ka pi2 Kim3-chiu2-ka khah-chiap8 tu2-tioh8 gui5-hiam2, ia7 khah oe7 hai7 lang5 tu2-tioh8 gui5-hiam2. Chhi3-siuN7 kiaN5-chhia e5 lang5 na7 chiu2-chui3, eng-kai tioh8 theng5-chhia e5 sou2-chai7, m7-theng5- chhia; a2-si7 iah8-tiuN2 na7 chiu2-chui3, sin3-ho7 loan7-pang3, sit8-chai7 chin gui5-hiam2.    
TaN lun7 chiu2-cheng kap siau-hoa3 u7 sim2-mih8 koan-he7, ia7 lim tam7-poh8 chiu2 oe7 chhi3-kek ui7, khah gau5 lau5 ui7-ek8; chong2-si7 chiu2-cheng-hun si7 chou2-tong3 ui7- ek8 e5 siau-hoa3. M7-ku2 lang5 na7 lim tam7-poh8 chiu2, chit8-si5 oe7 khah gau5 chiah8, si7 in-ui7 ui7-ek8 lau5 chin-choe7 e5 ian5-kou3; na7 chiu2- cheng siuN-choe7 jip8 siau-hoa3-khi3, oe7 sio- tioh8 liam5-mouh8 soe3-pau, chiu7-si7 ui7-piah, ia7 hou7 hiah e5 teh chhut siau-hoa3-ek8 e5 ki- koan khah bo5 oah8-tang7. An2-ni oe7 hou7 lang5 ti3-kau3 ui7 lam2, a2-si7 pat8-khoan2 e5 siau- hoa3 put-liong5.    
Chiu2 tui3 ha7-teng2 tong7-but8 a2-si7 phou2-thong tong7-but8 e5 kun-bah e5 chok-iong7, si7 tam7-poh8 oe7 kou2-bu2, hou7 in khah oah8-tang7.  Chong2-si7 na7 siong5-siong5 lim-chiu2 chiu7 piN3-choe3 ban7-seng3 tiong3-tok8, oe7 siN- khi2 chiong2-chiong2 e5 phoa3-piN7. Chhin- chhiuN7 lang5 siuN pui5 kap iu5 pau-sim, chit khoan2 e5 lang5 boe7 kham-tit peh-kia7, kiaN5 hng7-lou7 a2-si7 chau2, in-ui7 chin chhoan2; koh-chai3 na7 siong5 lim-chiu2 oe7 hou7 huih-kng2 ngi7.  Tui3 an2-ni khah boe7 chhun-kiu3; na7 hut-jian5 siuN tang7-tioh8 thau5-lo2, lo5- tong7 bo5 khun3, heng-hun3 e5 si5 oe7 lo2-ek- huih, ti3-kau3 hut-jian5 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7.    
M7-na7 an2-ni, ia7 oe7 hou7 lang5 tioh8 sim- chong7-piN7, a2-si7 hai7-tioh8 sin5-keng-he7, (chiu7-si7 io-chi2-piN7 a2-si7 koaN-chong7-piN7), a2-si7 kiam2 sin-the2 e5 ti2-khong3-lek8, ti3-kau3 khoai3 jiam2-tioh8 thoan5-jiam2-piN7, chhin- chhiuN7 hi3-piN7, thou3-sia3, tng5-a2-piN7. Lim- chiu2 m7-na7 oe7 tui3 lang5 e5 thau5-lo2 chhiuN2 lan2 e5 cheng3-khi3, ia7 oe7 hai7 lang5 lam2-sin, hai7 lang5 liau2-chiN5, san3-hiong. 
Lim-chiu2 te7-it oe7 chou2-tong3 lang5 kiaN5 ho2 e5 khui3-lat8. Lim-chiu2 e5 lang5 e5 kiaN2 pi2 phou2-thong lang5 e5 gin-na2 khah-khoai3 si2.  Tai7-pan2 Chhi7-lip8 Siau3-lian5 Chit- 
giap8 Siong5-tam5- sou2 e5 i-seng, gian2-kiu3 lim-chiu2-ka kap lin- ji5 si2-bong5 e5 koan-he7. I e5 kiat-ko2 pai5- liat8 ti7 e7-bin7:

Koh-chai3 toa7-chiu2-ka e5 kiaN2-sun khah oe7 chhut phoa3-siuN3 gong7-tuh-tuh e5 gin2-na2.    
Lim-chiu2 ia7 u7 hou7 lan2 thang kiaN e5 ui5- thoan5-seng3 ti7-teh, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 Bu2- chong5-ia2 Hak8-iN7. Ti7-hia si7 teh siu-iong5 phaiN2 gin2-na2 e5 Kam2-hoa3-iN7. IN7-tiong u7 63 e5 phaiN2 gin2-na2. Chiau3 ui5-thoan5 ka7 in tiau-cha, ki5-tiong 34 mia5 si7 toa7-chiu2-ka e5 chu2-te7, 10 mia5 si7 in si7-toa7-lang5 bat hoan7-choe7-e5, ki5-thaN 19 mia5 put-beng5.    
Koh-chai3 hoan7-choe7 kap lim-chiu2 u7 toa7 koan-he7.  Jit8-pun2 u7 chit8 keng heng5- bu7-sou2. Ti7 hit kaN-nih8 e5 lai5-bin7 2500 lang5 tiong 2075 mia5 si7 lim-chiu2-ka, bo5 lim- chiu2 e5 lang5 kan-ta 175 mia5 na7-tiaN7.    
ChhiaN2 lan2 oe7 ki3-tit chit8 hang7: hoat- lut8-siong7 i2-kip8 to7-tek-siong7 e5 hoan7-choe7, khah-choe7 si7 tui3 lim-chiu2 lai5 khi2 in. Chiau3 Eng-kok, Bi2-kok, Tek-kok e5 Liok8- kun-seng2 e5 tiau-cha hoat-piau2, tui3 kun-tui7, hak8-seng kap kau2 e5 sit8-giam7, piN5-piN5 tit-tioh8 saN-tang5 e5 kiat-ko2, chiu7-si7 eng7 chiu2 hou7 kun-tui7, a2-si7 hak8-seng kap kau2 chiah-e5 lim, ka7 in sit8-giam7 in se3-lek8 long2 pi2 bo5 lim-chiu2 e5 si7 khah-phaiN2 seng5-chek. Sou2-i2 lan2 tioh8 chai chiah8-chiu2 sui-jian5 chit8-si5 chhin-chhiuN7 khah u7 goan5-khi3, m7- ku2 he long2 si7 it-si5-tek, kau3 au7-lai5 hoan2- tng2 khah bo5 lat8.    
TaN goa2 ai3 chiong chiu2 kap seng-li2- tek chok-iong7 kap hit e5 kiat-ko2 kan2-liok8 sia2 kui2 ku3 li2-lun7 sit8-le7.  Na7 long2 sia2, kiaN- liau2 siuN-tng5, sou2-i2 chit-tiap8 kau3 chia chiu7 ho2.    
 
Na7 u7 ki-hoe7, au7-pai2 goa2 chiah chiong lang5 chaiN2-iuN7 ai3 lim-chiu2, hian7-tai7 tak8 kok sia7-hoe7 tui3 chiu2 e5 thai3-tou7 chaiN2-iuN7 tui3 keng-che3 hong-bin7, kau3-iok8 hong- bin7 ka7 i khoaN3--khi2-lai5, lim-chiu2 thang mah, kap lim-chiu2 e5 sun2-hai7 sit8-le7, soa3 chhian2-beng5 chong-kau3 sia7-hoe7 kim3-chiu2 e5 pit-iau3, pai5-liat8 ti7 tai7-ke e5 bin7-cheng5 choe3 chham-kho2.
