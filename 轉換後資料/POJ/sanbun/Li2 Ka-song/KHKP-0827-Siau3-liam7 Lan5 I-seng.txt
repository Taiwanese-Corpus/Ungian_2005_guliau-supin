
Siau3-liam7 Lan5 I-seng
Li2 Ka-siong 
Na7 kong2-tioh8 Lan5 I-seng chiu7 beh kong2 siau2-liam7 kam2-in e5 oe7 kam2-kak kong2 long2 be7 liau2 e5 lang5 un2-tang3 hui-siong5 che7.
U7 e5 si7 siu7-tioh8 i kiu3-tioh8 seN3-mia7 khi2-si2 hoe5-seng, u7 e5 si7 hou7 i kong-jian5 a2-si7 am3-chiN7 thiaN3-thang3 pang-chan7 khan-seng5 lai5 tit-tioh8 seng-ke3 oah8-lou7 a2-si7 the5-poat8 hoat-tian2.  U7 lang5 tui3 ti7 bat i, hou7 i jin5-keh sin3-giong2 e5 kam2-hoa3 lai5 hun3-hoat lip8-chi3 toe3 i e5 kha-pou7 lai5 cho3 Chu2 e5 hak8-seng.  U7 sou2-chai7 kau3-hoe7 siu7 i khek-ki2 hong7-hian3, tit-tioh8 chha-put-to choan5-giah8 e5 kian3-tiok-hui3.  U7 lang5 tui3 i e5 su7-giap8 siu7 kam2-tong7 tit-tioh8 to7-li2, tit-tioh8 bat Chu2 e5 thiaN3, tit-tioh8 sin3-giong2 e5 kou2-le7. Chong2-si7 chit e5 chhin-chhiuN7 seng3-jin5 e5 Lan5 I-seng taN khi3-se3 lah!  Tek-khak u7 lang5 kam2-kak iau2 u7 che7-che7 pai2 iau2-boe7 ka7 i piau2 kam2-in, ka7 i piau2 kam2-sia7, m7-ku2 taN i2-keng koe3-sin lah.
Lan5 I-seng ti7 87 ni5 cheng5 chhut-si3 ti7 Sou-kek-lan5 chit8 ui7 bok8-su e5 ke.  Lian5-chheng e5 si5 siu7 tiau3-beng7 ai3 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, lang5 kou7-bu2 i thak8 sin5-hak8; i ka-ki7 kou3-khiam kong2 thai3 bo5 khau2-chai5, m7-kaN2 tng chit ho7 chit.  Khiok u7 iaN2; Lan5 I-seng m7-si7 gian5-lun7 e5 lang5, m7-si7 khau2-chai5 e5 lang5, m7-ku2 i e5 un-sun5, khoan-su3, koaN2-bek8, jin5-chu5, tok-heng7, tiong-hou7, jim2-nai7, sin3-sit8, khek-khin5, bu5-su, hi-seng, hong7-su7, i2-goa7 iau2-ku2 chin che7 chin che7 e5 ho2 tek-heng7 beh pi2-kau3 ti7 tai5-teng2 che7-che7 hiong5-pian7 e5 kang2-ian2-su5 si7 iaN5-koe3 che7-che7 be7 sng3-tit lah!
I ti7 1895 ni5 12 goeh8 18 jit8 kau3 Tai5-oan5 tui3 An-peng5 chiuN7-liok8.  Tong5-heng5 e5 u7 Liam5 Tek-liat8 Bok8-su kap Mui5 Kam-bu7 Bok8-su 3 lang5, long2 si7 Sou-kek-lan5 chhut-sin e5 ho2 tong5-chi3, lip8-chi3 beh hai2-goa7 thoan5-kau3, ka-jip8 Eng-kok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 Soan-kau3 Hiap8-hoe7.  Lai5 kau3 Tai5-oan5 liau2, ko2-jian5 bo5 ui5-poe7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau-tai3.  Tai7-seng tui3 Lim5 Ian3-sin5 Sian-siN hak8-sip8 Tai5-oan5 oe7 e5 si5, chiu7 kam2-hoa3 Lim5 Sian-siN hou7 i jip8 sin3-giong2, jian5-au7 chiaN5-cho3 Lam5-pou7 kau3-hoe7 e5 toa7 goan5-lo2.  Li7-khui Tai5-lam5 Sin-lau5 kap Mui5 Bok8-su kau3 Tiong-pou7, ko2-jian5  
chit8 e5 thoan5-to7, chit8 e5 i-liau5, kian3-lip8 Chiong-hoa3 I-koan2, i2-kip8 Toa7-sia7, Tai5-tiong, Chiong-hoa3, Lok8-kang2, Khe-ou5, Toa7-siaN5, Ji7-lim5, Hoan-oah (Hong-oan2), Chhau2-tun, Lam5-tau5, Toa7-tou7 cheng3 kau3-hoe7. Nng7 lang5 tang5 tiN-khou2, tang5 seN-si2, tang5 iau-go7, tang5 chhui3-ta lai5 kian3-siat poe5-iuN2 chiah-nih8 che7 keng e5 kau3-hoe7.
Iu5-ki5-si7 ti7 Chiong-hoa3 I-koan2 e5 kong-chek sit8-chai7 si7 oh-tit u7 lang5 e7 thang pi2-gi2 e5.  In-ui7 i u7 kau3-giah8 e5 chin7-tiong, thun-lun2, khin5-khiam7, hi-seng kap kek8 iu-oat8 e5 i-sut8 lai5 i-ti7 lang5 e5 piN7-thiaN3, ng3-bang7 tui3 an2-ni thang chhoa7 lang5 sin3 Chu2 Ia5-sou.
I 25 hoe3 kau3 Tai5-oan5, 67 hoe3 li7-khui Tai5-oan5, 42 ni5 ku2 si7 phok-sou3, khek-ki2 Khian5-seng5 e5 koe3 jit8.  Pit-chia2 hak8-seng si5-tai7 ui7 phoa3-peN7 siu7 i chiau-thai7 toa3 I e5 chhu3 kui goeh8-jit8 ku2, tak8 jit8 u7 tit-tioh8 i chong-ko jin5-keh kam2-hoa3, siang7 si5 koh u7 chit8 ui7 choan5-sin moa2-moa2 ti7 thiaN3-sim chhin-chhiat ai3 chio-hou lang5, ai3 pang-chan7 lang5 e5 I-seng-niu5 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7, chiaN5-cho3 pit-chia2 chit8-si3-lang5 siu7 kam2-hoa3 choe3 chhim e5 chit8 e5 si5-ki5.
TaN siu7 in hu-hu7 thiaN3-thang3, khan-seng5, the5-poat8, chai-poe5--e5 un2-tang3 u7 kui-pah e5; siu7 in in2-chhoa7, kam2-hoa3, thiaN3-thang3, taN2-tiap8 peN7-thiaN3 lai5 bat Chu2, soa3 ke-thiN sin3-giong2 e5, un2-tang3 u7 kui2-na7 chheng; na7 kong2 siu7 i i-ti7, taN2-tiap8 peN7-thiaN3 koh kam2-kek in e5 thiaN3-sim e5, un2-tang3 u7 kui-ban7.  M7-ku2 chit e5 chiah-nih ho2 e5 lang5, taN i keng-koe3 si3 lah!
Koh i e5 si2 si7 chin kho2-siong, in-ui7 8 goeh8 boe2 e5 chit8-jit8, i chhut-khi3 toa7-lou7 kiaN5 e5 si5, hou7 chu7-tong7 chu7-choan2-chhia tong7-tioh8, poah8-to2, thau5-khak tioh8-siong, chu7 an2-ni sit--khi3, m7-chai-lang5, au7-lai5 chai-iaN2 nau2 u7 tioh8-siong, chiah ka7 chhiu2-sut8, chong2-si7 kho2-sioh bo5 hau7.  Chap8 goeh8 11 jit8, ti7 thang3-thiaN3 siong-sim e5 tiong-kan piat8-se3.  Ui5-chok8, u7 lau7 I-seng-niu5, Tai7-pit8 I-seng, Tai7-pit8 I-seng-niu5, cha-bou2-kiaN2 Jin5-ai3, sun 3 e5.  I-seng Tai7-pit8 ke3-siok8 ui5-giap8, iau2 ti7 Chiong-hoa3 I-koan2 hok8-bu7, hian7-si5 kui-kok hiu-ka3 tiong, che si7 lan2 sou2 chai, sim-pu7 ia7 si7 I-koan2 e5 i-seng; lau7 I-seng-niu5 chhu2-khi3 si7 kou-tok8 e5 lo2-keng2, cha-bou2-kiaN2 kap chit8 e5 ta-pou goa7-sun thang kap i cho3-phoaN7 na7-tiaN7.
 
Siau3-liam7 in e5 lang5 un2-tang3 hui-siong5 che7, su7-sit8 in e5 ui5-tek ia7 si7 chin-che7, pit-chia2 ti7 moa2-pak e5 kam2-kek kap siong-sim, seng sia2 kui2-ji7 lai5 po3-ko3, soa3 kiu5 Chu2 lin5-bin2 chiau3-kou3, lau7 I seng-niu5 kap chu ui5-chok8 e5 teng2-bin7, koh hou7 i e5 ui5-giap8 ti7 Chiong-hoa3 toa7-toa7 thang ke3-siok8 eng5-kng Chu2.  A-beng7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 827 ho7,1957 ni5 11 geh8)
