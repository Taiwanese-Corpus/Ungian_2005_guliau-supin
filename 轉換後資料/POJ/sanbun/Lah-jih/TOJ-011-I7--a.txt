I7..a
I7..a chit-e5 mia5-su5, m7-si7 beh hou7 goa2 kio3..e5, si7 kan-ta beh hou7 goa2 chheng-hou, hou7 goa2 siau3-liam7..e5, in-ui7 ti7 goa2 ki3-ti5 lai7-te2, chiong5-lai5 tioh8 m7-bat tng-bin7 kio3 i 1 siaN " I7..a", in-ui7 I7..a  un7-khi3 bai2, put-heng7 goeh8-lai7-hong soa3 lai5 koe3-sin, ma7 chhoa7 chau2 taN5(pian-chip: taN chiah) chhut-si3 bo5 goa7-ku2 e5 sio2-moai7, pang3-sak goa2 chit-e5 phaiN2-mia7 e5 ban-a2-kiaN2. Hit-ni5 goa2 5 hoe3, to iau2-boe7 4 chiok-hoe3--leh, thiaN kong2 I7..a ma7 chiah 40 thong2 hoe3 nia5(pian-chip: na7-tiaN7).
Ui7-tioh8 beh ka7 I7..a e5 han3-chhau2, bin7-bou5 khng3-tiam3 ki3-ti5 lai7-te2, thang ho2 si5-si5 siau3-liam7, tioh8 khai-si2 si3-ke3 peng2 toh-kui7-a2, hian chhu3-lai7 e5 hou7-khau2-mia5-phou7, sin-hun7-cheng3, A-hiaN thak8--koe3 e5 chheh, sui-jian5 bo5 goa7-che7 mih8 thang peng2, iu5-goan5 chhiau kui2 na7 pian3, long2 chhoe7 bo5 I7..a e5 siong3-phiN3, ma7 bo5 I7..a e5 ke3-chng, a2-si7 hoe-hun2 hit-lui7 e5 cha-bou2-lang5 mih8-kiaN7.
Bo5 siong3 thang khoaN3, iong7 mng7..e5. Mng7 A-pa, mng7 A-hiaN, mng7 A-peh, A-m2, mng7 chhu3-piN keh-piah, tit-tioh8 e5 kiat-lun7 si7: seN-cho3 lo3-lo3, han3-chhau2 be7 bai2, kha-chhiu2 liu2-liah8, ho2 cho3-hoe2, ai3 pang-chan7--lang5....
Seng5 chia5? Che si7 goa2 siong7 beh chai-iaN2..e5. “Seng5 ji7-hiaN, lang5 lo3-lo3”. “Seng5 lin2 ji7-che2--lah, bin7-a2 iN5-iN5”. “Kah-na2 seng5 Lin2 A-ku7, bak8-chiu chhim-o chhim-o”. “Siong7 kai3 seng5 lin2 ke3 hai2-khau2 hit-e5 A-i5”. A-i5 leh? A-i5--leh? “Si2 lau7-bu2, lou7-thau5 tng7“, hng7 ti7 hai2-khau2, ia7 m7-bat lai5-khi3. Thau5-khak lai7 iu5-goan5 bo5 heng5-iaN2. Ki3-ti5 e5 chhng-khou3, I7..a e5 hit-keh ui7-ti3, iu5-goan5 si7 khang-peh8, bang7-tiong e5 bo2-chhin, si7 bong5-bong5 chit8-phian3, chi2 u7 leng5-hun5 toe3 sin-piN.
I7..a phaiN2-mia7, se3-han3 tioh8 hou7 A-ma2 pho7 lai5 io, phiau-chun2 e5 sin-pu7-a2 mia7, sui-jian5 bo5-lang5 gek8-thai7..i, chong2--si7 bo5-thang hiong2-siu7 cho3 lang5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 e5 chu7-iu5, lang5 kong2 “kam-goan7 cho3 san3-hiong-lang5 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, m7 cho3 ho2-giah8-lang5 sin-pu7”, ho5-hong2 lan2 Siau--ka kang7-khoan2 si7 san3-phi2-phi2 e5 chng-kha-lang5.
I7..a ti7-si5 kap A-pa kat-pang5 sak-cho3-tui, goa2 m7 chai-iaN2. 40 thong2 hoe3 e5 jin5-seng, seN 6 e5 hau7-seN, 3 e5 cha-bou2 kiaN2, phah-sng2 2 e5, chhun e5 5 e5 hau7-seN, 2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 long2 u7 ho2 ke-kah, kian3-lip8 bi2-moa2 ka-teng5, sui-jian5 m7 si7 toa7-hu3 toa7-kui3, chong2--si7 lau2-sit8 phah-piaN3 koe3-jit8, m7-bat cho3 phaiN2 tai7-chi3, sng3 si7 u7 ho2 ui5-thoan5, bo5 pai7-hai7 tioh8 pe7-bo2 e5 mia5-siaN. I7..a e5 it-seng, sit8-chai7 bo5 ong2-hui3 i lai5 se3-kan kiaN5 chit-choa7.
I7..a! Li2 ti7 to2-ui7? Kiam2 be7 su-liam7 Li2 e5 ban-a2-kiaN2, i i2-keng 60 hoe3, jit8-jit8 siuN7 beh kap Li2 siong-hoe7, ng3-bang7 I7..a Li2 lai5 thok-bang7, iah8-si7 tan2-thai7 Li2 e5 ban-a2-kiaN2, se3-kan chek-jim7 oan5-moa2, chiah lai5 sio-chhoe7.
