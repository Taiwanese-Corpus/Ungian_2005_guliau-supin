
Hi-han2 e5 Kiu3-lang5
Tan5 Thiam-ong7
    Kun7-jit8 tui3 Tan5 Bit Thoan5-to7 thiaN-kiN3 e5 siau-sit, soa3 kio3 goa2 sia2 kui2-ji7 ti7 po3-choa2:--   
Sin-pou Kau3-hoe7 Un A-lim5 tiam3 ti7 Loe5-thau5-soaN sio-hia7, chhu3-au7 chit8 khut toa7-tham5, put-si5 poah8-lang5 sit-loh8 siN3-mai7; lang5 kong2, “Liah8 kau-thoe3.”    
Ku7-lek8 7 geh8 29 jit8, hut-jian5 A-lim5-hiaN e5 kiaN2 A-an, chhit he3, poah8-loh8 chui2. Kai-chai3 A-lim5 e5 sio2-ti7 A-hong e5 kiaN2 A-chui2, chap8-it he3 na7-tiaN7, khoaN3-kiN3 sio2-ti7 poah8-loh8-chui2, sui5-si5 thng3-saN loh8-chui2 siu5 khi3 kiu3 i, kau3 kiu3 khi2-lai5, gin2-na2 to chhin-chhiuN7 si2; kai-chai3 koh kiu3-oah8 khi2-lai5.    
Hit-si5 u7 toa7-lang5 tiam3 soaN-teng2 teh khoaN3, u7 lang5 khi3 kio3 gin2-na2 e5 lau7-pe7; siat-su2 in lau7-pe7 lai5 ia7 kiu3 bo5 lang5.    
Chiau3-khoaN3 chap8-it he3 kiaN2, u7 hit ho7 e5 sim-hiong, sit8-chai7 kho2-chhu2; thang khoaN3 ui7-tioh8 kut-jiok8 e5 thiaN3-sim chin toa7. Chit khoan2 e5 kiu3-lang5 sit8-chai7 chin hi-han2.    
Lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 hiaN-chi2, na7 oh8 chit e5 gin2-na2 e5 phe-mia7 kap thiaN3 kut-jiok8 e5 hiaN-ti7, m7-chai oe7 ke-kiu3 loa7-choe7 lang5 chhut ti7 tim5-lun5 e5 sou2-chai7 ?  Chin-choe7 lang5 si7 kan-ta khia7 teh khoaN3, kap kio3 lang5 chhut-lat8; sit8-chai7 tioh8 ka-ti7 chhut-lat8.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 488 koan3,1925 ni5 11 geh8)
