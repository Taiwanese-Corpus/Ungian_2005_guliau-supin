
 
Chan5 jim2 e5 tang5-ki
 TiuN Chheng-chun3    
Lun7 Tiong-lou7 e5 kau3-hoe7, tui3 siat kau3-taN oa2 u7 8,9 ni5 ku2; cheng5 bo5 pai3-tng5, sou2 chu7-chip8 chiu7-si7 hiaN-ti7 e5 chhu3:ia7 bo5 thoan5-to7, si7 Toa7-oh8 e5 peng5- iu2 lun5-liu5 teh lai5 le2-pai3; kau3 kin-ni5 Kau3-su7 phai3 goa2 lai5 toa3. Ia7 bo5 tiuN2-lo2, chi2-u7 chit8-e5 chip8-su7; chit-e5 kau3-hoe7 si7 siok8 Tai5-lam5 e5 chi-hoe7, li7 Tai5-lam5-siaN5 2 phou3 tng5-tng5, siok8 Hong7-soaN-thiaN koan2; oa2 ti7 Kong-soaN e5 sou2-chai7. Mui2 le2-pai3 khah siong5 u7 60 goa7 lang5 le2-pai3. Kau3 kin-ni5 chiu7 khi2 chit8-keng pai3-tng5, khai chheng-goa7-khou, si3-ui5 e5 chng-thau5 long2 chai. Hian7-si5 ia7 u7 che7-che7 lang5 ai3 lai5 khoaN3. Ia7 u7 sin thiaN e5 lang5 lai5-lai5 khi3-khi3.    
Ki5-tiong li7 Tiong-lou7 oa2 6 li2, u7 chit8 chng mia5 Ka-tang-kha, u7 3 lang5 lai5 sin thiaN, in-e5 mia5 chiu7-si7 Toa7-chheng5, Hoe- siuN7, Li2 ChoaN5. In lai5 thiaN 2 le2-pai3, chiu7 tu2-tioh8 in chng-tiong e5 tang5-ki,chiuN7-tang5, long2 bo5 koe3 ku7-lek8 9 goeh8 10 jit8 chiu7 beh sio in e5 chhu3, kong2 in khi3  jip8 kau3; taN i m7 koan2 in 3 lang5 khi3 jip8 kau3, tek-khak beh sio in-e5 chhu3, tui3 hit- tiap8 in chiu7 m7-kaN2 lai5 le2-pai3.    
Kau3 ku7-lek8 9 goeh8 7 jit8 chiu7-si7 le2-pai3-jit8, hit e7-pou goa2 chiu7 khi3 chhoe7 in, khou2-khng3 in tioh8 koh lai5; in kong2, Put8 beh sio goan2 e5 chhu3. Goa2 chiu7 ka7 in kong2 bo5-iaN2, na7 cho3 u7-iaN2 chiu7-si7 lang5 sio, put8 be7 sio. Au7-lai5 tam5-lun7 khou2- khng3 kau3 jit8 beh loh8, goa2 chiu7 koh tng2- lai5 kau3 Tiong-lou7 chiah8 e5-hng; chiah8 pa2 bo5 loa7-ku2 chha-put-to oa2 9 tiam2 cheng in e5 chhu3 chiu7 hou7 hoe2 sio khi3. Chin-chiaN3 toa7 kho2-lin5. In-ui7 hiah-e5 lang5 si7 san3-hiong, koh long2 bo5 theh8 poaN3 hang7 mih8 chhut--lai5. Sim siuN7 liau2 put-chi2 kan-khou2, goa2 chiu7 tham3-thiaN khoaN3 si7 an2-choaN2 sio.    
U7 lang5 ka7 goa2 kong2, si7 tang5-ki pang3-hoe2. I chiong hiuN chat hoe2-chha5 ka7 i  
he7 ti7 chhu3-teng2 e5 hm5-chhau2 sio. In chhu3-chu2 ia7 chai si7 tang5-ki pang3-hoe2,  chong2-si7 m7-kaN2 ka7 chhut-sou2 kong2. Au7-lai5 goan2 u7 chham-siong5 tek-khak tioh8 siat-hoat, tu2-ho2 chip-su7 u7 tng chong3-teng thoan5-tiuN2, hit-tiap8 chip-su7 u7 kap kui2-na7 e5 hiaN-ti7 khi3 liah8 hit-e5 tang5-ki, ka7 i liah8 e5 si5 u7 ka7 i kong2, kah i tioh8 poe5, na7 bo5 beh liah8 hou7 Chhut-sou2. Hit- tiap8 i u7 jin7, ia7 in3 beh poe5 20 khou. Au7 lai5 liah8 hou7 Chhut-sou2; Chhut-sou2 chiu7 tiau3 in-e5 po2-cheng3, kap hou7 hoe2 sio chhu3 e5 lang5; kho2-sioh hou7 hoe2 sio-chhu3 e5 lang5 kiaN tang5-ki e7 koh-chai3 hai7 i, soa3 m7-kaN2kong2 si7 tang5-ki sio, kong2 si7 sio chhau2- sap tou3--tioh8. Po2-cheng3 ia7-si7 kiaN an2-ni; ia7 the3 tang5-ki po2 bo5-su7 .Lian5-au7 phah- phah pang3 i khi3, kah i hit keng chhau2 e5 put8-bio7 tioh8 pang3-hoe2 sio, m7-thang koh cho3 tang5-ki. HaiN3 ah ! Hian7-si5 iau2 u7 chit ho7 lang5 oan3-hin7 to7-li2 an2-ni, sit8-chai7 chin thang hoan5-lo2. TaN chhiaN2 lin2 tioh8 the3 chiah-e5 kau3-hoe7 kiu5 Siong7- te3; hou7 chiah-e5 lang5 e7 tit-chheN2-gou7, chai in pai3 put8 e5 chho3; in-ui7 Siong7-te3 tek- khak e7 iaN5 tak8 teng2 e5 sin5-beng5. (KhoaN3 10 goeh8 20 ho7 e5 Chu2-jit8 Sin5-liong5). 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 272 koan3,1907 ni5 11 geh8)
