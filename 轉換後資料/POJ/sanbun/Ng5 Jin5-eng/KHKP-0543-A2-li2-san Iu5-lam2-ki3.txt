
A2-li2-san Iu5-lam2-ki3
(Ng5 Jin5-eng5)
Ti7 4 goeh8 2 jit8, phi2-jin5 kap kui2-na7 lang5 tui3 Ka-gi7 che7 7 tiam2 5 hun e5 A-li2-san-soaN3 jip8-khi3, u7 keng-koe3 18 e5 iah8-cham7 chiah kau3 ui7.  Au7-lai5 koh che7 chhia jip8 khi3 kau3 teh chho3-chhiu7 e5 sou2-chai7, si7 ti7 Hoh8-soaN kap Bin5-goeh8, i2-keng nng7 tiam2-poaN3.  Tou5-tiong u7 keng-koe3 69 e5 pong7-khang.  SoaN-lou7 e5 tng5 u7 4812 li2 3 hun.  Lou7-tou5 khi-khu, hoe2-chhia kap peN5-iuN5 e5 chhia bo5 siang7, khah se3-tiuN, ia7 chhia-thau5 e5 ki-khi3 bo5 siang7 e5 chin che7.  Beh jip8-khi3 chhia-thau5 si7 cho3 au7-piah sak,beh chhut-lai5 e5 si5 si7 cho3 cheng5, thang tong3 chhia hou7 i kiaN5-liau2 khah e7 sun7-su7.  Tou5-tiong san-lim5 chin che7, thau5-chams7? Jiat8-tai3-lim5.  Tiong-cham7 si7 Loan2-tai3-lim5, kau3 A-li2-san si7 Un-tai3-lim5.  Chhiu7-bak8 chin che7 khoan2, u7 sam, siong5-ngou5, ia7 u7 ou siong5-ngou5 chin sui2; u7-e5 10 goa7 tng7 koan5, u7-e5 si7 chin lau7-chang5, che7-che7 si7 chit8-nng7 chheng ni5.  Ki5-tiong u7 chit8 chang5 chiau3 hak8-chia2 cheng3-beng5 kong2 oa2 3000 ni5.  Eng5-lim5-sou2 i2-keng theh8 chin che7 sam chhut-lai5, hian7-si5 chit khoan2 e5 chha5 chin tat8 chiN5, chin sui2, khi2 chhu3 lang5 chin ai3 eng7, u7-e5 phoe3 khi3 Loe7-te7 chin che7.  Chiau3 sou2 an3-sng3 chiah u7 thang koh theh8 10 goa7 tang na7-tiaN7,chong2-si7 u7 chiam7-chiam7 koh chai sin-e5,m7-ku2 chit khoan2 chha5 teh hoat chin ban7,na7 toa7 kau3 chit8 chiu-ui5 e5 toa7, tioh8 ai3 10 goa7 ni5 ku2.  
Keng2-ti3 chin ho2, khi3-hau7 siang7 koan5 si7 70 tou7, siang7 ke7 si7 30 tou7, chit8 ni5 e5 peng5-kin si7 55 tou7.  Lu2-koan2 ia7 chin li7-pian7.  Hian7-si5 Tho5-hoe, Eng-hoe, It-hioh8-lan5 long2 toa7 khui.  Kau3 A-li2-sani2-keng u7 hai2-poat2 7500 chhioh.  Goan2 ti7 chhe-saN cha2-khi2 4 tiam2 goa7 u7 peh chiuN7 Chiok-soaN si7 8263 chhioh, ia7 tui3-bin7 u7 che7-che7 koan5-soaN.  (Sin-ko-soaN,Tang-soaN, Sai-soaN, Pak-soaN, Chian5-soaN, Lam5-soaN) long2 si7 ban7 chhioh i2-siong7 e5 koan5-soaN.  Ki5-tiong Sin-ko-soaNsiang7 koan5 u7 1,3635 chhioh.  
Jit8-thau5 beh chhut e5 si5 keng2-ti3 chiaN5 ho2 long2 khoaN3 chin beng5, koh bin7-cheng5 e5 sio2 soaN long2 u7 chhiu7-bak8, ia7 chhiu7-bak8 e5 e7-bin7 u7 peh8-hun5 chin sui2, ho7-cho3 Hun5-hai2; chit e5 keng2-ti3 si7 chap8-hun eb5i-le7.  Lang5 na7 ai3 peh Sin-ko-soaN tui3 chia khi3 si7 khah kin7, chiah iau2 7 li2 goal73.  Chong2-si7 ong2-hok8 iu5-goan5 tioh8 saN-jit8.  Ti7 A-li2-san e5 hu7-kin7 u7 nng7 sia7 echheN5-hoan.  Chit8 sia7 kio3-cho3 Tat2-pang-sia7, chit8 sia7 Lah2-lah2-chih, long2 si7 chin toa7 sia7, long2 be7 thai5 lang5, chin un-sun5.  Lang5 na7 kau3 hia khoaN3 tioh8 chit e5 chu7-jian5-kai3 e5 bi2-le7, li2 un2-tang3 hou7 lang5 ke-thiN keng3-ui3 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 koan5-leng5.  Lang5 na7 khoaN3 tioh8 se3-kan e5 toa7 kian3-tiok, chiu7 o-lo2 chin sui2 chin gau5, chong2-si7 na7 jip8 khi3 kau3 ti7 Chiok-soaN teng2 khoaN3-tioh8 bin7-cheng5 sou2 khoaN3-tioh8 ech5u-jian5-kai3, chiu7 chai Siong7 Chu2 e5 koan5-leng5 pi2 lang5 kui2 chheng ban7 poe7.  
Chong2-si7 lang5 na7 khi3 A-li2-san khoaN3chho3 chhiu7, tioh8 soa3 khoaN3 Ka-gi7 Eng5-lim5-sou2 e5 chok-giap8 chiah khah chiau5-pi7.
