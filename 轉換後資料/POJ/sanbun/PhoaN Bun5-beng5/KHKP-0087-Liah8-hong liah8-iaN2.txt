
Liah8-hong liah8-iaN2

(PhoaN Bun5-beng5 cho3--e5.)

Tai7-seng lai5 lun7-khi2 hong kap iaN2 , chiu7  chai liah8-hong liah8-iaN2 si7  khang-khang.   Lun7 chit-e5 hong si7 u7 thang thiaN i-e5 siaN , bo5 thang khoaN3  i-e5 heng5 ; chit-e5 hong si7 oah8-khi3 . Lang5 bo5 thang chai chit-e5 hong tiaN7-tioh8  tiam3 ti7 tah-loh8 chit8 sou2-chai7 , ia7 m7-chai chhoe khi3 hioh ti7 to2-ui7 ; put-ko3 lang5 khoaN3 i sou2 chhoe e5 chhau2-bak8 , thang chai si7 tah-lah8 lai5 , ia7 tah-loh8 khi3 .  Hong teh chhoe chiu7-si7 tui3 hit-e5 oah8-khi3 teh-chau2 ; hit-e5 oah8-khi3 na7 toa7-e7 chau2 , hong   chiu7 toa7 , ia7 chin-kin2 , liam5-piN chhoe lai5, liam5-piN koe3 khi3 , na7-si7 khoaN-khoaN chau2 chiu7 peng5-ho5 .  Chit-e5 hong si7 toa7 li7-ek lang5 , kap tak8-hang7 mih8 ; na7 bo5  chit-e5 hong , lang5 kap ban7-mih8 be7 tit-tioh8 oah8 .
Lun7 iaN8 iah8-si7 bo5 mih8 ti7 hit  lai7-bin7 , put-ko3 si7 tui3 u7-heng5 chiah u7--e5 . Na7-si7 ai3 khoaN3-tioh8 hit-e5 iaN2 , tioh8 u7 saN-hang7 chiau5-chng5 ; chiu7-si7 ,－1. Tioh8 u7 kng . 2. Tioh8 u7 heng5 .  3. Tioh8 u7 thou5-kha a2-si7 piah .  Siat-su2 na7 kan-ta u7 kng bo5 heng5 chiu7 bo5 iaN2 thang khoaN3 ; na7 u7 heng5 bo5 kng , iah8 bo5 thang khoaN3 iaN2; kiam2-chhai2 na7 u7 kng u7 heng5, na7 bo5 thou5-kha a2-si7 piah thang chio3--tioh8 , iah8 be7-e7 khoaN3 tioh8 iaN2 . Phi3-ju7 lang5 na7 khia7 ti7 lim2-kiN5 e5 sou2-chai7, i-e5 iaN2 na7 sia7 tui3 kham3-te2 loh8-khi3 , chiu7 long2 bo5 thang khoaN3--tioh8 i-e5 iaN2 . Teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2-khi2 hit nng7-hang7 mih8 ; chit8-ho7 si7 bak8-chiu khoaN3 e7-kiN3 , chiu7-si7 iaN2 , koh chit8-ho7 chiu7-si7 hi7-khang thiaN e7-tioh8 , chiu7-si7 hong .  Chit nng7-hang7 sui-si7 thiaN e7-tioh8 , khoaN3 e7-kiN3 , m7-ku2 be7-tit thang eng7 chhiu2 lai5 liah8--i .
Ki3-jian5 si7 an2-ni , chaiN2-iuN7 kong2 liah8 hong liah8 iaN2 ? Chiu7-si7 lang5 e5 sou2 cho3 che7-che7 si7 an2-ni , chhin-chhiuN7 hu3-kui3 , pin5-chian7 , i2-kip8  lang5  kap se3-kan long2-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hong chin-chhiuN7 iaN2 . Phi3-ju7  lang5 ai3 ke3-bou5 lai5 tit-tioh8 kong-beng5 , a2-si7  ai3 tit-tioh8 hu3-kui3 ,chit-ho7 lang5 e5 sou2 cho3 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 liah8-hong liah8-iaN2,in-ui7 m7 eng-kai tit-tioh8 kong-beng2 i iah8-si7 me5-jit8 lai5 siuN7 , u7-si5  kan-ta hui3 i-e5 sim-lat8 kau3 boe2 si7 khang-khang ; u7-si5  u7 hou7 i  tit-tioh8 , m7-ku2 lang5 bo5 sim2-mih8 o-lo2 hit-ho7 e5 lang5 .  KhoaN3 Thoan5-to7 4 : 16. Chhin-chhiuN7 chit-e5 lang5 si7 tu2-tu2 chiaN5 gu5-gong7 .  Ai3 tit-tioh8 ho2-giah8 e5 lang5 iu5-goan5 si7 an2-ni , sui-jian5 hit-e5 chiN5-chai5 ti7 hit-e5 hou2-chhui3 e5 lai7-bin7 , ia7 m7 kiaN ; kan-ta tit-tioh8 chiN5, bo5 iau3-kin2 ka-ti7 e5 siN3-mia7,chiu7 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7 i ia7 kam-goan7 . HaiN7 ah！lang5 kiam2 thang khoaN3-khin i-e5 siN3-mia7 , lai5 tui3-tiong7  chhin-chhiuN7 hong chin-chhiuN7 iaN2 mah?
Tui3  an2-ni lai5 khoaN3 , m7-na7 si7 hu3-kui3 pin5-chian7 , si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hong chhin-chhiuN7 iaN2 : chiu7-si7 chin toa7 chin khoah e5 mih8 ia7 e7 an2-ni , chiu7-si7 chit-e5 thiN-te7. KhoaN3 Ma2-thai3 24 : 35 . Kong2 , “ ThiN-te7 thang hoe3-bo5 ” , chit-ku3 oe7 m7-si7 pat8-lang5 kong2--e5 , chiu7-si7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou chhin-chhui3  kong2--e5 .  Koh khoaN3 ti7 I-sai3-a 51: 6 .  Kong2 , “ Lin2 siaN2-su7 m7 kiah8-thau5 khoaN3 thiN, aN3-loh8 khoaN3 te7?thiN tek-khak soaN3 chhin-chhiuN7 ian , thiN-e7 tek-khak phoa3 chhin-chhiuN7 saN ”. Ti7 Hi-pek-lai5 e5 phoe , kap ti7  Pi2-tek te7-ji7 tiuN e5 phoe , iah8 long2 u7 kong2-khi2 thiN-te7 beh pang-to2 .
Chiau3 se3-kan u7 mia5-siaN e5 kok lai5 lun7 , iah8-si7  bo5 ku2-tng5 ; u7-e5 kok cha2-si5 chin chhut-mia5 , ia7 chin kiong5-beng2 , kau3 taN si7  loan2-jiok8  koa3 soe-bi5 , ia7 soa3 hong-hoe3--khi3 ;  chhin-chhiuN7 Pa-pi2-lun5-siaN5pun2 si7  kek8 kian-kou3 , iah8 put-chi2 iong2-beng2 , khi3  kau-chian3  bo5-m7 khah-iaN5, chhin-chhiuN7 Kiu3-chu2 boe7 kang3-seng gou7 lak8 pah ni5 e5 tiong-kan , lian5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 iah8 hou7 i kong-phoa3 ;  ti7 si3-piN e5 kok bo5-m7 hang5-hok8--i : kau3 chit-tiap8 hiam2-hiam2 chhoe7 bo5 i-e5  chong-chek .  M7-na7 Pa-pi2-lun5-kok an2-ni , chiu7 pat8-kok iah8-si7 an2-ni ; chhin-chhiuN7 ki5-u5 , Bi2-thai3 , Pa-se, Hi-li7-ni5 , Lo5-ma2 , chiah-e5 kok , chenghit5-tiap8 si7 chin chhut-miaN5 e5 kok , Seng3-keng pi2-pheng7 in si7 kim gin5 tang thih .  KhoaN3 Tan7-i2-li2 ji7 chiuN , thong  chiuN sou2-ki3 chiu7 chai .  Kau3 chit-chun7 bo5 sim2-mih8 thiaN tioh8 in e5 miaN5-siaN lah !  Chit-ho7 kiam2 m7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hong chhin-chhiuN7 iaN2 mah?
Chiu7 an2-ni lai5  khoaN3 , lang5 toa3 ti7 se3-kan kiam2 e7 pi2 thiN-te7 khah ku2-tng5 mah？ in-ui7 se3-kan siong7-chhiaN2 e7 hoe3-bo5 , ho5-hong3 lan2 se3-kan lang5 .  Lang5  chhut-si3 toa3 ti7 chit se3-kan , put-ko3 si7 kui2-chap8 ni5 na7-tiaN7 , chiu7 kui thou2 .  Na7 khoaN3 Pi2-tek te7-it tiuN e5 phoe 1 : 17 sou2 kong2--e5 chiu7 chai , kong2 , “ Lang5 chiu7-si7 kia3-siN ti7 chit se3-kan “ .Koh khoaN3 ti7 Hi-pek-lai5 13 : 14 . Kong2 , ” Ti7 se3-kan lan2 bo5 ku2-tng5  e5 siaN5 lai5 khia7-khi2” . Tui3 an2-ni thang chai  lang5 iu5-goan5 chhin-chhiuN7 hong kap iaN2 , bo5 tiaN7-tioh8 tiam3 chit se3-kan ;  chhin-chhiuN7 lang5 kin-a2-jit8 ti7 se3-kan , bin5-a2-jit8 a2-si7 koe3 kui2-na7-jit8 si2--khi3 , kiam2-chhai2 kin-ni5  ti7 se3-kan , kau3 me5-ni5 seng-khu e5 bah i2-keng noa7-liau2 lah . Lang5 si7 tu2-tu2  chhin-chhiuN7 hong chhoe—lai5 liam5-piN koe3--khi3 , lang5 na7-si7 phah-sng3 i beh tiam3 se3-kan ku2-ku2 , hit-ho7 lang5 si7 liah8-hong liah8-iaN2--e5 , thang kong2 si7 gong-gong7 ; sou2 phah-sng3  si7 khang-khang. In-ui7 se3-kan si7 chioh lang5 toa3 , chiu7 se3-kan sou2 u7  tak8-hang7 mih8 iah8-si7 chioh lang5 eng7 : be7-tit thang chiong i ti7 siN-cheng5 sou2 ke3-bou5 sou2 chek-chu7 hiah-e5 mia5-siaN  hu3-kui3 saN-kap chong3 ti7 te7-hiat8 .  Na7-si7 an2-ni beh chaiN2-iuN7? Kiam2 thang eng5-eng5 koe3 jit8 lai5 theng3-hau7  si2 mah?  Sit8-chai7 bo5 chit-ho7 e5 i3-su3 , in-uil7ang u7 su3-chi , tek-khak tioh8 chin7 i-e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 cho3-kang , tek-khak  be7-tit thang eng5-eng5 koe3-jit8 lai5 theng3-hau7 si2 . Chhin-chhiuN7 Po2-lo5 kia3 phoe hou7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka e5 lang5 kong2 , “ Lang5 na7 bo5 beh cho3-kang chiu7  m7-thang hou7 i chiah8 ” . KhoaN3 Hou7-su 3 : 10 .  Put-ko3 ti7 cho3-kang e5 lai7-bin7 tioh8 siuN7 , chiu7-si7 m7-thang liah8 cho3-kang  khah  koe3-thau5 ti7 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 ;  iu5-goan5 tioh8 chun5 chit8-tiam2 keng3-khian5 hok8-sai7 e5 sim .  KhoaN3  The5-mou5-thai3  Chian5-su 4 : 8 . Kong2 , “ Tiau7-iong2 seng-khu--e5  hit-e5 li7-ek si7 sio2-khoa2 , keng3-khian5  hok8-sai7  Chu2 hit-e5 li7-ek si7 toa7 ” . Lang5 siong5-siong5 ui7-tioh8 cho3-kang ti3-kau3 chiaN5 liah8-hong liah8-iaN2  e5 sou2 cho3 ;si7 siuN7  i sou2 cho3 e5 kang , sou2 tit-tioh8--e5  si7 chhut tui3 i pun2-sin e5 pun2-su7 , chiu7 m7 chai hit-e5 e7 hou7 i cho3-kang--e5 si7 chi7-chui7 . M7-na7 an2-ni na7-tiaN7 , soa3 u7-si5 in-ui7 ti7 cho3-kang ai3  tit-tioh8 hu3-kui3 , lai5 sit-loh8  siN3-mia7 . Lang5  tek-khak m7-thang u7  chit-ho7 e5 pe7-peng7 ; tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7  Lo5-ma2 6 : 13 , sou2 kong2--e5 , “ Boh8-tit thong3-chhiong3 su3-the2  cho3 put-gi7 e5  khi3-khu7 , lai5 hoan7-choe7 , tek-khak  tioh8  chhin-chhiuN7 koh-oah8 e5 lang5 , hian3  pun2-sin hou7  Siong7-te3 , hou7 su3-the2 cho3 gi7 e5 khi3-khu7 lai5 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 ” .
TaN  hoan7 siok8 Chu2 e5 lang5 , tioh8 kap chit e5  liah8-hong  liah8-iaN2 bo5 kan-siap8 ; in-ui7 sin3  Chu2 e5 lang5 sou2 ke3-bou5--e5 ,  chiu7-si7 chin--e5 u7-heng5--e5 , ku2-tng5 eng2-eng2 ti7-teh e5 kok , bo5 pian3-oaN7 e5 sou2-chai7 , chin e5 chun-kui3  eng5-kng ,  chiu7-si7 thian-tong5 e5 hok-khi3 .  Seng3-keng kong2 si7 “ Eng2-oah8 Siong7-te3 e5 siaN5 ,  thiN-teng2 e5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 ” , khoaN3 Hi-pek-lai5  12 : 22 .  Lun7 chit e5 siaN5 si7 eng5-kng koa3  po2-poe3 , ti7  Bek8-si7-liok8  21 : 21 . Kong2 , “ Eng7 kim phou ke-lou7 ,  eng7 chin-chu cho3 siaN5-mng5 ” .  Ia7-si7 be7  hoe3-bo5  e5 chun-kui3 . Po2-lo5 kong2  tit-tioh8 be7  pai7-hoai7 e5 bian2-liu5 , khoaN3  I  Ko-lim5-to 9 : 25 . Lun7 ti7-hia  e5 ke-giap8 , su3-tou5 Pi2-tek  kong2 , －“ Tit--tioh8  bo5  pai7-hoai7 , bo5  la5-sam5 , bo5 hoe3-bo5 e5 ke-giap8 , the3-lan2 pi7-pan7 ti7  thiN-nih8 ” , I  1 : 4 .  M7-na7 chit kui2-chat u7 kan-cheng3 ,  lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou iah8 bat kong2 , “ Tioh8 chek chiN5-chai5  ti7  thiN--nih8 , ti7 hia thang5 be7  chiah8 , sian  be7  phah-phaiN2 , chhat8 be7  thau-theh8  e5 sou2-chai7 ” , khoaN3 Ma2-thai3  6 : 21 .  Chit-ho7 hok-khi3  kiam2-thang bo5 ke3-bou5 lai5 tit-tioh8 i mah？ In-ui7 ti7 se3-kan--e5 e7 hoe3-bo5 , chhin-chhiuN7 hong kap iaN2 e5 hok-khi3 , lang5 siong7-chhiaN2 kam-goan7  phoe-mia7 lai5 tit-tioh8 , lai5 ke3-bou5 , na7-si7 au7-lai5 e5 hok-khi3 koh khah tioh8 ke-thiN chhut-lat8 , lai5 keng-eng5--i .  Na7-si7 e7-hiausi2uN an2-ni , au7-lai5 iah8  e7-hiau2 thang lai5 chhoe7  chit-ho7 e5 hok-khi3 , chiu7 be7 chhin-chhiuN7 liah8-hong liah8-iaN2 e5 lang5 e5 sou2-cho3 , in-ui7  liah8-hong liah8-iaN2 si7 kan-ta khang-khang tioh8-boa5 bo5 li7-ek .
