Lai5-khi3 Seng-oah8-iaN5
Abon

Kin-ni5 si7 goa2 te7 3 pai2 chu2-pan7 Tai5-oan5 ko-tiong-seng Tai5-gi2 seng-oah8-iaN5, chit-pai2 e5 lang5 chin chio2, m7-koh hak8-oan5 e5 piau2-hian7 long2 hou7 goa2 chiok kam2-tong7--e5. Tak8-e5 lang5 e5 mia5, seN cho3 siaN2 khoan2, goa2 long2 u7 chiok chhim e5 in3-siong2, tan7-si7 beh long2 kong2--chhut lai5, khiong2-kiaN chit-hun7 thong-sin3 e7 bo5 kau3 sia2.

1993 ni5 hioh-joah, goa2 kha3 tian7-oe7 khi3 po3-mia5 hit-ni5 e5 Tai5-gi2 seng-oah8-iaN5. Hit-pian3 iaN5-tui7 e5 kang-chok jin5-oan5 pi2 hak8-oan5 khah che7. Tak8-chou e5 chou-thau5 chin phah-piaN3 chhoa7 hak8-oan5. Kang-chok jin5-oan5 ma7 chin jiat8-sim kap hak8-oan5 khai-kang2. Tak8-am3 long2 kap in kang2 kau3 beh thiN-kng chiah khi3 khun3.

Kap goa2 khai-kang2 e5 kang-chok jin5-oan5 long2 u7 ka-ki7 tui3 sia7-hoe7 e5 khoaN3-hoat, in ka7 goa2 kong2 in sim-
lai7 bi2-ho2 e5 Tai5-oan5, koh u7 in beh ui7 Tai5-oan5 cho3 siaN2.

Kap in kang2 chiah che7, goa2 tit8-tioh8 hak8-hau7 lau7-su bo5 ka3 lan2 e5 mih8-kiaN7, goa2 ma7 chai-iaN2 hak8-hau7 lau7-su theh8 chin che7 ke2 e5 mih8-kiaN7 ka7 lan2 phian3.

Ti7 iau2-boe7 chham-ka Tai5-gi2 seng-oah8-iaN5 chin3-cheng5, goa2 long2 kam2-kak Tai5-gi2 bo5 siaN2 lou7-iong7. Khi3 liau2-au7 chiah chai-iaN2 Tai5-gi2 e7-sai2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 chiok che7 chiok che7--e5.

Tai5-gi2 e7-sai2 kong2 sia7-hoe7 e5 tai7-chi3, e7-sai2 kong2 hak8-hau7 e5 mih8-kiaN7, koh chai-iaN2 u7 lang5 iong7 Tai5-gi2 cho3 i 1-si3-lang5 e5 chi3-giap8. in hou7 goa2--e5 si7 goa2 ti7 hak8-hau7 iong7 Pak-kiaN-oe7 chhoe7 chiok ku2 long2 chhoe7 bo5 e5 chhut-lou7. Ti7 in hia chhoe7 tioh8 tui3 thou5-te7 e5 koan-sim, tui3 Tai5-oan5 e5 ai3, m7-koh siong7 iau3-kin2--e5, si7 khoaN3 tioh8 in teh phah-piaN3, goa2 chhoe7 tioh8 goa2 ka-ki7, chai-iaN2 goa2 chit-si3- lang5 beh chhong3 siaN2!
