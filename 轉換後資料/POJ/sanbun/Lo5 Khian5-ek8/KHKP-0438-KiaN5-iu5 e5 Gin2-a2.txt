
 
KiaN5-iu5 e5 Gin2-a2

(Lo5 Khian5-ek ek8.)
Kou2-cha2 e5 si5 u7 chit8 e5 soe3-han3 gin2-na2 khi3 ti7 chhiu7-na5-lai7 beh thit-tho5.  I phah-sng3 i kou-toaN chit8-e5 ti7-teh na7-tiaN7, long bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 pe7 e5 bak8-chiu chim-chiok teh khoaN3 i, m7-ku2 i e5 pe7 u7 khoaN3 i.  Gin2-na2 hoaN-hi2 ti7 chhau2-pou chau2-lai5 chau2-khi3, thit-tho5, ban2 hoe; ia7 ti7 toa7 chang5 chhiu7-kha kiaN5-iu5.  Koh in-ui7 kek8 hoaN-hi2, sou2-i2 i chin toa7 siaN the hoah.  Tak8 hang7 khoaN3 liau2 long2 si7 sin,in-ui7 i iau2-be7 bat khoaN3-kiN3, sou2-i2 i long bo5 kiaN.  Teh khoaN3 hoe e5 si5, tui3 hoe-lai7 u7 chit8 be2 tok8 choa5 hut-jian5 so5-chhut-lai5.  Gin2-na2 khoaN3 i e5 phe5 chin sui2, soa3 chhun-chhiu2 beh liah8 i.  Hit-chun7 lau7-pe7 tui3 au7-bin7 kio3 kong2, Gui5-hiam2, gui5-hiam2.  Gin2-na2 khi2-lai5, choa5 chiu7 so5 chau2.  Chong2-si7 gin2-na2 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 pe7, soa3 boe7 ki3-tit i e5 siaN.  Tiap-a2-ku2 sin-piN u7 chit8 chiah iah8-a2 chin sui2 laihioh5 ti7 gin2-na2 e5 hoe, chiah pe khi3.  Gin2-na2 teh chhe7 e5 si5, iah8-a2 na2 pe na2 koaiN5 chiam7-chiam7 khoaN3 tit8-tit8 bo5--khi3.  Hit-chun7 lau7-pe7 chhun-chhiu2 chiu7 ki2 hit chiah iah8-a2 sou2 pe khi3 e5 hong-bin7, chiu7-si7 ti7 hun5-kan, m7-ku2 gin2-na2 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 lau7-pe7 e5 chhiu2.
Ti7 hia u7 chit8 e5 chui2-khut-a2, hit e5 chui2 long2 bo5 tin2-tang7, tiam7-tiam7 koh chin chhim.  Gin2-na2 kui7 ti7 khut-piN teh chhio3,khoaN3 i e5 bin7 ti7 chui2-toe2, phah-sng3 chin sim-sek, soa3 aN3-loh8 oa2 ti7 chui2 teh khoaN3,lau7-pe7 chiu7 koh kong2, Gui5-hiam2.  Gin2-na2 oat8-thau5 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 lau7-pe7 e5 bin7, phah-sng3 kaN2 si7 hong e5 siaN.  Au7-lai5 hong lai5 e5 si5, chui2 teh tin2-tang7, gin2-na2 chiong i e5 kha chhun-loh8-khi3 chhi3-khoaN3 leng2 a2 sio, chiu7 chai chui2 chin leng2.  Hit-chun7 u7 siaN koh khah toa7 siaN kio3 kong2, Gui5-hiam2, gui5-hiam2.  M7-ku2 gin2-na2 choan5 sim teh phah chui2 phin-phin phong5-phong5 sou2-i2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3.
Au7-lai5 khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 khut-te2 u7 mih8 chin sui2 kng--kng-kng, gin2-na2 chin ai3 tit-tioh8, sou2-i2 u7 aN3-loh8 beh bong i, soa3 poah8-loh8 ti7 chhim chui2.  Kho2-sioh khut put-chi2 chhim, gin2-na2 tim5-loh8-khi3, chiu7 toa7 siaN kio3, Kiu3 goa2, kiu3 goa2.  In lau7-pe7 ti7 hia sui5-si5 chhun i tng5-tng5 e5 chhiu2 khiu2 i khi2-lai5 ti7 an-un2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Gin2-na2 in-ui7 koaN5 koh kiaN, seng-khu ngiauh8-ngiauh8-chhoah.  Hit-chun7 lau7-pe7 eng7 i e5 chhiu2 tah i e5 sim kong2, Goa2 siong5-siong5 chiau3-kou3 li2, li2 long2 m7 chai; goa2 u7 chhut ki3-ho7 hou7 li2; m7-ku2 li2 long2 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3; goa2 u7 ka7 li2 kong2, m7-ku2 li2 long2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3; li2 na7 put-si5 siau3-liam7 goa2, chiu7 chaigoa2 kap li2 saN-kap ti7-teh.
Hit-chun7 gin2-na2 thiaN lau7-pe7 e5 oe7,chhun chhiu2 lai5 lam2 i e5 seng-khu, hou7 ie5 lau7-pe7 pho7.  Chiu7 kong2, Pe7 ah, chhiaN2 kap goa2 eng2-eng2 saN-kap ti7-teh, goa2 e5 bak8-chiu put-si5 him-bou7 thang khoaN3 li2 e5 ki3-ho7, iah8 goa2 e5 hi7-khang put-si5 him-bou7 thang thiaN li2 e5 siaN.
