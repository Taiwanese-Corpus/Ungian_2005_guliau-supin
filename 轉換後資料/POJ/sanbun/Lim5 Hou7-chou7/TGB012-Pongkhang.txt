Pong7-khang kap hong5-khong-ho5
Lim5 Hou7-chou7

Goan2 chhu3 au7-piah-kau u7 2-khang hong5-khong-ho5, che si7 goan2 a-chou ti7 Jit8-pun2 si5-tai7 ou2--e5.

Hong5-khong-ho5 e5 chok-iong7 tak8-e5 long2 chai-aN2, che si7 Jit8-pun2 cheng3-hu2 thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5 boe2-ki5, Bi2-kok hui-nng7-ki lai5 sau3-sia7 Tai5-oan5, Tai5-oan5-lang5 ui7-tioh chau2 khong-sip8 chiah san2-seng--e5. Hong5-khong-ho5 chit-e5 mih8-kiaN7 ti7 Tai5-oan5 choan5-to2 tak8 sou2-chai7 it-teng7 long2 u7.

M7-koh Tai5-oan5-lang5 hit-chiong2 "si5 kau3 si5 tam-tng, bo5 bi2 chu2 han-chi5-thng" e5 seng3-keh, ka7 Tai5-oan5 e5 hong5-khong-ho5 m7 si7 thun7--khi2-lai5, bo5, to7 si7 ka7 am--khi2-lai5. Sui-bong2 hong5-khong-ho5 si7 1-chiong2 jin5-cho7 kang- teng5, m7-koh kian-kou3 koh sit8-iong7, ti7 hit-e5 si5-tai7, hong5-khong-ho5 siong7 toa7 e5 kong-leng5, to7 si7 kiu3-tioh8 goan2 a-chou e5 mia7.

Ah na7 pong7-khang to7 bo5-kang5. Pong7-khang 2-khang si7 sio-thong--e5, siong7 kai3 u7-mia5 e5 1-tiau5-koa si7: "Che7 hoe2-chhia koe3 pong7-khang, sim-cheng5 chin khin-sang……"

Pong7-khang koh si7 se3-han3 sng2 bih-sio-chhoe7 e5 ho2-sou2-chai7. Goan2 chhu3 thau5-cheng5 hit-liap8 soaN to7 u7 1-e5 pong7-khang, goa2 se3-han3 to7 long2 ham7 goan2 chhu3-piN e5 gin2-a2 ti hia bih-sio-chhoe7, sng2 kau3 jit8-thau5 beh loh8-soaN chiah u7 tng2-khi3 chiah8-png7.
