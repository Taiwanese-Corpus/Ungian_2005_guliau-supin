Chu7-hun5-chia2
Pasuya

1989-ni5, TeN7 Lam5-iong5 chu7-hun5.

1998-ni5 4-geh8 chhe7-7, Tai5-pak 228-ki3-liam7-koan2 ka7 chu7-hun5-chia2 e5 tang5-siong3 chhai--khi3-lai5.

Hoan2-tui3-tong2 e5 chhi7-tiuN2, gi7-goan5, lip8-hoat-ui2-goan5 teh o-lo2 chu7-hun5-chia2, kong2 i e5"hi-seng", tai3-tong7 Tai5-oan5 bin5-chu2 e5 tiong7-tai7 chin3-tian2, in koh kong2"Kin-a2-jit8 lan2 chong2-sng3 u7 ki-hoe7 i2 1-e5 cheng3-hu2 e5 kak-sek lai5 kam2-liam7 Nai-long tui3 chit-te3 thou5-toe7 e5 kong3-hian3.....

Hoan2-the2-che3 e5 chu7-hun5-chia2 , 9-ni5 au7 , hou7 ke3-khi3 tau3-tin5 e5"tong5-chi3"iong7 the2-che3 e5 lip8-tiuN5 lai5 kheng2-teng7?

9-ni5 e5 si5-kan, goan5-pun2 teh tui3-khong3 thong2-ti7-chia2 e5 hoan2- tui3-tong2, gia5"toa7 ho5-kai2","kiong5-pun2 se-chin3"e5 toa7-ki5 beh in2-leng2 Tai5-oan5「khoa3-se3-ki2」, soah ti7 ka-ki7 tong2-lai7 chhou-soan2 to7 siak kah hi-le2-le2.

Chu-jim7-ui7 si7"chheng-liu5"e5 ke2- sian-ti, it-tit8 siuN7 beh leng7-goa7 chhai 1-liap soaN-thau5, m7-koh, ti7 "cheng2-hap8"e5 khau2-ho7 ha7, iu5-goan5 kui kang teh hou7-siong khan-thoa, lek8-su2 e5 kha-liah cha2 to7 hong5 pang3-boe7-ki3--e5-a.

Ti7 Tai5-pak koe-a2-lou7 teng2, sui-jian5"chin3-lip8 the2-che3 hoan2- the2-che3"e5 kong-chit lu2 lai5 lu2 choe7, m7-koh, chu7-hun5-chia2 9-ni5 cheng5 toa-siaN hou-hoah e5 chu-tiuN──"100% e5 gian5-lun7 chu7-iu5", iu5-goan5 si7 khong3-cheng e5 sou3-kiu5.

KhoaN3-hi3 e5 kun5-chiong3 oan3-than3 kong2:"Chu7-hun5-chia2 e5 hi-seng bo5 oaN7-lai5 kak-chhiN2", m7-koh, "oan3-than3" 1-tiam2-a2 to bo5-hoat-tou7 kai2- pian3 lou5-le7 e5 un7-mia7.

Li2-siong2 e5 se3-kai3 na7-chhiuN7 li5 lu2 lai5 lu2 hng5?

M7-koh na7 kau3 u7 1-kang, lan2 hiong5-hiong5 hoat-hian7, chu7-hun5-chia2 e5 soan2- tek m7-si7 hi-seng, lan2 to7 e7 kam2- siu7-tioh8 -chiong2 siN3-mia7 e5 tui-kiu5. He si7 1-chiong2 siN3-mia7 e5 chun-giam5, 1-chiong2 siN3-mia7 e5 bi2-kam2, m7-si7 hi-seng!

"Goa2 m7-goan7 khut ti7 ti-tiau5 lai7-toe2, cho3-hoe2 chiah8-phun!"

1989-ni5 5-geh8 19, Chiam Ek-hoa5 ti7 chong2-tok-hu2 kui2-lo7-ban7 kun5-chiong2 bin7-thau5-cheng5, in2-he2 chu7-hun5!
