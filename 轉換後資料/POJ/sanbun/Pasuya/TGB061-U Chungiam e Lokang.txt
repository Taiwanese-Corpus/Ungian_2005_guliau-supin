U7 Chun-giam5 e5 Lo5-kang
Pasuya

  2002 ni5 Tai5-ni2 kang-hoe7 kou3-bun7 kang-hoe7 kou3-bun7 Kian3-chi3 hiann jiat8-sim khan-soann3, TGB khai-si2 kap Chhiu7-na5-a2 Soann-a2-kha e5 Tai5-ni2 kang-hoe7 chih-chiap, Tai5-ni2 si7 “ni2-cheng2-chhiunn2”, si7 Lam5-a chip8-thoan5 lai7-te2 e5 su7-giap8 tan-ui7. 
  TGB 2002 ni5 chhim2 khi3 Tai5-ni2 kang-hoe7 e5 si5, in e5 hoe7-oan5 u7 400 goa7 e5, kau3 chit-ma2 chhun 200 thong2 e5 nia5, tang goa7 lai5, kong-si it-tit8 sok-pian, teng7-toann ma7 ju2 chiap ju2 chio2, chin che7 giap8-bu7soahsoa2-khi3 China, khoann3--lai5 koainn-chhiunn2 si7 kin2-cha2-ban7 nia5; m7-koh Tai5-ni2 kang-hoe7 kan3-pou7 chin phainn2 thih-thau5, i3-sek kiong5 koh thoan5-kiat, kong-si m7-kann2 ou-peh8 bu2, kau3-tann ma7 iau2 it-tit8 an-tah kong2 u7『永續經營』e5 phah-sng3. 
  Teng2 goeh8-jit8 khi3 kang-hoe7 e5 si5, A-peng5 hiann ka7 goan2 kong2 kang-hoe7 e5 chhu2-keng2 kap lip8-tiunn5, sui-bong2 kong-si chit8-pou7 chit8-pou7 teh tong7-chok, m7-koh kang-hoe7 iau2 bo5 beh chhut-chhiu2, hian7-chhu2-si5 khah iau3-kin2--e5, si7 i5-hou7 hoe7-oan5 hiann-ti7 e5 kang-chok po2-chiong, chhiunn2--nih jin7-ho5 jin5-su7 tiau3-tong7, na7 bo5 chun-tiong7 tong-su7-jin5 e5 i3-goan7, koh bo5 chun-siu2 kap kang-hoe7 e5 iok-sok, hou7 tiau3-chit-chia2 khah u7 po2-chiong e5 lo5-tong7 tiau5-kiann7, kang-hoe7 it-teng7 e7 chhut-bin7 chhap-chhiu2. 
  Chit goeh8-jit8 koh khi3 e5 si5, kang-hoe7 ai3 bin7-tui3--e5, soah ui3 “po2-chiong kang-chok-koan5” pian3-cho3 ai3 chun2-pi7 “cheng-chhu2 seng-chun5-koan5”--a. Chhiunn2--nih tng5-ku2 lai5 it-tit8 chhai5-oan5, giap8-bu7-liong7 koh it-tit8 sok--khi3, che long2 u7 kho2-leng5 si7 koainn-chhiunn2 e5 chun2-pi7. Chit-ma2 koh pang3 hong-siann beh sit8-heng5 “seng-san2 chek-jin7-che3”. 
  “Seng-san2 chek-jin7-che3” na7 cheng3-keng cho3--loh8, te7-it, lo5-kang sit8-che3 sin-sui2 pian3-chio2, chun2 ka-pan koann2 khang-khoe3 thang tat8-kau3 i7-teng7 kang-chok-liong7, ma7 bo5 ka-pan-hui3, che si7 pak-siah e5 chhiu2-lou7; te7-ji7, peng5-kun kang-chu ki-chun2 e7 kang3-ke7, bi7-lai5 the3-hiu-kim iah si7 chu-khain2-hui3 e5 kim-giah8 e7 kiu-chui2, kong-si e7-tang3 seng2 put-chi2-a2 toa7-pit e5 seng5-pun2, chun2 beh koainn-chhiunn2, ma7 e7-tang3 kiam3 lap8 chiann5 che7 hui3-iong7; te7-sann, bo5-lun7 si7 keng2-khi3 eng2-hiong2 iah si7 kong-si bo5-sim beh ke3-siok8 keng-eng5, liau2--e5 soah soa2-lai5 kio3 sin-lo5 hu7-tam, m7-koh lo5-kang bo5 siong-tui3 chham-u2 koan2-li2, keng-eng5 kap liau2-kai2 kong-si sit8-che3 keng-che3 ham7 chu-kim un7-iong7 chong7-hong2 e5 koan5-lek8. 
  Tai5-ni2 kang-hoe7 kan3-pou7 u7 kiong7-sek, hoan2-tui3 “seng-san2 chek-jin7-che3” chit-tiau5 te2-soann3 ai3 chiu2 hou7 tiau5, ma7 ai3 pi7-bian2 hoe7-oan5 hiann-ti7 hou7 kong-si pian2--khi3. Chit hang7 koan2-li2 che3-tou7 na7 bo5 keng-koe3 kang-hoe7 tong5-i3 to7 nge7 kiann5, sng3 si7 bo5 oan5-kang tong5-i3 to7 kai2-pian3 lo5-tong7 tiau5-kiann7 ham7 lo5-tong7 khe3-iok, kang-hoe7 be7 tiam7-tiam7 ku3-chai7 kong-si an2-ne bu2. Che si7 kui e5 Lam5-a ham7 Tai5-sok chip8-thoan5 sim7-chi3 si7 Tai5-oan5 lo5-kang lo5-tong7 tiau5-kiann7 e5 tau3-cheng, chiu2 u7 tiau5, tui3 kang-un7 e7 u7 kou2-bu2 e5 i3-gi7. 
  Lam5-a iah si7 Tai5-sok, na2 iong7 keng-eng5 khun3-lan5 cho3 chioh-khau2, tauh8-tauh8-a2 teh chhai5-oan5, koh na2 ke3-siok8 teh sin-chheng2 in2-chin3 goa7-lo5, chhiunn7 Be7-liau5 e5 lak8-khin koh beh chhiann3 ban thong2 e5 goa7-lo5, che piau2-si7 seng-san2-soann3 iau2 u7 khiam3 kang-lang5 , an2-ne si7 an2-choann2 koh beh chhai5 Tai5-oan5-kang? Kong2 keng-eng5 khun3-lan5 beh sok-sio2 kui-bou5, nah koh teh the5 China tau5-chu ke3-oe7? Kong2 chu-kim chiann5 an5 keng-eng5 chiah8-lat8, soah u7 chai5-tiau7 it-tit8 koainn Tai5-oan5-chhiunn2, soa2 chu-kim khi3 China tau5-chu siat-chhiunn2? 
  Chong2--si7 Tai5-oan5 lo5-tong7-chia2 e5 kang-chok-koan5 kap seng-chun5-koan5, m7-si7 chai5-thoan5 teh kou3-lu7--e5, pak-siah liau2 to7 bo5 ke3-tat8--a, tak8-tong2 e5 cheng3-kheh ham7 koann-liau5, ma7 kan-tann beh tih8 soan2-phio3 e5 si5, chiah e7 ka7 po2-chiong lo5-kang e5 cheng3-ti7 chi-phio3 sa chhut-lai5 lang7, choe3-au7 beh kau-oann7, hun-chong li7-ek e5 si5, hi-seng--tioh8-e5, iu5-goan5 si7 chiam3 siong7 che7 soan2-phio3 ma7 lap8 siong7 che7 soe3-kim e5 lo5-tong7 kai-kip. 
  A-peng5 hiann it-tit8 kiong5-tiau, Tai5-oan5 kang-lang5 ai3 thoan5-kiat, ai3 it-ti3, chiah u7 oah8--loh8-khi3 e5 ki-hoe7, m7-thang hou7 chai5-thoan5, cheng3-kheh kap koann-liau5 phian3 koh-chai3 phian3, it-teng7 ai3 kian-chhi5 lo5-tong7-chia2 e5 lip8-tiunn5, cheng-chhu2 lo5-tong7-chia2 e5 koan5-ek; chun2 kiann5 kah bo5-lou7--a, beh si2, ma7 tioh8 piann3 chit8-tu3, mai3 chhai7--leh tan2-si2, ai3 cho3 u7 chun-giam5 e5 lo5-kang. 
  (TGB Thong-sin3 te7 61 ki5, 2004 ni5 10 goeh8)
