  
SIO2 Kou3-SU7 TOA7 TOE5-BAK8
Tiong-gak8-seng,
Bi2-kok lang5, Jit8-pun2 lang5, kau3-hoe7 lai7, kau3-hoe7 goa7 teh hiap8-lek8 e5 Tai5-lam5 Phe5-hu chin2-sou2.
Tai5-lam5 goan5-pun2 e5 thoan5-jiam2 piN7-iN7 kio3-choe3 Thou5-siaN5-a2, ui7-ti3 si7 ti7 sai-peng5 chhi7-goa7, chiu-ui5 e5 khoan5-keng2 chin bai2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Chong2-si7 chu7 ti7 8 ni5 cheng5, u7 Bi2-kok e5 hou7-su7 Teng7 Kou-niu5 lai5 khai-pan7 Nai7-piN7 e5 chin2-sou2 liau2-au7, soa3 chiaN5-choe3 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 e5 sou2-chai7, Thau5 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 Teng7 Kou-niu5 pun2-sin koat-jian5 kap ti7 chin2-sou2 teh hok8-bu7 e5 pun2-seng2 lang5 Lim5 Teng5-hui Sian-siN kiat-hun, ti3-kau3 m7-na7 choan5 seng2, choan5 kok chin hong-tong7, koh ia7 chhut-mia5 kau3 Bi2-kok.  In-ui7 Teng7 Kou-niu5 ui7-tioh8 pun2-sin sou2 chi3-hiong3 e5 hi-seng su7-giap8, u7 piau2-hian7 hian3-sin tau3-te2 e5 cheng-sin5.
TaN chit e5 Phe5-hu chin2-sou2 liau2-au7 u7 tit-tioh8 Jit8-pun2 e5 i-seng Se-chhoan Phok-su7 lai5 chu2-chhi5 i-ti7 e5 kang, koh cheng5 u7 tit-tioh8 Jit8-pun2 choan5-kok Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 Seng3-tan3 hian3-kim lai5 tau3 eng3-oan7, chit pang ia7 u7 Jit8-pun2 kap Tai5-oan5 hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 kap chit8 pou7-hun7 hong7-hian3, thang tau3 chhiong-sit8 chin2-sou2 e5 kian3-tiok kap siat-pi7.
Kau3 kin-ni5 chin2-sou2 u7 sin-tiok chit8 cho7 oan5-kang siok-sia3, kau3 8 geh8 2 jit8 lok8-seng5 e5 si5, Tai5-lam5 chhi7 Oe7-seng-kiok8-tiuN2 Lau5 Lian5-ek Phok-su7 sou2 ti3-su5 e5 kui2 hang7, keng3 koh-khah u7 i3-gi7, hou7 lang5 tit-tioh8 chhim-khek e5 in3-siong7.  I kong2, Chit e5 Nai7-piN7 e5 phe5-hu chin2-sou2, u7 beng5-beng5 piau2-hian7 Ia5-sou thiaN3 e5 cheng-sin5, chit khoan2 e5 piau2-hian7, pi2 cheng3-hu2 sou2 choe3 e5 bo5 khah su.  TaN chiong5-cheng5 u7 Bi2-kok e5 Teng7 Lu2-su7, u7 Jit8-pun2 e5 Se-chhoan Phok-su7 lai5 pang-chan7.  Tan7-si7 chit chhu3 siok-sia3 e5 kian3-tiok u7 piau2-hian7 tioh8 pun2-seng2 lang5 hiap8-lek8 kap hong7-hian3 e5 cheng-sin5, in-ui7 pun2-seng2 lang5 sou2 ki3-hu3 e5 chiN5 chiam3 toa7 pou7-hun7, ji5-chhiaN2 m7-si7 kau3-hoe7 e5 sin3-tou5 na7-tiaN7, chiu7-si7 kau3-hoe7 i2-goa7 e5 pun2-seng2 tong5-pau ia7 chhut chin-choe7 chiN5 ti7 lai7-bin7, u7 piau2-hian7 tioh8 lan2 e5 Nai7-piN7 kiu3-ti7 su7-giap8 u7 tit-tioh8 lan2 ka-ki7 e5 tong5-pau e5 hiap8-lek8 kap chi-chhi5.  Koh chit8 hang7 ti7 chia sou2 choe3 chin u7 i3-gi7 e5, chiu7-si7 siu7 i-ti7 e5 lang5, u7 lang5  
kha-chhiu2 oe7 khah bo5 tau3-tah, chiah e5 lang5 m7-na7 ka7 in cheng2-heng5 chhiu2-sut8 hou7 in piN7-cheng3 tit-tioh8 ho2, chiu7-si7 koh hou7 in oh8 ki7-ge7, chiong-lai5 ti7 sia7-hoe7 thang kap lang5 seng-chun5 keng7-cheng.
▲Si2 liau2 10 ni5 iau2 chin chio2 lang5 chai e5 ho2 su7.
Kou3 Si Sek-ke Bok8-su ke3-sin liau2 i2-keng 10 ni5 khah ke.  I seng-chian5 in-ui7 choe3 lang5 tiong-tit8 ngi7-khi3, sou2-i2 chiap8-chiap8 siu7 gou7-kai2 a2-si7 hoat-seng mou5-chhat.  Chong2-si7 kau3 choe3-kun7 chiah thiaN-tioh8 i e5 kau3-hoe7 e5 tng-chit-chia2 kong2 i ti7 kong-hok8 au7 ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 keng-che3 khun3-lan5, khi3 kiam-chit ti7 kong-lip8 hak8-hau7 e5 si5, hun-bun5 to bo5 tui3 kau3-hoe7 nia2 sin-sui2 a2-si7 tin-thiap.  Hit si5 kau3-hoe7 in-ui7 i u7 choe3 kang bo5 nia2 kang-chiN5, m7 ke3-sim, choe7-choe7 pai2 beh theh8 tin-thiap a2-si7 sin-hong7 hou7 i, i long2 si5-the3, kio3 in siu-sip8 chek-chu7 khi2-lai5, kau3 u7 khui3-lat8 e5 si5, thang ho2 pheng3 choan-jim7 e5 bok8-su, in-ui7 i kong2 tek-khak bo5 beh nia2 siang-thau5 e5 sin-hong7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni, po2-chhi5 liam5-kiat tau3-te2, sou2-isiu2-jip8 si7 chin bi5-pok8, chong2-si7 tong-si5 ti7 hak8-hau7 sou2 ka3 e5 hak8-seng u7 nng7 e5 chi2-be7-a2, keng-che3 khun3-lan5 oh-tit ke3-siok8 hak8-giap8.  I thiaN-tioh8 an2-ni, bo5 kou3 tioh8 pun2-sin siu-jip8 bi5-pok8, iau2-ku2 to-to siau2-siau2 siong5-siong5 am3-chiN7 pang-chan7, hou7 in ke3-siok8 hak8-giap8.  Hian7-si5 chit nng7 e5 chi2-be7 chit8 e5 ti7 Bi2-kok, chit8 e5 choe3 bok8-su-niu5.  Tan7-si7 chai-iaN2 chit e5 su7 e5 lang5 iau2-ku2 hui-siong5 chio2.
▲Kau3-hoe7 e5 hou7-teng7 chhiuN5-ui5 kiam2 thang cho7 hou7 koan5-koan5?
Hian7-si5 thoan5-kau3 e5 hong-sek chiaN3 tit8-tit8 teh kou2-bu2 ai3 hou7 kau3-hoe7 e5 mng5-hou7 toa7 khui, hou7 kau3-hoe7 chin3-chhut sia7-hoe7, hou7 sia7-hoe7 iong5-iN7 chin3-jip8 kau3-hoe7.  Chong2-si7 ti7 Ko-hiong5-koan7 Bi5-to5 chit keng sin siat be7 moa2 10 ni5 e5 kau3-hoe7, sou2 chhai2-chhu2 e5 hong-sek siong5-siong5 chhin-chhiuN7 to3-hoan2 e5 khoan2-sit.  In-ui7 in chng-thau5 e5 lang5 na7u sim2-mih8 ho2 su7, phaiN2 su7, beh lai5 chioh khi3-ku7, i2 toh, teng2-teng2, long2 m7-kheng2 tah-eng3, chhin-chhiuN7 mng5-hou7 na2 siat na2 koan5, hou7 sia7-hoe7 oh-tit lai5 chhin-kun7, in-uiin7 sou2 chu2-tiuN e5, chiu7-si7 kong2, kiaN-liau2 chiah e5 ke-si chap8-but8 chioh chhut hou7 lang5 li7-pian7 khi3 choe3 che3-su7 pai3 ngou2-siong7 e5 lou7-eng7, an2-ni bo5-hah8.  Chong2-si7 kau3-hoe7  
ti7 ke3-khi3 chiaN5 chap8 tang ku2 hian2-jian5 u7 toa7 chin3-pou7, in-ui7 in u7 chit8 hang7 ho2 e5 hong-sek, chiu7-si7 ti7 in e5 toe7-thau5, na7 u7 lang5 ko2-jian5 tu2-tioh8 jiok8-the2 chiuN7, but8-chit chiuN7, i2-kip8 leng5-seng3 chiuN7 e5 khun3-lan5 e5 si5, kau3-hoe7 chiu7 thoan5-kiat it-ti3 choan5-the2 chhut-tong7, chip8-tiong khi3 in2-chhoa7, khi3 pang-chan7; sou2-i2 khai-siat kau3-hoe7 kau3 taN iau2-be7 10 ni5, kau3-se3 i2-keng chin oah8-tang7 koh seng7-hong2, mui2 Chu2-jit8 e5 chu7-hoe7 100 goa7 lang5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 947 ho7, 1964 ni5 12 geh8)
