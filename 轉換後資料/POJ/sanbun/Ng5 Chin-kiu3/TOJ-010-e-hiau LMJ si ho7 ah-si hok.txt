E7-hiau2 Lo5-ma2-ji7 (LMJ, Tai5-oan5-ji7) 
Si7 Ho7 A2-si7 Hok?
Ng5 Chin-kiu5,
          Ottawa, Canada
Kau2-ni5 it-koan3 e5 bo2-gi2 kau3-iok8 kin-ni5 9 goeh8 khai-si2 sit8-si. Te7-hng che7-che7 sou2-chai7 khai-si2 teh chun2-pi7 kau3-oan5, kau3-chai5 kap gian2-kiu3 choe3 iu2-hau7 e5 bo2-gi2 kau3-hak8 hong-hoat. Chin bo5-eng5. Kau3-iok8 pou7-tiuN2 Chan Chi3-long2 iau2 teh i3-ai3 Tiong-kok hit-tho3 Han3-gi2 pheng-im-hoat. Goa2 bat cheng7-cheng7 ka7 siuN7, tau3-te2 e7-hiau2 LMJ si7 ho7 a2-si7 hok?
Si7 “hok!”. Tui3 8 hoe3 iau2 boe7 jip8 Jit8-jin5 Kong-hak8-hau7 e5 si5, goan2 lau7-bu2 ti7 chhu3--nih8 khai-si2 ka3 goa2 thak8 LMJ. I si7 18 hoe3 ke3 hou7 chit8-e5 choh-sit-lang5. ThiaN kong2 hit-si5-chun7 goan2 lau7-pe7 iah8-si7 ti7 Sin-kang2 Ki-tok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 teh chhut-jip8. Goan2 lau7-bu2 kap goan2 lau7-pe7 long2 ti7 chu2-jit8-oh8 cho3 lau7-su. Tui3 se3-han3 i chiu7 ka3 goan2 2 hiaN-ti7 3 chi2-moai7 thak8 kap sia2 LMJ. I e5 ka3-hoat chin bo5 kho-hak8-tek, si7 chin kan2-tan. I tui3 Tai5-lam5 chheh-pang5 be2 chit8-tiuN(m7-si7 chheh) LMJ e5 ji7-bo2. Hit lai7-bin7 u7 long2-chong2 e5 kho3-theng5 kap LMJ sia2 e5 kou3-su7. B,Ch,Chh,G,H,J,K,Kh,L,M,N,Ng, P,Ph,S,T, Th,Ts; a, e, i, o, u, ou, m, ng, phiN7-im “n” kap Ke-a2 koa, “Chap8-liap8 ke-nng7 he7 bi2-tau2, chit8-liap8 kong3-phoa3 iau2 chhun 9...teng2”. Tui3 thau5 kau3 boe2 kap i cho3-hoe2 liam7 kui2-pai2 chiu7 ka7 ki3--khi2--lai5. Bo5 bun7-te5, e7-hiau2 liam7 chheh-koa. Au7--lai5, i thiau3 chit8-ji7, 2 ji7, 3 ji7, 4 ji7, goa2 ma7 e7-hiau2 thak8. Chong2-si7 i chai-iaN2 tioh8 tui3 thau5 thak8(liam7) chiah thak8 e7 chhut--lai5, an2-ne goa2 si7 teh liam7 chheh-koa, bo5 jin7-ji7. Sou2-i2 i chiu7 eng7 chit8-tiuN choa2 u7 hiah toa7-tiuN, e7-tang3 kham3 cheng5-au7 e5 ji7, tiong-ng ka chit8-khang kio3 goa2 thak8 choa2-phang7 lai7 hit-ji7. Chiah8-lat8! Goa2 be7-thang tui3 thau5 liam7 lai5 ioh ji7, goa2 iah8 be7 jin7-ji7, iN-iN ouhN8-ouhN8, na2-chun2 e2-kau2 hou7 phang teng3--tioh8, thak8(ioh) kah put-tap8 put-chhit. I chek-hoat8 goa2 long2 bo5 jin7-ji7, an2-ne be7 sai2--tit, ai3 heng5-hoat8. I e5 heng5-hoat8 chiu7-si7 liam3(ka2) bak8-chiu phoe5. Khi3 hou7 i liam3 kui2-na7-pai2 chiok m7 kam-goan7. Goa2 toa7-han3 e5 si5, soa3 tioh8 koa3 bak8-kiaN3. Mui2-pai2 teh chhit bak8-kiaN3 e5 si5, m7 tam su-iaN5, tioh8 ka7 i khong3-gi7 kong2, to si7 se3-han3 e5 si5, hou7 lau7-bu2 liam3 bak8-chiu-phoe5 liam3 kah soa3 tioh8 koa3 bak8-kiaN3--lah. Kong thiong3 Ma2 seN sun.
	Ti7 chu2-jit8-oh8 lau7-su mui2 le2-pai3 ma7 u7 teh ka3 LMJ. U7 iaN2 ti7 chhu3--nih8 goan2 lau7-bu2 ka7 goa2 ok-pou2, ti7 chu2-jit8-oh8 ke chin khuiN3-oah8. M7-bian2 chhin-chhiuN7 i2-goa7 hiah-e5 gin2-a2, hou7 lau7-su ka3 kah khi3-sin lou2-mia7. Bo5 tiuN-ti5 goa2 soa3 e7-hiau2 thak8 bo2-gi2 LMJ. Goa2 e7-hiau2 thak8 hit-tiuN ji7-bo2, e7 jin7-ji7, long2 bo5 chho3-ngou7. Thak8 kou3-su7, seng3-si, seng3-keng. Jit8-pun2 chheh iau2 be7-hiau2 thak8 goa2 chiu7 e7-hiau2 thak8 LMJ, tong-jian5 hit-si5-chun7 iau2 boe7 oh8 Hoa5-gi2. Goa2 e7-hiau2 khoaN3 “thian-lou7 lek8-theng5”, tong-jian5 i3-su3 m7-chai poaN3-hang7, kou3-su7 e5 chheh teng2-teng2. Kam2-kak chiaN5 hiau5-pai. Ti7 hoe2-chhia lai7 goa2 bat teh thak8 seng3-keng. Hit-chun7 e5 seng3-keng choa2 kau7 chin toa7-pun2. Goa2 khah sio2-liap8-chi2, tioh8 ai3 pheN7-pheN7-chhoan2 mouh teh thak8. Ti7 piN..a2 teh khoaN3 e5 tiong-hak8-seng toa3 au7-bin7 chhi7-bu2 chhi7-chhuh kong2, “Heh! Hit-e5 kau5-si2-gin2-a2 m7 chiaN5-kau5, hiah gau5 thak8 Eng-gi2 e5 chheh.” Goa2 ti7 sim-lai7 in3 i kong2, goa2 m7-si7 teh thak8 Eng-gi2, si7 teh thak8 lin2 teh kong2 e5 Tai5-oan5-ji7. Sia2 LMJ si7 khi3 chhou-tiong oh8 Eng-gi2 e5 si5, khai-si2 eng7 pit sia2 LMJ. Hit-e5 i2-cheng5 sui-jian5 iah8 bat oh8 sia2, chong2-si7 na2 teh in3-soat pai5-pan2 chit8-ji7 un5-un5-a2 khek.
	Si7 “ho7?” In-ui7 goan2 lau7-bu2 bo5 siu7-koe3 the2-che3 lai7 cheng3-sek e5 Jit8-bun5 a2-si7 Hoa5-gi2 kau3-iok8. Ti7 chhan5-chng cha-bou2 gin2-a2 bo5 siu7 kau3-iok8 e5 ki-hoe7. Cho3-kang u7 hun7, thak8-chheh bo5 hun7. Sou2-i2 i long2 be7-hiau2 Jit8-gi2 a2-si7 Hoa5-gi2. I chi2-u7 e7-hiau2 in pe7-bo2 ka3..i e5 pe7-bo2-oe7(Tai5-oan5-oe7). Koh tui3 chu2-jit8-oh8 oh8 thak8, sia2 (in3-pan2-sek) LMJ. I chai i e5 kiaN2 na7 toa7-han3, i5-it e5 kau-thong hong-hoat chiu7-si7 eng7 LMJ sia2-phoe thong-sin3. Tong-jian5 hit-chun7 iau2 bo5 chhiu2-ki tian7-oe7 e5 hong-pian7. Tioh8 si7 an2-ne i chin iau3-kin2 chiong goan2 5 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moai7 tui3 se3-han3 chiu7 ka7 goan2 ka3 LMJ. Goan2 lau7-pe7 thak8 koe3 Jit8-jin5 e5 kong-hak8-hau7, Jit8-gi2 e7-thong. Chong2-si7 au7-lai5 li7-khui choh-sit khi3 Toa7-pou-na5(Toa7-na5) cho3 seng-li2 e5 si5, chu3-bun5 hoe3, sia2 soeh-sia7 e5 phoe long2 tioh8 oa2-kho3 toa7-ku7(Sou Thian-sang5, teh khui Sin3-to7 Ioh8-pang5) the3 i sia2 Han3-ji7 kap Jit8-gi2 e5 phoe. In-ui7 Tai5-oan5 LMJ m7-si7 thong-iong7 e5 gian5-gu2.
	Goan2 i5-tiuN7(Chhoa3 Chioh8-lin5) chai7-seN e5 si5 e7-hiau2 phah kun-thau5, chiap-kut koh u7 oh8 chhau2-ioh8 e5 han7-te7-i. I siau3-lian5 e5 si5, na7 m7-si7 ti7 mng5-khau2 tiaN5 teh ka3 lang5 phah kun-thau5, chiu7-si7 khi3 Sin-kang2 chhi7-tiuN5 teh tok ioh8-thau5-a2. I e5 ioh8-chheh long2 kho3 am3-ki3, iong7 koan-chhat kap sit8-che3 chhai2-chip8 chhau2-ioh8 teh oh8. In-ui7 i be7-hiau2 jim7-ho5 e5 goa7-lai5-gi2 iah8 be7-hiau2 Tai5-oan5-ji7(LMJ). Ho2-ka-chai3, i u7 chit8 e5 te7 ji7 kiaN2(Chu2-tin) eng7 LMJ chiong sou2-u7 e5 chhau2-ioh8 chit8-pau chit8-pau long2 ka7 sia2 cho3 LMJ lai5 hun-beng5. Ioh8-chheh iah8 eng7 LMJ sia2--khi2--lai5. I koe3-sin liau2-au7 Chu2-tin chiu7 cho3 chhau2-ioh8-sian. Chiah m7-bian2 sit-thoan5 po2-kui3 e5 ioh8-hng. “Kang-ou5 chit8-tiam2 koat, chhe-chu2 put8-kho2 soat.” Goa2 put-si5 teh kou2-le7 i chiong in lau7-pe7 e5 ioh8-hng hoat-piau2 chhut-lai5 “cho7-hok jin5-kun5”. Ti7 tiong-hak8 si5-tai7, ni5-khin khah u7 oe7 kong2, goa2 sia2 siong7-che7 LMJ e5 phoe. Hit-si5 u7 kui2-na7 e5 Bok8-su TiuN2-lo2 e5 chu2-te7 long2-si7 chin ho2 e5 peng5-iu2 iah8 e7-hiau2 sia2, thak8 LMJ. Goa2 kam2-kak eng7 LMJ sia2-phoe koh sia2 jit8-ki3 chin u7 i3-su3. Ma2-kai Bok8-su lau5--loh8--lai5 e5 jit8-ki3 chiu7-si7 hit e5 le7. Hiah e5 phoe kap jit8-ki3 chin kho2-sioh chhut-kok liau2-au7 m7-chai i tui3 to2-ui7 khi3--lah. Phah-sng3 hou7 lang5 be7-khi3 pau-tang pau-sai.
Tai7-hak8 chhut-giap8 au7, goa2 khi3 chit8-ni5 e5 U7-pi7 Kun-koaN Kun-hun3 (te7 3 ki5). Goan2 si7 khi3 Hong7-soaN Ng5-pou Liok8-kun Kun-hau7 siu7-hun3. U7 chit8-am3 siau-teng liau2-au7 chi2-to7-oan5 kio3 goa2 khi3, chhiu2 theh8 chit8-tiuN phoe mng7 goa2 khoaN3 hit lai7-bin7 e5 bit8-ma2 si7 teh sia2 siaN2 mih8? Goa2 ka7 chiap koe3-lai5 khoaN3, chiah chai si7 goan2 lau7-bu2 sia2--lai5 e5 phoe. Goa2 chiu7 chhut-siaN thak8 hou7 i thiaN, in-ui7 he si7 eng7 LMJ sia2 e5 Tai5-oan5 oe7. I tong-jian5 put-tong2 Tai5-oan5 oe7. Chi2-u7 ka7 goa2 phaiN2 kong2, thai2 bo5-ai3 sia2 Tiong-bun5 a2-si7 Jit8-bun5 e5 phoe? Goa2 ka7 in3 kong2, chin sit-le2 goan2 lau7-bu2 cho3 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 e5 si5 bo5 ki-hoe7 khi3 hak8-hau7 oh8 Jit8-gi2 a2-si7 Hoa5-gi2. I chi2-u7 e7-hiau2 kong2, thak8 kap sia2 pe7-bo2 oe7. Chit-chiong2 e5 “phi-lo5 hong-cha3” liau2-au7 kio3 goa2 tng2 khi3 khun3. U7 chit8-jit8 i koh tng7-tioh8 goa2, chit-ma2 soa3 tian-to3-peng2 ka7 goa2 kong2, “He to-si7 Lo5-ma2-ji7.” Tai7-khai3 i u7 khi3 mng7 Song3 Choan5-seng7 hiaN, in-ui7 i si7 goa2 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 kap goa2 kang7-tui7 e5 chian3-iu2. Chi2-to7-oan5 chhio3 goan2 lau7-bu2 bun5-bong5. Goa2 ti7 sim lai7 in3 i kong2, se3-kai3 u7 40 goa7 ek e5 lang5 be7-hiau2 kong2 Hoa5-gi2, in kiam2-si7 long2 bun5-bong5? Mai3 o khah be7 chhau3-chho. 
	LMJ pang-chan7 goa2 oh8 chin che7 goa7-kok gi2, chhin-chhiuN7 Tek-gi2, Hoat-gi2, Se-pan-ga5-gi2, Sou-lian5-gi2, Eng-gi2 kap Kheh-gi2 teng2. Pat8-lang5 tioh8 oa2-kho3 lang5 a2-si7 chhiuN3-phiN3 hoat-im, goa2 e7-tang3 eng7 sou2 bat e5 LMJ ka7 chu3-im koh piau siaN-tiau7, sui5-si5 e7-tang3 hok8-sip8. Tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 TiuN Hok8-chu5 I-su sou2-kong2 i e7-tang3 eng7 LMJ khi3 oh8 Goan5-chu7-bin5 e5 oe7-gi2. Chit e5 kong-hau7 goa2 kaN2 po2-cheng3 chin ho2, chhiaN2 li2 chhi3-iong7 khoaN3 mai7.
	Goa2 thiaN chin che7 tiong2-poe3, tiong-lian5 lang5 a2-si7 siau3-lian5 lang5 kong2 Tai5-oan5-oe7, hoat-im chau2-cheng thai3 che7. Long2 be7-hiau2 hun b kap p, d kap t, g kap ch, k kap g. Che m7-si7 ChiuN2 Ui5-bun5 teh kong2 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 khiuN-khau2. Oan5-choan5 si7 Hoa5-gi2 toa3--lai5 e5 chho3-ngou7. M7-thang hiam5 Tai5-oan5-oe7. Tai5-oan5-oe7 chiah si7 bo5 an2-ni kong2. Tai5-oan5-oe7 u7 tok8-im, Hoa5-gi2 bo5 tok8-im long2 si7 chheng-im, sou2-i2 in teh kong2 e5 Tai5-oan5-oe7, thiaN tioh8 koai3-koai3. Chhin-chhiuN7 “m7-chai-iaN2” in chi2-u7 e7-hiau2 kong2 “mou2 chai5-iang2”. Tai5-oan5-oe7 bo5 m7-tioh8, si7 Pak-kiaN-gi2 e5 chha-chho3. Tai5-oan5-lang5 chin chio2 e7-hiau2 eng7 sun5-sui3 e5 Tai5-oan5-oe7 tui3-thau5 kong2 kau3-boe2. Na7 m7-si7 chham Hoa5-gi2 chiu7-si7 tioh8 chham i2-goa7 e5 goa7-kok gi2 chiah u7 hoat-tou7 thang piau2-tat8. Sit8-chai7 chin kho2-lian5 kap pi-ai.
Kho2-kian3 Tai5-oan5 su-iau3 cheng3-ti7, keng-che3, kho-ki, hoat-lut8, goa7-kau, jin5-khoan5, chok8-kun5 e5 iong5-ho5, bun5-hoa3 kap kau3-iok8 e5 hoat-tian2, bo2-gi2(tek8-piat8 si7 Ho7-lo2, Kheh-ka kap Goan5-chu7-bin5) tioh8 ai3 phou2-phian3-hoa3, tui3 sio2-oh8, tiong-oh8, kau3 tai7-hak8 tioh8 ai3 bo2-gi2 e5 phou2-phian3 kau3-iok8 kui2-na7 tang, chiah e7-tang3 chiong “e7-hiau2 Tai5-gu2” pian3 cho3 “Hok!”.
