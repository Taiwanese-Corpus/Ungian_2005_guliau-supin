Bo5 Chhin-chhiuN7..lang5              
			Babuza Chu5 
	Bo5 chhin-chhiuN7..lang5! Chit-ku3-oe7 si7 goan2 a-bo2 tiaN7 kong2 e5 oe7. Kau3 chit-ma2 goa2 e5 in3-siong7 iau2 chiok chhim, chiok chhim!
	Goan2 se3-han3 e5 si5, ka-keng2 chiok bai2. Na7 iong7 siok8-gi2 lai5 kong2, si7 san3 kau3 kui2 kiong7 beh liah8--khi3. M7-tioh8, goan2 san3 kau3 kui2 ma7 m7-kaN2 lai5 liah8! KiaN liah8 goan2 khi3 e7 ke-thiN in e5 ma5-hoan5 kap hu7-tam!
	Goa2 iau2 e7 ki3--tit, goa2 e5 te7-it kha chheh-pau si7 pat8-lang5 sang3..e5. Hit-chun7 goa2 thak8 sio2-hak8 4 ni5. Ti7 he chin3-cheng5, goa2 e5 chheh long2 iong7 kin-a2 pau, na2-chhiuN7 pau-hok8-a2 leh, chhiu2 koaN7. Chit-ma2 ka7 khoaN3--khi2--lai5, kai3-seng5 beh toe3-lang5-chau2 hit e5 khoan2-sit. Lang5 sang3..goa2 hit-kha chheh-pau si7 lang5 iong7 chin ku2 koh phoa3-kou5-kou5, bo5 ai3 tih8..e5. Goa2 theh8-tioh8 hit-kha chheh-pau e5 si5 si7 hoaN-hi2 kau3 kiong7 beh si2--khi3! Goa2 ti7 hia toa7-siaN jiang2 kong2 goa2 u7 chheh-pau..ah. Koh kui-e5 lang5 long2 tio5--khi2--lai5. Kau3 chit-ma2 goa2 koh ki3-tiau5-tiau5. Goa2 e5 ian5-pit ap8-a2 ma7 si7 pat8-lang5 hou7..goa2..e5. He si7 chha5 cho3..e5. Koa3 iong7 thoah..e5. Ti7 hit-e5 1960 ni5-tai7, beh u7 chit8-kai5 ian5-pit ap8-a2 m7-si7 tak8 khau2-chau3 long2 cho3 e7 kau3..e5. Chi3-chio2 ti7 goan2 e5 tong5-oh8 hia goa2 khoaN3--tioh8..e5 si7 an2-ne. U7 chit8-kai5 ian5-pit ap8-a2 si7 chin iang7 e5 tai7-chi3! Lang5 sang3..e5 ma7 kang7-khoan2! Goa2 chheng7 e5 khou3 si7 goan2 a-pa chheng7 phoa3..e5 chiah kai2 hou7 khah te2 hou7 goa2 chheng7. (Hit-e5 si5-tai7 goan2 long2 bo5 chheng7 lai7-khou3!) SaN ma7 si7 an2-ne. Phou2-thong goan2 ti7 chhu3--nih8 long2 bo5 chheng7-saN, thng3 pak-theh. Beh khi3 hak8-hau7 a2-si7 chhut-goa7 chiah chheng7-saN.
	San3 kau3 sim2-mih8 theng5-tou7 ? Li2 kho2-leng5 bo5 ai3 sin3! Put-si5 to ai3 ka7 lang5 chioh! Chioh bi2, chioh chiN5, chioh ke-si (chhiuN7 kong3-thui5-a2, ki3-a2). Chham7 se2 sin-khu iong7 e5 toa7 kha-thang2 ma7 si7 ai3 ka7 lang5 chioh! Cheng-chha m7-bat ka7 lang5 chioh ang, chioh kiaN2 nia5! Goa2 tiaN7-tiaN7 khoaN3-tioh8 goan2 a-bo2 khia7 ti7 lang5 e5 mng5-chhui3, “Lau2-ji7..e5, li2 kam2 u7? Gou7-chap8-khou poah..goa2 ho2--m7?” Thau5-a2 goa2 thiaN bo5 “poah” si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3? Boe2-a2 chiah chai-iaN2 si7 “chioh”. Goa2 thak8-chheh e5 hak8-hui3 u7 si5 si7 tong-oh8 koan..e5! Iau2 e7 ki3-tit thak8 gou7-ni5 si5 goan2 pan e5 tong5-oh8 koan chap8-goa7-khou hou7..goa2. Hit-chun7 e5 chu3-chheh chit8 hak8-ki5 ai3 gou7-chap8-khou, ho7-cho3 tai7-pan7-hui3. Goa2 thak8 chhou-tiong te7-it hak8-ki5 e5 chu3-chheh chiN5 400 goa7 khou (chu3-chheh, be2 chheh-pau kiam kho3-pun2 li-li khok-khok) ma7 si7 goa2 e5 chhin-chiaN5 hiahN i e5 se-chong khi3 tng3 chiah u7..e5! 
	Kong2 kau3 chia, “bo5 chhin-chhiuN7..lang5” chit-ku3-oe7 li2 tai7-khai3 e7-tang3 ke-kiam2 the2-hoe7--chit8--sut--a2 chiah tioh8! Sou2-i2 goan2 a-bo2 e7 an2-ne kong2 choat8-tui3 m7-si7 “bo5 peN7 teh khau3-chhan”. 
	“Bo5 chhin-chhiuN7..lang5” u7 nng7 chiong2 i3-su3:1, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 ti, kau2; 2, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 pat8-lang5.
	Te7-it e5 i3-su3 si7: lang5 bo5 seng5 lang5, put-koan2 si7 chheng7, chiah8 kap toa3, long2 bo5-seng5 si7 lang5, na2-chhiuN7 si7 tong7-but8! Che si7 chi2 it-poaN seng-oah8 but8-chit e5 hiang2-siu7. In-ui7 an2-ne, goan2 chha-put-to long2 hoat-iok8 put-liong5, ng5-sng-thang2 koh san2-pi-pa. Chin kho2-leng5 thau5-nau2 ma7 bo5 tit-tioh8 cheng3-siong5 e5 eng5-iong2 kap hoat-tian2. Bok8-koai3 goan2 e5 seng5-chiu7 bo5 koan5!
	Te7-ji7 e5 i3-su3 si7: bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 pat8-lang5. Pat8-lang5 u7 chhu3 thang toa3, u7 saN thang chheng7, u7 ho2-mih8 thang chiah8, u7 ho2 mia5-siaN koh u7 sia7-hoe7 te7-ui7. E7 tit-tioh8 lang5 e5 chun-tiong7. Goan2 long2 bo5! Goa2 e5 sio2-ti7 bat in-ui7 chheng7-chhah phoa3-lam3 phoa3-sam3 hou7 in hak8-hau7 e5 lau7-su ka7 thi2-chhio3, hai7 i tui3 hak8-hau7 chit8-lou7 khau3--tng2--lai5!
	Kau3 chit-ma2 goa2 gou7 hiaN-ti7 sui-jian5 long2 cho3 i-seng, u7 kou3-teng7 e5 siu-jip8, ma7 u7 sia7-hoe7 te7-ui7 kap ho2 mia5-siaN; m7-ku2 goa2 thau5-khak-te2 ma7 tiaN7-tiaN7 phu5-chhut hit-si5 e5 heng5-iaN2 kap hoe5-ek, siuN7-tioh8 iau2-si7 e7 sim-sng kap kha-nng2!
