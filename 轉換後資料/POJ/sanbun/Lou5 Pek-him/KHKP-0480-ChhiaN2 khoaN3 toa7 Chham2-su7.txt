
ChhiaN2 khoaN3 toa7 Chham2-su7

(Lou5 Pek-him)
Phi2-jin5 siu7 phai3 lai5 toa3 Chhan5-tiong,oa2 beh saN ni5 ku2; chiau3 sou2 keng-koe3,te7-hng e5 hoat-tat8 chin-kin2.  Chia si7 kin-chio e5 chhut-san2-te7, ti3-kau3 than3-tiohch8iN e5 lang5 chin che7.  Tng teh hoat-tat8,hoaN-hi2 e5 si5, hut-jian5 tu2-tioh8 toa7 chai-eh.Ti7 ji7-goeh8 20 jit8 ngou2-cheng5 oa2 nng7 tiam2 cheng, tui3 siang7 lau7-jiat8 e5 ke-tiong hoat-hoe2.  Hit-si5 tu2 lang5 teh siang3 tang7-bin5,ti3-kau3 hoah long2 bo5 kiu3-peng.  Tng lang5 kau3 e5 si5, hoe2 toa7-iam7, pak-hong toa7 thau3; ti3-kau3 tiong7-iau3 e5 tiam3-thau5 long2 sio--khi3, long2-chong2 sio-liau2 gou7-chap8-gou7 hou7.  Che cheng7 u7 Chhan5-tiong boe7-bat u7 e5 toa7 chai-eh;  sit8-chai7 chin toa7 kho2-lin5-tai7.  U7-e5 kan-ta bak8-khouang5, u7-e5 hoan5-lo2 kau3 gong7-gong7.  Hian7-si5 bo5 chhu3 khia7, bo5 mih8 chiah8, ti3-kau3 tioh8 khi3 pat8 lang5 tau hioh-khia7.   An2-ni siu7 sio--e5 ke3-keh long2-chong2 iok u7 ji7-chap8 goa7 ban7.  Kai-chai3 lang5 long2 bo5 siu7-hai7.  Ah !  chit teng2-ho7 e5 chham2-keng2, khoaN3-liau2 chin kan-khou2.  Siu7 sio hit cha2-khi2, bo5 thiaN to7-li2 e5 lang5, chin-che7 chu2 png7, moe5, chhut lai5 hou7 li5-chai--e5 chi2-ki;  koh hian7-si5 ia7 chin-che7 lang5 teh kia3-hu3 chiN5 kap bi2, lai5 teh kiu3-chou7.
Chhan5-tiong Kau3-hoe7 e5 sin3-chia2 khah che7 long2 toa3 ti7 ke-chhi7-nih8, chong2-si7 kai-chai3 kan-ta chit8 hou7 siu7 lian5-lui7 ti7 Chhan5-tiong.  I si7 Chhan5-tiong Kau3-hoe7 thau5 chit8-e5 sin3-chia2, hian7-si5 chiu2-koaN2,ia7 si7 kau3-hoe7 tiong lu2-kai3 e5 toa7 jiat8-sim-chia2, toa7 bou5-hoan7-chia2; le2-pai3-jit8 tu5 toa7 phoa3-piN7 i2-goa7 long2 m7-bat hioh.  Ka-teng5 lun5-liu5 e5 le2-pai3, sui-bong2 tu2-tioh8 hong, hou7, koaN5-leng2, koaiN2-a2 kiah8-teh ia7 chhut-sek8.  Ti7 ku7-lek8 e5 sin-chiaN koh khisaN2 keng-poaN3 chng-thiap8 e5 hia7-chhu3, hian7-si5 iau2-boe7 sim2-mih8 oan5-kang.  Khi2 chhu3 e5 chiN5, khah che7 si7 ka7 lang5 chioh-e5.  Chit-pang long2 siu7-sio, iok liau2 chheng-goa7 khou, kin-a2-jit8 siu7 lui7 ka3 an2-ni, lan2 kiam2-thang pang3 i teh kan-khou2 ?  Ah !  hian7-si5 bo5 thang chiah8, bo5 thang toa3, koh si7 chiu2-koaN2, cheng5 tiam3-khau2 soe3 lang5, siu tiam3-soe3 teh koe3-jit8; kin-a2-jit8 long2 cho3 chit-e7-khi3, chhu2-khi3 e5 seng-oah8 beh chaiN2-iuN7 leh ?  Goan2 khoaN3-liau2 chin kho2-lin5, sou2-i2 ai3 kiu5 liat8-ui7 tong siok8 Chu2 e5 hiaN-che2, kin2 kiah8 koan5-chhiu2,lai5 tau3 kiu3-chou7, ng3-bang7 tui3 lan2 e5 saN-thiaN3, e7-thang toa7 eng5-kng Kau3-hoe7 kap Siong7-te3, in-ui7 tui3 chit-e5 chai-lan7, Chhan5-tiong cheng3 lang5 kiu5 thiN e5 simlong2 hoat-hian7, tai7-ke kong2 si7 thiN-i3; ia7u thiaN lang5 teh kong2, in pai3 Siong7-te3e to ia7 e7 sio--khi3.  An2-ni kiam2 m7-si7 teh hian2-beng5 in m7 bat Siong7-te3 si7 in e5 ThiN-pe7 mah ?  sou2-i2 toa7 ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 e5 thiaN3-thang3 lai5 thiaN3 kau3 ti7 goan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 tiong e5 siu7-lan2-chia2.  Bang7 khoaN3 Po3 ehiaN5-che2 saN-the3 ki5-to2, thang tui3 khou2-lanl7ai siu chin-che7 ho2 koe2-chi2.  Na7 u7 ho2 sim-e5, chhiaN2 kia3 tui3 Chhan5-tiong Jin5-ho5 Siong-hoe7, Tan5 Sin-chai5 siu.
