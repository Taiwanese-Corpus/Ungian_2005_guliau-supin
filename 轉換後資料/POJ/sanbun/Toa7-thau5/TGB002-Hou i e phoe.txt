Toa7-thau5

Chhin-ai3 e5 H.P.:

Ti7 goa2 sia2 chit-tiuN phoe e5 si5-chun7, li2 eng-kai cha2 to7 tng2 khi3 kau3 chhu3--ni, kap chhu3--ni e5 lang5 cho3-hoe2--a!

Hit-mi5, goa2 ti7『河濱公園』, tiam7-tiam7 che7 ti7 li2 piN--a2, it-tit8 teh khoaN3 li2 e5 bin7, kau3 goa2 beh khun2--a, koh teh『描摩』hit-e7 iaN2. Au7--lai5, goa2 khai-si2 siuN7 li2 kong2 e5 oe7, siuN7 li2 si7 an2-choaN2 hiah-ni kin2 to7 beh ka7
goa2 e5 chhiu2 pang3--khui.

Goa2 sit8-chai7 m7 chai-iaN2 si7 an2-choaN2, boe2--a2, koh chau2 tng2-khi3 goan5-lai5 e5 sou2-chai7, lim-1-koa2 chiu2,to2 ti7 thou5-kha, khoaN3 thiN teng2 e5 chhiN. Ti7 chit-e7 si5-chun7, tang-peng5 chhoe lai5 『秋初』e5『晚風』, koh ka siong7 thiN-chhiN e5 kng, hou7 goa2 siuN7 khi2 1-ku3 oe7:

"Iong2-iu2 ai3 e5 lang5 bo5 su-iau3 hoan5-lo2; teh chau2-chhoe7 ai3 e5 lang5 bo5 si5-kan hoan5-lo2."

Goa2 teh tui-kiu5 li2 e5 ai3--a, kam2 m7 si7 ?

Hoan7-se3, li2 kam2-kak goa2 m7-si7 hit-e5 e7-tang3 khan li2 e5 chhiu2 e5 lang5; hoan7-se3, li2 to7 si7  hiah-ni-a2 khut-kiong5, beh 1-e7 lang5 kiaN5 ka-ki7 e5 lou7.

I don`t mind. Goa2 kan-taN beh ka7 li2 kong2: bo5-lun7 li2 si7 ti7『豐裕』e5『蘭陽平原』; iah si7  ti7 hoan5-hoa5 e5 Tai5-pak siaN5; sim7-chi2 ti7 Deutschland (Tek-kok) peng5-cheng7 e5 chng-kha, goa2 long2 goan7-i3 tiam7-tiam7 che7 ti7 li2 piN--a2, hoaN3 li2 e5 bin7, thiaN li2 kong2 li2 e5 khoai3-lok8 kap pi-siong.

『編織』ho2 e5 hong-chhoe, pit-su ai3 iong2-kam2 khi3 poe, jim7-ho5 lang5 long2 pit8-su ai3 chiok hok8. Goa2, e7 ki5-thai7 hit-kang lai5-kau3.

E7-sai2 tu2-tioh8 li2, goa2 sit8-chai7 chin heng7-un7!

Chiok li2 sun7-sim!
