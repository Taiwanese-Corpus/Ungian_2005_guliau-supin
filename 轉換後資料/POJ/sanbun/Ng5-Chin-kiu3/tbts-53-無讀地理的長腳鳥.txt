I2-li7-sa(Elisha) si7 chit8-chiah ti7 Chili chhut-si3 e5 lo3-kha-chiau2 (flamingo, ang5-ho7). Se3-chiah e5 si5 hou7 toa3 ti7 Litchfield, Connecticut chit8-e5 ho2-giah8-lang5 be2 lai5 cho3 ai3-niau2 (pet). Peng5-siong5-si5 i e5 sit8-kou2 si7 hou7 lang5 eng7 thih-soaN3 giap8-tiau5--le3, sou2-i2 i be7-tang3 sui5-pian7 poe-lai5 poe-khi3. Si7 ti7 chui2-khut-a2 piN kap 5,6 chiah e5 tang5-phoaN7 hoaN-hi2 teh koe3 khoai3-lok8 e5 jit8-chi2. 
Te7-li2 siong7 Connecticut si7 ti7 Bi2-kok e5 tang-pak peng5, chhiu-thiN na7 kau3 khi3-un e7 pian3 chin leng2, e7 loh8 toa7 seh, sou2-i2 chit-chiah Chili chhut-si3 e5 flamingo ti7 i e5 thau5-nau2 lai7 iau2 e7 ki3-tit8 ti7 Chili e5 si5 thiN-khi3 na7 khai-si2 leng2 tioh8 hiong3 pak poe, khi3 cho3 tou7-chiau2, khi3 khah sio-joah e5  sou2-chai7 tou7 tang-thiN. I u7 chhi3-khoaN3-mai7 beh thi2-sit8 poe, chong2-si7 i e5 sit8 hou7 lang5 giap8-tiau5--teh, bo5 hiah sui5-pian7 phut-lut8-sut8 to poe-chau2. Keng-koe3 che7-che7 pai2 e5 chhi3-poe, choe3-au7 hou7 i thoat-li7 chit e5 so2-lian7, khi2-sin hiong3 pak poe-khi3.
Pak-peng5 si7 Canada, si7 pi2 Connecticut koh khah koaN5 e5 sou2-chai7, m7-ku2 i bo5 thak8 te7-li2 m7-chai pak poaN3-kiu5 kap lam5 poaN3-kiu5 e5 cheng-chha, soah poe lai5 Ottawa, khoaN3-tioh8 Ottawa ho5 e5 ho5-piN u7 kui2-na7 chiah eng-kok lu2-ong5 sang3 hou7 Canada kap i beh-seng5 beh-seng5 e5 peh8-ho7 kap Ou-ho7 (Swan, thian-go5). I bo5 toa3 hou7-chiau3 (passport) iah bo5 chhiam-cheng3 (visa). Lun7-kau3 Ottawa che long2 bo5 bun7-te5.  Choe3-toa7 e5 bun7-te5 chiu7-si7 chia m7-si7 lo3-kha-chiau2 e5 lok8-hng5. U7 50 goa7 e5 ai3-hou7 ia2-niau2 tiong-sim e5 sin-su7 siok-lu2, bo5 chiah8 bo5 khun3, u7-e5 chheng2-ka3 tit8-tit8 siuN7 hong-hoat ai3 kiu3-hou7 chit-chiah lo3-kha-chiau2. 
 In iong7-sim ke3-kau3 siuN7-chhut kok-chiong2 e5 liah8-chiau2 e5 hong-hoat; eng7 bang7-a2, eng7 chhiu7-ki iam2-hou7 e5 chun5 sai2 oa2-khi3 beh pha-bang7, eng7 chhi7-liau7 ka7 siaN5, eng7 jin5-cho7 e5 flamingo beh ka siaN5 oa2-lai5, chi2-u7 chit8-hang7 in bo5 eng7--e5 chiu7-si7 eng7 chheng3 ka7 phah. Keng-koe3 chit8 goeh8 jit8 koh 3 jit8, choe3-au7 chiah eng7 chhi7-liau7 he7-ti7 chiau2-lam hou7  i chiah8, chiah liah8-tioh8 (Niau2 ui7 sit8 bong5) . In-ui7 si5-kan teh pek, na7-bo5 koaN2-kin2 kiu3-hou7 , hit-chiah chiau2-a2 m7-si7 e7 koaN5-si2 chiu7 e7 go7-si2. Ti7 chia i si7 ui7-tioh8 sit8 chiah hou7 lang5 liah-tioh8 ia7-si7 kiu3-tioh8 i chit8-tiau5 mia7. Chit8 goeh8 jit8 ku2 Ottawa e5 chhi7-bin5-po3 eng7 thau5-tiau5 sin-bun5 po3-to7 chit e5 siau-sit. Chit8-e7 liah8-tioh8, hiah-e5 ai3-hou7  ia2-niau2 e5 lang5 soah chiok8 m7-kam sang3 chit chiah chiau2-a2 to3-tng2 khi3 Bi2-kok. U7 e5 au3-hoe5 kong2, chaiN2-iuN7 kho-hak8 chiah hoat8-tat8 lian7 hit-chiah be7-siaN2 gau5 poe e5 chiau2-a2 to liah8 be7 tioh8. U7 kau3 png7-thang2.  Ai3-hou7  ia2-niau2 tiong-sim e5 chu2-jim7  Nihei lu2-su7 kong2, chu2-iau3 si7 goan2 tui3 chit-chiong2 jiat8-tai3 e5 chiau2-chiah bo5 siaN2-mih8 jim7-sek, m7-chai in e5 seng-oah8 sip8-koan3 lah. 
 Beh8 sang3 i tng2-khi3 Litchfield, Connecticut chin3-cheng5, Nihei lu2-su7 ho2-sim hiong3 Bi2-kok e5 i5-bin5-kiok8 the3 i sin-chheng2 visa, Air Canada hui-ki kong-si bian2-hui3 sang3 Nihei lu2-su7 kap flamingo che7 hui-ki. Nihei lu2-su7 ui7-tioh8 chit-chiah chiau2-a2  khi3 cho3 chit8-su hun2-ang5 e5 saN-khou3 , bo7-a2, eng7 chin sio-lo7 e5 eN-a2 phoeh8-toaN pau ho2-se3 (m7-chai u7 ka7 chiau-a2 cho3 sin-saN sin-khou3  hou7  chheng7 bo5 goa2  khiok m7-chai) sang3 chiau2-a2 tng2-khi3 goan5-siok8 e5 chu2-lang5 e5 chhu3. Litchfield e5 chu2-lang5 in-ui7 sit8-loh8 e5 lo3-kha-chiau2 koh to3-tng2--lai5 khui party toa7-toa7  kheng3-chiok. 
 Thang-kong2 hit-chiah lo3-kha-chiau2 chin heng7-hok, tu2-tioh8 chit8-kun5 ai3-hou7 ia2-niau2 e5 ho2-sim-lang5 the3 i koan-sim. Goa2 siuN7 chit-chiah chiau2-a2 bo5 khi3 thak8 te7-li2 chi2-u7 kho3 i e5 keng-giam7 tit8-tioh8 e5 ti3-sek ti7 Pak Bi2-chiu teh seng-oah8. Hit-chiah lo3-kha-chiau2 ti7 Ottawa cho3 chiok8 chit8-koh goeh8 e5  beng5-seng mui2-jit8 ian2-chhut thau5-tiau5 sin-bun5 ki3-su7 hou7 lang5 thang liu5-liam7, che si7 chit8-e5 hi2-kek8 a2-si7 pi-kek8?
