
 
KhoaN3 siong7 e5 hok-khi3
Bun5 Kou-niu5
Ti7 Tai7-Tek-kok u7 chit8 sou2-chai7, hiah e5 lang5 khah che7 siok8 ti7 Thian-chu2-kau3 Cheng5 kui2-na7 ni5 khia7-khi2 ti7-hia u7 chit8 e5 chhai2-oe7 e5 lang5, ki-ge7 oh8 kau3 chin cheng- kong, put-lun7 beh oe7 siong7, a2-si7 san-sui2 hoa-niau2 tak8-hang7 put-chi2 gau5 oe7. Hit- e5 lang5 iah8 than3 Thian-chu2-kau3, chiau3 in e5 hoat-tou7 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3.   
U7 chit8 jit8 in kau3-hoe7 chit8 e5 pa-le2 khi3 chhoe7 i, chiu7-si7 ai3 chhiaN2 i lai5 oe7 Ia5-sou hou7 lang5 teng3 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 kap hiah e5 ui5 teh khoaN3 e5 lang5, chit ho7 tou5, koh kap i iok, cho3 beng5-pek8 chiu7 beh kui2-na7 pah khou gin5 siuN2 i.   
Tu2-tu2 hit-si5 i teh oe7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2-lang5 e5 siong7, i tak8 jit8 u7 bat lai5 chhoe7 i che7. Teh che7 e5 si5-chun7, i e5 bak8-chiu si3-koe3 khoaN3, beh tham3-khoaN3 u7 sim2-mih8 sui2 e5 mih8, hut-jian5-kan khoaN3-kiN3 chhai2-oe7 e5 lang5 teh oe7 Ia5-sou e5 siong7, chiu7 chu3-sin5 chu-chu siong3 i, soa3 eng7 chhiu2 ki2, mng7 oe7 siong7 e5 lang5 kong2,Che si7 sim2-mih8 lang5?” In3 kong2.Si7 Ia5-sou.”I koh mng7,In hia e5 lang5 teh cho3 saN2-hoe3?” In3 kong2,Si7 beh ka7 teng3 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 hou7 i si2.”Hit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 koh mng7 chit8-ku3 kong2,“An3-chaiN2-iuN7 beh hou7 i si2, i cho3 sim2-mih8 phaiN2?” In3 kong2,TaN goa2 bo5 eng5 thang siong5-siong5 kap li2 kong2 oe7, chhiaN2 li2 tiam7-tiam7 hia che7.“   
Cha-bou2-lang5 hit jit8 m7 kaN2 koh mng7, chiu7 tit8-tit8 khoaN3 Ia5-sou e5 bin7. Pat8-jit8 koh mng7 chit8 koe3 kong2, Na7-si7 hit e5 lang5 cho3 chiau5-chng5 ho2, in an3-chaiN2-iuN7 khoan2-thai7 i kau3 an2-ni?” Chhai2-oe7 e5 lang5 in3 i kong2,Goa2 taN beh chiong hit chan5 su7 kong2 chit8 koe3 hou7 li2 thiaN, kong2-liau2 li2 m7 thang koh-chai3 kiau2-jiau2 goa2.” I chiu7 tui3 thau5 kau3 boe2, lun7 Ia5-sou lim5- kau3 se3-kan, chhut-si3 cho3 lang5, sou2 kiaN5 sou2 cho3 bo5 chho3-gou7, kiat-kiok8 hou7 phaiN2-lang5 liah8 khi3, bai7 kau3 si2, hit chan5 su7 long2- chong2 ka7 i kong2 beng5-pek8.   
Hit e5 oe7 siong7 e5 lang5 ka-ti7 e5 sim khah ngi7, sui-jian5 i teh oe7 Ia5-sou sou2 keng-koe3 e5 kan-khou2, iau2-ku2 i ka-ti7 be7 tong7 sim an-an jian5-jian5 long2 bo5 lin5-bin2 the2-thiap. Cha-bou2-lang5 hoan2-tng2 thau5 chit8 pai2 thiaN chit e5 tai7-chi3, i e5 sim siu7 kam2-kek, be7-bian2-tit lau5 bak8-sai2. I ka-ti7 e5 siong7 oe7 beng5-pek8, chhai2-oe7 e5 lang5 kio3 i lai5 khoaN3, I chit8-e7 khoaN3 na2-chun2 bo5 khoaN3 i, chiu7 liam5-piN oat8-thau5 lai5 khoaN3 Ia5-sou e5 siong7, soa3 tui3 cho3 oe7 e5 lang5 kong2,“Na7-si7 i the3 li2 tam-tng chit ho7 toa7 kan-khou2, li2 kiam2 bo5 chu7-jian5 thiaN3 I mah?”   
Kong2 hit ku3, oe7 siong7 e5 lang5 u7 ang5- Bin7 kian3-siau3, long2 bo5 oe7 thang in3. I khiok put-chi2 chhim chai Ia5-sou e5 tai7- chi3, put-ko3 kau3 kin-a2-jit8 long2 m7 bat siuN7- khi2 I sou2 keng-koe3, kap i pun2-sin u7 sim2-mih8 kan-siap8.   
Hit jit8 cha-bou2-lang5 sou2 kong2,“I the3 li2 tam-tng kan-khou2,“ hit ku3 siong5-siong5 chhi3-chhak8 i e5 sim, sui-jian5 ai3 pang3 be7- ki3-tit, iau2-ku2 be7-e7, tauh8-tauh8 na2-chhin- chhiuN7 u7 siaN teh kong2, “Li2 kiam2 bo5 chu7- jian5 thiaN3 I mah?”   
Koe3 kui2-na7 jit8 i e5 sim iu5-goan5 be7 an-un2, kham be7 tiau5, sui-jian5 i siok8 Thian- chu2-kau3 iau2-ku2 in bo5 hoat-tou7 thang ka7 i thau2-pang3, i e5 sim chin kan-khou2 ai3 beh chhoe7 peng5-an e5 lou7. U7 chit8 jit8 i ngou2-gu7- jian5 jip8 chit8 keng e5 hok-im-tng5, thiaN sian-siN teh kong2 in-tian2 e5 to7-li2 sim2-mih8 i3-su3, koh Siong7-te3 bat eng7 sim2-mih8 hong-hoat kiu3 lang5 thoat-li7 i e5 choe7-ok.   
Oe7 siong7 e5 lang5 thiaN-liau2 sim chiu7 hoaN-hi2 it-hoat sin3, iah8 chai chit-tiap8 e5 hok-khi3 kap ku7-khoan2 lai5 saN-pi2, sit8-chai7 u7 cheng-chha chin hng7. Cheng5 kan-ta than3 hoat-tou7, taN tit-tioh8 Ia5-sou pun2-sin, kap I kiat-lian5, ko2-jian5 si7 kau3-giah8. Chhai2-oe7 e5 lang5 taN ai3 chiong ka-ti7 hian3 hou7 Chu2, cho3 I e5 lou7-eng7, m7-ku2 i khoa3-lu7 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 chhui3-chih8 chin tun be7-hiau2 kong2 oe7, kiaN-liau2 bo5 lou7 thang  
po3-iong5 Chu2 e5 jin5-ai3. Tu2-tu2 hit-si5 hut-jian5-kan teh siuN7,”Ho2 kai-chai3 goa2 e7- hiau2 oe7 siong7, ng3-bang7 i the3 goa2 kong2 oe7 lai5 kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 toa7 thia3-thang3.”Sou2-i2 koh oe7 Ia5-sou teh keng-koe3 kan-khou2 hit ho7 tou5, oe7-liau2 chiu7 sang3 hit e5 siong7 hou7 i pun2 sou2-chai7 e5 lang5, cho3 kong-iong7, koh e7-te2 u7 sia2,“I the3 li2 tam-tng kan- khou2, li2 kiam2 bo5 chu7-jian5 thiaN3 I mah?” hit ku3. Tak8-jit8 che7-che7 lang5 lai5 khoaN3 hit e5 siong7, u7-e5 khoaN3 liau2 iah8 siu7 kam2- kek, kui-hok8 ti7 Chu2. Loe7-tiong cha2- cheng5 hit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 u7 koh lai5 khoaN3 soa3 sin3 Ia5-sou.
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 168 koan3, 1899 ni5 3 geh8)
