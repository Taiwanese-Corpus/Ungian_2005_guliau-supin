Lin2 m7-thang kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3
Toh Heng5-li7
 Ti7 Sin-iok I2-hut-sou2 te7 6 chiuN, thau5-cham7 u7 lun7 hau3-to7 e5 to7-li2.  Lun7 hau3-to7 si7 Tang-iuN5 choe3 te7-it iau3-kin2 e5 to7-tek, sou2-i2 kong2,萬惡淫為首百行孝為先; i3-su3, chiu7-si7 kong2 ban7 hang7 e5 phaiN2 si7 kan-im5 choe3 thau5, ia7 pah hang7 e5 phin2-heng7 chiu7-si7 iu2-hau3 choe3 tai7-seng.  Sou2-i2 Tang-iuN5 tui3 kou2-cha2 chiu7 khoaN3 hau3-to7 choe3 te7-it iau3-kin2, na7 boe7-hiau2 iu2-hau3, si7 khoaN3 choe3 khim5-siu3 siang7 khoan2.  TaN lun7 chit chan5 hau3-to7, khoaN3 choe3 phin2-heng7 tiong si7 pai5 ti7 tai7-seng, che khiok si7 tai7-ke kong-jin7 e5 to7-tek, chong2-si7 lun7-khi2 Khong2-chu2 sou2 ka3-si7 e5 hau3-to7 hit e5 kau3-iok8-hoat, tui3 lan2 Ki-tok-kau3 ka7 i khoaN3 si7 put oan5-choan5; chiu7-si7 boe7-oe7 hou7 choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5 lang5 lai5 chin7 i e5 hau3-to7.  In-ui7 sou2 ka3-si7--e5, long2 si7 kio3 kiaN2-ji5 tioh8 choat8-tui3-tek hok8-chiong5 sun7-than3 pe7-bu2, long2 bo5 kong2-khi2, choe3 pe7-bu2 e5 lang5 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 hou7 i e5 kiaN2 oe7 thang kiaN5 ti7 hau3-to7.  Na7-si7 Ki-tok-kau3 chiu7 m7-si7 an2-ni.  Lan2 na7 khoaN3 I2-hut-sou2 6 chiuN 1 chat kong2, Choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5, lin2 tiam3 ti7 Chu2 tioh8 sun7-than3 lin2 e5 pe7-bu2.  An2-ni kap Khong2-chu2 sou2 ka3-si7 e5 si7 siang7 khoan2, chong2-si7 an2-ni bo5 kau3-giah8; ti7 te7 4 chat u7 koh ka3 chit8 ku3 kong2, Choe3 lau7-pe7 e5, lin2 m7-thang kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3, an2-ni chiah u7 oan5-choan5.  In-ui7 na7 bo5 koh ke chit ku3 "m7-thang kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3." Sui-jian5 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 u7 ai3 iu2-hau3 lai5 sun7-than3 i e5 pe7-bu2, chong2-si7 pe7-bu2 put-si5 ai3 kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3, an2-ni choe3 kiaN2-ji5 u7 iu2-hau3 e5 sim soa3 piN3 choe3 put-hau3, an2-ni chit e5 put-hau3 e5 choe7 kiam2 si7 kiaN2-ji5 e5 mah?  Sou2-i2 hoan7-na7 tui3 pe7-bu2 e5 kong-kek lai5 put-hau3, chit e5 choe7 m7-si7 kiaN2-ji5 tioh8 tam-tng, chiu7-si7 i e5 pe7-bu2 tioh8 tam-tng.
TaN lan2 siok8 Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7, lan2 ai3 lan2 e5 chu2-te7 iu2-hau3 kiong-keng3 lan2, lan2 tioh8 chin soe3-ji7, m7-thang kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3.  Lan2 na7 kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3 lai5 hou7 kiaN2-ji5 put-hau3, si7 bo5 te3 thang thou2-khui3, kong2 chhut hou7 lang5 thiaN.
 
Kin-a2-jit8 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 choe3 pe7-bu2 e5 lang5, choe7-choe7 lai5 thiaN3 i e5 kiaN2; chiu7-si7 tui3 chhut-si3 chiu7 mi5-noa7 iuN2-chhi7 iN, boe2 ho2-mih8 chu-iong2-phin2 lai5 hou7 i chiah8, chian2 sui2 e5 pou3 choe3 saN hou7 i chheng7, boe2 choe7-choe7 e5 thit-tho5-mih8 hou7 i thit-tho5.  Au7-lai5 hou7 i thak8-chheh, hak8-hau7 chit8 keng thak8 ke3 chit8 keng, chiN5-gun5 khai kui2-na7 chheng; choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5 put-chi2 hoaN-hi2 kap boan2-chiok, ai3 kin2 chhut ti7 sia7-hoe7 lip8-kong kian3-giap8, lai5 po3-tap lau7-pe7.  Chong2-si7 it-tan3 chit8 e5 tiong2-seng5, ai3 beh oah8-tang7 ti7 sia7-hoe7 lai5 heng-khi2 i e5 ke, na7-si7 i e5 lau7-pe7 khoaN3-kiN3 kiaN2 e5 gau5, soa3 tioh8-kiaN, sou2 u7 e5 chai5-san2 kiaN i chai, sou2 u7 e5 tai7-chi3 kiaN i chhap.  M7-na7 an2-ni, soa3 koh pun chit8 e5 kiaN2 lai5 thiong2-ai3, mi5-jit8 ti7 i e5 sin-piN, thiaN3 i iaN5-ke3 ti7 i sou2 siN e5 kiaN2, ti3-kau3 hou7 i e5 hau7-siN sit-chi3 thou2-khui3 kong2, Ah! Goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 sit8-chai7 chin kho2-lin5, boe7-hiau2 hiong2-hok i e5 hok-khi3, kam-goan7 choe3 gu5-gong7.  Au7-lai5 chiu7 siuN7 kong2, Goa2 tioh8 kio3-chhiN2 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7, lai5 siuN7-khi2 cha2-cheng5 iong2-seng5 iuN2-chhi7 goa2 e5 bok8-tek, kau3 kin-a2-jit8 chaiN2-iuN7 chiah lai5 bu-si7 goa2.  Lau7-pe7 thiaN kiaN2-ji5 e5 kan-cheng3, m7-na7 bo5 cheng-sin5, hoan2-tng2 ai3 ka7 i li7-ian5, gia5 to ai3 thai5 i.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 lau7-pe7 lai5 kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3, i e5 kiaN2-ji5 beh chaiN2-iuN7 lai5 iu2-hau3 i e5 lau7-pe7, pat8 lang5 m7-chai u7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 bo5, na7 u7, chhiaN2 chi2-si7 goa2.  Chong2-si7 na7-si7 goa2, sit8-chai7 boe7-hiau2 iu2-hau3.
Sou2-i2 choe3 lau7-pe7 na7 ai3 ho2-mia7, tioh8 m7-thang kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3.  Na7 u7 chit khoan2 e5 lau7-pe7, si7 chin kho2-lin5, chin sam-pat, chin bo5 hok-khi3, si7 lau7 phaiN2-mia7.
Liat8-ui7 e5 hiaN-chi2 ah! lan2 u7 chu2-te7 bo5?  Na7 u7, i2-keng tiong2-seng5, tioh8 khoan2-thai7 i choe3 chit8 e5 toa7-lang5, m7-thang khoaN3 i si7 gin2-a2.  In-ui7 kiaN2-ji5 tiong2-seng5, u7 i e5 li2-seng3 (理性) kap i ko3-seng3 (個性), lan2 na7 bu5-si7 khoaN3-khin i e5 li2-seng3 kap ko3-seng3, si7 teh kong-kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3, si7 teh chau-that pou7-jiok8 i e5 jin5-keh, na7 an2-ni, chu7-jian5 choe3 kiaN2-ji5 e5 bo5 beh hang5-hok8 i e5 si7-toa7.  Choe3 lang5 e5 pe7-bu2 na7 kek kiaN2-ji5 e5 siu7-khi3, siong7-chhiaN2 hou7 i e5 kiaN2 sit-loh8 i e5 iu2-hau3 kap kiong-keng3, ho5-hong3 m7-si7 choe3 lang5 e5 pe7-bu2 lai5 kek lang5 e5 siu7-khi3, hit e5 hoan2-kam2 kiam2 si7 sio2-khoa2 mah?
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 Te7 609 Ho7, 1935 ni5 12 goeh8)
