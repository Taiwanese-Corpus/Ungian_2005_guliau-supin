Toh8-hoe2 e5 iah8-a2 (3)

Goan5-chok: Chan Sim-Gi5, 1989,11,2
Hoan-ek8: Loonng X, 1998,8,5


519 hit kang ti7 chong2-tok-hu2 thau5-cheng5 cheng7-cho7 e5 hian7-tiuN5, goa2 chai-iaN2 Chiam Ek-hoa5 u7 lau5 kui2 tiuN-a2 ui5-su. Hit am3, kun5-chiong3 kap tin3-pok8 pou7-tui7 tui3-khong3, kap tai7-seng soan-pou3 e5 kang7-khoan2, cheng7-cho7 ti7 12 tiam2 to7 lai5 kiat-sok.

Tng2 kau3 chhu3 e5 si5, thiaN chhu7-nih e5 lang5 kong2, am3-si5 e5 sin-bun5 u7 hong3-sang3 Chiam Ek-hoa5 in sio2-moe7 ku2-pan7 e5 ki3-chia2-hoe7, kong2 si7 Bin5-chin3-tong2 hai7 si2 in hiaN-ko......Chiam Ek-hoa5 in sio2-moe7 si7 keng2-chhat hong-bin7 an-pai5 chhut-lai5 kong2-oe7, chit chan5 tai7-chi3 gun2 ti7 Tai5-tai7 peN7-iN7 thai3-peng5-keng khoaN3 tioh8 A-hoa5 si2-the2 e5 si5-chun7 to7 chai-iaN2.  Goa2 kui e5 lang5 long2 iah ti7 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 e5 cheng5-keng2 lai7-toe2, kun-pun2 to7 bo5 teh chhap mui5-the2 si7 an2-choaN2 teh niu2-khiok A-hoa5 e5 chu7-hun5.

Sim-cheng5 chiaN5 bai2, keh-kang thau3-cha2 to7 tiam3 bin5-chhng5 teng2 bo5 siuN7 beh khi2-lai5. Po3-sia7 ki3-chia2 kha3 tian7-oe7 lai5 mng7 goa2 Tan5 Oan2-chin e5 siau-sit, in-ui7 10 kui2 ni5 cheng5, goa2 to7 i2-keng kap Tan Oan2-chin cho3-hoe2 phah-piaN3 koe3. Cha-hng 519 i ti7 Nailong e5 song7-le2 teng2-bin7 chhut-hian7, goa2 chiaN5-cho3 "po2-hou7" i e5 1 hun7-chu2.  Kui e5 iu5-heng5 e5 koe3-teng5, goa2 long7 tiam7-tiam7 khia7 ti7 i chiaN3-peng5. Kha3 tian7-oe7 lai5 e5 ki3-chia2 siuN7 beh chai-iaN2 1 koa2 siau-sit, goa2 ka7 kong2 bo5 siaN2 thang kong2 e5. Ki3-chia2 ma7 kap goa2 kong2 tioh8 Chiam Ek-hoa5 e5 sin-bun5, i kong2 po3-choa2 teng2-bin7 khan chin choe7 in sio2-moe7 kong2 kah bo5 siaN2 ho2 thiaN e5 oe7. Goa2 ka7 ki3-chia2 kong2, chu7 cha-hng khai-si2, goa2 bo5 teh chhap tian7-si7, mui5-the2 an2-choaN2 teh po3-to7.  Chi3-chio2, goa2 pun2-lang5 i2-keng chin3-jip8 Nailong kap A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 e5 keng3-kai3, mui5-the2 e5 po3-to7 tui3 goa2 lai5 kong2 si7 leng7-goa7 1 e5 se3-kai3.

Goa siong-sin3, goa2 e7-tang3 ka7 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 liau2-au7 e5 tai7-chi3 taN khi2-lai5. M7-koh chit kiaN7 khang-khoe3 m7 si7 te2-ki5 lai7 e7-tang3 oan5-seng5 e5 tai7-chi3. Ui7 Nailong chiu2-leng5 e5 si5-chun7, goa2 tit tioh8 siau-sit, 519 beh u7 khah chhiong e5 heng5-tong7. 519 hit kang, hian7-tiuN5 thoan5 lai5 "Chiam Ek-hoa5 chu7-hun5". Goa2 chiah chai-iaN2 si7 siaN2-mih lang5, iong7 siaN2-mih khoan2 e5 hong-sek. Goa2 bo5 hoat-tou7 ti7 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 liau2-au7, siong-sim, siau3-liam7 koe3 to7 ka7 chit chan5 tai7-chi3 khng3 toa3 piN-a2 hek8-chia2 si7 khng3 tiam3 sim-koaN te2. 1 e5 pi2 goa2 khah oaN3 chham-ka hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7 e5 ki-chan5 kang-chok-chia2, ka7 seN3-mia7 hong7-hian3 chhut-lai5, ji5-chhiaN2 si7 chhai2-chhu2 u7 ke3-oe7 e5 heng5-tong7. An3 kok hong-bin7 lai5 kong2, goa2 long2 u7 to7-gi7 siong7 e5 chek-jim7. Pat8-lang5 bo5 tiaN7 e7 iong7 in ka-ki7 e5 hong-sek, ka7 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 liau2 e5 hio7-su7 kng khi2-lai5. M7-koh, goa2 chai-iaN2, goa2 u7 tiat8-piat8 e5 chek-jim7, ka7 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 au7 e5 tang7-taN3 kng khi2-lai5.

Goa2 to7 tiam3 bin5-chhng5 teng2 teh siuN7, to7 sng3 kong2 boai3 chhap mui5-the2 an2-choaN2 niu2-khiok, goa2 ia7 si7 e7-tang3 thau3-koe3 mui5-the2 seng ui7 A-hoa5 lai5 cho3 1 koa2 tai7-chi3, hou7 koan-sim A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 e5 lang5 tu5-liau2 ok-i3 niu2-khiok e5 gian5-lun7 i2-goa7, iah e7-tang3 thiaN tioh8 1 koa2 bo5 kang5 e5 siaN.  Su7-sit8 siong7, chi3-chio2 u7 sou3 10 lang5, ui7 519 e5 heng5-tong7 su7-sian u7 sia2 ui7-su. Goa2 si7 ki5-tiong 1 e5. Goa2 e5 ui7-su e5 iaN2-in3-pun2 ti7 519 hit kang chai2-khi2 to7 i2-keng kau hou7 Chu7-lip8 Am3-po3 ki3-chia2 Gou5 Tian2-iong5. Lai7-iong5 tai7-khai3 si7: Chun2-kong2 goa2 na7 hong5 liah8, m7-bian2 the3 goa2 chhiaN3 lut8-su, ma7 m7-bian2 pian7-hou7. Na7 in-ui7 hoan2-khong3 Kok-bin5-tong2 lai5 si2, ng3-bang7 si2-the2 hoe2-hoa3, kut-hu to7 tiam3 Tai5-pak poe hiong3 Hoe2-sio-to2 e5 hui-heng5-ki teng2 ka7 ia7 loh8 khi3, chu7 khong-tiong ia7 tiam3 goa2 sou2 ai3 e5 chit phiN3 thou2-te7. 

Su7-sian i7-pi7 ui5-su, si7 m7 chai-iaN2 519 hit kang e7 hoat-seng siaN2-mih tai7-chi3, m7-koh goan7-i3 ui7 chham-ka 519 heng5-tong7 lai5 hong7-hian3 chhut seN3-mia7. A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 liau2-au7, goa2 chiah chai-iaN2, i keng-koe3 chiaN5 cheng-bit8 e5 siat-ke3, cho3 chhut siong7 thiat-te2 e5 hong7-hian3. Goa2 ka7 A-hoa5 u7 lau5 ui5-su chit-chan5 tai7-chi3 ka7 Gou5 Tian2-iong5 kong2. I koh mng7 goa2 kong2 ui5-su si7 beh sia2 hou7 siaN2?  Lai7-iong5 si7 siaN2-mih?

Goa2 ka7 kong2, u7 1 hun7 ui5-su si7 hou7 Chhoa3 Iu2-choan5. Ki5-tha e5 goa2 boe7-sai2 kong2. Goa2 e7 ka7 kong2 A-hoa5 e5 ui5-su lai7-toe2 u7 1 hun7 si7 beh hou7 Chhoa3 Iu2-choan5, si7 beh hou7 ki3-chia2 chai-iaN2 goa2 e5 siau-sit m7 si7 ka-ki7 pian e7, ji5-chhiaN2, Iu2-choan5 iah teh kaN-gak8 lai7, chit pou7-hun7 it-teng7 su-iau3 goa2 lai5 tau3-saN-kang7. Goa2 siong7-boe2 1 kai2 kap Chiu Hui7-eng khi3 Ku-soaN kaN-gak8 khoaN3 Iu2-choan5, si7 Nailong ka7 ka-ki7 koaiN khi2-lai5 e5 si5. Thau3-koe3 tian7-oe7, Iu2-choan5 ng3-bang7 goa2 ti7 kaN-gak8 goa7 e7-tang3 pang i chiau3-kou3 Hui7-eng.

Chit kang (520) e5 Chu7-lip8 Am3-po3 khan chhut-lai5 goa2 hou7 Gou5 Tian2-iong5 e5 siau-sit, A-hoa5 u7 lau5 ui5-su.

Soa3 loh8-lai5, kok po3 kong-khai A-hoa5 e5 kui2 tiuN ui5-su.

Oan2-chin ti7 519 am3-a2, peng5-iu2 ka7 an-pai5 toa3 e5 sou2-chai7 liau2-au7, pau-khoah sin-bun5-kai3, cheng5-ti7 tan-ui7 long2 m7 chai-iaN2 i ti7 to2-ui7. Oan2-chin te7 1 kai2 e5 ki3-chia2-hoe7 liau2-au7, goa2 koh khai-si2 "po2-hou7" i, e7-sai2 kong2 1-kha-pou7 to7 bo5 tah8 soa2-khui. An2-ne it-tit8 kau3 Oan2-chin e5 an3-kiaN7 chin3-lip8 khi3 su-hoat teng5-su5, goa2 chiah tau5-lip8 choan5-pou7 e5 si5-kan chun2-pi7 ui7 A-hoa5 lai5 chhut chheh e5 tai7-chi3. Che m7-taN7 si7 1 hang7 bun5-ji7 kang-chok, chhut-chheh nia7-tiaN7. Chin u7 kho2-leng5 bun5-ji7 kang-chok iah boe7 oan5-seng5, chheh bo5 hoat-tou7 chhut-pan2, goa2 kui e5 lang5 to7 i2-keng chin3-jip8 kau3 A-hoa5 si2-bong5 e5 se3-kai3, m7 chai-iaN2 ka-ki7 e7 pian3 kah an2-choaN2.

A-hoa5 chu7-hun5, pit-jian5 si7 1 kiaN7 lek8-su2-seng3 e5 su7-kiaN7. Goa2 si7 bun5-ji7 kang-chok-chia2, hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7 e5 ki-chan5 kang-chok-chia2, ji5-chhiaN2 koh si7 tit tioh8 i siong-koan siau-sit ki5-tiong e5 1 e5. Chun2-kong2 goa2 na7 boe7-tang3 chin7 goa2 ka-ki7 e5 khui3-lat8, cho3 1 tiam2-a2 tai5-chi3, goa2 giau-gi5, bi7-lai5, m7 chai e7 iong7 siaN2-mih khoan2 e5 heng5-sek lai5 ki3-liok8 A-hoa5 e5 chu7-hun5?

An3 519 liau2-au7, to7 i2-keng chhut-hian7 chin choe7 chho3-ngou7 e5 iau5-gian5 thoan5-lai5 thoan5-khi3. Chit chiong2 cheng5-heng5 na7 koh ke3-siok8 loh8-khi3, to7 e7 hong5 kam2-kak chin hoan5-lo2. Goa2 m7 si7 siong7 ho2 e5, ui5-it e5 ki3-liok8-chia2 iah si7 khoan5-sek-chia2. Goa2 kian-chhi5 e5 si7 ai3 sun7-ki5-chu7-jian5. Chi3-chio2 goa2 boe7-tang3 phian3 ka-ki7 sia2 chit8 koa2 ka-ki7 to bo5 hoat-tou7 siong-sin3 e5 tai7-chi3. Goa2 m7 si7 lek8-su2 hak8-ka, siok8-i2 lek8-su2 e5 pou7-hun7, eng3-kai hou7 lek8-su2 hak8-ka khi3 cho3 chhu2-li2. Goa2 cho3 koe3 ki3-chia2, sui5-si5 e7-tang3 iong7 chu7-iu5 ki3-chia2 e5 sin-hun7 cho3 sin-bun5 kang-chok. M5-koh, goa2 peng7 bo5 ka7 A-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3 tong3-cho3 sin-bun5 lai5 chhu2-li2. Na7 ka7 A-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3, iong7 sin-bun5 lai5 chhu2-li2, goa2 to7 e7 chhan-chhiuN7 kap chhan5-kiau5-a2 kang7-khoan2 hou7 lang5 tho2-iah. Goa2 iau-kiu5 ka-ki7, ti7 goa2 ka-ki7 chong-kau3-koan e5 ki-chhou2 teng2-bin7, koh chin3 1 pou7 lai5 su-kho2.

Ka7 chong-kau3 e5 sim-cheng5 khng3 toa3 hian7-sit8 e5 seng-oah8 lai5 khoaN3 chit chan5 tai7-chi3. Pau-khoah ha7-pit e5 mui2 1 ji7, 1 ku3. Goa2 tui3 bun5-hak8, ge7-sut8 e5 kam2-siu7 kap him-siong2, bo5 hoat-tou7 phiah-bian2 e5, ke-kiam2 e7 tui3 A-hoa5 chit chan5 tai7-chi3 cho7-seng5 eng2-hiong2. Ge7-sut8 chhong3-chok kap him-siong2 u7 bo5-han7 kho2-leng5 e5 khong-kan.

Goa2 the5-chhiN2 ka-ki7, na7 A-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3 hou7 goa2 u7 ge7-sut8-seng3 e5 khip-in2-lek8 e5 si5, goa2 ai3 giam5-keh lai5 chun-siu2 1 koa2 ki-pun2 goan5-chek. To7-si7 tui3 1 e5 lang5, iong7 siong7 thong3-khou2 e5 hong-hoat hong7-hian3 chhut i e5 seN3-mia7, goa2 pit-su iong7 giam5-siok e5 sim-cheng5 lai5 bin7-tui3 chit e5 lang5, chit e5 lang5 e5 seN3-mia7, chit e5 lang5 e5 si2-bong5. Goa2 chhian-ban7 boe7-tang3 be5-sit8 ti7 ge7-sut8-seng3 e5 khip-in2-lat8 lai7-toe2, hou7 cheng5-su5 loan7 chau2, kiaN5 m7 tioh8 lou7. Na7 an2-ne, goa2 m7-taN7 tui3 put khi2 A-hoa5, ma7 e7 siong tioh8 ka-ki7 kap ki5-tha e5 lang5.
