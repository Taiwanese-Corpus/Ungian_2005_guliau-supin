Goan5-chok: Chan Sim-Gi5, 1989,11,2
Hoan-ek8: Loonng X, 1998,8,5


<Chian5-gian5>
Lan2 tiaN7-tiaN7 "thiaN tioh8", ma7 tiaN7-tiaN7 "kong2 tioh8" e5 kong-gi7, ai3 kap chun-giam5, m7-koh lan2 kam2 u7 lok8-sit8 ka7 cho3 kau3?  Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7 siong7, jiok8-se3-chia2 e5 koan5-ek lan2 kam2 u7 lok8-sit8 khi3 the3 in cheng-chhu2?

Goa2 chit chun7 giah8 ti5-tau5, taN taN3-a2 e5 si5, tiaN7-tiaN7 teh su-kho2 chit koa2 bun7-toe5, Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7 e5 jiok8-se3-chia2 ti7 to2-ui7? In si7 an2-choaN2 na2 e7 pian3 choe3 jiok8-se3-chia2?  Che si7 siaN2-mih lang5 cho7-seng5 e5?  Si7 siaN2-mih tai7-chi3 ian2-pian3 kau3 an2-ne? Chit-ma2 goa2 m7 kaN2 u7 siaN2-mih kiat-lun7. Goa2 ka-ki7 teng7 1 e5 hong-hiong3, poah8-to2 lai5 chiaN5-cho3 jiok8-se3 e5 lang5, goa2 khia7 tiam3 hia ka7 in tau3-saN-kang7.
--An3 Chiam Ek-hoa5 sia2 hou7 kaN-gak8 lai7 Chhoa3 Iu2-choan5 e5 phoe in2 chhut-lai5

<Kan2-kai3>
A-hoa5, pun2-mia5 Chiam Ek-hoa5, 32 hoe3, bo5 chhoa7. 1957 ni5 2 goeh8 22 seN. Gin2-a2 si5-tai7 ti7 Ka-gi7-koan7 Tek-kia7 toa7-han3. Siau3-lian5 e5 si5, lai5 Tai5-pak thak8-chheh, Liong5-hoa5 Kang-choan thak8 bo5 pit8-giap8. I te2-te2 e5 seN3-mia7 lai7-toe2, jin5-seng siong7 boe2-a2 e5 4 tang choan5-pou7 hong7-hian3 hou7 hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7, keng-koe3 Khou2 Sin3-liong5 chiap-ki su7-kiaN7 hou7 kun-jin5 keng2-chhat lai5 phah tioh8-siong. Ti7 tong2-goa7 kong-cheng3-hoe7 tiam7-tiam7-a2 hong7-hian3 ka-ki7. Chham-u2 Bin5-chin3-tong2 kian3-tong2, bat ka7 Iu5 Chheng, Chia7 Tiong5-teng5 cho7u-soan2. Ma7 bat ti7 Khou Eng5-siok kap TeN7 Lam5-iong5 sou2 pan7 e5 chap8-chi3-sia7 tam-jim7 hoat-heng5 e5 khang-khoe3. I choan5-sim tau5-jip8 "Khou2 Cho5-tek, Chhoa3 Iu2-choan5 Tai5-tok8-an3" choan5-to2 siaN-goan5 e5 oah8-tang7. 520 long5-bin5 chheng2-goan7 su7-kiaN7 tiong, thiah Lip8-hoat-iN7 e5 khanpang. 1989 ni5 4 goeh8 chhoe 7, i ti7 Ko-hiong5-koan7 Long5-koan5-hoe7 chai-iaN2 TeN7 Lam5-iong5 chu7-hun5, sui5 chiuN7 pak, chhiat-tng7 i sim-lai7 sou2 kah-i5 e5 lam5 Tai5-oan5 long5-un7, chhau2-kin kang-chok. 4~5 goeh8 e5 si5 chun2-pi7 hian3-sin lai5 si2. 519 hit kang, sin-khu teng2 phaiN7 su7-sian chhoan5 ho2 e5 khi3-iu5, poe5 TeN7 Lam5-iong5 song7-le2 e5 tui7-ngou2, kiaN5 oan5 choan5-teng5. Chong2-tok-hu2 thau5-cheng5 e5 tin3-pok8 pou7-tui7 hiong3 ho5-peng5 e5 bin5-chiong3 phun3-chui2, in2-khi2 hun3-nou7.  A-hoa5 in2-hoe2 chu7-hun5, phak-tiam3 chhih seN kah bat8-bat8 e5 thih-soaN3-bang7-a2 teng2. Ui3 hit e5 si5-chun7 khai-si2, i e5 seN3-mia7 i2-keng ian5-siok8 ti7 lan2 mui5 1 e5 lang5 e5 sim-koaN lai7.
