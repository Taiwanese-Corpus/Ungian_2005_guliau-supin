Toh8-hoe2 e5 iah8-a2 (2)

Goan5-chok: Chan Sim-Gi5, 1989,11,2
Hoan-ek8: Loonng X, 1998,8,5

Ku7-ni5 (1988), Bin5-chin3-tong2 tong2-kheng3 e5 si5, ti7 Tai5-pak Su7-lim5 e5 hui3-ho5-to7 pan7 oan5-iu5-hoe7. Oan5-iu5-hoe7 lai5, mui5 1 e5 thoan5-the2 ka-ki7 pai5 taN-a2, u7 e5 boe7 mih8-kiaN7, u7 e5 siat-ke3 1 koa2 chhit-tho5 e5 oah8-tang7.  Goa2 u7-si5-a2 ka-ki7 1 e5 chau2-khi3 seh8, u7-si5-a2 kap peng5-iu2 cho3-hoe2 khi3.  U7 1 kai2 ti7 oan5-iu5-hoe7 lai7 khi3 tu2 tioh8 Ong5 Seng-hong5. I si7 Ko-hiong5-koan7, chin lau7-chu-keh e5 bin5-chu2 un7-tong7-chia2. I ka7 goa2 kong2, i kap Te3 Chin3-iau7 siuN7 beh tiam3 Ko-hiong5 A-lian5-hiong pan7 1 koa2 bin5-chu2 e5 cho7-tam5-hoe7 lai5 tham3-tho2 long5-bin5 bun7-toe5, i ng3-bang7 goa2 e7-tang3 khi3 kang2-ian2.

Chu7 goa2 sou2 chai, Chiam Ek-hoa5 kap Te3 Chin3-iau7 chin ba7, chin choe7 si5-chun7 long2 kap i teh cho3-hoe2. Giah8-thau5 hiong3 Ong5 Seng-hong5 in e5 taN-a2-ui7 khoaN3 koe3 khi3, tu2 ho2 khoaN3 tioh8 Chiam Ek-hoa5.

I the2-keh bo5 siaN2 koan5, khoaN3 khi3-lai5 tiam7-tiam7, u7 1 tiam2-a2 pi3-su3, goa2 chai-iaN2 hoe2-soaN3 iah boe7 pok-hoat chin3-cheng5 to7 si7 an2-ne tiam7-tiam7 koh pi3-su3. Goa2 siuN7 tioh8, ti7 "Khou2 Cho5-tek Chhoa3 Iu2-choan5 siaN-goan5-hoe7" oah8-tong7 e5 boe2-a2, goa2 bat kap i chha2 kah peng2 khi3. Nau7 kah bo5 hoat-tou7 siu-soah e5 si5, goa2 to7 chhoe7 Khu Gi7-jin5 lai5 cho3 kong-chhin. Au7-lai5, thiaN peng5-iu2 kong2, pat8-lang5 ma7 u7 1 koa2 lui7-su7 e5 cheng5-heng5, Khu Gi7-jin5 it-tit8 si7 Chiam Ek-hoa5 kap gun2 chi-kan e5 ban5-leng5-tan. HoaiN5-tit8 chit khoan2 oan-ke to7 chhan-chhiuN7 "ka-lai7-su7" kang7-khoan2, chha2 bong2 chha2, tau3-te2 iah si7 1 ke-hoe2-a2. M7-koh, chha2 koe3 liau2-au7, sim-lai7 iah si7 e7 kam2-kak ngai7-gioh8-ngai7-gioh8. Goa2 ka7 Ong5 Seng-hong5 kong2, goa2 boe7 siaN2 e7-hiau2 ian2-kang2, goa2 e7-sai2 kong2 1 koa2 goa2 ko3-jin5 chham-u2 cheng3-ti7 khong3-cheng e5 keng-giam7, ma7 e7-tang3 ka7 goa2 sou2 chhai2-hong2 e5 1 koa2 cheng3-ti7 an3-kiaN7 e5 chu-liau7 hou7 chham-u2 e5 lang5 chai-iaN2. Goa2 koh tiat8-piat8 ka7 hoan-hu3, goa2 beh khi3 kang2-ian2 e5 tai7-chi3, su7-sian ai3 tit tioh8 Chiam Ek-hoa5 e5 tong5-i3.

Ong5 Seng-hong5 kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3, ui7 siaN2-mih ai3 seng tit tioh8 Chiam Ek-hoa5 e5 tong5-i3? I ka7 goa2 kong2 pan7 oah8-tong7 si7 thoan5-the2 e5 tai7-chi3, Chiam Ek-hoa5 chi2-si7 ki5-tiong e5 1 e5 lang5 nia7-tiaN7.

Goa2 ka7 Ong5 Seng3-hong5 kong2, goa2 bat kap Chiam Ek-hoa5 oan-ke, khiong2-kiaN-e5 i bo5 hoan-geng5 goa2 khi3. Ji5-chhiaN2, goa2 chin chun-tiong7 tong2-kang hiaN-ti7 kap chhau2-kin e5 kang-chok-chia2. Goa2 chin keng3-tiong7 Chiam Ek-hoa5 kap Te3 Chin3-iau7 in sou2 teh cho3 e5 chhau2-kin khang-khoe3. Chin choe7 hong-bin7 goa2 long2 toe3 boe7 tioh8 pat8-lang5, iau-chhiaN2 goa2 khi3 ian2-kang2, kong2 khi2-lai5 ma7 u7 1 koa2 sim-hi, m7-koh goa2 chin hoaN-hi2 e5-tang3 chioh chit e5 ki-hoe7 lai5 kap un7-tong7 e5 sianpai kap chhau2-kin kang-chok-chia2 lai5 sek8-sai7.

Ong5 Seng3-hong5 it-tit8 kong2 Chiam Ek-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3 bo5 bun7-toe5, i tng2-khi3 kau3 Ko-hiong5 kap peng5-iu2 an-pai5 ho2 si5-kan chiah koh kap goa2 lian5-lok8.

Bo5 siuN7 tioh8 hit kai2, Ong5 Seng3-hong5 iau-chhiaN2 goa2 khi3 Ko-hiong5 ian2-kang2, keng3-jian5 pian3-choe3 Chiam Ek-hoa5 chu7-hun5 liau2-au7, goa2 siau3-liam7 i, ui7 i sia2 chheh e5 khai-si2.

1998
