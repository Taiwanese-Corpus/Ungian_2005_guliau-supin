
 
PhaiN2-siu-seng5, bang7 au7-tang
Tan5 Gi7-jin5 ek8 
Teng2-ni5 joah8--lang5 siu-koah e5 ki5-kan, tu2 tioh8 u2-ki5, toa7-hou7 lian5-soa3 loh8 kui2-na7 kang, chin-choe7 tiu7-a2 long2 gou7 liau2 siu-koah e5 jit8-chi2.  Toa7-che2 e5 tiu7-a2, ia7 in-ui7 hou7-chui2 chim3 siuN-ku2, bian2-kiong2 siu-koah liau2, koh bo5 jit8 thang phak8, siuN7 chin7 pan7-hoat pou2-kiu3, toa7-pou7-hun7 iu5-goan5 siN-kou, hoat-ge5.  M7-na7 siu-seng5 bai2, koh ai3 si3-koe3 pai3-thok chiah be7 e7-chhut--khi3, koh kan-na7 be7 tioh8 kong-teng7-ke3 e5 chit8-poaN3.  
Toa7-che2 mui2-pai2 tng2--lai5, kong2 tioh8 chit-e5 tai7-chi3, na5-au5-kng2 chiu7 tiN7.  A-bu2 long2 an-ui3 toa7-che2 kong2: “phaiN2-siu-seng5, bang7 au7-tang.  Lan2 cho3-sit-lang5, pun2-lai5 chiu7-si7 ai3 kap thiN taN2-tou2, tu2-tioh8 hong-thai kap u2-ki5 eng2 e7.  M7-ku2, lan2 cho3-sit-lang5, khah u7 te2”.  
Toa7-che2-hu bo5 chan3-seng5 a-bu2 e5 kong2-hoat, chiu7 kong2: “Tioh8 lah!” PhaiN2-siu-seng5 bang7 au7-tang lah, tan7-si7 chit8-tang bang7 koe3 chit8-tang, goa2 ia7 bang7 kau3 40 goa7 beh 50 hoe3 ah, m7-si7 long2 kang7-khoan2.  Khiam7-chiah8 khiam7-eng7, kan-na7 than3 tioh8 chhi7 kui2-e5 gin2-a2 toa7-han3.  Beh khi2 chit8-keng-a2 phou2-thong-chhu3, to7 ai3 ka7 long5-hoe7 tai7-khoaN2, heng5 kui2-ni5 ah, iau2 teh thoa.  
Liau2-au7 toa7-che2-hu kai2-pian3 keng-chok, thiaN kong2 cheng3-chhai3 e5 siu-ek iau2 sng3 boe7-bai2, chiu7 iong7-sim gian2-kiu3 an2-choaN2 cheng3, an2-choaN2 koan2-li2, put-toan7 kap u7 keng-giam7 e5 lang5 tho2-lun7, 5 hun-te7 choan5-pou7 kai2 cheng3-chhai3. Cheng3-chhai3 chin kau7-kang, tak8-jit8 bo5-si5 eng5, bo5 hoat-tou7 chhut-khi3 choe3-kang, chiu7 choan-sim it-i3 kap toa7-che2 cheng2-li2 chhai3-hng5.  
Te7 it pai2 beh cheng3 siaN2 ? Toa7-che2-hu kap toa7-che2 chham-siong5 liau2 koh chham-siong5, sng3 liau2 koh sng3, siuN7 kong2 cheng3 chhai3-thau5 e7 tu2 tioh8 ho2 si5-ki, tan7-si7 jin5-soan3 put-ju5 si5-un7, siu-seng5 e5 si5, sang3 khi3 kang-chhiuN2, chit8 kong-kin be7 bo5 chit8-khou, bian2-bian2 kiong2-kiong2 pun2-chiN5 siu--tng2-lai5, sou2-u7 tau5-jip8 si5-kan kap khui3-lat8 long2 ou-iu2--khi3.  
Toa7-che2 siuN7 kong2, te7-it pai2 khah bo5 keng-giam7, tong3-cho3 than3 tioh8 keng-giam7 to7 ho2! Chit8-cham7-a2 e5 sit-bang7 liau2 au7, koh chhiong-moa2 ng3-bang7 kai2 cheng3 ko-le5-chhai3.  Kiat-ko2 lu2 hai7, lian5 pun2-chiN5 to siu boe7 tng2--lai5.  
Khut-kiong5 koh kou3-chip e5 toa7-che2-hu, chin m7 kam-goan7, koat-sim koh cheng3 chit8-pai2 ko-le5-chhai3.  Chit-pai2 ke3-siau3 u7 lio5-a2 khah-koan5, goan2 long2 the3 toa7-che2 hoaN-hi2, ka7 toa7-che2 kun2-chhio3 kong2: “Chit-pai2 hou7 lin2 teh--tioh8 ah.”  Toa7-che2 ma7 hoaN-hi2 kah, kho2-sioh khi3-hau7 put-tui3, thang5-hai7 koh choe7, toa7-toa7 kiam2 siu.  
Ke3-siok8 cheng3-chhai3 neh ?  A2-si7 koh tng2-lai5 cheng3 tiu7-a2 ?  It-hiong3 cho3-su7 chin-u7 chu7-sin3 kap phek-lek8 e5 toa7-che2-hu, hoe-sim song3-chi3 khai-si2 tiu5-tu5, ia7 san2-seng gi5-hek8 (gi5-bun7, be5-hek8, siuN7-boe7-hiau2): “lan2 cho3-sit-lang5 ka7 lang5 be2 mih8-kiaN7, ke3-chiN5 chai7 lang5 khui, chit8-chiam to boe7-kiam2--tit; lan2-e5 long5-san2-phin2 beh be7, ke3-chiN5 ia7-si7 sui5-chai7 lang5 hoah, chit8-chiam to boe7-ke--tit.  Chin ki5-koai3.”  
Tiu5-tu5 to tiu5-tu5, siuN7-boe7-hiau2 to siuN7-boe7-hiau2, chhan5-hng5 chong2 boe7-sai2 pang3 hou7 pha-hng ah! Toa7-che2 oh8 a-bu2 e5 khau2-khi3 kong2: “PhaiN2-siu-seng5, kou-put-chiong tioh8 bang7 au7-tang, bo5 beh an2-choaN2 ?”
Toa7-che2-hu kam2-khai3 kong2: “Lang5 kong2 liau2-chiN5 seng-li2 bo5-lang5 cho3, lan2 m7-si7 siong5-siong5 teh cho3 hihN ?  phaiN2 siu-seng5 bang7 au7-tang, ho2 lah! Bang7 au7-tang lah!” 
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 15 ki5, 1992 ni5 3 goeh8)
