
 
Goa2 m7-eng7 sok-ka-te7-a2
Tan5 Gi7-jin5
I3-tai7-li7 teng7 chit8 e5 bok8-phiau, kau3 1990 ni5 e5 si5, chiu7 choan5-kok m7 seng-san2 ia7 m7 su2-iong7 sok-ka-te7-a2, si7 se3-kai3 siong7 te7-it-e5 kong-khai piau2-si7 ku7-choat8 sok-ka-te7-a2 e5 kok-ka.
Sok-ka-te7-a2 u7 sok-ka sou2-u7 e5 bun7-te5, che3-cho7 e5 koe3-theng5 giam5-tiong7 u3-jiam2, su2-iong7 au7, chiaN5-cho3 siong7 giam5-tiong7 e5 lo3-so3.
Kiam2-chio2 su2-iong7 sok-ka-te7-a2, u7 chit8-koa2 hong-hoat:
1.Be2 mih8-kiaN7, ka-ki7 chah e7-te2--tit e5 mih8-kiaN7, sim7-chi3 chhai3-na5-a2. Chin7-liong7 kiam2-chio2 bo5-pit-iau3 e5 sok-ka-te7-a2, ia7 khou2-khng3 tiam3-thau5-ke khah-chio2 hou7 kou3-kheh sok-ka-te7-a2. 
2.Soan2-tek8 su2-iong7-choa2-te7 e5 siong-tiam3, kian3-gi7 su2-iong7-sok-ka-te7-a2 e5 siong-tiam3 kai2-eng7 choa2-te7-a2.
3. M7-thang eng7 sok-ka-te7-a2 te2 chiah8-mih8, iu5-ki5 si7 te2 sio-thng3-thng3--e5, e7 chau2 tok8 chhut--lai5. 
4.Chit8 e5 sok-ka-te7-a2, eng3-kai ke eng7 kui2-pai2.
5. Chheng-khi3 e5 sok-ka-te7-a2 siu khi2--lai5, e7-tang3 sang3 hou7 be7 bou2 chit8-koa2 mih8-kiaN7 e5 seng-li2-lang5, chhin-chhiuN7 te2 e7-tang3 pak phoe5 e5 chui2-ko2, a2-si7 theh8 tng2--lai5 ai3 koh se2 e5 chhai3-se, ia7 m7-thang ku7-choat8 seng-li2-lang5 hou7 li2 m7-si7 choan5 sin, tan7-si7 chheng-khi3 e5 sok-ka-te7-a2.
6. Po2-sian2-mouh8 ia7 sng3 si7 sok-ka-te7-a2, Beh lok toa3 peng-siuN e5 chiah8-mih8, chin7-liong7 eng7 u7 ap8-a2-koa3 e5 mih8-kiaN7 te2--le3. 
7. Po2-sian2-mouh8 ia7 chin7-liong7 m7-thang kham3 sio, u7-tok8.
8. Be7-tang3 koh eng7 e5 sok-ka-te7-a2, chun2-kong2 beh hiat-kak8, ia7 ka lok cho3 kui te7, hou7 siu hoe5 chai3-che3 e5 lang5 e7-tang3 kui-e5 theh8--khi3.
Chit-chun7 kio3 lan2 kiam2-chio2 su2-iong7 sok-ka-te7-a2 chu7-jian5 si7 chin thong3-khou2 e5 tai7-chi3, tan7-si7, lan2 khoaN3-kiN3 sok-ka-te7-a2 lo3-so3 hiah-ni7 choe7, that toa3 pai5-chui2 khau2, cho7-seng5 chui2-chai, kap ki5-thaN tak8-chiong2 e5 chai-hai7, boe7-sai2-tit bo5 koan-sim.
Tong-jian5, siong7 thiat-te2 e5 hong-hoat, iu5-goan5 si7 cheng3-hu2 eng3-kai lip8-hoat, teng7 ki5-han7, kim3-chi2 seng-san2 kap hoan3-be7 sok-ka-te7-a2, chin7-liong7 eng7 ki5-thaN e5 mih8-kiaN7 tai7-the3, ia7 kiam2-chio2 bo5-pit-iau3 e5 pau-chong.
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 1 ki5, 1989 ni5 11 goeh8)
