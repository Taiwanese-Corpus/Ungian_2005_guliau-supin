

 
Put-su2 sian-jin5 e5 pi-ai
Tan5 Gi7-jin5
Chin chhut-mia5 e5 gin2-a2 kou3-su7 “Ka-li2-bo7 iu5-ki3”, u7 chit8-cham7 ki3-chai3, bou2 chit8 sou2-chai7, u7 chit8-koa2 Put-su2 (eng2-oan2 boe7 si2) sian-jin5.  Chit-chiong2 lang5, chit8-e7 chhut-si3, thau5-hiah8 to3-peng5 to7 u7 chit8-liap8 iN5-pan.  Lang5 na7 khoaN3 tioh8 bou2-lang5 u7 chit-liap8 iN5-pan, sui5 chai-iaN2 chit-e5 lang5 eng2-oan2 boe7 si2.  Chit-chiong2-lang5 bo5 goa7-choe7, choan5-kok chha-put-to 1100 e5.  
 Ka-li2-bo7 chit8-e7 thiaN tioh8 u7 chit-khoan2 lang5, sui5-si5 piau2-si7 hui-siong5 e5 him-sian7.  I chiu7 hoat-piau2 ko-kian3, na7 i si7 chit8-e5 put-su2 sian-jin5, i beh an2-choaN2 an2-choaN2, i beh phah-piaN3 than3-chiN5, beh choan-sim gian2-kiu3 kho-hak8 kap ge7-sut8, beh chin7-sim, kong-cheng3 lai5 ki3-chai3 koe3-khi3 sou2 hoat-seng e5 tai7-chi3, an2-ni i e7 chiaN5-cho3 ti3-sek kap ti3-hui7 e5 oah8-po2-poe3, chiaN5-cho3 choan5-kok e5 sian-ti.  I beh poe5-iuN2, chi2-to7 chheng-lian5 lang5 e5 sim-leng5, ti7 keng-che3-siong7, ia7 beh chin7 sou2-e7 pang-chan7 lang5 . . . . .  
 Ka-li2-bo7 kong2 liau2, hit-e5 kok-ka e5 lang5 long2 tong3-boe7-tiau5 chhio3 kah ki7-ka7-kio3.  In chi2 chhut i-e5 chho3-gou7, i sou2 ka2-siong2 e5 seng-oah8, m7-na7 bo5 hap8-li2, ia7 bo5 sek-tong3.  In-ui7 i sou2 siu7--e5, si7 ka2-teng7 lang5 u7 eng2-oan2 e5 chheng-chhun, eng2-oan2 kian7-khong, eng7-boe7-chin7 e5 oah8-lek8.  Tan7-si7, che si7 bo5-kho2-leng5.  In chiu7 ka7 Ka-li2-bo7 soeh-beng5, chiah-e5 put-su2 sian-jin5 sit8-che3 e5 cheng5-heng5.  
Put-su2 sian-jin5, chit8-pah-ni5 ia7 han2-tit e7 chhut-si3 nng7-saN-e5, in e5 it-ki2 it-tong7 kap phou2-thong-lang5 long2 kang7-khoan2.  30 hoe3 i2-au7 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 iu-ut, song3-chi3, tit8-kau3 80 hoe3.  Tng in oah8 kau3 it-poaN e5 lang5 siong7-koan5 e5 ni5-hoe3 80 hoe3 e5 si5, in m7-na7 u7 it-poaN lau7-lang5 sou2-u7 e5 gu5-gong7 kap jiok8-tiam2, koh in-ui7 in hoan5-lo2 ka-ki7 eng2-oan2 boe7-si2, sou2-i2 u7 koh khah choe7 e5 ma5-hoan5.  In pian3 kah chin kou3-chip, gau5 siu7-khi3, tham-sim, iu-chhiu5, chap8-liam7, boe7 sit8-chai7, boe7 ho2 khoan2-thai7 peng5-iu2 . . . . .  In siong7 beng5-hian2 e5 au3-nau2 chiu7-si7, oan3-tou3 kap bu5-leng5.  In oan3-tou3 e5 bok8-phiau na2-chhiuN7 si7 it-poaN siau3-lian5 lang5 e5 phaiN2-sip8-koan3 kap it-poaN lau7-lang5 e5 si2.  In chek-pi7 siau3-lian5 lang5 e5 sip8-koan3, ka-ki7 toan7-choat8 it-chhe3 e5 hiong2-siu7; tng in khoaN3 pat8-lang5 chhut-chong3, in chiu7 oan3-than3 chai7 sim-te2, kong2 pat8-lang5 u7 phiah-hong-kang2, kan-na7 in jip8-kang2 bo5-bang7.  Tu5 liau2 ti7 chheng-lian5 kap tiong-lian5 e5 si5 sou2 oh8 sou2 khoaN3--e5 i2-goa7, in bo5 jim7-ho5 chheng-chho2 e5 ki3-ek8.  Sou2-i2, in tui3 tai7-chi3 e5 liau2-kai2, long2 leng5-kho2 thiaN lang5 kong2.  In tiong-kan khah bo5 hiah chhi-chham2--e5, na2-chhiuN7 si7 hiah-e5 lau7 kah oan5-choan5 song3-sit ki3-ek8 e5 lang5.  In e7 tit-tioh8 khah choe7 e5 tong5-cheng5 kap pang-chan7, in-ui7 khah bo5 phaiN2-phiah. In na7 kau3 80 hoe3, ti7 hoat-lut8-siong7 chiu7 sng3 cho3 si7 si2-lang5 ah; in-e5 ke3-seng5-jin5 chiu7 ke3-seng5 in-e5 chai5-san2, kan-na7 lau5 tam7-poh8-a2 chiN5 hou7 in seng-oah8, chiah-e5 kho2-lian5 e5 lang5 to7 ai3 kho3 kong-hui3 lai5 bu2-iong2 ah.  Kau3 chit si5-chun7, pat8-lang5 chiu7 jin7-ui7 in bo5-hoat-tou7 chiong5-su7 sin3-thok a2-si7 than3-chiN5 e5 kang-chok.  Be7-tang3 be2, ia7 be7-tang3 seng5-chou thou2-te7; be7-tang3 ui7 jim7-ho5 e5 an3-kiaN7 cho3-cheng3, m7-na7 be7-tang3 ui7 bin5-su7 heng5-su7 an3-kiaN7 cho3-cheng3, lian5 siong7 se3-hang7 e5 tai7-chi3 to bo5 koat-teng7-koan5.  
Kau3 90 hoe3, chhui3-khi2 lak-liau2-liau2, thau5-khak kng-lu-lu, i2-keng boe7 hun-piat8 kiam5-sng-khou2-chiaN2, kan-na7 chiah8 e7-sai2 chiah8--e5, lim e7-sai2 lim--e5, bo5 sit8-iok8, ia7 bo5 ui7-khau2.  Kin2 kam2-jiam2--tioh8 e5 piN7, iu5-goan5 kam2-jiam2--tioh8.  In ki3-boe7-tiau5 tai7-chi3 kap pat8-lang5 e5 mia5, lian5 chi3-chhin kap ho2-iu2 e5 mia5 long2 boe7-ki3.  Ia7 in-ui7 chit8-ku3-oe7 to cheng5-au7 ki3-boe7-tiau5, sou2-i2, lian5 in chit-chun7 i5-it e5 siau-khian2 (chiu7-si7 thak8-chheh) ia7 bo5 hoat-tou7.  
In-ui7 kak-ka e5 gu2-gian5 put-toan7 teh pian3-chhian, sou2-i2 bo5-kang7 si5-tai7 e5 put-su2 sian-jin5 hou7-siong7 kong2-oe7 ia7 oh-tit liau2-kai2, kap phou2-thong e5 lang5 kong2-oe7 ia7 kang7-khoan2.  An2-ni, in-e5 seng-oah8 keng3-ka put-pian7, in pian3-cho3 na2 goa7-kok-lang5 leh.  
ThiaN tioh8 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3 liau2-au7, Ka-li2-bo7 ma2-siong7 sit-khi3 tui-kiu5 eng2-oan2 boe7-si2 e5 iok8-bong7.  
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 16 ki5, 1992 ni5 5 goeh8)
