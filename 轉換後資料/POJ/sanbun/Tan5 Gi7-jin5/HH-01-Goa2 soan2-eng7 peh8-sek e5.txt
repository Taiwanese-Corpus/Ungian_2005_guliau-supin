
 
Goa2 soan2-eng7 peh8-sek e5 oe7-seng-choa2
Tan5 Gi7-jin5
 Hah8 ti7 seng-thai3 goan5-chek e5 seng-oah8, Peng7 m7-si7 ai3 li2 tioh8 hong3-khi3 sou2-u7 bi2-le7 e5 mih8-kiaN7, si7 kong2, tioh8 ai3 u7 soan2-tek8-seng3. Ti7 boe2 saN-a2-khou3, boe2 bi2-sut8-teng a2-si7 lim-chiah8 e5 hui5-khi3, poe-a2, teng2 chiah e5 oe7-tang3 su2-iong7 khah ku2, koh oe7-tang3 eng7 liau2 koh chai3 eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7, bu5-hong5 kho2-i2 khah kang2-kiu3 chit8-tiam2-a2, chu3-tiong7 in e5 sek-chhai2 khoan2-sit teng2 teng2.
 Oe7-seng-choa2 oan5-choan5 si7 chit8 chiong2 bin5-seng pit-su-phin2, ji5-chhiaN2 eng7 chit8 pai2 chiu7 tan3 tiau7, na7 soan2-eng7 u7 sek-chhai2 u7 hoe-iuN7--e5, oan5-choan5 si7 chu7-ngou2 hi-eng5-sim e5 koan-he7, bo5 jim7-ho5 sit8-chit e5 i3-gi7, sim7-chi3 tui3 khoan5-keng2 iu2 hai7.
 Peh8-sek i2-goa7 e5 oe7-seng-choa2, bo5-lun7 ap-hoe in3-hoe a2-si7 ni2-sek, long2 su-iau3 ke chit8 e5 chhiu2-siok8, it-teng7 ai3 eng7 chit8-koa2 leng5-goan5 lai5 oan5-seng5 chit e5 chhiu2-siok8, koh eng7 tiau7 ni2-liau7, phang-cheng, put lun7 in si7 jin5-kang hap8-seng5 a2-si7 thian-jian5--e5, long2 si7 bo5 i3-gi7 e5 chu-goan5 long7-hui3.
 Peh8-sek e5 oe7-seng-choa2 chun2-kong2 pian3 seng5 lo3-so3 liau2 au7, in si7 e7-thang chu7-jian5 hun-kai2 kap hiu2-noa7 e5 lo3-so3, ju5-ko2 in e5 teng2-bin7 u7 ni2-liau7 a2-si7 phang-cheng, chiah-e5 hoa3-hak8 but8-phin2 chiu7 e7 u3-jiam2 thou5-jiong2 a2-si7 chui2-goan5.
Ho5-hong2, u7 sek e5 oe7-seng-choa2 khah kui3, tan7-si7, in-ui7 oe7-seng-choa2 e5 ke3-siau3 boe7-tang3 be7 kah chin koan5. Sou2-i2 chiu7 bo5 kho2-leng5 eng7 ko-kip e5 thian-jian5 ni2-liau7 a2-si7 phang-cheng, mai3 kong2 chiah-e5 hoa3-hak8 but8-phin2 tui3 jin5-the2 si7 m7-si7 iu2 hai7, chi3-chio2 in e5 sek-chhai2 kap phang-bi7 long2 si7 hui-siong5 chhou-siok8, put-ju5 soan2 eng7 peh8-sek e5 oe7-seng-choa2, keng-che3, koh an-choan5, koh tui3 khoan5-keng2 iu2 ek.
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 1 ki5, 1989 ni5 11 goeh8)
