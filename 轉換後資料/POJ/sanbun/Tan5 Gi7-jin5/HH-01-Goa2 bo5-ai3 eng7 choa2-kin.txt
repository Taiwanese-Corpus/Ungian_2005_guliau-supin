
Goa2 bo5-ai3 eng7 choa2-kin, choa2-poaN5, choa2-poe
Tan5 Gi7-jin5 ek8
Che3 choa2 si7 u3-jiam2 chin li7-hai7 e5 kang-giap8, tui3 chui2, tui3 khong-khi3, long2 u7 eng2-hiong2.
 Lan2 su2-iong7 choa2-kin, choa2-poaN5, choa2-poe, u7-si5 si7 in-ui7 lang5-kheh siuN choe7, chhu3-lai7 bo5 hiah-ni7 choe7 e5 chhan-kin a2-si7 poe-a2, poaN5-a2, ia7 u7-si5 si7 chhiaN2 lang5-kheh, pun7-toaN7 se2 hiah-ni7 choe7 e5 poe-a2 poaN5-a2. In-ui7 chhan-kin, poe-a2, poaN5-a2 bo5-kau3 chiah eng7 choa2--e5 iau2 bian2-kiong2 e7-kong2--tit, chong2-si7, ia7 eng3-kai phiah-bian2 su2-iong7 in3-soat chin iam7-le7 e5 choa2-khi3.
 Jit8-siong5 ka-teng5 seng-oah8, eng3-kai eng7 mi5--e5, a2-si7 moa5-chit e5 chhan-kin lai5 chhu2-tai7 choa2-kin, eng7 toh-pou3 lai5 chhu2-tai7 eng7 choa2 chhit, eng7 po-le5 kap hui5--e5 chhan-ku7 lai5 chhu2-tai7 choa2-poe choa2-poaN5, in-ui7 chiah-e5 mih8-kiaN7 m7-na7 e7-tang3 eng7 liau2 koh chai3-eng7, chiah-e5 mih8-kiaN7 ia7 khah u7 chit-kam2.
 It-poaN e5 chhan-thiaN, tai7-to-sou3 su2-iong7 hui5-khi3 kap po-le5-poe, u7 e5 taN3-a2 si7 su2-iong7 phou-li2-long2 chhan-ku7, koh choe3-kin7 chin liu5-heng5 e5 sok-sit8-tiam3, long2 si7 su2-iong7 choa2-poaN5 choa2-poe, ia7 su2-iong7 sok-ka kap phou-li2-long2 san2-phin2. Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 long7-hui3 choa2-kin choa2-poe choa2-te7-a2 .... e5 sou2-chai7, ia7 si7 koan-sim khoan5-keng2 e5 lang5 bo5 eng3-kai khi3 e5 sou2-chai7.  

(Hong-hiong3 Te7 1 ki5, 1989 ni5 11 goeh8)
