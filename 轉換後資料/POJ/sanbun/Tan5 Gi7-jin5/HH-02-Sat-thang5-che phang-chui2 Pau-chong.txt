
 
Sat-thang5-che si7 phang-chui2 Pau-chong e5 long5-ioh8
Tan5 Gi7-jin5 ek8
Kong2 tioh8 long5-ioh8, lan2 long2 chin kiaN, tan7-si7, chin chio2 lang5 chai-iaN2, sat-thang5-che put-ko3 si7 u7 koa3 phang-chui2 pau-chong e5 long5-ioh8, keng3-jian5 tak8-e5 ka-teng5 long2 tai7-liong7 teh su2-iong7.  
Na7-si7 chhu3-lai7-bin7 kap hu3-kin7 e5 khoan5-keng2 chin chheng-khi3, ia7 bo5 khng3 chin-ku2 e5 lo3-so3-tui (Mui2 chit8 e5 ka-teng5 long2 ai3 chin hap8-chok, ti7 chi2-teng7 e5 si5-kan chiah theh8 pau chin-an5 e5 lo3-so3 chhut--lai5).  Chiu7 e7 kiam2-chio2 bang2-a2 hou5-sin5 ka-choah8 kap niau2-chhi2.  
Chhu3-lai7 keng-siong5 po2-chhi5 chheng-khi3, bo5 si2-chui2 bih bang2-a2 hou5-sin5, bo5 si2-kak bih niau2-chhi2, bo5 ku7-po3-choa2 ku7-e5 mih8-kiaN7 tun2 kui-tui bih ka-choah8, chiu7 e7 kiam2-chio2 chiah-e5 thang5-hai7.  
Ka-ti7 e5 chhu3-lai7 chheng-khi3, chhu3-piN e5 khoan5-keng2 na7 bo5 chheng-khi3, in e5 hai7-thang5 ia7 e7 chau2-koe3--lai5, lan2 e7-sai2 eng7 e7-bin7 chit kui2-chiong2 pi2-kau3 khah an-choan5 koh chu7-jian5 e5 hong-hoat lai5 eng3-hu3:
1.Sat-bun5-chui2 e7-tang3 thai5-si2 ka-choah8.  Chiong chit8 poe e5 sat-bun5-si phau3 4 kong-seng e5 sio-chui2, he7 toa3 chui2-kau-a2, piah-kak, chin u7-hau7.  
2.Tui3-hu3 kau2-hia7, e7-sai2 ti7 chhu3-lai7 e5 chit8-koa2 kak-loh8, ia7 chit8-koa2 hou5-chio-hun2, a2-si7 cheng3 ku2-phun5 poh8-ho5-hioh8 lai5 koaN2-tiok8.  
3. Hong5-chi2 bi2-thang2, mi7-hun2-koan3 kap piaN2-koan3 seN-thang5, e7-sai2 khng3 chit8-koa2 ta e5 jiok8-kui3-hioh8 toa3 lai7-bin7.  
4.Hong5-chi2 hou5-sin5, e7-sai2 eng7 bang7-a2-mng5 bang7-a2-thang, pun3-so3-thang2 ai3 kham3-koa3, leng7-goa7 e7-sai2 ka-ti7 eng7 thng5-chiuN kap chui2 hun7-hap8 cho3 hou5-sin5-liam5.  Kang7-khoan2, niau2-chhi2-tauh ia7 pi2 niau2-chhi2-ioh8 koh khah an-choan5 u7-hau7.  
5.Tu5-pang5 po2-chhi5 chheng-khi3, iu5-ki5 si7 tu5-pang5 thou5-kha po2-chhi5 chheng-khi3, si7 koh khah tiong7-iau3.  Chiong peng5-sng-hun2 ia7 toa3 tu5-pang5 e5 phang7--nih kap chhu3-kak, e7-tang3 chiong ka-choah8 kap chit8-koa2 thang5 koaN2-chau2.  
Ju5-ko2 li2 sat-thang5-che eng7 liau2 koan3-si3, ia7 bo5-ai3 thiaN goa2 e5 kian3-gi7, ia7 m7-kiaN sat-thang5-che in2-khi2 koaN-chong7, phoe5-hu kap hou-khip he7-thong2 e5 piN7-cheng3, chi3-chio2 chhiaN2 li2, phun3 sat-thang5-che i2-au7, lang5 kap cheng-seN long2 eng3-kai li7-khui chit8-toaN7 si5-kan.  Sat ka-choah8 e5 ioh8-a2 kan-na e7-sai2 phun3 thou5-kha, choat8-tui3 be7-sai2 chhin-chhiuN7 phang-chui2 an2-ni loan7-phun3, put-tan7 bo5-hau7, koh giam5-tiong7 gui5-hai7 kian7-khong.  
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 2 ki5, 1990 ni5 1 goeh8)
