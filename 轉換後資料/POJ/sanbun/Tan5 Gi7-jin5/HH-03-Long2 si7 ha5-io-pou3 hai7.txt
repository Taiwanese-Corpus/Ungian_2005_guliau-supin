

 
Long2 si7 ha5-io-pou3 hai7--e5!
Tan5 Gi7-jin5 ek8
U7 chit8 e5 un2-su7 chiau3 in sai-hu7 e5 chi2-si7, khi3 kau3 chit8 e5 chin chio2 lang5 kiaN5-kha-kau3 e5 sou2-chai7, tah chit8 keng chin kan2-tan e5 se3 keng liau5-a2, ti hia toa3, ti7 hia cho3 leng5-seng3 e5 siu-lian7.  Tak8-jit8 cha2-khi2 se2-ek8 liau2 au7, chiu7 chiong tam5--e5 saN kap ha5-io e5 pou3 koa3 toa3 liau5-a2 piN e5 chhiu7-a2-teng2 phak8 hou ta.  
U7 chit8 jit8, khi3 hu3-kin7 e5 chng-lai7 ka7 lang5 pun chiah8--mih8 tng2--lai5, hoat-kak i e5 ha5-io e5 pou3 hou7 ia7-po5 ka7-phoa3 chit8 khang.  Keh-kang, i put-tek-put tioh8 khi3 chng-lai7 ka7 lang5 pun chit8 tiau5 sin--e5.  Koe3 kui2 kang, i chiong ha5-io-pou3 phak8 toa3 chhu3-teng2, chiau3-siong5 chhut-khi3 pun chiah8-mih8, tng2--lai5, ha5-io-pou3 iu7 koh hou7 ia7-po5 ka7 kah chhui3-kou5-kou5 ah.  
I chin au3-nau2.  Chng-lai7 e5 lang5 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Bo5 lang5 u7 hoat-tou7 tak8-jit8 long2 hou7 li2 chit8 tiau5 pou3, goa2 khoaN3-----kan-na chit8 e5 pan7-hoat.  Li2 e7-sai2 chhi7 chit8 chiah niau.  Ia7-po5 kiaN niau.” 
I chin-chiaN3 chhi7 chit8 chiah niau, tui3 hit-jit8 khi2, ia7-po5 bo5 koh lai5 kiau2-jiau2.  I chin hoaN-hi2, tak8-jit8 se3-ji7 chiau3-kou3 chit-chiah niau, ia7 ng3 chng-lai7 pun chit8-koa2 gu5-leng lai5 chhi7 chit-chiah niau.  
Koe3 kui2-jit8 au7, chng-lai7 e5 lang5 ka7 i kong2, ” Un2-su7 sian-siN ah, li2 tak8-jit8 ai3 gu5-leng, iong7 pun--e5 e7-tang3 chiah8 kui2-kang ? SiaN2-lang5 u7 hoat-tou7 kui-ni5 thang3-thiN kiong2-kip li2 gu5-leng--ah ?  Goa2 khoaN3---li2 tioh8 chhi7 chit8 chiah gu5.  Chiu7 li2 u7-thang chiah8, niau ia7 u7-thang chiah8.” 
Koe3 kui2-kang au7, un2-su7 tit-tioh8 chit8 chiah gu5, bian2 koh khi3 pun gu5-leng.  Koh lai5, i su-iau3 tho2 chit8-koa2 tiu7-chhau2 lai5 chhi7-gu5, chiu7 koh khi3 chng-lai7.  Lang5 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “Li2 e5 liau5-a2 piN u7 chin-choe7 khang3-te7, ka khai-khun2-khun2--leh, chiu7 m7-bian2 ui7-tioh8 chhi7-gu5 koh khi3 ka7 lang5 pun tiu7-chhau2 lah! ”
I chiu7 khai-si2 le5-chhan5.  Chiam7-chiam7, i tioh8 chhiaN3 chit8-koa2 kang-lang5.  Au7-lai5 kam2-kak u7 su-iau3 khi2 chit8 cho7 chhek-chhng.  Choe3-au7, i pian3-seng5 te7-chu2, chhoa7-bou2 seN-kiaN2, kap it-poaN e5 lang5 kang7-khoan2, tak8-jit8 bo5-eng5-chhih-chhih.
Koe3 chit8-toaN7 si5-kan, i e5 sai-hu7 lai5 chhoe7--i, khoaN3-tioh8 to3-chhu3 long2 si7 chap8-mih8 ka-khu7, siuN7 boe7-hiau2, chiu7 mng7 ti7 hia e5 lou5-pok8, “’ I2-cheng5 u7 chit8-e5 siu-to7-lang5 tah liau5-a2 toa3 ti7 chia, chit-ma2 poaN-tui3 to2-ui7 khi3 ah ?  “Lou5-pok8 boe7-hiau2 hoe5-tap.  Sai-hu7 chiu7 tit8-chiap jip8-khi3 chhu3-lai7, khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 tou5-te7 tu2 teh sng3 chiN5 sng3 li7-sek.  Sai-hu7 mng7 kong2, “Bo5 tou5-te7--ah, ia che si7 an2-choaN2. . . . . ? ” Tou5-te7 kian3-siau3 kah phak toa3 sai-hu7 e5 kha-cheng5 in3 kong2, “Sai-hu7--ah! Che long2 si7 hit tiau5 ha5-io-pou3 hai7--e5 lah! ”
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 3 ki5, 1990 ni5 3 goeh8)
