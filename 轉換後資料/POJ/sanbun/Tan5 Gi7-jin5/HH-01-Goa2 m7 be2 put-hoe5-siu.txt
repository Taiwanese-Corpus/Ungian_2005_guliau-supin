
 
Goa2 m7 be2 put-hoe5-siu iong5-khi3 e5 im2-liau7
Tan5 Gi7-jin5 ek8
Bi2-kok bat cho3 koe3 iu2-koan lo3-so3 bun7-te5 e5 tiau-cha, hoat-hian7 lo3-so3 chiam3 siong7 choe7 e5, keng3-jian5 si7 boe7-tang3 siu-hoe5 chai3-su2-iong7 e5 im2-liau7 iong5-khi3 (Te2 im2-liau7 e5 mih8-kiaN7), chiu7 che3-teng7 hoat-lut8 kui-teng7 sou2-u7 e5 im2-liau7 iong5-khi3 long2 ai3 siu hoe5, a2-si7 kui-teng7 ai3 su2-iong7 e7-tang3 eng7 liau2 koh eng7 e5 po-le5-koan3.  Kok lai7 chu7 chiong5 goa7-kok e5 lu7-poh8-pau (Goa7-bin7 si7 choa2-pau, lai7-bin7 si7 chit8-teng5 e5 lu7-poh8) kap Po2-tek8-koan3 (Chit8-chiong2 sok-ka-koan3-a2, phou2-thong te2 khi3-chui2) chiuN7-chhi7 liau2 au7, in-ui7 chin li7-pian7, chiu7 chin choe7 lang5 ai3 eng7. Bou2 chit8 keng sit8-phin2 kong-si kong-khai kong2, in i2-keng be7-chhut 10 ek e5 li7-poh8-pau, koh kun-ku3 thong-ke3, kok-lai7 mui2 ni5 eng7 tiau7 chit8-ek nng7-chheng-ban7 ki Po2-tek8-koan3. M7-na7 chin long7-hui3, koh, hiah-ni7 choe7 be7-noa7 e5 pun3-so3, beh an2-choaN2?
 Bok8-chian5 lan2 lo3-so3 e5 chhu2-li2, tai7-pou7-hun7 kan-na7 e7-tang3 eng7 tai5--e5, chiam3 sou2-chai7, koh boe7-noa7. Chun2-kong2 eng7 sio--e5, sio sok-ka-lui7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 e7 san2-seng chin tok8 e5 tok8-khi3. Lu7-poh8-pau sio boe7 tiau7, koh bo5-hoat-tou7 siu hoe5 su2-iong7, si7 chin long7-hui3 chu-goan5.
 Sim7-chi3, chin choe7 lang5 chiong lu7-poh8-pau kap Po2-tek8-koan3 ou-peh8-tan3, ti7 chui2-kau, lou7-piN, chui2-khou3, hong-keng2-khu, chhim-soaN......pho3-hoai7 lan2 e5 chu7-jian5 khoan5-keng2, koh, si3-koe3-lau5, that toa3 chui2-kau, ha7-chui2-to7, jip8-chui2-khau2 teng2 teng2, cho7-seng5 chui2-chai, kap chui2-goan5 chhu2-li2 siong7 chin toa7 e5 khun3-jiau2.
 Bok8-chian5 iu2-koan sit8-phin2 pau-chong e5 hoat-leng7, kin-pun2 bo5 kho2-lu7 kau3 chit chiong2 pau-chong sou2 toa3 lai5 e5 hio7-hoan7, sou2-i2, siuN7 beh kho3 lip8-hoat lai5 kim3-chi2 chit lui7 e5 san2-phin2 chha-put-to si7 bo5 kho2-leng5. Na7-si7 ai3 seng-san2-chia2 hong3-khi3 li7-ek mai3 koh eng7 hit chiong2 pau-chong, si7 koh khah bo5 kho2-leng5. I5-it e5 ng3-bang7 si7, siau-hui3-chia2 lian5-hap8 khi2 lai5, ti2-che3 chit lui7 gui5-hai7 khoan5-keng2 phin2-chit e5 san2-phin2, lan2 e5 bi7-lai5 chiah u7 ng3-bang7.
(Hong-hiong3 Te7 1 ki5, 1989 ni5 11 goeh8)
