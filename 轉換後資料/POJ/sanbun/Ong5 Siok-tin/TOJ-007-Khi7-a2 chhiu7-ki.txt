
                         Khi7-a2 chhiu7-ki
Ong5 Siok-tin ek8
                    Ti7  soaN--nih8, goa2 khoaN3  tioh8  chit8 e5 cho3-sit-lang5 teh ku3 chhiu7-ki beh ka7 khi7-a2-chhiu7 e5 chhiu7-ki chiap ti7 cho2-a2-chhiu7 teng2. In-ui7 cho2-a2-chhiu7 e5 ki-kut chhou koh iong2, chiap--khi2--khi3 liau2-au7, seN--chhut--lai5 e5 khi7-a2 pi2-kau3 khah ho2 chiah8 .
           Goa2 mng7 i kong2, “An2-ne khi7-a2 e5 chhiu7-ki kiam2  e7 seN  chhut cho2-a2? ” Cho3-sit-lang5 thiaN  chit8 e7 chhio3 kau3 chhui3-a2 li7-sai-sai, kong2, “Sui-jian5 chi2 si7 chit8-ki chhiu7-ki , m7-ku2 i eng2-oan2 be7 be7-ki3-tit ka-ki7 si7 khi7-a2, na2 e7 seN chhut cho2-a2 leh.”

           Khi7-a2 chhiu7-ki be7  bong7-pun2, se3-kan khiok u7 chin che7 lang5 put-ju5 chit8 ki khi7-a2 chhiu7-ki .
