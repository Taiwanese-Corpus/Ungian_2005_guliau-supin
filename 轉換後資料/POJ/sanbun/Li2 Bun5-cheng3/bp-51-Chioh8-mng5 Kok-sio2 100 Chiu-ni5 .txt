Chioh8-bng5 Kok-sio2 100 Chiu-ni5 Ki3-liam7 Ho7-su5

Li2 Bun5-cheng3 2000/12/22

Kin-ni5 si7 chhian-hi2 liong5-ni5, ma7-si7 An-peng5 Chioh8-bng5 Kok-sio2 100 chiu-ni5 e5 ki3-liam7. Ui7-tioh8 beh ngia5-chih chit-e5 u7 lek8-su2 i3-gi7 e5 jit8-chi2, ti7 IuN5 Hau7-tiuN2 leng2-to7 tai3-tong7 chi-ha7, kui-e5 hak8-hau7 long2 tin2-tang7--khi3-lai5. Choan5-hau7 lau7-su long2 hui-siong5-e5 sin-khou2. M7-koh, sui-jian5 sin-khou2, tai7-ke long2 kam2-kak chin-u7 i3-gi7. In-ui7, chit-pai2 100 chiu-ni5 ki3-liam7 oah8-tang7 tiu5-pan7 e5 tiong-kan, u7 chin-che7 jiat8-sim kau3-iok8 e5 te7-hng jin5-su7 i2-kip8 kiat8-chhut e5 hau7-iu2 ham7 cheng3-hu2 tan-ui7 long2 chin chi-chhi5 kap phoe3-hap8. Che e7-sai2 kong2 si7 lan2 An-peng5 e5 toa7 tai7-chi3. Siong-sin3 chioh-tioh8 chit-pai2 e5 oah8-tang7, hou7 lan2 An-peng5 u7 ho2-e5 chai3 chhut-hoat e5 ki-hoe7.
Ti7 choan5 Tai5-lam5-chhi7, sim7-chi3 choan5-kok, beh chhiau-koe3 100 tang e5 hak8-hau7 peng7-bo5 kui2-keng. (Tai5-lam5-chhi7 kan-ta u7 3 keng) Sou2-i2, Chioh8-bng5 Kok-sio2 e7-tang3 pai5-mia5 chai7-lai7, sit8-chai7 bo5 kan2-tan. Ti7 chit-e5 sou2-chai7, lan2 ma7 put-tek-put ai3 poe7-hok8 Jit8-pun2-lang5 tui3 kau3-iok8 e5 chu3-tiong7, sim7-chi3 si7 ti7 pi7 sit8-bin5 thong2-ti7 e5 Tai5-oan5. In-ui7, tong5-si5, Jit8-pun2-lang5 ti7 Tai5-oan5 khi2 chin-che7 hak8-hau7. Siong-sin3 che ma7-si7 chiaN5-cho3 kin-a2-jit8 Tai5-oan5 e7-tang3 hian7-tai7-hoa3 chin-toa7 e5 goan5-in. 
Hoe5-siong2 koe3-khi3 e5 bo2-hau7 kap chit-ma2 e5 bo2-hau7 u7 chin-toa7 e5 bo5-kang5. Tek8-piat8 si7 nge7-the2 e5 siat-pi7 long2-si7 sin-khi2 e5 kian3-tiok8-but8. Chit-ma2, mui2-chit8-pai2, goa2 na7 kiaN5-jip8 bo2-hau7, tian-to2 e7 khoa3-liam7 koe3-khi3 e5 keng2-ti3: ku7 e5 kau3-sek--lah, ku7 e5 un7-tong7-tiuN5--lah ham7 ku7 e5 pian7-sou2. In-ui7 chia si7 poe5-phoaN7 goa2 seng-tiong2 e5 sou2-chai7. Iu5-ki5 si7 Khou2 Heng7-chu2 Lau7-su ham7 Lau5 Giap8-jin5 Lau7-su giam5-keh jin7-chin e5 kau3-hak8 hou7 goa2 in3-siong7 chhim-khek.
Koe3-khi3 thak8-chheh e5 si5, goan2 si7 toa3 ti7 Ji7-khun-sin phau3-tai5 e5 sou2-chai7. (Chit-ma2 e5 Ek-chai3 Kim-siaN5.) Goan2 na7 beh kau3 hak8-hau7 thak8-chheh, long2 ai3 liong7-cha2 chhut-hoat, in-ui7 lou7-thau5 hng7 koh ai3 che7 tek-pai5-a2. U7-si5 in-ui7 lang5 chin che7 koh ka-siong7 chui2-eng2 e5 koan-he7 lai5 hoat-seng peng2 pai5-a2 e5 tai7-chi3. U7-si5 goan2 na7 thak8 e5-pou si5, goan2 tioh8 seng kau3 Ek-chai3 Kim-siaN5 khi3 liah8 sih-sut-a2. U7-si5 ha7-kho3 liau2, goan2 long2 e7 khi3 khoaN3 koa-a2-hi3 ah8-si7 pou3-te7-hi3. Sou2-i2, sui-jian5, hit-chun7 seng-oah8 khah chheng-khou2, bo5 chit-ma2 chiah hu3-ju7, m7-koh chiaN5 chhu3-bi7.
Choe3-kin7, sin-e5 hak8-hau7 1 keng 1 keng ti7-leh khi2, chin che7 ka-tiuN2 long2 jin7-ui5 sin hak8-hau7 siat-pi7 sin, lau7-su siau3-lian5, sou2-i2 long2 ai3 hou7 gin2-a2 khi3 thak8 sin-e5 hak8-hau7. Ma7 in-ui7 an2-ne, hou7 chit8-koa2 ku7 hak8-hau7, chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 e5 Chioh8-bng5 Kok-sio2, e7 khi3 tu2 tioh8 hak8-seng liu5-sit e5 bun7-te5. Lan2 hi-bang7 chioh tioh8 chit pai2 bo2-hau7 100 chiu-ni5 ki3-liam7 oah8-tang7, tai7-ke e7-tang3 u7 chiN5 chhut chiN5, u7 lat8 chhut lat8. Iong7 lan2 sit8-che3 e5 heng5-tong7 lai5 koan-sim lan2-e5 bo2-hau7, lai5 chiaN5-cho3 hian3 hou7 lan2 e5 bo2-hau7 100 chiu-ni5 ki3-liam7 siong7-kai3 ho2 e5 le2-but8.

【本文作者Li2 Bun5-cheng3(李文正)是現任台南市議員，台南市台語羅馬字研究協會會員。】
