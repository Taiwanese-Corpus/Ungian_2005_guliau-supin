

Jin5-seng e5 Siang-chhe-lou7
Chhoa3 An-teng7    
U7 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5; i tu2-tng7 teh siuN7 phok-su7 lun7-bun5, ho2-giah8, kap i e5 ai3-jin5 e5 si5, si2-lang5 e5 kut-thau5 chioh toa7-kou2 e5 heng5-chong7 hou7 i khoaN3-kiN3. Hit- si5 i chiu7 mng7 I, kong2, "Li2 si7 sim2-mih8" ? Toa7 kou2 chiu7 in3, kong2, "Goa2 e5 chit-bu7 chiu7-si7 teh koan2-li2 lang5 e5 oah8-mia7, goa2 chit e5 toa7-kou2 na7 kong3 chit8-e7, se3-kan e5 lang5 chiu7 si2 chit8-e5. Koh chit8-tiam2 cheng, chiu7 beh kong3 chit8-e7." I an2-ni in3-tap. Siau3-lian5 lang5 chiu7 soa3-chiap koh mng7, "M7-chai si7 beh ui7-tioh8 sim2- mih8 lang5 ?" In3, kong2, "Chiu7-si7 beh ui7- tioh8 li2." Ah ! hit-si5 siau3-lian5 lang5 kiaN chit8-e7, thou2 toa7-khui3, kong2, "Chhun chit8 tiam2 cheng e5 oah8-mia7 !     
Hit-si5 tu2-tu2 u7 chit8 e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 beh tham3 i, ka7 i kong2, "Ho2 peng5-iu2, li2 sit8- chai7 chin ho2 ji7-un7, li2 e5 phok-su7 lun7-bun5 i2-keng thong-koe3.  Koh bo5 ku2 phok-su7- ho7 beh kau3 li2 e5 chhiu2.  Tioh8 hoaN-hi2 ! tioh8 hoaN-hi2 !"  Hit-si5 chit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 long2 bo5 thiaN-tioh8 peng5-iu2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, in-ui7 i e5 sim-lai7 choan-choan teh siuN7 "si5-kan" e5 su7; in-ui7 i ti7 chit e5 beh si2 e5 si5-hau7, mia5-siaN u7 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7 ?    
Au7-lai5 koh te7-ji7 e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 ka7 i kong2, "Peng5-iu2 ah ! li2 chin heng7-hok lah ! lin2 a-peh e5 ui5-san2 siong-siok8 i2- keng koat-teng7; chit8-pah-ban7 khou teh-beh kau3 li2 e5 lak-te7-a2."  Hit-si5 siau3-lian5- lang5 ia7 si7 bo5 teh thiaN i e5 peng5-iu2 sou2 kong2 e5 oe7, in-ui7 i sou2 chhun e5 si5-kan si7 25 hun na7-tiaN7; ti7 chit-si5 u7 thang tit- tioh8 100 ban7 khou, u7 sim2-mih8 lou7-eng7 ? Sit8-chai7 si7 bo5 lou7-eng7.  Sou2-i2 siau3-lian5 lang5 e5 sim toa7 hoan5-lo2, u7 iu-chhiu5 e5 bin7-mau7.    
Te7-saN, chiu7-si7 u7 chit8 e5 sui2 e5 bin7-sek e5 hu7-jin5 lai5, chiu7 tui3 siau3-lian5 lang5 kong2, "Tioh8 saN-kap hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 lan2 nng7 lang5 e5 kiat-hun i2-keng ho2-se3 lah !  Bin5- a2-jit8 na7 siat-su2 beh ku2 kiat-hun-sek, ia7 oe7 eng7-tit."  Hu7-jin5-lang5 u7 chin toa7 hoaN-hi2 e5 bin7-iong5; chong2-si7 siau3-lian5 lang5 tu2-tu2 hoan2-tui3 hu7-jin5-lang5 sou2 tui3 i kong2 e5, ia7 long2 bo5 jip8 i e5 hi7-khang.    
I2-siong7 sou2 kong2 e5 ,si7 hi3-chhut, si7 lang5 sou2 choe3--e5, m7-ku2 ia7 si7 e7 thang kong2 si7 jin5-seng sou2 siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 e5 su7- sit8. Phok-su7 lun7-bun5 ia7 si7 ho2, pu3-u7 si7 lang5 sou2 i3-ai3--e5, him-bou7 ai3-jin5 ia7 m7-si7 phaiN2; chong2-si7 kau3 lang5 iau2 chhun u7 chit8-tiam2 cheng ku2 e5 oah8-mia7, hit-si5 hit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 boe7 thang tui3 hit saN hang7 lai5 tit-tioh8 an-ui3. Chiu7-si7 beng5-u7 e5 phok-su7-ho7, pah-ban7 e5 hu3-kui3, bi2-jin5 e5 ai3-cheng5; m7-si7 i tok8-it e5 hi- bong7.    
Chheng2-mng7 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7, lin2 sou2 khng3 tok8-it e5 hi-bong7 si7 sim2-mih8 ? Ia5- sou u7 kong2, "Lang5 tit-tioh8 thong se3-kan, lai5 sit-loh8 i e5 siN3-mia7, u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek ? Lang5 beh eng7 sim2-mih8 lai5 tui3-oaN7 i e5 siN3-mia7 ? Lang5 tok8-it e5 hi-bong7, chiu7-si7 eng2-oan2 e5 oah8-mia7 lah. Ia5-sou koh  kong2, "Ai3 kiu3 siN3-mia7 e5, hoan2-tng2 sit- loh8 i; ui7-tioh8 Goa2 lai5 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7 e5, beh tit-tioh8 i. " 
ChhiaN2 lan2 tai7-ke hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7, tioh8 hian3 ka-ki7 hou7 Chu2 tiau3, cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, ui7-tioh8 Siong7-te3 cho3 bo5 po2-sioh siN3-mia7 e5. ChhiaN2 lan2 tioh8 keng2 u7 kong-hui kiu3-leng5-chia2 e5 seng-gai5; na7 bo5, lan2 kiam2-chhai2 oe7 chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 beh khia7 ti7 sit-bang7 e5 Siang-chhe-lou7. Tioh8 hian3-sin ui7 Chu2 cho3 kang, lai5 chin2-kiu3 Tai5-to2 e5 tong5-pau, si7 sim sou2 goan7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 504 koan3,1927 ni5 3 geh8)
