
Hu7-jin5 e5 Sia7-kau
 (Tan5 Jin5-ai3 ki3) 
Siong5-siong5 thiaN-kiN3 liu5-thoan5 kong2, “He ! cha-bou2-lang5.”  ThiaN-tioh8 chit ku3, tak8 lang5 to liau2-kai2 hit e5 i3-su3.  Goa2 teh siuN7 hit e5 in-toaN m7-chai si7 chaiN2-iuN7? Hu7-jin5-lang5 tau3-ti2  u7 kui2 pou7-hun7 e5 khoat-tiam2 lai5 siN-chhut chit ku3 e5 liu5-thoan5.  TaN chiong sou2 siuN7 chit8-sut-a2 sia2 ti7-chia, khoaN3 oe7 choe3 chi3 sio2-khoa2 e5 bian2-le7 goa2 kap chi2-be7 a2-boe7 ? 
Chiu7-si7 Sia7-kau e5 su7.  Kou2-cha2 sin3 u7 Lu2-jin5 kok, Chhoan-sim kok, Siau2-jin5 kok.  Tai7-ke sin3 kau3 oe7 i5-soaN, na7-si7 chit chun7 bo5 lang5 ai3 sin3.  Chiu7-si7 tui3 lang5 na2 u7 sia7-kau, se3-kai3 e5 chong7-hong2 giam2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 bak8-chiu-cheng5.  Hit e5  kok na7-si7 ai3 kap pat8-kok kau-thong bou7-ek8, tui3-tiong7 sia7-kau, hit kok si7 bun5-beng5.  Na7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 cha2-si5 so2 kok e5 si5 khiam3 sia7-kau, chiu7 oh chin3-pou7, soa3 to3-the3.  Ka-teng5 sia7-kau na7 khiam3, chhin-chhiuN7 cha2-si5 hu7-jin5-lang5 bo5 chhut mng5, choan5 bo5 sia7-kau, na7 kiN3-tioh8 chhin-lang5 ngiauh8-ngiauh8-chhoah, khia7 boe7-tiau5, oe7 kong2 boe7 chhut, sit8-chai7 kho2-sioh.  Siat-su2 na7 kap chhin-chhek ong2-lai5 chhin-jiat8, tai7-ke saN pang-chan7, sit8-chai7 si7 ka-teng5 chin3-pou7 e5 toe7-ki. 
Koh kau3-hoe7 e5 sia7-kau, u7 chi2-be7 a2-si7 hiaN-ti7 tui3 kau3-hoe7 e5 kang chin kong3-hian3, lan2 ia7 tioh8 hoaN-hi2 choe3 chhin-chhiuN7 hit ho7 e5 lang5, kiam2-chhai2 sin-hun7 u7 pi2 pat8 lang5 khah koaiN5, ia7-si7 tioh8 siuN7 tioh8 Sia7-kau nng7-ji7 lai5 ka-ki7 kang3-ke7 lan2 e5 sim, hoaN-hi2 kap kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5-lang5 chhin-bit8 ong2-lai5, an2-ni hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 tek-khak hoaN-hi2 hoan-geng5 chit khoan2 e5 lang5.  Ia7 liu5-thoan5 e5 oe7 ia7 oe7 chiam7-chiam7 siau-bo5.  
Koh chit8 khoan2, chiu7-si7 te7-it ho2, te7-it iau3-kin2 e5 sia7-kau-hoat, chiu7-si7 chiong lan2 e5 Chu2 kek8 chhin-ai3 e5 to7-li2 po3-iong5 hou7 lang5 chai.  Chit koan2 e5 to7-li2 se3-kan lang5-lang5 to long2 u7 hun7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 teng2-bin7 sou2 lun7 e5 sia7-kau, u7 lang5 chin gau5 kau-poe5, chin khoah koh chhut-mia5.  Kho2-sioh khiam3 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2 choe3 sia7-kau e5 lou7, ti3-kau3 u7-si5 tu2-tioh8  sit-pai7.  TaN kek8 iau3-tiam2 chiu7-si7 ti7-chia.  Sou2-i2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit pang Pak-pou7 u7 siat Lu2 Soan-to7-hoe7, tai7-ke chhut-ian5 chhe Soan-to7-hu7 si3-koe3 thoan5 to7-li2. Lan2 na7 m7-si7 tui3 Chou2-ke Soan-to7-hoe7 phai3 lang5 thoan5 to7-li2 hou7 lan2, lan2 tek-khak boe7-tit kap Chu2 kau-poe5.  In na7 m7-si7 tui3 sit8-heng5 Sia7-kau nng7-ji7, lan2 sin3 Chu2 e5 khoai3-lok8 tui3 tah-loh8 oe7 tit-tioh8? Lan2 na7 u7 chhut-ian5 hou7 Soan-to7-hu7, chiu7-si7 i sou2 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7, lan2 e5 sia7-kau ia7 kau3 ti7-hia; na7 chhut chit8-sian2, sui-si7 sio2-khoa2, m7-ku2 tui3 Soan-to7-hoe7 e5 kang lan2 e5 sia7-kau oe7 kau3 ti7-hia.  Lu2 Soan-to7-hoe7 e5 bok8-tek chiu7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ni.  
Sou2-i2 ng3-bang7 sou2 thiaN3-thang3 e5 chi2-be7, tak8 lang5 eng7 lin2 e5 kian3-sek koh ke-thiN lai5 siuN7, lan2 na7 jip8 Lu2 Soan-to7-hoe7 u7 sim2-mih8 hui-siong5 e5 ke3-tat8 ti7-teh. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 465 koan3,1923 ni5 12 geh8)
