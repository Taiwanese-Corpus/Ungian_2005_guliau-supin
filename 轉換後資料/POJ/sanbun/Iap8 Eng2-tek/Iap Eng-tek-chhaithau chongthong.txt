Chhai3-thau5 Chong2-thong2----Li2-peh8-chhat8
Iap8 Eng2-tek

Chit kui2 ni5 na7 tu2 tioh8 soan2-ki2, to7 khoaN3 tioh8 1 e5 lang5, ti7 tian7-si7 teng2-thau5, siang-chhiu2 phang5 chhai2-thau5, heng-kham2 tng3 kah beh ou-chhiN, kong: "Pun2 tong2 thui-soan2 e5 jin5-chai5, u7 eng-hiong5 ho2-han3, u7 cho3-koaN chheng-liam5--e5, u7 bun5-khiok-chhiN ha7-hoan5--e5."kiat-ko2, chit e5 eng-hiong5 chhoa7 1 tin7 ho2-han3, ti7 1 e5 lau7-bu2 e5 bin7-thau5-cheng5, chiong i e5 hau7-siN, tong-tiuN5 loan7-chheng3 sia7-sat. Cho3-koaN chheng-liam5--e5, hou7 in ka-ki7 e5 tong2 khui e5 hoat-iN7, iong7 tham-u e5 choe7-mia5 phoaN3 bu5-ki5 tou5-heng5.  Chit e5 bun5-khiok-chhiN ha7-hoan5--e5, ti7 soan2-ki2 e5 si5-chun7, ui7-cho7 hak8-lek8 cheng3-kiaN7.

I e5 leng7-goa7 1 e5 choan-tiong2 si7 e7 the3 lang5 kai2-chiu2, m7 chai i si7 siaN2-meh iau-mou5 choan2-se2. I e5 chhiu2-lai7 u7 5~6 tiuN ui5-hoat e5 ko-lu2-huh kiu5-tiuN5 e5 kiu5-cheng3, ma7 e7-tang3 iong7 300 goa7 ban7 boe2 chhi7-ke3 1 ek 6 chheng goa7 ban7 e5 piat8-chong.

Choan5 Tai5-oan5 hoat-seng ti-un, tak8-ke m7 kaN2 chiah8 ti-bah, i chhoa7 1 tin7 koaN-goan5, hoat khi2 (chiah8 si2 bo5 poe5) "chiah8 ti-kha kiu3 Tai5-oan5" e5 sia7-hoe7 un7-tong7.

Ti7-an chin bai2, jin5-sim put-an, i kio3 jin5-bin5 tioh8-ai3 sim-leng5 kai2-cho7. Tai7-chiong3 put-hok8, ti7 504 kap 518, 4~5 ban7 e5 kun5-chiong3 kiaN5 hiong3 ke-thau5 khong3-gi7.

I si7 1997 choe3-ka lam5-chu2-kak, Tiong-hoa5-bin5-kok e5 chhai3-thau5 chong2-thong2----Li2 peh8-chhat8.

Tak8-ke lai5 oh8 Tai5-gi2-bun5, iong7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 sia2 lek8-su2, ki3-lok8 lek8-su2, chhong3-cho7 lek8-su2, khi2-cho7 sun5-choan5 e5 Tai5-oan5-kok.
