Tiah-iau3

Poe7-keng2: 30% e5 i-liau5 kiu3-hun si7 in-ui7 i-su kap hoan7-chia2 kou-thong put-liong5. Bok8-cheng5 i-hak8-iN7 e5 kau3-iok8 phou2-phian3 khiam3-khoeh poe5-iong2 i-peN7 koan-he7 ki-kha2 e5 kho3-theng5. Siau3-lian5 i-seng kap i-hak8-seng e5 pun2-thou2 gi2-gian5 leng5-lek8 e5 tui7-loh8 e7 hou7 kou-thong put-liong5 e5 bun7-te5 koh khah giam5-tiong7. Koh u7 ju2 khah phaiN2 kai2-koat--e5 si7 e7-bin7 kui2-hang7: (1) i-hak8-iN7 bo5 khui-siat cheng3-sek e5 pun2-thou2 gi2-gian5 kho3-theng5, (2) i-hak8-kai3 tui3 i-liau5 peN7-thiaN3 sou2 su2-iong7 e5 khau2-gi2 bo5 he7-thong2-seng3 e5 liau2-kai2 gian2-kiu3, (3) bo5 che3-teng7 phiau-chun2-hoa3 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 su5-lui7-khou3.

Chai5-liau7 kap Hong-hoat: Chit-e5 gian2-kiu3 ki3-liok8 1917 ni5 Tai5-lam5 Ki-tok-kau3 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 piN7-iN7 (Sin-lau5 piN7-iN7 e5 chian5-sin), Te3 Jin5-siu7 i-su eng7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 sou2 chhut-pan2 e5 "Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8 (LGK)" cho3 chai5-liau7, ki3-liok8 te7 8 chiuN (Lun7 Phe5-hu), te7 24 chiuN (Hoa-liu2-piN7), kap te7 37 chiuN (Thoan5-jiam2-piN7) lai7-bin7 sou2 siu-chip8 kap chhong3-cho7 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 kap i-hak8 khau2-gi2, hun-cho3 phoe5-hu kou3-cho7 ho7-mia5 (Pio2-1) kap phoe5-hu peN7-cheng3 mia5-chheng (Pio2-2) 2-e5 pou7-hun7. Koh khioh-keng2 Tai5-oan5 Ho7-lo2 chok8-kun5 tui3 kok-chiong2 phoe5-hu-peN7 peN7-mia5 e5 khau2-gi2 gi2-liau7. Chiau3 peN7-cheng3 e5 seng3-chit lai5 hun-lui7 cheng2-li2 (Pio2-3). Theh8 chit 2 chiong2 gi2-liau7 kap hian7-kim Eng-gi2, Jit8-gi2, kap Hoa5-gi2 phoe5-hu-kho i-hak8 sut8-gi2 cho3 pi2-tui3.

Kiat-ko2 kap Tho2-lun7: (1) LGK si7 ui7-tioh8 beh hou7 Tai5-oan5 i-su, hou7-su7, kap i-liau5 siong-koan jin5-oan5 khoai3-khoai3 e7-sai2 oh8-tit se-hng i-hak8. Chit-pun2 i-hak8 kau3-kho-su iu2-koan phoe5-hu kho-hak8 e5 pou7-hun7 m7-taN7 kap se3-kai3 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 e5 hoat-tian2 siang7-chit8-si5, ia7 the3 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 kap ti3-sek e5 pun2-thou2-hoa3 kian3-lip8 liau2 kian-kou3 e5 chioh8-poaN5. (2) Tai5-oan5 Ho7-lo2-oe7 lai7-bin7, iu2-koan phoe5-hu-peN7 e5 khau2-gi2 gi2-su5 hoan2-eng3 chhut chit-e5 chok8-kun5 e5 chu7-jian5 te7-li2 khoan5-keng2 kap khi3-hau7, sia7-hoe7 seng-oah8 koan3-si3, chong-kau3 sin3-giong2 kap hong-siok8 bun5-hoa3. --KIAN3-LIP8 PUN2-THOu2 PHOE5-HU KHO-HAK8 GI2-GIAN5 SU5-LUI7-KHOu3 KAP KHO3-THENG5

Poe7-keng2

I-liau5 e5 koe3-theng5 tui3 kong2-oe7 khai-si2 (phi3-ju7: "Ou-ba2-sang2, li2 toh-ui7 bo5 song2-khoai3"), an3 kong2-oe7 kiat-sok (phi3-ju7: "Tng2-khi3 chhu3--nih8 ai3 ho2-ho2-a2 po2-iong2). Beh an2-choaN2 kong2 cheng-bit8 cheng3-khak, hah8 poe3-hun7 kap si5-ki koh u7 le2-sou3 e5 gi2-gian5 si7 seng5-kong kou-thong e5 te7-it iau3-kin2. Kun-ku3 Oe7-seng-su2 e5 thong2-ke3, 30% e5 i-liau5 kiu3-hun si7 in-ui7 i-su kap hoan7-chia2 kou-thong put-liong5 sou2 san2-seng (Sim2 Phek-chheng, personal communication). Bok8-cheng5 i-hak8-iN7 e5 kau3-iok8 phou2-phian3 khiam3-khoeh poe5-iong2 jin5-bun5 sou3-iong2, sia7-hoe7 koan-hoai5 kap bun5-hoa3 jin7-ti i2-kip8 i-peN7 koan-he7 ki-khiau2 e5 kho3-theng5. I-hak8-seng kap siau3-lian5 i-su e5 pun2-thou2 gi2-gian5 (Ho7-lo2, Kheh-ka, Lam5-to2-gi2) leng5-lek8 e5 tui7-loh8 tek-khak pai7-hai7 i-su kap hoan7-chia2 e5 kou-thong.

E7-bin7 3-hang7 hou7 bun7-te5 koh khah giam5-tiong7: (1) I-hak8-iN7 bo5 khui-siat cheng3-sek e5 pun2-thou2 gi2-gian5 kho3-theng5. Siau3-lian5 i-seng ti7 i-hak8-iN7 e5 kai-toaN7 bo5 siu7-tioh8 sek-tong3 e5 gi2-gian5 kau3-iok8, boe2--a2 khai-si2 chih-chiap hoan7-chia2 e5 si5, gi2-gian5 leng5-lek8 bo5 kau3-giah8, cho7-seng5 kou-thong e5 khun3-lan5. (2) I-hak8-kai3 tui3 peN7-thiaN3 kap i-liau5 sou2 su2-iong7 e5 khau2-gi2 bo5 he7-thong2-seng3 e5 liau2-kai2 kap gian2-kiu3. I-su tui3 hoan7-chia2 sou2 piau2-tat8 e5 peN7-thiaN3 cheng3-thau5 bo5 chhiong-hun e5 li2-kai2, u7 si5 e7 tam-ngou7 ti7-liau5 hok8-kian7 e5 koe3-theng5. (3) Bo5 che3-teng7 phiau-chun2-hoa3 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 sut8-gi2. I-seng beh ka7 hoan7-chia2 kai2-soeh peN7-cheng5 iah-si7 oe7-seng ti3-sek e5 si5, bo5 phiau-chun2-hoa3 e5 i-liau5 sut8-gi2 e7-sai2 eng7, tiaN7-tiaN7 1 e5 i-seng kong2 1 khoan2, hou7 hoan7-chia2 thiaN liau2 bu7-sa3-sa3. Leng7-goa7 ia7-si7 chit-e5 ian5-kou3 hou7 i-su kap i-su tiong-kan teh tho2-lun7 hoan7-chia2 e5 peN7-cheng5, po3-ko3 i-liau5 sin ti3-sek e5 si5, tioh8-ai3 chham-lam7 goa7-lai5-gi2 (Eng-gi2, Jit8-gi2, iah-si7 Hoa5-gi2), bo5 hoat-tou7 su2-iong7 Tai5-gi2 chiaN5-cho3 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 choan-giap8 e5 gi2-gian5.

Chiam-tui3 te7 1 e5 bun7-te5, tu3-chia2 ti7 2000 ni5 3--goeh8 tiam3 Ko-hiong5 I-hak8 Tai7-hak8 khui-siat "I-hak8 Tai5-gi2 Jip8-mng5" soan2-siu kho3-theng5, the5-kiong i-hak8-seng i-hak8 Tai5-gi2 jip8-mng5 e5 kho3-theng5. Te7 2 kap te7 3 e5 bun7-te5 chiu7 khah phaiN2 kai2-koat. In-ui7 tu3-chia2 (TSC) si7 tai7-hak8 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 kang2-su, chin chu7-jian5 keng2 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 e5 su5-lui7 (lexicons) khai-si2 cheng2-li2, ng3-bang7 e7-sai2 chhu2-li2 chit 2 e5 bun7-te5.

Phoe5-hu-kho eng7 e5 gi2-su5, i-hak8 sut8-gi2 kap i-iong7 khau2-gi2 long2 sng3 chai7-lai7, pun2-chiaN5 tui3 i-hak8-seng tioh8 siong-tong khun3-lan5. Phoe5-hu-peN7 e5 heng5-thai3 chhian-pian3 ban7-hoa3, iu2-koan phoe5-hu-peN7 e5 khau2-gi2 kap siok8-mia5 ia7 pi2 sin-the2 ki5-tha he7-thong2 khah chap8 khah che7. Se-kok phoe5-hu-kho i-su chin cha2 tioh8 u7 pian-sia2 su5-tian2 (Carter 1992), ia7 u7 siat-ke3 hou7 i-hak8-seng liau2-kai2 phoe5-hu-kho gi2-gian5 e5 kho3-theng5 (http://www.fammed.wisc. edu/education/presentations/derm/Dermcurriculum.html#Intro, http:// dermatology.cdlib.org/DOJvol2num1/morphology/derm.html). Tai5-oan5 phoe5-hu-kho-kai3 kap cheng2-ko3 Tai5-oan5 i-hak8-kai3 tui3 chit hong-bin7 chhut e5 khui3-lat8 chin chio2. Chiau3 tu3-chia2 sou2 chai, phoe5-hu kho-hak8 ti7 Tai5-oan5 hoat-tat8 beh 100 tang, m7-chiah u7 1 pun2 chheh na7-tiaN7 (林仲2000). Tai5-gi2 i-liau5 khau2-gi2 e5 sit-loh8 kap i-hak8 sut8-gi2 gian2-kiu3 e5 pha-hng hou7 phoe5-hu-kho gi2-gian5 kho3-theng5 e5 kian3-lip8 ju2 khah kan-lan5.

Kai-chai3 Tai5-oan5 Se-hng I-hak8 e5 thoan5-po3-chia2, TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 e5 i-su phi3-ju7 Ma2 Nga2-kok 2-se3 (James L. Maxwell), Lan5 Tai7-pit (David Landsborough), Te3 Jin5-siu7 (G. Gushue-Taylor) teng2-teng2, lau5 loh8-lai5 po2-kui3 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 bun5-hian3. Bok8-cheng5 chai-iaN2--e5 u7 "Sin-the2-li2", "Tai5-lam5, Chiang-hoa3, TiuN2-lo2-kau3 I-koan2 Kong-iong7 e5 Ioh8-hng (Maxwell 1922)", kap "Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8 (Gushue-Taylor 1917)" 3 pun2. Ki5-tiong "Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8" si7 1917 ni5, Te3 Jin5-siu7 i-su ti7 Tai5-lam5 Ki-tok TiuN2-lo2-kau3 PiN7-iN7 sou2 chhut-pan2. Kui-pun2 chheh (loe7-iong5, gi2-lui7, sek-in2) long2-chong2 675 iah8, 503 tou5-17 u7 sek--e5, hun-choe3 40 chiuN, choan5-pou7 eng7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 sia2-chok. Loe7-iong5 tui3 ki-chhou2 e5 sin-the2 he7-thong2 kou3-cho7, kau3 peN7-cheng3 cheng3-thau5, peN7-in, ti7-liau5 khan3-hou7-hoat, it-chin7 long2 u7 kai2-soeh. E7-sai2 kong2 si7 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8-chheh lai7-bin7, siong7 kai3 kiat8-chok koh chiau5-chng5 e5 1 pun2.

Chai5-liau7 kap hong-hoat

Pun2 gian2-kiu3 eng7 "Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8" (LGK) cho3 gi2-bun5 chai5-liau7, theh8 te7 8 chiuN "Lun7 Phe5-hu", te7 24 chiuN "Hoa-liu2-piN7", kap te7 37 chiuN "Thoan5-jiam2-piN7" chiah-e5 kap hian7-tai7 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 u7 koan-he7 e5 kui2-chiuN, ki3-liok8 sou2 su2-iong7 e5 phoe5-hu kou3-cho7 seng-li2 kap phoe5-hu-peN7 e5 su5-lui7 kap bun5-toaN7 (text). Koh ka7 chiah-e5 ni lai5 kap hian7-kim phoe5-hu kho-hak8 sou2 eng7 e5 i-liau5 khau2-gi2 kap i-hak8 sut8-gi2 lai5 cho3 pi2-kau3. Chiah-e5 gi2-gian5 chu-liau7 chiau3 phoe5-hu he7-thong2 e5 kou3-cho7 kap kok-chiong2 peN7-cheng3 e5 ho7-mia5 lai5 hun-lui7 kap kai2-sek, jian5-au7 koh eng7 chiah-e5 cheng2-li2--koe3 e5 gi2-su5 lai5 kap hian7-tai7 Eng-gi2, Jit8-gi2, Hoa5-gi2 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 lai5 pi2-tui3. Bok8-phiau he7-ti7 kian3-lip8 pun2-thou2 phoe5-hu-kho gi2-gian5 su5-lui7-khou3 kap gi2-gian5 kho3-theng5. 

Gi2-liau7 e5 goan5-thau5 chiau3 e7-te2:

(1) Lek8-su2 chiuN7 e5 Tai5-gi2 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 su5-lui7: Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8

(2) Hian7-kim Tai5-gi2 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 i-hak8 sut8-gi2: Jit8-bun5 (石田名香雄1999, Ken-ichi Uyeno 1996), Eng-bun5 (Odom 2000, Dorland's Medical Dictionary 1989, Roenigk, 1996), kap Hoa5-bun5 (台灣皮膚科醫學會 2001, 華欣醫學大辭典 1993) phoe5-hu-kho su5-tian2 kap kau3-kho-su.

Hian7-kim Tai5-gi2 phoe5-hu-kho i-liau5 khau2-gi2: Tu3-chia2 tui3 ki5-tha phoe5-hu-kho i-su i2-kip8 hoan7-chia2 siu-chip8--tioh8 e5 gi2-liau7.

Chiah-e5 tui3 peN7-thiaN3 e5 kai2-soeh kap khoaN3-hoat tiaN7-tiaN7 long2 e7-sai2 hoan2-eng3 chhut hit e5 chok8-kun5 in e5 seng-oah8-koan, chu7-jian5-koan, se3-kai3-koan kap u2-tiu7-koan. 

Tui3 chiah-e5 kai2-soeh kap khoaN3-hoat san2-seng chhut che7-che7 oe7-seng po2-kian7 e5 khai3-liam7, heng5-ui5, kap hong-siok8. Leng7-goa7 ia7 san2-seng chhut i-liau5 su-siong2 kap heng5-the2. Chiah-e5 long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 beh chhiok-chin3 ko3-the2 e5 seng-chun5 kap chok8-kun5 e5 seN-thoaN3. Sou2-i2 biau5-sia2 kok-chiong2 cheng3-thau5 cheng2-lui7, thoan5-tat8 peN7-thiaN3 khin-tang7 chong7-hong2 e5 oe7-gi2, si7 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 kong2-oe7 eng3-tap e5 tiong7-iau3 pou7-hun7. Chau2-chhoe7 kian7-khong, ai3-sioh sin-mia7 e5 heng5-ui5 ma7-si7 sia7-hoe7 bun5-hoa3 oah8-tang7 e5 tiong7-iau3 te5-bok8 kap khang-khoe3. Mui2 1 e5 chok8-kun5 long2 u7 phong5-phai3, hong-hu3 e5 gi2-lui7 kap koan-liam7 lai5 heng5-iong5, kai2-soeh chiong2-chiong2 e5 peN7-cheng3 kap sin-the2 e5 kan-khou2. Chiah-e5 che7-che7 oe7-seng po2-kian7 e5 gi2-gian5 kap bun5-hoa3, m7-taN7 si7 jin5-lui7 beh kian7-khong oah8-mia7 e5 hoat-tou7 na7-tiaN7, ia7-si7 thoan5-seng5 chok8-kun5 ti7 chhim-tng5 e5 lek8-su2 khe-lau5 tiong kap chu7-jian5 poaN5-noa2 kau-poe5, kiong7-seng kiong7-chun5 e5 ti3-hui7.

Tai5-oan5 phoe5-hu-peN7 sou2 eng7 e5 khau2-gi2 hui-siong5 phong5-phai3. Tui3 peN7-thiaN3 e5 cheng3-thau5 u7 cheng-bit8 e5 koan-chhat, tui3 peN7-thiaN3 e5 goan5-thau5 kap peN7-in u7 oah8-thiau3-thiau3 e5 siong2-siong7 (imagination), hoan2-eng3 chhut Tai5-oan5 Ho7-lo2-lang5 chok8-kun5 e5 chu7-jian5 te7-li2 khoan5-keng2 kap khi3-hau7, sia7-hoe7 heng5-thai3 kap seng-oah8 koan3-si3, chong-kau3 sin3-giong2 kap hong-siok8.

Tai5-oan5 i-hak8-iN7 gi2-gian5 su2-iong7 e5 hian7-hong2 kap kai2-kek

Hian7-chhu2-si5 ti7 i-hak8-iN7 e5 gi2-gian5 su2-eng7, e7-sai2 kong2 si7 che7-chiong2 gi2-gian5 chham-lam7 teh eng7. I-hak8-seng thiaN Hoa5-gi2 (toa7-pou7-hun7) kap Eng-gi2 (kong2-tioh8 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 e5 si5) e5 kang2-kho3, thak8 Eng-gi2 (to-sou3) kap Hoa5-gi2 (goa7-kok kau3-kho-su e5 hoan-ek8-pun2) kau3-kho-su, khi3-kau3 peN7-iN7 eng7 Tai5-gi2 (khah che7) kap Hoa5-gi2 khoaN3 hoan7-chia2, eng7 Eng-gi2 sia2 kha-lu-teh (charts), eng7 Hoa5-gi2 ti7 peN7-iN7 kap i-hak8 hoe7-gi7 ka7 ki5-tha i-su po3-ko3 khe-suh (case), eng7 Eng-gi2 sia2 i-hak8 lun7-bun5.

I-hak8-seng ti7 chit-khoan2 che7-chiong2 gi2-gian5 chham-lam7 su2-eng7 e5 khoan5-keng2, na7 bo5 chiok-giah8 e5 gi2-gian5 leng5-lek8, tiaN7-tiaN7 e7 chhut-hian7 hun3-loan7. Ti7 i-hak8-iN7 e5 kau3-iok8 tiong, Eng-gi2 kap Hoa5-gi2 e5 hun3-lian7 long2 kau3-giah8, m7-koh tit8-chiap bin7-tui3 hoan7-chia2, su2-iong7 siong7 kai3 che7 e5 Tai5-gi2 soah hou7 lang5 tong3-cho3 bo5-iau3-bo5-kin2. Chiau3 tu3-chia2 sou2 chai, kau3 taN Tai5-oan5 choan5-kok e5 i-hak8-iN7 m7-chiah u7 Ko-hiong5 I-hak8 Tai7-hak8 u7 khui-siat u7 hak8-hun e5 I-hak8 Tai5-gi2 kho3-theng5. Ka7 bo5 siu7-koe3 i-hak8 Tai5-gi2 gi2-gian5 kau3-iok8 e5 hak8-seng tit8-chiap sak jip8-khi3 peN7-iN7 te7-it-soaN3 chih-chiap hoan7-chia2, m7-taN7 si7 eng2-hiong2 hak8-seng lim5-chhng5 hak8-sip8 e5 kong-leng5, iu5-koh pai7-hai7 hoan7-chia2 e5 khoan5-li7.

Tai5-oan5 e5 i-hak8 kau3-iok8 pun2-te2 tioh8-si7 tiong7 kho-ki khin jin5-bun5, tiong7 seng-but8 khin sia7-hoe7 (陳永興, 2002). Kin7-lai5 Tai5-oan5 i3-sek hok8-heng, che7-che7 tai7-hak8 long2 khai-si2 khui-siat Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 kho3-theng5, su-iau3 chiok che7 kong2-oe7 kou-thong e5 i-hak8-seng tian-to3 bo5 siu7-tioh8 kau3-giah8 e5 gi2-gian5 kau3-iok8, bok8-koai3 i-kai3 e5 chian5-poe3 put-si5 teh oan3-than3 i-peN7 e5 koan-he7 ju2 lai5 ju2 bai2, i-kai3 e5 jin5-bun5 sou3-chit si7 1 tai7 khah su 1 tai7.

Tu3-chia2 jin7-ui5 beh kai2-sian7 i-hak8-seng gi2-gian5 bun7-te5 ai3 tui3 3 hong-bin7 loh8-chhiu2, chiau3 e7-te2:

(1)	Ai3 ti7 i-hak8-iN7 lai7-te2 khui-siat pit-siu e5 phou2-thong Tai5-gi2-bun5 kap i-hak8 Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 kho3-theng5. Eng7 Ko-hiong5 I-hak8 Tai7-hak8 I-hak8-he7 cho3-le7, bok8-cheng5 I-hak8-he7 1-ni5 u7 "Tai5-gi2-bun5 Jip8-mng5" soan2-siu 2 hak8-hun kap I-hak8-he7 3-ni5 u7 "I-hak8 Tai5-gi2-bun5 Jip8-mng5" soan2-siu 2 hak8-hun. Chiau3 tu3-chia2 sou2 chai, che i2-keng si7 choan5 Tai5-oan5-kok i-hak8-iN7 lai7-bin7 siong7 kai3 chu3-tiong7 Tai5-gi2-bun5--lah! (Tai5-pak I-hak8 Tai7-hak8 u7 Tai5-oan5 Bun5-hak8 soan2-siu 2 hak8-hun), ia7 kho2-leng5 si7 hui Tai5-gi2 (bun5) he7-sou2 lai7-bin7 siong7 kai3 che7 Tai5-gi2-kho3 e5 hak8-hau7. M7-koh pi2-khi2 Ko-i Tai7-hak8 e5 ki5-tha gi2-bun5-kho3, chhin-chhiuN7 Hoa5-gi2 pit-siu 4 hak8-hun, Eng-gi2 pit-siu 6 hak8-hun. Lian5 Tek-gi2 (4 hak8-hun pit-siu, 4 hak8-hun soan2-siu), Jit8-gi2 (4 hak8-hun pit-siu) long2 khah iaN5 Tai5-gi2 chin che7.

(2)	Ai3 tui3 i-liau5 kap peN7-thiaN3 sou2 eng7 e5 Tai5-gi2 khau2-gi2 cho3 he7-thong2-seng3 e5 gian2-kiu3. Thak8-koe3 i-hak8-he7 e5 lang5 long2 chai, textbook teng2-bin7 u7 kong2, na7 chun2 hoan7-chia2 kong2 i u7 "chest tightness, chest discomfort, chest pain" long2 ai3 siuN7-tioh8 kiap-sim-cheng3 (angina) e5 kho2-leng5, na7-si7 hoan7-chia2 kong2 "heartburn" chiu7 siuN7-tioh8 si7 siau-hoa3-to7 e5 khui3-iong5. An2-ne na7-si7 hoan7-chia2 kong2 "sim-koaN-thau5 chak8-chak8, sim-thau5 iN2-a2 cho-cho, sim-koaN-thau5 bun7-bun7" si7 beh siuN7-tioh8 "kiap-sim-cheng3", iah-si7 "siau-hoa3-to7 e5 khui3-iong5"? I-hak8-kai3 tioh8-ai3 koaN2-kin2 cheng2-li2 Tai5-gi2 lai7-bin7 biau5-sia2 peN7-thiaN3 cheng3-chong7 e5 oe7-gi2, kap hian7-tai7 i-hak8 e5 cheng3-chong7-hak8 (symptomatology) cho3 pi2-tui3 kap hun-sek. An2-ne m7-taN7 tui3 i-hak8-seng e5 gi2-gian5 kau3-hak8 u7 pang-chan7, koh khah tiong7-iau3--e5 si7 Tai5-gi2 hoan7-chia2 e5 chin2-liau5 e7 khah cheng3-khak, chin3-pou7.

(3)	Ai3 che3-teng7 phiau-chun2-hoa3 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 sut8-gi2 su5-khou3. Tu5-liau2 chio2-sou3 chhiang5-chai7 tu2--tioh8 e5 peN7-cheng3 i2-goa7 (kiN3 pio2-3), long2 bo5 sek-tong3 phiau-chun2 e5 Tai5-gi2 mia5-chheng thang eng7. Na7 bo5, m7-taN7 i-hak8-seng bo5 hoat-tou7 oh8-tioh8 cheng3-khak cheng-bit8 e5 i-hak8 Tai5-gi2, beh tui3 hoan7-chia2 kai2-soeh peN7-cheng5, beh tui3 sia7-hoe7 chiong3-lang5 chin3-heng5 oe7-seng kau3-iok8, sim7-chi3 i-seng kap i-seng tiong-kan beh eng7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 tho2-lun7 kou-thong, long2 e7 hoat-seng bun7-te5.

Chit 3 e5 khang-khoe3 long2 su-iau3 i-hak8-kai3 kap gi2-gian5-kai3 hap8 khui3-lat8 e5 phah-piaN3.

Te3 Jin5-siu7 kap ki5-tha i-su sou2 cho3 e5 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 bun5-hian3, siu-chip8 kap chhong3-cho7 chin che7 Tai5-gi2 i-hak8 khau2-gi2 kap sut8-gi2, ui7-tioh8 se-hng i-hak8 e5 Tai5-gi2 bun5-hoa3 kian3-cho7 liau2 kian-teng7 e5 chioh8-poaN5. Koh u7 lan2 Ho7-lo2-lang5 chou2-kong-ma2 lau5--loh8-lai5 e5 u7 ti3-hui7 koh u7 kong-hau7 e5 i-hak8 khau2-gi2. Chit 2 khoan2 gi2-liau7 e7-sai2 chiaN5-cho3 kian3-lip8 choan5-bin7-seng3 Tai5-oan5 phoe5-hu kho-hak8 su5-lui7-khou3 kap gi2-gian5 kho3-theng5 e5 ki-chhou2.  (Hu7-liok8 1) Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8 te7 8 chiuN

LUN7 PHE5-HU

Phe5-hu pau ti7 seng-khu e5 goa7-bin7; i e5 chok-iong7 si7 chiau3 e7-toe2:

1.	Si7 po2-hou7 kun-bah.

2.	U7 chhiok-kak e5 chok-iong7, chiu7-si7 bong-tioh8 goa7-ui7 chiu7 chai e5 khoan2-sit. Si7 in-ui7 phe5-hu e5 chhiok-kak-sio2-the2; i e5 lai7-bin7 e5 sin5-keng u7 be2-liu. Chhiok-kak (觸覺, touch sense), hiah-e5 e5 te7 it gau5 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7-si7 chhiu2-chng2-thau5-a2, chih8 e5 be2-liu, chhui3-tun5.

3.	Phe5 si7 pai5-siat-khi3 e5 chit8-e5; i e5 chok-iong7 si7 chhut-koaN7. KoaN7 si7 chui2 e5 chit. KoaN7, chit8 pah hun7 e5 lai7-bin7, nng7 hun7 si7 koaN7-sng e5 iam5-hun, kap iu5.

4.	Phe5 u7 khip-siu e5 khui3-lat8, chhin-chhiuN7 sui2-gun5 lui7 e5 ioh8, oe7 khip-jip8 ti7 phe5-hu-lai7.

5.	Si7 tiau-ho5 lang5 seng-khu e5 the2-un; chhin-chhiuN7 na7 thiN-khi3 khah joah8 e5 si5, hiah e5 han7-choaN5-kng2 e5 be2-liu tiuN3 koh khui, sou2-i2 koaN7 na7 lau5-chhut-lai5, lang5 chiu7 khah liang5-leng2, khoaN3-oah8. Na7 thiN-khi3 khah leng2 e5 si5, hiah e5 kng2 oe7 khah kiu-oa2, khah bo5 hoat koaN7, ia7 hiah e5 khah oa2 ti7 phe5-hu e5 huih-kng2, oe7 kiu, jiat8-khi3 chiu7 khah boe7 tui3 phe5-hu li7-khui seng-khu, hoan2-tng2, toa3 ti7 seng-khu-lai7, sou2-i2 lang5 chiu7 oe7 sio-lo7.

Phe5-hu hun choe3 nng7 chan5. Teng2 bin7 chan5 kio3-choe3 piau2-phe5 (表皮, epidermis) ; te7 ji7 kio3-choe3 chin-phe5 (真皮, dermis).

Ti7 chit nng7 chan5 e5 e7-toe2 u7 kiat-te3-chit, chit-e5 kio3-choe3 phe5-e7-kiat-te3-chit (皮下結締織, subcutaneous connective tissue; te7 88 tou5).

Piau2-phe5 si7 chiau3 i e5 mia5 sou2 kong2, ti7 phe5-hu7 e5 choe3 te7 it goa7-khau2 e5 chou-chit, si7 kui2-na7 chan5 e5 soe3-pau lai5 chiaN5-e5.

Piau2-phe5 lai7 long2 bo5 huih-kng2; ti7 piau2-phe5 e5 choe3 te7 i(it?) chhim-chan5 e5 soe3-pau e5 tiong-kan, u7 te7 it soe3 tiau5 e5 sin5-keng.

Piau2-phe5 ia7 siong5-siong5 u7 phi2 poh8-phiN3 poh8-phiN3 teh hiauh-khi3-lai5. Thau5-khak u7 khah choe7, kio3-choe3 thau5-phou; thong seng-khu long2 si7 an2-ni, siong5-siong5 teh hiauh-khi3-lai5.

Chin-phe5 si7 phe5-hu chou-chit e5 te7-ji7 chan5; chit-e5 si7 iu3 koh bat8 e5 chou-chit lai5 chiaN5-e5. Chiap ti7 piau2-phe5 e7-bin7 bo5 piN5-piN5, u7 chin choe7 lui5-e5, kio3-choe3 leng-thau5 (乳頭, papilla). Chiah e5 leng-thau5 chiu7-si7 mng5-soe3-kng2, kap sin5-keng-be2 teh toa3 e5 sou2-chai7. Chit e5 sin5-keng-be2 khi3-ku7, kio3-choe3 chhiok-kak-sio2-the2, (觸覺小體, tactile corpuscles; te7 89 tou5). Chit e5 chhiok-kak si7 chai-iaN2 kun7-oa2 e5 but8-the2, piau2-bin7 si7 kut8-tiah8, a2-si7 chhou-chhou koh-chai3 but8-the2 e5 lai7, tun, teng7, nng2, e5 hun-piat.

E7-phe5 si7 chhou-chhou e5 chou-chit kap iu5 lai5 chiaN5-e5; si7 hou7 lai7-pou7 e5 kut-mouh, a2-si7 kun, oe7-tit kiat-lian5 ti7 chin-phe5, sou2-i2 hou7 phe5-hu oe7 i5-tong7 kap chai7-chai7. Chit e5 kio3-choe3 phe5-e7-kiat-te3-chit.

Na7 seng-khu sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 khah siong5 eng7, hit e5 phe5 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 piN3 khah kau7. Siong5 chheng7-oe5 e5 lang5, i na7 thng3-chhiah-kha, chiu7 put-chi2 oh-tit kiaN5, in-ui7 soa oe7 tiam kha; chong2-si7 siong5 thng3-chhiah-kha e5 lang5 put-lun7 kham5-khiat8, a2-si7 chhou-soa long2 bo5 kan-khou2, in-ui7 kha-chhioh-toe2 e5 phe5 i2-keng kau7. Chheng-eng5 e5 lang5 chhiu2-phe5 si7 iu3-iu3, chong2-si7 choe3 chhou-kang e5 lang5 e5 chhiu2-phe5 si7 put-chi2 chhou; i na7 beh siu3-hoe, a2-si7 choe3 iu3-mih8 boe7-eng7-tit.

Phe5 e5 sek-ti7 bo5 sio-tang5; u7-e5 ou, u7-e5 ang5, u7-e5 ng5, u7-e5 peh8; che m7-si7 phe5 u7 hit ho7 sek, si7 piau2-phe5 e5 liam5-ek8-chan5 soe3-pau-lai7 u7 iu3-iu3 e5 liap8. Hit e5 liap8 na7 ou, phe5 khoaN3 ia7 si7 ou, liap8 na7 ng5, phe5 khoaN3 ia7 si7 ng5, ki5-u5 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 sek, long2 si7 an2-ni. Hit e5 iu3-iu3 e5 liap8 na7 kiN3 jit8, chiu7-oe7 piN3 khah ou, sou2-i2 lang5 khah siong5 phak8 jit8, i e5 bin7-sek chiu7 oe7 khah ou; siong5-siong5 tiam3 chhu3-lai7, bin7-sek chiu7 oe7 khah peh8.

Chin-phe5 e5 lai7-bin7, m7-na7 u7 mng5-soe3-huih-kng2 kap sin5-keng; iah8 u7 phe5-chi-choaN5 (皮脂腺, sebaceous gland) kap han7-choaN5 (汗腺, sweat gland). Phe5-chi-choaN5 e7 hun-pi3 iu5 lau5 kau3 mng5-kng2 e5 sou2-chai7, lai5 lun7-tek8 mng5-kng2 kap phe5, hou7 i khah nng2-lun7. 

Han7-choaN5 oe7 pai5-siat koaN7; khoan2-sit si7 chhin-chhiuN7 iu3-iu3 e5 kng2, thau3 kau3 pe5-hu e5 goa7-bin7, e7-toe2 kng2-le5; siat-su2 na7 oe7 thiu-tit8, chiu7 iok-liok8 nng7 saN hun e5 tng5. Seng-khu-nih8 iok-liok8 u7 238 ban7 e5 choe7; siat-su2 na7 oe7 chiap choe3 chit8 tiau5, chiu7 u7 nng7 saN phou3 lou7 e5 hng7. Phe5 e5 lai7-bin7 tak8 sou2-chai7, u7 chin choe7 han7-choaN5, chong2-si7 choe7 chio2 bo5 saN-tang5. Chhiu2-chiuN2-nih8 hit sou2-chai7 chit8 chhun3 e5 su3-hong u7 2800-e5; iah8 u7 pat8 sou2-chai7 put-chi2 khah chio2, chit8 chhun3 su3-hong, chi3-u7 400-e5 na7-tiaN7. Teh choe3 kang e5 si5, a2-si7 joah8 thiN e5 si5, u7 lai5 koaN7; chiu7 oe7 khoaN3-kiN3 chin beng5; chit ho7 kio3-choe3 hoat-koaN7; chong2-si7 peng5-siong5 put-si5 thong seng-khu iah8 u7 teh lau5-koaN, na7-si7 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3; chit ho7 kio3-choe3 chui2-hun-cheng-hoat (水分蒸發, insensible perspiration). Phi2-ju7 lang5 ni5-saN ti7 tek-ko, un5-un5-a2 ta-khi3, boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 chui2-khi3, an3-chaiN2-iuN7 bo5-khi3; lang5 peng5-siong5 sou2 lau5 m7-chai e5 koaN7, iah8 si7 an2-ni2.

Chit e5 koaN7 chit8 jit8 sou2 lau5-e5, na7 oe7 long2-chong2 chu7-chip8 lai5 niu7, tai7-khai3 u7 kun-poaN3 e5 tang7. Kho2-kian3 lai7-saN bak-tioh8 la5-sam5, m7-thang chheng7 siuN ku2, tioh8 siong5 soe2. Lang5 beh khun3 e5 si5, tioh8 oaN7 khun3-saN, chiong jit8-si5 sou2 chheng7 e5 saN, tiau3-teh chhe hong, kau3 bin5-a2-jit8 thang koh chheng7; mi5-si5 e5 khun3-saN, jit8-si5 tioh8 chhe hong, an2-ni lai5 thoe3-oaN7.

Siat-su2 na7 kou5-bat8 han7-choaN5 e5 kng2, hou7 i boe7 lau5 koaN7, seng-khu chiu7 oe7 phoa3-piN7. Sou2-i2 tioh8 siong5-siong5 soe2 seng-khu, bian2-tit u7 la5-sam5 lai5 that-bat8 han7-choaN5 e5 kng2; che si7 put-chi2 iau3-kin2.
