Na7 kong2-khi2 bo2-gi2 kau3-iok8, toa7 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 long2 sui5 siuN7 tioh8 lan2 se3-han3 cho3 gin2-a2 si5 teh liam7 teh thak8 e5 koa-iau5, si-su5, a2-si7 liam7-chhui3-kou2. Chiah..e5 tong-jian5 si7 bo2-gi2 kau3-iok8 kau3-chai5 e5 chit8 pou7-hun7. Sng3-si7 lan2 bun5-hoa3 e5 chhai3-boe2. M7-ku2, he bo5 eng3-kai si7 chu2-iau3..e5. U7 chin-che7 bo2-gi2 e5 kau3-chai5 long2 kan-ta teh thng7 hiah-e5 chhin3-png7 kap chhin3-chhai3. An2-ne choat8-tui3 bo5 chian5-tou5! 
Chit-ma2, lan2 chin ai3 iong7 “lui5 na2 sin-su7(雷na2 紳士) “ chit e5 koan-liam7 lai5 phi2-ju7 lan2 tng-teh cho3 e5 khang-khoe3. Renaissance si7 teh kong2 Au-chiu tui3 1300 ni5-tai7 khai-si2 kau3 1650 ni5 cho2-iu7, chit 350 ni5 e5 bun5-ge7 hok8-heng un7-tong7. Chit e5 un7-tong7 siong7 ki-pun2-tek e5 thau5-lou7 chiu7-si7 “kut8 pun3-so3 tui.” Kiat-ko2 ti7 pun3-so3 tui kut8 tioh8 po2, au7--lai5 chiaN5-cho3 jin5-lui7 bun5-beng5 e5 khai-lou7 sian-hong! Su tong-si ti7 I-ta2-lih e tiong-san2 kai-kip chiah8-pa2 siuN-eng5 tioh8 khi3 thak8 kou2-cha2 e5 kou2-chheh, tek8-piat8 si7 kou2 Hi-lah8 kap Lo5-ma2 e5 bun5-hak8. Koh gian2-kiu3 chu7-iu5 e5 su-siong2. Hit e5 chit-ma2 choan5 se3-kai3 long2 bat e5 Lan2-teh (Dante Alighiere,1265~1321) ka7 i siong7-kai3 lau2 e5 La-teng bun5 tan3 ti7 piN..a m7 iong7, khiok thiau-kang iong7 i ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2 lai5 sia2 bun5-chiuN. TaN lan2 long2 chai-iaN2 i sia2 e5 <Sin5-khek> <Divine Comedy> si7 se3-kai3 chhut-mia5 e5 bun5-hak8 chok-phin2. I su2-iong7 e5 pe7-bo2 oe7 ma7 pian3-cho3 I-ta2-lih e5 cheng3-sek gi2-gian5. M7-ku2 ti7 hit tong-si5 i siu7--tioh8 e5 thai7-gu7 si7 an2-choaN2 leh? Hou7 ka-ki7 e5 lang5 khau-se2, khoaN3 be7 chiuN7-bak8!!
