

1917 Ni5 e5 Tai5-gi2-bun5
             LUN7 PHE5-HU                                                       Te3 Jin5-su7 I-su  
 
Ph?-hu pau t? seng-khu ? gaa-b?n; i ? chok-iang s? chi…u ?-tae:
1.	Si7 po2-hou7 kun-bah.
2.	U7 chhiok-kak e5 chok-iong7, chiu7-si7 bong-tioh8 goa7-ui7 chiu7 chai e5 khoan2-sit. Si7 in-ui7 phe5-hu e5 chhiok-kak-sio2-the2; i e5 lai7-bin7 e5 sin5-keng u7 be2-liu. Chhiok-kak (觸覺, touch sense), hiah-e5 e5 te7 it gau5 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7-si7 chhiu2-chng2-thau5-a2, chih8 e5 be2-liu, chhui3-tun5.
3.	Phe5 si7 pai5-siat-khi3 e5 chit8-e5; i e5 chok-iong7 si7 chhut-koaN7. KoaN7 si7 chui2 e5 chit. KoaN7, chit8 pah hun7 e5 lai7-bin7, nng7 hun7 si7 koaN7-sng e5 iam5-hun, kap iu5.
4.	Phe5 u7 khip-siu e5 khui3-lat8, chhin-chhiuN7 sui2-gun5 lui7 e5 ioh8, oe7 khip-jip8 ti7 phe5-hu-lai7.
5.	Si7 tiau-ho5 lang5 seng-khu e5 the2-un; chhin-chhiuN7 na7 thiN-khi3 khah joah8 e5 si5, hiah e5 han7-choaN5-kng2 e5 be2-liu tiuN3 koh khui, sou2-i2 koaN7 na7 lau5-chhut-lai5, lang5 chiu7 khah liang5-leng2, khoaN3-oah8. Na7 thiN-khi3 khah leng2 e5 si5, hiah e5 kng2 oe7 khah kiu-oa2, khah bo5 hoat koaN7, ia7 hiah e5 khah oa2 ti7 phe5-hu e5 huih-kng2, oe7 kiu, jiat8-khi3 chiu7 khah boe7 tui3 phe5-hu li7-khui seng-khu, hoan2-tng2, toa3 ti7 seng-khu-lai7, sou2-i2 lang5 chiu7 oe7 sio-lo7.
Phe5-hu hun choe3 nng7 chan5. Teng2 bin7 chan5 kio3-choe3 piau2-phe5 (表皮, epidermis) ; te7 ji7 kio3-choe3 chin-phe5 (真皮, dermis).
Ti7 chit nng7 chan5 e5 e7-toe2 u7 kiat-te3-chit, chit-e5 kio3-choe3 phe5-e7-kiat-te3-chit (皮下結締織, subcutaneous connective tissue; te7 88 tou5).
Piau2-phe5 si7 chiau3 i e5 mia5 sou2 kong2, ti7 phe5-hu7 e5 choe3 te7 it goa7-khau2 e5 chou-chit, si7 kui2-na7 chan5 e5 soe3-pau lai5 chiaN5-e5.
Piau2-phe5 lai7 long2 bo5 huih-kng2; ti7 piau2-phe5 e5 choe3 te7 i(it?) chhim-chan5 e5 soe3-pau e5 tiong-kan, u7 te7 it soe3 tiau5 e5 sin5-keng.
Piau2-phe5 ia7 siong5-siong5 u7 phi2 poh8-phiN3 poh8-phiN3 teh hiauh-khi3-lai5. Thau5-khak u7 khah choe7, kio3-choe3 thau5-phou; thong seng-khu long2 si7 an2-ni, siong5-siong5 teh hiauh-khi3-lai5.
Chin-phe5 si7 phe5-hu chou-chit e5 te7-ji7 chan5; chit-e5 si7 iu3 koh bat8 e5 chou-chit lai5 chiaN5-e5. Chiap ti7 piau2-phe5 e7-bin7 bo5 piN5-piN5, u7 chin choe7 lui5-e5, kio3-choe3 leng-thau5 (乳頭, papilla). Chiah e5 leng-thau5 chiu7-si7 mng5-soe3-kng2, kap sin5-keng-be2 teh toa3 e5 sou2-chai7. Chit e5 sin5-keng-be2 khi3-ku7, kio3-choe3 chhiok-kak-sio2-the2, (觸覺小體, tactile corpuscles; te7 89 tou5). Chit e5 chhiok-kak si7 chai-iaN2 kun7-oa2 e5 but8-the2, piau2-bin7 si7 kut8-tiah8, a2-si7 chhou-chhou  koh-chai3 but8-the2 e5 lai7, tun, teng7, nng2, e5 hun-piat.
E7-phe5 si7 chhou-chhou e5 chou-chit kap iu5 lai5 chiaN5-e5; si7 hou7 lai7-pou7 e5 kut-mouh, a2-si7 kun, oe7-tit kiat-lian5 ti7 chin-phe5, sou2-i2 hou7 phe5-hu oe7 i5-tong7 kap chai7-chai7. Chit e5 kio3-choe3 phe5-e7-kiat-te3-chit.
Na7 seng-khu sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 khah siong5 eng7, hit e5 phe5 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 piN3 khah kau7. Siong5 chheng7-oe5 e5 lang5, i na7 thng3-chhiah-kha, chiu7 put-chi2 oh-tit kiaN5, in-ui7 soa oe7 tiam kha; chong2-si7 siong5 thng3-chhiah-kha e5 lang5 put-lun7 kham5-khiat8, a2-si7 chhou-soa long2 bo5 kan-khou2, in-ui7 kha-chhioh-toe2 e5 phe5 i2-keng kau7. Chheng-eng5 e5 lang5 chhiu2-phe5 si7 iu3-iu3, chong2-si7 choe3 chhou-kang e5 lang5 e5 chhiu2-phe5 si7 put-chi2 chhou; i na7 beh siu3-hoe, a2-si7 choe3 iu3-mih8 boe7-eng7-tit.
Phe5 e5 sek-ti7 bo5 sio-tang5; u7-e5 ou, u7-e5 ang5, u7-e5 ng5, u7-e5 peh8; che m7-si7 phe5 u7 hit ho7 sek, si7 piau2-phe5 e5 liam5-ek8-chan5 soe3-pau-lai7 u7 iu3-iu3 e5 liap8. Hit e5 liap8 na7 ou, phe5 khoaN3 ia7 si7 ou, liap8 na7 ng5, phe5 khoaN3 ia7 si7 ng5, ki5-u5 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 sek, long2 si7 an2-ni. Hit e5 iu3-iu3 e5 liap8 na7 kiN3 jit8, chiu7-oe7 piN3 khah ou, sou2-i2 lang5 khah siong5 phak8 jit8, i e5 bin7-sek chiu7 oe7 khah ou; siong5-siong5 tiam3 chhu3-lai7, bin7-sek chiu7 oe7 khah peh8.
Chin-phe5 e5 lai7-bin7, m7-na7 u7 mng5-soe3-huih-kng2 kap sin5-keng; iah8 u7 phe5-chi-choaN5 (皮脂腺, sebaceous gland) kap han7-choaN5 (汗腺, sweat gland). Phe5-chi-choaN5 e7 hun-pi3 iu5 lau5 kau3 mng5-kng2 e5 sou2-chai7, lai5 lun7-tek8 mng5-kng2 kap phe5, hou7 i khah nng2-lun7. 
Han7-choaN5 oe7 pai5-siat koaN7; khoan2-sit si7 chhin-chhiuN7 iu3-iu3 e5 kng2, thau3 kau3 pe5-hu e5 goa7-bin7, e7-toe2 kng2-le5; siat-su2 na7 oe7 thiu-tit8, chiu7 iok-liok8 nng7 saN hun e5 tng5. Seng-khu-nih8 iok-liok8 u7 238 ban7 e5 choe7; siat-su2 na7 oe7 chiap choe3 chit8 tiau5, chiu7 u7 nng7 saN phou3 lou7 e5 hng7. Phe5 e5 lai7-bin7 tak8 sou2-chai7, u7 chin choe7 han7-choaN5, chong2-si7 choe7 chio2 bo5 saN-tang5. Chhiu2-chiuN2-nih8 hit sou2-chai7 chit8 chhun3 e5 su3-hong u7 2800-e5; iah8 u7 pat8 sou2-chai7 put-chi2 khah chio2, chit8 chhun3 su3-hong, chi3-u7 400-e5 na7-tiaN7. Teh choe3 kang e5 si5, a2-si7 joah8 thiN e5 si5, u7 lai5 koaN7; chiu7 oe7 khoaN3-kiN3 chin beng5; chit ho7 kio3-choe3 hoat-koaN7; chong2-si7 peng5-siong5 put-si5 thong seng-khu iah8 u7 teh lau5-koaN, na7-si7 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3; chit ho7 kio3-choe3 chui2-hun-cheng-hoat (水分蒸發, insensible perspiration). Phi2-ju7 lang5 ni5-saN ti7 tek-ko, un5-un5-a2 ta-khi3, boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 hit e5 chui2-khi3, an3-chaiN2-iuN7 bo5-khi3; lang5 peng5-siong5 sou2 lau5 m7-chai e5 koaN7, iah8 si7 an2-ni2.
Chit e5 koaN7 chit8 jit8 sou2 lau5-e5, na7 oe7 long2-chong2 chu7-chip8 lai5 niu7, tai7-khai3 u7 kun-poaN3 e5 tang7. Kho2-kian3 lai7-saN bak-tioh8 la5-sam5, m7-thang chheng7 siuN ku2, tioh8 siong5 soe2. Lang5 beh khun3 e5 si5, tioh8 oaN7 khun3-saN, chiong jit8-si5 sou2 chheng7 e5 saN, tiau3-teh chhe hong, kau3 bin5-a2-jit8 thang koh chheng7; mi5-si5 e5 khun3-saN, jit8-si5 tioh8 chhe hong, an2-ni lai5 thoe3-oaN7.
Siat-su2 na7 kou5-bat8 han7-choaN5 e5 kng2, hou7 i boe7 lau5 koaN7, seng-khu chiu7 oe7 phoa3-piN7. Sou2-i2 tioh8 siong5-siong5 soe2 seng-khu, bian2-tit u7 la5-sam5 lai5 that-bat8 han7-choaN5 e5 kng2; che si7 put-chi2 iau3-kin2.
Te7 it ho2 e5 hoat-tou7, chiu7-si7 cha2-khi2 si5 khun3-khi2-lai5 thong seng-khu lai5 soe2, au7-lai5 eng7 chhou-pou3 chhut lat8 chhit kau3 phe5-hu sio-lo7; kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 bo5 ek8-ti5, na7 kan-ta eng7 bin7-thang2 toe2 chui2, toa7 te3 pou3 lai5 jiu5, iah8 kho2-i2. Lang5 tu2-a2 chiah8-pa2, m7-thang soe2-ek8, kiaN-liau2 oe7 chou2-tong3 siau-hoa3 e5 tai7-chi3.
Lang5 e5 phe5-hu na7 u7 oan5-choan5, bong-tioh8 u7 soe3-khun2 e5 mih8 boe7 jiam2-tioh8; chong2-si7 na7 siu7-siong, put-lun7 bak8-chiu boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 e5, na7 bong-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 mih8, chiu7 hit e5 tok8 khoai3-khoai3 khip-siu ti7 huih-nih8. Tui3 chit e5 sio2-khoa2 e5 goan5-in, bat u7 i-seng kap khan3-hou7 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7.
Ti7 seng-khu e5 phe5-hu-nih8 u7 mng5 chin choe7. Tak8 ki e5 mng5-kng2 u7 teng3 jip8 ti7 phe5 e5 lai7-bin7. Mng5-kng2 e5 thau5 u7 kun, sou2-i2 oe7 hoat-mng5; hit e5 kun na7 bo5-khi3, chiu7 boe7 koh hoat; phi2-ju7 chhin-chhiuN7 chhau2-a2, na7 liam3 i e5 hioh8, a2-si7 liam3 ki oe7 koh hoat, kun na7 khau-khi2-lai5, chiu7 long2 bo5 hoat. Chiah e5 mng5 tak8 ki u7 goa7-bin7 teng7-teng7 e5 chou-chit lai7-bin7 khah nng2-e5, kio3-choe3 chhe2-chit (髓質, medulla te7 88 tou5) mng5 e5 lai7-bin7 bo5 sin5-keng, sou2-i2 chhut-chai7 lang5 chian2, iah8 boe7 thiaN3; na7-si7 phe5 e5 lai7-bin7 chiu7 u7, sou2-i2 chhoah-mng5 chiah oe7 thiaN3.
Ti7 chng2-thau5-a2 e5 be2 u7 chng2-kah, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 kah e5 lui7, chhah jip8 ti7 phe5 e5 lai7-bin7, hou7 lang5 ni mih8, liam3 mih8 khah li7-pian7, koh oe7 pang-chan7 chng2-thau5-a2 khah u7 lat8. Chng2-kah si7 soeh jip8 phe5 lai7-bin7 e5 khang-hun5, tui3 jiau2-chhng5 kap jiau2-bu2 tit-tioh8 iuN2-chhi7, chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 khah tng5. U7-si5 chng2-kah siu7-siong thng3 kah, hit e5 jiau2-bu2, na7 bo5 phaiN2-khi3, chiu7 oe7 koh hoat; jiau2-bu2 na7 phah-phaiN2, chiu7 boe7 koh hoat. Beh chai chng2-kah e5 kai2-pho3, tioh8 khoaN3 te7 90 tou5. (Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8, te7 8 chiuN, te7 110-115 iah8)
