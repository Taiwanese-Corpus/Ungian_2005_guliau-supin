Chhun-kiau kap Chi3-beng5 2 lang5 beh khi3 hou7 peng5-iu2 chhiaN2. Chhun-kiau seN-cho3 lang5 iau-kiau bi2-le7, chheng7-chhah koh ko-siong2. Che7 jip8 chhia-lai7 chiah kong2 i bo5 beh ha5 an-choan5-toa3, in-ui7 an-choan5-toa3 ha5 tiam3 seng-khu, e7 ka7 hiah sui2-tang-tang e5 chng-thaN7 pho3-hai7 liau2-liau2. Kau3 chia, Chi3-beng5 kam2-kak tai7-chi3 toa7-tiau5--louh! Na7-si7 bo5 ha5 an-choan5-toa3 khi3 hou7 kau-thong..e5 chah8--loh8--lai5, chiu7 ai3 hoat8-kim 1,500 khou, bo5 phah-sng3 na7 kap lang5 sio chong5, khiong2-kiaN u7 siN3-mia7 e5 gui5-hiam2. Sou2-i2 in ang-a2-bou2 ti7 chhia-lai7 chiu7 oan-ke sio-me7, hoah kah toa7-se3 siaN. Hit-tiap8 ke-po5-sin5 tai3 chin tang7 e5 Chong..a chiu7 oa2-khi3 ka7 in chiam3. Chhun-kiau sui5 tau5 kong2 Chi3-beng5 kan-ta e7-hiau2 gu5-tiau5 lai7 tak gu5-bo2 na7-tiaN7. Chi3-beng5..a chiok bo5-nai7 kong2 long2 ma7-si7 ui7-tioh8 an-choan5. Chong..a ia7 ka7 i chan3-chhui3 kong2 an-choan5-toa3 it-teng7 ai3 ha5, na7-bo5, gu5-leng-thng5-a2 si7 e7 chhak8 phoa3 ia5-chi2-khak. ThiaN kau3 chia in ang-a2-bou2 soa3 hiong5-hiong5 gang7--khi3, sa bo5 liau5-a2-mng5, m7-chai chit-e5 ke-po5 Chong..a si7 teh kong2 sim2-mih8.
	
 




Issac Newton  (1642-1727)

Eng-kok e5 But8-li2 hak8-ka Niu2-thun3 (Isaac Newton) e7-sai2 kong2 si7 But8-li2-hak8 e5 khai-ki-chou2, ti7 1686-ni5 i sou2 hoat-piau2 e5 Ban7-iu2 in2-lek8 teng7-lut8(Law of universal gravitatial) khui-thiah “lat8” e5 to7-li2. ia5-chi2-khak








     gu5-leng thng5-a2

 	F chiu7-si7 “lat8”, mih8-kiaN7 e5 tong7-liong7 pian3-hoa3 lai5 hou7 si5-kan pun. Chhin-chhiuN7 po-le5-poe-a2 ui3 toh-teng2 poah8--loh8--khi3, tiaN7-tiaN7 e7 siak phoa3. Chit e5 in-toaN chiu7-si7 poe-a2 kap thou5-kha chiap-chhiok e5 si5-kan chin te2, ti3-su2 thou5-kha chok-iong7 ti7 poe-a2 e5 lat8 pian3 chiaN5 chiok toa7, lai5 ka7 po-le5-poe-a2 long3 phoa3. Na7-si7 tiam3 thou5-kha phou 1 nia2 mi5-chioh-phoe7, po-le5-poe-a2 ui3 toh-teng2 poah8--loh8--khi3, chiu7 siak be7 phoa3. Sui-bong2 poe-a2 poah8--loh8 e5 tong7-liong7 pian3-hoa3 sio-siang7, m7-ku2 mi5-phoe7 ka7 poe-a2 kap thou5-kha sio long3 e5 si5-kan ian5-tng5, hou7 in e5 chok-iong7 lat8 pian3 se3. Ti7 un7-tong7-tiaN5 e5 thiau3-koan5 pi2-sai3, ui7-tioh8 un7-tong7-oan5 e5 an-choan5, it -teng7 ai3 phou 1 te3 kau7-kau7-kau7 e5 hai2-mi5-tiam, i e5 bok8-tek chiu7-si7 beh ian5-tng5 sio long3 e5 si5-kan, lai5 hou7 chok-iong7 lat8 pian3 se3, po2-hou7 un7-tong7-oan5.
	Chit-ma2 lai5 chhi3-giam7 gu5-leng thng5-a2 chhak8 phoa3 ia5-chi2-khak e5 tai7-chi3. Theh8 1 te3 gu5-leng thng5-a2 ka7 1 thau5 liap8 hou7 i chiam-chiam, leng7-goa7 hit thau5 khng3 tiam3 thou5-kha. Mouh 1-liap8 ia5-chi2-khak tiam3 gu5-leng thng5-a2 e5 teng2-koan5, chhin-chhiuN7 chiaN3-peng5 e5 tou5 an2-ni. Koh lai5 chiu7 si7 ka7 ia5-chi2-khak siong3-chin-chin ui3 gu5-leng thng5-a2 hmh--louh8--khi3, chit te3 gu5-leng thng5-a2 chiu7 e7 chhak8-jip8 ia5-chi2-khak. Si7 sim2-mih8 goan5-in chiam-chiam nng2-nng2 e5 gu5-leng thng5-a2 u7 hoat-tou7 chhak8 phoa3 ia5-chi2-khak? Che chiu7-si7 kap teng2-bin7 thou5-kha long3 phoa3 po-le5-poe-a2 sio-siang7, in-ui7 chiap-chhiok e5 si5-kan chin te2, ti3-su2 gu5-leng thng5-a2 chok-iong7 ti7 ia5-chi2-khak e5 lat8 pian3-chiaN5 chiok toa7. 
	Chit e5 ke-po5 Chong..a ka7 gu5-leng thng5-a2 chhak8 phoa3 ia5-chi2-khak e5 chhi3-giam7 cho3 hou7 Chi3-beng5 in ang-a2 bou2 khoaN3 liau2, Chhun-kiau chiah chai che7-chhia ha5 an-choan5-toa3 chiu7-si7 beh po2-hou7 ka-ki7 e5 an-choan5. Na7 bo5, chhia lai5 sio chong5, a2-si7 hiong5-hiong5 tong3-chhia, che7 tiam3 chhia-lai7 e5 lang5 chiu7 e7 khap8-tioh8 chhia-khak. Lang5 lai5 hou7 chiok toa7 e5 lat8 long3--tioh8, sou2 san2-seng e5 siong-hai7 chiu7 chin siong-tiong7--louh! 2 lang5 ka7 Chong..a soeh lou2-lat8 liau2, Chhun-kiau chiu7 ha5 an-choan5-toa3 hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2 khi3 hou7 peng5-iu2 chhiaN2--lah.
