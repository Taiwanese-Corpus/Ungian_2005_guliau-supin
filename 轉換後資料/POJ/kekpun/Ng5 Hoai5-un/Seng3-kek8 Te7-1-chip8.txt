CHOK-CHIA2 E5 OE7
Ti7 lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7, Seng3-tan3 cheh kap Koh-oah8 cheh e5 Seng3-kek8 piau2-ian2 si7 chiaN5-cho3 chit8-e5 phou2-siong5 e5 hian7-siong7; khiok sui-jian5 u7 che7-che7 kau3-phai3 teh hoan2-tui3 chit e5 su7, iau2-ku2 chit e5 Seng3-kek8 piau2-ian2 si7 chit8-e5 oh-tit hun-li7 e5 su7.
Seng3-kek8, goan5-lai5 e5 i3-gi7 si7 ai3 beh tai7-thoe3 soat-kau3, cheng3-to7, si7 kang2-to7 e5 leng7-goa7 chit8-e5 hong-sek, chiu7-si7 beh eng7 kui2-na7 lang5 e5 heng5-tong7 lai5 beh hou7 chit cham7 Seng3-keng e5 ka3-si7 tit-tioh8 chhian2-beng5. Seng3-kek8 na7 u7 kin-ku3 ti7 chit e5 i3-su3, koh soa3 oe7 hou7 lang5 ke-thiN bat Siong7-te3 e5 oe7, goa2 siuN7 si7 bo5 m7-tioh8 e5 su7. Chong2-si7 na7 sit-loh8 i2-siong7 e5 cheng-sin5, si7 put-chi2 u7 gui5-hiam2 seng3.
Chhin-chhiuN7 lan2 bo5 sui5-pian7 liah8 lai5 hou7 i khia7 ti7 Seng3 e5 te7-ui7 lai5 tui3 lan2 cheng3-to7, in-ui7 lan2 kiaN liau2 i oe7 pai7-hoai7 lan2, chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni, koan-he7 kau2-hoe7 ian2-kek8 e5 su7, choe3 iau3-kin2 lan2 tioh8 keng2 kek8-pun2,chiu7-si7 sit8-chai7 si7 oe7 pang-chan7 lan2 e5 kek8-pun2, m7-thang sui5-pian7 ian2, koh oe7-gu2, heng5-tong7 ia7 m7-thang koe3-thau5 ha7-chian7, chhou-siok8; ian2 e5 lang5 ia7 tioh8 chun5 kiaN-ui3 e5 sim, chun5 beh in2-chhoa7 lang5 bat Siong7-te3 e5 ka3-si7 e5 sim, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 soat-kau3-chia2 e5 sim-su thai7-tou7, eng7 ki5-to2 lai5 ian2 chiu7 bo5 m7-tioh8.
So2-i2 lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 koan-he7 chit e5 ian2-kek8 e5 su7 iau2 u7 chin-che7 tiam2 sit8-chai7 tioh8 ai3 kai2-kek e5 ......
TaN goa2 u7 chiong tam7-poh8 e5 chhai5-liau7 ai3 beh hou7 tai7-ke cho3 chham-kho2. Ki5-tiong chit phiN "富人與拉撒路" kin-ni e5 Koh-oah8 cheh e5 si5 goa2 lim5-si5 khoaN3 su-iau3 lai5 phian, soa3 hou7 chheng-lian5 ian2 e5......
Tui3 thau5 kau3 boe2 e5 si5-kan u7 1 tiam2 30 hun i2-siong7 kau3 2 tiam2 cheng ku2, thang kong2 si7 chin tng5, gian5-su5 tioh8 tak8-ku3 chin beng5, heng5-tong7 ia7 tioh8 chin chu7-jian5, ian2-oan5 e5 jin5-but8, phoe3-iah8 ia7 chin iau3-kin2. Khah ho2 si7 u7 tu2-ho2 e5 to7-ian2-chia2 lai5 in2-chhoa7. Tioh8 u7 siong-tong e5 si5-kan cho3 chun2-pi7, khai-si2 lian7-sip8 kau3 chhut-ian2 tioh8 su-iau3 chit8-goeh8 poaN3 ku2. Hok8-chong ia7 tam-tng chin iau3-kin2 e5 chek-seng5, so2-i2 tioh8 u7 chun2-pi7.
"我明天要尋你" si7 tui3 e5 kou3-su7 lai5 phian e5......, hou7 Seng3-keng kho e5 hak8-seng lai5 ian2 tu2-a2 ho2.
Goan7 Siong7-te3 chiok-hok ti7 chit e5 teng2-bin7.
