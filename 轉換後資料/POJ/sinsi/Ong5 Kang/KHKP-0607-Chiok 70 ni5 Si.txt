Ki5-au7 : Ong5 kang 
1.It-kiu2 sam-ngou2 Chu2 lim5-se3,     
Hian2-chhut toa7 kng chio3 thiN-e7;     
KiN3 kng chiu7 oe7 bat ThiN-pe7,     
Sin3 Chu2 oe7 kau3 An-lok8-ke.  
2. Ai3 hou7 thian-ha7 long2-chong2 chai,    
Eng-kok kui3-jin5 siu7 Chu2 phai3; 
It-pat liok8-ngou2 ni5 chiu7 lai5, 
Chiu7 eng7 Hok-im chio3 choan5-tai5. 
3.PhaiN2 lang5 tauh8-tauh8 ai3 ham7-hai7,     
Hak8-seng kho3 Chu3 sim shu7-chai7; 
In2-chhoa7 peN7 lang5 jip8 iN7-lai7,     
Koh eng7 chu5-pi ho2 khoan2-thai7.  
4. Mou5-ku2 siong5-siong5 ai3 jiau2-loan7, 
Chong2-si7 ThiN-pe7 u7 ban7-koan5; 
Pang-chan7 lou5-pok8 ho2-taN2 thoan5, 
Cheng2-chi2 ia7 kau3 moa2 Tai5-oan5.  
5. Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 kui3 hoe-hng5, 
Phang-hoe koe2-chi2 long2 chiau5-chng5; 
Mia5-siaN koan3-chhoan3 ti7 si3-hng, 
ThiN-pe7 Siong7-te3 tit eng5-kng.  
6. Thong Tai5 Lam5 Pak u7 pak keng2, 
Koah-chhai3 hioh8-e7 ho2 liang5-leng2; 
 
Poe-chiau2 hioh-khun3 sim cheng-eng, 
Khui-siaN gim5-si long2 bo5 theng5.  
7.TaN goan2 tai7-ke chin hoaN-hi2, 
Ai3 Chiok siat-kau3 chhit-chap8 ni5; 
Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chu5-pi, 
Gim5-si chheng-chan3 o-lo2 I.  
8.Kiu3-chu2 lai5-se3 choe3 ke-bo2, 
Eng-jin5 lai5 Tai5 choe3 ke-po5; 
Siu7 lang5 khun2-tiok8 bo5 hoan5-lo2,
Chhit-chap8 chiu-ni5 chhiuN3 seng3-ko.  
9.Khun2-kiu5 Siong7-te3 chhiaN2 Li2 thiaN,
Chun2 goan2 sou2 kiu5 oe7 tit chiaN5; 
ChhiaN2 eng7 Seng3 Sin5 kap goan2 kiaN5, 
Chhoa7 lang5 sin3 Chu2 chi3-chun mia5.  
10.Goan2 beh sit8-sim po3-tap Li2, 
Kiu5 Li2 Seng3 Sin5 lai5 hu5-chhi5, 
In2-chhoa7 choe7-jin5 teng5-thau5-siN, 
Choan5 Tai5 kau3-hoe7 toa7 heng-khi2.
