                            
Kam2-siong2 Koa
Ki5-au7:Ong5 Kang
1.Ki5-au7 hok-im cheng siaN te7, 
Choan5 Tai5 bo5 lang5 bat Siong7-te3, 
Be5 iuN5 si7 Chu2 chhut-lat8 chhoe7, 
Kau3-hoe7 kin-ni5 chhit-chap8 hoe3.  
2.Ko-hiong5 Kong-hng5 ji7 pat keng2, 
Ki5-au7 Kau3-hoe7 san-hai2 teng5, 
Koah-chhai3 toa7 chang5 chin bou7-seng7, 
Si7 Chu2 hu5-chou7 Seng3-hoe7 heng.  
3.Cheng2-chi2 si7 Chu2 theh8 lai5 cheng3,
Hioh e7 poe-chiau2 ho2 liang5 leng2, 
Jit8-ia7 poe lai5 jit8-ia7 choe7, 
Hng7 kin7 long2 lai5 ho2 kau-poe5.  
4.Chui2-lou7 Bok8-su gau5 keng-eng5, 
Seng3-tng5 kian3-ti3 na2 toa7 keng, 
Ki-tok hak8-seng lai5 chu7-hoe7, 
Hou7 goan2 tit-tioh8 hok chin-choe7.  
5.Tai7-ke kau-poe5 chiu7 na2 sek8, 
 
Pe3-hoe7 tit-tioh8 toa7 li7-ek, 
Jin7 cho3 hiaN-che2 chiu7 na2 chhin, 
Gim5-si o-lo2 tap Chu2 in.  
6.Lan2 si7 ThiN Pe7 sou2 keng2-soan2, 
Koh-chai3 su3 lan2 chin u7 koan5, 
Khi2-cho7 Seng3-tng5 ka thian-koan, 
Kok-hang7 siat-pi7 long2 oan5-choan5.  
7.To-sia7 hiaN-che2 chin kho2-chhu2, 
Kam-goan7 khek-ki2 lai5 kia3-hu3, 
Hou7 goan2 pe3-hoe7 oe7-tit chhiaN5, 
Goan2 tioh8 ka7 lin2 thoan5 mia5-siaN.  
8.Keng-eng5 Siong7-te3 kui3-hoe hng5, 
Mui5-kui3 pho5-to5 long2 chiau5-chng5, 
Phang-bi7 kong2-ko3 ti7 si3-hng, 
Bi7-sou3 kim ke3 long2 u7 sng3.  
9.Su3-ui5 pah kui8 chhu2 lai5 chai, 
Lam5-lu2 hiaN-che2 long2 chiau5 lai5, 
Phang-bi7 u7 sng3 ke3-tat8, 
Siong7-ha7 chong2-sng3 u7 chha-piat8.  
10.Hng5-lai7 pho5-to5 pek kheng7-hoe, 
 
Chheng3 kau3 su3-hai2 moa2-si3-koe3,
Chiu7-si7 hiaN-che2 lin2 e5 phang, 
Hou7 Chu2 siok8 chiaN5 thian-kok lang5.  
11.Su jin5 put-kho2 u7 su tin7, 
Kian-sim kho3 Chu2 jit8-jit8 chin3, 
Sui5 lang5 ka-ti7 siong5-se3 mng7, 
Lan2 si7 Kiu3-chu2 e5 eng5-kng.  
12.Ki5-au7 Kau3-hoe7 pho5-to5-chhiu7, 
Tang sai lam5 pak long2 si7 ki, 
Chhian-lang5 ban7-lang5 tit Chu2 kiu3, 
Chhit-chap8 chiu-ni5 ki3-liam7 I.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 609 koan3, 1935 ni5 12 geh8)
