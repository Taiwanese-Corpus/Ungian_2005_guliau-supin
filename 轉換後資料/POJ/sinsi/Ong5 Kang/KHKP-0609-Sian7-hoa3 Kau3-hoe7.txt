
Sian7-hoa3 Kau3-hoe7
Ong5 kang TiuN2-lo2
1. Sian7-hoa3 Kau3-hoe7 goan2 a-tia,     
Lou5 Siong2 Bok8-su goan2 a-nia5;     
Si7 goan2 a-pa chin ho2 mia7,     
Chhoa7 goan2 a-bu2 gau5 seN kiaN2.  
2. Chit8-the seN liau2 iu7 chit8-the,     
Sim-pu7 chhoa7 liau2 cho3 ta-ke;     
Chit goeh8 iu7 seN saN hiaN-che2,     
KiaN2-sun na2 ku2 thoan5 na2 che7.  
3. Goan2 nia5 ke-su7 chin gau5 hoaN7,     
SeN goan2 hiaN-moe7 na2 chang3-koaN7;     
Chit8-ke heng-ong7 chin lau7-jiat8,     
Eng5-kng Kiu3-chu2 chin ho5-kiat8.  
4. ChhiaN2 lin2 liat8-ui7 tau3 hoaN-hi2, 
HoaN-hi2 goan2 nia5 seN moe7 ti7; 
HiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 saN khan-chhiu2,
Tai7-ke hoaN-hi2 chiah8 bah chiu2.  
5. A-pa a-bo2 chin thiaN3-thang3, 
ChhiaN5 goan2 hiaN-moe7 long2 chiaN5 lang5; 
Su7-bu7 cho3 liau3 chin kng-thang, 
Eng5-kng Kiu3-chu2 mia5 chin phang.     
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 609 koan3, 1935 ni5 12 geh8)
