Chu3: Si I-su kong2 “saN-kha-hoe-thiau3” chiu7-si7 chin hoaN-hi2 e5 i3-su3.  Tai5-oan5-siok8-gi2 u7 1-ku3 “3-kha tio5, 2-kha thiau3”, eng3-kai chiu7-si3 Si I-su e5 i3-su3.)

Chhoa3--si3 soat-beng5: 1964 ni5 Si Tiat-sam I-su ti7 Ko-hiong5 I-hak8-iN7, Ko-hiong5 I-hak8-tai7-hak8 e5 chian5-sin, pit-giap8; 1965 ni5 the3-ngou2 liau2-au7, chiu7 khi3 Bi2-kok.  I si7 peN7-li2 kap goan5-chu2-leng5 i-hak8 e5 choan-ka. 1994 ni5-thau5 i li7-khui i-hak8 e5 khang-khoe3 choan-sim oe7-tou5.  2002 ni5 3 goeh8 19, Si Oe7-ka ti7 Ko-hiong5 Ai3-ho5 piN khoaN3 tioh8 chui2--nih8 e5 hi5-a2 oah8-thiau3-thiau3 iu7-koh choe7 kah e7 thiau3 chhut chui2-bin7.  I hui-siong5 hoaN-hi2 chiu7 sia2 1-siu2 si ho7-choe3 “Ai3-ho5”.  Te7-2 kang e7-pou Si Phok-su7 kap goa2 ti7 Ko-hiong5 Tek-seng Kau3-hoe7 tam5-khi2 Tai5-gi2 e5 tai7-chi3.  I the5-khi2 cheng5 1 kang ti7 Ai3-ho5 sou2 khoaN3--kiN3..e5 kap “Ai3-ho5” chit-siu2 si.  I kong2 u7 kui2-ji7 Han3-ji7 be7-hiau2 thak8 Tai5-gi2.  Chiu7 an2-ni goa2 chiong “Ai3-ho5” kai2-sia2 choe3 Lo5-ma2-ji7.  In-ui7 Ai3-ho5 u7 chin-choe7 chui2-chhau2 e5 koan-he7, ti7 Si Oe7-ka kap goa2 tu2-teh kong2 oe7 e5 si5, u7 choe7-choe7 si2 hi5-a2 phu5 ti7 Ai3-ho5 e5 chui2-bin7. Ho5--nih8 e5 chui2-chhau2 na7 siuN-choe7, me5--si5 chui2--nih8 e5 khong-khi3 bo5-ho2, hi5-a2 e7 si2.
