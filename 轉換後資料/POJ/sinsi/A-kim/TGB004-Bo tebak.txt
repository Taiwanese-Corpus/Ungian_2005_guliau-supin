Akim

Ui7 siaN2-mih beh oat8 koe3 thau5--khi3?
Tak8-pian3 bo5 se3-ji7 tu2--tioh8 e5 si5-chun7,
Tu2-a2-ho2『四眼交接』,
Li2 chong2--si5 to5-phiah goa2 e5 gan2-sin5,
ChhiuN7 1-e5 siu7-tioh8 kiaN-hiaN5 e5 gin2-a2,
M7 chai-iaN2 beh an2-choaN2,
Kan-taN chai-iaN2 siam2-phiah,
1-sut-a2 to bo5 am3-kham3 li2 e5 kiaN-hiaN5 ham7 thong3-khou2.
Ui7 siaN2-mih beh oat8 koe3 thau5--khi3?
Chu7 chiong5 hit-kiaN7 tai7-chi3 liau2-au7,
Li2 to7 it-tit8 si7 an2-ni e5 thai3-tou7,
Si7 sim-hi--a? Iah si7 tho2-iah--goa2?
Na7 si7 sim-hi,
Li2 bo5 eng-kai koh chai3 siam2-phiah goa2 e5 gan2-sin5.
ChhiaN2 li2 iong7-sim lai5 chu3-i3,
Li2 to7 chai-iaN2 goa2 i-keng m7 si7 hit-chun7 e5 goa2,
Hit-kiaN7 tai7-chi3 ma7 cha2 to7 kap si5-kan e5 khe-chui2 cho3-hoe2 lau5--khi3-a.
Na7 si5 tho2-iah--goa2,
Li2 ma7 m7 bian2 koh chai3 hoan5-lo2,
Goa2 e7 pi2 li2 koh khah kin2 soa2-khui gan2-sin5,
Goa2 e7 chiok chu7-jian5, chiok un-jiu5--e5 oat8 koe3 thau5--khi3,
ChhiuN7 1-e5 kiaN5 ti7 lou7--nih e5 lang5,
M7 bian2 koa3-i3 ma7 m7 bian2 sek8-sai7.
