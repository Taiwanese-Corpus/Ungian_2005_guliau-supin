
                        
Sim-sek Koa
E7-mng5 Lu2-oh8 e5 Hak8-seng
1. Khi2-thau5 khang-khang bo5 thiN-toe7,  
Chhin-chhiuN7 ou-am3 moa2 si3-koe3,  
Sam-ui7 it-the2 chin Chu2-chaiN2, 
Seng3-sin5 un7-tong7 ti7 hi2-lai7.             
2 .Chu2-chaiN2 e5 mia5 Ia5-ho5-hoa, 
Chi3-seng3 chi3-chun chi3 koaiN5-toa7,  
Peng3 bo5 khi2-thau5 bo5 lou7-be2, 
PiN5-piN5 eng2-eng2 cho3 Siong7-te3.       
3. ThiN-toe7 ban7-mih8 I chhong3-cho7, 
Kong2 u7 hit-ho7 chiu7 hit-ho7, 
Thau5-jit8 Siong7-te3 kong2 tioh8 kng, 
Chiu7 hun cha2-khi2 kap e5 hng.               
4.Ji7-jit8 cho7 thiN ho7 kiong-chhong, 
Hou7 teng2-e7 chui2 bo5 saN-thong,  
SaN-jit8 hun chui2 kap ta-toe7, 
Hoat-siN chhiu7-bak8 kap chhai3-soe.        
5. Si3-jit8 chhong3-cho7 jit8 geh8 chhiN, 
 
Lai5 hun jit8-mi5 tiaN7 si5 ni5, 
Gou7-jit8 cho7 chiau2 kap hi5-pih, 
SoaN-chiuN7 u7 chiau2 hai2 u7 hi5.               
6. Lak8-jit8 cho7 siu3 liok8-thiok thang5, 
Kiam cho7 lam5-lu2 kok chit-lang5, 
Chhit-jit8 ban7-mih8 long2 chiau5-pi7, 
Siong7-te3 an-hioh su3 hok-khi3.       
7. Lam5 si7 thou5 chhong3 mia5 A-tong,  
Siong7-te3 pun5 khi3 chiu7 chiaN5 lang5, 
Koh hou7 A-tong ho2-khun3 khi3, 
Thiu i hiap8-e7 kut chit8-ki.               
8. Cho7 chiaN5 hu7-jin5 mia5 Ha7-oa, 
SaN-chan7 liau7-li2 tang5 tioh8-boa5, 
Seng3-cheng5 long2 tang5 Siong7-te3 khoan2, 
Toe7-chiuN7 ban7-mih8 hou7 in koan2.               
9.Hou7 in kiat chiaN5 choe3 ang-bou2, 
Lai5 choe3 ban7-lang5 e5 si2-chou2, 
Tiam3 ti7 Ai-tian5 lok8-hng5 lai7, 
SaN-kang7 kou3-siu2 kap cheng3-chai.               
10. Hng5-lai7 ke2-chi2 ku3 in chiah8, 
 
Chi2 kim3 chit8-chang5 chiu7 kau3-giah8, 
Hit-chang5 ke2-chi2 si7 siaN2-ho7? 
Chiu7-si7 hun-piat sian7-ok ko2.               
11. Hu7-jin5 tai7-seng siu3 kui2 be5, 
Chio ang tang5 chiah8 gek8 Siong7-te3, 
Hoan7-choe7 pian3-phaiN2 chiah tioh8 si2, 
KoaN2-chhut lok8-hng5 sit hok-khi3.       
12. tong siu7 hoat8 tioh8 kan-khou2, 
Thoa-boa5 chiah thang pa2 pak-tou2, 
Ha7-oa siu7 hoat8 kan-khou2 siN, 
Lian5-lui7 au7-tai7 long2 an2-ni.              
13. Si2-chou2 gek8-beng7 li7 Siong7-te3, 
Au7-tai7 toa3 choe7 lai5 chhut si3, 
KiaN5 phaiN2 pai3 put8 pai3 bok8-chu2, 
Long2 tioh8 tim5-lun5 loh8 im-hu2.         
14. Seng3 Pe7 Siong7 mt3e si toa7-un, 
Ai3 kiu3 cheng3-lang5 bian2 tim5-lun5, 
Chhe khian2 Seng3-kiaN2 lai5 kiu3-se3, 
Ka7 lang5 koe2 hoe2 kui ThiN-pe7.        
15. Seng3-kiaN2 Ia5-sou kiu3-se3 Chu2, 
 
Tau5-thai Iu5-thai3 chai7-sek-lu2, 
The3 lang5 kiaN5 gi7 siu2 hoat-tou7, 
The3 lang5 siu7 si2 khui oah8-lou7.              
16. Thoan5-to7 i-piN7 koaN2-tiok8 kui2, 
Pian3 piaN2 hoah8 hong kiaN5 hai2-chui2, 
Koh oah8 si2-lang5 kiaN5 ki5-su7, 
Hian2-beng5 I si7 kiu3-se3 Chu2.      
17.Iu5-thai3 sin-su7 bo5 in-toaN, 
Ka2-bu5 Chu2 hoan2 liah8 sang3 koaN, 
Kiau-so cheng3-lang5 long2 saN2-te3, 
Kiong5-kiu5 teng3 Chu2 sip8-ji7-ke3.       
18. PhaiN2-lang5 siat-ke3 hai7 Kiu3-chu2, 
Tu2 chiaN5 Siong7-te3 kiu3 lang5 su7, 
Lan2 lang5 tui3 Chu2 kan-khou2 si2, 
Hou7 sia5 khun2-pak8 thang thoat-li7.        
19.Ia5-sou si2-liau2 tai5 loh8 bong7, 
SaN-jit8 koh-oah8 chhut chioh8-tong7, 
Koh-oah8 siap-jit8 tiam3 toe7-bin7, 
Ka3-si7 hak8 mseng3 kian-kou3 sin3.              
20.Au7-lai5 che7 hun5 koh chiuN7-thiN, 
 
Gou7-pah goa7 lang5 tang5 khoaN3-kiN3,
ThiN-teng2 chhut-siaN po3 in chai,  
Boat8-jit8 Kiu3-chu2 beh koh-lai5.              
21.Hit-si5 thiN, toe7, lang5, pian3-oaN7, 
Si2 e5 koh-oah8 siu7 sim2-phoaN3, 
Ho2 chiuN7 thian-tong5 hiong2 hok-khi3, 
PhaiN2 loh8 te7-gek8 eng2-oan2 si2.         
22.TaN khng3 cheng3-lang5 hoan2-hoe2 choe7, 
Pai3 put8 phaiN2-tai7 long2 tioh8 koe2, 
Bo5 liah8 koe2-phaiN2 choe3 kan-lan5, 
Khun2-kiu5 Seng3-sin5 I oe7 chan7.         
23.Sin3-kho3 Ia5-sou chiu7 tit kiu3, 
Keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3 u7 pi3-iu7, 
Sui-jian5 tiam3 ti7 chit se3-kan, 
Sin-khu kan-khou2 sim peng5-an.         
24.Cha2 am3 poah-kang thak8 Seng3-keng, 
M7-thang the-si5 kong2 bo5 eng5, 
Seng3-keng li7-ek put-chi2 toa7, 
Ka2 lang5 oh8 ho2 tit eng2-oah8 .          
25.Lak8-jit8 khun5-kin2 se3-kan su7, 
 
Chhit-jit8 an-hioh chiau3 kui-ku2, 
Gim5-si le2-pai3 thiaN to7-li2, 
Boe7 beng5 tioh8 kiu5 Chu2 chi2-si7 .          
26.Tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 long2 thang kiu5, 
Hap8 Chu2 chi2-i3 oe7 seng5-chiu7, 
Siong7-te3 un-tian2 si7 pian7-pian7, 
Chhim-sin3 chiu7 oe7 khoaN3 hian7-hian7 .           
27.Siong7-te3 na7 thiaN lan2 ki5-to2, 
Hou7 lan2 e7 tit tak8-hang7 ho2, 
Tioh8 po3 lang5 chai lai5 chhin-kun7, 
Hian2-beng5 lan2 sim u7 kam2-un.            
28.Hoan7 u7 thiaN-kiN3 e5 to7-li2,
Tu2-tioh8 peng5-iu2 tioh8 kong2-khi2, 
Pun2-sin kiaN5 ho2 cho3 bou5-iuN7, 
Chhin-chhiuN7 kng-teng chio3 lou7-chiuN7.             
29.In2-chhoa7 chhin-peng5 kau3 pai3-tng5, 
Hou7 in chai Chu2 kong-lo5 chng5, 
SaN-kap hok8-sai7 chin Siong7-te3, 
Kiam thiaN3 se3-kan cheng3 tai7-ke .             
30.Na7 an2-ni kiaN5 Chu2 hoaN-hi2, 
 
Bian2 kiaN chhio3 me7 u7 hok-khi3, 
Ke3-ong2 liau2-au7 oe7 chiuN7 thiN, 
Eng5-kng chun-kui3 ban7-ban7 ni5. 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 154 koan3, 1898 ni5 1 geh8)
