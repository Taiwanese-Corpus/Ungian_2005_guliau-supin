Simsng
Choan5 se3-kai3 to7 si7 China-lang5 khoaN3 ka-ti7 siong7 bo5, kek8 ai3 ho7 Eng-bun5 mia5.
China siong7 toa7 e5 ka-chok8 to7 si7 “Song3--si7 ka-chok8”, phou2-thong lang5 long2 kio3 in “Song3--si7 Ong5-tiau5”. Ong5-tiau5--nih u7 3 e5 kong-chu2, ho7-cho3 Song3 Kheng3-leng5 (宋慶齡), Song3 Bi2-leng5 (宋美齡) kap Song3 Ai2-leng5 (宋藹齡). In chu7 se3-han3 to7 oh8 Eng-gi2, koh ho7 chiaN5 sui2-khui3 e5 Eng-bun5 mia5: Jean, Joe, Holly.
U7 1 kang kok-ong5 chhoa7 3 e5 kong-chu2 khi3 chham-ka Bi2-kok leng2-su7 pan7 e5 am3-hoe7. Am3-hoe7 e5 si5, kok-ong5 chhoe7 tioh8 ki-hoe7 kin2 ka7 i hit 3 e5 siau7-kai3 hou7 Bi2-kok leng2-su7. I chiok kin2-tiuN, kong2 gah phih8-phih8 chhoah: “Chia--e5 si7 goan2 cha-bou2-kiaN2, toa7--e5 kio3 ‘Chin song2’ (Jean Song3), ji7--e5 kio3 ‘Chiok song2’ (Joe Song3), ban-kiaN2 kio3 ‘Hou7 li2 song2’ (Holly Song3). Na7 u7 in e7-tang3 hok8-bu7 e5 sou2-chai7, bian2 kheh-khi3, chhiaN2 chin7-liong7 hoan-hu3.”
