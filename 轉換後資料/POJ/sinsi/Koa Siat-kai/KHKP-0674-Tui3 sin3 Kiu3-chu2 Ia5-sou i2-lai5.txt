(Since Jesus came into my heart)

Koa Siat-kai 	
[Chit siu2 si si7 ?????聖歌te7 119 siu2]
[Tui3 goan5-bun5 chhi3-ek8]
1. Goa2 seng-gai5 toa7 kai2-pian3, 
In tit Chu2 toa7 un-tian2, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !
Goa2 sim-lai7 u7 chin kng, 
Si7 ku2-ku2 sou2 teh ng3,
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 ! 
Ho5  Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5,
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5, 
Sim hoaN-hi2, kau3 moa2-moa2, 
Na2 hai2-eng2 teh chhiuN3 koa, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5.  
2. Goa2 bo5 koh si3-koe3 seh8, 
KiaN5 bo5 lou7 kau3 sit be5, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !
Goa2 e5 choe7 moa2-moa2 si7, 
Tit soe2-tu5 kau3 chheng-khi3,
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !  
3. Goa2 sim-lai7 u7 ng3-bang7, 
Chin kian-kou3 koh un2-tang3, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 ! 
Bo5 koh u7 giau5-gi5 bun5, 
Lai5 jia-bat8 goa2 hu3-kun7, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !  
4. Si2 e5 khiN ia7 kng-beng5, 
Bian2 kiaN-hiaN5 thang chin3-heng5,
Tui sin Kiu-chu Ia-s i-lai ! 
ThiN-siaN5 mng5 ti7 thau5-cheng5, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !  
5. Goa2 beh khi3 thang khia7-khi2, 
Ti7 thiN-teng2 e5 siaN5-chhi7, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 ! 
Goa2 hoaN-hi2 koh moa2-i3, 
Na2 chin3-cheng5 na2 gim5-si, 
Tui3 sin3 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !  
(Kap gak8-phou2 e5 koan-he7, “Tui3 sin3 Kiu3- chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 !” chin ku2, ia7 thang oaN7 choe3 :-- “Tui3 sin3-kho3 Chu2 Ia5-sou i2-lai5 ! ”).  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 674 ho7,1941 ni5 5 geh8)
