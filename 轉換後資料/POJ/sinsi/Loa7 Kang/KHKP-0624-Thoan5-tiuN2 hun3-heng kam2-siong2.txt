Thoan5-tiuN2 hun3-heng kam2-siong2
(Chhau2-tun: Loa7 Kang)
1.Chhau2-tun sin3-tou5 chin hoaN-hi2, 
Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chu5-pi, 
Tit-tioh8 thoan5-tiuN2 lai5 hun3-si7, 
Kang2-ian2 Ki-tok e5 chin-li2.
2.Chit8-kun5 long2-chong2 kiong7 6 lang5, 
Lam5-lu2 kiong7-tong5 gau5 cho3 kang, 
Ka-teng5 su7-bu7 it-chin7 pang3, 
Ui7 Chu2 hi-seng gau5 cho3 kang.
3.Chin2-hong Bok8-su cho3 kou3-bun7, 
Ia7 lai5 chham-ka cho3 chit8 kun5, 
Soan-thoan5 Ki-tok e5 kiu3-un, 
Ai3 hou7 cheng3-lang5 lai5 kui-sun7.
 
4.Thoan5-tiuN2 chiu7-si7 Chhoa3 TiuN2-lo2, 
ChiuN7-tai5 pek-chhiat lai5 kang2-to7, 
U7 chiong boat8-se3 lai5 keng2-ko3, 
Hou7 goan2 sin3-sim toa7 kai2-cho7.
5.Chhoa3 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 u7 kian3-cheng3, 
Kong2 khoaN3 thian-tong5 chin hun-beng5,
Bian2-le7 tai7-ke tioh8 keng2-seng2,
Tui3 ti7 chin-li2 lai5 chin3-cheng5.
6.Tai5-tiong PiaN2-chim2 cho3 kian3-cheng3, 
Kong2 i kiaN2 si2 3 tiam2 cheng, 
Koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 toa7 soat-beng5, 
Khng3 lang5 sin3 Chu2 m7-thang theng5.
7.Khoan-ho5 Sian-seN chin hoe2-kai2, 
Tit-tioh8 tiong5-seng po3 lang5 chai, 
Cheng3-beng5 sou2 cho3 e5 phaiN2-tai7, 
 
Kam-goan7 long2-chong2 kong2 chhut-lai5.
8.I tit Seng3 Sin5 e5 chhiong-sit8, 
Tu2 si7 6 goeh8 17 jit8, 
Kap lang5 put-ho5 kiu5 liong7-kai2, 
Theh8 chhut pin5-ki3 hou7 lang5 chai.
9.Toa7-sia7 TiuN2-lo2 ka3 si-tiau7, 
Hou7 goan2 gim5-si khah e7 hiau2, 
I e5 sin3-giong2 chin kian-kou3,
Ui7-tioh8 kiu3 lang5 toa7 hun3-tou3.
10.Mui2 jit8 cho2-thian ki5-to2-hoe7, 
Leng5-seng3 chin ho2 e5 kau-poe5, 
Ki5-to2 tak8 e5 chin khun2-chhiat, 
Sim-lai7 Mou5-kui2 it-chin7 biat8.
11.Te5 bak8 long2-chong2 pan7 8 e5, 
 
Sou2 kau3 tak8 ui7 kong2 kang7 te5, 
Thau5-e5 si7 kong2 Sip8-ji7-ke3, 
Po3 Chu2 kiu3 in hou7 tai7-ke.
12.Te7 ji7 po2-hoeh e5 kiu3-siok8, 
Jiat8-chhiat chhian2-beng5 chin chim-chiok.  
Choe7-jin5 e7 thang tit kiu3-siok8, 
Tai7-ke thiaN-liau2 chin boan2-chiok.
13. Te7 saN Thian-lou7 kap Te7-gek8, 
Ku3-ku3 kong2 kau3 chin beng5-pek8, 
Tak8 lang5 thiaN-liau2 chin kam2-kek,
Ti3-kau3 sim-koaN toa7 chhiau-chhek8.
14. Te7 si3 thiat-ti2 e5 hoe2-kai2, 
M7-thang khng3 phaiN2 ti7 sim-lai7, 
Sim-koaN ok-tok8 kap che-tai2, 
Long2 tioh8 it-chin7 chheng chhut-lai5.
15. Te7 gou7 tiong5-seng chek eng2-seng, 
Tak8-ku3 to long2 chiau3 Seng3-keng, 
Put-chi2 chhut-lat8 lai5 pian7-beng5, 
Hou7 lang5 thiaN liau2 sin e7 chheng.
16. Te7 lak8 te5-bak8 si7 Sin3-sim, 
Cheng3-beng5 kou2-cha2 kap hian7-kim, 
Tak8-hang7 kong2-liau2 chiu pek-chhiat,
Theng3-chiong3 lang5-lang5 sim jiat8-jiat8.
17. Te7 chhit si7 Seng3 Sin5 kang-chok, 
Sin5 tiam3 ti7 sim chiu7 toa7 phok, 
Ho2-taN2 po3-iong5 Chu2 Ki-tok, 
Tai7-ke thiaN-liau2 chin kam2-hok8.
18.Chit hoe5 thoan5-tiuN2 lai5 hun3-heng,
Tu2-tioh8 siu-tang khah bo5-eng5,
Jit8-kan chip8-hoe7 lang5 khah chio2, 
 
Che si7 tui3 goan2 sin3-sim sio2.
19.Ki5-kiu5 Siong7-te3 kang3 Seng3 Sin5, 
Hou7 goan2 chhu2-khi3 khah jin7-chin, 
Ui7-tioh8 kiu3 lang5 khah iau3-kin2, 
Bo5 hou7 se3-cheng5 lai5 tiN5-sin.
20. Kiu5 Chu2 siuN2-su3 in iong2-kiaN7,
Chhut-lat8 cho3 kang gau5 phah-piaN3, 
Hou7 in sou2 cho3 e5 kang-tiaN5, 
Kui eng5-kng Kiu3-chu2 Seng3-mia5.
21. TaN goa2 kan2-tan sia2 chiah e5, 
Ui7-tioh8 choa2-bin7 e5 koan-he7, 
Liat8-ui7 ki5-to2 saN the5-he5, 
Choan5 To2 tong5-pau5 kui ThiN Pe7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 624 ho7, 1937 ni5 3 geh8)
