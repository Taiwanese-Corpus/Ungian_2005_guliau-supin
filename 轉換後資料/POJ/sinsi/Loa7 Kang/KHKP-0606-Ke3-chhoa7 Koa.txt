
Ke3-chhoa7 Koa
Chhau2-tun : Loa7 Kang
1.Hian7-si5 chit ku3 hui-siong5-si5, 
Sia7-hoe7 put-si5 teh kong2-khi2; 
Ke3-chhoa7 ia7-si7 hui-siong5-si5, 
Tai7-ke kaN2 ia7 long2 ti-ki2.  
2.Kau3-hoe7 ke3-chhoa7 oh tu2-ho2, 
In-ui7 thau5-nau2 u7 khah ko; 
Lam5-chu2 beh chhoa7 iu7 chhoa7 bo5, 
Lu2-chu2 beh ke3 be7 tu2-ho2.  
3.Kau3-hoe7 ke3-chhoa7 chin gau5 keng2, 
Ti3-kau3 cho2 chhin chin oh seng5; 
U-e5 beh keng2 ho2 phin2-heng: 
U-e5 beh keng2 khah chai5-cheng5.  
4.U-e5 beh keng2 khah khoaN3-oah8, 
KiaN-liau2 ke3-chhut e7 thoa-boa5; 
Lam5-chu2 u7-e5 bo5 tang2 chhoa7, 
Kou-put-chiong khi3 chhoa7 hoe7-goa7.  
5.Kau3-hoe7 ke3-chhoa7 khah kng-thang, 
Lang5 bo5 sin3-iong7 ti7 moe5-lang5; 
Pi2 lan2 khah ho2 chiaN3 beh pang3, 
Bo5 siuN7 lan2 si7 siaN2 ho7 lang5.  
6.Lun7 kau3 kau3-hoe7 cho3 chhin-chiaN5, 
Put-chi2 li7-pian7 thang tham3-thiaN; 
Sin3-sim phin2-heng7 u7 ou-iaN2, 
Beh cho3 u7-si5 cho3 be7 chiaN5.  
7. Iu7-koh u7-e5 bo5 li2-kai2,    
Bo5 siuN7 lan2 khia7 siaN2 sou2-chai7;    
Chhoa7 khoaN3 sian-lu2 thang loh8 lai5,    
Ke3 khoaN3 Phok-su7 a2 Siu3-chai5.  
8. Lam5-chu2 bo5 beh ka-ti7 pheng5,    
Chhoa7 beh Ko-teng2 lu2 hak8-seng;    
Lu2-chu2 bo5 siuN7 sim-mih8 teng2,    
Beh ke3 Phok-su7 a2 i2-seng.  
9.Lu2-chu2 u7-e5 tui3 oh ke3, 
Ni5-hoe3 ni5-ni5 it tit8 che7; 
U7-u7 khng3 kau3 saN-chap8 hoe3, 
Hap8-kiong7 kaN2 u7 sou3 pah--e5.   
10.Lang5 soeh lam5-chu2 m7 jin7 gong7, 
Chit ku3 siok8-gu2 to ia7 thong; 
Chhoa7 beh sian-lu2 bo5 tang3 chong5, 
Beh chhoa7 chhian-kim bo5 tang3 bong.  
 
11.Iu7-koh lu2-chu2 m7 jin7 bai2, 
Chit ku3 e5 oe7 ia7 sit8-chai; 
Cho3-chhin na7-si7 bo5 tai7-khai3, 
Gou7-liau2 chheng-chhun tit8-tit8 lai5.  
12.Kau3-hoe7 lu2-chu2 chhun-chin che7, 
Ong5 Oa2 Bok8-su e5 thong2-ke3; 
M7-ku2 beh chhoa7 oh tu2-ho2, 
Beh ke3 iu7 the keng2 to bo5.  
13.Goa2 siuN7 cho3 chhin tioh8 tai7-khai3, 
M7-bian2 beh keng2 lang5 tang sai;
Lan2 na7 sin3-giong2 lai5 khia7-chai7, 
Ho2 phaiN2 keng2 gu7 cho3 i lai5.  
14.Goa2 siuN7 cho3 chhin tioh8 kak-chhiN2, 
M7-bian2 beh keng2 loa7 ni7 tiN; 
In5 lang5 e7 piN2, piN2 e7 iN5, 
San3-hiong kiam2 tioh8 be7 chhun chiN5.  
15.Goa2 siuN7 cho3 chhin ai3 teng2-tui3, 
M7-bian2 beh keng2 loa7 ni7 sui2; 
A2-si7 beh keng2 loa7 koan5-ui7, 
Na7 u7 teng2-tui3 be7 khek-khui.  
 
16.Lu2-chu2 kong2 si7 chhai3-chi2 mia7,
Chit ku3 e5 oe7 si7 u7 iaN2; 
Kiam2 bo5 ke3 hou7 hu3-kui3 kiaN2, 
Kau3-boe2 khi3 loh8 khit-chiah8 iaN5.  
17.U7-e5 chhoa7 khi3 chin san3-hiong, 
Au7-lai5 ni5-ni5 na2 chhun-tiong5; 
U7-e5 chhoa7 khi3 e7 chhut-tiong3, 
Keng-eng5 seng-li2 cho3 toa7 siong.  
18.Goa2 siuN7 ke3-chhoa7 si7 ui5-lan5, 
Lan2 beh keng2 lang5,lang5 keng2 lan2; 
Lam5-chu2 che7 hoe3 bo5 siong-kan, 
Lu2-chu2 che7 hoe3 chin ui5-lan5.  
19.Ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 oh ke3-chhoa7,    
Che7-che7 cho3 chhut ti7 hoe7-goa7;    
Chong-kau3 bo5 siang7 kan-khou2 toa3,    
Kau3-hoe7 sun2-sit si7 chin toa7.  
20.Goa2 sia2 ke3-chhoa7 e5 bun7-te5, 
KiaN-liau2 choa2-bin7 e5 koan-he7; 
Kau3-hoe7 si7 toa7 tau3 the5-he5, 
TaN2-phoa3 hun-in e5 lan5-te5.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 606 koan3, 1935 ni5 9 geh8)
