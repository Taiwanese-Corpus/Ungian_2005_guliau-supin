
 
Sin3-tou5 heng5-ui5 Koa
LOA7 Kang       
1. Goa2 sia2 sin3-tou5 heng5-ui5 koa, 
Che7-che7 sin3 Chu2 bo5 pian3 oaN7,
Heng5-ui5 kiaN lang5 chin phaiN2 khoaN3, 
Si7 bo5 Seng3 Sin5 ti7 sim-koaN.  
2. Lan2 cho3 Ki-tok e5 hak8-seng, 
Si7 ai3 u7 ho2 e5 phin2-heng7,  
Gian5-gu2 phin2-heng7 tioh8 toan-cheng3, 
Su7-su7 si7 ai3 chiau3 Seng3-keng.  
3. Kho2-sioh u7-e5 bo5 sit8-chai7, 
Lai5 chhang3 mou5-kui2 ti7 sim-lai7, 
Heng5-ui5 iu5-goan5 si7 phaiN2-tai7,
Bu5-ok put-chok lam7-sam2 lai5.  
4. U7-e7 chit khoan2 to7-li2 chiaN, 
Lang5 na7 khou2-khng3 choan m7 thiaN, 
Kong2 i to7-li2 loa7 chai-iaN2, 
PhaiN2 lou7 cho3 i tit8-tit8 kiaN5.  
5. Kau3-hoe7 u7-e5 be7 chin2-heng, 
U7-e5 tian-to3 si7 khah leng2, 
Si7 tui3 sin3-tou5 u7 mau5-peng7, 
Cho3 chhut che7-che7 phaiN2 hong-pheng5.  
6. Kin7-lai5 kau3-hoe7 ai3 pou3-to7, 
Kong2 sin3 Ki-tok e7 kai2-cho7, 
Lang5 e5 phaiN2 heng7 e7 piN3 ho2, 
Sin3 Chu2 phaiN2-tai7 chiu7 e7 bo5.  
7. Chong2-si7 lan2 na7 phaiN2 phin2-heng7,
Chiu7 e7 siu7 lang5 e5 phoe-pheng5,  
Bou2 lang5 to ia7 bo5 toan-cheng3, 
SiaN2 lang5 to ia7 hiah khok heng5.  
8. Sui-jian5 kong2 kau3 chin phah-piaN3, 
Chhut chin7 khui3-lat8 toa7 se3 siaN, 
Lan2 e5 kiaN5-cho3 na7 bo5 iaN2,
Ong2-hui3 pou3-to7 e5 kang-tiaN5. 
9. Kau3-hoe7 na7 beh tit hok8-heng, 
Goa7 tioh8 pou3-to7 toa7 chhut-cheng, 
Lai7 tioh8 phin2-heng7 toa7 siu-cheng2, 
Kiong7-tong5 hiap8-lek8 ban7-su7 seng5.  
10. Sin3-tou5 tak8 jit8 tioh8 keng2-seng2, 
Put-si5 ki5-to2 Chu2 bo5 theng5, 
Kiam2-tiam2 lan2 kiaN5 e5 phin2-seng3,
Tioh8 hou7 sia7-hoe7 lai5 hoan-geng5. 
11. Kap lang5 chih-chiap tioh8 sin3-iong7, 
Sim-koaN m7-thang siuN7 kan-hiong5, 
Jin5-keh ia7-tioh8 saN chun-tiong7, 
M7-thang tiong7-hu3 khi san3-hiong.  
12. Sin3-tou5 ia7 tioh8 tiong7 sit8-chai7, 
M7-thang ke-kui pun5 chhut-lai5, 
Kap lang5 cho3 hoe2 tioh8 tai7-khai3, 
Su7-su7 si7 ai3 chiau3 pou7-lai5.  
13. Sin3-tou5 ia7-tioh8 ho2 seng3-te7,
M7-thang tin2-tang7 ok pe5-pe5, 
M7-thang khioh-oan3 ai3 oan-ke, 
Pi2-kun5 pi2-bu2 pi2 koan5-se3.  
14. Sin3-tou5 ia7-tioh8 gau5 thun-lun2, 
Gek8-lai5 sun7-siu7 hoaN-hi2 tng, 
Jim2-nai7 e5 lang5 u7 hok-khi3, 
Kiat-kiok8 tek-khak e7 song3-li7.  
15. Sin3-tou5 ia7-tioh8 gau5 khiam-pi, 
Su7-su7 un-jiu5 koh se3-ji7, 
Ia7-tioh8 m7-thang khoai3 siu7-khi3,
Tak8-hang7 kam-goan7 hong5 khah phiN.  
16. Sin3-tou5 bo5-lun7 toa7 sio2 su7,
Tak8-hang7 lan2 tioh8 jin7 khah su, 
Hian2-beng5 lan2 si7 tui3 sin3 Chu2, 
ChiaN3 o7 chit khoan2 bo5 ai3 su7.  
17. Hoan7-su7 si7 ai3 u7 saN niu7, 
M7-thang chip lan2 e5 chu2-tiuN, 
Tak8 lang5 kiaN5-cho3 ka-ti7 siuN7,   
M7-thang cho3-chhut phaiN2 bou5-iuN7.  
18. Sin3-tou5 cho3 su7 tioh8 chim-chiok, 
M7-thang cho3-liau2 hai7 tai7-kiok8, 
Hoan7-su7 long2-chong2 tioh8 chiau3-iok, 
M7-ho2 e5 su7 tioh8 khu-tio 
19. Sin3-tou5 si7 ai3 u7 liong7-sou3, 
Su7-su7 si7 ai3 saN jiong7-pou7 ; 
M7-thang kou3-chip sio oan3-tou3, 
Hian2-beng5 lan2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5.  
20. Sin3-tou5 m7-thang khng3 oan3-hin7, 
Na7 u7 kam2-chong5 liam5-piN chhin,
M7-thang liah8-cho3 bo5 iau3-kin2, 
Na7 khng3 oan3-hin7 chin kho2-lin5.  
21. Sin3-tou5 ka-teng5 tioh8 oan5-boan2, 
Ka-chok8 m7-thang kiat si2-oan, 
Pat8-lang5 m7-tioh8 lan2 bo5 koan2, 
Chu7-jin7 m7-tioh8 chiu7 be7 oan.  
22. M7-thang eng7 phaiN2 lai5 po3 phaiN2, 
Eng7 ho2 iaN5 phaiN2 chiah eng-kai,
Lan2 na7 eng7 ho2 saN khoan2-thai7, 
Bian2 kiaN lang5 oan3 u7 lou7 lai5.  
23. Sin3-tou5 tak8 e5 tioh8 saN-thiaN3, 
SaN-thiaN3 tioh8 liah8 na2 seN3-mia7, 
Tak8-hang7 eng7 thiaN3 cho3 seng kiaN5, 
Chiu7 e7 toa7-toa7 eng5 Chu2 mia5.  
24. Lan2 na7 put-si5 u7 thiaN3-cheng5, 
Chiu7 lan2 sim-koaN beh loa7 chheng, 
Seng-oah8 put-si5 na2 sian-keng2,
Thian-tong5 li2 goa2 e5 bak8-cheng5.  
25. Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 pun iong2-lu2, 
Tioh8 sioh na2-chun2 chhin kiaN2-ji5, 
M7-thang khou2-tok8 heng5-gek8 i, 
Hian2-beng5 lan2 u7 thiaN3 pi-bi5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 659~660,663 ho7,1940 ni5 2~3,6 geh8)
