
Kheng3-ho7 Hi-lian5 e5 Si
[ Tiong-hoe7 gi7-tiaN7 ku7-ni5 11 goeh8 21 ho7 tak8 sou2-chai7 e5 kau3-hoe7 tioh8 cho3 kheng3-ho7 Hi-lian5-hoe7, ia7 u7 thok-tiong7 Pa Bok8-su pi7-pan7 chit8 tiuN sun7-su7-toaN, hou7 tak8 sou2-chai7 thak8 e5 Seng3-chheh kap gim5 si long2 tioh8 saN-tang5.  Chiu7-chhiaN2 Gou5 Bun5-pin Sian-siN sia2 chit8 siu2 e5 si, cho3 hit-jit8 e5 lou7-eng7.Sia2-liau2 kia3-lai5 Hu2-siaN5, m7-ku2 Bok8-su i2-keng khi3 Jit8-pun2, chiu7 bo5 chiap-tioh8.  TaN chiong hit-siu2 e5 si in3 ti7 e7-te2 hou7 tai7-ke khoaN3, ia7 thang siau3-liam7 Gou5 Bun2-pin Sian-siN; sui-jian5 i taN bo5 ti7-teh, lan2 iau2-ku2 na7 chhin-chhiuN7 teh thiaN i e5 oe7.]  
1.Tai5-oan5 chin-li2 e5 khi2-thau5, 
Goan5 tui3 Eng-kok TiuN2-lo2-kau3 : 
TiuN2-lo2 kau3-hoe7 cheng3 sin3-tou5, 
Kam-goan7 khek-ki2 thiaN3 Ia5-sou.  
2.Chun2 sim siuN7 beh kiu3 leng5-hun5, 
Pi7-pan7 hok-im kiu3-leng5-chun5; 
Bou7-chip8 ian5-kim chheng ban7 oan5, 
Chiu7 chhiaN2 Bok8-su koe3 Tai5-oan5.  
3.M7-na7 kiu3 lang5 thoat choe7-kou, 
Iah8 ai3 i-peN7 e5 kan-khou2, 
Chiu7 kah I-seng pan7 ioh8-chai5, 
 
Soa3 kap Bok8-su cho3-hoe2 lai5.  
4.Li7-khui pe7-bo2 kap hiaN-ti7, 
Tah chun5 koe3-iuN5 chheng ban7 li2, 
Na2 chun5 khi3 ui5 koh sit toa7, 
Sui5 hong phiau-liu5 chai7 hai2-goa7.  
5.Chiong sin eng7 sim kau Siong7-te3, 
M7 kiaN siN si2 e5 koan-he7, 
Chiu7 kau3 Tai5-oan5 e5 Ki5-au7, 
ChiaN3 lai5 siat-lip8 kiu3-se2-kau3.  
6.Jin5-te7 chhiN-sou sim bong5-bong5, 
Bin7-mau7 im-gi2 put siong-tong5, 
Khia7-khi2 lim-chiah8 kap i-chiuN5, 
Jin5-cheng5 le2-mau7 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7.  
7.Ai3 beh kau-poe5 bo5 hoat-tou7, 
Khi2-thau5 sit8-chai7 toa7 kan-khou2, 
Au7-lai5 oh8 oe7 kau3 cheng-kong,
ChiaN3 e7 kap lang5 lai5 kau-thong.  
8.Ai3 chiong Siong7-te3 e5 in-su3, 
Po3 lang5 sin3-kho3 kiu3-se3 Chu2. 
Kho2-sioh jin5-sim siu7 be5-hek8, 
 
Soa3 kap to7-li2 cho3 tui3-tek8 .  
9.Kong2 tang kong2 sai oe7 sek che7, 
Hiam5 lang5 pai3 hoan e5 Siong-te3, 
Cheng2-cheng2 khun2-pek oh kong2-khi2 
Au7-lai5 na2 chai na2 hoaN-hi2 
10.Siong7-te3 in-tian2 it-hoat toa7, 
Pai3-tng5 na2 siat na2 khui-khoah. 
M7-na7 liau2 kang koh liau2 chiN5, 
I2-keng lek8-koe3 gou7-chap8 ni5 .  
11.Tak8 teng2 hak8-hau7 u7 siat-lip8, 
Hou7 lang5 li7-pian7 thang hak8-sip8, 
Chi2 be5 jip8 gou7 hoat-tou7 che7, 
Hou7 lan2 e7 hiau2 pai3 Siong7-te3 .  
12.Siong7-te3 un-tian2 kek8-ki5 toa7, 
Lan2 tioh8 kam2-sia7 long2 bo5 soah; 
Eng-kok sin3-tou5 toa7 jin5-ai3, 
Lan2 tioh8 ki3-liam7 chai7 sim-lai7 .  
13.M7-thang bong5-in koh poe7-gi7, 
An2-ni chiaN3-si7 u7 hap8-li2; 
 
Goa2 cho3 kui2 ku3 lai5 hian2-beng5, 
Ng3-bang7 tai7-ke tioh8 kam2-cheng5 .  
14.Hap8-kai khia7 pai5 po3 lang5 chai, 
Iah8 thang liu5-thoan5 kau3 tai7-tai7, 
Chiong in thiaN3 Chu2 lai5 ki3-chai3, 
Siong5-se3 khek ti7 hit chioh8-pai5 .  
15.Tak8 tai7 sin3-tou5 na7 iu2-gi7, 
Ai3 khoaN3 chioh8-pai5 e5 sou2-ki3; 
Cho3 si put-ko3 si7 tai7-liok8,
Bo5 thang tak8-hang7 kong2 chim-chiok.   
16.Na7 ai3 tak8-hang7 kong2 siong5-se3,
Tioh8 khi3 kang2-kiu3 hit ho7 chheh; 
Lan2 taN ki3-liam7 che jit8-chi2,
Tai7-ke tang5-sim teh hoaN-hi2 .  
17.ThiN-pe7 thiaN3-thang3 kek8-ki5 toa7, 
Khng3 lin2 tai7-ke saN in2-chhoa7, 
Kiu3-chu2 lau5-hoeh cho3 tiong-po2, 
Lan2 sin3 eng-kai kiat sian7 ko2. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 370 koan3, 1916 ni5 1 geh8)
