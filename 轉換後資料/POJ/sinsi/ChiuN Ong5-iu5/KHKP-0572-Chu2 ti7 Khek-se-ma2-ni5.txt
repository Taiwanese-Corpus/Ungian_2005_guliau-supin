                  
Chu2 ti7 Khek-se-ma2-ni5
Chiong Ong5-iu5
Tiau7 eng7 “Gethseneiae” chok-kheh- Chia2 CHAS.H.GABRIEL.  
1.Ia5-sou ti7 Ju5-oat8-choeh hit mi5, 
I kap hak8-seng siu2 choeh e5 si5, 
Siat-lip8 seng3-le2 bo5 koh koe2-oaN7, 
Gim5-si liau2-au7 khi3 Ka-na2-soaN.  
Ho5   	PoaN3-mi5 kui7 tihek7-se-ma2-ni5, 
Chu2 beh lim khou2-poe bo5 the-si5,  
Ki5-to2 liau2 chiu7 tit-tioh8 khah iaN5, 
Chu2 kong2 goan7 Pe7 chi2-i3 tit-chiaN5.  
2.Am3-mi5 na2 chhim hak8-seng ai3-khun3,    
Keng-ke3 cheng7-cheng7 mi5-kan si5-chun7,    
ThiN-sai3 thong mm3i chiu2-kiN bo5 hioh,    
In thiaN-kiN3 Kiu3-chu2 teh kiu5-kio3.  
3.Chit e5 poe na7 oe7 li7-khui goa2, 
Chit e5 poe na7 oe7 li7-khui goa2, 
Chong2-si7 m7-thang than3 goa2 sou2 ai3,    
Seng3-kiaN2 ki5-to2 an2-ni saN pai2.  
 
4.Chu2 ki5-to2 ti7 Khek-se-ma2-ni5, 
Li2 tioh8 chai che mia5 e5 i3-gi7, 
Chu2 lau5 huih-koaN7 ti7 hia iu-chhiu5, 
Chhin-chhiuN7 siu7 iu5-chhia teh ka2-iu5.  
5. Li2 na7 siuN7-tioh8 Khek-se-ma2-ni5, 
Tioh8 ki3-tit Ia5-sou u7 thiaN3 li2, 
I e5 ki5-to2 lou7-be2 tek-seng3, 
Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou koat-sim hi-seng. 
（Khek-se-ma2-ni5 e5 i3-su3 hoan-ek8 chiu7-si7 iu5-chhia）                        
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 572 koan3, 1932 ni5 11 geh8)
