A-tong kap Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5
（Chiong Ong5-iu5 choe3)
（Tiua7 ai3 eng7 march）……….
1. A-tong Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5,
Tak8 hang7 hok-khi3 long2 chiau5-chng5;
Kui kun5 khim5-siu3 in bo5 kiaN,
Tak8 chiah tong7-but8 long2 ho7 mia5;
Hng5 lai7 u7 mih8 thang chiah8,
Siong7-te3 pi7-pan7 chin kau3-giah8;
Tai7-ke long2 bo5 siong-hai7,
Chhut chai7 A-tong the an-pai5.
2. A-tong Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5,
Tak8 hang7 hok-khi3 long2 chiau5-chng5;
Siong7-te3 u7 chhong3-cho7 tak8 hang7,
Long2-chong2 siuN2-su3 in nng7 lang5,
Ke2-chi2 chheng-chheng ban7-ban7,
In u7 chu7-iu5 thang lai5 ban2;
Sian7-ok ko2 u7 kim3-chi2,
Na7 chiah8 e5 si5 tek-khek si2.
3. A-tong Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5,
Tak8 hang7 hok-khi3 long2 chiau5-chng5;
  Mou5-kui2 oan3-hun7 in nng7 lang5,
Ai3 in chhiN-chham2 kap i tang5;
Chiong choa5 chhui3 lai5 koai2-iu2,
Ha7-oa siu7 be5 soa3 chhun-chhiu2;
Chiah8 kim3 ko2 siu7 chek-pi7,
Kian5-lui7 A-tong u7 tai7-chi3.
4. A-tong Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5,
Tak8 hang7 hok-khi3 long2 chiau5-chng5;
Hoan7-choe7 liau2-au7 chau2 khi3 bih,
Chin kiaN siu7 hoat8 aisi3am-pi7;
Nng7 lang5 ki3-jian5 hoan2-poe7,
Bo5 thiaN Siong7-te3 kong2 e5 oe7;
Tui3 si2-chou2 siu7 be5-hek8,
Ti3-kau3 lang5 tioh8 loh8 te7-gek8.
5. A-tong Ha7-oa ti7 Lok8-hng5,
  Tak8 hang7 hok-khi3 long2 chiau5-chng5;
  Mou5-kui2 phaiN2-sim ai3 ham7-hai7,
  Tui3 cha2 thau3 kau3 ban7-se3-tai7;
  Ai3-lan2 ku7-choat8 Siong7-te3,
  Eng7 ban7 poaN kan-kui2 ok-ke3;
  Ia5-sou choe3 lan2 Kiu3-chu2,
  Sin3 I chiu7 bian2 loh8 im-hu2.
