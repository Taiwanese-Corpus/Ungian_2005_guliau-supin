                     
Ia5-sou Siu7-chhi3
Chiong Ong5-iu5
（Tiau7 eng7 “Save by grace,” chok- khek-chia2 Geo, c Stebbins.）  
1.Seng3 Sin5 chhoa7 Ia5-sou kau3 khong3-ia2,    
Ti7-hia si3-chap8 mi5-jit8 kim3-chiah8;    
Mou5-kui2 lai5 chhi3 Siong7-te3 Seng3-kiaN2,    
Kio3 I eng7 chioh8-thau5 piN3 choe3 piaN2;    
Ia5-sou in3 kong2, lang5 e5 oah8-mia7,    
M7-na7 oa2-kho3 chit khoan2 e5 piaN2;    
Chi2-kho3 Siong7-te3 sou2 chhut e5 Oe7,    
Siong7-te3 i2-goa7 bo5 lang5 oe7 choe3.  
2.Te7-ji7 pai2, Mou5-kui2 koh chin3-cheng5, 
Chhoa7 Chu2 kau3 Seng3-siaN5 e5 tian7-teng2; 
Kong2 I na7-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2,
Tui3 tian7-teng2 tio5-loh8 ia7 bian2-kiaN; 
Ia5-sou in3 kong2, boh8-tit chhi3 Chu2, 
In-ui7 Seng3-keng u7 ki3 chit8 ku3; 
Mou5-kui2 u7 si5 oe7 in2 Seng3-keng, 
Chu2 in2-khi2 pat8-chat lai5 tek-seng3.  
 
3.Te7-saN pai2, Mou5-kui2 hian2 chhiu2-toaN7,
Chhoa7 Ia5-sou kau3 kek8-koan5 e5 soaN; 
Eng7 tak8 kok eng5-hoa5 lai5 in2-iu2, 
Kong2 na7 pai3 goa2 chiu7 kau Li2 chhiu2; 
Ia5-sou in3 kong2, Sat-tan3 the3-khi3, 
Lun7 Chu2 Siong7-te3 li2 tioh8 pai3 I; 
Mou5-kui2 iu2-hek8 long2 bo5 seng5-kong, 
Jian5-au7 thiN-sai3 chiu7 lai5 keng3-hong7.  
4.Siong7-te3 e5 KiaN2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, 
Mou5-kui2 chin ai3 I hoan7 choe7-ok; 
Ho5-hong3 sin3 e5 lang5 ti7 kim-se3, 
Eng-kai the5-hong5 in e5 khui2-ke3; 
Ia5-sou i2-keng khah-iaN5 Mou5-kui2, 
Lan2 oa2-kho3 I chiu7 bian2 kiaN-ui3; 
Tai7-ke na7 ki3-tit chit chan5 su7, 
Kap Mou5-kui2 kau-chian3 chiu7 bian2-su. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 571 koan3, 1932 ni5 10 geh8)
