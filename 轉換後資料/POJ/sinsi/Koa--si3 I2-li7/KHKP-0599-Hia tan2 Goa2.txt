
Hia tan2 Goa2
Koa--si7 I2-li7 ek8
1.Ti7 hit-peng5 si7 lan2 chou2-ke, 
Seng3-tou5 kau3 hia kiN3 ThiN Pe7; 
Si2-sit, kau3-be2 bo5 koan5-se3,      
Hia tan2 Goa2. 
Chhin-chhiuN7 am3-mi5 na2 ke3-khi3, 
Chiu7 oe7 chiaN5-choe3 toa7 hoaN-hi2; 
Ai3 to3-khi3 kap Chu2 bo5 li7,      
Hia tan2 Goa2.     
Ho5    Hia tan2 Goa2.          
Hia tan2 Goa2.      
Hia u7 Oah8-mia7 e5 chhiu7 teh khui,  
Hia tan2 Goa2.      
Si2-sit, kau3-be2 bo5 koan5-se3,      
Ti7 hit-peng5 si7 lan2 chou2-ke;      
Seng3-tou5 kau3 hia kiN3 ThiN Pe7,          
Hia tan2 Goa2.  
2.Chia sou2 ng3-bang7 si7 khang-khang, 
SaN-thiaN3 e5 cheng5 na2 bin5-bang7; 
 
Thian-tong5 e5 toa7 hok bo5 han7,    
Hia tan2 Goa2. 
Ti7 Ho5-kiN, kng-kng phou2-pian3, 
Seng3-siaN5 lai7 hou7 lang5 him-sian7; 
Lan2 e5 sin3-giong2 tioh8 tu3-hian7,    
Hia tan2 Goa2.  
3.ThiN-sai3 sou2 toa3 e5 Kim-siaN5, 
Si7 teh o-lo2 Chu2 e5 Mia5; 
Hong5-kiong chiu7-si7 Ong5 e5 SiaN5,
Hia tan2 Goa2. 
Ti7-hia chin-bit8 teh kau-poe5, 
Long2-chong2 toa3 hia tang5 chu7-hoe7; 
Ku2-tng5 hok-khi3 eng2 boe7-hoe3,     
Hia tan2 Goa2.
