
Chu2 chhe su3-tou5 khi3 thoan5-to7
Koa I5-su
1.Ia5-sou kio3 lan2 hun-tui7 thoan5-to7, 
Sou2 eng7 mih8-kiaN7 ju2 chio2 si7 ju2 ho2, 
ToaN-saN bian2 te7 bian2 koaiN2 iah8 bian2 to, 
In-ui7 Seng3-keng sou2 kong2 long2 boe7 chho3.  
2.Na7 kau3 chng-sia7 sou2 tiam3 tak8-ui7 ho2,
Thoan5-po3 chin-to7 hou7 in kiam ki5-to2, 
Siat-su2 u7 lang5 kiau-ngou7 bo5 ai3 oh8, 
PoaN7--khi3 kha--nih thou5-hun2 he chiu7 ho2.  
3.U7 lang5 seng5-siu7 ho2-i3 chhiaN2 lin2 khi3, 
Jip8 in ke-lai7 gim5-si ka3 to7-li2, 
Na7 beh kap in li7-piat8 hit-tang-si5, 
Tioh8 eng7 Chu2 mia5 ka7 in chiok hok-khi3.  
4.Kiam2-chhai2 u7-e5 m7-sin3 sim giau5-gi5, 
Lin2 u7 ka7 in chiok-hok kui ka-ki7, 
Sia3-choe7 e5 ioh8 m7 chiah8 chiu7 bo5 i , 
Si7 in nge7-sim sit-loh8 eng2 hok-khi3.  
5.Kiu3-chu2 beng7-leng7 tai7-ke long2 tioh8 thiaN, 
Chhut-lat8 thoan5-po3 kiu3 leng5 e5 chin siaN, 
Sip8-ke3 chin-to7 si3-hng toa7 soan-thoan5, 
Au7-jit8 Hok-im phou2-pian3 thong Tai5-oan5. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 611 tiuN, 1936 ni5 2 geh8)
