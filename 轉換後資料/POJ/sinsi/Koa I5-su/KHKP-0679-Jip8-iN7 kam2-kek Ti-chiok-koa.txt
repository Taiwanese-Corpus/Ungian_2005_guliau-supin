
 
Jip8-iN7 kam2-kek Ti-chiok-koa
Koa I5-su
 Chhit geh8 27 am3 jip8-iN7, 8 geh8 14 jit8 sia2 koa. 
1. Goa2 jip8 keh-li7 te7 3 sek,
Thang mng5 kap piah chhat sam sek. 
Thou5-kha pun2 sek thian-pan2 pek8, 
Song2 chhng3 ji7-teng2 pai5 kok sek. 
2. Jit8 mi5 sok-cheng7 bo5 siaN seh. 
To2-chhng5 an-cheng7 m7-thang che7, 
Hoan7-chia2 chhou lai5 si7 lim am2, 
Jiat8-tou7 kang3-ke7 chiu7 chiah8 be5. 
3. Chiah8 be5 bo5 he5 si7 phe3 hi5, 
Moa5-sat-bak8, bian2, si7 ho2 hi5,
Moa5-sat-bak8 hi5 u7 am3-chhi3, 
U7-si5 tau7-hu7 kap bian2-hi5. 
4. Jit8-sit8 long2 si7 chu-iong2 hun, 
Hoan7-chia2 tak8 e5 phe3 u7 chhun, 
Kho2 kiong kho2 ho7 cheng3 i-oan5,
Lo2-iu3 hoan7-chia2 sim an-choan5. 
5.  Lai7-iong5 goa7-koan chin iu-siu3, 
Khan3-hou7 hong-hoat si7 it liu5, 
Hoan7-chia2 jip8 iN7 chin chheng-hiu, 
Un2-tang3 peng5-an thiam hok-siu7. 
6. Kam2-sia7 kau3-hoe7 gau5 i5-chhi5, 
Hian3-kim boe7 pi2 kou2-cha2 si5,
Geng5-chiap se-I oa2 chit8-ni5,
IN7-tiuN2 i-oan5 gau5 phoe3-ti3. 
7. Ma2-kai I-iN7 e5 su7-giap8, 
Chiu7-si7 kau3-hoe7 toa7 kau-siap8 
Choan5-oan5 pan7-su7 kek8 siap-tiap8, 
Ui7 Chu2 hong7-su7 chin ho5-hiap8. 
8. Tng-si5 Ia5-sou thoan5 to7-li2, 
Chou-chip8 hak8-seng I ka3-si7. 
KoaN2-kui2 thoan5-to7 kap i-ti7,
Hoan-hu3 hak8-seng chhin-chhiuN7 I. 
9. Ia5-sou kong2 lan2 chiu7-si7 siaN5, 
Eng-kai kian-kou3 kiam toan-chiaN3,
Lan2 tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 chio3-sin-kiaN3,
Cheng3-lang5 oe7 khoaN3 kiaN3-lai7 iaN2.
 10. Ia5-sou kong2 lan2 chiu7-si7 iam5, 
Lan2 chhui3 m7-thang kek tiam7-tiam7, 
Po3 lang5 choe7-ok e5 gui5-hiam2, 
Seng3-keng chiaN3-si7 chi2-lam5-chiam. 
11. Lan2 lang5 sim-seng3 na2 hi5-bah,
Na7 u7 chham-iam5 chiah oe7 hah8, 
Bo5-lun7 loa7 ho2 e5 koe ah, 
Sit iam5 hiat-tiu2 cheng3-lang5 tah8. 
12. Ia5-sou kong2 lan2 chiu7-si7 kng,
Lan2 kng m7-si7 beh siu-khng3, 
Eng-kai tioh8 chio3 kau3 chin hng7,
Ti3-i3 chhio lang5 jip8 ThiN-mng5. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 679 koan3, 1941 ni5 10 geh8)
