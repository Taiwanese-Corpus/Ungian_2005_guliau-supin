Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5
Ko Thian-su3 hoan-ek8
1.Tioh8 thoan5 hoaN-hi2 e5 siaN, 
Khi3 kau3 tak8-hiuN tak8-siaN5, 
KiN3-na7 tioh8-boa5 kan-khou2, 
A2-si7 iu-bun7 kiaN-hiaN5, 
Goa2 u7 kek8-ho2 siau-sit, 
Toa7-siaN po3 hou7 lin2 thiaN, 
Po2-hui-su i2-keng lai5!    
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5,    
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5 !    
ThiN-pe7 siuN2-su3 Seng3-sin5,    
I e5 eng3-un2 si7 chin;    
Tioh8 thoan5 hoaN-hi2 e5 siaN,    
Po3 hou7 cheng3-lang5 long2 thiaN,    
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng-lai5 !  
2.Hian7-si5 m7-si7 mi5--si5, 
Si3-ke3 i2-keng cha2-khi2; 
Hong-hou7 e5 siaN long2 soah, 
KiaN-hiaN5 piN3 cho3 hoaN-hi2; 
ChhiN-chhui3 soaN-teng3 u7 jit8, 
 
Goa2 sim hi2-lok8 bo5-pi2 ! 
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5 !     
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5...un5-un5  
3.Ban7-ong5 e5 Ong5 Siong7-te3,
Choan5-leng5 ti7 I sit8-e7, 
Kang3-lim5 chin2-kiu3 ban7-bin5, 
Thoat-li7 Mou5-kui2 koan5-se3, 
Im-hu2 e5 kaN e7 khang,
Tek-seng3 e5 koa bo5 the3 : 
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5 !   
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5...un5-un5  
4.Toa7-in sit8-chai7 koh-iuN7, 
Goa3 sim be7-hiau2 thang siuN7, 
Chun2 goa2 kek8 phaiN2 choe7-jin5, 
Eng5-kng kap I chhin-chhiuN7, 
Chit ho7 jin5-ai3 kek8 chhim, 
Pi2 hai2 koh khah oh niu5, 
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5 !   
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5...un5-un5  
5.TaN tioh8 o-lo2 hoaN-hi2, 
 
SiaN-im hiang2-liang7 kau3 thiN, 
Soa3 ho7 thiN-teng2 Seng3-sai3, 
Toe3 lan2 saN-kap gim5-si, 
Tang5 gim5 saN-thiaN3 e5 tiau7, 
Eng2-oan2 toa7 o-lo2 I, 
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5 !   
Po2-hui7-su i2-keng lai5....un5-un5
