
Sin Chan3-bi2-koa 152
1.ThiaN khoaN3-bai7 leh ! SiaN teh hiang2,     
Si7 teh chhiuN3 Hi-lian5 e5 koa;     
Na2-chun2 siang3 tioh8 lou2-kou2-chhang,     
Hai2-kiN5 kong5-eng2 the toa7 poa3. 
2.Ha-le2-lu7-iah !! Chu2 e5 ni5,    
Tioh8 thoan5-po3 che ho2 siau-sit;    
Tit8 kau3 soaN-kan kap hai2-kiN5,    
Soan-po3 cheng3-kiaN2 hian2-beng5 jit8.  
3. Ha-le2-lu7-iah !! ThiaN khoaN3-bai7,     
Siu7-cho7 e5 mih8 long2 chhut siaN;     
Tang5-sim che5-chhiuN3 teh keng3-pai3,     
Kek8 ho5-hai5 im-un7 ho2 thiaN.  
4.Kiah8 bak8 khoaN3 leh ! Chu2 teh lai5,    
Ia5-ho5 hoa e5 ki5 koan5-koan5;    
Seng3 KiaN2 ti7-li2 e5 se3-kai3,    
HoaN-hi ah!! lim5-kau3 ti7 goan2.  
5.Ku7 e5 thiN beh siu khi3 khng3,    
Te7 kap hai2 ia7 bo5 koh u7;    
Sin-thiN sin-te7, taN ku2-tng5,    
Kok koan5 eng5-knh kui ti7 Chu2.                  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 616 tiuN, 1936 ni5 7 geh8)
