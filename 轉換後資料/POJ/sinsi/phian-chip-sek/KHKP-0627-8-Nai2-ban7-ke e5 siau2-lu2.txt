
Nai2-ban7-ke e5 siau2-lu2
1.Kou2-cha2 u7 chit8 toa7 kiong5-kok, 
Sou2-chai7 si7 ti7 Iu5-thai3 kok; 
Mia5-ji7 chheng-hou cho3 A-lan5, 
Che7-che7 toa7-ho5 kap beng5-san.
2.Chia e5 thou5 si7 chin pui5-sui2, 
Tong-si5 Liat8-kok pai5 thau5-ui7; 
Hoe-chhau2, chhiu7-bak8 kek8 bou7-seng7, 
Goa7-lang5 ai3 khoaN3 chit ho7 keng2.
3.U7 soaN mia5 cho3 Li7-pa-lun7, 
Hng7-hng7 saN tui3 si7 Hek-bun5; 
Teng2-bin7 long2 cheng3 pek-hiuN-bok8, 
Sng3 si7 chhiu7-tiong ho2-liau7 chok.
4.Tai7-ma2-sek si7 in kiaN-siaN5, 
Lau5-tai5, koan5-thah kai3 u7 mia5; 
Tiau-khek oe7-siong7 chin-chiaN3 sui2, 
Kian3-tiok iaN5 koe3 chin che7 ui7.
5.Goa2 taN m7-bian2 siong5-se3 lun7, 
ChhiaN2 kong2 kok-tiong chit8 chiong-kun; 
Mia5-ji7 chheng-hou kio3 Nai2-ban7, 
Chin Sin5 eng7 i peng5 tai7-lan7.
 
6.It-seng cho3 lang5 chin eng-hiong5,
Tit-tioh8 kun-ong5 sou2 tiong7-iong7; 
Kho2-sioh au7-lai5 siu7 put-heng7, 
Choan5-sin hoat-siN thai2-ko peng7.
7.Sui5 chhiaN2 beng5 i lai5 tiau7-ti7, 
Khang hui3 chiN5-gin5 be7 thang i; 
In-ui7 che piN7 si7 chin ok, 
Lai7-bin7 hoat-chhut u7 toa7 tok8.
8.Cha2 bat kap I-sek-liat8 chian3, 
Liah8-tioh8 chit8-e5 lu2 siau3-lian5; 
Tiau5-ti5 chhoa7 kau3 i e5 chhu3, 
Hou7 i cho3 kan2 chhe chap8-su7.
9.Siau3-siau3 lian5-lian5 li7 pun2-hiuN, 
Li7-piat8 chhin-lang5 kap tia-niu5; 
Ma7 u7 hiaN-ti7 kap chi2-moe7, 
Be7 thang kap in koh cho3-hoe2.
10.Ti7-chia moa2-bin7 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3, 
Long2 si7 chhiN-sou sit khiam-pi; 
Tok8-tok8 chin Sin5 bu5 put-chai7, 
Cho3 I kiaN2-ji5 I chin ai3.
 
11.Ki5-tiong u7 o3-biau7 e5 i3, 
Goa2 taN put-tek-put kong2-khi2; 
Tioh8 chai Nai2-ban7 pun2 u7 lat8, 
Jiam2-tioh8 thai2-ko sim phah-kat.
12.Chin Sin5 tek8-i3 beh kam2-hoa3, 
Beh engsi7au-lu2 thang thoan5-oa7; 
U7 tui3 i e5 chu2-bo2 kong2, 
Goa2 siuN7 chu2-lang5 e5 khou2-thang3.
13.Chi2-u7 goan2 kok e5 kiaN-siaN5, 
Chit8-ui7 Sian-ti chin chhut-mia5; 
Tek-khak e7 i chit khoan2 piN7, 
ChhiaN2 tioh8 khi2-sin tui3 hia khi3.
14.Nai2-ban7 sit8-chai7 chhoe7 bo5 hoat, 
I-seng iah8 bo5 sim2-mih8 koat; 
TaN tui3 siau2-lu2 chit-ku3 gian5, 
Iau3-i3 chau3-beng5 ti7 ong5 chian5.
15.A-lan5 e5 ong5 hit chit8-si5, 
Beng7-leng7 chiong-kun kin2-kin2 khi3; 
Soa3 kia3 phoe hou7 I-sek-liat8, 
Sou2 sia2 siong5-se3 oe7 koh chhiat.
 
16.Nai2-ban7 khi2-sin bo5 ian5-ti5, 
Ia7 toa3 chin-che7 kim kap mih8; 
Chun5-sim ng3-bang7 e7 kin2-ho2, 
M7-kiaN kan-khou2, m7-kiaN lo5.
17.Giau5-gi5 si7 teh chhoe7 khang-khiah, 
Thiah-phoa3 i-chiuN5 long2 boe7 chiah8; 
Tiau5-lai7 jin5-sin5 it soa3 it, 
Iah8 si7 choan5-pou7 boe7 hiau2 tit.
18.Kai-chai3 u7 I2-li7-sa siaN, 
An-ui3 kun-ong5 m7-bian2 kiaN; 
Hoan-hu3 tioh8 kau3 goa2 sou2-chai7, 
I chai u7 sian-ti chiah lai5.
19.Nai2-ban7 chit8 thiaN chit ho7 gian5, 
Liam5-piN che7 chhia kau3 mng5-chian5; 
Teh siuN7 i beh chau2 chhut-lai5, 
The3 kiu5 thiN-teng2 toa7 Chu2-chaiN2.
20.Jian5-au7 chiah bong sou2 hoan7--chhu3, 
Thai2-ko chiu7 thang tit i-tu5; 
Put-liau7 I2-li7-sa eng7 hoat, 
Chi2-u7 chhe lang5 lai5 thoan5-soat.
 
21.Kio3 i tioh8 khi3 Iok-tan3 ho5, 
Se2-ek8 chhit pai2 chiu7 e7 ho2; 
Nai2-ban7 chit8 thiaN chit ho7 oe7, 
Nou7-khi3 chhiong-thian ai3 hoe5-ke.
22.SiuN7 goan2 Tai7-ma2-sek kiaN-tou, 
Hoat-hoat kap A-pa-na2 ho5; 
Pi2 lin2 chia e5 chui2 khah chheng, 
Hia se2 keng3 koh-khah hah8 eng7.
23.Ho2 tit kin-toe3--e5 kin7-cheng5, 
Ho2-taN2 tui3 i tit8 kong2-beng5; 
Siat-su2 Sian-ti u7 beng7-leng7, 
E7 i li2 peN7 si7 khak-teng7.
24.Hong3 koh chi2 kong2 se2 sin-khu, 
Chhim-goan7 chu2-lang5 bo5 tiu5-tu5; 
Nai2-ban7 tui3 chia ko2-jian5 khi3, 
Se2 liau2 chhit pai2 ho2 li7-li7.
25.Kau3 ho2 sin-the2 na2 iu3-tong5, 
Kng-kng kut8-kut8 chin kian7-khong; 
Sim-lai7 u7 siuN7 kau3 chin-cheng5, 
M7-si7 ho5-chui2 e7 ti7-peng7.
 
26.Bo5 m7-si7 ti7 u2-tiu7-lai7, 
Chin-sin5 Ia5-ho5-hoa chun5-chai7; 
ChiaN3 si7 chit chok8 e5 Siong7-te3, 
Kiong-kiong keng3-keng3 hian3 toa7 le2.
27.Bo5-liau7 chit ui7 toa7 Sian-ti, 
Chai3-saN m7 siu ka7 i si5; 
Cheng5-goan7 khek-khou2 koe3 kong-im, 
M7-ai3 hoat-chai5 phau7 pu3-sim.
28.Tong-si5 Nai2-ban7 sim thong-tat8, 
Koat-sim m7 koh pai3 Phou5-sat; 
Siat-su2 be7 hiau2 u7 chho3--chhu3, 
Beh kiu5 chin Sin5 si khoan-su3.
29.Toa7 siaN gim5-si o-lo2 Chu2, 
Chit8 lou7 kam2-sia7 I jin5-chu5; 
Chit khoan2 thai2-ko e7 tit i, 
Lang5-lang5 to si7 khoaN3 cho3 ki5.
30.Chi2 in siau2-lu2 e5 chit8 ku3, 
Chiong-kun tang7-taN3 thang thoat-tu5; 
Chia khi2 siau2-lu2 seng-oah8 lou7, 
To siu7 chu2-lang5 chhim ai3-kou3.
 
31.Hian7-kim hong7-khng3 kok siau2-lu2, 
Te7-it tioh8 keng3-ui3 Siong7-chu2; 
Sui-si7 tu2-tioh8 u7 chhi3-lian7, 
Boh8-tit sim leng2 siuN7 ia3-sian7.
32.Tioh8 chai chin-sSin5 e5 o3-biau7, 
Tek-khak siuN2-su3 li2 chai5-tiau7; 
Put-lun7 u7 kau3 siaN2 sou2-chai7, 
Li7 chhin li7 chhek pun2 m7-ai3.
33.Chin-sin5 eng7 choan5-sim hok8-sai7, 
Hoan7-su7 e7 tit I an-pai5; 
Si5-khek m7-thang sit ki-hoe7, 
Jiat8-seng5 kap Chu2 saN kau-poe5.
34.M7-thang the-si5 be7 hiau2 kong2, 
U7-si5 chit8 ku3 ia7 hap8-tong3; 
Iu7-koh boh8 siuN7 goa2 iu3-chiN2, 
KiaN-liau2 kio3 lang5 kio3 be7 chhiN2.
35.Chhi3 khoaN3 chit ui7 toa7 eng-hiong5, 
Tui3 ti7 siau2-lu2 it gian5 tiong; 
Sin-the2 thai2-ko tit kiu3-ti7, 
Lin2 siuN7 che su7 kiam2 bo5 ki5.
 
36.Hong3 koh cho3 lang5 suui7 siau3-lian5, 
Kong2 chhut sit8-chai7 cheng3-keng gian5; 
Hou7 lang5 e7 tit thang beng5-pek8, 
E7 seN hau7-ko2 toa7 iu2-ek.
37.Hian7-si5 lang5-lang5 thai2-ko tok8, 
Chiu7-si7 leng5-hun5 e5 choe7-ok; 
Kin2-kin2 tioh8 khi3 po3 lang5 chai, 
Chhoa7 kau3 Chu2 e5 sou2-chai7 lai5.
38.In-ui7 Kiu3-chu2 toa7 chu5-pi, 
Beh chiong choe7-giat8 tu5 kau3 li7; 
Sit8-sim khak-sin3 Chu2 koan5-leng5, 
Chiong-lai5 it-teng7 siu7 tai7 eng5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 627~628 ho7, 1937 ni5 6~7 geh8)
