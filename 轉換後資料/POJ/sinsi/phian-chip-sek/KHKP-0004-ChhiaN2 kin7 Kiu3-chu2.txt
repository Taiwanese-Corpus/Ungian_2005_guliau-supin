
ChhiaN2 kin7 Kiu3-chu2
[Ku7-ni5 ti7 E5-mng5 u7 koh chhut chit8-pun2 chheh, ho7 cho3 Seng3-si-koa, si7 eng7 44 siu2 sin e5 si lai5 khioh cho3 chit8-pun2. Chiong-tiong goan2 u7 keng2 chit8-siu2 Lai5 in3 ti7 chia.]  
Goa2 thiaN Ia5-sou e5 siaN kong2-beng5,    
Lin2 long2 tiam3 chit se3-kan, 
KiN3-na7 lo5-khou2 lai5 goa2 bin7-cheng5    
Tak8-lang5 long2-u7 peng5-an. 
Goa2 thiaN i kong2 chin7-chai7 hoaN-hi2,   
Sui5-si5 chiu7 khichh3oe i,  
Tit-tioh8 siong5-siong5 peng5-an bo5-si2,   
Eng2-tiam3 Ia5-sou sin-piN.  
Goa2 thiaN Ia5-sou ho2-siaN e5 im,   
Chin-chheng oah8-choaN5 si7 goa2, 
Lang5 na7 chhui3-ta beh-sang3 i lim,   
Eng-kai kin2-kin2 kin7-oa2. 
Goa2 thiaN i kong2 siu7-tioh8 bian2-le7,   
HoaN-hi2 than3 i chiu7 lim, 
Put-chi2 song2-khoai3 cheng-sin5 ka-poe7,   
Eng2-bo5 kan-khou2 e5 sim.  
Goa2 thiaN Ia5-sou e5 siaN ia7 kong2,   
 
Goa2 si7 se3-kan e5 kng, 
Sin3 goa2 e5 lang5 e7 tit chheng-song2,   
Na2 khia7 kng-kng tiong-ng. 
Goa2 thiaN i kong2 kiah8-thau5 koan-khoaN3,   
Eng5-kng chin-chiaN3 tek-i3, 
Ia5-sou chin-kng eng2-eng2 bo5-soaN3   
Chio3 goa2 kiaN5-kau3 chit8-si3.
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 4 tiuN, 1885 ni5 10 geh8)
