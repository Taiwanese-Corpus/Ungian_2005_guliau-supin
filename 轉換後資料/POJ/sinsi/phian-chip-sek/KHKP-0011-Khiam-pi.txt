
KHIAM-PI 
（Chit-siu2 e5 si pun2 si7 hoan-ek8 SoaN3-thau5 e5 khiuN, in3 ti7 in-e5 Kong-hoe7 Chap8-liok8; au7-lai5 goan2 hoan-ek8 Tai5-oan5 e5 khiuN, lai5 in3 ti7 chia.）  
1.KhoaN3 Eng-ji5 ti7 be2-cho5 lai7,    
I kha-e7 bok8-chia2 keng3-pai3:       
Si7 goa2 Chu2! Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3,      
Si7 goa2 Chu2! Eng5-kng e5 Chu2-chaiN2.      
Eng7 khiam-pi, eng7 sim pai3-kui7,       
Jin7 goa2 Chu2 chi3-chun chi3-kui3.  
2.KhoaN3 chit-lang5 ti7 Na2-sat-lek8,     
Sun7 pe7-bo2, chin u7 hau3-tek: 
（Mui2-chat tioh8 soa3 gim5 e7-bin7 si3-choa7.） 
Si7 goa2 Chu2! Seng3-keng sou2 ki3-chai3,  
Si7 goa2 Chu2! Eng5-kng e5 Chu2-chai2 . 
Eng7 khiam-pi, eng7 sim pai3-kui7,  
Jin7 goa2 Chu2 chi3-chun chi3-kui3.  
3.KhoaN3 chit-lang5 moa2-sim hoan5-lo2, 
Ti7 khong3-ia2 kim3-chiah8 iau-go7:         
（Koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.）  
4.Liap8-sat-lou7 bong7-cheng5 lai5 thiaN, 
 
Si7 chit-lang5 ai-khau3 e5 siaN:        
（koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.）     
5.Am-mi5 ti7 Khek-se-ma2-ni5,  
Kiu5 Siong7-te3 chiaN5 I chi2-i3:        
（koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.）  
6.KhoaN3 I si2 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3,  
Lau5 po2-hoeh lai5 kiu3 ThiN-e7:         
（koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.）  
7.Phoa3-khui bong7 tui3 si2 koh-oah8,  
Lin5-bin2 lang5, si-in bo5-soah:          
（koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.）  
8.Kiah8-bak8 khoaN3 thiN-teng2 cho7-ui7,  
Chip koan5-peng3 koan2-li2 ban7-lui7:            
（koh gim5 hit si3-choa7.） 

(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 11 tiuN, 1886 ni5 6 geh8)
