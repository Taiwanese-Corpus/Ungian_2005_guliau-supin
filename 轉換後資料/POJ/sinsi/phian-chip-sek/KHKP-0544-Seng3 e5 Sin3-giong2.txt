
Seng3 e5 Sin3-giong2
1. Tui3 Chu2 lau5 hoeh, goa2 tit li7-ek,   
Kiam2 e7 an2-ni? goa2 bo5 to7-tek.    
I the3 goa2 si2, siaN2 lang5 kah I ?    
Sim2-mih8 lang5 pek I the3 goa2 si2 ?    
Goa2 e5 Siong7-te3 goan7 the3 goa2 si2 .    
ChaiN2-iuN7 an2-ni ? thiaN3-thang3 bo5 pi2 !    
Goa2 e5 Siong7-te3 goan7 the3 goa2 si2 .    
ChaiN2-iuN7 an2-ni ? thiaN3-thang3 bo5 pi2 !  
2.Chin si7 o3-biau7 ! Put-biat8, u7 si2 ! 
SiaN2 lang5 chai Chu2 ke3-ek8 sin-ki5? 
Chu2 thiaN3 chhim-tim5, thiN-sai3 chhui-chhek,     
Sui-jian5 chin7-lat8, oh-tit beng5-pek8 . 
Lin5-bin2 toa7 ah !lan2 tioh8 kam2 in; 
ThiN-sai3 gian2-kiu3, in bian2 koh chin3 .  
3.ThiN-teng2 ong5-ui7, I goan7 hong3-khi3;
In-tian2 bu5 kiong5 si-loh8 tui3 I. 
PiaN3-khang ka-ti7, choan-choan jin5-ai3; 
Lau5 hoeh,ui7-tioh8 loan2-jiok8 se3-kai3. 
Lin5-bin2 toa7 ah !  khong2-khoah bu5 pian,  
Goa2 e5 Siong7-te3, bo5 hiam5 goa2 chian7.  
 
4.Choe7 kap ok-seng3 pak8 goa2 chin an5,

So2-lian7 long2 lut, sim-koaN chu7-iu5, 
Peh-khi2-lai5 toe3 chi2-ho2 Peng5-iu2 .  
5.Goa2 taN bian2 kiaN teng7-choe7 tim5-lun5, 
Ia5-sou sou2 u7, goa2 long2 u7 hun7 .
