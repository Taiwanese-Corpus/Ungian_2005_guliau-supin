Sin hoan-ek8 e5 Si
1.Ia5-sou si5-siong5 ti7 goa2 sin-piN,    
Sit8-chai7 thiaN3 goa2 kau3 kek8,  
Cheng5-goan7 li7-khui thiN-teng2 hu3-kui3,     
Kou3 goan2 se3-kiaN2 chin7-lek8.  
2.Li e5 bin7-mau7 eng5-kng chhan3-lan7,     
Kin7-oa2, goa2 khoaN3 be7 kiN3,  
Sui-bong2 se3-siaN kap goa2 kong2-oe7,     
Goa2 thiaN long2 bo5 jip8 hiN7.     
3.Chu2 Li2 e5 chhiu2 kin7 oa2 ti7 goa2,     
Hian2-chhut chu5-pi thiaN3-thang3,  
Chhin-chhiuN7 pe7-bo2 ai3 khan se3-kiaN2, 
Hou7 goa2 e7-tit chiaN5 lang5.    
4. Ti7 goa2 sim-lai7 si7 Li2 teh toa3,     
Chan7 goa2 iaN5-koe3 tui3-tek8,  
Goa2 thiaN3 Siong7-te3 ka-ti7 e7 chai,   
Chu2 e5 jin5-ai3 bu5-kek8.     
5.Siat-su2 cha2-am3 kui7-loh8 ki5-to2,   
Kap Li2 kau-poe5 kiu5 in, 
 
Seng3-sin5 khia7-khi2 ti7 goa2 sim-lai7,   
Kan-cheng3 Kiu3-chu2 chhin-kin7.      
6.Chiu7-kin7 Siong7-te3 hit-si5 Kiu3-chu2,     
The3 goa2 pek-chhiat ki5-to2,  
Goa2 khun3 loh8 bin5 Ia5-sou eng2-chhiN2,     
Kou3 goa2 peng7 bo5 hoan5-lo2.   
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 149 koan3, 1897 ni5 8 geh8)

1.Se3-kiaN2 chu7-hoe7 tioh8 khiam-pi,

Lan2 tioh8 khian5-sim lai5 kiong-keng3.

Iah8 siuN7 Thian-tong5 kek8 hu3-ju7; 
Lan2 kha sou2 kiaN5, chhiu2 sou2 cho3, 
Hou7 i teh khoaN3 thang o-lo2.

Cha2-am3 sou2 siuN7 e5 sim-liam7,
