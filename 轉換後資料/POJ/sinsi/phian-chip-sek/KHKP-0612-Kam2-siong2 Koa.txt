
Kam2-siong2 Koa
Ong5 kang
1. Ki5-au7 kau3-hoe7 lau7 ian5-tau7,   
Chheng-lian5 bi2-lu2 chai7 kha-tau;   
Chhit-chap8 ni5 hoe3 lau7 kiaN2-sai3,   
Khan-chhiu2 iong2-kiaN7 gau5 hoai5-thai.  
2.A-pa a-bo2 ho2 phin2-heng7,    
Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 toa7 chhut-teng;    
Tak8-ni5 tiaN7-tioh8 seN siang-the,    
KiaN2-sun na2 ku2 thoan5 na2 che7.  
3.A-bo2 ke-su7 chin gau5 hoaN7,    
SeN goan2 hiaN-moe7 na2 chang3-koaN7;    
Chit8-the iu7 seN sip8 hiaN-che2,    
Toa7-toa7 eng5-kng ti7 kau3-hoe7.  
4. ChhiaN2 lin2 tai7-ke tau3 hoaN-hi2, 
HoaN-hi2 a-bo2 seN sio2-ti7; 
HiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 saN khan-chhiu2, 
Tai7-ke khoai3-lok8 chiah8 bah-chiu2.  
5.A-pa a-bo2 gau5 iuN2-chhi7, 
Chit8 ke heng-ong7 toan-toan-iN5; 
Siong7-te3 khoaN3-liau2 chin hoaN-hi2, 
Pang-chou7 kau3-hoe7 toa7 heng-khi2. 
 (Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 612 tiuN, 1936 ni5 3 geh8)
