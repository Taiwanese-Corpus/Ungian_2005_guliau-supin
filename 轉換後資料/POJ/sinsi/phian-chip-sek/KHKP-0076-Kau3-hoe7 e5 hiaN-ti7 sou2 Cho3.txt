Kau3-hoe7 e5 hiaN-ti7 sou2 Cho3 e5 si kap koa.
Siok8-chu2 e5 lang5 siong5-siong5 tioh8 hoaN-hi2 ; na7 bo5, sng3 si7 be7-hiau2 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 in,jit8-jit8 siuN2-su3 lan2,koh eng3-un2 lan2 tit-tioh8 ku2-tng5 e5 khoai3-lok8 ti7 thiN--nih8.Chit-ho7 sim an3-chaiN2-iuN7 e7 hian2-chhut lai5? Po2-lo5 kia3 phoe hou7 I2-hut-sou2 e5 seng3-tou5 kong2, “Tioh8 eng7 si eng7 koa , eng7 sin5 eh5u , chhui3-gim5 sim-ho5 lai5 o-lo2 Chu2.”  
TaN lun7 lan2 peng5-sou3 teh gim5 e5 si kap koa, kho2-sioh si7 chin chio2, in-ui7 khah-siong5 teh eng7 put-ko3 si7 Iong2-sim-sin5-si , hit pun2 na7-tiaN7 . Sui-jian5 lang5 liok8-siok8 cho3 kui2-siu2 in3 ti7 Sin-bun5 chap8-liok8 , ti7 Kau3-hoe7-po3, kap Seng3-hoe7-po3, m7-ku2 Kau3-taN long2 bo5 thong-heng5.   
Kai-chai3 E7-mng5 e5 Khip sian-siN tui3 Si-phian tiah-chhut koh teh cho3, ai3 pou2 kau3-hoe7 e5 toa7 khiam3-khoeh.  Ng3-bang7 kau3 ni5-boe2 hit pun2 e7 cho3 chiaN5. U7-sip5un-te7 lang5 chiong in ka-ti7 sou2 cho3- -e5 kia3 lai5 hou7 goan2, bo5 tek-khak si7 lun7-khi2 seng3-keng e5 to7-li2 a2-si7 hou7 lan2 ti7 pai3-tng5 thang eng7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 kin7-lai5 hiaN-ti7u kia3 chit chit8 pun2 ; goan2 khoaN3 liau2, phah-sng3 e7 kham-tit keng2-chhut chit8-siu2 in3 ti7 chit-nih8 hou7 lin2 ia7 thang khoaN3. KiaN-liau2 i sia2--e5 bo5 thang chap8-chng5 ho2, Sou2-i2 chhiaN2 thoan5-to7 e5 sian-siN kap hiaN-ti7 tioh8 chu2-se3 chim-chiok, khoaN3 u7 sim2-mih8 ji7-ku3 m7-tu2-ho2 , to2-loh8 chit8 chat bot5u-tu2 hap8 ti7 seng3-chheh e5 ka3-si7.  Chhut-chai7 lin2 ho2-taN2 lai5 pian7-pok i, in-ui7 goanchai2 cho3 chit-siu2 si e5 hiaN-ti7 tek-khakbe7 siu7-khi3 lang5 hiam5; chong2-si7 sou2 pian7-pok-e5 tioh8 u7 in-toaN hiam5 u7 cheng5-li2 , chiah ho2-se3. Liat8-ui7 chaiN2-iuN7 chu2-chhais5oa sia2 beng5 kia3-lai5 Hu2-siaN5, kiam2-chha2 ithang in3 ti7 au7-tiuN e5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 . Si sichi7au e7-te2 sia2 :
