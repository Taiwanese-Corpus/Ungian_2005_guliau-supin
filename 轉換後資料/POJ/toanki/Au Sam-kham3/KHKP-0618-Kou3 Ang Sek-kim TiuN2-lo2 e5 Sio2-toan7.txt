Kou3 Ang Sek-kim TiuN2-lo2 e5 Sio2-toan7
Ou Sam-kham3
Ang5 Sek-kim TiuN2-lo2, ti7 Chu2-au7 1855 ni5 2 geh8 18 jit8 chhut-si3, i goan5-pun2 toa3 ti7 Koan-im-khiN Lai7-giam5 e5 sou2-chai7, i e5 ka-se3 sui-jian5 m7-si7 hu3-ju7, chong2-si7 i ti7 iu3-lian5 e5 si5 chin un-khun5 khou2-hak8, thau5-boe2 chin bian2-kiong2, kau3-boe2 tit-tioh8 seng5-kong.
Hit-si5 i ti7 18 he3 e5 si5, chiu7 chin ho2-taN2, ti7 Khe-chiu-te2 khai-siat oh8-tng5, chiu7 si3-koe3 chio hak8-seng put-chi2 choe7, i chiu7 chin jin7-chin ka3-si7 hak8-seng, bo5 joa7-ku2, tit-tioh8 chin ho2 e5 hong-pheng5, tui3 hit-si5 chiu7 chin chhut-mia5.
Kau3 Chu2-au7 1875 ni5, i chiu7 hou7 Kai Bok8-su chhiaN2 lai5 Pat-li2-hun ka3 gi7-oh8.  I ti7 hit-si5 sui-jian5 teh ka3 gi7-oh8, chong2-si7 ti7 le2-pai3-jit8 thoan5-to7 teh kong2 to7-li2, I sui-jian5 che7 ti7 toh-a2 cheng5, bin7 si7 khoaN3 ti7 toh-a2 au7. Thoan5-to7 chiu7 mng7 i, i chiu7 chin kiau-ngou7 in3 i kong2, Goa2 si7 nia2 Kai Bok8-su ka3 chheh e5 sin-sui2, m7-si7 nia2 i thiaN to7-li2 e5 sin-sui2; au7-lai5 to7-li2 na2 thiaN na2 u7.
Kau3 1891 ni5 11 geh8 15 jit8, hit jit8 I beh nia2 soe2-le2, Kai Bok8-su u7 kio3 i khi3 Tai5-pak Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 e5 pai3-tng5 ka7 i kiaN5 soe2-le2.  I tui3 hit-si5 liau2-au7, to7-li2 jit8-jit8 na2 chhim-sin3, iah8 siong5-siong5 kaN2 eng7 to7-li2 ka3-si7 i e5 hak8-seng.
Kau3 1899 ni5 6 geh8 18 jit8, i chiu7 hou7 kau3-hoe7 keng2 choe3 chip-su7, lian5-soa3 tng chip-su7 20 ni5 ku2.
Kau3 1919 ni5 6 geh8 15 jit8, chiu7 hou7 kau3-hoe7 kam3-soan2 choe3 tiuN2-lo2, chiap-soa3 15 ni5 ku2. I tui3 thiaN to7-li2 kau3 kin-ni5 60 ni5 ku2, lun7 i tui3 jip8 Ki-tok-kau3, thau5-boe2 sit8-heng5 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 kau3-gi7.  I chhut-chhui3 chiu7 kong2 ho5-peng5 e5 oe7, khoan2-thai7 lang5-kheh chin khiam-sun3, khek-khiam7 houN3-si, thiaN3 lang5  
chhin-chhiuN7 ka-ki7, giam5-siu2 hau3-to7, gian5-heng7 it-ti3, tui3 sin3 to7-li2 kau3 i ke3-ong2, m7-bat hioh le2-pai3.
Lun7 Ang TiuN2-lo2, tui3 Beng5-ti7 28 ni5 siu7 Tai5-pak koan7-thiaN beng7-leng7 i choe3 Tiong-chng e5 iok-siu2, kau3 Beng5-ti7 30 ni5 3 geh8 25 jit8, koh jim7-beng7 choe3 E7-kou-a2 e5 iok-siu2, soa3-chiap kau3 Beng5-ti7 31 ni5 8 geh8, kai2-jim7 choe3 Hai2-kiN5-chhu3 e5 po2-kah-kiok8-tiuN2.  Kau3 Beng5-ti7 32 ni5 koh kai2-jim7 choe3 Hou7-boe2 pan7-hu7-su7 te7 2 khu Chng-tiuN2, soa3-chiap kau3 Beng5-ti7 37 ni5 1 geh8 1 jit8, jim7-beng7 choe3 Sio2-pat-li2-hun e5 Khu-tiuN2.  Soa3-chiap kau3 Tai7-cheng3 9 ni5 3 geh8 31 jit8, in-ui7 sin-teh2 soe-lam2, chiu7 i-goan7 bian2-chit choan-bu7 hiu-iong2.
Lun7 Ang TiuN2-lo2, lek8-jim7 27 ni5-ku2 e5 kong-bu7, pan7 tai7-chi3 si7 chi3 kong bu5 su, un-khun5 chiat-che3, 27 ni5 ku2 long2 bo5 chhiaN3 siau2-su2, i ka-ki7 soa3 kiam bu5 liau7 e5 siau2-su2, koh chiong i e5 chhu3 choe3 iah8-tiuN5 e5 su7-bu7-sou2, long2 bo5 siu in e5 chhu3-soe3.  I chin gau5 thiaN3 i e5 pou7-ha7, sou2-i2 chin thang choe3 lan2-e5 bou5-hoan7.  U7 chit8-pai2 Chong2-tok-hu2 e5 siok8-koaN lai5 ti7 iah8-tiuN5, i chiu7 phau3 te5 chhiaN2 i, hit-si5 hit e5 siok8-koaN chiu7 mng7 i kong2, Khu-tiuN2 u7 ti7-teh a2 bo5 ?  I chiu7 in3-tap i kong2, Goa2 chiu7-si7 Khu-tiuN2, hit-si5 siok8-koaN chin7-chai7 kam2-sim--i.
TaN lun7 i e5 sou2 choe3, m7-na7 choe3 pun2 chng-bin5 e5 ho2 bou5-hoan7, ti7 khu-tiuN2 hoe7-gi7 e5 si5, soa3 chiong i choe3 oe7 lai5 bian2-le7 cheng3 khu-tiuN2, i it-seng e5 sou2 choe3, hou7 teng2-si-koaN sou2 kam2-sim e5 sou2-chai7 chin choe7.  Sou2-i2 cheng3-hu2 u7 siuN2-su3 i 6 e5 bok8-poe, nng7 e5 gun5-poe, kong-chiong, sin-chiong chap8-goa7 e5, ki5-thaN, siuN2-chng7, po-chng7 iah8 chin-choe7.
Lun7 Ang TiuN2-lo2 e5 thian-seng3 houN3 hak8, sou2-i2 i chin ti3-i3 ti7 kau3-iok8, i khoaN3 chng-bin5 e5 chu2-te7 chhin-chhiuN7 ka-ki7 chit8-iuN7, chiu7 hoaN-hi2 hi-seng chin7-lat8 un7-tong7 kau3-hoa3 e5 su7.  Tui3 hit-si5 i chiu7 tui3 kok hong-bin7 un7-tong7, chheng2-kiu5 koaN-thiaN tioh8 un2 i kian3-siat kong-hak8-hau7, i chiap8-chiap8 chheng2-goan7 bo5 soah, au7-lai5 cheng3-hu2 siu7 kam2-tong7, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 hi2-kho2 hou7 i kian3-siat kong-hak8-hau7, hit-si5 chih-tioh8 hi2-kho2, hit e5 hoaN-hi2 kong2 boe7 chin7.  Au7-lai5 sui5-si5 chit8-bin7 i7-pi7 keng-hui3, chit8-bin7 siat-ke3, bo5 ku2 chiu7 heng-kang.  Tui3 kong-hak8-hau7 khi2 ho2, chiu7 chhut-lat8 si3-koe3 khoan3-iu2 seng-tou5, tui3 hit-si5 liau2-au7 ni5-ni5 ke-thiN, kau3 kin-na2-jit8 siu7 chit keng hak8-hau7 chai-poe5 e5 lang5, hian7-si5 ti7 koaN-thiaN, hak8-hau7, siong-tiuN5, kok kai3 teh oah8-tang7 e5 lang5, che chiu7-si7 i e5 kong-chek.
TaN i hiong2-siu7 80 hoe3 chiu7 li7-khui-se3-kan, sit8-chai7 chin thang hou7 lan2 siong-pi e5 sou2-chai7, in-ui7 sit-loh8 chit8 e5 chiah ho2 e5 TiuN2-lo2, hoan2 to3-tng2 lai5 siuN7, lan2 na7 oh8 i, au7-lai5 lan2 oe7 kap i hiong2-siu7 bo5 piN7, bo5 kan-khou2, sian7-chiong kui Chu2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 618 ho7, 1936 ni5 9 geh8)
