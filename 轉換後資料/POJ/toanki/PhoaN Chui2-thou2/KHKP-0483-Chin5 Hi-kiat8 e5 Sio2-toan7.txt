Chin5 Hi-kiat8 e5 Sio2-toan7
PhoaN Chui2-thou2
Hi-kiat8-kun si7 Bang2-kah e5 hoe7-iu2, ia7 si7 tok8-sin e5 chheng-lian5.  I e5 goan5-chip8 ti7 Toa7-khoe.  I soe3-han3 chiu7 thak8 Han3-bun5, au7-lai5 koh thak8 Kong-hak8-hau7 kau3 chut-giap8, liau2-au7 chiu7 lai5 Bang2-kah hou7 i e5 a-chek khan-seng5; na7-si7 i e5 sin-the2 soe-jiok8, siong5-siong5 phoa3-piN7, keng-ke3 choe7-choe7 i-seng e5 chhiu2-thau5 ia7 si7 peng5-siong5.  Put-heng7 ti7 ku7 ni5 7 geh8 koh hoat-piN7 chiu7 jip8 Siong5-san PiN7-iN7, long2 bo5 khah ho2, jian5-au7 koh choan2-jip8 Tai5-pak PiN7-iN7, na7-si7 i e5 piN7-cheng3 jit8-jit8 na2 tang7, kau3 2 geh8 28 ho7 chiu7 piat8-se3.  Hiong2-siu7 24 he3.
Hit e7-pou goa2 chhoa7 hoe7-iu2 chap8-goa7 lang5 kau3 Chhiah-sip8-ji7-sia7 e5 chong3-sek-tiuN5 chip-heng5 chong3-sek e5 le2-pai3, ia7 choe7-choe7 lang5 sang3-kau3 thiong2-pou--nih8.
Chit e5 Hi-kiat8-kun chiau3 goa2 chha5-kho2 u7 thang choe3 lan2 e5 ho2 bou5-hoan7.  
1.Lun7 i e5 seng3-chit.  Tui3 soe3-han3 chin un-sun5, khaim-pi, thun-lun2, jin5-ai3, chun-keng3 si7-toa7, thiaN3 si7-soe3, kap lang5 ho5-bok8, bo5 tham-sim, bo5 chiah8-hun, bo5 lim-chiu2, hou7 lang5 sin3-iong7,hoan7-su7 kin2-sin7, i an2-ni kiaN5 u7 hah8 ti7 Seng3-chheh e5 ka3-si7. 
2.Lun7 i e5 sin3-giong2.  I tui3 tiam3 i e5 a-chek e5 bi2-tiam3 chiu7 siong5-siong5 kap peng5-iu2 lai5 thiaN Ian2-soat-hoe7.  Le2-pai3-jit8 soa3 lai5 thiaN to7-li2, bo5-ku2 chiu7 boe2 Seng3-keng, Seng3-si, khun5-khun5 kang2-kiu3 hit e5 i3-su3.  Bo5-ku2 beng5-pek8 Ki-tok-kau3 si7 Chong-kau3-tiong te7-it ko-siong7 e5 Chong-kau3; sou2-i2 ti7 Tai7-cheng3 12 ni5 9 geh8 2 ho7, hou7 Tan5 Bok8-su mng7 to7-li2 soa3 jip8 Seng3-hoe7.  I tui3 soe2-le2 liau2, tu5-khi3 sin-the2 put-an, i2-goa7 han2-tit hioh le2-pai3.  Koh thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, i na7 eng5 e5 si5 chiu7 thak8 Seng3-keng, ki5-to2, u7-si5 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 tiam3-lai7 e5 lang5 thiaN, ia7 bian2-le7 in a-chek saN e5 gin2-na2 lai5 choe3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 hak8-seng, iu7-koh na7 beh lai5 
le2-pai3 e5 si5 khah-siong5 u7 chio in e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 thiaN to7-li2.  Sui-jian5 ti7 phoa3-piN7 tiong iu5-goan5 siong5-siong5 thak8 Seng3-keng kap gim5-si, ki5-to2, ia7 u7 thak8 choe7-choe7 e5 Chong-kau3-chheh.  I an2-ni u7 teh chin7 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 pun2-hun7.
3.Lun7 i e5 ui5-gian5.  I beh si2 e5 si5 khou2-khng3 i e5 a-ma2, lau7-bu2, tioh8 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, au7-jit8 chiah thang saN-kiN3-bin7 ti7 Thian-tong5.  Koh khou2-khng3 in m7-thang hoan5-lo2, se3-kan si7 chhin-chhiuN7 kheh-koan2.  Kau3 teh-beh ke3-khui3 e5 si5, ka7 i e5 a-ma2, lau7-bu2 kong2. “Goa2 beh lai5-khi3 lah!”  Bin7 chhio3-chhio3 chiu7 ke3-khui3.  Ke3-jit8 si7 le2-pai3-jit8, i e5 a-ma2 kap lau7-bu2 u7 lai5 pai3-tng5 thiaN to7-li2 soa3 ka7 hu3-chong3-sek e5 lang5 seh-sia7.
TaN ng3-bang7 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7 thoe3 in ki5-to2, ng3-bang7 in oe7 chiaN5-choe3 Siong7-te3 e5 peh-siN3, si7 goa2 e5 sim sou2-goan7.   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 483 koan3, 1925 ni5 6 geh8)
