Sio2-toan7  Tan5 Lok8 Sian-siN
PhoaN To7-eng5
Sian-siN si7 PheN5-ou5 Ou5-sai-chng sai khe e5 lang5, chhut-si3 ti7 Beng5-ti7 23 ni5. I e5 chou2-chiuN7 che7-che7 long2 u7 chin3-oh8, sou2-i2 i ti7 se3-han3 e5 si5, pe7-bo2 chiu7 sang3 i jip8 chu-pang5 kang2-kiu3 Han3-hak8.  Au7-lai5 u7 khi3 thak8 Kong Hak8-hau7 lak8 ni5 ku2, i-tioh8 iu-teng2 e5 chut-giap8.  Hak8-hau7 e5 sian-siN ai3-sioh i e5 chai5-tiau7, chiu7 lau5 i tiam3 pun2-hau7 cho3 kau3-oan5.  I si7 chin seng5-sim keng3 sin5-beng5 e5 lang5; mui2-jit8 tui3 oh8-nih8 hioh-kang tng2-lai5, chiu7 sui5-si5 khi3 sio-hiuN tiam2-chek.
Sui-jian5 sin ku kau3-iok8 e5 chit, iu7-koh hiah-nih8 jiat8-sim teh keng3-tiong7 sin5-beng5; chong2-si7 ti7 i e5 sim-lai7 put-si5 u7 teh khoa3-lu7 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7: “Lang5 kau3 si2-au7 m7-chai siaN2 khoan2?”  Sou2-i2 u7 chit8-pai2 bat koat-sim beh li7-khui bou2-kiaN2 khi3 cho3 chhai3-iu2.
Tu2-ho2 u7 chit8 ni5 e5 ha7-ki5 hiu-ha7-tiong, siu7 siong7-koaN phai3 koe3-lai5 Tai5-oan5 hu3 Kau3-oan5 e5 Kang2-sip8-hoe7.  U7 peng5-iu2 chhoa7 i khi3 Thai3-peng5-keng2 e5 pai3-tng5 le2-pai3; hit jit8 tu2-tu2 si7 kou3 Song3 Tiong-kian Bok8-su teh soat-kau3.  Te3 bak8 chiu7-si7, “Tham-chai5 si7 ban7-ok e5 kin-goan5” (1 The5. 6:10).  I thiaN-liau2 sim-tiong am3-am3 u7 kam2-kek e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 siuN7 beh lai5 sin3 Ia5-sou-kau3.  Kau3 Kang2-sip8-hoe7 beng5-pek8 beh tng2-khi3 e5 si5, u7 peng5-iu2 po3 i lai5 Sin-lau5 Chheh-pang5 be2 chit8 pun2 “Thian-to7 Sok-goan5.”  I chiu7 me5-jit8 khin5-khin5 thak8 hit pun2 chheh.  Tui3 an2-ni na2 beng5 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 to7-li2 si7 chin-sit8, chiu7 ho2-taN2 pang-sak cheng5 sou2 chun-tiong7 e5 kui2-sin5, lai5 keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3.
Ti7 i e5 pun2-hiuN long2 bo5 lang5 sin3 Chu2.  Hit-si5 tu2-ho2 IuN5 Se3-chu3 Bok8-su tiam3 ti7 Ma2-keng cho3 PheN5-ou5 Soan-to7-hoe7 e5 Bok8-su.  I chiu7 chio IuN5 Bok8-su khi3 in Sai-khe siat chit8 keng Hok-im-tng5.  Kho2-sioh au7-lai5 in ji7-ui7 lng2 koe3-lai5 Tai5-oan5, ia7 bo5 pat8 lang5 thang ke3-siok8 kou2-bu2, ti3-kau3 chit-tiap8 soa3 hoe3-chi2 khi3.

TaN i tui3 sin3 Chu2 liau2-au7, chin ti3-i3 khe-kho2 Seng3-keng e5 to7-li2, chiu7 ti7 Ma2-keng tui3 IuN5 Bok8-su Nia2 se2-le2.  Koh koe3 bo5 loa7-ku2, soa3 hian3-sin beh lai5 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang.  Sou2-i2 ti7 Tai7-cheng3 7 ni5, chiu7 lai5 jip8 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 thak8 chheh si3 ni5 ku2.  Ti7 oh8-tiong hoan7-su7 kap peng5-iu2 chin ho5-bok8, ia7 gau5 keng3-tiong7 sian-siN.  I e5 thian-chu beng5-bin2, thak8-chheh ia7 bian2-kiong2, sou2-i2 chut-giap8 te7-it mia5.  Thoan5-to7-kiok8 chiu7 phai3 i khi3 tiam3 Tang-kang2 e5 kau3-hoe7; ti7-hia nng7 ni5 ku2.
Tai7-cheng3 13 ni5 4 goeh8 kan, u7 koh siu7 Thoan5-to7-kiok8 ka7 i choan2-jim7 khi3 tiam3 Pak-kang2.  Khi3 hia oa2 nng7 goeh8-goa7 jit8, chhut-lat8 pou3-to7, sun5-si7 hoe7-iu2, soa3 tu2-tioh8 na5-au5 khi2 thiaN3, bo5 siaN thang cho3 kang, chiu7 lai5 I-koan2 taN2-tiap8 chha-put-to saN le2-pai3 ku2.  Kau3 khah ho2, chiu7 koh tng2-khi3 cho3 kang. Lan2 chai Pak-kang2 si7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 Put8-kau3 e5 tiong7-iau3-te7; lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 siat-lip8 boe7 loa7-ku2, sin3-tou5 iau2 chio2, koh loan2-jiok8; hian7-chai7 e5 pai3-tng5 si7 eng7 soe3--e5, eh8-eh8 koh bo5 hap8-eng7.  Tu2-ho2 Liam5 Bok8-su khi3 sun5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7 kap i ki3 chhoe7 te7, pi7-pan7 thang kian3-tiok sin pai3-tng5.
In-ui7 i e5 na5-au5 peN7 iau2-boe7 chap8-hun ho2, siuN chhut-lat8 cho3 kang, ti3-kau3 na5-au5 e5 peN7 koh hoat-choh khah siong-tiong7.  I pun2-sin teh siuN7, tioh8 chiam7-si5 hioh-khun3 khah ho2, sou2-i2 chiu7 tng2-khi3 in PheN5-ou5.  Sim-i3 si7 siuN7 beh liau5-iong2 sin-mia7; chong2-si7 khoaN3-tioh8 pun2-hiuN chhin-lang5 e5 ou-am3, chiu7 bo5 sioh-tioh8 pun2-sin phoa3-piN7 e5 sin-the2, iu5-goan5 chhoe7 ki-hoe7 pou3-to7.  Bat u7 lang5 ki-chhi3, kong2 i e5 peN7 si7 khi3 hou7 Pak-kang2-ma2 thau7-tioh8 tok8-ioh8, in e5 Pak-kang2-ma2 si7 khah-siaN3; kio3 i m7-thang thoan5 hit ho7 hoan-a2-kau3.  Sui-jian5 si7 an2-ni, Sian-siN long2 bo5 tam3-chi3, iu5-goan5 ho2-taN2 kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 mia5.
Tui3 an2-ni i e5 cheng3-thau5 na2 tim5-tang7.  Ti7 ku7-ni5 10 goeh8-kan chiu7 koh koe3-lai5 Tai5-lam5 Sin-lau5 I-koan2 taN2-tiap8.  Chiau3 I-seng ka7 i chin2-toan3, chit ho7 peN7-cheng3 chin ui5-lan5.  Jit8-koe3-jit8 sin-the2 na2 loan2-jiok8, be7 chiah8, be7 khi2-lai5.  Kau3 chiaN-goeh8 18 mi5, tu2-ho2 si7 le2-pai3 mi5-hng, oa2 8 tiam2-cheng, chiu7 chhiu2 kiah8 
koan5, pi2 tui3 thiN-teng2 khi3, ka7 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, “Goa2 beh tng2-khi3 lah.”  Chiu7 an2-ni an-jian5 koe3-khui3, khi3 Thian-tong5 kap Chu2 tiam3.  Hiong2-siu7 36 hoe3.
Keh jit8, chiu7-si7 19 e7-pou 3 tiam2 cheng, Pa Phok-su7 cho3 chu2-li2, chip-heng5 chong3-gi5 e5 le2-pai3.  Lai5 hoe7-chong3--e5 oa2 u7 200 lang5.  U7-e5 chin hng7, chhin-chhiuN7 tui3 Tang-kang2 Kau3-hoe7 u7 Lim5 Ian3-sin5 Bok8-su kap in e5 tiuN2-lo2 chip-su7 cho3 tai7-piau2; ki5-u5 u7 i e5 sian-siN, peng5-iu2, kap kau3-hoe7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 lai5 hu3 chit e5 chong3-sek.  Che7-che7 lang5 khoaN3-liau2 bo5 m7 siong-sim lau5 bak8-sai2.
Le2-pai3 soah, chu7 hip-siong7.  Jian5-au7 chiah sang3 i khi3 tai5 ti7 Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7 e5 kong-thiong2--nih8.
Lun7 Sian-siN tiam3 se3-kan, i e5 lek8-su2 khiok si7 te2-te2.  Chong2-si7 phi2-jin5 tui3 sou2 khoaN3, sou2 thiaN ai3 koh ke sia2 kui2-ku3, ng3-bang7 e7 thang cho3 lan2 e5 li7-ek.
1.Chin gau5 thun-lun2.  Ti7 phoa3-peN7 tiong, i m7-bat tui3 bou2-kiaN2 lai5 siak-sau, a2-si7 tui3 peng5-iu2 lai5 kong2 sim2-mih8 oan3-thian iu5-jin5 e5 oe7.  Siong5-siong5 si7 hoaN-hi2 kau-poe5 Chu2, pi7-pan7 theng3-hau7 Chu2 e5 chin2-tiau3.  I chai i e5 peN7 bo5 thang koh ng3-bang7 tiam3 se3-kan; sou2-i2 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 khng3 i tioh8 jiat8-sim toe3 Chu2 Ia5-sou.  Khiok choe3-lang5 ti7 te7-chiuN7 be7 bian2-tit u7 jin5-cheng5 ti7-teh; in-ui na7 u7 i e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 lai5 khoaN3 i; i pun2-sin chiu7 lau5 bak8-sai2.  I3-su3 chiu7-si7 koh bo5 loa7-ku2 tioh8 kap i sou2 thiaN3 e5 peng5-iu2 pun-li7.
2.Cho3 Chu2 chin7-tiong e5 lou5-pok8.  I pun2-sin m7-si7 sim2-mih8 gau5 soat-kau3 e5 sian-siN.  M7-ku2 i ti7 le2-pai3 cha2-khi2 long2 kim3-chiah8 ki5-to2, chiah soat-kau3; sou2-i2 sou2 kong2 e5 to7-li2 u7 toa7 kam2-tong7 lang5 e5 sim.  I chin gau5 chhoe7 ki-hoe7 pou3-to7; ia7 gau5 the2-sut kan-khou2 e5 lang5.  I e5 seng3-chit chin un-sun5, hou7 gin2-na2 chin ho2 chhin-kin7.  Sou2-i2 thiaN-tioh8 i koe3-sin, lian5 i sou2 ka3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 hak8-seng ia7 lau5 bak8-sai2.
3.I gau5 thiaN3 kau3-hoe7, kau3-hoe7 ia7 chin thiaN3 i.  Lun7 i peN7-tiong e5 kian3-bu2, hek8-si7 Thoan5-to7-kiok8, peng5-iu2, ko3-jin5, hit-ho7 m7-bian2 kong2-khi2.  Sian-siN i2-keng li7-khui Tang-kang2 e5 kau3-hoe7, chong2-si7 hia e5 hoe7-iu2 thiaN-kiN3 i e5 phoa3-peN7, chiu7 the3 i ki5-to2, kia3 chiN5, pi7-pan7 ioh8, sang3 mih8, teng2-teng2.  Kau3 si2 e5 si5, koh kia3 50 khou3 lai5 ui3-bun7 i e5 ui5-chok8.  Khiok Pak-kang2 e5 kau3-hoe7, ia7 u7 chhe lang5 kap toa3 chiN5 lai5 tiau3-song, che ia7 si7 tui3 Chu2, chin saN toa3-tioh8.  Phi2-jin5 khoaN3-liau2, sim chin siu7 kam2-kek.  In-ui7 Sian-siN sit8-chai7 u7 ho2 e5 jin5-keh thang kam2-tong7 hoe7-iu2 e5 sim, ti3-kau3 tai7-ke chha-put-to bo5 hian2-chhut tong5-cheng5 be7-tit koe3-sim.  Chit ho7 e5 kau3-hoe7 thang kong2 si7 “Jin5-ai3 e5 Kau3-hoe7”.  Ng3-bang7 Chu2 ka7 lin2 chiok-hok, hou7 lin2 na2-gau5 hian2-chhut jin5-ai3 e5 koe2-chi2, lai5 cho3 pat8 kau3-hoe7 e5 bou5-iuN7.
4.KhoaN3-phoa3 se3-kan.  I m7-na7 hian3 pun2-sin cho3 thoan5-kau3 e5 kang na7-tiaN7.  I u7 chiong sou2 eng3-tit e5 hian7-kim 3, 4 chheng khou, long2 hou7 i e5 hiaN-ti7 khi3, bo5 kap in pun.  Chi2-u7 pun tam7-poh8 khah m7 chiaN5-mih8 e5 chhan5-hng5, kaN2 tat8 u7 chiaN5-chheng khou.  Na7 u7 chiN5, i khah-siong5 ai3 be2 sin-chheh lai5 kang2-kiu3.  I ia7 siuN7 i pun2-sin hian3 hou7 Chu2 si7 bo5 kau3-giah8, kau3 beh si2 hit kui2-jit8 cheng5, u7 bian2-le7 i e5 toa7-kiaN2 tioh8 chiap-soa3 i e5 kha-pou7.
5.Tui3 i e5 ui5-gian5 u7 eng2-oan2 e5 ng3-bang7.  Sian-siN u7 nng7 e5 hau7-siN, nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 long2 iau2 se3-han3.  In-ui7 i e5 chhin-lang5 long2 si7 se3-siok8 lang5, sou2-i2 Sian-siN u7 ui5-gian5, m7-ai3 i e5 bou2-kiaN2 tng2-khi3 tiam3 PheN5-ou5.  TaN i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 u7 tit-tioh8 Lou5 Kou-niu5 e5 ho2-i3 hou7 i tiam3 Lu2-oh8 pang-chan7 chhui-su7-he7 e5 kang.  Ng3-bang7 thak8 Po3 e5 lang5, si5-siong5 the3 i e5 ka-chok8 ki5-to2.  PheN5-ou5 sng3 si7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 Soan-to7-hoe7, Sian-siN si7 PheN5-ou5 chhut-sin thau5-chit8 e5 Thoan5-to7-su.  Lan2 tioh8 the2-chhat Sian-siN ui5-gian5 e5 i3-su3, ti3-i3 the3 PheN5-ou5 kap i e5 ui5-chok8 lai5 ki5-to2.
  6.Tui3 i beh tng7-khui3 e5 si5, u7 ki5-koai3 e5 am3-si7.  Sian-siN beh koe3-sin hit e7-pou e5 si3-tiam2 poaN2-cheng, phi2-jin5 u7 koh khi3 ka7 i khoaN3; khoaN i e5 khoan2, chai si7 bo5 loa7-ku2 e5 lang5.  Soa3 mng7 i khoaN3 i e5 sim u7 long2 peng5-an bo5?  I thim3-thau5, hian2-beng5 u7 chin peng5-an e5 i3-su3.  Chiu7 the3 i ki5-to2, kiu5 Siong7-te3 thau2-pang3 i e5 lou5-pok8 sin-the2 e5 kan-khou2.  Kau3 hit-am3, oa2 chhit-tiam2 poaN3, phi2-jin5 ka-teng5 e5 le2-pai3 soah, chiu7 hian-khui Chu2-jit8 Sin5-liong5 4 goeh8 26 ho7: “Su7-the5-hoan2 si7 cho3 thau5-chit8 e5 sun5-to7-chia2” e5 su7, soa3 sia2 kui2-ku3 ai3 cho3 ka3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 hak8-seng e5 lou7-eng7: “TaN Su7-the5-hoan2 i u7 peng5-an khi3 thiN-teng2 kap Chu2 saN-kap tiam3.  In-ui7 i si7 chin7-tiong ui7-tioh8 Chu2 e5 mia5 lai5 pang-sak siN3-mia7; Siong7-te3 u7 pi7-pan7 sui2-sui2 oah8-mia7 e5 bian2-liu5 lai5 ti3 ti7 i e5 thau5-khak--nih8.”  Chiah e5 oe7 sia2-liau2, chiu7 thiaN-tioh8 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 khui-siaN teh khau3.  Phi2-jin5 koaN2-kin2 khi3 ka7 i khoaN3, to si7 Sian-siN teh beh koe3-khui3.  KhoaN3 si5-cheng tu2-tu2 si7 khiam3 10 hun 8 tiam2.  Chit-tiap8 teh sia2 i e5 sio2-toan7, chiu7 ai3 eng7 chit-chan5 cho3 kiat-boe2.  In-ui7 siuN7-liau2 sit8-chai7 u7 ki5-koai3 e5 am3-si7 ti7-teh.  Sian-siN chin-chiaN3 e7 kham-tit liat8 ti7 Su7-the5-hoan2 hit poe3, chin7-tiong kau3 si2, Chu2 e5 lou5-pok8.  Sou2-i2 chhim-bang7 lan2 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 tioh8 toe3 chit khoan2 e5 bou5-iuN7, pat8 jit8 chiu7 thang saN-kap hiong2-hok ti7 thiN-teng2 Beng5-kiong e5 sou2-chai7.   “Li2 tioh8 chin7-tiong kau3 si2, goa2 chiu7 beh eng7 oah8-mia7 e5 bian2-liu5 siuN2-su3 li2” (Khe2-si7-liok8 2:10; che si7 4 goeh8 26 ho7 sou2 tioh8 liam7 e5 Kim-ku3),
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 480 koan3, 1925 ni5 3 geh8)
