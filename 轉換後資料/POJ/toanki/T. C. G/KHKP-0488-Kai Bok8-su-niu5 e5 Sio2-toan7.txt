Kai Bok8-su-niu5 e5 Sio2-toan7
T.C.G.
Kai Bok8-su-niu5, si7 siN3 TiuN mia5 kio3 Chhong-beng5, Tai5-pak Gou7-kou2-khiN chng e5 lang5, ti7 Chu2-au7 1878 ni5, 5 geh8 27 jit8 u7 ke3 Kai Bok8-su, tiam3 ti7 Tam7-chu2 put-chi2 ku2, au7-lai5 u7 poaN lai5 Tai5-pak khia7-khi2.  I bat kap Kai Bok8-su chhut-goa7 si3-koe3 sun5-si7 kau3-hoe7, ia7 bat kap i choe3-tin7 khi3 Ka-na2-tai7 nng7-pai2, chit8 pai2 si7 tui3 sai-peng5 khi3, u7 keng-ke3 kui2-na7 kok; chiu7-si7 In3-tou7-kok, Iu5-thai3-kok, Hoat-kok, Eng-kok, Bi2-kok,chiah e5 kok-tou7.  Tai5-oan5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kiaN5 hiah hng7, kap Se3-kai3 it-chiu, kaN2-si7 i na7-tiaN7.
Seng3-keng sou2 kong2 e5 sou2-chai7, i ia7 u7 khoaN3 put-chi2 choe7 ui7.  Bat thiaN i kong2, u7 khi3 khoaN3 Ai-kip8 e5 thah, kap Hoat-lo2-ong5 e5 sin-si, ia7 u7 khi3 khoaN3 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2, Pek-li7-heng5, Iok-tan3-ho5, Si2-hai2; koh u7 khi3 khoaN3 hui sou2 bat iu5 e5 he2-soaN, kap Pong-pe-a e5 kou2-chek; ia7 u7 khoaN3 Se3-kai3 chhut-mia5 e5 toa7-siaN5, chiu7-si7 Lo5-ma2, Pek-lim5, Pa-li2, Lun5-tun.  Sng3 chin u7 hok-khi3, u7 khoaN3 hiah choe7 e5 kou2-chek kap beng5-sou2. 
Lun7 i it-seng e5 hiong2-hok, put-chi chiau5-pi7, i e5 sin-the2 ia7 chin iong2-kiaN7.  Bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 ku7-ni5 6 geh8 18 jit8, hut-jian5 tu2-tioh8 lo2 chhut-huih e5 piN7, chin siong-tiong7, u7 chhiaN2 Te3 I-seng ka7 i i-ti7, kai-chai3 u7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 ho2, oe7 khi2-lai5 kiaN5.  Kau3 chit-pai2 koh-chai3 khi2, chiu7 jip8 ti7 Ma2-kai I-iN7; Liok8 I-seng kap Gou5 I-seng ia7 chin chin7-lat8 teh ka7 i i-ti7.  Kho2-sioh bo5 chhai2-kang, chiu7 ti7 9 geh8 15 jit8, e7-pou 6 tiam2 40 hun, li7-khui se3-kan; hiong2-siu7 65 he3.
I e5 hou7-e3 long2 ti7 Tai5-oan5.  I u7 chit8 e5 hau7-siN, nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2; i e5 hau7-siN Kai Joe7-liam5, teh choe3 soan-kau3-su, chiu7-si7 Tam7-chui2 Tiong-hak8-tiuN2, i e5 sim-pu7 si7 Ka-na2-tai7 Ross Bok8-su e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.  I e5 toa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2, Kai--si7 Ma2-lian5 u7 ke3 Bang2-kah Tan5 Chheng-gi7 Bok8-su.  I e5 te7-ji7 cha-bou2-kiaN2, Kai--si7 I2-li7 u7 ke3 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 Koa I5-su TiuN2-lo2.  Hian7-si5 i e5 lai7-goa7 sun, put-chi2 choe7 e5 , ia7 u7 chit8 e5 ka-na2-sun.  Ti7 i e5 sun e5 tiong-kan, u7 chit8 e5 i2-keng tiong2-seng5, chiu7-si7 Koa Siat-kai, hian7-si5 teh choe3 Tam7-chui2 Tiong-hak8 e5 sian-siN, ki5-u5--e5 iau2 teh thak8-chheh.
TaN lun7 i it-seng e5 choe3-lang5, put-chi2 kho2-chhu2, choe7-choe7 lang5 teh siau3-liam7 i.  I chin u7 jin5-ai3, gau5 thiaN3 lang5, pang-chan7 lang5, ia7 gau5 khoan2-thai7 lang5.  Cha2-si5 ti7 Tam5-chui2 e5 Sin5-hak8-seng kap Lu2-hak8-seng, tak8-e5 u7 tit-tioh8 i e5 khoan2-thai7.  Si3-hng e5 hoe7-iu2 kap thoan5-to7-su, na7 kau3 Tam7-chui2, long2 u7 tit-tioh8 i e5 khoan2-thai7, in-ui7 i si7 chin toa7-liong7 e5 lang5.
I e5 khoan2, siong5-siong5 hoaN-hi2, han2-tit khoaN3 i u7 hian2-chhut hoan5-lo2 e5 khoan2, ia7 bo5 kiau-ngou7, bo5 kek-phai3, bo5 ai3 kap lang5 ke3-kau3, chin gau5 thun-lun2; hoan7-na7 kap i saN-bat e5 lang5, tak8 e5 o-lo2 i.  I ti7 Ma2-kai I-iN7 ke3-ong2 liau2, i e5 kou2 peng5-iu2, Gou5 Bok8-su-niu5, u7 tui3 chhin-lang5 tho2 ai3 i e5 sin-si he7 ti7 in tau, i chiu7 kap i nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ka7 i oaN7 saN, chhit seng-khu; chiong i ka-ki7 e5 bin5-chhng5 hou7 i to2, chiong i e5 lang5-kheh-thiaN hou7 i he7, chin thiaN3-thang3 i, long2 bo5 kiaN.  Ti7 Tai5-oan5 iau2-be7-bat khoaN3-kiN3 an2-ni; hoan7-na7 ai3 choe3 lang5 e5 peng5-iu2 tioh8 oh8 chit khoan2 e5 bou5-iuN7.  Koh-chai3 Lau5 Bok8-su chin chhut-lat8 siat-hoat choe3 koan-bok8, si7 Se-iuN5-sek, eng7 ou-pou3 lai5 piN ti7 goa7-bin7, u7 chhong3 chit8 e5 khang, jip8 po-le5, hou7 lang5 thang khoaN3 i e5 bin7; i e5 sin-si u7 chiam ioh8, khng3 lak8-jit8 ku2 chiah bai5-chong3, in-ui7 teh theng3-hau7 Kai Sian-siN tui3 Loe7-toe7 tng2-lai5 thang khoaN3; ti7 hit lak8-jit8 e5 tiong-kan, chin-choe7 lang5 lai5 bai7-song.
Chong3-sek hit jit8, i e5 koan-bok8 u7 tui Gou5 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 chhu3, kng kau3 Siang-lian5 theng5-chhia-tiuN5, si7 i e5 chhin-lang5 kap thoan5-kau3-su ka7 i kng.  Au7-lai5 tui3 he2-chhia chai3 kau3 Tam7-chui2 e5 sek-tiuN5, chiu7-si7 Tam7-chui2 Le2-pai3-tng5.  Hit jit8 lai5 hoe7-chong3 e5 lang5, chong2-kiong7 u7 lak8-pak goa7 lang5.
