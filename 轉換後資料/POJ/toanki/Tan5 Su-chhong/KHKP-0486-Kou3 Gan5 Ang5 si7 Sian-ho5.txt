Kou3 Gan5 Ang5 si7 Sian-ho5
Tan5 Su-chhong
Ang5--si7 Sian-ho5 chit8 mia5 kio3 Eng2-chim2, hian7-si5 lang5 chheng-hou7 i cho3 Gan5 Sian-seN-ma2.
Ti7 1846 ni5, 4 goeh8 8 jit8 chhut-si3 ti7 Tai5-lam5 chhi7 Lam5-ho5-ke; lau7-pe7 Ang5 Khian5-heng, lau7-bo2 Chng--si7.  Pe7-bo2 cho3 chun5-thau5 hang5 e5 chit-giap8; se3-han3 e5 si5 pe7-bo2 ka-ke3 khah kan-khou2.  I u7 nng7 e5 hiaN, nng7 e5 sio2-ti7; ji7-peh hoe3 e5 si5 ke3 hou7 Hui5-a2-hang7 ke Gan5 Eng2-seng5 chek, au7-lai5 chiu7 seN chit8 e5 cha-pou kap nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.
Ti7 37 hoe3 e5 si5 tui3 chit8 e5 chi2-moe7 Tan5--si7 Lian5 (Koe3-m2) tit-tioh8 to7-li2.  In-ui7 Eng2-chek cho3 seng-li2 ti7 Oan-li2-ke(Sian7-hoa3), Eng2-chim2 tioh8 kap i khia7-khi2 ti7 hia.  Sui-si7 toa3 Oan-li2-ke li7 chin-hng7, iau2-ku2 siong5-siong5 lai5 Tai5-lam5 le2-pai3.
Tng-si5 Thau5-sia7 kap Hoan-a2-chhan5 ia7 siat pai3-tng5, chong2-si7 bo5 sian-seN toa3 hia.  Hit-tiap8 khah-siong5 u7 bok8-su, thoan5-to7 sian-seN kiaN5-lou7 kau3 Hoan-a2-chhan5, Thau5-sia7, cho3 chu2-li2 kong2 to7-li2.  In-ui7 tng-si5 boe7 u7 hoe2-chhia, lou7 tioh8 tui3 Oan-li2-ke keng-koe3, mui2 pai3 lak8 khah siong5 hioh ti7 Eng2-chim2 tau.  Eng2-chim2 ia7 chin ho2 khoan2-thai7 thoan5-kau3--e5; thoan5-kau3--e5 chiu7 than3 ki-hoe7 pou3-to7 ti7 hit te7-hng.  Tui3 an2-ni oan-li2-ke te7-hng e5 peh-seN3 kap kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 lang5 long2 kong-jin7 i si7 jip8 kau3--e5, him-bou7 to7-li2--e5.
Ti7 40 hoe3 e5 si5 Eng2-chim2 chiu7 chhoa7 i e5 kiaN2 (Gan5 Chin2-seng Sian-seN) lai5 Tai5-lam5 kau-thok Ko Thoan5-to-su Tiong2-peh ka7 i ka3 ji7-bo2, to7-li2, kau3 keh ni5 chiu7 hou7 i jip8 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 Tiong-oh8.  Liau2-au7 ia7 hou7 i nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 chiap-soa3 lai5 thak8 Lu2-oh8.
Ti7 47 hoe3, chiu7-si7 Beng5-ti7 26 ni5, 9 goeh8 10 jit8, Kam Bok8-su ti7 Oan5-li2-ke ka7 i kiaN5 se2-le2.  Bo5 phah-sng3 jip8-kau3 liau2-au7 i e5 tiong7-hu Eng2-chek seN-khi2 khun2-tiok8, oan3-hin7, kong2, “Li2 hou7 goa2 chiah e5 kiaN2 long2 khi3 jip8-kau3 beh lian5-lui7 goa2 bo5 chou2-chong, bo5 kong-ma2.”  Tui3 an2-ni Eng2-chim2 tu2-tioh8 toa7-toa7 
khun2-tiok8, che7-che7 chhi-chham2, iau2-ku2 gau5 thun-lun2.  Sim7-chi3 kau3-boe2 ia7 in2-chhoa7 i e5 tiong7-hu, tit8-hu7, chhin-chhek, peng5-iu2, kui2-na7 lang5 lai5 sin3 to7-li2.
Tui3 chit-si5 chiu7 chiong i e5 chhu3 cho3 Le2-pai3-tng5.  Tai5-lam5 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 mui2 pai3 lak8 chiu7 phai3 hak8-seng khi3 hia cho3 chu2-li2 le2-pai3.
Gou7-chap8 hoe3 e5 si5 Moa5-tau7 Kau3-hoe7 sin3-tou5 tu2-tioh8 siu7 thai5.  Hit-si5 hui2-lui7 ia7 chhoa7 lai5 Oan-li2-ke, beh thai5 in chit8 ke; kai-chai3 in tai7-seng chau2, thai5 bo5 tioh8.
Ti7 53 hoe3 e5 si5 put-heng7 tiong7-hu Eng2-chek koe3-sin; 54 hoe3 chiu7 toe3 i e5 kiaN2 Gan5 Chin2-seng Sian-seN lai5 khia7-khi2 ti7 Tai5-lam5.  Ti7-hia chin-ku2 kap Bun5 Kou-niu5, Chu Kou-niu5 khi3 siaN5-lai7, siaN5-goa7 pou3-to7, chin7-lat8 kiaN5 bo5 ia3-sian7. 
Koh ti7 An-peng5 chhou-chhou siat kau3-hoe7 e5 si5, i ia7 chin u7 kan-siap8.  Ti7 kau3-hoe7 tiong i chin jiat8-sim siu2 to7-li2, siu2 An-hioh-jit8, chhut-lat8 hian3-chiN5 ti7 kau3-hoe7.  Tak8-ni5 i e5 kiaN2 na7 beh ka7 i cho3 seN-jit8, i chiu7 chiong hit tiau5 chiN5 theh8 lai5 hian3 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 lou7-eng7.
Koh ti7 sin3-tou5 tiong-kan na7 u7 m7 tu2-ho2--e5, siong5-siong5 saN bian2-le7, saN an-ui3, saN pang-chan7, bo5 sioh i e5 khui3-lat8.
Koh na7 u7 bou2 kau3-hoe7 bou7-chip8 ian5-hang7 i ia7 chin7 sou2 e7 tau3 chhut-lat8.  Chhin-chhiuN7 pun2-ni5 Sian7-hoa3 pai3-tng5 i5-choan2 kian3-tiok, i ia7 chin7 sou2 e7 kia3-hu3 chiN, kia3-hu3 i2-liau5.  Kau3 beh si2 hit-si5 ia7 siau3-liam7 Sian7-hoa3 e5 kau3-hoe7. 
Kho2-sioh in-ui7 siau3-lian5 e5 si5 kan-khou2 thoa-boa5, kau3 lau7 sin-the2 khah loan2-jiok8.  Tui3 he-ku sau3 e5 peN7, be7 thang chiau3 ki-hoe7 kau3 pai3-tng5 le2-pai3.  Sui si7 an2-ni, siong5-siong5 ti7 chhu3-lai7 chu7-chip8 ka-teng5 e5 le2-pai3, chhin-chhiuN7 le2-pai3-jit8 sio-siang7.
Lun7 i e5 ti7-ka chin khin5-khiam7; gau5 ka3-si7 kiaN2-sun ki5-to2, thak8 Seng3-keng; 
ti3-i3 siu2 le2-pai3-jit8.  I pun2-sin ia7 chin ti3-i3 ki5-to2.  Na7 u7 hoaN-hi2, a2-si7 iu-bun7, chiu7 ki5-to2, beh than3 Chu2 e5 chi2-i3.
Bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 6 goeh8-boe2 jiam2-tioh8 kam2-mou7; sui-jian5 u7 se3-ji7 chiau3-kou3 i-ti7, chong2-si7 peN7-se3 na2 tang7, sin-the2 na2 loan2-jiok8, chiu7 ti7 7 goeh8 27 jit8 hou7 Chu2 tiau3 i to3-khi3 Thian-tong5.  Hiong2-siu7 79 hoe3.
I e5 ui5-chok8: U7 chit8 e5 hau7-seN, chit8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, ji7-chap8 e5 sun-a2.
Hau7-seN chiu7-si7 Gan5 Chin2-seng Sian-seN, cho3 I-su chin ku2, ia7 si7 Tai5-lam5 iu2-beng5 e5 I-seng e5 chit8-e5.  Koh ia7 si7 Tai5-lam5 tong5-hoe7 e5 TiuN2-lo2.
Cha-bou2-kiaN2 chit8-e5 ke3 hou7 thoan5-kau3--e5, in-ui7 tiong7-hu koe3 sin chiu7 tng2-lai5 toa3 Tai5-lam5.
Toa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ke3 hou7 Gou5 To7-goan5, bo5 ku2 si2-khi3, u7 pang3 lak8 e5 sun-a2 ti7 E7-mng5, Jiau2-a.
Chit8 e5 ti7 Bi2-kok, Hak8-su7 i2-keng theh8-liau2, bo5 ku2 chiu7 u7 Sek8-su7 e5 te7-ui7; nng7-e5 ti7 Jit8-pun2 Tang-kiaN Beng5-ti7 Tai7-hak8 Hoat-kho; chit8-e5 ti7 Tiong-hoa Pak-kiaN I-choan; chit8-e5 ti7 Tang-kiaN Lu2-chu2 I-choan; chit8-e5 ti7 Tang-kiaN I-choan; chit8 e5 ti7 Loe7-te7 Tiong-hak8 chut-giap8 liau2, hian7-si5 u5-pi7 jip8-hak8; chit8 e5 ti7 Tai5-lam5 te7-it Ko-lu2; chit8 e5 ti7 Tai5-lam5 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 Lu2-hak8-hau7; si3 e5 ti7 Jit8-pun2 Sim-siong5 Sio2-hak8-hau7.  An2-ni thang kong2 si7 chin ho2-mia7.
Ti7 8 goeh8 6 jit8 ngou2-hou7 nng7 tiam2, ti7 Thai3-peng5-keng2 Le2-pai3-tng5 u7 cho3 i e5 ko3--piat sek, chhiaN2 Ko Kim-seng Bok8-su cho3 chu2-li2.  Lai5 hu3-hoe7 e5 lang5 u7 1000 goa7 mia5, ia7 u7 tiau3-tian7, tiau3-su5, lian5-tek8, hoe-chhia, jin5-lek8-chhia, chheN-hoe, I-seng-hoe7 e5 thoan5-the2, Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 thoan5-the2, hui-siong5 e5 che7 lang5, thang kong2 chin lau7-jiat8.  Che thang ki2-chhut Sian-seN-ma2 it-seng e5 cho3 lang5 kap jiat8-sim sin3 Chu2, sou2-i2 u7 seN-chhut chit ho7 ho2 e5 kiat-ko2.  Sit8-chai7 thang hou7 lan2 oh8.
