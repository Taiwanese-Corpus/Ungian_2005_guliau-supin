Ng5 Chip-su7 e5 Sio2-toan7
Ng5 kun mia5 Tiong3.  Tai5-lam5 Tang-mng5 kau3-hoe7 e5 Chip-su7; Sin-lau5 Chheh-pang5 In3-soat-pou7 e5 Chu2-jim7--lah.  Goan5-chek8 si7 Pak-mng5-kun7, Ka-li2-chng, Pou-teng2-liau5 Ng5-seng3 e5 tai7-chok8.  Tng Beng5-ti7 36 ni5, (28 ni5 cheng5) lai5 Sin-lau5 ka7 Kam Bok8-su cho3 kang.  In-ui7 i un-sun5 khin5-bian2, put-chi2 hou7 Kam Bok8-su o-lo2, chiah ku2-chian3 i jip8 Chheh-pang5 cho3 khioh ji7 in3 soat e5 kang. Tui3 an2-ni chiah poaN-chhiaN2 i e5 lau7-bu2 lai5 toa3 Tai5-lam5, kian3-lip8 ke-au7, soa3 chiap kau3 chit-tiap8, beh kau3 saN-chap8 ni5.
Ng5 kun tui3 siu7 Kam Bok8-su eng7 to7-li2 in2-chhoa7 liau2-au7, chiu7 pang3-sak i i2-cheng5 ke2 e5 sin3-giong2, choan-sim lai5 kui-hok8 ti7 Chu2.  In-ui7 i sou2 cho3 e5 kang, ia7 pi2 pat8-lang5 ke tit-tioh8 che7-che7 to7-li2 e5 ka3-si7.  Mui2 le2-pai3-jit8 chiu7 pang-chan7 Tang-mng5 kau3-hoe7 Chu2-jit8-oh8 e5 kang, au7-lai5 soa3 jim7 Tang-mng5 Kau3-hoe7 e5 Chip-su7.
Put-heng7 ti7 kin-ni5 gou7-goeh8 boe2 jiam2-peN7, chit8-jit8 chit8-jit8 khah-tang7, au7-lai5 kiam2-cha, chai kio3 si7 jiam2-tioh8 Tiong5-chit hu5-su e5 cheng3, chiu7 khi3 jip8 Tai5-lam5 I-iN7, in-ui7 i u7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, ti7 hit I-iN7 chai7-chit, thang li7-pian7 lai5 chiau3-kou3 i.
Chong2-si7 Chu2 e5 chi2-i3 i2-keng chiaN5, ai3 hioh I se3-kan e5 kang, sou2-i2 lang5 e5 cheng-sin5, ioh8 e5 khui3-lat8 long2 bo5 kong-hau7, soa3 ti7 6 goeh8 23 jit8 ngou2-hou7 7 tiam2 li7-khui se3-kan, hou7 Chu2 in2-chhoa7 jip8 Thian-tong5, hiong2 siu7 48 hoe3, pang3 he7 i ke3-sek kap kiaN2-ji5 chap8-lang5.  Lam5 6 e5, lu2 4 e5.  I e5 toa7 kong-chu2 hian7-chai7 ti7 Hong7-thian Boan2-chiu I-kho Tai7-hak8 thak8-chheh. Toa7 leng7-ai3 ti7 Chiang-hoa3 Ki-tok-kau3 I-iN7 jim7 khan3-hou7 e5 chit; ia7 chhu3 leng7-ai3 ti7 Tai5-lam5 I-iN7 cho3 khan3-hou7; ki5-u5 4 e5 iau2 ti Kong-hak8-hau7 ia7 u7 e5 se3-han3 ti7 ka-teng5, long2 boe7 hun-phoe3.

tiong-hou7 koh khin5-khiam7. I u7 9 e5 kiaN2-ji5, chiau3 khoaN3 si7 khah u7 siu7 thoa-lui7, chong2-si7 i7 khin5-khin5 khiam7-khiam7 lai5 in2-chhoa7 cheng3 kiaN2-ji5, sou2-i2 tui3 ti7 keng-che3-chiuN7 m7-na7 bo5 siu7 taN2-kek, hoan2-tng2 si7 iu5-iu5 chu7-chai7, i2-keng poe5-iuN2 saN e5 kiaN2-ji5 jip8 sia7-hoe7 kho2-i2 chu7-lip8.
