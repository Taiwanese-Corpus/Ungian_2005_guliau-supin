Ang5--si7 Chin e5 Sio2-toan7
Ang5--si7 Chin khah siong5 kio3-cho3 Hou-m2, si7 Te3 Hoan2 Bok8-su e5 leng7 sian-bo2. Chu2-au7 1854 ni5 chhut-si3, kau3 kin-ni5 ( = 1940 ni5), tu2 hiong2-siu7 86 hoe3.  46 hoe3 e5 si5 sin3 Chu2, kiong7 40 ni5 ku2 keng- koe3 sin3 e5 seng-oah8.  E7 lai5 chiu7-kin7 Chu2 e5 tong7-ki, si7 tui3 ti7 tu2-tioh8 piN7-thiaN3, kam2-kak peng5-si5 sou2 chong-pai3 e5 sin5- put8, be7 thang hou7 i e5 sim peng5-an, chiu7 siu7 khou2-khng3 koat-sim chun-than3 to7-li2 jip8 sin3-giong2 keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3.  I chin chiau3 pun2-hun7 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, Siong7- te3 si-in su3-hok hou7 i, hou7 i kiaN2-sun chin heng-ong7, kiaN2-ji5 sim-pu7, lai7 goa7 sun chong2 sng3 chiaN5 pah lang5.  Lun7 kau3 i e5 seng-peng5 chin kho2-chhu2, u7 che7-che7 bi2-tiam2.  Chit-tiap8 ki3 kui2-na7 hang7 hou7 lan2 cho3 siau3-liam7 :---
 1. Sin3-giong2 kian-kou3 :  Sin3 Chu2 liau2, sui siu7 lang5 kong-kek, phi2-pok8, iam3-chian7, ki-chhi3, i2-kip8 tu2-tioh8 hoan7- lan7 khun3-khou2, ia7-si7 iu2-chin3 bo5 the3. 
2. Ti3 i3 ka-teng5 :  Chin ki5-biau7 ! ! ! KiaN2-sun toa7 to-sou3 kap i tang5-sim keng3- pai3 Siong7-te3 kiaN5 thiN-lou7.  E7 an-ni, cha- kho2 khi2-lai5, bo5 m7-si7 tui3 ti7 i e5 sin3-sim kap sin3 e5 ho2 sou2 kiaN5 sou2 kam2-hoa3 lai5 chiaN5--e5, 
3. Gau5 thiaN3 Chu2 :  Si5-siong5 chiau3 sou2 e7 hong7-su7 kau3-hoe7, koan-sim ti7 Chu2. Tek8-piat8 thang hian2-chhut i e5 thiaN3--e5, chiu7-si7 hian3 kiaN2 hou7 Siong7-te3.  Cha-bou2 kiaN2 hian3 cho3 Siong7-te3 e5 lu2 chhe-eng7, chiu7-si7 khng Hoan2 Sian-siN-niu5.  Hau7- siN hian3 cho3 Siong7-te3 e5 lou5-pok8, chiu7-si7 Te3 Hoan2 Bok8-su. 
4. Jin5-ai3 ho5-peng5 :  I e5 cho3 lang5, tui3 lai7 tui3 goa7 chin oan5-boan2.  Tak8-lang5  
to o-lo2 i e5 ho2 lu2-tek, hou7 kiaN2-ji5 sim-pu7 chin hau3-sun7. 
5. Ai3 ki5-to2 :  Hoan7 u7 tu2-tioh8 toa7 sio2 su7 gau5 oa2-kho3 Siong7-te3, kau-hu3 I chhui-pai5.  I ti7 3 goeh8 chhe 8 jit8 khi2- piN7 ; ti7 3 goeh8 15 jit8 cha2-khi2 chiu7 siu7 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 tng2-khi3 ThiN Pe7 e5 ke. Tng ti7 peN7 chin loan2-jiok8 e5 si5, iau2-ku2 ki5-to2, kiu5 kong2 “Chu2 ah kiu3 goa2, Chu2 ah kiu3 goa2.”  
Phi2-jin5 chiau3 sou2 chai ki3 chiah--e5, chin siu7 kam2-kek.  Chhim-bang7 i e5 ui5- chok8 kap lan2 tai7-ke e7 oh8 i e5 ho2 bou5- hoan7 ! ! !   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 663 ho7,1940 ni5 6 geh8)
