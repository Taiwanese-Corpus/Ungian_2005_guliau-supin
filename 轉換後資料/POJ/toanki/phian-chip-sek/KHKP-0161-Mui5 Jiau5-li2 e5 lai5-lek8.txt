Mui5 Jiau5-li2 e5 lai5-lek8.
Chu2-au7 1805 ni5, ti7 Tai7-Tek-kok u7 chit8-e5 gin2-na2 chhut-si3, siN3 Mui5 mia5 Jiau5-li2, hit-si5 khiok bo5 sim2-mih8 mia5-siaN, chong2-si7 au7-lai5 chha-put-to thong se3-kan khoaN3 I-e5 ho2 sou2 kiaN5 long2 siu7 kam2-kek.  I-e5 pe7-bo2 pun2 si7 jiat8-sim pai3 Siong7-te3, iah8 toa7 ng3-bang7 hit e5 gin2-na2 tiong2-seng5 cho3 hak8-bun7 e5 lang5.  Sou2-i2 iau3-kin2 hou7 i khi3 oh8-nih8 thak8-chheh.  Hit-si5 i chap8-goa7 hoe3 na7-tiaN7, iau2-ku2 bu5-ok put-chok, u7 bat chiah8-chiu2, poah-kiau2, hong3-chhiong3 kiat-kau hui2-lui7.
I-e5 lau7-bo2 si2 hit-tiap8 Jiau5-li2 cho3 i tian-tian chui3, iah8 bo5 to3-khi3 khoaN3 i.  Koh chit8 pai2 kap i-e5 tong5-chhong chhut-goa7 hong3-tong7, bo5 loa7-ku2 i-e5 chiN5-chai5 hui2-liau2, bo5 poaN3-iN5 thang kap tiam3-chu2 sng3-siau3, chiu7 hou7 i liah8 khi3 koaiN-kaN, ho2 tit i-e5 lau7-pe7 the3 i heng5, chiah pang3 chhut--lai5.  
Tui3 chit si5 i na2 kiaN5 phaiN2, chiu7 na2 kan-khou2, ka-ti7 siuN7 beh khi3-sia5 kui-cheng3 chiu7 khin5-khin5 thak8-chheh, oh8-nih8 tak8-hang7 e5 kui-le7 iau3-kin2 beh than3, siong5-siong5 khi3 le2-pai3, jip8 kau3-hoe7, chiah8 boan2-chhan iah8 u7, chong-si7 an2-ni kiaN5 si7 than3 goa7-phoe5 na7-tiaN7, sim iu5-goan5 nge7, am3-cheN7 ai3 kiaN5 phaiN2, chiau3-ku7 e5 khoan2-sit.  Chit si5 jip8 pat8 keng oh8, ti7-hia u7 bat tu2-tioh8 cheng5 sou2 bat e5 chhong-iu2, chit e5 peng5-iu2 i2-keng u7 hoan2-hoe2 sin3 Kiu3-chu2, chiu7 chio Jiau5-li2 kap i khi3 chit8 ui7 cho3 ki5-to2-hoe7.  Jiau5-li hit me5-hng sou2 thiaN-kiN3 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3 toa7-toa7 kam2-kek i-e5 sim, kong2 taN koat-i3 beh pang3-sak tak8-hang7 e5 phaiN2, kui-hok8 Chu2 Ia5-sou; chiu7 tui3 chit si5 i ko2-jian5 sit8-chai7 ke2-piN3, na2-chhin-chhiuN7 chhut-si2 jip8 oah8.
I iah8 chin gau5 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, tak8 sou2-chai7 po3-iong5 in-tian2 e5 to7-li2.
Chhut oh8 liau2, i koe3-hai2 kau3 Tai7-Eng-hok, ti7 Lun5-tun-siaN5 iu5-goan5 chin jiat8-sim kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 mia5, au7-lai5 ti7 chit ui7 e5 kau3-hoe7 kui2-na7 ni5 tng Bok8-su e5 chit. 
Chit si5 Mui5 Jiau5-li2 chin khoa3-lu7 chit8 hang7, chiu7-si7 u7 hit-ho7 bo5 pe7-bo2 e5 gin2-na2 chin-che7, in long2 bo5 si7-toa7-lang5 thang chiau3-kou3, bo5 thang chiah8-chheng7, bo5 sou2-chai7 thang toa3, jit8--si5 mi5--si5 si3-ke3 liu5 ia7 bo5 lang5 lin5-bin2.  I che7-che7 jit8 teh siuN7 chit chan5 su7, iah8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 chi2-s i7 i-e5 lou7, khoaN3 u7 sim2-mih8 hoat-tou7 thang chin2-kiu3 hit-ho7 kiong5-khou2 e5 gin2-na2.  Ki5-to2 liau2 Siong7-te3 kam2-tong7 i-e5 sim, chi2-tiam2 i tioh8 soe3 chit8-keng chhu3 lai5 chiap-lap8 hiah-e5 se3-kiaN2 thang iuN2-chhi7 kiam ka3-si7, in2-chhoa7 in bat Kiu3-chu2, ng3-bnang7 kau3 toa7-han3 e7 cho3 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 lang5.
Tai7-seng chi2-u7 chiap-lap8 saN-chap8 e5, bo5 loa7-ku2 hit keng siuN se3 taN beh chaiN2-iuN7?  I iah8 long2 bo5 kap lang5 chham-siong5, 36 jit8 e5 tiong-kan tit8-tit8 ki5-to2 pek-chhiat kiu5 Siong7-te3.  Siong7-te3 chiu7 kam2-hoa3 chit8-e5 peng5-iu2 e5 sim, hou7 i kia3 $8000 tu2-ho2 kau3-giah8 thang khi2 sin e5 chhu3.  Au7-lai5 i chiam7-chiam7 ke-thiN kian3-ti3 kau3 gou7-keng, in-ui7 i liok8-siok8 u7 chiap-tioh8 gin5 92,0000.  Sui-jian5 tit-tioh8 hiah che7 gin5,ku2 m7-bat tui3 lang5 lai5 te5,si7 kan-ta kho3 kiu5 Siong7-te3 chiah tit-tioh8.
Gin2-na2 chiu7 un5-un5-a2 ke-thiN kau3 u7 2050 e5, kap teh ka3-s-i in e5 sian-siN leng7-goa7 u7 sng3 112 e5.
Sui-jian5 u7 hiah che7 thau5-chhui3 i2-kip8 in-chiuN5 kap pat8 mih8 sou2-hui3, i long2 m7-bat tui3 lang5 lai5 kong2, iah8 m7-bat sia chioh, teh khiam3-eng7 e5 si5-chun7 i tok8-tok8 jip8 ka-ti7 e5 pang5-keng kui7-loh8 tit8-tit8 kiu5 ThiN-pe7 siuN2-su3.
Kin7-lai5 chhut chit8 e5 toaN teh kong2 cho3 gin2-na2 e5 sou2-hui3, long2-kiong7 u7 chih-tioh8 gin5 1139,7168.  Che m7-si7 tui3 lang5 tho2 kan-ta pek-chhiat ki5-to2 chiah tit-tioh8.

Siong7-te3 che7-che7 pai2 khiok u7 bat chhi3 i-e5 lou5-pok8 e5 sim, u7-si5 gin5 long2 eng7 liau2 bo5 lau5 poaN3 iN5.  U7 chit8 pai2 tu2-a2 chiah8 e7-tau3 png7; hian7-chai7 bo5 bi2 thang chu2 e5-hng.  Mui5 Jiau5-li2 hit jit8 koh khah ti3-i3 kap ThiN-pe7 kau-poe5, sou2 khiam3-eng7 pai5-liat8 ti7 Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, kiu5 I si-in lai5 pou2-chou7 in e5 put-chiok.  Tu2-tu2 teh ki5-to2 u7 lang5 phah-mng5 theh8 gin5 kui2-na7 khou hou7 i thang cho3 sou2-hui3 e5 khai-eng7.
Tak8-jit8 Mui5 Jiau5-li2 bat kui2-na7 tiam2-cheng ku2 ki5-to2, kau-poe5 Siong7-te3, put-lun7 beh pan7 sio2-khao2 su7 a2-si7 khiam3 saN2-hoe3, iu5-goan5 si7 pai5-liat8 ko3-sou3 ti7 ThiN-pe7 e5 bin7-cheng5.  Seng3-keng tui3 thau5 kau3 boe2 tang5 ni5 i u7 thak8 si3-pian3.
Mui2 Jiau5-li2 ku3 chhit-chap8 hoe3 bat kui2-na7 pai2 khi3 goa7-kok che7-che7 sou2-chai7.  Hit-si5 put-lun7 ti7 Au-lo5-pa chiu, a2-si7 ti7 A-se-a chiu e5 Tiong-kok, Jip8-pun2-kok, i2-kip8 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka chiu, Ou-te7-li7-a chiu, I u7 nng7-hang7 e5 i3-su3, chit8 hang7 si7 beh kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 mia5, hou7 m7-bat to7-li2 e5 lang5 thang thiaN-kiN3.  Koh chit8 hang7 si7 ai3-beh chhoe7 Siong7-te3 e5 peh-siN3 lai5 saN kau-poe5, bian2-le7 an-ui3 hou7 in koh khah jiat8-sim cho3 Chu2 e5 kang.
Chit kui2-na7 pai2 kap i-e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chhut-goa7, i ka-ti7 bo5 gin5 thang cho3 lou7-hui3, long2 si7 teh kiu5 Siong7-te3.  Siong7-te3 tak8-pai2 u7 kia3 tu2-ho2 kau3-giah8 thang hu3 i eng7.  Khi3 goa7-kok hit-tiap8 u7 bat pun Seng3-chheh ke3-kiong7 174,3314 pun2.  Mui5 Jiau5-li2 hoe3-siu7 chiah8 kau2-chap8-goa7 hoe3 chiah si2; beh tai5 loh8 bong7 hit-tiap8 u7 kui2-na7 chheng lang5 khi3 sang3-chong3, hiah-e5 gin2-na2 iah8 teh toe3 ian5-lou7 kiaN5 ian5-lou7 hau2, put-chi2 toa7 chhiN-chham2 hoan5-lo2 in chit8 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 koe3-ong2, bo5 thang koh kiN3-tioh8 bin7.  TaN Mui5 Jiau5-li2 koe3-ong2 khoaN3 peh-siN3 kau3 chiah ai3 keng3 i, si7 in-ui7 siaN2-su7?  Chit8 hang7 i si7 put-chi2 toa7 sin3 e5 lang5. I iah8 liah8 kau-poe5 Siong7-te3 cho3 kek8 toa7 e5 hok-khi3; sou2-i2 put-lun7 teh ki5-to2, a2-si7 kang2-kiu3 Seng3-chheh, i-e5 sim kap Siong7-te3 tang5 chit8 e5 i3-su3, na2-chhin-chhiuN7 peng5-iu2 cho3-hoe2 tam5-lun7 e5 khoan2-sit.  I long2 m7-bat siuN7 ka-ti7 e5 li7-ek, iah8 bo5 it-tioh8 se3-kan sim2-mih8 mia5-siaN, i sou2 iau3-kin2 
si7 ai3 Chu2 e5 mia5 tit-tioh8 eng5-hian2, hou7 cheng3-lang5 thang chai Ia5-ho5-hoa chin-chiaN3 si7 Siong7-te3 chin-sit8 bo5 ke2, sou2 kong2-chhut tek-khak beh kiaN5.
I2-keng lau7 u7 bat cho3 kan-cheng3 kong2, “Hiah-che7 ni5 e5 tiong-kan Siong7-te3 u7 chiau3-kou3 iuN2-chhi7 goa2, kap goa2 choan5-ke e5 lang5, sou2 tioh8 eng7 e5 bi2-niu5 siuN2-su3 goan2 bo5 khiam3-khoeh, m7-bat chit8 pai2 sit-loh8.”
TaN hit-e5 lang5 sou2 kan-cheng3 e5 oe7 chin-chiaN3 u7 iaN2, in-ui7 tui3 i si2-au7 u7 lang5 ka7 Mui5 Jiau5-ii2 chha5-kho2 i sou2 chhun e5 ke-hoe2, chha-put-to tat8 chiuN7 pah-goa7 e5 gin5 chiN5 na7-tiaN7.  
Lan2 tai7-ke khoaN3 Mui5 Jiau5-li2 sou2 cho3 chin-chiaN3 e7 kham-tit cho3 lan2 e5 kui-ki2 hoat-tou7, lan2 iah8 tioh8 oh8 i-e5 ho2 bou5-iuN5, lai5 chhin-chhiuN7 i chhim sin3, jiat8-sim cho3 ho2-su7, chiah e7-tit thang kui eng5-kng lan2 e5 ThiN-pe7.
