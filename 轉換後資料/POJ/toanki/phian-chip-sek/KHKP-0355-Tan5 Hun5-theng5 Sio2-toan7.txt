
Tan5 Hun5-theng5 Sio2-toan7
Tan5 Hun5-theng5 Thoan5-to7 si7 Tang5-oaN siok8, Kim-tun hiuN e5 lang5, chhut-si3 ti7 A.D.1852.  I saN he3 e5 si5 pe7-bu2 u7 i5-ku lai5 Tai5-oan5, khia7-khi2 ti7 Gou7-khou2-khiN tsng tiong2-seng.  I 21 he3 e5 si5 u7 khi3 tsoe3 Kai Bok8-su e5 hak8-seng, Au7-lai5 i chiu7 tsoe3 thoan5-to7, toa3 ti7 kui2-na7 sou2-chai7 e5 pai3-tng5.  An2-ni tsoe3 kang u7 26 ni5 ku2, i2-au7 u7 hioh-khun3 2 ni5 goa7, kau3 Hoa5 bok8-su kau3 Tai5-oan5 e5 si5, I u7 lai5 ka3-si7 I-e5 thou2-im, ia7 u7 pang-chan7 I-koan2 kap Lu2-oh8-tng5 e5 kang, koh siong5-siong5 thoan5 to7-li2.  Tui3 hit-si5 I e5 sin-the2 chiap8-chiap8 u7 tu2-tioh8 phoaN3-piN7, chiu7 boe7-oe7 koh tam-tng khah tsoe7 e5 kang-tiaN5.  Au7-lai5 I u7 siu7-phai3 khi3 thoan5 to7-li2 ti7 Su7-lim5-koe e5 le2-pai3-tng5.  I u7 tu2-tioh8 sim-tsong-piN7 chiu7 hut-jian5 hun7-khi3 put-chi2 ku2 chiah tsai lang5.  Tui3 an2-ni sin-the2 chiu7 koh-khah soe-lam2, put-tek-put tioh8 hioh kang, lai5 cheng7-iong2 sin-the2.  Hit-ni5 I tu2-tu2 si7 60 he3, u7 tsoe3 kang ti7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 37ni5 e5 ku2.  Tui3 an2-ni I chiu7 chhoa7 I-e5 ke-au7 ke3 hai2 khi3 Hok-chiu, kap I-e5 toa7 kiaN2 khia7-khi2, ti7 hia 4 geh8 jit8 ku2, I-e5 piN7 na2 tang7 bo5 khi2 chhng5.  U7 Bi2-pou7-hoe7 e5 Bok8-su, TiuN2-lo2 chiap8-chiap8 lai5 tham3 I , soa3 thoe3 I ki5-to2; koh siong5-siong5 u7 tsu7-chip8 I e5 kau3-hoe7 lai5 ke-lai7 le2-pai3.  I2-au7 chiaN5 tsap8 jit8 e5 tiong-kan lang5 u7 khah song2-khoai3, chiu7 siuN7 beh to3 lai5 Tai5-oan5, bo5 phah-sng3 soa3 koh hou7 I kam2-tioh8 chin siong-tiong, chiu7 ti7 A.D.1914,3 geh8 27 ho7 an-jian5 sian7-chiong.  I u7 hiong2-siu7 62 he3.  U7 bai5-tsong3 ti7 Ban5-ip Lam5-koan goa7 Si5-seng-li2, Go5-thau5-hong7, nia2 kau3-hoa7 e5 hun5-te7.
