Gou5 Ke-bo2 e5 Sio2-toan7
Tsai7 11 geh8 27 ho, ti7 Pi-thau5 pai3-tng5 e5 pang5-keng, Siong7-te3 u7 tiau3 lan2 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 Gou5 Ke-bo2 (ju7-mia5, Bun5-pin) khi3 kap Tsu2 eng2-oan2 toa3.  Lan2 tsu7-jian5 tioh8 hoan5-lo2 boe7-oe7 thang ti7-chia koh-tsai3 kap i saN-kiN3; tsong2-si7 sim iah8 tioh8 peng5-an toa7 kam2-un, in-ui7 Gou5 Sian-siN 36 ni5 ku2 tsoe3 kau3-hoe7 chin7-tiong e5 chhe-eng7.  I bat ti7 kui2-na7 sou2-tsai7 tng thoan5-to7 e5 chit, chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 Ka-poa3-soa2, Giam5-cheng5, Ka-tang-a2, Au7-soaN, Kam-a2-na5, PheN5-ou5, A-li2-kang2, Gu5-taN-oan, Ban7-tan kap Pi-thau5.
Hoe7-iu2 ti7 tak8-ui7 tsun-keng3 i, gau5 thiaN3 i, in-ui7 thoan5 to7-li2 e5 si5-tsun7, i sou2 kong2 put-chi2 u7 eng3-hau7, oe7 kham-tit bian2-le7 an-ui3 cheng3 lang5 e5 sim.  Toa3 ti7 Pi-thau5 e5 si5, Siong7-te3 toa7 siuN2-su3 I e5 lou5-pok8.  Kau3-hoe7 ti7 hia khah ku2 chin7 leng2-tam7; m7-ku2 Gou5 Sian-siN, sui-jian5 hoan5-lo2, i e5 sim bo5 loe2-chi3, hoan2-tng2 siong5-siong5 chhut-goa7 pou3-to7, sun5 hiaN-ti7, ia7 chhiaN2 sio bat e5 lang5 lai5 pai3-tng5 siu7 ka3-si7; tui3 an2-ni bo5 loa7-ku2 kau3-hoe7 lioh8-a2 heng-khi2, khah tsoe7 lang5 oa2-lai5 thiaN to7-li2.  Iu7-koh Gou5 Sian-siN hit-si5 gau5 siat-hoat kian3-ti3 chit keng sin5 e5 le2-pai3-tng5 kap siok-sia3 ti7 Pi-thau5 koe.  Ui7-tioh8 chit tsan5-su7, i chin7 chhut-lat8 si3-koe3 kiu5 khah khoaN3-oah8 e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 pang-tsan7.  Kau3 lok8-seng5 hit-tiap8 chin7 tsoe7 lang5 tsu7-chip8 kap hoe7-iu2 saN-kap hoaN-hi2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.
Gou5 Sian-siN beh lim5-chiong e5 jit8, i-e5 sin-the2 bo5 toa7 ut-tsut kan-khou2, oe7 jin7-tit lang5, soa3 an-ui3 chhin-lang5 e5 sim.  I kan-ta ai3 Ia5-sou kin2-kin2 lai5 chhoa7 i khi3.  Tsu2 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, “ Ho2 ah, lou5-pok8 tiong-hou7 ah, li2 ti7 chio2-e5 i2-keng chin7-tiong, Goa2 beh eng7 tsoe7-tsoe7-e5 thok-tiong7 li2, thang jip8 li2 e5 Tsu2 e5 khoai3-lok8.”
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 370 koan3,1916 ni5 1 geh8)
