Tan5 Teng2-khoan5 sio2-toan7

Tan5 Teng2-khoan5 si7 Sin-hang7 Kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5. I chhut-si3 ti7 1911 ni5 2 goeh8 10 jit8.  Kau3 3 hoe3 e5 si5, in chiah tit-tioh8 to7-li2. Ti7 7 hoe3 e5 si5 lau7-pe7 koe3-sin. A-chek, a-hiaN hou7 i thak8 Kong-hak8-hau7, seng-oah8 khun3-lan5, chong2-si7 i chin siu2-che3, u7-si5 png7-pau tioh8 chah han5-chu5-peng5, iau2-ku2 i hoaN-hi2, u7-si5 bo5 chiah8 koe3 tng3. Kau3 chut-giap8, u7 kap lang5 toa3 tiam3, au7-lai5 khi3 cho3 Ka-lam5 toa7-chun3 Kam3-si7-sou2 e5 siau2-su2, chin lau2-sit8, sin3-iong7, bian2-kiong2, bo5 loa7-ku2 ka7 i seng cho3 kang-chhiu2.  I bo5 thak8 tiong-teng2 hak8-hau7, chong2-si7 tak8 hang7 u7 gian2-kiu3. I e5 peng5-iu2 bat kong2, Siau3-lian5-poe3 teh cho3 khang-khoe3, bo5 su hiah e5 lau2-lian7 e5 lang5. Lun7 i toa3 pun2-chng, kap lang5 chin ho5-peng5, koh chhin-chhiat, u7 toa7, u7 se3; si7 chit8 e5 iu2-ui5 e5 jin5-but8. Ti7 kau3-hoe7 chin oah8-tang7, koh jiat8-sim; bo5-lun7 pou3-to7 a2-si7 kong2 to7-li2, put-chi2 u7 khau2-chai5, ti3-hui7. Lun7 cho3 chheng-lian5, u7 chit khoan2 e5 sin3-giong2, thang kong2 si7 bou5-hoan7-chia2. I ti7 chit8 le2-pai3 kim3-chiah8 chit8-tng3, cho3 chu5-sian7 e5 lou7-eng7. Sit8-chai7 ti7 kau3-hoe7, ti7 sia7-hoe7, si7 chit8 e5 ho2 tek-heng7 e5 lang5.
Kho2-sioh ti7 4 goeh8 chhe, khi2 tng5-a2-peN7, kau3 4 goeh8 30 ho7, chiu7 ti7 Sin-lau5 I-koan2 koe3-sin, hiong2-siu7 27 hoe3, siu7 Chu2 chhoa7 tng2-khi3 Pe7 tau. Chit8-e7 si2, chng-tiong toa7-se3 long2 kong2 bo5-chhai2, che7-che7 lang5 ui7-tioh8 i lau5 bak8-sai2.  Kau3-hoe7 sit-loh8 chit8 e5 iau3-kin2 e5 lang5, sit8-chai7 siong-sim. Siok8-gu2 kong2, “Ho2 chiam khoai3 lih8-phiN7, ho2-lang5 khoai3 koai3-si3.” I si7 chin iu2-hau3 e5 kiaN2, i e5 lau7-bo2 na7 siuN7-tioh8, chiu7 khau3. I e5 sin-si ti7 Tai5-lam5 hoe2-chong3, chong2-si7 i e5 kut-thau5-hu u7 theh8 tng2-lai5 chhu3--nih.
Ti7 5 goeh8 chhe-ji7 e7-pou 4 tiam2, ti7 Sin-hang7 le2-pai3-tng5 ki2-heng5 chong3-sek.  Hoe7-chong3-chia2 u7 Ka-lam5 toa7-chun3 e5 chit-oan5 iok 30 lang5, kap hoe7-iu2 iok 120 lang5 chhut-sek8.  Chiong i e5 kut-thau5-hu tai5 ti7 Sin-hang7 e5 kiong7-tong5 bong7-te7.  Hit chit8-jit8, ui7 i lau5 bak8-sai2 e5 lang5 che7, siau3-liam7 i e5 ho2 tek-heng7. Siong7 Chu2 e5 i3-su3 be7 ui5-keh8--tit, chong2-si7 kiu5 Chu2 si-in an-ui3 i e5 chhin-chok8.
