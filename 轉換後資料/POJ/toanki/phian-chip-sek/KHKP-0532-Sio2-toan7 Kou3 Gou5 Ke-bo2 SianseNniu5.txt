Sio2-toan7  Kou3 Gou5 Ke-bo2 (Bun5-pin) Sian-seN-niu5 Khi3-se3
Sian-seN-niu5 seN3 Chng, mia5 He5 (Toan).Ti7 1862 ni5, 9 goeh8 15 jit8 chhut-si3 ti7 Lo5-tong-chng, kau3 15 hoe3 toe3 Lau7-bo2 lai5 sin3 to7-li2, sou2 siok8 e5 kau3-hoe7 si7 Ka-poa3-soa2.  Ti7 1880 ni5 12 goeh8 5 ho7 tui3 Si Bok8-su siu7 soe2-le2 jip8-kau3, thiaN to7-li2 thau5-boe2 5 ni5 chiu7 jip8 seng3-hoe7. Kau3 20 hoe3 ke3 hou7 Gou5 Bun5-pin Sian-seN, tui3 hit si5 khi2 chin7-lat8 pang-chan7 sian-siN, li7-ek kau3-hoe7, ti3-i3 ka3 chi2-moe7 bat Seng3-keng, hou7 toa7-se3 chun-kui3 pi-chian7 e5 hoe7-iu2 ho2 chhin-kin7, ti7 che7-che7 kau3-hoe7 u7 keng-koe3 khun2-tiok8 kap hoe7-iu2 hoan7-choe7 e5 khou2-sim, chong2-si7 long2 bo5 loe2-chi3, hoan2-tng2 na2 ng3 Chu2 Ki-tok.  Seng-khu khah kan-khou2 ia7 thoa-mia7 ka3 chi2-moe7 oh8 Ji7-bo2, thak8 si, thak8 Seng3-keng, sit8-chai7 bo5 su chit8-e5 Lu2-oh8 e5 chut-giap8-seng.
TaN i u7 keng-koe3 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 Ka-gi7, Bak8-sa, Kam-a2-na5, Ka-poa3-soa2, Ka-lah8-Pou, Kam-a2-na5, Kong-a2-na5, Giam5-cheng5, Hong7-soaN, PheN5-ou5(Ma2-keng). Ou-gu5-lan5 Chiang-hoa3, A-li2-kang2, (Li2-kang2), Kam-a2-na5(chiu7-si7 Kau-phiaN5), Hong7-soaN, Ka-tang-a2 (chiu7-si7 Sai-le5), Gu5-taN-oan, Ban7-tan, Hong7-soaN.  Kau3 sian-seN siu7-chiong e5 si5, u7 koh kap i e5 kiaN2 Chiong-sin Sian-seN koh khi3 kui2-na7 keng kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 Kong-a2-na5, Bak8-sa, Kong-san, Chiang-hoa3, Teng2-soaN-kha, kin-ni5 1929 ni5 4 goeh8 3 jit8 ti7 Teng2-soaN-kha Kau3-hoe7 piat8-se3, hiong2-siu7 68 hoe3.  Hian7-si5 i e5 kiaN2-sai3 kap cha-bou2-kiaN2 ia7 ti7 Li2-kang2 tam-tng bok8-su e5 chit.  I e5 lai7-goa7 sun 13 e5.
Lun7 i cho3 lang5 ti7 sia7-hoe7 u7 ho5-peng5, u7 thun-lun2, jiat8-sim thiaN3 Chu2, khin5-khiam7, phok-sou3.  Ti7 kau3-hoe7 hoe7-iu2 chin thiaN3 i, i ia7 chin thiaN3 in, pai3-tng5 lai7-goa7 chheng-khi3-siuN3. TaN kau3 lau7 e5 si5, i ia7 gia5 ti-thau5 kou3 koe2-chi2 lai5 pang-chan7 i e5 kiaN2, ai3 in an-sim cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, sui bak8-chiu hoe, ia7 koa3 bak8-kiaN3 tau3 ka3 Chu2-jit8-oh8, ti7 ke-lai7 kap toa7-se3 ho2 chham-siong5, kau3 i piat8-se3 e5 si5 i e5 kiaN2, sim-pu7 chin m7-kam, bo5 su Lou7-tek e5 ta-ke.  Sit8-chai7 thang hou7 lang5 khim-keng3.  KhoaN3 i phoa3-peN7 e5 si5 ia7 siong5-siong5 teh ki5-to2, sui-si7 chin thong3-khou2, i lun2-thiaN3 lai5 ki5-to2, bo5 hiaN bo5 chhan, kiaN i e5 si7-se3 hoan5-lo2 ti3-peN7 bo5 sim thang cho3-kang, m7-ai3 lang5 kou3 i ti7 sin-piN, kiaN-liau2 i e5 peN7 khi3 oe3-tioh8 toa7-se3.  Hoan2-tng2 jiat8-jiat8 kiat-lian5 ti7 Chu2 ki5-to2 kau3 lim5-chiong, che hou7 au7-tai7 e5 lang5 thang oh8.
Ti7 4 goeh8 21 ho7, Seng3-jit8 teng2- pou 9 tiam2 Thoan5-to7 Gou5 Tek8-ki5 e5 chhu3-lu2 Gou5-si7 Gek8-ke, ti7 Teng2-soaN-kha piat8-se3 hiong2-siu7 21 hoe3.
