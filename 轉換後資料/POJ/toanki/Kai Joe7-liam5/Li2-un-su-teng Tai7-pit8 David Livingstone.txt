Li2-un-su-teng Tai7-pit8 (David Livingstone)
Kai Joe7-liam5 ek8
Ni5-li2 Li2-un-su-teng kap i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5, khia7-khi2 ti7 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 chit8 e5 tou-chhi7 Kek-liap8-su-ko.  In nng7 lang5 si7 lau2-sit8 keng3-khian5 e5 lang5, ia7 kau3-kek8 jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3.  Se3-kan e5 chiN5-chai5 in khiok si7 bo5, na7-si7 in nng7 lang5 bo5 m7 kut-lat8 choe3-kang laii5uN-chhi7 in e5 kiaN2-ji5.
In u7 chit8 e5 ta-pou kiaN2 ho7-choe3 Tai7-pit8, ti7 1813 ni53 geh8 19 jit8 chhut-si3.  In-ui7 pe7-bu2 san3-chhiah, Tai7-pit8 tui3 soe3-han3 chiu7 khi3 hou7 lang5 chhiaN3.  I chap8 he3 e5 si5 u7 jip8 chit-pou3 e5 kang-tiuN5, ti7 hia tui3 thau3-chak2au e5-hng, khun5-khun5 choe3 kang.  Sim chin him-bou7 ai3 thak8-chheh, m7-ku2 bo5 tu2-ho2 e5 ki-hoe7, Tai7-pit8 kou-put-chiong khiam7 chiN5 lai5 boe2 chheh.  Ti7 kang-tiuN5 chit-pou3 e5 si5, i na2 chit na2 thak8.  Choe3 kang liau2, ti7 e5-hng si5 i koh khi3 ia7-hak8 bian2-kiong2, koh khoaN3 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 chheh.
Kau3 chap8-ji7 he3 e5 si5, i siong5-siong5 ka-ki7 siuN7 chiong-lai5 i tioh8 choe3 sim2-mih8 khoan2 e5 kang.  Tit-tioh8 pe7-bu2 e5 ho2 ka3-si7 kap Seng3-chheh e5 kam2-tong7, iu7-koh thiaN-kiN3 soan-kau3-su kong2, ti7 iN7-pang u7 toa7 khiam3-kheh lang5 khi3 hia thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im kap i-piN7.  Hit-si5 Tai7-pit8 iau2 chio2 he3, m7-ku2 i oe7 hiau2 khng3 chit e5 chi3-goan7, kong2, Pat8-jit8 i na7 toa7-han3, i tek-khak beh choe3 soan-kau3-su.  Chit e5 chi3-goan7 hou7 i e5 sim-koaN na2 iong2-kam2, na2 him-bou7 ai3 thak8 khah koan5 e5 hak8-hau7.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 liau2-au7, i kam-goan7 choe3 khou2 hak8-seng, koh bo5 m7 chin7 i sou2 oe7 lai5 bian2-kiong2.  Na7-si7 i long2 m7-bat tit-tioh8 pat8 lang5 chit8 sian2 chiN5 e5 pang-chan7.
Ti7 1838 ni5, hit-si5 i tu2-tu2 ji7-chap8-gou7 he3, Li2-un-su-teng Tai7-pit8 siu7-tioh8 Lun5-tun Kau3-hoe7 (London Missionary Society) e5 chhui-chian3, i chiu7 khi3 hu3 ine5 
 
chhi3-giam7.  Chong2-si7 i u7 chit8 hang7 khoat-tiam2,chiu7-si7 i e5 khau2-chai5 bo5 kau3 loa7 ho2.  Khia7 ti7 cheng3-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 lai5 soat-kau3, i chin-chiaN3 m7 koan3-si3.  Sou2-i2 u7 chit8 pai2 tu2 beh soat-kau3 e5 si5, i sou2 pi7-pan7 e5 to7-li2 choan5-jian5 boe7 ki3-tit.  I bin7 ang5, put-tek-i2 koh loh8--lai5.
Chong2-si7 i chin chhut-lat8 koh gian2-kiu3, koh chun2-pi7.  Lek8-ke3 saN geh8 jit8 ku2, chiu7 tak8 hang7 long2 u7 kip8-te7.  I e5 tiam2-sou3 ia7 chin koan5.  Iau2-ku2 Soan-to7-pou7 siuN7, khah ho2 m7-thang phai3 i khi3 Tiong-kok,a2-si7 In3-tou7; tek-khak tioh8 phai3 i khi3 A-hui-li7-ka, in-ui7 hit sou2-chai7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 khah bo5 khai-hoa3.
Hit-si5 chhut-mia5 e5 Soan-kau3-su Mou5-hat Phok-su7(Dr. Moffat) tu2-tu2 tui3 A-hui-li7-ka to3-lai5, Li2-un-su-teng thiaN-kiN3 i sou2 kong2--e5, koh-khah koat-sim ai3 khi3 A-hui-li7-ka choe3 Chu2 e5 kang.  Hit-si5 i i2-keng thak8 chut-giap8, koh u7 tit-tioh8 i-hak8-su7 e5 hak8-ui7.
Ti7 1840 ni5 11 geh8 20 jit8, ti7 Lun5-tun chhi7 e5 le2-pai3-tng5 u7 siat-lip8 i choe3 bok8-su.  Chit si5 i tu2-tu2 ji7-chap8-poeh he3.  I e5 sin-the2 put-chi2 iong2-kiaN7.  I e5 keng-thau5 khoah, i e5 bin7-mau7 put-chi2 ho2 siN-choe3. Na7-si7 lun7 kong2-oe7 i chin un-sun5.  I ia7 kau3-kek8 khiam-pi koh chin u7 le2-sou3.
I tai7-seng tah-chun5 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka e5 lam5-peng5, au7-lai5 che7 gu5-chhia chiuN7 pak, iok u7 700 Eng li2, kau3 chit8 sou2-chai7 ho7 choe3 Be5-khu-a-lan5 (Bechuanaland). Mou5-hat Phok-su7, saN-chap8 ni5 cheng5 u7 khi3 hia siat-kau3.  Li2-un-su-teng e5 bok8-tek, si7 ai3 khi3 koh-khah Pak-peng5, koh-khah koan5 e5 sou2-chai7, ng3-bang7 ti7 hia thang kian3-siat sin e5 kau3-hoe7.  In-ui7 ti7 hia, khi3-hau7 u7 khah ho2.  Chhou-chhou kau3-ui7 e5 si5, i tiam3 ti7 Be5-khu-a-lan5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan lak8 geh8 jit8 ku2.  Ti7 hia soa3 oh8 in e5 khiuN-khau2.  Au7-lai5 i koh chiuN7 pak, khi3 kau3 Ma5-bo5-sa (Mabotsa) e5 toe7-hng, ng3-bang7 ti7 hia thang thoan5-po3 Chu2 po2-poe3 e5 to7-li2.
Ti7 hit sou2-chai7, sai chin-chiaN3 choe7, koh chin hiong5-ok.  Put-si5 chhut-lai5 ka7-si2 lang5 e5 gu5, iuN5.  Lang5 ia7 siong5-siong5 hou7 in ka7-si2.  SoaN-nih e5 lok8, kiuN-a2, soaN-ti ia7 si7 
 
put-si5 hou7 in ka7--khi3.  Hiah e5 sai chin u7 lat8 koh toa7 chiah, sui-jian5 toa7 chiah e5 chhiah-gu5 ia7 thoa-tit chau2.  Bo5 ki5-koai3 hit toe7-hng e5 ou-bin7 lang5 chin kiaN sai, ti3-kau3 m7-kaN2 chhut-khi3 kap in ti2-tek8.  Sou2 u7 e5 gu5 kan-ta hou7 sai tit8-tit8 ka7 khi3 chiah8.  Iu7-koh in chin gau5 khng3 be5-sin3, tui3 an2-ni in it-hoat bo5 ai3 khi3 phah sai.  Siat-su2 na7 kaN2 khi3, ia7 si7 bo5 sim2-mih8 tu2-ho2 e5 khi3-hai5, put-ko3 si7 kiah8 pio-a2 na7-tiaN7, chong2-si7 in e5 pio-a2 put-chi2 li7-hai7, in-ui7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 chin gau5 sia7.  In siong5-siong5 khi3 phah lah8,na7 tu2-tioh8 ok e5 ia2-siu3, in chiong pio-a2 ka7 i sia7.
TaN Li2-un-su-teng khoaN3-kiN3 hit toe7-hng e5 gu5, iuN5 sit-loh8 chin-chiaN3 choe7, in-ui7 siong5-siong5 hou7 sai ka7 khi3.  Iu7-koh in-ui7 hiah e5 lang5 tioh8-kiaN m7-kaN2 khi3 ti2-khong3 hiah e5 sai, i boe7 bian2-tit ho2-taN2 chhut-thau5,chio-hou hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 khi3 phah sai.  In tai7-ke jip8 chhiu7-na5 lai7, Li2-un-su-teng pun2-sin kiah8 siang-kong2 chheng3; ki5-u5 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 kiah8 pio-a2, koh u7 chit8 tin7 lang5 tui3 chiu-ui5, tit8-tit8 pau-lai5, ia7 toa7 siaN hoah.  Ng3-bang7 na7 u7 sai bih ti7 am7-am7 e5 koaN-be2 lai7, thiaN-kiN3 lang5 e5 siaN, i ka-ki7 oe7 thiau3-chhut--lai5.  Ko2-jian5 chiau3 in sou2 phah-sng3.
Koh tiap-a2-ku2 chit8 chiah toa7 chiah sai tui3 koaN-be2 lai7 chau2 chhut--lai5.  I e5 thau5-khak kiah8 koan5-koan5 si3-koe3 khoaN3.  I e5 be2-liu phoah8-lai5 phoah8-khi3, chin-chiaN3 u7 chit8 e5 ui-hong e5 khoan2-sit.  Hit-si5 i li7 Li2-un-su-teng iok u7 si3-chap8 kha-pou7.  Iau2-be7 thiau3 e5 si5, Li2-un-su-teng tai7-seng khui chheng3.  Chheng3-chi2 phah-tioh8 sai e5 keng-thau5, siong chin tang7.  Chong2-si7 sai bo5 hian7 si2.  Tu2-tng7 ti7 i sin-piN u7 chit8 te3 toa7 te3 chioh8,hit chiah sai chiu7 tui3 chioh8-thau5 eng7 chhui3 ka7 i tit8-tit8 khoe3.  Kiah8-thau5 khoaN3-kiN3 Li2-un-su-teng, hit-si5 i kiah8 chheng3 tu2-tu2 beh koh phah, na7-si7 sai thiau3 oa2--lai5, tit8-tit8 ka7 i ka7; na2 chhin-chhiuN7 niau ka7 niau2-chhu2 chit8-iuN7.  Li2-un-su-teng hou7 i ka7-teh tit8-tit8 hiu3, kau3 thong seng-khu long2 ba5-pi3, m7-chai thiaN3.  Chhin-chhiuN7 niau-chhu2 hou7 niau ka7, ia7 boe7 hiau2-tit chau2.  Li2-un-su-teng e5 bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 sai,na7-si7 i e5 seng-khu boe7-oe7 tin2-tang7.  I e5 to3-chhiu2 e5 kut long2 ka7 kau3 chhui3-chhui3.  Sai koh khui chhui3 beh koh ka7 e5 si5, hut-jian5 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 lang5 chhiu2 kiah8 pio-a2, sai chiu7 pang3-sak Li2-un-su-teng, thiau3-oa2 beh ka7 hit e5 lang5.  Chit e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng sou2 sin3-iong7 e5 pou7-ha7.  I khoaN3-kiN3 chu2-lang5 hou7 sai ka7, kin2 chau2-oa2 beh khi3 kiu3 i; na7-si7 hit chiah tioh8 tang7-siong e5 sai, thiau3 oa2 lai5 khoe3 i e5 kha-kut, hiam2-hiam2 soa3 ka7 i ka7-si2.
 
Chong2-si7 sai e5 lat8 i2-keng beh chin7, in-ui7 i i2-keng siu7 tang7-siong.  Koh tiap-a2-ku2, i ka-ki7 to2-loh8--khi3,khui3 chiu7 tng7.
Na7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 to3-chhiu2, kau3 chit8-si3-lang5 long2 boe7 koh oan5-choan5; in-ui7 kut i2-keng hou7 hit chiah sai ka7 kau3 chhui3-chhui3.  I e5 to3-chhiu2 tui3 chit si5 liau2-au7, long2 boe7 koh kiah8 koan5.
Li2-un-su-teng khia7-khi2 ti7 Ma5-bo5-sa kau3 1884 ni5.  Siang7 hit-ni5, i chhoa7 Mou5-hat Phok-su7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 choe3 i e5 bok8-su-niu5.  In ng3-bang7 beh ku2-ku2 tiam3 tihia7 lai5 thoan5-po3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 hok-im, na7-si7 u7 pat8 e5 tang5 tioh8-boa5 e5 lou5-pok8 kap in bo5 tang5-i3.  Li2-un-su-teng ka-ki7 hoaN-hi2 jiong7-pou7.  Heng5-li2 khoan2 ho2,i kap bok8-su-niu5, bo5 kiaN kan-khou2, hui-hui chiu7 koh khi2-sin khi3 koh-khah pak-peng5 e5 sou2-chai7; ng3-bang7 ti7 hia thang koh-chai3 kian3-ti3 chit8 e5 kau3-hoe7.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, tu2-tioh8 chit8 e5 thau5-bak8 mia5 Se-ke-le2 (Sechele)put-chi2 chhin-chhiat.  In-ui7 khiam3-kheh sou2-chai7, Li2-un-su-teng tui3 i boe2 chit8 te3 toe7, thang choe3 khi2 chhu3 e5 lou7-eng7.  Chit e5 thau5-bak8 put-chi2 u7 ti3-sek, ia7 chin kho2-chhu2.  Chai-iaN2 hak8-bun7 oe7 choe3 lang5 e5 li7-ek, i ia7 ho2-taN2 chiu7-kun7 Li2-un-su-teng, kiu5 i tek-khak tioh8 ka3-si7 i thak8 tam7-poh8 chheh.  Tui3 i e5 bian2-kiong2, i ia7 oh8 kau3 oe7-hiau2 sia2-ji7.  Au7-lai5 soa3 sin3 to7-li2.  I e5 phin2-heng7 ia7 na2 toan-chiaN3.  Lun7 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, i put-chi2 jiat8-sim.  To7-li2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 bat.  SaN ni5 au7, i u7 tui3 Li2-un-su-teng nia2 soe2-le2.
Kho2-sioh ti7 chit si5-chun7 thong hit toe7-hng, long2 tu2-tioh8 toa7 kho2-oaN7.  Khoe-chui2 long2 ta-khi3.  Koh ku2-ku2 long2 bo5 hou7.  Sit8-chai7 chin-chiaN3 si7 toa7 khong3-han7.  Chhai3-soe long2 phak8 si2, ngou2-kok bo5 thang siu-seng5.  Ki-hng e5 jit8 liam5-piN kau3.  Li2-un-su-teng kap thong chng e5 lang5, koaN2-kin2 khoan2 heng5-li2 khi3 khah pak-peng5 u7 chui2 e5 sou2-chai7.  TaN i koat-i3 beh chhe7 khah u7 chui2, khah tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7, lai5 choe3 i sin e5 thoan5-to7-toe7.  Ng3-bang7 chiong-lai5 ti7 hia thang ku2-tng5 khia7-khi2.  Koh thang thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im ti7 hit toe7-hng.  Koh bian2 ui7-tioh8 kho2-oaN7 kap ki-hng e5 in-toaN lai5 poaN-soa2.  I ia7 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, pak-peng5 si7 chin ho2 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 hia u7 koan5-soaN, u7 toa7 chhiu7-na5, u7 toa7 e5 ou5, ia7 u7 toa7 tiau5 ho5.  Hit toe7-hng e5 chhut-san2 ia7 chin choe7.  ThiaN-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 ho2 siau-sit, Li2-un-su-teng ju2 ai3 chiuN7 pak.  Sou2-i2 ti7 1849 ni5 e5 lak8 geh8 tiong, i kap kui2-na7 e5 tang5-phoaN7, 
 
tiong-kan u7 pun2-kok e5 ho2 peng5-iu2, i2-kip8 saN, si3 chap8 e5 ou-bin7 lang5, saN-kap choe3-tin7 beh khi3 pak-si3.  Ti7 lou7-tiong e5 si5, in hut-jian5-kan hoat-kian3 tioh8 chit8 e5 chiaN2-chui2 e5 ou5.  Chit e5 ou5 e5 mia5 chiu7-si7 Na5-bi2 (Lake Ngami).  Pun2-jian5 si7 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, taN ko2-jian5 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3.  Chin-chiaN3 u7 iaN2, ti7 hit toe7-hng u7 chhiu7-na5, u7 choe7-choe7 ou5, ia7 u7 toa7 tiau5 ho5.  Hia e5 jin5-sou3 ia7 si7 chin-choe7.
Chhe7-tioh8 chit e5 khah an-un2, khah ho2 e5 sou2-chai7, Li2-un-su-teng kau3-kek8 hoaN-hi2.  I sui5-si5 koh to3-tng2 khi3 chhoa7 i e5 bou2-kiaN2 kap Se-ke-le2 thau5-bak8, i2-kip8 ji7-chap8-goa7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 lai5 kau3 chit e5 sou2-chai7.  Hit si5 si7 4 geh8, (1850 ni5).  Kho2-sioh koh bo5 loa7 ku2 in tioh8 koh chhian-soa2.  Si7 in-ui7 ti7 hit toe7-hng u7 choe7-choe7 tui3-tek8 siong5-siong5 ai3 chou2-chi2 Li2-un-su-teng e5 kang.  Iu7-koh i e5 te7-si3 kiaN2 ia7 si2 ti7 hia.  Bok8-su-niu5 ia7 siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 phoa3-piN7.  Tui3 an2-ni put-tek-i2 koh-chai3 khi3 khah pak-peng5 e5 sou2-chai7.
Khi2-sin liau2, in tit8-tit8 kiaN5 kau3 Ma2-ko-lo5 (Mako-lolo).  Koh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 Sam-pi2-se ho5 (Zambesi)  Chit tiau5 ho5 hui-siong5 toa7, li7 hai2 ia7 chin hng7.  Ti7 Li2-un-su-teng sou2 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7 hit tiau5 ho5 e5 khoah iok u7 500 pou7.  Pun2-jian5 bo5 lang5 chai ti7 A-hui-li7-ka u7 chit tiau5 Sam-pi2-se ho5.  Hit e5 toa7 kap tng5 sit8-chai7 kap se3-kai3 chhut-mia5 e5 toa7 ho5 oe7 saN-pi2--tit.  Bo5 ki5-koai3 Li2-un-su-teng hit jit8 kau3-kek8 hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 chit tiau5 ho5 si7 i te7-it tai7-seng hoat-kian3--tioh8.
M7-ku2 u7 chit8 hang7 hou7 i e5 sim chin-chiaN3 hoan5-lo2,hou7 i put-si5 teh khoa3-lu7.  Chiu7-si7 ti7 hit sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 khoan2 chin phaiN2 e5 hong-siok8.  Chiu7-si7 lang5 liah8 lang5 khi3 choe3 lou5-le7.  Tui3-tek8 chit8-e7 kau3, kui chng e5 lang5 na7 bo5 chau2, chiu7 hou7 in liah8-khi3 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 boe7 choe3 lou5-chai5.  Lau7--e5, bo5-lunl7am, lu2, long2 hou7 in thai5.  Siau3-lian5--e5, eng7 thih-lian7 lian7, au7-lai5 kui-tin7 khan khi3 hai2-khau2 boe7.  Phoa3-piN7 boe7 kiaN5--e5, pang3 ti7 lou7-nih hou7 in si2.  Lau7-bu2 pho7 gin2-na2, na7-si7 sian7 kiaN5 boe7 khi3, in chiu7 chiong gin2-a2 liah8 khi3 hiat-sak.  Lau7-bu2 e5 khou2-sim,thi5-khau3, kap kiu5-cheng5 ia7 si7 bo5 chhai2-kang.  Na7 kau3 hai2-kiN5 e5 sou2-chai7, in chiong sou2 liah8 e5 lang5 long2 khi3 boe7.  Chit8 lang5 boe7 chit8 lou7; ka-chok8 long2 si3-soaN3.  Pe7-bu2 khoaN3-kiN3 kiaN2-ji5 hou7 lang5 boe7; kiaN2-ji5 khoaN3-kiN3 pe7-bu2 ia7 hou7 lang5 boe7.  Tai7-ke kau3 si2 bo5 thang koh-chai3 saN-kiN3.  Chit khoan2 e5 hong-siok8 sit8-chai7 chan5-jim2 kau3-kek8.   M7-na7 an2-ni, na7-si7 boe2-khi3 liau2, in e5 chu2-lang5 na7 beh phah chiu7 phah, na7 beh kong3 chiu7 kong3.  Sit8-chai7 in e5 kan-khou2 si7 oh-tit kong2.
KhoaN3-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 chan5-jim2, Li2-un-su-teng tui3 hit si5 chiu7 koat-sim ai3 eng7 hoat-tou7 lai5 thau2-pang3 chiah e5 tu2-tioh8 chhiN-chham2 e5 lang5.
I koat-i3 ai3 hoe3-bo5 chit khoan2 e5 phaiN2 hong-siok8.  Chong2-si7 i chai che su7 m7-si7 hiah khoai3 soah.  Kiam2-chhai2 tioh8 koh choe7-choe7 ni5 chiah oe7 hoe3-bo5 chit khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3.
Chhut-goa7 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka e5 chhim-soaN sit8-chai7 chin-chiaN3 bo5 li7-pian7.  Li2-un-su-teng taN siuN7 khah ho2 tioh8 hou7 i e5 bou2-kiaN2 tai7-seng to3-khi3 pun2-kok.  Chong2-si7 lou7-hui3 bo5 kau3-giah8, i e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 Ou5-se(Oseveel) thoe3 i siat-hoat.   Ou5-se si7 i pun2-kok e5 lang5.  I cho3 lang5 chin un-sun5.  U7 kui2-na7 pai2 i kap Li2-un-su-teng saN-kap chhut-goa7, saN-kap tham3-hiam2.  I khoaN3 Li2-un-su-teng si7 chit8 e5 ho2 koh chin7-tiong e5 lang5.  Li2-un-su-teng pun2-sin ia7 chin thiaN3 i.  TaN in tai7-ke saN-kap choe3-tin7 loh8 lam5-peng5.  Li2-un-su-teng beh tui3 hia siat-hoat hou7 i e5 ka-chok8 tah chun5 kau3 Eng-kok.  Kap ka-chok8 saN li7-piat8 liau2-au7, ti7 6 geh8 8 jit8 (1852 ni5), Li2-un-su-teng pun2-sin koh chiuN7 pak.  Si3-ni5 ku2 e5 tiong-kan tu2-tioh8 chiong2-chiong2 e5 chhiN-chham2, poaN5-soaN ke3 nia2, liau5-khoe ke3 ho5, siong5-siong5 iau-go7, i ia7 bo5 siuN7 sian7, put-ko3 chin7 i sou2 oe7 beh chhe7 chit8 ui7 tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 thang lai5 kian3-siat chit8 e5 thoan5-to7-kai3.  Iu7-koh ai3 pang-chan7 hiah e5 siu7-liah8 e5 lou5-le7, ng3-bang7 oe7-tit-thang hou7 in tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.
I siong5-siong5 kong2, Hok-im tioh8 thoan5 kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7, tioh8 thoan5 kau3 tak8 chok8 oe7 bat Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 mia5.  Sou2-i2 m7-thang put-si5 tiam3 ti7 chit sou2-chai7 lai5 choe3 Chu2 e5 kang.  Khah iau3-kin2 tioh8 po3-iong5 Ki-tok e5 mia5 ti7 choe7-choe7 lang5 e5 tiong-kan.

i chiu7 chin7-lat8 phau2-chau2 khi3 kau3 gui5-hiam2, bo5 lang5 bat kau3 e5 sou2-chai7.  Lou7-tiong tu2-tioh8 tui3-tek8 ai3 ham7-hai7 i, i ia7 si7 bo5 pang3-soah i sou2 ke3-ek8 e5 kang.
Hit si5 u7 Ho5-lan5 lang5 e5 hou7-e3 ti7 hit toe7-hng teh khai-khun2. In-ui7 chiah e5 Ho5-lan5 lang5 sou2 ai3 e5, chiu7-si7 oe7 hou7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 lai5 choe3 in e5 lou5-chai5.  In long2 si7 siuN7 ka-ki7 e5 li7-ek. Ai3 chhe7 lou7 kau3 Sai-peng5
Li2-un-su-teng tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 keng2-gu7, sim-koaN kau3-kek8 kan-khou2.  TaN kou-put-chiong tioh8 koh chhian-soa2 khi3 kau3 khah hng7 e5 sou2-chai7.  Tai7-seng chun2-pi7 lou7-tiong sou2 tioh8 khiam3-eng7 e5 kan-niu5,i2-kip8 pat8-hang7, chhin-chhiuN7 te5, thng5, piaN2, ioh8, chhiuN7-ge5 thang choe3 tau2-oaN7 e5 lou7-eng7, hoan3-teng, i-chiuN5, phe7-phou teng2, i chiu7 chhoa7 ji7-chap8-chhit e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 khi3.  Sou2 toa3 e5 mih8-kiaN7 lim2-lim2-a2 kau3-giah8 thang hou7 hiah toa7-tin7 e5 lang5 eng7.
In ti7 lou7-tiong lak8-geh8 jit8 ku2.  Sou2 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7,tu5-khi3 ou-bin7 lang5 i2-goa7, bo5 lang5 bat kau3.  Ti7 11 geh8 11 jit8 (1853 ni5), in an-jian5 kau3 Sam-pi2-se ho5-kiN.
Chit sou2-chai7 e5 khi3-hau7 hui-siong5 joah8, iu7-koh chin sip--lun7, sui-jian5 khang kiaN5, lang5 ia7 liam5-piN sian7.  Ho5-hong3 in bo5 tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 thang an-hioh.  In e5 jit8-sit8 ia7-si7 bo5 kau3.  Tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 keng2-gu7, Li2-un-su-teng e5 sim-sin5 iau2 chin oah8-tang7.  I koh si7 houN3-hak8 e5 lang5, sou2-i2 bo5 m7 siong5-siong5 gian2-kiu3 hit toe7-hng sou2 u7 e5 sit8-but8 kap tong7-but8.
 
Siat-su2 lou7-tiong na7 u7 ki-hoe7, i bo5 m7 siong5-siong5 thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 mia5 kap I e5 hok-im hou7 hiah e5 be5-sin3 e5 lang5 thiaN.  In-ui7 i si7 i-seng, i ia7 siong5-siong5 ka7 lang5 i-piN7.
Loh8-hou7 e5 si5 i2-keng kau3.  Ti7 hia mui2-jit8 long2 si7 loh8 toa7 hou7, beh chhut-mng5 chin-chiaN3 put-pian7.  Tak8 si3-koe3 long2 chin sip.  Lang5 chin khoai3 phoa3-piN7, Li2-un-su-teng choe7-choe7 pai2 tu2-tioh8 jiat8-cheng3,na7-si7 in sou2 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7, thou2-toe7 chin pui5, jin5-sou3 ia7 chin choe7.  Chong2-si7 hit sou2-chai7 e5 lang5 chin ia2-ban5.  In siong5-siong5 ai3 thai5 lang5.  U7-si5 soa3 chiah8 lang5-bah.  Chit khoan2 e5 lang5, bin7 ou chhin-chhiuN7 tiaN2-toe2.  In e5 thau5-mng5 khiu5-khiu5; in e5 chhui3-tun5 ou-ou, koh hui-siong5 kau7.  U7-e5 chin lo3, koh chhou-iong2.  Ia7 u7 chit8 khoan2 oe2-jin5 toa3 ti7 chhiu7-na5 lai7.  Ta-pou--e5 si3-chhioh goa7 koan5 na7-tiaN7; cha-bou2--e5 koh-khah soe3-han3.  In bo5 cheng3-choh, kan-ta oa2-kho3 phah-lah8 kap chiah8 chhiu7-na5 lai7 sou2 chhut e5 mih8 lai5 seng-oah8.  Li2-un-su-teng tui3 hit khoan2 e5 toe7-hng keng-ke3, siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 gui5-hiam2, in-ui7 chhiN-hoan chin-kiap.
Che7 sio2-chun5-a2 ti7 hit tiau5 toa7 ho5 i2-keng nng7-geh8 jit8 ku2.  In ti7 lou7-tiong, chiah sio2-khoa2 hioh-khun3, au7-lai5 tui3 hia kiaN5-lou7 beh khi3 Lo5-an3-ta3 (Loanda),ti7 sai-peng5 oa2 hai2 e5 sou2-chai7, Li2-un-su-teng in-ui7 bo5 be2 thang khia5, chiu7 khia5 gu5, chu7-jian5 pi2 be2 khah ban7 kiaN5.  Chit khoan2 e5 lu2-heng5 si7 chin bo5 song2-khoai3 koh chin ban7; put-ko3 si7 jit8-jit8 un5-un5-a2 teh chin3.  Keng-ke3 kui2-na7 geh8 jit8 ku2, in iau2 teh nng3 toa7 chhiu7-na5.  Chhiu7-na5 lai7 e5 chhiu7 kap tin5-a2 chin-chiaN3 chat8.  Na7 bo5 eng7 pou2-thau5 lai5 chho3, lang5 kiaN5 bo5 lou7.  Iu7-koh u7 chit8 hang7 toa7-toa7 chou2-chi2 in e5 chin3-pou7,  Toa7-hou7 chit8-e7 loh8, tak8 si3-koe3 tam5 lok-lok.
In sou2 keng-ke3 e5 sou2-chai7 oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 lam3-chhan5.  Sou2 u7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 i2-kip8 saN-khou3 put-si5 siN-kou.  E7-hng si5 ia7 bo5 chit8 ui7 ta e5 ui7 thang khun3.  In kou-put-chiong tioh8 khun3 ti7 tam5-tam5 e5 thou5-kha.  In ti7 lou7-tiong sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 m7-na7 chinok, koh chin gau5 giau5-gi5 lang5.  Na7 bo5 choe7-choe7 mih8-kiaN7 sang3 in, lou7 in toan3-toan3 m7 chioh lang5 ke3.  Siat-su2 na7 tioh8 ka7 in boe2 chiah8 mih8, khah choe7 mih8-kiaN7 kap in tau2-oaN7, in ia7 boe7 boan2-chiok.  In e5 tham-sim chin-chiaN3 tang7.  In koh si7 kip-seng3 e5 lang5, chin khoai3 piN3-bin7; chin khoai3 kap lang5 oan-ke.  Sou2-i2 tui3 hit toe7-hng keng-ke3, Li2-un-su-teng kap te3 i e5 lang5 long2 si7 khia7-khi2 ti7 gui5-hiam2 tiong.
 
In bat kau3 ti7 chit8 e5 hiuN-sia7, tu2-tng7 hit sia7 e5 thau5-bak8 chin-chiaN3 tham-sim.  I kong2, Na7 bo5 chiong chit8 e5 lang5 hou7 i choe3 lou5-chai5, a2-si7 chiong chhiah-gu5 sang3 i, i tek-khak m7-kheng2 Li2-un-su-teng tui3 i e5 toe7-kai3 keng-ke3.  M7-ku2 Li2-un-su-teng boe7-tit-thang chiong hiah choe7 mih8 hou7 i.  Hit e5 thau5-bak8 sui5-si5 piN3-bin7.  I kio3 i e5 pou7-ha7 tioh8 ui5 Li2-un-su-teng.  KhoaN3-kiN3 m7-si7 se3, Ma2-ko-lo5 kin2 oa2 lai5 po2-hou7 i.  Kai-chai3 tai7-ke bo5 chhut-chhiu2.
Kho2-sioh Li2-un-su-teng ti7 lu2-heng5 tiong, siong5-siong5 phoa3-piN7, siong5-siong5 jiat8.  I e5 sin-the2 na2 ku2 na2 lam2, lang5 ia7 tit8-tit8 na2 san2.  Koh u7 choe3-li7 e5 in-toaN, i it-hoat kan-khou2.  Kau3 lou7-be2 i soa3 long2 boe7 kiaN5, put-ko3 kan-ta to2 ti7 chhau2-liau5-a2 lai7.  Jit8-jit8 ti7 hia teh kan-khou2, ia7 bo5 i-seng thang lai5 khoaN3 i.  Na7-si7 u7 thang tiam7-tiam7 hioh-khun3 ia7-si7 ho2.  M7-ku2 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 it-hoat put-ti-chiok.  In e5 tham sim na2 hoat-hian7.  In kan-ta kiong5-kiong5 beh tho2-mih8.  Hou7 in liau2, in ia7-si7 beh koh tho2.  Chin-chiaN3 bo5 chit8 e5 kui-ku2.  Kau3 lou7-be2, siuN7 bo5 hoat-tou7, Li2-un-su-teng chiah chiong i sou2 khia5 hit chiah gu5 khan-khi3 sang3 in.  Hit-si5 in kui-tin7 chiah hoaN-hi2 the3--khi3.
Koh keng-ke3 kui2-na7 jit8 ku2, in iu5-goan5 koh tu2-tioh8 pat8 chong-chok8 e5 lang5.  Chiah e5 ia7-si7 chin ai3 tho2 mih8, na7 bo5 hou7 in, in tek-khak m7-chun2 lang5 tui3 in hia keng-ke3.  Mih8-kiaN7 i2-keng hou7 in theh8 chin-choe7, kau3-be2 in soa3 kiah8 to, chheng3, lai5 chah8 lou7; in-ui7 in iau2 beh koh tho2 mih8.  TaN beh chaiN2-iuN7?
Kau3 siong7 lou7-be2, Li2-un-su-teng kou-put-chiong chiong i sou2 chhun e5 mih8, hek8-si7 ka-ki7 e5 lai7-saN, kui koaN7 chu-a2, kap te3 i e5 lang5 sou2 u7 e5 mih8, it-chin7 theh8 hou7 in.  Na7 si7 in iau2-be7 boan2-chiok.  In put-ko3 si7 beh kiong5 chhiuN2 chiu7-si7.  SiuN7 kau3 bo5 lou7, Li2-un-su-teng chiah chiong chit8 chiah gu5, kap chhiuN7-ge5 theh8 hou7 in.
Tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 keng2-gu7, boe7 bian2-tit hou7 in tai7-ke e5 sim-koaN kau3-kek8 sit-chi3.  Tai7-ke chai-iaN2 tui3 hit toe7-hng keng-ke3, sit8-chai7 si7 chin gui5-hiam2.  Hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 tui3 an2-ni tiu5-tu5 m7 kap Li2-un-su-teng choe3-tin7 khi3.  Sui-jian5 khou2-khng3 in, in ia7-si7 bo5 beh thiaN.  Tu2-tioh8 an2-ni, Li2-un-su-teng kui7-loh8 ki5-to2, chhiat-i3 kiu5 Siong7-te3 m7-thang pang3-sak in.  I chhut-siaN kiu5 kong2, “Chi3-toa7 e5 Siong7-te3 ah, kiu5 Li2 pang-chan7 goan2, kiu5 Li2 pang-chan7 goan2.  Kiu5 Li2 kiu3 goan2 thoat-li7 tui3-tek8 e5 chhiu2.”
Koh tiap-a2-ku2, hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5, chit8-e5 chit8-e5 lai5 Li2-un-su-teng e5 bin7-cheng5 jin7 in e5 m7-tioh8,kong2, TaN in koat-i3 bo5 beh pang3-sak in e5 chu2-lang5.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 liau2-au7, in chin7-tiong chiau3 sou2 iok--e5,it-chin7 lai5 kiaN5.
In ti7 lou7-tiong koh-chai3 tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 kan-khou2.  Chong2-si7 kiaN5 kau3 Pho5-to5-ga5 e5 leng2-thou2 e5 si5, in tu2-tioh8 chit8 e5 Pho5-to5-ga5 lang5, soa3 hou7 i ho2 khoan2-thai7.  Li2-un-su-teng tiam3 ti7 i e5 chhu3 liau5-iong2 chit8 le2-pai3 ku2.  I e5 sin-the2 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 khah iong2-kiaN7.  In tui3 chia koh khi2-sin beh khi3 Lo5-an-ta3 (Loanda).  I e5 lou7-cham7 iok u7 300 Eng-li2.  Tui3 chit-tiap8 khi2, koh kiaN5 lou7, chhe hong, phak8 jit8, iau-go7.  Li2-un-su-teng e5 sin-the2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 sit-lat8.  Koh bo5 loa7-ku2, i soa3 long2 boe7 kiaN5.  Au7-lai5 chiong gu5 hou7 i khia5, i ia7 khia5 boe7 tiau5.  Sin-the2 loan2-jiok8 kui2-na7 pai2 kiong7 beh hun7--khi3.  Sin-the2 hiah-nih lam2, i boe7-kou3-tit sia2 jit8-ki3,lian5 jit8-chi2 i ia7 boe7 ki3-tit.
Ho2 kai-chai3, koh bo5 kui2-jit8, in tui3 hng7-hng7 chiu7 khoaN3-kiN3 hai2.  Ti7 5 geh8 31 jit8 (1854 ni5), in tai7-kean-jian5 kau3 ti7 Lo5-an-ta3 e5 siong-kang2.  Ti7 hia Li2-un-su-teng tit-tioh8 chit8 e5 Eng-kok lang5 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7.  I chin sio2-sim teh ka7 i chiau3-kou3.
Tui3 Sai-peng5 kau3 Tang-peng5
Li2-un-su-teng tiam3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka i2-keng chap8-saN ni5 ku2.  Sou2-i2 i siuN7 ai3 chhoa7 hiah e5 lang5 koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 in e5 pun2 sou2-chai7.  Sun7 chit e5 ki-hoe7 soa3 thang chhe7 chit tiau5 Sam-pi2-se ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  In-ui7 ti7 hit si5-tai7 iau2 bo5 lang5 chai chit tiau5 toa7 ho5e chui2 tui3 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 lai5.  Chiau3 lang5 sou2 chai,put-ko3 si7 ho5 e5 chhut-khau2 na7-tiaN7.  Sou2-i2 na7 oe7-tit-thang chhe7 chit tiau5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5, sit8-chai7 m7-si7 sio2-khoa2 su7.
 
Kho2-sioh i e5 sin-the2 iau2 loan2-jiok8.  I sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 ia7 chin chio2.  I beh chin3-cheng5 e5 lou7-cham7 iu7 kau3-kek8 gui5-hiam2.  M7-na7 lou7 chin hng7,koh si7 chhiN-sou e5 sou2-chai7.  Sui-bong2 si7 an2-ni, i iau2 chin koat-i3 beh koh chin3-cheng5.  I sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 kan-khou2 kong2 boe7 liau2.  Iau-go7, piN7-thiaN3 tak8 jit8u.  In lian5-lian5 keng-ke3 chit8 ni5 koh poeh geh8 jit8 chiah kau3 hai2 e5 sou2-chai7.  In chhut-hoat hit jit8 chiu7-si7 9 geh8 20 jit8 (1854 ni5).  Hit jit8 i kap hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 saN-kap choe3-tin7 khi2-sin.  Jit8-jit8 keng-ke3 soaN-kan, i2-kip8 pui5-toe7 e5 sou2-chai7; in long2 bo5 phah-sng3 koh bo5 loa7 ku2 oe7 tu2-tioh8 toa7-chui2 im e5 su7.  Ti3-kau3 in long2 bo5 tu2 ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 thang an-hioh.  Kou-put-chiong eng7 tam5 thou5 lai5 thiap8 kui tui, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 bong7 e5 khoan2-sit.  In chiong chit khoan2 e5 thou5-tui lai5 choe3 bin5-chhng5.
Li2-un-su-teng chin gau5 khoan2-thai7 kap i choe3-tin7 hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5; put-si5 long2 si7 khoa3-lu7 in e5 khiam3-kheh.  TaN in tai7-ke lian5-lian5 kiaN5 oa2 nng7 ni5 ku2 e5 lou7-cham7.  Tai7-ke saN-kap siu7-khou2.  Na7-si7 sim-koaN siuN7 bian2 koh loa7 ku2, tek-khak u7 thang to3-tng2 kap in e5 bou2-kiaN2, peng5-iu2 saN-kiN3, in tui3 an2-ni it-hoat ju2 gau5 kiaN5.  Ng3-bang7 u7 chit8 jit8, in tai7-ke oe7-tit an-jian5 koh kau3 chhu3.  Hit si5 kap saN li7-khui e5 koh-chai3 saN-kiN3, in tai7-ke e5 hoaN-hi2 m7-chai beh kau3 chaiN2-iuN7.
Chit khoan2 e5 toa7 tham3-hiam2, chit khoan2 e5 lu2-heng5, ti7 se3-kai3 tham3-hiam2 e5 lek8-su2 tiong, sit8-chai7 iau2-be7 u7.  TaN in tai7-ke koh-chai3 to3-tng2-lai5, boe7 bian2-tit in teh hoaN-hi2. Sui-bong2 in an2-ni teh phah-sng3, iau2-ku2 in u7 chiau3 in e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 teh po2-hou7 Li2-un-su-teng sou2 kau-tai3 in e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 kap in e5 thau5-bak8 Se-ke-le2-tu5 (Sekeletu) chin-chiaN3 chin7-tiong.  Li2-un-su-teng sou2 kia3 in e5 mih8-kiaN7 long2 bo5 chit8 hang7 sit-loh8.  Sou2-i2 kau3 ui7 e5 si5, i u7 mih8-kiaN7 pian7-pian7 thang eng7.  Koh-chai3 tui3 Mou5-hat Bok8-su e5 ho2-i3, kia3 mih8 choe7-choe7 sang3 i, taN Li2-un-su-teng e5 khiam3-kheh, thang kong2 i2-keng chin kau3-giah8.
TaN ti7 chit sou2-chai7, thou2-mia5 kio3-choe3 Lin5-ian3-ti5 (Linyanti), in sun7 ki-hoe7 soa3 
 
tiam3 hia kui2-na7 geh8-jit8 ku2.  Sui-bong2 kong2 si7 ti7 hia beh hioh-khun3,ki5-sit8 Li2-un-su-teng bo5 chit8 si5 eng5.  Jit8-jit8 ka3-si7,jit8-jit8 i-piN7, jit8-jit8 thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im, i sou2 choe3--e5 bo5 m7 toa7-toa7 li7-ek ti7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5.  In ia7 chin ai3 chiu7-kun7 i.  U7-e5 tui3 chin hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 hoaN-hi2 lai5 chhe7 i.
I ti7 hia jit8-jit8 pang-chan7 hiah e5 lang5.  TaN koh-chai3 chun2-pi7 beh li7-khui Lin5-ian3-ti5.  Chit pai2 siuN7 beh te3 hit tiau5 Sam-pi2-se ho5 tit8-tit8 kau3 hai2.  Hiah e5 sou2-chai7 bo5 lang5 bat kau3.  Lai7-toe2 siN-choe3 sim2-mih8 khoan2 ia7 bo5 lang5 chai.  SoaN-lai7 u7 kui2-chok8; lang5 siN-choe3 siaN2-khoan2, che ia7 si7 bo5 lang5 chai-iaN2.  Thang kong2 hit e5 khoah-toa7 e5 sou2-chai7, tu5-khi2 soaN-lai7 hiah e5 thou2-jin5 i2-goa7, bo5 lang5 bat kiaN5-tah8. Li2-un-su-teng sou2 beh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 an2-ni.
I tai7-seng chim-chiok chun2-pi7 lou7-tiong sou2 tioh8 khiam3-eng7 e5 kan-niu5, koh khan kui2-na7 chiah chhiah-gu5 thang kap i choe3-tin7 khi3.  Leng7-goa7 koh kau-siap8 chhiaN3 pah-goa7 e5 chhou-iong2 e5 lang5 thang tau3 pang-chan7 un7-poaN hiah e5 mih8.  A-hui-li7-ka e5 lang5 boe7-hiau2 eng7 pin2-taN taN-taN3. Siat-hoat un7-poaN e5 su7, long2 si7 oa2-kho3 thau5-bak8 Se-ke-le2-tu5 lai5 tau3 kha-chhiu2.  Lun7 chiah e5 tai7-chi3 i put-chi2 chai-iaN2.  I ia7 chin chhin-chhiat koh ai3 pang-chan7.  M7-na7 an2-ni, Se-ke-le2-tu5 pun2-sin koh hoaN-hi2 kap Li2-un-su-teng saN-kap choe3-tin7 kiaN5 choe7-choe7 jit8 e5 lou7-cham7.  I e5 bok8-tek si7 beh po2-hou7 Li2-un-su-teng.
KiaN5 bo5 kui2 jit8, in kui-tin7 chiu7 kau3 Sam-pi2-se chit8 e5 boe7 kong2-tit toa7, boe7 kong2-tit koan5 e5 chui2-chhiang5.  Lun7 chit e5 ki5-koai3 se3-kai3 toa7 e5 chui2-chhiang5, in i2-cheng5 bat thiaN-kiN3 lai7-soaN e5 ou-bin7 lang5 kong2.  ThiaN-kiN3 in teh kong2, teh heng5-iong5,sit8-chai7 hou7 lang5 oe7 thang kiaN.  Chit tiau5 toa7-ho5 e5 chui2 kek8 chhim, lau5 koh kin2.  Ti7 chit8 sou2-chai7 tai7-iok u7 3000 pou7 khoah.  Kau3 chia khoe-lau5 hut-jian5-kan chhang3 loh8-khi3 ti7 chit8 e5 oeh8-oeh8 e5 chhim-khiN, iok u7 300 goa7 chhioh chhim.  Tui3 an2-ni thang chai chui2-chhiang5 e5 koan5 si7 300 chhioh.
 
Chit kang2 toa7 kang2 chui2 chit8-e7 chhiang5 loh8-khi3,tit8-tit8 kau3 khiN-toe2, i e5 chui2-chhiu koh chheng3 koan5,oan2-jian5 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 hun5-bu7 e5 khoan2-sit, a2-si7 nat2a-bong5.  Tui3 hng7-hng7 khoaN3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 ki toa7-ki ian-tang5 teh chhut-hun.  Sui-jian5 li7 koh-khah hng7, ia7 oe7-tit khoaN3 kau3 hian7-hian7.  I e5 siaN na2 lui5 teh tan5.  KhoaN3-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 keng2-ti3, Li2-un-su-teng e5 sim-koaN siu7-tioh8 toa7 kam2-tong7.  I soa3 ka7 i ho7 mia5 kio3-choe3 “I5-to-li7-a e5 Chui2-chhiang5.”
In-ui7 hit si5 tu2-tu2 si7 I5-to-li7-a ti7 Eng-kok teh choe3 Lu2-ong5.  Sou2-i2 Li2-un-su-teng ho7 chit e5 mia5 lai5 choe3 ki3-liam7.
Tui3 chit e5 toe7-hng koh khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, jin5-sou3 chin-choe7.  Keng2-ti3 ia7-si7 ho2.  Hiah e5 peh-siN3 koh chhin-chhiat.  In khoaN3 Li2-un-su-teng lai5 kau3 in hia, tai7-ke chin hoaN-hi2, chau2-lai5 chau2-khi3 beh ngia5-chih i.  KhoaN3-kiN3 in an2-ni, Li2-un-su-teng khia7 ti7 in e5 bin7-cheng5 thoan5 Sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 to7-li2.  In put-chi2 ti3-i3 thiaN.
U7 chit8 hang7 hou7 Li2-un-su-teng put-chi2 thiaN3-thang3 chiah e5 bo5 lang5 thang chiau3-kou3 e5 kan-khou2 lang5.  In-ui7 ti7 gou7-chap8 ni5 e5 tiong-kan in u7 siu7 tui3-tek8 e5 phaiN2 khoan2-thai7.  Bo5 phah-sng3 e5 si5, tui3-tek8 hut-jian5-kan lai5 pau-ui5 in e5 hiuN-sia7.  Au7-lai5 chiong sou2 liah8 e5 lang5, khan khi3 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 boe7.  ThiaN-kiN3 in siu7 chit khoan2 e5 leng5-jiok8, Li2-un-su-teng put-chi2 m7-kam.  Sou2-i2 i it-hoat ai3 hoe3-bo5 chit khoan2 lam7-sam2 liah8 lang5 choe3 lou5-chai5 e5 hong-siok8.  I2-keng chai Li2-un-su-teng u7 sit8-i3 ai3 pang-chan7 in, ia7 koh thiaN-kiN3 kong2 i si7 chin gau5 e5 i-seng, in tai7-ke ju2 ai3 chhin-kun7 i.  Phoa3-piN7 lang5 kui-tin7 ai3 lai5 hou7 i khoaN3 piN7.  Ui7-tioh8 chit e5 in-toaN, in soa3 toa3 choe7-choe7 chiah8-mih8 lai5 sang3 Li2-un-su-teng kap te3 i hiah el5ang.
In saN-kap li7-khui chit e5 toe7-hng.  Au7-lai5 koh khi3 kau3 chit8 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 hia tu2-tioh8 chit8 chok8 hui-siong5 phaiN2.  In long2 si7 kiah8 to, chheng3, ai3 phin2 se3-lek8.  U7 kui2-na7 pai2, hiam2-hiam2 hou7 in pau-ui5 teh.  KiaN-liau2 soa3 hou7 in thai5--khi3, Li2-un-su-teng chhiat-i3 kiu5 Siong7-te3 chiau3-kou3 in.  Koh bo5 loa7-ku2, in an-jian5 keng-koe3 chit tiau5 toa7 tiau5 ho5 ia7 long2 bo5 siu7 tui3-tek8 e5 ham7-hai7.  Khia7 ti7 ho5-kiN, Li2-un-su-teng kap hiah e5 ok-lang5 saN-si5.  Soa3 ho2-le2 ka7 in seh-sia7,in-ui7 u7 tui3 in e5 ho5 keng-ke3.
Koh-chai3 khi3 e5 sou2-chai7, hou7 tai7-ke chin-chiaN3 kan-khou2; in-ui7 lou7 chin kham-khiat8; chhiu7-na5 iu7-koh am7.  Khoe-chui2 bu5-sou3 tiau5.  Koh-chai3 bo5 kio5 thangke3.  Sou2-i2 in put-si5 tioh8 liau5-chui2.  Chit8 tiau5 liau5-liau2, tioh8 koh liau5 pat8-tiau5, ti3-kau3 hou7 tai7-ke boe7 kong2-tit e5 sian7.
Ti7 3 geh8 3 jit8 (1856 ni5), Li2-un-su-teng kiaN5 kau3 Te3-te7 (Tette).  Chit sou2-chai7 si7 ti7 Sam-pi2-se ho5-kiN, Pho5-to5-ga5 sou2 koan2-hat e5 sou2-chai7.  I tiam3 hia chhit le2-pai3 ku2.  Au-lai5 tui3 Sam-pi2-se ho5 che7 sio2-chun5,lian5-lian5 ko3 chit8 geh8 jit8 ku2.  Thang chai chit tiauh5o, si7 loa7 toa7, loa7 tng5.
Iau2-be7 chhut-hoat e5 tai7-seng, Li2-un-su-teng u7 siat-hoat thang hou7 hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 tiam3 ti7 Te3-te7.  Hit si5 u7 Pho5-to5-ga5 lang5 chiong tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 chioh in khia7-khi2 kap cheng3-choh.  Hou7 in ti7 hia thang theng3-hau7 Li2-un-su-teng to3-khi3 pun2-kok koh to3-tng2 lai5.  Bo5-lun7 loa7-ku2, in kong2, in hoaN-hit2? hia tan2.  Li2-un-su-teng kap in iok, kong2, Tu5-khi2 si2-sit i2-goa7, i tek-khak beh koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 ti7 in e5 tiong-kan.  Au7-lai5 i kap in saN-si5.  SaN li7-piat8 e5 tiong-kan, in tiam3 ti7 Te3-te7 teh choh-sit.  Na7-si7 in sim-koaN khng3 ng3-bang7 pat8-jit8 Li2-un-su-teng tek-khak oe7 koh-chai3 to3-tng2 lai5.
In tit8-tit8 tan2 nng7 ni5 ku2, au7-lai5 Li2-un-su-teng chiaN5-sit8 koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kap in saN-kiN3.  Hit-si5 inboe7 kong2-tit e5 hoaN-hi2.  Tai7-ke tio5-thiau3.
Ti7 hit kui2 ni5 e5 tiong-kan, Li2-un-su-teng sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 kan-khou2 kong2 boe7 chin7.  I ia7 kau3-kek8 kou-toaN.  In-ui7 ti7 saN ni5 ku2, i long2 bo5 siu-tioh8 i e5 bou2-kiaN2 e5 phoe.  Na7-si7 in sou2 sia2 e5, choan5-pou7 long2 sit-loh8--khi3.
Koh u7 chit8 hang7 hou7 i e5 sim toa7-toa7 hoan5-lo2, chiu7-si7 i u7 siu-tioh8 Lun5-tun Kau3-hoe7 e5 phoe, kong2, “I sou2 choe3 e5 kang kap thoan5 hok-im bo5 koan-he7,” sou2-iin2 boe7 tit koh hu7-tam i e5 keng-hui3.  Li2-un-su-teng sia2 phoe he5-tap kong2, i tek-khak bo5 pang3-sak thoan5 hok-im e5 gi7-bu7.  Na7-si7 kau3-hoe7 bo5 beh hu7-tam i e5 keng-hui3, i ia7 koat-i3 beh ti7 A-hui-li7-ka choe3-kang.  Koh bo5 loa7 ku2, i loh8 chun5 beh to3-tng2 khi3 Eng-kok.
To3-tng2 kau3 Pun2-kok
Chun2-pi7 beh to3-tng2 e5 si5, ti7 lou7-tiong chiap-tioh8 phoe kong2, i e5 lau7-pe7 i2-keng ke3-ong2 lah.  ThiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, i e5 sim-koaN kau3-kek8 iu-bun7.  In-ui7 ti7 chit se3-kan bo5 thang koh-chai3 kap i saN-kiN3.  I put-si5 ng3-bang7 u7 chit8 pai2 oe7 thang koh-chai3 che7 ti7 lau7-pe7 e5 sin-piN, thang kap i saN-kap kong2-oe7.  TaN chit e5 ki-hoe7 i2-keng bo5 lah.
Li2-un-su-teng kap i e5 bou2-kiaN2 saN li7-khui i2-keng gou7 ni5 ku2.  Ti7 chit tiong-kan, i e5 bok8-su-niu5 e5 taN3 thang kong2 sit8-chai7 si7 chin-chiaN3 tang7.  Ka3-si7 i2-kip8 chiau3-kou3 kiaN2-ji5, long2 si7 oa2-kho3 i chit8 lang5.  An2-ni i2-keng chin kau3-giah8, ho5-hong3 tiong7-hu koh bo5 ti7-teh.  Sou2 u7 e5 keng-hui3 iu7 eng7 bo5 sim2-mih8 kau3.  Sou2-i2 Li2-un-su-teng koh-chai3 to3-tng2-lai5 e5 si5, boe7 bian2-tit i hoaN-hi2 kau3 boe7-kou3-tit.
1856 ni5 12 geh8, Li2-un-su-teng to3-tng2 kau3 Eng-kok.  Chap8-lak8 ni5 cheng5, siau3-lian5 e5 si5, i li7-khui pun2-kok khi3 A-hui-li7-ka chiu.  TaN i koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 pun2-kok, tit-tioh8 choan5 kok e5 hoan-geng5.  Cheng3-lang5 tai7-ke saN-chiN ai3 chun-keng3 i.  Hek8-si7 peh-siN3, a2-si7 ong5-chhin long2 si7 an2-ni.  Chhut-mia5 e5 hak8-chia2 sang3 i kim e5 hun-chiong.  Lun5-tun chhi7, i2-kip8 pat8 e5 toa7 tou-chhi7 ka7 i toa7 lau7-jiat8.  U7 saN keng Tai7-hak8 sang3 i phok-su7 e5 hak8-ui7.  Kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7, lang5 oe-oe tin7-tin7 ai3 khoaN3 i.  U7 si5 ti7 lou7-nih8 teh kiaN5 e5 si5 hou7 lang5 jin7-tioh8, cheng3-lang5 liam5-piN ka7 iui5-teh, long2 si7 ai3 kap i khan-chhiu2, chheng2-an.  Ti7 le2-pai3-tng5 nih na7-si7 soat-kau3 liau2, kui toa7-tin7 e5 hoe7-iu2, ia7 si7 ai3 chhin-kun7 i.  Tai7-ke ai3 kap i kong2-oe7.  In kiaN5 boe7 khui-kha.
Koh u7 tai7-hak8 e5 hak8-seng, thiaN i ian2-kang2, simsiu7 kam2-tong7, soa3 hoaN-hi2 chi3-goan7 beh khi3 A-hui-li7-ka thoan5-po3 Ki-tok kiu3-se3 e5 hok-im.  Li2-un-su-teng tui3 in kong2, “Goa2 kiu5 lin2 tioh8 chu3-bak8 khoaN3 A-hui-li7-ka.  Goa2 tai7-seng khi3 hia khui-lou7.  Hian7-si5 chiah e5 lou7 long2 khui.  TaN koh bo5 kui2 ni5, goa2 chiu7 bo5 ti7-teh.  Lin2 m7-thang hou7 sou2 khui e5 lou7, koh-chai3 that-bat8.  Goa2 beh koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka,beh koh chiap-soa3 khui Ki-tok e5 lou7.  Goa2 e5 kang lin2 tioh8 soa3-chiap lai5 choe3.  TaN goa2 kau-thok lin2 an2-ni lai5 kiaN5.”
Li2-un-su-teng m7-na7 si3-koe3 ian2-kang2, m7-na7 si3-koe3 kong2 A-hui-li7-ka e5 cheng5-heng5 hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 thiaN.  I ia7 chiong i chap8-lak8 ni5 ku2, sou2 keng-ke3 es5u, tu3 ti7 chheh-nih.  Ti3-kau3 hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 hoan-geng5 i sou2 sia2--e5.  I sou2 tu3 chit pun2 chheh, siau chin toa7.  Tui3 an2-ni i ka-ki7 ia7 u7 tit-tioh8 toa7-toa7 e5 li7-ek.  I e5 siu-jip8 u7 kui2-na7 ban7 gun5.  Na7-si7 i2-cheng5 bo5 kau3-giah8 khui3-lat8 thang hou7 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 thak8-chheh.  TaN i oe7-tit-thang siat-hoat hou7 in soa3-chiap koh-chai3 thak8.  Koh-chai3 u7 thang hu7-tam ka-ki7 e5 sou2-hui3.  I m7-bian2 koh oa2-kho3 Lun5-tun Kau3-hoe7 e5 pou2-chou7.  Tui3 chit-tiap8 liau2-au7, i koh chun2-pi7 beh to3-tng2 khi3 A-hui-li7-ka.
Koh to3-tng2 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka
Te7 ji7 pai2 beh koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka,chit pai2 u7 tit-tioh8 chin-choe7 kho-hak8-chia2 e5 chan3-seng5.  In gi7-koat hoaN-hi2 beh hu7-tam i e5 sou2-hui3.  In e5 bok8-tek si7 beh chhe7 chit tiau5 Sam-pi2-se ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  Koh-chai3 ai3 gian2-kiu3 A-hui-li7-ka tiong-pou7 e5 te7-li2 i2-kip8 jin5-cheng2.  KhoaN3 hit toe7-hng siN-choe3 siaN2 khoan2.  In chiong chek-jim choan5-pou7 kau-tai3 i khi3 chhe7, khi3 gian2-kiu3 hiah e5 sou2-chai7.  Siat-su2 hiah e5 toe7-hng na7 si7 ho2, chiu7 thang ti7 hia siat-kau3, thang ti7 hia thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im.  Hit toe7-hng e5 thou2-toe7 na7-si7 pui5, in ia7 ng3-bang7 oe7-tit-thang hou7 lang5 khi3 hia khai-khun2.  Au7-lai5 tek-khak oe7 chiaN5-choe3 ho2 khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Bian2 koh-chai3 tu2-tioh8 lang5 liah8 lang5 khi3 choe3 lou5-chai5.  Sou2-i2 sou2 iau3-kin2 e5, si7 tioh8 chin7-lat8 pang-chan7 hiah e5 khun3-khou2 e5 ou-bin7 lang5.  Koh tioh8 eng7 Ki-tok e5 thiaN3 lai5 kam2-hoa3 in.  U7 khng3 chit khoan2 e5 hi-bong7, boe7 bian2-tit hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 siu7 kam2-tong7, soa3 toa7 chan3-seng5 i sou2 beh choe3 e5 su7-giap8.
 
Li2-un-su-teng keng2 lak8-e5 pun2-kok lang5 kap i tau3-tin7.  Tiong-kan u7 i e5 sio2-ti7 Chha5-li2 (Charles) cho3 ie5 pi3-su, koh u7 Ke5 Iok-han7 I-seng (John Kirk M. D).  I2-goa7 koh si3-e5 lang5; chit8 e5 si7 kho-hak8-chia2, chit8 e5 si7 bi2-sut8-ka, chit8 e5 si7 ki5-su.
U7 chit8 hang7 hou7 i put-chi2 eng5-kng, chiu7-si7 iau2-be7 chhut-hoat e5 tai7-seng, i u7 tit-tioh8 I5-to-li7-a Lu2-ong5 e5 chan3-seng5.  Lu2-ong5 chhiaN2 Li2-un-su-teng khi3 kau3 ong5-kiong hou7 i saN-kiN3.  Koh chin ho2 khoan2-thai7 i.
Li2-un-su-teng hou7 cheng3-lang5 ka7 i song3-piat8 liau2-au7, kap Bok8-su-niu5 i2-kip8 i e5 soe3-kiaN2 Ou5-se (Oswell)ti7 3 geh8 10 jit8 (1858 ni5) li7-khui pun2-kok, tah chun5 beh khi3 A-hui-li7-ka.  I siong5-siong5 khou2-khng3 i e5 tang5-phoaN7 kong2, Na7-si7 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka, in tai7-ke tioh8 chin7 sou2 oe7--e5 lai5 pang-chan7, lai5 khan-seng5, lai5 khoan2-thai7 hiah e5 bo5 khai-hoa3 e5 ou-bin7 lang5.  Tioh8 hou7 in tit-tioh8 to7-tek e5 ka3-si7.  Ia7 tioh8 hou7 in e5 seng-oah8 khah an-teng7.
Kau3-ui7 e5 si5, in ko2-jian5 u7 an2-ni kiaN5.  Tai7-ke put-chi2 thiaN3-sioh hia e5 pun2-toe7 lang5.  Sou2-i2 kau3 ti7 kin-a2-jit8, put-lun7 sim2-mih8 chhut-goa7 lang5 na7 khi3 kau3 in hia, ia7 m7-bian2 kiaN u7 sim2-mih8 siong-hai7  Che long2 si7 ui7-tioh8 Li2-un-su-teng e5 gau5 ka3-si7 kap gau5 thiaN3-thang3 hiah e5 lang5.
Chun5 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka e5 lam5-peng5, Li2-un-su-teng Bok8-su-niu5 e5 sin-the2 na2 ku2 na2 bo5 iong2-kiaN7, sou2-i2 i kap soe3-kiaN2 Ou5-se tiam3 ti7 hia liau5-iong2.  Li2-un-su-teng pun2-sin kap tang5-phoaN7 choe3-tin7 tit8-tit8 kau3 Sam-pi2-se ho5 khau2.  Siang7 hit si5 i soa3 toa3 chit8 chiah soe3-chiah he2-chun5 ho7-choe3 Ma7-lo5 (Ma-Robert)kau3 ti7 ho5 khau2.  Tui3 chia khi2-sin, in sai2 hit chiah he2-chun5 tit8-tit8 kau3 ho5 e5 lai7-toe7.
Ti7 9 geh8 8 jit8 (1858 ni5), chun5 pha-tiaN3 ti7 Te3-te7.  Li2-un-su-teng chiuN7-liok8 kap i nng7 ni5 cheng5 sou2 pang3 he7-teh e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 koh saN-kiN3.  In khoaN3-kiN3 i koh-chai3 lai5, hoaN-hi2, tio5-thiau3 kau3 boe7-oe7 kong2--tit.  In ti7 soaN-lai7 tit8-tit8 tan2 nng7-ni5 ku2.  TaN chu2-lang5 i2-keng koh lai5, in boe7 kim3-tit toa7 hoaN-hi2.  Koh-chai3 tit-tioh8 in e5 chu2-lang5 toa3 choe7-choe7 mih8 lai5 sang3 in, in e5 hoaN-hi2 koh-khah kong2 boe7 chin7.
 
In tui3 Sam-pi2-se ho5 khau2 sai2 chit chiah he2-chun5,ti7 lou7-tiong u7 tu2-tioh8 khoe-lau5, sou2-i2 u7 si5 sai2 long2 boe7 chin3.  Ui7-tioh8 chit e5 in-toaN, in chiu7 sai2 tui3 pat8 tiau5 khi2-khi3.  Chit tiau5 Se-le2 ho5 (Shire River) chin oan-oat, koh long2 si7 thoaN.  Sou2-i2 sai2 chun5 tui3 hia khi3 sit8-chai7 put-chi2 khun3-lan5.  Keng-ke3 loa7-choe7 kan-khou2, kiaN5 kui2 pah Eng-li2, in chiah kau3 lai7-toe7 khah koan5 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 hia khi3-hau7 cham2-jian5 khah ho2.  In tai7-ke e5 sin-the2 ia7 khah iong2-kiaN7.
Pi2-kau3 pat8 sou2-chai7, chit toe7-hng e5 lang5 ia7 khah bun5-beng5.  In oe7-hiau2 phah thih, oe7-hiau2 sio hui5, oe7-hiau2 cheng3 mi5-hoe kap pat8-hang7 chhai3-soe.
Ti7 9 geh8 16 jit8 (1859 ni5), Li2-un-su-teng thau5-pai2 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 chiaN2-chui2 e5 toa7 ou5.  Tng5 u7 350 Eng-li2.  I e5 chui2 ia7 chin chheng.  Chit e5 ou5 ho7-choe3 Na5-a2-sah (Nyasa).
Kho2-sioh kiaN5 kau3 chit sou2-chai7, i e5 he2-chun5 e7 lau7.  Chun5-toe2 long2 si7 khang, khah pou2 ia7 boe7 bat8.  Koh in jit8-jit8 sou2 tioh8 eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 ia7 chin khiam3.  Sou2-i2 Li2-un-su-teng kap i e5 hiaN-ti7 Chha5-li2, kap Ke5 Iok-han7 I-seng chun2-pi7 beh chhoa7 hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 to3-tng2 khi3 in e5 pun2-hiuN.  Na7-si7 in tiong-kan u7 lang5 i2-keng tiam3 ti7 Te3-te7 hiah-nih ku2, koh-chai3 u7 kian3-ti3 ke-koan3, taN soa3 long2 bo5 ai3 to3-tng2 khi3.  Chi2-u7 kui2-na7 lang5 hoaN-hi2 kap Li2-un-su-teng choe3-tin7 kiaN5.  In sou2 keng-ke3 e5 toe7-hng jin5-sou3 put-chi2 choe7,ia7 chin chhin-chhiat.  Na7-si7 ti7 hit sou2-chai7 choe3 kang, kang-chiN5 chin-chiaN3 siok8; chit8 e5 lang5 chit8 jit8 sou2 than3 si7 chio2-chio2.  Sou2-i2 hiah e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 kng mih8 na7-si7 sian7, in chiu7 kau-siap8 chhiaN3 pat8 lang5 lai5 thoe3 in kng.  Kang-chiN5 si7 kui2-liap8 siok8-siok8 e5 chu-a2 na7-tiaN7.  Tu2-tng7 u7 chit8 e5 pin5-toaN7 koh lau7 e5 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 siuN7 sian7 bo5 ai3 kng heng5-li2.  I kio3 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5--e5 lai5 thoe3 i, hit e5 lang5 lian5-soa3 ka7 i thoe3 kng saN-jit8 ku2.  I sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 kang-chiN5 si7 chit8 nia2 bo5 lang5 ai3, phoa3-phoa3 e5 khou3 na7-tiaN7.

Lin5-ian3-ti5 (Linyanti) u7 tu2-tioh8 phoa3-piN7.  U7 e5 soa3 si2.  Ki5-u5-e5 toa3 boe7 tiau5, kou-put-chiong li7-khui in e5 thoan5-to7 toe7, koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 pun2-kok.  In chit kui2 lang5 peng5-sou3 teh choe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 kang ti7 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan.
Koh-chai3 saN geh8 jit8 ku2, Li2-un-su-teng kap i e5 tang5-phoaN7 tit8-tit8 kiaN5 kau3 Ma2-ko-lo5 lang5 e5 toe7-hng.  In e5 thau5-bak8 Se-ke-le2-tu5 khoaN3-kiN3 in lai5, sim-koaN kau3-kek8 hoaN-hi2.  I kong2, sui-jian5 i tan2 kui2-na7 ni5 ku2, i chai Li2-un-su-teng u7 chit8 jit8 tek-khak oe7 koh-chai3 lai5.
Chhit ni5 cheng5, Li2-un-su-teng sou2 kau-tai7 in kou3 e5 mih8-kiaN7 tak8-hang7 long2 ti7-teh.  Long2 tit-tioh8 Se-ke-le2-tu5 kap i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 chiau3-kou3.  Bo5 chit8 hang7 sit-loh8. Ti7 chit si5, hit chiah he2-chun5 (Ma7-lo5) na2 ku2 na2 phaiN2-khi3.  Tauh8-tauh8 teh siu-li2, na7-si7 bo5 hoat-tou7.  Kau3 lou7-be2 kou-put-chiong tioh8 ka7 i pang3-sak.  TaN Li2-un-su-teng na7-si7 chhut-goa7 si7 eng7 soe3 chiah chun5-a2.
Ti7 1861 ni5, i kap i e5 tang5-phoaN7 eng7 sio2-chun5 tit8-tit8 sai2 kau3 hai2-khau2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Kau3-ui7 e5 si5, in tit-tioh8 kui2-na7 e5 kou2 peng5-iu2 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7.  Koh usiu7-tioh8 i e5 ka-chok8 sou2 kia3-lai5 e5 phoe.  Hiah e5 phoe sui-bong2 si7 ni5 poaN3 cheng5 sou2 sia2-e5, iau2-ku2 thak8 liauh23 i e5 sim tit-tioh8 chin toa7 e5 an-ui3.
Tu2-tioh8 chhi3-lian7.
Ti7 1861 ni5 e5 ni5-thau5, Li2-un-su-teng u7 koh choch7…t chiah sin e5 he2-chun5.  Ka7 i ho7 mia5 choe3 Pai2-o-ni5 (Pioneer).  In-ui7 hit chiah ku7 e5, i2-keng phaiN2-phaiN2, bo5 lou7-eng7.
Siang5 chit chun7, tui3 pun2-kok u7 siau3-lian5 e5 soan-kau3-su, chi3-goan7 beh khi3 A-hui-li7-ka pang-chan7 i thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im.  In chiah e5 soan-kau3-su tai7-ke put-chi2 u7 hak8-lek8.  Koh long2-si7 iu2-beng5 Tai7-hak8 e5 chut-giap8 seng.
 
Chhoa7 chiah e5 soan-kau3-su choe3-tin7 khi3--e5, chiu7-si7 Ma2-khian Kam-tok (Bishop Moekenyie).  I u7 chhoa7 gou7 e5 Eng-kok lang5 kap gou7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 khi3.  I ng3-bang7 u7 thang chhe7 tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7 lai5 he7 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 toe7-ki.

Chiah e5 chan5-jim2 el5ang u7 e5 si7 Pho5-to5-ga5 lang5, u7 e5 si7 A-lat-pek lang5.  Tu2-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 su7, choe7-choe7 sou2-chai7 khng3 teh pha-hng, in-ui7 bo5 lang5 thang cheng3-chok.

In e5 seng-khu koh long2 si7 siong.  Tai7-ke go7 kau3 san2-san2.  KiaN5 oa2 ka7 in khoaN3, Li2-un-su-teng kap Kam-tok it-hoat kho2-lin5 in chiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5.
In koaN2-kin2 koah-tng7 sou2 pak8 e5 soh, koh chiong thih-lian7 ka7 i kong3-tng7, hou7 in tai7-ke tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.  In hiah e5 hap8-kiong7 u7 poeh-chap8-si3 lang5. Hiah e5 soan-kau3-su khoaN3-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 ki-hoe7,tai7-ke koaN2-kin2 ke3-ek8 ai3 khi2 in e5 siok-sia3.  In ia7 jit8-jit8 bian2-kiong2 oh8 hiah e5 lang5 e5 khiuN-khau2.  Li2-un-su-teng tui3 i e5 keng-giam7, chiong choe7-choe7 hang7 lai5 bian2-le7 chiah e5 sin lai5 e5 soan-kau3-su; kio3 in tioh8 oe7 hiau2 thun-lun2, gau5 jim2-nai7. gui5-hiam2, a2-si7 siu7-tioh8 lang5 e5 chou2-tong3, i ia7 tiaN7-tioh8 beh choe3-chiaN5 i e5 gi7-bu7.  Sui-jian5 keng-ke3 loa7 choe7 ni5, i ia7 bo5 sit-chi3.  Thang chai i si7 kau3-kek8 chin7-tiong, gau5 jim2-nai7 kap iong2-kam2 e5 lang5.
Ti7 si3 geh8 (1864 ni5), i li7-khui A-hui-li7-ka, tah ika-ki7 e5 soe3-chiah he2-chun5 beh khi3 In3-tou7, in ti7 lou7-tiong u7 tu2-tioh8 hong-eng2 kap po3-thau5.  Na7-si7 kau3 lou7-be2 tai7-ke an-jian5 kau3 ui7.  Ti7 In3-tou7 kok, i tit-tioh8 choe7-choe7 lang5 e5 hoan-geng5.  Tai7-ke saN-chiN ai3 ho2 khoan2-thai7 i.
Koh bo5 loa7 ku2, i chun2-pi7 beh li7-khui In3-tou7.  I siat-hoat chiong ka-ki7 e5 chun5 pang3-he7 ti7 In3-tou7, au7-lai5 tah pat8 chiah toa7 chun5 kau3 Eng-kok.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, tit-tioh8 Lun5-tun chhi7 e5 chhi7-bin5, hek8-si7 bun5-bu2 koaN-oan5 tai7-ke ka7 i toa7-toa7 hoan-geng5.  Tai7-ke piau2-chhut in e5 chun-keng3.  “Li2-un-su-teng to3--lai5 lah !”  Chit khoan2 e5 siaN-im thoan5 kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7.  Lun7 i ti7 A-hui-li7-ka sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 su7, kok toe7-hng e5 sin-bun5 u7 kong2 chin-choe7.  Ia7 m7-na7 ti7 Eng-kok, chiu7 ti7 A-chiu, Bi2-chiu ia7-si7 an2-ni.  Sou2-ik2au ui7 e5 si5 i chin bo5 eng5.  In-ui7 siu7 chhiaN2 khi3 choe7-choe7 sou2-chai7 ian2-kang2, bo5-lun7 hoe7-iu2, a2-si7 kho-hak8-chia2, tai7-ke long2 ai3 thiaN A-hui-li7-ka e5 su7.
I  m7-na7 si3-koe3 ian2-kang2; koh-chai3 eng7 choe7-choe7 si5-kan tu3 chit8 pun2 chheh.  Lai7-tiong long2 si7 lun7-khi2 i ku2-ku2 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka chhim-soaN e5 lu2-heng5.  Tak8 hang7 kong2-khi2 chin siong5-se3, koh sia2 liau2 chinu7 chhu3-bi7.
Li7-khui Pun2-kok
1865 ni5 poeh geh8 Li2-un-su-teng koh-chai3 chitp8ai li7-khui pun2-kok i2-kip8 kiaN2-ji5, beh to3-tng2 khi3 A-hui-li7-ka.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, i e5 pou7-ha7, hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 hoaN-hi2 kau3 boe7 kou3-tit.  In tiong-kan u7 nng7 lang5 chin-chiaN3 chin7-tiong; chit8 e5 ho7-choe3 Chu7-ma2(Chuma), chit8 e5 ho7-choe3 Su3-si7 (susi).  Chu7-ma2 khah cha2 bat hou7 lang5 liah8-khi3 choe3 lou5-chai5, chong2-si7 tui3 Li2-un-su-teng kap Ma2-khian Kam-tok e5 pang-chan7,i u7 tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.  I khah ku2 to oe7 ki3-tit chit e5 un.  Su3-si7 chai7-cha2 e5 chit-giap8 si7 teh ka7 lang5 phoa3-chha5, au7-lai5 i lai5 Li2-un-su-teng e5 he2-chun5 choe3 
 
chui2-hu.  In chit nng7 lang5 i2-kip8 chap8-saN e5 In3-tou7 lang5, kap ji7-chap8-si3 e5 ou-bin7 lang5, hap8-kiong7 chiu7-si7 saN-chap8-chhit lang5; koh kap chit8 tin7 lok8-to5, chhiah-gu5, lu5-a2 kap lo5, long2 saN-kap choe3-tin7 beh chin3-jip8 A-hui-li7-ka bo5 lang5 bat kau3 e5 sou2-chai7.  hit-pai2 bo5 chit8 e5 peh8-bin7 lang5 kap Li2-un-su-teng choe3-tin7.  Chi2-u7 In3-tou7 kap ou-bin7 lang5 na7-tiaN7.
In khi3 bo5 loa7 hng7, Li2-un-su-teng chiah hoat-kian3 tioh8 chiah e5 In3-tou7 lang5 e5 pin5-toaN7 seng3, hou7 langb5oe sin3-iong7--tit.  Put-ko3 jit8-jit8 si7 ai3 khun3, ai3 chiah8 na7-tiaN7.  Hiah e5 cheng-siN in ia7 boe7-hiau2-tit chiau3-kou3, ti3-kau3 lok8-to5 kap chhiah-gu5 si2 chin-choe7.  Li2-un-su-teng kou-put-chiong tioh8 siat-hoat hou7 chiah e5 lan2-to7 e5 In3-tou7 lang5 koh-chai3 to3-tng2 khi3 kau3 in ep5un-kok.  I pun2-sin kap hiah e5 khah oe7 kham-titkan-khou2 e5 ou-bin7 lang5, chiah beh koh chin3-heng5.
Put-lun7 khi3 kau3 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7, in beng5-beng5 khoaN3-kiN3 lang5 liah8 lang5 khi3 choe3 lou5-le7 e5 cheng3-ku3.  Loan2-jiok8--e5, phoa3-piN7--e5, boe7 kiaN5--e5, chiah e5 pang3 ti7 lou7-nih hou7 in un5-un5-a2 si2.  Sou2-i2 tak8 sou2-chai7 kut-thau5 moa2 si3-koe3.  In lian5-soa3 kiaN5 gou7-jit8 ku2, long2 si7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit khoan2 chan5-jim2, put-hoat e5 su7.  Au7-lai5 in kiaN5 kau3 Na5-a2-sah ou5.
In ti7 lou7-tiong tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 kan-khou2, na7-si7 in koat-i3 beh khi3 Than-gan5-i-ka ou5(Tanganika) e5 sou2-chai7.  Chit e7 ou5 hui-siong5 toa7, ia7 tng5, ia7 khoah.  Tui3 lam5 kau3 pak u7 kui2-na7-pah Eng-li2.  Ti7 hit sou2-chai7 bo5 poaN3-lang5 chai siaN2-su7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 mui2 ni5 im chit8 pai2 toa7-chui2.  Li2-un-su-teng chinai3 chai chit e5 im chui2 e5 goan5-in, i ti3-i3 ai3 chhe7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  I ng3-bang7 ti7 Than-gan5-i-ka ou5 e5 toe7-hng oe7-tit hoat-kian3-tioh8 chit tiau5 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 siong7 goan5-thau5.
Chong2-si7 tui3 Na5-a2-sah ou5 kau3 Than-gan5-i-ka ou5, lou7-cham7 cham2-jian5 hng7, lou7-tiong ia7 long2 si7 chhiu7-na5, koh si7 hoan-kai3 e5 sou2-chai7.  ThiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, hiahe5 In3-tou7 lang5 it-hoat tioh8-kiaN, m7-kaN2 khi3.  In tai7-ke choe3 chit8 khun3 chau2 kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni, koh-chai3 iau-gian5 kong2, “Li2-un-su-teng i2-keng si2 lah; khi3 hou7 chhiN-hoan thai5--khi3.”
 
Na7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng kap hiah e5 chin7-tiong, bo5 sioh ka-ki7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 jit8-jit8 ti7 soaN-lai7, na2 teh chin3-jip8 bo5 lang5 bat kau3 e5 sou2-chai7.  In nng3 am7-am7 e5 chhiu7-na5, liau5 chui2, peh koan5-soaN, loh8 nia2; u7 si5 liau5-ke3 boe7 kong2-tit hng7 e5 lam3-thou5.  Oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 kui jit8 teh liau5 lam3-chhan5.  Siong5-siong5 iau-go7, seng-khu tam5, ia3-sian7, phoa3-piN7, koh-chai3 sit-loh8 i sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 ioh8, boe7 bian2-tit i e5 sin-the2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 loan2-jiok8.  I e5 seng-khu san2-san2 long2 bo5 bah; chi2-u7 chhun phe5-hu kap kut-thau5.  Sui-bong2 tu2-tioh8 kau3 an2-ni, i ia7-si7 ho2-taN2 koh chin3-cheng5.  I e5 i3-chi3 sit8-chai7 chin kian-kou3.
Jian5-au7 tu2-tioh8 jiat8-cheng3, seng-khu bo5 lat8, bak8-chiu na2 beh chhiN-mi5, kiaN5 lou7 kiaN5 boe7 khi3, i ia7-sit7ak jit8 kiah8 pit lai5 sia2 in sou2 keng-ke3 e5 su7 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3.  Na7 m7-si7 ui7-tioh8 i e5 jiat8-sim, i e5 iong2-kam2 kap i e5 tioh8-boa5, A-hui-li7-ka lai7-toe7 e5 cheng5-heng5 lantek2-khak boe7-tit-thang chai.
Ni5-lo5 Ho5, a2-si7 Khong-go5 Ho5 ?
Ti7 si3 geh8, Li2-un-su-teng kau3 Than-gan5-i-ka ou5 e5 lam5-peng5.  Chit e5 ou5 e5 chui2 chin chhim.  Ti7 ou5-kiN es5i-ui5 long2 si7 chhiu7-na5.  Koan5-soaN ia7 put-chi2 choe7.  Keng2-ti3 sit8-chai7 kau3-kek8 sui2.035
Kau3-ui7 e5 si5, Li2-un-su-teng tiam3 ti7 chit8 keng chhau2-liau5-a2.  In-ui7 i e5 seng-khu chin ia3-sian7.  Ti3-kau3 ilong2 boe7 kiaN5.  Chong2-si7 i e5 sim-sin5 iau2 chin oah8-tang7.  I tiaN7-tioh8 beh chhe7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  TaN in i2-keng kau3 Than-gan5-i-ka lah.  I pun2-sin tauh8-tauh8 ka-ki7 mng7 kong2, “Chit e5 Than-gan5-i-kaou5 tau3-ti2 si7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5, a2-si7 Khong-go5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5?”  Chiau3 i ka-ki7 teh phah-sng3, Than-gan5-i-ka ou5 kap Ni5-lo5 ho5 kiam2-chhai2 u7 saN lian5-lok8.
TaN Li2-un-su-teng i2-keng ti7 chhim-soaN lai7 nng7 ni5 ku2.  Ti7 chiah ku2 e5 tiong-kan, i long2 bo5 siu-tioh8 i e5 kiaN2-ji5, a2-si7 peng5-iu2 e5 phoe-sin3.  Sou2-i2 i e5 sim chinbo5-liau5.  M7-ku2 ui7-tioh8 ai3 chhe7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5, i koh chun2-pi7 beh chin3-jip8 hiah e5 koh-khah chhim e5 lai7-soaN.  ThiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, te3 i e5 ou-bin7 lang5, tai7-ke 
 
kau3-kek8 kiaN.  U7-e5 sui5-si5 thau-chau2.  Chi2-u7 chhun gou7 e5 lang5 phe-si2 kap i khi3.  Chit gou7 e5 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 nng7 ni5 ku2, i2-keng chin ia3-sian7.  Chong2-si7 in tai7-ke phe-mia7 koh kap Li2-un-su-teng loh8 lam5-peng5.
Ti7 7 geh8 18 jit8, in kiaN5 kau3 Bang7-goe7-lo5 ou5 (Lake Bangweolo).  Chit e5 ou5 chin-chiaN3 toa7, i e5 chui2 koh chhim.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, Li2-un-su-teng chiah chai chit e5 toa7 ou5, chiu7-si7 Khong-go5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  Chit toe7-hng e5 lang5 long2 iau2-be7 khai-hoa3.  In hut-jian5-kan khoaN3-kiN3 Li2-un-su-teng tai7-ke chin gong5-ngiah8.  In-ui7 in i2-cheng5 m7-bat khoaN3-kiN3 peh8-bin7 lang5.  Cheng3-lang5 long2 chau2 oa2 lai5.  Tai7-ke ai3 khoaN3 peh8-bin7 lang5 siN-choe3 si7 siaN2-khoan2.  Sun7 chit e5 ki-hoe7, Li2-un-su-teng hian-khui i e5 Seng3-keng thak8 hou7 in thiaN.  Au7-lai5 thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 mia5.  Che si7 chiah e5 soaN-lai7-lang5 thau5-pai2 thiaN-kiN3 Sip8-ji7-ke3 kap Kiu3-se3 e5 to7-li2.
Chit toe7-hng si7 chin tam5-sip e5 sou2-chai7.  Hia u7 ou5,u7 khoe, u7 ho5, ia7 u7 lam3-thou5.  Sui-bong2 khi3 koh-khah hng7, ia7 si7 boe7-oe7 thoat-li7 im chui2 e5 sou2-chai7.
Ti7 chhit geh8 be2, Li2-un-su-teng koh-chai3 chiuN7 pak,bok8-tek si7 beh to3-tng2 kau3 U5-ji7-ji5 (Ujiji).  I ng3-bang7 na7 kau3 hia chiu7 thang theng5-kha hioh-khun3.  Koh thang tit-tioh8 i i2-cheng5 sou2 chu3-bun5 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Kiam2-chhai2 ia7 u7 phoe ti7 hia teh tan2 i.  Chong2-si7 long2 bo5 phah-sng3 lou7-cham7 u7 hiah hng7, ia7 lou7-thau5 u7 hiah phaiN2.  I e5 seng-khu na2 ku2 na2 loan2-jiok8.  Kau3 ti7 1868 ni5, i iau2 ti7 lai7-soaN teh kan-khou2.  Jian5-au7 i soa3 tu2-tioh8 choe3-li7 e5 piN7, an2-ni hou7 i koh-khah bo5 khui3-lat8.
TaN hit sou2-chai7 sng3 si7 ti7 jiat8-to7, sou2-i2 hui-siong5 joah8.  KiaN5 kau3 chia, Li2-un-su-teng i2-keng chin thiam2 lah.  Kap i choe3-tin7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 khoaN3 i teh kan-khou2, tai7-ke koaN2-kin2 siat-hoat lai5 ka7 i kng.
Ti7 3 geh8 14 jit8 (1869 ni5), in chiah kau3 U5-ji7-ji5.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, i chin toa7 sit-chi3.  
 
In-ui7 i sou2 chu3-bun5 hiah e5 mih8, long2 hou7 lang5 thau-theh8 liau2-liau2.  Chi2-u7 chhun chit8 nia2 saN, chit8-sut-a2 te5 kap tam7-poh8 thng5.  Sou2 iau3-kin2, sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 ioh8, ia7 long2 sit-loh8--khi3.
Sui-jian5 sit-chi3, kan-khou2, bo5 thang chiah8, i ia7 si7 koh chin3-cheng5.  KiaN5 lou7 kau3 kha-toe2 siN-thiaN3, i ia7 si7 tit8-tit8 pai2.  I e5 sim koat-i3 ai3 chhe7 Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5.  U7-si5 tu2-tioh8 hong-hou7, boe7-tit chhut-mng5, i chiu7 tiam3 ti7 liau5-a2-lai7, thak8 Seng3-keng.  Bat chit8 pai2, choan5 pun2 lian5-soa3 thak8 si3 pian3.
Ti7 2 geh8 (1871 ni5), chap8 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 lai5 beh chhe7 i.  In si7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 peng5-iu2, ti7 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 sou2 phai3--lai5-e5.  Chong2-si7 chin kho2-sioh, hiah e5 lang5 kong2 oe7 long2 bo5 su7-sit8, in koh chin pin5-toaN7, tai7-ke long2 si7 kong2-thong-ho2 kap eng7 ke3-chhek beh chou2-tong3 Li2-un-su-teng khi3 khah chhim e5 soaN-lai7.  In e5 i3-su3 si7 ai3 i chhoa7 in to3-tng2 kau3 ine5 pun2 sou2-chai7.  Na7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 bok8-tek si7 beh chhoa7 in kau3 Lu7-a-la5-ba5 (Lualaba).  In-ui7 iai3 chai i sou2 thiaN-kiN3 hit tiau5 ho5, si7 Ni5-lo5 ho5, a2-si7 Khong-go5 ho5.  Tau3-ti2 to2-chit8 tiau5 ?
Ti7 chhun-thiN e5 si5, in ko2-jian5 kau3 A-hui-li7-ka chhim-soaN e5 siong7 lai7-bin7.  Ti7 hia i chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3 Khong-go5 ho5.  Sui-jian5 li7 hai2 iau2 hui-siong5 hng7, ti7 hit sou2-chai7 chit tiau5 ho5 e5 khoah iau2 u7 5000 goa7 pou7, m7-na7 hiah khoah, koh-chai3 ia7 chin chhim.  Sui-bong2 toa7-chiah chun5 ia7 oe7 kiaN5 kau3 hia.
Nng7 ni5 cheng5, i khi2-sin beh khi3 kau3 chit sou2-chai7, nng7 ni5 ku2 e5 kan-khou2 sit8-chai7 kong2 boe7 liau2.  TaN i i2-keng khoaN3-kiN3 Khong-go5 ho5, i boe7 bian2-tittioh8 koh-chai3 to3-tng2 kau3 U5-ji7-ji5.  Beh koh to3-tng2e lou7-cham7 u7 600 Eng-li2, sit8-chai7 si7 kau3-kek8 hng7.  Ti7 lou7-tiong koh u7 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 gui5-hiam2.  U7 nng7 pai2 i hiam2-hiam2 hou7 chhiN-hoan thai5--khi3; hiam2-hiam2 hou7 in eng7 pio-a2 sia7-tioh8.
Ui7-tioh8 khoa3-lu7 thoa-boa5, piN7-thiaN3 kap iau-go7, sou2-i2 i e5 sin-the2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 soe-jiok8.  I e5 chhui3-khi2 ia7 lak kau3 beh liau2.  In-ui7 ti7 hiah choe7 ni5 e5 tiong-kan, i long2 si7 eng7 chhui3 pou7 chhiN e5 hoan-beh8 lai5 chi2-ki.
 
Pun2-jian5 i chin chhou-iong2, chin ho2-han3, taN go7 kau3 san2-san2.  I e5 bak8-chiu thap-thap, i e5 seng-khu chhun kut-thau5 na7-tiaN7.  Na7-si7 lun7 ni5 he3, i chiah gou7-chap8-poeh he3.  Tu2-tioh8 phoa3-piN7 ku2-ku2 e5 ian5-kou3, taN i e5 chhiu2 beh kiah8 pit ia7-si7 bo5 hoat-tit.
Keng3-jian5 tu2-tioh8 kau3 an2-ni, i kou-put-chiong un5-un5-a2 thoa kau3 U5-ji7-ji5.  I sou2 ng3-bang7 e5, si7 ti7 hia u7 thang an-hioh, u7 thang eng7 i sou2 khiam3 e5 mih8.  I choan5-jian5 bo5 phah-sng3 i sou2 kau-thok e5 lang5 oe7 chiong i sou2 u7 e5 mih8 long2-chong2 ka7 i lam7-sam2 theh8-khi3 boe7.  In tiong-kan bo5 chit8 e5 oe7 sin3-iong7--tit, hoan2-tng2 koh eng7 bo5 liong5-sim e5 oe7 si3-koe3 kong2 Li2-un-su-teng i2-keng si2 liau2 lah.
Ti7 che sit-chi3 e5 si5, Li2-un-su-teng sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “Goa2 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 tui3 Ia5-lou7-sat-lengbeh2 khi3 Ia5-li7-ko siaN5, lou7-nih hou7 chhat8 chhiuN2--e5.  Na7-si7 goa2 long2 bo5 thang ng3-bang7 ti7 lou7-tiong u7 chit8 ech5e-si, u7 chit8 e5 Li7-bi7, a2-si7 chit8 e5 ho2 e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a lang5 thang tui3 goa2 e5 sin-piN ke3.”
I ti7 sit-chi3 e5 si5 kong2 an2-ni, keng3-jian5 m7-chai ho2 e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a lang5 i2-keng kun7 lah.  I i2-keng kiaN5 beh kau3 ti7 i e5 sin-piN lah !  hit jit8 i kiong5-kiong5 thoa kau3 U5-ji7-ji5, tu2-tu2 si7 chap8 geh8 ji7-chap8-saN jit8.  I khang-chhiu2 kau3 ui7, oan2-jian5 na2 khit-chiah8 chit8-iuN7, in-ui7 i ku2-ku2 kiaN5 hiah e5 oan2-siap8 e5 lou7, i sou2 u7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 ia7 long2 eng7 chin7 lah.
Chong2-si7 koh gou7 jit8 ku2, ti7 bo5 phah-sng3 e5 si5, i u7 tit-tioh8 lang5 e5 chin2-kiu3, lang5 e5 pang-chan7.  Ho2 e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a lang5 ko2-jian5 kau3 ti7 i e5 sin-piN lah.
Hit jit8 sou2 keng-ke3 ki5-koai3 e5 su7, i u7 sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “U7 chit8 cha2-khi2, Su3-si7 tit8-tit8 chau2 lai5, i toa7 siaN hoah kong2, “Eng-kok lang5, chit8 e5 Eng-kok lang5, goa2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 i, i koaN2-kin2 chau2 chin3-cheng5 beh khi3 tan2-chih i.”
Hit si5 Li2-un-su-teng u7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 tin7 lang5, kiaN5 siong7 thau5-cheng5 e5, kiah8 chit8 ki Bi2-kok ki5,ti7 i au7-bin7 hiah e5 lang5 teh kng kui pau e5 mih8-kiaN7.  
 
Iah8 u7 tiaN2, iah8 u7 khaN, iah8 u7 te5-kou2, u7 pou3-piN5, kap choe7-choe7 pat8 hang7 e5 mih8.  Li2-un-su-teng sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “Chit e5 lu2-heng5-ka chin-chiaN3 oe7-hiau2-tit hiong2-hok.  I bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 teh kan-khou2.”

Li2-un-su-teng kap Su7-tan-le5
Li2-un-su-teng ti7 lai7-soaN teh kan-khou2, hit-si5 Su7-tan-le5 i2-keng kau3 A-hui-li7-ka.  I e5 bok8-tek si7 beh khi3 kau3 Than-gan5-i-ka.  I2-goa7 i e5 sim-koaN bo5 khng3 pat8-hang7.  Kan-ta ti3-i3 beh chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng.  SiaN2-su7 beh khi3 chhe7 i?  In-ui7 i u7 siu7-tioh8 beng7-leng7, kong2, “Chiau3 li2 ka-ki7 khi3 phah-sng3, chong2-sil7i tioh8 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng.”  Che si7 i e5 thau5-ke, Niu2-iok Sin-bun5-sia7-tiuN2 Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok (James Gordon Bennett), sou2 kau-tai3 i e5 oe7.  I an2-ni beng7-leng7, si7 in-ui7 choe7-choe7 ni5 ku2 long2 bo5 lang5 chai Li2-un-su-teng ti7 tah-loh8.  Bo5 lang5 chai i tau3-ti2 si7 si2,a2-si7 oah8.  U7 lang5 phah-sng3 i iau2 oah8 ti7-teh.  U7 lang5 kong2 i i2-keng si2-liau2 lah.  Chit e5 bun7-toe5 chin choe7 lang5 teh gi7-lun7.  Koh u7 chit8 khoan2 e5 lang5 phah-sngi3 iau2 oah8 ti7 lai7-soaN, put-ko3 si7 chhe7 bo5 lou7, boe7-tit chhut-lai5.  Gi7-lun7 chit khoan2 e5 su7, put-tan3 ti7 i e5 pun2-kok, a2-si7 ti7 kau3-hoe7-lai7, chiu7 ti7 thong Au-chiu, Bi2-chiu, a2-si7 thong se3-kai3, ia7 u7 lang5 siong5-siong5 teh pian7-lun7 chit e5 su7.  Khah choe7 lang5 siuN7 i i2-keng si2-liau2 chin ku2 lah.  In-ui7 choe7-choe7 ni5 ku2 long2 boi5 e5 siau-sit.
Na7-si7 ti7 Bi2-kok u7 chit8 e5 lang5 m7-sin3 i u7 si2.  I sin3 Li2-un-su-teng iau2 oah8 ti7-teh.  Put-ko3 si7 be5-lou7 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka e5 soaN-lai7, boe7-tit thang chhut-lai5.  Sou2-i2 m7-thang ian5-chhian5, tioh8 kin2 phai3 lang5 khi3 chhe7 i.  Chit e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok, Niu2-iok chhi7 chit8 e5 toa7 sin-bun5-sia7 e5 sia7-tiuN2.
I siong5-siong5 teh siuN7 kong2, na7-si7 iau2 oah8, tek-khak tioh8 phai3 lang5 khi3 chhe7 i, soa3 thang chhoa7 i to3-lai5.  Sui-jian5 sou2-hui3 tioh8 khai chin choe7, ia7 si7 tioh8 eng7.  Chit tiau5 sou2-hui3 i hoaN-hi2 beh ka-ki7 hu7-tam.
Sim-lai7 i2-keng an2-ni koat-teng7, Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok sui5-si5 kong3 tian7-po3 beng7-leng7 i e5 ki3-chia2 Su7-tan-le5, tioh8 kin2-kin2 li7-khui Se-pan-ga5 kok, khi3 Pa-li2 chhe7 i.  Su7-tan-le5 kau3 ui7 e5 si5, Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok i2-keng ti7 lu2-koan2 teh khun3.  Su7-tan-le5 toa7 lat8 ka7 i kha3-mng5.  Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok mng7 kong2, “Li2 si7 siaN2 lang5?” In3 kong2, “Goa2 si7 Su7-tan-le5.” Koh kong2, “Jip8-lai5 che7.  Goa2 u7 iau3-kin2 e5 tai7-chi3 beh kap li2 chham-siong5.  Li2 siuN7, Li2-un-su-teng chit-tiap8 ti7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7?”  In3 kong2, “Goa2 m7-chai.” Koh mng7 kong2, “Li2 siuN7 i iau2 oah8, bo5?” In3 kong2, “Kiam2-chhai2 iau2 oah8, kiam2-chhai2 si2 lah.” Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok kong2, “Goa2 sin3 i iau2 oah8.  Na7 u7 lang5 khi3 chhe7, i tek-khak ti7-teh.  Goa2 beh phai3 li2 khi3 chhe7 i.”
Su7-tan-le5 thiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, sim put-chi2 gong5-ngiah8, chiu7 in3 kong2, “Goa2, li2 chiaN-sit8 sin3 goa2 chhe7 i oe7 tioh8.  Li2 kiam2 u7-iaN2 ai3 goa2 khi3 A-hui-li7-ka ?”
Be7-ni5 Nga2-kok toa7 siaN in3 kong2, “Si7, goa2 ai3 li2 khi3 chhe7 i.  Siong7-bo5, li2 ia7 oe7 khi3 tham3-thiaN i e5 siau-sit.  TaN i i2-keng lau7 lah.  Kiam2-chhai2 i chin khiam3-kheh.  Li2 tioh8 toa3 khah choe7 mih8 khi3 hou7 i.  Ban7-su7 kau-tai3 li2 khi3 siat-hoat, li2 chiah ka-ki7 phah-sng3.  Chong2-si7 li2 tioh8 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng.”
Hut-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 beng7-leng7, Su7-tan-le5 bo5 m7 toa7-toa7 gong5-ngiah8.  I tui3 Sin-bun5-sia7-tiuN2 kong2, “Beh khi3 hiah hng7, lou7-hui3 tioh8 chin choe7.  M7-chai li2 u7 chim-chiok siuN7 a2 bo5 ?” In3 kong2, “HouN3, na7-si7 an2-ni, li2 tai7-seng khi3 gun5-hang5 nia2 chit8-ban7 gun5.  Chit ban7 gun5 na7 eng7 liau2,chiah koh khi3 nia2 chit8-ban7.  Chit nng7-ban7 khou na7 eng7 liau2, koh khi3 nia2 chit8-ban7.  Tit8-tit8 nia2 chiu7-si7.  Chong2-si7 li2 tioh8 khi3 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng.”
 
Sui-bong2 Su7-tan-le5 si7 chit8 e5 put-chi2 iong2-kam2 e5 lang5.  I bat kiaN5-chun5, bat choe3 peng, ia7 bat choe3 sin-bun5 ki3-chia2 ti7 chian3-tiuN5-nih.  M7-ku2 beh khi3 A-hui-li7-ka hiah khoah e5 sou2-chai7; chhiu7-na5 hiah am7,chhiN-hoan hiah-kiap, lou7-thau5 hiah oan2-siap8, beh-thai3 oe7 hiah khoai3 sui5-pian7 khi3 chhe7 chit8 e5 lang5.
A-hui-li7-ka sng3 si7 chit8 e5 toa7 chiu.  Li2-un-su-teng siat-su2 na7 iau2 oah8, beh thai3-oe7 chai i ti7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7.  I si7 ti7 lam5, a2-si7 ti7 pak, si7 ti7 tang, a2-si7 ti7 sai,che ma7-si7 bo5 poaN3 lang5 chai.  Tui3 hai2-khau2 kau3 lai7-soaN, lou7-cham7 si7 kang2 chheng li2.  Beh chhe7 chit8 e5 lang5 kiam2 si7 hiah khoai3.  Che su7 si7 oh lah !  Sit8-chai7 si7 na2 ti7 hai2-toe2 teh bong chiam.
Iau2-ku2 Su7-tan-le5 long2 bo5 the-si5, Sia7-tiuN2 e5 beng7-leng7 i tek-khak beh than3.  Tui3 an2-ni i siat-ke3, chun2-pi7 lou7-tiong sou2 tioh8 eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  I ia7 bou7-chip8 lang5 tau3 un7-poaN chiah e5 mih8.
Ti7 ji7 geh8 chap8-poeh jit8 (1871 ni5), i tui3 A-hui-li7-ka e5 tang hai2-hoaN7 khi2-sin beh jip8 lai7-soaN.  Hap8- kiong7 u7 200 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 khi3.  Tiong-kan u7 nng7 e5 si7 Eng-kok lang5.   Chit nng7 lang5 long2 bat kiaN5 chun5, ia7 bat choe3 chui2-siu2.
Su7-tan-le5 ia7 toa3 choe7-choe7 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  I2-goa7 u7 kui2-na7 chiah be2, u7 lu5-a2, ia7 u7 sio2-chun5.  Ti7 hit si5-tai7 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5 na7 ai3 jip8 A-hui-li7-ka e5 lai7-soaN, boe7 bian2-tit e7 tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 hong-hiam2, chhin-chhiuN7 Li2-un-su-teng sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 khoan2-sit.  Su7-tan-le5 ia7-si7 boe7 bian2-tit an2-ni.
In ti7 lou7-nih e5 si5, ti7 chit nng7 e5 Eng-kok chui2-siu2 e5 tiong-kan, chit8 e5 sit-loh8 i e5 siN3-mia7, koh chit8 e5 in-ui7 ti7 lou7-nih tu2-tioh8 kan-khou2, i e5 seng-khu tong3-boe7-tiau5, sou2-i2 kou-put-chiong chiah koh-chai3 to3-tng2-khi3.  TaN chhun Su7-tan-le5 pun2-sin, kap hiah e5 kap i choe3-tin7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5, kiong7 u7 200 cho2-iu7 lang5.
 
In ti7 lou7-tiong tu2-tioh8 toa7-hou7 kap im toa7-chui2, koh tu2-tioh8 chin phaiN2 e5 chhiN-hoan i2-kip8 chiong2-chiong2 e5 kan-khou2.  Sui-bong2 an2-ni, in ia7-si7 tit8-tit8 chin3-cheng5.  Su7-tan-le5 koat-i3 beh chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng, ti7 i e5 sim-lai7 chhin-chhiuN7 u7 siaN teh kong2,“Tioh8 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng, tioh8 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng,kau3 si2 ia7 tioh8 chhe7 i.”  Su7-tan-le5 khng3 chit khoan2 e5 koat-i3; hou7 i it-hoat oe7 kham-tit siu7 kan-khou2.
Sui-bong2 in ti7 lou7-nih kui2-na7 geh8 jit8 ku2, koh tu2-tioh8 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 gui5-hiam2.  Chong2-si7 kau3 bein2 u7 tat8-tioh8 in e5 bok8-tek.  Ti7 10 geh8 28 jit8 (1871 ni5), in ko2-jian5 kiaN5 kau3 U5-ji7-ji5.  Chit sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 ti7 Than-gan5-i-ka e5 ou5-piN.  Ti7 poeh geh8-jit8 ku2 e5 tiong-kan, in sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 kan-khou2 kap in e5 khoa3-lu7 sit8-chai7 oh-tit kong2 kau3 liau2.  TaN Su7-tan-le5 i2-keng kau3 U5-ji7-ji5.  I choan5-jian5 bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 chia,i u7 thang kiN3-tioh8 Li2-un-su-teng, sou2-i2 chhe7-tioh8 i e5 si5, Su7-tan-le5 e5 sim-koaN hoaN-hi2 kau3 beh piak-lih8.  Poeh geh8-jit8 ku2 e5 ng3-bang7 i2-keng chiaN5 lah !  Li2-un-su-teng iau2 oah8, iau2 ti7-teh lah.
KhoaN3-kiN3 i e5 si5, Su7-tan-le5 chin ai3 toa7-siaN hoah,ai3 tio5-thiau3, ai3 kin2 chau2 chin3-cheng5 khi3 kiN3 chit e5 iong2-kam2, kou-toaN e5 lau7-lang5.  Chong2-si7 i bo5 tio5-thiau3, ia7 bo5 chhut toa7-siaN hoah.  I kan-ta toa7-pou7,toa7-pou7, kiaN5 chin3-cheng5, au7-lai5 i liu3 i e5 bo7-a2 kiaN5 le2 kong2, “Li2-un-su-teng Phok-su7, phah-sng3 si7 li2.”  In3 kong2, “ChiaN3 si7,” siang7 hit-si5 liu3 i e5 bo7-a2 kiaN5 le2.  Au7-lai5 in nng7 lang5 saN khan-chhiu2 chheng2-an.
Su7-tan-le5 koh kong2, “Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, Phok-su7 ah, I u7 iong5-un2 goa2 kiN3 li2 e5 bin7.”  In3 kong2, “U7 thang ti7 chia hoan-geng5 li2, goa2 ia7-si7 u7 kam2-sia7 e5 sou2-chai7.”
Hit jit8 Su7-tan-le5 e5 hoaN-hi2 kong2 boe7 chin7.  Ti7 hit si5-chun7 Li2-un-su-teng tiam3 ti7 chit8 keng chhau2-liau5-a2, i bo5 i2 thang che7, chi2-u7 eng7 chit8 nia2 iuN5-phe5 chhu ti7 siuN-a2 teng2, chun2 i2 lai5 teh che7.  Na7-si7 i ai3 ho2 khoan2-thai7 Su7-tan-le5, i chiong hit nia2 iuN5-phe5 tit8-tit8 beh hou7 i e5 lang5-kheh che7.
Au7-lai5 Su7-tan-le5 kong2 choe7-choe7 se3-kai3-chiuN7 e5 siau-sit hou7 i thiaN.  Kong2 liau2 chiah theh8 chit8 pou3-te7 phoe hou7 i.  Peng5-iu2, kiaN2-ji5 e5 phoe long2 ti7 hit lai7-bin7.  Thau2 khui khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 chhin-chhiu2 sia2 e5 phoe, Li2-un-su-teng bo5 m7 toa7-toa7 siu7 kam2-kek.
Su7-tan-le5 be7 lai5 e5 si5, Li2-un-su-teng sou2 u7 e5 ioh8 i2-keng eng7 liau2-liau2.  I ia7 bo5 tu2-ho2 e5 mih8 thang choe3 i e5 sit8-liau7.  Chit8 jit8 ke3 chit8 jit8, kan-ta teh iau-go7.
Sou2-i2 i siong5-siong5 tui3 Su7-tan-le5 seh-sia7 kong2, “Li2 lai5 chin2-kiu3 goa2, li2 na7 koh ban7 kui2-na7 jit8 chiah lai5,chiu7 i2-keng siuN7 ban7 lah !”
Su7-tan-le5 khoaN3 i hiah san2, hiah loan2-jiok8, koh khoaN3 i e5 saN chheng7 kau3 hiah phoa3, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 kio3 te3 i e5 lang5 phau3 sio-sio e5 ko-pi-te5 hou7 i lim.  Li2-un-su-teng chin hoaN-hi2, kong2, i ti7 kui2-na7 ni5 ku2 m7-bat chiah8-tioh8 thng5.  TaN soa3 u7 ko-pi-te5 chham thng5 thang lim.  Au7-lai5 Su7-tan-le5 chhong3 choe7-choe7 ho2-mih8 hou7 i chiah8.  Tui3 an2-ni, Li2-un-su-teng chiam7-chiam7 khah iong2.  I e5 bin7 ia7 khah ho2 sek.  Chiau3 Su7-tan-le5 sou2 kong2, Li2-un-su-teng ke siau3-lian5 chap8 he3.
TaN Li2-un-su-teng ke chit8 e5 tang5-phoaN7.  I khah boe7 bo5-liau5.  In nng7 lang5 siong5-siong5 choe3-tin7 khi3 lai7-soaN e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 lou7-tiong in tu2-tioh8 kui2-na7 pai2 e5 toa7 gui5-hiam2.  In-ui7 hit toe7-hng e5 lang5 chin kiong5.  Na7 bo5 chin soe3-ji7, chhut-goa7 lang5 tui3 in hia ke3, siong5-siong5 oe7 hou7 in thai5--khi3.
Khi2-thau5 Su7-tan-le5 phah-sng3 Li2-un-su-teng kaN2 sich7…t e5 sim-koaN oeh8-oeh8 kap kou3-chip e5 lang5.  Kap i choe3-tin7 khah ku2, chiah chai i si7 chit8 e5 chin gau5,chin u7 ti3-sek e5 lang5.  Koh choan-choan si7 ai3 li7-ek ti7 pat8 lang5.  I ia7 si7 chit8 e5 jiat8-sim e5 Ki-tok-tou5.  Tui3 an2-ni Su7-tan-le5 tit-tioh8 toa7 kam2-tong7.  Tui3 taN liau2-au7, i khoaN3 thoan5-po3 Chu2 e5 hok-im ti7 A-hui-li7-ka lang5 e5 tiong-kan, m7-si7 phah-sng2 kong-im.  Hoan2-tng2 si7 tit-chiaN5 Chu2 e5 su3-beng7.  I ia7 chin chun-keng3,chin thiaN3-thang3 Li2-un-su-teng.  Koh khoaN3 i si7 chit8e toa7 e5 jin5-but8; iong2-kam2 iu7 khiam-pi.  Koh kong2, Li2-un-su-teng chin-chiaN3 u7 chong-kau3 sim.  I sou2 choe3--e5, sit8-chai7 chin u7 hap8 ti7 to7-li2.  I u7 thiaN3-sim.  I gau5 lin5-bin2, gau5 khoan2-thai7 lang5.  I ia7 chin un-jiu5.
 
Su7-tan-le5 pun2-sin si7 chit8 e5 chin kip-seng3 e5 lang5.  Sui-bong2 sio2-khoa2 su7, ia7 si7 hou7 i khoai3-khoai3 siu7-khi3.  Na7-si7 kap Li2-un-su-teng choe3-tui, koh khoaN3 i e5 ho2 bou5-hoan7, Su7-tan-le5 ia7 put-chi2 pian3 khoan2.
I2-keng chhe7-tioh8 Li2-un-su-teng, koh kap i saN-kap choe3-tui hiah-nih ku2, Su7-tan-le5 boe7 bian2-tit tioh8 li7-khui A-hui-li7-ka, thang to3-tng2 khi3 he5-hok i e5 Sin-bun5-sia7-tiuN2, kan-siap8 i sou2 keng-ke3 it-chhe3 e5 su7.  Bel7?-piat8 e5 tai7-seng, i khou2-khng3 Li2-un-su-teng tek-khak tioh8 kap i choe3-tin7 tng2-khi3 pun2-kok; thangkap i e5 ka-chok8, i2-kip8 peng5-iu2 koh saN-kiN3.  Su7-tan-le5 chin7 i sou2 oe7 lai5 khou2-khng3 Li2-un-su-teng kong2,in-ui7 i toaN-sin ti7 lai7-soaN i2-keng lak8 ni5 ku2 lah, sin-the2 ia7 chin lam2, taN tek-khak m7-thang koh tiam3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka.  Tek-khak tioh8 kap i saN-kap choe3-tin7 to3-khi3.  I an2-ni pek-chhiat khou2-khng3 i sou2 chun-keng3 e5 Li2-un-su-teng.  ThiaN-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 oe7, Li2-un-su-teng ti3-i3 ka7 Su7-tan-le5 seh-sia7, kong2, i sou2 pai5-liat8 e5 li2-khi3 chin-chiaN3 si7 beng5-hian7.  Na7 chiau3 li2-iu5 lai5 khoaN3, i pun2-sin ia7 si7 chin ai3 to3-khi3.  In-ui7 i ti7 A-hui-li7-ka i2-keng u7 saN-chap8 ni5 ku2 lah.  Tui3 siau3-lian5 tiam3 kau3 lau7, na7-si7 i e5 sin-the2 ia7 si7 bo5 iong2-kiaN7.  U7 chit khoan2 e5 li2-iu5, chu7-jian5 i e5 sim-koaN put-si5 ia7 si7 khng3 pat8-jit8 beh kap kiaN2-ji5 saN-kiN3 e5 ng3-bang7.  M7-ku2 gi7-bu7 e5 chek-seng5 poaN5-ke3 i sim-koaN e5 i3-ai3.  “Kang iau2-be7 chiaN5.”  I siong5-siong5 an2-ni kong2, Ni5-lo5 ho5 e5 goan5-thau5 iau2-be7 chhe7-tioh8, sou2-i2 i boe7-tit thang li7-khui A-hui-li7-ka.  ThiaN-kiN3 chit khoan2 hiah koat-sim e5 oe7, Su7-tan-le5 chin-chiaN3 hoan5-lo2.  I koh kiu5, koh khou2-khng3 Li2-un-su-teng tioh8 kap i choe3-tin7 to3-khi3, kong2, i e5 kiaN2-ji5 kap i e5 peng5-iu2 long2 teh tan2.  Na7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng koh in3 kong2, in-ui7 i e5 kang iau2-be7 chiaN5, sou2-i2 i boe7-tit thang li7-khui A-hui-li7-ka.
Ko2-jian5 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang khou2-khng3 i, Su7-tan-le5 chiah tui3 i kong2, “Na7-si7 li2 tiaN7-tioh8 beh tiam3 tichia7, li2 ia7 tioh8 kap goa2 saN-kap choe3-tin7 lai5 kiaN5 chit8 cham7.  In-ui7 goa2 u7 tun2 tam7-poh8 mih8 ti7 hia.  Li2 na7 kap goa2 khi3, goa2 oe7 chiong li2 sou2 khiam3-eng7 e5 mih8 hou7 li2 thang eng7.  Li2 na7 koh tiam3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka chiu7 khah m7-bian2 khiam3-kheh.”
Ti7 12 geh8 27 jit8 (1871 ni5), in nng7 lang5 saN-kap khi2-sin, Su7-tan-le5 beh to3-tng2 kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni2, Li2-un-su-teng beh kau3 lou7-tiong chit8 sou2-chai7 kio3-choe3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2 (Unyanyembe).  In choan5-jian5 bo5 phah-sng3 lou7 oe7 hiah phaiN2, hou7-chui2 oe7 hiah kau7, ia7 lang5 oe7 tu2-tioh8 hiah choe7 khoan2 e5 chhiN-chham2.  In m7-na7 kiaN5 lou7 kau3 chin sian7, m7-na7 choe7-choe7 pai2 tu2-tioh8 jiat8-piN7, ui7-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 ian5-chhian5, in keng-ke3 nng7 geh8-jit8 ku2 chiah kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2.
Ti7 lou7-nih in thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, cheng5-pang hit e5 Eng-kok chui2-siu2 oat8 to3-tng2 beh to3-khi3, taN i i2-keng si2 ti7 poaN3-lou7.  ThiaN-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 siau-sit, Su7-tan-le5 chin toa7 hoan5-lo2 koh sit-chi3.  Chong2-si7 Li2-un-su-teng chin gau5 an-ui3 i.
Kau3 chia in sou2 toa3 e5 chiah8-mih8 ia7 long2 eng7 kau3 beh liau2.  Tui3 chit-tiap8 liau2-au7, in long2 si7 chiah8 pun2-soaN sou2 chhut e5 thou2-san2.
Ti7 1 geh8 1 jit8 (1872 ni5), Li2-un-su-teng sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “Goan7 chi3-toa7 e5 Siong7-te3 chan7 goa2 e5 khui3-lat8, hou7 goa2 oe7-tit chiaN5 goa2 sou2 tioh8 choe3 e5 gi7-bu7.”  Koh keng-ke3 chap8-chhit jit8 ku2, in an-jian5 kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, in toa7 toa7 sit-chi3,in-ui7 Su7-tan-le5 sou2 kau-tai3 hiah e5 lang5, m7-na7 bo5 chiau3-kou3 i e5 mih8, in hoan2-tng2 chiong sou2 u7 e5 mih8 ka7 i thau-theh8 kau3 beh liau2, koh-chai3 eng7 choe7-choe7 bo5-iaN2  e5 oe7 lai5 moa5-phian3 i kong2, mih8-kiaN7 si7 hou7 chhat8 thau.  Sui-jian5 tu2-tioh8 an2-ni, Su7-tan-le5 ia7 bo5 sioh ka-ki7. Siu7 Keng2-tiau3
Su7-tan-le5 kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni2 e5 si5, i sui5-si5 khi3 chio-hou gou7-chap8-chhit e5 lang5, soa3 kau-thok in chiong choe7-choe7 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8, kin2 theh8 to3-tng2 khi3 hou7 Li2-un-su-teng.  I chiong choe7-choe7 oe7 un5-un5-a2 khoan3-bian2 chiah e5 lang5.  Kio3 in tai7-ke tioh8 chin7-tiong, ia7 tioh8 sun7-than3 Li2-un-su-teng.  In-ui7 i si7 chit8 e5 ho2 tek-heng7 e5 lang5, chin kong-to7, ia7 chin gau5 thiaN3-thang3, chin gau5 khoan2-thai7 ou-bin7 lang5.
Su7-tan-le5 kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni2 hai2-khau2 e5 sou2-chai7, Li2-un-su-teng ti7 lai7-soaN, ka-ki7 chit8 e5 chin bo5-liau5.  I tiam3 ti7 Un5-ian-eng-bi2, jit8-jit8 teh tan2 Su7-tan-le5 e5 siau-sit, teh theng3-hau7 i sou2 khiam3-kheh e5 mih8-kiaN7. U7 chit8 jit8, i kiah8 pit sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3, kong2, “Kin-a2-jit8 si7 goa2 e5 siN-jit8.  Ia5-sou, Li2 si7 goa2 e5 Ong5, goa2 e5 Oah8-mia7, goa2 e5 Choan5-the2.  Goa2 koh chit8 pai2 chiong goa2 pun2-sin sou2 u7--e5, long2 hian3 hou7 Li2.  ThiaN3-thang3 e5 ThiN Pe7 ah, kiu5 Li2 chiap lap8 goa2.  Ia7 hou7 goa2 ekang5 bian2 kau3 ni5-be2 oe7-tit thang chiaN5.  Kho3 Ia5-sou e5 mia5 lai5 kiu5.  A3-beng7.  Li2-un-su-teng Tai7-pit8.”
Chiau3 i teh phah-sng3, koh si3 geh8 jit8 ku2, Su7-tan-le5 sou2 chhe e5 lang5 tek-khak oe7 kau3 i hia.  Bo5 phah-sng3 i tioh8 koh tan2 gou7 geh8 jit8 ku2.  Tan2 hiah-nih ku2,boe7 bian2-tit hou7 i chin sian7 kap chin bo5-liau5.  I koh sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3, kong2, “Ti7 goa2 bo5-liau5 e5 si5, goa2 sou2 ai3 kong2-chhut--e5, chiu7-si7 goan7 ThiN Pe7 kong3-loh8 toasi7-un hou7 ban7 lang5, bo5 lun7 si7 Bi2-kok, Eng-kok, Thou2-ni2-ki5, a2-si7 pat8-kok e5 lang5.  Goan7 in oe7-tit hou7 thong se3-kai3 thoat-li7 chit ho7 choe3 lou5-le7 e5 chhiN-chham2.”
Chiah e5 oe7, chiu7-si7 ti7 i beh ke3-ong2 hit ni5 sou2 sia2--e5.  Kau3 i ke3-sin liau2-au7, u7 chiong chiah e5 po2-poe3 e5 oe7, lai5 phah ti7 i e5 bong7-pai5.  Bong7-pai5 chiu7-si7 ti7 thong Eng-kok te7-it chhut-mia5 e5 toa7 keng pai3-tng5-lai7.  Chit keng kou2-cha2 pai3-tng5 khi2 ti7 Lun5-tun chhi7,koh si7 kok-lai7 e5 peh-siN3 te7-it tui3-tiong7--e5.
Li2-un-su-teng ti7 Un5-ian-eng-bi2 tit8-tit8 teh theng3-hau7.  Hit-si5 i u7 siu-tioh8 chit8 tiuN phoe, chiu7-si7 i e5 te7-ji7 kiaN2 sou2 sia2--e5.  Phoe-lai7 u7 kong2, i kap kui2-nal7ang u7 tui3 Eng-kok chit8 e5 Te7-li2-hoe7 siu7-phai3 khi3 A-hui-li7-ka.  In e5 bok8-tek choan-choan si7 beh khi3 chhe7 Li2-un-su-teng.  In tui3 Eng-kok chhut-hoat, au7-lai5 ko2-jian5 kau3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka e5 lai7-toe7.  Na7-si7 ti7 lou7-tiong, chhau3-tu2-kham2, tu2-tioh8 Su7-tan-le5. Li2-un-su-teng ti7 lai7-soaN siu-tioh8 i e5 kiaN2 kap ka-chok8 e5 phoe, sim-koaN kau3-kek8 hoaN-hi2. In-ui7 chai ti7 pun2-kok iau2 u7 choe7-choe7 lang5 teh siau3-liam7 i, ia7 u7 teh khoa3-lu7 i e5 seng-oah8.
Ti7 8 geh8 14 jit8, Su7-tan-le5 sou2 chhe hiah e5 lang5, kiong7 u7 gou7-chap8-chhit mia5, i2-kip8 in sou2 toa3 e5 mih8-kiaN7, long2-chong2 an-jian5 kau3-ui7. U7 chit khoan2 e5 ki-hoe7, Li2-un-su-teng sui5-si5 koh siat-pi7 beh chhoa7 chiah e5 lang5 jip8 lai7-soaN; ng3-bang7 ti7 hia oe7-tit hoat-kian3 i sou2 beh chhe7 e5 sou2-chai7.

Li2-un-su-teng jit8-jit8 tu2-tioh8 chiong2-chiong2 e5 kan-khou2.  I e5 sin-the2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 soe-lam2.  Ti7 chit tiong-kan i koh tu2-tioh8 choe3-li7 kap jiat8-piN7,ti7 lou7-tiong poeh jit8 ku2, i boe7 chiah8, ia7 boe7 thun poaN3 hang7. Hioh-khun3 e5 si5, khi3-hau7 koh pian3-chhian, mui2-jit8 jit8-thau5 na2 iam7, thiN-khi3 na2 sio-joah8, tai7-ke hou7 jit8 phak8 kau3 tong3-boe7-tiau5. Tu2-tioh8 kau3 an2-ni, Li2-un-su-teng ia7-si7 koat-i3 ai3 koh-chai3 chin3, i long2 bo5 siuN7 sian7.  Na7 oe7-tit chiaN5 i e5 kang, i sou2 tioh8 choe3 e5 sui-jian5 khah kan-khou2, i ia7-si7 oe7 jim2-nai7--tit.
Tu2-tioh8 hiah ku2 e5 kho2-oaN7, thiN-khi3 it-hoat ut-joah8.  Chong2-si7 ti7 bo5-phah-sng3 e5 si5, khi3-hau7 hut-jian5-kan koh pian3, in kiah8 thau5 chit8-e7 khoaN3, khoaN3 thiN-teng2 i2-keng teh khi2 ou-hun5.  Koh tiap-a2-ku2 toa7-hou7 tit8-tit8 loh8.  Ti7 chit e5 loh8-hou7 thiN, hou7 loh8 long2 boe7 soah.  Ti3-kau3 hia e5 piN5-iuN5 it-khai3 long2 im toa7-chui2.  Soe3-tiau5 khoe-a2 tiong3 chui2 piN3-choe3 toa7-ho5.  Sou2-i2 lang5 na7 chhut-mng5 chin bo5 li7-pian7,put-si5 tioh8 liau5 chui2.  Khah ke7 e5 sou2-chai7, boe7 kham-tit hou7 chui2 chim3, piN3-choe3 noa7-thou5, sou2-i2 lang5 na7 beh chhut-mng5 chin-chiaN3 si7 kan-khou2.
Kau3 be2 Li2-un-su-teng kou-put-chiong kau-siap8 hia e5 chai7-toe7 lang5 lai5 chhoa7 lou7.  Chong2-si7 in bo5 beh hiah khoai3 tah-eng3.  In-ui7 in e5 sim-koaN ukh7￠g phaiN2-i3.  Sui-jian5 in e5 chhui3 in3 ho2, na7-si7 in tisim7-lai7 bo5 beh sit8-i3 lai5 pang-chan7. Ti7 chit si5 hou7 loh8 koh-khah toa7, thiN-khi3 pian3 leng2, sng-hong ia7 kau3.Che hou7 in tai7-ke koh-khah sit-chi3, ia7 hou7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 sin-the2 it-hoat put-an.  TaN ni5-be2 i2-keng kun7, Kiu3-chu2-tan3 e5 jit8-chi2 ia7 teh-beh kau3, Li2-un-su-teng sui-jian5 ka-ki7 chit8 lang5 tiam3 ti7 soaN-lai7, i ia7-si7 ai3 siau3-liam7 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 un.  In-ui7 Siong7-te3 u7 chiong tok8-siN e5 KiaN2 siuN2-su3 se3-kan.  Hit jit8 Li2-un-su-teng beng7-leng7 te3 i e5 lang5 tioh8 thai5 chit8 chiah gu5, thang hou7 tai7-ke lai5 choe3 ki3-liam7. Chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 phah-sng3 ti7 hit si5 toa7-chui2 oe7 im hiah koan5.  Sui-jian5 toa7-tiau5 ho5 e5 hoaN7 ia7 long2 im kau3 bat8-bat8.  Tui3 an2-ni bo5 lang5 chai to2 chit8 tiau5 ho5 lau5 jip8 chit e5 ou5, ia7 m7-chai to2 chit8 tiau5 ho5 koh tui3 chit e5 ou5 lau5 chhut.
Ti7 3 geh8 19 jit8 (1873 ni5), hit jit8 tu2-tu2 si7 i e5 siN-jit8, i sia2 ti7 i e5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “Kam2-sia7 choan5-leng5 e5 Siong7-te3, po2-hou7 ban7-bin5 e5 Chu2.  In-ui7 Li2 u7 jiau5 goa2 e5 siN3-mia7 kau3 ti7 chit si5.  Goa2 e5 kang oe7-tit chiaN5 a2 boe7 ?  Hian7-si5 u7 choe7-choe7 hang7 teh chou2-chi2 goa2, boh8-tit hou7 Sat-tan3 khah-iaN5 goa2.  Goan7 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok pang-chan7 goa2.”
Sui-jian5 ti7 kan-khou2, hoan7-lan7 tiong, i ia7-si7 teh siau3-liam7 i e5 Chu2.  Tui3 chit tiap8 liau2-au7, i e5 sin-the2 chiam7-chiam7 na2 soe-jiok8.  In-ui7 si7 loh8-hou7 thiN, i e5 seng-khu i2-kip8 i e5 bin5-chhng5, i e5 i-chiuN5, kap i e5 heng5-li2, siong5-siong5 phah tam5.  Mi5-si5 i ia7 soa3 boe7 khun3-tit, iau2-ku2 i long2 bo5 sit-chi3.  I koh-chai3 sia2 ti7 ie5 jit8-ki3 kong2, “Ti7 se3-kan bo5 poaN3-hang7 oe7 hou7 goa2 pang3-soah goa2 eng-kai choe3 e5 kang.  Goan7 Chu2 Siong7-te3 ke-thiN goa2 e5 khui3-lat8, hou7 goa2 oe7 koh chin3-cheng5.”
Chio2-chio2 lang5 bat tu2-tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 khou2-lan7.  Kou-toaN ti7 chhim-soaN lai7, sin-the2 phoa3-piN7, si3-ui5 si7 tui3-tek8 kap ia2-siu3, siong5-siong5 iau-go7, seng-khu ia3-sian7, put-si5 ti7 gui5-hiam2 tiong, iia7-si7 koat-i3 beh choe3-chiaN5 i sou2 tioh8 choe3 e5.
Ti7 chit e5 si5-chun7, toa7-ou5 e5 chui2 pi2 i2-cheng5 koh-khah im, ou5 e5 chui2 i2-keng boan2 hoaN7 kau3 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7.  Tui3 ou5-kiN sng3 khi2, iok u7 ke im si3-chap8 Eng-li2.  Tui3 an2-ni Li2-un-su-teng ka-ki7 chhui-li2 kong2, “Bok8-koai3 Ni5-lo5 ho5 ni5-ni5 im toa7-chui2.”

Koh kau-tai3 thau5-bak8 tioh8 siong5-siong5 thoaN2 hit chang5 soaiN7-a2 chhiu7 kha, m7-thang hou7 i hoat chhau2.
Tak8 hang7 kau-tai3 ho2-se3, ti7 in be7 khi2-kiaN5 e5 tai7-seng, Oe7-lian5 Nga2-kok kap i e5 tang5-phoaN7 chhai7 nng7 ki chin koan5 koh chin toa7 e5 thiau7 ti7 hit chang5 soaiN7-a2 chhiu7 e5 siang-peng5.  Chit nng7 ki thiau7 chhai7 liau2, in koh khi3 kng chit8 ki tng5-tng5 than2-hoaiN5 e5 thiau7 lai5 teng3 ti7 hit nng7 ki thiau7 e5 teng2-bin7.  I e5 khoan2-sit tam7-poh8 chhin-chhiuN7 mng5-kheng.  I chiong che lai5 ki3-liam7 Li2-un-su-teng lou7-be2 pai2 ti7 se3-kan sou2 an-hioh e5 sou2-chai7.
Hit-tiap8 in chiah kam-goan7 khi2-sin.  In e5 bin7-hiong3 tang, in-ui7 in koat-i3 beh khi3 kau3 hng7-hngtang7-peng5 e5 toa7-iuN5.
Li2-un-su-teng be7 piat8-se3 e5 si5, hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 ui7-tioh8 thoa-boa5 kap piN7-thiaN3, tai7-ke i2-keng chin lam2-sin.  Sou2-i2 kiaN5 lou7 be7 saN jit8 ku2, in tiong-kan 
 
poan3-sou3 i2-siong7 kou-put-chiong tioh8 theng5-kha hioh-khun3 lai5 liau5-iong2.  Liau5-iong2 chit8 geh8-jit8 ku2, in e5 khui3-lat8 chiah koh hok8-to3.
Ti7 in thau5-cheng5 sou2 beh kiaN5 e5 lou7 m7-si7 peng5-siong5 e5 lou7.  In-ui7 ti7 lou7-tiong toa7 e5 chhiu7-na5 lai7 u7 choe7-choe7 noa7-noa7 lam3-lam3 u7 chui2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Kohu7 iong2-beng2 e5 tui3-tek8 chhut ke-si beh lai5 kap in su-iaN5.  Kap in teh ti2-khong3 e5 si5, tiong-kan u7 kui2-na7 lang5 sit-loh8 in e5 siN3-mia7.
Chong2-si7 tui3 chia khi2 kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2, inbo5 koh tu2-tioh8 hiah-nih phaiN2 e5 tui3-tek8.  U7 chit8 jit8,tu2-tu2 beh kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2 e5 si5, in hut-jian5-kan tu2-tioh8 Eng-kok chit8 e5 Te7-li2-hoe7 sou2 phai3 e5 tai7-piau2-chia2 kio3-choe3 Kam-a2-liong5 (Cameron).  In lai5 A-hui-li7-ka e5 bok8-tek si7 beh pang-chan7, beh chin2-kiu3 Li2-un-su-teng.
In-ui7 Kam-a2-liong5 tui3 tang-peng5 In3-tou7 iuN5 e5 hai2-hoaN7, tit8-tit8 kiaN5 kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2, sou2-i2 lou7-tiong sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 gui5-hiam2, i long2 u7 keng-giam7--tioh8.  Chhin-chhiuN7 hit sou2-chai7 hoan-sia7 tui3 hoan-sia7 ka-ki7 teh oan-ke, teh saN-thai5, Kam-a2-liong5 pun2-sin ia7 u7 khoaN3-kiN3.  Sou2-i2 i chai-iaN2 put-lun7 u7 sim2-mih8 chhut-goa7 lang5 na7 beh tui3 hia ke3, tioh8 chin soe3-ji7,in-ui7 in oe7 siong5-siong5 siu7-tioh8 tui3-tek8 e5 kong-kek.  U7-e5 ia7 bat hou7 in thai5-si2.
TaN in-ui7 lou7-nih u7 chit khoan2 e5 gui5-hiam2, sou2-i2 Kam-a2-liong5 chin7 i e5 khui3-lat8 khoan3-bian2 in boh8-tit koh kng Li2-un-su-teng e5 sin-si, khah ho2 tioh8 chiong i e5 sin-si hian7 tai5 ti7 hia.  Ia7 kong2, Li2-un-su-teng pun2-sin tek-khak ia7-si7 ai3 bai5-chong3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka.
Chong2-si7 hiah e5 u7 sin3-iong7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 bo5 beh thiaN-than3 chit khoan2 e5 oe7.  In keng3-jian5 i2-keng koat-teng7 beh kng in e5 un-jin5 kau3 In3-tou7 iuN5 e5 sou2-chai7.  Chit e5 chi3-goan7, in tek-khak bo5 hiah sui5-pian7 pang3-soah.  Koh-khah hng7, koh-khah kan-khou2, in ia7 kam-goan7.  Chong2-si7 in tiaN7-tioh8 beh kiaN5 kau3 hai2-kiN5 e5 sou2-chai7.  In thiaN3 in e5 un-jin5 Li2-un-su-teng kau3 chit khoan2.
Sou2-i2 in tai7-ke sui5-si5 koh khi2-kiaN5, in e5 sim-koaN tit8-tit8 ng3-bang7 u7 chit8 jit8, in 
 
tek-khak oe7-tit kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni2 e5 hai2-khau2.  Kho2-sioh u7 chit8 hang7 hou7 in e5 sim put-chi2 iu-bun7.  Chiu7-si7 ti7 lou7-tiong intu2-tioh8 Kam-a2-liong5 e5 si5-chun7, hit tiap8 Kam-a2-liong5 kap i e5 tang5-phoaN7 chin ti3-i3 chheng2 ai3 khoaN3 Li2-un-su-teng e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Hou7 in khoaN3-liau2, in sun7 chit e5 ki-hoe7 chiong Li2-un-su-teng sou2 u7 e5 siuN tak8 kha long2 theh8 khi3 khui.  SiuN-lai7 u7 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8 in theh8 khi3 eng7.  In sou2 choe3 e5 hou7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5 e5 sim chin kan-khou2.  In-ui7 in ai3 chiong hiah e5 mih8 choan5-pou7 tit8-tit8 theh8 kau3 Song-kip-pa-ni2.  Kau3-ui7 e5 si5, in chiah beh kau-tai3 tho3-tong3 u7 chek-jim7 e5 lang5, chiong hiah e5 mih8 it-khai3 kia3 khi3 Eng-kok hou7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 ka-chok8.  Chong2-si7 boe7-thang chiau3 sim sou2 ai3.
Ti7 lou7-tiong hou7 in te7-it khoa3-lu7--e5, chiu7-si7 ui7-tioh8 A-hui-li7-ka lang5 e5 be5-sin3.  Put-lun7 kiaN5 kau3 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7, in boe7-bian2-tit tu2-tioh8 hit toe7-hng e5 lang5 e5 hoan2-khong3.  Che si7 in-ui7 in hou7 be5-sin3 khun2-pak8 chin an5, sou2-i2 in tek-khak m7-kheng2 hou7 lang5 kng sin-si tui3 in hia ke3.  Sui-jian5 kap in u7 sek8-sai7, in ia7 bo5 beh un2-chun2.
Sou2-i2 na7 beh chioh lou7 tui3 hia ke3, na7 bo5 eng7 sim2-mih8 ke3-chhek, a2-si7 eng7 tu2-ho2 e5 hong-hoat, bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5, in tek-khak beh chou2-tong3.  Tu2-tng7 u7 chit8 pai2, in tu2-tu2 beh tui3 chit8 e5 hoan-sia7 keng-ke3, hit-si5 in khoaN3-kiN3 sia7-nih e5 lang5 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 beh piN3-bin7 e5 khoan2.  KiaN-liau2 oe7 siu7-tioh8 in e5 kong-kek, in tai7-ke kin2-kin2 chham-siong5 khoaN3 u7 sim2-mihh8o hoat-tou7 thang lai5 soah in e5 siu7-khi3 kap thoat-li7 in e5 chhiu2-thau5.
In ke2-i3 bo5 beh koh kng Li2-un-su-teng e5 koan-bok8 tui3 hia ke3.  Siat-su2 na7 u7 koh kng, ia7 si7 beh kng to3-tng2 kau3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2, beh ti7 hia ka7 i bai5-chong3.
Chong2-si7 ti7 am3-chiN7 e5 sou2-chai7, in pi3-bit8 thau2 khui pau Li2-un-su-teng hiah e5 chhiu7-phe5 kap phang5-pou3, koh-chai7 khun2 khah an5, hou7 i pi2 i2-cheng5 khah soe3 pau, chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 e5 pau-hok8 e5 khoan2, hou7 tui3-tek8 khah bo5 gi5, in chiong chit pau saN chham ti7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 heng5-li2.  Au7-lai5 in kui2-na7 lang5 lun5-liu5 ti7 hia chim-chiok kou3.
 
Chit e5 tai7-chi3 chhong3-chiaN5 liau2-au7, in sui5-si5 eng7 kui2-na7 ki chhiu7-le pak8 kui pe2, lai5 ke2 choe3 lang5 e5 sin-si.  Hit-si5 jit8 i2-keng beh loh8, sou2-i2 na7 bo5 chim-chiok khoaN3, chiu7 hit khun2 chhiu7-le khoaN3-liau2 na2 chun2 lang5 e5 heng5.
Jit8 beh am3 e5 si5, in tiong-kan lak8 e5 lang5 eng7 chin keng3-khian5 e5 khoan2-sit oa2-lai5 kng hit pau chhiu7-le.  Siang5 hit-si5 in tui3 cheng3-lang5 hoat-piau2 kong2, in si7 beh kng-khi3 Un5-ian-eng-bi2 bai5-chong3.  Kng ke3-chhiu2,ti7 chhiu7-na5 lai7 in chiah chiong hit khun2 chhiu7-leun5-un5-a2 ka7 i thau2-khui, chiah chiong hiah e5 chhiu7-le, chit8-ki chit8-ki theh8 khi3 hng7-hng7 e5 sou2-chai7 hiat-sak.  Au7-lai5 in chit8-e5 chit8-e5 kiaN5 oan-lou7, chiah koh-chai3 kap in e5 tang5-phoaN7 saN-kap chu7-chip8 ti7 in i2-cheng5 sou2 iok e5 sou2-chai7.  In eng7 chit e5 ke3-chhek lai5 thoat-li7 tui3-tek8 e5 chhiu2.  In ia7 siong5-siong5 nng3 am7-am7 e5 chhiu7-na5 kap kiaN5 oan-oat e5 lou7, lai5 siam2-pi7 in e5 tui3-tek8.  In-ui7 in kiaN5 e5 si5, m7-kaN2 siuN hian2-jian5,kiaN-liau2 oe7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3, sou2-i2 thiN na7 kng, inchiu7 chau2-khi3 chhiu7-na5 lai7 bih.  Theng3-hau7 jit8 na7 am3, in chiah koh chhut-lai5 kiaN5 am3-lou7.  Na7 m7-si7 tuiin3 gau5 jim2-nai7 chit khoan2 e5 kan-khou2, in tek-khak tam-tng boe7 khi2.
Chong2-si7 sui-jian5 kiaN5 lou7 kau3 seng-khu thiam2,kha kiaN5 kau3 chin sng, iau2-ku2 ti7 lou7-tiong in boe7 kou3-tit hioh-khun3.  In e5 sim-koaN kan-ta tit8-tit8 ng3-bang7 chiong-lai5 in tek-khak oe7 kiaN5 kau3 hai2.
1874 ni5 2 geh8, in ko2-jian5 chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3 In3-tou7 iuN5.  Ti7 hia u7 chit8 sou2-chai7 kio3-choe3 Ba7-ga5-mou5-io (Bagamoio).  In tui3 chhut-hoat e5 si5, kiaN5 kau3 Ba7-ga5-mou5-io tu2-tu2 kau2-geh8 jit8 ku2.  Kng chu2-lang5 e5 koan-bok8 kau2 geh8 jit8 ku2, keng-ke3 soaN-nia2, chhiu7-na5, lam3-thou5, toa7 ho5, chhim-khiN, koh siong5-siong5 tu2-tioh8 gui5-hiam2 kap tui3-tek8 e5 kong-kek, chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 e5 sang3-chong3, ti7 lek8-su2 tiong phah-sng3 bo5 thang chhe7.
KiaN5 kau3 Ba7-ga5-mou5-io e5 si5, in chiong Li2-un-su-teng e5 sin-si kau-tai3 hit toe7-hng e5 Eng-kok Leng2-su7. Chong2-si7 Leng2-su7 mng7 kong2, “Goa2 beh chaiN2-iuN7 thang sin3 chit pau chin-chiaN3 si7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 sin-si.  Lin2 u7 sim2-mih8 cheng3 ku3?  Na7 u7 iaN2 si7 Li2-un-su-teng, hit pau chhiaN2 thau2 khui.  Goa2 ai3 giam7 khoaN3.
 
Goa2 ai3 khoaN3 i e5 to3-chhiu2.”  In chiu7 thau2 khui, Leng2-su7 chim-chiok kiam2-cha hit ki chhiu2-kut.  Giam7-liau2, i chhut-siaN kong2, “Si7, chin-chiaN3 si7 Li2-Un-su-teng.  I e5 to3-chhiu2 iau2 u7 siong-jiah ti7-teh.  In-ui7 i cha2-si5 khi3 phah sai e5 si5, i e5 to3-chhiu2 uh73 sai ka7--tioh8.  Hiah e5 siong long2 iau2 ti7-teh.  Che si7 te7-it ho2 e5 cheng3-ku3.”  Tui3 an2-ni i hoaN-hi2 seng5-siu7.  I ia7 chin ho2 khoan2-thai7 hiah e5 ou-bin7 lang5.  Tak8 lang5 o-lo2 in e5 iong2-kam2 kap chin7-tiong.  In-ui7 tui3 1866 ni5 khi2, in chit kui2 lang5 put-si5 teh kun-sui5 Li2-un-su-teng; lian5-lian5 poeh ni5 ku2 ti7 i e5 seng-khu piN teh chiau3-kou3 i.  Siang7 hit-ni5 Li2-un-su-teng tui3 Ba7-ga5-mou5-io beh jip8-khi3 lai7-soaN e5 si5, uchin7 toa7 tin7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 kap i choe3-tin7 khi3.  Liau2-au7 u7 e5 tu2-tioh8 si2-sit, u7-e5 li7-khui--khi3.  Chong2-si7 sou2 chhun chit gou7 e5 ou-bin7 lang5 long2 bo5 pang3-sak Li2-un-su-teng.  In sit8-chai7 u7 un-cheng5.
TaN Li2-un-su-teng e5 kut-thau5 i2-keng kau-tai3 Ba7-ga5 mou5-io e5 Eng-kok Leng2-su7.  Sou2-i2 i kin2-kin2 siat-hoat beh eng7 tu2 ho2 e5 chun5 lai5 chai3 hiah e5 kut-thaukh5i kau3 Eng-kok.  In-ui7 kang2-lou7 chin hng7, chun5 ti7 4 geh8 16 jit8 chiah kau3 Eng-kok e5 lam5-peng5.  Kau3 ui7 hit jit8, chin-choe7 lang5 khi3 tan2-chih.  Tui3 chun5-nih kau3 he2-chhia-thau5, lou7-piN siang-peng5 long2 si7 lang5.  Chhi7-tiuN2 i2-kip8 choe7-choe7 chhut-mia5 e5 lang5 kap chhi7-bin5 long2 khi3 ngia5-chih.  Tai7-ke sang3 i kau3 chhia-thau5.  Au7-lai5 he2-chhia chai3 koan-bok8 kau3 Lun5-tun chhi7.  Kau3 ui7 e5 si5, kang2 ban7 lang5 oe-oe tin7-tin7 khi3 tan2-chih.  Thong Lun5-tun chhi7 long2 chiau5 chai.
Chong2-si7 kau3 te3 e5 si5, u7 hou7 i-seng chim-chiok koh giam7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 chhiu2-kut.  I-seng kong2 to3-chhiu2 hou7 sai sou2 ka7 e5 jiah long2 iau2 ti7-teh.  In-ui7 to3-chhiu2 e5 chhiu2-kut hou7 sai e5 chhui3-khi2 ka7 kau3 chhui3-chhui3, au7-lai5 kut chiap bo5 oa2, chiah e5 cheng3-ku3 long2 iau2 ti7-teh.  Sou2-i2 cheng3-lang5 kong-jin7 kong2,“Sit8-chai7 si7 Li2-un-su-teng lah!”
Chong3-sek hit jit8, thang kong2 si7 kok-chong3.  Hitjit8 chiu7-si7 4 geh8 18 jit8.
TaN beh ka7 i bai5-chong3 ti7 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7?  Beh ka7 i bai5-chong3 ti7 Eng-kok te7-it chhut-mia5 e5 le2-pai3-tng5 lai7 (Westminister Abbey).  Chit keng pai3-tng5 chiu7-si7 ti7 Lun5-tun chhi7-lai7, ia7-si7 kou2-cha2 khi2 e5 le2-pai3-tng5.  Khi2 ho2 kau3 taN oa2 u7 chit8-chheng ni5 ku2.
 
Eng-kok hong5-te3, bun5-bu2 koan-oan5, i2-kip8 kok-lai7 e5 chhut-mia5 lang5 kap kong-lo5-chia2, u7-e5 iu5-goan5 u7 bai5-chong3 ti7 chit keng pai3-tng5 lai7.  Chong2-si7 khoan2-thai7 Li2-un-su-teng u7 chin tek8-piat8.  I sou2 tai5 e5 ui7-ti3, chiu7-si7 ti7 tiong-ng, tui3 tiong-chit loh8-lai5 e5 sou2-chai7.
Khia7 ti7 bong7-piN hit jit8, u7 ong5-chhin, bun5-bukoaN2-oan5, hak8-chia2, chong-kau3-ka, kap chhin-chok8.  I2-goa7 u7 chin-choe7 lang5.  Hit-tiap8 teh iu-bun7 e5 chhin-lang5 i2-goa7, ki5-tiong u7 Su7-tan-le5, Ou5-se kap Oe7-lian5 Nga2-kok.  Oe7-lian5 Nga2-kok hit jit8 hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 chu3-bak8 khoaN3 i.  Tai7-ke sim-lai7 siuN7 kong2, “Li2 sit8-chai7 si7 chin7-tiong e5 lou5-pok8.”
Na7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng e5 li2-lek8 sit8-chai7 hou7 lan2 thang siau3-liam7.  I u7 chong-kau3 sim, i e5 sim-koaN moa2-moa2 si7 jin5-ai3.  I kek8 gau5 jim2-nai7, kek8 gau5 siu7 kan-khou2.  I ia7 chin khiam-pi, chin un-sun5, chin chhin-chhiat, chin iong2-kam2.  Ti7 gui5-hiam2 tiong i boe7 kiaN, ti7 kan-khou2 tiong i gau5 thun-lun2.  Ui7-tioh8 kek8 pin5-chian7 e5 lou5-le7 i thoe3 in siu7-khou2.  Ui7-tioh8 Thian-kok e5 su7, i ho2-taN2 thoan5-po3 hok-im.  Ki-tok chin7-tiong e5 lou5-pok8 chiu7-si7 Li2-un-su-teng.
