Gou5 Teng5-hong Thoan5-to7 e5 Sio2-toan7
Kai Joe7-liam5
Gou5 Teng5-hong Thoan5-to7 e5 pun2 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 Bang2-kah.  I ti7 Beng5-ti7 3 ni5, 10 geh8 24 jit8 chhut-si3.  Soe3-han3 e5 si5 u7 thak8 Han3-oh8; kau3 khah toa7 han3 chin jin7-chin gian2-kiu3 Khong-chu2 chheh.  Kau3 15 he3 hit-si5 Hoat-kok lai5 phah Tai5-oan5(Se-a2-hoan2) ti7 toe7-hng choe7-choe7 sou2-chai7 put-chi2 jiau2-loan7.  Ti7 Bang2-kah hiah e5 Sam-tai7-seng3(Ng5, Lim5, Gou5) toa7 khun2-tiok8 kau3-hoe7, kong2, na7 u7 Le2-pai3-tng5 chiu7 bo5 Sam-tai7-seng3, na7 u7 Sam-tai7-seng3 chiu7 bo5 le2-pai3-tng5.  Peh-siN3 toa7 jiau2-loan7 beh thiah Le2-pai3-tng5.  Hit-si5 Gou5 Teng5-hong phah-lo5 ti7 koe-lou7, chhut-lat8 kio3 lang5 lai5 thiah pai3-tng5.  Bo5-ku2 kui2-na7 chheng lang5 kiah8 ke-si, lai5 thiah pai3-tng5.
Keng-ke3 kui2-na7 ni5 ku2, Gou5 Teng5-hong chiu7 khi3 kiaN5 ge5-mng5, i2-kip8 choe3 Sian-siN.  Kau3 Beng5-ti7 29 ni5 4 geh8,I poaN khi3 Sek-khau2 (Siong5-san) choe3 sian-siN.  Ti7 hia Sek-khau2 e5 Thoan5-to7 Tan5 Leng5 in2-chhoa7 i lai5 chiu7-kun7 Chu2.  Tui3-tek8 piN3-choe3 peng5-iu2, Khong2-chu2 e5 hak8-seng chiaN5-choe3 Ki-tok e5 chhin-peng.  Gou5 Teng5-hong Sian-siN chhe7-tioh8 oah8-mia7 e5 lou7 koh thiaN-kiN3 Ki-tok e5 siaN kong2, “Te3 goa2,” i chu7 pang3-sak it-chhe3 lai5 te3 Chu2.
U7 chit8 jit8 chha-put-to 32 ni5 cheng5, Tan5 Leng5 Thoan5-to7 chhoa7 i kau3 Bang2-kah Le2-pai3-tng5, kong2 u7 chit8 e5 thak8-chheh-lang5 him-bou7 ai3 jip8 Sin5-hak8 thak8-chheh.  Hit jit8 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 tu2-tu2 ti7 Bang2-kah, goa2 ia7 ti7-teh.  Tan5 Leng5 Thoan5-to7 kong2, “kai Bok8-su, chit ui7 sian-siN ai3 choe3 thoaN5-to7, kun7-lai5 siong5-siong5 lai5 Sek-khau2 le2-pai3.”  Gou5 Sian-siN chheng chit8 nia2 chhiN2-saN, ti3 oaN2-bo7 che7 ti7 i2-nih8, bak8-chiu kim-kim khoaN3 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7.  I chiu7 kap i kong2 choe7-choe7 oe7, soa3 mng7 kong2, “Li2 siuN7 ai3 choe3 thoan5-to7?  Gou5 Sian-siN in3 kong2. “Kai Bok8-su, goa2 beh choe3 thoan5-to7 kau3 chit8-si3-lang5.” Siang7 hit ni5, 1897, Gou5 Sian-siN jip8 Tam7-chui2 Sin5-hak8 choe3 hak8-seng.
 
In-ui7 si7 thak8-chheh-lang5, han3 bun5 i bat put-chi2 chhim, i ia7 chin gau5 sia2-ji7.  Ti7 Tai5-oan5 chin-choe7 lang5 chin him-bou7 i e5 ji7.  
Ti7 1898 ni5, 4 geh8 22 jit8, phai3 i khi3 Chu2-tng2-kha choe3 thoan5-to7.  Ti7 hia kui2-na7 ni5 ku2 choe3 kang, kap tai7-ke put-chi2 chhin-chhiat.  Au7-lai5 khi3 tiam3 Tho5-hng5, Sin-tek, Keo-lang5, Pak-tau5, Su7-lim5 chiah e5 le2-pai3-tng5.  Bo5-lun7 khi3 sim2-mih8 sou2-chai7 i kap cheng3-lang5 chin khoai3 sek8-sai7, in-ui7 i gau5 kau-poe5 peng5-iu2.
Ti7 chit 30 ni5 koh 3 geh8 jit8 ku2, Gou5 Thoan5-to7 put-chi2 un-khun5 choe3 Chu2 e5 kang.  In-ui7 i chin ho2 khau2-chai5, siaN-im ho2, koh oah8-tang7, tui3 an2-ni choe7-choe7 lang5 ai3 thiaN i e5 ian2-soat.  I ia7 chin ai3 po3-iong5 Chu2 e5 hok-im; long2 boe7 ia3-sian7, na7 chhiaN2 i ian2-kang2 i m7-bat the-si5.
Ti7 chit kui2 ni5 e5 tiong-kan i tim3 ti7 Su7-lim5 choe3 thoan5-to7, chong2-si7 mui2 le2-pai3 i ia7 chhut-lat8 pou3-to7 ti7 E7-kot-hu2-teng Le2-pai3-tng5.  Lou7-be2 pai2 soat-kau3 ia7-si7 ti7 hia.   Hit am3 i chiong “Ai3” choe3 toe5-bak8.  Theh hun2-pit sia2 chit8 ji7 “Ai3” ti7 ou-pang nih8.  Sin-the2 sui-jian5 tam7-poh8 loan2-jiok8, i ia7-si7 chin7 i sou2 oe7 e5 lai5 thoan5. Hit-jit8 chiu7-si 6 geh8 5 jit8.  Kau3 6 geh8 11 jit8 e7-pou 4 tiam2 cheng hut-jian5 hou7 siong7-chu2 keng2-tiau3.  Hit cha2-khi2 i e5 seng-khu bo5 piN7-thiaN3.  Kau3 e7-tau3 iau2 kap ke-lai7 che7-toh, au7-lai5 khi3 the, hioh-khun3, koh bo5 loa7-ku2 ThiN-pe7 keng2-tiau3 i; hiong2-siu7 60 he3.
Ti7 6 geh8 18 jit8, e7-pou 2 tiam2 cheng ti7 Su7-lim5 Le2-pai3-tng5 u7 choe3 chong3-sek.
