Kou3 Li2 Tek TiuN2-lo2 e5 sio2-toan7
Sia7 Hok-goan5
Kou3 Li2 Tek TiuN2-lo2 si7 Kou3 Li2 Kiong Sian-siN e5 te7-ji7 kiaN2, Chu2-au7 1878 ni5 chiaN-goeh8 4 jit8 chhut-si3 ti7 Sia-a2 Lun7-a2-teng2.  Siang7 hit ni5 Lun7-a2-teng2 e5 pai3-tng5 ti7 4 goeh8 14 jit8 ia7 chhut-si3.  Ti7 Chu2-au7 1936 ni5 12 goeh8 7 jit8, ngou2-chian5 5 tiam2, chiu7 siu7 Chu2 e5 seng3-tiau3 li7-khui se3-kan, hiong2-siu7 59 he3.
Ti7 1888 ni5, Li2 TiuN2-lo2 10 he3 e5 si5, chiu7 tui3 Tan5 Eng5-hui Bok8-su siu7 soe2-le2, au7-lai5 chiap-lap8 Seng3-chhan.  Tui3 soe3-han3 e5 si5 siu7 ka-teng5 e5 chong-kau3 kau3-iok8, sou2-i2 ti7 it-seng chin thiaN3-thang3 Siong7-te3 e5 seng3-hoe7.
Hian7-si5, i u7 lau5 saN ui7 kong-chu2 kap saN ui7 e5 chhian-kim.  Lun7-khi2 i e5 gau5 liau7-li2 ka-teng5, a2-si7 thiaN3 Siong7-te3 e5 seng3-hoe7, toa7-toa7 choe3 hian7-tai7 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 bou5-hoan7.
Tiong2-lam5 Chin3-seng Sian-siN, u7 chut-giap8 Su-hoan7 hak8-hau7, chiu7 siu7 jim7-beng7 choe3 Kong-hak8-hau7 e5 Sian-siN, ia7 bat choe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 chip-su7, hian7-si5 hong-chit ti7 Li7-tek8-kan2 Kong-hak8-hau7.
Si3-lam5 I2-lok8 hiaN, u7 chut-giap8 Tai5-pak ji7-tiong, soa3-chiap jip Ko-teng2 hak8-hau7, in-ui7 sin-the2 loan2-jiok8 e5 ian5-kou3, chiu7 the3-hau7, ti7 ka-teng5 teh pang-chan7 ka-su7.
Gou7-lam5 Nga2-kok hiaN, u7 chut-giap8 Gi5-lan5 Long5-lim5 hak8-hau7, chiu7 hun3-hoat ai3 ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang, jip8 Tai5-pak Sin5 Hak8-hau7.
Tiong2-lu2 Sim-chu5 chi2, u7 pit-giap8 Tam7-chui2 Lu2 Hak8-iN, au7-lai5 chhut-ke3 Te5-siong kong-hoe7 li2-su7, Bang2-kah Chhoa3 Thian-chu3 Chip-su7, hian7-chai7 u7 saN-ui7 kong-chu2 long2 jip8 tiong-teng2 hak8-hau7.
Sam-lu2 Chhun-chi2, u7 pit-giap8 Tam7-chui2 Lu2 Hak8-iN, chiu7 u7 jip8 Tai5-pak PiN7-iN7 Chou7-san2-hu7 kang2-sip8-sou2, pit-giap8 liau2-au7, chiu7 ke3 hian7-hak8 Ko-hiong5 chiu ki-su Tan5 Chui2-seng Sian-siN, ia7 hian7-chai7 khai-giap8 San2-po5 ti7 Kong-hiong5-chhi7, put-chi2 siu7 it-poaN e5 san2-ka hoan-geng5.
Si3-lu2 Chhiu-lan5 chi2, pit-giap8 Tai5-pak Te7-saN ko-lu2 Su-hoan7-kho, chiu7 jim7-beng7 choe3 Kong hak8-hau7 e5 Sian-siN, au7-lai5 chiu7 chhut-ke3 ti7 hian7-jim7 Gi5-lan5 Bok8-su TiN7 Chhong-kok Sian-siN.
Li2 Tek TiuN2-lo2 it-seng chin7-lat8 ti7 chu2-lu2 e5 kau3-iok8, put-si5 i e5 lo5-khou2, tui3 siau3-lian5 chiu7 khun5-khun5 choe3 kang, ia7 put-chi2 chiat-khiam7, put-lun7 chaiN2-iuN7 si7 ai3 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 tit-tioh8 kau3-iok8 thang lai5 li7-ek sia7-hoe7, i2-kip8 Siong7-te3 e5 seng3-kok.
Ti7 Chu2-au7 1924 ni5 3 goeh8 30 jit8, siu7 hoe7-iu2 e5 kong-ku2, siat-lip8 choe3 SaN-kak-pou kau3-hoe7 e5 TiuN2-lo2, lek8-jim7 choe7-choe7 ni5, ia7 bat siu7-phai3 choe3 Tai7-gi7 TiuN2-lo2, put-chi2 ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 lai5 hi-seng.
Li2 TiuN2-lo2 put-chi2 ho2-taN2 kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 Hok-im, lai5 chhian2-beng5 to7-li2, ia7 put-chi2 choe7 pai2 kap lang5 pian7-lun7.  U7 chit8 pai2 kap chit8 e5 Chin Ia5-sou-kau3 e5 lang5 pian7-lun7, in-ui7 i siong5-siong5 beh lai5 in2-iu2 in e5 ke.  Sou2-i2 kong2 Siong7-te3 si7 chit8 e5, Ki-tok si7 chit8 e5, mih-sai2 kong2 lin2 e5 chiah si7 chin, goan2 e5 si7 ke2--e5, tok8-tok8 tioh8 siuN7, goa2 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 kiaN2 a2 m7-si7 ? Tioh8 sit8-chai7 lan2 e5 choe7 u7 hou7 Chu2 e5 po2-huih lai5 soe2-chheng choe3 iau3-kin2.  Ia7 tui3 Siong7 Chu2 tioh8 u7 keng3-khian5.  I tak8 jit8 khun5-khun5 choe3 kang, na7 kau3 Siong7-te3 e5 seng3-jit8, chiu7 tek-khak hioh-khun3, na7 m7-si7 tu2-tioh8 kek8 toa7 e5 chou2-tong3, tek-khak kau3 pai3-tng5.  Sui-jian5 sin-the2 tui3 khah cha2 chiu7 u7 chit e5 ui7-piN7, iau2-ku2 choe3 kang, a2-si7 hok8-sai7 Siong7 Chu2 bo5 lang3.  U7 kui2-na7 pai2 ui7-thiaN3, iau2-ku2 kau3 pai3-tng5, ia7 cheng7-cheng7 thiaN to7-li2.  Tak8 pai2 long2 khah cha2 kau3 pai3-tng5, chiu7 ti7-hia hian-khui Seng3-chheh lai5 thak8.
 
Ti7 1936 ni5 12 goeh8 6 jit8 e5 seng3-jit8 iau2-ku2 kau3 pai3-tng5, ti7 hit e5-hng iau2 teh kong2 kin-e5-hng beh u7 ka-teng5 le2-pai3, chiu7-si7 lau2 tau, kau3 hit am3 tai7-iok chit8 tiam2 cheng e5 si5, u7 khah kan-khou2, chiu7 ai 2, 3 siaN khah toa7 siaN, chiu7 tui3 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kong2, goa2 hian7-si5 chin kan-khou2, goa2 e5 thiaN3 u7 thiu thau3 kah-chiah sim khi3, ia7 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 chiu7 chin7 sou2 oe7 e5 lai5 ka7 i chiau3-kou3, i chiu7 ki5-to2, “Chu2 Ia5-sou ah ! Goa2 hian7-si5 e5 sin-the2 thang3-thiaN3kau3-kek8, na7-si7 Li2 e5 chi2-i3, kiu5 Li2 hou7 goa2 an-jian5 li7-khui chit e5 kan-khou2 e5 se3-kan.  Chu2 ah ! Kiu5 Li2 tioh8 chiap-lap8 goa2 e5 leng5-hun5......”  Au7 lai5 chiu7 khah ho2.  Kau3 ngou2-chian5 5 tiam2, chiu7 siu7 Chu2 e5 seng3-tiau3, khoaN3 i e5 bin7-iong5 bo5 chit8-sut-a2 e5 iu-khou2, chin chhio3-iong5 ia7 bo5 sit-sek.
Ti7 12 geh8 10 jit8 ngou2-chian5 9 tiam2-poaN3, tui3 Tan5 Khoe-chun3 Bok8-su e5 su-sek, ti7 ka-ki7 e5 chhu3 ku2-heng5 seng7-tai7 e5 ko3-piat8-sek.  Hoe7-chiong3 tai7-iok oa2 200 mia5, tak8 lang5 bo5 m7 ui7-tioh8 i e5 si2 lai5 ai-siong.  Sit8-chai7 Li2 Tek TiuN2-lo2 put-lun7 tui3 lai7, tui3 goa7 long2 hou7 lang5 o-lo2.  Sit-loh8 Li2 Tek TiuN2-lo2, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 sit-loh8 kau3-hoe7 tiong7-iau3 e5 niu5-thiau7.
Goan7 Siong7 Chu2 pang-chan7 i e5 ui5-chok8, eng2 kap in bo5 li5, lai5 an-ui3 in.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 624 ho7,1937 ni5 3 geh8)
