Phin2-heng7 toan-chong, jiat8-sim sin3 Chu2, Ti7 An-hioh-jit8 siong5-siong5 am3-chiN7 khi3 Tai5-lam5 kin7-hu7 e5 chi-hoe7 cho3 chu2-li2, chhoa7 hiaN-ti7 le2-pai3.
