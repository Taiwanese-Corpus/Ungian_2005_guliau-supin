Sio2-toan7 TiN7 Teng-thiam
Iap8 Kim-bok8
TiN7 Teng-thiam si7 Ba5-u5 Kau3-hoe7 e5 TiuN2-lo2, I sin3 to7-li2 u7 chap8-goa7-ni5 ku2; tui3 siau3-lian5 u7 chiah8 a-phian3 kau3 gou7-chap8-goa7 he3.  I u7 chit8-pai2 lai5 Ba5-u5 khoaN2 hi3, tu2-tioh8 Tan5 Ki5-siong5 Sian-siN kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i thiaN; i chiu7 hoaN-hi2 lai5 sin3, soa3 kam-goan7 koe2 a-phian3, tui3 oa2-kho3 Siong7-te3 eng7 ki5-to2, bo5 oa2-kho3 ioh8.
Tui3 hit-tiap8 liau2-au7 le2-pai3-jit8 tu5 phao3-piN7 i2-goa7 m7-bat hioh-khun3, tui3 kau3-hoe7 bo5 lun7 hian3-kang, a2-si7 hian3-kim long2 chin kam-goan7, tak8-pai2 na7 u7 Pou3-to7-hoe7 a2-si7 Ian2-soat-hoe7 i tui3 cha2-khi2 tioh8 kau3 pai3-tng5 hoat8-loh8 boe2-chhai3, sau3 lai7-goa7, choaN-te5, sit8-chai7 chin kam2-sim, koh i chin chun-keng3 thoan5-to7-li2 e5 lang5, m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 i u7 hiam5 thoan5-to7 e5 si7-hui.
I tui3 ku7-ni5 phoa3-piN7, sin-the2 loan-jiok8, boe7 kau3 pai3-tng5, iau2-ku2 put-si5 teh khoa3-lu7 kau3-hoe7 e5 su7, ti7 bin5-chhng5-lai7 put-si5 kap Siong7-te3 kau-poe5.  Goan2 teng2-pang khi3 pou3-to7 u7 kap chiah e5 Thoan5-to7 khi3 tham3 i; u7 ka7 i kong2 u7 tit-tioh8 sin e5 Chip-su7, Keh Chhun-siu7 put-chi2 jiat8-sim teh pang-chan7 kau3-hoe7 e5 su7.  I chin hoaN-hi2, kong2, “Goa2 ku2-ku2 tui3 Siong7-te3 tho2, taN tioh8 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3;” ia7 kong2 i kin-ni5 hoaN- hi2 toe5 50 khou choe3 ni5-ian5, koh bian2-kiong2 peh-khi2-lai5 kap goan2 saN-kap ki5-to2.  Koh ti7 saN le2-pai3 cheng5 Tan5 Hong-pun2 Thoan5-to7 u7 chio hoe7-iu2 khi3 tham3 i.  I kiN3-bin7 u7 khou2-khng3 chit8-e5 hoe7-iu2 tioh8 nia2 soe2-le2; ia7 koh kong2 Siong7-te3 chhong3-cho7 ban7-mih8, tak8-hang7 u7 i e5 siu7, ia7 u7 thoe3 lan2 pi7-pan7 siu7, chiu7-si7 ti7 Thian-tong5, lan2 tioh8 ti3-i3 beh kau3 hit e5 siu7.  Thoan5-to7 koh mng7 , kong2 “ Li2 u7 khoa3-lu7 sim2-mih8?  u7 kan-khou2 bo5”?  I kong2, “Long2 bo5; kan-ta an-sim theng3-hau7 Chu2 tiau3.”
Put-heng7 ti7 7 geh8 11 jit8 hou7 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 i to3-tng2 khi3 Pe7 tau, soah Se3-kan e5 tioh8-boa5.  I u7 saN e5 kiaN2; chit8 e5 TiN7 Chhong-kok kun, chit8-tiap8 ti7 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 teh thak8-chheh, ng3-bang7 chiong-lai5 oe7 chiaN5 lau7-pe7 e5 chi3-goan7.  Goan2 ti7 Thoan5-to7 Chong2-hoe7 tiong tit-tioh8 tian7-po3 kong2 i li7-khui Se3-kan.  Tai7-ke long2 chin siong-sim kong2 kau3-hoe7 sit-loh8 chit8 e5 tong3-liong5.  M7-ku2 ti7 ThiN-nih8 teh hoaN-hi2 ke tit-tioh8 chit8 e5 Seng3-tou5 ti7 ThiN-nih8 kap Pe7 saN-kap tiam3. Kau3 15 jit8 u7 chhut-chong3, chin-choe7 lang5 khi3 hu3.
Goa2 kan2-liok8 sia2 tam7-poh8 chiau3 sou2 chai--e5 hou7 tai7-ke oh8 i e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7, koh ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 thoe3 i e5 ke-lai7 kap Ba5-u5 e5 kau3-hoe7 lai5 ki5-to2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 485 koan3, 1925 ni5 8 geh8)
