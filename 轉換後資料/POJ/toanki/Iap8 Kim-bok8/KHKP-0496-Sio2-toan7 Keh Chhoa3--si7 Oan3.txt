Sio2-toan7 Keh Chhoa3--si7 Oan3
I e5 goa7-ke chiu7-si7 Lok8-kang2 Chhoa3 Chhi2 e5 toa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2, chap8-chhit he3 ke3 Keh Oai TiuN2-lo2.  I e5 tiong7-hu sin3 to7-li2, gou7 ni5 ku2 kap i choe3 tui3-tek8, ka-ki7 pai3 ngou2-siong7; kau3 saN-chap8-kau2 he3 e5 si5, chiah kap i e5 tiong7-hu saN-kap sin3 Hok-im.  Tui3 hit tiap8 khi2, chin ti3-i3 siu2 le2-pai3; sui-jian5 seng-khu loan2-jiok8, ia7 che7 chhia khi3 hu3; koh i ia7 chin ti3-i3 to3-tng2 khi3 Lok8-kang2 in2-chhoa7 i e5 pe7-bu2 le2-pai3.
I e5 choe3 lang5 khun5-khiam7 khek-ki2, gau5 khoan2-thai7 lang5, gau5 liau7-li2 ke-su7, chun-keng3 tiong7-hu.  Kau3 beh lim5-chiong e5 i2-cheng5, iau2-ku2 kau-tai3 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 tioh8 phau3 ke-nng7 hou7 i e5 tiong7-hu chiah8.  Khoan2-thai7 lang5 bo5 hun chhin-soe, sou2-i2 bo5-lun7 hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5 na7 kau3 i e5 chhu3, i long2 hian2-chhut i hoaN-hi2 e5 bin7, lai5 ho2 khoan2-thai7 lang5.  Sou2-i2 bat i e5 lang5 chin siau3-liam7 i.  I e5 thiaN3 kiaN2 bo5 hun-piat ta-pou cha-bou2, long2 chit8-iuN7 sim.  I u7 siN saN e5 ta-pou: toa7--e5 kap te7-ji7--e5 i2-keng ke3-ong2; te7-saN--e5, Teng2-sun7 kun, hian7-si5 ti7 KiaN-tou Tong5-chi3-sia7 Tai7-hak8.  Toa7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ke3 IuN5 ChoaN5 Chip-su7, kui2-na7 ni5 cheng5 ke3-si3.  Te7-ji7 ke3 Ng5 Theng5-chhiau kun, te7-saN ke3 Tan5 Kheng5-iau5 Bok8-su.
I chin chun-keng3 thoan5 to7-li2 e5 sian-sin: sou2-i2 i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ke3 thoan5 to7-li2 e5 lang5, i chin hoaN-hi2.  Poeh-ni5 cheng5 Kheng5-iau5 Bok8-su ti7 Gi5-lan5 thoan5 to7-li2 e5 si5, i e5 Bok8-su-niu5 phoa3-piN7 chin siong-tiong7, i chiu7 chhin-sin khi3 Gi5-lan5 chiau3-kou3 i; kau3 khah-ho2 chiu7 chhoa7 i tng2-lai5 chhu3-nih8 chiau3-kou3 i.
Put-heng7 ti7 bo5 joa7-ku2 cheng5 u7 jiam2-tioh8 hi3-piN7, u7 jip8 Tai5-tiong piN7-iN7 iok nng7 geh8-jit8 kan.  I ti7 piN7-chhng5 chin jiat8-sim, pek-chhiat ki5-to2 lai5 siu7 an-ui3.  Kau3 beh lim5-chiong kui2 jit8 cheng5, ka7 i tiong7-hu kong2, “Pai3 Siong7-te3 hun-ho5 m7-thang kio2-kan2.”  Put-heng7 ti7 5 geh8 8 jIt8 teng2-pou 9 tiam2, ti7 ka-ki7 e5 chhu3, hou7  
Chu2 keng2-tiau3 i li7-khui se3-kan, hiong2-siu7 59 he3.  I beh lim5-chiong e5 si5, tu2 i tiong7-hu pek-chhiat ki5-to2 tiong, an-jian5 siu7-tiau3 to3-tng2 Pe7 tau, hiong2-siu7 ku2-tng5 e5 hok-khi3.
Kau3 ti7 5 geh8 20 jit8 e7-pou nng7-tiam2 u7 chhut-chong3: hoe7-chong3-chia2 u7 Jit8-pun2 Ki-tok Kau3-hoe7 e5 bok8-su, kap Chiong-hoa3, Tai7-kah Kau3-hoe7 e5 Bok8-su, koh u7 kui2-na7 ui7 e5 thoan5-to7-su, i2-kip Tai5-tiong pun2-tng5 e5 tiong2-chip hoe7-iu2, kap kui2-na7 sou2-chai7 e5 hoe7-iu2, long2 chong2 u7 500 goa7 lang5, thang kong2 chin seng7-hong2.  Che si7 hian2-chhut i e5 gau5 choe3 lang5.  
Goa2 khi3 hu3 chong3-sek, u7 siu7 tioh8 in song-ka e5 ho2-i3, thok goa2 kong2 i e5 li2-lek8, chiah chiong sou2 tiau-cha sou2 chai--e5 sia2 tam7-poh8 hou7 liat8-ui7 thang siau3-liam7, ia7 thang oh8 i e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7.  ChhiaN2 liat8-ui7 soa3 thoe3 in ke-lai7 toa7-soe3 ki5-to2, hou7 in long2 oe7 chiap-soa3 i e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7, koh hou7 in tui3 Chu2 lai5 siu7 an-ui3.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 496 koan3, 1926 ni5 6 geh8)
