Kou3 Sit8-chhoan Cheng3-kiu2 Sian-seN
Lau5 Chin2-hong
Sit8-chhoan(Uemura) Sian-seN put-heng7 ti7 chiaN-goeh8 peh-jit8 ngou2-hou7 oa2 6 tiam2 teh chiah8-png7 tiong, hut-jian5 nau2 ek-hoeh, siu7 Siong7-te3 keng2-tiau3 i li7-khui se3-kan.  Hiong2-siu7 69 hoe3.
Ti7 pun2-goeh8 12 jit8 ngou2-chian5 10 tiam2 ti7 i pun2 kau3-hoe7 (Hu3-su7-kian3-teng) u7 ku2-heng5 kau3-hoe7-chong3.  Chit jit8 e5 hoe7-chong3-chia2, m7-na7 choan5-kok Jit8-pun2 Ki-tok kau3-hoe7 u7 phai3 tai7-piau2 lai5 hoe7-chong3 na7-tiaN7, i2-goa7 iau2 u7 Sian-seN seN-cheng5 sou2 kau-poe5 hoe7-goa7 e5 ti-ki2, beng5-su7, lai5-liat8-sek ia7 be7-chio2.  Put-chi2 chong3-giam5, seng7-tai7 e5 ko3-piat8-sek.
Ti7-chia lun7 Sian-seN e5 su7-giap8 loa7-nih8 teh seng5-kong, a2-si7 tui3 kau3-hoe7, sia7-hoe7, loa7-nih8 hi-seng kong3-hian3, chit-ho7 su7, m7-bian2 goa2 koh ti7 chia teng5 pai5-liat8, liat8-ui7 toa7 to-sou3 si7 peng5-sou3 chiau-chai e5 sou2-chai7.
Sian-seN ti7 hoe7-lai7 m7-bian2 kong2, chiu7 hoe7-goa7, a2-si7 khah-hng7 kau3 Au-bi2 ia7 si7 jit8-siong5 hou7 lang5 sou2 teh khim-giong2, keng3-hok8 e5 jin5-but8. Na2 chun2 ko3-piat8-sek hit-jit8 i e5 tiau3-tian7, i2-kip8 tiau3-su5 si7 hiah-nih8 che7, ti3-kau3 be7-thang it-it thak8 ti7 lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5.  Kan-ta tui3 chit-hang7 lan2 thang chhui-chhut, cho3 put-chi2 beng5 e5 cheng3-ku3.  Iu7-koh Sian-seN m7-na7 kan-ta si7 cho3 chhut-mia5 e5 hak8-chia2, koh chit8-bin7 si7 toa7 sin3-giong2-ka, hui-siong5 e5 jin5-keh-chia2, ko2-jian5 si7 leng5-kai3 e5 ui2-jin5 eng-hiong5.
Sian-seN sui si7 cheng3-lang5 sou2 kong-jin7, m7-si7 hiong5-pian7-ka, gau5 ian2-soat.  Iau2-ku2 ti7 sou2 soat2-kau3-tiong, chhai5-liau7 e5 hong-hu3, loe7-iong5 e5 chhiong-sit8, i2-kip8 u7 oah8-khi3 e5 gian5-su5, kau3-giah8 u7 thang kam2-tong7, kio3 chheN2 lang5 e5 leng5-lek8.  U7 chit8 khoan2 e5 ke3-ti3, ia7 si7 cheng3-lang5 sou2 seng5-jin7.  M7-na7 an2-ni, Sian-seN goan5-lai5 
thau5-nau2 beng5-sek, tong7-chok bin2-chiat8, put-chi2 u7 tok8-chhong3-tek e5 cheng-sin5, chin-chiaN3 thang kong2 u7 chhiau-kun5 poat8-chiong3 e5 thian-chai5.  I e5 kau3-hoe7 kin-a2-jit8 e7 hiah-nih8 toa7-keng, hiah-nih8 chin3-pou7; hoat-tian2, chit8-bin7 kiam2-chhai2 u7-iaN2 si7 hou7 chhiu2-oan2 sou2 ti3-im3, iau2-ku2 khah toa7 pou7-hun7, bo5 m7-si7 tui3 Sian-seN phau7-chiong-si2 it-koan3 e5 sin3-giong2, phun-pho ti7 tang-sai, ti3-kau3 u7-si5 be7 ki3-tit e5-hng cha2-khi2; na2-chun2 Seng3-keng kong2, “Hoan-hu3 li2 tioh8 thoan5 to7-li2, bo5-lun7 tek-si5 put tek-si5, tioh8 choan-bu7 i, eng7 pah-poaN e5 khoan-iong5 ka3-si7 lai5 chek-pi7, keng2-kai3, khoan3-bian2” (2 The5. 4:2).  Sit8-chai7 Sian-seN it-seng si7 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 tiong-pok8, in-khin5 chu5-sim e5 bok8-chia2. Seng3-keng kong2, “I2-keng cheng-chian3 ho2 e5 cheng-chian3, i2-keng chin7 phau2-chau2 e5 lou7 cham7, i2-keng siu2 sou2 sin3 e5 to7-li2”(2 The5. 4:7), chap8-hun thang cho3 lan2 sin3-chia2-kan e5 bou5-hoan7.
Am3-cheN7 siuN7 Sian-seN tui3 lan2 Tai5-to2 put-lun7 ko3-jin5, a2-si7 it-poaN, tit8-chiap a2-si7 kan3-chiap, i siong5-siong5 piau2-chhut tong5-cheng5.  Tui3 i chit8-khoan2 sui2 e5 cheng-sin5, lan2 ai3 hian2-chhut kam2-in e5 sim.  Na2-chun2 ti7 chong3-sek hit-jit8 m7-na7 goa2 chit8 lang5, chiu7 to-sou3 e5 lang5, khoaN3-tioh8 tiuN5-lai7 hit-khoan2 kong-keng2, hit-khoan2 hian7-siong7,chu7-jian5 na5-au5-kng2 tiN7, pu-ti put-kak e5 tiong-kan bak8-sai2 ia7 bo5 se3-ji7 na2 hou7 tit8-loh8.
Choe3-hou7 ng3-bang7 lan2 ti7 ki5-to2-tiong ui7-tioh8 i e5 ui5-chok8, i2-kip8 i e5 su7-giap8 (Tang-kiaN Sin5-hak8-sia7, Kau3-hoe7, Chap8-chi3-sia7) lai5 saN siau3-liam7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 479 koan3, 1925 ni5 2 geh8)
