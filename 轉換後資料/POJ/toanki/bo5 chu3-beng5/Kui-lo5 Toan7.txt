Kui-lo5 Toan7
Te7 it chiuN
Hoan7 Kui-lo5 kong2, Goan2 tau ti7 Ui-lek8-su7 kok e5 pak-si3, khia7-khi2 ti7 hai2-kiN5, chit8 keng sio2 thou5 chhu3, m7 si7 kap goan2 pe7-bu2 saN-kap tiam3, ia7 boe7 ki3-tit goan2 pe7-bu2 siN choe3 chaiN2-iuN7.     
Kau2 ni5 cheng5, u7 chit8 mi5 ti7 hai2-nih8 khi2 toa7 hong, u7 chit8 chiah toa7 chun5 tu2-tioh8 toa7 hong loe5-tioh8 chioh8-thau5 phah-phoa3. Te7 ji7 jit8 u7 pun2-toe7 lang5 khi3 khioh mih8, khoaN3-kiN3 u7 chit8 e5 chhiu2-nih8 pho7 e5 cha-bou2 kiaN2-a2 to2 ti7 hai2-piN, chha-put-to si2 lah; hit e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 chiu7-si7 goa2.  Sou2 u7 chiau3-kou3 goa2 e5 lang5 long2 im-si2 liau2, kau3 chit tiap8 ia7 bo5 lang5 chai chhoa7 goa2 khi3 tah hit chiah toa7 chun5 e5 si7 chi7-chui7, ia7 bo5 thiaN-kiN3 che7 hit chiah chun5 e5 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5.  
Hit e5 lai5 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 e5 chiu7 chiong goa2 khioh-khi2-lai5 pho7, pho7 kau3 chit8 e5 hu3- kun7 e5 hiuN-sia7, chiu7-si7 in tau; i e5 bou2 kap chhu3-piN long2 kong2, m7 tah8-tioh8 sang3 goa2 khi3 hou7 Iong2-seng-tng5, hit e5 pho7 goa2 e5, phian-phian beh siu goa2 choe3 gi7-lu2.  Hit lang5 siN3 Hoan7, sou2-i2 piau2 goa2 e5 mia5 choe3 Hoan7 Kui-lo5, goa2 chiu7 jin7 in choe3 pe7-bu2, in chin7 thiaN3 goa2, liah8 goa2 choe3 chhin-siN e5 chit8 iuN7.  In ke-nih8 pun2 u7 si3 e5 gin2-a2, chin7 soe3 e5 chiah u7 si3 he3.  Kau3 goa2 lak8 he3 e5 si5, goa2 e5 gi7-hu7 si2 lah, goa2 sui-bong2 si7 gin2-a2, ia7 tang5 goan2 gi7-bu2 kap tak8 e5 gin2-a2, long2 chin7 ai-siong. 
Chit si5 goa2 chin7 kiaN gi7-bu2 chiong goa2 khi3 hou7 Iong2-seng-tng5.  In-ui7 goan2 gi7-hu7 iau2 ti7-teh e5 si5, u7-si5 goan2 gi7-bu2 siu7-khi3, bat kong2 boe7 chhi7 goa2, hian7 si5 ke-lai7 chin7 chheng-tam7, goa2 chin7 kiaN i iau2-ku2 u7 khng3 chit ho7 i3-su3.     
Goan2 gi7-hu7 pun2 si7 choe3 khui ian5-khong3 e5 kang, kiN3-na7 choe3 chit ho7 e5 kang khah choe7 bo5 tng5 he3-siu7, in-ui7 sin-the2 boe7-oe7 khui3-oah8, khong3-lai7 u7 khng3 tok8-khi3, lang5 chin7 koe7 jiam2 piN7.  Goan2 sang3-chong3 liau2, gi7-bu2 siuN7 tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 chiah oe7 tou7 jit8, u7 sim2-mih8 hong-hoat chiah oe7 iuN2-chhi7 chit ke lang5.  Cha2 goan2 gi7-hu7 sou2 than3 e5 kang-chiN5, tu5 chiah8 chheng7 eng7 i2-goa7 bo5 sim2-mih8 chek-chu7, in-ui7 an2-ni boe7-oe7 iu5-goan5 khia7 hit keng ho2 chhu3, goan2 gi7-bu2 chiu7 kiu5 khong3-chu2 hou7 toa7-kiaN2 Hou2-ni2 tit-tioh8 chit8 e5 choe3 kang e5 sou2-chai7.  Hit e5 khong3-chu2 toa3-tioh8 goan2 gi7-hu7 ti7-teh e5 si5, si7 lau2-sit8 koh chin7 khun5-kin2 e5 lang5, chiah in2 i.  Goan2 gi7-bu2 hit tiap8 iau2-be7 khi3 kiu5 i, teh kiaN i m7 kheng2, si7 in-ui7 Hou2-ni2 tui3 soe3-han3 ti7 pun2 hiuN bo5 ho2 mia5-siaN, pin5-toaN7 koh kau7 seng3- te7, tauh8-tauh8 kap gin2-a2 phoaN7 choe3-tui, chhoa7 in kiaN5 phaiN2.  Goan2 gi7-bu2 ka7 i chhe7 chit8 e5 choe3 kang e5 sou2-chai7, ng3-bang7 i un2- tang3 oe7 koe2-piN3 i e5 seng3-phiah, jin7-chin choe3 kang, goan2 thong ke thang oa2-kho3 i ke3 jit8.    
Hit e5 te7 ji7 e5, mia5-kio3 Ma2-li7, si7 cha-bou2 kiaN2, khi3 ka7 choh-sit lang5 choe3 kang, i e5 thau5-ke-niu5 chin ho2 khoan2-thai7 i, i chiu7 cha2-khi2 am3-khun3, bo5 hiam5 kan-khou2.  Sin7 goan2 chiah e5 soe3-han3 e5, boe7-oe7 choe3 sim2-mih8, siuN7 long2 bo5 lou7.    
Hit e5 te7 saN kiaN2, mia5 kio3 Pi2-tek, si7 phoa3-siuN3 e5, tui3 soe3-han3 io siak phoa3-siuN3,  chin7 kiaN koaN5, tak8 jit8 che7 ti7 i2-a2-nih8 hang he2, a2-si7 pe5 kau3 goa7-bin7 phak8 jit8.  U7 chit8 jit8 goa2 e5 gi7-bu2 ka-ki7 che7 teh siuN7 goan2 tioh8 khi3 ta2-loh8 chiah oe7 iuN2-chhi7 chit8 ke lang5.  U7 chit8 e5 chhu3-piN thoe3 i siuN7-chhut, lai5 ka7 i kong2, Lin2 tioh8 poaN khi3 hai2-piN khia7, hiah e5 toe7 long2 bo5 lang5 chiang2-koan2.  (Hit sou2-chai7 e5 kui-ku2, chi7-chui7-a2 oe7 chit8 mi5 khi2 ho2 chhu3 lai5 tiam2 he2, hit sou2-chai7 chiu7 sng3 si7 i e5.)  Goa2 beh kap cheng3 chhu3-piN kong2 tiaN7-tioh8 jit8, khi3 ti7 hia ka7 lin2 khi2 chhu3 ui5 li5-pa, lin2 thang ti7 hia cheng3 chhai3, chhi7 gia5, ia7 thang khi3 bang7 he5 boe7, a2-si7 ka7 lang5 soe2 i-chiuN5, koh u7 Hou2-ni2 than3 e5 kang-chiN5, saN-chham eng7, goa2 siuN7 an2-ni chiu7  thang ke3 jit8.  Chiah e5 chhu3-piN long2 si7 goan2 gi7-hu7 tong-jit8 e5 he2-ki3, goan2 gi7-bu2 thiaN- liau2 chiah e5 oe7, chiu7 chiau3 in sou2 kong2 e5 khi3 pan7.  Koh ka7 goa2 kong2, Li2 
iu5-goan5 kap goan2 saN-kap khi3, pang-chan7 bang7 he5.  Koh kong2, Li2 chi2 kui2 ni5 ti7 goan2 tau, choe7-choe7 lian5-lui7 goan2, hian7 si5 choe3 gin2-a2 kang, than3 tam7-poh8-a2 chiN5, lai5 pang-chan7 goan2, ia7 si7 eng-kai e5.  Goa2 thiaN-kiN3 chiah e5 oe7, chiu7 chin ai3, taN bo5 kiaN goa2 bo5 sou2-chai7.      
In iau2 u7 chit8 e5 kiaN2, mia5 kio3 Kho-ni2, i kap goa2 teh siuN7 au7-lai5 oe7 siong5-siong5 saN- kap tiam3, chiu7 chin7 hoaN-hi2.  Goa2 tai7-seng bo5 kong2-khi2 Kho-ni2, taN beh tui3 thau5 kau3 be2 kong2 goa2 e5 tai7-chi3.  Kho-ni2 kap goa2 chin7 saN-thiaN3, i e5 chiaN3 mia5 kio3 Ko-li5-niu5, ti7 in hiah e5 gin2-a2 si7 te7 it soe3-han3 e5, toa7 goa2 saN he3, sin-khu toa7 goa2 teng5-pe7, chit8 thau5-khak ang5 thau5-mng5, toa7-han3 khah ho2 khoaN3, toa7 taN2 koh lau2-sit8.      
Pat8 lang5 long2 kong2 i bo5 ki-khiau3, na2 gong7 e5, in-ui7 i si7 ta-pou e5, boe7 hiau2-tit choe3 ke-lai7 su7; goa2 siuN7 put-lun7 sim2-mih8 lang5, bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 Kho-ni2 chiah ho2.  Goa2 iau2 oe7 ki3-tit chhu3-piN ka7 goan2 khi2 chhu3 e5 su7-cheng5, in tai7-seng chhong3-pian7 thou5-pheh8 ti7 khi2 chhu3 e5 sou2-chai7, kau3 sou2 tiaN7-tioh8 hit e5-hng, u7 ji7-chap8 goa7 lang5 lai5 khi2, goan2 long2 khia7-teh khoaN3, khoaN3-kiN3 in chit8 un5 chit8 un5 thiap8 khi2 chhiuN5, eng7 kui2 ki chha5 choe3 iN5-a2 khoa3 ti7 chhu3-teng2, phou kek8 kau7 e5 lou5-tek, chhiuN5-teng2 lau5 chit8 e5 ian-tang5, chhiuN5-kha choh chit8 e5 chau3.  In tiam3 hia khi2, goan2 lau7-bu2 chiu7 ti7 goa7-bin7 khioh chha5, theng3-hau7 in khi2-ho2, chiu7 jip8-khi3 hiaN5 he2, goan2 hiah e5 gin2-a2 ti7 goa7-bin7 khoaN3-kiN3 tui3 ian-tang5 lai7 he2-hun khi2-lai5, chiu7 tai7-ke tah chhiu2 kong2, U7 sou2-chai7 thang khia7-khi2 lah.  Hiah e5 ka7 goan2 khi2 chhu3 e5 chhu3-piN, ia7 thoe3 goan2 hoaN-hoaN hi2- hi2, tak8 lang5 si3-soaN3 to3-khi3, goan2 lau7-bu2 ka- ki7 oa2 he2 pi-siong, goa2 siuN7 boe7 chhut si7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3.    
Bin5-a2-jit8 koh u7 kui2 e5 chhu3-piN lai5, ka7 goan2 he7 mng5, iau2 u7 chit8 e5 nng7 siN3 e5 thang- a2, koaN2-kin2 chhong3 kau3 chin7 ho2, goan2 chiu7 poaN khi3 khia7.  Chit e5 chhu3 na7 u7 khi2 nng7 keng, sui-jian5 soe3 koh ke7, chiau3 goan2 gin2- a2 khoaN3-khi2-lai5 khiok si7 chin ho2, mng5- khau2 u7 kek8 toa7 e5 khang3-toe7, thit-tho5 kiaN5- kiaN5, a2-si7 phah-kiu5, that-kian3 tak8 hang7 ho2.  Goan2 lau7-bu2 boe2 chit8 tiuN he5-a2 bang7, tak8 jit8 chui2-tiN7 chui2 
kho2, kap Kho-ni2 kau3 hai2-piN, chiong bang7 pha loh8 chui2, theng3-hau7 bang7 tim5-loh8-khi3,chiah thoa khi2-lai5 hoaN7-teng2, chiong bang7-lai7 sou2 u7 e5 long2  piaN3-khi2-lai5.  Goa2 chiu7 khioh hit e5 chhiu- tng5 khah kim koh oe7 thiau3 e5, khah siong5 si7 goa2 chit8 lang5 teh chhong3 chit e5 kang, u7-si5 Pi2-tek ia7 bat lai5 chan7 goa2 keng2 kau3 moa2 kheng, goan2 lau7-bu2 chiu7 khi3 sah8, kau hou7 Kho-ni2 kap goa2 khi3 li7 ke kau2-li2 lou7 e5 sio2 siaN5 boe7.     
Ti7 he7-thiN chhiu-thiN e5 si5, siaN5-nih8 choe7-choe7 lang5 lai5 hai2-piN chhiu-chhin3, he5 chin7 ho2 boe7.  Boe7 e5 si5 Kho-ni2 koaN7 kheng-a2, goa2 toa3 au7-bin7 te3 i, in-ui7 goan2 lau7-bu2 khoaN3 goa2 pi2 Kho-ni2 khah soe3, lang5 na7 khoaN3- kiN3 tek-khak siN-khi2 lin5-bin2 e5 sim, chiu7 thang tit-tioh8 chiN5.  Hit siaN5-nih8 e5 lang5, chin-chiaN3 ke3-chiN5 hou7 goan2 liau2, koh ke theh8 koa2 hou7 goan2; chit e5 tai7-chi3 goa2 iau2 bo5 boe7 ki3-tit. 
Te7 Ji7 chiuN
Goan2 ti7 chit keng chhu3 khia7 nng7 ni5, u7 chit8 jit8 Kho-ni2 kap goa2 koaN7 chit8 kheng he5 khi3 siaN5-nih8 boe7; hit si5 si7 he7-thiN chin7 joah8, bo5 chit8 tiam2-a2 hong, hut-jian5 sai-si3 khi2 chit8 phian3 ou hun5.  Chit jit8 goan2 pha he5 chin7 choe7, sou2-i2 kheng chin7 tang7, chhut mng5 hit si5 goan2 lau7-bu2 kong2, Lin2 tioh8 boe7 liau2 chiah thang to3-lai5.    
Goan2 kiaN5 u7 poaN3-si5 ku2, chiu7 loh8 hou7, chit8 chun7 chit8 chun7 khah toa7, goan2 thong sin- khu long2 ak-tam5, m7 kaN2 khi3 siam2 hou7, thau3 hou7 jip8 siaN5, ti7 koe-nih8 that chui2 tah8 thou5, toa7 siaN kio3 boe2 he5 e5, u7 lang5 toa3 ti7 mng5- khau2, u7-e5 tui3 thang-a2-nih8 lui7-loh8-lai5 ka7 goan2 boe2 he5, bo5 loa7 ku2 chiu7 boe7 liau2.  Chiu7 khi3 ti7 lang5 e5 mng5-khau2 siau3 chiN5, chiong chiN5 pau ti7 Kho-ni2 e5 chhiu2-kun beh to3-khi3, Kho-ni2 chiong khang kheng khoa3 ti7 chioh8- thau5 teng2, phah-sng3 chiong goa2 thau5-mng5 kap saN e5 chui2 long2 lai5 chun7 taN.    
Chit e5 mng5-khau2 tui3-bin7, u7 chit8 keng toa7 kheh-tiam3, lai7-bin7 hioh nng7 e5 lang5- kheh, chit8 e5 thai3-thai3, chit8 e5 sio2 kou-niu5, tu2-a2 ti7 thang-a2-nih8 teh khoaN3 loh8-hou7; goan2 khia7 ti7 mng5-khau2 bo5 loa7 ku2.  Hit e5 sio2 kou-niu5 chiu7 khui mng5, eng7 chhiu2 
iat8 kah goa2 ke3-khi3, goa2 phah-sng3 i si7 beh boe2 he5, chiu7 chiong khang kheng hou7 i khoaN3, hou7 i chai goa2 boe7 liau2 lah.  I iau2-ku2 khia7 ti7 hi2 mng5- khau2 teh theng3-hau7 goa2, goa2 siuN7 ke3-khi3 hit peng5, khoaN3 i beh sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3.  Chiu7 khiN5 Kho-ni2 e5 saN kong2, Li2 kap goa2 lai5 khi3.  I m7 kheng2, ka7 goa2 sak, kah goa2 ka-ki7 chit8 e5 khi3.    
U7 chit8 e5 bu2-a2, khia7 ti7 sio2 kou-niu5 e5 ka- chiah-au7, khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 khia7 ti7 gim5-kiN5 teng2, saN-a2 chui2 chhiau7-chhiau7 tih, i e5 bin7 chiu7 piN3 tong3-boe7-tiau5, chiu7 kong2, I-ti2 kou- niu5, li2 khah ho2 theh8 kui2-e5 chiN5 hou7 i, kah i khi3, chit ho7 e5 gin2-a2 li2 beh kap i kong2 sim2-mih8 oe7?    
Hit e5 kou-niu5 phian-phian kio3 goa2 tioh8 ke3-khi3, i tai7-seng khia7 ti7 kha-tah8-i2 teng2, au7-lai5 chiu7 kiaN5 kau3 gim5-kiN5-kha toa7 siaN kio3 kong2, An2-ne2 ah, hit e5 gin2-a2 ti7 chia, i e5 saN hou7-chui2 chhiau7-chhiau7 tih, bu2-a2 m7 hou7 i chiuN7-khi3 lau5-teng2, chhiaN2 li2 loh8-lai5 chia kap i kong2 khah ho2.  Kong2-liau2 chiu7 u7 chit8 ui7 thai3-thai3 tui3 lau5-teng2 loh8-lai5.      
Goa2 chit8 e7 khoaN3-kiN3 chiu7 ka7 i chheng2-an, in-ui7 chit e5 lang5 put-siok8, goa2 long2 iau2 be7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 e5.   
Hit e5 thai3-thai3 eng7 chhiu2 tah goa2 e5 thau5-khak kong2, Li2 mia5 kio3 sim2-mih8?   Goa2 chiu7 kiah8-khi2-thau5 lai5 kong2, Kio3 choe3 Hoan7 Kui-lo5.  Thai3-thai3 kong2, Loh8 kau3 chiah toa7 hou7, siaN2-su7 iau2 toa3 ti7 chi2 koe- nih8?  Li2 khoaN3 li2 e5 saN long2 tam5 lah.    
Goa2 kong2, Kho-ni2 kap goa2 he5 boe7 iau2- be7 liau2, chiah toa3 ti7 chi2 koe-nih8, goan2 lai5 e5 si5, goan2 lau7-bu2 ka7 goan2 kong2, Boe7 bo5 liau2 m7 thang to3-khi3.  Chit tiap8 boe7 liau2 lah, goan2 si7 choe3 sio2-khoa2 seng-li2 e5, m7 kiaN hou7 tam5.    

Thai3-thai3 koh ki2 Kho-ni2 kong2, I si7 lin2 chi7-chui7?  Toa3 ti7 au7-bin7 teh te3 li2, si7 kap li2 choe3 phoaN7 mah?  Goa2 kong2, Si7, i si7 goa2 e5 hiaN, mia5 kio3 Kho-ni2, choan5 kho3 i chiau3- kou3 goa2, tu2-a2-chiah thai3-thai3 kio3 goa2 e5 si5,  Kho-ni2 tu2 beh ka7 goa2 chun7 ta saN.   
Hit e5 thai3-thai3 chiu7 chhoa7 goa2 khi3 chau3- kha kong2, Li2 toa3 chia hang he2, goa2 ho2 thang kap li2 kong2-oe7.  Ti7 hia u7 kek8 sio e5 he2, teng2- bin7 teh pek bah, si7 goa2 long2 be7 bat chiah8- tioh8 e5.  Cha2 Kho-ni2 kap goa2 jip8 siaN5, siong5- siong5 khia7 ti7 png7-tiam3 khau2, khoaN3-kiN3 he2- ki3 phang5 chit ho7 chhai3.  Goan2 chiu7 kong2, Goan2 na7 u7 chiN5, ia7 chiah boe2 koaN2-lai5 chhi3 chiah8 khoaN3-teh.  TaN lai5 kau3 thai3-thai3 e5  chau3-kha, khoaN3-kiN3 thou5-kha chin7 chheng- khi3, sim-lai7 teh siuN7 ka7 i tah8 kau3 long2 si7 kha-jiah, kiam2 m7 tioh8 hou7 i hiam5, chiu7 thau5-khak tam3-tam3 hang he2, m7 kaN2 khoaN3 tang khoaN3 sai, kau3 thai3-thai3 kap goa2 kong2 koaN2 oe7, goa2 chiu7 boe7 ki3-tit ka-ki7 e5 khoan2- sit, sim-lai7 chin hoaN-hi2.    
Thai3-thai3 koh mng7 kong2, Li2 u7 thak8-chheh a2 bo5?  Goa2 kong2, Bo5, goan2 Kho-ni2 u7 thak8 chit8 le2-pai3, i long2 boe7 hiau2 jin7 ji7, goan2 lau7-bu2 kong2 i tun7, chiu7 bo5 hou7 i khi3 thak8.  Thai3-thai3 koh mng7 kong2, Li2 khoaN3-kiN3 ji7 koe7 jin7 a2 oh jin7?  Goa2 kong2, M7 chai, in-ui7 thong jit8 goa2 teh boe7 he5, goa2 to bo5 chhi3- khoaN3.     
Thai3-thai3 koh kong2, Li2 chit e5 eN-a2, li2 chai Siong7-te3 a2 m7 chai?  Goa2 chiu7 koaN2- kin2 kong2, Goa2 bat thiaN-kiN3, lan2 si7 I chhong3- cho7 e5.  Thai3-thai3 kong2, Tioh8, li2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok mah?  Goa2 kong2, Cha2 chun2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 nih8, boe7 ki3-tit chi7- chui7-a2 teh kong2.  Thai3-thai3 kong2, Chit e5 gin2-na2 sit8-chai7 thang kho2-lin5, goa2 beh siong5-se3 ka7 li2 kong2, Ia5-sou si7 lan2 e5 peng5-iu2, i chin7 thiaN3 lang5, li2 m7 bat I, I ku2 bat li2, koh kho2-lin5 li2, chiau3-kou3 li2.    
Goa2 kong2, KaN2 m7 si7 an2-ni, tui3 goan2 lau7-pe7 si2-liau2, chiau3-kou3 goan2 e5 si7 Kho-ni2.  Thai3-thai3 koh kong2, Kui-lo5 ah, hit e5 te7 it ho2 e5 peng5-iu2, siong5-siong5 kap lan2 choe3 chit8 tui ti7-teh, chong2-si7 lan2 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 I.    

Goa2 siuN7 hit e5 thai3-thai3 khoaN3 goa2 e5 khoan2-sit, chai goa2 sim-lai7 long2 boe7 beng5- pek8.  Koh kong2, Li2 siong5-siong5 lai5, goa2 beh siong5-se3 ka7 li2 kong2, koh ka7 goa2 kong2, Kui-lo5 ah, li2 ai3 thak8-chheh mah?  Goa2 chiu7 ka7 thai3-thai3 seh-sia7 kong2, Beh.  Chhui3 an2-ni teh kong2, sim-lai7 khiok m7 si7, goa2 kiaN-liau2 na7 kong2 m7, thai3-thai3 oe7 m7 hoaN-hi2, koh goan2 lau7-bu2 bat ka7 goan2 kong2, Bo5 lun7 tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 lang5, na7 khah chun- kui3 e5, chiu7 tioh8 sun7 i e5 chhui3-be2 chiah ho2.    
Hit e5 thai3-thai3 kong2, Li2 na7 beh, thang ka7 lin2 lau7-bu2 kong2, tak8 chhit jit8 nng7 pai2 li2 he5 boe7-liau2, tioh8 lai5 goa2 chia, goa2 ka3 li2 chiaN5 tiam2-cheng chheh, goa2 iau2 beh ti7 chia tiam3 kui2 geh8-jit8 ku2, li2 na7 lai5 chia tioh8 khah chheng-khi3-siuN3 teh, li2 teh chheng7 e5 saN iau2 bo5 pi2 chit nia2 khah ho2 e5 mah?  Goa2 kong2, Bo5 ah.  Goa2 ku2 chai iau2 u7 nng7 nia2 ho2 saN goan2 lau7-bu2 ka7 goa2 siu ti7 siuN-nih8, goa2 bo5 beh chiau3-sit8 ka7 i kong2, ng3-bang7 hit e5 thai3-thai3 oe7 chit8 nia2 hou7 goa2, beng5-ki chai kong2 peh8-chhat8 si7 m7 tioh8, kou-put-chiong tioh8 tui3 i an2-ni kong2, I kong2, Si7 an2-ni mah?  goa2 hou7 li2 chit8 nia2 chheng-khi3 chheng-khi3 e5 saN, li2 lai5 chia tioh8 chheng7-lai5, ti7 chia li2  thang theh8-khi3. Kho-ni2 thiaN liau2, chiu7 tah chhiu2 kong2, He2, an2-ni chin ho2, chhiaN2 li2 ka7 Au7-bun5 kong2, mng7 i beh siaN2 si5 khi3. Goa2 kap Kho-ni2 che7 ti7 chhia-teng2, Au7-bun5 chiu7 khia7 ti7 chhia-nih koaN2 chhia.  Kho-ni2 ka7 goa2 pho7-teh, in-ui7 hia long2 si7 chhan5- nih e5 chhiN lou7, chin7 bo5 piN5.   
Kho-ni2 mng7 Au7-bun5 kong2, Li2 phah-sng3 chiong chhia lai5 khoa3 ti7 hai2 piN teng2, a2-si7 thoa khi3 soaN-nih, khoa3 ti7 soa-pho teng2, theng3-hau7 goa2 khau chhai3? phah-sng3 chiong chhia thoa chiuN7 soaN khi3, khoa3 ti7 oe7 thoa-tit chhia e5 sou2-chai7; goa2 kap li2 tau3 theh8 soh-a2 kap kheng, kau3 kek8 koaiN5 e5 sou2-chai7, li2 chiah khi3 hi2 soaN-pou-nih khau chhai3, theng3-hau7 khau kau3 kheng moa2, goa2 kap Kui-lo5 eng7 soh-a2 tioh chiuN7-lai5, khi3 he7 chhia-nih, li2 chiah eng7 kheng koh khi3 khau, chi2 nng7 khia kheng oaN7-lai5 oaN7-khi3.   Kho-ni2 kong2, He2, chin ho2-se3, lan2 chiu7 thang chai3-tit chit8 chhia chhai3. Kho-ni2 chiu7 kap goa2 loh8 chhia.

Che lou7 sui-bong2 phaiN2-kiaN5, pi2 pe5 kau3 soaN-piN khi3 khau chhai3 khiok u7 khah koe7.  Goan2 kau3 kek8 koaiN5 e5 sou2-chai7 Au7-bun5 m7 kaN2 pe5 loh8 soaN.  Kho-ni2 chiu7 thng3 saN beh ka-ki7 loh8-khi3, goa2 chiu7 kah i hou7 goa2 kap i saN-kap loh8-khi3, kong2, Li2 chai goa2 kap li2 chit8 iuN7 oe7 peh soaN2 poaN5 nia2, long2 bo5 chit8 tiam2-a2 kiaN, na7 si7 lan2 nng7 lang5 loh8-khi3, tau3 kha-chhiu2, khau khah kin2. leng2-cheng7, bo5 poaN3 lang5, sui si7 chheng-thian pek8-jit8, goa2 e5 sim iah8 kiaN.  Kho-ni2 ti7 thau5-cheng5 kiaN5, goa2 kio3 i to3-tng2 lai5 hu5 goa2 loh8-khi3. Kau3 hit e5 chhut chhai3 e5 sou2-chai7, Kho- ni2 ka7 goa2 chhe7 chit8 e5 piN5 chheng-khi3 e5 sou2- chai7, kah goa2 toa3 hia soe2 chhai3. Te7 kau2 chiuN
Goan2 kiN3-tioh8 Au7-bun5 kap i su-niu5 beng5-pek8, kah i bin5-a2 cha2-khi2 jip8 siaN5 khi3 boe7 chhai3. Goa2 thiaN Kho-ni2 an2-ni kong2, chiu7 ka7 i kong2, Goa2 sit8-chai7 ai3 thiaN chit ho7 oe7; goa2 tak8 jit8 kap li2 choe3 chit8 tui, thai2-tho2 m7 chai chit e5 tai7-chi3? Goa2 kong2, Li2 na7 u7 piN7 goa2 tek-khak thong jit8 kiu5 Chu2 hou7 li2 tiam3 ti7 se3-kan.  I kong2, Kui-lo5, li2 bo5 siuN7 kau3 Chu2 Ia5-sou tiam3 e5 sou2-chai7 si7 chaiN2-iuN7 ho2 mah? Te7 chap8-saN chiuN
U7 chit8 jit8 tu2-a2 khun3-chhiN2, khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 goa2 chim2-thau5 piN khoa3-tioh8 chit8 ki kek8 sui2 e5 geh8-kiN3 hoe, goa2 kong2, Kho-ni2 li2 tui3 ta2-loh8 theh8-lai5?  I kong2, Li2 ioh khoaN3 goa2 tui3 ta2-loh8 tit-tioh8 che ho2 hoe, li2 na7 ioh m7 tioh8, thang koh-chai3 ioh.  Goa2 kong2, KaN2 si7 hit e5 i-seng theh8-lai5 mah?  I chiu7 chhio3 kong2, M7 tioh8, li2 koh ioh khoaN3 cheh.   
Goa2 khoaN3 i e5 bin7 hiah hoaN-hi2, chiu7 siuN7 khi2-lai5, mng7 i kong2, Si7 goa2 hit ui7 thai3-thai3 mah? Teng2 e5 pai3-si3, goa2 khi3 boe7 he5, in khoaN3-kiN3 iau2 oe7 jin7-tit goa2, ia7 chin7 hoaN-hi2, mng7 goa2 kong2, Sio2 Kui-lo5 ti7 ta2-loh8? Goa2 chiu7 chiong lan2 tiam3 ti7 ta2-loh8 ka7 i kong2.  I kong2, Li2 to3-khi3, m7 sai2 ka7 Kui- lo5 kong2 goa2 lai5 chia lah, theng3-hau7 goa2 ke3-khi3 tham3 i.  Kin cha2-khi2 i kap I-ti2 kou-niu5 lai5, chhin chhiu2 chiong che hoe khoa3 ti7 chia. Goa2 kong2, In lai5 goa2 ia7 bo5 kiN3-tioh8 in. Kho-ni2 kong2, Li2 m7 sai2 hau2; che si7 thai3- thai3 m7 hou7 lau7-bu2 kio3 li2 chhiN2 e5.  Lau7-bu2 ia7 kong2, li2 na7 chhiN2 chai i lai5, tek-khak kong2, Hit si5 siaN2-su7 bo5 beh kio3 goa2 chhiN2.  Hit e5 thai3-thai3 kong2, Bin5-a2-jit8 goa2 beh koh lai5.    
Ke3 jit8 i chin-chiaN3 koh lai5, khoeh ti7 goa2 e5 bin5-chhng5 che7, goa2 chiu7 chiong tui3 i khi2-sin liau2, kui2 geh8-jit8 e5 tai7-chi3 ka7 i kong2, ia7 chiong goa2 e5 Seng3-keng theh8  lai5 hou7 i khoaN3.  Sui-jian5 chheh phe5 kap chheh piN e5 kim long2 ku7 lah, i ia7 bo5 kong2 sim2-mih8, chin7 hoaN-hi2 goa2 oe7 beng5-pek8 chheh tiong e5 i3-su3.  Goa2 koh chiong Lek8- tek-kou sou2 ka3 e5 hi2 kui2 chiuN chheh, thak8 hou7 i thiaN.  Hi2 tiong-ng 
iau2 u7 choe7-choe7 i3- su3 boe7 hiau2-tit e5, i long2 siong5-se3 ka7 goa2 kong2 beng5.    
Au7-lai5 hit e5 thai3-thai3 tak8 jit8 lai5 ka3-si7 goa2, lau7-bu2 ia7 siong5-siong5 lai5 thiaN.  Hi2  kui2 jit8 sng3 si7 kek8 ho2 ki-hoe7, goa2 chin hoaN-hi2, sin-khu ia7 chai-kio3 khah u7 khui3- lat8, chiam7-chiam7 thang chhut-khi3 mng5- khau2, kap Pi2-tek che7 ti7 soa thou5 teng2, a2- si7 Kho-ni2 hu5 goa2, ia7 thang kau3 hai2 piN.    
U7 chit8 jit8, si7 chap8-it geh8 chhoe nih, hit e5 thai3-thai3 tui3 goa2 kong2, Kui-lo5, chit tiap8 si7 tang-thiN, goa2 beh to3-khi3 goan2 chhu3; ti7 hia iau2 u7 nng7 e5 kou-niu5 pi2 I-ti2 kou-niu5 khah soe3, goa2 chin7 ai3 in thiaN-kiN3 li2 e5 tai7- chi3, li2 chiong sou2 u7 e5 tai7-chi3, li2 long2 siong5- se3 ka7 goa2 kong2, goa2 beh sia2 i, theng3-hau7 kau3 goan2 chhu3 thak8 hou7 in thiaN.    
In-ui7 chit e5 thai3-thai3 siuN7 beh sia2 goa2 e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 chiu7 kong2 chhut-lai5, i chiah chhong3 choe3 chit8 pun2 chheh.  Goa2 siuN7 ia7 bo5 sim2-mih8 oe7 kham-tit sia2, chong2-si7 u7 chit8 hang7 goa2 chin hoaN-hi2, lang5 khoaN3 chit pun2 chheh, thang chai Kho-ni2 u7 sim2-mih8 ho2 phin2-heng7, chaiN2-iuN7 toa7 taN2.  Tui3 cha2 kau3 chit tiap8 Kho-ni2 bo5 lun7 choe3 sim2- mih8 tai7-chi3, long2 bo5 kong2 ka-ki7 ho2, tau3-ti2 si7 goan2 ke-lai7 e5 hok-khi3, toa7 poaN3 si7 i e5 khui3-lat8.    
Goa2 sou2 ka7 thai3-thai3 kong2 e5 tai7-chi3, i long2 sia2-liau2, koh mng7, Lo7-be2 kui2 ku3 li2 beh sia2 mah?  Goa2 kong2, Lou7-be2 beh sia2 Kho- ni2 chit chat chheh.  Mng7 goa2, Ta2-loh8 chit8 chat sng3 si7 i e5?  Goa2 chiu7 oat8-liam7 hit jit8 goan2 tui3 Lek8-tek-kou hit pun2 toa7 pun2 sou2 thak8 e5 chheh kui2 ku3 kong2, “Lan2 chiah e5 oe7 e5 lang5 eng-kai tioh8 tam-tai3 boe7 e5 lang5 e5 loan2-jiok8, m7 thang kiu5 ka-ki7 e5 hoaN-hi2....  Ki-tok ia7 bo5 kiu5 ka-ki7 e5 hoaN-hi2;” che sng3 si7 Kho-ni2 chit8 chat e5 chheh.
