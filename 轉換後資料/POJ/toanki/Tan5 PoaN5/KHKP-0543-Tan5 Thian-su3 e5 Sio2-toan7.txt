Tan5 Thian-su3 e5 Sio2-toan7
Tan5 PoaN5	   
Sin-hang7 Tan5 Thian-su3 chhut-si3 ti7 Chu2-au7 1873 ni5.  I toa3 ti7 chhau2-te7, u7 gou7 e5 hiaN-ti7, i si7 toa7-kiaN2, in lau7-pe7 u7 ka3 i thak8-chheh, ji7 bat chin chhim.  I boe7 tit-tioh8 to7-li2 e5 si5, chng-tiong e5 lang5 lai5 iang i khoaN3-jit8, poaN-si5, i ia7 chin khin5-khoai3, bo5 than3 lang5 e5 chiN5, chng-tiong e5 lang5 put-chi2 o-lo2 i e5 ho2 iang chhiaN3.
Kau3-ti7 Te3-kok lai5 koan2 Tai5-oan5, siat i cho3 thou2-te7 ui2-goan5, kam-tok cho7 toa7- lou7, chin7-lat8 phah-piaN3 siuN koe3-thau5 soa3 ti3 sin-mia7.  Kau3 ti7 1906 ni5 lai5 Tai5-lam5 i sin-mia7, ti7-hia tit-tioh8 to7-li2.  Au7-lai5 tng2-lai5, hit-si5 goan2 chit chng-nih8 iau2-boe7 u7 lang5 thiaN to7-li2, in lau7-pe7 chin khun2-tiok8, lai7-te2 m7 hou7 i toa3, chiong i e5 Seng3-chheh pang3 hoe2 sio, ka7 i siat-tok8, i ia7 bo5 sit-chi3.
Koh u7 chit8 pai2 in sio2-ti7 chiong i e5 bin5-chhng5 ka7 i thiah chhut-khi3, i kau3 beh khun3 e5 si5 chiah koh gia5 jip8-khi3 phah, tian-to3 hoaN-hi2. Kau3 au7-lai5 in lau7-pe7 chiam7-chiam7 khah be7 khun2-tiok8 i, chong2-si7 u7 hou7 i ka-ti7 chiah8, chin kan-khou2 koe3-jit8, tiaN7-tiaN7 tu2-tioh8 chhi3-lian7, i ia7 chin jiat8-sim. Lun7 i it-seng cho3 lang5 u7 thiaN3 lang5 e5 sim-koaN, toa7 se3-han3 to o-lo2, i sui-jian5 si7 san3-hiong, tiaN7-tioh8 m7 phiN lang5, beh hou7 lang5 piN, se3-kan na7 chit ho7 lang5 m7-bian2 siat koaN-hu2 ia7 e7 eng7-tit.  Kau3 ti7 chit kui2-ni5 i e5 bak8-chiu ia7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, hi7-khan ia7 bo5 thiaN-kiN3.  Sui-jian5 tu2-tioh8 hoan7-lan7 siu2 to7-li2 si7 tian-to3 kian-kou3.  
Kau3 1930 ni5 5 goeh8 1 ho7 khi2 phoa3-peN7, ti7 4 ho7 Chu2-jit8 ngou2-au7 2 tiam2 hou7 Chu2 tiau3 tng2-khi3 Thian-tong5, hiong2-siu7 57 hoe3. I u7 3 e5 hau7-seN 2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.  Beh lim5-chiong e5 si5, i hoaN-thau5 hi2-bin7, hit si5 i u7 mng7 khoaN3 kin-na2-jit8 tang-si5?  In kiaN2-sai3 ka7 i kong2, kin-na2-jit8 le2-pai3, taN li2 tang-si5 beh tng2-khi3 Thian-tong5? I kong2 kin-na2-jit8 le2-pai3 khah kiap, pai3-it chiah lai5-khi3 khah liau5-leng7.  Khah-theng5  
chit8-tiap8-a2-ku2 kong2, le2-pai3-jit8 lai5-khi3 ia7 ho2, kong2 an2-ni, leng5-hun5 li7-khui seng-khu, tng2-khi3 Thian-tong5.  Tui3 hit jit8 chng-tiong e5 lang5 tai7-ke kong2 bo5 chhai2 chit e5 lang5, chin tiong-hou7, chin lau2-sit8, kong2 oe7 chhin-chhiuN7 chioh8-nih8 cham7-ji7.  Chit e5 lang5 si7 goan2 chit chng--nih8 sin3 Chu2 e5 sian-kak-chia2.  Chit chng u7 20 cho2-iu7 lang5 tit-tioh8 to7-li2 si7 tui3 i e5 in2-chhoa7, goan2 chiah u7 chit ho7 e5 hok-khi3, chit lang5 e7 kham-tit hou7 lang2 cho3 bou5-hoan7-chia2.
ChhiaN2 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-che2 the3 in ka-teng5 lai5 ki5-to2, hou7 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 toe3 in si7-toa7-lang5 e5 kha-pou7 lai5 eng5-kng Chu2, si7 sim sou2 goan7.
( Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 543 koan3,1930 ni5 6 geh8)
