Sio2-toan7  Tio7 Chiok-siong5
PheN5 Su7-chong5
Tio7 Chiok-siong5 Sian-siN si7 Tai5-lam5 chhi7 e5 lang5.  I chhut-si3 ti7 Chu2-au7 1847 ni5.  Chu7 se3-han3 u7 thak8 chheh, i e5 si7-toa7 lang5 si7 cho3 seng-li2.  Sian-siN hiaN-ti7 nng7 lang5, ti7 20 hoe3 e5 si5 lau7-bu2 koe3-sin.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 liau2-au7 sim khah ai3 hong3-su3, si3-ke3 iu5-siuN2.  Ti7 22 hoe3 e5 si5 chiah tit-tioh8 to7-li2, si7 tui3 i e5 chhu3-piN u7 chit8 e5 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, mia5 kio3 Kim seng3 hiaN chio i khi3 le2-pai3.  Hit-tiap8 pai3-tng5 ti7 Teng5-a2-kha ke, tu2-tu2 ti7 in e5 tiam3-au7.  Hit-si5 Sian-siN hoaN-hi2 kap i siong5-siong5 khi3 le2-pai3, Chu2 u7 khui i e5 sim, to7-li2 chiam7-chiam7 beng5-pek8; chong2-si7 i e5 hiaN bo5 ai3 i khi3, siong5-siong5 ki-chhi3 phi2-pok8 i, ia7 oan3-hin7 Kim-seng-hiaN, kong2 na7 bo5 i chio i chiu7 bo5 khi3 jip8 kau3.  Sian-siN long2 bo5 liah8-cho3 iau3-kin2, kam-goan7 thun-lun2 siu7-jiok8.  Kau3 25 hoe3 chiu7 nia2 se2-li2 jip8 kau3, 26 hoe3 lau7-pe7 si2.  Hit-tiap8 i2-keng u7 cho3 chit8 e5 chhin-chiaN5, chiu7 soa3 ka7 sun7-ha3 chhoa7 lai5 sang3-chong3.  Hian7-chai7 Sian-siN-niu5 iau2 ti7-teh.
Sian-siN 27 hoe3 ti7 Hu2-siaN5 u7 tng chip-su7, 28 hoe3 u7 siu7 Kong-hoe7 phai3 kap Pa3-hiaN chhut-mng5 si3-ke3 be7 Seng3-chheh, au7-lai5 in-ui7 kau3-hoe7 khiam3-kang, chiu7 phai3 i toa3 Ka-gi7 cho3 Thoan5-to7 Sian-siN, chiu7 saN-soa3 toa3 A-kau5, Ki5-au7, Hong7-soaN, Kong-a2-na5, Sio2 Liu5-kiu5, Tek-a2-kha, Tang-kang2, PheN5-ou5, Tiam3-a2-khau2, Giam5-cheng5, Koan-a2-nia2, Thou5-khou3.  Ia7 bat tau3 ka3 Toa7-oh8.  Jit8-pun2 leng2 Tai5 hit-si5 i ti7 Koan-a2-nia2.
Hit-chun7 Moa5-tau7 e5 se3-siok8 lang5 gou7-kai2 kong2 lan2 jip8-kau3 e5 lang5 khi3 thong-hoan.  Sou2-i2 hit sou2-chai7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 hou7 in thai5-si2 19 lang5.  Sian-siN chit8 e5 toa7 hau7-siN 22 hoe3 ia7 si2 ti7 hit tiong-kan.  Sian-siN kap Sian-siN-niu5 tu2-tioh8 chit ho7 toa7 hoan5-lo2 e5 su7, be7 bian2-tit u7 chin toa7 khou2-sim, chong2-si7 long2 bo5 siuN7 beh po3-oan.  I kam-goan7 chiong chit chan5 tai7-chi3 kau-hu3 Siong7-chu2 chu2-i3.  I iu5-goan5 tiam2 ti7 kau3-hoe7 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang.  Kau3 50 hoe3 chiah tui3 Thoan5-to7-kiok8 pin2 theng5.  Sui-jian5 hioh-kang, iau2-ku2 u7 ki-hoe7 iu5-goan5 pou3-to7, kap lang5 tam5-lun7 to7-li2.  U7-si5 Le2-pai3-jit8 ia7 hoaN-hi2 chu2-li2 le2-pai3.  Kau3 kin-ni5 i2-keng 71 hoe3, lang5 sui-jian5 lau7, iau2-ku2 e7 si3-ke3 kiaN5, ia7 iau2 e7 tam-poh8 tau3 siat-hoat ke-lai7 sio2-khoa2 su7.
Put-heng7 ti7 chit e5 sin-lek8 5 goeh8 chhe 3 mi5, ti7 Tai5-lam5 Tang-mng5 le2-pai3-tng5, 
i e5 te7 4 kiaN2 Thian-chu5 sou2 toa3 e5 siok-sia3, hou7 Chu2 tiau3 i to3-khi3 ti lan2 Pe7 e5 chhu3.  Kau3 te7 10 ho7 e7-pou chhut-chong3.  Pa Bok8-su chu2-li2 le2-pai3, u7 lioh8-a2 kong2-khi2 i e5 li2-lek8 lai5 saN khoan3-bian2; ia7 u7 kui2-na7 ui7 sian-siN khi2-lai5, su7-khi2 i e5 heng5-chek hou7 lang5 siau3-liam7.  Chiong5-tiong Ng5 Su7-beng7 Sian-siN ia7 khi2-lai5 kan-cheng3 kong2 i e5 lau7-pe7, Ng5 Chi3-seng5(Bok8-su), thau5 chit8 pai2 thiaN to7-li2 siu7 kam2-kek, si7 in-ui7 thiaN-kiN2 Tio7 Chiok-siong5 Sian-siN sou2 kong2-e5. An2-ni i e5 kang-tiaN5 put-chi2 u7 kong-hau7.
Hit-jit8 lai5 sang3-chong3 long2-kiong7 u7 300 lian5 lang5.  TaN goa2 sia2 chit kui2 ku3, si7 siau3-liam7 sian-siN e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7.
1.Sian-siN tui3 22 hoe3 siu7 to7-li2 kau3 71 hoe3, ke3-kiong7 50 ni5 ku2, i long2 bo5 lang3 cham7 lai5 pang3-sak to7-li2, bo5 li7-khui Chu2, tit8-tit8 toe3 Chu2 kau3 tiau3 i e5 jit8.  Kin-na2-jit8 Sian-siN bo5 khoaN3 au7-bin7, tit8-tit8 hiong3-chian5 phau2-chau2, chau2 kau3 chhah-pio hit-nih8.  TaN tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 in-ui7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou teng2-bin7 tiau3 e5 siuN2-su3.
2. Sian-siN m7-na7 pun2-sin hoaN-hi2 siu7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 na7-tiaN7, ia7 gau5 in2-chhoa7 i e5 chhin-lang5, kap i e5 choan5-ke toa7-se3, hau7-siN, cha-bou2-kiaN2, sim-pu7, sun-a2, kin-na2-jit8 long2 siok8 ti7 Seng3-hoe7, cho3 kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 lang5.  Lan2 kin-na2-jit8 lang5-lang5 na7 long2 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 i an2-ni, chiu7 bian2 kiaN kau3-hoe7 bo5 ki-te2, ia7 bian2 kiaN kau3-hoe7 be7 heng-ong7?
3.Sian-siN sui-jian5 bo5 tng thoan5-to7 e5 chek-jim7, i iau2-ku2 m7-kaN2 liah8 thoan5 to7-li2 e5 kang cho3 kap i bo5 kan-siap8, sou2-i2 siong5-siong5 ia7 ai3 pang-chan7 thoan5-to7 e5 kang, U7-si5 ia7 bat saN-kap chhut-mng5 pou3-to7, sui-jian5 ni5-lau7, ia7 iau2 u7 chin7-tiong iong2-kam2 e5 sim.  Ng3-bang7 lan2 tai7-ke e7 oh8 i chit-teng2-ho7 e5 sou2 kiaN5.  Ia7 goan7 Siong7-te3 si-in ti7 i ka-chok8 tiong e5 lang5, hou7 in koh e7 toe3 in si7-toa7-lang5 e5 kha-pou7 lai5 kiaN5, toe3 Chu2 tui3 khi2-thau5 kau3 lou7-boe2, che si7 sim sou2-goan7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 399 koan3,1918 ni5 6 geh8)
