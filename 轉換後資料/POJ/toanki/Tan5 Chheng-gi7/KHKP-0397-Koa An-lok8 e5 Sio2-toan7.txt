Koa An-lok8 e5 Sio2-toan7
Tan5 Chheng-gi7 
Koa Bok8-su si7 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 E7-gu5-bo7-chhia koe e5 lang5.  I iau2-be7 chhut-si3 e5 si5 i e5 pe7-bu2 i2-keng si7 pai3 Siong7-te3, siok8 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 e5 kau3-hoe7.  I soe3-han3 e5 si5 u7 thak8 kau3-hoe7 e5 Gi7-siok8.  Kau3 1894 ni5 u7 jip8 Tam7-sui2 Sin5-hak8-hau7.  1899 ni5 u7 chut-giap8, chiu7 siu7 phai3 choe3 thoan5-to7-su khi3 tiam3 kui2-na7 ui7 e5 kau3-hoe7, chiu7 si7 Pat-li2-hun, IN5-khut-a2, Koe-lang5, Ki5-li2-gan7, Lam5-kham3, Toa7-kho-ham7; Pang-kio5, SaN-kak-pou.  1914 ni5 u7 kho2 choe3 Kau3-su, tang5-ni5 4 geh8 14 jit8 u7 siat-lip8 choe3 SaN-kak-pou Tong5-hoe7 e5 Bok8-su.  Put-heng7 u7 jiam2-tioh8 kui2-na7 khoan2 e5 phoa3-piN7, chiah8 ioh8 bo5 eng3-hau7, chiu7 lai5 jip8 ti7 Ma2-kai i-iN7 ti7-liau5 iah8 bo5 chhai2-kang.  Pun2-ni5 2 geh8 17 jit8 e7-pou 2 tiam2 poaN3 cheng ke3-ong2, hiong2-siu7 42 he3.  Au7-lai5 u7 chiong si2-the2 kng tng2-khi3 in chhu3, kau3 19 jit8 e7-pou 2 tiam2 poaN3 cheng u7 lai5 Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 le2-pai3-tng5 chong3-sek.  Gou5 Bok8-su choe3 chu2-li2, Siau Bok8-su ki5-to2, Tan5 Bok8-su thak8 Seng3-keng, Keh Bok8-su kong2 lai5-lek8, Gou5 Bok8-su soat-kau3 kap chiok-to2, lai5 sang3-chong3 e5 lam5-lu2 kiong7 u7 200 goa7 lang5.
Sang3-chong3 e5 hang5 ngou2 put-chi2 chhu3-su7.  Tak8 lang5 chin iu-bun7, in-ui7 Koa Bok8-su it-seng e5 choe3 lang5, put-chi2 tek jin5 ho5, i kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7 thoan5 to7-li2 u7 chin7 pun2-hun7, m7-na7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 e5 lang5 khim-keng3 i, chiu7 kau3-hoe7 goa7 e5 lang5 ia7 o-lo2 i, sou2-i2 it-tan3 thiaN-kiN3 i ke3-ong2 tak8 lang5 chin siong-sim.  I choe3 Chu2 e5 kang thau5-be2 20 ni5, choe3 thoan5-to7-su 15 ni5, choe3 Bok8-su oa2 4 ni5.  TaN i e5 kang i2-keng beng5-pek8, tit-tioh8 Chu2 e5 keng2-tiau3, tng2-khi3 chou2-ke.  Si-nih8 u7 kong2:
“U7 thou2-toe7 i e5 kng iaN5 jit8-tau3.
Na7 chhim-sin3 chiu7 oe7 khoaN3 kau3 chin beng5.
Lan2 e5 Pe7 tiam3 ti7-hia teh theng3-hau7.
U7 pi7-pan7 kek8 an-lok8 e5 pang5-keng.”

Koa An-lok8 Bok8-su i2-keng khi3 kau3 an-lok8 e5 pang5-keng.  Lan2 iah8 tioh8 ng3-bang7 au7-jit8 e5 an-lok8, oe7 saN-kiN3 ti7 eng5-kng e5 thian-kok, chiu7-si7 thang koh-chai3 kap Koa An-lok8 Bok8-su saN-kiN3, lai5 tiam3 ti7 an-lok8 e5 pang5-keng.
Hian7-si5 Koa Bok8-su niu5 kap i e5 kiaN2-ji5 iau2 tiam3 ti7 SaN-kak-pou le2-pai3-tng5, chhiaN2 tai7-ke thoe3 i ki5-to2, hou7 i e5 sim oe7 tit-tioh8 siu7 an-ui3.  I e5 tiong7-hu An-lok8 i2-keng tit-tioh8 an-lok8 e5 pang5-keng, sou2-i2 m7 bian7 ke3-thau5 iu-bun7, tioh8 ng3-bang7 pat8 jit8 oe7 kau3 an-lok8 e5 pang5-keng, lai5 kap i e5 tiong7-hu An-lok8 saN-kiN3, hiong2-siu7 ku2-tng5 an-lok8 e5 hok-khi3.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 397 koan3,1918 ni5 4 geh8)
