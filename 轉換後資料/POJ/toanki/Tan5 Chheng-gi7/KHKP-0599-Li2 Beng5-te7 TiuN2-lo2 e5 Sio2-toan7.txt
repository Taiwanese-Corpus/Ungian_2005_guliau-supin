Li2 Beng5-te7 TiuN2-lo2 e5 Sio2-toan7
Tan5 Chheng-gi7
Li2 TiuN2-lo2 si7 Choan5-chiu e5 lang5. I lai5 ti7 Bang2-kah choe3 kim-a2-tiam3, u7 lai5 thiaN to7-li2.  1900 ni5 u7 nia2 soe2-le2, si7 Kai Bok8-su ka7 i nia2 soe2-le2, au7-lai5 u7 choe3 chip-su7. Kau3 1905 ni5 u7 seng choe3 TiuN2-lo2.  I siong5-siong5 hu3 Sio2-hoe7, Tiong-hoe7, Tai7-hoe7, ia7 kui2-na7 pai2 khi3 Tai5-lam5. 1934 ni5 12 geh8 6 jit8 piat8-se3, an2-ni thau5 kau3 be2, choe3 TiuN2-lo2 29 ni5. Li2 TiuN2-lo2 tui3 pai3 Siong7-te3 khi2, chin jiat8-sim, han2-tit bo5 kau3 pai3-tng5 choe3 le2-pai3, i lai5 le2-pai3, si5-kan chin chun2.
1904 ni5, chiu7-si7 31 ni5 cheng5, i u7 the5-chhiong3 kau3-hoe7 tioh8 tok8-lip8.  I chiu7 kap cheng3 tiuN2-lo2, chip-su7 chham-siong5, hit-tiap8 Pak-pou7 iau2-be7 u7 Tiong-hoe7.  I kap cheng3 tiong2-chip, khi3 Tam7-sui2 kap Hoa5 Bok8-su, Gou5 Bok8-su chham-siong5, in chin hoaN-hi2, chiu7 thoe3 in pin2 Ka-na2-tai7 e5 Bu2-hoe7, chun2 in siat Tiong-hoe7 ti7 Pak-pou7.  Bu2-hoe7 u7 chun2, chiu7 teng7 hit ni5 10 geh8 4 jit8, ti7 Tam7-sui2 Sin5 Hak8-hau7 khui Pak-pou7 e5 Tiong-hoe7, tui3 hit-tiap8 khi2, chiu7 keng2 kui2-na7 lang5 lai5 kho2 kau3-su.  Kau3 1906 ni5 4 geh8 3 jit8, Bang2-kah Kau3-hoe7 chiu7 thau5-chit8 ui7 tok8-lip8 chhiaN2 bok8-su.  Kau3 chit-tiap8 bok8-su iau2 ti7-teh.  I-keng keng-ke3 29 ni5 ku2, chit e5 si7 Li2 TiuN2-lo2 e5 kou2-chhui.  TaN i i2-keng bo5 ti7 se3-kan, Bang2-kah e5 Kau3-hoe7 tioh8 siau3-liam7 i.  TaN i i2-keng ke3-ong2, Li2 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 ti7 10 geh8 29 jit8 ia7 ke3-ong2, nng7 e5 piN5-piN5 69 he3.  Hu-hu7 chin ho5-bok8, Li2 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 chin un-sun5, m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 i kap lang5 oan-ke, nng7 e5 choe3-tin7 tng2-khi3, chin u7 hok-khi3.  TaN i u7 chit8 e5 hau7-siN, i2-keng ke3-ong2, u7 chit8 e5 sim-pu7 iau2 ti7-teh.  Koh u7 chit8 e5 toa7-sun, hian7-si5 ti7 Tam7-sui2 Tiong-oh8 teh thak8-chheh, iau2 u7 3 e5 cha-bou2-sun, i e5 ka-chok8 long2 u7 lai5 le2-pai3, chhiaN2 tai7-ke thoe3 in ki5-to2.
 (Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 599 koan3, 1935 ni5 2 geh8)
