Sio2-toan7 Kin-m2
Tan5 Chhin-teng2
Kin-m2 seN3 TiuN mia5 Ian5, si7 A-li2-kang2 e5 Na5-a2 chng lang5.  Chhut-si3 ti7 Chu2 kang3-seng 1880 ni5 5 goeh8 30 jit8, 19 hoe3 chio tiong7-hu si7 choh-sit lang5; kau3 28 hoe3 poaN lai5 A-li2-kang2 ke cho3 seng-li2.
Kin-m2 tui3 e7 ti jin5-su7 chiu7 chin khian5-seng5 hok8-sai7 put8; mui2-goeh8 e5 chhe-it chao8-gou7 chiu7 be2 chin-che7 kim-hiuN-choa2-chek, lai5 sio hou7 hiah e5 put8.  Ui7-tioh8 an2-ne than3 chiN5 bo5 kau3 liau2; ti3-kau3 san3-phi2-phi2.  Iu7-koh m7-na7 an2-ne, sou2-seN e5 kiaN2 cha-bou2-kiaN2 kui2-na7 e5 , tng5-te2 tng5-chhiam si2 kau3 bo5 poaN3-e5.  Sui-jian5 tu2-tioh8 hit ho7 tang7 chai-e7, chong2-si7 hok8-sai7 put8 e5 sim long2 bo5 pian3-oaN7; iu5-goan5 si7 khiam7-chiah8 teh sio-kim teh hau3-keng3.  U7 chit8 jit8 i teh hau3-keng3 hiah e5 put8-a2 e5 si5, tu2-ho2 u7 chit8 e5 chi2-moe7 ho7-cho3 Goan5-so2 lai5 chhoe7 i thit-tho5, pun2-te2 si7 chin chai i e5 cheng5-heng5, taN koh khoaN3-kiN3 i chit-ho7 cheng5-keng2, chiu7 ka7 i kong2 u7 chit8 e5 chhong3-cho7 thiN-te7 ban7-mih8 e5 Siong7-te3; I si7 Chin-sin5, chiah e5 put8-a2 si7 chha5 khek--e5, be7 po2-pi3 lang5.  Chit nng7 ku3 oe7 khi3 kou5 ti7 Kin-m2 e5 thau5-nau2, tui3 an2-ne tiaN7-tiaN7 siuN7, siuN7-liau2 beng5-pek8, chiu7 koat-sim kui-seng5 Siong7-te3; liam5-piN chiu7 chiong hit 17 sian put8-a2 kap hiah-e5 bok8-chu2-a2 cho3 chit8-e5 hiat-loh8 chau3-khang khi3 sah8 ti-liau7.  Soa3 in2-chhoa7 tiong7-hu lai5 sin3 Chu2, tiong7-hu au7-lai5 ia7 tng kau3-hoe7 e5 chit.  Hit-si5 si7 Li2 Bok8-su teh sun5 kau3-hoe7, Bok8-su-niu5 ka3 i bat Seng3-keng.
Kau3 32 hoe3 tiong7-hu si2, bo5 poaN3 e5 kiaN2, lau5 Kin-m2 kou-sin, 35 hoe3 chiah koh kai2-ke3 PheN5 Kin thoan5-to7 sian-seN; u7 toa3 kui2 na7 e5 kau3-hoe7, ia7 u7 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 chit8-lam5 chit8-lu2.  Kau3 43 hoe3 e5 si5 sian-seN chiu7 tng2-khi3 Thian-tong5.  Kin-m2 chiu7 ti7 Tang-kang2 khui hui5-a2-tiam3, saN ni5 ku2 hou7 hoe2 sio saN pai2; tui3 an2-ne chiu7 siuN7-tioh8 in eng2-koe3 toa3 Iam5-pou e5 kau3-hoe7, hian7-si5 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 iuN5 bo2 bok8-chia2; chiu7 chiong nng7 e5 kiaN2 chit8-e5 sang3 kau3 Tiong-oh8, chit8-e5 kau3 Lu2-oh8, toaN-sin chiu7 khi3 Iam5-pou, chio-hou kou2-bu2 lang5 siu-li2 le2-pai3-tng5, in2-chhoa7 lang5 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3.  Koh i e5 peng5-seng gau5 kap tu2-tioh8 si2-sit iu-bun7 e5 lang5 tong5-cheng5, ia7 chin gau5 khek-ki2 the2-sut kan-khou2 phaiN2-mia7 lang5.  Koh put-chi2 ai3 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 lang5 thiaN, bo5-lun7 chhin-chiaN5 peng5-iu2, saN-bat m7 saN-bat, kiN3 lang5 chiu7 po3-iong5 Siong7-te3 e5 in-tian2.  Che si7 goa2 bat kap i toa3 siang7 le2-pai3-tng5 chit8 ni5 ku2, chhin-bak8 khoaN3-kiN3, chhin-hiN7 thiaN-kiN3 chiah chai.  Kin-m2 toa3 chit e5 kau3-hoe7 ke3-kiong7 25 ni5 ku2.  Ti7 kin-ni5 sin-lek8 3 goeh8 21 jit8 chiu7 koe3-ong2, hiong2-siu7 75 hoe3.  TaN i e5 kang beng5-pek8, hok-khi3 ia7 toe3 i.  
Pok8 Kan2-liok8 sia2 chiah e5 si7 beh hou7 liat8-ui7 hiaN-che2 chai, lan2 tak8 lang5 long2 u7 chhin-chhiuN7 Kin-m2 siu7 sia5-mou5 khou2-tok8.  Siong7-te3 eng7 in-tian2 the5-poat8 lan2 chhut Mou5-kui2 e5 koan5-se3, sou2-i2 tioh8 oh8 Kin-m2 e5 jiat8-sim lai5 po3-tap Siong7-chu2 chin2-kiu3 e5 toa7-in, eng-kai kiN3 lang5 tioh8 po3-iong5 Chu2 e5 mia5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 367 koan3,1915 ni5 10 geh8)
