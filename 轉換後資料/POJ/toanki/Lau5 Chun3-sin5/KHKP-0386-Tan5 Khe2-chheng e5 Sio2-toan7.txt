Tan5 Khe2-chheng e5 Sio2-toan7
Lau5 Chun3-sin5
Tan5 khe2-chheng ti7 1861 ni5 chhut si3 ti7 Ji7-pat-chui2 ke.  Sian-siN se3-han3 pe7-bo2 chiu7 koe3-sin, ke-lai7 san3-hiong.  Boe7 thiaN to7-li2 teh be7 te5.  Chha-put-to 31 hoe3 ti7 Ji7-pat-chui2 thiaN-kiN3 Iang2-hiaN kong2 to7-li2.  Sim-koaN teh siuN7 chit e5 to7-li2 chin ho2, na7 hou7 goa2 u7 than3 tam7-poh8 chiN5 goa2 chiu7 beh lai5 khi3 le2-pai3-tng5 thiaN to7-li2 pai3 Siong7-te3.  U7 chit8 pai2 cho3 seng-li2 than3 nng7 khou gin5, chiu7 ko2-jian5 lai5 le2-pai3.  Iu5-goan5 cho3 be7 te5 e5 seng-li2.  U7 chit8 pai2 tui3 Pak-kang2 be2 sek8-hun jip8 Pou-li2-sia7 be7, tiam3-lai7 e5 hoe2-ki3 ke theh8 nng7 pau hou7 i, kau3 to3-lai5 kheh-tiam3 sng3 liau2 u7 ke, chiu7 liam5-piN koh theh8 khi3 heng5 i.  Che si7 Sian-siN sin3 Chu2 e5 tiong-tit8, tit-tioh8 kiu3 e5 pin5-ku3.
Beng5-ti7 26 ni5 Tio7 Si5-hoe5 Sian-siN oaN7 khi3 Lun7-a2-chng cho3 thoan5-to7 sian-siN kap i put-chi2 chhin-bit8.  Na7 khi3 pat8-ui7 cho3 seng-li2, to3-lai5 chiu7 khi3 chhoe7 i che7, chheng2-kau3 to7-li2.  An-hioh-jit8 cha2-cha2 chiu7 kau3 pai3-tng5 thak8 Seng3-keng oh8 to7-li2.  Tui3 an2-ni Tio7 Sian-siN khoaN3 i si7 jiat8-sim e5 hoe7-iu2, chiu7 ki2-chian3 i khi3 Tai5-lam5 I-koan2 cho3 thoan5 Hok-im e5 kang.  Ko2-jian5 chin7 pun2-hun7, hou7 I-seng kap hak8-seng i2-kip8 peN7-lang5 tai7-ke put-chi2 chun-keng3 i.  
M7-na7 ti7 I-koan2 kong2 to7-li2 na7-tiaN7, ia7 siong5-siong5 ti7 siaN5 lai7 si3-ke3 sun5 hoe7-iu2 e5 ke;  in-ui7 an2-ni Tai5-lam5 e5 tiong2-chip na2 chai Sian-siN e5 jiat8-sim kho2-chhu2, chiu7 chhiaN2 i cho3 pun2-tong5 e5 Soan-to7 su.  Kho2-kian3 siaN5-lai7 bo5 lun7 hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 che7-che7 lang5 bat i.  Ia7 che7-che7 lang5 tui3 i e5 chhui3 thiaN-kiN3 kiu3-se3 e5 chin-li2 lai5 hoan2-hoe2 sin3 Chu2.
Sian-siN se3-han3 bo5 thak8 chheh, na2 chin7 pun2-hun cho3 kang, chiu7 na2 chai i be7 kham2-tit, chiu7 kap tng-chit e5 lang5 chham-siong5 tioh8 chit8 pou hou7 i khi3 Sin5-hak8 thak8-chheh, chit8 pou chhoe7 hoe7-iu2.  Au7-lai5 Kau3-su7-hoe7 seng5-jin7 i cho3 
Thoan5-to7-su, kap pat8 lang5 peN5-teng2; chiu7 phai3 i khi3 tiam3 Siau-lang5, Gu5-taN-oan, Bak8-sa, Hoan-oah chiah e5 kau3-hoe7.  Ko2-jian5 chin7-tiong cho3 i e5 kang, hoe7-iu2 tui3 i tit-tioh8 li7-ek.
Toa7 ng3-bang7 i ku2-ku2 cho3 chit e5 kang, bo5 phah-sng3 i ti7 pun2-ni5 chiaN-goeh8 kan hoan7-tioh8 peN7-cheng3, chiu7 lai5 Tai5-lam5 Ng5 Jin5-eng5 Sian-siN e5 tiam3 chiah8 ioh8 goeh8-goa7 jit8, u7 chiam7-chiam7 khah ho2, ia7 e7 khi3 si3-ke3 chhoe7 peng5-iu2.  Au7-lai5 chiap-tioh8 phoe kong2 i e5 sian-siN-niu5 phoa3-piN7 siong-tiong7, chiu7 liam5-piN to3-khi3 Hoan-oah.  Lian5-lian5 saN mi5 lai5 chiau3-kou3 i, ui7-tioh8 an2-ni bo5 bin5 ku7-peN7 koh khi2.  Koh lai5 Ng5 Sian-siN e5 tiam3 nng7 jit8, chiu7 ti7 pun2 ni5 2:22 me5 an-jian5 koe3-sin.  I e5 Sian-siN-niu5 i2-kip8 2 e5 hau7-siN, 3 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 iau2 se3-han3, kiu5 Siong7-te3 an-ui3 in e5 sim, hou7 in bo5 hoan5-lo2 koe3-thau5.  Ia7 ng3-bang7 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hoe7-iu2 e7 oh8 Sian-siN e5 jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2, si7 sim sou2 goan7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 386 koan3,1917 ni5 5 geh8)
