I5 Sian-siN
Ko Kim-seng
I5 Jiau5-li2 sian-siN, lin2 bat i mah?  Bat i, ji7-chap8 it ni5 cheng5 lai5 Tai5-oan5 khai-siat Tiong-oh8, poe5-iuN2 hak8-seng.  Khi2-thau5 Tiong-oh8 siat ti7 Khou2-chhu3, au7-lai5 oh8-lai7 hak8-seng e5 siok-sia3 beh to2, chiu7 lai5 kian3-ti3 chit8 keng sin Tiong-oh8 ti7 Sin-lau5 lai7.  Goan2 ng3-bang7 i e7 thang ku2-tng5 toa3 Tai5-oan5 chai-poe5 Tiong-oh8 e5 hak8-seng, put-heng7 ti7 kau2 ni5 cheng5 sian-siN-niu5 phoaN3-piN7, bo5 hap8 chui2-thou2, chiu7 poaN-soa2 khi3 SoaN3-thau5 e5 lai7-soaN Gou7-kiN-phou3, tiam3 hia siat Tiong-oh8, ka3-si7 kheh-lang5 e5 chu2-te7.  Ia7 bat thiaN-tioh8 i u7 hoan7-tioh8 pian3-sui7 e5 piN7, ti7 Eng-kok tiau-iong2 hou7 is-seng i-ti7, chong2-si7 bo5 chiau5-pi7 ho2; na7-si7 i thiaN3-tioh8 chit tang-peng5 e5 lang5, sou2-i2 phoe-mia7 koh koe3-lai5 Gou7-kiN-phou3 ka3 hak8-seng.
TaN I5 Sian-siN in hian7-si5 an3-choaN2?  Hai7, chin kho2-sioh!  Ti7 Tng5-soaN koh khi2 piN7, kau3 se-lek8 chiaN goeh8 7 ho7 ti7 Gou7-kiN-phou3 koe3-ong2.  I long2 bo5 gin2-na2.  Sian-siN-niu5 toaN-sin toa3 se3-kan, iu7-koh lam2 sin-mia7.  Sian-siN e5 bin7 taN bo5 thang koh khoaN3-tioh8!  Cha2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 chin7-tiong cho3 kang, chin gau5 chai-poe5 cheng3 siau3-lian5 lang5, khui-thiah hok-im kek8-ki5 beng5-lang2, khoan2-thai7 pat8-lang5 choan5-sim thiaN3-thang3.  Sian-siN sui-jian5 bo5 ti7 Tai5-oan5, na7-si7 i sou2 cho3 e5 kang iau2 lau5 ti7 Tai5-oan5, sian-siN sui-jian5 li7-khui se3-kan, na7-si7 i sou2 kiaN5 e5 ho2 iau2 teh li7-ek lang5, sou2-i2 be7 kim3-tit tioh8 sia2 kui2-ji7 chiuN7-po3 lai5 siau3-liam7.  Bang7 khoaN3 po3 e5 lang5 the3 sian-siN-niu5 ki5-to2, hou7 i kho3 Chu2 lai5 siu7 an-ui3.
Lan2 Pe7 e5 chhu3 chheng-khi3 eng5-kng, Bo5 choe7, bo5 peN7, bo5 si2, 
Bo5 koh li7-piat8 hok-khi3 chiau5-chng5, Eng2-oan2 o-lo2 hoaN-hi2.
 (Tai5-oan5-hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 239 koan3,1905 ni5 2 geh8)
