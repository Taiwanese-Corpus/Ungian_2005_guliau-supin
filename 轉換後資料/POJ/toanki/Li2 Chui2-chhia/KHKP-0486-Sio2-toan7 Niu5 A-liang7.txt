
Sio2-toan7 Niu5 A-liang7
Li2 Chui2-chhia
A-liang7-peh chhut-si3 ti7 Sin-tek e5 KiN-liau5-khiN, au7-lai5 soa2-khi3 Geh8-bai5, ti7-hia thiaN to7-li2.  Kau3 56 he3 e5 si5, tui3 Kai Bok8-su siu7 soe2-le2; tang5 hit-ni5 siat-lip8 choe3 tiuN2-lo2.

Ti7 20 ni5 cheng5 u7 soa2-lai5 Hoe-lian5-kang2 e5 Kun-ui-chng, keng-long5.  Hit-si5 choe3-tin7 lai5 Hoe-lian5-kang2 toa7-soe3 kiong7 17 lang5 na7-tiaN7, taN choan5-ke u7 43 lang5, sit8-chai7 chin chhut-teng.  I ti7 chit8-geh8-jit8 cheng5 u7 phoa3-piN7.  Kau3 ti7 7 geh8 29 ho7 chiu7 hou7 Siong7-te3 tiau3 i li7-khui se3-kan.  Lun7 i e5 li2-lek8 ai3 sia2 kui2-na7 hang7 po3 tai7-ke chai i e5 choe3 lang5.
1.I e5 lang5- khoan2 si7 chin phoh-sit8, chin khiam-pi, tui3 lang5 chin chhin-chhiat, chin u7 tou7-liong7, hoaN-hi2 cho3 chu5-sian7 e5 su7, m7-bat hoan7 koaN hoat, gau5 ti7-li2 ke-lai7.  Sui-jian5 ke-lai7 hiah choe7 lang5, m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 put-ho5 e5 su7.

3.Lun7 i e5 jiat8-sim, sit8-chai7 put-chi2 kho2-chhu2.  Tui3 goa2 lai5 kau3-taN, na7 m7-si7 tu2-tioh8 hong-hou7 kap sin-the2 phoa3-piN7 e5 chou2-tong3, long2 u7 lai5 le2-pai3.  I u7 siong5-siong5 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 chhu3-piN thiaN, in2-chhoa7 in lai5 pai3 Siong7-te3, in e5 ke-lai7 choan5-pou7 long2 u7 pai3 Siong7-te3. chai7-jim7.  I e5 te7-saN kiaN2 iah8 bat choe3 chip-su7; in-ui7 sin-the2 soe-lam2 chiah hioh-khun3. I sui-jian5 bak8-chiu hoe, iau2-ku2 oe7 oat8-liam7 choe7-choe7 Seng3 -keng kap chhit-chap8 goa7 siu2 e5 si.  Lun7 chhut-ian5 iah8 put-chi2 hiong3-cheng5, sit8-chai7 chin kam2-sim.
4.Lun7 i e5 chong3-sek, si7 kap pat8-lang5 u7 koh-iuN7.  I be7-si2 e5 tai7-seng u7 hoan-hu3 i e5 kiaN2, i na7 si2 tioh8 ka7 i ku2-heng5 he2-chong3.  I e5 kiaN2-ji5 than3 beng7-leng7, chioh Jit8-pun2 lang5 e5 he2-chong3-tiuN5 lai5 sio.  I ia7 u7 hoan-hu3 i e5 kiaN2-ji5 m7-bian2 hoan5-lo2, thang kong2 si7 chin beng5-pek8 e5 lang5.  Hit-jit8 lai5 sang3-chong3 e5 lang5 u7 pah-gou7-lak8 lang5, tak8-lang5 put-chi2 m7-kam.  I u7 chit8 khoan2 e5 sin3-sim, oe7 thang tit-tioh8 boe7 pai7-hoai7 e5 hok-khi3.
