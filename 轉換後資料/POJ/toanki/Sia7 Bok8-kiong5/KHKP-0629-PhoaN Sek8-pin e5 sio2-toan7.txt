PhoaN Sek8-pin e5 sio2-toan7
Chia7 Bok8-kiong5

I bat cho3 sun5-cha 15 ni5-ku2, au7-lai5 tng2-lai5 chhu3-nih choh-sit.  Ti7 kau3-hoe7 u7 tng-chit che7-che7 ni5, bat cho3 Chip-su7, ia7 bat cho3 TiuN2-lo2. I u7 nng7 e5 hau7-siN, 5 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2; hian7-si5 lai7-goa7-sun 30--e5. I e5 toa7 kiaN2-sai3 ti7 Lak8-kah cho3 i-seng, mia5 Si Chu2-keh; i2-keng piat8-se3.  I e5 te7-ji7 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ti7 Lak8-kah cho3 san2-po5. I e5 te7 3 cha-bou2-kiaN2 kap tiong7-hu ti7 Giam5-tian5-chou teh than3-chiah8.  I e5 te7 4 kiaN2-sai3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 tian7-lek8 teh hong7-chit. Te7 5 kiaN2-sai3 ti7 Ko-hiong5 teh cho3 i-seng, mia5 Gan5 Chhong-hoai5.  (Phok-ai3 I-iN7).

Lun7 i e5 cho3 lang5 chin tiong-hou7, tui3 to7-li2 chin jiat8-sim, chin kho2-chhu2. Ti7 peN7-chhng5 e5 si5, phi2-jin5 siong5-siong5 khi3 tham3--i, khoaN3-kiN3 i sin-piN put-si5 chit8-pun2 Seng3-keng.  Sui-jian5 to2-chhng5 chin ku2, be7 khi2 lai5, iau2-ku2 oh8 Iok-pek e5 khoan2, jim2-nai7 kan-khou2, siong5-siong5 khoaN3 Seng3-keng, ki5-to2.  Ia7 u7 chi3-goan7 Siong7-te3 na7 beh koh lau5 i e5 seN3-mia7, 3 ni5-ku2 i beh hu-hu7 saN chhoa7 chhut-khi3 kan-cheng3 Chu2 e5 mia5, kau3 hou7 choan5 chng e5 lang5 tai7-ke long2 bat to7-li2.  Chong2-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3 i2-keng chiaN5, soah i e5 tioh8-boa5, hou7 i eng2-oan2 an-hioh.  Toa7 ng3-bang7 i e5 kiaN2-sun oh8 i e5 khoan2, put-lun7 sim2-mih8 keng2-gu7 bo5 pang3-sak Chu2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 629 ho7,1937 ni5 8 geh8)
