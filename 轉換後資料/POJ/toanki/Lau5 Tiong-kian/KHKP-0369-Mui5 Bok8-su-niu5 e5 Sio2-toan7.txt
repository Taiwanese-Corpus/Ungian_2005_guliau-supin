Mui5 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 Sio2-toan7
Lau5 Tiong-kian
Ti7 11 geh8 8 ho7 Mui5 Bok8-su-niu5 li7-khui se3-kan.  Tai5-oan5 e5 kau3-hoe7 thiaN-kiN3 chit-e5 siau-sit chin-chiaN3 hoan5-lo2, thoe3 Bok8-su iu-bun7.  Kin-a2-jit8 ai3 ki3 kui2 ku3 po3 hoe7-iu2 tsai Mui5 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 li2-lek8.
Mui5 Bok8-su-niu5 mia5 kio3 Ma2-kek-liat8, si7 Sou-kek-lan5 e5 lang5.  I e5 lau7-pe7 si7 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 e5 bok8-su.  In-ui7 hak8-bun7 chin chhim chiu7 chin3-mia5 tsoe3 Phok-su7.  Ma2-kek-liat8 13 he3 hit-si5 lau7-bu2 piat8-se3.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 lau7-pe7 put-chi2 thiaN3-sioh i e5 tsa-bou2-kiaN2.  Tui3 se3-han3 i siu7-tioh8 pe7-bu2 e5 ho2 ka3-si7.  Kau3 ji7-tsap8-goa7 he3 hit-tiap8 i u7 jiat8-sim thoe3 se3-siok8 lang5 hoan5-lo2, in-ui7 in m7-bat ThiN-pe7 e5 un, chiu7 ai3 hian3 ka-ki7 tsoe3 Siong7-te3 e5 kang ti7 se3-siok8 lang5 e5 tiong-kan.  Sim-koaN chin jiat8 chiu7 sia2 phoe chheng2-kiu5 saon-to7-hoe7.  Sia2-phoe liau2-au7, be7 kia3 sim koh chim-chiok siuN7, chiah te3-be2 kiaN-liau2 boe7 kham-tit tam-tng chit khoan2 e5 chit-hun7, chiu7 thiah-phoa3 hit tiuN phoe, m7-kaN2 kia3.  Tui3 an2-ni thang tsai i e5 sim tui3 soe3-han3 kau3 tiong2-seng5 u7 the2-thiap be7-bat Tsu2 e5 lang5.
Thiah hit tiuN phoe liau2-au7, koh tsap8-ji7 ni5 Mui5 Bok8-su tng2-khi3 pun2 kok.  Hit-si5 chiu7-si7 ti7 1907 ni5, kap Ma2-kek-liat8 siong-hoe7.  Ti7 siong-hoe7 e5 tiong-kan Mui5 Bok8-su siong5-siong5 kong2-khi2 Tai5-oan5 kau3-hoe7 e5 siau-sit.  Ma2-kek-liat8 thiaN-liau2 chin kam2-sim.  Bok8-su khoaN3-kiN3 i hiah jiat8-sim ti7 kau3-hoe7, sou2-i2 ka7 i pheng3-tiaN7 tsoe3 hu7-jin5.  Ti7 1908 ni5 Mui2 Bok8-su u7 kap Bok8-su-niu5 koh lai5 Tai5-oan5.  Kau3-ui7 hit jit8 Chiang-hoa3 e5 sin3-chia2 u7 eng7 chhin-ai3 e5 cheng5 ka7 in hoan-geng5.  Kho2-sioh toa3 Tai5-oan5 bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7 hian2-chhut sin-the2 bo5 sim2-mih8 iong2-kiaN7.  Na2 ku2 sin-mia7 na2 loan-jiok8, kau3 lou7-be2 kou-put-chiong tioh8 than3 i-seng e5 beng7-leng7, chiu7 li7-khui Tai5-oan5 khi3 pat8 sou2-chai7 tiau7-iong2 sin-the2.

Mui2 Bok8-su tu2-tioh8 chit-e5 tai7-chi3 boe7 bian2-tit hoan5-lo2, m7-ku2 put-tek-i2 tioh8 li7-khui Tai5-oan5 khi3 O3-ti7-li7-a.  Au7-lai5 chiu7 khi3 Sin-se-lan5-to2.  Ti7 hia tiam3 kui2-na7 ni5 soa3 tsoe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 bok8-su, si7 ng3-bang7 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 sin-the2 oe7 chiam7-chiam7 khah iong2.  Kho2-sioh bo5 khah ho2, hoan2-tng2 khah siong-tiong7.  Hit peng5 e5 i-seng u7 beng5-beng5 kong2 i e5 piN7 boe7 ho2 sou2-i2 tsun2 in li7-khui O3-te7-li7-a tng2-khi3 tsou2-ke a2-si7 pat8 sou2-tsai7.  Hit kui2-na7 ni5 Mui5 Bok8-su kap Bok8-su-niu5 siong5-siong5 siau3-liam7 Tai5-oan5 e5 kau3-hoe7, sou2-i2 ti7 1914 ni5 in u7 to3-tng2-lai5 Tai5-oan5, I chiau3-ku7 tiam3 Chiang-hoa3.  Kau3-ui7 hit-si5 kau3-hoe7 pi2 ku7-si5 ke chin3 chit8 chan5 e5 chhin-chhiat lai5 khoan2-thai7 in-e5 Bok8-su kap Bok8-su-niu5.  Hit ni5 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 sim-koaN khah peng5-an chiu7 koh chhiaN2 sian-siN lai5 oh8 Tai5-oan5 e5 khau2-im.
Pun2-ni5 in ji7-ui7 khi3 Jit8-pun2 hioh-joah8.  Tui3 Jit8-pun2 tng2-lai5 liau-au7, koh bo5 loa7-ku2 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 piN7 khah siong-tiong7.  Ti7 11 geh8 5 ho7 i pun2-sin u7 sia2-phoe kia3 hou7 tsou2-ke e5 chhin-chhek peng5-iu2 po3 in tsai ThiN-pe7 koh bo5 ku2 beh tiau3 i tng2-khi3.  Ti7 pheo-lai7 u7 khou2-khng3 in boh8-tit iu-bun7 hoan5-lo2, in-ui7 i ka-ki7 put-chi2 ai3 tng2-khi3 ThiN-pe7 e5 tau.
Hit lou7-be2 le2-pai3 i e5 sim chin-chiaN3 peng5-an, toa7 him-bou7 li7-khui se3-kan e5 kan-khou2, ai3 khah kin2 kap lan2 e5 Tsu2 Ia5-sou saN-kap khia7-khi2.  I chin ai3 gim5 siok8 ti7 Thian-kok e5 si.  U7 chit8 siu2 si i sou2 ai3 gim5-- e5, na7 khoaN3 Bek8-si7-liok8 7 chiuN: 13-18 tsat chiu7 tsai hit siu2 e5 i3-su3.
Ti7 le2-pai3-jit8, chiu7-si7 7 ho7, i u7 hian2-chhut lim5-chiong e5 heng5-tsong7.  Le2-pai3-jit8 teng2-pou sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 siok8 ti7 Thian-tong5 e5 cheng5-heng5.  U7-si5 i u7 kong2, “TaN ng3-bang7 bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7 kau3 ui7.  TsaiN2-iuN7 ian5- chhian5 chiah ku2?  Na7 kau3 ui7 chiu7 ho2, chin-chiaN3 hok-khi3.” Ti7 8 ho7 e7-pou beh lim5-chiong e5 si5 koh chhut-lat8 ai3 gim5 hit siu2 si.  Hit-tiap8 i-seng u7 eng7 khun3-ioh8 hou7 i chiah8, hou7 i e5 sin-the2 khah khoaN3-oah8.  Mui5 Bok8-su u7 ka7 i kong2, “ Chit pai2 khun3 na7 cheng-sin5 kiam2-chhai2 
si7 ti7 Thian-tong5, li2 beh tsaiN2-iuN7?” I in3 kong2, “An2-ni chin hok-khi3, si7 goa2 e5 sim sou2 goan7.”  Bok8-su koh kong2, “Goa2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 hian5-chhe, li2 koh bo5 ku2 chiu7 oe7 kau3 Pe7 tau, hoaN-hi2 bo5?” “HoaN-hi2 lah!”  Koh bo5 ku2 chiu7 tng7-khui3, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 an-jian5 ho2-khun3 e5 khoan2-sit.
Hit saN jit8 e5 tiong-kan Lan5 I-seng kap Te3 I-seng i2-kip8 I-seng-niu5 put-si5 kap i ti7-teh, gau5 chiau3-kou3 lai5 hong7-seng5 i.  Bok8-su-niu5 tit-tioh8 i e5 thiaN3, tak8 pai2 chiu7 ka7 in seh-sia7.  Kiong-kip i-e5 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 khoan2-sit chin kam2-sim.
Lun7 tsong3-gi5-sek beh ki3 ku2-ku3.  Ti7 9 ho7 e7-pou si3 tiam2 cheng, cheng3 hoe7-iu2 tui3 si3-ui7 lai5 tsu7-chip hu3 le2-pai3 soa3 sang3-tsong3.  Tai5-tiong kap pat8 ui7 e5 hoe7-iu2 i2-kip8 thoan5-to7-su u7 lai5 hu3.  Tai5-pak e5 Lau5 Bok8-su tui3 Ka-gi7 tng2-lai5 hioh-kha ti7 Chiang-hoa3. Lim bok8-su tu2-tng7 bo5 ti7-teh, u7 lang5 chhiaN2 Lau5 bok8-su tso3 chu-li2 le2-pai3. Hu3 le2-pai3 e5 lang5 chin tsoe7.  Le2-pai3 soah Mui5 Bok8-su u7 kong2 kui2 ku3 chiu7 kong2-khi2 Bok8-su-niu5 lim5-chiong e5 cheng5-heng5 hou7 tai7-ke thiaN, soa3 seh-sia7.  Kau3-hoe7 thiaN-liau2 sim-koaN chiu7 siu7 kam2-kek.  Le2-pai3 soah, ti7 lou7-tiong beh kiaN5 e5 si5 kau3 bai5-tsong3 e5 sou2-chai7 cheng3 hoe7-iu2 gim5-si.  Hit e7-pou thiN-khi3 chin chheng, geh8 tu2-a2 chhut-lai5, bong7 e5 bin7-cheng5 khi3-siong7 chheng-siu3, tak8 hang7 tu2-ho2 lai5 hap8 Bok8-su-niu5 i2-keng sou2 tit-tioh8 e5 thian-hok.  Beh an-tsong3 hit si5 Eng-kok Bok8-su, I-seng, I-seng-niu5 i2-kip8 Kou-niu5 chiong i peng5-seng sou2 ai3 e5 si lai5 gim5.  Hit siu2 si sit8-tsai7 ho2-thiaN.  Hou7 hoe7-iu2 thiaN liau2 siu7-tioh8 kam2-liam7.
TaN lan2 tai7-ke thak8 chit phiN tioh8 hoaN-hi2 lai5 oh8 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 khoan2-sit.  I tui3 soe3-han3 thiaN3 Tsu2 Ia5-sou, kau3 tiong2-seng5 ai3 tsoe3 Tsu2 e5 kang.  Tit-tioh8 ki-hoe7 tsoe3 Tsu2 e5 chhe-eng7 chiu7 hoaN-hi2 li7-khui tsou2-ke chhin-chhek, lai5 tiam3 ti7 lan2 e5 tiong-kan.  Jiok8-the2 sui-bong2 si5-si5 tsai thiaN3, bo5 hiam5 kan-khou2, hoan2-tng2 si7 ai3 the2-thiap pat8 lang5.  Iu7-koh tit-tioh8 ThiN-pe7 e5 keng3-tiau3, hit-si5 i kam-goan7 li7-khui hoan7 kan e5 sou2-tsai7 ai3 kap lan2 e5 Tsu2 Ia5-sou eng2-oan2 khia7-khi2.

TaN liat8-ui7 peng5-iu2 lan2 tai7-ke tioh8 kiu5 ThiN-pe7 pang-tsan7 lan2 lai5 oh8 Mui5 Bok8-su-niu5 e5 khian5-seng5, ia7 tioh8 thoe3 Mui5 Bok8-su kiu5 ThiN-pe7 lai5 an3-ui3 i e5 sim, ng3-bang7 i hoaN-hi2 koh-tsai3 tiam3 ti7 lan2 e5 tiong-kan in2-chhoa7 tsoe7-tsoe7 lang5 lai5 jip8 ti7 Siong7-te3 Seng3-hoe7, si7 sim sou2 goan7. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 369 koan3,1915 ni5 12 geh8)
