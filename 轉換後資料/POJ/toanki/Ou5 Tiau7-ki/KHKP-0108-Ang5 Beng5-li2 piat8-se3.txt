Ou5 Tiau7-ki
Ang5 Beng5-li2 chiu7-si7 Liong7-a2, tui3 ti7 1893 ni5 jip8-kau3.  I tui3 chhut-si3 i2-lai5 seng-khu siong5-siong5 bat u7 phoa3-piN7; kau3 chiuN7 10 hoe3 i2-lai5 chiu7 khah han2-tit phoa3-piN7.  Lun7-kau3 i-e5 cho3 lang5 phoh-sit8 khiam7-seng2; i cho3 lang5 e5 kiaN2-ji5 u7 hau3, u7 gi7, m7-bian2 hou7 in si7-toa7-lang5 siu7-khi3 lai5 hiam5 i; i si7 put-chi2 gau5 pang-chan7 si7-toa7-lang5.  Tui3 kui2-na7 ni5 i2-lai5 e7 hiau2 ki3-siau3, be2-be7 put-chi2 u7 chhu3-su7.  I kap chhu3-piN hiuN-sia7 e5 gin2-na2 cho3-hoe2, i m7 bat kap lang5 oan-ke, iah8 m7 bat kap lang5 saN-phah saN-me7, chit-ho e5 su7 long2 m7 bat.  Goa2 lai Tang5-kang2, i iah8 bat chiaN5 ni5 ku2 lai5 kap goa2 thak8-chheh; ti7 oh8--nih8 i iah8 si7 hian2-beng5 ho2 e5 khoan2-sit.  I bat ka7 goan2 kong2 i chin ai3 thak8-chheh, bat kiu5 in lau7-pe7 kau3 lau5 bak8-sai2.  Lun7 i thiaN to7-li2 u7 put-chi2 ti3-i3, i-e5 sim cheng7, sou2 thiaN siong5-siong5 e7 ki3-tit.  Lun7 i ai3 jip8-kau3, nng7 pai2 lai5 hou7 Bok8-su mn7, i eng2 si7 khah cho3 cheng5 lai5, na7 u7 pi2 i khah toa7 e5 lang5 ti7-teh, i chiu7 ai3 niu7 lang5 tai7-seng. Tui3 i jip8-kau3 liu2-au7, thiaN-kiN3 Hu2-siaN5 u7 sin khi2 chit8-keng Tiong-oh8-tng5, i chin toa7 him-bou7 ai3 khi3 hia thak8-chheh, chiu7 kou-chiaN5 in lau7-pe7 lau7-bo2 i-e5 hiaN.  I-e5 pe7 u7 khoa3-lu7 i-e5 sin-the2, kiaN-liau2 be7 kham, chong2-si7 i chin chhiat-i3 kiu5 i-e5 pe7 hiaN, in na7 kong2 ho2, i-e5 sim chiu7 peng5-an, in na7 kong2 m7 hou7 i khi3, i-e5 sim iu-bun7 kan-khou2 be7 peng5-an. In-ui7 i phoa3-piN7 e5 si5 chin gau5 thun-lun2, bo5 chhan bo5 kio3, i ka-ti7 e5 sim siong5-siong5 ai3 kap Chu2 kau-poe5, tauh8-tauh8 u7 ka-ti7 teh ki5-to2. I sou2 chiah8 e5 ioh8 long2 bo5 sim2-mih8 eng3-hau7.  Kau3 ji7-goeh8 chhe gou7 e7-pou7 gou7-tiam2-goa7 cheng, Chu2 tiau3 i an-jian5 koe3-ong2.  Ai-ah! U7 chit-ho7 siau3-lian5 lang5 chin7-tiong-hou7 e5 lang5 koe3-ong2 lah.  TaN ti7 se3-kan bo5 thang koh kiN3-tioh8 i lah, i un2-tang3 hou7 Chu2 chiap-lap8, eng2-eng2 kap Chu2 Ia5-sou saN-kap ti7-teh, ti7 hia u7 thang hiong2-siu7 Chu2 Ia5-sou sou2 eng3-un2 e5 chun-kui3 eng5-kng toa7 hok-khi3.
Chap8-ji7 jit8 si7 le2-pai3-jit8, thoan5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2, be7 bian2-tit tioh8 lioh8-lioh8-a2 kong2 Liong7-a2 e5 tai7-chi2; sou2-i2 chit8-e7 thiaN-kiN3 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moai7 bo5 m7 siong-sim lau5 bak8-sai2, i-e5 pe7-bo2 siong-sim iu-bun7 eng2 be7-bian2-tit.  Tui3 an2-ni soa3 lai5 bian2-le7 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moai7.
1.Lan2 thang iu-bun7 i bo5 toa3 ti7 se3-kan thang lai5 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, in-ui7 i pun2-sin sou2 him-bou7 tai7-seng jip8 Tiong-oh8, ng3-bang7 au7-lai5 e7 jip8 Toa7-oh8, hak8-sip8 to7-li2, ai3 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang, ai3 kui eng5-kng Chu2 e5 mia5.
2.Lan2 tai7-ke chiu7 ka7 i hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i hou7 Chu2 tiau3 i kau3 thian-tong5, long2 be7 thang giau5-gi5, i si7 tui3 cheng3-lang5 e5 tiong5-ng thai5-iaN5, i u7 chin7 i-e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 phau2-chau2 kau3 tit-tioh8 kiu3, tit-tioh8 po3-siuN2 e5 hok-khi3.  ThiaN-kiN3 i koe3-ong2 m7-na7 kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5 siong-sim, chiu7 hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5 iah8 u7 siong-sim lau5 bak8-sai2.  Chit-e5 tai7-chi3 thang bian2-le7 lan2 tai7-ke tioh8 cho3 ho2 lang5.  In-ui7 lan2 e5 siN3-mia7 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8; siau3-lian5 e5 lang5 e7 koe3-ong2, ho5-hong3 lan2 khah u7 hoe3 e5 lang5 mah?  Kiam2 m7 tioh8 keng2-seng2 pi7-pan7?  Chu2 na7 lai5 beh kio3 lan2 to3-khi3, lan2 chiu7 hoaN-hi2 thiaN.  Kiu3-chu2 tiau3 lan2 li7-khui se3-kan to3-khi3 Pe7 e5 ke, chiu7 hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7-kou3--tit.
