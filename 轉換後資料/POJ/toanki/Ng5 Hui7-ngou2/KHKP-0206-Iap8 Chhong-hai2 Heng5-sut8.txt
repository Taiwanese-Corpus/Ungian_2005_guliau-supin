Iap8 Chhong-hai2 Heng5-sut8
Ng5 Hui7-ngou2  
Ho2 peng5-iu2 Iap8 Chhong-hai2 si7 Tai5- lam5 siaN5-lai7 e5 lang5.  I e5 kong bat cho3 Cho2- si7-nng5 e5 koaN; lau7-pe7 bat cho3 Koan7--nih8 e5 su-ia5, ia7 bat cho3 Iam5-koan2 e5 lo2-tia.  I chhut-si3 ti7 Tong5-ti7 chap8-saN-ni5 (chiu7-si7 Chu2-au7 1873).  Chu7 se3-han3 chiu7 u7 thak8- chheh.  Kau3 chap8-ji7-hoe3 lau7-pe7 si2, chap8- lak8-hoe3 lau7-bo2 ia7 si2.  I si7 te7-ji7--e5, i u7 chit8 e5 hiaN cha2-cha2 chiu7 koe3-sin.  Tui3 chap8- lak8-hoe3 khi2 i u7 cho3 sio2-khoa2 seng-li2.   
Kau3 ti7 1896, kha u7 seN-thiaN3, chiu7 lai5 Hu2-tang-hang7 ku7-i koan2 chiu7 I-seng, soa3 sin3 to7-li2.  Kau3 ti7 1898 hou7 Bak8-sa e5 kau3-hoe7 chhiaN2 khi3 ka3-oh8.  Ti7 1899 u7 jip8 Tiong-oh8 thak8 poaN3-ni5; hit ni5 e5 e7-poaN3-tang u7 hou7 Tiam3-a2-khau2 chap8-hoe3-tiam3 Hap8-chhun chhiaN2 khi3 cho3 chiang2-kui7. Hoan7-su7 chin7-tiong, pan7-su7 khin5-kin2; hou7 che7-che7 lang5 sou2 o-lo2. Koe3-ni5 1900 chiu7 lai5 thak8 Toa7-oh8, si7 kap goan2 kui2-na7-lang5 peN5-peN5 tui3 Song3 Bok8-su phoa3-pit.  Lun7 i thak8-chheh, si7 in-khin5 dhhut-lat8; teh kau-poe5 lang5, ia7 chin lau2-sit8 koh tiong- hou7.  Chai7 oh8-lai7 thak8-chheh khah bo5 nng7-ni5-poaN3.   
Ti7 hit nng7-ni5-lai7 u7 pang3-ke3 si3-pai2, i bat khi3 toa3 saN-sou2-chai7 e5 kau3-hoe7. Chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 Ka-poa3-soa2 u7 chin gau5 siat-hoat kau3-hoe7 e5 su7; the3 hiaN-ti7 siat- hoat te5-gin5 e5 hoat-tou7, chiu7-si7 tiah8 ngou2- kok lai5 seng-sek.  Koh chin gau5 li7-ek gin2- na2, poe5-iuN2 hiaN-ti7.  Ia7 u7 khi3 Kian3-kong- chng, Tiong-lou7.  Sou2 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7 chin u7 iu2-ek lang5; sit8-chai7 hou7 chin che7 lang5 put- chi2 toa7 o-lo2; chin-chiaN3 si7 Seng3-hoe7 e5 toa7 eN5-thiau7.  Bo5-phoe kau3 ti7 1901, ni5-thau5 u7 khi2-sau3, chiah8-ioh8 bo5 sim2-mih8 kong- hau7; au7-lai5 u7 ti3 kha-khi3 e5 cheng3, chhoan2 toa7-khui3 e5 peN7.  Kho2-sioh! na2-ku2 na2- tang7.  Kau3 ku7-lek8 4 goeh8 chhe 6 (se-lek8 5 goeh8 13 ho7), hit cha2-khi2 toa7-khui3 long2 bo5 thang chhoan2; ti7 tiong-tau3 chiu7 li7- khui goan2 to3-khi3 Pe7 
e5 tau.  Beh si2 e5 si5 u7 kong2 chit8-ku3, “Goa2 chin kan-khou2, goa2 beh to3--khi3”.  Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni kui2-na7- pai2.  Che si7 hian2-beng5 i thoat-li7 se3-kan e5 khou2-hai2.   
Lun7 i-e5 chhin-chhek khiok long2 bo5 lai5 khoaN3--i; chong2-si7 Toa7, Tiong, Lu2, kap sio2-oh8, koh hiaN-ti7 kui2-na7-lang5, ke3- kiong7 oa2 u7 pah-gou7-lang5 sang3-chong3.   Lun7 khoaN3 chit-chan5-su7, chiu7 eng-kai kiu5 Siong7-te3 “hou7 lan2 e7-hiau2 sng3 lan2 e5 jit8- chi2” (Si-phian 90 : 12). 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 206 koan3, 1902 ni5 5 geh8)
