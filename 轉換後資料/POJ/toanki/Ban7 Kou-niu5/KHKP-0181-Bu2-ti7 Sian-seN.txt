Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN
Ban7 Kou-niu5
“Kin2-a2-jit8 ti7 “I2-sek-liat8 chok8-tiong, Goan5-soe3 si2, lin2 kiam2 m7 chai?”  II Sat-bo2-ni2 3:38.
Ni5-lai7 12 goeh8 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN hou7 Siong7-te3 tiau3 li7-khui se3-kan.  Hit-si5 tang-sai lam5-pak e5 lang5 si3-ke3 na7 tu2-tioh8 thau5 chiu7 kong2-khi2, Li2 kiam2 m7-chai te7-it toa7 Thoan5-to7 sian-seN i2-keng koe3-ong2?
I chin-chian3 e7 kham-tit ho7-cho3 toa7, peng5-sou3 bat kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7 pian3-pian3 thoan5 hok-im; han2-tit u7 lang5 chhin-chhiuN7 i ti3-kau3 hou7 hiah che7 lang5 thiaN, hiah gau5 kam2-kek lang5 e5 sim.
Lun7 i e5 sin3 kek8 toa7, sou2-i2 u7-si5 pat8-ui7 e5 bok8-su ai3 chhiaN2 khi3 hia kui2-jit8 ku2 kong2 to7-li2.  I chiu7 tai7-seng kia3 phoe lai5 mng7, “KhoaN3 ti7 lin2 hia e5 pai3-tng5 loa7 toa7 keng, u7 thang che7-tit loa7 che7 lang5?”  Bok8-su chiap-tioh8 phoe tai7-ke chiu7 sit-bang7 kong2, “Beh to2-ui7 chhoe7 pai3-tng5 hiah toa7 keng, ia7 kiam2 u7 hiah che7 lang5 ai3 lai5?”  In hoe5 phoe ka7 i kong2.  M7-ku2 chhin-chhiuN7 Ou-ni5-lan5 u7 lang5 tui3 choat8-bang7 liau2, tu2-tioh8 chit8 e5 toa7 koaN beh lai5, tai7-ke chiu7 chhut lat8 kong-ke khi2 chit8 keng chha5-chhu3 kau3-giah8 khoah toa7.  Pun2 si7 beh cho3 gi7-su7 e5 kong-sou2, au7-lai5 bo5 eng7,chiu7 tu2-ho2 chiong hit-keng lai5 cho3 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN e5 lou7-eng7.
Hit-si5 Sian-seN kau3-te3 chiah kong2, “Goa2 kam2 bo5 ka7 lin2 kong2 lang5 na7 u7 kian-sim chhim-sin3, Siong7-te3 tek-khak e7 the3 lang5 khui lou7,chhin-chiuN7 kin-a2-jit8 ti7 chit e5 sou2-chai7, chiah li7-pian7?”  Hit-tiap8 si3-hng e5 lang5 toa7-toa7 kam2-tong7 heng-khi2, u7-e5 lai5 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang; u-e5 chhut-goa7 cho3 thoan5-to7.  Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN m7-si7 toa7 bat-ji7 e5 lang5.  Lau7-bu2 si7 chiu2-koaN2-lang5 , bo5 ki-hoe7 thang hou7 i e5 kiaN2 khi3 thak8; i ia7 bo5 saN2 ai3 thak8.  Se3-han3 choh-sit, kau3 17 hoe3 khi3 toa3 tiam3 ka7 in sia2-ji7 ki3-siau3.  
Ti7-hia nng7 ni5 ku2, au7-lai5 khi3 pat8-siaN5 cho3 kang.  Ti7 hit siaN5 sin3 Chu2, tui3 khi2-thau5 chin jiat8-sim, liam5 piN khi3 chhoe7 bo5 he7-loh8 e5 gin2-na2 siat Chu2-jit8-oh8 ti7 in e5 tiong-kan, pun2-sin khi3 kou3 liau7-li2.  Cho3 kang-liau2, tak8 e7-hng si3-koe3 chio lang5 khi3 hok-im-tng5 thiaN to7-li2.  Koh bo5 loa7-ku2 Bi2-kok u7 khi3 kau-chian3,peh seN3 chhiaN2 I kap peng khi3 cho3 thoan5 to7.  Kau-chian3 liau2-au7 si3-koe3 cho3 thoan5-to7; ia7 si7 cho7 kui2-na7 pun2 si-chheh i2-kip8 pat8-mih8 chheh.  Hiah e5 chheh chin ho2 be7, sng3 si7 toa7 e5 seng-li2, toa7 than3.
Sian-seN eng7 sou2 than3 e5 chiN5 khi2 nng7 keng toa7-oh8.  Chit8-keng ta-pou--e5, chit8-keng hou7 cha-bou2--e5; nng keng e7 chiap-lap8 800 e5 hak8-seng.  Tak8-ni5 tui3 be7 chheh sou2 than3 e5 chiN5 khi3 chan7 hit nng7 keng e5 sou2-hui3, in-ui7 hak8-seng thiap bo5 kau3 chiok.
ThiaN Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN kong2 to7-li2 sit8-chai7 si7 toa7 hok-khi3, kong2 chhin-chhiuN7 oah8 e5 khoan2, chai sou2 thiaN e5 lang5 na7 bo5 sin3, eng2 e7 tim5-lun5; i chin m7-goan7, soa3 kong2 chhin-chhiuN7 boe2 pai2 e5 ki-hoe7.  Seng3-keng tui3 thau5 kau3 boe2 chin sek8, gau5 khui-thiah, gau5 eng7 phi3-ju7, sou2 phi3-ju7 chin tu2-ho2 hap8 i sou2 kong2-e5.
I koh gau5 liau7-li2 tai7-chi2.  Na7 kau3-te7 bok8-su hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 chin hoaN-hi2 tau3-chan7 i; u7-e5 si3-koe3 chio lang5; u7-e5 khoaN3 u7 ui7 hou7 lai5 thiaN e5 lang5 thang che7.  U7-e5 chan7 chit hang7 u7-e5 chan7 hit-hang7, ku2 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN pun2-sin long2 teh chong2 liau7-li2.  U7 hok-im-tng5 e7 che7 kau3 1,5000 lang5 iu5-goan5 bo5 kau3-giah8, u7-e5 eng2 bo5 ui7 thang che7.  KoaN5-thiN, loh8 sng-seh tak8-lang5 long2 bo5 hiam5.  Tak8 keng che7 kau3 chat8 thong2-thong2 bo5 lang3 chit8 ui7.
I ki5-sit8 m7 si7 ai3 lau7-jiat8, sou2 ai3 si7 kiu3 lang5, sou2 kong2 e5 to7-li2 kek8-chhian2 koh beng5.  I siau3-lian5 e5 si5 sou2 kong2 put-ko3 peng5-siong5, chiu7 bat theng5 kui2-na7 jit8 , Goa2 sou2 kong2-e5 long2 bo5 kam2-kek lang5 e5 sim tau3-ti2 an3-choaN2?  Tui3 hit-tiap8 kau3 si2 m7 bat koh khoa3-lu7, tak8 pai2 kong2 u7 che7-che7 lang5 sin3, put-lun7 chun-kui3 khit-chiah8 tak8 teng2-ho7 e5 lang5 kam-goan7 kianN5 kui2-na7 phou3 lou7 khi3 thiaN i kong2. 
Sui-bong2 60 goa7 hoe3 iau2-ku2 long2 bo5 theng5-kang, nng7 keng oh8 ia7 kui2 i teh 
liau7-li2, i2-kip8 chheh-pang5 kok hang7 su7, i long2 teh hoat8-loh8.
PhoaN3-peN7 hoat-choh hit-si5 iu5-goan5 teh kong2 to7-li2 ti7 toa7 keng e5 tng5.  Chin7-chai7 chhut-lat8 kiaN-liau2 tui3 an2-ni lo5-sim ko3-tou7 ka-ti7 bo5 phah-sng3 e7 si2.  CHong2-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3 si7 tiaN7-tioh8 beh soah i se3-kan e5 tioh8-boa5, kau3 lim5-chiong hit-si5 ka7 in ke-lai7--e5 kong2, “TaN te7 na2-li7 na2-hng7; thiN na2-oa2 na2-kin7.  Siong7-te3 teh kio3 goa2 khi3.”
Koh kong2, “Goa2 si7 tham-sim e5 lang5, ku2 m7 si7 tham-sim chiN5-chai5; tham beh lau5 kang hou7 lin2 si7-se3 cho3.  TaN hit nng7 keng oh8 kap chheh-pang5 lin2 teng2 khi2 lai5 hoaN7.”  I e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 bo5 ai3 i khi3, ka7 i kong2.  I chiu7 in3 kong2, “Goa2 m7 si7 beh pang3-sak seN3-mia7; Siong7-te3 na7 iau2 beh eng7 goa2 cho3-kang, goa2 chiu7 be7 si2.”
Bo5 loa7-ku2 khui3 chiu7 tng7.
Tui3 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN thang chai, lang5 na7 hian3 pun2-sin hou7 Siong7-te3; koh choan5-sim cho3 Chu2 e5 kang; sui-bong2 chit8 lang5 na7 tiaN7, iau2-ku2 e7 in2-chhoa7 lang5 chin-che7.
TaN Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN hou7 Siong7-te3 tiau3, chit se3-kan sit8-chai7 toa7-toa7 khiam3-khoeh.  Te7-it chhut-lat8 ho2-taN2, te7-it gau5 in2-chhoa7 lang5, si7 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN; oah8 si7 ui7-tioh8 Ki-tok si2 si7 i e5 li7-ek. 
Che si7 tam7-poh8 kan2-kan2 lai5 kong2 na7-tiaN7.  Ng3-bang7 sou2 thak8 e5 lang5, u7-e5 siu7 kam2-kek, him-bou7 ai3 cho3 Bu2-ti7 Sian-seN e5 khoan2.
“Chiong-sin e5 kang to long2 beng5-pek8.  TaN i chiuN7 -kau3 Thian-kok; Eng2-oan2 kap Chu2 cho3-hoe2 kau-poe5,  Tit-tioh8 ban7-ni5 toa7 hok.”
(Tai5-oan5-hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 181 koan3,1900 ni5 4 geh8)
