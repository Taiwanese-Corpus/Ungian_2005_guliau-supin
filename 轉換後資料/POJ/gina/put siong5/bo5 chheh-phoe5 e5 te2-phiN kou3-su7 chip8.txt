Soe3 Chiah Thou3 E5 Kou3-Su7 

chhe7 to3 ; chong2-si7 i e5 iu-bun7 e5 siaN hou7 kui2-na7 chiah chhek-chiau2-a2 iu2 thiaN-kiN3. In pe-lai5 chin7 ti3-i3 kiu5 i ka-ki7 chhut-lat8 leng3-khui.
Bou2 Sian-siN theh8 chit8 e5 thou5-thai lai5, ai3 tui3 Pi2-tek e5 teng2-bin7 lai5 hip-loh8-khi3.  Chong2-si7 Pi2-tek tu2-tu2 ti7 hit si5 leng3-khui i e5 saN liam5 ti7 bang7-nih8.  I chiu7 chau2 kau3 toe2 ke-si e5 chhu3-a2, thiau3-jip8 chit8 e5 ak-hoe thang2.  Siat-su2 na7 bo5 chui2 ti7 lai7-bin7, chiu7 chin7 ho2-se3 bih.
Bou2 Sian-siN siuN7 Pi2-tek tek-khak bih ti7 chhu3-a2-lai7 e5 bou2 sou2-chai7, kiam2-chhai2 si7 ti7 hoe-khaN e7-toe2. I chiu7 soe3-ji7 chit8 e5 chit8 e5 hian khi2-lai5, khoaN3. 
Koh chit8 tiap-a2 ku2 Pi2-tek phah-kha-chhiuN3, Bou2 Sian-siN chiu7 koaN2-kin2 jip i, iah8 beh eng7 i e5 kha lai5 tah8 i. I chiu7 thiau3 tui3 thang-a2 chhut-khi3, chhia-to2 saN khaN hoe.
Chit e5 thang-a2 si7 siuN soe3, sou2-i2 Bou2 Sian-siN boe7 tit-ke3, iah8 i chau2 sian7 m7 ai3 jip, chiu7 to3-khi3 choe3 i e5 kang.
Pi2-tek che7-teh hioh-khun3, i haN7-haN7 chhoan2, koh kiaN kau3 gi7-gi7-chun3, long2 bo5 siuN7 tioh8 tui3 ta2-loh8 chit8 tiau5 lou7 to3-khi3. Koh i si7 chin7 tam5, in-ui7 i u7 jip8-khi3 hit e5 ak-hoe-thang2. 
Koh chit8 tiap-a2 ku2, i chiu7 kiaN5-lai5, kiaN5-khi3, ban7-ban7 tong7, un5-a2 kiaN5 si3-koe3 khoaN3.
I chhe7-tioh8 chit8 e5 mng5 ti7 piah-nih8, chong2-si7 so2-teh, iah8 bo5 khang thang hou7 chit8 chiah pui5 e5 soe3 chiah thou3-a2 soeh tui3 e7-toe2 jip8-khi3.
Chit8 chiah lau7 niau2-chhu2-a2 chau2-jip8 chau2-chhut, ke3 chit e5 chioh8 hou7-teng7, ka7 tau7-a2 kau3 chhiu7-na5 in tau.  Pi2-tek mng7 i tui3 ta2-loh8 thang kau3 toa7 mng5, chong2-si7 i e5 chhui3 u7 toa7 liap8 tau7-a2 ti7-teh boe7 in3. I chiu7 ka7 i io5-thau5.  Pi2-tek soa3 hau2.
I chiu7 chhi3-khoaN3 beh chhe7 lou7 tit8-tit8 tui3 hoe-hng5 ke3-khi3, chong2-si7 i na2 ku2 na2 loan7.  Koh chit8 tiap-a2 ku2 i kau3 ti7 chit8 e5 tham5-a2, si7 Bou2 Sian-siN teh iuN2 chui2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Chit8 chiah peh8 niau teh khoaN3 koa2 kim-hi5, che7 kau3 tiaN7-tiaN7-tiaN7, chi2-u7 u7 si5 I e5 be2 io5 cheh, hian2-beng5 i si7 oah8 e5. Pi2-tek siuN7 khah ho2 i tioh8 chau2 m7 thang  kap i kong2, in-ui7 i bat tui3 i e5 ke3-pak hiaN-ti7 thiaN-kiN3 niau e5 tai7-chi3. 
I koh to3-tng2 ng3 hit keng chhu3-a2, chong2-si7 hut-jian5-kan i thiaN-kiN3 ti5-thau5 siaN chin7 kun7 ti7 i, siak8-siak8-siak8.  Pi2-tek koaN2-kin2 chau2 ti7 chhiu7-a2 kha. Chong2-si7 tiap-a2-ku2 chhin-chhiuN7 bo5 tai7-chi3, i chiu7 chhut-lai5 peh-chiuN7 ti7 thou5-chhia thau hoaN3 ke3-khi3. Tai7-seng i khoaN3-kiN3 Bou2 Sian-siN teh kut8 chhang.  I e5 ka-chiah ng3 Pi2-tek, au7-lai5 khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 hit peng5 si7 toa7 mng5. 
Pi2-tek khin-khin-a2 loh8 chhia, chiau3 I sou2 oe7 e5 kin2 chau2 tit8-tit8 tui3 ke2-chi2 chhiu7-a2 au7-bin7 khi3. 
Bou2 Sian-siN khoaN3-kiN3 i ti7 hoe-hng5 kak, chong2-si7 Pi2-tek bo5 iau3-kin2.  I nng3 tui3 mng5-e7 ke3-khi3, iah8 lou7-be2 peng5-an chhut hoe-hng5 jip8 ti7 chhiu7-na5. 
Bou2 Sian-siN tiau3 Pi2-tek e5 soe3 nia2 saN kap oe5 choe3 ke2-lang5 lai5 kiaN chiau2-a2. 
Pi2-tek long2 bo5 theng5 kha, a2-si7 khoaN3 au7-bin7, kau3 i kau3 in tau ti7 toa7 chang5 e5 sam-chhiu7. 
I chin7 sian7 sou2-i2 i phiah8 loh8-khi3 ti7 thou3-khang e5 thou5-kha, ti7 chin7 ho2 koh nng2 e5 soa-teng2, bak8-chiu khoeh-khi3.  I e5 bu2 bo5 eng5 teh chu2-chiah8.  I teh siuN7 m7 chai Pi2-tek e5 saN chaiN2-iuN7. Si7 ti7 nng7 le2-pai3 ku2 e5 tiong-kan Pi2-tek u7 sit-loh8 te7 ji7 nia2 e5 saN kap oe5.
Chin7 kho2-sioh chit e7-hng Pi2-tek bo5 sim2-mih8 peng5-an.
I e5 lau7-bu2 hou7 i khi3 khun3, iah8 hou7 I chiah8 chit8 thiap ioh8.
Chong2-si7 Put8-si, Mou5-si, kap Mi5-be2 ti7 e7-hng tng3 thang chiah8 mi7-pau, gu5-leng kap hou2-m7. 
Mng7.  Pi2-tek m7 thiaN lau7-bu2 e5 ka3-si7 u7 tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 kan-khou2 ? 
(1) Hiam2 hou7 lang5 liah8-khi3. 
(2) Sit-loh8 i e5 saN kap oe5. 
(3) Ti3-kau3 phoa3-piN7. 
(4) Bo5 thang chiah8 e5-hng e5 ho2 tng3. 

Seng3-keng kong2, “Tioh8 thiaN lau7-pe7 e5 ka3-si7, m7 thang khi3-sak lau7-bu2 e5 chi2-kau3.”  Chin-gian5 1 chiuN 8 chat. 
CHIT8 TE3 HUN5
Ti7 he7-thiN chit8 cha2-khi2 chin7 joah8, u7 chit8 te3 soe3 te3 hun5 tui3 hai2-nih8 khi2-lai5, iah8 khin-khin-a2 phu5, hoaN-hoaN hi2-hi2, keng-ke3 ti7 chheng e5 thiN.
Li7-khui ti7 chit8 e5 in-ui7 khong3-han7, ti3-kau3 chhiah-chhiah ta-ta pha-hng e5 toe7 chin7 hng7, chit te3 soe3 te3 hun5 oe7 khoaN3-kiN3 chiah e5 kho2-lin5 e5 lang5 ti7 toe7-nih8 teh choe3 kang kan-khou2, ti7 chiah e5 chin7 joah8 e5 chhan5, hit si5 i ka-ki7 phu5 ti7 cha2-khi2 si5 bi5-bi5 e5 hong, chia, hia, bo5 khoaN3-kiN3 poaN3 hang7.
I chiu7 siuN7, “Aih8 ! goa2 m7 chai oe7 pang-chan7 chiah e5 e7-toe2 kho2-lin5 e5 lang5 ; hou7 in choe3 kang khah khoai3, a2-si7 hou7 iau e5 lang5 thang chiah8, chhui3-ta e5 lang5 thang lim boe7 ?
Tui3 chit si5 chit8 te3 hun5 na2 ku2 na2 khah toa7, hou7 i e5 sim koh khah toa7 ng3-bang7 oe7 tam7-poh8-a2 pang-chan7 toe7-nih8 e5 lang5.
Ti7 toe7-nih8 na2 ku2 na2 joah8 ; chit e5 jit8 sio kau3 chin7 hiong5, sou2-i2 lang5 tui3 hit e5 jit8-soaN3 e5 sio kau3 hun7-khi3 ; tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 beh joah8 si2, chong2-si7 in kou-put-chiong tioh8 choe3 kang, in-ui7 in chin7 song3-hiong.  U7 si5 in khia7 teh kiah8-thau5 khoaN3 hun5, chhin-chhiuN7 teh kiu5 kong2, “Aih8 ah ! li2 oe7 pang-chan7 gun2 boe7.”
Hun5 in3 kong2, “Beh ouh ; goa2 beh pang-chan7 lin2.”  I chiu7 khin-khin-a2 loh8-lai5 khah kun7 ti7 toe7-nih8. 
I teh loh8 e5 si5 hut-jian5-kan ki3 tit chit8 hang7 tai7-chi3, chiu7-si7 i ti7 soe3-soe3 e5 si5, u7 thiaN-kiN3 kong2 hun5 na7 siuN kun7 ti7 toe7-nih8 oe7 si2.  I ki3 tit chit e5 e5 si5, chiu7 m7 kaN2 khah loh8-lai5, tng2-khi3, ti7 bi5-bi5 e5 hong, siuN7 koh siuN7.
Kau3 lou7-be2 i chiu7 khia7 teh chin7 tiaN7, ho2 taN2 kiau-kiah8 kong2, “Lin2 toe7-nih8 e5 lang5 ah ! bo5 lun7 tu2-tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 goa2 beh pang-chan7 lin2.”
Chit e5 su-siuN2 hou7 i hut-jian5-kan hui-siong5 toa7 koh u7 lat8.  I m7 bat siuN7 tioh8 oe7 chiah toa7.
Chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 e5 u7 koan5 oe7 kang3 hok-khi3 e5 thiN-sai3 khia7 ti7 toe7-chiuN7, kiah8-thau5, thi2-khui i e5 sit8 keng-ke3 chhan5-nih8 kap chhiu7-na5.
I si7 chin7 toa7, chin7 ui-hong, sou2-i2 lang5 kap oah8-mih8 khoaN3-kiN3 i, keng3 koh ui3. Chiah e5 chhiu7 kap chhau2-a2 phak ti7 i e5 bin7-cheng5 ; chong2-si7 long2-chong2 e5 oah8-mih8 chai i si7 ho2 e5 i3-su3. 
Hun5 chiu7 koh kong2, “Si7 ah, goa2 beh pang-chan7 lin2 ; lin2 tioh8 chiap-lap8 goa2 ; goa2 beh eng7 goa2 e5 siN3-mia7 hou7 lin2.”
I kong2 chit ku3 oe7 e5 si5, u7 chin7 kng chio3 chhut-lai5, lui5 e5 siaN moa2 thiN khu7-khu7 hau2, hun5 moa2-moa2 e5 thiaN3 kong2 boe7 chhut ; i loh8-khi3 kau3 ti7 toe7-nih8, pang3-sak i e5 siN3-mia7 chiah choe3 chit8 chun7 hok-khi3 kiu3-mia7 e5 hou7. 
Hit chun7 hou7, si7 hun5 toa7 e5 sou2 choe3, chiu7-si7 i u7 si2, chong2-si7 si7 choe3 i e5 eng5-kng. Keng-ke3 long2-chong2 e5 thou2-toe7 hou7 sou2 u7 loh8 kau3 e5 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 e5 ho2 khoaN3 e5 kheng7 chhut-hian7 iah8 long2-chong2 thiN-nih8 e5 kng-soaN3 u7 hian2-chhut i e5 sek ; chiu7-si7 lou7-be2 chit8 hang7 e5 thiaN3 i sou2 hian3 e5.
Bo5 joa7 ku2 chiu7 bo5 khi3, chong2-si7 tui3 hit tiap8 liau2-au7 chiah e5 u7 hou7 hun5 kiu3 e5 lang5 kap oah8-mih8 ki3-tit i e5 un-tian2 ti7 in e5 sim kau3 ku2-ku2.
(1)Mng7. Hun5 siuN7 beh li7-ek lang5 chiu7 chaiN2-iuN7 ? Chiu7 ka-ki7 na2 piN3 na2 toa7. 
(2)Hun5 chaiN2-iuN7 chiah oe7 li7-ek lang5 ? Kam-goan7 khek-ki2. 
(3) Hun5 ka-ki7 tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8 ? Hou7 i kiu3 e5 lang5 kap oah8-mih8, ku2-ku2 ki3-tit i e5 un-tian2. 
Seng3-keng kong2, “M7 thang toaN-toaN kou3 ka-ki7 e5 tai7-chi3, ia7 tioh8 kou3 pat8 lang5 e5 tai7-chi3. ” Hui-lip8-pi2 2 chiuN 4 chat. 
Koh kong2, “U7 chit8 ku3 oe7 thang sin3 thang o-lo2, chiu7-si7 Ki-tok Ia5-sou lim5-kau3 se3-kan kiu3 u7 choe7 e5 lang5.”  The5-mou5-thai3 chian5 1 chiuN 15 chat.
SOE3 CHIAH ANG5 KOE BU2	
Chit8 chiah soe3 chiah e5 ang5 koe bu2 kap I e5 koe-a2 ti7 chhan5-nih8, I chhe7 tioh8 chit8 liap8 sio2 beh8.
Chiu7 mng7 kong2, “Chi7-chui7 beh chai chit liap8 sio2 beh8? ”
Go5 chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Ah iah8 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Chit chiah soe3 chiah ang5 koe bu2 kong2, “Goa2 chiu7 beh, ” iah8 I khi3 chai chit liap8 sio2 beh8.
Chit chang5 beh8 sek8 e5 si5,I mng7 kong2, “Chi7-chui7 beh theh8 chiah e5 beh8 kau3 bo7-nih8?”
Go5 chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Ah iah8 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Chit chiah ang5 koe bu2 kong2, “Goa2 chiu7 beh,” iah8 I theh8 chiah e5 beh8 khi3 ti7 bo7-nih8.
Au7-lai5 theh8 mi7-hun2 to3-lai5 I mng7 kong2, “Chi7-chui7 beh eng7 chit e5 mi7-hun2, choe3 mi7-pau? ”
Go5 chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Ah iah8 kong2, “Goa2 m7.”
Chit chiah soe3 chiah e5 ang5 koe bu2 kong2, “Goa2 chiu7 beh, ”
Mi7-pau sio ho2 e5 si5, I mng7 kong2,
“Chi7-chui7 beh chiah8 chit e5 mi7-pau? ” 
Go5 chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 beh.” 
Ah iah8 kong2, “Goa2 beh.” 
Chit chiah soe3 chiah ang5 koe-bu2 kong2 “Bo5, lin2 bo5 thang chiah8.  Goa2 beh ka-ki7 chiah8, Tok8, tok8 !”  I chiu7 kio3 i e5 koe-a2 lai5 pang-chan7 i chiah8.
(1)Mng7. Ang5 Koe-bu2 chaiN2-iuN7 thang tit-tioh8 mi7-pau ? U7 cheng3 beh8, bo7 choe3 mi7-hun2, ia7 sio mi7-pau, 
(2)Go5 kap ah chaiN2-iuN7 bo5 thang chiah8 mi7-pau ?  In-ui7 pin5-toaN7 m7 ai3 choe3 kang.
Seng3-keng kong2, “Na7 u7 lang5 m7 kheng2 choe3 kang chiu7 m7 thang chiah8 png7.”
KiuN-piaN2 Ang-a2. 	
Cha2 u7 chit8 pai2 u7 chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5, kap soe3-han3 e5 lau7 ta-pou-lang5, in ka-ki7 khia7 ti7 chit8 keng soe3 keng ku7 chhu3.  In long2 bo5 soe3-han3 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, a2-si7 ta-pou gin2-na2.  Sou2-i2 chit8 jit8 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 eng7 kiuN-piaN2 choe3 chit8 e5 ta-pou gin2-na2; ka7 i choe3 chi- ku-lat8 saN, eng7 jiok8-kui3-chi2 choe3 liu2-a2; bak8-chiu si7 eng7 sui2 koh phauh e5 phu5-to5 choe3; chhui3 eng7 ang5 sek e5 thng5 choe3; iah8 eng7 kam thng5 choe3 chit8 teng2 sui2-sui2 soe3 teng2 e5 bo7-a2.
Chit si5 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 choe3 ho2, chiu7 ka7 i chheng7 saN, iah8 nih8 i e5 kha, chhin-chhiuN7 chheng7 oe5 e5 khoan2-sit, he7 ti7 chit8 e5 poaN5. Au7-lai5 chiu7 chiong chit e5 poaN5 he7 ti7 he2-lou5 ia7 koaiN mng5. I siuN7 “TaN goa2 u7 chit8 e5 goa2 ka-ki7 e5 soe3-han3 ta-pou gin2-na2.”
Kau3 sio ho2 e5 si5, i khui he2-lou5 mng5, thoa chhut chit e5 poaN5.  Chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2, ta-pou gin2-na2, thiau3-chhut-lai5 ti7 thou5-kha, iah8 chau2-chhut mng5-khau2 kau3 ti7 koe-lou7 nih8.  Chit e5 soe3-han3 lau7 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kap chit e5 soe3-han3 lau7 e5 ta-pou-lang5 chiau3 in sou2 oe7 e5 kin2, jip i, chong2-si7 i chhio3 ia7 toa7 siaN kong2, “Chau2, chau2, khoaN3 lin2 oe7 joa7 kin2.  Lin2 liah8 goa2 boe7 tioh8, goa2 si7 chit8 e5 kiuN-piaN2 lang5.” In liah8 i boe7 tioh8.  
Chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2 tit8-tit8 chau2, kau3 ti7 lou7-piN u7 chit8 chiah gu5. 
Gu5 kong2, “Soe3-han3 kiuN-piaN2 e5 gin2-na2, theng5 ah, Goa2 ai3 chiah8 li2.” 
Chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 e5 gin2-na2 chhio3, soa3 kong2, “Goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5, kap chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 ta-pou-lang5. Goa2 iah8 oe7 chau2 li7-khui li2, oe7 ouh.” 
Hit si5 chit chiah gu5 jip i, i oat8-ke3 khi3 khoaN3, kong2, “Chau2, chau2, khoaN3 li2 oe7 joa7 kin2. Li2 liah8 goa2 boe7 tioh8; goa2 si7 chit8 e5 kiuN-piaN2 lang5.” 
Gu5 liah8 i boe7 tioh8.
Chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 ta-pou gin2-na2 tit8-tit8 chau2, koh chau2 kau3 chit8 e5 chhau2-tiuN5 u7 chit8 chiah be2.  Chit chiah be2 kong2, “ChhiaN2 theng5, soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2, li2 khoaN3 liau2 chin ho2 chiah8.”
Chong2-si7 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2 toa7 siaN chhio3, kong2, “Oh ah ! oh ah ! Goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5, chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 ta-pou-lang5, koh chit8 chiah gu5.  Iah8 goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui li2, oe7 ouh.” 
Hit si5, be2 jip i, i oat8-ke3 khi3 khoaN3 kong2, “Chau2, chau2, khoaN3 li2 oe7 joa7 kin2. Li2 liah8 goa2 boe7 tioh8, goa2 si7 chit e5 kiuN-piaN2 lang5.” 
Be2 liah8 i boe7 tioh8. 
Bo5 joa7 ku2 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiun-piaN2 gin2-na2 kau3 ti7 chhek-chhng, u7 choe7-choe7 siak-tiu7 e5 lang5. Chiah e5 lang5 phiN7-kiN3 chit e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2 ai3 ka7 i liah8 lai5, kong2, “Soe3-han3 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2, m7 thang chau2 hiah kin2, li2 khoaN3 liau2 chin ho2 chiah8.”
Chong2-si7 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-Na2 pi2 tai7-seng khah chhut-lat8 chau2, iah8 I chau2 e5 si5 kio3 kong2, “Goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5, chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 ta-pou-lang5, chit8 chiah gu5, koh chit8 chiah be2. Ia7 goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui lin2, oe7 ouh.”
Hit si5 i khoaN3-kiN3 i2-keng chau2 li7-khui siak-tiu7 e5 lang5, i oat8-ke3 khi3 khoaN3, toa7 siaN ka7 in kong2, “Chau2, chau2, khoaN3 lin2 oe7 joa7 kin2. Lin2 liah8 goa2 boe7 tioh8, goa2 si7 chit8 e5 kiuN-piaN2 lang5.”   
Iah8 siak-tiu7 e5 lang5 liah8 i boe7 tioh8. 
Au7-lai5 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2 pi2 tai7-seng chau2 khah kin2.  I tit8-tit8 chau2, kau3 i kau3 chit8 khu chhan5, u7 choe7-choe7 koah-tiu7 e5 lang5.  Hiah e5 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 i chin7 ho2 khoaN3, in jip i kio3 kong2, “Theng3-hau7 chit8 e7 ! Theng3-hau7 chit8 e7, soe3-han3 kiuN-piaN2 gia2-na2 !  Gun2 ai3 chiah8 li2.”
Chong2-si7 chit e5 soe3-han3 e5 kiuN-piaN2 gin2-na2 pi2 tai7-seng khah chhut-lat8 chhio3, iah8 chhin-chhiuN7 hong tit8 chau2. 
I kong2, “Oh hou3 !  Oh hou3 !  Goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5, chit8 e5 soe3-han3 e5 lau7 ta-pou-lang5, chit8 chiah gu5, chit8 chiah be2, koh chit8 e5 chhek-chhng e5 siak tiu7 e5 lang5.  Iah8 goa2 oe7 chau2 li7-khui lin2 oe7 ouh.”
Hit si5 i khoaN3-kiN3 i u7 chau2 chhut hiah e5 koah-tiu7 e5 lang5, i oat8-ke3 khi3 khoaN3, toa7 siaN ka7 in kong2, “Chau2, chau2, khoaN3 lin2 oe7 joa7 kin2.  Lin2 liah8 goa2 boe7 tioh8, goa2 si7 chit8 e5 kiuN-piaN2 lang5.” Iah8 chiah e5 koah-tiu7 e5 lang5 liah8 i boe7 tioh8.

I chai in khin-khin-a2 bong i e5 thau5, kap toa7 ki, soe3 ki.  I chiu7 ka-ki7 chiau3 i sou2 oe7 e5 hng7-hng7 khoaN3 loh8 lai5, i khoaN3-kiN3 i u7 tiau3 kim kap gun5 e5 lian7-a2.  U7 peh8-peh8, khin-khin e5 mih8 kui tiau5 sui5 ti7 i e5 si3-ui5 ; I e5 ki u7 he7 soe3 liap8 kim e5 jin5 kap chui2-ang5, tho5-ang5 e5 kiu5 kap gun5 e5 chhiN ; iah8 u7 sui2-sui2 soe3 ki ang5 kap peh8 e5 lah8-chek, ti7 i e5 ki ; chong2-si7 lou7-be2 te7 it ki5-koai3 e5 si7 chiah e5 gin2-na2 u7 tiau3 chit8 sian sui2 koh peh8, teh pe e5 ang-a2 thiN-sai3 ti7 i e5 thau5-khak.  Chit chang5 soe3 chang5 sam-hhiu7 hoaN-hi2 ki5-koai3 kau3 boe7 chhoan2-khui3.  I chit tiap8 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2 ?  In-ui7 chaiN2-iuN7 hou7 i chiah eng5-kng ?
Koh chit8 tiap-a2 ku2 tak8 lang5 long2 khi3, chhun i chit8 e5. Lioh8-a2 beh am3, chit chang5 soe3 chang5 sam-chhiu7 chiu7 thiaN-kiN3 ki5-koai3 e5 siaN, tui3 koaiN-teh e5 mng5 ke3-lai5. U7 si5 thiaN-kiN3 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 teh hau2.  I chin7 cheng7-chiauh, na2 ku2, na2 am3. Hut-jian5-kan mng5 khui, chit nng7 e5 gin2-na2 jip8-lai5.  Nng7 e5 sui2-sui2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 saN-kap lai5.  In kau3 ti7 soe3 chang5 sam-chhiu7, kin2-kin2 tiam2 long2-chong2 ang5 kap peh8 e5 lah8-chek.  Au7-lai5 chit nng7 e5 sui2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7 kng chit8 te3 u7 soe3 chang5 sam-chhiu7 e5 toh, khin-khin sak i, kin2-kin2 tui3 mng5-nih8 chhut-khi3, keng-ke3 chit8 e5 thong-hang7 jip8 ti7 pat8 keng pang5.
Chit chang5 soa3 chang5 sam-chhiu7 hut-jian5-kan khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 keng tng5-tng5 e5 pang5, u7 choe7-choe7 soe3 tiuN peh8 e5 bin5-chhng5, gin2-na2 the ti7 bin5-chhng5 e5 chim2-thau5, iah8 koh u7 gin2-na2 ti7 toa7 liau5 e5 i2, koh u7 e5 teh io a2-si7 teh che7 ti7 soe3 te3 i2.  I ki5-koai3 siuN7 chaiN2-iuN7 chiah e5 gin2-na2 khoaN3-liau2 chiah peh8, chiah sian7 ; i m7 chai i si7 ti7 i-koan2.  i boe7 koh siuN7 pat8 mih8, in-ui7 chiah e5 soe3-han3 e5 peh8 e5 gin2-na2, toa7 siaN kio3 hou7 i kau3 boe7 chhoan2-khui3.  In kio3, kong2, “Hoh !  Hoh !  Hm7 !  Hm7 !   Chin7 sui2 ! Chin7 ho2 khoaN3 !  ah!  kiam2 m7 si7 chin7 sui2 !” 
I chai in teh kong2 i , in-ui7 in long2-chong2 e5 kim-kim e5 bak8-chiu tit8-tit8 teh khoaN3 i.  I khia7 tit8-tit8 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 ki ui5, iah8 tak8 e5 chiam hioh8-be2 hoaN-hi2 kau3 gi7-gi7-chun3.
Bo5 joa7 ku2 u7 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 bo5 siaN2 lat8 e5 SiaN kong2, “Chit chang5 si7 goa2 sou2 bat khoaN3-kiN3 te7 it sui2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 tan3 chhiu7.” Kau3 lou7-be2 soe3 chang5 sam-chhiu7 chiu7 chai i si7 sim2-mih8 ; i si7 chit8 chang5 Kiu3-chu2 tan3 chhiu7.  Iah8 tui3 i e5 kng-kng e5 thau5-khak kau3 i e5 kha, moa2-moa2 hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i si7 tu2 ho2 lai5 choe3 ti7 se3-kan chit8 ho7 te7 it ho2 e5 chhiu7.
