Ho-lian-tau7 Ong5-hui
U7 chit8 e5 ong5-chu2, beh tih8 ong5-hui, ji7-chhiaN2 ai3 si7 hap8-keh--e5, m7-chiah si3-ke3 khi3 chhoe7, m7-koh bo5-lun7 i khi to2-ui7, tai7-chi3 long2 be7 sun7.  Se3-kai3-siong7 u7 chin-che7 ong5-hui, m7-koan2 in si7-m7-si7 hap8-keh--e5, ong5-chu2 long2 chhoe7 bo5.  Jin5-seng put-ju5-i3, si7 chin chu3-siong5 e5 tai7-chi3.  In-ui7 i chiok siuN7 beh tih8 chiaN3-kang2 e5 ong5-hui, m7-chiah toa3 tioh8 hi-bi5 e5 sim-cheng5 tng2-lai5 chhu3.  
U7 chit8-kang hong-thai-thiN e5 e7-pou, lui5-kong sih-na3 siam2-sih, thiN teh to3-hou7!  U7-kau3 khiong2-pou3!   Hit e5 si5-chun7 u7 lang5 teh khok8 siaN5-mng7, lau7-kok-ong5 to7 kiaN5 chhut-khi3 ka7 khui-mng5.  
Khia7 ti7 goa7-khau2 m7-si7 siong7-te3, si7 chit8 e5 ong5-hui.  ThiN-khi3 chiah bai2, si7 an2-choaN2 i e7 chhut-hian7--le[leh]!  I e5 thau5-mng5 kah[kap] saN hou7 hou7-chui2 ak kah tam5-lok-lok, lian5 chheng7-e5 e5 kha to7 tam5 kah kha-au7-teN, ji7-chhiaN2 kong2 i si7 chin-chin-chiaN3-chiaN3 e5 ong5-hui!  
“Ou3, goan2 chin-kin2 to7 e7 chha5 chheng-chho2.”  Lau7-hong5-hio7 an2-ne teh siuN7, m7-koh tiam7-tiam7 bo5 kong2 siaN2.  Chit e5 ong5-hui jip8-khi3 pang5-keng liau2, ka7 chhu bin5-chhng5 e5 mih8-kiaN7 pak-tiau7, khng3 chit8 liap8 ho-lian-tau7 to7[ti7] bin5-chhng5-kha.  Jian5-au7 theh8 20 nia2 than2-a2, kham3 ti7 chit liap8 ho-lian-tau7 e5 teng2-bin7, koh iong7 20 nia2 ah-mng5 cho3 e5 jiong5-a2-phoe7 kham3 tou7[ti7] than2-a2 bin7-teng2.  Hit am3 chit e5 ong5-hui to7 khun3 hia.  
Keh-kang chai2-khi2, in mng7 ong5-hui khun3 liau2 ho2--bo5?  Ong5-hui in3-kong2: “Ou3, u7 kau3 kan-kou2, kui am3 long2 khun3 be7-loh8-bin5!  Siong7-te3 chai-iaN2 goa2 khun3 siaN2-mih mih8-kiaN7--a!  Khun3 e5 mih8-kiaN7 siuN teng7 ti3 su2 kui sin-khu choan5 siong-hun5, na3[nah] e7 chiah khiong2-pou3!”  
M7-chiah lau7-kok-ong5 kah[kap] lau7-hong5-hio7 chiah khak-jin7 chit e5 si7 chin-chin-chiaN3-chiaN3 e5 ong5-hui, in-ui7 chhu 20 ian5 than2-a2 kah[kap] 20 nia2 ah-mng5 cho3 e5 jiong5-a2-phoe7, iau2 koh kam2-kak e7 tioh8 chit liap8 ho-lian-tau7.  Kan-taN7[taN] chin-chiaN3 e5 ong5-hui chiah u7 chit chiong2 iu3- mi[mi5]-mi[mi5] e5 phoe5-hu.  
Chu7-an2-ne ong5-chu2 chhoa7 i cho3 khan-chhiu2, chit-ma2 ong5-chu2 chheng-chho2 chhoa7--tioh8-e5 si7 chin-chiaN3 e5 ong5-hui, chit liap8 ho-lian-tau7 khng3 ti7 tian2-lam2-koan2, hian7-chhu2-si5 ma7 khoaN3 e7 tioh8, kam2 bo5 lang5 theh8 khi3 chhi3!  Chu3-i3 thiaN ou5!  Che7 si7 khak-sit8 e5 kou2.
