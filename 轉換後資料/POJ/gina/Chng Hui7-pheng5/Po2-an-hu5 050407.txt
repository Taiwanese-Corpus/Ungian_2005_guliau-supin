
Po2-an-hu5
Ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui ti7 bit8-goat8 lu2-heng5 e5 si5, in kam2-kak se3-kai3 hoaN-hi2, kan-taN hoan5-lo2 chit8 hang7 nia5, he tioh8-si7: Lan2 kam2 u7-hoat-tou7 eng2-oan2 chhiuN7 chit-ma2 chiah khoai3-lok8?  M7-chiah beh tih8 chit8 e5 po2-an-hu5, ti7 hun-in tang-tiong tu2 tioh8 chou2-gai7 e5 si5 lai5 po2-hou7--in.  
Chit-chun7 in tiaN7-tiaN7 thiaN-tioh8 chit8 e5 lang5, toa3 ti7 chhiu7-na5, tak8-e5 tui3 i e5 ti3-hui7 long2 chin chun-tiong7.  I chai-iaN2 tui3 mui2-e5 khun3-lan5, put-heng7 beh an2-choaN2 hou7 in siong7-ho2 e5 i3-kian3.  M7-chiah ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui khi3 chhoe7--i, ka7 i kong2 in hoan5-lo2 e5 tai7-chi3.  Chit e5 ti3-hui7-chia2 thiaN liau2, in3-kong2: “Khi3 khoan5-iu5 se3-kai3, na7 tng7-tioh8 chit8-tui3 chin-chiaN3 boan2-chiok e5 ang-a2-bou2, ka7 in tho2 chit8 te3 se3-te3 e5 lai7-saN-pou3, toa3 ti7 sin-khu, to7 u7-hau7.”  
In khia5-be2 khi3-sng2, bo5-joa7-ku2 tioh8 thiaN-tioh8 chit8 e5 bu2-su7 kap in bou2 koe3-tioh8 siong7 khoai3-lok8 e5 seng-oah8.  Ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui to7 kau3 hit e5 siaN5 khi3 mng7--in, ai3 in chhin-chhui3 kong2: in e5 hun-in kam2 chin-chiaN3 chhiuN7 lang5 teh kong2--e5 hiah-ni[nih] khoai3-lok8?  In in3-kong2: “Bo5-m7-tioh8, m7-koh u7 chit8 hang7 gui5[ui5]-ham7: in bo5 gin2-a2!”  Hit-chun7 ti7 hia chhoe7 bo5 ti3-hui7-chia2, m7-chiah ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui ke3-siok8 lu2-heng5 khi3 chhoe7 siong7 boan2-chiok e5 ang-a2-bou2.  
Soa3--loh8 in kau3 chit8 e5 tou-chhi7, ti7 hia thiaN-tioh8 chit8 e5 lau2-sit8 e5 chhi7-bin5, kap in bou2 tau3-tin7 liau2 siong7 hah8 kap siong7 boan2-chiok.  Ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui kang7-khoan2 khi3 chhoe7--in, mng7-kong2 in si7-m7-si7 khak-sit8 chhiuN7 thoan5-soat--e5 an2-ne.  Chit e5 chhi7-bin5 hoe5-kong2: “Si7--a, goan2 u7-iaN2 si7 an2-ne, goan2 na7 bo5 hiah che7  gin2-a2, goa2 kap goan2 bou2 to7 e7-tang3 koe3 siong7 ho2 e5 seng-oah8, in-ui7 gin2-a2 toa3 hou7 gun2 iu-bun7 kap hoan5-lo2!”  
Ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui kang7-khoan2 chhoe7 bo5 ti3-hui7-chia2, in khoan5-iu5 se3-kai3 liau2, iau-chhiaN2 tak8 sou2-chai7 in sou2 pai3-hong2 koe3, boan2-chiok e5 ang-a2-bou2, m7-koh bo5 lang5 lai5.  
U7 chit8 kang ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui ti7 ia2-goa7 chhau2-te7 khia5-be2 e5 si5, chu3-i3 tioh8 chit8 e5 chhi7-iuN5--e5, chin hoaN-hi2 teh pun5 phin2-a2, hit si5-chun7, khoaN3-tioh8 chit8-e5 chit8 chhiu2 pho7 gin2-a2, chit8 chhiu2 khan gin2-a2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 kiaN5 koe3-lai5 chhi7-iuN5--e5 hia.  
 
Chhi7-iuN5--e5 chit8 khoaN3-tioh8 in bou2, to7 khi3 chiap in bou2.  I na2 tui3 in bou2 mng7-ho2 na2 ka7 gin2-a2 chiap koe3-lai5 chim koh so.  Chhi7-iuN5--e5 e5 kau2-a2 chau2 lai5 gin2-a2 e5 piN--a2, chuiN7 gin2-a2 e5 chhiu2, ma7 hoaN-hi2 kah na2 pui7 na2 thiau3-lai5-thiau3-khi3.  Kang7 hit e5 si5-chun7 in bou2 theh8 chhut i chah--lai5, chhoan5-ho2 e5 te5-koan3-a2, kong2: “Ang--e[e5], lai5 chiah8-te5--ou[ouh].”  
Chhi7-iuN5--e5 che7--loh8-lai5, ka-ti7 chhoan5 oaN2-poaN5. M7-koh thau5-chit8 chhui3  hou7 gin2-a2 chiah8, te7-ji7 chhui3 pun hou7 gin2-a2 kap kau2.  Kui e5 koe3-teng5 ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui long2 khoaN3 u7 tioh8, thiaN u7 tioh8.  Chit-ma2 ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui kiaN5 oa2 in kah[kap] in kong2-oe7, kong2: “Lin2 ang-a2-bou2 kam2 chin-chiaN3 si7 khoai3-lok8 koh boan2-chiok--e5?”  Chhi7-iuN5--e5: “Si7--a, cheng3-keng si7 an2-ne!  Kam2-sia7 siong7-te3!  Bo5 chit8 e5 ong5-chu2 iah si7 ong5-hui e7-tang3 pi2 gun2 koh khah khoai3-lok8.”  
Ong5-chu2: “ThiaN hou7[hou] ho2, tui3 gun2 khah ho2--le[leh], bo5 lin2 e7 hio7-hoe2.  Hou7 gun2 chiau5 se3 te3 e5 lai7-saN-pou3.”  Chhi7-iuN5--e5 in ang-a2-bou2 tui3 chit e5 iau-kiu5 kam2-kak ki5-koai3, nng7 e5 soah khoaN3-lai5-khoaN3-khi3.  Boe2--a, chhi7-iuN5--e5: “Siong7-te3 chai-iaN2 gun2 chin goan7-i3 hou7--lin2, m7-koh tu3-liau2 gun2 ui5-it e5 chit tho3 kun5 kap chit tho3 saN i2-goa7, bo5 poaN3 te3 pou3, ma7 bo5 cho3 pou3 e5 soaN3.”  
M7-chiah ong5-chu2 kap ong5-hui ke3-siok8 in e5 lu2-heng5, ma7 bo5 ki5-chek chhut-hian7.  Lou7-boe2 in ia3-sian7 chit chiong2 tng5 koh peh8-kang e5 phun[pun]-pho, m7-chiah tui3 chhu3 tng2--khi3.  Tng in kau3 ti3-hui7-chia2 e5 chhu3, ma7-kong2 siaN2[siang2] hou7 in chiah bai2 e5 kian3-gi7.  Ti3-hui7-chia2 thiaN in kong2 kui e5 lu2-heng5 koe3-teng7.  
Koh lai5 ti3-hui7-chia2 chhio3-chhio3 kong2: “Lin2 lu2-heng5 kam2 chin-chiaN3 peh8-kang--e5?  Lin2 kam2 bo5 toa3 chin che7 keng-giam7 tng2--lai5?”
Ong5-chu2 kong2: “U7--a, goa2 u7 oh8 tioh8 boan2-chiok si7: se3-kan lan5-tit e5 un-hui7.”  
Ong5-hui: “Goa2 u7 oh8 tioh8: beh boan2-chiok, tu5 liau2 boan2-chiok i2-goa7 bo5 pat8 hang7.”  
Jian5-au7 ong5-chu2 khan ong5-hui e5 chhiu2, in nng7 lang5 i2 siong7 un-ai3 e5 piau2-cheng5 tit8-tit8-khoaN3, soa3--loh8 ti3-hui7-chia2 ka7 in chiok-hok koh kong2: “Ti7 lin2 e5 sim-koaN lai7, i2-keng hoat-hian7 chin-chiaN3 e5 po2-an-hu5.  Ho2-ho2-a2 po2-sioh, chiah be7 hou7 be7-boan2-chiok e5 sia5-mou5  lai5 ka7 lin2 chiam3--khi3!”
