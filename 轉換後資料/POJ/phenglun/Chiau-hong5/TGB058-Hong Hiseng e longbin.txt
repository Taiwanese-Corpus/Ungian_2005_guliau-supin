Hong5 Hi-seng e5 Long5-bin5
Chiau-hong5

Tai5-oan5 koe3-khi3 si7 long5-chhoan ui5 chu2 e5 sia7-hoe7, ti7 long5-chhoan lai7-te2 kou3 3 tng3 chhou-pa2 si7 tak8-e5 siong7 toa7 e5 seng-oah8 bok8-phiau. Sia7-hoe7-su7 iah si7 cheng3-hu2 cheng3-chhek long2 si7 hak8-chia2 koaN-oan5 hit koa2 toa7-thau5-a2 teh che3-teng7.

M7-koh chit koa2 koaN-oan5 hak8-chia2 teh che3-teng7 cheng3-chhek e5 si5-chun7, long2 koaiN ti7 pan7-kong-sek lai7 khui-hoe7 tho2-lun7, long2 bo5 khak-sit8 khi3 liau2-kai2 siong7 sek-hap8 jin5-bin5 e5 cho3-hoat.

To7 chhiuN7 chit kai2 iaN5-tui7 lai5 kau3 Pi-thau5-poe3, to7 tu2-tioh8 a-peh teh kan3-kiau7 Khoan5-po2-kiok8, in-ui7 choh-sit a-peh teh sio tiu7-chhau2, soah khi3 hou7 Khoan5-po2-kiok8 khui hoat8-toaN, a-peh kiau7 kong2: "Goa2 ai3 koe3 seng-oah8, hit koa2 koaN-oan5 u7 siaN2-mih lang5 lai5 khoaN3 koe3 goan2 choh-sit e5 koe3-teng5? U7 toh 1 e5 lai5 mng7 koe3 goan2 si7 an2-choaN2 beh sio tiu7-chhau2?"

Sio tiu7-chhau2 hoan7-se3 e7 eng2-hiong2 tioh8 khong-khi3 kap kau-thong, m7-koh koaN-liau5 he7-thong2 it-hiong3 koan3-si3 eng7 "kim3-chi2" e5 chhou-lou2 chhiu2-hoat, tu2-tioh8 siaN2-mih bun7-te5, na7 m7-si7 eng7 chhou-lou2 e5 hong-sek, to7-si7 hou7-siong sak koe3-lai5 koh sak koe3-khi3. Cheng3-hu2 kam2 m7-si7 beh the3 jin5-bin5 hok8-bu7--e5? Nah e7 tit8-chiap kim3-chi2, m7-si7 tau3 siuN7 ki5-thaN the3-tai7 e5 pan7-hoat?

Sio tiu7-chhau2 e7 eng2-hiong2 khong-khi3 kap kau-thong, e7-sai2 kai2-cho3 hun-khu sio, iah si7 soan2-tek hong e5 hiong3 lai5 sio, ma7 e7-sai2 cheng3-hu2 khai-chiN5 be2 tiu7-chhau2 hoe5-siu chai3 li7-iong7, chhu2-li2 e5 hong-hoat chin che7, hian7-chhu2-si5 ma7 u7 hoe5-siu e5 ki-che3, si7 an2-choaN2 chiah-e5 koaN-liau5 bo5 beh chu2-tong7 lai5 kai2-koat bun7-te5? In si7 an2-choaN2 beh am-kham3, chiah koh eng7 hoat8-toaN lai5 pek long5-bin5--leh?

Cheng3-hu2 chit-ma2 chek-kek beh khai-hong3 sam-thong, koh u7 ka-jip8 WTO e5 eng2-hiong2, toa7-liong7 ke3-siau3 ke7 e5 long5-san2-phin2 siau lai5 Tai5-oan5 chhi7-tiuN5, ap-pek tioh8 Tai5-oan5 long5-giap8 e5 hoat-tian2 kap seng-chun5, tu5-khi3 long5-san2-phin2 siu-jip8 kiam2-chio2, ma7 eng2-hiong2 tioh8 long5-bin5 ke3-siok8 cheng3-choh e5 i3-goan7.

Hian7-chhu2-si5 chhan5-chng-lang5 ju2 cho3 ju2 liau2-chiN5, m7-koh chong2--si7 koh e7 siuN7 kong2 chhan5-te7 khang-khang chiaN5 phah-sng2, m7-koh siau3-lian5-poe3--e5 to7 bo5 an2-ne siuN7--a. Chit chiong2 cheng5-heng5 na7 ke3-siok8 loh8--khi3, Tai5-oan5 long5-giap8 tioh8-ai3 soan-ko3 si2-bong5, lan2 seng-chun5 siong7 ki-pun2 e5 su-iau3, na7-si7 tioh8 oa2-kho3 chin3-khau2, oa2-kho3 China, lan2 si7 beh an2-choaN2 kiaN5 hiong3 khi2-kok--leh?
