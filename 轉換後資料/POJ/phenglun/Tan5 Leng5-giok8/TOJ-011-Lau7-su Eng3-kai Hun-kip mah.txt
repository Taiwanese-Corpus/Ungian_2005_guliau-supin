
Lau7-su Eng3-kai Hun-kip mah?                                                                    Tan5 Leng5-giok8
Chin-choe7 lang5 long2 chai-iaN2 kau3-iok8 si7 liong5-sim e5 su7-giap8, ma7 si7 eng2-hiong2 kok-ka chhim-oan2 koh ku2-tng5 e5 su7-giap8. M7-ku2 beh an2-choaN2 hou7 cho3 lau7-su e5 lang5 long2 chin u7 “liong5-sim” leh? Ki5-sit8 m7-koan2 ti7 sim2-mih8 thoan5-the2 lai7-te2, long2 u7 lang5 cho3 ho2, u7 lang5 cho3 bai2. Lau7-su ma7 si7  kang7-khoan2, u7 ho2 lau7-su, u7 bai2 lau7-su, na7 beh iau3-kiu5 tai7-ke long2 chin ho2, si7 chin khun3-lan5 e5 tai7-chi3. Lan2 ai3 siuN7..e5 si7 beh an2-choaN2 the5-seng lau7-su e5 leng5-lek8 kap ai3-sim, ka7 bo5 sek-hap8 cho3 lau7-su e5 lang5 chhoe7--chhut--lai5. Kong2 chit8-ku3 khah sit8-chai7..e5, u7 chin chio2-sou3 e5 lau7-su kin-pun2 bo5 siuN7 beh ka3-chheh, kan-ta tham-tioh8 u7 kou3-teng7 e5 sin-sui2. I m7-si7 be7-hiau2 ka3, m7-ku2 i bo5 beh iong7-sim, chhin3-chhai2 bu2-bu2--leh, si5-kan koe3--khi3 tioh8 hou2--lah. HoaiN5-tit8 m7-koan2 an2-choaN2 ka3, ma7 si7 nia2 hiah choe7 sin-sui2 na7-tiaN7, hiah kan-khou2 beh chhong3 sim2-mih8? Goa2 siuN7 cho3 lau7-su e5 lang5 ai3 ka-ki7 e7-hiau2 siuN7, lan2 bi7-lai5 long2 ai3 kho3 chiah e5 gin2-a2. Ka3 bo5 ho2-se3, hai7--tioh8..e5 si7 lan2 ka-ki7! Lan2 chit-ma2 chit8 chiam chit8 hun e5 hu3-chhut, si7 bi7-lai5 tiam2-tiam2 tih-tih e5 siu-hek8. Hu3-chhut..e5 long2-si7 teh the3 lan2 ka-ki7 cho7-hok. Na7 tai7-ke long2 e7-hiau2 an2-ne siuN7, ho2 lau7-su it-teng7 ju2-lai5 ju2-choe7.
Chit-pai2 ui7-tioh8 kau3-su hun-kip kap kho2-chek it-teng7 ai3 u7 kah-teng2 kap it-teng2 han7-che3 e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 u7 chin-chhim chin-chhim e5 kam2-chhiok.
Te7-it, lau7-su e5 khang-khoe3 kap kong-bu7 jin5-oan5 u7 chin toa7 e5 chha-piat8. Lau7-su 
tui3 gin2-a2 e5 chek-jim7 si7 tai3 kui-jit8..e5, bo5 hoat-tou7 hioh-khun3. Chiah8-png7, khun3-tau3, chhit-tho5, oan-ke, sio-phah, siu7-siong...long2 ai3 chhau-hoan5. Cho3 koe3 pe7-bo2 e5 lang5 long2 e7 kam2-kak gin2-a2 oah8 beh hoan5--si2, siuN7 beh khah kin2 sak-sak khi3 hak8-hau7 hou7 lau7-su koan2. Ka-ki7 chhu3--nih8 1, 2 e5 tioh8 koan2 kah kiong5 beh khi2-siau2 lah. Lau7-su ai3  koan2 hiah choe7 e5, si7-m7-si7 koh ju2 kan-khou2--leh? Kou2-cha2 u7 1 ku3 oe7 kong2, “Kam-goan7 gia5 1-te7 bi2, m7-goan7 phian3 1 e5 gin2-a2-phi2!” Tioh8-si7 teh kong2 gin2-a2 chin oah8-tang7 chin phaiN2 kou3. Ha7-pan liau2-au7, ka-tiuN2 chhoe7, a2-si7 kha3 tian7-oe7, gin2-a2 bo5 tng2-khi3 chhu3--nih8, long2 ai3 chhu2-li2. Kiam2 e7-tang3 chhiuN7 kong-bu7 jin5-oan5 kang7-khoan2, kong2 “goa2 i2-keng ha7-pan--lah, chhiaN2 li2 bin5-a2-chai3 chiah koh chhoe7..goa2.” Na7 chiau3 kong-bu7 jin5-oan5 e5 kui-teng7 si7-m7-si7 che long2 ai3 sng3 ka-pan--leh?
Kong-bu7 jin5-oan5 e7-tang3 iong7 hok8-bu7 e5 phin2-chit lai5 hun kah-it-teng2. Lau7-su leh?  Chin-choe7 lau7-su chin sin-khou2, ha7-pan liau2-au7 khi3 ka-teng5 hong2-mng7, ai3 liau2 chin-choe7 si5-kan. Hak8-seng bo5 khi3 siong7-kho3, tioh8 chhoe7 gin2-a2 chhoe7 kau3 “oang3-kha” khi3. Khai ka-ki7 e5 chhiN5 chhiaN2 gin2-a2 chiah8 gu5-pai5, beh ka3 in chiah8 se-chhan e5 le2-gi5! Chiah e5 tai7-chi3 kau3-iok8-kiok8 e5 lang5 long2 m7-chai. U7 lau7-su loan7 ka3, kho3-pun2 liam7 soah tong3-cho3 ka3 liau2--lah, che kau3-iok8-kiok8 e5 lang5 ma7 m7-chai. Chun2-kong2 chai, ma7 be7-hiau2 chhu2-li2. Koh-chai3 kong2, lau7-su ka3 liau2 ho2 bai2, m7-si7 te2-te2 e5 si5-kan lai7 khoaN3 e7 chhut--lai5..e5. In-ui7 hak8-seng e5 seng5-chiu7 si7 ai3 phah ki-chhou2, ban7-ban7 lui2-chek--khi2--lai5..e5, m7-si7 kin-a2-jit8  ka3, bin5-a2-chai3 chiu7 e7-hiau2..e5. Lau7-su sou2-iong7 e5 sim, si7 bo5 hoat-tou7 iong7 khoaN3..e5, niu5..e5, a2-si7 pi2-kau3 lai5 phoaN3-toan7..e5. “Chit8-iuN7 bi2 chhi7  pah iuN7 lang5”, hak8-seng u7 kui2-chiong2, chiu7 u7 kui2-chiong2 ko3-seng3, ka3 e5 hong-hoat tioh8 ai3 u7 kui2 chiong2. Tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 i-seng kang7-khoan2, ai3 phoaN3-toan7 hak8-seng e5 cheng5-heng5 chiah koat-teng7 beh iong7 sim2-mih8 ka3-oh8 e5 hong-hoat. Ti7 ka3 e5 koe3-theng5 tiong koh ai3 sui5-si5 chu3-i3 gin2-a2 e5 hoan2-eng3 kap bun7-toe5, ai3 koh siuN7 “ ioh8-hng” lai5 kai2-sian7. Chiah e5 tai7-chi3 si7 lan2 khoaN3 be7 tioh8..e5. Khong2-chu2 m7-si7 kong2 “in chai5 si kau3 (因材施教)”, bo5 lang5 e7-tang3 po2-nia2 to2 chit8-chiong2 ka3-hoat si7 it-teng7 tioh8..e5.
Hak8-hau7 e5 heng5-cheng3 jin5-oan5 ma7-si7 lau7-su kiam..e5, bo5 lang5 beh cho3 phaiN2-lang5. Sou2-i2 u7..e5 kong2 beh iong7 liu khau-a2, u7..e5 kong2 beh tui3 lau7 chu-keh e5 lang5 seng lun5, m7-koan2 an2-choaN2 chhong3, long2-si7 ke2..e5, beh cho3 hou7 goa7-hang5-lang5 khoaN3..e5 na7-tiaN7. Ti7 chit-chiong2 cheng5-heng5 chi ha7, lan2 beh an2-choaN2  ka7 lau7-su hun teng2-kip--leh? Kong2 khah phaiN2 thiaN--leh, si7 song2-tioh8 hiah e5 pin5-toaN7 lau7-su! HoaiN5-tit8 it-teng7 ai3 u7 chit8-pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 si7 te7-ji7-teng2..e5, jin7-chin ma7 bo5 it-teng7 si7 kah-teng2..e5, hiah jin7-chin beh chhong3 sim2-mih8--leh? Jin7-chin kap pin5-toaN7 long2 phiau-ki3 kang7-khoan2 si7 “te7-ji7-teng2” e5 si5, kau3-iok8 kai3 e5 lang5 kho2-leng5 chai si7 ke2..e5, ka-tiuN2 kiam2 chai?  Tui3 hiah e5 jin7-chin e5 lau7-su lai5 kong2
 “chhing5 ho5 i5 khan(情何以堪)”?
Ma7 u7 lang5 kong2 in-ui7 chai5-cheng3 khun3-lan5, che si7 cheng3-hu2 pian3-siong3 e5 kiam2-sin. Goa2 siuN7, chai5-cheng3 khun3-lan5 tai7-ke long2 liau2-kai2, sin-sui2 e7-tang3 mai3 cheng-ka. M7-koan2 cheng3-hu2 u7 sim2-mih8 li2-iu5, beh ka7 lau7-su hun-teng2, ui7-tioh8 chio2-sou3 e5 lang5, lai5 hi-seng tai7-to-sou3 iong7-sim e5 lau7-su si7 chin chan5-jim2 e5 tai7-chi3. M7-thang chhoe7 bo5 hiong-chhiu2, tioh8 ka7 u7 koan-he7 e5 lang5 long2 phoaN3-choe7--louh. Na7 it-teng7 beh hun, chhiaN2 the5-chhut hou7 lang5 sim-hok8  khau2-hok8 e5 hong-an3. E7-tang3 ka7 bo5 jin7-chin e5 lang5 chhoe7--chhut--lai5, chhiaN2 in kai2-chin3, si7 lan2 tai7-ke siong7-toa7 e5 ng3-bang7.  
Te7-ji7, hak8-lek8 koan5 e5 lau7-su khah ko-kip khah gau5 ka3 mah? Lan2 long2 chai-iaN2 ju2 jin7-hun7 e5 lang5 cho3 khang-khoe3 ju2 jin7-chin, chiah-e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 thoan5-the2 lai7-te2 an-teng7 chin3-pou7 siong7-toa7 e5 lek8-liong7. Goa2 siong7 kam2-phoe3 e5 si7 chin choe7 tiam7-tiam7 hu3-chhut e5 lau7-su. Chiah-e5 lau7-su si7 hou7 kau3-iok8 kai3 an-teng7 e5 “tin3-kok chi-po2”. In-ui7 in an-hun7 siu2-ki2 jin7-chin ka3-chheh, sui-jian5 bo5 thak8-kau3 phok-su7, m7-ku2 in ka3-oh8 e5 keng-giam7 si7 pi2 phok-su7 lun7-bun5 koh khah u7 ke3-tat8, koh khah tin-kui3! In bo5 ki3-kong ka-chiong2, m7-ku2 pat8-lang5 ki3-kong ka-chiong2 chin tek8-i3 e5 si5, in long2 si7 bou7-au7 siong7-toa7 e5 kong-sin5. Tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 khoaN3 tian7-iaN2, lan2 khoaN3..e5 si7 hiah-e5 iaN2-chheN, m7-ku2 chit8-chhut hi3 e5 oan5-seng5 kiam2-kong2 kan-ta iaN2-chheN na7-tiaN7? Lan2 khoaN3 bo5 tioh8..e5, chiah si7 siong7 sin-khou2..e5, m7-ku2 chiah e5 bou7-au7 eng-hiong5 lan2 long2 m7-bat in e5 mia5. Ti7 hak8-hau7 lai7-te2, na7 tai7-ke long2 beh khi3 thak8-chheh cho3 phok-su7, kan-taN thak8-chheh tioh8 bo5 kau3 si5-kan--lah, ka3-chheh e7 goa7 jin7-chin, lang5 ma7 bo5 siaN2 beh sin3. Tak8-pai2 na7 u7 lang5 beh khi3 chin3-siu, lan2 long2 e7 the2-liong7..in, bo5 phai3 heng5-cheng3 khang-khoe3 hou7 in cho3, kho3-pio2 koh ai3 phoe3-hap8 in pai5. Cho7-seng5 hak8-hau7 lai7-te2 ki5-tha lau7-su e5 hu7-tam. Tong-jian5, goa2 m7-si7 kong2 chin3-siu m7-ho2, chi2-si7 goa2 kam2-kak chit-chiong2 cheng5-heng5 bo5 hap8-li2. Thak8-liau2 ka-sin..e5 si7 in, u7 hak8-lek8..e5 ma7 si7 in, m7-ku2 in e5  khang-khoe3 si7 pat8 -lang5 teh cho3, sit8-chai7 bo5 eng3-kai. Goa2 chan3-seng5 chin3-siu ai3 an-pai5 ti7 siong7-pan i2-goa7 e5 si5-kan chiah tioh8. Chiam3-iong7 siong7-pan si5-kan thak8-chheh e5 lang5, sit8-chai7 bo5 eng3-kai ka-sin thiau3-kip. Koh u7 chin-choe7 lang5 thak8 chheh si7 ui7-tioh8 beh khi3 pat8-e5 sou2-chai7 ka3-chheh, tui3 sio2-hak8 thiau3 khi3 ko-tiong a2-si7 tai7-hak8, chit-chiong2 e5 lau7-su, khah ko-kip mah?
Tui3 chit8-e5 ho2 lau7-su lai5-kong2, ka3-chheh e5 keng-giam7 si7 bo5 lang5 u7 hoat-tou7 kap in pi2-pheng7. Sim2-mih8 khoan2 e5 gin2-a2 ai3 iong7 sim2-mih8 hong-hoat ka3, i bak8-chiu sio2 gio2--chit8--e7 tioh8 chai--lah. Koh-chai3 kong2, hak8-bun7 koan5 bo5 it-teng7 tioh8 e7-hiau2 ka3, chin-choe7 lang5 si7 moa2-pak e5 bak8-chui2, beh kio3 i kong2, i soa3 kong2 be7 chhut--lai5. Sou2-i2 kiu5-tui7 e5 kau3-lian7 bo5 it-teng7 si7 siong7 gau5 phah..e5, m7-ku2 i e7-hiau2 ka3.
U7 chit8-e5 sio2-sio2 e5 kou3-su7 m7-chai lin2 u7 thiaN--koe3--bo5? Chit8-keng kong-si beh choe7 1 e5 cho3 chap8-bu7 e5 oan5-kang, lai5 eng3-tin e5 lang5 u7 ko-tiong pit-giap8..e5, ma7 u7 tai7-hak8 pit-giap8..e5. Kiat-ko2 in liok8-chhu2 e5 lang5 si7  ko-tiong pit-giap8..e5. Chu2-koan2 e5 kai2-soeh si7: “ko-tiong pit-giap8 e5 lang5 e7 khah jin7-hun7, khah jin7-chin, be7 hai7 goa2 tiaN7-tiaN7 teh eng3-tin sin e5 oan5-kang.” Lan2 si7-m7-si7 ai3 siuN7 khoaN3 lan2 chit-ma2 e5 bun7-toe5 pun2-chit si7 sim2-mih8--leh?  Chit-ma2 e5 sia7-hoe7 kap hak8-hau7 sou2 chhut-hian7 e5 bun7-toe5 si7-m7-si7 kang7-khoan2--leh? Goa2 siuN7 tu2-tu2 ho2 chiu7 ho2, lan2 e5 leng5-lek8 u7 kau3 thang ka3 hak8-seng chiu7-ho2, lang5-kong2 “thai5-ke m7-bian2 iong7 kau3 gu5-to--lah!” Sio2-hak8 iong7 sio2-hak8 e5 hong-hoat ka3 sio2-hak8 e5 theng5-tou7, tai7-hak8 iong7 tai7-hak8 e5 pou7-sou3 ka3 tai7-hak8 e5 ti3-sek. Tiong7-iau3..e5 si7 lau7-su iong7-sim, gin2-a2 thiaN u7 koh oh8 u7 mih8-kiaN7. Kok-lang5 kok siu2 pun2-hun7, sia7-hoe7 chiah e7 ho5-hai5 koh an-teng7. “U7 lek8-liong7(leng5-lek8) sui-jian5 chin tiong7-iau3, m7-ku2, u7 sim iau2 koh khah tiong7-iau3.”  Lau7-su it-teng7 ai3 chin3-siu, in-ui7 si5-tai7 teh chin3-pou7, khoan5-keng2 teh kai2-pian3, gin2-a2 e5 koan-liam7 bo5 kang7--lah. M7-ku2, chin3-siu u7 chin choe7 chiong2, m7-si7 thak8 phok-su7 chiah si7 chin3-siu, tioh8--bo5?
Kau3-iok8 chin si7 chit8-kiaN7 bo5 kan2-tan e5 tai7-chi3, in-ui7 eng2-hiong2 kiat-ko2 e5 in-sou3 sit8-chai7 thai3-choe7--louh. Gin2-a2 e5 ka-teng5, seng5-tiong2 e5 khoan5-keng2, tu2--tioh8 e5 lang5, sia7-hoe7 hong-siok8, kok-ka e5 cheng3-chhek, kau3-iok8 e5 hong-hoat, long2 tui3 gin2-a2 u7 eng2-hiong2. It-poaN lang5 e5 ti3-hui7 long2 chha-put-to si7 tiong-teng2 e5 theng5-tou7, chiok-gau5 e5 lang5 lau7-su m7-bian2 siuN ka3; hoat-tian2 khah ban7..e5, nge7 beh ka7 ka3, ma7 bo5 lou7-iong7. U7 chit8-ku3 oe7 kong2 “ U7 chiong7-goan5 hak8-seng, bo5 chiong7-goan5 lau7-su”, beh ka7 1 e5 oe7-tou5 thian-chai5 ka3 hou7 chiaN5-cho3 sou3-hak8 thian-chai5 si7 bo5 kho2-leng5 e5 tai7-chi3. Na7-si7 chiaN3-kang2 e5 sou3-hak8 thian-chai5 ma7 m7-bian2 iong7 kau3 lau7-su--lah. Lau7-su siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 si7 ka7 gin2-a2 ka3 hou7 pian3-seng5 1 e5 u7 lou7-iong7 e5 lang5. Chhong-beng5 bo5 it-teng7 u7 lou7-iong7. Chin choe7 lang5 si7 “智慧型罪犯”, si7 sia7-hoe7 e5 pai7-lui7. Lang5, chit8 seN--loh8--lai5 chiu7 bo5 peng5-teng2, u7 e5 lang5 ho2-giah8, u7 e5 lang5 san3, u7 e5 lang5 khiau2, u7 e5 lang5 pun7. M7-ku2, ti7 lan2 e5 seng-oah8 tiong, sim2-mih8 khang-khoe3 long2 ai3 u7 lang5 cho3. Tak8-hang7 khang-khoe3 long2 chin tiong7-iau3. Chi2-iau3 si7 cheng3-cheng3 tong-tong sin-khou2 than3 chiN5..e5, long2 si7 u7 kong3-hian3 e5 lang5, lan2 long2 ai3 ka7 chun-tiong7, kam2-un koh kam2-sia7. Na7-si7 tai7-ke long2 beh cho3 chhiau-koe3 ka-ki7 leng5-lek8 e5 tai7-chi3, tai7-ke it-teng7 be7 khoai3-lok8, sia7-hoe7 ma7 be7 ho5-hai5. Chai-iaN2 ka-ki7 e5 leng5-lek8 kau3 to2-ui7, phah-piaN3 khi3 cho3 chiu7 ho2--louh. Lang5 kong2 hang5-hang5 chhut chiong7-goan5, hong-sui2 si7 e7 tng2-seh8..e5, thiN-kong ma7 e7 thiaN3 ho2-lang5. Chun2-kong2 bo5 chhut-thau5-thiN, lan2 e5 it-seng ma7 koe3 liau2 sit8-sit8 chai7-chai7, bo5 phah-sng2. Lan2 ai3 ka3 hou7 gin2-a2 e5 si7  “jin7-chin seng-oah8, iong7-sim choe3 lang5.”
