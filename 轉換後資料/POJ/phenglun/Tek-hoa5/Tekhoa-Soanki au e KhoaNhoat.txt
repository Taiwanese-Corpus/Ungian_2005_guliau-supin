Soan2-ki2 au7 e5 KhoaN3-hoat
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Tan5 Tek-hoa5
Ti7 chit-pai2 koan7-chhi7-tiuN2 e5 soan2-ki2 tang-tiong, Bin5-chin3-tong2 iaN5 koe3 Kok-bin5-tong2, che si7 Tai5-oan5 lek-su2 siong7 e5 chit-e5 toa7 kai2-pian3, chin choe7 lang5 tui3 chit-e5 soan2-ki2 kiat-ko2 kam2-kak chiok hoaN-hi2--e5, ma7 jin7-ui7 che si7 Tai5-oan5 hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7 e5 chit-e5 chin3-tian2, sim3-chi3 u7 lang5 e7 jin7-ui7 che si7 hoan2-the2-che3 e5 seng3-li7.

Tong-jian5, chit-pai2 soan2-ki2 e5 kiat-ko2, tui3 Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7 lai5 kong2 u7 chiaN5 toa7 e5 chiaN3-bin7 i7-gi7. Kok-bin5-tong2 thong2-ti7 the2-che3 tng5-ki5 i2-lai5 tui3 Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7 e5 khong3-che3, in-ui7 chit-pai2 soan2-ki2, e7 khai-si2 io5-tong7. Tong5-si5, che ma7 hian2-si7 Tai5-oan5 chu7-chu2 e5 sia7-hoe7 lek8-liong7 u7 kiong5-chong7 e5 chhu-se3, kap koh khah lok8-koan e5 khong-kan. 

M7-koh, goa2 jin7-ui7 u7 chit8-koa2 bun7-te5 su-iau3 lan2 koh khah chhim-tin5 khi3 su-kho2:

1. Kok-bin5-tong2 chit-pai2 e5 sit-pai7, pit-teng7 e7 ka-kin2 siu-cheng3 e5 kha-pou7, In khong3-che3 Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7 e5 chhiu2-lou7 it-teng7 e7 koh khah sim7-bit8, hoan2-the2-che3 un7-tong7 e5 thui-tong7, e7 u7 koh khah toa7 e5 thiau-chian3.

2. Chit-pai2 Kok-bin5-tong2 e7 sit-pai7, e7 su2-tit lan2 san2-seng chho3-ngou7 e5 ki5-thai7, jin7-ui7 e7-tang3 kho3 soan2-ki2 (sou2-ui7 e5 bin5-chu2 hong-sek) lai5 piaN3 to2 Kok-bin5-tong2, sin3-chi3 e7 jin7-ui7 e7-tang3 kho3 soan2-ki2 lai5 khi2-cho7 lan2 e5 Tai5-oan5-kok, tian-to3 hou-si7 tioh8 un7-tong7 chin-chiaN3 e5 chu2-the2 kap i7-gi7, ham7 lip8 soan2-ki2 e5 ham3-chiN2 tang-tiong. 

3. Lan2 eng-kai koh khah chek-kek8 cho3 chun2-pi7, eng-kai koh khah chek-kek8 lai5 kiam2-tho2 Tai5-oan5 kui-e5 sia7-hoe7 kiat-ko3 e5 bun7-te5. Lan2 beh tui-kiu5--e5 bo5 eng-kai chi2-si7 1-e5 heng5-sek siong7 e5 tok8-lip8 kok-ka na7-nia7, lan2 ai3 su-kho2--e5 si7, sin kok-ka the2-che3 sit8-chit e5 loe7-han5, lan2 ai3 su-kho--e5 si7 beh an2-choaN phah-phoa3 ku7-iu2 e5 chi-phoe3 the2-che3, beh an2-choaN2 heng5-seng5 1-e5 koh khah cheng3-gi7 kap peng5-teng5 e5 sia7-hoe7 kiat-ko3. Goa2 jin7-ui7 che chiah si7 tui-kiu5 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 chu2-the2-seng3 siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 i7-gi7.

4. Lan2 m7 si7 beh siau-kek8 pi7-bian2 Tiong-kok tui3 lan2 e5 ui-hiap8 na7-nia7. Un7-tong7 eng-kai koh khah chek-kek8 khi3 cham7 tng7 the2-che3 tui3 lang5 e5 khong3-che3. Koh chin 1-pou7 lai5 kong2, un7-tong7 tioh8-ai3 hiong3 the2-che3 toat8-hoe5 jin5-bin5 e5 su-siong2 bu2-khi3, kap bun5-hoa3, cheng-ti7, keng-che3 siong7 e5 chu7-chu2-koan5, hou7 jin5-bin5 e7-tang3 heng5-seng5 ka-ki7 e5 chu2-the2 lek8-liong7, chiah e7 chin-chiaN3 chhiok-chin3 sia7-hoe7 e5 pian3-kek ham7 lek8-su2 e5 thui-chin3, lang5 e5 ke3-tat8 kap chun-giam5 ma7 chiah e7 thut8-hian2.
