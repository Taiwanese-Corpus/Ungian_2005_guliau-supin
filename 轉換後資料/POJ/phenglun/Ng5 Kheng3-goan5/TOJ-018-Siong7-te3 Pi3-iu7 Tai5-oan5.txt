              Peh8-oe7-ji7: N? Kheng3-goan5


Keng-bun5︰ In-ui7 in long2 tui3 te7-it soe3 kau3 te7-it toa7--e5, it-bi7 tham-chai5 kap saN-chiN koan5-lek8, . . . In chhin3-chhin3 chhai2-chhai2 i-ti7 Goa2 e5 peh-siN3 e5 siong, kong2, “Peng5-an lah, peng5-an lah”, ki5-sit8 bo5 peng5-an.  In kiaN5 kho2-ouN3 e5 su7, u7 chai kian3-siau3 mah ? (Ia5-li7-bi2-su 6 chiuN 13~15 chat) 


Ti7 Liat8-kiong5-tiong Sio2-kok Seng-chun5 e5 Lou7
Tai7-pit8 (David) sou2 kian3-lip8 e5 ong5-kok, ti7 i e5 kiaN2 Sou2-lo5-bun5 (Solomon) si2 hit-ni5 (Kong-goan5-cheng5 (BC) 922 ni5) hun choe3 lam5-pak-tiau5, chiu7-si7 Pak I2-sek-liat8 Ong5-kok kap Lam5 Iu5-tai7 Ong5-kok.  Pak-ong5-kok ti7 chiaN3-chiaN3 200-ni5-au7 (BC 722), pi7 tang-pak-hong e5 kiong5-tai7 te3-kok A-sut8 (Assyria, hian7-chai7 Iraq tang-pak-hong) biat8-bong5.
Lam5-ong5-kok thoa kau3 Hi-se-ka Ong5 e5 si5-tai7, lam5-hong e5 toa7-kok Ai-kip8 tng teh kap A-sut8 cheng-toat8 chi-phoe3 Tiong-tang se3-kai3. In-ui7 an2-ni tiam3-ti7 liong2-kok tiong-kan e5 sio2-kok Iu5-tai7 si5-siong5 siu7 ka2-jip8 chian3-cheng. Ti7 BC 701 ni5, A-sut8 tai7-kun pau-ui5 Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 (Jerusalem). Iu5-tai7-kok hian5-beng5 e5 kun-ong5 Hi-se-ka ti7 kok-ka chun5-bong5 e5 si5-khek, khiam-pi khi3 chheng2-kau3 sian-ti I2-sai3-a (Isaiah). Bat u7-gian5 kok-che3 ho5-peng5 (I2-sai3-a-su 2:4) e5 chit-ui7 toa7 sian-ti bian2-le7 kok-ong5, kong2: Sio2-kok seng-chun5 e5 lou7, chiu7-si7 chi2-u7 sin3-kho3 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa, m7-bian2 kiaN-hiaN5 toa7-kok e5 kun-lek8 (I2-sai3 31:1). Ko2-jian5 ti7 it-ia7 chi kan, A-sut8 thiat-kun koaN2-tng2-khi3 pun2-kok, kok-ong5 Se-na2-ki-lip8 au7-lai5 hou7 i e5 kiaN2 am3-sat. (Liat8-ong5-ki3 Ha7 19 chiuN 32~37 chat) 
Tui3 kok-che3 hong-hun5 hoah-khi2 hoah-to2 e5 A-sut8 Te3-kok to ia7 chiam7-chiam7 soe-bi5.  Tng kok-che3 kiok8-se3 teh choan2-pian3 e5 si5, iah8 chiu7-si7 Iu5-tai7-ong5 Iok-se-a e5 si5-tai7 (BC 626), chheng-lian5 Ia5-li7-bi2 (Jeremiah) siu7 Siong7-Chu2 e5 tiau3-beng7 chiaN5-choe3 “Ban7-kok e5 sian-ti.” (Ia5-li7 1:5) Si5-kiok8 e5 ian2-pian3 chiau3 chit-e5 iu-kok e5 sian-ti sou2 u7-gian5 an2-ni, ti7 BC 612 ni5 sin-heng e5 Pa-pi2-lun5 Te3-kok (Babylonia, hian7-chai7 Iraq tong-hong) kong-phoa3 A-sut8 e5 siu2-tou Ni5-ni5-bi5 (Nineveh). Chit-si5 Ia5-li7-bi2 keng2-ko3 Iu5-tai7 kok-bin5, chit-e5 sin e5 kiong5-toa7 te3-kok bo5-ku2 oe7 “Lai5 kong-kek lin2.” (Ia5-li7 5:15) Sian-ti siong7-chhiaN2 kiong5-liat8 chi2-chek in-ui7 sian-ong5 Ma2-na2-se ok-cheng3 e5 hou7-ui5-cheng3, cho7-seng5 tek8-koan5 kui3-chok8, cheng3-kheh, kap sin5-kun3 teng2, toa3-tioh8 bo5 liam5-thi2 e5 tau5-ki phin2-keh lai5 tham-iok8 koan5-lek8, gou7-to7  kok-bin5 tui3 beh lai5 chhim-liok8 kok-keng2 e5 Pa-pi2-lun5 phau7 chit8-chiong2 houN3-ki5 koh ham3-ham3 e5 ho2-kam2 hoan3-siong2, peng7-chhiaN2 hou7 jin5-bin5 tui3 tek8-kok sit-khi3 keng2-kak-sim.  Sou2-i2 Ia5-li7-bi2 chiah chiu3-chou2 bo5 chek-jim7 e5 cheng3-kheh kong2: “U7 chai-e5 lah !  Hiah-e5 bo5 peng5-an kong2, ’Peng5-an, peng5-an--e5'.

Ti7 Liat8-kiong5-tiong e5 Ho-lu2-mou-sa (FORMOSA)
Ti7 Thai3-peng5-iuN5 e5 sai-peng5 hai2-hek8 kap A-chiu Tai7-liok8 tiong-kan e5 Tai5-oan5-to2, kau3 Kong-goan5 AD 16 se3-ki2 chiu7 tui3 Lam5-hong Hai2-to2 kap Lam5-A Tai7-liok8 ian5-hai2, u7 chit8-koa2 bo5-siang7 e5 chok8-kun5 ui7-tioh8 beh khai-pit8 sin e5 seng-chun5 khong-kan, chiu7 i5-bin5 lai5 Tai5-oan5.  Formosa e5 te7-li2 ui7-ti3 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 Pa-lek8-su-than2 (Palestine) giap8 ti7 toa7-kok e5 tiong-kan, khia7-khi2 ti7 kiong5-kok cheng-toat8 pa3-koan5 e5 lou7-tiong.  Thau5-cheng5 chit-e5 si7 ti7 lam5-pak poaN3-kiu5 hai2-lou7-siong7 e5 Bi2-le7-to2, au7-piah chit-e5 chiu7-si chiau3 Seng3-keng sou2 kong2 “Lau5 leng kap bit8” (Chhut-Ai-kip8-ki3 3:8) e5 Toe7-tiong-hai2 kap A-lah-pek (Arabia) Soa-bou5 tiong-kan e5 lam5-pak liok8-lou7 toe7-tai3. Formosa kan-ta ti7 17 se3-ki2 tioh8 i2-keng hou7 si3-e5 pang-kok thong2-ti7--koe3. Kau3 1945-ni5, te7-gou7 e5 thau5-ke Jit8-pun2 ti7 Thai3-peng5-iuN5 Chian3-cheng chian3-pai7. Chiau3 Lian5-kun e5 beng7-leng7 iu5 Tiong-hoa5 Bin5-kok ChiuN2 Kai3-sek8 e5 kun-tui7 lai5 chiap-siu7 Jit8-kun e5 tau5-hang5. Bo5 joa7-ku2 Kok-bin5-tong2 ti7 kap Kiong7-san2-tong2 e5 koan5-lek8 tau3-cheng sit-pai7, ChiuN2--ka pe7-kiaN2 chau2-thau5 bo5-lou7 chiu7 an2-ni che7--loh8--khi3, chiaN5-choe3 te7-lak8 e5 thong2-ti7-chia2.
Lian5-kun kok-kok ti7 1951-ni5 kap Jit8-pun2 sou2 kiat-lip8 e5 “Ku7-kim-soaN Ho5-peng5 Tiau5-iok” (San Francisco Peace Treaty), Jit8-pun2 seng-beng5 hong3-khi3 thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5. Chong2-si7 iu2-koan Tai5-oan5 toe7-ui7, tng-si5 iau2 ti7 ChiuN2 cheng3-koan5 pa3-chiam3 thong2-ti7 ha7, chiu7 eng7 tan5-seng3 e5 am3-si7, tiau5-bun5-siong7 chiah bo5 the5-khi2 jim7-ho5 kui-siok8. 1971-ni5 kia3-kha chai7 Bi2-le7-to2 e5 ChiuN2 Kai3-sek8 ti7 Lian5-hap8-kok e5 tai7-piau2 pi7 koaN2-chhut, tui3 an2-ni “Tiong-hoa5 Bin5-kok” su7-sit8-siong7 ti7 ChiuN2--si7 e5 chhiu2, tui3 kok-che3-siong7 lai5 siau-sit. Ti7 1972-ni5 chhe, tng Bi2-kok Chong2-thong2 Ni5-khek-sun3 (Richard Nixson) teh-beh hong2-bun7 Pak-kiaN e5 si5, Kok-bin5-tong2 Cheng3-koan5 m7-chai beh ju5-ho5 si7 ho2, ti3-su2 choan5 Tai5-oan5 e5 bin5-sim hoat-seng toa7 kiaN-hiaN5, cho7-seng5 “Kiam2-chhai2 oe7 ti7 tai7-kok tiong-kan e5 ki5-poaN5-siong7 siu7 chhut-boe7” e5 gui5-ki-kam2.  Ti7 chit-si5, Tai5-oan5 Ki-tok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7, chiu7-si7 pun2-to2 siong7-toa7 e5 bin5-kan thoan5-the2 e5 chit8-e5, ti7 Nixson hong2-bun7 Tiong-kok e5 chin3-cheng5, mou7-hiam2 koh kip8-si5..e5, hiong3 kok-lai7-goa7 hoat-piau2 sian-ti-seng3 e5 “Kok-si7 Seng-beng5” kong2, “Jin5-koan5 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 siuN2-su3, iah8 si7 Lian5-hap8-kok sou2 po2-chiong3--e5.” Koh kong2, “Tai5-oan5 chian5-tou5 tioh8 eng3-kai iu5 choan5-the2 chu7-bin5 kong-tau5 lai5 koat-teng7.” Che Tai5-oan5-lang5 kong-khai seng-beng5 iau3-kiu5 kong-bin5 tau5-phio3, i2-keng ti7 33-ni5 cheng5 chiu7 kong2 lah. Lo5-ma2 Kau3-teng5 hoe5-phoe lai5 kong2: “Kau3-chong oe7 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-Chu2 pi3-iu7 Tai5-oan5.” Bi2-kok Kok-bu7-iN7 iah8 hoe5-tap kong2:  “Chong2-thong2 oe7-ki3-tit lin2 e5 i3-goan7.” (Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 1972 .01) Oe7 si7 an2-ni kong2, m7-ku2, ti7 1972-ni5 ji7-goeh8 e5 “Siong7-hai2 Kong-po3” (Shanghai Communique) kong2: “Bi2-kok “jim7-chai” Tai5-oan5 si7 Tiong-kok e5 chit8-pou7-hun7.” Che put-tan7 hou7 chit-e5 sio2-to2-kok e5 jin5-bin5 sit-bong7, ma7 u7 siu7 Nixson kap Ki5-sin-kiat (Henry Kissinger) chhut-boe7 e5 kam2-kak .  M7-ku2 Siong7-te3 peng7 bo5 khi3-sak I ti7 Formosa e5 chu2-bin5. Ti7 kong-khai e5 “Siong7-hai2 Kong-po3” ki5-poaN5-siong7 bun5-chiuN, Bi2-kok su2-iong7 iau2-ku2 lau5 u7 tan5-seng3 kai2-sek e5 ji7-gan2 “Acknowledges (jim7-chai),” leng5-kho2 m7-si7 eng7 “Recognizes (seng5-jim7).” Kin-ku3 choe3-kun7 (1999. 01. 21; 2003. 12. 11) kai2-bit8 e5 Peh8-kiong choe3-ko ki-bit8 tong3-an3, ti7 “Kong-po3-siong7 khoaN3 boe7-tioh8--e5, chiu7-si7 Kissinger ti7 1972 ni5 2-goeh8 24-jit8 ti7 Nixson e5 bin7-cheng5 ka7 Chiu Un-lai5 kong2: “Goan2 boe7 chi-chhi5 Tai5-oan5 Tok8-lip8 Un7-tong7, goan2 iah8 boe7 su2-iong7 bu2-lek8 tin3-ap che un7-tong7.” OaN7-oe7 lai5 kong2 , Tai5-oan5 si7-m7-si7 oe7 tok8-lip8 si7 chai7-ti7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 pun2-sin.
1977 ni5 Bi2-kok Kha3-tek8 (Jim Carter) Chong2-thong2 hong2-bun7 Tiong-kok chin3- cheng5, TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 koh chit8-pai2 mou7-tioh8 Kok-bin5-tong2 tek8-bu7 e5 ui-hiap8, hoat-piau2 “Jin5-koan5 Soan-gian5”, chu2-tiuN tioh8 kian3-lip8 Tai5-oan5 chiaN5-choe3 “Sin koh tok8-lip8 e5 kok-ka.”  Au7-lai5 Bi2-kok Kok-bu7-iN7 phai3 koaN-oan5 lai5 Tai5-lam5 Sin5-hak8-iN7, ti7 iau2 boe7 thiah-tiau7 kou2-lo2 e5 Lek8-su2 Bun5-but8-koan2 ji7-lau5, chiu7-si7 ti7 cheng5-iN7-tiuN2 N? Chiong- hui Bok8-su-koan2, siong-tong iu2-sian7 koh chhin-chhiat e5 thai3-tou7 tui3 TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 jin5-su7 soat-beng5: Tai5-oan5 kiok8-se3 sou2 bin7-tui3 e5 bun7-toe5 si7 eng7 “Evolution (chiam7-chin3)” e5 hong-sek pi2-kau3 khah sek-tong3 (Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 1977~78). Ti7 1979 ni5 ni5-chhe, Bi2- kok kap Tiong-hoa5 Jin5-bin5 Kiong7-ho5-kok kian3-kau e5 si5, chit-e5 Pak-Bi2 tai7-kok koh chin3 chit8-pou7 eng7 kok-lai7 hoat, chiu7-si7 kin-ni5 beh ki3-liam7 25 chiu-ni5 e5 “Tai5-oan5 Koan-he7-hoat” (Taiwan Relations Act) po2-hou7 lan2 chit-te3 sio2-to2.  I e5 li2-iu5 chin beng5-hian2 keng2-ko3 kong2: Ui7-tioh8 ti7 Se-Thai3-peng5-iuN5 Toe7-khu Bi2-kok e5 koan5-ek, eng7 ho5-peng5 i2-goa7 e5 jim7-ho5 chhiu2-toaN7 lai5 koat-teng7 Tai5-oan5 bi7-lai5 e5 te7-ui7, si7 Bi2-li7-kian Hap8-chiong3-kok sou2 giam5-tiong7 koan-chu3..e5 (grave concern). Teng2-bin7 sou2 the5-khi2 e5 “Siong7-hai2 Kong-po3” kap i poe7-au7 e5 “Kissinger Pi7-bong7-liok8”, 30-goa7-ni5 lai5, put-koan2 chong2-thong2 oaN7 siaN2-lang5, chiaN5-choe3 Bi2-kok ui7 Tai5-oan5 tui3-hu3 Tiong-kok cheng3-chhek e5 ki-pun2 lip8-tiuN5.
1988-ni5 ChiuN2 Keng-kok koe3-sin liau2-au7, ChiuN2--ka Ong5-tiau5 ti7 chia kiat-sok.  Thong-koe3 keng3-khian5 sin3 Chu2 e5 hiaN-ti7 Li2 Teng-hui e5 chhiu2, Tai5-oan5 bo5 keng-koe3 lau5-hoeh chiam7-chiam7 sit8-heng5 bin5-chu2-hoa3 kap pun2-thou2-hoa3. Thong2-ti7 poaN3-se3-ki2 goa7-lai5 e5 Kok-bin5-tong2, kiat-kiok8 choe3-au7 ti7 2000-ni5 tai7-soan2 sit-pai7, oaN7 pun2-thou2 cheng3-koan5 chip-cheng3. Ti7 2004-ni5 chit-pai2 tai7-soan2 e5 si5, bian7-tui3 Tiong-kok 500 liap8 hui-toaN5 e5 ui-hiap8, Chong2-thong2 Tan5 Chui2-piN2 the5-chhut 400-ni5 lai5 te7-it-pai2 kong-bin5 tau5-phio3, koh ti7 “228 Ho5-peng5-jit8” u7 200-goa7-ban7 e5 lam5-lu2 lo2-iu3 chu7-hoat-tek eng7 khang-chhiu2 chhiah-kha e5 hong-sek, m7-na7 hiong3 tui3-hoaN7 e5 a3-pa3 Tiong-hoa5 tai7-kok toa7-siaN hoah: “No！”, iah8 hiong3 kok-che3 soan-pou3 kong2: ”Goan2 ai3 ho5-peng5, ”koh hiong3 Siong7-te3 ki5-kiu5, “SiuN2-su3 Bi2-le7-to2 peng5-an.” Ti7 Chu2-au7 2004-ni5 3-goeh8 20-jit8 Tai5-oan5 ti7 lek8-su2-siong7 te7-it-pai2 kong-bin5 tau5-phio3, si7 beh koat-teng7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 tui3 taN i2-au7, tai7-tai7 si7-m7-si7 oe7-tit-thang ka-ki7 chu7-iu5 choe3 chu2-lang5, a2-si7 ke3-siok8 beh hou7 goa7-lai5 cheng3-koan5 thong2-ti7, e5 koat-chian3-tiam2 lah.  
Tai5-oan5 chit-si5 khia7 ti7 chit-e5 lek8-su2 e5 hun-chui2-nia2, iau2 u7 chit8-koa2 toa3 tioh8 ChiuN2--ka Tiong-hoa5 Te3-kok hou7-ui5-cheng3 e5 tek8-koan5 cheng3-kheh, eng7 boe7 kian3-siau3 e5 phin2-keh tham-iok8 chai5-po2, koh ui7 in ka-ki7 teh cheng-chhu2 thong2-ti7 koan5-lek8, lai5 hou-iok8 kok-bin5 hong3-khi3 kong-tau5. In jim7-tong5 kiong5-tai7 e5 Tiong-hoa5 tek8-kok choe3 lau7-pe7, peng7-chhiaN2 gou7-to7 Tai5-oan5 kok-bin5 tui3 Tiong-kok phau7 chit8-chiong2 chho3-gou7 e5 bi2-keng2 hoan3-siong2, hou7 jin5-bin5 sit-khi3 kho2-leng5 oe7 pi7 cheng-hok8 e5 keng2-kak-sim. Sian-ti Ia5-li7-bi2 sou2 chiu3-chou2--e5 tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 chiah-e5 cheng3-kheh,ti7 500 liap8 hui-toaN5 thau5-cheng5 iau2-ku2 teh moa5-phian3 kok-bin5 kong2: “Peng5-an, peng5-an,” ki5-sit8 bo5 peng5-an.

Kiat-gu2︰Siong7-te3 Pi3-iu7 Tai5-oan5
Pun2-lang5 siong-sin3, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-te3 chhe-khian2 Mou5-se in2-chhoa7 I2-sek-liat8 chu2-bin5 chin3-jip8 Se-nai2 Khong3-ia2 (Chhut-Ai-kip8-ki3 15 chiuN), tui3 lou5-le7 soaN3-bin5 chhau-lian7 chiaN5-choe3 u7 kok-bin5 kut-keh e5 bin5-chok8 an2-ni, Siong7-Chu2 iah8 beh chiong Tai5-oan5 e5 su3-chok8-kun5 chhau-lian7 heng5-seng5 u7 Bi2-le7-to2 tek8-iu2 e5 kok-bin5 seng3-keh.  Chong2-si7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 tioh8 ai3 khiam-pi keng3-ui3 Siong7-te3 e5 pi3-iu7, sin3-kho3 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 jin5-ai3 kiu3-siok8.  Lan2 tioh8 siong-sin3 u7 chit8-jit8 Chu2 oe7 in2-chhoa7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 kau3 chhut-thau5-thiN. Che chiah si7 hian7-kim Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 e5 si5-tai7 su3-beng7 lah.                      
Chhiau-kiong5 Bi2-kok eng7 kok-lai7-hoat po2-hou7 Tai5-oan5, si7 ui7 in pun2-sin e5 kok-ka koan5-ek .  M7-ku2 Seng3-keng e5 si-jin5 keng3-ko3 sio2-kok, m7-thang oa2-kho3 toa7-kok e5 peng-ma2 chian3-chhia (Si-phian 20:7), eng3-kai tioh8 sin3-kho3 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa (Si-phian 46:1). Siong7-Chu2 tiaN7-tiaN7 teh li7-iong7 tai7-soan2 kap kong-bin5 tau5-phio3, lai5 kho2-giam7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 si7-m7-si7 u7 mai3-hiong3 choe3 chu2-lang5 e5 i3-goan7, chiu7-si7 kian3-lip8 “Eng7 jin5-ai3 sit8-heng5 I e5 kong-gi7” e5 kok-tou7 (Chim-gian5 21:21) a2-bo5. M7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 hiah-e5 ke2-ho2 e5 cheng3-kheh, ti7 am3-chiN7 tham-loan5 su-iok8 kap sia5-ok (Lou7-ka Hok-im 11:39). Tai5-oan5 Ki-tok TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 ti7 i e5 “Sin3-giong2 Ko3-pek8” hou-iok8 su3-chok8-kun5, kong2 tioh8 hou7-siong7: “Jim7-tong5 sou2-u7 chu7-bin5.” Ki-tok-tou5 ti7 kian3-kok lou7-chiuN7 e5 su3-beng7, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 “Tai5-oan5 Chhui3-chhiN” te7-ji7-chat sou2 gim5-chhiuN3 an2-ni, “Lan2 beh kian3-siat jin5-ai3 kong-gi7, Chou2-kok moa2-toe7 chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 thiN.”

Pun2-phiN kang2-to7 si7 Tai5-lam5 Sin5-hak8-iN7 Beng5-u7 Kau3-siu7 TiN7 Ji5-giok8 Bok8-su ti7 2004-ni5 2-goeh8 29-jit8 ti7 Tai5-lam5 Tang-mng5 Pa-khek-le2 Ki3-liam7 Kau3-hoe7 Chu2-jit8 Le2-pai3 sou2 kang2--e5. Tai5-gu2 Han3-ji7 goan5-ko2 khan ti7 Tang-mng5 Kau3-hoe7 Chiu-po3.
