Ko3-jin5 kap sia7-hoe7

Ko3-jin5 chiu7-si7 toaN-sin lang5 e5 i3-su3.nng7 e5 i2-siong7 e5 toaN-sin lang5 cho3-hoe2 khia7-khi2 e5 si5 chiu7 seng5 chit8 e5 sia7-hoe7.Sou2-i2 thang chai sia7-hoe7 u7 chiong2-chiong2;ka-teng5,chong-chok8,hiuN-li2,ke-chhi7,kok-ka to long2 si7 chit8-cheng2 e5 sia7-hoe7.Koh kong2 chit8 ku3 khah-beng5,sia7-hoe7 chiu7-si7 nng7-e5 i2-chiuN7 e5 lang5,ui7-tioh8 chit e5 bok8-tek cho3-hoe2 kiat-hap8 e5 thoan5-the2.Chit-ku3 『Ui7-tioh8 chit8 e5 bok8-tek,』chit ku3 put-chi2 tioh8 ai3 jin7-chin;na7 bo5 bok8-tek,sui-jian5 u7 nng7-e5 i2-chiuN7 e5 lang5 ti7-teh,ia7 put-seng5 sia7-hoe7.Koh-chai3 chiu7-si7 u7 kong-kiong7 e5 bok8-tek,hit e5 bok8-tek na7-si7 sio2-se3 a2-si3 bo5 beng5-lang2 ,sou2 kiat-chiaN5 e5 sia7-hoe7 ia7-si7 m7-chiaN5-mih8,si7 se-sap- - e5.Pi2-pheng7 sia7-hoe7 chhin-chhiuN7 chhu3,ko3-jin5 chiu7-si7 eN-a2,kak-a2,chng,thiau7,hit chit8 lui7.Chhu3 ai3 iong2;bian2 kong2 chng,chioh8,sam-thiau7 ai3 long2 iong2,chong2-si7 kan-ta an3-ni be7-iong7-tit;khi2 hit keng chhu3 e5 bok8-tek ai3 beng5-pek8 iau3-kin2.chhu3 e5 bok8-tek,m7-na7 si7 beh chah8 hong-hou7,hong5 chui2-hoe2;koh-chai3 tioh8 ai3 u7 thang-hong,u7 oe7-seng,e7 kham-tit te7-tang7,chiah e5 bok8-tek na7 siuN7 u7 kau3-giah8,koh u7 beng5-lang2 ,chu7-jian5 hiah e5 chhai5-liau7 tau3-chheng7 chiu7 e7 ho2-se3,e7 tau3-tah;khi2-liau2 chhu3 chiu7 tek-khak chin iong2,chin su3-si7.Sia7-hoe7 kap kou3-jin5 e5 koan-he7,sit8-chai7 si7 kap che siang7-khoan,ai3 sia7-hoe7 bun5-beng5 chiu7 tioh8 ko3-jin5 long2 u7 kau3-iok8,kok-lang5 kok seng5 chit8-cheng2 e5 jin5-chai5;iah8 ko3-ko3 long5 u7 beng5-pek8 in e5 sia7-hoe7 kiong7-thong e5 bok8-tek,chit khoan2 e5 sia7-hoe7 khi2-thau5 chiah u7 kian-kou3.

1.	ChaiN2-iuN7 tioh8 ai3 u7 sia7-hoe7 
Lang5 chaiN2 -iuN7 tioh8 kap lang5 cho3-hoe2 koe3-jit8, bo5, kiam2 be7 iong7-tit? Lang5 m7 kap lang5 cho3-hoe2 koe3-jit8?  Lang5 m7 tiam3 sia7-hoe7 seN-oah8 , khiok m7-si7 choat8-tui3 be-iong7-tit. se3-kan to u7 hit ho7 chhut-ke lang5 (hoe5-siuN7 ); i long2 bo5 kap se3-kan sia7-hoe7 koan-he7, ka-ti7 un2-ki chhim-soaN chhiu7-na5 e5 lai7-te2 ; si2-jit8 na7 kau3 chiah chu7 an2-ni soah. Na7-si7 ka-ti7 kam-goan7 an2-ni cho3 boan2-chiok, khiok ia7-si7 sui5-i3 cho3 chiu7 ho2.

Chong2-si7 chit khoan2 kaN2 m7-si7 tak8-lang5 kam-goan7- -e5 . Kam-goan7 m7 kam-goan7 , chhiaN2 chit8-piN , chiau3-sit8 che-si7 to7-li2 sou2 m7 iong5-un2 e5 tai7-chi3 . Li2 khoaN3 , siat-su2 lang5-lang5 na7 chin-chiaN2 oh8 chhut -ke lang5 e5 khoan2, chin-chiaN2 hou7 i siu-khi3 cho3 tou5 -te7 , lang5 bo5 loa7-ku2 tek-khek long2 tioh8 ai3 cheh8-cheng2 . Niu7 chit8-pou7 kong2, chhin-chhiuN7 hit khoan2 e5 seng-oah8 u7 sim2-mih8 chhu3-bi7, u7 sim2-mih8 i3-gi7 , u7 sim2-mih8 ho2- -chhu3 thang giu2 lang5 e5 sim neh? Kan2 -tan kong2 chit8 ku3, lang5 kap lang5 cho3-hoe2 koe3-jit8 , ai3 toa3 sia7-hoe7 seN-oah8 , che si7 lang5 e5 thian-seng3, si7 cheng5-li2 sou2 eng-kai- -e5.

Se-iuN5 lang5 u7 kong2 , 『Lang5 si7 sia7-hoe7 e5 tong7-but8 .』Chit-ku3-oe7 chiu7-si7 kong2 lang5 ia7-si7 chit8-cheng2 e5 tong7-but8 , kap phou2-thong e5 tong5-but8 koh-iuN7 e5 tiam2  , chiu7-si7 e7-hiau2 chou-chit sia7-hoe7. Sou2-i2 thang chai lang5 na7-si7 pang3-sak sia7-hoe7 , bo5 khoaN3 sia7-hoe7 e5 su7 cho3 iau3-kin2 , che si7 poe7-hoan2 cho3 lang5  pun2-lai5 e5 seng3-chek, chit khoan2 lang5 be7-iong7-tit kong2 si7 「sia7-hoe7 e5 tong7-but8 ;」kin7-kin7 si7 phou2 -thong e5 tong7-but8 , chiu7-si7 kap cheng-seN siang7 cheng2-lui7.

ChaiN2-iuN7 tioh8 ai3 u7 sia7-hoe7? Chiau2 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 si7 tui3 lang5 e5 thian-seng3 sou2 iau-kiu5- -e5 . An2-ni kiam2-chhai2 kong2-liau2 khah hun7-hoa3, be7 thang kau3-giah8 chheng-chho2 ,i2-ha7 ai3 ki2 kui2-taim2 khah u7 heng5-the2 e5 su7 lai5 pou2-chiok.
(A)	Thai3-kou2 lan2 lang5 iau2-boe7 bun5-beng5 e5 si5-tai7, in-ui7 chiah8 chheng7 e5 mih8 bo5 kau3-giah8 , sou2-i2 seN-chhut lang5 tioh8 ai3 sio-cheN sio-chhiuN2 e5 tai7-chi3 . Ti7 sio-chhiuN2 e5 tiong-kan bian2-kong2 si7 lat8 khah kiong5 e5 lang5 khah-iaN5 . Lat8 ai3 khah kiong5 ,chit8 lang5 e5 lat8 si7 iu2-han7, chu7-jian5 tioh8 ai3 kap pat8-lang5 hap8 cho3-hoe2. Chit-e5 chiu7-si7 hoat-seng ka-chok8 chong-chok8 e5 li2-iu5. Tui3 an2-ni tit8 lian5-kiat , tit8 khoah-toa7 , ka-chok8 bo5 kau3 , chiu7 siN -chhut hiuN-li2 chng-sia7 , kau3-boe2 chiu7 hian2-chhut kok-ka e5 chou-chit lai5. Che e7 thang kong2 si7 sia7-hoe7 hoat-hian te7-it e5 li2-iu5.
(B)	Koh-chai3 lang5 kap phou2-thong e5 tong7-but8 u7 chit8-tiam2 toa7 bo5 siang7 e5 thian-seng3, chiu7-si7 lang5 u7 chit8-cheng2 put-si5 ai3 chin3-pou7 hiong3-siong7 e5 sim-seng3. Lang5 u7 chit8 e5 pun2-seng3 , sou2-i2 tiaN7 - tioh8 bo5 ai3 tiaN7-tiaN7 kou3-siu2 chit8-khoan2 ; na7 u7 khah tioh8- -e5 , khah ho2- -e5 , khah sui2- -e5, chu7-jian5 chhut khui3-lat8 khi3 kiu5 khi3 cho3. In-ui7 an2-ni , chu7-jian5 tioh8 ai3 kap che7-che7 lang5 hap8 khui3-lat8, be7-iong7-tit li7-khui sia7-hoe7.
Khah ia2-ban5 e5 lang5 be7-hiau2 kap lang5 hap8 khui3-lat8 , ka-ti7 ia7 tioh8 cheng3-choh, ia7 tioh8 khi2-chhu3 , ia7 tioh8 phang2-chit, iong7 chit8 lang5 sio2-sio2 e5 leng5 -lek8 beh cho3 chit8-pah ban7-ban7 khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3 ; sou2-i2 in sou2 cho3 e5 si7 chhian2-kin7 chin m7-chiaN5-mih8, ku2-ku2 to long2 be7 chin3-pou7. Bun5-beng5 e5 lang5 chiu7 m7-si7 an2-ni , i kek8 ai3 kap lang5 hap8 khui3-lat8, i sou2 chou-chit e5 sia7-hoe7 kek8 chiau5-chng5; su7 long5 kong siong ban7-hang7 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 u7 hun-piat, kok lang5 hun-tam i sou2 kian3-tiong5 e5 su7, chhut-lat8 khi3 cho3 khi3 gian2-kiu3. M7 han7-tiaN7 an2-ni , chiu7-si7 tam-tng siang7 khoan2 chit-giap8 e5 lang5 , in ia7 si7 kek8 gau5 cho3-hoe2 chham-siong5 pi2-chiap. Tui3 chit-khoan2 in sou2 cho3 e5 tai7-chi3 hui-siong5 khoai3  chin3-pou7 , lang5-lang5 chiah chiong in sou2 tit-tioh8 choe3-ho2 e5 kiat-ko2 hian3-chhut ti7 sia7-hoe7 , cho3 sia7-hoe7 cheng3-lang5 e5 lou7-iong; lang5-lang5 long2 si7 an2-ni cho3, sou2-i2 hit e5 sia7-hoe7 tak8-lang5 to chin khoai3 chin3-pou7 , chiah e7 chiaN5-cho3 bun5-beng5 e5 lang5. Sou2-i2 thang chai ai3 cho3 bun5-beng5 e5 lang5 , chiu7 tioh8 gau5 hap8 cheng3-lang5 e5 khui3-lat8, chiu7-si7 ai3 u7 sia7-hoe7 chiah e7 eng7-tit.

(c) Koh ki2 chit8 tiam2 kong2 . 『Sam-ju7-keng』e5 tahu5 chit8-ku3 kong2 , 『Lang5 khi2-thau5 e5 seng3-cheng5 pun2 si7 ho2.』sit8-chai7 lang5 si7 long2 u7 liong5-sim , i3-ai3 cho3 ho2. In-ui7 lang5 ai3 chin7 i e5 ho2-sim , i tioh8 khi3 chhoe7 , kheng2 siu7 i e5 ho2-sim e5 tui3-chhiu2; na7 m7-si7 , i e5 ho2-sim beh khi3 cho3 tiam3 to2-ui7 leh? Sou2-i2 tioh8 ai3 u7 sia7-hoe7 , lang5 e5 liong5-sim tui3 an2-ni chiah e5 thang siu7 an-ui3, iah8 lang5 chiah e7 hoat8-phoat, chit8-si3-lang5 chiah u7 chin-chiaN3 e5 chhu3-bi7.  
	Chit8-tiam2 e5 to7-li2 si7 chin chhim , ia7 si7 cho3 iau3-kin2- -e5 . Kou2-cha2 kau3 hian7-kim e5 sia7-hoe7 tai7-khai3  long2 si7 chiau3 (A) kap (B) hit nng7 tiam2 lai5 chiaN5- -e5 khah che7 . Che be7 thang kong2 si7 bun5-beng5 e5 sia7-hoe7 . Na7-si7 chin-chiaN3 bun5-beng5 e5 sia7-hoe7 , chiu7 tioh8 chiau3 tak8-lang5 e5 to7-tek i3-sek (chiu7 -si7 liong7-sim ) lai5 chou-chit chiah si7. Lang5 ui7-tioh8 ai3 sit-heng5 i e5 ho2-sim chou-chit ho2 sia7-hoe, hou7 i e5 liong5-sim e7 boan2-chiok, che si7 cho3 lang5 eng-kai e5 pun2-hun, ia7 si7 jin5-se3 choe3-au7 e5 bok8-tek.

2.	Sia7-hoe chou-chit e5 pun2-siong3 

Chit soaN3 chiaN5 pou3, hap8 lang5 chiu7 seng5 sia7-hoe7 . Pou3 e5 hoe-ji7 u7 pah-pah khoan2 , chong2-si7 sia7-hoe7 e5 heng5-siong7 kaN2 m7-na7 chheng-chheng ban7-ban7 iuN7. Sia7-hoe7 chou-chit e5 khoan2-sit sit8-chai si7 hok8-chap8 chi3-kek8 . In-ui7 sia7-hoe7 e5 heng5-siong7 hok8-chap8 kau3 an2-ni, u7 chit8 poe3 nng7-chiaN2 e5 lang5 seN-chhut chit8 khoan2 iam3-se3 e5 kam2-liam7 kong2 , 『Se2-thai3 bu5-siong5 』chiu7-si7 khoaN3 sia7-hoe7 chhin-chhiuN7 chui2-pho hui-siong5 tong7-iau5 bo5 tiaN7-tioh8. Chong2-si7 chit-khoan2 si7 khoaN3 phoe5 bo5 khoaN3 kut sou2 ti3- -e5; na7 chin3 khah chhim kiam2-tiam2 lai5 khoaN3 , chiu7 tek-khak e7 thang khoaN3-tioh8 sai7-hoe7 e5 pun2-siong7.
ChhiaN2 jin7-chin khoaN3 , pou3 e5 hoe-ji7 sui-jian5 u7 che7-che7 khoan2 , chong2-si7 chi2-u7 nng7-cheng2 e5 soaN chit chiaN5- -e5; chit8-cheng2 si7 tit8-soaN3 ,chit8-cheng2 si7 hoaiN5-soaN3. Sia7-hoe7 ia7 si7 an2-ni . Sia7-hoe7 e5 heng5 -siong7 khoaN3-kiN3 si7 chhian-pian3 ban7-hoa3 ; m7-ku2 na7 jin7-chin ka7 hun-piat , chiu7 chai put-ko3 si7 『Li2 ,』『Goa2 ,』chit nng7 khoaN2 e5 lang5 sou2 chou-chit e5 sia7-hoe7 kiu3-keng3 si7 u7 kui2-khoan2 ? Iah8 kok-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 e5 koan-he7 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2-sit ? Koh-chai3 sim2-mih8 khoan2-sit e5 si7 khah-ho2 ? ChhiaN2 tai7-ke gian2-kiu3 khoaN3 . 『Li2 ,』『Goa2 ,』nng7-khoan2 e5 lang5 chong2-kong2 chiah u7 saN -khoan2 e5 koan-he7 . Chit saN-khoan2 e5 koan-he7 e7 thang eng7 saN ku3-oe7 lai5 piau2-pek8.
(1)	Li2 si7 li2, goa2 si7 goa2 .
(2)	Goa2 si7 goa2, li2 ia7 si7 goa2.
(3)	Li2 si7 li2, Goa2 ia7 si7 li2.
Te7-it khoan2 『Li2 si7 li2, goa2 si7 goa2 .』Chit-khoan2 koan-he7 e5 sia7-hoe7 si7 siang7 boe7 hoat-tat8 e5 si5-tai7 chiah u7 . Hian7-si5 chai7 A-hui-li7-ka e5 ia2-ban5 lang5 a2-si7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 SoaN-lai7-lang5 , chiu7 si7 khia7 ti7 bun5-beng5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan; na7-si7 hit-ho7 siu-iong2 iau2-boe7 kau3 e5 lang5 , sou2 kiat e5 sia7-hoe7 tai7-khai3 si7 chit-khoan2. Chit khoan2 e5 sia7-hoe7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 poaN5 e5 soa, tai7-ke long2 bo5 koan-lian5, put-ko3 chit8-liap8 chit8-liap8 saN-thah8 cho3 chit8-tui; na7-si7 hong chhoe kau3 , chiu7 chit8-liap8 soaN3 chit8-lou7 . Chai7 chit-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 e7 giu2 cheng3-lang5 hap8 cho3-hoe2 e5 kin-goan5 , chiu7-si7 chhut ti7 tak8-lang5 e5 pun2-leng5. (M7-bian2 lang5 ka3, ka-ti7 e7-hiau2 e5 chai5-leng5 ). Phi3-lun7 kong2 , pak-tou2 iau chiu7 ka-ti7 e7-hiau2 ai3 tit-tioh8 mih8 , tui3-tek8 a2-si7 gui5-hiam2 e5 tai7-chi3 kau3 chiu7 e7 hiau2 kiaN-hiann5 siuN7 beh hong5-pi7, a2-si7 ai3 sek-iok8 , ai3 thoan5 kiaN2-sun e5 iok8-bong7 , che long2 si7 pun2-lai5 chiu7 e7-hiau2-tit. Ia2-ban7 lang5 e5 sia7-hoe7 long2 si7 chit-khoan2 pun2-leng5 kio3 in cho3-chiaN5- -e5. Kok-lang5 e5 pun2-leng5 sou2 iau-kiu5 e5 mih8 na7-si7 tit-tioh8 liau2, iah8 chiu7 『Li2 si7 li2, goa2 si7 goa2 ;』bo5 siuN7 koh kap lang5 cho3-hoe2 , bo5 khoaN3 sia7-hoe7 cho3 iau2-kin, kok-lang5 chiu7 kiaN5 kok-lang5 sou2 ai3 kiaN5 e5 lou7 .

KhoaN3 chit khoan2 su7 cho3 eng-kai e5 lang5 chiu7-si7 li7-ki2 chu2-gi7 e5 lang5. Chu7 kou2-cha2 kau3 kin-a2 jit8 , chai7 se3-kai3 sou2 u7 biat8-bong5 e5 sia7-hoe7 kok-ka , to long2 si7 chit-khoan2 li7-ki2 chu2-gi7 e5 lang5 sou2 kiat-chiaN5- -e5. Sui-jian5 an2-ni chai7 chit khoan2 , 『Li2 si7 li2, goa2 si7 goa2 ;』e5 sia7-hoe7 ia7 u7 chit8 hang7 ho2. In-ui7 tak8-lang5 si7 siuN7 『Li2 si7 li2, goa2 si7 goa2 ;』sou2-i2 hia bo5 cho3 koe3-thau5 e5 tai7-chi3 , bo5 kan-siap8 lang5 , bo5 sok-pak8  lang5 , ia7 bo5 oa2-kho3 lang5, ka-ti7 cho3 ka-ti7 tng, put-chi2 u7 tok8-lip8 e5 hong-khi3, ia7 khah u7 peng5-teng2 e5 koan-liam, sia7-hoe7 e5 chou-chit ia7 si7 khah kan2-tan.

Te7 -ji7 khoan2, 『Goa2 si7 goa2 , li2 ia7 si7 goa2 .』 Chai7 chit-khoan2 e5 sia7-hoe7 si7 goa2 cho3 tiong-sim e5 sia7-hoe7 . Goa2 si7 chit8 lang5 , li2 si7 cheng3-lang5 ; sou2-i2 thang chai chit-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 si7 chit8 lang5 cho3 thau5 , cheng3-lang5 cho3 kha-chhiu2; si7 chit8 lang5 koan2 cheng3-lang5 ; thang kong2 si7 choan-che3 chu2-gi7 e5 sia7-hoe7 . Chai7 chit-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 , te7-it, -- cho3 thau5 e5 lang5 ai3 u7 sit8-lek8 chim-chiok; te7-ji7 , -- tioh8 u7 chin giam5 e5 hoat-tou7 thang hou7 kha-chhiu2 e5 lang5 siu2, chiah e7 thang po2 peng5-an. Na7 m7-si7 chiu7 tek-khak e7 chin loan7.

Tiong-kok chu7 kou2-cha2 kau3 hian7-si7 e5 kok-ka, to long2 si7 eng7 chit-khoan2 , 『Goa2 si7 goa2 , li2 ia7 si7 goa2 .』e5 su-siong2 lai5 chou-chit e5 khah-che7. M7-na7 Tiong-kok , se3-kai3-tiong , kok tai7-khai3 to ia7 si7 siok8 chit8-khoan2 . Chai7 chit-khoan2 , kok-ka tek-khak bo2 peng5-teng2 , ia7 bo5 chu7-iu5 ; na7-si7 u7 chu7-iu5 chiu7-si7 kan-ta chit8 lang5 , a2-si7 chit8 sio2 pou7-hun7 e5 lang5 na7-tiaN7.

Chai7 chit-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 si7 bo5 kong-li2 ia7 bo5 to7 -tek ; na7-si7 u7, chiu7-si7 chiong chit8 lang5 a2-si7 kui2 e5 lang5 e5 su-khia cheng5-li2 , kiong5-kiong5 au2-chhih8 cheng3-lang5 tioh8 ai3 sin3, tioh8 chiau3 an2-ni kiaN5. Chong2 kong2 , chai7 chit-khoan2 sia7-hoe7 , 『kiong5-koan5 chiu7-si7 kong-li2.』 U7 se3-lek8 e5 lang5 sou2 kong2 e5 chiu7 si7 kong-li2 , chiu7-si7 to7-tek lah ! Ban7-su7 si7 phin2 lat8 ; kun5-thau5-bu2 khah toa7-liap8 e5 lang5 chiu7 chheng 『Ong5 』chheng『Te3 ;』se3-liap8 e5 lang5 tiaN7-tioh8 si7 ku3-chai7 i pai5-pi2. In-ui7 an2-ni , lang5 khui3-lat8 bo5 kau3 e5 si5 chiu7 lun2-khi3 thun-siaN, si2-sim hok8-chiong5 hau3-sun7. M7-ku2 kau3 chit8-jit8 sit8-kou2 mng5 na7 hoat kau3-giah8 , i chiu7 siuN7 beh poe koan5; m7-na7 m7 hou7 pat8 lang5 koan2 , hoan2-tng2 soa3 siuN7 koan2 pat8 lang5 . Thang chai chit-khoan2 e5 kok-ka sia7-hoe7 si7 put-si5 bo5 peng5-an, put-si5 toa7 jiau2-loan7.

Na7-si7 tek-khak beh po2 peng5-an , tok8-tok8 u7 chit8-pou7 , chiu7 tioh8 chhong3 hou7 tak8-lang5 gong7-gong7 , hou7 i m7-bat poaN3-hang7 su7. Chhin-chhiuN7 Beng7 Hu-chu2 sou2 kong2, 『Lang5 na7 bo5 ka3-si7 , m7-bat tai7-chi3 , chiu7 kap khim5-siu3 tang5 chit8-iuN7.』Na7 chhin-chhiuN7 khim5-siu3 chiu7 m7-bat tai7-gi7 , ia7 be7-hiau2 phah-sng3 khah chhim khah hng7 e5 tai7-chi3 , ti3-kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 toa7-chui2 lau5 chha5 , chit8 lang5 soaN3 chit8 tah. Thang hou7 kun5-thau5-bu2 khah toa7-liap8 e5 lang5 khah ho2 pai5-pi2, khah khoai3 he7-loh8.

Chin5-se3-hong5 chiong thiN-kha-e7 e5 thak8-chheh-lang5 long2 liah8 khi3 tai5, chiong chheh long2 poaN khi3 sio , chiu7-si7 an2-ni e5 chu2-i3. Sou2-i2 thang chai, cheng3-lang5 na7-si7 bo5 kau3-iok8 , m7-bat to7-li2 , chit khoan2 『Goa2 si7 goa2 , li2 ia7 si7 goa2 .』 e5 sia7-hoe7 chiu7 e7 chiaN5; iah8 chit khoan2 sia7-hoe7 na7 chiaN5 , lang5 e5 seN3-mia7 chiu7 m7-tat8 chit8 chiah kau2 !

KhoaN3 hian7-kim , lan2 e5 ka-teng5-lai7 ia7 si7 iau2-ku2 chiau3 『Goa2 si7 goa2 , li2 ia7 si7 goa2 .』 e5 chu2-gi7 teh kiaN5.
