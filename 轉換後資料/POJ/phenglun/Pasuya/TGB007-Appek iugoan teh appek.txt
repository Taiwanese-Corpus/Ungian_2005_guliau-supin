Ap-pek iu5-goan5 teh ap-pek
Pasuya

2000-ni5 3-geh8 18, Tai5-oan5 lek8-su2 toa7-choan2-pian3, 500-ban7 tiuN soan2-phio3, hou7 bin5-chin3-tong2 e5 Tan5, Chui2-piN2 tong3-soan2 Tiong-hoa5-bin5-kok te7-10-jim7 chong2-thong2, thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 Kok-bin5-tong2 cheng3-koan5 te7-1-pai2 sit-khi3 "chip-cheng3-koan5", 50-ni5 lai5, ut-chut e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 chong2-sng3 thau2 1-khau2-khui3.

Kok-bin5-tong2 loh8-tai5--a, m7-koh Tiong-hoa5-bin5-kok the2-che3 e5 thong2-ti7 ki-chhou2 tian-to2 koh khah kiong2-kou3, Tai5-oan5-lang5 bin7-tui3 e5 ap-pek kap gui5-ki ma7 bo5 an2-ne lai5 kai2-tu5.

Tai5-oan5 ti7 cheng3-ti7-siong7 kap Chi-na na2-chhiuN7 lu2 li7 lu2 hng7, m7-koh ui3 koh-chou hau7-soan2-jin5 e5 cheng3-chhek soan-si7, kap Tai5-oan5 khi3-giap8-ka e5 eng2-hiong2-lat8 lai5 khoaN3, khai-hong3 sam-thong kap Tiong-chu i2-keng si7 bo5-hoat-tou7 chou2-tong3 e5 chhu-se3, Chi-na leng2-to7-chia2 koh beng5-khak kiong5-tiau7 "1-e5 Tiong-kok" e5 chian5-toe5 kap goan5-chek boe7-tang3 kai2-pian3, an2-ne loh8--lai5, Tai5-oan5 to7 beh khai-si2 ku7-the2 kam2-siu7-tioh8 Chi-na "chhiok tam5" "chhiok thong" e5 ap-lek8--a.

Soan2-ki2 koe3-teng5 tiong, koh-chou hau7-soan2-jin5 ui7-tioh8 cheng-chhu2 chu-pun2-ka chi-chhi5, piaN3 siN3-mia7 teh hoah an-teng7 kap hoat-tian2, kang-un7-thoan5-the2 si3-koe3 chau2-chong5, soah 1-tiam2-a2 to bo5 tit-tioh8 jim7-ho5 tiong7-si7, lo5-tong7 kai-chan5 e5 seng-oah8 chhu3-keng2 kap lo5-tong7 tiau5-kiaN7, khoaN3--lai5, si7 bian2 ng3-bang7 e7 u7 siaN2-mih kai2-sian7--loh.

Tai5-oan5 to2-lai7 e5 Chi-na se3-lek8 m7-kam-goan7 sit-khi3 cheng3-koan5, soan2 su tian-to2 koh khah hiau-pai, niu2-khiok e5 chok8-kun5 koan-he7 e7 koh khah niu2-khiok, ap-pek-chia2 pian3-sin cho3 kai2-kek-chia2, pun2-toe2 ai3 chheng e5 lek8-su2-siau3 m7-taN7 boe7 chheng, Tai5-oan5-lang5 soah tioh8 koh khah thun-lun2.

Kok-bin5-tong2 loh8-tai5, m7-koh lek8-su2 su7-hui bo5 chheng-sng3, Tai5-oan5-lang5 ma7 bo5-iaN2 ka7 thong2-ti7-koan5 theh8--tng2-lai5, Chi-na e5 ap-lek8 ka-chhim, kai-kip thong2-ti7 bo5 pian3, chok8-kun5 cheng5-su5 bo5 siau-thau2, ok-chit e5 Tiong-hoa5-bin5-kok the2-che3 ma7 iu5-goan5 ke3-siok8 teh thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5-lang5.

Lek8-su2 teh choan2-pian3, Tai5-oan5-lang5 bin7-tui3 e5 ap-pek kap gui5-ki peng7 bo5 kai2-tu5.

Ai3 kiaN5 e5 lou7, lan2 iu5-goan5 tioh8 ke3-siok8 kiaN5--loh8-khi3!
