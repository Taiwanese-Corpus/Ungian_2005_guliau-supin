              Chhio3-khoe                         
Ke-chhui3 Tau3 Ah-chhui3
 
Ng5 Kheng3-goan5
A-Tiong peh kap A-Tiong m2 si7 chit8 tui3 tiong-hou7 iu7-koh lau2-sit8 e5 cho3-sit lang5. Ang-a2-bou2 nng7 lang5 sui-jian5 bo5 siu7 sim2-mih8 kau3-iok8, m7-ku2 khiok chai-poe5 chhut chit8 e5 chiaN5 u7 seng5-chiu7 e5 hau7-seN, i si7 ti7 Bi2-kok chit8 keng chin chhut-mia5 e5 kong-si teh chiah8 thau5-lou7, sng3--khi2--lai5 ka2-na2 chin ho2-koe3 e5 khoan2-sit. 
	U7 chit8 ni5 e5 joah8-thiN, A-Tiong peh chiap tioh8 in kiaN2 tui3 Bi2-kok kia3--lai5 e5 phoe, kong2 beh kio3 in nng7 e5 lau7..e5 khi3 Bi2-kok thit-tho5, sun7-soa3 toa3--chit8--cham7--ah. Nng7 e5 lau7..e5 chham-siong5 e5 kiat-ko2, chiu7 koat-teng7 beh khi3--lah. In-ui7 tui3 in e5 cha-pou sun saN tang cheng5 ti7 Bi2-kok chhut-si3 i2-lai5, iau2-koh m7 bat kiN3-koe3 i chit8 bin7, ma7 m7 chai seN-cho3 iN5, a2-si7 piN2.
	A-Tiong peh kap A-Tiong m2 nng7 lang5 long2 u7 cha2 khi2--lai5 e5 sip8-koan3. Chai2-khi2-si5 thiN iau2 boe7 kng, nng7 e5 lau7..e5 chiu7 sio-chio khi3 hai2-piN-a2 san3-pou7, ti7 lou7-tou5 tiong soa3 tu2 tioh8 chit8 e5 jiat8-cheng5 e5 a-tok-a2 tui3-bin7 kiaN5--koe3--lai5. Chu2-tong7 giah8 chhiu2 ka7 in sio-chioh-mng7 kong2, “Gu5 bo5 ni.” A-Tiong peh thiaN bo5 i3-su3 chiu7 soa3-chhui3 ka7 i in3 kong2, ”Goa2 A-Tiong.” Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2 cheng5-heng5 sio lian5-soa3 tai7-khai3 u7 chit8 le2-pai3 ku2. U7 chit8 am3, tng choan5 ke cho3-hoe2 teh chiah8 am3-tng3 e5 si5, A-Tiong peh tong3 boe7 tiau5 chiu7 khui-chhui3 mng7 i e5 hau7-seN kong2, “Gu5 bo5 ni” tau3-te2 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3? In hau7-seN bun5-bun5-a2 chhio3 i kong2, “He to7-si7 gau5-cha2 e5 i3-su3--lah.” A-Tiong peh tim3-thau5 se3-siaN hoe5-eng3 kong2, “HouN3! an2-ni--ouh,” sim-lai7 u7 sou3 e5 khoan2.
	Keh-tng2 cha2-khi2 A-Tiong peh kap A-tiong m2 tek8-piat8 koh khah cha2 khi2--lai5, lai5 kau3 ti7 peng5-siong5-si5 kap A-Tok-a2 sio tu2-bin7 e5 sou2-chai7 theng3-hau7, tng A-Tok-a2 kiaN5 kin7-oa2--lai5 e5 si5, chiu7 koaN2-kin2 seng kiah8 chhiu2 chhut toa7-siaN kio3 kong2, “Gu5 bo5 ni.” Chit hoe5 oaN7 A-Tok-a2 chu7-jian5 teh in3 i kong2, “Goa2 A-Tiong.” Nau2-kin chit8 si5 tng2 boe7--koe3--lai5 e5 A-Tiong peh, sim-lai5 teh siuN7, “ngh, ngh, na2 e7 an2-ni? Chin ki5-koai3--ouh?”
