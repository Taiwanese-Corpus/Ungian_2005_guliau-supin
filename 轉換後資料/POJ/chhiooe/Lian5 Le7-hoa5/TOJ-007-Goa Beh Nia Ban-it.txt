
Goa Beh Nia Ban-it
                                      Lian5 Le7-hoa5 
Cheng3-hu2 ui7-tioh8 beh pou2-thiap long5-bin5 e5 seng5-oah8. U7 sit8-si chin-choe7 e5 cheng3-chhek. U7 chit8 kang, long5-hoe7 e5 hong3-sang3-ki teh kong2-po3 kong2, “Na7 theng5-keng (休耕) e5 long5-te7, e7-tit-thang nia2 gou7-chheng. Na7 u7 phun3-bu7-khi3..e5, tioh8 e7-sai2 nia2 lak8 chheng. Ai3 toa3 in3-a2 kap sin-hun7 cheng3 kau3 long5-hoe7 pan7-su7 chhu3 lai5 nia2.”
Hit e7-pou, khoaN3 u7 chit8 e5 lau7 a-po5 chhiu2 theh8 tioh8 in3-a2 kap sin-hun7 cheng3 beh lai5 nia2 chiN5. Pan7-su7-oan5 kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3, chiu7 mng7 i kong2, “A-po5..a2, li2 kiam2 koh u7 teh choe3 sit?” I in3 kong2, “Bo5..ah! M7-ku2 goa2 i2-keng lak8-chap8 goa7 hoe3, long2 theng5-keng (停經)--ah, iu5-koh tak8 am3 long2 khun3 boe7 khi3, sou2-i2 goa2 long2 chong2 beh lai5 nia2 ban7-it! ”
