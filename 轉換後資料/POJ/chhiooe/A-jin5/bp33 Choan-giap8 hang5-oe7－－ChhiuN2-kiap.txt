PoaN3 kong2-chhio3
Choan-giap8 hang5-oe7－－ChhiuN2-kiap
A-jin5
U7 chit8-keng gin5-hang5 ui7-tioh8 lang5-kheh long2 e7 tan2 beh ha7-pan e5 si5-kan chiah pai5-liat8 beh kia3-khoaN2 iah-si7 nia2-khoaN2，gin5-hang5 chin thau5-khak thiaN3，to7 ka7 piah teng2 e5 si5-cheng tiau5 hou7 kin2 gou7-hun-cheng。U7 chit8-pai2,chit8-e5 chhiuN2-kiap--e5 jip8-lai5 chhiuN2 chiN5，chiN5 chong2 sa li7,beh chau2 e5 si5 khoaN3 tioh8 piah teng2 e5 si5-cheng bo5-chun2,to7 ka7 tiau5 to3-tng2--lai5 chiah li7-khui。Su7-au7,keng2-chhat-a2 lai5 pan7-an3,chai-iaN2 chhiuN2-hui2 ka7 si5-cheng siu-li2 ho2 chiah chau2--e5,chit8-e5 keng2-chhat-a2 kong2:
「Goa2  chai-iaN2 chhiuN2-kiap--e5 si7 siang2--a。」
「I u7 hok8-bin7,li2 na2-e7 chai si7 siaN2-lang5?」Gin5-hang5 e5 keng-li2 bo5-siaN2 beh sin3。
「Goan2 tau lau5-kha hit-e5 TiuN--sian-siN，i tiaN7-tiaN7 chhiang3-kong2,pian7-na7 hou7 i khoaN3--tioh8 e5 si5-cheng iah-si7 chhiu2-pio2-a2 na7 u7 si5-kan bo5 chun2 --e5,i choat8-tui3 bo5 beh pang3 i koe3。」Keng2-chhat koh pou2-chhiong kong2:「TiuN--sian-siN leh khui cheng-pio2-hang5。」
