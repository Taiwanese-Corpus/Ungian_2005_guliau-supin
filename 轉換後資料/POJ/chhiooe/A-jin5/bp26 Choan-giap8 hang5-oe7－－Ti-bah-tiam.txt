－－Ti-bah-tiam
A-jin5 
U7 chit8-e5 be7 ti-bah--e5,in tau kui2-tai7 long2 si7 cho3 bah-tiam--e5,i tu2 toe3 in lau7-pe7 oh8 cho3 seng-li2 e5 si5-chun7,lau7-pe7 to7 ka3 i khou2-lian7 bian2 iong7 chhin3-a2 kap pong7-a2 hit khoan2 e5 ke-si e7-tit cham7-chat ti-bah joa7-tang7 e5 hoat-tou7。Kou2-cha2 si5-tai7 chhin3-a2 kap pong7-a2 long2 iau2 chin han2-mih8,bo5 chit-pe2 kang-hu be7-tang3 chia5?-cho3 bah-hoan3-a2。Sui-bong2 si5-tai7 bo5-kang7--a，chit-e5 hoan3-a2 iau2-si7 koan3-si3 bo5 iong7 chhin3-a2,beh tok chit8-kin ti-bah to7-si7 chun2-chun2 chit8-kin，chit8-niu2 to be7 hou7 chau2-cheng--khi3。
U7 chit8-pai2,chit8-e5 lang5-kheh kong2 beh phah kin-poaN3 e5 sam-chan5,i kang7-khoan2 iong7 to liau5 chit8-te3,bo5 chhin3 to7 hou7--i，koe3 bo5-joa7-ku2,lang5-kheh to3-tng2--lai5,kheng5-hun kong2 i tng2-khi3 koh pong7--koe3,hit-te3 sam-chan5 chiah kin-lak8 nia5,chiok-chiok kiam2 nng7-niu2。Bah-tiam--e5 lian7 thau5-khak to bo5 giah8--khi2-lai5,soa3-chhui3 to7 in3 k?ng：「Lin2 tau e5 pong7-a2 ai3 kin2 khi3 hou7 lang5 siu-li2。」
