∮PoaN3-kong2-chhio3

Choan-giap8 hang5-oe7-－Kiam2-pui5

A-jin5
U7 chit8-e5 cha-bou2 khi3 chit8-keng bi2-iong5 hok8-kian7 tiong-sim kiam2-pui5,kho3-theng5 kiat-sok e5 si5,soah khoaN3-tioh8 chit8-e5 u7-kau3 toa7-khou e5 cha-bou2,to7 mng7 i kam2 ma7 si7 lai5 beh kiam2-pui5--e5。Hit-e5 cha-bou2 kong2 i si7 chia e5 hu7-chek-jin5。I kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3,mng7 kong2:
「Li2 chit-keng kiam2-pui5 kong-si m7-si7 kong2 joa7  u7-hau7?Li2 ka-ti7  nah-e7 bo5 seng kiam2-pui5--chit8-e7?」
Toa7-khou e5 hu7-chek-jin5 in3 kong2:「Lang5-kheh lai5 kiam2-pui5 liau2-au7,pian7-na7 khoaN3-tioh8 goa2  to7 e7 kam2-kak i kiam2-pui5 u7 seng5-kong。」
