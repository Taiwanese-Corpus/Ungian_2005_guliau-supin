Goa2 e5 lau7-su


Homosapiens ACT hoan-ek8
U7 chit8 pai2,sai、him5 kap hou5-li5 cho3-hoe2 khi3 phah-lah8,sai phah tioh8 chit8 chiah be2;Him5 phah tioh8 chit8 chiah li5-a2;Hou5-li5 phah tioh8 chit8 chiah thou3-a2。In beh pun-pheN chia-e5 lah8-mih8。
Sai ka7 him5 kong2:「TaN，li2 tai7-seng kong2,lah8-mih8 beh an2-choaN2pun？」Him5 in3 kong2:「Be2 siong7 toa7-chiah，hou7 tai7-ong5 li2 chiah8;Li5-a2   khah se3,hou7 goa2  chiah8;Thou3-a2 siong7-kai3 se3-chiah，tioh8 hou7 hou5-li5 chiah8。」
Sai thiaN liau2,bo5-kong2-bo5-taN3 to7 iong7 i song5-koh-u7-lat8 e5 jiau2  chhut lat8 ka7 him5 mau--loh8-khi3;Him5 chit8 siaN to7 si2--khi3 a。Soa3--loh8  khi3 mng7 hou5-li5 khoaN3 lah8-mih ai3 an2-choaN2 pun chiah ho2。Hou5-li5 chin kin2 soa3-chhui3 to7 kong2:「Tai7-ong5 chai2-khi2 khun3-chheN2 e5 si5,seng chiah8 thou3-a2 seh chit8 e7 chhui3-khi2 phang7;Tiong-tau3  pak-tou2 iau，chiah8 li5-a2 thang-ho2 khun3-tau3;Kau3 am3-si5,tai7-ong5 pak-tou2 chin iau e5 si5 chiah chiah8 be2,an2-ne tai7-ong5 chiah e7-tang3 tong3 kau3 keh-tng2-kang chai2-khi2。」
Sai thiaN liau2 chin hoaN-hi2、chiok boan2-i3,i mng7 hou5-li5 kong2:「Li2 na2-e7 hiah gau5?Si7 siaN2-lang5 ka3--li2 e5?」
Hou5-li5 iong7 i kan-kui2 koh kau2-koai3 e5 jiau2 ki2 hit chiah him5 kong2:「I to7-si7 goa2  e5 lau7-su。」
