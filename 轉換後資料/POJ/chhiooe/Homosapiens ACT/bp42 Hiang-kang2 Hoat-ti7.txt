Homosapiens ACT
Chit8 e5 Hiang-kang2 lang5 kiaN5-jip8 keng2-si7-thiaN (Keng2-chhat chong2-kiok8) tui3 Keng2-tiuN2 kong2, “Keng2-tiuN2 sian-siN, goa2 cha-hng u7 lai5 chia po3-an3, kong2 goa2 u7 chit8 phoe hoe3 bo5--khi3. Kin-a2-jit8 goa2 i2-keng chhoe7--tioh8-a. Sou2-i2 goa2 lai5 thiat-siau hit e5 an3-kiaN7.”
Keng2-tiuN2 thiaN oan5, bak8-thau5 kat-kat ka7 kong2, “Li2 nah m7 khah cha2 kong2--leh? E-chai2-khi2 goan2 i2-keng liah8-tioh8 chhat8-a2. Ji5-chhiaN2 hoat-koaN ma7 i2-keng phoaN3-heng5--a!”
