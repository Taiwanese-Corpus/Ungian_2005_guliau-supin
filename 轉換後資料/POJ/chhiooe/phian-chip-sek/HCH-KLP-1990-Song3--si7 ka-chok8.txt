Song3--si7 ka-chok8
Teng2-kang2 u7 mia5-siaN, e7-kang2 u7 chhut-mia5, thong-lang5 chai e5 Song3--si7 ka-chok8, u7 saN-e5 kiau-kiau-lu2. In lau7-pe7 Charlie Song3 hou7 in chu7 soe3-han3 toh8 siu7 goa7-kok kau3-iok8, sou2-i2 ma7 ka7 in hou7 Eng-bun5-mia5, hun-piat8 kio3-choe3 : Jean, Joey, Holly.
U7 chit8-kang, i chhoa7 chit saN-e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 khi3 chham-ka chit8-e5 goa7-kok leng2-su7 e5 am3-hoe7, hit-e5 si5-chun7, i hiong3 tak8-ke siau7-kai3 in cha-bou2-kiaN2 : " They are my daughters ( in si7 goa2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2), Chin song2, Chiok song2, Hou7-li2 song2 ! "
