Iu5-eng2-ti5 e5 thiau3-chui2 pang-a2

                                                         Lou5,Eng2-hong hoan-ek8

Joah8-thiN kau3--a, kong-hng5 e5 iu5-eng2-ti5 long2 e7 khai-hong3.
Kiu3-seng-oan5 kiaN5 oa2 khi3 ka7 se3-han3 A-Jin5 an2-ne kong2:
"Li2 be7-tang3 ti7 iu5-eng2-ti5 ou5-loan7 pang3-jio7. Goa2 beh ka7 li2 liah8 lai5 khi3 keng2-chhat-kiok8 koaiN." 
A-jin5 an2-ne cheN3: "Tak8-e5 gin2-a2 long2 ma7 ti7 iu5-eng2-ti5-a2 lai7-te2 thau pang3-jio7. Li2 nah e7 kan-na liah8 goa2 chit8-e5?"
Kiu3-seng-oan5 in3 A-Jin5--a2 kong2: 
"Kho2-leng5 u7 lang5 thau pang3-jio7; tan7-si7 bo5 chit8-e5 chhiuN7 li2 an2-ne7, peh-khi2-lih thiau3-chui2 pang-a2 bin7-teng2 teh pang3-jio7--e5."


Little Johnny is approached by the lifeguard at the public swimming pool.
"You're not allowed to pee in the pool," said the lifeguard, 
"I'm going to have to report you."

"But everyone pees in the pool, "said Little Johnny.

"Maybe," said the lifeguard, "but not from the diving board!"
