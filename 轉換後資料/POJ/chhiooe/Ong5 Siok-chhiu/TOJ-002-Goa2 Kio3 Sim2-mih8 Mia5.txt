

Chhio3-khoe
Goa2 Kio3 Sim2-mih8 Mia5
Ong5 Siok-chhiu Ek8  
	U7 chit8 jit8, chit8 e5 seng-but8 kau3-siu7, beh ka7 hak8-seng kho2-chhi3. I koaN7 chit8 kha chiau2-lang2-a2 jip8 lai5 kau3-sek. Chiau2-lang2-a2 lai7-bin7 u7 chit8 chiah chiau2-a2, goa7-bin7 iong7 chit8 te3 ou-pou3 kham3--leh. 
	Chit e5 kau3-siu7 hian-khui chit8-sut-a2 ou-pou3, hou7 hak8-seng khoaN3 chiau2-a2 e5 kha. Kau3-siu7 mng7 hak8-seng kong2, "Chit chiah chiau2-a2 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2 chiau2-a2, ka7 i e5 mia5 sia2--chhut--lai5 ".
	U7 chit8 e5 hak8-seng chin siu7-khi3. I siuN7 kong2 kan-taN khoaN3 chiau2-a2-kha, beh na2 e7 chai-iaN2 si7 sim2-mih8 khoan2 chiau2-a2. Sou2-i2 i chiu7 bo5 ai3 chham-ka kho2-chhi3, tui3 kau3-sek kiaN5--chhut--khi3. Kau3-siu7 khoaN3--tioh8, chiu7 ka7 kio3 lai5 mng7 kong2 "Li2 si7 an2-choaN2 m7-ai3 kho2-chhi3. Li2 kio3 sim2-mih8 mia5? "Chit e5 hak8-seng long2 bo5 kong2-oe7, kan-taN lek8 i e5 khou3-kha--khi2--lai5 hou7 kau3-siu7 khoaN3 i e5 kha, kong2, " Li2 khoaN3 goa2 e5 kha, goa2 kio3 sim2-mih8 mia5?".
