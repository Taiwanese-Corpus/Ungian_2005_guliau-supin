Pou2-sip8 Eng-gi2
     U7 chit8 ke-hoe2-a2, lau7-pe7 cho3 i-seng, lau7-bo2 ka3 Eng-gi2. In tau kan-ta u7 chit8-e5 po2-poe3-a2 cha-bou2 kiaN2. In ang-a2-bou2 pang5-keng lai7 e5 tai7-chi3 long2 iong7 am3-oe7: "Khan-chhiu2..e5, li2 e5-am3 ka7 goa2 pou2-sip8 Eng-gi2! "
     Boe2-a2, in koat-teng7 beh i5-bin5 khi3 Kha-na2-tah (Canada). M7-ku2, lau7-pe7 chiam7-si5 ai3 lau5 tiam3 chia than3-chiN5.

     Tng in teh bo5-eng5 khoan2 heng5-li2 e5 si5, po2-poe3-a2 cha-bou2 kiaN2 hiong5-hiong5 siuN7--tioh8, mng7 kong2: "Au7-pai2 pa-pa pou2-sip8 Eng-gi2 beh an-choaN2 ?" In lau7-bo2 chit8 si5 soa3 m7 chai beh an2-choaN2 in3, kan-ta toa7 siaN jiang2: " Put-chun2 khi3 pou2-sip8 pan! Be7-sai2 chhiaN3 ka-kau3! Ka-ki7 tiam3 chhu3-lai7 phah-piaN3 lian7-sip8! "
