A-pa gia5 chheh-pau
A-pa-cheh hit-kang, lau7-su kio3 hak8-seng siau7-kai3 A-pa e5 ui2-tai7 kap sin-khou2.
A- kiat--a kong2: “Goan2 a-pa chiok ui2-tai7, tak8-kang long2 chin phah-piaN3 leh gia5 chheh-pau, than3-chiN5 hou7 goa2 thak8-chheh, goa2 chiok-ai3 goa2 e5 a-pa.”
Lau7-su mng7: “A-kiat--a, lin2 a-pa ti7 chheh-tiam3 leh chiah8 thau5-lou7 si7--bou3 ?”
A- kiat--a: “M7-si7--lah, i ti7 long5-hoe7 leh sai2 thou-lak-khuh.”
      The5-kiong-chia2 : Siau Lah-jih
