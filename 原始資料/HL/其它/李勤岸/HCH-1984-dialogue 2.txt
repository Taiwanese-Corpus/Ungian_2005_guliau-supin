Dialogue 2
A.	Lin2 hak8-hau7 u7 joa7-che7 lang5?				How many people does your class have?
恁學校有偌濟人？

B.	Hak8-seng chha-put-to u7 si3 ban7 e5;			About forty thousand students;
學生差不多有四萬個；

Lau7-su chhap-put-to u7 nng7-chheng e5.		About two thousand teachers.
老師差不多有二千個。

A.	An2-ne chong2-kiong u7 joa7-che7 e5?			So how many people total?
按呢總共有偌濟？

B.	Chong2-kiong7 chha-put-to u7 si3 ban7 nng7 chheng e5.	We have forty-two thousand total.
總共差不多有四萬二千個。

A.	Ou~oh! U7-kau3 che7.  Lin2 hak8-hau7 u7-kau3 toa7.		Oh! That’s a lot. Your school is big enough.
哦~! 有夠濟。恁學校有夠大。



B.	A lin2 hak8-hau7 u7 joa7-che7 hak8-seng?			Then how many students does your school have?
啊恁學校有偌濟學生？

A.	Goan2 hak8-hau7 kan-na u7 si3 chheng e5 hak8-seng.	 Our school only has four thousands students.
阮學校干單有四千個學生。

B.	Hiah-nih8 chio2.  Lau7-su u7 kui2 e5?			That little.  How many teachers?
Hiah-nih 少。老師有幾個？

A.	Lau7-su u7 saN pah e5.					Three hundred teachers.
老師有三百個。

B.	Hiah-nih8 che7.						That much.
Hiah-nih8 濟。
