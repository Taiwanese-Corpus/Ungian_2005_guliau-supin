Lesson 1, Greetings (Chio-hou) 招呼
 
Dialogue 1
 
Student: Lau7-su, gau5-cha2!/ Good morning, sir./ 老師，gau5早！
Teacher: Gau5-cha2!  Chiah8-pa2--boe7?/Good morning.  Have you eaten?/ gau5早！食飽未? 
Student: Chiah8-pa2--ah.  Li2 kam2 chiah8-pa2--ah?/ I’ve eaten. Have you eaten?/ 食飽啊。你敢食飽啊？
Teacher: Goa2 ma7 chiah8-pa2--ah./ I’ve eaten too./ 我嘛食飽啊。
Lan2 khai-si2 lai5 siong7-kho3./ Let’s start the lesson now./ 咱開始來上課。
