Li2-siong2 kap kian-chhi5 e5 koe2-chi2

Li7, Heng-chhiong

BONG-po3 e5 hiaN-che2:
Kiong-hi2, kiong-hi2! 
Sit8-chai7 chin bo5 kan2-tan, 6 ni5 72 ki5, m7-si7 te2-te2 e5 si5-kan, che si7 li2-siong2 kap kian-chhi5, hi-seng kap hong7-hian3 sou2 kiat--chhut-lai5 e5 koe2-chi2. Kam2-sia7 lin2 ui7 Tai5-oan5 e5 seN3-mia7-su2 lui7-chek e5 kong-tek.
Kho2-sioh 12 goeh8 15 goa2 bo5 hoat-tou7 khi3 chham-ka seng7-hoe7, ti7-chia, iong7 chit-tiuN phoe ka7 lin2 chiok-hok, ng3-bang7 BONG-po3 e7-tang3 chiaN5-cho3 Tai5-oan5-lang5 bo5-khoaN3 e7 sim sng e5 khan-but8.
