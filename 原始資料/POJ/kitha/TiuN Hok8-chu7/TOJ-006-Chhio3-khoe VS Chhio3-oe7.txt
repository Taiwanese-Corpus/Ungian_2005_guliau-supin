Chhio3-khoe VS Chhio3-oe7
Eng-gi2 e5 "joke", u7 2 e5 gi2-i3 seng5-hun: amusing kap ridiculous.Ti7 Hoa5-gi2 long2 ka7 chheng-hou cho3 ¯º¸Ü. Ti7 Tai5-gi2 lan2 ai3 ka7 pun chheng-chho2: amusing e5 seng5-hun khah tang7..e5, kio3 chhio3-khoe; na7-si7 ridiculous khah tang7..e5, kio3 chhio3-oe7. An2-ne lan2 e5 thau5-khak e7 ke khah chheng-chho2 koh cheng-bit8!
Chhio3-khoe u7 pau-ham5 phou2-thong-pan2..e5 kap chhai2-sek-pan2..e5 nng7 chiong2. Chhai2-sek-pan2..e5  chiu7-si7 loe7-iong5 kap seng3 u7 koan-he7. M7-ku2 li2 thak8 liau2 be7 hoan2-kam2. Pi2-ju5 lan2 chit-ki5 e5 <Ka-ku7-siong> kap <Pou2-sip8 Eng-gi2>. Na7-si7 chhio3-oe7, chiu7-si7 hu3-bin7 e5 i3-su3 khah che7. Chhio3-oe7 koh thang pun-cho3 ang5-sek..e5, kap Kiong7-san2-tong2 u7 koan-he7; ou-sek..e5, hou7 lang5 thak8-liau2 ou-thiN am3-te7; peh8-sek..e5, khoaN3-tioh8 e7 kiaN; kap phu2-sek..e5, m7-chai teh kong2 siaN2. Ka7 koan-liam7 pun hou7 chheng-chho2 si7 hian7-tai7 lang5 e5 ki-pun2 tiau5-kiaN7 chi it.
