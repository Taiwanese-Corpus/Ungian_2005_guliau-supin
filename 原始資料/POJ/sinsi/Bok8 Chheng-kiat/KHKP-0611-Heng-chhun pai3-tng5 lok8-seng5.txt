Bok8 Chheng-kiat 
1.Heng5-chhun pai3-tng5 kian3-tiok khi2 ho2, 
Chhut-siaN gim5-si moa2-sim o-lo2; 
Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 si I toa7-in, 
Pang-chan7 khi2 chiaN5 o-lo2 bo5 chin7.  
Ho5 :     O-lo2,o-lo2, lan2 Chu2 in-hui7,          
Ko2-jian5 khi2 chiaN5 kek8-ki5 chhut-lui7; 
A-li2-lu2-ia7, siaN-im kek8 ho5, 
Heng5-chhun kau3-hoe7 eng2 tit o-lo2.  
2.Heng5-chhun pai3-tng5 ko2-jian5 tiok seng5, 
Hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 hoaN-hi2 hoan-geng5; 
Kiah8-bak8 tit khoaN3 kek8-ki5 kek8 sin, 
Goan7 Chu2 thiaN3-thang3 na2 ku2 na2 chhim.  
3.Hian3-tng5 e5 jit8 tai7-ke hoaN-hi2, 
Gim5-si hiang2-liang7 thau3 kau3 thiN-nih8; 
Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 si I toa7-in, 
Pang-chan7 khi2 chiaN5, o-lo2 bo5 chin7.  
 
4.Kau3-hoe7 toa7 heng, tui3 chia khi2 tiam2,    
Ui7 Chu2 hi-seng bo5 kiaN gui5-hiam2;    
Tai7-ke khng3 che sin3-sim him-bou7,    
Siong7-chu2 seng3-chhiu2 koan5-leng5 hu5-chou7.  
5.TiuN2-lo2, chip-su7, chi2-moe7, hoe7-iu2, 
Seng3-keng khin5-thak8, Seng3-jit8 khin5 siu2; 
ChhiuN-to, chian3-khi3, cheng2-liat8 khia7-pai5, 
Ho2-taN2 chhut-tin7, kap kui2 saN-thai5.   
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 611 tiuN, 1936 ni5 2 geh8)
