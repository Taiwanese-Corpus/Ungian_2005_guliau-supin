 Tan5-lui5 –i

Chit-tai7 koe3 liau2 koh chit-tai7  ,
Chhun-thiN koe3 liau2 joah8- thiN lai5 ,
Lang5 kong2 chia si7 ho2 sou2-chai7  ,
Giok8-lan5 khui hoe bian2 lang5 chai .

Chit-tai7 koe3 liau2 koh chit-tai7  ,
Sin-lo5 phah-piaN3 tioh8 chhiN5-chai ,
Lang5 kong2 chia si7 sio2 sou2-chai7 ,
Khe-lau5 ta khi3 bo5 lang5 ai3  . 

Chit-tai7 koe3 liau2 koh chit-tai7  ,
ChhiaN2 mng7 chia si7 siaN2 sou2-chai7  ?
A-ma2 kong2 oe7 sun m7  chai , 
Lang5 kong2 che si7 ho2 lou5-chai5 .


Chit-tai7 koe3 liau2 koh chit-tai7 ,
Chhun-thiN koe3 liau2 joah8-thiN lai5  ,
Su3-kui3 chhau2 -bok long2 chu7-chai7  ,
ChoaN2-iuN7 cho3 lang5 bo5 chu2 chai7 ?
