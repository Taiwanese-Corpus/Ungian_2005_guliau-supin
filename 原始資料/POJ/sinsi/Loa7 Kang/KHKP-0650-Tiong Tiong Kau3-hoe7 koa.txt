Tiong Tiong Kau3-hoe7 koa
Chhau2-tun: Loa7-kang  
1.Tai5-tiong Tiong-hoe7 e5 koan2-lai7, 
Long2-chong2 saN-chap8-si3 sou2-chai7, 
Tit-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 in-ai3, 
Kau3-hoe7 chiam7-chiam7 na2 khia7-chai7.  
2.Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chu5-pi, 
Sui-jian5 ti7 chit hui-siong5-si5, 
Tai7-ke it-hoat khah hun3-chi3, 
Kam2-kak tok8-lip8 e5 si5-ki5.  
3.Chhin-chhiuN7 chit pai2 e5 jiat8-seng5, 
Sin chhiaN2 bok8-su u7 5 keng, 
Ji7-ui7 Tong5-hoe7 koh seng-keh, 
Po2-tau2, Ji7-lim5 chit nng7--e5.  
4.Ui7-tioh8 chit khoan2 e5 hoaN-hi2, 
Goa2 khah chhiam3-koan5 lai5 kong2-khi2, 
Po3 chhut kau3-hoe7 e5 ui7-ti3, 
I2-kip8 bok8-su e5 mia5-ji7.  
5.Hong5-bou5 Bok8-su ti7 Ho5-bi2, 
Toa7-tou7 Bok8-su Chia7 Chheng-gi7. 
Chiong-hoa3 Bok8-su Ong5 Siu2-iong2, 
Chin2-hong Bok8-su ti7 Tai5-tiong.  
6.Lok8-kang2 Bok8-su IuN5 Se3-chu3, 
Lam5-tau5 Bok8-su Gou5 Thian-su3, 
Khe-ou5 Bok8-su Ki2 Un-jiu5, 
Tian5-tiong Bok8-su Ng5 Jin5-siu7.  
7.Ian3-choan5 Bok8-su ti7 Sia7-thau5, 
Chhoan-beng5 Bok8-su tng7 Pat-tau2, 
Bun5-hok Bok8-su tiam3 Chheng-chui2, 
Chiau-kam3 Bok8-su ti7 Ji7-sui2.  
8.Oan5-lim5 Bok8-su Ng5 Su7-beng7, 
Chhau2-tun Bok8-su Koeh Tiau5-seng5, 
I2-siong7 long2-chong2 chap8-si3 keng, 
Pheng3-chhiaN2 Bok8-su teh keng-eng5.  
9.I2-goa7 Tong5-hoe7 iau2 lak8-keng, 
Bo5-ku2 ia7 e7 saN kiong7-beng5, 
Chin-chiaN3 tok8-lip8 e5 oan5-seng5, 
Chi-hoe7 hi-bong7 kin2 ko-seng.  
10.Lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 ti7 Tiong-pou7, 
Tai7-ke chhut-lat8 lai5 hun3-tou3, 
Ko3-ko3 kou3 hou7 e7 hun3-tou7, 
Eng2-hiong2 choan5-tai5 Lam5 Pak pou7.  
11.Bok8-su chit8-lang5 ti7 chit8-ui7, 
E7 ui7 lan2 Chu2 toa7 hoat-hui, 
IuN2-chhi7 iuN5-kun5 e7 pui5-sui2, 
Gau5 chhoa7 be5-iuN5 tit hoe5-kui.  
12.IuN5-kun5 tioh8 thiaN Bok8-chia2 siaN, 
M7-thang hong3-tong7 ka-ti7 kiaN5, 
Tai7-ke put-chi2 gau5 saN-thiaN3, 
Kau3-hoe7 oan5-boan2 ho2 mia5-siaN.  
13.Hi-bong7 tai7-ke saN hap8-ho5, 
Hoa3-tek8 ui5-iu2 si7 chin ho2. 
Li7-ek kau3-hoe7 lan2 chiah cho3, 
Na7 u7 chou2-gai7 tioh8 siau-bo5.  
14.Ki5-kiu5 Seng3 Sin5 lai5 lim5-chai7, 
Hou7 goan2 kau3-hoe7 long2 khia7-chai7, 
Teh-beh hoaN-hi2 lai5 hat-chhai2, 
O-lo2 lan2 Chu2 hoah ban7-jai2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 650 ho7,1939 ni5 5 geh8)
