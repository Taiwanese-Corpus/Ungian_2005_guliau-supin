                         
Chhau2-tun Chheng-lian5-hoe7 Siau7-kai3 ui2-oan5 koa
Loa7 kang  
1.Pe3 Chheng-lian5-hoe7 e5 hoat-khi2, 
Kau3-taN i2-keng moa2 nng7 ni5, 
Chiu7 khui Chong2-hoe7 lai5 soan2-ki2, 
Ai3-soan2 ui2-oan5 thang ti7-li2.  
2.Hoe7-tiuN2 iu5-goan5 Hoe2-thou2 sang3, 
I si7 teh khui hi5-pou2 hang5, 
Le2-pai3 kui jit8 koaiN tiam3-thang,
Ai3 hou7 sin3-sim khah khin-sang.  
3.Hu3 hoe7-tiuN2 si7 Ang5 Goan5-teng7, 
Lau7-pe7 si7 Kim-kang I-seng, 
Chheng-lian5 phok-hak8 na7 hun3-chi3, 
Pun3-hoe7 ban7-su7 be7 hui3-khi3.  
4.Su-ki3 chiu7-si7 Khou2 Chui2 ng5, 
Keng-eng5 siong-giap8 ti7 pun2-chng,
U7-si5 chhut-tiuN khi3 chin hng7, 
Tui3 ti7 chit-bu7 kam-goan7 tng.  
5.Koe3-ke3 chiu7-si7 Heng-ong7 sang3, 
Teh be7 long5-ku7 kiam khou-thang2, 
Chhau2-tun sin3-chia2 thau5 chit8-e5, 
 
Chiu7-si7 i e5 kou3 lau7-pe7.  
6.Chong-kau3 pou7 si7 Tan5 Chui2-lai5, 
I si7 chhut-mia5 li2-hoat sai, 
Cho3 lang5 put-chi2 chin khong2-khai3, 
Soan-thoan5 to7 li2 chin i3-ai3.  
7.Im-gak8-pou7 Chu-ai3 lu2-su7, 
Lim5 Eng-seng5 si7 i tiong7-hu,
Tui3 ti7 im-gak8 u7 chhu3-bi7, 
Kou2-bu2 gim5-si gau5 chi2-si7.  
8.Chu5-sian7-pou7 si7 A-giok8 sang3, 
Teh cho3 san2-po5 tiong7-iau3 kang, 
Tui3 ti7 pin5-bin5 gau5 thiaN3-thang3, 
Lai7 goa7 beng5-u7 put7-chi2 phang.  
9.Kau3-iok8-pou7 Siok-cheng lu2-su7, 
Lau7-pe7 si7 goan2 e5 Bok8-su, 
Phin2-heng7 put-chi2 thang kho2-chhu2, 
Ka3 Iu3-ti7 kho gau5 kou2-bu2.  
10.I-siong7 kai3-siau7 e5 ui2-oan5, 
Ui7 Chu2 hi-seng chin kam-goan7, 
Ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 toa7 kang7 hok, 
Ho5-peng5 eng5-kng kui Ki-tok.    
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 599 koan3, 1935 ni5 2 geh8)
