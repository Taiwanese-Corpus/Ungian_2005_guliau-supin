
Mou5-se toan7
1.U7 chit8 I-sek-liat8 e5 chok8, 
Chou2-kong chhut-goa7 Ai-kip8 kok; 
Au7-lai5 kiaN2-sun soa3-soa3 thoan5, 
Kun-ong5 kiaN-liau2 in bou5-hoan2.
2.Sou2-i2 giam5-siu2 chhut ho7-leng7, 
Hoan-hu3 khi2-cho7 nng7 tai7-seng5; 
Lo5-lo5 lok8-lok8 cho3 khou2-kang, 
Sio-io5 cho3-chng bo5 lang3-phang7.
3.Tan3-si7 sui-jian5 siu7 khou2-chhou2, 
Hoan2-tng2 jit8-jit8 thiN jin5-sou3; 
In-ui7 thiN-teng2 chin oah8 Sin5, 
Am3-tiong po2-iu7 I jin5-bin5.
4.Kun-ong5 e5 sim koh-khah phaiN2, 
Ai3 beh biat8 in gin2-na2 chai; 
Beng7-leng7 nng7-ui7 e5 siN po5, 
Seng-san2 e5 si5 khoaN3 hou7 ho2.
5.Na7-si7 sou2 siN ta-pou-kiaN2,
Lip8-khek tioh8 hai7 i e5 mia7; 
Chhut-si3 khoaN3--tioh8 si7 lu2-ji5, 
Lau5 mia7 m7-bian2 hou7 i si2.
 
6.Ho2-tit chit nng7-e5 san2-po5, 
Chit khoan2 tok8-chhiu2 m7-kaN2 cho3; 
Kun-ong5 koat-i3 ai3 seng5-su7, 
Sou2-i2 koh-chai3 chhut seng3-ju7.
7.It-poaN seng-san2 na7 lam5-ji5,
Tioh8 hiat ho5-tiong chim3-chui2 si2; 
Tu2-tu2 hoat-chhut chit ho7 i3, 
Chit8 e5 toa7 ui2-jin5 chhut-si3.
8.Pe7-bo2 khoaN3-kiN3 chin khiau2-khi3, 
M7-kam hiat i ti7 ho5--nih; 
Sou2-i2 khng3-liau2 saN goeh8-jit8, 
KiaN-liau2 boe7 thang koh pi3-bit8.
9.Choan5-ke seN3-mia7 e7 lan5-po2, 
SiuN7-lai5 siuN7-khi3 sim chap8-cho5; 
Chiu7 cho3 lou5-tek siuN lai5 khng3, 
Chiong iu3 eN-a2 he7 tiong-ng.
10.Bo2-chhin u7 kap i toa7-chi2, 
Pang3 tiam3 ho5-piN lou5-tek--nih; 
Hiat-liau2 hng7-hng7 khia7 teh khoaN3, 
KhoaN3 i sio2-ti7 beh an3-choaN2.
 
11.Ko2-jian5 teh lai5 lu2 kong-chu2, 
Ia7 toa3 chhe-eng7 e5 siau2-lu2; 
Chau2-loh8 ho5-tiong se2 sin-khu, 
ThiaN gin2-na2 hau2 bak8-nih-ku2.
12.KhoaN3 siuN ti7 hit lou5-tek--nih, 
Chhe-kah lu2-pi7 khi3 theh8 i; 
Khui khoaN3 u7 chit8 e5 lam5-hai5, 
Chai si7 chhut ti7 Hi-pek-lai5.
13.In che2 lip8-khek chau2 kin7-cheng5, 
Khui-chhui3 ng3 kong-chu2 seng-beng5; 
Boeh, m7, chhiaN3 leng-bo2 lai5 io, 
Goa2 chiah sui5-si5 lai5 khi3 kio3.
14.Kong-chu2 thiaN-liau2 chiu7 in3 ho2, 
In che2 chiu7 khi3 chhiaN3 chhin-bo2;
Tng7 leng sang3 kau3 ong5-kiong siaN5, 
Tit-tioh8 chheng-cho3 Mou5-se mia5.
15.I3-su3 tui3 chui2 kiu3 khi2-lai5, 
Bian2 siu7 chim3-si2 e5 chai-hai7; 
Tit-tioh8 seng5-jin7 beng5-leng5 chu2, 
Thak8-chheh chhang-mia5 choe3 kho2-chhu2.
 
16.Au7-lai5 kau3 ti7 si3-chap8-hoe3, 
Chhut-goa7 khoaN3-tioh8 in e5 poe3; 
Tu2 u7 chit8 e5 Ai-kip8 lang5, 
Phah in pun2-chok8 chho3-sim tang7.
17.Mou5-se khoaN3 i e5 cho2-iu7, 
Lip8-khek phah-si2 chit hiong-chhiu2; 
Chiu7 chiong sin-si iam2 soa-tiong, 
Na2 kiaN5 na2 siuN7 hoe5 ong5-kiong.
18.Keh-jit8 koh-chai3 khi3 goa7-ui7, 
KhoaN3-kiN3 tong5-pau teh saN-chiN; 
Mou5-se chhiat-khng3 bu5-li2--e5, 
M7-thang saN hai7 saN oan-ke.
19.Kho2-sioh m7-thiaN in3 chit khoan2, 
Li2 kiam2 ia7 beh phah-si2 goan2; 
Mou5-se thiaN-liau2 sim kiaN-hiaN5, 
KiaN cheng5 sou2 cho3 lang5 chai-iaN2.
20.Sou2-i2 to5-chau2 kau3 Bi2-tian5, 
Chit8 lou7 tit Chu2 si kho2-lian5; 
Ti7 hia u7 chhoa7 che3-si lu2, 
Chiu7 tiam3 in tiuN7-lang5 e5 chhu3.
 
21.Kam-goan7 khi3-sak ong5-kiong hok, 
Kai2 cho3 bok8-chia2 hoan2-tng2 lok8; 
Chit khoan2 keng-koe3 si3-chap8 chhiu, 
Tong5-pau siu7-khou2 i siong5 siuN7.
22.Chit8 jit8 koaN2 iuN5 kau3 soaN-te7, 
KhoaN3-tioh8 chhi3-phe3 hoat kng-hoe2; 
Sui-jian5 siu7 sio long2 bo5 hui2, 
Sim-tiong gi5-hek8 koh-iuN7 lui7.
23.Chim-chiok ka7 khoaN3 khia7 tiaN7-tiaN7, 
Chhi3-tiong hoat-chhut chin toa7 siaN;
Lian5-lian5 nng7 ku3 kio3 Mou5-se, 
Li2 taN sou2 khia7 si7 Seng3-te7.
24.Ai3 chai siaN-im to2-ui7 lai5 ? 
Chiu7-si7 ThiN-teng2 toa7 Chu2-chaiN2; 
Kong2 goa2 I-sek-liat8 peh-siN3, 
Siu7 toa7 kan-khou2 Ai-kip8--nih8.
25.Kim-jit8 li2 khi3 hit sou2-chai7, 
Kiu3 in kau3 ho2 sou2-chai7 lai5; 
Mou5-se sio2-taN2 pun2 m7-kheng2, 
Chin Sin5 chi2-si7 toa7 koan5-leng5.
 
26.Sui5-si5 li7-piat8 in gak8-hu7, 
Chun-than3 chin Sin5 sou2 hoan-hu3; 
Soa3 toa3 ke-koan3 Ai-kip8 khi3, 
HiaN-ko A-lun5 lai5 ngia5-chih.
27.Nng7-lang5 saN-chhoa7 kiN3 Hoat-lo2, 
Chin Sin5 e5 oe7 long2 keng2-ko3; 
Bo5 gou7 kun-ong5 chin kau2-khiat, 
Koh-khah thiau-lan5 I2-sek-liat8.
28.Chin Sin5 boe7-oe7 koh jim2-nai7, 
Chioh eng7 Mou5-se kang3 chap8-chai; 
Te7-it ho5-chui2 piN3-cho3 hoeh, 
Te7-ji7 chui2-ke moa2 kiong-lai7.
29.Te7-saN tin5-ai koa3 bang2-lui7,
Te7-si3 hou5-sin5 moa2 tak8-ui7;
Te7-gou7 un-ek8 ti7 cheng-seN, 
Te7-lak8 lang5 seN tok8-chhng peN7.
30.Te7-chhit peng, phauh, phah chhan5-te7, 
Te7-peh chhau2-meh si3-koe3 poe; 
Te7-kau2 saN-jit8 saN-me5 am3, 
Te7-chap8 ke-ke si2 tiong2-lam5.
 
31.Tui3-chia Ai-kip8 Hoat-lo2 ong5, 
Chin-chiaN3 bo5 hoat chiah khai-hong3, 
Mou5-se hit-si5 chiu7 in2-chhoa7, 
Chau2-chhut Ai-kip8 bo5 koh toa3.
32.Tit8-tit8 ng3 tui3 khong3-ia2 khi3, 
An2-ni khou2-lan7 chiu7 thoat-li7; 
Hoat-lo2 chit-si5 koh hou7-hoe2, 
Chiu7 chhoa7 tai7-peng khi3 tui chhoe7.
33.Peh-siN3 kiaN-hiaN5 khui-siaN ai, 
Au7 u7 kun-be2, cheng5 u7 hai2; 
Chin Sin5 u7 hoan-hu3 Mou5-se, 
Eng7 koaiN2 ki2 chui2 na2 ta-te7.
34.Tiong-kan hian2-hian7 chit8 tiau5 lou7, 
Lam5, lu2, teh koe3 eng7 kha-pou7; 
Ai-kip8 tai7-peng ia7 tui7 chui2, 
Chin7-lat8 tit-chan2 chin7-lat8 tui.
35.I2-sek-liat8 lang5 long2 koe3 bin7, 
Ai-kip8 kun-be2 chhiong2-kong5 phin5; 
Mou5-se koh-chai3 eng7 koaiN2 ki2, 
Hai2-chui2 hap8-oa2 long2 im si2.
 
36.Chhu2-au7 chhoa7 in si3-chap8 tang, 
Lai5-ong2 choan ti7 Sin5 kap lang5; 
Seng3-cheng5 seN-cho3 kek8 un-jiu5,
ThiN-e7 cheng3-lang5 bo5 chit8 iu2.
37.Sou2 kiaN5 iau3-su7 u7 to-sou3, 
Sou2 choe3 ki5-su7 kham him-bou7; 
Na7-si7 sui5 hang7 kong2 siong5-se3, 
Cheng5 tng5, choa2 te2, hin7 be7-e7.
38.Choe7-iau3 e5 su7 ti7 Se-nai2, 
Chiong Sin5 e5 Su7 ka3 lang5 chai; 
Sin5-jiah koai2-tiong7 cho7 tang5-choa5, 
Hou7 si2 e5 lang5 e7 koh-oah8.
39.Gu7-tioh8 peh-siN3 cho3 m7-ho2, 
Tiong-sim jiat8-chhiat the3 ki5-to2; 
Si5-si5 chin7-tiong ti7 Siong7-chu2, 
Koh cho3 sian-ti kong2 chham3-gu2.
40.Au7 si2 ti7 Ni5-pho3-soaN--nih, 
Lian5-ki2 i2-keng chit8-pah-ji7; 
Bak8-chiu bo5 hoe, hi7 bo5 tang7, 
Giam2-jian5 m7-si7 choe3 lau7-lang5.
 
41.Chit toaN7 kou3-su7 choe3 chhu3-bi7, 
Chu-kun chhiaN2 kau3 pai3-tng5--nih; 
Na7 kheng2 siong5-se3 koh kham3-kheng5,
Loe7-tiong su7-chek koh-khah beng5.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 629~630 ho7,1937 ni5 8~9 geh8)
