《恭喜 鄭雅怡得著第一屆李江卻台文獎》
離婚
鄭雅怡

Tioh8 an2-ni koah-li7:
Siong7-te3 bin7-cheng5 seng3-kiat e5 iok
TaN phah-chhui3, ti7 li2 goa2  chhiu2-thau5.
Bo5 koh chheng-hou7 tui3-hong
Si7 ka-ki7 kut-tiong e5 kut, bah-tiong e5 bah.

Chit8-si3-lang5 e5 beng5-se3,
Boe7 kham-tit saN-chheng-jit8 ku2:
Chit8-pah-jit8 e5 ho5-peng5,
Chit8-chheng-jit8 e5 chian3-cheng,
Chheng-peh-jit8 e5 leng2-tam7.
TaN it-chin7 chhat-siau, kan-ta
Pin5 chit8-tiuN hui2-iok e5 iok.

SiN3-mia7-tiong e5 toa7  hong-thai koe3-khi3.
Poah8-to2 e5 thiaN3,  si5-khek chhak-tioh8 goa2:
An2-choaN2 khia7--khi2-lai5, ti7 sit-pai7-tiong
Oh8-tioh8 goan5-liong7 pat8-lang5,
Seng5-jim7 ka-ki7 bo5 oan5-choan5.
