0 hou5-chhiu: 多鬍鬚 having heavy beard.
1 cha-pou: 男性 man; male.　cha-bou2: 女性 woman; female.　na7-si7: 如果 if.　long2: 都 all.
2 kui: 整個 the whole.　bin7: 臉 the face.　chhun: 剩 be left with; remain.　nng7: 二 two.　lui2: 隻 (classifier for eyes).　bak8-chiu: 眼睛 the eye.
3 jin5-seng: 人生 life.　kiam2: 減少 less; reduce.　chit8: 一 one.　bi7: 味 flavor.
4 sio: 互相 with each other; mutually.　tu2: 相遇 encounter; run into.　m7-chai: 不知道 not know.　lam5: 男 man; male.　ah8: 或 or.　li2: 女 woman; female.
5 bo5-thang: 沒得 not get to.　siong3: 瞪著眼注視 stare at.　thau: 偷偷兒地 secretly.　hoaN-hi2: 高興 feel happy.
7 e5: 的 (possessive marker).　mou: 毛 hair.　tng5-liu-liu: 很長 very long.
8 ke: 加多 more; add.
9 chim: 親；吻 to kiss.　phah-kat-khiu5: 糾纏在一起 to tangle, as string.
10 pak-be7-khui: 拆不開 unable to separate.　thiaN3-tiuh-tiuh: 疼得很 hurt very much; love dearly.
