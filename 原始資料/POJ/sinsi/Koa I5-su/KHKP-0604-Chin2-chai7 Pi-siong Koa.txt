
Chin2-chai7 Pi-siong Koa
1. Lak8 jit8 e5 kang choe3 oan5-pi7,
Bo5 su7 an-bin5 kau3 koe thi5,
Chit8 chheng kau2 pah saN-gou7 ni5,
Si3-geh8 ji7-it e5 cha2-khi2.
2. Khi2 chhng5 teh che7 sun3-sut si5,
Si5-kan lak8-tiam2 chiah ke3-khi3,
Cheng3-lang5 chau2-lai5 iu7 chau2-khi3,
KhoaN3-kiN3 tian7-teng tong7-tong7 hiN3.
3. Toe7-tang7 Chu2 chaiN2 teh chu2-i3
Khai-se3 i2-lai5 u7 an2-ni,
Bi5-chin2 peh-siN3 choan5 put-ti,
Cheng3-ku3 chhiong-hun pin5 ki-khi3.
4. Choa2 siong7 sou2 po3 kek8 siong-pi,
Khai Tai5 be7-bat u7 an2-ni,
Tang5-si5 kui2 chheng lang5 teh-si2,
KhoaN3,thiaN,bo5 lang5 bo5 siong-pi.
5 .Chit8-si5 choan5-biat8 sou3 koe-chhi7,
Kian3-but8 jin5 thiok teh-piN2-piN2,
Chit ho7 chai-iong iu5-chai7 ThiN,
Sou2-i2 li2,goa2 tioh8 chu3-i3.

6. U7 lang5 teh kau3 lau3 chhui3-chih8,
U7 lang5 thau5-khak teh-piN2-piN2, 
U7 lang5 teh kau3 kha-chhiu2 chih8, 
U7 e5 choan5-ke long2 teh-si2. 
7. SiuN7 chhut biau7 hoat thang siam2-pi7, 
Thian-chai te7-pian3 si7 thiN-i3,
Se3-kai3 bo5 lang5 oe7 jia thiN, 
Keng2-seng2 theng3-hau7 boat8-jit8-ki5.
8. Hian7-si5 pi7-lan7 e5 hiaN-chi2, 
Lan2 tioh8 chin7-lat8 pang-chan7 i,
Thoe3 in chiau3-kou3 in kiaN2-ji5,
Sin-the2 siu7-siong thoe3 i-ti7.
9. Ka-ok to2-hoai pang-chan7 khi2,
Hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 bou7-chip8 chiN5,
Goan7 lan2 tai7-ke bo5 kou3 ki3,
HoaN-hi2 hiong3-cheng5 hian3 gun5-chiN5.
10. Se3-kai3 siok8 Chu2 e5 peh-siN3,
Tong5-cheng5 khek-ki2 kia3-hu3 chiN5,
Chan3-chou7 pi7-lan7 e5 hiaN-chi2,
Kau-thok koaN-oan5 pun hou7 i.
11. Chit pai2 se3-jin5 choan5 kak-chhiN2,
Beng5 chai bo5 lang5 oe7 keh8 thiN,
Hng7 kun7 peh-siN3 long2 tang5-i3,
Oe-khoeh hiong3-cheng5 kia3-hu3 mih8.
12. Long5-hu kia3-hu3 hoan-chu5 bi2,
Hu7-lu2 kia3-hu3 lai7-goa7 i,
Tiam3-oan5 kia3-hu3 chhai3, kiam5-hi5,
Siong-jin5 kia3-hu3 bi2-hun2 mi7.
13. Bok8 siong kia3-hu3 sam-a2-ki,
Kim-siong kia3-hu3 ma2-khau2-thih,
Phoe3 kau3 pi7-lan7 e5 hiuN-li2,
Thih-to7 un7-poaN bo5 siu chiN5.
14. Sun7 ki pou3-to7 thoan5 chin-li2,
Hok, ho7, tong5-cheng5 saN chi-chhi5,
Jin5-lui7 ai3-jin5 ju5 ai3 ki2,
Chiah hah8 Kiu3-chu2 sou2 ka3-si7.
15. Se3-chiuN7 saN-bat si7 chiam7-si5,
Peh8, ng5, ang5, ou, chhiah, hiaN-chi2,
Lan2 e5 Lau7-pe7 tiam3 ti7 thiN,
Sou2 bang7 bo5 ku2 oe7 saN-kiN3. 
16.Thian-chai te7-pian3 siong5-siong5 u7,
Jim7 lan2 khi2-cho7 thih chioh8 chhu3,
Siong7 iong2 koh tong3 iah8 bo5 ku2,
Boat8-ki5 na7 kau3 piN3 he2-hu.
17.TaN lan2 tai7-ke tioh8 kak-chhiN2,
