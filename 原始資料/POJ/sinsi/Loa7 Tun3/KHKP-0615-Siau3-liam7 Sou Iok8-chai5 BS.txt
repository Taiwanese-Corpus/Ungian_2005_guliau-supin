
Siau3-liam7 Sou Iok8-chai5 Bok8-su
Pin5-tong Tio7--si7 Tun2
1. Pin5-tong Bok8-su Sou Iok8-chai5,    
Tong5-i3 pheng3-chhiaN2 i chiu7 lai5;    
Lai5 kau3 Pin5-tong chin hoat-tat8,    
Siong7-te3 Seng3-keng khin5-khin5 thak8.  
2.Bok8-su u7 kam2 Seng3 Sin5 lat8,    
Au7-lai5 Pin5-tong chin hoat-tat8;
Chiu7-si7 khiam3 chiN5 u7 kui2 chheng,
Liam5-piN te5-ian5 chhong3 lai5 heng5.  
3.Lai5 kong2 Bok8-su e5 li2-lek8,    
Hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 toa7 li7-ek;    
I si7 tiong-sin5 koh ngeN7-khi3,    
Chek-pi7 khoan3-bian2 long2 si7 i .  
4.U7-e5 kam-goan7 siu7 khou2-khng3,    
Sim-koaN kek8 ngeN7 piN3-cho3 nng2;    
Iah8 u7 hit khoan2 mou5-kui2 kiaN2,    
Chhoan3 kong2 peh8-chhat8 kaN2 bo5 iaN2.  
5.Khi2-thau5 sio2-chhu3 si7 tek-liau7,    
 
Chiu7 oaN7 sio2-chhu3 kaN5 thah8-lau5;    
HiaN-ti7 chi2-moaiN7 long2 chiau5-kau3,    
O-lo2 Bok8-su li2 chin gau5.  
6.Bok8-su chin thiaN3 chiu2-koaN2-hu7, 
Lin5-bin2 pang-chan7 i long2 u7; 
Hek8-si7 thau3-cha2 tiong-tau3 si5, 
Liam5-piN kin2-sin7 bo5 the-si5.  
7.An2-ni thang chai i li2-lek8, 
Hou7 lan2 thang chai i bok8-tek; 
I cho3 Bok8-su chap8-goa7 ni5, 
Long2 si7 ho2 tek cho3 bou5-iuN7.  
8.Goan2 taN siau3-liam7 Bok8-su-niu5, 
Thang cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 bou5-iuN7; 
Oh i chin-chhiat ho2 kam2-cheng5, 
Ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 chin bo5 eng5.  
9.I e5 lu2-tek gau5 Khiu5-khiam7, 
Hou7 goan2 toa7-toa7 thang siau3-liam7; 
KhoaN3 i sou2 cho3 chin toan-chiaN3, 
Hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 ho2 mia5-siaN.  
 
10.U7-e5 lam7-sam2 lai5 kong2 oe7, 
Hit ho7 Bok8-su bo5 tang3 chhoe7; 
KhoaN3 i tak8 hang7 e5 tai7-chi3, 
Long2 si7 tui3 Chu2 e5 chi2-si7.  
11.Lai5 kong2 Mou5-kui2 e5 li7-hai7, 
Tak8 hang7 ke3-bou5 si7 phaiN2-tai7; 
Lan2 tioh8 koaN2-kin2 li7-khui i ,
M7-thang hoaN-hi2 toe3 i khi3.  
12.TaN tioh8 hoan2-hoe2 kho3 Ia5-sou, 
Ia5-sou tek-khak oe7 chhoa7 lou7; 
Chhoa7 lan2 kau3 thiN bo5 oai-chhoah8, 
Hou7 lan2 tit-tioh8 si7 eng2-oah8.  
13. Lai5 kau3 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7-cheng5, 
Tak8-hang7 tai7-chi3 beh sou3-beng5; 
Tek-khak m7-thang lam7-sam2 kong2, 
KiaN-liau2 au7-lai5 chin oan-ong2.  
14. Goa2 sia2 tam7-poh8 sio2-khoa2 si, 
Hou7 lan2 khoaN3-liau2 chin siong-pi; 
Ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 e5 su7-cheng5. 
Bok8-su put-si5 chin bo5-eng5.  
 
15.U7 lang5 hit khoan2 e5 put-liong5, 
M7-bat bok8-su si7 ho2 lang5; 
Chong2-si7 au7-lai5 oat8 tng2 sin, 
Bok8-su tek-khak m7 seng5-jin7.      
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 615 tiuN, 1936 ni5 6 geh8)
