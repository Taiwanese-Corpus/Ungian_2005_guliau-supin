Sio2-toan7  Chiam TiuN2-lo2
Lau5 A-siu3
Chiam A-hian2 si7 Kong-koan2-chng Kau3-hoe7 e5 TiuN2-lo2.  I ti7 Beng5-ti7 12 ni5 9 geh8 7 jit8 chhut-si3; i e5 chhut seng-toe7 si7 Kong-koan2-chng Sin-kang2-liau5; 8 he3 e5 si5 soa2 khi3 Pak-liau5 khia7-khi2, keng-eng5 long5-giap8 lai5 seng5-ka.  I iau2-be7 sin3 Chu2 e5 si5 than3 se3-siok8, hok8-sai7 sin5-beng5 chin jiat8-sim.
Ti7 19 he3 e5 si5, tui3 Chiong A-mui2 Thoan5-to7 thiaN-tioh8 to7-li2, chiu7 pang3-sak se3-siok8 lai5 sin3 Chu2.  Kau3 i 24 he3, chiu7-si7 Beng5-ti7 36 ni5, u7 siu7 Kong-koan2-chng Kau3-hoe7 keng2 i choe3 Chip-su7 6 ni5 ku2; kau3 30 he3 e5 si5, chiu7-si7 Beng5-ti7 42 ni5, chiu7 siat-lip8 i choe3 TiuN2-lo2, 20 ni5 ku2 bo5 li7-jim7.
I u7 nng7 e5 ta-pou-kiaN2, nng7 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2.  Toa7-kiaN2 Chiam Tek-kian3, iau2 ti7 Tai5-pak Sin5 Hak8-hau7 chai7-hak8 tiong, 3 ni5 seng; te7-ji7 kiaN2 teh pang-chan7 ke-lai7; soe3-han3 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 kin-ni5 14 he3, iau2 ti7 Kong-hak8-hau7 pou2-sip8- kho thak8.  I ti7 11 geh8 10 jit8 iau2 khi3 Biau5-lek8 Kau3-hoe7 hu3 Tok-hoe7, ti7 13 jit8 e7-pou 5 tiam2 hut-jian5 tu2-tioh8 nau2 chhut-hui e5 piN7, kau3 14 jit8 teng2-pou 11 tiam2-poaN3 chiu7 ke3-ong2, hiong2-siu7 50 he3.  Ti7 18 jit8 u7 chong3-sek, u7 7 ui7 Thoan5-kau3-su lai5 hoe7-chong3, u7 tiau3-tian7 20 goa7 thong, ia7 u7 kui2-na7 ui7 chiuN7 tai5 tiau3-su5.
TaN ai3 sia2 kui2-na7 hang7 lai5 siau3-liam7 i:
1.I si7 khek-khun5 khek-khiam7 e5 lang5.  Cha2-khi2 si5 chin cha2 chiu7 khi2-lai5, le2-pai3-jit8 kap kong-ek e5 su7 e5 i2-goa7, bo5 chit8 si5 eng5; i chin-chiaN3 gau5 po2-sioh si5-kan.
2. I ho2 khoan2-thai7 chhut-goa7 lang5.  Bo5-lun7 hng7-kun7, bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 teng2-ho7 e5 lang5, na7 kau3 i e5 ke, long2 it-li2 ho2 khoan2-thai7 i.
3. I choan-siu2 le2-pai3-jit8.  Chiu7-si7 bo5-lun7 sim2-mih8 kin2 kang e5 si5, na7 le2-pai3-jit8 chiu7 long2 hioh-khun3 lai5 le2-pai3; m7-na7 i e5 ke-lai7 e5 lang5 hioh-khun3 na7-tiaN7, lian5 tng5-kang kap sou2 chhiaN3 e5 choe3 kang lang5 ia7 long2 hioh-khun3 bo5 choe3 kang.
4. I si7 chin seng5-sit8 e5 lang5.  Kong2 oe7 si7, chiu7-si7; m7-si7, chiu7 m7-si7; sou2-i2 hoe7-lai7, oe7-goa7 ho2 mia5-siaN, bo5-lun7 hoe7-lai7 hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5 thiaN-tioh8 i si2-khi3, tak8 lang5 to kong2 chin phah-sng2.
5. I chin chin7-tiong.  I u7 choe3 Chip-su7 6 ni5, choe3 TiuN2-lo2 20 ni5; i khoaN3 Siong7-te3 e5 kok pi2 i e5 ke khah iau3-kin2, na7 kau3-hoe7 u7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3, sui-jian5 si7 chin bo5 eng5, ia7 hoaN-hi2 pang3 kang.  I u7 choe3 po2-chiaN3 kui2-na7 ni5 ku2, tui3 sia7-hoe7 ia7 si7 chin7 gi7-bu7, thang kong2 si7 pe3-hoe7 chit8 e5 bou5-hoan7-chia2.  TaN ka-teng5 sit chit8 e5 liong5-hu7, kau3-hoe7 sit chit8 ki tong3-liong5, boe7 bian2-tit thong3-sim siong-pi.
( Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 538 koan3,1930 ni5 1 geh8)
