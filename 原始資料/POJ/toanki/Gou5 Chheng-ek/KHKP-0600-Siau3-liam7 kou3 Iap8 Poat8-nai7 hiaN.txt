Siau3-liam7 kou3 Iap8 Poat8-nai7 hiaN
   Gou5 Chheng-ek
Kong2-khi2 i2-keng ke3-ong2, jiat8-sim e5 seng3-tou5 e5 mia5, lai5 tui-liam7 i thang o-lo2 e5 sin3-tek, sit8-chai7 si7 choe3 lan2 tai7-ke e5 li7-ek; iah8 si7 hian2-beng5 Siong7-te3 e5 eng5-kng.  Poat8-nai7 hiaN, chin-chiaN3 si7 lan2 tai7-ke sou2 tioh8 siau3-liam7 e5 seng3-tou5. I ti7 Chiau-ho5 9 ni5, 12 geh8 22 jit8 e7-pou 7 tiam2, chai-kioh Siong7-te3 tiau3 i kui-thian e5 si5 i2-keng kau3, chiu7 tiau3-chip8 ke-lai7 toa7-soe3 lai5 choe3 le2-pai3, ka-ki7 keng2 Seng3-si te7 19 siu2 ¡§Goa2 bak8 kiah8 khi2 ng3 thiN, Siong7-te3 sou2 tiam3 sou2-chai7¡¨, kap ke-lai7 e5 lang5 saN-kap gim5.  Le2-pai3 soah, kong2 I sian7, chiu7 to2-loh8 bin5-chhng5 an-bin5, ho2 khun3; hiong2-siu7 67 hoe3.
Peng5-sou3 kap i saN-bat-e5, thiaN-tioh8 chit e5 siau-sit, tek-khak beh kong2 Tiong-lek8 Kau3-hoe7 u7 sit-loh8 chit8 e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7 e5 sin3-chia2.  Poat8-nai7 hiaN chin-chiaN3 si7 ho2 e5 bou5-iuN7; lun7 siu2 le2-pai3, tui3 goa2 ku7-ni5 4 geh8 lai5 hu3-jim7 i2-lai5, i m7-bat hioh chit8 le2-pai3, m7-na7 an2-ni, i e5 chhu3 li7 pai3-tng5 put-chi2 hng7, iau2-ku2 i si7 te7-it cha2 chiu7 kau3 pai3-tng5. Lun7 hian3-kim I iah8 put-chi2 kam, thang kong2 i sit8-chai7 si7 chin7 i e5 khui3-lat8 ti7 teh hian3.  Lun7 tui3 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7 e5 thai3-tou7, iah8 si7 chin kho2-chhu2.  Ti7 Seng3-keng-hoe7, ti7 thiaN soat-kau3, i bo5 peh-khi2 peh-loh8, si7 chin koan-sim aN3 hi7-khang teh thiaN. Sui-bong2 sou2 thiaN--e5 bo5 tak8-ku3 oe7 beng5-pek8, iau2-ku2 i e5 sim chin jiat8.  Goa2 sin3, i teh thiaN to7-li2 e5 thai3-tou7, tek-khak m7-si7 ai3 beh thiaN kong2-kou2 lai5 tit-tioh8 ho2-chhio3; chiu7-si7 tui3 iau, chhui3-ta, him-bou7 gi7 e5 sim, ai3 beh thiaN Siong7-te3 e5 oe7. Siong7-te3 e5 oe7 kong2 chit8-pah ku3 e5 si5, I na7 oe7-thang tit-tioh8 nng7-ku3 na7-tiaN7, I iah8 si7 tek-khak chin boan2-chiok. In-ui7 i e5 choe3 lang5 sit8-chai7 khiam-pi, i chai-iaN2 ka-ki7 e5 khui3-lat8, i m7-kaN2 eng7 ka-ki7 e5 khui3-lat8, beh li2-kai2 Siong7-te3 long2-chong2 e5 chin-li2.  Tui3 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7, i si7 khiam-pi, si7 eng7 kam2-sia7 e5 sim teh thiaN, tek-khak m7-si7 khia7 ti7 phoe-pheng5 e5 thai3-tou7 lai5 teh thiaN. Koh-chai3 i ti7 kau3-hoe7 e5 seng-oah8, si7 sun7-than3 e5 seng-oah8, si7 kam2-sia7 e5 seng-oah8.  Ti7 kau3-hoe7, i si7 ai3 hong7-su7, m7-si7 ai3 chi-phoe3; sou2-i2 long2 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 i tui3 kau3-hoe7 u7 khng3 sim2-mih8 put-peng5, a2-si7 sim2-mih8 bo5 hoaN-hi2.  I siong5-siong5 ko3-pek8 ka-ki7 e5 bo5 kau3-giah8, m7-bat hiam5 pat8-lang5.
Chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2, sit8-chai7 thang kong2 si7 chin-sit8 bat Chu2 e5 Ki-tok-tou5, sit8-chai7 thang choe3 lan2 e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7.  Lan2 tioh8 siau3-liam7 chit e5 Seng3-tou5 lai5 oh8 i e5 sin3-tek.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 600 koan3, 1935 ni5 3 geh8)
