Ang5 Khu--si7 A-le7 e5 Sio2-toan7
Giam7 Khoah-hoai5	 
Ang5 Khu--si7 A-le7 chhut-si3 ti7 Beng5-ti7 21 ni5 9 geh8 26 jit8.  I si7 Toa7-khou-ham7 lang5, i e5 pe7-bu2 long2 bo5 thiaN to7-li2.  Chong2-si7 A-le7 chin ai3 khi3 pai3-tng5 thiaN lang5 kong2 Ia5-sou e5 lai5-lek8, pai3-tng5 e5 sian-siN chiu7 ka3 i thak8 Lo5-ma2-ji7.  Au7-lai5 soa3 ka7 i ku2-chian3 ke3 hou7 hoe7-iu2 Ang5 Hian2-li2 choe3 hu7-jin5-lang5 tiong7-hu hit-si5 teh thak8 Sin5-hak8-hau7.  Kiat-hun liau2 chiu7 kap tiong7-hu khi3 toa3 pai3-tng5 kui2-na7 ni5 ku2.  Liau2-au7 tiong7-hu pang3-soah thoang5-to7 e5 kang, i chiu7 ka-ki7 ka3 kong-hak8-hau7 lai5 pang-chan7 ka-teng5 e5 seng-oah8.  I u7 keng-ke3 kong-hak8-hau7, te7-ji7 hu3-siok8, chou7-san2 hu7 kang2-sip8-hoe7 long2-chong2 u7 kau3 chut-giap8.  Che thang kong2 si7 i e5 tek8-sek; in-ui7 i m7-si7 chhut-si3 ti7 bun5-beng5 u7 kau3-iok8 e5 ka-teng5, che choan-choan si7 tui3 i e5 houN3-hak8 khun5-bian2 lai5 tit-tioh8 e5.
Au7-lai5 u7 siu7 chiok-thok choe3 Koan-se e5 chng-iah8-tiuN5 e5 chou7-san2-hu7, tiam3 chia keng-ke3 5 ni5 ku2 put-chi2 ho2 seng5-chek, hou7 chng-bin5 tai7-ke put-chi2 o-lo2 siau3-liam7.  Kho2-sioh i e5 seng-khu chiam7-chiam7 soe-jiok8, chiah8-ioh8 iah8 bo5-hau7, chiu7 ti7 7 geh8 26 jit8 u7 hou7 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 i e5 leng5-hun5, hiong2 siu7 42 he3. I ti7 lim5-chiong e5 si5 chhui3 iah8 tiaN7-tiaN7 teh liam7 Chu2 e5 Ki5-to2-bun5.
Tan lun7 chit e5 chi2-be7 u7 kui2-na7 hang e5 kho2-chhu2 thang hou7 cheng3 chi2-be7 choe3 bou5-hoan7.  Chiu7-si7 put-chi2 ti3-i3 in2-chhoa7 kiaN2-ji5 jiat8-sim hak8-sai7 Siong7-te3, siong5-siong5 ti3-i3 in tioh8 siu7 le2-pai3, koh khun5-khun5 ka3-si7 in bat Seng3-keng.  I chai7-seng u7 him-bou7 thiN-kok khah ke3-thau5 ti7 Se3-kan, sou2-i2 Siong7-te3 tek-khak oe7 chiau3 i e5 ho2 sou2 kiaN5 lai5 khoan2-thai7 i.  ChhiaN2 liat8-ui7 khoaN3 Po3 e5 hiaN-chi2 oe7-hiau2 oh8 i gau5 in2-chhoa7 kiaN2-ji5 hok8-sai7 Chu2.  Si7 sim sou2 goan7.
( Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 535 koan3,1929 ni5 10 geh8)
