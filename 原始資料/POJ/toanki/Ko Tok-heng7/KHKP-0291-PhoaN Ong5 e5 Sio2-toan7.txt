PhoaN Ong5 e5 Sio2-toan7
Ko Tok-heng5
Ong5 peh si7 Kiam5-chui2-kang2 thiaN Thau5-sia7 e5 kau3-hoe7 Tek-theh8-chng e5 lang5.  I sng3 si7 Thau5-sia7 kau3-hoe7 khi2-ki e5 lang5.  U7 Thau5-sia7 e5 kau3-hoe7 i chiu7 tit-tioh8 to7-li2, chha-put-to ti7 40 hoe3 e5 si5-chun7.  Ia7 tui3 siat kau3-hoe7 chiu7 hou7 lang5 keng2 i tng-chit cho3 chip-su7, kau3 Thau5-sia7 siat cho3 tong5-hoe7 liau2-au7 chiu7 seng cho3 tiuN2-lo2, kau3 i 73 hoe3 chiu7 bo5 tng-chit; cheng3 hoe7-iu2 koh lau5 chit, i tui3 bok8-su tit8-tit8 si5 kong2, i lau7 lah hi7-khang chhau3-lang5, ti7 Tai7-hoe7 e5 si5 beh kap lang5 gi7-lun7 kau3-hoe7 e5 su7, long2 bo5 thiaN-kiN3.  Sou2-i2 bok8-su the2-thiap i ni5 lau7 tng-chit ia7 chin ku2 hou7 i si5, chiah chhiaN2 hoe7-iu2 koh keng2 pat8 lang5.  Tui3 i bo5 tng-chit iu5-goan5 si7 chin7-tiong ti7 kau3-hoe7 bo5 the3.  Lun7 Ong5 peh it-seng sin3 Chu2 e5 ho2 bou5-iuN7 ai3 pai5-liat8 kui2-na7 hang7: -- 
1. Ong5 peh e5 chin7-tiong.  Tui3 Ong5 peh tng-chit u7 lek8-koe3 khi2 saN pang e5 le2-pai3-tng5.  Thau5 chit8 pang si7 chhau2-chhu3 chhak8 tek e5, si7 Ong5 peh pun2-sin kap i ka-ti7 chhu3-lai7 e5 lang5 khi2 e5, long2 bo5 khai kau3-hoe7 e5 chiN5.  Koh le2-pai3-tng5 lai7 e5 bin5-chhng5, tek-bih8-lou7 chiu7 si7 thoan5-to7 sou2 eng7 e5 chhou ke-si long2 si7 Ong5 peh pun2-sin e5 kang chhong3 e5, iu5-goan5 ia7 bo5 khai kau3-hoe7 e5 chiN5.  Au7-lai5 kau3-hoe7 khah chin3-pou7 chhau2-chhu3 siuN eh8, chiu7 koh chhak8 saN keng kham3-hia7 e5, e7 iong5-un2 tit chiaN5 pah lang5 chu7-chip8.  TaN khi2 chit te3 hia7 e5, iu5-goan5 si7 i ka-ti7 e5 kang, khi2-thau5 u7 kui2-na7 e5 hiaN-ti7 tau3 pang-chan7.  Kau3 au7-lai5 hiaN-ti7 cho3 liau2 sian7 tit8-tit8 liu, chiu7 soa3 chhun Ong5 peh chit8 lang5 teh cho3, i long2 bo5 siuN7 sian7, ia7 hit si5 si7 ha7-thiN si5 jit8 tng joah8.  Pun2 chng e5 se3-siok8 lang5 chiu7 thi2-chhio3 kong2, ¡§HaiN7 ah!  Ong5 peh li2 chiah--nih8 lau7 lah, ia7 jit8-thau5 tng joah8, long2 bo5 lang5 pek li2, li2 an3-choaN2 hiah gong7, tak8 jit8 toa3 chia teh khok8-khok8¡¨.  Ong5 peh thou2-khui3 chiu7 kong2 ai3-ah!  Lin2 chiah e5 lang5 m7-bat Siong7-te3 e5 in.
Ia7 hit si5 chin ku2 bo5 loh8 hou7, pun2-chng se3-siok8 lang5 chiu7 kong2, Ong5 peh ah li2 
e5 le2-pai3-tng5 tioh8 kin2 kham3, in-ui7 lin2 e5 Siong7-te3 khoaN3 li2 boe7 kham3 soa3 m7 loh8 hou7.  Kau3 Ong5 peh le2-pai3-tng5 tu2 kham3 liau2 chiu7 loh8 toa7 hou7.  M7-ku2 pun2-chng e5 lang5 bo5 chheN2-gou7 ko2-jian5 m7-bat Siong7-te3 e5 in.  TaN chit pang si7 te7 ji7 pai2 khi2 e5.
Ia7 te7 saN pang koh khi2 si7 goa2 ti7 Thau5-sia7 e5 si5.  Chu2 1906 ni5 e5 si5-chun7 chiu7 koh khi2 chit8 keng sin pai3-tng5 e5 chu7-chip8 nng7 pah goa7 lang5.  Khi2 chit keng si7 hou7 sai-hu7 pau cho3, sio2 kang si7 tak8 hoe2-chau3 e5 hiaN-ti7 nge7-kha-keng lun5-liu5; Ong5 peh sui-jian5 hia lau7 chhit-chap8 goa7 hoe3 lah, i ia7 lun5-liu5 kap lang5 cho3 sio2 kang thau5 toe3 kau3 boe2.  Che chin chiaN3 si7 Ong5 peh e5 chin7-tiong lah!
2. Ong5 peh e5 khek-ki2.  I lai5 thiaN to7-li2 e5 si5-chun7 si7 kan-khou2 long2 bo5 chhan5-giap8, kau3 thiaN to7-li2 liau2-au7 Siong7-te3 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 siuN2-su3 kau3 hou7 i u7 chhan5-giap8.  Chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 seN kiaN2 thang tit i e5 chhan5-giap8; sou2-i2 i boe7 si2 e5 si5-chun7, chiu7 chiong i hiah e5 chhan5-giap8 pun cho3 4 hun7 hou7 i e5 chhin chok8, chit8 lang5 chit8 tiuN khau-su, long2 be7 sai2 be7 tit.  Ia7 mui2 hun7 mui2 ni5 tioh8 lap8 5 khou hou7 kau3-hoe7 cho3 thoan5-to7 sin-kim e5 lou7-eng7, Ong5 peh bo5 beh tui3 in lai5 thiu cho3 ka-ti7 e5 lou7-eng7 kau3 ti7 pah-hoe3 ni5 lau7.  Che si7 i e5 khek-ki2.  Chong2-si7 Ong5 peh sin-khu-piN u7 chit8 pah khou e5 hian7 gin5, m7-ku2 i ia7 bo5 beh lau5 cho3 ka-ti7 e5 lou7-eng7; i u7 tui3 kau3-hoe7 kong2-beng5, Thau5-sia7 e5 kau3-hoe7 na7 koh khi2 sin le2-pai3-tng5, i beh chiong hit pah khou cho3 khi2 pai3-tng5 e5 sou2-hui3.  Sou2-i2 ti7 1906 ni5 koh khi2 sin le2-pai3-tng5, i chiu7 theh8 hian3 hou7 kau3-hoe7.
Koh chit8 hang7 chhan5-giap8 pun hou7 i e5 chhin chok8, ia7 hit pah khou kong2-beng5 beh hian3 hou7 kau3-hoe7 liau2-au7; i chiu7 ka-ti7 koh-chai3 phah piaN3, khui soaN kut8 phiaN5 cheng3 hoan-chu5, ou7-a2, kiuN, li2-a2, chhek-a2, an2-ni siu an2-ni chiah8, m7-bian2 kho3 pat8 lang5; sui-jian5 hiah lau7 m7-ku2 sin-the2 chin iong2-kiaN7, chin gau5 thoa-boa5.  Ia7 i sou2 chiah8 e5 jit8-sit8 chiu7-si7 hoan-chu5-chhiam phoe3 tau7-chiuN3 chin khiam7; goa2 na7 khi3 tham3 i, khoaN3 i chiah8 hoan-chu5-chhiam phoe3 tau7-chiuN3, chiu7 ka7 i kong2, Ong5 peh 
li2 chiah nih8 lau7 lah, m7 thang choan chiah8 hoan-chu5-chhiam tioh8 chham khah che7 bi2, iah8 tioh8 be2 tam7-poh8 khah ho2 e5 mih8 lai5 phoe3.  I chiu7 in3 kong2, Chit ho7 jit8-sit8 goa2 to iau2 chiah8 e7 loh8-khi3.  Na7 i e5 cha-bou2-sun khoaN3 i sin-the2 tam7-poh8 phoa3-peN7, hoan-chu5 chhiam chham khah kau7 bi2, i chiu7 hiam5 i, kio3 i khah khiam7, bi2-chhek chin kui3.  TaN i e5 khin5-khiam7 sou2 chek-chu7 e5 chiN5 hian3 hou7 Siong7-te3 cho3 kau3-hoe7 e5 lou7-eng7, chhin-chhiuN7 thoan5-to7 sin-kim, pun2 tong5 e5 chap8-hui3 a2-si7 chan7 pat8 ui7, ni5-ni5 long2 cho3 thau5 tai7-seng lai5 kau.  TaN chit e5 si7 Ong5 peh khek-ki2 ti7 kau3-hoe7.
3. Ong5 peh e5 jin5-ai3.  Tui3 Ong5 peh thiaN to7-li2 liau2-au7 khi2-thau5 kau3 lou7-boe2 chin gau5 chio-hou lang5 lai5 sin3 Chu2.  Ia7 chin gau5 thiaN3-thang3 lang5 the2-thiap san3-hiong lang5.  Sou2-i2 u7 che7-che7 lang5 tui3 khoaN3 Ong5 peh e5 jin5-ai3 thiaN3 lang5 siu7 kam2-kek lai5 sin3 Chu2.  Na7 hit ho7 san3-hiong kan-khou2 e5 lang5 ka7 i chioh chiN5, i bo5 the-si5, ia7 bo5 sng3 in e5 li7-sek.  Lun7 i kap lang5 kau-chiap ong2-lai5 sim-koaN chin khui-khoah, sui-jian5 hou7 lang5 khah phiN i bo5 ai3 kap lang5 ke3-kau3, chin gau5 thun-lun2.  Na7 kau3-hoe7 lai7 a2-si7 kau3-hoe7 goa7 e5 lang5 kau3 i e5 ke put-chi2 khian5 i3 ho2 le2 khoan2-thai7 lang5.  Lun7 thoan5-to7 e5 lang5 i koh khah ka-poe7 e5 keng3-tiong7 khoan2-thai7.  Che si7 Ong5-peh e5 jin5-ai3.
TaN lun7 Ong5 peh hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3 e5 jiat8-sim kap i e5 ho2 phin2-heng7 chip-siu2 seng3 to7 pang-chan7 kau3-hoe7 chin7 sim chin7 lat8 m7-bian2 long2-chong2 kong2.
