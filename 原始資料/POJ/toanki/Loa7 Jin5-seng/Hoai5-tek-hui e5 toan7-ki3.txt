HOAI5-TEK-HUI E5 TOAN7-KI3
1
Chhi7-tiuN2 lai5 sun5-si7 hak8-hau7 e5 jit8 kau3-lah, hak8- hau7 cheng2-tun3 kau3 chin chheng-khi3, hak8-seng long2 pai5-liat8 ngia5-chih.  Hit-jit8 kong2 hoan-geng5-su5--e5 iu5- goan5 keng2 tioh8 goa2, goa2 e5 mia5 kio3-choe3 Hoai5-tek-hui.  Goa2 si7 chit8 e5 phaiN2-mia7 gia2-na2, goa2 chhut-si3 ti7 Chu2-au7 1714 ni5 12 goeh8 14 jit8, ti7 Eng-kok Kat-lo5 su-tek (Gloucester) siaN5 e5 Poe3-ni2 lu2-koan2 (Bell Inn) lai7.  Nng7 hoe3 e5 si5 chiu7 bo5 pe7, lau7-bu2 chiuN2- koaN2, kap ki-u5 e5 hiaN-ti7 chit8-ke kiong7 8 e5 lang5.  Lu2-koan2 keng-eng5 liau2 be7 khi2-sek, 8 ni5 au7 goa2 e5 lau7-bu2 chiu7 koh tiong5-hun (重婚).  In-ui7 ka-teng5 san3-hiong kau3 12 hoe3 lau7-bu2 chiah sang3 goa2 jip8 u5-pi7 hak8-hau7.  In-ui7 lang5 jin7-teng7 goa2 u7 hiong5-pian7 gau5 kong2-oe7 e5 chai5, sou2-i2 mui2-ni5 long2-si7 keng2 goa2 kong2 hoan-geng5-su5.  Koh chit8-bin7 goa2 chin ho2 ki3-ti5, gau5 oh8 bok8-su oat8-liam7 ki5-to2-bun5, jian5-au7 ng3 tang5-oh8 lai5 choe3 le2-pai3 kiat-sok e5 khoan2-sit lai5 liah8 choe3 sim-sek, kau3-boe2 hou7 hak8-hau7 pek thoe3 oh8.  Hit- si5 15 hoe3, to3--lai5 chhiN2-lam5-sek e5 ui5-su-kun5-a2 ha5-leh chiu7 pang-chan7 lau7-bu2 sau3 thou5-kha, jiu5 te7-pan2, choe3 kou3-kheh chap8-chhe-a2 e5 gin2-na2 kang.  U7 chit8- pai2 khi3 Bu-nai su-tok (Bristol) chhoe7 goan2 hiaN-ko.   Ti7 hia thiaN bok8-su kang2-to7, put-chi2 hou7 goa2 kam2- kek, tui3 hit-si5 khi2 thau5-pai2 tit-tioh8 sim-lai7 siok8- leng5 e5 in3-siong7.  Tui3 an2-ni goa2 to3--lai5 chiu7 chiong hi3-kiok8 (戲劇) kap bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 chheh long2 pang3 hiat8-kak8, choan-sim oh8 sia2 kang2-ian2 e5 bun5-chiuN.  Chiong5-chian5 e5 seng-oah8 kap sip8-koan3 goa2 kat-tit chin bo5 chu-bi7.  Che m7-si7 kong2 goa2 i2-keng u7 sim2-mih8 chhim-khek siok8-leng5 e5 keng-giam7.  Si7 chit8-si5 siuN7 pi7- pan7 chiong-lai5 ai choe3 chit8 e5 thoan5-kau3-ka na7-tiaN7.
Kau3 18 hoe3 e5 si5 kam2-sia7 Chu2 hou7 goa2 tit-tioh8 chit8 e5 ki-hoe7 thang choe3 khou2-hak8-seng, poaN3 choe3-kang poaN3 thak8-chheh e5 lou7 chiu7 khi3 jip8 Giu5-chin toa7-oh8.  Khi2-thau5 chit8-ni5-ku2 chin peng5-siong5 chiam7-chiam7 keng-koe3, sou2 u7 e5 si5-kan long2 iong7 ti7 hak8-kho3, khiok bat thak8 goa7-chheh.  Ti7 sou2 thak8 e5 goa7-chheh  tiong Lo5--si7 sou2 tu3 e5 “Giam5-siok e5 hou-tiau3” (Law’s Serious Call to the Unconverted) hit-pun2 si7 goa2 siang7 jiat8-sim ai3 thak8 e5 chheh.  Hit-si5 ti7 Giu5-chin tai7-hak8 u7 kui2-ui7 tong5-oh8, keng-siong5 ti7 pi2-chhu2 e5 khun2-pang5 lai7 chu7-hoe7, ki5-to2 gian2-kiu3 tho2-lun7.  Chiah e5 tai7-chi3 i2- keng sit8-heng5 liau2 kui2-na7-ni5.  Oe7-su-li2 in  hiaN-ti7 Iok-han7 kap Cha-li2 (John and Charles Wesley) si7 chit e5 se3-se3 e5 thoan5-the2 e5 leng2-siu3.  In siu7 tong5-oh8 e5 leng2- chhio3 ki-chhi3, ka7 in ho7 che7-che7 mia5.  U7--si5 kio3 in choe3  “Seng3-kiat8-thoan5” (Holy Club), “Keng3-khian5-thoan5”  (Godly Club), a2-si7 “Seng3-keng chiu3-thang5” (Bible  Moths), kap “Sun5-kui-chia2” (Methodists).  Tng goa2 jip8-oh8 e5 si5, chit e5 chip8-hoe7 chi2-u7 chhun 5, 6 e5 lang5 na7-tiaN7.  Lang5 keng-the2 in choe3 “Sun5-kui-chia2” (循規者), si7 in-ui7 in e5 seng-oah8 thai3 kui-lut8-hoa3.  Mui2-goeh8 peh-piaN2 siu2 boan2-chhan, siong5-siong5 khi3 tham3- mng7 peN7-lang5, a2-si7 kaN-hoan7.
Goa2 chin hi-bong7 ai3 ka-jip8 chit e5 chu7-hoe7, na7- si7 m7 kap in saN-bat, ji5-chhiaN2 chit8-si5 iu7 bo5 lang5 thang kai3-siau7 chiu7 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang ka-jip8.  Keh chit8-ni5 au7 u7 chit8 e5 tek8-piat8 e5 ki-hoe7, goa2 chiu7 kap Cha-li2 Oe7-su-li2 (Charles Wesley) sek8-sai7.  U7 chit8- jit8 u7 chit8 e5 san3-hiong hu7-jin5-lang5 iam3-se3 khi3 chu7- sat, heng7-tit chu7-sat bo5 si2, goa2 chiu7 sia2 chit8-tiuN phoe chhiaN2 ti7 goan2 hau7-lai7 teh be7 pin-ko2 e5 lau7 hu7- jin5-lang5 theh8 hou7 Oe7-su-li2 sian-siN, chhiaN2 in khi3 tham3-hong2 hit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5. Ia7 kio3 hit e5 lau7 a-po5 m7-thang kong2 goa2 e5 mia5, bo5 phah-sng3 hit e5 a-po5 soa3 chiong goa2 e5 mia5 ka7 in kong2.  Cha-li2 koe3-khi3 bat thiaN-kiN3 goa2 ia7 bat khi3 tham3 kaN-gak8 e5 hoan7- lang5, ia7 chham-ka kau3-khu e5 seng3-chhan, koh si5- siong5 bat gu7-kiN3 goa2 ka-ki7 chit8 e5 teh san3-pou7, tui3 an2-ni i hoaN-hi2 chiap-siu7 goa2 e5 phoe, soa3 kio3 hit e5 lau7 a-po5 lai5 chio goa2 saN-kap ti7 bin5-a2-jit8 choe3-hoe2 chiah8 tiong-tau3-tng3.  Goa2 e5 sim tu2-tu2 him-bou7 ai3 tit- tioh8 u7 siok8-leng5 e5 peng5-iu2.  I ia7 chin khoai3 chiu7 chai goa2 si7 chit e5 i3-su3, chiu7 sang3 goa2 chit8-pun2 Hoat-lan5- khek sou2 tu3 e5 “Lun7 m7 kiaN lang5” (Franke’s Treatise Against the Fear of Man) kap “Kau3-bok8 e5 tiong- ko3” (The County Parson’s Advice to His Parishioners)  chit nng7-pun2 chheh.  Keh liau2 bo5 loa7-ku2, chiu7 koh hou7 goa2 chit8-pun2 “Sin5 e5 oah8-mia7 ti7 lang5 e5 lai7-bin7”  (The Life of God in the Soul of Man).  Goa2 chiong5- lai5 m7-chai sim2-mih8 si7 chin-sit8 e5 keng3-khian5, tit8 kau3 Siong7-te3 chiong chit-pun2 ho2 e5 chheh sang3 kau3 goa2 chhiu2-nih8 goa2 chiah chai.  Sin5 liam-piN chi2-si7 goa2, chin-sit8 e5 keng3-khian5 chiu7-si7 lang5 kap Sin5 e5 lian5-hap8, si7 Ki-tok e5 khoan2-sit chiaN5-choe3 ti7 lan2 e5 lai7- bin7.  Chit-si5 goa2 chiah hiau2-ngou7 lang5 tek-khak tioh8 chiaN5-choe3 chit8-e5 chhong3-cho7 e5 lang5.  Chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 Sat-ma2-li7-a e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 chit8-iuN7.  Goa2 sia2 phoe hou7 goa2 e5 chhin-chhek, ka7 in kong2 chit e5 su7, ka7 in kong2 goa2 i2-keng tit-tioh8 teng-thau5-siN, ia7 sit8-chai7 u7 teng-thau5-siN e5 un-su3.  In long2 liah8 goa2 choe3 hoat-kong5.

2
Tui3 an2-ni goa2 chiu7 khai-si2 u7 kui-lut8 e5 seng-oah8, m7- ai3 phah-sng2 chit8-sut8-a2 si5-kan.  Put-lun7 in khi3 tham3 piN7-lang5, a2-si7 pang-chan7 song3-hiong-lang5, chham-ka in it-chhe3 e5 oah8-tang7, sim-lai7 kam2-kak chin song2- khoai3.  Chong2-si7 ho2 kong-keng2 bo5 thang ku2, bian2 loa7 ku2, phoa3-piN7-kui2 put-si5 lai5 kau-tiN5, ti3-kau3 goa2 tioh8 chiam7-si5 theng5-chi2 it-chhe3 e5 oah8-tang7.  Na7-si7 goa2 tui3 it-chhe3 e5 kiu3-un khiok it-hoat chheng-chho2, ia7 leng2-to7 chit8-ui7 tong5-oh8 kui Chu2.
Goan2 chit-pan kek8 sio2-sou3 keng3-khian3 e5 tong5-oh8 tong-jian5 siong5 gu7-tioh8 lang5 hoan2-tui3.  Oe7-su-li2 in hiaN-ti7-a2 tai7-seng kong-khai seng5-jin7 Ki-tok.  Goa2 chin chu7-khui3, khi2-thau5 goa2 m7-kaN2 ti7 kong-kiong7 e5 sou2-chai7 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 kap in hiaN-ti7-a2 kau-poe5.  U7 chit8-pai2 goa2 beh khi3 chhoe7 Cha-li2, khoaN3 tioh8 tong5-oh8 tui3 hit-peng5 teh lai5 beh tui3 i mng5- khau2 koe3, goa2 chiu7 ke2 bo5-i3 oat tui3 piN-a3 m7-kaN2 kio3 Cha-li2 e5 mng5.  Chit khoan2 pi-phi2 thang kian3-siau3 e5 sim kap thai3-tou7, khiok bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 bo5 khi3, au7-lai5 goa2 chiu7 iong2-kam2 na2 Oe7-su-li2 in hiaN-ti7 chit8-iuN7.  Chong2-si7 iau2-ku2 boe7-oe7 thoat-li7 Ni5- ko-ti3-bo2 sek e5 chiau3-ku7 eng7 am3-si5 chiah lai5 kap in chu7-chip8.
U7 goa7 choe7 tong5-oh8 tu2-tioh8 chhi3-lian7 kau3 chhiu2 e5 si5 long2 li7-khui goan2 khi3.  Tui3 tok-hak8 e5 hoan2-tui3 ia7 choe7-choe7 tong5-oh8 kiu khi3.  “Ai3-bou7 lang5 e5 o-lo2, khah koe3-thau5 ti7 ai3-bou7 tui3 Sin5 lai5 e5 o-lo2, kap chit8-chiong2 pi-phi2 khut-hok8-seng3 kiaN lang5 biau2-si7 khoaN3-khin e5 sim, ti3-kau3 hou7 che7- che7 lang5 hoan2-poe7 khi3-sak goan2.”  Goa2 (Hoai5-tek-hui) si7 chit8 e5 song3-hiong e5 khou2-hak8 seng, tui3 ka-jip8 sun-kui-thoan5 kap Oe7-su-li2 in ong2-lai5 i2-au7, koh khah si7 siu7 lang5 chiong2-chiong2 e5 bu2-jiok8.  Goa2 siong5-siong5 siu7 lang5 tim3 la5-sam5-mih8, u7 e5 pak-siah goa2 e5 kang-chiN5, u7 nng7-ui7 goa2 ti-ki2 e5 peng5-iu2, khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 koat-sim hu7-khi2 sip8-ji7-ke3 beh kun-toe3 Ki-tok, chiu7 liah8 goa2 choe3 thang kian3-siau3--e5, kau3-boe2 keng2- jian5 pang3-sak goa2.  Lian5 ?N-tiuN2 ia7 siong5-siong5 hang2-hoah goa2.  Sim7-chi3 u7 chit8-pai2 haN2 kong2 beh ka7 goa2 kek-chhut oh8.  Kong2 goa2 na7 koh ke3-siok8 khi3 tham3 peN7-lang5, song3-hiong e lang5 tek-khak m7 pang3 goa2 soah.  Goa2 khi2-thau5 liau2 chin kiaN, chiu7 khut-hok8 chit e5 ui-hek, na7-si7 bian2 koh loa7-ku2 goa2 chiu7 koh ho2-taN2 khi2--lai5, chiu7 chiau3-siong5 koh ui7 Chu2 kang-chok.   Kai-chai3 u7 chit8-ui7 chin jin5-chu5 e5 to7-su (導師 chi2- to7 e5 sian-siN) siong5-siong5 eng7 chheh chioh--goa2, tui3 goa2 kek8 ho2 cheng5-i3, chiong5-lai5  m7-bat hoan2-tui3 goa2 e5 kang-chok, put-ko i si7 kong2 goa2 tui3 sin3-giong2 u7 khah koe3-thau5 jiat8-sim.
Goa2 ti7 Kat-lo5-su-tek e5 chhin-iu2, tui3 goa2 sia2-phoe hou7 in chai goa2 u7 kai2-pian3 liau2-au7, in chin gong5-ngiah8 kiaN-hiaN5, tui3 goa2 seN-chhut kiong5-liat8 e5 phian- kian3, sim7-chi3 u7 lang5 kong2 goa2 si7 khi2 siau2.  Na7-si7 goa2 liah8 chiah e5 choe3 se3-se3 e5 chhi3-lian7 hoan2-tng2 si7 u7 li7-ek goa2.  Bian2 loa7-ku2 goa2 chiu7 hoat-hian7 tioh8 lang5 ui7-tioh8 Chu2 e5 mia5 lai5 li7-khui pe7-bo2, bou2-kiaN2, a2-si7 chhan5-hng5 chhu3-theh8--e5, bo5 m7-beh tit-tioh8 pah- poe7 e5 po3-siuN2.
3
Tng-si5 goa2 e5 sim-keng2, tui3 goa2 thau5-pai2 chhiN2- ngou7 i2-au7, goa2 kam2-kak u7 chit8-chiong2 tek8-piat8 e5 iau chhui3-ta, him-bou7 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 khiam3-sun3, koh ho2 chhin-chhiuN7 Ki-tian5 tui3 Sou3-kat e5 lang5 kong2  “Goa2 chiu7 beh eng7 khong3-ia2 e5 chhi3 kap chhi3-a2 phah-siong lin2 e5 bah.”  Sin5 chiau3-iuN7 ia7 iong7 kok-chiong2 kiong5-liat8 e5 chhi3-giam7 lai5 ka3-si7 in2-chhoa7 goa2.  Pun2- lai5 goa2 chhin-kun7 Sin5 e5 si5 siong5-siong5 si7 kam2-kak chin tiN-bit8 chin bi2-ho2.  Chiah e5 an-ui3 bian2 loa7-ku2 Sin5 long2 sin khi2--lai5, hoan2-tng2 eng7 thang kiaN--e5 lai5 ap-to2 goa2.  Goa2 chheng2-kau3 goa2 e5 peng5-iu2 Cha-li2 Oe7-su-li2, i khou2-khng3 goa2 tioh8 keng2-seng2 kin2-siu2, hou7 goa2 chit8-pun2 chham-kho2-su chiu7-si7 Kheng2-poe5 To-ma2 e5 “Hau7-hoat Ki-tok” (The Imitation of Christ by Thomas a3 Kempis).  Chit-chiong2 kiaN-hiaN5 e5 tang7-taN3 tiok8-chiam7 cheng-ka, hou7 goa2 sit khi3 sou2 u7 bek8-siong2 e5 leng5-lek8, sim7-chi3 su-siuN2 e5 leng5-lek8 tou kam2-kak ti5-tun7 khi3.  Chi2-u7 Sin5 Siong7-te3 chai-iaN2, u7 che7-che7 pai2 goa2 tng5-tng5 mi5 to2 ti7 bin5-chhng5-teng2, ui7-tioh8 sou2 kam2-kak e5 tang7-taN3, haiN-haiN-chhan bo5 soah.   Kui-jit8, kui le2-pai3, goa2 phak-loh8 ti7 te7-bin7, kiu5 Siong7- te3 hou7 goa2 chu7-iu5, chin2-kiu3 goa2 thoat-li7 tui3 te7-gek8-nih8 chhut--lai5 hit-khoan2 kiau-ngou7 e5 su-siuN2, hiah e5  su-siuN2 siong5-siong5 kheh-jip8-lai5 goa2 e5 thau5-nau2-nih8, lai5 hun-loan7 goa2 e5 sim-su7 (心緒).  Hou7 goa2 seN-khi2 chu7-ai, chiau3 ka-ki7 e5 i3-su3, kiau-ngou7, chit-tou3, tit8-tit8 lun5-liu5 lai5 kiau2-jiau2 goa2, chong2-si7 goa2 koat-sim beh iaN5 koe3 chiah e5.  Che-iuN7 keng-koe3 liau2 kui2-na7 goeh8-jit8, goa2 iu-goan5 hoat-hian7 kiau-ngou7 ia7-si7 chiau3- ku3 chham-jip8 ti7 goa2 e5 su-siuN2, chha-put-to it-chhe3 e5 gian5-gu2 tong7-chok i to long2-chong2 u7 ti7 hit lai7-bin7.  Ti7 chit-khoan2 sim bo5 chheng bo5 hoaN-hi2 e5 e7-bin7, u7 chit8-jit8 thak8-tioh8 Koa--si7 tu3 e5 “Siok8-leng5 e5 chian3- cheng” (Custauza’s Spiritual  Combat) lai7-bin7 e5 chit8-toaN7 oe7 kong2: “Hoan7-na7 beh ti7-si2 (治死) ka- ki7 e5 i3-chi3 e5 lang5, hit e5 khun3-lan5 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 beh hou7 In3-ti7-an lang5 hoe2-kai2 e5 oh”.  Tui3 an2-ni goa2 chiu7 ka-ki7 koaiN tiam3 thak8-chheh-pang5-lai7, koat-i3 ka-ki7 koaiN tiam3 hia, tit8-kau3 goa2 e7 ti7 hoan7-su7 chi2-u7 eng7 Siong7-te3 e5 eng5-iau7 choe3 bok8-tek8.  Hian7-chai7 sat-tan chng-choe3 kng-beng5 e5 thiN-sai3 lai5--lah.  I chu2-iau3 e5 hong-hiong3 si7 beh chhoa7 goa2 jip8 ti7 chit8-chiong2 siok-cheng7 e5 kong-keng2, Mui2-mui2 tng Seng3-leng5 ho2 e5 su- siuN2 a2-si7 sin3-liam7 khng3 ti7 goa2 sim-tiong e5 si5, sat-tan3 siong5-siong5 lai5 chiong hiah e5 ka7 i sak-kau3 kek8-toan.  Pi2-pheng7 kong2 goa2 ti7 jit8-ki3 ai3 ki3 goa2 u7 eng7 goa7-choe7 chiN5, sat-tan3 chiu7 lai5 chhi3-tham3 goa2, ai3 goa2 m7-thang ki3 jit8-ki3.  Koa--si7 khou2-khng3 goa2 khng3 goa2 khah mai3 kong2-oe7, sat-tan3 chiu7 kong2 goa2 eng3-kai tioh8 oan5-choan5 mai3 kong2 oe7.  Goa2 pun2-lai5 choe3 jiat8- sim khoan3-bian2 tang5-phoaN7, hian7-chai7 keng3-jian5 thong-mi5 che7 ti7 hia, ia7 bo5 kong2 poaN3-ku3 oe7.  Koa-- si7 kong2 tioh8 chhi3 eng7 an-cheng7 e5 hoan2-seng2 lai5 theng3-hau7 Siong7-te3 e5 si5, sat-tan3 chiu7 koh lai5 ka7 goa2 kong2, tek-khak tioh8 hong3-khi3 it-chhe3 e5 heng5-sek, sim7-chi3 m7-thang khui siaN ki5-to2.  Na7-si7 mui2 pai2 tng su7-cheng5 ian2-pian3 kau3 kek8-toan e5 si5, Siong7-te3 long2 u7 choing goa2 e5 chho3-gou7 chi2-beng5 hou7 goa2 khoaN3.   Koh choing I e5 leng5 chi2-si7 in2-chhoa7 goa2 chit8-tiau5 to5-pi7 e5 lou7.
Goa2 chha-put-to u7 6 le2-pai3 e5 ku2, ka-ki7 koaiN ti7 thak8-chheh-pang5-lai7.  Hian7-chai7 koh tit-tioh8 am3-si7, beh sit8-heng5 koh-khah giam5-keh e5 che3-iok8 (制慾).   Goa2 mui2 le2-pai3 kim3-chiah8 nng7-pai2, goa2 e5 chheng7- chhah si7 chin phok-sou3.  Goa2 siuN7 chit8 e5 chham3-hoe2 e5 lang5 thau5-mng5 m7 eng3-kai boah-iu5.  Goa2 koa3 iuN5- mng5 e5 chhiu2-tho3, chheng7 u7 pou2 e5 saN, chheng7 am-cham e5 phoe5-e5.  Chu7-cha2 goa2 chiu7 chai chap8-hun beng5-pek8 Siong7-te3 e5 kok, put-chai7 lim-chiah8 na7- si7 goa2 kian-koat beh sit8-heng5 chiah e5 su7, chu7-goan7 pang3-sak ka-ki7, in-ui7 goa2 siuN7 choe3 chiah-e5 e7 pang-chan7 goa2 chin3-pou7 ti7 siok8-leng5 e5 oah8-mia7.  Kau3 chit-si5 goa2 i2-keng pang3-sak goa2 e5 it-chhe3, koh bo5 u7 sim2-mih8 thang pang3-sak lah.  Tu5 hui hong3-khi3 kong-tong5 e5 chu7-hoe7, kap li7-khui khi3-sak keng3-khian5 e5 peng5-iu2.  Goa2 i2-keng bo5 ki3 jit8-ki3 lah, bo5 chhut-siaN ki5-to2 lah, ia7 m7 tham3 peN7-lang5 kap siu5- hoan7 lah.  Hian7-chai7 koh lai5 chit8 e5 am3-si7, ai3 goa2 ui7- tioh8 Ki-tok e5 ian5-kou3 li7-khui goa2 e5 keng3-khian5 e5 peng5-iu2.  Ho2, goa2 ia7 koat-i3 beh hoan2-poe7 khi3-sak in.  Keh-jit8 goa2 m7-khi3 chham-ka cheng3 hiaN-ti7 pai3- saN e5 kim3-chiah8, hoan2-tng2 chhut-khi3 kau3 ia2-goa7, ka-ki7 chit8 e5 bek8-to2.  Goa2 ia7 m7 hu3 am3-si5 e5 chu7-hoe7. Goan2 to3-lai5 kau3 pou3-piN5, goa2 siang-peng5 e5 te7-a2-lai7 te2 moa2 hiah e5 siu7 kam2-tong7 ui7-tioh8 ka-ki7 e5 leng5-hun5 tioh8-kip e5 lang5 sou2 sia2 e5 choa2-toaN-a2, goa2 ti7 kui2-na7-chheng lang5 hoaN-hi2 au5-kio3 o-lo2 Chu2 e5 tiong-kan sui5-tiuN sui5-tiuN thak8 hou7 tai7-ke thiaN.  Keh-jit8 goa2 siu7 chhiaN2 kau3 ti7 Ma2-li7-lai5-pho e5 toa7 chhau2-pou (Marylebone Fields), ti7 hit si7 phah kun5-thau5, kap keng3-ki e5 lang5 sou2 chu7-chip e5 sou2-chai7.   Goa2 khoaN3-kiN3 u7 che7-che7 lang5 chu7-chip8, chiu7 eng7  “Goa2 bo5 liah8 hok-im choe3 kian3-siau3, che hok-im pun2 si7 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 koan5-leng5, beh kiu3 it-chhe3 siong-sin3 e5 lang5” (Lo5-ma2 1:16).
Goa2 sou2 khia7 e5 kang2-tai5 si7 chap8-hun bo5 chai7, mui2-pai2 kiaN5 tin2-tang7 e5 si5, io5-io5 pai2-pai2 lok8-lok8-hian2 beh peng2-koe3 e5 khoan2.  Tui3-tek8 khoaN3-chhut chit e5 gui5-hiam2 chiu7 thiau-ti5 e-kheh kin7 kang2-tai5 e5 lang5, kai-chai3 goa2 long2 bo5 siu7 hai7.  Tng goa2 li7-khui kang2-tai5, kiaN5 ng3 be2-chhia e5 si5, kak-tit au7-bin7 u7 lang5 eng7 sim2-mih8 lai5 khap8-tioh8 goa2 e5 bo7-a2 kap thau5-mng5 e5 khoan2-sit, goa2 kin2 oat8-thau5 koe3-lai5 khoaN3, chiah chai tui3-bin7 u7 chit8 e5 lang5 chhiu2 giah8 kiam3, i e5 kiam3-boe2 tu2-tu2 tui3 goa2 e5 pin3-piN chhak8-- lai5.  Kai-chai3 u7 pat8-lang5 khoaN3-kiN3, eng7 koaiN2-a2 phah  ka-lauh8 hit ki kiam3.  Kun5-chiong3 khoaN3 hit e5 khoan2-sit toa7 siu7-khi3, chiu7 kui-kun5 khi2-lai5 kong-kek hit e5 giah8-kiam3 e5 lang5, siat-su2 na7 m7-si7 goa2 e5 peng5- iu2 kiu5-cheng5, hit e5 lang5 khiong2-kiaN e7 siu7 cheng3- lang5 phah chin thiam2.
Goa2 chit8 sut-a2 to bo5 lun2-neh sit-taN2, keh-jit8 koh khi3 hit e5 goan5-ui7.  Tui3-tek8 koh lai5 soan-jiong2, khiong2-hek, na7-si7 long2 bo5 kong-hau7.  U7 chit8 e5 lang5 pi2 pat8-lang5 koh-khah hiong5-ok, peh-chiuN7 oa2-kin7 kang2-tai5 e5 chhiu7-a2, choe3-chhut phaiN2-khoaN3 e5 heng5- hai5.  Khi2-thau5 goa2 bo5 tiuN-ti5 tioh8 chit8-kiaN, goa2 siuN7 sat-tan3 hian7-chai7 bi7-bian2 thai3-koe3-thau5 lah.  Kau3 goa2 sim an-tiaN7 e5 si5, goa2 chiu7 khou2-khng3 cheng3-lang5 kong2, lin2 ki3-jian5 chhin-bak khoaN3-kiN3 chit-khoan2 e5 cheng5-heng5, chiu7 e7 chu7-toan3 goa2 tui3 ti7 lang5 e5 phoaN3- toan3 hit e5 oe7 si7 chho3-gou7 a2 m7-si7.  Chiau3-tioh8 keng3- khian5 e5 Hok-ni2 Chu2-kau3 (Bishop Hall) e5 oe7 kong2,  “Chhut-chai7 lang5 ka-ki7 e5 si5, lang5 si7 poaN3 kui2 poaN3 khim5-siu3,” tui3 an2-ni choan5-tiuN5 tiong5-sin siok-cheng7.   Goa2 jiat8-seng5 khoan3-bian2 in, jian5-au7 kap goa2 e5 tang5-phoaN7 to3-lai5 pou3-piN5.  Goan2 chiu7 ti7 hia lai5 long2 thak8 sia2 hou7 goa2 e5 choa2-toaN, lai5 kiat-sok chit e5 cheh-jit8 e5 kang-chok, kui2-na7 chheng lang5 chu7-hoe7 o-lo2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.
Goa2 ti7 chia beh tek8-piat the5-khi2 chit8-hang7 kek8 chhu3-bi7 e5 su7.  Chiu7-si7 tng-ti7 goa2 teh kang2-ian2 e5 si5, tak8-pai2 u7 chin-che7 gin2-na2 hoaN-hi2 ui5-ui5 lai5 che7 ti7 tai5-a2 e5 kia7-a2-teng2, pang-chan7 theh8 theng3-chiong3 e5 choa2-tiau5-a2.  In tng ti7 kiau2-jiau5 hoah-hiam3 e5 tiong-kan in chiong5-lai5 long2 m7-bat piau2-si7 kiaN-hiaN5 beh chau2.  Mui2-pai2 goa2 siu7 phah e5 si5, in long2 giah8-khi2 lau5 bak8-sai2 se3-se3 e5 bak8-chiu khoaN3 goa2, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 in ng3-bang7 e7-tit thoe3 goa2 hou7 lang5 phah.  Chit-pai2 e5 pou3-to7 tit-tioh8 350 goa7 lang5 hoan2-hoe2.
9
Chu2-au7 1769 ni5 goa2 te7 13 pai2 koe3-iuN5 khi3 Bi2- kok, kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7 kang2-to7, moa2-moa2 tit-tioh8 Siong7-te3 chiok-hok.  Keh-ni5 ng3 tang-peng5 pou3-to7, lou7 keng-koe3 Ai3-sai3-tek (Exeter), tit-tioh8 tong-te7 e5 lang5 kian-i3 e5 pheng3-chhiaN2, sui5-si5 ti7 lou7-thian khai-hoe7.  U7 lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 goa2 ia3-sian7 e5 cheng5-heng5, khou2- khng3 goa2 hioh-khun3, m7-thang koh kong2 to7-li2.  Goa2 in3 kong2: “Sit8-chai7” oat8-tng2-sin, goa2 siang-chhiu2 hap8-chiuN2 kiong2-chhiu2 giah8-bak8 ng3 thiN kong2: “Chu2 Ia5-sou ah!  Goa2 in-ui7 Li2 e5 kang-chok lai5 ia3-sian7, chong2-si7 peng7 m7-si7 pin5-toaN7 ti7 li2 e5 kang.   Siat-su2 goa2 e5 lou7 iau2-boe7 chau2 oan5, kiu5 Li2 hou7 goa2 koh chit8- pai2 ti7 ia2-goa7 ui7-tioh8 Li2 kong2-oe7, kian3-cheng3 li2 e5 chin-li2, ji5-au7 to3-khi3 thiN-nih.”  Jian5-au7 goa2 tai7-iok kong2 nng7-tiam2 cheng, kam2-kak hui-siong5 ia3-sian7.  Che-si7 goa2 choe3-au7 e5 kang2-to7.
10
Hoai5-tek-hui sian-siN si7 kin7-tai7 siang7 tai7-seng chhai2-eng7 ia2-goa7 thoan5-to7 e5 lang5, i eng7 lou7-thian pou3-to7 lai5 kiat-sok i e5 kang-chok, sit8-chai7 si7 chin tu2-ho2.  Chhin-bak8 khoaN3-tioh8 i hit-jit8 e5 cheng5-heng5 e5 lang5 kian3-cheng3 kong2: “I tui3 cho7-sek khi2-lai5 khia7 e5 si5.  I e5 bin7-mau7 i2-keng si7 chit8-phiN kek8 u7 leng5-lek8 e5 to7-li2.  I e5 bin7-mau7 chiau-chui7, bin7-sek chheN-peh8, hian2-si7 thiN-teng2 e5 kng ti7 i soe-lam2 e5 sin-the2-nih8 hoat-chhut.  Chiah e5 kam2-tong7 lang5 kek8-chhim.  Sim-leng5 sui-jian5 goan7-i3, na7-si7 jiok8-the2 i2-keng tong3-be7-tiau5.  I khia7 khi2-lai5 tiam7-tiam7 kui2-na7 hun-cheng ku2 bo5 kong2-oe7.  Au7-lai5 chiah khui-chhui3 kong2: “Goa2 chiaN3-chiaN3 si7 theng3-hau7 Siong7-te3 e5 si-un pang-chan7, in-ui7 goa2 chhim-chai I beh koh chit8-pai2 hu5- chhi5 goa2, hou7 goa2 e7 hong7 I e5 mia5 kong2-oe7.”  I ko2-jian5 thoan5-chhut chit8-phiN kek8-ho2 e5 siau-sit (soat- kau3).  Hit e5 soa3-boe2 tai7-khai3 si7 kong2 an2-ni: “Goa2  (chi2 Hoai5-tek-hui pun2-sin) beh khi3 lah, beh khi3 hiong2-siu7 chit8 ui7 i2-keng u5-pi7 liau2 ho2 e5 an-hioh; Goa2 e5 jit8-thau5 i2-keng chiuN7-khi2--lai5, tui3 thiN phou2-chio3 che7-che7 lang5, hian7-chai7 khoai3 beh loh8 lah,....m7-si7, eng2-oan2 be7-e7 an2-ni, si7 tian-to3 khoai3 beh seng-koan5 kau3 be7 hui2-hoai7 eng5-iau7 e5 kek8-koan5.  Goa2 pi2 che7-che7 lang5 ti7 te7-chiuN7 oah8 khah ku2,......Goa2 e5 jiok8-the2  sui-jian5 soe-lam2, na7-si7 goa2 e5 leng5 khiok si7 iong2- chong3.  Goa2 ho5-teng2 goan7-i3 eng2-oan2 oah8 teh lai5 thoan5-iong5 Ki-tok, tan3-si7 goa2 khi3, si7 kap I tong5- chai7.  Goa2 e5 it-seng si7 ho5-teng2 e5 te2 koh chiam7-si5, kap pai5 ti7 goa2 bak8-cheng5 toa7 ji5-chhiaN2 boe7 oan5- seng5 kang-chok pi2-kau3 khi2-lai5, chin-si7 toan2-chhiok, na7-si7 goa2 it-tan3 li7-khui se3-kan, peng5-an e5 Siong7-te3 tek-khak beh koan3-kou3 lin2, in-ui7 kin-a2-jit8 koan-sim siok8 thiN-teng2 e5 su7 e5 lang5 si7 ho5-teng2 e5 hi-han2.”
Ti7 hit-am3 toa7-khui3 chhoan2 e5 peN7 koh hoat, hou-khip kan-lan5.  Keh cha2-khi2 6 tiam2 chiu7 li7-khui se3-kan to3-khi3 thiN-nih8.  Si7 ti7 Chu2-au7 1770 ni5 9 geh8 30 jit8, hiong2-siu7 56 hoe3.

11
Hoai5-tek-hui si7 tui3 Su3-tou5 i2-au7 kau3-taN lan5 tit u7 e5 toa7 thoan5-to7-chia2.  Kham-tit chheng choe3 “chit8- ki jiat8-liat8 chhan3-lan7 e5 hoe2-pe2” ti7 18 se3-ki2 e5 Eng- kok siok8-leng5 e5 su7 chin-ke7 chin tui7-loh8.  Na7-si7 kin7- kin7 ti7 i choe3-kang e5 30 goa7 ni5 ku2, chiu7 chiong kok lai7 siok8-leng5 e5 cheng5-heng5 toa7 pian3, heng-khi2 toa7- kng lai5 pian3-chio3 chit e5 ou-am e5 si5-tai7, ban2-kiu3 liau2 Eng-kok e5 un7-mia7.  I piaN3-mia7 choe3-kang, m7-kaN2 chit8-bak8 nih ku2 lai5 be7-ki3-tit Siong7-te3 kau-tai3 seng3 e5 chit.  I ti7 kui2 le2-pai3 ku2 e5 sou2 choe3, chiu7 pi2 pat8- lang5 chit8-ni5 e5 sou2-choe3 khah che7 30 goa7 ni5 ku2 m7-kaN2 chit8-jit8 pin5-toaN7.  Che7-che7 ni5 ku2 siong5-siong5 mui2 le2-pai3 hiong3 kui2-na7-chheng e5 theng3-chiong3 lai5 kong2 to7-li2 tui3 40 tiam2-cheng kau3 60 tiam2-cheng e5 ku2.  Kang-chok au7 bo5 hioh-khun3, ti7 chhu3-nih8 ke3- siok8 ki5-to2, thoe3 lang5 kiu5, a2-si7 gim5-si thak8-keng, che7-che7 lang5 tui3 i lai5 peh8-peh8 tit-tioh8 hok-im.  Bok8- koai3 lang5 o-lo2 i kong2 “Chai5-tek e5 lam5-chu2 che7-che7, na7-si7 tok8-tok8 li2 iaN5-koe3 it-chhe3.”  TaN i leng5-lek8 e5 pi3-koat chai7 ti7 to2-ui7?  Chiu7-si7 chai7 ti7 jiat8-ai3 leng5- hun5.  Tng i ng3 choe7-jin5 kong2-oe7 e5 si5, i siong5-siong5 lau5 bak8-sai2.  I e5 thi5-khau3 kam2-tong7 lang5 chin chhim, chio2-chio2 lang5 hoan2-khong3.  I kong2 “Lin2 chek-pi7 goa2 siong5-siong5 khau3, na7-si7 goa2 chaiN2-iuN7 e7 tit kim3- chi2 bo5 khau3 nih8?  Lin2 be7 si2 e5 leng5-hun5 i2-keng ti7 hui2-biat8 e5 lip8-kiN5, na7-si7 lin2 ka-ki7 m7-chai thang ai-chham2 thi5-khau3.  Lin2 tioh8 chai lin2 kho2-leng5 chiu7- si7 thiaN choe3-au7 chit8-phiN e5 to7-li2, i2-au7 koh bo5 u7 ki- hoe7 thang chiap-siu7 Ki-tok lah!”
Bi2-i2-bi2 hoe7 (Sun5-li2-hoe7) e5 Oe7-su-li2 Iok-han7 kian3-cheng3 kong2 “Sou2 su3 e5 Seng3-leng5 chiong Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3 koan3-ak ti7 i e5 sim, hou7 i tui3 lang5 moa2-moa2 u7 un-jiu5 ho5-peng5 bo5 phian-su e5 thiaN3.  Tui3 chit e5 chheng3-chhut oang-oang lau5 e5 khau2-chai5, lau5 kau3 theng3-chiong3 e5 sim-pang5, ti3-kau3 hou7 tai7-ke toa7 siu7 kam2-tong7, hou7 ngi7-sim e5 choe7-jin5 oh-tit ti2-khong3.  Chit e5 thiaN3-sim si5-siong5 hou7 i e5 thau5-khak chhin-chhiuN7 chui2-ti5 bak8-chiu na2-chun2 bak8-sai2 e5 chui2-choaN5.”
