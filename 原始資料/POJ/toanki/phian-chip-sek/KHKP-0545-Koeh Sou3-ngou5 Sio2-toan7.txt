Koeh Sou3-ngou5 Sio2-toan7
Koeh Sou3-ngou5 chiu7-si7 Chhau2-tun kui3 Tong5-hoe7 Koeh Tiau-seng5 Bok8-su e5 toa7 Leng7-ai3, kin-ni5 19 hoe3.  SaN goeh8-boe2 ti7 lan2 Tai5-lam5 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 Lu2 Hak8-hau7 chut-giap8, to3-khi3 chhu3-nih8 sun7-hau3 i e5 pe7-bo2, iu2 ai3 i e5 sio2-ti7 sio2-moe7 chi2-u7 3 goeh8-jit8 ku2 na7-tiaN7.  Tui3 phoa3-peN7 kau3 lim5-chiong thau5-boe2 11 jit8, ti7 ki5-to2 tiong kui thian.
Kin e7-tau3 si7 le2-pai3-jit8, hak8-hau7 chiap-tioh8 chit8 tiuN toan-su, po3 Koeh Sou3-ngou5 ti7 sin-lek8 17 ho7 koe3-ong2, long2 iau2-boe7-bat thiaN-kiN3 u7 phoa3-peN7, hut-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, be7 bian2-tit chin gong5-ngiah8 koh chin tong5-cheng5 i e5 pe7-bo2, saN-kap siong-sim lau5 bak8-sai2 ah!  Peng5-si5 koh si7 sin-the2 kian7-khong, u7 chheng-lian5 e5 khi3-khai3, chhin-chhiuN7 chho2-bok8 tng bou7-seng7 teh-beh puh sin iN2 e5 khi3-siong7 chit8 iuN7, bo5 phah-sng3 e5 tiong-kan thiaN-kiN3 chit ho7 siau-sit, be7 kim3-tit giah8 pit sia2 tam7-poh8 lai5-lek8 hou7 liat8-ui7 peng5-iu2 chai, u7 thang hou7 lan2 siuN7 chhut sin chin3-heng5 e5 heng5-ui5, sin3-giong2, li2-siong2 ia ku2 kaN2.

Kau3-hoe7 si7 khiam3 chit8 e5 gau5 pang-chan7 e5 jin5-but8. Sia7-hoe7 bo5 chhai2 chit8 e5 Lu2 Eng-hiong5, thiaN-kiN3 e5 lang5 bo5 m7 kio3 bo5-chhai2.  Siong-sim ah!
