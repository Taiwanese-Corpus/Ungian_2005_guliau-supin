IuN5 Lau7-seng Sio2-toan7
IuN5 Lau7-seng(lang5 khah siong5 kio3 i Gong7-hiaN), siok8 Chioh8-hou7-soaN3 e5 kau3-hoe7; ti7 1881 ni5, chiu7-si7 Bok8-su thau5 chit8-pai2 khi3 Au7-soaN siat kau3-hoe7, hit-tiap8 i siu7 se2-le2, ia7 u7 tam-tng Chip-su7 kap TiuN2-lo2 e5 chit.  
Au7-lai5 kau3-hoe7 chin leng2-tam7, chin chio2 lang5 lai5 le2-pai3, chi2-u7 i chit8 lang5 bat peh8-oe7-ji7 thang thak8 Si kap Seng3-chheh hou7 lang5 thiaN.  Chong2-si7 i chhut lat8 kou2-bu2, chiau3 i sou2 e7 lai5 kong2 hou7 lang5 thiaN, ia7 ka3 lang5 bat-ji7; kau3-hoe7 lioh8-lioh8-a2 khah heng, khah toa7-bin7 si7 tui3 i-e5 chin7-tiong.  Iu7-koh Bok8-su a2-si7 Kou-niu5 na7 koe3-khi3, i gau5 chih-chiap in; u7-si5 koe3-soaN kau3 lai7 peN5-pou e5 sou2-chai7, chhoa7 in kau3 pai3-tng5.
Ku7-ni5 koe3-ong2, hit-si5 m7-si7 sim2-mih8 phoa3-peN7, kan-ta in-ui7 i-e5 kiaN2 koe3-sin, tui3 an2-ni hoan5-lo2 kau3 koe3-thau5, goan5-khi3 chiam7-chiam7 sit.  Chiu7 tai3 hit-e5 sin-the2 bo5 lat8 khun3-chhng5.  Ti7 khah toa7 kan-khou2 e5 si5, i-e5 sim toa7 peng5-an, put-si5 kiu5 Chu2 koaN2-kin2 chhoa7 i tng2--khi3.  I-e5 chhui3 ti7 khun3-bin5 e5 si5 khah-siong5 si7 ki5-to2 e5 oe7.
Kau3 au7-lai5 u7 chit8 me5 chit8 jit8 siong5-siong5 thou2 toa7 khui3, ka7 lang5 kong2 i put-chi2 khoa3-lu7.  Mng7 i khoa3-lu7 sim2-mih8, i kong2, ¡§goa2 hoan5-lo2 iau2-boe7 u7 pin5-ku3 thang hou7 Chu2 chiap-lap8.¡¨
Kau3 u7 chit8 cha2-khi2 chiu7 hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7 kou3-tit, kong2 i i2-keng u7 pin5-ku3 thang hou7 Chu2 chipa-lap8 lah! Sim peng5-an lah! Mng7 sim2-mih8 pin5-ku3?  Chiu7 liam7 i sou2 siuN7-tioh8 e5 Seng3-chheh chat hou7 lang5 thiaN; chiu7-si7 liam7 ti7 Lo5-ma2 4:7,8. Lang5 u7 choe7 tit-tioh8 sia3, u7 m7-tioh8 hou7 Siong7-te3 jia-kham3, u7 hok-khi3; Chu2 bo5 liah8 i si7 u7 choe7 e5, u7 hok-khi3.  Chiu7 chheng sim hoaN-hi2 kau3 koe3-sin. 
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 359 koan3,1915 ni5 2 geh8)
