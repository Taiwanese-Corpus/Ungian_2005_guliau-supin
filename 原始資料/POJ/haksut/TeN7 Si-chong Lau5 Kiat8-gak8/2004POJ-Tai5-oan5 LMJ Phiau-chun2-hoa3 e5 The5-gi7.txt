Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3 chong2-pian-chip, Tai5-bun5 BONGPO chong2-pian-chip
Gi2-bun5 e5 phiau-chun2-hoa3 (PCH) u7 chu7-jian5 ian2-pian3 heng5-seng5 e5 gi2-gian5-tek e5 PCH, u7 kui-hoan7-seng3 gi2-hoat-ka kap gi2-gian5 sun5-chiaN3-lun7-chia2 sou2 kui-hoan7 e5 i3-sek heng5-thai3 e5 PCH, kap beh po2-chun5, ban2-kiu3, hok8-tin3 gi2-gian5 sou2 che3-teng7 e5 gi2-gian5 ke3-ek8 e5 PCH.
Tai5-oan5 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 soan-kau3-su che3-teng7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 (POJ), pian-sia2 su5-tian2, chhui-sak POJ kau3-iok8, chhut-pan2 keng-tian2, hoat-heng5 po3-choa2, lui2-chek che7-che7 e5 bun5-hian3 chu-liau7, i2-keng kian3-lip8 Tai5-oan5-ji7 (TOJ, POJ e5 chiaN3-mia5) chiaN3-su5-hoat (orthography) e5 thoan5-thong2. Sui-bong2 keng-koe3 Tiong-kok Kok-bin5-tong2 cheng3-khoan5 thun2-tah8 e5 ou-am3-ki5, chong2-si7 ti7 1987-ni5 i2-au7 TOJ tit-tioh8 jin7-tong5 lai5 phong5-phai3 hoat-tian2.
Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3 si7 Ko-hiong5 Lo2-ma2-ji7 gian2-sip8-hoe7 ti7 2000-ni5 sou2 chhut-khan e5 TOJ Peh8-oe7-bun5 chap8-chi3. Goan2 teh pian-chip Tai5-oan5-ji7 e5 si5-chun7 sou2 tu2--tioh8 e5 siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 bun7-te5 si7 kang7 chit8-e5 su5 bo5 kang7 hoat-im. Ti7 chit phiN lun7-bun5 u7 cheng2-li2 goan2 soan2-tek phiau-chun2 pian3-the2-sek e5 goan5-chek hou7 tai7-ke chham-kho2.
ChiaN3-su5-hoat si7 iok-teng7 siok8-seng5--e5, m7-si7 chit8 si5 chit8 khek e7-sai2 oan5-seng5--e5. Lan2 ai3 kin2-kin2 gian2-kiu3, che3-teng7 phiau-chun2-hoa3 e5 Tai5-gi2-bun5 lai5 chiaN5-cho3 chhiu-sak hok8-tin3 Tai5-gi2 e5 ki-chhou2.
Koan-kian3-su5 : Tai5-gi2 Lo5-ma2-ji7, phiau-chun2-hoa3, Peh8-oe7-ji7, Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3, chiaN3-su5-hoat

1. Phiau-chun2-hoa3
Assembly of First Nations  (Te7-it Bin5-chok Gi7-hoe7, si7 Kanat2ai to-sou3 goan5-chu7-bin5 bin5-chok gi7-hoe7 sou2 chou-chit) ti7 in 1990-ni5 e5 po3-ko3 "Towards Linguistic Justice for First Nation (Mai7 hiong3 gi2-gian5 cheng3-gi7)" lai7-bin7 u7 kian3-gi7:
"establishing standards for the written and oral languages by approving terminology, developing a dictionary, and approving standard orthographies"1
(Beh kian3-lip8 su-bin7-gi2 kap khau2-gi2 phiau-chun2 tioh8-ai3 che3-teng7 sut8-gi2-hak8, khai-hoat su5-tian2, kap che3-teng7 phiau-chun2 e5 bun5-gi7 pheng-sia2 chiaN3-su5-hoat.)
Chau2-chhoe7, hoat-tian2 kap che3-teng7 su-bin7-gi2 e5 phiau-chun2 si7 Tai5-gi2 gi2-bun5 ke3-ek8(language planning) e5 ki-chhou2 su7-kang, si7 gi2-bun5 hian7-tai7-hoa3, kho-hak8-hoa3, kap chu-sin3-hoa3 e5 kin-ku3, tui3 gi2-gian5 hok8-tin3 (language revival) ia7-si7 tiong7-iau3 e5 khang-khoe3.
2. Pian3-iN7 kap phiau-chun2
Gi2-gian5 e5 pian3-iN7 (variations) si7 chit8-e5 gi2-gian5 chiap8-chiap8 hoat-seng, chu7-jian5 ian2-pian3 e5 hian7-siong7. Gi2-gian5 tiong e5 pian3-iN7 e7-sai2 hun-cho3 3 chiong2. Mui2 chit8-e5 lang5 kong2-oe7 tek8-piat8 koan3-si3 su2-iong7 e5 "ko3-jin5-gian5" (idiolect). Lan2 long2 e7-tang3 chai-iaN2 kong2-oe7 e5 lang5 si7 teh hoat-piau2 cheng3-sek e5 ian2-kang2 a2-si7 ti7 bio7-khau2 teh kap lang5 oe7 sian pho3-tau7, si7 hoaN-hi2 kam-goan7 a2-si7 be7-song2 pi-siong. Leng7-goa7 chit8 khoan2  si7 kok hiuN-li2, te7-khu, a2-si7 sia7-hoe7 kun5-the2 su2-iong7--e5, kap pat8 sou2-chai7 e5 gian5-gi2 u7 he7-thong2-seng3 cheng-chha--e5 kio3-cho3 "hong-gian5" (dialect). "KiuN-khau2 (accent)"  thong-siong5 si7 teh kong2 lang5 kong2-oe7 e5 hoat-im, sou2-i2 theh8 lai5 chun2-si7 kok hong-gian5 tiong bo5 siang5 gi2-im a2-si7 im-un7 e5 pian3-the2 (phonetically or phonologically different varieties).2
Phiau-chun2-gi2 si7 chit8-e5 gi2-gian5 tiong tek8-su5 e5 hong-gian5, u7 hoat-teng7 (legal) a2-si7  chun2-hoat-teng7 (quasi-legal) e5 te7-ui7.3
Phiau-chun2-gi2 u7 kui2-na7 e5 tek8-sek.3 Chiau3 e7-te2:
(1) U7 siu7 jin7-teng7 e5 su5-tian2 lai5 ki3-liok8 siu-chip8 phiau-chun2 e5 pheng-sia2-hoat kap su5-lui7.
(2) U7 phiau-chun2 he7-thong2 e5 hoat-im kap gi2-hoat (chiaN3-su5-hoat).
(3) U7 khoan5-ui e5 ki-kou3 lai5 teng7-gi7 kap chhui-sak hit-e5 gi2-gian5, chhin-chhiuN7 Hoat-gi2 u7 "Academie francaise".
(4) U7 hian3-hoat a2-si7 ki5-tha hoat-lut8 che3-teng7 cho3 koaN-hong hoat-teng7 gi2-gian5.
(5) Ti7 kong-kiong7 tou-hap8, chhin-chhiuN7 hoat-iN7 a2-si7 gi7-hoe7 su2-iong7 e5 gi2-gian5.
(6) U7 hong-hu3 e5 bun5-hian3 chu-liau7.
(7) U7 hoan-ek8 se3-kai3 khoan5-ui-tek e5 bun5-pun2, chhin-chhiuN7 Seng3-keng.
(8) Hak8-hau7 u7 ka3 phiau-chun2-gi2 e5 gi2-hoat kap pheng-sia2-hoat.
(9) Theh8 lai5 ka3 goa7-kok-lang5 hak8-sip8 pun2 kok gi2-gian5 e5 phiau-chun2 hong-gian5.
Chit8-e5 gi2-gian5 e5 phiau-chun2 hong-gian5 si7 ui7-tioh8 beh kiat-hap8 jin5-bin5 beh the5-kiong hou7 to-chiong2 hong-gian5 e5 su2-iong7-chia2 kiong7-tong5 e5 su-sia2 heng5-sek, m7-koh i peng7 bo5 pi2 ki5-tha e5 hong-gian5 khah u7 piau2-tat8-seng3 (expressive), khah u7 lochek5 (logical), khah hok8-chap8, a2-si7 khah u7 kui-lut8.4
3. Sim2-mih8 si7 phiau-chun2-hoa3 (PCH)?
3.1 Gi2-gian5-tek e5 PCH (linguistic Standardization)5
Ti7 jin7-ho5 gi2-gian5 hoat-tian2 e5 koe3-theng5, PCH si7 chit8-e5 pit-iau3 kap oah8-tang7 e5 koe3-theng5. Chit8-e5 gi2-gian5 ti7 lek8-su2 tng5 si5-kan e5 su2-iong7 tiong, pian3-iN7 kiam2-chio2, chit8-koa2 tek8-tiam2 (gi2-im kap gi2-hoat teng2) chhu-hiong3 it-ti3 (uniformity). Chit khoan2 chu7-jian5 hoat-seng e5 PCH kio3-cho3 "gi2-gian5-tek e5 PCH". William Haas bat hoat-piau2--koe3, "Standardization is an intrinsic feature of the use of a language; and whenever we set out to describe a "language", we are seeking to extract its standards".6 (PCH si7 gi2-gian5 su2-iong7 e5 si5 e5 loe7-chai7 seng3-chit. Lan2 teh sia2-beng5 ki3-liok8 chit8 chiong2 gi2-gian5, ki5-sit8 tioh8-si7 teh theh8-liah8 i e5 phiau-chun2). Ti7 kang7 chit8-e5 gi2-gian5 sia7-kun5, gi2-gian5 hun-hoa3 (linguistic differentiation) m7-na7 cho7-seng5 kou-thong bo5 li7-pian7 kap kou-thong seng5-pun2 cheng-ka, ia7 u7 kho2-leng5 san2-seng phoa3-lih8: in-ui7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 te7-khu kap sia7-khu kian-chhi5 in e5 gi2-gian5 chha-iN7 e7-thang-tit pai7-hai7 "kok-ka loe7-pou7 chip8-chu7 cho3-hoe2 e5 kiong7-tong5-kam2 (sense of national cohesion)". 6
3.2 Sia7-hoe7 i3-sek heng5-thai3 e5 PCH (ideology of standardization)7
Ti7 Se-hng hian7-tai7-hoa3 kok-ka e5 gi2-gian2 lek8-su2 tiong, koh u7 chit8-khoan2 PCH e5 thoan5-thong2, si7 ki-kou3-hoa3 ke3-ek8 kap kong-khoan5-lek8 kiong5-che3 sit8-heng5--e5, si7 kui-hoan7-seng3 gi2-hak8-ka (prescriptive grammarians) a2-si7 gi2-gian5 sun5-chiaN3-lun7-chia2 (language purists) chu2-tiuN--e5. In liah8 cheng3-ti7 cheng-eng kap sia7-hoe7 siong7-liu5 hu3-ju7 kai-chan3 sou2 kong2--e5, bun5-hak8 siong7 a2-si7 kong-bun5 tiong sou2 iong7--e5, kap hak8-hau7 sou2 ka3--e5, chiaN5-cho3 cheng3-khak e5 gi2-gian54.
3.3 Gi2-gian5 ke3-ek8 hok8-tin3 e5 PCH (standardization for language planning)
Ti7 gi2-gian5 siu7-tioh8 ap-pek a2-si7 kim3-chi2 e5 chok8-kun5, gi2-gian5 e5 hok8-tin3 su-iau3 u7 kong-khoan5-lek8 e5 kai3-jip8, chin3-heng5 gi2-bun5 ke3-ek8 e5 su7-kang, pau-koat soan2-teng7 chit8 khoan2 gi2-gian5 a2-si7 hong-gian5 pian3-the2 chiaN5-cho3 ki-chun2 (selecting the norm), ka7 khau2-gi2 e5 hoat-im, gi2-hoat, kap su5-lui7 phiau-chun2 su-bin7-gi2-hoa3 (codification, chhin-chhiuN7 su5-tian2, phiau-chun2 gi2-hoat kho3-pun2 teng2-teng2), chiong gi2-su5 hian7-tai7-hoa3 (modernization), koh lai5 ka7 sit8-si chhui-heng5 (implementation).8 Ki5-tiong cheng5-3-e5 pou7-sou3 long2 si7 iu2-koan PCH e5 khang-khoe3.
4. Tai5-gi2 e5 gi2-bun5 hian7-tai7-hoa3 (HTH) kap phiau-chun2-hoa3 (Modernization and standardization of Taiwanese)
4.1 Sim2-mih8 si7 gi2-bun5 e5 HTH?
Gi2-gian5 kap bun5-ji7 si7 kun-toe3 sia7-hoe7 kha-pou7 lai5 hoat-tian2--e5. Chit8 chiong2 gi2-bun5 na7 beh ke3-siok8 u7 se3-mia7-lat8 bo5 biat8-choat8 tioh8 ai3 e7-sai2 eng3-hu3 kho-ki si5-tai7 e5 su-iau3, toe3 e7 tioh8 si5-tai7 e5 kha-pou7 teh kai2-chin3.
Kin7-tai7 hoat-tian2-tiong kok-ka gi2-bun5 HTH e5 bok8-phiau si7 che3-teng7 phiau-chun2 bun5-ji7 kap chiaN3-su5-hoat, phou2-kip8 kok-ka kiong7-tong5-gi2, chhui-tian2 bat-ji7 un7-tong7, koh u7 bun5-the2 e5 khau2-gi2-hoa3 (peh8-oe7-bun5 un7-tong7), bun5-ji7 piau2-im ji7-bo2-hoa3, kap gi2-su5 e5 hian7-tai7-hoa3 teng2 tek8-sek9.
20 se3-ki2 boat8 kap 21 se3-ki2 chhe tu2 khai-si2 e5 chu-sin3 kek-beng7,  PC kap internet e5 hoat-beng5 kap phou2-kip8 hou7 gi2-gian5 e5 kau-thong chhiau-koe3 kok-ka e5 kai3-han7. Kok-che3 chu-sin3 e5 liu5-thong su-iau3 chit8-e5 kok-che3 kiong7-thong-gi2 (chhin-chhiuN7 chit-ma2 e5 Eng-gi2) kap chit8 tho3 thong2-it e5 ji7-bo2 hu5-ho7 lai5 su-sia2 se3-kai3 e5 gi2-gian5 (tioh8-si7 bun5-ji7 e5 Lo5-ma2-hoa3).
5. Tai5-oan5 gi2-gian5 HTH/PCH
Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 HTH kap ki5-tha kok-ka e5 gi2-gian5 kang7-khoan2, pun2-te2 chiu7-si7 chit8 tiau5 tng5-lo3-lo3 e5 lou7-theng5. Goa7-lai5 cheng3-khoan5 ti7 chit 100 ni5 tui3 Tai5-gi2 e5 taN2-ap thun2-tah8, hou7 HTH/PCH e5 bun7-te5 koh khah hok8-chap8. Ti7 chit phiN lun7-bun5 lai7-bin7, goan2 i2 Tai5-oan5 siong7 u7 lek8-su2-seng3, phou2-phian3-seng3, kap kok-che3-seng3 e5 Peh8-oe7-ji7 (POJ) cho3 tiong-sim, liah8 POJ su-sia2 kui-hoan7 e5 heng5-seng5, POJ kau3-iok8 e5 chhui-heng5, POJ e5 liu5-thoan5 kap siu7-lan7, POJ thoat-Kau3-hoe7 se3-siok8-hoa3 e5 lek8-su2, chiaN5-cho3 Tai5-gi2-bun5 HTH/PCH siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 goan5-sou3. E7-sai2 kong2 POJ si7 taN2-cho7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 HTH/PCH e5 po2-to lai7-kiam3.
6. Tai5-oan5 gi2-gian5 HTH/PCH e5 lek8-su2.
6.1 Puh-iN2-ki5 (Ho5-lan si5-tai7 kau3 1820-ni5 cho2-iu7) 
Tui3 17 se3-ki2 Ho5-lan soan-kau3-su eng7 Lo5-ma2-ji7 pheng-sia2 Siraya chok8-gi2 sou2 lau5--loh8--lai5 e5 "Ma2-thai3 hok-im-su", kap koh loh8--lai5 150 tang ku2 e5 "hoan-a2-kheh" thoan5-thong2, au7-tai7 hak8-chia2 ka7 chheng-hou cho3 "Sin-kang2 Bun5-su",10 e7-sai2 kong2 si7 Tai5-oan5 pun2-thou2 gi2-gian5 hian7-tai7-hoa3 lek8-su2 e5 "puh-iN2-ki5".
6.2 Seng5-tiong2-ki5 (1865-1951)
TiuN2-lo2 Kau3-hoe7 soan-kau3-su Ma2 Nga2-kok i-su ti7 1865-ni5 tui3 TaN2-kau2 Ki5-au7 (Kin-a2-jit8 e5 Ko-hiong5 Ki5-tin) chiuN7 hoaN7, hian-khui Tai5-oan5 ki-tok-kau3 soan-kau3-su2 e5 tong5-si5, cheng5-au7-tai7 e5 soan-kau3-su long2 cheng-thong Tai5-gi2, ti7 sia7-hoe7 chhui-heng5 Peh8-oe7-ji7 (POJ), ti7 hak8-hau7 thoan5-ka3 POJ, chhut-pan2 Tai5-oan5 te7-1 hun7 po3-choa2 "Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3", pian-sia2 su5-tian2, hoan-ek8 Seng3-keng, che3-cho7 chheN-me5-lang5 eng7 e5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 tiam2-ji7-chheh, tu3-chok Tai5-gi2-bun5 i-hak8 chheh "Lai7-goa7-kho Khan3-hou7-hak8", hou7 che7-che7 e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 thoat-li7 m7-bat-ji7 e5 sok-pak8, e7-sai2 u7 ho2-oh8 ho2-eng7 e5 POJ lai5 thak8 chu sia2 ji7.11 Tong5-si5 POJ e5 goan5-li2: thak8 chheh chiu7-si7 teh oh8 oe7/choh bun5 chiu7-si7 teh sia2 oe7, ka7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 bun5-ji7 kap gi2-gian5 kiat-hap8 cho3 chit8-e5 (e7-sai2 kong2 si7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 Peh8-oe7-bun5 un7-tong7) . Chit-e5 si5-ki5 sui-bong2 kan-ta u7 Ki-tok-kau3 sin3-ngiong2 e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 e7-tit-thang thak8-sia2 Tai5-oan5-ji7, POJ e5 eng2-hiong2-lat8 iau2 han7-tiaN7 ti7 Kau3-hoe7 khou-a2 lai7, chong2-si7 the3 Tai5-gi2-bun5 e5 HTH/PCH, kian3-lip8 liau2 kian-kou3 e5 chioh8-poaN5. Lan2 ka7 chit-e5 si5-ki5 ho7-cho3 "seng5-tiong2-ki5".
6.3 Ou-am3-ki5 (1951-1987) 12,13
1945-ni5 Tiong-kok Kok-bin5-tong2 chiap-siu Tai5-oan5, 1946-ni5 seng5-lip8 Tai5-oan5-seng2 Kok-gi2 Chhui-heng5 Ui2-oan5-hoe7, eng7 kau3-iok8 the2-che3 lai5 chhui-heng5 "Kok-gi2". Ti7 1951-ni5 Kau3-iok8-thiaN beng7-leng7 kok kip8 hak8-hau7  e5 kau3-hak8 giam5 kim3 "hong-gian5". 1957-ni5 kim3-chi2 Kau3-hoe7 su2-iong7 POJ, 1969-ni5 kim3-chi2 Kau3-hoe7-po3 eng7 POJ chhut khan, 1975-ni5 boat8-siu Peh8-oe7-ji7 Tai5-gi2 Seng3-keng, 1976-ni5 thong-koe3 Kong2-po3 Tian7-si7-Hoat han7-tiaN7 Tai5-gi2 si5-kan be7-sai2-tit chhiau-koe3 20%, ji5-chhiaN2 ai3 tak8 ni5 kiam2 chio2. Chit-e5 si5-ki5 m7-na7 Tai5-gi2 gi2-bun5 e5 hian7-tai7-hoa3 bo5 tit-tioh8 pang-chan chu-iong2, sim7-chi3 ui3 iu-se3-gi2 (dominant language)/kiong7-thong-gi2 (Lingua Francas) e5 te7-ui7, kiong5 beh pian3-cho3 pin-lim5 choat8-cheng2 e5 gi2-gian5.14
6.4 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Se3-siok8-ki5 kap hoat-tian2-ki5 (1987-taN)
Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 se3-siok8-hoa3 un7-tong7 (haN7 chhut Kau3-hoe7 khou-a2 goa7) tui3 1920 ni5-tai7 khai-si2, it-tit8 kau3 80 ni5-tai7 chiah u7 tit-tioh8 to-sou3 lang5 e5 tiong7-si7. 1920 ni5-tai7 Chhoa3 Poe5-hoe2 teng2 lang5 ka7 POJ chhui-tian2 kau3 Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7, tong3-cho3 bun5-hoa3 the5-seng e5 lou7-eng7. Chiau3 Ng5 Tian2-seng5 1953-ni5 e5 thong2-ke3 Tai5-oan5 iok-liok8 u7 3 ban7 lang5 teh su2-iong7 POJ. M7-koh siu7-tioh8 ti3-sek hun7-chu2 Han3 bin5-chok8 i3-sek e5 pai5-thiat, peng7 bo5 chin phou2-kip8.9
Ai3 kau3 1987 i2 Han3-Lo5-bun5 ui5 chu2 e5 Tai5-gi2-bun5 un7-tong7, tai7-chiong3 the2-hoe7 tioh8 Lo5-ma2-ji7 e7-sai2 kai2-koat "u7 im bo5 ji7" e5 su-sia2 lan5-te5, chiah tit-tioh8 chin3-pou7, chhong3-chok liong kap chhong3-chok loe7-iong5 chhiau-koe3 chin3-cheng5 chiok che7.9
Leng7-goa7 ti7 Tai5-oan5 gi2-bun5 ke3-ek8 teng2-bin7, ia7 u7 thut8-phoa3-seng3 e5 chin3-tian2. Keng-koe3 Bo2-gi7 un7-tong7 jin5-su7 e5 hou-iok8 kap chau2-chong5, Kau3-iok8-pou7 cheng3-sek ti7 sio2-hak8 kui-teng7 mui2 le2-pai3 u7 1-2 tiam2-cheng e5 bo2-gi2 kho3-theng5, ia7 khai-si2 ti7 the2-che3 lai7 cheng3-sek poe5-hun3 bo2-gi2 su-chu.
Lan2 ka7 chit-e5 si5-ki5 chheng-hou7 cho3  Se3-siok8-ki5 kap Hoat-tian2-ki5.
7. Tai5-oan5 gi2-gian5 HTH/PCH bi7-lai5 e5 bok8-phiau
Tai5-gi2-bun5 HTH ai3 oan5-seng5 e5 su7-kang u7: (1)ai3 eng7 Se-hng chin3-pou7 e5 gi2-gian5-hak8 goan5-li2 lai5 gian2-kiu3 Tai5-gi2 gi2-im, gi2-su5, gi2-hoat. Ti7 koe3-khi3 e5 su-bin7 thoan5-thong2 tiong chhiau5-chhek kiong7-sek, che3-teng7 bun5-ji7 su-sia2 e5 kui-hoan7 phiau-chun2 (ia7 tioh8-si7 chiaN3-su5-hoat), (2)ka7 gi2-lui7 hian7-tai7-hoa3, cheng-bit8-hoa3, kap ti3-sek-hoa3 (intellectualization), (3)ka7 Tai5-gi2-bun5 chu-sin3-hoa3 (Tai5-gi2-bun5 bang7-iah8, tian7-nau2 su5-tian2 kap hoan-ek8-ki, Tai5-gi2 gi2-im pian7-sek he7-thong2 teng2-teng2 e5 gi2-gian5 chu-sin3 kho-ki). Ki5-tiong chiaN3-su5-hoat si7 HTH/PCH e5 ki-chhou2. 
8. Tai5-oan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 (TOJ, ia7 chiu7-si7 Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 chiaN3-mia5) chiaN3-su5-hoat e5 loe7-iong5
8.1 Sim2-mih8 si7 chiaN3-su5-hoat
ChiaN3-su5-hoat si7 bun5-ji7 phiau-chun2-soa3, ia7-si7 bun5-ji7 e5 su-sia2 kui-hoan7. TOJ kap ki5-tha e5 Tai5-gi2 pheng-im im-phiau siong7 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 TOJ u7 chiaN3-su5-hoat.
8.2 TOJ chiaN3-su5-hoat e5 lek8-su2

10. Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3 e5 TOJ pheng-sia2-hoat
Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3 si7 Ko-hiong5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 gian2-sip8-hoe7 ti7 2000-ni5 chhut-khan e5 TOJ peh8-oe7-bun5 ki5-khan, kau3 taN 4-ni5 ku2 e5 si5-kan, u7 chhut-pan2 koe3 17-ki5, mui2 ki5 A-4 16 iah8, long2-chong2 tai7-iok 120,000 e5 su5. Sou2 chhut-pan2 e5 bun5-pun2, i e5 keh-sek, ji7-bo2 pheng-sia2 long2 keng-koe3 3-e5 pian-chip (TeN7 Si-chong, Koa Kha2-le7, TiuN Hok8-chu5) seng2-chhat kap siu-kai2, chiu7 an2-ne lui2-chek chiaN5-cho3 Tai5-oan5-ji7 chap8-chi3 e5 TOJ pheng-sia2-hoat.
