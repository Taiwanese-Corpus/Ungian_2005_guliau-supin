Lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 te7 khoah-toa7 bo5 chhin-chhiuN7 Tiong-kok , In3-tou7 hit khoan2 , m7 iong5-un2 soaN-khim5 ia2-siu3 e5 phau2-chau2 ; chong2-si7 sio2-se3 ia7 chiok-giah8 e7 thang iong5-un2 kui2-na7 pah-ban7 lang5 , iu7-koh te7-thou2 kiam2 m7-si7 chin pui5 , chhut-san2 kiam2 m7-si7 chin hong-hu3 mah ? Lan2 e5 soaN-lai7 u7 toa7-chang5 chhiu7 chho3 be7-liau2 , u7 chiuN-lo2 si7 se3-kai3 sou2 hi-han2 e5 , u7 ng5-kim , ia7 u7 chioh8-thoaN3 . 2. Tui3 te7-li2 lai5 kong2 , oan2-jian5 chit8 e5 sio2 Tang-iuN5 

Se3-kai3 e5 te7-bin7 , hun-koah cho3 saN khoan2 ; chit8- khoan2 kio3-cho3 jiat8-tai3-te7 , chit8-khoan2 un-tai3-te7 , koh chit8-khoan2 chiu7-si7 han5-tai3-te7 . Lan2 lang5 sou2 khia7-khi2 e5 te7-bin7 , khoaN3-kiN3 si7 peN5-peN5 , ki5-sit8 m7-si7 peN5- -e5 , si7 iN5- -e5 . In-ui7 te7 si7 iN5-iN5 chhin-chhiuN7 kiu5 , sou2-i2 lan2 sou2 khia7-khi2 chit-e5 te7 si7 kio3-cho3 te7-kiu5 , lang5 kap chhau2-bak8 chau2-siu3 chiu7-si7 ti7 te7-kiu5 e5 bin7-teng2 teh oah8 . Thang kong2 te7-kiu5 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8-liap8 chin-chiaN3 toa7 e5 hou2-thau5-kam ; kam-phoe5 phong3-koan5 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 -si7 soaN , thap-loh8 e5 sou2-chai7 chek-chui2 chiu7-si7 hai2 ; iah8 poaN3-tou7 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 jiat8-tai3-te7 , poaN3-tou7 kap thau5-boe2 e5 tiong-kan chiu7-si7 un-tai3-te7 , thau5 kap boe2 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7-si7 han5-tai3-te7 . Jiat8-tai3-te7 si7 te7-it joah8 , un-tai3-te7 khah sio-lo7,han5-tai3-te7 chiu7 chin koaN5 .

Chiong te7-kiu5 phoa3 cho3 nng7-peng5 , chit8-peng5 si7 kio3-cho3 Tang-iuN5 , koh chit8-peng5 si7 kio3-cho3 Se-iuN5 . Lan2 Tai5-oan5 si7 ti7 Tang-iuN5 e5 lai7-bin7 . Lan2 e5 Tai5-oan5 iu7 tu2 si7 ti7 jiat8-tai3-te7 kap un-tai3-te7 e5 kau-kai3 . Lan2 to2-lai7 e5 soaN iu7 chin koan5 , sou2-i2 peN5-te7 sui-jian5 si7 joah8 , ju2 chiun7 soaN2-teng2 chiu7 ju2 liang5 ju2 leng2 . Na7-si7 pat8 sou2-chai7 jpah8 chiu7-si7 joah8 , koaN5 chiu7-si7 koaN5 , lan2 Tai5-oan5 in-ui7 si7 teng2-bin7 sou2 kong2 hit khoan2 e5 te7-se3 , sou2-i2 tang5 chit8 e5 si5-chun7 , ai3 joah8 e5 sou2-chai7 iah8 u7 , ai3 un-loan2 e5 sou2-chai7 ia7 u7 , ai3 liang5-leng2 e5 sou2-chai7 ia7 si7 u7 .

Chiong-lai5 soaN-lai7 hiah e5 lang5 , in-ui7 lan2 na7 khah thiaN3-thang3 in , na7 kap lan2 ho5-ho2 bo5 saN-hai7 , iah8 lan2 e5 ti3-sek chai5-leng5 khah hoat-tat8 , khui3-lat8 khah chhiong-chiok e5 si5 , soaN-lai7 soaN-teng2 it-chhe3 long2 khai-khun2 , toa7 chhiu7-na7 tioh8 ka7 i khui , kim-gin5 chioh8-thoaN3 chiong2-chiong2 e5 khong3-san2 na7 u7 , chiu7 ka7 i kut8 ; e7 thang chai-cheng3 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 chai-cheng3 , be7- -e5 chiu7 hou7 i cho3 bok8-tiuN5 chhi7 gu5 , chhi7 iuN5 , chhi7 cheng-seN .  Chit8-bin7 chiong chiah e5 sou2 siu-seng5 e5 li7-ek lai5 khui toa7-lou7 , cho7 hoe2-chhia tian7-chhia , a2-si7 siat  chin un2-tang3 e5 hui-leng5 -ki , hui-heng5-chun5 , kau-thong hou7 i toa7 li-pian7 . Iah8 koh chit8-bin7  , kian3-siat hiong-chhoan chhi7-ke ti7 kek8 ho2 keng2-ti3 , kek8 chheng-liang5 e5 chou2-chai7 . Na7-si7 an2-ni , kiam2 m7-si7 kui2 tiam2-cheng , a2-si7 kui2 hun-cheng e5 tiong-kan ,chiu7 e7 thang ti7 joah8 e5 sou2-chai7 kiaN5 kau3 liang5- -e5 , ti7 liang5- -e5 kiaN5 kau3 koaN5 e5 te7-hng mah ? 

Chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni lai5 kong2 , chai7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 kin7-kin7 chit8 utiap5-a2-ku2 e5 kang-hu , ai3 soaN chiu7 u7 soaN , ai3 hai2 chiu7 u7 hai2 , beh joah8 chiu7 u7 joah8 ,koaN5 chiu7 u7 koaN5 . Sou2-i2 thang kong2 Tai5-oan5 si7 chit8 e5 sio2 Tang-iuN5 . Lan2 Tai5-oan5 u7 chit-khoan2 thian-jian5 e5 ho2-keng2 ,ho2 khi3-hau7 , chiong-lai5 na7-si7 eng7-sim ke lang5 e5 kang-hu toa7-toa7 lai5 cheng2-tun3 , tek-khak e7 chiaN5-cho3 Tang-iuN5 e5 toa7 kong-hng5 , hou7 Tang-iuN5 e5 lang5 chip8-oa2 lai5 hiong2-hok an-lok8 .

3.	Tui3 lang5 lai5 kong2 , si7 chit8  e5  sio2 Se3-kai3 

Tui3 te7-li2 lai5 kong2, lan2 e5 Tai5-oan5 si7 hiah u7 chhu3-bi7 e5 sou2-chai7, chong2-si7 na7 tui3 lang5 e5 hong-bin7 lai5 kong2 , si7 iau2 koh-khah sim-sek , khah u7 i3-gi7 , thang kong2 si7 chit8 e5 sio2 se3-kai3 .

Thong te7-kiu5-bin7 sou2 u7 e5 lang5 , chong2-kiong7 kong2 u7 chap8-lak8-ek goa7 (chit8-ek si7 chit8-ban7 e5 chit8-ban7 ). Chiah e5 lang5 tai7-khai3 hun cho3 gou7-cheng2 , chiu7-si7 peh8-sek- -e5, ng5-sek- -e5, chhiah-sek- -e5, chang-sek- -e5 , ou-sek- -e5 . Se-iuN5-lang5 ( phou2-thong kong2 Ang5-mng5-lang5 ), chiu7-si7 peh8-sek- -e5 , in-ui7 in e5 phoe5-bah e5 sek chin peh8; lan2 chiu7-si7 ng5-sek- -e5 ; Lam5-iuN5 e5 Ma2-lai5-lang5 chiu7-si7 chang-sek- -e5;Bi2-kok kin7 soaN e5 sou2-chai7 u7 cheng2 phoe5-bah chin ang5 e5 lang5 , che chiu7-si7 chhiah-sek lang5 ; iau2 u7 chit8-cheng2 chiu7-si7 i e5 phoe5-bah ou kau3 chhin-chhiuN7 hoe2-thoaN3, tiam3 ti7 A-hui-li7-ka kap A-bi2-li7-ka e5 sou2-chai7.

Chit gou7-cheng2 lang5 bun5-beng5 e5 theng5-tou7 put-chi2 u7 cheng-chha. U7-e5 si7 chin gau5, chin iong2, chin ho2-giah8 ; chong2-si7 u7-e5 si7 chin gong7, chin ham-ban7 , chin san3-hiong. In-ui7 chit- khoan2, tai7-ke  e5 khui3-lat8 bo5 peN5 , ti3-kau3 seN-chhut chiong2-chiong2 put-heng7 e5 tai7-chi3 ; khiau2- -e5 phian3 gong7- -e5, u7- -e5 chiah8 bo5- -e5, kiong5- -e5 ap-to2 jiok8- -e5, Thang kong2 koe3-khi3 e5 se3-tai7 , chit gou7-cheng2 lang5 tai7-ke si7 sio-thai5 teh koe3-jit8 .

Chha-put-to lak8 ni5 cheng5 , Se-iuN5 u7 toa7 sio-thai5. Chit e5 sio-thai5 si7 chu7 khai thian-te7 i2-lai5 choe3 te7-it toa7- -e5 , ti7 Tai7-cheng3 3 ni5 8 goeh8 thai5-khi2, thau5-boe2 thai5 u7 gou7 ni5. Ti7 chit e5 chian3-cheng si2 e5 lang5 , u7 chit8-chheng ban7 ; tioh8-siong e5 lang5 , si7 nng7-chheng ban7; chong2-kiong7 si7 siong u7 saN-chheng ban7 lang5. (Lan2 Tai5-oan5 long2-chong2 chiah u7 saN-pah kau2-chap8 ban7 lang5). Koh-chai3 sou2 khai e5 chian3-hui3 si7 saN-chheng chhit-pah ek gin5 (Hian7-si5  Jit8-pun2 choan5-kok e5 kok-hui3 chit8-ni5 si7 chha-put-to chap8-si3 ek gin5). Kin7-kin7 tui3 an2-ni , chiu7 chiok-giah8 thang chai chit-pang Se-iuN5 e5 toa7 chian3-cheng kau3 chaiN2-iuN7 chham2! KhoaN3-kiN3 chit khoan2 e5 chham2-su7 , kin7 -lai5 se3-kai3 tak8 cheng2 lang5 , to long2 chhim-chhim kam2-kak chian3-cheng si7 m7-ho2 , tek-khak tioh8 ai3 peng5-ho5. Put-lun7 to2-chit8 cheng2 lang5 tai7-ke long2 ai3 sio chun-tiong7 sio chiau3-kou3 ; gau5- -e5 khan-chhoa7 gong7- -e5 , iong2- -e5 chiau3-kou3 jiok8- -e5; u7- -e5 pou2-chou7 bo5- -e5; chiah e7 eng7-tit. Koan-he7 chit chan5 su7 , thong se3-kai3 e5 lang5 hian7-si5 hui-siong5 jin7-chin ten gian2-kiu3.

Liat8-ui7 , se3-kai3 e5 tai7-se3 si7 an2-ne ; taN chhiaN2 oat8-thau5 khoaN3 lan2 e5 Tai5-oan5 . Lan2 chit e5 sio2-sio2 e5 Tai5-oan5 u7 kui2 cheng2 lang5 teh cho3-hoe2 tiam3 ?
