Kou3 Liau7 Seng5-phi Ong
Lim5 Kin2-sin7    
Liau7 Seng5-phi Ong, ti7 5 goeh8 6 ho7 piat8-se3, hiong2-siu7 69 hoe3.  Se3-han3 e5 si5, kap i e5 lau7-pe7 gian2-kiu3 han3-hak8.  Tui3 Beng5-ti7 37 ni5 (hit-si5 i 34 hoe3) tng Po2- cheng3 khi2, tit8-kau3 seng-thian, tui3 kok- ka, kau3-hoe7, te7-hng kong3-hian3 chin toa7.
I bat cho3 Ke-chng-tiuN2, Sin3-iong7 chou- hap8-tiuN2, Hiap8-gi7-hoe7-oan5 teng2, chin ku2. Siu7 koaN-thiaN kap te7-hng put-chi2 chun- keng3 tin-tiong7.    
Ti7 Beng5-ti7 38 ni5, i u7 chheng3-au7 po3 kok, hian3 ¢D18.60 cho3 kun-chu.  Tit-tioh8 ha7-su3 Bok8-poe.  I2-au7 u7 tit-tioh8 siuN2 Tai5- oan5 Sin-chiong, Tai7-le2 Ki3-liam7-chiong, Chhiah-sip8-ji7-sia7 chong2-chhai5 piau2- chiong, Liok8-kun Tai7-sin5 Kam2-sia7-chng7, SiuN2-hun-kiok8 chong2-chhai5 Po-chng7, Ai3- kok Hu7-jin5-hoe7-tiuN2 Sia7-chng7.  Ki5-thaN iau2 chin-che7, m7-bian2 koh kong2.    
Lun7 i e5 chu2-lu2: Tiong2-lam5 Liau7 Un- jin5, put-heng7 3 ni5 cheng5 ti7 KiaN-tou piat8- se3, i si7 Bun5-hak8-su7, I-hak8 Phok-su7. Chhu3-lam5 Liau7 Un-khoe, si7 Tiat-hak8 Phok-su7.  Sam-lam5 Liau7 Un-gi7, si7 Hoa3- hak8 Phok-su7.  Si3-lam5 Liau7 Un-cheng3, gou7-lam5 Liau7 Un-chin, si7 Siong-hak8-su7. Te7 2 sim-pu7 kap te7 3 sim-pu7, pun2 long2 si7 Bi2-kok chek8.  Te7 2 sim-pu7 e5 hak8-ui7 si7 B. A. (Bun5-hak8-su7).  Te7 3 sim-pu7 e5 hak8-ui7 si7 B. A. (Bun5-hak8-su7).  I 3 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 long2 si7 Ko-lu2 chhut-sin, 3 e5 kiaN2-sai3 long2 si7 siong-tong e5 jin5-but8.    
Lun7 i e5 hiaN-ti7:  Liau7 Hoan3-chiong kap i 3 e5 kiaN2 long2 si7 I-hak8 Phok-su7. (Kho2-sioh Liau7 Hoan3-chiong Phok-su7, chun5-ni5 ti7 Siong7-hai2 piat8-se3).  Liau7 Sam- tiong7 ti7 Cho2-tiu7-tian5 Tai7-hak8 chai7-hak8 tiong piat8-se3.  Liau7 Heng5-seng si7 Keng-  
che3 hak8-su7.  Mng5-lai7 choan-si7 hak8-su7, phok-su7, long2 si7 Seng5-phi ong chit8 lang5 tit8-chiap kan3-chiap e5 oan7-chou7.  Che kaN2 si7 hai2-su7 tiong sou2 han2-iu2.    
Tui3 kau3-hoe7 lai5 kong2, chu7 Beng5-ti7 29 ni5 cho3 Chip-su7 i2-lai5, long2-chong2 tng Chip-su7 8 ni5, TiuN2-lo2 24 ni5.  Kin7-lai5 in- ui7 su7-giap8 kap seng-khu loan2-jiok8, iau2- ku2 sim siong5-siong5 teh siau3-liam7 kau3- hoe7.  Bat peng5-siong5-jit8 kui2-na7 pai2 kau3 pai3-tng5, si3-ui5 sun5-sun5 khoaN3-khoaN3, chim-chiok ke3-ek8, khoa3-lu7 kau3-hoe7 e5 keng-che3 kap it-poaN e5 chong7-hong2.    
Hian7-si5 kau3-hoe7 chhu3-te7 si7 i 28 ni5 cheng5 hian3--e5, hian7-si5 tat8 beh 8, 9 chheng khou.  Pai3-tng5 si7 i kou2-bu2 hoe7- iu2, kap hoe7-iu2 nge7-kha-keng khi2--e5.  Tui3 3 ni5 cheng5 u7 teh ke3-ek8 pai3-tng5 tioh8 i5- choan2 ti7 khah tu2-ho2 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 ti7 ku7-ni5 be2 chit8 te3 3 hun goa7 beh cho3 pai3- tng5 te7, oa2 5000 khou.  Kau3 beh koe3-si3, u7 ui5-gian5 tioh8 koh hian3 tau3 chit8 ban7, si7 tui3 2, 3 ni5 lai5 chiu7 u7 an3-sng3 beh hian3--e5.    
I e5 cho3 lang5: si7 chin u7 li2-ti3.  Na7 u7 li2- khi3, 3 hoe3 gin2-a2 i to thiaN i.  Tui3 kau3- hoe7 hian3-kim: i na7 siong5-se3 chim-chiok liau2-au7, u7 in2 m7-bat bo5 kia3.  U7 kui2-na7 pai2, si7 bo5 in2 lai5 kia3.  I na7 chai hoe7-iu2 ko2-jian5 u7 chin chin7-lat8 ia7 cho3 be7 kau3--e5, i long2 hoaN-hi2 pau-boe2.  I beh cho3 ho2- su7, bo5 ai3 lang5 chai.  I kui2-na7 pai2 kio3 chip-su7 tioh8 chim-chiok tiau-cha kau3- hoe7 u7 loa7-che7 kan-khou2 lang5, chiu7 am3- chiN7 pang-chan7 in.  I ia7 bat kio3 lang5 tioh8 te5 chiN5 pang-chan7 kan-khou2 lang5. I ia7 bat kia3 chiN5 pang-chan7 bou2 chu7-iu5 e5 thoan5-to7-chia2.    
I e5 chu3-i3 lat8 chin kiong5.  Seng-khu sui-si7 chin ia3-sian7, iau2-ku2 i si5-si5 chin kin2-tiong.  Che si7 peng5-siong5 lang5 sou2 bo5--e5.    
Peng5-siong5 kiam2-chhai2 khah bo5 eng5, choe3-kin7 ti7 peN7-chhng5-tiong, khak-sit8  
chin ti3-i3 sia3-choe7 chheng-gi7 e5 in-tian2. Chhut-lat8 pi7-pan7 sim beh kiN3 Chu2 e5 bin7. Bo5 kiaN si2.  Lang5 na7 khi3 tham3 i, i siang7 ai3, si7 kap i ki5-to2.    
TaN ThiN-pe7 e5 chi2-i3 chiaN5, thau2-pang3 i e5 lo5-khou2, jip8 Chu2 e5 eng5-kng khoai3-lok8. Kau3-hoe7 thiaN i koe3-sin, loa7-che7 lang5 u7 ai-ai chip8-chip8, ui7 i lau5 m7-kam e5 bak8-sai2. Kiu5 Chu2 an-ui3 i e5 ui5-chok8.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 653 ho7, 1939 ni5 8 geh8)
