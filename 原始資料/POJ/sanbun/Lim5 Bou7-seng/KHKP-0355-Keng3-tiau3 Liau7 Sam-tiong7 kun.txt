  Te7 chap8-peh se3-ki2 e5 tiong-iap8 Tek-kok u7 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 e5 sian-seN mia5-kio3 Le5-sin3(Lessing) tu3 chhut-mia5 e5 si-jin5 Kou2-lai5-su-tek(Kleist) e5 toan7-ki3, tiong-kan u7 chit8 cham7 teh kong2, lang5 ti7 se3-kan u7 saN teng2-ho7: thau5 chit8 teng2-ho7 si7 thian-chu chin ho2, koh u7 chiau5-chng5 e5 ki-hoe7 thang poe5-iuN2 kau3 chiaN5-mih8, kau3-boe2 kian3-lip8 be7 hiu2-noa7 e5 kang-tiaN5 ti7 te7-bin7 chiuN7; chit ho7 lang5 han2-tit u7;  Gi-seng3(Goethe)chiu7-si7 chit khoan2 e5 lang5.
