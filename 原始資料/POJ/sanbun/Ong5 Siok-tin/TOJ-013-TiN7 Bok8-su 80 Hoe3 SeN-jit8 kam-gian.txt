

TiN7 Bok8-su 80 Hoe3 SeN-jit8 Kam2-gian5		Ong5 Siok-tin
 1995-ni5 e5 chhun-kui3, goa2 toe3 TiuN I-su kap 1-tin7 peng5-iu2 khi3 Han-chi5-hng5 chham-ka Tai5-lam5 Sin5-hak8-iN7 ki2-pan7 e5 Tai5-gi2 Bun5-hoa3 Kau3-sek. Hit-e5 si5-chun7 Goa2  tui3 oh8 Tai5-gi2 oan5-choan5 bo5 khai3 -liam7, ma7 bo5 beng5-khak e5 bok8-phiau. Kan-ta si7 ti7 sin-the2 siong7 hi-jiok8, sim-leng5 siong7 bo5 oa2-kho3 e5 si5, siuN7 beh theh8-lai5 pai5-khian2 si5-kan cho3 gi7-niu7..e5. SiuN7-be7-kau3 soa3 in-ui7 an2-ne lai5 chiap-chhiok tioh8 po2-kui3 koh cheng-bit8 e5 Lo5-ma2-ji7. Koh sek8-sai7 ka7 it-seng hong7-hian3 hou7 Siong7-te3 kap Tai5-oan5 e5 TiN7 Ji5-giok8 Bok8-su, hou7 goa2 chhim-chhim the2-hoe7 tioh8 Tai5-gi2 kap Tai5-oan5 bun5-hoa3 thoan5-seng5--loh8--khi3 e5 tiong7-iau3-seng3, bo5 heng5-tiong ia7 kai2-pian3 goa2 e5 1-koa2 siuN7-hoat.
Kong2--khi2--lai5 chin kian3-siau3, goan2 chit-te7 sng3-si7 chin heng7-un7, chin ho2-mia7 e5 1-te7-lang5. Goa2 chhut-si3 e5 si5, Tai5-oan5 e5 keng-che3 tu2 khai-si2 teh khi2-poe. Chiah8-chheng7 bo5 khoeh-khiam3--tioh8, koh u7-thang khi3 hak8-hau7 thak8-chheh, bat koa2 ji7. Ui5-ham7 e5 si7 goan2 siu7 e5 kau3-iok8 oan5-choan5 thoat-li7 Tai5-oan5 sia7-hoe7. Hak8-hau7 bo5 ka3 goan2 jin7-bat ka-ki7 khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7, Tai5-oan5 chit-te3 thou2-te7 kap Tai5-oan5 e5 lek8-su2, te7-li2 kap bun5-hoa3. Goan2 e5 su-siong2 siu7-tioh8 sok-pak8, goan2 be7-hiau2 tok8-lip8 su-kho2. Chiong5-lai5 m7-bat khi3 su-kho2 ka-ki7 tau3-te2 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5, beh cho3 sim2-mih8 khoan2 lang5, eng-kai ui7 sia7-hoe7 cho3 sim2-mih8 khah chiaN3-bin7 u7 i3-gi7 e5 tai7-chi3.
Kau3 chham-ka Tai5-gi2 Bun5-hoa3 Kau3-sek, khai-si2 oh8 Tai5-gi2 liau2-au7, chiah chai-iaN2 goan5-lai5 goa2 it-tit8 hou7 lang5 khoaN3-khin e5 bo2-gi2 keng3-jian5 u7 ji7, ji5-chhiaN2 ia7-u7 bun5-hoat, u7 kui-lut8 kap ki5-tha e5 gi2-gian5 kang7-khoan2. Koh hoat-hian7 ti7 goa2 iau2 teh khun3, iau2 be7 kak-chheN2 e5 si5, che7-che7 ni5 lai5, i2-keng u7 che7-che7 lang5 kian-sim it-chi3 teh cho3 po2-liu5, thui-kong2, kap hoat-tian2 

Tai5-oan5 gi2-gian5 kap bun5-hoa3 e5 su7-kang. TiN7 Bok8-su chiu7-si7 ki5-tiong e5 1-e5 leng2-to7-chia2. I nia2-chhoa7 tai7-ke chin3-heng5 chit-e5 ui2-tai7 koh u7 i3-gi7 e5 su7-kang. Ti7 i beh oa2 80 hoe3 e5 hoe3-thau5 iau2  
bo5 siuN7 beh the3-hiu tiam3 chhu3--nih8 khiau-kha lian2-chhui3-chhiu, hiang2-siu7 jin5-seng e5 te7-2 e5 chhun-thiN. Tak8-kang ui7-tioh8 Tai5-oan5-ji7 pe7-bu2-oe7 si3-ke3 teh chau2-chong5, koaN2 hoe2-chhia lai5-lai5 khi3-khi3 teh siong7-kho3. Bo5-lun7 thiN-khi3 koaN5 joah8 ho2 bai2, hoaN-hi2 kam-goan7 hu3-chhut i choan5-pou7 e5 sim-hiat. Chit-khoan2 cheng-sin5 sit8-chai7 hou7 lang5 u7-kau3 khim-phoe3.
TiN7 Bok8-su eng2-oan2 si7 lan2 hak8-sip8 e5 bou5-hoan7. Lan2 tai7-ke u7-ian5 tau3-tin7 oh8 Tai5-gi2, cho3 TiN7 Bok8-su e5 hak8-seng, si7 lan2 e5 hok-khi3. Kin-a2-jit8, tai7-ke chin bo5 kan2-tan chiah thang cho3-hoe2 ti7 chia kheng3-chiok TiN7 Bok8-su 80 hoe3 e5 seN-jit8, goa2 beh chiok-hok Bok8-su seN-jit8 khoai3-lok8, sin-the2 kian7-khong, bo5 hoan5-lo2. Koh beh chiok-hok lan2 Tai5-oan5 kiaN5-jip8 21 se3-ki2 e5 sin si5-tai7. Kian3-lip8 chin-chiaN3 chu7-iu5 bin5-chu2, u7 jin5-khoan5, u7 kong-gi7, u7 bun5-beng5 e5 pang-kok.
