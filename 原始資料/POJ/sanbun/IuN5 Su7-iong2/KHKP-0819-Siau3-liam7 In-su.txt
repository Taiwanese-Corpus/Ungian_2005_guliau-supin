
Siau3-liam7 In-su
IuN5 Su7-iong2
Phi2-jin5 ti7 Tai5-lam5 Sin5-hak8-hau7 chai7-hak8 tiong, u7 3 ui7 sio chiap-soa3 cho3 hau7-tiuN2:  Liam5 Tek-liat8 Bok8-su kap Pa Khek-le2 Bok8-su, Song3 Tiong-kian Bok8-su.  Liam5 Bok8-su seng3-cheng5 un-sun5, bin7-iong5 chhio3-chhi7, hou7 lang5 ho2 chhin-kin7; hoan7-su7 ia7 chin jin7-chin.  Sit8-chai7 si7 ho2 e5 hau7-tiuN2.  Pa Bok8-su e5 su7 m7-bian2 goa2 kong2, che7-che7 long2 chai.  I si7 kiat8-chhut e5 soan-kau3-su, phok-hak8 to-chai5 koh ia7 to-ge7:  Cheng-thong che7-che7 e5 hak8-sut8 kap gu2-gian5.  I u7 ka3 cha2-tai7 e5 sin5-hak8-seng soa3 gian2-kiu3 but8-li2, hoa3-hak8, thian-bun5, te7-li2 teng2-teng2.  I m7-na7 u7 ko-chhim kong2-tai7 ji5-chhiaN2 u7 sun5-cheng5 e5 sin5-hak8 su-siuN2 gau5 ka3 hak8-seng; koh ia7 kai2-ek8 Peh8-oe7-ji7 Sin Ku7-iok chin chiau3 goan5-bun5.  Pa Phok-su7 han3-hak8 chin pa2, eng7基督道綱 a2-si7 基督教與科學 cho3 kho3-pun2 lai5 ka3 hak8-seng, koh u7 thak8 三國誌,二度梅 teng2-teng2 e5 han3-chheh kap siau2-soat.  I e7 toaN5-khim5 ka3 seng-gak8, ia7 e7 phah-kiu5 kap kiaN5-ki5.  Pa Phok-su7 tui3-tiong7 leng5-siu, mui2-jit8 thak8 Siong7-te3 e5 oe7:  Sin Ku7-iok Seng3-keng mui2 ni5 tiaN7-tioh8 thak8 chit8 pian3; chhut-goa7 lu2-heng5 tiong ia7 chiau3 siong5 bo5 lang3.  Ti7 Sin5-hak8-hau7 i u7-si5 kong2, ‘Lan2 lai5 le2-pai3,’ si7 kan-ta ki5-to2 na7-tiaN7.  Pa Phok-su7 kap phi2-jin5 tui3 Siong7-hai2 beh to3-lai5 e5 si5 (1922 ni5), ti7 Hok-chiu saN-kap keh chit8 me5.  Beh khun3 e5 i2-cheng5 Pa Bok8-su kong2, ‘Lan2 lai5 le2-pai3.’  Goa2 siuN7 si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ti7 hak8-hau7 e5 si5 kan-ta beh ki5-to2 na7-tiaN7.  Bo5 phah-sng3 i theh8 chit8 pun2 Sin Ku7-iok toa7-toa7 pun2 e5 Seng3-keng chhut-lai5 kau-thok8 chiah saN-kap ki5-to2.  Le2-pai3 liau2, goa2 kong2 kan-ta ti7-chia keh chit8 me5 na7-tiaN7, li2 ia7 theh8 Sin Ku7-iok lai5!  Pa Bok8-su kong2, ‘Tioh8--nou3! ai3 thak8 m7 to7 theh8 lai5.’ Sit8-chai7 u7 chin ho2 e5 bou5-hoan7.  Hak8-seng na7 khi3 chhoe7 Pa Bok8-su7 kong2 oe7, beh li7-khui e5 si5 i siong5-siong5 chio lang5 ki5-to2. I2-cheng5 ti7 Sin5-hak8-hau7 pai3-lak8 bo5 thak8-chheh, u7 ki5-to2-hoe7.  Cheng3 sian-siN kap hak8-seng long2 chu7-chip8.  Tak8 lang5 u7 chit8 te3 chhau2-tun2, ki5-to2 e5 si5 tai7-ke long2 kui7 ti7 chhau2-tun2 e5 teng2-bin7.  Lang5 kong2 Pa Bok8-su e5 khou3 ti7 kha-thau5-u e5 sou2-chai7 che7-che7 u7 phoa3-khang, si7 in-ui7 i siong5-siong5 kui7-teh ki5-to2 e5 ian5-kou3.  (Hian7-si5 Eng-kok Kiam3-kio5 Sin5-hak8-iN7 ia7 si7 an2-ni le2-pai3 ki5-to2 e5 si5 tai7-ke long2 kui7 loh8-khi3).
Song3 Tiong-kian Bok8-su cho3 Sin5-hak8 hau7-tiuN2, ia7 u7 lau5 chin che7 e5 kam2-hoa3 ti7 hak8-seng e5 nau2-lai7.  I e5 seng3-chit sui-jian5 u7 khah kip, chong2-si7 kau3-hak8 jin7-chin, tui3 hak8-seng u7 jiat8-liat8 e5 thiaN3-cheng5.  Sit8-chai7 si7 thiaN3 kau3-hoe7, thiaN3 hak8-hau7, ia7 chin thiaN3 hak8-seng e5 hau7-tiuN2.  I ka-ki7 e5 jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 hui-siong5 khiam7-phok, chong2-si7 u7 thiaN3-sim gau5 tong5-cheng5 kan-khou2 lang5.  Song3 Bok8-su chin giam5-siu2 si5-kan: m7-bat ban7 lai5, m7-bat thoa-koe3 tiam2.  Tak8 hang7 chin ai3 chiau3 kui-ki2, kong2 oe7 na2 chioh8 cham7 ji7.
Ai3 koh sia2 chit8 hang7 siau3-liam7 Song3 Bok8-su chiah lai5 kiat-sok.  A. D. 1918 ni5, PheN5-ou5 Tang-kiat e5 hiaN-ti7 Lim5 Chong lai5 tui3 Song3 Bok8-su chheng2 phai3 hak8-seng khi3 ha7-ki5 thoan5-to7.  I2-keng koat-teng7 phai3 IuN5 Chiau-gi7 kap IuN5 Su7-iong2 beh khi3 Tang-kiat.  Lim5 Chong hiaN ti7 6 goeh8 boe2 lai5 Tai5-lam5 be2 sam-liau7, ai3 to3-khi3 thang khi2 chhu3 cho3 khia7-ke kap chu7-hoe7 e5 sou2-chai7.  Chhai5-liau7 pan7 ho2, chiu7 lai5 ka7 chit nng7 e5 hak8-seng kong2, lan2 cho3-hoe2 tui3 chia che7 chun5 kau3 Tang-kiat khah li7-pian7; m7-bian2 hou7 lin2 che7 chun5 kau3 Ma2-keng chiah koh lai5 Tang-kiat e5 kan-khou2.  Goan2 thiaN-liau2 chin hoaN-hi2, m7-ku2 tioh8 koh kui2-na7 jit8 hak8-hau7 chiah hioh-joah8.  Sou2-i2 chiu7 khi3 ka7 Song3 Bok8-su kong2.  Song3 Bok8-su thiaN liau2 chiu7 kong2, “Goa2 koat-toan3 m7 chun2, in-ui7 hak8-hau7 iau2-boe7 hioh-khun3.”  Hit-si5 goan2 sit8-chai7 u7 put-boan2, in-ui7 cheng-chha 4, 5 jit8 na7-tiaN7 Song3 Bok8-su m7-chun2 goan2 khi3.  Au7-lai5 tioh8 ke khai sou2-hui3, hou7 goan2 che7 chun5 oan chin hng7 ke loa7-che7 kan-khou2.  Chong2-si7 hau7-tiuN2 m7-chun2 to7 si7 m7-chun2, bo5 hoat-tou7 chi2-u7 sun7-hok8.  1918 ni5 6 goeh8 30 jit, Lim5 Chong hiaN in e5 chun5 tui3 An-peng5 chhut kang2 liau2-au7, chiu7 khi2 hong-thai.  Hit chiah chun5 kap chun5-lai7 e5 lang5 put-ti khi3-hiong3; i2-goa7 u7 sun2-sit chin che7 chun5-chiah.  Au7-lai5 ti7 Hou2-cheN2 u7 lang5 khioh-tioh8 nng7 ki sam, koh 3 e5 sin-si, si7 kap Lim5 Chong siang7 chun5 e5 lang5.
Kau3 7 goeh8 chhe, goan2 tui3 Ko-hiong5 che7 hoe2-chun5 kau3 Ma2-keng.  Goan2 che7 Hong7-thian-oan5 jip8 kang2 e5 si5, Iu5 Se3-chu3 Bok8-su kap kui2-na7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chiuN7 chun5 chhiN-chin tit8-tit8 chhoe7.  IuN5 Bok8-su khoaN3-tioh8 goan2 e5 si5, tah sim-koaN kong2, “Ho2 ka-chai3, ho2 ka-chai3!  Goan2 chin hoan5-lo2, kiaN-liau2 lin2 che7 Chong hiaN e5 chun5.”  In-ui7 Chong-hiaN kap chun5 hoan7-hong, put-ti khi3-hiong3!  Hit tiap8 goan2 thiaN liau2 chiah chai tioh8 kam2-sia7 ThiN-pe7, ia7 tioh8 kam2-sia7 Song3 Bok8-su.  Na7 m7-si7 Song3 Bok8-su kong2, “Goa2 koat-toan3 m7-chun2,” chiu7 goan2 m7-chai tui3 to2-ui7 khi3 lah!  (Iok-han7 13:7).
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 819 ho7, 1957 ni5 3 geh8)
