Lan2 khoaN3 koe3-khi3 chiong-kin7 100 ni5 e5 TiuN2-lo2 kau3-hoe7, Tai5-oan5 chong-kau3-su2 hit e5 kau3-se3, sin3-giong2 kap ki-thaN e5 chin3-pou7, long2-si7 tit-tioh8 peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 kong3-hian3 chin toa7. TaN cheng3-hu2 chun2 lan2 ke3-siok8 eng7 peh8-oe7-ji7, m7-ku2 lan2 ka-ki7 u7 hoat-seng mau5-tun2 e5 siau-sit. Ti7 Tai5-lam5 to u7 an2-ni le3, siong-sin3 pat8 ui7 kiam2 bo5 chit e5 su7 mah?   
SiaN2-su7 Chu2-jit8-oh8 hau7-tiuN2 hoe7-gi7 beh chiong Kok-bun5 cho3 Ki-tok-kau3 kau3-iok8 e5 bun5-ji7?  Kok-lang5 kiam2 m7-si7 tui3 peh8-oe7-ji7 lai5 thak8 Seng3-keng khah khoai3, khah beng5-pek8.  Na7 siang7 si5-chun7 hak8-sip8 peh8-oe7-ji7 kap hak8-sip8 Kok-bun5 kau3 e7 hiau2 thak8 Seng3-keng lai5 tui3-pi2, tah-loh8 chit8 peng5 khah Khoai3. Peh8-oe7-ji7 si7 kui2 le2-pai3 chiu7 e7, na7 eng7 Kok-bun5 kiam2-chhai2 tioh8 joa7 ku2, oh-tit an3-sng3; in-ui7 Kok-bun5 ji7 che7, kai2-sek hok-chap8, che si7 cheng3-lang5 sou2 chai.  Chiau3 Chia7 Keng Sian-siN e5 in2-le7, u7 si5 siong5-siong5 thak8 be7 chheng-chho2, hou7 lang5 thiaN be7 beng5, che si7 su7-sit8, goa2 bian2 koh the5-khi2.  Na7 eng7 peh8-oe7-ji7 cho3 ki-chhou2, chiu7 i3-su3 e7 khah beng5, chiu7 im ia7 khah chiaN3; m7-na7 an2-ni, goa2 sou2 chai e5 sit8-le7, bo5 jip8 hak8-hau7 e5 lau7-lang5, tui3 hak8-sip8 peh8-oe7-ji7 e7 thak8 Kok-bun5, ia7 e7 sia2 phoe, tui3 peh8-oe7-ji7 tok8-hak8 khi3 hak8-sip8 Kok-bun5 kau3 e7 cho3 thoan5-to7-su ia7 kui2-na7 lang5.  Na7 siat-su2 m7-si7 u7 peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 ki-chhou2, chiu7 bo5 chit ho7 ki-hoe7.  Ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5, chong-kau3-siong7 e5 khan-but8 (chap8-chi3 a2-si7 chheh) chin chio2, kai-chai3 kin7-ni5 u7 Oah8-mia7 bi2-niu5, Ka-teng5 e5 peng5-iu2, Lu2 Soan-to7-hoe7-chi3, chai7-lai5 e5 Kong-po3, long2 eng7 peh8-oe7-ji7, chiah e7 thang phou2-kip8, chiah e7 thang ka-kiong5 poe5-iuN2 kian7-choan5 e5 sin3-giong2, hou7 kau3-se3 chin3-tian2.  Peh8-oe7-ji7 e5 kong3-hian3 m7-si7 sio2-khoa2, chhu2-au7 kau3-hoe7 kau3-iok8 eng-kai ai3 ka-kiong5 ka3 peh8-oe7-ji7 si7 choe3 iau3-kin2 e5 bun7-te5.
