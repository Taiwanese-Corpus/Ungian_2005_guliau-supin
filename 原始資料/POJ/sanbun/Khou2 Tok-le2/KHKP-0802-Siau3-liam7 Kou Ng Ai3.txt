Khou2 Tok-le2
Ti7 1898 ni5, Mui5 Bok8-su kap Lim5 Hak8-kiong Bok8-su lai5 Khe-ou5 pou3-to7 e5 si5, thau5-chui2 e5 koe2-chi2 Sia7 Kau2 TiuN2-lo2 hu-hu7 lai5 chiap-siu7 to7-li2, na7-si7 i2-cheng5 e5 Kau2 TiuN2-lo2-niu5 si7 jiat8-sim keng3 sin5-hut8, chiah8-chhai3 3, 6, 9.  Chong2-si7 ke-lai7 boe7 peng5-an, chit8 e5 siN, chit8 e5 si2; tang7, chhiong, thou2, soah; cho3 ngou2-siong7 kui2-sin5 e5 lou5-chai5.  1899 ni5 chiaN-goeh8 chhe-kau2 thiN-kong siN, kap hiah e5 ngou2-siong7 toan7-choat8, chiu7 sin3 Ia5-sou cho3 Chu2 e5 peh-siN3.  Ti7 1901 ni5 6 goeh8 23 jit8, 36 hoe3 tui3 Kam Ui5-lim5 Bok8-su kap tiong7-hu kang7-jit8 nia2 se2-le2 jip8-kau3, 55 ni5 ku2 choe3 Chu2 chin7-tiong e5 lu2-tou5, gau5 pang-chan7 tiong7-hu, thang kong2 si7 bou5-hoan7 e5 sin3-tou5. Lun7-khi2 i e5
1) Sin3-sim.  Jiat8-sim siu2 le2-pai3, tu5-khi2 tang7-peN7 i2-goa7, m7-bat bo5 chhut-sek8, ki5-to2-hoe7 bo5-lun7 hng7, kin7 toe3 kau3 tioh8, sui-jian5 70 goa7 hoe3 ia7 chham-ka pou3-to7-tui7, kiaN5 8, 9 li2 lou7, thau3 hong, loh8 hou7 kiaN5 choe3 thau5-cheng5. 2) ThiaN3-sim.  Tui3 ke-lai7, tui3 hoe7-lai7, hoe7-goa7 gau5 chio-hou, khoan2-thai7 lang5 it-lut8 peng5-teng2.  Tek8-piat8 san3-hiong kan-khou2 e5 lang5 gau5 khan-seng5, gau5 khoaN3-kou3, tui3 lek8-tai7 thoan5-kau3-chia2 bu5 bi5 put-chi3, khoan2-thai7 in chhin-chhiuN7 ke-lai7 e5 lang5.
3)	Hong7-su7.  Tui3 Khe-ou5 siat kau3-hoe7 mui2 le2-pai3-jit8, tiong-tau3 chu2-png7 hu7-tam chhui-su7 e5 chek-jim7, bi2, kiam5, chha5, iu5, iam5, na7 bo5 kau3, chiu7-si7 bo5 hun kong, su theh8 lai5 pou2 kah kau3, soa3 hian3 kang chu2 hou7 lang5 chiah8, m7-si7 saN gou7 ni5, chiu7-si7 kau3 hui-siong5-si5 phoe3-kip bi2, bo5 pan7-hoat thang koh ke3-siok8 e5 si5 chiah cheng5-chi2.  Chheng-sau3 pai3-tng5, tak8 hang7 e5 kang hoaN-hi2 chhut-lat8 bo5 e-the, hiong3-cheng5 khioh khi3 cho3.  Hoe7-iu2 na7 u7 song hi2-su7, tek8-piat8 koan-sim, kap hoaN-hi2 e5 lang5 saN-kap hoaN-hi2, kap iu-bun7 e5 lang5 saN-kap iu-bun7.  Seng-oah8
