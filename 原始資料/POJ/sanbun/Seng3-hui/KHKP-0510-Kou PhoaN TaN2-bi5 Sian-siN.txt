Kou PhoaN TaN2-bi5 Sian-siN
Seng3-hui
PhoaN Sian-siN chhut-si3 ti7 1894 ni5.  Se3-han3 e5 si5 pe7-bo2 lip8-chi3 beh chiong i hian3 hou7 Siong7-te3 cho3 Chu2 e5 kang.  Sou2-i2 Kong-hak8-hau7 chut-giap8 au7 chiu7 jip8 Tai5-lam5 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 Tiong hak8-hau7.  Au7-lai5 chut-giap8 liau2 koh jip8 Sin5-hak8-hau7, pit8-giap8 si7 tu2 i 22 hoe3 e5 si5.
Ti7 1916 ni5 4 goeh8 i ho7 siu7 phai3 tiam3 SoaN-pa3 Kau3-hoe7 iok saN ni5 ku2.  Au7-lai5 choan2-jim7 khi3 Chhau2-tun, Pou-li2, Toa7-lam3, Gu5-khun3-soaN chiah e5 kau3-hoe7.  Thau5-boe2 ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang tioh8-boa5 iok peh-ni5 ku2.  Au7-lai5 ui7-tioh8 ka-teng5 e5 tou-hap8 si5-jim7 to3-lai5 su7 hau7 pe7-bo2.  Put-heng7 ti7 8 goeh8 1 ho7 siu7 Chu2 keng2-tiau3 chiap-lap8 chiuN7-thiN; hiong2-siu7 34 hoe3.  Hoe7-lai7 e5 hiaN-che2 oan2-jian5 hou7 ou-hun5 jia-bat8, bo5-m7 ui7-tioh8 i thong3-chhiat thi5-khau3.  Chhe-ji7 ti7 i e5 pun2-chhu3 ku2-heng5 chong3-sek, hoe7-chong3-chia2 iok 400 mia5, thang kong2 kek8-ki5 seng7-hong2.  Lun7 i e5 it-seng choe3 lang5:
1.Jin5-ai3.  Sian-siN e5 seng3-cheng5 kek8-ki5 un-jiu5, khiam-sun3, put-chi2 ai3 ho5-peng5.  Tiam3 kau3-hoe7 m7-bat thiaN-kiN3 kap hoe7-iu2 u7 khau2-kak, kap chhu3-piN peng5-iu2 ia7 chin-ho2 lai5-khi3.  Bo5-lun7 lo2-iu3 i put-chi2 ho2 chhin-kin7, sou2-i2 bat kap i kau-poe5 e5 lang5 bo5-m7 siau3-liam7 i e5 ho2 choe3-lang5.

3.Hian3-kang.  Tui3 si5 Thoan5-to7-su to3-lai5 pun2 kau3-hoe7, chiu7 tam-tng Chip-su7 e5 chit-hun7.  Ui7-tioh8 kau3-hoe7 e5 chai5-cheng3 put-chi2 chhut-lat8 liau7-li2.  Ia7 cheng5-ni5 bat choe3 Chu2-jit8-oh8 Hau7-tiuN2 lai5 kou2-bu2 Chu2-jit8-oh8. Kau3-hoe7 e5 su7 put-chi2 koan-sim, hoaN-hi2 hian3-kang.
