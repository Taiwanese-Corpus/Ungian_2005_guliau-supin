Hou7 loh8 kui-kang, a-bu2 chheng7 hou7-saN, ti3 chhau2-leh8-a2 koaN7 bi2-khgn, chhun-png7, chhun-chhai3 khi3 chhi7-ti.  Bo5-goa7-ku2, a-bu2 teh kio3--goa2.
