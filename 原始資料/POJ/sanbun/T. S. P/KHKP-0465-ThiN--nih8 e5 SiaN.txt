Thang kong2 u7 300 ni5 lai5 e5 bun5-hoa3, se3-kai3 sou2 sng3 u7 tioh8 e5 Tang-kiaN te3-tou chit pang in-ui7 siu7-tioh8 hui-siong5 e5 te7- tang7, i2-kip8 hoe2-chai, it-si5-kan kok cheng2 e5 su7-giap8, tak8-iuN7 e5 ki-koan, chiu7 keng- che3 kai3, kau3-iok8 kai3, cheng3-ti7 kai3 kok hong-bin7 siu7 toa7 eng2-hiong2.  Chit e5 hoan5-hoa seng7-koan e5 te3-tou, oa2 chiaN5- poaN3, ti7 chit8-bak8-nih-ku2 e5 tiong-kan oan2-jian5 piN3-cho3 khong3-ia2 hoe2-sio-pou.  
HaiN7-ah ! Chin2-chai cheng5 hiah e5 toa7 keng koan5-lau5, chhong3 sui2-sui2 e5 siong- tiam3, kou2-chek, beng5-sou2, ke-lou7, si7 phou2- thong cheng3-lang5 him-bou7 ai3 kau3, ai3 khoaN3 e5 sou2-chai7.  Na7-si7 kau3 chit chun7 sou2 chhun--e5 si7 hiah e5 sio bo5 khi3 e5 chng- a2-koeh8, thih-phiaN2 kap hoe2-sio-hu na7- tiaN7.  Chin-chiaN3 si7 chham2-tam7, thang kho2-lin5 e5 chong7-thai7.  
Goa2 sui-jian5 ti7 chin2-chai au7 chiaN5 goeh8-jit8 chiah jip8 KiaN, sui-si7 bo5 khoaN3- tioh8 hit ho7 lui2-lui2 tui7-tui7 e5 sin-si, a2-si7 hoe2 teh toh8 e5 sit8-keng2; iau2-ku2 khoaN3- tioh8 sou2 chhun e5 pi7-lan7-bin5, hoe2-sio jiah, kut-thau5-hu e5 hian7-siong7, chu7-jian5 ia7 e7- tit liau7-siong2 tng-si5 chham2-chong7 e5 kong-keng2.  Ia7 m7-si7 pit-siat8 sou2 heng5- iong5 e7 chin7.  Lun7 chit-ho7 m7-bian2 goa2 koh ti7-chia sia2 kau3 loa7 siong5-se3 lai5 kiau2-jiau2 liat8-ui7 e5 bak8-chiu, ia7 si7 sou2 chai-iaN2 e5 su7.  Put- ko3 chit pang chit ho7 te7-tang7, kiam2-chhai2 u7 hak8-chia2 tui3 te7-chit-hak8 lai5 kong2, si7 te7-chit e5 goan5-li2 ia7 ku2-kaN2, khiok u7-iaN2, boe7 bian2-tit; iau2-ku2 ti7-chia lan2 cho3 sin3- chia2 e5 chit8-hun7-chu2, kiam2 e7 bian2-tit siuN7 chit khoan2 thian-chai te7-pian3, bo5 koh-iuN7 oan2-jian5 si7 chit8 cheng2 ThiN--nih8 e5 SiaN ?  
Chit khoan2 kan3-chiap e5 siaN, tui3 ti7 chap8-goa7 ban7 e5 si2-sit e5, thau3-koe3 kau3 ti7 chhi7-bin5 e5 sim-lai7.  Chit khoan2 siaN ko2-jian5 che7-chio2 u7 kio3-chheN2, kam2- chhiok tioh8 chhi7-bin5 leng5-tek e5 sim.  
He si7 an3-choaN2 kong2 ? In-ui7 liat8-ui7 long2 chai, tou-chhi7 ju2 toa7, chhi7-ke ju2 lau7-jiat8, si7 hou7 jin5-sim chhia-chhi2, hi-eng5 ju2 kiong5.  Bun5-beng5 hak8-bun7 ju2 koan5, chiu7 lun5-li2 to7-tek ju2 pai7; jin5-cheng5 hong-siok8 ju2-bai2.  Tui3 an2-ni, chong-kau3- sim, sin3-tek chiu7 jit8-goeh8 na2 tui7-loh8.
