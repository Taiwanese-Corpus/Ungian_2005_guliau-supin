Ji5-tong5 Kau3-iok8 e5 Chit8-hong-hiong3
Ong5 Siu2-iong2  
Gin2-a2 u7 nng7 khoan2 e5 seng3, a2-si7 kong2 u7 nng7 khoan2 e5 kheng-hiong3 (tendency).  Chiu7-si7 u7 oe7 oh8--tit e5 seng3 (learned tendency) kap boe7-oe7 oh8--tit e5 seng3, (unlearned tendency).  Na7 oaN7 oe7 lai5 kong2, chiu7-si7 gin2-a2 u7 tioh8 ka3 kap m7- bian2 ka3 e5 seng3 ti7-teh. Phi3-lun7 lai5 kong2, gin2-a2 chhut-si3 liau2-au7, bo5 ku2 chiu7 oe7  hiau2 suh-leng, lau7-bu2 kiam2 u7 ka3 i tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 suh?  I long2 m7-bian2 lang5 ka3,  chhut-si3 chiu7 oe7-hiau2. Che2 chiu7-si7 boe7- oe7 ka3--tit e5 seng3. Koh chit8 hang7 chiu7-si7 tioh8 ka3, chiah oe7-hiau2. Chhin-chhiuN7 oh8 thak8-chheh, oh8 sia2-ji7, oh8 sng3-siau3, che long2 tioh8 ka3 chiah oe7-hiau2. Sou2-i2 kau3-iok8 e5 gi7-bu7 chiu7-si7 ti7-chia. Tioh8 poe5-iuN2 chit-khoan2 oe7 ka3--tit e5 seng3, hou7 i tit-tioh8 hoat-tat8, thang lai5 tit oan5- choan5, chiu7-si7 lang5 e5 pun2-hun7.  
TaN beh ka3 gin2-a2 tioh8 koh chai nng7 hang7 si7 iau3-kin2, chiu7-si7 Ui7-ti3 (situation)  kap Hoan2-eng3 (response). Phi3-ju7 lai5 kong2, lan2 khoaN3 kou-koe-a2 he7 ti7 na5-a2-lai7 e5 si5,  che chiu7-si7 Ui7-ti3. TaN chiong chit chiah koe-a2-kiaN2 ka7 i pang3 chhut-lai5, koh ia7  bi2 ti7 i e5 bin7-cheng5 e5 si5, chit chiah koe- a2-kiaN2 e5 Ui7-ti3 chiu7-si7 khia7 ti7 bi2 e5 thau5- cheng5. TaN chit-chiah koe-a2-kiaN2, sui- bong2 bo5 lang5 ka3 i tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 chiah8 bi2, iau2-ku2 ka-ki7 oe7-hiau2 teh khi2 lai5 chiah8, che chiu7-si7 Hoan2-eng3. A2-si7 kong2 gin2-a2  na7 hou7 Lau5-bo2 pho7 ti7 heng-cheng5 e5 si5, i e5 Ui7-te3 chiu7-si7 ti7 nng7 liap8 leng e5 sin- piN, sou2-i2 aN3-loh8 khi3 suh chiu7-si7 kio3-choe3 Hoan2-eng3. Lan2 chai kou2-cha2 Beng7-chu2 e5 lau7-bu2, saN-pai2 soa2 sou2-chai7 lai5 ka3 kiaN2, chiu7-si7 teh soat-beng5 Ui7-ti3 kap Hoan2- eng3 e5 le7. 
TaN lan2 e5 bok8-tek chiu7-si7 Chong- kau3 Kau3-iok8. Na7-si7 an2-ni, Chong-kau3  tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 ka3? Tioh8 in2-chhoa7 gin2-a2 bat Siong7-te3, kap ka3-si7 chiong2-chiong2 e5   
to7-li2 si7 iau3-kin2, m7-ku2 koh-khah iau3-kin2 e5 chiu7-si7 Chong-kau3-tek e5 Ui7-ti3. Tioh8  hou7 gin2-a2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang saN-kap hun7 ti7 Chong-kau3, tioh8 in2-chhoa7 in lai5 hu3 Chu2- jit8-oh8, hou7 in u7 hun7 ti7 le2-pai3, a2-si7 ti7 chong-kau3 chip8-hoe7 e5 si5, tioh8 hou7 gin2-a2 u7 hun7 chiah oe7 eng7-tit. Tui3 Chong-kau3 e5 Ui7-ti3, hou7 gin2-a2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang hoan2- eng3 in e5 chong-kau3 sim.  Lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 khah-siong5 toa7-lang5 chhoa7 gin2-a2 lai5 le2- pai3, chit khoan2 goa2 siuN7 chin ho2, in-ui7 hou7 gin2-a2 kap toa7-lang5 piN5-piN5 u7 hun7 ti7 le2-pai3.  Sui-bong2 gin2-a2 to7-li2 thiaN m7-bat, m7-ku2 in e5 chong-kau3 sim e5 hoan2-eng3 bo5 khah-su toa7-lang5.  ChhiaN2 tai7-ke thiaN3 gin2-a2, ti3-i3 gin2-a2, in2-chhoa7 gin2-a2 lai5 jip8 Siong7-te3 e5 kok, hou7 in thang siu7 Chu2 chiok-hok. Che si7 Chu2 sou2 hoaN-hi2.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 510 koan3,1927 ni5 9geh8)
