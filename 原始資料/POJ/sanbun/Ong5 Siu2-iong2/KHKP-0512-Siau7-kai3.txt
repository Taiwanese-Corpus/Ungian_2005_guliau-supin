Siau7-kai3
Ong5 Siu2-iong2    
Lou7-tek Ma2-teng kong2, "Te7-it ho2 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 m7-si7 kan-ta khng3 choe7-choe7 chheh, koh thak8 choe7-choe7 chheh e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 tui3 choe7-choe7 chheh e5 lai5 tit- tioh8 e5 ka3-si7, hoaN-hi2 koh pun hou7 pat8- lang5, u7 an2-ni lai5 sit8-heng5--e5 chiah si7 chin e5 Ki-tok-tou5.  Chong2-si7 an2-ni lai5 sit8-heng5 e5 si7 tit-tioh8 Seng3 Sin5 e5 chhiong-moa2 e5 lang5 chiah oe7."  Lan2 thang kong2 ho2 e5 Ki-tok-tou5 tioh8 t hak8-chheh choe7-choe7, thiaN ho2 ka3-si7 choe7-choe7, sit8-heng5 pun hou7 lang5 choe7-choe7, chiah oe7 eng7-tit.    
Sou2 ai3 siau7-kai3 e5 si7 kou3 Tou7-sit8 (Tsuge) Sian-siN e5 ui5-tu3 "Gou7-sun5-choeh e5 Cheng5- au7;" si7 sia2 Ho5-bun5, kiong7 267 bin7, hun choe3 32 chiuN, ki5-tiong 9 chiuN u7 sia2 lai5 lan2 Tai5-oan5 thoan5-to7 li2-heng5 e5 su7. Teng7- ke3 peng7-che3 khou-ji7, siong7-che3 khou-lak8. Ti7 Tai5-tiong chhia-thau5-cheng5 Kong2- un7 Kong-si, Tan5 Kheng5-iau5 Bok8-su hia u7 teh boe7. Na7 bat Jit8-pun2 oe7 e5 lang5 chhiaN2 boe2 lai5 khoaN3.    
Lan2 chai Tou7-sit8 Sian-siN m7-si7 hak8- chia2, si7 chong-kau3-ka, si7 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5. I u7 lai5 lan2 Tai5-oan5 thoan5-to7.  I oah8 e5 si5 put-chi2 teh siau3-liam7 lan2 hiaN-ti7 chi2-be7. I choe3 Thian-bou7 Pou3-to7-tui7 kau3 lan2 chia e5 si5, lan2 toa7 hoan-geng5. Chit-tiap8 lan2 khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 chheh e5 si5 oan2-jian5 khoaN3- tioh8 i. Chit pun2 chheh si7 to7-li2 e5 chheh. I e5 chong-kau3 keng-giam7 tam5 choe7-choe7. Lan2 thang kong2 i si7 Jit8-pun2 e5 Sat-hu Sun-tai7.  An2-ni sio-po3 chiu7-si7.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 512 koan3,1927 ni5 11 geh8)
