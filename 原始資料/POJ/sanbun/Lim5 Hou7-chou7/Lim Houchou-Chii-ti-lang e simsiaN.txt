Chhi7-ti-lang5 e5 Sim-siaN
Lim5 Hou7-chou7

Ti7 1997 ni5 e5 3 goeh8 20, pok8-hoat ti-che i2-lai5, lan2 long2 kan-taN khoaN3 tioh8 cheng3-hu2 koaN-goan5 ti7 hia chiah8 ti-kha-khou, hou7 jin5-bin5 khoaN3 long2 bo5, khoaN3 bo5 chit e5 cheng3-hu2 the5-chhut iu2-hau7 e5 cheng3-chhek lai5 kai2-koat bun5-te5.

Lan2 ai3 chai-iaN2, Tai5-oan5 pun2-lai5 si7 hui-ek-khu, chiah-e5 ti-che(khau2-the5-ek) e5 peN7-khun2 si7 ui3 Tiong-kok hit-peng5 thoan5 lai5 Tai5-oan5--e5. Tai5-oan5 e5 seng-li2-lang5 kap put-siau3 e5 cheng3-hu2 koaN-goan5 kau-kiat, ui3 Tiong-kok chau2-su ti-a2-kiaN2 lai5 Tai5-oan5, cho7-seng5 Tai5-oan5 kin-a2-jit8 chit e5 kiok8-bin7.  Lan2 long2 bo5 khoaN3 tioh8 chit e5 cheng3-hu2 the5-chhut tui3-chhek lai5 kai2-koat bun7-te5, kan-taN khoaN3 tioh8 chit-koa2 koaN-goan5 ti7 hia chiah8 ti-kha-khou, kio3 jin5-bin5 ka7 ti-bah chu2 hou7 sek8, an2-ne kam2 si7 kai2-koat bun7-te5 e5 hong hoat?

Lan2 khoaN3 tioh8 Pang-kio5 e5 chhi7-ti-hou7, in ka7 in it-seng e5 sim-hoeh long2 he7 ti7 chhi7-ti teng2-koan5, kau3 choe3-au7, in e5 sim-hoeh oan5-choan5 chhin-chhiuN7 am3-mi5 kang-khoan7 m7 chai tang-si5 chiah e7 thiN-kng.

Kai2-koat e5 pan7-hoat to7 si7 m7-thang koh ui3 Tiong-kok chau2-su long5-san2-phin2 lai5 Tai5-oan5, koh lai5 to7 si7 ka7 u7 peN7 e5 peN7-ti koaN2-kin2 chhu2-li2 hou7 ho2, bo5 peN7 e5 ti kin2 ka7 keh-li5--khui. Tan7-si7 tui3 Tiong-hoa5-bin5-kok chit-e5 goa7-lai5 cheng3-khoan5 lai5-kong2, goa2 sou2 kong2 e5 pan7-hoat, in choat8-tui3 cho3 be7 kau3.

Tai5-oan5 si7 chit e5 ti7 tai7-chi3 hoat-seng liam-mi to7 pang3 hou7 boe7 ki3 e5 bin5-chok8, koe3 2~3 ko3 goeh8, Tai5-oan5 jin5-bin5 to7 iu7-koh si7 teh chiah8 ti-bah--louh.
