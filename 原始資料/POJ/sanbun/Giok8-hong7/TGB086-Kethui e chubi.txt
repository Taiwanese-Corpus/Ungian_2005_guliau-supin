Ke-thui2 e5 chu-bi7
Giok8-hong7

Ke-thui2 si7 goa2 chit8-si3-lang5 siong7 ia3-sian7 e5 chiah8-mih8, in-ui7 i tai7-piau2--e5 si7 goa2 se3-han3 tit be7 tioh8 e5 thiann3 kap siu7-siong e5 thiann3.
Tak8 pai2 koe3-ni5 koe3-chiat, khoann3-tioh8 a-bu2 koh teh thai5-ke thai5-ah khoan2 seng-le2 teh pai3, to7 e7 hou7 goa2 siunn7-tioh8 khah-cha2 chin che7 tai7-chi3. Tek8-piat8 si7 a-bu2 na7 khai-si2 khu5 ti7 chau3-kha teh tok ke-a2 ah-a2 e5 si5, goa2 e5 sim-cheng5 to7 be7 peng5-cheng7.
Se3-han3 e5 si5, a-bu2 long2 si7 ka7 ke-thui2 gia5 hou7 sio2-ti7 (leng7-goa7 chit8-ki si7 lau5 hou7 a-kong chiah8), kio3 goa2 chiah8 sit8-kou3. Khut-kiong5 e5 goa2 ui7-tioh8 chit8-ki ke-thui2 siu7-chin7 ui2-khut kap put-hok. Goa2 kan-tann si7 siunn7 bo5 si7 an2-choann2 cha-pou--e5 chiah e7-tang3 chiah8 ke-thui2 , cha-bou2 gin2-a2 tioh8-ai3 jin7 pun2-hun7. Goa2 na7 tho2 bo5 ke-thui2 , to7 e7 khioh-hun7, put-peng5 kap kui pak-tou2 e5 ui2-khut.
Sio2-ti7 si7 a-bu2 lian5-soa3 senn sann-e5 cha-bou2 gin2-a2 liau2 chiah senn--tioh8 e5 cha-pou--e5. Goa2 kap sio2-ti7 chha chit8-hoe3 goa7, a-bu2 senn sio2-ti7 liau2-au7, sou2-u7 e5 koan-ai3 to7 long2 tui3 sio2-ti7 hia khi3. Sio2-ti7 senn--loh8-lai5, siu7-siong siong7 toa7--e5 to7-si7 goa2. Sio2-sio2 e5 ni5-ki2 to7-si7 siunn7 bo5 si7 an2-choann2 tak8-hang7 ai3 niu7 kap goa2 ni5-hoe3 chha chit8-hoe3 goa7 e5 sio2-ti7, goa2 to7-si7 m7 kam-goan7, pun2-leng5 to7 piann3 senn3-mia7 kap sio2-ti7 chhiunn2, kap sio2-ti7 chenn. Goa2 ju2 chenn, to7 ju2 be7 tit-lang5-thiann3; ju2 bo5 tit-tioh8 lang5 e5 koan-ai3, goa2 to7 ju2 ai3 nau7 ju2 ai3 ou-peh8-hoe. M7-koh jit8-chi2 ku2--lai5, goa2 ma7 bo5 tho2-tioh8 siann2-mih ho2--chhu3, tian-to3 chiah8 be7-chio2 tek-a2-si. A-bu2 sui-bong2 si7 chhenn-me5-gu5, m7-bat poann3-ji7, soah ma7 chai ¡§kun3-a2 kap ang5-chhai3-thau5 e5 li2-lun7¡¨. I e7 khai-si2 loh8-nng2 hou7 goa2 khah jin7-hun7--e5, tng sio2-ti7 u7 nng7-liap8 thng5-a2, i e7 khou2-khng3 sio2-ti7 hou7 goa2 chit8-liap8, goa2 ma7 tauh8-tauh8-a2 e7 phoe3-hap8, chai-iann2 koai-koai--a to7 khah ho2 koe3 jit8.
Tauh8-tauh8-a2, goa2 e5 sim-thai7 pian3-cho3 chiok kiann sio2-ti7, kiann i poah8-to2, kiann i hau2, tak8-hang7 chu7-tong7 beh niu7 sio2-ti7, bo5 beh chenn bo5 beh chha2 to7-si7 beh hou7 a-bu2 hoann-hi2. Goa2 chong2-sng3 chai-iann2 sio2-ti7 si7 a-bu2 e5 sim-koann po2-poe3, goa2 si7 senn lai5 po2-hou7 i e5 po2-poe3. Jin7-mia7 koh jin7-hun7, kong2-oe7 heng5-tong7 long2 khoann3 lang5 bin7-sek, in-ui7 goa2 si7 ¡§cha-bou2 gin2-a2-phi2¡¨. Tng goa2 chenn be7 iann5 e5 si5, to7 khai-si2 tian2-hian7 goa2 e5 lou5-chai5 seng3-keh, ui3 hoan2-khong3 kau3 oan5-choan5 phoe3-hap8, tui3 goa2 lai5 kong2, che to7-si7 seng-chun5 e5 to7-li2.
Chit-ma2 e5 gin2-a2 it-teng7 siunn7 long2 bo5 ui7-tioh8 chit8-ki ke-thui2, u7 siann2-mih thang ho2 chenn thang ho2 siu7-siong--e5, m7-koh goa2 soah chiok chheng-chhou2 i ti7 goa2 senn3-mia7 tiong, lau5 loh8 tng5-tng5 am3-am3 e5 iann2-jiah.
