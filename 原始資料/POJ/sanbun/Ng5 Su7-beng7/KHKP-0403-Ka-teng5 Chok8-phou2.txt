
Ka-teng5 Chok8-phou2

(Ng5 Su7-beng7 ki3).
Lan2 na7 jip8 bo5 thiaN to7-li2 e5 lang5 e5 ke, chiu7 liam5-piN e7 khoaN3-kiN3 in e5 thiaN-tng5 u7 che7-che7 bok8-chu2.  Sia2 in e5 chou2-kong pe7-bo2 e5 mia5, kap kan2-kan2 e5 lai5-lek se3-he7.  Kho2-sioh in tui3-tiong7 koe3-thau5, soa3 eng7 chiah-e5 lai5 kui7-pai3 keng3-hong7.  Ui-tioh8 an2-ni chiu7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 u7 tu5-khi3.  In-ui7 lan2 e5 Seng3-kau3 u7 ka3-si7 lang5 m7-thang cho3 siong7 kui7-pai3.  Chong2-si7 lan2 kan-ta long2 tu5-khi3, chou2-kong pe7-bo2 e5 lai5-lek8 se3-he7 lan2 long2 m7-chai, ia7 bo5 te3 thang chha5-kho2, an2-ni ia7 si7 bo5 tu2-ho2.  Kin7-lai5 u7 lang5 hip si7-toa7 lang5 e5 siong7 lai5 cho3 ki3-liam7; m7-ku2 che7-che7 bo5 li7-pian7 thang hip-siong7.  Koh na7 kan-ta siong7, bo5 lioh8-a2 ki3-chai3 i e5 lai5-lek8, se3-he7, koe3 nng7 saN tai7 ia7 si7 long2 m7-chai.
Ti7 kui2-na7 ni5 cheng5 u7 chit8 lang5 mia5 kio3 Ong5 kim-leng5, i u7 chiong goa2 souteh2 pi7-pan7 e5 the2-sek khi3 in3.  Cho3-liau2 khiok ho2-khoaN3 koh hap8-eng7.  Kho2-siohi siuN7 ai3 tui3 chit pun2 phou7 lai5 than3 chiN5,soa3 bo5 sim2-mih8 lang5 ka7 i be2.  Put-heng7 chit lang5 bo5 loa7-ku2 chiu7 si2-khi3.  TaN inu7 chiong chiah e5 phou7 lai5 be7 lan2 Tiong-oh8 Hau7-iu2-hoe7.  Hau7-iu2-hoe7 chu2-tiuN ai3 hou7 tai7-ke khah khoai3 be2, sou2-i2 chit8 pun2 teng7 ke3-chiN5 4 kak.  (Iu5-hui3 chai7-goa7, na7 khah che7 lang5 be2 cho3-hoe2 kia3, chiu7 hit e5 sou2-hui3 e7 ju2 seng2).  Sit8-chai7 chin siok8, koh si7 lan2 lang5-lang5 sou2 tioh8 khiam3-eng7 e5 mih8.  TaN na7 u7 lang5 ai3 be2 thang tui3 lin2 pun2-tong5 e5 thoan5-to7 sian-siN kong2, a2-si7 lin2 hap8 cho3-hoe2 sia2 phoelai5 hou7 Tai5-lam5 Jin5-eng5 Ioh8-pang5, Ng5 Jin5-eng5 kun, i chiu7 e7 ka7 li2 kia3 khi3.Na7 beh kia3 chiN5 lai5 hou7 Ng5 Sian-siN, tioh8 tui3 Iu5-pian7-kiok8 kia3 Chin2-the3.  Chin2-the3 e5 ho7 chiu7-si7 (臺灣第三六壹番黃仁榮) M7-thang kia3 Ui5-the3 (為替)in-ui7 u7 khah siong-tiong7 sou2-hui3.
Na7 u7 lang5 ai3 hit ho7 khah che7 iah8-e5 ia7 u7.  U7 teh pi7-pan7 hit ho7 pi2 peng5-siong5 saN poe7 che7-e5, koh-chai3 teng5 kap pou3-phoe5 khah kian-kou3 -- e5; chit khaon2 e5 chit8 pun2 khou-peh.  Kiam2-chhai2 u7 lang5 be7 hiau2 ka-ti7 chhau-sia2, ia7 ti3-i3 tioh8 chhiaN2 lang5 ka7 i chhau.  In-ui7 na7 bo5 kin2-kin2 cho3, kiaN-liau2 bo5 loa7-ku2 lan2 sui-lang5 e5 se3-he7, si7-toa7-lang5 e5 su7, soa3 long be7 ki3-tit liau2-liau2, che si7 lan2 sui lang5 chit8 hang7 iau3-kin2 e5 su7.  Ia7 thang that-bat8 goa7-bin7 lang5 chit hang7 e5 ki-chhi3.
