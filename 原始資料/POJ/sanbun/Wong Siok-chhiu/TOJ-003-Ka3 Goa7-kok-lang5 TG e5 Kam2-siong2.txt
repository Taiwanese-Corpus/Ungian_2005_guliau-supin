 Ka3 Goa7-kok-lang5 Tai5-gi2 e5 Kam2-siong2 

Wong Siok-chhiu
 
Tai5-oan5 400 ni5 lai5, keng-koe3 Se-pan-ga5-lang5,Ho5-lan5-lang5, Jit8-pun2-lang5, Kok-bin5-tong2 Cheng3-hu2 e5 thong2-ti7, bun5-hoa3 kap gi2-gian5 u7 siu7 tioh8 goa7-lai5 cheng3-koan5 e5 eng2-hiong2, tek8-piat8 si7 kin7 chit8-pah ni5 lai5, Tai5-oan5 ti7 Jit8-pun2 Cheng3-hu2 kap Kok-bin5-tong2 Cheng3-hu2 e5 thong2-ti7 chi-ha7, Tai5-gi2 m7-na7 siu7 tioh8 eng2-hiong2, koh siu7 tioh8 ta2-ap. Jit8-pun2 si5-tai7 eng7 Jit8-pun2-oe7  cho3 koaN-hong e5 gi2-gian5. Hak8-hau7 e5 kau3-iok8 eng7 Jit8-pun2-oe7 cho3-chu2. Kau3 Kok-bin5-tong2 si5-tai7, koaN-hong gi2-gian5 kap hak8-hau7 kau3-iok8 ma7 eng7 Pak-kiaN-gi2 cho3-chu2. Tai5-gi2 kan-ta si7 ka-teng5 pe7-kiaN2, bu2-kiaN2 e5 kau-thong gi2-gian5 kap siong-tiuN5 be2-be7 e5 kang-ku7 na7-tiaN7.

	Chit kui2-ni5 lai5, pun2-thou2 i3-sek e5 kak-chheN2, Tai5-gi2 chiah chiam7-chiam7 khah siu7 tiong7-si7, m7-na7 goa7-kok-lang5 lai5 Tai5-oan5 siuN7 beh oh8 Tai5-gi2. Lian5 kong-si teng-kiu5 jin5-chai5 ma7 iau3-kiu5 ai3 e7-hiau2 kong2 Tai5-gi2. Tai5-gi2 keng3-jian5 chiaN5-cho3 kiu5-chit e5 pit-su e5 tiau5-kiaN7 chi-it. Sim7-chi2 be7-hiau2 Tai5-gi2 e5 hak8-hau7 lau7-su (goa7-seng2 te7-ji7-tai7) ma7 lai5 oh8 Tai5-gi2. In e5 bok8-tek si7 beh kap hak8-seng kau-thong. Che kap hit tong-si5 ti7 hak8-hau7 kong2 Tai5-gi2 hou7 lang5 ta2-ap e5 cheng5-heng5 lai5 pi2, chin-chiaN3 si7 bo5 kang7-khoan2.

	In-ui7 Tai5-gi2 tng5-ki5 siu7 tioh8 ta2-ap, cha2-ki5 gian2-kiu3 Tai5-gi2 bun5-hak8 e5 lang5, cha2 to7 bo5-ti7 se3-kan--lah. Au7-ki5 u7 sim beh gian2-kiu3 Tai5-gi2 Bun5-ji7,  Bun5-hak8 e5 lang5, in-ui7 siu7 tioh8 goa7-chok8 e5 thong2-ti7, chit8-koa2 hak8-chia2, choan-ka ma7 m7-kaN2 siuN beng5-hian2 e5 gian2-kiu3. Kan-ta eng7 su-jin5 e5 heng3-chhu3 siu-chip8 cheng5-jin5 lau5--loh8--lai5 e5 chu-liau7 koh cheng2-li2--chit8--e5, ke ko3-jin5 e5 koan-tiam2. Sou2-i2 ti7 bok8-cheng5 chiaN5-cho3 chin che7 phai3-he7. Cheng3-hu2 iau2 bo5 siuN7 beh toa3 tai7-hak8 siat Tai5-oan5 Bun5-hak8-he7, thong2-it kok hak8-chia2, choan-ka e5 koan-tiam2 kap khoaN3-hoat. Sou2-i2 ti7 it-poaN-lang5 e5 koan-liam7--nih, Tai5-gi2 kan-ta u7 im bo5 ji7. Si7 ti7 a-bu2 e5 sin-piN oh8--tioh8..e5. Bok8-koai3 peng5-iu2 na7 mng7 goa2 ti7 to2-ui7 kang-chok. Goa2 in3 kong2 ti7 "Tiong-hoa5 Gi2-bun5 Gian2-sip8 Tiong-sim" ka3 goa7-kok-a2 kong2 Tai5-gi2. In long2 chin ki5-koai3 e5 piau2-cheng5 kong2 "Tai5-gi beh an3-choaN2 ka3? 
 
Che ia7 bok8-koai3--lah. Ti7 Tai5-oan5 toa7-han3, siu7 kau3-iok8 e5 lang5, ti7 hak8-hau7 sou2 oh8--e5 si7 Pak-kiaN-oe7. Oe7-tit-thang eng7 ㄅㄆㄇㄈ oh8, oe7-tit-thang eng7 han3-ji7 oh8. M7-ku2 Tai5-gi2--leh? Tai5-gi2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 K.M.T.(Kok-bin5-tong2) kong2 si7 "hong-gian5" si7 pe7-bu2-oe7, si7 ti7 a-bu2 e5 sin-piN oh8--tioh8--e5. Hak8-hau7 bo5 chit-khoan2 kho3-teng5. It-poaN-lang5 tui3 "ka3 goa7-kok-lang5 kong2 Tai5-gi2", chit e5 khang-khoe3 e5 hoan2-eng3 si7 kam2-kak chin ki5-koai3 koh chin chhu3-bi7.
	Oh8 Tai5-gi2 e5 goa7-kok jin5-su7, oe7-tit-thang hun Tang-A jin5-su7 (u7 siu7 han3-bun5-hoa3 eng2-hiong2, bat Han3-ji7) chhin-chhiuN7 Jit8-pun2-lang5, Han5-kok-lang5, Hiong-kang2-lang5. Au-Bi2 jin5-su7, oan5-choan5 m7-bat Han3-ji7. In-ui7 Tai5-gi2 hoat-im be7-tit-thang eng7 ㄅㄆㄇㄈoh8, be7 tit thang eng7 Han3-ji7 oh8, sou2-i2 hoat-im e5 kai-toaN7, goan2 it-lut8 chhai2-iong7 "Tai5-Lo5" (a.k.a. Kau3-hoe7 Peh8-oe7-ji7, ia7 chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5-ji7) ka3. An2-ne goan2 hoat-kian3 Au-Bi2 jin5-su7 oh8--khi2--lai5 khah khoai3, (peh-im kap pian3-im bo5 kong2). Tang-A hak8-seng iong7 Tai5-Lo5 oh8, hoan2-eng3 khah ban7. U7-si5 chhin-chhiuN7 u7 chho3-chek-kam2, kau3 te7-ji7 kai-toaN7, khai-si2 oh8 seng-oah8 hoe7-oe7 e5 si5, u7 nng7-chiong2 kau3-chhai5; chit8-chiong2 si7 Tai5-Lo5 kap Eng-bun5 tui3-chiau3..e5, si7 hou7 Au-Bi2 jin5-su7 oh8..e5. Leng7-goa7 chit8-chiong2 si7 Tai5-Lo5 kap Han3-ji7 tui3-chiau3..e5, si7 hou7 u7 Han3-ji7 ki-chhou2 e5 goa7-chek jin5-su7 kap goa7-seng2-lang5 oh8--e5. Chit nng7-khoan2 bun5-hoa3 poe7-keng2 oan5-choan5 bo5 kang7 e5 lang5 oh8 Tai5-gi2, iong7 Tai5-Lo5 oh8 e5 Au-Bi2 jin5-su7, khah khoai3 chiap-siu7. Goan2 hou7 in peh-im kap pian3-im e5 goan5-chek, chiu7 e7-tit-thang kong2 kau3 chin sun7. Tian-to3 u7 Han3-ji7 ki-chhou2 e5 lang5 be7 koan3-si3 khoaN3 Tai5-Lo5, ong2-ong2 ai3 chioh Han3-ji7 lai5 poe7 Tai5-gi2 e5 hoat-im. Chit-khoan2 oh8-hoat chiu7 khah khun3-lan5. Ji5-chhiaN3 Tai5-gi2 Han3-ji7 iau2-boe7  piau-chun2-hoa3, tiaN7-tiaN7 Tai5-gi2 Han3-ji7 kap Pak-kiaN-gi2 Han3-ji7 lam7 cho3-hoe2, eng2-hiong2 hak8-sip8 hau7-lut8, chhin-chhiuN7 kong2: Tai5-gi2 kong2 "kiaN5" si7 Pak-kiaN-gi2 "走" e5 i3-su3, m7-ku2 Tai5-gi2 Han3-ji7 sia2 "行". Na7-si7 Tai5-gi2 Han3-ji7 "走" si7 chau2 e5 i3-su3, a2-si7 li7-khui e5 i3-su3. Ji5-chhiaN2 Han3-ji7 na7 eng7 Tai5-gi2 lai5 thak8, u7-si5 u7 nng7-khoan2 e5 hoat-im. Chit8-khoan2 si7 "thok8-im". Leng7-goa7 chit8-khoan2 si7 "oe7-im". Phi2-ju5 kong2 "thok8-but8 cheng-soan2" kap "thak8 chheh " kang7 khoan2 sia2 "讀" ji7, m7-ku2 hoat-im bo5 kang7-khoan2, "tai7 hak8" kap "oh8 Tai5-gi2" kang7-khoan2 sia2 "學" ji7, hoat-im ma7 bo5 kang7-khoan2. 


Tai7-jin5" kap "toa7-lang5" kang7-khoan2 sia2 "大" ji7, m7-na7 hoat-im bo5 kang7, i3-su3 ia7 oan5-choan5 bo5 kang7. Hoat "thok8-im" e5 si5, si7 koan7-koaN, keng2-chhat e5 i3-su3. Hoat "oe7-im" e5 si5 si7 seng5-jin5 e5 i3-su3. Au7-Bi2 e5 hak8-seng (thoan5-kau3-su) oh8 kau3 bou2-chit8-e5 kai-toaN7 e5 Tai5-gi2, e7 oh8 chit8 koa2 Han3-ji7, che kap oh8 Pak-kiaN-gi2 e5 hak8-seng oh8 Han3-ji7 lai5 pi2, ke chiok khun3-lan5. In-ui7 Tai5-gi2 e5 hak8-seng ai3 ke poe7 chit8-chiong2 hoat-im. I2-cheng5 lai5 Tai5-oan5 oh8 Tai5-gi2 e5 hak8-seng toa7-pou7-hun7 si7 thoan5-kau3-su khah che7. Sou2-i2 tu5-liau2 oh8 it-poaN e5 oe7 i2-goa7, koh ai3 oh8 chong-kau3 e5 kho3-teng5. Che tui3 it-poaN e5 lang5 lai5 kong2 si7 khah bo5 sek-hap8. Tui3 siuN7 beh ke3-siok8 oh8 Tai5-gi2 e5 lang5 lai5 kong2 u7 hoat-seng kau3-chhai5 e5 khiam3-khoeh kap tng7-cham7. An2-ne e5 si5, goan2 long2 kou2-le7 hak8-seng kau Tai5-oan5 peng5-iu2, khah chiap8 thiaN La-ji2-ouh, khah chiap8 khoaN3 Tian7-si7--leh. M7-ku2 tau3-te2 ia7-si7 iu2-han7. In-ui7 Tai5-gi2 chiat-bok8 pit-keng3 iau2 bo5 chin che7.

	Tai5-oan5 kap Jit8-pun2 e5 ian5-kou3 u7 50 ni5 ku2. Gi2-gian5 kap bun5-hoa3 ia7 ke-kiam2 siu7 eng2-hiong2. Bok8-cheng5 chit8-koa2 khah che7 hoe3 e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 sou2 kong2 e5 Tai5-gi2 iau2 tiaN7-tiaN7 ian5-iong7 Jit8-pun2 e5 Han3-ji7. Chhin-chhiuN7 "pian7-tong", "lip8-tiuN5", "tou-hap8", "chhut-tiuN" ma7 u7 theh8 chit8-koa2 Jit8-pun2 e5 goa7-lai5-gi2 chiaN5-cho3 ka-ki7 e5 gi2-gian5. Chhin-chhiuN7 "la-ji2-ouh", "ou-tou2-bai2", "a-phah-touh". Tai5-gi2 kap Pak-kiaN-gi2 e5 koan-he7 ma7 chin chhim. Tai5-gi2 e5 to3-peng2 chiaN5-cho3 Pak-kiaN-gi2 e5 i3-su3, chhin-chhiuN7 "lang5-kheh" si7 "客人" e5 i3-su3. "Hong-thai" si7 "颱風" e5 i3-su3. "Siau7-kai3" si7 "介紹" e5 i3-su3. "I2-toh" si7 "桌椅" e5 i3-su3, "chhun3-chhioh" si7 "尺寸" e5 i3-su3, chin che7--lah.
	Ka3 Tai5-gi2 chin3-cheng5, goa2 m7 chai-iaN7 Tai5-gi2 u7 peh-im. Ma7 m7 chai-iaN2 ai3 pian3-im. Kan-ta chai-iaN2 si7 Ma-ma ka3--goa2--e5, bo5 iong7 Han3-ji7 ka3, ma7 bo5 iong7 Lo5-ma2-ji7 ka3. Chit-ma2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 chin che7 ka-tiuN2 u7 chit8-chiong2 bo5 cheng3-siong5 e5 sim-thai3. Ti7 chhu3--nih jit8-siong5 seng-oah8 kap gin2-a2 kong2-oe7 e5 si5 khui-chhui3 hap8-chhui3 long2 si7 Pak-kiaN-gi2. Che peng7 m7-si7 pai5-thek kong2 Pak-kiaN-gi2. Si7 in-ui7 an2-ne, pe7-bu2-oe7 soa3 chiam7-chiam7 teh siau-sit. U7 e5 koh kip teh sang3 gin2-a2 khi3 oh8 Eng-gi2. Ti7 Tai5-oan5, goa2 siuN7 oh8 goa7-gi2 bo5 sim2-mih8 hau7, in-ui7 bo5 khoan5-keng2.
	Si3-chap8 kui2 ni5 lai5, Tai5-oan5 ti7 cheng3-hu2 tai7-lek8 thui-heng5 kong2 Pak-kiaN-gi2 cheng3-chhek e5 kau3-iok8 e5 e7-bin7, e7-hiau2 kong2 Pak-kiaN-gi2 e5 lang5, i2-keng chin phou2-phian3. M7-ku2 u7 chit8-ho2 to bo5 nng7-ho2. Pe7-bu2-oe7 chiam7-chiam7 teh  
siau-sit. Sou2-i2 goa2 hi-bang7 cheng3-hu2 e7-tang3 kou2-le7 kong2 pe7-bu2-oe7. Chhin-chhiuN7 hit tong-si5 teh thui-heng5 Pak-kiaN-gi2 kang7-khoan2. M7-na7 si7 Tai5-gi2, Kheh-oe7, Goan5-chu7-bin5-oe7 ma7 kang7-khoan2. In-ui7 pit-keng3 gi2-gian5 si7 bun5-hoa3 e5 kin, gi2-gian5 na7 bo5, bun5-hoa3 chiu7 e7 siau-sit. Gi2-gian5 e5 thoan5-siok8 si7 po2-chun5 bun5-hoa3 e5 ki-pun2 iau3-kiaN7.
