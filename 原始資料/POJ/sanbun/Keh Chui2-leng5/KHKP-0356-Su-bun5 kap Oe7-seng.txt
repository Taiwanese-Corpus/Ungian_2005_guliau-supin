
Su-bun5 kap Oe7-seng
Si5-se3 pian3-chhian, bun5-hoa3 jit8-jit8 chin3-pou7; Tai5-oan5 e5 jin5-cheng5 hong-siok8, to7-lou7, chhi7-khu, kap ji7-tsap8 ni5 cheng5 toa7 koh-iuN7.  Ki5-tiong toa7 chin-pou7 chiu7-si7 su-bun5 kap oe7-seng.  Peh-siN3siu7 bun5-beng5 e5 kau3-hoa3 na2 kau3, bun5-beng5 e5 te7-ui7 chiu7 oe7-seng na2 bat, iah8 cheng3-hu2 oe7-seng chin giam5-tiong7.  TaN ho2-taN2 eng7 chit-e5 tai7-liok8 lai5 gi7-lun7.
Lun7 su-bun5 u7 lai7-goa7 e5 hun-piat; lais7? hak8-bun7, goa7 si7 goa7-koan.  Ku7-chheng e5 si5-tai7 chiong Han3-hak8 e5 ju5-chia2 kio3 tsoe3 su-bun5, hian7-si5 chiu7 boe7-kham--tit kong2, chhiaN2 chiong hak8-bun7 he7-teh,bian2-lun7, lai5 lun7 goa7-koan e5 su-bun5.
1.Gian5-gu2 se3-kai3-tiong si7 Tai5-oanl5ang e5 chhou-chhui3 phaiN2 oe7 thang chhui tsoe3 te7-it phaiN2.  Ti7 kong2 oe7 e5 si5, hoaN-hi2e si5, siu7-khi3 e5 si5, khau2-thau5-gu2 long2 si7 phaiN2-oe7; tam5-lun7 e5 si5 toa7-siaN soan-jiong2; bo5 theng3-hau7 pat8 lang5 kong2-liauchi2u chin3-cheng5 kong2, che si7 phaiN2 sip8-koan3 bo5 su-bun5.  Po2-lo5 kong2, seng3-tou5 bok5ong kan-im5 phu5-phio3 hi3-hiauh e5 oe7 si7 hap8-gi5.  (I-hut-sou2 5 : 4, Ma2-kho2 7 : 15).
2.I-koan che si7 goa7-koan e5 iau3-kin2.  Lang5 kong2, "Sian keng3 i-koan, hou7 kengj3in."  Chheng7-saN tioh8 u7 phoe3-hap8 sin-hun7 tsu-keh, tsai5-san2.  M7-thang chhengk7au hou7 lang5 khoaN3 gong7-ngiah8.  U7 chit8 khoan2 chhin-chhai2 chheng7, na7-si7 chian2-hoat lai5 kong2, saN-khou3 chhin-chhai2 kou2-but8-chiong boe2 lai5 chheng7, bo5 hap8-sin.  U7-e5 iuN5-hok8 hu3-siok8-phin2 koa3 m7-tioh8, e-lih, kha-hu2-suh koa3 kau3 chin ou iau2 teheng7.  U7-e5 bo5 ut, bo5 chheng-khi3, khi3 pat8 lang5 tau, iah8 si7 ti7 cheng3-lang5 etiong5-kan ian2-soat, i-chiuN5 chhin-chhai2, sit-le2 bo5 su-bun5.
Koh chit8 khoan2 chheng7 siuN koe3-thaui5-chiuN5 chhou2-chhou2, tiong-teng2 lang5 chheng7 siong7-teng2 saN e5 taN2-pan7 bo5 hap8 sin-hun7, tsoan5 ke-giap8 he7 ti7 sin-khu, souj2¡Kp put-kiong sou2-chhut, chhut-mng5 na2 koaN-ka, giap8-hou7, che si7 tsng-tsoe3, boe7 kong2 si7 su-bun5.
Koh chit8 khoan2 chheng7 saN tioh8 tek-tiong, pou3 bian2 chin tat8-chiN5, iau3-kinti2oh chiau3 si5-che3 tsoe3 hah8-sin, tak8 hang tioh8 chheng-khi3, tioh8 chheng7 khah sou3,che  chiu7-si7 chiau3 su-bun5.
3.Le2-sou3.  Tui3 koaN-hu2 tui3 si7-toa7 tui3 peng5-iu2 m7-thang bo5-le2.  Tsong2-si7 le2-sou3 iah8 m7-thang siuN tsoe7. "Le2-to pit-tsa3."Tsu7-chheng7 siuN pi-bi5 hou7 lang5 khoaN3 tsoe3 ha7-teng2, chheng pat8 lang5 siuN koaiN5 piN7-tsoe3 thiam2-bi7 .  Sou2-i2 le2-sou3 tioh8 tek-tiong, keng2 hian7-si5 Tai5-oan5 sou2 eng7 tu2-ho2 e5 le2-sou3 lai5 eng7, hek8-si7 seh-sia7 thim3-thau5, sou2 u7 tak8 hang7 e5 le2.
Oe7-seng si7 chiau3-kou3 hou7 lang5 oe7 iong2-kiaN7, iau3-kin2 tak8 hang7 tioh8 chheng-khi3,chheng-khi3 si7 eng5-kng, la5-sam5 si7 kian3-siau3.
1.Sin-the2 tioh8 siong2 soe2, chiah khah boe7 phoa3-piN7.  Lai7-saN tioh8 siong5 oaN7,thau5-mng5 m7-thang boah-iu5, na7 eng7 ngi7 e5 thau5-mng5-bin2, siong5 bin2, thau5-mng5 chiu7 nng2.
2. Khun3 si7 oe7-seng e5 toa7 iau3-kin2.  Lang5 poaN3-se3 tsoe3 kang poaN3-se3 tioh8 khun3.  Khun3 chhiong-chiok lang5 chiu7 khah iong2-kiaN7, sim hoaN-hi2, gau5 thak8-chheh, cheng-sin5 pa2-chiok.  Khun3 bo5 kau3-giah8 bin5, chiu7 khah oe7 phoa3-piN7 e5 khoan2, sim bo5 peng5-an.  Tioh8 khun3 joa7-ku2 ki3 ti7 e7-toe2 :--
Gin2-na2 4 he3 tioh8 khun3 12 tiam2 cheng.
Gin2-na2 7 he3 tioh8 khun3 11 tiam2 cheng.
Gin2-na2 8-9 he3 tioh8 khun3 10 tiam2-poaN3 cheng.
Gin2-na2 10-11 he3 tioh8 khun3 10 tiam2 cheng.
Gin2-na2 12 he3 tioh8 khun3 9 tiam2 cheng.
Toa7 lang5 tioh8 khun3 8 tiam2 cheng.
Khun3 e5 si5 m7-thang eng7 phe7 kham3-bin7 chiah oe7 siu7 ho2 khong-khi3.  Chim2-thau5 m7-thang eng7 siuN-koaiN5, thau5-khak na7 khah koaiN5 heng-kham2, huih khah oh-tit thau3 kau3 thau5-lo2.  Kun7-lai5 khah ai3 eng7 mng5 e5 chim2-thau5, iah8 u7 lang5 thoan3-lian7 bo5 eng7 chim2-thau5.  Sou2-i2 khun3 m7-thang m7 kin2-sin7.
3.Lim-chiah8 tak8-hang7 mih8 tioh8 u7 chheng-khi3 chiah thang chiah8.  Chiau3 hian7-si5 teh khe-kho2 u7 chin tsoe7 khoan2 e5 piN7 tui3 chiah8 lai5 thoan3-jiam2, sou2-i2 tioh8 soe2 chheng-khi3, tsu2-sek8 tioh8 kham3, hi5-lui7 bah-lui7 ke3-lau5 bo5 chhiN m7-thang chiah8.
4.khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-tsai7 thiaN-tng5 tioh8 siong5-siong5 chheng-kiat,  chhu3-teng2 thou5-kha tioh8 sau3-tu5, tak8-hang7 tioh8 chheng2 chhit, i2-toh tioh8 soe2 tioh8 chheng2; pang5-keng chhng5-e7 tioh8 chheng-khi3.  Phe7-chhioh8 tioh8 siong5 phak8, tioh8 hou7 khong-khi3 jit8-kng oek7au, chiu7 bo5 phaiN2-bi7, iah8 oe7 siau-tok8.  Tsui2-kau tioh8 thong-liu5 m7-thang chek-ti3.  Pian7-sou2 tioh8 siong5 piaN3 chheng-khi3, chhi7 cheng-siN e5 sou2-tsai7 tioh8 li7 khah hng7, iau3-kin2 tioh8 bo5 gai7 oe7-seng.  TaN lun7 oe7-seng e5 su7 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 tioh8 khah-iaN5 se3-siok8 lang5.  U7 khoaN3-kiN3 esin5-the2 na7 boe7 hiau2 chheng-khi3, beh-thai3-oe7 eng7 chheng-khi3 e5 sim-sin5 lai5 hok8-sai7 seng3-kiat e5 Siong7-te3 ? An2-nisu-bun5 oe7-seng si7 jit8-jit8 iau3-kin2.
(Keh Tsui2-leng5).
