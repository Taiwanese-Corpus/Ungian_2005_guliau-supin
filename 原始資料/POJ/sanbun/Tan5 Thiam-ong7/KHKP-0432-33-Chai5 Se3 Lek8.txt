 1. Chai5 :  Chiu7-si7 chiN5-chai5.  Che si7 tak8 lang5 sou2 khiam3-eng7, hou7 lang5 siu-jip8 eng7-chhut e5 pin5-ku3.  Kou2-cha2 si7 eng7 mih8 oaN7 mih8.  Hian7-kim chhiN-hoan ia7 u7. Seng3-keng kong2, ChiN5-chai5 si7 Siong7-te3 sou2 siuN2-su3, tioh8-boa5 lai5 tit-tioh8, chiah an- lok8 hiong2-siu7.  Lang5 tak8 hang7 ai3 eng7 ke3- chhek lai5 tou5-bou5 than3 chiN5, chong2-si7 kun- chu2 ai3 chai5, chhu2 chi iu2-to7.  Seng3-keng kong2, Tham-chai5 si7 ban7-ok e5 kun-goan5. KhoaN3 chit-tiap8 sia7-hoe7 u7 chin choe7 khoan2.  U7-e5 song3-hiong lai5 thiam2-bi7 lang5, u7-e5 choe3 ha7-chian7 ti3-kau3 hoan7-choe7, ia7 u7 kau-thok su7-giap8 chiu7 siu7-iu7, ia7 siu7-thok pan7 su7 chit8-sut-a2 koan5 chiu7 siu7 mai2- chiok, u7 choe3 toa7-koaN soe3-koaN ia7 siu7-iu7 put-siau2, soa3 liau2 koaN, pai7 mia5 si7 chin choe7.  U7-e5 kau3 ho2-giah8 ai3 eng7 gun5-hun2 siN7 si2 lang5 : chhin-chhiuN7 Khap-ban7 ai3 hai7 Bok8-ti2-kai2 kap Iu5-tai7-chok8, goan7 eng7 i e5 chiN5 lap8-jip8 kok-khou3.  M7-ku2 lou7- be2 hai7 ka-ki7. 
Hian7-kim ia7 u7 eng7 chiN5 e5 se3-thau5 lai5 khi-bu2 lang5, hai7 lang5.  U7-e5 lam7-sam2 eng7, phiau5, tou2, im2, hai7 mia7, hai7 seng-khu. Che long2 si7 sun2-hai7 kong2 boe7 liau2. Seng3-keng kong2, Siong7-te3 siuN2-su3 lang5 chiN5-chai5, ai3 lang5 gau5 eng7. Ia5-sou kong2, Siong7-te3 kok kap I e5 gi7 tioh8 kiu5, chiu7 chiah e5 mih8 chu7-jian5 ke hou7 lin2. An2-ni chiN5-chai5 si7 lang5 sou2 ai3, na7 bo5 chim-chiok siong5-siong5 oe7 hoan7-choe7. 2.Se3 :  chiu7-si7 u7 koan5-se3.  Che ia7 si7 bo5 han7.  Tak8 lang5 sou2 tioh8 u7-e5.  Tai7- tiong7-hu m7 thang chit8 si5 bo5 koan5, chong2-si7 tioh8 u7 chun2-chat, che3-han7 kap khoan-iong5.  Po2-lo5 kong2, Goa2 u7 koan5, chong2-si7 goa2 m7 eng7 i; koh kong2, Siong7- te3 siuN2-su3 goa2 u7 koan5, m7 kaN2 lam7-sam2 eng7, hoan2-tng2 ai3 pang-chan7 lang5.  Na7 oe7 kau3 an2-ni u7 koan5 si7 chin ho2, bian2 kiaN gou7-su7, hoan2-tng2 u7 li7-ek lang5 e5 sou2- chai7.  Ia5-sou e5 hak8-seng khoaN3-kiN3  in Sian-siN hiah u7 koan5, chit8 pai2 khoaN3 lang5 m7 hoan2-hoe2, kio3 I tui3 thiN kiu5 he2 lai5 sio si2 hiah e5 lang5.  Ia5-sou bo5 an2-ni, I u7 toa7 lin5-bin2 e5 sim.  Sai-kong Se-bun5 khoaN3 Su3-tou5 u7 koan5, chhiu2 hoaN7 chiu7 siu7 Seng3 Sin5, ai3 eng7 chiN5 boe2 hit e5 koan5. Pi2-tek kong2, ChiN5 kap li2 long2 tioh8 biat8- bo5, li2 phah-sng3 Siong7-te3 e5 siuN2-su3 oe7 eng7 chiN5 boe2 mah ? Hiah e5 lang5 khoaN3 Ia5-sou kiaN5 sin5-jiah, chiu7 mng7 : Li2 eng7 sim2-mih8 koan5 kiaN5 an2-ni, chi7-chui7 siuN2- su3 li2 chit e5 koan5 ah ? An2-ni thang chai, lang5 na7 khoaN3-kiN3 u7 koan5 chin ai3, m7-ku2 u7 si5 oe7 eng7 chho3, chhin-chhiuN7 So3-lo5 be7 hoan2-hoe2 e5 si5, chioh kong-hoe7 e5 koan5 hai7 kau3-hoe7, sou2-i2 au7-lai5 chhim-chhim hoan2-hoe2 i2-cheng5 e5 m7-tioh8, m7 kaN2 eng7 i e5 koan5.  
Lun7 Se3, hian7-si5 lang5 chin choe7 lam7-sam2 eng7.  Cha2 A-khap ong5 chia3-koan5 chiam3 sian7-liong5-bin5 Na2-pok8 e5 hng5, Siong7-te3 beng7-leng7 sian-toi chiu3-chou2 i, hou7 i lau5 huih lai5 si2.  Tai7-pit8 ong5 chia3-se3 chiam3 chin7-tiong peng Ou-li7-a e5 bou2.  Siong7- te3 beng7-leng7 sian-ti chiu3-chou2 i, au7-lai5 chai-e7 bo5 li7 i e5 ke.  I2-li7 nng7 e5 kiaN2 kho3 in lau7-pe7 choe3 che3-si, tiam3 tian7-lai7 lam7- sam2 choe3, ti3-kau3 long2 biat8-bo5.  Siok8- gu2 kong2, Kho3 se3 hou7 se3 gou7, chit8 tai7 phiN lang5, tioh8 saN-tai7 hou7 lang5 phiN.  An2-ni sui-jian5 u7 se3, tioh8 gau5 pang-chan7 lang5, m7 thang khi-bu2 lang5, hiap8-che3 lang5, chiah u7 hap8 thiN-li2.  Na7 chia3 koan5-se3, put-lun7 ke, a2-si7 kok long2 oe7-pai7. An2-ni Siong7-te3 su3 lang5 chai5-ti3 bo5 piN5- piN5, u7 koan5-se3-e5, tioh8 khoan-iong5 pang-chan7 nng2-chiaN2-e5; hu5-chhi5 i, khan-kiu3 i, the2-thiap i, chai5-poe5 i, chiah u7 chiau3 liong5-sim hap8 thiN-li2.  
3.  Lek8 : Chiu7-si7 khui3-lat8, iong2-kam2. Chit hang7 tioh8 tak8 lang5 u7, m7-ku2 toa7 soe3 si7 bo5 piN5.  Cha2 u7 bu2-chiong7-goan5, si7 ai3 kiu3-ka3 po2-hou7 kun-ong5. Chiau3 sou2 khoaN3-kiN3, ia7 chham-kho2 ke3- sin kap kun7-tai7 i2-kip8 bak8-cheng5 e5 su7, na7 eng7 chit saN hang7 gou7-eng7 e5 lang5 sit8-chai7 u7 toa7 sun2-hai7 lah. Tai7-pit8 be7 chiap ong5 ui7 e5 si5, u7 ho2 khoan2-thai7 hou7 i hai7 e5 tui3-tek8, chiu7-si7 So3-lo5 ong5.
