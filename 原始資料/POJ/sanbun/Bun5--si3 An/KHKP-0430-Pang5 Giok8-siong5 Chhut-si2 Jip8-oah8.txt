Pang5 Giok8-siong5 Chhut-si2 Jip8-oah8
Bun5--si7 An  
Pang5 Giok8-siong5 si7 hian7-si5 Chi-na2 chhut-mia5 bun5-beng5 e5 chiong-kun.  I i2-keng sin3 Chu2; e7-te2 hit chan5 su7 long2 si7 pun2-sin sou2 kan-cheng3--e5.
Chin kho2-sioh goa2 bo5 chhut-si3 ti7 sin3 Chu2 e5 ke, tui3 se3-han3 chiu7 oan3-hin7 to7-li2.  Ti7 1900 ni5 hit-tiap8 goa2 tng peng, Gi7-ho5-thoan5 hui2-lui7 khi2 jiau2-loan7, in chin hiong-ok ai3 biat8-bo5 Ki-tok kau3.  KhoaN3-tioh8 in hiah chan5-jim2 thai5-si2 goa7-kok e5 bok8-su, i-seng, sian-siN niu5, kou-niu5 i2-kip8 pun2-te7 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5, goa2 be7 bian2-tit the2-thiap in e5 chhi-chham2, chong2-si7 nge7-sim m7 ai3 hang5-hok8 Siong7-te3.
Tng 1901 ni5 goa2 kau3 Po2-teng7 Hu2, u7 chit8 jit8 jip8 pai3-tng5, thiaN goa7-kok bok8-su teh lun7 ma2-thai3 5:39, 40.  Le2-pai3 soah, goa2 chek-si5 chiong chit8 te3 toh poe7 ti7 keng-thau5 teng2 beh chhut-khi3.  Bok8-su teh mng7, ¡§ Li2 siaN2-su7 gia5 goa2 e5 toh?¡¨  goa2 in3 i kong2, ¡§ Chiau3 li2 sou2 thoan5 e5 to7-li2, m7 na7 chit te3 toh, i2-liau5 ia7 tioh8 soa3 hou7 goa2.¡¨
Ti7 1906 ni5 goa2 seng cho3 siau3-ui3 hit-tiap8 hou7 tok8 thang5 chhioh-tioh8, pak-tou2 piN siN-thiaN3, chiu7 hou7 pun2-te7 sian-siN khoaN3, giam7-liau2 in kong2 tioh8 kou5 nng7 goeh8 jit8 chiah e7 ho2.  Goa2 chai in kan-ta it-tioh8 goa2 e5 gin5, chin siu7-khi3 m7 chhap i.  U7 lang5 po3 Pak-kiaN siaN5-goa7 u7 se-kok i-koan2, goa2 khi3 hia hou7 kui2-na7 ui7 e5 i-seng giam7, chiah ka7 goa2 kong2, ¡§Li2 na7 lai5 jip8 iN7, nng7 le2-pai3 ku2 chiu7 e7 ho2.¡¨  I-seng chin sio2 sim, chit8 jit8 nng7 pai2 kou5 ioh8, pun2-te7 sian-siN iah8 put-si5 lai5 sun5 chin gau5 the2-thaip.
I2-keng taN2-tiap8 ho2, goa2 chin ai3 be2 ti-bah kap ho2 chiu2 lai5 sang3 i-seng, ka7 i 
seh-sia7.  I-seng m7 chun2, kong2 m7 bian2 tui3 in sia7 in, hoan2-tng2 tioh8 siau3-liam7 tok8-it chin e5 Siong7-te3 e5 toa7 thiaN3-thang3, chai I e koan5 i-ho2 goa2 e5 peN7.  ThiaN hit ku3 sim siu7 kam2-kek, m7 kaN2 chiau3 ku7 oan3-hin7 to7-li2.  Chit-tiap8 iah8 bat siuN7 Siong7-te3 e5 su7.
Tng 1909 ni5 goa2 oaN7-khi3 Tang-peng5 seng2 chat-iaN5 ti7 Sin-bin5 Hu2, hit ni5 siaN5-lai7 khi2 un-ek8; goa2 tauh8-tauh8 chhoa7 peng khi3 peN7-iN7.  Hit e5 i-seng khou2-khng3 goa2 tioh8 hou7 i chu3-sia7, na7 bo5 kiaN-liau2 pun2-sin jiam2-tioh8 tok8.  Chu3-sia7 liau2 goa2 mng7 sian-siN le2 tioh8 loa7-che7?  I in3 kong2, ¡§ Long2 m7 bian2 chiN5, iau2 chit8 hang7 koh khah iau3-kin2, li2 tioh8 e7 ki3-tit thiN-nih8 u7 chit8 e5 Siong7-te3 chin thiaN3 li2.¡¨  Chit ku3 si7 tu2-ho2 cheng5 jit8 pat8 ui7 e5 sian-siN iu5-goan5 tui3 goa2 kong2 an2-ni.  Che koh chit8 pai2 thiaN-tioh8 si7 hou7 goa2 oan3-hin7 e5 sim na2 siau-tu5.
Ti7 1911 ni5 Tiong-kok khi2 kek-beng7, goa2 hit si5 ti7 Lam5-chian5, ti7-hia hiam2-hiam2 song3-sit siN3-mia7.  Au7-lai5 khi3 Pak-kiaN cho3 hu3-chiong3, u7 chit8 jit8 ti7 ke-lou7 teh kiaN5, khoaN3 chit8 tiuN ko3-peh8 teh thong-ti bou2 jit8 u7 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 sian-siN Bok8-tek Phok-su7 beh khui ian2-soat-hoe7.  Siok8 chheng-lian5-hoe7 e5 lang5 teh pun toaN, soa3 chhiaN2 goa2 khi3 hu3.
Thau5 chit8 jit8 goa2 chiu7 che7 nng7 tiam2 cheng ku2, bin5-a2-chai3 koh khi3 chin ti3-i3 teh thiaN sian-siN sou2 thoan5 e5 to7-li2, thiaN-liau2 goa2 e5 sim na2 chhim sin3, be7 bian2-tit siuN7 sou2 po3 e5 Kiu3-chu2 chin u7 lat8 thang kiu3 goa2 pun2-sin kap kok-lai7 e5 peh-siN3.  Ka7 sian-siN thong-ek8-e5, si7 Ong5 Cheng3-teng5, i bat ti7 Pa-li2 Peng5-ho5-hoe7 cho3 ki3-su7.  Ian2-soat-hoe7 beng5-pek8, iau2-ku2 u7 ki-hoe7 thang koh kang2-kiu3 Ki-tok kau3 e5 su7, in-ui7 siaN5-lai7 u7 siat 24 ui7, chhiaN2 sian-siN pian7-pian7 thang kap lang5 chu7-chip8 tam5-lun7 to7-li2.  Goa2 hoaN-hi2 sun7 chit e5 ki-hoe7 kap 11 lang5 khi3 Ong5 Cheng3-teng5 e5 chhu3, ti7-hia sou2 seng5-siu7 e5 ka3-si7, chin u7 tit-tioh8 li7-ek.  Goa2 taN siong5-siong5 ai3 khi3 le2-pai3 hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, khah ban7 chiu7 beh jip8-kau3. 
Tu2 chit si5 goa2 e5 hiaN u7 put-ho5 e5 su7, hou7 goa2 toa7 siu7-khi3, na7 bo5 to7-li2 ti7 goa2 e5 
sim, su-iok8 un2-tang3 e7 khah-iaN5, chiu7 hit e5 tai7-chi3 e7 na2 tiN5 na2 hai7.  Ho2 kai-chai3 goa2 kho3 Chu2 chiu7 tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3.  Iau2-boe7 tit-kiu3 e5 si5 seng3-te7 chin phaiN2, kap ke-lai7 siong5-siong5 u7 su7; goa2 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 na7 sio2-khoa2 cheng-chha bo5 hah8 goa2 e5 i3, chiu7 hoat siu7-khi3 phah bou2-kiaN2. 
Tng hu3-siong3 e5 si5 iah8 si7 an2-ni, chhut-leng7 na7 bo5 liam5-piN kiaN5, goa2 chiu7 khi2 ok-sim, m7 na7 phah peng, lian5 kong3 goa2 e5 siok8-koaN ia7 kaN2.  Goa2 long2 bo5 the2-thiap sou2 koan2 e5 peng, in ia7 oan3-hin7 goa2, tng bin7 tui3 kiaN chiah than3 goa2 e5 chhui3, bo5 ti7-teh e5 si5, chiu7 ka7 goa2 chiu3-chou2.  Goa2 long2 bo5 chu5-pi lin5-bin2 lang5.
TaN hou7 Ia5-sou kiu3 e5 liau2-au7, Siong7-te3 e5 thiaN3 thang3 chhui-pek goa2, chiah ai3 khoan2-thai7 sou2 koan2-e5 chhin-chhiuN7 ka-ki7 e5 lang5. In na7 kiaN5 chho3, chiu7 eng7 un-jiu5 khou2-khng3 bian2-le7, hoaN-hi2 sia3-bian2 in e5 koe3-sit.  Khah siong5 chit ho7 e5 hong-hoat e7 iaN5 in e5 phaiN2, ti3-kau3 hoan7 sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 gui5-hiam2, kan-khou2 in kap goa2 tang5-sim hiap8-lek8.  Hian7-si5 goa2 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 ia7 kaN2 kan-cheng3 goa2 si7 na2 sin e5 lang5, tui3 goa2 hang5-hok8 Ia5-sou, long2 m7 bat khek-khui phah i.
Goa2 toa3 ti7 Lam5-chhiong hit-tiap8 cheng3-hu2 bo5 chiau3 kong-to7 khoan2-thai7 goa2, goa2 m7 kheng2 hang5-hok8 chiu7 ka7 goa2 si5.  Sou2 koan2 e5 peng chin m7 kam.  Koe3 kui2-na7 goeh8-jit8 TiuN Hun ai3 oaN7 Bin5-chu2 kok, koh keng2 Soan-thong2 cho3 hong5-te3.  Hit-tiap8 cheng5 sou2 koan2 e5 koaN kio3 goa2 tioh8 khi2 peng; goan2 chhut-chian3 soa3 thai5-iaN5, au7-lai5 po2-ku2 goa2 cho3 chiong-kun, cheng3-hu2 chiah phai3 goa2 khi3 Ou5-lam5 e5 Siong5-tek.
Sui-jian5 goa2 mia5 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5, iau2-ku2 ka-ki7 chai-iaN2 u7 che7-che7 hang7 kiaN5 bo5 kau3, be7 bian2-tit khoa3-lu7 sim bo5 kau3-giah8 jiat8 thang put-si5 po3-iong5 tit-kiu3 e5 to7-li2.
Siong5-tek siaN5 e5 bok8-su bok8-su-niu5 tiam3 siaN5-lai7 siong5-siong5 kap Pang5 Giok8-siong5 kau-poe5, chiah u7 sun7 chit si5 cho3 kan-cheng3 kong2, Lun7-kau3 Pang5 Giok8-siong5 goa7 phoe5, i e5 bin7-mau7 chin ho2 khoaN3, seng-khu lo3-lo3, koan5 lak8-chhioh it.  Sui-jian5 i si7 chin khaim-pi e5 khoan2-sit, iau2-ku2 cho3 chiong-kun u7 jin5-chai5, ui-gi5 gau5 tng i e5 chit.

I sin3 Chu2 e5 liau2-au7 tit8-tit8 chin3-pou7 thiN e5 lou7, kau3 1909 hit ni5 Siong7-te3 eng7 i chit8 ui7 e5 lou5-pok8 sou2 thoan5 e5 to7-li2 hou7 i koh-khah chai-iaN2 tit-kiu3 e5 chhim-i3, kap Seng3 Sin5 e5 chai5-leng5, i chiu7 na2 jiat8-sim.
Hian7-si5 chi2-u7 iau3-kin2 chit8 hang7, si7 ai3 I sou2 koan2-e5, bo5 lun7 peng-teng, a2-si7 hiah e5 siok8-koaN peN5-peN5 e7 bat Ia5-sou, lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  In long2 bo5 chiah8 hun, iah8 m7 bat lim chiu2, phaiN2-chhui3 ia7 m7 chun2, ti3-kau3 in e5 ho2 tek-heng7 si3-bin7 po3-iong5. 
Siong5-tek siaN5-lai7 pun2-jian5 ou-am3, hian7-si5 i5-sin.  Pang5 Giok8-siong5 koat-i3 ai3 tong3 lang5 khui phaiN2-kiaN2 keng, lian5 u7 sim2-mih8 put-hap8 e5 ang-a2, chiu7 liam5-piN thiah phoa3.  e-hng-si5 boe7 kau3 chap8 tiam2 cheng, ke-lou7 peng5-cheng7, siaN5-nih8 peh-siN3 an-un2 khia7-khi2.
U7 siat peng-teng e5 tou5-su koan2, lai7-bin7 u7 loa7-a2-che7 khoan2 e5 chheh kap sin-bun5, eng5 e5 si5, chhut-chai7 in thang tiam3 hia khoaN3.  Ia7 u7 ho2 chhit-tho5 e5 sou2-chai7, hou7 in chai7-tiuN5 tau3 chau2-lai5 chin7 phau2-chau2 e5 lou7-cham7, soa3 eng7 lat8 tau3 su-iaN5.
Iau2 u7 koh-khah ho2 e5 hoat-tou7 beh li7-ek hiah e5 khah lau7, tioh8-siong koh soe-lam2 e5 peng, kiaN-liau2 in au7-lai5 bo5 chiN5, e7 piN3 phaiN2 cho3 chhat8-hui2; sou2-i2 chit si5-i2 keng u7 khui chit8 keng hou7 in tak8 jit8 chit8 pou thang oh8 kui2-na7 khoan2 e5 kang-ge7, kau3 in bo5 tng peng e5 si5 chheng-eng5 u7 thang ka-ti7 than3 chiah8.  
Hit keng an-pai5 chin ho2-se3, lai7-bin7 chheng-khi-siuN3, sou2 siat tak8 hang7 chiau3 chhu3-su7.  Ti7-hia u7 100 goa7 tiuN chhiah-boeh8 e5 khi3-khu7; 30 goa7 tiuN e5 kui teh chit bin7-kin, 24 tiuN chhia-saN e5 khi3-khu7 thang cho3 peng e5 ho7 i.  Koh u7 in3-chheh koan2 teh be7 choa2 pit, ji7-phou7, hit ho7 hap8 lou7-eng7 e5 mih8-kiaN7.  Ia7 u7 soa3 siat chit8-ui7 lai5 cho3 tin5-i2, tin5-chhng5, kok hang7 cho3 pian7 chiu7 be7 peng khah chio2 chiN5.
Chit kui2-na7 ni5 e5 tiong-kan Pang Giok8-siong5 e5 siok8-koaN, kap peng-teng chheng- goa7 mia5 i2-keng nia2 siu7 se2-le2 jip8-kau3,chin jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2.  Gou7 keN 
e5 si5 chiu7 khi2-lai5, boe7 chiah8 png7 e7 thiaN-tioh8 in teh gim5-si kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.  Ti7 kun iaN5 moa2 si3-bin7 chin u7 hian2-chhut keng3-khian5 e5 sim.  Koh ti7 An-hioh-jit8 beh khi3 le2-pai3 tak-e5 gim7 Seng3-keng, u7 si5 kiaN5 lou7 ai3 gim5-si, bin7-mau7 ia7 put-chi2 chhio3-iong5. 
Sin-lek8 1919 ni5 Pang5 Chiong-kun chin ti3-i3 ai3 beh chhiaN2 chit8 ui7 e5 bok8-su khui hun3-heng-hoe7, hit kui2-jit8 lai7 tit-tioh8 hui-siong5 e5 ki-hoe7.  Sian-siN tak8 jit8 nng7 pou chu7-chip8 ian2-soat, chheng goa7 mia5 e5 peng kap siok8-koaN hoaN-hi2 lai5 hu3.
Bat chit8 jit8 Pang5 Chiong-kun teh ki5-to2 e5 si5, toa7 siau3-liam7 pun2-kok, the3 in kiu5 chin khun2-chhiat, soa3 lau5 bak8-sai2 thi5-khau3.  Hiah e5 siok8-koaN peng-teng kap i siong-sim tong3 be7 tiau5, che7-che7 khui siaN lian5-liang5 ki5-to2, ti3-kau3 u7 lau5 bak8-sai2, tang sim chit8 i3, ui7 tioh8 kok-ka khoa3-lu7, kiu5 Siong7-te3 lin5-bin2 peh-siN3.
Ni5-boe2 koh chhiaN2 bok8-su lian5-lian5 chap8 jit8 kap peng kang2-kiu3 Seng3-keng.  Chit8 pou Pang5 Chiong-kun pun2-sin cho3 chu2-li2, chit8 pou chhe i khah koan5 e5 siok8-koaN the3 i liau7-li2.
Hit e5 bok8-su chin siu7 i ho2 khoan2-thai7, u7 si5 chhiaN2 i kap i cho3 hoe2 chiah8, kau3 beh khi3 hit-tiap8, phai3 chit mia5 peng taN heng5-li2, koh chhe chit8 e5 koaN sang i kau3 loh8 chun5, soa3 ka7 i thiah toaN.
Lun7 kau3 Pang5 Giok8-siong5 sou2 kiaN5 e5 ho2 su7 beh li7-ek siaN5-lai7 e5 peh-siN3, si7 sng3 be7 liau2.  I ia7 si7 chin iau3-kin2 hiah e5 siok8-koaN e5 hu-jin5 u7 ki-hoe7 thang siu7 ka3-si7, lai5 bat Ia5-sou; sou2-i2 u7 si5 chhiaN2 bok8-su-niu5, kou-niu5 tiam3 in tiong-kan, in2-chhoa7 in khah bat to7-li2.  Ia7 u7 leng7-goa7 khui chit keng oh8, hou7 hiah e5 hu-jin5 thang bat ji7, soa3 lai5 kang2-kiu3 Seng3-keng.  Kin7-lai5 chap8-goa7 e5 nia2 se2-le2 jip8-kau3.
Teng2-bin7 sou2 ki3 chin e7 kam2-kek lang5 e5 sim.  Ti7 than3 to7 li2 e5 kok siong7-chhiaN2 m7 bat khoaN3 chit8 e5 chiong-kun kap i e5 peng hiah jiat8-sim, ho5-hong3 ti7 goa7-kok Pang5 Giok8-siong5 i2-goa7 m7 bat thiaN-tioh8.  
ChhiaN khoaN3 Po3 e5 lang5 tioh8 chin ti3-i3 the3 Pang5 Chiong-kun ki5-to2, bang7 Siong7-te3 eng7 i na2 ku2 e7 na2 li7-ek pun2 kok, soa3 in2-chhoa7 na2 che7 peng bat Ia5-sou.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 430 koan3, 1921 ni5 1 geh8)
