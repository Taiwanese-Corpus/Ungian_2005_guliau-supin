
Khit-chiah8

(Chu Kou-niu5 ek8)
U7 chit8 e5 goa7-kok lang5 toa3 ti7 Tiong-kok.  Bou2 jit8 u7 thiaN-kiN3 phah mng5 esiaN5, chhe i e5 chu2-chiah8 khi3 khoaN3 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5.  Chu2-chiah8 ka7 i kong2,Si7 chit8 e5 kiu5-khit e5 lang5, kong2 si7 beh go7 si2,	ai3 ka7 li2 tho2 mih8, goa2 thang hou7 i a2 m7 thang ?  Thau5-ke kong2,  Thang ah !  li2 kin-a2-jit8 u7 cho3 mi7-pau, theh8 chit8 pong7 hou7 i.  Chu2-chiah8 in3 i kong2, m7-ku2 lang5 chiah8 chit ho7 mi7-pau m7 koan3-si3, kiam2-chhai2 m7 chiah8, i khah ai3 chiah8 png7.  Thau5-ke kong2,  Chong2-si7 goa2 han2-tit chiah8 png7, sou2-i2 chit chun7 put-pian7 bo5 thang hou7 i.  I na7 beh go7 si2 chiu7 un2-tang3 ai3 chiah8 mi7-pau lai5 chi2-ki.
Chu2-chiah8-e5 chiu7 theh8 chit8 te3 mi7-pau hou7 i;  na7-si7 hit e5 khit-chiah8 ka7 i khoaN3,ka7 i phiN7-phiN7-leh, chiu7 hiat-kak8 khi3 m7 tihN8, m7 ai3 chiah8 cho3 i khi3.
Bin5-a2-jit8 ti7 goa7-kok lang5 e5 chhu3 kohu7 phah mng5 e5 siaN.  I e5 chu2-chiah8 khi3 khui, khui-liau2 chiu7 khi3 ka7 thau5-ke kong2, u7 lang5 beh lai5 kiu5 chiN5 khi3 bech2¡Kt khu7 koaN-chha5 tai5 si2-lang5. 
Thau5-ke kong2, Sim2-mih8 lang5 ?  Kong2,M7-chai.  U7 lang5 kong2 ti7 Tang-mng5-goa7 u7 chit8 e5 lang5 si2, chim2-a2 to2 ti7 lou7-piN, bo5 lang5 bat i .  Thau5-ke kong2, Li2 khi3 khoaN3-mai7 chiah lai5 ka7 goa2 kong2.  Kong2,Ho2, chiu7 khi3.  Tiap-a2-ku2 chiu7 tng2-lai5 hoe5-hok kong2,  U7 iaN2, li2 phah-sng3 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 ?  Chiu7-si7 cha-bou7-jit8 lai5 ti7 chia kiu5-khit hit-e5.  I hiat-kak8 li2 sou2 sang3 e5 mi7-pau ti3-kau3 go7-si2 !
TaN eng7 chit-e5 lai5 cho3 phi3-ju7.  Seng3-keng kong2 Ia5-sou si7 thiN loh8-lai5 e5 piaN2;kiN3-na7 hoaN-hi2 seng5-siu7-e5, leng5-hun5 e7 tit-tioh8 oah8 bian2 go7 si2.  Lang5 na7 m7 tihN8 (chhin-chhiuN7 hit e5 kiu5-khit e5 lang5 m7 siu7 mi7-pau) be7 bian2-tit i e5 leng5-hun5 iau-go7 kau3 si2-bo5 e5 sou2-chai7.
Goan7 Siong7-te3 hou7 lan2 tai7-ke boh8-tit m7 tihN8 Siong7-te3 e5 siuN2-su3, pang3-sak Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou ti3-kau3 tim5-lun5 ti7 eng2-oan2 bo5 thang ng2-bang2 e5 sou2-chai7.
