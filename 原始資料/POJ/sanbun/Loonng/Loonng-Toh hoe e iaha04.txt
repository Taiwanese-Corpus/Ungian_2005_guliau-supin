Toh8-hoe2 e5 iah8-a2 (4)

Goan5-chok: Chan Sim-Gi5, 1989,11,2
Hoan-ek8: Loonng X, 1998,8,5


Li7-khui Tai5-pak, kau3 Ko-hiong5-koan7 e5 Long5-khoan5-hoe7 khi3 chau2-chhoe7 A-hoa5 e5 kha-jiah, si7 lim5-si5 koat-teng7 e5. Phoe5-pau-a2 lai7-toe2 kan-taN chhun 2 chheng khou, to7 an2-ne lai5 chhut-hoat.

Tng5-ki5 tau5-jip8 ki-chan5 e5 khang-khoe3, thong-siong5, na7 chham-ka 1 tiuN5 cheng3-ti7 khong3-cheng, sou2 bin7-tui3 e5 si7 kui e5 thong2-ti7 kai-chan5 choan5-bin7-seng3 e5 taN2-kek. To7 sng3 kong2 si7 1 tiuN5 sio2-heng5 e5 khong3-cheng, goa2 ma7 si7 choan-sim lai5 tau5-jip8, sui5-si5 chu3-i3 thong2-ti7 kai-chan5 e5 chian3-sut8, i2 iu2-han7 e5 jin5-lek8 kap mih8-kiaN7 kiat-hap8 bin5-chiong3 lai5 tui3-khong3 thong2-ti7-chia2.

Ti7 chit chiong2 chong7-hong2 ha7, siuN7 beh kho3 sia2 ko2 lai5 than3 ko2-hui7 to7 bo5 hoat-tou7. Goa2 i2-keng u7 chiok ku2 e5 si5-kan bo5 kou3-teng7 e5 siu-jip8, ka7 teng2 pian3 e5 1 koa2 sai-khia khai liau2, to7 kan-taN e7-tang3 hiong3 chhin-chiaN5 khui chhui3 chioh-chiN5 kap chan3-chou7. To7 an2-ne, tiam7-tiam7-a2 ti7 hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7 e5 ki-chan5 lai5 kang-chok, chham-ka 1 tiuN5 koh 1 tiuN5 e5 khong3-cheng.  SiaN-goan5 1 e5 koh 1 e5 jiok8-se3-chia2, pi7-ap-phek-chia2. Goa2 tit tioh8 e5 si7 siaN2-mih? Tan2 hou7 tin3-pok8 pou7-tui7 phah, liah8 khi3 kaN-gak8 nia7-tiaN7!

Ui7 Nailong chiu2-leng5 e5 si5-chun7, goa2 ka7 519 e5 heng5-tong7 tong3-cho3 siong7 tiong7-iau3 e5 tai7-chi3. Goa2 ka7 ui5-su long2 sia2 ho2-se3, tong-jian5 ma7 bo5 kho2-leng5 ti7 chit toaN7 si5-kan koh khi3 than3 chiN5. Tan2-thai7 519 heng5-tong7 e5 si5, goa2 kun-pun2 bo5 hoat-tou7 khi3 siuN7, ia7 m7 goan7 khi3 siuN7 519 liau2-au7, goa2 e7 ti7 to2-ui7? Na7 e7-tang3 peng5-an tng2 kau3 chhu3, to7 chin ka-chai3 a. 519 heng5-tong7 liau2-au7, u7 1 kai2 hiong5-hiong5 siuN7 tioh8, goa2 keng3-jian5 peng5-an to3-tng2-lai5 a, goa2 na2 e7 it-tit8 bo5 hoat-kak chit hang7 tai7-chi3 leh?

Tui3 chin choe7 lang5 lai5 kong2, 519 liau2-au7, in khai-si2 ai3 khi3 bin7-tui3 hian7-sit8 e5 seng-oah8. 519 heng5-tong7 hou7 lan2 chai-iaN2 hoan2-tui3 tin7-iaN5 lai7-toe2, chin choe7 hong-bin7 long2 iah chin chhui3-liok8. A-hoa5 thiat-te2 hong7-hian3 e5 sit8-chian3 leng5-lek8, hou7 i chiaN5-cho3 Tai5-oan5 e5 thoan5-ki5 jin5-but8. I i2-keng kiaN5 jip8 lek8-su2, bo5 siok8-ti7 chit e5 siok8-se3. 519 liau2-au7, goa2 1 si5 iah khoaN3 boe7 chhut-lai5, ia7 u7 siaN2-mih tai7-chi3 tat8-tit goa2 iong7 seN3-mia7 khi3 tui3-khong3? Jin7-bat hoan2-tui3 tin7-iaN5 e5 khoat-tiam2, chin3 1 pou7 khak-teng7 ki-chan5 e5 khang-khoe3 su-iau3 koh khah phah-piaN3 khi3 choe3, che ma7 sng3 si7 1 chiong2 khe2-hoat. M7-koh, che u7 chin toa7 e5 pou7-hun7 si7 an3 A-hoa5 chu7-hun5 e5 chhi3-khek chiah su-kho2 tit tioh8 e5. Tui3 A-hoa5 lai5 kong2, i hu3-chhut e5 tai7-ke3 siuN toa7 a!

ThiaN kong2, A-hoa5 ti7 chun2-pi7 ka-ki7 519 chu7-hun5 heng5-tong7 e5 si5, seng-khu teng2 i2-keng bo5 poaN3-sian2-chiN5. 519 beh oa2-a2 beh oa2 e5 si5, u7 lang5 beh hou7 i chiN5, khi2-thau5-a2 kan-taN siu lang5 100 khou, tai7-khai3 si7 theh8-khi3 cho3 chhia-chiN5. Au7-lai5 i siu 1 tiuN 500 khou, kho2-leng5 si7 m7 goan7 hong5 peng2 si2-the2 e5 si5, hong5 tong3-cho3 si7 chau2-thau5-bo5-lou7 e5 san3-chiah-kui2. Che si7 koan-he7 A-hoa5 beh si2 chin3-cheng5 u7 1 tiuN 500 khou e5 thoan5-soat.

519 heng5-tong7 liau2-au7, goa2 to7 bo5 kam2-kak hoan2-tui3 tin7-iaN5 u7 siaN2-mih tat8-tit goa2 lai5 hong7-hian3 seN3-mia7. M7-koh, goa2 ka7 chit chiong2 kho2-leng5 po2-liu5 hou7 chhan-chhhiuN7 A-hoa5 e5 lang5. Na7 u7 lang5 beh cho3 chhan-chhiuN7 A-hoa5 an2-ne e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 bo5 hoat-tou7 chou2-tong3, goa2 kho2-lu7 kap i cho3-hoe2 kiaN5. Goa2 jin7-ui7 che long2 si7 seN-si2 e5 ai3. Chit chiong2 kho2-leng5 bat chhut-hian7 koe3  (M7-koh au7-lai5 goa2 hoat-hian7, goa2 bat jin7-ui7 kap A-hoa5 chin chiap-kin7 e5 lang5, chiam7-chiam7 bo5 hiah tan-sun5).

Goa2 ka-ki7 che7 hoe2-chhia loh8 lam5. Kau3 Ko-hiong5-koan7 Long5-koan5-hoe7 liau2-au7, goa2 to7 chin chu7-jian5 khi3 chiap-chhiok hit koa2 A-hoa5 chiap-chhiok koe3 e5 lang5. Goa2 bo5 siat-ke3 bun7-toe5, ma7 bo5 in2-chhoa7 in kong2 siaN2-hoe3.  Goa2 ka7 in kong2, goa2 beh sia2 1 pun2 A-hoa5 e5 chheh, goa2 tiam7-tiam7-a2 thiaN in kong2 1 koa2 A-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3. Thong-siong5, in e7 chu2-tong7 ka7 goa2 kong2 1 koa2 A-hoa5 e5 tai7-chi3. In na7 the5-chhut bun7-toe5, chiu7 goa2 sou2 chai e5 hoan7-ui5 lai7, goa2 ma7 e7 kong2 hou7 in chai. Tu2 tioh8 bo5 hong-pian7 kong2 e5 tai7-chi3, goa2 to7 tiam7-tiam7, peng2 bo5 kong2 peh8-chhat8 ka7 in eng3-hu3.

Ti7 Ko-hiong5-koan7 e5 Long5-koan5-hoe7, hou7 goa2 kam2-kak chhiong-kek chin toa7 e5 si7 1 ui7 TiuN2-lo2-kau3-hoe7 e5 sin3-tou5 A-pin, i chin liau2-kai2 A-hoa5 ti7 seng-oah8 siong7 kap cheng3-ti7 siong7 e5 1 koa2 khun3-jiau2. I chhoa7 A-hoa5 li7-khui su7-hui khou-a, kou2-le7 A-hoa5 khi3 khai-hoat long5-bin5 un7-tong7. Chit ui7 peng5-iu2 pang-chan7 A-hoa5 tit tioh8 chhau2-kin kang-chok lai7-toe2 chin lan5-tit u7 e5 khoai3-lok8.

A-pin in-ui7 hu7-chek 520 iu5-heng5 e5 khang-khoe3, 519 soah bo5 khi3 Tai5-pak. A-hoa5 e5 chu7-hun5, tui3 i e5 sim-leng5 cho7-seng5 chin toa7 e5 siong-hai7. Goa2 chin siau3-liam7, A-pin teh kap long5-bin5 tau3-tin7 e5 si5, bin7 teng2 khiam-pi e5 chhio3-iong5. Goa2 ma7 eng2-oan2 bo5 hoat-tou7 pang3 boe7 ki3, u7 1 kai2 goa2 kap A-pin kong2 tioh8 A-hoa5 e5 si5, u7 1 koa2 oe7 i boe7-tang3 liong7-kai2, to7 theng5-chi2 tui3-oe7, ham7 ti7 chhim-chhim e5 thong3-khou2 lai7-toe2. Chit e5 siau3-lian5-lang5 hou7 goa2 chin chhim e5 in3-siong2. I chin siau3-lian5, chhan-chhiuN7 1 bin7 kiaN3 kang7-khoan2, chio3 chhut tai7-chi3 kan2-tan e5 su7-hui. I hou7 goa2 hoat-hian7, siuN7 beh kai2-pian3 A-hoa5 hou7 lang5 kam2-kak bo5 ho2 tau3-tin7 e5 in3-siong2, kap siuN7 beh kai2-pian3 A-hoa5 seng-oah8 e5 khong-kan si7 u7 kho2-leng5 e5.

Su7-sit8 siong7, A-pin to7 i2-keng cho3 kau3 a. A-pin pang-chan7 A-hoa5, ti7 hoan2-tui3 un7-tong7 lai7-toe2, e7-tang3 tong5-si5 cho3 1 koa2 u7 i3-gi7 e5 tai7-chi3, koh e7-tang3 tong5-si5 hiang2-siu7 tioh8 khoai3-lok8, tiN-bit8 e5 seng-oah8.

M7-koh, che beh an2-choaN2 kai2-soeh leh, A-hoa5 keng3-jian5 bo5 ka7 chu7-hun5 e5 ke3-oe7 ka7 i chit ui7 ho2 peng5-iu2 kong2?  Goa2 an2-choaN2 kong2 ma7 kong2 boe7 chheng-chho2, i keng3-jian5 ka7 chhau2-kin kang-chok kap ho2 peng5-iu2 pang3 leh, chau2 khi3 Tai5-pak chu7-hun5! Lai5-chu7 lam5 Tai5-oan5 chhau2-kin kang-chok hiong-thou2 e5 siaN hou7 i tiuN-tu5 e5 si5 soah su hou7 si2-sin5 e5 in2-chhoa7. Si2-sin5 si7 thau3 koe3 siaN2-mih khoan2 e5 hong-sek, ti7 se3-kan ka7 A-hoa5 liah8 khi3? Che si7 A-hoa5 si2 liau2-au7 lau5 loh8-lai5 e5 bun7-toe5, goa2 ka7 i lau5 hou7 lek8-su2.
