Sat-bun5 chiau3 lan2 sou2 chai, si7 ka-ka hou7-hou7 mui2-jit8 sou2 tioh8 eng7 e5 jit8-iong7- phin2, hou7 lan2 thang soe2 seng-khu, i-chiuN5kap tak8 hang7.  Ki5-thaN ti7 kang-giap8 hong-bin7 ia7-si7 put-chi2 iau3-kin2, chhin-chhiuN7 che3-choa2 kang-giap8 tioh8 engchhi7u-ka sat-bun5, in3-sek, hong5-chui2-pouti3oh eng7 ?????? sat-bun5, chu2 iu5- chat-iu5 (boiled oil) e5 si5 u7 eng7 ????sat-bun5, an2-ni thang kong2 sat-bun5 e5 lou7-eng7 si7 chin toa7.               
Chit e5 sat-bun5 u7 lang5 kong2 chin cha2 chiu7 i2-keng hoat-beng5, chiu7-si7 ti7 I2-tai7-li7           kok hai2-kiN2 u7 chit8 e5 sio2 kang2-khau2, mia5 kio3-choe3 Savona.  Ti7 hia e5 tho2-hi5 lang5 ti7           hai2-kiN5 koaN5-thiN-si5 au3-he2-tui, chiong hit he2-hu kiau2-chui2, ia7 hit e5 chui2 bo5           phah-sng3 khi3 lam7-tioh8 hi5 e5 iu5, soa3 ngou2-gu5-jian5 chiaN5 sat-bun5.  In chiong chit e5           sat-bun5 khi3 soe2 chhiu2 koh put-chi2 oe7 khi3 la5-sam5 kap iu5-kau2. Tui3 an2-ni tit8-tit8           chin3-pou7, hoat-tat8, soa3 u7 lang5 choan-bun5 teh che3-cho7 chit hang7 mih8 lai5 teh boe7.           Au7-lai5 chai3 kau3 Au-chiu tak8 kok khi3 boe7, put chi2 ho2-eng7, sou2-i2 goa7-kok lang5 m7-           chai chit hang7 mih8 si7 sim2-mih8 choe3--e5, kan-ta chai si7 tui3 I2-tai7-li7 e5 Savona lai5,           sou2-i2 Hoat-lan5-se lang5 ka7 i ho7 choe3 ．Savon・; lan2 kio3-choe3 sat-bun5, si7 kong2-           liau2 chau2 im khi3, Eng-gu2 kong2 ．Soap・ ia7-si7 tui3 an2-ni lai5 ho7--e5.        
Ti7 Chu2-cheng5 79 ni5 I2-tai7-li7 e5 Vesuvius he2-soaN piak-lih8 e5 si5, kun7-piN u7 chit8 e5 siaN5-chhi7 kio3 choe3 Pompeii u7 hou7 he2-soaN e5 he2-hu kham3-bat8. Kin7-ni5 u7 kut8-tioh8 hit e5            siaN5-chhi7, ia7 u7 hoat-kian3--tioh8 teh boe7 sat-bun5 e5 tiam3, an2-ni hou7 lan2 thang            chai sat-bun5 ti7 hit si5 chiu7 i2-keng u7 lang5 che3-cho7 hoan3-boe7 lah !                
Liau2-au7 se3-kan chiam7-chiam7 chin3-pou7,lang5 e5 ti3-sek tit8-tit8 khui-khoah, che3-cho7            sat-bun5 e5 hoat-tou7 ia7 toa7-toa7 chin3-pou7 kai2-liong5; se3-kai3 bun5-beng5 e5 tak8 kok long2            u7 teh che3-cho7, Jit8-pun2 ni5-ni5 e5 che3-cho7 giah8 ia7-si7 chiuN7 chit8 chheng-ban7 khou            i2-siong7.  Hian7-si5 se3-kan te7-it hoat-tat8, choe3 te7-it choe7, te7-it ho2 e5 sat-bun5 chiu7-            si7 Eng-kok kap Hoat-kok, au7-lai5 chiah Bi2-kok, Tek-kok. Sui-jian5 Jit8-pun2 kok            ka-ki7 u7 teh choe3, iau2-ku2 kek8 ho2 e5 hoa3-chng sat-bun5 ia7-si7 m7-tat8 Eng-kok, Hoat-            kok e5 che3-phin2. 
Sat-bun5 che3-cho7 kang-giap8, hak8-li2-tek e5 li2-lun7 chhiaN2 mai3 kong2-khi2, chiong            i e5 kang-tiaN5 kap kui-bou5 lai5 kong2, thang kong2 si7 hoa3-hak8 kang-giap8 e5 tiong-kan            te7-it khoai3 choe3 e5 su7-giap8.  Toa7 chu-pun2 chiu7 choe3 khah toa7, chhin-chhiuN7 hoe7-sia7            e5 chou-chit an2-ni. Na7 sio2 chu-pun7, chiu7 choe3 khah sio2, ia7-si7 bo5 siong-kan.                
Goa2 khoaN3 lan2 Tai5-oan5 e5 bin5-tou7 chiam7-chiam7 teh khah koaiN5, chu7-jian5 ka-ka            hou7-hou7 teh eng7 sat-bun5 e5 lang5 ni5-ni5 tit8- tit8 ke-thiN.  Pun2-chiaN5 sat-bun5 long2 si7 tui3 Hiong-kang2, E7-mng5, a2-si7 Jit8-pun2 Loe7- te7 chai3-jip8-lai5, au7-lai5 ti7 Tai5-pak Bang2- kah u7 khi2 chit8 keng che3-cho7 sat-bun5 e5 hoe7-sia7, chu-pun2-kim si7 5 ban7-khou, bo5 kui2-ni5 choe3 liau2 put-chi2 u7 than3, chiau3 khoaN3 chiong-lai5 ia7-si7 oe7 than3, sou2-i2 chit khun3 chu-pun2 ke-thiN kau3 50 ban7.  Hian7- si5 u7 choe3 choe7-choe7 khoan2 e5 sat-bun5 hoan3-boe7 ti7 lan2 Tai5-oan5 choan5-to2.  Ki5- thaN iau2 u7 kui2-na7 keng soe3 keng--e5, ia7 thiaN-kiN3 Lam5-pou7 ia7 u7 sio2 kui-bou5 e5 hoe7- sia7 teh choe3.  Sui-jian5 an2-ni lan2 na7 khi3 tiam3-thau5 boe2 sat-bun5 e5 si5 chiu7 chai Loe7-te7 sat-bun5 iau2-ku2 si7 tit8-tit8 teh lai5.
