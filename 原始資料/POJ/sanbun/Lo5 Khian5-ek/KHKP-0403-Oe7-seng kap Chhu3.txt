Oe7-seng kap Chhu3
Lo5 Khian5-ek 
Oe7-seng u7 kui2-na7 khoan2, chiong5- tiong lai5 kong2-khi2 chhu3 iah8 u7 put-chi2 toa7 iau3-kin2 e5 sou2-chai7. Chhu3 si7 lang5 khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7, na7 bo5 tu2-ho2 chiu7 lang5 tiam3 hit e5 chhu3, sin-the2 siong5- siong5 boe7 peng5-an.
Pun2-toe7 lang5 e5 chhu3 khah choe7 thang-a2 kap mng5 chin soe3, koh jit8-si5 jit8- kng chio3 boe7 tioh8, mi5-si5 mng5-hou7 koh koaiN-bat8, chiu7 lian5 chit8 tiam2-a2 khong- khi3 ia7 boe7 tit thang liu5-thong. Hit e5 khong-khi3 e5 tiong-kan u7 kui2-na7 hang7 ti7-teh, chiu7-si7 u7 sng-sou3 kap chi2-sou3 kap than3-khi3, saN hang7 e5 tiong-kan sng-sou3 kap chi2-sou3 si7 te7-it choe7. Chit nng7 chiong2 si7 bo5 heng5-sek, bo5 bi7-sou3 e5 khi3. Toe7-bin7 tak8-hang7 e5 tong7-but8 a2-si7 sek8- but8, na7 bo5 oa2-kho3 chit e5 khi3 chiu7 bo5 oah8-mia7. Sou2-i2 lang5 tioh8 siong5-siong5 pou2-khip sin-sian e5 khong-khi3 jip8 kau3 hi3 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 lang5 oe7 chin2-khi2 cheng-sin5.    
Hit-e5 than3-khi3 si7 u7 tok8, na7 u7 ho2 e5 khong-khi3, than3-khi3 chiu7 khah chio2, sng-sou3 chiu7 khah choe7. Lang5 sou2 chhoan2 chhut-e5 u7 hit e5 than3-khi3 pi2 khong-khi3 tiong-kan e5 than3-khi3 khah choe7, sou2-i2 lang5 na7 ti7 bat8-bat8 e5 sou2-chai7 bo5 ku2 hit e5 than3-khi3 chiu7 moa2-moa2, lang5 chiu7 boe7 oah8.    
M7-na7 lang5 tioh8 eng7 hit e5 khong-khi3, chiu7 hit e5 teng a2-si7 chek oe7 toh8 iu5-goan5 si7 hit e5 khong-khi3 hou7 i toh8. An2-ni na7-si7 chit8 keng chhu3 e5 lai7-bin7 chit8 pha teng chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ke saN si3 e5 lang5. Si3 gou7 ki lah8-chek si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ke chit8 e5 lang5 teh tau3 chiah8 hit e5 sng-sou3.    
Na7-si7 chhu3-lai7 ou-am3, khong-khi3 boe7 liu5-heng5 chiu7 koaN5-jiat8 cheng3, a2-si7 hi3-piN7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 piN7 e5 thang5 chiu7 chin gau5 hoat-siN. Phi3-ju7 na7 theh8 hi3-piN7 e5 thang5 khng3 ti7 jit8-kng e5 sou2-chai7 hit e5 thang5 chiu7 liam5-piN si2.    
 
Lang5 na7-si7 ai3 khah u7 oe7-seng chiu7 tioh8 tui3 chhu3 lai5 siu-cheng2, tai7-seng tioh8 chhong3 thang-a2 khah toa7-e5, hou7 khong-khi3 liu5-thong, kap jit8-kng oe7 chio3 jip8-lai5.  Phah-sng3 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 hoe-thang kap thoah-thang si7 put-chi2 ho2, chong2-si7 koaN5-thiN lang5 ai3 koaiN in- ui7 si7 kiaN koaN5.  TaN beh chaiN2-iuN7 chhong3 ho7 i boe7 koaN5?  Tioh8 tui3 lai7-bin7 chhong3 chit8 te3 pang tui3 thang-a2 ui5 chhu-chhu khi2-lai5, hou7 koaN5-khi3 tui3 thang-a2 khi2 chiah boe7 koaN5, chong2-si7 khong-khi3 oe7 liu5-thong.  In-ui7 lang5 sou2 chhoan2-chhut e5 khong-khi3 si7 khah khin, sou2-i2 oa2 thian- lo5-pan2 e5 e7-kha tioh8 chhong3 chit8 e5 hoe- thang a2-si7 thoah-thang hou7 phaiN2 e5 khong-khi3 oe7 chhut-khi3.     
Chhu3-lai7 e5 thou5-kha kap chhu3-piN e5 kau-a2 na7 siong5-siong5 tam5 chiu7 oe7 siN bang2, a2-si7 tak8 hang7 e5 tok8-thang5 oe7 hai7 lang5 chin choe7.  Sou2-i2 na7-si7 ai3 thou5-kha boe7 sip tioh8 phou-chng a2-si7 boah iuN5-he, na7-si7 beh koh khi2 sin-e5 tioh8 tah8 te7-pan2 li7 thou5 nng7 saN chhioh, hou7 sip-khi3 boe7 khi2 lai5 chiu7 lang5 e5 seng-khu oe7 iong2.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 403 koan3, 1918 ni5 10 geh8)
