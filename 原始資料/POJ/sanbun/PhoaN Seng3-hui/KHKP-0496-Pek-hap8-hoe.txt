
Pek-hap8-hoe

(PhoaN Seng3-hui)
" Chhi3-siuN7 Pek-hap8-hoe chaiN2-iuN7 na2 toa7-chang5."  
ChhiaN2 khoaN3 te7-chiuN7 sou2 u7 chiah e5 giok8-lau5, tai5-koh, in kiam2 oe7 ka-ki7 chu7-jian5 ti7-teh ?  Tok8-tok8 si7 tui3 huih, koaN7 sou2 kiat-chiaN5--e5.  Ui7-tioh8 se3-chiuN7 but8-chit e5 koan-he7, soa3 ti3-kau3 hoe3-chhim2,bong5-chhan; mi5-jit8 tioh8-boa5 lo5-khou2,kan-ta ui7-tioh8 toe7-chiuN7 e5 kong-beng5,chai5-po2, chiu7 li2 ki3-jian5 u7 giok8-lau5, tai5-koh; chek-chu7 choe7-choe7 chai5-but8.  Na7-si7 Siong7-te3 tui3 li2 kong2, "Gong7-lang5 ah !kim-mi5 beh tui3 li2 tho2 li2 e5 leng5-hun5; chiu7 li2 sou2 chek-thiok--e5 beh kui ti7 sim2-mih8 lang5 ah ?"  
" Lang5 na7 tit-tioh8 choan5 Se3-kai3, lai5 song3-sit i e5 siN3-mia7, u7 sim2-mih8 li7-ek ?" Song3-hiong lang5 ah ! lin2 boh8-tit ui7-tioh8 sin-khu sou2 phaiN7 e5 ka-chi3 lai5 lau5 bak8-sai2,thi5-khau3, iu-chhiu5, khoa3-lu7.  Kiah8-thau5 khoaN3, khong-tiong e5 pe-chiau2, in bo5 cheng3-choh, bo5 siu-seng5, ia7 bo5 khou3-pang5,bo5 chhek-chhng, lan2 e5 Siong7-te3 siong7-chhiaN2 teh chhi7 in.  Ho5-hong3 lan2 si7 I e5 kiaN2-lui7 mah ?  
Ho2-giah8 lang5 ah ! lin2 boh8-tit ui7-tioh8 oe7 pai7-hoai7 e5 giok8-lau5, chai5-po2 lai5 hoaN-hi2 khoai3-lok8, ti3-kau3 koe3-thau5 khoa3-lu7 ti7 toe7-chiuN7 lai5 song3-sit siN3-mia7.  
Chhi3-siuN7 Pek-hap8-hoe chaiN2-iuN7 na2 toa7-chang5.  I bo5 tioh8-boa5 bo5 phang2-cheh, to chheng7 kau3 an2-ni.  Tng Sou2-lo5-bun5 kek8 hang5-ngou2 e5 si5,i sou2 chheng7--to m7-tat8-tioh8 chit-ho7 hoe e5 chit8-lui2.  
Chai7-cha2 Chin5-si2-hong5 tng-si5 bat thiaN-kiN3 u7 chit8-e5 tiong5-seng put-lo2 e5 sian-keng2; lai7-bin7 u7 pat-chiat8 tiong2-chhun-chhau2; ki5-tiong u7 put-si2-chhau2, chiah8 chit-e5 chiu7 be7 si2, thang eng2-oah8.  I moa2-sim him-bou7, chek-si5 chhe lang5 khi3 Tong-hai2 chhai2-chhu2.  O7--ah !  Kek8 bu5-to7 e5 Jin5-kun siong7- chhiaN2 pau5 chit ho7 liam7-thau5, beh chhoe7-chhut eng2-oah8, ho5-hong3 lan2 sio2-sin3 e5 lang5 mah ?  Chu2 kong2," Boh8-tit ui7-tioh8 bin5-a2-jit8 lai5 khoa3-lu7;in-ui7 hit e5 bin5-a2-jit8 i beh ka-ki7 khoa3-lu7.Chit8 jit8 e5 kan-khou2 ti7 chit8 jit8 kau3-giah8. "" Tok8-tok8 lin2 tioh8 kiu5 I e5 kok, chiu7 chiah e5 mih8 beh soa3 hou7 lin2.
1. "KhoaN3-kiN3 Pek-hap8 e5 sek-ti3,
Hian2-chhut cho3 Chu2 toa7 leng5-ti3,
Se3-chiuN7 hoe-bak8 sui5-jian5 choe7,
Long2-chong2 m7-tat8 Pek-hap-hoe.
2. Boh8-tit khoa3-lu7 chiah8, chheng7, eng7,
Chiah-e5 Chu2 chai lan2 khiam3-eng7,
Chhi3-khoaN3 khong-tiong e5 poe-chiau2,
Siong7-te3 hou7 be7 go7-liau2.
3. In bo5 cheng3-choh, bo5 siu-seng5,
Tak8-jit8 khoai3-lok8 kek8 chu7-jian5,
Chhi3-siuN7 khong3-ia2 Pek-hap8-hoe,
Toa7-toa7 ka3-si7 lan2 tai7-ke.
4. In bo5 tioh8-boa5 bo5 phang2-cheh,
Sek-ti3 iau2-ku2 chhin-chhiuN7 seh,
Chai7-cha2 hian5-ong5 Sou2-lo5-bun5,
Kim, gin5, kok-po2 sou3-ban7 kun.
5. Mia5-siaN liu5-chun5 su3-hai2 lai7,
Hoe7-lai7 toa7-se3 long2 beng5 chai,
Sou2 chheng7 tiu5-toan7, hiah-nih8 sui2,
M7-tat8 che hoe e5 chit8 lui2.
6. Lan2 lang5 chiam7 toa3 te7-bin7 chiuN7,
SiN3-mia7 chhin-chhiuN7 bang7 chit8-iuN7,
Mih8-sai2 tou-bou5 tham chai5-li7 ?
ChiaN3-si7 bo5 hap8 Chu2 ka3-si7.
7. Seng3-chheh beng5-chai3 che to7-li2,
Khng3 lan2 tai7-ke e7 hiau2-li2.
Boh8-tit koe3-thau5 ti7 te7-nih8,
Tok8-tok8 chek-chu7 ti7 thiN-nih8.
