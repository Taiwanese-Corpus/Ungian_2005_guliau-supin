
Ui3 Bo2-gi2 e5 Hak8-sip8 Kian3-lip8 Chok8-kun5 e5 Chu7-sin3-sim
Lau5 Iong5-eng ek8-sia2
	Siong-sin3 u7 chiok che7 lang5 it-tit8 long2 siuN7 boe7-thong, ui7 sim2-mih8 tioh8 ai3 lai5 oh8 bo2-gi2--leh? Ma7 u7 lang5 kun-pun2 tioh8 m7-bat siuN7-koe3 ai3 hak8-sip8 bo2-gi2. Sim7-chi3 khoaN3-boe7-khi2 ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2, tek8-piat8 si7 siu7-koe3 ko-teng2 kau3-iok8..e5, u7 chit-khoan2 koan-liam7 e5 lang5 koh khah giam5-tiong7! Sou2-i2 ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 thak8-chheh-lang5, tui3 i sin-sin khou2-khou2 sou2 oh8--lai5 e5 "pat8-lang5 e5 gi2-gian5", long2 khah tiong7-si7. Phi3-ju7 khoaN3-tang7 "Hoa5-gi2", "Eng-gi2", khoaN3-khin "A-nia5 e5 oe7". Sou2-i2 kin-a2-jit8 ti7 kau3-iok8 the2-he7 lai7-te2, tak8-e5 piaN3-mia7 teh oh8 Hoa5-gi2, oh8 Eng-gi2, oh8 Jit8-gi2 teng2-teng2. In-ui7 oh8 che khah u7 chui2-chun2, ma7 khah u7 chhut-lou7. Ti3-su2 kin-a2-jit8 u7 hoat-tou7 iong7 bo2-gi2 choe3 chu2-the2-seng3 lai5 siong7-kho3, ian2-kang2 e5 lang5, soa3 piN3-chiaN5 chit8-chiong2 hi-han2 e5 tong7-but8. Che e7-tang3 hou7 lan2 lai5 choe3 chit8-e5 su-kho2, na7 si7 chit8-e5 chok8-kun5 in toa7-to-sou3 e5 lang5 long2 "boe7 ai3 iong7", a2-si7 "boe7 hiau2 iong7" bo2-gi2, che kam2 u7 kho2-leng5 piN3-chiaN5 chit8-e5 u7 chun-giam5, u7 chu7-chu2 e5 chok8-kun5? Kin-a2-jit8 ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 4 toa7 chok8-kun5, in e5 chun-giam5 kap in sou2 su2-iong7 bo2-gi2 e5 cheng5-heng5, u7 chin bit8-chhiat e5 koan-he7. Phi3-ju7 kong2, Tai5-oan5 e5 PeN5-pou-chok8, pi-chham2 kah ham7 chit8-ku3 in e5 gi2-gian5 long2 bo5 lang5 teh kong2, sou2-i2 in e5 chun-giam5 chiu7 oan5-choan5 phah bo5--khi3--lah. I2-sek-liat8(以色列）chit-e5 kok-ka e5 lang5, in-ui7 ka7 in ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2 po2-liu5 kah ho2-se3 liu-liu, sui-jian5 in e5 kok-ka bo5 toa7, m7-ku2 in iau2-si7 koe3 kah chin u7 chun-giam5.
Lan2 lai5 khoaN3 kou2-cha2 a2-si7 hian7-tai7 Tai5-oan5 kap goa7-kok e5 lek8-su2, u7 chit8 e5 chiok ho2 e5 kian3-cheng3, chu7-si7 sou2-u7--e5 thong2-ti7-chia2, long2 si7 siuN7 chin7 pan7-hoat, beh kim3-chi2 siau-biat8 pi7-thong2-ti7-chia2 e5 gi2-gian5. Lan2 na7 lai5 khoaN3 hit-tong-si5 "Phou2-hoat Chian3-cheng" liau2-au7, Prussia(普魯士) chiam3-leng2 Lok8-lim5(洛林), A-ni2-sat-su(亞爾薩斯), chiu7 chek-si5 kim3-chi2 in kong2 Hoat-gi2, siong-sin3 tak8-e5 tui3 "Choe3-au7-chit8-kho3" e5 bun5-chiuN, long2 u7 chin chhim e5 in3-siong7 chiah tioh8. Tai5-oan5 e5 cheng5-heng5 ma7 si7 kang7-khoan2! Jit8-pun2-lang5 thong2-ti7 Tai5-oan5 e5 si5, ui7 beh thui-heng5 "Hong5-bin5-hoa3 Un7-tong7", chek-kek8 thui-heng5 "Kok-gi2 Ka-teng5", beh lai5 siau-biat8 Tai5-oan5 kok chok8-kun5 e5 bo2-gi2. Tai7-chian3 liau2-au7, Kok-bin5-tong2 lai5 chiap-siu cheng3-koan5, ma7-si7 iong7 kang7-khoan2 pou7-sou3 lai5 thui-heng5 "Kok-gi2". Iong7 hoat8-chiN5, koa3-kau2-pai5 teng2-teng2 bu5-sou2-put-chi3 e5 chhiu2-toaN7, lai5 kim3-chi2 kong2 Tai5-oan5 bo2-gi2. Che si7 chit8-chiong2 bo5 thiaN3-sim kap ok-tok8 e5 cho3-hoat. Thong2-ti7-chia2 e5 bok8-tek, chiu7-si7 beh siau-biat8 pi7-thong2-ti7-chia2 e5 bun5-hoa3. In-ui7 bo2-gi2 si7 chit8-e5 chok8-kun5 e5 oah8-hoa3-chioh8(活化石). Sou2-i2 bo2-gi2 it-tan7 siau-sit--khi3, chit e5 chok8-kun5 tioh8 boe7 ki3-tit ka-ki7 e5 kun-goan5, song3-sit jin7-tong5 kap kui-siok8-kam2, an2-ni chiu7 hong-pian7 pi7 lang5 lai5 thong2-ti7--lah.
	Kou2-kim Tai5-goa7 tui3 ka-ki7 bo5 sin3-sim e5 chok8-kun5, in e5 thak8-chheh-lang5 kap ti3-sek hun7-chu2, long2 e7 piaN3-mia7 khi3 oh8 pat8-lang5 e5 kiong5-se3 gi2-gian5. Phi3-ju7 kong2, koe3-khi3 Lo5-ma2 e5 thak8-chheh-lang5, piaN3-mia7 oh8 "Hi-liap8-bun5"(希臘文). Au7--lai5 chiah to3-tng2--khi3 kian3-lip8 "La-teng-bun5"(Latin) e5 pun2-chit kap chu2-the2. Bun5-ge7-hok8-heng si5-tai7, I3-tai7-li7(Italy) e5 bun5-jin5, piaN3-se3 oh8 "La-teng-bun5", it-tit8 kau3 Lan3-teh(Alighiere Donte 1265-1321) iong7 ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2 I3-tai7-li7-bun5 chhong3-chok "Sin5-khiok"(Divine Comedy神曲), chiah kian3-lip8 I3-tai7-li7-bun5 e5 chu2-the2-seng3. Eng-gi2 e5 mia7-un7 ma7 si7 kang7-khoan2. 14 Se3-ki2 i2-cheng5 e5 Eng-kok-lang5, chu7-jin7 "Eng-gi2" si7 bo5 chui2-chun2 e5 gi2-gian5, sou2-i2 piaN3-mia7 oh8 "Hoat-bun5" "La-teng-bun5". U7 chit8-ui7 Eng-kok mia5 kio3 Hoai5-khek-li7-hu(Wycliffe 1382) thoan5-kau3-su7, chiong La-teng-bun5 Seng3-keng hoan-ek8 choe3 Eng-gi2, kiat-ko2 in2-khi2 Eng-kok kau3-hoe7 e5 hun3-nou7, pi7 koaiN jip8 kaN-lo5, sim7-chi3 au7--lai5 hou7 lang5 pian-si e5 chhi-chham2 mia7-un7. Jit8-pun2 ti7 Beng5-ti7-i5-sin chin3-cheng5, hit-si5 e5 thak8-chheh-lang5, pheng7 bo5 jin7-tong5 ka-ki7 e5 "kha-nah"(假名). Han5-kok e5 hak8-chia2, ma7 khoaN3 boe7 khi2 ka-ki7 e5 "Gan7-bun5"(諺文), long2 piaN3-mia7 beh oh8 Han3-bun5, an2-ni chiah e7-tang3 hian2-si7 ka-ki7 e5 toe7-ui7. Chit-khoan2 e5 hian7-siong7 kap chit-ma2 e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 chin kang7-khoan2, long2 piaN3-mia7 teh su2-iong7 in kam2-kak khah u7 chui2-chun2 e5 "Hoa5-bun5", "Eng-bun5". Che long2 si7 chu7-pi e5 sim-thai3. Chiau3 gi2-gian5 hak8-ka e5 kong2-hoat, chit8 e5 lang5, ke bat chit8-chiong2 gi2-gian5, chiu7 ke chit8-liap8 thau5-nau2. Sou2-i2, oh8 pat8-lang5 e5 gi2-gian5 si7 ho2 tai7-chi3, si7 tat8-tit kou2-le7..e5. M7-ku2, chin3-cheng5 u7 chit8 e5 chiok tiong7-iau3 e5 tiau5-kiaN7, chiu7-si7 ai3 tai7-seng oe7-hiau2 "Chhui3 kong2 pe7-bu2-oe7, chhiu2 sia2 Tai5-oan5-bun5". Iong7 ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2 lai5 oh8 pat8-lang5 e5 gi2-gian5; m7-si7 iong7 oh8--tioh8 e5 "Pak-kiaN-gi2" koh lai5 oh8 goa7-kok-gi2, ke tng2 chit8-lian3. Bok8-koai3 Tai5-oan5-lang5 oh8 goa7-kok-gi2 ke chiok sin-khou2. OaN7 chit8-ku3-oe7 lai5 kong2, chiu7-si7, iong7 ka-ki7 e5 bo2-gi2 choe3 chu2-the2-seng3 lai5 oh8 goa7-kok-gi2 khah kin2 oh8, khah ho2 oh8. Lan2 na7 tit8-chiap iong7 Tai5-gi2 oh8 Jit8-bun5, li2 e7-tang3 hoat-hian7 u7 chiok che7 beh-a2-beh kang7 e5 sou2-chai7. Sim7-chi3 Eng-gi2 ma7 kang7-khoan2; phi3-ju7 theh8--khi3(提去)─take it, tng2(轉)─turn, hou(呼)─call, tin7(陣)─team, lou7(路)─road, sip(濕)─seep, hui3(費)─fee, hiap8(協)─help, sio(燒)─sear; hou7(予)─for teng2-teng2. Chin-chiaN3 si7 u7 chin-che7 chhu3-bi7 koh e7-tang3 sio-thong..e5.
	Bo2-gi2 tau3-te2 u7 sim2-mih8 tiong7-iau3-seng3, tat8-tit lan2 tioh8-ai3 he7-mia7 ka7 chhiuN2-kiu3 kap chau2-chhoe7--leh? Tui3 gi2-gian5 u7 ki-pun2 siong5-sek e5 lang5 long2 chai-iaN2, "Bo2-gi2" put-tan7 si7 chit8-chiong2 kau-thong e5 kang-ku7, ia7 si7 chit8-chiong2 kam2-cheng5 e5 kau-liu5, bun5-hoa3 e5 tu5-a2, su-kho2 e5 hong-sek, chok8-kun5 e5 hu5-ho7, piau2-tat8 e5 phiau-ki3, koh khah tiong7-iau3..e5 si7 "chun-giam5 e5 i5-hou7" kap "chu7-sin3-sim e5 kian3-lip8". Sou2-i2 u7 Tai5-oan5 i3-sek e5 lang5, long2 siuN7 chin7 pan7-hoat teh chhong3-cho7 bo2-gi2 e5 khoan5-keng2, pau-koat "Gi2-gian5 peng5-teng2-koan5" kap "Bo2-gi2 kau3-iok8 koan5" e5 cheng-chhu2. M7-ku2 tng5-ki5 (1895-1996) siu7 goa7-lai5 cheng3-koan5 thong2-ti7 e5 Tai5-oan5-lang5, u7 chin che7 i2-keng piN3-chiaN5 "ban5-phoe5 sin-pu7", tui3 bo2-gi2 e5 su2-iong7 leng5-lek8 ia7 i2-keng song3-sit, ti7 chai-bat siong7 ma7 i2-keng ba5-pi3. Sou2-i2 jin7-ti kap koan-liam7 e5 kai2-pian3, chiaN5-choe3 "Bo2-gi2" si7-m7-si7 e7-tang3 teng3-kun kap seN-thoaN3 e5 koan-kian7.
	Ui3 teng2-bin7 sou2 ki2 e5 le7, hou7 lan2 phou2-phou2-a2 chai-iaN2, bo2-gi2 kap chok8-kun5 chu7-sin3-sim e5 kian3-lip8 u7 chin toa7 e5 koan-lian5. Chit-ma2 e7-tang3 lai5 choe3 chit8-e5 kiat-lun7, na7-si7 tng lan2 e5 ti3-sek-hun7-chu2 kap thak8-chheh gin2-a2, long2 e7-hiau2 "chhui3 kong2 pe7-bu2-oe7, chhiu2 sia2 Tai5-oan5-bun5" e5 si5, chiu7-si7 Tai5-oan5-lang5 "Bun5-ge7 hok8-heng", "Tai5-oan5 i3-sek", "Chun-giam5 chu7-chu2" kap "Kian3-lip8 chu7-sin3-sim" e5 si5-ki kau3--lah.
