
Siau2-kau2
Chiu Iau7-chhai2   
Lun7 Tai5-oan5 lang5 ai3 chhi7 kau2, si7 in- ui7 kiaN u7 chhat8-a2, na7 u7 chhi7 kau2 chhat8 lai5 chiu7 thang pui7, hou7 chu2-lang5 chai chhiN2 cheng-sin5, thang ti5-hong5 u7 phaiN2- lang5 beh lai5 chhu2-thau. Che7-che7 lang5 m7 chai siau2-kau2 piN7, na7 ka7-tioh8 lang5 e7 hiah li7-hai7. Jip8-pun2 lang5 kaN2-si7 u7 oh8 se-hak8 chiah chai soa3 chiong kau2 tuh8-luh8 phah-lah--si2. Ti7 Tai5-pak Toa7-tiu7-tiaN5 goa2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 teh phah-si2 kau2, goa2 long2 m7 chai si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3.    
Cheng5 bo5 kui2-jit8 ia7 u7 thiaN-kiN3 lang5 kong2, Ka-gi7 siaN5-lai7 Jit8-pun2 lang5 teh phah-si2 kau2, chhong3 chhia-a2 chhia khi3 tai5. Tai5-oan5 lang5 u7-e5 m7 chai si7 sim2-mih8  tai7-chi3, soa3 u7 lang5 teh giau5-gi5 am3-chiN7 teh kong2, Jit8-pun2 lang5 tai7-seng tu5 chiah e5 kau2, au7-lai5 beh thai5 lang5, chiah bo5 kau2 thang pui7. Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 siN3-mia7 si7 chiah8 kau3 chap8-sou2 goeh8 na7-tiaN, e7 hou7 Jit8-pun2 lang5 cho3 chit8-e5 chau5-lau5 khi3. Ia7 u7 lang5 ka7 goa2 kong2, Sian-siN--ah, na7 u7 chiN5 tioh8 be2 le2 lai5-chiah8, na7 m7 chiah8 bo5 lou7-eng7. Goa2 mng7 in khoaN3 choaN2-iuN7 ? In chiu7 kong2 Jit8-pun2 lang5 phah-si2 kau2 e5 in-iu5; si7 beh chau5-biat8 Tai5-oan5 lang5, chiah bo5 kau2 thang pui7.    
Kin7-lai5 u7 lang5 tui3 Tai5-pak sia2-phoe ka7 goa2 kong2, siau2-kau2 e5 tok8 si7 ti7 i-e5 noa7 kap hoeh-nih8. Lang5 na7 hou7 siau2- kau2 ka7-tioh8, bo5 liam5-piN hoat siau2-kau2 e5 piN7, u7 si5 saN goeh8 jit8, u7 si5 lak8 goeh8 jit8, u7 si5 ni5-goa7 chiah e7 hoat. Ka7 liau2-au7 nng7 le2-pai3 ku2 chiu7 hoat piN7-e5 ia7 u7, chong2-si7 chin chio2. PiN7 na7 hoat, piN7 lang5 khoaN3-tioh8 chui2 e7 kiaN, sou2-i2 u7 si5 chiong chit e5 piN7 ho7-cho3 kiaN-chui2-piN7.  Siau2 kau2 piN7, u7-si5 niau, chhai5-long5, hou5-li5, iu5-goan5 e7 tioh8 chit ho7 piN7. M7-na7 teh siau2 e5 kau2 ka7-tioh8 lang5 e7 hoat chit-ho7 e5 piN7, chiu7-si7 boe7 hoat piN7 e5 kau2, ka7-tioh8 lang5 iu5-goan5 e7 hoat. Che si7 in-ui7 kau2 na7 jiam2-tioh8 chit ho7 siau2 kau2 e5 piN7-tok8, bo5 liam5-piN hoat piN7, iu5-goan5 tioh8 kui2-na7 goeh8 jit8, a2-si7 ni5-goa7 chiah e7 hoat piN7; chong2-si7 sui-jian5 boe7 hoat piN7 i  
e5 seng-khu i2-keng u7 chit ho7 tok8 ti7-teh.  Siau2-kau2 teh siau2 e5 si5 khah siong5 ai3 chiah8 thau5-mng5, tiu7-chiau2, noa7-thou5.   Lang5 na7 hou7 siau2 kau2 ka7-tioh8, iau3-kin2 tioh8 khoaN3 hit chiah kau2 si7 siau2 kau2 a2 m7 si7; na7 si7 chiu7 ti7 boe7 hoat piN7 e5 si5 tioh8 chhong3 ioh8; na7 kau3 hoat piN7 e5 si5 chiu7 bo5 ioh8, tek-khak e7 si2.  Kau2 hek8-si7 siau2, hek8-si7 bo5, na7 ka7-tioh8 lang5 e5 si5 chiu7 tioh8 chiong hit chiah kau2 pak8- teh chit8 le2-pai3 ku2, hit chiah na7-si7 siau2 kau2 tek-khak e7 chai.    
Thang pun cho3 saN ki5 lai5 kong2:---    
Te7-it-ki5 si7 iu-bun7-ki5. Tui3 hoat piN7 te7- it jit8 kau3 te7-saN jit8, si7 chhin-chhiuN7 teh iu-bun7 e5 khoan2-sit.    
Te7-ji7 ki5 si7 hoat-kong5-ki5. Tui3 hoat piN7 e5 te7-saNjit8 kau3 te7-si3 jit8, chiah8, lim, kap kiaN5 lou7 long2 kan-khou2, ia7 ai3 lam7-sam2 ka7.    
Te7-saN ki5 si7 chui3 e5 ki5, chiu7 e7 sau- siaN, ai3 khun3. Chit8 le2-pai3 ku2 hit chiah kau2 chiu7 si2.  Lang5 na7 chho3-gou7 chiong kau2 phah si2 chiu7 tioh8 theh8 kau2 e5 liong5-chun5-kut- chhoe2 chim3 ti7 kam-iu5-nih8 kia3 hou7 i-seng giam7.  
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 219 koan3, 1903 ni5 6geh8)
