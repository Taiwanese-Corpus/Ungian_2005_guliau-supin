Choa2 e5 Khi2-in


 Choa2 si7 chin seng7 e5 mih8, tak8 sou2-chai7 long2 u7, put-chi2 choe7 khoan2, ia7 chin choe7 hang7 e5 lou7-eng7.  
 Kou2-cha2 Khong2-chu2 e5 si5 long2 bo5.  Hit si5 tioh8 eng7 tek khun poh8-poh8, chiah eng7 pit sia2, ia7 u7 eng7 chhiu7-hioh8 kap chit8 khoan2 e5 thou5, cheng-siN kap chhiu7 e5 phe5, chin bo5 li7-pian7 koh chin kui3.  Te7 it cha2 cho7 choa2 chiu7-si7 phang, oan-iuN, ng5 phang, kim-sit8-a2 long2 oe7 hiau2 choe3.  In khi3 chhe7 chit8 te3 hu-hu e5 chha5 choe3 chiaN5 iu3-iu3, na2 chhin-chhiuN7 soa.  Tui3 i e5 chhui3 chiah thou3 chhut noa7 kap ka lai5 chhiau kui-liap8.  Au7-lai5 eng7 cheng5 kha ngoeh8 hit liap8 chha5-sut, chiong chhui3 ka7 chiu7 pe khi3 I e5 siu7, jian5-au7 eng7 i e5 chih8, chhui3-khi2, kap kha thoaN hou7 i poh8-poh8, kau3 ta e5 si5 chiu7 piN3 chiaN5 phu2-sek iu3-iu3 e5 choa2.  Lang5 in-ui7 an2-ni khoaN3, chiah hoat-beng5 lai5 choe3 choa2, ia7 chai choa2 e5 lou7-eng7, koh chai tioh8 sim2-mih8 mih8 lai5 cho7 choa2.  
 Hiah e5 phang au7 kha e5 kha-chhioh toe2 si7 piN5-piN5.  An2-ni chiah oe7 chan7 in thoaN chha5-sut poh8-poh8 lai5 thiap8 choe3 kui2-na7 teng5 choh-siu7, hou7 i chin iong2, hou7 ia7 boe7 phoah jip8.   
Tiong-kok chin cha2 cho7 choa2 si7 eng7 tek chim3 kau3 noa7 lai5 cho7-e5.  Ti7 goa7-kok khah choe7 si7 eng7 ku7 pou3, sou2-i2 lang5 si3-koe3 khioh lai5 boe7 hou7 che3-choa2 hoe7-sia7.  Koh khia7-ke e5 lang5 u7 siu khng3-teh, chhin-chhiuN7 Tiong-kok e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 khioh khi2 lai5 pau oe5-toe2, in khah m7 chai oe7 thang choe3 choa2 e5 lou7-eng7.  
 Ku7 pou3 iu5-goan5 chim3 kau3 noa7-noa7 chiah che3 choe3 choa2, chin kau7, chin iong2, ia7 u7 chin iu3, u7 choe7-choe7 khoan2, put-chi2 ho2 eng7.  Sou2-i2 lang5 na2-ku2 na2 chai, kau3 hian7-si5 ia7 u7 eng7 chhau2 i2-kip8 choe7-choe7 hang7 teh che3-cho7 choa2.  Sou2 cho7 e5 choa2 na2 choe7 khoan2, ia7 choe3 na2 choe7 khoan2 e5 lou7-eng7.  
 Siong7 gau5 siuN7-chhut cho7 choa2 e5 hoat, chiu7-si7 Jit8-pun2 lang5.  In e5 choa2 kek8 iu3, ia7 chin iong2, oe7 thang ka2 choe3 toa7 tiau5 soh, u7-e5 thang choe3 toa7 khi3-khu7 e5 lou7-eng7.  U7 sin-bun5 chhut kong2 chiong-lai5 beh eng7 choa2 cho7 chun5, cho7 chhia, kap chin choe7 hang7 toa7 lou7-eng7.  Iau2-be7 sit8-heng5 bo5 tek-khak chai ko2-jian5 oe7 chiaN5 lang5 sou2 ng3-bang7 a2-boe7.  Chiau3 an2-ni khoaN3 choa2 hou7 lang5 cham2-jian5 choe3 toa7 li7-ek.  
 Chhi3 siuN7 kou2-cha2 si5-tai7 choa2 chin kui3, song3-hiong lang5 boe7 thang sui5-pian7 tit-tioh8.  TaN si3-koe3 long2 u7 chin siok8, che long2 si7 tui3 soe3-soe3 e5 su7 hoat-tat8.  Kho2-kian3 thang chai sio2-sio2 khoa2-khoa2 e5 thang5-thoa7 oe7 tit thang hou7 lang5 kek chhut chin toa7 lou7-eng7 e5 m5¡Kh.  
  
Khong2-chu2 

 Tiong-kok 2000 goa7 ni5 cheng5 u7 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 chhut-si3, chhin-chhek peng5-iu2 toa7 o-lo2 i e5 ti3-sek, chin soe3-han3 hou7 i khi3 thak8-chheh.  U7 chit8 jit8 i u7 che7 ti7 in e5 hoe-hng5 lai7 teh thak8-chheh u7 soe3 chiah niau-a2 kap i choe3 phoaN7.  
 I kiah8 bak8 khoaN3 ka7 in choh-hng5-e5 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 pi2 i khah soe3-han3 kun7-oa2 ti7 chit8 e5 kim-hi5-ou5 piN teh thit-tho5.  Soe3-han3-a2 boe7 hiau2 soe3-ji7 chiu7 peh khi2-lai5; i e5 bin7 chio3 ti7 ou5-te2, khoaN3-kiN3 u7 iaN2 chiu7 chhun-chhiu2 beh khi3 bong, soa3 hut-jian5-kan to3-thau5-chai poah8-loh8 ou5-lai7.  Hit e5 teh thak8-chheh-e5 chiu7 khoaN3-kiN3, kin2-kin2 peh khi2-lai5 beh khi3 kiu3, i long2 bo5 hoat-tit i ta-oa5, in-ui7 i si7 lak8 he3 e5 gin2-na2 na7-tiaN7.  Chiah chhut-siaN hau2 kio3 lang5 lai5 kiu3, m7-ku2 kio3 chin ku2 long2 bo5.  I chiu7 kiah8 bak8 khoaN3 jit8-thau5, ia7 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 u7 kiu5 jit8-thau5 thoe3 i siat-hoat.  Jit8 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chun2 i sou2 kiu5.  Hut-jian5 hou7 i siuN7 chit8 hang7 e5 ho2 ke3-chhek, sui5-si5 khioh chit8 liap8 chioh8-thau5 kong3-phoa3 hit e5 kim-hi5-ou5, chui2 lau5 moa2 si3-koe3.  Tui3 an2-ni hit e5 gin2-na2 u7 tit-kiu3, put-ti3 hou7 i chim3--si2.  Hit e5 lak8 he3 e5 gin2-na2 chiu7 chhoa7 tit-kiu3-e5 beh khi3 kau i e5 pe7-bu2.  
 Hut-jian5 tu2-tioh8 pun2-sin e5 pe7 chin kiaN, in-ui7 tui3 chhut-si3 kau3 hit-tiap8 m7 bat hou7 in pe7 hiam5, siuN7 hit e5 kim-hi5-ou5 si7 tat8-chiN5 e5 mih8, m7 chai i e5 lau7-pe7 oe7 siu7-khi3 kau3 sim2-mih8 khoan2.  I chiu7 chiong kou3-hng5 e5 gin2-na2 thau5 kau3 be2 sou2 tu2-tioh8 ka7 in pe7 kong2; lau7-pe7 long2 bo5 siu7-khi3, hoan2-tng2 pho7 i o-lo2 i gau5 siuN7 ho2 hoat-tou7.  
 I e5 ti3-sek m7 na7 soe3-han3 chhut-mia5, tiong2-seng5 koh khah choe7 lang5 o-lo2 toa7-toa7 siau3-liam7, keng3 i, chheng-hou choe3 tai7-seng3 tai7-gi7 e5 seng3-jin5.  Tiong-kok e5 Khong2-chu2 chiu7-si7 hit lang5; ia7 mia5 kio3-choe3 Tiong7-ni5, si7 Tiong-kok lang5 sou2 toa7 chun-keng3 e5 seng3-jin5.  
 
Ou Him5

Tai5-oan5 e5 him5 sek-ti7 ou, khah soe3 chiah, toa3 ti7 soaN-nih8 kap chhiu7-na5 lai7.  Ou him5 chin gau5 peh chhiu7, ai3 chhe7 khang e5 chhiu7-sin choh i e5 siu7 lai5 khun3 ti7 khang lai7.  Chhe7 bo5, chiu7 ti7 soaN-khang lai5 choh.  Him5 ai3 chiah8 ke2-chi2, te7 it ai3 chiah8 chiu7-si7 bit8, chin gau5 chhe7 ia2 phang e5 siu7, chiong in e5 bit8 chiah8 liau2-liau2, long2 bo5 lau5 poaN3 te3.  
SoaN lai7 e5 phang choh-siu7 ti7 chhiu7-khang lai7.  Him5 oe7 phiN7-tioh8 bit8 e5 bi7 chiu7 khi3 ka7 chhiu7-bak8 e5 phe5, chhut-lat8 ka7 kui2-na7 tiam2-cheng ku2, long2 bo5 hiam5 kau7 kang.  Na7 ka7-tioh8 khang kau3-giah8 toa7 chiu7 chhun i e5 kha jip8-khi3.  Phang chiu7 pe chhut lai5, teng3 ke3 lai5 teng3 ke3 khi3, him5 e5 mng5 chin chat8, sou2-i2 teng3 chiu7 bo5 chhai2-kang, long2 bo5 kau3 i e5 bah.  I e5 kha na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chhiu2, u7 lai7-lai7 e5 jiau2, ti7 kha-toe2 oe7, thoa chhut phang e5 bit8-pou5, long2-chong2 ka7 in chiah8.  Ou him5 si7 bit8-phang te7-it oan3-hun7--e5.  
Him5 chin gau5 kou3 ka-ki7, cho7 i e5 bin5-chhng5 chin sio chin nng2.  Na7 ti7 khah koaN5 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 jip8 i e5 siu7 khun3 ti7 i song2-song2 e5 bin5-chhng5 kui2-na7 geh8 jit8, kau3 sio-joah8 e5 si7-chun7 chiu7 chhut lai5 tho2-chiah8, leng5-jiok8 ia2-phang e5 lui7, lian5 lang5, him5 ia7 ai3 chiah8.  
 
 
 
 
 
 
Bo5 oe7-seng e5 Hai7

 
 Tak8 ui7 e5 sin-bun5-po3 u7 kong2 kau-chian3 e5 su7 si7 chin hiong5, koh beng2, thai5 lang5 chheng-ban7.  sou2-i2 tak8 kok u7 khai chin choe7 kun-hui3 siat-hoat keng2-chhat, peng i2-kip8 chian3-chun5 lai5 kou3.  Che long2 si7 ho2 hoat-tou7 thang ti5-hong5 tui3-tek8; chong2-si7 lan2 e5 tui3-tek8 m7-na7 tui3 goa7-ui7 lai5.  Koh khah siong-tiong7 chiu7-si7 piN7.  U7 hi3-lo5, tok8-jiat8, koaN5-jiat8, au2-thou3, tng5-a2-piN7, peh8-au5 cheng3.  Tak8 ni5 u7 kui2-na7 chheng ban7 lang5 jiam2-tioh8 chit ho7 tok8-piN7, soa3 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7 pi2 kau-chian3 koh khah siong-tiong7.  I-seng kong2 hit ho7 piN7 tak8 khoan2 long2 u7 piN7-thang5 chiah ti3-kau3 an2-ni.  
 Bi2-kok u7 chit8 ui7 koe-chhi7 soe3-soe3 chha-put-to 8000 lang5 na7-tiaN7.  Hit chhi7-lai7 e5 lang5 sou2 chiah8 e5 chui2 si7 tui3 chit8 e5 chui2-khut lau5-lai5.  Kun7 hit e5 khut u7 lang5 jiam2-tioh8 tng5-a2-piN7, i piN7 e5 si5, chhu3 e5 si3 chiu-ui5 long2 u7 loh8 seh, kou3 e5 lang5 piaN3 i e5 kiaN-lang5 mih8 ti7 seh teng2.  Kau3 sio-lo7 e5 si5 seh chiu7 iuN5 choe3 chui2, lau5 jip8 hit e5 khut, ti3-kau3 bo5 loa7-ku2 chhi7-lai7 e5 lang5 choe7-choe7 jiam2-tioh8 hit ho7 piN7.  I-seng si3-koe3 chha5 chiah chai si7 ui7-tioh8 hit e5 tng5-a2-piN7 e5 lang5 e5 piN7-thang5 te3 seh lau5 jip8 ti7 hit e5 chui2-khut.  Hit-ui7 e5 peh-siN3 ui7-tioh8 chiah8 chit ho7 chui2 u7 1004 lang5 jiam2-tioh8 piN7, ia7 114 lang5 sit-loh8 siN3-mia7, long2 in-ui7 tui3 hit chit lang5 e5 lian5-lui7 kau3 an2-ni.  
 Tiong-kok lang5 m7 chai tioh8 soe3-ji7 in sou2 chiah8 e5 chui2, sou2-i2 ti7 chiN2-piN soe2 kiaN-lang5 saN kap la5-sam5 e5 thang2, soe2 liau2 chui2 piaN3 hiat-kak8 ti7 chiN2-piN.  Hit e5 kiaN-lang5 chui2 koh siap8 jip ti7 chiN2-lai7, a2-si7 chui2-khut i2-kip8 kau-a2 chiu7 siN chhut tok8-thang5.  
Lang5 ti7 koe-chhi7 teh boe7 chiah8-mih8 ia7 bo5 soe3-ji7 chhin-chhiuN7 kam-chia3 a2-si7 pat-mih8 tauh8-tauh8 hou3 chui2 ka7 i soe2 tam5.  Hit e5 chui2 na7 bo5 chheng-khi3 ia7 oe7 hoat siN tok8-thang5.  Koh u7 lang5 ak chhai3 chiong lang5 e5 pun3 lai5 
 
koan3-ak; ia7 beh taN khi3 boe7 chhai3 e5 si5 ti7 chui2-khut soe2, hit e5 chui2 ia7 oe7 siN-chhut tok8-thang5.  Lang5 ui7-tioh8 an2-ni si7 gui5-hiam2, na7 bo5 soe3-ji7 chu2 hit ho7 e5 chhai3 chin kau3-giah8 thiam2, tok8-thang5 boe7 si2.  
U7-si5 lang5 siuN cha2 chu2 he7-the ku2-ku2 bo5 chim-chiok kham3, hou5-sin5 si3-koe3 pe lai5 hioh-teh.  Hou5-sin5 sng3-si7 chin kiaN-lang5 e5 mih8, in-ui7 mih8 na2 chhau3, na2 noa7, i na2 ai3 khi3 hioh, ti3-kau3 i e5 kha bak-tioh8 kiaN-lang5.  Au7-lai5 pe lai5 hioh ti7 chiah8 mih8 hou7 i jiam2-tioh8 tok8-thang5, lang5 chiah8 liau2 chiu7 oe7 thoan5-jiam2.  Ke2-chi2 na7 bo5 sek8 a2-si7 siuN sek8 ti3-kau3 au3-lok, chiah8 liau2 ia7 u7 hai7 ti7-teh.  
 Koh chit8 hang7 koh khah tioh8 kin2-sin7 ti5-hong5-e5, chiu7-si7 oe7 thoan5-jiam2 e5 piN7, ho2 lang5 saN-kap toa3 ia7 oe7 thoan5-jiam2.  U7 si5 chhu3 kek8-oeh8 ia7 lang5 choe7 oh-tit keh li7.  Siat-su2 na7 liah8-choe3 bo5 iau3-kin2, hit e5 hai7 chiu7 na2-toa7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chhut-phiah8, choe7-choe7 lang5 phah-sng3 bo5 iau2-kin2, sou2-i2 long2 bo5 siat-hoat, hou7 bo5 chhut-*e5 saN-kap sio-lam7, ti3-kau3 thoan5-jiam2 na2 choe7.  U7-e5 chhut phiah8 liam5-piN ho2, bo5 iau3-kin2; m7 ku2 na7 tui3 i jiam2-tioh8-*e5, bo5 hiah khoai3 ho2.  U7 si5 chin choe7 hang7 e5 piN7 u7 te3 i lai5 hoat-choh, u7-e5 siong-tiong7 ti7 bak8-chiu ti3-kau3 sit-beng5, u7-e5 pat8 khoan2 e5 hai7 lian5-lui7 kau3 chit8-si3-lang5, a2-si7 ti3-kau3 si2.  TaN tak8 hang7 thoan5-jiam2 e5 piN7 long2 si7 an2-ni; sou2-i2 eng-kai tioh8 chim-chiok than3 oe7-seng e5 hoat-tou7 lai5 po2-hou7 siN3-mia7.  
 
Toa7 lat8 e5 Mih8


 Ti7 toe7-kiu5 lai7 ia7 ti7 lan2 e5 tiong-kan u7 kui2-na7 hang7 chin toa7 lat8, ia7 choe3 lang5 e5 toa7 lou7-eng7, gau5 chan7 lang5 sou2 boe7-oe7 choe3 e5 su7.  
 1.  Kun2-chui2 ian.  He2-hun chhia chiu7-si7 tui3 kun2-chui2 e5 ian chheng3-khi2 lai5, chiah u7 lat8 thang kiaN5.  He2-hun chun5 ia7 si7 an2-ni.  Hit nng7 hang7 choe3 lang5 e5 toa7 pang-chan7.  Ki-khi3 choe7-choe7 si7 eng7 kun2-chui2 ian hou7 i kiaN5.  Na7 teh choe3 e5 si5 chiu7 bo5 khoaN3-kiN3, na7 theng5 kang chiu7 khoaN3 ian phu7-phu7 chheng3.  
 2.  Tian7-khi3.  Na7 kap kun2-chui2 ian saN-pi2 chiu7 koh khah u7 lat8; khah cha2 lang5 bat khoaN3-kiN3 m7 chai thang eng7, toa7-toa7 phah-sng2.  U7-si5 khoaN3 ti7 hun5-*nih8 chau2 chhut lai5, ti7 chui2 lai7 ia7 u7.  Ti7 koe-lai7, koe-lou7, hoe7-sia7 si3-koe3 thang khoaN3 tian7-kng chio3 am3-lou7, tian7-khi3 ia7 oe7 thoa chin tang7 e5 chhia, ia7 oe7 chan7 peng5-iu2 kap peng5-iu2 saN-li7 chin hng7 oe7 saN-kap kong2-oe7 pan7 tai7-chi3.  I-seng ia7 u7 eng7 choe7-choe7 khoan2.  Chong2 kong2 sou2 oe7 choe3, lang5 iau2-be7 u7 kang2-kiu3 kau3 chin7.  
 3.  Chui2.  Chui2 ia7 si7 toa7 lat8 e5 mih8, ia7 oe7 thoa chhia, koh oe7 hou7 ki-khi3 e5 lun5 tng2 kiaN5.  Hai2-bin7 u7 chin toa7 chiah chun5 teh kiaN5, ti7 ho5-*nih8, ou5-*nih8, toa7 khoe long2 u7 chun5.  Chui2 na7 hiong5-lau5 chiu7 oe7 hai7-tioh8 toa7 hang7 e5 mih8, hng7-hng7 thiaN i e5 siaN lang5 chiu7 kiaN si2, in-ui7 chai chau2 boe7-li7.  
 4.  Hong.  Hong ia7 u7 toa7 lat8, ia7 u7 ho2 lou7-eng7; na7 ti7 joah8 e5 sou2-chai7 li-li-a2 hong lang5 ai3, thang chhiu-chhin3.  Na7 u7 hong-thai chin toa7 lat8 hai7-tioh8 chhu3, chhiu7-bak8, ngou2-kok tak8 hang7.  Na7 ti7 hai2-*nih8 koh khah tioh8 kiaN, piNchui2 oe7 piN3 toa7 eng2, chun5 si3-koe3 u7 phoa3 sit-loh8 khi3.  Na7 long2 bo5 hong ia7 si7 toa7 hai7, in-ui7 choe7-choe7 hang7 oa2-kho3 hong hou7 i kiaN5.  Chhin-chhiuN7 phang5-chun5, ia7 u7 chin choe7 sou2-chai7 u7 hoe7-sia7 chioh hong e5 lat8 teh hou7 i kiaN5.  
 5.  Peng.  Oa2 ti7 Pak-kek8 ia7 u7 khah koaN5 e5 sou2-chai7 chiu7 oe7 khoaN3-tioh8 peng e5 lat8.  Hai2-chui2 u7 kiat peng : ho5, khoe kiat kau3 long2 boe7 lau5.  Gu5-chhia, be2-chhia chiu7 u7 kiaN5 ke3 peng e5 teng2-bin7.  Peng kek8 lai7 e5 mih8 oe7 hai7-tioh8 chun5; chin toa7 te3 chioh8 na2 soaN, na7 khap8-tioh8 chiu7 oe7 piak-lih8.  Lang5 bo5 hoat-tit, chi2-u7 jit8 e5 lat8 u7 iaN5 i na7-tiaN7.  
 6.  He2.  He2 e5 lat8 boe7 khah su, si7 ho2 eng7 ia7 u7 toa7 li7-hai7.  Lou7-eng7 sng3 boe7 liau2, hou7 lang5 chu2 chiah8, ia7 hou7 lang5 sio-lou7.  Na7 bo5 soe3-ji7 eng7 chiu7 hai7, he2 sio chhu3 chin thang kiaN.  Na7 ti7 Bi2-kok, Kan-a2-tai7 u7-si5 tui3 lang5 bo5 soe3-ji7 chiu7 sio chhu3, cheng-siN, mih8 kau3 kui2-na7 phou3-lou7 e5 khoah.  
 
 
Tan5 Un-tian2

 
 Ti7 Eng-lan5 e5 tang pak si3 e5 hai2-kiN5, u7 choe7-choe7 toa7 chioh8.  Li7 hoaN7 chit8 phou3-lou7 u7 ji7-chap8 kui2 e5 hai2-su7.  U7-e5 si7 chioh8-poaN5, hoat hai2-chhau2 na7-tiaN7.  Chong2-si7 khah choe7 u7 hoat chhau2-a2 kap hoe, ia7 u7 choe7-choe7 teng2-ho7 e5 hai2 chiau2.  Hi2 ji7-chap8 kui2 e5 hai2-su7 e5 tiong-kan u7 teng-thah nng7-e5.  Li7 hoaN7 te7-it hng7 e5 kio3-choe3 Tng5-chioh8-thah.  M7 na7 u7 teng-thah, ia7 u7 toa7 e5 seng-tang5; tau3-bu7 e5 si5 oe7 chhut toa7-siaN po3 lang5 siam2-pi7; in-ui7 tau3-bu7 e5 si5, jit8-si5 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 hai2-su7, mi5-si5 ia7 boe7 khoaN3-kiN3 teng e5 kng.

 Tai5-oan5 hai2-su7 ti7 Jit8-pun2 e5 sai-lam5 peng5.  Lam5 kau3 pak chha-put-to 240 Eng-li2 e5 tng5, si3-chiu-ui5 long2 si7 hai2.  Toe7 e5 heng5-se3 si7 tng2-tu, lam5 kap pak-pou7 khah oeh8, tiong-pou7 khah khoah.  Tang bin7 khah choe7 si7 soaN, sai bin7 u7 khoah e5 piN5-iuN5.

 Sin-khu tioh8 chaiN2-iuN7 chiau3-kou3 ?  U7 lang5 kong2 tioh8 chiah8, chheng7 sio, kiam2 u7 pat8 hang7 ?  Gun5.  Gun5 bo5 sim2-mih8 khah su kim.  Bi2-kok lam5-pak u7 chhut chit8 ho7 gun5-lui7 e5 mih8.  Na2-ui kok kap i e5 keh-piah kok, ia7 u7 gun5-khong3.  Ian5.  Ian5 si7 chin tang7 e5 mih8; lang5 khah siong5 choe3 tui7-a2 e5 lou7-eng7, chhin-chhiuN7 chui2-siu2 u7 eng7 ian5-tui7 chhi3-khoaN3 hai2-chui2 e5 chhim chhian2. II.   Kim-siok8

 Jiu5-ng5 ia7 tui3 toe7 lau5-chhut-e5, oe7 choe3 kui2-na7 hang7 ioh8 e5 lou7-eng7.  
 He, u7 sou2-chai7 si7 tui3 o5-khak lai5 sio chiah u7; chong2-si7 khah choe7 si7 tui3 khong3-lai7 e5 he-chioh8 lai5 sio.  Choe3 boah-piah, khi2 chhu3, choe3 ioh8, siau tok8 e5 lou7-eng7.  
Kim siok8 e5 chui2.  U7 kok, chin choe7 kim-siok8 e5 chui2 tui3 soaN-*nih8 lau5-chhut-lai5, kun2--e5, sio--e5 leng2--e5, long2 u7.  Ti7 Ou-liap8 soaN lang5 u7 chhe7-tioh8 chit8 ho7 e5 kim-siok8 chin tat8 chiN5, chin oh-tit chhe7.  Sek-ti7 ou-phu2, m7-ku2 chheng-chheng na2 po-le5, ia7 chin chhe3, u7-si5 chhe7 chit8 sut-a2 ti7 gun5 kap ian5 tiong saN pau-teh.  Ti7 O3-kok kap Eng-kok e5 lam5-peng5 u7 bat chhe7-tioh8 tam7-poh8.

Lun7 sio si7 pi2 he2 kui2-na7 pe7 e5 lat8, ia7 chin lai7, he7 ti7 sin-khu oe7 siN thiaN3, oh-tit i.  Chit ho7 e5 kim-siok8 si7 i-seng kap hoa3-hak8 sian-siN e5 toa7 lou7-eng7; li7-ek sng3 boe7 liau2, chong2-si7 kang2-kiu3 iau2-be7 chin7, oh-tit kong2-khi2 i e5 lat8; na7 u7 chit8 sut-a2 boah-tioh8 si5-cheng e5 lun5, chiu7 oe7 kiaN5 100 ni5 ku2 m7-bian2 choan7.  Jit8-pun2 lang5 kong2 ti7 in e5 soaN u7 chit ho7 e5 Radium; ia7 kong2 Tai5-oan5 e5 Pak-tau5 kun2-chui2 lai7 ia7 u7.  
 
 
Lou7-kok e5 Pa-ko-li2


 Lou5 Pa-ko-li2 e5 lau7-pe7 si7 tng toa7 koaN e5 chit. I e5 pe7-bu2 chai i iau2 si7 soe3-han3 gin2-na2, sou2 ke3-bou5-e5 kiam2-chhai2 kui ti7 khang-khang; sou2-i2 m7 chun2 i khi3.  Pa-ko-li2 bo5 sit-bang7, tit8-tit8 kiu5 Siong7-te3 kam2-kek i e5 pe7-bu2 e5 sim kam-goan7 hou7 i khi3.  
 SaN ni5 ku2 e5 tiong-kan pek-chhiat kiu5, bo5 chit8-si5-a2 pang3-sim, ti3-kau3 boe7 chiah8 boe7 khun3-tit; sin-the2 siu7 siong-hai7. Pa-ko-li2 tui3 kou-niu5 kong2 i e5 cheng5-heng5; kou-niu5 thiaN-liau2 thoe3 i sia2 phoe hou7 tiau5-lai7 siang3-ia5 e5 hu-jin5, kong2-khi3 i e5 li2-lek8, koh pun2-sin sang3 i chiuN7 kiaN kiN3 hu-jin5.  Hu-jin5 chiu7 thoe3 i eng5-ui5, hou7 i thang khi3 tiau5-kiN3 Hong5-thai3-hou7.  Hong5-thai3-hou7 eng7 tek8-piat8 e5 un khoan2-thai7 i, kap i tam5-lun7; au7-lai5 chai siong5-se3, un2-chun2 beh thoe3 i chau3 Hong5-te3; soa3 kiong-kip i e5 sou2-hui3.  Ke3 bo5 loa7 ku2 Hong5-te3 ko2-jian5 eng3-un2 kia3 phoe kap lou7-hui3 tiau3 Pa-ko-li2 e5 lau7-pe7 to3-lai5 pun2-kok.  Pa-ko-li2 hoaN-hi2 kau3 boe7 kou3-tit, khi3 poaN3-lou7 tan2-chih i e5 pe7-bu2.  Pe7-bu2 khoaN3 in e5 kiaN2, chiu7 lau5 bak8-sai2, in-ui7 chai kin-a2-jit8 oe7 tit-tioh8 thau2-pang3, tek-khak si7 ui7-tioh8 chit e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 e5 kong-lek8.  
 Pa-ko-li2 siuN7-tioh8 sou2 keng-ke3 e5 su7, chai Siong7-te3 e5 un kek8 toa7, chiu7 koat-i3 beh hian3 sin-khu choe3 Chu2 e5 kang.  In-ui7 i lek8-ke3 hiah choe7 kan-khou2 e5 su7, sin-khu lam2 ti3 piN7; bo5 loa7 ku2 soa3 li7-khui se3-kan kau3 Thian-tong5, tit-tioh8 i e5 siuN2.  
 
 
He2-chhia e5 Khi2-in

He2-chhia si7 toa7 ho2 eng7 e5 khi3-khu7, hou7 lang5 che7 khah seng2 si5, seng2 kang, seng2 sou2-hui3.  Chha-put-to siat ti7 tak8 kok choe3 peh-siN3 e5 toa7 lou7-eng7.  Khah siong5 toa7 hang7 khi3-khu7 si7 tui3 sio2 hang7 lai5 khi2-in.  He2-chhia ia7 si7 an2-ni.  
Eng-kok e5 chhu3 khah choe7 u7 ian-tang5 thang hou7 chau3-kha e5 he2-hun thau2-chhut.  khoan2-sit lioh8-a2 chhin-chhiuN7 hoe7-sia7 khah soe3-keng-e5.  U7 chit8 jit8 chit8 e5 ta-pou gin2-na2 teh pi7-pan7 kun2 chui2 beh ka7 i e5 lau7-bu2 phau3 te5.  Jian5-au7 u7 chit8 e5 ho2 sim e5 lang5 khui lou7 hou7 i khi3 Tai7-hak8 thak8 kau3 chhut-giap8.  Teh thak8 e5 si5 sim-lai7 long2 bo5 pang3-sak i sou2 i3-ai3--e5.  Chheh--nih8 u7 ki3 kong2 i put-si5 teh lian7-sip8, m7 na7 eng7 thih teh tau3, chiu7 ia7 eng7 choa2 teh ui7-iuN7.  
 Kau3 tiong2-seng5 e5 si5 kap chit8 e5 tang5-sim e5 phoaN7 saN-kap khui chit8 e5 thih-khi3 e5 hoe7-sia7, ia7 ko2-jian5 cho7-chiaN5 chit8 tiuN he2-chhia, oe7 kiaN5, ia7 oe7 chai3 lang5 kap mih8.  Toe7-kiu5

Toe7-kiu5 u7 hun gou7-e5 IuN5, lak8-e5 Chiu, chiu7-si7 Pak A-bi2-li7-ka chiu, Lam5 A-bi2-li7-ka chiu, A-hui-li7-ka chiu, O3-tai7-li7-a chiu (si7 siang7 soe3), A-se-a chiu, Au-lo5-pa chiu. Au-lo5-pa chiu u7 ji7-chap8 goa7 kok, u7-e5 toa7, u7-e5 soe3, peh-siN3 chin chat8.  
 Toe7-kiu5 e5 peh-siN3 hun choe3 gou7 cheng2.  U7 ou-sek (phe5-hu long2 ou), chhin-chhiuN7 In3-tou7 cheng2.  Ia7 u7-e5 ang5, ng5, peh8 e5 sek, chhin-chhiuN7 In3-te7-an, Chi-na2, Eng-kok.  
 Toe7-kiu5 tiong Se-chong7 si7 te7-it koaiN5 e5 toe7, koaiN5 kau3 1,5000 chhioh.  ThiN-khi3 ta-so3 koh koaN5, bah khoai3 ta, m7-bian2 eng7 iam5 lai5 siN7.  U7 chui2-ou5 ti7 hia.  Ho5-lan5-se kok chiu7 si7 te7-it ke7 e5 toe7, pi2 hai2 cham2-jian5 khah ke7, sou2-i2 u7 cho7 hai2-chhiuN5, hou7 hai2-chui2 boe7 im-tioh8.  ThiN-khi3 tam5-sip, peh-siN3 chheng-khi3-siuN3, gau5 choe3 seng-li2, koh gau5 khun5-khiam7.

 Te7 it toa7 e5 chui2-chhiang5 ti7 ka-na2-tai7 e5 tang lam5 peng5, khoah 1900 chhioh, koaiN5 160 chhioh.  
 Te7 it tng5 e5 ho5 ti7 Lam5 A-bi2-li7-ka, khoah 96 Eng-li2, tng5 4000 Eng-li2.  Pak A-bi2-li7-ka-e5 kap ti7 Ai-kip8 e5 Ni5-lo5-ho5 si7 chha-put-to piN5 toa7.  
 KiaN-siaN5 Eng-kok e5 Lun5-tun siaN5 si7 te7 it toa7, koh te7 ji7-e5 si7 Tek-kok, te7 saN si7 Hoat-kok.  Tiong-kok e5 Kng2-tang siaN5 u7 khah choe7 lang5-giah8.  Tiong-kok kap Jit8-pun2 e5 kiaN-siaN5 ia7 si7 chin toa7.

Chhiu7-bak8
 
 Ti7 Tiong-kok choe7-choe7 seng2 chhiu7-bak8 khah chio2, sou2-i2 peh-siN3 chin kan-khou2.  Khah cha2 phah-sng3 kiam2-chhai2 u7, in-ui7 lang5 chho3 loh8-lai5 bo5 koh-chai cheng3, chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 khah chio2, ti3-kau3 chin choe7 ui7 taN bo5 lah ! Ng5-ho5 e5 piN5-iuN5, chin toa7 chin khoah, chong2-si7 long2 bo5 chhiu7-bak8.  Na7-si7 thau3 hong Ng5-ho5 e5 chui2 hoat eng2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 hai2 e5 toa7 eng2 chit8-poaN-iuN7.  Na7 loh8 hou7 e5 si5, a2-si7 soaN-*nih8 e5 seh iuN5 lau5 loh8-lai5 chin hiong5.  Ng5-ho5 chio2 pun-chhe, ti3-kau3 pang lang5 e5 chhan5-hng5, chhu3-theh8, peh-siN3 soa3 hou7 chui2 lau5-*khi3, tak8 ni5 sou2 liau2 e5 chiN5 sng3 boe7 liau2.  Siat-su2 ti7 hit e5 ho5-piN na7 khi3 cheng3 chhiu7, chiu7 boe7 an2-ni, in-ui7 chhiu7-bak8 oe7 suh chui2-khi3 chin choe7.  Koh oe7-po2-hou7 thou2-toe7 boe7 pang ham7.  Lau2-lian7 e5 sian-siN teh kong2, na7 kok-ka chhut beng7-leng7 kah peh-siN3 lai5 cheng3 chhiu7, bo5 loa7-ku2 kok-lai7 kui2-na7 seng2 oe7 tit-tioh8 toa7 li7-ek.  
 U7 kok chhin-chhiuN7 A-lat-pek si7 chin khoah e5 toe7, chong2-si7 long2 si7 soa-bou5, in-ui7 bo5 chui2-khi3 ti3-kau3 bo5 chhiu7 bo5 hoat-chhau2, sou2-i2 chin ta-so3, jit8 thau5 iam7, ti3-kau3 soa kek8 sio, lang5 tui3 hia ke3 chin kan-khou2 kau3 kek8.  
 U7 sou2-chai7 in-ui7 bo5 chhiu7-bak8 ku2-ku2 long2 bo5 loh8 hou7, u7-si5 chit8 ni5 ku2 chi2-u7 chit8 pai2 loh8 na7-tiaN7, ia7 u7 sou2-chai7 tioh8 theng3-hau7 saN ni5 ku2 chiah u7 loh8.  Soa-toe7 bo5 thang khui chiN2, ia7 bo5 khut-a2-chui2, si3-koe3 ta khok-khok, kan-khou2 kau3 boe7 kong2-tit.  Chit ho7 e5 toa7 khiam3 m7 si7 chhut tui3 pat8 mih8 e5 in-toaN, long2 si7 in-ui7 bo5 chhiu7-bak8 e5 goan5-in.  
 Na7 u7 chhiu7-bak8, toe7 oe7 tam5-sip, koh pui5.  Ngou2-kok oe7 bou7-seng7, ho2 siu-seng5.  
 Iu5-thai3 kok cha2-cheng5 u7 chin choe7 chhiu7, u7-e5 si7 chin chhut mia5 e5 chhiu7, siu7 tioh8 tui3-tek8 chho3 loh8-lai5 bo5 koh cheng3, ti3-kau3 kok lai7 choe7-choe7 ui7 piN3 choe3 khong3-ia2.  
 Ai-kip8 kok ia7 u7 toa7 e5 khong3-ia2, khah choe7 si7 soa-toe7, in-ui7 khiam3 hou7 chui2 bo5 chhiu7-bak8.  
 Chong2 lai5 kong2, Tai5-oan5 kap PhiN5-ou5 saN pi2.  Tai5-oan5 e5 chhiu7-bak8 khah chhiong-seng7, tui3 an2-ni thou2-toe7 khah pui5, ngou2-kok ni5-ni5 ho2 siu.  PhiN5-ou5 long2 bo5 chhiu7-bak8, na7 khi2 hong chin thau3, ti3-kau3 ngou2-kok phah hai7, m7 na7 bo5 ho2 siu-seng5, tauh8-tauh8 sit-siu, che long2 si7 in-ui7 bo5 cheng3-chhiu7 e5 in-toaN.  TaN siat-su2 u7 sou2-chai7 chhiu7-bak8 siuN choe7, u7 sou2-chai7 siuN chio2, ia7 u7 sou2-chai7 long2 bo5, ai3 keng2 to2 chit8 sou2-chai7 choe3 khah ho2 ? Oe7-seng kap Chhu3
 
 Oe7-seng u7 kui2-na7 khoan2, chiong5-tiong lai5 kong2-khi2 chhu3 iah2 put-chi2 toa7 iau3-kin2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Chhu3 si7 lang5 khia7-khi2 e5 sou2-chai7, na7 bo5 tu2-ho2 chiu7 lang5 tiam3 hit e5 chhu3, sin-the2 siong5-siong5 boe7 peng5-an.  
 Tiong-kok lang5 e5 chhu3 khah choe7 thang-a2 kap mng5 chin soe3, koh jit8-si5 jit8-kng chio3 boe7 tioh8, mi5-si5 mng5-hou7 koh koaiN-bat8, chiu7 lian5 chit8 tiam2-a2 khong-khi3 ia7 boe7 tit thang liu5-thong.  Hit e5 khong-khi3 e5 tiong-kan u7 kui2-na7 hang7 ti7-teh, chiu7-si7 u7 sng-sou3,chi2-sou3 kap than3-khi3, saN hang7 e5 tiong-kan sng-sou3 kap chi2-sou3 si7 te7-it choe7.  Chit nng7 chiong2 si7 bo5 heng5-sek, bo5 bi7-sou3 e5 khi3.  Toe7-bin7 tak8 hang7 e5 tong7-but8 a2-si7 sit8-but8, na7 bo5 oa2-kho3 chit e5 khi3 chiu7 bo5 oah8-mia7.  Sou2-i2 lang5 tioh8 siong5-siong5 pou2-khip sin-sian e5 khong-khi3 jip8 kau3 hi3 e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7 lang5 oe7 chin2-khi2 cheng-sin5.  
 Hit e5 than3-khi3 si7 u7 tok8, na7 u7 ho2 e5 khong-khi3, than3-khi3 chiu7 khah chio2, sng-sou3 chiu7 khah choe7.  Lang5 sou2 chhoan2 chhut-e5 u7 hit e5 than3-khi3 pi2 khong-khi3 tiong-kan e5 than3-khi3 khah choe7, sou2-i2 lang5 na7 ti7 bat8-bat8 e5 sou2-chai7 bo5 ku2 hit e5 than3-khi3 chiu7 moa2-moa2 lang5 chiu7 boe7 oah8.  
 M7-na7 lang5 tioh8 eng7 hit e5 khong-khi3, chiu7 hit e5 teng a2-si7 chek oe7 toh8 iu5-goan5 si7 hit e5 khong-khi3 hou7 i toh8.  An2-ni na7-si7 chit8 keng chhu3 e5 lai7-bin7 chit8 pha teng chiu7-si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ke saN si3 e5 lang5.  Si3 gou7 ki lah8-chek si7 chhin-chhiuN7 ke chit8 e5 lang5 teh tau3 chiah8 hit e5 sng-sou3.  
 Na7-si7 chhu3-lai7 ou-am3, khong-khi3 boe7 liu5-heng5 chiu7 koaN5-jiat8 cheng3 a2-si7 hi3-piN7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 piN7 e5 thang5 chiu7 chin gau5 hoat-siN.  Phi3-ju7 na7 theh8 hi3-piN7 e5 thang5 khng3 ti7 jit8-kng e5 sou2-chai7 hit e5 thang5 chiu7 liam5-piN si2.  
 Lang5 na7-si7 ai3 khah u7 oe7-seng chiu7 tioh8 tui3 chhu3 lai5 siu-cheng2, tai7-seng tioh8 chhong3 thang-a2 khah toa7-e5, hou7 khong-khi3 liu5-thong, kap jit8-kng oe7 chio3 jip8-lai5.  Phah-sng3 Tai5-oan5 lang5 e5 hoe-thang kap thoah-thang si7 put-chi2 ho2, chong2-si7 koaN5-thiN lang5 ai3 koaiN in-ui7 si7 kiaN koaN5.  TaN beh chaiN2-iuN7 chhong3 hou7 i boe7 koaN5 ?  Tioh8 tui3 lai7-bin7 chhong3 chit8 te3 pang tui3 thang-a2 ui5 chhu-chhu khi2-lai5, hou7 koaN5-khi3 tui3 thang-a2 khi2 chiah boe7 koaN5, chong2-si7 khong-khi3 oe7 liu5-thong.  In-ui7 lang5 sou2 chhoan2-chhut e5 khong-khi3 si7 khah khin, sou2-i2 oa2 thian-lo5-pan2 e5 e7-kha tioh8 chhong3 chit8 e5 hoe-thang a2-si7 thoah-thang hou7 phaiN2 e5 khong-khi3 oe7 chhut-khi3.  
 Chhu3-lai7 thou5-kha kap chhu3-piN e5 kau-a2 na7 siong5-siong5 tam5 chiu7 oe7 siN bang2, a2-si7 tak8 hang7 e5 tok8-thang5 oe7 hai7 lang5 chin choe7.  Sou2-i2 na7-si7 ai3 thou5-kha boe7 sip tioh8 phou-chng a2-si7 boah iuN5-he, na7-si7 beh koh khi2 sin-e5 tioh8 tah8 te7-pan2 li7 thou5 nng7 saN chhioh, hou7 sip-khi3 boe7 khi2-lai5 chiu7 lang5 e5 seng-khu oe7 iong2.  
 
 
Sin O-lian5 e5 Ma2-ka-lek8
 
 Lin2 na7 khi3 Bi2-chiu e5 Hap8-chiong3-kok, tui3 O-lian5 seng2 keng-ke3 khoaN3 in sui2 e5 O-lian5 siaN5, chiu7 un2-tang3 u7 lang5 oe7 chhoa7 lin2 loh8 khi3 kau3 siaN5-lai7 e5 kou2-chhi7.  Ti7-hia iau2 u7 gun5-hang5, tiam3, kap lu2-koan2, ia7 ti7 si3-kak thau5 u7 chioh8-siong7 oe7 po3 li2 khoaN3.  Chit e5 siong7 si7 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 che7 ti7 ke7-ke7 e5 kau-i2, eng7 chhiu2 lam2 chit8 e5 gin2-na2 the oa2 i e5 sin-khu.  Ti7 e7-toe2 boeh ki3 tam7-poh8 chit lang5 e5 siN-seng5 kap lai5-lek8.  
 Chit e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 bo5 thang ho2 khoaN3, kha chheng7 kau7-kau7 chhou-iong2 e5 oe5, sou3-sou3 e5 i-chiu5, moa soe3 tiau5 e5 keng-thau5 kun, thau5-khak u7 ti3 chit8 teng2 na2 hong-bo7 e5 khoan2, heng5-the2 oe2-oe2 pui5-pui5, bin7 si3-pang hou7-tiong7, chong2-si7 khoaN3 i e5 bak8-chiu chin un-jiu5 e5 khoan2-sit.  
 TaN lun7 chit e5 siong7 u7 chin ki-iN7 e5 su7.  Che si7 Bi2-kok chhong3 lai5 keng3-liam7 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 thau5 chit-e5.  Lian5 Au chiu ki3-liam7 hu7-jin5 lang5 e5 chioh8-pi, to chin chio2, khiok u7 chit8 poaN3-e5 si7 mia5-siaN e5 Lu2-ong5 a2-si7 chheng7 sui2 saN e5 kong-chu2.  Chong2-si7 ti7 Sin O-lian5 chit e5 siong7 long2 m7 si7 an2-ni.  
 I e5 mia5 kio3 choe3 Ma2-ka-lek8.  Soe3-han3 e5 si5 i e5 pe7-bu2 chiu7 si2, u7 hou7 nng7 e5 san3-hiong jin5-ai3 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 chhi7.  Kau3 toa7 han3 chhut ke3 siN chit8 e5 gin2-na2.  Chong2-si7 bo5 loa7-ku2 i e5 tiong7-hu si2, kiaN2 ia7 soa3 si2-khi3, kou-toaN chhun i ka-ki7.  I sui-jian5 san3-hiong m7 ku2 chin iong2-kiaN7 gau5 choe3 kang.  
 Tui3 thiN-kng kau3 e7-hng thau3-jit8 ti7 soe8 saN-a2 tiam3 ut saN.  Tak8 jit8 choe3 kang e5 si5 tui3 thang-a2 khoaN3-kiN3 keh-piah kou-ji5 iN7 e5 kou-ji5 teh choe3 kang thit-thou5.  Put-heng7 siaN5-lai7 khi2 un-ek, lang5 si2 chin choe7, ti3-kau3 kou-ji5 it-hoat choe3.  Kou-ji5 iN7 bo5 hoat-tit thang siu-iong5 chiau3-kou3.  Tng hit-si5 O-lian5 siaN5 e5 peh-siN3 sit8-chai7 u7 khiam3 chit ho7 ho2 sim e5 peng5-iu2 lai5 pang-chan7.  Li2 un2-tang3 m7 kaN2 phah-sng3 tiam3 ti7 soe2 saN-a2 tiam3 choe3 kang san3-hiong e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 oe7 pang-chan7 in, chong2-si7 Ma2-ka-lek8 ko2-jian5 oe7.  I liam5-piN khi3 chhe7 hiah e5 koan2 kou-ji5 iN7 e5 chi2-be7 ka7 in kong2, i beh thoe3 in choe3 kang, koh chiong i e5 kang-chiN5 tam7-poh8 beh pang-chan7 in.  I chhut-lat8 choe3 kang, tui3 i e5 kang-chiN5 liap8-chek tam7-poh8, chiong chiah-e5 khi3 boe2 nng7 chiah gu5-bu2 kap chit8 tiuN chhia.  Tui3 hit-tiap8 i tak8 cha2-khi2 chai3 gu5-leng khi3 boe7, soa3 tui3 lu2-koan2 a2-si7 ho2-giah8 lang5 e5 chhu3 tho2 mi7-pau toa3 to3-lai5 hou7 kou-ji5 iN7 iau e5 gin2-na2 chiah8.  Kui2-na7 pai2 ti7 kek8 oh ke3 e5 si5, chiu7-si7 kou-ji5 sou2 u7-e5 kan-ta si7 chiah-e5 na7-tiaN7.  Kou-ji5 iN7 tak8 le2-pai3 tit-tioh8 Ma2-ka-lek8 sou2 kia3 e5 chiN5.  Ke3 bo5 kui2 ni5 au7 u7 siu-li2 cham2-jian5 khah toa7 keng, khah chiaN5 mih8.  
 Ma2-ka-lek8 si7 chin gau5 choe3 seng-li2.  ia7 chin khun5-khiam7, sui-jian5 hian3 hiah choe7 chiN5, i iau2-ku2 u7 chhun, u7 koh boe2 khah choe7 gu5 than3 khah choe7, eng7 hiah e5 chiN5 koh khi2 chit8 keng chhu3, thang chiap-lap8 soe3-han3 e5 kou-ji5 eN-a2. Che3-cho7 Choa2

 Lang5 u7 eng7 choe7-choe7 mih8 choe3 choa2.  Ti7 Tiong-kok khah siong5 si7 eng7 chiN2-chiN2 e5 tek; ti7 Se-kok na7 beh choe3 khah ho2 e5 choa2 chiu7 u7 eng7 pou3, si7 lang5 sou2 pang3-sak e5 ku7 i-chiuN5, hit ho7.  TaN beh lioh8-a2 gi7-lun7 chiong pou3 choe3 choa2 e5 hong-hoat.
