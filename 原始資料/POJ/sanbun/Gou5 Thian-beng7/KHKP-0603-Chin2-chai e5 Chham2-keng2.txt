
Chin2-chai e5 Chham2-keng2
(Lau5 A-siu3)
Si3 goeh8 21 jit8 cha2-khi2 6 tiam2 khong3-cheng, hut-jian5 toe7 toa7 tang7, hit jit8 goa2 ti7 Sai-tham5, toa3 ti7 Sou Chhun-seng7 Chip-su7 tau, i e5 chhu3 si7 nng7-chan3 echh5u, goa2 ti7 lau5-kha khun3, tu2 beh khi2-chhng5, tu2-tioh8 toe7 toa7 tang7, i e5 chhu3 liam5-piN to2--loh8-lai5, goa2 kin2 bih ti7 mng5-khia7, chiah bian2 siu7-si2, i e5 ke-lai7 kap goa2 kiong7 11 lang5, chi2-u7 goa2 kap Chhun-seng7 e5 toa7-kiaN2 12 he3 siu7-siong, ki5-u5--e5 9 lang5 long2 bo5 siu7-siong, che beng5-beng5 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 po2-hou7.  Sai-tham5 e5 chng u7 kui2-chap8 hou7 na7-tiaN7, thang kong2 chhu3 long2 to2--khi3, lan2 e5 pai3-tng5 ia7 to2--khi3.  Hit sou2-chai7 u7 hian7 si2 kui2-na7 lang5, goa2u siu7-siong ti7 thau5-khak kap chhiu2,Chhun-seng7 e5 kiaN2 ia7-si7 ti7 thau5-khak kap seng-khu siu7-siong, jin5-su7 put-seng2.Chhu3 to2 e5 si5, goa2 ti7 lau5-kha oah8-tai5,toe7-tang7 soah, chiah tui3 chhu3-khang pe5--chhut-lai5, goa2 e5 siong keng-ke3 chap8-goa7 jit8, chiu7 long2 ho2, chin kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.
Toe7-tang7 au7 tiam2-goa7 cheng, lioh8-a2 khah chu7-chai7, teh khoa3-lu7 Kong-koan2-chng e5 kau3-hoe7 kap ke-lai7, chiu7 thoa-mia7 kiaN5-lou7 poaN5-soaN tng2-khi3, kiaN5-lou75 tiam2 cheng ku2, in-ui7 iau2-be7 khi2-chhng5, goa2 e5 saN-khou3, bo7-a2, si5-pio2, oe5, Seng3-keng, Seng3-si, it-khai3 tai5 ti7 goa2 khun3 e5 sou2-chai7, kan-ta chheng7 lai7-saN lai7-khou3 pe5--chhut-lai5, seng-khu bo5 chit8-sian2, bo5 ioh8 thang kou5, ai3 kiaN5-lou7, bo5 chheng7 oe5 boe7 kiaN5, chiah ka7 lang5 pun chit8-siang un7-tong7-oe5 lai5 chheng7.  Bo5 ioh8, bo5 ioh8-pou3 thang pau, chiah ka7 lang5 pun bin7-kun lai5 pau.  Hit jit8 bo5 loh8-hou7, si3-ke3 pang-soaN, ti3-kau3 bo5 lou7 thang kiaN5,khoe-chui2 ia7 lo5-lo5, chhin-chhiuN7 choe3 hong-thai e5 lo5-chui2, che si7 boe7-bat khoaN3-kiN3 e5 su7.  Chit8-e7 tng2 kau3-ui7, khoaN3-kiN3 Kong-koan2 e5 chhu3 choan5-biat8, khoaN3-kiN3 pai3-tng5 long2 phoa3-liau2-liau2, goa2 e5 ka-chok8 khia7 ti7 hu7-kun7 e5 thiN-tiong-ng, boe7 kim3-tit chhut-siaN thi5-khau3; goa2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 Chhun-kiau chhoa7 goa2 khi3 Kong-hak8-hau7 houki7u-hou7-pan hok8-ioh8.  Chhu3 to2, koe-loub7o lou7 thang kiaN5, tioh8 kiaN5 chhan5-hoaN7 kau3 Kong-hak8-hau7, khoaN3-kiN3 ti7 un7-tong7-tiaN5 e5 siu7-siong-chia2 moa2-tiaN5, si2--e5 ia7 kui-tui, koan-bok8 ia7 chin-choe7, u7-e5 bo5 koan-bok8 thang toe2, eng7 pang teng3 choe3 koan-bok8, ia7 u7-e5 eng7 chhau2-chhioh8 pau,thi5-khau3 e5 siaN, ai-chhan e5 siaN moa2 hi7-khang kiN3-tioh8 peng5-iu2 chhin-lang5 long lau5 bak8-sai2.  Chit pai2 goa2 sou2 lau5 e5 bak8-sai2, pi2 si2 pe7-bu2 sou2 lau5--e5 khah choe7, sou2-u7 e5 chham2-keng2, m7-si7 eng7 pit sia2 oe7 chin7.  Kong-koan2-chng sou2 sun2-sit e5 chai5-san2 sng3 boe7 liau2, lang5 siu7-siong--e5 1000 goa7, si2--e5 oa2 300 lang5, kau3 kin-a2-jit (5: 14 ), siong-tiong7 e5 siu7-siong-chia2 iau2 chin-choe7, lim5-si5 piN7-iN7 iau2 kui2-na7 ui7,hian7-si5 tak8 lang5 iau2 toa3 ti7 lim5-si5 e5 liau5-a2.
Lan2 e5 kau3-hoe7 ia7 toa7 sun2-hai7, hoe7-iu2 20 goa7 hou7, chi2-u7 nng7 hou7 toa3 ti7 khah lai7-soaN--e5, chhu3 bo5 to2, u7 20 hou7 chhut3o, bo5 chhu3 thang toa3 ; hoe7-iu2 siu7 teh-si2--e5 chit8 mia5, siu7-siong--e5 5 mia5,hian7-si5 nng7 mia5 tang7-siong--e5 iau2 beh7o, ti7 lim5-si5 piN7-iN7 ti7-liau5.  Sui-si7 an2-ni,lan2 hoe7-iu2 e5 si2-siong-sou3 khah chio2, chesi7 Siong7-chu2 e5 tek8-piat8 un-kou3.  Goan2 siu7 chai-hai7, u7 tit-tioh8 liat8-ui7 e5 thiaN3 chin-choe7:  U7 Ui2 Bok8-su, KaiSian-siN, Te3 I-seng, Chhi5 Bok8-su, Jit8 Ki Chheng-lian5-hoe7, Tai5-lam5 Ki-tok-kau3 Chheng-lian5-hoe7, Tai5-lam5 Sin5 Hak8-hausian7-siN kap hak8-seng, ia7 Khou2 Hong5-boSian5-siN, TeN7 Chhong-kok Sian-siN, Tai5-pak Sin5 Hak8-hau7 sian-siN kap hak8-seng,Koa Sian-siN-niu5 teng2, kok thoan5-the2 o5-lek8 kau3 chai-hai7 toe7 lai5 ui3-bun7 goan2,ia7 u7 choe7-choe7 ha7-su3 kian3-bu2-kim kapbut8-phin2, ia7 tit-tioh8 Ma2-kai PiN7-iN7 Su5 I-seng sang3 ioh8-phin2 lai5 pang-chan7 goan2, ia7 u7 tit-tioh8 choe7-choe7 ui3-bun7 ephoe5.  TaN goa2 choe3 pe3-hoe7 e5 tai7-piau2,sia2 kui2-ji7 keng3-i3 ka7 liat8-ui7 seh-sia7.  Goa2 hian7-si5 iau2 toa3 ti7 7, 8 chhioh khoah e5 liau5-a2, u7 tit-tioh8 Kau3-su7-hoe7 e5 thiaN3,thoe3 goan2 siat-hoat khi2 lim5-si5 a-ian5,pang-a2 e5 siok-sia3 kap le2-pai3-sou2, ng3-bang7 koh bo5 kui2-jit8, oe7 khi2 ho2, ia7 ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 oe7 thoe3 goan2 ki5-to2, kiu5 Siong7-chu2 khui-lou7, hou7 goan2 u7 hok8-heng e5 ki5.
