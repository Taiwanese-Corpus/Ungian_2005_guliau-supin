
Nga2-kok khi3 kiN3-tioh8 Lu2-ong5
Ti7 Sou-kek-lan5 u7 chit8-e5 gin2-na2 mia5 kio3  Nga2-kok.  I-e5 pe7-bo2 i2-keng koe3-ong2 lah.   Kho2-sioh bo5 peng5-iu2 thang chiu3-kou3 i.  
Chit-e5 gin2-na2 ai3 jip8-oh8 khi3 thak8-chheh siu7 ka3-si7; m7-ku2 bo5 lang5 thang  the3 i chhut-chiN5, sou2-i2 be7-tang3 khi3 thak8.   I siong5-siong5 teh hoan5-lo2 chit-chan5 tai7- chi3, kau3 u7 chit8-jit8 i ka-ti7 siuN7 kong2,  ¡§TaN goa2 bo5 pe7-bo2 lah, iah8 bo5 chhin-chhek lah, goa2 beh khi3 kiN3 goan2 e5 Lu2- ong5, kiu5 i pang-chan7 goa2.¡¨  
I an2-ni kong2, si7 in-ui7 chai hit-e5 Lu2-ong5 put-cho2 ho2 sim, iah8 gau5 lin5-bin2 the2- thiap san3-hiong lang5.  
Sou2-i2 chit-e5 gin2-na2 u7 kiaN5-kau3 Lu2-ong5  sou2 toa3 e5 sou2-chai7.  Ti7 ong5-kiong e5 mng5- khau2 u7 chit8 mia5 peng teh pe2-siu2.  I khoaN3 gin2-na2 beh jip8--khi3, chiu7 liam5-piN  chou2-chi2, hoah i kong2, ¡§Li2 ai3-beh chhong3  sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3?  M7-thang jip8--khi3.¡¨  
Nga2-kok in3 kong2, ¡§Goa2 ai3 khi3 kiN3  Lu2-ong5, chhiaN2 li2 hou7 goa2 jip8--khi3.¡¨  
Hit-e5 peng-teng kan-ta chhio3 i kong2,  ¡§Kham2-me2 khi3 ah!  Li2 be7-kham-tit kiN3 i.¡¨  
Nga2-kok thiaN-kiN3 an2-ni, chiu7 iu-bun7 to3-tng2-khi3, ian5-lou7 kiaN5, ian5-lou7 hau2, ka-ti7  teh phah-sng3 kau3 chia si7 bo5 lou7-eng7 lah.  
Tu2-tu2 teh-beh theng5-kha, u7 chit8 lang5  tui3 i bin7-cheng5 keng-koe3; khoaN3-kiN3  Nga2-kok iu-thau5 khou2-bin7, chiu7 mng7 i  kong2, ¡§Gin2-na2 ah, li2 in-ui7 sim2-mih8 tai7- chi3 teh iu-bun7 kau3 an2-ni?¡¨  
Nga2-kok chiu7 chiong i-e5 tai7-chi3 long2- chong2 ka7 i kong2.  Hit-e5 lang5 chiu7 an-ui3  i kong2, ¡§TaN li2 thang an-sim, m7-bian2  hoan5-lo2, goa2 beh chhoa7 li2 kau3 Lu2-ong5 e5  bin7-cheng5.¡¨  
I chiu7 in3 kong2, ¡§M7-ku2 ti7 mng5-khau2  u7 chit8-e5 peng-teng, i m7 hou7 goa2 jip8--khi3.¡¨  
Hit-e5 lang5 kong2, ¡§Li2 m7-bian2 khoaN3-lu7, m7-sai2 kiaN hit-e5 peng-teng m7 hou7 li2  
jip8-- khi3; li2 tioh8 oa2-kho3 goa2, goa2 un2-tang3  chhoa7 li2 kau3 Lu2-ong5 e5 bin7-cheng5.¡¨  
Kong2-liau2 chiu7 khan Nga2-kok e5 chhiu2,nng7 langch5o-hoe2 kiaN5 kau3 mng5-khau2.  Hit-tiap8 peng-teng long2 m7-kaN2 chou2- tong3 i, koh ka7 hit-e5 chhoa7-i e5 lang5 kiaN5-le2.  
KiaN5 u7 hiah hng7, koh khoaN3-kiN3 chit8- e5 peng-teng ti7-hia teh khia7.  Nga2-kok e5  sim iu7-koh loe2-chi3.  
I chiu7 kong2, ¡§Ti7  hia iau2 u7 chit8-e5 peng-teng, li2 phah-sng3  i beh hou7 lan2 jip8--khi3 a2-m7?¡¨  I kong2, ¡§M7-sai2 kiaN, goa2 ka7 li2 kong2, tioh8 oa2-kho3 goa2, koat-toan3 e7-thang jip8.¡¨  
TaN hit-tiap8 Nga2-kok siuN7 be7-hiau2-tit,  m7-chai chit-e5 lang5 si7 chi7-chui7; iah8 m7- chai ho2 khoan2-thai7 i kau3 an2-ni, si7 ui7-tioh8 sim2-mih8 ian5-kou3.  
Sou2-i2 chiu7 ho2-taN2 lai5 mng7.  Hit-e5 lang5  kong2, ¡§Goa2 si7 Lu2-ong5 e5 kiaN2.¡¨  
Nga2-kok thiaN-kiN3 chit-ku3-oe7, chiu7  liam5-piN chai i un2-tang3 e7 chhoa7 i ti7 Lu2- ong5 e5 bin7-cheng5; sou2-i2 bo5 koh khoa3-lu7;  hoan2-tng2 hoaN-hi2 hou7 Thai3-chu2 tit8-tit8  chhoa7 i kau3 Lu2-ong5 e5 pang5.  
Lu2-ong5 kap i kong2-oe7, iah8 put-chi2 ho2 khoan2-thai7 i; kau3 au7-lai5 chiau3 Nga2-kok  sou2 i3-ai3, hou7 i jip8-oh8 thek8-chheh sia2-ji7, i2- kip8 hak8-sip8 pat8-mih8 e5 ka3-si7; kau3 toa7- han3 e5 si5-hau7 chiu7 seng5-khi3, chiaN5-cho3 u7  toa7 lou7-eng7 e5 lang5.  
TaN lan2 tai7-ke chiong chit-e5 gin2-na2 e5  su7 thang lai5 siuN7 chit8-hang7 te7-it iau3-kin2  e5 tai7-chi3, chiu7-si7 lan2 tioh8 oa2-kho3 lan2 e5  Chu2 Ia5-sou, chhin-chhiuN7 Nga2-kok oa2- kho3 hit-e5 Lu2-ong5 e5 kiaN2 chit8-poaN-iuN7.  
Lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou si7 ThiN-teng2 Siong7- te3 e5 KiaN2; lan2 na7 oa2-kho3 i, i un2-tang3 e7- tit-thang in2-chhoa7 lan2 kau3 Siong7-te3 e5  bin7-cheng5, iah8 e7 hou7 lan2 ti7 ban7 Ong5 e5  Ong5 e5 sou2-chai7 khia7-khi2 kau3 eng2-oan2.
Eng2-oan2 kap Chu2 khia7-khi2,  
ChiaN3-si7 goa2 sim sou2 goan7;  
Thian-tong5 tng5 oah8, ban7 se3 bo5 si2,  
Hiong2-hok tit8 kau3 eng2-oan2. 
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3,te7 63 tiuN, 1890 ni5 8 geh8)
