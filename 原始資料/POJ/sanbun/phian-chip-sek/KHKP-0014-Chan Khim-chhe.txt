Chan Khim-chhe
Sou3-chap8-ni5 cheng5 Tiong-kok kap Goa7- kok bo5 sim2-mih8 thong-siong ong2-lai5, chiam7-chiam7 khah u7 kau-chiap.  Kau3 kui2-na7-ni5 cheng5 Tiong-kok u7 chhe chit8- e5 Khim-chhe, chiu7-si7 Chan Sip8-hou5, khi3 tiam3 ti7 Se-kok; tu2-gu7 Tiong-kok chham Goa7-kok u7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3, long2 si7 tiong7 i tiam3 hia kap in gi7-lun7.  U7 cho3 kui2- na7 chan5 hou7 cheng3-lang5 put-chi2 o-lo2; kau3 kin-ni5 jim7 moa2, teh-beh to3--lai5, chiu7 chiong Goa7-kok e5 te7-hng kiaN5-iu5. Khi3 kau3 Pak--si3, Sou-kiat-lan5, u7 kiaN- siaN5 mia5 cho3 It-teng-pit-ni5-kek.  Ti7-hia tu2-tioh8 TiuN2-lo2-kau3 Bok8-su chham TiuN2-lo2 kui2-na7 pah lang5 chu7-chip8 Tai7-hoe7.  I ia7 u7 jip8-khi3 khoaN3, tai7-ke hoaN-hi2 chiap-lap8--i; ia7 thok-tiong7 chit8-e5 Bok8-su theh8 chit8-pun2 Seng3-chheh sang3--i, chit8-peng5 si7 Tng5-lang5-ji7, chit8-peng5 si7 Eng-kok ji7. I ia7 hoaN-hi2 siu; chong2-si7 i khah be7- Hiau2--tit kong2 goan2 e5 oe7, chiu7 choan2- thok i-e5 Su-ia5 the3 i she to-sia7, ia7 soa3 kong2, i khi3 hia kiN3-tioh8 goan2 e5 lang5 put- chi2 hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 i chai Sou-kiat-lan5 e5 Kau3-su lai5 Tiong-kok si7 toa7 li7-ek chiah- e5 peh-siN3.     
Iu5-goan5 ti7 hit-tiap8, hit-e5 siaN5 u7 che7- che7 lang5 chu7-chip8, ai3 thiaN-kiN3 Tai5-oan5 chham Tng5-soaN kau3-hoe7 e5 siau-sit. Chiong5-tiong An I-seng ti7-hia u7 tai7- liok8 kong2 Tai5-oan5 kau3-hoe7 e5 tai7-chi3. In-ui7 thiaN-kiN3 Chan Sip8-hou5 u7 tu2-ho2 kau3 ti7 in-e5 siaN5, in chiu7 sun7-soa3 sia2 chit8-tiuN- ji7, kong2 i lai5 Sou-kiat-lan5 si7 chin-ho2, in- ui7 hia u7 che7-che7 lang5 khi3 Tiong-kok thoan5 to7-li2; chiu7 chiong chit-tiuN ji7 kau- tai7 cheng5 ti7 SoaN3-thau5 Lai5 I-seng chham Ki2 Bok8-su khi3 pai3-tham3 Chan Sip8-hou5. Chan Sip8-hou5 chiu7 koh hoe5-phoe lai5 hou7 in, chiu7-si7 kong2:---     
：Lin2 in-ui7 si7 Tiong-kok e5 ho2 peng5-iu2 u7 kia3 chit8-tiuN ji7 lai5 hou7 goa2 chin hoaN-hi2 siu.  Hian7-si5 pe3-kok kap kui3-kok put- chi2 ho5-hap8, chiu7-si7 goa2 lai5 ia7-si7 tiau5-ti5 beh an-pai5 chit-e5 su7; taN goa2 e5 sou2-cho3 chin- chiaN3 si7 sio2-khoa2, na7-si7 lin2 chiah-e5 hoaN- hi2 lai5 chun-chheng goa2, goa2 sit8-chai7 ti3-i3 hoe5-phoe ka7 lin2 seh to-sia7.  Lin2 Sou- kiat-lan5 e5 Kau3-su sou2 u7 khi3 Tiong-kok put-chi2 thiaN3-tioh8 goan2 hiah-e5 lang5, chiu7- si7 goa2 chham peh-siN3 pun2-te2 chai.  Chong2-si7 goan2 lang5 khah-che7 bo5  
lai5 chit-peng5, kiaN-liau2 khah bo5 chhim-chai; heng7-tit goa2 kin-a2-jit8 lai5 chia, chham lin2 ong2- lai5 kau-chiap, chiu7 na2 chin chai lin2 chhe lang5 khi3 goan2 hit-peng5 sit8-chai7 chin ho2- i3 thang o-lo2.     
：Lin2 sou2 kia3--lai5 e5 ji7 koh u7 toa7 o-lo2 goa2 chham peh-siN3 long2 u7 chai5-cheng5, chiok-kian3 goan2 e5 lang5 pun2-jian5 si7 chin7- lat8 ai3 beh hou7 lin2 hoaN-hi2.  TaN koh tit- tioh8 lin2 o-lo2 chiaN3 si7 bian2-le7 goan2 it- hoat tioh8 chin7-sim chin7-lat8, hou7 lin2 chham goa2 nng7-kok e5 peh-siN3 na2 e7 chhin-jiat8 kau-poe5.     
：TaN than3 iau2-boe7 li7-khui it-teng-pit-ni2-kek siaN5 goa2 keng3-i3 hoe5 phoe hou7 lin2. ：
(Tai5-lam5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 14 tiuN, 1886 ni5 8 geh8)
