Tai5-oan5 Te7-tang7
Ti 4 goeh8 14 ho7 koh te7-tang7, u7 lang5 kong2 chit pang e5 te7-tang7 pi2 cheng5-goeh8 khah tang7.¡]Cheng5-pang te7-tang7 si7 ti7 3 goeh8 17 ho7, m7 -si7 3 goeh8 20 ho7).Chit-jit8 Ka-gi7 thiaN koan2-hat e5 sou2-chai7 koh to2 chhu3 552 keng; to2 bo5 li7 e5, 947 keng; toa7 phah-phaiN2 kap sio2-khoa2 sun2-hai7 e5, kiong7 1313 keng. Teh si2 6 lang5; tang7-khiu-siong 34 lang5.  Ti7 kin7 Ka-gi7 lai7-soaN e5 sou2-chai7 soaN lih2-khui, u7 e5 pang loh8-lai5 that tioh8 lou7. Iu7-koh u7 lang5 kong2 ti7 Kiam5-chui2-kang2 thiaN koan2-lai7 lai7-soaN e5 sou2-chai7 iu5-goan5 u7 soaN pang, ti3-kau3 thou5-soa eng-khi2-lai5 bong5-ian san3 bu7 e5 khoan2.   
Chiau3 Jit8-pun2 chit8 e5 li2-hak8 phok-su7 teh kong2, chit-pang e5 te7-tang7 u7 soa2 khah lam5-peng5 e5 khoan2.  Sou2-i2 ti7 Kiam5-chui2-kang2 thiaN koan2-lai7 u7 khah siong-tiong7. Chhui3-to2 678 keng; to2 bo5 li7--e5, 529 keng. The si2 3 lang5; tang-khin-siong 17 lang5. Chit tiong-kan Tiam3-a2-khau2 chi-thiaN koan-hat te7-it siong-tiong.  Chhu3-to2 546 keng; to2 bo5 li7--e5 348 keng.    
Chiu3 Tai5-lam5 e5 Chhek-hau7 sou2 kong2, tui3 chiaN-goeh8 ku3 si3 goeh8 chhe 4 jit8 ke3-kiong7 te7-tang7 87 pai2, tiong-kan 7 pai2 hou7 lang5 khah e7 chai. Iu7-koh chit-e5 4 goeh8 14 ke3-kiong7 tang7 18 pai2, ti7 3 tiam2 kap 8 tiam2 hit-cham7 cham2-jin5 toa7-tang7. I- au7 tak8-jit8 ia7 u7 te7-tang7, put-ko3 u7 e5 si7 hou7 lang5 khah m7-chai. Chit nng7-pang te7-tang7 chin li7-hai7, m7-na7 teh-si2 chheng-goa7 lang5, ia7 u7 sun2-hai7 hoe3-mih2 pah goa7 ban7 gin5. Hong5-te3 kap Hong5-hou7 su3 chit8 ban7 gin5 lai5 lin3-bin2 hiah-e5 siu7-siong kan-khou2 e5 lang5.    
Cheng5-goeh8 tu2-tioh8 te7-tang7 e5 si5 Tau2-lak8 e5 pai3-tng5 bo5 to2, chong2-si7 chhu3-teng2 kap piah u7 tam7-poh8 phaiN2,pai3-tng5 chit8-peng5 e5 pui-thau5 u7 hiaN3 jip8; taN teh gi7-lun7 beh koh khi2 sin--e5; i2-keng u7 be2 chhu3-te7 pian7-pian7. Ka-gi7 sin le2-pai3-tng5 ia7 tam7-poh8 phai2, iau2-ku2 e7 khia7-khi2-tit.    
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 254 koan3, 1906 ni5 6 geh8)
