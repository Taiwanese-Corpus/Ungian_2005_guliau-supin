Ti7 chit kin7-jit8 u7 chiap-tioh8 Jit8-pun2 e5 Mui2-jit8 sin-bun5 chap8-po3 ; ki5-tiong  u7 kong2-khi2 Im-gak8-hoe7 e5 su7-cheng5 , taN ai3 chiong khah iau3-kin2-e5 tai7-i3 tiah-khi2  lai5 po3 hou7 tai7-ke chai.  Lan2 Tai5-lam5-hu2 kui2- ni5 cheng5 Kam Bok8-su siat chit8 keng Hun3-kou2-tong5 beh ka3 chhiN-mi5-lang5 oh8 thak8 chheh , iah8 u7 kio3 kui2-na7 e5 chhiN-mi5 gin2-na2 lai5 thak8 .  Chiau3 Kam Bok8-su e5 i3-su3 ai3 chhe kui2-e5 khi3 Jit8-pun2 e5 chhiN- mi5-oh8 thang thak8 khah chhim , ng3-bang7 au7-lai5 khah toa7 lou7-eng7 ; m7-ku2  bo5 kau3- giah8 sou2-hui3.  U7 sia2-phoe mng7 Tai5-pak teh koan2 oh8 e5 koaN khoaN3 chaiN2-iuN7 . Chit e5 koan2 oh8 e5 koaN bo5 loa7-ku2 to3-khi3 Jit8- pun2 u7 kong2-khi2 chit-chan5 su7 ; u7 chit8-e5 chhiN-mi5 e5 gak8-su thiaN-kiN3 toa7-toa7 the2- thiap chit-e5 tai7-chi3 , chiu7 ki2-gi7  tioh8 tiau3 Im-gak8-hoe7 chu7-chip8 lai5 chok-gak8 hou7 lang5 thiaN , sou2 than3 e5 chiN5 long2 pang-chan7 Tai5-lam5 chhiN-mi5 gin2-na2.  Sou2-i2 Tai5-oan5 koan2-oh8 e5 koaN kap kui2-na7 ui7 e5 sian-siN ia7 u7 se-kok e5 sian-siN chit8 ui7 in long2 toa7 chhut-lat8 siat-hoat hou7 chit e5 Im-gak8-hoe7 chu7-chip8 . Chit e5 Im-gak8-hoe7 chiu7-si7 Jit8-pun2 u7 lang5 tiau5-ti5 khi3 hak8-sip8 im-gak8 kau3 chin cheng-kong lai5 siat-chiaN5 chit-e5 hoe7 ; hoan7-na7 u7 sim2-mih8 ho2-su7 ai3 chan3- seng5 , in chiu7 chu7-chip8 chok-gak8 hou7 lang5 thiaN, sou2 than3 e5 chiN5 chiu7 thang cho3-chiaN5 ho2-su7 .  Lang5 na7 beh khi3 thiaN gak8 in tioh8 chhut chiN5 be2 chit8-tiuN toaN chiaN3 u7 ui7 thang khi3 thiaN.  Chit pang chu7-chip8 chok-gak8 u7 che7-che7 peh-siN3 khi3 be2 oaN , ia7 u7 Hong5-kiong-lai7 e5 ong5-chhin khi3  e2 siong7-teng2 ui7 e5 toaN 100 tiuN .  Tui3 an2-ni chit koe3 e5 Im-gak8-hoe7 sou2 than3 e5 chiN5 u7 gou7-pah goa7 khou, long2 kia3 khi3  Tang-kiaN chhiN-mi5-oh8, beh cho3 Kam Bok8-su tui3 Tai5-lam5 chhe-khi3 e5 chhiN-mi5 gin2-na2 e5 sou2-hui3.  Hiah-e5 chu2-su7 kap chan3-seng5 e5 lang5, khoaN3-tioh8 Hong5-kiong-lai7 e5 ong5-chhin ia7 be2 hiah che7 tiuN toaN, in toa7 hoaN-hi2 kau3-kek8, in-ui7  Hong5-te3 siong5-siong5 ai3 khui in-su3 lai5 chan3-seng5 ho2-su7 .
