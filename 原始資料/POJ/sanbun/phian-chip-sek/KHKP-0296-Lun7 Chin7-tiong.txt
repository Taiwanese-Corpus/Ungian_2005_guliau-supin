
 
Lun7 Chin7-tiong
Su7-mui2-na2 kau3-hoe7 e5 chhe-sai3 sou2 tit- tioh8 e5 phoe, chiong5-tiong u7 chit8 ku3  kong2, ¡§Tioh8 chin7-tiong phoe-si2¡¨ (Bek8. 2:10).  Chai7 Su7-mui2-na2 siaN5 tak8 e5 Ki- tok-tou5 long2 chai ¡§Tioh8 chin7-tiong¡¨, hit chit8 ku3 si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3.  In-ui7 khah cha2  Lo5-ma2 thong2-hat thiN-e7 hit si5 teh kap tui3-tek8 toa7 chian3, kun-peng khun3-khou2,  Su7-mui2-na2 e5 peh-seN3 khek-ki2 thng3 ka-ti7 e5 i-chiuN5 kia3 khi3 chiu chou7 in.  Kau3 thai3- peng5 liau2-au7 Lo5-ma2 toa7 heng, Hong5-te3 ki3-liam7 in e5 tiong-gi7, chiu7 thek-hong in  e5 siaN5 chheng cho3 ¡§Chin7-tiong¡¨.  
Chha-put-to chit8 pah ni5 cheng5 Hoat- kok e5 Hong5-te3 Na2-pho-lun5 pun2 si7 iong2- beng2 gau5 chian3 e5 lang5.  Ti7 1814 ni5 i khi3 kong-kek Ngou5-kok, kho2-sioh toa7 pai7.  Hit-tiap8 tu2-gu7 tang-thiN si5 chin koaN5, chui2 khah che7 kian peng.  In teh  the3 peng hit si5 Ngou5-peng tit8-tit8 tui-jiok, chong2-si7 tioh8 keng-koe3 chit8 tiau5  ho5 chiah chau2 e7 li7.  Ngou5-peng i2-keng phah hoai7 hiah e5 kio5, sou2-i2 Na2-pho-lun5  chhut-leng7 kah i e5 peng lim5-si5 tioh8 koh tah, hou7 peng-tui7 u7 lou7 thang koe3.  Hit- tiap8 ho5 chui2 chin hiong5 koa3 gan3, thau5 chit8-e7 loh8-khi3 hiah-e5 long2 hou7 chui2 lau5- khi3; m7-ku2 liam5-piN u7 pat8-e5 chiap-siok8 loh8-khi3, u7-e5 tu3 bo5 kui2 hun ku2 chiu7 koaN5- si2 chong2-si7 pat8 lang5 liam5-piN khi3 the3 kang chhah kio5 thiau7.  
Kio5 cho7 beng5-pek8 kun-peng koe3 liau2, chiu7 beng7-leng7 hiah e5 loh8 chui2 hu5 thiau7-a2 tioh8 khi2-lai5, chong2-si7 in bo5 chit8 lang5 tong7-cheng7 chiuN7 hoaN7.  Kau3 au7-lai5 chiaN3 chai hiah e5 lam2 kio5 thiau7 e5 lang5 i2-keng koaN5 si2 nge7-nge7 chhin-chhiuN7 kian peng e5 khoan2.  Na2-pho-lun5 khoaN3 i e5 peng chin7-tiong kau3 an2-ni-seN, chiu7 be7 kim3- tit siong-sim lau5 bak8-sai2. 
(Tai5-lam5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 296 koan3, 1909 ni5 11 geh8)
