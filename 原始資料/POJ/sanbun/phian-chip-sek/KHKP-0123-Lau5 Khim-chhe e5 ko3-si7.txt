
Lau5 Khim-chhe e5 ko3-si7
Khim beng7 Pang-pan7 Tai5-lam5-hu2 hong5- bu7 ki3 mia5 The5-tok-kun-bun5 soan2 cho3 Ban5, Oat8, Lam5-o3, su7-li2 Tai5-oan5, chui2- liok8 chiah-e5 te7-hng e5 Chong2-tin3, I-phok- tek-in Pa-tou5-lou2 Lau5.     
In-ui7 chhut ko3-si2 hoan-hu3 lin2 tioh8 chai e5 su7: chiau3-tit Tai5-oan5 An-peng5 e5 te7- hng u7 chak Eng-kok peng-iong2 si3-chap8- mia5, pun2 si7 ui7-tioh8 po2-hou7 iuN5-hang5 e5 kheh-siong khi2-in--e5; pun2 Pang-pan7 in- ui7 kiaN-liau2 chhap-chap8 seN-chhut ho7- toan, so2-i2 chham-siong5 chun2 Tai7-eng- kok Leng2-su7-koaN chiong iuN5-peng hou7 in poaN loh8-chun5 to3--khi3; so2-u7 chiah-e5 iuN5- hang5 kau3-tng5 i-koan2 e5 lang5, chiu7 tui3 pun2 Pang-pan7 jin7 cho3 po2-hou7, eng-kai koaN2- kin2 chhut ko3-si7.  Ui7-tioh8 che ko3-si7, chhiaN2 sin-su7 siong-bin5 kun-peng peh-seN3 chia3- e5 lang5 it-the2 chiau5-chai; tioh8 chai bo5 ho5- sun7 kap lan2 teh tong7-kan-ko--e5, tok8-tok8 Jit8-pun2 chit8-kok na7-tiaN7; ki5-u5 Se-iuN5 kok- kok long2 si7 siok8-ti7, ho5-ho2 e5 kok, peng7- bo5 hiap8-hiam5 oan3-hin7.  Hoan7-na7 thong- siong kai3-an7 lai7 e5 Leng2-su7-hu2 Hai2-koan Kau3-tng5 I-koan2 e5 lang5 kap sou2-chai7, iau3- kin2 chiu7 tioh8 chiau3-siong5 po2-hou7, be7-sai2- tit seN-khi2 su7-toan ti3-kau3 kiau2-jiau2 saN- kan-gai7 lai5 sit-liau2 ho5-ho2 e5 cheng5-gi7. Che si7 pun2 Pang-pan7 kap i beng5-iok; lin2 sin-su7 peh-seN3 peng-iong2 chia3-e5 lang5-sek, kok lang5 eng-kai an-hun7 cho3 seng-li2, be7- sai2-tit kou3-i3 ui5-keh8 ti3-kau3 hoan7-tioh8 tang7 pan7 kin-kiu3; chhiat-chhiat kin2-sin7 chun- than3, m7-thang ui5-keh8 che ko3-si7.     
Kong-su7 21 ni5 lun7 5 goeh8 chhe chap8 jit8.    
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 123 koan3,1895 ni5 6 geh8).
