
Chin7-tiong kau3-si2
U7 kun-ong5 na7 beh chio peng-iong2 chiu7 u7 po3-iong5 in beh tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8 po3-siuN2 , lonh2 bo5 kong2-khi2 beh tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 kan-khou2 . Ia5-sou bo5 an2-ni-seN.  I u7 beng5-beng5 ka7 lang5 kong2 in na7 beh toe3 I tek-khak beh hou7 tui3-tek8 oan3-hin7, hui2-pong3,kap leng7-goa7 phaiN2-khoan2-thai7 che7-che7 hang7 e5 khoan2-sit .    
Kng kap am3 long2 be7 ho5, sou2-i2 than chin-li2 e5 lang5 siong5-siong5 hou7 se3-siok8 lang5 kap in cho3 tui3-tek8 . Sui-jian5 si7 an2- ni, iau2-ku2 thang siong5-siong5 hoaN-hi2, in- ui7 sim-lai7 e5 peng5-an si7 cham2-jian5 toa7 . Chit-ho7 e5 peng5-an Mou5-kui2 e5 peh-seN3 bo5 chit8-tiam2-a2 e7 hiau2-tit . Tit-tioh8 peh8-bi2 si7 in-ui7 u7 keng-koe3 phah kap cheng .  Che7-che7 ho7 e5 cheng2- chi2 na7 bo5 geng2-tioh8 chiu7 long2 bo5 chhut ho2-bi7 . An2-ni-seN , tui3 Chu2 e5 peh-seN3 siu7 khun2-tiok8 in-e5 seng3-tek e7 khah-beng5 hian2-chhut lai5 .  Ia5-sou e5 kau3-hoe7 te7-it- toa7 e5 gui5-hiam2 chiu7-si7 in-ui7 bo5 tu2-tioh8 tui3-tek8 chiah cho3 leng2-tam7 . Kui2-na7 pah-ni5 cheng5 ti7 Tai7-Eng-kok u7 chit8-e5 siau3-lian5 thai3-chu2 ai3 cho3 chit8- hang7 tai7-chi3 chong2-si7 khoaN3 pun2-sin si7 bo5 kau3-giah8 toa7-han3 . U7 chit8-e5 chhe-eng7 khoaN3-kiN3 an2-ni chiu7 kin2-kin2 theh8 toa7- pun2 Seng3-chheh lai5 he7 ti7 thou5-kha chhiaN2 thai3-chu2 tah8 ti7 teng2-bin7 chiah e7-cho3-tit . Thai3-chu2 m7 kam-goan7, koh u7 hiam5 hit- e5 chhe-eng7 e5 put-keng3. I chai si7 Siong7- te3 e5 Seng3-chheh sou2-i2  m7-thang lam7-sam2 eng7 , iah8 chai ti7 cheng5 se3-tai7 u7 che7-che7 lang5 in-ui7 ai3 chiau3-kou3 Seng3-chheh u7 siu7 khun2-tiok8 kau3 si2 . Lan2 tai7-ke tioh8 siau3-liam7 hit-e5 thai3-chu2 e5 i3-su3 ,  siong5-siong5 tioh8 siuN7 Seng3-chheh si7 chhin-chhiuN7 che7-che7 se3-tai7 siu7-si2 e5 lang5 liu5-thoan5 .    
Tng Ia5-sou toa3 se3-kan e5 si5 ban7-kok u7 pai3 ngou2-siong7, kaN2-si7 tok8-tok8 Iu5-thai3 kok bo5 . Ti7 hit-si5 te7-it koan5-peng3 e5 kok chiu7- si7 Lo5-ma2 kok . Kau3 Ia5-sou e5 chin-li2 u7 kong2-khoah soan-thoan5 Lo5-ma2 e5 lang5 khah-che7 u7 toa7 oan3-hin7. U7-si5 hong5-te3 pun2-sin ai3 chhiam3-koan5 hou7 peh-seN3 pai3 in na2-chun2 Siong7-te3 chit8-iuN7 . Kho2-kian3 than3 Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2 si7 chin-hiam2. U7 chheng-ban7 lang5 hou7 koaN-hu2 liah8 soa3 hai7-si2 . Sui- jian5 si7 an2-ni , Ia5-sou e5 chin-li2 si7 na2-ku2 na2 heng-ong7 . Che7 che7 sin3-Chu2 e5 lang5 toa7 him-bou7 siu7 khun2-tiok8 kau3 si2 . Na7 bo5 siu7-tioh8 chit-ho7 chiu7 u7 hoan5-lo2 . Tai7-ke saN-bian2-le7 . Hong5-te3 ju2-ku2 ju2-chan5-jim2, m7-ku2 bo5-chhai2-kang. Khun2-tiok8 e5 hong- hoat si7 kek8-che7 kek8-phaiN2 khoan2 .    
Chiong5-tiong seng3-tou5 sou2 keng-koe3 e5 khou2-lan7 u7-e5 bat hou7 in-e5 tui3-tek8 pu5 in ti7 thih-seN3 , u7-e5 hou7 in boah-iu5 ti7 pian3 seng- khu chiu7 tiam2-hoe2 sio-tioh8, u7-e5 hou7 in thoa in-e5 kut-thau5 tui3 kut-lun5 long2 khi2- lai5 , u7-e5 hou7 in koaiN ti7 hi3-tiuN5 chiu7 pang3- lang3 kek8 beng2 kap iau-go7 e5 ia2-siu3 chhut- lai5 ka7-tioh8 , u7-e5 hou7 in thin5 kun2-chhiang5- chhiang5 e5 ian5 jip8 ti7 in-e5 chhui3, bak8 kap hi7, iah8 u7 pat8-hang7 e5 jim2-hoat kong2 be7- liau2 .   
Kau3 Ia5-sou chiuN7-thiN liau2-au7 saN-pah-ni5 e5 tiong-kan u7 chap8-koe3 e5 toa7 khun2- tiok8 . Kok-ka na7 tu2-tioh8 sim2-mih8 chai- e7 lang5 long2 u7 hiam5 si7 Ki-tok-tou5 ti3-kau3 , iah8 u7-si5 tui3-tek8 u7 chiong che3-hian3 e5 chiu2 lam5-tioh8 lang5 ti7 chhi7-nih8 sou2 be7 e5 hoe3- mih8 , i3-su3 chiu7-si7 beh bian2-kiong2 hiah-e5 Ki-tok-tou5 chiah8 che3 ngou2-siong7 e5 mih8 a2- si7 thun-lun2 go7-si2 .    
Ti7 e7-te2 e5 ang-a2-tou5 tai7-ke thang khoaN3- kiN3 chit8-e5 siau3-lian5 hu7-jin5-lang5 i2-keng hou7 siu5-jin5 chiong thih-lian7 pak8 ti7 chit8- ki-thiau7 tiaN7-tioh8 beh sio-si2 . Hoan-hu3 cho3 chit-ho7-su7 e5 Lo5-ma2 chiang2-koan5 e5 lang5 chham hu7-jin5-lang5, peng5-iu2, i2-kip8 peng-teng tui3 tai3-teng2 teh khoaN3-kiN3. Iah8 ti7 e7-te2 u7 lou5-pok8 tu2-a2 teh khi2-hoe2 . Lu2 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 bin7-mau7 si7 sui2 iah8 si7 peng5-an, chiaN3-si7 toa7 koh-iuN7 ti7 pat8-lang5--e5 . Lan2 tioh8 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 in-ui7 u7 loan2- jiok8 e5 seng3-tou5 kau3 hiah ho2-taN2 kan-cheng3 I-e5 to7-li2 , iu7-koh lan2 tioh8 kiu5 seng3- sin5 chan7 lan2 si5-si5 lai3 hian2-chhut kek8 kian- kou3 e5 sim, chiu7 kau3 lou7-boe2 lan2 thang hiong2-siu7 eng2-oan2 e5 hok-khi3 . 
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-poe3 te7 106 koan3, 1894 ni5 2 geh8)
