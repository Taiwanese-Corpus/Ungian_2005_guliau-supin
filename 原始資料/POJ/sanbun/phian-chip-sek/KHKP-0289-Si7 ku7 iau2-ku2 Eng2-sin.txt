
Si7 ku7 iau2-ku2 Eng2-sin
Lang5 e5 hong-siok8 bo5 saN-chhin-chhiuN7; iau2-ku2 u7 chit8 hang7 saN-chhin-chhiuN7, chiu7-si7 tak8 kok e5 peh-seN3 chin tui3-tiong7 ku7 e5 mih8, iah8 sin-e5 mih8 iu5-goan5 chin ai3.    
U7 sim2-mih8 kou2-chek, kou2-cha2 sou2 khi2 e5 chhu3, a2-si7 phau3-tai5, chheng3-kui7, sui-si7 chit-tiap8 bo5 teh eng7, iau2-ku2 khah siong5 ai3 se3-ji7 chiau3-kou3, m7 ai3 thiah. A2-si7 u7 sim2- mih8 kou2-tong2 lang5 chin ai3 khoaN3. U7 sim2- mih8 hong-siok8 ban7-kou2 liu5-thoan5, lang5 chiu7 ai3 than3.  U7 sim2-mih8 kou2 lang5-gian5 chiu7 ai3 kong2; u7 sim2-mih8 kou3-su7, ai3 thiaN.    
U7-si5 peh-seN3 tiau5-kou3-i3 khi2-keng beh khng3 kou2-tong2 hou7 lang5 khoaN3.  Chhin- chhiuN7 Jit8-pun2 kok e5 KiaN-siaN5 u7 khi2 chit8 keng toa7 lau5 thang te2 tak8 hang7 e5 kou2- tong2, chiong5-tiong u7 Jit8-pun2 e5 Hong5- te3 kou2-cha2 sou2 che7 e5 kio7, chin toa7 teng2, iah8 u7 kou2-cha2 lang5 sou2 eng7 e5 ke-si.  
Eng-kok u7 khi2 chin che7 keng toa7 lau5 lai5 te2 kou2-cha2 lang5 sou2 eng7 e5 ke-si khi3- hai5.  U7 sim2-mih8 kou2-che3 e5 saN, chheng3, to, hoe2-chhia, long2 khng3 ti7 hit lai7-te2;  iah8 u7 kou2-cha2 mia5-siaN e5 thak8-chheh lang5, cho3 si e5 lang5, chhin-pit sou2 sia2 e5 si-su5.   U7-si5 beh be7, hit-ho7 e5 pit-chek tat8--tioh8 kui2-na7 chheng khou, kui2-na7 ban7 iah8 kaN2.   Chhin-chhiuN7 cheng5-ni5 u7 chit8-pun2 se3- pun2-a2 chheh, be7 saN chap8 ban7 gin5.  
Eng-kok iah8 u7 chin che7 kham2-tiam3 choan-kang teh be7 kou2-tong2.  Chhin- chhiuN7 lan2 beh kia3 phoe hia-e5 ang-a2- thau5 bo5 sim2-mih8 tat8-chiN5 e5 mih8, chit8- tiuN 3 tiam2 chiN5 na7-tiaN7; iau2-ku2 hiah-e5 ku7--e5 hian7-chhu2-si5 lan2 khah han2--tit khoaN3-kiN3, iah8 lang5 chin ai3 khioh; cheng5 jit8 Sin-bun7-po3 teh po3 2 tiuN be7 $6000.  
Ku7 e5 mih8 lang5 iah8 chin ai3, sin e5 mih8 iah8 si7 chin ai3.  Mih8 na7 khah si5-kiaN5 lang5 ai3 eng7, saN na7 khah si5-sek lang5 ai3 chheng7.  Na7 u7 sim2-mih8 m7-bat khoaN3 e5 mih8, put-lun7 hoe2-chhia, tian7-khi3-chhia, tok8-lian2-chhia, khin-khi3-kiu5, chiu7 u7 lang5 saN-cheN beh khoaN3.  A2-si7 u7 sim2-mih8 han2--tit thiaN e5 su7 sim2-mih8 sin e5 siau-sit lang5 iu5-goan5 chin ai3 thiaN.  
TaN li2 na7 ai3 ku7 e5 mih8, to7-li2 si7 chin ku7, ai3 sin e5 mih8, to7-li2 si7 kek8 sin.  Ai3 thiaN lang5 kong2 kou2, to7-li2 chiu7-si7 kek8 kou2-cha2 e5 su7, ai3 thiaN sin e5 tai7-chi3 to7-li2 si7 ho2 e5  siau-sit, koan-he7 lan2 tai7-ke hoaN-hi2.  
To7-li2 si7 kong2 Siong7-te3.  U7-si5 lang5 teh mng7, Khong2-chu2 khah cha2 a2-si7 Siong7- te3 khah cha2.  Siong7-te3 khah cha2.  Khong2- chu2 iah8 u7 kong2 Siong7-te3.  Siong7-te3 pi2 Giau5 Sun3 iau2 khah cha2.  Giau5 Sun3 iah8 pai3 Siong7-te3.  Siong7-te3 pi2 Tiong-kok, pi2 Eng-kok, Jit8-pun2-kok, tak8-kok, koh khah cha2.  
ThiN-te7 khah cha2, a2-si7 Siong7-te3 khah cha2?  Siong7-te3 khah cha2; Siong7-te3 si7  chhong3-cho7 ThiN-te7, ban7 mih8 e5 Sin5.  
Siong7-te3 ti7-si5 siN-jit8?  Siong7-te3 bo5 seN-jit8.  I na7 u7 seN-jit8 chiu7 u7 pe7-bo2, chou2- kong; pe7-bo2 chou2-kong khah toa7, khah cha2.  M7-ku2 Siong7-te3 kai3 te7 it toa7, siong7  te7 it cha2, khi2-thau5 e5 khi2-thau5.  Siong7- te3 si7 bo5 khi2-thau5 e5 Siong7-te3, te7 it kou2- cha2 chiu7-si7 I.  
Li2 na7 ai3 thiaN lang5 kong2 kou2, kong2 Siong7-te3 chiu7-si7 kong2 kou2.  
Kin-na2-jit8 Siong7-te3 ti7 to2-ui7?  Kin-na2-jit8 Siong7-te3 ti7-chia.  Chit-tiap8 goa2 teh kong2-oe7 si7 in-ui7 Siong7-te3 ti7--teh teh pi3- iu7 goa2, I na7 bo5 ti7-chia, goa2 liam5-piN si2 khiau3-khiau3.  Sim2-mih8 lang5 chhoa7 li2 lai5 kau3 chit te3?  Chiu7-si7 Siong7-te3 chhoa7 li2 lai5.  Lan2 tak8-jit8 teh chiah8 si7 chiah8 Siong7-te3, teh chheng7 si7 chheng7 Siong7-te3, teh eng7 si7 eng7 Siong7-te3.  
Kin-na2-jit8 u7 sim2-mih8 siau-sit?  Chiu7- si7 kong2 Siong7-te3 ti7 chia.  
M7-ku2 Siong7-te3 ti7-chia, tui3 to2-ui7 thang  chai chin-chiaN3 u7 iaN2 si7 an2-ni?  Tou m7 bat khoaN3--kiN3.  
 
TaN ai3 beh beng5-pek8 chit chan5 e5 to7-li2, tioh8 tai7-seng kong2 chit chan5 kou3-su7.   Cha2-cha2 m7-na7 Tiong-kok lang5 jin7 thiN- teng2 chit8-e5 Chu2-chaiN2, chiau3 khoaN3 pat8- kok, chhin-chhiuN7 Thian-tek kok (In3-tou7) iu5-goan5 si7 an2-ni jin7.  M7-ku2 tai7-ke khi3- chin kui-sia5, kau3 2000 ni5 cheng5 bo5 sim2- mih8 lang5 jin7 Siong7-te3, chi2-u7 chit8 kok  na7-tiaN7, chiu7-si7 Iu5-thai3-kok.  Siong7-te3 eng7 che7-che7 hang7 koh-iuN7 e5 hoat-tou7, koh eng7 Seng3-jin5 che7-che7, ka3-si7 in chai- iaN2, pat8-kok na2 li7-khui Siong7-te3 e5 si5, in  chit8-kok na2 tit--tioh8 thang kian-kou3 sin3, chin-chiaN3 u7 iaN2 u7 chit8-e5 Siong7-te3.  Tui3  2000 goa7 ni5 cheng5 in long2 m7-bat giau5- gi5; hoan2-tng2 pai3 put8, in kam-goan7 si2,  tiaN7-tioh8 m7 pai3 pat8-mih8 e5 sin5-beng5.  
Iau2-ku2 peh-seN3 bo5 kau3-giah8 beng5 chai, sou2-i2 kau3 si5 Siong7-te3 chhe chit8 e5 
Ia5-sou lai5, thang ka3-si7 in, koh chhoa7 in kap Siong7-te3 chhin-kin7.  Ia5-sou lai5 loa7 ku2?  Kin-ni5 si7 Beng5-ti7 42 ni5, iah8 si7 Soan-thong2 goan5-ni5.  Kau3 kin- ni5 Ia5-sou kang3-seng i2-keng 1909 ni5. Siong7-te3 si7 goa2 e5 Pe7 iah8 si7 lin2 e5 Pe7, lin2 na7 ai3 beh kau3 ti7 I sou2 toa3 e5 sou2- chai7 tioh8 kho3 goa2; na7 bo5 goa2 chiu7 bo5 lou7.
