Ti7 Eng-kok u7 chit8 cha2-khi2 hoe2-chhia e5 lang5-kheh tah kau3 moa2-moa2,beh khi3 Lun5-tun.Chhia tu2-ho2 chhut-lat8 teh chau2 e5 si5,hut-jian5-kan ki-koan-chhiu2 khoaN3-kiN3 cheng5-bin7 lou7-piN e5 sin3-ho7 u7 po3 m7 thang koe3-khi3 . In-ui7 an2-ne hoe2-chhia liam5-piN choan7 hou7 theng5 . Koh bo5 loa7-ku2 au7-piah lai5 e5 hoe2-chhia ia7 tu3-leh,na2 chek na2 che7, long2 tong3-teh m7-kaN2 koe3-khi3, tah-kheh long2 chin kiaN-hiaN5, m7 chai beh chaiN2-iuN7 .
Khi2-thau5 thih-lou7 e5 chit-oan5 long2 m7 chai an3-choaN2 sin3 ho7 e7 po3 an2-ne , kau3 chhe lang5 siong5-se3 tiau7-cha ,chiaN3 chai si7 chit8 khoan2 e5 thang5 kiaN5 m7-tioh8 lou7 e5 ian5-kou3 .
Chai7 Eng-kok u7 chit8 cheng2 e5 thang5 chhin-chhiuN7 kau2-hia7 e5 khoan2,peng5-sou3 chin ui3 kng,sou2-i2 me5--si5 chiah chhut-lai5 tho2-chiah8,jit8-si5 chiu7 bih ti7 thou5-khang-lai7, lai5 siam2-pi7 jit8 e5 kng . Hit chiah chou2-tong3 hoe2-chhia e5 thang5 , kui me5 chhut lai5 chhoe7 chiah8 , thiN-kng beh to3-khi3, kiaN5 m7-tioh8 lou7, so5 tui3 hit ki sin3-ho7 e5 lai7-bin7,seng-khu hou7 tian7 e5 ki-khi3 teh tioh8 , hit e5 tian7 chiong an2-ne in2 koe3-khi3,soa3 hian2-chhut hit e5 po3 m7-thang koe3--khi3 e5 ho7, ti3-kau3 hiah e5 hoe2-chhia long2 m7-kaN2 koe3--khi3 . Hit-si5 thiaN-kiN3 e5 lang5 bo5-m7 chin ho2-chhio3 .
