O3-ka chok8 san-te7 e5 sou2-chai7 iau2 si7 bo5 chheng7 saN, bo5 chheng7 khou3 e5 ia2-ban5 chok8, siong5-siong5 u7 thai5-lang5 cho3 che3-su7 e5 chok8.
Gou7 e5 soan-kau3-su kau3 chit e5 sou2-chai7 soa3 hou7 hiah e5 chheN-hoan thai5-si2, hip ti7 siong7-nih e5 cheng7-heng5, si7 hui-ki e5 tham3-hiam2-tui7 sou2 hip e5, ia7 u7 sin-si phu5 ti7 khe-nih, hou7 lang5 hoat-kian3--tioh8 e5.
Kau3 1957 ni5 8 goeh8, bo5 phah-sng3 chiah e5 soan-kau3-su e5 chit8 e5 thai3-thai3 kap eN-a2 tu2 chhut-si3 chit8 ni5 goa7 e5, saN-chhoa7 khi3 hiah e5 chheN-hoan e5 sou2-chai7 beh khi3 thoan5-kau3.  Hiah e5 siau-sit kap siong7 koh chhut ti7 Seng7-oah8 oe7-po3-nih, leng7-goa7 ia7 u7 siu7 thai5-si2 e5 chit8 e5 soan-kau3-su e5 sio2-moe7 ia7 koh khi3 hia thoan5 to7-li2, hit e5 siong7 kap siau-sit ia7 u7 chhut ti7 Seng-oah8 chap8-chi3--nih8.
Kau3 1958 ni5 e5 12 goeh8 ho7 u7 koh chhut in e5 siong7 ti7 Seng-oah8 chap8-chi3--nih.  TaN hiah e5 siong7 u7 chhut 3 tiuN ti7 pun2 Po3, chhiaN2 thok8-chia2 ia7 khoaN3, ia7 siuN7, tui3 hia lai5 siu7 ka3-si7 kap kek-le7.
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-kong-po3 te7 843 ho7, 1959 ni5 3 geh8)
