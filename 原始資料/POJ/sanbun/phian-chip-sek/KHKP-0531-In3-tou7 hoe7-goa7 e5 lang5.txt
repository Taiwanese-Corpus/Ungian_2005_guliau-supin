In3-tou7 hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5 kan-cheng3 to7-li2
Srinivasa Sastri si7 In3-tou7 chit8 e5 chhut-mia5 tsoe3 u7 keng-lek8 e5 lang5, i si7 e7 thang hou7 lang5 tsun-keng3 tso3 In3-tou7 sit8- chai7 e5 peh-siN3 e5 tai7-piau2-tek e5 lang5.    
Ti7-Tsu2-au7 1921 ni5 u7 tai7-piau2 In3-tou7 khi3 hu3 Kok-che3 lian5-beng5, koh siu7 ui2- thok khi3 si7-chhat O3-te7-li7-a, Sin-ngi2-lan5, Ka-na2-tai7, the3 i e5 tong5-pau ti7-hia siat- hoat in e5 bun7-te5. I si7 Su-bit8-iN7 e5 Kou3- bun7-koaN e5 chit8 e5, si7 Lun5-tun e5 tsu7-iu5- bin5.  Thang kong2 si7 In3-tou7 lang5 e5 siong7- kip e5 chit8-e5 chhut-mia5 lang5. Lun7 Ki- tok-kau3 e5 to7-li2 i u7 an2-ne kong2.

Chong2-si7 ti7 che7-che7 ni5 tiong-kan, goa2 bat koan-khoaN3 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 oah8-tong7. Tui3 goa2 sou2 keng-giam7 Ki-tok-kau3 e5 sin3-tou5, thang kong2 se3-kai3 chong-kau3 tiong tu3-mia5 e5 te7-ui7 si7 siok8 Ki-tok-kau3, in-ui7 i u7 kam2-tong7-lat8 hou7 lang5 hian3-sin kap phok-ai3 e5 hong7-su7.    
Ki-tok-kau3 u7 chou-chit Soan-to7-hoe7 ti7 se3-kai3 tak8 sou2-chai7, ti7 bun5-beng5 e5 kok- bin5 kap ia2-ban5 e5 peh-siN3 e5 tiong-kan. Tauh8-tauh8 tu2-tioh8 lang5 bo5 tong5-cheng5, lang5 hoan2-tui3, hui2-pong3, iau2-ku2 bo5 sian7 bo5 lan2, tsai u7 sim2-mih8 mih8 thang heng- khi2 lang5 te7-it koan5 kap te7-it ko-siong7 e5 phin2-seng3.    
In3-tou7 e5 kan-khou2 toa7-bin7 si7 in-ui7 bo5 ti3-sek koh pin5-toaN7. Soan-kau3-su teh ka3 m7-bat ji7 e5 lang5 ia7 si7 teh khui-khoah Ki- tok-kau3. In-ui7 in bo5 sioh chiN5 kap khui3- lat8 oan5-chiaN5 chiah e5 tiong7-iau3 e5 su7, Ki- tok-kau3 teh cheng-hok8 se3-kai3, chiong-lai5 e7 koh-khah toa7 seng5-kong.    
 
Ki-tok-kau3 iau2 u7 che7-che7 tioh8 cho7- chiaN5 e5 su7. Kau-chian3 iau2-boe7 hoe3-chi2. Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 tsong-kau3, si7 peng5-an ong5 e5 tsong-kau3, boe7 hoe3-chi2 kau-chian3 e5 sim be7 thang kong2 si7 oan5-chiaN5 i e5 tsu2-chi2.
