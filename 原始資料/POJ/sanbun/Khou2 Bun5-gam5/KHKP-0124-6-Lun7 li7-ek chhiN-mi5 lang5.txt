Lun7 i-e5 gau5 boa5 m7-si7 tui3 chhiN-mi5  liau2 chiah oh8 boa5-e5, si7 i pun2 be7 chhiN-mi5 e5 si5 chiu7 teh boa5, sou2-i2 kau3 chit-tiap8 iah8 si7 choe3 chit ho7 e5 kang; i tak8-jit8 chhut- mng5 long2 bian2 hou7 lang5 khan, iah8 koh oe7 taN-taN3 ; si7 choe3 put-chi2 jiat8-sim e5 hiaN-ti7 , siong5-siong5 ti7 boa5 ka-to e5 si5 , teh po3- iong5 to7-li2 .    
An2-ni goa2 chiong sou2 khoaN3-kiN3--e5 lai5 ki3 ; ki5-tiong long2  si7  in2-kou2 pi2-kim e5 sit8-su7 ; ng3-bang7 liat8-ui7 sian-siN chhai5-chiaN3 .
(Tai5-oan5 Hu2-siaN5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 124-126 koan3, 1895 ni5 7-9 geh8)
