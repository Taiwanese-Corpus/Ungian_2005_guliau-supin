
 
I5 Sian-seN li7-piat e5 phoe
Goa2 sou2-thiaN3 sou2-him-bou7 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moai7 i2-kip8 siau3-lian5 e5 peng5-iu2, chhiaN2 li2 chun2 goa2 tui3 lin2 kong2-khi2 kui2-na7 ku3-oe7.   
Chiau3 lin2 chai Sian-seN-niu5 lai5 Tai5-oan5 put-chi2 ai3 toa3 ti7 lin2 chia, iau2-ku2 u7 hoan7-tioh8 tang7-peN7 kau3 eng-kai tioh8 to3-khi3 chou2-ke.  In-ui7 ng3-bang7 i na7 toa3 ti7 Tng5-soaN e7 khah iong2-kiaN7, Eng-kok e5 Kau3-hoe7 u7 phai3 goa2 soa2-khi3 ti7 SoaN3-thau5 khah lai7-soaN e5 sou2-chai7, chiu7-si7 Kheh-lang5 e5 tiong-kan, ti7 hia cho3 Chu2 e5 kang. Goa2 li7-khui Tai5-oan5 ki5-sit8 m7-si7 pun2-i3, sng3 si7 kou-put-chiong, chiu7-si7 hap8 ti7 Siong7-te3 ech5i-i3.   
Goa2 toa3 Tai5-oan5 i2-keng chap8-sou2-ni5. Tng chiah-ku2 chiap8-chiap8 u7 kap lin2 kau-poe5, si7 hou7 goa2 e5 sim hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7-kou3-tit. Lin2 siong5-siong5 thiaN3-thang3 goa2, tui3-tiong Siong7-te3 chun2 goa2 thoan5 e5 ka3-si7. Goa2 eng-kai tioh8 ka7 lin2 soeh to-sia7; goa2 eng2-eng2 siau3-liam7 lin2, iah8 sui-jian5 goa2 e5 sin bo5 thang khah ku2-tng5 ti7-chia, iau2-ku2 goa2 ai3 lin2 beng5-beng5 chai goa2 e5 sin5 si5-si5 kap lin2 ti7-teh.   
Tui3 goa2 lai5 Tai5-oan5 kau3-taN goa2 tok8-tok8 u7 i3-su3 ai3 chan7 lin2 bat Chu2, ia7 siong5-siong5 ai3 kui eng5-kng ti7 I.  Goa2 chin-chiaN3 hoaN-hi2 in-ui7 ti7 chi2 chap8-sou3-ni5 e5 tiong-kan u7 khoaN3-kiN3 Tai5-lam5 e5 Kau3-hoe7 toa7 chin3-pou7.  Kau3 chit-tiap8 u7 siat hoat-tou7 put-chi2 chiau5-pi7, iah8 goa2 toa7-toa7 ng3-bang7 Kau3-hoe7 thang chiam7-chiam7 heng-khi2.  Chong2-si7 na7 ai3 tit-tioh8 chit-ho7 e5 li7-ek tai7-ke, put-lun7 si7 lam5-hu7 lo2-iu3, tek-khak tioh8 chin7 i-e5 pun2-hun7,iah8 si5-si5 tioh8 him-bou7 oa2-kho3 Seng3-sin5 e5 koan5-leng5.  Tu5 che i2-goa7 sit8-chai7 bo5 pat8 lou7 thang chin3-ek.  
Lan2 toa3 ti7 chit se3-kan si7 na2-chhin-chhiuN7 chhut-goa7. Lan2 eng5-kng e5 tau ti7 thiN--nih. Ti7-hia long2 si7 hok-khi3 liau2-liau2. Lan2 tiam3 ti7 te7-bin7-chiuN7 tauh8-tauh8 tioh8 tioh8-boa5, ia7 u7-si5 tu2-tioh8 chhi3-lian7 iu-bun7 e5 tai7-chi3. Iau2 -ku2 lan2 koat-toan3 m7-thang loe2-chi3 pang3-sak chin-li2. Tek-khak tioh8 kiat-lian5 ti7 Chu2, tak8-si5 tioh8 choan-choan oa2-kho3 I.  Na7 sit-loh8 pun2-hun7 chiu7 be7-bian2-tit siu7-hai7, iah8 na7 bo5 hoan2-hoe2 sit8-chai7 beh eng2-oan2 tim5-lun5.  Goa2 sou2 thiaN3 e5 ho2 peng5-iu2, chhiaN2 li2 chun2 goa2 kiu5 lin2 tioh8 thoat-li7 chit-e5 siang7-toa7 e5 chai5-eh. Ti7 si2-au7 bo5 thang i5-ek8, sou2-i2 tui3 chit-chun7 tioh8 koat-i3 toe3 Chu2, bo5 hou7 sim2-mih8 lang5 sim2-mih8 mih8 be5-hek8 lin2 e5 sim. Che chiaN3-si7 goa2 pek-chhiat e5 oe7, goa2 e5 choan-sim sou2-ai3. In-ui7 lan2 e5 kok ti7 thiN--nih8, in-ui7 Chu2 beh koh lai5 sim2-phoaN3 cheng3-lang5, iah8 in-ui7 lan2 thang ng3-bang7 hou7 Chu2 pian3-hoa3 lan2 pi-chian7 e5 seng-khu oh8 I eng5-kng sin5-leng5 e5 the2, lan2 tai7-ke tioh8 siok8 Chu2 kian-kou3 khia7-chai7. (KhoaN3 Hui-lip8-pi2 te7-saN-chiuN boe2 kap te7-si3-chiuN thau5).  
Go2an sou2 thiaN3 sou2 him-bou7 e5 hoe7-iu2 cho3 chin7-tiong kau3 lou7-boe2, chiu7 lan2 sui-jian5 ti7 chit se3-kan na7 bo5 koh-chai3 sio-kiN3-bin7, iau2-ku2 thang tiaN7-tiaN7 ng3-bang7 e7-tit cho3-hoe2 chu7-chip8 ti7 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 ui7-cheng5, thang saN-kap hiong2-siu7 eng2-oan2 bu5-han7-liong7 e5 hok-khi3. Sim-sou2-goan7.       
I5 jiau5-li2 sia2--e5.
