Jip8-chhiu e5 Tam7-chui2 koe-a2
Nga2-ki5

Jip8-chhiu liau2-au7 e5 Tam7-chui2 koe-a2-thau5 u7 tam7-poh8-a2 leng2, m7-koh tak8-e5 sim-lai7 long2 bo5 sio2-khoa2-a2 koaN5 e5 kam2-kak, in-ui7 goan2 kap 1-e5 chin chhu3-bi7 ma7 chin jiat8-cheng5 e5 lang5 cho3-hoe2, ti7 leh 1-e5 bo5 joa7 toa7 e5 sou2-chai7 ma7 kam2-kak un-loan2, chit-e5 lang5 to7-si7 Hou7-chou7 hiaN.

Hou7-chou7 hiaN 10 goa7 hoe3-a2 to7 lai5 kau3 Tai5-pak cho3 sai-a2, hit tong-si5 tak8-e5 seng-oah8 long2 bo5 ho2 koe3. Hou7-chou7 hiaN ko-tiong chhut-giap8 liau2-au7, tai7-seng ti7 Tai5-pak Se-mng5-teng Ban7-kok Hi3-iN7 piN--a2 e5 li2-hoat-thiaN cho3 sam-chhit-a2, keng-koe3 2 ko3 goa7 goeh8, Hou7-chou7 hiaN kap hit-chun7 e5 siau3-lian5 khi2 chhiong-thut8, to7 koh tng2-khi3 e7-kang2, m7-koh e7-kang2 khak-sit8 phaiN2 koe3-jit8, Hou7-chou7 hiaN 18 hoe3 e5 si5 koh lai5 kau3 Tai5-pak, ti7 1-e5 peng5-iu2 e5 li2-hoat-thiaN cho3 khang-khoe3, chin tng5 1-toaN7 si5-kan liau2-au7, Hou7-chou7 hiaN lai5 kau3 Tam7-chui2, ka-ti7 khui li2-hoat-thiaN, boe2--a2 oaN7 khan-chhiu2 keng-eng5, ka-ti7 khi3 sang3 hai2-san2.

Hou7-chou7 hiaN chit-e5 lang5 chin khai-long2, ka7 goan2 kong2 hai2-san2, koh kong2-tioh8 i soe3-han3 chng-kha sou2-chai7 e5 seng-oah8, i koh kong2-tioh8 ka-ti7 soe3-han3 e5 si5, ti7 Toa7-na5 thau-chau2 khi3 khoaN3 thoat-ni-bu2 e5 tai7-chi3. Chit-koa2 tai7-chi3 kong2 kah hou7 kui chhu3-keng e5 lang5 chhio3-hai-hai7, khoaN3 boe7 chhut-lai5 kong2, ki5-sit8 Hou7-chou7 hiaN pun2-chiaN5 si7 kong2-oe7 soe3-siaN, ma7 bo5 siaN2 kaN2 hoat-piau2 ka-ti7 siuN7-hoat e5 lang5, tui3 cheng3-ti7 1-sut-a2 to m7-bat, m7-koh chu7 chham-ka "Chheng-lian5 Tian7-tai5" liau2-au7, chiap-chhiok tioh8 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 koan-tiam2, ma7 kap chin choe7 lang5 chih-chiap, chiah tauh8-tauh8-a2 ka7 ka-ti7 e5 sim phah--khui, chit-ma2 Hou7-chou7 hiaN i2-keng si7 i choh-sit hit-e5 sou2-chai7 e5 i3-kian3 leng2-siu3, ma7 chin u7 ka-ti7 e5 siuN7-hoat, siong7 kai3 beng5-hian2--e5, to7-si7 tak8-kai2 soan2-ki2, Hou7-chou7 hiaN long2 e7 choan-kang tng2-khi3 Ka-gi7 ku7-chhu3 tng3 hui3-phio3, pat8-lang5 soan2-ki2 si7 u7 chiN5 thang thio, m7-koh Hou7-chou7 hiaN soan2-ki2 tian-to2 ai3 ka-ti7 khai-chiN5 che7 poe-leng5-ki chau2 tng2--khi3 tng3 hui3-phio3.

Cho3 tian7-tai5 e5 si5, Hou7-chou7 hiaN chham-ka koe-thau5 iu5-heng5 long2 si7 su3-siong5--e5, i ma7 ti7 tian7-tai5 chiat-bok8--nih kong2 boe7-chio2 phoe-phoaN3 e5 gian5-lun7, kan-taN ui7-tioh8 1-e5 li2-siong2 teh chhiong, ma7-si7 an3 chit-chun7 chiah khai-si2 oh8 Tai5-bun5. I tiam3 e5 khangpang to7 iong7 Tai5-bun5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 sia2 "Tek Ko Chhu".

Ui7-tioh8 1-e5 kap pat8-lang5 bo5 kang7-khoan2 e5 li2-siong2, Hou7-chou7 hiaN bat hou7 Po2-chhit-chong2-tui7 chhoe7 ma5-hoan5--koe3, tiam3-a2 e5 khangpang ma7 bat hou7 lang5 long3-phoa3 kui2-lo7-kai2, chin choe7 si5-chun7, i e5 peng5-iu2 boe7-tang3 li2-kai2 i e5 siuN7-hoat, m7-koh Hou7-chou7 hiaN long2 chai-iaN2 ka-ti7 beh cho3 siaN2, boe7 hou7 lang5 eng2-hiong2, put-si5 ma7 kap lang5 pian7-lun7 i3-kian3.

Hou7-chou7 hiaN u7-si5-a2 ma7 chin kam2-khai3 kong, chit-ma2 e5 Tai5-pak gin2-a2 long2 boe7-hiau2 kong2 Tai5-gi2--a, i kong2-tioh8 ka-ti7 ti7 chhu3--nih e7 iau-kiu5 gin2-a2 kong2 Tai5-gi2, m7-koh gin2-a2 ti7 goa7-khau2 iah-si7 hak8-hau7 long2 kong2 Pak-kiaN-gi2, ti3-su2 tiam3 chhu3-lai7 6~7 siaN5 ma7 long2 teh kong2 Pak-kiaN-gi2. Chit-ma2 e5 sia7-hoe7, lian5 Hou7-chou7 hiaN chit-khoan2 u7 i3-sek e5 lang5, to bo5 hoat-tou7 kai2-pian3 chit-khoan2 cheng5-heng5, an2-ne si7 m7 si7 bun7-toe5 e7 ju2 lai5 ju2 giam5-tiong7? Lan2 chin-chiaN3 tioh8 ho2-ho2-a2 su-kho2, ma7 tioh8 khah kin2 heng5-seng5 Tai5-oan5-lang5 ka-ti7 e5 i3-sek.

Kap Hou7-chou7 hiaN khai-kang2, hou7 goa2 khoaN3-tioh8 1-e5 goa2 m7-bat chiap-chhiok--koe3 e5 se3-kai3, chai-iaN2 1-e5 lang5 e5 kai2-pian3, ma7 khoaN3-tioh8 i e5 jiat8-cheng5, che si7 lan2 chit-tai7 e5 gin2-a2 kap in boe7-tang3 pi2-pheng7--e5. KhoaN3-tioh8 Hou7-chou7 hiaN, hou7 goa2 siuN7 chin choe7, ma7 su-kho2 tioh8 au7-pai2 beh an2-choaN2 kiaN5--loh8, sui-bong2 chit-ma2 e5 siau3-lian5-a2 tui3 seng-khu-piN e5 tai7-chi3 long2 bo5 koan-sim, ah si7 long2 m7-bat, m7-koh thiaN-tioh8 Hou7-chou7 hiaN 1-lou7 kiaN5--koe3 e5 sim-lai7-oe7, goa2 soah kam2-kak lan2 e5 bi7-lai5 koh u7 tam7-poh8-a2 ng3-bang7, koh u7 tam7-poh8-a2 kng-soaN3.
