Keng-koe3 cha-hng sin-khou2 chun2-pi7, kin-a2-jit8 e5 oah8-tang7 chong2-sng3 cheng3-sek khai-si2.

Beh 200-e5 uI3 bo5-kang5 sou2-chai7, bo5-kang5 chit-giap8 lai5 e5 hak8-goan5, ui7-tioh8 Tai5-oan5 e5 bun5-hoa3 chu-goan5, tak8-e5 ti7 chit-e5 sou2-chai7 oa2 cho3-hoe2, theh8-chhut tui3 gi2-gian5 bun5-hoa3 e5 khoaN3-hoat kap kai2-koat e5 cheng3-chhek.

Ui3 mui2 1-chiat kho3 lai7-te2, kang2-su kap hak8-goan5 teh tho2-lun7, i3-kian3 hun-hiong2, long2 e7-tang3 khoaN3-chhut siong7-kho3 khi3-hun chin lau7-jiat8, tiong-iong e5 hioh-khun3 si5-kan, hou7 tak8-e5 e7-tang3 hoe5-hok cheng-sin5, koh u7 chun2-pi7 tiam2-sim, hou7 tak8-e5 lang5 pou2-chhiong the2-lat8, ke3-siok8 siong7 au7-1-chiat-kho3.

Am3-si5 e5 am3-hoe7, hou7 lang5 kian3-sek-tioh8 1-chiong2 bo5-kang5 e5 piau2-ian2, ma7 hou7 lang5 sim-cheng5 khah khin-sang.
