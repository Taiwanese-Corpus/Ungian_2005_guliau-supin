Na7-si7 Tan5 Soan-leng7 Bok8-su e5 tong5- hoe7 iah8 chin oah8-tang7. Chiau3 goa2 khoaN3 in jin7-ui7 Bok8-su siat chit nng7-khoan2 e5 hoe7, u7 chin-toa7 e5 li7-ek. (1) Thang li7-ek kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5, hou7 hiaN-ti7 chi2-moe7 khah be7 hoan7-choe7. (2) Thang li7-ek kau3-hoe7-goa7 e5 lang5, hou7 se3- siok8 lang5 khah u7 ki-hoe7 thang bat Kiu3- chu2. Chit-e5 Kiu3-chhin-hoe7 goan2 Hu2-siaN5 ti7 mui2 le2-pai3 me5-hng i2-keng u7 siat kui2-na7 ni5, mia5 sui-bong2 bo5 siang7, chong2-si7 i3-su3 u7 saN-tang5. Goa2 ng3-bang7 siok8 siaN5- chhi7 kap pou-thau5 e5 kau3-hoe7, tioh8 oh8 chit-e5 ho2 hoat-tou7 khi3 kiaN5, un2-tang3 e7 hou7 kau3-hoe7 oah8-tang7 heng-khi2.    
Ti7 Lam5-ke e5 tong5-hoe7, koh ti7 pai3-saN me5 u7 siat chit8 e5 Bian2-le7-hoe7, mia5-kio3 Chong-to7-hoe7. Hit me5-hng, u7 kui2-na7 chap8 e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 chu7-chip8. Ti7 Choan5-chiu siaN5-lai7 Kok-ka u7 siat chit8-keng Tiong-oh8 tng5, tiong-kan ia7 u7  ka3 Thian-bun5, Te7-li2, Han3-bun5, Eng-bun5, Pit-soan3 hiah-e5 hak8-bun7; u7 chhiaN2 lan2 kau3-hoe7-lai7 e5 lang5 khi3 ka3.
