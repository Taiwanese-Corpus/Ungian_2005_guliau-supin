
Bunun Seng3-keng hoan-ek8 e5 keng-ke3
Ou5 Bun5-ti5
Bunun si7 tiam3 ti7 Tai5-oan5 San-te7 e5 chit8 chok8, goan5-cha2 ti7 Sin-ko-san e5 hu7-kun7, hian7-chai7 chhian-soa2 kau3 Tai5-tiong, Ko-hiong5, Hoe-lian5, Tai5-tang e5 san-te7.  In si7 bun5-hoa3 choe3-ke7 e5 lang5, kau3 3 ni5 cheng5 bo5 u7 jip8 tiong-teng2 hak8-hau7 e5 lang5.  In sui-jian5 oe7 kong2 Jit8-pun2 oe7, chong2-si7 boe7-hiau2 thak8 Jit8-pun2 oe7 e5 Seng3-keng kau3 beng5-pek8.  Na7 ai3 in2-chhoa7 chit khoan2 e5 lang5 lai5 sin3 Chu2, tioh8 ai3 eng7 Seng3-keng lai5 hoan-ek8 choe3 in e5 oe7, koh eng7 sim2-mih8 lai5 hou7 in khia7-chai7 ti7 si3-chiaN3 e5 sin3-giong2.  Sou2-i2 ti7 3 ni5 cheng5, San-te7 Soan-to7-hoe7-tiuN2 Sun Nga2-kok Bok8-su, eng7 chit e5 hoan-ek8 Bunun Seng3-keng e5 kang kau-tai3 goa2 e5 si5, goa2 sui5-si5 hoaN-hi2 seng5-siu7, chong2-si7 chit8 hong-bin7 ia7 kiaN-liau2 tam-tng chit e5 kang boe7 khi2.  Chit-tiap8 kan2-tan po3-ko3 hit e5 keng-ke3 hou7 lin2 chai.
I.	Chun2-pi7.
1947 ni5 e5 9 geh8, goa2 u7 tui3 Tai7-kah kau3-hoe7 lai5 Koan-san.  In-ui7 chia u7 chin-choe7 Bunun, chit8-peng5 ia7 thang pang-chan7 Koan-san bo5 bok8-chia2 e5 kau3-hoe7.  Goa2 khi2-seng iau3-kin2 e5 kang si7 gian2-kiu3 Bunun e5 oe7.  Chia u7 tu2-tioh8 3 hang7 e5 khun3-lan5.
(1)	Chiu7-si7 tng-si5 lan2 bo5 u7 Bunun e5 sin3-tou5, koh Tai5-tang koan7-tiuN2 m7-chun2 lan2 jip8 soaN thoan5-to7, sou2-i2 bo5 ki-hoe7 thang kap in chiap-chhiok kong2-oe7.
(2)	Si7 ai3 chhiaN3 tu2-ho2 e7 ka3-oe7 e5 sian-siN chin oh.  In-ui7 in long2-si7 bo5 hak8-bun7 e5 lang5, ka-ti7 oe7-hiau2 kong2-oe7, beh ka3 lang5 chiu7 boe7-oe7.  Sou2-i2 nng7 ni5 ku2 u7 oaN7 chin-choe7 e5 sian-siN.
(3)	Si7 in-ui7 in bo5 ka-ki7 e5 ji7, sou2-i2 bo5 u7 phiau-chun2 e5 oe7, ti3-kau3 tak8-e5 hoan-sia7 e5 oe7 u7 cheng-chha, chit e5 sian-siN kap pat8 e5 sian-siN sou2 ka3--e5, siong5-siong5 bo5 siang7.
II.	Che3-teng7 Bunun e5 ji7.
Goa2 gian2-kiu3 oe7 e5 si5 chiu7 chai in e5 oe7 u7 hun nng7 khoan2 (Take Bunowad kap Bubukun) in e5 jin5-khau2 si7 bo5 che7, siat-su2 na7 tioh8 hoan-ek8 choe3 nng7 khoan2 e5 Seng3-keng, che si7 chin khun3-lan5 e5 su7.  Kau3-be2 goa2 u7 hoat-kian3-tioh8 chit nng7 khoan2 e5 oe7 u7 it-teng7 e5 kui-chek teh pian3.  An2-ni goa2 chiu7 eng7 19 e5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 lai5 sia2 in e5 long2-chong2 e5 oe7.  Goa2 khi2-seng eng7 theng5-sia2-pan2 in3 ji7-bu2 hou7 in thak8, au7-lai5 chiu7 eng7 oah8-pan2 in3 ji7-bu2.  Chit e5 u7 seng5-kong e5 si5, goa2 chiah sio2-khoa2 u7 an-sim kap hoaN-hi2.  In-ui7 na7 bo5 in e5 ji7, chiu7 boe7-oe7 hoan-ek8 in e5 Seng3-keng.
Koh in e5 kou3-su7 u7 kong2, in kou2-cha2 u7 in e5 ji7, si7 hong5-chui2 hoan3-lam7 e5 si5, ji7 soa3 hou7 i lau5--khi3, ti3-kau3 kin-a2-jit8 soa3 bo5.  An2-ni in m7-kam bo5 ji7, si7 u7 ai3 ji7.  Sou2-i2 na7 eng7 in e5 ji7 lai5 ka3 in, in chu7-jian5 chin ai3, an2-ni thang choe3 thoan5-to7 ho2 e5 ki-hoe7.  U7 chit8 e5 Ko-no-hong e5 chhoan-tiuN2, in-ui7 ai3 oh8-ji7, lai5 hu3 Kang2-sip8-hoe7, u7 piN3 jiat8-sim e5 sin3-tou5.  Te7-it gau5 thak8--e5, nng7 tiam2 cheng chiu7 oe7-hiau2 thak8 19 ji7 e5 Lo5-ma2-ji7.
