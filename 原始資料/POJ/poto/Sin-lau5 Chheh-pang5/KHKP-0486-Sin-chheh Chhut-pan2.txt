
Sin-chheh Chhut-pan2
Chap8-hang7 Koan2-kian3  
Chit pun2 si7 Chhoa3 Poe5-hoe2 Sian-siN sin tu3 e5 chheh, eng7 Lo5-ma2-ji7 lai5 in3. Chiong i peng5-si5 ai3 kap tong5-pau tam5-l5, keng2 khah tiong7-iau3 e5 chap8-hang7. Hit tiong-kan long2 hun si3, gou7 tiau5 siong5-se3 e5 bok8-liok8, lai5 chhian2-beng5 hit e5 li2-khi3.  Ku3-ku3 to si7 tui3 i e5 sim-lai7 thou3-lou7 chhutul3ai, e7-thang kio3-chheN2 lan2 e5 cheng-sin5.  Sou2 kong2 e5 oe7 si7 chin peN5-pang7, sou2 lun7 e5 su7 ia7-si7 lan2 mui2-jit8 peng5-siong5 sou2 ch5. Loe7-iong5 chhiong-chiok, chai5-liau7 hong-hu3.  Na7 thak8-tioh8 chit pun2 chheh, bo5 koh-iuN7 chhin-chhiuN7 tng-bin7 siu7 i e5 chi2-kau3, sit8-chai7 si7 khui-khe2 ti3-sek, poe5-iuN2 to7-tek e5 ho2 chheh lah!    
Chit pun2 chheh i2-keng in3-ho2 teh kap, si7 su3-liok8 pan2, chit8-pah lak8-chap8 goa7 bin7, lak8-ban7 lak8-chheng goa7 ji7.  Ti7 chit goeh8-poaN3 beh hoat-heng5; mui2-pun2 lak8-kak gin5, iu5-hui3 si3-chiam.  Na7 be2 chiuN7 chap8-pun2, iu5-hui3 m7-bian2; iah8 hak8-seng lian5-hap8 be2 30 pun2 i2-siong7, beh sng3 kiam2 nng7-koah.    
Hoat-heng5-sou2 si7 Chhoa3 Poe5-hoe2 Sian-siN in tau, chiu7-si7 Tai5-lam5-chhi7 Kang2-teng, It-teng-bok8, 19 hoan-te7. 臺南市港町一丁目十九番地蔡培火   Goan2 Tai5-lam5 Sin-lau5 Chheh-pang5 ia7 u7 the3 i be7.  
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 486 koan3,1925 ni5 9 geh8)
