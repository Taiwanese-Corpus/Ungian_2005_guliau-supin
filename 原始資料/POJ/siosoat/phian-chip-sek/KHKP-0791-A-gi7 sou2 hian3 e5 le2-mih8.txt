
A-gi7 sou2 hian3 e5 le2-mih8
Chit e5 Kam2-sia7-cheh e5 kou3-su7 si7 ti7 te7 ji7 chhu3 se3-kai3 tai7-chian3 tiong, tui3 Tiong-kok thoan5--lai5 e5.
A-gi7 si7 chit8 e5 chap8-si3-gou7 hoe3 e5 gin2-a2.  U7 chit8 jit8, teh kiaN5 lou7 e5 si5 chin siong-sim, in-ui7 i bo5 pe7-bu2, bo5 hiaN-che2, bo5 chhin-lang5, chi2-u7 lau5 i ka-ki7 chit8 e5 na7-tiaN7.  Sou2-i2 na2 kiaN5 na2 siuN7 i sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 keng2-gu7.  Tu2 chit8 le2-pai3 cheng5, A-gi7 ti7 hak8-hau7 teh thak8-chheh, ka-teng5, chhu3-lai7 long2 an-choan5, bo5 sim2-mih8 thang ho2 khoa3-lu7.  Chong2-si7 taN kan-ta chhun i chit8-e5, si7 ui7-tioh8 tui3-tek8 e5 cha3-tan5 cha3-liau2 i e5 chhu3, it-chin7 siu7 hoe2 sio biat8.  A-gi7 e5 ka-chok8 long2 si3-soaN3, A-gi7 nng7 jit8 ku2 ti7 ke-lou7 si3-koe3 kiaN5, si3-koe3 chhoe7, chhoe7 long2 bo5 i e5 chhin-lang5.  Peh-seN3 ia7 long2 si3-soaN3 to5-chau2 kau3 sai-peng5 e5 soaN khi3.  A-gi7 ia7 toe3 lang5 chau2, kiaN5 4 jit8 ku2, jit8--si5 tioh8 it-tit8 kiaN5, am3-si5 tioh8 khun3 ti7 lou7-piN.  Pak-tou7 na7 iau, A-gi7 tioh8 tui3 lang5 tho2 mih8 lai5 chiah8, pian3 cho3 chit8 e5 chhin-chhiuN7 khit-chiah8 e5 khoan2, sit8-chai7 chin kho2-lian5.  A-gi7 boe7 tu2-tioh8 chit e5 chhi-chham2 e5 si5, ti7 i e5 sim-lai7 u7 khng3 chit8 e5 ng3-bang7, chiu7-si7 ng3-bang7 i u7 chit8 jit8 beh keng-koe3 tai7-hak8 tit-tioh8 hak8-ui7.  TaN bo5 hoat-tou7, in-ui7 bo5 chhu3, ia7 bo5 chiN5, bo5 hak8-hau7, lian5 ka-chok8 ia7 bo5--khi3 lah.  I kam2-kak chin loe2-chi3, chin bo5 pou7.
Hut-jian5 ti7 lou7 e5 oat-kak u7 khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 tui7 peng ti7 lou7-piN teh hioh-khun3.  A-gi7 chin kiaN, ai3 siam2-pi7, chiu7 chau2 tui3 chhiu7-a2-kha khi3, in-ui7 m7-chai peng si7 siok8 ti7 to2-chit8 peng5 e5.  Chong2-si7 siam2 bo5 lou7, kun-tiuN2 i2-keng khoaN3-tioh8 A-gi7, eng7 toa7 siaN hoah i kong2, ¡§Khit-chiah8 gin2-a2, theng5 chit8-e7; li2 khoaN3 tui3-bin7 soaN-kha u7 te5-hng5, chau2 khi3 ka7 te7-chu2 kong2, peng, kun-tiuN2 beh hioh-me5 ti7 in tau, tioh8 pi7-pan7 sou2-chai7 kap chu2 chheN-chhau hou7 8 e5 lang5 thang chiah8 kau3 pa2.  PhaiN2-mia7 gin2-a2...... li2 kaN2 thau-chau2.¡¨
A-gi7 thiaN an2-ni, ti7 sim-lai7 teh kam2-sia7.  I teh siuN7, bo5 tek-khak i ia7 oe7 tit-tioh8 te7-chu2 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7 thang tou7-pa2, in-ui7 i chin iau.  Koh chit8 hang7, A-gi7 hoaN-hi2 pang-chan7 kok-ka e5 peng, sou2-i2 toa7-pou7 chau2, chau2 kau3 te7-chu2 e5 toa7-mng5-khau2 phah-mng5.  Kou3-mng5 e5 khi2-thau5 khoaN3 chit e5 phoa3-lam3 e5 gin2-a2 m7-chun2 i jip8.  Chong2-si7 A-gi7 it-tit8 kong2 u7 iau3-kin2 e5 su7 ai3 po3 chu2-lang5 chai; kau3 be2, kou3-mng5 e5 chiah chhoa7 ijip8 chu2-lang5 e5 kheh-thiaN.
 
Chu2-lang5 mng7 A-gi7 kong2, ¡§Li2 u7 sim2-mih8 siau-sit thang po3?¡¨  A-gi7 eng7 chin ho2-le2 e5 oe7 kong2-beng5.  Chu2-lang5 thiaN-liau2 tioh8-kiaN kong2, ¡§TaN, chham2-ah! Tu2-ho2 chit si5 lai7-bin7 toa7 khiam3 kang, Lam5--e5 chhut-khi3 cho3 peng, lu2--e5 teh ban2 te5, te5 teh-beh siu-seng5, tai7-ke hui-siong5 e5 bo5 eng5......¡¨  A-gi7 sui5-si5 in3 kong2 , ¡§Tai7-jin5!  Li2 na7 kheng2, goa2 chiu7 hoaN-hi2 tau3 pang-bang5, goa2 oe7 koaN7chui2, tau3 cho3-kang, tang-sai, chhiaN2 hou7 goa2 cho3.¡¨
It-tan3 ti7 mng5-khau2 i2-keng u7 thiaN-kiN3 peng teh phah-mng5 e5 siaN, sou2-i2 chu2-lang5 chiu7 in3 kong2, ¡§Ho2 lah, ho2 lah,chhiaN2 toa3 chia; kin2-kin2 khi3 chau3-kha,tioh8 gau5 thiaN chu2-png7 e5 oe7.¡¨  Te7-chu2 chiu7 kin2 khi3 mng5-khau2 hoan-geng5 8 mia5 e5 peng-tui7 jip8-lai5.  Peng-tui7 u7 siu7 te7-chu2 e5 ho2 khoan2-thai7, keh-jit8 thau3-cha2 chiu7 li7-khui khi3 pat8 sou2-chai7.
A-gi7 chin hoaN-hi2 i u7 sou2-chai7 thang khun3, u7 png7 thang chiah8, u7 kang thang cho3, hek8-si7 taN chui2, phang5 png7, hek8-si7 sau3-te3, ban2 te5, bo5-lun7 siaN2 khoan2 e5 kang, i chin kut-lat8 jin7-chin cho3.  Sou2-i2 chit8 jit8 koe3 chit8 jit8, A-gi7 e5 sim hoaN-hi2 khoai3-lok8, chiam7-chiam7 boe7-ki3-tit ii2-cheng5 e5 kan-khou2.
Chi2-u7 chit8 hang7 i e5 chu2-lang5 hou7 A-gi7 chin ki5-koai3 e5, chiu7-si7 ti7 6 jit8 lai7 ka-jin5 kap chhiaN3-kang lang5 long2 chhut-lat8 cho3 kang, kau3 te7 7 jit8, tai7-ke long2 hioh-khun3.  Ti7 hit jit8 choan5 ka-chok8 long2 chheng7 sui2 saN, kiaN5 saN eng-li2 e5 lou7 khi3 chit8 keng soat-kau3-sou2 choe3 le2-pai3.  A-gi7 thau5 chit8 pai2 thiaN to7-li2, thiaN long2 bo5; chong2-si7 I chin ai3 gim5-si, ai3 thiaN gin2-a2 kou3-su7.  I kam2-kak hu3-hoe7 e5 lang5 long2 chin hoaN-hi2 koh chhin-chhiat.  A-gi7 sit8-chai7 iau2 m7-bat tu2-tioh8 chhin-chhiuN7 chit khoan2!  Lian5 kou3-mng5 e5 lang5 ia7 tui3 i piau2-si7 ho2-i3.  Sou2-i2 A-gi7 mui2 le2-pai3 m7-bat khiam3-sek8, na2 khi3, na2 ai3 khi3.  Chu2-lang5 u7 si5 kong2 Ia5-sou e5 kou3-su7 a2-si7 soat-beng5 to7-li2 hou7 A-gi7 thiaN.  Tui3 an2-ni A-gi7 chiam7-chiam7 bat ThiN-pe7 e5 thiaN3.
  U7 chit8 jit8, bok8-su po3-ko3 beh siu2 Kam2-sia7-cheh (Siu-tang-cheh), u7 chhiaN2 liat8-ui7 lai5 chun2-pi7.  A-gi7 thiaN liau2 chin hoaN-hi2 teh siuN7, ¡§M7-chai goa2 u7 sim2-mih8 thang hian3 hou7 ThiN-pe7.¡¨  Chu2-lang5 ia7 si7 teh chun2-pi7 le2-mih8; in-ui7 i e5 soaN long2 si7 te5-hng5, sou2-i2 teh chun2-pi7 sin-te5 thang cho3 i e5 le2-mih8.  Tak8 jit8 kang-lang5 u7 ban2 iu3-iu3 e5 te5-hioh8 leng7-goa7 he7 ti7 chit8 kha na5, si7 tek8-piat8 keng2 te7-it ho2 e5 te5-sim te5 beh cho3 Kam2-sia7-cheh e5 le2-mih8.   
In-ui7 Kam2-sia7-cheh teh-beh kau3, sou2-i2 mui2 chit8 e5 toa7 lang5 a2-si7 gin2-a2 long2 teh liam7 i beh hian3 sim2-mih8, i beh hian3 sim2-mih8.  U7-e5 kong2,¡§Goa2 beh hian3 chit8 chiah iuN5-a2,¡¨  ¡§Goa2 beh hian3 ke-nng7,¡¨ ¡§Goa2 beh hian3 chit8 chiah ah,¡¨ u7-e5 kong2, ¡§Goa2 beh hian3 chit8-koa2 chhai3,¡¨ teng2.  A-gi7 na2 thiaN sim na2 hoan5-lo2, in-ui7 i bo5 sim2-mih8 thang hian3 hou7 ThiN-pe7.  Hut-jian5-kan siuN7-tioh8 chit8 hang7, chiu7 kin2-kin2 chau2 khi3 kau3 chit8 e5 ou5-piN koah lou5-tek, koah liau2 tng2-khi3 chhu3-nih8 beh ti7 chit8 sou2-chai7 am3-cheN7 cho3 chit8 kha na5-a2.
Kam2-sia7-cheh le2-pai3-jit8 kau3.  Tai7-ke teh chun2-pi7 le2-mih8.  A-gi7 ia7 si7 teh chun2-pi7 i e5 le2-mih8.  A-gi7 jip8-khi3 te5-hang5 ka-ki7 liam7 kong2, ¡§Goa2 beh chiong goa2 e5 chu2-lang5 sou2 beh hian3 e5 te5-sim piaN3 ti7 goa2 chit kha sui2-sui2 koh sin e5 na5-a2, in-ui7 sin e5 te5-sim tioh8 te2 ti7 sin e5 na5 khah hah8!¡¨  Tu2-tu2 hit si5 chu2-lang5 khui-mng5 jip8-lai5 beh theh8 i sou2 pi7-pan7 e5 te5-sim khi3 le2-pai3-tng5.  A-gi7 khoaN3-kiN3 chu2-lang5, tioh8 chit8-kiaN, soa3 chhia-to2 te5-sim, koh te5-sim hou7 hong chhoe-soaN3.  Chu2-lang5 khoaN3-kiN3 an2-ni chin bo5 hoaN-hi2 kong2, ¡§Sim2-mih8 lang5 bong goa2 po2-poe3 e5 le2-mih8?¡¨  A-gi7 chin kiaN, chek-si5 ti7 chu2-lang5 e5 bin7-cheng5 phak-teh, m7-kaN2 khui chhui3, sim-koaN phok8-phok8-chheng2.  Chu2-lang5 oat8-thau5 khoaN3-tioh8 chit8 kha sui2-sui2 e5 na5, chiu7 chhio3-iong5 tui3 A-gi7 kong2, ¡§KiaN2 ah!taN goa2 beng5-pek8 li2 e5 i3-su3.  Chit si5 kin2 khi3 te5-hng5 koh ban2 sin e5 te5, lou7-chu2 i iau2-boe7 hou7 jit8 phak8 ta, hioh8-a2 iau2 chheN-chheN, sui2-sui2.  Che si7 ThiN-pe7 e5 i3-su3.  Sin e5 te5 tioh8 ai3 te2 ti7 sin e5 na5 chiah tioh8.  Li2 pi2 goa2 u7 pi7-pan7 khah ho2, khah hah8 e5 le2-mih8!¡¨
A-gi7 thiaN chit ku3 boe7 kong2-tit e5 hoaN-hi2, chiu7 chau2 kau3 te5-hng5 ban2 sui2-sui2 sin-sin e5 te5-hioh8 he7 ti7 sin e5 na5, koaN7 kau3 le2-pai3-tng5.  Ti7 le2-pai3-tng5 lang5 che kau3 tiN7-tiN7, tai7-ke hui-siong5 e5 hoaN-hi2. Le2-mih8 long2 pai5 ti7 thau5-cheng5, chin nau7-jiat8, chin ho2 khoaN3.  A-gi7 khoaN3 isou2 cho3 e5 na5 te2 i e5 chu2-lang5 sou2 hian3 e5 te5, sim-koaN hoaN-hi2 tio5-thiau3, kam2-sia7 ThiN-pe7 e5 thiaN3-thang3 kap  I e5 khoaN3-kou3.
