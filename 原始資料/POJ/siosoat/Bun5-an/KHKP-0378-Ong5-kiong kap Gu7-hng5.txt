
Ong5-kiong kap Gu7-hng5
Bun5 An
Kou2-cha2 e5 si5-chun7 u7 chit8 e5 goa7 kok ong5, I e5 ong5-kiong hang5-ngou2 kau3 kek8. Iu7-koh kin7 ti7 khah lau7-jiat8 e5 sou2-chai7 u7 chit8 khu hoe-hng5, in-ui7 hit sou2-chai7 si7 chin koaN5, sou2-i2 u7 chiong po-le5 khi2 chhu3 lai5 kou3 hit khu hoe-bak8, kiaN-liau2 hou7 sng-seh tang3 si2, u7 eng7 chin toa7 e5 sim-sin5 teh chai-kou3, cheng2-tun3 kau3 put-chi2 che5-chaN2 ho2-khoaN3; iah8 u7 chai-cheng3 che7-che7 hi-han2 e5 hoe, i2-kip8 siong7-teng2 e5 koe2-chi2, long2 si7 i tiau5-chhoan5 i3-ai3--e5.  
Sui-jian5 khai-hui3 che7-che7 i iah8 kam-goan7 chhut; bo5 hiam5 hng5-hui3 tang7, in-ui7 teh ng3-bang7 sou2 beh kiat e5 koe2-chi2 kap sou2 e7 khui e5 hoe, put-koan2 si5 thang khi3 hia gu7-siong2, hou7 sim-cheng7 hoaN-hi2.  
Tu2-gu7 chit8 pai2 si7 tang thiN e5 si5, sou2-i2 bo5 khui hoe, kan-ta u7 chit8 chang5 phu5-to5, siN chit8 sui7 chin7 toa7 pha chin7 sui2-e5, khoaN3-kiN3 e5 lang5 bo5 m7 him-sian7.  Ti7 hit po-le5 chhu3, lian5 koe3 lou7 e5 lang5 iah8 khoaN3 hian7-hian7.  
Bou2 jit8 u7 chit8 e5 song3-hiong e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 tui3 hia keng-koe3, i iah8 theng5 kha kim-kim teh siong3 hit pha phu5-to5, sim-lai7 siuN7 kong2,¡§Na7-si7 goa2 e7 tit be2 che phu5-to5 siang7 u7 hok-khi3.¡¨ I him-bou7 che koe2-chi2, khiok m7-si7 ka-ti7 ai3 chiah8, ki5-sit8 si7 it-tioh8 beh hou7 i sou2 thiaN3 e5 cha-bou2 kiaN2 chiah8, in-ui7 hit si5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 u7 hoan7-tioh8 tang7 peN7, bo2 thiaN3 kiaN2 e5 cheng5 chhim; tui3 an2-ni chiu7 siuN7 hoat-tou7, me5 jit8 phang2-chit cho3 kang than3 chiN5.  Tu2-a2 tit-tioh8 chit8 khou gin5 e5 si5, chiu7 kin2 khi3 beh be2 hit pha phu5-to5, khi3 kau3 te3 tui3 kou3 hng5 e5 lang5 kong2-khi2.  
Hit e5 lang5 bo5 beh in3 i, hoan2-tng2 thi2-chhio3 i, mng5 sak oa2 cho3 i khi3.  
Hu7-jin5-lang5 sim-lai7 ut-chut teh siuN7 kaN2-si7 i e5 chiN5 bo5 kau3-giah8, sou2-i2 hit e5 hng5-teng m7 kheng2 chiong koe2-chi2 be7 i.  
I to3-khi3 chhu3-nih8 chiu7 siong5-se3 siuN7 khoaN3 u7 sim2-mih8 pou7-sou3 thang koh ke tit-tioh8 chiN5, siuN7-lai5 siuN7-khi3 long2 bo5 pou7, in-ui7 i ke-keng5 loh8-poh8, long2 bo5 siaN2 tat8 chiN5 e5 mih8 thang pian3; chi2-u7 bin5-chhng5-nih8 chit8 nia2 than2-a2 si7 i peng5-si5 teh eng7-e5.  I chiu7 chiong hit nia2 than2-a2 hiahN khi3 tng3, tng3 chit8 khou gin5.  
TaN chham teng2-jit8 e5 chiN5 long2-kiong7 si7 nng7 khou, koaN2-kin2 koh khi3 mng7 hit e5 hng5-teng khoaN3 u7 kam-goan7 be7 i a2-bo5? Kau3 ui7 e5 si5 kiN3 chit8 bin7, hng5-teng hoat toa7 siu7-khi3, phaiN2 siaN-sau3 him2-hoah tit8-tit8 koaN2 i chhut.  
Tu2 ho2 hit-si5 ong5 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ti7 gu7-hng5-lai7 iu5-siuN2, hut-jian5 thiaN-kiN3 chha2-jiong2 e5 siaN, chiu7 mng7 si7 sim2-mih8 tai7-chi3.  
Hng5-teng chiu7 liam5-piN chiong hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 sou2 ai3 ka7 kong-chu2 kong2-beng5.  ThiaN-liau2 liam5-piN chhe kiong-ngou5 khi3 jiok hu7-jin5-lang5 to3-tng2 lai5. Hu7-jin5-lang5 kau3 kong-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, kiaN-hiaN5 ngiauh8-ngiauh8 chhoah kau3 be7 kou3-tit.  
KhoaN3-kiN3 kong-chu2 bo5 chek-choe7, hoan2-tng2 si in eng7 ho2 oe7 an-ui3, hu7-jin5-lang5 chiu7 ho2-taN2 chiong sit8-cheng5 kong2 hou7 i thiaN.

TaN chit e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 sou2 lek8-koe3 e5 su7, tu2-tu2 thang chioh lai5 phi3-ju7 lan2 lang5 u7 toa7 khiam3-khoeh, Siong7-te3 hoaN-hi2 si-in, hou7 i keng2-soan2 e5 peh-seN3.  
Choe7-jin5 tit kiu3 m7 si7 ui7-tioh8 ka-ti7 e5 ho2 sou2 kiaN5, ia7 m7 si7 tui3 gau5 ki5-to2, a2-si7 te5-chiN5 chun2 cho3 kong-lo5 lai5 tit-tioh8 eng2-oan2 oah8 e5 hok-khi3. Chhian-ban7 m7 thang chu7-gou7.  Ki5-sit8 si7 Siong7-te3 peh8-peh8 e5 in-hui7.  In-ui7 Seng3-chheh beng5-beng5 u7 kong2,¡§Long2 bo5 u7 ho2 lang5.  In-ui7 cheng3-lang5 i2-keng hoan7-choe7.¡¨
