
TE2-PHIN SIAU2-SOAT--HUN-IN E5 PI-KEK8
Teng Eng5-lim5
“Bok8-su u7 ti7-teh bo5?”  Goa2 thiaN-kiN3 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 siaN ti7 goa7-bin7 teh mng7, chek-si5 chau2 chhut-lai5.
“Goa2 chiu7-si7, u7 sim2-mih8 kui3-su7?”  Si7 chit8 e5 tiong-lian5 hu7-jin5, iok 30 he3, tiong-teng2 sin-chhai5, chheng7 chit8 su Su7-lim5 pou3 e5 iuN5-chong, chit8 tui3 sio2-sio2 e5 hong7-gan2, phiN7-liong5 chin koaiN5, bin7-mau7 chheng-siu3, na7 m7-si7 toa3-tioh8 iu-bun7 e5 bin7-chhiuN kap kui2-na7 jit8 bo5 soe-thau5 chin phong7-sang e5 thau5-mng5, khoaN3 khi2-lai5 it-teng7 si7 put-chi2 sui2 e5 cha-bou2-lang5.  Goa2 chhiaN2 i jip8-lai5 che7, jip8-khi3 lai7-bin7 phang5 chit8 poe te5 chhut-lai5 hou7 i, i khia7 khi2-lai5 chiap-siu7, tit8-tit8 kong2 to-sia7, i lim chit8 chhui3 liau2-au7 chiah kong2.
“Bok8-su, goa2 u7 chit8 hang7 iau3-kin2 e5 su7 beh lai5 pai3-thok li2, m7-chai li2 goan7-i3 pang-bang5 goa2 bo5?”
“Chi2-iau3 goa2 choe3 oe7 kau3, bo5 bun7-toe5.  ChhiaN2 kong2.”
“Goa2 si7 lan2 kau3-hoe7 Li2 Chai3-thoan5 (李再傳) TiuN2-lo2 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2, mia5 kio3 Goat8-khim5 (月琴), chu7 5 ni5 cheng5 li7-khui ka-teng5 liau2-au7, long2 m7-bat tng2-lai5, goa2 ia7 bo5 bin7 thang tng2-lai5.  M7-ku2 kau3 chia lai5 bo5 tng2-lai5 ia7 si7 boe7-eng7-tit, sou2-i2 ma5-hoan5 bok8-su li2 chhoa7 goa2 jip8-khi3, thoe3 goa2 tui3 si7-toa7-lang5 kiu5-cheng5.”  Kong2 kau3 chia, i e5 na5-au5 chhin-chhiuN7 that-leh, kong2 boe7 chhut-lai5 lah.
“Ah!  goa2 bat thiaN lang5 kong2, Li2 TiuN2-lo2u chit8 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ui7-tioh8 ai3-cheng5 thau-chau2 chhut-khi3, goan5-lai5 chiu7-si7 li2 ouh!”
I tam3-thau5, thau5-khak aN3-aN3, chhin-chhiuN7 chin kian3-siau3 e5 khoan2, bak8-sai2 tui3 nng7 lui2 bak8-chiu lau5 chhut-lai5, na2 kong2 na2 teh hau2.
 
“Goa2 bo5 phah-sng3 oe7 tu2-tioh8 chiah-nih8 put-heng7, che it-teng7 si7 Siong7-te3 tui3 goa2 e5 khian3-chek.  Goa2 i2-keng kiaN5 kau3 chin3 bo5 pou7, the3 bo5 lou7 e5 toe7-pou7, kin-a2-jit8 goa2 chiah chhim-chhim kam2-kak tioh8 goa2 e5 choe7-koa3 chin tang7, chi2-u7 tng2-lai5 kiu5 pe7-bu2 e5 sia3-bian2 na7-tiaN7.”
Hut-jian5 i khui siaN toa7-khau3, che chin-chiaN3 si7 ai-siong koh chham3-hoe2 e5 siaN-tiau7.
“Li2 m7-thang siong-sim, goa2 oe7 thoe3 li2 siat-hoat, chhiaN2 li2 khah leng2-cheng7, siong5-se3 kong2 li2 e5 keng-ke3 hou7 goa2 thiaN, ho2 bo5?”
I  eng7 chhiu2-kun chhit bak8-sai2, bo5 koh hau2, thau5-khak kiah8 khi2-lai5, khai-si2 i e5 hoe5-ek (回憶), i e5 chhui3 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 tai5 e5 hong3-iong3-ki, chit8 bou7 chit8 bou7 e5 ong2-su7 hong3-iong3 ti7 goa2 e5 bin7-cheng5.  Si5-kan to3-the3 kiaN5 kau3 10 ni5 cheng5.

1.  Soan2-tek8 tui3-siong7.
Ti7 goa2 e5 in3-siong7 tiong, chin chio2 u7 ka-teng5 chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 e5 ka-teng5 hiah-nih bi2-boan2 khoai3-lok8, che si7 jiat8-sim keng3-pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5, hou7 Chu2 chiok-hok e5 kiat-ko2.
Goa2 e5 ka-teng5 8 e5 lang5: pe7-bu2, hiaN-ko,hiaN-so2, goa2 kap chit8 e5 sio2-be7, nng7 e5 sio2-ti7.  Pa-pa ti7 pun2 S. K. chhoan khui pah-he3-tiam3, seng-li2 chin ho2.  A-hiaN ti7 S. M.Tiong-hak8 choe3 kau3-oan5; in nng7 e5 pe7-kiaN2 long2 ti7 kau3-hoe7 jim7-chit.  Pa-pa choe3 tiuN2-lo2, a-hiaN choe3 chip-su7.  Choan5-ke jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2, mui2 am3 choe3 ka-teng5 le2-pai3,le2-pai3-jit8 long2 koaiN-mng5, toa7-soe3 choan5-siu2 Seng3-jit8, hou7 tng-si5 e5 Lau5 Bok8-su chheng-chan3 goan2 si7 “Na2-a e5 ka-teng5,”  “bou5-hoan7 e5 Ki-tok-hoa3 ka-teng5.”  Goa2 ti7 kau3-hoe7 ia7 choe3 T. K. C. e5 su-ki3, Chu2-jit8-oh8 kau3-oan5, koh si7 Seng3-koa-tui7 lu2 ko-im e5 jin5-but8.  Hit si5 goa2 20 he3, tui3 Ko-tiong pit-giap8 i2-keng si7 teng5-teng5 giok8-lip8 (亭亭玉立) e5 siau3-lu2, tai7-khai3 si7 goa2 e5 siN-choe3 boe7 bai2, sou2-i2 lang5 long2 chheng goa2 si7 pun2-toe7 “C. K. chhoan chi hoa” (C. K.村之花).  Chit e5 nga2-ho7, goa2 ia7 chin moa2-i3, goa2 chiaN5-choe3 pun2-chhoan e5 sin-bun5 jin5-but8.
 
Ui7-tioh8 an2-ni, chit8 jit8 kau3-am3 mui5-lang5-po5 kiaN5-chhut kiaN5-jip8; sim2-mih8 chhoan-tiuN2 e5 kong-chu2 lah, Long5-hoe7 chhui-kong2 kou2-tiuN2 e5 siau3-ia5 lah ...... Goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 na7 thiaN-tioh8 m7-si7 hoe7-iu2, khah ho2 e5 tiau5-kiaN7 to it-khai3 sia7-choat8.  I e5 tiau5-kiaN7 si7 an2-ni:te7-it, jiat8-sim e5 Ki-tok-tou5; te7-ji7, sin-the2 kian7-khong; te7-saN, u7 cheng3-tong e5 su7-giap8 (si7 choh-sit, a2-si7 choe3 kang, hak8-lek8 si7 kok-hau7 a2-si7 chhou-tiong e5 long2 bo5 iau3-kin2).  Na7-si7 goa2 u7 chit8 e5 chin iau3-kin2 e5 tiau5-kiaN7, i long2 bo5 ka7 goa2 kho2-lu7 chai7-lai7, goa2 chu7-sin3, chhin-chhiuN7 goa2 u7 ho2 siN-choe3, ho2 khoan5-keng2, koh u7 ho2 e5 theng5-tou7 (學歷)beh chhe7 chit8 e5 chhi7-lai7 e5 tui3-siong7 it-teng7 bo5 bun7-toe5.  Kun7-lai5 e5 lang5 long2 siuN7 chin7 pan7-hoat beh li7-khui hiong-chhoan, thiau3-kau3 kou2-chiN2 goa7 e5 tou-chhi7, kiu5 an-lok8 e5 seng-oah8, ho5-hong3 chit8 e5 lu2-chu2, ui7-tioh8 i e5 chiong-sin tai7-su7 koh-khah tioh8 u7 chit8 khoan2 e5 oan2-kian3 chiah oe7 eng7-tit.  M7-ku2 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 i bo5 liah8 chit e5 choe3 iau3-kin2, na7-si7 goa2 kian-chhi5 chit e5 i3-kian3, sou2-i2 pun2 kau3-hoe7 u7 kui2-na7 e5 chheng-lian5 put-chi2 chin7-cheng5 teh ai3 goa2, tui goa2, tui3 in chiah e5 goa2 long2 bo5 khng3 ti7 goa2 e5 gan2-lai7, sou2-i2 long2 kong goa2 boe7 to2, ki5-tiong u7 chit8 e5 chheng-lian5 mia5-kio3 Keh Hong-seng7 (郭豐盛) hui-siong5 i3-ai3 goa2; iang Tan5 TiuN2-lo2 tui3 goa2 chit8 jit8 kong2 kau3 am3.  Lau5 Bok8-suia7 kui2-na7 pai2 khou2-khng3 goa2 tioh8 tap-eng3; koh-khah hai7 e5, si7 goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 hui-siong5 chan3-seng5 chit e5 hun-su7, siong5-siong5 eng7 ho2-chhui3 khng3 goa2 kong2.
“Goat8-khim5, goa2 chiah8 kau3 60 goa7 he3 lah, goa2 e5 bak8-chiu khoaN3 oe7 chhut chit e5 chheng-lian5 si7 chit8 e5 iu2-ui5 u7 chian5-tou5 e5 lang5, sui-jian5 khoan5-keng2 hian7-si5 put-pi2 lan2, iau2-ku2 chiong-lai5 it-teng7 ti7 su7-giap8-siong7 oe7 chin seng5-kong ......”
“Goa2 chim-chiok siuN7: U7! chit e5 chheng-lian5 si7 chin jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Chu2, koh u7 phau7-hu7 m7-ku2 i chi2-u7 kap goa2 tong5 teng2 hak8-lek8, keng-che3 pi2 goa2 khah bai2, ti7 chit e5 chhun-lok8 (村落) choe3 chit8 e5 poaN3-bun5 poaN3-bu2 e5 chhoan-kan3-su7 (村幹事) u7 sim2-mih8 chian5-tou5, i kiam2 u7 pan7-hoat choan2 khi3 chhi7-lai7?  Boe7! i eng2-oan2 si7 chit8 e5 chhau2-te7 sin-su7 na7-tiaN7.  Goa2 e5 tui3-siong7 kiam2 kan-ta chit khoan2 chu-keh?  SiuN7-lai5 siuN7-khi3, goa2 boe7 thang khut-ke7 goa2 e5 li2-siong2.
Chiu7-si7 an2-ni, goa2 kap lau7-pe7 e5 i3-kian3 boe7 hah8, ti3-kau3 bo5 cheng5 e5 kong-im, chit8 ni5, nng7 ni5, 5 ni5 ke3-khi3.
 
2.  Se3-cheng5 e5 chiN3.
Goa2 i2-keng 25 he3 lah.  Chit e5 lian5-leng5 be7 chhut-ke3 e5 lu2-chu2, ti7 hiong-chhoan lang5 khoaN3-lai5 si7 chin giam5-tiong7 e5 su7, goa2 e5 pe7-bu2 hui-siong5 e5 tioh8-kip, goa2 ia7 tam7-poh8 khoa3-lu7 khi2-lai5, m7-ku2 goa2 iau2 m7-goan7 kang3-ke7 goa2 e5 tiau5-kiaN7.
Su7-cheng5 chiu7 hoat-seng ti7 chit ni5 e5 Seng3-tan3-choeh; u7 chit8 ki ai3-cheng5 e5 chiN3(箭) sia7-jip8 goa2 e5 sim-lai7, chit ki chiN3 m7-na7 sia7 thang3 goa2 e5 sim, ia7 sia7 thau3 goa2 e5 choan5 seng-khu, choan5 seng-gai5.
Hit e5-hng kau3-hoe7 chin nau7-jiat8 teh ku2-heng5 Seng3-tan3 kheng3-chiok-hoe7, goan2 Thoan5-khe3 ian2 “Thian-lou7 lek8-theng5” (天路歷程) e5 seng3-kek8, goa2 pan7 choe3 thiN-sai3, tiau5-kang khi3 soe3 chit8 nia2 sin-niu5 saN.  Tng goa2 khia7 ti7 bu2-tai5 teng2 khoaN3 loh8-khi3 e5 si5, u7 nng7 lui2 chin toa7 koh chin kng chhin-chhiuN7 niau e5 bak8-chiu tui3 thau5-cheng5 pai5 e5 i2-a2 sia7-lai5, tit8-tit8 siong3 long2 bo5-khoeh.  Goa2 beh loh8 tai5-kha e5 si5, goa2 tiau5-kang oat8-thau5 ka7 i thau-khoaN3, chit si5 4 lui2 bak8-chiu chiaN5 chit8 tit8 soaN3, goa2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 hou7 lui5-tian7 chhiok-tioh8, kui e5 sim-thau5 phok8-phok8-chheng3.  Tng goa2 kiaN5 jip8 bok8-su koan2 e5 si5, i tui3 kun5-chiong3 e5 tiong-kan kiong5-kiong5 nng3 chhut-lai5 chhe7 goa2, ka7 goa2 kong2:
“Li2 Sio2-chia2, kin-a2-jit8 li2 m7-na7 gau5 ian2-kek8, koh tek8-piat8 sui2.”  Ki5-koai3! i thai2 oe7 bat goa2?  Goa2 tiN3 m7-chhap i, su7-sit8 lai7-sim hou7 i hit e5 phiau-phiat e5 gi5-piau2 khip-in2 lah, ti3-kau3 sim7-chi3 kui thau5-khak oat8 ke3-khi3 kap i kong2 oe7, ti7 am3-am3 e5 goa7-bin7, goa2 khoaN3-chhut i si7 chit8 e5 kun-koaN, chheng7 chit8 su cheng2-che5 e5 lek8-sek kun-hok8, nia2-a2 koa3 chit8 tiau5 kim-tiau5 (少尉), bin7-sek chin peh8 koh chin ian-tau5, tek8-piat8 hit nng7 lui2 oe7 hoat-sia7 e5 bak8-chiu si7 choe3 khip-in2 goa2 e5 sou2-chai7.  Goa2 ui7-tioh8 beh po2-chhi5 siau3-lu2 e5 hong-tou7, bo5 kap i kong2 choe7-choe7 oe7, chi2-u7 eng7 bi5-bi5-a2 chhio3 lai5 to-sia7 i e5 o-lo2.  Goa2 hoat-kian3 i m7-si7 chit8 e5 chhau2-te7 lang5.
I2-au7 tak8 le2-pai3 long2 u7 khoaN3-kiN3 i jip8-lail5e-pai3, na7-si7 tui3 hit nng7 lui2 bak8-chiu  
chai-iaN2 i bo5 sim teh choe3 le2-pai3, long2 si7 ng3 Seng3-koa-tui7 e5 sou2-chai7 teh kim-kim siong3 goa2, goa2 chai-iaN2 i si7 lai5 teh keng3-pai3 goa2.  Ki5-koai3!  goa2 ia7 siang7 khoan2, tui3 le2-pai3 sim put-chai7 ian (心不在焉), siong5-siong5 oat8 tuii3 hia khi3, goa2 i2-cheng5 m7-bat an-ni, goa2 chai-iaN2 goa2 i2-keng tui7-loh8 cheng5-bang7 lah.
Goa2 khai-si2 kap i iok-hoe7, chai-iaN2 i si7 Tai5-pak lang5 mia5 kio3 Chng Bun5-kiat8 (莊文傑), ku7-ni5 B. H. choan hau7 pit-giap8, ti7 pun2-toe7 hu3-kun7 e5 iaN5-pang5 hok8-iah8 u7-pi7 kun-koaN, koh nng7 ko3 geh8 chiu7 beh thoe3-ngou2, i ke goa2 chit8 he3, ka-teng5 ti7 Tai5-pak e5 Bin5-seng-lou7 khui pah-he3 tiam3, thiaN kong2 keng-che3 chin ho2, an3-sng3 the3-ngou2 tng2-khi3 beh ti7 chhi7-lai7 e5 chit8 sou2-chai7 choe3 seng-li2, in-ui7 i si7 thak8 siong kho (商科).   Kho2-sioh i m7-si7 Ki-tok-tou5, ia7 bo5 i3-su3 beh choe3 Ki-tok-tou5, i kong2 sin3-giong2 oe7 sok-pak8 lang5 e5 chu7-iu5, lang5 eng-kai seng-oah8 ti7 hian7-sit8 e5 hiong2-lok8-tiong ke3 jit8 chiu7-ho2, i e5 oe7 chin u7 li2.  Goa2 kam2-kak tu5-khi2 sin3-giong2 bo5 siang7 i2-goa7, sou2 u7 e5 tiau5-kiaN7 hui-siong5 sek-hap8 goa2 sou2 bong7-siong2 e5 tui3-siong7: Tai7 Choan pit-giap8; tiam3 ti7 tou-chhi7, i siN-choe3 eng-chun3;ka-teng5 keng-che3 ho2, chiah e5 ho2 tiau5-kiaN7 i2-keng kau3-giah8 hou7 goa2 m7-bian2 kho2-lu7 sin3-giong2 e5 bun7-toe5 lah, goa2 ia7 siong-sin3 pe7-bu2 oe7 ui7-tioh8 chiah e5 tiau5-kiaN7 goan7-i3 hong3-khi3 chong-kau3 e5 su7.  Goa2 ho2-taN2 tui3 in kong2 chit chan5 su7, bo5 phah-sng3 in kian-teng7 e5 sin3-giong2 boe7 thang tu5-khi3 hit e5 te7 it tiau5-kiaN7 “Tioh8 Ki-tok-tou5” e5 kou3-chip.  Ti7 chit8 am3 e5 ka-teng5 le2-pai3, goa2 e5 lau7-pe7 chhin-chhiuN7 chin khi3, i chek-pi7 goa2 kong2,
“Goat8-khim5, lan2 si7 Ki-tok-tou5, boh8-tit kap m7-sin3 e5 lang5 chhap-chap8, tioh8 chai bo5 tang5 sin3-giong2 e5 hun-in boe7 heng7-hok.  Siong7-te3 bo5 hoaN-hi2, li2 m7-thang khoaN3 che choe3 sio2-su7, m7-si7 Siong7-te3 chi2-i3 e5 kiat-hun, Chu2 boe7 chiok-hok, li2 tioh8 ho2-ho2 siuN7 khoaN3-mai7.”
“Ka2-su2 goa2 na7 oe7 thang in2-chhoa7 i lai5 kui Chu2 kiam2 ia7 m7-si7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3? Chin-choe7 ta-pou lang5 pun2-lai5 bo5 sin3 Chu2, na7-si7 kap Ki-tok-tou5 e5 lu2-chu2 kiat-hun au7,hou7 hu7-jin5-lang5 in2-chhoa7 lai5 chiaN5-choe3 jiat8-sim e5 lang5 e5 le7 boe7 chio2, chhin-chhiuN7 Ong5 TiuN2-lo2, chiu7-si7 tui3 tiuN2-lo2-niu5 in2-chhoa7 e5.” Goa2 bo5 soe3-ji7, chin siu7-khi3 tui3 lau7-pe7 hoan2-pok.   
 
“Li2 kong2 an2-ni ia7 u7 iaN2, chong2-si7 goa2 siong5-se3 ka7 thong2-ke3, chit khoan2 e5 le7 chin chio2, chap8 e5 bo5 chit8 e5, ki5-thaN e5 9 e5, m7-na7 boe7 in2-chhoa7 tiong7-hu, lian5 i ka-ki7 e5 sin3-giong2 to te3 i khi3, li2 kiam2 oe7 khak-sin3 li2 u7 hoat-tou7 thang in2-chhoa7 i.  Na7 bo5 chu7-sin3, choat8-tui3 m7-thang beh chhi3 Chu2 li2 e5 Siong7-te3.”  Lau7-pe7 ia7 bo5 hoaN-hi2, an2-ni in3 goa2.
Chit si5 goa2 chiah kam2-kak, u7 iaN2 chong-kau3 oe7 sok-pak8 lang5, tu2-tu2 chiau3 Bun5-kiat8 sou2 kong2 e5.  Goa2 m7-thiaN pe7-bu2 e5 oe7, koat-sim beh ai3 i.  Goan2 e5 kau-poe5 keng-ke3 nng7 geh8 jit8, ai3-cheng5 e5 un-tou7 tat8-kau3 hut-tiam2 (沸點)  Bun5-kiat8 si7 chit8 e5 lau2-sit8 e5 chheng-lian5, chi3-chio2 ti7 goa2 e5 in3-siong7-tiong si7 thang oa2-kho3 e5 lang5.
Chiu7 ti7 i beh the3-ngou2 e5 cheng5-jit8, goan2 iok-sok ti7 kok-hau7 e5 kau3-sek kiN3-bin7, hitam3 i tui3 goa2 kiu5 hun kong2, na7 tng2-khi3, liam5-piN beh lai5 chhoa7 goa2, goa2 ui7-tioh8 beh tui3 i piau2-si7 chin7-cheng5, goa2 bo5 ku7-choat8 i tui3 goa2 e5 bo5 le2, su7-sit8 goa2 ia7 hiong2-siu7 tioh8 be7 bat u7 e5 ai3-cheng5, goa2 sit-khi3 li2-ti3; sin3-giong2, pe7-bu2 long2 boe7 ki3-tit lah.  Goa2 chiong lu2-chu2 choe3 po2-poe3 beh hian3 hou7 tiong7-hu e5 le2-mih8 ti7 chit si5 oan5-choan5 hian3 hou7 Bun5-kiat8.
3.  Thau chau2 chhut-khi3 kiat-hun.
Chit8 geh8 jit8 au7, goa2 hoat-kak-tioh8 goa2 e5 seng-khu u7 koh-iuN7, chai-iaN2 pak-tou2-lai7 it-teng7 ke chit8 e5 oah8-mia7 chhut-lai5, hui-siong5 e5 tioh8-kip, sia2 phoe thong-ti Bun5-kiat8 chai-iaN2, i hoe5-phoe kong2, in-ui7 pe7-bu2 bo5 chan3-seng5 i chhoa7 chhau2-te7 lu2-chu2, sou2-i2 hou7 im7-chai beh an2-choaN2 phah-sng3.  Goa2 khoaN3 liau2 kiong5-kiong5 beh hun7 loh8-khi3, kau3 chia lai5 na7 bo5 koaN2-kin2 lai5-khi3 chhe7 i, un2-tang3 beh pang3-soah khi3, khoaN3 goa2 kau3 si5 beh an2-choaN2 kiN3 lang5, choe3 chit8 e5 bo5 chhut-ke3 e5 lau7-bu2 sia3-tioh8 chou2-kong, kau3-hoe7, ka-teng5 e5 bin7-the2-phe5, oe7 kian3-siau3 si2, pe7-bu2 iau2 m7-chai chit e5 su7, na7 chai, un2-tang3 kong3 si2 goa2.  Tioh8! goa2 ui5-it e5 lou7 si7 lai5-khi3 chhe7-i.
Hit am3, goa2 thau chun2-pi7 heng5-li2 kap tong-si5 sou2 chek-chu7 e5 chiN5 iok u7 5000 khou, chu7 an2-ni ti7 in khun3-khi3 e5 am3-mi5 thau chau2 chhut-lai5 che7 ia7 khoai3-chhia kau3 Tai5-pak.  Chhe7 tioh8 Bun5-kiat8, hou7 i tioh8 chit8 kiaN, o-lo2 goa2 e5 iong2-kam2, na7-si7 i e5 pe7-bu2 hoan2-tui3, kou-put-chiong goan2 leng7-goa7 se3-chhu3 ti7 pat8-ui7 ka-ki7 khia7.
 
Chit8 geh8 jit8 au7, goan2 cheng3-sek ti7 Hoat-iN7 ku2-heng5 kong-cheng3 kiat-hun, ti7 kiat-hun cheng3-su u7 kai3-chiong koh hip-siong7, hit jit8 chi2-u7 chhiaN2 kui2 e5 peng5-iu2, i2-goa7 long2 bo5 sim2-mih8 nau7-jiat8.
Kiat-hun au7 goan2 e5 seng-oah8 ke3-tit chin khoai3-lok8. 4.  Chham2 lah.
