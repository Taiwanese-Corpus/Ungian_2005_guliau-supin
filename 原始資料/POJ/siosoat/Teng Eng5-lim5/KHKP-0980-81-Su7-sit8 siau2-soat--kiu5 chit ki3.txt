
SU7-SIT8 SIAU2-SOAT--KIU5 CHIT KI3
Teng Eng5-lim5
1.  Ho2 siau-sit.
Goa2 hut-jian5 ti7 po3-choa2 khoaN3-tioh8 chit8 eh5o siau-sit, hui-siong5 e5 hoaN-hi2, chek-si5 chiong hit tiuN po3-choa2 eng7 chau2-e5 theh8-lai5 kau3 le2-pai3-tng5 beh po3 Lim5 Bok8-su.
“Bok8-su, bok8-su!  goa2 i2-keng u7 thau5-lou7 lah.”
“U7-iaN2!  An2-choaN2 tit-tioh8?”
“Li2 khoaN3 chit tiuN po3-choa2 sou2 khan e5,tu2-tu2 si7 goa2 chit kui2 ni5 lai5 tak8 jit8 teh bong7-siong2 e5 tiau5-kiaN7.  Lim5 Bok8-su chiong hit tiuN po3-choa2 theh8 khi2-lai5 khoaN3, Kip-teng giap8-bu7-oan5 khe2-su7 (急徵業務員啟事):“Cheng3-tai7 Hoa3-kang Iu2-han7 Kong-si (正大化工有限公司), ui7-tioh8 beh khok-tiong giap8-bu7, beh chiau-bou7 giap8-bu7-oan5 10 mia5, thai7-gu7 mui2 geh8 2000 khou i2-siong7, na7-si7 Chhou-tiong pit-giap8 theng5-tou7, iu2 i3 eng3-teng chia2 chhiaN2 kia3 li2-lek8-su hu3 han7-si5 iu5-phio3 kia3 lai5 Tai5-lam5 iu5-siuN 3856 ho7, giap8-bu7-chhu3 siu.  Na7 sim2-cha hap8-keh chek thong-ti bian7 hiap8.”
“Goa2 khoaN3 chit e5 khe2-su7 u7 tam7-poh8 giau5-gi5 e5 sou2-chai7, kun7-lai5 chhe7 chit-giap8 m7-si7 hiah-nih kan2-tan e5 su7, na2-li2 u7 chhou-tiong pit-giap8 oe7 thang than3 2000 khou e5 thai7-gu7?  Che kiam2-chhai2 si7 teh phian3 lang5 ia7 bo5 tek-khak.”  “Che si7 kong-khai khan ti7 po3-choa2, na2 oe7 phian3 lang5 e5 li2-iu5?”Goa2 in3.
“Bun5-seng (文生), goa2 siuN7 li2 m7-thang chit8-sim chit8-i3 teh him-bou7 beh khi3 chhi7-lai7 chiah8 thau5-lou7, li2 sui-jian5 bo5 chit-giap8, m7-ku2 li2 u7 kui2 hun chhan5-hng5, li2 na7 phah-piaN3 choan-sim choh-sit, goa2 khoaN3 bo5 khah-su khi3 chhi7-lai7 chiah tioh8, ho5-hong3 li2 e5 pe7-bu2 i2-keng ni5-lau7, ia7 bo5 ai3 li2 li7-khui in, tiam3 chhu3 ho2-ho2 hong7-thai7 si7-toa7 lang5, Siong7-te3 it-teng7 oe7 chiok-hok li2 e5 kang-chok.”
 
“Goa2 to si7 hou7 goan2 lau7-pe7 chiah e5 m7-chiaN5 chhan5-hng5 pak8 tiam3 chit e5 kou2-chiN2 lai7 chiah tioh8 chhiN-chham2; chit e5 si5-tai7 e5 chheng-lian5 na7 boe7-hiau2 siuN7 boh bun3 chhut-khi3 goa7-bin7 phah-piaN3, tiam3 chia ban7-si3 boe7 chhut-thau5-thiN.”
“Chong2-si7 tiam3 hiong-chhoan ma7 u7 hiong-chhoan e5 ho2, tiam3 tou-chhi7 ma7 u7 tou-chhi7 e5 bai2, bo5 it-teng7 tioh8 long2 ti7 chhi7-lai7, li2 khoaN3 lan2 kau3-hoe7 e5 Lu7 TiuN2-lo2, Li2 Chip-su7, in kiam2 m7-si7 tiam3 soaN-nih phah-piaN3, keng-eng5 chhan5-hng5, ma7 si7 hou7 Siong7-te3 toa7 chiok-hok.  Ia7 khi3 chhi7-lai7 e5 lang5 ma7u chin-choe7 tng2-lai5 teh kio3 khou2 lian5-thian.”
“Bo5-iaN2-ou5! Na7 u7 thau5-lou7 khah kong2 ma7 beh lai5-khi3 chhi7-lai7, li2 khoaN3 Gou5 TiuN2-lo2, Ng5 Chip-su7, in na7 bo5 khi3 chhi7-lai7, eng2-oan2 to si7 teh kiah8 ti5-thau5, koh khoaN3 A-sam ko, hok-chai5 hiaN, chit8-e7 khi3 chhi7-lai7, chheng7-chhah long2 sin-su7 khi2-lai5, i2-cheng5 e5 chhau2-toe7 song5 bi7 long2 bo5--khi3.”  “M7-ku2u chit8 hang7 li2 m7-chai-iaN2,” Lim5 Bok8-su kong2, “Chia e5 lang5 ti7 lan2 kau3-hoe7 si7 chin jiat8-sim hok8-sai7 Siong7-te3, le2-pai3 hu3 chu7-hoe7, choe3 kau3-hoe7 e5 kang hui-siong5 phah-piaN3, sui-jian5 seng-oah8 bo5 thang ho2, na7-si7 sim-leng5 chin khoai3-lok8 teh ke3-jit8, taN ti7 chhi7-lai7, chiau3 goa2 sou2 chai, u7 chin-choe7 lang5 e5 sin3-giong2 i2-keng chiam7-chiam7 leng2-tam7, sim7-chi3 u7 e5 i2-keng li7-khui Siong7-te3, bo5 teh choe3 le2-pai3 lah, in boe7 kham-tit siu7 hia e5 choe7-ok in2-iu2, chhin-chhiuN7 Bok8 TiuN2 lo2, Bou7-siong5 hiaN ...... long2 i2-keng sit-khi3 sin3-sim, piN3 choe3 Mou5-kui2 e5 lou5-chai5 lah.”
“Li2 kong2 an2-ni si7 su7-sit8, put-ko3 he si7 chai7 lang5 e5 sin3-sim u7 kian-teng7 bo5?  Goa2 si7 hou7 goa2 bo5 ki-hoe7 thang khi3 hia, na7 chit pai2 Chu2 pang-chan7 goa2 oe7 thang lai5-khi3 chiu7-chit, it-teng7 beh ti7 hu3-kun7 e5 kau3-hoe7 ka-pe7 jiat8-sim lai5 ui7 Chu2 choe3 kang.”
“Lang5 long2 oe7 loan2-jiok8, li2 tioh8 chai-iaN2 chhi7-lai7 e5 choe7-ok, khui2-khiat si7 lan2 siuN7 boe7 kau3 e5, tek8-piat8 hoan7-choe7 e5 iu2-hek8 sit8-chai7 hou7 lan2 kho2-phaN3.”  Lim5 Bok8-su kong2 kau3 chia koh than2-peh8 ka7 goa2 kong2, “Goa2 chai-iaN2 li2 si7 chit8 e5 jiat8-sim kho2-chhu2 e5 chheng-lian5, ti7 lan2 kau3-hoe7 tam-tng T.K.C. hoe7-tiuN2, Chu2-jit8-oh8  
kau3-oan5 iau3-kin2 echit5, chit keng jiok8-siau2 e5 kau3-hoe7 Siong7-te3 u7 toa7 khiam3-eng7 li2 ti7 chia choe3 kang, goa2 siuN7 ui7-tioh8 li2 e5 pe7-bu2, ui7-tioh8 lan2 kau3-hoe7 e5 ian5-kou3, choe3 ho2 hong3-khi3 kau3 tou-chhi7 e5 liam7-thau5, lou2-lek8 ti7 ka-ki7 e5 chit-tiuN5, goa2 siong-sin3 Siong7-te3 oe7 chiok-hok li2 seng5-kong e5 chit8 jit8.  Ho5-hong3 po3-choa2 teh po3 chit e5 siau-sit, m7-chai si7 chin a2-si7 chit8 tiuN5 e5 phian3-kiok8?  Ka2-su2 na7-si7 chin-e5, kan-ta chhi7-lai7 lang5 ma7 in2 kau3 bo5 hun7, kiam2 u7 hoat-tou7 thang tioh8 lan2?”
“Si7, sou2-i2 goa2 m7-thang sit-khi3 chit e5 ki-hoe7, tioh8 lai5-khi3 chhi3 khoaN3-mai7, Siong7-te3 na7 bo5 ai3 goa2 khi3, chit e5 su7 chiu7 boe7 seng5.”
Goa2 tui3 le2-pai3-tng5 chhut-lai5, na2 kiaN5 na2 siuN7, che si7 chhian-chaiN2 it-gu7 (千載一遇) e5 ki-hoe7, goa2 bo5-lun7 ju5-ho5 m7-thang hou7 bok8-su e5 oe7 kai2-pian3 goa2 e5 liam7-thau5, bok8-su long2 si7 teh kho2-lu7 i ka-ki7 e5 lip8-tiuN5, tong-jian5 bo5 ai3 goa2 khi3 pat8-ui7, u7 iaN2 goa2 na7 chau2, kau3-hoe7 i2-keng bo5 lang5 thang koh oah8-tang7.  Chong2-si7 i long2 bo5 kho2-lu7 goa2 e5 bun7-toe5, chit8 e5 bo5 thak8-chheh e5 lang5 siong7-chhiaN2 bo5 ai3 tiam3 chhan5-chng lah, ho5-hong3 goa2 chit e5 Ko-tiong pit-giap8-seng kiam2 m7-si7 koh-khah tioh8 than3 ki-hoe7 nng3 jip8 chhi7-lai7 mah?  Goa2 ti7 hia ma7 si7 oe7 thang ui7 Chu2 jiat8-sim lai5 li7-ek kau3-hoe7, kau3-hoe7 si7 Siong7-te3 e5, goa2 na7 chau2, Siong7-te3 chu7-jian5 oe7 heng-khi2 tu2-ho2 e5 lang5 theN3 khi2-lai5, m7-bian2 khoa3-lu7 chit e5 su7.
Goa2 tng2-lai5 chhu3, tui3 thoah-a2 (抽屜) khui khi2-lai5, theh8 hit tiuN i2-keng sia2 ho2 theng3-hau7 chin ku2 e5 li2-lek8-su (履歷書), sui5-si5 chau2 lai5 iu5-kiok8 boe2 chit8 tiuN han7-si5 e5 phe-long5 kap boe2 chit8 tiuN 2.50 e5 iu5-phio3, kap li2-lek8-su lok ti7 phoe-long5 lai7, chiu7 an2-ni toa3 tioh8 moa2-hoai5 e5 sin3-sim, lok loh8-khi3 iu5-tang5, goa2 long2 bo5 kong2 hou7 pe7-bu2 chai-iaN2, ho5-pit tioh8 hou7 in chai-iaN2.
II.  Eng3-teng.
Chit8 jit8, 2 jit8 ke3-khi3, goa2 chha-put-to boe7 chiah8, boe7 khun3-tit, put-si5 bak8-chiu khoeh-khoeh teh ki5-to2, koh-khah bo5 sim thang choe3 kang, ti7 mng5 khau2 kiaN5-lai5 kiaN5-khi3 teh liau5-bang7 lek8-sek e5 thiN-sai3 kong-lim5.  Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, te7 3 jit8 siong7-ngou2 hoe5 phoe lai5 lah, si7 chit8 tiuN beng5-sin3-phiN3, eng7 ian5-ji7 (鉛字) in3 ho2,  
kan-ta thau5-cheng5 hit kui2 ji7 “黃文生” eng7 mou5-pit sia2 e5 na7-tiaN7.
Pun2 kong-si chiap-tioh8 chheng-ban7 hong eng3-teng e5 sin3-kiaN7 tiong, ti7 cha7-hng e5 tang2-su7-hoe7 gi2-teng7 tai5-toan ui7 hap8-keh jip8-soan2, chhiaN2 ti7 3 jit8 lai7 chiong sin-hun7-cheng3, pit-giap8 cheng3-su, in3-chiong kap 300 khou e5 chiu7-chit  chhiu2-siok8-hui3 chhin-sin lai5 pun2 kong-si giap8-bu7-chhu3 teng-ki3 bian7-tam5.

Phoe-lai7 u7 tng3 toa7 e5 kong-si e5 koan-hong5 kap chu2-jim7 e5 in3-chiong chin chheng-chho2.  goa2 lian5-soa3 khoaN3 kui2-na7 pai2, na2 thak8 na2 chhio3, an2-ni u7 chu7-sin3 lah, hong2-kiam goa2 si7 ko-tiong pit-giap8 e5, it-teng7 oe7 tui3 goa2 chit e5 hak8-lek8 khah koan5 e5 iu-sian.  Tioh8! Si5-kan iau3-kin2, it-chhun3 kong-im it-chhun3 kim, ia7 tioh8 cheng-chhu2 si5-kan, na7 bo5 koaN2-kin2 lai5-khi3, oe7 hou7 chhi7-lai7 e5 lang5 chhiuN2-khi3.  Kau3 Tai5-lam5, goa2 theh8 chit tiuN beng5-sin3-phiN3 chhin-chhiuN7 teh tiau7-cha hou7-khau2,tit8-tit8 tui3 nau7-jiat8 e5 chhi7 tiong-sim lai5, bak8-chiu chit8-e7 tui3 hng7-hng7 khoaN3-khi3, ti7 Un7-ho5 piN ko2-jian5 u7 koa3 chit8 e5 chin toa7 te3 e5 chiau-pai5 sia2 tioh8 “政大化工股份有限公司業務處’’ e5 ji7 chin chheng-chho2.  Goa2 chin hoaN-hi2 eng7 chau2--e5 kau3 mng5-khau2, tai7-seng khia7 ti7 goa7-bin7 siong5-se3 khoaN3 jip8-khi3 hian2-jian5 si7 chit8 keng sin khai-giap8 e5 kong-si, lai7-bin7 u7 pai5 5, 6 te3 e5 pan7-kong-toh, i2-goa7 long2 bo5 sim2-mih8, tiong-ng hit te3 khah toa7 te3 e5 toh u7 khng3 chit8 ki tian7-oe7, u7 sia2 giap8-bu7 chu2-jim7 e5 ji7 pai5 ti7 toh-cheng5, che7 tihia7 hit e5 lang5 it-teng7 si7 chu2-jim7, piN-a2 e5 toh-a2 ia7 u7 che7 nng7 e5 su7-bu7-oan5 kap chit8 e5 lu2--e5,in e5 bak8-chiu put-si5 teh khoaN3 goa7-bin7.chiau3 khoaN3 bo5 sim2-mih8 kong-su7 thang pan7 e5 khoan2, goa2 khoaN3-liau2 chiu7 kiaN5 jip8-khi3, tu2-tu2 toh-kha u7 poah8 chit8 tiuN choa2 goa2 hut-jian5 siuN7-khi2 Lim5 Bok8-su ti7 kang2-to7 e5 si5 bat in2 chit8 e5 phi3-ju7 kong2, u7 chit8 e5 kong-si beh chiau-bou7 chit8 e5 su7-bu7-oan5,chong2-si7 eng3-teng-chia2 chin-choe7, chong2-keng-li2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang koat-teng7 sim2-mih8 lang5, chiu7 chiong chiah e5 lang5 tiau3 lai5 sui5-e5 khau2-chhi3, tiau5-kang ti7 in e5 bin7-thau5-cheng5 thau khng3 chit8 ki chiam (針)ti7 toe7-nih, an3-sng3 sim2-mih8 lang5 na7 oe7-hiau2 chiong  
hit-ki chiam khioh khi2-lai5, chiu7 beh chhai2-iong7 hit e5 lang5, in-ui7 na7 oe7-hiau2 ti7 sio2 su7 chin7-tiong, chu7-jian5 chiu7 oe7 ti7 toa7 e5 tiong-sim (忠心), sou2-i2 goa2 chai-iaN2 chit tiuN choa2 kiam2-chhai2 si7 teh chhi3 goa2, lai5 koat-teng7 goa2 e5 mia7-un7, goa2 sui5-si5 aN3-loh8 theh8 khi2-lai5, khng3 ti7 chu2-jim7 e5 toh-teng2, koh kiaN5 90 tou7 e5 kiok-kiong tui3 chu2-jim7 kong2,“ x x chu2-jim7, goa2 tiau5-kang tui3 hiong-chhoan lai5 beh eng3-teng e5, chhiaN2 ban7-su7 pai3-thok.”  Kong2 liau2 tui3 te7-a2 lai7 theh8 hiah e5 su-lui7 kap 300 khou khi2-lai5 kau hou7 i, i liah8 goa2 siong3 chit8-e7, chiah eng7 Kok-gu2 ka7 goa2 mng7 chin-choe7 tai7-chi3, goa2 ia7 eng7 Kok-gu2 ka7 i in3-tap, i na2 kong2 e5 si5 na2 khoaN3 hiah e5 su-lui7, khoaN3 liau2 heng5 goa2, chiong hit 300 khou siu jip8-khi3, koh tui3 thoah-a2 theh8 chit8 thong e5 thiam-po3-toaN ka7 goa2 kong2.
“Hu5-hap8 goan2 e5 tiau5-kiaN7 lai5 teng-ki3 e5 lang5 kau3 chit si5 u7 50 mia5 i2-siong7, na7-si7 goan2 chi2-u7 beh chhai2-iong7 10 e5 na7-tiaN7, sou2-i2 goan2 koat-teng7 beh hou7 hiong-chhoan e5 lang5 iu-sian, tong-jian5 li2 si7 hap8 ti7 iu-sian e5 chu-keh, chong2-si7 u7 chit8 e5 tiau5-kiaN7 li2 na7 tah-eng3, chiu7 sui5-si5 oe7 thang koat-teng7 beh chhai2-iong7 li2, m7-chai li2 goan7-i3 bo5?”“Tong-jian5 goan7-i3,” goa2 tit8-tit8 tam3-thau5.
“Pun2 kong-si kio3-choe3 股份有限公司’’ li2 chai-iaN2 chit e5 i3-su3 houN3?  Sou2-ui7 “股份”chiu7-si7 eng7 chin-choe7 lang5 hap8-kou2 lai5 keng-eng5 e5 kong-si, sou2-i2 hoan7-na7 beh choe3 pun2 kong-si e5 chit-oan5, chiau3 chiong-theng5 it-teng7 tioh8 ka-jip8 choe3 kou2-tong (股東)chiah oe7 eng7-tit.”
Goa2 thiaN-tioh8 kong2 tioh8 hun7 kou2-tong, sim-lai7 chai-iaN2 an2-ni bo5 hoat-tou7, chit8 kou2 kaN2 tioh8 kui2 chap8 ban7, a2-si7 kui2 ban7 khou leh, chiu7 mng7 i kong2, chit8 kou2 si7 joa7-choe7 chiN5?  Chit8 e5 lang5 tioh8 hun7 kui2 kou2? I in3 kong2, “Chit8 kou2 si7 3000 khou, mui2 chit8 e5 chit-oan5 chi3-chio2 ai3 ka-jip8 chit8 kou2 i2-siong7, chong2-si7 mui2 ni5 oe7 thang tit hong5-li7(紅利).” 
Kah si7 3000 khou bo5 bun7-toe5, kun7-lai5 thiaN lang5 kong2, na7 beh tit-tioh8 chit8 e5 chit-ui7, long2 tioh8 eng7 5000 khou i2-siong7, sng3 khi2-lai5 iau2 chin seng2, chiu7 ka7 i kong2,
“Chu2-jim7, m7-ku2 goa2 kin-a2-jit8 bo5 toa3 chiN5 lai5?”“Bo5 iau3-kin2, han7 li2 nng7 jit8 lai7  
theh8-lai5 ka-jip8, tui3 li2 ka-jip8 hit jit8 khi2, chiu7 cheng3-sek si7 goan2 kong-si e5 chit-oan5, sou2-i2 na2 kin2 na2 ho2.”
“An2-ni ho2, chhiaN2 iong5-un2 goa2 tng2-lai5 chun2-pi7, kiam2-chhai2 ti7 bin5-a2-jit8 goa2 chiu7 theh8-lai5.”“Ho2! TaN chhiaN2 li2 thiam chia e5 su-lui7, chit8 thong si7 店保, tioh8 ai3 pun2-chhi7 nng7 keng siong-tiam3 e5 po2-cheng3, koh chit8 thong si7 khe3-iok-su (契約書), i3-su3 si7 li2 na7 ka-jip8 kong-si i2-au7, tioh8 chun-siu2 chia e5 kui-chek, u7 sim2-mih8 su7 long2 bo5 iN7-gi7, kham3-in3 choe3 cheng3, chiong chiah e5 su-lui7 kap kou2-kim choe3-hoe2 theh8-lai5.”
“To-sia7, toa7-toa7 pai3-thok.”
Tui3 kong-si chhut-lai5. na2 kiaN5 na2 siuN7,nng7 e5 tiam3-po2 bo5 bun7-toe5, goa2 thak8 ko-tiong e5 si5, u7 kui2-na7 e5 tong5-oh8 si7 chhi7-lai7 lang5, lai5-khi3 ka7 in pai3-thok it-teng7 u7, na7-si7 3000 khou beh an2-choaN2?  Tng2-lai5 ka7 si7-toa7-lang5 theh8, m7-ku2 in bo5 hoaN-hi2 goa2 chhut-lai5, un2-tang3 m7 hou7 goa2.  Ah! tioh8 ! sun7-soa3 ka7 Tai5-lam5 e5 peng5-iu2 chioh, chit8 ui7 chioh 1000 khou, goa2 na7 chiu7-chit liau2, mui2 geh8-jit8 u7 2000 khou e5 siu-jip8, oe7 thang khiam7 1000 khou khi2-lai5, an2-ni lai5 heng5 in, 3 geh8-jit8 chiu7 oe7 heng5 liau2.
III.  Chiu7-chit.
Tit-tioh8 peng5-iu2 e5 pang-bang5, tiam3-po2 kap 3000 khou long2 chun2-pi7 ho2.  Te7 2 jit8 chheng-cha2, goa2 chin kin2-kip beh kiaN5 lai5 kong-si teng-ki3 chiu7-chit, in-ui7 kiaN-liau2 hou7 lang5 khah tai7-seng cheng-chhu2 khi3 chiu7 bo5 hau7.  Teh kiaN5 e5 si5, goa2 hut-jian5 tui3 sim-lai7 u7 chit8 e5 te7 6 kam2 gia5 khi2-lai5, EN5! che kiam2-chhai2 si7 Lim5 Bok8-su sou2 kong2 e5 phian3-kiok8 ou5?  U7 giau5-gi5 e5 sou2-chai7 ou5?  Teh pan7-kong hiah e5 lang5 khoaN3 khi2-lai5 bo5 seng5 kong-bu7-oan5 e5 khoan2?  Tau3-ti2 in kong-si e5 kang-chhiuN2 ti7 tah-loh8? ...............Hai3, li2 m7 teh sin5-keng, li2 bo5 khoaN3 cheng3-cheng3 tong-tong ti7 nau7-jiat8 tiong-sim khan3-pan2 (招牌) koa3 hiah toa7-te3, pan7-kong-thiaN hiah toa7 keng, koh u7 tian7-oe7, ji5-chhiaN2 tioh8 eng7 tiam3-po2, na2-li2 si7 teh phian3 lang5? Goa2 nng7 e5 sim teh kau-chian3, chong2-si7 put-ti put-kak kau3 ui7 lah.  Goa2 khia7 ti7 goa7-bin7 siuN7 beh koh siong5-se3 kho2-lu7 chit8 pai2, na7-si7 chit8 e7 khoaN3 tui3 lai7-bin7 jip8-khi3, u7 chiaN5 10 e5  
lang5 ui5 ti7 hia teh pan7 chhiu2-siok8 lah.  Goa2 bo5 kho2-lu7 chiu7 chong5 jip8-khi3, kai-chai3 chia e5 lang5 long2 ban7 goa2 chit8 pou7, teh pan7 thau5-pai2 e5 chhiu2-siok8 na7-tiaN7.  Tng goa2 chiong su-lui7 kap 3000 khou kau ho2-se3, chu2-jim7 chhio3-chhio3 tui3 goa2 kong2, “Tui3 taN khi2, li2 si7 pun2 kong-si e5 chit-oan5, bin5-a2-jit8 li2 khai-si2 lai5 siong7-pan.”
“Chu2-jim7, goa2 tioh8 ai3 tng2-khi3 chhu3-nih chun2-pi7 chit8-e7, m7-chai oe7 thang hou7 goa2 3 jit8 au7 chiah cheng3-sek lai5 siong7-pan ho2 bo5?”
“Kho2-i2.”  Chu2-jim7 tim3-thau5.
Goa2 phau7-tioh8 moa2-hoai5 e5 kam2-sia7 kap hoaN-hi2 tng2-lai5 chhu3-nih, ki3-jian5 u7 thau5-lou7, chiu7 than2-peh8 tui3 pe7-bu2 kong2, ia7 khi3 kiN3 Lim5 Bok8-su kong2, sui-jian5 in long2 bo5 koh chou2-tong3 goa2, chong2-si7 ia7 bo5 ka7 goa2 piau2-si7 kiong-hi2.  Te7 2 jit8 am3-si5, Lim5 Bok8-su tiau3-chip8 cheng3 hoe7-iu2 ka7 goa2 khui sang3-piat8-hoe7.  Bok8-su tai7-seng o-lo2 goa2 ke3-khi3 tik7au-hoe7 e5 jiat8-sim choe3 kang, jian5-au7 bian2-le7 goa2 ti7 chhi7-lai7 tioh8 kin2-sin7 goa2 e5 heng5-ui5, i kong2: 
Ti7 siaN5-chhi7 hoan7-choe7 e5 su7 pi2 chng-kham7-chai ke kui2 pah pe7, ti7 hia u7 pi2 chia tiam3 chin choe7 e5 phaiN2 lang5.  Hiah e5 choe7-toa7 ok-kek8, ti7 chheng-thian pek8-jit8 ha7 chhiuN2-kiap, kiong5-kan, sat-jin5 long2 tui3 hia chhut.
Hia u7 thun-biat8 song3-hiong, khi-cha3 tiong-hou7 e5 chip8-thoan5, kok chiong2 chhia-hoa5 iu2-hek8, hi-eng5, tou2-phok, khi-phian3, im5-loan7 chiu7-si7 i e5 tai7 mia5-su5.
Hia si7 chip8 se3-kan choe7-ok e5 chong2-hoe7, koan5-se3 kap kiong5-po7 e5 tiong-sim.
Sou2-i2 satyricus tui3 siaN5-chhi7 ai-to7 kong2, Goan2 beh tit-tioh8 sim2-mih8 ah?  Goan2 sou2 oe7 thang tit-tioh8 e5, put-kos3? kek8 toa7 e5 kian3-siau3 na7-tiaN7.
Bok8-koai3 I2-sai3-a ia7 thou2-khui3 kong2, “Hai7 ah, tiong-sin3 e5 siaN5 piN3-choe3  
chhiong-ki; chiong5-cheng5 chhiong-moa2 kong-peng5, kong-gi7 tiam3 ti7 hit lai7-bin7; hian7-kim si7 hiong-siu2 khia7-khi2, Li2 e5 gun5 piN3-choe3 che-tai2; li2 e5 chiu2 thau3-chui2.  Li2 e5 koaN-tiuN2 hoan7-hoat, kap chhat8 tau3-phoaN7; tak8 e5 ai3 siu7 pou3-lou7, siau3-siuN7 sia7-le2; in bo5 ui7-tioh8 bo5 pe7 e5 kiaN2 sin-oan, chiu2-koaN2 lang5 e5 an3-kiaN7 boe7 tit-tioh8 kau3 i e5 bin7-cheng5 (I2-sai3-a 1: 21---23).
Bok8-su kong2 soah, chin choe7 hoe7-iu2 khi2-lai5 thoe3 goa2 ki5-to2.  Hoe7 soah, tai7-ke saN ak-chhiu2, tui3 goa2 kong2 chiok-hok e5 oe7, choan5 kau3-hoe7 sang3 goa2 chit8 pun2 kim-kiN5 e5 Seng3-keng.
Te7 3 jit8, goa2 koaN7 chit8 toa7 phe5-siuN e5 heng5-li2 lai5 Tai5-lam5 peng5-iu2 e5 chhu3, goa2 e5 peng5-iu2 kap goa2 chhut-khi3 se3 chhu3, m7-ku2 hiong5 pong7-pong7 chhe7 long2 bo5, kou-put-chiong peng5-iu2 chhiaN2 goa2 chiam7-si5 tiam3 in hia.
IV.  Goan5-lai5 ju5-chhu2.
Siong7-pan hit cha2-khi2, goa2 chheng7 tioh8 han2-tit chheng7 e5 chit8 su se-chong, thau5-mng5, bin7, phe5-oe5 long2 cheng2-li2 chin phiat-se3, oan2-jian5 m7-si7 i2-cheng5 e5 Bun5-seng; tong-jian5 kin-a2-jit8 e5 Bun5-seng si7 Tai5-lam5 chhi7 Cheng3-tai7 kong-si e5 chit-oan5.  Ti7 hit keng chin toa7 keng e5 giap8-bu7-chhu3 u7 goa2 e5 chit8 ui7 kau-i2 ti7 hia, na2-li2 bo5 hoaN-hi2 e5 li2-iu5.  Goa2 8 tiam2 chiu7 kau3 kong-si, khoaN3 tioh8 iau2 u7 lang5 teh pan7 chhiu2-siok8, goa2 chit8 bin7 hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 goa2 tai7 seng lai5, sou2-i2 chiah u7 ng3-bang7, koh chit8 bin7 chin kam2-khai3 kin-a2-jit8 sit-giap8 e5 lang5 chiah nih choe7, sit8-chai7 tat8-tit tong5-cheng5.  Goa2 kiaN5 chin3-cheng5 lai5 tui3 chu2-jim7 kap tong5-su7 kong2 gau5-cha2, ki5-koai3, in na2 chhin-chhiuN7 m7-bat goa2 e5 khoan2, kim-kim ka7 goa2 siong3, tai7-khai3 si7 goa2 e5 chng-thaN7 hou7 in it-si5 jin7 boe7 chhut-lai5, bo5 joa7-ku2, chu2-jim7 kap nng7 e5 chit-oan5 kio3 goa2 jip8-khi3 lai7-bin7 ka7 goa2 kong2-oe7.
“Hoan-geng5 li2 choe3 pun2 kong-si e5 chit-oan5, li2 e5 pan7-kong-toh si7 pai5 ti7 choe3  
goa7-bin7 hit te3, chong2-si7 chiau3 goan2 e5 kui-chek beh choe3 su7-bu7-oan5 tioh8 ai3 tai7-seng sit8-sip8 choe3 goa7-bu7-oan5 liau2-au7 chiah oe7 eng7-tit.”
Goa2 iau2-be7 beng5-pek8 che si7 sim2-mih8 i3-su3e si5, u7 chit8 e5 siN-choe3 bo5 chin sui2 e5 sio2-chia2 tui3 lau5-teng2 loh8-lai5, chhiu2 koaN7 chit8 toa7 te7 e5 mih8-kiaN7 kap chit8 tui e5 gin2-na2 oan2-ku7(玩具), hi5, he5, la7-pa teng2.  Chu2-jim7 tui3 te7-a2 thau2 khui hou7 goa2 khoaN3, ka7 goa2 kong2.
“Che si7 pun2 kong-si chhut-phin2 e5 Tai7-kong (大光) soe2-i-hun2, 3 pau kap chit8 chih oan2-ku7 si7 boe7 10 khou.  Chit 10 khou-gun5 li2 tit-tioh8 nng7 khou e5 li7-sun5, sou2-i2 li2 chit8 jit8 na7 boe7 u7 500 khou, chiu7 oe7 thang than3 100 khou lah, chit8 geh8-jit8 chiu7 u7 3000 khou e5 siu-jip8, li2 na7 seng5-chek ho2, nng7 ko3-geh8 au7 chiu7 oe7 thang seng khi2-lai5 choe3 su7-bu7-oan5.  Ui7-tioh8 beh hou7 li2  oh8 chhui-siau e5 ki7-sut8, thau5 chit8 le2-pai3 goan2 phai3 chit ui7 Chhoa3 Sio2-chia2 kap li2 chhut-khi3 ........”
Goa2 i2-keng thiaN boe7 loh8-khi3, goa2 e5 thau5-khak gong5 khi2-lai5, kha long2 khia7 boe7 tiau5, khoaN3 chhu3-teng2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 liah8 to3-tiau3, sim-lai7 e5 he2 tit8-tit8 chheng3 khi2-lai5, goa2 siuN7 beh toa7-siaN jiong2 chhut-lai5, na7-si7 bian2-kiong2 khong3-che3 soe3-siaN tui3 inkong2.
“Na7-si7 an2-ni, goa2 bo5 hoat-tou7 thang choe3, goa2 boe7-hiau2 choe3 seng-li2, chhiaN2 li2 chiong hiah e5 chiN5 heng5 goa2.”“Li2 teh kong2 sim2-mih8?  ChhiaN2 li2 khoaN3 li2 ka-ki7 tng3-in3 chit tiuN khe3-iok-su kong2 sim2-mih8?”  I chiong hit tiuN khe3-iok-su theh8 lai5 eng7 chhiu2 chi2 hou7 goa2 khoaN3, te7 10 tiau5 an2-ni sia2.
Na7-si7 tiong-tou5 beh li7-khui pun2 kong-si, sou2 ka-jip8 e5 kou2-kim (股金) kap ki5-thaN e5 chiN5 it-chhe3 bo5 heng5.
Phian3-kiok8! phian3-kiok8!” goa2 jim2 boe7 tiau5 hoah chhut-lai5, hut-jian5 u7 chit8 e5 lang5 tui3 goa2 e5 heng-a2 sa leh, soe3-siaN kong2. “Li2 e5 bak8-chiu-mng5 sa khah kim leh,khoaN3 chia e5 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5, li2 na7 m7-goan7 choe3, tiam7-tiam7 tng2-khi3, na7 beh toa7-siaN hoah, chiu7 bian2 siuN7 beh u7 siN3-mia7 li7-khui chia.”
 
Goa2 tui3 hiah e5 lang5 chit8-e5 chit8-e5 ka7 in siong3, ko2-jian5 tak8 sian long2 chin toa7 sian,koh ke chin choe7 e5, chia e5 it-teng7 si7 phah-chhiu2, boe7 tho2-tit, goa2 e5 siu7-khi3 kap hun3-khai3 oaN7 choe3 pi7-pi7-chhoah, thau5-khak ka7 in tim3-thau5, i chiah ka7 goa2 pang3-khi3.  E2-kau2 teh-si2 kiaN2 bo5 oe7 kong2, phiN7-a2 bong-bong leh, tiam7-tiam7 chhut-lai5.  Goa2 khoaN3-tioh8 hiah choe7 lang5 ti7 hia teh tan2-hau7 beh pan7 chhiu2-siok8, boe7 bian2-tit thou2 chit8 e5 khui3 thoe3 in kho2-lin5 siong-sim, goa2 siong-sin3 in m7-bian2 nng7 jit8 ia7 siang7 khoan2 chiah8 tioh8 kiN.
Goa2 hui-siong5 m7-goan7 tng2-lai5 peng5-iu2 e5 chhu3, chiong chia e5 tai7-chi3 kong2 hou7 in thiaN, in long2 thoe3 goa2 hun3-khai3 kong2, it-teng7 tioh8 tho2 hit 3000 khou tng2-lai5 chiah oe7 eng7-tit.  Hit e7-pou goa2 e5 peng5-iu2 khi3 chio i e5 peng5-iu2, long2-chong2 u7 chiaN5 chap8 e5 lai5 kau3 chit e5 mou7-pai5 kong-si, iau2-be7 jip8-khi3 chiu7 thiaN-tioh8 tng3-i2 tng3-toh e5 siaN, goan5-lai5 i2-keng u7 chit8 kun5 e5 lang5 lai5 teh khi2 hoe lah.  Goan2 khia7 ti7 goa7-bin7 thiaN in teh jiong2 e5 siaN, “Lin2 si7 beh heng5 a2 m7 heng5? Na7 m7 heng5 beh kio3 keng2-chhat (警察) lai5, a2-si7 tui3 Hoat-iN7 ko3 lin2 cha3-khi.”
“Goan2 m7-kiaN, goan2 ki3-jian5 kaN2 cheng3-sek eng5-giap8, chu7-jian5 u7 pan7-hoat, goan2 m7-si7 bo5 chit-giap8 beh hou7 lin2, si7 lin2 m7 chiap-siu7 e5, beh an2-choaN2 ko3?”
“Si7 m7-si7 tioh8 eng7 bu2-lek8 chiah beh heng5 si7 bo5?”
“M7-si7, chong2-si7 lin2 e5 kun5-thau5-bu2 na7 u7 khah toa7 liap8, lai5 chhi3 khoaN3-mai7 ia7 si7 ho2.”
Thian ah!  U7-iaN2 bo5 hoat i ta-oa5, 10 goa7 e5 toa7 liu5-bang5 long2 lam2-heng khia7 ti7 si3-piN teh tan2, goan2 tai7-ke khoaN3-phoa3 beh liu-soan e5 si5, keh-piah siong-tiam3 e5 lau2-pan2 khia7 ti7 mng5-khau2 ka7 goan2 kong2,“Lin2 tioh8 khoaN3-phoa3 lah, SaN-chheng goa7 khou boe2 chit8 e5 ka3-si7, che si7 chit8 e5 ou-sek e5 chip8-thoan5, to3-chhu3 khan sin-bun5 pian2 lang5, lin2 bo5 hoat-tou7 in lah.”
“Chong2 bo5 ti7-an ki-koan, hoat-lut8 ia7 bohoat5-tou7 thang che3-chhai5 in.?”  Goa2 mng7.
 
“U7! m7-ku2 道高一尺, 魔高一丈, chit8 le2-pai3 cheng5 in soe3 chhu3 ti7 chia, na7 kau3 hou7 lang5 hoat-kak liau2-au7, in chiu7 sui5-si5 poaN-khi3 pat8-ui7, eng7 pat8 e5 mia5-chheng ke3-siok8 khan sin-bun5 khi-phian3 lang5, sou2-i2 put-si5 kap keng2-chhat teh liah8-ku chau2-pih, kun-pun2 bo5 hoat-tou7 ti7 in.”
Goa2 beng5-pek8 lah, goa2 chin-chiaN3 siu7 phian3, goa2 na2 siuN7 bak8-sai2 na2 lau5 chhut-lai5, sui-jian5 peng5-iu2 long2 an-ui3 goa2, peng3 goan7-i3 chiong in chioh goa2 e5 chiN5, hou7 goa2 khiam3 kau3 u7 chiah heng5, na7-si7 goa2 na2 u7 bin7 koh tng2-khi3 kiN3 pe7-bu2 kap kau3-hoe7 cheng3 hoe7-iu2?
Goa2 si5-piat8 peng5-iu2, chhiu2 koaN7 tang7-tang7 e5 heng5-li2, kha kiaN5 ia3-sian7 e5 kha-pou7, thau5-khak aN3-aN3 ng3 hiong-chhoan e5 kou3-hiong tng2-lai5, goa2 na2 chhin-chhiuN7 chit8 e5 hoan7 toa7 choe7 e5 kaN-hoan7 chhut-kaN, ia7 chhin-chhiuN7 hong3-tong7-chu2 toa3 tioh8 iu-ut, thong3-khou2, kian3-siau3 e5 sim chit8 iuN7.  Goa2 chhim-chhim kam2-kak Lim5 Bok8-su e5 oe7 si7 tioh8 e5; tui3 taN khi2, goa2 koat-chi3 beh chiau3 i e5 oe7 lai5 kiaN5.      
(Tai5-oan5 Kau3-hoe7 Kong-po3 te7 980-981 ho7,1966 ni5 4-5 geh8)
