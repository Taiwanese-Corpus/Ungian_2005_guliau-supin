		Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 The5-To E5 Phoe (56)
[1]	Siong7-te3 e5 lou5-pok8, Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 su3-tou5 Po2-lo5, ui7-tioh8 Siong7-te3 keng2-soan2 e5 peh-siN3 e5 sin3, kap beh hou7 in bat keng3-khian5 e5 chin-li2,  2). ti7 eng2-oan2 oah8 e5 ng3-bang7, chiu7-si7 bo5 peh8-chhat8 e5 Siong7-te3 ti7 eng2 se3-tai7 e5 tai7-seng sou2 eng3-un2 e5;  3). na7-si7 kau3 ki5 chiu7 hian2-beng5 I e5 to7-li2, chiu7-si7 chiau3 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Siong7-te3 e5 beng7-leng7 sou2 kau-thok goa2 thoan5 e5;  4). phoe kia3 hou7 The5-to, chiu7-si7 chiau3 saN-kap u7 e5 sin3 choe3 goa2 chin e5 kiaN2 e5: goan7 li2 tui3 Pe7 Siong7-te3 kap lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ki-tok Ia5-sou tit-tioh8 un-tian2 peng5-an.
	5). Goa2 lau5 li2 ti7 Kek-li2-ti2 e5 ian5-kou3, chiu7-si7 ai3 li2 chiong hiah e5 pan7 be7 beng5-pek8 e5 su7 long2 pan7 kau3 cheng2-choe5, koh chiau3 goa2 sou2 hoan-hu3 li2 e5, ti7 tak8 e5 siaN5 siat-lip8 tiuN2-lo2;  6). na7 u7 lang5 bo5 thang hiam5, choe3 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 tiong7-hu, kiaN2-ji5 u7 sin3, bo5 lang5 ko3 in hong3-tong7 put-hoat e5, chiah thang.  7). In-ui7 kam-tok tioh8 bo5 thang hiam5, chhin-chhiuN7 Siong7-te3 e5 koan2-ke, bo5 chu7-si7, bo5 khoai3 siu7-khi3, bo5 in-ui7 chiah8 chiu2 lai5 khi2 su7, bo5 ai3 phah lang5, bo5 tham put-gi7 e5 chiN5;  8). tioh8 ho2 khoan2-thai7 chhut-goa7 lang5, ai3 ho2, siu2-ki2, kong-gi7, seng3-kiat, chiat-che3;  9). kian-kou3 siu2 hit e5 chiau3 cheng3-kau3 chin-sit8 e5 to7-li2, hou7 i oe7 eng7 toan-chiaN3 e5 ka3-si7 lai5 khoan3-bian2 lang5, koh oe7 pian7-to2 hiah e5 pok-mng7 e5 lang5.
	10). In-ui7 u7 choe7-choe7 m7 sun7-hok8 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 kong2 phu5-phio3 e5 oe7, khi-phian3 lang5 e5; siok8 kat-le2 e5 lang5 koh khah si7 an2-ni.  11). Chit ho7 lang5 e5 chhui3 tioh8 ka7 i that-bat8; in ui7-tioh8 put-gi7 e5 chai5-li7 ka3-si7 sou2 m7 eng-kai ka3-si7 e5, lai5 pai7-hoai7 lang5 e5 choan5 ke.  12). In tiong-kan chit8 lang5, chiu7-si7 in hit chok8 e5 sian-ti, u7 kong2, Kek-li2-ti2 lang5 siong5-siong5 kong2 peh8-chhat8, choe3 phaiN2 siu3, tham-chiah8, koh pin5-toaN7.  13). Chit e5 kan-cheng3 si7 chin-sit8; sou2-i2 li2 tioh8 giam5-giam5 chek-pi7 in, hou7 in tui3 sin3 oe7 sun5-choan5;  14). bo5 ai3 thiaN Iu5-thai3 lang5 e5 bo5-iaN2 oe7, kap poe7-biu7 chin-li2 e5 lang5 e5 kai3-beng7.  15). Chai7 chheng-khi3 e5 lang5, ban7 mih8 long2 si7 chheng-khi3; chai7 hiah e5 u3-oe3 bo5 sin3 e5 lang5, bo5 chit8 hang7 mih8 chheng-khi3, lian5 in e5 sim-chi3 kap liong5-sim ia7 long2 u3-oe3.  16). In ka-ki7 seng5-jin7 choe3 bat Siong7-te3 e5, na7-si7 ti7 in e5 sou2 kiaN5 chiu7 m7 jin7 I; in si7 kho2-ouN3 e5, poe7-gek8 e5, ti7 it-chhe3 e5 ho2 su7 choe3 put-tiong3-iong7 e5.

[2]	Hiah e5 hap8 ti7 toan-chiaN3 e5 ka3-si7 e5, li2 tioh8 kong2 i,  2). chiu7-si7 lau7 lang5 m7 thang ai3 chiah8 chiu2, tioh8 toan-chong, siu2-ki2, koh ti7 sin3, ti7 jin5-ai3, ti7 thun-lun2, bo5 m7 sun5-choan5.  3). Lau7 hu7-jin5-lang5 ia7 tioh8 an2-ni, heng5-tong7 ku2-chi2 tioh8 hap8 ti7 keng3-khian5, boh8-tit hui2-pong3, ia7 boh8-tit choe3 chiu2 e5 lou5-chai5, tioh8 eng7 ho2 ka3-si7 lang5;  4). chiah thang kau3-tok siau3-lian5 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 thiaN3 in e5 tiong7-hu, thiaN3 in e5 kiaN2-ji5,  5). siu2-ki2, cheng-kiat, liau7-li2 ke su7, sian7-liong5, sun7-hok8 ka-ki7 e5 tiong7-hu, bian2-tit Siong7-te3 e5 to7-li2 siu7-tioh8 pong3-tok8.  6). Siau3-lian5 e5 ta-pou-lang5, li2 ia7 tioh8 an2-ni khng3 in siu2-ki2.  7). Li2 hoan7 su7 tioh8 ka-ki7 choe3 ho2 sou2 kiaN5 e5 bou5-iuN7;  8). ti7 ka3-si7 tioh8 toan-chong bo5 sia5, gian5-gu2 toan-chiaN3, bo5 thang hiam5, hou7 tui3-tek8 ka-ki7 kian3-siau3, bo5 te3 thang kong2 lan2 e5 phaiN2.  9). Khng3 lou5-pok8 tioh8 sun7-hok8 in e5 chu2-lang5, hoan7 su7 hou7 in hoaN-hi2, bo5 tu2-tak,  10). bo5 thau-theh8, tak8 hang7 tioh8 hian2-beng5 tiong-sin3, hou7 in hoan7 su7 eng5-kng lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Siong7-te3 e5 kau3.  11). In-ui7 Siong7-te3 kiu3 cheng3 lang5 e5 un i2-keng hian2-beng5, 12). keng2-kai3 lan2 tioh8 khi3-sak put-khian5 kap chit se3-kan e5 su-iok8, eng7 siu2-ki2, kong-gi7, keng3-khian5 lai5 ke3-jit8 ti7 chit se3-tai7;  13). him-bou7 hit e5 toa7 hok-khi3 e5 ng3-bang7, kap lan2 chi3 toa7 e5 Siong7-te3 kap Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok eng5-kng e5 hian2-hian7.  14). I ui7-tioh8 lan2 hian3 ka-ki7, beh siok8 lan2 thoat-li7 it-chhe3 e5 put-hoat; koh ui7-tioh8 pun2-sin soe2-chheng lan2 choe3 I ka-ki7 e5 peh-siN3, chiu7-si7 jiat8-sim ti7 chiah e5 ho2 sou2 kiaN5 e5.
	15). Chiah e5 su7 li2 tioh8 kong2, tioh8 khou2-khng3, eng7 long2-chong2 e5 koan5 lai5 chek-pi7 lang5.  M7 thang hou7 lang5 biau2-si7 li2.

[3]	Tioh8 hou7 lang5 ki3-tit eng-kai hang5-hok8 pan7 cheng3-su7 e5, chiang2-koan5 e5, lai5 sun7-than3 i, siong5-siong5 pian7 lai5 kiaN5 tak8 hang7 e5 ho2 su7,  2). bo5 hui2-pong3, bo5 saN-chiN, tioh8 un-ho5, tui3 cheng3 lang5 hian2-beng5 tak8 iuN7 e5 un-jiu5.  3). In-ui7 lan2 cheng5 ia7 si7 bu5-ti, poe7-gek8, siu7 be5-hek8, hang5-hok8 ti7 pah-poaN e5 su-iok8 kap khoai3-lok8, oah8 ti7 ok-tok8 oan3-tou3 e5 tiong-kan, si7 kho2-ouN3 e5, ia7 tai7-ke saN oan3-hun7.  4). Na7-si7 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Siong7-te3 e5 jin5-chu5 kap I e5 thiaN3 lang5 hian2-beng5 e5 si5,  5). I chiu7 kiu3 lan2, m7 si7 tui3 ti7 lan2 sou2 kiaN5 e5 gi7, si7 chiau3 I e5 lin5-bin2, tui3 teng5-thau5-siN e5 soe2 kap Seng3 Sin5 e5 oaN7-sin,  6). Seng3 Sin5 chiu7-si7 I tui3 lan2 e5 Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok sou2 toa7-toa7 kang3-loh8 ti7 lan2 e5;  7). hou7 lan2 tui3 I e5 un lai5 chheng-choe3-gi7 e5, thang chiau3 eng2-oah8 e5 ng3-bang7 lai7 chiaN5-choe3 hou7-su5.  8). Chit e5 oe7 si7 chin-sit8; koh lun7 chiah e5 su7 goa2 ai3 li2 ngi7-ngi7 kan-cheng3 i, hou7 sin3 Siong7-te3 e5 lang5 ti3-i3 choe3 chiah e5 ho2 e5 su7-giap8.  Chiah-e5 long2 si7 ho2, u7 li7-ek lang5 e5.  9). Chong2-si7 li2 tioh8 siam2-pi7 gong7-gong7 e5 pian7-pok, se3-he7 e5 tam5-lun7, saN-chiN, kap ui7-tioh8 lut8-hoat e5 cheng-chian3; in-ui7 chiah-e5 si7 bo5 li7-ek, koh khang-khang.  10). Kiat-tong2 e5 lang5, i2-keng keng2-kai3 i chit8 pai2 koh chit8 pai2, chiu7 tioh8 ku3-chai7 i;  11). in-ui7 chai chit ho7 lang5 si7 poe7-biu7, ka-ki7 gi2 i e5 choe7, iu5-goan5 teh hoan7.
	12). Gao2 chhe A-the5-ma2 a2-si7 Chhui-ki-kou2 khi3 chhe7 li2 e5 si5, li2 tioh8 ti3-i3 kau3 Ni-ko-pho-lip8 lai5 chhe7 goa2; in-ui7 goa2 i2-keng tiaN7-tioh8 ti7 hia ke3 tang-thiN.  13). Hoat-lut8-su7 Se-lap8 kap A-pho-lo5, li2 tioh8 ti3-i3 ka7 i song3-heng5, hou7 in bo5 khiam3-kheh.  14). Koh lan2 chiah e5 lang5 tioh8 hak8-sip8 choe3 ho2 e5 su7-giap8, lai5 pi7-pan7 hiah e5 tek-khak tioh8 e5 sou2-hui3, bian2-tit bo5 kiat-sit8.
	15). Kap goa2 ti7-teh e5 lang5 long2 chheng2-an li2. Hoan7-na7 ti7 sin3 lai5 thiaN3 goan2 e5 lang5 li2 tioh8 chheng2-an in.
	16). Goan7 un-tian2 kap lin2 cheng3 lang5 saN-kap ti7-teh.
