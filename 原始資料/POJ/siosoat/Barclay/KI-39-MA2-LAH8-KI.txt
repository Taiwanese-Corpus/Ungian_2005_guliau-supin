Lin2 koh kong2, KhoaN3 ah, kau3 hiah lo-so hui3-khi3; iah8 ng-phiN7 iam3-chian7 i; che si7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2--e5.  Lin2 eng7 sou2 chhiuN2--e5, pai2-kha--e5, phoa3-siuN3--e5, theh8-lai5 hian3-choe3 le2-mih8, Goa2 kiam2 beh tui3 lin2 e5 chhiu2 chiap-lap8 mah? Che si7 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2--e5.  14). Ke2-ho2 e5 lang5, ti7 kui-kun5-tiong u7 kang e5 cheng-siN, i he7-goan7 chiu7 eng7 u7 ha5-chhu5--e5, hian3 hou7 Chu2, chit-ho7 e5 lang5 thang chiu3-chou2, in-ui7 Goa2 si7 toa7 kun-ong5, Goa2 e5 mia5 ti7 liat8-kok-tiong si7 thang kiaN-ui3--e5; che si7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2--e5.

[2]	 Lin2 chiah e5 che3-si ah, chit e5 beng7-leng7 si7 hou7 lin2--e5.  Ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2, Lin2 na7 m7-thiaN, m7 he7 ti7 sim--nih, lai5 kui eng5-kng Goa2 e5 mia5, Goa2 chiu7 hou7 chiu3-chou2 kau3 ti7 lin2, hou7 lin2 e5 hok-khi3 pian3-choe3 chiu3-chou2; Goa2 i2-keng chiu3-chou2, in-ui7 lin2 bo5 chiong beng7-leng7 he7 ti7 sim--nih.  3). KhoaN3 ah, Goa2 beh ui7-tioh8 lin2 lai5 phah-phaiN2 cheng2-chi2, koh eng7 pun3 kou5 lin2 e5 bin7, chiu7-si7 lin2 e5 seng-le2 e5 pun3; lin2 beh kap i piN5-piN5 tu5--khi3.  4). Lin2 chiu7 chai Goa2 eng7 chit e5 beng7-leng7 hou7 lin2, thang choe3 Goa2 kap Li7-bi7 sou2 lip8 e5 iok; che si7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2--e5.  5). Goa2 kap i sou2 lip8--e5, si7 oah8-mia7 kap peng5-an e5 iok; Goa2 chiong che hou7 i, hou7 i kiaN-ui3; i chiu7 kiaN-ui3 Goa2, keng3-ui3 Goa2 e5 mia5.  6). Chin-li2 e5 lut8-hoat ti7 i e5 chhui3-lai7; ti7 i e5 chhui3-tun5 chhe7 bo5 put-gi7; i eng7 peng5-an cheng3-tit8 kap Goa2 tang5-kiaN5, hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 oat8-tng2 li7-khui phaiN2.  7). Chong2-si7 lin2 u7 li7-khui hit tiau5 lou7, hou7 choe7-choe7 lang5 ti7 lut8-hoat tioh8-tak; lin2 pai7-hoai7 Li7-bi7 e5 iok; che si7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2--e5.  9). Sou2-i2 Goa2 hou7 lin2 siu7 cheng3-lang5 khoaN3-khin, khoaN3-choe3 ha7-chian7; in-ui7 lin2 bo5 siu2 Goa2 e5 to7-lou7, ti7 lut8-hoat eng7 phian-su khoan2-thai7 lang5.
	10). Lan2 kiam2-m7-si7 tang5 chit8 e5 pe7 mah? Kiam2-m7-si7 chit8 ui7 Siong7-te3 chhong3-cho7 lan2 mah? Lan2 tak8-lang5 siaN2-su7 eng7 khui2-cha3 khoan2-thai7 hiaN-ti7, pai7-hoai7 liat8-chou2 e5 iok ah?  11). Iu5-tai7 kiaN5 khui2-cha3 koh ti7 I2-sek-liat8 kap Ia5-lou7-sat-leng2 e5 tiong-kan kiaN5 kho2-ouN3 e5 su7; in-ui7 Iu5-tai7 siat-tok8 Ia5-ho5-hoa sou2 thiaN3 e5 seng3-sou2; chhoa7 hok8-sai7 goa7-pang e5 siong7-te3 e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 choe3 bou2.  12). KiaN5 chit-ho7 su7 e5 lang5, goan7 Ia5-ho5-hoa tui3 Nga2-kok e5 pou3-piN5-tiong, tu5-khi3 i sou2-u7 e5 lang5, hek8-si7 chhiN2--e5, hek8-si7 in3--e5, i2-kip8 hian3 le2-mih8 hou7 ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa--e5.  13). Lin2 koh an2-ni choe3, hou7 bak8-sai2 thi5-khau3 kap thou2-khui3 pian3-moa2 Ia5-ho5-hoa e5 toaN5, ti3-kau3 I bo5 koh khoaN3-kou3 le2-mih8, ia7 bo5 hoaN-hi2 tui3 lin2 e5 chhiu2 chiap-lap8.  14). Lin2 iau2-ku2 kong2, Che si7 siaN2-su7? I e5 sin5 sui-jian5 u7 chhun, kiam2-m7-si7 cho7 chit8-e5 mah? SiaN2-su7 chi2-u7 chit8-e5? chiu7-si7 beh kiu5 seng3 e5 hou7-e3.  Sou2-i2 tioh8 kin2-siu2 lin2 e5 sim, tak8-lang5 m7-thang eng7 khui2-cha3 khoan2-thai7 siau3-lian5 sou2 chhoa7 e5 bou2.  16). 	17). Lin2 eng7 oe7 thiau-lan5 Ia5-ho5-hoa.  Lin2 iau2-ku2 kong2, Goan2 eng7 sim2-mih8 thiau-lan5 I ah? In-ui7 lin2 kong2, Hoan7-na7 choe3-phaiN2--e5, Ia5-ho5-hoa khoaN3 i choe3 ho2, koh hoaN-hi2 i; a2-si7 kong-gi7 e5 Siong7-te3 ti7 toh-loh8 ah?

[3]	Ban7-kun e5 Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2, KhoaN3 ah, Goa2 chhe Goa2 e5 su3-chia2, i beh ti7 Goa2 e5 thau5-cheng5 pi7-pan7 lou7; lin2 sou2 chhe7 e5 Chu2, beh hut-jian5 kau3 I e5 tian7, beng5-iok e5 su3-chia2, chiu7-si7 lin2 sou2 him-bou7--e5, khoaN3 ah, i lai5 lah.  2). Tok8-tok8 I lai5 e5 jit8, chi7-chui7 tng-oe7-khi2; I hian2-hian7 e5 si5, chi7-chui7 khia7-oe7-tiau5? In-ui7 I chhin-chhiuN7 thoan3-lian7--e5 e5 he2, koh chhin-chhiuN7 phio3-pou3--e5 e5 kiN.  3). 	7). 	13). Ia5-ho5-hoa kong2, Lin2 eng7 ngi7 e5 oe7 kek Goa2.  Lin2 iau2-ku2 kong2, Goan2 eng7 sim2-mih8 oe7 kek Li2 ah?  14).
