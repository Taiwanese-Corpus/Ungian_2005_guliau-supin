Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Thiap-Sat-Lo5-Ni5-Ka-Lang5 E5 Phe
(Hou7 su)

Te7 1 ChiuN

	1) Goa2 Po2-lo5 kap Se-lah i2-kip8 The5-mou5-thai3 sia2 phe hou7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 siok8 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lang5:
	2) Goan7 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok, siuN2-su3 lin2 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.

Ki-tok lai5-lim5 e5 si5 e5 sim2-phoaN3:
	3) HiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 tioh8 ui7 lin2 si5-siong5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2! Che si7 tong-jian5 e5, in-ui7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim toa7-toa7 seng5-tiong2, iu7-koh lin2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 hou7-siong e5 ai3-sim ia7 chiam7-chiam7 teh cheng7-ka.  4) Sou2-i2 goan2 chai7 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7 tiong, ui7 lin2 khoa-iau7, in-ui7 lin2 chai7 it-chhe3 e5 pek-hai7 hoan7-lan7-tiong, e7-tang3 po2-chhi5 jim2-nai7 kap sin3-sim.
	5) In-ui7 lin2 an2-ne teh siu7-khou2, sou2-i2 lin2 e7-kham-tit  chin3-jip8 Siong7-chu2 e5 Kok, che chiu7-si7 teh cheng3-beng5 Siong7-chu2 kong-gi7 e5 sim2-phoaN3.  6) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 e7 chiau3 kong-gi7, eng7 hoan7-lan7 lai5 po3-eng3 hiah-e5 hou7 lin2 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5;  7) ia7 e7 hou7 lin2 chiah-e5 siu7 hoan7-lan7 e5 lang5, kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 tit-tioh8 an-leng5.  8) Chiu7-si7 Chu2 Ia5-sou chai7 hoe2-iam7-tiong, chhoa7 hiah-e5 tai7-leng5 e5 thian-sai3, tui3 thiN-teng2 hian2-hian7 e5 si5, lai5 chhu2-hoat8 hiah-e5 m7-bat Siong7-chu2 e5, kap bo5 hok8-chiong5 koan-he7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 Hok-im e5 lang5.  9) In e7 siu7 eng2-oan2 biat8-bong5 e5 chhu2-hoat8, li7-khui Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, kap I eng5-iau7 e5 tai7-leng5.  10) Chai7 Chu2 lai5-lim5 hit chit8 jit8, Chu2 e7 tui3 I it-chhe3 e5 chu2-bin5 siu7 eng5-iau7, tui3 it-chhe3 e5 sin3-tou5 siu7 chheng-chan3. In-ui7 goan2 ka7 lin2 cho3-cheng3 e5 oe7, lin2 i2-keng u7 sin3 a3.
	11) Goan2 chiu7-si7 in-ui7 an2-ne siong5-siong5 ui7 lin2 ki5-to2. Goan2 ki5-kiu5 lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 hou7 lin2 e7-tang3 hu5-hap8 I e5 seng3-tiau3 lai5 seng-oah8. Goan7 I eng7 I e5 tai7-leng5 lai5 seng5-chiu7 lin2 cho3 ho2-su7 it-chhe3 e5 goan7-bong7, oan5-seng5 lin2 eng7 sin3-sim sou2 cho3 e5 kang-chok.  12) An2-ne lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou, chiu7 e7 tui3 lin2 siu7-tioh8 eng5-iau7, lin2 oa2-kho3 lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 in-hui7, ia7 e7 tui3 I tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7.

Te7 2 ChiuN

Put-hoat e5 lang5:
	1) HiaN-ti7 a3, koan-he7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lai5-lim5, i2-kip8 lan2 siu7 tiau3-chip8 kap I cho3-hoe2 e5 tai7-chi3: goan2 khun2-kiu5 lin2,  2) bo5-lun7 si7 sim2-mih lang5 i7-gian5 e5, sim2-mih lang5 kong2 e5, a2-si7 kong2 goan2 e5 phe u7 an2-ne sia2, kong2 Chu2 e5 Jit8-chi2 kau3 a3, lin2 e5 sim m7-thang sui5-pian7 tong7-iau5 a2-si7 tioh8-kiaN.  3) Bo5-lun7 lang5 eng7 sim2-mih hoat-tou7, lin2 long2 m7-thang hou7 in phian3-khi3. In-ui7 hit e5 jit8-chi2 iau2-boe7 kau3 i2-cheng5, e7 tai7-seng hoat-seng poe7-kau3, Put-hoat e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 chu3-tiaN7 biat8-bong5 e5, e7 chhut-hian7.  4) I e7 hoan2-tui3 it-chhe3 lang5 sou2 keng3-pai3 e5, i2-kip8 lang5 siuN7-cho3 si7 thian-siong7 e5 su7-but8 e5. I e7 chu7-chheng pi2 chiah-e5 khah ui2-tai7, sim7-chi3 jip8-khi3 che7 toa3 Siong7-chu2 e5 seng3-tian7 lai7, soan-gian5 i ka-ti7 si7 Siong7-chu2!
	5) Goa2 iau2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, sou2 ka7 lin2 kong2-koe3 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, lin2 kam2 be7-ki3-tit a3?  6) Hian7-chai7 lin2 ia7 chai-iaN2 si7 sim2-mih teh ap-che3 chit e5 Put-hoat e5 Lang5. hou7 i kau3 sek-tong3 e5 si5-ki5 chiah thang chhut-hian7.  7) In-ui7 hit e5 (NNp)ut-hoat e5 (NNi)m-bou5 i2-keng teh khai-si2 a3, tan7-si7 tioh8 tan2-hau7 kau3 ka7 i ap-che3-e5 thiat-li7 liau2-au7, chiah e7 hoat-seng.  8) Hit e5 si5, hit e5 Put-hoat e5 Lang5 chiu7 e7 chhut-hian7, Chu2 Ia5-sou lai5-lim5 e5 si5, e7 eng7 I e5 chhui3 pun5-khui3 ka7 i sat-hai7, eng7 I eng5-iau7 e5 hian2-hian7 ka7 i hui2-biat8.  9) Hit e5 Put-hoat e5 Lang5 e7 kho3 Sat-tan3 e5 koan5-se3, lai5 hian2 kok chiong2 e5 sin5-chek, ke2 e5 chian5-tiau7 kap ki5-su7,  10) iu7-koh eng7 kok chiong2 sia5-ok e5 khui2(NNkui2)-ke3 lai5 khi-phian3 hiah-e5 teh-boeh biat8-bong5 e5 lang5. In e7 biat8-bong5 si7 in-ui7 in bo5 ai3 chin-li2, bo5 chiap-siu7 chin-li2 lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  11) In-ui7 an2-ne, Siong7-chu2 chiu7 hou7 in sim-lai7 u7 chit8 chiong2 e7 hoat-seng chho3-gou7 e5 leng5-lek8, lai5 hou7 in siong-sin3 hi-ke2 e5.  12) Che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh hou7 it-chhe3 bo5 sin3 chin-li2, tian-to3 ai3 put-gi7 e5 lang5, long2 e7 siu7 teng7-choe7.

Lin2 si7 siu7 keng2-soan2 lai5 tit-kiu3 e5:
	13) Chu2 sou2 thiong2-ai3 e5 hiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 tioh8 siong5-siong5 ui7 lin2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2. In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 tui3 khi2-thau5 chiu7 keng2-soan2 lin2, boeh hou7 lin2 oa2-kho3 Seng3 Leng5 lai5 cho3 Siong7-chu2 ka-ti7 e5 chu2-bin5, kap hou7 lin2 siong-sin3 chin-li2 lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3.  14) Siong7-chu2 thong-koe3 goan2 sou2 thoan5 hou7 lin2 e5 Hok-im, tiau3 lin2 lai5 tit-tioh8 kiu3; thang hou7 lin2 hun-hiong2 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 eng5-iau7.  15) Sou2-i2 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 tioh8 khia7 hou7 chai7, tioh8 kian-siu2 goan2 sou2 ka3-si7 lin2 e5 chin-li2, bo5-lun7 si7 goan2 eng7 chhui3 thoan5 e5, hek8-si7 goan2 eng7 phe sia2 e5.
	16) Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok pun2-sin, i2-kip8 thiong2-ai3 lan2, eng7 I e5 in-hui7 siuN2-su3 lan2 eng2-oan2 e5 an-ui3 kap bi2-sian7 e5 hi-bong7 Hit-ui7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7,  17) lai5 an-ui3 lin2 e5 sim, peng3(NNpeng7)-chhiaN2 hou7 lin2 kian-kiong5 lai5 sit8-heng5, lai5 soan-kang2 it-chhe3 e5 ho2-su7.

Te7 3 ChiuN

Ui7 goan2 ki5-to2:
	1) TaN hiaN-ti7 a3, chhiaN2 lin2 ui7 goan2 ki5-to2, tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 chai7-lai5 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan an2-ne, goan7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 sin3-sok thoan5-iong5, tit-tioh8 eng5-iau7,  2) ia7 ki5-kiu5 Siong7-chu2 chin2-kiu3 goan2 thoat-li7 phaiN2-sim sia5-ok e5 lang5; in-ui7 m7-si7 tak8 lang5 long2 u7 siong-sin3 to7-li2.
	3) Tan7-si7 Chu2 si7 choat8-tui3 kho2-kho3-e5; I e7 kian-kou3 lin2, po2-hou7 lin2 thoat-li7 hit e5 sia5-ok-e5.  4) Chu2 hou7 goan2 sin3-jim7 lin2; goan2 khak-sin3 lin2 hian7-chai7 u7 teh chun-heng5, au7-lai5 ia7 e7 ke3-siok8 chun-heng5 goan2 sou2 ka7 lin2 hoan-hu3 e5.
	5) Goan7 Chu2 in2-chhoa7 lin2 e5 sim lai5 keng3-ai3 Siong7-chu2, lai5 hau7-hoat Ki-tok e5 nai7-sim.

Kang-chok e5 pun2-hun7:
	6) HiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 chiau3 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 chi2-i3 beng7-leng7 lin2: na7 u7 hiaN-ti7 seng-oah8 pin7-toaN7, bo5 chiau3 goan2 ka7 in ka3-si7 an2-ne cho3, lin2 tioh8 li7-khui chit khoan2 e5 hiaN-ti7.  7) Lin2 ka-ti7 ia7 chai-iaN2 lin2 tioh8 an3-choaN2 lai5 hau7-hoat goan2 sou2 cho3 e5; in-ui7 goan2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, long2 m7-bat pin7-toaN7;  8) goan2 ui7-tioh8 bo5 ai3 hou7 lin2 jim7-ho5 lang5 hu7-tam, bo5 peh8 chiah8 lang5 e5 png7, tian-to3 me5-jit8 lo5-khou2 kang-chok.  9) Goan2 an2-ne cho3, peng3(NNpeng7) m7-si7 in-ui7 goan2 bo5 koan5 lai5 beng7-leng7 lin2 i5-chhi5 goan2 e5 seng-oah8; goan2 ui7-tioh8 boeh i2-sin chok-chek lai5 hou7 lin2 hau7-hoat goan2.  10) Goan2 iau2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, u7 ka7 lin2 kong2-koe3: ¡§Bo5 ai3 kang-chok e5 lang5, m7-thang hou7 i chiah8.¡¨
	11) Goan2 an2-ne kong2, si7 in-ui7 goan2 u7 thiaN-kiN3 lin2 tiong-kan u7 lang5 seng-oah8 chin pin7-toaN7, chhap pat8-lang5 e5 eng5-su7, i2-goa7 bo5 cho3 poaN3 hang7 kang-chok.  12) Goan2 chiau3 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 chi2-i3, beng7-leng7 chiah-e5 lang5, keng2-ko3 in tioh8 an-hun7 kang-chok, ka-ti7 tho2-than3 lai5 seng-oah8.
	13) Tan7-si7 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 cho3 ho2-su7 m7-thang siuN7 sian7.  14) Na7 u7 lang5 bo5 boeh sun7-chiong5 goan2 sia2 hou7 lin2 chit tiuN phe e5 oe7, tioh8 ka7 i ki3 khi2-lai5, m7-thang kap i lai5-ong2, an2-ne i chiu7 e7 kian3-siau3.  15) Tan7-si7 m7-thang ka7 i khoaN3-cho3 tek8-jin5; tioh8 cho3 hiaN-ti7 ka7 i keng2-ko3.

Choe3-au7 e5 chheng2-an:
	16) Goan7 siuN2-su3 lan2 peng5-an e5 Chu2, si5-siong5 eng7 chiong2-chiong2 e5 hong-hoat, chhin-sin siuN2-su3 lin2 peng5-an. Goan7 Chu2 kap lin2 tai7-ke tong5-chai7.
	17) Goa2 chhin-chhiu2 sia2 chit ku3: ¡§Goa2 Po2-lo5 ka7 lin2 chheng2-an.¡¨ Goa2 tak8 tiuN phe long2 si7 an2-ne chhiam-mia5; goa2 long2 an2-ne sia2.
	18) Goan7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 in-hui7 kap lin2 tai7-ke tong5-chai7.
