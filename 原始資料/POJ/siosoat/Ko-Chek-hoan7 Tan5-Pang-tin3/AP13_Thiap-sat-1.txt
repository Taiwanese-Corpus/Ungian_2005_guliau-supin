Po2-Lo5 Kia3 Hou7 Thiap-Sat-Lo5-Ni5-Ka-Lang5 E5 Phe
(Chian5 su)

Te7 1 ChiuN

1) Goa2 Po2-lo5 kap Se-lah i2-kip8 The5-mou5-thai3 sia2 phe hou7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka kau3-hoe7 e5 lang5, chiu7-si7 siok8 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 i2-kip8 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok e5 lang5.
	Goan7 lin2 tit-tioh8 in-hui7 kap peng5-an.

Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka-lang5 e5 seng-oah8 kap sin3-sim:
	2) Goan2 siong5-siong5 ui7 lin2 tai7-ke kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2, ki5-to2 e5 si5 put-toan7 ui7 lin2 ki5-kiu5.  3) Chai7 lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 Thian-hu7 e5 bin7-cheng5, si5-siong5 e7-ki3-tit lin2 ju5-ho5 eng7 heng5-tong7 lai5 teh piau2-hian7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim, ju5-ho5 sin-khou2 kang-chok lai5 teh piau2-si7 lin2 e5 ai3-sim, ju5-ho5 eng7 nai7-sim lai5 teh giong2-bong7 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok.  4) HiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 chai-iaN2 lin2 si7 Siong7-chu2 sou2 thiong2-ai3 e5, ia7 si7 I sou2 keng2-soan2 e5.  5) In-ui7 goan2 thoan5 Hok-im khi3-kau3 lin2 hia, m7-si7 chi2-u7 eng7 gian5-gi2, si7 oa2-kho3 koan5-leng5 kap Seng3 Leng5, i2-kip8 tui3 Hok-im u7 oan5-choan5 e5 khak-sin3. Lin2 ia7 chai-iaN2 goan2 kap lin2 cho3-hoe2 e5 si5, goan2 an3-choaN2 seng-oah8; he long2 si7 ui7 lin2 e5 li7-ek.  6) Iu7-koh lin2 sui-jian5 chai7 chin-che7 hoan7-lan7-tiong, lin2 hoaN-hoaN-hi2-hi2 nia2-siu7 Seng3 Leng5 sou2 khe2-si7 e5 chin-to7. Che chiu7-si7 hau7-hoat goan2, ia7 si7 hau7-hoat Chu2.  7) An2-ne lin2 lai5 cho3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 kap A-kai-a it-chhe3 sin3-tou5 e5 bou5-hoan7.  8) In-ui7 Chu2 e5 to7-li2 tui3 lin2 put-tan7 thoan5 chhut khi3-kau3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 kap A-kai-a, lin2 tui3 Siong7-chu2 e5 sin3-giong2 ia7 i2-keng thoan5 khi3-kau3 tak8 sou2-chai7 a7. Sou2-i2 goan2 bo5 su-iau3 koh kong2 sim2-mih.  9) In-ui7 hiah-e5 lang5 long2 ka-ti7 thoan5 kong2 goan2 khi3 hong2-bun7 lin2 e5 si5, lin2 an3-choaN2 hoan-geng5 goan2, lin2 an3-choaN2 pang3-sak ngou2-siong7 lai5 kui-hiong3 Siong7-chu2, lai5 hong7-su7 chin-sit8 eng2-seng e5 Siong7-chu2,  10) ia7 lai5 theng3-hau7 I e5 KiaN2 tui3 thiN-teng2 chai3-lim5 --- chit e5 KiaN2 chiu7-si7 Siong7-chu2 hou7 I tui3 si2-lang5-tiong koh-oah8 khi2-lai5 e5 Ia5-sou. I chai7 Boat8-jit8 e7-tang3 kiu3 lan2 pi7-bian2 Siong7-chu2 e5 toa7 siu7-khi3.

Te7 2 ChiuN

Po2-lo5 chai7 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka e5 kang-chok:
	1) HiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 ka-ti7 ia7 chai-iaN2 goan2 teng2 pai2 khi3 hong2-bun7 lin2, m7-si7 bo5 hau7-ko2 e5.  2) Lin2 ia7 chai-iaN2 goan2 khi3 lin2 Thiap-sat-lo5-ni5-ka i2-cheng5, chai7 Hui-lip8-pi2 siu7-chin7 gek8-thai7 kap bu2-jiok8. Goan2 sui-jian5 siu7-tioh8 chin toa7 e5 khou2-chhou2, lan2 e5 Siong7-chu2 iau2-si7 siuN2-su3 goan2 u7 iong2-khi3 lai5 ka7 lin2 thoan5 I e5 Hok-im.  3) In-ui7 goan2 tui3 lin2 e5 khoan3-bian2 m7-si7 kin-ki3 chho3-gou7, a2-si7 put-sun5 e5 tong7-ki, ia7 m7-si7 eng7 cha3-khi boeh lai5 hai7 lang5.  4) Sit8-che3-siong7, Siong7-chu2 sin3-jim7 goan2, chiong Hok-im thok-tiong7 goan2, sou2-i2 goan2 chiu7 chiau3 Siong7-chu2 ai3 goan2 kong2 e5 lai5 kong2. Goan2 bo5 siuN7-boeh be2 lang5 e5 hoan-sim, chiu7-si7 boeh hou7 kho2-giam7 goan2 e5 sim e5 Siong7-chu2 moa2-i3.  5) In-ui7 lin2 chai-iaN2 goan2 m7-bat tui3 lin2 eng7 phou5-thaN2 e5 oe7, ia7 m7-bat eng7 hoa-gian5 khau2-gi2 lai5 am-kham3 tham-sim --- che Siong7-chu2 e7-tang3 ka7 goan2 cho3-cheng3!  6) Goan2 bo5-lun7 tui3 lin2 a2-si7 tui3 pat8-lang5, long2 m7-bat tui3 lang5 kiu5 beng5-i7,  7) sui-jian5 goan2 cho3 Ki-tok e5 Su3-tou5, pun2-lai5 e7-tang3 iau-kiu5 lin2 chun-tiong7 goan2. Tan7-si7 goan2 khi3 ti7 lin2 hia e5 si5, tui3-thai7 lin2 chin un-jiu5, chhin-chhiuN7 lau7-bo2 teh iong2-iok8 kiaN2-ji5.  8) Goan2 an2-ne thiaN3-sioh lin2, put-tan7 chiong Siong7-chu2 e5 Hok-im thoan5 hou7 lin2, lian5 goan2 ka-ti7 e5 seN3-mia7 ia7 hoaN-hi2 ui7 lin2 hi-seng. In-ui7 lin2 si7 goan2 sou2 chhin-ai3 e5.  9) HiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 it-teng7 e7-ki3-tit goan2 loa7-nih lo5-khou2 kang-chok! Goan2 thoan5 tui3 Siong7-chu2 lai5 e5 Hok-im e5 si5, me5-jit8 thoa-boa5 lai5 i5-chhi5 seng-oah8, si7 ui7-tioh8 bo5 ai3 thoa-lui7 tioh8 lin2 jim7-ho5 lang5.
	10) Goan2 tui3 lin2 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 sou2 cho3 e5 heng5-ui5 si7 loa7-nih seng3-kiat, kong-gi7, bo5 koe3-sit; u7 lin2 thang cho3-cheng3, ia7 u7 Siong7-chu2 thang cho3-cheng3.  11) Lin2 ia7 chai-iaN2 goan2 khoan2-thai7 lin2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5, tu2-tu2 chhin-chhiuN7 lau7-pe7 teh khoan2-thai7 i ka-ti7 e5 kiaN2-ji5.  12) Goan2 khoan3-bian2 lin2, an-ui3 lin2, ia7 kou2-le7 lin2 cho3-su7 tioh8-ai3 hou7 Siong7-chu2 moa2-i3. I si7 tiau3 lin2 lai5 hun-hiong2 I e5 Ong5-kok kap I e5 eng5-iau7 e5.
	13) Sou2-i2 goan2 ia7 ui7-tioh8 an2-ne put-toan7 teh kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2. In-ui7 lin2 thiaN-kiN3 goan2 sou2 thoan5 e5 Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-to7, m7-si7 siuN7-cho3 si7 lang5 e5 oe7, chiu7-si7 siuN7-cho3 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-to7 lai5 chiap-siu7, su7-sit8 si7 an2-ne. Siong7-chu2 e5 chin-to7 chai7 lin2 sin3 Chu2 e5 lang5 e5 tiong-kan teh chok-iong7.  14) HiaN-ti7 a3, chai7 Iu5-thai3 te7-hng Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hoe7 sou2 hoat-seng e5, lin2 chiah-e5 siok8 Ki-tok Ia5-sou e5 lang5, ia7 u7 tu2-tioh8 kang7-khoan2 e5 tai7-chi3. Chhin-chhiuN7 in siu7-tioh8 Iu5-thai3-lang5 e5 pek-hai7, lin2 ia7 siu7-tioh8 tong5-pau e5 pek-hai7.  15) Hiah-e5 Iu5-thai3-lang5 thai5-si2 Chu2 Ia5-sou kap Siong7-chu2 e5 tai7-gian5-jin5, ia7 pek-hai7 goan2; in hou7 Siong7-chu2 loa7-nih bo5 hoaN-hi2! In chiong cheng3-lang5 khoaN3-cho3 tek8-jin5!  16) In siong7-chhiaN2 chou2-tong3 goan2 thoan5 to7-li2 hou7 bi7-sin3-chia2, thang hou7 in tit-tioh8 kiu3. In si5-siong5 hoan7-choe7 i2-keng kau3 kek8-tou7 a7. TaN Siong7-chu2 be7-tang3 koh iong5-jim2 a3, soah tui3 in hoat toa7 siu7-khi3.

Po2-lo5 hi-bong7 koh khi3 hong2-bun7 in:
	17) HiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 chiam7-si5 kap lin2 li7-khui. Sui-jian5 seng-khu li7-khui, sim-koaN bo5 li7-khui --- goan2 loa7-nih pek-chhiat siuN7-boeh koh kap lin2 sio kiN3-bin7.  18) Goan2 ai3-boeh koh to3-tng2-khi3 lin2 hia. Goa2 Po2-lo5, kui2-na7 pai2 siuN7-boeh koh to3-tng2-khi3 lin2 hia, tan7-si7 Sat-tan3 ka7 goan2 chou2-tong3.  19) Lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou chai3-lim5 e5 si5, chai7 I e5 bin7-cheng5, kiu3-keng3 sim2-mih lang5 si7 goan2 e5 hi-bong7, goan2 e5 hi2-lok8, hou7 goan2 choe3 e7-tang3 khoa-iau7 e5? Bo5 pat8-lang5, chi2-u7 si7 lin2!  20) Lin2 sit8-chai7 si7 goan2 e5 kong-eng5, goan2 e5 hi2-lok8.

Te7 3 ChiuN

1) In-ui7 goan2 be7-tang3 koh thun-lun2 a3, sou2-i2 koat-i3 kan-taN goan2 boeh ke3-siok8 toa3 Nga2-tian2.  2) Goan2 chhe-phai3 goan2 e5 hiaN-ti7 The5-mou5-thai3 khi3 lin2 hia. I chiu7-si7 kap goan2 cho3-hoe2 ui7 Siong7-chu2 teh soan-pou3 koan-he7 Ki-tok Hok-im e5. Che si7 ui7-tioh8 boeh ka7 lin2 bian2-le7, hou7 lin2 e5 sin3-sim kian-kiong5.  3) An2-ne lin2 chiah bo5 lang5 e7 tu2-tioh8 khun3-khou2 lai5 tong7-iau5. Lin2 ka-ti7 ia7 chai-iaN2 chit khoan2 e5 khun3-khou2 si7 Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 sou2 an-pai5 e5.  4) In-ui7 goan2 iau2 ti7 lin2 hia e5 si5, goan2 u7 su7-sian ka7 lin2 kong2 lan2 e7 siu7 pek-hai7; au7-lai5 chiaN5-sit8 an2-ne hoat-seng, che lin2 ia7 sou2 chai-iaN2.  5) In-ui7 an2-ne goa2 chiah tioh8 chhe-phai3 The5-mou5-thai3 khi3 lin2 hia. Goa2 be7-tang3 koh thun-lun2 a3, sou2-i2 goa2 chhe-phai3 i khi3 khoaN3 lin2 e5 sin3-sim kiu3-keng3 ju5-ho5. Goan2 kiaN-liau2 Mou5-kui2 ka7 lin2 iu2-hek8 e5 kiat-ko2, hou7 goan2 e5 kang-chok long2 ou-iu2 khi3.
	6) Hian7-chai7 The5-mou5-thai3 tui3 lin2 hia tng2-lai5 a3, chiong lin2 e5 sin3-sim kap ai3-sim e5 ho2 siau-sit po3 hou7 goan2 chai-iaN2 a3. I ia7 kong2 lin2 siong5-siong5 teh siau3-liam7 goan2, lin2 teh su-liam7 boeh kiN3 goan2, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 teh su-liam7 boeh kiN3 lin2.  7) Sou2-i2 hiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 chai7 it-chhe3 e5 khun3-khou2 hoan7-lan7-tiong, thiaN-tioh8 lin2 e5 sin3-sim tit-tioh8 an-ui3;  8) in-ui7 lin2 na7 kap Chu2 kiat-hap8 khia7 chai7-chai7, goan2 chiu7 sit8-chai7 kam2-kak teh oah8 u7 i3-gi7.  9) TaN goan2 e7-tang3 ui7 lin2 lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2. Goan2 chai7 lan2 Siong7-chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, in-ui7 lin2, goan2 e7-tang3 tit-tioh8 chit e5 hoaN-hi2, sit8-chai7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-chu2!  10) Goan2 me5-jit8 seng5-sim ki5-to2 hou7 goan2 e7-tang3 chhin-sin kap lin2 kiN3-bin7, lai5 pou2-chhiong lin2 sin3-giong2-siong7 sou2 su-iau3 e5.
	11) Goan7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7, i2-kip8 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou ka7 goan2 khui lou7, hou7 goan2 thang khi3 lin2 hia.  12) Goan7 Chu2 hou7 lin2 hou7-siong e5 ai3-sim i2-kip8 tui3 cheng3-lang5 e5 ai3-sim it-tit7 cheng7-tiong2, chhin-chhiuN7 goan2 tui3 lin2 e5 ai3-sim hiah-nih8 toa7.  13) An2-ne I chiu7 e7 kian-kou3 lin2 e5 sim, hou7 lin2 chai7 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou kap I e5 chiong3 seng3-tou5 lai5 e5 si5, chiu7 e7 chai7 Siong7-chu2 lan2 e5 Thian-hu7 e5 bin7-cheng5 chiaN5-cho3 oan5-choan5 seng3-kiat e5.

Te7 4 ChiuN

Hou7 Siong7-chu2 moa2-i3 e5 seng-oah8:
	1) HiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 iau2-koh u7 oe7 boeh ka7 lin2 kong2. Lin2 i2-keng tui3 goan2 hak8-sip8 tioh8-ai3 an3-choaN2 seng-oah8 lai5 hou7 Siong7-chu2 moa2-i3. Tong-jian5 lin2 chai7-lai5 u7 chiau3 an2-ne teh seng-oah8. TaN goan2 peN5-peN5 cho3 Ki-tok-tou5 e5 sin-hun7 khun2-kiu5 lin2, khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 ka-poe7 chin7-lek8.  2) In-ui7 lin2 ia7 chai-iaN2 goan2 eng7 Chu2 Ia5-sou e5 koan5-ui ka7 lin2 chi2-si7 sim2-mih tai7-chi3.  3) Siong7-chu2 e5 chi2-i3 chiu7-si7 boeh hou7 lin2 chiaN5-cho3 seng3-kiat e5, hou7 lin2 oan5-choan5 kai3-choat8 sia5-im5.  4) Lin2 mui2 chit8 e5 lang5 ai3 chai-iaN2 tioh8 an3-choaN2 eng7 seng3-kiat kap cheng3-tong e5 hong-sek lai5 chhoa7-bou2.  5) M7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 m7-bat Siong7-chu2 e5 bi7-sin3-chia2 eng7 sia5-im5 e5 iok8-bong7 lai5 chhoa7.  6) Koan-he7 chit hang7 tai7-chi3, lang5 m7-thang cho3 phaiN2 lai5 chhim-hoan7 i e5 hiaN-ti7. Goan2 i2-cheng5 bat ka7 lin2 kong2-koe3, chhiat-sit8 ka7 lin2 keng2-ko3 kong2 Chu2 e7 chhu2-hoat8 cho3 chit khoan2 phaiN2 tai7-chi3 e5 lang5.  7) Siong7-chu2 m7-si7 tiau3 lan2 chai7 sia5-im5-tiong seng-oah8, si7 boeh hou7 lan2 chai7 seng3-kiat-tiong seng-oah8.  8) Sou2-i2 ki7-choat8 chit e5 kau3-hun3 e5 lang5, m7-si7 ki7-choat8 lang5, si7 ki7-choat8 siuN2-su3 lin2 Seng3 Leng5 e5 Siong7-chu2.
	9) Koan-he7 lin2 sin3-tou5 hou7-siong e5 iu2-ai3, goa2 bo5 su-iau3 koh sia2 sim2-mih. In-ui7 lin2 ka-ti7 u7 siu7-tioh8 Siong7-chu2 e5 kau3-hun3 tioh8 an3-choaN2 lai5 siong-chhin siong-ai3.  10) Lin2 ia7 u7 tui3 Ma2-ki5-tun3 choan5 Seng2 e5 hiaN-ti7 an2-ne cho3, tan7-si7 hiaN-ti7 a3, goa2 khou2-khng3 lin2 tioh8 ka-poe7 chin7-lek8.  11) Ia7 tioh8 khat-bong7 koe3 an-cheng7 e5 seng-oah8, kou3 lin2 ka-ti7 e5 tai7-chi3, i5-chhi5 ka-ti7 e5 seng-oah8, tioh8 chiau3 goan2 i2-cheng5 sou2 hoan-hu3 lin2 e5 an2-ne cho3.  12) An2-ne lin2 chiu7 e7 tit-tioh8 bi7-sin3-chia2 e5 chun-keng3. Lin2 sou2 su-iau3 e5, ia7 chiu7 m7-bian2 oa2-kho3 pat8-lang5.

Chu2 e5 lai5-lim5:
	13) HiaN-ti7 a3, koan-he7 hiah-e5 si2-khi3 e5 lang5, goan2 chin ai3 lin2 liau2-kai2 sit8-cheng5, an2-ne lin2 chiu7 be7 chhin-chhiuN7 hiah-e5 bo5 hi-bong7 e5 lang5 pi-siong.  14) Lan2 ki3-jian5 siong-sin3 Ia5-sou si2-khi3 koh-chai3 oah8 khi2-lai5, ia7 tioh8 siong-sin3 Siong7-chu2 e7 chhoa7 hiah-e5 sin3 Ia5-sou lai5 si2-khi3 e5 lang5, kap Ia5-sou cho3-hoe2 lai5.
	15) Goan2 chiau3 Chu2 e5 oe7 ka7 lin2 kong2 chit hang7 tai7-chi3: chiu7-si7 lan2 chiah-e5 chai7 Chu2 chai3-lim5 e5 si5 iau2 teh oah8 e5 lang5, be7 pi2 hiah-e5 i2-keng si2-khi3 e5 lang5 khah iu-sian.  16) In-ui7 ho7-leng7 chit8-e7 hoat, thian-sai3-tiuN2 e5 siaN chit8-e7 chhut, Siong7-chu2 e5 la-pa chit8-e7 hiang2, Chu2 pun2-sin e7 tui3 thiN-teng2 kang3-lim5! Hiah-e5 u7 sin3 Ki-tok lai5 si2-khi3 e5 lang5, Siong7-chu2 e7 hou7 in tai7-seng koh-oah8 khi2-lai5;  17) Jian5-au7 lan2 chiah-e5 kau3 hit e5 si5 iau2 teh oah8 e5 lang5, e7 kap in pi7 tiau3-chip8 cho3-hoe2 toa3 hun5-lai7, thang chai7 khong-tiong geng5-chiap Chu2. An2-ne lan2 chiu7 e7 kap Chu2 eng2-oan2 tong5-chai7.  18) Sou2-i2 lin2 tioh8 eng7 chiah-e5 oe7 lai5 hou7-siong kou2-le7.

Te7 5 ChiuN

Chun2-pi7 geng5-chiap Chu2 e5 lai5-lim5:
	1) HiaN-ti7 a3, koan-he7 chiah-e5 tai7-chi3, sim2-mih si5 sim2-mih jit8 e7 hoat-seng, goa2 bo5 su-iau3 sia2.  2) In-ui7 lin2 ka-ti7 beng5-beng5 chai-iaN2 Chu2 chai3-lim5 e5 Jit8-chi2, e7 chhin-chhiuN7 am3-si5 chhat8-la2(NNa2) lai5 an2-ne, bo5-i3-tiong chiu7 lai5.  3) Lang5 teh kong2, ¡§It-chhe3 chin peng5-cheng7 an-un2¡¨ hit e5 si5, hut-jian5-kan in e7 siu7 hui2-biat8! Chhin-chhiuN7 boeh seng-san2 e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 e7 siu7-tioh8 chhui-chun7 e5 thong3-khou2, in be7-tang3 to5-pi7 chit e5 lan5-koan.  4) Tan7-si7 hiaN-ti7 a3, lin2 m7-si7 toa3 ti7 ou-am3-tiong, sou2-i2 hit e5 jit8-chi2 be7 chhin-chhiuN7 chhat8-la2(NNa2) an2-ne lai5 ka7 lin2 phah tioh8-kiaN.  5) Lin2 long2 si7 kong-beng5 e5 lang5, si7 jit8-si5 e5 lang5. Lan2 m7-si7 siok8 ti7 am3-si5 e5, ia7 m7-si7 siok8 ti7 ou-am3 e5.  6) Sou2-i2 lan2 m7-thang chhin-chhiuN7 pat8-lang5 teh khun3; tioh8 chheN2-chheN2, tioh8 kin2-sin7.  7) In-ui7 khun3 e5 lang5 long2 si7 am3-si5 khun3; chiu2-chui3 e5 lang5 long2 si7 am3-si5 chiu2-chui3.  8) Tan7-si7 lan2 siok8 ti7 jit8-si5 e5 lang5, tioh8 kin2-sin7. Lan2 tioh8 chiong sin3-sim kap ai3-sim lai5 cho3 chian3-kah chheng7, chiong tit-kiu3 e5 hi-bong7 lai5 cho3 kng3-khoe ti3.  9) In-ui7 Siong7-chu2 m7-si7 keng2-soan2 lan2 lai5 siu7 heng5-hoat8, si7 boeh hou7 lan2 oa2-kho3 lan2 e5 Chu2 Ia5-sou Ki-tok lai5 tit-kiu3.  10) I the3 lan2 si2, si7 boeh hou7 lan2 chai7 I lai5-lim5 e5 si5, bo5-lun7 lan2 teh oah8 a2-si7 si2-khi3, hou7 lan2 e7-tang3 kap I cho3-hoe2 seng-oah8.  11) Sou2-i2 lin2 tioh8 chiau3 hian7-chai7 lin2 sou2 teh cho3 e5 an2-ne hou7-siong kou2-le7, hou7-siong pang-bang5.

Choe3-au7 e5 khoan3-bian2 kap chheng2-an:
	12) HiaN-ti7 a3, goan2 khun2-kiu5 lin2 tioh8 chun-keng3 hiah-e5 ti7 lin2 tiong-kan teh kang-chok e5 lang5. In chiu7-si7 Chu2 keng2-soan2 lai5 teh chi2-to7 lin2, ka3-si7 lin2 e5 lang5.  13) Ui7-tioh8 in sou2 teh cho3 e5 kang-chok, lin2 tioh8 eng7 choe3 toa7 e5 chun-keng3 kap ai3-sim lai5 khoan2-thai7 in. Lin2 tai7-ke tioh8 ho5-peng5 siong-chhu3.
