Chu7-su7
M7-chai e7 hap8 tai7-ke e5 bak8-chiu, kap li7-ek a2 be7 ? Chin sit-le2, goa2 si7 Bok8-su, m7-si7 Tu3-chok-ka, ia2 m7- si7 Siau2-soat ka; chong2-si7 bo5 lun7 tui3 to2 chit8 hong- bin7, goa2 long2 u7 chhu3-bi7.  M7-ku2 sip8-hak8 kiu2 put-seng5, siong5-siong5 oe7 hou2 soa3 hoan2-tng2 piN3 chiaN5 kau2, ti3-kau3 m7-na7 bo5 li7-ek, soa3 hoan2-tng2 hou7 lang5 chhio3. Cheng5 goa2 ia7 bat giah8-pit sia2 chit8 pun2 “Sek-eng3 Ji5-tong5 sim- li2 e Jit8-iau7 hak8-hau7 kau3-siu7-hoat”. Hit-si5 tit-tioh8 che7-che7 ho2 peng5-iu2 ai3 thak8, ti3-kau3 u7 be7 bo5 kau3; m7-ku2 u7 lang5 hiam5 siuN chhim, sou2-i2 bo5 koh chhut-pan2.  
TaN chit-pang ui7-tioh8 kui2-na7 e5 in-toaN chiu7 koh sia2 chit pun2 chheh.  
1.Ui7 ai3 kou2-bu2 kau3-hoe7 e5 hoe7-iu2 khah chin3-pou7 ti7 Lo5-ma2-ji7.  Chiong siau2-soat hou7 in thak8. Tai-ke soa3 oh8 bat-ji7, khah be7 ia3-sian7.  Goa2 bat chhut-po3 lai5 lun7 “Lo5-ma2-ji7 e5 tiong3-tok8”, ia7 ti7 chia lai5 kou2-bu2 Lo5-ma2-ji7, u7 chhin-chhiuN7 chu7- siong mau5-tun2; sit8-chai7 bo5. In-ui7 goa2 m7-si7 hoan2- tui3 Lo5-ma2-ji7; si7 chan3-seng5.  Chong2-si7 m7-ai3 lang5 bit8-thau5 bit8-tau7 ti7 Lo5-ma2-ji7; Han3-bun5 ia7 tioh8 tui3- tiong7 e5 i3-su3. Sou2-i2 kio3 lan2 tioh8 chu3-i3.  
2.Ui7-tioh8 ai3 kou2-bu2 hoe7-iu2 e5 sin3-sim, kap phin2-heng7. 
3.Iu7 tioh8 ai3 hou7 cho3 Ki-tok kau3 ka-teng5 khah lau7- jiat8, hou7 hu7-lu2 kap se3-kiaN2 khah u7 kang-ge7.  Chiu7- si7 tai7-ke thak8 iu2-ek e5 kou3-su7, bian2-tit kong2 eng5-oe7, a2-si7 thiaN bo5 li7-ek kou2, khoaN3 bo5 cheng3-keng e5 hi3 kap chheh.  
4.Goan7 chheng-lian5 e5 lam5-lu2, sou2 thiaN3 e5 hiaN-ti7 chi2- moai7, ia7 beh tui3 chia lai5 tit-tioh8 che7-chio2 e5 bian2-le7.

Tui3 chit kui3-na7 hang7, chiu7 goan7 cho3 hi-seng, giah8 pit lai5 sia2 chit pun2.  
Chit-pun2 siau2-soat ki5-sit8 si7 2 pun2: Chit8-pun2 kio3 cho3:- “ An2-nia5 e5 Bak8-sai2 ”  Chit8-pun2 kio3-cho3: “ Sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 ki3-ho7 ”  
Goa2 pun2 m7-kaN2 chhut-pan2, si7 u7 che7-che7 ho2 peng5- iu2 thak8 liau2 chin chan3-seng5, kio2 goa2 tioh8 chhut-pan2. Sou2-i2 kin-a2-jit8 chiah e7 hian3-chhut ti7 lin2 e5 bin7- cheng5.  
Koh chit8-hang7, lin2 thak8 chit-pun2 chheh, m7 thang be7 ki3-tit--e5, chiu7-si7 TeN7 Khe-phoan3 Bok8-su.  In-ui7 chit-pun2 chheh tit-tioh8 i e5 kau3-cheng3 e5 kang che7- che7; na7 m7 si7 an2-ni, iu5-goan5 si7 tiaN7 kui5 ti7 pak-tou2 lai7, seN be7 chhut--lai5. Sou2-i2 choat (chok)-chia2 hoaN-hi2 sia2 kui2-ku3 ti7 chia ka7 toa7 toa7 seh to7-sia7.

An2-nia2 e5 Bak8-sai2
1.Pe7-kiaN2 e5 oan-ke
Ah ! Cha2-khi2 chin-chiaN3 si7 ho2 e5 cha2-khi2. Phang- hoe e5 bi7 tui3 hoe-hng5 hou7 hong chit8 chun7 chit8 chun7 ah--lai5. Chhek-chiau2-a2 ki-bu2 ki-kiauh8, thiau3-bu2 ti7 tiaN5-cheng5 koe2-chi2 chhiu7 e5 tiong-kan. Jit8-thau5 tu2 chhut, kng-soaN3 chio3 kau3 thiaN-tiong. Hok-goan5 peh thau3-cha2 khi2-lai5 teh se2-bin7, giah8-thau5 khoaN3-kiN3 i e5 hau7-seN Thian-su3 ah tui3 toa7 mng5 jip8 lai5, bin7 ang5 ki3-ki3 khong7-khong7 tian, sin-khu chhau3 chiu2- hian3. Hok-goan5 peh chin siu7-khi3 tit8-tit8 me7--i. Thian-su3 ah teN3 bo5 thiaN-kiN3, chiu7 phin5 tui3 pang5- nih jip khi3. I e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 m7 kap i sio-chioh mng7, cho3 in tai7-ke khi3 chiah8 png7. Chit e5 hu7-jin5 lang5 si7 sim-pu7-a2 io-e5, seN cho3 koe3-tit khi3; kho2- sioh bo5 thak8-chheh, koh pak8 se3-kha; sou2-i2 tak8 hang7 khah nng2-chiaN2. Sio2-theng5 Po2-bi2 lau5, khui chhai3- toaN kap phiau5-chhiong-hui3 kiong7 15 khou beh lai5 theh8 chiN5. Ah ! Lun7 Thian-su3 ah, sit8-chai7 chin kho2- ouN3: chiah8 si7 san-tin hai2-bi7; chheng7 si7 chhoan5 leng5-lo5 phang2-si. In lau7-pe7 cha2-si5 u7 koa2 ke-hoe2 long2 hou7 i khai-liau2 khi3. Koh pau-chhi7 chit8 e5 cha-bou2 ti7 Thai3-lok8 hng5; bo5 siuN7 beh cho3 cheng3-giap8. HiaN-ti7 che7-che7 long2 phah-sng2 khi3; chi2 u7 chhun chit8 e5 sio2- moai7 kin-ni5 8 hoe3, kap hit e5 sim-pu7-a2 bou2 chit8 ke kiong7 5 lang5. Pe7-bo2 iu7 lau7, sio2-moai7 li7 se3-han3, pun2-sin m7 cho3 kang, koh phah-phun2 khai; sou2-i2 ke- lai7 chin siap, lau7-pe7 sui bong2 60 lian5 hoe3 lah, ia7 tioh8 siong5-siong5 chhut-khi3 cho3 sio2 khoa2 seng-li2-a2 lai5 thiN-thau5 thiap-boe2. Cha2-cheng5 Hok-goan5 peh si7 ho2-koe3 e5 lang5, in Lau7-pe7 bat cho3 Ti-koan7, i siau3- lian5 tong5-seng-na5 ia7 koaN7-phoa3 kui2-na7 kha. Siau3- lian5 e5 si5 kek8 hu3-ju7, beh chiah8-png7 tioh8 khau-cheng lui7-kou2; kau3 kin-a2 jit8 chaih8 lau7 lah chiah lai5 siu7- khou2 kau3 an2-ni, put-chi2 oan3-sin chheh-mia7.  
Koh cha2-khi2 chiap-tioh8 chhai3-tiam3 khui toaN lai5 tho2-chiN5. Ah ! lau7-hoe2-a2 khi3 kau3 chhui3-chhiu phun3- phun3-phun3, kha-chhiu2 lak8-lak8-chhoah. (chit chun7 teh khi3 siuN7 ban7 lah ! chu7-se3 bo5 tiau-tok si7 pe7-bo2 e5 chho3-gou7) Hok-goan5 peh kong2 i m7 
chai, i m7 heng5; chong2-si7 Hok-goan5-m2 kiaN liau2 nau7-su7, ia7 hou7 lang5 tiam3 mng5-khau2 jiang2 tho2 chiN5 phaiN2-se3, chiu7 kin2 jip8 khi3 lai5-bin7, chiong i e5 ou-kin theh8 lai5 giah8 chit8 lui2 ou-kin (kim?) hoe, oa2 3 chiN5 tang7, tat8 oa2 16 khou, thau iap hou7 hit e5 lang5. ( khah siong5 lau7-bo2 khah gau5 seng7 kiaN2.) Tai7-khai3 Hok-goan5-m2 e5 kim-khi3 ia7 pian3 beh liau2 lah; chhun 2,3 hang7, taN ou-kin hoe koh hou7 lang5 ti2-siau3, chiu7 chhun 2 hang7 sio2-khoa2--e5 na7-tiaN7; m7 ku2 ui7 thiaN3 kiaN2 chiu7 bo5 sioh (Bo5 tek-khak an2- ni chiu7 ho7-cho3 thiaN3).  
Hok-goan5 peh chai Hok-goan5 m2 chiong kim-a2 hou7 lang5 ti2-siau3, chiu7 koh khah bo5 hoaN-hi2 khi3, giah8 chit8 ki kun3-a2 tui3 in sim-pu7 e5 pang5-nih chiu7 jip8-khi3, chhut toa7 lat8 tui3 Thian-su3 ah chiu7 kong3 loh8-khi3. Siau3-lian5 ho2 kha-chhiu2, siam2-pi7 kong3 bo5 tioh8, Thian- su3 ah tui3 in tia e5 thui5-a2 chhiuN2 khi2 lai5, ka7 i bo7 hou7 to2. Lau7-hoe3-a2 siak tui3 hou7-teng7 loh8 khi3, ai chit8-e5 soa3 tiam7-tiam7. Hok-goan5 m2 ah ti7 chau3-kha thiaN- kiN3 sim-pu7 e5 pang5 phin-phong3 kio3, lau7--e5 tit8-tit8 chhoh, chiu7 chau2 lai5 khoaN3. Kau3 tiong-thiaN, thiaN tioh8 lang5 siak to2 e5 siaN, chit8-e5 soa3 tiam7-tiam7.  Chau2 kau3 pang5-mng5 khoaN3 lau7-e5 to2 ti7 pang5 lai7 e5 hou7-teng7 kha tiam7-tiam7, koh sim-pu7 teh khau3 A-pa o-oe3 ! Chiu7 chhiN-kiaN hoah kiu3-lang5, ( seng7-ti gia5 chau3, seng7-kiaN2 put-hau3).  Chhu3-piN kong7-kong7 kun2, kin2 chau2 lai5 khoaN3, u7-e5 kin2 koeh, u7-e5 kin2 ka2 kiuN- bo2-chiap.  Hok-goan5 m2-ah chit8-si5 sim-loan7, khauh8- khauh8 chong5.  TaN ai3 chai Hok-goan5-peh chaiN2-iuN7 chhiaN2 khoaN3 e7 hoe5.  
2.Phu5-long7 chia2 liu5-ti3 chhut-hoat
Kiu3 oa2 poaN3 tiam2-cheng, Hok-goan5-peh ah chiah hoe5-hun5, lang5 teh kiu3 in lau7-pe7 e5 si5, Thian- su3 ah cho3 i chhut-khi3 ( put-hau3 kau3 kek8).  Au7-lai5 chhiaN2 kun5-thau5 sai-hu7 lai5 khoaN, chiah8 siong-ioh8, to2 chiaN5 le2-pai3 chiah khi2-chhng5.  Chhu3-piN khoaN3-kiN3 i chit ho7 kiaN2, chiu7 kap Hok-goan5-peh chham-siong5 kong2, sia2 pin2 tui3 koaN-thiaN jip8, hou7 i khi3 koaiN ti7 phu5-long7 chia2 e5 siu-iong5 sou2.  Hok-goan5-peh khi2-thau5 chin m7 kam, au7-lai5 khoaN3-phoa3 ka3 bo5 hoat, chiu7 ka7 i jip8 pin2.  Koh 3,4 jit8, keng2-chhat-thiaN chiu7 lai5 tiau7-cha, tai7-seng kio3 khi3 liu5-ti3 kui2-na7 jit8, mng7 pe7- bo2 e5 i3-kian3, long2 tang5-i3 chiu7 sang3 loh Hoat-iN7. Chhit goeh8 chhe 8 teng2 pou 8 tiam2 kong-phoaN3, i e5 ku7-an3 cho3 chhat8 poah8-kiau2 8 pai2, tiong-kan kan- im5 che7-che7 hang7; koh chit pai2 e5 ngou2-gek8, toa7-toa7 put-hau3.  Chiu7 teng7 choe7 beh ka7 i koaiN ti7 Phu5-long7 sou2 6 ni5.  Koat-teng7 7 goeh8 chhe 10 beh chhut-hoat.  
7 goeh8 chhe 10 thau3-cha2 6 tiam2 goa7, sou2 u7 e5 hoan7-lang5 long2 khan beh khi3 che7 hoe2-chhia.  In sou2  tiam3 e5 siaN5 si7 ti7 Po2-teng7, tui3 Hoat-iN7 liu5-ti3-tiuN5 kau3 chhia-thau5 oa2 3 li2 lou7.  Hit jit8 70 goa7-e5 beh siu7 ke3-sang3 kau3 Phu5-long7 chia2 e5 siu-iong5-sou2; tiong-kan u7 chit8 e5 hu7-jin5 lang5, eng7 a-phian3 hun kiau2 moai5 lai5 thau7 si2 in ang; ia7 chit8-e5 si7 sio2-ti7 ui7 chai5-san2 saN-chiN e5 su7, thai5 si2 hiaN-ko, ka7 hiat hou7 chui2 lau7. Ah ! kong2 be7 liau2, long2 si7 sia7-hoe7 e5 chiu3-thang5. 70 goa7-e5 pai5 chit8 liat8; u7-e5 saN phoa3-phoa3, u7-e5 kha seN-thiaN3 noa7 hap-hap; u7-e5 toa7 tou2-thang2, bin7 ng5 phi-phi; u7-e5 giah8 koaiN2-a2, u7-e5 thau5-mng5 sam3-sam3. 7 tiam2 si5-cheng tan5, tak8-e5 tui3 kaN-gak8 chhut-lai5; nng7-e5 pak8 cho3-hoe2, chit8-tui3 chit8-tui3, long2 chheng7 ang5-saN koa3 thih-lian7, ia7 u7-e5 pho7 kiaN2 teh chiah8 ni. Ah ! chin kho2-lin5; u7-e5 gin2-na2 go7 kau3 san2 pi-pa, ia7 bo5 sim2-mih8 ni thang hou7 in chiah8.  Hoe2-chhia 7 tiam2 45 hun beh chhut-hoat, sou2-i2 chin koaN2-kin2, khan3-siu2 chin heng5-gek8, chin ok, tit8-tit8 ka7 in thoa, kiaN5 khah ban7-e5, eng7 kha ka7 in cham3 (choe7 e5 po3- eng3, eng3-kai) long2 bo5 lin5-bin2 e5 sim.  Ah ! Hiah e5 hoan7-lang5 e5 pe7-bo2, bou2-kiaN2 che7-che7 lai5 ti7 kaN-gak8 e5 mng5-khau2 teh tan2 beh khoaN3 i e5 kiaN2.  Hok-goan5 m2-ah chhoa7 i e5 sim-pu7 kap i e5 cha-bou2 kiaN2 Bi2-hoai5- ah khia7 ti7 kaN-gak8 cheng5 lou7-piN e5 chheng5 kha teh khoaN3.  Chit8-tui3 chit8-tui3 tit8-tit8 koe3, khoaN3 kau3 boe2- a2 te7 3 tui3, chiu7-si7 i e5 kiaN2 kap iau2 chit8 e5 thai5 lang5 hoan7 lian5 cho3-hoe2, Khan3-siu2 khan leh.  Bo5 tiuN-ti5 Hok-goan5 m2-ah soa3 sim sng hau2 chhut lai5, teh kio3 i e5 kiaN2; sim-pu7, cha-bou2 kiaN2 ia7 lau5 bak8- sai2 (khah phaiN2 ia7 si7 kiaN2).  Thian-su3 ah theng5 kha oat8 koe3 lai5 khoaN3, hou7 Khan3-siu2 ka7 I sai loh8 kong2 m7-chun2 i lou7-tiong kap lang5 kong2 oe7.  Tiap- a2-ku2 thou5-hun2 phong7-phong7 eng, chiu7 kau3 chhia-thau5.  Phu5-long7 chia2 siu-iong5 sou2 ti7 Bong7-kou2, kin7 Se- pi2-li7-a chit e5 siaN5 ho7-cho3 Mai7-mai2 siaN5.  Hoe2-chhia tioh8 tui3 Pak-kiaN, TiuN-ka-khau2, Lun5-khou2 keng- koe3.  Lou7-thau5 tioh8 che7 oa2 chiaN5 chap8 jit8 e5 hoe2- chhia.  Hok-goan5 m2 khoaN3 kiaN2 hou7 khan3-siu2 koat, chiu7 na2 m7 kam sim, toe3 hoan7-lang5 e5 au7-bin7 tit8- tit8 kiaN5 kau3 chhia-thau5.  Kau3 ui7 i3-keng 7 tiam2 30 hun, koh 15 hun cheng chiu7 beh chhut-hoat. Sou2-i2 bo5 hioh-khun3, liam-piN chit8 tui3, chit8 tui3, khan chiuN7 hoe2-chhia, si7 hou7 in che7 ti7 hoe3-but8 chhia, chhu tiu7-ko2 hou7 in che, khoan2-thai7 in cho3 chhin- chhiuN7 ti. Kau2 tiam2 hoe2-chhia pi-a2 tan5, chiu7 khui chhia.  Hok-goan5 m2 kap sim-pu7, cha-bou2 kiaN2 Bi2- hoai5 ah khui siaN khau3 kau3 oN3-oN3 kio3. Thian-su3 ah bin7 long2 bo5 oat8 chhut lai5 khoaN3 in, kaN2-si7 teh m7 goan7 in ka7 i jip8-pin2 a2-si teh iu-bun7. Bo5 cheng5 e5 hoe2- chia tit8-tit8 kiaN5 khi3.  Hok-goan5 m2 chit8 si5 soa3 hun7 to2 ti7 thou5-kha.  
3. PeN7-chhng5 e5 bak8-sai2
Ti7 theng5-chhia-tiuN5 e5 Keng2-chhat kin2 chau2-oa2--lai5. Iah8-tiuN2 tai7-ke chiu7 chhah jip8 it, 2-teng2 e5 tai7-hap8-sek, hou7 i to2 ti7 nng2-nng2 e5 i2, kin2 iuN2 leng2 chui2 lai5 ka7 i u3 thau5-khak, kap heng-kham2, theng5 tiap-a2-ku2 chiu7 chai lang5. Iah8-tiuN2 u7 chiong tam7-poh8 pho5-to5 chiu2 hou7 i lim, chiu7 cheng-eng. Che si7 lau7-lang5 hiat- khi3 soe-lam2 koh chit8-si5 koe3-thau5 hoan5-lo2, hoeh soa3 pho7-sim. I cheng-sin5 khi2-lai5, kap sim-pu7 cha-bou2 kiaN2 ian5-lou7 hau2. SiuN7-kiN3 ka-ti7 e5 phaiN2-mia7: cheng5 ho2-giah8, taN san3-hiong; kiaN2 lian5-lian5 si2, chhun chit e5 iu7 put-hau3, sui-bong2 oah8, iau2-ku2 chhin-chhiuN7 si2. 3-e5 oan2-jian5 chhin-chhiuN7 sang3-chong3 to3-lai5 chit8-iuN7. Chhu3-piN tai7-ke chin tong5-cheng5 lai5 an-ui3, kong2 Giok8-put-tok put-seng5-khi3. Ah ! He to7 si7 the3 lan2 ka3-si7, kin-tu2 na7 be7 ke2-piN2, put-si5 tiaN7 lou2-khi3, phaiN2 mia5-siaN, m7-tat8-tioh8 li7 lan2 hng7-hng7, ia7 seN-si2 si7 thian-sou3. Kin-tu2 na7 beh si2, ti7 chhu3-nih tiaN7 lam7- sam2 cho3, iau2 khah gui5-hiam2. Khi3 hia chiah8, khun3 chiau3-si5, koh cho3 kang un7-tong7. Phoa3-peN7 u7 phu5- long7-sou2 e5 i-seng pian7-pian7, pi2 chhu3-nih iau2 khah un2 ouh ! Tai7-ke chit8 chhui3 chit8 chih8 teh ka7 i khou2- khng3; tiong-kan ia7 u7 hit ho7 bu5-ti-e5 lai5 kau3 te3 kap i hau2 kau3 chhngh-chhngh kio3; chong2-si7 Hok-goan5 m2 thiaN tai7-ke sou2-kong2 u7 li2-khi3 chiu7 siu7 an-ui3. Koh Hok-goan5-peh ia7 teh me7 an3-choaN2 hiah m7-tam- su-iaN5, chiu7 m7 kaN2 hau2.  
Chia chhiaN2 an3-ha7, lai5 kong2 Thian-su3 ah Ti7 chhia- lai7, Khan3-siu2 kau3 saN-tng3 chiong png7-oan5 chit8 lang5 chit8 oan5, iok-liok8 oa2 2 oaN2 png7, ia7 chiong chit8 liap8 kiam5-moai5-a2, kap tam7-poh8 chhai3-thau5-a2 hou7 in phoe3. Peng5-sou3 chit8 me5 khai tioh8 10-kui2 khou : ngou2- liu2-ki, ang5-sio-hi5, siu3-kiu5-oan5, hoah-kun5 lim chiu2, Chiok-hu7 koaN7-pan5; kin-a2-jit8 lai5 kau3 chia tioh8 chiah8 png7-oan5. Png7-oan5 e5 bi2 bo5 sim2-mih8 peh8, chho3- chho3 chiah8 tioh8 siap-siap; koh bo5 thng thang phoe3, hou7 i go7 kui2-na7 tng3. Keh jit8, si7 te7 ji7 jit8 e5 am3 si5, kau3 kin7 TiuN-ka-khau2 e5 ui7, tai7 ke khun3 tiam7- cheng7, tng hoe2-chhia teh kiaN5 e5 tiong-kan, khoaN3 hoe2-chhia kiaN5 chin ban7 chiu7 chhoah8 tng7 thih- lian7.  Thian-su3 ah tui3 hoe2-chhia thang-a2 thiau3 chhut- khi3. 4.Bo2-kiaN2 thi5-khau3
ThiN bo5 pang3-sak lang5, ki3-jian5 u7 chit8 sut-a2 hoan2- hoe2, peN7 chiu7 chiam7-chiam7 ho2.  Kau3 goeh8-goa7-jit8 au7 chiu7 iu5-goan5 chiau3-goan5.   
Ti7 KaN-lai7 e5 kui-ki2 si7 chin giam5. Mui2 le2-pai3 u7 chhiaN2 kui2-na7 e5 Bok8-su lai5 ti7 KaN lai7 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 hoan7-lang5 thiaN; chong2-si7 Thian-su3 ah kau3-kek8 nge7- sim, u7-si5 teh phoa3-peN7, chit hang7 hit hang7 m7-goan7 seng5-siu7 to7-li2. Chia chhiaN2 an3-ha7.  
Kong2 in lau7-bo2 tui3 chhia-thau5 to3-khi3, kau3 kin-a2- jit8 4 ni5 lah, tak8-si5 liam7 i e5 kiaN2, koh tui3 hoat-iN7 thong-ti, kong2 in kiaN2 ti7 poaN3-lou7 chau2-hoan7, ke- teng7 2 ni5.  Sim na2 kan-khou2, siuN7 tioh8 chiu7 hau2. Ia7 Thian-su3 ah e5 bou2, ti7 i boeh khi3 e5 si5 i2-keng u7 sin, khi3 liau2 chiaN5 poaN3-ni5, chiu7 seN chit8 e5 cha- bou2-kiaN2, in ma2 ka7 i ho7-cho3 Ma2-tan2 ah, i3-su3 si7 a-ma2 tit8-tit8 teh tan2 in a-pa5 to3--lai5, sim-pu7 na7 khoaN3 chit e5 kiaN2, ia7-si7 bak7-sai2 chun2 png7 thun. Kin ni5 4 hoe3, phut-phut thiau3, chin oah8-tang7,tit lang5 thiaN3; chong2-si7 m7 bat khoaN3-kiN3 lau7-pe7. Hok-goan5 m2 e5 bak8-chiu tiaN7-tiaN7 hau2 soa3 piN3 chin bu7; Hok-goan5 peh lau7-hoe3-a2 khi3 be7 kiam5-sng-tiN; ta-ke sim-pu7 ka7 lang5 se2 saN, giah8-chiam, lai5 tou7 koe3 jit8; sio2-moai7  Bi2-hoai5 ah kin ni5 12 hoe3 lah, sio2 hak8-hau7 2 lian5 seng, seN-cho3 chin sui2, koai, koh gau5 thak8-chheh,  thang cho3 thong-ke e5 an-ui3.  
Koh kong2 kau3 Thian-su3 ah tui3 peN7-au7, sim chit8 sut-a2 khah hoan2-hoe2, sou2-i2 khah kut-lat8 cho3 kang, tak8 hang7 chiau3 kui-ki2 u7 le2-sou3. Tui3 an2-ni Phu5-long7  sou2-tiuN2 chiu7 tek8-sia3 i chhun 5 ni5. Kau3 5 ni5 chiok, koaiN-kaN te7 5 ni5 e5 6 goeh8 boe2, tui3 kaN-gak8 u7 chit8 tiuN phoe kia3 to3 lai5, thiah khui chai si7 in kiaN2 Thian- su3 ah kia3-e5, thong-ke hoaN-hi2 kau3 lau5 bak8-sai2.  Phoe tai7-khai3 si7 kong2 I ti7 hia gu7-peN7 hiam2 si2, ia7 taN i u7 hoan2-hoe2, kaN-gak8-tiuN2 beh hou7 i ti7 au7 goeh8 jit8 to3-lai5.  Thak8-liau2 tai7-ke ma3-ma3 khau3, Hok- goan5 m2 chhut khi3 tiaN5 tiong-ng kui7 teh pai3 thiN-te7.   
Kau3 7 goeh8 15 jit8 cha2-khi2 thiN phu2 kng, u7 chit8 e5 lang5 giah8 koaiN2-a2, chheng7 chhau2-e5, i-koan cheng2-che5, poe7 hit e5 pau-hok8-a2 tit8-tit8 tui3 toa7-mng5 jip8-lai5.  Hok-goan5 m2-ah thau3-cha2 khi2-lai5 beh khui mng5, khoaN3 tioh8, chai si7 Thian-su3 ah ! 2 e5 bo2 a2 kiaN2 lam2 teh tit8-tit8 khau3.  Thian-su3 ah kui7 loh8 kio3 kong2, An2-nia2!  Goa2 u7 tek-choe7 thiN ia7 u7 tek- choe7 li2, goa2 chin kian3-siau3 m7 kaN2 kio3-cho3 li2 e5 kiaN2.  Chit8 si5 kiaN-chheN2 Hok-goan5 peh, lai7-bin7 e5 lang5 chong5-chong5 chhut lai3. Ma2-tan2 ah 5 hoe3 ia7 kap lang5 khau3 kah ma3-ma3 kio3, chhu3-piN tai7-ke long2 chau2 lai5 khoaN3, m7 chai, kio3 si7 siaN2 lang5 cha-me5 si2. (Che si7 hoaN-hi2 e5 kek8-thau5).  Chhu3-piN khoaN3 liau2, ia7 hoaN-hi2 kau3 be7 kou3-tit. Thian-su3 e5 bou2 ia7 chin hoaN-hi2, ui7 tiong7-hu chiu2 seN-koaN2 5 ni5, ia7 kou3 chit e5 kiaN2. Thian-su3 ah chiu7 pho7 kiaN2, chiong pau-hok8- a2 hou7 hu7-jin5 lang5 koaN7, tai7-ke jip8 thiaN nih, Ma2- tan2 ah kim-kim siong3 in lau7-pe7, m7 bat khoaN3-kiN3. Chit8 si5 ke-lai7 toa7 hoaN-hi2, chiu7 liam5-piN pi7-pan7 chiah8 cha2-tng3. Chiah8 pa2 thau2 pau-hok8, jip8 kaN-gak8 5 ni5 than3 oa2 300 khou, ia7 oh8 chit8 hang7 kang-ge7: e7 hiau2 cho3 tin5-i2, tin5-na5, kang-hu chin ho2. Chiu7 chiong chiN5 theh8 kau-tai7 lau7-pe7. Lau7-pe7 ia7 hoaN- hi2 kau3 lau5 bak8-sai2. Chit8 ke e5 hoaN-hi2 oh tit heng5-iong5, chhin-chhiuN beh si2 e5 ka-teng5 koh hok8- heng. Sou2-i2 tit Thian-su3 ah thiaN3, khoan2-thai7 toa7 bou2 soa3 piN3 cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 cha-bou2-kan2, Ma2-tan2 ah beh chit8 chiam ia7 tioh8 tui3 in a-nia5 theh8. Beh chhut-khi3 na7 bo5 mng7 se3-i5 u7 siaN to7 m7 kaN2 khi3; chong2-si7 Ta-ke khah thiaN3 toa7-e5: in-ui7 si7 i chu7 se3 io-e5, na2 cha-bou2 kiaN2 leh. Sou2-i2 u7 chit8 jit8, Thian-su3 ah chhut-goa7 pou2-hoe3, chhu3-nih 2 e5 toa7- bou2 se3-i5 oan-ke. Ta-ke u7 phah se3-i5. 5. Hui-siong5 e5 tiau7-cha

Thian-su3 ah in toa7-bou2 tui3 au7-boe2 mng5 chhut-khi3, ti7 in au7-boe2 u7 chit8 tiau5 khe-a2, lang5 goa7 chui2. I ia7 siong5-siong5 ti7 hia se2-saN. Kau3 khe-kiN5 khoaN3 tioh8 hiah e5 se2-saN e5 chioh8-thau5 ui7, na2 m7 goan7. Thian- su3 ah siu7-koaiN e5 si5 i ti7 hia se2-saN lai5 tou7 koe3-jit8, iuN2-chhi7 ta-ke-koaN e5 kan-khou2, chiu7 khui siaN toa7 khau2, siuN7 i kin-a2-jit8 chhut-si3 chin m7-tat8. SiuN7 i e5 phaiN2-mia7 cho3 lang5 e5 sim-pu7-a2, toe3 ang siu7-khou2  tam-iam5 un3-chhou3, chiu2 seN-koaN2, kau3 kin-a2-jit8 khah ho2-koe3, chiu7 hou7 se3-i5 khe2-cheh8 . Na2 siuN7 na2 m7- goan7; koh cha7-jit8 hou7 Thian-su3 ah phah, kong3 kau3 lau5 phiN7-hoeh bak tioh8 saN, khan saN-a2 khi2- lai5 khoaN3, na2 m7-goan7, chiu7 koat-i3 beh thiau3 loh8 khi3 si2; m7-ku2 chit8 sim siuN7 tioh8 seN3-mia7 kiaN2 Ma2-tan2 ah, kin ni5 7 hoe3, i chit8 lang5 na7 si2, kiaN2 chiu7 bo5 bo2. SiuN7 liau2 chiu7 koh un loh8 khi3 khe-piN hau2.  
Li7 kui2-na7 chap8-pou7, u7 chit8 e5 lang5 am3-si5 teh he7 ko5, pang3 tio3 beh liah8 hi5, i siuN7 kong2 si7 cha-bou2 lang5 e5 ang si2 teh khau3, ia7 put-kai2 ki5-i3, koh si7 siau3 lian5 lang5, Sou2-i2 bo5 oa2 lai5 ka7 i khoaN3.  Hu7-jin5 lang5 hau2- hau2 leh siuN7 kong2, Kin-tu2 oah8 ia7 bo5 chhai2 kong2, in-ui7 oah8-teh ma7 bo5 koan5 thang ka3 kiaN2, lian5 chit8 chiam-chiN5 ia7 tioh8 tui3 se3-i5 tho2, oah8 teh si7 peh8 cho3 cha-bou2-kan2.  Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 khia7 khi2-lai5, chheh- sim thiau3 tui3 khe-te2 loh8 khi3.  Hit e5 tng-hi5 e5 cha- pou lang5 kin2 chau2 lai5 khoaN3, i2-keng tim5 loh8 khi3 ah.  Tng hi5-e5 kin2 chhang3-chui2-bi7 loh8 khi3 ka7 i gia5 khi2-lai5, chiah8 chin che7 chui2. I kin2 ka7 i pho7 khi3 khe-piN, ka7 i kho3 toa7 chioh8, hou7 i thou3-chui2 chhut- lai5, kai-chai3 iau2 teh chhoan2-khui3.  
Kau3 10 tiam2 goa7 chhu3-nih Ma2-tan2 ah teh chhui3-ta tho2 beh chiah8 te5, chhoe7 bo5 bo2, teh hau2, phah chheN2 a-ma2, hit-tiap8 tai7-ke chiah chai lai7-bin7 bo5 lang5.  Chhoe7 lai5 chhoe7 khi3 long2 bo5, kin2 khi3 po3 keng2-chhat tau3 chhoe7, chiah chhoe7 tioh8 ti7 au7 piah li2 goa7 lou7 e5 khe-kiN, ti7 chhiu7-kha naih-a, naih.  Kau3 ui7 tng hi5-e5 ia7 teh hoah kiu3-lang5, chin tu2-ho2.  An2- ni tai7-ke chiu7 siat-hoat to3-khi3, sin-khu tam5 lok8-lok8, kin2 ka7 i oaN7 saN-khou3, kah hou7 i sio, choaN ko-le5 hou7 i chiah8, bin7 long2 peh8 si2-sat, keh chiaN5 goeh8 jit8 chiah iong2.  Thian-su3 ah chit8 si5 ia7 chin kiaN, se3-i5 lian5 kiaN5 oa2 lai5 khoaN3 to7 bo5, cho3 i khun3, keng2- chhat chin siu7-khi3.  Kio3 Thian-su3 ah khi3 phai3-chhut- sou2 soat-ju7, (se3-i5 thang khouN2-ouN3).  Tui3 hia khi2, Thian- su3 ah ia7 chit8 sut-a2 khah m7-kaN2 koh phah i.

Lang5 khi3 po3-ko3, liam5-piN Kiam2-chhat-i kap Po2- teng7 siaN5 koaN-lip8 e5 peN7-iN7-tiuN2 lai5 giam7, giam7 liau2 pi5-chong7 phoa3-lih8, in-ui7 hit e5 lang5-kheh peng5-si5  tai3 ha7-siau, teng7-pi5 chin toa7. 6. ThiN-kong-toaN5 e5 chhiam-si
Thian-su3 ah tui3 phah si2 lang5 chiu7 tit8-tit8 chau2, ke2 mia5 oaN7 seN3, cheng5 seN3 Tan5 mia5 Thian-su3, chit pang ke2-cho3 Lim5 Thian-lai5, kin ni5 30 hoe3 cho2-iu7,  chhui3-chhiu te2 chin ho2, sou2-i2 chiu7 lau5 chhui3-chhiu, jit8 bih, am3-si5 kiaN5, na7 kian3 hioh kheh-tiam3 chiu7 oaN7-mia5. Kong2 kau3 chhu3 nih, Hok-goan5 peh in nng7 lau7-e5 hoan5-lo2 kau3 be7 kong2 tit.  Tiam3 siu-siu khi2- lai5. Ti7 chhia-thau5 tai7-ke sio-jin7, khi2-thau5 Khan2-siu2 kap ah-sang3 e5 kaN-gak8 Kam-tok, m7 chun2 in kong2-oe7; na7-si7 Bok8-su the3  Hok-goan5 m2 kiu5, chiu7 chun2 i 10 hun cheng ku2 bo2- kiaN2, ang-bou2, hiaN-moai7, pe7-kiaN2, tai7-ke kiN3-bin7. Hit si5 chit8-si5 5 e5 lam2-lam2-teh, khau3 kau3 ou3-ou3 kio3  chhin-chhiuN7 gu5. KiaN-tioh8 thong chhia-tiuN5 e5 lang5, chit8-si5 chhia-thau5 e5 lang5-kheh loan7 kun2-kun2: Khan3- siu2 kap Keng2-chhat hoah in tioh8 tiam7-tiam7, m7 thang hau2; na7 bo5 m7-chun2 in kong2-oe7. In chiu7 tiam7-tiam7, Hok-goan5 m2 chiu7 ka7 i kong2, kiaN2 ah, Li2 e5 A-pa i2-keng ti7 ku7-ni5 8 goeh8 15 tiong-chhiu-me5 si2-khi3 lah,  chit8-ke chhun 4 lang5: Bi2-hoai5 ah khi3 jip8 Lu2-oh8, Ma2-tan2 ah khi3 thak8 Siau2-hak8-hau7, kan-khou2 teh koe3- jit8. Ia7 siong7 iau2-kin2 ka7 I kong2, TaN choan5-ke  long2 u7 pai3 Siong7-te3. Chong2-si7 Thian-su3 ah iau2 si7 oan3-hin7 to7-li2; m7-ku2 chit tiap8 thiaN in lau7-bu2 kong2 Bok8-su e5 hou7-thai7 kap sio2-moai7 siu7 Kou-niu5 pang- chan7 khi3 thak8 Lu2-oh8, chit8 si5 khiok ia7 si7 kam2-sim; m7-ku2 si7 chit hiN7 jip8 hit hiN7 chhut.  Hok-goan5 m2 kong2-liau2 chiu7 kui7 loh8 the3 i ki5-to2, kiu5 Siong7-te3 kiu3--i. I cho3 i bak8-chiu kim-kim, khiok ia7 si5 bok8- koai3; in-ui7 i to7 bo5 thiaN to7-li2.  
Koh kui2 hun cheng hoe2-chhia beh chhut-hoat lah, chiu7 m7-chun2 in koh kong2, chiu7 ka7 i thoa chiuN7 hoe2- chhia. Bo2-kiaN2 tai7-ke bak8-sai2 nih-nih lau5 pun-khui, Tui3 an2-ni Thian-su3 ah chiu7 khi3 koaiN ti7 Pak-kiaN e5 kaN-gak8, cho3 bu5-ki5-tou5 e5 siu5-hoan7. Sim-pu7 i2-kip8 Bi2-hoai5 ah, kap Hok-goan5 m2, bok8-su tai7-ke  ian5-lou7 lau5 bak8-sai2 to3-lai5. Hit jit8 tu2 si7 pai3-it, keh- jit8 pai3-ji7 e5 Ki5-to2-hoe7, Hok-goan5 m2 kap sim-pu7 long2 chhoa7 khi3 hu3. Kou-niu5 kong2 to7-li2, soa3 chhiaN2  cheng3 chi2-moai7 pek-chhiat ui7 Hok-goan5 m2 e5 kiaN2 lai5 ki5-to2. Tai7-ke chin tong5-cheng5, ki3-to2 kau3 lau5 bak8- sai2 . Ia7 u7 chiaN5 10 e5 chi2-moai7 kap Hok-goan5 m2  iok-sok, kong2 in long2 beh tak8-si5 the3 i ki5-to2. Kau3 3 tiam2 chiu7 soaN3 hoe7. Hit-me5 Hok-goan5 m2 thong- me5 kui7 ti7 bin5-chhng5 cheng5 pek-chhiat ki5-to2, bo5 pat8 ku3 oe7; chi2-u7 tit8-tit8 liam7 " Chu2 ah, Chu2 ah! Li2 tioh8 kiu3 goa2 e5 kiaN2 ouh! Goa2 e5 Chu2 ah! " Bak8-sai2 si3-lam5-sui5, hit e5 pek- chhiat e5 siaN, thiaN kau3 ti7 Siong7-te3. Li2 e5 ki5-to2 i2-keng u7 thiaN kau3 Pe7 e5 tau lah! Kau3 u7 chit8 jit8 un2-tang3 e5 chiau2 li2 sou2 sin3 lai5 chiaN5. Hut-jian5 bo5--khi3, pang5-lai5 iu5-goan5 am3-am3. (Pek-chhiat e5 ki5- to2 Chu2 e7 thiaN). Tui3 an2-ni Hok-goan5 m2 toa7 kiaN, kin2 chau2 chhut-lai5, hit-si5 si7 poaN3-me5 au7 tiam2 goa7 lah. Kin2 chau2 chhut-lai5 ka7 sim-pu7 kong2. Keh thiN-kng, chhu3-piN se3-siok8-lang5, mng7 in cha- me5 an2-choaN2 poaN3-me5 au7 liam7 kau3 i7-o-kio3 ? Khoan2- thai7 hiah-e5 tiong7-hoan7 si7 chun2-cho3 bo5 lou7-eng7 e5 hoe3- mih8 lah. Sou2-i2 Thian-su7 ah thang kong2 si7 choat8-   bang7. M7-ku2 chiau3 in lau7-bu2 Hok-goan5 m2 e5 sin3 si7 Chu2 beh kiu3 i e5 kiaN2, an2-ni kiam2 be7 gong7 sin3 mah ? Hok-goan5 m2 e5 ki5-to2 bo5 ia3-sian7. 12.To5-kaN
Thian-su3 ah me5-jit8 ti7 kaN-lai7 cho3-kang, han5-ha7 ia7 siong5-siong5 siau3-liam2 tioh8 hit-jit8 ti7 chhia-thau5 kap lau7-bu2 bo2-kiaN2 koh sio-jin7 e5 kong-keng2. Chiu7 tiaN7- tiaN7 ka-ti7 lau5 bak8-sai2 Chia chhiaN2 an3-ha7.   
Kong2 chhu3-nih Hok-goan5 m2 e5 ki5-to2 iau2-ku2 bo5 ia3. Chai kiaN2 si7 bu5-ki5-tou5 e5 si2-hoan7; chong2-si7 i e5 sin3 bo5 pian3-oaN7, tui3 thiaN to7-li2 kau3 kin-a2-jit8, i2-keng ui7 kiaN2 me5-jit8 ki5-to2 2 ni5 goa7 lah. Kau3 kin-a2-jit8 i bo5 chit8 sut-a2 e5 leng2-sim; m7-ku2 chiau3 jin5-cheng5 lai5 siuN7, Hok-goan5 m2 e5 ki5-to2 si7 chin bo5 li2-khi3, si7 gong7- gong7 e5 ki5-to2.  
Ah! Ti7 hit ni5 e5 8 goeh8 12 jit8, si7 Thian-su3 ah jip8-kaN cho3 bo5-ki5-tou5 hoan7 e5 moa2 chit8-ni5 koe3 3 goeh8- jit8. Ti7 hit me5 u7 hui-siong5 e5 toa7 te7-tang7, si7 chha- put-to Tiong-kok iu2-su2 i2-lai5, bi7 cheng5-iu2 e5 toa7 te7- tang7. Ia7 tui3 te7-tang7 chiu7 koan5-lau5, kang-chhiuN2, hoe7-sia7 long2 to2. PoaN3-me5 au7 tui3 kui2-na7-ui7 hoat-hoe2, toa7-poaN3 si7 tui3 tian7-hoe2 soaN3 chhut-hoe2. Sou2-i2 chit8- si5 ia7 toa7 hoe2-sio. Pak-kiaN e5 kaN-gak8 ia7 to2 3 hun 2 khah ke, chhun toa7 mng5 , kap chau3-kha, chhng- khou3 bo5 to2; ki5-u5 e5 kaN-gak8 pang5 kap kaN-gak8 e5 kang- tiuN5, tai7-khai3 long2 to2, u7-e5 long2 soa3 sio-khi3.  Hit si5 Pak-kiaN e5 chhi7-lai7, long2 toa7 loan7: Sou2-i2 Khan3-siu2 che-che long2 sui-lang5 kou3 seN3-mia7 to5-chau2; u7-e5 kin2 khi3 khoaN3 ka-ki7 e5 khia7-ke.  
Hit si5 Thian-su3 ah e5 kaN-gak8 pang5 ia7 long2 to2, tai7- khai3 hoan7-lang5 ia7 long2 si2; chong2-si7 hit-si5 Thian-su3 ah bo5 si2. Hit-tiap8 i sun7 ki-hoe7 chiu7 thau to5-kaN.   Chhi7-lai7 chin loan7: sou2-i2 i tu2-tioh8 ho2 ki-hoe7 tit8-tit8 to5-chau2. Chau2 kau3 chhan5-chng tit8-tit8 khi3.  Au7-lai5 jit8 si5 bih, me5-si5 chau2. Chit pang i m7 kaN2 chau2 tui3 pat8 e5 tou-chhi7 khi3, si7 tit8-tit8 chau2 jip8 soaN. Chiu7 chau3-kau3 li7 Pak-kiaN 60 lian5 phou3 e5 chhim-soaN.   Khi3 hia cho3 soaN-chhat8. Chia chiaN2 an3-ha7. Kong2 kaN-gak8 hoe2-sio au7 keh-jit8, chiu7 toa7 tiau7- cha, chiu7 chai kaN-gak8 kiong7 liau2 kui2-na7 chap8 ban7, hoan7-lan5 to5-chau2 chin-che; chong2-si7 khah che7 lang5 si7 siuN7 Thian-su3 ah i2-keng si2: in-ui7 in tiam3 e5 gak8- pang5 long2 to2; ia7-si7 sio liau2-liau2, koh te7-tang7 poaN3- me5 au7; chong2-si7 in m7-chai hit si5 Thian-su3 ah kap tang5 lian5 cho3-hoe2 hit e5 lang5, 2 e5 kun5-thau5 long2 chin ho2, e7 poe-thiau3 chin koan5, tu2 te7-tang7 e5 si5 , chhu3 boe7 to2, in i2-keng piaN3 chhut lai5. KhoaN3 tang-lam5 kak e5 gak8 chhiuN5 u7 to2 chit7 ui7-a2, chhun-oa2 chiaN5 tng7 koan5.  Hit si5 2-e5 i2-keng thiau3 chhut chhiuN5 chau2 khi3 lah. Khan3-siu2 e5 lang5 kiaN si2 boe7 cheng7 boe7 chiu7 chau2: sou2-i2 m7-chai Thian-su3 ah in tui3 to2-khi3, ia7 kiaN kaN-gak8 tiuN2 heng5-hoat8: sou2-i2 tit8-tit8 cheng3- beng5 Thian-su3 ah si7 sio-si2 lah. Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 tai7- ke bo5 sim2-mih8 teh ti3-i3 Thian-su3 ah e5 su7. Chia chhiaN2 an3-ha7.  
Kong2 chhu3-nih Bi2-hoai5 i2-keng seng-koe3 Lu2-oh8 2 lian5, seng-lian5 e5 seng5-chek ia7 si7 chin ho2. Kin- ni5 i2-keng 17 hoe3, lai5 tui3 Hok-goan5 m2 kiu5-chhin e5  lang5 ia7-si7 chin-che7. U7-e5 si7 chin ho2-giah8, u7-e5 kiaN2- sai3-chai5 seN cho3 chin ho2; chong2-si7 Hok-goan5 m2 i e5 i3-su3 long2 bo5 teh tiong7 se3-kan: in-ui7 cheng5 in ia7 bat  ho2-giah8 koe3, sou2-i2 i chai ho2-giah8 m7-si7 thang him- soan7 e5 su7. Hok-goan5 m2 che7- chio2 ia7 si7 chai Kou-niu5 in e5 i3-kian3. Sou2-i2 lang5 lai5 cho3, long2 ka7 lang5 teh khah ban7.

13.Thou5-ham7 tiong e5 Koai3-lang5
Keh jit8 e5 e7-pou, Hok-goan5 m2 kap Bi2-hoai5 ah chiu7 chiaN5-sit8 beh khi3 sun5-bong7, lau5 sim-pu7 kou3- chhu3. 15.Hun7 to2 ti7 ho5-piN

18.Thoan5-oan5
Thian-su3 ah chhut-kaN chiu7 khi3 se2-ek8 thi3-thau5, ia7 khi3 chhai5-hong5 tiam3 be2 sin saN-a2-khou3, be2 e5, be2 bo5-a2, oaN7 kau3 chhiN-taN7-taN7, chin-chiaN3 si7 teng5-thau5-seN e5 lang5, sim ia7 chin-chiaN3 kap goa7-phoe5 sio-siang7, chiu7 tio5-tio5 thiau3-thiau3 to3-khi3.  Kau3 3 goeh8 15 jit8 chiu7 kau3 chhu3. Ah! Tai7-ke ke-lai7 chin hoaN-hi2, chit pang e5 hoaN-hi2 si7 chin e5 hoaN-hi2; cheng5-pang tui3 phu5-long7-sou2 to3-lai5 si7 chiam7-si5 e5 hoan2-hoe2; chit pang si7 chin hoan2-hoe2.  
To3-lai5 choan5-ke sin3 Chu2.  Hok-goan5 m2 kau3 chia ia7 oa2 70 hoe3 kha-tau lah.  Bak8-chiu su; chong2-si7 iau2 lau7-khong-kian7. Ma2-tan2 ah ia7 11, 2 hoe3 lah. Sio2-hak8-hau7 4 lian5 lah, chheh ia7-si7 chin gau5 thak8 koh koai. Thian-su3 ah e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ia7 chin gau5 ka3 kiaN2, jiat8-sim kho3 Chu2, ui7-tioh8 tiong7-hu siu2-chiat khiN5-ke (ai3 se3-kan khah-che7 chit ho7 hu7-jin5-lang5 leh) Kau3 kin-a2-jit8 tai7-ke thoan5-oan5, hoaN-hi2 kau3 lau5 bak8- sai2, lai5 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3. Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 koh khui tin5-i2-tiam3,`tiam3-ho7 ho7-cho3 Chai3-si-heng. Tui3 an2-ni seng-li2 iu5-goan5 tit8-tit8 chin2. Bo2 kiaN2, pe7-kiaN2, ang-bou2 chin ho5. Thian-su2 kau3 chit tiap8 toa7-toa7 hoan2-hoe2 i2-cheng5 e5 put-hau3, lam7-hui3, hong3-tong7, pin5-toaN7, tham-sim, khok-tok8 bou2-kiaN2, chhoa7 se3-i5 e5 m7-tioh8. Kau3 chia chai i e5 bou2 chin-chiaN3 si7 po2-poe3 e5 hian5 loe7- chou7 : sou2-i2 chiu7 chin thiaN3--i, chiong ka-su7 long2 kau- tai7 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 siat-hoat. I e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 ia7-si7 chin gau5 li2-ke: sou2-i2 tit8-tit8 chin2. Si5-jit8 chin khoai3 koe3, liam5-piN koe3 2,3 ni5. Hok-goan5 m2 ia7 iau2 ti7- teh, ke-lai7 chiu7 na2 chhun chiN5. Jit8-jit8 koai3-lok8 jiat8- sim hok8-sai7 Chu2.  
Chia chhiaN2 an3-ha7.Kong2 Iau7-chong ah ia7 bak8 chit8-choah8 Sin5-hak8 Tai7- hak8 ia7 chut-giap8, tu2 25,6 hoe3 na7-tiaN7. Chut-giap8 thau5-mia5, thang kong2 si7 chit8 e5 siau3-lian5 siu3-chai5. Chheng-ho7 kio3-cho3 Pak-kiaN Sin5-hak8 Tai7-hak8 Sin5 hak8-su7. Chut-giap8 hit-jit8 u7 soa3 kiat-hun. Au7-lai5 chiu7 si7 Pak-kiaN Chim3-le2-hoe7 e5 bok8-su khoaN3 liau2 kam2-sim chiong i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 ke3-i, i e5 cha-bou2-kiaN2 iu5-goan5 seN3 Tan5, mia5 kio3 Siu3-hian5.  Kin-ni5 23 hoe3, si7 ti7 Lu2- chu2 ko-teng2 Su-hoan7 chhut-sin. SeN cho3 ia7 si7 chin ho2, chin jiat8-sim, thang kong2 iu5-goan5 si7 chin hap8 lou7- eng7 e5 loe7-chou7. Tui3 an2-ni kiat-hun au7, chiu7 siu7 Thoan5- to7-hoe7 phai3 khi3 pang-chan7 loe7-te-hoe7 thoan5 kau3, ti7 lai7 Bong5-kou2. Khah iau3-kin2 si7 ai3 in khi3 hia siat Seng3-to7-oh8, ti7 hia thang poe5-iuN2 pun2-to2 lang5 lai5 cho3 hia e5 thoan5-to7.  

Chai3 kong2 kau3 Thian-su3 ah thiaN i e5 moai7-sai3 siu7 phai3 beh khi3 thoan5-to7, i ia7 chin hoaN-hi2, Iau7-chong ah ia7 si7 u7 chhoa7 i e5 hu7-jin5-lang5 Siu3-hian5 lai5 kap cheng5 e5 tiuN7-m2 sio-jin7, tai7-ke chin hoaN-hi2.  Hok- goan5 m2 ia7 thiaN3 i cho3 chhin-chhiuN7 Bi2-hoai5 ah koh- oah8. Siu3-hian5 khoaN3 Hok-goan5 m2 e5 jiat8-sim kap thiaN3-sim, chiu7 ia7 chun-keng3 i cho3 chhin chhiuN7 chin- sit8 e5 lau7-bo2. Thian-su3 ah siu7 moai7-sai3 e5 kam2-tong7, chiu7 tiam3 siu-siu khi2-lai5, ia7 goan7 chan7 moai7-sai3 jip8 soaN khi3 Bong5-kou2 thoan5-to7, i sui-bong2 bo5 thak8 Sin5- hak8; iau2-ku2 tui3 i e5 jiat8-sim, kap sou2 keng-koe3 e5 toa7 hoan2-hoe2, sou2 kong2 e5 pou3-to7 toa7 e7 kam2-tong7 lang5, sou2-i2 Iau-chong SinseN toa7 hoaN-hi2, chiu7 tai7- ke saN chhoa7, bo5 koh iuN7 chit8 ke e5 lang5, saN-thiaN3 bo5 khah su kong2 chhin-chhiuN chhoa7 Bi2-hoai5 ah. Tui3 an2-ni chiu7 tai7-ke chhut-hoat, chit8 ke kiong7 6 lang5 jip8 Bong5-kou2 khi3 thoan5-to7, hiong2-siu7 khoai3-lok8 toa7 thoan5-oan5 e5 ka-teng5, koh kui eng5-kng Siong7- te3 bo5 chin7.
