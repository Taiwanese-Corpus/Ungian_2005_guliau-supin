Ⅰ. Hong2-mng7
“Bok8-su peng5-an”.  “Oan5-che2 lai5 che7”.  Bok8-su tui3 au7-bin7 chheng7 chit8- nia2 khun3-saN, chhiu2 giah8 khi2-bin2, bin7 iau2 tai3 ai3-khun3-sin5, chiu7 tui3 chau3-kha chhut-lai5.  Cha2-khi2 goa7-bin7 long2 ta3-bu7, thiN-khi3 chin leng2.  Bok8-su sou2 chheng7 e5 saN chin kau7; chin koaN5 chhiu2 kiu-kiu. Chiu7 tui3 Oan5-che2 kong2, “Oan5-che2 li2 cha2-khi2 thai3 chiah gau5 cha2.”  ”Bo5 gau5 cha2 ne7 !  I2-keng 8 tiam2 goa7 lah”.  “Hou3 ! Goa2 cha2-khi2 chin oaN3 khi2-lai5.  Si7 in-ui7 goa2 cha-am3 chin sian7, khi3 pou3-to7 kiaN5 chin hng7, ho2 ki-hoe7 soa3 siuN am3 soah. To3-lai5 i2-keng chin am3, sou2-i2 cha2-khi2 chiah ban7 khi2-lai5; si3-ke3 long2 ta3-bu7, goa2 liah8-chun2 iau2 cha2 leh.” 
 “Hou3 !”  Si7 an2-ni ouh8 !  Chin kiau2-jiau2, chin kiau2-jiau2.”  “Be7, be7.”  “Bok8-su, ki5-sit8 goa2 si7 u7 chit8 hang7 iau3-kin2 e5 su7 ai3 pai3-thok li2, ia7 chin bo5 si5-kan thang lai5 chhoe7 li2, chiah sun7 chit e5 beh khi3 chhi7- tiuN5 be2 chhai3 e5 si5-kan oan tui3 li2 chia lai5.”  “Si7 an2-ni ouh8, chhiaN2 li2 kong2, si7 sim2-mih8 iau3-kin2 e5 su7.”  ‘M7-si7 goa2 ka-ti7 e5 su7, si7 goan2 keh-piah e5 su7.”  “Keh-piah sim2-mih8 lang5 an3-choaN2 ?”    
“Ti7 chit cheN2-kha kong2 khah phaiN2- se3, lai5 khi3 lai7-bin7 goa2 chiah ka7 li2 kong2.” Tu2 teh kong2 e5 si5, Bok8-su-niu5 chhiu2 phang5 chit8 te3 tau7-hu7 jip8-lai5 chiu7 iah8 kap Oan5-che2 saN-chioh-mng7 chheng2-an. Tui3 an2-ni 3 e5 chiu7 saN chhoa7 jip8-khi3 kheh-thiaN che7.  Bok8-su-niu5 chiu7 kong2, “Oan5-che2, li2 thau5-tu2-a2 kong2, lin2 keh- piah si7 sim2-mih8 lang5 an2-choaN2 ?    
“Goan2 keh-piah chit8 e5 chi2-moe7 kio3- cho3 Teng-hu7-so2, goa2 chin ai3 Bok8-su li2 khi3 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i thiaN.”  “An2-ni nouh3, i si7 an3-choaN2 ?”  “Chit e5 Teng-hu5-so2 in tiong7-hu si7 ti7 .............sou2-chai7 teh cho3 kam3-tok, chin u7 te7-ui7 e5 lang5.  Na7-si7 ke-lai7 chin be7 ho5, cha-am3 nng7 e5 ang-bou2 chiah saN-phah, phah kau3 toa7-khang se3- lih8, kiaN3-tai5 siak-siak phoa3, tiu5-saN li3-li3 phoa3, gin2-na2 toa7-lang5 khau3 be-be.” “He si7 chaiN2-iuN7”  Bok8-su kap Bok8-su- niu5 chit8 e7 thiaN Oan5-che2 kong2 an2-ni, chit8 sim the3 Teng-hu5-so2 kho2-lin5, chit8 sim houN3-ki5 chin ai3 chai.  Nng7 e5 bak8- chiu liah8 Oan5-che2 kim-kim siong3.  Oan5- che2 ia7 chin iau3-i3 e5 khoan2-sit, chiu7 chiap- soa3 koh kong2. 
“ThiaN-kiN3 kong2, peng5-seng Teng-hu5- hiaN chin gau5 khai-hoa7, ia7 cha-hng kap peng5-iu2 chio khi3 Chui3-goat8-lau5 lim. Na7-si7 Teng-hu5-so2 chin gau5 ka7 i tng, cha-hng kau3 11 tiam2 goa7 bo5 to3-lai5, i chiu7 koh khi3 ka7 i chhoe7, chhoe7 kui2-na7 keng, chhoe7 bo5, chhoe7 kau3 Chui3-goat8- lau5 tu2 gu7-tioh8 i e5 tiong7-hu ti7-hia.  Chit8- si5 chhou3 hoe2 toa7 toh8, chiu7 tng-tiuN5 ti7 chiu2-lau5 oan chit8-khun3.  Tai7-ke khoaN3-liau2 kiok8-bin7 chin bai2, peng5-iu2 chiah khou2-khng3 Teng-hu5-hiaN kap i to3-khi3. Kau3 chhu3 to hoan poaN3-me5 au7, tiam2 goa7 lah.  To3-lai5 kau3-te3 kiu3-kiu3 jiong2, chit8-e5 chhoh, chit8-e5 le2, sio-me7 chit8-khun3; goan2 chhu3-piN long2 hou7 i chha2 cheng-sin5.  Bo5 phah-sng3 theng5 chit8 e7-a2 soa3 pin3 piang3 kio3, kiaN3 siak-siak phoa3, phah kau3 ai-ai kio3.  Teng-hu5-so2 ka-ti7 bo5 siN, pun chit8 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2, kin-ni5 tu2 7 hoe3, ia7 khau3 be-be.  Chit8-si5 ke-lai7 loan5 kun2- kun2.  Kan-ta ti7 khau3 be-be e5 siaN-tiong thiaN chit8-ku3, “kin-a2-jit8 lai5 kau3 chia goa2 goan7 si2 phoe3 li2 lah” e5 siaN.  Hit e5 siaN si7 chin bou5 lat8, hu7-jin5-lang5 e5 siaN.  Hit-si5 goa2 chiah kio3 goa2 e5 tiong7-hu khi2-lai5, ka7 i kong2, phah-sng3 lan2 tioh8  
lai5 khi3 ka7 i chiam3.  Khi-thau5 goan2 si7 siuN7 kong2 ang-bou2 tai7, chhui3 kap chih8 eng2 e7 sio- gai7; chhng5-thau5 phah, chhng5-boe2 chiu7 ho5 lah.  M7-ai3 khi2-lai5 ka7 in chiam3, an3- sng3 lang5 e5 ke-lai7 tai7, u7-si5 tioh8-lat8 koh m7-ho2 khoaN3.  Chong2-si7 thiaN in phah kau3 an2-ni pin3 piang3 kio3, koh khau3 kau3 be-be kio3, sim ia7 put-jim2 chiah khi3 ka7 in chiam3.  Khi3 kau3 ui7, goa2 e5 tiong7-hu chiah tui3 Teng-hu-hiaN ka7 i lam2 chau2, goa2 chiah tui3 Teng-hu5-so2 ka7 i giu2 khui. Teng-hu5-so2 thau5-hiah8 chit8 khang, tai7- khai3 si7 khi3 hou7 Teng-hu5-hiaN eng7 kiaN3 sai-tioh8.  Teng-hu5-hiaN chhiu2-kut chit8 khang, khoaN3 si7 hou7 Teng-hu5-so2 ka7-- tioh8.  Nng7 e5 iau2 chhiauh-chhiauh jiong2; i ia7 kong2 beh phoe3--i, i ia7 kong2 beh phoe3--i.  Goan2 ka7 i chiam3 khui, hoan7 iau2 si7 kui2 pai2 jih8 oa2-khi3 beh koh saN phah. Chit8 e5 iu7 gau5 chhoh, kiau7; chit8 e5 li7 gau5 le2, khut8.  Ah !  Ka-teng5, sim-lai7 bo5 to7-li2 e5 kng, chin-chiaN3 oan2-ju5 oah8 te7-gek8. Goa2 kap goan2 gin2-na2 in a-pa nng7-e5 kap i tiam3 hia tiN5 kui-me5, kau3 thiN beh kng lah, chiah kong2, taN thiN-kng lah, be7 khoaN3-tit, e7 hou7 lang5 chhio3, chiah soah kou2; goan2 chiah to3-khi3.  Be7 su poaN chit8 piN5 be7 hian7-si3 e5 toa7-hi3; m7-si7 kong2 m7-bat oan ne7, si7 u7 lang3 jit8 bo5 lang3 le2- pai3--e5, si7 cha-me5 tek8-piat8 toa7 poaN.  Sou2-i2 goan2 chiah u7 koe3-khi3 ka7 i chiam3.    
Bok8-su li2 siuN7 an2-choaN2 ?  Li2 khi3 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 in thiaN ho2-bo5 ?”  “Ho2 nouh ! chhin3-chhai2 lang5 lan2 ma7 ai3 kong2 hou7 in thiaN.”  Chong2-si7 Bok8-su-niu5 chin houN3- ki5, chiu7 koh tui3 Oan5-che2 kong2, “Oan5- che2, iau2 cha2-khi2 taN an2-choaN2 ?”  “Cha2- khi2 to bo5 thiaN-kiN3 teh koh jiang2, ia7 bo5 thiaN-kiN3 khi2-lai5 chu2-png7, ia7 goa7 cha2 Teng-hu5-hiaN chiu7 cho3 i chhut-khi3-lah.”
“Ah !  Bo5 to7-li2 sit8-chai7 chin kho2- sioh.”  Bok8-su chiap Bok8-su-niu5 e5 oe7- kong2 “Oan5-che2, li2 peng5-si5 bat kong2 to7- li2 hou7 in thiaN a2-bo5 ?”  “Han2-tit ne7, Teng-hu5-hiaN thau3-cha2 chhut-khi3 kau3 am3-am3 to3-lai5; koh u7-si5 am3-si5 tong-tit8, goan2 chin han2-tit tu2-tioh8 i.  Na7 tu2-tioh8 si7 kap i saN chioh mng7 na7-tiaN7, koh chin oh chhin-kin7; m7-chai si7 cho3 kam3-tok e5 te7-ui7 a2 m7-si7.  Peng5-si5 bin7 han2-tit chhio3, na7 bo5 seng mng7--i, i m7-bat beh mng7 lang5--e5, sou2-i2 goa2 han2-tit kap i kong2-oe7. Ia7 na7-si7  
Teng-hu5-so2 tu2-tng7 u7-si5-a2, goa2 bat kong2 tam7-poh8 hou7 i thiaN.  He si7 bo5 hiah khoai3, si7 hui-siong5 e5 be5-sin3, chhu2-cheng5 au7-piah to he7, to pai3.  Lan2 chia e5 bio7 pai3 bo5 kau3, ku7-ni5 chiah khi3 Pak-kang2 Tiau5-thian-kiong Ma2- chou2-po5 hia koah-hiuN, kin-ni5 khi3 Sin- tek he7 Seng5-hong5.  ThiaN-kiN3 kong2, Sin-tek Seng5-hong5-bio7 hou7 Boan2-chiu e5 cheng5 goa7-kau tai7-sin5 Sia7 Kai3-sek8 lai5 su7-pian2, kong2 goa7 heng leh, chiu7 khi3 he7.  Chiau3 goa2 khoaN3 hit e5 lang5 si7 chin oh, khoaN3 Bok8-su li2 khi3 kong2 hou7 i thiaN u7 pian3 a2-bo5.”   
“Bok8-su ma7-si7 bo5 pan7-hoat, tu5-khi3 Siong7-te3 e5 sin5 beh kiu3--i i2-goa7.”    
“TaN si5-kan chin oaN3 lah, Bok8-su li2 chhiaN2, goa2 ai3 kin2 lai5 khi3 chhi7-tiuN5. Chong2-si7 chhiaN2 Bok8-su li2 iau3-i3 khi3 kong2 to7-li2 hou7 i thiaN he3.”  “Ho2, kiam2-chhai2 e7-pou goa2 na7 u7 ki-hoe7 chiah kap Bok8- su-niu5 lai5 khi3 chhoe7 Teng-hu5-so2 che7, chong2-si7 goa2 m7-bat kap i siong-hoe7.” “Bo5 iau3-kin2, li2 na7 lai5-khi3 goa2 chiah ka7 li2 siau7-kai3.”  “Ho2, ho2, an2-ni e7-pou goan2 chiah lai5-khi3.”
“Bok8-su, Bok8-su-niu5 taN lin2 chhiaN2.” “Chiah koh lai5 che7, e7-pou goan2 chiah lai5 khi3 chhoe7-li2.”  “Ho2.”  Tui3 an2-ni Oan5- che2 kap Bok8-su in saN-si5 kin2-kin2 khi3 chhi7-tiuN5, kau3 e7-pou Bok8-su kap Bok8- su-niu5 ko2-jian5 saN-chhoa7 khi3 chhoe7 Oan5- che2 kap i khi3 Teng-hu5-so2 in tau.    
“Teng-hu5-so2 chhiaN2 li2 khui-mng5”, Teng-hu5-so2 thiaN-kiN3 Oan5-che2 e5 siaN chiu7 chhut-lai5, thau5-khak pau pheng5- toa3, sio2-khoa2 u7 hoeh ang5-ang5 tou3 chhut- lai5 ti7 goa7-bin7.  Chhin-chhiuN7 chin bo5-i3 phaiN2-se3 e5 khoan2-sit, in-ui7 tui3 lai7-bin7 thiaN goan2 e5 siaN, koh tui3 sam e5 li5-pa- phang7 u7 khoaN3 goan2 ti7 goa7-bin7 teh khia7. Kau3 khui sam li5-pa e5 toa7-mng5 e5 si5, goan2 chiu7 ka7 i tim3-thau5; i liah8 goan2 kim- kim siong3, in-ui7 tai7-ke m7-bat siong-hoe7.    
Oan5-che2 chiu7 ka7 i siau7-kai3 kong2, “Teng-hu5-so2, chit e5 chiu7-si7 goan2 kau3- hoe7 e5 bok8-su, chit e5 chiu7-si7 bok8-su- niu5.”  Teng-hu5-so2 e5 bin7 bo5 hoaN-hi2 e5 khoan2,  
chit8 toa7-ku3 chiu7 in3 kong2 “chiah8 kau3 sian-siN tioh8-m3?”  Mng7 an2-ni bo5 khoa3 theng3-hau7 Oan5-che2 ka7 i in3, chu7 an2-ni oat tui3 lai7-bin7 jip8-khi3, ia7 liam5-piN koh chhut-lai5, chhiu2 me chit8-lak8 peh8- peh8 e5 mih8, chhui3 phui3-phui3 kio3 kong2, “soe, soe; phui2-phui2, Ia5-sou Siong7-te3 phui2-phui2.”  Chiong chhiu2-nih8 sou2 theh8 peh8-peh8 e5 mih8, tit8-tit8 ia7-lai5.  Chit-si5 Bok8-su in hu-hu7 kap Oan5-che2 3 e5 soa3 gang7-gang7.    
2. It8-to soat-hin7(一刀雪恨)
 Khi2-thau5 Bok8-su, Bok8-su-niu5 khoaN3 i tit8-tit8 ia7--lai5, liah8 chun2 i teh ia7 sim2-mih8 leh.  Na7-si7 thiaN i phui3-phui3 kio3 chiah siong5-se3 ka7 i khoaN3, to si7 iam5 kap bi2. Chiah chai in chit8-hang5 saN e5, si7 khi3 hou7 siak iam5-bi2.  SiuN7 laiu2 ia7 ho2 khi3, ia7 ho2 chhio3, ia7 kho2-lin5.  Oan5-che2 chit8 si5 soa3 gang7-gang7, sim-lai7 am3-am3 teh siuN7 chit e5 lang5 si7 siong-tong e5 te7-ui7 e5 hu7-jin5- lang5, ia7 thak8 koe3 ko-lu2, an2-choaN2 kin-a2- jit8 e7 an2-ni.  Tui3 Bok8-su kap Bok8-su-niu5 chin phaiN2-se3, soa3 tai7-ke chit8-si5 sio-khoaN3, tai7-ke long2 gang7-gang7 bo5 oe7.    
Teng-hu5-so2 kiau-thau5 koh ban2-lan5 e5 khoan2-sit, bin7 chhau3-chhau3, huiN-leh cho3 i jip8-khi3.  ThiaN mng5 soa3 koaiN--khi3.    
Chit8 si5 Bok8-su siuN7-liau2 soa3 ai3-chhio3, siuN7 kong2 taN thai3 soa3 an2-ni.  Oan5-che2 kap Bok8-su-niu5 teh lau5 bak8-sai2, sim chin kan-khou2, Bok8-su chiu7 kong2, 
“Lan2 tiam3 chia khia7 lai5 the3 i ki5-to2 kui2 ku3, bang7 Chu2 khui lou7 kiu3 i, chiah to3-lai5.” Tai7-ke chiu7 chhih-chhih khia7 ti7 in toa7- mng5 e5 lai7-bin7 piN ki5-to2.  SaN lang5 ti7 hia ki5- to2 liau2-au7, Bok8-su, Bok8-su-niu5 kap Oan5- che2 saN-si5 to3-khi3 pai3-tng5.  Oan5-che2 chin m7-koe3-sim, siuN7 kong2 i kin-a2-jit8 thiau- kang chhoa7 Bok8-su kap Bok8-su-niu5 lai5 hou7 lang5 chau-that.  Na7-si7 Bok8-su in hu- hu7 chin hoaN-hi2, bo5 liah8 cho3 i3-su3, kek8- lek8 an-ui3 Oan5-che2 e5 sim.  Oan5-che2 am3- am3 siuN7 kong2, ui7 Chu2 e5 mia5 siu7 lang5 hui2-pong3, siat-tok8, phui3-noa7 phah-sng3 si7 chit e5 khoan2-sit.  Chit8 sim siuN7 kong2, Chu2 kong2, ui7-tioh8 I e5 mia5 lai5 siu7 khun2- tiok8, thi2-chhio3, le2-me7 e5 u7 hok-khi3, in-ui7 thian-kok si7 i e5.  Na7 siuN7 an2-ni, sim chiu7 siu7 an-ui3, siuN7 kong2 it-seng ia7 si7 kin-a2-jit8 thau5-pai2 siu7 chit ho7 leng5-jiok8, lai5 kap Chu2 tam-iu, chit8-si5 sim na2-chun2 chin ho2-taN2, chin hoaN-hi2, in-ui7 chai u7 kap Chu2 saN-kap u7 hun7 ti7 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 kan-khou2.  Chong2-si7 koh chit8-bin7 siuN7 hit-si5 e5 cheng5-keng2, chit8-ku3 ia7 phui2, nng7-ku3 ia7 phui2, ia7 lian5 kio3 Chu2 e5 seng3 mia5 lai5 phui3.  SiuN7-liau2 ui7 Chu2 chin m7- goan7.  SiuN7 to3-tng2, khiok si7 in-ui7 i m7- bat.  Koh siuN7-tioh8 Teng-hu5-so2 in e5 ka- teng5 e5 chham2-keng2, chiu7 seN-khi2 kho2-lin5 sia3-bian2--i e5 sim.    
Chong2-si7 koh chit8-bin7 chin m7-goan7 Mou5-kui2 e5 cho3-kang.  Oan2-jian5 Chu2 e5 lou5-pok8 u7 thai5-su siu7 leng5-jiok8 e5 khoan2- sit8.  Sou2-i2 sim chin kan-khou2, kha-pou7 chin tang7, kap Bok8-su saN-si5 chiu7 thau5 chhih-chhih chin loe2-chi3 ia7 oat tui3 i e5 tiaN5 jip8-khi3.    
Bok8-su in hu-hu7 kap Oan5-che2 saN-si5 to3-khi3.  Hit am3 ka-teng5 le2-pai3 tu2-gu7 u7 pat8 e5 hiaN-che2 lai5 saN chhoe7 che7, chiu7 tai7-ke ui7 chit chan5 su7 lai5 ki5-to2.    
Keh kui2 jit8 au7, Bok8-su ka-ti7 chit8 lang5 koh khi3 hong2-mng7 Teng-hu5-so2.  Chit- pai2 bo5 tui3 toa7-mng5, si7 tui3 au7-bin7 tiaN5 e5 sio2-mng5 khi3. 3. To5-chau2
Teng-hu5-so2 thai5 Teng-hu5-hiaN.  ThiaN-kiN3 Teng-hu5-hiaN hoah kiu3-lang5, sim7-chi3 khoaN3 hoeh chim-chim lau5, thiN koh beh kng lah.  Ia7 kiaN ka-ti7 e5 lat8 tu3 Teng-hu5 hiaN be7 tiau5, sou2-i2 put-koan2 i saN-chhit ji7-chap8-it, saN-pah lak8-chap8-ke3 chau2 ui5 te7-it.  Lian5-bang5 chit8 khun3 piaN3 kau3 Chhoan-siong7 Kong-hak8-hau7 cheng5 chiah theng5-kha.  Lian5 kaN2 oat8-thau5 to m7-kaN2, in-ui7 kiaN-liau2 lang5 tui-lai5.  Chit8 si5 sim ia7 khi3, ia7 kiaN, khoaN3-kiN3 hoeh tit8- tit8 lau5 chhut--lai5, tau3-ti2 hit-si5 i si7 ka7 Teng-hu5 hiaN thai5 goa7-toa7 khang i ma7 m7-chai.  Teng-hu5 hiaN lim-chiu2 lim kau3 chui3-chui3, thiN beh kng tng ho2-khun3. Khun3 than2-khi-sin, bin7 ng3 ti7 piah hit peng5, bo5-hai7-si3 chiah e7 khi3 ho7 i thai5- tioh8.  Na7-si7 Teng-hu5-sou2 i chu7 cha-hng beh am3, khoaN3 Teng-hu5-hiaN to3-lai5, i chiu7 u7 chhun5-pan7 leh.  Sou2-i2 chiah e7 oaN7 kau3 hiah cheng2 che5, koh un sio-chiu2 hou7 Teng-hu5-hiaN lim, Teng-hu5-hiaN iu7 m7- chai si2-jit8 beh kau3 lah, tit8 lim.  Kai- chai3 hit ki thi3-thau5-to bo5 kau3 goa7 lai7. Hit ki thi3-thau5-to Teng-hu5-so2 beh chau2 e5 si5 hiat-kat8 ti7 au7-bin7 hoe-hng5 e5 lian5- chiau-hoe chin am7 e5 sou2-chai7, kau3 au7-lai5 chiah chai-iaN2.    
Na7-si7 hit cha2-khi2 thiN iau2 chin am3, sou2-i2 i chau2 kau3 Chhoan-siong7 Kong- hak8-hau7 cheng5, long2 bo5 sim2-mih8 tu2-tioh8 lang5, chi2-u7 gu7-tioh8 kui2 e5 chhan5-chng- lang5, tui3 hiuN-e7 taN chhai3 beh lai5 hu3 chhi7-tiuN5 e5 chhai3-hi-chhi7 hou7 lang5 koah e5 na7-tiaN7.  Si5 iau2 chin cha2, taN3 koh tang7, thau5-khak chhih-chhih tit8-tit8 kiaN5, poaN3- kau2-chau2, sou2-i2 bo5 teh ka7 i ti3-i3 chit e5 cha-bou2-lang5 si7 teh chau2 chaiN2-iuN7.  M7-ku2 Teng-hu5-so2 na7 kian3 gu7-tioh8 chit8 e5 lang5-iaN2, a2-si7 chit8 chiah kau2 kap i saN- tng7-thau5 i to chin kiaN.  I to liam5-piN liau5-siong2 siuN7 kong2 m7-chai si7 keng2-chhat tui kau3 lah, a2-si7 hui-siong5 keng2-soaN3 teh ui5 i, kau2 poh-kaN2 si7 keng2-chhat-su7 e5 keng2-chhat  
choan-iong7-kau2, sou2-i2 lian5 tu2-tioh8 kau2 i to kiaN. Na7-si7 chau2 lai5 kau3 Chhoan-siong7 kong-hak8-hau7 cheng5, oat8-thau5 chai bo5 lang5 teh tui i, chiah an-sim ti7 hia theng5-khun3.    
Bian2 kui2 hun cheng beh khi3 Chheng- chui2 hai2-hoaN7-soaN3 e5 ba-suh lai5, i chiu7 peh khi3 che7.   
Chhia-lai7 chin chio2 lang5, i e5 sim chin kiaN, chit8 sim hoan5-lo2 kong2 chhia-chiang2, un7-choan2-chhiu2 m7-chai chai i thai5-lang5 a2 bo5 ?  Ia7 chiah e5 lang5-kheh m7-chai u7 thiaN lang5 kong2 bou2-sou2-chai7 u7 bou2 thai5- si2 ang--e5 a2 bo5 ?  Ki5-sit8 su7 beh thai2 u7 hiah kin2 chai, m7-ku2 i e5 sim chin put-an. Koh chit8 sim siuN7 kong2 cha2-khi2 e5 chu7- tong7-chhia thai2-e7 chiah ban7, thai2 m7 sai2 khah kin2, ia7 tak8 pai2 na7 theng5 chhia teh hou7 lang5-kheh chiuN7-loh8, i e5 sim chin peh-pak, bak8-chiu chin chheN-kiaN e5 khoan2.  I siang7 hoan5-lo2 keng2-chhat, a2- si7 heng5-su7 khi2-lai5 che7 chhia, sou2-i2 kiaN kau3 bin7 se3-se3 e5, che7 ti7 siang7 au7-bin7 e5 kak-a2, chhia-lai7 e5 lang5 khoaN3 chit e5 hu7- jin5-lang5 seN cho3 to be7 bai2, taN2-pan7 hiah chheng-chho2, khoaN3 m7-si7 peng5-siong5 ka- teng5, a2-si7 hiuN-e7 e5 chhan5-chng-lang5, che7 chhia thai3-e7 hiah chheN-kiaN e5 khoan2. Sou2-i2 tak8 e5 chin ai3 ka7 i khoaN3.  Lang5 na2 khoaN3--i, i na2 kiaN, soa3 chhin-chhiuN7 gong7-sin5, gong7-sin5 e5 khoan2, chong2-si7 bo5 lang5 mng7--i.    
Kau3 Chheng-chui2 tai7-ke loh8 chhia, i iau2 teh che7.  I e5 toaN si7 thiah kau3 Ko-bi2 hai2-chui2-ek8-tiuN5.  I e5 sim-te2 m7-si7 beh khi3 se2 hai2-chui2-ek8, si7 beh khi3 thiau3 hai2 chu7- chin7, in-ui7 hit-si5 si7 2 goeh8 boe2, chin koaN5.

Chhia-chiang2 chiah ka7 i kong2, sim2-mih8 so2, li2 beh khi3 Ko-bi2 tioh8 oaN7 chhia, chhiaN2 li2 loh8 chhia, tui3 an2-ni i chiah loh8 chhia, na7-si7 chit8-ki tiu5 hou7-soaN3 be7 ki3-tit8 giah8, chu7 an2-ni pang3 tiam3 chhia-lai7, chhia-chiang2 ka7 i kio3, chiah giah8 heng5 i, put-ko2 hit-si5 i si7 gong5-gong5.

“KiN3-na7 kan-khou2 poe7 tang7-taN3 e5 tioh8 chiu7-kin7 Goa2” (goa2 chi2 Ia5-sou). In leng7-chun si7 the3-jim7 thoan5- to7, sou2-i2 chhu3 bo5 tiam3 ti7 pai3-tng5, si7 ti7 pat8-ui7, m7-ku2 kheh-thiaN u7 tiau3 Ia5-sou teng3 sip8-ji7-ke3 e5 siong7. TaN i choe3-chheh chiu7-si7 loh8-kau3--e5, bo5 phah-sng3 tng ti7 khou2-chham2 e5 jit8 tui3 i siang7 chhin-chhiat e5 lang5 koh si7 loh8- kau3--e5.  I m7-ai3 kiN3 loh8-kau3 lang5 e5 bin7, bo5 phah-sng3 koh soa3 lai5 loh8-kau3 lang5 e5 chhu3.  Sou2-i2 chit8-si5 kam2-kak chin kian3- siau3, chin phaiN2-se3.  In-ui7 i ka-ti7 sim-lai7 liam5-piN siuN7-tioh8 cheng5-jit8 ka7 Bok8-su siak iam5-bi2, koh Bok8-su toaN-sin khi3 chhoe7 i, i m7 chhap Bok8-su, liah8 Bok8-su chun2-cho3 kau2 e5 bo5 le2, chiu7 sim chin kian3-siau3, che7 long2 be7 tiau5, chin ai3 kin2 koh chhut-khi3.    
Koh sim siang7 kan-khou2--e5, chiu7-si7 khoaN3 hit pak nng7 ang-bou2 kap chit8 e5 se3-kiaN2 thoan5-oan5 beh chiah8-png7 teh kam2-sia7 e5 ang-a2.  SiuN7 kong2 pat8 lang5 e5 ka-teng5 thai3 e7 haih ho5-peng5, na7-si7 ka- ti7 e5 ka-teng5 thai3 e7 hiah loan7, chhin- chhiuN7 oah8-te7-gek8 e5 kan-khou2. Theng5 tiap-a2-ku2 lai7-bin7 phang5 gu5-leng kap theh8 tam7-poh8 e5 ke-nng7-ko chhut-lai5, chin in-khin5 chhiaN2 i eng7, chit8 si5 sim toa7 siu7 kam2-kek.  Koh chit8-bin7 lau2-sit8 kong2 ia7 si7 iau, tui3 an2-ni bo5 se3-ji7 chiu7 eng7 gu5-leng, chong2-si7 sim kan-khou2 chiu7 chit8-bin7 ia7 si7 chiah8 be7 loh8 khi3.  Sou2-i2 ke-nng7-ko bo5 chiah8 chu7 an2-ni khng3 leh.    
Tui3 an2-ni tai7-ke chin ho2-i3 saN-kap ti7 kheh-thiaN che7.  Thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 si7 chin chhut-mia5 e5 san2-po5, chin jin5-jia2 chu5-pi e5 khoan2-sit.  Teng-hu5-so2 khoaN3 chit e5 ka-teng5 e5 khong-khi3 oan5-choan5 siu7 kam2-kek, am3-am3 teh siuN7 ka-ti7 e5 ka- teng5 na7 e7 chhin-chhiuN7 an2-ni m7 chin ho2. Chong2-si7 siuN7 to3-tng2, che si7 loh8-kau3--e5 beh si2.  Liam5-piN chiu7 koh siuN7 lang5 teh keng-the2 “loh8-kau3 si2-liau2 bo5 lang5 khau3,” ia7 siuN7-tioh8 Lam5-iau5-kiong chit8 e5 goan7 boe7 sia7, beh an2-choaN2 kap i hun7-kau3.  Ia7 siuN7-tioh8 chin m7-goan7, beh khi3 Seng5- hong5-bio7, chhiaN3 bio7-cheng5 hit e5 hiuN- tiam3, kiam cho3 Seng5-hong5-hu2 e5 tai7-su- jin5, beh chhiaN3 i cho3 chit8-tiaN5 pin2 lai5 ko3 in Teng-hu5-hiaN.  Beh tui3 Seng5-hong5 ko3, kio3 Seng5-hong5 lai5 liah8 in Teng-hu5 -a2 khi3 gia5-ke5, i si2-bak8 chiah goan7 kheh. Sim chap8-e5 gou7-e5, lang5 sui-jian5 tiam3 thoan5-to7-su e5 tau che7, na7-si7 sim teh siuN7 hiah e5 su7.  Sou2-i2 chhiu2 tiaN7 loah8 sim-koaN thou2 toa7-khui3, u7-si5 chiu7 siuN7-tioh8 thiN beh kng thai5 in tiong7-hu e5 cheng5-heng5, bin7 chiu7 sit-sek chin kiaN e5 khoan2, chong2- si7 kiaN lang5 chai i e5 sim.    
Na7-si7 thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 kap leng7 chhian-kim long2 m7-chai Teng-hu5-so2 sim teh siuN7 sim2-mih8, chong2-si7 it-bi7 kong2 to7-li2, khou2-khng3 i.  KhoaN3 i long2 bo5 siu7 an-ui3 e5 khoan2, chiu7 siuN7 tioh8 thak8 Seng3- keng hou7 i thiaN, chiu7 hian Ma2-thai3 11: 28 chat.      
“Lin2 kiN3-na7 tioh8-boa5 taN tang7-taN3 e5, tioh8 chiu7-kin7 goa2, goa2 beh hou7 lin2 an- hioh” (Goa2 beh siuN2-su3 lin2 peng5-an).    
Teng-hu5-so2 chit8-e7 thiaN-tioh8 chit ku3 hou7 i peng5-an, beh the3 i taN tang7-taN3,  
chit8-si5 sim-koaN hoang3 chit8--e7, chek-si5 bin7 tng2 chhio3-iong5, mng7 kong2 sim2-mih8 lang5 e7 ka7 i taN tang7-taN3 ?  Ia7 se3-kan kiam2 chin-chiaN3 u7 hit ho7 e7 su3 lang5 peng5- an e5 lang5 ?  Thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 kap leng7 chhian-kim chiah cheng5 thau5 it-li2 it-gou7 it-chap8, chiong Ia5-sou e5 to7-li2 koh kong2 chit8 pian3 hou7 i thiaN.  Kau3 hit-si5 Teng-hu5-so2 chiah khui-siaN hau2, chiong i e5 khou2-cheng5 kong2 hou7 in thiaN, ia7 ka7 in kong2 cha2-khi2 i si7 beh khi3 Ko-bi2 thiau3-hai2.  Hai7 thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 in bo2-kiaN2 thiaN chit8-e7 tioh8 toa7 kiaN.  Tai7- ke chiah ti7-hia kui7-loh8 lau5 bak8-sai2 lai5 kam2-sia7 Chu2.  Ia7 in-khin5 chhiaN2 Teng- hu5-su2 tioh8 sin3 Ia5-sou.
4. Hi2-le7(喜淚)
SaN lang5 tai7-ke kui7-loh8 lau5 bak8-sai2 ki5-to2.  Ki5-to2 soah si5 i2-keng oaN3, in chiu7 lau5 i ti7-hia keh e7-tau3.  Hit e7-tau3 sian-siN-niu5 eng7 chin ho2-i3 lai5 chiap-thai7 i, sian-siN chhut-khi3 chhoe7--lang5 ia7 to3--lai5, se3-kiaN2 kui2-na7 e5 khi3 thak8 Kong-hak8-hau7 ia7 to3-lai5.  Tai7-ke chin u7 le2-sou3, chin u7 toa7-se3, beh chiah8-png7 long2 ki5-to2 kam2- sia7, chit8 e5 ka-teng5 oan2-jian5 lok8-hng5, oan2- jian5 thian-tong5 ti7 se3-kan sit8-hian7-hoa3. Hit-si5 Teng-hu5 so2 moa2-sim kam2-kek, chhim-chhim him-sian7, chaiN2-iuN7 pai3 Siong7-te3 e5 ka-teng5 kap ka-ti7 e5 ka-teng5 e7 hiah toa7 e5 cheng-chha.  Chit8-si5 sim kim3 be7-tiau5, chiah kong2 cheng5 jit8 i tui3 Bok8-su e5 bo5 le2 hou7 sian-siN-niu5 in thiaN. Kong2 beh chai pai3 Siong7-te3 si7 hiah ho2, i tek-khak beh khah-cha2 pai3 Siong7-te3.    
Sian-siN-niu5 in chiah an-ui3--i, ka7 i kong2 cheng5-jit8 tui3 Bok8-su kap Oan5-che2 e5 su7 bo5 iau3-kin2, si7 in-ui7 m7-bat, Bok8-su in un2-tang3 bo5 khioh-hun7, un2-tang3 hoaN- hi2 sia3-bian2, ia7 kio3 i chit pai2 to3-khi3 tek- khak tioh8 khi3 le2-pai3.    
M7-ku2 Teng-hu5 so2 choe3 khoa3-lu7--e5, chiu7-si7 Lam5-iau5-kiong e5 goan7 iau2-boe7 sia7.  Sian-siN-niu5 chiah koh cheng5-thau5 it-li2 soat-beng5 hou7 i thiaN, sim kui2 hun u7 khah beng5-pek8 e5 khoan2.    
M7-ku2 iau2 chit8 chan5 toa7-su7, chiu7-si7 cha2-khi2 thiN-kng-cha2 thai5 tiong7-hu e5  
choe7 beh an2-choaN2 liau2-kiat.  Ia7 m7-chai taN i e5 tiong7-hu hou7 i thai5 chit8-e7, tau3-ti2 si7 chaiN2-iuN7 lah.  Sou2-i2 sim chin tiu-tu5.    
Chong2-si7 chit-si5 Teng-hu5 so2 ka-ti7 i2- keng bo5 chu7-sat e5 iong2-khi3 lah.  Ia7 tui3 thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 kap sian-siN e5 leng7 chhian-kim thiaN hiah che7 to7-li2, koh tai7- ke choe3-hoe2 ki5-to2, sou2-i2 ia7 kui2-hun ka-ti7 chai ka-ti7 e5 chho3, koh chai chu7-sat si7 m7- tioh8.  Sou2-i2 beh chu7-sat e5 sim thang kong2 i2-keng bo5 khi3 lah.    
TaN beh chaiN2-iuN7 ?  Liah8 chun2 thai5-si2 tiong7-hu liau2, ka-ti7 ia7 beh chu7-sat, it-sin3 tai7-ke si2, bian2 teh koh saN-kiN3, saN kau-tiN5. Na7-si7 taN ka-ti7 tu2-tioh8 ho2 kui3-jin5 iu7 si2 bo5 khi3.  Ban7-it na7 to3-khi3, tiong7-hu na7 i2-keng si2 beh an2-choaN2 ? Ia7 tiong7-hu na7 bo5 si2 beh an2-choaN2 ?  Sim toa7 kan-khou2 ? beh to3-khi3 m7 thih, m7 to3-khi3 m7-thih. Beh to3-khi3 chhu3-nih, taN m7 chai chaiN2-iuN7, Poh-kaN3 keng2-chhat, heng5-su7 long7-long7- kun2 lah, na7 m7 to3 khi3 tiaN7 toa3 thoan5-to7 sian-siN-niu5 tau ia7 si7 be7 eng7-tit.  Siat-su2 tiong7-hu na7 i2-keng si2, i tiam3 chia ia7 si7 bih be7-tiau5.  Koh Sian-siN-niu5 in ia7 chin pek-chhiat khou2-khng3 i tioh8 to3-khi3.  Su7 lai5 kau3 chia tek-khak tioh8 tam-su-iaN5, ka-ti7 kaN2 choe3 tioh8 kaN2 tng.  Siat-su2 tiong7-hu na7 i2-keng si2, ia7 tioh8 khi3 chu7- siu2, ia7 chhut-chai7 Siong7-te3 e5 chi2-i3, chi2-u7 jin7-choe7 ti7 Chu2 e5 bin7-cheng5, oan2-jian5 sip8-ji7-ke3 teng2 e5 chhat8.  Sin-the2 hok8- choe7 siu7 heng5, na7-si7 ng3-bang7 Chu2 khoan- su3 lin5-bin2 kiu3 li2 e5 leng5-hun5, chong2-si7 li2 tioh8 chhim-chhim e5 hoan2-hoe2 jin7-choe7.    
Teng-hu5 so2 thiaN kau3 chia koh khui-siaN hau2, siuN7 kong2 kin-a2-jit8 i thai3 e7 hou7 Mou5-kui2 jip8-sim, cho3 kau3 chiah-nih thang kiaN e5 su7.  Sian-siN-niu5 in bo2, kiaN2 khoaN3 chit e5 cheng5-heng5 ia7 si7 chin kan-khou2, chiu7 koh chit8 pai2 tai7-ke saN-chio kui7-loh8 lai5 ui7-tioh8 Teng-hu5 so2 jin7-choe7, Teng- hu5 so2 ia7 kui7-loh8 ka-ti7 jin7 kui2-ku3 choe7 ti7 Siong7-te3 e5 bin7-cheng5.  I chi2-u7 siuN7 i un2-tang3 e7 hou7 cheng3 hu2 liah8-khi3, chit8-si5 siuN7 kong2 kin-a2-jit8 i thai5 e7 cho3 kau3 chiah kian3-siau3, m7-na7 hian7-chai7 tiong7-hu e5 ka- teng5 e5 the2-bian7, kap au7-thau5 e5 pe7-bo2 e5 bin7-phoe5, koh phah-sng2 i thak8 hiah che7 chheh.  Kin-a2-jit8 na7 it-tan3 khia7 ti7 hoat- teng5 lai5 siu7 kong-phoaN3,  
cheng3-lang5 teh khoaN3, lai5 siu7 sat-hu e5 si2-heng5 phoaN3-koat, hit-si5 i beh thai2 u7 bin7 thang kiN3 lang5. Chit8-si5 kiaN kau3 ngiauh8-ngiauh8-chhoah, koh-khah hau2.  Hau2 kau3 bak8-chiu ham-ham, phiN7-chui2 khok8-khok7-tin.     
Na7-si7 sian-siN-niu5 in khoaN3 i an2-ni, soa3 siuN7 bo5 sim2-mih8 oe7 thang an-ui3 i, ia7 si7 kap i thin7-thau5 hau2, koh chit8 pai2 chhim- chhim kam2-kak choe7 e5 li7-hai7.  Sian-siN- niu5 koh the3 i ki5-to2, chai chi2-u7 ki5-to2 kiu5 Chu2 seng3 chhiu2 giah8 koan5, i2-goa7 bo5 lou7.    
Au7-lai5 an-ui3--i, ka7 i kong2, ng3-bang7 Teng-hu5 hiaN bo5 kau3 si2 e5 te7-pou7, ng3-bang7 Chu2 in-tian2 lin2, e7 koh u7 chit8 pai2 thoan5-oan5 e5 ki-hoe7.  An-ui3 soah, jit8 i2-keng mng2-pou, Teng-hu5 so2 chiu7 ka-ti7 koat-sim, bo5-lun7 chaiN2-iuN7 bo5 to3-khi3 chit8 choa7 ia7 si7 be7 eng7-tit, chiu7 koat-i3 beh to3-khi3.    
Sian-siN-niu5 in bo2-kiaN2 chiu7 sang3 i khi3 kau3 theng5-chhia-tiuN5, ia7 chiong chit8 pun2 Seng3-keng sang3--i, soa3 chhiaN2 i siong5 thak8 Seng3-keng, ki5-to2.  Teng-hu5 so2 chiu7 che7 chhia to3-khi3.    
Chiah kong2 Teng-hu5 hiaN hou7 lang5 siat-hoat khi3 i-iN7.  Sui-jian5 m7-si7 chin tang7 siong, iau2-ku2 tioh8 chiaN5-chap8 jit8 e5 ti7-liau5 chiah e7 ho2.  Ui7 se3-kan-thai3 (世 間態) kiaN-liau2 phaiN2-khoaN3-siuN3, koh ka- ti7 e5 te7-ui7, bin7-phoe5 e5 koan-he7, koh bo5 ti3- kau3 si2 e5 siong, chiu7 bo5 khi3 po3 keng2-chhat. Ia7 jip8-iN7 khah be7 hou7 lang5 lai5 bian7- hoe7, kou5-ioh8 ia7 khah li7-pian7, chu7 an2-ni bih tiam3 i-iN7 ti7-liau5, sia2 bian7-hoe7 sia7- choat8 e5 toaN tah ti7 goa7-bin7.  Oan5-che2 khoaN3 se3-bin7 an2-ni ia7 chin kho2-lin5 in, cheng5-jit8 hou7 in siak iam5-bi2 e5 su7 long2 bo5 koh ki3 ti7 sim, chhu3-piN tai7 koe3-khi3 lai7- bin7 ka7 i khioh-khioh ho2-se3, chong2-phou e5 hoeh ka7 i chhit-chhit leh, mng5 chhoe7 chit8 mng5 so2 ka7 i so2 leh, ia7 chiong hit e5 7 hoe3 e5 cha-bou2 gin2-na2 ka7 i chhoa7 khi3 chhu3-nih.  Ia7 khi3 ka7 Teng-hu5 hiaN kong2 i u7 the3 i siat-hoat lai7-bin7, koh gin2- na2 chhoa7 khi3 ti7 i hia,  
Teng-hu5 hiaN peng5- seng chai Oan2-che2 in chit8 ke si7 pai3 Siong7-te3. Ia7 tak8 hang7 chin gau5-cho3, chin u7 thiaN3-sim, chiu7 chin seh-sia7.    
Kong2 Teng-hu5 so2 che7 chiuN7 hoe2-chhia, sim chin hoan5-lo2, long2 m7-kaN2 giah8-thau5 khi2-lai5 khoaN3 lang5, chi2-u7 sim-lai7 am3-am3 ki5-to2.  Kong2, Siong7-te3 ah ! Ia5-sou Kiu3- chu2 ah !  Li2 na7-si7 u7-iaN2, u7 leng5, u7 siaN3, Li2 tioh8 kiu3 goa2 chit8 tiau5 kau2 mia7.  Goa2 to si7 m7-bat chiah e7 cho3 kau3 chit ho7 choe7. Li2 na7 su3 goa2 peng5-an, Ia5-sou li2 na7 the3 goa2 taN chit e5 taN3, goa2 chian7-pi7 tek-khak goan7 cho3 li2 e5 lou5-chai5, cha-bou2-kan2, goan7 po3 li2 e5 in, si3-ke3 lai5-khi3 kong2 li2 e5 to7 hou7 lang5 thiaN.  Sim-te2 chit8 ku3 ia7 goa2 Ia5-sou li2 tioh8 cho3 goa2 e5 Chu2, li2 e5 chhiu2 tioh8 giah8-koan5, jiau5 goa2 chian7-pi7 chit8 tiau5 kau2-mia7.  Nng7-ku3 ia7 goa2 Chu2 Ia5-sou li2 tioh8 cho3 goa2 e5 Chu2, jiau5 goa2 chian7-pi7 kau2- mia7.  Cheng5-jit8 e5 phui2-phui2 Ia5-sou phui2- phui2, long2 be7 ki3-tit lah.  Bak8-sai2 sip-sip lau5, chhiu2-kin na2 chhit, bian2 loa7-ku2 chiu7 kau3 ui7 loh8 chhia, kio3 chit8 tiuN jin5-lek8- chhia chit khun3 piaN3 kau3 in chhu3.    
Kau3-ui7 khoaN3-kiN3 mng5 so2 leh, sim-koaN chhiak8 chit8--e7, siuN7 kong2 taN hai7 lah, tai7- khai3 si7 chong3-sek liau2 lah.  Ia7 gin2-na2 tai7-khai3 si7 siu7 cheng3-hu2 po2-hou7, chiong- lai5 ti7 hoat-teng5 thang cho3 i5-it e5 cheng3- jin5.  In-ui7 teh thai5 hoah kiu3-lang5 e5 si5, lai7-bin7 chi2-u7 chit e5 gin2-na2 ti7-teh na7-tiaN7. Tu2 teh kiaN e5 si5, Oan5-che2 tui3 keh-piah khoaN3-kiN3 chit8 e5 cha-bou2-lang5 che7 jin5- lek8-chhia, ia7 jit8 i2-keng beh am3, hoan2-ou lah, chhia-phang5 kham3-kham3 leh, m7-ai3 lang5 khoaN3.  Tau3-ti2 chit e5 si7 sim2-mih8 lang5, chit8-e7 khoaN3 to si7 Teng-hu5 so2.  Oan5-che2 koaN2-kin2 ka7 i kio3 jip8-khi3, gin2-na2 ia7 lai5 chhoe7 in lau7-bo2, Oan5-che2 chiah chiong chiah e5 su7 cheng5-thau5 it-li2 kong2 hou7 i thiaN.    
Kong2 Teng-hu5 hiaN e5 siong bo5 loa7 tang7, i-seng kong2 oa2 chiaN5-chap8 jit8 chiu7 e7 ho2, hian7-chai7 jip8-iN7 tiong.  Teng-hu5- so2 sim-koaN hoang3 chit8-e7, sim khui-khui khi3.  Put-ti put-kak kong2 chit8 ku3, ah ! Kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3, Kiu3-chu2 Ia5-sou li2 u7 kiu3 goa2 chian7-pi7.    
Oan5-che2 chin ki5-koai3, chit e5 peng5-si5 to si7 phui2-phui2 Ia5-sou e5 lang5, kin-a2-jit8  
na2 e7 kong2 Siong7-te3 oe7, chin ki5-koai3 ! Oan5-che2 kin2 mng7 Teng-hu5 so2, li2 thai2 e7 kong2 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3 ?  Teng-hu5 so2 chiah chiong i kin-a2-jit8 beh khi3 Ko-bi2 thiau3 hai2, kau3 Chheng-chui2 sou2 tu2-tioh8 e5 su7.  Ia7 chiah thau2 i e5 chhiu2-kin, chiong Seng3-keng hou7 Oan5-che2 khoaN3.  Ah ! Oan5-che2 hoaN-hi2 kau3 lau5 bak8 sai2, nng7- lang5 chiah koh kui7-loh8 kam2-sia7 Chu2. Teng-hu5 so2 ia7 chai Teng-hu5 hiaN ui7 kiaN- liau2 ka-ti7 e5 te7-ui7 tui3 sia7-hoe7 phaiN2 khoaN3, koh bo5 kau3 loa7 tang7-siong, sou2-i2 bo5 khi3 po3 keng2-chhat.  Su7 i3-goa7 e5 an-cheng7 bo5 tong7-iau5, sou2-i2 Teng-hu5 so2 koh chit8 hoan e5 chhim-chhim kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3.    
Hit am3 Oan5-che2 lau5 Teng-hu5 so2 ti7 i e5 tau chiah8-png7, Teng-hu5 hiaN ti7 peN7-chhng5 kui2 hun ia7 si7 siuN-tioh8 ka-ti7 e5 chho3, chiah e7 lai5 pek hu7-jin5-lang5 kiaN5 kau3 chit tiau5 lou7.  Ia7 teh hoan5-lo2 ban7-it Teng-hu5 so2 na7 khi3 chu7-sat, i ia7 si7 chin toa7 e5 chek- seng5.  Koh kam2-kek Oan5-che2 chit8 ke cho3 chhu3-piN, si7 pai3 Siong7-te3 lang5, chin chhin-chhiat.  Sou2-i2 sim am3-am3 ia7 teh siuN7 kong2 chhu2-khi3 na7 e7 tit chit8 ke peng5- ho5, ia7 chin ai3 hou7 hu7-jin5-lang5 khi3 sin3-kau3.    
Kong2 kau3 hit am3 Oan5-che2 chiu7 chio Teng-hu5 so2 khi3 i-iN7 khoaN3 Teng-hu5 hiaN. Teng-hu5 so2 khi2-thau5 m7-kaN2 khi3.  Na7-si7 i tit8-tit8 ka7 i khou2-khng3.
