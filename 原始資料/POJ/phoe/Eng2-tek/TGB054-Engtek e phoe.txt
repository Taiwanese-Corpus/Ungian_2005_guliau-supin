¡qMou5-li7-chok8 e5 kou3-su7>

Lau7 thau5-bak8 e5 sin-pu7 seng-san2 soah koe3-sin--khi3, siang-seN-a2 lam5-eng bong5, lu2-eng oah8. Lau7-lang5 oan3-chheh nah e7 m7-si7 cha-pou gin2-a2 oah8-- loh8-lai5, hou7 in sit-khi3 1 e5 tong-jian5 e5 ke3-seng5-jin5 (ku7 thoan5-thong2, ku7 su-ui5).

Chit-e5 si5-chun7, khoaN3 chit-ki Mou5-li7-chok8, na2-chhiuN7 khai-si2 kiaN5 hiong3 ou-am3, chhoe7 bo5 chhut-lou7.
