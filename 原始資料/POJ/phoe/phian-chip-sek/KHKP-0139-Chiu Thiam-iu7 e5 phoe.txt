
Chiu Thiam-iu7 e5 phoe
Ku7-ni5 siok8 lan2 kau3-hoe7 nng7-e5 siau3-lian5 lang5 u7 hou7 Jit8-pun2 e5 peng5-iu2 chhiaN2 in khi3 Jit8-pun2 thak8-chheh.  Chiong5- tiong chit8-e5 si7 tiam3 ti7 PheN5-ou5 Ma2-keng e5 Tan5 Chheng-kang; chit8-e5 si7 Chiu Thiam-iu7, chiu7-si7 thoan5-to7 sian-siN Chiu Pou7-ha5 e5 hau7-siN. Hian7-kim in nng7 lang5 tiam3 ti7 Tang-kiaN thak8-chheh, sou2-hui3 long2 si7 Jit8-pun2 e5 peng5-iu2 tam-tng.     
Kin7-lai5 goan2 u7 chih-tioh8 Thiam-iu7 e5 phoe; taN chiong i-e5 siau-sit in3 ti7 e7-te2:-    
：Goa2 ti7 8 goeh8 8 jit8 chiu7-si7 pai3-lak8 u7 khi3 Yokohama [HoaiN5-pin] chhoe7 Se3- chhoan Sin-seN. Le2-pai3-jit8 ti7 hia le2-pai3, hit-keng pai3-tng5 si7 oa2 ti7 hai2-kiN5 mia5-kio3 Hai2-gan7 kau3-hoe7. Hit me5-hng u7 koh chu7-chip8, chong2-si7 bo5 iu5-goan5 ti7 hit sou2-chai7; si7 ti7 pat8 sou2-chai7, mia5 San-chhiu2-kiong7-lip8 lu2-hak8-hau7; u7 5, 6 chap8 lang5 ti7-hia chu7-chip8. Teh le2-pai3 e5 si5 Se3- chhoan Sin-seN u7 kah goa2 gim5 Tai5-oan5 e5 si kap thak8 Seng3-chheh hou7 in thiaN, au7 lai5 koh thak8 Jit8-pun2 e5 Seng3-chheh iu5- goan5 siang7 hit chiuN. Au7 lai5 in tiong- kan u7 chit8 lang5 peh--khi2-lai5 kong2, tui3 hit e5-hng khi2 beh chit8 le2-pai3 ku2, tak8 me5- hng kau3 hit keng oh8-tng5 cho3 ki5-to2-hoe7, it, the3 Tai5-oan5 e5 kau3-hoe7 kap peh-seN3 ki5-to2. It, the3 Jit8-pun2 ki5-to2. An2-ni hou7 lan2 thang toa7-toa7 kam2-sia7 Siong7-te3. Kau3 pai3-it goa2 chiu7 koh to3-tng2--lai5 Tang-kiaN ： Cheng5 chit Tang-kiaN ia7 u7 chhe chit8-e5  thoan5-to7--e5 khi3 Tai5-pak thoan5-to7, i-e5 seN3 Ho5-hap8 [Ka-oai]. Chit kin7-jit8 koh thiaN- kiN3 i ti7-hia u7 phoa3-peN7, chong2--si7 ng3-bang7 i kin2-kin2 ho2 thang cho3 Chu2 e5 kang li7-ek toa3 ti7 Tai5-pak e5 Jit8-pun2-lang5. Hian7-si5 Goa2 u7 peng5-an・・.  
(Tai5- lam5 Hu2-siaN5 kau3-hoe7-po3 te7 139 koan3,1896 ni5 10 geh8).
